 '''''PopMatters''''' is an [[online magazine]] about [[popular culture]]. It was started in 1999 by Sarah Zupko. ''PopMatters'' publishes reviews, [[interview]]s, and [[essay]]s about [[music]], [[television]], [[movies]], [[books]], [[video games]], [[comics]], [[sports]], and the [[internet]]. < ref > {{cite web | url=http://www.popmatters.com/about/ | title=About, PopMatters | date=8 April 2018 }} < /ref >
