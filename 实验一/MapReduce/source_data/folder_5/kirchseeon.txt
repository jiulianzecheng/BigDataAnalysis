 '''Kirchseeon''' is a market town in the [[Upper Bavaria]]n district of [[Ebersberg (district)|Ebersberg]] and is 15 & nbsp;km east of Munich city limits. 
 
 The nearest communities are [[Grafing]] and [[Ebersberg]]. The Bavarian capital, [[Munich]], can be reached by [[Munich S-Bahn|S-Bahn]] (line S4), which runs over the [[Munich–Salzburg railway|Munich–Salzburg line]]. [[Rosenheim]] and [[Wasserburg am Inn]] are each about 25 & nbsp;km away.
