 '''Lancy''' is a [[municipality]] of the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Geneva (canton)|Geneva]] in [[Switzerland]]. The municipality is on the left bank of the [[Rhone]] river. As of December 2016, 31,661 people lived there.
