The '''Konariotes''' were descendants of [[Turkish people|Turkish]] [[shepherd]]s from [[Konya]], who settled in [[Macedonia (region)]] around 1360 and called by the Greek as Konariotes.  
 Big settlements were in [[Ptolemaida]] and [[Kozani]].
