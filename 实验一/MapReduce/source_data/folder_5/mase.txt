 '''Mason Betha''', known simply as '''Mase''', (stylized as Ma$e; born August 27, 1975) is an [[Americans|American]] [[rap]]per and [[Minister (Christianity)|minister]]. Mase first performed under the name '''Murda Mase'''.  
 ==Life== 
 Mase was born on August 27, 1975 in [[Jacksonville, Florida]]. He grew up in [[Harlem]], [[New York City]]. In 1993, he formed the group Children of the Corn ( " corn "  short for  " corner " ) with fellow Harlem rappers Killa Cam, [[Big L]], Herb McGruff and Bloodshed.  
 
 Mase's first album, ''Harlem World'' (1997), was certified [[Music recording sales certification|quadruple platinum]] by the [[Recording Industry Association of America|RIAA]]. His other albums, ''Double Up'' (1999) and ''Welcome Back'' (2004), were certified gold.
