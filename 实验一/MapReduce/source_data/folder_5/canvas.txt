 '''Canvas''' is a thick [[material]] usually made of [[Weaving|woven]] [[cotton]].  It is used for tents and sails on boats and other places where a sturdy material would be good.  It is similar to the material in blue jeans. It is often a white or pale [[Tan (color)|tan]] color. It can also be used for [[paint]]ing or even making [[shoe]]s.
