 '''Buchbach''' is a [[Municipalities of Germany|municipality]] in [[Mühldorf (district)|Mühldorf]] in [[Bavaria]] in [[Germany]]. 
 
 == Geography == 
 The municipality is in the region of southeastern Upper Bavaria in the [[Alpine foothills|foothills of the Alps]]. Buchbach borders directly on the districts of [[Erding]] and [[Landshut]]. The state capital Munich is about 63 km away.
