 '''Pomarkku''' ({{lang-sv|Påmark}}) is a [[municipality]] in [[Satakunta]] in [[Finland]]. It's nearby [[Pori]] and [[Kankaanpää]]. The municipality has a population of 2,196 (31 August 2017). 
 
 In the [[civil war]], Pomarkku had a big battle with several deads.
