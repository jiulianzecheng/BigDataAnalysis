 '''Marga''' can mean: 
 
 ==Religion== 
 *Marga in [[Hinduism]] refers to a way of accomplishing something such as [[yoga]] or [[sadhana]] 
 *[[Buddhist Paths to liberation|Marga]] ([[Sanskrit]] ''mārga''; [[Pali|Pãli]] ''magga'',  " path " ) in Buddhism refers to the path to enlightenment 
 
 ==Places== 
 *[[Marga, Estonia]], a village in Estonia 
 *[[Marga (East Syrian Diocese)]], a mediaeval diocese of the Church of the East 
 *[[Marga, Caraş-Severin|Marga]] ({{lang-hu|Márga}}), a commune in Caraş-Severin County, western Romania 
 *Marga, a village in [[Godeanu]] Commune, Mehedinţi County, western Romania 
 *[[Marga, Tabanan]], a subdistrict (Kecamatan) of Tabanan Regency, Bali, Indonesia 
 *[[Marga Marga Province]], one of the eight provinces in the central Chilean region of Valparaíso 
 *[[Marga River]], a tributary of the Bistra River in Romania
