'''Yemenis''' or '''Yemenites''' are citizens of the country of [[Yemen]]. Currently there are 31,461,438 Yemenis in the country of Yemen. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Yemen Population (2022) - Worldometer|url=https://www.worldometers.info/world-population/yemen-population/|access-date=2022-11-13|website=www.worldometers.info|language=en}} < /ref >  There are also [[:wikt:significant|significant]] populations of Yemenis in [[Saudi Arabia]], [[Egypt]], [[Israel]], [[United States]], [[UAE]], and the [[UK]].
