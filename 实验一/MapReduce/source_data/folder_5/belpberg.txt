 '''Belpberg''' is a former [[municipality]] of the [[Bern-Mittelland (administrative district)|Bern-Mittelland]] [[administrative district]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Canton of Bern|Bern]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 The municipality of Belpberg merged on 1 January 2012 into the municipality of [[Belp]].
