 '''Sadomasochism''' is a word which is mostly used in a social (non-medical) way. It means that people get together to either give or receive pain and humiliation. Usually it's a form of [[sexual intercourse|sex]] play.  
 
 ''[[Sadism|Sadists]]'' enjoy inflicting pain on others or humiliating them. ''[[Masochism|Masochists]]'' get pleasure from being tortured, hurt, or humiliated.
