 '''Hemiphractidae''' is a [[family (biology)|family]] of [[frog]]s.  They live in [[South America|South]] and [[Central America]].  Previously, this group was classified as a subfamily under family [[Hylidae]].   
 
 ==Breeding behavior== 
 [[File:Flectonotus pygmaeus02.jpg|thumb|A female ''[[Flectonotus pygmaeus]]'' with eggs enclosed on the back]] 
 This frog is different in that the eggs grow into froglets on the back of the female.  In most there is no free-swimming [[tadpole]] stage. In some of the frogs the eggs are in a pouch until they hatch. 
 
 == Taxonomy == 
 The Hemiphractidae family is divided into the following [[genus|genera]]: 
 * ''[[Cryptobatrachus]]'' - Backpack Frogs 
 * ''[[Flectonotus]]'' 
 * ''[[Gastrotheca]]'' - Marsupial Frogs 
 * ''[[Hemiphractus]]'' - Horned Treefrogs 
 * ''[[Stefania]]'' - Carrying Frogs
