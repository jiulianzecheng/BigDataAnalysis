 The '''Sambre''' is a river that flows through France and Belgium. It empties into the [[Meuse (river)|Meuse]] at the city of [[Namur (city)|Namur]] from the left side, and has a length of 193 km. 
 
 The Sambre river springs from the French commune of [[Fontenelle, Aisne|Fontenelle]] in the [[Hauts-de-France]] region at an altitude of 210 m. It continues through the Belgian provinces of [[Hainaut]] and [[Namur (province)|Namur]], passing the city of [[Charleroi]].
