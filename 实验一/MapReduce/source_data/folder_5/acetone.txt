 '''Acetone''', or '''propanone''', is an [[organic compound]] with the [[chemical formula]] (CH3)2CO. This clear, mobile, easy-to-burn [[liquid]] is the simplest example of the [[ketone]]s. Acetone can be mixed with [[water]]. It is an important [[solvent]], often to clean things in the [[laboratory]]. Common uses of acetone in the home are as the active ingredient in nail polish remover and as paint thinner. It is a common building block in [[organic chemistry]].
