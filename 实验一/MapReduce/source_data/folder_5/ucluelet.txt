 '''Ucluelet''' is a [[district municipality]] in the [[Canada|Canadian]] [[Provinces and territories of Canada|province]] of [[British Columbia]]. Ucluelet is found on the Ucluelet Peninsula on the west coast of [[Vancouver Island]]. The name Ucluelet means  " people of the safe harbour "  in the native [[Nuu-chah-nulth language|Nuu-chah-nulth]] (Nootka) language. The population of Ucluelet was 1,627 in 2011. < ref name=bcstats > {{cite web|url=http://www.bcstats.gov.bc.ca/StatisticsBySubject/Census/2011Census/PopulationHousing/MunicipalitiesByRegionalDistrict.aspx|accessdate=2014-07-06|title=2011 Census Total Population Results: Municipalities by Regional District|publisher=BCStats|archive-date=2014-06-17|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140617161230/http://www.bcstats.gov.bc.ca/StatisticsBySubject/Census/2011Census/PopulationHousing/MunicipalitiesByRegionalDistrict.aspx|url-status=dead}} < /ref >  The mayor of Ucluelet is Bill Irving. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://ucluelet.ca/community/district-of-ucluelet-council|accessdate=2014-06-07|title=Council|publisher=Ucluelet}} < /ref >
