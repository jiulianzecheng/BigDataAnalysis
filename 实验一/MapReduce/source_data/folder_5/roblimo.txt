 '''Robin  " Roblimo "  Miller''' (October 30, 1952 â€“ May 24, 2018) was an American journalist specializing in [[Technology journalism |technology]]. He worked for [[SourceForge, Inc.|Open Source Technology Group]], the company that owned [[Slashdot]], [[SourceForge.net]], [[freshmeat]], [[Linux.com]], [[NewsForge]], and [[ThinkGeek]] from 2000 to 2008. He was born in [[Elkridge, Maryland|Elkridge]], [[Maryland]]. 
 
 Miller had been a judge for the [[Lulu Blooker Prize]] and was on the online advisory board of the ''[[Online Journalism Review]]'' of the [[Annenberg Center for Communication]] at the [[University of Southern California]].
