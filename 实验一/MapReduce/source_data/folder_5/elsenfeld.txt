 '''Elsenfeld''' is a market community in the [[Miltenberg (district)|Miltenberg district]] in the [[Lower Franconia]] [[Regierungsbezirk|region]] in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. It is on the [[Main (river)|river Main]] between the hills of the [[Spessart]], east of the river, and [[Odenwald]], west of the river. Elsenfeld has about 9,000 people living there. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 ===Place=== 
 Elsenfeld is in the  " Bavarian Lower Main Region "  (''Bayerischer Untermain'') on the [[Main (river)|Main]]'s right edge. Running through the community is the brook [[Elsava]], which releases into the Main at Elsenfeld. 
 
 ===Subdivisions=== 
 The community has the following districts: Eichelsbach, Elsenfeld, Rück, Schippach and Himmelthal (formerly a monastery estate).
