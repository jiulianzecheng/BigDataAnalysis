 '''Langley''' is a village and [[civil parish]] in [[Uttlesford]], [[Essex]], England. In 2001 there were 334 people living in Langley. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.ons.gov.uk/help/localstatistics|title=Local statistics - Office for National Statistics|website=www.ons.gov.uk}} < /ref >  The name Langley means 'a long clearing in woodland'. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.recordinguttlesfordhistory.org.uk/langley/langleyhistory.html|title=Local History of Langley Village in Essex|website=www.recordinguttlesfordhistory.org.uk}} < /ref >
