 '''Butterscotch''' is a [[confectionary|confection]] (like [[candy]]) made from [[brown sugar]], [[butter]] and [[cream]]. It is a bit like [[toffee]] but is made differently. Butterscotch is made by [[boiling]] [[sugar]] until it is at the  " soft crack "  stage, which is a slightly lower [[temperature]] than for toffee. Butterscotch can be used as a [[sauce]] for [[dessert]]s like [[ice cream]].
