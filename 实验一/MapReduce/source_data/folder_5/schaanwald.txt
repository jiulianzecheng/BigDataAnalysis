 '''Schaanwald''' is a village of [[Liechtenstein]]. It is in the [[municipality]] of [[Mauren]]. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 The village is in north-central Liechtenstein. It is on the main road that links Liechtenstein with [[Buchs, St. Gallen|Buchs]], [[Switzerland]] and [[Feldkirch]], [[Austria]].
