 '''Kirchnerism''' ({{lang-es|Kirchnerismo}} {{IPA-es|kiɾʃneˈɾizmo|}}) is an Argentine political movement which supports the [[populism|populist]] ideals of [[Néstor Kirchner]] and his wife [[Cristina Fernández de Kirchner]], both of which were [[President of Argentina|Presidents of Argentina]].
