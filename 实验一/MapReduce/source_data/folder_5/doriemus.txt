 '''Doriemus''' (17 November 1990  & ndash; 11 January 2015) was a [[Thoroughbred]] [[Horse racing|racehorse]]. He began his career in [[New Zealand]] and became famous [[Australia]] by winning the [[Caulfield Cup|Caulfield]] and [[Melbourne Cup]]s in 1995. He became just the ninth horse to win both cups. 
 
 Doriemus was [[Euthanasia|euthanised]] due to a [[hoof]] infection after a paddock accident on 11 January 2015.
