 '''Lobamba''' is the [[Legislature|legislative]] [[Capital (city)|capital]] of [[Swaziland|Eswatini]] (which used to be called Swaziland). The [[Parliament (Swaziland)|Parliament]] of Eswatini and home of the [[Ntombi of Swaziland|Queen Mother]] are in this city. It is located in the west of the [[country]], in the district of [[Hhohho]]. Its [[population]] is about 10,000 people. < ref > As of [[1995]] < /ref > 
 
 Lobamba is famous for two [[Ceremony|ceremonies]]. The [[Reed Dance]] is in August and September. This [[celebration]] is in honour of the Queen Mother. The [[Incwala Kingship]], in December and January, is in honour of the King. These ceremonies include [[dance|dancing]], [[song|singing]], and celebrations with [[tradition]]al [[clothing]]. 
 
 The [[Embo State Palace]], the [[Royal Kraal]], [[Swazi National Museum]], [[Swazi Parliament]] and a [[museum]] about [[Sobhuza II of Swaziland|Sobhuza II of Eswatini]] are in the town. The [[Mlilwane Wildlife Sanctuary]] and [[Matsapha Airport]] lie nearby.
