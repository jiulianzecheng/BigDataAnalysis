 '''Enguinegatte''' is a former [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is found in the [[Regions of France|region]] [[Nord-Pas-de-Calais]] in the [[Pas-de-Calais]] [[Departments of France|department]] in the north of [[France]]. On 1 January 2017, it was merged into the new commune of [[Enquin-lez-Guinegatte]]. < ref > [https://www.legifrance.gouv.fr/eli/arrete/2016/6/30/INTB1619043A/jo/texte Arrêté préfectoral] 30 June 2016 {{in lang|fr}} < /ref >
