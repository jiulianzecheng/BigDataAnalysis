 '''Germering''' is a [[Town#Germany|town]] of about 40,500 within [[Fürstenfeldbruck (district)|Fürstenfeldbruck]], in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. It is directly near to the city of [[Munich]] and borders it to the west.
