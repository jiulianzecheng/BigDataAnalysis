 '''Bagan''' ({{IPA|bəɡàɴ|IPA}}; also called '''Pagan''') is an ancient [[city]] in the [[Mandalay]] region of [[Myanmar]]. It was the [[Capital (city)|capital]] of the [[Pagan Kingdom|Kingdom of Pagan]] from the 9th to 13th centuries. During the kingdom's [[golden age]] between the 11th and 13th centuries, over 10,000 [[Buddhism|Buddhist]] [[temple]]s, [[pagoda]]s and [[Monastery|monasteries]] were built in Bagan. 
 
 Now, the [[Archaeology|archaeological]] remains of over 2,200 of these temples and pagodas are in the surrounding plains. It is one of the biggest places for tourism in the country.
