 '''Duiven''' ([[Liemers (dialect)|Liemers]]: ''Duve'', [[Dutch Low Saxon|Low Saxon]]: ''Duven'') is a municipality and town in the province of [[Gelderland]], the Netherlands. About 25,000 people were living there in 2021. 
 
 Duiven lies in the [[Liemers]] region, on the east bank of the [[Rhine]]  river.
