 '''Minerva''' was the [[Roman mythology|Roman]] [[goddess]] of crafts and [[wisdom]] whose symbol was the [[owl]]. The Greeks called her [[Athena]] but the Romans didn't make her as warlike as Athena. As Minerva Medica she was goddess of medicine and doctors. Many coins showed her.
