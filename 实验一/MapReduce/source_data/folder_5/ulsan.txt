 '''Ulsan''' is a [[city|metropolitan city]] in the southeast of [[South Korea]]. Big [[company|companies]] such as [[Hyundai Heavy Industries]], [[Hyundai Hysco]], [[Hyundai Mipo Dockyard]], [[Hyundai Motor Company]], [[SK Innovation]] and [[S-Oil]]. are in Ulsan. Making [[motor]]s, [[shipbuilding]] and [[petrochemical industry|petrochemical companies]] are the main [[industry|industries]] in Ulsan. It has the largest [[area]] of Korean metropolitan cities but it has the lowest [[population]]. In Ulsan, there is a Whale Festival every year to celebrate [[whale]]s.
