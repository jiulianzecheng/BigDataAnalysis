 '''Louvaines''' is a former [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is in [[Pays de la Loire]] in the [[Maine-et-Loire]] [[Departments of France|department]] in west [[France]]. On 15 December 2016, it was merged into the new commune of [[Segré-en-Anjou Bleu]]. < ref > [https://www.legifrance.gouv.fr/eli/arrete/2016/9/28/INTB1629265A/jo/texte Arrêté préfectoral] 28 September 2016 {{in lang|fr}} < /ref >
