 '''News''' is when people talk about '''current events''' (things that are happening right now). News Media is a portrayal of current affairs, perspectives and social influence. The news can be given in [[newspaper]]s, [[television]], [[magazine]]s, or [[radio]]. There are several news channels on [[cable television]] that give news all day long, such as [[Fox News]] and [[CNN]].  There are several news magazines, such as ''[[Time (magazine)|Time]]'', ''[[The Economist]]'', and ''[[Newsweek]]''. A [[newsman]] is a person who helps out with the news. News media can be viewed in many forms, such as newspaper, television, and radio.
