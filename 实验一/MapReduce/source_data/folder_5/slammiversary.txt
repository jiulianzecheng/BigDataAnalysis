'''Slammiversary''' is a yearly [[pay-per-view]] made by [[Total Nonstop Action Wrestling]] (TNA). The event is usually produced in [[June]]. The first Slammiversary event took place on June 19, 2005 at the [[Impact Zone]] in [[Orlando, Florida]]. The name of the pay-per-view is a reference to the anniversary of the first TNA event which took place on June 19, 2002.
