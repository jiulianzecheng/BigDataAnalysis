 '''Hohtenn''' is a village and former [[municipality]] of the [[district]] of [[Raron (district)|Raron]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Valais]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 On 1 January 2009, the former municipalities of Hohtenn and [[Steg, Switzerland|Steg]] merged into the new municipality of [[Steg-Hohtenn]].
