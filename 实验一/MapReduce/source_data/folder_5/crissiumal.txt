 '''Crissiumal''' is a [[municipality]] in the [[States of Brazil|state]] [[Rio Grande do Sul]], [[Brazil]]. It has a [[population]] of 14,726 people (2007 â€“ [[IBGE]]). It has a river border with [[Argentina]] along the [[Uruguay River]].  
 
 In [[sports]], Crissiumal is known as  " The land of goalkeepers " . This is because it was the home of several [[goalkeeper]]s. The most known of them is [[Taffarel]], who played in the [[Brazilian national soccer team|Brazilian Soccer Team]] in the [[1994 FIFA World Cup]], in [[Italy]]. 
 
 The [[economy]] of Crissiumal is based on small [[rural]] properties. Crissiumal is one of the greatest Brazilian [[milk]] producers. It has a daily production of about 70,000 [[liter]]s of milk.
