 " '''Hearteater''' "  (written in all caps) is the second single off of [[Americans|American]] [[Rap music|rapper]] [[XXXTentacion]]'s fourth and final [[studio album]] ''[[Bad Vibes Forever]]''. The song was released on October 22, 2019. 
 
 The song was able to make it to #36 on [[Recorded Music NZ|RMNZ]]'s Hot Singles Chart. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://nztop40.co.nz/chart/hotsingles?chart=4766|accessdate=September 7, 2023|title=NZ Hot Singles Chart (28 October 2019)|publisher=[[Recorded Music NZ]]}} < /ref >   
 
 Geneva Ayala, the woman who accused X of [[domestic abuse]] appears on the cover art of the song. She has a mouth covered in blood and is holding a heart. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://genius.com/a/xxxtentacion-s-hearteater-cover-art-features-the-woman-he-was-charged-with-abusing|accessdate=September 7, 2023|title=XXXTentacion’s “Hearteater” Cover Art Features The Woman He Was Charged With Abusing|publisher=[[Genius (company)|Genius]]}} < /ref > 
 
 In the song, X sings over an instrumental with drums and an [[electric guitar]]. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://uproxx.com/music/xxxtentacion-hearteater-bad-vibes-forever/|accessdate=August 31, 2023|title=XXXTentacion Goes Full-On Alternative Rock With The New Posthumous Single 'Hearteater'|publisher=[[Uproxx]]}} < /ref >  He sings about a woman who broke his heart took advantage of his love. It is believed that he is talking about Ayala. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.hotnewhiphop.com/194303-xxxtentacions-hearteater-earns-official-release-new-song|accessdate=August 31, 2023|title=XXXTentacion’s “Hearteater” Earns Official Release|publisher=[[HotNewHipHop]]}} < /ref > < ref name= " GenYT " > {{cite web|url=https://youtube.com/watch?v=DjfyZHeBKLg|accessdate=September 7, 2023|title=XXXTENTACION’s “HEARTEATER” Explained - Song Stories|publisher=[[YouTube]]}} < /ref > 
 
 A music video for the song was released. It was directed by JMP. In the video, Geneva Ayala appears in a forest. She is tearing apart and [[Cannibalism|eating]] a man's dead body and heart. She walks out of the forest, naked and covered in blood. The man who she is eating is believed to be X since the body is wearing the same clothes X was in his  " [[Moonlight (song)|Moonlight]] "  music video. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.billboard.com/music/rb-hip-hop/xxxtentactions-hearteater-video-8540618/|accessdate=September 7, 2023|title=XXXTentaction’s Bloodthirsty ‘Hearteater’ Video Features Ex-Girlfriend as a Cannibal|publisher=[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]}} < /ref > < ref name= " GenYT " / > 
 
 In a voice memo recorded before his murder, X said  " I feel like tying her into it makes the video amazing, and it almost, like, really captures everyone's minds and gives me the power that I truly deserve.
