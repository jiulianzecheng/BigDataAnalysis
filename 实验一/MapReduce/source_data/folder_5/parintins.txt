 '''Parintins''' is a [[Brazil]]ian city of the state of [[Amazonas (Brazil)|Amazonas]]. It has 107,250 inhabitants and an area of 5.952 [[km²]]. It was founded in [[1852]] and it is one of the most important cities in state of Amazonas.
