 '''Bellagio''' is a small town on the shores of [[Lake Como]] in northern Italy. The southern part of Lake Como is split into two branches by a [[peninsula]]. The peninsula is shaped like a [[triangle]]. Bellagio is on the northern tip of the peninsula. In 2017 about 3,700 people lived in Bellagio.
