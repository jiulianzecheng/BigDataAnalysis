 '''''Begonia''''' is a [[genus]] of [[perennial]] [[flowering plant]]s. The genus contains about 1,400 different plant [[species]]. Begonias are found in moist [[subtropics|subtropical]] and [[tropics|tropical]] [[climate]]s. Some species are often grown indoors as [[houseplants]].
