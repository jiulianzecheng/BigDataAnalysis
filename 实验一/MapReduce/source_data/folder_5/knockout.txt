'''Knockout''', also known as the '''knockout game''' and '''knockout king''', is a violent activity played by teenagers. The object of the game is to render unconscious (knockout) an innocent [[pedestrian]] with one punch. < ref name= " Salter " > {{cite news |title=Unprovoked attacks at heart of 'Knockout King' |author=Salter, Jim |url=http://hosted2.ap.org/APDEFAULT/3d281c11a96b4ad082fe88aa0db04305/Article_2011-12-24-Thrill%20Assaults/id-ec1b19d37a334c6e909beb03e1b6237f |publisher=[[Associated Press]] |date=December 24, 2011 |accessdate=December 29, 2011 |archiveurl=https://www.webcitation.org/64J2wq7iH?url=http://hosted2.ap.org/APDEFAULT/3d281c11a96b4ad082fe88aa0db04305/Article_2011-12-24-Thrill%20Assaults/id-ec1b19d37a334c6e909beb03e1b6237f |archivedate=December 30, 2011 |url-status=live }} < /ref >  The game can result in serious injuries or death for its victims. Players face criminal charges. 
 
 In 1992, a teen stabbed an [[MIT]] student through the heart, killing him. < ref > {{cite news|title=MIT student's death attributed to violent  " knockout "  game|url=https://news.google.com/newspapers?id=2pZGAAAAIBAJ & sjid=lvgMAAAAIBAJ & pg=6167,4733376 & dq=violent+knockout+game & hl=en|accessdate=22 April 2011|newspaper=[[The Day (New London)|The Day]]|date=September 23, 1992}} < /ref >  In another case, a [[St. Louis, Missouri]] teenager allegedly attacked a pedestrian who hit his head on the ground and died in hospital. < ref > {{cite news|last=Mann|first=Jennifer|title=Violence for fun may be spreading among teens|url=http://www.stltoday.com/news/local/crime-and-courts/article_a234c676-46b7-5c66-a193-dd09fc49d8b8.html|accessdate=22 April 2011|newspaper=[[St. Louis Post-Dispatch]]|date=April 22, 2011}} < /ref >  In May 2013, a 51 year-old man died from his injuries after he was attacked in [[Syracuse, New York]], by two teenagers. < ref > {{cite news|last=Benny|first=Michael|title=Deadly and savage attack on Syracuse man was part of teen game called 'Knock Out'|url=http://www.cnycentral.com/news/story.aspx?id=904124#.Uaf7gStgaZK|accessdate=30 May 2013|newspaper=[[CNYCentral.com]]|date=May 30, 2013|archive-date=9 June 2013|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130609003815/http://www.cnycentral.com/news/story.aspx?id=904124#.Uaf7gStgaZK|url-status=dead}} < /ref >
