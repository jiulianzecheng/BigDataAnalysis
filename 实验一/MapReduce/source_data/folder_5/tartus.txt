 '''Tartus''' ({{lang-ar|طرطوس}} / [[ALA-LC]]: ''Ṭarṭūs''; also known as '''Tartous''') is a city on the Mediterranean coast of [[Syria]]. It is the second largest port city in Syria (after [[Latakia]]), with 115,769 inhabitants (2004 census).  It is on the eastern coast of the [[Mediterranean Sea]].
