 '''Miiverse'''{{efn|A portmanteau of  " [[Mii]] "  and  " [[universe]] " }} was a [[social network]] for [[Nintendo 3DS]] and [[Wii U]]. It was made by [[Nintendo]] and [[Hatena (company)|Hatena]]. It was powered by the [[Nintendo Network]]. Miiverse was made for people to talk with [[Handwriting|handwritten]] messages. In some [[Community|communities]], they made videos of games. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Nintendo 3DS Gets A Big Update Next Month|url=https://kotaku.com/nintendo-3ds-gets-a-big-update-next-month-1463684684|access-date=2021-02-17|website=Kotaku|language=en-us|archive-date=2021-08-28|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210828062921/https://kotaku.com/nintendo-3ds-gets-a-big-update-next-month-1463684684|url-status=live}} < /ref >  You could use Miiverse with any [[web browser]]. There was also an [[Mobile app|app]] version. It was planned to be made for [[Tablet computer|tablets]] and [[smartphones]]. All [[User (computing)|users]] who got a Nintendo Network ID were given a Miiverse account. They were given their own [[Mii]] avatar for that Nintendo Network ID.
