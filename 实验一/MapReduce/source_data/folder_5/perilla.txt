 '''''Perilla''''' is a [[genus]] of [[annual]] [[herb]] which is a member of the [[Mentha|mint]] [[family (biology)|family]]. In mild [[climate]]s the plant can reseed itself. There are both green [[leaf]]ed and purple leafed versions of this plant. The plant's leaves resemble stinging [[nettle]] leaves, though they are slightly rounder in shape. 
 
 ==Uses== 
 * Koreans use ''Perilla frutescens'' leaves and seeds in cooking. 
 * Japanese use ''Perilla frutescens'' var. ''crispa'' leaves in cooking.
