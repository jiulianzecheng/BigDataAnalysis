 The community of '''Isterberg''' in [[Lower Saxony]]’s district of [[County of Bentheim (district)|Bentheim]] came into being in the 1970s through the combination of the two previous communities of Wengsel and Neerlage. It is between [[Bad Bentheim]] and [[Nordhorn]] and belongs to the Joint Community (''Samtgemeinde'') of [[Schüttorf]], whose administrative seat is in the like-named town.
