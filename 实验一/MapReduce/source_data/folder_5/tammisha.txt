'''Tammisha''' (also spelled '''Tamisha''' or '''Tammishe'''; {{Lang-fa|تمیشه|Tamīšeh}}) was a [[mediaeval]] city in Tabaristan. It is in the north of [[Iran]], at the foot of the [[Alborz Mountains|Alborz]] mountain. It was a very important place, and kings often lived there.{{sfn|Bivar|Fehérvári|1999|p=36}} The city was near Gurgan. It had a famous wall called the Tammisha Wall, which was built during the [[Sasanian Empire|Sasanian]] era. It went from the Gorgan Bay and was 11 kilometres (6.8 miles) long.{{sfn|Rekavandi|Sauer|2013}}
