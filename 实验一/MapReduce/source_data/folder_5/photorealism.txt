 '''Photorealism''' is a style of [[painting]] or [[drawing]]. The picture is done with a lot of detail to make it look exactly like a photograph. The subjects pictured were often boring, without any special interest. < ref name= " pr " > {{cite web |url= http://www.artcyclopedia.com/history/photorealism.html |title=Photorealism |first= |last= |work=artcyclopedia.com |year=2011 |accessdate=31 January 2013}} < /ref >  Photorealism developed as an art style in the 1960s and 1970s. Two famous photorealist artists are [[Richard Estes]] and [[Chuck Close]]. < ref name= " pr " / >
