'''Borre''' is a small town in [[Horten]] municipality, [[Vestfold og Telemark]] county. Around 1056 (2013) lives there. < ref > {{Cite journal|date=2020-10-24|title=Borre|url=https://no.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Borre & oldid=20853200|journal=Wikipedia|language=nb}} < /ref > 
 
 == History == 
 Borre was a center of power during the Viking age, and is mentioned in Snorre Sturlason's  " Heimskringla " . < ref > {{Cite web|last=Bjønnes|first=av: Fredrik|title=Borre - et maktsenter i Vestfold|url=https://vestfoldmuseene.no/fordypning/borre-et-maktsenter-i-vestfold/|access-date=2020-11-04|website=Vestfoldmuseene|language=nb-NO}} < /ref > 
 [[File:Middelalderkirken på Borre i Horten, Borre kirke, med den flotte steinmuren i forgrunnen.jpg|thumb|Borre Church]] 
 Borre and Horten was originally one town, but was divided in 1857. In 1988 the two towns was joined under the municipality Borre, but was changed to Horten in 2002. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Borre – lokalhistoriewiki.no|url=https://lokalhistoriewiki.no/wiki/Borre|access-date=2020-11-04|website=lokalhistoriewiki.no}} < /ref >
