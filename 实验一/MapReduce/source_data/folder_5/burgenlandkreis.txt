 '''Burgenlandkreis''' is a district in [[Saxony-Anhalt]], [[Germany]]. It was formed by joining the old districts of [[Burgenlandkreis (former district)|Burgenlandkreis]] and [[Weißenfels (district)|Weißenfels]] as part of the boundary changes of [[2007]]. 
 
 On [[16 July]] [[2007]] the district parliament decided to change the name back to Burgenlandkreis from [[1 August]] [[2007]]. < ref > [http://www.lk-burgenland.de/index.php?cid=118024002410 Namechange announcement]{{Dead link|date=January 2021 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }} < /ref >
