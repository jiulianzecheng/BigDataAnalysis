A '''tourniquet''' is a tightly-compressed [[bandage]] used to stop [[bleeding]]. A tourniquet does this by stopping the flow of [[blood]] through a large [[artery]] in a [[limb]]. 
 It can be made from [[clothing]] or any tightly-compressible material in the case of an [[emergency]].
