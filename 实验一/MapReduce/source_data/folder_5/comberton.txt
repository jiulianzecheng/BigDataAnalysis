 '''Comberton''' is a village and [[civil parishes in England|civil parish]] in [[South Cambridgeshire]], [[England]]. It is about 10 & nbsp;km (6 & nbsp;miles) south-west of the city of [[Cambridge]]. 
 
 Comberton has a population of about 2,400. < ref > {{cite web|title=South Cambridgeshire District Council population forecasts|url=http://www.cambridgeshire.gov.uk/NR/rdonlyres/69A34FA3-F74F-4263-A4EB-B53C97BFA16C/0/SCpopLA1207.csv|access-date=2009-12-19|archive-date=2011-06-09|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110609191020/http://www.cambridgeshire.gov.uk/NR/rdonlyres/69A34FA3-F74F-4263-A4EB-B53C97BFA16C/0/SCpopLA1207.csv|url-status=dead}} < /ref >  It has two churches: an [[Church of England|Anglican]] church dating from the 13th century, < ref > {{cite web|title=St. Mary's website: History|url=http://www.combertonchurch.co.uk/Index.asp?MainID=6816|access-date=2009-12-19|archive-date=2011-05-30|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110530010908/http://www.combertonchurch.co.uk/Index.asp?MainID=6816|url-status=dead}} < /ref >  and a [[Baptist]] church dating from 1861. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.c-b-c.org.uk/Index.asp?MainID=7065|title=CBC website|access-date=2009-12-19}} < /ref > 
 
 Its neighbouring village is Barton. < ref > {{Cite web|url = http://barton-cambs.org.uk/Barton%20Parish%20Council.shtml|title = google!|archive-date = 2016-01-11|archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20160111065550/http://barton-cambs.org.uk/Barton%20Parish%20Council.shtml|url-status = dead}} < /ref >
