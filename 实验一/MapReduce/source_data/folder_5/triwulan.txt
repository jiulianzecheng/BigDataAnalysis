 '''Triwulan''' (born 1934) was a female Indonesian sprinter from [[Malang]]. Her main achievements were between 1950 and 1953. She was a small but had a good style. < ref name=preview > {{cite web|url= https://resolver.kb.nl/resolve?urn=ddd:010474968:mpeg21:a0080 |title=IndonesiÃ« heeft kansen op de eerste Aziade|work= [[De nieuwsgier]]|via=[[Delpher]]|date=5 March 1951|access-date=27 June 2022|language=nl}} < /ref > 
 
 At the age of 16 years old Triwulan won the 100 metres event at the 1950 PASI athletics competitions in a time of 13.3 seconds. < ref name=ref1 > {{cite web|url= https://resolver.kb.nl/resolve?urn=MMNIOD04:000093880:mpeg21:a0066 |title=Malangse (16 jaar) won 100 mtr. in 13.3 kansen op de eerste Aziade|work= [[Nieuwe courant]]|via=[[Delpher]]|date=6 November 1950|access-date=27 June 2022|language=nl}} < /ref > 
 
 As a preperation for the 1951 Asian Games she went with the national team on a training camp in [[Djokja]] in January 1951. < ref name=training > {{cite web|url= https://resolver.kb.nl/resolve?urn=ddd:010863237:mpeg21:a0098 |title=Athleten naar trainingskamp|work= [[Java-bode]]|via=resolver.kb.nl|date=5 January 1951|access-date=27 June 2022|language=nl}} < /ref > < ref name=training2 > {{cite web|url= https://resolver.kb.nl/resolve?urn=MMNIOD04:000093200:mpeg21:a0081 |title=Mevr. Soeriowati kwam, zag en ging weg|work= [[Nieuwe courant]]|via=resolver.kb.nl|date=22 January 1951|access-date=27 June 2022|language=nl}} < /ref >  Afterwards she was selected for the Games to compete in the 100 metres and 4x 100 metres relay. < ref name=selection > {{cite web|url= https://resolver.kb.nl/resolve?urn=MMNIOD04:000093214:mpeg21:a0051 |title=Indonesische ploeg voor Aziatische Spelen|work= [[Nieuwe courant]]|via=resolver.kb.nl|date=8 February 1951|access-date=27 June 2022|language=nl}} < /ref >  Ahead of the Games, she was named as a favorite to win a medal. < ref name=preview / >  Still 16 years old, she was the youngest athlete at the Games. < ref name=games > {{cite web|url= https://resolver.kb.nl/resolve?urn=ddd:010863290:mpeg21:a0122 |title=Japanners boekten veel successen|work= [[Java-bode]]|via=resolver.kb.nl|date=9 March 1951|access-date=27 June 2022|language=nl}} < /ref > 
 
 At the [[1951 Asian Games]] she was eliminated in the heats of the 100 metres, finishing last in her heat. < ref name=games / >  She won with the relay team the bronze medal in the 4x 100 
 metres event behind Japan and India. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://resolver.kb.nl/resolve?urn=ddd:011208451:mpeg21:a0100 |title=Olympische deelnemers thuis. Niet ontevreden over resultaten|work= [[De Vrije Pers]]|via=[[Delpher]]|date=16 March 1951|access-date=27 June 2022|language=nl}} < /ref > 
 
 Triwulan won after the Games in Indonesia several tournaments and competitons in the 100 metres, including at the 1951 National Sports Week. < ref name=PON > {{cite web|url= https://resolver.kb.nl/resolve?urn=ddd:010864123:mpeg21:a0081 |title=Nationale Spoetweek II|work= [[Java-bode]]|via=[[Delpher]]|date=27 October 1951|access-date=27 June 2022|language=nl}} < /ref >   
 
 She continued competing in the later years, with her last main competition being the 1953 National Sports Week. < ref name=PON1953 > {{cite web|url= https://resolver.kb.nl/resolve?urn=ddd:011210536:mpeg21:a0081 |title=PON-successen in ploegverband|work= [[De vrije pers]]|via=[[Delpher]]|date=5 October 1953|access-date=27 June 2022|language=nl}} < /ref >
