 '''Tilapia''' are [[fish]] that are often farmed for food, or kept as pets in [[aquarium]]s. They live in warm, [[fresh water]], but some [[species]] live in [[brackish]] water. The fish are found in [[Africa]], [[North America|North]] and [[South America]], [[India]], and [[Sri Lanka]]. < ref name=dpi/ >
