'''Fallowfield''' is an area of the city of [[Manchester]], [[England]]. It lies two miles south of Manchester City Centre and is bisected east/west by the Wilmslow Road and north/south by the former ''Fallowfield Loop railway line'', now a cycle path. According to the 2001 UK census it has a population of 14,132. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.ons.gov.uk/help/localstatistics|title=Local statistics - Office for National Statistics|website=www.ons.gov.uk}} < /ref >
