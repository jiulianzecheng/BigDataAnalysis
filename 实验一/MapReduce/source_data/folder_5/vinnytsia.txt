 '''Vinnytsia''' ({{lang-uk|Вінниця}}) is a city on the banks of the [[Southern Bug]], the administrative center of the [[Vinnytsia Oblast]] in [[Ukraine]]. Vinnytsia is an important historical center of eastern [[Podillya]], a historical part of Ukraine. As of January 1, 2021, the population was 370,600 people. The city's history begins in the [[Middle Ages]]. It was founded in 1363 and for a long time remained under [[Kingdom of Poland (1385–1569)|Polish]] and [[Russian Empire|Russian]] control. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Vinnytsya {{!}} Ukraine|url=https://www.britannica.com/place/Vinnytsya-Ukraine|access-date=2021-02-15|website=Encyclopedia Britannica|language=en}} < /ref >
