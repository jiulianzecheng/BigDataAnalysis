 '''Cognac''' is an [[alcohol]]ic drink. It is made from [[grape]]s. It is a kind of [[brandy]]. This means there is 40 percent of alcohol or more in it. It is made around the city of [[Cognac, Charente|Cognac]]. Only a few very special kinds of grapes may be used to produce the brandy.  
 
 The most important cognac importers are the [[United States]], followed by [[Great Britain]] and [[Japan]].
