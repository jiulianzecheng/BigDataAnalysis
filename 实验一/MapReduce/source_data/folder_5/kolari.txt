 '''Kolari''' is a [[municipality]] in [[Finnish Lapland]]. It had almost 3,900 living there in January 2014. The municipalities next to it are [[Kittilä]], [[Muonio]], [[Pello]] and [[Rovaniemi]]. Ilpo Väisänen planned the railway station building.
