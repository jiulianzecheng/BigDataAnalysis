 '''Portlethen''' is a [[town]] 7 & nbsp;mi south of [[Aberdeen]], [[Scotland]] along the [[A90 road|A90]]. It has expanded rapidly over the last 30 years. Portlethen has a population of about 7000, and more than 800 houses are, as of 2007, were being built on nearby farmland. To the east of Portlethen lie three fishing villages: [[Findon, Aberdeenshire|Findon]], [[Downies]] and [[Old Portlethen]] (now often referred to as ''Portlethen Village''). Although Portlethen is a town in terms of population (and it has been granted official town status), it resembles a residential [[suburb]] without a clear 'town centre' or focal point.
