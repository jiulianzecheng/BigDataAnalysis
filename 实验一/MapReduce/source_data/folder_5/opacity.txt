 '''Opacity''' or '''opaque''' means something that is difficult to see through or perceive. It can also mean: 
 
 * Opacity (optics), the degree to which light is blocked. 
 * Opaque context, a linguistic context in which it is not possible to substitute co-referential terms while guaranteeing the preservation of truth values. 
 * Opaque data, in [[computer programming]], data that can only be manipulated through an external interface. 
 * Opaque is a famous restaurant that serves customers in complete darkness.
