 '''''Abadzekhia''''' is an extinct genus of [[snake mackerel]] that lived from the early to late [[Oligocene]] epoch in what is now the [[Caucasus Mountains]] of Southern Russia. 
 
 == Description == 
 It resembles today's [[escolar]]. The dorsal fin from the [[snake mackerel]] is now shorter and larger.  
 
 === Body shape === 
 The body is [[torpedo]]-shaped. 
 
 === Teeth === 
 It has small sharp teeth.
