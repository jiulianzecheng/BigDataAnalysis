 '''Czechs''' ({{lang-cs|Češi}}, {{IPA-cs|ˈtʃɛʃɪ}}, [[archaism|archaic]] {{lang-cs|Čechové}} {{IPA|[ˈtʃɛxɔvɛː]}}) are a [[West Slavs|western Slavic]] people of [[Central Europe]]. Most live in the [[Czech Republic]]. Small amounts of Czechs also live in [[Slovakia]], [[Austria]], [[United States|U.S.]], [[Brazil]], [[Argentina]], [[Canada]], [[Germany]], [[Russia]] and other countries. They speak the [[Czech language]], which is closely related to the [[Slovak language|Slovak]] and [[Upper Sorbian language]]. < ref > [http://www.omniglot.com/writing/czech.htm Czech language, alphabet and pronunciation < !-- Bot generated title -- > ] < /ref >
