 '''Altamura''' is an [[Italy|Italian]] city in the [[Apulia]] Region. The city is {{convert|45|km|0|abbr=off}} southwest of [[Bari]]. It is close to the border with [[Basilicata]]. {{As of|2017}}, Altamura has about 70,500 inhabitants. 
 
 The city is known for its traditional [[bread]] called [[Pane di Altamura]].
