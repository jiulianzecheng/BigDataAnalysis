 '''Graduation''' is the [[confirmation]] of the finishing of an [[university]], [[military academy]], [[academy]] or [[high school]]. Graduation is celebrated in a [[ceremony]] done at the day of that confirmation; the name of that ceremony is usually the degree day, commencement, invocation, or convocation. Graduation also means the moving of a [[student]] from one grade to another higher grade.
