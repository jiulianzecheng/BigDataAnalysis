 '''Debenhams''' was the [[United Kingdom]]'s largest [[department store]] chain. < ref name=Varley21 > Rosemary Varley, ''Retail Product Management: Buying and Merchandising'' (Oxford; New York: Routledge, 2005), p. 21 < /ref >  It was formed in 1778. It is a company that has opened shops across the world, first opening in [[London]], [[England]]. Debenhams operated nearly 100 [[retail]] stores in the [[UK]] in 2005. < ref name= " Varley21 "  / >  It is one of the [[Company|companies]] on the [[FTSE 250 Index]]. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://www.lse.co.uk/index-constituents.asp?index=IDX:MCX & indexname=ftse_250 |title=FTSE 250 Constituents |author= < !--Staff writer(s); no by-line.-- >  |publisher=London South East |access-date=14 December 2014}} < /ref >  The 2014 Christmas [[advertising|advert]] was filmed in the [[Sutton, London]] shop in August 2014.
