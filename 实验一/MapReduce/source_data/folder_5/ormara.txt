'''Ormara''' ({{lang-ur|اورمارا}}) is a port city in the Makran or coastal region of the [[Balochistan (Pakistan)|Balochistan province]] of Pakistan. It is at 25° 16' 29N 64° 35' 10E. It is {{convert|240|km}} west of [[Karachi]] and {{convert|230|km}} east of Gwadar on the [[Arabian Sea]].
