'''Lahor''' is a town and [[tehsil]] of [[Swabi District]] in [[Khyber Pakhtunkhwa]], [[Pakistan]]. The town is a settlement of 35,000 people and is west of Swabi, on the northern bank of the [[Indus River]]. Lahor is surrounded by Yar Hussain, Tordher, Ambar and the Indus River.
