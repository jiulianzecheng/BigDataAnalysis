 '''Jonava''' is a city in [[Lithuania]]. Jonava has a [[population]] of about 26 thousand people, which makes it the ninth-largest city in Lithuania. It is in [[Kaunas County]]. The city has a [[fertilizer]] factory. 
 
 == History == 
 
 Jonava was established (built) in 1750. A bishop named [[Juozapas Kazimieras Kosakovskis]] ordered for a wooden church to be built here in 1776. < ref > {{Cite book|title=Lietuva. 100 vietų, kurias turite pamatyti|publisher=Terra Publica|isbn=978-9955-652-88-5|language=lt|location=Kaunas|pages=106 p}} < /ref >
