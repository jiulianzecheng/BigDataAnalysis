 An '''astronomer''' is a [[scientist]] who studies [[astronomy]]. Observational astronomers work in [[observatory|observatories]] with [[telescope]]s to collect information from things in [[outer space]] such as [[planet]]s, [[star]]s, or [[galaxy|galaxies]]. Theoretical astronomers study the collected information, and use it to help us understand how the [[universe]] behaves.
