 '''Pamiers''' ({{lang-oc|Pàmias}}) is a [[Communes of France|''commune'']] in the [[Ariège (department)|Ariège]] [[Departments of France|department]] in the [[Occitanie]] [[Regions of France|region]] in southwestern France. It is a [[Prefectures in France|subprefecture]] of the department. Although Pamiers is the largest city in Ariège, the capital is [[Foix]], a smaller city. 
 
 ==History== 
 In 1111, the city was officially founded by the [[Count]] Roger II of Foix and the [[:wikt:abbot|abbot]] of the [[:en:wikt:sanctuary|sanctuary]] of [[Antoninus of Pamiers|Saint Antonin of Pamiers]]; they built a [[castle]] (now demolished) and named it ''Castrum Appamiae'', after a [[Syria]]n city, [[Apamea, Syria|Apamea]], that was visited by Roger II during the [[Crusades|First Crusade]]. A church was built at the foot of the castle (Our Lady of Mercadal) where is now the [[cathedral]]. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 The city of Pamiers is along the [[Ariège (river)|Ariège]] river in the [[plain]] formed by this river in the northern part of the [[Ariège (department)|Ariège]] department, in southern France. 
 
 The ''commune'' has an area of {{Convert|45.9|km2|sqmi|abbr=on }}. < ref name= " insee-geo " > {{cite web |url=https://www.insee.fr/fr/statistiques/1405599?geo=COM-09225 |title=Commune de Pamiers (09225) |work=Comparateur de territoire |publisher=Institut national de la statistique et des études économiques - INSEE |format= |language=French |accessdate=23 March 2017 |date= }} < /ref >  Its average [[altitude]] is {{Convert|365|m|ft|abbr=on}}; at the [[City and town halls|city hall]], the altitude is {{Convert|295|m|ft|abbr=on }}. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://www.map-france.com/Pamiers-09100/ |title=Pamiers |publisher=Map-France.com |language= |accessdate=14 June 2014 |date= }} < /ref > 
 
 Pamiers is at {{Convert|64|km|mi|0|abbr=on }} to the south of [[Toulouse]], {{Convert|20|km|mi|0|abbr=on }} to the north of [[Foix]] and {{Convert|53|km|mi|0|abbr=on }} to the west of [[Saint-Girons, Ariège|Saint-Girons]].

 == Administration == 
 Pamiers is a [[Prefectures in France|subprefecture]] of the Ariège department, the capital of the ''arrondissement'' of [[Arrondissement of Pamiers|Pamiers]] and the [[administrative centre]] ({{lang-fr|chef-lieu}}) of two [[Cantons of France|cantons]]: 
 * [[Canton of Pamiers-1|Pamiers-1]], with 12,332 inhabitants  < small > (2014) < /small > . 
 * [[Canton of Pamiers-2|Pamiers-2]], with 14,702 inhabitants  < small > (2014) < /small > . 
 
 It is part of the [[Communes of France|intercommunality]] ''Les Portes d'Ariège - Pyrénées'' ({{lang-fr|Communauté de communes des Portes d'Ariège - Pyrénées}}).
