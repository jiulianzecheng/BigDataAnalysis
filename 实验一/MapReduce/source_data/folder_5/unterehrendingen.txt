 '''Unterehrendingen''' is a village and former [[municipality]] in the [[district]] of [[Baden (district)|Baden]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Aargau]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 On 1 January 2006 the former municipalities of Unterehrendingen and [[Oberehrendingen]] merged into the new municipality of [[Ehrendingen]]
