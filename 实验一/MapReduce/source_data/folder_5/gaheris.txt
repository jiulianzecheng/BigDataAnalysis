'''Gaheris''' is a legendary character, a member of the [[Knights of the Round Table]] in [[Arthurian legend]]. He was a nephew of [[King Arthur]]. He was the third son of Arthur's sister [[Morgause]] and her husband [[Sir Lot]]. He was described as being [[valiant]], [[agility|agile]], [[handsome]], and his left arm was shorter than his right arm.  He was killed by [[Lancelot]] in the ''[[Lancelot-Grail|Prose Lancelot]]'' He was [[Sir Gawain]]'s brother.
