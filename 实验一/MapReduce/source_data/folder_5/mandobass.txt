 The '''Mandobass''' is an [[Musical instrument|instrument]] similar to the [[double bass]]. It is in related to the [[mandolin]], and like the mandolin (and the [[lute]]), it is usually [[Plucked string instrument|plucked]]. The most common version has four strings, which are usually tuned to  E < sub > 1 < /sub > –A < sub > 1 < /sub > –D < sub > 2 < /sub > –G2.  Another version exists, which is tuned to G < sub > 1 < /sub > –D < sub > 2 < /sub > –A < sub > 2 < /sub > –E < sub > 3; < /sub >  this is two [[Octave|octaves]] lower than the mandolin.  
 
 Other instruments in the family are the [[mandola]], the [[octave mandolin]] and the [[mandocello]]. 
 
 These instruments have been in use since the 17th century.
