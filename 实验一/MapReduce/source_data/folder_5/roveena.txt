 ==Career== 
 Roveena graduated from University of Waterloo where she studied piano and composition. < ref > {{cite web|title=Singing Sensation, Roveena Gnanabakthan|url=http://tamilyouth.ca/singing-sensation-roveena-gnanabakthan/|website=CTYA`s Blog|access-date=5 January 2018}} < /ref >  Roveena started her music career in 2010, when she began posting song covers on YouTube including the Etta James classic ''At Last'', and was invited to perform live on CITY TV Breakfast Television. < ref > {{cite news|title=Roveena: ‘Wherever I Go’ ~ ‘My Heart will go on’ ~ ‘Make you feel my love’|url=http://tamilweek.com/thiruvedkai/archives/657|access-date=5 January 2018|work=Thiruvedkai ~|date=25 August 2011|archive-date=26 August 2016|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160826050653/http://tamilweek.com/thiruvedkai/archives/657|url-status=dead}} < /ref > < ref > {{cite news|title=Roveena: Fearless|url=http://www.fyimusicnews.ca/articles/2017/10/27/roveena-fearless|access-date=15 January 2018|work=FYIMusicNews|date=27 October 2017|language=en|archive-date=20 November 2017|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20171120195851/http://fyimusicnews.ca/articles/2017/10/27/roveena-fearless|url-status=dead}} < /ref >  Roveena was also invited to sing the national anthem at various Toronto Blue Jays and NFL games. < ref > {{cite book|title=Canadian Tamils’ Chamber of Commerce 12th Annual Awards Gala at Fairmont Royal York Hotel to honour outstanding Tamil Business  &  Community Leaders|date=2010|publisher=Monsoon Journal|page=48|url=http://monsoonjournal.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/02/05_Archive_1-May-2010.pdf|access-date=5 January 2018}} < /ref >  In 2012, Roveena made it to the Top 10 on the singing competition show TVI’s Superstar. 
 
 In 2016, she collaborated with Lymphoma Cancer Canada and became a Brand Ambassador of the organization. < ref > {{cite news|title=Roveena is Ambassador for Lymphoma Canada {{!}} Lymphoma Canada|url=https://www.lymphoma.ca/news/roveena-brand-ambassador-lymphoma-canada|access-date=15 January 2018|work=Lymphoma.ca|language=en|archive-date=25 November 2017|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20171125052318/https://www.lymphoma.ca/news/roveena-brand-ambassador-lymphoma-canada|url-status=dead}} < /ref >
