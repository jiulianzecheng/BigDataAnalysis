 '''Moutier''' is a [[town]] of the [[administrative district]] of [[Jura bernois (administrative district)|Jura bernois]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Bern (canton)|Bern]] in [[Switzerland]]. There were two votes, whether they wanted to be with Bern or Jura. In the last one, a majority voted for Jura. From 2026, the town will become part of Jura.
