 '''Fehmarn''' ({{lang-da|Femern}}) is an island in the [[Baltic Sea]], off the eastern coast of [[Schleswig-Holstein]], [[Germany]]. It is the third largest island in Germany, after [[Rügen]] and [[Usedom]]. Fehmarn is about 18 & nbsp;km south of the [[Denmark|Danish]] island of [[Lolland]] by the [[Fehmarn Belt]]. The island belongs to [[Ostholstein]].
