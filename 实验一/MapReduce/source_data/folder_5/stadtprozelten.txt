 '''Stadtprozelten''' is a town and municipality in the [[Miltenberg (district)|Miltenberg district]] in the [[Lower Franconia]] [[Regierungsbezirk|region]] in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]] and the seat of the [[Municipal association (Germany)|municipal association]] of Stadtprozelten. 
 
 == Geography == 
 
 === Place === 
 Stadtprozelten is on the southern edge of the [[Spessart]] (range) and on the right edge of the [[Main (river)|Main]] in the middle of the south side of the ''Mainviereck'' (“Main Square”), 8 & nbsp;km northwest of [[Wertheim am Main]]. Stadtprozelten is directly opposite [[:de:Mondfeld]], which is on the left edge of the Main. They are linked by a ferry. 
 
 === Subdivisions === 
 The [[Ortsteil|district]] of Neuenbuch is 2 & nbsp;km north of the town centre. 
 
 === Neighbouring communities=== 
 Stadtprozelten is bordered by (from the north, clockwise): [[Altenbuch]], [[Faulbach]], Wertheim (Baden-Württemberg), [[Dorfprozelten]] and [[Eschau]].
