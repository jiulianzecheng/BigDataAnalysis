'''Vagra''' (or '''Vaghara''', {{lang-gu|વાગરા}}) is a town in [[Gujarat]], [[India]]. It is a [[taluka]] of the district of [[Bharuch district|Bharuch]]. It is located north-west of the town of Bharuch. It contains around 70 villages. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://bharuchdp.gujarat.gov.in/bharuch/english/vagra-taluka.htm|title=Villages of Vagra Taluka|publisher=Gujarat Government, Bharuch District Panchayat|accessdate=19 February 2014|archive-date=9 June 2014|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140609061845/http://bharuchdp.gujarat.gov.in/bharuch/english/vagra-taluka.htm|url-status=dead}} < /ref >  These are spread between the coast of the [[Gulf of Khambhat]] and the northern side of the [[Narmada River]].
