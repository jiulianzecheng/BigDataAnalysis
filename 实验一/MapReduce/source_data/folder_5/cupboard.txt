 A '''cupboard''', also known as a '''cabinet''', is [[furniture]] used for [[storage]].  Usually, a cupboard will be in the [[kitchen]] or [[bathroom]] of a [[house]].  It usually has one or more shelves to put items on. It can also have a [[door]] to close off the open shelves.
