 '''Eiffage S.A.''' is a [[France|French]] [[civil engineering]] [[construction]] company.  In 2010, it was the third largest company of its type in France, It was also the fifth largest in [[Europe]]. 
 
 == History == 
 The company was formed in 1992, after several companies merged. These companies were Fougerolle (founded 1844), Quillery (founded 1863), Beugnet (founded 1871), and La Société Auxiliaire d'Entreprises Électriques et de Travaux Public, better known as SAE (founded in 1924). < ref > {{Cite web|title=Société auxiliaire d'entreprises électriques et de travaux publics|url=http://data.bnf.fr/12233649/societe_auxiliaire_d_entreprises_electriques_et_de_travaux_publics/|access-date=16 November 2018|publisher=Data.bnf}} < /ref >
