 '''Aberaeron''' is a [[town]], a [[Community (Wales)|community]] and an [[electoral ward]] situated between [[Aberystwyth]] and [[Cardigan, Ceredigion|Cardigan]] in [[Ceredigion]], [[Wales]]. The [[Ceredigion|Ceredigion County Council]] offices are based in Aberaeron. The name of the town is [[Welsh language|Welsh]].
