 '''Wiesloch''' is a town in [[Baden-Württemberg]], [[Germany]]. It is 13 & nbsp;km south of [[Heidelberg]]. It is the fourth largest town in the [[Rhein-Neckar-Kreis]], after [[Weinheim]], [[Sinsheim]] and [[Leimen (Baden)|Leimen]].
