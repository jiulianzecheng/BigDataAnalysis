{{nihongo|'''Shinsei'''|しんせい||}} is the name of the third [[Japan]]ese artificial [[satellite]] which was put into [[orbit]]. < ref name= " osumi " > Nussbaum, Louis-Frédéric. (2005). [https://books.google.com/books?id=p2QnPijAEmEC & pg=PA762 & dq=  " ''Ōsumi'' " ] in ''Japan Encyclopedia'', p. 762. < /ref >   
 
 The launch was a project of the [[Institute of Space and Astronautical Science]] of the [[University of Tokyo]]. 
 
 The satellite was sent into orbit on September 28, 1971. < ref > JAXA, [http://www.isas.ac.jp/e/enterp/missions/catalogue.shtml   " Catalogue of ISAS Missions " ] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20141231025049/http://www.isas.ac.jp/e/enterp/missions/catalogue.shtml |date=2014-12-31 }}; retrieved 2012-4-2. < /ref >
