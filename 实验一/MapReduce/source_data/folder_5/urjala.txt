 '''Urjala''' is a municipality in [[Pirkanmaa]], [[Finland]]. As of 30 September 2023, 4,581 people lived there. Neighbouring municipalities are [[Akaa]], [[Forssa]], [[Humppila]], [[Hämeenlinna]], [[Punkalaidun]], [[Sastamala]], [[Tammela, Finland|Tammela]] and [[Vesilahti]].
