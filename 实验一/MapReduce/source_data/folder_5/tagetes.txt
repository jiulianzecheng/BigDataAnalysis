 '''''Tagetes''''' is a [[genus]] of flowers commonly called '''marigold'''.  Tagetes has 56 species   < ref name= " Soule1996 " > Soule, J. A.  1996.  Infrageneric Systematics of Tagetes.  Pgs. 435-443 in Compositae: Systematics, Proceedings of the International Compositae Conference, Kew 1994, Vol. I, Eds. D.J.N. Hind  &  H.J. Beentje. < /ref >   of plants in the sunflower family ([[Asteraceae]] or [[Compositae]]). The genus is native to [[North America|North]] and [[South America]]. 
 
 ==Description== 
 ''Tagetes'' species vary in size from 0.1 to 2.2 m tall. Most species have [[pinnate]] green [[Leaf|leaves]].  Blooms  naturally occur in golden, orange, yellow, and white colors.  In [[horticulture]], they are usually planted as [[Annual plant|annuals]].
