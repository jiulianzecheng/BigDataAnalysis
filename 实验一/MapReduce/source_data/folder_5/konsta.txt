 '''Konsta''' (''real name'' '''Abdullayev Sharifjon Shokir oʻgʻli''', [[1992]] [[September 9]], [[:En:Sirdaryo Region|Sirdarya]], [[Uzbekistan]]) Uzbekistani [[rapper]]. He is known for his music dedicated to some problems in society. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Konsta – Биография, личная жизнь, новости, фото - MuzFm.UZ|url=https://muzfm.tv/12298-konsta-biografija.html|access-date=2023-07-27|website=MuzFm.UZ® — Узбекский музыкальный медиа портал!|language=ru}} < /ref > 
 == Biography == 
 He was born in [[1992]], [[September 9|September 9]] in [[:En:Guliston|Guliston]]. After graduating a college, he worked at the market in [[:En:Guliston|Guliston]] for short time. For a while, the family prepares and sells food, but things didn't work on their way. < ref > {{Cite web|last=uz|first=Kun|title=Ijodlari kserokopiya emas: SUBeKTIV'da reper, stendaper va muallim bilan suhbat|url=https://kun.uz/uz/27206399|access-date=2023-07-27|website=Kun.uz|language=uz}} < /ref >  Sharif Abdullayev went to [[Russia]] as a labor migrant in [[2012]]. < ref > {{Cite web|last=uz|first=Daryo|title=O & lsquo;zbek rep evolyutsiyasi vakillari: Konsta, Timur Alixonov va boshqa reperlar ijodini qanday boshlagan?|url=https://daryo.uz/2023/01/10/ozbek-rep-evolyutsiyasi-vakillari-konsta-timur-alixonov-va-boshqa-reperlar-ijodini-qanday-boshlagan|access-date=2023-07-27|website=Daryo.uz|language=uz-latn}} < /ref > 
 == Career == 
 Konsta's been writing songs since [[2011]]. When he was working in Moscow, he recorded his songs by a guy from Khorezm who worked in a studio owned by rappers such as Sentr, Guf and Ptaha. In [[2012]]-[[2013]], he had become well-known in his hometown by the song  " Koʻchalar guvoh "  (Street Witness). Konsta became famous after the song [[:Uz:Gulim|Gulim]]. The track  " Boy " , which he sang together with blogger Timur Alikhanov, made a sharp turn in his work.  " There is no tomorrow " ,  " Uzbegim " ,  " Utopia " ,  " Ant " ,  " People can fly " ,  " Where we are not "  the songs which he sang focused on problems in society.
