 '''Skegness''' is a town and civil parish on the A52 road and A158 road in [[East Lindsey]], [[Lincolnshire]], England. In 2001 there were 18,910 people living in Skegness. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.ons.gov.uk/help/localstatistics|title=Local statistics - Office for National Statistics|website=www.ons.gov.uk}} < /ref > 
 
 It is known for being bracing, which means that it often has high winds. This is due to its exposed position on the [[North Sea]]. 
 
 Skegness is known as the home of [[Butlins]], a [[theme park]].
