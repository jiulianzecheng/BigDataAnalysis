'''Periodate''' is an [[ion]]. Its chemical formula is either IO < sub > 4 < /sub > < sup > - < /sup >  (metaperiodate) or IO < sub > 6 < /sub > < sup > 5- < /sup >  (orthoperiodate). They are the [[salt]]s of [[periodic acid]]. The iodine is in its +7 [[oxidation state]]. Periodates are similar to [[perchlorate]]s. They are strong [[oxidizing agent]]s. [[Sodium periodate]] is the most common periodate.
