 '''Pattensen''' is a town in the [[Hanover (district)|district of Hanover]], in [[Lower Saxony]], [[Germany]]. It is about {{convert|12|km|0|abbr=off}} south of [[Hanover]]. 
 
 Pattensen is twinned with [[Saint-Aubin-lès-Elbeuf]] in [[France]].
