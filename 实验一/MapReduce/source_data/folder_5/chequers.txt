 '''Chequers''', or '''Chequers Court''', is the [[English country house|country house]] of the [[Prime Minister of the United Kingdom]]. It is in [[Buckinghamshire]], [[England]] and is about {{convert|40|mi}} north-west of [[central London]]. Chequers has been the country home of the serving Prime Minister since 1921 after the [[estate]] was given to the British [[government]] by the [[aristocrat]] and [[politician]] [[Sir Arthur Lee]].
