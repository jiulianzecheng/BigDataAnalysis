 An '''academy''' ([[Greek language|Greek]] ἀκαδημία) is an institution of higher learning, research, or honorary membership. 
 The name traces back to [[Plato]]'s school of [[philosophy]], founded about [[385 BC]] at '''[[Academia|Akademia]]''', a sanctuary of [[Athena]], the goddess of wisdom, north of [[Ancient Athens|Athens]], [[Greece]].
