 '''''Klebsiella''''' is a dangerous [[bacteria|bacterium]] in the family [[Enterobacteria|Enterobacteriaceae]]. It is a main cause of [[pneumonia]] in children. A new [[drug]] resistant [[Strain (biology)|strain]] was discovered recently. The type species is ''[[Klebsiella pneumoniae]]''. It can also cause [[donovanosis]], a [[sexually transmitted disease]] (STD).
