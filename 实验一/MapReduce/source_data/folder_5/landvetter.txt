 '''Landvetter''' is a [[Urban areas in Sweden|locality]] in [[Härryda Municipality]] in [[Västra Götaland County]] in [[Sweden]]. In 2010, 7,152 people lived there. < ref name=scb / >  The [[Göteborg Landvetter Airport]] is located near the locality. The airport is even named after the locality.
