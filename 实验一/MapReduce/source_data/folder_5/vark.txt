 '''Vark''' is a super fine [[filigree]] [[Foil (metal)|foil]] sheet of pure metals. It is usually made of [[silver]] but sometimes [[gold]]. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Gold For Food {{!}} Gold To Eat - Delafee|url=https://www.delafee.com/edible-gold-creations_gold-for-food/|access-date=2020-10-21|website=www.delafee.com}} < /ref >  It is used to decorate [[South Asia|South Asian]] [[sweets]] and [[food]]. It is also placed on [[saffroned rice]] to make it look more appetising. < ref name= " :0 " > {{Cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=_v1tAAAAMAAJ & q=vark+edible+silver+foil+manufacturing|title=Tirtha, the Treasury of Indian Expressions|last1=Ghose|first1=Vijaya|last2=Ramanathan|first2=Jaya|last3=Khandekar|first3=Renuka N.|date=1992|publisher=CMC Limited|isbn=978-81-900267-0-3|language=en}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=NuZFAAAAYAAJ & q=varak+silver+foil+making|title=Ayurveda: A Life of Balance : the Complete Guide to Ayurvedic Nutrition and Body Types with Recipes|last=Tiwari|first=Maya|date=2005|publisher=Motilal Banarsidass Publishers|isbn=978-81-208-2076-0|language=en}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=j9tTHWAF_VEC & q=edible+varaq+silver+making|title=The Illustrated Weekly of India|date=1989|publisher=Published for the proprietors, Bennett, Coleman  &  Company, Limited, at the Times of India Press|language=en}} < /ref >  The silver and gold are edible and [[Flavor|flavorless]]. Varak is made by pounding silver into sheets a few [[Micrometre|micrometres]] (Î¼m) thick. < ref > {{Cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=mY3cO8b5iToC & dq=vark+edible+silver+foil+manufacturing & pg=PA39|title=New Indian Home Cooking: More Than 100 Delicious Nutritional, and Easy Low-fat Recipes!|last=Gadia|first=Madhu|date=2000|publisher=Penguin|isbn=978-1-55788-343-8|language=en}} < /ref >  It breaks easily into smaller pieces if touched.
