 '''Nick van de Wall''', better known by his stage name '''Afrojack''', is a [[Dutch]] music producer and [[DJ]]. He was born and raised in [[Spijkenisse]], [[the Netherlands]]. Afrojack's song  " [[Take Over Control]] " , which features [[Eva Simons]], has charted in a number of countries. he was listed at number 19 on DJ MAG's top 100 DJs of 2010. < ref > '''2010''' ⟨{{IPAc-en|t|uː|θ|au|s|ə|n|d|ə|n|d|t|ɛ|n}}⟩ was a common year starting on Friday, and ending on a Friday. < /ref >
