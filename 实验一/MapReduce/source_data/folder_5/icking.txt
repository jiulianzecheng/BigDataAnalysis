 '''Icking''' is a [[municipality]] in [[Bad Tölz-Wolfratshausen]], a district ([[Districts of Germany|''Landkreis'']]) in [[Upper Bavaria]]. 
 
 == Geography == 
 
 === Townships === 
 It consists of: 
 
 * Attenhausen 
 * Dorfen 
 * Holzen 
 * Icking 
 * Irschenhausen 
 * Meilenberg 
 * Obere Alpe 
 * Schützenried 
 * Schlederloh 
 * Spatzenloh 
 * Wadlhausen 
 * Walchstadt
