'''Parasomias''' are [[sleep disorder]]s that interrupt sleep. They can be broadly classified: 
 *Problems with arousal ([[NREM sleep]]): [[Sleepwalking]] and night terrors ([[Pavor nocturnus]]) 
 *Problems associated with [[REM sleep]]: [[Nightmare]]s, [[sleep paralysis]] 
 *Other problems, including for example [[hallucination]]s, or [[eating disorder]]s.
