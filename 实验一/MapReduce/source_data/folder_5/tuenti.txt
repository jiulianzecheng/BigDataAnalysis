 Before, Tuenti was also the most popular [[Social networking service|social network]] in Spain between 2009 and 2012 with up 15 million registered users. < ref > {{Cite web |url=http://corporate.tuenti.com/en/blog/a-huge-thank-you-to-our-10-million-active-users-and-15-million-registered-users |title=A huge thank you to our 10 million active users and 15 million registered users! |access-date=2017-04-17 |archive-date=2015-01-14 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150114164753/http://corporate.tuenti.com/en/blog/a-huge-thank-you-to-our-10-million-active-users-and-15-million-registered-users |url-status=dead }} < /ref > 
 
 Tuenti works in Spain as a full virtual mobile operator (MVNO) using Telefónica's mobile network. Tuenti was launched by Telefónica in [[Latin America|Latin-America]] in 2014 as a whole new brand.
