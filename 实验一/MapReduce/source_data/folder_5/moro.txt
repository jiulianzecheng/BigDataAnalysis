'''Moro''' is a city and a tehsil in the [[Naushahro Feroze District]] of [[Sindh]], [[Pakistan]]. It is in the centre of Sindh. It is at 26°40'0N 68°0'0E. It has an altitude of {{convert|28|m|ft|abbr=on}}. It is {{convert|12|km|abbr=on}} east of the [[Indus River|River Indus]].
