 '''''Piranhamesodon''''' is an extinct genus of [[Pycnodontiformes|pycnodont]] from the [[Jurassic|Late Jurassic]]. It had sharp, serrated teeth highly reminiscent of a [[piranha]], which is an unusual trait seen in other [[Pycnodontiformes]].
