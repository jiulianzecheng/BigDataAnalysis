 '''Conficker''' (also known as '''Downup''', '''Downadup''' and '''Kido''') is a [[computer worm]] that first showed up in October 2008. It targeted the [[Microsoft Windows]] [[operating system]]. < ref > {{cite news|url = http://news.bbc.co.uk/1/hi/technology/7832652.stm|title = Three million hit by Windows worm|date = 2009-01-16|access-date = 2009-01-16|work = [[BBC News Online]]|publisher = [[BBC]]}} < /ref > < ref name= " markoff-1 " > {{citation|title = Computer Experts Unite to Hunt Worm|url = https://www.nytimes.com/2009/03/19/technology/19worm.html?_r=1 & ref=us|work = New York Times|date = 2009-03-19|access-date = 2009-03-29|first = John|last = Markoff}} < /ref > < ref name= " sri-1 " > {{citation|url = http://mtc.sri.com/Conficker/|title = An Analysis of Conficker|first1 = Phillip|last1 = Porras|first2 = Hassen|last2 = Saidi|first3 = Vinod|last3 = Yegneswaran|publisher = SRI International|date = 2009-03-19|access-date = 2009-03-29|archive-date = 2009-04-01|archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20090401084403/http://mtc.sri.com/Conficker/|url-status = dead}} < /ref >  It is not known where the name  " conficker "  came from.    " Internet experts "  have guessed that the name comes from a mix between two different words:  " configure "  and  " ficken " , the German word for  " [[fuck]] " . < ref > {{citation|first = Richard|last = Grigonis|url = http://ipcommunications.tmcnet.com/topics/ip-communications/articles/50562-microsofts-5000000-reward-the-conficker-worm-creators.htm|title = Microsoft's $5,000,000 Reward for the Conficker Worm Creators|publisher = IP Communications|date = 2009-02-13|access-date = 2009-04-01}} < /ref > 
 
 Conficker was believed to be the worst computer worm since the [[SQL slammer worm]] in 2003. < ref > {{cite news|last=Markoff|first=John|author-link=John Markoff|date=2009-01-22|title=Worm Infects Millions of Computers Worldwide|newspaper=[[New York Times]]|url=https://www.nytimes.com/2009/01/23/technology/internet/23worm.html?em}} < /ref >   It was estimated 15 million computers were infected with Conficker. < ref name= " Downadup virus exposes millions of PCs to hijack " > {{cite news|url=http://edition.cnn.com/2009/TECH/ptech/01/16/virus.downadup/?iref=mpstoryview|title=Downadup virus exposes millions of PCs to hijack|first=Barry|last=Neild|date=16 January 2009 |publisher=CNN|access-date = 2009-01-18}} < /ref > < ref > {{cite news|url = http://www.upi.com/Top_News/2009/01/25/Virus_strikes_15_million_PCs/UPI-19421232924206/|title = Virus strikes 15 million PCs|publisher = [[UPI.com]]|date = 2009-01-26|access-date = 2009-03-25}} < /ref >  The virus was supposed to get worse on April 1, 2009, which caused many people to worry about it. However, there were no bad effects. < ref > {{cite web|author=Ian Paul|title=Conficker D-Day Arrives; Worm Phones Home (Quietly)|date=April 1, 2009|publisher=PCWorld|access-date=2009-04-01|url=http://www.pcworld.com/article/162381/conficker_dday_arrives_worm_phones_home_quietly.html|archive-date=2009-04-02|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20090402143840/http://www.pcworld.com/article/162381/conficker_dday_arrives_worm_phones_home_quietly.html|url-status=dead}} < /ref >
