 '''Shantae''' is a [[Indie game|indie]] [[video game]] series developed and published by [[WayForward Technologies]]. The series was created by Matt Bozon and Erin Bozon whose create the series main protagonist Shantae, a half-genie and the guardian of her town. < ref > {{Cite web|last=Parish|first=Jeremy|date=August 16, 2005|title=Wayforward's slinky genie was a Game Boy dream|url=http://www.1up.com/features/shantae|website=1up.com|access-date=April 6, 2023|archive-date=June 5, 2016|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160605121904/http://www.1up.com/features/shantae|url-status=bot: unknown}} < /ref >  The series had five games and many other appearances including ''[[Super Smash Bros. Ultimate]].'' In 2020, the series had sold three million copies. < ref > {{Cite web|last=Yoerider|date=2020-06-08|title=Shantae Series Crosses 3 Million In Total Sales Worldwide|url=https://nintendosoup.com/shantae-series-crosses-3-million-in-total-sales-worldwide/|access-date=2023-04-06|website=NintendoSoup|language=en-US}} < /ref > 
 
 == History == 
 The character Shantae was created by Erin Bell  the wife of Matt Bozon, the series's creator. Erin got a flash of inspiration while coming back from her [[camping]] days and created the character, naming her  " Shantae "  after one of the campers and developing her dance moves. < ref > {{Cite web|last=Isaac|first=Chris|date=2015-12-02|title=Shantae Co-Creator Matt Bozon Interview: Smash and More!|url=https://www.themarysue.com/interview-with-shantae-co-creator-matt-bozon/|access-date=2023-06-06|website=The Mary Sue|language=en}} < /ref > 
 
 == Gameplay == 
 ''Shantae'' is an [[Action adventure game|action-adventure game]] series whose the player control as Shantae. The series gameplay was similar to [[The Legend of Zelda]] and [[Metroid (series)|Metroid]] and the player can progress by collecting new powers, solving puzzles and getting information in town. < ref name= " :0 " > {{Cite web|date=September 24, 2015|title=［TGS 2015］待望の日本語版発売が決定した「シャンティ -海賊の呪い-」の開発者 マット・ボゾン氏にインタビュー|trans-title=[TGS 2015] Interview with Mr. Matt Bozon, the developer of the long-awaited Japanese version of  " Shanti -The Pirate's Curse- " |url=http://www.4gamer.net/games/318/G031887/20150924040/|access-date=June 4, 2023|website=4Gamer|language=ja}} < /ref > 
 
 The main character, Shantae main attack was whipping her long hair and transform herself by belly dancing. In the third installment, Shantae has lost her power as a genie. < ref name= " :0 "  / > 
 
 == Characters == 
 
 * '''Shantae''' is a half-genie and the daughter of a human father and genie mother. She was a guardian genie and having magical powers including her whipping hair and magical dances that change her into magical animals. < ref name=manual > {{cite book |author=Hanshaw Ink  &  Image |title= Shantae manual|publisher=[[Capcom]]|pages=27–30 |date=June 2, 2002}} < /ref > 
 *'''Mimic''' is a member of the Relic Hunters who has made many discoveries. He is also Shantae's adoptive uncle, as he raised her after her parents' disappearance. < ref name= " manual "  / > 
 *'''Risky Boots''' is a pirate and  the series' primary antagonist known for her love of wealth and powers. She normally seeks to take over Sequin Land or take revenge on Shantae. She also commands an army of Tinkerbats, a silent shadow humanoid creatures. < ref name= " manual "  / > 
 *'''Sky''' is Shantae's life-long friend and the daughter of a Relic Hunter herself. Never seen without her pet war bird Wrench. < ref name= " manual "  / > 
 *'''Bolo''' is Shantae's friend and sparring partner. He is described as being rather slow and very easily attracted to girls, though he tires to prove himself as capable of being a hero. < ref name= " manual "  / > 
 *'''Rottytops''' is undead zombie girl who often friendly but never had a friend. She was described as normally looking for trouble for fun. < ref name= " manual "  / >
