 '''Gomaespuma''' is a [[radio]] show, presented by Juan Luis Cano and Guillermo Fesser. The program is [[broadcast]] by  [[Onda Cero]]. It also has been in M80 radio and in [[Antenna 3]] Radio. Gomaespuma [[organise]]s the [[Flamenco pa tos]] [[festival]], the most important [[Flamenco]] festival of [[Spain]]. Gomaespuma directs a foundation with the same name.
