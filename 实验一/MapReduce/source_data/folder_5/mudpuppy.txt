 A '''mudpuppy''' or '''waterdog''' is an [[aquatic]] [[salamander]]. They belong to the [[family (biology)|family]] [[Proteidae]]. Mudpuppies make a [[dog]]-like [[bark]]ing [[sound]], which is why they are called mudpuppies. Mudpuppies belong to the [[genus]] ''[[Necturus]]''. They live in [[Canada]] and [[Midwestern United States]].
