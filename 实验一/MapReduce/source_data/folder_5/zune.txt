 '''Zune''' was an entertainment [[Platform (computing)|platform]] and [[media|portable media player]] made by [[Microsoft]]. 
 
 The Zune Marketplace was an [[Online shopping|online]] music, video and podcast store that integrates with Zune software, Zune devices, phones with Zune support, and [[Xbox 360]]. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.seattlepi.com/business/305042_software26.html|title=Software Notebook: Microsoft, others tap music veterans|publisher=Zune Insider|first=Todd|last=Bishop|date=2007-02-26}} < /ref > 
 
 In 2011, the Zune series of media players was discontinued. < ref > {{Cite web|last=Williams|first=Nate|date=2022-08-19|title=The Real Reason Zune Failed Spectacularly|url=https://history-computer.com/the-real-reason-zune-failed-spectacularly/|access-date=2023-07-19|website=History-Computer|language=en-US}} < /ref >
