 '''Barjora''' is a locality in the Barjora community development block, located in the Bankura Sadar subdivision of the Bankura district in the state of [[West Bengal]], [[India]]. It is part of the Bishnupur Lok Sabha constituency and Barjora Vidhan Sabha constituency. The area covers approximately 7.25 square kilometers and has an elevation of 5 meters. As of the 2011 census, Barjora had a population of 14,012 people. The official languages spoken in Barjora are [[Bengali language|Bengali]] and [[English language|English]], and it follows Indian Standard Time (IST) with a UTC offset of '''+5:30'''. 
 
 Barjora is a large town with a large area. It stretches from the bridges of Tarasing in the north to the outskirts of Bankura in the south. Its western border is with [[Baguli]]. The eastern border is on the rivers of the 7 villages.
