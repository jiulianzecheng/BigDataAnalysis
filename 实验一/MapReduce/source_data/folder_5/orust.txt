 '''Orust''' is an [[island]] close to [[Gothenburg]], on the west coast of [[Sweden]]. With a land area of {{convert|346|km2|abbr=off}}, it is the third biggest island in Sweden. Orust also is a municipality, [[Orust Municipality]].
