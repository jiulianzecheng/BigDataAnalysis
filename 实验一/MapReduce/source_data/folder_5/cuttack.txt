 '''Cuttack''' is the former [[Capital city|capital]] of the state of [[Odisha]] in [[East India]]. It is the [[headquarters]] of the [[Cuttack district]]. < ref > Shiv Sharma, ''India - A Travel Guide'' (New Delhi: Fusion Books, 2008), p. 331 < /ref >  Cuttack is [[nickname]]d the  " Millennium City "  and the  " Silver City " . < ref name=explmir > {{cite web |url=http://www.anitaexplorer.com/2015/04/silver-city-cuttack.html |title=Silver City Cuttack |date=April 16, 2015 |publisher=The Explorer of Miracles |access-date=December 31, 2016}} < /ref >  Cuttack has over 1000 years of history, and has a famous silver [[jewelry]] works. < ref name=explmir/ >  The High Court of Odisha is located in Cuttack. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://cuttack.nic.in/history/HISTORY-CUTTACK.htm |title=History of Cuttack |author=S.Chand; S. Tripathy |publisher=Department of History, Revenshaw University |access-date=December 31, 2016 |archive-date=August 22, 2017 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170822112213/http://cuttack.nic.in/history/HISTORY-CUTTACK.htm |url-status=dead }} < /ref >  Cuttack is also called as the commercial capital of Odisha. This is due to the large number of trading and business houses in the city. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.nasscom.in/looking-beyond-tier-landscape?fg=127463|title=Looking Beyond Tier Landscape|work=Tier II|publisher=NASSCOM|access-date=2014-08-24|archive-date=2016-03-07|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160307012452/http://www.nasscom.in/looking-beyond-tier-landscape?fg=127463|url-status=dead}} < /ref >
