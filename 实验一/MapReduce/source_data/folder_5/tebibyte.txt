A '''tebibyte''' (TiB) is a unit of measurement in computers and similar electronic devices. Tebibytes hold 1024 [[gibibyte]]s (GiB) or 1,099,511,627,776 [[byte]]s. Some people incorrectly call this number of bytes a [[terabyte]], which is actually one trillion bytes, or 1000 [[gigabyte]]s (GB). 
 
 1024 tebibytes make one [[pebibyte]].
