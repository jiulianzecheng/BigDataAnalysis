 '''Hesselberg''' ({{IPA-de|ˈhɛsl̩ˌbɛʁk|pron|De-Hesselberg.ogg}}; 689 & nbsp;m above sea level) is the highest point in [[Middle Franconia]] and the [[Franconian Jura]] and is situated 60 & nbsp;km south west of [[Nuremberg]], Germany.
