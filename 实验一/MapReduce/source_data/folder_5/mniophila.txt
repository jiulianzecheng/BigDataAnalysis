 '''''Mniophila''''' is a [[genus]] of [[leaf beetle]]s. It can be found nearly everywhere in [[Europe]]. < ref > {{cite web|url= https://data.nbn.org.uk/Taxa/NHMSYS0020152656|title=''Mniophila muscorum'' (Koch, 1803)|publisher=NBN Gateway}} < /ref > 
 
 It contains two species: < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.biolib.cz/en/taxonsubtaxa/id12168/|title=''Mniophila'' Stephens, 1831|work=BioLib|accessdate=16 February 2014}} < /ref > 
 * ''Mniophila bosnica''  < small > (Apfelbeck, 1914) < /small > 
 * ''Mniophila muscorum''  < small > (Koch, 1803) < /small >
