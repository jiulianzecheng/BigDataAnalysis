 '''Aub''' ({{IPA-de|aʊp|lang|Aub.ogg}}) is a town in [[Würzburg (district)|Würzburg]], in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. It is {{convert|29|km|mi}} southeast of [[Würzburg]], and {{convert|21|km|mi}} northwest of [[Rothenburg ob der Tauber]]. 
 
 It is on the river [[Gollach]], not far from the border of [[Baden-Württemberg]]. Aub is divided into three parts: Aub, Baldersheim, and Burgerroth.
