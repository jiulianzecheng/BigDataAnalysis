 '''Avry''' is a [[municipality]] of the district [[Saane (district)|Saane]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Fribourg (canton)|Fribourg]] in [[Switzerland]]. The municipality was created on 1 January 2001, when the municipalities of Avry-sur-Matran and Corjolens joined together to become Avry. < ref name=Fusion > [http://www.bfs.admin.ch/bfs/portal/de/index/infothek/nomenklaturen/blank/blank/gem_liste/03.html Amtliches Gemeindeverzeichnis der Schweiz] published by the Swiss Federal Statistical Office {{in lang|de}} accessed 19 July 2011 < /ref >
