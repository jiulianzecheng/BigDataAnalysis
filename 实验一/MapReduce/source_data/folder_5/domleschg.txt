 '''Domleschg''' is a new [[municipality]] of the [[district]] [[Hinterrhein (district)|Hinterrhein]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Graubünden]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 On 1 January 2015, the former municipalities of [[Almens]], [[Paspels]], [[Pratval]], [[Rodels]] and [[Tomils]] merged into the new municipality of Domleschg.
