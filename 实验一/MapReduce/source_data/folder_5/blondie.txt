 '''Blondie''' is an [[United States|American]] [[new wave]] [[pop music|pop]]-[[rock music|rock]] [[band]] who were most popular in the [[1970s]] and [[1980s]]. The lead [[singer]] of the band is [[Debbie Harry]]. Their best-known hit was ''Call Me!''. The group's music mixes pop, rock and [[disco]]. They were inducted into the [[Rock and Roll Hall of Fame]] 
 
 == Studio albums == 
 * ''[[Blondie (album)|Blondie]]'' (1976) 
 * Plastic Letters (1977) 
 * Parallel Lines (1978) 
 * Eat to the Beat (1979) 
 * Autoamerican (1980) 
 * The Hunter (1982) 
 * No Exit (1999) 
 * The Curse of Blondie (2003) 
 * Panic of Girls (2011) 
 * Ghosts of Download (2014) 
 * Pollinator (2017)
