'''Myocarditis''', also known as '''inflammatory cardiomyopathy''', is [[inflammation]] and [[infection]] of the [[cardiac muscle|heart muscle]]. Symptoms can include [[shortness of breath]], [[chest pain]], unable to exercise, and an [[irregular heartbeat]]. The duration of problems can vary from hours to months.
