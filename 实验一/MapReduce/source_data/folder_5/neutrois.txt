 '''Neutrois''' is a [[non-binary]] [[gender identity]] that is usually associated with [[gender]] neutrality or the nullification of gender. Neutrois is often translated as indifference towards or lack of [[gender]] in the individual, similar to or including those who are [[agender]]. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Definition of neutrois {{!}} Dictionary.com|url=https://www.dictionary.com/browse/neutrois|access-date=2020-01-09|website=www.dictionary.com|language=en}} < /ref >  The term is often used by those who experience [[gender dysphoria]] and/or [[gender euphoria]] who may seek [[medical]] and/or social [[transition|gender transitions]] to have a more neutral or [[Androgyny|androgynous]] appearance. Those who are neutrois may have complex gender identities, or be indifferent to their gender. Many people who are neutrois avoid [[gender expression]] which is strictly associated with [[femininity]] or [[masculinity]]. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Neutrois|url=http://genderkit.org.uk/identity/neutrois/|access-date=2020-11-11|website=Gender Construction Kit|language=en}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=R0UzDwAAQBAJ & dq=neutrois & pg=PT118|title=The ABC's of LGBT+|last=Mardell|first=Ashley|date=2016-11-08|publisher=Mango Media Inc.|isbn=9781633534087|language=en}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite web|title=Neither Man Nor Woman: Meet the Agender|url=https://www.thecut.com/2012/08/neither-man-nor-woman-meet-the-agender.html|access-date=2020-11-11|website=The Cut|language=en-us}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite web|title=What is Neutrois? - Agender.  ==Etymology== 
 Neutrois combines {{lang|fr|neutre}} ({{lang-fr|neutral}} or [[neuter]]), and {{lang|fr|trois}} ({{lang-fr|three}}), meaning neutral third, as in [[third gender]]. < ref name= " :0 "  / >
