
 '''Diabetic ketoacidosis  (DKA)''' is caused by untreated high blood [[glucose]] ([[Hyperglycemia|hyperrglycemia]]). If does not get treated, it can develop into a life threatening sickness. The body breaks down fat at a high rate. < ref name= " :0 " > {{Cite web|title=Diabetic ketoacidosis: MedlinePlus Medical Encyclopedia|url=https://medlineplus.gov/ency/article/000320.htm|access-date=2023-07-19|website=medlineplus.gov|language=en}}{{PD-notice}} < /ref > < ref name= " :1 "  / >   It is caused by a lack of [[insulin]] and a high number of [[Ketone|ketones]] which causes blood to become acidic. This sickness is an emergency that can lead to coma or death. 
 
 Symptoms of ketoacidosis include: 
 
 * Vomiting 
 * Confusion 
 * [[Dehydration]] 
 * Breathing problems 
 * Painful belly  
 * Breath that smells like fruit 
 * Fast heart rate 
 * Unconsciousness < ref > {{Cite web|title=Hyperglycemia|url=https://my.clevelandclinic.org/health/diseases/9815-hyperglycemia-high-blood-sugar|access-date=2023-07-18|website=Cleveland Clinic|language=en}} < /ref > < ref name= " :1 " > {{Cite web|title=Diabetic ketoacidosis: Know the warning signs-Diabetic ketoacidosis - Symptoms  &  causes|url=https://www.mayoclinic.org/diseases-conditions/diabetic-ketoacidosis/symptoms-causes/syc-20371551|access-date=2023-07-19|website=Mayo Clinic|language=en}} < /ref > 
 Those who have type 2 diabetes can also get sick with ketoacidosis. DKA can happen with high blood sugar, when medicine is stopped, because of a  bad sickness or [[infection]]. < ref name= " :0 "  / >
