 '''Cimadera''' was a [[municipality]] of the district [[Lugano (district)|Lugano]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Ticino]] in  [[Switzerland]]. On 14 April 2013, the former municipalities of [[Bogno]], [[Cadro]], [[Carona, Switzerland|Carona]], [[Certara]], Cimadera, [[Valcolla]] and [[Sonvico]] merged into the city of [[Lugano]]. < ref name=Fusion > {{cite web|url=http://www.bfs.admin.ch/bfs/portal/de/index/infothek/nomenklaturen/blank/blank/gem_liste/03.html|title=Amtliches Gemeindeverzeichnis der Schweiz|publisher=Swiss Federal Statistical Office |accessdate=14 January 2010|language=de}} < /ref >
