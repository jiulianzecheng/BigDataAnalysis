[[Image:Традиционално сервиран ајвар.jpg|thumb|250px|Ajvar with bread, garlic, pepper and salami]] 
 '''Ajvar''' ([[Cyrillic script]]: Ajвар) is a type of [[condiment]] made from [[Capsicum|bell pepper]]s and [[eggplant]]s. It is popular in the [[Balkans]]. Ajvar tastes hot and spicy. It is also a [[bread]] spread.
