 '''Khandesh''' (Marathi: खान्देश) is a region of central [[India]]. It is the northwest part of [[Maharashtra]] state. 
 
 There are 5 districts in Khandesh: 
 * [[Nandurbar]] 
 * [[Dhule]] 
 * [[Jalgaon]] 
 * [[Nashik]] 
 * [[Aurangabad Division|Aurangabad]]
