 A '''drawer''' is a piece of [[furniture]] for [[storage]]. They are usually very short and wide and are part of a [[chest of drawers]], where lots of drawers are stacked. Most have [[Handle (grip)|handles]] or [[knob]]s so they can be pulled out.
