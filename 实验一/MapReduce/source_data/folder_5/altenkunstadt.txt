 '''Altenkunstadt''' is a [[Municipalities of Germany|municipality]] in [[Lichtenfels (district)|Lichtenfels]] in [[Bavaria]], Germany. It is on the left or south edge of the river [[Main (river)|Main]] across the valley from [[Burgkunstadt]] which is on the hillside to the north. 
 
 ==Sons and daughters== 
 * Wolfgang Mack (1808-1883), German surgeon  
 * Alfred Nikolaus Witt (1914-1999), orthopedist and surgeon, university professor in Berlin and Munich 
 * Josef Seiz (1934-2010), German table tennis player
