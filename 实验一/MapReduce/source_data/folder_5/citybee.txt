'''CityBee''' is a company in [[Vilnius]] which [[Renting|rents]] [[Scooter|electric scooters]], [[Bicycle-sharing system|bicycles]] and cars  in [[Lithuania]], [[Latvia]], and [[Estonia|Estonia.]]  It started in 2013. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Vilniuje startuoja dalinimosi automobiliu paslauga – „CityBee“|url=https://www.15min.lt/naujiena/aktualu/lietuva/vilniuje-startuoja-dalinimosi-automobiliu-paslauga-citybee-56-229602|access-date=2023-08-22|website=15min.lt|language=lt}} < /ref >    
 
 It is owned by Modus Mobility. < ref > {{Cite web|last=Butkus|first=Linas|title=„Modus Mobility“ įsikėlė į naują biurą|url=https://www.vz.lt/transportas-logistika/2023/06/20/modus-mobility-isikele-i-nauja-biura|access-date=2023-08-22|website=vz.lt|language=lt}} < /ref > 
 
 It has more than 2,000 cars and 3,000 electric scooters.  In 2019 more than 400,000 people used CityBee services. 
 
 In 2018 it started renting vans in Poland, but this was not a success. < ref > {{Cite web|last=Miknevičius|first=Lukas|title=„CityBee“ stabdo veiklą Lenkijoje: tai buvo bandomasis projektas|url=https://www.vz.lt/transportas-logistika/2020/10/06/citybee-stabdo-veikla-lenkijoje|access-date=2023-08-22|website=vz.lt|language=lt}} < /ref > 
 
 People can rent cars, e-kick scooters and bicycles using a [[smartphone]] [[app]].  The company uses the [[technology]] of [[Onfido]] to check who they are.
