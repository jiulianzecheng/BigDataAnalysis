 '''Thymelaeaceae''' is a family of [[flowering plant]]s omposed of 50 genera (listed below) and 898 species.  
 
 The family is more diverse in the southern hemisphere than in the northern, with its major concentrations of species in [[Africa]] and [[Australia]]. 
 
 The species include mostly trees and [[shrub]]s, with a few [[vine]]s and [[herbaceous plant]]s.
