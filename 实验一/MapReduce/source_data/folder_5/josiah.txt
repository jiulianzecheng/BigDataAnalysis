 '''Josiah''' was a [[child]] [[king]] of [[Judah]]. He was born around [[640 BC]]. Josiah started his rule as king at the age of 8 when his father, [[Amon of Judah|Amon]], died. Josiah was thought of as a good king. He following in the steps of his [[ancestor]] [[King David]]. 
 
 Josiah ruled for 31 years in [[Jerusalem]] and died in [[609 BC]]. He was [[kill]]ed by [[Necho II|King Necho]] of [[Egypt]] while Josiah was helping the king of [[Assyria]] fight off the attacks of the Egyptian. Josiah's son [[Jehoahaz of Judah|Jehoahaz]] became the new King of Judah.
