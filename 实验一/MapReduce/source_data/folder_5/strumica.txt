 '''Strumica''' ({{lang-mk|Струмица}}) is a city in southeastern [[North Macedonia]]. About 35,000 people lived there. It is near the border with [[Bulgaria]] and [[Greece]]. The city is named after the [[Strumica (river)|Strumica River]] which runs through it.
