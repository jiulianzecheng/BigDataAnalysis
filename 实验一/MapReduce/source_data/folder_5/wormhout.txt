 '''Wormhout''' ({{IPA-fr|wɔʁmut}}; older version: ''Wormhoudt''; {{lang-vls|Wormout}}) is a [[Communes in France|commune]] in [[Nord (department)|Nord]] in north [[France]]. In 2018, 5,665 people lived there.
