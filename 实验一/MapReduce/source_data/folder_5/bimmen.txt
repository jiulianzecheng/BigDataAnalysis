 '''Bimmen''' is a [[hamlet (place)|hamlet]] in the German municipality of [[Kleve]], [[North Rhine-Westphalia]]. 
 
 Bimmen is low-lying. It borders on the [[Netherlands]] in the west and on the [[Rhine River]] in the north. 
 
 About 170 people are living there.
