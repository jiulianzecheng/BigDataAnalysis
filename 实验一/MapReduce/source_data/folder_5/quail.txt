 '''Quail''' [pronounced KwƒÅl] is a collective name for several [[genera]] of mid-sized [[bird]]s in the order [[Galliformes]]. The birds in this group are often referred to as  " [[fowl]] " . 
   
 Old World quail are in the family [[Phasianidae]], and New World quail are found in the family [[Odontophoridae]]. Many of the common larger species are farm-raised for [[cookery|table food]] or [[Quail eggs|egg consumption]], and are [[hunting|hunted]] on game farms or in the wild, where they may be released to supplement the wild population, or extend into areas outside their [[Range (biology)|natural range]]. Quail are also raised as pets.
