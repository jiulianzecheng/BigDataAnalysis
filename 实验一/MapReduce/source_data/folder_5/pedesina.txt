 '''Pedesina''' is a ''[[comune]]'' (municipality) in the [[Province of Sondrio]] in the [[Italy|Italian]] region [[Lombardy]]. It is the least populated municipality in Italy. 
 
 Pedesina is about {{convert|70|km|mi}} northeast of [[Milan]] and about {{convert|25|km|mi}} southwest of [[Sondrio]]. As of 31 December 2008, it had a population of 33, and an area of {{convert|6.3|km2|mi2}}. < ref name= " istat " > All demographics and other statistics: Italian statistical institute [[National Institute of Statistics (Italy)|Istat]]. < /ref > 
 
 Pedesina borders the following municipalities: [[Bema, Lombardy|Bema]], [[Gerola Alta]], [[Premana]], [[Rasura]], [[Rogolo]].
