 '''Marvelise''' ({{IPA-fr|maʁvəliz}}) is a [[Communes of France|commune]] in the [[Doubs]] [[Departments of France|département]] in [[Bourgogne-Franche-Comté]] in eastern [[France]]. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 Marvelise is {{convert|35|km|mi|abbr=on}} from the [[Switzerland|Swiss]] border in the [[Jura mountains]]. On the east are the Hauts des Roches (502 m) and on the west the Chanois (516 m).
