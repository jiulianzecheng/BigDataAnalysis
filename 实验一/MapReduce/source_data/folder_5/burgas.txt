 '''Burgas''' ({{lang-bg|Бургас}}) is an [[Bulgaria]]n [[List of cities in Bulgaria|city]] on the [[Black Sea]] coast. It is the fourth-most populous city in the country, after [[Sofia]], [[Plovdiv]], and [[Varna, Bulgaria|Varna]]. About 200,000 people lived there and is an important industrial, transport, cultural and tourist centre.
