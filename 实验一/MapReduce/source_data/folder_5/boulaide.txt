 '''Boulaide''' ({{lang-lb|Bauschelt}}, {{lang-de|Bauschleiden}}) is a [[Communes of Luxembourg|commune]] in [[Luxembourg]]. It is part of the [[Wiltz (canton)|canton of Wiltz]]. 
 
 As of 2019, 1,307 people live here.
