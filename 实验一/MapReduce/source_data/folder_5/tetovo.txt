 '''Tetovo''' ({{lang-mk|Тетово}}; {{lang-sq|Tetovë/Tetova}}) is a city in the northwestern part of [[North Macedonia]]. It is on the foothills of [[Šar Mountains]] and separated by the [[Pena (river)|Pena River]]. 52,915 people lived there.
