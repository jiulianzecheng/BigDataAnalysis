 '''Monpazier''' is a [[city]] in [[France]]. It is in the [[Dordoge departement|Dordogne]] departement, in [[Aquitaine]]. In 1999, 516 people lived there. It has been named one of the most beautiful villages in France by the association [[Les Plus Beaux Villages de France]]. It was built as a [[bastide]]. For some time, [[Eleanor of Aquitaine]] and [[Richard III of England|Richard III]] lived there.  
 
 The city was founded by [[Edward I of England]] in 1284. It is of rectangular design, built around a main square.
