 '''Lindberg''' is a [[municipality]] in [[Regen (district)|Regen]] in [[Bavaria]] in [[Germany]] in the neighbourhood of [[Zwiesel]]. 
 
 == Geography == 
 Lindberg is in the Danube Forest (''Donau-Wald'') region in the middle of the [[Bavarian Forest]] on the [[Glasstraße]] at the foot of the 1,315-metre-high [[Großer Falkenstein|Falkenstein]], four kilometres from [[Zwiesel]] and 14 km northeast of the county town of [[Regen (town)|Regen]], 30 km northwest of [[Grafenau (Lower Bavaria)|Grafenau]] and 14 km from the border at [[Bayerisch Eisenstein]].
