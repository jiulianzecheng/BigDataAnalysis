 '''Lynton''' is a small [[England|English]] [[town]] on the [[Exmoor]] coast in [[Devon]]. It stands on top of the cliffs above the picturesque [[harbour]] village of [[Lynmouth]]. They are connected by the [[Lynton and Lynmouth Cliff Railway]]. 
 
 Lynton was once the terminus for the [[narrow gauge]] [[Lynton and Barnstaple Railway]], which served both towns.
