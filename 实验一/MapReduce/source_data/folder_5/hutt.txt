'''Hutts''' are a [[slug]]-like species from [[Star Wars]]. Hutts are very large in size and are very heavy. They are most well known as [[crime]] lords and run the crime worlds of Star Wars. Hutts control their own area of [[The galaxy (Star Wars)|the galaxy]] known as  " Hutt Space "  which the Hutts have controlled for thousands of years. Hutts come from the swampy crime world of [[Nal Hutta]] but are all over the galaxy. [[Jabba the Hutt]] is the most well known member of the species. The species first appeared in ''[[Star Wars Episode VI: Return of the Jedi]]'' and have also appeared in ''[[Star Wars Episode IV: A New Hope]]'' (albeit through the special edition) and ''[[Star Wars Episode I: The Phantom Menace]]''. < ref name= " Wookieepedia " > {{cite web |title=Wookieepedia |url=https://starwars.fandom.com/wiki/Hutt |access-date=1 January 2021}} < /ref >
