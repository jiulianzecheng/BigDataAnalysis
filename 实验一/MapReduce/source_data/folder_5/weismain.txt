 '''Weismain''' is a town in [[Lichtenfels (district)|Lichtenfels]], in northern [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. It is 15 & nbsp;km west of [[Kulmbach]], and 15 & nbsp;km southeast of [[Lichtenfels, Bavaria|Lichtenfels]].
