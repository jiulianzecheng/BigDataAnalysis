 '''Rosario''' is a [[city]] in the center-east of [[Argentina]], about {{convert|300|km|abbr=on}} northwest from [[Buenos Aires]]. It is the main city of [[Santa Fe Province]]. As of 2008 Rosario metropolitan area has a population of around 1,300,000 inhabitants, making it Argentina's third-largest city (after [[Buenos Aires]] and [[Córdoba, Argentina|Córdoba]]). The city is on the [[Paraná River]]. 
 
 It is called Rosario because of the Virgin of the Rosary, whose image remains at the cathedral, in the same place as the original chapel.
