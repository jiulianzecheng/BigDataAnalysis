'''Reichswehr''' was the name of the [[Germany|German]] [[military]] from [[1919]] to [[1935]]. During these years, their number was severely restricted by the [[Treaty of Versailles]]. From [[1935]] to [[1946]] they were called [[Wehrmacht]], and since [[1946]] they have been called [[Bundeswehr]].
