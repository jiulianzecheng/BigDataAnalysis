A '''Granzyme''' is an [[enzyme]] contained within [[cytotoxin|cytotoxic]] [[T cell]]s and [[natural killer cell]]s.  These [[substance]]s are used to destroy [[virus]]-infected [[cell]]s. 
 
 Granzyme [[secretion]] can be detected and measured using the [[ELISPOT]] and [[ELISA]] techniques.
