'''Greed''' is a [[wikt:desire|desire]] to have more [[Product (consumer goods)|goods]], or more [[wealth]] than is needed. [[Christianity]] says that greed is one of the [[Seven deadly sins]]. There it is listed as ''avarice'', or in [[Latin]] ''avaritia''.
