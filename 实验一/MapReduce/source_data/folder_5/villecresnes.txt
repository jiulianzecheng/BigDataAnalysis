 '''Villecresnes''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is in the [[Val-de-Marne]] [[Departments of France|department]] in [[France]]. In 2019, 11,779 people lived there. 
 
 The commune is in the southeastern [[suburb]]s of [[Paris]], {{convert|20.1|km|mi|abbr=on}} from the center.
