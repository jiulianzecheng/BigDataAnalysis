 '''Glattbach''' is a [[community]] in the [[Aschaffenburg (district)|Aschaffenburg district]] in the ''[[Regierungsbezirk]]'' of [[Lower Franconia]] (''Unterfranken'') in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. It has around 3,300 inhabitants (2020). 
 
 == Geography == 
 
 === Location === 
 [[File:Gemeinde Glattbach.svg|thumb|Constituent communities]] 
 The community is in a valley north of [[Aschaffenburg]] on the western edge of the [[Spessart]] (range).
