 '''Hecuba''' (also '''Hekuba''' or '''Hekabe''') was a person in [[Greek mythology]]. She was the wife of King [[Priam]] of [[Troy]]. 
 
 Her children with Priam were: Sons: [[Hector]], [[Paris (mythology)|Paris]], Deiphobos, [[Helenos]], Pammon, Polites, Antiphus, Hipponous, [[Polydoros]]. And daughters: Ilione, Creusa, Laodice, [[Polyxena]], and [[Kassandra]]. 
 
 After the [[Trojan War]] she became a [[slave]] of [[Odysseus]].
