 '''Moers''' ({{lang-nds|Meurs}}, formerly ''Mörs'') is a city in the [[Germany|German]] [[States of Germany|state]] of [[North Rhine-Westphalia]]. Moers is near to [[Duisburg]] and belongs to the [[Ruhr area]]. 
 It has about 110,000 [[inhabitant]]s.
