 '''''Pisanosaurus''''' (meaning  " Pisano lizard " ) is a genus of primitive [[ornithischia]]n [[dinosaur]] from the [[Upper Triassic]] of what is now [[South America]]. It was a bipedal herbivore described by [[Argentina|Argentine]] paleontologist Rodolfo Casamiquela in 1967. Only one species the type, ''Pisanosaurus mertii'', is known, based on a single partial skeleton.  
 
 It is the oldest known ornithischian.
