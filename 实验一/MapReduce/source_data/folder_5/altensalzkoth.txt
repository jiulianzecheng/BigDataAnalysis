'''Altensalzkoth''' is a village in the [[Lower Saxony|Lower Saxon]] town of [[Bergen (Landkreis Celle)|Bergen]] in north Germany. It belongs to the parish of [[Eversen (Bergen)|Eversen]] in [[Landkreis Celle|Celle]] on the [[Lüneburg Heath]]. It is 13 & nbsp;km north of [[Celle]] on the ''[[Landesstraße]]'' L 240.
