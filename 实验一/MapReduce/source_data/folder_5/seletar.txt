'''Seletar''' is located in the North-Eastern region of [[Singapore]]. Seletar derives its name from ''Orang Seletar'', a Malay subgroup who were indigenous to the area. This area generally refers to a region by the Ponggol River to the east, the Seletar River to the west, [[Ang Mo Kio]] to the south, and the [[Straits of Johore]] to the north. < ref > {{Cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=-ApRngEACAAJ|title=Down the Seletar River: Discovering a Hidden Treasure of Singapore|last=Wijeysingha|first=Eugene|date=2013|publisher=Seletar Hills Estate Residents' Association|isbn=978-971-0579-20-4|language=en}} < /ref >
