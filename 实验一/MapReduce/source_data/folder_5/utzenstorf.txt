 '''Utzenstorf''' is a [[municipality]] in the [[administrative district]] of [[Emmental (administrative district)|Emmental]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Bern (canton)|Bern]] in [[Switzerland]]. It is regionally famous for its [[medieva]] castle, [[Landshut Castle]].
