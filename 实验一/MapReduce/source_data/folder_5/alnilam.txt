'''Alnilam''', also called '''Epsilon Orionis''', is a blue [[supergiant]] [[star]] and the central star of [[Orion's Belt]] in the [[Orion (constellation)|Orion constellation]]. It is 42 times the [[Sun]]'s diameter but over 800,000 times brighter. < ref > https://arxiv.org/abs/1511.09365 < /ref >  It is located over 400 [[parsec|parsecs]] or 1,300 [[light year|light year]]s away. < ref > https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/1997A & A...323L..49P < /ref >
