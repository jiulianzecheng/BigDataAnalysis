 '''Blankenbach''' is a [[community]] in the [[Aschaffenburg (district)|Aschaffenburg district]] in the ''[[Regierungsbezirk]]'' of [[Lower Franconia]] (''Unterfranken'') in [[Bavaria]], Germany, and a member of the  ''Verwaltungsgemeinschaft'' (Administrative Community) of Schöllkrippen. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 
 ===Location=== 
 [[File:Gemeinde Blankenbach.svg|thumb|Constituent communities]] 
 The community placed some 17 & nbsp;km from [[Aschaffenburg]] and [[Alzenau]]. Together with the communities of [[Kleinkahl]], [[Krombach, Bavaria|Krombach]], [[Schöllkrippen]], [[Sommerkahl]], [[Westerngrund]] and [[Wiesen, Bavaria|Wiesen]], Blankenbach forms the  ''Verwaltungsgemeinschaft'' (Administrative Community) of Schöllkrippen in the [[Kahlgrund]].
