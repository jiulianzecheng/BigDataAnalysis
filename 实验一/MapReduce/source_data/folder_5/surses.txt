 '''Sursés''' ([[Romansh language|Romansh]], {{lang-de|Oberhalbstein}}) is a valley region ({{lang-de|Talschaft}}) and new [[municipality]] of the [[district]] [[Albula (district)|Albula]] of the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Graubünden]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 On 1 January 2016, the former municipalities of [[Bivio]], [[Cunter]], [[Marmorera]], [[Mulegns]], [[Riom-Parsonz]], [[Salouf]], [[Savognin]], [[Sur, Switzerland|Sur]] and [[Tinizong-Rona]] merged to form the new municipality of Surses.
