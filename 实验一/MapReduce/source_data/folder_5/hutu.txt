The '''Hutu''' or ''Abahutu'' are a [[Central Africa]]n [[ethnic group]]. They live mainly in [[Rwanda]] and [[Burundi]]. 
 
 ==Population statistics== 
 The Hutu are the largest of the three [[ethnic group]]s in [[Burundi]] and [[Rwanda]].
