 '''Fellen''' is a community in [[Main-Spessart]] in the ''[[Regierungsbezirk]]'' of [[Lower Franconia]] (''Unterfranken'') in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]] and a member of the ''Verwaltungsgemeinschaft'' (Administrative Community) of Burgsinn. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 
 ===Location=== 
 Fellen is in the ''[[Mittelgebirge]]'' [[Spessart]], in the Würzburg Region. 
 
 ===Subdivisions=== 
 The community has the following ''Gemarkungen'' (traditional rural cadastral areas): Fellen, Rengersbrunn, Wohnrod and Neuhof.
