 '''Massader''' of Palestine is the arm of the Palestinian Investment Fund for Development of Natural Resources and Infrastructure Projects. The company plays an important role in developing large-scale strategic projects that aim to develop and use Palestinian natural resources (hydrocarbon resources and renewable resources) and provide various sources of national energy sources, in addition to developing the basic infrastructure for the electricity sector, all with the aim of establishing a firm foundation for an independent [[Palestine|Palestinian state]]. < ref > https://web.ppgc.ps/articles/view/26 < /ref > < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.devex.com/organizations/massader-company-69806|title=Massader Company - Devex|website=www.devex.com}} < /ref > 
 
 The company aims to develop the natural resources of Palestinian oil and gas, develop productive infrastructure for the electricity and water sectors, contribute to achieving economic independence in the energy and water sectors, reduce the level of dependence on imported supplies, contribute to enabling the development of heavy industries such as cement and steel. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.eib.org/en/press/all/2019-078-school-rooftops-will-generate-solar-energy-for-16000-houses-in-the-west-bank|title=Palestine: School rooftops will generate solar energy for 16000 houses in the West Bank|website=European Investment Bank}} < /ref > < ref > {{cite web|url=https://mercomindia.com/ifc-invest-rooftop-solar-projects-west-bank/|title=IFC to Invest Over ₹18 Million in Rooftop Solar Projects in West Bank|first=Nithin Thomas|last=Prasad|date=20 February 2020|publisher=}} < /ref > < ref > {{cite news|url=https://www.reuters.com/article/us-israel-palestinians-solar-energy-idUSKBN1KU164|title=Palestinians turn to the sun to reduce their power shortfall|newspaper=Reuters |date=10 August 2018|publisher=|via=www.reuters.com}} < /ref > < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.al-monitor.com/pulse/originals/2018/10/palestinian-waste-to-energy-landfill-gas-renewable-energy.html|title=Palestine seeks to achieve energy independence|date=29 October 2018|website=Al-Monitor}} < /ref > 
 
 ==The Middle East Solar Energy Industries Association Award== 
 Massader has won the award for the best [[solar energy]] project, from [[Middle East Solar Industry Association]], for the project of installing [[photovoltaic]] panels on the roofs of 500 schools and universities throughout [[Palestine]], for a total of 35 megawatts of solar energy. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.renewableenergymagazine.com/pv_solar/mesia-unveils-2019-solar-award-winners-20190117|title=PV - MESIA Unveils 2019 Solar Award Winners|last=Viaintermedia.com|website=Renewable Energy Magazine, at the heart of clean energy journalism}} < /ref > < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.wattan.net/ar/news/273774.html|title=مشروع الطاقة الشمسية على أسطح المدارس الحكومية في فلسطين يحصل على جائزة أفضل مشروع على مستوى منطقة الشرق الأوسط وشمال افريقيا|website=وكالة وطن للأنباء}} < /ref >
