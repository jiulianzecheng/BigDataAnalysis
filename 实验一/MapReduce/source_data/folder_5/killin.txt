 '''Killin''' ({{lang-gd|Cill Fhinn}}, 'the White [or Fair] Church') is a village with about 640 people. It is at the western head of [[Loch Tay]] in [[Stirling (council area)|Stirling]] (formerly [[Perthshire]]), [[Scotland]].
