 '''Cucurbitaceae''' is the [[plant]] family with [[melon]]s, [[gourd]]s or '''cucurbits'''. It includes crops such as [[cucumber]]s, [[Squash (plant)|squash]]es (including [[pumpkin]]s), [[luffa]]s, [[melon]]s and [[watermelon]]s.  
 
 The family lives mostly in the [[tropics]]. Its edible fruits were amongst the earliest cultivated plants.
