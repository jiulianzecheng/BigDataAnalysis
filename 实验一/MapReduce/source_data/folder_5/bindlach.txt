 '''Bindlach''' is a [[Municipalities of Germany|municipality]] in the district of [[Bayreuth (district)|Bayreuth]] in [[Upper Franconia]] in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. The town is adjacent immediately north of the town of [[Bayreuth]]. The population count of December 2006 was 7197 residents.
