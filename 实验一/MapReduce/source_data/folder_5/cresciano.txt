 '''Cresciano''' was a [[municipality]] of the district [[Riviera (district)|Riviera]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Ticino]] in [[Switzerland]]. On 2 April 2017, the former municipalities of Cresciano, [[Iragna]], [[Lodrino, Switzerland|Lodrino]] and [[Osogna]] merged to form the new municipality of [[Riviera, Ticino|Riviera]].
