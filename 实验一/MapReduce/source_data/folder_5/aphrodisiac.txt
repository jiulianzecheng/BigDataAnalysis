An '''aphrodisiac''' is a type of [[food]] or [[drink]] that gives one a higher [[libido]]. The name comes from the [[Ancient Greece|Greek]] goddess [[Aphrodite]]. A food or drink that makes a person less aroused is called an [[anaphrodisiac]]. 
 
 Some things thought by some to be aphrodisiacs are [[ginko]], [[ashwaganda]], [[oyster]]s and [[chocolate]].
