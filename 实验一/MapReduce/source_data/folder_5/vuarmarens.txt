 '''Vuarmarens''' was a [[municipality]] in [[Glâne (district)|Glâne]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Fribourg (canton)|Fribourg]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 == History == 
 On 1 January 2006, [[Esmonts]] became part of Vuarmarens.
