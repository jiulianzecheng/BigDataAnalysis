 '''Regensburg''' ( < small > German pronunciation: < /small >  [[Help:IPA/Standard German|[ʁɛ:gənsbʊɐ̯k]]]; [[Czech language|Czech]]: ''Řezno'',  < small > [[Austro-Bavarian]]: < /small >  [[Help:IPA|[ʁɛŋsbʊɐ̯k]]]; ''Rengschburg'' or ''Rengschbuach'' [[Help:IPA|[ʁɛŋsbʊax]]]) is a city in the south of [[Germany]], in the east of the state of [[Bavaria]]. 
 It has about 138,000 [[inhabitant]]s and a [[university]].
