 '''Skullhead''' was a [[Rock Against Communism]] [[band]] from [[Newcastle upon Tyne|Newcastle]], [[England]]. Their type of [[music]] is a blend of [[rock and roll|rock]], [[Oi!]], and [[heavy metal music|heavy metal]] music. The band and lead singer, Kev Turner (known to many by the tattoo's he sports, including  " Doc martin "  boots on the side of his face, which appear like sideburns) were also heavily influenced by the Odinist religion, to which many of their songs refer too.
