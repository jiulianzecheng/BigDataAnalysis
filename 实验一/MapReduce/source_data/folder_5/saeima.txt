 The '''Saeima''' is the [[parliament]] of the [[Latvia|Republic of Latvia]]. It is a [[unicameral]] parliament made up of 100 members who are elected by [[proportional representation]]. Elections are scheduled to be held once every four years. 
 
 The [[President of Latvia]] can dismiss the Saeima and call early elections. < ref > {{Cite news |url=http://www.delfi.lv/news/national/politics/zatlers-nolemj-atlaist-saeimu.d?id=38755691 |title=Zatlers nolemj rosināt Saeimas atlaišanu |trans-title=Zatlers decides to initiate thedissolution of the Saeima |language=lv |date=May 28, 2011 |access-date=May 28, 2011 |work=Delfi}} < /ref >
