'''Rudeness''' (also called '''effrontery''') is showing disrespect to people or cultures. These have been created by [[personal boundaries|boundaries]] of normally [[acceptance|accepted]] behavior.  
 
 Forms of rudeness include acting inconsiderate, insensitive, deliberately offensive, [[Politeness|impolite]], a faux pas, obscenity, [[profanity]] and violating [[taboo]]s such as [[deviancy]].
