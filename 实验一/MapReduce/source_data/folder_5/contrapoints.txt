 == Personal life == 
 Wynn was born in [[Arlington, Virginia]]. She is a [[Trans woman|transgender woman]] < ref name= " NYT " > {{Cite news|last=Bergner|first=Daniel|date=2019-06-04|title=The Struggles of Rejecting the Gender Binary|language=en-US|work=The New York Times|url=https://www.nytimes.com/2019/06/04/magazine/gender-nonbinary.html|access-date=2019-06-09|issn=0362-4331}} < /ref >  and a [[lesbian]]. < ref name= " Shame " > {{Cite web|last=Wynn|first=Natalie|date=February 16, 2020|title=Shame|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=K7WvHTl_Q7I|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=|website=YouTube}} < /ref >  Her videos' main themes are not usually her own identity, but viewers get to follow her journey as she explores her own identity as side notes during her philosophical videos.
