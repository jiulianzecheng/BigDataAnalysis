 '''Lalling''' is a [[Municipalities of Germany|municipality]] in the Lower [[Bavaria]]n district of [[Deggendorf (district)|Deggendorf]] in [[Germany]]. It is a nationally recognized resort town. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 Lalling is along the [[Danube]] in the south of the [[Bavarian Forest]], specifically in what is called the Lallinger angle.
