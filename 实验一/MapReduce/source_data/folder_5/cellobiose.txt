'''Cellobiose''' is a kind of [[sugar]]. 
 
 This word is from [[biochemistry]] and cellobiose is part of the [[disaccharide]] group of sugars. This sugar was called ''cellose''. < ref > 
    {{cite web 
    | title=Oxford English Dictionary 
    | url=http://dictionary.oed.com/cgi/entry/50035390?single=1 & query_type=word & queryword=cellobiose & first=1 & max_to_show=10 
    | accessdate=2008-09-19}} 
 < /ref > 
 
 Cellobiose is made from [[cellulose]], which comes from the cells of plants. Things that have a lot of cellulose are good for making cellobiose, like [[cotton]] or [[paper]]. Water and cellulose make cellobiose: this process is called [[hydrolysis]]. 
 
 Pure cellobiose is a white powder. < ref > {{cite web
