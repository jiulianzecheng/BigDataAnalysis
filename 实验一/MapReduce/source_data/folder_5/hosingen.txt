 '''Hosingen''' ({{Lang-lb|Housen}}) is a small village and a former [[Communes of Luxembourg|commune]] in northern [[Luxembourg]]. It was part of the [[Clervaux (canton)|canton of Clervaux]] 
 
 On 1 January 2012, the commune was merged with [[Consthum]] and [[Hoscheid]] to form the new commune of [[Parc Hosingen]].
