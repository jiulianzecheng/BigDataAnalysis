 '''Geleen''' is a city in the [[Netherlands|Dutch]] province of [[Limburg (Netherlands)|Limburg]]. In 2020, 31,670 people lived there. 
 
 Since 2001, it is part of the new municipality of [[Sittard-Geleen]].
