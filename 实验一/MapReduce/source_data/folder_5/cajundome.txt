 The '''Cajundome''' is a 13,500-seat multi-purpose [[arena]] in [[Lafayette, Louisiana]]. The [[Louisiana Ragin' Cajuns]] [[Louisiana Ragin' Cajuns men's basketball|men's]] and [[Louisiana Ragin' Cajuns women's basketball|women's]] basketball programs play there. University events and commencement ceremonies including high school graduations also happen there.
