 '''Kronoby''' ({{lang-fi|Kruunupyy}}) is a [[municipality]] in [[Ostrobothnia (region)|Ostrobothnia]], [[Finland]]. It had about 6,700 people living there in January 2014. The municipalities next to it are [[Evijärvi]], [[Kaustinen]], [[Kokkola]], [[Luoto]], [[Pedersöre]] and [[Veteli]].  < ref > {{Cite web|url=https://www.maanmittauslaitos.fi/sites/maanmittauslaitos.fi/files/attachments/2018/01/Suomen_pa_2018_kunta_maakunta.pdf|title=FINLANDS AREAL KOMMUNVIS 1.1.2018}} < /ref > 
 
 The municipality was established in 1607.
