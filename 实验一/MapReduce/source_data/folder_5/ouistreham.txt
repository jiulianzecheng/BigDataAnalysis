 '''Ouistreham''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]] in [[Lower Normandy]] in the [[Calvados (department)|Calvados]] [[Departments of France|department]] in northwestern [[France]]. 
 
 It is a small port with fishing boats, leisure craft and a [[ferry]]-harbour. It serves as the port of the city of [[Caen]].
