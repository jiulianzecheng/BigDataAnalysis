 '''Kleenex''' is a [[brand]] [[name]] for many paper products, such as [[facial tissue]], [[Toilet paper|bathroom tissue]], [[Paper towel|paper towels]], [[Tampon|tampons]], and [[Diaper|diapers]]. The name is a popular [[genericized trademark]] for facial tissue in the United States. < ref > {{Cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=VWkfaTRQa5YC & q=kleenex+generic & pg=PA118|title=Studies in the History of the English Language II: Unfolding Conversations|last1=Minkova|first1=Donka|last2=Curzan|first2=Anne|last3=Stockwell|first3=Robert P.|last4=Emmons|first4=Kimberly|date=2004|publisher=Walter de Gruyter|isbn=978-3-11-018097-8|language=en}} < /ref >  It is a [[trademark]] of [[Kimberly-Clark Worldwide, Inc.]] Kleenex products are made in 30 countries and sold in more than 170 countries.
