 '''Festung Ehrenbreitstein''' is a [[fortress]]. It was built on a mountain called Ehrenbreitstein on the right side of the [[Rhine]] opposite to the town of [[Koblenz]] in the [[Germany|German]] [[States of Germany|state]] of [[Rhineland-Palatinate]]. 
 
 It was built as part of the  " Festung Koblenz "  (Fortress Koblenz), by [[Prussia]] between [[1817]] and [[1832]] and guarded the middle Rhine region. This area had often been invaded by the [[France|French]]. The fortress was never attacked.
