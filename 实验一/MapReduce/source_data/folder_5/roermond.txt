 '''Roermond''' is a [[List of municipalities of the Netherlands|municipality]] in the province [[Limburg (Netherlands)|Limburg]] in the east of the [[Netherlands]]. It has about 45,000 [[inhabitant|people living in it]]. 
 
 Since 2007, [[Swalmen]] became a part of the municipality of Roermond.
