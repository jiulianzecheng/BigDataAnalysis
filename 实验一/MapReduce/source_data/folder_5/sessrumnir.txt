In [[Norse mythology]], '''Sessrumnir''' ([[Old Norse]]: “seat-room” or “seat-roomer”) is an enormous hall belonging to the goddess [[Freyja]]. There she receives half of those killed in battle, as chosen by the [[Valkyries]]. It is said to be very large and has many seats.
