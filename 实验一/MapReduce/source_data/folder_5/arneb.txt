'''Arneb''', also called '''Alpha Leporis''', is a yellow [[supergiant]] [[star]] in the constellation [[Lepus (constellation)|Lepus]]. It is 75 times the diameter of the [[Sun]] < ref > {{Cite web|title=Arneb|url=http://stars.astro.illinois.edu/sow/arneb.html|access-date=2023-08-26|website=stars.astro.illinois.edu}} < /ref >  and over 2,000 light years away. < ref > {{Cite web|last=van Leeuwen|first=F.|date=2007-08-13|title=Validation of the new Hipparcos reduction|url=https://arxiv.org/abs/0708.1752v1|access-date=2023-08-26|website=arXiv.org|language=en}} < /ref >
