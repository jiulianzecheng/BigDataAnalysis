 '''Offering''' can mean any of the following: 
 
 *An '''offering''' is a donation during [[religion|religious]] [[worship]]. See also [[alms]], [[tithe]], [[Charity (virtue)|charity]], [[gift]] and [[virtue]]. 
 
 *An '''offering''' can mean a religious [[sacrifice]] of plant, animal or human life (blood sacrifice), or a sacrifice of precious things. An offering may also be in money, or may be of a purely [[Spirituality|spiritual]] nature, like offering one's heart or life to God (see also [[Oblation]]). 
 
 *An [[Offering (Buddhism)|offering, in Buddhism]], is a form of [[prayer]].
