 The '''Ozarks''',  also called the '''Ozark Mountains''' and '''Ozark Plateau''', are a [[Physiography|physiographic]], [[Geology|geologic]], and [[culture|cultural]] highland region of the central [[United States]]. It covers much of the [[south]] half of [[Missouri]] and an large portion of [[northwest]] and North central [[Arkansas]]. The region also goes westward into [[northeast]] [[Oklahoma]] and extreme [[southeast]] [[Kansas]]. The name Ozarks comes from the [[French language|French]] ''Aux Arcs''.
