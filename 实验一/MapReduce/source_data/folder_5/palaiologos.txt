The '''House of Palaiologos''' (also spelled as '''Palaeologus''' or '''Palaeologue''') was a [[Byzantine Greeks|Byzantine Greek]] royal family. They ruled the [[Byzantine Empire]] for almost two hundred years, from 1259 to the [[Fall of Constantinople]] in 1453.
