 '''''Crooklyn''''' is a 1994 [[Autobiography|semi-autobiographic]] [[drama]] [[movie]]. It was [[movie director|directed]] by [[Spike Lee]]. He also wrote the movie and acted in it. The movie takes place in the 1970s in [[Brooklyn, New York]]. The stars [[Alfre Woodard]], [[Delroy Lindo]], [[David Patrick Kelly]], and [[Zelda Harris]]. ''Crooklyn'' was released in May 1994 and did well at the [[box office]].
