 '''Cheste''' ([[Valencian language|Valencian]]: ''Xest'') is a city in [[Spain]]. It is in the [[Valencian Community]]. It is {{convert|26| km|abbr=off}} west of [[Valencia]] and about {{convert|350| km|abbr=off}} east of [[Madrid]]. It covers {{convert|71.4|km2|abbr=off}}. In 2008, 8,127 people lived there according to the National Institute of Statistics [[census]].

 == Economy == 
 Cheste's main industry is agriculture, specifically the [[vineyard]]s. Cheste is also known for the [[MotoGP]]'s [[Circuit Ricardo Tormo]], built in 1999.
