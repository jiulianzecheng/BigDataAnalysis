 '''Gamestar''' is a [[Germany|German]] monthly released PC computer game magazine. It is published by the IDG Entertainment Media GmbH in [[Munich]]. The first edition was 10/1997 with a prize of 6,90 [[Deutsche Mark|DM]]. Current prize is 6,30€ (XL version), 5,30€ (DVD version) and 3,50€ (Magazin version).
