 '''Noordwijk''' is a [[List of municipalities of the Netherlands|municipality]] in the [[Netherlands|Dutch]] [[Provinces of the Netherlands|province]] of [[South Holland]]. In 2019, 42,859 people lived there. 
 
 In 2019, the former municipality of [[Noordwijkerhout]] became a part of Noordwijk.
