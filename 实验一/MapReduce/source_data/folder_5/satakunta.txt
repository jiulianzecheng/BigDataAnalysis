 '''Satakunta''' is a region in western [[Finland]]. As of November 2013, there were almost 225,000 people living there. Neighbouring regions are [[Southwest Finland]], [[Pirkanmaa]], [[Southern Ostrobothnia]] and  [[Ostrobothnia (region)|Ostrobothnia]].
