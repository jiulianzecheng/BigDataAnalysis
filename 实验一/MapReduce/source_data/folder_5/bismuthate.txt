'''Bismuthate''' is an [[ion]]. Its chemical formula is BiO < sub > 3 < /sub > < sup > - < /sup > . It has [[bismuth]] in its +5 [[oxidation state]]. It is a very strong [[oxidizing agent]]. It reacts with hot water to make [[bismuth(III) oxide]] and oxygen. It also reacts with [[acid]]s. [[Sodium bismuthate]] is the most common bismuthate. It is one of the few [[sodium compounds]] that does not dissolve in water.
