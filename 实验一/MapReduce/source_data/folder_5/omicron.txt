 '''Omicron''' (uppercase/lowercase '''Ο ο''') is a letter of the [[Greek alphabet]]. It is used to represent the  " o "  sound in [[Ancient Greek|Ancient]] and [[Modern Greek language|Modern]] Greek. In the system of [[Greek numerals]], it has a value of 70. Letters that came from it include the Roman [[O]] and Cyrillic [[О]].
