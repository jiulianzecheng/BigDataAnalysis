The '''Oireachtas''' is the [[country|national]] [[parliament]] of [[Republic of Ireland|Ireland]]. 
 
 There are three parts of the Oireachtas: 
 
 *The [[President of Ireland]] 
 *[[Dáil Éireann]] - The lower house. It is similar to the [[House of Commons of the United Kingdom|House of Commons]] in the [[United Kingdom]] or the [[United States House of Representatives|House of Representatives]] in the [[United States]].  
 *[[Seanad Éireann]] - The upper house. It is similar to the [[House of Lords]] and the [[United States Senate|Senate]] 
 
 Only the [[Irish language|Irish]] names are used, never the [[english language|English]]
