 '''Eibelstadt''' ({{IPA-de|ˈaɪ̯bl̩ˌʃtat|-|De-Eibelstadt.ogg}}) is a [[Town#Germany|town]] in [[Würzburg (district)|Würzburg]], in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 Eibelstadt is among vineyards on the right edge of the [[Main (river)|Main]], 10 & nbsp;km southeast of [[Würzburg]].
