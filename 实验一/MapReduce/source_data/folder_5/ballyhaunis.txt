'''Ballyhaunis''' is a town in [[County Mayo]]. It is  Ireland's most [[diverse]] town. It has the  first purpose-built [[mosque]] in Ireland. It was built in 1986. A [[Halal|halal meat]] plant was built there by a [[Pakistanis|Pakistani]] businessman in the 1970s.
