 '''Cetartiodactyla''' is the [[Taxonomy|taxonomical]] [[order (biology)|superorder]] which includes [[whale]]s (and [[dolphin]]s) and [[even-toed ungulate]]s.  The name was created by merging the name for the two orders, [[Cetacea]] and [[Artiodactyla]], into a single word.  
 
 Cetartiodactyla is used because it is thought that whales [[evolution|evolved]] from the artiodactyls.  Because of this, their closest living land relative may be the [[hippopotamus]].
