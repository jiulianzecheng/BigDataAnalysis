 The [[order (biology)|order]] '''Ostreoida''' includes the [[oyster]]s and other families of [[bivalve]]s such as [[Pectinidae]] (mostly [[scallop]]s).  
 
 ==2010 taxonomy== 
 In 2010, a new proposed classification system for the Bivalvia was published by Bieler, Carter  &  Coan, revising the classification of the Bivalvia, including the order Ostreoida. < ref > Bieler R; Carter J.G.  &  Coan E.V. 2010. Classification of bivalve families. In: Bouchet P.  &  Rocroi J.P. 2010. Nomenclator of bivalve families. ''Malacologia'' '''52'''(2): 1-184 < /ref >
