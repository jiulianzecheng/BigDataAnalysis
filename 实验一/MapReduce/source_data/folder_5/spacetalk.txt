 '''Spacetalk''' is an Australian technology company headquartered in [[Adelaide]], South Australia. It was founded in 2001 as MGM Wireless and changed its name to Spacetalk Ltd in November 2020 to reflect its focus on developing wearable devices. < ref > {{Cite news|last=Spence|first=Andrew|date=18 November 2020|title=Name change to help Adelaide tech company walk the talk|work=InDaily|url=https://indaily.com.au/news/business/2020/11/18/name-change-to-help-adelaide-tech-company-walk-the-talk|access-date=24 November 2020}} < /ref >  The company's education division provides school-to-home communication services for absence management, news, and emergencies using SMS, apps, and cloud-based technology. < ref > {{Cite news|last=Henderson|first=Leila|date=5 March 2012|title=Emergency Text Messaging System Offered to Flood Stricken School Communities by MGM Wireless|work=Newsmaker|url=https://www.newsmaker.com.au/news/15228/emergency-text-messaging-system-offered-to-flood-stricken-school-communities-by-mgm-wireless#.X7y9ZmgzZhE|access-date=24 November 2020}} < /ref >  Spacetalk launched its family location and child safety wearable devices under the Spacetalk brand in September 2017. < ref > {{Cite news|date=2017-09-20|title=Helicopter parents to double MGM revenue with child-tracking smartwatch - Stockhead|language=en-US|work=Stockhead|url=https://stockhead.com.au/tech/helicopter-parents-to-double-mgm-revenue-with-child-tracking-smartwatch/|access-date=2017-12-10}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite web|title=The kid-friendly smartwatch designed to keep youngsters safe|url=https://www.9news.com.au/technology/2017/10/09/20/31/the-new-smartwatch-designed-to-keep-kids-safe|access-date=2017-12-10|website=www.9news.com.au}} < /ref >  The product is available in Australia, New Zealand, and the UK.
