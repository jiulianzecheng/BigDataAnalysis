 The '''haplorhines''', the  " dry-nosed "  primates (the Greek name means  " simple-nosed " ), are members of the '''Haplorhini''' clade: the prosimian [[tarsier]]s and all of the true simians. The simians are the catarrhines ([[Old World monkey]]s and [[ape]]s, including [[human]]s) and the platyrrhines ([[New World monkey]]s).
