 '''''Steatoda''''' is a [[spider]] genus. It includes more than 120 recognized [[species]] around the world. They are often dark spiders. 
 
 The spiders are often mistaken for widow spiders. They are often called false black widows. These spiders are less harmful to [[human]]s. They are shaped similar to widow spiders.
