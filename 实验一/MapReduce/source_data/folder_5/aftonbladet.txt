 '''''Aftonbladet''''' is a [[newspaper]] in [[Sweden]]. It was started on 6 December 1830 by [[Lars Johan Hiertha]]. It was originally [[Liberalism|liberal]], before being bought by the [[Swedish Social Democratic Party]]/[[trade union]] movement in early October 1956.
