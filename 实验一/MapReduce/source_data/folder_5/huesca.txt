 '''Huesca''' ({{IPA-es|ˈweska|lang}}; {{lang-an|Uesca}}) is a city in north-eastern [[Spain]], within the [[Autonomous communities of Spain|autonomous community]] of [[Aragon]]. It is also the capital of the Spanish [[Huesca (province)|province of the same name]] and of the [[comarca]] of [[Hoya de Huesca/Plana de Uesca|Hoya de Huesca]].
