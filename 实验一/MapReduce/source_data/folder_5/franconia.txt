 '''Franconia''' ({{lang-de|Franken}} {{IPA-de|ˈfʁaŋkn̩}}; [[Franconian Dialect|Franconian dialect]]: ''Franggn'' {{IPA-dedia|ˈfrɑŋɡŋ̍|}}) is the northern part of the [[States of Germany|German federal state]] of [[Bavaria]]. This region is divided in three smaller regions: [[Upper Franconia Government Region|Oberfranken]], [[Middle Franconia|Mittelfranken]], and [[Lower Franconia Government Region|Unterfranken]]. The biggest [[city]] in Franconia is [[Nuremberg]]. 
 
 Other large [[city|cities]] in Franconia are [[Coburg]], [[Aschaffenburg]], [[Würzburg]], [[Erlangen]], [[Fürth]], [[Bamberg]], [[Bayreuth]], [[Schweinfurt]] and [[Hof]]. 
 
 == Rivers == 
 The most important river in Franconia is the [[Regnitz]], which is 63.7 km (39.6 mi) in length.{{commonscat}}
