'''Psylocke''' is the name of two fictional mutant characters and superheroes from the [[Marvel Comics]] universe, commonly in association with the mutant superhero team, the [[X-Men]]. The two character who have assumed the role of Psylocke are [[Betsy Braddock|'''Betsy Braddock''']] and '''[[Kwannon]]'''.
