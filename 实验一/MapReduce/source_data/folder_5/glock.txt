 The '''Glock''' [[pistol]] is a series of [[polymer]]-framed [[semi-automatic firearm|semi-automatic pistols]]. They are designed and produced by Glock Ges.m.b.H. in [[Deutsch-Wagram]], [[Austria]]. It entered [[Austrian]] Armed Forces and police service by 1982. < ref name=Kasler > Kasler, Peter Alan 1992. ''Glock: the new wave in combat handguns''. Boulder, CO: Paladin Press. {{ISBN|9780873646499}} < /ref >  Different models use different [[ammunition]]. 
 
 There was resistance from the market to accept a  " plastic gun " , but this was overcome. Glock pistols have become the company's most [[profit]]able line of products, with 65% of the market share of handguns for [[United States]] [[law enforcement]] agencies.They also supply many national armed forces and security agencies around the world. < ref name= " Sweeney 2008 " > {{cite book |title=The Gun Digest book of the Glock |last=Sweeney |first=Patrick |year=2008 |publisher=2nd ed, Krause Publications |location=Iola, WI |isbn=0896896420}} < /ref >  Glocks are also popular firearms amongst civilians in the U.S., for recreational and competition shooting, and for home or self defense. < ref > Barret, P.M. ''Glock: the rise of America's gun''. < /ref >
