 '''Heerenveen''' ({{lang-fy|It Hearrenfean}}) is a [[List of municipalities of the Netherlands|municipality]] in the [[Netherlands|Dutch]] [[Provinces of the Netherlands|province]] of [[Friesland]]. In 2019, 50,257 people lived there. 
 
 On 1 January 2014, [[Boarnsterhim]] is a part of the municipality of Heerenveen.
