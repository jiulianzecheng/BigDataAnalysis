 An '''autograph''' is some writing written only in the handwriting of its [[author]]; it is ''not'' typing. 
 
 An autograph can also mean someones [[signature]]. This term is used for collecting autographs of [[celebrity|celebrities]]. The collection of autographs is called 'philography'.
