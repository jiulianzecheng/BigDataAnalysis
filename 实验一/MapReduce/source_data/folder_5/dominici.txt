 '''Dominici''' is a [[progressive metal]] band that started in 2005 by former [[Dream Theater]] vocalist, [[Charlie Dominici]]. Their only published works are the three albums that make up the [[O3: A Trilogy|O < sub > 3 < /sub >  Trilogy]]. 
 
 Dominici also opened for three Dream Theater concerts, during Dream Theater's  " [[Systematic Chaos#Tour|Chaos in Motion Tour]]. "
