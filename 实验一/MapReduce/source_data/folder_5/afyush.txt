 '''Afyush''' ({{Lang-ar|أفيوش}}) is a village in Islah in Mudhaykhirah District in [[Al Udayn District]]. It is in the [[ibb Governorate]]. In the 2004 census its population was 8870 people. < ref > {{Cite web|url=http://yemenna.com/index.php?go=guide & op=show_pri & ide=1265|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20191121203959/http://yemenna.com/index.php?go=guide & op=show_pri & ide=1265|url-status=dead|archive-date=2019-11-21|title=الدليل الشامل - محافظة إب - مديرية مذيخره - عزلة الافيوش|date=2019-11-21|access-date=2020-04-29}} < /ref >
