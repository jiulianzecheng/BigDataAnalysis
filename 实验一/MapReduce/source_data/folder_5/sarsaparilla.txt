 '''Sarsaparilla''' (IPA: /ˌsæspəˈɹɪlə/) (''Smilax regelii'') is a perennial trailing vine with prickly stems; it is from tropical America and the West Indies. Its name (which is ''zarzaparrilla'' in Spanish) comes from the Spanish words ''zarza'' for  " shrub "  and ''parrilla'' for  " little grape vine.
