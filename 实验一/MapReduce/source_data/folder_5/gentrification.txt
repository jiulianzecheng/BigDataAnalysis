 '''Gentrification''' is the process of changing how a [[neighborhood]] looks like by building better buildings, [[condominiums]] or tearing down old buildings to make it look better so that [[wealth]]ier people and businesses can come live in this neighborhood. < ref > {{cite web|title=Gentrification|url=http://dictionary.reference.com/browse/gentrification|website=Dictionary.com}} < /ref > 
 
 == Background == 
 Gentrification means the transformation of a working-class or vacant area of the main part of a city to middle class residential or commercial area. < ref > {{Cite book|url=https://www.worldcat.org/oclc/432315115|title=The gentrification reader|date=2010|publisher=Routledge|others=Loretta Lees, Tom Slater, Elvin K. Wyly|isbn=978-0-415-54839-7|location=London|oclc=432315115}} < /ref >  Gentrification often increases the economic value of a neighborhood, but changes the [[Demography|demographic]] of that neighborhood often causing people who lived in this neighborhood for a long time to leave because of rising prices. < ref name= " :02 " > {{Cite news|last=West|first=Allyn|date=5 March 2020|title=Baffled City: Exploring the architecture of gentrification|work=Texas Observer|url=https://www.texasobserver.org/gentrification-architecture/|url-status=dead|access-date=21 June 2020|archive-date=22 June 2020|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200622141519/https://www.texasobserver.org/gentrification-architecture/}} < /ref >  Gentrification mainly happens in neighborhoods where there is a large [[minority]] population such as [[African-American]] or [[Hispanic and Latino Americans|Latino]]s. < ref name= " :12 " > {{Cite journal|last1=Harrison|first1=Sally|last2=Jacobs|first2=Andrew|date=2016|title=Gentrification and the Heterogeneous City: Finding a Role for Design|url=http://www.theplanjournal.com/article/gentrification-and-heterogeneous-city-finding-role-design|journal=The Plan|volume=1|issue=2|doi=10.15274/tpj.2016.01.02.03|doi-access=free}} < /ref > < ref name= " CDC " > {{cite web |url=https://www.cdc.gov/healthyplaces/healthtopics/gentrification.htm |title=Health Effects of Gentrification |date=24 March 2015 |website=[[Centers for Disease Control]] |publisher= Centers for Disease Control|access-date=24 March 2015}} < /ref >
