 '''Littenseradiel''' ({{lang-nl|Littenseradeel}}) is a former [[List of municipalities of the Netherlands|municipality]] in the [[Netherlands|Dutch]] [[Provinces of the Netherlands|province]] of [[Friesland]]. 
 
 == History == 
 It was created on 1 January 1984 from the former municipalities Baarderadeel and Hennaarderadeel. On 1 January 2018, the municipality was dissolved and its territory was split between three other municipalities: [[Waadhoeke]], which was created that day, [[Leeuwarden]] and [[Súdwest-Fryslân]].
