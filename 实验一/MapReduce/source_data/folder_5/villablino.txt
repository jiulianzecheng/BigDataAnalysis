 '''Villablino''' (''Vitsablinu'', in [[Leonese language]]) is a municipality in the [[León (province)|province of León]], [[Castile and León]], [[Spain]]. According to the [[2004]] [[census]] ([[Instituto Nacional de Estadística (Spain)|INE]]), the municipality has 12,212 people.
