 The '''PVP''' for ''Petit Véhicule Protégé'' ({{lang-en|small protected [[vehicle]]}}) is a light [[military]] [[car]] designed and built in [[France]] by [[Panhard]]. It is a [[four-wheel drive]] car. It is used by the [[France|French]] [[Army]] for [[reconnaissance]]. In [[2013]] the other countries that use it are [[Chile]], [[Romania]], and [[Togo]]. In France this vehicle will replace [[Peugeot P4]].
