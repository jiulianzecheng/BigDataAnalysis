 '''Ormuri''' ({{lang-fa|ارموری}}) is an [[Iran|East Iranian]] [[language]] spoken in the [[city]] of [[Kaniguram]] in [[Pakistan]]. It may also be spoken by a few people in [[Logar]], [[Afghanistan]]. It is notable for its unusual [[sound]] [[wikt:inventory|inventory]]. This includes a [[voice]]less [[alveolar trill]] and voiced and unvoiced [[alveolo-palatal fricatives]].
