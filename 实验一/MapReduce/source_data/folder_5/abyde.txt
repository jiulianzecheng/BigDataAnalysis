 == History == 
 Established in 2016 in [[Clearwater, Florida]], Abyde began to modernize compliance practices, with a focus on addressing [[:en:Health_Insurance_Portability_and_Accountability_Act|Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act]] (HIPAA) and [[:en:Occupational_Safety_and_Health_Administration|Occupational Safety and Health Administration]] (OSHA) regulations. < ref name= " :2 "  / > < ref > {{Cite web |title=OSHA for Healthcare {{!}} Abyde |url=https://abyde.com/osha-for-healthcare/ |access-date=2023-08-27 |language=en-US}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite web |title=Abyde |url=https://www.vadental.org/vda-services/abyde |access-date=2023-08-27 |website=www.vadental.org}} < /ref > 
 
 Abyde helps healthcare organizations with; Automated [[:en:Risk_analysis_(business)|Risk Analysis]] for MIPS and [[:en:Medicare_Access_and_CHIP_Reauthorization_Act_of_2015|MACRA]] compliance, Audit Protection during audits, Customized Policy Generation for tailored compliance and Educational Resources like videos and quizzes. < ref name= " :3 " > {{Cite web|last=Igor|date=2021-11-10|title=Your HIPAA Compliant Abyde|url=https://hipaa-software.com/abyde/|access-date=2023-08-27|website=hipaa-software.com|language=en-US}} < /ref >
