 '''Cachitos''' are a Venezuelan type of [[sandwich]] filled with [[ham]] and [[cheese]]. < ref > {{Cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=YrZ5-1eHjFQC & q=%22Cachitos%22+venezuela|title=Venezuela Handbook|last1=Murphy|first1=Alan|last2=Day|first2=Mick|date=2000|publisher=Footprint|isbn=978-1-900949-58-3|language=en}} < /ref >  It is similar to a [[croissant]]. It is a traditional food in [[Venezuela]] where it is usually eaten for [[breakfast]]. Sometimes, it is served with [[juice]], [[coffee]], or [[Malta (soft drink)|malta]]. 
 
 == Preparation == 
 The ingredients are different in different states and bakeries but the most common ingredients are: Wheat flour, butter, eggs, room temperature milk, water, salt, yeast and a pinch of sugar. < ref > {{Cite web|title=La Historia del Cachito de Jamón – La Cachiterie I Panaderia y Cafeteria en Madrid|url=https://www.la-cachiterie.com/historia-del-cachito-de-jamon/|access-date=2021-05-24|language=es-ES}} < /ref >  Other meats and things can go inside.
