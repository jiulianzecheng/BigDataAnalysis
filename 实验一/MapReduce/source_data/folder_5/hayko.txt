 '''Hayk'''  " '''Hayko''' "  '''Hakobyan''' ({{lang-hy|Հայկ Հակոբյան}}; 25 August 1973 – 29 September 2021) was an Armenian singer. He represented [[Armenia]] in the [[Eurovision Song Contest 2007|2007]] [[Eurovision Song Contest]] in [[Helsinki]]. He performed  " [[Anytime You Need]] "  and came in eighth place in the contest. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.esc-history.com/details.asp?key=4705|title=Armenia 2007: Hayko,  " Anything You Need " |publisher=[[Eurovision Song Contest]]|access-date=20 August 2011|archive-date=9 October 2012|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20121009051641/http://www.esc-history.com/details.asp?key=4705|url-status=dead}} < /ref >  He was born in [[Yerevan]], Armenia. 
 
 Hayko died on 29 September 2021, in [[Yerevan]], from [[COVID-19]] at aged 48. < ref > {{cite web |last1=Ghazanchyan |first1=Siranush |title=Armenian singer, songwriter Hayko dies aged 48 |url=https://en.armradio.am/2021/09/29/armenian-singer-songwriter-hayko-dies-aged-44/ |website=Public Radio of Armenia |access-date=29 September 2021 |date=29 September 2021}} < /ref >
