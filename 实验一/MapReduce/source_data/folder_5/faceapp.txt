'''FaceApp''' is a photo and video editing application for [[iOS]] and [[Android (operating system)|Android]] developed by FaceApp Technology Limited. The program is able to realistically change the face in the photo according to the selected parameters: make it older, younger or change gender, hairstyle, add glasses or a smile, using [[Neural network|neural networks]]. 
 
 The app has been criticized and accused of supporting racism for lightening the skin color of dark-skinned people and for so-called ethnic filters of  " white " ,  " black " ,  " Asian " ,  " Indian " .
