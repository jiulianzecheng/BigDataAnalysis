 '''Mafraq''' is a city in [[Jordan]]. It is the Capital of [[Mafraq Governorate]]. It is located 80 km north of the Jordanian capital, [[Amman]], at the crossroads of [[Syria]] from north and [[Iraq]] from east. It has an air and [[military base]]. The Amman-Damascus railway runs through the city. There is only one university in the city, [[Al Al-Bayt University]]. The university was established in 1992 and is in the eastern suburbs of Mafraq.
