 '''Arnedo''' is a [[Municipalities of Spain|municipality]] in the [[Provinces of Spain|province]] and [[Autonomous communities of Spain|autonomous community]] of [[La Rioja]] in [[Spain]]. 
 
 The municipality has about 15,000 people.
