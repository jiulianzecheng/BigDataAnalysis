 '''Sarthe''' is a [[departments of France|department]] found in the west of [[France]] in the [[Pays de la Loire]] region. Its [[prefecture]] is [[Le Mans]] and its population is about 543,000 ([[2006]] estimation). It is one of the 83 first French departments made during the [[French revolution]].
