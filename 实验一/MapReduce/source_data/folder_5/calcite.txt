 '''Calcite''' is one of the most common [[mineral]]s on the [[Earth]]'s surface. It is a often part of [[sedimentary rock]]s, especially [[limestone]]. It is also the primary mineral in [[Metamorphic rock|metamorphic]] [[marble]]. 
 
 It is a chemical or biochemical [[calcium carbonate]] with the formula [[Calcium carbonate|CaCO < sub > 3 < /sub > ]]. It is a [[carbonate]] mineral. It can be a [[Vein (geology)|vein]] mineral in [[Deposition (geology)|deposits]] from [[hot spring]]s, and in [[cavern]]s as [[stalactite]]s and [[stalactite|stalagmite]]s.
