 A '''hoof''' is the [[foot]] of certain animals known as [[ungulate]]s (such as deer, cows, pigs, sheep, and  giraffes). These animals walk on their toes. The toes have a hard cover made out of the same material as [[fingernail]]s. This is called [[keratin]].
