 '''Oise''' is a [[departments of France|department]] found in the north of [[France]] in the [[Hauts-de-France]] [[region]]. Its [[prefecture]] is [[Beauvais]]. Its population is about 780,000 (2006 estimation). It is one of the 83 first French departments made during the [[French revolution]].
