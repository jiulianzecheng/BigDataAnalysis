 '''Holostei''' are [[bony fish]] that show primitive characteristics. There are eight [[species]] divided among two orders, the Amiiformes represented by a single living species, the [[bowfin]] (''Amia calva''), and the Lepisosteiformes, the [[gar]]s. There are more species to be found in the fossil record.
