 '''Valbirse''' is a new [[municipality]] of the [[administrative district]] of [[Jura bernois (administrative district)|Jura bernois]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Bern (canton)|Bern]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 On 1 January 2015, the former municipalities of [[Bévilard]], [[Malleray]] and [[Pontenet]] merged into the new municipality of Valbirse.
