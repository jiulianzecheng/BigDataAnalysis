'''Chittoor''' is a city and district headquarters of the [[Chittoor district]] of [[Andhra Pradesh]], India. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://chittoor.cdma.ap.gov.in/en|title=About Chittoor Municipal Corporation - Chittoor Municipal Corporation|website=chittoor.cdma.ap.gov.in|access-date=2016-12-15|archive-date=2019-06-03|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190603042825/http://chittoor.cdma.ap.gov.in/en|url-status=dead}} < /ref >  It is the headquarters of the Chittoor mandal and Chittoor revenue division. [[Telugu]] is the widely spoken language in this region. The total population of Chittoor city was 353,766 as of the 2011 Census of India. < ref name=population > {{cite web|url=http://www.citypopulation.de/php/india-andhrapradesh.php|title=Andhra Pradesh (India): Districts, Cities, Towns and Outgrowth Wards - Population Statistics in Maps and Charts|work=citypopulation.de}} < /ref > 
 
 The total area of the city is {{convert|95.97|km2|abbr=off}}. 
 
 The main attraction in this city is the Chandragiri Fort. 
 
 [[Nara Chandrababu Naidu]] was born in Chittoor. He is the [[Chief Minister of Andhra Pradesh]] and the leader of the [[Telugu Desam Party]].
