 '''Marktredwitz''' ({{IPA-de|maʁktˈʁɛt.vɪt͡s|lang|De-Marktredwitz.ogg}}) is a town in [[Wunsiedel (district)|Wunsiedel]], in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]], close to the [[Czech Republic|Czech]] border. It is 22 & nbsp;km west of [[Cheb]], 50 & nbsp;km east of [[Bayreuth]] and 50 & nbsp;km south of Hof/Saale. [[Marktredwitz station]] is at the junction of the [[Nuremberg–Cheb railway]] and the [[Weiden–Oberkotzau railway|Munich–Hof railway]].
