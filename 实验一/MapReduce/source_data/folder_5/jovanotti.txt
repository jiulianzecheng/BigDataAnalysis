 '''Lorenzo Cherubini''' (born 27 September 1966), better known as '''Jovanotti''' is an [[Italy|Italian]] [[singer-songwriter]]. 
 
 Jovanotti first sang a mix of [[Hip hop music|hip hop]], [[Rap music|rap]] and [[disco]]. Then he included [[funk]], [[world music]] and even [[classical music]] and [[ska]]. Most of his songs are sung in [[Italian language|Italian]]. He has also made a [[Spanish language]] greatest hits [[album]]. 
 
 == Biography == 
 === Private life === 
 Cherubini is the third of four brothers. < ref > [http://www.musicalnews.com/articolo.php?codice=12503 & sz=5 Musicalnews.com: Jovanotti - Lorenzo Cherubini ospite Domenica 8 giugno dei giovani redattori di Screensaver su Rai Tre < !-- Titolo generato automaticamente -- > ] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150224181326/http://www.musicalnews.com/articolo.php?codice=12503 & sz=5 |date=2015-02-24 }} {{in lang|it}} < /ref >  His oldest brother, Umberto was a flying instructor at Touchandgo School until his death in a [[plane crash]] on 22 October 2007. After that, Jovanotti composed ''In orbita'' and ''Fango'' as tribute. The second brother, Bernardo, is a showman. 
 
 Jovanotti attended at Liceo Malpighi in Rome where he get a degree in science. 
 
 === Career === 
 Jovanotti's musical career began in 1988. ''Jovanotti for President'' was his first album.
