 '''Ameland''' is an [[island]] and [[List of municipalities of the Netherlands|municipality]] of the province of [[Friesland]], the [[Netherlands]]. In 2016 the municipality had about 3,600 people. The municipality covers an area of 269 km², the island an area of 59 km². 
 
 Ameland makes part of the [[West Frisian Islands]] ({{lang-nl|Waddeneilanden}}). 
 
 It lies west of the island of [[Schiermonnikoog]] and east of the island of [[Terschelling]].
