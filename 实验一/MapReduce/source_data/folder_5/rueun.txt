 '''Rueun''' was a [[municipality]] of the [[district]] of [[Surselva (district)|Surselva]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Graubünden]] in [[Switzerland]].  On 1 January 2014, the former municipalities of Rueun, [[Castrisch]], [[Ilanz]], [[Ladir]], [[Luven]], [[Pitasch]], [[Riein]], [[Ruschein]], [[Schnaus]], [[Sevgein]], [[Duvin]], [[Pigniu]] and [[Siat]] merged into the new municipality of [[Ilanz/Glion]].
