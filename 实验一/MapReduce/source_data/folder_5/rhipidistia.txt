 The '''Rhipidistia''' are [[lobe-finned fish]], and include the ancestors of the [[tetrapod]]s. The Rhipidistia are a subgroup of [[Sarcopterygii]] which includes the lungfish ([[Dipnoi]]) and the [[tetrapodomorph]]s. 
 
 As [[cladistic]] understanding of the vertebrates has improved over the last few decades a [[monophyletic]] Rhipidistia is now understood to include the ancestor of the whole [[Tetrapoda]].
