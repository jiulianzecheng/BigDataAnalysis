 '''Heteroflexibility''' is a kind of [[sexual orientation|sexual]] or [[romantic orientation]] characterized by mostly [[heterosexual]] [[sexual attraction|attraction]] to the opposite [[sex]] or [[gender]] with occasional [[homosexual]] attraction to the same sex or gender. It is described as  " mostly straight " . < ref > {{Cite journal |last1=Thompson |first1=E.M. |last2=Morgan |first2=E.M. |year=2008 |title= " Mostly straight "  young women: Variations in sexual behavior and identity development |journal=Developmental Psychology |volume=44 |issue=1 |pages=15–21 |doi=10.1037/0012-1649.44.1.15 |pmid=18194001 |s2cid=14336659}} < /ref >  It falls under the [[bisexual]] umbrella term. Some heteroflexibles identify as a [[straight ally]] and/or heterosexual. < ref > {{Cite journal |last=Frank |first=Katherine |year=2008 |title='Not Gay, but Not Homophobic': Male Sexuality and Homophobia in the 'Lifestyle' |journal=Sexualities |volume=11 |issue=4 |pages=435–454 |doi=10.1177/1363460708091743 |s2cid=143060180}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite journal |last=Smorag |first=Pascale |date=14 May 2008 |title=From Closet Talk to PC Terminology : Gay Speech and the Politics of Visibility |url=http://transatlantica.revues.org/index3503.html |journal=Transatlantica |issue=1 |doi=10.4000/transatlantica.3503 |access-date=21 October 2010 |doi-access=free}} < /ref >
