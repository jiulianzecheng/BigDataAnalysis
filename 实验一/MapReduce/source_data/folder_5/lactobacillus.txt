 '''''Lactobacillus''''' is a [[genus]] of [[bacteria]]. Most ''Lactobacillus'' species are [[Endospore|non-spore-forming]] and ferment [[glucose]] into [[lactose]]. This genus also contains several bacteria that make up part of the natural flora of the human [[vagina]]. 
 They are really helpful in the gut. It is a rod-shaped non-spore forming bacteria. It can be found in foods like yogurt.
