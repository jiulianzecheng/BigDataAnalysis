 '''''Viridiana''''' is a 1961 Spanish-Mexican comedy drama movie directed by [[Luis Buñuel]] and is loosely based on the 1895 novel ''Halma'' by [[Benito Pérez Galdós]]. It stars [[Silvia Pinal]], Francisco Rabal and Fernando Rey.
