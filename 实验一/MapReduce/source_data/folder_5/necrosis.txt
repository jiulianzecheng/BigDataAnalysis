'''Necrosis''' is a [[medical condition]] in which parts of a person's [[body]] [[death|die]], turn black, and fall off. This happens when the there is not enough blood reaching the [[Tissue (biological)|tissues]]. This can happen for many reasons including injury, [[poison]]s, [[bacteria]] or [[radiation|radiation damage]]. The dead tissue will not grow back. < ref > {{Cite web
