 '''Treuchtlingen''' is a town in [[Weißenburg-Gunzenhausen]] in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 ===Place=== 
 Treuchtlingen is on the river [[Altmühl]], 9 & nbsp;km southwest of [[Weißenburg in Bayern]], and 45 & nbsp;km northeast of [[Donauwörth]]. 
 
 It is on the [[European Watershed]] between the [[Rhine]] and [[Danube]]. 
 
 ===Subdivisions=== 
 In addition to the town itself, the municipality of Treuchtlingen today has 53 [[Hamlet (place)|hamlet]]s and [[village]]s. The municipal territory is divided into 12 districts (including the town) and several hamlets:
