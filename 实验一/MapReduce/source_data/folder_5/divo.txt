O. (rapper)|D. O.]], [[Classified (rapper)|Classified]], [[Dan-e-o]], [[Eternia (rapper)|Eternia]], [[D-Sisive]] and [[DL Incognito]]. < ref > {{cite web|title=Veteran Latino Hip Hop Artist, Divo Releases Inside the Vault Mixtape|url=https://latin-e.com/insidethevault/|publisher=Latin é|access-date=14 August 2017|date=27 January 2014}}{{Dead link|date=January 2023 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }} < /ref >  In 2016, Divo's fourth album ''Transatlantic Soul'' won East Coast Rap Album of the Year at the Canadian Urban Television Hip Hop Awards. < ref > {{cite news|title=CUT HIP HOP AWARDS 2016 WINNERS September 12th 2016|url=http://www.cuthiphopawards.com/cut-hiphop-awards/cut-hip-hop-awards-2016-winners-september-12th-2016/|access-date=28 August 2017|work=CUT Connex 2017 TORONTO|date=8 November 2016|language=en|archive-date=13 November 2016|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20161113181705/http://www.cuthiphopawards.com/cut-hiphop-awards/cut-hip-hop-awards-2016-winners-september-12th-2016/|url-status=dead}} < /ref > 
 
 ==Career== 
 Divo was born in Guatemala in 1980 and later moved with his family to Canada. < ref > {{cite web|title=Toronto Rapper Divo - LatinRapper.com Featured Artist|url=http://www.latinrapper.com/artistsjuly.html|publisher=LatinRapper.com|access-date=14 August 2017}} < /ref >  In 1999, Divo released his debut hip hop music album, ''Sparks of Friction''. His second album, released in 2003 was titled ''The Evolution Theory'', followed by ''This Iz Bizness'' in 2004. < ref > {{cite web|title=Divo :: Thiz Iz Bizness :: URBNET Records|url=http://www.rapreviews.com/archive/2004_04_bizness.html|publisher=RapReviews|access-date=14 August 2017}} < /ref >  In 2015, Divo released his fourth album, ''Transatlantic Soul'' which won East Coast Rap Album of the Year at the 2016 Canadian Urban Television Hip Hop Awards. ''Transatlantic Soul'' was shown in the Pan Am Path music compilation during the [[Pan American Games]], 2015. 
 
 Divo is also known for creating his own album artwork for ''Transatlantic Soul'' and Classified's Boy-Cott-In the Industry and Hitch Hikin Music. < ref > {{cite web|title=Immortal, by Dan-e-o|url=https://daneo-urbnet.bandcamp.com/album/immortal|website=Dan-e-o|publisher=[[Bandcamp]]|access-date=28 August 2017}} < /ref > < ref > {{cite web|title=Transatlantic Soul (2015), by Divo  &  GMJ|url=https://divo.bandcamp.com/album/transatlantic-soul-2015|publisher=[[Bandcamp]]|access-date=28 August 2017}} < /ref > < ref > {{cite web|title=♫ Perseverance - Divo.
