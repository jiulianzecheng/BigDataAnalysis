 A '''Twinkie''' is a golden [[sponge cake]] which has a creamy filling. It was made by James A. Dewar in 1930, and is made by [[Hostess]]. They can also be deep-fried and this was inspired by the [[deep frying|deep-fried]] Mars bar. The Twinkie was originally filled with [[banana]] cream inside but was forced to switch to a vanilla cream [[taste]] during the [[World War II|Second World War]] because the bananas were rationed. Twinkies have around 150 [[calories]] (627.6 kJ) in each bar. Twinkies have a cookbook and it has over 60 recipes. < ref > {{Cite web |url= https://www.hostesscakes.com/products/twinkies/classic/ |title=Hostess Twinkies|access-date=August 1, 2021}} < /ref >  Twinkies also have a mascot called ''[[Twinkie the Kid]]'' which is an [[Anthropomorphism|anthropomorphized]] Twinkie.
