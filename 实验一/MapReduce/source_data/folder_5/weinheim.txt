 '''Weinheim''' ({{IPA-de|ˈvaɪnhaɪm|lang|Weinheim.ogg}}) is a town with about 43,000 people living there in northwest [[Baden-Württemberg]], [[Germany]]. It is in the Rhine-Neckar Metropolitan Region, about {{convert|15|km|0|abbr=on}} north of [[Heidelberg]] and {{convert|10|km|0|abbr=on}} northeast of [[Mannheim]]. Weinheim is known as the  " Zwei-Burgen-Stadt " , the  " town of two castles " , because of the two fortresses at the edge of the [[Odenwald]] in the east.
