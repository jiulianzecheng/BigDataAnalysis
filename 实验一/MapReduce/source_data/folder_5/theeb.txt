 '''''Theeb''''' ({{lang-ar|ذيب}} ''dhīb'' {{IPAc-ar|dh|ii|b}},  " wolf " ) is a 2014 Jordanian British United Arab Emirates Qatari adventure drama thriller movie directed by [[Naji Abu Nowar]] and starring [[Jack Fox (actor)|Jack Fox]], [[Hussein Salameh Al-Sweihiyeen]], [[Hassan Mutlag Al-Maraiyeh]], [[Jacir Eid Al-Hwietat]]. It was nominated for an Academy Award in 2016.
