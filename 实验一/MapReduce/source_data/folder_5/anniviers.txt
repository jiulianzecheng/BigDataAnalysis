 '''Anniviers''' is a [[municipality]] in the [[district]] of [[Sierre (district)|Sierre]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Valais]] in [[Switzerland]]. It was formed through the merger of six municipalities in [[Val d'Anniviers]]: [[Ayer, Switzerland|Ayer]], [[Chandolin]], [[Grimentz]], [[Saint-Jean, Switzerland|Saint-Jean]], [[Saint-Luc, Switzerland|Saint-Luc]] and [[Vissoie]] merged into the new municipality of Anniviers. The merger became on 1 January 2009, creating the third largest municipality of Switzerland by area.
