 '''''Karjolsteinen''''' (The Carriage Stone) is a 1977 Norwegian drama movie directed by [[Knut Andersen]] and was based on the novel of the same name by [[Sigbjørn Hølmebakk]]. It stars [[Marit Grønhaug]], [[Sverre Anker Ousdal]], [[Erik Øksnes]], [[Grethe Ryan]], [[Arne Lindtner Næss]].
