 '''Varazze''' ({{lang-lij|Väze}}) is a ''[[comune]]'' in the [[Province of Savona]] in the [[Liguria]] region in [[Italy]]. It is about {{convert|30|km|mi}} west of [[Genoa]] and about {{convert|11|km|mi|0}} northeast of [[Savona]] in the [[Riviera di Ponente]].
