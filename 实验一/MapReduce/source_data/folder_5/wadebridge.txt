 '''Wadebridge''' is a town on the A39 road in [[Cornwall]], England. In 2001 there were 6,351 people living in Wadebridge. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.ons.gov.uk/help/localstatistics|title=Local statistics - Office for National Statistics|website=www.ons.gov.uk}} < /ref >  The town is on the south side of the River Camel a few miles inland from the north coast of Cornwall. It is well known for the road bridge across the river which was built to replace the dangerous ford in the Middle Ages. 
 
 The showground for the Royal Cornwall Show is near Wadebridge.
