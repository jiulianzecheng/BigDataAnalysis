 '''Filicide''' is the act of a [[parent]] [[Murder|killing]] his or her [[son]] or [[daughter]]. The word ''filicide'' comes from the [[Latin]] words ''filius'' meaning  " son "  or ''filia'' meaning  " daughter "  and the [[suffix]] ''-cide'' meaning  " to kill, murder or cause death " .
