 '''Boxmeer''' is a former municipality in the [[Netherlands|Dutch]] [[Provinces of the Netherlands|province]] of [[North Brabant]]. In 2021, about 29,600 people lived there.  
 
 Boxmeer has been a part of [[Land van Cuijk]] since 2022.
