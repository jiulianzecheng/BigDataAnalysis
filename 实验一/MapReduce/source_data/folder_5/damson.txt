 A '''damson''' is a kind of [[plum]]. Like the plum, it comes from the [[Rosaceae]] [[family (biology)|family]]. Originally, the [[fruit]] comes from the [[Mediterranean]]. Damsons are thought to be members of the 'European Plum' family, which also includes [[greengage]], [[mirabelle]], [[quetsche]], and [[Italian prune plums]]. The damson is generally less sweet than other kinds of plum and is used in jams, jellies, and liqueurs.
