 A '''quesadilla''' is a typical [[Mexico|Mexican]] dish. It is a [[tortilla]], with added [[cheese]]. The quesadilla is then folded, and either baked or fried. In the area around [[Mexico City]], other [[Ingredient|ingredients]] such as [[meat]] or [[Mushroom|mushrooms]] are added as well.
