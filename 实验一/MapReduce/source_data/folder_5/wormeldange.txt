 '''Wormeldange''' ({{lang-lb|Wuermeldeng}}, {{lang-de|Wormeldingen}}) is a [[Communes of Luxembourg|commune]] in [[Luxembourg]]. It is part of the [[Grevenmacher (canton)|canton of Grevenmacher]]. 
 
 As of 2019, 2,921 people live here.
