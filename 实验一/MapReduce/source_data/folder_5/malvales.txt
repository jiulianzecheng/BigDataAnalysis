 '''Malvales''' is the name of an order of [[flowering plant]]s. As circumscribed by [[Angiosperm Phylogeny Group|APG II]]-system, it includes about 6000 [[species]] within nine families. The order is placed in the [[eurosids II]], which are part of the [[eudicot]]s.
