 '''DWAD''' (1098 AM) '''Now Radio''' is a [[radio]] station in the [[Philippines]]. [[Crusaders Broadcasting System]] owns DWAD.  Crusaders Broadcasting System is a subsidiary of [[Audiovisual Communicators]]. Its studio is at 304 Shaw Boulevard, [[Mandaluyong City]] and its transmitter is at 209 E. De la Paz Street, [[Mandaluyong City]]. < ref > {{Cite web |url=https://www.dwad1098.com/ |title=DWAD 1098 kHz test broadcast |access-date=2021-04-06 |archive-date=2021-04-24 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210424192759/https://dwad1098.com/ |url-status=dead }} < /ref > 
 
 ==History== 
 On March 5, 2021 DWAD's studio moved to Dulmao Sports Center in Mandaluyong. It was formerly at 209 E. Dela paz street, Mandaluyong.
