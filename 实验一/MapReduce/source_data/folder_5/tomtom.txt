 '''TomTom N.V.''' is a [[Netherlands|Dutch]] company that provides devices for car and personal [[Global Positioning System|GPS]] [[navigation]]. Its production is famous and actively used in [[Europe]], [[North America]] and some [[Asia|Asian]] countries. < ref > {{Cite web|title= " TomTom Annual Report and Accounts 2018 "  (PDF). TomTom NV. 2018. Retrieved 2 August 2018.|url=https://corporate.tomtom.com/static-files/76759d8b-132e-4dc9-a9ce-9472967db1f6}} < /ref >   
 
 == History == 
 TomTom was founded in 1991.{{Citation needed|date=August 2022}} 
 
 In September 2005, TomTom bought Datafactory AG. < ref > {{Cite web|title=TomTom to acquire Datafactory|url=https://www.telecompaper.com/news/tomtom-to-acquire-datafactory--485035|access-date=2021-04-01|website=www.telecompaper.com|language=en}} < /ref > 
 
 In 2006, TomTom Traffic was created after buying Applied Generics. < ref > {{Cite web|title=TomTom navigates way to a takeover of Applied Generics|url=https://www.scotsman.com/business/tomtom-navigates-way-takeover-applied-generics-2454641|access-date=2021-04-01|website=www.scotsman.com|language=en}} < /ref > 
 
 In 2008, the company owned Tele Atlas. < ref > {{Cite news|date=2009-02-24|title=TomTom CEO has no regrets about Tele Atlas buy|language=en|work=Reuters|url=https://www.reuters.com/article/idUSWEA868520090224|access-date=2021-04-01}} < /ref > 
 
 In 2012, TomTom was the main to provide mapping data for [[Apple Inc.|Apple's]] iOS 6 Maps. < ref > {{Cite web|date=2012-06-12|title=TomTom to become Apple phone default satnav program|url=http://www.theguardian.com/technology/2012/jun/12/tomtom-apple-phone-satnav-program|access-date=2021-04-01|website=the Guardian|language=en}} < /ref > 
 
 In 2014, the company was a partner with [[Volkswagen]] Group during the research on Highly Automated Driving (HAD) systems. < ref > {{Cite web|date=2018-08-07|title=TomTom and Volkswagen partner to shape the future of Highly Automated Driving (AMS:TOMTOM)|url=http://corporate.tomtom.com/releasedetail.cfm?releaseid=874192|access-date=2021-04-01|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180807001511/http://corporate.tomtom.com/releasedetail.cfm?releaseid=874192 |archive-date=2018-08-07 }} < /ref >  TomTom N.V. provided Volkswagen Group, [[Mercedes-Benz|Mercedes]], [[Toyota]] and other big carmakers with their navigation devices. < ref > {{Cite news|date=12 February 2015|title=TomTom signs deal to supply satnavs in Volkswagen cars|newspaper=Financial Times|url=https://www.ft.com/content/1d10c730-b215-11e4-80af-00144feab7de|access-date=2021-04-01}} < /ref > 
 
 In 2015, [[Uber]] started using TomTom's maps and traffic data in 300 cities. < ref > {{Cite news|last=Proper|first=Levien de Feijter and Ellen|date=2015-11-12|title=Uber Strikes Digital Maps Deal With TomTom|language=en-US|work=Wall Street Journal|url=https://www.wsj.com/articles/uber-strikes-digital-maps-deal-with-tomtom-1447334540|access-date=2021-04-01|issn=0099-9660}} < /ref > 
 
 In 2020, [[Huawei]] replaced [[Google Maps]] with TomTom's maps on their [[Smartphone|smartphones]]. < ref > {{Cite news|date=2020-01-17|title=TomTom closes deal with Huawei for use of maps and services: spokesman|language=en|work=Reuters|url=https://www.reuters.com/article/us-tomtom-huawei-tech-idUSKBN1ZG1SW|access-date=2021-04-01}} < /ref > 
 
 == Structure == 
 TomTom is oriented on 2 main market segments: location technology and [[consumer]]. < ref name= " :0 " > {{Cite web|title=staff (18 March 2019).  " Annual report  &  accounts 2019 " . TomTom corporate. Retrieved 22 January 2021.|url=https://corporate.tomtom.com/static-files/ae9c9c28-5aad-47e1-9b4b-3ee1ad0a8757}} < /ref > 
 
 Location technology refers to the company's automotive and enterprise businesses. The company provides cars, tech specialists, some companies and government bodies with maps and navigation [[software]]. < ref name= " :0 "  / > 
 
 The consumer segment is related to selling portable, personal satellite navigation devices. < ref > {{Cite news|date=2020-07-15|title=UPDATE 1-TomTom posts 41% slump in revenue but sees signs of recovery|language=en|work=Reuters|url=https://www.reuters.com/article/tomtom-results-idUSL5N2EM0UL|access-date=2021-04-01}} < /ref >  Recently TomTom offered software [[Application software|applications]] with digital maplinked services instead of devices. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Bloomberg Technology - Bloomberg|url=https://www.bloomberg.com/technology|access-date=2021-04-01|website=Bloomberg.com|language=en}} < /ref > 
 
 == Products and services == 
 TomTom N.V. offers three types of products: maps, connected services and (navigation) software. The company sells Navigation devices (PNDs) and TomTom GO navigation apps. < ref > {{Cite web|title=TOMTOM (TOM2.AS) company profile  &  facts – Yahoo Finance|url=https://uk.finance.yahoo.com/quote/TOM2.AS/profile/|access-date=2021-04-01|website=uk.finance.yahoo.com|language=en-GB}} < /ref >   
 
 A TomTom's traffic monitoring service uses different sources to give traffic information by combining data from: 
 
 * traditional sources: governmental/third-party data such as induction loops in the roads, cameras and traffic surveillance 
 * new sources: traffic flow of millions of anonymous mobile phone users. < ref > {{Cite web|date=2021-03-22|title=TomTom {{!}} Maps and Services Shop|url=https://www.tomtom.com/en_gb/sat-nav/maps-services/|access-date=2021-04-01|website=Tomtom|language=en}} < /ref >
