 '''Silence''' means no [[sound]] that can be heard. It can also mean no communication. 
 
 A [[moment of silence]] is a common way to remember [[tragedy|tragedies]]. 
 
 The right to silence is a legal protection for people being interrogated by [[police]] in some countries.
