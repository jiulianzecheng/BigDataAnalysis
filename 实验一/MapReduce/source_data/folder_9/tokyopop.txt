 '''Tokyopop''', formerly known as '''Mixx''', is an [[United States|American]] [[distributing|distributor]], [[Liscense|licensor]] and [[publishing|publisher]]. They work with [[anime]], [[manga]], [[manhwa]] and [[Original English-language manga]] in [[English language|English]], [[German language|German]], and [[Japanese language|Japanese]]. Tokyopop has its headquarters in [[Los Angeles, California|Los Angeles]], [[California]]. It has branches in the [[United Kingdom]] and [[Germany]]. Tokyopop products are available internationally.
