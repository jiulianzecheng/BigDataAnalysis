 '''Neusorg''' is a [[Municipalities of Germany|municipality]] in [[Tirschenreuth (district)|Tirschenreuth]] in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. The town itself is the seat of [[Neusorg federation|Neusorg]] [[Municipal association (Germany)|municipal federation]]. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 Neusorg has nine districts: Neusorg, [[Riglasreuth]], [[Schwarzenreuth]], [[Stockau]], [[Stöcken, Bavaria|Stöcken]], [[Wäsch]], [[Weihermühle]], and [[Wernersreuth]]. < ref > {{cite web|title=Neusorg|url=https://www.bayerische-landesbibliothek-online.de/orte/ortssuche_action.html?anzeige=voll & modus=automat & tempus=+20111108/152711 & attr=OBJ & val=1092|website=bayerische-landesbibliothek-online.de|publisher=Bayerische Landesbibliothek Online|language=de}} < /ref >
