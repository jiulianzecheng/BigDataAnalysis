 '''Bovelles''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is in [[Hauts-de-France]] in the [[Somme]] [[Departments of France|department]] in north [[France]]. In 2018, 431 people lived there. 
 
 == Geography == 
 The commune is about {{Convert|5|mi|km|0}} west of [[Amiens]].
