 '''Dendrobium''' is one of the largest [[genus]] of [[orchid]]s with about 1000 [[species]] being known. Being such a large group creates much [[diversity]]. Dendrobiums are found only in the [[Eastern Hemisphere]] and range from [[Australia]], throughout the [[South Pacific]] and [[Philippines]], [[Southeast Asia]], and [[India]], and a small representation in [[Japan]].
