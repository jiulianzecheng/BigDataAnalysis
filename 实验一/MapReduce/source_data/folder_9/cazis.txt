 '''Cazis''' ({{lang-rm|Tgazas}}) is a [[municipality]] of the [[district]] of [[Hinterrhein (district)|Hinterrhein]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Graubünden]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 On 1 January 2010, the former municipalities of [[Portein]], [[Präz]], [[Sarn, Switzerland|Sarn]] and [[Tartar, Switzerland|Tartar]] merged into the municipality of Cazis.
