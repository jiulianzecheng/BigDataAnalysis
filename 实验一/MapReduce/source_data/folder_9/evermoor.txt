 ==Plot== 
 The series is about Tara Crossley. She is an [[Americans|American]] [[teen]] who moves from the [[United States]] to an [[England|English]] [[village]] called Evermoor. As her mother and father unpack at their new home, Tara, her brother, and her British [[sibling|step-siblings]] adjust to their new life. [[Danger]], [[mystery]] and [[wikt:intrigue|intrigue]] come along with the [[adventure]]. 
 
 ==Cast and characters==

 * George Sear as Seb Crossley 
 * [[Georgia Lock]] as Bella Crossley  
 * Georgie Farmer as Jake Crossley   
 * Jordan Loughran as Sorsha Doyle 
 * [[Naomi Sequeira]] as Tara Crossley 
 
 ==Development== 
 On 17 March 2014, the series was announced. < ref name= " variety " > {{cite web|url=https://variety.com/2014/tv/news/disney-greenlights-england-set-spooky-house-tale-evermoor-1201136103/|title=Disney Greenlights England-set Spooky House Tale ‘Evermoor’|last=Barraclough|first=Leo|publisher=Variety|date=17 March 2014|accessdate=8 October 2014}} < /ref >  [[Filming]] took place in the [[spring]] of 2014 at Arley Hall in [[Cheshire]]. < ref name= " variety " / >  The series has been described as  " a hugely exciting project for the team here in EMEA with a gripping story that I’m sure will captivate our global Disney Channel audience. The movie’s intriguing cast of characters also gives us a great opportunity to find exciting new talent” by David Levine. < ref name= " variety " / >  Lime Pictures produced the [[television special|special]], which was created by Diane Whitley and Tim Compton. The [[executive producers]] was Rebecca Hodgson. The [[producer]] was Compton. < ref name= " deadline " > {{cite web|url=http://deadline.com/2014/03/disney-channels-evermoor-to-shoot-in-uk-as-part-of-rising-location-destination-trend-700116/|title=Disney Channel’s ‘Evermoor’ To Shoot In UK As Part Of Rising Location Destination Trend|last=Tartaglione|first=Nancy|date=17 March 2014|publisher=Deadline|accessdate=8 October 2014}} < /ref >  It was a co-production between Disney U.S. and the Walt Disney Company EMEA. < ref name= " deadline " / >
