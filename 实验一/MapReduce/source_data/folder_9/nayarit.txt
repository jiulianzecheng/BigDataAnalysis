 '''Nayarit''' is a middle sized [[Mexico|Mexican]] state. It is on the West coast of Mexico (on the [[Pacific Ocean]] shore). 
 Native people from Nayarit are called Nayaritas (singular: Nayarita). 
 
 The Nayarit capital is [[Tepic]].
