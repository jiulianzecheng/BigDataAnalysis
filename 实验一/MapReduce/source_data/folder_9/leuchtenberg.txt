 '''Leuchtenberg''' is a [[Municipalities of Germany|municipality]] in [[Neustadt an der Waldnaab (district)|Neustadt an der Waldnaab]] in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]], naturally a suburb of nearby [[Weiden in der Oberpfalz]], and a larger historical region in the [[Holy Roman Empire]] governed by the [[Landgraves of Leuchtenberg]].
