 '''Misrata''' {{IPAc-en|m|ɪ|z|ˈ|r|ɑː|t|ə}} ({{lang-ar|مصراتة}} '''{{transl|ar|UNGEGN|Mişrātah}}''', [[Libyan Arabic]]: {{IPA-ar|məsˤˈrˤɑːtæ|IPA}}), also known as '''Misurata''' or '''Misratah''', < ref > {{Cite web|url=http://www.geographic.org/geographic_names/name_non-roman_misrata_libya.html |title=مصراتة: Libya |work=Geographical Names |accessdate=2011-02-26}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite web|url=http://www.geographic.org/geographic_names/name.php?uni=-4477557 & fid=3755 & c=libya |title=Mişrātah: Libya |format= |work=Geographical Names |accessdate=2011-02-26}} < /ref >  is a city in northwestern [[Libya]].  It is in the [[Misrata District]]. 
 
 With a population of 281,000, Misrata is the [[:en:List of cities in Libya|third largest city]] in Libya, after [[Tripoli]] and [[Benghazi]].  
 
 [[Misrata University]] is in the city.
