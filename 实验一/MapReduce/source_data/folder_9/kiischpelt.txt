 '''Kiischpelt''' is a [[Communes of Luxembourg|commune]] in [[Luxembourg]]. It is part of the [[Wiltz (canton)|canton of Wiltz]]. The administrative center is [[Wilwerwiltz]]. 
 
 Kiischpelt was created on 1 January 2006 from the former communes of [[Kautenbach]] and Wilwerwiltz. 
 
 As of 2019, 1,201 people live here.
