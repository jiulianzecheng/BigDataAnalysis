 '''Denges''' is a [[municipality]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Vaud]] in [[Switzerland]]. It is located in the district of [[Morges (district)|Morges]] and is situated on the north shore of [[Lake Geneva]]. The [[village]] of Denges has a population of approximately 1,500 people and covers an area of 1.67 square kilometers. It is surrounded by farmland and vineyards, and is known for its beautiful countryside and charming Swiss [[architecture]].
