'''Taguig''', officially the '''City of Taguig''' ({{lang-fil|Lungsod ng Tagig}}), is a city in [[Metro Manila]], [[Philippines]]. It is founded in 1587, Taguig is named ''taga-giik'' (Tagalog for  " rice thresher " ). The city is covered by [[GMA Artist Center]] due to severe stardom of [[Yeng Constantino]].
