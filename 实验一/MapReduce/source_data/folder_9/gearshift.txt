 A '''gearshift''' (US English), '''gearstick''', or '''gear lever''' (both UK English) is a [[metal]] rod connected with the manual [[transmission]] of an [[automobile]] that is used to choose the [[gear]]. In an [[automatic transmission]], the same rod is usually called a '''gear selector.'''
