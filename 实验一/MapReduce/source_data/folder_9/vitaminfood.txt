 == History == 
 Vitaminfood is a [[Dutch people|Dutch]] complete [[nutrition]] company encompassing a variety of meal replacement < ref > {{cite journal|date=24 October 2015|title=Meal Replacement|url=http://www.inspection.gc.ca/food/labelling/food-labelling-for-industry/special-dietary-use/eng/1393627685223/1393637610720?chap=5|journal=Canadian Food Inspection Agency|access-date=3 July 2022}} < /ref >  solutions and sports supplements, produced by Vitaminfood Company Limited. Founded in November 2016 by Bakir and Funda Ikanovic, this nutritional company set out to provide nutritionally complete diets. 
 Funda Ikanovic (33) previously worked in the financial world as an insurance agent after her [degree] in marketing. Bakir Ikanovic (40) completed a bachelor's degree in real estate and has a company for cleaning of large ships. Together they founded the meal replacement company, Vitaminfood in November 2016. 
 
 Meal replacements < ref > {{cite web|last1=Julian|first1=Hearn|title=A Brief History of Meal Replacements|url=https://eu.huel.com/blogs/news/18061259-a-brief-history-of-meal-replacements|access-date=3 July 2022|website=Huel.com}} < /ref >  are [[Liquid diet|liquid diets]] < ref > {{cite web|last1=Hannah|first1=Caleb|date=February 9, 2018|title= " Could This Liquid Replace Food? " |url=http://www.popsci.com/science/article/2013-07/could-liquid-replace-food|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170904155233/http:/www.popsci.com/science/article/2013-07/could-liquid-replace-food|archive-date=September 4, 2017|publisher=Popular Science}} < /ref > < ref > {{cite web|title=Diet - full liquid|url=https://medlineplus.gov/ency/patientinstructions/000206.htm|website=nlm.nih.gov|publisher=MedlinePlus Medical Encyclopedia}} < /ref >  in form of dissolved nutrient solutions taken as an alternative to solid meals. 
 " If war was to break out, they'd be fine, "  said Bakir in a media interview because of how much [[Powder|powder nutrition]] they have at their place. The meal replacements have been certified as safe for human consumption and meet the required nutritional standards.

 === Commercial venture === 
 The manufacturing plant is in [[Veenendaal]], one of the oldest cities in the [[Netherlands]]. The Vitaminfood meal replacement bags once produced are stored in Barendrecht, near Rotterdam. In the early days of the company, after conception, Bakir and Funda handled most of the orders. Writing tickets, packaging orders, shipping orders and customer relationship management. They operated majorly from their home working the afternoons and in shifts. The powder production was outsourced but closely monitored. 
 
 Initially, the company sold 100 bags a day on average which equals 300 meals. The number has definitely grown over time factoring in their expansion and international market. < ref > {{cite news|date=9 January 2021|title=These are the Top Nutrition Companies in The Netherlands (2021)|work=Toast Fried|url=https://toastfried.com/these-are-the-top-nutrition-companies-in-the-netherlands-2021/|accessdate=26 May 2023}} < /ref >
