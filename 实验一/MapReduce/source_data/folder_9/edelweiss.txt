 '''Edelweiss''' is a well-known [[Europe]] [[mountain]] [[flower]]. 
 
 == Description == 
 [[Leaf|Leaves]] and [[flower]]s are covered with [[white]] hairs and look [[wikt:woolly|woolly]]. Each Edelweiss bloom has [[five]] to [[six]] small [[yellow]] flower heads surrounded by leaflets in a [[star]] shape. The flowers are in bloom between [[July]] and [[September]]. 
 
 == Range == 
 The Edelweiss prefers [[Rock (geology)|rocky]] [[limestone]] places. It has been used [[tradition]]ally in folk [[medicine]].
