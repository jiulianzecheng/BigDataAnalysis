 '''''Lythraria''''' is a [[genus]] of [[leaf beetle]]s. It was first described in 1897 by [[Ernest Marie Louis Bedel]]. It contains a single species: ''Lythraria salicariae''. < ref name= " usda " > {{cite web|url=http://www.sel.barc.usda.gov/Coleoptera/fleabeetles/327.htm|title=Lythraria Bedel|work=Guide to Palearctic Flea Beetle Genera|publisher=United States Government, Department of Agriculture|accessdate=16 February 2014|archive-date=27 February 2013|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130227132305/http://www.sel.barc.usda.gov/Coleoptera/fleabeetles/327.htm|url-status=dead}} < /ref > 
 
 This species lives is found in [[Europe]], the [[Caucasus]], across northern [[Asia]] to [[Korea]] and [[Japan]]. < ref name= " usda " / > < ref > {{cite web|url= https://data.nbn.org.uk/Taxa/NBNSYS0000011281|title=''Lythraria salicariae'' (Paykull, 1800)|publisher=NBN Gateway}} < /ref >
