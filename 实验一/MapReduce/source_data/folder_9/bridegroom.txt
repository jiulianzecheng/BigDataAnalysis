 '''''Bridegroom''''' (full title: '''''Bridegroom: A Love Story, Unequaled''''') is a 2013 American [[documentary|documentary movie]]. The movie is about two [[gay]] men. It was [[movie director|directed]] and [[movie producer|produced]] by Linda Bloodworth-Thomason. < ref name=post > {{cite news|title=An American Love Story|url=http://www.huffingtonpost.com/shane-bitney-crone/an-american-love-story_b_1631465.html|work=The Huffington Post|date=June 27, 2012}} < /ref >  ''Bridegroom'' [[wikt:premiere|premiered]] at the 2013 [[Tribeca Film Festival]] on April 23, 2013. < ref > {{cite web|title=Here Are the 22 Films Premiering in the Viewpoints Section of the 2013 Festival|url=http://www.tribecafilm.com/news-features/2013_Tribeca_Film_Festival_Viewpoints.html|publisher=Tribeca Film Festival|access-date=2014-10-29|archive-date=2013-03-08|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130308013853/http://www.tribecafilm.com/news-features/2013_Tribeca_Film_Festival_Viewpoints.html|url-status=dead}} < /ref >  The movie gained a lot of [[press release|press coverage]]. This was because former [[US president|president]] [[Bill Clinton]] introduced the movie at the festival. < ref > [http://www.huffingtonpost.com/2013/04/24/bill-clinton-bridegroom-tribeca-_n_3146636.html  " Bill Clinton Introduces 'Bridegroom,' Shane Bitney Crone's Documentary, At Tribeca Film Festival " ]. ''[[Huffington Post]]'', April 24, 2013. < /ref >

 == About the movie == 
 ''Bridegroom'' talks about the story of Shane Bitney Crone and his [[same-sex marriage|same-sex partner]] Thomas Lee  " Tom "  Bridegroom who died in a [[wikt:tragic|tragic]] accident. After Bridegroom's death, Crone is not given any legal protection. The movie tells the story of their 6-year-long [[relationship]]. It also talks about Crone's struggles after Bridegroom's death. Crone faces Bridegroom's family not allowing Crone to attend the [[funeral]] of his life partner. < ref name= " imdb " > {{IMDb title |2357788}} < /ref >
