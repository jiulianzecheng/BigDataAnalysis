'''Marksistskaya''' is a [[Railway station|station]] of [[Moscow]] metro, [[Kalininskaya Line]]. It is situated between Tretyakonskaya and Ploshad Il'icha stations. It Was opened on 30 December 1979. It is connected with two other stations - Taganskaya-koltsevaya and Taganskaya-Radialnaya. The station is about {{convert|60|m}} under the ground. It has only one exit - to Marksistskaya Street (or Taganskaya, if you cross Marksistskaya street).
