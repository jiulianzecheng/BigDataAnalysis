'''Supertec''' was a [[Formula One]] [[engine]] supplier in 1999 and 2000. Supertec supplied engines that were designed by [[Renault F1]]. They took engines built by [[Mecachrome]] from the [[1998 Formula One season|1998]] season, and updated them for 1999 and 2000.
