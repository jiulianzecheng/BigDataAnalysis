 '''Rangpo''' is a town in East Sikkim in the Indian state of [[Sikkim]]. The town borders [[West Bengal]]. It is on the [[Teesta river]]. All vehicles entering Sikkim have to stop here. 
 
 == Industrialization == 
   
 Rangpo is now the center for almost all the pharmaceutical companies. The town is one of the fastest growing towns in the state.
