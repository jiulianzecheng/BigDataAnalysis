 '''Morrinsville''' is a provincial [[town]] in the [[Waikato]] region of [[New Zealand]]'s [[North Island]], with a population of 7,000 in the 2013 Census The town is located at the northern base of the Pakaroa Range, and on the south-western fringe of the [[Hauraki Plains]].  Morrinsville is around 33 kilometres east of [[Hamilton, New Zealand|Hamilton]]. < ref name= " MorrinsvilleHistory_1 " > {{cite web|url=http://www.teara.govt.nz/en/1966/morrinsville/|title=An Encyclopaedia of New Zealand|year=1966|access-date=8 February 2016}} < /ref >
