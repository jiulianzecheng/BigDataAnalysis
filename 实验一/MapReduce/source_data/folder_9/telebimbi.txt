 '''Telebimbi''' is a Canadian [[Category B services|Category B-exempt]] [[Italian language]] [[specialty channel]] owned by [[TLN (TV channel)|TLN Media Group]]. Telebimbi broadcasts for children in addition to select family-oriented programming. 
 
 ==Programming== 
 Telebimbi broadcasts programming for kids of all ages, as well as family-oriented programming.
