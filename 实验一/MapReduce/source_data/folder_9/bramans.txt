 '''Bramans''' is a former [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is in [[Auvergne-Rhône-Alpes]] in the [[Savoie]] [[Departments of France|department]] in southeast [[France]]. On 1 January 2017, it was merged into the new commune of [[Val-Cenis]]. < ref > [https://www.legifrance.gouv.fr/eli/arrete/2016/8/8/INTB1623405A/jo/texte Arrêté préfectoral] 8 August 2016 {{in lang|fr}} < /ref >
