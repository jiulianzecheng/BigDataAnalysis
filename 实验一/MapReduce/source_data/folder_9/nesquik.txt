 '''Nesquik''' (previously called '''Nestle Quik''') is a brand of products made by the [[Switzerland|Swiss]] [[company]] [[Nestlé]]. In 1948 Nestlé launched a drink mix for [[flavored milk|chocolate-flavored milk]]. It was called Nestle Quik in the [[United States]]. It was released in [[Europe]] during the 1950s as '''Nesquik'''. < ref > {{cite web|title=About Nesquik|publisher=Nestle|url=https://www.nestle-family.com/en/brand/nesquik|access-date=May 15, 2021}} < /ref >   
 
 Beginning in 1999, the brand was called '''Nesquik''' all over the world. The Nesquik name appears on powdered mixes for flavored milk, ready-to-drink products, [[hot chocolate]] mix and many other products. 
 
 Nesquik is available in [[strawberry]] and [[chocolate]] flavors.
