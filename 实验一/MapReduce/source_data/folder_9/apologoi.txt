An '''apologoi''' is a [[Greek language|Greek]] term which means a story inside a story. It comes from the word ''apologos'' which means  " story " . A famous example of an apologoi is in books 9 to 12  of [[Homer]]'s [[epic poem]], the ''[[Odyssey]]''.
