 '''Innovecs''' is an international software development company founded by Alex Lutsky in 2011. < ref > {{Cite web|last=Lutskiy|first=Alex|title=Council Post: Investing In Spirit: Why Build Community Rather Than Company?|url=https://www.forbes.com/sites/forbestechcouncil/2020/10/16/investing-in-spirit-why-build-community-rather-than-company/|access-date=2021-06-07|website=Forbes|language=en}} < /ref >  The company has offices in the [[United States]], [[Great Britain]], [[Australia]], [[Israel]] and [[Ukraine]]. R & D centers are located in Ukraine. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Innovecs Completes International Leadership Team|url=https://innovecs.com/blog/innovecs-completes-international-leadership-team/|access-date=2021-06-07|website=Innovecs|language=en-US}} < /ref > 
 
 == History == 
 Innovecs was founded in September 2011. < ref > {{Cite web|title=IT-ринок України: хто працює в найдинамічнішій галузі країни|url=https://daily.rbc.ua/ukr/show/it-rynok-ukrainy-rabotaet-samoy-dinamichno-1614070059.html|access-date=2021-06-07|website=РБК-Украина|language=ru}} < /ref > 
 
 In 2017-2020, Innovecs was included in the Inc. rating. 5000. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Check out Innovecs on Inc.com|url=https://www.inc.com/profile/innovecs|access-date=2021-06-07|website=Inc.com|language=en}} < /ref >  Also, the company is included in the IAOP rating 'The Global Outsourcing 100 - a list of the world's best outsourcing companies. < ref > {{Cite web|date=2020-02-18|title=Вышел рейтинг лучших аутсорсеров. 11 из них работают в Украине|url=https://tech.liga.net/technology/novosti/vyshel-reyting-luchshih-autsorserov-11-iz-nih-rabotayut-v-ukraine|access-date=2021-06-07|website=LIGA|language=ru}} < /ref > 
 
 The company currently has more than 800 employees in Ukraine, the United States and Europe. 
 
 In 2016-2019, Innovecs achieved 186% growth, serving more than 100 customers. Innovecs' core expertise is the development of B2B software solutions with a focus on software engineering. 
 
 In 2015, Innovecs launched a research and development center based on blockchain technology in Kyiv. The center was founded with the participation of the Bitcoin Foundation Ukraine. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Ukraine collaborates with US Company to open Blockchain R & D Center in Kiev|url=https://cointelegraph.com/news/ukraine-collaborates-with-us-company-to-open-blockchain-rd-center-in-kiev|access-date=2021-06-07|website=Cointelegraph|language=en}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite web|last=News|first=Ukraine Digital|date=2015-09-29|title=Software developer Innovecs opens internationally-oriented Blockchain R & D center in Kiev {{!}}|url=https://www.uadn.net/2015/09/30/software-developer-innovecs-opens-internationally-oriented-blockchain-rd-center-in-kiev/|access-date=2021-06-07|language=en-US}} < /ref > 
 
 In 2018, Innovecs acquired the outsourcing division of the Ukrainian mobile game development company Tatem Games as part of a strategic decision to implement the direction of video game development. Now Innovecs Gaming is a separate division designed to promote the company in the international field of video game outsourcing. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Innovecs|url=https://www.igamingsuppliers.com/vendor/innovecs/|access-date=2021-06-07|website=www.igamingsuppliers.com}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite web|title=Ukrainian IT outsourcing company Innovecs has aquired game division Tatem Games|url=https://inventure.com.ua/en/news/ukraine/ukrainian-it-outsourcing-company-innovecs-has-aquired-game-division-tatem-games|access-date=2021-06-07|website=inventure.com.ua|language=en}} < /ref > 
 
 == Public initiatives and projects == 
 In August 2018, Innovecs opened InnoHub, a 600 m2 multimedia environment for educational activities in business and technology. < ref > {{Cite web|last=Vector|date=2018-08-23|title=Innovecs открыл в Киеве свой хаб. Зачем компании площадка для мероприятий|url=https://vctr.media/innovecs-innohub-5306/|access-date=2021-06-07|website=Vector|language=ru-RU}} < /ref > 
 
 In June 2019, Innovecs launches InnoCamp, an education development program for IT professionals based on international business practices. InnoCamp experts and managers follow the EduScrum methodology. < ref > {{Cite web|last=Agency|first=Volta|title=IT Ukraine Association - QA Automation_InnoCamp|url=https://itukraine.org.ua/en/qa-automation-innocamp.html|access-date=2021-06-07|website=itukraine.org.ua|archive-date=2021-06-07|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210607131733/https://itukraine.org.ua/en/qa-automation-innocamp.html|url-status=dead}} < /ref > 
 
 In 2020, Innovecs created the Innovecs [[COVID-19 pandemic|COVID-19]] fund in the amount of UAH 1,500,000 (one and a half million). The main priority of the fund is assistance in the fight against coronavirus in Ukraine. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Украинский бизнес против коронавируса: кто и чем помогает|url=https://24tv.ua/ru/ukrainskij_biznes_protiv_koronavirusa_kto_i_chem_pomogaet_n1309658|access-date=2021-06-07|website=24 Канал|language=ru}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite web|title=Innovecs foundation will install new oxygen highway in clinical hospital in Kyiv|url=https://chamber.ua/news/innovecs-foundation-will-install-new-oxygen-highway-in-clinical-hospital-in-kyiv/|access-date=2021-06-07|website=The American Chamber of Commerce in Ukraine|language=en|archive-date=2021-06-07|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210607132015/https://chamber.ua/news/innovecs-foundation-will-install-new-oxygen-highway-in-clinical-hospital-in-kyiv/|url-status=dead}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite web|title=З 24 апаратів ШВЛ лише 2 зможуть рятувати хворих на коронавірус - аудит лікарні в Києві|url=http://www.pravda.com.ua/news/2020/04/2/7246228/|access-date=2021-06-07|website=Українська правда|language=uk}} < /ref >
