 '''Kitzingen''' ({{IPA-de|ˈkɪt͡sɪŋən|lang|De-Kitzingen.ogg}}) is a town in [[Bavaria]], capital of [[Kitzingen (district)|Kitzingen]]. It is part of the [[Franconia]] geographical region and has around 21,000 residents. Surrounded by [[vineyard]]s, Kitzingen County is the largest [[Winemaking|wine producer]] in [[Bavaria]]. It is said to be [[Franconia]]'s [[wine]] trade center.
