 '''Malolos''', officially the '''City of Malolos''' ({{lang-fil|Lungsod ng Malolos}}), is a city in the [[Philippines]]. It is the capital of the [[Provinces of the Philippines|province]] of [[Bulacan]]. According to the 2020 census, 261,189 people lived there.{{PH census|current}}  
 
 Malolos was the site of the [[Constitutional convention (political meeting)|constitutional convention]] of 1898, known as the Malolos Convention, that led to the establishment of the [[First Philippine Republic]].
