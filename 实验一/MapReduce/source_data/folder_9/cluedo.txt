'''''Cluedo''''' (also known as '''''Clue''''') is a [[board game]]. It is for three to six people to play. It started being sold in the United Kingdom in 1949. < ref > L. A. Petrosjan, V. V. Mazalov (2002).  " Game Theory and Applications, Volume 8 " . p. 26. Nova Publishers < /ref >  Many different versions of the game have been made. It is owned and sold by [[Hasbro]], an American toy company. 
 
 The game is about trying to find how a [[murder]] happened. Players do this by moving around the board, and getting clues from other players. The board is shaped like a [[mansion]], with many rooms to get clues in. A player wins by saying where the murder happened, what weapon it was done with, and who did it.
