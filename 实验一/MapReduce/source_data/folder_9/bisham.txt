 '''Bisham''' (said  " Bissam " ) is a village in [[Windsor and Maidenhead]], [[Berkshire]], England, UK. It is on [[River Thames]]. Across the river is [[Marlow, Buckinghamshire|Marlow]] in [[Buckinghamshire]]. In 2001 there were 1,149 people living in Bisham. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.ons.gov.uk/help/localstatistics|title=Local statistics - Office for National Statistics|website=www.ons.gov.uk}} < /ref >
