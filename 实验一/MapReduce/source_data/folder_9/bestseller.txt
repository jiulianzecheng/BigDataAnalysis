A '''bestseller''' is a [[book]] or other media that is known for their top selling status, meaning they sold many copies. They are often seen on bestselling lists by [[newspaper]]s, [[magazine]]s, and [[bookstore]] chains. An author may also be called a bestseller if their work are seen on a bestseller list many times.  Well-known bestseller lists in the U.S. are published by [[Publishers Weekly lists of bestselling novels in the United States|''Publishers Weekly'']], ''[[USA Today]]'',  ''[[The New York Times]]'' and ''[[The Washington Post]]''. < ref > {{cite book |author=Diamond, Edwin |date=1995 |title=Behind the Times: Inside the New New York Times |url=https://books.google.com/books?id=LAfZ-Y13obwC & pg=PA364 |page=364|isbn=9780226144726 }} < /ref >
