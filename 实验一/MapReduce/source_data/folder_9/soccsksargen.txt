 '''Soccsksargen''' (sometimes stylised as SOCCSKSARGEN) is an [[Regions of the Philippines|administrative region]] of the [[Philippines]]. It is also known as '''Region XII'''. The regional center is [[Koronadal]]. 
 
 There are four [[Provinces of the Philippines|provinces]] and 1 city. They are [[South Cotabato]], [[Cotabato]], [[Sultan Kudarat]], [[Sarangani]] and [[General Santos]].  
 
 [[Mindanao River]] is in the region. It empties into the [[Illana Bay]] of the larger [[Moro Gulf]].
