 '''SWV''' ('''Sisters with Voices''') are an [[African American]] [[female]] group that sing [[R & B]] music. The group was founded in 1990 and had multiple [[hit song]]s between then and 1998, one of which sampled the [[Michael Jackson]] hit song ''Human Nature''. The group broke up in 1998 but then reunited around 2005 and have been making music since. 
 
 == Albums == 
 * It's About Time (1992) 
 * New Beginning (1996) 
 * Release Some Tension (1997) 
 * I Missed Us (2012) 
 * Still (2016)
