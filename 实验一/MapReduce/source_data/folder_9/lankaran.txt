 '''Lankaran''' (also called '''Länkäran''', '''Lencoran''', and '''Lenkoran’''') is a city in [[Azerbaijan]]. It is on the coast of the [[Caspian Sea]], near the southern border of [[Iran]]. There were 83,300 people living there in 2010. It is next to [[Lankaran (rayon)|Lankaran rayon]]. Lankaran was for a long time the capital of the Talysh Khanate. Lankaran International Airport is the [[airport]] for the city. There are sandy [[beach]]es near Lankaran. 
 
 The old form of the name was  " Langarkanan " .  In [[Persian language|Persian]], it means  " the place of pulling up the [[anchor]](s) " . 
 ==Education== 
 Lankaran State University was started in [[1991]].
