 '''Hypercourt''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is in [[Hauts-de-France]] in the [[Somme]] [[Departments of France|department]] in north [[France]]. The municipality was created on 1 January 2017 and consists of the former communes of [[Pertain]] (the seat), [[Hyencourt-le-Grand]] and [[Omiécourt]]. < ref > [https://www.legifrance.gouv.fr/eli/arrete/2016/5/25/INTB1617389A/jo/texte Arrêté préfectoral] 25 May 2016 {{in lang|fr}} < /ref >
