 '''Spania''' ({{lang-la|Provincia Spaniae}}) was a [[Roman province|province]] of the [[Byzantine Empire]] (Eastern Roman Empire) from 552 until 624. < ref > Dates vary. Some (Collins) put the date of landing as early as 551, other (Wallace-Hadrill) as late as 554. The conquest of the last vestiges of the province has been dated to 625 (Collins) or 629 (W-H). < /ref >  Spania was created after the [[Western Roman Empire|fall of the Western Roman Empire]]. During this time, [[Visigoths|Visgothic]] and [[Suebi]] people had took over the [[Iberian Peninsula]]. 
 
 == History == 
 {{main|Western Roman Empire|Byzantine Empire}} 
 [[File:Justinian555AD.png|thumb|the Byzantine Empire in 555 CE]] 
 [[List of Byzantine emperors|Byzantine emperor]] [[Justinian I]] tried to restore the western provinces of the Empire. Spania was established in what is now the [[Balearic Islands]] and southern [[Andalusia]].
