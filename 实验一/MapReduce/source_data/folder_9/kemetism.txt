'''Kemetism''' (also called '''Kemeticism''', '''Neterism''', or '''Egyptian neopaganism''') is a [[new religious movement]] that wants to revive the [[Ancient Egyptian religion]]. The movement started in the 1970s; most of its followers live in the [[United States]], in [[France]] or in the [[Czech Republic]].
