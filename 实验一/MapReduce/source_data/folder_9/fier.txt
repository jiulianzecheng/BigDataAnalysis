 '''Fieri''' is a [[city]] in southwest [[Albania]].  It is the capital of the [[Fier District|district]] and [[Fier County|county]] of the same name. About 82,297 people live in Fier. Fier is {{convert|8|km|mi|0|abbr=on}} from the ruins of the [[ancient Greek]] city of [[Apollonia, Illyria|Apollonia]].
