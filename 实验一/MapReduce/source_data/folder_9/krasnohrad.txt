 '''Krasnohrad''' ({{lang-uk|Красноград}}) is a city in [[Kharkiv Oblast]], [[Ukraine]]. It is the administrative center of [[Krasnohrad Raion]]. In 2022, 19,674 people lived there. 
 
 From 1784 to 1922, it was known as '''Kostyantynohrad'''.
