 '''Sestao''' is a [[Municipalities of Spain|municipality]] of 27,342 people (2021). It is in the [[Provinces of Spain|province]] of [[Biscay]] in the [[Autonomous communities of Spain|autonomous community]] of [[Basque Country (autonomous community)|Basque Country]] in [[Spain]]. It is on the left bank of the river [[Nervión]] and is part of the [[Bilbao]] [[Greater Bilbao|metropolitan area]]. < ref > {{cite web |url= http://www.euskomedia.org/aunamendi/105732 |author= Ainhoa Arozamena Ayala |title= Auñamendi Encyclopedia: Sestao |publisher= [[Eusko Ikaskuntza]]|access-date=3 July 2010|language=es}} < /ref > 
 
 Sestao is the home of the most important [[steel]] industry of Biscay, [[Altos Hornos de Vizcaya]].
