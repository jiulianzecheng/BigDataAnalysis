 '''Tholen''', [[Zeelandic dialect|Zeelandic]]: ''Tole'', is a small city and municipality of the province of [[Zeeland]], the [[Netherlands]]. In 2016 the municipality had about 25,000 people. 
 
 The municipality of Tholen consists of the (former) island of [[Tholen (island)|Tholen]] and the (former) island of [[Sint Philipsland (island)|Sint Philipsland]]. 
 
 It borders the province of [[North Brabant]].
