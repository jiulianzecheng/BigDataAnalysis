 '''Lexicography''' is the study of [[lexicon]]s. It is divided into two separate [[academic]] fields: 
 
 * '''Practical lexicography''' is the art or [[craft]] of writing and editing [[dictionary|dictionaries]]. 
 * '''Theoretical lexicography''' is the [[scholar|scholarly]] study of a [[language]]. 
 
 A person whose works are about lexicography is called a '''lexicographer'''. < ref > {{Cite web|url=https://www.prospects.ac.uk/job-profiles/lexicographer|title=Lexicographer job profile {{!}} Prospects.ac.uk|website=www.prospects.ac.uk|language=en|access-date=2018-10-29}} < /ref >
