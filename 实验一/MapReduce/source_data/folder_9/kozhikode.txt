'''Kozhikode''', also known as '''Calicut''', is a [[city]] in the [[state]] of [[Kerala]] in southern [[India]]. Kozhikode is the third largest city in [[Kerala]]. It is part of the [[List of most populous urban agglomerations in Kerala|second largest urban agglomeration in Kerala]] with a metropolitan population of 2,030,519 as per 2011 census. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://www.censusindia.gov.in/towns/ker_towns.pdf |title=Alphabetical List of Towns and Their Population|publisher= Census of India 2001 |accessdate=15 February 2015}} < /ref >  The city lies about {{convert|380|km|mi|0}} north of the state capital [[Thiruvananthapuram]].
