 The town of '''Carluke''' is in the centre of the Scottish countryside in [[South Lanarkshire]], [[Scotland]], 5.4 [[mile]]s North-West of [[Lanark]] and 5 miles South-East of [[Wishaw]]. Many people in Carluke are commuters, with a variety of small stores and supermarkets available at the centre of the town. 
 
 ==Notable Figures== 
 Three people who were born in the town have received the [[Victoria Cross]]. The inventor of ''Slush Puppies'' previously stayed on a street in Carluke. The famous ex-Motherwell striker Dougie Arnott lives in Carluke.
