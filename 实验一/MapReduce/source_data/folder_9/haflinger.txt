 '''Haflinger''' is an all-round breed of [[horse]]. It is a [[mountain]] horse but today people use it for spare time. A Haflinger counts as a [[pony]] breed of horse. The first [[Cloning|clone]]d horse, [[Prometea]] is of this breed. A Haflinger has a [[gallant dry head]] with big eyes, wide-dilating nostrils, and small, attentive ears. People who raise this breed want them to have a good muscled body and a smart neck.
