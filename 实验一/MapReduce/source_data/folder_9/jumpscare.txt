A '''jumpscare''' is a way to scare the [[audience]] using sudden, loud [[noise]]s and/or quick visual changes. It is not only used in [[horror movie]]s, but also in many video games as well. They are often [[Criticism|criticized]], with people saying it is a cheap and/or lazy way to scare the audience.
