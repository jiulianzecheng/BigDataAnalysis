 '''Selb''' is a town in [[Wunsiedel (district)|Wunsiedel]], in [[Upper Franconia]], [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. It is in the [[Fichtelgebirge]], on the border with the [[Czech Republic]], 20 & nbsp;km northwest of [[Cheb]] and 23 & nbsp;km southeast of [[Hof, Bavaria|Hof]].
