 '''Manizales''' is a city in [[Colombia]]. It is the capital of [[Caldas Department]]. About 430,000 people live there. The city is known for the production of Colombian [[coffee]] and is near the [[volcano]] [[Nevado del Ruiz]]. 
 
 Manizales was founded on October 12, 1849.
