 '''Davos''' ({{lang-rm|Tavau}}, {{lang-it|Tavate}}) is a [[ski resort]] and [[municipality]] in the [[district]] of [[Davos (district)|Davos]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Graubünden]] in [[Switzerland]]. The [[ice hockey]] club EHC Davos has a [[stadium]] in the town. 
 
 Davos is the host town of the yearly [[World Economic Forum]] meeting.

 [[Davos Dorf]], [[Davos Platz]], [[Frauenkirch]], [[Davos Glaris]], [[Davos Wiesen]], [[Davos Monstein]], [[Davos Clavadel]], Laret, Wolfgang, Obem See, Meierhof, Stilli, Bünda, Spina, Tschuggen, Dörfji, In den Büelen, Hof, Teufi, Gadmen, Am Rin, Dürrboden, Sertig Dörfli, Oberalp and Inneralp.
