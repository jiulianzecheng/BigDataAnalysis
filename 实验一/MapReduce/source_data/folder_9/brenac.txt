 '''Brenac''' is a former [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is in the [[Regions of France|region]] [[Occitanie]] in the [[Aude]] [[Departments of France|department]] in the south of [[France]]. On 1 January 2016, it was merged into the commune of [[Quillan]]. < ref > [https://www.legifrance.gouv.fr/eli/arrete/2015/12/21/INTB1532034A/jo/texte Arrêté préfectoral] 21 December 2015 < /ref >
