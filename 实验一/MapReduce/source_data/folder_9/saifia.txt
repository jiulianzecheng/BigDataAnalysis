The Silsila-e-'''Saifia''' ({{lang-ur| سیفیہ}}; {{lang-ar| الطریقۃ السیفیہ}}) is a [[Muslim]] [[Sufi]] order based in [[Pakistan]]. It has a following in the [[United Kingdom]], [[Europe]], [[United States]], [[Canada]], [[Afghanistan]], [[India]], [[Bangladesh]] and various countries of the [[Middle East]]. The order is linked to the lineage of [[Naqshbandiyyah]] [[Mujaddad Alf Sani|Mujaddadiyyah]]. The order's grandmaster is [[Akhundzada Saif-ur-Rahman Mubarak]]. < ref > https://apkpure.com/silsila-e-saifia/com.appesta.silsilasaifia < /ref >
