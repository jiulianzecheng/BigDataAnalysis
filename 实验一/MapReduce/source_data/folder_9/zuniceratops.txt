 '''Zuniceratops''' was a [[ceratopsian]] [[dinosaur]]. It grew up to 13 [[Foot (unit of length)|feet]] (or 4 [[Meter (unit of length|meter long]]. His dinosaur family came from [[Asia]]. 
 His scientific name is ''Zuniceratops christopheril'', had 2 tons.
