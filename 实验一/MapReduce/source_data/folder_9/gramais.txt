 '''Gramais''' is a [[municipality]] of the [[Districts of Austria|district]] [[Reutte District|Reutte]] in the [[Austria|Austrian]] [[States of Austria|state]] of [[Tyrol (state)|Tyrol]]. 
 
 Gramais is the smallest independent municipality of Austria. It lies in a side valley off the [[Lech (river)|Lech]] valley.
