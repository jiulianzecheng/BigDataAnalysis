 '''Oatmeal''' is a [[:wikt:meal#Etymology_2|meal]] made of [[oats]]. It is normally made for [[breakfast]]. Oatmeal can also be steel-cut oats, crushed oats, or rolled oats. Occasionally, brown sugar, honey, jelly, or other items are added to oatmeal. It makes a tasty treat for all to enjoy. It is looked forward to as a light meal.
