 '''Gersthofen''' ([[Swabian German|Swabian]]: ''Geäschdhoofe'' < ref > {{Cite journal|date=2018-04-09|title=Geäschdhoofe|url=https://als.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Gersthofen & oldid=820993|journal=Alemannische Wikipedia}} < /ref > ) is a [[Town#Germany|town]] in the [[Augsburg (district)|district of Augsburg]], in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. It is on the west bank of the river [[Lech (river)|Lech]], about {{convert|7|km|2|abbr=on}} north of [[Augsburg]]. 
 
 Gersthofen is split into five districts ([[German language|German]]: Stadtteile): Batzenhofen, [[Edenbergen]] (with Gailenbach and Gailenbacher Mühle), Gersthofen, Hirblingen, and Rettenbergen (with Peterhof).
