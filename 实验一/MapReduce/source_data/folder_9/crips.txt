The '''Crips''' are a [[gang|street gang]] that were founded in 1969 by [[Stanley Williams]] and [[Raymond Washington]] in [[Los Angeles, California]]. They are rivals with the [[Bloods]], another street gang. Some notable Crips are [[Snoop Dogg]] and [[Eazy-E]].
