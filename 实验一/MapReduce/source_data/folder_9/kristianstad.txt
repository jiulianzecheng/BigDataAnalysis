 '''Kristianstad''' is a town in the [[counties of Sweden|county]] of [[Scania County|Scania]] in [[Sweden]]. It is the seat of [[Kristianstad Municipality]]. Until [[1997]], Kristianstad was also the seat of [[Kristianstad County]], before it was disbanded.
