 }}'''Virtustream''' is a provider of [[cloud computing]] management software. < ref > {{cite web|last=Gardner|first=Dana|title=Virtustream delivers cloud software to run private, public and hybrid enterprise-class clouds|url=http://www.zdnet.com/blog/gardner/virtustream-delivers-cloud-software-to-run-private-public-and-hybrid-enterprise-class-clouds/4665|publisher=ZDnet}} < /ref >  It is a subsidiary of [[Dell Technologies]]. 
 
 Virtustream is headquartered in [[McLean, Virginia]]. It has offices in [[Atlanta]], [[Bangalore]], [[Dallas]], [[Frankfurt]], [[Kaunas]], [[London]], [[San Francisco]], [[Santa Fe, New Mexico|Santa Fe]], [[Sydney]], [[Tokyo]], and [[Washington, D.C.|Washington D.C]]. < ref > {{cite web|title=Virtustream Company Profile|url=https://www.virtustream.com/about-us/corporate-profile|access-date=2 February 2019}} < /ref >  Virtustream's global infrastructure includes data centers in Frankfurt, [[Japan]], London, [[Paris]], [[San Francisco]], [[Seoul]], Sydney, [[Las Vegas]], Washington D.C., [[Chicago]], and Virginia. < ref > {{cite web|title=Virtustream Global Infrastructure|url=https://www.virtustream.com/cloud/global-infrastructure-locations|access-date=4 June 2018}} < /ref >
