 '''Dobrich''' ({{lang-bg|Добрич}}) is a [[List of cities in Bulgaria|city]] in  northeastern [[Bulgaria]] and is the capital of [[Dobrich Province]]. It is the ninth-largest city by population of the country. In 2019, 83,584 people lived here. 
 
 The city is 30 & nbsp;km west of the [[Black Sea]] coast, not far from resorts such as [[Albena]], [[Balchik]] and [[Golden Sands]].
