 '''''Atractosteus''''' is a genus of [[Gar|gars]] in the family [[Lepisosteidae]], with three species. 
 
 == Species == 
 
 === Extant === 
 
 * ''[[Atractosteus spatula]]'' (Alligator gar)  < small > Lacépède, 1803 < /small > 
 * ''[[Atractosteus tristoechus]]'' (Cuban gar)  < small > J. G. Scheider, 1801 < /small > 
 * ''[[Atractosteus tropicus]]'' (Tropical gar)  < small > T. N. Gill, 1863 < /small > 
 
 === Extinct === 
 
 * ''[[Atractosteus africanus]]''  < small > Arambourg  &  Joleaud, 1943 < /small > 
 * ''[[Atractosteus grandei]]''  < small > Brownstein  &  Lyson, 2022 < /small >
