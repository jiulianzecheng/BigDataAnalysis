 '''Akhfennir''' is a small town in [[Tarfaya Province]], in the [[Laâyoune-Sakia El Hamra]] region of [[Morocco]]. The area is noted for [[Khenifiss National Park]] and [[Tarfaya-Akhfenir Wind Farm]].
