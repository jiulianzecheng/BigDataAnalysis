 '''Pinzberg''' is a [[Municipalities of Germany|municipality]] in the district of [[Forchheim (district)|Forchheim]] in [[Bavaria]] in [[Germany]]. 
 
 Pinzberg is in the region Upper Franconia west. 
 
 The following districts exist: Dobenreuth, Gosberg, Pinzberg, Elsenberg.
