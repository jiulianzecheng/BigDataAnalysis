 '''Jagmohan Malhotra''' (25 September 1927  & ndash; 3 May 2021), known by the mononym '''Jagmohan''', was an Indian politician and member of the [[Bharatiya Janata Party]]. He was the [[Lieutenant Governor (India)|Lieutenant Governor]] of Delhi and Goa, as well as the [[Governor (India)|Governor]] of [[Jammu and Kashmir]]. He was also elected to the [[Lok Sabha]] (the lower house of the Indian Parliament) and was the Union Minister for [[Ministry of Urban Development (India)|Urban Development]] and [[Ministry of Tourism (India)|Tourism]]. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.indian-elections.com/keycontenders/jag-mohan.html|title=Jag Mohan, Key Contenders for India Election 2004|publisher=|access-date=19 November 2015|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20040620140233/http://www.indian-elections.com/keycontenders/jag-mohan.html|archive-date=20 June 2004|url-status=dead}} < /ref > 
 
 Jagmohan died on 3 May 2021 in [[New Delhi]], India at the age of 93. < ref > [https://www.indianexpress.com/article/india/former-jk-governor-jagmohan-passes-away-7301157/ Former J & K Governor Jagmohan passes away] < /ref >
