 '''Wadowice''' {{IPAc-pl|w|a|d|o|'|w|J|i|c|e}} ({{lang-ger|Frauenstadt – Wadowitz}}) is a town in [[Poland]] in [[Lesser Poland Voivodeship]] in the mountainous range known as [[Zakopane]] region, {{convert|50|km|0|abbr=off}} southeast of [[Krakow]] - (former Polish capital). 
 
 [[Pope John Paul II]] (Karol Wojtyla) was born there on May 18 1920. He also attended elementary and high school there, graduating with honors in 1938. 
 
 The town is twinned with 4 others, all in [[Italy]].
