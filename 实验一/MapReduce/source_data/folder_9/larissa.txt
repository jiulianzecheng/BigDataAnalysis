 '''Larissa''' ({{lang-el|Λάρισσα}}) is the [[capital (city)|capital]] [[city]] of the [[Thessaly]] [[Peripheries of Greece|periphery]] of [[Greece]], and capital of the [[Larissa Prefecture]]. Larissa is found in the north of the Thessaly plain near at the foot of the mountain [[Olympus]]. Its [[population]] is 131,496 inhabitants. Larissa is crossed by [[Pineios river]]. The most interest sight in Larissa is the ancient theater which is located in the center of the city.
