 '''Mushing''' is a [[sport]] or [[transport]] method powered by [[dog]]s. It includes [[carting]], [[pulka]], dog scootering, [[sled dog racing]], [[skijoring]], [[freighting]], and [[weight pulling]]. Mushing suggests the use of one or more dogs to pull a sled, most commonly a specialized type of [[dog sled]] on snow, or a rig on dry land.
