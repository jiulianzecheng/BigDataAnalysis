'''Piranshahr''' ({{lang-fa|پیرانشهر}}) is a city in northwest of [[Iran]]. The people of Piranshahr are mostly [[Kurds]] who consider themselves to be [[descendant]]s of the [[Medes]], one of the three [[Aryan]] tribes who [[migrate]]d to Iran. Piranshahr is the capital of [[Mokrian district]].
