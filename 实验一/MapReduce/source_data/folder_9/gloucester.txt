 '''Gloucester''' (pronounced [ˈglɒstə]) is a [[city]] and [[district]] in the [[England|English]] county of [[Gloucestershire]]. It is near the [[Wales|Welsh]] border on the [[River Severn]]. < ref > {{cite web |title=Visit the City of Gloucester |url=http://www.visitgloucester.co.uk/ |website=www.visitgloucester.co.uk}} < /ref > 
 
 == Culture == 
 The annual [[Three Choirs Festival]], which started in the [[18th century|eighteenth century]] and one of the oldest [[music festival]]s in [[Europe]], takes place in Gloucester every third year. The other places the festival is held are [[Hereford]] and [[Worcester]]. 
 
 Some parts of the [[Harry Potter]] films were filmed at Gloucester [[cathedral]]. 
 
 [[Gloucester Rugby]], founded in 1873, play here. The team plays in the Guinness Premiership, the top level of English rugby. Their homeground is Kingsholm. 
 
 == Transport == 
 
 === Road === 
 The M5 motorway by-passes Gloucester. 
 
 The A38, A40, A417, A430, A4301, A4302, B4008, B4063, B4072 and B4073 roads pass through Gloucester.
