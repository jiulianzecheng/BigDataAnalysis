'''Eskdale, Victoria''' is a small Australian town in the Mitta Valley with [[Mitta Mitta]] and [[Dartmouth, Victoria|Dartmouth]]. The Mitta Valley is in the north east of [[Victoria (Australia)|Victoria]] about {{convert|60|km|mi|0|lk=on|abbr=on}} from [[Albury, New South Wales|Albury]] and [[Wodonga, Victoria|Wodonga]]  
 
 It has one shop, one cafe, a hotel, two churches and one school.
