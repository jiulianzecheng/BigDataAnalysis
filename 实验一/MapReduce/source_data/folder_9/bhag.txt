'''Bhag''' ({{lang-ur|بھاگ}}) is a city in [[Sibi District]] in the [[Balochistan (Pakistan)|Balochistan]] province of [[Pakistan]]. The city is the capital of the tehsil of the same name ([[Bhag Tehsil]]), and has an altitude of 90 metres (298 & nbsp;feet).
