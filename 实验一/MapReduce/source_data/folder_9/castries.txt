 '''Castries''' is the [[capital city]] of [[Saint Lucia]]. Saint Lucia is an island in the [[Windward Islands]], which is part of the [[Caribbean]]. Castries has a population of about 3,600 people, about 16,000 live near the city. Castries is known for its [[port]]. The main products shipped from there include [[bananas]], [[sugarcane]] and [[rum]].
