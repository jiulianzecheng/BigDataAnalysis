 '''Sneek''' ('''[[Frisian]]''': ''Snits'') is a city in [[Friesland]], in the [[Netherlands]]. In history, it is well known. The legendary warrior [[Pier Gerlofs Donia]], fought a battle there. He is buried in the town in the [[Grote of Martinikerk]]. Since 2011, it has been part of the new municipality of [[Súdwest-Fryslân]].
