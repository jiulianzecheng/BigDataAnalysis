{{Kackeschbaach}} 
 
 The '''Kackeschbaach''' (part of it is also known as the Kettebaach) is a tributary of the [[Syre (river)|Syre]] in the communes of [[Sandweiler]] and [[Contern]] in [[Luxembourg]]. It flows from the South of Sandweiler out east before heading further south, roughly following Route de Remich through [[Eitermillen]] and it ends in [[Oetrange]].
