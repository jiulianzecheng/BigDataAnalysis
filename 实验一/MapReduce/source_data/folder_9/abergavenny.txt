 '''Abergavenny''' ({{lang-cy|Y Fenni}}) is a [[market town]] and community in [[Monmouthshire]], [[Wales]]. As of 2011, Avergavenny had a population of 12,515. The town is promoted as ''The Gateway to Wales''. < ref > {{Cite web|url=http://www.visitabergavenny.co.uk/|title=Visit Abergavenny â€“ The Gateway to Wales|publisher=Visit Abergavenny|access-date=30 January 2020}} < /ref > 
 
 ==Transport== 
 [[Abergavenny railway station]] is a [[train station]] on the [[Welsh Marches line]]. Passengers at the station can board direct trains to places such as [[Hereford]], [[Shrewsbury]], [[Manchester]], [[Chester]], [[Wrexham]], [[Holyhead]], [[Cardiff]], [[Swansea]] and [[Carmarthen]].
