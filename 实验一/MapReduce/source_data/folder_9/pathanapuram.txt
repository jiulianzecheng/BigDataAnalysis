 '''Pathanapuram''' is a town in [[Kollam district]], [[Kerala]], [[India]]. It is in Pathanapuram [[taluka]], of which it is also the headquarters. 
 
 == Geography == 
 Pathanapuram is spread over an area of {{Convert|24.20|km2|abbr=on}}. 
 
 == Demographics == 
 {{As of|2011}} [[census]] of India, Pathanapuram Panchayat had a population of 32,339. 
 
 == Transport == 
 The area is connected to the rest of the state by bus services of [[Kerala State Road Transport Corporation|KSRTC]]. The nearest railway stations to the town are {{rws|Chengannur}}, {{rws|Kollam}}, [[Kayamkulam]], {{rws|Punalur}} and {{rws|Avaneeswaram}}. KSRTC sub depot also in Pathanapuram near Forest Range Office. Nearest airport is [[Trivandrum]] (80 & nbsp;km).
