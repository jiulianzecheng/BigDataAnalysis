 '''Baccara''' was a [[Spain|Spanish]] pop group. They were formed in 1977. It had two members: Mayte Mateos and María Mendiola. They were successful with their first song, ''[[Yes Sir, I Can Boogie]]''. Baccara represented [[Luxembourg]] in the [[Eurovision Song Contest]] in 1978. They performed ''[[Parlez-vous français?]]''. In 1981, Baccara split. Both Mateos and Mendiola then began work on music by themselves. revived several times in 1987-onwards.
