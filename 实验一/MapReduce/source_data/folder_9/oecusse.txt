 '''Oecusse''' is a Special Administrative Region of [[East Timor]]. < ref > {{Cite web|url=https://www.cia.gov/library/publications/the-world-factbook/geos/tt.html|title=Timor-Leste|access-date=2020-04-11|website=CIA World Factbook|archive-date=2018-01-28|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180128152225/https://www.cia.gov/library/publications/the-world-factbook/geos/tt.html|url-status=dead}} < /ref >  The capital is [[Pante Macassar]]. Oecusse is separated from the rest of East Timor by [[West Timor]]. 
 
 The province of [[Nusa Tenggara Timur]], [[Indonesia]] surrounds Oecusse in all directions except the north, where it borders the [[Savu Sea]]. 
 
 ==Administrative posts== 
 [[File:Sucos Oecussi.png|thumb|Subdivisions of Oecusse]] 
 
 Oecusse is divided into four administrative posts (formerly sub-districts): < ref > {{cite web|title=Oe-Cusse Suco Reports|url=https://www.mof.gov.tl/about-the-ministry/statistics-indicators/sensus-fo-fila-fali/download-suco-reports/oe-cusse-suco-reports/?lang=en|publisher=Timor-Leste Ministry of Finance}} < /ref > < ref > {{HD of East Timor|title=Oecusse District|page=134}} < /ref > 
 * [[Nitibe Administrative Post|Nitibe]] 
 * [[Oesilo Administrative Post|Oesilo]] 
 * [[Pante Macassar Administrative Post|Pante Macassar]] (with capital [[Pante Macassar]]) 
 * [[Passabe Administrative Post|Passabe]] 
 
 The administrative posts are divided into 18 sucos ( " villages " ) in total.
