 '''Unterleinach''' is a village outside of [[Würzburg]], [[Germany]]. It is part of the municipality of [[Leinach]]. It is south of [[Oberleinach]]. 
 
 Unterleinach and Oberleinach have rival [[Association football|football]] teams.
