 '''Calvillo''', is a city in the [[States of Mexico|Mexican state]] of [[Aguascalientes]] and the capital of the [[Calvillo Municipality|municipality of the same name]], it is located in the southwestern area and is the second most important city in Aguascalientes, after [[Aguascalientes City]]. 
 
 == Demographics == 
 Calvillo had a population of 58,250 inhabitants (icluding the arounds) in [[2020]]
