 The '''''Actinidiaceae''''' are a family of around 360 species of plants in the [[Ericales]] order. < ref > {{Cite journal|last1=Anderberg|first1=Arne A.|last2=Rydin|first2=Catarina|last3=Källersjö|first3=Mari|date=2002|title=Phylogenetic relationships in the order Ericales s.l. : analyses of molecular data from five genes from the plastid and mitochondrial genomes|url=http://urn.kb.se/resolve?urn=urn:nbn:se:su:diva-170431|journal=American Journal of Botany|volume=89|issue=4|pages=677–687|doi=10.3732/ajb.89.4.677|pmid=21665668}} < /ref >  The most important genus is ''[[Actinidia]]'', the kiwifruits.
