 '''Abakan''' ({{lang-ru|Абака́н|p=ɐbɐˈkan}}; [[Khakas language|Khakas]]: {{lang|kjh|Ағбан}} or {{lang|kjh|Абахан}}) is the [[capital city]] of the Republic of [[Khakassia]], [[Russia]]. As of the 2021 Census, 189,963 people lived there.
