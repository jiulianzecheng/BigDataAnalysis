 '''Eibar''' ({{lang-eu|Eibar}}; {{lang-es|Éibar}}) is a city and [[Municipalities of Spain|municipality]] within the [[Provinces of Spain|province]] of [[Gipuzkoa]], in the [[Basque Country (autonomous community)|Basque Country]] in [[Spain]]. It is home to [[football (soccer)|football]] team [[SD Eibar]].
