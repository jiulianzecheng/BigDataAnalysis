 " '''HiiiPoWeR''' "  is the debut [[Single (music)|single]] by [[Kendrick Lamar]]. It is a [[conscious hip hop]] song. It was released in April 2011 through [[Top Dawg Entertainment]]. It was a promotional single that was produced by [[J.Cole]]. Its lyrics make references to [[African-American]] [[activist]]s such as [[Martin Luther King]] and are about [[racism]] and the [[mass media|media]].  " HiiiPoWeR "  was the only single released out of the album ''[[Section.80]]''. The reason why the title has 3 ‘i’s is because it stands for [[Honor]], [[Respect]] and [[Heart]] according to Lamar. Lamar was influenced by [[Thug Life]], a [[hip hop]] group led by [[Tupac Shakur]].
