'''Fangames''' are [[computer game]]s created by fans based on [[Popularity|popular]] [[video games]]. They are often made using [[C++]], [[The Games Factory]], [[Multimedia Fusion Express]], and [[GameMaker]]. But now with free versions available of powerful game development tools available, some fangames are made in either Unity or Unreal Engine.
