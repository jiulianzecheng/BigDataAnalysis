Photonics is the [[science]] of and [[technology]] using photons, the fundamental particle of [[light]]. The word's [[etymology]] comes from the Greek word  " ''phos'' " , meaning light, and the [[suffix]]  " -''ics'' " , meaning 'matters relevant to'. The etymology is similar to that of the field of [[Electronics]], in which [[Electron|electrons]] are the main physical particle used in the science and technology.  
 
 Photonics technologies respond to the needs of humankind by providing access to information, promoting sustainable development, and increasing societal health and well-being. It has many applications across [[medicine]], [[Communication|communications]], [[entertainment]] and [[culture]].
