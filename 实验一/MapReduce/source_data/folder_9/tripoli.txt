 '''Tripoli''' is a [[city]] in northwest [[Libya]].  It is the [[Capital (city)|capital]] of the [[country]].  More than three million people live in the metropolitan area.  
 
 Originally a [[Phoenicia]]n colony, Tripoli became in turn part of Greek and Roman [[Cyrenaica]], a pirate port, a part of the [[Ottoman Empire]], and the capital of the [[Italian Empire|Italian empire]] in [[North Africa]]. < ref > [https://lu1960.blogspot.com/2020/12/italian-tripoli.html Italian Tripoli] < /ref >
