 '''Schwechat''' is a town in the district of [[Bruck an der Leitha District|Bruck an der Leitha]] in the [[Austria]]n state of [[Lower Austria]], southeast of [[Vienna]]. In 2018, 18,026 people lived there. 
 
 It is known for the [[Vienna International Airport]] and Schwechater [[beer]]. 
 
 Schwechat is named after the river [[Schwechat (river)|Schwechat]], which flows through the centre of town.
