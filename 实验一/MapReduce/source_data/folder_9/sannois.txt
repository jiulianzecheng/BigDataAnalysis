 '''Sannois''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is in [[Île-de-France]] in the [[Val-d'Oise]] [[Departments of France|department]] in north [[France]]. In 2019, 26,565 people lived there. 
 
 The commune is in the northwestern [[suburb]]s of [[Paris]], {{convert|15.2|km|mi|abbr=on}} from the center.
