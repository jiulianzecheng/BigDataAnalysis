'''Tiscali SpA''' ({{Ise|IT0001453924|TIS}}) is a [[Europe]]an [[telecommunications]] company, based in [[Cagliari]], [[Italy]], and provides [[internet]] and [[telecommunications]] services to [[Italy]], the [[United Kingdom]], [[Germany]] and the [[Czech Republic]]. The company acquired many European [[Internet service provider]]s (ISPs) throughout the late [[1990s]], although in subsequent years many of those assets were sold on to other companies.
