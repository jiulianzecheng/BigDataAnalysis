 The '''rastacap''' (also called a '''rastafar''' or '''toppa''') is a tall, [[Knitting|knitted]], round [[cap]], which is often brightly coloured. It comes from [[Jamaica]] and [[Barbados]] and is commonly thought of as a [[Rastafarian]] hat.
