'''Quasimodo''' is the hunchback in the novel [[The Hunchback of Notre Dame]] written by [[Victor Hugo]]. He was raised by [[Claude Frollo]]. Though the townspeople believe Quasimodo is a monster, he befriends Esmeralda.
