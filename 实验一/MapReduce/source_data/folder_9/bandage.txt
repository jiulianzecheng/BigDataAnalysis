 A '''bandage''' is strip of [[cloth]] or other [[material]] that is used to stop and absorb [[blood]] from [[wound]]s. 
 
 In common speech, the word  " bandage "  is often used to mean a [[Dressing (medical)|dressing]], though technically the two are different things.
