 '''''Piatnitzkysaurus''''' (meaning  " Piatnitzky's lizard " ) is a [[genus]] of [[theropod]] [[dinosaur]]. It lived about 166 to 164 million years ago in the middle [[Jurassic|Jurassic Period]]. Its remains were found in [[Argentina]]. The animal was a fairly large, lightly built, [[Bipedalism|bipedal]], land-based [[carnivore]]. It grew up to {{Convert|6.6|m|ft|1|abbr=on}} long.
