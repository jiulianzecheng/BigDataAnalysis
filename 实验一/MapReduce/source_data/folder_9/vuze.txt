 '''Vuze''' (formerly '''Azureus''') is a [[Java (programming language)|Java]]-based [[BitTorrent]] used for file sharing. The program's logo is the Blue Poison Dart Frog ([[Dendrobates azureus]]). The Azureus name was given to the project by co-creator [[Tyler Pitchford]], who uses the Latin names of [[poison dart frog]]s as  " nickname "  for his development projects. 
 
 Azureus was first released in June 2003 at [[SourceForge.net]]. It is now one of the most popular BitTorrent clients
