 '''Karhu''' (in english  " The Bear " ) is [[Finland|Finnish]] brand of [[beer]]. It is the most popular beer in Finland. Its history begins in 1920, in [[Pori]]. Production of Karhu stopped in the early 1930s, but continued again from 1958.
