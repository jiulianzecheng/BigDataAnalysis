'''Sudjati''' (13 March 1954  & ndash; 8 December 2020) was an Indonesian politician. He was born in [[Semarang]], [[Indonesia]]. He was Regent of the [[Bulungan Regency]] from 2016 until his death.  
 
 Sudjati died on 8 December 2020 in [[Tanjung Selor]], Indonesia from [[COVID-19]] at the age of 66. < ref > [https://www.antaranews.com/berita/1883216/bupati-bulungan-meninggal-dunia-setelah-dikonfirmasi-tertular-covid-19 Bupati Bulungan meninggal dunia setelah dikonfirmasi tertular COVID-19] {{in lang|id}} < /ref >
