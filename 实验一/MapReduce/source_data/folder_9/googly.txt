In [[cricket]], a '''googly''' is a type of way the ball is [[Bowler|bowled]]. A googly is bowled by a right-arm [[leg spin]] bowler. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/sport2/hi/cricket/skills/4173812.stm|title=The googly explained|publisher=BBC}} < /ref >  It is occasionally called a '''Bosie''' (or '''Bosey''') after its inventor [[Bernard Bosanquet]].{{cn}}
