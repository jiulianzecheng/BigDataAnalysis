 '''Oxberg''' is a [[urban areas in Sweden|minor locality]] in [[Mora Municipality]] in [[Sweden]]. In 2010, 97 people lived there. < ref name=scb / >  [[Vasaloppet]] goes through Oxberg. It is also the start site for [[Tjejvasan]], [[Kortvasan]] and [[Halvvasan]]. The [[Oxberg Bridge]] is also located here.
