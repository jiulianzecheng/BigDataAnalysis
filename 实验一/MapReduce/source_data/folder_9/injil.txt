The '''Injil''' ({{lang-ar|[[wikt:إنجيل|إنجيل]]|ʾInjīl}}, alternative spellings: ''Ingil'' or ''Injeel'') is the [[Arabic language|Arabic]] word for the [[Gospel]]. [[Muslim]]s believe the Injil is to be followed, but has been corrupted over time. Muslims believe the [[Qur'an]] is the true successor.
