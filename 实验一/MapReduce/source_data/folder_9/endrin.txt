 '''Endrin''' is an [[insecticide]] that was commonplace among [[cotton]] growers and for the treament of [[cereal]]s. It was also used to kill [[rodent]]s. It is a [[neurotoxin]]. It is a [[stereoisomer]] of [[Dieldrin]]. In the soil, it can take up to twelve years to decay. It is one of the twelve substances listed in the [[Stockholm Convention on Persistent Organic Pollutants]], and its production, trade and use is banned since 2004.
