'''Coir''', also called '''coconut fibre''', is a natural fibre extracted from the [[Coconut palm|coconu]] < nowiki/ > t. It is made into rope, floor mats, doormats, brushes, and mattresses. It is found between the hard, internal shell and the outer coat of a coconut.
