 '''''Cumhuriyet''''' (English: ''The Republic'') is a [[Turkey|Turkish]] nationwide daily [[newspaper]] based in [[Istanbul]]. It was founded on May 7, 1924 by [[Yunus Nadi Abalıoğlu]]. 
 
 Cumhuriyet newspaper includes columnists that are generally considered to share [[Social democracy|social democratic]], [[secularism|secularist]] and [[republic]]an ideas with its readers. Cumhuriyet is sold about 60,000 everyday. 
 
 [[İlhan Selçuk]], the [[editor]] of Cumhuriyet arrested by police after the [[conservatism|conservative]]-[[Islamic]] government party [[AKP]] has wanted to closed.
