 '''Harderwijk''' ([[Dutch Low Saxon]]: ''Harderwiek'') is a [[List of municipalities of the Netherlands|municipality]] found in the [[Netherlands|Dutch]] [[Provinces of the Netherlands|province]] of [[Gelderland]]. As of 2021, about 49,000 are living there.
