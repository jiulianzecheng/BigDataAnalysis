 '''Denpasar''' ({{lang-ban|ᬤᬾᬦ᭄ᬧᬲᬃ|Denpasar}}) is the capital city of the province of [[Bali]], [[Indonesia]]. It is also the site of [[Ngurah Rai Airport]], the main gateway to Bali. It has a population of 897,300 (2017).
