'''Chloride''' is an [[ion]] of chlorine. It occurs when [[chlorine]] gets [[reduction (chemistry)|reduced]], gaining an electrons and becoming an ion. Chloride is necessary for life. It is normally found in the salt [[sodium chloride]], which is very common in diets.
