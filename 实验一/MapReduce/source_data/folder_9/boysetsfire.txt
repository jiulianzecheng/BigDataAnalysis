 '''Boysetsfire''' is an [[United States|American]] [[post-hardcore]] [[band]] from [[Newark, Delaware]]. Boysetsfire has five members in the band; the current band members are Nathan Gray, Chad Istvan, Robert Ehrenbrand, Josh Latshaw and Matt Krupanski. 
 
 ==Band members== 
 ;Current 
 *Nathan Gray – lead vocals, [[keyboard instrument|keyboard]]  
 *Josh Latshaw – [[guitar]], backing vocals 
 *Chad Istvan – [[guitar]], backing vocals, [[piano]], keyboard  
 *Robert Ehrenbrand – [[bass guitar]], backing vocals 
 *Matt Krupanski - [[drum]]s  
 
 ;Former 
 *Rob Avery - Bass guitar 
 *Darrell Hyde - Bass guitar 
 
 ==Discography== 
 So far as of [[2015]], Boysetsfire has released 6 studio albums: 
 
 ===Albums=== 
 *''[[The Day the Sun Went Out]]'' (1997) 
 *''After the Eulogy'' (2000) 
 *''[[Tomorrow Come Today]]'' (2003) 
 *''The Misery Index: Notes from the Plague Years'' (2006) 
 *''While a Nation Sleeps...'' (2013) 
 *''Boysetsfire'' (2015)
