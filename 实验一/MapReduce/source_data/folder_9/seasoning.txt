 '''Seasoning''' is when [[salt]], [[herb]]s, or [[spice]]s is added to food to make it [[taste]] better. There are many ways of adding seasoning in different [[culture]]s. For example, in some [[cultures]], [[meat]] may be seasoned by pouring [[sauce]] over the [[dish]] when it is on the table.
