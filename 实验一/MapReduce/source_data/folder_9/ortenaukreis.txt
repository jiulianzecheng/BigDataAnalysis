 '''Ortenaukreis''' is a [[Districts of Germany|''Landkreis'' (district)]] in the west of [[Baden-Württemberg]], [[Germany]]. 
 
 [[France]] and Germany have created the [[Strasbourg-Ortenau Eurodistrict|Strasbourg-Ortenau]] [[eurodistrict]] across the [[Rhine]] and combining Ortenaukreis and Greater [[Strasbourg]].
