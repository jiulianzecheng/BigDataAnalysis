'''Futurists''' are persons who are interested in [[futurology]]. They try to explore predictions about the [[future]] to other persons. One of the most famous futurists is [[Ray Kurzweil]]. For example, he predicts that the [[technological singularity]] will happen in 2045. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://futurism.com/kurzweil-claims-that-the-singularity-will-happen-by-2045|title=Kurzweil Claims That the Singularity Will Happen by 2045|website=Futurism|language=en|access-date=2020-07-13}} < /ref >
