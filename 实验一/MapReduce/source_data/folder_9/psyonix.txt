'''Psyonix Inc.''' is an [[United States|American]] [[video game]] [[Video game developer|development]] studio. It is based in [[San Diego]], [[California]]. They were founded in 2000. They were officially [[Corporation|incorporated]] on April 30, 2001. Psyonix are best known for publishing and developing ''[[Rocket League]]'' since 2015. ''Rocket League'' is a [[sequel]] to their other game, ''[[Supersonic Acrobatic Rocket-Powered Battle-Cars]]'' (2008). 
 
 Psyonix also worked on games such as ''[[Gears of War]]'', ''[[Bulletstorm]]'' and ''[[Homefront (video game)|Homefront]]''.
