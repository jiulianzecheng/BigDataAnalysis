'''''Tlatoani''''' ({{lang-nci|tlàtoāni}} {{pronounced|tɬaʔ.to.ˈaː.ni}}; plural ''{{lang|nci|tlàtòquê}}'', {{IPA|[tɬaʔ.ˈtoʔ.keʔ]}}) is the [[Nahuatl]] word for the ruler of an ''[[altepetl]]'', a [[pre-Hispanic]] state. The word means  " speaker " , but may be translated into English as  " [[king]] " . < ref > Lockhart (2001, p.238); Schroeder (2007, p.3).
