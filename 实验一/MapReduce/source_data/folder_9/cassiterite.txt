 '''Cassiterite''' is a [[mineral]]. Its chemical formula is SnO < sub > 2 < /sub > . It contains [[tin dioxide]]. It is black because of impurities. It is the main [[ore]] of [[tin]]. Its [[Mohs hardness]] is 6-7.
