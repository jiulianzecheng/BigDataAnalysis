An '''hour''' (abbreviation: '''h''' or '''hr''') is a [[unit of measurement]] used to measure [[time]]. An hour is equal to 60 [[minute]]s. 24 hours are equal to one [[day]]. Unlike the [[second]], the hour is not a base [[SI unit]].
