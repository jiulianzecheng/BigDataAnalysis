 '''Alexa''' was an [[Internet]] service that measures how many Internet users visit a website. At Alexa.com, people entered a [[website]] address and Alexa showed them how well-visited the website is. It can also tell them how its popularity has changed over [[time]]. 
 Alexa had 6 million unique visits monthly. 
 
 Alexa was created in 1996 by American businessman Brewster Kahle and Bruce Gilliat. 
 In 1999, Alexa was bought by Amazon.com for about US$250 million in Amazon stock. 
 Alexa provided a free [[API]] that is used by services and toolbars all around the world to easily display Alexa Rating. 
 
 Some virus companies mark Alexa [[toolbar]] as [[adware]] (McAfee), trackware ([[Symantec]]) and potentially unwanted Program. 
 
 At December 2021 [[Amazon.com|Amazon]] announced that they will turn Alexa off. Service was discontinued May 1 of 2022.
