'''Metaphilosophy''' is the study of the nature of [[philosophy]]. Philosophy is the study of [[idea]]s connected with existence, knowledge, values, reason, mind, and language. Metaphilosophy investigates the aims, boundaries, and [[scientific method|method]]s of philosophy. Metaphilosophers ask the question,  " What is philosophy? "  and seek to understand what its [[teleology|purpose]] is.
