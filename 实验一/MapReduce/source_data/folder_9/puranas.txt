In the [[Sanskrit]] language, '''purana''' means story of ancient times. For [[Hindu]]s, a purana is a scripture that tells what happened in ancient times. Puranas tell about history, ancient [[India]]n [[tradition]]s, and what Hindus believe. It is basically in [[Sanskrit]] and [[Tamil language|Tamil languages]].
