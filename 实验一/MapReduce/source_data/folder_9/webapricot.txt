The '''WebApricot Pan-Armenian Film Festival''' is the first online film festival for [[Armenians]]. It was established in 2011 by the [[:en:Yerevan International Film Festival|Golden Apricot International Film Festival]] and Webtv.am Internet Channel. In the festival filmmakers from [[Armenia]] and other countries can participate.
