 '''Marmorera''' ({{lang-de|Marmels}}, {{lang-rm|Murmarera}}) was a [[municipality]] of the [[district]] [[Albula (district)|Albula]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Graubünden]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 On 1 January 2016, the former municipalities of [[Bivio]], [[Cunter]], Marmorera, [[Mulegns]], [[Riom-Parsonz]], [[Salouf]], [[Savognin]], [[Sur, Switzerland|Sur]] and [[Tinizong-Rona]] merged to form the new municipality of [[Surses]]. < ref > [http://www.bfs.admin.ch/bfs/portal/de/index/infothek/nomenklaturen/blank/blank/gem_liste/03.html Amtliches Gemeindeverzeichnis der Schweiz] published by the Swiss Federal Statistical Office {{in lang|de}} accessed 27 April 2016 < /ref >
