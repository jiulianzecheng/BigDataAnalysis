 }}'''Schandmaul''' is a medieval [[folk rock]] band from [[Munich]], [[Germany]]. They formed in [[1998]]. The members right now are: [[Thomas Lindner]], [[Birgit Muggenthaler]], [[Anna Kränzlein]], [[Martin Duckstein]], [[Stefan Brunner]], [[Matthias Richter]].  < br / > 
 The first song (Teufelsweib) was recorded in 1998. In the following year they released their very first Album  " Wahre Helden " . In 2000 the Album  " Von Spitzbuben und anderen Halunken "  has been redorded and released. In 2001 the band was discorvered by a record label, which led to the rerelease of  " Von Spitzbuben und Halunken " .
