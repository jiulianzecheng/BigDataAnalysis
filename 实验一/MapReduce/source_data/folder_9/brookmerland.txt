 '''Brookmerland''' is a ''[[Samtgemeinde]]'' ( " collective municipality " ) in [[Aurich (district)|Aurich]], in [[Lower Saxony]], [[Germany]]. It is southeast of [[Norden, Lower Saxony|Norden]]. Its seat is in the village Marienhafe.
