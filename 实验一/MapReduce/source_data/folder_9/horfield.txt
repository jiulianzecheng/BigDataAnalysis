'''Horfield''' is a [[suburb]] of the [[city]] of [[Bristol]], in southwest [[England]]. 
 
 Horfield is home to the [[Memorial Stadium (Bristol)|Memorial Stadium]], a sports stadium built for the [[Bristol Rugby|Bristol Rugby Club]]. In 1996, the stadium also became home to [[Bristol Rovers F.C.|Bristol Rovers Football Club]].
