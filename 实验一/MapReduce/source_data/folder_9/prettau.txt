 '''Prettau''' ({{lang-it|Predoi}}) is a ''[[comune]]'' in [[South Tyrol]] in [[Trentino-Alto Adige/Südtirol]], [[Italy]]. It is the northernmost comune of Italy, on the border with [[Austria]].
