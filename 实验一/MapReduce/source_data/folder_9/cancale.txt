 '''Cancale''' ({{Lang-br|Kankaven}}; [[Gallo language|Gallo]]: ''Cauncall'') is a small commune in western [[France]]. It is near [[Saint-Coulomb]]. A famous place there is the [[Pointe du Grouin]].
