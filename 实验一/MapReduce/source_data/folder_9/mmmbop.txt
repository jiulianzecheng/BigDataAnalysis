''' " MMMBop " ''' is a song by [[United States|American]] [[pop rock]] group [[Hanson (band)|Hanson]]. It was from their 1997 album ''Middle of Nowhere''. The song hit #1 in several countries, including United States, [[United Kingdom]], [[Australia]], [[Germany]] and [[Mexico]]. < ref > {{Cite web|last=Pingitore|first=Silvia|date=2022-05-19|title=From the 1997 world hit MMMBop to 30 years in music: interview with Hanson|url=https://the-shortlisted.co.uk/hanson-mmmbop-interview/|access-date=2022-06-24|website=the-shortlisted.co.uk|language=en-GB}} < /ref > 
 
 The song was released on April 15, 1997 in the United States.
