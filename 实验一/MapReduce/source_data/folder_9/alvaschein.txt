 '''Alvaschein''' ({{lang-rm|Alvaschagn}}) is a former [[municipality]] of the [[district]] of [[Albula (district)|Albula]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Graubünden]] in [[Switzerland]]. On 1 January 2015 the former municipalities of Alvaschein, [[Alvaneu]], [[Brienz/Brinzauls]], [[Mon, Switzerland|Mon]], [[Stierva]], [[Surava]] and [[Tiefencastel]] merged into the new municipality of [[Albula/Alvra]].
