 '''Lav''' or '''Lava''', in [[Hindu]] mythology, was the son of the deity and hero [[Rama]] (see the classical Indian epic poem [[Ramayana]]) and his wife, [[Sita]]. According to legend, Lav founded the ancient [[South Asia]]n city of [[Lahore]], now in [[Pakistan]]; while his brother Kash, or Kashava, founded the nearby city of [[Kasur]].
