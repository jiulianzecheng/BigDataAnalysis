 '''Rohtak''' ({{Audio|Rohtak.ogg|listen}}) is a city and the administrative headquarters of the [[Rohtak district]] in the Indian state of [[Haryana]]. It lies {{convert|70|km}} north-west of [[New Delhi]] and {{convert|250|km}} south of the state capital [[Chandigarh]].
