 A '''nanny''' is someone hired to [[child care|take care]] of the [[children]] in a [[family]].  They are usually [[female]] but there are also [[male]] nannies. Many nannies can begin their employment in their late teens. Many nannies live in their employers' home most of the days in the year. Some employers require their nanny/nannies to wear a [[uniform]].
