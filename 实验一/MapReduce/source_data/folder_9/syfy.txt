 '''Syfy''' ({{pron-en|ˈsaɪfaɪ}}; < !-- \ˈsī-ˈfī\; -- >  the same way as ''sci-fi''), formerly known as the '''Sci-Fi Channel''', is an American [[cable television]] channel that specializes in [[science fiction]], [[fantasy]], [[horror film|horror]], [[professional wrestling]], and [[paranormal TV|paranormal]] programming. Launched on 24 September 1992, it is part of the entertainment [[conglomerate (company)|conglomerate]] [[NBC Universal]]. The name Syfy was officially adopted on 7 July 2009.
