A '''ball''' is a round object (usually [[sphere|spherical]], but can sometimes be [[oval|ovoid]]) with several uses.  
 
 Ball is used metaphorically sometimes to denote something spherical or spheroid, e.g., [[armadillo|armadillos]] and [[human]] beings curl up into a ball, making a [[fist]] into a ball.
