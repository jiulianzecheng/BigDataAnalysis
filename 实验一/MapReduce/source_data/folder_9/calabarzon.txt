 '''Calabarzon''' is an [[Regions of the Philippines|administrative region]] in the [[Philippines]]. It is also known as '''Southern Tagalog Mainland''' or '''Region IV-A'''. It is on [[Luzon]] island. The capital is [[Calamba, Laguna|Calamba]]. 
 
 The region has five [[Provinces of the Philippines|provinces]]: [[Cavite]], [[Laguna (province)|Laguna]], [[Batangas]], [[Rizal]], and [[Quezon]]. There is also one independent city: [[Lucena, Quezon|Lucena]].
