 '''Ausserferrera''' ({{lang-rm|Farera}}) is a village and former [[municipality]] in the [[district]] of [[Hinterrhein (district)|Hinterrhein]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Graubünden]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 On 1 January 2008 the former municipalities of Ausserferrera and [[Innerferrera]] merged into the new municipality of [[Ferrera, Switzerland|Ferrera]].
