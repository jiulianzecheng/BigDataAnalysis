 '''Fahrenzhausen''' is a [[Municipalities of Germany|municipality]] located on the river [[Amper]] in the district of [[Freising (district)|Freising]] in [[Bavaria]] in [[Germany]]. The town is about 30 Kilometers north of [[Munich]].  
 
 ==Geography== 
 Fahrenzhausen has several villages in its community: Appercha, Bachenhausen, Bergfeld, Grosseisenbach, Grossnöbach, Jarzt, [[Kammerberg (Fahrenzhausen)|Kammerberg]], Lauterbach, Unterbruck, [[Viehbach (Fahrenzhausen)|Viehbach]] and Weng.
