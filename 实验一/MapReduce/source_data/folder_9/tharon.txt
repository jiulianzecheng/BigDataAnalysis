 '''''Thāron''''' or '''''Tharol''''' is an ancient Meitei literary work ([[Puya (Meitei texts)|puya]]), about the lore of the twelve [[lunar]] [[month]]s of a year. It describes the changing pattern of nature in the following months. It is one of the masterpieces of the writers of the ancient times. < ref > {{cite book| last = Singh| first = Ch Manihar| title = A History of Manipuri Literature| url = https://books.google.com/books?id=yiBkAAAAMAAJ| year = 1996| publisher = Sahitya Akademi| isbn = 978-81-260-0086-9 }} < /ref > < ref name =  " c276DwAAQBAJ > {{cite book| last1 = Meitei| first1 = Sanjenbam Yaiphaba| last2 = Chaudhuri| first2 = Sarit K.| last3 = Arunkumar| first3 = M.C.| title = The Cultural Heritage of Manipur| url = https://books.google.com/books?id=c276DwAAQBAJ & pg=PA136| year = 2020| publisher = Routledge| isbn = 978-1-00-029637-2| page = 136 }} < /ref > < ref > {{cite book| last = Singh| first = Moirangthem Kirti| title = Folk Culture of Manipur| url = https://books.google.com/books?id=q5eBAAAAMAAJ| year = 1993| publisher = Manas Publications| isbn = 978-81-7049-063-0 }} < /ref > 
 
 The terms,  " Tha "  means month or moon and  " ron " , derived from  " lon "  means knowledge, lore or tradition. < ref name= " c276DwAAQBAJ " / >
