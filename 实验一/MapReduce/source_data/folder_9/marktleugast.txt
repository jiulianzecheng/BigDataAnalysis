 '''Marktleugast''' is a municipality in [[Kulmbach (district)|Kulmbach]] in [[Bavaria]] in [[Germany]]. 
 
 == Geography == 
 Marktleugast is on the southeastern edge of the [[Franconian Forest|Frankenwald]] Nature Park.
