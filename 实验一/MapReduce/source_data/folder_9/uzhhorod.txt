 '''Uzhhorod''' ([[Ukrainian language|Ukrainian]] and {{lang-rue|У́жгород|Užhorod}}; {{lang-ru|Ужгород|Uschgorod}}; {{lang-hu|Ungvár}}; {{lang-de|Ungwar}}; [[Czech language|Czech]] and {{lang-sk|Užhorod}}; {{lang-yi|אונגוואַר‎|Ungwir}}) is a city in western [[Ukraine]], at the border with [[Slovakia]] and near the border with [[Hungary]]. It is the [[administrative centre]] of [[Zakarpattia Oblast]]. In 2020, 115,512 people lived there. 
 
 The historical name of the city is Hungarian, Ungvár. The Ukrainian name Uzhhorod is a recent construct, and has been used only since the beginning of the 20th century.
