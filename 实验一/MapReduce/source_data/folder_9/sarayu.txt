 '''Sarayu''' is a river which flows in [[Uttar Pradesh]] of [[India]]. Sarayu river originates in [[Himalaya]]. It flows across 350 & nbsp;km and merges into the [[Ganga]] river. Sarayu river is mentioned in [[Ramayana]]. Ramayana mention that city of [[Ayodhya]] was on the bank of Sarayu river.
