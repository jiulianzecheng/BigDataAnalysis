 }}'''Hammelburg''' is a town in the [[Bad Kissingen (district)|district of Bad Kissingen]], in [[Lower Franconia]], [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. It is on the river [[Franconian Saale]], 25 & nbsp;km west of [[Schweinfurt]]. 
 
 == In popular culture == 
 
 === TV Series === 
 In [[:en:Hogan's_Heroes|Hogan's Heroes]] the fictional  ''[[:en:Stalag|Luft Stalag]]'' 13 is located just outside of Hammelburg with the protaganist visiting the town on numerous occasions.
