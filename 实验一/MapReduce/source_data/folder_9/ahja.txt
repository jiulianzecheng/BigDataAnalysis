 '''Ahja''' is a small borough (''{{lang|et|alevik}}'') in Põlva Parish, [[Põlva County]] in southeastern [[Estonia]]. < ref > {{cite web|title=NGA GeoNames Database|url=http://geonames.nga.mil/ggmagaz/|access-date=2008-07-09|publisher=[[National Geospatial-Intelligence Agency]]}} < /ref >  Called after the [[Ahja River]], it is located 191  & nbsp;km & nbsp; southeast of [[Tallinn]] and about 16km north of Põlva.
