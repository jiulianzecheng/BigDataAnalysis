 " '''Levitating''' "  is a song by English recording artist [[Dua Lipa]], from her second studio album, ''[[Future Nostalgia]]'' (2020). The song was released as a remix with American rapper [[DaBaby]] on October 1, 2020.  It was written by Dua Lipa, Sarah Hudson, Clarence Coffee Jr., and KOZ, who produced the song with Stuart Price. < ref > {{cite web |title=Dua Lipa – Levitating |url=https://genius.com/Dua-lipa-levitating-lyrics#song-info |website=Genius |access-date=22 May 2021}} < /ref > 
 
 It is a [[Funk music|funk]] < ref > {{cite web |last1=Bernas |first1=Daniel |title=“Future Nostalgia” is the Sound of Tomorrow’s Past |url=https://unewsonline.com/2020/03/future-nostalgia-is-the-sound-of-tomorrows-past/ |website=The University News |access-date=22 May 2021}} < /ref >  and [[disco]]-[[boogie]] song < ref > {{cite web |title=Levitating (feat. DaBaby) - Single by Dua Lipa |url=https://music.apple.com/us/album/levitating-feat-dababy-single/1532788351 |website=Apple Music |access-date=22 May 2021 |date=2020-10-01}} < /ref >  that runs for three minutes and 24 seconds. 
 
 The DaBaby remix of the song has peaked at number one on the [[Canadian Hot 100]] < ref > {{cite web |title=Billboard Canadian Hot 100 Chart |url=https://www.billboard.com/charts/canadian-hot-100/2021-05-15 |website=Billboard |access-date=22 May 2021}} < /ref >  and at number two on the [[Billboard Hot 100|''Billboard'' Hot 100]] in the US, < ref > {{cite web |title=Levitating by Dua Lipa Featuring DaBaby {{!}} Billboard The Hot 100 Chart |url=https://www.billboard.com/charts/hot-100?rank=2 |website=Billboard |access-date=22 May 2021}} < /ref >  tying her peak there with  " [[Don't Start Now]] " .  After DaBaby allegedly made homophobic remarks at a concert, his credit was removed from the remix on the Hot 100. < ref > {{Cite web|date=2021-08-26|title=DaBaby's credit on Dua Lipa's 'Levitating' is now absent from Billboard Hot 100|url=http://attitude.co.uk/article/dababys-credit-on-dua-lipas-levitating-now-absent-from-billboard-hot-100-listing-1/25692/|access-date=2022-09-15|website=Attitude.co.uk|language=en|archive-date=2022-09-15|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20220915192804/https://attitude.co.uk/article/dababys-credit-on-dua-lipas-levitating-now-absent-from-billboard-hot-100-listing-1/25692/|url-status=dead}} < /ref >   
 
 " Levitating "  topped the US year-end Hot 100 for 2021, marking the first time in 20 years that a song that did not reach number one on the weekly chart topped the year-end chart, since Lifehouse's  " Hanging by a Moment "  in 2001. < ref > {{Cite web|last=Trust|first=Gary|last2=Caulfield|first2=Keith|date=2021-12-02|title=The Year in Charts 2021: Dua Lipa’s ‘Levitating’ Is the No. 1 Billboard Hot 100 Song of the Year|url=https://www.billboard.com/music/chart-beat/dua-lipa-levitating-2021-hot-100-top-song-year-in-charts-1235004941/|access-date=2021-12-04|website=Billboard|language=en-US}} < /ref >  It also topped the year-end Canadian Hot 100 for 2021. < ref > {{Cite web|date=2013-01-02|title=Canadian Hot 100|url=https://www.billboard.com/charts/year-end/canadian-hot-100/|access-date=2021-12-04|website=Billboard|language=en-US}} < /ref >  It also became the longest-running song in the top ten by a lead female in ''Billboard'' history, breaking the record set by [[LeAnn Rimes]]'  " [[How Do I Live]] " . < ref > {{Cite web|date=2021-08-26|title=DaBaby's credit on Dua Lipa's 'Levitating' is now absent from Billboard Hot 100|url=http://attitude.co.uk/article/dababys-credit-on-dua-lipas-levitating-now-absent-from-billboard-hot-100-listing-1/25692/|access-date=2022-09-15|website=Attitude.co.uk|language=en|archive-date=2022-09-15|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20220915192804/https://attitude.co.uk/article/dababys-credit-on-dua-lipas-levitating-now-absent-from-billboard-hot-100-listing-1/25692/|url-status=dead}} < /ref >  It also charted for 65 weeks, the longest-charting song by a female in ''Billboard'' history. < ref > {{Cite web|title='Levitating': Dua Lipa Makes History with Decade's First Diamond Hit by a Female Vocalist [RIAA] - That Grape Juice|url=https://thatgrapejuice.net/2023/11/levitating-dua-lipa-makes-history-with-decades-first-diamond-hit-by-female-vocalist-riaa/|access-date=2023-11-11|website=thatgrapejuice.net}} < /ref > 
 
 On November 10, 2023, the Recording Industry Association of America (RIAA) announced that  " Levitating "  was certified Diamond in the US for shipments and streams equal to 10 million copies sold there. < ref > {{Cite web|title='Levitating': Dua Lipa Makes History with Decade's First Diamond Hit by a Female Vocalist [RIAA] - That Grape Juice|url=https://thatgrapejuice.net/2023/11/levitating-dua-lipa-makes-history-with-decades-first-diamond-hit-by-female-vocalist-riaa/|access-date=2023-11-11|website=thatgrapejuice.net}} < /ref >
