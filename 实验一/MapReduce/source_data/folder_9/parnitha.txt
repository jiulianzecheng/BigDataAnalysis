 '''Parnitha''' is a [[mountain]] of [[Greece]]. It is in the south of Greece, in [[Attica]] peninsula. This mountain is the highest mountain in [[Attica]], 1,413 [[meter]]s high. The mountain is covered by dense [[vegetation]]. In the lower points is covered by [[Pine]] [[forest]]s and in the higher points by Greek-[[fir]] forests. In the summer of 2007 a big part of forest was burnt because of a destructive [[wildfire]]. < ref > [http://news.bbc.co.uk/2/hi/europe/6252676.stm BBC, the fire in Parnitha mountain] < /ref >
