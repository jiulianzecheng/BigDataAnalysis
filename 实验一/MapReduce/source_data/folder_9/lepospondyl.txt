 The '''Lepospondyli''' are a large group of [[batrachomorpha|batrachomorph]] [[Tetrapod|tetrapods]] from the [[Palaeozoic]] [[era]]. They are sometimes included in the [[Labyrinthodont]]s.  
 
 They lived during the [[Carboniferous]] to the early [[Permian]]. There are six different groups. The [[Acherontiscidae]], [[Adelospondyli]], [[AÃ¯stopoda]], [[Lysorophia]], [[Microsauria]] and [[Nectridea]]. The included [[species]] which looked somewhat like [[newt]]s, [[eel]]s, [[snake]]s and [[lizard]]s. Some of them were [[aquatic]], semi-aquatic, and [[terrestrial animals]]. None of them were big animals.
