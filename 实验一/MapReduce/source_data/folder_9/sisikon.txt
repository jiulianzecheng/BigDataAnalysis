 '''Sisikon''' is a [[municipality]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Uri (canton)|Uri]] in [[Switzerland]]. It has an [[area]] of {{convert|16.30|km2|abbr=off}}.Sisikon, the gateway to Canton Uri, lies on the delta of the Riemenstalderbach stream on the eastern shore of the Urnersee, the southernmost arm of the Vierwaldstättersee.  < ref > {{Cite web|url=https://www.schweizmobil.ch/en/hiking-in-switzerland/services/places/ort-061.html|title=Sisikon - Wanderland|website=www.schweizmobil.ch|language=en|access-date=2020-04-21}} < /ref >
