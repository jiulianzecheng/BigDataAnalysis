 '''Wank''' is a [[mountain]] in the Bavarian Alps, by Garmisch-Partenkirchen in [[Germany]], close to the [[Austria]]n border. 
 Wank is 1780[[metre|m]] tall, and has a [[cable car]], the ''Wankbahn'', which takes people almost all the way to the top.  Near the peak, there is an inn called the ''Wankhaus'' which was built in [[1911]] that allows people to spend the night.
