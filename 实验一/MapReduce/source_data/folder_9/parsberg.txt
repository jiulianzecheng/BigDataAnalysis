 '''Parsberg''' is a town in [[Neumarkt (district)|Neumarkt]] in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. It is 23 & nbsp;km southeast of [[Neumarkt in der Oberpfalz]], and 33 & nbsp;km northwest of [[Regensburg]], next to the [[Autobahn|A3 autobahn]], the main route from [[Nuremberg]] to Regensburg (and then on into [[Austria]]).  It was formerly the seat of the [[Count]]s of Parsberg, whose [[coat of arms]] is now used by the town.
