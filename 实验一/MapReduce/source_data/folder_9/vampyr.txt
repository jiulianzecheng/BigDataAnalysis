 '''''Vampyr''''' is a 1932 German Danish French horror movie directed by [[Carl Theodor Dreyer]] and based on the 1872 story by [[Sheridan Le Frau]]. It stars [[Nicolas de Gunzburg]], [[Rena Mandel]], [[Sybille Schmitz]], [[Maurice Schutz]].
