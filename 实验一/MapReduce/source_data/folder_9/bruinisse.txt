 '''Bruinisse''', [[Zeelandic dialect|Zeelandic]]: ''Bru'', is a village in the municipality-island of [[Schouwen-Duiveland]], in the Province of [[Zeeland]], the [[Netherlands]]. 
 
 About 4,000 people are living there. It is located on the very east of the island. 
 
 Bruinisse is an agricultural and fishing community. Breeding of the [[mussel|blue mussel]] is an important business there.
