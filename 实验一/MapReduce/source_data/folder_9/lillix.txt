 '''Lillix''' (formerly '''Tigerlily''') was a [[Canada|Canadian]] [[pop rock]] [[band]] from [[Cranbrook, British Columbia|Cranbrook]], [[British Columbia]]. Lillex has five members in the band; the current band members are Tasha-Ray Evin, Lacey-Lee Evin, Eric Hoodicoff, Cameron Brass and Alex Varon. They released their third studio album, [[Tigerlily (Lillix album)|Tigerlily]] on August 24, 2010.
