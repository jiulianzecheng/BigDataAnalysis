 '''Lumbrein''' was a [[municipality]] of the [[district]] [[Surselva (district)|Surselva]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Lucerne (canton)|Lucerne]] in [[Switzerland]]. The municipalities of [[Cumbel]], [[Degen, Switzerland|Degen]], Lumbrein, [[Morissen]], [[Suraua]], [[Vignogn]], [[Vella]], and [[Vrin]] merged on 1 January 2013, into the new municipality of [[Lumnezia]].
