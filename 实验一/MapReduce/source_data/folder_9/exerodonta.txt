 '''''Exerodonta''''' is a genus of [[frog]]s in the family [[Hylidae]]. Scientists stopped using this genus many years ago, but they started using it again in 2005.  This was part of a larger scientific project to look at all the frogs in Hylidae [http://digitallibrary.amnh.org/dspace/bitstream/2246/462/1/B294.pdf] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20070221131729/http://digitallibrary.amnh.org/dspace/bitstream/2246/462/1/B294.pdf |date=2007-02-21 }}. Eleven species that had been in the genus ''[[Hyla]]'' were moved to this genus. They all live in south-central Mexico.
