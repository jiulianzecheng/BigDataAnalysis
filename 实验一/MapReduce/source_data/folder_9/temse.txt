 '''Temse''' is a [[municipality]] in [[Belgium]]. It is in the [[province]] of [[East Flanders]], in the region of [[Flemish region|Flanders]]. There are four [[town]]s in the municipality: Temse, [[Elversele]], [[Steendorp]], and [[Tielrode]]. In 2007, 26667 people lived there. < ref > {{Cite web |url=http://statbel.fgov.be/downloads/pop200701com.xls |title=Werkelijke bevolking per gemeente op 1 januari 2007 / Population de droit par commune au 1 janvier 2007 (.xls) |access-date=2008-07-15 |archive-date=2008-11-23 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20081123144002/http://statbel.fgov.be/downloads/pop200701com.xls |url-status=dead }} < /ref >  It is at 51° 07 North, 04° 12 East. < ref > {{Cite web |url=http://statbel.fgov.be/pub/d1/p120_nl.xls |title=Geografische coördinaten van de gemeenten (.xls) |access-date=2008-07-15 |archive-date=2004-07-23 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20040723190930/http://statbel.fgov.be/pub/d1/p120_nl.xls |url-status=dead }} < /ref > 
 
 The name ''Temse'' comes from the [[Gaul]] ([[Gallo-Roman]]) word ''Tamisiacum'' or ''Tamasiacum''. Until the early 20th century (1900s), the French name of the city was used: ''Tamise''.
