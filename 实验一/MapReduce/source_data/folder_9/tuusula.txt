 '''Tuusula''' is a [[town]] in [[Finland]]. It is in the province of [[Southern Finland]] and is part of the [[Uusimaa]]. Neighbouring municipalities are [[Vantaa]], [[Nurmijärvi]], [[Hyvinkää]], [[Sipoo]], [[Järvenpää]] and [[Kerava]].
