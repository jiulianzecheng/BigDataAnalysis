 '''Midco''' (until 2016, was called '''Midcontinent Communications''') is a regional cable provider. They give a [[Triple play (telecommunications)|triple play]] service of [[Cable television]], [[Cable Internet access|Internet]] and [[Cable telephony|telephone]] service. They give this service for [[North Dakota]], [[South Dakota]], some of [[Minnesota]], and some cities in [[Kansas]] and [[Wisconsin]]. < ref > {{cite web|title=Midcontinent Communications Overview and Coverage|url=http://broadbandnow.com/Midcontinent-Communications|publisher=broadbandnow.com|access-date=2015-06-01}} < /ref >
