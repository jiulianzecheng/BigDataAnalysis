 '''Pignia''' is a former [[municipality]] of the [[district]] [[Hinterrhein (district)|Hinterrhein]] in the [[Switzerland|Swiss]] [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Graubünden]]. Since 2009, it was part of the municipality of [[Andeer]].
