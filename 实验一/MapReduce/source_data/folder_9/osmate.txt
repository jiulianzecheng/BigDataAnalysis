'''Osmate''' was a ''[[comune]]'' in the [[Province of Varese]] in the [[Lombardy]] region in [[Italy]]. 
 
 Since 2019, the former municipalities of [[Cadrezzate]] and Osmate merged to form the new municipality of [[Cadrezzate con Osmate]].
