 '''Windsbach''' ({{IPA-de|ˈvɪnt͡sˌbax|3=De-Windsbach.ogg}}) is a town in [[Ansbach (district)|Ansbach]], a district ([[Districts of Germany|''Landkreis'']]) in [[Middle Franconia]]. It is {{convert|20|km|abbr=off}} east of [[Ansbach]], and {{convert|29|km|abbr=off}} southwest of [[Nuremberg]]. 
 
 == Geography == 
 
 === Location === 
 Windsbach is in the Rangau area at the Franconian Rezat River. The city's position is {{convert|20|km|abbr=off}} eastward of the district capital [[Ansbach]] and {{convert|40|km|abbr=off}} southwestern of [[Nuremberg]]. Beside the Franconian Rezat River the [[Aurach (Rednitz)|Aurach]], a side arm of the [[Rednitz]] River flows through the community area. 
 
 The following communities surround Windsbach (beginning north going clockwise direction): [[Rohr (Mittelfranken)|Rohr]], [[Kammerstein]], [[Abenberg]], [[Spalt (Stadt)|Spalt]], [[Mitteleschenbach]], [[Wolframs-Eschenbach]], [[Lichtenau (Mittelfranken)|Lichtenau]], [[Neuendettelsau]] and [[Heilsbronn]].
