 '''Kulmbach''' is the capital of the district of [[Kulmbach (district)|Kulmbach]] in [[Bavaria]] in [[Germany]].  The town is famous for [[Plassenburg]] Castle, which houses the largest tin soldier museum in the world, and for its  sausages, or ''[[Bratwürste]]''. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 
 ===Place=== 
 Kulmbach is in the middle of the Bavarian province of [[Upper Franconia]], about {{convert|25|km|1|abbr=off}} northwest of the city of [[Bayreuth]]. To the south of Kulmbach, the River [[Main (river)|Main]] begins at the convergence of its headstreams, the [[White Main]] and  [[Red Main]].
