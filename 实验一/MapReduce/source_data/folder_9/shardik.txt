 '''''Shardik''''' is a [[fantasy]] novel written by [[Richard Adams]] in [[1974]]. 
 
 ==Plot== 
 ''Shardik'' takes place in an [[:wikt:imaginary|imaginary]] region called the Beklan Empire.  A lonely hunter, Kelderek, pursues Shardik, a giant bear. Both of them become unwillingly drawn into the politics of the entire empire. The story's key themes include the personal struggles of [[sin]] and [[atonement]], the strength and potential of children, and the responsibility of [[adult]]s to protect and nurture children in hope of a better future.
