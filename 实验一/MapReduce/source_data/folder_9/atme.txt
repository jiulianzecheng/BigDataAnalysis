'''Atme''' ({{lang-ar|أطمة}}, also spelled '''Atma''', '''Atima''', '''Atmeh''') is a town in northern [[Syria]], part of the [[Idlib Governorate]]. It is  located north of [[Idlib]] and just east of the [[Syria–Turkey border|border]] with [[Turkey]]. In the 2004 census, it had a population of 2,255. < ref name= " CBS " > [http://www.cbssyr.org/new%20web%20site/General_census/census_2004/NH/TAB07-15-2004.htm General Census of Population and Housing 2004] {{webarchive|url=https://archive.today/20121220111907/http://www.cbssyr.org/new%20web%20site/General_census/census_2004/NH/TAB07-15-2004.htm |date=2012-12-20 }}.
