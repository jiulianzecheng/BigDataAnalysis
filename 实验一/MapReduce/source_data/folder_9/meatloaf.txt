 '''Meatloaf''' is a dish of [[ground meat]] formed into a [[wikt:loaf|loaf]] shape, mixed with other ingredients, then [[baking|baked]] or [[smoking (cooking)|smoked]]. Meatloaf is usually made from [[ground beef]], although [[Lamb and mutton|lamb]], [[pork]], [[veal]], [[venison]], and [[poultry]] are also used.
