 '''Korkeasaari''' ({{lang-sv|Högholmen}}) is an island in [[Helsinki]], [[Finland]]. The exact meaning of Korkeasaari is  " Tall Island/Islet " . It is part of the [[Mustikkamaa–Korkeasaari]] district.  It has Finland's largest and oldest [[zoo]], called [[Korkeasaari Zoo]]. 
 
 The island of Korkeasaari is a {{Convert|22|ha|sing=on}} rocky island. Two smaller islands are located next to it: ''Hylkysaari'' and ''Palosaari''.
