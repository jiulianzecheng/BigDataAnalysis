 '''Sare''' is a [[Commune in France|commune]] of the [[Pyrénées-Atlantiques]] ''[[departments of France|département]]'' in the southwestern part of [[France]]. 
 
 It is about {{convert|15|km|abbr=on}} to the east of [[Saint-Jean-de-Luz]] and the [[Atlantic Ocean]].  It is in the western foothills of the [[Pyrenees]].
