 '''''Mermaids''''' is a 1990 [[United States|American]] [[comedy]]-[[drama movie]]. It set during 1963 in [[Massachusetts]]. It is based on a [[novel]] of the same name. The movie stars [[Cher]] as Rachel, [[Winona Ryder]] as her elder daughter Charlotte and [[Christina Ricci]] as her younger daughter Kate. This movie was released December 14, 1990 in the U.S. It got positive [[review]]s from movie [[critic]]s.
