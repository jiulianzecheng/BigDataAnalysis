 The '''Nanticoke people''' are [[Native American]] people from present-day [[Chesapeake Bay]] and [[Delaware]]. They are part of the [[Algonquian languages|Algonquian]] language group. The [[Nanticoke language]] is an extinct language. They are part of the [[Indigenous peoples of the Northeastern Woodlands|Northeastern Woodland]] < nowiki/ > s culture. Their tribes include the [[Choptank people|Choptank]], the [[Assateague people|Assateague]], the [[Piscataway people|Piscataway]], and the [[Doeg people|Doeg]]. The Nanticoke live in many places today. They include the [[Northeastern United States]] and [[Canada]], [[Delaware]], [[Ontario]] and [[Oklahoma]]. Some Nanticoke people are recognized by states and governments. This includes the [[Six Nations of the Grand River First Nation]].
