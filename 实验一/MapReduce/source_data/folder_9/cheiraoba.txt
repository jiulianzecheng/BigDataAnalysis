 '''Meitei Cheiraoba''' or '''Sajibu Cheiraoba''' or '''Sajibu Nongma Panba''' or '''Meitei Manipuri New Year Festival''' is a festival celebrated by the [[Meitei ethnicity]] in the 1st lunar day of the '' " Sajibu " '' month of the [[Meitei calendar]] ([[Manipuri calendar]]).
