 '''Henjunaha Lairoulembi''' ({{lang-omp|Hensunaha Lailoulempi}}), or simply known as '''Henjunaha''', is a folk tale in the ancient [[Moirang Kangleirol]] legends of [[Meitei mythology|Meitei Manipuri mythology]] and [[Meitei folklore|folklore]]. < ref name=Devi > {{cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=kqJVDwAAQBAJ & dq=henjunaha & pg=PT81|author= Nunglekpam Premi Devi|title=A Glimpse of Manipuri Literary Works|accessdate=July 2, 2021|publisher=Media Publications|date=April 14, 2018 }} < /ref > < ref name=Singh > {{cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=S_4dAAAAMAAJ & q=henjunaha+fabulous+account|title=The History of Manipur: An early period|author=Wahengbam Ibohal Singh|page=440|publisher=Manipur Commercial Company|year=1986}} < /ref > < ref name=Imphal > {{cite news|title=Henjunaha, Masculinity Redefined|author=Rubani Yumkhaibam|date=November 10, 2019|url=https://www.imphaltimes.com/it-articles/item/16010-henjunaha-masculinity-redefined|publisher=Imphal Times|accessdate=July 2, 2021|archive-date=August 12, 2022|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20220812234552/https://www.imphaltimes.com/it-articles/item/16010-henjunaha-masculinity-redefined|url-status=dead}} < /ref >  It is the love story of ''Henjunaha'' and his lady love ''Lairoulembi'', who are the ideal lovers in the second line of re[[incarnation]] in the epic of [[Ancient Moirang|Moirang kingdom]]. < ref name=Devi / > < ref name=Singh / > < ref name=Pao1 > {{cite web|url=http://e-pao.net/epSubPageExtractor.asp?src=manipur.Folks.Folk_Tales.Henjunaha_Part_1_Folktale_By_James_Oinam|title=Henjunaha: Part 1|author=K.B.  The legend was first reproduced in the written form during the reign of Maharaja Bhagyachandra, the King of [[Medieval Manipur]]. < ref name=Devi / > 
 
 ==In later versions== 
 * In 2010, ''Henjunaha'', a drama, was written by K. B. Sharma, based on the story of the hero. < ref name=Sharma > {{cite video|url=https://archive.org/details/in.ernet.dli.2015.466018/mode/2up|author=K.B. Sharma|title=Henjunaha|accessdate=July 2, 2021|year=2010|publisher= Digital Library of India}} < /ref >   
 * In 2012, a book ''Henjunaha Lairoulembi'', was published in the Manipur Hindi Parishad, Imphal. It's based on the saga of the hero Henjunaha and his lady love Lairoulembi. < ref > {{cite web|publisher=Manipur News|title=Book on Henjunaha|date=August 29, 2012|accessdate=July 2, 2021|url=http://www.manipur.org/news/2012/08/29/book-on-henjunaha/}} < /ref > 
 * In 2017, a folk play named  " Henjunaha "  premiered, based on the saga of the legendary hero. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://kanglaonline.com/2017/04/henjunaha-folk-play-premiered/|title='Henjunaha' folk play premiered|date=April 19, 2017|publisher=Kangla Online|accessdate=July 2, 2021}} < /ref >
