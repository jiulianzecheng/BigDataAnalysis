 '''Kavum''' (Konda Kavum) is a [[Taste#Sweetness|sweet]] food often eaten in [[Sri Lanka]]. It may also be known as 'oil cakes'. They are made on the [[Sinhalese]] New Year's Day and on other special, traditional occasions. The main ingredients are [[rice]] [[flour]] and [[treacle]], which are [[fried]] together.
