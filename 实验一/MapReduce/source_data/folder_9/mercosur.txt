 '''Mercosur''' or '''Mercosul''' ({{lang-es|Mercado Común del Sur}}, {{lang-pt|Mercado Comum do Sul}}, Guarani: ''Ñemby Ñemuha'', {{lang-en|Southern Common Market}}) is a [[Regional Trade Agreement]] (RTA) between [[Argentina]], [[Brazil]], [[Paraguay]] and [[Uruguay]]. It was founded in 1991. Its purpose is to improve [[free trade]] and the easy movement of [[Product (consumer goods)|goods]], people, and [[currency]].
