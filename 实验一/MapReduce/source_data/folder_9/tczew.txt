 '''Tczew''' ({{IPAc-pl|AUD|Pl-Tczew.ogg|t|cz|e|f}}, {{lang-csb|Dërszewò}}; formerly {{Audio-de|Dirschau|Dirschau.ogg}}) is a city on the [[Vistula]] River in [[Pomerelia|Eastern Pomerania]], [[Kociewie]], northern Poland. In 2009, about 60,279 people lived here. The city played a key role in the [[Invasion of Poland]] during [[World War II]].
