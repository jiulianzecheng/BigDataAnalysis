 '''Malubiting ''' is a {{convert|7458|m|ft}} [[mountain]] in the [[Karakoram]] [[Mountain range|range]], in [[Pakistan]]. < ref name= " peakbagger " > {{cite peakbagger|pid=18676|name=Malubiting, Pakistan|accessdate=26 November 2019}} < /ref >  Malubiting is part of the [[Rakaposhi-Haramosh Mountains|Rakaposhi-Haramosh]] subrange. It is the 56th highest mountain in the world. < ref name= " peakbagger "  / > 
 
 The first people to reach the top were Horst Schindlbacher and his expedition, in 1971. < ref > {{cite peakware |id=5647 |name=Malubiting |accessdate=26 November 2019}} < /ref > < ref > {{cite magazine |last1=Schindlbacher |first1=Horst |editor-last=Mehta |editor-first=Soli S. |title=The First Ascent of Malubiting, 1971 |url=https://www.himalayanclub.org/hj/32/13/the-first-ascent-of-malubiting-1971/ |magazine=The Himalayan Journal |publisher=The Himalayan Club |volume=32 |year=1974 |access-date=26 November 2019}} < /ref >
