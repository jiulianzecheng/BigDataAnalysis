 '''Voorhout''' ({{IPA-nl|ˈvoːrɦʌut|-|Nl-Voorhout.ogg}}) is a village and former [[municipality]] in the western [[Netherlands]]. It is in the province of [[South Holland]]. It had a population of 14,792 in 2004. Together with [[Sassenheim]] and [[Warmond]], it has become part of the [[Teylingen]] municipality on January 1, 2006.
