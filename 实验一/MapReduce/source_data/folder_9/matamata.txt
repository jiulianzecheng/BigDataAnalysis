 '''Matamata''' ({{IPAc-mi|lang|ˈ|m|a|t|a|ˌ|m|a|t|a}}) is a town in the [[Waikato]], [[New Zealand]] and located on State Highway 27. It is part of the [[Matamata-Piako District]] and is near the [[Kaimai Ranges]]. It is the home of [[Hobbit Movie Set]] in the Peter Jackson movie set of ''Lord of the Rings''.
