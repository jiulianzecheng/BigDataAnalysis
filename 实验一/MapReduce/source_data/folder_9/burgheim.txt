 '''Burgheim''' is a [[Municipalities of Germany|municipality]] in [[Neuburg-Schrobenhausen]] in [[Bavaria]] in [[Germany]].  It is a [[market town]]. 
 
 ==Divisions== 
 
 The municipality contains: 
 *Burgheim 
 *[[Biding, Neuburg-Schrobenhausen|Biding]] 
 *[[Dezenacker]] 
 *[[Eschling]] 
 *[[Illdorf]] 
 *[[Kunding]] 
 *[[Leidling]] 
 *[[Längloh]] 
 *[[Moos, Neuburg-Schrobenhausen|Moos]] 
 *[[Ortlfing]] 
 *[[Strass, Neuburg-Schrobenhausen|Straß]] 
 *[[Wengen, Neuburg-Schrobenhausen|Wengen]]
