 '''Monnaz''' was a [[municipality]] in [[Morges (district)|Morges]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Vaud]] in [[Switzerland]]. On 1 July 2011 the municipalities  [[Colombier, Vaud|Colombier]], Monnaz and [[Saint-Saphorin-sur-Morges]] joined together to become a new municipality called [[Echichens]]. < ref name=Fusion > [http://www.bfs.admin.ch/bfs/portal/de/index/infothek/nomenklaturen/blank/blank/gem_liste/03.html Amtliches Gemeindeverzeichnis der Schweiz] published by the Swiss Federal Statistical Office {{in lang|de}} accessed 17 February 2011 < /ref >
