'''Meehanite''' is a form of [[iron]] made through a process invented by [[Meehanite Worldwide]]. When a [[foundry]] produces Meehanite, it means that Meehanite Worldwide has allowed them to use their method. 
 
 The process was invented in the 1930's when the founder of Meehanite Worldwide discovered you can use [[Ionic compound|Calcium Silicide]] to make very pure iron.
