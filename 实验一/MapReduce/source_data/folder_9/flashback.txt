A '''flashback''' is a [[Skill|technique]] used by [[writer]]s which is often used in [[movies]], [[television]] and [[literature]]. It involves a [[Fictional character|character]] remembering something that happened to him in the past.  ''[[Scrubs (TV series)|Scrubs]]'' is a [[television programme|television show]] that uses ''flashbacks'' often. 
 
 The term is also used [[colloquialism|colloquially]], to describe lingering effects of a drug (such as [[lysergic acid diethylamide|LSD]]), or memories of a bad experience triggered by things in the present.
