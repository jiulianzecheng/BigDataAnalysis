 '''Venison''' is the meat of a [[game animal]], normally a  [[elk]] or [[deer]]. < ref > {{cite web|title=Webster Dictionary|url=http://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/venison}} < /ref > 
 
 Venison may be eaten as [[steak]]s, [[Beef tenderloin|tenderloins]], [[Roasting|roasts]], [[sausage]]s, [[Jerky (food)|jerky]] and [[Ground meat|minced meat]]. Venison has less fat than similar cuts of beef.  However, like beef, leaner cuts can be tougher as well.  Venison can be [[kosher]].
