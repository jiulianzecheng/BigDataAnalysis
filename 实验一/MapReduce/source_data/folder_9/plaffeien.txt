 '''Plaffeien''' ({{lang-fr|Planfayon}}) is a [[municipality]] of the district of [[Sense (district)|Sense]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Fribourg (canton)|Fribourg]] in [[Switzerland]]. On 1 January 2017, the former municipalities of [[Oberschrot]] and [[Zumholz]] merged into the municipality of Plaffeien. < ref > [https://www.bfs.admin.ch/bfs/de/home/grundlagen/agvch.html Amtliches Gemeindeverzeichnis der Schweiz] {{in lang|de}} accessed 10 January 2017 < /ref > 
 
 It is the third-largest municipality in the canton of Fribourg.
