 '''Elisabeth Corrin Maurus''' (born November 21, 1982), better known by her [[stage name]] '''Lissie''', is an American [[country music]]ian and singer-songwriter.  
 
 Lissie was raised in [[Rock Island, Illinois|Rock Island]], [[Illinois]].  
 
 == Debut EP == 
 ''Why You Running'' contains the single  " Oh Mississippi "  which was co-written by Ed Harcourt, and she met a mutual friend.  " Oh Mississppi "  was released on Fat Possum. Lissie has also toured with Band of Horses and Badly Drawn Boy.  
 
 == Early life == 
 Elisabeth Corrin Maurus was born on 21 November 1982 in the [[United States]]. Lissie moved to [[Ojai, California|Ojai]], [[California]], after when she left Colorado. She wanted to live closer to [[Los Angeles]], and said  " I'll live very close to L.A. "  She loved [[Los Angeles|LA]].
