 '''Avaneeswaram''' is a small [[village]] located on [[Kunnicode]]-[[Pathanapuram]] road in [[Kollam]] district, [[Kerala]]. It is about 7 & nbsp;kilometers south of [[Pathanapuram]] town and 36 & nbsp;kilometers from [[Kollam]]. It is a part of Vilakkudy panchayat. Avaneeswaram has a FCI (Food corporation of India) storage facility. Economy of this village primarily is agricultural.  Most common farm produce includes coconut, tapioca, areca, and cashews. Some [[rice]] cultivation is also seen. 
 
 ==Transport== 
 The roads to Thalavoor and Pattazhy pass through Avaneesaram. Avaneeswaram is connected to [[Kollam]] city by Kollam-Punalur Broad gauge railway line.
