 '''Montboudif''' is a [[communes of France|commune]]. It is in [[Auvergne-Rhône-Alpes]] in the [[Cantal]] [[Departments of France|department]] in south-central [[France]]. It is the birthplace of [[Georges Pompidou]], [[President of France]] from 1969 to his death.
