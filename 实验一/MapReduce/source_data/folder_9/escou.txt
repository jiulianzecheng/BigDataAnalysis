'''Escou''' is a [[Commune in France|commune]] of the [[Pyrénées-Atlantiques]] ''[[departments of France|département]]'' in the southwestern part of [[France]]. It is notable for a [[:en:Grape|type of grape]] that it is believed by the locals to have healing properties.
