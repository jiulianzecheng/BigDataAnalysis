 '''''Megatherium''''' was a huge, bulky, slow-moving mammal that lived during the [[Pleistocene]] epoch in [[South America]]. It went extinct about 11,000 years ago. This [[Ice age|ice-age]] mammal was the largest of the [[ground sloth]]s; Megatherium's name means  " great beast. "  Megatherium was named by paleontologist [[Richard Owen]] in 1856; the first Megatherium fossil was found in [[Argentina]] in 1789.
