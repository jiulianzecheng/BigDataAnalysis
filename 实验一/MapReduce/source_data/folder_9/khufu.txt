
 '''Khufu''' was a [[Pharaoh]] (or leader) of [[Ancient Egypt]]'s [[Old Kingdom]]. He was the Pharaoh of Egypt from around 2589 to 2566 B.C.E . Khufu was the second pharaoh of the Fourth dynasty.  He is also thought to be the one who built the [[Great Pyramid of Giza]], one of the [[Seven Wonders of the Ancient World]].
