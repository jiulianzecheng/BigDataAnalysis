 '''Sastamala''' is a city in [[Pirkanmaa]], [[Finland]]. As of January 2014, almost 25,500 people lived there. Nearby municipalities are [[Huittinen]], [[Hämeenkyrö]], [[Ikaalinen]], [[Kankaanpää]], [[Kokemäki]] , [[Lavia]], [[Nokia, Finland|Nokia]], [[Punkalaidun]], [[Ulvila]], [[Urjala]] and [[Vesilahti]]. 
 
 == Some villages == 
 Sileekallio, Hoipola, Jaara, Häijää and Mustianoja  
 
 == Culture == 
 In July 2011 in Sastamala was opened Museum called Pukstaavi ''Suomalaisen kirjan museo'', museum of Finish book.  Museum is national and tells generally about culture history and social meaning of Finnish book.  
 
 ==Sights== 
 * River Kokemäenjoki 
 * Car and tractor museum
