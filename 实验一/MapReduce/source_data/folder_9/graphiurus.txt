 The '''African dormouse''', '''''Graphiurus''''',  is a [[genus]] of [[dormouse]] that lives throughout [[sub-Saharan Africa]] in different [[habitats]].  They are good at climbing. They have bushy tails.  They eat [[invertebrates]] and small [[vertebrate]]s. < ref > Kingdon, Jonathan  2004. ''The Kingdon pocket guide to African mammals, '' Russel Friedman. {{ISBN|1-875091-14-9}} < /ref >
