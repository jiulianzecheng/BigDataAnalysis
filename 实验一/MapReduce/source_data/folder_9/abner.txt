'''Abner''' is a [[Hebrew language|Hebrew]] name that signifies  " father of light "  or  " my father is a light. "  It has been commonly used by Jewish families for many generations. The name Abner appears multiple times in the Bible and is linked with qualities like bravery, power, and guidance. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Abner name Meaning and Origin|url=https://babys.name/detail-Abner-Meaning-Origin-Famous-Similar-Sibling.html|access-date=2023-04-13|website=babys.name}} < /ref > 
 
 == People == 
 
 * Abner Coburn (1803–1885), 30th governor of Maine 
 * Abner Cole (1783–1835), American newspaper editor 
 * Abner Cook (1814-1884), Texas architect and general contractor
