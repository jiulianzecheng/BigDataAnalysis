 }}'''Lumen Technologies, Inc. (formerly CenturyLink)''' is an [[United States|American]] [[company]] from [[Monroe, Louisiana]]. It provides [[internet]], [[cable television]] and [[telephone service]] to many areas of the United States. < ref > {{cite web|title=CenturyLink Overview|publisher=Broadband Now|url=https://broadbandnow.com/CenturyLink|access-date=April 19, 2019}} < /ref > 
 
 The original names for the company were the Oak Ridge Telephone Company, Central Telephone, and Century Telephone. 
 
 CenturyLink was established in 1930 in [[Oak Ridge, Louisiana]].  
 
 CenturyLink completed a merger with [[Qwest]] on Friday, April 1, 2011. < ref > {{cite news|title=CenturyLink|publisher=United States SEC|url=http://edgar.secdatabase.com/1709/1892611000016/filing-main.htm|access-date=April 1, 2019}} < /ref >  CenturyLink was the surviving company.
