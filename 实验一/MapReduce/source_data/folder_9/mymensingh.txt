 '''Mymensingh''' is a city of [[Bangladesh]] on the river [[Brahmaputra River|Brahmaputra]]. It is the capital of [[Mymensingh Division]].  The city is known for institute like [[Bangladesh Agricultural University]], [[Mymensingh Medical College]], [[Mymensingh Engineering College]], [[Ananda Mohan College]], [[Mymensingh Muminunnesa Women College]], Mymensingh Polytechnique Institute and [[Mymensingh Girls Cadet College|Mymensingh Girls' Cadet College]]. Other landmark institutions include Mymensingh Army Cantonment. Mymensingh city is about {{convert|120|km|mi|0|abbr=off}} north of [[Dhaka]] which is the capital of the country.
