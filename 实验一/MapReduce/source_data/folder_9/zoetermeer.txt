 '''Zoetermeer''' is a city in [[South Holland]] in the [[Netherlands]] (it has no [[city rights]]).  
 
 About 125,000 people are living there in 2017. 
 
 It is between [[Utrecht (city)|Utrecht]] and [[Den Haag]]. It is part of the [[Randstad]].
