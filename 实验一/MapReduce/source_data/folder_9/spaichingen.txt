 '''Spaichingen''' is a town of the district of [[Tuttlingen (district)|Tuttlingen]] in the [[States of Germany|state]] of [[Baden-Württemberg]], [[Germany]]. As of 31 December 2018, it has about 13,000 people lived there. 
 
 == Geography == 
 Spaichingen lies on the [[Prim (Neckar)|Prim]] at the foot of the [[Dreifaltigkeitsberg]] (Holy Trinity Mountain). It is {{convert|11|km|abbr=off}} northwest of [[Tuttlingen]], and {{convert|13|km|abbr=off}} southeast of [[Rottweil]]. It has an elevation of {{convert|660|m|ft|abbr=off}} above sea level.
