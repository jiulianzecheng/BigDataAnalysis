 '''Badakhshan''' is a part of the northeast of [[Afghanistan]] and the southeast of [[Tajikistan]]. 
 
 == People == 
 The majority of the people in this area are the [[Pamiri people|Pamiris]] and [[Tajik people|Tajiks]].
