 '''Liden''' is a [[Urban areas in Sweden|locality]] in [[Sundsvall Municipality]] in [[Västernorrland County]] in [[Sweden]]. In 2010, 254 people lived there. < ref name=scb / >  The locality is near the [[Indalen River]]. The locality is located circa 50 &  kilometers (road 86) northwest of the town of [[Sundsvall]].
