'''Bankia''' is a Spanish banking conglomerate formed in December 2010 as an alliance of seven regional savings banks. With 12 million customers (both individuals and legal entities), the bank was the fourth-largest bank in [[Spain]]. < ref > {{cite web|title=Bankia Profile|url=http://www.bankia.com/Portal/Home/cruce/0,0,84682%24P1%3D601,00.html|url-status=dead|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110917010329/http://www.bankia.com/Portal/Home/cruce/0,0,84682$P1=601,00.html|archive-date=17 September 2011|access-date=17 May 2012|publisher=Bankia.com}} < /ref >  In 2012, he experienced great financial problems and was partially nationalized (90% of the shares in exchange for assistance of 23.5 billion euros). In 2021, the bank was taken over by CaixaBank.
