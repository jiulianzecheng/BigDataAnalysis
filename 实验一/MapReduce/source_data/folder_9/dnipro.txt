 '''Dnipro''' ({{lang-uk|Дніпро}}, {{lang-ru|Днепр}}, '''Dnepr'''), called '''Dnipropetrovsk''' until May 2016 ({{lang-uk|Дніпропетро́вськ}} {{IPA-uk|ˌdʲnʲiprɔpɛˈtrɔu̯sʲk|}}; {{lang-ru|Днепропетро́вск}} {{IPA-ru|dʲnʲɪprəpʲɪˈtrofsk|}} '''Dnepropetrovsk''') is a [[city]] in [[Ukraine]] and is the third largest city in Ukraine. About 1 million people live there. It lies southeast of Ukraine's capital city, [[Kyiv]]. It was founded in 1776. Dnipro enjoys a borderline [[Semi-arid climate|semi-arid]]/[[Mediterranean climate]] ([[Köppen climate classification]] ''BSh/Csa''), with relativity mild temperatures and plentiful sunshine year-round. 
 
 Dnipro is one of the key places for the [[Nuclear power|nuclear]], [[Arms industry|arms]] and [[Soviet space program|space]] industries of the former Soviet Union.
