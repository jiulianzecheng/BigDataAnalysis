 '''Epidendroideae''' is a [[subfamily]] of the [[orchid]] family. Epidendroideae is larger than all the other orchid subfamilies together, comprising more than 15,000 species in 576 genera. Most Epidendroid orchids are tropical [[epiphyte]]s, typically with [[pseudobulb]]s. There are, however a some terrestrials such as ''[[Epipactis]]'' and even a few myco-heterotrophs, which are parasitic upon [[mycorrhiza]]l [[fungi]].
