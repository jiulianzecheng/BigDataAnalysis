The name '''Josephine''' [ '''joh'''-z''uh''-feen, -s''uh''- ] < ref > {{Cite web|title=Definition of Josephine {{!}} Dictionary.com|url=https://www.dictionary.com/browse/josephine|access-date=2023-04-06|website=www.dictionary.com|language=en}} < /ref >  is a female [[given name]]. Josephine is a timeless name that has been well-liked for hundreds of years. It is the feminine version of the name Joseph and has a meaning of  " God will increase "  or  " may Jehovah add/give increase. " < ref > {{Cite web|title=Josephine name Meaning and Origin|url=https://babys.name/detail-Josephine-Meaning-Origin-Famous-Similar-Sibling.html|access-date=2023-04-06|website=babys.name}} < /ref >  There are many variants. The name is a female version of ''Joseph'' (which is a [[Hebrew language|Hebrew]] version of Yosef). There are many celebrities with the name Josephine and related forms, although the name is only marginally popular in the [[United States]].
