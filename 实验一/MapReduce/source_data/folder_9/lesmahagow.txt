'''Lesmahagow''' ([[Scottish Gaelic|Gaelic]]: ''Lios MoChuda'') is a small town on the edge of [[moorland]], near [[Lanark]] in the [[central belt]] of [[Scotland]], and is also known as '''Abbey Green'''. In 2021 the population of the village was around 4310. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Lesmahagow (South Lanarkshire, Scotland, United Kingdom) - Population Statistics, Charts, Map, Location, Weather and Web Information|url=https://citypopulation.de/en/uk/scotland/south_lanarkshire/S19002324__lesmahagow/|access-date=2023-11-20|website=citypopulation.de}} < /ref > 
 
 The ruins of a 12th century church are located in the village. The village is also the headquarters of the Scottish branch of the International Society of Krishna Consciousness (ISKCON). < ref > {{Cite web|title=ISKCON SCOTLAND|url=https://iskconscotland.org}} < /ref >
