'''Thanet''' is the most northeastern part of [[Kent]], [[South East England]]. About 135,000 people live in Thanet. It was originally an island and was created as a local council area on 1 April 1974. It includes the towns of [[Broadstairs]] and [[Ramsgate]] and [[Margate]], where its [[headquarters]] are.
