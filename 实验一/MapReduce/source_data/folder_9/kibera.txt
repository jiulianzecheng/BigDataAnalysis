 '''Kibera''' is a [[slum]] in southwest [[Nairobi]], [[Kenya]]. It is often called the biggest slum in [[Africa]]. < ref > [http://hal.inria.fr/docs/00/75/18/33/PDF/Amelie_Desgroppes_Sophie_Taupin_-_KIBERA.pdf Amelie Desgroppes Sophie Taupin] < /ref >  Most people in Kibera don't have running water or electricity. Kibera has thirteen [[village]]s. It is a very poor slum and has very bad conditions.
