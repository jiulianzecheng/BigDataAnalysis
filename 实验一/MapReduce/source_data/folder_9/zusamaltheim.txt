 '''Zusamaltheim''' ({{Lang-gsw|Aalta|label=[[Swabian German]]}}) is a [[Municipalities of Germany|municipality]]  in the district of [[Dillingen (district)|Dillingen]] in [[Bavaria]] in [[Germany]]. The town is a member of the [[Municipal association (Germany)|municipal association]] [[Wertingen]]. 
 
 ==Mayor== 
 The mayor is Stephan Lutz, elected in March 2020. < ref name=mayor/ >
