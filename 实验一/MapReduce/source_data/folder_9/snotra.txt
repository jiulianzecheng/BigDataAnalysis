'''Snotra''' is a [[goddess]] of [[wisdom]] and prudence in [[Norse mythology]]. The ''[[Prose Edda]]'' says that Snotra is  " high and courteous " . She is a goddess that, according to [[Snorri Sturluson]],  " should be worshiped as a high-ranking goddess " . She also is one of the few goddesses that dies during Ragnarok.
