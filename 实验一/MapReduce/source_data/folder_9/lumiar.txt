 '''Lumiar''' ({{IPA-pt|luˈmjaɾ}}) is a ''[[freguesia]]'' (civil parish) and district of [[Lisbon]], the capital of [[Portugal]]. 
 
 ==Landmarks== 
 There interesting places in the neighbourhood. 
 * [[Alvalade Stadium]] (home of [[Sporting CP|Sporting Lisbon]]) 
 * [[National Museum of Costume (Portugal)|National Museum of Costume]] 
 * [[Colégio de Santa Doroteia]] 
 * [[Monteiro-Mor Palace|Monteiro-Mor Park]] 
 * [[National Theatre and Dance Museum]]
