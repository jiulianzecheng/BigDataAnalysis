 '''Leeston''' ([[Māori language|Māori]]: ''Karumata'') is a town in the [[Selwyn District]], [[New Zealand]]. It is situated 30 kilometers southwest of [[Christchurch]]. It has a primary school and high school. It has three camping grounds located on the [[Selwyn River / Waikirikiri|Selwyn River]] and opened its first art gallery in 2017.
