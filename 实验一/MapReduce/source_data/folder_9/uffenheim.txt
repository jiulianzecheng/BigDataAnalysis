 '''Uffenheim''' is a city in [[Neustadt (Aisch)-Bad Windsheim]], in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. It is 14 & nbsp;km west of [[Bad Windsheim]], and 36 & nbsp;km southeast of [[Würzburg]].
