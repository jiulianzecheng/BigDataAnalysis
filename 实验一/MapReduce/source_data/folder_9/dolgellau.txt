 '''Dolgellau''' (pronounced ''Dol-gell-eye'' or ''Dol-gel-ee'') is a small town and community in [[Gwynedd]], a county of [[Wales]]. It is the county town of the [[Historic counties of Wales|historic county]] of [[Merionethshire]]. In 2011, it had a population of 2,688. 
 
 The area is famous for its beautiful countryside and historic buildings and attractions. It is very popular with [[hiking|hikers]], [[equestrianism|horse riders]], white-water [[rafting|rafters]] and [[rock climbing|climbing]]. 
 
 Since [[1992]], the town has held [[Sesiwn Fawr Dolgellau]] ({{lang-en|Big Session Dolgellau}}), an annual world music festival. It originally started as a small event and would be held in the streets of the town centre but has since grown too big and is now held in the town's outskirts.
