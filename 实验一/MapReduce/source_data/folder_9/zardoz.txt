 '''''Zardoz''''' is a 1974 [[Ireland|Irish]]-[[United States|American]] [[science fiction movie]] directed, produced and written by [[John Boorman]]. It stars [[Sean Connery]], [[Charlotte Rampling]], [[Sara Kestelman]], [[John Alderton]], and [[Niall Buggy]]. The movie was shot by cinematographer [[Geoffrey Unsworth]] and was distributed by [[20th Century Fox]].
