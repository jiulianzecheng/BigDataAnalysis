Lord Ayyappa was the son of Vishnu  &  ShivaLord Ayyappa is a very popular Hindu [[deity]], which is mainly worshipped in South [[India]]. He is also spelt as Ayyappa. It is believed that he was born out of the union between Lord [[Shiva]] and the mythical Mohini, who is also regarded as an avatar of Lord Vishnu. Therefore, Ayyappa is also called as ‘ Hariharan Puthiran’ which means the son of both Hari or Vishnu and ‘ Haran’ or Shiva.
