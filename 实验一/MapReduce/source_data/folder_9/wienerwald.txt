 The '''Wienerwald''' ( < small > [[East Central Bavarian]]: < /small >  ''Weanawoid'', lit.'Vienna Woods') is a large [[forest]] of over 1,000 square kilometers. It lies southwest of [[Vienna]] in [[Lower Austria]]. It covers the start of the [[Alps]]. It is about 45 & nbsp;km long, and between 20 and 30 & nbsp;km wide.
