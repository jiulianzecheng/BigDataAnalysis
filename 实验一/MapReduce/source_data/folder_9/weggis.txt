 '''Weggis''' is a [[municipality]] of the [[district]] [[Lucerne-Land (district)|Lucerne-Land]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Lucerne (canton)|Lucerne]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 It is on the northern shore of [[Lake Lucerne]].
