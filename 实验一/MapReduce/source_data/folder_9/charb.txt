 '''Stéphane Charbonnier''' ({{IPA-fr|ʃaʁbɔnje|lang}}; 21 August 1967 & nbsp;– 7 January 2015), better known as '''Charb''' ({{IPA-fr|ʃaʁb|}}), was a [[French people|French]] [[cartoonist]], [[journalist]], and [[Caricature|caricaturist]]. He was best known for his work on the satirical newspaper ''[[Charlie Hebdo]]''. 
 
 Charb was born in [[Conflans-Sainte-Honorine]]. 
 
 Charb was among the victims who were killed in a [[Charlie Hebdo shooting|shooting]] at ''Charlie Hebdo'' < nowiki > ' < /nowiki > s [[Paris]] headquarters on 7 January 2015.
