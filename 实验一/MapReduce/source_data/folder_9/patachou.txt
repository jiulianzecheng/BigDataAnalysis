 '''Henriette Ragon''' (10 June 1918  & ndash; 30 April 2015), better known by her [[stage name]] '''Patachou''', was a [[French people|French]] [[actress]] and [[singer]]. She appeared on [[stage]], [[television]] and [[movies]] between 1953 and 2006. She also wrote at least twenty songs. On 1 January 2009, she was awarded the [[Legion of Honour]]. 
 
 Patachou was born in [[Paris]]. Her son, [[Pierre Billon (songwriter)|Pierre Billon]] (born 1946), is a [[songwriter]].
