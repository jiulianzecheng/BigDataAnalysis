 The '''Regnitz''' is a river in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. It is a left [[tributary]] of the [[Main]] and is 58 km in length. 
 
 The river is formed by the [[confluence]] of the rivers Rednitz and [[Pegnitz River|Pegnitz]], which meet in the city of [[Fürth]]. From there the Regnitz runs northwards through the cities of [[Erlangen]] and [[Forchheim]]. It finally meets the Main in the city of [[Bamberg]].
