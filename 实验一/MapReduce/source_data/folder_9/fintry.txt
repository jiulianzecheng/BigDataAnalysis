 '''Fintry''' is a small village in central [[Scotland]], nestled in the [[strath]] of the [[Endrick Water]] in the [[Campsie Fells]] some 35 & nbsp;km north of [[Glasgow]]. It is part of [[Stirling (district)|Stirling District]].  As of the 2001 census, Fintry and the surrounding rural area had a population of 2300 people.
