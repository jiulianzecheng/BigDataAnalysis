 '''Krefeld''' (until November 25, 1925 - ''Crefeld'') is a city in the [[Germany|German]] [[States of Germany|state]] of [[North Rhine-Westphalia]], near [[Düsseldorf]]. 
 
 Krefeld has about 235,000 [[inhabitant]]s.
