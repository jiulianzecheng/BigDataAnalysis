 '''Groesbeek''' is a town and former municipality in the province of [[Gelderland]], the [[Netherlands]]. On 1 January 2015, the former municipality merged with [[Millingen aan de Rijn]] and [[Ubbergen]]. The larger area was known as Groesbeek until 1 January 2016, when its name was changed to [[Berg en Dal (municipality)|Berg en Dal]]. < ref > {{cite web|title=Naam Berg en Dal toch op kombord Groesbeek|url=http://www.gelderlander.nl/regio/nijmegen-e-o/groesbeek/naam-berg-en-dal-toch-op-kombord-groesbeek-1.5368163|publisher=de Gelderlander|access-date=8 January 2016|date=24 October 2015|url-status=dead|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20151025134433/http://www.gelderlander.nl/regio/nijmegen-e-o/groesbeek/naam-berg-en-dal-toch-op-kombord-groesbeek-1.5368163|archive-date=25 October 2015}} < /ref >
