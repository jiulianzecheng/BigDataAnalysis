 The '''Frigatebirds''' are a [[Family (biology)|family]] of [[Seabird|seabirds]]. In Latin, the family is called '''Fregatidae'''. There are five different [[species]]; all are classified in the [[genus]] '''Fregata'''. They occur in tropical and subtropical regions. The birds are adapted to a life at sea. The wingspan is between {{Convert|175|cm}} and {{Convert|255|cm}}.
