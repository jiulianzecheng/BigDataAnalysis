 '''Bauquay''' is a former [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is found in the [[Regions of France|region]] [[Basse-Normandie]] in the [[Calvados (department)|Calvados]] [[Departments of France|department]] in the northwest of [[France]]. On 1 January 2017, it was merged into the new commune [[Les Monts d'Aunay]]. < ref > [https://www.legifrance.gouv.fr/eli/arrete/2016/9/26/INTB1634319A/jo/texte Arrêté préfectoral] 26 September 2016 {{in lang|fr}} < /ref >
