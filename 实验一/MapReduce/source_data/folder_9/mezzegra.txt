 '''Mezzegra''' was a ''[[comune]]'' in the [[Province of Como]] in the [[Lombardy]] region in [[Italy]]. On 4 February 2014, the former municipalities of [[Lenno]], Mezzegra, [[Ossuccio]] and [[Tremezzo]] merged to form the new municipality of [[Tremezzina]].
