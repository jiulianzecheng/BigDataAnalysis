 The '''Stylophone''' is a instrument with keyboard and stylus. It was invented in 1967 by Brian Jarvis and marketed the next year by Dubreq. < ref > {{Cite web|date=2009-07-06|title=Hey, what's that sound: Stylophone|url=http://www.theguardian.com/music/2009/jul/06/whats-that-sound-stylophone|access-date=2022-02-27|website=the Guardian|language=en}} < /ref >  The instrument is mostly known because it is easy to use and learn.
