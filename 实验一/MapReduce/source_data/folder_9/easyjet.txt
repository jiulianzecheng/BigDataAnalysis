 '''EasyJet Airline Company Limited''', styled as '''easyJet''', is a [[United Kingdom|British]] low cost [[airline]] founded in 1995. Sir Stelios Haji-Ioannou started the airline in 1995. He was in charge of EasyJet until 2002.  < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.dailymail.co.uk/news/article-110325/Stelios-step-easyJet-chairman.html|title=Stelios to step down as easyJet chairman|website=Mail Online}} < /ref >  Starting in 2003, EasyJet opened many bases at airports throughout Europe.  < ref > {{cite web | url=http://corporate.easyjet.com/latest-news-archive/news-year-2003/05-11-03-en.aspx?sc_lang=en | title=Home }}{{Dead link|date=October 2023 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }} < /ref >  As of 2016, EasyJet is based at [[London Luton Airport]]. EasyJet currently has 26 bases and its largest base is at [[London Gatwick Airport]].  < ref > {{cite web|url=https://careers.easyjet.com/|title=easyJet Careers|website=careers.easyjet.com}} < /ref >  It is the second largest low-fare airline in [[Europe]]. It flies domestic and international scheduled services.
