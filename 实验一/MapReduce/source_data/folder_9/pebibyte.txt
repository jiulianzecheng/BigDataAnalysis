A '''pebibyte''' ('''pe'''ta '''bi'''nary '''byte''', abbreviated PiB) is a large unit of measurement of [[bytes]] (computer memory).  < br / > 
 1 pebibyte = 2 < sup > 50 < /sup >  bytes = 1,125,899,906,842,624 bytes = 1,024 [[tebibyte]]s.  < br / > It is related to the [[petabyte]] which is 10 < sup > 15 < /sup >  bytes. 
 
 1024 pebibytes make one [[exbibyte]].
