 The '''Alagnon''' is a [[France|French]] [[river]] that starts in the [[Massif Central]] within the Regional Natural Park of the Volcanoes of Auvergne ({{lang-fr|Parc Naturel Régional des Volcans d'Auvergne}}) and that flows through the [[Auvergne-Rhône-Alpes]] [[Regions of France|region]]. It is a left [[tributary]] of the [[Allier (river)|Allier]]. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 The Alagnon river has a length of {{Convert|85.9|km|mi|1|abbr=on}} and a [[drainage basin]] with an area of {{Convert|1042|km2|sqmi|0|abbr=on }}. < ref name= " sandre " > {{cite web |url=http://services.sandre.eaufrance.fr/Courdo/Fiche/client/fiche_courdo.php?CdSandre=K25-0300 |title=L'Alagnon (K25-0300) |publisher=SANDRE - Portail national d'accès aux référentiels sur l'eau |language=fr |access-date=25 January 2017 }} < /ref >   
 
 Its average yearly [[Discharge (hydrology)|discharge]] ([[volume]] of [[water]] which passes through a section of the river per [[Unit of measurement|unit]] of [[time]]) is {{Convert|11.90|m3/s|cuft/s|abbr=on }} at [[Lempdes-sur-Allagnon]] in the Haute-Loire department. < ref name= " hydro " > {{cite web |url=http://www.hydro.eaufrance.fr/stations/K2593010%26procedure%3Dsynthese |title=L'Alagnon à Lempdes |work=Banque Hydro |publisher=Ministère de l'Ecologie, du Développement Durable et de l'Energie |language=fr |access-date=25 January 2017 |archive-date=3 March 2016 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160303182058/http://www.hydro.eaufrance.fr/stations/K2593010%26procedure%3Dsynthese |url-status=dead }} < /ref >

 ===Course=== 
 The Alagnon river starts  in the [[Mounts of Cantal]] ([[Massif Central]]), near the ''Puy Bataillouse'' [[mountain]], ''commune'' of [[Laveissière]], at an [[altitude]] of about {{Convert|1590|m|ft|0|abbr=on }}, in the Cantal department. < ref > {{cite web |url=https://www.geoportail.gouv.fr/carte?c=2.721389,45.099444 & z=16 & l0=GEOGRAPHICALGRIDSYSTEMS.MAPS.SCAN-EXPRESS.STANDARD::GEOPORTAIL:OGC:WMTS(1) & permalink=yes |title=Source de l'Alagnon |publisher=Géoportail |language=fr |access-date=25 January 2017 }} < /ref >  First, it flows to the east, near the Super Lioran [[ski resort]], and gets into a [[gorge]] and, then, into a [[valley]] near [[Murat, Cantal|Murat]]. The river flows in general to the northeast and gets into deep gorges in [[Lempdes-sur-Allagnon]]. 
 
 The Alagnon river flows through the [[Auvergne-Rhône-Alpes]] region, 3 departments and 23 ''communes''. Two ''communes'' are named after the river: [[La Chapelle-d'Alagnon]] and [[Lempdes-sur-Allagnon]]. It flows through the following ''communes'', among others: < ref name=sandre / > 
 * [[Auvergne-Rhône-Alpes]] region 
 ** [[Cantal]] department:  [[Laveissière]], [[Murat]], [[La Chapelle-d'Alagnon]], [[Neussargues-Moissac]], [[Ferrières-Saint-Mary]], [[Molompize]], [[Massiac]] 
 ** [[Haute-Loire]] department: [[Grenier-Montgon]], [[Torsiac]], [[Lempdes-sur-Allagnon]] 
 ** [[Puy-de-Dôme]] department: [[Charbonnier-les-Mines]], [[Beaulieu, Puy-de-Dôme|Beaulieu]], [[Auzat-la-Combelle]] 
 
 Finally, it flows into the [[Allier (river)|Allier]] river in the town of [[Auzat-la-Combelle]], in the place known as ''Le Saut du Loup'', in the [[Puy-de-Dôme]] department. < ref > {{cite web |url=https://www.geoportail.gouv.fr/carte?c=3.303889,45.452222 & z=15 & l0=GEOGRAPHICALGRIDSYSTEMS.MAPS.SCAN-EXPRESS.STANDARD::GEOPORTAIL:OGC:WMTS(1) & permalink=yes |title=Embouchure de l'Alagnon |publisher=Géoportail |language=fr |access-date=25 January 2017 }} < /ref >
