 '''Basutoland''' was a [[British Empire|British colony]] that existed from 1884 to 1966 in present-day [[Lesotho]]. 
 
 Basutoland was completely surrounded by the [[Cape Colony]] from 1884–1910, the [[Union of South Africa]] from 1910–1961 and the [[South Africa|Republic of South Africa]] from 1961–1966. 
 
 On 4 October 1966, Basutoland became independent as the [[Kingdom of Lesotho]].
