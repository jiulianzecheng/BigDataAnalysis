 '''Viechtach''' is a town in [[Regen (district)|Regen]] in [[Bavaria]] in [[Germany]]. It is on the river [[Regen River|Schwarzer Regen]], 31 & nbsp;km northeast of [[Straubing]], and it is known for the quartz mountains nearby.
