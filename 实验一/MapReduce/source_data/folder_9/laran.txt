 '''Laran''' is the god of war and bloodlust in [[Etruscan mythology]]. He was typically portrayed as a naked youth wearing a helmet and carrying a spear, and was associated with [[fire]] and the [[Sun]]. He was the lover of [[Turan (mythology)|Turan]], goddess of love, beauty and fertility; Laran himself was the Etruscan equivalent of the [[Greek mythology|Greek god]] [[Ares]] ([[Mars]] in [[Roman mythology]]).
