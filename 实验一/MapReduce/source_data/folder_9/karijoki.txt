 '''Karijoki''' is a municipality in [[Southern Ostrobothnia]], [[Finland]]. As of 28 February 2023, 1,192 people lived there. Neighbouring municipalities are [[Isojoki]], [[Kauhajoki]], [[Kristiinankaupunki]] and [[Teuva]].
