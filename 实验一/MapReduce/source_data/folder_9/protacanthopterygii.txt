 '''Procanthopterygii''' is a [[superorder]] of [[teleost]] [[fish]]es. < ref > {{cite web|title = Integrated Taxonomic Information System|url = https://www.itis.gov/servlet/SingleRpt/SingleRpt?search_topic=TSN & search_value=161928#null}} < /ref >  These fish mostly live in [[temperate]] waters, and in the [[northern hemisphere]]. They may live in both fresh and salt water. 
 
 Best-known examples of this group are the [[salmon]], [[trout]],  and [[pike]]s.  
 
 The main groups are: 
 * [[Argentiniformes]] 
 * [[Esociformes]] 
 * [[Osmeriformes]] 
 * [[Salmoniformes]]
