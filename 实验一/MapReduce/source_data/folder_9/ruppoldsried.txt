 '''Ruppoldsried''' was a [[municipality]] in the [[administrative district]] of [[Seeland (administrative district)|Seeland]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Bern (canton)|Bern]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 On 1 January 2013, the former municipality of Ruppoldsried merged into the municipality of [[Rapperswil, Bern|Rapperswil]].
