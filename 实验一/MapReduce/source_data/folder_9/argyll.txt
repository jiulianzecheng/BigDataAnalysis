 '''Argyll''' is a [[county]] on the west coast of [[Scotland]]. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 Argyll has many spits of land and many [[island]]s. The largest islands are the [[Isle of Mull]] and the [[Isle of Islay]]. The county town is [[Lochgilphead]]. [[Inveraray]] is the home of the [[Duke of Argyll]]. 
 ==Name of the county== 
 Argyll's name comes from the [[Gaelic]] words for  " Borderland of the Gael " , because it was the first place in [[Great Britain]] settled by Gaelic-speaking settlers from [[Ireland]]. 
 ==Politics== 
 For local [[government]], most of Argyll is in the [[Argyll and Bute]] council area.
