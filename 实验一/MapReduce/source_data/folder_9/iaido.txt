{{Nihongo|'''''Iaido'''''|居合道}} is a [[Japanese people|Japanese]] [[martial art]] that focuses on drawing a sword (typically a [[katana]]). It is about being aware and being capable of quickly drawing the sword and responding to a sudden attack. < ref name= " Encyclopedia553 " > Christensen, Karen and Allen Guttmann  2001. ''International Encyclopedia of Women and Sports: H-R''. Macmillan Reference USA, page 553. < /ref > 
 
 Iaido movements are elegant movements. New learners often start using a wooden sword and then move on to an ''iaitō'' (A metal but fairly blunt sword). People that do Iaido may be called ''iaidoka.
