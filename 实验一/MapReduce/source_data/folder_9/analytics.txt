'''Analytics''' is the systematic processing of [[data]] or [[statistics]] and of discovering, interpreting, and communicating significant patterns in data to get meaningful information. < ref > {{Cite web|url=https://www.oracle.com/in/business-analytics/what-is-analytics.html|title=What is Analytics? {{!}} Oracle India|website=www.oracle.com|access-date=2020-03-27}} < /ref > 
 
 In simple language, analytics is turning raw data into useful one for making better decisions. Analytics uses the application of [[statistics]], [[computer programming]], and [[operations research]] to gain information from meanings of data. < ref > {{Cite web|url=https://www.techopedia.com/definition/30296/analytics|title=What is Analytics? - Definition from Techopedia|website=Techopedia.com|language=en|access-date=2020-03-27}} < /ref >
