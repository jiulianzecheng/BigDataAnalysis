 '''Ahipara''' is a town in the [[Far North District]], [[New Zealand]]. It is located 14 kilometers south west of [[Kaitaia]] and [[Pukepoto]] is in between these two. It has a primary school in the township and is very popular with surfing. It has suffered from two fires in [[February 2019]] and [[October 2020]].
