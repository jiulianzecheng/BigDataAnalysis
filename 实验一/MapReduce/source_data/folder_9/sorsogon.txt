 '''Sorsogon''' is a [[Provinces of the Philippines|province]] in the [[Bicol Region]] of the [[Philippines]]. It is the farthest south province of [[Luzon]]. As of 2020, 828,655 people live there. < ref name=population / >  [[Sorsogon City]] is its [[Capital city|capital]] and biggest city. 
 
 Its area is {{convert|2119|km2|mi2}}. < ref name=area / >  [[Albay]] is to the north, the [[Philippine Sea]] is to the east, the [[San Bernardino Strait]] is to the south, the [[Ticao Pass]] is to the west, and the [[Burias Pass]] is to the northwest.
