'''Tarsals''' are bones found in the [[foot|feet]]. They are also known as [[ankle]] bones. There are seven of them in each foot. The seven tarsals are: 
 * [[Medial cuneiform]] 
 * [[Intermediate cuneiform]] 
 * [[Lateral cuneiform]] 
 * [[Navicular]] 
 * [[Talus]] 
 * [[Calcaneus]] 
 * [[Cuboid (bone)|Cuboid]]
