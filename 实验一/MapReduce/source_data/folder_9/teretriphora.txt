 '''''Teretriphora''''' is a [[genus]] of very small [[sea snail]]s, [[Ocean|marine]] [[Gastropoda|gastropod]] [[Mollusc|mollusks]]. They are in a [[Family (biology)|family]] called Triphoridae. 
 
 == Species == 
 Species within the genus ''Teretriphora'' include: 
 * ''[[Teretriphora distorta]]''  < small > (Laseron, 1958) < /small > 
 * ''[[Teretriphora gemmegens]]''  < small > (Verco, 1909) < /small > 
 * ''[[Teretriphora huttoni]]''  < small > [[Henry Suter|Suter]], 1908 < /small > 
 * ''[[Teretriphora novapostrema]]''  < small > (Verco, 1910) < /small > 
 * ''[[Teretriphora ponderorum]]''  < small > Marshall, 1983 < /small > 
 * ''[[Teretriphora spica]]''  < small > (Verco, J.C., 1909) < /small > 
 * ''Teretriphora kesteveni'' (Hedley, 1902): [[synonym]] of ''[[Latitriphora kesteveni]]'' (Hedley, 1903)
