 '''Dhoti''' is a white piece of [[cloth]] basically worn by [[Indian people]]. 
 
 
 They have different ways be worn. In [[West Bengal]] their wearing style is known as koncha style. Nowadays people use them in occasional purposes. Earlier they were worn by rich zamindars of India. The Dhoti is a commonly worn national dress-code for Indian men.
