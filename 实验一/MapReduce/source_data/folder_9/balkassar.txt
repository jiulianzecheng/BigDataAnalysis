'''Balkassar''' is a village and [[Union Councils of Pakistan|Union Council]], an administrative subdivision, of [[Chakwal District]] in the [[Punjab (Pakistan)|Punjab Province]] of [[Pakistan]], it is part of Choa Saidan Shah [[Tehsil]] < ref > {{Cite web |url=http://www.nrb.gov.pk/lg_election/union.asp?district=6 & dn=Chakwal |title=Tehsils  &  Unions in the District of Chakwal |access-date=2008-07-21 |archive-date=2008-01-24 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20080124031142/http://www.nrb.gov.pk/lg_election/union.asp?district=6 & dn=Chakwal |url-status=dead }} < /ref >  and is located at 32°55'60N 72°39'0E. Also, Balkassar is famous for its '[[Rain of Flaming Dogs]]' ceremony which happens once in a year. < ref > [http://www.fallingrain.com/world/PK/4/Balkassar.html Location of Balkassar - Falling Rain Genomics] < /ref >
