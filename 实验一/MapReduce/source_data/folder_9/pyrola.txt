 '''Pyrola''', commonly called wintergreens (also applied to [[Gaultheria]]), are [[plant]]s in the [[Calluna|heath]] family ''[[Ericaceae]]''. They are in the subfamily [[Pyroloideae]] of the family [[Ericaceae]]. [[Pyroloideae]] was treated as a separate family, '''Pyrolaceae.'''
