 '''Terneuzen''', [[Zeelandic dialect|Zeelandic]] and [[East Flemish]]: ''Terneuzen'' or ''Neuzen'', is a [[municipality]] and town in [[Zeeuws-Vlaanderen]], Province of [[Zeeland]], the [[Netherlands]]. 
 
 About 55,000 people were living in the municipality in 2017. < br/  > 
 About 25,000 people were living in the town in 2015. 
 
 The municipality borders the [[Western Scheldt]], and the Belgian Province of [[East Flanders]]. 
 
 Terneuzen received [[city rights]] in 1584. 
 
 The south entrance of the [[Western Scheldt Tunnel]] is in Terneuzen.
