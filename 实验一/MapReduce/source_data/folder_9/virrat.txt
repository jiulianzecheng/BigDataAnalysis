 '''Virrat''' is a [[city]] in [[Finland]]. As of 31 May 2013, 7,344 people lived there. Nearby municipalities are [[Alavus]], [[Keuruu]], [[Kihniö]], [[Mänttä-Vilppula]], [[Ruovesi]], [[Seinäjoki]], [[Ylöjärvi]] and [[Ähtäri]].
