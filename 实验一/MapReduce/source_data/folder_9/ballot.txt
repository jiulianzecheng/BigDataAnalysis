A '''ballot''' is a system used to cast votes in an election and may be found as a piece of paper or a small ball used in secret [[voting]]. < ref > {{cite web| url=http://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/ballot|title=Ballot|publisher=Merriam-Webster |access-date=2012-11-07}} < /ref >  It was invented in [[Italy]] around the [[16th century]]. < ref > {{Cite web|url=https://www.etymonline.com/word/ballot|title = Ballot  & #124; Origin and meaning of ballot by Online Etymology Dictionary}} < /ref > 
 
 Each voter uses one ballot, and ballots are not shared. In the simplest elections, a ballot might be a scrap of paper. The voter casts their ballot in a [[Ballot box|box]] at a [[polling station]].
