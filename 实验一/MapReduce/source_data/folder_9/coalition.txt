 A '''coalition''' is an [[alliance]], where the members work together.  
 
 ==Politics and government== 
 A coalition [[government]], in a [[parliamentary system]], is a government made from a coalition of [[Political party|parties]]. This means the union of different political parties or groups for a purpose, usually for a short time. 
 
 In [[international relations]], a coalition can be a group of nations united for a specific purpose, such as fighting a war.
