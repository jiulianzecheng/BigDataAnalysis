'''Sheikh''' —  also spelled '''Sheik''' or '''Shaikh''' or '''Shekh''' — is an [[honorific]] in the [[Arabic language]]. It means  " elder "  and often means  " leader and/or governor " . It is commonly used for the front man of a [[tribe]]. He can be an [[Ulama|Islamic scholar]] who got this title after graduating from a basic Islamic school. '''Sheikha''' is the female term. These are uncommon in the Arabic world. A sheikh who is wise is called ''ḥakīm'' and can rule. 
 
 The word is also generally used for a man over forty < ref name= " autogenerated1 " > [http://www.islamic-dictionary.com/index.php?word=Sheikh & x=0 & y=0 The Islamic Dictionary online, 'Sheikh'] < /ref >  or fifty < ref > [http://www.al-baz.com/islam/abdalqadirjilani/about.htm About the Sheikh] < /ref >  years of age. 
 
 ==Surname== 
 In [[Bangladesh]], [[India]] and [[Pakistan]],  " Sheikh "  is a common surname among Muslims.  It generally means there is Arab ancestry.
