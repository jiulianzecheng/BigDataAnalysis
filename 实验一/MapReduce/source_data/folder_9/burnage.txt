'''Burnage''' is a [[suburb]] of the City of [[Manchester]] in [[North West England|North West]] [[England]]. It lies about four & nbsp;miles (6.4 & nbsp;km) south of [[Manchester city centre]]. According to the [[2001]] census it had a population of 13,706. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.ons.gov.uk/help/localstatistics|title=Local statistics - Office for National Statistics|website=www.ons.gov.uk}} < /ref >
