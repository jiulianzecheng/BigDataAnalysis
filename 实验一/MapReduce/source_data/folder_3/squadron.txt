A '''squadron''' is a [[military]] [[unit]] on land, at sea and in the air. 
 
 [[Army|Armies]]: 80 to 600 soldiers with [[tank]]s, [[motorcycle]]s, [[Cavalry|horses]] or [[armoured car]]s. 
 
 [[Navy|Navies]]: A number of [[destroyer]]s, [[submarine]]s, [[minesweeper]]s or other warships.  
 
 [[Air force]]s. This usually includes several [[aircraft]] and [[pilot]]s, [[crew]]s and mechanics responsible for their action.
