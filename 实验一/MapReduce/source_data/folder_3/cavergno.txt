 '''Cavergno''' is a village in the [[Vallemaggia (district)|district of Vallemaggia]], in the [[canton of Ticino]], [[Switzerland]]. Cavergno is not a free municipality anymore after merging with the village [[Bignasco]] to form the municipality [[Cevio]].
