 '''{{lang|de|Sturzkampfflugzeug}}''' is a [[German language|German]] [[word]] that can be [[translation|translated]] as ''[[dive bomber]]''. Its common [[abbreviation]] is ''Stuka''. It was used for two principal types of [[aircraft]]: [[Henschel Hs 123]] and [[Junkers Ju 87]].
