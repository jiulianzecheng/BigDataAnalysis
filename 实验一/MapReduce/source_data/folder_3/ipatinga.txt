 '''Ipatinga''' is a [[Brazil]]ian city in the [[Vale do Aço metropolitan area]], state of [[Minas Gerais]]. Its population in 2009 was 244.508 inhabitants. Its area is {{convert|165.509|km2|abbr=off}}. The city was founded in April 29, 1964. 
 
 It's limited with [[Caratinga]], [[Coronel Fabriciano]], [[Mesquita, Minas Gerais|Mesquita]], [[Santana do Paraíso]] and [[Timóteo]].
