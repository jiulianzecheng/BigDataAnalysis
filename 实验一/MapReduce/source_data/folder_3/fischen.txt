 '''Fischen im Allgäu''' is a [[municipality]] in the district of [[Oberallgäu]] in [[Bavaria]] in [[Germany]]. Fishcen is found {{convert|5.5|km}} north of [[Oberstdorf]]. It is on the banks of the river [[Iller]].
