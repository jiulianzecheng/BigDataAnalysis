 '''Galway''' is a [[port]] [[city]] in the western part of [[Republic of Ireland|Ireland]]. It is in the [[Provinces of Ireland|historic province]] of [[Connacht]]. Over 70,000 people live there as of 2011. It is located on the [[Galway Bay]].
