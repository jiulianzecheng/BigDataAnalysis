'''Munchos''' are a [[potato chip]]s snack food. They are manufactured from dehydrated [[potato]]es by [[Frito-Lay]].  
 
 They are somewhat similar to [[Lay's Stax]] and [[Pringles]]. These are also made with dried potatoes. They are kosher. < ref > {{cite web|title=Munchos|publisher=Frito Lay|url=https://www.fritolay.com/dietary-needs/kosher/us-products-certified-kosher|access-date=Dec 27, 2020}} < /ref > 
 
 The chips were first made in 1969.
