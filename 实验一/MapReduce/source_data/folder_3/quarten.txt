 '''Quarten''' is a [[municipality]] in [[Sarganserland]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[St. Gallen (canton)|St. Gallen]], [[Switzerland]]. It is above [[Walensee|Lake Walen]] and is divided into villages: Oberterzen, Unterterzen, Quarten, Quinten, Mols, Murg, and Tannenbodenalp.
