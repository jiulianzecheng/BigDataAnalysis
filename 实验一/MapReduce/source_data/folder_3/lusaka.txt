 '''Lusaka''' is the [[Capital (city)|capital]] of [[Zambia]]. It is also the largest [[city]] in that [[country]]. It is in south central Zambia on a [[plateau]] at 1300 [[metre|m]] (4265 [[foot (unit of length)|ft]]) in [[altitude]]. Lusaka has 1,084,703 people living in it.
