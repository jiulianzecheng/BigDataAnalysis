 '''Jougne''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is in [[Bourgogne-Franche-Comté]] in the [[Doubs]] [[Departments of France|department]] in east [[France]]. 
 
 == Geography == 
 The commune is about {{convert|19|km|mi|abbr=on}} of [[Pontarlier]] and {{convert|4.5|km|mi|abbr=on}} from the [[Switzerland|Swiss]] border.
