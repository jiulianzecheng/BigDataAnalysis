 '''Gordonstoun School''' is a co-educational independent school for boarding and day pupils in [[Moray]], Scotland. It is in  the north-west of [[Elgin, Moray|Elgin]]. < ref > {{cite web | title = Gordonstoun Manor History | year = 2004 | url = http://www.houseofgordonva.com/Gordonstoun.html | access-date = 31 March 2010 | archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20110712223743/http://www.houseofgordonva.com/Gordonstoun.html | archive-date = 12 July 2011 | url-status = dead }} < /ref >   
 
 Admission is not just a matter of money. There is an interview and references and exam results are considered. It is one of the few full boarding schools in the United Kingdom today. < ref > {{Cite web|url=https://www.goodschoolsguide.co.uk/schools/gordonstoun-elgin#tab_review|title=Good School's Guide Gordonstoun Review|access-date=21 August 2020|website=Good School's Guide}} < /ref > 
 
 It is famous as the secondary school which [[King Charles III]] went to as a teenager. His father, [[Prince Philip, Duke of Edinburgh|Prince Philip]], went there as a teenager.
