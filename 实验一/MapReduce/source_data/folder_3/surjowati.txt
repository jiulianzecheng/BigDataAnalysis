 '''Surjowati''' also written as '''Soeriowati''' (born 1920s) was a female Indonesian [[Sport of athletics|athlete]] from [[Madiun]]. Her main achievements were between 1951 and 1953. She was described a slender but strong woman. < ref name=preview > {{cite web|url= https://resolver.kb.nl/resolve?urn=ddd:010474968:mpeg21:a0080 |title=Indonesië heeft kansen op de eerste Aziade|work= [[De nieuwsgier]]|via=[[Delpher]]|date=5 March 1951|access-date=27 June 2022|language=nl}} < /ref > 
 
 As a preperation for the 1951 Asian Games she went with the national team on a training camp in [[Djokja]] in January 1951. < ref name=training > {{cite web|url= https://resolver.kb.nl/resolve?urn=ddd:010863237:mpeg21:a0098 |title=Athleten naar trainingskamp|work= [[Java-bode]]|via=resolver.kb.nl|date=5 January 1951|access-date=27 June 2022|language=nl}} < /ref >  However, Surjowati left the camp already after the first day stating  " I have to look after my children anyway " . < ref name=training2 > {{cite web|url= https://resolver.kb.nl/resolve?urn=MMNIOD04:000093200:mpeg21:a0081 |title=Mevr. Soeriowati kwam, zag en ging weg|work= [[Nieuwe courant]]|via=resolver.kb.nl|date=22 January 1951|access-date=27 June 2022|language=nl}} < /ref > 
 
 Altough she left the camp, she was selected for the Games to compete in the javelin throw, discus throw and 4x 100 metres relay. < ref name=selection > {{cite web|url= https://resolver.kb.nl/resolve?urn=MMNIOD04:000093214:mpeg21:a0051 |title=Indonesische ploeg voor Aziatische Spelen|work= [[Nieuwe courant]]|via=resolver.kb.nl|date=8 February 1951|access-date=27 June 2022|language=nl}} < /ref >  In the throwing numbers, it was stated she might win because she threw further than the other Asian women prior to the Games. < ref name=preview / >  At the [[1951 Asian Games]] she didn’t win a medal in the throwing events but won with the relay team the bronze medal in the 4x 100 
 metres event behind Japan and India. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://resolver.kb.nl/resolve?urn=ddd:011208451:mpeg21:a0100 |title=Olympische deelnemers thuis. Niet ontevreden over resultaten|work= [[De Vrije Pers]]|via=resolver.kb.nl|date=16 March 1951|access-date=27 June 2022|language=nl}} < /ref > 
 
 At the 1951 National Sports Week she finished third in the high jump event. < ref name=PON > {{cite web|url= https://resolver.kb.nl/resolve?urn=ddd:010893875:mpeg21:a0048 |title=Mevr. Saleh beste athlete op technische nummers|work= [[Algemeen Indisch dagblad]]|via=resolver.kb.nl|date=26 October 1951|access-date=27 June 2022|language=nl}} < /ref >  She won again the bronze medal in 1953. < ref name=PON1953 > {{cite web|url= https://resolver.kb.nl/resolve?urn=ddd:010477331:mpeg21:a0057 |title=Tot nu toe opvallendste prestaties in Medan bij athleten en zwemmers|work= [[De nieuwsgier]]|via=resolver.kb.nl|date=25 September 1953|access-date=27 June 2022|language=nl}} < /ref >
