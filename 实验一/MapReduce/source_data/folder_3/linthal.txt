 '''Linthal''' was a [[municipality]] of the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Glarus (canton)|Glarus]] in [[Switzerland]]. On 1 January 2011, the former municipalities of Linthal, [[Betschwanden]], [[Braunwald]], [[Elm, Switzerland|Elm]], [[Engi]], [[Haslen]], [[Luchsingen]], [[Matt, Switzerland|Matt]], [[Mitlödi]], [[Rüti, Glarus|Rüti]], [[Schwanden, Glarus|Schwanden]], [[Schwändi]] and [[Sool, Switzerland|Sool]] merged into the municipality of [[Glarus Süd]].
