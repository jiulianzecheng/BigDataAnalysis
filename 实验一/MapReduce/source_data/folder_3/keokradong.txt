'''Keokradong''' is a [[peak]] located in [[Bandarban District]], [[Bangladesh]], with an [[elevation]] of 986 & nbsp;metres (3,235 & nbsp;ft). < ref > [https://www.cia.gov/library/publications/the-world-factbook/fields/2020.html Elevation extremes] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181123022934/https://www.cia.gov/library/publications/the-world-factbook/fields/2020.html |date=2018-11-23 }}, CIA: the World Factbook. < /ref >  Some sources claim it as the highest point of Bangladesh.
