 '''Azamgarh''' is a [[city]] in the [[India]]n [[States and union territories of India|state]] of [[Uttar Pradesh]]. It is the [[headquarters]] of [[Azamgarh division]]. The division includes [[Ballia district|Ballia]], [[Mau district|Mau]] and Azamgarh districts. Azamgarh is on the [[Tamsa River]]. It is located {{convert|268| km|mi|abbr=on}} east of the state [[Capital city|capital]] [[Lucknow]].
