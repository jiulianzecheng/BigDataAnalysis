 '''Bathgate''' is a town in [[West Lothian]], Scotland, 5 miles (8 km) west of [[Livingston]] and adjacent to the [[M8 motorway]]. Nearby towns are [[Armadale]], [[Blackburn, West Lothian|Blackburn]], [[Linlithgow]], [[Livingston]], [[West Calder]] and [[Whitburn]]. Situated 2 miles (3 km) south of the ancient [[Neolithic]] burial site at [[Cairnpapple Hill]], Bathgate and the surrounding area show signs of habitation since about 3500 BC.
