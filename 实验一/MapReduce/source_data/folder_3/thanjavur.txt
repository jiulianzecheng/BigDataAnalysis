 '''Thanjavur''' or '''Tanjore''' is a city in [[Tamil Nadu]], [[India]]. It was the [[capital (city)|capital]] of earlier [[Cholas|Chola Kingdom]]. It is called the ''Rice Bowl'' of Tamil Nadu.
