 '''ZooMontana''' is a {{Convert|70|acre|adj=on}} [[zoo]] located in [[Billings, Montana]], [[United States]] and is [[Montana]]'s only zoo and [[botanical garden]]. It currently has nearly 100 [[animal]]s, representing 58 [[species]]. These animals all live in [[habitat]]s designed to [[:wikt:imitate|imitate]] their [[natural]] habitats. 
 The zoo was established in 1992 as a [[nonprofit organization]]. < ref name= " Zoo " > {{Cite web |url=https://www.zoomontana.org/about |title=About|website=Zoomontana.org|publisher=ZooMontana|date=2023|access-date=2023-11-12}} < /ref >  It focuses on year-round [[wildlife]] [[native]] to Montana, the [[Rocky Mountains]], and other cold temperature regions at or above the [[45th parallel north|45th parallel]]. Indoor habitats include animals from around the world. < ref name= " Zoo " / >  The zoo gets over 80,000 visitors per year. < ref > {{Cite web |url=https://www.visitbillings.com/outdoor/zoomontana-and-botanical-gardens |title=ZooMontana and Botanical Gardens|website=Visitbillings.com|publisher=Visit Billings|date=2023|access-date=2023-11-12}} < /ref >
