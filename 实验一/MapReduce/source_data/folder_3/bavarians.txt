 '''Bavarians''' ([[Standard German]] and [[Bavarian language|Bavarian]]: ''Bayern'') are an ethnographic group of [[Germans]] of [[Bavaria]]. The group's dialect is [[Bavarian language|Bavarian]] and [[German language|German]]. 
 [[File:Oktoberfest_2006_(1).jpg|right|thumb|The [[Oktoberfest]] in [[Munich]], the most widely known festival of Bavarian culture, held since 1810 (2006 photograph).]]
