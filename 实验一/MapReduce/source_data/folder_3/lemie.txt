 '''Lemie''' is a ''[[comune]]'' in the [[Metropolitan City of Turin]] in the [[Piedmont]] region in [[Italy]]. It is about {{convert|35|km|mi}} northwest of [[Turin]]. 
 
 Lemie is next to these municipalities: [[Ala di Stura]], [[Balme]], [[Mezzenile]], [[Usseglio]], [[Viù]], and [[Condove]].
