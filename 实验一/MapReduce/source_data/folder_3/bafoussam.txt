 '''Bafoussam''' is a city in [[Cameroon]]. It is the capital and largest city of the [[West Region (Cameroon)|West Region]], in the [[Bamboutos Mountains]]. In 2019, about 600,000 people lived in the urban area. 
 
 The city is the region's center of trade. [[Coffee]], [[tobacco]] and [[tea]] are grown here. It has also a coffee processing facility and [[brewery]]. The city is the home of the [[Bamileke]] people. 
 
 It has three communes: Commune of Bafoussam I (Bafoussam), Commune of Bafoussam II (Baleng) and Commune of Bafoussam III (Bamougoum).
