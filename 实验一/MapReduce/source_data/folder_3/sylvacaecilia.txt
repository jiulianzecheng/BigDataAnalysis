 '''''Sylvacaecilia grandisonae''''' (commonly known as the '''Aleku caecilian''' or '''Ethiopian caecilian''') is a [[species]] of [[caecilian]]s. They belong to the [[family (biology)|family]] [[Indotyphlidae]]. They live in [[Ethiopia]]. Their [[habitat]]s are [[subtropical]] or [[tropical]] dry [[forest]]s. They also live near [[river]]s and [[spring (hydrosphere)|spring]]s.
