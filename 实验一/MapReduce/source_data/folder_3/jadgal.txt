The '''Jadgal''' people an [[Indo-Aryan people|Indo-Aryan]] community, which live in [[Pakistan]] and [[Iran]], they are also found in [[Oman]], [[United Arab Emirates|UAE]] where they are called as Al Zadjali. < ref > {{Cite book|title=The Baloch and their neighbours: ethnic and linguistic contact in Balochistan in historical and modern times|date=2003|publisher=Reichert|isbn=978-3-89500-366-0|editor-last=Jahani|editor-first=Carina|location=Wiesbaden}} < /ref >  The Jadgal people speak the [[Jadgali language]]. The population of Jadgal people living in Pakistan was at about 100,000 in 1998. Jadgal people are mostly [[Muslim]].
