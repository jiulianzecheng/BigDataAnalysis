 '''Airai''', located on the southern coast of [[Babeldaob]] island, is the second-most populous [[States of Palau|state]] of [[Palau]]. It contains the country's chief airport, [[Roman Tmetuchl International Airport]], and is connected by the [[Koror–Babeldaob Bridge]] to nearby [[Koror Island]]. Its capital, largest village and third largest village of Palau is the village of the same Name Airai.
