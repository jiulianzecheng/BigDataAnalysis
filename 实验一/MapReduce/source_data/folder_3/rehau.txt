 '''Rehau''' is a [[Town#Germany|town]] in [[Hof (district)|Hof]], in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. The first documented name of Rehau was  " Resawe "  in the year 1234. Rehau is in the [[Fichtelgebirge]], 12 & nbsp;km southeast of [[Hof, Germany|Hof]], and 12 & nbsp;km west of [[Aš]]. It is near both Czech and former East German borders, with the fall of The Wall, it is now in the center of a growing area, with a new Autobahn providing access from Nuremberg and Munich.
