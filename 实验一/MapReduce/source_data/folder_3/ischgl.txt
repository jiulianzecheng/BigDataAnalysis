 '''Ischgl''' is a [[town]] in the [[Paznaun]] valley in the [[Austria]]n [[state]] of [[Tyrol (state)|Tyrol]]. The [[ski resort]] ''Silvretta Arena Ischgl-Samnaun'' is connected with the ski resort of [[Samnaun]] across the border in [[Switzerland]]. Together this ski area belongs to the largest skiing resorts in the [[Alps]]. Its 238 & nbsp;km of groomed pistes are served by over 45 mechanical lifts including [[cable car]]s, [[gondola lift|gondola]]s, [[detachable chairlift]]s and some [[T-bar lift|T-bars]].
