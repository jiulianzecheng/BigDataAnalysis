 '''Pork''' is [[meat]] that comes from [[pig]]s. It is the most commonly consumed meat in the world < ref name= " fao.org " > {{cite web |title=Sources of Meat |url=http://www.fao.org/ag/againfo/themes/en/meat/backgr_sources.html |publisher=Food and Agriculture Organization of the United Nations |date=16 March 2016 |accessdate=1 February 2021}} < /ref >  Evidence of pig [[animal husbandry|husbandry]] dates back to 5000 BC. 
 
 A simple meal to make which contains pork is [[afelia]]. [[Sausage]]s, [[bacon]] and [[ham]] are the most common uses of pork in the [[United Kingdom]], the [[United States]], and other parts of the [[Western world]].{{cn|date=February 2021}} Pork is forbidden by the food [[taboo]]s of [[kosher]] and [[halal]].
