 '''''Afrosataspes''''' is a [[genus]] of [[Moth|moths]]. It is in the [[Family (biology)|family]] of [[Sphingidae]]. It has only one [[species]], '''''Afrosataspes galleyi'''.'' It is from the [[Central African Republic]] and the [[Democratic Republic of the Congo]]. 
 
 It is a [[Mimicry|mimic]] of the ''Xylocopa'' carpenter bees. The adults are black and the antennae are short.
