 '''Gordevio''' was a [[municipality]] of the district of [[Vallemaggia (district)|Vallemaggia]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Ticino]] in [[Switzerland]]. On 20 April 2008, [[Avegno, Switzerland|Avegno]] and Gordevio merged to form the new municipality of [[Avegno Gordevio]]. < ref name=Fusion > [http://www.bfs.admin.ch/bfs/portal/de/index/infothek/nomenklaturen/blank/blank/gem_liste/03.html Amtliches Gemeindeverzeichnis der Schweiz] published by the Swiss Federal Statistical Office {{in lang|de}} accessed 14 January 2010 < /ref >
