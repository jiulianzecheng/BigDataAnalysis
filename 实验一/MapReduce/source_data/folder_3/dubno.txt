 '''Dubno''' ({{lang-uk|Ду́бно}}) is a city in [[Rivne Oblast]] ([[Oblast|province]]) of western [[Ukraine]]. It is the [[administrative centre]] of [[Dubno Raion]] ([[Raion|district]]). In 2020, around 37,464 people lived there.
