A '''calibre''' (or '''caliber''' in [[American English]]) is the diameter of the barrel of a [[gun]]. For example, a rifle with a diameter of 0.22 & nbsp;inch is a .22 cal. Guns that have a particular calibre are designed to fit and use bullets of the same calibre.
