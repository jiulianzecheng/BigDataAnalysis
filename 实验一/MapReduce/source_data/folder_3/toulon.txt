 '''Toulon''' is a city in the south of [[France]]. It has about 170,000 people. Toulon is the prefecture of [[Var (department)|Var]]. Toulon is the third biggest city of the region [[Provence-Alpes-Côte d'Azur]] after [[Marseille]] and [[Nice]]. It is the main base of the [[Mediterranean Sea]] fleet of the [[French Navy]]. It is {{convert|190|km|abbr=off}} from the Italian border.
