 '''''Saurosuchus''''' ( " lizard crocodile " ) was a huge [[extinct]] crocodile-like  [[pseudosuchia]]n [[archosaur]]. < ref > Heckert A.B; Lucas S.G.  &  Krzyzanowski S.E. 2002. The rauisuchian archosaur Saurosuchus from the Upper Triassic Chinle Group, Southwestern U.S.A., and its biochronological significance. In Heckert A.B.; Lucas S.G. (eds) Upper Triassic Stratigraphy and Paleontology, vol. 21, New Mexico Museum of Natural History and Science Bulletin. pp. 241–247. < /ref >   
 
 It lived in [[South America]] during the [[Upper Triassic]] period. It was a heavy, ground-dwelling, [[quadrupedal]] [[carnivore]]. Probably it was the [[apex predator]] in its territory (the [[Ischigualasto Formation]]. < ref name=Reig1959 > {{cite journal|last1=Reig|first1=O.
