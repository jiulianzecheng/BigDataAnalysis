 '''Valkeala''' is a former municipality in [[Kymenlaakso]], [[Finland]]. In 2003, 11,238 people lived there. It has an area of 1,004.40 & nbsp;km². In 2009, the six municipalities [[Anjalankoski]], [[Elimäki]], [[Jaala]], Kouvola, [[Kuusankoski]] and Valkeala were merged to form the city of [[Kouvola]]. 
 
 Valkeala is known for [[Repovesi National Park]].
