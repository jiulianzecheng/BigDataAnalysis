 '''Shontelle Layne''' (born October 4, 1985), or just '''Shontelle''', is a [[Barbados|Barbadian]] [[singer-songwriter]]. She mainly performs [[Contemporary R & B|R & B]], [[Pop music|pop]] and [[reggae]] music. She released her debut album ''[[Shontelligence]]'' in 2008 and her second album ''[[No Gravity (Shontelle album)|No Gravity]]'' in September 2010. Some if her recent songs achieved only modest success. She co-wrote  " Man Down "  by R & B singer [[Rihanna]] in 2010. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.elle.com/fashion/news/a6542/shontelle-on-being-rihannas-drill-sergeant-22128/|title=Shontelle on Being Rihanna's Drill Sergeant|work=Elle}} < /ref >
