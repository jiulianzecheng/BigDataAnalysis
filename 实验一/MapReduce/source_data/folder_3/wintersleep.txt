 '''Wintersleep''' is a [[Canada|Canadian]] [[indie rock]] [[band]] from [[Halifax Regional Municipality|Halifax]], [[Nova Scotia]]. Wintersleep has five members in the band; the current band members are Paul Murphy, Mike Bigelow, Loel Campbell, Tim D'eon and Jon Samuel. The band formed in 2001. They won a [[Juno Award]] for ''New Group of the Year'' in 2008.
