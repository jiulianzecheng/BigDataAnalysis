 '''Edgware''' ({{IPAc-en|ˈ|ɛ|dʒ|w|ɛər|}}) is a suburban town in northern [[Greater London]], mostly in the [[London Borough of Barnet]]. 
 
 As of 2011, the town had a population of 76,506 and is made up of five wards from both Barnet and Harrow boroughs. < ref > With the addition of the Hale ward http://hidden-london.com/gazetteer/edgware/ < /ref >
