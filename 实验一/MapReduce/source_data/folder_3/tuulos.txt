 '''Tuulos''' ({{lang-sv|Tulois}}) is a former [[municipality]] in [[Kanta-Häme]], [[Finland]]. In 2003, the municipality had a population of 1,536. Neighbouring municipalities were [[Hauho]], [[Hämeenlinna]], [[Janakkala]] and [[Lammi]]. 
 
 It was merged with [[Hämeenlinna]] in 2009.
