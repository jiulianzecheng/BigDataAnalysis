 '''''Ivanhoe''''' is a [[historical]] [[romantic]] [[novel]] set around the reign of [[Richard I of England]]. It was written by [[Scottish people|Scottish]] [[author]] [[Walter Scott]]. It was first printed in 1820. A 1952 movie version of the story starred [[Robert Taylor]], [[Elizabeth Taylor]], and [[Joan Fontaine]].
