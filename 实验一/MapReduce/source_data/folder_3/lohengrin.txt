 '''Lohengrin''' is a character in some [[Germany|German]] stories of [[Arthurian]] literature. He is the son of [[Parzival]] (Percival), he is a knight of the [[Holy Grail]] who is sent in a boat pulled by [[swan]]s to rescue a girl who is falsely accused of murder.  He must never tell anyone his name. His story is a version of the [[Knight of the Swan]] legend. It is particularly famous now as an [[opera]], ''[[Lohengrin (opera)|Lohengrin]]'' by [[Richard Wagner]] ([[1849]]).
