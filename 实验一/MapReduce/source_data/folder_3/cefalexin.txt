'''Cefalexin''' also known as '''cephalexin''' and sold under the brand names  Keflex and Ceporex is a type of [[medication|drug]] called an [[antibiotic]]. It is a type of antibiotic called a [[cephalosporin]]. < ref name= " :0 " > [https://www.drugs.com/monograph/cephalexin.html Cefalexin] at Drugs.com < /ref >  Cefalexin can treat some [[bacteria]]l infections in areas including the [[Otitis|ear]], [[bone]], [[joint]], [[skin]], and [[bladder]].  It may also be used for some types of [[pneumonia]] and [[strep throat]]. Like other antibiotics, cefalexin cannot treat [[Virus|viral]] infections, such as the [[Influenza|flu]] or [[common cold]]. < ref name= " :0 "  / >
