 '''''Rhinella''''' ('''Beaked Toads''' or '''Rio Viejo Toads''') is a subgenus of '''''Bufo'''''.  Some people call it its own [[genus]].  It has 71 species of [[true toad]]s.  They live in [[Central America|Central]] and [[South America]].
