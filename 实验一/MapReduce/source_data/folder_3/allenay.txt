 '''Allenay''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]] in the [[Somme]] [[Departments of France|department]] in [[Hauts-de-France]] in northern [[France]]. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 The commune is about 5 & nbsp;km from the [[English Channel]]. It is on the border of the departments of the [[Somme]] and [[Seine-Maritime]].
