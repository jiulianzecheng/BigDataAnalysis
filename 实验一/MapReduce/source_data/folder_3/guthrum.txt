 '''Guthrum''' or '''Guðrum''' (died c. 890 AD), christened Æthelstan when he converted to [[Christianity]] in 878 AD, was [[King]] of the [[Denmark|Danish]] [[Vikings]] in the Danelaw, the part of England ruled by the Danes. He is mainly known for his conflict with [[Alfred the Great]].
