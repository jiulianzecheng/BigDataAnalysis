 '''Untersteckholz''' was a [[municipality]] in [[Aarwangen (district)|Aarwangen]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Bern (canton)|Berne]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 On 1 January 2010 the Untersteckholz became part of [[Langenthal, Switzerland|Langenthal]].
