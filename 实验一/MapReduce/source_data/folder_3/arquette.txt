'''Arquette''' is a [[surname]]. People with this name include an [[Americans|American]] family, many of whom are [[actor]]s: 
 
 *[[Cliff Arquette]] (1904-1974), an actor who was popular on ''[[The Tonight Show]]'' 
 *Cliff's son [[Lewis Arquette]] (1935-2001) 
 *Lewis' children: [[Rosanna Arquette]] (born 1959), [[Richmond Arquette]] (born 1963), [[Patricia Arquette]] (born 1968), [[Alexis Arquette]] (1969-2016) and [[David Arquette]] (born 1971).
