 '''Scarperia''' was a ''[[comune]]'' in the [[Metropolitan City of Florence]] in the [[Italy|Italian]] region of [[Tuscany]]. On 1 January 2014, the former municipalities of Scarperia and [[San Piero a Sieve]] merged to form the new municipality of [[Scarperia e San Piero]].
