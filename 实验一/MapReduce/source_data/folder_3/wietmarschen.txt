 '''Wietmarschen''' is a unitary community (''Einheitsgemeinde'') in the [[Grafschaft Bentheim|County of Bentheim]] in [[Lower Saxony]], [[Germany]]. It is split into the villages of Wietmarschen, F端chtenfeld, Schwartenpohl, Lohnerbruch, Nordlohne and Lohne with Lohne being the biggest and having the town hall while Wietmarschen, which is the second biggest, having the name. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 Wietmarschen is about {{convert|8|km|mi|0}} west of [[Lingen]], and {{convert|13|km|mi|0}} northeast of [[Nordhorn]]. The community's highest elevation is the Rupingberg in Lohne at {{convert|50|m|ft}} above [[sea level]].  There are plans to construct a viewing tower on it. 
 
 ===Constituent communities=== 
 The community is divided into six [[Ortsteil|districts]] named Wietmarschen, F端chtenfeld, Schwartenpohl, Lohnerbruch, Nordlohne and Lohne. 
 
 ===Partnership=== 
 [[Mortagne-au-Perche]], [[France]] became a partner community of Wietmarschen on 2 July 1989.
