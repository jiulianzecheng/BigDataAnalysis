A '''pedal''' is something that is made to work by someone's [[foot]].  The word  " pedal "  comes from the [[Latin]] word for  " foot " .  Many things can be made to work by pedals.  These include: 
 
 *a [[car]] ([[automobile]]) which has a [[brake]] pedal, an accelerator pedal and sometimes a [[clutch]]; 
 *a [[musical instrument]] such as [[piano]], [[harp]] or [[pipe organ|organ]] (see [[pedal (music)]]); 
 *a [[spinning wheel]] 
 *a [[sewing machine]] (in the days before [[electricity]])
