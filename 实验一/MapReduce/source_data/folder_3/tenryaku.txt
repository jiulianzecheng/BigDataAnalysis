{{nihongo|'''Tenryaku'''|天暦}} was a {{nihongo|[[Japanese era name]]|年号,|''nengō'',|lit.  " year name " }} after ''[[Tengyō]]'' and before ''[[Tentoku]].''  This period started in April 947 and ended in October 957. < ref > Nussbaum, Louis-Frédéric. (2005). [https://books.google.com/books?id=p2QnPijAEmEC & pg=PA961  " ''Tenryaku'' " ] in ''Japan Encyclopedia'', p. 961. < /ref >  The reigning emperor was {{nihongo|[[Emperor Murakami|Murakami]]''-tennō''|村上天皇}}. < ref > [[Isaac Titsingh|Titsingh, Isaac]]. (1834). ''Annales ds empereurs du japon'', [https://books.google.com/books?id=18oNAAAAIAAJ & pg=PA139  pp. 139-142]; Brown, Delmer ''et al.'' (1979). [https://books.google.com/books?id=w4f5FrmIJKIC & pg=PA295 ''Gukanshō'', pp. 295-298]; Varley, H. Paul. (1980). ''Jinnō Shōtōki'', pp. 183-190. < /ref > 
 
 ==Events of the ''Tenryaku'' era== 
 * '''947''' (''Tenryaku 1, 9th month''): Construction began on the [[Kitano Tenman-gū|Kitano Shrine]]. < ref name= " titsingh139 " > Titsingh, [https://books.google.com/books?id=18oNAAAAIAAJ & pg=PA139 p. 139]. < /ref > 
 * '''947''' (''Tenryaku 1, 11th month''): The emperor went hunting at [[Uji]]. < ref name= " titsingh139 " / > 
 * '''948''' (''Tenryaku 2''): There was a great drought in the summer and strong rains in the autumn. < ref name= " titsingh139 " / > 
 * '''29 September 948''' (''Tenryaku 2, 24th day of the 8th month'') < !-- NengoCalc 天暦二年八月二十四日 -- > : The sun and the moon were both visible in the sky at the same time. < ref name= " titsingh139 " / > 
 * '''949''' (''Tenryaku 3, 14th day of the 8th month''): Fujiwara no Tadahira died at the age of 70.  He had been ''sesshō'' for 20 years, and he was ''kampaku'' for 8 years. < ref name= " titsingh140 " > Titsingh, [https://books.google.com/books?id=18oNAAAAIAAJ & pg=PA140 p. 140]. < /ref > 
 * '''9 September 949''' (''Tenryaku 3, 9th month''): Former-[[Emperor Yōzei]] died at the age of 82. < ref > Titsingh, [https://books.google.com/books?id=18oNAAAAIAAJ & pg=PA140 p. 140]; [[Imperial Household Agency]] (''Kunaichō''), [http://www.kunaicho.go.jp/ryobo/guide/057/index.html (陽成天皇 (57)]; retrieved 2012-5-22. < /ref > 
 * '''950''' (''Tenryaku 4, 7th month''): Murakami causes a proclamation that his infant son, Norihira, will be his official heir and Crown Prince. < ref name= " titsingh140 " / > 
 * '''951''' (''Tenryaku 5''): The pagoda at [[Daigo-ji]] is now the oldest building in Kyoto. 
 * '''7 October 952''' (''Tenryaku 6, 15th day of the 8th month'') < !-- 天暦六年九月十六日  -- > : Former-[[Emperor Suzaku]] died at the age of 30. < ref > Brown, p.295; Varley, p. 130; ''Kunaichō'', [http://www.kunaicho.go.jp/ryobo/guide/061/index.html 朱雀天皇 (61)]; retrieved 2012-5-22. < /ref >
