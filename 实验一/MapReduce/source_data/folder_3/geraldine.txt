'''Geraldine''' is a female [[given name]].The pronunciation of the name is [ '''jer'''-''uh''l-deen ]. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Definition of Geraldine {{!}} Dictionary.com|url=https://www.dictionary.com/browse/geraldine|access-date=2023-03-22|website=www.dictionary.com|language=en}} < /ref >   It is a female version of the name ''Gerald''. The name means  " ruler of spear " < ref > {{Cite web|title=Geraldine name Meaning and Origin|url=https://babys.name/detail-Geraldine-Meaning-Origin-Famous-Similar-Sibling.html|access-date=2023-03-22|website=babys.name}} < /ref >  .This is a unique name (not in the top 1000 newborn names in the US in 2021). < ref > {{Cite web|last=OACT|title=Popular Baby Names|url=https://www.ssa.gov/cgi-bin/popularnames.cgi|access-date=2023-03-22|website=www.ssa.gov}} < /ref >  There are many famous people having the name, such as [[Geraldine Ferraro]], [[Geri Halliwell]] and [[Geraldine James]].
