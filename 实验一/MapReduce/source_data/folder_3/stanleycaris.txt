 '''''Stanleycaris''''' (meaning  " crab of Stanley [[Glacier]] " ) is an extinct, [[monotypic]] [[genus]] of [[Anomalocarididie|anomalocaridid]] which existed in [[Canada]], during the middle [[Cambrian]].
