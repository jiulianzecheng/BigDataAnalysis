 '''''Maladolescenza''''' is a 1977 [[love|romance]]-[[drama movie]]. It is about three children: Sylvia, Laura and Fabrizio. Fabrizio is a sinister and cruel boy. He kills one of the teenage girls so she will never leave him. This movie was very controversial in several countries. It was refused classification or banned in several areas, particularly [[Germany]]. Modern reviews have called the movie a strong artistic work, although some disapprove of the material inside this movie.
