 '''Innertkirchen''' is a [[municipality]] in the [[administrative district]] of [[Interlaken-Oberhasli (administrative district)|Interlaken-Oberhasli]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Bern (canton)|Bern]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 On 1 January 2014, the former municipality of [[Gadmen]] merged into the municipality of Innertkirchen.
