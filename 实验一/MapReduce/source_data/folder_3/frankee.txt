 '''Nicole Francine Aiello''' (born June 9, 1983) is an [[Americans|American]]  [[Contemporary R & B|R & B]] [[singer-songwriter]] and [[Model (people)|model]] known by her [[stage name]], '''Frankee'''. She grew up with her parents, Matthew and Francine, and her two siblings, elder brother Matthew Jr. and younger sister Monique. Nicole was given the nickname  " Frankee, "  which came from her middle name (and is also her grandfather's nickname). < ref name=myspace > [http://www.myspace.com/therealfrankee Frankee's MySpace page] < /ref >  She is best known for her debut single  " [[F.U.R.B. (Fuck You Right Back)]] "  which is a answer back to fellow American R & B singer-songwriter [[Eamon (singer)|Eamon]]{{'}}s debut single  " [[Fuck It (I Don't Want You Back)]] " . 
 
 ==Career== 
 " F.U.R.B. (Fuck You Right Back) "  reached number one in the [[United Kingdom]] in May 2004, < ref name= " British Hit Singles  &  Albums " / >  and [[Australia]] in June 2004. She then released her debut [[studio album]] later on that year. 
 She then left her label in 2006 and signed with Big Management < ref name= " yahoo " > [http://music.yahoo.com/release/33623143  " Frankee - Watch Me download on Yahoo! Music - which shows record label " ] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20061120160544/http://music.yahoo.com/release/33623143 |date=2006-11-20 }}. ''yahoo.com''. < /ref >  She has also modelled for ''[[FHM]]'' and [[Maxim (magazine)|''Maxim'']] magazines.
