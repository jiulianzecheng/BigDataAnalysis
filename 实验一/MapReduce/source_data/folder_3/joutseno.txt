 '''Joutseno''' is a former town and municipality of Finland. It is now part of [[Lappeenranta]]. Joutseno had a population of 10,821 in 2004. It covers an area of {{convert|498.8|km2|abbr=on}}, of which about {{convert|188|km2|abbr=on}} is water.
