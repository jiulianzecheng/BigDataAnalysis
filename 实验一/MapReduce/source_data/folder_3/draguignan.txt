 '''Draguignan''' ({{lang-oc|Draguinhan}}) is a [[Communes of France|commune]] in the [[Var (department)|Var]] [[Departments of France|department]] in the [[Provence-Alpes-Côte d'Azur]] [[Regions of France|region]], in southeastern [[France]]. 
 
 It is a [[Prefectures in France|subprefecture]] of the department. 
 
 ==History== 
 Draguignan became the [[Prefectures in France|prefecture]] of the [[Var (department)|Var]] in 1797, at the beginning of the [[French Revolution]]. This was even if the town was not the biggest city in the department. It remained as the prefecture until 1974 when it was moved to [[Toulon]]. < ref name=splaf > {{cite web |url=http://splaf.free.fr/83his.html |title=Historique du Var |work=Le SPLAF |language=fr |access-date=26 November 2016 }} < /ref > 
 
 ==Geography== 
 The city is at the foot of the Malmont [[mountain]], in a [[valley]] that is about {{Convert|2|km|mi|abbr=on }} long, from northwest to southeast. The Nartuby, a [[tributary]] of the Argens [[river]], flows to the west of the city. 
 
 Draguignan has an area of {{Convert|53.8|km2|sqmi|abbr=on }}, < ref name=insee > {{cite web|url=https://www.insee.fr/fr/statistiques/1405599?geo=COM-83050 |title=Comparateur de territoire - Commune de Draguignan (83050) |publisher=Institut national de la statistique et des études économiques - INSEE |language=fr |access-date=26 November 2016 }} < /ref >   and its average [[altitude]] is {{Convert|376|m|ft|abbr=on}}; at the [[City and town halls|city hall]], the altitude is {{Convert|200|m|ft|abbr=on }}. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://www.map-france.com/Draguignan-83300/ |title=Draguignan |publisher=Map-France.com |access-date=27 November 2016 }} < /ref >
