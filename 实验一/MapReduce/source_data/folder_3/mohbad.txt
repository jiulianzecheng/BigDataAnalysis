'''Ilerioluwa Oladimeji Aloba''' (8 June 1996 - 12 September 2023) was a Nigerian musician who was known all over the world as a rapper. He died in suspicious circumstances on 12 September 2023 only to be buried the following day. However, the Lagos State Police Command has exhumed his body and an autopsy has been carried out in order to determine the actual cause of his death. The Police has also arrested the nurse who treated the late singer before he died.
