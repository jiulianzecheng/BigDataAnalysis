 The city of '''Rajshahi''' is the headquarters of [[Rajshahi Division]] and Rajshahi District.  It is one of the six metropolitan cities of [[Bangladesh]]. It is called ''Silk City'' and ''Education City''. There are around 1,000,000 people. < ref name=bangladesh2 > {{Cite web |url=http://www.bbs.gov.bd/dataindex/pby/pk_book_08.pdf |title= " Statistical Pocket book 2008, Bangladesh Bureau of Statistics "  |access-date=2012-05-21 |archive-date=2009-04-19 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20090419080055/http://www.bbs.gov.bd/dataindex/pby/pk_book_08.pdf |url-status=dead }} < /ref >  It is on the northern banks of the river [[Padma]].
