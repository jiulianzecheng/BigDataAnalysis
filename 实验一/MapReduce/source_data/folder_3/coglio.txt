 '''Coglio''' is a village and former municipality in the [[district]] of [[Vallemaggia (district)|Vallemaggia]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Ticino]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 On 4 April 2004 the former municipalities of [[Aurigeno]], Coglio, [[Giumaglio]], [[Lodano]], [[Moghegno]] and [[Someo]] merged into the municipality of [[Maggia, municipality|Maggia]].
