 To distort is to change the [[shape]] of something. Distortion may refer to: 
 
 *Distortion in [[Signal (electrical engineering)|electrical signal]] 
 *Distortion, a type of musical effect that can be controlled using an [[effect pedal]] 
 *[[Distortion (economics)]], a state where the agent does not maximize its [[profit]] or [[utility]] 
 *[[Cognitive distortion]], irrational thought patterns 
 *In [[statistics]], the [[bias of an estimator]] is sometimes called distortion 
 *In [[medicine]], a [[Strain (injury)]] is called distortion
