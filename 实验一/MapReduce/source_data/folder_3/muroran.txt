 '''Muroran''' {{nihongo|室蘭市|Muroran-shi}} is a [[city]] and [[port]] in [[Iburi subprefecture|Iburi]], [[Hokkaidō]], [[Japan]].  It is the capital city of Iburi Subprefecture.
