 '''Gommiswald''' is a [[municipality]] in [[See-Gaster (Wahlkreis)|See-Gaster]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[St. Gallen (canton)|St. Gallen]] in [[Switzerland]].  On 1 January 2013, the municipalities [[Ernetschwil]] and [[Rieden, Switzerland|Rieden]] joined together to become the municipality called Gommiswald. < ref name=Fusion > [http://www.bfs.admin.ch/bfs/portal/de/index/infothek/nomenklaturen/blank/blank/gem_liste/03.html Amtliches Gemeindeverzeichnis der Schweiz] published by the Swiss Federal Statistical Office {{in lang|de}} accessed 2 January 2013 < /ref >
