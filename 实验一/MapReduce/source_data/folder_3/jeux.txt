 '''''Jeux''''' ({{lang-fr|Games}}) is a modern [[ballet]]. It was choreographed by [[Nijinksy]] to music by [[Claude Debussy]]. It starred [[Nijinsky]], [[Tamara Karsavina]], and Ludmilla Schollar. It was first performed in the Théâtre des Champs Elysees in [[Paris]] on May 15, 1913. Grace Robert writes that the ballet was received  " very tepidly " .
