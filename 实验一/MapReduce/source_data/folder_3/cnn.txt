 The '''Cable News Network''' ('''CNN''') is an [[cable television|American cable]] news [[television channel]]. It was founded in 1980 by [[Ted Turner]]. < ref > [http://meandted.com/author.htm Reese Schonfeld Bio.] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20080513025424/http://meandted.com/author.htm |date=2008-05-13 }} (January 29, 2001) ''MeAndTed.com''. Retrieved 2007-06-18. < /ref > < ref > [http://cnn.com/COMMUNITY/transcripts/2000/5/8/bierbauer/ Charles Bierbauer, CNN senior Washington correspondent, discusses his 19-year career at CNN.] (May 8, 2000). ''CNN.com''. Retrieved 2007-06-18. < /ref >  The Cable News Network first aired on television on June 1, 1980. The Cable News Network's first newscast was anchored (hosted) by [[David Walker (journalist)|David Walker]] and his wife Lois Hart. < ref > {{Cite web|title=CNN's first broadcast: June 1, 1980|url=https://cnnpressroom.blogs.cnn.com/2011/06/01/cnns-first-broadcast-june-1-1980/|access-date=2022-03-23|language=en-US}} < /ref >  In its first year CNN hired many political analysts, including [[Rowland Evans]] and [[Robert Novak]]. < ref > {{Cite web|title=The Telegraph - Google News Archive Search|url=https://news.google.com/newspapers?nid=2209 & dat=19810516 & id=P6krAAAAIBAJ & sjid=IP0FAAAAIBAJ & pg=4291,3826132|access-date=2022-03-23|website=news.google.com}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite web|title=Star-News - Google News Archive Search|url=https://news.google.com/newspapers?nid=1454 & dat=19800529 & id=v8gsAAAAIBAJ & sjid=RhMEAAAAIBAJ & pg=7138,6800547|access-date=2022-03-23|website=news.google.com}} < /ref > < ref > {{cite book| last1 = Turner| first1 = Ted| last2 = Burke| first2 = Bill| title = Call Me Ted| url = https://books.google.com/books?id=O8bQBgj2znwC & pg=PA126| year = 2008| publisher = Grand Central Publishing| isbn = 978-0-446-54336-1| page = 126 }} < /ref >  On January 1, 1982 CNN launched a 24-hour sister newscast channel with no talk shows or commentary shows called [[:en:HLN (TV network)|CNN2]]. < ref > {{Cite web|title=St. Petersburg Times - Google News Archive Search|url=https://news.google.com/newspapers?nid=888 & dat=19810821 & id=8Z4oAAAAIBAJ & sjid=OnsDAAAAIBAJ & pg=3119,5087088|access-date=2022-03-23|website=news.google.com}} < /ref >  CNN [[broadcast]]s programs from its headquarters at the [[CNN Center]] in [[Atlanta]], or from the [[Deutsche Bank Center]] in New York City, or from studios in [[Washington, D.C.]], and Los Angeles. CNN is owned by [[Warner Bros. Discovery]], and the U.S. news channel is a part of the [[CNN Global]]. 
 
 ==Shows== 
 The hosts of its opinion shows are [[Don Lemon]], [[Chris Cuomo]],  Fredricka Whitfield,  Erin Burnett, Brianna Keiler and Brooke Baldwin.
