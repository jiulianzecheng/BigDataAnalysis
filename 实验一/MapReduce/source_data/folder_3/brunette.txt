 '''Elen Yeremyan''' (born 27 May 2001) is an [[Armenia|Armenian]] singer-songwriter who is known by her [[stage name]] '''Brunette'''. 
 
 Born in the Armenian capital [[Yerevan]], she has been singing since the age of 4 and has been songwriting since 15. < ref > {{cite web|title=Big news from Armenia: Brunette was chosen to represent her home country at the Eurovision Song Contest 2023 in Liverpool|url=https://eurovoxx.tv/brunette-will-represent-armenia-at-eurovision-2023/|website=eurovoxx.tv}} < /ref >  Brunette released her first song at the age of 18 in September 2019. < ref > {{cite web|title=Armenian Pop Singer Brunette Releases 'Love the Way You Feel' in Support of Non-Profit Nvak|url=https://www.onestowatch.com/en/blog/armenian-pop-singer-brunette-releases-love-the|website=onestowatch.com}} < /ref > 
 
 In 2023, she was chosen to represent Armenia at the [[Eurovision Song Contest 2023]] with her song  " Future Lover " . The public voted for her to be one of the 26 finalists.
