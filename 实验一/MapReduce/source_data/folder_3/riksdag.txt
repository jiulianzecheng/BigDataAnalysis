 The '''Parliament of Sweden''' ({{lang-sv|Sveriges riksdag}}) is the legislative government of [[Sweden]]. The [[parliament]] building is on the [[Stockholm]] [[island]] of [[Helgeandsholmen]]. The parliament has 349 seats since 1976 (350 between 1971 and 1976). Between 1867 and 1971, the parliament had two chambers, but has been reduced to one. [[Elections in Sweden|Elections]] are held in [[September]] every fourth year (third year between 1970-1994).
