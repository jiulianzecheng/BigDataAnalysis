 '''''Bajaichthys''''' is an extinct genus of Eocene [[Dory (fish)|zeid]]. It includes one species, '''''Bajaichthys elegans'''''. It is sometimes mistaken for a [[Lampriformes|lampriform]].  
 
 == Description == 
 The holotype is four centimeters in length. It has a deep, [[oblong]]-shaped body, a deep, but short [[head]], a long [[tail]] composed of 27 caudal [[Vertebra|vertebrae]], a long, crest-like [[dorsal fin]], a long, banner-like [[Fin|anal fin]] with 29 to 30 rays running from the posterior region of the body down the entire length of the tail, and two enormous, wing-like [[Pelvic fin|pelvic fins]].
