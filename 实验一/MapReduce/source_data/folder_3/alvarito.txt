'''Álvaro Rodríguez Ros''' (16 January 1936 – 16 June 2018), commonly known as '''Alvarito''', was a Spanish [[Association football|football]] [[Defender (association football)|defender]] and [[Manager (association football)|manager]]. He was born in [[Ujo]], [[Mieres, Asturias|Mieres]]. In 1960, he played for the [[Spain national football team]]. From 1957 to 1963, Rodríguez Ros played for [[Atlético Madrid]].
