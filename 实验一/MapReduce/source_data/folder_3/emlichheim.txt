 '''Emlichheim''' ([[Low German]]: '''Emmelkamp''') is a municipality in the [[County of Bentheim (district)|County of Bentheim]] in Lower Saxony, Germany, about 20 & nbsp;km south of [[Emmen, Netherlands|Emmen]], and 25 & nbsp;km northwest of [[Nordhorn]]. 
 
 == Geography == 
 Emlichheim is right on the German-[[Netherlands|Dutch]] [[border]]. The community is bordered on the south by the river [[Vechte]] and on the north by the Coevorden-Picardy Canal. It is the administrative seat of the ''Samtgemeinde Emlichheim'', whose members also include [[Hoogstede]], [[Laar (Grafschaft Bentheim)|Laar]] and [[Ringe, Germany|Ringe]].
