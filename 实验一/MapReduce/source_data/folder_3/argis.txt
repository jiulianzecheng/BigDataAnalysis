 '''Argis''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is in the [[Auvergne-Rhône-Alpes]] [[Regions of France|region]] of the [[Ain]] [[Departments of France|department]]; in the east of [[France]]. In 2009, 421 people lived in the commune.  
 
 The [[inhabitant]]s are the ''Argissiens''. In the [[Middle Ages]] there was a [[castle]] in Argis; but it does not exist anymore. There are also two [[cascade]]s in the commune, the ''cascade de la côte'' and the ''cascade de la Pissoire''.
