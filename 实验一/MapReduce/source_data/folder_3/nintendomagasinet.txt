 '''Nintendomagasinet''' ('''NM''') was a [[Sweden|Swedish]] [[video game]] [[magazine]]. It was published between the years of [[1990]] and [[1994]]. < ref > [http://www.seriesam.com/cgi-bin/guide?s=Nintendomagasinet+(1990) Seriesamlarna: Accessdate: 10 November 2013] < /ref >  The magazine contained news, reports and comics based on video games. It also had a subscriber-only  " Power Player "  supplement which consisted of game reviews. The comics were translations of the [[Nintendo Comics System]] comics. Later it changed to the [[Nintendo Power]] comics ''[[Super Mario Adventures]]'' and ''The Legend of Zelda''.
