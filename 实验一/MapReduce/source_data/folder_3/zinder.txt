'''Zinder''' is a city in [[Niger]]. It is the capital of [[Zinder Region]]. About 323,000 people live in Zinder. Until 1926, Zinder was the capital of Niger, since then, [[Niamey]] is the capital. Zinder is important for trade. In the region around Zinder [[Peanut|peanuts]] are grown.
