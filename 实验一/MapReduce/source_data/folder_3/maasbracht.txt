 '''Maasbracht''' used to be a [[municipality]] in the [[Netherlands|Dutch]] province of [[Limburg (Netherlands)|Limburg]]. Since 2007, it has been part of the new municipality of [[Maasgouw]].  
 
 Football player [[Mark van Bommel]] was born here.
