 The '''Lepidosauromorphs''' are a large group of [[reptile]]s. It includes all [[diapsid]]s closer to [[lizard]]s than to [[archosaur]]s.  
 
 The only living sub-group is the [[Lepidosauria]]: lizards, [[snake]]s, and the [[tuatara]].  
 
 The most important [[fossil]] group, the [[Sauropterygia]], includes the [[plesiosaur]]s.
