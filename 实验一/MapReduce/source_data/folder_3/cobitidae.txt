The '''Cobitidae''', also known as '''true loaches''', are a family of Old World freshwater fish. Cobitidae is also known as '''freshwater loach''', '''snake carp''', '''weatherfish''', or '''elongated freshwater snakecarp loach'''. They live in freshwater lakes.
