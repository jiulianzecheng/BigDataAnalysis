 '''Prayagraj''' ({{lang-hi|प्रयागराज}}), is a city in [[Uttar Pradesh]] state of [[India]]. It is located where three [[Ganges]] and [[Yamuna]] and [[Saraswati]] rivers join. This city is a holy city for [[Hindus]]. It is best known for Kumbha Mela. This fair occurs every 12th year in Prayagraj. The city was named ''Allahabad'' by the [[Mughal Empire|Mughal]] emperor [[Akbar]]. 
 
 Previously Prayagraj known as '''Allahabad'''.  ===Administration=== 
 A civic body called Prayagraj Nagar nigam adminiters the whole city,City comprises 1 police zone and 3 circles of district. And yes its administration is very strict 
 
 ===Roadways=== 
 
 As a main city, various roads connecting the other places. 
 G.T road passes through the city. 
 
 ===Railways=== 
 
 There are 7 main stations in the city. Prayagraj junction is the headquarter of North Central Railway Zone. Daily thousands of trains go to the different routes.   
 
 ===Proposed Rapid transit=== 
 
 In city ,Many lines are proposed for better connections a light rail metro is proposed.
