 '''Hartlepool''' is a town near the [[A689 road]] in [[County Durham]], England. 
 
 ==The Hartlepool monkey== 
 There is a [[legend]] that during the [[Napoleonic Wars]], a French ship was wrecked off the coast near Hartlepool. The captain's pet, a monkey, was washed ashore. < ref name=hpm > {{cite web |url= http://www.thisishartlepool.co.uk/history/thehartlepoolmonkey.asp |title=The Hartlepool Monkey, Who hung the monkey? - This is Hartlepool |first= |last= |work=thisishartlepool.co.uk |year=2011 |accessdate=30 April 2011}} < /ref >  The people from Hartlepool thought it was a person and tried to ask it questions. When they were unable to understand the monkey they assumed it was a French spy. < ref name=hpm/ >  A trial was held on the beach and the monkey was sentenced to death and hanged. < ref name=hpm/ >  This event is remembered in several songs. The local football team, [[Hartlepool United F.C.]], used to greeted with cries of  " Who hung the monkey? " . < ref name=hpm/ >  Their [[mascot]] is a monkey called  " H'Angus the Monkey " . The rugby football team, the Hartlepool Rovers, are known as the Monkeyhangers. < ref name=hpm/ >
