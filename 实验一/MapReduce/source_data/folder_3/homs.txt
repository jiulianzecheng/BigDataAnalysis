 '''Homs''' ({{lang-ar| < big > حمص < /big > }} ''{{transl|ar|DIN|Ḥimṣ}}'') is the third largest city in [[Syria]]. It used to be called ''Emesa'' ([[Ancient Greek|Greek]]: Ἔμεσα, ''Emesa'').  It is the capital of both the [[Homs District]], and the [[Homs Governorate]]. It has 652,609 people (2004).  It is on the [[Orontes River]]. 
 
 Homs is home to the [[Al-Baath University]].  The university is one of four major universities in Syria. 
 
 Homs has two [[association football|football]] clubs. The [[Al-Karamah SC|Al-Karamah]] Sports Club was founded in 1928 and is one the oldest sports club in Syria. < ref name= " welt " > {{cite web|url=http://www.weltfussballarchiv.com/Vereinsprofilnew.php?ID=22055|title=Al-Karamah Sports Club|publisher=Welt Fussball Archive|accessdate=27 February 2009|archive-date=16 February 2010|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20100216113334/http://www.weltfussballarchiv.com/Vereinsprofilnew.php?ID=22055|url-status=dead}} < /ref >  The [[Al-Karamah SC|Al-Karamah]] Sports Club has won eight [[Syrian League]] titles, and eight [[Syrian Cup]] titles. < ref > Carter, 2004, p. & nbsp;67. < /ref >  The second sports club of the city is [[Al-Wathba]] Sports Club.  They started in 1937. The [[Khaled Ibn Al Walid Stadium]] has a 35,000-seat capacity.
