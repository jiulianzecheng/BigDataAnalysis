'''Monday''' is a day of the week. In some countries, it is the second day of the [[week]]. In other parts of the world, including some countries in [[Europe]], Monday is the first day of the week. 
 
 Monday is the day in which is celebrated [[Easter Monday]]. 
 
 The [[International Organization for Standardization|International Standards Organization]] considers Monday the first day of the week.
