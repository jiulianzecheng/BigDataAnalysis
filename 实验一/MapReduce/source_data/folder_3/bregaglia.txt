 '''Bregaglia''' ({{lang-rm|{{Audio|Roh-putèr-Bregaglia.ogg|italic=yes|Bregaglia}}}}) is a [[municipality]] of the [[district]] of [[Maloja (district)|Maloja]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Graubünden]] in [[Switzerland]]. It was formed by the 2010 merger of the municipalities of [[Bondo, Switzerland|Bondo]], [[Castasegna]], [[Soglio, Switzerland|Soglio]], [[Stampa]] and [[Vicosoprano]], all in the [[Val Bregaglia]].
