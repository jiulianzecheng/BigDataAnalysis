 '''Neelo''' (born '''Abida Riaz'''; 30 June 1940  & ndash; 30 January 2021) was an [[actress]] from [[Lahore City]]. She was the mother of the [[movie]] actor [[Shaan]]. She was born June 29, 1945 in [[Bhera]], near [[Sargodha]].  
 
 Later, when she got married to [[Riaz Shahid]] she changed her name to Abida Riaz, and also became a [[Muslim]]. Shahid died in 1972, but Riaz has kept her name the same.
