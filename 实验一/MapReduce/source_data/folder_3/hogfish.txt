 The '''hogfish''' (''Lachnolaimus maximus''), also known as '''boquinete''', '''doncella de pluma''' or '''pez perro''' in Mexico) is a [[species]] of fish which lives in the [[Atlantic Ocean|Western Atlantic Ocean]]. The hogfish lives in the area from [[Nova Scotia]], [[Canada]], to northern [[South America]], including the [[Gulf of Mexico]]. It is classified as a [[wrasse]].
