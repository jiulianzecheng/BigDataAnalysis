 The River '''Eider''' is the largest [[river]] in the [[Germany|German]] county [[Schleswig-Holstein]]. It is the border river between Schleswig and Holstein. The spring is near [[Kiel]]. The Eider flows into the [[North Sea]] at a barrage diversion dam.
