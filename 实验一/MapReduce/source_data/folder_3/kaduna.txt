 '''Kaduna''' is the state capital of [[Kaduna State]] in north-western [[Nigeria]], on the [[Kaduna River]]. It is the capital of the former Northern Region of Nigeria < ref > {{Cite web|title=Kaduna State|url=https://kdsg.gov.ng/about-kaduna/}} < /ref > . The first premier of Northern Nigeria, Alhaji Ahmadu Bello, conducted government business from Kaduna < ref > {{Cite web|title=ahmadubello.com|url=https://www.arewaonline-ng.com/sardauna/about%20sardauna.html|access-date=2023-12-15|website=www.arewaonline-ng.com}} < /ref > .
