 '''Kulanu''', Hebrew: כולנו , meaning ''All of Us'', was an [[Israel]]i political party. 
 
 == Overview == 
 Its ideology was [[centrist]] [[social liberalism|social liberal]], and [[Zionist]]. It was interested mainly with economic matters. Its political leaders were [[Yifat Shasha-Biton]] and then [[Moshe Kahlon]]. 
 
 Kulanu was founded as a branch of [[Likud]] in 2014 and rejoined Likud in July 2019. It participated in the [[Fourth Netanyahu Government]] with 10 seats, and since April 2019 with 4 seats.
