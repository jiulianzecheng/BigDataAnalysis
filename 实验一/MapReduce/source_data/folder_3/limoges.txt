 '''Limoges''' ({{lang-oc|Lemòtges}} or ''Limòtges'') is a city and [[Communes of France|commune]], the [[Capital city|capital]] ([[Prefectures in France|prefecture]]) of the [[Haute-Vienne]] [[Departments of France|department]] and the [[historical]] [[Capital city|capital]] of the old [[Limousin]] region in west-central [[France]]. It is also the [[Capital city|capital]] of the ''[[Arrondissements of France|arrondissement]]'' of the same name. 
 
 Limoges is known for its medieval and Renaissance [[Vitreous enamel|enamels]] on copper, for its 19th-century [[porcelain]] and for its [[oak]] [[barrel]]s which are used for [[Cognac (drink)|Cognac]] and Bordeaux production. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 The city of Limoges is near the western limits of the [[Massif Central]], in the centre of the Haute-Vienne department. The [[Vienne (river)|Vienne]] [[river]] flows through the city. 
 
 Limoges is at about {{Convert|392|km|mi|abbr=on }} from [[Paris]], {{Convert|290|km|mi|abbr=on }} from [[Toulouse]], {{Convert|221|km|mi|abbr=on }} from [[Bordeaux]] and {{Convert|692|km|mi|abbr=on }} from [[Marseille]]. < ref name= " annuaire " > {{cite web |work=Annuaire-Mairie.fr |publisher= |title=Ville de Limoges |url=http://www.annuaire-mairie.fr/ville-limoges.html |language=French  |accessdate=14 April 2017}} < /ref > 
 
 The ''commune'' has an area of {{Convert|78|km2|sqmi|abbr=on }}. < ref name= " insee-geo " > {{cite web |url=https://www.insee.fr/fr/statistiques/1405599?geo=COM-87085 |title=Commune de Limoges (87085) |work=Comparateur de territoire |publisher=Institut national de la statistique et des études économiques - INSEE |format= |language=French |accessdate=14 April 2017 |date= }} < /ref >  Its average [[altitude]] is {{Convert|320|m|ft|abbr=on}}; at the [[City and town halls|city hall]], the altitude is {{Convert|306|m|ft|abbr=on }}. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://www.map-france.com/Limoges-87280/ |title=Limoges |publisher=Map-France.com |language= |accessdate=14 April 2017 |date= }} < /ref >

 ===Climate=== 
 The climate of Limoges, in the [[Köppen climate classification]], is '''Cfb''' - [[Oceanic climate]] with template summers. < ref name= " annuaire "  / > 
 
 The [[average]] amount of [[precipitation]] for the year in Limoges is {{Convert|726.4|mm|in|1|abbr=on }}. The month with the most precipitation on average is October with {{Convert|86.4|mm|in|1|abbr=on }} of precipitation. The month with the least precipitation on average is February with an average of {{Convert|45.7|mm|in|1|abbr=on }}. < ref name= " wbase " > {{cite web|publisher=Weatherbase |title=Limoges, France - Köppen Climate Classification |url=http://www.weatherbase.com/weather/weather-summary.php3?s=598858 & cityname=Limoges%2C+Limousin%2C+France & units= |language= |accessdate=14 April 2017}} < /ref > 
 
 The average temperature for the year in Limoges is {{Convert|11.4|°C|°F }}. The warmest month, on average, is July with an average temperature of {{Convert|19.3|°C|°F }}. The coolest month on average is January, with an average temperature of {{Convert|4.2|°C|°F }}.
