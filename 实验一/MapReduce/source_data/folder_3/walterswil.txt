'''Walterswil''' is the name of three places in Switzerland: 
 * [[Walterswil, Solothurn]], a municipality in the district of Olten 
 * [[Walterswil, Berne]], a municipality in the district of Trachselwald (today administrative district of Oberaargau) 
 * Walterswil, Zug (Walterswil-Sihlbrugg), part of [[Baar, Switzerland|Baar]]
