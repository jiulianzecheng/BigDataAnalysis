 '''Dobryanka''' ({{lang-ru|Добрянка}}, 58°27′N, 56°25′E) is a town in [[Perm Krai]], [[Russia]]. It is a center of [[Dobryansky District]].  As of 2005, there were about 36,100 people. 
 
 Dobryanka was founded in 1623 and became a town in 1943. It is one of the oldest settlements of Perm Krai.
