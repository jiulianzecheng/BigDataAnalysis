 '''Knetzgau''' is a [[Municipalities of Germany|municipality]] in [[Haßberge (district)|Haßberge]] in [[Bavaria]] in [[Germany]].  It consists of eight villages: Oberschwappach, Unterschwappach, Westheim, Hainert, Eschenau, Neuhaus, Zell am Ebersberg, Wohnau and Zeil.
