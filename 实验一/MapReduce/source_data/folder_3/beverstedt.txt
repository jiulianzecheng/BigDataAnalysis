 '''Beverstedt''' is a municipality in [[Cuxhaven (district)|Cuxhaven]], in [[Lower Saxony]], [[Germany]]. It is about 20 & nbsp;km southeast of [[Bremerhaven]], and 40 & nbsp;km north of [[Bremen]].
