 '''Nagaland''' is a northeastern state in the [[Republic of India]]. It has an area of {{convert|6,401|mi2}}. It is bigger than [[East Timor]] but smaller than [[Eswatini]]. In traditional Indian geography it falls under the [[North-east India]]n zone.
