 '''Maasgouw''' is a [[List of municipalities of the Netherlands|municipality]] in the [[Netherlands|Dutch]] province of [[Limburg (Netherlands)|Limburg]]. In 2019, 23,716 people lived there. 
 
 It was created on 1 January 2007 from the former municipalities of [[Heel, Netherlands|Heel]], [[Maasbracht]] and [[Thorn, Netherlands|Thorn]].
