 '''Kaufland''' {{IPA-de|ˈkaʊ̯flant|}} is a [[Germany|German]] [[hypermarket]] chain. It is a part of the [[Schwarz Gruppe]]. Schwarz Gruppe also owns [[Lidl]] and [[Handelshof]]. In 1984, the first store was opened in [[Neckarsulm]] and quickly expanded to become a leader in what was formerly [[East Germany]]. The chain operates over 1,200 stores in [[Germany]], the [[Czech Republic]], [[Slovakia]], [[Poland]], [[Romania]], [[Bulgaria]] and [[Croatia]].
