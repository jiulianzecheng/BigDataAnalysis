 '''Oachira''' is a town in [[Karunagappally]] taluk, [[Kollam district]] in [[Kerala]], [[India]]. 
 
 ==Temple town== 
 
 Oachira is one of the famous holy places of [[Kerala]] and in India. < ref > {{Cite web |url=http://www.kerala.nic.in/kollam/fest.html |title=Fairs and Festivals |access-date=2018-05-13 |archive-date=2007-05-02 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20070502224808/http://www.kerala.nic.in/kollam/fest.html |url-status=dead }} < /ref >  Oachira is on the border of [[Kollam District]] and [[Allappuzha District]]. < ref > [http://www.ochira.com/ Ochira.com main page] < /ref >   
 The famous temple of Para Brahmam (OMkaram) is located here. 
   
 ==Festivals== 
 
 The major annual festivals are ''Oachira Kali'' or ''Oachirakali'' and the twelve-day Panthrandu Vilakku , which is held in November/December.  Oachira Kali is celebrated in commemoration of the battle of Kayamkulam, fought between [[Marthanda Varma]], the Maharaja of [[Travancore]] and the Raja of [[Kayamkulam]]. < ref > {{Cite web |url=http://www.kerala.gov.in/knowkerala/other.htm |title=Oachira Kettukazhcha |access-date=2018-05-13 |archive-date=2010-08-01 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20100801041004/http://www.kerala.gov.in/knowkerala/other.htm |url-status=dead }} < /ref >  It is a mock fight enacted between groups of men dressed as warriors on the ''padanilam'' (battlefield).  
 
 Yet another festival celebrated in [[Oachira Temple]] is the  " Irupathiyettam Onam "  (Thiruvonum in the month of Kanni). It is considered the [[Onam]] of cows and bulls and is celebrated on [[Thiruvonam]]. These concepts are related to ''Shiva Vahana''. 
 
 ==Celebrities== 
 
 Eminent cartoonist Shankar hails from near Oachira. The government of Kerala paid a tribute to him, in the form of  a museum in memory of the country’s best known and most revered political cartoonist,near his birthplace Oachira. Kerala Lalithakala Akademi was behind the ambitious projec,which costs an estimated initial cost of Rs. 3. 5 crore which spreads  over 15,000 sq.ft. of land. 
 
 ==To reach== 
 
 To reach Oachira, by air, the nearest airports are Trivandrum International Airport and Cochin International Airport. By rail,Oachira and [[Kayamkulam]] Jn. is nearest station 
 
 ==Education== 
 RVSM Higher Secondary School, situated at Prayar is the oldest and biggest educational institution in Oachira. Oachira Govt. Higher Secondary School is another famous educational institution. PRAGATHI Hindi Vidyalay is a famous Hindi college
