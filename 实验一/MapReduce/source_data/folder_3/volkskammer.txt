The '''Volkskammer''' ({{IPA-de|ˈfɔlkskamɐ|lang}}, ''People's Chamber'') was the [[Unicameralism|unicameral]] [[legislature]] of the German Democratic Republic (colloquially known as [[East Germany]]). < ref > {{cite book|last=Sebetsyen|first=Victor|title=Revolution 1989: The Fall of the Soviet Empire|publisher=[[Pantheon Books]]|location=New York City|year=2009|isbn=978-0-375-42532-5|url-access=registration|url=https://archive.org/details/revolution1989fa00sebe}} < /ref >  It was a bicameral legislature with a senate but was dissolved in 1952. This legislature was disbanded on October 3, 1990 and replaced by the [[Bundestag]].
