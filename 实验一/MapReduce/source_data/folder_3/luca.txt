 '''''Luca''''' is a [[2021]] American [[Computer animation|computer-animated]] [[comedy]] movie produced by [[Walt Disney Pictures]] and [[Pixar|Pixar Animation Studios]]. It is directed by Enrico Casarosa. 
 
 
 ''Luca'' was released direct-to-streaming on [[Disney+]] due to the [[COVID-19 pandemic]].
