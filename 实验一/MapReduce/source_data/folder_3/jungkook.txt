 '''Jeon Jung-kook''' (born September 1, 1997) is a South Korean [[singer|singer]], [[dancer]] and [[rapper]]. He is also better known mononymously as '''Jungkook'''. He is known for being a member of the South Korean [[Boy band|boy group]] [[BTS]]. < ref name= " Russell2014 " > {{cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=etDZAwAAQBAJ|title=K-Pop Now!: The Korean Music Revolution|author=Mark Russell|date=2014-04-29|publisher=Tuttle Publishing|isbn=978-1-4629-1411-1|page=67}} < /ref >
