{{mergeto|Christmas}} 
 
 '''Nochebuena''' (literally  " good night " ) is the [[Spanish language|Spanish]] word for [[Christmas Eve]]. In [[Spain]], [[Latin America]], and the [[Philippines]], the evening consists of a traditional family dinner. Roasted [[pork|pig]] is often the center of Nochebuena for feasts around the world.
