 '''Sardar''' or '''Sirdar''' or '''Serdar''', is a title of nobility in [[India]], [[Pakistan]], [[Iran]] and [[Afghanistan]].  
 
 The word is from the old [[Sanskrit]] and [[Avesta (religious scripture)|Avesta]] languages. It means a prince, nobleman, tribal chief or leader or other aristocrat. In some tribes and ethnic groups in India and Pakistan, especially among the [[Baloch people]] and [[Punjabi people|Punjabis]], it is quite commonly used to denote even certain families of noble origins.
