 '''Gallarate''' {{IPA-it|ɡallaˈraːte|}} ([[Lombard language|Lombard]]: ''Galaraa'') is a [[city]] in northern [[Italy]]. Gallarate is in the [[Lombardy]] Region. It has a population of 52,857 people.
