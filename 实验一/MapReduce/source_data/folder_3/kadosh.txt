 '''''Kadosh''''' ({{lang-he|קדוש}})  (''lit.'' Sacred) is a 1999 Israeli French drama movie directed by [[Amos Gitai]] and starring [[Yaël Abecassis]], [[Yoram Hattab]], [[Meital Barda]], [[Uri Klauzner]], [[Yussuf Abu-Warda]]. It was distributed by [[Ocean Film]].
