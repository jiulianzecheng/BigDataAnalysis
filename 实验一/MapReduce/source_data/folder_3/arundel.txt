'''Arundel''' is a small town on the [[River Arun]] in [[West Sussex]], [[England]]. It is the site of [[Arundel Castle]], the seat of the [[Duke of Norfolk]], and the [[Catholic]] [[Arundel Cathedral]]. It is 10 miles east of [[Chichester]].
