 '''Abaiara''' is a [[city]] in the south region of the [[States of Brazil|state]] of [[Ceará]], [[Brazil]]. 
 
 == History == 
 Abaiara's history goes back to the 19th century, when two [[farmer]]s, José Leite da Cunha and José Júlio Sampaio, settled in the area.
