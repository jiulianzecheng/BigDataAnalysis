 '''Tauriko''' is a suburb in [[Tauranga]], [[Bay of Plenty]] region. It is home to the Tauranga Crossing complex and has [[State Highway 29]] and [[State Highway 36]] passing through the area. There is a primary school in the suburb. Its neighbouring suburbs are [[Greerton]] to the east, [[Pyes P훮]] to the southeast, [[Gate P훮]] to the northeast and [[Bethlehem, New Zealand|Bethlehem]] to the north.
