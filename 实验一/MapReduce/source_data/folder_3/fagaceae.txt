 The family '''Fagaceae''', also called '''beech family''', is a family of [[Flowering plant|flowering plants]]. It has about 900 species of [[evergreen]] and [[deciduous]] [[tree]]s and [[shrub]]s. They have alternate simple leaves with pinnate venation, unisexual [[flower]]s in the form of catkins, and fruit in the form of nuts.  
 
 All the [[Oak|oaks]], [[beech]]es and [[chestnut]]s are in this family.
