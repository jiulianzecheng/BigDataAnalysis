 '''Bielefeld''' ({{IPA-de|ˈbiːləfɛlt|-|De-Bielefeld.ogg}}; {{lang-nds|Builefeld}}) is a city in northwest Germany, in the [[States of Germany|state]] of [[North Rhine-Westphalia]]. 
 
 Bielefeld has about 327,000 people living there. It has a [[university]].
