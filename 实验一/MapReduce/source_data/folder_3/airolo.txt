 '''Airolo''' is a [[municipality]] of the [[district]] of [[Leventina (district)|Leventina]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Ticino]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 Airolo is also a small ski-resort with 30 kilometres of ski slopes.
