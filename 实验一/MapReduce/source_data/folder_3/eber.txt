 According to the [[Bible]], '''Eber''' was an ancestor of [[Jesus]] and [[Abraham]]. He was the son of [[Salah (Biblical figure)|Salah]] and the father of two sons, [[Peleg]] and [[Joktan]]. Jesus and Abraham came through Peleg's line. Some people think the word ''Hebrew'' comes from his name.
