The '''Tonian''' was the first [[geological]] [[Period (geology)|period]] in the [[Neoproterozoic]] [[era]]. It lasted from 1000 million years ago (mya) to 720 mya. The [[supercontinent]] [[Rodinia]] began breaking in this period. [[Acritarch]]s [[adaptive radiation|radiated]] during the Tonian.
