 '''Bedfield''' is a village and [[civil parish]] in [[Mid Suffolk]], [[Suffolk]], England. In 2001 there were 298 people living in Bedfield. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.ons.gov.uk/help/localstatistics|title=Local statistics - Office for National Statistics|website=www.ons.gov.uk}} < /ref >  Bedfield has a church called St Nicholas. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.suffolkchurches.co.uk/bedfield.htm|title=Suffolk Churches|website=www.suffolkchurches.co.uk}} < /ref >  Bedfield is about 4 miles east of [[Debenham]]. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.onesuffolk.net/|title=One Suffolk|website=www.onesuffolk.net}} < /ref >
