'''Laminitis''' is a disease that affects the feet of [[hooved animals|ungulates]] and is found mostly in [[horse]]s and [[cattle]]. Clinical signs include foot tenderness which makes it harder for them to walk. It also increased digital pulses, and increased temperature in the hooves.
