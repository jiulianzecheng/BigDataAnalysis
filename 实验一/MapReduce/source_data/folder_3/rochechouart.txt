 '''Rochechouart''' ({{Lang-oc|Rechoard}}) is a [[Communes of France|''commune'']] in the [[Haute-Vienne]] [[Departments of France|department]] in the [[Nouvelle-Aquitaine]] [[Regions of France|region]] in west central [[France]]. It is a [[Prefectures in France|subprefecture]] of the [[Haute-Vienne]] [[Departments of France|department]]. It is also the [[Capital city|capital]] of the ''[[Arrondissements of France|arrondissement]]'' of the same name. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 Rochechouart is in the western part of the Haute-Vienne department, along the [[valley]] of the [[Grêne]] [[river]]. The [[confluence]] of the Grêne with the [[Vayres (river)|Vayres]] river is to the south of the town. Another river, the [[Gorre]], forms the northeastern border of the ''commune''. 
 
 The city of Rochechouart is at about {{Convert|45|km|mi|1|abbr=on}} west of Limoges, the largest town and capital of the department, {{Convert|185|km|mi|abbr=on }} from [[Bordeaux]] and {{Convert|434|km|mi|abbr=on }} from [[Paris]]. < ref name= " annuaire " > {{cite web|title=Ville de Rochechouart|url=http://www.annuaire-mairie.fr/ville-rochechouart.html|access-date=3 April 2017|work=Annuaire-Mairie.fr|language=fr}} < /ref > 
 
 The ''commune'' has an area of {{Convert|53.9|km2|sqmi|abbr=on }}. < ref name= " insee-geo " > {{cite web |url=http://www.insee.fr/fr/statistiques/1405599?geo=COM-87126 |title=Commune de Rochechouart (87126) |work=Comparateur de territoire |publisher=Institut national de la statistique et des études économiques - INSEE |language=fr |access-date=3 April 2017 }} < /ref >  Its average [[altitude]] is {{Convert|236|m|ft|abbr=on}}; at the [[City and town halls|city hall]], the altitude is {{Convert|250|m|ft|abbr=on }}. < ref > {{cite web|title=Rochechouart|url=http://www.map-france.com/Rochechouart-87600/|access-date=3 April 2017|publisher=Map-France.com}} < /ref >

 == Administration == 
 Rochechouart is a [[Prefectures in France|subprefecture]] of the Haute-Vienne department, the capital of the ''arrondissement'' of [[Arrondissement of Rochechouart|Rochechouart]] and the [[administrative centre]] ({{lang-fr|chef-lieu}}) of the [[Cantons of France|canton]] of [[Canton of Rochechouart|Rochechouart]], with 17,479  inhabitants  < small > (2014) < /small > . 
 
 It is part of the [[Communes of France|intercommunality]] ''Porte Océane du Limousin'' ({{lang-fr|Communauté de communes Porte Océane du Limousin}}).
