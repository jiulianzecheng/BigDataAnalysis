 '''Siegen''' is a city in the [[Germany|German]] [[States of Germany|state]] [[North Rhine-Westphalia]]. About 110,000 people live there and a university. 
 
 == Twin Towns == 
 It has been [[twin town|twinned]] with the borough of [[Spandau]] in [[Berlin]] since 1952. Other twin cities are: 
 * {{flag|Netherlands}}, [[Rijnsburg]], since 1963, continuation of partnership with amalgamated town of [[Katwijk]] as of 2006 
 *United Kingdom Morley, since 1966 continuation of partnership with amalgamated City of Leeds as of 1974 
 *{{flag|United Kingdom}}, [[Leeds]], since 1974 
 * {{flag|Belgium}}, [[Ypres]], since 1967 
 * {{flag|Poland}}, [[Zakopane]], since 1989 
 * {{flag|Germany}}, {{flag|Saxony}}, [[Plauen]], since 1990
