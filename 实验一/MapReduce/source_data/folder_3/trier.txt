 '''Trier''' is a [[city]] at the [[Moselle River]] in [[Rhineland-Palatinate]] in [[Germany]]. The population is about 100,000. 
 
 Trier is famous for some [[Ancient Rome|Roman]] buildings like the Porta Nigra and others. It is famous as the birthplace of [[Karl Marx]]. Trier is also famous for the wine of the wine-growing-region [[Mosel-Saar-Ruwer]].
