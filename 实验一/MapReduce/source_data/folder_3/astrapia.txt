 '''''Astrapia''''' (Vieillot, 1816) is a [[genus]] of [[Bird-of-paradise|birds-of-paradise]]. The [[genus]] contains five [[species]], all [[Endemism|endemic]] to [[New Guinea]].  
 
 The males have highly [[iridescent]] plumage and remarkably long tails. Females are duller and have shorter tails. < ref name= " :0 " > {{cite book|last=Peckover|first=William S.|title=Papua New Guinea, Birds of Paradise|date=1990}} Brown: Carina. < /ref >
