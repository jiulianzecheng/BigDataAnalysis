'''Abra''' is a unisex name with a rich history and meaning, rooted in [[Hebrew language|Hebrew]] and [[Arabic language|Arabic]]. In Hebrew, Abra means  " father of many "  or  " father of a multitude, "  while in Arabic, it means  " passage "  or  " crossing. "  The name is associated with significant figures such as Abraham and Prophet Ibrahim, and it has been used in literature and popular culture.
