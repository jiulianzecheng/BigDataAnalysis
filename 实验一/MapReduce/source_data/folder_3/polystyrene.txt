'''Polystyrene''' is a form of [[chemical compound]] that is mainly used for a wide variety of [[container|packaging]]. < ref name= " test " > [http://plastics.americanchemistry.com/pfpg Plastics] < /ref > < ref name= " test1 " > {{cite web | url=http://www.chemtube3d.com/polymer/_PolystyreneF.html | title=Polystyrene (PS) }} < /ref >  It is an [[Aromaticity|aromatic]] [[polymer]] made from the [[monomer]] [[styrene]], a liquid [[hydrocarbon]] that is  manufactured from [[petroleum]] by the chemical industry. Polystyrene is one of the most widely used [[plastic]]s, the scale being several billion kilograms per year. 
 
 Polystyrene can be in either a [[Thermosetting polymer|thermoset]] or a [[thermoplastic]] form.
