 '''Damageplan''' was a [[heavy metal music|heavy metal]] [[band]] from [[Dallas]], [[Texas]]. They started playing in 2003 after [[Pantera]]'s guitarist and drummer decided to start a new band. They only released one [[album]], called ''New Found Power''. On December 8, 2004, the [[guitarist]] [[Dimebag Darrell]] was shot and killed by an obsessed fan named [[Nathan Gale]] during a concert. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.nytimes.com/2004/12/10/arts/music/10abbott.html?_r=1|title=Darrell Abbott, 38, a Guitarist Featured in Heavy-Metal Bands, Dies|website=[[The New York Times]]}} < /ref >  After the incident, the band broke up.
