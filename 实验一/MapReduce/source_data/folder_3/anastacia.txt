 '''Anastacia Lyn Newkirk''', best known as '''Anastacia''' {{IPAc-en|ˌ|æ|n|ə|ˈ|s|t|eɪ|ʒ|ə}}, (born September 17, 1968) is an [[Americans|American]] [[singer]], [[songwriter]] and [[style|stylist]]. 
 
 Anastacia's father was of [[Germany|German]] [[ancestry]]; her mother is of [[Irish people|Irish]] ancestry. 
 
 Anastacia has had several health problems in her life. She was diagnosed with [[Crohn's disease]] when she was 13. In early 2003 she was [[diagnosis|diagnosed]] with [[breast cancer]] and had [[surgery]] to remove it. < ref > {{cite news|url=http://news.bbc.co.uk/2/hi/entertainment/2817287.stm|title=Anastacia has cancer surgery|date=March 4, 2003|via=news.bbc.co.uk}} < /ref >  She was again diagnosed with breast cancer in early 2013. On October 1, 2013 she told the media that she has had both of her breasts removed. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.dailymail.co.uk/tvshowbiz/article-2440010/Anastacia-undergoes-double-mastectomy-diagnosed-breast-cancer-second-time.html|title=Anastacia undergoes double mastectomy after being diagnosed with breast cancer for the second time|date=October 1, 2013|website=Mail Online}} < /ref >
