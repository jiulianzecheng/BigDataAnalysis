 '''Meretz''', Hebrew: מרצ , meaning ''Vigour'', is an [[Israel]]i political party. 
 
 Its ideology is [[left-wing]] [[social democratic]], [[green politics|green]], [[secular]], and [[Zionist]].  
 
 Political leader is [[Nitzan Horowitz]] 
 
 Meretz was founded in [[1992]] as a result of a merger of three parties: [[Ratz]], [[Mapam]] and [[Shinui]]. 
 
 From the previous election results, in [[2020 Israeli legislative election|March 2020]], the number of seats in the 23rd [[Knesset]]: 3 (/). In the most recent election, held on 23 March 2021 for the 24th Knesset, the party obtained six seats, pending the final count outcome of surplus vote sharing with the Labor party. 
 
 Participating in the [[Bennett-Lapid Government]].
