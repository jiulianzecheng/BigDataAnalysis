 '''Benzenschwil''' was a [[municipality]] of the [[district]] [[Muri (district)|Muri]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Aargau]] in [[Switzerland]]. On 1 January 2012 the former municipality of Benzenschwil merged into the municipality of [[Merenschwand]].
