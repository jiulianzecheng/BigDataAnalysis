 '''''Whiffs''''' is a 1975 American crime comedy movie directed by [[Ted Post]] and starring [[Elliott Gould]], [[Eddie Albert]], [[Harry Guardino]], [[Godfrey Cambridge]], [[Jennifer O'Neill]]. It was distributed by [[20th Century Fox]] and was nominated for an Academy Award in 1976.
