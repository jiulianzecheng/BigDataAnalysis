 '''Fraubrunnen''' is a [[municipality]] in the [[administrative district]] of [[Bern-Mittelland (administrative district)|Bern-Mittelland]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Bern (canton)|Bern]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 On 1 January 2014, the former municipalities of [[Büren zum Hof]], [[Etzelkofen]], [[Grafenried, Switzerland|Grafenried]], [[Limpach, Switzerland|Limpach]], [[Mülchi]], [[Schalunen]] and [[Zauggenried]] merged into the municipality of Fraubrunnen.
