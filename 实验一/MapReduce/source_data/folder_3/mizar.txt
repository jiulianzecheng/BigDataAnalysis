'''Mizar''' or '''Zeta Ursae Majoris''' is a [[binary star]] located in the [[Ursa Major]] [[constellation]]. It is also a companion to the fainter binary star of [[Alcor (star)|Alcor]]. Both are white stars a few times larger than the [[Sun]] and are located 83 [[light year|light years]] away from the [[Sun]].
