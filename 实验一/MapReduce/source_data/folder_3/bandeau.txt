[[File:Abril Alonso.jpg|Bandeau|alt=Bandeau|thumb|300x300px]]Bandeau is a [[clothing]] comprising of a strip of [[Textile|cloth]] around a woman's [[Breast|breasts]]. It is usually part of a [[Bikini]]. It is strapless, off the shoulder and sleeveless.
