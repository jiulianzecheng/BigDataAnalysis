 A '''liger''' is a [[:wiktionary:mix|mix]] between a male [[lion]] (''Panthera leo'') and a female [[tiger]] (''Panthera tigris''). It is different from a similar mix called a [[tigon]] and is the biggest of all known [[:wiktionary:extant|extant]] [[cat]]s.
