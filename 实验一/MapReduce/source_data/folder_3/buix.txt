 '''Buix''' is a former [[municipality]] of the [[district]] of [[Porrentruy (district)|Porrentruy]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Jura (canton)|Jura]] in [[Switzerland]]. On 1 January 2009 the former municipalities of Buix, [[Courtemaîche]] and [[Montignez]] merged into the new municipality of [[Basse-Allaine]].
