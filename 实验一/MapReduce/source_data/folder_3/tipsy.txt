 " '''Tipsy''' "  is the first single off of [[Americans|American]] [[Rap music|rapper]] [[J-Kwon]]'s debut [[studio album]] ''[[Hood Hop]]''. The song was released on April 6, 2004. The song was able to make it to #2 on the Billboard Hot 100. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.billboard.com/charts/hot-100/2004-04-17/|accessdate=June 11, 2023|title=Billboard Hot 100 - April 17, 2004|publisher=[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]}} < /ref >  it is J-Kwon's most well known song. 
 
 A music video for the song was released. It opens with a shot of the [[Gateway Arch]] in [[St. Louis]], [[Missouri]]. The rest of the video features J-Kwon hosting a [[house party]] with everyone partying. It ends with a reference to the 1983 movie ''[[Risky Business]]'' and J-Kwon getting caught by his parents the day after.
