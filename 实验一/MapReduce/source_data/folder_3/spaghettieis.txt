 '''Spaghettieis''', or '''spaghetti ice cream''', is a [[Germany|German]] [[ice cream]] dish. It is made to look like a plate of [[spaghetti]]. 
 
 Spaghettieis was created by [[Dario Fontanella]] in the late 1960s in Mannheim, Germany. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Kid-Friendly Food: German 'Spaghetti' Ice Cream Sundae|url=https://www.thespruceeats.com/spaghetti-ice-cream-sundae-from-germany-1446804|access-date=2021-03-26|website=The Spruce Eats|language=en}} < /ref >
