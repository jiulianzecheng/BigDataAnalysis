 '''Jasmine''' is a [[genus]] of [[plant]]s, ''Jasminum''. They are [[shrub]]s or [[vine]]s that grow in moderately warm [[climates]].  
 
 There are about 200 different species of ''Jasminum''. They are also a favourite in [[garden]]s. [[Tea]] can be made from the flowers. Some species are used to make special [[oil]], [[perfume]]s or [[incense]]. Women, especially from [[Asia]] sometimes wear jasmine flowers in their [[hair]]. 
 
 Jasmine flowers are white or yellow in colour, although in rare instances they can be slightly reddish. Jasmine flowers are well known for their lovely smell. Jasmine is also a female name.
