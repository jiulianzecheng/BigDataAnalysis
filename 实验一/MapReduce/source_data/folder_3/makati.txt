 '''Makati''', officially known as the '''City of Makati''' ({{IPAc-en|m|ə|ˈ|k|ɑː|t|ɪ}} {{respell|mə|KAH|tee}}; {{lang-tl|Makati|}}), in the [[Philippines]], is one of the sixteen [[Cities of the Philippines|cities]] that make up [[Metro Manila]]. Makati is the financial center of the Philippines. As the host of various [[diplomatic mission|embassies]], it is also an important center for international affairs. 
 
 With 529,039 people, Makati is the 16th-largest city in the country and ranked as the 41st most [[List of cities proper by population density|densely populated city]] in the world with 19,336 inhabitants per square kilometer. 
 
 Makati became the financial center of the Philippines during the 1960s. It is home to the [[Philippine Stock Exchange]] and the [[Makati Business Club]]. < ref > {{Cite web |url=http://ww.whereinmanila.com/philippine-stock-exchange-ayala-tower-1 |access-date=2019-05-21 |title=Archived copy |archive-date=2016-03-03 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160303221101/http://ww.whereinmanila.com/philippine-stock-exchange-ayala-tower-1 |url-status=dead }} < /ref > < ref > {{cite web|url=https://mbc.com.ph/|title=Makati Business Club - non-profit business association - business forum Philippines|publisher=}} < /ref > 
 
 Makati is notable for its exceptionally multicultural lifestyle, as a significant center for intercontinental matters and for its reputation as a major entertainment center in the metropolis. < ref name= " makaticity.com " > {{cite web|url=https://www.makaticity.com/about/|title=About Makati, Philippines|website=www.makaticity.com}} < /ref > 
 The city is also known for its [[Cosmopolitanism|cosmopolitan culture]] because of its major shopping centers, condominiums, financial hubs, hotels, and entertainment hubs, which are located around the city. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.touristcenter.com.ph/maintenance.html|title=Site Maintenance|website=www.touristcenter.com.ph}} < /ref > 
 
 Makati first became as the financial center of the Philippines during the 1960s.
