 An '''audience''' is a [[group]] of [[wikt:people|people]] that see, hear or otherwise experience the same thing.  It may be many different kinds of thing, such as a [[Play (theatre)|play]], a [[movie]], a [[book]], or a [[broadcast]].  Audience members [[participate]] in different ways in different kinds of art; some live performance events invite the audience to be part of the [[performance]], while others allow only [[applause]] and [[criticism]]. [[Mass media]] seldom provide for audience participation. 
 
 An audience at a sporting event are called ''spectators'' or  a ''live audience''
