 '''Tribadism''' is a [[lesbian]] sexual act in which a woman rubs her [[vulva]] against her partner's vulva or another part of her partner's body for sexual stimulation. It is also known as '''scissoring''', a [[List of sex positions|sex position]] in which the women intertwine their legs and rub their vulvas together, or as '''tribading''' or '''tribbing'''.
