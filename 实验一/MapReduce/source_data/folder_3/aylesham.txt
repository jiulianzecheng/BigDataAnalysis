'''Aylesham''' is a [[village]] in [[Kent]]. It lies about halfway between [[Dover]] and [[Canterbury]]. Dover is about two [[mile]]s closer than Canterbury. The village was built in the 1920s to house people working at [[coal mine]]s in the region. In 2012, 3999 people lived there. the village has a [[railway station]] on the [[Chatham Main Line|line from London to Dover]].
