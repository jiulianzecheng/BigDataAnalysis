 '''Hawkgirl''' is the name of several fictional [[superheroines]] appearing in [[American comic book]]s published by [[DC Comics]]. The original Hawkgirl, Shiera Sanders Hall, was created by writer [[Gardner Fox]] and artist [[Dennis Neville]]. She first appeared in ''[[Flash Comics]]'' #1 (January 1940). She is a member of the [[The Justice League|Justice League]]. 
 
 She often works with and sometimes has a romantic relationship with [[Hawkman]].
