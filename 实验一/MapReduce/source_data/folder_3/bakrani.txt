 '''Bakrani''' ({{lang-sd|باڪراڻي}}) is a town in [[Larkana District]], [[Sindh]] province of [[Pakistan]]. This town is 12 & nbsp;km south of [[Larkana]] city on the [[Larkana]]-[[Mohenjo-daro]] road. It has an altitude of 53 metres < ref > [http://www.fallingrain.com/world/PK/5/Baqirani.html Location of Bakrani - Falling Rain Genomics] < /ref >  and is the headquarters of [[Bakrani Taluka]].
