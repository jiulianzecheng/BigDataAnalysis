 '''Finnmark''' < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.kartverket.no/kart/stedsnavn/stadnamn-og-rettskriving/|title=Stadnamn og rettskriving|language=no|access-date=2018-07-13|publisher=[[Kartverket]]|archive-date=2018-07-01|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180701054759/https://kartverket.no/kart/stedsnavn/stadnamn-og-rettskriving/|url-status=live}} < /ref >  ({{IPA-no|ˈfɪ̀nːmɑrk|lang|Finnmark.ogg}}; {{lang-se|Finnmárku}} {{IPA-se|ˈfinːmaːrku|}}; {{lang-fkv|Finmarku}}; {{lang-fi|Ruija}} {{IPA-fi|ˈrui̯jɑ|}}; {{lang-ru|Финнмарк}}) is a former [[counties of Norway|county]] in the northern part of [[Norway]], and it is scheduled to become a county again in 2024. 
 
 On 28 October 2021, the Norwegian government [[confirmation|confirmed]] that work has started in regard to Finnmark becoming a separate county again.
