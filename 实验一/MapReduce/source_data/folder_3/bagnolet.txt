 '''Bagnolet''' is a town in the east suburbs of [[Paris]], [[France]]. It is 5.2 & nbsp;km (3.2 & nbsp;mi) from the center of [[Paris]] and is in the department of [[Seine-Saint-Denis]]. About 34,595 people lived there in [[2006]].
