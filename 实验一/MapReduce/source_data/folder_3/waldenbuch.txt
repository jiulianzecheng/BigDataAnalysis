 '''Waldenbuch''' is a town in [[Böblingen (district)|Böblingen]] in [[Baden-Württemberg]], [[Germany]]. It is known for its chocolate brand [[Ritter Sport]]. 
 
 == Geography == 
 [[File:Waldenbuch vm01.jpg|thumb|left|Panoramic view over Waldenbuch]] 
 Waldenbuch is at an elevation reaching from 340 to 460 meters on the northern edge of [[Schönbuch]] forest. It is 16 & nbsp;km south of [[Stuttgart]]. 
 
 === Districts === 
 Waldenbuch has following districts of Sonnenhang, Kalkofen, Weilerberg, Glashütte, Stadtkern ( " Städtle " ), Liebenau and Hasenhof.
