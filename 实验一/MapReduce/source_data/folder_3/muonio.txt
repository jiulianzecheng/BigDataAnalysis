 '''Muonio''' is a [[municipality]] in [[Finnish Lapland|Lapland]], [[Finland]]. As of January 2014, about 2,385 people lived there. Neighbouring municipalities are [[Enontekiö]], [[Kittilä]] and [[Kolari]]. On the western side of it is [[Sweden]]. Distance to [[Tornio]] is about 260 km and to [[Kilpisjärvi]], 200 km. There are a lot of [[lake]]s in the area of Muonio.
