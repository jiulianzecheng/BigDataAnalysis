'''DeSaad''' (also spelled '''Desaad''') is a fictional [[supervillain]]. He appeared in books published by [[DC Comics]]. He is one of the followers of [[Darkseid]] from the planet of [[Apokolips]]. < ref name= " dc-ency " > {{Cite encyclopedia | last = Wallace | first = Dan | contribution = Demons Three | editor-last = Dougall | editor-first = Alastair | title = The DC Comics Encyclopedia | pages = 98 | publisher = [[Dorling Kindersley]] | place = New York | year = 2008 | isbn = 0-7566-4119-5 | oclc = 213309017}} < /ref > 
 
 DeSaad is Darkseid's master [[torture|torturer]]. At one point, DeSaad had an assistant named ''Justeen''. 
 
 [[Steve Byers]] played DeSaad in the television series ''[[Smallville]]''. The character made his cinematic debut in ''[[Zack Snyder's Justice League]]'' (2021), by [[Peter Guinness (actor)|Peter Guinness]].
