 '''Lelystad''' is a [[List of municipalities of the Netherlands|municipality]] in the middle of the [[Netherlands]]. 
 It has about 71,000 [[inhabitant]]s. It is also the capital of [[Flevoland]], one of the [[provinces]] of the Netherlands.
