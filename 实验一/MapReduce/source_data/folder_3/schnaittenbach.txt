 '''Schnaittenbach''' ([[Bavarian language|Bavarian]]: ''Schnaittenboch'') is a town in [[Amberg-Sulzbach]], a district [[Districts of Germany|''(Landkreis)'']] in the [[Upper Palatinate]]. It is {{convert|16|km|abbr=off}} northeast of [[Amberg]]. 
 
 == Sister town == 
 The town is twinned with [[Buchberg]], Switzerland.
