 '''Kirwee''' is a town in the [[Selwyn District]], [[New Zealand]]. It is situated west of [[Christchurch]] and north of [[Darfield, New Zealand|Darfield]] on State Highway 73. It has a primary school and the historic pub burnt down on [[25 December]] [[2019]].
