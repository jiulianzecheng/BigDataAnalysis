 '''Hanglem''' is a Meitei Manipuri surname or family name or  " Yumnak "  in Meiteilon belonging to a branch of Khuman clan of Meitei ethnicity.  " Hanglem Apokpa "  is the clan ancestral deity worshipped by the members of Hanglem family. The Hanglem Apokpa temple is situated at Utlou, Manipur and the present Hanglem Piba or Hanglem Headman is Hanglem Sarat of Utlou.
