 '''Ubbergen''' is a former [[List of municipalities of the Netherlands|municipality]] in the [[Netherlands|Dutch]] [[Provinces of the Netherlands|province]] of [[Gelderland]]. Since 2015, it has been part of the new municipality of [[Berg en Dal (municipality)|Berg en Dal]] (formerly known as Groesbeek).
