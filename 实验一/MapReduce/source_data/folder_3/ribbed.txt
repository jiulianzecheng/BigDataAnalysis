 '''''Ribbed''''' is the third [[studio album]] by [[United States|American]] [[punk rock]] band, [[NOFX]]. ''Ribbed'' was released on March 26, 1991. The producer of the album was [[Brett Gurewitz]]. ''Ribbed'' was also the last album produced for the band by Brett Gurewitz.
