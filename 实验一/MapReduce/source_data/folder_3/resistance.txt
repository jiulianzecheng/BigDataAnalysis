 '''Resistance''' could mean: 
 
 * The general ability to stop something from happening (Ex. ''resistance to water'' means that the object can avoid being hurt by water)  
 * Resistance, an ability to reject [[infection]] 
 * [[Electrical resistance]], the repulsion of a current in a circuit 
 * A struggle against an oppressive government. See also [[Rebellion]] 
 *The [[Resistance (Star Wars)|Resistance]] was a fictional military set up by [[Princess]] [[Leia Organa]] to combat the growing threat of the [[First Order]] in the [[Star Wars]] universe.
