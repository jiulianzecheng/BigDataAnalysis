 '''Rantasalmi''' is a municipality in [[Southern Savonia]], [[Finland]]. As of January 2014, almost 3,900 people lived there. Neighbouring municipalities are [[Joroinen]], [[Juva]], [[Savonlinna]], [[Sulkava]] and [[Varkaus]]. 
 
 Rantasalmi was established in 1578. 
 
 ==Known people== 
 *[[Eliel Saarinen]], architect 
 *[[Jarkko Immonen]], ice hockey player  
 *[[Maria Hänninen]], musician 
 *Jussi Leskinen, a person who died at the age 108. He was the oldest person in Finland on 28 February 2010.
