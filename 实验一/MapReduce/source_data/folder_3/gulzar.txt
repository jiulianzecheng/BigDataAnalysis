 '''Sampooran Singh Kalra''', known as '''Gulzar''' (born 18 August 1936) is a [[songwriter]], [[poet]] and [[movie director]] from [[India]]. He has written songs for many [[Hindi]] movies. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://www.tribuneindia.com/2004/20040104/spectrum/book2.htm |title=The poet as the father |accessdate=2010-12-08 |last=Gulzar |first=Meghna |date=2004-01-04 |location=Rupa, New Delhi |work=tribuneindia.com |publisher=Raj Chengappa, The Tribune Trust}} < /ref > 
 
 Gulzar co-wrote the song  " [[Jai Ho (song)|Jai Ho]] "  for the movie ''[[Slumdog Millionaire]]''. The song won the [[Academy Award for Best Original Song]].
