 '''Cauco''' is a former [[municipality]] of the [[district]] of [[Moesa (district)|Moesa]] in the [[Swiss]] [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Graubünden]]. 
 
 On 1 January 2015, the former municipalities of Cauco, [[Arvigo]], [[Braggio]] and [[Selma, Switzerland|Selma]] merged into the new municipality of [[Calanca, Switzerland|Calanca]].
