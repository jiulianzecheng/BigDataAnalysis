 '''Villarzel''' is a [[municipality]] in the district of [[Broye-Vully (district)|Broye-Vully]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Vaud]] in [[Switzerland]]. On 1 July 2006, the municipalities of [[Rossens, Vaud|Rossens]] and [[Sédeilles]] became part of Villarzel.
