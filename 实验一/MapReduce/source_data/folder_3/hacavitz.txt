 '''Hacavitz''' is a [[Mexico|Mexican]] [[death metal]] band formed in 2003. The band took their name from the [[Maya civilization|Mayan]] god [[Jacawitz]].  
 
 ==Members== 
 ===Current members=== 
 *Antimo Buonnano - Guitars, Bass, Vocals ([[Profanator]], [[Blood Reaping]], [[Demonized (band)|Demonized]], ex-[[Impiety]], ex-[[Disgorge (Mexican band)|Disgorge]], [[Domain (Mexican band)|Domain]], [[Ravager]]) 
 *Oscar Garcia - Drums ([[Demonized (band)|Demonized]], ex-[[Impiety (band)|Impiety]], [[Domain (Mexican band)|Domain]], [[Ravager]]) 
 
 ===Live members===

 ===Former members===
