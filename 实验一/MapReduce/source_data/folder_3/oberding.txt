 '''Oberding''' ({{lang-de|Oberding}}, {{lang-bar|Obading}}) is a [[Municipalities of Germany|municipality]] in the district of [[Erding (district)|Erding]] in [[Upper Bavaria]] in [[Germany]], and member of the [[Administrative Community Oberding|same administrative community]]. Almost half of the area of [[Munich Airport]], including both terminals, falls within the municipal bounds. 
 
 == Geography == 
 Oberding is in the Munich area in the middle of [[Erdinger Moos]] between the rivers Sempt and village. The village is about 6 & nbsp;km north-west of the county [[Erding]], 17 & nbsp;km south of [[Freising]] and 36 & nbsp;km from the state capital [[Munich]] in the immediate sector of the [[Munich Airport]].

For the planned construction of the third take-off and landing strip another partial removal of moss Schwaiger is necessary.
