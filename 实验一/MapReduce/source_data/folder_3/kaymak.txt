 '''Kaymak'''  is  a Turkish [[dairy product]]. It is similar to [[clotted cream]]. 
 
 Traditional Turkish kaymak is made from [[water buffalo]] [[milk]], but it can also be made from cow milk. 
 
 Kaymak is main part of Turkish breakfast. It can be eaten at breakfast with honey or served with [[dessert]]s.
