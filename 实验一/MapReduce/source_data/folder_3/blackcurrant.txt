 The '''blackcurrant''' (''Ribes nigrum'') is a small, bitter-tasting [[fruit]]. It can be found in central and northern [[Europe]] and northern [[Asia]]. 
 
 Blackcurrant has a lot of [[vitamin C]] in it. Blackcurrant gets its name from its dark [[color]]. It is also used to make [[jelly]], [[ice cream]], and [[liqueur|cordial]]. Blackcurrant is a popular flavor in the [[United Kingdom]]. Blackcurrants are also used in [[salad]]s and in [[baking]], similar to [[raisin]]s.
