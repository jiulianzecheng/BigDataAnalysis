 A '''tractor''' or a '''road locomotive''' is a strong work vehicle used for [[farming]] < ref > {{cite book|page=[https://archive.org/details/colouroxfordengl02edunse_d9n3/page/747 747]|publisher=Oxford University Press|title=Colour Oxford English Dictionary|url=https://archive.org/details/colouroxfordengl02edunse_d9n3|date=2002|edition=Second|isbn=0198605692}} < /ref >  and [[construction]]. It usually has a seat for only the driver, and can be used to pull many different [[tool]]s or [[Trailer (vehicle)|trailers]]. 
 
 Most tractors use big wheels, but some use rubber tracks to spread their weight over a large area. This is useful for farmers in places with very soft or muddy ground where a tractor with wheels might get stuck. < ref > {{cite web |title=Wheels vs tracked tractor - advantages and disadvantages |url=https://www.utilben.ro/article/wheels-vs-tracked-tractor---advantages-and-disadvantages |website=UTILBEN |accessdate=9 May 2022 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20220509154654/https://www.utilben.ro/article/wheels-vs-tracked-tractor---advantages-and-disadvantages |archivedate=9 May 2022 |date=12 September 2018}} < /ref >
