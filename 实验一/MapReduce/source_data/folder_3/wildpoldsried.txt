 '''Wildpoldsried''' is a [[municipality]]  in the district of [[Oberallgäu]] in [[Bavaria]] in [[Germany]]. The village has been awarded for reducing it's carbon footprint and using renewable energy. < ref > {{Cite web|url=https://www.biocycle.net/2011/08/16/german-village-achieves-energy-independence-and-then-some/|title=BioCycle - Wildpoldsried|language=de|access-date=26 February 2017}} < /ref >  Wildpoldsried now produces 321 percent more energy than it needs. Last year it gained over 4 million euros of [[revenue]].
