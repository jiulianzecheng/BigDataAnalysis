 '''Polygyros''' is a city in the area of [[Macedonia (Greece)|Macedonia]] in [[Greece]]. It is the capital of the [[Chalkidiki prefecture]] and of the [[Chalcidice|homonym municipality]]. It is found on the foothill of mountain [[Cholomon]].
