 '''Mosgiel''' {{IPAc-en|ˈ|m|ɒ|z|ɡ|iː|l}} ([[Māori language|Māori]]: ''Te Konika o te Matamata'') is a urban satellite town and is fifteen kilometers west of [[Dunedin]], [[Otago]], [[New Zealand]]. It has 5 primary schools and one high school in the town. It is the home of All Black coach [[Steve Hansen]] and rugby union and rugby league player [[Brad Thorn]].
