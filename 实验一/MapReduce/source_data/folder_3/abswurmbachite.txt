'''Abswurmbachite''' is a [[black]] or dark [[brown]] [[metal]]lic [[mineral]]. It is named after [[Irmgard Abs-Wurmbach]], who is a [[Germany|German]] [[mineralogist]].  It is found in Mili, Avia or Andros Island, [[Greece]].  It is not radioactive. < ref > http://www.webmineral.com/data/Abswurmbachite.shtml Mineral Data Sheet < /ref >
