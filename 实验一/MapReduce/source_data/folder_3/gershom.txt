[[Gershom]]  was the son of [[Moses]] and [[Zipporah]], grandson of [[Jethro]] and older brother of [[Eliezer|Eliezer .]] Gershom was born in [[Midian]]. According to the [[Book of Exodus]], Moses named him Gershom because he said “I am a stranger in a strange land”.
