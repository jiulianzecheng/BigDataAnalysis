 '''Puolanka''' is a [[municipality]] in [[Kainuu]], [[Finland]]. It had 3,000 living there in January 2014. The municipalities next to it are [[Hyrynsalmi]], [[Paltamo]], [[Pudasjärvi]], [[Ristijärvi]], [[Suomussalmi]], [[Utajärvi ]] and [[Vaala]]. 
 
 ==Some villages== 
 Aittokylä, Askankylä, Leipivaara, Lylykylä, Naulaperä,Puolanka, Väyrylä, and Yli-Oterma.
