 The '''Alzette''' ({{Lang-lb|Uelzecht}}, {{Lang-de|Alzig}}) is a [[river]] in [[France]] and [[Luxembourg]]. It is a right [[tributary]] of the [[Sauer]] river, and so it is part of the [[drainage basin]] of the [[Rhine river]]. 
 
 The Alzette river is sung in ''Ons Heemecht'', the [[national anthem]] of Luxembourg ({{Lang-lb|Wou d'Uelzécht durech d'Wisen zéit,...}}, {{Lang-fr|Où l'Alzette arrose champs et prés...}}, {{Lang-de|Wo die Alzette durch die Wiesen zieht,...}}). 
 
 ==Geography== 
 The Alzette river has a length of {{Convert|73|km|mi|0|abbr=off}} but only {{Convert|2.7|km|mi|1|abbr=off}} of them are in France. < ref name= " sandre " > {{cite web | url=http://services.sandre.eaufrance.fr/Courdo/Fiche/client/fiche_courdo.php?CdSandre=A89-0200 |title=L'Alzette (A89-0200) |publisher=SANDRE - Portail national d'accès aux référentiels sur l'eau |language=fr |access-date=8 May 2014 }} < /ref >  Its [[drainage basin]] has an area of {{Convert|1172|km2|sqmi|0|abbr=off}}, most of it in Luxembourg. 
 
 ===Course=== 
 The Alzette has its source in France in the territory of the ''commune'' of [[Thil, Meurthe-et-Moselle|Thil]] in the department of [[Meurthe-et-Moselle]] ([[Lorraine]]), at an [[altitude]] of about {{Convert|305|m|ft|0|abbr=off}}. It passes through the [[France|French]] ''communes'' of [[Villerupt]] ({{Lang-lb|Weller}}) and [[Audun-le-Tiche]] ({{Lang-de|Deutsch-Oth}}, {{Lang-lb|Däitsch-Oth}}. < ref name= " sandre "  / > 
 
 After a few kilometers, the Alzette enters Luxembourg, flowing through the city of [[Esch-sur-Alzette]] ({{lang-lb|Esch-Uelzecht}}, {{lang-de|Esch-an-der-Alzette}} or ''Esch-an-der-Alzig'', {{lang-fr|Esch-sur-Alzette}}). At [[Lameschmillen]] (near [[Bergem]]) it is joined by the [[Mess (river)|Mess]] river. 
 
 It flows on a wide [[floodplain]] before getting into a narrow [[valley]] near [[Hesperange]]. Then it flows through the [[Luxembourg]]ish cities of [[Luxembourg City|Luxembourg]] (where it is joined by the [[Pétrusse]] river) and [[Mersch]] (where is joined by the [[Mamer (river)|Mamer]] and [[Eisch]] rivers). It joins, as a right tributary, the [[Sauer]] river near [[Ettelbruck]], at about {{Convert|193|m|ft|0|abbr=off}} of altitude.
