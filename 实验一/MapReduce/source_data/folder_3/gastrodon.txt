'''Gastrodon''' is one of the [[fiction]]al [[species]] of [[Pokémon]] from the ''[[Pokémon]]'' media franchise owned by [[Nintendo]] and [[Game Freak]]. 
 
 It is a Water/Ground Pokémon from the fifth generation of games; Diamond, Pearl and Platinum. It has two forms, named east sea and west sea. Whilst they are the same statistically, they have completely different designs. Their pre-evolution, Shellos, also has varying forms.
