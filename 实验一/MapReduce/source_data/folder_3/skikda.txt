 '''Skikda''' ⵙⴽⵉⴽⴷⴰ ({{lang-ar|سكيكدة}}), formerly Philippeville, is a city in northeastern [[Algeria]] and a port on the [[Mediterranean]]. It is the capital of [[Skikda Province]] and [[Skikda District]].
