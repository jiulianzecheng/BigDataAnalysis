A '''Juggalo''' is someone who listens to [[Psychopathic Records]] and or likes [[Insane Clown Posse]]. They usually drink a drink called [[Faygo]]. 
 
 The name started at a 1994 [[concert]] by Insane Clown Posse.  In the song  " The Juggla " , [[Joseph Bruce|Violent J]] called the [[audience]]  " Juggalos " .  It became popular.  They wrote the songs  " What Is a Juggalo?  The '''Gathering of the Juggalos''' is a yearly festival put on by Psychopathic Records.  It has performances by the entire label as well as many well-known musical groups and [[underground music |underground artists]]. It was started by Robert Bruce, Insane Clown Posse, and their label in 2000.
