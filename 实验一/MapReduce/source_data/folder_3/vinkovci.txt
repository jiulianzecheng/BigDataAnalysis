 '''Vinkovci''' is a city in [[Vukovar-Syrmia County]], [[Croatia]]. It is in the eastern part of the [[Slavonia]] region. The city is on the [[Bosut (river)|Bosut]] river. In 2021, 31,057 people lived there.
