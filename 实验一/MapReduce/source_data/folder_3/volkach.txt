 '''Volkach''' is a town in [[Kitzingen (district)|Kitzingen]] in the ''[[Regierungsbezirk]] [[Unterfranken]]'' (Lower Franconia) in [[Bavaria]], Germany. It is on the river [[Main (river)|Main]] and has a population of around 8,700.
