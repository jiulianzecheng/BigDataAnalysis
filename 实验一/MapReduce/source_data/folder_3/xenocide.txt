 '''''Xenocide''''' (1991) is a [[science fiction]] book that is third in the [[Ender's Game series|''Ender's Game'' series]].  It was written by [[Orson Scott Card]] and was nominated for the [[Hugo Award]] for Best Novel in 1992.  It was followed by ''[[Children of the Mind]]''.
