In [[Norse mythology]], '''Borr''' is the father of the [[Creation myth|creation]] [[Deity|gods]] [[Odin]], [[Vili and Vé]]. His wife is Bestla. Borr was the son of [[Búri]]. He was one of the first three gods, and one of the creator's of humanity.
