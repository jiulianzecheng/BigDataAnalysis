 '''Marshmello''' is an [[electronic music]] producer and [[Disc jockey|DJ]]. His songs  " [[Silence (Marshmello song)|Silence]] " ,  " [[Wolves (Selena Gomez and Marshmello song)|Wolves]] " ,  " [[Friends (Marshmello and Anne-Marie song)|Friends]] " ,  " [[Happier (Marshmello and Bastille song)|Happier]] " , and  " [[Alone (Marshmello song)|Alone]] "  reached the Top 30 of the [[Billboard Hot 100|''Billboard'' Hot 100]]. They have been certified as multi-platinum in several countries. Marshmello has over 55 million subscribers on [[YouTube]] because of his unique work. He was born on May 19th, 1992. His real name is '''Christopher Comstock'''. Marshmello hides his identity because he wants to hide from fame. 
 
 ==Awards== 
 Marshmello is concluded the richest DJ in the world. His net worth is 40 million.
