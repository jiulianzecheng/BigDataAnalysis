 '''Edsbyn''' is an [[urban area]] in the [[counties of Sweden|county]] of [[Gävleborg County|Gävleborg]] in [[Sweden]]. It is in the [[Ovanåker Municipality]]. Edsbyn is the home of the sports club [[Edsbyns IF]], which has won several Swedish national men's [[bandy]] championship titles.
