 '''Zenica''' is the fourth-largest [[city]] in [[Bosnia and Herzegovina]]. It is about {{convert|70|km|mi|0|abbr=on}} north of [[Sarajevo]] and is on the [[Bosna (river)|Bosna]] river, surrounded by a mountainous and hilly landscape. The city is home to 115,134 < ref name= " ds " / >  inhabitants as of 2013.
