 '''Melissa Viviane Jefferson''' (born April 27, 1988), known professionally as '''Lizzo''', is an [[Americans|American]] singer, rapper, songwriter, and flutist. < ref > {{cite web | url=https://www.vulture.com/2019/02/lizzos-flute-sasha-flute-is-the-most-legendary-flute.html | title=Lizzo's Flute, Sasha Flute, is the Most Legendary Flute of All Time | date=8 February 2019 }} < /ref > 
 
 Lizzo released two studio albums:''[[Lizzobangers]]'' (2013), and ''[[Big Grrrl Small World]]'' (2015). In 2014, ''[[Time (magazine)|Time]]'' named her one of fourteen music artists to watch. Lizzo's first major-label [[extended play|EP]], ''[[Coconut Oil (EP)|Coconut Oil]]'', was released in 2016. 
 
 In 2019, she became popular after her third studio album, ''[[Cuz I Love You]]'', which peaked inside the top five of the [[Billboard 200|''Billboard'' 200]]. The album spawned two singles:  " [[Juice (Lizzo song)|Juice]] "  and  " [[Tempo (Lizzo song)|Tempo]] " . Lizzo's 2017 single  " [[Truth Hurts (song)|Truth Hurts]] "  became a [[Viral video|viral]] hit, topping the [[Billboard Hot 100|''Billboard'' Hot 100]] two years after its release.  
 
 Lizzo has been nominated for [[Grammy Award for Best New Artist|Best New Artist]] and received eight nominations at the [[62nd Annual Grammy Awards]], including [[Grammy Award for Album of the Year|Album of the Year]] for the deluxe version of ''Cuz I Love You'' and [[Grammy Award for Song of the Year|Song of the Year]] and [[Grammy Award for Record of the Year|Record of the Year]] for  " Truth Hurts " .
