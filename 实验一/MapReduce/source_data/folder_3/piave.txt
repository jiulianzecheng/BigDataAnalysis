 The '''Piave''' ({{lang-la|Plavis}}) is a [[river]] in northern [[Italy]]. It begins in the [[Alps]] and flows southeast for more of {{convert|220|km|mi}} into the [[Adriatic Sea]] near the city of [[Venice]]. It is the longest river of the [[Veneto]] region. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 The Piave river has a length of {{Convert|220|km|mi|0|abbr=on}}, and a [[drainage basin]] with an area of approximately {{Convert|4100|km2|sqmi|0|abbr=on }}. < ref name= " trecani " > {{cite web |url=http://www.treccani.it/enciclopedia/piave/ |title=Piave |publisher=Treccani.it - Enciclopedie on line |language=it |access-date=8 April 2017 }} < /ref >   It is the fifth river of Italy for length among those which flow directly into the sea. 
 
 Its [[average]] yearly [[Discharge (hydrology)|discharge]] ([[volume]] of [[water]] which passes through a section of the river per [[Unit of measurement|unit]] of [[time]]) is {{Convert|137.0 |m3/s|cuft/s|abbr=on }} at [[Nervesa della Battaglia]] in the [[Treviso]] province. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://www.adbve.it/Documenti/piani/Piave/RELAZIONE%20PSSI.pdf |title=Bacino del Fiume Piave - Piano stralcio per la sicurezza idraulica del medio e basso corso |publisher=ADBVE |language=it |access-date=10 April 2017 |archive-date=30 December 2016 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20161230024417/http://adbve.it/Documenti/piani/Piave/RELAZIONE%20PSSI.pdf |url-status=dead }} < /ref > 
 
 ===Course=== 
 [[File:Cartello sorgenti Piave.jpg|left|thumb|[[Source (river or stream)|Source]] of the Piave river]] 
 The Piave river starts on the Eastern [[Alps]] (the [[Carnic Alps]]), on the southern slopes of the [[Monte Peralba]], in the [[Commune|comune]] of [[Sappada]], [[province of Belluno]], at an [[elevation]] of about {{Convert|2037|m|ft|0|abbr=on}}, near the border with the [[Friuli-Venezia Giulia]] [[Regions of Italy|region]]. 
 
 The river flows only through the [[Province of Belluno|Belluno]], [[Province of Treviso|Treviso]] and [[Province of Venice|Venice]] provinces in the Veneto region and flows into the [[Adriatic Sea]] to the northwest of [[Venice]], next to the [[port]] of ''Cortellazzo'', between the ''[[Commune|comuni]]'' of [[Eraclea]] and [[Jesolo]]. To the left of the mouth, there is a [[lagoon]] called ''Laguna del Mort'' formed by an old branch of the river.

 == History == 
 There was an important [[battle]] in 1809 along the river during the [[Napoleonic Wars]] where the Franco-Italian [[army]] fought against [[Austria]]n troops.   
   
 In 1918, during [[World War I]], it was the place of the  " Battle of the Piave River " , the last major [[Austria-Hungary|Austro-Hungarian]] attack during the [[Italian Campaign]], which failed. The Battle of the Piave was the decisive battle of World War I on the [[Italian Front]].
