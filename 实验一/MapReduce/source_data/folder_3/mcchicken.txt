 The '''McChicken''' is a kind of [[chicken sandwich]] from [[McDonald's]] restaurants. It is made with chicken [[breast]] meat in a [[fry|fried]] coating made from [[bread]] [[crumb]]s. A man used a McChicken in a [[crime]] in [[Des Moines]], [[Iowa]]. He threw the sandwich at his wife. The [[force]] of the hit broke her nose. < ref > {{cite web|last=Elmer|first=MacKenzie|title=Police: Man in McChicken assault broke wife's nose|url=http://www.desmoinesregister.com/story/news/crime-and-courts/2014/12/03/mcchicken-used-weapon/19823923/|publisher=The Des Moines Register|accessdate=21 February 2016}} < /ref >
