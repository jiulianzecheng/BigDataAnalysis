 '''Cruelty''' is [[pleasure]] in causing [[suffering]] to other living things or even themselves. < ref > {{Cite web|url=https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/cruel|title=Definition of CRUEL|website=www.merriam-webster.com|language=en|access-date=2019-03-17}} < /ref >  [[Sadomasochism|Sadism]] can also be related to this as it means someone really enjoying this. Cruel ways of causing suffering may involve [[violence]]. 
 
 For example, if a person is drowning and begging for help and another person is able to help with no cost or risk, but watches happily. < ref name= " brainyquote1 " > {{cite web|url=http://www.brainyquote.com/quotes/keywords/cruelty.html#z9RzI1eSJFMuWO4h.99 |title=Cruelty Quotes |publisher=BrainyQuote |access-date=2013-08-18}} < /ref >  [[Bertrand Russell]] said that  " the [causing] of cruelty with a good conscience is a delight to moralists. That is why they invented Hell. " < ref name= " brainyquote1 " / >
