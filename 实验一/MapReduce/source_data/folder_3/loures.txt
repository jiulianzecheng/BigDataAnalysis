 '''Loures''' ({{IPA-pt|ˈlowɾɨʃ|-|Pt-pt Loures FF.ogg}}) is a city in the central [[Portugal|Portuguese]] [[Grande Lisboa Subregion]]. It is 13 & nbsp;km to the north of [[Lisbon]]. It was created on 26 July 1886.
