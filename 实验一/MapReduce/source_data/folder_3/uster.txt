 '''Uster''' is a [[city]] and [[Capital city|capital]] of the [[district]] of [[Uster (district)|Uster]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Zurich (canton)|Zurich]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 It is the third-largest city in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Zurich (canton)|Zurich]]. About 35,000 people live there. It is one of the twenty largest cities in Switzerland. It is next to lake [[Greifensee]]. 
 
 The city of Uster received the [[Wakker Prize]] in 2001. 
 
 ==History== 
 In 1099, the [[House of Rapperswil]] gave the city [[St. Andreas Church]].
