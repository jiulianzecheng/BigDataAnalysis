 '''Eggnog''' is a sweet [[dairy]] drink made with [[milk]], [[cream]], [[sugar]], beaten [[egg (food)|egg]]s, and flavoured with [[cinnamon]] and [[nutmeg]]. Alcoholic kinds are also made. Eggnog is a popular drink in the [[United States]] and [[Canada]], and is usually drank with winter parties such as [[Christmas]] and [[New Year]]. [[Brandy]], [[rum]] or [[bourbon whiskey|bourbon]] may be added. As for its origins, it is agreed upon that eggnog originated in present day [[United Kingdom]] in the early medieval period. The drink would then make its way to North America in the 18th century and become a popular beverage during the winter holidays. < ref > {{Cite web|url=http://time.com/3957265/history-of-eggnog/|title=A Brief History of Eggnog|website=Time|language=en|access-date=2018-08-19}} < /ref >
