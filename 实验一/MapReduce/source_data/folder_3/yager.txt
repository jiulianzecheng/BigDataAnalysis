 '''''Yager''''' is a combat flight [[simulation]] [[video game]]. It was developed by Yager Development. It was published by THQ, Kemco, and DreamCatcher Interactive. It was first released as an [[Xbox (console)|Xbox]] exclusive. Later being ported to [[Microsoft Windows]], both in 2003. The Xbox version was released in North America in later 2004, while the PC version was released in North America as '''''Aerial Strike: Low Altitude - High Stakes: The Yager Missions''''' in February 2005.  
 
 The game was not a commercial success in larger part because of a delayed North American release in a lack of advertising.
