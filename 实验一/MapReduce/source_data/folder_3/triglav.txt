 '''Triglav''' is a [[mountain]] in the [[Alps]]. It is the highest mountain in [[Slovenia]]. It is also the highest [[summit (topography)|peak]] of the [[Julian Alps]], in the [[southeast]]ern part of the Alps.  
 
 Its name means  " three-headed "  in [[Slovene language|Slovene]]. While this can describe the mountain, it is more likely the mountain was named for the Old Slovene [[deity|god]], [[Triglav (mythology)|Triglav]]. The mountain is shown on the [[Flag of Slovenia]].  
 
 This mountain is in a [[national park]].
