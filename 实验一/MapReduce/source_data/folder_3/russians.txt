 '''Russians''' are an ethnic group and nation native to [[Russia]]. They are the world’s largest Slavic ethnic group. 14% of Russians live in the former Soviet Union states, especially in [[Ukraine]] and [[Kazakhstan]]. Most Russians are Orthodox Christians. < ref > [https://www.worldatlas.com/amp/articles/largest-ethnic-groups-in-russia.html Largest Ethnic Groups In Russia] < /ref >
