 == History == 
 Cypronia was founded in 1997 as Ablaze Entertainment. In 2001, the name was changed to Cypron Studios. Later, the name was changed again to Cypronia. 
 
 The studio became popular after they released the 2015 video game ''[[Cube Life: Island Survival]]''. It reached #1 in the Europe, #2 in the American and #3 in the Japanese [[Nintendo eShop]] charts. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Cypronia - Cube Life: Island Survival (PS4)|url=http://www.cypronia.com/93-cube-life-island-survival-switch-ps4|access-date=2019-06-17|website=www.cypronia.com}} < /ref >
