 '''DekaBank''' '''Deutsche Girozentrale''' is the central provider of asset management and capital market solutions of the [[German public bank|German Savings Bank Finance Group]]. Registered in [[Frankfurt am Main]] and [[Berlin]], its headquarters is based in Frankfurt. 
 
 ==History== 
 DekaBank was formed in 1999 after the merger of Deutsche Girozentrale Deutsche Kommunalbank and DekaBank GmbH. Together with its subsidiaries it forms the Deka Group, making it one of the largest securities services providers in Germany.
