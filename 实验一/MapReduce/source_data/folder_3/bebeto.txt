 '''José Roberto Gama de Oliveira''' (born 16 February 1964), known as '''Bebeto''' ({{IPA|pt-BR|beˈbɛtu|lang}}), is a Brazilian former professional [[Forward (association football)|football]] player who played as a [[Forward (association football)|forward]]. He entered politics in the [[2010 Brazilian general election|2010 Brazilian general elections]] and was elected to the [[Legislative Assemblies of Brazilian states|Legislative Assembly]] of Rio de Janeiro representing the [[Democratic Labour Party (Brazil)|Democratic Labour Party]].
