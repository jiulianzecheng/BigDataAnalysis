 '''''Titanotylopus''''' is an [[extinct]] [[genus]] of [[Terrestrial animal|terrestrial]] [[herbivore]] in the [[family (biology)|family]] [[Camelidae]]. It was [[endemic]] to [[North America]] from the [[Miocene]] through [[Pleistocene]] 10.3 million years ago—300,000 years ago. It existed for about {{Mya|10.3-0.3|million years}}. < ref > [http://paleodb.org/cgi-bin/bridge.pl?action=checkTaxonInfo & taxon_no=42555 & is_real_user=1 PaleoBiology Database: ''Titanotylopus'', basic info] < /ref > 
 
 Its name is derived from the [[Greek language|Greek]] words Τιτάν, τύλος and πούς —  " [[Titan (mythology)|Titan]] " ,  " knob "  and  " foot " ; thus,  " giant knobby-foot. "
