For organisms that [[Sexual reproduction|reproduce sexually]], '''insemination''' is the transfer of [[male]] [[Gamete|gametes]] (usually [[sperm]]) into the [[female]] reproductive tract, for the purpose of generating offspring. In [[Mammal|mammals]], this is the [[uterus]], in birds, this is the oviduct. Insemination happens naturally during [[sexual intercourse]], or copulation. It can also be done artificially, in which case it is called [[artificial insemination]].
