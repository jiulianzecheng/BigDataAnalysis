'''Gossip''' is when someone spreads a [[rumor]], mostly about personal or private affairs of others, which can be true or false. < ref > [http://dictionary.reference.com/browse/gossip?s=t gossip] < /ref >   The name ''gossip'' mostly became known during the 16th Century. The word is from [[Old English]] ''godsibb'', from ''[[god (word)|god]]'' and ''[[sibb]]''.
