 '''Morcote''' is a [[municipality]] of the district [[Lugano (district)|Lugano]] in the [[Switzerland|Swiss]] [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Ticino]]. It is on the shore of [[Lake Lugano]], about {{convert|10|km|abbr=off}} from [[Lugano]] in the district of [[Lugano (district)|Lugano]].
