 '''Astrakhan''' ({{lang-ru|Астрахань}}; {{lang-tt|Әстерхан|Ästerxan}}) is a city on the [[Volga River]] in [[Russia]]. It is the largest city and [[administrative center]] of [[Astrakhan Oblast]]. In 2010, 520,339 people lived there.
