 '''Bolbanabad''' ({{lang-ku|Bolwanawa}}, {{lang-fa|بلبلان آباد}}) is a city in the Kurdistan province of [[Iran]]. Its people speak [[Kurdish language|Kurdish]]. They are followers of [[Islam]].  At the 2016 census, its population was 3,207, in 943 families. < ref > {{IranCensus2006|12}} < /ref >
