 '''Herbstadt''' is a [[Municipalities of Germany|municipality]] in [[Rhön-Grabfeld]] in [[Bavaria]] in [[Germany]]. It has the following villages: Breitensee, Herbstadt, Ottelmannshausen and a hamlet: Dörfleshof.
