 '''Nutmeg''' (''Myristica fragrans'') is a species of [[evergreen]] [[tree]] in the genus ''[[Myristica]]''. It is found in [[tropics|tropical]] [[Southeast Asia]] and [[Australia]]. 
 
 Nutmeg powder can  cause [[Allergy|allergic]] reactions.
