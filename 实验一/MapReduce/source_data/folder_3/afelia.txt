'''Afelia''' is a [[Greece|Greek]] [[food]]. It is popular in the [[island]] [[nation]] of [[Cyprus]]. Afelia is made from [[pork]], [[wine|red wine]], [[mushroom]]s, [[potato]]es and [[coriander]] seed.
