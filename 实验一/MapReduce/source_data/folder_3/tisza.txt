 The '''Tisza''' is one of the [[wikt:major|major]] [[river]]s of [[Central Europe]]. It starts in [[Ukraine]], with the White Tisza in the [[Chornohora]] and Black Tisza in the [[Gorgany]] range, and flows partially along the [[Romania]]n border.  It then enters [[Hungary]] at [[Tiszabecs]] and passes through  [[Hungary]] touching the border with [[Slovakia]].  It then into the [[Danube]] in central [[Vojvodina]] in [[Serbia]].  The Tisza drains an area of about 157,186 & nbsp;km². 
 
 Names for the river in the countries it flows through are: 
 *{{lang-ro|Tisa}}; 
 *{{lang-uk|Тиса}} (''Tysa''); 
 *{{lang-sk|Tisa}}; 
 *{{lang-hu|Tisza}} ({{pronounced|'tisɒ}}); 
 *{{lang-sr|Тиса}} (''Tisa'').
