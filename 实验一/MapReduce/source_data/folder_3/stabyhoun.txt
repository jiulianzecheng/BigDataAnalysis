 The '''Stabyhoun''' or '''Stabij''' is a rare [[List of dog breeds|dog breed]]. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.blueridgenow.com/news/20140307/saving-rare-breed-one-litter-at-a-time|title=Saving rare breed one litter at a time|website=Blueridgenow.com|access-date=11 December 2017|archive-date=21 March 2021|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210321220103/https://www.blueridgenow.com/news/20140307/saving-rare-breed-one-litter-at-a-time|url-status=live}} < /ref >  There are only about 6000 dogs of this type in the world. < ref name= " StUK "  / >  It is a [[spaniel]] type of [[working dog]]. 
 
 ==History== 
 The Stabyhoun was first noted in the [[Netherlands]] in the early 1800s where it was used as a working dog on farms and for hunting, specifically in the province of [[Friesland]]. < ref name= " StUK " > {{cite web |title=History |url=https://stabyhounuk.com/breed-history/ |publisher=Stabyhoun UK |access-date=21 March 2021}} < /ref > 
 
 ==Colour== 
 These dogs are usually black and white, with a smaller number being brown and white and very small number of yellow and white examples. < ref name= " AKC " > {{cite web |title=Stabyhoun |url=https://www.akc.org/dog-breeds/stabyhoun/ |publisher=AKC}} < /ref > 
 
 ==Temperment== 
 The Stabyhoun is a gentle breed that is patient with children and other dogs. To keep them happy, like all [[gun dog]]s, they need a lot of exercise. < ref name= " PG " > {{cite web |title=Stabyhoun |date=6 May 2015 |url=https://www.petguide.com/breeds/dog/stabyhoun/ |publisher=petguide.com |access-date=21 March 2021}} < /ref >
