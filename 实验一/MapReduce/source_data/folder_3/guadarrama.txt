 '''Guadarrama''' is a [[village]] in the nothwestern part of the community of [[Madrid]], in [[Spain]]. The population of Guadarrama is almost 15,000 people. In summer, there can be 60,000 people. 
 
 Guadarrama has become an important tourist point. It is a second home for many people from Madrid. 
 
 Guadarrama is near the  " Sierra of Guadarrama " .
