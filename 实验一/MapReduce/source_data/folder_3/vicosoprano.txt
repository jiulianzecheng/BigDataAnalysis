 '''Vicosoprano''' is a former [[municipality]] of the [[district]] [[Maloja (district)|Maloja]] in the [[Switzerland|Swiss]] [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Graubünden]], and is the largest village in the [[Bregaglia Range|Val Bregaglia]]. It is part of the municipality of [[Bregaglia]].
