 '''Wertingen''' ({{IPA-de|ˈvɛʁtɪŋən|-|De-Wertingen.ogg}}; {{Lang-gsw|Weartinga|label=[[Swabian German]]}}) is a [[Town#Germany|town]] in the [[Dillingen (district)|district of Dillingen]] in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. It is along the river [[Zusam]] in 13 & nbsp;km east of [[Dillingen, Bavaria|Dillingen]], and 28 & nbsp;km northwest of [[Augsburg]].  The city is the seat of the [[Municipal association (Germany)|municipal association]] Wertingen. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.wertingen.de/ |title=City of Wertingen on WertingenStadt}} < /ref >
