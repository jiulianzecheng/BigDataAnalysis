'''Benedito Custódio Ferreira''', nicknamed '''Escurinho''' (3 July 1930 – 12 December 2020) was a Brazilian [[association football|footballer]] who played as an [[attacking midfielder]] and [[Midfielder#Winger|left winger]]. He was born in [[Nova Lima]], [[Brazil]]. Escurinho played for [[Villa Nova Atlético Clube|Villa Nova]], [[Fluminense FC|Fluminense]], [[Atlético Junior]], [[Associação Atlética Portuguesa (RJ)|Portuguesa (RJ)]] and [[Bonsucesso Futebol Clube|Bonsucesso]]. < ref name= " auto " > {{Cite web|url=https://globoesporte.globo.com/futebol/times/fluminense/noticia/aos-90-anos-morre-escurinho-idolo-do-fluminense-e-quinto-jogador-que-mais-atuou-pelo-clube.ghtml|title=Aos 90 anos, morre Escurinho, ídolo do Fluminense e quinto jogador que mais atuou pelo clube|website=ge}} < /ref > 
 
 He played eight times for the [[Brazil national football team|Brazilian national team]] between 1955 and 1956. 
 
 He died on 12 November 2020 from [[Alzheimer's disease]] and [[multiple organ failure]] in Nova Lima at the age of 90. < ref name= " auto " / >
