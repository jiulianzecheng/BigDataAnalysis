 '''Allaman''' is a [[municipality]] in [[Morges (district)|Morges]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Vaud]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 The village is famous for the medieval [[Allaman Castle]] (Château d'Allaman), built by the [[Count of Vaud]] in 1253.
