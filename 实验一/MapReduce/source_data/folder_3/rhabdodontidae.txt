 '''Rhabdodontids''' were [[herbivorous]] [[ornithopod]] [[dinosaur]]s from the [[Cretaceous]]. They were similar to large, robust [[hypsilophodont]]s, with deep skulls and jaws. The [[family (biology)|family]] were defined as  " the most recent common ancestor of ''[[Zalmoxes]] robustus'' and ''[[Rhabdodon]] priscus'' and all the descendants of this common ancestor " .
