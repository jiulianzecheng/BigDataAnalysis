 '''Teylingen''' is a [[List of municipalities of the Netherlands|municipality]] in the [[Netherlands|Dutch]] [[Provinces of the Netherlands|province]] of [[South Holland]]. In 2019, 37,061 people lived there. 
 
 It was created on 1 January 2006 from the former municipalities of [[Sassenheim]], [[Voorhout]], and [[Warmond]].
