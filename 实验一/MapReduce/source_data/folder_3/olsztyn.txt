 '''Olsztyn''' is a city in northeast [[Poland]], on the [[Łyna]] river. Olsztyn is the biggest city and the capital of the [[Warmian-Masurian Voivodeship]]. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 The town is near a [[lake]] region of [[forest]]s and [[plain]]s. There are 13 lakes in the town and many more surrounding it: (Krzywe, Długie, Żbik, Redykajny and others).
