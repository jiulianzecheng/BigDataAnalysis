 '''Hostomel''' ({{lang-uk|Гостомель}}) is a city in [[Kyiv Oblast]], northwest of the [[capital city|capital]] city of [[Kyiv]].  
 
 Hostomel was badly damaged during the [[2022 Russian invasion of Ukraine]]. < ref >  [https://www.indiatoday.in/world/russia-ukraine-war/video/fire-reported-buildings-ukraine-hostomel-1921355-2022-03-06  " Fire reported in many buildings in Ukraine's Hostomel " ], India Today, 6 March 2022 < /ref >  Russian troops lost a battle at the Hostomel airport.
