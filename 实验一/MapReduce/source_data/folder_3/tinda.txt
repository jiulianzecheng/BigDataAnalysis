 '''Tinda''' ({{lang-ur|'''ٹنڈه''' or '''ٹنڈے'''}}), also called  " Indian squash " ,  " round [[melon]] " , < ref name= " Newly notified " > {{cite web |url=http://seednet.gov.in/Material/Horticulture_Variety.htm |title=NEWLY NOTIFIED VARIETIES OF SEEDS |website=Seednet.gov.in |access-date= March 4, 2017}} < /ref >   " Indian round [[gourd]] " ,  " apple gourd "  or  " Indian baby [[pumpkin]] " , is a [[Squash (plant)|squash]]-like [[Cucurbitaceae|cucurbit]] grown for its immature [[fruit]]. The fruit is a [[vegetable]] that is especially popular in [[Southern Asia]]. It is NOT the '' " National vegetable of India " .''
