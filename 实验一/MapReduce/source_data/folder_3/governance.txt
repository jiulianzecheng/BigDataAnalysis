'''Governance''' is the term for the way a group of people such as a [[country]] do things. Many groups create a [[government]] to decide how things are to be done. Governance is different from [[politics]]. Politics deals with people with different [[idea]]s working together to create an [[agreement]] about what to do, and governance is doing what politics decided needed to be done.Governance is also how government decision making affects people in that nation.
