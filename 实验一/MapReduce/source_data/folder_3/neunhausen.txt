 '''Neunhausen''' ({{lang-lb|Néngsen}}) is a former [[Communes of Luxembourg|commune]] in [[Luxembourg]]. It was part of the [[Wiltz (canton)|canton of Wiltz]]. 
 
 On 1 January 2012, the commune was merged with [[Heiderscheid]] to form the commune of [[Esch-sur-Sûre]].
