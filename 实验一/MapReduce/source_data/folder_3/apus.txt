 '''Apus''' is a [[constellation]] in the southern sky.  It is hard to see because it is not very bright.  Apus means  " no feet "  in [[Greek language|Greek]].  It was named  " no feet "  because it looks like a [[Bird-of-paradise|Bird-of-Paradise]], and people used to think that birds-of-paradise did not have [[feet]].
