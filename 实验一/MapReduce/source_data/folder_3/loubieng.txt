'''Loubieng''' is a [[Commune in France|commune]] of the [[Pyrénées-Atlantiques]] ''[[departments of France|département]]'' in the southwestern part of [[France]]. 
 
 == Geography == 
 Loubieng has a total area of 23.4 square kilometers.{{France-geo-stub}}
