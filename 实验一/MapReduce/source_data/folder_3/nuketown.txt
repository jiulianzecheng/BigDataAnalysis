 " '''Nuketown''' "  is the second song off of [[Americans|American]] [[Rap music|rapper]] [[Ski Mask the Slump God]]'s first [[studio album]] ''[[Stokeley]]''. The song was released on November 30, 2018. The song also featured American rapper [[Juice Wrld]]. The song's title is a reference to the ''[[Call of Duty: Black Ops]]'' map Nuketown. 
 
 The song was able to make it to #10 on the New Zealand Hot Singles, #32 on the [[Hot R & B/Hip-Hop Songs]], #63 on the [[Billboard Hot 100|''Billboard'' Hot 100]], and #81 on the [[Canadian Hot 100]] charts. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://nztop40.co.nz/chart/hotsingles?chart=4565|accessdate=September 3, 2023|title=New Zealand Hot Singles (10 December 2018)|publisher=[[Recorded Music NZ]]}} < /ref > < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.billboard.com/artist/Ski-Mask-the-Slump-God/chart-history/BSI|accessdate=September 3, 2023|title=Ski Mask the Slump God - Hot R & B/Hip-Hop Songs Chart History|publisher=[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]}} < /ref > < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.billboard.com/artist/Ski-Mask-the-Slump-God/chart-history/HSI|accessdate=September 3, 2023|title=Ski Mask the Slump God - Hot 100 Chart History|publisher=Billboard}} < /ref > < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.billboard.com/artist/Ski-Mask-the-Slump-God/chart-history/CAN|accessdate=September 3, 2023|title=Ski Mask the Slump God - Canadian Hot 100 Chart History|publisher=Billboard}} < /ref >  It was also certified Platinum by the [[Recording Industry Association of America]] (RIAA). < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.riaa.com/gold-platinum/?tab_active=default-award & ar=Ski+Mask+The+Slump+God & ti=Nuketown & format=Single & type=#search_section|accessdate=September 3, 2023|title=Ski Mask The Slump God â€“ Nuketown|publisher=[[Recording Industry Association of America]]}} < /ref > 
 
 During a [[Genius (company)|Genius]] lyrics breakdown, Ski calls out fake friends and raps about not caring what other people think about him. Ski said that his loud, shouting chorus of  " Cutthroat! Cutthroat! "  was inspired by his late friend and fellow rapper [[XXXTentacion]]. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://youtube.com/watch?v=UL3wwec0hok|accessdate=September 3, 2023|title=Ski Mask The Slump God  " Nuketown "  Official Lyrics  &  Meaning - Verified|publisher=[[Genius (company)|Genius]]}} < /ref > 
 
 A music video for the song was released. It was directed by [[Cole Bennett]]. In the video, a man holds a picture of Ski and tells his group that they need to kill him. Ski is flying on a plane when it is shot down. Ski survives and starts shooting the men. Juice Wrld drives into the scene on an [[All-terrain vehicle|ATV]] and joins Ski in the gun fight. The two kill their enemies continue rapping on the tarmac. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.complex.com/music/a/cmplxtara-mahadevan/ski-mask-juice-wrld-nuketown|accessdate=September 3, 2023|title=Ski Mask and Juice WRLD Join Forces in  " Nuketown "  Video|publisher=[[Complex Networks|Complex]]}} < /ref >
