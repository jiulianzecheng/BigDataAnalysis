 '''Blueberry''' refers a wild section of [[purple]] or [[blue]] [[berry|berries]]. It grows in a type of woody plant called a [[shrub]]. Many types of blueberries grow in [[North America]] and eastern [[Asia]]. Blueberries are more common between [[May]] and [[October]]. 
 
 Blueberry has a sweet taste, with a little [[acid|acidic]] hint. Wild blueberries have a stronger taste.  Blueberries are good for making [[jelly]], [[jam]], [[pie]], [[muffin]]s, pancakes, and many other foods.The most widely cultivated species of blueberry is [[Vaccinium corymbosum]]. Some similar kinds of berries are called blueberry or [[huckleberry]] in different places.
