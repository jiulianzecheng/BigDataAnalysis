 '''Savonlinna''' is a [[town]] in [[Finland]]. The population was almost 36,250 in January 2014. Neighbouring municipalities are [[Enonkoski]], [[Heinävesi]], [[Kitee]], [[Liperi]], [[Parikkala]], [[Rantasalmi]], [[Ruokolahti]], [[Rääkkylä]], [[Sulkava]] and [[Varkaus]]. Some parts of central Savonlinna has been built over [[island]]s. Over one-third of the area is water or islands. 
 
 The former municipalities of [[Kerimäki]] and [[Punkaharju]] were merged with Savonlinna in 2013.
