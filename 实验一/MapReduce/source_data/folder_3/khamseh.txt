The '''Khamseh''' is a tribal confederation in the province of Fars in southwestern [[Iran]]. It consists of five tribes.It was created in 1861 by  Naser al-Din Shah [[Qajar dynasty|Qajar]] to decrease strength of the Qashqai, who were gaining so much power. The Khamseh tribes were a mixture of Persians, Turks, and Arabs.
