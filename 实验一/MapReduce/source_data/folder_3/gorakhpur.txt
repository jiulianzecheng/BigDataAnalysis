 '''Gorakhpur''' is a city in the north-eastern region of [[Uttar Pradesh]] state of India. It is the administrative headquarters of [[Gorakhpur district]]. The city is home to the [[Gorakhnath Math]], a [[Gorakhnath]] temple. The city also has an [[Indian Air Force]] station, since 1963. According to [[2011 Indian Census]], Gorakhpur had a total population of 673,446, of which 353,907 were males and 319,539 were females. < ref name= " Census2011Gov " > {{cite web |title=Census of India: Gorakhpur |url=http://www.censusindia.gov.in/pca/SearchDetails.aspx?Id=212078 |website=www.censusindia.gov.in |access-date=16 December 2019}} < /ref >
