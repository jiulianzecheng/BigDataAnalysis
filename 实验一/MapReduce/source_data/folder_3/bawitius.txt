 '''''Bawitius''''' is an extinct [[genus]] of giant [[bichir]] from the [[Upper Cretaceous]]. The type species is '''''[[Bawitius bartheli|B. bartheli]]'''''. 
 
 == Description == 
 Compared to modern [[Bichir|bichirs]], ''Bawitius'' was an enormous, monster-sized [[Bichir|polypterid]]. The scales were different, apart from size, from those of modern [[bichirs]]: they feature a discontinuous ganoine layer, a rectilinear shape, and small articular processes.
