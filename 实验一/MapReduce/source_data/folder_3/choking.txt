'''Choking''' happens when breathing is made difficult. It happens when the [[windpipe]] is blocked. < ref name= " :3 " > {{Cite book|title=Injury facts|last=National Safety Council. Research and Statistics Department.|isbn=9780879123345|edition=2015|location=Itasca, IL|oclc=910514461|year = 2015}} < /ref >   
 
 In some cases the air flow is completely blocked, in other cases some amount of air can pass to the lungs. Insufficient [[oxygen]] being delivered to the body will result in [[oxygen deprivation]].  
 
 Although oxygen stored in the blood and lungs can keep a person alive for several minutes after breathing stops. < ref > {{Cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=BFl6GGZsqp4C & dq=Asphyxiation+and+Ventilatory+Requirements & pg=PA49|title=Sudden Deaths in Custody|author1=Ross, Darrell Lee|author2=Chan, Theodore C|year=2006|isbn=978-1-59745-015-7}} < /ref >
