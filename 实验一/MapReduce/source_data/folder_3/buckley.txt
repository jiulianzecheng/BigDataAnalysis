 '''Buckley''' ({{lang-cy|Bwcle}}''' '''{{IPA-cy|ˈbʊklɛ|}}) is a town and [[community (Wales)|community]] in [[Flintshire]], north-east [[Wales]], {{convert|2|mi|km}} from the county town of [[Mold, Flintshire|Mold]]. 
 
 Buckley is the second largest town in Flintshire in terms of population. < ref name=2001census > {{Cite journal|url=http://neighbourhood.statistics.gov.uk/dissemination/LeadTableView.do?a=3 & b=801648 & c=buckley & d=16 & e=15 & g=414380 & i=1001x1003x1004 & m=0 & r=1 & s=1237379535258 & enc=1 & dsFamilyId=779|title=2001 Census: Buckley|publisher=Office for National Statistics|access-date=18 March 2009}}{{Dead link|date=January 2021 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }} < /ref >  At the [[United Kingdom Census 2011|2011 Census]], its community had a population of 15,665. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.neighbourhood.statistics.gov.uk/dissemination/LeadTableView.do?a=7 & b=11121379 & c=CH7+2EF & d=16 & e=62 & g=6490066 & i=1001x1003x1032x1004 & m=0 & r=0 & s=1476232987766 & enc=1 & dsFamilyId=2473|title=Town population 2011|access-date=12 October 2016}}{{Dead link|date=January 2021 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }} < /ref >  When the contiguous [[Argoed, Flintshire|Argoed]] community is included, Buckley has a population of 21,502.
