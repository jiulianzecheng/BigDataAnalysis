 " '''Pillowtalk''' "  is a song recorded by English singer [[Zayn Malik]].  It is his first single since leaving the boy band [[One Direction]].  The song is a [[ballad]] about sex.  It entered at the top of the charts in the UK, US, Ireland, Australia, and Canada. 
 
 The music video features model Gigi Hadid as Malik's love interest. 
 
 " Pillowtalk "  was certified triple-platinum in the US for shipments of over three million copies in that country. < ref > {{Cite news|url=https://www.riaa.com/gold-platinum/?tab_active=default-award & ar=Zayn & ti=PILLOWTALK|title=Gold  &  Platinum - RIAA|work=RIAA|access-date=2018-06-20|language=en-US}} < /ref >
