 '''Llangefni''' is a town in [[Anglesey]], [[Wales]]. According to the 2011 census, it has a population of 5,116 people. 
 
 It is [[Anglesey]]'s [[county town]], but the largest town is [[Holyhead]]. 
 
 It is located near the centre of the Isle of Anglesey and is on the banks of the [[Afon Cefni]], the river that the town takes its name from. 
 
 Llangefni is a commercial and farming town and once had the largest [[cattle]] market in Anglesey. 
 
 According to the 2011 census, Llangefni has the 6th highest percentage of [[Welsh language]] speakers in the whole of [[Wales]].
