 '''Mitterteich''' ([[Northern Bavarian]]: ''Miederdeich'') is a municipality in [[Tirschenreuth (district)|Tirschenreuth]], in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. It is 10 & nbsp;km northwest of [[Tirschenreuth]], and 17 & nbsp;km southwest of [[Cheb]].
