 '''Om''' (also spelled Aum), is the most [[sacred]] [[symbol]] in [[Hinduism]]. [[Hindu scripture]]s tell many things about this symbol. In Hinduism, Om is like calling god's name towards you. This name is generally said three times, before chanting any prayers. Om is usually related to the Hindu God [[Shiva]], who is the destroyer god.
