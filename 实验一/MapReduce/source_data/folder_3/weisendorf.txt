 '''Weisendorf''' is a municipality in the district of [[Erlangen-Höchstadt]], in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. It belongs to the administrative region of [[Middle Franconia]]. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 Weisendorf is 15 & nbsp;km west of [[Erlangen]].

 ==Transport== 
 Weisendorf is near the motorway [[Bundesautobahn 3|A3]] ([[Frankfurt am Main]]-[[Regensburg]]). The next railroad station is in [[Erlangen]] (c. 15 & nbsp;km). The [[Nuremberg Airport]] (c. 30 & nbsp;km) provides access to many cities in Europe and the rest of the world.
