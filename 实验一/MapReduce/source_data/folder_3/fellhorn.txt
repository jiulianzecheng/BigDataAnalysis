 The '''Fellhorn''' is a {{convert|2038|m}} tall [[mountain]] in the [[Allgäu Alps]] near [[Oberstdorf]], [[Germany]], on the border with [[Austria]]. According to Austrian information the mountain is 2039 m tall. < ref > Austria: ''[http://www.amap.at Austrian Map online (Austrian 1:50,000 map series)]''. Retrieved 19 January 2011. < /ref > 
 
 == Access == 
 In 1972, a [[gondola lift]], the Fellhorn Lift, was built that goes up the mountain from the [[Stillach]] valley. Its [[top station]] is at 1,967 metres.  The Fellhorn/[[Kanzelwand]] Ski Region has 24 kilometres of [[piste]] and 14 lifts. < ref > [http://www.das-hoechste.de/winter/skigebiete/fellhorn-kanzelwand/ Skigebiet Fellhorn/Kanzelwand], retrieved 5 April 2012 < /ref >
