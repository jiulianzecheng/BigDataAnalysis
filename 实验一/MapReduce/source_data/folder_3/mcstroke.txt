 " '''McStroke''' "  is the 8th [[episode]] of ''[[Family Guy]]''{{'}}s [[Family Guy (season 6)|6th season]]. It was first [[broadcast]] on the [[Fox network]] on January 13, 2008. It was [[screenwriter|written]] by [[Wellesley Wild]]. [[Brian Iles]] is the [[wikt:director|director]] of the episode. The episode is about [[Peter Griffin]] having a [[stroke]] after eating too many [[hamburger]]s. 
 
 == Story == 
 
 Peter wants to get a [[mustache]] after reading a [[magazine]] about them. He gets one and thinks that he is a better person. He walks by a [[restaurant]] and sees it [[burning]]. A [[firefighter]] sees Peter's mustache and thinks he is also a firefighter. Peter goes in the restaurant and [[wikt:rescue|rescues]] the [[manager]], but his mustache burns off. The manager gives him a big [[supply]] of burgers. Peter is sad that his mustache burned off, so he eats many burgers to feel better. He gets a stroke that [[paralyze]]s [[wikt:half|half]] of his body. He gets [[cure]]d at a place for [[stem cell]] [[research]]. He ties to [[lawsuit|sue]] the restaurant, but he loses at a [[trial]] because he does not have any [[evidence (law)|evidence]].  
 
 Peter and [[Brian Griffin|Brian]] go to the restaurant's [[headquarters]]. They take a [[wikt:tour|tour]] of the place to try to find evidence. They find a [[slaughterhouse]] with a talking cow in it. The cow talks about how the restaurant has harmed many other cows. Peter and Brian help the cow leave the headquarters while [[security guard]]s run after them. The cow makes a [[public speaking|public speech]] that makes the restaurant look bad. 
 
 [[Stewie Griffin|Stewie]] makes a [[bet]] to Brian that he will be the most [[popularity|popular]] [[kid]] in a [[high school]]. He gets a [[teenage]] girlfriend and wins the bet. He tries to have [[sexual intercourse]] with his girlfriend, but she [[laugh]]s at him for having a small [[human penis|penis]]. On the next day, Stewie gets [[bullied]] for his small penis. He gets his girlfriend [[arrest]]ed by telling people she is a [[pedophile]].
