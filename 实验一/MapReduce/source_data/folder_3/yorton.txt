 '''Yorton''' is a small [[village]] in [[Shropshire]], [[England]]. It is in the [[civil parish]] of [[Clive, Shropshire|Clive]]. 
 
 It is 7.01 miles (11.28 km) north of [[Shrewsbury]] and 3.31 miles (5.33 km) south of [[Wem]]. 
 
 The village is known for its [[Yorton railway station|railway station]]. It is on the [[Welsh Marches line]] between [[Newport railway station (Wales)|Newport]] and [[Crewe railway station|Crewe]].
