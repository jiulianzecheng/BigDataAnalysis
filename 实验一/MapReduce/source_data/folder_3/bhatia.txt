 ==Distribution== 
 They are settled in [[Azad Kashmir]], Pakistani Punjab, [[Sindh]], [[Islamabad]] and [[Balochistan]] Azad Kashmir parts of Pakistan. They're also inhabit in Himachal Pradesh, Rajasthan < ref name= " :2 "  / >  Jammu and Kashmir, [[Haryana]], [[Dehli]] and [[Punjab, India|Indian Punjab]]. < ref name= " :0 "  / >
