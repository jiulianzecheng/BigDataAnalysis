A '''hypertext''' is a text that is organized like a [[network]]. Parts of the text reference other text messages or texts which can directly be accessed. The parts that provide the reference are known as ''hyperlinks''. An example of a hypertext is the [[HTML]] standard used in the [[World Wide Web]]. [[Wiki]] < nowiki/ > s also use hypertext.
