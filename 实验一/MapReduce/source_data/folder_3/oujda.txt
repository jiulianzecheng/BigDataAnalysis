 '''Oujda''' ({{lang-ar|وجدة}}; {{lang-ber|ⵡⵓⵊⴷⴰ|Wujda}}) is a major [[Morocco|Moroccan]] city near the border with [[Algeria]]. Oujda is the capital city of the Oriental region of northeastern Morocco and has a population of about 558,000 people living there.
