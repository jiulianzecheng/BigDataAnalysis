 The '''Nive''' ({{lang-eu|Errobi}}, {{lang-oc|Niva}}) is a [[river]] in [[France]]. It flows through the [[Pyrénées-Atlantiques]] department, [[Nouvelle-Aquitaine]] [[Regions of France|region]]. The river starts in the [[Pyrenees]]. It is a left [[tributary]] of the [[Adour]] river. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 The Nive river has a length of {{Convert|79.3|km|mi|1|abbr=off}}, < ref name= " sandre " > {{cite web |url=http://sandre.eaufrance.fr/app/chainage/courdo/htm/Q9--0250.php?cg=Q9--0250 |title=La Nive (Q9--0250) |publisher=SANDRE - Portail national d'accès aux référentiels sur l'eau |language=French |accessdate=31 March 2014 |date= }} < /ref >  and a [[drainage basin]] with an area of approximately {{Convert|1030|km2|sqmi|0|abbr=off}}. 
 
 Its average yearly [[Discharge (hydrology)|discharge]] ([[volume]] of [[water]] which passes through a section of the river per [[Unit of measurement|unit]] of [[time]]) is {{Convert|30.00|m3/s|cuft/s|abbr=off}} at [[Cambo-les-Bains]] in the [[Pyrénées-Atlantiques]] department. < ref name= " hydro " > {{cite web |url=http://www.hydro.eaufrance.fr/stations/Q9312510 & procedure=synthese |title=La Nive à Cambo-les-Bains |publisher=Banque Hydro |language=French |accessdate=8 June 2017 |date= |archive-date=3 March 2016 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160303202319/http://www.hydro.eaufrance.fr/stations/Q9312510%26procedure%3Dsynthese |url-status=dead }} < /ref >

 ===Course=== 
 The Nive proper is formed from three small rivers in [[Saint-Jean-Pied-de-Port]]: 
 * The Nive de Béhérobie (main stream) 
 * The Laurhibar - {{convert|28.1|km|abbr=off}} < ref name= " laurhi " > {{cite web |url=http://services.sandre.eaufrance.fr/Courdo/Fiche/client/fiche_courdo.php?CdSandre=Q9030500 |title=Le Laurhibar (Q9030500) |publisher=SANDRE - Portail national d'accès aux référentiels sur l'eau |language=French |accessdate=31 March 2014 |date= }} < /ref > 
 * The Nive d'Arnéguy - {{convert|20.8|km|abbr=off}} < ref name= " Arneguy " > {{cite web |url=http://services.sandre.eaufrance.fr/Courdo/Fiche/client/fiche_courdo.php?CdSandre=Q90-0400 |title=La Nive d'Arnéguy (Q90-0400) |publisher=SANDRE - Portail national d'accès aux référentiels sur l'eau |language=French |accessdate=31 March 2014 |date= }} < /ref > 
 
 The Nive passes through the towns of [[Estérençuby]] (Nive de Béhérobie), [[Saint-Jean-Pied-de-Port]], [[Bidarray]], [[Cambo-les-Bains]], [[Ustaritz]], [[Villefranque, Pyrénées-Atlantiques|Villefranque]] and [[Bayonne]], where it flows into the Adour. 
 
 It joins the Adour in the city of Bayonne, on its left bank, at an [[altitude]] of {{Convert|3|m|ft|1|abbr=off}} and at about {{Convert|10|km|mi|1|abbr=off}} from the [[Bay of Biscay]] ([[Atlantic Ocean]]).
