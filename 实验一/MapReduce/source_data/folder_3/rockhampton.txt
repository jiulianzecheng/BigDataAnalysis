 '''Rockhampton''' is a city in [[Queensland, Australia]]. In June 2018, 78,592 people lived there. It is built on the banks of the [[Fitzroy River]] about 30 & nbsp;km from the coast. It is called the  " Beef Capital of Australia. " < ref > {{Cite web
