 '''KolibriOS''' is a [[free software|free]] and [[open source]] [[operating system]] written all in [[assembly]]. This makes it very fast and small. KolibriOS can fit on a [[floppy disk]] and run on a [[Pentium]] (and possibly on late [[Intel 80486|486]]) with 8 MB of [[RAM]].
