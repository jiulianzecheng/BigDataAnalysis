 '''Boarnsterhim''' ({{lang-nl|Boornsterhem}}) is a former [[List of municipalities of the Netherlands|municipality]] in the [[Netherlands|Dutch]] [[Provinces of the Netherlands|province]] of [[Friesland]]. On 1 January 2014, the municipality was dissolved and its territory was split between four other municipalities: [[De Fryske Marren]], which was created that day, [[Leeuwarden]], [[Heerenveen]] and [[Súdwest-Fryslân]].
