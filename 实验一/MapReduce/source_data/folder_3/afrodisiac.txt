 '''''Afrodisiac''''' is the fourth studio album by [[United States|American]] [[Contemporary R & B|R & B]]/[[pop music|pop]] singer [[Brandy Norwood]], released by [[Atlantic Records]] on [[June 28]], [[2004]] outside North America and on [[June 29]], [[2004]] (see [[2004 in music]]) in [[Canada]] and the [[United States]]. It was mostly [[producer|produced]] by [[Timbaland]], with more production from Warryn Campbell, [[Organized Noise]], Walter Millsap, Ron Feemster, Big Chuck, and [[Kanye West]]. A lot of [[critic]]s liked the album, but it did not sell many copies. It has sold fewer copies than any other album she has made so far.
