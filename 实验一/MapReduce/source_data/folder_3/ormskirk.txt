'''Ormskirk''' is a market town in west [[Lancashire]], England. It is 13 miles (21 km) north of [[Liverpool]], 11 miles (18 km) northwest of [[St Helens, Merseyside|St Helens]], 9 miles (14 km) southeast of [[Southport]] and 18 miles (29 km) southwest of [[Preston]]. The name is [[Old Norse]] in origin 
 
 Ormskirk is known for its [[gingerbread]].
