 {{nihongo|'''Riho'''|里歩|Riho|born June 4, 1997}} is an Japanese [[Professional wrestling|professional wrestler]]. She currently wrestles for [[All Elite Wrestling]] (AEW). 
 
 Riho has also competed for [[Ice Ribbon]], [[DDT Pro-Wrestling]], [[Union Pro Wrestling]], [[Gatoh Move Pro Wrestling]], and [[World Wonder Ring Stardom|Stardom]]. 
 
 During her time in AEW, Riho has been a one-time [[AEW Women's World Championship|AEW Women's World Champion]] and was the very first champion.
