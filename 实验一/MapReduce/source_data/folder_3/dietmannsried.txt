 '''Dietmannsried''' is a [[municipality]] in the district of [[Oberallgäu]] in [[Bavaria]] in [[Germany]]. It is located {{convert|20|km}} south of [[Memmingen|Memmingem]], and {{convert|15|km}} north of [[Kempten (Allgäu)|Kempten]]. From 1988 to 2008 Dietmannsried grew by 1729 people. Since 2014 the mayor has been Werner Andres.
