 '''Leporidae''' is a [[mammal]] family in the order [[Lagomorpha]]. It has about 60 species of [[rabbit]]s and [[hare]]s. The animals of the family Leporidae are called '''Leporids'''. The names  " rabbit "  and  " hare "  are not names of separate biological groups, but are  " randomly "  given to different leporid species.
