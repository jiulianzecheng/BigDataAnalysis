 '''PASOK''', the '''Panhellenic socialist movement''', is a [[Social democracy|social democratic]] [[political party]] in [[Greece]]. PASOK stands for '''Panellinio Sosialistiko Kinima''' ({{lang-el|Πανελλήνιο Σοσιαλιστικό Κίνημα}}). It was started by [[Georgios Papandreou]]. Until 2015, it was one of two major political parties, the other was its main political rival, [[New Democracy (Greece)]]. In 2019, it joined forces with other similar parties, and formed [[Kinima Allagis]].
