 A '''curtain''' is a piece of [[cloth]] used to block [[light]], water, or cold air from entering a place (some curtains are better at blocking than others because of the type of [[fabric]] used). < ref name= " freedictionary-curtain " > {{cite web| url= http://www.thefreedictionary.com/curtain|title=Curtain |publisher=The Free Dictionary By Farlex|access-date=23 May 2012}} < /ref > < ref name= " britannica-curtain " > {{cite web| url= http://www.britannica.com/EBchecked/topic/147125/curtain/|title=Curtain|publisher=Encyclopædia Britannica|access-date=5 May 2012}} < /ref > < ref > {{cite book|title=Window Dressings: Beautiful Draperies  &  Curtains for the Home|author=Brian D. Coleman|year=2006|isbn=1-58685-816-5|url-access=registration|url=https://archive.org/details/windowdressingsb0000cole}} < /ref > < ref name= " Rèm cửa " > {{cite web |last1=Những điều cần biết khi lần đầu chọn vải may |title=Rèm cửa |url=https://nganhangrem.com/ |website=nganhangrem.com |access-date=3 June 2023|language=vi}} < /ref >  Curtains are hung around [[shower]]s to keep [[water]] off the floor around the shower.  Curtains are also hung around [[window]]s to keep light from coming in. A curtain is raised and lowered during a play or other theatrical production. Curtains can come in many different [[color]]s and different types of fabric. It is also originally a [[French language|French]] word. 
 
 Curtains can also be made from [[air door|air]].
