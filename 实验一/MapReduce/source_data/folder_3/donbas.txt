 The '''Donbas''' or '''Donbass''' ({{IPAc-en|UK|d|ɒ|n|ˈ|b|ɑː|s}}, < ref > {{Cite Oxford Dictionaries|Donbas|access-date=6 September 2019}} < /ref >  {{IPAc-en|US|ˈ|d|ɑː|n|b|ɑː|s|,_|d|V|n|ˈ|b|æ|s}}; < ref > {{Cite American Heritage Dictionary|Donbas|access-date=6 September 2019}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite Merriam-Webster|Donbas|access-date=2021-12-26}} < /ref >  {{lang-uk|[[wikt:Донбас|Донба́с]]}} {{IPA-uk|donˈbɑs|}}; < ref name= " Donets-Basin " > {{cite encyclopedia|url=https://www.britannica.com/place/Donets-Basin|title=Donets Basin – region, Europe|encyclopedia=Encyclopaedia Britannica}} < /ref >  {{lang-ru|[[wikt:Донбасс|Донба́сс]]}}) is a historical, cultural, and economic [[region]] between [[Russia]] and [[Ukraine]], who are fighting each other over who owns the region in the [[Russo-Ukrainian War]]. It includes the [[Donetsk Oblast]] and [[Luhansk Oblast]].
