'''(65803) Didymos I Dimorphos''' ([[provisional designation]] '''S/2003 (65803) 1''') is a [[minor-planet moon]] of the [[near-Earth object|near-Earth asteroid]] [[65803 Didymos]]. It has a diameter of {{convert|170|m|ft}} and has been called a low-density [[rubble pile]]. It was discovered in 2003 at the [[Ond≈ôejov Observatory]].
