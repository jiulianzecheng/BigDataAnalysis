 '''Overbetuwe''' is a [[List of municipalities of the Netherlands|municipality]] found in the [[Netherlands|Dutch]] [[Provinces of the Netherlands|province]] of [[Gelderland]]. In 2019, 47,543 people lived there. 
 
 It was created on 1 January 2001 from the former municipalities of Elst, Heteren and Valburg.
