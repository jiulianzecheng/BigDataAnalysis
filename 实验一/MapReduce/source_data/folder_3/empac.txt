 The '''Curtis R. Priem Experimental Media and Performing Arts Center''' ('''EMPAC''') is a big concert hall and place for arts at [[Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute]] in [[Troy, New York|Troy]], [[New York State|New York]]. It opened on October 3, 2008. It has a lot of high tech things in it.
