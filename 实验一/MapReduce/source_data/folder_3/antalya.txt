 '''Antalya''' is a [[city]] in southern [[Turkey]]. The city is in the [[Mediterranean Region, Turkey|Mediterranean Region]] and along the coast of Mediterranean Sea. 2.158.265 people live there. Antalya is the most important [[tourism]] destination in Turkey and one of the best in Mediterranean. The city created a good image and its name started to be known to tourists. Every year mostly British, [[Russia]]n, and [[Germany|German]] tourists come to city for beautiful [[beach]]es and the [[Ancient history|ancient]] destinations.
