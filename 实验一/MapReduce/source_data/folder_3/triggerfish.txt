'''Triggerfish''' are about 40 species of often brightly colored [[fish]] of the [[Family (biology)|family]] '''Balistidae'''. Triggerfish are native to [[Tropics|tropical]] waters. An prehistoric [[pufferfish]] called ''[[Zignoichthys]]'' bore a resemblance to them.
