'''TACV''' is the [[flag carrier]] of [[Cape Verde]] in Africa. It was founded in [[1958]]. < ref > {{Cite web |url=http://www.suvarnabhumiairport.com/en/262-airlines-information?code=TCV |title=Airlines Information - Suvarnabhumi Airport Thailand Official Website - Bangkok Flights Bangkok Airport Suvarnabhumi Airport (BKK) |access-date=2016-05-07 |archive-date=2014-08-13 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140813224157/http://suvarnabhumiairport.com/en/262-airlines-information?code=TCV |url-status=dead }} < /ref >  The government of Cape Verde owns 100% of TACV. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://macauhub.com.mo/2016/03/10/president-of-cabo-verde-airline-tacv-confirms-redundancies/|title=President of Cabo Verde airline TACV confirms redundancies|date=10 March 2016|publisher=|access-date=15 February 2019|archive-date=5 December 2021|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20211205080324/https://macauhub.com.mo/2016/03/10/president-of-cabo-verde-airline-tacv-confirms-redundancies/|url-status=dead}} < /ref >  [[Sal International Airport]] is TACV's largest [[Airline hub|hub]]. [[Praia International Airport]] and [[Cesária Évora Airport]], both in Cape Verde, are also hubs for the airline. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.revolvy.com/page/Cabo-Verde-Airlines?smv=26774782|title= " Cabo Verde Airlines "  on Revolvy.com|first=Revolvy|last=LLC|website=www.revolvy.com}} < /ref >  TACV currently goes to several cities across Cape Verde, the United States, Brazil, Africa and Europe.  < ref > {{Cite web |url=https://flytacv.com/nossos-destinos/descubra-cabo-verde/?lang=en |title=Discover Cape Verde  & #124; TACV Cabo Verde Airlines |access-date=2016-05-07 |archive-date=2016-03-24 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160324203302/https://flytacv.com/nossos-destinos/descubra-cabo-verde/?lang=en |url-status=dead }} < /ref >
