 The '''Didache''' (pronounced  " ''did'' ah kay " ) or '''The Teaching of the Twelve Apostles''' is an [[Early Christianity|early Christian]] hand [[book]]. < ref name=bbc  > {{cite web|url=http://www.bbc.co.uk/religion/religions/christianity/texts/didache.shtml|title=Didache|publisher=BBC|access-date=2012-07-21}} < /ref >  It was written in the first or early second century AD. 
 It gives information about Christian [[ethics]], [[ritual]]s such as [[baptism]] and [[Eucharist]], and Church organization. < ref name=bbc / >
