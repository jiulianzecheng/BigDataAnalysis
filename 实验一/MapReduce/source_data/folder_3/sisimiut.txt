 '''Sisimiut''' (also known by its [[Danish_language|Danish]] name 'Holsteinsborg') is a town in southwestern [[Greenland]]. With 5,247 people (as of [[2005]]), it is the second biggest town in Greenland. It is 75 kilometers north of the [[Arctic Circle]], and is Greenland's most northern year-round port which has no ice.
