 The '''Oreo''' is a [[cookie]] [[sandwich]] or cream [[biscuit]]. It is made by the Nabisco division of Mondelēz International. The cookie has a sweet, white filling of  " creme "  or  " [[cream]] " . The creme is in between two circle-shaped chocolate or golden cookie pieces. 
 
 Oreos come in many different flavours like [[chocolate]], < ref > {{Cite web|access-date=2022-02-22|title=OREO Chocolate Creme Sandwich Cookies|url=https://www.oreo.com/oreo-chocolate-cookies|website=www.oreo.com}} < /ref >  [[vanilla]], < ref > {{Cite web|title=Oreo Golden Oreo Review|url=https://www.shespeaks.com/Nabisco-Golden-Oreo-Review|access-date=2022-03-19|website=SheSpeaks|language=en}} < /ref >  red velvet, < ref > {{Cite web|last=Salaky|first=Kristin|date=2020-02-21|title=Red Velvet Oreos Are Being Discontinued And It's OK To Cry|url=https://www.delish.com/food-news/a31046467/red-velvet-oreos-discontinued-2020/|access-date=2022-03-19|website=Delish|language=en-US}} < /ref >  caramel coconut, < ref > {{Cite web|access-date=2022-02-22|title=OREO Caramel Coconut Flavored Creme Chocolate Sandwich Cookies|url=https://www.oreo.com/oreo-carmel-coconut-cookies|website=www.oreo.com|archive-date=2022-02-22|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20220222163408/https://www.oreo.com/oreo-carmel-coconut-cookies|url-status=dead}} < /ref >  chocolate [[marshmallow]] < ref > {{Cite web|access-date=2022-02-22|title=REO Chocolate Marshmallow Sandwich Cookies|url=https://www.oreo.com/oreo-chocolate-marshmellow-cookies|website=www.oreo.com|archive-date=2022-02-22|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20220222163406/https://www.oreo.com/oreo-chocolate-marshmellow-cookies|url-status=dead}} < /ref >  and many more. < ref > {{Cite web|title=OREO Flavors {{!}} OREO Cookies|url=https://www.oreo.com/oreo-cookies/oreo-flavors|access-date=2021-07-07|website=www.oreo.com}} < /ref >
