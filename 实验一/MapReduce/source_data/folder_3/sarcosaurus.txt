 '''''Sarcosaurus''''' (meaning  " flesh lizard " ) is a [[genus]] of basal [[theropod]] [[dinosaur]]. ''Sarcosaurus'' is one of the earliest known [[Jurassic]] [[theropods]]: it is one of only a few from this time.
