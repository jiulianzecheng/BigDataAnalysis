 '''Retzstadt''' is a community in the [[Main-Spessart|Main-Spessart district]] in the ''[[Regierungsbezirk]]'' of [[Lower Franconia]] (''Unterfranken'') in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]] and a member of the ''Verwaltungsgemeinschaft'' (Administrative Community) of Zellingen. 
 
 == Geography == 
 
 === Location === 
 Retzstadt is in the Main-Spessart district in a side-valley of the [[Main (river)|Main]], on the river Retz. 
   
 The community has only the ''Gemarkung'' (traditional rural cadastral area) of Retzstadt.
