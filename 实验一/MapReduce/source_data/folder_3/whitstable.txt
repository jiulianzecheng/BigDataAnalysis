 '''Whiststable''' is a small seaside town in [[Kent]], southeast England. It is a few miles north of [[Canterbury]], near [[Herne Bay]]. The town is famous for its [[oyster]]s which have been collected there since the [[Ancient Rome|Roman]] period.
