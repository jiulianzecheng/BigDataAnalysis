 The '''Noppenspitze''' is a rocky mountain in the [[Allgäu Alps]]. It is {{convert|2,594|m}} high and made of [[Dolomite]]. It is in the central part of the [[Hornbach chain]]. The Noppenspitze was [[first climbed]] on 16 August 1890 by  Antony Spiehler and his guide, Johann Schiffer. The Noppenspitze is not climbed very much. According to the summit book in 2006, it was only being climbed about 5 - 15 times per year.
