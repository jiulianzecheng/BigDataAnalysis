 '''Sophie''' is a version of the female given name [[Sophia (given name)|Sophia]], meaning  " [[wisdom]] " .  < ref > {{Cite web|title=Sophie name Meaning and Origin|url=https://babys.name/detail-Sophie-Meaning-Origin-Famous-Similar-Sibling.html|access-date=2023-03-26|website=babys.name}} < /ref > Sophie is a name of Greek origin, derived from the word “sophia” which means “wisdom”. It is a popular name for girls, and is often seen as a symbol of intelligence and knowledge. The name is also associated with the goddess of wisdom, Sophia. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Sophie name Meaning and Origin|url=https://babys.name/detail-Sophie-Meaning-Origin-Famous-Similar-Sibling.html|access-date=2023-03-08|website=babys.name}} < /ref >  The pronunciation of the name is [ '''soh'''-fee ]. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Definition of Sophie {{!}} Dictionary.com|url=https://www.dictionary.com/browse/sophie|access-date=2023-03-26|website=www.dictionary.com|language=en}} < /ref >  This name was ranked #76 on the US Popular Names in 2021.  < ref > {{Cite web|last=OACT|title=Popular Baby Names|url=https://www.ssa.gov/cgi-bin/popularnames.cgi|access-date=2023-03-26|website=www.ssa.gov}} < /ref >
