 '''Palawan''' ({{lang-tl|Lalawigan ng Palawan}}) is a [[province]] of the [[Philippines]].  It is in the western [[MIMAROPA]] [[region]]. < ref > Philippine 2010 Census, [https://www.census.gov.ph/sites/default/files/attachments/hsd/pressrelease/Population%20and%20Annual%20Growth%20Rates%20for%20The%20Philippines%20and%20Its%20Regions%2C%20Provinces%2C%20and%20Highly%20Urbanized%20Cities%20Based%20on%201990%2C%202000%2C%20and%202010%20Censuses.pdf   " Population and Annual Growth Rates for The Philippines and Its Regions, Provinces, and Highly Urbanized Cities, "  p. 2] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130928012059/http://www.census.gov.ph/sites/default/files/attachments/hsd/pressrelease/Population%20and%20Annual%20Growth%20Rates%20for%20The%20Philippines%20and%20Its%20Regions%2C%20Provinces%2C%20and%20Highly%20Urbanized%20Cities%20Based%20on%201990%2C%202000%2C%20and%202010%20Censuses.pdf |date=2013-09-28 }}; retrieved 2013-4-23. < /ref >
