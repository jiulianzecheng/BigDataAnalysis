 '''Winklarn''' is a market town and [[Municipalities of Germany|municipality]] in [[Schwandorf (district)|Schwandorf]] in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. Winklarn is in the [[Upper Palatinate]]. In the North of Winklarn, there is a large forest, the Frauenstein.
