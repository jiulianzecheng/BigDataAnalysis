 '''Peiting''' is a [[Municipalities of Germany|municipality]]  in [[Weilheim-Schongau]] in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. It is on the right edge of the [[Lech (river)|Lech]], 3 & nbsp;km southeast of [[Schongau, Bavaria|Schongau]], and 17 & nbsp;km west of [[Weilheim in Oberbayern]].
