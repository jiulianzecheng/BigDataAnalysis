 '''Mukeshpuri''' is the highest [[mountain]] in the Nathiagali Hills. It is {{convert|2800|m}} high. It is in the [[Abbottabad District]], [[Pakistan]]. [[Islamabad]] is {{convert|90|km}} south of Mukeshpuri. 
 
 Mukeshpuri is a [[Sanskrit]] word. It means  " place of quietness " . The [[Jhelum River]] can be seen from the top of the mountain.
