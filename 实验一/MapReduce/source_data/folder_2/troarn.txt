 '''Troarn''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is found in the [[Regions of France|region]] [[Basse-Normandie]] in the [[Calvados (department)|Calvados]] [[Departments of France|department]] in the northwest of [[France]]. On 1 January 2017, it was merged into the new commune of [[Saline, Calvados|Saline]], < ref > [https://www.legifrance.gouv.fr/eli/arrete/2016/7/29/INTB1623401A/jo/texte Arrêté préfectoral] 29 July 2016 {{in lang|fr}} < /ref >  but this merger was undone on 31 December 2019. < ref > {{cite news|url=https://www.ouest-france.fr/normandie/troarn-14670/calvados-1er-janvier-2020-saline-n-existe-plus-rebonjour-troarn-et-sannerville-6674921|title=Calvados. 1er janvier 2020, Saline n'existe plus, rebonjour Troarn et Sannerville|date=2020-01-01|work=[[Ouest-France]]|lang=fr}} < /ref >
