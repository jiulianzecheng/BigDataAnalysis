An '''autobiography''' is a [[biography]] in which the [[author]] writes about his or her own life. It is a self-written account of one's own life. 
 
 It is one of the earliest form of [[literature]], but the word itself is quite modern. < ref name=Pascal > {{cite book|last = Pascal| first = Roy|title = Design and truth in autobiography |url = https://archive.org/details/designtruthinaut0000pasc|publisher = Harvard University Press | year = 1960 | location = [[Cambridge, Massachusetts|Cambridge]] }} < /ref >
