'''Risperidone''' (which is also marketed as '''Risperdal''') refers to an extremely potent [[antipsychotic]] drug that is used primarily to treat [[schizophrenia]], [[autism spectrum]] matters and [[bipolar disorder]]. Side effects of Risperidone include although are not limited to unwanted [[weight]] gain and metabolic troubles like high [[blood sugar]]. It was first approved in 1994.
