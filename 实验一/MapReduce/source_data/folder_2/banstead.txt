'''Banstead''' is a village in the borough of [[Reigate and Banstead]] in [[Surrey]], [[England]].  
 It has been there since 967 AD. It has a high street with lots of [[coffee shop]]s, such as Cafe Nero, Costa, and Caffe Italia. 
 Towns close by include [[Reigate]], [[Sutton, London|Sutton]], [[Epsom]] and Belmont.
