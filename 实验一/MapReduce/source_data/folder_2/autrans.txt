 '''Autrans''' is a [[village]] near [[Grenoble]]. About 1,541 [[people]] live there. It is a well-known [[skiing|ski]] resort. On 1 January 2016, it became a part of the new commune of [[Autrans-Méaudre-en-Vercors]]. < ref > [http://www.isere.gouv.fr/content/download/24567/192134/file/2015-N72-REC.pdf Arrêté préfectoral] 20 November 2015 < /ref >
