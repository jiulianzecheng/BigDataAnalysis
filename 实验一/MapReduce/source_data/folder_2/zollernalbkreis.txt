 The '''Zollernalbkreis''' is a [[Districts of Germany|''Landkreis'' (district)]] in the middle of [[Baden-Württemberg]], [[Germany]]. 
 
 The district is located in the [[Swabian Alb]]. It has the second highest elevation of this range, the {{convert|1011|m|ft|adj=on}} high ''[[Oberhohenberg]]''.
