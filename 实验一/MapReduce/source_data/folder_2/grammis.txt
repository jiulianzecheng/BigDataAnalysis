 The '''Grammis''' are music awards given out every year in [[Sweden]]. They are the oldest Swedish music awards. < ref > {{Cite web|last=Nyheter|first=S. V. T.|date=2017-02-28|title=Vad betyder en Grammis?|url=https://www.svt.se/kultur/musik/vad-betyder-en-grammis|access-date=2022-11-07|website=SVT Nyheter|language=sv}} < /ref >  They were made as a Swedish version of the [[Grammy Award|Grammy Awards]]. The Grammis started being given out in 1969. They were cancelled in 1972, but came back in 1987. 
 
 There are about 20 kinds of awards given out for different types of music. Swedish [[Musician|musicians]] and musicians who live in Sweden can get an award. Musicians from other countires can also get awards if they make their music in Sweden, and the music is for Swedish people.
