 '''''Doug''''' is an [[Animation|animated]] [[television program]]. The program was created by [[Jim Jinkins]]. It was [[Producer|produced]] by [[Nelvana]]. The program is about the early [[Adolescence|adolescent]] life of its title [[Fictional character|character]], Douglas  " Doug "  Funnie.  Doug, was 11-12 years old in the first 4 [[Season (television)|seasons]] of the series. He was 13 years old in seasons 5 to 7. 
 
 
 ''Doug'' was broadcast on [[Nickelodeon]] in from 1991 until 1994. The series was on [[American Broadcasting Company|ABC]] ([[The Walt Disney Company|Disney]])  from 1996 until 1999. 
 
 == Characters == 
 
 * Douglas Yancey  " Doug "  Funnie was voiced by [[Billy West]] in the Nickelodeon series and Tom McHugh in the Disney series.  Doug is a shy 11 (later 12)-year-old boy. Most of the time, he is dealing with his fear of failure. He is good at writing, drawing, making [[music]] and caring for [[Animal|animals]]. Doug [[Narrator|narrates]] every [[episode]]. He also writes his experiences in his [[Diary|journal]]. He has an [[alter ego]], Quailman.
