 '''Hemavan''' or '''Bierke''' is a [[urban areas of Sweden|locality]] in [[Storuman Municipality]] in [[Västerbotten County]] in [[Sweden]]. In 2010, 222 people lived there. < ref name=scb / > 
 
 It is located on [[European route E12]] between [[Storuman]] in Sweden and [[Mo i Rana]] in [[Norway]]. Hemavan is also known as a ski. The [[Hemavan Airport]], is also here.
