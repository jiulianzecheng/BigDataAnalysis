 '''Punkalaidun''' is a municipality in [[Pirkanmaa]], [[Finland]]. As of 30 September 2023, 2,646 people lived there. Neighbouring municipalities are [[Huittinen]], [[Humppila]], [[Loimaa]], [[Urjala]] and [[Sastamala]].
