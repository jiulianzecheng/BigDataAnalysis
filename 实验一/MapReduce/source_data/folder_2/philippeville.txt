 '''Philippeville''' is a [[municipality]] in the [[Belgium|Belgian]] province of [[Namur (province)|Namur]], 28 kilometres south of Charleroi. 
 
 In 2007, 8482 people lived there. < ref > {{Cite web |url=http://statbel.fgov.be/downloads/pop200701com.xls |title=Werkelijke bevolking per gemeente op 1 januari 2007 / Population de droit par commune au 1 janvier 2007 (.xls) |access-date=2008-07-15 |archive-date=2008-11-23 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20081123144002/http://www.statbel.fgov.be/downloads/pop200701com.xls |url-status=dead }} < /ref > 
 
 It is at 50° 11 North, 04° 32 East. < ref > {{Cite web |url=http://statbel.fgov.be/pub/d1/p120_nl.xls |title=Geografische coördinaten van de gemeenten (.xls) |access-date=2008-07-15 |archive-date=2008-11-24 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20081124205332/http://www.statbel.fgov.be/pub/d1/p120_nl.xls |url-status=dead }} < /ref >
