 '''''Adweek''''' is an American [[advertising]] [[trade magazine]] that started in 1979. < ref > {{Cite news|url=https://www.investopedia.com/terms/a/adweek.asp|title=Adweek|first=Will|last=Kenton|date=January 25, 2011|work=Investopedia|access-date=July 9, 2018}} < /ref >   
 
 ==History== 
 In 1990, Affiliated Publications Inc., which publishes ''[[The Boston Globe]]'', agreed to buy 80 percent of A/S/M Communications Inc., which published ''Adweek''. < ref name= " ap90 " > " [https://archive.today/20130130214352/http://query.nytimes.com/gst/fullpage.html?res=9C0CEFDB1E31F936A35755C0A966958260 ''Adweek'' Parent Acquisition Set] "  June 5, 1990, ''[[The New York Times]]'' < /ref > < ref name= " elliott98 " > Elliott, Stuart,  " [https://www.nytimes.com/1998/11/10/business/media-business-advertising-adweek-magazine-commemorating-its-20th-anniversary.html ''Adweek'' magazine is commemorating its 20th anniversary with a record 232-page special issue] " , ''[[The New York Times]]'', 10 November 1998. < /ref > 
 
 On June 2, 2020, investment firm Shamrock Capital bought ''Adweek'' < ref > {{Cite web|date=June 2, 2020|title=Adweek Acquired by Shamrock Capital|url=https://www.adweek.com/brand-marketing/adweek-acquired-by-shamrock-capital/|access-date=2020-07-24|website=adweek.com|language=en-US}} < /ref >  from a private equity firm Beringer Capital, which had bought Adweek in July 2016. < ref name= " tc-sold " > {{cite web|url=https://techcrunch.com/2016/07/18/adweek-acquired-by-private-equity-firm-beringer-capital/|title=Adweek acquired by private equity firm Beringer Capital|last=Ha|first=Anthony|date=July 18, 2016|website=[[TechCrunch]]|access-date=19 July 2016}} < /ref > 
 
 On July 26, 2023, Will Lee was announced as the [[Chief executive officer|CEO]] of ''Adweek''. 
 
 On November 30, 2023, Zoë Ruderman was announced as Chief Content Officer and Drew Schutte as Chief Revenue Officer. < ref >  {{Cite web |date=November 30, 2023 |title= Adweek Builds New Executive Team, Naming Former People GM Zoë Ruderman Chief Content Officer and Former Condé Nast Executive Drew Schutte Chief Revenue Officer |url=https://www.adweek.com/media/adweek-zoe-ruderman-chief-content-officer-drew-schutte-chief-revenue-officer/|access-date=2023-12-08 |website=adweek.com|language=en-US}} < /ref >
