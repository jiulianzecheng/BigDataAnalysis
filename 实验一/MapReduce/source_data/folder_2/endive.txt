 '''Endive''' (''Chicorium endivia'') is a [[leaf]]y [[plant]] of the [[family (biology)|family]] [[Asteraceae]]. The plants are either annual, or they live for two years. Originally, these plants grew in the countries around the Mediterranean. There are many [[wikt:varieties|varieties]], but they can be split into two groups 
 
 * the curly-leaved. or narrow-leaved endive 
 * Batavian or broad-leaved endive 
 
 Since [[Antiquity]], these plants have been used to make [[salad]]s. Sometimes they are cooked, and prepared similar to spinach or [[chard]]. Endive has a good sorce of fiber and has all kinds of vitamins like a,c,and e.
