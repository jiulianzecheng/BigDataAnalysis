 == Role of a technologist == 
 A technologist may be known by different [[International Standard Classification of Occupations|job titles]] in various [[organization]]s. < ref name=lstk > {{cite web |url=http://learningspacetoolkit.org/technology/role-of-technologist/ |title=Role of Technologist |website=Learning Space Toolkit |publisher=North Carolina State University/Brightspot Strategy/AECOM Strategy Plus |access-date=15 October 2016}} < /ref >  For example, a Director of Information Technology is often a technologist. < ref name=lstk/ >  The same is true for a Technology Project Manager. < ref name=lstk/ >  Smaller [[organizations]] may use a technologist to perform many functions. In some cases, a project may be started using a staff member or technician, then handed off to a technologist as the project progresses. < ref name=lstk/ >  The technologist is a specialist in how technologies can be used to meet the [[wikt:goal|goal]]s of the project [[Ownership|owner]]. < ref name=lstk/ >  In [[Canada]], [[engineer]]s with training from other [[countries]] may find themselves working as an engineering technologist.
