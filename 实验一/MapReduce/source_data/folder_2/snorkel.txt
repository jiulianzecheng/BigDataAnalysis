A '''snorkel''' is a tool that human beings use to breathe in water.  A snorkel is a tube with two ends: One end goes in the swimmer's mouth.  The other reaches up into the air.  Then the swimmer, or snorkeler, can breathe. < ref name=Types > {{cite web|url=https://oceanscubadive.com/how-does-a-snorkel-work/|access-date=April 2, 2021|date=March 16, 2020|author=Kristy Wood|publisher=Ocean Scuba Dive|title=How Does a Snorkel Work?}} < /ref >
