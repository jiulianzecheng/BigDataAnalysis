Brownface is when a white or light-skinned person attempts to portray themselves as a brown person, or a person of colour. This is most commonly done to mimic people of the [[Middle East|Middle Eastern]], [[North Africa|North African]], [[Southeast Asia|Southeast Asian]], [[Melanesia|Melanesian]], [[Micronesia|Micronesian]], [[Polynesian people|Polynesian]], [[Hispanic]]/[[Latino]], [[Native American]] or other Indigenous, [[South Italy|Southern Italians]], [[Sicilians]], and/or [[South Asia|South Asian]] ethnic identity by using [[makeup]], [[Hair coloring|hair-dye]], and/or by wearing traditional ethnic clothing.
