 '''Milos''' or '''Melos''' is a volcanic [[Greece|Greek]] [[island]] in the [[Aegean Sea]], just north of the [[Sea of Crete]]. Milos is the southwesternmost island in the [[Cyclades]] group. Melos is one of the [[Aegean Islands]] where many ancient [[artifact]]s were found, including the [[Venus de Milo]]. This island covers {{convert|160.147|mi2|abbr=off}}.
