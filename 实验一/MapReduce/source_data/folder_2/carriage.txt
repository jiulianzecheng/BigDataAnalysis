 A '''carriage''' is a type of vehicle that was used before [[cars]] were invented. It is a fancy type of [[cart]] or [[wagon]], usually with four wheels and [[Suspension (vehicle)|suspension]].  The carriages may be pulled by [[horse]]s. < ref > Royal Household (UK), [http://www.royal.gov.uk/TheRoyalHousehold/Transport/Carriages.aspx  " Carriages " ] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110605034532/http://www.royal.gov.uk/TheRoyalHousehold/Transport/Carriages.aspx |date=2011-06-05 }}; retrieved 2012-7-2. < /ref >  Carriages are often used for weddings.
