 '''Austrobaileyales''' is the botanical name for an [[order (biology)|order]] of [[flowering plant]]s. There are about 100 species of woody [[plant]]s, perhaps the most famous of which is the spice [[star]] [[anise]], from ''Illicium verum''.  
 
 The [[Angiosperm Phylogeny Group|APG]] system, of 1998, did not recognize such an order. However, the APG II system, of 2003, did accept this order and placed it among the [[basal]] angiosperms. < ref >  Angiosperm Phylogeny Group 2009.
