 '''Schlans''' is a [[municipality]] of the [[district]] of [[Surselva (district)|Surselva]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Graubünden]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 The former municipality of Schlans merged on 1 January 2012 into the municipality of [[Trun, Switzerland|Trun]].
