'''Swallowing''' happens when [[food]] goes through the [[mouth]] of a [[person]] or [[animal]] into their [[throat]]. During swallowing, the food passes through a few [[Organ (anatomy)|organs]] called the [[pharynx]], [[esophagus]] and lastly the [[stomach]].
