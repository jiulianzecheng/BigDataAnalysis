 '''Hellebores''' are a type of flowering [[plant|plants]] in the [[Ranunculaceae]] family. Although some of these plants have names such as  " winter rose " , < ref name= " yates.co.nz " > {{cite web|url=http://www.yates.co.nz/flowers/grow/hellebores-roses-of-winter/|title=Hellebores' roses of winter - Yates|website=www.yates.co.nz}} < /ref > ,  " Christmas rose " , and  " Lenten rose " , they are not related to [[Rose|roses]]. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.britannica.com/EBchecked/topic/115734/Christmas-rose| title=Christmas Rose|access-date= 2014-06-18}} < /ref >  Some of these flowers can also be [[poison|poisonous]].
