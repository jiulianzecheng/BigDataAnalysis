 '''Kim Ji-soo''' ({{Korean|hangul=김지수}}; born January 3, 1995) is a [[South Korea]]n singer, actress, and model. She goes by the [[Mononymous person|mononym]] Jisoo. She is known for being a member of [[Blackpink]], which as of 2019 is the highest-charting female [[k-pop]] group.
