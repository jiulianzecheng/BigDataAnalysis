 '''Autavaux''' is a village of the district [[Broye (district)|Broye]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Fribourg (canton)|Fribourg]] in [[Switzerland]]. On 1 January 2006 the former municipalities of Autavaux, [[Forel, Fribourg|Forel]] and [[Montbrelloz]] merged to form [[Vernay, Switzerland|Vernay]]. < ref name=Fusion > [http://www.bfs.admin.ch/bfs/portal/de/index/infothek/nomenklaturen/blank/blank/gem_liste/03.html Amtliches Gemeindeverzeichnis der Schweiz] published by the Swiss Federal Statistical Office {{in lang|de}} accessed 19 July 2011 < /ref >
