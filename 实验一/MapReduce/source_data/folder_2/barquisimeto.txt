 '''Barquisimeto''' is a city in [[Venezuela]]. It is the capital of the [[Lara (state)|Lara]] State. It is the fourth largest city by population and area in Venezuela, after [[Caracas]], [[Maracaibo]] and [[Valencia, Venezuela|Valencia]].
