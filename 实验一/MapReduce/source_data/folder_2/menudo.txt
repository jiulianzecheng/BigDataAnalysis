 In Mexican cuisine, '''Menudo''', also known as '''pancita''' (''[little] gut'' or ''[little] stomach'') or '''mole de panza''' ( " stomach sauce " ), is a traditional [[Mexican cuisine|Mexican]] soup. It is made with cow's [[stomach]] ([[tripe]]) in [[broth]] with a red chili pepper base. [[Hominy]], lime, onions, and oregano are used to season the broth.
