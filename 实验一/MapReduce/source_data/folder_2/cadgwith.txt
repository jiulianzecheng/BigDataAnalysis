 '''Cadgwith''' is a [[village]] and [[fishing]] [[cove]] in [[Cornwall]], [[United Kingdom]]. The village is on [[The Lizard|The Lizard Peninsula]]. 
 
 Fishing is the main [[industry]] in the village. [[Seafood]], such as [[crab]]s, [[lobster]]s and many different types of [[fish]], are very important to the village.{{Commonscat}}
