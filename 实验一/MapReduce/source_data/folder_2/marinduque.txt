 '''Marinduque''' ({{IPA-tl|maɾinˈduke}}) is an [[island]] [[Provinces of the Philippines|province]] of the [[Philippines]]. It is in the [[MIMAROPA]] [[Regions of the Philippines|region]]. The capital is the municipality of [[Boac, Marinduque|Boac]].
