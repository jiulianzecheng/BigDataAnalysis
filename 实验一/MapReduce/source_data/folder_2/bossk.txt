'''Bossk''' was a [[male]] [[Trandoshan]] [[bounty hunter]] from ''[[Star Wars]]''. He first appeared in ''[[Star Wars Episode V: The Empire Strikes Back]]'' on [[Darth Vader]]'s flagship with other bounty hunters. Vader tasked the bounty hunters with finding the [[Millennium Falcon]] which [[Boba Fett]] ended up finding. Bossk is also seen in the background in ''[[Star Wars Episode VI: Return of the Jedi]]''. Like the rest of his species, Bossk has a hatred for [[Wookiee]]s and a personal hatred for [[Chewbacca]]. Bossk also appears in ''[[Star Wars: The Clone Wars (TV series)|Star Wars: The Clone Wars]]'' where he helps a young Boba Fett learn the ways of bounty hunting with other bounty hunters. Bossk has also appeared in many [[book]]s and [[comic]]s.
