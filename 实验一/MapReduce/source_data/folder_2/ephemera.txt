 '''Ephemera''' are temporary written or printed matters. It covers a lot of things: [[trade cards]], [[bookmarks]], [[Postcard|postcards,]] [[poster]]s, many variants of the [[advertising]] etc. 
 
 This term is originally used to [[mayfly]] or something transitory or short-lived. < ref > {{Cite journal|title=ephemera|url=https://www.thefreedictionary.com/ephemera|journal=The Free Dictionary}} < /ref >
