 '''Nanguneri''' is a [[panchayat town]] in the [[India]]n [[States and territories of India|state]] of [[Tamil Nadu]]. Nanguneri is in [[Tirunelveli district]], about {{convert|18|mi|km|0}} from the headquarters of the district. The place became important for its famous [[Vaishnavism|Vaishnavite]] temple, which is commonly known as Totadri Mutt. 
 
 Nanguneri has an average elevation of 141 & nbsp;metres (462 & nbsp;[[foot (length)|feet]]). As of the [[census]] of India, Nanguneri had a population of 6,764. About 49% of the population were men, and 51% were women. Nanguneri has an average literacy rate of 76%, higher than the national average of 59.5%. About 10% of the population is under 6 years of age.
