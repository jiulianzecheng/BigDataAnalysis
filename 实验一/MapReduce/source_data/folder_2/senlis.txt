 '''Senlis''' ({{IPA-fr|sɑ̃lis}}) is a [[Communes of France|commune]] in the northern French [[Departments of France|department]] of [[Oise]]. 
 
 The monarchs of the early French dynasties lived in Senlis. It is known for the [[Chantilly, Oise|Chantilly]] forest nearby. It is also known for its Gothic [[Senlis Cathedral|cathedral]] and other historical monuments.
