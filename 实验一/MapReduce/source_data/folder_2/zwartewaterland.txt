 '''Zwartewaterland''' is a municipality in the province of [[Overijssel]], the Netherlands. About 23,000 people were living there in 2021. It lies both in the region of [[Salland]] and the region of [[Kop van Overijssel]]. 
 
 It was created from the former municipalities of [[Genemuiden]], [[Hasselt, Netherlands|Hasselt]], and [[Zwartsluis]] on 1 January 2001.
