 '''Ngardmau''' is a [[Palau|Palauan]] state and is located in the northwest of the country's main island [[Babeldaob]]. Though it's Palau's sixth largest state by area it's the fourth least populous one. There are three settlements, [[Ngerutoi]], [[Ngetbong]] and the capital [[Urdmang]].
