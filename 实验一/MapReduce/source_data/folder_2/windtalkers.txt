 '''''Windtalkers''''' is an 2002 American [[war movie]] that was directed by [[John Woo]] and produced by Terence Chang, Tracie Graham-Rice and Alison Rosenzweig. It is based on the real story of [[Navajo]] [[code talker]]s during [[World War II]]. The movie was released on [[June 14]], [[2002]] in the [[United States]]. 
 
 It was a [[box office bomb]], which made $77.6 million worldwide at the [[box office]], compared to its [[budget]] of $115 million. 
 
 The movie also received negative [[movie review]]s with a 33% rating on [[Rotten Tomatoes]] and [[Roger Ebert]] gave the movie 2 stars out of 5.
