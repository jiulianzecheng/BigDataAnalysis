 ''' Cheranmahadevi''' is a [[panchayat town]] in the state of [[Tamil Nadu]], [[India]]. It is in the [[Tirunelveli district]], on the banks of the river Thamirabarani. The town has many [[temple]]s. In the 2001 [[census]] of India, Cheranmadevi had a population of 16,320. < ref > {{GR|India}} < /ref >  About 49% of the population were men, and 51% were women. Cheranmahadevi has an average literacy rate of 77%, higher than the national average of 59.5%. About 9% of the population is under 6 years of age.
