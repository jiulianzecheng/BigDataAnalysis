 '''Silverchair''' was a [[rock band]] from [[Newcastle, New South Wales|Newcastle]], [[Australia]]. The band's members were Daniel Johns, Chris Joannou and Ben Gillies, and were only around 12 and 13 when they founded the band. Silverchair is the only artist to score five number-one [[album]]s in the Australian Charts. Their studio albums are ''Frogstomp'', ''Freak Show'', ''Neon Ballroom'', ''Diorama'' and ''Young Modern''. The band has released many [[single (music)|singles]], their number-one singles being  " [[Tomorrow (Silverchair song)|Tomorrow]] " ,  " [[Freak]] "  and  " [[Straight Lines]] " . < ref > https://australian-charts.com/showinterpret.asp?interpret=Silverchair < /ref >
