 '''Guilder''' is the [[English language|English]] [[translation]] of the [[Dutch language|Dutch]] '''''gulden''''', meaning  " golden " . The guilder used to be a [[gold]] [[coin]], but has been a used for [[silver]] or [[metal]] coins for some [[century|centuries]]. The name is also called [[florin]].
