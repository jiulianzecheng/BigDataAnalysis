 '''Transhumanism''' is a [[cultural]] movement that thinks that humans could benefit from [[technology]] (mostly the [[biological]] type) that improves the condition of life. < ref name= " Mercer " > {{Cite book|title = Religion and Transhumanism: The Unknown Future of Human Enhancement|last = Mercer|first = Calvin|publisher = Praeger|isbn = |location = |pages = }} < /ref > < ref name= " Bostrom 2005 " > {{cite journal| last=Bostrom | first=Nick | authorlink = Nick Bostrom |title = A history of transhumanist thought|journal = [[Journal of Evolution and Technology]] |year = 2005 |url = http://www.nickbostrom.com/papers/history.pdf| accessdate=February 21, 2006}} < /ref >  It seeks to find a solution to [[aging]], [[disease]], and [[death]] (especially of the [[natural]] variety) by making use of such technology. The word was coined by [[Julian Huxley]] in 1957.
