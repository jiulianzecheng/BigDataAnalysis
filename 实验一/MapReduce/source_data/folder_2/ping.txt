'''Ping''' can have several meanings 
 *A [[tone]], usually of a high frequency 
 *A [[network]]ing tool that shows if two computers are connected, and how long a signal takes to go from one to the other 
 * [[Ping River]], a river in [[Thailand]] 
 *Ping, a manufacturer and brand name of [[golf]] equipment 
 
 ==People== 
 *[[Jean Ping]] is a politician from [[Gabon]] 
 *[[Lioe Tiong Ping]] is a badminton player from [[Indonesia]] 
 *[[Ping Ping]] is a singer from [[Suriname]]
