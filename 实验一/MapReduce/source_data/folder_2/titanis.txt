 '''''Titanis''''' was a  prehistoric flightless [[bird]]. Its name means 'giant'. ''Titanis'' was a [[genus]] of [[terror birds]] (the '''Phorusrhacidae'''). They were dominant land [[carnivore]]s in [[South America]] through much of the [[Cainozoic]].
