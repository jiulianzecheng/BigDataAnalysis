 '''Wilihof''' is a village and former [[municipality]] of the [[district]] of [[Sursee (Amt)|Sursee]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Lucerne (canton)|Lucerne]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 On 1 January 2005 the former municipalities of Wilihof and Kulmerau merged into the municipality of [[Triengen]].
