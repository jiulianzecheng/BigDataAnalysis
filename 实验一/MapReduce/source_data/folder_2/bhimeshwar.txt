'''Bhimeshwar''', (formerly ''Charikot''), is a municipality in north-eastern [[Nepal]] and the headquarters of [[Dolakha District]] in [[Bagmati Province]] that was established in 1997 by merging the former [[Village development committee (Nepal)|Village development committees]] Charikot, [[Dolakha Town, Nepal|Dolakha Town]], [[Makaibari]] and [[Mati, Nepal|Mati]]. < ref > {{cite web|title=भीमेश्वर नगरपालिका|url=http://www.bhimeshwor.gov.np/|language=Nepali|archiveurl=https://archive.today/20130222230216/http://www.bhimeshwor.gov.np/#selection-389.376-389.380|archivedate=22 February 2013}} < /ref >  At the time of the [[2011 Nepal census]] it had a population of 32,486 people living in 8,639 individual households. < ref > {{cite web|date=November 2017|title=National Population and Housing Census 2011|url=http://cbs.gov.np/image/data/2017/Population_Ward_Level_753_Local_Unit.pdf|url-status=dead|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180127085017/http://cbs.gov.np/image/data/2017/Population_Ward_Level_753_Local_Unit.pdf|archive-date=27 January 2018|publisher=Central Bureau of Statistics|accessdate=1 October 2018}} < /ref >
