 '''''Cnemidophorus''''' is a [[genus]] of [[lizard]]s which belong to the family of [[Teiidae]], which are commonly referred to as '''whiptail lizards''' or '''racerunners'''. Over 30% of the ''Cnemidophorus'' genus are [[parthenogenic]].
