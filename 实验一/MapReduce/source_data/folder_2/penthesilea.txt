'''Penthesilea''' ({{lang-el|Πενθεσίλεια}}, ''Penthesileia'') was a queen of the [[Amazons|Amazonian]] in [[Greek mythology]]. Her parents were [[Ares]] and [[Otrera]]. She was the sister of [[Hippolyta]], [[Antiope (Amazon)|Antiope]] and [[Melanippe]]. She and her sisters were allies of the [[Troy|Trojans]] during the [[Trojan War]].
