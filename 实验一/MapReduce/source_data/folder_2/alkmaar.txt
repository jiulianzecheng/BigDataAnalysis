 '''Alkmaar''' is a [[List of municipalities of the Netherlands|municipality]] in [[North Holland]] in the [[Netherlands]]. In 2019, 108,582 people lived there. 
 
 On 1 January 2015, the former municipalities of [[Graft-De Rijp]] and [[Schermer]] were merged into the municipality of Alkmaar.
