 '''Himmelstadt''' is a community in [[Main-Spessart]] in the ''[[Regierungsbezirk]]'' of [[Lower Franconia]] (''Unterfranken'') in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]] and a member of the ''Verwaltungsgemeinschaft'' (Administrative Community) of Zellingen. 
 
 Because of the community’s “heavenly” name – ''Himmel'' means “heaven” or “sky” in [[German language|German]] – the ''Weihnachtspostamt Himmelstadt'' (“Himmelstadt Christmas Post Office”) has existed since 1986, run by [[Deutsche Post]] and the community. 
 
 == Geography == 
 
 === Location === 
 Himmelstadt is in the Würzburg Region. 
   
 The community has only one ''Gemarkung'' (traditional rural cadastral area), also called Himmelstadt.
