 '''Pokki''' is a free [[digital distribution]] platform and [[Windows Shell]] [[Software extension|extension]]. It was made by [[SweetLabs]], Inc. It gives computers running Windows a [[Mobile application|mobile app]]-like interface. It is available for [[Windows XP]] and later; the [[Windows 8]] version takes over the [[Start menu#Third version|Start screen]] to imitate a look and feel of the [[Start menu#second version|second generation Start menu]] in earlier versions of Windows. 
 
 Pokki is adware, according to Malwaretips.  < ref > {{Cite web|date=2015-02-04|title=Remove  " Start Menu Updated "  pop-up virus (Removal Guide)|url=https://malwaretips.com/blogs/remove-start-menu-updated-popup/|access-date=2020-08-20|website=MalwareTips Blogs|language=en-US}} < /ref >  Meanwhile, [[Sophos|Sophos AV]] has said Pokki is  " viruses and spyware " . < ref > {{Cite web|title=Pokki - Viruses and Spyware - Advanced Network Threat Protection {{!}} ATP from Targeted Malware Attacks and Persistent Threats {{!}} sophos.com - Threat Center|url=https://www.sophos.com/en-us/threat-center/threat-analyses/viruses-and-spyware/Pokki.aspx|access-date=2020-08-20|website=www.sophos.com}} < /ref >   
 
 ==History== 
 Pokki first launched as a beta version in June 2011. < ref > {{cite web|last=Albro|first=Edward|date=28 June 2011|title=Pokki Brings Smartphone-style Apps to Your Desktop|url=http://www.pcworld.com/article/231269/pokkidesktopapps.html|work=PC World|access-date=13 November 2012}} < /ref > < ref > {{cite web|last=Cho|first=Eddie|date=30 June 2011|title=Pokki: Serving the Web like bite-sized candy|url=http://download.cnet.com/8301-2007_4-20075585-12/pokki-serving-the-web-like-bite-sized-candy/|work=CNET|access-date=13 November 2012|archive-date=19 October 2011|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20111019112208/http://download.cnet.com/8301-2007_4-20075585-12/pokki-serving-the-web-like-bite-sized-candy/|url-status=dead}} < /ref > < ref > {{cite web|last=Geron|first=Tomio|date=28 June 2011|title=Pokki Brings Mobile-Style Apps To Desktop PCs|url=https://www.forbes.com/sites/tomiogeron/2011/06/28/pokki-brings-mobile-style-apps-to-desktop-pcs/|work=Forbes|access-date=13 November 2012}} < /ref >  Pokki's framework is built on [[Chromium (web browser)|Chromium]], allowing developers to make desktop applications with normal internet languages, such as [[HTML5]], [[CSS3]], and [[JavaScript]]. < ref name= " bizapp " > {{cite web|last=Kincaid|first=Jason|date=30 June 2011|title=OpenCandy's Pokki Brings Web Apps To The Desktop, With Style|url=https://techcrunch.com/2011/06/28/opencandys-pokki-brings-web-apps-to-the-desktop-with-style/|work=TechCrunch|access-date=13 November 2012}} < /ref > 
 
 In April 2012, Pokki launched the Pokki Store, a marketplace and [[app store]] for desktop apps for social networking services and [[social network game|social gaming]] apps. < ref > {{cite web|last=Alex|first=Wilhelm|date=17 April 2012|title=Meet Pokki: Bringing the power of mobile apps to your desktop|url=https://thenextweb.com/insider/2012/04/17/meet-pokki-bringing-the-power-of-mobile-apps-to-your-desktop/|work=The Next Web|access-date=13 November 2012}} < /ref >  With the Pokki Store, users can browse, search, and download applications in various categories. Developers submit applications using the Pokki Developer Center. 
 
 In August 2012, Pokki finished being in the beta stage. It said it had 1 million [[monthly active users]]. It released the Pokki Menu, an app launcher for access, management, and discovery of its apps, and a notification center for real-time updates. < ref > {{cite web|last=Van Groove|first=Jennifer|date=30 August 2012|title=Pokki exits beta, picks up 1M users, and sets out to prove that desktop apps can be cool, too|url=https://venturebeat.com/2012/08/30/pokki-exits-beta/#s:pokki-2-0-taskbar|work=VentureBeat|access-date=13 November 2012}} < /ref > < ref > {{cite web|last=Gallagher|first=Billy|date=30 August 2012|title=Pokki Surpasses 1 Million Monthly Active Users, Releases Angry Birds, Pandora and Pinterest Desktop Apps|url=https://techcrunch.com/2012/08/30/pokki-surpasses-1-million-monthly-active-users-releases-angry-birds-pandora-and-pinterest-pc-desktop-apps/|work=TechCrunch|access-date=13 November 2012}} < /ref > < ref name= " theverge " > {{cite web|last=Streams|first=Kimber|date=31 August 2012|title=Must-have Windows widget app Pokki leaves beta, OS X version on the way|url=https://www.theverge.com/2012/8/31/3282299/pokki-windows-7-desktop-notifications-os-x|work=The Verge|access-date=13 November 2012}} < /ref >
