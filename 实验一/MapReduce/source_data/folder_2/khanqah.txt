 A '''khanqah''' or '''khaniqah''' ({{lang-fa|خانقاه}}) is a place where members of the [[Sufism|Sufi faith]] meet and do their [[Spirituality|spiritual rituals]]. Today, there are a lot of khanaqahs around the world, especially in [[Islamic world|muslim countries]].
