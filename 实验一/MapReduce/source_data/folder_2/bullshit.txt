 '''Bullshit''' (also '''selmo''', '''bullplop''',  '''bullbutter''', '''bovine stool''') is a common [[English language]] [[expletive]]. It can be used also as an [[interjection]] or as many other [[Part of speech|parts of speech]].  Usually, it is a [[noun]], [[verb]] or interjection that describes a lie.  It shows that someone is trying to deceive, e.g.  " Stop bullshitting me "  ( " Don't try to deceive me.   I know what you are saying is a lie.
