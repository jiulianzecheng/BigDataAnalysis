'''Tendring''' is a borough in the north-east of [[Essex]] in the [[East of England]]. It was created on 1 April 1974. Its population is about 140,000, a high proportion of whom are older people or [[Old age|senior citizens]]. The largest settlement is [[Clacton-on-Sea]], which has over a third of the borough's people, it was called a deprived area by many.
