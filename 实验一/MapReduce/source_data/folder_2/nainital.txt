'''Nainital''' is a town in the [[India]]n [[States and territories of India|state]] of [[Uttarakhand]] and headquarters of [[Nainital district]] in the [[Kumaon division|Kumaon]] [[foothills]] of the outer [[Himalayas]]. 
 
 ''Naini Lake'' has too low a level of [[oxygen]] in the bottom layer to sustain [[fish]]. This is mostly due to [[pollution]], which includes illegal dumping of [[garbage]].
