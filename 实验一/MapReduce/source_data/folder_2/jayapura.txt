 '''Jayapura''' is the capital and largest city of the [[Indonesia|Indonesian]] [[Provinces of Indonesia|province]] of [[Papua (province)|Papua]]. 
 
 Jayapura is the [[List of Indonesian cities by GDP|fourth largest city by economy]] in [[Eastern Indonesia]]—after [[Makassar]], [[Denpasar]], and [[Manado]]—with a 2016 [[gross domestic product|GDP]] at [[Indonesian rupiah|Rp]]19.48 trillion.
