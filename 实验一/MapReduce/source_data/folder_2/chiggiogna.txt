 '''Chiggiogna''' is a village and former [[municipality]] of the [[district]] [[Leventina (district)|Leventina]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Ticino]], [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 On 29 January 2006 the former municipalities of [[Calonico]], Chiggiogna and [[Rossura]] merged into the municipality of [[Faido]].
