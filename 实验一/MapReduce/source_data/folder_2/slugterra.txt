 '''''SlugTerra''''' is a [[Canada|Canadian]] [[computer animation]] [[television series]] by [[Nerd Corps Entertainment]]. < ref name=CBC > {{cite web |url=http://www.cbc.ca/news/business/dhx-media-buys-vancouver-animation-studio-nerd-corps-1.2857777 |title=DHX Media buys Vancouver animation studio Nerd Corps |author= < !--Staff writer(s); no by-line.-- >  |date=2 December 2014 |website= |publisher=CBC News |accessdate=31 December 2014}} < /ref >  The show first aired in 2012. There were 39 episodes in 2 seasons. The plan also called for three Slugterra movies. The second movie was release on August 2, 2014. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://www.toonzone.net/2014/08/toonzone-interviews-asaph-fipke-supreme-commander-nerd-corps-slugterra-movies/ |title=Toonzone Interviews Asaph Fipke, Supreme Commander of Nerd Corps, on the  " Slugterra "  Movies |author=Ed Liu |date=August 20, 2014 |website= |publisher=Toon Zone LLC |accessdate=31 December 2014 |archive-date=13 December 2014 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20141213213728/http://www.toonzone.net/2014/08/toonzone-interviews-asaph-fipke-supreme-commander-nerd-corps-slugterra-movies/ |url-status=dead }} < /ref >   
 The show airs on [[Disney XD]] in Canada and Disney XD Worldwide. < ref name=CBC/ >  The series was released on [[DVD]] in Canada by [[Entertainment Eone]]. It was released in the United States by Shout! Factory.
