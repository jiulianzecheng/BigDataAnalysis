 '''''Futureworld''''' is a 1976 American science fiction thriller movie directed by [[Richard T. Heffron]] and stars [[Peter Fonda]], [[Blythe Danner]], [[Arthur Hill]], [[Stuart Margolin]], [[John P. Ryan|John Ryan]], [[Yul Brynner]]. It is a sequel to the 1973 movie ''[[Westworld (movie)|Westworld]]'' and is the second movie in the ''[[Westworld]]'' franchise. It was distributed by [[American International Pictures]].
