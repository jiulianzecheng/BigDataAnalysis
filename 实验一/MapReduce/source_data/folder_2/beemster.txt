 '''Beemster''' is a former municipality in the [[Netherlands|Dutch]] [[Provinces of the Netherlands|province]] of [[North Holland]]. In 2021, about 10,100 people lived there.  
 
 Since 2022, it has been part of the municipality of [[Purmerend]].
