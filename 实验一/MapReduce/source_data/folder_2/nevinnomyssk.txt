 '''Nevinnomyssk''' ({{lang-ru|Невинномы́сск}}) is a [[city]] in [[Stavropol Krai]], [[Russia]]. It is occupies both [[bank]] (geography)s of the [[Kuban River]] at its [[confluence]] with the [[Bolshoy Zelenchuk River]], {{convert|54|km|sp=us}} south of [[Stavropol]]. Population: 132,141 (2002 Census); < ref name= " 2002Census " > {{ru-pop-ref|2002Census}} < /ref >  92,000 (1973); 40,000 (1959).
