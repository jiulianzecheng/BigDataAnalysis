 '''Hakīm Abul-Qāsim Firdawsī Tūsī''' ([[935]]-[[1020]]) ({{lang-fa|حكیم ابوالقاسم فردوسی توسی}}) or '''Ferdowsi''' is a [[Persian people|Persian]] [[poet]]. Ferdowsi was born in Tus. He wrote the ''[[Shahnameh|Shāhnāma]]''. Shahnama is the national epic of [[Iran]].
