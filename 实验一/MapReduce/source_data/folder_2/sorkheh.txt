 '''Sorkheh''' ({{lang-fa|سرخه}}, also [[Romanize]]d as '''Surkheh''') < ref > {{GEOnet3|-3086232}} < /ref >  is a city in and the capital of [[Sorkheh County]], in [[Semnan Province]], [[Iran]]. At the 2006 census, its population was 9,062, in 2,686 families. < ref > {{IranCensus2006|20}} < /ref >  Its inhabitants speak [[Sorkhei language|Sorkhei]], a [[Semnani languages|Semnani]] language or dialect.
