 '''Diceware''' is a method of creating [[password]]s and [[Cryptography|cryptographic letters]] using ordinary [[dice]] as a [[random]] number generator. < ref > {{Cite web|last=Brodkin|first=Jon|date=2014-03-27|title=Diceware passwords now need six random words to thwart hackers|url=https://arstechnica.com/information-technology/2014/03/diceware-passwords-now-need-six-random-words-to-thwart-hackers/|access-date=2021-04-28|website=Ars Technica|language=en-us}} < /ref > 
 
 ==Examples== 
 Here are some examples created from diceware: 
 * dobbs bella bump flash begin ansi 
 * easel venom aver flung jon call 
 * conjoined sterling securely chitchat spinout pelvis 
 * rice immorally worrisome shopping traverse recharger
