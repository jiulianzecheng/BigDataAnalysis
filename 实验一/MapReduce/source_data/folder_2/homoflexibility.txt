 '''Homoflexibility''' describes a [[Sexual orientation|sexual]] or [[romantic orientation]] characterized by experiencing [[Sexual attraction|attraction]] primarily to individuals of the same [[sex]] or [[gender]] with occasional exceptions directed to the opposite gender or sex.
