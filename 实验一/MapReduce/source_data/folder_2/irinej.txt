 '''Irinej''' ({{lang-sr-cyr|Иринеј}}, {{IPA-sh|irǐneːj|pron}}, {{lang-en|Irenaeus}}; 28 August 1930  & ndash; 20 November 2020) was the 45th [[List of heads of the Serbian Orthodox Church|Patriarch of the Serbian Orthodox Church]], the spiritual leader of Eastern Orthodox [[Serbs]], from 22 January 2010 to 20 November 2020. < ref > [[B92]]: [http://www.b92.net/info/vesti/index.php?yyyy=2010 & mm=01 & dd=22 & nav_category=12 & nav_id=405930 Vladika niški Irinej novi patrijarh (''Bishop of Nis Irinej is the new Patriarch'')], 22 Jan 2010 {{in lang|sr}} < /ref > < ref name=MaC > {{cite web|url=http://www.monstersandcritics.com/news/europe/news/article_1527595.php/PROFILE-Serbia-s-new-patriarch-Irinej-a-traditionalist-and-diplomat|title=Profile: Serbia's new Patriarch Irinej, a Traditionalist and Diplomat|last=Babic|first=Boris|date=22 January 2010|access-date=22 January 2010|archive-date=3 October 2012|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20121003180659/http://www.monstersandcritics.com/news/europe/news/article_1527595.php/PROFILE-Serbia-s-new-patriarch-Irinej-a-traditionalist-and-diplomat|url-status=dead}} < /ref > 
 
 His full title was ''[[His Holiness]] the [[Archbishop]] of [[Patriarchal Monastery of Peć|Peć]], [[Metropolitan bishop|Metropolitan]] of [[Archbishopric of Belgrade and Karlovci|Belgrade and Karlovci]], and [[Serbian Patriarch]] Irinej''.  
 
 He was the [[Bishop]] of [[Niš]] between 1975 and 2010. 
 
 On 4 November 2020, Irinej tested positive to [[COVID-19]]. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Patrijarh Irinej pozitivan na koronavirus|url=http://rs.n1info.com/Vesti/a668410/Patrijarh-Irinej-pozitivan-na-koronavirus.html|access-date=2020-11-04|website=N1 Srbija|language=sr-Latn|archive-date=2020-11-04|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20201104201739/http://rs.n1info.com/Vesti/a668410/Patrijarh-Irinej-pozitivan-na-koronavirus.html|url-status=dead}} < /ref >  He died a few weeks later on 20 November from the infection in [[Belgrade]], aged 90. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Упокојио се у Господу Његова Светост Архиепископ пећки, Митрополит београдско-карловачки и Патријарх српски г. Иринеј|url=http://www.tvhram.rs/vesti/vesti-iz-crkve/1594/upokojio-gospodu-njegova-svetost-arhiepiskop-pecki-mitropolit-beogradsko-karlovacki-patrijarh-srpski-irinej|access-date=2020-11-20|website=Televizija Hram|language=en}} < /ref >
