'''Nanorobots''', or '''nanobots''' for short, are a hypothetical type of [[Robot|robots]]. Their parts are about as big as a nanometer (0,0000000001 or 10 < sup > −9 < /sup >  meters). < ref > Vaughn JR (2006).  " Over the Horizon: Potential Impact of Emerging Trends in Information and Communication Technology on Disability Policy and Practice " . ''National Council on Disability, Washington DC'': 1–55. < /ref > 
 
 == Use == 
 Nanobots could be useful in branches like medicine. They could do a diagnosis and targeted drug-delivery for [[cancer]]. < ref > Lavan, D. A.; McGuire, T.; Langer, R. (2003).  " Small-scale systems for in vivo drug delivery " . ''Nature Biotechnology''. '''21''' (10): 1184–91. < /ref >
