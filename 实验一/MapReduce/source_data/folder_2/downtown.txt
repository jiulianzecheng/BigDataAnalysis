 '''Downtown''' (called a '''city centre''' in [[British English]]) is a word used in [[North America]] which refers to the main part of a city. It is usually the [[central business district]] where most [[Commerce|commercial]] and [[office]] areas are; many of them being in [[skyscraper]]s. [[Tourism]] also concentrates there.
