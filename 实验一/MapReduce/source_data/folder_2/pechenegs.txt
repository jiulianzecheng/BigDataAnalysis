 The '''Pechenegs''' or '''Patzinaks''' ({{lang-tr|Peçenekler}}, {{lang-hu|Besenyő}}, {{lang-el|Patzinaki/Petsenegi}} or {{lang|el|Πατζινάκοι/Πετσενέγοι/Πατζινακίται}}, {{lang-la|Pacinacae, Bisseni}} /in Hungarian diplomas) were a [[nomad|semi-nomadic]] [[Turkic peoples|Turkic]] people of the [[Central Asia]]n steppes speaking the [[Pecheneg language]] which belonged to the [[Turkic languages|Turkic language family]].
