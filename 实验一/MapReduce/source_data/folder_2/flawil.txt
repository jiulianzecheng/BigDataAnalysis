 '''Flawil''' is a [[municipality]] of the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[St. Gallen (canton)|St. Gallen]] in [[Switzerland]]. About 10,000 people live there. 
 
 It is the regional centre of [[Toggenburg|Untertoggenburg]], but is part of the [[Wil (Wahlkreis)|Wil]] electoral district.
