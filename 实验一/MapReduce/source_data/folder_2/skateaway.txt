 " '''Skateaway''' "  is a 1980 song by British rock band [[Dire Straits]] and is the second single from their third studio album ''[[Making Movies]]''. It went to number 58 in the United States, number 37 in the United Kingdom and number 47 in New Zealand. It features the daughter of Nigeria's first president in the music video.
