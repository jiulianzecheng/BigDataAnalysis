 '''Khulna''' is the fourth largest city in [[Bangladesh]]. It is on the banks of the [[Rupsha River|Rupsha]] and Bhairab rivers in [[Khulna District]]. It is the headquarters of [[Khulna Division]].  It is a major industrial and commercial center. It has a seaport named [[Port of Mongla|Mongla]] on its outskirts, 38 & nbsp;km from Khulna City. There were about 1,000,000 people in 2010 estimation. 
 
 The city is 333 & nbsp;km southwest of Bangladesh's capital [[Dhaka]].  It is connected by air, road, train and water transportation. 
 
 Khulna is known as the city of shrimp, because 75% of shrimp exported from Bangladesh are from the Khulna zone.  It is also known for its [[lobster]], [[prawn]], [[catfish]], and [[crab]].
