 '''Boyzone''' are an [[Republic of Ireland|Irish]] [[boy band]]. The group is a [[quartet]], its members are [[Keith Duffy]] (born 1974), [[Mikey Graham]] (born 1972), [[Ronan Keating]] (born 1977) and [[Shane Lynch]] (born 1976). [[Stephen Gately]] (1976-2009) was also a member until his death. The band were formed in [[Dublin]] by [[Louis Walsh]] in 1993. They split in 2000 and reformed in 2007.
