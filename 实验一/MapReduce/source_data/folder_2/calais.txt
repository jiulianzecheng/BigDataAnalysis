 '''Calais''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]] in the [[Pas-de-Calais]] [[Departments of France|department]] in north  [[France]]. It is an important [[ferry]] [[port]] opposite [[Dover]]. The [[Channel Tunnel]] is also nearby. About 73,000 people live in Calais.
