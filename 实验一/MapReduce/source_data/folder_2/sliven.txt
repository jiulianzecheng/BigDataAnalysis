 '''Sliven''' ({{lang-bg|Сливен}}) is a [[List of cities in Bulgaria|city]] in [[Bulgaria]]. It is the capital of [[Sliven Province]]. In 2011, 91,620 people lived there. 
 
 Sliven is famous for its Bulgarian [[Hajduk|haidut]]s who fought against the [[Ottoman Turks]] in the 19th century.
