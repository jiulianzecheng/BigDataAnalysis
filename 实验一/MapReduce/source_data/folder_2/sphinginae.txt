 The '''Sphinginae''' are a [[subfamily]] of the [[hawkmoth]]s (Sphingidae), [[moth]]s of the order [[Lepidoptera]]. Notable [[taxa]] include the [[Agrius cingulata|pink-spotted hawkmoth]], ''Agrius cingulata'', a very common and easily spotted species, the [[Deaths head moth|death's-head hawkmoth]] (''Acherontia'' spp.) of ''[[Silence of the Lambs]]'' fame, and ''[[Xanthopan morgani]]'' with its huge [[proboscis]].
