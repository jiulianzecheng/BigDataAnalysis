 '''Swissporarena''' is a [[Association football|football]] [[stadium]] in [[Lucerne]], [[Switzerland]] and is the home stadium of [[FC Luzern]]. It has a [[capacity]] of 16,800 people and was opened in 2011. It replaced the demolished [[Stadion Allmend]].
