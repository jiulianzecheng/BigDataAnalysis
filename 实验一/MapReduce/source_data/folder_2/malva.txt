 '''''Malva''''' is a [[genus]] of about 25-30 species of plants commonly called [[mallow]]s. It is the [[type genus]] in the family Malvaceae. Several species are widely grown as garden flowers, while some are [[wikt:invasive|invasive]] weeds,such as [[Malva neglecta|M. neglecta]]. The color [[mauve]] is named after the French name of the plant. The word Mallow comes from the old English  " malwe "  which was imported from the word  " Malva " .
