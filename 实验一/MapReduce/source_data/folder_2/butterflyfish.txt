 '''Butterflyfish''' are a group of brightly marked tropical marine [[fish]] in the family '''Chaetodontidae'''. The '''bannerfish''' and '''coralfish''' are also included in this group. They live mostly on the [[reef]]s of the [[Atlantic Ocean|Atlantic]], [[Indian Ocean|Indian]] and [[Pacific Ocean]]s. There are about 120 [[species]] in 10 [[genera]].
