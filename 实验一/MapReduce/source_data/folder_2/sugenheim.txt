 '''Sugenheim''' is a [[Municipalities of Germany|municipality]]  in [[Neustadt (Aisch)-Bad Windsheim]] in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. 
 The municipality has the following eleven villages: 
 {| 
 | valign= " top "  | 
 * Krautostheim 
 * Krassolzheim 
 * Deutenheim 
 * Ezelheim 
 * Ullstadt 
 * Sugenheim 
 | valign= " top "  | 
 * Ingolstadt 
 * Neundorf 
 * Rüdern 
 * Hürfeld 
 * Dutzenthal  
 |}
