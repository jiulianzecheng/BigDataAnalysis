 '''Jorge Arias''' < ref name= " OWOW " > {{cite web|url=http://www.onlineworldofwrestling.com/profiles/h/hunico.html|title=Hunico|accessdate=2011-08-10|work=Online World of Wrestling}} < /ref >  (born September 5, 1977) < ref name=OWOW/ >  is a [[Mexican American]] [[Professional wrestling|professional wrestler]]. He currently works for the [[WWE]]. He is currently portraying the '''[[Sin Cara]]''' character. He was formerly known by the [[ring name]] '''Hunico'''. < ref name= " WWE " > {{cite web|url=http://www.wwe.com/shows/smackdown/hunico|title=Hunico|accessdate=2011-10-28|work=[[WWE]]|archive-date=2011-12-22|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20111222032214/http://www.wwe.com/shows/smackdown/hunico|url-status=dead}} < /ref >  Before working with WWE, Arias was better known under the ring name '''Incognito'''. He mainly worked for the [[Asistencia Asesoría y Administración]] (AAA) in [[Mexico]] and [[Chikara (professional wrestling)|Chikara]] in the [[United States]]. Arias first wrestled under the name  " Místico " 
 
 ==Career== 
 Incognito is perhaps best known in the USA for being a member of [[Team Mexico]] along with [[Puma (wrestler)|Puma]], Magno, and [[Shocker (wrestler)|Shocker]] during [[Total Nonstop Action Wrestling]]'s [[TNA 2006 World X Cup Tournament|2006 World X-Cup Tournament]]. Team Mexico finished in 3rd place with [[Team USA]] in 1st place, [[Team Canada (TNA)|Team Canada]] in 2nd place, and [[Team Japan]] in last place. As well as his career in Mexico working for [[Asistencia Asesoría y Administración|AAA]], he is also known for competing in [[Chikara (professional wrestling)|Chikara]], where he won both the 2008 [[King of Trios]] (alongside El Pantera and [[Lince Dorado]]) and the 2008 Rey de Voladores tournament.
