 '''Oran''' ([[Arabic language|Arabic]]: وهران‎‎  - Wahrān, [[Berber languages|Berber]]: ⵡⴰⵀⵔⴻⵏ - Wahren) is a city in western [[Algeria]], on the coast. In 2010, about 853,000 people were living there.  
 [[File:GARE D'ORAN 2.jpg|thumb|left|Oran rail station]] 
 [[File:Coat of Arms of Oran.png|thumb|coat of arms]] 
 
 It is the capital of [[Oran Province]] and was established in 903. 
 
 Oran is the second biggest city in Algeria after [[Algiers]]. It is well-known because of its business activities and culture.
