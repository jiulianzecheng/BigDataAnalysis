 '''Minnale''' is a 2001 Indian Tamil-language romance movie. It was directed and written by Gautham Vasudev Menon. This is the debut movie of music composer, Harris Jayaraj. This movie stars [[R. Madhavan]], [[Reema Sen]], Abbas, Vivek and Nagesh. 
 
 The movie released on February 2nd, 2001 to positive reviews. It became a commercial success. < ref > {{Cite news|last=Kamath|first=Sudhish|author-link=Sudhish Kamath|date=27 October 2001|title=More for Maddy's crowd|work=[[The Hindu]]|url=https://www.thehindu.com/todays-paper/tp-miscellaneous/tp-others/more-for-maddys-crowd/article27983911.ece|url-status=live|access-date=11 July 2022|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20220711151009/https://www.thehindu.com/todays-paper/tp-miscellaneous/tp-others/more-for-maddys-crowd/article27983911.ece|archive-date=11 July 2022}} < /ref >  This movie was a turning point in R. Madhavan's career.  
 
 == Remake == 
 
 The movie was remade in [[Hindi]] as [[Rehnaa Hai Terre Dil Mein]] in the same year. Madhavan did the same role, which did average at the box office.
