 '''Jason  " DaHeala "  Quenneville''' is a Canadian [[record producer]], [[songwriter]], and engineer. He is best known for his collaborations with [[The Weeknd]]. Quenneville co-wrote and co-produced his albums ''Kiss Land'' (2013) and ''[[Beauty Behind the Madness]]'' (2015). In 2016, he was nominated for an Academy Award for Best Original Song at the 88th Academy Awards for co-writing his song  " Earned It "  from ''[[Fifty Shades of Grey]]''; the nomination is shared with [[The Weeknd]], [[Belly (rapper)|Belly]], and Stephen Moccio.
