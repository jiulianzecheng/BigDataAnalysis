 A '''cargo ship''' or '''freighter''' is any kind of [[ship]] that carries [[cargo]], goods, and materials from one port to another. Thousands of cargo carriers travel on the world's seas and oceans each year, and handle most international [[trade]]. Cargo ships are usually specially designed for a special task, often carrying tools like [[crane (machine)|crane]]s to load and unload. They come in all sizes.
