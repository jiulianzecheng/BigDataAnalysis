 '''Ofterschwang''' is a [[municipality]] in [[Oberallgäu]], in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. It is a [[Winter sport|winter sports]] venue. It is often used for [[Alpine skiing World Cup]] events. It is found west of [[Sonthofen]], but east of the [[Austria|Austrian]] border. Between 1988 and 2008 Ofterschwang grew by 577 people.
