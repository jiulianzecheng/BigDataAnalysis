 '''Itacoatiara''' is a [[Brazil]]ian city of the state of [[Amazonas (Brazil)|Amazonas]]. It has 89.840 inhabitants and an area of 8.600 [[km²]]. It was founded in [[1874]] and it is one of the most important cities in state of Amazonas.
