 '''CCXP''' (originally called '''Comic Con Experience''') is a [[Brazil|Brazilian]] [[entertainment]] and [[Comic book|comic]] [[Fan convention|convention]]. It is based on the [[San Diego Comic-Con]]. The convention has attractions about comics, [[Television program|television series]], [[Movie|movies]], [[Video game|video games]], [[Literature|literature]] and the [[internet]].
