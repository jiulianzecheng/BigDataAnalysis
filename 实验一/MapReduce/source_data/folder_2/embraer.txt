'''Embraer S.A.''' is a [[Brasil|Brazilian]] company that produces all kinds of [[aircraft]], both civil and military. 
 
 Its headquarters are in [[São José dos Campos]]. 
 
 Some aircraft’s are the Embraer [[Embraer ERJ family|ERJ family]], or [[Embraer E-Jet family|the embraer E-jet family]], many are popular amongst the regionals.
