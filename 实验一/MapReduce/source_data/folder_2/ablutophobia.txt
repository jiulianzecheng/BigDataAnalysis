'''Ablutophobia''' is a [[fear]] of [[bathing]], [[washing]], or [[cleaning]]. < ref > {{cite book |last=Saul |first=Helen |year=2001 |title=Phobias: Fighting the Fear |url=https://archive.org/details/isbn_9781559706476 |publisher=Arcade Publishing |isbn=1-55970-647-3 |ref=Saul}} < /ref >  It is a type of [[specific phobia]]. It is most often seen in children and women.
