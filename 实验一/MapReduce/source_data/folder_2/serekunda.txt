 '''Serekunda''' ({{lang-ar|سيريكوندا}}, sometimes spelled '''Serrekunda''') is the largest [[city]] in [[The Gambia]]. 
 
 It is close to the [[Atlantic Ocean|Atlantic]] coast, {{convert|13|km|0|abbr=on}} southwest of the capital, [[Banjul]].
