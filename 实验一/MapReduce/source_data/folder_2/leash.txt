A '''leash''' or '''lead''' is a [[strap]] used to control the movement of an [[animal]],. It is normally connected to a [[collar]], [[harness]] or [[halter]] to control a [[pet]], Leashes are commonly used on [[dog|dogs]] during walks and other times when the pet is outside the owner's home or property. It is used to make sure the animal does not wander away from its owner. A leash can also be used on [[toddler]]s and other small children to prevent them from wandering away from their [[parent]]s or [[guardian]]s during outings.
