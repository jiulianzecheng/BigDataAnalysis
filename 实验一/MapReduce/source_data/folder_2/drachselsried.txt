 '''Drachselsried''' is a [[Municipalities of Germany|municipality]] in [[Regen (district)|Regen]], in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]].  Drachselsried is in the [[Zeller Valley]] (German: ''Zellertal''), and is part of the Glass Road (''Glasstrasse'') The area is part of the [[Bavarian Forest National Park]].
