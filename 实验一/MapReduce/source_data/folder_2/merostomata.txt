 '''Merostomata''' is a name given to the now [[extinction|extinct]] sea scorpions ([[Eurypterida]]) and the horseshoe crabs ([[Xiphosura]]). There are four known species of horsehoe crabs still alive. [[James Dwight Dana]] introduced the name to refer to the horseshoe crabs only. [[Henry Woodward (geologist)|Henry Woodward]] later extended it to refer to both [[taxon|taxa]].
