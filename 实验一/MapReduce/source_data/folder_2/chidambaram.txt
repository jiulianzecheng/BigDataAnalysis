Chidambaram is a town and municipality in [[Cuddalore district]], [[Tamil Nadu]]. It is the headquarters of the Chidambaram taluk.  
 
 == Geography == 
 Chidambaram covers an area of {{convert|4.8|sqkm|sqmi|abbr=on}} and had a population of 62,153 as of 2011. [[Annamalai University]], established in 1929 in Chidambaram, is one of the oldest and most prominent universities in the state. This town is known famous for the great [[Thillai Nataraja Temple]].
