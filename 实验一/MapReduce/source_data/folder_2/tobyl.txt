'''Tobyl''' ({{lang-kk|Тобыл}}) is a [[city]] in the [[Kostanay Region]] of [[Kazakhstan]]. As of 2023, 28,453 people live in the city. < ref name=pop2023 > {{Cite web|date=2023-01-01|title=Population of the Republic of Kazakhstan by gender and type of area (as of 2023-01-01)|url=https://stat.gov.kz/api/getFile/?docId=ESTAT478686|url-status=dead|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20230305101742/https://stat.gov.kz/api/getFile/?docId=ESTAT478686|archive-date=2023-03-05|website=QazStat}} < /ref >  This city is in the [[urban area]] of Kostanay.  
 
 Tobyl was founded in 1863. At first it was named ''Zatobolsk'' ({{lang-ru|Затобольск}}). It got city status in 2020. < ref > {{cite web|date=2020-01-02|title=Указ президента Казахстана «Об отнесении поселка Затобольска Костанайского района Костанайской области к категории городов районного значения и его переименовании»|url=http://www.akorda.kz/ru/legal_acts/decrees/ob-otnesenii-poselka-zatobolska-kostanaiskogo-raiona-kostanaiskoi-oblasti-k-kategorii-gorodov-raionnogo-znacheniya-i-ego-pereimenovanii|url-status=dead|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200102113223/http://www.akorda.kz/ru/legal_acts/decrees/ob-otnesenii-poselka-zatobolska-kostanaiskogo-raiona-kostanaiskoi-oblasti-k-kategorii-gorodov-raionnogo-znacheniya-i-ego-pereimenovanii|archive-date=2020-01-02|access-date=2023-12-02|website=akorda.kz}} < /ref >
