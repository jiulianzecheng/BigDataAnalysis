'''Urmia''' ({{lang-fa|ارومیه}}) is a city in [[Iran]]. It is the capital of the [[West Azerbaijan province|West Azerbaijan]] province. In 2016, 736,224 people lived there. [[Lake Urmia]], the second largest salt lake in the world. The majority of the city's people are [[Armenians]], [[Assyrian people|Assyrians]], [[Azerbaijanis]], [[Kurds]] and [[Persians]].
