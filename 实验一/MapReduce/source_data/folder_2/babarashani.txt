 '''Babarashani''' ({{lang-ku|Babarreşanî}}, {{lang-fa|بابارشانی}}) is a city in the Kurdistan province of [[Iran]]. Its people speak [[Kurdish language|Kurdish]]. At the 2006 census, its population was 481, in 128 families.
