 '''Verbenaceae''' is a family of variously [[wood]]y or herbaceous dicotyledons in the order [[Lamiales]]. They have opposite or whorled [[Leaf|leaves]] and regular or irregular [[flower]]s, usually with four or two functional [[stamen]]s.
