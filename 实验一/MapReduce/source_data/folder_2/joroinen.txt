 '''Joroinen''' is a municipality in [[Northern Savonia]], < ref name= " region " > {{cite web |url=https://valtioneuvosto.fi/en/-/10623/government-decided-on-division-into-regions|title=Government decided on division into regions |access-date=1 January 2021}} < /ref >  [[Finland]]. As of January 2014, over 5,200 people lived there. Neighbouring municipalities are [[Juva]], [[Leppävirta]], [[Pieksämäki]], [[Rantasalmi]] and [[Varkaus]]. 
 
 Joroinen was established in 1631. 
 
 The nearest [[airport]] is in Varkaus. < ref > Factum, part 3, page 147, ''Finnish'' {{ISBN|9513566420}} 003 1 398 < /ref >
