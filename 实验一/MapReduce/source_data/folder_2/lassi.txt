 '''Lassi''' ([[Urdu]]:لسی) is a popular traditional [[dahi (curd)|dahi]] ([[yogurt]])-based drink that originated in the [[Indian subcontinent]]. < ref > {{cite web|title=Indian Lassi: The Ultimate Summer Refresher|url=http://imbibemagazine.com/indian-lassi/|website=Imbibe|access-date=29 May 2018}} < /ref > < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.britannica.com/topic/lassi|title=Lassi|work=Encyclopædia Britannica}} < /ref >  Lassi is a blend of yoghurt, water, [[spices]] and sometimes fruit. Namkeen (salty) lassi is similar to [[doogh]], while sweet and mango lassis are like [[milkshakes]].
