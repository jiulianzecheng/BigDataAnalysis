 '''Riederalp''' is a [[municipality]] of the [[district]] of [[Raron (district)|Raron]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Valais]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 On 1 November 2003, the former municipalities of Goppisberg, Greich and Ried-Mörel merged into the new municipality of Riederalp.
