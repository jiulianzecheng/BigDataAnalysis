 '''Sandalwood''' is a form of [[wood]] and also the [[oil]] made from it. It is used for [[perfume]] and [[medicine]]. They are found in [[India]], [[Australia]], [[Indonesia]], and the [[Oceania|Pacific Islands]]. Its [[genus]] is ''Santalum''. In [[Indonesia]] and [[Malaysia]], its local name is ''Cendana''.
