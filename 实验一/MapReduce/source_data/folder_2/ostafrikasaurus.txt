 '''''Ostafrikasaurus''''' was a [[carnivorous]] [[theropod]] [[dinosaur]] from the [[Late Jurassic]] of southeastern [[Tanzania]]. < ref name= " Ostafrikasaurus " > {{cite journal|last=Buffetaut|first=Eric|year=2012|title=An early spinosaurid dinosaur from the Late Jurassic of Tendaguru (Tanzania) and the evolution of the spinosaurid dentition|journal=Oryctos|volume=10|issue=|pages=1–8}} < /ref >  It is known only from a [[fossil]] [[tooth]] that is {{convert|49|mm|in|abbr=on}} long. [[paleontologists|Paleontologist]] [[Eric Buffetaut]] claims it is the oldest evidence of a [[Spinosauridae|Spinosaurid]] dinosaur (a type of [[crocodile]]-like dinosaur that ate [[fish]]); It appeared 148 million years ago. < ref name= " Veterupristisaurus " > {{cite journal|last=Rauhut|first=Oliver W.
