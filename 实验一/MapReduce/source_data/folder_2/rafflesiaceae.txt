 '''Rafflesiaceae''' is a family of [[flowering plant]]s in the [[Malpighiales]] order. They are [[parasitic]] plants found in east and southeast [[Asia]], including ''[[Rafflesia arnoldii]]'', the plant with the largest [[flower]] of all plants.
