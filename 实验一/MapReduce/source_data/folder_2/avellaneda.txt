 '''Avellaneda''' is a city in the [[Buenos Aires Province]] in [[Argentina]]. In 2010, 342,677 people lived there. It is {{convert|7|km|mi|0|abbr=on}} south of [[Buenos Aires]], the capital of Argentina.
