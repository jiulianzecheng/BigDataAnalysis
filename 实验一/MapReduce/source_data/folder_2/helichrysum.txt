 '''Helichrysum''' is a type of flower.  It has about 600 [[species]] in the sunflower family ([[Asteraceae]]). The name Helichrysum comes from two [[Greece|Greek]] words: ''helisso'' means to turn around and ''chrysos'' means gold. 
 
 It grows in Africa, Madagascar, Australasia and Eurasia.  There are many species in South Africa.
