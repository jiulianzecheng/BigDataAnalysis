 '''Leggiuno''' is a ''[[comune]]'' in the [[Province of Varese]] in the [[Italy|Italian]] region of [[Lombardy]]. 
 
 Former football player [[Luigi Riva]] was born here. 
 
 == Main sights == 
 The ''[[frazione]]'' of Reno is home to the hermitage of [[Santa Caterina del Sasso]], which overlooks [[Lake Maggiore]].
