'''Buckingham''' is a town in [[Buckinghamshire]], England. It is to the west of [[Milton Keynes]] and to the north of [[Aylesbury]]. In 2001, 11,572 people were living in Buckingham. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.ons.gov.uk/help/localstatistics|title=Local statistics - Office for National Statistics|website=www.ons.gov.uk}} < /ref >
