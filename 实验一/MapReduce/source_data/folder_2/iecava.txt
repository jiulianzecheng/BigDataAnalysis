 '''Iecava''' ({{audio|Lv-Iecava.ogg|pronunciation}}) is a [[town]] in [[Latvia]] with [[town rights]] since 2021. < ref > [https://likumi.lv/ta/id/315654-administrativo-teritoriju-un-apdzivoto-vietu-likums Law of administrative territories] (in Latvian) < /ref >  It is in the [[Semigallia]] region of southern Latvia, in [[Bauska District]]. As of 2022, Iecava had a [[population]] of 5500 people. < ref > [https://vietvardi.lgia.gov.lv/vv/to_www_obj.objekts?p_id=3434 Latvian Geospatial Information Agency] < /ref >  Iecava is 40 & nbsp;km south of [[Riga]] and 23 & nbsp;km north of [[Bauska]]. Until 1 July 2021, Iecava was a village and was the center of Iecava District. Iecava is on river Iecava. 
 
 The town produces [[Egg (food)|egg]]s, [[vegetable oil]] and [[alcoholic drink]]s. 
 
 == Roads == 
 Roads in Iecava: [[Riga]]—[[Warsaw]] (A7), Iecava—Stelpe (P92), [[Jelgava]]—Iecava (P93), Iecava—Emburga (V1008), [[Baloži]]—Iecava (V7), Daugmale—Iecava (V9), Lambārte—Iecava (V1047). 
 
 Railway line Jelgava—Krustpils passes near Iecava. This line no has [[passenger]] [[train]]s.
