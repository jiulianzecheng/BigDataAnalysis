 '''Faisalabad''', formerly known as '''Lyallpur''', is the third largest [[metropolis]] in [[Pakistan]]. It is in the [[Punjab (Pakistan)|Punjab]] province and is the capital of [[Faisalabad District]]. The city is an important centre of trade and industry.
