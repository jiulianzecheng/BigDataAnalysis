An '''airline''' is a [[company]] that [[transport]]s people and items using [[Airliner|airliners]]. An airline may have as few as one airplane or a [[Fleet (ships)|fleet]] of hundreds of airplanes. 
 
 Most airlines offer regular flights between a group of [[airport]]s. 
 
 An airline has many [[cost]]s to establish and maintain air services: [[labor]], [[fuel]], [[airplane]]s, [[engine]]s, spares and parts, IT services and networks, airport equipment, airport handling services, booking commissions, [[advertising]], catering, training, [[aviation]] [[insurance]] and other costs.
