 '''''Wahlenbergia''''' is a genus of between 150 and 270 [[species]] of [[flowering plant]]s in the family [[Campanulaceae]]. It is found everywhere except for [[North America]]. Most of species come from [[Africa]] and [[Australasia]]. ''Wahlenbergia'' species have spread widely, even to oceanic islands, and there are four species known from the island of [[Saint Helena|St Helena]], including the now [[extinct]] species ''[[Wahlenbergia roxburghii|W. roxburghii]]''.
