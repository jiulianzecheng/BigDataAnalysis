 '''Ittigen''' is a [[municipality]] in the [[administrative district]] of [[Bern-Mittelland (administrative district)|Bern-Mittelland]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Bern (canton)|Bern]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 The municipality was formed in 1983 when it and [[Ostermundigen]] were separated from territory once part of [[Bolligen]]. 
 
 [[File:Ittigen,Talgut 2.jpg|thumb|left|250px|Ittigen]] 
 Since 1991, Ittigen expanded its source of budget to the highway resting site parking fees. The well known example is the resting site Grauholz. As it is very pioneeric economic area, world rare and extraordinary, at the same time but controversy, it has been published in several newspaper [https://www.blick.ch/schweiz/bern/ittigen-be-verteilt-parkbussen-auf-der-autobahn-raststaette-kunden-sind-sauer-das-ist-modernes-raubrittertum-id15182526.html articles]. The municipality maintains the income source up to nowadays.
