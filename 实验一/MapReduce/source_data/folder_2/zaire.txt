 '''Zaire''', officially the '''Republic of Zaire''' ({{lang-fr|République du Zaïre}}) was the name of a country that is now called the [[Democratic Republic of the Congo]]. It used this name from 27 November 1971 to 17 May 1997. The name  " Zaire "  comes from a [[Portugal|Portuguese]] corruption of the [[Kongo language|Kongo]] word ''nzare'', meaning  " river " .
