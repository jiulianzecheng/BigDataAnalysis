'''Andropause''' is when a man's sperm count gets lower in his 40s. This condition is also called '''penopause''' and '''late-onset hypogonadism'''. Other changes that happen are fewer erections and a lower [[libido]]. < ref name=NHSChoices > {{cite web|title=Male Menopause|url=http://www.nhs.uk/conditions/Male-menopause/Pages/Introduction.aspx|website=www.nhs.uk|publisher=NHS Choices|date=April 8, 2016|access-date=October 7, 2016}} < /ref >
