 '''Tamezret''' or '''Tamazrat''' ({{lang-ar|تامزرط}}) is a [[Tunisia|Tunisian]] [[Berbers|Berber]] village located in the south-east of the country, about ten kilometers from [[Matmata, Tunisia|Matmata]] and forty kilometers southwest of the capital of the governorate of [[Gabès]] on which it depends. Ridge village built on the four slopes of one of the highest hills of the [[Matmata, Tunisia|Matmata]] mountains.(480 m altitude). < ref > {{Cite web|url=http://whc.unesco.org/fr/listesindicatives/6444/|title=Habitat troglodytique et le monde des ksour du Sud tunisien - UNESCO World Heritage Centre|last=mondial|first=UNESCO Centre du patrimoine|website=UNESCO Centre du patrimoine mondial|language=fr|access-date=2020-04-21}} < /ref >
