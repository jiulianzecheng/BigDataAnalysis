 == Transport == 
 
 === Road === 
 The D994, D48 and D49 roads pass through the Oze commune. 
 
 === Rail === 
 Despite the fact that there is a railway line passing through the commune, there are no train stations. 
 
 The nearest train station is in [[Aspres-sur-Buëch]].
