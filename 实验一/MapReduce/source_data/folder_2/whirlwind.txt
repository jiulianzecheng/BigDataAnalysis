 A '''whirlwind''' is a [[vortex]] of [[wind]] (a straight rotating column of air) that forms because of [[turbulence]] created by heating and [[Flux|flow]] ([[Air current|current]]) differences.  Whirlwinds happen all over the world and in any season.
