'''Loireauxence''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]] of 7,522 people (2017). It is found in the [[Regions of France|region]] [[Pays de la Loire]] in the [[Loire-Atlantique]] [[Departments of France|department]] in western [[France]]. The municipality was created on 1 January 2016 from the former communes of [[Varades]] (the seat), [[Belligné]], [[La Chapelle-Saint-Sauveur, Loire-Atlantique|La Chapelle-Saint-Sauveur]] and [[La Rouxière]]. < ref > [https://www.legifrance.gouv.fr/eli/arrete/2015/12/18/INTB1532335A/jo/texte Arrêté préfectoral] 18 December 2015 {{in lang|fr}} < /ref >
