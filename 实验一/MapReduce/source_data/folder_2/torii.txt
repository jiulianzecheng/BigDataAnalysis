A '''''torii'''''  is a traditional [[Japan|Japanese]] [[gate]] in [[shinto shrine]]. This is a symbol of Japanese religion [[shinto]].  
 [[File:Itsukushima torii angle.jpg|thumb|A Japanese Torii at [[Itsukushima Shrine]], a [[Shinto shrine]] in [[Hiroshima Prefecture]].]] 
 Some ''torii'' have tablets with writing mounted between the crossbars. Traditionally, torii are made of [[wood]] or [[stone]].
