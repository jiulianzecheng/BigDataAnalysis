 '''Vejle''' is a city in [[Denmark]], in southeast of [[Jutland]] peninsula at the head of Vejle Fjord. As of 2023, the city has 61,310 people lived there, < ref > {{Cite web |date=2022-06-08 |url=https://vafo.dk/artikel/i-fire-dage-var-vejle-danmarks-ottendest%C3%B8rste-by-men-s%C3%A5-blev-tallet-rettet-byen-bider-dog-konkurrenterne-i-haserne-og-er-t%C3%A6t-p%C3%A5-at-rykke-op-i-by-hierarki |title=I fire dage var Vejle Danmarks ottendestørste by - men så blev tallet rettet: Byen bider dog konkurrenterne i haserne og er tæt på at rykke op i by-hierarkiet  |access-date=2022-06-08 |website=vafo.dk |language=da}} < /ref >  which makes it the ninth largest city in the country. Vejle is known for its textile industry.
