 '''''Gargoyles''''' is an [[animation|animated]] [[The Walt Disney Company|Disney]] [[television programme|television series]]. The series was originally shown from October 24, 1994 to February 15, 1997, airing in [[syndicated|syndication]] from 1994 to 1996, and on [[American Broadcasting Company|ABC]] from 1996 until the end of its run. It had a total of 78 [[episode]]s.
