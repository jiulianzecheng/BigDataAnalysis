 '''Total Operations Processing System''', or '''TOPS''', is a computer system for managing the [[locomotive]]s and [[rolling stock]] ([[railroad car]]s) owned by a rail system.  It was originally developed by the [[Southern Pacific Railroad]] and was widely sold; it is best known in the [[United Kingdom]] for its use by [[British Rail]].
