A '''gonad''' is a [[gland]] that makes [[Gamete|gametes]] and [[Sex hormone|sex hormones]]. The male gonad is the [[testicle]]. Testicles make [[Sperm|sperm cells]]. The female gonad is the [[ovary]]. Ovaries make [[Ovum|egg cells]]. < ref > {{cite web|title=gonad (noun) American English definition and synonyms - Macmillan Dictionary|url=http://www.macmillandictionary.com/dictionary/american/gonad|url-status=live|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180508154610/http://www.macmillandictionary.com/dictionary/american/gonad|archive-date=8 May 2018|access-date=8 May 2018|website=www.macmillandictionary.com}} < /ref >  Some animals have different gonads because they are [[Hermaphrodite|hermaphroditic]]. They may have gonads such as the [[ovotestis]].
