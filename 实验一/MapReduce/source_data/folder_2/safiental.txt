 '''Safiental''' ({{lang-rm|Val Stussavgia}}) is a [[municipality]] in [[Surselva (district)|Surselva]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Graubünden]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 On 1 January 2013, the former municipalities [[Valendas]], [[Versam]], [[Safien]] and [[Tenna, Switzerland|Tenna]] joined together to become the new municipality, Safiental.
