A '''Ballast''' is a large [[resistor]] that regulates the amount of electrical current in a circuit. Most ballasts are used for [[Fluorescent lamp|fluorescent lamps]] to regulate the [[Electric current|current]] thru it, which might rise to a dangerous level and burn it out. 
 
 '''Ballast''' is also crushed stone used in [[railway track]] < nowiki/ > s. See [[ballast tamper]].
