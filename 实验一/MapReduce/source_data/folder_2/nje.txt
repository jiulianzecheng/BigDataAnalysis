 The [[Cyrillic alphabet|Cyrillic]] letter '''nje''' (Њ, њ) is a something like a combination of [[En (Cyrillic)|Н]] and [[Soft sign|Ь]]. It is used in [[Macedonian language|Macedonian]] and [[Serbian language|Serbian]] and pronounced like Spanish ñ in  " señor "  (cf. Polish ń, Czech and Slovak ň). Like [[Lje|Љ]], Nje has been created by [[Vuk Karadžić]]. It also takes the form of Нь.
