 '''The International House of Pancakes''', better known by its acronym '''IHOP''', is a [[restaurant]] chain. It serves mostly breakfast foods but also serves lunch and dinner. There are more than 1,500 restaurants in all 50 states as well as in [[Canada]], [[Mexico]], [[Guatemala]], [[Puerto Rico]] and the [[U.S. Virgin Islands]]. 
 
 == History == 
 IHOP was founded in 1958 by Jerry Lapin, Al Lapin and Albert Kallis. The first IHOP opened in [[Toluca Lake, Los Angeles]], [[California]].
