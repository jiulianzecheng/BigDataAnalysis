 '''Ritchies''' is a large [[supermarket]] [[Chain store|chain]] in [[Australia]]. The chain is owned by a group of private investors. [[Metcash]] is a part of that group. The chain is also known as  " Ritchies Supa [[IGA (Australian supermarket group)|IGA]] " . Most Ritchies stores are in [[Victoria (Australia)|Victoria]]. Ritchies earns about $1.4b each year.  
 
 In recent years, Ritchies has had grown a large amount.  In 2000, there were less than 20 Ritchies stores. Ritchies is now on the top 20 Australian retailers list.
