 '''Schrobenhausen''' ({{IPA-de|ʃʁoːbm̩ˈhaʊ̯zn̩|pron}}) is a [[Town#Germany|town]] in [[Neuburg-Schrobenhausen]] in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. It is on the River [[Paar]] about {{convert|25|km|0|abbr=off}} south-west of [[Ingolstadt]] and {{convert|35|km|0|abbr=off}} north-east of [[Augsburg]].
