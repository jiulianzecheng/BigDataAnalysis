'''Seppiana''' was a ''[[comune]]'' in the [[Province of Verbano-Cusio-Ossola]] in the [[Italy|Italian]] region of [[Piedmont]]. On 1 January 2016, the former municipalities of Seppiana and [[Viganella]] merged into the new municipality of [[Borgomezzavalle]].
