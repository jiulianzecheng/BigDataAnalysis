 '''Farvagny''' was a [[municipality]] of the district [[Saane (district)|Saane]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Fribourg (canton)|Fribourg]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 == History == 
 In 1996, the former municipalities of Farvagny-le-Grand, Farvagny-le-Petit, Grenilles and Posat merged to form it. < ref name=Fusion > [http://www.bfs.admin.ch/bfs/portal/de/index/infothek/nomenklaturen/blank/blank/gem_liste/03.html Amtliches Gemeindeverzeichnis der Schweiz] published by the Swiss Federal Statistical Office {{in lang|de}} accessed 19 July 2011 < /ref >  On 1 January 2016, the former municipalities of [[Corpataux-Magnedens]], Farvagny, [[Le Glèbe]], [[Rossens, Fribourg|Rossens]] and [[Vuisternens-en-Ogoz]] merged to form the new municipality of [[Gibloux]]. < ref > [http://www.bfs.admin.ch/bfs/portal/de/index/infothek/nomenklaturen/blank/blank/gem_liste/02.html Amtliches Gemeindeverzeichnis der Schweiz] published by the Swiss Federal Statistical Office {{in lang|de}} accessed 19 January 2016 < /ref >
