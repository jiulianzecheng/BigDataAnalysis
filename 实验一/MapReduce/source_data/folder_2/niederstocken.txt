 '''Niederstocken''' was a [[municipality]] in the [[administrative district]] of [[Thun (administrative district)|Thun]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Bern (canton)|Bern]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 On 1 January 2014, the former municipalities of Niederstocken, [[Höfen, Thun|Höfen]] and [[Oberstocken]] merged into the new municipality of [[Stocken-Höfen]].
