 '''Wiltshire''' (short: ''Wilts'') is an [[England|English]] [[county]]. It borders the counties of [[Hampshire]], [[Dorset]], [[Somerset]], [[Gloucestershire]], [[Oxfordshire]] and [[Berkshire]], and contains the [[unitary authority]] of [[Borough of Swindon|Swindon]]. The [[county town]] is [[Trowbridge]], in the west of the county at {{coor dms|51|19|11|N|2|12|32|W|}}. The county covers 858,931 [[acre]]s (3476 & nbsp;km²)
