 '''Lisberg''' is a community in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. It is in the [[Upper Franconia]]n district of [[Bamberg (district)|Bamberg]] and a member of the administrative community (''Verwaltungsgemeinschaft'') of Lisberg, being in the Steigerwald (forest).
