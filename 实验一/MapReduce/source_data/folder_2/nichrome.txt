'''Nichrome''' is an [[alloy]]. It is made of [[nickel]] and [[chromium]]. It has an 80/20% composition. It is used as a [[wikt:heating element|heating element]]. It gets hot when electricity goes through it. It is normally silver in colour and a wire.
