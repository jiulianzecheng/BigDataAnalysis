 '''Digimon''' is a [[Japan]]ese media franchise made by [[Bandai]]. It features a group of [[digital]] [[creature]]s called Digimon. Humans befriend Digimon as partners and work with them to protect both worlds from enemies. The series has [[movies]], [[manga]], [[anime]], [[toy]]s, [[video game]]s and [[card]] [[game]]s.
