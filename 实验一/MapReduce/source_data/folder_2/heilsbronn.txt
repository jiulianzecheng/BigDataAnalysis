 '''Heilsbronn''' ({{IPA-de|haɪ̯lsbʁɔn|3=De-Heilsbronn.ogg}}) is a town in [[Ansbach (district)|Ansbach]], a district ([[Districts of Germany|''Landkreis'']]) in [[Middle Franconia]], in the wooded valley of the [[Rangau]]. Its hallmark is the ''Katharinenturm'', a medieval tower.
