 '''Forchheim''' ({{IPA-de|ˈfɔrçhaɪ̯m|3=}}; [[East Franconian German|East Franconian]]: ''Forchham'') is a [[Germany|German]] [[town]] in [[Upper Franconia Government Region|Upper Franconia]]. Next bigger cities are [[Nuremberg|Nürnberg]], [[Bamberg]], [[Fürth]], [[Erlangen]] and [[Coburg]]. There live is about 32.000 people.
