 '''Pierremont''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is found in the [[Regions of France|region]] [[Nord-Pas-de-Calais]] in the [[Pas-de-Calais]] [[Departments of France|department]] in the north of [[France]]. 
 
 == Location == 
 Pierremont lies 28 miles (45 km) west of Arras. Here the D99 and the N39 roads cross each other.
