'''Islamism''' is a group of [[ideology|ideologies]] that say that the [[Muslim world]] should be run by [[sharia]] (Islamic laws). Generally, it is the idea that [[Islam]] should guide both [[society]] and [[politics]], as well as people's personal lives. < ref name= " Berman, S 2003, p. 258 " > Berman, S,  " Islamism, Revolution, and Civil Society, Perspectives on Politics " , Vol. 1, No. 2, June 2003, American Political Science Association, p. 258 < /ref >  Important people who have inspired Islamist movements include [[Yusuf al-Qaradawi]], [[Malcolm X]], [[Ayatollah Khomeini]], [[Sayyid Qutb]], [[Muhammad Iqbal]].
