 '''Ferrera''' is a [[municipality]] of the [[district]] of [[Hinterrhein (district)|Hinterrhein]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Graubünden]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 ==History== 
 It was formed on 1 January 2008 through the merger of [[Ausserferrera]] and [[Innerferrera]].
