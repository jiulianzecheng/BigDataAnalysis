'''Smartism''' is a sect of [[Hinduism]] in which, in contrast with [[Shaivism]] and [[Vaishnavism]], one may worship multiple gods. < ref > {{Cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=2aqgTYlhLikC & q=Basham+1991|title=The Origins and Development of Classical Hinduism|last=Basham|first=Arthur Llewellyn|date=1991|publisher=Oxford University Press|isbn=978-0-19-507349-2|language=en}} < /ref >  It is often referred as a  " liberal "  sect and is passed along familial ties. It is a very small sect of Hinduism and has more importance on [[Hindu texts]]. < ref > {{Cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=kfyzAAAAQBAJ|title=The Religious Traditions of Asia: Religion, History, and Culture|last=Kitagawa|first=Joseph|date=2013-09-05|publisher=Routledge|isbn=978-1-136-87597-7|language=en}} < /ref >
