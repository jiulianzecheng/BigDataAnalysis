 '''Yasukand''' ({{lang-ku|Yasûkend}}, {{lang-fa|یاسوکند}}) is a city in the [[Kurdistan]] province in [[Iran]]. Its people speak [[Kurdish language|Kurdish]]. They are followers of [[Islam]].
