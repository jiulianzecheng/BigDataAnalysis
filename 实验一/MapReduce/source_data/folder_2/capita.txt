 '''Capita plc''', commonly known as '''Capita''', is an international [[business process outsourcing]] and [[professional services]] company headquartered in [[London]]. 
 
 It is the largest [[Outsourcing|business process outsourcing]] and professional services company in the UK, with an overall market share of 29% in 2016, and has clients in central government, local government and the private sector. < ref name= " annualrep " > {{cite web|title=The Capita Group Plc Annual Report and Accounts 2016|url=http://investors.capita.com/~/media/Files/C/Capita-IR-V2/documents/capita-annual-report-2016.pdf|access-date=22 November 2017|publisher=The Capita Group Plc|archive-date=19 February 2018|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180219141038/http://investors.capita.com/~/media/Files/C/Capita-IR-V2/documents/capita-annual-report-2016.pdf|url-status=dead}} < /ref >  Roughly half of its turnover comes from the private sector and half from the public sector. Whilst UK-focused, Capita also has operations across Europe, Africa and Asia. < ref > {{cite web|title=Capita facts and stats – year end 2009|url=http://www.capita.co.uk/investors/Documents/FactsStats2009.pdf|url-status=dead|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20100924175200/http://capita.co.uk/investors/Documents/FactsStats2009.pdf|archive-date=24 September 2010|access-date=30 August 2010|publisher=The Capita Group Plc}} < /ref > 
 
 It is listed on the [[London Stock Exchange]] and is a constituent of the [[FTSE 250 Index]]. 
 
 Capita was founded in 1984, as a part of the non-profit [[CIPFA]] (Chartered Institute of Public Finance and Accountancy). < ref > {{cite web|title=Capita - Company Summary|url=http://www.londonstockexchange.com/exchange/prices-and-markets/stocks/summary/company-summary/GB00B23K0M20GBGBXSET1.html|url-status=dead|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160309073317/http://www.londonstockexchange.com/exchange/prices-and-markets/stocks/summary/company-summary/GB00B23K0M20GBGBXSET1.html|archive-date=9 March 2016|access-date=20 March 2016|website=[[London Stock Exchange]]}} < /ref >
