 '''Wilhermsdorf''' is a [[Municipalities of Germany|municipality]]  in [[Fürth (district)|Fürth]] in [[Bavaria]] in [[Germany]]. As of 2020 it has a population of 5,479. It is [[Twin town|twinned]] with [[Feld am See]] in Austria and [[Jahnsdorf]], also in Germany.
