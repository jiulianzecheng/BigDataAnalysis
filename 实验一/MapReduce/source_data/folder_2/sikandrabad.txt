'''Sikandrabad''' is a [[city]] in [[India]]. In 2011, about 385.000 people lived there. The city is located in [[Uttar Pradesh]]. It is about {{convert|76|km}} from [[Delhi]] and part of India's national capital region. The capital of Uttar Pradesh, [[Lucknow]], is about {{convert|504|km}} away.
