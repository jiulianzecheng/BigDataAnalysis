 '''Kingstown''' is the [[capital city]] of [[Saint Vincent and the Grenadines]]. The population of Kingstown as of 2008 is 24,518. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://population.mongabay.com/population/saint-vincent-and-the-grenadines/3577887/kingstown|title=Population of Kingstown, Saint Vincent and the Grenadines|date=2008|work=Mongabay.com|accessdate=2009-11-16|archive-date=2010-06-09|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20100609203638/http://population.mongabay.com/population/saint-vincent-and-the-grenadines/3577887/kingstown|url-status=dead}} < /ref >  Kingstown exports [[bananas]], [[Coconut palm|coconuts]], and arrowroot. Kingstown was started as a settlement by the [[France|French]] a little after 1722. The botanical garden in Kingstown is one of the oldest ones in the [[Western Hemisphere]]. < ref > http://www.bgci.org/garden.php?id=314 < /ref >
