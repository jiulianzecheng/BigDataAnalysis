 '''Landes''' ([[Gascon language|Gascon]]: ''Lanas'') is a [[departments of France|department]] in the [[Nouvelle-Aquitaine]] [[region]] of [[France]]. Landes is the plural of the [[French language|French]] word ''lande'' (in English:''[[heath]]land''); it was named so because it was the most common [[habitat]] in the region. 
 
 It is well known for the ''Côte d'Argent'' beach that attracts many [[Surfing|surfers]] to [[Mimizan]] and [[Hossegor]] each year. 
 
 ==History== 
 The department was formed during the [[French Revolution]] on 4 March 1790 with parts of the old [[province]]s of [[Guyenne]] and [[Gascony]]. It was divided into four [[district]]s: Dax, Mont-de-Marsan, Saint-Sever and Tartas. The [[Capital (city)|capital]] was Mont-de-Marsan. 
 
 The ''arrondissement'' of Dax, Mont-de-Marsan and Saint-Sever were established on 17 February 1800. The ''arrondissement'' of Saint-Sever was dissolved on 10 September 1926 and integrated into the ''arrondissements'' of Dax and Mont-de-Marsan. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 Landes is part of the [[Nouvelle-Aquitaine]] region, southwestern France, and is, after [[Gironde]], the second largest department of [[Metropolitan France]] with an area of {{Convert|9242.6|km2|sqmi|0|abbr=on }}. < ref name= " insee-geo "  / > 
 
 The department is bordered to the west by the [[Atlantic Ocean]], to the north by the [[Gironde]], to the northeast by [[Lot-et-Garonne]] and the south by the [[Pyrénées-Atlantiques]]; these three departments are, like Landes, of the [[Nouvelle-Aquitaine]] region. To the east, it borders with the [[Gers]] department of the [[Occitanie]] region.

 The most important river is the [[Adour]], which crosses the southern part of the department in an east-west direction; in [[Dax, Landes|Dax]], the river turns to the southwest and is joined by the [[Gaves réunis]] before flowing into the Atlantic Ocean. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://services.sandre.eaufrance.fr/Courdo/Fiche/client/fiche_courdo.php?CdSandre=Q---0000 |title=L'Adour (Q---0000) |publisher=SANDRE - Portail national d'accès aux référentiels sur l'eau |language=French |accessdate=12 March 2015 |date= }} < /ref > 
 
 The [[forest]] of Landes is the largest forest in France. It covers about 67% of the department in its northern part, and also covers a large part of the [[Gironde]] and [[Lot-et-Garonne]] ([[arrondissement of Nérac]]). The main [[species]] of tree is the maritime pine (''[[Pinus pinaster]]''). 
 
 === Climate === 
 The climate in Mont-de-Marsan is [[Oceanic climate|Marine West Coast Climate]] ([[Köppen climate classification]] '''''Cfb'''''), with mild winters and warm summers. < ref name= " wbase " > {{cite web|work= |publisher=Weatherbase |title=Mont-de-Marsan, France - Köppen Climate Classification |url=http://www.weatherbase.com/weather/weather-summary.php3?s=598821 & cityname=Mont-de-Marsan%2C+Aquitaine%2C+France & units= |language= |accessdate=12 March 2015}} < /ref > 
 
 The average temperature for the year in Mont-de-Marsan is {{Convert|13.5|°C|°F }}. The warmest month, on average, is July with an average temperature of {{Convert|21.4|°C|°F }}. The coolest month on average is January, with an average temperature of {{Convert|6.2|°C|°F }}. 
 
 The average amount of precipitation for the year in Mont-de-Marsan is {{Convert|693.4|mm|in|1|abbr=on}}. The month with the most precipitation on average is October with {{Convert|71.1|mm|in|1|abbr=on}} of precipitation. The month with the least precipitation on average is August with an average of {{Convert|40.6|mm|in|1|abbr=on}}. < ref name= " wbase "  / > 
 
 ==Administration== 
 The department is managed by the ''Departmental Council of the Landes'' in [[Mont-de-Marsan]].

 ==Demographics== 
 The inhabitants of Landes are known, in [[French language|French]], as ''Landais'' (women: ''Landaises''). < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.habitants.fr/habitants_departement_landes_40.html|title=Landes (40)|publisher=habitants.fr |language=French |accessdate=12 March 2015 |date= }} < /ref > 
 
 The department of Landes has a population, in 2014, of 400,477, < ref name=insee / >  for a population density of {{Pop density|400477|9242.6|km2|prec=1|disp=num}} inhabitants/km < sup > 2 < /sup > . The ''arrondissement'' of Dax has 220,656 inhabitants and the ''arrondissement'' of Mont-de-Marsan, where is the prefecture of the departement, has only 179,821 inhabitants. < ref name=insee2 / >
