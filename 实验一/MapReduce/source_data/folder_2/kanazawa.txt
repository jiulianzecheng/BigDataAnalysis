 ==History== 
 During the [[Edo period]], Kanazawa was the center of the Maeda clan. < ref name= " nussbaum " / > 
 
 Kanazawa has a big [[Japanese garden]] called [[Kenroku-en]]. Kenroku-en was made as a garden of the Kanazawa castle at the [[Edo period]].  It is regarded as one of the most famous gardens in Japan. < ref > Nussbaum, [https://books.google.com/books?id=p2QnPijAEmEC & pg=PA510 & dq=  " ''Kenroku-en'' "  at p. 510]; other famous gardens are [[Kairaku-en]] in [[Mito, Ibaraki|Mito]] and [[Koraku-en]] in [[Okayama]]. < /ref >   
 
 ==Geography== 
 Kanazawa is on the western coast of Honshu. < ref name= " nussbaum " / >   It faces the [[Sea of Japan]].
