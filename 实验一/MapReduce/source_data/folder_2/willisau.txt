 '''Willisau''' is a [[town]] of the [[district]] of [[Willisau (district)|Willisau]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Lucerne (canton)|Lucerne]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 [[File:Willisau untertor.jpg|thumb|left|200px|Unteres Tor]] 
 
 ==History== 
 On 1 January 2006, the former municipalities of [[Willisau Land]] (W. Country) and [[Willisau Stadt]] (W. Town) merged into the new town of Willisau.
