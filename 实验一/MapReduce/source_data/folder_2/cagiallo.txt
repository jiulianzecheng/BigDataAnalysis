'''Cagiallo''' is a village and former [[municipality]] of the [[district]] [[Lugano (district)|Lugano]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Ticino]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 On 15 October 2001 the former municipalities of Cagiallo, [[Lopagno]], [[Roveredo, Ticino|Roveredo]], [[Sala Capriasca]], [[Tesserete]] and [[Vaglio]] merged to form the new municipality [[Capriasca]]. < ref name=Fusion > [http://www.bfs.admin.ch/bfs/portal/de/index/infothek/nomenklaturen/blank/blank/gem_liste/03.html Amtliches Gemeindeverzeichnis der Schweiz] published by the Swiss Federal Statistical Office {{in lang|de}} accessed 14 January 2010 < /ref >
