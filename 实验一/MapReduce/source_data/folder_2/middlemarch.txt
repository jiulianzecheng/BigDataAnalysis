 '''''Middlemarch, A Study of Provincial Life''''' is the best-known book written by [[English people|English]] [[author]] [[George Eliot]]. It was first published in eight volumes during 1871–72. The novel is set in the fictional [[The Midlands|Midlands]] town of Middlemarch during 1829–32. < ref > ''The Bloomsbury Guide to English Literature'', ed. Marion Wynne–Davies. New York: Prentice Hall, 1990, p. 719.
