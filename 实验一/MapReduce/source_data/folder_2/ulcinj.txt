 '''Ulcinj''' ({{lang-sr-Cyrl|Улцињ}}, {{lang-sq|Ulqin}} or ''Ulqini'', {{lang-it|Dulcigno}}, {{lang-tr|Ülgün}}) is a town on the southern coast of [[Montenegro]]. In 2011, 10,707 people lived there.
