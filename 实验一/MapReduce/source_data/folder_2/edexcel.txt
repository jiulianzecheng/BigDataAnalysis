 '''Edexcel''' is a [[United Kingdom|UK]] [[examination board]]. It is one of [[England]], [[Wales]] and [[Northern Ireland]]'s five main examination boards. Edexcel is owned by the private-sector [[Pearson PLC]].  The company offers many qualifications including [[A-level|A level]]s and [[GCSE]]s. Edexcel's name is from the words ''education'' and ''excellence''.
