 '''Mund''' was a [[municipality]] in the district of [[Brig (district)|Brig]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Valais]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 On 1 January 2013, the former municipalities of Mund and [[Birgisch]] merged into the municipality of [[Naters]].
