 '''Goes''', [[Zeelandic dialect|Zeelandic]]: ''Goes'' or ''Hoes'', is a municipality and town on the former island of [[Zuid-Beveland]], Province of [[Zeeland]], the [[Netherlands]]. 
 
 About 37,000 people were living in the municipality in 2017. < br/  > 
 About 27,000 people were living in the town in 2010. 
 
 Goes has had [[city rights]] since 1405.
