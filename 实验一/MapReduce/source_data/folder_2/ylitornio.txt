 '''Ylitornio''' ({{lang-sv|Övertorneå}}) is a [[municipality]] in [[Finnish Lapland|Lapland]], [[Finland]]. As of 31 December 2020, the municipality had a population of 3,890. Neighbouring municipalities are [[Pello]], [[Rovaniemi]], [[Tervola]] and [[Tornio]]. Bordering Ylitornio in Sweden is [[Övertorneå Municipality|Övertorneå]].
