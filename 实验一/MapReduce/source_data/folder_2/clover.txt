 '''Clover''' ('''''Trifolium''''') is a [[genus]] of about 300 [[species]] of [[plant]]s in the pea family. It grows worldwide, mainly in the temperate [[Northern Hemisphere]], but many species also in [[South America]] and [[Africa]], including at high altitudes on mountains in the tropics. 
 
 They are small [[Herbaceous plant|herbaceous]] plants. It has three sections to each leave, which looks like three leaves. This is called trifoliate. 
 
 Clover is often associated with [[Ireland]].
