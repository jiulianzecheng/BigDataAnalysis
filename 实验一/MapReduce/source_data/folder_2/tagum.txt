 '''Tagum''', officially the '''City of Tagum''' ({{lang-ceb|Dakbayan sa Tagum}}; {{lang-fil|Lungsod ng Tagum}}) is a city in the [[Philippines]]. It is the capital of the province of [[Davao del Norte]]. In 2020, 296,202 people lived there.{{PH census|current}}
