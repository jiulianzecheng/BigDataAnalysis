 '''Birgu''' ({{lang-mt|Il-Birgu}} {{IPA-mt|ɪlˈbɪrɡʊ|}}, {{lang-it|Vittoriosa}}), also known by its title '''Città Vittoriosa''', is an old city on the south side of the [[Grand Harbour]] in the [[South Eastern Region]] of [[Malta]]. In the 1950s, it was the ''de-facto'' capital city of Malta.
