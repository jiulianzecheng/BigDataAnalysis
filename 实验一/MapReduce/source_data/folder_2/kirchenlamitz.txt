 '''Kirchenlamitz''' ({{IPA-de|kɪʁçn̩ˈlaːmɪt͡s|lang|De-Kirchenlamitz.ogg}}) is a [[Town#Germany|town]] in [[Wunsiedel (district)|Wunsiedel]], in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. It is in the [[Fichtelgebirge]], 13 & nbsp;km northwest of [[Wunsiedel]] and 19 & nbsp;km south of [[Hof, Germany|Hof]].
