 '''Gourds''' are from the [[pumpkin]]  family. They are about 15 to 12 inches long. Gourds are mostly found in the [[United States]] or in [[Mexico]]. Their shape is like a [[Squash (plant)|squash]]. Their color is [[Orange (color)|orange]], [[brown]], or [[green]]. It is eaten in curries, spaghetti, tacos, and salad.
