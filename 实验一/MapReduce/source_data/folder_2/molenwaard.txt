 '''Molenwaard''' is a former [[List of municipalities of the Netherlands|municipality]] in the [[Netherlands|Dutch]] [[Provinces of the Netherlands|province]] of [[South Holland]]. 
 
 It was created on 1 January 2013 from the former municipalities of [[Graafstroom]], [[Liesveld]] and [[Nieuw-Lekkerland]]. Since 2019, it has been part of the new municipality of [[Molenlanden]].
