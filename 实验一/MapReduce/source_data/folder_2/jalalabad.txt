 '''Jalalabad ''' ({{lang-ps|جلال اباد}}) is a city in eastern [[Afghanistan]]. Jalalabad is the capital of [[Nangarhar Province]]. By road, it is about {{convert|90|mi|km}} from [[Kabul]] to the west. It is also about the same distance from [[Peshawar]] in [[Pakistan]] to the east. About 360.000 people live there. 
 
 Although most of the people of Jalalabad are [[Pashtun]], a few [[Dari]]-speaking [[Tajiks]] live there, too. [[Sunni Islam]] is the main religion of the people.
