 An '''appliance''' is a [[machine]] that only has limited functionality. A [[prosthesis]] is an example. Many appliances need [[electricity]] to work. 
 *''Household appliances'' can be used for cleaning (like the [[vacuum cleaner]]) or cooking. If they are easy to carry around, they are ''small appliances.'' 
 *''Major appliances'' are big machines like [[dishwasher]]s and [[refrigerator]]s. 
 *A ''Fire appliance'' is the name of a [[fire engine]] the [[firefighter]]s use to extinguish a [[fire]]
