 '''Alvaneu''' is a former [[municipality]] in the [[district]] of [[Albula (district)|Albula]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Graubünden]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 It consists of Alvaneu-Dorf and Alvaneu-Bad. The [[area]] is [[1 E7 m²|35.68 km²]] and the [[population]] is 421 people. < ref > {{cite web |title=Mountain village converts to a princedom |url=https://www.swissinfo.ch/eng/mountain-village-converts-to-a-princedom/5791878 |website=SWI swissinfo.ch |access-date=8 January 2021 |language=en}} < /ref > 
 
 On 1 January 2015 the former municipalities of Alvaneu, [[Alvaschein]], [[Brienz/Brinzauls]], [[Mon, Switzerland|Mon]], [[Stierva]], [[Surava]] and [[Tiefencastel]] merged into the new municipality of [[Albula/Alvra]].
