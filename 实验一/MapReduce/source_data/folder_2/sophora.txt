 '''''Sophora''''' is a [[genus]] of about 45 [[species]] of small [[tree]]s and [[shrub]]s. It is in the [[subfamily]] ''Faboideae'' of the [[pea]] [[family (biology)|family]] ''[[Fabaceae]]''. Many [[student]]s see Sophora as three genera: ''Sophora'', ''[[Calia]]'' (S. secundiflora) and ''Styphnolobium'' ([[Styphnolobium|S. japonica]]).
