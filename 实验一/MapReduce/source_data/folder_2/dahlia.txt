 '''Dahlia''' is a genus of [[plant]]s with large brightly-colored flowers. They are from [[Mexico]], [[Central America]], and [[Colombia]]. There are at least 36 [[species]] of Dahlia. Dahlia are often grown in flower gardens. This flower grows well away from strong, direct sunlight.
