 '''Hiltenfingen''' ({{Lang-gsw|Hiltefenge|label=[[Swabian German|Swabian]]}} < ref > {{Cite journal|date=2020-01-15|title=Hiltefenge|url=https://als.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Hiltenfingen & oldid=949587|journal=Alemannische Wikipedia}} < /ref > ) is a [[municipality]] in the [[Augsburg (district)|district of Augsburg]] in [[Bavaria]] in [[Germany]]. It is part of the administrative region of [[Swabia (Bavaria)|Swabia]] and of the ''[[Verwaltungsgemeinschaft]]'' of Langerringen. 
 
 It is located on the river [[Wertach (river)|Wertach]], about 30 km south of [[Augsburg]] and one kilometer southwest of [[Schwabmünchen]].
