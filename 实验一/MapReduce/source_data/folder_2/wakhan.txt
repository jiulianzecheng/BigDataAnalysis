'''Wakhan''' or  " the Wakhan "  (also spelt '''Vakhan'''; [[Persian language|Persian]] and واخان, is a region of [[Afghanistan]]. It is part of the [[Pamir Mountains]] and the [[Karakoram Range]]. Not very many people live there.
