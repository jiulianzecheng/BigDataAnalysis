 A '''bookstore''' is a [[Shop|store]] that sells [[book]]s, and where people can buy them. A '''used bookstore''' or '''second-hand bookshop''' sells and often buys used books. 
 
 Some modern bookstore combine the books (including digital books) and coffee or CDs.
