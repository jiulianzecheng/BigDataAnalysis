 '''Bohuslav''' ({{lang-uk|Богуслав}}) is a city in [[Kyiv Oblast]] ([[Oblast|province]]) of [[Ukraine]], on the [[Ros (river)|Ros River]]. It is the [[administrative centre]] of [[Bohuslav Raion]] ([[Raion|district]]). In 2020, 16,190 people lived there.
