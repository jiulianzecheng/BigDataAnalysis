 '''Surava''' was a [[municipality]] of the [[district]] [[Albula (district)|Albula]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Graubünden]] in [[Switzerland]]. On 1 January 2015, the former municipalities of [[Alvaschein]], [[Mon, Switzerland|Mon]], [[Stierva]], [[Tiefencastel]], [[Alvaneu]], [[Brienz/Brinzauls]] and Surava merged to form the new municipality of [[Albula/Alvra]].
