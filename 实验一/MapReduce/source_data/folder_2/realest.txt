 " '''Realest''' "  is a song by [[Filipinos|Filipino]]-[[Americans|American]] [[Rap music|rapper]] [[Ez Mil]] and American rapper [[Eminem]]. The song was released on August 4, 2023. The song was the first single on the deluxe version of EZ Mil's third [[studio album]] ''[[DU4LI7Y]]''. It is the first single by EZ Mil after signing with [[Shady Records|Shady]], [[Aftermath Entertainment|Aftermath]], and [[Interscope Records|Interscope]]. 
 
 The song was able to make it to #3 on the New Zealand Hot Singles, #25 on the ''[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]'' [[Bubbling Under Hot 100]] and #95 on the [[Canadian Hot 100]] charts. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://nztop40.co.nz/chart/hotsingles?chart=5638|accessdate=August 16, 2023|title=Hot 40 Singles (14 August 2023)|publisher=[[Recorded Music NZ]]}} < /ref > < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.billboard.com/artist/Eminem/chart-history/HBU|accessdate=August 16, 2023|title=Eminem - Bubbling Under Hot 100 Singles Chart History|publisher=[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]}} < /ref > < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.billboard.com/artist/Eminem/chart-history/CAN|accessdate=August 16, 2023|title=Eminem - Canadian Hot 100 Chart History|publisher=[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]}} < /ref > 
 
 In the song, EZ raps about how he is real and aims at the people who are jealous of his success and signing. During Eminem's verse, he responds to people who have [[Diss (music)|dissed]] him in the past and criticized him because he is white. Eminem disses [[The Game (rapper)|The Game]] (who said they don't play Eminem's songs in nightclubs), [[Lord Jamar]] (who said he was a guest in hip hop), [[Melle Mel]] (calling Mel a  " juicehead "  ([[Anabolic steroid|steroid]] user)), and [[Generation Z]] (saying they should stick to [[School shooting|shooting up schools]]). < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.xxlmag.com/eminem-the-game-melle-mel-diss-track/|accessdate=August 16, 2023|title=Eminem Goes at The Game and Melle Mel on Scathing Diss Track|publisher=[[XXL (magazine)|XXL]]}} < /ref > < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.thefader.com/2023/08/04/eminem-disses-the-game-melle-mel-and-gen-z-on-ez-mils-realest|accessdate=August 16, 2023|title=Eminem disses The Game, Melle Mel, and Gen Z on EZ Mil’s “Realest " |publisher=[[The Fader]]}} < /ref >  Melle Mel released a response but it was very disliked. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.complex.com/music/a/markelibert/melle-mel-responds-to-eminem-with-new-diss-track|accessdate=August 16, 2023|title=Melle Mel Responds to Eminem With New Diss Track: 'Why They Put Pee-Wee Herman In The Hall Of Fame'|publisher=[[Complex Networks|Complex]]}} < /ref >  Mel later took his diss track down and apologized to Eminem. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://hiphopdx.com/news/melle-mel-regret-eminem-diss-track|accessdate=August 16, 2023|title=Melle Mel Regrets Making Eminem Diss Track, Says His Execution ‘Fell Short’|publisher=[[HipHopDX]]}} < /ref >
