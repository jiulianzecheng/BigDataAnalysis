 '''Roundhay''' is an upmarket area of [[Leeds]], best known for its large park, [[Roundhay Park]].  Roundhay has many shops, pubs and restaurants on Street Lane.  Roundhay also has historic landmarks within the park such as Roundhay Mansion and Roundhay Castle.  Roundhay is in North Leeds, close to the Outer Ring Road.
