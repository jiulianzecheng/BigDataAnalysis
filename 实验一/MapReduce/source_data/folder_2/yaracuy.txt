 The state capital is [[San Felipe, Yaracuy|San Felipe de Yaracuy]]. Other important towns are Aroa, Chivacoa, Cocorote, Marín, Nirgua, Sabana de Parra, Yaritagua, and Urachiche. 
 
 Yaracuy State covers a total surface area of 7,100 & nbsp;km². Population: 597,700 inhabitants (2007 est).
