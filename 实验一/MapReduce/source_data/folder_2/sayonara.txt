 '''''Sayonara''''' is a 1957 American drama movie directed by [[Joshua Logan]] and based on the 1954 novel of the same name by [[James Michener]]. It stars [[Marlon Brando]], [[Patricia Owens]], [[James Garner]], [[Martha Scott]], [[Red Buttons]], [[Miiko Taka]], [[Miyoshi Umeki]], [[Douglass Watson]] and was distributed by [[Warner Bros.]]. It was nominated for 10 Oscars and won 4 Academy Awards in 1958.
