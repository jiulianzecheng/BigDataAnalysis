In [[Greek mythology]], '''Iphicles''' ({{lang-grc|Ἰφικλῆς}}, ''Iphikles'') was the half brother of [[Herakles]]. His parents were [[Alkmene]] and her husband [[Amphitryon]]. His wife was Automedusa. Their son [[Iolaos|Iolaus]] was Herakles' [[chariot]]eer.
