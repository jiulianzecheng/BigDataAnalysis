 '''WRKI''' (95.1 [[Hertz|MHz]],  " I-95 " ) is an American commercial FM radio station licensed in [[Brookfield, Connecticut]], and serves [[Fairfield County, Connecticut|Fairfield]], [[Litchfield County, Connecticut|Litchfield]] and [[New Haven County, Connecticut|New Haven]] Counties in [[Connecticut]]. From its tower at more than 1200 feet above sea level (HAAT:194 m./674 & nbsp;ft.) WRKI's 29,500 [[watt]] signal can be heard in such large Connecticut cities as [[Bridgeport, Connecticut|Bridgeport]], [[Danbury, Connecticut|Danbury]], [[Stamford, Connecticut|Stamford]] and [[New Haven, Connecticut|New Haven]].
