[[Image:Maple Leaves Koyo and Shamisen by Kitagawa Utamaro c1803.png|thumb|220px|[[Geisha]] playing the shamisen, [[1800]]]] 
 The '''shamisen''' or '''samisen''' ([[Japanese language|Japanese]]: 三味線, [[literal translation|literally]]  " three taste [[string]]s " ), also called '''sangen''' (literally  " three strings " ) is a [[Japan|Japanese]] [[musical instrument]] with three strings, that is played with a big [[plectrum]] called a ''bachi''. The pronunciation in [[Japanese language|Japanese]] is usually  " shamisen " .
