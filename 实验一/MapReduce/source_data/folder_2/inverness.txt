 '''Inverness''' is a [[city]] in the northern part of [[Scotland]]. It is often called the [[capital (city)|capital]] of the [[Highlands]]. It is on the A9 [[road]]. [[Inverness Airport]] is an important way to get to the [[Scottish Highlands]].
