A '''barangay''' is a local term for an [[administrative division]] in the Philippines. It is the smallest administrative division in the Philippines. As of 2011, it is recorded that there are a total of 42,026 barangays throughout the Philippines. < ref > {{cite web|title=NUMBER OF PROVINCES, CITIES MUNICIPALITIES AND BARANGAYS, BY REGION|url=http://www.nscb.gov.ph/activestats/psgc/NSCB_PSGC_SUMMARY_Dec06.pdf|access-date=9 March 2013|archive-date=15 June 2007|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20070615010416/http://www.nscb.gov.ph/activestats/psgc/NSCB_PSGC_SUMMARY_Dec06.pdf|url-status=dead}} < /ref >
