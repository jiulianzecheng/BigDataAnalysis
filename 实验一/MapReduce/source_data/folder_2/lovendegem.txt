 '''Lovendegem''' is a former [[municipality]] in the [[Belgium|Belgian]] province of [[East Flanders]]. On 1 January 2019, Lovendegem, [[Waarschoot]] and [[Zomergem]] were merged into the new municipality of [[Lievegem]]. 
 
 In 2007, 9393 people lived there. < ref > {{Cite web |url=http://statbel.fgov.be/downloads/pop200701com.xls |title=Werkelijke bevolking per gemeente op 1 januari 2007 / Population de droit par commune au 1 janvier 2007 (.xls) |access-date=2008-07-15 |archive-date=2009-02-06 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20090206001542/http://statbel.fgov.be/downloads/pop200701com.xls |url-status=dead }} < /ref > 
 
 It is at 51° 05 North, 03° 36 East. < ref > {{Cite web |url=http://statbel.fgov.be/pub/d1/p120_nl.xls |title=Geografische coördinaten van de gemeenten (.xls) |access-date=2008-07-15 |archive-date=2008-11-24 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20081124205332/http://www.statbel.fgov.be/pub/d1/p120_nl.xls |url-status=dead }} < /ref >
