 '''Igling''' is a municipality in [[Landsberg (district)|Landsberg]] in [[Bavaria]] in [[Germany]]. 
 
 ==Subdivisions== 
 The municipality Igling consists of the subdivisions (''Ortsteile'') Holzhausen, Unterigling and Oberigling.
