 '''''Annona''''' is a genus of plants in the [[Annonaceae]] family. It contains species like the [[custard apple]] and [[sugar apple]]. It is the second-largest genus in its family after ''[[Guatteria]]''. < ref > {{Cite web|title=ITIS Standard Report Page: Annona|url=https://www.itis.gov/servlet/SingleRpt/SingleRpt?search_topic=TSN & search_value=18095#null|access-date=2020-08-13|website=www.itis.gov}} < /ref >  It has about 166 < ref > {{Cite web|title=Annona—The Plant List|url=http://www.theplantlist.org/browse/A/Annonaceae/Annona/|access-date=2020-08-13|website=www.theplantlist.org}} < /ref >  species of shrubs and trees. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Annona in Flora of North America @ efloras.org|url=http://www.efloras.org/florataxon.aspx?flora_id=1 & taxon_id=101891|access-date=2020-08-13|website=www.efloras.org}} < /ref >  Its name comes from ''anón'', a [[Hispaniolan Taíno]] word for it. < ref name= " Austin " > {{cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=eS7lX_rC3GEC|title=Florida Ethnobotany|last=Austin|first=Daniel F.|publisher=CRC Press|year=2004|isbn=978-0-8493-2332-4|page=95}} < /ref >
