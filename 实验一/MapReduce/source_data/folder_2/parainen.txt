 '''Parainen''', ''Pargas'', is a [[city]] in [[Southwest Finland]]. About 15,500 people lived there in January 2014. The municipalities next to it are [[Brändö]], [[Sottunga]], [[Kökar]], [[Kemiönsaari]], [[Sauvo]], [[Kaarina]], [[Turku]], [[Naantali]] and [[Kustavi]]. 
 
 It was created as Väståboland on 1 January 2009, when the former municipalities of [[Pargas (former municipality)|Pargas]], [[Nagu, Finland|Nagu]], [[Korpo]], [[Houtskär]] and [[Iniö]] were merged into a single municipality. 
 
 Most people speak [[Swedish language|Swedish]].
