 '''Ugley''' is a village and [[civil parish]] in [[Uttlesford]], [[Essex]], England. In 2001 there were 402 people living in Ugley. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.ons.gov.uk/help/localstatistics|title=Local statistics - Office for National Statistics|website=www.ons.gov.uk}} < /ref >  Ugley has a church called St Peter. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.essexchurches.info/church.aspx?p=Ugley|title=St Peter, Ugley Church, Essex|website=www.essexchurches.info}} < /ref >
