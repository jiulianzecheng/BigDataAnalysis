 '''Talca''' is a [[Chile]]an city with 201.797 inhabitants (2002 census). 
 
 == History == 
 Talca was founded like ''Villa San Agustín de Talca'' by the [[Spain|Spanish]] [[José Antonio Manso de Velasco]] on 1881.
