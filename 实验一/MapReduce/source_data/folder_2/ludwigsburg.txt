 '''Ludwigsburg''' is a city in the center of [[Baden-Württemberg]]. It is about {{convert|12|km|abbr=off}} from the [[Stuttgart]] city center. It is the district town and largest city of the [[Ludwigsburg (district)|Ludwigsburg]] district, as well as after [[Esslingen]] at the [[Neckar]] the second largest medium-sized town of Baden-Wuerttemberg. 
 
 Together with [[Kornwestheim]], Ludwigsburg forms a central center for the surrounding municipalities. Since 1 April 1956, Ludwigsburg is a large district town.
