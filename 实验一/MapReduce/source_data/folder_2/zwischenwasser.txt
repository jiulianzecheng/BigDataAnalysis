 '''Zwischenwasser''' is a municipality of the district [[Feldkirch (district)|Feldkirch]] in the [[Austria]]n state of [[Vorarlberg]]. As of 1 January 2019, 3,257 people lived there. 
 
 == Geography == 
 Zwischenwasser has an elevation of {{convert|525|m|abbr=on}}. Because of its location between two rivers Frutz and Frödisch. The highest mountain is [[Hoher Freschen]] ({{convert|2004|m|abbr=on}}). It is next to the following municipalities: [[Dornbirn]], [[Laterns]], [[Rankweil]], [[Röthis]], [[Sulz, Vorarlberg|Sulz]] and [[Viktorsberg]]. 
 
 The municipality has three villages, Muntlix the largest part of Zwischenwasser, Batschuns and Dafins.
