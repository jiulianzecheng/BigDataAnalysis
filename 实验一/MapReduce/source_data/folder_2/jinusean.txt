 '''Jinusean''' is a South Korean hip-hop duo formed by YG Entertainment in 1997, and is known as YG Entertainments 1st major k-pop group. The group's debut song,  " Tell Me "  brought the group instant fame. After their album ''Nora Bossa'', in 2004, the group became inactive until 2015 when they returned with the single,  " Tell Me One More Time, "  performed with (then) YG trainee, Jang Hana.
