 '''Saulzoir''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is in the [[Nord (French department)|Nord]] [[Departments of France|department]] in north [[France]]. 
 
 It is on the river [[Selle (Scheldt tributary)|Selle]]. About 1700 people lived here in 2019. The commune was the site of the [[Battle of the Sabis]] between [[Julius Caesar]] and the [[Nervii|Nervians]]. < ref > Pierre Turquin ( " La Bataille de la Selle (du Sabis) en l' An 57 avant J.-C. "  in Les Études Classiques 23/2 (1955), 113-156) has proved beyond reasonable doubt that the battle was fought at the river [[Selle (Scheldt tributary)|Selle]]. < /ref >
