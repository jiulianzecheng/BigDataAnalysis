 '''Withington''' is a district of the city of [[Manchester]], in [[North West England]]. It is about four miles south of Manchester city centre, and intersected by the busy thoroughfare of [[Wilmslow Road, Manchester|Wilmslow Road]]. It is between [[Fallowfield]] and [[Didsbury]], to the north and south respectively.
