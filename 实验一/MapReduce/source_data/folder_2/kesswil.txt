 '''Kesswil''' is a [[municipality]] of the [[district]] of [[Arbon (district)|Arbon]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Thurgau]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 The [[village]] was the birthplace of the [[psychiatrist]] [[Carl Jung]].
