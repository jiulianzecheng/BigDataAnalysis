 '''Fordham''' is a village and [[civil parish]] in [[Colchester borough]], [[Essex]], England. In 2001 there were 818 people living in Fordham. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.ons.gov.uk/help/localstatistics|title=Local statistics - Office for National Statistics|website=www.ons.gov.uk}} < /ref >  Fordham has a church called All Saints, 2 pubs, one called Shoulder of Mutton and the other called Three Horse Shoes. Fordham also has a village store, which includes the post office. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.camulos.com/fordham.htm|title=Fordham Village Home Page|website=www.camulos.com|access-date=2013-06-14|archive-date=2012-02-29|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120229085828/http://www.camulos.com/fordham.htm|url-status=dead}} < /ref >
