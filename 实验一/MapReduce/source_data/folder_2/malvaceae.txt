 '''Malvaceae''' is a [[family (biology)|family]] of [[flowering plant]]s. The family includes about 1000 [[species]] in 75 [[Genus|genera]]. They are found in [[tropics|tropical]] and [[temperate]] regions. They are not found very often in places such as Antarctica because those places are too cold for Malvaceae plants.
