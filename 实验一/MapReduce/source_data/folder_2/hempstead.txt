 '''Hempstead''' is a village and [[civil parish]] in [[Uttlesford]], [[Essex]], England. In 2001 there were 422 people living in Hempstead. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.ons.gov.uk/help/localstatistics|title=Local statistics - Office for National Statistics|website=www.ons.gov.uk}} < /ref >  It is 7 miles east of [[Saffron Walden]]. < ref > http://www.hempstead-essex.org.uk/ < /ref >
