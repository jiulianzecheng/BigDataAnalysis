 '''Tarlac''' ({{lang-tl|Lalawigan ng Tarlac}}) is a [[landlocked]] [[Provinces of the Philippines|province]] of the [[Philippines]] in [[Central Luzon]]. Tarlac is surrounded by [[Pampanga]] at the south, [[Nueva Ecija]] at the east, [[Pangasinan]] at the north and [[Zambales]] at the west. It is a part of [[Central Luzon]]. Tarlac is populated by 1,273,240 people as of 2010. < ref > {{cite web|title=Total Population by Province, City, Municipality and Barangay:  as of May 1, 2010|url=https://www.census.gov.ph/sites/default/files/attachments/hsd/pressrelease/Central%20Luzon.pdf|publisher=Philippine Census|accessdate=16 March 2013|date=1 May 2010|archive-date=15 November 2012|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20121115103249/http://www.census.gov.ph/sites/default/files/attachments/hsd/pressrelease/Central%20Luzon.pdf|url-status=dead}} < /ref >
