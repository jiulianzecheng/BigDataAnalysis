 '''Toome''' (or '''Toomebridge''') is a village in [[Northern Ireland]], in the [[United Kingdom]]. It is {{convert|7|mi|km|0}} from [[Magherafelt]] and {{convert|11|mi|km|0}} from [[Antrim]]. It is close to the [[River Bann]]. 
 
 ==Population facts== 
 On [[29 April]] [[2001]] the United Kingdom government had a [[United Kingdom Census 2001|census]] and saw that there were 722 people living in Toome. They also saw that: 
 *27.2% of the people were under 16 years old and 10.9% were over 60 years old 
 *48.8% of the people were male and 51.3% were female 
 *96.3% of the people were from a [[Roman Catholicism|Roman Catholic]] background and 2.9% were from a [[Protestantism|Protestant]] background
