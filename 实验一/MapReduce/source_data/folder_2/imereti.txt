 '''Imereti''' is a [[Mkhare|region]] of [[Georgia (country)|Georgia]]. The [[capital city]] is [[Kutaisi]]. It is along the [[Rioni River]].

 *[[Tqibuli Municipality]]  
 *[[Chiatura Municipality]]

 *[[Khoni Municipality]]
