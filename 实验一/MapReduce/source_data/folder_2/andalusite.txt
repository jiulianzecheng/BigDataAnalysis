 '''Andalusite''' is an [[aluminium]] [[Silicate minerals|nesosilicate]] [[mineral]] with the chemical formula Al < sub > 2 < /sub > SiO < sub > 5 < /sub > .  At higher temperatures and pressures, andalusite may convert to [[sillimanite]]. Andalusite is an [[index mineral]]: it gives clues to the depth and pressures the host rock went through in the past.
