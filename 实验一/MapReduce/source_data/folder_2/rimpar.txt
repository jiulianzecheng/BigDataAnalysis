 '''Rimpar''' is a [[market town]] in [[Würzburg (district)|Würzburg]] in [[Bavaria]]. It is about 10 & nbsp;km (6 & nbsp;mi) north of the City of [[Würzburg]]. The municipality has the villages of Gramschatz and [[Maidbronn]], incorporated in 1978.
