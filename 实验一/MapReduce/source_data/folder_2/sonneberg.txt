 '''Sonneberg''' is a [[Germany|German]] [[city]] in the south of [[Thuringia]]. The nearest large city is [[Coburg]]. The [[population]] of Sonneberg is about 20,000 people. The [[mayor]] of the city is Sibylle Abel.
