 '''Bolsterlang''' is a [[Municipalities of Germany|municipality]] in the district of [[Oberallgäu]] in [[Bavaria]] in [[Germany]]. From 1988 to 2008 Bolsterlang grew by 178 inhabitants. On March 16, 2014, Monika Zeller was again elected Mayor of Bolsterlang.
