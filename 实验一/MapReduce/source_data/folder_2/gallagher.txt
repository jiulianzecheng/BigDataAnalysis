 '''Leo Anthony Gallagher, Jr.''' (July 24, 1946  & ndash; November 11, 2022), known [[Mononymous person|mononym]]ously as '''Gallagher''', was an [[United States|American]] [[comedian]]. He was best known for smashing [[watermelon]]s as part of his [[prop comedy]] act. 
 
 On November 11, 2022, Gallagher died of [[organ failure]] at age 76. < ref > {{cite web |url=https://variety.com/2022/tv/news/gallagher-dead-comedian-smashing-watermelons-1235430439/?fbclid=IwAR2aot1ZeMnrT6CWIMjr-FsQq4UeIzXtz7H4YOz_XHXFHEcqO7r60S9GtbM |title=Gallagher, Comedian Known for Smashing Watermelons, Dies at 76 |date=November 11, 2022 |accessdate=November 11, 2022}} < /ref >
