 '''Kholod''' (Холод) is the name of a [[rocket]] [[project]]. This project was developed in [[Russia]]. The engine of the rocket is called a [[scramjet]]. It was created to be faster than [[Mach number|Mach]] 5.75. The prototype includes a [[Soyuz TMKB]] with [[liquid hydrogen]] and modified fillings from the [[SA-5 Gammon]] rockets.
