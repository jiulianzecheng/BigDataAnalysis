 '''''Tsotsi''''' is a 2005 South African British crime drama movie directed by [[Gavin Hood]] and was based on the novel of the same name by [[Athol Fugard]]. It stars [[Presley Chweneyagae]], [[Mothusi Magano]], [[Kenneth Nkosi]], [[Zenzo Ngqobe]], [[Jerry Mofokeng]], [[Terry Pheto]] and won an Academy Award in 2006.
