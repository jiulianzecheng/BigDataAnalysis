'''Crookes''' is an area of the [[Sheffield|City of Sheffield]], [[England]], about 2.5 kilometers (1.5 miles) west of the center of the city. It borders [[Broomhill]] to the south, [[Walkley]] and [[Crookesmoor]] to the east and open countryside around the [[River Rivelin]] to the north. The area is   
 thought to have got its name from the Old Norse word  " Krkor "  which means a corner of land. < ref > http://freepages.genealogy.rootsweb.com/~engsheffield/webpages/derivisions.htm Sheffield area name dervations, Rootsweb < /ref >
