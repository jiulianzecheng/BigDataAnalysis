 '''''Poekilopleuron''''' (meaning  " varied ribs " ) is a [[Megalosauroidea|megalosauroid]]  dinosaur. The genus has been used under many different spelling variants, although only one, ''Poekilopleuron'', is valid. The [[type species]] is ''P. bucklandii'' (see also ''[[Megalosaurus]]''), named after [[William Buckland]], and many [[Junior synonym|junior synonyms]] of it have also been erected. Few material is currently known, as the [[holotype]] was destroyed in [[World War II]], although many casts of the material still exist. 
 
 == Discovery and naming == 
 ''Poekilopleuron'' is a genus of theropod with a long and complex history. The genus was first named and described by Jacques-Amand Eudes-Deslongchamps in a report published in 1836, < ref name= " eudes-deslongchamps1836 " > {{cite journal|author=Eudes-Deslongchamps J.-A.|year=1836|title=Plusieurs parties d'un mémoire sur un très-grand animal fossile découvert, l'an dernier, dans les carrières de la Maladrerie à un quart de lieu de Caen.|url=|journal=Analyse des travaux de la Société pendant l’année académique 1835–1836, Séance Publique de la Société Linnéenne de Normandie tenue à Vire le 24 mai 1836|volume=|issue=|pages=14–25}} < /ref > < ref name= " brignon2018 " > {{cite journal|author=Brignon A.|year=2018|title=New historical data on the first dinosaurs found in France.|url=|journal=BSGF - Earth Sciences Bulletin|volume=189|issue=4|pages=|doi=10.1051/bsgf/2018003}} < /ref >  based on holotype material that is now destroyed. In 1837, Eudes-Deslongchamps published a more detailed account of this discovery in a monograph < ref name= " eudes-deslongchamps1837 " > Eudes-Deslongchamps J.-A. (1837) Mémoire sur le Poekilopleuron bucklandii, grand saurien fossile, intermédiaire entre les crocodiles et les lézards. A. Hardel, Caen, 114 p., 8 pl. < /ref >  which was also inserted next year in volume 6 of the  " Mémoires de la Société Linnéenne Normandie " . < ref name= " eudes-deslongchamps1838 " > {{cite journal|author=Eudes-Deslongchamps J.-A.|year=1838|title=Mémoire sur le ''Poekilopleuron bucklandii'', grande saurien fossile, intermédiaire entre les crocodiles et les lézards, découvert dans les carrières de la Maladrerie, près Caen, au mois de juillet 1835|url=|journal=Mémoires de la Société Linnéenne Normandie|volume=6|issue=|pages=37–146, 8 pl}} < /ref >

 In the same 1836 publication, Eudes-Deslongchamps also named the [[type species]] of ''Poekilopleuron'', ''P. bucklandii''. < ref name= " deslongchamps1836 " > {{cite journal | author = Eudes-Deslongchamps J.-A. | year = 1836 | title = Plusieurs parties d'un mémoire sur un très-grand animal fossile découvert, l'an dernier, dans les carrières de la Maladrerie à un quart de lieu de Caen. | url = | journal = Analyse des travaux de la Société pendant l’année académique 1835–1836, Séance Publique de la Société Linnéenne de Normandie tenue à Vire le 24 mai 1836 | volume = | issue = | pages = 14–25 }} < /ref > < ref name= " brignon20181 " > {{cite journal | doi = 10.1051/bsgf/2018003 | author = Brignon A. | year = 2018 | title = New historical data on the first dinosaurs found in France. | url = | journal = BSGF - Earth Sciences Bulletin | volume = 189| issue = 4| pages = }} < /ref >  Eudes-Deslongchapms noted similarities with some material of ''[[Megalosaurus]] bucklandii'' and ''Poekilopleuron'', and chose the species name ''bucklandii'' for ''Poekilopleuron'', so that it the two genera were synonymized, only the genus name would be suppressed. < ref name= " allain & chure2002 "  / >  The generic name is derived from Greek ποίκιλος, ''poikilos'',  " varied " , and πλευρών, ''pleuron'',  " rib " , a reference to the three types of rib present. The specific name, honouring [[William Buckland]], was deliberately identical to that of ''[[Megalosaurus]] bucklandii''.{{Fact|date=November 2014}}
