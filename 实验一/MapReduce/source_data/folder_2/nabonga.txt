 '''''Nabonga''''' is a 1943 giant [[gorilla]] [[movie]] [[wikt:star#Verb|starring]] [[Buster Crabbe]] and [[Julie London]]. It tells of a young adventurer (Crabbe) searching for money stolen from his father's bank many years in the past. In [[Africa]], he learns the money is in the hands of a  " white witch "  in the [[jungle]] (London) who is guarded by a giant gorilla.
