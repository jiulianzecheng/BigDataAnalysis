 '''Torrelavega''' ([[Cantabrian dialect|Cantabrian]]: ''Torlavega'') is a [[municipality]] in the single province [[Autonomous communities of Spain|Autonomous Community]] of [[Cantabria]] in northern [[Spain]]. 
 
 Its highest point is 606 metres and its lowest point is 12 metres.
