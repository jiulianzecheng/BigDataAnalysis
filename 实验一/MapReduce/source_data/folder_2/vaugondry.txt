 '''Vaugondry''' was a [[municipality]] in the [[Jura-Nord vaudois (district)|Jura-Nord vaudois]] district in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Vaud]] in [[Switzerland]]. On 1 July 2011, the municipalities of [[Fontanezier]], [[Romairon]], Vaugondry and [[Villars-Burquin]] joined together to become the new municipality of [[Tévenon]]. < ref name=Fusion > [http://www.bfs.admin.ch/bfs/portal/de/index/infothek/nomenklaturen/blank/blank/gem_liste/03.html Amtliches Gemeindeverzeichnis der Schweiz] published by the Swiss Federal Statistical Office {{in lang|de}} accessed 17 February 2011 < /ref >
