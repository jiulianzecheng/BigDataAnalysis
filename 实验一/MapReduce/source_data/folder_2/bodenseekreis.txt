 '''Bodenseekreis''' ( " Lake Constance district " ) is a [[Rural districts of Germany|rural district]] (''Landkreis'') in the southeast of [[Baden-Württemberg]], [[Germany]]. Neighboring districts are [[Konstanz (district)|Konstanz]], [[Sigmaringen (district)|Sigmaringen]] and [[Ravensburg (district)|Ravensburg]], and in [[Bavaria]], [[Lindau (district)|Lindau district]]. To the south, on the opposite shores of [[Lake Constance]] ({{lang-de|Bodensee}}), lies [[Switzerland]]. The capital is [[Friedrichshafen]]. 
 
 == History == 
 The district was created in 1973, when the previous district [[Tettnang]] was merged with most of the district [[Überlingen]].
