 '''Lavizzara''' is a [[municipality]] of the district [[Vallemaggia (district)|Vallemaggia]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Ticino]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 The municipality was created in 2004 by a merger of [[Broglio, Switzerland|Broglio]], [[Brontallo]], [[Fusio]], [[Menzonio]], [[Peccia]] and [[Prato-Sornico]]. < ref name=Fusion > [http://www.bfs.admin.ch/bfs/portal/de/index/infothek/nomenklaturen/blank/blank/gem_liste/03.html Amtliches Gemeindeverzeichnis der Schweiz] published by the Swiss Federal Statistical Office {{in lang|de}} accessed 14 January 2010 < /ref >
