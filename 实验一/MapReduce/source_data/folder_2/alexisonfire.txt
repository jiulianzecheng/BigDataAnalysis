 '''Alexisonfire''' is a [[Canada|Canadian]] [[post-hardcore]] [[band]] from [[St. Catharines]], [[Ontario]]. Alexisonfire has six members in the band; they are George Pettit, Chris Steele, Wade MacNeil, Dallas Green, Jesse Ingelevics and Jordan  " Ratbeard "  Hastings. The band was formed in 2001 by the band members from Condemning Salemn, Helicon Blue and Plan 9. They released their self-titled first album on September 9, 2002. Alexisonfire split up in 2011. But returned in 2022 with the album otherness, which they won a Juno for that year.  < ref > {{cite web|url=https://theonlybandever.com/|title=ALEXISONFIRE|website=ALEXISONFIRE}} < /ref >
