 '''Quillota''' is a [[List of cities in Chile|city]] and [[Communes of Chile|commune]] in the [[Valparaíso Region]], [[Chile]]. It is the capital and largest city of [[Quillota Province]]. According to the 2002 census, 75,916 people lived there.
