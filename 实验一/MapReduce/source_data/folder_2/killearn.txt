 '''Killearn''' ({{lang-gd|Cill Earnain}}) is a small village of approximately 1700 people in the [[Stirling (council area)|Stirling]] [[council area]] of [[Scotland]].  The village is located about {{convert|18|mi|km|1|lk=on}} north of [[Glasgow]], and {{convert|9|mi|km|1}} away from [[Loch Lomond]].  The [[Glengoyne]] distillery is also situated just outside the village.
