 '''Suffolk''' is an [[East Anglia|East Anglian]] [[county]] in [[England]] ([[United Kingdom|UK]]) The counties around Suffolk are [[Norfolk]] to the north, [[Cambridgeshire]] to the west and [[Essex]] to the south. The [[North Sea]] is on the east. The county town is [[Ipswich]] and other big towns include [[Lowestoft]] and [[Bury St Edmunds]]. [[Felixstowe]] is one of the largest container ports in [[Europe]]. 
 
 == Geography == 
 Suffolk is mostly flat and has very fertile soil which is good for both growing crops and grazing animals on.
