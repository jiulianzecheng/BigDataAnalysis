 '''Kleinheubach''' is a market community in the [[Miltenberg (district)|Miltenberg district]] in the [[Regierungsbezirk|region]] of [[Lower Franconia]] in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]] and the seat of the municipal association. About 3,600 people live in Kleinheubach. 
 
 [[File:Schloss Löwenstein Kleinheubach Aerial fg196.jpg|thumb|''Schloss Löwenstein'', residence of the Princes of [[Löwenstein-Wertheim]]-Rosenberg since 1720]] 
 
 == Geography == 
 === Place === 
 Kleinheubach is between the [[Spessart]] and [[Odenwald]] ranges, on the left edge of the [[Main (river)|Main]]. Across the river is [[Grossheubach]], linked to Kleinheubach by a bridge. Upstream the closest town is [[Miltenberg]]. The municipal territory comes in contact on the state of [[Hesse]] in the far west. 
 
 ===Neighbouring communities=== 
 Kleinheubach borders on (from the north, clockwise): Grossheubach, Miltenberg, [[Rüdenau]], [[Michelstadt]] (in Hesse) and [[Laudenbach, Bavaria|Laudenbach]].
