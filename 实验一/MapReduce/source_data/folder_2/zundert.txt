 '''Zundert''' is a [[List of municipalities of the Netherlands|municipality]] in the [[Netherlands|Dutch]] [[Provinces of the Netherlands|province]] of [[North Brabant]]. In 2019, 21,612 people lived there. 
 
 [[Post-impressionism|Post-impressionist]] painter [[Vincent van Gogh]] was born here.
