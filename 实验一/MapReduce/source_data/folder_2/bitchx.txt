 '''BitchX''' is an [[IRC]] client. The first public release was in 1996. The latest stable version is 1.1 and was updated in 2004. It is free of charge and was written in [[C (programming language)|C]]. Its last version has lots of [[security]] [[software bugs]] and its use is generally discouraged. 
 
 In 2010, people started working on BitchX again under the name of 'BitchX2'
