'''Haridwar''' is a city in the [[India]]n state of [[Uttarakhand]]. It is one of the seven holiest places for [[Hindu]] people. There are several temples and [[ashram]]s in the city. The river [[Ganga]] flows in the city and is worshipped by Hindus for whom the river is sacred.
