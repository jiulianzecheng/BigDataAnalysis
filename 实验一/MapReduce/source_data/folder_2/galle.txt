 '''Galle''' (formerly Point de Galle) is a city on the southwest coast of [[Sri Lanka]]. It is also known as Galle Fort, the [[Defensive wall|fortified]] old city founded by [[Portuguese colonist]]s in the [[16th century]]. Its stone [[sea wall]]s were expanded by the Dutch. Its building designs shows Portuguese, Dutch and British [[Influence|influences]]. Some of the notable buildings include the 18th-century [[Dutch Reformed Church]].
