 '''Sprouting''' is growing sprouts from seeds ([[Germination|germinating]]) to eat. Bean sprouts are a common ingredient, especially in [[Asian]] cuisine, made from sprouting beans. The typical beansprout is made from the greenish-capped [[mung bean]]s (''[[Vigna]] radiata''). Other common bean sprouts are the usually yellow, larger-grained soy sprouts.
