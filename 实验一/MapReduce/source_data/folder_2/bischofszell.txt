 '''Bischofszell''' is a [[municipality]] of the [[district]] of [[Weinfelden (district)|Weinfelden]] in the [[cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Thurgau]] in the country of [[Switzerland]].  It has a [[population]] of 5,500 people. 
 
 The local industry is Bischofszell Konserven. It now belongs to [[Migros]]. They make jams, fruit juices, various cans, convenience food, among them Anna's Best.
