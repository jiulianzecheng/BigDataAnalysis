 He stated that his goal is to show that the [[Southern United States|South]] can produce quality lyricists. He is also a hook writer and generally sings and harmonizes with himself (using vocals from various tracks) on his own hooks. Chamillionaire is a founder and original member of The Color Changin' Click. < ref name= " Allmusic " > {{cite web|last=Kellman|first=Andy|date=2007|title=Chamillionaire - Biography|url=http://www.allmusic.com/cg/amg.dll?p=amg & searchlink=CHAMILLIONAIRE & sql=11:g9fyxqtaldke~T1|publisher=[[Allmusic]]|accessdate=2008-02-02}} < /ref >  In 2007, he won a [[Grammy Award]] for ''Best Rap Performance by a Duo or Group'' for his number-one single  " [[Ridin']] " , a number-one hit in the [[United States]] for two weeks in 2006. < ref > {{cite web|title=Best Rap Performance By A Duo Or Group |url=http://www.grammy.com/GRAMMY_Awards/49th_show/list.aspx#07|publisher=[[Grammy Awards|Grammy.com]]|date=2007|accessdate=2008-02-04}} < /ref >  He is a former artist on Michael 5000 Watts' [[Swishahouse]] label. < ref name= " Allmusic " / >
