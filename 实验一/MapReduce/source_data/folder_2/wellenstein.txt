 '''Wellenstein''' ({{lang-lb|Wellesteen}}) is a former [[Communes of Luxembourg|commune]] in [[Luxembourg]]. It was part of the [[Remich (canton)|canton of Remich]]. 
 
 On 1 January 2012, the commune was merged with [[Burmerange]] to form the commune of [[Schengen, Luxembourg|Schengen]].
