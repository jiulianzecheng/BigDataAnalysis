 '''Tetuin''' is a [[flavone]]. It is a type of flavonoid. It is the 6-O-[[glucoside]] of [[baicalein]]. It can be isolated from the seeds of ''[[Oroxylum indicum]]'', the Indian trumpetflower, < ref > Mehta C. R. and Mehta T. P., 1959 Journal of the Indian Chemical Society 36:468 < /ref >  known as टेटु ''tetu'' in Marathi.
