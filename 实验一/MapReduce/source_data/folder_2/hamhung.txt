 '''Hamhung''' is a city in [[North Korea]]. It is the capital of South Hamgyong Province. It is the second largest city in North Korea and has the population of 768,551 {{As of|2008|lc=y}}. < ref name= " Nkorea2008 " > [http://unstats.un.org/unsd/demographic/sources/census/2010_PHC/North_Korea/Final%20national%20census%20report.pdf United Nations Statistics Division; 2008 Census of Population of the Democratic People’s Republic of Korea conducted on 1–15 October 2008 ('''pdf-file''')] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20100331091148/http://unstats.un.org/unsd/demographic/sources/census/2010_PHC/North_Korea/Final%20national%20census%20report.pdf |date=31 March 2010 }} Retrieved on 2009-03-18. < /ref >
