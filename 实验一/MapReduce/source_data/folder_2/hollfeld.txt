 '''Hollfeld''' is a town in the [[Bayreuth (district)|district of Bayreuth]], in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. 
 
 It is 20 & nbsp;km west of [[Bayreuth]], and 30 & nbsp;km east of [[Bamberg]].
