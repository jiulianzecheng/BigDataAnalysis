 '''Eggo''' is a brand of frozen [[waffle]]s sold in the [[United States]], [[Canada]], and [[Mexico]]. It is owned by the [[Kellogg Company]]. There are several kinds of Eggo, including Homestyle, [[blueberry]], [[strawberry]], [[apple]] [[cinnamon]], and [[chocolate chip]]. It was introduced in 1953. It is one of the largest frozen waffle companies in the United States. < ref > Belsie, Laurent.
