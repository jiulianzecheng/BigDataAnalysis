 '''Gunzwil''' was a [[municipality]] of the [[district]] of [[Sursee (district)|Sursee]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Lucerne (canton)|Lucerne]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 In 2009, it became part of the municipality of [[Beromünster]].
