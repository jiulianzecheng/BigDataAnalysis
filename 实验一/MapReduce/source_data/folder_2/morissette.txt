 '''Johanne Morissette Daug Amon-Lamar''' (born June 2, 1996), better known by her stage name '''Morissette''', is a [[Philippines|Filipina]] [[singer]], [[songwriter]], [[producer]], [[actress]], and [[artist]]. Morissette, who was born and raised in [[Cebu]], rose to popularity when she became runner-up on TV5's Star Factor at the age of 14. In 2012, she made her Broadway debut in the Repertory Philippines production of Disney's Camp Rock. 
 
 Morissette appeared on the first season of ABS-CBN's The Voice of the Philippines in 2013, where she joined singer Sarah Geronimo's team. On ABS-CBN's show, ASAP, she debuted in January 2014 as one of the  " Homegrown Divas, "  which eventually became ASAP's  " Birit Queens. "  In early 2014, she signed with Star Music, and her debut album, Morissette, was released in March 2015. In 2017, Morissette was branded  " The Next Big Diva.
