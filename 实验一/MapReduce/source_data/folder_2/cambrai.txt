 '''Cambrai''' ({{IPA-fr|kɑ̃bʁɛ|-|Fr-Paris--Cambrai.ogg}}; {{lang-pcd|Kimbré}}; {{lang-nl|Kamerijk}}; historically in English Camerick and Camericke) is a [[Communes of France|commune]] in the [[Nord (French department)|Nord]] [[Departments of France|department]] and in the [[Hauts-de-France]] [[Regions of France|region]] of [[France]] on the [[Scheldt]] river, which is known locally as the Escaut river.
