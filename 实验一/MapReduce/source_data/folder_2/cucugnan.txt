 '''Cucugnan''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]] of 123 people (2017). It is in the [[Regions of France|region]] [[Occitanie]] in the [[Aude]] [[Departments of France|department]] in the south of [[France]]. The small village lies in a valley in the [[Corbières Massif]], overlooked by the ruined [[Château de Quéribus]].
