 The '''Pipidae''' is a [[family (biology)|family]] of [[frog]]s. They do not have [[tongue]]s. There are over 30 [[species]] of Pipidae frogs. They are found in [[tropical]] places in [[South America]] and [[Africa]]. 
 
 == Genera == 
 Family '''Pipidae''' 
 * ''[[African dwarf frog|Hymenochirus]]'' - Dwarf clawed frogs 
 * ''[[Pseudhymenochirus merlini|Pseudhymenochirus]]'' - Merlin's clawed frog 
 * ''[[Xenopus]]'' - Common clawed frogs 
 * ''[[Silurana]]'' - Tropical clawed frogs 
 * ''[[Surinam toad|Pipa]]'' - Surinam toads
