 A '''Cuscus''' is a [[marsupial]] from [[Papua New Guinea]]. It has a [[wikt:pouch|pouch]] on its front where its baby begins to grow and develop in. The cuscus has thick white [[fur]] and a long [[tail]], with a slightly [[pink]] tip.
