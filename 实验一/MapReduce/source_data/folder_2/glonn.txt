 '''Glonn''' is a [[market town]] in the [[Ebersberg (district)|Ebersberg district]] in [[Upper Bavaria]], [[Germany]], about {{convert|30|km|mi|abbr=on}} southeast of [[Munich]]. 
 
 == Geography == 
 The market town of Glonn is a [[health spa]] (''Erholungsort''), and the Glonn Valley is ringed by wooded hills, carved by the former [[Inn Glacier]]. To the south, the [[Alps]] are clearly visible on the horizon. 
 
 The rivers [[Glonn (Mangfall)|Glonn]], {{ill|Kupferbach (Glonn)|lt=Kupferbach|de}} and [[Schrannenbach]] rise within Glonn's municipal area, where the lake [[Kastensee]] is also to be found. Just inside the neighbouring community of [[Moosach]] finds the [[Steinsee (Bavaria)|Steinsee]], another lake. Glonn is also home to three protected areas. 
 
 == Neighbouring communities == 
 Glonn's immediate neighbours are the communities of [[Baiern]], [[Bruck, Germany|Bruck]], [[Egmating]], Moosach and [[Oberpframmern]], which together with Glonn form an administrative community (''Verwaltungsgemeinschaft''). 
 
 The town of [[Grafing]] and the centres of [[Kirchseeon]], [[Zorneding]] and [[Höhenkirchen-Siegertsbrunn]] are about 15 & nbsp;km away. Nevertheless, it is there that regional and long-distance rail service is to be found. 
 
 Glonn is about 30 & nbsp;km from the cities of Munich and [[Rosenheim]].
