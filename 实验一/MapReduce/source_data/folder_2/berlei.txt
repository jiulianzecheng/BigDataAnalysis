 Berlei bras are now sold worldwide by [[Hanesbrands]]. 
 
 The name came from that of the firm's founder, Frederick R. Burley, who felt that his name as normally spelled was not appropriate to his products. 
 
 Berlei Limited was one of the first Australian companies to expand outside Australia. < ref > Clune, page 439 < /ref >  Berlei ([[New Zealand]]) Limited was incorporated in 1923 and Berlei (U.K.) Limited was organised in 1930.
