'''Basildon''' is a large [[town]] in [[Essex]], [[England]]. Basildon was recorded to have a population of 1,159 in 1931. It was then expanded when it became a new town in the late 1940s. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.basildon.com/|title=Basildon History Services Information Guide|website=www.basildon.com}} < /ref >  It now has a population of over 100,000.
