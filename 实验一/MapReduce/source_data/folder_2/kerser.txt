 '''Scott Froml''' (born 21 August 1987), better known by his [[stage name]] '''Kerser''', is an [[Australians|Australian]] [[rapper]]. His best known song is  " [[Kerser Is the Sickest]] " . He was released a number of [[mixtape]]s and three [[album]]s since 2011. His second album ''[[No Rest For the Sickest]]'' reached #15 on the [[ARIA Charts|ARIA albums chart]].
