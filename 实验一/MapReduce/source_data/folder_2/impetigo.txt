 '''Impetigo''' is a very [[wikt:contagious|contagious]] [[bacteria]]l [[skin]] infection. It is most common in pre-school [[child]]ren. < ref > [http://www.nhs.uk/conditions/Impetigo/Pages/Introduction.aspx NHS Impetigo] < /ref >  People who play close-contact [[sports]] like [[Rugby football|rugby]] and [[American football]] are also at risk. Impetigo is not very common in [[adult]]s.
