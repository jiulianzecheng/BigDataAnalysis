'''Topoxte''' ({{lang-es|Topoxté}}) was a [[Maya civilisation|Maya]] [[archaeological site]] in northern [[Guatemala]]. It was the [[capital city]] of the Ko’woj Maya. Topoxte is located on an island on [[Yaxha Lake]] across from the important centre of [[Yaxha]].
