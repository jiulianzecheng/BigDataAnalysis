 '''Parkano''' is a town in [[Pirkanmaa]], [[Finland]]. As of 28 February 2023, 6,236 people lived there. Neighbouring municipalities are [[Ikaalinen]], [[Jämijärvi]], [[Kankaanpää]], [[Karvia]], [[Kihniö]], [[Kurikka]] and [[Ylöjärvi]]. Most people speak [[Finnish language|Finnish]].
