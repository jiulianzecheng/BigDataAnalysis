 '''Talence''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is found in the [[Regions of France|region]] [[Aquitaine]] in the [[Gironde]] [[Departments of France|department]] in the southwest of [[France]]. The campus of the ''[[École nationale supérieure d'électronique, informatique, télécommunications, mathématiques et mécanique de Bordeaux]]'' is located in the city.
