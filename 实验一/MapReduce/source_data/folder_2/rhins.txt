 The '''Rhins''' or '''Reins''' is a [[river]] in central [[France]] in the [[Auvergne-Rhône-Alpes]] [[Regions of France|region]]. It is a right [[tributary]] of the [[Loire River|Loire]] river. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 The Rhins river has a length of {{Convert|59.8|km|mi|1|abbr=on}}, and a [[drainage basin]] with an area of {{Convert|468|km2|sqmi|0|abbr=on }}. < ref name= " sandre " > {{cite web |url=http://services.sandre.eaufrance.fr/Courdo/Fiche/client/fiche_courdo.php?CdSandre=K09-0300 |title=Rivière Le Rhins (K09-0300) |publisher=SANDRE - Portail national d'accès aux référentiels sur l'eau |language=fr |access-date=14 October 2017 }} < /ref > 
 
 ===Course=== 
 The Rhins starts in the ''Monts du Beaujolais'', in the [[Communes in France|''commune'']] of [[Ranchal]], ([[Rhône (department)|Rhône]] department), at an [[altitude]] of about {{Convert|760|m|ft|0|abbr=on }}. < ref > {{cite web |url=https://www.geoportail.gouv.fr/carte?c=4.365278,46.135556 & z=12 & l0=GEOGRAPHICALGRIDSYSTEMS.MAPS.SCAN-EXPRESS.STANDARD::GEOPORTAIL:OGC:WMTS(1) & permalink=yes |title=Source du Rhins |publisher=Géoportail |language=fr |access-date=14 October 2017 }} < /ref >  From here, the Rhins flows first to the south, then to the west and ends flowing to the northwest. 
 
 The Rhins river flows within the [[Auvergne-Rhône-Alpes]] region, 2 departments and 21 ''communes''. It flows through the following ''communes'', among others: < ref name=sandre / > 
 * [[Rhône (department)|Rhône]] : [[Saint-Vincent-de-Reins]], [[Cublize]], [[Amplepuis]] 
 * [[Loire]] : [[Saint-Victor-sur-Rhins]], [[Régny]], [[Le Coteau]], [[Roanne]] 
 
 Finally, it flows into the [[Loire River|Loire]] river in the ''commune'' of [[Roanne]]. < ref > {{cite web |url=https://www.geoportail.gouv.fr/carte?c=4.109167,46.050833 & z=12 & l0=GEOGRAPHICALGRIDSYSTEMS.MAPS.SCAN-EXPRESS.STANDARD::GEOPORTAIL:OGC:WMTS(1) & permalink=yes |title=Confluence du Rhins |publisher=Géoportail |language=fr |access-date=14 October 2017 }} < /ref >

 The Rhins is a very irregular small river; it shows typical [[season]]al changes. The highest levels of the river are in [[winter]] and [[spring]], from November to May inclusive, with the highest level in February. They are followed by a rapid fall in the flow during the low water period that goes from late June to early October.
