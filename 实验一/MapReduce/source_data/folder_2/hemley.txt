 '''Hemley''' is a village and [[civil parish]] in [[Suffolk Coastal]], [[Suffolk]], England. In 2001 there were 66 people living in Hemley. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.ons.gov.uk/help/localstatistics|title=Local statistics - Office for National Statistics|website=www.ons.gov.uk}} < /ref >  Hemley has a church called All Saints. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.suffolkchurches.co.uk/hemley.htm|title=Hemley|website=www.suffolkchurches.co.uk}} < /ref >  Hemley village is on the west side of the Deben estuary. < ref > {{Cite web |url=http://www.crsbi.ac.uk/search/county/site/ed-sf-hemle.html |title=Corpus of Romanesque Sculpture in Britain and Ireland: All Saints, Hemley, Suffolk |access-date=2013-08-26 |archive-date=2012-09-05 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120905053714/http://www.crsbi.ac.uk/search/county/site/ed-sf-hemle.html |url-status=dead }} < /ref >
