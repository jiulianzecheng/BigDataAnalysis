 '''Strijen''' is a former [[List of municipalities of the Netherlands|municipality]] in the [[Netherlands|Dutch]] [[Provinces of the Netherlands|province]] of [[South Holland]]. On 1 January 2019, it was merged with the former municipalities of [[Binnenmaas]], [[Cromstrijen]], [[Korendijk]], and [[Oud-Beijerland]] to form the new municipality of [[Hoeksche Waard]].
