 Maharaj Shri '''Ranvirsinhji''' (7 October 1919 – 4 April 1962), a member of the Jamnagar royal family, played [[first-class cricket]] in India from 1936 to 1952. He toured Australia with the [[Indian cricket team in Australia in 1947–48|Indian team in 1947-48]] but did not play [[Test cricket]]. < ref > [https://cricketarchive.com/Archive/Players/40/40393/40393.html Prahlad Singh at CricketArchive] < /ref >
