 '''Tureberg''' is a [[neighbourhood]] < ref > {{cite web |url=http://www.sollentuna.se/Sollentuna-kommun/Om-Sollentuna/Historia/Tureberg/ |title=Tureberg - Sollentuna kommun |publisher=Sollentuna.se |date=2012-08-21 |accessdate=2012-10-23 |archive-date=2012-12-21 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20121221033036/http://www.sollentuna.se/Sollentuna-kommun/Om-Sollentuna/Historia/Tureberg/ |url-status=dead }} < /ref >  in [[Sollentuna Municipality]], [[Stockholm County]], [[Sweden]]. In 2011, 15,560 people lived there. < ref > {{Cite web |url=http://www.sollentuna.se/Sollentuna-kommun/Om-Sollentuna/Fakta-och-statistik/Befolkningsprognos/ |title=Befolkningsprognos - Sollentuna kommun |access-date=2014-08-11 |archive-date=2012-12-21 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20121221032929/http://www.sollentuna.se/Sollentuna-kommun/Om-Sollentuna/Fakta-och-statistik/Befolkningsprognos/ |url-status=dead }} < /ref >  It is a [[Stockholm]] [[suburb]] and houses the [[city and town hall|local government seat]] for the municipality as well as a large [[shopping mall]] and a [[Stockholm commuter rail]] station. 
 
 Tureberg has also evolved as a law enforcement center for the northern part of metropolitan Stockholm, with police station, < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.polisen.se/Kontakta-oss/Polisstationer/Stockholms-lan/Norrort1/Sollentuna/ |title=Sollentuna - Stockholms län - Adresser och öppettider |publisher=www.polisen.se |date=2008-05-20 |accessdate=2012-10-23}} < /ref >  court house, < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.attundatingsratt.domstol.se/ |title=Attunda tingsrätt |publisher=Attundatingsratt.domstol.se |date=2012-10-16 |accessdate=2012-10-23}} < /ref >  prosecutor's office, < ref > {{cite web |url=http://www.aklagare.se/Sok-aklagare/Stockholm/Stockholm---Norrorts-aklagarkammare/ |title=Stockholm - Norrorts åklagarkammare - Aklagarmyndigheten |publisher=Aklagare.se |date= |accessdate=2012-10-23 |archive-date=2012-06-29 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120629181018/http://www.aklagare.se/Sok-aklagare/Stockholm/Stockholm---Norrorts-aklagarkammare/ |url-status=dead }} < /ref >  and a jail, < ref > http://www.kriminalvarden.se/sv/Hakte/Vara-hakten/Sollentuna/accessdate=2013-03-28{{Dead link|date=January 2021 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }} < /ref >  all in buildings built in the 1980s and onwards. The present courthouse was inaugurated in 2010.
