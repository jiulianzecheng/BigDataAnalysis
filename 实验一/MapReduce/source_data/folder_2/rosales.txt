 '''Rosales''' is an [[order (biology)|order]] of [[flowering plant]]s. It includes nine [[family (biology)|families]]. The plants are grouped together because their [[genetics]] are similar. Some well-known members of Rosales include: roses; strawberries, blackberries and raspberries; apples and pears; plums, peaches and apricots; almonds; rowan and hawthorn; elms; figs; nettles; and hops and cannabis. The true roses are in the ''[[Rosaceae]]''.
