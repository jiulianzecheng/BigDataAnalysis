 '''Berchtesgaden''' ({{IPA2|bɛʁçtəsˈgaːdən}};  < small > [[West Central Bavarian]]: < /small >  ''Berchtsgoan'') is a [[Municipalities|municipality]] in the [[Germany|German]] [[Bavaria|Bavarian]] [[Alps]]. It is found in the south district of [[Berchtesgadener Land]] in [[Bavaria]], near the border to [[Austria]]. It lies north of the [[Nationalpark Berchtesgaden]].
