'''Ooti''' or '''Uti''' is a [[Meitei cuisine|Meitei Manipuri dish]], made up of soaked [[rice]] and soaked [[pea]]s. It is a gruel of [[rice]], green [[gram]]s and a concoction of green [[onion]]s.
