 '''Ljusdal''' is a locality in the [[counties of Sweden|county]] of [[Gävleborg County|Gävleborg]] in [[Sweden]]. It is the seat of [[Ljusdal Municipality]]. Ljusdal is the home of [[bandy]] club [[Ljusdals BK]], which has hosted the [[Bandy World Cup]] several times.
