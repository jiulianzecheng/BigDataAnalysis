 '''Uppsala''' is a [[city]] in [[Sweden]].  It is the 4th biggest city in Sweden, and the center of the [[Church of Sweden]]. 
 
 The city is home to the well-known student residential area of Flogsta.  It is the largest student residence in the city. It is also known for the 'Flogsta scream'. At 10 pm every evening, students scream their frustrations from windows and balconies.
