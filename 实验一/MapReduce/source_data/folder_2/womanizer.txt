" '''Womanizer''' "  is a 2008 song. It is the first song on [[Britney Spears]]' sixth album ''[[Circus (Britney Spears album)|Circus]]''. It was the first single released off that album. It went to number one on the [[Billboard Hot 100|''Billboard'' Hot 100]]. It is an [[electropop]] song referring to a womanizing man.
