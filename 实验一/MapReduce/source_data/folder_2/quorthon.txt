'''Tomas  " Ace "  Börje Forsberg''' (often called '''Quorthon''';born on 17 February 1966 in [[Sweden]]) was a [[Swedish people|Swedish]] [[musician]]. He was the founder and songwriter for the [[extreme metal]] band [[Bathory (band)|Bathory]]. Bathory was known for laying out the groundwork for [[black metal]] and later [[viking metal]]. < ref > Johannesson, Ika  &  Klingberg, Jon Jefferson. (2011) ''Blod Eld Död - En svensk metalhistoria'', Alfabeta Bokförlag AB, p. 51-68. < /ref >  Quorthon was a [[multi-instrumentalist]]. He wrote all the music and lyrics for Bathory, and on some albums he played all the instruments ([[guitar]], [[bass guitar|bass]], [[drums]], etc.). He also made solo albums. Quorthon died around 3 June 2004 in [[Stockholm]], [[Sweden]].
