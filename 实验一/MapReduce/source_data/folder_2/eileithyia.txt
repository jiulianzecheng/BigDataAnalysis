'''Eileithyia''' ({{IPAc-en|ɪ|l|ᵻ|ˈ|θ|aɪ|.|ə}}; {{lang-el|Εἰλείθυια}}) was the goddess of childbirth and midwifery in [[Greek mythology]]. She is the daughter of [[Zeus]] and [[Hera]], king and queen of the gods. While she is not a prominent deity in [[Ancient Greek religion]], Eileithyia does feature in a number of myths. Her Roman counterpart was [[Lucina (mythology)|Lucina]].
