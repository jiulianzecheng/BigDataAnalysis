 '''NeXT Inc.''' was an American [[computer]] and [[software]] company. It was created after Steve Jobs quit his job at [[Apple Inc.]]. It was a failure but it did get him back at Apple and as CEO. The [[macOS|Mac OS x]]'s core was based entirely on the NeXT computer. Apple bought NeXT for $429 million.  < ref > {{Cite web|url=https://www.businessinsider.com/steve-jobs-12-million-dollar-failure-saved-apple-next-2019-8|title=Steve Jobs left Apple to start a new computer company.
