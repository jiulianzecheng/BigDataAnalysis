'''Sterilisation''' (which some people spell '''Sterilization''') is a [[wikt:process|process]] that makes [[animal]]s or [[human]]s unable to re[[Generation|generate]] through re[[birth]]. Sterilisation completely stops people from having children physically. 
 
 Sterilisation often is done for a way of [[birth control]] or [[childfree]]. But it have been often done against the will of the person, this is called  " [[compulsory sterilization|compulsory sterilisation.]] "
