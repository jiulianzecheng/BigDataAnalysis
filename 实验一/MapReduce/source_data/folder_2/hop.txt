 '''Hop''' or '''hops''' can mean several things: 
 
 * Hop, a kind of small [[jumping|jump]], usually using only one leg, but can also be on two 
 * ''[[Humulus]]'', a genus of flowering plants 
 ** [[Hops]], the female flower clusters of a species of hop 
 * Hop, informal word for [[opium]], a type of drug 
 * [[Hop (networking)]], next gateway along a path to a destination host 
 * [[Hop (movie)|''Hop'' (movie)]], a movie from 2011
