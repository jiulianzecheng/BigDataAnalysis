 '''Bronchitis''' is [[inflammation]] of the [[bronchi]] caused by [[irritation]]. It may also refer to acute bronchitis or chronic bronchitis. It can be caused by infection or other causes such as smoking, or [[passive smoking|being exposed to cigarettes]].

 * '''[[Cough]]'''
