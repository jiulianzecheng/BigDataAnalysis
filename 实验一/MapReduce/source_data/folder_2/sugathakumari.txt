 '''Sugathakumari''' (22 January 1934 – 23 December 2020) was an Indian [[poet]] and activist. She was an important person in the environmentalist and [[Feminism|feminist]] movements in [[Kerala]], [[South India]]. 
 
 She was the founder secretary of the Prakrithi Samrakshana Samithi, an organization for the protection of nature and a day-care center for the mentally ill.  
 
 She chaired the Kerala State Women's Commission. < ref name=VanithaCommission > {{cite news|title=Status of women declining: Sugathakumari|url=http://www.hindu.com/2000/11/03/stories/0403404f.htm|work=[[The Hindu]]|date=3 November 2000|access-date=27 May 2013|location=[[Thiruvananthapuram|Thiruvananthapuram, India]]|archive-date=29 January 2002|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20020129215035/http://www.hindu.com/2000/11/03/stories/0403404f.htm|url-status=dead}} < /ref >
