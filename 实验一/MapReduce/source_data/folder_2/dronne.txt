 The '''Dronne''' ({{lang-oc|Drona}}) is a river in southwestern [[France]], right [[tributary]] of the [[Isle (river)|Isle]] river. It flows through the [[Nouvelle-Aquitaine]] [[Regions of France|region]]. 
 
 Its name appears, in an old form, in two ''[[commune]]s'' of the [[Dordogne]] [[Departments of France|department]]ː [[Saint-Méard-de-Drône]] and [[Saint-Pardoux-de-Drône]]. Its modern name appears in three ''communes''ː [[Aubeterre-sur-Dronne]], [[Saint-Méard-de-Drône]], [[Saint-Pardoux-de-Drône]]. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 The Dronne river has a length of {{Convert|200.6|km|mi|0|abbr=on}}, < ref name= " sandre " > {{cite web |url=http://services.sandre.eaufrance.fr/Courdo/Fiche/client/fiche_courdo.php?CdSandre=P7--0250 |title=La Dronne (P7--0250) |publisher=SANDRE - Portail national d'accès aux référentiels sur l'eau |language=fr |access-date=18 July 2016 }} < /ref >  and a [[drainage basin]] with an area of approximately {{Convert|2798|km2|sqmi|0|abbr=on }}. 
 
 ===Course=== 
 The Dronne starts in the [[Massif Central]], in the ''[[commune]]'' of [[Bussière-Galant]], [[Creuse]] department, [[Nouvelle-Aquitaine]] region. It starts in the ''[[Parc naturel régional Périgord Limousin]]'' at an [[altitude]] of about {{Convert|486|m|ft|0|abbr=off}}. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://www.geoportail.gouv.fr/accueil?c=1.080556,45.648333 & z=0.000224579 & l=GEOGRAPHICALGRIDSYSTEMS.MAPS.3D$GEOPORTAIL:OGC:WMTS@aggregate(1) & permalink=yes |title=Source de la Dronne |publisher=Géoportail |language=fr |access-date=18 July 2016 }} < /ref > 
 
 It flows to the southwest and, finally, the river flows into at [[Coutras]], in the [[Gironde]] department, at an altitude of about {{Convert|4|m|ft|0|abbr=off}}. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://www.geoportail.gouv.fr/accueil?c=-0.148333,45.037778 & z=0.000224579 & l=GEOGRAPHICALGRIDSYSTEMS.MAPS.3D$GEOPORTAIL:OGC:WMTS@aggregate(1) & permalink=yes |title=Confluence de la Dronne |publisher=Géoportail |language=fr |access-date=18 July 2016 }} < /ref > 
 
 The Dronne flows through 5 departments and 52 ''communes'', all in the [[Nouvelle-Aquitaine]] region; the main ''communes'' areː < ref name= " sandre "  / > 
 * [[Haute-Vienne]] : [[Bussière-Galant]] 
 * [[Dordogne]] : [[Saint-Pardoux-la-Rivière]], [[Champagnac-de-Belair]], [[Brantôme, Dordogne|Brantôme]], [[Bourdeilles]], [[Mialet, Dordogne|Mialet]], [[Tocane-Saint-Apre]], [[Ribérac]], [[Saint-Aulaye]], [[La Roche-Chalais]] 
 * [[Charente]] : [[Aubeterre-sur-Dronne]]  
 * [[Charente-Maritime]] : [[Saint-Aigulin]] 
 * [[Gironde]] : [[Les Églisottes-et-Chalaures]], [[Coutras]]
