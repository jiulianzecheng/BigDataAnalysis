In [[Norse mythology]], '''Sleipnir''' ([[Old Norse language|Old Norse]]:  " slippy "  or  " the slipper " ) is the eight-legged horse who serves as [[Odin|Odin's]] trusted steed. He is the son of [[Svaðilfari]] and the [[god]]/[[Jötnar|jötunn]] [[Loki]], the latter of whom is actually his mother, having given birth to Sleipnir in the form of a mare. Sleipnir is described as the best and fastest of all horses.
