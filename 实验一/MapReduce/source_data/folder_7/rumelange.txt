 '''Rumelange''' ({{lang-lb|Rëmeleng}}; {{lang-de|Rümelingen}}) is a [[Communes of Luxembourg|commune]] with [[List of cities in Luxembourg|town status]] in [[Luxembourg]], on the border with [[France]]. It is part of the [[Esch-sur-Alzette (canton)|canton of Esch-sur-Alzette]]. 
 
 As of 2019, 5,608 people live here.
