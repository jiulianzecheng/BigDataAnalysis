 '''Schelten''' ({{lang-fr|La Scheulte}}) is a [[municipality]] in the [[administrative district]] of [[Jura bernois (administrative district)|Jura bernois]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Bern (canton)|Bern]] in [[Switzerland]]. This is one of two [[German language|German]] speaking cities in the [[majority]] [[French language|French]]-speaking Bernese Jura (Jura Bernois).
