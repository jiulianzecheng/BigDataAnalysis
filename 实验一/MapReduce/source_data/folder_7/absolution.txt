 '''Absolution''' is the name of a [[sacrament]] of the [[Roman Catholic Church|Roman Catholic]], [[Eastern Orthodox Church|Eastern Orthodox]], [[Anglican]] and [[Lutheranism|Lutheran]] churches. Using absolution, a priest or the church can forgive a [[sin]], after [[confession]].
