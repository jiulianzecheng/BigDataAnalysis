 '''Beuningen''' ({{pronunciation|Nl-Beuningen.ogg}}) is a [[municipality]] and a town in the eastern [[Netherlands]]. It is near the city of [[Nijmegen]]. The river [[Waal (river)|Waal]] is {{convert|1.5|km|mi|0}} to the north. The other towns in the municipality are: [[Ewijk]], [[Weurt]], and [[Winssen]]. 
 
 There is a restored [[windmill]] in the town, [[De Haag, Beuningen|De Haag]].
