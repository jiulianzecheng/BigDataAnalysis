 '''Raipur''' is the capital city of the Indian state of [[Chhattisgarh]]. < ref name= " Husain " > {{cite book|author=Majid Husain|title=Understanding: Geographical: Map Entries: for Civil Services Examinations: Second Edition|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=_9VGKfQ-sQsC & pg=RA3-PA32|accessdate=15 July 2012|publisher=Tata McGraw-Hill Education|isbn=978-0-07-070288-2|pages=3–}} < /ref >  The state of Chhatisgarh was formed on 1 November 2000. < ref name= " (CHRI) " > {{cite book|author=Commonwealth Human Rights Initiative (CHRI)|title=The Right to Information and Panchayati Raj Institutions:Chhattisgarh as a Case Study (2006)|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=ywaWPnt9UfEC & pg=PA3|accessdate=15 July 2012|publisher=Chri|isbn=978-81-88205-24-0|pages=3–}} < /ref >  Before this, Raipur was in the state of [[Madhya Pradesh]]. 
 
 In this city, many types of [[rice]] are grown. So, this city is also called the  " rice bowl of India " . < ref name= " BasileMukhopadhyay2009 " > {{cite book|author1=Elisabetta Basile|author2=Ishita Mukhopadhyay|title=The Changing Identity of Rural India: A Socio-Historic Analysis|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=A5LiM-BR1EsC & pg=PA272|accessdate=15 July 2012|date=1 July 2009|publisher=Anthem Press|isbn=978-81-907570-2-7|pages=272–}} < /ref >
