 '''Steenbecque''' ({{IPA-fr|stɛ̃bɛk}}; {{lang-nl|Steenbeke or Steenbeeke}}) is a [[Communes in France|commune]] in the [[Nord (department)|Nord]] department in north [[France]]. In 2019, 1,673 people lived there.
