 '''Oberstammheim''' was [[municipality]] in the [[district]] of [[Andelfingen (district)|Andelfingen]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Zurich (canton)|Zurich]] in [[Switzerland]]. On 1 January 2019, the municipalities Oberstammheim, [[Unterstammheim]] and [[Waltalingen]] joined together to become new municipality [[Stammheim, Zurich|Stammheim]].
