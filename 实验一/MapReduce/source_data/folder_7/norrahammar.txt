 '''Norrahammar''' is a part of the [[Jönköping]] locality ( " town " ). It has been part of the town since the 1970s. It is located within the [[Jönköping Municipality]] in the [[counties of Sweden|county]] of [[Jönköping County|Jönköping]] in [[Sweden]].
