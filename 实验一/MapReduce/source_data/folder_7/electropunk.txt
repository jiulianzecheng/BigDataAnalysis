'''Electropunk''' (or '''electronic punk''', '''synthpunk''') is a type of music.  It is a mixture of electronic [[rock and roll|rock]] with [[punk rock]]. Famous artists who make electropunk music are [[the Screamers]], [[the Units]] and [[Le Shok]].
