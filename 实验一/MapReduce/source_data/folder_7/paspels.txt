 '''Paspels''' ({{lang-rm|Pasqual}}) was a [[municipality]] in the [[district]] of [[Hinterrhein (district)|Hinterrhein]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Graubünden]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 On 1 January 2015, the municipalities of Paspels, [[Almens]], [[Pratval]], [[Rodels]] and [[Tomils]] joined together and became a new municipality called [[Domleschg]].
