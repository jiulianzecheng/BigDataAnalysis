 A '''mug''' is a [[container]] for [[drinking]]. It has one [[Handle (grip)|handle]] on the side. It is usually made of [[ceramic]] or [[steel]]. People might talk about getting ''a mug of [[beer]]'' at a local [[Public house|pub]]. They might also drink a mug of [[coffee]] in the morning.
