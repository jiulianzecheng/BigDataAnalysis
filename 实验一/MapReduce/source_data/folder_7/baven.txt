 '''Baven''' is a village in the municipality of [[Südheide (municipality)|Südheide]] in the north of [[Celle (district)|Celle]] in [[Lower Saxony]], [[Germany]]. It is on the western edge of the [[Südheide Nature Park]], on the [[Lüneburg Heath]].
