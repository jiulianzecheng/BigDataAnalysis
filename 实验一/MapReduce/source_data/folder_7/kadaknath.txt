 '''Kadaknath''', also called '''Kali Masi''' ({{translation}} " [[fowl]] having black flesh " ), is an [[India]]n breed of [[chicken]]. Kadaknath birds are grey-black all over. They have gold [[plumage]] with greenish [[iridescence]]. The greyish black colour is in the legs and toenails, [[beak]], tongue, [[comb (anatomy)|comb]] and [[wattle (anatomy)|wattles]]. Even the meat, bones and organs are grey. This bird is considered the National [[poultry]] of India.
