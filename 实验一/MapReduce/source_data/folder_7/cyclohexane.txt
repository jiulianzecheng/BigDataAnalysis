 '''Cyclohexane''' is the [[cycloalkane]] [[molecule]] with the [[chemical formula|formula]] {{chem|[[Carbon|C]]|6|[[Hydrogen|H]]|12}}, with 6 carbon [[atom]]s connected to each other in a ring shape, and each connected to two hydrogen atoms. It is used when making [[adipic acid]] and [[caprolactam]], which can them be used to make [[nylon]]. It has no [[color]] and is [[flammable]].
