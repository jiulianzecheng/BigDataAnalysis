 {{nihongo|'''Sakigake'''|さきがけ||}} is the name of the a [[Japan]]ese [[space probe]] which was sent on an interplanetary mission. < ref > Sakigake was identical in construction and shape to [[Suisei]], but carried a different payload. < /ref > 
 
 The launch was a project of the [[Institute of Space and Astronautical Science]] of the [[University of Tokyo]]. 
 
 The satellite was launched on January 7, 1985. < ref > JAXA, [http://www.isas.ac.jp/e/enterp/missions/catalogue.shtml  " Catalogue of ISAS Missions " ] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20141231025049/http://www.isas.ac.jp/e/enterp/missions/catalogue.shtml |date=2014-12-31 }}; retrieved 2012-4-16. < /ref >
