 '''Kohima''' ([[Angami language|Angami Naga]]: ''Kewhira'') is the [[capital city]] of the [[India]]n state of [[Nagaland]]. It has a population of about 100,000. The [[Battle of Kohima]] was fought in Kohima and it is often referred to as one of the bloodiest battles of [[World War II]].
