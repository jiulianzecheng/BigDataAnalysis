 '''Ghirone''' was a [[municipality]] of the district [[Blenio (district)|Blenio]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Ticino]] in [[Switzerland]]. On 22 October 2006, the former municipalities of [[Aquila, Switzerland|Aquila]], [[Campo (Blenio)]], Ghirone, [[Olivone]] and [[Torre, Switzerland|Torre]] merged to form the new municipality of [[Blenio]]. < ref name=Fusion > [http://www.bfs.admin.ch/bfs/portal/de/index/infothek/nomenklaturen/blank/blank/gem_liste/03.html Amtliches Gemeindeverzeichnis der Schweiz] published by the Swiss Federal Statistical Office {{in lang|de}} accessed 14 January 2010 < /ref > 
 
 It is no longer a municipality.
