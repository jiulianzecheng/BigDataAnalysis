 '''Tittmoning''' ({{IPA-de|ˈtɪtmɔnɪŋ|lang|De-Tittmoning.ogg}}) is a [[Town#Germany|town]] in [[Traunstein (district)|Traunstein]], in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 It is in the historic [[Rupertiwinkel]] region, on the left edge of the river [[Salzach]], which forms the border with [[Ostermiething]] in the [[Austria]]n state of [[Salzburg (state)|Salzburg]]. The two communes are linked by a bridge. Tittmoning is about {{convert|30|km|mi}} northwest of the [[Salzburg]] city centre.
