 '''Peter Senerchia''' (born [[October 11]] [[1967]]) is an [[Americans|American]] retired [[professional wrestling|professional wrestler]] and current [[color commentator]] best known by his [[ring name]] '''Tazz''', (originally '''Tazmaniac''' and later shortened to simply '''Taz'''). As of 2023, he worked for [[All Elite Wrestling]] (AEW} as a commentator. He has also worked for [[Total Nonstop Action Wrestling]] (TNA). On the 3rd April, 2009 he was dismissed by [[WWE]].
