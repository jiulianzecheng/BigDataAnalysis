 '''Uppalapati Venkata Suryanarayana Prabhas Raju''', popularly known as '''Prabhas''', is an Indian actor who works mainly in [[Telugu cinema]] industry. He is one of the highest-paid actors in [[Indian cinema]]. < ref > {{Cite news|last=Amena|first=Rasti|date=26 November 2021|title=With 150cr per film, who is the highest paid actor in India?|work=The Siasat Daily|url=https://www.siasat.com/with-150cr-per-film-prabhas-is-the-highest-paid-actor-in-india-2231120/}} < /ref >  In the year 2015, Prabhas starred in the movie, ''[[Baahubali: The Beginning|Baahubali]]'', which is one of the highest grossing movies of Indian cinema and later reprised his titular role in ''[[Baahubali 2: The Conclusion]]'' (2017), which became the first Indian film in the history of Indian cinema to gross ₹1000 crores at the box office.
