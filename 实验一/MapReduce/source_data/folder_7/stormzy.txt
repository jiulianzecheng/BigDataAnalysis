 '''Michael Ebenezer Kwadjo Omari Owuo Jr.''' (born 26 July 1993), better known by his [[stage name]] '''Stormzy''', is a [[United Kingdom|British]] [[Rapping|rapper]] and [[singer]]. He has collaborated with [[Ed Sheeran]] on the singles  " [[Take Me Back to London]] "  and  " [[Own It]] "  (alongside [[Burna Boy]]).  
 ==Works== 
 In 2017, he released his first album ''[[Gangs, Signs,  &  Prayers]]''. The album included the single  " [[Blinded By Your Grace]] "  featuring [[MNEK]]. He featured on a remix of Ed Sheeran's  " [[Shape of You]] "  which reached number one on the [[UK Singles Chart]]. In December 2019, he released his second album ''[[Heavy Is The Head]]''. The album included the singles  " [[Vossi Bop (song)|Vossi Bop]] "  and  " [[Own It]] "  (featuring Ed Sheeran and [[Burna Boy]]).  
 
 He has worked with other artists like [[Charlie Sloth]] and [[Jaykae]].  
 
 Stormzy has supported [[Aitch (rapper)|Aitch]] and [[Chip (rapper)|Chip]] supported Stormzy. He won the [[Mercury Prize]].
