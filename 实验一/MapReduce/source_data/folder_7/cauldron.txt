 A '''cauldron''' or '''caldron''' (from Latin ''[[caldarium]]'', hot bath) is a large [[metal]] [[Cooking pot|pot]] ([[kettle]]) for cooking and/or boiling over an open [[fire]].  It is often associated with [[witch]]es.  Usually, the metal used is [[cast iron]].  A cauldron is also a measure used in measuring [[coal]].
