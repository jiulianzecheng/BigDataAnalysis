'''Hippopotomonstrosesquippedaliophobia''' is a [[phobia]], meaning the fear of long [[word|words]]. Ironically, it is a extraordinary lengthy word itself.  
 
 The phobia isn't considered an actual phobia, but more of a social phobia. < ref > {{Cite web|date=2021-03-18|title=What Is Hippopotomonstroses-quippedaliophobia?|url=https://www.healthline.com/health/hippopotomonstrosesquippedaliophobia|access-date=2021-04-16|website=Healthline|language=en}} < /ref >  The phobia is the second longest word in the [[English language]]. Another word for the phobia is Sesquipedalophobia.
