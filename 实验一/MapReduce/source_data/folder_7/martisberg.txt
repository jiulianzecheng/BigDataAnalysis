 '''Martisberg''' was a [[municipality]] in the [[district]] of [[Raron (district)|Raron]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Valais]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 On 1 January 2014, the former municipalities of Martisberg and [[Betten]] merged into the new municipality of [[Bettmeralp]].
