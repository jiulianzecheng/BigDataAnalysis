 '''''Interiors''''' is a 1978 American drama movie directed by [[Woody Allen]] and stars [[Geraldine Page]], [[Diane Keaton]], [[Mary Beth Hurt]], [[Maureen Stapleton]], [[Sam Waterston]], [[Kristin Griffin]]. It was distributed by [[United Artists]] and was nominated for 5 Oscars in 1979.
