'''Postum''' is a [[drink]] first made by [[C. W. Post]] in [[1895]]. It is a [[powder]] [[substitute]] for [[coffee]] that is mixed with [[water]]. The [[ingredient]]s are [[wheat bran]], [[wheat]], [[molasses]], and [[corn dextrin]]. 
 
 It is [[advertisement|advertised]] as a healthier choice of drink than coffee.
