 '''Neoaves''' is a [[clade]]. It includes all modern [[bird]]s ([[Neornithes]] or [[Aves]]) except [[Paleognathae]] ([[ratite]]s and kin) and [[Galloanserae]] ([[duck]]s, [[chicken]]s and [[kin]]). < ref name= " jarvis2014 " > Jarvis E.D. et al. 2014 Whole-genome analyses resolve early branches in the tree of life of modern birds. ''Science''. '''346''' (6215): 1320–1331. [https://science.sciencemag.org/content/346/6215/1320] < /ref > 
 
 The [[implication (grammar)|implication]] of this is that the two exceptions  may have originated from [[dinobird]] lines other than the great mass of birds. That is not proven, but it is suggested by this [[taxonomy]].
