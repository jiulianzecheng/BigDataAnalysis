 '''Teublitz''' ({{IPA-de|ˈtɔɪplɪt͡s|lang|De-Teublitz.ogg}}) is a [[town]] in [[Schwandorf (district)|Schwandorf]], in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. It is on the river [[Naab]], 12 & nbsp;km south of [[Schwandorf]], and 23 & nbsp;km north of [[Regensburg]].
