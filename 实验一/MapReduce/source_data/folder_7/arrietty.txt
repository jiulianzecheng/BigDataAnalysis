 '''''Arrietty''''' (called {{nihongo|'''''The Borrower Arrietty'''''|借りぐらしのアリエッティ|Kari-gurashi no Arietti}} in [[Japan]] and  '''''The Secret World of Arrietty''''' in [[North America]]) is a 2010 [[Anime|Japanese animated]] [[fantasy]] [[movie]] directed by [[Hiromasa Yonebayashi]], written by [[Hayao Miyazaki]] and [[Keiko Niwa]] and produced by [[Studio Ghibli]], based on [[Mary Norton (author)|Mary Norton]]'s novel ''[[The Borrowers]]''. The movie is about a young Borrower named Arrietty. The movie stars the voices of [[Mirai Shida]] as the titular character, [[Ryunosuke Kamiki]] as Sho, and [[Tatsuya Fujiwara]] as Spiller. 
 
 The North American version of the movie was made by [[Walt Disney Pictures|Disney]], and stars [[Bridgit Mendler]] as Arrietty, [[David Henre]] as Sho (renamed Shawn), and [[Moisés Arias]] as Spiller. It also has [[Will Arnett]] as Arrietty's father Pod, and [[Amy Poehler]] as her mother Homily.
