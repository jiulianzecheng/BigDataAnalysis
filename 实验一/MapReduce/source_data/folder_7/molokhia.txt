 '''Molokhia''' or '''Molokhiyya''' (various spellings based on the {{lang-arz|ملوخيه}}) is an Egyptian [[soup]]y [[vegetable]] dish. It is made from [[leaves]] of ''Corchorus olitorius'', commonly known as Nalta jute, tossa jute, and Jew's mallow. < ref > {{citation |url=http://www.ars-grin.gov/cgi-bin/npgs/html/taxon.pl?11458 |title=USDA GRIN Taxonomy |access-date=21 April 2016 |archive-date=24 September 2015 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150924133804/http://www.ars-grin.gov/cgi-bin/npgs/html/taxon.pl?11458 |url-status=dead }} < /ref >   It is an Egyptian dish in Egypt. It is commonly served in other forms in the eastern [[Mediterranean]] area, the [[Levant]] and [[North Africa]].
