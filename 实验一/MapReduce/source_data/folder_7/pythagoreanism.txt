 '''Pythagoreanism''' is a term used for the [[esoteric]] and [[metaphysics|metaphysical]] beliefs held by [[Pythagoras]] and his followers, the Pythagoreans, who were much influenced by [[mathematics]]. They thought the world was made of numbers, and that various [[integer]] < nowiki/ > s had distinct sacred meanings in their [[numerology]]. < ref > {{Cite news|url=http://classicalwisdom.com/cult-of-pythagoras/|title=The Cult Of Pythagoras|date=2014-07-21|newspaper=Classical Wisdom Weekly|access-date=2018-01-21|language=en-US}} < /ref > 
 
 Later resurgence of ideas similar to those held by the early Pythagoreans are collected under the term [[Neopythagoreanism]].
