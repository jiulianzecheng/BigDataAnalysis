 '''vistlip''' is a [[Japan]]ese [[visual kei]] [[Rock music|rock]] band. The band formed in 7 July 2007 and is currently signed to Delfi Sound and [[Marvelous Entertainment]]. 
 
 == Members == 
 *'''Tomo''' (born 13 January in [[Fukuoka Prefecture]]), singer 
 *'''Yuh''' (born 28 July in [[Tokyo]], < ref > [http://d3j5vwomefv46c.cloudfront.net/photos/large/730352263.jpg?1360596027  " SINDRA Trading Card No. 002 " ]{{Dead link|date=January 2021 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }} Picture on Twitpic.com < /ref >  [[Japan]]), guitarist 
 *'''Umi''' (born 20 July in [[Tokyo]]), guitarist 
 *'''Rui''' (born 15 November in [[Hyōgo Prefecture]]), bass guitarist  < ref > {{Cite web|author=Marvelous Entertainment|date=2008-12-11|url=http://www.myspace.com/vistlip|title=About vistlip|work=vistlip official myspace|publisher=[[MySpace]]|accessdate=2009-01-25}} < /ref > 
 *'''Tohya''' (born 3 January in [[Kanagawa Prefecture]]), drummer
