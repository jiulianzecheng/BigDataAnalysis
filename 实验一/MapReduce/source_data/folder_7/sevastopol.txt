 '''Sevastopol''' ({{lang-uk|Севастополь}}, formerly called  '''Sebastopol''') is a large [[seaport]] and city. It is on the [[Crimea]]n Peninsula, on the north side of the [[Black Sea]]. 380,000 people live in the city. It was started in 1783. The port was shared by naval forces of [[Ukraine]] and [[Russia]] until 2014 when Russia took control.{{Subdivisions of Russia}}{{Hero Cities of the Soviet Union}}{{commonscat}}
