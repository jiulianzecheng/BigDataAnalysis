 '''''Charade''''' is a 1963 American romantic mystery comedy movie directed by [[Stanley Donen]] and was based on the 1961 short story by [[Peter Stone]] and [[Marc Behm]]. It stars [[Cary Grant]], [[Audrey Hepburn]], [[James Coburn]], [[George Kennedy]], [[Walter Matthau]] and was distributed by [[Universal Pictures]] and was nominated for an Academy Award in 1964. It was remade as ''[[The Truth About Charlie]]'' in 2002.
