'''Iroh''' is a [[fictional character]] in [[Nickelodeon (TV channel)|Nickelodeon's]] [[anime|animated]] [[television programme|television series]] ''[[Avatar: The Last Airbender]]''. The character and series were created by [[Michael Dante DiMartino]] and [[Bryan Konietzko]].  
 
 Iroh is [[voice actor|voiced]] by [[Mako Iwamatsu|Mako]] in seasons 1 and 2, [[Greg Baldwin]] & nbsp;onwards.
