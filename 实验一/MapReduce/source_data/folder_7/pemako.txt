'''Pemako''' is a [[region]] in the southeast corner of [[Tibet]]. It is considered to be the [[Sacred site|sacred place]] for [[Tibetan people|Tibetans]], and a place for [[pilgrimage]]s. Many popular [[lama]]s visited the place in search of [[terma]]s, which were hidden during the crisis in Tibet.
