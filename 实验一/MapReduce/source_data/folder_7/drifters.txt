 '''Drifters''' is [[dansband]] from [[Skövde]] in [[Sweden]]. It was formed in 1962 and has won many prizes. They are signed to the Lionheart International record label. 
 
 ==Members== 
 In its existence since 1962 there were a number of important changes. 
 ;Lead singers 
 *1962–1989: Johnny Wendelnäs 
 *1989-1989: Ann-Charlotte Berndtsson 
 *1989–1996: Marie Arturén 
 *1996–1999: Ann-Charlotte Andersson (married and changed to Strandell in 2003) 
 *1999–: Erica Sjöström 
 ;Bass 
 *1968–2004: Leif Svensson 
 *2004–2009: Kent Liljefjäll  
 *2009– Henrik  " Turbo "  Wallrin  
 ;Keyboards 
 *1969–1980: Lennart Green 
 *1983– Stellan Hedevik 
 ;Drums 
 *1983–2005: Robert Muhrer 
 *2005–2011: Mattias Berghorn 
 *2011– Tim Nilsson 
 
 ==In popular culture== 
 They also sang the theme song for television series ''Möbelhandlarens'', named  " När du var här " , in 2006. 
 
 ==Awards and nominations== 
 *In 2004, the band was voted as best dansband at Svenska dansbandsveckan i Malung in 2004. They got 50% of the people's vote. They were awarded the Guldklaven competing against [[Lasse Stefanz]] (35% of the votes) and Black Jack (15%). 
 *In 2007, they were nominated for a Swedish Grammy for  " Best Dance/Pop " .
