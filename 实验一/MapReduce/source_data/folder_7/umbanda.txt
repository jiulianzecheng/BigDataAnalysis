'''Umbanda''' is the only 100% Brazilian religion. It mixes [[Candomblé]], [[Spiritism|Kardecism]] as well as [[Roman Catholicism]]. It was created on November 15, 1908 in Niterói, a city from [[Rio de Janeiro]], by Zélio de Morais and a [[Spirit]] called Caboclo das Sete Encruzilhadas.
