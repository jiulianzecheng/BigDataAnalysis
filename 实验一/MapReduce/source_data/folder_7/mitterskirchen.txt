 '''Mitterskirchen''' is a [[Municipalities of Germany|municipality]] in [[Rottal-Inn]] in [[Bavaria]] in [[Germany]]. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 Mitterskirchen is in a small side-valley of the [[Rott (Inn, Neuhaus am Inn)|Rott]], bordering on [[Upper Bavaria]]. Mitterskirchen is at the federal highway B588, about seven kilometers south-west of [[Eggenfelden]], 18 kilometers north of [[Altötting]], 23 kilometers north-east of [[Mühldorf am Inn|Mühldorf]], as well as 22 kilometers south-west of [[Pfarrkirchen]]. The next train station on the railway line [[Rottalbahn|Passau - Mühldorf]] is in Eggenfelden.
