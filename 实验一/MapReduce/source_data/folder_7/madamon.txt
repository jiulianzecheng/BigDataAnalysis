 '''Madamon''' is a village in [[Pathanamthitta District]], [[Kerala]] state, [[India]]. < ref > {{Cite web |url=http://iay.nic.in/netiay/pv_ntnew.aspx?page=d & state_name=KERALA & state_code=16 & fin_year=2012-2013 |title=Indira Awaas Yojana |access-date=2020-07-23 |archive-date=2014-03-04 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140304034601/http://iay.nic.in/netiay/pv_ntnew.aspx?page=d & state_name=KERALA & state_code=16 & fin_year=2012-2013 |url-status=dead }} < /ref >  It is situated near [[Pamba river]] in the [[Western Ghats]]. 
 
 ==Economy== 
 
 Agriculture is the main income of the people. [[Rubber]] plantation, cattle rearing, and cultivation of crops like [[tapioca]], [[betel leaf]], etc are the other major sources of money. 
 
 ==Worship centres== 
 
 ===Hrishikesha Temple=== 
 Hrishikesha temple is one of the major temple in this village. ''Pathamudayam'' is the major festival in this temple. 
 
 ==Tourism== 
 
 This village is place in the way of Sabarimala piligrims.
