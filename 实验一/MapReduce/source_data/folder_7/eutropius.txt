'''Eutropius''' was a [[polytheist]] [[historian]] for [[Julian the Apostate]] in the late 4th century. 
 
 == Writings == 
 Eutropius wrote a ten-book summary of [[Ancient Rome|Roman]] [[history]] called ''Historiae romanae breviarium'' between the years of 350 and 370. This is his only known writing.
