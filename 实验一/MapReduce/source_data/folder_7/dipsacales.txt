 The '''Dipsacales''' are an order of [[flowering plant]]s, included within the asterid group of [[dicotyledon]]s. Under the APG II definition some well-known members of Dipsacales are [[honeysuckle]], [[Elderberry|elder]], [[viburnum]], and [[valerian]].
