 '''''Beginners''''' is a 2010 [[United States|American]] [[romance movie|romantic]]-[[comedy movie|comedy]]-[[drama movie]] directed by [[Mike Mills (director)|Mike Mills]]. It is about the story of Oliver, a man remembering the life and death from [[cancer]] of his widowed [[gay]] father, Hal, while trying to work a new romantic relationship with a [[French people|French]]woman, Anna.  
 
 It stars [[Christopher Plummer]] and [[Ewan McGregor]]. It was released to positive [[review]]s on June 3, 2011. This movie lead to Plummer's first [[Academy Award]] win.
