 '''Echosmith''' is an [[United States|American]] [[indie pop]] group. The group consists of four siblings: Graham, Sydney, Noah and Jamie Sierota. < ref > {{cite news|author=Brian Mansfield|date=31 August 2014|title=On the Verge: Now, the kids of Echosmith are cool|newspaper=USA Today|url=https://www.usatoday.com/story/life/music/2014/08/31/echosmith-cool-kids-on-the-verge/14615615/|accessdate=6 September 2014}} < /ref >  They are best known for their songs  " Cool Kids "  and  " Bright " . Their first album, ''Talking Dreams'', was released in October 2013. 
 
 They also had [[cover version|cover]]s of  " Lights "  by [[Ellie Goulding]] and  " [[Set Fire to the Rain]] "  by [[Adele]]. 
 
 The group is from [[Los Angeles]].
