 '''Osilo''' (''Ósile, Ósili, Ósilu'') is a [[town]] and ''[[Commune|comune]]'' (municipality) in the [[Province of Sassari]] in [[Sardinia]], [[Italy]]. As of 2016, 3,059 people lived there. < ref > {{Cite web |title=Istituto Nazionale di Statistica - ''Bilancio demografico al 30/11/2016'' |url=http://demo.istat.it/bilmens2016gen/index02.html |access-date=2019-04-12 |archive-date=2019-03-11 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190311225842/http://demo.istat.it/bilmens2016gen/index02.html |url-status=dead }} < /ref >  Its area is 98.03 & nbsp;km². It is 672 meters above sea level.
