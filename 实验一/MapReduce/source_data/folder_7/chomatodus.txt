'''''Chomatodus''''' (from [[Greek language|Greek]]: χωμα ''choma'', 'mound' and Greek: ὀδούς ''odoús'' 'tooth') is an extinct genus of [[Chondrichthyes|cartilaginous fish]] belonging to the family [[Petalodontidae]].

 == Description == 
 We don't know what it looks like. Some people draw it as a slender [[shark]]. Others mistake it for a [[Perciformes|perciform]] fish. So far, people have found [[Tooth|teeth]] and tooth [[Fossil|fossils]] instead of complete fossils.
