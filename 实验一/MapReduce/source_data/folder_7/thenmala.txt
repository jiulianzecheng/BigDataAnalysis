 '''Thenmala''' is a town of tourist attraction near [[Punalur]] town, [[Kollam district]] in [[Kerala]], [[India]]. 
 
 ==Eco-tourism== 
 Thenmala is the first planned [[Ecotourism|eco-tourism]] centre in India. The Thenmala Dam is a popular ecotourism centre in Kerala. Thenmala dam is the second largest irrigation project in Kerala. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.tourinkerala.com/thenmala|title=Touring in Thenmala}} < /ref > < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.nativeplanet.com/thenmala/photos/2927/|title=Photos of Thenmala}} < /ref >
