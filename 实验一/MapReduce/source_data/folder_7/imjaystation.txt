'''Jason Matthew Ethier''' (born February 14, 1990), also known as '''ImJayStation''' or '''JayStation''', is a Internet personality and former YouTuber from [[Canada]]. 
 
 He began as JayStation. In 2016, he was arrested by [[Ottawa]] police for trespassing for doing  " 24-hour overnight challenges " . < ref > {{Cite web|last=Schnurr|first=Joanne|date=2016-11-15|title=Ottawa Police say trespassing charges to be laid against 24-hour 'overnight challenger'|url=https://ottawa.ctvnews.ca/ottawa-police-say-trespassing-charges-to-be-laid-against-24-hour-overnight-challenger-1.3162136|access-date=2021-08-14|website=Ottawa|language=en}} < /ref >  As such, his channel got demonetized. After the arrest he went under the new name ImJayStation.
