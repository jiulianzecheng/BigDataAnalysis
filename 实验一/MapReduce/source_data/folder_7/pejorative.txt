A '''pejorative''' (also called a '''derogatory term''', < ref > {{cite dictionary|url=http://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/derogatory|title=Definition of derogatory|dictionary=[[Merriam Webster]]|access-date=2 May 2015}} < /ref >  a '''slur''', a '''term of disparagement''') is a word or grammatical form of expressing a negative term of someone or something, showing a lack of respect for someone or something. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://dictionary.reference.com/browse/pejorative |title=Pejorative  & #124; Define Pejorative at Dictionary.com |publisher=Dictionary.reference.com |access-date=2012-04-25}} < /ref > 
 
 It is also used to express [[criticism]], [[hatred|hostility]], or [[wikt:disregard|disregard]]. 
 
 Sometimes, a term is seen as pejorative in some social or ethnic groups but not in others, or may be originally pejorative and eventually be adopted in a non-pejorative sense.
