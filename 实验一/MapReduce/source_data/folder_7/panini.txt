 '''Paninis''' are a type of [[Italy|Italian]] sandwich that is made of Italian [[bread]] and fillings that are just either [[meat]], [[cheese]], or [[Vegetable|vegetables]]. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Panini {{!}} Traditional Sandwich type From Italy {{!}} TasteAtlas|url=https://www.tasteatlas.com/panini|access-date=2021-05-09|website=www.tasteatlas.com}} < /ref >  The panini is then [[Toaster|toasted]] or [[Grill (cooking)|grilled]], and served at a warm temperature.
