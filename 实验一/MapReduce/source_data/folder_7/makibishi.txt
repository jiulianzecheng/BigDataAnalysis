[[Image:Makibishi.JPG|thumb| Japanese  " makibishi "  iron spikes, a type of [[caltrop]]]] 
 '''Makibishi''' are a small flat [[spiked]] objects that were used in [[feudal]] [[Japan]]. The [[samurai]] used them to slow down or stop their enemies.
