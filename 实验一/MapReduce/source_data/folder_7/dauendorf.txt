 '''Dauendorf''' is a [[Communes in France|commune]]. It is in [[Grand Est]] in the [[Bas-Rhin]] [[Departments of France|department]] in northeast [[France]]. It is the site of [[Neubourg Abbey]], a former [[Cistercian]] monastery, destroyed during the [[French Revolution]].
