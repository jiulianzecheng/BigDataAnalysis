'''Toninho''' is a [[Portuguese language|Portuguese]] diminutive [[nickname]] for Antônio or António in use in [[Portugal]], [[Brazil]], [[South Africa]], [[Namibia]], [[Mozambique]] and [[Angola]]. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.name-doctor.com/name-toninho-meaning-of-toninho-14517.html|title=Toninho|website=Name-doctor.com|access-date=November 1, 2019}} < /ref >  It is loosely equivalent to Little Tony in [[English language|English]].
