 '''Liperi''' is a [[municipality]] in [[North Karelia]], [[Finland]]. It had about 12,500 living there in April 2014. The municipalities next to it are [[Heinävesi]], [[Joensuu]], [[Kontiolahti]], [[Polvijärvi]], [[Rääkkylä]], [[Savonlinna]] and [[Outokumpu]].
