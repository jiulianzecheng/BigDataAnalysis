'''Terminal''' can mean: 
 
 * [[Airport terminal]] - one of the main [[building]]s at an [[airport]]. 
 * A [[bus station]] or [[train station]] 
 * Another [[transportation]] building 
 * [[Computer terminal]] - text-based system to access computers 
 * [[Terminal velocity]] - the fastest speed in [[freefall]]. 
 * [[Terminal illness]] - a rapidly progressing [[disease]] likely to cause death.
