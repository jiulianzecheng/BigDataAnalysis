'''Bhaderwah''' ({{lang-ur|تحصیل برامئللاھ}}) is a town and a notified area committee in [[Doda District]] in the Indian-occupied state of [[Jammu and Kashmir|Maqbuza Kashmir]], Kashmir region. Bhaderwah valley is mostly a mountainous town that is 80 & nbsp;km from Batote.
