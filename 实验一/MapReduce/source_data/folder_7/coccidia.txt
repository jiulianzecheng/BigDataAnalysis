 '''Coccidia''' is a [[Class (biology)|subclass]] of microscopic, spore-forming, single-celled obligate parasites belonging to the [[apicomplexa]]n class [[Conoidasida]]. Coccidian parasites infect the intestinal tracts of animals, and are the largest group of apicomplexan [[protozoa]].
