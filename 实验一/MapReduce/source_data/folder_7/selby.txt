 '''Selby''' is a town in [[North Yorkshire]], [[England]], [[UK]].   In the 2021 UK Census 19,760 people were living there. < ref name= " 2021 census " > {{cite web |title=Yorkshire and the Humber (United Kingdom): Counties and Unitary Districts  &  Settlements - Population Statistics, Charts and Map |url=https://www.citypopulation.de/en/uk/yorkshireandthehumber/ |website=www.citypopulation.de |access-date=24 January 2023}} < /ref > 
 
 It is on the [[River Ouse]] which is joined to Leeds by the [[Selby Canal.|Selby Canal]] and used to be a centre of ship building.  It is not far from the border with the [[East Riding of Yorkshire]]. 
 
 [[Selby Abbey]], started in the 11th century is in the centre of the town. There was a great [[fire]] there in [[1906]]. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Fire and Restoration|url=https://www.selbyabbey.org.uk/fire-restoration/|access-date=2023-07-09|website=Selby Abbey|language=en-GB}} < /ref >
