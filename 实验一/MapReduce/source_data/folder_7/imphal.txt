 '''Imphal''' ({{lang-mni|ꯏꯝꯐꯥꯜ|eem-phaal}}, {{lang-omp|ꯌꯨꯝꯐꯥꯜ|yoom-phaal}} {{IPA-all|ɪmˈfɑl||Imphal.ogg}}) is the [[capital city]] of the [[India]]n [[States of India|state]] of [[Manipur]]. The ancient [[Kangla]] is in the heart of the city, which was the center of administration of many rulers in both [[Ancient Manipur]] as well as [[Medieval Manipur]].
