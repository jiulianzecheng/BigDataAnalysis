'''Pugieu''' is a former [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is found in the [[Regions of France|region]] [[Auvergne-Rhône-Alpes]] in the [[Ain]] [[Departments of France|department]]  in the east of [[France]]. On 1 January 2017, it was merged into the commune of [[Chazey-Bons]]. < ref > [https://www.legifrance.gouv.fr/eli/arrete/2016/6/30/INTB1619096A/jo/texte Arrêté préfectoral] 30 June 2016 {{in lang|fr}} < /ref >
