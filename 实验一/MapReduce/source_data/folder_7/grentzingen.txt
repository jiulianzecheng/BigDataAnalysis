 '''Grentzingen''' is a former [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is found in the [[Haut-Rhin]] [[Departments of France|department]] of eastern [[France]]. On 1 January 2016, it was merged into the new commune of [[Illtal]]. < ref > [http://www.haut-rhin.gouv.fr/content/download/12381/85035/file/RAA%20n%C2%B0%2056%201%C3%A8re%20partie.pdf Arrêté] 17 December 2015 {{in lang|fr}} < /ref > 
 
 It is 25 & nbsp;km west of [[Basel]] and 10 & nbsp;km southeast of [[Altkirch]].
