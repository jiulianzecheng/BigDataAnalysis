 '''Saefullah''' (11 February 1964  & ndash; 16 September 2020) was an Indonesian politician and educator. He was born in [[Jakarta]], [[Indonesia]]. He was the Mayor of [[Central Jakarta]] from 2010 to 2014. He was also Regional Secretary of Jakarta from 2014 until his death.  
 
 Saefullah died on 16 September 2020 at a hospital in Jakarta from [[COVID-19]], aged 56. < ref > {{cite news |last1=Utama |first1=Pradita |title=Jenazah Sekda DKI Saefullah Dimakamkan di Makam Keluarga |url=https://news.detik.com/foto-news/d-5175899/jenazah-sekda-dki-saefullah-dimakamkan-di-makam-keluarga |access-date=16 September 2020 |work=detiknews |date=16 September 2020 |language=id-ID}} < /ref >
