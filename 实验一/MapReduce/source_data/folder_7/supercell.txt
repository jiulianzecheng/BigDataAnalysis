 A '''supercell''' is a strong type of [[thunderstorm]] with a thick, rotating [[vertical draft|updraft]] (a [[mesocyclone]]). < ref > {{Cite web |url=http://www.stormchasers.au.com/lemon7.htm |title=12B |access-date=2007-09-25 |archive-date=2013-07-30 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130730065045/http://www.stormchasers.au.com/lemon7.htm |url-status=dead }} < /ref >  Supercell thunderstorms are the largest, most dangerous type of thunderstorms.{{fact|date=April 2009}} There are really only two types of thunderstorms: supercell and ordinary, but some have four classifications: single-cell, multi-cell, [[squall|squall line]], and supercell.
