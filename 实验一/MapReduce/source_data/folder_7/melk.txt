 '''Melk''' is a city in the [[Austria]]n state of [[Lower Austria]]. It is the capital of the district of the [[Melk District|same name]]. It is best known as the site of a massive [[baroque]] [[Benedictine]] [[monastery]] named [[Melk Abbey]]. The city has a long history, it was first mentioned in the 9th century. Part of the city is a [[World Heritage Site|UNESCO world heritage site]].
