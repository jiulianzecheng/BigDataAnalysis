 '''''Altron''''' is a [[Japan]]ese [[video game]] developer and publisher founded in 1983. It has developed or published games for the [[Nintendo 64]], [[PlayStation]], [[Sega Saturn]], [[Super Nintendo]], [[Sega Mega Drive]], [[Nintendo Entertainment System]], [[Nintendo DS]], [[Game Boy]] and [[Game Boy Color]] video game systems as well as for the [[personal computer|PC]]. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Altron {{!}} Video Game Developer {{!}} VideoGameGeek|url=https://videogamegeek.com/videogamepublisher/13258/altron|access-date=2021-05-20|website=videogamegeek.com|language=en-US}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite web|title=Altron Corporation|url=https://www.mobygames.com/company/altron-corporation|access-date=2021-05-20|website=MobyGames}} < /ref >
