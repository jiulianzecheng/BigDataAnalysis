 '''Babahoyo''' ({{IPA-es|baβaˈoʝo}}), founded September 30, 1948, is the capital of the [[Los Ríos Province|Los Ríos]] province of [[Ecuador]]. Its population is cited around 153,000.  It is bordered by two rivers, the [[San Pablo River (Ecuador)|San Pablo]] and the [[Caracol River (Ecuador)|Caracol]].
