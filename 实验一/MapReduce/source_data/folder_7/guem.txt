 '''Guem''', stage name of '''Abdelmajid Guemguem''' (9 March 1947 – 22 January 2021) was an Algerian percussionist. He composed ''Le Serpent'', re-recorded in 1996 for the program ''{{ill|Ça se discute|fr}}'' on [[France 2]]. In 2011, he composed ''Mon Paris''. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://cba.fro.at/294393|title=GUEM – le rythme c´est la vie|date=15 August 2015|work=Cultural Broadcasting Archive|language=fr|url-status=dead|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160106202046/http://cba.fro.at/294393|archive-date=6 January 2016|access-date=27 January 2021}} < /ref > 
 
 Guem died on 22 January 2021 in [[Paris]], aged 73. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.liberte-algerie.com/culture/abdelmadjid-guemguem-alias-guem-est-decede-352895|title=Abdelmadjid Guemguem, alias Guem, est décédé|date=24 January 2021|work=Liberté Algérie|language=fr}} < /ref >
