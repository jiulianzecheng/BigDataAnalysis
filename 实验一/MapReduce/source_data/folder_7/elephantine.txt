 '''Elephantine''' is an island in the [[Nile|River Nile]], which forms part of the city of [[Aswan]], [[Egypt]]. It is about {{Convert|1200|m||abbr=}} by {{Convert|400|m||abbr=}} in size. People have lived there since [[Ancient Egypt|Ancient Egyptian]] times. Today, there are a number of [[ruins]] on the island. It also features a [[botanical garden]] with a large collection of [[Palm tree|palm trees]].
