 '''Suez''' ({{lang-ar|السويس As Suwais}},  Egyptian pronunciation ''Es Suwais'') is a [[town]] in Northern Egypt, on the coast of the [[Gulf of Suez]].  Almost 750.000 people live in the city. The city is the southern terminus of the [[Suez Canal]]. It has two harbours, and a large [[seaport]].  An [[oil refinery]] is in Suez. [[Highway]]s and [[railroad]] tracks connect Suez to the cities of [[Cairo]], [[Port Said]] and [[Ismailia]].
