 '''Moratuwa''' is a large [[suburb]] of the city of [[Colombo]], in [[Sri Lanka]]. It is 18 & nbsp;km south of the city centre. Moratuwa is surrounded on three sides by water, except in the north of the city, by the [[Indian Ocean]] on the west and the Bolgoda lake on the east. The 2001 census recorded a population of 177,190. < ref name= " World Gazetteer " > {{cite web|url=http://www.world-gazetteer.com/wg.php?x=1208411424 & men=gcis & lng=en & des=wg & geo=-127 & srt=npan & col=adhoq & msz=1500 & pt=c & va= & srt=pnan|title=Sri Lanka: largest cities and towns and statistics of their population|accessdate=2010-10-09|archiveurl=https://archive.today/20121216141527/http://www.world-gazetteer.com/wg.php?x=1208411424 & men=gcis & lng=en & des=wg & geo=-127 & srt=npan & col=adhoq & msz=1500 & pt=c & va= & srt=pnan|archivedate=2012-12-16|url-status=dead}} < /ref > 
 
 The [[University of Moratuwa]] is in the suburb. There is also a public [[library]] on Galle Road, which was opened in 1987. It was built using the funds raised by Moratuwa Municipal Council to the amount of Rupees 50 Lakhs and 15 laks by the Japanese Organization  " World Expansion conservative " . The library was built according to Japanese architecture.
