 '''Pallor''' (also known as '''paleness''') is when the [[skin]] is a pale color. < ref > {{cite web|title=Paleness|publisher=Healthline|url=http://www.healthline.com/health/paleness#Definition1|accessdate=Feb 21, 2015}} < /ref >  It can be caused by [[illness]], stress or even shock.  
 
 Pallor is seen most easily on the [[face]] and [[hand|palms]]. It can develop suddenly or slowly. 
 
 Causes of pallor may include [[migraine]]s, [[headache]], [[hypoglycemia]], [[anemia]] or [[scarlet fever]].
