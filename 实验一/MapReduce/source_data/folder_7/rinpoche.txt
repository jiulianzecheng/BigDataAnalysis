'''Rinpoche''' is a title in [[Tibetan Buddhism]] which may be given to a religious teacher known as a [[lama]].  The word  " Rinpoche "  literally means  " precious one " . < ref > http://www.diamondway-teachings.org/export/en/resources/glossary-r.html - Rinpoche: Honorific title meaning  " Precious One. " < /ref >   
 
 In [[Tibet]] and [[Bhutan]], when used as  " Guru Rinpoche "  it refers to [[Padmasambhava]], who first brought [[Tibetan Buddhism]] to the [[Himalaya]]s. When used as  " Je Rinpoche " , it refers to [[Je Tsongkhapa]], the founder of the [[Gelug]] School.
