 '''Courchavon''' is a [[municipality]] of the [[district]] of [[Porrentruy (district)|Porrentruy]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Jura (canton)|Jura]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 Courchavon is also famous for its Swiss [[chocolate]].
