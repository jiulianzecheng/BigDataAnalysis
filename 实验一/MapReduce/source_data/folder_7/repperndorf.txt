'''Repperndorf''' is a [[village]] in [[Regierungsbezirk]], [[Lower Franconia]], [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. Since 1977 it is part of the town of [[Kitzingen]].  Repperndorf has a [[population]] of 260 people. The village has a [[graveyard]] and two [[church]]es, one for [[Roman Catholism|Catholics]] and one for [[protestantism|Protestants]]. It also has one [[restaurant]] and one [[football (soccer)|soccer]] club. There was the famous  " Schnitzel factory "  but now it moved to Kitzingen.
