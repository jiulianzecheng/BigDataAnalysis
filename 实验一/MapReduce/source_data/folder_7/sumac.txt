 '''Sumac''', also spelled '''sumach''', is a [[flowering plant]]. 
 
 Sumacs are small [[tree]]s. The [[leaf|leaves]] are arranged like spirals. The [[flower]]s are in spikes. Each flower is very small, [[green]]ish, creamy [[white]] or [[red]], with five [[petal]]s.
