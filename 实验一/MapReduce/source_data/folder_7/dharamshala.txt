[[File:Looking down over Dharamsala and Beas River.jpg|thumb|View looking over [[McLeod Ganj]], on the left with Dharamshala at the base of the hill; Forsyth Ganj to the right and the [[Beas River]] below. Sketch by [[Alfred Hallett]], c. 1980]] 
 '''Dharamshala''' is a city in northern [[India]]. It is the winter seat of government of the state of [[Himachal Pradesh]] and the district headquarters of the [[Kangra district]]. 
 [[File:Dharamsala-Chhawani-02-gje.jpg|thumb]] 
 [[McLeod Ganj]], a village within Dharamshala municipality, is the home of the [[Dalai Lama]] and the [[Government of Tibet in Exile|exiled Tibetan government]]. It is a popular place for Indian and foreign tourists, including students studying Tibet.
