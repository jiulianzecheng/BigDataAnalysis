 The '''Oxalidaceae''' is a [[family (biology)|family]] of [[flowering plant]]s. This family has about 900 [[species]], mainly of [[annual plant|annual]] or [[perennial]] [[herb]]s. < ref > {{cite web |title=Western Weeds |url=http://www.wswa.org.au/western_weeds/oxalidaceae.htm |publisher= Weeds Society of Western Australia |accessdate= 12 August 2013 |agency= Plant Protection Society of Western Australia |author= B.M.J. Hussey, G.J. Keighery, J. Dodd, S.G. Lloyd and R.D. Cousens |website= wswa.org.au |year=2007}} < /ref >   These are found mainly in [[tropical]] and [[subtropics|subtropical]] areas of the world, although some occur in [[temperate zone|temperate]] locations.
