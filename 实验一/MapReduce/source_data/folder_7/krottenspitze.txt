 The '''Krottenspitze''' is a mountain in the [[Allgäu Alps]]. It is {{convert|2,551|m}} high. It is one of the few rocky summits of the main ridge that is visible from [[Oberstdorf]]. There are no marked routes to the Krottenspitze. It was first climbed in 1854 for survey work. It is made of [[Dolomite]].
