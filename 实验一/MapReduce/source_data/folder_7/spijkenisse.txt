 '''Spijkenisse''' is a former [[List of municipalities of the Netherlands|municipality]] in the [[Netherlands|Dutch]] [[Provinces of the Netherlands|province]] of [[South Holland]]. Since 2015, it has been part of the new municipality of [[Nissewaard]]. 
 
 The city has about 72,500 people.
