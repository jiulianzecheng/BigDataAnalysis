 '''Titanite''', or '''sphene''' (from the [[Greek language|Greek]] ''sphenos'' (σφηνώ), meaning wedge < ref name= " Dana "  / > ), is a [[calcium]] [[titanium]] [[silicate]] [[mineral]]. Its [[chemical formula]] is [[Calcium|Ca]][[Titanium|Ti]][[Silicon|Si]][[Oxygen|O]] < sub > 5 < /sub > . [[Iron]] and [[aluminium]] are sometimes found in it. Also sometimes found in it are [[Rare earth element|rare earth]] metals including [[cerium]] and [[yttrium]], and the [[actinide]] [[thorium]]. < ref > {{Cite book|title=Introduction to the Rock-Forming Minerals|last1=Deer|first1=W.
