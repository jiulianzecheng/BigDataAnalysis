 '''Worship''' is a word often used in [[religion]].  It means to have much [[respect]].  If someone [[prayer|prays]] or says thank to [[God]], people say that they are worshipping God. Worship can also be used as the name for a [[church]] service or similar event. This can be called an act of worship. 
 
 The [[Quran]] talks about worshipping [[Allah]].
