 '''Kremenchuk''' ({{lang-uk|Кременчу́к|Kremenčúk}}; {{lang-ru|Кременчу́г|Kremenčúg}}) is a city in [[Poltava Oblast]] ([[Oblast|province]]) of central [[Ukraine]]. It is the [[administrative centre]] of [[Kremenchuk Raion]] ([[Raion|district]]). It is an important industrial city and 219,022 people lived here in 2020. Although not as large as some other oblast centers and cities of regional significance, Kremenchuk has importance as a large industrial center in Ukraine and Eastern Europe.
