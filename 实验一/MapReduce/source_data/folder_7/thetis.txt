 In [[Greek mythology]], '''Thetis''' ({{lang-el|Θέτις}}) is a sea nymph. She is one of the fifty [[Nereid|Nereids]], the daughters of [[Nereus]] and [[Doris (mythology)|Doris]]. Like other sea figures, Thetis bore the gift of prophecy as well as the ability to change her shape at will. She was married to the mortal hero [[Peleus]]. Their son is [[Achilles]].
