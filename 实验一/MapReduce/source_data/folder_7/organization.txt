'''Organization''' ([[British English]]: '''Organisation''') is the idea of putting things together in a [[logic]]al order. The verb is  " to organize " . 
 
 An organization is a [[group]] of [[wikt:people|people]] who work together. Organizations exist because people working together can achieve more than a person working alone.   
 
 To make sure people work together, organizations usually have [[Leader|leaders]].
