'''Bermondsey''' ({{IPAc-en|ˈ|b|ɜr|m|ə|n|d|z|i}}) is a town in the [[London Borough of Southwark]], England. It is {{convert|2.5|mi|km|1}} southeast of [[Charing Cross]]. To the west of Bermondsey is [[Southwark]], to the east is [[Rotherhithe]] and [[Deptford]], to the south is [[Walworth, London|Walworth]] and [[Peckham]], and to the north is the [[City of London]] and [[Whitechapel]].
