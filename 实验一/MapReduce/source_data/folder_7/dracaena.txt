 '''''Dracaena''''' is a [[genus]] of about 40 [[tree]]s and [[succulent]] [[shrub]]s.  
 
 The name comes from [[Ancient Greek language|Ancient Greek]] ''drakaina'', meaning female [[dragon]]. Most of them are [[native]] to tropical [[Africa]], some occur in Southeast Asia, and tropical South America. They are usually classified into ''Dragon trees'' and ''shrubby dracenas'' according to how they grow. The latter are also often grown as house plants.
