 '''IGN''' is a [[game|gaming]] [[website]] that was first launched in 2000. It has [[FAQ]]'s, guides, and walkthroughs about many of the games on [[GameCube]], [[personal computer|PC]], [[PlayStation 2]], [[Xbox]], [[Wii]], [[PlayStation 3]], [[Xbox 360]], [[Xbox One]], and the [[PlayStation 4]], as well as older systems. IGN's main competitor is [[GameSpot]], another gaming website. 
 
 IGN also features a popular message board, with a lot of members.
