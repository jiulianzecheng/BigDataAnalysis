 '''''Eurypterus''''' is a type of extinct [[sea scorpion]]. It is the most famous type of sea scorpion, although it was far from the largest. It lived during the [[Silurian]] era and out has been found in the [[United States]], [[Canada]] and [[Europe]]. The first ''Eurypterus'' fossil was discovered in 1818. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.ucmp.berkeley.edu/arthropoda/chelicerata/eurypteridfr.html|title=Eurypterida: Fossil Record|publisher=University of California Museum of Paleontology|access-date=May 21, 2011}} < /ref >
