 '''Scheinfeld''' is a town in [[Neustadt (Aisch)-Bad Windsheim]], in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. It is 14 & nbsp;km northwest of [[Neustadt (Aisch)]], and 40 & nbsp;km east of [[Würzburg]]. [[House of Schwarzenberg|Schloss Schwarzenberg]] is next to the town. The town is home to an [[Adidas]] testing factory.
