 '''Polysexuality''' is a [[sexual orientation]] in which the person feels [[Sexual attraction|attraction]] to many, but not necessarily all [[genders]]. Polysexuality, not to be confused with [[pansexuality]] or [[omnisexuality]], is under what some people would call the [[bisexual umbrella]], sometimes seen as non-[[monosexual]] spectrum. < ref >
