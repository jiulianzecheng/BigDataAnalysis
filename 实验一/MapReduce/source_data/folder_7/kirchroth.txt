 '''Kirchroth''' is a [[Municipalities of Germany|municipality]] in [[Straubing-Bogen]] in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. It is on the [[Danube River]]. By the local government reform of May 1978, villages Kößnach, Oberzeitldorn, Obermiethnach, Pillnach, Pondorf, Niederachdorf, as well as Aufroth and Neuroth were incorporated.
