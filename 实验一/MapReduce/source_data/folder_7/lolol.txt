 '''Lolol''' ({{lang-arn|land of crabs and holes}}) is a [[Chile]]an [[Communes of Chile|commune]] in the [[Province of Colchagua]], [[Region of O'Higgins|O'Higgins]]. The town was founded in 1830. < ref > {{Cite web |url=http://www.municipalidadtimaukel.cl/?p=185 |title=MUNICIPIO DE TIMAUKEL FIRMÃ“ CONVENIO EDUCATIVO Y CULTURAL CON COMUNA DE LOLOL |access-date=2010-05-16 |archive-date=2009-10-05 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20091005010035/http://www.municipalidadtimaukel.cl/?p=185 |url-status=dead }} < /ref >  It has 2,118 inhabitants, as of 2002.
