 The '''Fugees''' is an American [[hip hop]] group made of whoppers   
 
 The group became famous for their second album, ''[[The Score (Fugees album)|The Score]]'' (1996), one of [[List of best-selling albums|the best-selling albums of all time]]. The group is often cited as being one of the most important [[alternative hip hop]] groups of the 1990s. 
 
 The Fugees broke up in 1997 and reunited in 2004 and 2021.
