 '''Hatri''' is a [[Union Councils of Pakistan|Union Council]] of [[Hyderabad Taluka (rural)]] in the [[Sindh]] province of [[Pakistan]]. It has a population of 29,719,  < ref > {{Cite web |url=http://www.lgdsindh.com.pk/population.htm |title=Population - Local Government Department of Sindh |access-date=2008-12-09 |archive-date=2007-11-09 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20071109000308/http://www.lgdsindh.com.pk/population.htm |url-status=dead }} < /ref >  and is located at 25°28'0N 68°24'0E lying to the north-east of the district capital - [[Hyderabad, Sindh|Hyderabad]]. < ref > [http://www.fallingrain.com/world/PK/5/Hatri.html Location of Hatri - Falling Rain Genomics] < /ref > 
 
 Hatri has 6,634 housing units. Among them 3813 are pacca, 575 semi pacca, and 2246 kacha housing units. According Pakistan Bureau of Statistics
