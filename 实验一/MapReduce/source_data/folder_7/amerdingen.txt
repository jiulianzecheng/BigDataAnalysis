 '''Amerdingen''' is a [[municipality]] in the district of [[Donau-Ries]] in [[Bavaria]] in [[Germany]]. There is an 18th-century castle called [[Schloss Amerdingen]]. It was built by the family of the Stauffenbergs. Count Alfred Schenk von [[Stauffenberg]] (*1923) and his wife live there now. The mayor is currently Hermann Scmidt
