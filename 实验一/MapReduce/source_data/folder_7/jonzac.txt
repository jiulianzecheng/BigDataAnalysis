 '''Jonzac''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]] and a [[Prefectures in France|sub-prefecture]] in southwestern [[France]], in the [[Charente-Maritime]] [[Departments of France|department]] ([[Nouvelle-Aquitaine]] [[Regions of France|region]]). 
 
 It is the [[Capital (city)|capital]] of the southern ''arrondissement'' of the Charente-Maritime department. 
 
 ==History== 
 Jonzac is in the [[History|historical]] region of ''Haute Saintonge''. The city started on two [[hill]]s, ''Balaguier'' et ''Montguimar'', where are the main historical buildings of the city: the ''Saint-Gervais-Saint-Protais de Jonzac'' [[church]] and the ''Château de Jonzac'' [[castle]]. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 The city of Jonzac is far from the [[Prefectures in France|prefecture]] of Charente-Maritime, [[La Rochelle]], that is at {{Convert|105|km|mi|abbr=on }} to the northwest, but it is at only {{Convert|55|km|mi|abbr=on }} from [[Angoulême]], prefecture of the neighbour department of [[Charente]], and at {{Convert|90|km|mi|abbr=on }} from [[Bordeaux]], prefecture of the department of [[Gironde]] and main city of the Nouvelle-Aquitaine region. 
 
 Jonzac has an area of {{Convert|13.09|km2|sqmi|abbr=on }} and its average [[altitude]] is {{Convert|57|m|ft|abbr=on}}; at the [[City and town halls|city hall]], the altitude is {{Convert|40|m|ft|abbr=on }}. < ref > {{cite web|title=Jonzac|url=http://www.map-france.com/Jonzac-17500//|publisher=Map-France.com}} < /ref >

 The [[Seugne]] [[river]], a left [[tributary]] of the [[Charente (river)|Charente]] river, flows through the city from southeast to northwest. 
 
 ===Climate=== 
 The climate of Jonzac, in the [[Köppen climate classification]], is '''Cfb''' - [[Oceanic climate]] with template summers. < ref > {{cite web |work=Annuaire-Mairie.fr |title=La ville de Jonzac |url=http://www.annuaire-mairie.fr/ville-jonzac.html |language=fr |access-date=15 August 2016}} < /ref > 
 
 ==Population== 
 With a population of 3,465, < ref name=insee2 > {{cite web |url=http://www.insee.fr/fr/ppp/bases-de-donnees/recensement/populations-legales/departement.asp?dep=17 & annee=2013 |title=Populations lègales 2013 - 17-Charente-Maritime |work= Populations légales 2013 des départements et des collectivités d'outre-mer |publisher=Institut national de la statistique et des études économiques - INSEE |language=fr |access-date=15 August 2016 }} < /ref >  Jonzac has a [[population density]] of {{Pop density|3465|23.63|km2|prec=0|disp=num}} inhabitants/km < sup > 2 < /sup > . 
 
 The inhabitants of Jonzac are known, in [[French language|French]], as ''Jonzacais'' (women: ''Jonzacaises''). < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.habitants.fr/habitants_departement_charente-maritime_17.html |title=Les gentilés de Charente-Maritime (17) |publisher=habitants.fr |language=fr |access-date=15 August 2016 }} < /ref > 
 
 ==Administration== 
 Jonzac is a subprefecture of the Charente-Maritime department since 1800. Its [[INSEE]] [[code]] is 197 (combined with the code of the department: 17197). It is also the capital of the canton of [[Canton of Jonzac|Jonzac]]. < ref name=insee1 > {{cite web|url=http://www.insee.fr/fr/methodes/nomenclatures/cog/fichecommunale.asp?codedep=17 & codecom=197 |title=Commune de Jonzac (17197) |publisher=Institut national de la statistique et des études économiques - INSEE |language=fr |access-date=15 August 2016 }} < /ref >
