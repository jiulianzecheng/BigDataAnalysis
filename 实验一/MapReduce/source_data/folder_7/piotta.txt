 '''Piotta''' is a village in the [[municipality]] of [[Quinto, Switzerland|Quinto]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Ticino]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 Piotta has an [[ice hockey]] Club, called the [[HC Ambrì-Piotta]], that plays in indoor sporting arena of  " [[Valascia]] " .
