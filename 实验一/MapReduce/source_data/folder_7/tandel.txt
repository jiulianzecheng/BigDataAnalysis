 '''Tandel''' is a [[Communes of Luxembourg|commune]] in [[Luxembourg]]. It is part of the [[Vianden (canton)|canton of Vianden]]. As of 2019, 2,083 people live here. 
 
 The commune was created on 1 January 2006 from the former communes of [[Bastendorf]] (in the [[Diekirch (canton)|canton of Diekirch]]) and [[Fouhren]] (in the canton of Vianden).
