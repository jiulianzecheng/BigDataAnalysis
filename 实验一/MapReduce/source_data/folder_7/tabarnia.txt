 '''Tabarnia''' (''Tabàrnia'' in [[Catalan language|Catalan]]) is a suggested name and a [[Satire|satirical political]] project for a portion of the [[Catalonia|Catalonian territory]], [[Spain]]. < ref > {{cite web|title='Tabarnia', el reverso satírico del independentismo catalán - RTVE.es|publisher=RTVE.es|language=es|url=http://www.rtve.es/noticias/20171227/tabarnia-reverso-satirico-del-independentismo-catalan/1651625.shtml|date= 27 December 2017}} < /ref > < ref > {{cite web|title=Tabarnia - the 'region' that wants to leave Catalonia|publisher=The Local|language=en|url=https://www.thelocal.es/20171227/tabarnia-region-independence-catalonia-spain|date=27 December 2017}} < /ref >  It was created by the organization  " Barcelona is not Catalonia "  (AKA ''Plataforma por la Autonomía de Barcelona'',  " Movement for an Autonomous Barcelona " ) as a satirical response towards the [[Catalan independence|Catalonians separatists]]. Their main target is to establish up a new region in Spain besides Catalonia with some districts (''Comarcas'' or ''Comarques'') from the provinces of [[Barcelona]] and [[Tarragona]]. < ref name= " enTabarnia " > {{cite news|last=Rodríguez Martínez|first=Marta|title=Tabarnia: the new Catalan secessionist movement boosted by Twitter|url=http://www.euronews.com/2017/12/27/tabarnia-the-new-catalan-secessionist-movement-boosted-by-twitter|access-date=29 December 2017|newspaper=Euronews|date=27 December 2017}} < /ref >   
 
 Its nomenclature is a [[neologism]] created from the provinces' names: '''Ta'''rragona and '''Bar'''celona, and it would span these areas: 
 
 * Tarragonès/Tarragonés 
 * Baix Camp/Bajo Campo 
 * Baix Penedès/Bajo Penedés 
 * Alt Penedés/Alto Penedés  
 * Garraf 
 * Baix Llobregat/Bajo Llobregat 
 * Barcelonès/Barcelonés 
 * Vallès Oriental/Vallés Oriental 
 * Vallès Occidental/Vallés Occidental 
 * El Maresme 
 
 Proponents believes the area somewhat corresponds to the historic [[County of Barcelona]], although its extension is considerably different. < ref name= " enTabarnia "  / >

 === Process of Independence of Catalonia === 
 Proponents of Tabarnia demands to segregate themselves from the Catalan separatist movement and reclaims the right of a number of territories near the area of Barcelona and Tarragona to constitute themselves as an Autonomous Community within Spain. In the [[Catalan regional election, 2015|2015]] and [[Catalan regional election, 2017|2017]] Catalan regional elections, parties opposed to secession (mainly [[Citizens (Spanish political party)|C's]], [[Socialists' Party of Catalonia|PSC]] and [[People's Party of Catalonia|PP]]) received a majority of votes and seats in some of these ''comarques'' (Tarragonès, Baix Penedès, Garraf and Baix Llobregat) including the city of Tarragona, but excluding the most populated Catalan city: Barcelona.  
 
 They allude to a series of fiscal arguments similar to those of Catalan separatism in regards to Spain, < ref > {{cite web|title=Tabarnia, por una Barcelona fuera de Cataluña|url=http://www.abc.es/espana/abci-tabarnia-barcelona-fuera-cataluna-201712262016_noticia.html|language=es|publisher=[[ABC (newspaper)|ABC]]|date=26 December 2017|access-date=27 December 2017}} < /ref >  although in last Catalan election, Tarragona province showed more support for separatism than unionism.

 == Reactions == 
 [[File:Flag of Tabarnia.SVG|thumb|right|200px|Proposed flag]] 
 The rise of this movement creates tension among the Catalan separatist movement because of argumental contradiction and geostrategic risk using their same arguments for independence word by word. as a result, the catalan separatists contradicts themselves when they uses the  " unionist "  counter-arguments in order to discredit the Tabarnia movement. < ref > {{cite web |url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=D8z6M9283ks |title=Margallo and Joan Tardà during a congress session of foreign affairs. |language=es |publisher=[[Youtube video]] |date=7 February 2017 |access-date=27 December 2017}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite web|url=https://twitter.com/sextaNoticias/status/946014302967926784|title=VÍDEO @DebatAlRojoVivo {{!}} @gabrielrufian:  " Si Ciudadanos y FAES, que están detrás de Tabarnia, ganan las elecciones, el Govern no les encarcelará "  |publisher=laSexta|date=27 December 2017|language=es}} < /ref >  The territorial area that Tabarnia would comprise includes geostrategic assets that are fundamental for a viable independent Catalonia. [[The Port of Barcelona]] and [[Barcelona–El Prat Airport]], some of the richest industrial sectors fall in fact inside areas that hold a majority of voters in favor of unionism, though in the city of [[Barcelona]] the pro-independence won the election. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://www.elperiodico.com/es/barcelona/20171222/arrimadas-se-impone-en-siete-distritos-pero-el-bloque-independentista-aun-gana-en-votos-6511752 |title=Arrimadas se impone en siete distritos pero el bloque independentista aún gana en votos |access-date=28 December 2017 |publisher=El Periódico |date=22 December 2017 |language=es}} < /ref > 
 
 The fact that Tabarnians are asking to be separated from the Catalonia that wants to be separated from Spain, has been interpreted by some newspapers as a reductio ad absurdum of the nationalistic logic in a XXI century Europe. Many Tabarnians believe that a sustained international media attention to this movement would defeat the catalan independence project by simply following their own arguments instead of opposing them. 
 
 An ongoing petition is taking place at [[Change.org]] in order to be sent to the Spanish [[Congress of Deputies]] to consider the creation of this new autonomous region. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.elcatalan.es/la-campana-apoyo-tabarnia-change-org-ronda-las-100-000-firmas/|title= 100.000 signatures to request consideration of Autonomous Region of Tabarnia in Spanish Congress.|language=es|publisher=[[elcatalan.es]]|date=7 February 2017|access-date=27 December 2017}} < /ref >
