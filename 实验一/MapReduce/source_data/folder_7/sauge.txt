 '''Sauge''' is a [[municipality]] of the [[administrative district]] of [[Jura bernois (administrative district)|Jura bernois]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Bern (canton)|Bern]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 On 1 January 2014, the former municipalities of [[Plagne, Switzerland|Plagne]] and [[Vauffelin]] merged into the new municipality of Sauge.
