 '''MHPArena''' ({{IPA-de|ɛmhaːpeː ʔaˌʁeːnaː}}) is a stadium located in [[Stuttgart]], [[Baden-Württemberg]], Germany and home to German [[Bundesliga]] club [[VfB Stuttgart]]. 
 
 Before 1993 it was called '''Neckarstadion''' {{IPA-de|ˈnɛkaʁˌʃtaːdi̯ɔn||}}, named after the nearby river [[Neckar]] and between 1993 and July 2008 it was called '''Gottlieb-Daimler-Stadion''' {{IPA-de|ˌɡɔtliːpˈdaɪmlɐˌʃtaːdi̯ɔn||}}. From the 2008–09 season, the stadium was renamed the '''Mercedes-Benz Arena'''. < ref > [http://www.arsenal.com/news/news-archive/friendly-against-vfb-stuttgart-announced Arsenal: Friendly against VfB Stuttgart announced] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20151118045059/http://www.arsenal.com/news/news-archive/friendly-against-vfb-stuttgart-announced |date=18 November 2015 }} < /ref >  On 1 July 2023, it was renamed the ''MHPArena''. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://newsroom.porsche.com/en/2023/company/porsche-mhp-vfb-stuttgart-ag-position-paper-32923.html|title=Porsche, MHP and VfB Stuttgart AG sign position paper|work=porsche.com|publisher=[[Porsche]]|date=27 June 2023|accessdate=1 July 2023}} < /ref >
