'''Feldgrau''' is a greenish-gray color. In [[World War I]], the color [[Green|feldgrau]] was a light [[gray]]-[[green]], though there were variations of the shade ranging from [[Gray|grays]] to [[Brown|browns]]. The shade is called [[light feldgrau]]. 
 
 == History == 
 In 1907, the so-called '''feldgrau peace uniform''' with colored [[cuffs]], facings, shoulder [[Strap|straps]] and gorgets was issued by decree in [[Prussia]].
