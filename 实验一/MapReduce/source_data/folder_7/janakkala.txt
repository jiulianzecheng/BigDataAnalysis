 '''Janakkala''' is a [[municipality]] in [[Finland]]. 16,881 people lived there in 2013. Population centers: [[Turenki]], [[Tervakoski]], [[Leppäkoski]] and a village called [[Tarinmaa]]. 
 
 Neighbour municipalities, in south: [[Hausjärvi]], [[Loppi]] and [[Riihimäki]]. [[Hämeenlinna]] is on the north. 
 
 It was founded in 1866. 
 
 ==Coat of arms== 
 Janakkalas coat of arms has been designed by [[Onni Oja]] in 1950.
