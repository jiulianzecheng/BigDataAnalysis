 '''Radebeul''' is a town (''große Kreisstadt'') in the district of [[Meißen Rural District|Meißen]] in [[Saxony]], [[Germany]], a suburb of [[Dresden]]. The [[Meißen]] area, where Radebeul is located, is one of the northeasternmost areas where [[wine]] is grown today.
