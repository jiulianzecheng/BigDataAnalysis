 '''Savitaipale''' is a [[Finland|Finnish]] [[municipality]] in the [[South Karelia]] region. As of January [[2014]], more than 3,700 people lived in Savitaipale. 
 Savitaipale has an area of 690.78 & nbsp;km². 151.63 & nbsp;km² of it is water. The municipality is on the coast of the Lake [[Kuolimo]]. [[Helsinki]] is 220 & nbsp;km away. Neighbouring municipalities are [[Kouvola]], [[Lemi]], [[Luumäki]], [[Mikkeli]], [[Mäntyharju]], [[Puumala]] and [[Taipalsaari]]. The municipality was established in 1639.
