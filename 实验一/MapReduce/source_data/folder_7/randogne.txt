 '''Randogne''' was a [[municipality]] in [[Sierre (district)|Sierre]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Valais]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 On 1 January 2017, the former municipalities of [[Chermignon]], [[Montana, Switzerland|Montana]], [[Mollens, Valais|Mollens]] and Randogne merged into the new municipality of [[Crans-Montana]].
