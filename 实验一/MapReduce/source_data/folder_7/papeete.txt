 '''Papeete''' is the [[capital city]] of [[French Polynesia]], an [[French overseas collectivities|overseas collectivity of the French Republic]]. The [[Communes of France|commune]] is located on the north-west of the island of [[Tahiti]] in the [[Pacific Ocean]].
