'''MxPx''' is an American pop punk band from [[Bremerton, Washington]]. It was formed in 1992. 
 
 == Musical style and influences == 
 MxPx's influences include [[Descendents]], [[Nirvana (band)|Nirvana]], [[The Clash]], [[Green Day]], [[Minor Threat]], [[Samiam]], [[The Replacements (band)|The Replacements]], [[Foo Fighters]], [[All (band)|All]], [[Melvins]], [[NOFX]], [[Suicidal Tendencies]] and [[The Vandals]]. 
 
 == Band members == 
 * [[Mike Herrera]] - lead vocals, bass guitar (1992-present) 
 * Yuri Ruley - drums, percussion (1992-present) 
 * Tom Wisniewski - lead guitar, backing vocals (1995-present) 
 * Chris Adkins - rhythm guitar, backing vocals (2016-present)
