 '''Pskov''' ({{lang-ru|Псков}}, ancient Russian spelling Пльсковъ (''Pleskov'')) is an old [[types of inhabited localities in Russia|city]] in the northwest of [[Russia]]. It is the [[Administrative centre|administrative center]] of [[Pskov Oblast]]. It is about {{convert|20|km}} east of the [[Estonia]]n border, on the [[Velikaya River]]. 
 
 ==History== 
 There was [[Pskov republic|Pskov feudal republic]] depended of [[Novgorod republic|Novgorod]].
