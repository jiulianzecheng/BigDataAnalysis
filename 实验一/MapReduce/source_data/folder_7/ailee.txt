 '''Amy Lee''' (born May 30, 1989) is a [[Koreans|Korean]]-[[Americans|American]] [[singer]] and [[actress]] in [[South Korea]]. She is known by the [[stage name]] '''Ailee'''. Born in [[Denver]], she grew up in [[New Jersey]], in the [[United States]]. She started as a [[Youtube]] singer, with some success. < ref name= " vocalanalysis "  / >  In 2010 she moved to [[South Korea]] with the goal of being a professional singer. Her first [[Single (music)|single]],  " Heaven " , was released in 2012. < ref > {{cite web |title=Ailee releases her debut music video,  " Heaven "  |url=http://www.allkpop.com/2012/02/ailee-releases-her-debut-music-video-heaven |website=allkpop |access-date=5 December 2019}} < /ref >  Within one month of her [[wikt:debut|debut]] she had already won two music awards. < ref > {{cite web |title=Rookie singer Ailee wins two titles from Cyworld Music Awards |url=https://www.allkpop.com/article/2012/03/rookie-singer-ailee-wins-two-titles-from-cyworld-music-awards |website=allkpop}} < /ref >   
 
 On September 13, 2011, Ailee along with Wheesung held a performance on the MBC's Chuseok special episode of  " Singer and Trainee " . Ailee's stage presence was immediately felt by the audience, who was amazed that she was like a professional singer, even though she had not yet officially debuted. Following her solo performance of  " [[Halo (song)|Halo]] "  by [[Beyoncé]], singer BMK expressed,  " Wherever she goes, she has the potential to be a big star. She definitely has the voice. "  After the judges had graded everyone, Ailee won first place. < ref > [http://kpopselca.com/forum/korean-artists/official-ailee-thread-t329/''official-aileeforum page'']. < /ref > 
 
 Ailee is known for her strong vocal quality and range. < ref name= " vocalanalysis " > {{cite web |title=Ailee’s Vocal Analysis |url=https://kpopvocalanalysis.net/2015/08/26/ailees-vocal-analysis-newly-updated/ |website=K-pop Vocalists' Vocal Analyses |access-date=5 December 2019 |language=en |date=27 August 2015 |archive-date=5 December 2019 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20191205152407/https://kpopvocalanalysis.net/2015/08/26/ailees-vocal-analysis-newly-updated/ |url-status=dead }} < /ref >  By 2016, she had won 26 music awards.
