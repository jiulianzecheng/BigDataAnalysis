 The '''Merginae''' or '''seaducks''' are a subfamily of the [[Anatidae]], the [[duck]], [[goose]] and [[swan]] family of [[bird]]s. 
 
 As the name suggests, most are [[Marine biology|marine]] outside the breeding season. Many species have specialized salt glands to allow them to tolerate salt water, but these have not yet developed in young birds. Some species prefer [[river]]ine [[habitats]].
