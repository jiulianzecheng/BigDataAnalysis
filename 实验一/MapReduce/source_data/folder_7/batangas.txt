 '''Batangas''', officially the '''Province of Batangas''' ({{lang-tl|Lalawigan ng Batangas}} {{IPA-tl|bɐˈtaŋgɐs|IPA}}), is a [[Provinces of the Philippines|province]] of the [[Philippines]] located in the [[Calabarzon]] [[Regions of the Philippines|region]] in [[Luzon]]. Its capital is the city of [[Batangas City|Batangas]].
