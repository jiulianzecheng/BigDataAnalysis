 '''Laupheim''' ({{IPA-de|ˈlaʊ̯phaɪm}}) is a [[Große Kreisstadt|major district town]] in southern Germany in the [[States of Germany|state]] of [[Baden-Württemberg]]. Laupheim was first mentioned in 778. It became a city in 1869.
