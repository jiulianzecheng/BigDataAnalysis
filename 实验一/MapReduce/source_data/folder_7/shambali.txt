 '''Shambali''' ({{lang-tr|Şambalı}}) is a Middle Eastern sweet cake. It is cake made of [[semolina]] sweetened with [[syrup]] and topped with [[peanut]]. Its shape is rectangular. 
 
 It is also known in most of the countries which formed the [[Ottoman Empire]]. Its name means  " Damascus honey "  ({{lang-tr|Şam balı}}) and shambali can also be called '''Damascus dessert'''.
