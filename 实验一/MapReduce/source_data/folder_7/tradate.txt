 '''Tradate''' is a ''[[comune]]'' in northern [[Italy]]. Tradate is in the [[Lombardy]] Region. It has a population of 18,768 people. 
 
 It is {{convert|15|km|mi|0|abbr=on}} from the city of [[Varese]] (the province's capital).
