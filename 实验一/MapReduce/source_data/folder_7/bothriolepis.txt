 '''''Bothriolepis''''' is a [[genus]] of [[antiarch]] [[placoderm]]s. It was widespread and diverse in the middle to late [[Devonian]].  
 
 ''Bothriolepis'' lived in environments across every continent including [[inshore|near-shore]] [[ocean|marine]] and [[freshwater]] settings. Most species of ''Bothriolepis'' were  small, [[benthic]], freshwater [[detritivore]]s. They got nutrients by eating decomposing plant/animal material. They were about {{convert|30|cm|in}} in length.  However, the largest species, ''B. maxima'', had a [[carapace]] about {{convert|100|cm|in}} in length.
