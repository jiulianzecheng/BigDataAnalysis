 '''Lalibela''' ({{lang-am|ላሊበላ}}) is a town in [[Lasta]] district of [[North Wollo Zone]] in [[Amhara Region]], Ethiopia. It is famous for [[rock-cut architecture|rock-cut]] [[monolithic church]]es. < ref name= " unesco " > {{cite web|url=https://whc.unesco.org/en/list/18|title=Rock-Hewn Churches, Lalibela|author=UNESCO World Heritage Centre|work=unesco.org}} < /ref >   
 
 The Rock-Hewn Churches were declared a [[World Heritage site]] in 1978.
