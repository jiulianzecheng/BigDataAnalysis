 '''Akaa''' is a city in [[Finland]]. About 17,000 people live there. Nearby municipalities include [[Hämeenlinna]], [[Lempäälä]], [[Urjala]], [[Valkeakoski]] and [[Vesilahti]]. In Akaa is the biggest mämmi factory in world. There were [[mämmi]]-eating [[competition]]s in 2005 and 2007.
