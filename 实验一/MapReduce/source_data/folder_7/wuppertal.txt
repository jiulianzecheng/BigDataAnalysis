 '''Wuppertal''' is a city in the west of Germany, in the [[States of Germany|state]] of [[North Rhine-Westphalia]]. It is next to [[Düsseldorf]]. 
 
 Wuppertal has about 360,000 [[inhabitant]]s. It has a [[university]]. 
 
 A well-known [[tourist attraction]] is the [[Wuppertal Schwebebahn]], a [[tram]] hanging about {{convert|10|m|abbr=off}} above ground.
