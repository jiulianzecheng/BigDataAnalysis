 '''Heibronn''' ( < small > German: < /small >  [[Help:IPA/Standard German|[haɪlˈbʁɔn]]]; {{lang-gsw|Hailbrunn}}, local dialect: ''Hallbrunn'', ''Haalbrunn'') is a city in [[Germany]] in the [[States of Germany|state]] of [[Baden-Württemberg]]. With about 117,000 people, Heilbronn is the sixth largest town in Baden-Württemberg. 
 Heilbronn is also on the river [[Neckar]] and is known for the wine which was grown in their region and the [[Kätchen]] a famous sign.
