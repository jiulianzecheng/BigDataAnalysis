 '''Fugazza con queso''', or just '''Fugazza''', is a type of [[Argentina|Argentine]] [[pizza]] with a thick crust topped with [[Onion|onions]], [[cheese]], and sometimes [[olives]]. It's a combination of [[Naples-style pizza]] with [[focaccia]] bread. It was created in [[Buenos Aires]].
