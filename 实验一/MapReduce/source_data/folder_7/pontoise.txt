 '''Pontoise''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]] of the [[Val-d'Oise]] department in the northwestern suburbs of [[Paris]], [[France]]. It is {{convert|28.4|km|mi|abbr=on}} from the centre of Paris, in the  " [[new town#France|new town]] "  of [[Cergy-Pontoise]]. 
 
 ==Twin towns== 
 Pontoise is twinned with: 
 * {{flagicon|GER}} [[Böblingen]], Germany 
 * {{flagicon|UK}} [[Sevenoaks]], England 
 * {{flagicon|Netherlands}} [[Geleen]], Netherlands
