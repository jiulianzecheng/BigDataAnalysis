 '''Aubignosc''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]] in the [[Alpes-de-Haute-Provence]] [[Departments of France|department]] in the [[Provence-Alpes-Côte d'Azur]] region of south-eastern [[France]]. 
 
 Aubignosc is in the west of the [[Durance]] valley. There are the hamlets of Les Jardins and Le Forest. < ref name=Google > [https://www.google.com/maps/place/Aubignosc/@44.1154232,5.9628796,6569m/data=!3m1!1e3!4m2!3m1!1s0x12cba48ba95fe807:0x1e6fd82208a63109?hl=en Google Maps] < /ref >
