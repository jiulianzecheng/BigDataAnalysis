 '''''Battletruck''''' (also known as '''''Warlords of the 21st Century''''' in the U.S. and '''''[[:it:Destructors|Destructors]]''''' in [[Italy]]) is a 1982 New Zealand British science fiction action movie directed by [[Harley Cokliss]] and starring [[Michael Beck]], [[Annie McEnroe]], [[James Wainwright]], [[John Ratzenberger]], [[Bruno Lawrence]], [[Mark Hadlow]].
