 '''Mayanglambam''' is a [[Yumnak|Meetei family]]. It is native to [[Ancient Kangleipak]] ([[Antique Manipur]]). People of this family mainly inhabit in [[Manipur]], [[India]]. < ref > {{Cite web|url=http://www.indiachildnames.com/mobile/surname.aspx?surname=Mayanlambam|title = Mayanlambam surname}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite web|url=http://www.indiachildnames.com/mobile/originoffamilyname.aspx?surname=Mayanlambam|title=Origin of Mayanlambam surname}} < /ref > 
 == History == 
 During the era of King Samuroiba Ningthou, the temple dedicated to God [[Khamlangba]] was built in [[Kakching|Kakching Khullen]]. The temple was maintained by the Mayanglambam family. < ref > {{Cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=Z6d-IyINtk4C & dq=Khamlangba+Kakching+kullen+Khullen+Samuroiba+Ningthou+mayanglambam & pg=PA686|title=Encyclopaedia Of Manipur (3 Vol.)|last=Lisam|first=Khomdan Singh|date=2011|publisher=Gyan Publishing House|isbn=978-81-7835-864-2|language=en}} < /ref >
