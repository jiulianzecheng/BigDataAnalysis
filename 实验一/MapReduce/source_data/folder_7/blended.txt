 '''''Blended''''' is a 2014 [[United States|American]] [[romantic comedy]] movie. This movie is about two people who meet who each have children. [[Adam Sandler]] and [[Drew Barrymore]] play the couple. [[Bella Thorne]] plays Hillary. It was released on May 23, 2014.
