'''Rosvopaisti''' is a traditional [[Finland|Finnish]] food. It is cooked in a 50 & nbsp;cm-deep hole. At the bottom of the hole is put a [[camp fire]]. It is cooked there for 3-4 hours. After that, a steak is put in the hole. Cooking takes about 8-12 hours.
