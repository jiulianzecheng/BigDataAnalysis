 '''Arnouville''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is in [[Île-de-France]] in the [[Val-d'Oise]] [[Departments of France|department]] in north [[France]], 18 & nbsp;km north of [[Paris]]. 
 
 Until 11 July 2010, it was known as Arnouville-lès-Gonesse. < ref > [https://www.insee.fr/fr/metadonnees/cog/commune/COM95019-arnouville Commune d'Arnouville (95019)], INSEE < /ref >
