 '''Penzance''' ({{lang-kw|Pennsans}}) is a town and port in [[Cornwall]], [[South West England]]. It is near [[Land's End]] and the most westerly major town in England. Many people live and work in Penzance. In 2001, there were 21,168 people living in Penzance. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.ons.gov.uk/help/localstatistics|title=Local statistics - Office for National Statistics|website=www.ons.gov.uk}} < /ref >  The main Cornish railway line ends at Penzance. The fishing port of Newlyn is close to Penzance.
