 '''''Leuconostoc''''' is a [[gram-positive]] [[bacteria]], emerging [[human]] [[pathogen]] found in environment and [[Food|food products]]. Risk factors of infection include antibiotic pressure, foreign device, or underlying immune defects. Commercial diagnostics are commonly used to identify gram-positive bacteria, with errors mostly at the species level.
