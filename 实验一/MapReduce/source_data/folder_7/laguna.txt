 '''Laguna City''' is a little city of south of the Brazil, in [[Santa Catarina (state)|Santa Catarina]] State.  This city have more than 50000 people.  It was the capital of the short-lived Juliana Republic in 1839.  The city is known for having [[dolphin]]s 
 
 Some popular beaches of this city: 
 *Mar Grosso beach 
 *Iró beach 
 *Gravatá beach 
 *Gi beach.
