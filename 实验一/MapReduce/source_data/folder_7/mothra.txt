 '''Mothra''' is a ''[[kaiju]]'', a type of [[fiction]]al [[monster]] who first appeared in the book series ''[[The Luminous Fairies and Mothra]]'' by Takehiko Fukunaga, Shinichiro Nakamura, and Yoshie Hotta. The first movie she appeared in was ''[[Mothra (1961 film)|Mothra]]''.
