 A '''phablet''' ({{IPAc-en|'|f|æ|b|l|ə|t}}) is a type of [[mobile phone|mobile]] [[device]] designed to combine the format of [[smartphone]]s and [[tablet computer]]s. The name itself is a combination of the words ''phone'' and ''tablet''. 
 
 Phablets have screens that measure [[Display size|diagonally]] {{convert|5.1 - 6.99|inch|mm|abbr=}}. < ref > {{cite news| url = http://techland.time.com/2013/04/02/phablets-are-a-niche-not-a-fad/| title = Phablets Are a Niche, Not a Fad|publisher=[[Time (magazine)|Time]]|last=Newman |first=Jared |date=April 2, 2013}} < /ref >  This size allows for activities such as [[mobile web|mobile web browsing]] and watching [[video]]s.
