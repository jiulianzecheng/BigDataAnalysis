 The '''Seugne''' is a  [[river]] in southwestern [[France]] and a left [[tributary]] of the [[Charente (river)|Charente]] river. It flows through the [[Charente-Maritime]] [[Departments of France|department]] in the [[Nouvelle-Aquitaine]] [[Regions of France|region]]. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 The Seugne river has a length of {{Convert|82.4|km|mi|0|abbr=on}}, < ref name= " sandre " > {{cite web |url=http://services.sandre.eaufrance.fr/Courdo/Fiche/client/fiche_courdo.php?CdSandre=R5--0250 |title=La Seugne (R5--0250) |publisher=SANDRE - Portail national d'accès aux référentiels sur l'eau |language=fr |access-date=16 August 2016 }} < /ref >  and a [[drainage basin]] with an area of approximately {{Convert|902|km2|sqmi|0|abbr=on }}. 
 
 Its average yearly [[Discharge (hydrology)|discharge]] ([[volume]] of [[water]] which passes through a section of the river per [[Unit of measurement|unit]] of [[time]]) is 7.6 [[cubic metre]]s per [[second]] at ''Saint-Seurin-de-Palenne''. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://www.hydro.eaufrance.fr/stations/R5123320 & procedure=synthese |title=La Seugne à Saint-Seurin-de-Palenne [La Lijardière] |publisher=Banque Hydro |language=fr |access-date=15 August 2016 |archive-date=3 March 2016 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160303235137/http://www.hydro.eaufrance.fr/stations/R5123320%26procedure%3Dsynthese |url-status=dead }} < /ref > 
 
 ===Course=== 
 [[File:FR 17 Courcoury·Saint-Sever-de-Saintonge·Chaniers - Confluent de la Charente et du bras oriental de la Seugne.JPG|thumb|right|[[Confluence]] of the right branch with the [[Charente (river)|Charente]] river]] 
 The Seugne river starts in the ''[[commune]]'' of [[Montlieu-la-Garde]], near the place called ''Chez Bourdeau'', at an [[elevation]] of about {{Convert|93|m|ft|0|abbr=on}}. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://tab.geoportail.fr/?c=-0.281389,45.248611 & z=0.000316906 & l=GEOGRAPHICALGRIDSYSTEMS.MAPS.3D$GEOPORTAIL:OGC:WMTS@aggregate(1) & permalink=yes |title=Source de la Seugne |publisher=Géoportail |language=fr |access-date=15 August 2016 }} < /ref >  It flows generally north-northwest. 
 
 The river flows through the small cities of [[Jonzac]], [[Prefectures in France|subprefecture]] and [[Pons, Charente-Maritime|Pons]], both in the Charente-Maritime department. 
 
 After the small town of [[Colombiers, Charente-Maritime|Colombiers]], the river flows into the Charente river in  [[Les Gonds]], a [[suburb]] of [[Saintes, Charente-Maritime|Saintes]]. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://tab.geoportail.fr/?c=-0.618056,45.727778 & z=0.000224579 & l=GEOGRAPHICALGRIDSYSTEMS.MAPS.3D$GEOPORTAIL:OGC:WMTS@aggregate(1) & permalink=yes |title=Confluence de la Seugne |publisher=Géoportail |language=fr |access-date=15 August 2016 }}{{Dead link|date=April 2023 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }} < /ref > 
 
 The Seugne flows through 2 ''[[Arrondissements of France|arrondissements]]'' ([[Arrondissement of Jonzac|Jonzac]] and [[Arrondissement of Saintes|Saintes]]), 7 [[Cantons of France|cantons]] and 34 ''communes'', all in the Charente-Maritime department, Nouvelle-Aquitaine region: < ref name= " sandre "  / > 
 
 The river starts in the canton of Montlieu-la-Garde, flows through the cantons of Montendre, Jonzac, Saint-Genis-de-Saintonge, Pons, Gémozac and, finally, ends in the canton of Saintes-Est. 
 
 It flows through the following communes, from its starts to its [[confluence]] : [[Montlieu-la-Garde]], [[Chepniers]], [[Pouillac]], [[Sainte-Colombe, , Charente-Maritime|Sainte-Colombe]], [[Polignac, Charente-Maritime|Polignac]], [[Chatenet]], [[Le Pin, Charente-Maritime|Le Pin]], [[Sousmoulins]], [[Mérignac, Charente-Maritime|Mérignac]], [[Pommiers-Moulons]], [[Vibrac, Charente-Maritime|Vibrac]], [[Chaunac]], [[Fontaines-d'Ozillac]], [[Léoville]], [[Saint-Médard, Charente-Maritime|Saint-Médard]], [[Champagnac, Charente-Maritime|Champagnac]], [[Ozillac]], [[Jonzac]], [[Saint-Germain-de-Lusignan]], [[Lussac, Charente-Maritime|Lussac]], [[Clion, Charente-Maritime|Clion]], [[Saint-Georges-Antignac]], [[Mosnac, Charente-Maritime|Mosnac]], [[Fléac-sur-Seugne]], [[Belluire]], [[Pons, Charente-Maritime|Pons]], [[Bougneau]], [[Saint-Léger, Charente-Maritime|Saint-Léger]], [[Colombiers, Charente-Maritime|Colombiers]], [[Montils]], [[La Jard]], [[Berneuil, Charente-Maritime|Berneuil]], [[Les Gonds]], [[Courcoury]], [[Saint-Sever-de-Saintonge]].
