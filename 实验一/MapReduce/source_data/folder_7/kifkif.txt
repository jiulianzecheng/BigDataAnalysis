The '''Association of Lesbians, Gays, Bisexuals and Transgenders persons in Morocco''' ('''KIFKIF''') is a [[Morocco|Moroccan]] non-profit organization working on improving the legal and social status of [[lesbian]], [[gay]], [[bisexual]] and [[transgender]] people. It was founded in 2005 in and is the first LGBT advocacy group in North [[Africa]].
