 '''Rovereto''' ({{IPA-it|roveˈreːto}};  " wood of [[sessile oak]]s " ) is a city and ''[[Communes of Trentino|comune]]'' in [[Trentino]] in northern [[Italy]]. It is located in the [[Vallagarina]] valley of the [[Adige River]].
