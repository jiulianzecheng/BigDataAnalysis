 '''Marche''' is one of the twenty [[regions of Italy]], in [[Central Italy]] on the [[Adriatic Sea]]. The [[Capital city|capital]] is the city of [[Ancona]]. 
 
 == Geography == 
 The region is located in [[Central Italy]] bordered by [[Emilia-Romagna]] and the republic of [[San Marino]] to the north, [[Tuscany]] to the west, [[Umbria]] to the southwest, [[Abruzzo]] and [[Lazio]] to the south, and the [[Adriatic Sea]] to the east.

 Marche is the 15th largest region in Italy, with a total area of {{Convert|9401.38|km2|sqmi|1|abbr=on}}. < ref name= " area " > {{cite web|url=http://www.tuttitalia.it/marche/ |title=Marche |publisher=Tuttitalia.it |language=Italian |accessdate=14 June 2016}} < /ref >  The main rivers in the region are: 
 * [[Metauro]], {{Convert|121|km|mi|1|abbr=on}} 
 * [[Tronto]], {{Convert|115|km|mi|1|abbr=on}} 
 * [[Potenza (river)|Potenza]], {{Convert|95|km|mi|1|abbr=on}} 
 * [[Chienti]], {{Convert|91|km|mi|1|abbr=on}} 
 * [[Esino]], {{Convert|75|km|mi|1|abbr=on}} 
 
 The [[Nera (Italy)|Nera]] river is {{Convert|116|km|mi|1|abbr=on}} long but is a [[tributary]] of the [[Tiber River|Tiber]] and flows mainly in the [[Lazio]] region. 
 
 The highest mountain in the region is [[Monte Vettore]], in the [[Province of Ascoli Piceno|Ascoli Piceno]] [[province]], with an [[altitude]] of {{Convert|2476|m|ft|0|abbr=on }}. < ref name= " peakbagger " > {{cite web |url=http://peakbagger.com/peak.aspx?pid=10197 |title=Monte Vettore, Italy |publisher=Peakbagger.com |language= |accessdate=27 June 2017 |date= }} < /ref >
