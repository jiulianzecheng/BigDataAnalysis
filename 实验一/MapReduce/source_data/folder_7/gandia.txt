 '''Gandia''' ({{IPA-va|ɡanˈdi.a}}; {{lang-es|Gandía}} {{IPA-es|ɡanˈdi.a|}}) is a city in the [[Valencian Community]], eastern [[Spain]] on the [[Mediterranean]]. Gandia is located on the [[Costa del Azahar]], {{Convert|65|km|mi|abbr=out}} south of [[Valencia, Spain|Valencia]] and {{convert|110|km|0|abbr=on}} north of [[Alicante]].
