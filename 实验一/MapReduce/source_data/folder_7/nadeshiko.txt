The Japanese word '''Nadeshiko''' may mean: 
 * ''[[Dianthus superbus]]'' (also known as  " large pinks " ), a special kind of wild pink flowers which are native to the Japanese islands  
 * [[Japan women's national football team|''Nadashiko'' Japan]] is the name of the women's national football team 
 * ''[[Yamato nadeshiko]]'', a Japanese term for a traditional ideal women or daughter of Japan
