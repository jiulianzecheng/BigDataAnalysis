 '''Horticulture''' is the practical [[botany]] of [[garden]]s. It includes both decorative and [[vegetable]] aspects of gardens. A '''horticulturist''' is a person who works and conducts [[research]] in the [[science]] and [[skill]] of growing [[flower]]s, [[fruit]]s, [[vegetable]]s or [[ornamental plant]]s. [[Plant breeding]] is an important part of horticulture.
