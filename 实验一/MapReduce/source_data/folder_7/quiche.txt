 A '''quiche''' (Pronounced: ''keesh'') is a type of [[pie]]. It is [[wikt:savory|savory]]. It normally has a [[pie crust]]. The filling is made of [[egg (food)|egg]]s. Meat and/or vegetables and cheese are sometimes also in the filling. 
 
 The name is French.
