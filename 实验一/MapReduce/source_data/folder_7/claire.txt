The name '''Claire''' is a female [[given name]]. It is pronounced [ klair ]. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Definition of Claire {{!}} Dictionary.com|url=https://www.dictionary.com/browse/claire|access-date=2023-03-16|website=www.dictionary.com|language=en}} < /ref >  It means ''clear'' in [[French language|French]]. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Claire name Meaning and Origin|url=https://babys.name/detail-Claire-Meaning-Origin-Famous-Similar-Sibling.html|access-date=2023-03-16|website=babys.name}} < /ref >  The popularity of the name in [[United Kingdom]] peaked in the 1970s and 1980s. Around 1997, the name fell out of the top 100 girls' names there. Many famous people have this name. This name was ranked #59 on the US Popular Names in 2021. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Popular Baby Names|url=https://www.ssa.gov/OACT/babynames/|access-date=2023-03-16|website=www.ssa.gov|language=en}} < /ref > 
 
 One of them is the actress [[Claire Danes]].
