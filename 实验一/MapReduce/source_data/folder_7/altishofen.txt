 '''Altishofen''' is a [[municipality]] in [[Willisau (district)|Willisau]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Lucerne (canton)|Lucerne]] in [[Switzerland]]. On 1 January 2020 the municipality of [[Ebersecken]] became part of Altishofen.
