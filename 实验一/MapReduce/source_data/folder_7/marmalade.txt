[[Image:Marmalade spread on bread.jpg|thumb|right|250px|Marmalade spread on a slice of bread]] 
 '''Marmalade''' is a topping, like a [[jam]] or [[jelly]], that is usually made with [[Orange (fruit)|oranges]]. Marmalade can also be made from [[lemon]]s, [[lime]]s, [[grapefruit]]s, or a combination of citrus fruits. 
 
 The word Marmalade comes from the [[Galician language|Galician]] word  " marmelada "  meaning  
 ''quince jam''.
