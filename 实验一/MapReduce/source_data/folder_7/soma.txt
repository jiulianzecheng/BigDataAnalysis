'''Soma''' is a term with two different origins: 
 
 *[[Soma (drink)]]: a ritual drink of early Indo-Iranians, continued in Vedic and Persian sources. 
 *A word of [[Ancient Greek]] origin meaning  " body " . 
 **[[Somatic cell]]: a basic term in biology, describing the body cells as opposed to the reproductive cells 
 **[[Neuron|Soma (nerve cell]]), the body ( " perikaryon " ) of a [[nerve cell]] as opposed to its dendrites or axon 
 *[[Soma (fiction)]], a hallucinogenic drug in [[Aldous Huxley]]'s novels ''[[Brave New World]]'' and ''Island''.
