 '''Ohrid''' ({{lang-mk|Охрид}}; {{lang-sq|Ohër}}) is a city in [[North Macedonia]]. About 42,000 people lived there. It is on the [[Lake Ohrid]]. 
 
 The city has 365 churches, one for every day of the year. In 1979 and in 1980 respectively, Ohrid and Lake Ohrid were accepted as Cultural and Natural [[World Heritage Site]]s by [[UNESCO]].
