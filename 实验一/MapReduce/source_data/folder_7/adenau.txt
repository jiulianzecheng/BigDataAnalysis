 '''Adenau''' is a town in [[Rhineland-Palatinate]], [[Germany]]. It is located within [[High Eifel]]. The population in 2020 was about 3,000. There is a volcano east of the town. A rock festival [[Rock am Ring]] is held each year. In the Middle Ages, [[Order of Saint John (Bailiwick of Brandenburg)|Order of Saint John]] was located in the town. This was a German [[Protestantism|Protestant]] group of [[Knight|knights]]. The town is also called ''Johanniterstadt.''
