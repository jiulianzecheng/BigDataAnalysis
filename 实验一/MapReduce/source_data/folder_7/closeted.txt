'''Closeted''' and '''in the closet''' are [[metaphor]]s used to describe [[lesbian]], [[gay]], [[bisexual]], [[transgender]], [[queer]] and [[intersex]] (LGBTQI) people who for some reason have not disclosed their [[sexual orientation]], [[gender identity]] or intersex characteristics. People who have declared this identity are said to have  " [[coming out|come out of the closet]]. "
