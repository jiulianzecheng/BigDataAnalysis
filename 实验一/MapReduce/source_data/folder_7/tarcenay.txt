 '''Tarcenay''' is a former [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is in [[Bourgogne-Franche-Comté]] in the [[Doubs]] [[Departments of France|department]] in east [[France]]. On 1 January 2019, it was merged into the new commune of [[Tarcenay-Foucherans]]. < ref > [https://www.legifrance.gouv.fr/eli/arrete/2018/9/26/TERB1831184A/jo/texte Arrêté préfectoral] 26 September 2018 {{in lang|fr}} < /ref >
