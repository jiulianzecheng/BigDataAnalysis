'''Katara''' is a [[fictional character]] in [[Nickelodeon (TV channel)|Nickelodeon's]] [[anime|animated]] [[television programme|television series]] ''[[Avatar: The Last Airbender]]''. The character and series were created by [[Michael Dante DiMartino]] and [[Bryan Konietzko]].  
 
 Katara is [[voice actor|voiced]] by [[Mae Whitman]] and will be played by [[Kiawentiio Tarbell]] in the [[Avatar: The Last Airbender (upcoming TV series)|live action television remake]]. < ref > {{Citation|title=Avatar: The Last Airbender: Netflix Live-Action Series Reveals Cast and Creative Team - IGN|date=12 August 2021 |url=https://www.ign.com/articles/avatar-the-last-airbender-netflix-cast-aang-zuko-katara-sokka|language=en|access-date=2021-08-12|archive-date=2021-08-12|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210812145745/https://www.ign.com/articles/avatar-the-last-airbender-netflix-cast-aang-zuko-katara-sokka|url-status=live}} < /ref >  Her family is her brother Sokka, her father Hakoda, her mother Kya, and her grandmother Kanna.
