 '''Niedernberg''' is a [[Municipalities of Germany|municipality]] in the [[Miltenberg (district)|Miltenberg district]] in the [[Lower Franconia]] [[Regierungsbezirk|region]] in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. 
 
 == Geography == 
 
 === Place === 
 Niedernberg is just under 10 & nbsp;km from [[Aschaffenburg]] to the north, which itself is about 50 & nbsp;km southeast of [[Frankfurt|Frankfurt am Main]]. Niedernberg is the Miltenberg district's northernmost municipality and is found right on the [[Main (river)|Main]]’s left bank. In both the east and the west, it is hilly.
