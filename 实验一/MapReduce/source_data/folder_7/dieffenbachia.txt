 '''''Dieffenbachia''''' is a genus of tropical plants in the [[Family (biology)|family]] [[Araceae]] noted for their patterned [[leaves]]. Members of this genus are popular as [[houseplant]]s because of their tolerance to shade. The name commemorates [[Ernst Dieffenbach]], a German physician.
