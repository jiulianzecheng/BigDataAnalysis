 '''''Churamiti''''' is a [[genus]] of the [[true toads]].  The only species is '''''Churamiti maridadi'''''.  They live in [[Tanzania]]. It is listed as a [[critically endangered]] species.  This is due to a restricted range and [[habitat loss]].
