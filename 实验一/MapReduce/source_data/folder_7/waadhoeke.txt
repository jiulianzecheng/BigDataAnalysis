 '''Waadhoeke''' is a municipality of the Province of [[Friesland]], the [[Netherlands]]. It lies in the northeast of Friesland at the [[Wadden Sea]]. 
 
 The municipality was established on January 1, 2018. It was made by combining the former municipalities of [[het Bildt]], [[Franekeradeel]], [[Menameradiel]], and parts of [[Littenseradiel]]. 
 
 About 47,000 people live in Waadhoeke. It covers an area of about {{convert|305|km2|abbr=off}}. 
 
 The main settlement is the small [[city rights|city]] of [[Franeker]].
