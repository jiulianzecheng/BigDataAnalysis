 '''Lisse''' ({{IPA-fr|ˈlɪsə|lang|Nl-Lisse.ogg}}) is a town and municipality in the western [[Netherlands]], in the province of [[South Holland]].  
 
 It covers an area of 16.05 km2 (6.20 sq mi). Its population was 22,800 in 2019.
