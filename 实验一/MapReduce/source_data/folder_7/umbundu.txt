 '''South Mbundu''', or '''''Umbundu''''' is a language used by the [[Southern Mbundu people]].  They live in the central highlands of [[Angola]]. Umbundu is the most widespread [[Bantu languages|Bantu]] language in Angola. About one third of Angola is the Ovimbundu people.
