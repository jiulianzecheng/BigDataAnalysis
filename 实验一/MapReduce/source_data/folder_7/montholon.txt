 '''Montholon''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is in [[Bourgogne-Franche-Comté]] in the [[Yonne]] [[Departments of France|department]] in central [[France]]. The municipality was created on 1 January 2017 and consists of the former communes of [[Aillant-sur-Tholon]] (the seat), [[Champvallon]], [[Villiers-sur-Tholon]] and [[Volgré]]. < ref > [https://www.legifrance.gouv.fr/eli/arrete/2016/4/1/INTB1611585A/jo/texte Arrêté préfectoral] 1 April 2016 {{in lang|fr}} < /ref >
