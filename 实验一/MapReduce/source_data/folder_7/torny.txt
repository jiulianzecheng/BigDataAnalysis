 '''Torny''' is a [[municipality]] in the district of [[Glâne (district)|Glâne]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Fribourg (canton)|Fribourg]] in [[Switzerland]]. On 1 January 2004, the former municipalities of [[Middes]] and [[Torny-le-Grand]] merged into the municipality of Torny. < ref name=Fusion > [http://www.bfs.admin.ch/bfs/portal/de/index/infothek/nomenklaturen/blank/blank/gem_liste/03.html Amtliches Gemeindeverzeichnis der Schweiz] published by the Swiss Federal Statistical Office {{in lang|de}} accessed 19 July 2011 < /ref >
