'''Climatology''' is the study of [[climate]], scientifically defined as [[weather]] conditions [[average]]d over a period of [[time]], < ref > Climate Prediction Center. [http://www.cpc.noaa.gov/products/outreach/glossary.shtml#C Climate Glossary.] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20061006013653/http://www.cpc.noaa.gov/products/outreach/glossary.shtml#C |date=2006-10-06 }} Retrieved on [[November 23]], [[2006]]. < /ref >  and is a branch of the [[atmosphere|atmospheric]] [[science]]s.
