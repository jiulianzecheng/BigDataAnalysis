 '''Tecophilaeaceae''' is a [[Family (biology)|family]] of [[flowering plant]]s in the [[Order (biology)|order]] [[Asparagales]]. It includes 8 genera and about 25 species. 
 These are mainly [[Southern Hemisphere]] genera although there is one genus with one species from [[North America]]. The rootstock is a corm and the flowers are solitary or arranged on a simple or branched raceme. Flowers are yellow, white, violet, or blue and have six tepals and six stamens and a half-inferior three chambered ovary. The fruit is a capsule and the seeds are black, ovoid and often wrinkled.
