'''Periyakulam''' is a town and a [[municipality]] in the [[Theni district]] in the [[South India|South]] [[India]]n [[States and territories of India|state]] of [[Tamil Nadu]]. As of 2011, the population was 42,976. < ref name= " dashboard " > {{cite web|title=Census Info 2011 Final population totals|url=http://www.censusindia.gov.in/2011census/censusinfodashboard/index.html|publisher=Office of The Registrar General and Census Commissioner, Ministry of Home Affairs, Government of India|year=2013|access-date=26 January 2014}} < /ref >  Periyakulam is one of the oldest town in Tamil Nadu carrying rich cultural heritage. 
 
 ==Places of Worship== 
 * The Balasubramaniam Swamy temple is next to the Varaha nadhi (Varaha river). 
 * The [[Periyakal Temple]] and Karanamoorthy Swamy temple are next to the Periyakulam pond. 
 * The South-eastside Kailasanathar temple is in the hills. 
 * The Mounaguru Swamigal Madalayam is in Periaykulam Town. 
 * A temple for Varadharaja Perumal is in the center of Periyakulam Region.
