 '''Blindheim''' ({{IPA-de|ˈblɪnthaɪm|lang}}); {{Lang-gsw|Blĩĩdi|label=[[Swabian German]]}}; traditionally known in English as '''Blenheim''' ({{IPAc-en|ˈ|b|l|ɛ|n|ɪ|m}} {{respell|BLEN|im}}), is a [[Municipalities of Germany|municipality]] in the [[Bavaria]]n district of [[Dillingen, Bavaria|Dillingen]] in southern [[Germany]], containing of several villages. It is north of [[Augsburg]], on the left bank of the [[Danube]] River. Its population is about 1,700. 
 
 For administrative purposes, it is affiliated with the [[Municipal association (Germany)|municipal association]] of [[Höchstädt an der Donau]].
