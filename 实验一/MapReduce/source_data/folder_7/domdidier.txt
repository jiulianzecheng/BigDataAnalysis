 '''Domdidier''' ({{lang-frp|{{audio|Frp-greverin-Dondedi.ogg|Dondedi}}}} < ref > ''Dictionnaire-Dikchenéro: Français-Patois/Patê-Franché'', Société cantonale des patoisans fribourgeois, Fribourg, 2013, p. 148 < /ref > ) was a [[municipality]] of the district of [[Broye (district)|Broye]], in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Fribourg (canton)|Fribourg]], in [[Switzerland]]. On 1 January 2016, the former municipalities of Domdidier, [[Dompierre, Fribourg|Dompierre]], [[Léchelles]] and [[Russy, Switzerland|Russy]] merged to form the new municipality of [[Belmont-Broye]]. < ref > [http://www.bfs.admin.ch/bfs/portal/de/index/infothek/nomenklaturen/blank/blank/gem_liste/02.html Nomenklaturen – Amtliches Gemeindeverzeichnis der Schweiz] {{in lang|de}} accessed 3 February 2016 < /ref >
