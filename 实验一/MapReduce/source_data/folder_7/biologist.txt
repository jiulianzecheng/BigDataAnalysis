 There are many different kinds of biologists. Some of these include: 
 
 *Those who study [[fish]] and [[ocean]] plants. They are called [[marine biologist]]s.  
 *Those who study very small [[bacteria]] or [[virus|viruses]].  
 *Other scientists study groups of [[animal]]s.  
 *People who look at [[DNA]] in [[cell]]s are called [[geneticist]]s.  
 *Some biologists study using cells in [[factory|factories]] and [[company|companies]], and that is called [[biotechnology]].
