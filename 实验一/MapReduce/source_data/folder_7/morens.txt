 '''Morens''' was a [[municipality]] of the [[Broye (district)|district of Broye]], in the [[canton of Fribourg]], [[Switzerland]]. On 1 January 2017, the former municipalities of [[Bussy, Fribourg|Bussy]], [[Estavayer-le-Lac]], Morens, [[Murist]], [[Rueyres-les-Prés]], [[Vernay, Switzerland|Vernay]] and [[Vuissens]] joined together and became the new municipality of [[Estavayer]]. < ref > [https://www.bfs.admin.ch/bfs/de/home/grundlagen/agvch.html Historisiertes Gemeindeverzeichnis der Schweiz] accessed 5 January 2017 < /ref >
