 '''Stargard Szczeciński''' ({{lang-de|Stargard in Pommern}}) is a town in [[Pomerania]], northwestern [[Poland]]. It has 71,400 [[inhabitant]]s (2003). It is on the Ina river. It is the capital of [[Stargard County]] in [[West Pomeranian Voivodship]].
