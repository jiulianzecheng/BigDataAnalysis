'''Meshech''' is a person mentioned in the [[Bible]], described in [[Genesis]] 10 as the son of [[Japheth]], son of [[Noah]].  
 
 Meshech in the Bible can also mean the tribe that came from his children, or the country where they lived, for example in [[Psalm]] 120:5. Many Bible [[Atlas]]es such as [http://www.ivpress.com IVP] and [http://zondervan.com/9780310270508 Zondervan] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20121207084348/http://zondervan.com/9780310270508 |date=2012-12-07 }} state it was in [[Asia Minor]], while others say the [[Caucasus]].
