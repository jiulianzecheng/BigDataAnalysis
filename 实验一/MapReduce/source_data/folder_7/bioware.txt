 '''BioWare''' is a [[Canada|Canadian]] [[video game developer]] company that was started in 1995. The headquarters for BioWare is in [[Edmonton]], [[Alberta]]. It was started by medical doctors Ray Muzyka, Greg Zeschuk, and Augustine Yip. The company currently has six studios: BioWare [[Edmonton]], BioWare [[Montreal]], BioWare Mythic, BioWare [[Austin, Texas|Austin]], BioWare [[San Francisco]], and BioWare [[Ireland]]. They were acquired by the [[United States|American]] company [[Electronic Arts]] in 2008. < ref name=Gamespot > {{cite web|url=http://www.gamespot.com/news/ea-buying-bioware-pandemic-for-860m-6180818|access-date=2012-07-07|title= EA buying BioWare/Pandemic for $860M|publisher=[[GameSpot]]}} < /ref >  They developed their first game ''[[Shattered Steel]]'' in 1996. The company is probably best known for the ''[[Mass Effect]]'' and ''[[Dragon Age]]'' video game series.
