 '''Kenneth Brian  " Babyface "  Edmonds''' (born April 10, 1959) is an American [[R & B]] musician. He released his first album ''Lovers'' in 1986. He has won ten [[Grammy Awards]]. He started a record label called Soda Pop Records in 2009.
