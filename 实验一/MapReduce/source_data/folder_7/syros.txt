 '''Syros''' is a [[Greece|Greek]] [[island]]. It is in the [[Aegean Sea]]. Sometimes the name of the island is spelled '''Siros''' or '''Syra'''. 
 
 Syros is located {{convert|78|nmi|km|0}} south-east of [[Athens]]. [[Ermoupoli]] is the capital of the island.
