 '''Bulma''' is a [[fictional character]] from the [[manga]] and franchise ''[[Dragon Ball]]'' created by [[Akira Toriyama]]. She is introduced as a young but brilliant [[scientist]] girl searching for the magic Dragon Balls in order to make a wish. In her research he met [[Goku]] and later Yamcha, who will be is her [[boyfriend]] for a long time.
