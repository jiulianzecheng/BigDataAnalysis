'''Neravy''' is a town in the [[Karaikal district]] of [[Puducherry]] Union territory, India. This town is located 145km away from [[Pondicherry]], the capital of this union territory. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.wikiwand.com/en/Neravy|title=Neravy - Wikiwand|website=www.wikiwand.com}} < /ref > 
 
 ==Population== 
 The population of Neravy is 10890 according to 2001 Census of India. The male population is 5392 and female population is 5494. The literacy rate of the town is 81.19%. < ref > http://karaikal.gov.in/InfrastructureProfile/infrastructure_profile.htm < /ref > 
 
 ==Commune== 
 Neravy commune is one of the 5 communes in Karaikal district.
