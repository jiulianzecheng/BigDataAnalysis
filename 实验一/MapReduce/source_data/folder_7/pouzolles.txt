 '''Pouzolles''' ({{lang-oc|Posòlas}}) is a [[Communes of France|commune]] in the [[Hérault]] [[Departments of France|department]] and [[Occitanie]] in southern [[France]]. 
 
 == Activities == 
 The village has a small collection of shops (bakery, superette, tobacconist) and several [[bed and breakfast|''chambres d'hôtes'']]. There are other activities, such as a [[post office]], a nearby paintball, a seasonal gallery and of course the local wine industry.
