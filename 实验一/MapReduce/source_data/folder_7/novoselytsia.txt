 '''Novoselytsia''' ({{lang-uk|Новоселиця}}; {{lang-de|Nowosielitza}}; {{lang-pl|Nowosielica}}; {{lang-ro|Noua Suliță}}; {{lang-yi|נאוואסעליץ|Novoselitz}}) is a city in [[Chernivtsi Raion, Chernivtsi Oblast|Chernivtsi Raion]] ([[Raion|district]]), [[Chernivtsi Oblast]] ([[oblast|province]]) of [[Ukraine]]. In 2020, 7,566 people lived there.
