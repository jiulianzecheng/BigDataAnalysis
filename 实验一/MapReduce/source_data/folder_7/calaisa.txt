 '''Calaisa''' is a quartet from the town of [[Malmö]] in [[Sweden]]. It consists of the systers, [[Caisa Troedsson|Caisa]] and [[Lisa Troedsson]] and the sisters [[Malin Törnquist |Malin]] and [[Anna Törnquist]]. The band participated at [[Melodifestivalen 2008]] with the song ''If I Could'', which was knocked out during the [[Karlskrona]] competition on [[1 March]] [[2008 in music|2008]]. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.gp.se/kulturnoje/1.79334-perrelli-och-bengtzing-br-vidare-till-globen?m=print|title=Perrelli och Bengtzing vidare till Globen|publisher=Göteborgsposten|language=sv|author=Johan Rylander|date=1 March 2008|access-date=12 November 2013}} < /ref >
