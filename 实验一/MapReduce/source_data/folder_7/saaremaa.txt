 '''Saaremaa''' is the largest [[island]] in [[Estonia]]. It is {{convert|2673|km2|abbr=on}}. < ref > {{Cite web |url=http://www.saaremaa.ee/index.php?option=com_content & view=article & id=207 & Itemid=259 & lang=en |title=Official Web page of Saaremaa |access-date=2020-04-22 |archive-date=2017-08-10 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170810052409/http://www.saaremaa.ee/index.php?option=com_content & view=article & id=207 & Itemid=259 & lang=en |url-status=dead }} < /ref >  The island is in the [[Baltic Sea]], south of [[Hiiumaa]] island and west of [[Muhu]] island. It belongs to the [[West Estonian Archipelago]]. 
 
 The highest point on Saaremaa is 54 & nbsp;m above sea level. One feature found on the island is the [[Kaali crater]]. The island has lots of forested land. One of the symbols of the island is the [[juniper]].
