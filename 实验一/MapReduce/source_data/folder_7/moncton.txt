 '''Moncton''' {{IPAc-en|ˈ|m|ʌ|ŋ|k|t|ən}} is a [[Canada|Canadian]] city in [[Westmorland County, New Brunswick|Westmorland County]] in southeastern [[New Brunswick]]. Moncton is the largest city in the [[Canada|Canadian]] [[Provinces and territories of Canada|province]] of [[New Brunswick]], and the second largest in the maritime provinces.
