 '''Backwell''' is a [[village]] in the [[county]] of [[Avon (county)|Avon]], in [[North Somerset]]. 5,455 people live in Backwell. It is 7 miles from [[Bristol]]. [[Nailsea]], [[Flax Bourton]], [[Yatton]], Brockley and [[Barrow Gurney]] are nearby. 
 
 There is a railway station in the village called ''Nailsea and Backwell''. [[Bristol International Airport]] is less than three miles away.
