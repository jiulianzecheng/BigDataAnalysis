 '''FuboTV Inc.''' is an [[United States|American]] [[television]] [[Streaming media|streaming  service]]. It serves customers in the [[United States]], [[Canada]] and [[Spain]]. The service focuses mainly on [[Television network|channels]] that deal with live [[Sport|sports]]. Depending on country, the channels offered by Fubo may include access to [[Premier League|EPL]], [[National Football League|NFL]], [[Major League Baseball|MLB]], [[National Basketball Association|NBA]], [[National Hockey League|NHL]], [[Major League Soccer|MLS]] and international soccer, The service also provides [[news]], network [[Television program|television series]] and [[Movie|movies]] < ref name= " adage " > {{cite web|date=2017-09-12|title=FuboTV Reveals Its Package as It Takes on OTT Rivals in First TV Ads|url=http://adage.com/article/media/fubotv-reveals-package-tv-ads/310424/|access-date=2017-09-14|website=adage.com}} < /ref >
