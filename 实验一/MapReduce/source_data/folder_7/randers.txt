 '''Randers''' is a city in [[Randers Municipality]], [[Central Denmark Region]] on the [[Jutland]] peninsula. As of 1 January 2022, 62,802 people lived there, < ref name=pop > [https://m.statbank.dk/TableInfo/BY3?lang=en BY3: Population 1st January by urban areas, area and population density] The Mobile Statbank from [[Statistics Denmark]] < /ref >  which makes it the sixth-largest city in [[Denmark]] by population.
