 '''Tarquinia''' is an [[Italy|Italian]] city in [[Lazio]]. Tarquinia is in the [[Province of Viterbo]]. About 16,000 people live there. It is known for its ancient [[Etruscans|Etruscan]] tombs, which became a [[UNESCO World Heritage Site]] in 2004. 
 
 In 1922, it was renamed after the ancient city of '''Tarquinii''' (Roman) or '''Tarchuna''' (Etruscan).
