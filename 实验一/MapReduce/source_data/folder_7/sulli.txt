 '''Choi Jin-ri''' (March 29, 1994 – October 14, 2019), better known by her stage name '''Sulli''', was a South Korean singer-songwriter, actress and model. She played the young [[Princess Seonhwa of Silla]] in the [[Seoul Broadcasting System|SBS]] [[Korean drama|drama]] ''[[Ballad of Seodong]]'' (2005). 
 
 In 2009, Sulli became a member of the South Korean girl group [[F(x) (band)|f(x)]].
