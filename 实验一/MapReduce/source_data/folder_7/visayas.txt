 The '''Visayas''' or '''Visayan Islands''' ([[Cebuano language|Cebuano]] and [[Waray-Waray|Wáray-Wáray]]: ''Kabisay-an'', [[Tagalog language|Tagalog]]: ''Kabisayaan'', [[Spanish language|Spanich]]: ''Bisayas''), is one of the three principal geographical divisions of  the [[Philippines]]; the other two divisions are [[Mindanao]], to the south, and [[Luzon]], to the north. 
 
 People living in this islands are called ''Visayans''. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 The Visayas consists of several islands in central Philippines around the [[Visayan Sea]], with the [[Philippine Sea]] to the east and the [[Sulu Sea]] to the west. 
 
 The main islands (and area) are: 
 * [[Bohol (island)|Bohol]], {{Convert|3269|km2|sqmi|abbr=on}} 
 * [[Cebu (island)|Cebu]], {{Convert|4468|km2|sqmi|abbr=on}} 
 * [[Leyte (island)|Leyte]], {{Convert|7367.6|km2|sqmi|abbr=on}} 
 * [[Negros (island)|Negros]], {{Convert|13328.4|km2|sqmi|abbr=on}} 
 * [[Panay]], {{Convert|12011|km2|sqmi|abbr=on}} 
 * [[Samar Island|Samar]], {{Convert|12849|km2|sqmi|abbr=on}}

 The major islands, from west to east, are [[Panay Island|Panay]], [[Negros Island|Negros]], [[Cebu Island|Cebu]], [[Bohol (island)|Bohol]], [[Leyte Island|Leyte]], and [[Samar Island|Samar]].]] 
 Administratively, the Visayas is divided into 3 [[Regions of the Philippines|regions]]: [[Western Visayas]], [[Central Visayas]] and [[Eastern Visayas]]. Each region is headed by a Regional Director. 
 
 The Visayas is composed of 16 [[Provinces of the Philippines|provinces]], each headed by a [[governor]]. 
 
 ===Western Visayas (Region VI)=== 
 Western Visayas consists of the islands of [[Panay Island|Panay]] and the western half of [[Negros Island|Negros]]; it has a population, in 2010, of 7,102,438. < ref name=nscb > {{cite web |url=http://www.nscb.gov.ph/activestats/psgc/listreg.asp |title=NSCB ActiveStats - List of Regions |publisher=National Statistical Coordination Board - NSCB |language= |accessdate=15 November 2013 |date= |archive-date=27 October 2008 |archive-url=https://www.webcitation.org/5bscdln3Z?url=http://www.nscb.gov.ph/activestats/psgc/listreg.asp |url-status=dead }} < /ref >  The regional center is [[Iloilo City]]. Its provinces are: 
 * [[Aklan Province|Aklan]] 
 * [[Antique Province|Antique]] 
 * [[Capiz]] 
 * [[Guimaras]] 
 * [[Iloilo Province|Iloilo]] 
 * [[Negros Occidental]] 
 
 ===Central Visayas (Region VII)=== 
 Central Visayas includes the islands of [[Cebu Island|Cebu]] and [[Bohol (island)|Bohol]] and the eastern half of [[Negros Island|Negros]]; it has a population, in 2010, of 6,800,180. < ref name=nscb / >  The regional center is [[Cebu City]]. Its provinces are: 
 * [[Bohol (province)|Bohol]] 
 * [[Cebu Province|Cebu]] 
 * [[Negros Oriental]] 
 * [[Siquijor]] 
 
 ===Eastern Visayas (Region VIII)=== 
 Eastern Visayas consists of the islands of [[Leyte (island)|Leyte]] and [[Samar (island)|Samar]]; it has a population, in 2010, of 4,101,322. < ref name=nscb / >  The regional center is [[Tacloban]]. Its provinces are: 
 * [[Biliran]] 
 * [[Leyte Province|Leyte]] 
 * [[Southern Leyte]] 
 * [[Eastern Samar]] 
 * [[Northern Samar]] 
 * [[Samar Province|Samar]]
