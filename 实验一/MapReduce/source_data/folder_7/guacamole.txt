 '''Guacamole''' (called '''Guacamol''' in [[Central America]] and [[Cuba]]) is a [[Mexico|Mexican]] [[sauce|dip]] made of [[avocado]]s, [[salt]] and sometimes [[tomato]]es.  
 
 Various herbs may be added, such as [[chives]]. It is a green, creamy dip or garnish. It is often served as a dip or garnish with Mexican foods such as [[taco]]s and [[tortilla]] [[chips]].
