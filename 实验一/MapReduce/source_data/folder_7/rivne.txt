 '''Rivne''' ({{lang-uk|Рівне}}; {{lang-ru|Ровно}}; {{lang-pl|Równe}}) is a city in [[Ukraine]]. It is the [[administrative centre]] of [[Rivne Oblast]]. In 2020, 246,003 people lived there.
