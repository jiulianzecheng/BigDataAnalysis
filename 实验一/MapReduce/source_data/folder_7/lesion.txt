 A '''lesion''' is any damage or abnormal change in the tissue of an organism, usually caused by [[disease]] or [[Trauma (medicine)|trauma]]. ''Lesion'' is derived from the Latin ''laesio''  " injury " . < ref name= " :0 " > {{Cite web|url=http://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=9695|title=Lesion...What Does The Doctor Mean?|website=MedicineNet|access-date=2016-03-03|archive-date=2021-11-30|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20211130040059/https://www.medicinenet.com/script/main/art.asp?articlekey=9695|url-status=dead}} < /ref >  Lesions may occur in plants as well as animals.
