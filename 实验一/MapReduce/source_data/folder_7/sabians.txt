The '''Sabians''' is a type of [[monotheism|monotheistic]] [[people of the Book]], mentioned three times in the al-[[Quran]]. The Sabians are the [[Mandaeism|Mandaeans]], the [[Hermeticism|Hermetics]] and the [[Jew]]ish-[[Christian]] group of [[Elcesaites]].
