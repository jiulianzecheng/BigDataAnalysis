 '''''Tlalocohyla''''' is a genus of [[frog]]s in the family [[Hylidae]]. < ref name=Frost/ > < ref name=AmphibiaWeb/ >  They are called '''rain treefrogs''' and '''Middle American yellow-bellied treefrogs'''. They live in [[Middle America (Americas)|Middle America]] between Mexico and Costa Rica. < ref name=Frost/ >  Scientists made this genus in 2005 after they looked at the Hylidae again. Before 2005, the four species in this genus were in the genus ''[[Hyla]]''. < ref name= " Faivovich et al. 2005 " / >
