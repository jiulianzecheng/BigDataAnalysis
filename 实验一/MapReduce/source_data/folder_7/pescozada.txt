 '''Pescozada''' is a [[hip-hop]] [[group]] from [[El Salvador]]. Their members are two men named [[Debil Estar]] and [[Fat Lui]]. They [[hip hop|rap]] about [[gang]]s, [[politics]], and the future of El Salvador. Their songs include  " Primer Acto "  and  " I Could Fly " . They rap in [[Spanish language|Spanish]].
