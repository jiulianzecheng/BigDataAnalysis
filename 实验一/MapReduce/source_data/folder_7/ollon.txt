 '''Ollon''' is a [[municipality]] in the [[Aigle (district)|Aigle]] [[district]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Vaud]] in [[Switzerland]]. The municipality is in the foothills of the mountains southeast of [[Lake Geneva]].
