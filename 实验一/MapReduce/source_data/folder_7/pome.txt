 A '''pome''' is a type of [[fruit]] produced in plants of subtribe [[Malinae]] (in the tribe [[Maleae]] in the subfamily [[Amygdaloideae]]) in the family [[Rosaceae]]. Examples include [[Pear|pears]] and [[Apple|apples]]. They consist of [[carpel]]s surrounded by accessory tissue.
