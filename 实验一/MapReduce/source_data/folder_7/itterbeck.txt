 '''Itterbeck''' in [[County of Bentheim (district)|Bentheim]] in [[Lower Saxony]] is an old farming community that has developed into an independent community with almost 1,800 residents (2005). Itterbeck belongs to the Joint Community (''Samtgemeinde'') of [[Uelsen]], whose administrative seat is in the like-named community. 
 
 Itterbeck’s constituent communities (''[[Ortsteil]]e'') are Itterbeck, Egge, Itterbeckermoor, Kleine Striepe, Balderhaarmoor, Itterbeckerdoose and Ratzel.
