 '''Absurd''' can refer to several different things: 
 * [[Absurdity]], very poor reasoning; ridiculous, or nonsense 
 * [[Absurdism]], a philosophy about how the human search some meaning in the universe and the impossibility of finding that meaning 
 * Absurd or [[surreal humour]] 
 * [[Absurd (band)]], a German heavy-metal band 
 * Absurd, a term used in [[logic]] to describe a [[contradiction]] 
 ** ''[[Reductio ad absurdum]]'', a type of logical argument
