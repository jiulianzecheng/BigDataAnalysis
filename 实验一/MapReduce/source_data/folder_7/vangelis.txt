 '''Evángelos Odysséas Papathanassíou''' ({{lang-gr|Ευάγγελος Οδυσσέας Παπαθανασίου}} {{IPA-el|eˈvaɲɟelos oðiˈseas papaθanaˈsi.u|}}; 29 March 1943 – 17 May 2022), known professionally as '''Vangelis''' ({{IPAc-en|v|æ|ŋ|ˈ|ɡ|ɛ|l|ᵻ|s}} {{respell|vang|GHEL|iss}}; {{lang-gr|Βαγγέλης|links=no}} {{IPA-el|vaɲˈɟelis|}}), was a Greek musician and composer. His music was [[electronic music|electronic]], [[Progressive rock|progressive]], [[Ambient music|ambient]], [[jazz]], and [[orchestra]]l. < ref name= " Hischak " > {{cite book |title=The Encyclopedia of Film Composers |author=Thomas S. Hischak |year=2015 |url=https://books.google.com/books?id=Xz99CAAAQBAJ |publisher=Rowman  &  Littlefield |pages=386–388 |isbn=9781442245501}} < /ref >  He was best known for his [[Academy Awards|Academy Award]]-winning score to ''[[Chariots of Fire]]'' (1981), as well as for composing scores to the movies ''[[Blade Runner]]'' (1982), ''[[Missing (1982 movie)|Missing]]'' (1982), ''[[Nankyoku Monogatari|Antarctica]]'' (1983), ''[[The Bounty (1984 movie)|The Bounty]]'' (1984), ''[[1492: Conquest of Paradise]]'' (1992), and ''[[Alexander (2004 movie)|Alexander]]'' (2004). 
 
 He also composed music for documentaries such as ''[[Cosmos: A Personal Voyage]]'' by [[Carl Sagan]]. < ref name= " Hischak "  / >  He was born in [[Agria]], [[Hellenic State (1941–1944)|Hellenic State]] (present-day Agria, [[Greece]]). His career began in 1963. 
 
 Vangelis died on 17 May 2022 at a hospital in [[Paris]], [[France]] from [[heart failure]] while receiving treatment for [[COVID-19]], aged 79. < ref > {{cite news |last1=Mackenzie |first1=James |last2=Tagaris |first2=Karolina  |date=19 May 2022 |title=Vangelis, composer of 'Chariots of Fire' score, dies at 79 |url=https://nationalpost.com/pmn/entertainment-pmn/vangelis-composer-of-chariots-of-fire-score-dies-at-79-2 |work=[[National Post]] |access-date=19 May 2022}} < /ref > < ref > [https://www.ot.gr/2022/05/19/english-edition/vangelis-papathanasiou-oscar-winning-composer-passes-away-at-the-age-of-79/ Vangelis Papathanasiou: Oscar-winning composer passes away at the age of 79] < /ref > 
 
 ==Influences== 
 Vangelis stated composers like [[Hans Stockhausen]], [[Delia Derbyshire]], [[Michel Legrand]], [[Klaus Schulze]], [[Soft Machine]], [[Brian Eno]], [[Jerry Goldsmith]], [[Morton Subotnick]], [[Max Corbacho]] and [[Neu!]] as influences.
