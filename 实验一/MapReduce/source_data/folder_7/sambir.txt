 '''Sambir''' ({{lang-uk|Самбір}}; {{lang-ru|Самбор}}; {{lang-pl|Sambor}}) is a city in [[Lviv Oblast]] ([[oblast|province]]) of [[Ukraine]]. It is the administrative center of [[Sambir Raion]] ([[raion|district]]) and is close to the border with [[Poland]]. In 2021, 34,444 people lived there.
