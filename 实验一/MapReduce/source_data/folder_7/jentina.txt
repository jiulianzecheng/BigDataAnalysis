 '''Jentina Rose Rees''' (née Chapman) (born 6 March 1984 in [[Woking]], [[England]]) known simply as '''Jentina''', is an English rapper, singer, songwriter and model. She also is of [[Romani people]] descent. 
 
 ==Music career== 
 Jentina got her first record [[contract|deal]] at the age of nineteen. She was working in a computer shop at the time. She then came to fame in 2004 with her first [[Single (music)|single]]  " [[Bad Ass Strippa]] " . It was a rap song that sampled  " For the Love of Money "  by [[Contemporary R & B|R & B]] group [[the O'Jays]]. It reached number twenty two in the UK. Her follow up single  " [[French Kisses]] "  was more R & B than hip hop sounding and reached number twenty. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://www.officialcharts.com/artist/12521/jentina/ |title=Jetina |publisher=The Official UK Charts Company |access-date=26 August 2015}} < /ref >  Her third single  " [[Mysterious]] "  didn't chart in the UK and was only released in Italy. Her album ''[[Jentina (album)|Jentina]]'' was released in Italy in 2005.  " Bad Ass Strippa "  was later sampled and turned into a [[Diss (music)|diss song]] by fellow female rapper [[Lady Sovereign]] called  " Sad Arse Stripah " . 
 
 ==Modeling career== 
 Apart from being a musician, Jentina was also a model. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nH3M9kkjMwM |title=A Gypsy Life For Me Episode1 (A) |publisher=YouTube |date=17 November 2011 |access-date=13 April 2012}} < /ref >  In November 2007, she modeled for the underwear brand [[Wonderbra]]. She was also featured in a jokey video < ref > {{cite web|url=http://video.aol.com/video-detail/cadburys-gorilla-vs-wonderbra-/3672287583|title=Video.aol.com|access-date=17 September 2014}} < /ref >  of the chocolate bar [[Cadbury's]] ''Gorilla'' advert where a gorilla played the [[drum]]s to a [[Phil Collins]] song called  " [[In The Air Tonight]] " . < ref > {{cite web|url=http://theinspirationroom.com/daily/2007/wonderbra-two-cups-full-of-joy|title=Wonderbra - Two Cups Full of Joy|work=The Inspiration Room|access-date=17 September 2014}} < /ref > 
 
 ==Personal life== 
 Jentina was born to a Romani family that consisted of fourteen children. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://www.mtv.com/artists/jentina/biography/ |title=About Jentina|publisher=MTV Artists/Viacom |access-date=26 August 2015}} < /ref >  When she was one year old, her family moved to [[Molesey|West Molesey]], [[Surrey]].  
 
 In 2011, Jentina was on the judging panel for  " Travellers Got Talent " . < ref > {{cite web |url=http://www.thebiographychannel.co.uk/shows/a-gypsy-life-for-me/meet-the-gypsies.html |title=Meet the Gypsies - A Gypsy Life For Me on Bio |publisher=Thebiographychannel.co.uk |access-date=2015-08-22 |archive-date=2011-12-11 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20111211080445/http://www.thebiographychannel.co.uk/shows/a-gypsy-life-for-me/meet-the-gypsies.html |url-status=dead }} < /ref >  She also appeared in the eight-part documentary  " A Gypsy Life For Me "  which aired on [[:en:Bio (TV channel)|Bio]] in November 2011. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://www.thebiographychannel.co.uk/shows/a-gypsy-life-for-me.html |title=A Gypsy Life For Me - TV Shows on Bio |publisher=Thebiographychannel.co.uk |access-date=2015-08-22 |archive-date=2012-01-06 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120106071529/http://www.thebiographychannel.co.uk/shows/a-gypsy-life-for-me.html |url-status=dead }} < /ref >   
 
 Jentina is married and has three children. They live in London.
