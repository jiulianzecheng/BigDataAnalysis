 '''''Sooryavansham''''' is a 1999 Indian Hindi-language romantic musical drama film directed by E. V. V. Satyanarayana. It stars [[Amitabh Bachchan]] in dual roles, along with Soundarya, Jayasudha, Rachna Banerjee, Anupam Kher, and [[Kader Khan]]. The film is a remake of the 1997 Tamil-language film ''Suryavamsam.'' The story revolves around a traditional father and his uneducated yet obedient son, following their tense relationship and showing how the strict patriarchy causes them to fall apart. 
 
 == Plot == 
 Bhanu Pratap Singh, the village head, does not accept his youngest son, Heera, due to his illiteracy. However, he is determined to overcome all obstacles to fulfil his father's dream of building a hospital.
