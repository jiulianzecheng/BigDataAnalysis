 The '''karambit''' or '''kerambit''' is a kind of curved [[knife]] of [[Indonesia|Indonesian]] origin that is used as a [[weapon]]. < ref > {{Harvard citation|Inc|2004|p=82}} < /ref > 
 The shape of its blade resembles a tiger's claw. < ref > {{Harvard citation|PutraDanayu|2021|p=198}} < /ref >  Many kerambits have a ring at the end of the handle to retain the index finger. < ref > {{Harvard citation|PutraDanayu|2021|p=197}} < /ref >
