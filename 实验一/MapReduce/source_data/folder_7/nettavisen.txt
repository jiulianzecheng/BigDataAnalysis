 '''''Nettavisen''''' is an online newspaper from [[Norway]]. It was launched in 1996. < ref > {{cite book|author1=Trine Syvertsen|author2=Gunn Enli|author3=Ole J. Mjøs|author4=Hallvard Moe|title=The Media Welfare State: Nordic Media in the Digital Era|publisher=New Media World|url=http://quod.lib.umich.edu/n/nmw/12367206.0001.001/1:4/--media-welfare-state-nordic-media-in-the-digital-era?g=dculture;rgn=div1;view=fulltext;xc=1|access-date=2023-05-31|chapter=The Press}} < /ref > < ref name= " :0 " > {{Citation|last=Pettersen|first=Øyvind Breivik|title=Nettavisen|date=2023-01-23|url=https://snl.no/Nettavisen|work=Store norske leksikon|language=no|access-date=2023-05-31}} < /ref >  It was the country's first online-only newspaper. The current editor is [[Gunnar Stavrum]]. < ref name= " :0 "  / >  In 2015, it became one of the most popular news websites in Norway. < ref name= " :2 " > {{Cite web|last=Kantar|title=Tnslistene.no|url=https://tnslistene.no/?list_id=6 & week=22 & year=2015 & report=week & metric=uv|access-date=2023-05-31|website=tnslistene.no|language=en}} < /ref > 
 
 == History == 
 The newspaper was made by Odd Harald Hauge, Stig Eide Sivertsen, and Knut Ivar Skeid. < ref name= " :1 " > {{Cite web|last=Bradshaw|first=Paul|date=2008-01-25|title=Online Journalism Atlas: Norway|url=https://onlinejournalismblog.com/2008/01/25/online-journalism-atlas-norway/|access-date=2023-05-31|website=Online Journalism Blog|language=en}} < /ref >  It was first called  " ''Nettavisen'' "  in Norwegian. In 1999, it was bought by Spray Sweden. Spray Sweden later became part of Lycos Europe. < ref name= " :0 "  / >  The founders of ''Nettavisen'' also helped launch a site called Netzeitung. < ref name= " :1 "  / > 
 
 In 2002, ''Nettavisen'' was bought by TV 2. < ref name= " :0 "  / >  TV 2 sold the newspaper in 2008. < ref > {{Cite web|date=2009-07-30|title=Dundrende underskudd i Nettavisen|url=https://journalisten.no/nettavisen-imedium-egmont/dundrende-underskudd-i-nettavisen/167254|access-date=2023-05-31|website=journalisten.no|language=nb-no}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite web|last=Jensen|first=Martin Huseby|date=2008-10-23|title=TV 2 selger Nettavisen|url=https://journalisten.no/nettavisen-tv-2-a-pressen/tv-2-selger-nettavisen/176179|access-date=2023-05-31|website=journalisten.no|language=nb-no}} < /ref >  They did this because they didn't have enough money. Since 2009, ''Nettavisen'' has been owned by Egmont and Amedia. < ref name= " :0 "  / > < ref > {{Cite web|last=Mossin|first=Bjørn Åge|date=2015-09-08|title=Se hvem som er blitt nye ledere i Nettavisen|url=https://journalisten.no/thomas-gulbrandsen-nettavisen-farid-ighoubah/se-hvem-som-er-blitt-nye-ledere-i-nettavisen/308038|access-date=2023-05-31|website=journalisten.no|language=nb-no}} < /ref > 
 
 ''Nettavisen'' is part of the media company Mediehuset Nettavisen. It has been owned by them since 2008. < ref name= " :1 "  / >  In 2014, the company had 39.7% yearly income growth. < ref > {{Cite web|last=Fossbakken|first=Erlend|date=2023-05-30|title=Nytt rekordresultat fra Nettavisen|url=http://kampanje.com/medier/2015/03/nytt-rekordresultat-fra-nettavisen/|access-date=2023-05-31|website=kampanje.com|language=nb}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite web|last=Jensen|first=Martin Huseby|date=2015-03-23|title=Rekordår i Nettavisen|url=https://journalisten.no/gunnar-stavrum-amedia-egmont/rekordar-i-nettavisen/313717|access-date=2023-05-31|website=journalisten.no|language=nb-no}} < /ref >  It is the second most visited news website on mobile in Norway. < ref name= " :2 "  / > 
 
 ''Nettavisen'' is known for being somewhat right-of-center politically. < ref > {{Cite web|date=2018-04-09|title=Nettavisen-redaktør blant de best likte kommentatorene|url=https://www.nettavisen.no/12-95-3423435218|access-date=2023-05-31|website=Nettavisen|language=no}} < /ref >  It is considered the only large national newspaper in Norway. It expanded in 2017 by launching editions in Polish, Arabic, Russian, and Somali. < ref > {{Cite web|date=2017-06-30|title=Nettavisen oversettes til fire språk|url=https://www.nettavisen.no/12-95-3423353716|access-date=2023-05-31|website=Nettavisen|language=no}} < /ref >  In the same year, Amedia bought Nettavisen. < ref > {{Cite web|date=2017-10-16|title=Amedia kjøper Nettavisen|url=https://www.nettavisen.no/12-95-3423381133|access-date=2023-05-31|website=Nettavisen|language=no}} < /ref >
