 '''Staldenried''' ([[Walliser German]]: ''Schtaaluried'') is a [[municipality]] of the [[district]] of [[Visp (district)|Visp]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Valais]] in [[Switzerland]]. It has the largest [[Hamlet (place)|hamlet]] of Gspon, the hamlets of Zur Kirche and Zur Tanne along with about twenty small villages.
