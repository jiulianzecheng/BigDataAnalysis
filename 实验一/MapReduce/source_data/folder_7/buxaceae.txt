 '''Buxaceae''' is a [[family (biology)|family]] of plants. There are five [[genus|genera]], with about 70 [[species]]. The family has a [[cosmopolitan]] distribution. Many family members are grown as [[ornamental plant]]s. The plants are [[Herbaceous plant|herbaceous]] and [[evergreen]]. Some are [[shrub]]s or small [[tree]]s.
