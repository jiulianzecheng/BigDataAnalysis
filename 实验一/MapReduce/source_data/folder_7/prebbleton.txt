 '''Prebbleton''' is a small town in the [[Selwyn District]] in [[New Zealand]]. It is one of earliest settlements on the [[Canterbury Plains]]. It is 11km south west of [[Christchurch]] and 2km south of [[Hornby, New Zealand]]. It was named after the Prebble brothers from Kent, England.
