 '''Legnica''' ({{lang-de|Liegnitz}}) is a city in [[Poland]]. The [[river]] [[Katzbach]] runs through Legnica. There live over 100,000 people. The city is in west part of Poland (near [[Germany]] and [[Czech Republic|Czech]] border). Legnica is near a bigger city, [[Wrocław]]. 
 
 Legnica is in the middle of [[Silesia]]. The area used to be part of [[Prussia]], and Legnica was the capital of one of the three Silesian government areas ([[Regierungsbezirk]]).
