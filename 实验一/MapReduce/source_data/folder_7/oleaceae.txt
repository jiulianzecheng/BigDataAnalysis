 The '''olive family''' ('''Oleaceae''') is a family of [[flowering plant]]s, known to botanists have about 25 genera and over 500 species. The best known trees of this family are [[Olive (fruit)|olive]] and [[Ash tree|ash]], while the most familiar shrubs are privet, [[lilac]], and [[golden bell]], all popular ornamental plants.
