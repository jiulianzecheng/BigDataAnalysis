 '''Bruksvallarna''' is a [[urban areas of Sweden|minor locality]] in [[Härjedalen Municipality]] in [[Jämtland County]], [[Sweden]]. In 2010, 95 people lived there. < ref > Statistics from från [[SCB]]: [http://www.scb.se/statistik/MI/MI0811/2010A01/MI0811_2010A01_SM_MI38SM1203.pdf Småorter 2010 (updated 15 October 2012)] < /ref > 
 
 The place is 720 meters above sea level. < ref > {{cite web|title=Bruksvallarna|url=http://www.ne.se/lang/bruksvallarna|work=Nationalencyklopedin|access-date=30 October 2013}} < /ref > 
 
 [[Cross-country skiing]] competition [[Bruksvallsloppet]] is held here in November each year.
