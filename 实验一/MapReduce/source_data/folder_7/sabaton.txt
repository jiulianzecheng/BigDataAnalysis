 '''Sabaton''' is a Swedish [[heavy metal music|heavy metal]] [[band]]. It was formed in 1999. Sabaton have released 6 albums: Metalizer from 2000, Primo Victoria from 2004, Attero Dominatus from 2005, The Art of War from 2008, Coat of Arms from 2010, and Carolus Rex from 2012. Their lyrics are based on [[war]]s and battles, especially the two World Wars. They sing and have many albums in both [[Swedish language|Swedish]] and [[English language|English]]. 
 
 
 Sabaton holds the lead for most successful Swedish heavy metal band.
