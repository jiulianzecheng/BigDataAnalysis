 ==History== 
 
 In 2005, Blake Mallen, Ryan Blair, and Miamian Nick Sarnicola founded ViSalus. < ref name= " MIH " / > < ref name= " PRN " / >  In 2008, Blyth, Inc. purchased a stake in the company and increased its ownership to almost seventy-five percent. < ref name= " MIH " / >  In 2013, the company expanded to the [[United Kingdom]], with an office in Guilford. < ref name= " BMM " / >  Ros Simmons was appointed as Managing Director of ViSalus for the United Kingdom. < ref name= " BMM " / > 
 
 ==Product== 
 
 ViSalus is best known for its Vi 90-Day Challenge, a health challenge for customers. < ref name= " MIH " / > < ref name= " CRU " / > < ref name= " BMM " / >  ViSalus also markets products including NEURO, Vi-Net, Vi-Net Mobile, and Vi-Shape Nutritional Shake Mix. < ref name= " CRU " / >  The company sells its products in the [[United States]], [[Canada]], and the United Kingdom. < ref name= " MIH " / > < ref name= " PRN " / >  ViSalus uses direct marketing and employs 92,000 promoters. < ref name= " MIH " / > < ref name= " CRU " / >  In 2012, the Miami Herald reported that [[Hulk Hogan]] and Alfonso Ribeiro endorsed ViSalus products. < ref name= " MIH " / >
