 '''Dippoldiswalde''' is a town in the [[Free State of Saxony]], [[Germany]]. It was the [[capital city]] of the [[Weißeritzkreis Rural District|Weißeritzkreis]] district. Today, Dippoldiswalde is a city in [[Sächsische Schweiz-Osterzgebirge Rural District]]. It is {{convert|23|km|mi|0|abbr=off|lk=on}} east of [[Freiberg, Saxony|Freiberg]], and {{convert|18|km|mi|0|abbr=off|lk=on}} south of [[Dresden]]. 
 
 The town is on the [[Weisseritz railway]], a [[narrow gauge]] railway powered by [[steam locomotives]].
