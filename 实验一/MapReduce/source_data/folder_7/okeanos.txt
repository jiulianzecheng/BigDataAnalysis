'''Okeanos''' (Latin: '''Oceanus''') is a [[Titan (mythology)|Titan]] in [[Greek mythology]]. He is titan of the earth-encircling river Okeanos. His parents are [[Gaia (mythology)|Gaia]] and [[Uranos]]. With his sister [[Tethys (mythology)|Tethys]] his children are the river gods, the sea and spring [[nymphs]], the [[Okeanid]]s.
