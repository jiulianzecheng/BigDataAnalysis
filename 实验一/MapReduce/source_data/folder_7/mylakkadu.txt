 '''Mylakkadu''' is a small village in the [[Adichanalloor]] grama punchayath in the [[Kollam district]], [[Kerala]], [[India]]. Making clay and bricks is an important business in the village. Factories started there when India was a colony. [[Kottiyam]], [[Paravur, Kollam|Paravur]] and [[Chathannoor]] are the nearest towns.
