 }}'''Bishapur''' ([[Middle Persian]]: ''Bay-Šāpūr''; {{lang-fa|بیشاپور|}}, ''Bishâpûr'') was an ancient city in [[Sasanian Empire|Sasanid Persia]] ([[Iran]]) on the ancient road between [[Persis]] and [[Elam]]. The road linked the [[Sassanid]] capitals [[Estakhr]] (very close to [[Persepolis]]) and [[Ctesiphon]]. It is located south of modern [[Faliyan]] in the [[Kazerun County]] of [[Fars province|Fars Province]], [[Iran]].
