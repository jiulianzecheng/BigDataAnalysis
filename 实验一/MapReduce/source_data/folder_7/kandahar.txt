 '''Kandahar''' is the second largest city in [[Afghanistan]]. < ref > {{cite book|first=Chris|last=Ryan|title=Battleground (Code Red)|year=2009|url=https://archive.org/details/battlegroundcode0000ryan_d8w2|publisher=Doubleday|page=[https://archive.org/details/battlegroundcode0000ryan_d8w2/page/307 307]|isbn=9780385612999}} < /ref >  [[Alexander the Great]] visited the town and named it after himself. Alexander the Great founded Kandahar in the 4th century BC. He called it  " Alexandria Arachosia " . < ref name= " WDL " > {{cite web |url = http://www.wdl.org/en/item/11738/ |title = The Empire and Expeditions of Alexander the Great |website = [[World Digital Library]] |date = 1833 |accessdate = 2013-07-26 }} < /ref >  About 500,000 people live there in 2015. It is in the central-south of Afghanistan. < ref name=eb  > {{cite encyclopedia|title=Kandahār|encyclopedia=Encyclopædia Britannica|publisher=Encyclopædia Britannica}} < /ref >  Kandahar is the [[Capital (city)|capital]] of [[Kandahar Province]].  It is an important trading centre for sheep, wool, cotton, silk, felt, food grains, fresh and dried fruit, and tobacco. Kandahar has an [[international airport]]. < ref name=eb / >
