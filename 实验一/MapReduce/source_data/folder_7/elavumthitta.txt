 ==Educational institutions== 
 
 *Sreebuddha College of Engineering (now mixed) 
 *Salvation Army L P school, Thumpamon North - This is the first School of Elavumthitta, established in the year 1903. It celebrated its Centenary year in 2003. The land for the school was donated by Sri Neelakanta Pillai of Alakkatu. Incidentally he was also the first headmaster of the School. The school is located 1 & nbsp;km away from Elavumthitta market on the way side of Ramanchira road. {{Citation needed|date=September 2017}}
