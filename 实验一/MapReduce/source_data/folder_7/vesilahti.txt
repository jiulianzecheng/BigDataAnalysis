 '''Vesilahti''' is a [[municipality]] in [[Pirkanmaa]], [[Finland]]. It had about 4,4800 people living there in January 2014. The municipalities next to it are [[Akaa]], [[Lempäälä]], [[Nokia, Finland|Nokia]], [[Pirkkala]], [[Sastamala]] and [[Urjala]].
