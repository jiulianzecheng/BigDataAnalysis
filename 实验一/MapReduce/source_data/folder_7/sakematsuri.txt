 '''Saijo Sake Festival''' is a [[sake]] [[festival]] held in [[Hiroshima]] prefecture in [[west]] [[Japan]]. < ref name= " SSF " > {{cite web |url=https://www.insidejapantours.com/experience-japan/i-fst08-13/saijo-sake-festival-/ |title=Saijo sake festival |author= |date= |website= |publisher=InsideAsia Tours Ltd. |accessdate=2 March 2015}} < /ref >  [[Sake]] is Japan's traditional [[alcoholic drink]]. The sake festival is held [[year]]ly on the second Saturday and Sunday of October. < ref name= " SSF "  / >  Every year between 100,000 and 200,000 people attend the festival. < ref name= " enJ " > {{cite web |url=http://en.japantravel.com/view/saijo-sake-festival |title=Saijo Sake Festival; A chance to sample over 900 sakes from around Japan |author=Ciara Long |date=October 2012 |website= |publisher=Japan Travel K.K., Tokyo Japan |accessdate=2 March 2015}} < /ref >   
 
 == Breweries == 
 Saijo is [[wikt:famous|famous]] all over Japan for its sake. There are 10 [[Brewery|breweries]] here. < ref name=SSF/ >  Sake has been made in Saijo since 1650. [[Visitor]]s may walk from brewery to brewery [[wikt:sample|sampling]] all the varieties of sake. < ref name=HP > {{cite web |url=http://visithiroshima.net/things_to_do/seasonal_events/autumn/sake_festival.html |title=Sake Festival |author= |date= |website= |publisher=Hiroshima Prefecture |accessdate=2 March 2015 |archive-date=26 February 2015 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150226150218/http://visithiroshima.net/things_to_do/seasonal_events/autumn/sake_festival.html |url-status=dead }} < /ref > 
 
 == Event == 
 During the festival the [[road]] from the [[railway station]] to Chuo Park is lined with [[food]] [[wikt:booth|booth]]s. The park is divided into two sections, [[east]] and west. < ref name=SSF/ >  The events on the east side include more food, [[Stage (theatre)|stages]] with [[performer]]s and is [[wikt:free|free]]. < ref name=SSF/ >  The west side, called Sake Square, requires an [[Ticket (admission)|admission ticket]] which costs 1,800 [[yen]]. < ref name=enJ/ >   
 
 Visitors can try any of over 900 different kinds of sake. < ref name= " SSF "  / >  On entry to this side, visitors receive a small saki [[wikt:cup|cup]] they can re[[wikt:fill|fill]] as many times as they like. < ref name= " enJ "  / >
