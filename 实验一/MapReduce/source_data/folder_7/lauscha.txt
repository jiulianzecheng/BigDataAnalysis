 '''Lauscha''' is a German city in [[Thuringia]]. Next bigger cities are [[Sonneberg]] and [[Coburg]]. Its population is ca 4,000. Its mayor is Norbert Zitzmann. 
 
 Lauscha is in the middle of the Thuringian forest.
