'''Interjet''' was a [[budget airline|low-cost airline]] based in [[Mexico]] begun in 2005. < ref > {{Cite web |url=http://airwaysnews.com/blog/2013/08/27/interjet-a-brief-history-and-flight-review/ |title=Interjet- A Brief History and Flight Review - Airchive |access-date=2015-03-09 |archive-date=2015-02-15 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150215013020/http://airwaysnews.com/blog/2013/08/27/interjet-a-brief-history-and-flight-review/ |url-status=dead }} < /ref >  [[Mexico City International Airport]] is Interjet's hub. Interjet mainly flies [[Airbus A320 family|Airbus A320]] and [[Sukhoi Superjet]] airplanes from Mexico to the [[United States|USA]], [[Central America]] and [[South America]].
