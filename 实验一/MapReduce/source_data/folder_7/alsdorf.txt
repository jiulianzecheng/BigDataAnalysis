 '''Alsdorf''' is a [[municipality]] in [[North Rhine-Westphalia]], [[Germany]]. It is in the [[Aachen (district)|Aachen]] district. It is very close to The [[Netherlands]]. The town has 17 districts. The population was 47,000 in 2020. The main political [[Political party|party]] is [[Social Democratic Party of Germany|SPD]]. The castle Alsdorf is a main tourist attraction. The town used to be a [[mining]] center and there is a mining museum. There is also a [[zoo]].
