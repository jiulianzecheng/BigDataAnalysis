 '''Herzogenaurach''' is a town in the district of [[Erlangen-Höchstadt]] in [[Bavaria]] in [[Germany]]. It is best known for being the headquarters of two major international sportswear companies [[Adidas]] and [[Puma (brand)|Puma]].
