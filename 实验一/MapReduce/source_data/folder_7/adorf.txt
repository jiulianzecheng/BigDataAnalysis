 '''Adorf''' is a small town in [[Saxony]], [[Germany]]. It is within [[Vogtlandkreis]] and in the southeast of Saxony. The town is found within the [[Elstergebirge]]. In 2020, the population was about 5,000. The rivers [[White Elster]] and Schwarzbach meet at Adorf.
