 '''Gundagai''' is a town in [[New South Wales]], [[Australia]]. It is a small town, but [[author]]s often write about Gundagai. It is seen to be the typical Australian country town. < ref name=SMH > {{cite web|publisher = [[Sydney Morning Herald]]|title = Gundagai|date = 8 February 2004|url = http://www.smh.com.au/news/New-South-Wales/Gundagai/2005/02/17/1108500193671.html |accessdate = 2008-05-08}} < /ref >  It is built on the banks of the [[Murrumbidgee River]]. It is near the Muniong and Yambla Mountain ranges. Gundagai is 390 [[kilometre|km]] south-west of [[Sydney]]. Gundagai is part of the  [[Gundagai Shire Council]] [[Local Government Areas of Australia|Local Government Area]].  In 2006 there were 1,998 people living in Gundagai. < ref name =  " 2006pop " > {{Census 2006 AUS|id=UCL136600|name=Gundagai (Urban Centre/Locality)|quick=on|accessdate=2008-02-28}} < /ref > 
 
 The [[bushranger]], [[Captain Moonlight]], is buried in the Gundagai Cemetery.
