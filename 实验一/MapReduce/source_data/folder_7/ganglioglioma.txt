'''Gangliogliomas''' are [[tumor]]s that start from groups of [[Neuron|nerve cell]]s (ganglion and glial cells) and grow in the [[brain]]. They are usually small and noncancerous, and they do not spread to other parts of the body. 
 
 Gangliogliomas are rare tumors. Most gangliogliomas occur in children and young adults. 
 
 == Symptoms == 
 [[Seizure]]s, [[headache]], [[vomiting]], 
 [[fatigue]] and weakness on one side of the body.
