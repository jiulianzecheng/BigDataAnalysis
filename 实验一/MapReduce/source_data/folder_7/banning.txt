 '''''Banning''''' is a 1967 American sports drama movie directed by [[Ron Winston]]. It stars [[Robert Wagner]], [[Anjanette Comer]], [[Guy Stockwell]], [[Gene Hackman]], [[Jill St. John]], [[Howard St. John]], [[Sean Garrison]] and was distributed by [[Universal Pictures]]. It was nominated for an Academy Award in 1968.
