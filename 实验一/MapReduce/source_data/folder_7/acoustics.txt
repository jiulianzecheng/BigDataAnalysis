'''Acoustics''' is a science. It studies the [[mechanical wave]]s in gases, liquids, and solids.  These waves can make [[echo (acoustics)|echo]], [[vibration]], [[sound]], [[ultrasound]] and [[infrasound]]. 
 
 A scientist who works in the field of '''acoustics''' is an acoustician.  Someone who works with acoustics technology may be called an acoustical engineer.
