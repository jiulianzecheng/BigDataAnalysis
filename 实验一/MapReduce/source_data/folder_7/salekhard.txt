 '''Salekhard''' ({{lang-ru|Салехард}}; {{lang-yrk|Саляʼ харад}}) is a town in [[Russia]]. It is the administrative center of [[Yamalo-Nenets Autonomous Okrug]]. In 2010, 42,544 people lived there.
