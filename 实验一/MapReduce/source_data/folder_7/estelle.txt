 '''Estelle Fanta Swaray''', known by her [[Mononymous person|mononym]] '''Estelle''' (born 18 January 1980) < ref name=MTVuk > {{cite web|url=http://www.mtv.co.uk/artists/estelle|title=Estelle {{!}} MTV UK|accessdate=6 February 2012|archive-date=26 January 2010|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20100126070150/http://www.mtv.co.uk/artists/estelle|url-status=dead}} < /ref >  is an [[British people|British]] [[singer]]-[[songwriter]], [[rapper]] and [[actress]]. She was born in [[Hammersmith]], [[London]], [[England]]. < ref name=MTVuk/ > 
 ==Career== 
 In 2004, two of Estelle's [[Single (music)|singles]] made it into the [[record chart|charts]]. These were  " 1980 "  and  " Free " . She also won a [[Music of Black Origin Awards|MOBO Award]] for  " Best Newcomer " . < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.mobo.com/2004%20Royal%20Albert%20Hall|title=BOBO Awards 2004|accessdate=6 February 2012|archive-date=24 July 2011|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110724150749/http://www.mobo.com/2004%20Royal%20Albert%20Hall|url-status=dead}} < /ref >  The [[music video|videos]] to both of her songs were [[movie director|directed]] by Andy Hylton. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.andyhylton.com |title=Andy Hylton |publisher=Andy Hylton |accessdate=2010-06-20}} < /ref >   " Free "  was [[songwriter|written]] by Estelle. It was [[producer|produced]] by Jimmy Hogarth. Her first [[album]],''[[The 18th Day]]'', reached number 35 in the [[UK Albums Chart]]. < ref name= " UKCHARTS " > {{cite web| url=http://www.chartstats.com/artistinfo.php?id=218| title=Estelle UK Chart Positions and Trajectories| publisher=chartstats.com| accessdate=10 April 2009| archiveurl=https://archive.today/20130119014137/http://www.chartstats.com/artistinfo.php?id=218| archivedate=19 January 2013| url-status=live}} < /ref > 
 
 In 2008, Estelle worked with rapper/producer [[Kanye West]] on her song  " [[American Boy]] " . This song reached the top 10 in many [[country|countries]]. It was number one on the [[UK Singles Chart]] < ref name= " UKCHARTS " / > < ref > {{cite web| url=http://www.nme.com/news/estelle/35520| title=Estelle  &  Kanye West hold on to Number One spot| publisher=[[New Musical Express]]| accessdate=10 April 2009 }} < /ref >  and in [[Belgium]] < ref name= " BEL " > {{cite web| url=http://www.ultratop.be/en/showitem.asp?interpret=Estelle+feat%2E+Kanye+West & titel=American+Boy & cat=s| title=Estelle feat. Kanye West - American Boy Belgian Charts/Worldwide| publisher=[[Ultratop]]/ultratop.be| accessdate=10 April 2009 }} < /ref >  and [[Israel]]. The song won many [[award]]s. These included a [[Grammy Award]] for best [[hip-hop|Rap / collaboration]]. The song was also [[wikt:nominate|nominated]] for the Grammy for  " Song of the Year " .  " American Boy "  reached number 7 on ''[[Rolling Stone]]'''s list of the 100 Best Songs of 2008. < ref > [https://www.rollingstone.com/news/story/24947047/the_100_best_singles_of_2008/  " The 100 Best Songs of 2007 " ] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20090505215547/http://www.rollingstone.com/news/story/24947047/the_100_best_singles_of_2008 |date=2009-05-05 }} ''Rolling Stone'' (25 December 2008) Retrieved 25 December 2008 < /ref >  Her album ''[[Shine (Estelle album)|Shine]]'' was released after the single. It was on the albums chart in most countries. It was number 6 in the [[UK Albums Chart|UK]]. < ref name= " UKCHARTS "  / >   Estelle also released a new single with  rapper [[Busta Rhymes]]. It is called  " [[World Go Round]] " . The single was on Rhymes' album ''[[Back on My B.S.]]'', which was released on 19 May 2009. 
 
 Estelle's third album ''All of Me'' was released on 28 February 2012 in the US.  
 
 Estelle does the voice of Garnet, one of the main characters in the cartoon [[Steven Universe]]. < ref > http://www.cartoonnetwork.com/PR/pr-stevenuniverse/ < /ref >
