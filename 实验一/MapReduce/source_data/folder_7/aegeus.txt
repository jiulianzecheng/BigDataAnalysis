'''Aegeus''' is a figure in [[Greek mythology]] and the father of [[Theseus]]. He is part of the [[founding myth]] of [[Athens]]. After he was defeated by [[Minos]] in a war, Athens sent seven men and seven women to [[Crete]] every nine years. These people would be sacrificed to the [[Minotaur]].
