 The '''Ostalbkreis''' is a [[Districts of Germany|''Landkreis'' (district)]] in the east of [[Baden-Württemberg]], [[Germany]]. 
 
 The district is located in the eastern part of the [[Swabian Jura]] mountains. The main rivers in the district are the [[Rems (river)|Rems]], the [[Jagst]] and the [[Kocher]].
