 '''Evergy''' is an [[electricity|electric utility]] in [[Kansas City, Missouri|Kansas City]], [[Missouri]], [[United States]]. The company was created from a merger of '''Westar Energy''' of [[Topeka, Kansas|Topeka]] and '''[[Great Plains Energy]]''' of Kansas City, Missouri. Evergy is the largest electric company in [[Kansas]]. They serve 690,000 residential, commercial and industrial customers < ref > [https://www.reuters.com/finance/stocks/companyProfile?symbol=EVRG.N Evergy summary, Reuters, October 8, 2013] < /ref >  in the eastern third of Kansas.
