 '''''Nepeta''''' is a [[genus]] of about 250 [[species]] of [[flowering plant]]s in the family [[Lamiaceae]].  
 
 Some members of this group are known as '''[[catnip]]''' or catmint because of their effect on [[house cat]]s. The oil in the leaves of some ''Nepeta'' species gives cats euphoria, that is, it makes them feel good.
