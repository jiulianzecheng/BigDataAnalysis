 '''HaTnu'a''', Hebrew: התנועה , meaning ''The Movement'', is an [[Israel]]i political party.   
 
 Its ideology is [[social liberal]], [[secular]] and [[Zionist]].  
 
 HaTnu'a was founded in 2012 by [[Tzipi Livni]] (also political leader), as a splitting from [[Kadima]]. 
 
 Number of seats in the 20th [[Knesset]]: 5 (down one from 19th Knesset). < br/  > 
 HaTnu'a is cooperating with [[HaAvoda]] ( " Labor " ) and [[HaTnu'a HaYeruqa]] ( " The Green Movement " ) as [[HaMahaneh HaTziyoni]] (''The Zionist Union''). Together they have 24 seats.
