'''Pronunciation''' is a way a [[word]] is usually [[Speech|spoken]]. It can be affected by many different [[factor]]s: 
 
 # Geographical location or [[dialect]] 
 # Presence of speech defect 
 # [[Ethnic group]] 
 # [[Education]] level 
 # [[Social class]] 
 
 '''Mispronunciation''' is an actual [[wikt:mistake|mistake]] in speaking. It is not used for how a speaker of a [[dialect]] pronounces a word. Examples occur in many place names and personal names. For instance, [[Leicester Square]] is actually pronounced like  " Lester Square " .
