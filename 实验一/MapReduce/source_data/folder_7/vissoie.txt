 '''Vissoie''' is a village of the [[district]] [[Sierre (district)|Sierre]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Valais]] in [[Switzerland]]. The village was made in 1905 by splitting from the municipality of Ayer and Grimentz. On 1 January 2009 the former municipalities of Vissoie, [[Ayer, Switzerland|Ayer]], [[Chandolin]], [[Grimentz]], [[Saint-Jean, Switzerland|Saint-Jean]] and [[Saint-Luc, Switzerland|Saint-Luc]] merged into the new municipality of [[Anniviers]].
