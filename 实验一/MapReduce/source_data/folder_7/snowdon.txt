 '''Snowdon''' is the highest [[mountain]] in [[Wales]].  Its top is known as ''Yr Wyddfa'' (Old Welsh meaning  " the grave " ).  The [[English language|English]] name Snowdon comes from [[Anglo-Saxon|Saxon]]  " Snow Dun " , meaning  " [[snow]] [[hill]] " . 
 
 It is in [[Snowdonia|Snowdonia National Park]]. As the highest mountain in Wales, Snowdon is one of three mountains climbed as part of the  [[Three Peaks Challenge]].
