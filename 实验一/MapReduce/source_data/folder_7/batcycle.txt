 The''' Batcycle''' is the fictional personal motorcycle for [[Batman]] that has built-in guns. It also as a bullet proof window guard. The batcycle either comes alone or it is built into the [[Batmobile]]. The batcycle appeares in ''[[Batman (1966 movie)|Batman]]'' and as the''' Bat Pod''' in ''[[The Dark Knight (movie)|The Dark Knight]]'' and in ''[[The Dark Knight Rises]]''. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://www.batmanytb.com/movies/thedarkknight/gadgets/batpod.php |title=Movies - The Dark Knight - Gadgets - The Batpod |publisher=Batmanytb.com |access-date=2010-09-16 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20100901133629/http://www.batmanytb.com/movies/thedarkknight/gadgets/batpod.php |archive-date=2010-09-01 |url-status=dead }} < /ref >  In the [[Christopher Nolan]] movies, ''The Dark Knight, ''the cycle was built into the Batmobile,'' ''and in'' The Dark Knight Rises'' the cycle was given to [[Catwoman]]. The cycle was built in [[Wayne Enterprises]] by [[Lucius Fox]].
