 '''Karditsa''' is a Greek city. It is in the center of [[Greece]] in the district of [[Thessaly]]. It had 35,971 people according to the 2001 census. Karditsa is build on the large plain which is named Thessaly plain. It is the second largest [[plain]] in [[Greece]]. So it is an agricultural city. Near Karditsa is the city of [[Trikala]]. The west of Karditsa is the [[Agrafa]] range. Agrafa is a part of [[Pindus]] range.
