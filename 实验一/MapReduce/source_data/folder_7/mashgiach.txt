A '''mashgiach''' (Hebrew: משגיח, lit.  " supervisor " ; pl. משגיחים, mashgichim) is a [[Jew]] who supervises the [[kashrut]] status of a [[kosher]] establishment. They usually supervise places where food is produced, such as [[Slaughterhouse|slaughterhouses]], [[Food industry|food manufacturers]], [[hotels]], [[caterer|caterers]], nursing homes, restaurants, [[butcher|butchers]] or groceries.
