 '''Aleppo''' ({{lang-ar|حلب}} ['ħalab], {{lang-tr|Halep}}, {{lang-el|Αλέππο}}) is a [[city]] in northern [[Syria]].  From 2012 to 2016 it was a battlefield in the [[Syrian civil war]].   
 
 In 2010 it had an official population of 4.6 million. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.aksalser.com/?page=view_news & id=dea2316c19d685faf12f9850768f87b7|title=بـ 4.6 ملايين نسمة .. محافظة حلب الأعلى سكانياً في سورية - عكس السير دوت كوم|website=www.aksalser.com}} < /ref >  Then, Aleppo was the largest Syrian city. After 2010, however, Aleppo is probably the second-largest city in Syria after the capital [[Damascus]]. 
 
 Aleppo is one of the oldest continuously lived in (lived in all the time) cities in the world. People may have arrived in the 6th millennium [[BC]]. < ref > ''Columbia Encyclopedia'', 6th edition, 2010. < /ref >   
 
 There was a [[1138 Aleppo earthquake|large earthquake]] near Aleppo in 1138.
