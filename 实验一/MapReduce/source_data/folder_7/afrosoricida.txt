 '''Afrosoricida''' is an [[Order (biology)|order]] of [[mammal]]s, which includes the [[Tenrecidae|Tenrecs]] and the [[Golden mole]]. < ref name=msw3 > {{Citation|author= Bronner G.N.  &  Jenkins P.D.|year=2005|contribution-url=http://www.bucknell.edu/msw3|contribution=Order Afrosoricida|editor=D.E. Golden moles look like [[mole]]s. [[Sequence analysis]] found that these animals are not closely related to other [[insectivore]]s. 
 
 Some biologists use '''Tenrecomorpha''' as the name for the tenrec-golden mole [[clade]], but Gary Bronner and Paulina Jenkins argue that Afrosoricida is more appropriate, despite their misgivings about the similarity between the name  " Afrosoricida "  and the unrelated shrew [[subgenus]] ''[[Afrosorex]]''. < ref name=msw3/ >
