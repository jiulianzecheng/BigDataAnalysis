 '''Lugano''' ([[Latin language]]: ''Luganum'') is a [[city]] of the district [[Lugano (district)|Lugano]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Ticino (canton)|Ticino]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 == History == 
 Lugano has existed as a town since at least [[984]]. The territory of Lugano was fought over by [[Milan]] and [[Como]] throughout the [[Middle Ages]]. The city joined [[Switzerland]] in [[1513]]. In [[1956]], Lugano hosted the first ever [[Eurovision Song Contest]]. 
 
 == Municipalities == 
 Since 2004 the former municipalities of [[Breganzona]], [[Cureggia]], [[Davesco-Soragno]], [[Gandria]], [[Pambio-Noranco]], [[Pazzallo]], [[Pregassona]] and [[Viganello]] were incorporated into the city. In 2008, they were followed by [[Barbengo]], [[Carabbia]] and [[Villa Luganese]]. < ref name=Fusion > {{cite web | url = http://www.bfs.admin.ch/bfs/portal/de/index/infothek/nomenklaturen/blank/blank/gem_liste/03.html | title = Amtliches Gemeindeverzeichnis der Schweiz |trans-title=Official community register of Switzerland | publisher = Swiss Federal Statistical Office | language = German | accessdate = 14 January 2010}} < /ref >  In 2013 the former municipalities of [[Bogno]], [[Cadro]], [[Carona, Switzerland|Carona]], [[Certara]], [[Cimadera]], [[Sonvico]] and [[Valcolla]] were incorporated into the city.
