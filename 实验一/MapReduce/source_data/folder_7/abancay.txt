 '''Abancay''' is a city in [[Peru]]. It is the capital of [[Apurímac Region]]. 
 
 ==Location== 
 Abancay is located at an elevation of {{convert|2377|m|ft}} above sea level in the southern [[Andes|Peruvian Andes]], above the [[Pachachaca River]], and straddles the [[Marino River]]. < ref name=eb/ >
