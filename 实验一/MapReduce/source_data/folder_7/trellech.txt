 '''Trellech''' (sometimes spelt '''Trelech''', '''Treleck''' or '''Trelleck'''; {{lang-cy|Tryleg}}) is a village and parish in [[Monmouthshire]], south-east Wales. It is {{convert|5|mi|0}} south of [[Monmouth]] and {{convert|4|mi}} north-north-west of [[Tintern]]. 
 
 In the 13th century, it was one of the largest towns in Wales. It is a site of archaeological interest.
