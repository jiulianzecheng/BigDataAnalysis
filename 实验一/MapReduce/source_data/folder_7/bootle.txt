 == Transport == 
 
 === Road === 
 The A565, A5057 and A5058 roads pass through Bootle. 
 
 === Rail === 
 Bootle has two train stations: Bootle Oriel Road < ref > {{Cite web|title=National Rail Enquiries - Station facilities for Bootle Oriel Road|url=https://www.nationalrail.co.uk/stations/BOT/details.html|access-date=2021-01-13|website=www.nationalrail.co.uk}} < /ref >  and Bootle New Strand. < ref > {{Cite web|title=National Rail Enquiries - Station facilities for Bootle New Strand|url=https://www.nationalrail.co.uk/stations/BNW/details.html|access-date=2021-01-13|website=www.nationalrail.co.uk}} < /ref > 
 
 === Ferry === 
 There are ferries available to Dublin from Bootle. However, these are freight only.
