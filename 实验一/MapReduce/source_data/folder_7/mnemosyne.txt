'''Mnemosyne''' is the [[Titan (mythology)|Titan]] of [[memory]] in [[Greek mythology]]. Her parents are [[Gaia (mythology)|Gaia]] and [[Uranos]]. With [[Zeus]] her children are the nine [[Muses]]. Her patron symbol is a mask and she is often seen wearing one. Her patron animal is the partridge.
