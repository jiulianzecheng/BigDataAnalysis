 '''Staind''' is an [[United States|American]] [[rock and roll|rock]] [[band]] from [[Massachusetts]], [[United States]]. The band was started in 1994. Its first [[album]], ''Tormented'' was released in 1996. Staind's newest album, ''The Illusion of Progress'' was released on August 19, 2008. Some of its biggest hits have been ''Outside'', ''[[It's Been Awhile]]'', and ''So Far Away''. 
 
 In 2013, They announced that they were going on hiatus but were not breaking up. < ref name= " Billboard "  / >
