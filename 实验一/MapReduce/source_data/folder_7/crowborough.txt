 '''Crowborough''' is a town in [[Wealden]], [[East Sussex]], [[England]]. In 2001 there were 19,939 people living in Crowborough. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.ons.gov.uk/help/localstatistics|title=Local statistics - Office for National Statistics|website=www.ons.gov.uk}} < /ref >  It is on the [[Weald]] and at the edge of [[Ashdown Forest]].
