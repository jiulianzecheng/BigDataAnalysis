A '''cyclohexene''' is a six-sided ([[hexagon]]al) shaped [[hydrocarbon]] ring made when [[carbon]] [[atom]]s join with [[hydrogen]] atoms. < ref > {{cite web |url= http://www.krysstal.com/organic.html |title= An Introduction to Organic Chemistry |first=Kyrss|last=Tal |work=krysstal.com |year=2005 |access-date=30 July 2011}} < /ref >  It is [[flammable]], [[toxic]], and colourless liquid which does not dissolve in water. < ref > {{cite web |url= http://www.credoreference.com/entry/apdst/cyclohexene |title=Cyclohexene |work=Academic Press Dictionary of Science and Technology |year=1992 |access-date=30 July 2011}} < /ref >  It will, however, dissolve in [[alcohol]].
