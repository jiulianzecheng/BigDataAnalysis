 '''Mariehamm''' ({{lang-fi|Maarianhamina}}) is the capital city of the [[Åland Islands]], an [[autonomous region]] in [[Finland]]. As of January 2014, more than 11,400 people lived in Mariehamm. Many ships sail between Mariehamn and the port of [[Turku]].
