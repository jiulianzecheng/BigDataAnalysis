'''Soteira''' ({{lang-el|Σώτειρα}}, male '''Soter''', {{lang|el|Σωτήρ}}) is an [[epithet]] that was added to the names of [[List of Greek gods and goddesses|gods and goddesses]] in [[Greek mythology]]. It means  " [[Saviour]] " . It expresses the fact that the speaker expects the deity to [[salvation|save them]] and cleanse them of [[evil]]. It has been used with [[Artemis]], [[Persephone]], and [[Athena]]. < ref > {{cite book | url=http://quod.lib.umich.edu/m/moa/ACL3129.0003.001/896?rgn=full+text;view=image;q1=Soteira | title=A Dictionary of Greek and Roman biography and mythology | accessdate= 22 July 2013 | editor = William Smith}} < /ref >
