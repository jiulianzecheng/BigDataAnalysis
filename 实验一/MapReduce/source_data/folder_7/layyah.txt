'''Layyah''' is a city in the [[Punjab (Pakistan)|Punjab]] province of [[Pakistan]] and is the capital of [[Layyah District]]. Layyah gets its name from the wild short [[shrub]] commonly known as [[Layyan]]. Layyah is located between the [[river]]s [[River Indus|Indus]] and [[River Chenab|Chenab]] in central Pakistan.
