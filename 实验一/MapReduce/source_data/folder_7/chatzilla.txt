 '''ChatZilla''' is an [[IRC]] client made by [[Mozilla]] that is free of charge. It is programmed in [[JavaScript]] and [[XUL]]. Its first public release date was in [[2001]]. The latest stable version (0.9.86) was made in [[2009]]. It is licensed under [[Mozilla Public License|MPL]], [[GNU General Public License|GPL]], and [[GNU Lesser General Public License|LGPL]].
