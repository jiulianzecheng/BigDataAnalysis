 '''''Spiderland''''' is the second [[Album|studio album]] by [[Slint]]. Slint was an American [[Rock music|rock]] band. Touch and Go Records released it on March 27, 1991. The band recorded the album in four days in August 1990. When Slint recorded the album, the members of the band were Brian McMahan (singing and [[guitar]]), David Pajo (guitar), Todd Brashear ([[bass guitar]]) and Britt Walford (drums). ''Spiderland'' was [[Audio engineer|engineered]] by Brian Paulson. 
 
 The band ended before the album was released.
