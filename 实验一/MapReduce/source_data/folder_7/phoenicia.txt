 '''Phoenicia''' was a [[civilization]] to the north of [[Canaan]]. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://phoenicia.org/syria.html|title=What was Historical Syria and Phoenicia? Is there confusion?|work=phoenicia.org|accessdate=3 May 2015}} < /ref >  that existed from 1200 to 900 [[BC]]. People from Phoenicia had their own language, [[Phoenician language|Phoenician]], which is the origin of most of the world's [[Alphabet|alphabets]]. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://phoenicia.org/alphabet.html|title=Phoenician Alphabet|work=phoenicia.org|accessdate=3 May 2015}} < /ref >
