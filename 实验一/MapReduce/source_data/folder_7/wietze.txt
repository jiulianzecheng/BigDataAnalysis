 '''Wietze''' is a municipality in [[Celle (district)|Celle]], in [[Lower Saxony]], [[Germany]]. It is at the meeting of the river [[Aller (Germany)|Aller]] and its tributary [[Wietze (Aller)|Wietze]], about 15 & nbsp;km west of [[Celle]].
