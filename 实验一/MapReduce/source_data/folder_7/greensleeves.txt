'''Greensleeves''' is a song from the 1500s.  People say King [[Henry VIII]] wrote it about [[Anne Boleyn]], but that is probably not true. < ref name=BI > {{cite news|publisher=Business Insider|access-date=April 16, 2021|date=December 25, 2017|author=Aine Cain|title=One of the most famous Christmas carols was originally a salacious pop hit|url=https://www.businessinsider.com/christmas-carol-what-child-is-this-greensleeves-history-2017-12}} < /ref >  Greensleeves is in [[minor key]].  Its verses have a (CESH) pattern, and it has a major key chorus. Ending traditionally Tierce de Picardie. 
 
 The [[Christmas song]]  " What Child Is This? "  is sung to the same music as  " Greensleeves "  but the words are different. < ref name=BI / >
