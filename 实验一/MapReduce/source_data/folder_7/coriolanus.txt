 '''''The Tragedy of Coriolanus''''' is a [[play (theatre)|play]] by [[William Shakespeare]].  It is a [[tragedy]]. Shakespeare's source was [[Plutarch]]'s ''Life of Caius Martius Coriolanus''. It was probably was first acted between 1607 and 1608. It was probably first printed in 1623 in the [[First Folio]]. < ref > [http://www.william-shakespeare.info/shakespeare-play-coriolanus.htm ''Coriolanus''] < /ref >
