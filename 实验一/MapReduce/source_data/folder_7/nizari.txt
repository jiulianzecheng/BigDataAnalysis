The '''Nizari''' are the third biggest group of [[Shi'a Islam|Shiite]] [[Ismailism|Ismailite]]s. They emphasize [[social justice]], [[Pluralism (political philosophy)|pluralism]] and human [[reason]] in the framework of Islam. They are the second-largest group of Shiites. There are about 15-20 million, spread over many countries; in [[Pakistan]] up to 05% of the people may follow this faith. Nizari still have an [[imam]] that is descended from Ali, the current imam is [[Karim Aga Khan IV]].
