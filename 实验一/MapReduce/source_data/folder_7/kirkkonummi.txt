 '''Kirkkonummi''' ({{lang-sv|Kyrksl√§tt}}) is a [[municipality]] in [[Uusimaa]], [[Finland]]. As of January 2014, almost 38,000 people lived in Kirkkonummi. It has an [[area]] of {{convert|393.3|km2}}.  
 
 Most people speak [[Finnish language|Finnish]], almost 80%. 
 
 Kirkkonummi is close to the capital city, [[Helsinki]]. Other neighbouring municipalies are [[Espoo]], [[Vihti]] and [[Siuntio]]. 
 
 Kirkkonummi was established in 1330.
