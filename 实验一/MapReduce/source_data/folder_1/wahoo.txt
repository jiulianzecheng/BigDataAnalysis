 '''Wahoo''' (''Acanthocybium solandri'') is a [[Scombridae|scombrid]] fish found worldwide in [[Tropics|tropical]] and [[Subtropics|subtropical]] seas. It is best known to sports [[Fisherman|fishermen]], as its speed and high-quality [[flesh]] makes it a prized and valued [[game fish]]. In [[Hawaii]], the wahoo is known as '''''ono'''.'' The species is sometimes called '''''hoo''''' in the [[United States]]. 
 
 Wahoo is also a term for excitement.
