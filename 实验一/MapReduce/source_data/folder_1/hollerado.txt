 '''Hollerado''' is a [[Canada|Canadian]] [[indie rock]] [[band]] from [[Manotick, Ontario|Manotick]], [[Ontario]]. Hollerado has four members in the band; the current band members are Dean Baxter, Jake Boyd, Nixon Boyd and Menno Versteeg. Their name is a parody of the United States state, [[Colorado]]. 
 
 == Band members == 
 ;Current 
 *Dean Baxter - [[bass guitar]]  &  vocals 
 *Jake Boyd - [[drum]]s  &  vocals 
 *Nixon Boyd - [[guitar]]  &  vocals 
 *Menno Versteeg - guitar  &  lead vocals
