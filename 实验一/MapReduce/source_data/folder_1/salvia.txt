 '''''Salvia''''' is a [[genus]] in the mint family, [[Lamiaceae]]. It is one of three [[genus|genera]] commonly referred to as '''Sage'''. Sage generally means [[common sage]] (''Salvia officinalis''). This genus includes [[shrub]]s, [[herbs|herbaceous]] [[perennial plant|perennials]], and [[annual plant|annuals]]. Different species of sage are grown as herbs and as [[ornamental plant]]s. The ornamental species are commonly referred to by their scientific name ''Salvia''.
