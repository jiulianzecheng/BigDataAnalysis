 '''Peldon''' is a village and [[civil parish]] in [[Colchester borough]], [[Essex]], England. In 2001 there were 501 people living in Peldon. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.ons.gov.uk/help/localstatistics|title=Local statistics - Office for National Statistics|website=www.ons.gov.uk}} < /ref >  Peldon has a church called St Mary the Virgin. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.essexchurches.info/church.aspx?p=Peldon|title=St Mary the Virgin, Peldon Church, Essex|website=www.essexchurches.info}} < /ref >
