'''Hastings''' is a town on the coast of [[East Sussex]] in [[England]]. It is the administrative centre for the [[borough]] of the same name. 
 
 {{coord|50.85971044158446|0.5723190307617187|display=title}}Hastings gave its name to the [[Battle of Hastings]] in [[1066]].
