 '''Courroux''' is a [[municipality]] in the [[district]] of [[Delémont (district)|Delémont]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Jura (canton)|Jura]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 It is three [[kilometer]]s southeast of the city of Delémont. It is the third largest municipality in the canton of Jura by area.
