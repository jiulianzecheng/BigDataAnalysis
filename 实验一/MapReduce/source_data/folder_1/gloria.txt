The name '''Gloria''' is a [[female]] [[given name]] which is very common in the [[English language]]. The pronunciation of the name is [ '''glawr'''-ee-''uh'', '''glohr'''- ]. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Definition of Gloria {{!}} Dictionary.com|url=https://www.dictionary.com/browse/gloria|access-date=2023-03-22|website=www.dictionary.com|language=en}} < /ref >  The name means  " glory "  or  " fame "  in [[Latin language|Latin]]. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Gloria name Meaning and Origin|url=https://babys.name/detail-Gloria-Meaning-Origin-Famous-Similar-Sibling.html|access-date=2023-03-22|website=babys.name}} < /ref >  This name was ranked #572 on the US Popular Names in 2021. There are several notable [[movie]]s under the name ''Gloria''. Notable celebrities under the name include [[Gloria Gaynor]], [[Gloria Reuben]], [[Gloria Vanderbilt]] and the singer [[Gloria Estefan]]. The name was used on a [[Hurricane Gloria|hurricane]] that struck the East Coast of the [[United States]] in September 1985.
