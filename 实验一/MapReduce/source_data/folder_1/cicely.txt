 '''Cicely''' (''Myrrhis odorata''), also called '''sweet cicely''', is a [[plant]] in the family [[Apiaceae]]. It is a tall [[perennial plant]], growing to 2 meters tall. The [[leaf|leaves]] are divided. The [[flower]]s are [[white]]. The [[seed]]s are [[wikt:slender|slender]]. 
 
 == Uses == 
 Cicely [[leaves]] are sometimes used as a [[herb]], with a strong [[taste]] of [[anise]].
