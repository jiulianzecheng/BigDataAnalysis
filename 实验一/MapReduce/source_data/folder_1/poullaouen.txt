 '''Poullaouen''' ({{lang-br|Poullaouen}}) is a [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is found in the [[Regions of France|region]] [[Brittany]] in the [[Finistère]] [[Departments of France|department]] in the northwest of [[France]].
