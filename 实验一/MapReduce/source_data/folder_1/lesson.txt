A '''lesson''' in [[school]] is a planned period of time for learning. It involves one or more [[student]]s being taught by a [[teacher]]. A ''lesson'' may be either one section of a [[textbook]] or a  period of time during which learners are taught about a subject or how to perform an activity. ''Lessons'' are mostly taught in a [[classroom]] but they may take place [[Online and offline|online]], or outside.  Not all lessons are for children. 
 [[File:Falconry lesson.jpg|thumb|[[Falconry]] lesson]] 
 
 A lesson  means someone learning something he did not know before. Such a ''lesson'' can be either planned or accidental, enjoyable or painful. The slang phrase  " to teach someone a ''lesson'' " , means to [[punish]] or [[scold]] a person for a [[mistake]] they have made making sure that he does not make the same mistake again. 
 
 Lessons can also be made [[entertainment|entertaining]]. When the term [[education]] is combined with [[entertainment]], it is called  " [[edutainment]] " .
