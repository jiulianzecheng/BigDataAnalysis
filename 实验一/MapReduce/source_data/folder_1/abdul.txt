'''Abdul''' is a well-liked name among [[Arabic language|Arabic]]-speaking communities, typically given to boys. Its origin comes from two Arabic words:  " abd, "  which translates to  " servant "  or  " slave, "  and  " al, "  which means  " the. "  As a result, Abdul signifies  " servant of the "  followed by one of the 99 names of Allah, as per [[Islam|Islamic]] tradition. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Abdul name Meaning and Origin|url=https://babys.name/detail-Abdul-Meaning-Origin-Famous-Similar-Sibling.html|access-date=2023-04-11|website=babys.name}} < /ref >
