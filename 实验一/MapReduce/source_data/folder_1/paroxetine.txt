'''Paroxetine,''' also known as '''Paxil,''' is a [[medicine]] used for the treatment of [[Depression (mental illness)|depression]]. It belongs to a class of drugs called [[Selective serotonin reuptake inhibitor|selective serotonin reuptake inhibitors]] (SSRIs). It has many side effects including possible [[insomnia]] and tremors. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.drugs.com/paxil.html|title=Paxil|publisher=Drugs.com|accessdate=20 November 2011}} < /ref >
