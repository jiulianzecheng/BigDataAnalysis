 '''Geisenhausen''' is a municipality with [[market town]] status in [[Landshut (district)|Landshut]], in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. It is 14 & nbsp;km southeast of [[Landshut]] in the valley of the [[Kleine Vils]].
