'''Cherreau''' is a former [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is found in the [[Regions of France|region]] [[Pays de la Loire]] in the [[Sarthe]] [[Departments of France|department]] in the west of [[France]]. On 1 January 2019, it was merged into the new commune of [[Cherré-Au]]. < ref > [https://www.legifrance.gouv.fr/eli/arrete/2018/9/13/INTB1826516A/jo/texte Arrêté préfectoral] 13 September 2018 {{in lang|fr}} < /ref >
