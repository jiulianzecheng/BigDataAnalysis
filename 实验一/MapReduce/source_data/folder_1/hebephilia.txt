 Hebephilia is not a disorder in the [[Diagnostic and Statistical Manual of Mental Disorders]], Fifth Edition. It was a hotly contested debate when the recent edition was created. Some research suggests that hebephilia can be distinguished and diagnosed, and that it occurs more frequently than [[pedophilia]].
