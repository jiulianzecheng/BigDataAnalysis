 '''Sulu''' ({{IPA-tl|sʊˈlu|}}; [[Tausug language|Tausūg]]: ''Wilāya sin Lupa' Sūg''; {{lang-tl|Lalawigan ng Sulu}}) is a [[Provinces of the Philippines|province]] of the [[Philippines]] in the [[Sulu Archipelago]] and part of the [[Bangsamoro|Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao]] (BARMM).
