 The '''Sava''' ({{lang-de|Save}}; {{lang-sr-cyr|Сава}}; {{lang-hu|Száva}}) is a river in [[Central Europe|Central]] and [[Southeast Europe]], a right tributary of [[Danube]] at [[Belgrade]]. It flows through [[Slovenia]], [[Croatia]], [[Bosnia and Herzegovina]] and [[Serbia]].
