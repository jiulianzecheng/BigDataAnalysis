 '''''Yaaba''''' is a 1989 Burkinabé Swiss French family drama movie directed by [[Idrissa Ouedraogo]] and starring [[Noufou Ouédraogo]], [[Fatimata Sanga]], [[Roukietou Barry]]. It was distributed by [[New Yorker Films]].
