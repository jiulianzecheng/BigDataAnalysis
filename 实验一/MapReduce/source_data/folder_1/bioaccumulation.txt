'''Bioaccumulation''' is how [[toxic]] substances (such as [[pesticide]]s) build up in living organisms. This is bad for health, life, and the [[environment]].  
 
 Similar terms are bioconcentration, biological concentration, or biological magnification. < ref > Alexander D.E.  1999. Bioaccumulation, bioconcentration, biomagnification. In Environmental Geology: ''Encyclopedia of Earth Science''. Dordrecht: Springer-Verlag. < /ref >
