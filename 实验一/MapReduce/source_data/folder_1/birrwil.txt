 '''Birrwil''' is a [[municipality]] in [[Kulm (district)|Kulm]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Aargau]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 It is on the western shore of [[Lake Hallwil]].
