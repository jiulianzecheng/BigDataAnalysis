 Breakcore is an [[Electronic music|electronic]] [[Musical genre|music genre]]. It is made of complex rhythms (called [[Breakbeat|breakbeats]]) and it is usually played quick. The drums are often made from the Amen Break (an unique rhythm) or other breaks.
