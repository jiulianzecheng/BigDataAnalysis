 '''Kaiserslautern''' ({{IPA-de|ˌkaɪzɐsˈlaʊtɐn|-|de-Kaiserslautern2.ogg}}; {{lang-pfl|Lautre}}) is a city in the southwest of [[Germany]], in the [[States of Germany|state]] of [[Rhineland-Palatinate]]. It has about 100,000 [[inhabitant]]s, a [[university]] and a well-known soccer team.
