 '''Rakhiv''' ({{lang-uk|Рахів}}; {{lang-rue|Рахово|Rachowo}}; {{lang-ru|Рахов|Rakhov}}; {{lang-hu|Rahó}}; {{lang-ro|Rahău}}; {{lang-sk|Rachov}}; {{lang-de|Rauhau}}; {{lang-pl|Rachów}}; {{lang-yi|ראחוב|Rakhev}}  < small > or < /small >  ''Rakhyv'') is a city in the [[Zakarpattia Oblast]] ([[Oblast|province]]) in western [[Ukraine]]. It is the [[administrative centre]] of [[Rakhiv Raion]] ([[Raion|district]]). In 2020, 15,601 people lived there.
