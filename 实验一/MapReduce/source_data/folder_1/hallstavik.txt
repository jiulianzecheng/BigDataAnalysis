 '''Hallstavik''' is a [[Urban areas in Sweden|locality]] in [[Norrtälje Municipality]] in [[Stockholm County]] in [[Sweden]]. In 2010, 4,476 people lived there. < ref name=scb / >  The locality is known for its paper-factory and the [[Rospiggarna]] [[motorcycle speedway|speedway team]] .
