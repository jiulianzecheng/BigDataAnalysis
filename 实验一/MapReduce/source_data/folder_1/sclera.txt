 The '''sclera''' is the white part of the [[eye]]. Unlike most [[Animal|animals’]] eyes, the [[human eye|human eye’s]] sclera is pale. This makes it easier for us to see where other people are looking. For most [[mammal|mammals]], the sclera is the same [[color]] as the [[iris]].
