 '''Lunatic''' is an old term that was used to refer to a person with a [[mental illness]], or a [[Intellectual disability|mental disability]]. The term goes back to the Latin word lunaticus, which means ' of the [[moon]]', or moonstruck. It was also used in English law until about 1930, when it was  replaced by  " person of unsound mind " . Starting 1959, English law speaks about  " mental illness " .
