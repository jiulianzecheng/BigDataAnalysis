'''Ailuromancy''' or '''aeluromancy''' (from Greek αἴλουρος aílouros meaning  " [[cat]] " ), also known as '''felidomancy''', is a form of [[theriomancy]]. It is divination using cats' movements to [[predict]] future events, especially the [[weather]].
