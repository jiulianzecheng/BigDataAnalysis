'''Nanobiotix''' is a [[biotechnology]] company. It uses nanomedicine to develop new radiotherapy techniques for cancer patients. The [[Home Office|home office]] of the company is in [[Paris]]. Also, it has offices in [[New York]] and [[Massachusetts]]. 
 
 Nanobiotix is a spin-off of the [[State University of New York]] (SUNY) at Buffalo.
