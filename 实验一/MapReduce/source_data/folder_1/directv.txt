 '''DirecTV''' (stylized as '''DIRECTV''') is an [[United States|American]] [[satellite television]] service provider. The company is based in [[California]]. It is part of [[AT & T]]. < ref > {{cite web|title=AT & T Completes Acquisition of DirecTV|publisher=AT & T|url=http://about.att.com/story/att_completes_acquisition_of_directv.html|access-date=Feb 13, 2018}} < /ref > 
 
 Its satellite service began on June 17, 1994. The service provides digital satellite television to households in the United States, [[Latin America]] and the [[Caribbean]] Islands. < ref > {{cite web|title=DirecTV Service in Caribbean|url=http://www.directvcaribbean.com/location/backto=/index|access-date=Feb 13, 2018}} < /ref > 
 
 DirecTV also has [[internet television]] service. 
 
 == Customer Service ==  
 In 2007, DirecTV was awarded a J.D. Power award for cable and satellite TV satisfaction. 
 
 The customer service of DirecTV is provided by many third-party owned call centers both inside and outside the U.S. 
 
 ===Review from Better Business Bureau=== 
 In August 2008, ''The Boston Globe'' reported that DirecTV had been receiving as many as 20,000 complaints in almost three years to [[Better Business Bureau]] about DirecTV slipping on cancellation fees. < ref > {{cite report|title=DirecTV Says its Cancellation Fee is Fair|publisher=The Boston Globe|url=http://archive.boston.com/business/articles/2008/08/31/directv_says_its_cancellation_fee_is_fair_since_installation_is_free|access-date=Feb 13, 2018}} < /ref > 
 
 In August 2010, Better Business Bureau reported that it had gotten over 39,000 complaints in the previous three years. The BBB gave DirecTV an F (failing) grade. Many of these complaints concerned the fees for early termination, billing and service issues. < ref > {{cite web|title=DirecTV Customer Service: Fail|publisher=Internet Archive Wayback Machine|url=http://www.bbb.org/us/post/directv-customer-service-fail-5588|access-date=Feb 13, 2018|archive-date=April 25, 2013|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130425234855/http://www.bbb.org/us/post/directv-customer-service-fail-5588|url-status=bot: unknown}} < /ref >
