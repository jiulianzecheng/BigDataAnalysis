'''Tehkummah''' is a [[Township (Canada)|township]] in the [[Canada|Canadian]] province of [[Ontario]]. It is on [[Manitoulin Island]]. At the [[Canada 2011 Census|2011 census]], 406 people lived there. 
 
 The township was incorporated in 1881. It has an area of {{convert|132.48|km2|abbr=off}}. There are several communities in the township, including South Baymouth, Tehkummah, and Snowville.
