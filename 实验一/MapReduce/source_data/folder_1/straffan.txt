 '''Straffan''' ({{lang-ga|Teach Srafáin}}) is a village in [[County Kildare]], [[Republic of Ireland|Ireland]] on the banks of the [[River Liffey]], 25 & nbsp;km upstream of the Irish capital [[Dublin]].  
 
 With a [[population]] (according to the [[census]] of [[2006]]) of 439 – an increase since [[2002]] of 32.2% – Straffan is the 31st most populous centre in County Kildare.
