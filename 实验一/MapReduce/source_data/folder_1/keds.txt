'''Keds''' is an [[United States|American]] brand of canvas shoe. It has [[rubber]] soles.  It was established around 1916. The shoes became very popular from the 1960s to the 1980s. They waned in popularity in the 1990s. They became very popular again in the 2000s and 2010s. Keds usually manufactures [[sneakers]]. The shoes were worn by participants of [[Kids Incorporated]] and on other television shows.
