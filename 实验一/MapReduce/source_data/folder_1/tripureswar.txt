 '''Tripureshwar''' is a [[village]] in [[Dhading District]] in the [[Bagmati Zone]] of central [[Nepal]].  
 
 [[File:Nathravathi River in Manglore 001.jpg |left |thumb |190px |[[Netravati River]] in [[Mangalore]] ]] 
 
 At the time of the 1991 Nepal [[census]] it had a [[population]] of 2707 and had 497 [[house]]s in it.
