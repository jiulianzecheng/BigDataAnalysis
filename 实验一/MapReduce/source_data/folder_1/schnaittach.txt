 '''Schnaittach''' is a market town in [[Middle Franconia]], [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 
 Schnaittach is on the river [[Schnaittach (Pegnitz)|Schnaittach]], a tributary of the [[Pegnitz (river)|Pegnitz]].
