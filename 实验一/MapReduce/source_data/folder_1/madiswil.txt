 '''Madiswil''' is a [[municipality]] in the [[administrative district]] of [[Oberaargau (administrative district)|Oberaargau]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Bern (canton)|Bern]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 Since January 1, 2007, [[Gutenburg, Switzerland|Gutenburg]] is part of the municipality. On 1 January 2011, [[Kleindietwil]] and [[Leimiswil]] were merged with the municipality of Madiswil. < ref name=Mutation > [http://www.bfs.admin.ch/bfs/portal/de/index/infothek/nomenklaturen/blank/blank/gem_liste/02.html Nomenklaturen – Amtliches Gemeindeverzeichnis der Schweiz] {{in lang|de}} accessed 4 April 2011 < /ref >
