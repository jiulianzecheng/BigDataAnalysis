 '''Juan Esteban Aristizábal Vásquez''' (born 9 August 1972), known as '''Juanes''', is a one-time [[Grammy Award]]-winning and nineteen-time [[Latin Grammy Award]]-winning [[Colombia]]n [[singer]], [[songwriter]], [[musical instrument|instrumentalist]], [[record producer]]. He was a member of [[Heavy metal music|heavy metal]] band [[Ekhymosis]] and is now a solo artist. Juanes has sold more than 15 million albums worldwide. < ref > {{cite web|url = http://www.voanews.com/spanish/news/entertainment/juanes-mtv-unplugged-135433933.html|title = Juanes ha vendido 15 millones de copias|access-date = 2012-06-03|archive-date = 2012-02-13|archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20120213025806/http://www.voanews.com/spanish/news/entertainment/juanes-mtv-unplugged-135433933.html|url-status = dead}} < /ref > < ref > {{cite web |archiveurl= https://web.archive.org/web/20110714123716/http://noticias.latino.msn.com/internacionales/articulos.aspx?cp-documentid=26144296 |title=Juanes inicia con 'PARCE' un giro hacia un tipo de pop-rock 'más básico' -  Noticias internacionales en MSN Latino |work=msn.com |accessdate=8 December 2011|url=http://noticias.latino.msn.com/internacionales/articulos.aspx?cp-documentid=26144296|archivedate=2011-07-14}} < /ref > 
 
 Raised in [[Medellín]], [[Colombia]], Juanes began playing guitar at age seven.
