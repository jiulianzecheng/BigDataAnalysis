 '''''Dysalotosaurus''''' was a small plant-eating [[ornithopod]] [[dinosaur]] that lived in forests around 150 [[million]] [[year]]s ago.  
 
 ==Classification== 
 ''Dysalotosaurus'' is a member of the family, the [[Dryosauridae]], which were precursors (ancestors) of the [[Iguanodon]]s, and lived from the [[Middle Jurassic]] to the [[Lower Cretaceous]]. < ref >  Tom R.
