 ''' Chenneerkara ''' is a village in [[Pathanamthitta district]] in the state of [[Kerala]], [[India]].  < ref > [https://web.archive.org/web/20090410023822/http://pathanamthitta.nic.in/Administration1.htm] 
 < /ref > 
 
 ==Demographics== 
 
 As of the 2011 India census, Chenneerkara had a population of 19124 people, with 8882 males, and 10242 females.
