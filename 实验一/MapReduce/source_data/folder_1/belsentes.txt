 '''Belsentes''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]] of the [[Ardèche]] [[Departments of France|department]], in the southern part of [[France]]. The municipality was created on 1 January 2019 and consists of the former communes of [[Nonières]] (the seat) and [[Saint-Julien-Labrousse]]. < ref > [https://www.legifrance.gouv.fr/eli/arrete/2018/12/12/TERB1900506A/jo/texte Arrêté préfectoral] 12 December 2018 {{in lang|fr}} < /ref >
