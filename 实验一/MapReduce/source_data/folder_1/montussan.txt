 '''Montussan''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is found in the [[Regions of France|region]] [[Aquitaine]] in the [[Gironde]] [[Departments of France|department]] in the southwest of [[France]]. 
 
 Montussan is predominantly a rural landscape, with 52% cultivated land and 9% woods and meadows. It is renowed of its castles in the heart of the estates.
