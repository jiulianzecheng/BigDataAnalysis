 '''Defoliants''' are a type of [[chemical]] that destroys [[plant]]s like [[tree]]s, [[shrub|bushes]] and [[grass]] . 
 
 A very common defoliant is [[Agent Orange]] used by the US Airforce in the [[Vietnam War]] to destroy cover of the enemy. It killed the [[rainforest]] and caused [[birth defect]]s as well as other illnesses among Vietnamese and US soldiers < ref > [http://news.bbc.co.uk/2/hi/health/3798581.stm Tom Fawthrop. Vietnam's war against Agent Orange] < /ref >
