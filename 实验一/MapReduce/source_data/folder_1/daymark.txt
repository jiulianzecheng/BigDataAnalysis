 A '''daymark''' is a sign used to help people [[Navigation|navigate]] in [[Lake|lakes]], [[River|rivers]], or the [[ocean]]. It is fixed, but does not meet the criteria of a [[lighthouse]]. Very often, it is there for orientation, or to regulate traffic.
