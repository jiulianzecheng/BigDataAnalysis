 '''Simplon''' is a [[municipality]] of the [[district]] of [[Brig (district)|Brig]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Valais]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 A member of the [[Swiss Federal Council]] [[Josef Escher]] was born in Simplon.
