 '''DZBB''' (pronounced DZ-double-B; 594 [[AM broadcasting|AM]]) '''Super Radyo''' is a radio station owned and operated by [[GMA Network (company)|GMA Network]]. It serves as the flagship station of the Super Radyo network and one of the assets of [[List of GMA Network radio stations|GMA Radio]] and [[GMA Integrated News and Public Affairs|GMA Integrated News]]. The station's studio is located at the 3rd floor of [[GMA Network Studio Annex]], [[EDSA]] corner GMA Network Drive, Diliman, [[Quezon City]], while its transmitter is located along Camia St., Brgy. Panghulo, [[Obando, Bulacan]].
