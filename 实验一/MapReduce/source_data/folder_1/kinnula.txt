 '''Kinnula''' is a [[municipality]] in [[Central Finland]]. Almost 1,780 people lived there in January 2014. The municipalities next to it are [[Kivijärvi]], [[Lestijärvi]], [[Perho]], [[Pihtipudas]], [[Reisjärvi]] and [[Viitasaari]].
