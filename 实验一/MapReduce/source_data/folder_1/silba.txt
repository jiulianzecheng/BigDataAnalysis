It is found between the islands of [[Premuda]] and [[Olib]]. It has an [[area]] of 15 & nbsp;km² and its [[population]] (as of [[2001]]) was 265; with the village of Silba located at the narrowest point in the middle. The main administrative centre is [[Zadar]], found on the [[mainland]].
