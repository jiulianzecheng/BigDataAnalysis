 '''''Homogenic''''' is an album by [[Björk]].  It was released in 1997. Music critic Sal Cinquemani said that it is the best electronic music album of the 1990s. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.slantmagazine.com/music/bjork-homogenic/|title=Review: Björk, Homogenic|website=[[Slant Magazine]] |date=6 May 2007|publisher=}} < /ref >  Pitchfork gave it 9.9 out of 10. < ref > https://www.webcitation.org/5g3suOZf7 < /ref >  Five singles were released from the album. It was nominated in the Best Alternative Music Performance at the 1998 Grammy Awards.
