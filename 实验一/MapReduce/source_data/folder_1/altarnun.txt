 '''Altarnun''' is a village and parish in [[Cornwall]], [[United Kingdom]], on the north-eastern edge of [[Bodmin Moor]] < ref > Ordnance Survey: Landranger map sheet 201 ''Plymouth  &  Launceston'' {{ISBN|978-0-319-23146-3}} < /ref >  at {{gbmapping|SX 223 811}}. 
 
 The parish of Altarnun includes the village of '''Fivelanes''' and the hamlets of Bolventor, Treween and Trewint, and had a population of 976 according to the 2001 census. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.bodminmoor.co.uk/altarnun.html|title=Altarnun|access-date=2009-06-22}} < /ref >  The moorland area of the parish is large and lies west of the village towards Rough Tor and southwards towards Dozmary Pool. The village is in the valley of the Penpont Water and the parish is divided by the [[A30 road|A30]] trunk road which passes through Fivelanes.
