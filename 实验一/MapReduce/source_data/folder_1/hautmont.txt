 '''Hautmont''' is a [[Communes in France|commune]] in [[Nord (department)|Nord]] in north [[France]]. In 2019, 14,517 people lived there. 
 
 On 3 August 2008, a strong F4 [[tornado]] swept through the town. The tornado killed 3 people and injured 17 others.
