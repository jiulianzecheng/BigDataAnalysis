'''Thirunageswaram''' is a panchayat town in [[Kumbakonam Taluk|Kumbakonam taluk]], [[Thanjavur district]], [[Tamil Nadu]]. It is a famous for its two temples namely the [[Nagannatha Swami Temple]] < ref > {{cite web|url=http://templesoftamilnadu.co.in/thirunagaswaram-rahu-sthalam/|title=Thirunageswaram, Rahu Temple|first=K.|last=Narayanan|date=29 January 2010|website=Temples of Tamilnadu|access-date=20 May 2017|archive-date=18 May 2017|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170518053748/http://templesoftamilnadu.co.in/thirunagaswaram-rahu-sthalam/|url-status=dead}} < /ref >  and the Vaishnavite [[Oppiliappan Temple]]. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.oppiliappanswamytemple.tnhrce.in/|title=Official Website of Arulmigu Oppiliappan Temple|website=www.oppiliappanswamytemple.tnhrce.in|access-date=2017-05-20|archive-date=2017-05-19|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170519092140/http://www.oppiliappanswamytemple.tnhrce.in/|url-status=dead}} < /ref >
