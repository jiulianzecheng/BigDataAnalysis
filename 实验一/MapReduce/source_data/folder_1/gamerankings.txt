 '''GameRankings''' was a [[video game|gaming]] review [[website]]. The website received reviews from other publications and gave an [[average]] [[wikt:rating|rating]]. < ref name= " grhelp " > {{Cite web |url=http://www.gamerankings.com/help.html |title=GameRankings Help |access-date=2013-07-15 |archive-date=2017-10-22 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20171022214603/http://www.gamerankings.com/help.html |url-status=dead }} < /ref >  It had over 315,000 articles relating to more than 14,500 video games. 
 
 GameRankings was owned by [[CBS Interactive]]. Similar websites include [[GameStats]], [[Metacritic]], [[MobyGames]], and [[TopTenReviews]]. 
 
 == Shutdown == 
 In late 2019, GameRankings was shutting down, with every link being moved to Metacritic. < ref > {{Cite web|title=RIP Gamerankings.com|url=https://kotaku.com/rip-gamerankings-com-1840250954|access-date=2021-03-24|website=Kotaku|language=en-us}} < /ref >
