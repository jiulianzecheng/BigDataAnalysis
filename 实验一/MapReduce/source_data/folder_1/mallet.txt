 A '''mallet''' is a [[tool]] for [[woodworking]]. Usually, it is used together with a [[chisel]] and for transferring [[force]], much like hitting a [[nail]] with a [[hammer]]. Some tools used to play [[Musical instrument|musical instruments]], like [[Drum stick|drum sticks]] are also called mallets.
