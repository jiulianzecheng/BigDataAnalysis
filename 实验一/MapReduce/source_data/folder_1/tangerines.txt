 '''''Tangerines''''' ({{lang-ka|მანდარინები}} ''Mandarinebi'', {{lang-et|Mandariinid}}) is a 2013 Estonian Georgian movie directed by [[Zaza Urushadze]] and starring [[Lembit Ulfsak]], [[Giorgi Nakashidze]], [[Elmo Nüganen]], [[Mikheil Meskhi]]. It was distributed by [[Samuel Goldwyn Films]] and was nominated for an Academy Award in 2015.
