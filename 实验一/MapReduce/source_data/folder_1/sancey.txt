 '''Sancey''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is in [[Bourgogne-Franche-Comté]] in the [[Doubs]] [[Departments of France|department]] in east [[France]]. The municipality was created on 1 January 2016 and consists of the former communes of [[Sancey-le-Grand]] (the seat) and [[Sancey-le-Long]]. < ref > [http://www.doubs.gouv.fr/content/download/13828/104244/file/RAA%20N%C2%B029%20DU%2025%20SEPTEMBRE%202015.pdf Arrêté préfectoral] 23 September 2015 {{in lang|fr}} < /ref >
