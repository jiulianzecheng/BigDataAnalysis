 '''Riedenburg''' ({{IPA-de|ˈʁiːdn̩ˌbʊʁk|lang|De-Riedenburg.ogg}}) is a town in [[Kelheim (district)|Kelheim]], in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. It is on the river [[Altmühl]], {{convert|16|km|0|abbr=on}} northwest of [[Kelheim]] and {{convert|29|km|0|abbr=on}} northeast of [[Ingolstadt]]. Prunn Castle ([[:de:Burg Prunn|de]]) is in Riedenburg. 
 
 The town is on the [[Rhine–Main–Danube Canal]].
