 '''Amfissa''' is a [[Greece|Greek]] city. It is in the center of Greece, at the foot of [[Parnassos]] mountain. Near Amfissa is Delfi. According to the 2001 census, 6,947 lived there. During the Middle Ages it was named Salona, but today is named with its ancient name, Amfissa. Amfissa is an agriculturist city. The main financial source in Amfissa is [[olive oil]]. Oil-tree is the main cultivation in district of Amfissa. The most interest sight in the town is the ancient castle above town.
