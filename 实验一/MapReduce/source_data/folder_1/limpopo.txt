 '''Limpopo Province''' is one of the nine [[Provinces of South Africa|South African provinces]]. It borders three countries namely [[Zimbabwe]], [[Mozambique]] and [[Botswana]]. < ref > [https://www.bbc.com/news/world-africa-44736772   South African boy of three drowns in Limpopo toilet -] BBC News < /ref > < ref > [https://www.bbc.com/news/av/world-africa-36225502/why-are-south-africans-burning-schools-in-limpopo   Why are South Africans burning schools in Limpopo? -] BBC News < /ref >  It was started in 1994 with the name of Northern [[Transvaal]] but was renamed for the [[Limpopo River]].
