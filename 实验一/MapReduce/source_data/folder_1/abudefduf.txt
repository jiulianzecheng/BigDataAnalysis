 '''''Abudefduf''''', also known as the '''sergeant-majors''', is a [[genus]] of fish in the [[family (biology)|family]] [[Pomacentridae]]. 
   
 The name is from [[Arabic language|Arabic]] ''abu'',  " the one with " ; and ''def'',  " side " , and the plural ending ''-duf''. The name thus means  " the one with [[wikt:prominent|prominent]] sides " . < ref > {{Cite web|url=http://www.nhm.ac.uk/hosted-sites/iczn/code/index.jsp?article=30|title=International Code of Zoological Nomenclature|access-date=March 1, 2018}} < /ref >
