'''Righteousness''' is the quality of a person being morally right or [[Justification (theology)|justifiable]]. < ref > {{Cite web|url=https://en.oxforddictionaries.com/definition/righteousness|title=righteousness {{!}} Definition of righteousness in English by Oxford Dictionaries|website=Oxford Dictionaries {{!}} English|accessdate=2017-11-24|archive-date=2017-12-01|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20171201044012/https://en.oxforddictionaries.com/definition/righteousness|url-status=dead}} < /ref >  It is an important [[concept]] and [[Theology|theological]] [[concept]]. It is a concept in [[Islam]], [[Judaism]], [[Christianity]], and also in Dharmic traditions. The quality of true righteousness is [[Pleasure|pleasing]] to [[God]]. It is a concept similar to integrity. Perfect righteousness is who God is. (See [[Psalm]] 30:4)
