 The '''Archonta''' is a deprecated [[superorder]] of [[placental mammal]]s, in which are included [[bats]], [[primates]], [[colugo]]s, and [[treeshrew]]s. It was only used in linnaean classification 
 
 Archonta  consists of these orders: 
 *[[Primate]]s 
 *[[Treeshrew|Scandentia]] (treeshrews) 
 *[[Colugo|Dermoptera]] (colugos) 
 *[[Chiroptera]] (bats)
