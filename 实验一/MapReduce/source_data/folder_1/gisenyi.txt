 '''Gisenyi''' is the second largest city in [[Rwanda]]. It is in the [[Western Province, Rwanda|Western Province]]. It is on the border with the [[Democratic Republic of the Congo]]. Gisenyi is on the northern shore of [[Lake Kivu]]. It has many [[hotel]]s and three sandy beaches. The area is also known for water sports.
