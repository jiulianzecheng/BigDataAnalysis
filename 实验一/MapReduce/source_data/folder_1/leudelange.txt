 '''Leudelange''' ({{lang-lb|Leideleng}}, {{lang-de|Leudelingen}}) is a [[Communes of Luxembourg|commune]] in [[Luxembourg]]. It is part of the [[Esch-sur-Alzette (canton)|canton of Esch-sur-Alzette]]. 
 
 As of 2019, 2,668 people live here.
