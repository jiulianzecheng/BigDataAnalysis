 '''Ramadi''' ({{lang-ar|ٱلرَّمَادِي}} ''Ar-Ramādī''; also formerly rendered as ''Rumadiyah'' or ''Rumadiya'') is a [[city]] in central [[Iraq]], about {{convert|110|km|mi|sp=us}} west of [[Baghdad]] and {{convert|50|km|mi|sp=us}} west of [[Fallujah]]. 
 
 The area has been a war center between [[ISIL]] and Iraq. < ref > {{cite news|title=Iraqi Army Says It Has Retaken City Of Ramadi From ISIS|url=http://www.huffingtonpost.com/entry/iraqi-troops-close-in-on-islamic-state-stronghold-in-ramadi_567ebde7e4b0b958f6597daf|access-date=28 December 2015|agency=Reuters|publisher=Huffington Post|date=28 December 2015}} < /ref >
