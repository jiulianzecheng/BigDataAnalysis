 '''Tocopilla''' is a [[city]] and [[Communes of Chile|commune]] in the [[Antofagasta Region|Antofagasta]] [[Regions of Chile|region]] in the north of [[Chile]]. It is the [[Capital city|capital]] of the [[Provinces of Chile|province]] of [[Province of Tocopilla|Tocopilla]]. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 The commune of Tocopilla has an area of {{Convert|4038.8|km2|sqmi|abbr=on }}. < ref name= " ineDPA "  / > 
 
 The commune is bordered on the north by the [[Tarapacá Region|Tarapacá]] region, on the east by the [[María Elena]] commune, on the south by the [[Province of Antofagasta|Antofagasta]] province and on the west by the [[Pacific Ocean]].

 ==Population== 
 {{As of|2002}} (last national [[census]]), there were 23,986 people living in the commune, giving it a population density of {{Pop density|23986|4038.8|km2|prec=1|disp=num}} inhabitants/km². < ref name= " ineDPA "  / > 
 
 The inhabitants of Tocopilla are known as ''Tocopillian''; in [[Spanish language|Spanish]], ''tocopillano'' for men and''Tocopillana'' for women.
