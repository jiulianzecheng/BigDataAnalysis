 '''Laksa''' is a popular spicy [[noodle soup]] from [[Peranakan]] culture. '''Curry laksa''' (sometimes called just ''laksa'') is a [[coconut]] soup made with prawns, beef, [[bean sprouts]] and [[Chili pepper|chili]]. It is very [[spicy]] but can be made milder by leaving out some of the chilis. 
 
 Laksa is a popular food in [[Malaysia]]. A well-known '''Asam Laksa''' can be found in Air Itam, [[Penang]].
