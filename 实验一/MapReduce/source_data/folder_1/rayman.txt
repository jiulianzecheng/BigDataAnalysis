'''Rayman''' is a [[video game]] series created by Michael Ancel, It was distributed by [[Ubisoft]] < ref > {{Cite web|title=Rayman Origins Michel Ancel Video Interview|url=https://www.gamespot.com/videos/rayman-origins-michel-ancel-video-interview/2300-6316230/|access-date=2022-04-01|website=GameSpot|language=en-US}} < /ref >  The first game was launched on 1995. 
 
 The series is about [[Rayman (character)|Rayman]], a [[magic]] [[Fictional character|character]] on [[Fantasy|fantastic]] worlds.
