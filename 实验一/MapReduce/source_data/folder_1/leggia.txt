 '''Leggia''' was a [[municipality]] of the [[district]] of [[Moesa (district)|Moesa]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Graubünden]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 On 1 January 2017, the former municipalities of Leggia and [[Verdabbio]] merged into the municipality of [[Grono, Switzerland|Grono]].
