[[File:Appleton Estate V-X Jamaica Rum-front shot.jpg|thumb|A bottle of Rum from [[Jamaica]]]]'''Rum''' is an [[alcohol]]ic [[Liquor|spirit]]. It is made from [[molasses]] and other [[sugarcane]] by-[[product (chemistry)|product]]s through [[fermentation]] and [[distillation]]. Rum is mostly made in or around the [[Caribbean Sea|Caribbean]]. Rum is also made in other parts of the world, like [[Fiji]] or [[Australia]].
