 '''Rozenburg''' is a former [[List of municipalities of the Netherlands|municipality]] in the [[Netherlands|Dutch]] [[Provinces of the Netherlands|province]] of [[South Holland]]. On 18 March 2010, it has become a part of the municipality of [[Rotterdam]].
