 '''Escholzmatt''' is a [[municipality]] of the [[district]] [[Entlebuch (district)|Entlebuch]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Lucerne (canton)|Lucerne]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 On 1 January 2013, the former municipalities of Escholzmatt and [[Marbach, Lucerne|Marbach]] merged into the new municipality of [[Escholzmatt-Marbach]].
