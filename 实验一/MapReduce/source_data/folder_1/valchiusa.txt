 '''Valchiusa''' is a ''[[comune]]'' in the [[Metropolitan City of Turin]] in the [[Piedmont]] region in [[Italy]]. It was created on 1 January 2019 from the former municipalities of [[Meugliano]], [[Trausella]] and [[Vico Canavese]].
