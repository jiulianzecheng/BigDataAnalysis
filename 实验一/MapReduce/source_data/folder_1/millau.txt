 '''Millau''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]] in southern [[France]], in the [[Aveyron]] [[Departments of France|department]], [[Regions of France|region]] of [[Occitanie]]. It is the [[Prefectures in France|''sous-prefecture'']] ([[Capital city|capital]]) of the ''[[Arrondissements of France|arrondissement]]'' of the same name in the [[Aveyron]] [[Departments of France|department]]. It is also the capital of two [[Cantons in France|''cantons'']]: [[Canton of Millau-1|Millau-1]] and [[Canton of Millau-2|Millau-2]]. 
 
 The territory of the municipality is part of the [[Regional Natural Park of Grands Causses]]. < ref group= " N " > ''Causse'' is an [[Occitan language|Occitan]] word meaning  " [[limestone]] [[plateau]] " . The term is used in France mainly for plateaus in the [[Massif Central]]. < /ref > 
 
 ==History== 
 During the first century, there was an important [[settlement]] where the Tarn and Dourbie rivers meet; this settlement, a couple of kilometres from the current city, was known because here there was an important centre of production of fine [[ceramic]]s that were exported to many places of the [[Roman Empire]]. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 [[File:FR-12-Millau7.JPG|left|thumb|The [[Tarn (river)|Tarn]] river at Millau]] 
 The town is in the valley of the [[Tarn (river)|Tarn]] river and is at the [[confluence]] of the Tarn and [[Dourbie]] rivers. The [[territory]] of the ''commune'' is in the [[Grand Causses]] over part of the [[Causse Rouge]] (to the east of the [[Lévézou]] plateau), the [[Larzac|Causse du Larzac]], and the [[Causse Noir]]. 
 
 The ''commune'' is at {{Convert|641|km|mi|0|abbr=on }} from [[Paris]], {{Convert|50|km|mi|0|abbr=on }} from [[Rodez]], {{Convert|205|km|mi|0|abbr=on }} from [[Marseille]], {{Convert|303|km|mi|0|abbr=on }} from [[Bordeaux]] and {{Convert|142|km|mi|0|abbr=on }} from [[Toulouse]]. 
 
 Millau has an area of {{Convert|168.2|km2|sqmi|abbr=on }}, < ref name= " insee-geo " > {{cite web |url=https://www.insee.fr/fr/statistiques/1405599?geo=COM-12145 |title=Commune de Millau (12145) |work=Comparateur de territoire |publisher=Institut national de la statistique et des études économiques - INSEE |format= |language=French |accessdate=17 March 2017 |date= }} < /ref >  and its [[average]] [[altitude]] in the city is {{Convert|379|m|ft|abbr=on }}; at the [[City and town halls|city hall]], the altitude is {{Convert|366|m|ft|abbr=on }}. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.map-france.com/Millau-12100/|title=Millau |publisher=Map-France.com |language= |accessdate=19 April 2014 |date= }} < /ref > 
 
 The ''commune'' of Millau is surrounded by the ''communes'' [[Aguessac]], [[Compeyre]], [[Paulhe]], [[Comprégnac]], [[Creissels]], [[Saint-Georges-de-Luzençon]], [[Lapanouse-de-Cernon]], [[La Cavalerie]], [[Nant]], [[La Cresse]], [[La Roque-Sainte-Marguerite]], [[Castelnau-Pégayrols]], [[Saint-Beauzély]] y [[Verrières, Aveyron|Verrières]]. < ref name= " mairie " > {{cite web |work=Annuaire-Mairie.fr |publisher= |title=Ville de Millau |url=http://www.annuaire-mairie.fr/ville-millau.html |language=French  |accessdate=17 March 2017}} < /ref > 
 
 ===Climate=== 
 The climate of the Millau is subtropical with dry-summer, '''Csb''' (Mediterranean Climate) in the [[Köppen climate classification]]. 
 
 The average temperature for the year in Millau is {{Convert|10.9|°C|°F }}. The warmest month, on average, is July with an average temperature of {{Convert|19.9|°C|°F }}. The coolest month on average is January, with an average temperature of {{Convert|3.2|°C|°F }}. 
 
 The average amount of precipitation for the year in Millau is {{Convert|731.5|mm|in|1|abbr=on }}. The month with the most precipitation on average is September with {{Convert|78.7|mm|in|1|abbr=on }} of precipitation. The month with the least precipitation on average is July with an average of {{Convert|40.6|mm|in|1|abbr=on }}. < ref name= " wbase " > {{cite web|work= |publisher=Weatherbase |title=Millau, France - Köppen Climate Classification |url=http://www.weatherbase.com/weather/weather-summary.php3?s=85570 & cityname=Millau%2C+Midi-Pyr%E9n%E9es%2C+France & units= |language= |accessdate=17 April 2014}} < /ref >

 == Administration == 
 Millau is a [[Prefectures in France|subprefecture]] of the Aveyron department, the capital of the ''arrondissement'' of [[Arrondissement of Millau|Millau]] and the [[administrative centre]] ({{lang-fr|chef-lieu}}) of two [[Cantons of France|cantons]] 
 * [[Canton of Millau-1|Millau-1]], with 14,297 inhabitants  < small > (2014) < /small > . 
 * [[Canton of Millau-2|Millau-2]], with 14,519 inhabitants  < small > (2014) < /small > . 
 
 It is part of the [[Communes of France|intercommunality]] ''Millau Grands Causses'' ({{lang-fr|Communauté de communes de Millau Grands Causses}}).
