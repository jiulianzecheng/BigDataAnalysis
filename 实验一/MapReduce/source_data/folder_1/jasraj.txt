 '''Pandit Jasraj''' (28 January 1930   & nbsp;– 17 August 2020) < ref name= " et " > {{cite web | url=https://economictimes.indiatimes.com/magazines/panache/pandit-jasraj-90-passes-away-in-new-jersey-modi-mourns-exceptional-mentor/articleshow/77593833.cms | title=Pandit Jasraj, 90, passes away in New Jersey; Modi mourns 'exceptional mentor' | publisher=[[The Economic Times]] | date=17 August 2020 | access-date=17 August 2020}} < /ref >  was an [[India]]n [[Hindustani classical music|classical vocalist]]. His songs were related to ''[[Mewati gharana]]''. < ref name= " :2 " > {{cite news|url=http://archive.indianexpress.com/news/pandit-jasraj-casts-magic-spell/398561/|title=Pandit Jasraj casts magic spell|newspaper=The Indian Express|first=Pranav|last=Kulkarni|date=15 December 2008|access-date=4 August 2017}} < /ref >  His musical career lasted for 75 years. He was born in present-day [[Fatehabad, Haryana|Fatehabad]], [[Haryana]]. 
 
 He was also the founder of schools for Indian classical music in [[Atlanta]], [[Tampa]], [[Vancouver]], [[Toronto]], [[New York (state)|New York]], [[New Jersey]], [[Pittsburgh]], Mumbai, and [[Kerala]]. < ref > {{cite news|url=http://news.wabe.org/post/sangeetayan-promotes-indian-classical-music-atlanta|title=Sangeetayan Promotes Indian Classical Music In Atlanta|date=27 July 2017|website=WABE 90.1|first=Gabbie|last=Watts}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite journal|title=Pandit Jasraj Looks to Enlighten Americans on Indian Music|first=Nitish|last=Rele|url=http://www.khaasbaat.com/may2006/news.htm|journal=Khaas Baat|access-date=19 May 2020|date=May 2006}} < /ref >  At age 90, he was teaching some of his international students through [[Skype]]. < ref > {{Cite interview|title=Pandit Jasraj on turning 90, teaching music through Skype: ‘I don’t like when people say woh zamaana achha tha’|url=https://www.hindustantimes.com/music/pandit-jasraj-on-turning-90-teaching-music-through-skype-i-don-t-like-when-people-say-woh-zamaana-achha-tha/story-T13sw5sPTMAmWtmUQCIfBL.html|date=28 January 2020|website=Hindustan Times|language=en|access-date=22 May 2020|interviewer=[[Press Trust of India]]}} < /ref > < ref > {{cite interview|url=https://zeenews.india.com/entertainment/idiotbox/pandit-jasraj-feels-reality-shows-have-benefited-classical-music_106333.html|title=Pandit Jasraj feels reality shows have benefited classical music|via=Zee News|date=21 February 2012|access-date=22 June 2020|interviewer=[[Indo-Asian News Service]]}} < /ref >
