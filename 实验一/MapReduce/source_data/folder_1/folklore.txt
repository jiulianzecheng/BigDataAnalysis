 '''Folklore''' is part of the [[culture]] of a group of people. It may include [[story|storie]]s, [[music]], [[dance]], [[legend]]s, [[oral history]], [[proverb]]s, [[joke]]s, and [[superstition|popular belief]]s. This also includes the [[oral tradition]]s of that culture. The academic study of folklore is sometimes called ''folkloristics''. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://folklore-society.com/about/william-john-thoms/ |title=William John Thomas |work=The Folklore Society |access-date=9 March 2022 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200715184703/https://folklore-society.com/about/william-john-thoms/ |archive-date=15 July 2020 |url-status=live}} < /ref >  Folkloristics is often [[ethnology|ethnographic]]. 
 
 [[Little Red Riding Hood]] is a famous folktale in European culture.
