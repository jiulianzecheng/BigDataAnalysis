 ==Geography== 
 Suginami is in the western part of Tokyo's special wards. It is next to [[Setagaya, Tokyo|Setagaya]] to the south. It's western neighbors are the cities of [[Mitaka, Tokyo|Mitaka]] and [[Musashino, Tokyo|Musashino]]. [[Nerima, Tokyo|Nerima]] is on the northern border. [[Nakano, Tokyo|Nakano]] is to the east. 
 
 The Kanda river runs through the ward.
