 '''Vulliens''' is a [[municipality]] in the [[district]] of [[Broye-Vully (district)|Broye-Vully]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Vaud]] in [[Switzerland]]. Daniel Schorderet has been mayor since 2002 and there are four municipal councillors.
