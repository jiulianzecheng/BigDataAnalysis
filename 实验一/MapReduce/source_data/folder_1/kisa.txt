 '''Kisa''' is an [[urban area]] in the [[counties of Sweden|county]] of [[Östergötland County|Östergötland]] in [[Sweden]]. It is the seat of [[Kinda Municipality]]. In [[May]] [[1999]], a bank robbery was carried out here, famous for its connection to the [[Malexander Police Murders]].
