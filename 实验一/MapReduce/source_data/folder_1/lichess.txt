'''Lichess''' is [[Free and open-source software|free and open-source]] [[Internet chess server]]. The [[chess]] server is run by a [[Nonprofit organization|non-profit organization]], which is also called Lichess. < ref > {{cite magazine|url=https://www.wired.com/story/best-chess-apps/|title=8 Great Chess Apps for Beginners and Grand Masters|magazine=[[WIRED]]|access-date=2021-07-25|last1=Poindexter|first1=Owen}} < /ref >  The Lichess chess server was created in 2010.  
 
 In April 2021, the [[United States Chess Federation]] reviewed how Lichess deals with [[cheating]] in online chess. After this review, it [[Endorsement|endorsed]] Lichess as a secure place to play chess for its members. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://new.uschess.org/news/us-chess-endorses-lichess-fair-play-methodology|work=[[US Chess Federation]]|title=US Chess Endorses LiChess Fair Play Methodology|date=April 2021|access-date=2021-07-25}} < /ref > 
 
 ==Titled Arenas== 
 Lichess runs tournaments called Titled Arenas, in which only players who have a [[Chess master titles|title]] are able to play. As of February 2021, [[World Chess Champion]] [[Magnus Carlsen]] has the most Titled Arena wins, with 15. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://lichess.org/blog/YDLy4hMAACMAEiKs/alireza-wins-a-13th-titled-arena.|title=Alireza wins a 13th Titled Arena|work=Lichess|access-date=2021-07-25}} < /ref >
