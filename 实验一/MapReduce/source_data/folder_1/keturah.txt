'''Keturah''' was a wife of [[Abraham]]. Abraham married Keturah after the death of Sarah. She had six children with Abraham, whoes names are Zimran, Jokshan, Medan Mdian, Ishbak, Shuah (Gen 25:1-2). [[Rashi]] said that Keturah was actually [[Hagar]] known by another name. < ref > https://www.chabad.org/parshah/article_cdo/aid/4171660/jewish/Who-Was-Keturah-and-Why-Did-Abraham-Marry-Her.htm < /ref >
