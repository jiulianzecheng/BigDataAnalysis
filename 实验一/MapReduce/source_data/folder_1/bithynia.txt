 '''Bithynia''' was an old [[kingdom]] of a [[Roman Empire|Roman]] [[province]] in the northwest of [[Asia Minor]]. < ref > Matthew Bunson, ''Encyclopedia of the Roman Empire'' (New York: Facts On File, 2002), p. 76 < /ref >  It was at the coast of the [[Black Sea]].
