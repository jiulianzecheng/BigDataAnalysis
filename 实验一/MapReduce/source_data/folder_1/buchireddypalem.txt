 '''Buchireddypalem''' is a [[town]] in the [[Nellore district]] of [[Andhra Pradesh]], [[India]]. It is 15 & nbsp;km from [[Nellore]] city. It is on the state [[highway]] between Nellore and [[Mumbai]]. The closest [[railway station]] is [[Nellore railway station]]. The closest [[seaport]] is [[Krishnapatnam]]. The closest [[Airport|airports]] are [[Tirupati Airport]] in [[Tirupati]] and [[Chennai Airport]] in [[Chennai]].
