'''Drama''' is a type of [[literature]]. A drama can be in the form of a [[novel]], [[Television programme|television show]], [[movie]], [[Play (theatre)|play]] or [[dance]]. Although drama is a [[Greek language|Greek]] word meaning  " [[action]] " , most dramas focus more on the [[Intimate relationship|relationship]]s between people than on the actions. Dramas usually have a serious [[feel]] to them. Drama is considered the opposite of [[comedy]], but drama and comedy can be used at the same time. Drama is full of sensational action, dialogue, incidents etc.   
 
 [[William Shakespeare|Shakespeare]] influenced the theatre industry by showing what could be done with plot and language.
