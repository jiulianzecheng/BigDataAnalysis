 '''Brindle''' is a [[fur]] coloring in animals. It mainly appears on [[dog]]s, [[cat]]s, [[cattle]], and sometimes [[horse]]s. The brindle color is similar to the stripes of a [[tiger]], but much less visible. Animals with a brindle-colored coat are said to be '''brindled'''.
