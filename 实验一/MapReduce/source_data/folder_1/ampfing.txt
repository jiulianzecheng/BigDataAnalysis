 '''Ampfing''' is a [[Municipalities in Germany|municipality]] in [[Mühldorf (district)|Mühldorf]] in [[Bavaria]] in [[Germany]]. 
 
 == Geography == 
 Ampfing is in the region Southeast-Upper-Bavaria (Südostoberbayern) in the valley of river Isen.
