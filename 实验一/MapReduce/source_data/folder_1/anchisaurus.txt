 '''Anchisaurus''' was a small [[sauropod]] [[dinosaur]] from the early [[Jurassic]]. It was shown in the [[movie]] ''[[When Dinosaurs Roamed America]]'' getting attacked by a pack of ''[[Megapnosaurus]]'' and eaten by a ''[[Dilophosaurus]]''.
