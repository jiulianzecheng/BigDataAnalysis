 '''Pachuca''' (officially  " Pachuca de Soto " ) is the capital city of the state of [[Hidalgo (state)|Hidalgo]], in [[Mexico]]. It is also the capital of the [[municipality]] named Pachuca. < ref > {{Cite web |url=http://www.e-local.gob.mx/work/templates/enciclo/hidalgo/municipios/13048a.htm |title=Pachuca de Soto-Encyclopedia of the municipalities of Mexico. |access-date=2012-03-18 |archive-date=2007-05-08 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20070508200741/http://www.e-local.gob.mx/work/templates/enciclo/hidalgo/municipios/13048a.htm |url-status=dead }} < /ref > 
 
 Pachuca's [[nickname]] is  " The Windy Beauty "  or  " Bride of the Wind " , because in the city there are seasons where the winds are very strong. Even local legend is depicted on a monument (the monument of the victory of the wind). 
 
 The city has a [[soccer]] club  " Pachuca " . It has a traditional dish called  " paste "  which consists of a small pie filled with a mixture of chopped potatoes, ground beef and spicy. It has a clock tower dedicated to the [[Mexican War of Independence]].
