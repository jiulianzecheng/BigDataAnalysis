 '''Gorizia''' ({{Lang-sl|Gorica}}, {{Lang-fur|Gurize}}) is an [[Italy|Italian]] city in [[Friuli-Venezia Giulia]]. It is on the border with [[Slovenia]]. About 35,000 people live there. 
 
 Until 2017, it was the capital of the [[Province of Gorizia]].
