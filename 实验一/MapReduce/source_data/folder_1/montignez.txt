 '''Montignez''' was a [[municipality]] of the [[district]] of [[Porrentruy (district)|Porrentruy]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Jura (canton)|Jura]] in [[Switzerland]]. On 1 January 2009, the former municipalities of Montignez, [[Buix]] and [[Courtemaîche]] merged into the new municipality of [[Basse-Allaine]].
