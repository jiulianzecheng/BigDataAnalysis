 '''Alverstone''' is a village 3 kilometres from the east coast of the [[Isle of Wight]], near Sandown. When [[Richard Webster, 1st Viscount Alverstone|Richard Webster]] became [[Lord Chief Justice of England and Wales|Chief Justice of England]] in 1900, he chose the title [[Lord Alverstone]] because it was the title he was permitted to choose which was  " closest "  to Sandown, one of his favorite locales. < ref > [http://www.bbc.co.uk/dna/h2g2/A492022 ''Sandown Pier, Isle of Wight, UK''], [[h2g2]], BBC.co.uk, 17th January 2001. < /ref >
