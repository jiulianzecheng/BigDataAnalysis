 '''Kangasniemi''' is a municipality in [[Southern Savonia]], [[Finland]]. As of January 2014, almost 5,800 people lived there. Neighbouring municipalities are [[Hankasalmi]], [[Hirvensalmi]], [[Joutsa]], [[Mikkeli]], [[Pieksämäki]] and [[Toivakka]]. 
 
 Kangasniemi was established in 1867. It has over 40 villages.
