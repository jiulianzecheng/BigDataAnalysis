'''Scanlation''' (sometimes '''scanslation''') is a word for [[manga]] comics which have been [[image scanner|scanned]] and [[translation|translated]] by fans from the language they were in before (usually [[Japanese language|Japanese]] or [[Korean language|Korean]]) to a different language, usually [[English language|English]], [[French language|French]] or [[Spanish language|Spanish]].
