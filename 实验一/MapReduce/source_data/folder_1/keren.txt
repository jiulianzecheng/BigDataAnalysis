 '''Keren''' ([[Ge'ez]]: '''ከረን'''  {{lang-ar|كرن}} or كيرين), formerly known as '''Cheren''' and '''Sanhit''', is the second largest [[city]] in [[Eritrea]]. In 2005, 86,483 people lived there.
