 The '''sitar''' ({{IPAc-en|lang|pron|ˈ|s|ɪ|t|ɑr}} or {{IPAc-en|s|ɪ|ˈ|t|ɑr}}; {{lang-hi|सितार}}, [[Punjabi language|Punjabi]]: ਸਿਤਾਰ, [[Urdu]]: {{Nastaliq|ستار}} {{transl|hi|IAST|sitāra}} {{IPA-hi|sɪˈtaːr|pron}}) is a plucked string instrument. It uses [[sympathetic strings]] along with a long hollow neck and a [[gourd]] [[resonance|resonating]] chamber to make a rich sound with complex harmonic resonance. It has been used throughout [[India]] and [[Iran]] for many centuries.
