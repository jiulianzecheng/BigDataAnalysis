 '''''Wolbachia''''' is a [[genus]] of [[bacteria]] which infects [[arthropod]] species, including a high proportion of [[insect]]s. It is one of the world's most common parasitic microbes and is possibly the most common reproductive [[parasite]] in the [[biosphere]].
