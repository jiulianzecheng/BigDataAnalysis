 '''Hernicourt''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is found in the [[Regions of France|region]] [[Nord-Pas-de-Calais]] in the [[Pas-de-Calais]] [[Departments of France|department]] in the north of [[France]]. It is 106 miles north of Paris on the River Ternoise. < ref name=wolfram > {{cite web|url=http://www.wolframalpha.com/entities/cities/hernicourt/90/je/2t/|title=City Data for Hernicourt|first=|last=|work=wolframalpha.com|year=2011|accessdate=6 November 2011}}{{Dead link|date=January 2021 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }} < /ref >  In 2004 there were about 424 people living in Hernicourt. < ref name=wolfram/ >
