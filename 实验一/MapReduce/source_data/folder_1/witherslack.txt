 '''Witherslack''' is a village and [[civil parish]] in [[South Lakeland]], [[Cumbria]], England. In 2001 there were 482 people living in Witherslack. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.ons.gov.uk/help/localstatistics|title=Local statistics - Office for National Statistics|website=www.ons.gov.uk}} < /ref >  It has 15 listed buildings. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://britishlistedbuildings.co.uk/england/cumbria/Witherslack|title=Listed Buildings in Cumbria|website=britishlistedbuildings.co.uk}} < /ref >  It has a church called St Paul's, which was built in about 1669. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.visitcumbria.com/churches/witherslack-st-pauls-church/|title=Witherslack St Pauls Church|publisher=}} < /ref >
