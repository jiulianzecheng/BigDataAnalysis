 '''Ilmajoki''' ({{lang-sv|Ilmola}}) is a municipality in [[Southern Ostrobothnia]], [[Finland]]. There were about 12,090 people living there on January 31, 2014. It is next to the municipalities of [[Isokyrö]], [[Jalasjärvi]], [[Kurikka]], [[Laihia]] and [[Seinäjoki]]. Most of the people speak [[Finnish language|Finnish]].
