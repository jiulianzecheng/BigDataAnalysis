 The '''Pelodryadinae''' are a [[subfamily]] of frogs. How these frogs are classified is disputed. They are found in Australo-Papuan regions. < ref > {{Cite web|title=frog and toad - Classification {{!}} Britannica|url=https://www.britannica.com/animal/Anura/Classification|access-date=2022-04-11|website=www.britannica.com|language=en}} < /ref > 
 
 == Classification == 
 The [[Amphibian Species of the World]] < ref name= " Duellman et al. 2016 "  / >  puts the Pelodryadinae in the family [[Pelodryadidae]]. There are two [[genera]]: < ref name= " Frost "  / > 
 
 * ''[[Nyctimystes]]''  < small > Stejneger, 1916 < /small > 
 * ''[[Ranoidea (genus)|Ranoidea]]''  < small > Tschudi, 1838 < /small >  — including ''[[Cyclorana]]''  < small > Steindachner, 1867 < /small >  and parts of ''[[Litoria]]''  < small > Tschudi, 1838 < /small > 
 
 The AmphibiaWeb says that Pelodryadinae are in the family [[Hylidae]] and have three [[genera]]: < ref name= " AmphibiaWeb "  / > 
 
 * ''Cyclorana''  < small > Steindachner, 1867 < /small > 
 * ''Litoria''  < small > Tschudi, 1838 < /small > 
 * ''Nyctimystes''  < small > Stejneger, 1916 < /small >
