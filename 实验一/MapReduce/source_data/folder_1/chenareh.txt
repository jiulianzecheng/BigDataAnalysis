 '''Chenareh''' ({{lang-ku|Çinare}}, {{lang-fa|چناره}}) is a city in the Kurdistan province of [[Iran]]. Its people speak [[Kurdish language|Kurdish]]. They are followers of [[Islam]].  At the 2006 census, its population was 433, in 101 families. < ref > {{IranCensus2006|12}} < /ref >
