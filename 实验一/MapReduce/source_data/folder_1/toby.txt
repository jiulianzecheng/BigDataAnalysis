'''Toby''' [ '''toh'''-bee ] < ref > {{Cite web|title=Definition of toby {{!}} Dictionary.com|url=https://www.dictionary.com/browse/toby|access-date=2023-04-04|website=www.dictionary.com|language=en}} < /ref >  is a male name.  Toby is a well-liked name for male individuals with a rich history and significance. Its origin can be traced back to England and is a derivative of the name Tobias, which means  " God is good "  or  " God is my goodness. "  The name has been widely used since the Middle Ages and has remained a popular choice for boys in English-speaking countries. Apart from Toby, the name has also been spelled in different variations such as Tobie, Tobey, and Tobias. The name has also been referenced in literature and popular culture, with characters such as Toby Belch in Shakespeare's  " Twelfth Night "  and Toby Ziegler in the television show  " The West Wing "  bearing the name. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Toby name Meaning and Origin|url=https://babys.name/detail-Toby-Meaning-Origin-Famous-Similar-Sibling.html|access-date=2023-04-04|website=babys.name}} < /ref >  It is used in [[English language|English]] speaking places.
