'''Grevena''' is a Greek city and it is one of 51 [[prefecture]]s of [[Greece]]. Grevena is a small city which is in the west of [[Macedonia (Greece)|Macedonia]]. Its population is 12,037 inhabitants according to 2001 census. It is built at a height of 500 meter approximately. West of Grevena is the mountain range of [[Pindos]] with many [[forest]]s and with a [[skiing|ski]] center which it is named [[Vasilitsa]]. This ski center has a lot of [[snow]] throughout the [[winter]].  
 
 == Geography == 
 Greneva has a total area of 1,860 square kilometers.
