 It conducts business through a number of affiliated companies in more than a hundred countries and territories. Amway was ranked No. 29 among the largest [[privately-held company|privately held]] companies in the United States by ''[[Forbes]]'' in 2015 based on revenue, and No. 1 among multi-level marketing companies by ''[[Direct Selling News]]'' in 2016. < ref > {{cite news|url=https://www.forbes.com/companies/amway/|title=Amway on the Forbes Largest Private Companies List|date=October 1, 2014|work=Forbes|access-date=July 19, 2015}} < /ref > < ref > [http://www.amway.co.uk/cms/about_amway_and_direct_selling/who_is_amway Amway (uk) – Who is Amway?] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20090316051739/http://www.amway.co.uk/cms/about_amway_and_direct_selling/who_is_amway|date=March 16, 2009}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite web|url=http://directsellingnews.com/index.php/view/2016_dsn_global_100_list#.V9JBRvnhCM-|title=2016 DSN Global 100 List — Direct Selling News|website=directsellingnews.com|access-date=2016-10-10|archive-date=2016-04-11|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160411013820/http://directsellingnews.com/index.php/view/2016_dsn_global_100_list#.V9JBRvnhCM-|url-status=dead}} < /ref > 
 
 It was founded in 1959 by [[Jay Van Andel]] and [[Richard DeVos]] and is based in [[Ada Township, Michigan]].
