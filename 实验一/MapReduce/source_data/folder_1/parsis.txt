 The '''Parsis''' are a [[Ethnic group|ethnic]] and [[Religion|religious]] group in [[India]] and [[Pakistan]] who follow [[Zoroastrianism]]. They are descendants of [[Persian people|Persians]] who moved to the region to escape [[religious persecution]] during the [[Arab conquests|Arab conquest]] of the [[Persian Empire]]. There are only around 60-70 thousand Parsis in both [[India]] and [[Pakistan]]. < ref > {{Cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=yXYKAgAAQBAJ|title=Encyclopedia of the World's Minorities|last=Skutsch|first=Carl|date=2013-11-07|publisher=Routledge|isbn=978-1-135-19388-1|language=en}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=QIogEAAAQBAJ|title=The Colonial Public and the Parsi Stage: The Making of the Theatre of Empire (1853-1893)|last=Nicholson|first=Rashna Darius|date=2021-02-27|publisher=Springer Nature|isbn=978-3-030-65836-6|language=en}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite web|last=Notezai|first=Muhammad Akbar|date=2016-07-22|title=The untold story of Quetta's Parsi community|url=http://www.dawn.com/news/1270123|access-date=2023-12-12|website=DAWN.COM|language=en}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite news|date=2016-01-09|title=Why is India's wealthy Parsi community vanishing?|language=en-GB|work=BBC News|url=https://www.bbc.com/news/world-asia-india-35219331|access-date=2023-12-12}} < /ref >
