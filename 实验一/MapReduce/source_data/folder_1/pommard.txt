{{Infobox French commune|name=Pommard|intercommunality=Beaune Côte et Sud|population date={{France metadata Wikidata|population_as_of}}|population={{France metadata Wikidata|population_total}}|area km2=10.05|elevation max m=411|elevation min m=215|elevation m=248|coordinates={{coord|47.0100|4.7958|format=dms|display=inline,title}}|term=2014 & ndash;2020|commune status=[[Communes of France|Commune]]|mayor=Jacques Frotey|postal code=21630|INSEE=21492|canton=Ladoix-Serrigny|arrondissement=Beaune|image coat of arms=Blason de la ville de Pommard (21).svg|caption=A general view of Pommard|image=IMG Pommard et Beaune.JPG|population footnotes={{France metadata Wikidata|population_footnotes}}}}'''Pommard''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]] in the [[Côte-d'Or]] [[Departments of France|department]] and [[Bourgogne-Franche-Comté]] [[Regions of France|region]] of eastern [[France]]. 
 
 Pommard is near [[Beaune]] and on the [[Route des Grands Crus]]. 
 
 The D973 and D17 pass through the commune. 
 
 Pommard is twinned with [[Nackenheim]], [[Germany]].
