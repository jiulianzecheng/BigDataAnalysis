 '''Botnang''' (formerly ''Bothnang'') is a district of the City of [[Stuttgart]] and is between [[Feuerbach]], [[Stuttgart-West]] and [[Stuttgart-Vaihingen|Vaihingen]]. 
 
 ==Notable people== 
 
 * [[Jürgen Klinsmann]], German professional [[Association football|football]] manager and former player. 
 [[File:Klinsmann Bakery 20060521.jpg|thumb|The Klinsmann Bakery]] 
 * [[Ulf Merbold]], German physicist and the first West German astronaut. 
 * [[Gerda Herrmann]], a German poet and composer, has been living in Botnang since the 1960s and has composed almost 400 songs there.
