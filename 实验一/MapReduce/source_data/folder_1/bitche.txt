 '''Bitche''' is a [[Communes in France|commune]] in the [[Lorraine]] [[Regions of France|region]] in northeastern France. About 5,000 people live there. 
 
 It is known for its large [[citadel]]. The surrounding area is known as le Pays de Bitche ( " Land of Bitche " ) and Bitscherland in German.
