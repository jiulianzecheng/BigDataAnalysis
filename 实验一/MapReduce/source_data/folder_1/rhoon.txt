 '''Rhoon''' is a village in the Dutch province of [[South Holland]]. It is in the [[Albrandswaard]] municipality and had 16,066 inhabitants in 2020. < ref > [https://allecijfers.nl/woonplaats/rhoon/ Allecijfers.nl] < /ref > 
 
 Rhoon used to be a municipality. It became part of the new Albrandswaard municipality on 1 January 1985.
