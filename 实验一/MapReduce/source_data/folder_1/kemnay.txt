 '''Kemnay''' ([[Scottish Gaelic language|Gaelic]]: ''Ceann a' Mhuigh'') is a village 16 miles west of [[Aberdeen]] in [[Scotland]]. It has a population of about 7040 (2007). 
 
 ==Sports== 
 The golfer [[Paul Lawrie]], who famously won the 1999 Open Championship is a former pupil of Kemnay Academy, as is [[Aberdeen FC]] footballer [[Darren Mackie]].
