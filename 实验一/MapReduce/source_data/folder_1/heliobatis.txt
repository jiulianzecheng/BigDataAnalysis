 '''''Heliobatis''''' is an [[extinct]] [[genus]] of [[stingray]]. < ref name = " Grande84 " / >   At present the genus contains the single species '''''Heliobatis radians'''''. 
 
 The [[fossil]] is found in the early [[Eocene]], [[Wasatchian]] stage, [[Green_River_Formation#Fossil_zones|Fossil Lake]] deposits. Fossil Lake is part of the [[Green River Formation]] in southwest [[Wyoming]]. < ref name = " Grande84 " / >   
 
 ''Heliobatis'' is one of only two known [[rays]] to have been found in the Green River formation.
