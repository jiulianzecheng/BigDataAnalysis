 '''Ghemme''' is a ''[[comune]]'' in the [[Province of Novara]] in the [[Piedmont]] region in [[Italy]]. It is on the river [[Sesia]] about {{convert|80|km|mi}} northeast of [[Turin]] and about {{convert|25|km|mi}} northwest of [[Novara]]. 
 
 It is the birthplace of architect [[Alessandro Antonelli]]. The main sight is the castle (''Castello-ricetto''), which was built in the 11th to 15th centuries. 
 
 Ghemme is known for its [[Ghemme DOCG|wine of the same name]].
