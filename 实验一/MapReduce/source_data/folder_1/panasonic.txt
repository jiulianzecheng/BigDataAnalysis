 '''Panasonic Corporation''', formerly known as '''Matsushita Electric Industrial Co., Ltd''', is a [[Multinational corporation|multinational]] [[electronics]] company. Panasonic is known for their electronics products. The company was created by [[Kōnosuke Matsushita]] in 1918. It is the fourth-largest television manufacturer in the world. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://finapps.forbes.com/finapps/jsp/finance/compinfo/CIAtAGlance.jsp?sedol=6572707|accessdate=2014-03-20|title=Forbes Global 2000 Profile|work=[[Forbes]]|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20100202064838/http://finapps.forbes.com/finapps/jsp/finance/compinfo/CIAtAGlance.jsp?sedol=6572707|archive-date=2010-02-02}} < /ref >  The company was known, as Matsushita Electric Industrial Co., Ltd. from 1935 to October 1, 2008. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://panasonic.co.jp/corp/news/official.data/data.dir/jn081001-3/jn081001-3.html|accessdate=2014-03-20|title=松下電器産業株式会社が「パナソニック株式会社」に社名変更|publisher=Panasonic Corporation (in Japanese)|archive-date=2014-10-05|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20141005163707/http://panasonic.co.jp/corp/news/official.data/data.dir/jn081001-3/jn081001-3.html|url-status=dead}} < /ref >
