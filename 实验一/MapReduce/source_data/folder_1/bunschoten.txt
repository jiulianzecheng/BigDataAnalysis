 '''Bunschoten''' is a [[List of municipalities of the Netherlands|municipality]] in the [[province]] [[Utrecht (province)|Utrecht]], the [[Netherlands]]. Bunschoten is also called Bunschoten-spakenburg because the two villages Bunschoten and [[Spakenburg]] “grew” into each other. Bunschoten was in the early ages a farmers village and Spakenburg a fishers village.
