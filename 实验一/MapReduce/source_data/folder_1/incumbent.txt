 An '''incumbent''' is the current holder of a  [[political office]] or position.  This word is usually used when talking about [[election]]s, which are often between the incumbent and one other person or several other people.  For example, [[Joe Biden]] is the incumbent [[President of the United States]] since he is still president. It can also be used in sports to let people know if a [[Sportsperson|sportsman]] still holds the position or award in question.
