'''Juju''' (Fr: Joujou) is a word used to describe [[fetish]] beliefs and practices. The word Juju is inspired by the French word Joujou which means  " Plaything "  or toy. Juju includes use of dolls, other fetishes and seeking for omens or consulting oracles, etc.
