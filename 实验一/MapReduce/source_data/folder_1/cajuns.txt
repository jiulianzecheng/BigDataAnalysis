 The '''Cajuns''', also known as ''[[Acadians]]'', are an ethnic group mainly living in the U.S. states of [[Louisiana]] and [[Texas]], and in the [[The Maritimes|Canadian maritime provinces]] consisting in part of the descendants of the original [[Acadians|Acadian]] exiles-French-speakers from [[Acadia]] in what are now [[The Maritimes]] of Eastern Canada.
