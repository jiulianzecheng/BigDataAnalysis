'''Abdi''' is a name that is well-known in different cultures and languages. The name has been in use for centuries and its meaning varies based on its origin and the context.  Abdi is derived from the [[Arabic language|Arabic]] word  " abd, "  which means  " servant "  or  " worshipper. "  The name has Arabic roots and means  " servant of God "  or  " worshipper of God.
