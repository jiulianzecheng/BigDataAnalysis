 '''Alcester''' is a [[Roman Empire|Roman]] [[market town]] in [[Warwickshire]], [[England]]. It is about 8 miles west of [[Stratford-upon-Avon]]. In 2001, 6,214 people lived there. 
 
 The River Alne joins with the River Arrow at Alcester.
