 '''Nephelinite''' is a fine-grained or [[igneous rock]] made up mostly of [[nepheline]] and [[clinopyroxene]] (also [[augite]]). 
 
 Nephelinite is [[Darkness|dark]] and may look like [[basalt]] in a hand. However, [[basalt]] consists mostly of [[augite]] and calcic.
