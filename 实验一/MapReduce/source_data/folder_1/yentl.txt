 '''''Yentl''''' is a 1983 American romantic musical drama movie directed and starring [[Barbra Streisand]] and based on the Leah Napolin and [[Isaac Bashevis Singer]] play and short story of the same name. It also stars [[Amy Irving]], [[Mandy Patinkin]], [[Nehemiah Persoff]] and was nominated for 5 Oscars including one win at the Academy Awards in 1984.
