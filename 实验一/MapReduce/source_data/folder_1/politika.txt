 '''Politika''' ([[serbian language|sr.]] '''Политика''') is a daily [[newspaper]] that is very popular in [[Serbia]]. It is the longest-published newspaper in this [[country]]. It is over one hundred [[year]]s old (established in [[1904]]). This newspaper costs around  30 [[Dinar]]s in Serbia. Its founder is [[Vladislav Ribnikar]].
