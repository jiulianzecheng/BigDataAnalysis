 '''Transavia''' is a Dutch and French [[low-cost carrier]]. It is currently owned by [[Air France-KLM]].  < ref > {{cite web | url=http://www.airfranceklm.com/en/transavia | title=Transavia  & #124; AIR FRANCE KLM }} < /ref >  It began on January 3, 1966. It was the third airline founded in the Netherlands. The first scheduled flight happened on October 26, 1986.  < ref > {{Cite web |url=http://www.suvarnabhumiairport.com/en/262-airlines-information?code=TRA |title=Airlines Information - Suvarnabhumi Airport Thailand Official Website - Bangkok Flights Bangkok Airport Suvarnabhumi Airport (BKK) |access-date=2016-03-20 |archive-date=2014-08-13 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140813231004/http://suvarnabhumiairport.com/en/262-airlines-information?code=TRA |url-status=dead }} < /ref >  It has scheduled (planned) and charter (irregular) flights to many destinations in [[Europe]].
