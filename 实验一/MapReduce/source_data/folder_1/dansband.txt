 '''Dansband''' ('''dance band''') is a term in [[Swedish language|Swedish]] that describes a band that plays '''dansbandsmusik''' ( " dance band music " ). It is used for bands in [[Denmark]], [[Finland]], [[Norway]] and [[Sweden]] that perform live music from stage, allowing the audience to dance [[partner dance]]. Famous Swedish bands have been [[Curt Haagers]], [[Ingmar Nordstr√∂ms]], [[Matz Bladhs]], [[Sten  &  Stanley]], [[Sven-Ingvars]], [[Thorleifs]], [[Vikingarna]] and [[Wizex]]. 
 
 Such bands were very popular in Sweden by the mid [[1970s]]. By the end of the same decade, the genre began to go through hard times, due to the arrival of [[disco]] music.
