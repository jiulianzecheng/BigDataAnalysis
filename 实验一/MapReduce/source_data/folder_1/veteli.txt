 '''Veteli''' ({{lang-sv|Vetil}}) is a municipality in [[Central Ostrobothnia]], [[Finland]]. It had 3,005 people living there on 31 December 2021. The municipalities next to it are [[Evijärvi]], [[Halsua]], [[Kaustinen]], [[Kronoby]], [[Lappajärvi]], [[Perho]] and [[Vimpeli]].
