 '''Tschiertschen''' is a village in the [[municipality]] of [[Tschiertschen-Praden]] of the [[district]] of [[Plessur (district)|Plessur]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Graubünden]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 The formerly independent municipality merged with [[Praden]] to form Tschiertschen-Praden on January 1, 2009.
