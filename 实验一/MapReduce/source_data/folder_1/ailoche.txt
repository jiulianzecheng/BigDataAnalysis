 '''Ailoche''' is a ''[[comune]]'' in the [[Province of Biella]] in the [[Piedmont]] region in [[Italy]]. The main sights are the sanctuary ''Santuario della Brugarola'' and the parish church ''Parrocchiale di San Bernardo''.
