 A '''Sestertius''' (sometimes called '''sesterce''' in English was an [[Ancient Rome|Ancient Roman]] [[coin]]. It was frequently used in trade. Its value was two and a half asses, another Roman coin. Four sestertii (or setercves) were worth a denarius, another oin worth ten Asses.
