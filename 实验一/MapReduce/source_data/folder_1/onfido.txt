'''Onfido''' is a [[technology company]] that helps businesses check people's identities using a photo-based [[identity document]], a [[selfie]] and [[artificial intelligence]] [[Algorithm|algorithms]]. It started in July 2012.  Onfido is based in London.  More than  650 people work for the company all round the world.  It is especially helpful for people who have migrated and have to prove who they are.
