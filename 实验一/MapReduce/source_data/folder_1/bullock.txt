 * Bullock (in British English), a [[Castration|castrated]] [[bull]] of any age 
 * Bullock (in North America), a young uncastrated bull 
 * Bullock (in Australia and New Zealand), an [[ox]], an adult male [[Bovinae|bovine]] used for draught (usually but not always castrated)
