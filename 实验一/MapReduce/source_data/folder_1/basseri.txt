The '''Basseri'''  are a [[persian people|Persian]] [[nomad|nomadic]] and pastoral tribe of the Fars Province in [[Iran]].Their migratory area is around [[Shiraz]].The Basseri are a tribe of [[khamseh|Khamseh confederation]]. 
 [[file:Basseri handcrafts.png|thumb|right|275px|Basseri handcrafts]] 
 Basseries are [[Iran|Persian]].They are the survivors of Pasargadean tribe, the biggest tribe of Persia in Achaemenid Empire period.They speak Persian with a different accent.They are [[Shia Islam|Shia Muslims]].
