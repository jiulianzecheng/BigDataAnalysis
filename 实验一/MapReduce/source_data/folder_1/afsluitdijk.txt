 '''Afsluitdijk''' is a large dam in the Netherlands. It is {{cvt|32|km}} long. The dam separates the [[IJsselmeer]] [[lake]] from the [[Wadden Sea]]. It was built between 1927 and 1932. The dam was built because of floods that affected the country's low-lying lands. It also serves as a road between [[Friesland]] and [[North Holland]] [[Provinces of the Netherlands|provinces]]. Afsluitdijk has become a popular [[Tourist attraction|tourist destination]]. People go there for its scenic beauty, [[Recreation|recreational opportunities]], and [[History|historical]] importance.
