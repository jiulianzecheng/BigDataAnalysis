The '''Suica''' is a card that people can use to pay for [[train]] fares and goods at [[convenience store]]s in [[mainland Japan]], as well as other store types and regions. Cash amount can be added or removed electronically, simply by hovering the card over a card reader. It is convenient and faster to use than coins and bills, and magnetic stripe cards. The technology used is called [[RFID]], and a chip by [[Sony]] is used, called [[FeliCa]].
