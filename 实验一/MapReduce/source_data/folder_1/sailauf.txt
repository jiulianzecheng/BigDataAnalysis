 '''Sailauf''' is a municipality in the [[Aschaffenburg (district)|Aschaffenburg district]] in the ''[[Regierungsbezirk]]'' of [[Lower Franconia]] (''Unterfranken'') in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]].
