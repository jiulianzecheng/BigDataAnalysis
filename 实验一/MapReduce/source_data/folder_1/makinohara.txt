 '''Makinohara''' is a [[city]] in [[Shizuoka Prefecture]], [[Japan]]. It is a coastal city on the island of [[Honshū]] next to [[Suruga Bay]]. Nearby cities are: [[Omaezaki, Shizuoka|Omaezaki]], [[Kikugawa, Shizuoka|Kikugawa]], [[Shimada, Shizuoka|Shimada]], and [[Yoshida, Shizuoka|Yoshida]].
