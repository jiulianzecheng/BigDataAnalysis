 '''Chrishall''' is a village and [[civil parish]] in [[Uttlesford]], [[Essex]], England. In 2001 there were 507 people living in Chrishall. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.ons.gov.uk/help/localstatistics|title=Local statistics - Office for National Statistics|website=www.ons.gov.uk}} < /ref >  It has a church called Holy Trinity. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.essexchurches.info/church.aspx?p=Chrishall|title=Holy Trinity, Chrishall Church, Essex|website=www.essexchurches.info}} < /ref >  Other names for Chrishall include Chrisshall, Cresshall, and Cryotissale. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.recordinguttlesfordhistory.org.uk/chrishall/chrishall.html|title=local history of Chrishall village in Essex|website=www.recordinguttlesfordhistory.org.uk}} < /ref >
