 '''Seduction''' is to convince someone to do something which he or she did not want to do at the start. < ref > {{cite web |title=Definition of SEDUCING |url=https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/seducing |website=www.merriam-webster.com |access-date=24 February 2021 |language=en}} < /ref >  It can also mean convincing the person to do something he or she ought not to do (such as acting against a [[rule]]). Most commonly the term is used to convince another person to have a [[sexual relationship]].
