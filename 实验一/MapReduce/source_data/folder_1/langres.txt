 '''Langres''' is a [[Communes of France|commune of France]] and a [[Prefectures in France|subprefecture]] of the [[Haute-Marne]] department in northeastern [[France]], in the [[Grand Est]] [[Regions of France|region]]. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 The city of Langres is on the southen part of the [[Haute-Marne]] department in northeast France; the city is on a [[:en:wikt:promontory|promontory]] that is to the north of the ''[[Plateau]] de Langres''. 
 
 It has an area of {{Convert|22.3|km2|sqmi|abbr=on }}. < ref name= " insee-geo " > {{cite web |url=https://www.insee.fr/fr/statistiques/1405599?geo=COM-52269 |title=Commune de Langres (52269) - Comparateur de territoire |work=Publications et statistiques pour la France ou les régions |publisher=Institut national de la statistique et des études économiques - INSEE |language=fr |access-date=11 July 2017}} < /ref >  Its average [[altitude]] is {{Convert|401|m|ft|abbr=on}}; at the [[City and town halls|city hall]], the altitude is {{Convert|450|m|ft|abbr=on }}. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://www.map-france.com/Langres-52200/ |title=The city of Langres |publisher=Map-France.com |access-date=11 December 2016 }} < /ref >

 ===Climate=== 
 The [[Köppen climate classification]] type for the climate at Langres is a  " Marine West Coast Climate "  and of the subtype  " Cfb " . < ref name= " wbase " > {{cite web|publisher=Weatherbase |title=Langres, France - Köppen Climate Classification |url=http://www.weatherbase.com/weather/weather-summary.php3?s=598829 & cityname=Langres%2C+Champagne-Ardenne%2C+France & units= |access-date=11 July 2017 }} < /ref > 
 
 The [[average]] amount of [[precipitation]] for the year in Langres is {{Convert|746.8|mm|in|1|abbr=on }}. The month with the most precipitation on average is December with {{Convert|83.8|mm|in|1|abbr=on }} of precipitation. The month with the least precipitation on average is August with an average of {{Convert|40.6|mm|in|1|abbr=on }}. 
 
 The average temperature for the year in Langres is {{Convert|9.7|°C|°F }}. The warmest month, on average, is July with an average temperature of {{Convert|18.7|°C|°F }}. The coolest month on average is January, with an average temperature of {{Convert|1.1|°C|°F }}.

 ==Population== 
 The inhabitants of Langres are known, in [[French language|French]], as ''Langonais'' (women: ''Langonaises'') or ''Langrois'' (women: ''Langroises''). < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.habitants.fr/langres/banque/|title=Langres (52200) |publisher=habitants.fr |language=fr |access-date=11 July 2017 }} < /ref >

 == Administration == 
 Langres is a [[Prefectures in France|subprefecture]] of the Haute-Marne department, the capital of the ''arrondissement'' of [[Arrondissement of Langres|Langres]] and the [[administrative centre]] ({{lang-fr|chef-lieu}}) of the canton [[Canton of Langres|Langres]] formed by 18 ''communes'' (including Langres), with 12,160 inhabitants  < small > (2014) < /small > . 
 
 It is part of the [[Communes of France|intercommunality]] ''Le Grand Langres'' ({{lang-fr|Communauté de communes du Grand Langres}}).
