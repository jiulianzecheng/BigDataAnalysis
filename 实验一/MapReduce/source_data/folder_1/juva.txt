 '''Juva''' is a [[municipality]] in [[Southern Savonia]]. It had about 6,700 [[people]] living there in January 2014. The municipalities next to it are [[Joroinen]], [[Mikkeli]], [[Pieksämäki]], [[Puumala]], [[Rantasalmi ]] and [[Sulkava]].
