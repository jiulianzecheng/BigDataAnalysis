 The '''monkfish''' (''Lophius piscatorius''), also known as the '''goosefish''', is an angler from the genus ''[[Lophius]]''. It is also known as an [[Angelshark|angel shark]]. It lives in the Atlantic waters of [[North America]]. They have large mouths.
