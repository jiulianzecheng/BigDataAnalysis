 ''' " Offal " ''' is the name for the internal organs or entrails of a dead animal. < ref name= " what " > {{cite web |url=http://www.offalgood.com/what-is-offal |title=What is Offal? |first= |last= |work=offalgood.com |year=2012 |accessdate=13 March 2012 |archive-date=12 March 2012 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120312061513/http://www.offalgood.com/what-is-offal |url-status=dead }} < /ref > 
 
 It means  " eatable parts apart from the muscles " . It includes the [[heart]], [[kidney]]s, [[lung]]s, [[Liver (food)|livers]], [[intestine]]s and [[brain]]s. The word can also be used to cover other parts of the animal including the [[tongue]], tail and feet. The word offal means the parts that fall off when the animal is cut up for meat. < ref name= " what " / >  Offal from birds are called  " giblets " . < ref name= " what " / >  In some parts of the world [[human|people]] eat offal, and in others doing so is considered [[taboo]] or strange.
