 '''Rickenbacker International Corporation''', also known as '''Rickenbacker''' ({{pronEng|ˌrɪkənˈbækɚ}}) [http://www.rickenbacker.com/faq_item.asp?faq_id=2] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20080112083632/http://www.rickenbacker.com/faq_item.asp?faq_id=2 |date=2008-01-12 }}), is an [[United States|American]] [[company]] that makes [[electric guitar]]s.  The company [[invention|invented]] the first electric guitar during the [[1930s]]. The guitars are made at its [[headquarters]] in [[Santa Ana, California|Santa Ana]], [[California]]. Rickenbacker is the largest guitar company to make all of their guitars within the [[United States]].
