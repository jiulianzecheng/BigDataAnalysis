 '''Bellinzona''' ([{{IPA|bɛllin'dzoːna}}] in [[French language|French]] ''Bellinzone'' [{{IPA|bɛlɛ̃'zon}}] and ''Bilitio'' [{{IPA|bi'litsjo}}] in [[Latin]]) is the [[capital city|capital]] of the [[Cantons of Switzerland|Swiss Canton]] of [[Ticino]]. Its [[German language|German]] name ''Bellenz'' [{{IPA|'belents}}] is no longer in use. With about 17.300 people living there, it is the second biggest city of the canton (after [[Lugano]], with about 52.000 people). 
 
 On 2 April 2017 the former municipalities of [[Camorino]], [[Claro, Ticino|Claro]], [[Giubiasco]], [[Gnosca]], [[Gorduno]], [[Gudo]], [[Moleno]], [[Monte Carasso]], [[Pianezzo]], [[Preonzo]], [[Sant'Antonio]] and [[Sementina]] merged into the municipality of Bellinzona.
