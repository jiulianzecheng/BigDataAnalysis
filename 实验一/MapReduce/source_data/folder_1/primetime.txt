'''Primetime''' (also known as '''prime time''') is a timeslot in the [[United States]] on most networks running from 8:00 to 11:00 p.m. [[Eastern Time Zone (North America)|Eastern]] and 7:00 to 10:00 p.m. [[Central Time Zone|Central]] and [[Mountain Time Zone|Mountain]]. It is the most watched timeslot on [[television]]. It is before [[late night television]].
