 '''Pierre de Barrigue de Montvallon''', known as '''Piem''', (12 November 1923 – 12 November 2020) was a French cartoonist. He was born in [[Saint-Étienne]], [[France]]. Many of his works appeared on French newspapers. They mainly appeared for ''[[Le Figaro]]'', ''[[Le Point]]'' and ''[[La Croix]]''. His career began in 1947.  
 
 Piem died in [[Notre-Dame-d'Oé]], France on 12 November 2020, his 97th birthday. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.lefigaro.fr/culture/mort-de-piem-dessinateur-satirique-du-petit-rapporteur-et-du-figaro-20201112|title=Mort de Piem, dessinateur satirique du Petit Rapporteur et du Figaro|date=12 November 2020|work=Le Figaro|language=French}} < /ref >
