 The '''budgerigar''' ([[nickname]]d '''''budgie''','' [[Binomial nomenclature|binomial name]] '''''Melopsittacus undulatus''''') is a small [[parrot]]. It belongs to the [[family (biology)|family]] of [[Old World]] [[Parakeet|parakeets]] (Psittaculidae). Budgerigars are often called ''parakeets'', especially in [[American English]]. The term ''Parakeet'' refers to many types of small parrots with long flat tails. The budgerigar is found in drier parts of [[Australia]].
