 '''Trasquera''' is a ''[[comune]]'' (municipality) in the [[Province of Verbano-Cusio-Ossola]] in the [[Italy|Italian]] region [[Piedmont]]. 
 
 Trasquera is about {{convert|120|km|mi}} northeast of [[Turin]] and about {{convert|35|km|mi}} northwest of [[Verbania]]. It is in the [[Val Divedro]], on the border with [[Switzerland]]. As of 2004, 247 people lived there. It has an area of {{convert|39.3|km2|mi2}}. < ref name= " istat " > All demographics and other statistics: Italian statistical institute [[National Institute of Statistics (Italy)|Istat]]. < /ref > 
 
 Trasquera is next to these municipalities: [[Bognanco]], [[Crevoladossola]], [[Domodossola]], [[Varzo]], [[Zwischbergen]] (Switzerland).
