 '''Toontown''' is a common name for a [[fiction]]al [[anthropomorphism|human-like]] city or town where [[cartoon]] [[fictional character|characters]], known as Toons, live. 
 
 ==''Who Framed Roger Rabbit''== 
 In the 1988 movie ''[[Who Framed Roger Rabbit]]'', Toontown is a cartoonish city near [[Los Angeles]].
