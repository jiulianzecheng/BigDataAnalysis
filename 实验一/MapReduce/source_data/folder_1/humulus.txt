 '''''Humulus''''' (or '''hop''') is a [[flowering plant]]. It is mostly found in the [[northern hemisphere]]. It's [[female]] [[flowers]] are often called ''[[cone]]s''. Hop is used in [[brewery|breweries]] for making [[beer]].
