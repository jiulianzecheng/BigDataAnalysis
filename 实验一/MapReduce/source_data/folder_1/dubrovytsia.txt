 '''Dubrovytsia''' ({{Lang-uk|Дубровиця}}, {{Lang-pl|Dąbrowica}}, {{Lang-ru|Дубровица}}, {{Lang-yi|דומברוביצא}}) is a town in [[Rivne Oblast]], [[Ukraine]]. It is [[capital (political)|administrative center]] of [[Dubrovytsia Raion]], and is the site of the now ruined Jewish [[shtetl]] of Dombrovitza. In 2001, 9,644 people lived there.
