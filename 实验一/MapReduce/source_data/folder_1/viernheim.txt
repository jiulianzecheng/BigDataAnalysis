 '''Viernheim''' is a medium-sized industrial town northeast of [[Mannheim]] and is in the [[Rhine Neckar Area|Rhine-Neckar]] area. About 34,000 people live there which makes it the second-largest town in [[Bergstraße (district)|Bergstraße]] in [[Hesse]], [[Germany]]. 
 
 Since 1994 it has also borne the title ''[[Brundtland Commission|Brundtlandstadt]]'', as it has been taking part in a experimental project on [[energy conservation]].
