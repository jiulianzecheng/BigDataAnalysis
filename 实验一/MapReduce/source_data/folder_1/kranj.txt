 '''Kranj''' ({{lang-de|Krainburg}}) is the third-largest city in [[Slovenia]]. In 2020, 37,941 people lived there. It is about {{convert|20|km|0|abbr=off}} northwest of [[Ljubljana]]. The city is the seat of the [[City Municipality of Kranj]] and of the traditional region of [[Upper Carniola]]. It is an important industrial city with [[electronics]] and [[rubber]] industries.
