'''Buuhoodle''' is a [[city]] in northern [[Somalia]]. The area of [[Dareema Caddo]] in Buuhoodle was the first capital of the [[Daraawiish]]. < ref > Xasuus qor: timelines of Somali history, 1400-2000 - Page 25 < /ref > 
 
 Control over the city has been contested between [[SSC State]], [[Khatumo]] and [[Puntland]].
