 '''Aubessagne''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]] in the [[Hautes-Alpes]] [[Departments of France|department]]. The municipality was created on 1 January 2018 and consists of the former communes of [[Chauffayer]] (the seat), [[Saint-Eusèbe-en-Champsaur]] and [[Les Costes]]. < ref > [https://www.legifrance.gouv.fr/eli/arrete/2017/11/23/INTB1735874A/jo/texte Arrêté préfectoral] 23 November 2017 {{in lang|fr}} < /ref >
