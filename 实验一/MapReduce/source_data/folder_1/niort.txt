 '''Niort''' is a [[Communes of France|''commune'']] in the [[Deux-Sèvres]] [[Departments of France|department]] in the [[Nouvelle-Aquitaine]] [[Regions of France|region]] in western [[France]]. It is a [[Prefectures in France|prefecture]] of the Deux-Sèvres department. It is also the [[Capital city|capital]] of the ''[[Arrondissements of France|arrondissement]]'' of [[Arrondissement of Niort|Niort]]. 
 
 == Geography == 
 Niort is to the southwest of the Deux-Sèvres department on the left side of the [[Sèvre Niortaise]] [[river]]. The city is the main place to get into the '''Marais Poitevin''', a large area of [[marsh]]land in western France. 
 
 Niort is at about {{Convert|41|km|mi|abbr=on }} from [[Parthenay]] and about {{Convert|64|km|mi|abbr=on }} from [[Bressuire]], the two subprefectures of the department. 
 
 The city is also at about {{Convert|187|km|mi|abbr=on }} from [[Bordeaux]], {{Convert|411|km|mi|abbr=on }} from [[Paris]], {{Convert|827|km|mi|abbr=on }} from [[Marseille]] and {{Convert|423|km|mi|abbr=on }} from [[Toulouse]]. < ref name= " annuaire " > {{cite web |work=Annuaire-Mairie.fr |publisher= |title=Ville de Niort |url=http://www.annuaire-mairie.fr/ville-niort.html |language=French |accessdate=21 April 2017}} < /ref > 
 
 The ''commune'' has an area of {{Convert|68.2|km2|sqmi|abbr=on }}. < ref name= " insee-geo " > {{cite web |url=https://www.insee.fr/fr/statistiques/1405599?geo=COM-79191 |title=Commune de Niort (79191) |work=Comparateur de territoire |publisher=Institut national de la statistique et des études économiques - INSEE |format= |language=French |accessdate=21 April 2017 |date= }} < /ref >  Its average [[altitude]] is {{Convert|40|m|ft|abbr=on}}; at the [[City and town halls|city hall]], the altitude is {{Convert|28|m|ft|abbr=on }}. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://www.map-france.com/Niort-79000/ |title=Niort |publisher=Map-France.com |language= |accessdate=21 April 2017 |date= }} < /ref >

 ===Climate=== 
 The climate of Niort is an [[oceanic climate]] with template summers, '''Cfb''' (Marine West Coast Climate) in the [[Köppen climate classification]]. < ref name= " wbase " > {{cite web|work= |publisher=Weatherbase |title=Niort, France - Köppen Climate Classification |url=http://www.weatherbase.com/weather/weather-summary.php3?s=598850 & cityname=Niort%2C+Poitou-Charentes%2C+France & units= |language= |accessdate=21 April 2017}} < /ref > 
 
 The [[average]] amount of [[precipitation]] for the year in Niort is {{Convert|647.7|mm|in|1|abbr=on }}. The month with the most precipitation on average is September with {{Convert|94|mm|in|1|abbr=on }} of precipitation. The month with the least precipitation on average is July with an average of {{Convert|25.4|mm|in|1|abbr=on }}. 
 
 The average temperature for the year in Niort is {{Convert|12.5|°C|°F }}. The warmest month, on average, is July with an average temperature of {{Convert|20.2|°C|°F }}. The coolest month on average is January, with an average temperature of {{Convert|5.5|°C|°F }}.

 The inhabitants of Niort are known, in [[French language|French]], as ''Niortais'' (women: ''Niortaises''). < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.habitants.fr/deux-sevres-79 |title=Deux-Sèvres (79)|publisher=habitants.fr |language=French |accessdate=21 April 2017 |date= }} < /ref >

 == Administration == 
 Niort is the [[Prefectures in France|prefecture]] of the Deux-Sèvres department, the capital of the ''arrondissement'' of [[Arrondissement of Niort|Niort]] and the [[administrative centre]] ({{lang-fr|chef-lieu}}) of three [[Cantons of France|cantons]]: 
 * [[Canton of Niort-1|Niort-1]], with 19,481 inhabitants  < small > (2014) < /small > . 
 * [[Canton of Niort-2|Niort-2]], with 18,806 inhabitants  < small > (2014) < /small > . 
 * [[Canton of Niort-3|Niort-3]], with 20,024 inhabitants  < small > (2014) < /small > . 
 
 It is part of the [[Communes of France|intercommunality]] ''Le Niortais'' ({{lang-fr|Communauté d'agglomération du Niortais}}).
