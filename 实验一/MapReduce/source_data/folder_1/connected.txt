''' " Connected " ''' is an [[electronic music|electronic]] [[pop music|pop song]] by [[Stereo MCs]]. This song was released in September 1992. It was #20 on [[Billboard Hot 100|''Billboard'' Hot 100]] and #18 on UK Top 75 Chart. The song appeared in several [[television program]]s and [[movie]]s. 
 
 The song samples the disco classic song  " Let Me (Let Me be Your Lover) "  by Jimmy Bo Horne. The full length of the song is five minutes and 16 seconds.
