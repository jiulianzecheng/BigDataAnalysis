'''Dhudial''' is a village and [[Union Councils of Pakistan|Union Council]], an administrative subdivision, of [[Chakwal District]] in the [[Punjab (Pakistan)|Punjab Province]] of [[Pakistan]], it is part of [[Chakwal Tehsil]]. < ref > {{Cite web |url=http://www.nrb.gov.pk/lg_election/union.asp?district=6 & dn=Chakwal |title=Tehsils  &  Unions in the District of Chakwal - Government of Pakistan |access-date=2008-07-21 |archive-date=2008-01-24 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20080124031142/http://www.nrb.gov.pk/lg_election/union.asp?district=6 & dn=Chakwal |url-status=dead }} < /ref >  It is one of the largest villages of Chakwal District and is located at 33°3'52N 72°58'24E < ref > [http://www.fallingrain.com/world/PK/4/Dhudial.html Location of Dhudial - Falling Rain Genomics] < /ref >  about 70km south of the capital [[Islamabad]]. 
 
 ==Tribes== 
 
 The following are the majority tribe population. 
 * Awan  (اعوان) 
 * Quresh (قریش) 
 * Rana (انڈین مہاجر) 
 * Ansar (انصار) 
 * Gondal (گوندل) 
 * Sadaat (سادات) 
 * Mughal Kasar (مغل کسر) 
 * Minhas (منہاس) 
 * Bazigar (بازی گر). 
 * [Bhatti] (بھٹی)
