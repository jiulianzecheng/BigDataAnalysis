 '''Brunnadern''' was a [[municipality]] in [[Toggenburg (Wahlkreis)|Toggenburg]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[St. Gallen (canton)|St. Gallen]] in [[Switzerland]]. It was an independent municipality until 1 January 2009, when it joined together with [[Mogelsberg]] and [[St. Peterzell]] to become the municipality called [[Neckertal]]. < ref name=Fusion > [http://www.bfs.admin.ch/bfs/portal/de/index/infothek/nomenklaturen/blank/blank/gem_liste/03.html Amtliches Gemeindeverzeichnis der Schweiz] published by the Swiss Federal Statistical Office {{in lang|de}} accessed 14 January 2010 < /ref >
