'''Patuxai''' ({{lang-lo|ປະຕູໄຊ}}, which means  " Gate of Victory "  or  " Gate of Triumph " ) is a [[monument]] located in [[Vientiane]], [[Laos]]. It is similar to the [[Arc de Triomphe]] in [[Paris]], but it is designed in [[Lao people|Lao]] style.
