 '''Alsike''' is a [[urban areas of Sweden|locality]] in [[Knivsta Municipality]] in [[Uppsala County]] in [[Sweden]]. In 2010, 2,681 people lived there. < ref name=scb / >  Alsike is located circa 50 kilometers north of [[Stockholm]] and 25 kilometers away from the [[Arlanda Airport]]. Alsike is located on the [[Ingegerdsleden]], which is a historic [[pilgrimage]] route between the [[Uppsala Cathedral]] and the [[Stockholm Cathedral]]. The [[Alsike Abbey]] is also located here. [[Trifolium hybridum|Alsike clover]] gets its common name from the settlement. < ref > [http://www.animalrangeextension.montana.edu/articles/forage/species/legumes/alsikeclover.htm Publications  &  Information: Alsike Clover].
