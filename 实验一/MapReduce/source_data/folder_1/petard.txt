A '''petard''' is a  " small bomb used to blow in doors and blow holes in walls "  and comes from the French pétard, which, through Middle French (péter) and Old French (pet), comes from the Latin pedere ( " to break wind " ) or, much more commonly, the slang form  " to fart " .
