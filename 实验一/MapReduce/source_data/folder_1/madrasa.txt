 '''Madrasa''' ({{lang-ar|مدرسة}}, ''madrasa'' [[plural|pl.]] {{lang|ar|مدارس}}, ''madāris'') is the Arabic word for any type of educational institution, [[secular]] or religious (of any religion). It is variously [[Arabic transliteration|transliterated]] as '''madrasah''', '''madarasaa''', '''medresa''', '''madrassa''', '''madraza''', '''madarsa''', '''madrasseh''', etc.
