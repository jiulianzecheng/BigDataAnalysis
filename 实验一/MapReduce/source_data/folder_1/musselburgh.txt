 '''Musselburgh''' ({{lang-sco|Musselburrae}}; {{lang-gd|Baile nam Feusgan}}) is the largest town in [[East Lothian]], [[Scotland]]. It is on the coast of the [[Firth of Forth]], {{convert|5|mi|km|0}} east of [[Edinburgh]] city centre. About 21,100 people live there. 
 
 The [[Battle of Pinkie]] was fought between Scotland and England in 1547.
