 '''Goofy''' is a [[The Walt Disney Company|Disney]] character. He is a dog/hyena-like character and the friend of [[Mickey Mouse]] and [[Donald Duck]]. He is a tall, [[Anthropomorphism|anthropomorphic]] dog/hyena hybrid. He typically wears a turtle neck and vest, with pants, shoes, white gloves, and a tall hat originally designed as a rumpled fedora. 
 
 He has been voiced by [[Bill Farmer]] since 1987.
