'''Ekrejegbe''' is a village in Ughelli South Local Government Area of [[Delta State]], [[Nigeria]], < ref name= " ekrejegbe " > {{cite web|url=http://www.nigeriazipcodes.com/6002/list-of-towns-and-villages-in-ughelli-south-lga|title=List of Towns and Villages in Ughelli South LGA|publisher=Nigeriazipcodes.com}} < /ref >  having boundaries with [[Ekakpamre]]. The people are mostly [[Christians|Christian]] and traditional worshippers. < ref name= " ekrejegbe "  / > 
 One of the streets in Ekrejegbe village is called Ekrogbe Quarters. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.sunnewsonline.com/delta-guber-drama-as-okowa-opens-defence-at-tribunal/|title=Delta guber: Drama, as Okowa opens defence at tribunal|last=Osuyi|first=Paul|date=24 July 2019|accessdate=25 October 2022|publisher=Sun News Online}} < /ref > 
 
 On 17 September 1999, Ekrejegbe was among some Ughievwen communities affected by an oil spillage that resulted in a fire. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://waado.org/Organizations/UNA/EkakpamreFire.html|title=OIL SPILLAGE AND FIRE DISASTER IN FOUR URHOBO COMMUNITIES|date=2 October 1999|accessdate=28 October 2022|publisher=Waado.org|archive-date=27 January 2020|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200127184433/http://waado.org/Organizations/UNA/EkakpamreFire.html|url-status=bot: unknown}} < /ref >
