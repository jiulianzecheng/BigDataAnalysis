 '''Rots''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is found in the [[Regions of France|region]] [[Basse-Normandie]] in the [[Calvados (department)|Calvados]] [[Departments of France|department]] in the northwest of [[France]]. On 1 January 2016, the former communes of [[Lasson, Calvados|Lasson]] and [[Secqueville-en-Bessin]] were merged into Rots. < ref > [https://www.legifrance.gouv.fr/eli/arrete/2015/12/22/INTB1601488A/jo/texte Arrêté préfectoral] 22 December 2015 {{in lang|fr}} < /ref >
