 '''Kaul''' (also spelled '''Koul'''; {{lang-ks|कौल  < small > ([[Devanagari]]) < /small > }}) is the caste used by most of the Kashmiri [[Pandit]]s in [[India]]. They are [[Brahmin]]s who come from [[Kashmir]]. After the protest in 1991 from [[Muslim]]s, all Kashmiri Pandits forcefully left their houses. 
 
 Some famous Kashmiri Pandits are:-

 *[[Jawaharlal Nehru]] 
 *[[Indira Gandhi]] 
 *[[Raaj Kumar]], [[Bollywood]] actor 
 *[[Jeevan (actor)|Jeevan]], Bollywood actor 
 *[[Mani Kaul]], filmmaker
