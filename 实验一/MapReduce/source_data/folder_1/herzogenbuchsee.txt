 '''Herzogenbuchsee''' is a [[municipality]] in the [[administrative district]] of [[Oberaargau (administrative district)|Oberaargau]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Bern (canton)|Bern]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 On 1 January 2008, the former municipality of [[Oberönz]] merged into the municipality of Herzogenbuchsee.
