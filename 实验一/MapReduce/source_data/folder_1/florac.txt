 '''Florac''' is a former [[France|French]] [[Communes of France|commune]], a ''[[Prefectures in France|subprefecture]]'' of the [[Lozère]] [[Departments of France|department]], in the [[Occitanie]] [[Regions of France|region]]. It is also the [[Capital city|capital]] of the ''arrondissement'' of [[Arrondissement of Florac|Florac]]. On 1 January 2016, it was merged into the new commune of [[Florac Trois Rivières]]. < ref > [https://www.legifrance.gouv.fr/eli/arrete/2015/12/2/INTB1530827A/jo/texte Arrêté préfectoral] 2-14 December 2015 < /ref > 
 
 The inhabitants of this city are known, in [[French language|French]], as ''Floracois'' (women: ''Floracoises''). < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.habitants.fr/habitants_florac_48061.html|title=Florac (48400) |publisher=habitants.fr |language=fr |access-date=1 July 2015 }} < /ref > 
 
 ==Geography== 
 Florac is in the valley of the [[Tarnon]] river, a small river that flows through the Lozère department before joining the [[Tarn (river)|Tarn]] river. The ''commune'' is found at the start of the [[Gorges du Tarn]] (a [[canyon]] formed by the Tarn river). 
 
 The city and the Tarnon valley are within the [[Cévennes National Park]] but they are not part of the park. 
 
 The ''commune'' of Florac has an area of {{Convert|29.89|km2|sqmi|abbr=on }} and its average [[altitude]] is {{Convert|542|m|ft|abbr=on}}; at the [[City and town halls|city hall]], the altitude is {{Convert|560|m|ft|abbr=on }}. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://www.map-france.com/Florac-48400/ |title=Florac |publisher=Map-France.com |access-date=1 July 2015 }} < /ref >

 ==Population== 
 With a population of 1,958, < ref name=insee > {{cite web |url=http://www.insee.fr/fr/ppp/bases-de-donnees/recensement/populations-legales/departement.asp?dep=48 & annee=2012 |title=Populations lègales 2012 - 48-Lozère |work= Populations légales 2012 des départements et des collectivités d'outre-mer |publisher=Institut national de la statistique et des études économiques - INSEE |language=fr |access-date=1 July 2015}} < /ref >  its [[population density]] is of {{Pop density|1958|29.89|km2|prec=0|disp=num}} inhabitants/km < sup > 2 < /sup > . 
 
 Florac is the third smallest subprefecture of France, after [[Castellane]] in the [[Alpes-de-Haute-Provence]] department (1,557 inhabitants) and [[Largentière]] in the [[Ardèche]] department (1,789 inhabitants).
