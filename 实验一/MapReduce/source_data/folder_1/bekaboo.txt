 '''''Bekaboo''''' ({{translation|Uncontrollable}}) is an Indian [[fantasy]] television series. It premiered on 18 March 2023 on [[Colors TV]]. < ref > {{Cite web|url=https://www.bollywoodhungama.com/news/bollywood/shalin-bhanot-returns-colors-bekaboo-co-starring-eisha-singh-monalisa/|title=Shalin Bhanot returns to Colors with Bekaboo, co-starring Eisha Singh and Monalisa : Bollywood News - Bollywood Hungama|first=Bollywood|last=Hungama|date=February 23, 2023}} < /ref >  It was produced by [[Ekta Kapoor]] and [[Shobha Kapoor]] under [[Balaji Telefilms]]. < ref > {{Cite web|url=https://www.tribuneindia.com/news/lifestyle/eisha-singh-gets-a-new-avatar-in-fantasy-revenge-drama-bekaboo-483256|title=Eisha Singh gets a new avatar in fantasy revenge drama Bekaboo : The Tribune India}} < /ref >
