'''Ostara''' is one of the eight [[festival]]s held by [[Wicca]]ns every year. It is normally held at the time of the [[vernal equinox]]; that is, around 21 March in the [[Northern Hemisphere]] and 21 September in the [[Southern Hemisphere]]. At Ostara Wiccans celebrate the return of [[Spring]].
