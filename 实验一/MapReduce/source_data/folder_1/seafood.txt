 '''Seafood''' is [[food]] made from [[fish]] or other [[sea]] animals such as [[shrimp]] and [[lobster]]s. The [[harvest]]ing (collecting) of seafood is called [[fishing]]. 
 [[File:Seafood stall.jpg|thumb|Seafood stall, Bolton Market]] 
 Seafood is rich in [[protein]] and is usually thought as [[health]]y food.
