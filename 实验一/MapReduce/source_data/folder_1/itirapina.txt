 '''Itirapina''' is an [[Brazil]]ian municipality in the state of [[São Paulo (state)|São Paulo]]. 18,387 people lived here in 2020 and its area is 565 & nbsp;km < sup > 2 < /sup > . < ref > [http://cod.ibge.gov.br/23A5F Instituto Brasileiro de Geografia e Estatística] < /ref >  The elevation is 770 m.
