A '''clinometer''' is an instrument that measures vertical slopes, usually the angle between the ground or the observer and a tall object. The name  " clinometer "  comes from the [[Greek language|Greek]] words  " κλίνω "  (klino), meaning to slope or lean, and  " μέτρο "  (metro), meaning  " to measure " .
