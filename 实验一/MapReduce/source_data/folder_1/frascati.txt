 '''Frascati''' is an [[Italy|Italian]] city in [[Lazio]], on the [[Alban Hills]]. It is {{convert|20|km|mi|abbr=on}} southeast of [[Rome]]. About 22,500 people live there. Frascati is known for the [[white wine]] with the [[Frascati (wine)|same name]].
