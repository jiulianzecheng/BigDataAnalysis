 '''Leontica''' is a village and municipality in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Ticino]], [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 In 2004 the municipality was merged with the other, neighboring municipalities [[Castro, Ticino|Castro]], [[Corzoneso]], [[Dongio]], [[Largario]], [[Lottigna]], [[Marolta]], [[Ponto Valentino]] and [[Prugiasco]] to form a new and larger municipality of [[Acquarossa]].
