 '''Kimbra Lee Johnson''' (born [[27 March]] [[1990]]), known as '''Kimbra''', is a [[New Zealand]] singer, musician, and singer-songwriter who lives in [[Melbourne]], [[Australia]]. Kimbra's musical style is inspired from many different styles- [[electropop]], [[R & B]], [[jazz]] and [[Soul music|soul]]. Her first collection of songs, ''Vows'',was released in Australia in 2011.  
 
 Kimbra is featured in the 2012 single  " [[Somebody That I Used to Know]] "  by [[Gotye]]. 
 On 10 February 2013, she and Gotye won two awards at the 2013 Grammy Awards for  " Somebody That I Used To Know " .
