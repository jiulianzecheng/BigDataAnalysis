 '''Campinas''' ([[IPA]]: {{IPA|kɐ̃ːˈpinɐs}}) is a [[Brazil]]ian city in the state of [[São Paulo (state)|São Paulo]]. In [[2005]], 1,045,706 people lived there. It is about {{convert|90|km|abbr=off}} from the city of [[São Paulo]]. Campinas has an area of {{convert|795697|km2|abbr=off}}.
