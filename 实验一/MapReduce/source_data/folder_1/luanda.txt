 '''Luanda''' is the [[Capital (city)|capital]] and the largest city of [[Angola]]. Its former name was '''Loanda'''. It is a main [[seaport]] of the country on the [[Atlantic Ocean]]. In 1995, about 3 million people lived there. 
 
 It is a center of manufacturing, but very destroyed because of a [[Angolan Civil War|long civil war]]. 
 
 It was founded by [[Portugal|Portuguese]] in [[1575]] as '''São Paulo de Luanda''' and has been the administrative center of Angola since [[1627]] (except for [[1640]]–[[1648]]). When Angola got independence in [[1975]], it became the capital of Angola.
