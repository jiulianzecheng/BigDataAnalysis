 '''Fridingen''' is a [[town]] of the district [[Tuttlingen (district)|Tuttlingen]] in the [[States of Germany|state]] of [[Baden-Württemberg]], [[Germany]]. The town is about {{convert|10|km|abbr=off}} east of [[Tuttlingen]], and {{convert|23|km|abbr=off}} west of [[Sigmaringen]]. It is on the [[Danube]] river.
