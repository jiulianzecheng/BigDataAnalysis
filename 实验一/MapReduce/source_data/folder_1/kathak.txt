 '''Kathak''' is a classical northern [[India]]n traditional [[dance]] style. It is one of the classical ten styles. The other nine are: [[Bharatanatyam]], [[Kuchipudi]], [[Odissi]], [[Manipuri]], [[Cchau]], [[Kathakali]], [[Satriya]], [[Yaksagana]] and [[Bhagavata Mela]].
