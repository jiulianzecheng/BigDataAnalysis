 '''Seynod''' is a former [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is in [[Auvergne-Rhône-Alpes]] in the [[Haute-Savoie]] [[Departments of France|department]] in southeast [[France]]. On 1 January 2017, it was merged into the commune of [[Annecy]]. < ref > [https://www.legifrance.gouv.fr/eli/arrete/2016/7/14/INTB1622452A/jo/texte Arrêté préfectoral] 14 July 2016 {{in lang|fr}} < /ref >
