 }}'''''Bangerz''''' is the fourth [[studio album]] by [[Americans|American]] [[singer]] [[Miley Cyrus]]. It was released on September 30, 2013 by [[RCA Records]].  Cyrus says the album is  " [[Southern hip hop|dirty south hip-hop]] " . The album is a change in sound from [[teen pop]] and [[country music]] Cyrus had played before. She worked with [[Mike Will Made It]] and [[Hip hop music|hip hop]] producers including [[Pharrell Williams]], Oren Yoel, Rami Samir Afuni, [[Dr. Luke]], and Michael McHenry. The album has other people singing with Cyrus. They include [[Britney Spears]], [[Big Sean]], [[French Montana]], [[Future (rapper)|Future]], [[Ludacris]], and [[Nelly]].
