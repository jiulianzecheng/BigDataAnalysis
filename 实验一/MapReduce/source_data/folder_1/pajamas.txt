 '''Pajamas''' ('''pyjamas''' in the [[United Kingdom]]) are loose [[clothes]] that people wear while they are sleeping. Pyjamas usually include [[trousers]] and a shirt. Pyjamas are often made of [[cotton]]. They are usually worn without [[underwear]]. The word comes from {{lang-hi|पजामा}}, Pajāmā; {{lang-fa|پايجامه}}. Pajamas are often worn without underwear.
