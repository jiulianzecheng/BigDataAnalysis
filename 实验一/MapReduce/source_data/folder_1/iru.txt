 '''Irina Khechanovi''' (born 3 December 2000), known by her stage name '''Iru''', is a [[Georgia (country)|Georgian]] singer. She was a member of the girlband Candy, who won the [[Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2011]]. < ref name= " Eurovision " > {{cite web|date=2 February 2023|title=Georgia: Iru Khechanovi wins ticket to Liverpool 2023|url=https://eurovision.tv/story/georgia-iru-khechanovi-wins-ticket-liverpool-2023|url-status=live|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20230202204546/https://eurovision.tv/story/georgia-iru-khechanovi-wins-ticket-liverpool-2023|archive-date=2 February 2023|access-date=3 February 2023|website=Eurovision.tv|publisher=[[European Broadcasting Union|EBU]]|language=en}} < /ref > 
 
 Iru later represented Georgia at the [[Eurovision Song Contest 2023]] with the song  " Echo " . She placed 12th in the second semi-final and did not qualify for the final.
