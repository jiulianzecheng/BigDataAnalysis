 '''''Nanuqsaurus''''' ('meaning [[polar bear]] lizard') is a [[genus]] of  [[theropod]] [[dinosaur]]. < ref > Fiorillo A.R  &  Tykoski R.S. 2014. ''A diminutive new tyrannosaur from the top of the world''. PLOS One. [http://journals.plos.org/plosone/article?id=10.1371/journal.pone.0091287] < /ref >  It is only known from one specimen, DMNH 21461, a partial [[skull]] roof found in [[Alaska]]. This dinosaur may grown to about 6 m (~20 ft) long, half the size of ''[[Tyrannosaurus]]''. 
 
 The skull would have been about 50 cm (1.65 ft) when complete.
