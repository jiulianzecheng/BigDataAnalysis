 '''Tweety''' (or '''Tweety Bird''') is a fictional [[character]] from ''[[Looney Tunes]]''. He is a [[yellow]] [[canary]]. He has also appeared in [[comic books]]. He was voiced by [[Mel Blanc]]. He is mainly known for saying “I tought I taw a puddy tat”, which is a babyish way of saying that he saw a [[cat]].
