 The '''illustration''' is a [[picture]] which tries to get the person looking at it to pay more attention to the subject than to the art. Illustrations can be in the form of a [[drawing]], [[painting]], [[photograph]] or other work of [[art]].  
 
 Illustrations often have one of two purposes. One is to give light to the subject. The other purpose is to [[wikt:decorate|decorate]] an informational text (such as a [[newspaper]] or [[article]]).
