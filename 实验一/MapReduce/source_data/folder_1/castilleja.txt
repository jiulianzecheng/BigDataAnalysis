 '''''Castilleja''''', commonly known as '''Indian paintbrush''' or '''prairie-fire''', is a [[genus]] of about 200 species of [[Annual plant|annual]] and [[Perennial plant|perennial]] [[herbaceous plant]]s native to the west of the [[Americas]] from [[Alaska]] south to the [[Andes]], northern [[Asia]], and one species as far west as the [[Kola Peninsula]] in northwestern Russia. 
 
 ==Symbolism== 
 ''[[Castilleja linariifolia]]'' is the state flower of [[Wyoming]]. < ref > Wyoming Statute 8-3-104  [http://legisweb.state.wy.us/statutes/statutes.aspx?file=titles/Title8/Title8.htm (Title 8)] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20080511115306/http://legisweb.state.wy.us/statutes/statutes.aspx?file=titles%2FTitle8%2FTitle8.htm# |date=2008-05-11 }} < /ref >
