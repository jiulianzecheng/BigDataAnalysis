'''Chronophilia''' is a form of [[paraphilia]] where someone is sexually attracted to a particular age group or at least has a sexual preference for them. It is normally restricted to specific age groups. 
 
 == Chronophilia Types == 
 
 === Attraction to [[Child|children]] === 
 '''''Nepiophilia''''' (sometimes called '''''Infantophilia''''') is a sub-type of pedophilia describing the sexual attraction to pre-[[Puberty|pubescent]] children which is restricted to [[Baby|infancy]] (referring to [[Baby|babies]]/[[Toddler|toddlers]] ranging from ages 1-4. 
 
 '''[[Pedophilia]]''': is the sexual attraction to pre-[[Puberty|pubescent]] children which are [[Child|children]] who have not begun [[puberty]] (Or) (Usually under 15 years old). 
 
 === Attraction to [[Adolescence|adolescents]] === 
 '''''[[Hebephilia]]''''': is the sexual attraction to early adolescent ([[Puberty|pubescent]]) youth (ranging from ages 11 to 14). Due to the varying onset of puberty, there is overlap between pedophilia and hebephilia. 
 
 '''''[[Ephebophilia]]''''': is the sexual attraction to post-pubescent mid-to-late [[Teenager|teens]] (ranging from ages 15 to 19). 
 
 === Attraction to [[Adult|adults]] === 
 '''''[[Teleiophilia]]''''': is the sexual attraction to young [[Adult|adults]]. (Ranging from the early 20s to late 30s) 
 
 '''''Mesophilia''''': is the sexual attraction to [[Middle age|middle-aged]] adults. (Ranging from early 40s to 60s) 
 
 '''''Gerontophilia''''': is the sexual attraction to [[Old age|old people]]. (From ages 65 and older) 
 
 ==== Both ranges ==== 
 '''''Pedohebephilia''''': is the proposed sexual attraction to both pre-[[Puberty|pubescent]] children and early adolescent youth. 
 
 '''''Hebe-ephebophilia''''': is the attraction to both early adolescent youth and mid to late adolescent youth.
