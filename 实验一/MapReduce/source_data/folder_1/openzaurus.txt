 '''OpenZaurus''' is a [[Debian]]-based embedded [[Linux distribution]] for the [[Sharp Corporation|Sharp]] [[Zaurus]] personal mobile tool [[Personal Digital Assistant|PDA]]. 
 
 == Status == 
 On April 26, 2007, it was announced < ref > {{cite web |url=http://www.openzaurus.org/wordpress/2007/04/26/openzaurus-time-is-over-long-live-angstrom/ |title=OpenZaurus time is over - long live Ångström |accessdate=2007-04-29 |last=Juszkiewicz |first=Marcin |date=2007-04-26 |archive-date=2013-02-28 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130228065241/http://www.openzaurus.org/wordpress/2007/04/26/openzaurus-time-is-over-long-live-angstrom/ |url-status=dead }} < /ref >  that the OpenZaurus project was over.  Future development efforts are to focus on the [[Ångström distribution]] for [[embedded systems]].
