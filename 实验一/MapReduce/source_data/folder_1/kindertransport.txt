'''Kindertransport''' is the [[German language|German]] word for children's transport. It was used during [[World War II]] and a group that rescued refugee children during [[the Holocaust]]. 
 
 [[Geertruida Wijsmuller-Meijer]] helped more than 10,000 jewish children to escape from Germany to Great Britain and other countries.
