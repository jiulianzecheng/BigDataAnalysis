A '''psychiatrist''' is a medical [[Medical doctor|doctor]] who specializes in [[psychiatry]].  They treat people with [[mental illness]]. Psychiatrists can prescribe [[Psychiatry#Prescribing Psychiatric Medications|psychiatric medication]]. 
 
 ==Education== 
 In the [[United States]], psychiatrists train by  going to [[university]] first, then [[medical school]] for 4 years. After medical school, they must complete a [[Residency (medicine)|residency]]. When they graduate, they get to use the initials [[Doctor of Medicine|M.D.]] or [[Doctor of Osteopathic Medicine|D.O.]] after their names.  Next, these doctors specialize by training for 3 or 4 years in Psychiatry. In other countries psychiatrists train in similar ways but may not have to go to a separate university and medical school.
