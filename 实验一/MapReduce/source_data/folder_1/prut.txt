 '''Prut''', or '''Pruth''', ({{lang-uk|Прут}}) is a river in [[Eastern Europe]]. It is {{convert|953|km|abbr=off}} long. It flows to the southeast to join the [[Danube]] river near [[Reni, Ukraine|Reni]].  This is east of [[Galaţi]].  
 
 ==Towns== 
 These towns can be found along the Prut River, from source to mouth: [[Delatyn]], [[Kolomyia]], [[Sniatyn]], [[Chernivtsi]], [[Novoselytsia]], [[Darabani]], [[Ungheni]], [[Cahul]].
