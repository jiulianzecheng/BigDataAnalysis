 '''Dough''' is a thick, malleable and sometimes elastic paste made out of [[flour]] by mixing it with a small amount of water. Dough can be used to make [[bread]], [[pastry|pastries]], [[cookie]]s, [[muffin]]s, and other foods. 
 
 In many parts of central [[India]], people use the quick method of making an instant roasted dough ball or baati. Flat, [[yeast|unleavened]] breads known as [[roti]], lavash, yufka, [[matzo]], and [[tortilla]] are used in many parts of the world today. 
 
 [[yeast|Leavened]] dough, made from dry, ground grains mixed with water and [[yeast]], is used all over the world. It includes all kinds of breads made from [[wheat]], [[maize]], [[rice]] and other grains or similar crops used today in the world. 
 
 Fried dough foods are common in many cultures. In the United States,  " elephant ears "  is a common name for fried dough. 
 
 In [[slang]], dough is [[money]].
