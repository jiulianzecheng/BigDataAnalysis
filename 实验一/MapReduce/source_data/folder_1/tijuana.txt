 '''Tijuana''' ({{IPAc-en|t|iː|ə|ˈ|w|ɑː|n|ə}} {{respell|tee-ə|WAH|nə}} or {{IPAc-en|t|iː|ˈ|w|ɑː|n|ə}}; {{IPA-es|tiˈxwana|lang}}) is a city in [[Mexico]]. It is in the north of the country and across the border from [[California]] in the [[United States]]. It is the largest city in [[Baja California (state)|Baja California]]. Tijuana is well known destination for [[US]] [[Tourist]]s.
