 '''Keuruu''' is a town and [[municipality]] in [[Central Finland]]. It is 60 & nbsp;km to the west of [[Jyväskylä]]. There were more than 10,300 people living there in January 2014. More than 10% of the area is water. Keuruu is next to the municipalities of [[Jämsä]], [[Multia]], [[Mänttä-Vilppula]], [[Petäjävesi]], [[Virrat]] and [[Ähtäri]].
