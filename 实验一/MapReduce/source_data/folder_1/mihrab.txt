 The '''Mihrab''' ({{lang-ar|محراب pl. محاريب}}) is a [[niche (architecture)|niche]] in the wall of a [[mosque]]. It indicates the ''[[qibla]]'' (the direction of the [[Kaaba]] in [[Mecca]], the direction that [[Muslim]]s should face when praying). The wall in which a ''mihrab'' appears is the  " ''qibla'' wall.
