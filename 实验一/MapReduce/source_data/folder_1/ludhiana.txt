'''Ludhiana''' is a city in the [[India]]n state of [[Punjab (India)|Punjab]]. The city has 1,618,879 people living in it as per the 2011 census. According to the [[World Bank Group]], Ludhiana is the best city in India to do business. It was founded in 1489 by [[Sultan]] [[Sikandar Lodi]] of the [[Islam]]ic [[Delhi Sultanate]].
