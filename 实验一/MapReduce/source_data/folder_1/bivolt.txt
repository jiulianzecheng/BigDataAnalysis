 == Biography and career == 
 At the age of 15, he joined in rhyming battles in São Paulo. He released the first debut album Bivolt which marked a new moment in his career before releasing the album where he wrote three of his own compositions  " Lágrimas " ,  " Entre Tu e Meu Som " ,  " Olha Pra Mim " . were important songs to win over the public. < ref > {{Cite web|last=Mascari|first=Felipe|date=2021-02-28|title=Bivolt, empoderada e sobrevivente: 'O rap é tudo o que sou e conquistei'|url=https://www.redebrasilatual.com.br/cultura/2021/02/bivolt-empoderamento-sobrevivencia-rap/|access-date=2022-08-09|website=Rede Brasil Atual|language=pt-BR}} < /ref >  In July 2013, she started releasing footage from her live performances. In 2016, she received even more popularity when she recorded a song with fellow Brazilian rapper Cafuzo. In [[2018|September 2018]], he signed with Som Livre. 
 
 In 2020, she won the RAP TV National Award for Best Female Artist, < ref > {{Cite web|title=“Espero ainda ser a primeira mulher a fazer muitas coisas, mas não a última”, revela Bivolt após representar o rap brasileiro no SXSW 2022|url=https://glamour.globo.com/lifestyle/noticia/2022/03/espero-ainda-ser-a-primeira-mulher-a-fazer-muitas-coisas-mas-nao-a-ultima-revela-bivolt-apos-representar-o-rap-brasileiro-no-sxsw-2022.ghtml|access-date=2022-08-08|website=Glamour|language=pt-br}} < /ref >  the awards ceremony was broadcast virtually. < ref name= " rollings " > {{Cite web|last=Internet (amdb.com.br)|first=AMDB|date=2020-12-14|title=BK’, Bivolt e mais: Veja a lista de vencedores do Prêmio Nacional RAP TV|url=https://rollingstone.uol.com.br/noticia/bk-bivolt-e-mais-veja-lista-de-vencedores-do-premio-nacional-rap-tv/|access-date=2022-08-08|website=Rolling Stone|language=pt-br}} < /ref >  In 2018, she was a guest on the TV show Cultura Manos e Minas where she performed singing the release of the song  " Olha Pra Mim " .  < ref > {{Citation|title=Olha pra mim {{!}} Bivolt|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=D2gskiZc36w|language=pt-BR|access-date=2022-08-09}} < /ref >
