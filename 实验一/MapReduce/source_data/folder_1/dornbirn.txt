 '''Dornbirn''' is the largest city in [[Vorarlberg]], [[Austria]]. About 48,000 people are living there (2006). 
 
 Dornbirn has six districts named Markt, Hatlerdorf, Oberdorf, Haselstauden, Rohrbach and Schoren.
