 '''Tuntange''' ({{lang-lb|Téinten}}, {{lang-de|Tüntingen}}) is a former [[Communes of Luxembourg|commune]] in [[Luxembourg]]. It was part of the [[Mersch (canton)|canton of Mersch]]. 
 
 On 1 January 2018, the commune was merged with [[Boevange-sur-Attert]] to form the new commune of [[Helperknapp]].
