 '''Chhattisgarh''' is a state within the [[Republic of India]]. It occupies {{convert|52199|mi2}}. In traditional Indian geography it falls under the [[East India]]n zone. 
 
 The capital at present is [[Raipur]] which would change to 'Naya Raipur' near Raipur. It has eighteen [[district]]s: Raigarh, Bilaspur, Bijapur, Korba, Durg, Janjgir, Raipur, Koria, Sarguja, Bastar, Dantewada, Narayanpur, Rajnandgaon, Kanker, Mahasamund, Dhamtari, Jashpur and Kabirdham (Kawardha). 
 The main [[language]]s spoken are [[Hindi]] and Chhattisgarhi. The main [[religion]] is [[Hindu]].
