 '''Pielavesi''' is a municipality in [[Northern Savonia]], [[Finland]]. As of 28 February 2023, 4,137 people lived there. Neighbouring municipalities are [[Iisalmi]], [[Keitele]], [[Kiuruvesi]], [[Maaninka]], [[Pihtipudas]], [[Pyhäjärvi]] and [[Tervo]].
