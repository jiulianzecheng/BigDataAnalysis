 '''''Rubbadubbers''''' is a [[British]] [[stop motion]] [[children's television series]] created by [[Peter Curtis]] and produced by [[HIT Entertainment]] and [[HOT Animation]]. it focuses on seven friendly anthropomorphic bath toys as they tell stories. Tubb, a pink frog, is the leader of the Rubbadubbers. The series was broadcast in the United Kingdom on [[BBC Two|BBC2]] and [[CBeebies]] from 2002 to 2005. In the [[United States]], the series was broadcast on [[Nickelodeon]], [[Nick Jr. Channel|Noggin]] and [[Universal Kids|PBS Kids Sprout]].
