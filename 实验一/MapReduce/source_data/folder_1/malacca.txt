 '''Melaka''' (formerly known as '''Malacca''') ({{lang-zh|马六甲，馬六甲}}) is a state in [[Malaysia]] that is on the southern part of [[Malay Peninsula]]. The [[Strait of Malacca]] is to the south. The capital city of Malacca is [[Malacca City]]. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.tourism.gov.my/en/destinations/state.asp?state=melakan|title=Melaka: Historical City|work=[[Tourism Malaysia]]|accessdate=October 4, 2010}}{{Dead link|date=January 2022 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }} < /ref >
