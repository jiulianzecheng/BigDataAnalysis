 '''Goldhanger''' is a village and [[civil parish]] in [[Maldon District]], [[Essex]], England. In 2001 there were 647 people living in Goldhanger. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.ons.gov.uk/help/localstatistics|title=Local statistics - Office for National Statistics|website=www.ons.gov.uk}} < /ref >  Goldhanger has a pub called The Chequers Inn. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.thechequersgoldhanger.co.uk/|title=Home - The Chequers is a classic 15th Century Pub  &  Restaurant located in Goldhanger near Maldon in Essex.|website=www.thechequersgoldhanger.co.uk}} < /ref >  It is located on the north bank of the [[Blackwater Estuary]]. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.churchside1.plus.com/Goldhanger-past/|title=Goldhanger - in the past|website=www.churchside1.plus.com}} < /ref >
