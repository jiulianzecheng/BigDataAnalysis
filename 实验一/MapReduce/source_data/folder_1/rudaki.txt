'''Abu Abdullah Jafar ibn Mohammad ibn Hakim ibn Abdurrahman ibn Adam Rudaki Samarghandi''' ([[859]] - c. [[941]]) ({{lang-fa|ابوعبدالله جعفربن محمد بن حکیم بن عبدالرحمن بن آدم رودکی سمرقندی}}) or '''Rudaki''' was a [[Persian people|Persian]] [[poet]]. He was born in [[Panjakent]], [[Tajikistan]] and lived in [[Samarghand]]. Rudaki translated the old Indian fable book [[Panchatantra]] into Persian verse. His poems are collected in [[diwan]].
