 '''''Eudibamus''''' is an [[Extinction|extinct]] [[genius]] of biped [[Bolosauridae|bolosaurid]] (a reptile) from the early [[Permian]] some 290 million years ago of [[Germany]]. ''Eudibamus'' is claimed to be the first [[Bipedalism|bipedal]] [[vertebrate]].
