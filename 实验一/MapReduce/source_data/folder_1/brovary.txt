 '''Brovary''' ({{lang-uk|Бровари}}, {{lang-pl|Browary}}; before 1969 known as '''Brovari''') is a city in [[Kyiv Oblast]] (Region) in northern [[Ukraine]], an eastern suburb of the country's capital, [[Kyiv]]. It is the [[Capital city|administrative centre]] of [[Brovary Raion]] ([[Raion|district]]).
