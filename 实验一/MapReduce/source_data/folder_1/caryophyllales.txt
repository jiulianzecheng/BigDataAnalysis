 '''Caryophyllales''' is a [[flowering plant]] [[Order (biology)|order]] that includes the [[cactus|cacti]], [[Carnation|carnations]], [[amaranth]]s, [[Aizoaceae|ice plants]], and most [[carnivorous plant]]s.  Many members are [[succulent plant|succulent]], having fleshy [[Plant stem|stems]] or [[Leaf|leaves]].
