 '''Heroldstatt''' is a [[municipality]] of the district of [[Alb-Donau (district)|Alb-Donau]] in the [[States of Germany|state]] of [[Baden-Württemberg]], [[Germany]]. As of 31 December 2018, 2,857 people lived there.
