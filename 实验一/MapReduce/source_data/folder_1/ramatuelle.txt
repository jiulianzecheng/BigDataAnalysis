 '''Ramatuelle''' ([[Provençal dialect|Provençal]]: ''Ramatuela'') is a [[Communes of France|commune]] in the [[Var (department)|Var]] [[Departments of France|department]] of the [[Provence-Alpes-Côte d'Azur]] [[Regions of France|region]] in Southeastern [[France]]. In 2016, it had a population of 2,077.
