 '''''Kitbull''''' is a 2019 American [[animated]] [[short movie]] directed and written by [[Rosana Sullivan]], produced by [[Pixar|Pixar Animation Studios]], and made by [[Walt Disney Studios Motion Pictures]].  
 
 It is the third movie in Pixar's  " [[SparkShorts]] "  program. It is about stray [[kitten]] and an abused [[pit bull]], who form an unlikely friendship. The short premiered at [[El Capitan Theatre]] on January 18, 2019, before being released on [[YouTube]] on February 18, 2019.  
 
 The short was also released on [[Disney+]] on November 12, 2019. 
 
 The movie had strong positive reviews.
