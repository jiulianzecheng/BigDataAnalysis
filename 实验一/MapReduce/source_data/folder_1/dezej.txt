 '''Dezej''' ({{lang-ku|Dizec}}, {{lang-fa|دزج}}) is a city in the Kurdistan province of [[Iran]]. Its people speak [[Kurdish language|Kurdish]]. They are followers of [[Islam]]. At the 2006 census, its population was 2,292, in 559 families. < ref > {{IranCensus2006|12}} < /ref >
