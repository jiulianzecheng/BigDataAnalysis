 The '''caduceus''' ({{lang-la|cādūceus}}; from {{lang-el|κηρύκειον}}, ''kērykeion'') is the staff of the messenger god [[Hermes]] in [[Greek mythology]]. It is also called the  " herald's wand " . The caduceus appeared as a golden staff with wings and a pair of [[snake|serpents]] wrapped around it.
