 '''Gauteng''' ({{IPAc-en|x|aʊ|ˈ|t|ɛ|ŋ}} {{respell|khow|TENG}}; < ref > [https://en.oxforddictionaries.com/definition/gauteng Gauteng] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181116113301/https://en.oxforddictionaries.com/definition/gauteng |date=2018-11-16 }}, Oxford Dictionaries. Retrieved 09-10-2018 < /ref >  {{lang-tn|Gauteng}}; [[Sotho language|Sotho]]: ''Gauteng'' ; {{lang-zu|eGoli}}; Tsonga: ''Gauteng/eXilungwini;'' [[Southern Ndebele language|Ndebele]], {{lang-xh|iRhawuti}}; {{lang-nr|I-Gauteng}}; {{lang-ve|Gauteng}}) is a [[Provinces of South Africa|province]] of [[South Africa]]. It is the smallest province by area, though it also has the greatest number of people. Its [[capital city|capital]] is [[Johannesburg]], the largest city in South Africa. It also hosts South Africa's [[Executive (government)|executive]] capital, [[Pretoria]]. 
 
 == Geography == 
 The province borders the provinces of [[North West (South African province)|North West]] (west), the [[Free State]] (south), [[Limpopo]] (north), and [[Mpumalanga]] (east).
