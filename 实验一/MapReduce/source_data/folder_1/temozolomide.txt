'''Temozolomide''' is a [[Chemotherapy|chemotheraputic]] drug used in the treatment of brain cancer, specifically [[Glioblastoma multiforme]] and [[Anaplastic astrocytoma]]. In patients with these two types of brain cancer, temozolomide has been shown to extend survival. It is currently an experimental [[treatment]] in [[Metastatic melanoma]] patients.
