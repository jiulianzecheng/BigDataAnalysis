 '''Omnitrans''' is a [[public transit]] agency in [[Southern California]]. It provides [[bus]] service to the [[San Bernardino Valley]] area. In 1976, the transit agency was created by the Joint Powers Agreement (JPA), under the amended Government Code of the State of California.
