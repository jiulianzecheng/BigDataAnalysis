'''Oceanography''' (also called '''oceanology''' or '''marine science''') is the study of the [[ocean]], its [[properties]] and its [[characteristics]]. This can include, and is not limited to, studying the [[Marine biology|marine life]], the [[geography]] of the [[Seabed|ocean floor]], and the water itself. Many sciences are useful in oceanography, so it is a [[Interdisciplinarity|multidisciplinary]] field. Oceanographers spend much time on boats.  
 == Sub-Categories == 
 The study of oceanography may be divide branches: 
 * '''[[Marine biology]]''' or '''biological oceanography''' 
 * '''[[Chemical oceanography]]''' or '''marine chemistry''' 
 * '''[[Marine geology]]''' or '''geological oceanography''' 
 * '''[[Physical oceanography]]''' 
 * '''[[Marine engineering]]'''
