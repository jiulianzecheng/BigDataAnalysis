'''''Triciti''''' ([[polish language|Polish]] Trójmiasto) is the unofficial name of an [[urban area]] with 3 [[city|cities]]: [[Gdańsk]], [[Gdynia]] and [[Sopot]] in [[Pomeranian Voivodeship]]. Tricity is a big centre for [[tourism]].
