'''Oddanchtram''' is a town in the [[Dindigul District]] of [[Tamil Nadu]]. It is known as the vegetable city of Tamil Nadu. Plants grown here are [[onion]],[[maize]], [[tomato]], [[chilli]], [[cauliflower]], [[bean]]s and other vegetables. The vegetables are exported to nearby states.
