 '''Losser''' ([[Low Saxon]]: ''Losker / Loster / Lösser'') is a municipality and village in the province of [[Overijssel]], the Netherlands. About 23,000 people were living there in 2021. It lies in [[Twente]] region and borders [[Germany]]. 
 
 Losser is a rural municipality with a mainly [[Roman Catholic]] population. 
 
 The Tankenberg, which is in Losser, is the highest hill of the province of Overijssel and has an altitude of 85 m.
