 {{nihongo|'''''Cinnamoroll'''''|シナモロール|Shinamorōru|lead=yes}} is a franchise created by [[Sanrio]] in 2001. The [[Fictional character|character]] is from [[Miyiki okumara]]. The main character, Cinnamoroll is a young male white puppy with long ears, blue eyes, pink cheeks, a small plump body, and a tail that resembles a [[Cinnamon roll|cinnamon roll]]. He starred his own [[manga]], < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.viz.com/content/viz-media-launches-fun-adorable-kids%E2%80%99-manga-fluffy-fluffy-cinnamoroll|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120430042025/http://www.viz.com/content/viz-media-launches-fun-adorable-kids%E2%80%99-manga-fluffy-fluffy-cinnamoroll|archive-date=30 April 2012|url-status=dead|title=Viz Media Launches Fun, Adorable Kids' Manga Fluffy, Fluffy Cinnamoroll  & #124; Viz Media|publisher=Viz.com|date=12 January 2012|access-date=29 April 2012}} < /ref >  an [[anime]] [[movie]] and various short [[Animation|animations]].
