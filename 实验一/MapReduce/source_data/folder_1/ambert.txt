 '''Ambert''' is a [[Communes of France|''commune'']] in the [[Puy-de-Dôme]] [[Departments of France|department]] in the [[Auvergne-Rhône-Alpes]] [[Regions of France|region]] in central-southern [[France]]. 
 
 It is a [[Prefectures in France|subprefecture]] of the department and the [[Capital (city)|capital]] of the ''arrondissement'' of [[Arrondissement of Ambert|Ambert]]. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 Ambert is in the southeast of the [[Puy-de-Dôme]] department, at {{Convert|37|km|mi|abbr=on }} to the south of [[Thiers, Puy-de-Dôme|Thiers]], {{Convert|39|km|mi|abbr=on }} to the east of [[Issoire]] and {{Convert|57|km|mi|abbr=on }} to the southeast of [[Clermont-Ferrand]]. 
 
 The ''commune'' is in the [[plain]] of Ambert, surrounded by [[mountain]]s: the ''Monts du Forez'' to the east and the ''Monts du Livradois'' to the west. The town is along the [[Dore (river)|Dore]], a [[tributary]] of the [[Allier (river)|Allier river]]. 
 
 Ambert has an area of {{Convert|60.5|km2|sqmi|abbr=on }}, < ref name= " insee-geo " > {{cite web |url=https://www.insee.fr/fr/statistiques/1405599?geo=COM-63003 |title=Commune d’Ambert (63003) |work=Comparateur de territoire |publisher=Institut national de la statistique et des études économiques - INSEE |language=fr |access-date=24 October 2017 }} < /ref >  and its average [[altitude]] is {{Convert|940|m|ft|abbr=on}}; at the [[City and town halls|city hall]], the altitude is {{Convert|560|m|ft|abbr=on }}. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://www.map-france.com/Ambert-63600/ |title=City of Ambert |publisher=Map-France.com |access-date=24 October 2017 }} < /ref >

 ==Administration== 
 Ambert is a [[Prefectures in France|subprefecture]] of the Puy-de-Dôme department. It is also the capital of the ''arrondissement'' of [[Arrondissement of Ambert|Ambert]] and the [[administrative centre]] ({{lang-fr|chef-lieu}}) of the canton of [[Canton of Ambert|Ambert]], formed 30 ''communes'', including Ambert, with 18,178 inhabitants  < small > (2014) < /small > . 
 
 It is part of the [[Communes of France|intercommunality]] ''Ambert Livradois Forez''.
