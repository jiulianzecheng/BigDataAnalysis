'''Shoplifting''' is [[theft]] by stealing [[product (consumer goods)|goods]] or [[money]] from a retail [[Shop|store]]. It can be done by placing the item in pockets, under clothes or in bags and leaving the store without paying for the item in question. 
 
 Shoplifting, when detected, is punishable by [[law enforcement]].
