 '''Lace''' is a [[fabric]], patterned with open holes in the work, made by [[machine]] or by hand. Lace is very lightweight and open, and the patterns usually have many holes and [[frill]]s. 
 
 Lace is made by [[knitting]], [[crocheting]] and [[weaving]].
