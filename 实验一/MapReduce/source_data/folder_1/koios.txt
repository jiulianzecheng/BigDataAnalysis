'''Coeus''' is the [[Titan (mythology)|Titan]] of [[intelligence]] in [[Greek mythology]]. With his sister [[Phoebe (titan)|Phoebe]] his children are [[Leto]], and [[Asteria]].  
 
 After the war against the [[Twelve Olympians|Olympians]] he was banned to the [[Tartarus]].
