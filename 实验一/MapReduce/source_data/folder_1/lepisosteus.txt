 '''''Lepisosteus''''' is a genus of [[gars]] in the family [[Lepisosteidae]] only known across [[North America]]. 
 
 == Species == 
 The currently recognized species are: 
 
 * ''[[Spotted gar|Lepisosteus oculatus]]''  < small > [[Alexander Winchell|Winchell]], 1864 < /small >  (Spotted gar) 
 * ''[[Longnose gar|Lepisosteus osseus]]''  < small > [[Carl Linnaeus|Linnaeus]], 1758 < /small >  (Longnose gar) 
 * ''[[Shortnose gar|Lepisosteus platysomus]]''  < small > [[Constantine Samuel Rafinesque|Rafinesque]], 1820 < /small >  (Shortnose gar) 
 * ''[[Florida gar|Lepisosteus platyrhincus]]''  < small > [[James Ellsworth De Kay|DeKay]], 1842 < /small >  (Florida gar)
