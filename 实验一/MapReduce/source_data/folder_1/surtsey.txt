 '''Surtsey''' is an [[island]] in [[Iceland]] that appeared in 1963 as a result of an underwater [[Volcano|volcanic]] eruption that released smoke, ash and matter that joined together to form the islet. < ref > [https://earthsky.org/earth/surtsey-and-the-birth-of-new-islands/ Today in science: An island is born] in earthsky.org < /ref >  It takes its name from a [[giant]] from Norse mythology. 
 
 It serves [[biologist]]s and other scientists to study and see how fungi, plants and animals reach new territory and interact with it.
