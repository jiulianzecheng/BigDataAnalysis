 '''Carlos Efrén Reyes Rosado''' (born May 2, 1991), also known by his stage name '''Farruko''', is a retired Puerto Rican singer-songwriter. He became famous after working with [[Daddy Yankee]], [[J Balvin]], [[Don Omar]] and [[J Álvarez]]. He released his first studio album, ''El Talento Del Bloque'', was released in 2010. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://www.allmusic.com/artist/farruko-mn0002444093 |title=Farruko, Allmusic}} Retrieved February 2, 2013. < /ref >
