 '''Bahun''' ('''Khas Brahmin''') ({{lang-ne|बाहुन}}) are group of people belonging to [[Brahmin]] caste in [[Khas people|Khas group]], an [[Indo-Aryan]] ethno-linguistic group. Generally, the Parvate/Pahari (hilly) Brahmins are called as Bahuns or '''Khas Bahuns'''. Bahuns mainly served as priests, teachers and astrologers as per their caste. Bahuns were able to hold government offices, administration and politics. 
 [[File:Gaze of a priest.JPG|thumb|Bahun (priest) with traditional Dhaka topi]]
