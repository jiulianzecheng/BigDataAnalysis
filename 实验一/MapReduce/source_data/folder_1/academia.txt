 '''Academia''' is a word for [[higher education]] and [[research]]. The word comes from the ''[[Academy|akademeia]]'' just outside ancient [[Athens]], where the [[Gymnasium (ancient Greece)|gymnasium]] was made famous by [[Plato]].
