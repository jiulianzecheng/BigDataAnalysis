 '''Jokioinen''' is a municipality in [[Kanta-Häme]], [[Finland]]. About 5,600 people lived there in January 2014. The municipalities next to it are [[Forssa]], [[Tammela]], [[Somero]], [[Ypäjä]]  and [[Humppila]].  
 
 Jokioinen was established in 1873.
