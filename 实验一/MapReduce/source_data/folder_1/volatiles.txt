In [[planetary science]], '''volatiles''' are the group of chemical [[chemical element|elements]] and [[chemical compound|compounds]] with a low [[boiling point]]. They are often found in the [[Crust (geology)|crust]] or atmosphere of a planet or moon. Examples of volatiles are [[nitrogen]], [[water]], and [[ammonia]].
