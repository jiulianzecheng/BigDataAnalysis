 '''''Spun''''' is a 2002 [[melodrama]]tic crime comedy movie. It is about meth abuse and drug addiction. [[Brittany Murphy]] plays Nikki, [[John Leguizamo]] plays Spider Mike and [[Mena Suvari]] plays Cookie. This melodramatic comedy was rated [[NC-17]] by the MPAA, although the rating was later surrendered. This movie got negative review from critics when it was released.
