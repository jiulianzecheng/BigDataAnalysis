 '''Pewsum''' is a village in the municipality of [[Krummhörn]] ([[Landkreis Aurich|Aurich]]) in the west of [[East Frisia]]. Pewsum is both the administrative seat as well as the trade and craft centre for the municipality. The village is at a height of {{Höhe|0|DE-NN|link=true}}.
