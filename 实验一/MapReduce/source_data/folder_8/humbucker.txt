They uses two wire coils to cancel out the noisy interference picked up by coil pickups.  < ref > {{Cite web|title=Home {{!}} DiMarzio {{!}} Guitar Pickups, Bass Pickups, Pedalboard Cables, Straps and more.|url=https://www.dimarzio.com/|access-date=2022-08-24|website=www.dimarzio.com}} < /ref > 
 
 Humbuckers are one of the two main types of guitar pickup, the other being single coil. < ref > {{Cite web|title=USRE20070.pdf|url=https://docs.google.com/viewer?url=patentimages.storage.googleapis.com/pdfs/USRE20070.pdf|access-date=2022-08-24|website=docs.google.com}} < /ref > 
 
 In any magnetic pickup, a vibrating guitar string, magnetized by a fixed magnet within the pickup, induces an alternating voltage across its coil.
