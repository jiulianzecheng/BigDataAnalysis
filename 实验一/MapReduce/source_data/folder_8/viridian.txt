 '''Viridian''' is a [[teal (colour)|teal]]-[[green]] [[color]] with a hue toward  green.  The name comes from the [[Latin]] word ''viridis'' which means  " green " .    
 
 ==Meaning of viridian== 
 * Viridian is a [[pigment]] used in [[art]]. 
 
 * Viridian is a popular color for painting [[dining room]]s.   
 
 * ''Viridian'' is the name of a city in the English translation of [[Pok√©mon Red and Blue]]. 
 
 * Viridian is a name on several cards in [[Magic: The Gathering]] 
 
 * The color is similar to the color ''forest green'', and both colors are thought of as having to do with [[forest]]s and [[woods]]. 
 
 * Viridian represents the ''Viridian Design Movement'', an effort to encourage ''green architecture''--[[architecture]] based on [[Ecology|ecological]] principles.
