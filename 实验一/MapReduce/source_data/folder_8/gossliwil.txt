 '''Gossliwil''' was a [[municipality]] in the [[Bucheggberg (district)|district Bucheggberg]], in the [[canton of Solothurn]], [[Switzerland]].  On 1 January 2014, the former municipalities of Gossliwil, [[Tscheppach]], [[Brügglen]], [[Aetingen]], [[Aetigkofen]], [[Bibern, Solothurn|Bibern]], [[Hessigkofen]], [[Mühledorf, Solothurn|Mühledorf]], [[Küttigkofen]], [[Kyburg-Buchegg]] merged to form the new municipality of [[Buchegg]]. < ref name=Mutation > [http://www.bfs.admin.ch/bfs/portal/de/index/infothek/nomenklaturen/blank/blank/gem_liste/02.html Nomenklaturen – Amtliches Gemeindeverzeichnis der Schweiz] {{in lang|de}} accessed 13 December 2014 < /ref >
