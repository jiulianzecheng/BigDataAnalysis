'''Marsden''' is a large village in the Metropolitan Borough of [[Kirklees]].   In 2020 about 3,768 lived there.  It is close to the [[Huddersfield Narrow Canal]]. 
 
 It was an important centre for the production of woollen cloth, but the last woollen mill closed in 2003. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Bank Bottom Mill 1|url=https://www.theviewfromthenorth.org/bank-bottom-mill-1|access-date=2023-07-09|website=www.theviewfromthenorth.org}} < /ref >
