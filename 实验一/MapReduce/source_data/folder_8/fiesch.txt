 '''Fiesch''' is a [[municipality]] in [[Goms (district)|Goms]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Valais]] in [[Switzerland]]. It is at the foot of the [[Eggishorn]] mountain. 
 
 Fiesch has been designated a [[World Heritage Site|UNESCO World Heritage Site]] for its view of the [[Jungfrau-Aletsch Protected Area]].
