 TurboTax is [[software]] made by Intuit to make [[Taxes|filing taxes]] easier. Created in 1984, TurboTax has risen in popularity over time. The rise of programs such as TurboTax can be blamed on the difficulty of the [[Free File]] program offered by the [[U.S. Government]].
