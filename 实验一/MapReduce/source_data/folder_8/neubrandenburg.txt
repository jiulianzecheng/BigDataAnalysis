 '''Neubrandenburg''' ({{lang-nds|Niegenbramborg}} or shortened: ''Bramborg'')  (''New [[Brandenburg an der Havel|Brandenburg]]'', {{IPA2|nɔʏˈbʁandənbʊʁk}}) is a city in [[Mecklenburg-Western Pomerania]], [[Germany]]. It is in the southeastern part of the state, at the shore of a lake called the ''[[Tollensesee]]''. 
 
 The city was founded in the year 1248.  
 
 Till September 4, 2011 Neubrandenburg was a [[urban districts of Germany|urban district]]. 
 
 Since March 2015 [[Silvio Witt]] is the mayor of Neubrandenburg. He is the succesor of [[Paul Krüger]] ([[CDU]]). 
 
 Neubrandenburg was founded by the knight Herbord von Raven in the year 1248.
