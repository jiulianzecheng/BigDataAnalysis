 '''Neustrelitz''' is a town in the south of [[Mecklenburg-Western Pomerania]] and the old capital city of the grand duchy Mecklenburg-Strelitz. Neustrelitz is 30 kilometers away from [[Neubrandenburg]]. 
 
 About 21,000 people were living there in 2016.
