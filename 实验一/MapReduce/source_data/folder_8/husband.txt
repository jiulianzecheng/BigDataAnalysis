{{Other uses}} 
 A '''husband''' is a [[married]] [[man]].  " Married "  means that the law says two people are [[legal]]ly  " joined " . During the [[wedding]] [[ceremony]], the husband is called the '''groom''' (or '''bridegroom'''). In [[Christianity]] and [[Judaism]] a [[husband]] can have one [[wife]] ([[monogamy]]). In [[Islam]] a husband can have up to four wives ([[polygamy]]).
