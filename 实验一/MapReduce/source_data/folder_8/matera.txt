 '''Matera''' is a [[city]] in the [[area]] of [[Basilicata]], in the southern part of [[Italy]]. It is the [[capital (city)|capital]] of the [[province of Matera]]. On October 17, 2014, Matera was declared Italian host of [[European Capital of Culture for 2019]]
