 '''''Brochevarevarura''''' ({{trans|Who shall save the day?}}) is a 2019 Indian [[Telugu language|Telugu]]-language movie.  It is a [[comedy]] about crime.  [[Vivek Athreya]] wrote and directed the movie, and Vijay Kumar Manyam produced it under Manyam Productions. The film stars [[Sree Vishnu]], [[Nivetha Thomas]], and [[Satyadev Kancharana]]. [[Priyadarshi (actor)|Priyadarshi]] and [[Rahul Ramakrishna]] also have small parts. It released on June 28, 2019. 
 
 ==Plot== 
 Three students meet a woman whose father controls her life.  They try to help her escape him, but they all get into trouble.
