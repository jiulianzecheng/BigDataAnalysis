'''Messmate''' is a [[common name]] for a group of [[Eucalyptus]]'' ''trees''. ''They all have rough [[bark]]. It is not known where the name came from. < ref name= " Boland 1984 " > {{cite book | author = Boland, D. J. | display-authors = etal | year = 1985 | title = Forest Trees of Australia | edition = 4th | isbn = 0-643-05423-5}} < /ref > 
 
 Species commonly known as  " messmate "  include: < ref name= " APCND " > {{cite web | title = Australian Plant Common Name Database | url = http://www.anbg.gov.au/common.names/ | accessdate = 2007-07-15 | publisher = [[Australian National Botanic Gardens]]}} < /ref > 
 * ''[[Eucalyptus acmenoides|E. & nbsp;acmenoides]]'' (Yellow Messmate) 
 * ''[[Eucalyptus cloeziana|E. & nbsp;cloeziana]]'' (Messmate, Gympie Messmate, [[Queensland]] Messmate, Yellow Messmate) 
 * ''[[Eucalyptus exserta|E. & nbsp;exserta]]'' (Messmate, Yellow Messmate)

 * ''[[Eucalyptus obliqua|E. & nbsp;obliqua]]'' (Messmate, Messmate Stringybark) 
 * ''[[Eucalyptus resinifera|E. & nbsp;resinifera]]'' (Red Messmate)
