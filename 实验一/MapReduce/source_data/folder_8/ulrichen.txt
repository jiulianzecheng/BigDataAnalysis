 '''Ulrichen''' is a village and former [[municipality]] of the [[district]] [[Goms (district)|Goms]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Valais]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 On 1 January 2009, the former municipalities of Ulrichen, [[Obergesteln]] and [[Oberwald]] merged into the new municipality of [[Obergoms]].
