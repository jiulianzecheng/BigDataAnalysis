 The '''Whopper''' is the signature [[hamburger]] line sold by the international [[Fast-food restaurant|fast food restaurant]] [[Chain store|chain]] [[Burger King]]. Its [[Australia|Australian]] [[Franchising|franchise]] is [[Hungry Jack's]]. The hamburger is well known in the fast-food industry, Burger King [[advertising]] itself as  " the Home of the Whopper. "  It names its [[kiosk]] stores the BK Whopper Bar. In response to the Whopper, Burger King's competitors have developed similar products to compete against it. An example of this is [[McDonald's]] [[Quarter Pounder]]. 
 
 Burger King sells different types of the Whopper. They are either [[Season|seasonal]] or made to local tastes or [[customs]]. To promote the product, the restaurant sometimes releases limited-time versions. It is often the main point of advertising promotions, product tie-ins, and corporate [[practical jokes]] and [[Hoax|hoaxes]]. In the [[United States]], a standard Whopper comes with a quarter-pound [[beef]] patty, [[pickle]]s, [[onion]]s, [[ketchup]], [[tomato]]es and [[lettuce]]. [[Cheese]] can be added as well. The customer can add extra patties by having the [[Burger King]] stackers. < ref > {{cite web |title=Burger King Whopper |url=https://burgerking.com.my/Menu/Item/%208 |accessdate=1 September 2023}} < /ref >  Previously the Whopper was a part of a [[meme]] because of a song about it called [[Whopper Whopper| " Whopper Whopper " ]]. 
 
 ==History== 
 The Whopper was introduced in 1957. It has had many changes over the years. These include changes to the size of the burger and the type of [[bread]] that is used. When it was introduced, it only cost 37 cents in the [[United States]]. < ref > {{cite web |last1=Anaya |first1=Cynthia |title=Here's How Much The First Burger King Whopper Cost |url=https://www.thedailymeal.com/1052363/heres-how-much-the-first-burger-king-whopper-cost/ |website=The Daily Meal |accessdate=1 September 2023 |date=13 October 2022}} < /ref >  In mid 2023 it was announced that Burger King would have to face a [[class action]] [[lawsuit]] because of a change to the size of the Whopper. < ref > {{cite web |title=Burger King faces legal claim over size of Whopper |url=https://www.bbc.com/news/business-66654440 |website=BBC News |accessdate=1 September 2023 |date=30 August 2023}} < /ref >
