 '''Eaubonne''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is in [[Île-de-France]] in the [[Val-d'Oise]] [[Departments of France|department]] in north [[France]]. In 2019, 25,335 people lived there. 
 
 The commune is in the northern [[suburb]]s of [[Paris]], {{convert|16.1|km|mi|abbr=on}} from the center.
