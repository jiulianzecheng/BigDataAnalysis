 '''Kivik''' is a [[Urban areas in Sweden|locality]] in [[Simrishamn Municipality]] in [[Skåne County]] in [[Sweden]]. In 2010, 960 people lived there. < ref name=scb / >  It is in [[Österlen]]. Kivik is known for its annual [[Kivik Market|market]] in July every year.
