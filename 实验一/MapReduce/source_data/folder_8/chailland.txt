'''Chailland''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]] of 1136 people (1999). It is found in the region [[Pays de la Loire]] in the [[Mayenne]] department in the northwest of [[France]]. 
 
 Chailland is also a very common last name of a family living in the United States mostly located in southeast Missouri and Southern FL.
