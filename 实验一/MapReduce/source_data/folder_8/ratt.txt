 '''Ratt''' is an American [[glam metal]] band. It was made in [[San Diego, California]]. The band is best known for the songs  " Round and Round "  and  " Lay It Down " , which were on the [[Billboard Hot 100|''Billboard'' Hot 100]] [[Record chart|music chart]]. Many of their [[Album|albums]] have been given [[RIAA certification|certifications]] by the [[Recording Industry Association of America|RIAA]]. The most popular members of the band are singer Stephen Pearcy, [[Guitar|guitarist]] Robbin Crosby, guitarist Warren DeMartini, [[Bass guitar|bassist]] Juan Croucier, and drummer Bobby Blotzer. 
 
 Ratt is known for being one of the bands that helped make [[Heavy metal music|metal]] popular in [[Los Angeles]] in the early 1980s. < ref > {{Cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=PxWasD0U6pcC & pg=PA104|title=Studies in Symbolic Interaction|last=Denzin|first=Norman K.|date=2010-10-11|publisher=Emerald Group Publishing|isbn=978-0-85724-361-4|language=en}} < /ref >  They released their last album, ''Infestation'', in 2010.
