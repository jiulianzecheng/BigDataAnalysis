 '''Gadancourt''' is a former [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is in [[Île-de-France]] in the [[Val-d'Oise]] [[Departments of France|department]] in north [[France]]. On 1 January 2018, it was merged into the commune of [[Avernes]]. < ref > [https://www.legifrance.gouv.fr/eli/arrete/2017/9/25/INTB1726955A/jo/texte Arrêté préfectoral] 25 September 2017 {{in lang|fr}} < /ref >
