 '''GarageBand''' is [[music]] [[software]] made by [[Apple Inc.]] It allows you to make your own music and even make music with a [[MIDI]] [[Keyboard instrument|keyboard]]. GarageBand includes hundreds of beats and [[Audio loop|audio loops]].  
 
 The newest version, Version 10.0.2, has a new presentation of looks and with more music and sounds.
