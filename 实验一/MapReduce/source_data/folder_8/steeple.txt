 A '''steeple''' is the [[tower]] at the top of a [[church]], which is a [[building]] or [[temple]] of the [[Christianity|Christian]] [[religion]]. It often has a [[cross]] at the top, in memory of [[Jesus]]' [[crucifixion]].
