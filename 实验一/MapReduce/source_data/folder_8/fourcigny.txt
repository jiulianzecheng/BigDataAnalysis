 '''Fourcigny''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is in the [[Picardie]] [[Regions of France|region]] in the [[Somme]] [[Departments of France|department]] in the north of [[France]]. In 2011, 183 people lived there. It is found 26 miles southwest of [[Amiens]].
