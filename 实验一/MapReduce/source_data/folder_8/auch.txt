 '''Auch''' ([[Gascon language|Gascon]]: ''Aush'' or ''Aux'') is a [[Communes of France|commune]] in southwestern [[France]], in the [[Regions of France|region]] of [[Occitanie]]. It is the [[Prefectures in France|''prefecture'']] ([[Capital city|capital]]) of the [[Gers]] [[Departments of France|department]]. It is also the capital of 3 [[Cantons in France|''cantons'']]: [[Canton of Auch-1|Auch-1]], [[Canton of Auch-2|Auch-2]] and [[Canton of Auch-3|Auch-3]]. 
 
 Auch is the historical capital of [[Gascony]] and the people of this city are known, in [[French language|French]], as ''auscitain'' (women: ''auscitaines''). < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.habitants.fr/habitants_auch_32013.html|title=Auch|publisher=habitants.fr |language=fr |access-date=4 April 2014 }} < /ref > 
 
 ==History== 
 [[Image:Auch 1883.jpg|thumb|left|Auch in 1883]] 
 Auch is an ancient town. The name of ''Auch'' comes from the people that inhabited the area at the time of the [[Roman Empire|Roman]] conquest. The name of this [[tribe]] was ''Ausci'', singular ''Auscus''. 
 
 The Romans named the town where that tribe lived as ''Augusta Auscorum'' (or ''Augusta Ausciorum''), which means  " Augusta of the Ausci " . Eventually, ''Augusta'' was dropped, and the name changed into modern [[Gascon language|Gascon]] ''Aush'', and modern French ''Auch''. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 [[Image:Vieille ville d'Auch.jpg|thumb|left|The old city]] 
 Auch is in the southwest of France, in [[Armagnac (province)|Armagnac]] (Gascony), in the valley of the [[Gers (river)|Gers]] river. It extends along the sides of the river that gives its name to the department. 
 
 The river [[:wikt:split|splits]] the city in two parts: 
 * The '''Upper Town''' ({{lang-fr|Haute-Ville}}), on a small [[hill]] on the right side of the river. It is a [[Middle Ages|medieval]] city with narrow streets with stairs and where most of the old [[monument]]s are found. 
 * The '''Lower Town''' ({{lang-fr|Basse-Ville}}), on a [[plain]] on the left side of the river. 
 
 The ''commune'' is, in straight line, at {{Convert|75|km|mi|0|abbr=on }} from the [[Pyrenees]], {{Convert|150|km|mi|0|abbr=on }} from the [[Bay of Biscay]], {{Convert|160|km|mi|0|abbr=on }} from [[Bordeaux]], {{Convert|70|km|mi|0|abbr=on }} from [[Toulouse]] and {{Convert|85|km|mi|0|abbr=on }} from [[Pau, France|Pau]]. 
 
 It has an area of {{Convert|72.48|km2|sqmi|abbr=on }} and its average [[altitude]] is {{Convert|166|m|ft|abbr=on}}.

 ===Climate=== 
 The climate of the department is an oceanic climate with template summers, '''Cfb''' (Marine West Coast Climate) in the [[Köppen climate classification]]. < ref name= " wbase " > {{cite web|publisher=Weatherbase |title=Auch, France - Köppen Climate Classification |url=http://www.weatherbase.com/weather/weather-summary.php3?s=598817 & cityname=Auch%2C+Midi-Pyr%E9n%E9es%2C+France & units= |access-date=4 April 2014}} < /ref > 
 
 The average temperature for the year in Auch is {{Convert|13.4|°C|°F }}. The warmest month, on average, is July with an average temperature of {{Convert|21.6|°C|°F }}. The coolest month on average is January, with an average temperature of {{Convert|5.9|°C|°F }}. 
 
 The highest recorded temperature in Auch is {{Convert|40.9|°C|°F }}, which was recorded in August. The lowest recorded temperature in Auch is {{Convert|-20|°C|°F }}, which was recorded in January. 
 
 The average amount of [[precipitation]] for the year in Auch is {{Convert|690.9|mm|in|1|abbr=on }}. The month with the most precipitation on average is May with {{Convert|83.8|mm|in|1|abbr=on }} of precipitation. The month with the least precipitation on average is February with an average of {{Convert|30.5|mm|in|1|abbr=on }}. < ref name= " wbase "  / >

 == Administration == 
 Auch is the [[Prefectures in France|prefecture]] of the Cantal department, the capital of the ''arrondissement'' of [[Arrondissement of Auch|Auch]] and the [[administrative centre]] ({{lang-fr|chef-lieu}}) of three [[Cantons of France|cantons]]:  
 # [[Canton of Auch-1|Auch-1]] : 5 ''communes'' + part of Auch with 11,098 inhabitants   < small > (2014) < /small > 
 # [[Canton of Auch-2|Auch-2]] : 13 ''communes'' + part of Auch with 10,298 inhabitants  < small > (2014) < /small > 
 # [[Canton of Auch-3|Auch-3]] : 9 ''communes'' + part of Auch with 11,390 inhabitants  < small > (2014) < /small > 
 
 It is part of the [[Communes of France|intercommunality]] ''Le Grand Auch'' ({{lang-fr|Le Grand Auch Cœur de Gascogne Agglomération}}).

 ==Places of interest== 
 A city founded by the Romans, Auch had its period of power during the [[Middle Ages]], when it was the capital of the old province of [[Gascony]]. The high quality of the [[limestone]] is an important component of its monuments and buildings, such as its [[cathedral]]. 
 
 Some of these places of interest are: 
 * ''Cathédrale Sainte-Marie d'Auch'' (St Mary's Cathedral). This cathedral took 200 years to build; from the 15th – 17th centuries. 
 * ''Hôtel de ville'' (City hall). 
 * ''Tour d'Armagnac'' (Tower of Armagnac). An ancient prison from the 14th century 
 * ''Escalier monumental'' (Monumental Staircase). 370 steps built in 1863 
 * ''Musée des Jacobins'' 
 * ''Pont de la Treille''
