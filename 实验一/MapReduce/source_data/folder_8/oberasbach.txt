 '''Oberasbach''' is a municipality in [[Fürth (district)|Fürth]], in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. It is 6 & nbsp;km southwest of [[Fürth]], and 10 & nbsp;km west of [[Nuremberg]] (centre). 
 
 == Stadtrat == 
 [[File:Rathaus Oberasbach.jpg|thumb|150px|Oberasbach town hall]] 
 {{update section|date=July 2021}} 
 The local council has 24 members.  
 The election in 2014 showed the following results: 
 * 9 seats (CSU) 
 * 9 seats (SPD) 
 * 3 seats (Free voters) 
 * 2 seats (The Greens) 
 * 1 seat (FDP)
