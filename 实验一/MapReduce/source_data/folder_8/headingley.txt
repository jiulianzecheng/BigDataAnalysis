 '''Headingley''' is an area of [[Leeds]], [[West Yorkshire]].  It is situated about 3 miles from Leeds City Centre.  Headingley has a large student population and is also known for the [[Headingley Stadium]].  [[Headingley railway station]] has train services to [[Leeds City Centre]], [[Harrogate]] and [[York]].
