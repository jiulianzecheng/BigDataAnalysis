 The '''Bundesliga''' ({{IPA-de|ˈbʊndəsˌliːɡa|lang|De-Bundesliga.ogg}}; {{literal translation|Federal League}}), sometimes referred to as the '''Fußball-Bundesliga''' ({{IPA-de|ˌfuːsbal-|}}) or '''1. Bundesliga''' ({{IPA-de|ˌeːɐ̯stə-|}}), is a [[Association football|football]] league which is top division in [[Germany]]. The league has 18 teams, and the teams can be [[relegation|relegated]] to [[2. Fußball-Bundesliga]]. Most games are played on Saturdays and Sundays, with some games being played during the week. 
 
 Since 1963, [[FC Bayern Munich]] has almost completely dominated the league, winning 30 times. However, the league has seen other champions, such as [[Borussia Dortmund]], [[Borussia Mönchengladbach]], [[Hamburger SV]], [[Werder Bremen]], [[VfB Stuttgart]], [[VfL Wolfsburg]] and so on. The Bundesliga is one of the top leagues, currently ranked 4th place in [[UEFA|UEFA's]] [[UEFA coefficient]]. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.uefa.com/memberassociations/uefarankings/country/|title=Member associations - UEFA rankings - Country coefficients|publisher=Uefa.com|accessdate=2019-05-20|archive-date=2014-05-28|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140528234814/http://www.uefa.com/memberassociations/uefarankings/country/|url-status=live}} < /ref >  The league has a very high average attendance, the top [[association football]] league in the world.
