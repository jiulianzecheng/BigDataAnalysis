 '''Goodreads''' is a free database of books, authors, and book reviews. In December 2007, it had 10,000,000 books in its database. < ref > {{Cite web|url=http://social.techcrunch.com/2007/12/17/goodreads-raises-angel-round-to-help-you-find-that-perfect-book/|title=Goodreads Raises Angel Round To Help You Find That Perfect Book|website=TechCrunch|language=en-US|access-date=2020-03-26}}{{Dead link|date=July 2023 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }} < /ref >   
 
 In Goodreads, a user can do the following things: 
 
 * Sign up and register books 
 * Rate  &  Review books 
 * Generate library catalogs 
 * Generate Reading lists 
 * Create their own groups of book suggestions 
 * Surveys, polls, blogs, and discussions 
 
 Goodreads is owned by [[Amazon (company)|Amazon]]. < ref > {{Cite news|url=https://www.nytimes.com/2013/03/29/business/media/amazon-to-buy-goodreads.html|title=Amazon to Buy Social Site Dedicated to Sharing Books|last=Kaufman|first=Leslie|date=2013-03-28|work=The New York Times|access-date=2020-03-26|language=en-US|issn=0362-4331}} < /ref >
