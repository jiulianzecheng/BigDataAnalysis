 '''''Agriotes''''' is a [[genus]] of [[beetle]]s in the family [[Elateridae]]. These beetles are called  " click beetles " . The genus contains many species. They are found in the [[Americas]], [[Asia]] and much of [[Europe]]. < ref > {{cite web |url= https://insects.tamu.edu/research/collection/hallan/test/Arthropoda/Insects/Coleoptera/Family/Elateridae.txt |title=Elateridae |publisher= Texas A & M University, Department of Entomology |access-date=16 February 2014 |format=TXT}} < /ref > 
 
 The [[larva]]e, called  " wireworms " , feed on roots of plants and also on seeds lying on the ground. They are considered [[pest]]s by farmers because they damage [[crop]]s. < ref > {{cite book |title= International Wildlife Encyclopedia |author=Maurice Burton |author2= Robert Burton |volume=4 |publisher= Marshall Cavendish |year=2002 |isbn= 978-0-76147-266-7 |pages=466â€“467 |url= https://books.google.com/books?isbn=0761472703 }} < /ref >
