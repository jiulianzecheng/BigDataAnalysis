'''Pinner''' is an area in [[Greater London]], in the [[London Borough of Harrow]]. It is 12 miles (19 km) northwest from [[Charing Cross]]. Previously it was in [[Middlesex]], as that county used to be. 
 
 Pinner is close to the northern border of the [[London Borough of Hillingdon]].
