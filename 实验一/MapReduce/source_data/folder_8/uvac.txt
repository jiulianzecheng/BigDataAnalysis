 The '''Uvac''' is a [[river]] which rises in [[Serbia]]. It flows through Serbia and [[Bosnia-Herzegovina]]. Its is about {{Convert|119|km|abbr=off}} long. Near the locality of Uvac, the river flows into the [[Lim (river)|Lim]]. For some of its length, the river is the [[border]] between both countries.
