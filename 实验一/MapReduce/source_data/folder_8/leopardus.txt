 '''''Leopardus''''' is a [[genus]] of small spotted cats that are native to Middle and [[South America]]. Very few species belonging to ''Leopardus'' are in the southern [[United States]]. ''Leopardus'' may be the oldest branch of the [[Felinae]] to go into the [[Americas]], followed by the genera ''[[Lynx]]'' and ''[[Puma (genus)|Puma]]''.
