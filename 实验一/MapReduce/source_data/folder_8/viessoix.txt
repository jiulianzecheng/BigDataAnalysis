 '''Viessoix''' used to be a [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is in [[Basse-Normandie]] in the [[Calvados (department)|Calvados]] [[Departments of France|department]] in the northwest of [[France]]. On 1 January 2016, it became part of the new commune of [[Valdallière]]. < ref > [https://www.legifrance.gouv.fr/eli/arrete/2015/10/13/INTB1600507A/jo/texte Arrêté préfectoral] 13 October 2015 {{in lang|fr}} < /ref >
