 '''Brahman''' is the Ultimate Reality of Universe in [[Hinduism]]. It is a [[Sanskrit language]] word. Brahman is said to be [[infinite]], with no beginning or end. Brahman is changeless and is the source of the universe in [[Hinduism|Hindu]] beliefs.
