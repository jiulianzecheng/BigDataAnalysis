 '''Hermisende''' is a municipality located in the [[Zamora (province)|province of Zamora]], [[Castile and León]], Spain. According to the 2004 [[census]] ([[Instituto Nacional de Estadística (Spain)|INE]]), the municipality has a population of 383 inhabitants.
