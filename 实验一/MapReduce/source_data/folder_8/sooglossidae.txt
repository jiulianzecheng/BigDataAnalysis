 The '''Sooglossidae''' ('''Seychelles frogs''') are a [[family (biology)|family]] of [[frogs]].  They are found on the [[Seychelles|Seychelles Islands]] and [[India]]. 
 
 All of the species are small land frogs, about {{convert|4|cm|in}} in length.  They hide under fallen leaves or in rock crevices. They lay their eggs on moist ground, rather than in water. One species lacks [[tadpole]]s, with the eggs hatching directly into froglets.
