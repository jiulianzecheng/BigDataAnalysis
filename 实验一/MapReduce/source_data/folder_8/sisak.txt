 '''Sisak''' is a city in central [[Croatia]]. It is at the confluence of the [[Kupa]], [[Sava]] and [[Odra (Kupa)|Odra]] rivers. The city is {{convert|57|km|mi|abbr=on}} southeast of the Croatian capital [[Zagreb]]. In 2011, 47,768 people lived there.
