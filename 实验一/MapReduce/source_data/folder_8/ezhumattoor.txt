 ''' Ezhumattoor''' is a village in [[Pathanamthitta district]], [[Kerala]], [[India]]. 
 
 ==Demographics== 
 
 {{As of|2011}} India [[census]], Ezhumattoor had a population of 11,423 with 5,540 males and 5,883 females. < ref > {{Cite web|url=http://www.censusindia.gov.in/2011census/Listofvillagesandtowns.aspx|title=Census of India Website : Office of the Registrar General  &  Census Commissioner, India|website=www.censusindia.gov.in}} < /ref > 
 
 
 == Economy == 
 
 [[File:Rubber trees in Kerala, India.jpg|thumb|Rubber Trees in a Plantation]] 
 The main occupation of people is farming. The major crops are [[coconut]], [[tapioca]] and [[Black pepper|pepper]].
