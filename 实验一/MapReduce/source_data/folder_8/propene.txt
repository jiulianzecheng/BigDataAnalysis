 '''Propene''' is an [[organic chemistry|organic]] [[Chemical compound|compound]]. The substance is also known as '''propylene''' and has the formula C < sub > 3 < /sub > H < sub > 6 < /sub > . It is the second-simplest [[alkene]]. Since it is only made of [[hydrogen]] and [[carbon]] [[atom]]s, it is a [[hydrocarbon]]. At [[room temperature]] and [[Standard temperature and pressure|normal pressure]] it is a [[gas]]. 
 
 ==Uses== 
 Propene is produced from [[fossil fuel]]s, and from [[coal]]. Propene is the second most important product used in the [[petrochemical industry]], after [[ethene]]. About two thirds are used to produce [[polypropylene]]. Propene and [[benzene]] are converted to [[acetone]] and [[phenol]] via the [[cumene process]].
