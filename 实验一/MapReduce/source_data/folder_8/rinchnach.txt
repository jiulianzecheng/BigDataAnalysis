 '''Rinchnach''' is a [[Municipalities of Germany|municipality]] in [[Regen (district)|Regen]], of [[Germany]]. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 Rinchnach is in the middle of the [[Bavarian Forest]], 7 kilometers southeast of the town [[Rain, Lower Bavaria|Rain]], and 9 kilometers south of [[Zwiesel]]. 
 
 The river [[Rinchnacher Ohe]] flows through Rinchnach.
