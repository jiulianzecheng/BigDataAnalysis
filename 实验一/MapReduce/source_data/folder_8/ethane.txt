 '''Ethane''' is an [[Organic compound|organic]] [[chemical compound]] with [[chemical formula]] C < sub > 2 < /sub > H < sub > 6 < /sub > .  
 
 At [[Standard conditions for temperature and pressure|standard temperature and pressure]], ethane is a colourless, odorless [[gas]]. It is isolated from [[natural gas]], and as a byproduct of [[oil refinery|petroleum refining]].  
 
 Its main use is in the chemical industry, to make [[ethylene]] by [[cracking (chemistry)|steam cracking]].  
 
 At room temperature, the gas is [[flammability|flammable]], and it [[explosion|explodes]] when mixed with air. When ethane is liquid, touching it causes a very serious [[frostbite]].
