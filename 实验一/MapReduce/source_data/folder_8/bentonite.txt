'''Bentonite''' is a kind of [[clay]].  Bentonite usually forms from [[weathering]] of [[volcanic ash]]. It is volcanic ash mixed with water under  pressure. 
 
 The different types of bentonite have different proportions of [[sodium]], [[calcium]] or [[potassium]]. They can absorb quite a lot of water, and are used as fillers and/or [[lubrication|lubricators]] in various industries. The clay is used for many practical purposes.
