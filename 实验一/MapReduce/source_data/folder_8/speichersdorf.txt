 '''Speichersdorf''' is a [[Municipalities of Germany|municipality]]  in the [[Bayreuth (district)|district of Bayreuth]] in [[Bavaria]] in [[Germany]]. It is near the [[Fichtelgebirge]], 18 & nbsp;km eastern of [[Bayreuth]]. It has a population of about 6,200.
