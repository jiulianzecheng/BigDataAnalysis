 '''Mistelbach an der Zaya''' ({{IPA-de|ˈmɪstl̩ˌbax|-|De-Mistelbach.ogg}}; Mistelbach on the (River) Zaya) is a town in the northeast of [[Austria]] in [[Lower Austria]], one of Austria's nine [[States of Austria|federal states]]. It is about 40 & nbsp;km northeast of Austria's capital [[Vienna]]. Furthermore, it consists of 8 subordinated municipalities.
