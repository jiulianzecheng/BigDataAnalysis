 '''Cirrina''' is one of the two suborders of [[octopus]]. Cirrina is also known as the finned, deep sea octopus. They have two fins on the head and a small internal shell. The paddle shaped fins help the Cirrina swim through the ocean. < ref > {{cite book |url= https://books.google.com/books?id=gvpMylmRUScC & dq=cirrina+octopus & pg=PA72 |title=Cephalopods: ecology and fisheries |first=P.R. and Paul Rodhouse |last=Boyle |year=2012  |isbn=9781405145435 |access-date=19 January 2012}} < /ref >
