 '''Arianespace''' is a French company for space launch systems developed by ArianeGroup, namely the Ariane and Vega launcher families, created in 1980. The company defines itself in its own terms as a  " launch operator " . Since 2007, following an agreement between the [[European Space Agency]] (ESA) and the Russian space agency Roscosmos, Arianespace has also been responsible for launching the [[Soyuz]] launchers. < ref > [https://www.latribune.fr/entreprises-finance/industrie/aeronautique-defense/mais-pourquoi-arianespace-lance-des-satellites-telecoms-avec-soyuz-de-kourou-634106.html Mais pourquoi Arianespace lance des satellites télécoms avec Soyuz de Kourou] < /ref >  The Ariane launch base, located in Kourou in [[French Guiana]] (Guyana Space Center), offers Arianespace a significant advantage over its competitors due to its proximity to the equator in an area free of any risk of tropical cyclones. < ref > [https://www.latribune.fr/entreprises-finance/industrie/aeronautique-defense/kourou-lancement-reussi-pour-la-fusee-ariane-5-854975.html Kourou : lancement réussi pour la fusée Ariane 5] < /ref >
