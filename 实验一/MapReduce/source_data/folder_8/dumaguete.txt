'''Dumaguete''' is a city in the [[Philippines]]. It is the capital of the [[Provinces of the Philippines|province]] of [[Negros Oriental]]. According to the 2020 census, 134,103 people lived there. 
 
 The city is known for [[Silliman University]], the first Protestant and American university in the country and in Asia.
