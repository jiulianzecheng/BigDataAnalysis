 '''Hoogstede''' is a community in [[County of Bentheim (district)|Bentheim]] in [[Lower Saxony]]. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 
 ===Place=== 
 Hoogstede is northwest of [[Nordhorn]] on the [[Germany|German]]-[[Netherlands|Dutch]] [[border]]. The [[Vechte]] flows through town, and its tributary, the Lee releases into it here. The community belongs to the Joint Community (''Samtgemeinde'') of [[Emlichheim]], whose administrative seat is in the like-named community. 
 
 ===Constituent communities=== 
 The community’s seven centres are Hoogstede, Kalle, Tinholt, Arkel, Bathorn, Scheerhorn and Berge.
