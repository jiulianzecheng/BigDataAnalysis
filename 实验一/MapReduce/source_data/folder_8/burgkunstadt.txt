 '''Burgkunstadt''' is a town in [[Lichtenfels (district)|Lichtenfels]], in northern [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. It is on the right edge of the [[Main (river)|Main]], 15 & nbsp;km west of [[Kulmbach]], and 24 & nbsp;km southeast of [[Coburg]].
