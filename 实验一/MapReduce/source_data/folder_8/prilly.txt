 '''Prilly''' ({{IPA-fr|pʁiji}}) is a [[municipality]] in [[Switzerland]] in the canton of [[Vaud]], in the district of [[Ouest lausannois (district)|Ouest lausannois]]. It is a western suburb of the city of [[Lausanne]].
