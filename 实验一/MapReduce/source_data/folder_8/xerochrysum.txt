 '''''Xerochrysum''''' (or ''Bracteantha'') is a type or [[genus]] of [[flowering plant]]s native to [[Australia]]. It was defined by Russian [[botanist]] [[Nikolai Tzvelev]] in 1990. It was identified before (and takes precedence over) ''Bracteantha'' which was described the following year. < ref name=AP173 > {{Cite journal|author=Wilson, Paul G.|year=2002|title=''Xerochrysum'' the correct name for the genus ''Bracteantha''|journal=Australian Plants|volume=21|issue=173|page=398}} < /ref >  A 2002 molecular study of the tribe [[Gnaphalieae]] found the genus is likely [[polyphyletic]], with ''X. bracteatum'' and ''X. viscosum'' removed from each other. < ref > {{cite journal|last=Randall J. Bayer, David G. Greber, Neil H. Bagnall|year=2002|title=Phylogeny of Australian Gnaphalieae (Asteraceae) Based on Chloroplast and Nuclear Sequences, the trnL Intron, trnL/trnF Intergenic Spacer, matK, and ETS|journal=Systematic Botany|volume=27|issue=4|pages=801â€“14|doi=10.1043/0363-6445-27.4.801|doi-broken-date=1 August 2023|url=https://bioone.org/journals/systematic-botany/volume-27/issue-4/0363-6445-27.4.801/Phylogeny-of-Australian-Gnaphalieae-Asteraceae-Based-on-Chloroplast-and-Nuclear/10.1043/0363-6445-27.4.801.short|jstor=3093925|s2cid=56423927}} < /ref > 
 
 There are currently 7 recognized species: < ref name=APNI > {{cite web |url=http://www.anbg.gov.au/cgi-bin/apni?TAXON_NAME=Xerochrysum|title=''Xerochrysum'' |accessdate=28 March 2011 |work= [[Australian Plant Name Index]] (APNI), IBIS database|publisher = Centre for Plant Biodiversity Research, Australian Government, Canberra}} < /ref > 
 
 *''[[Xerochrysum bicolor]]'' 
 *''[[Xerochrysum bracteatum]]'' (Strawflower or Golden Everlasting; syn. ''Bracteantha bracteata'') 
 *''[[Xerochrysum collierianum]]'' A.M.Buchanan  &  Schah. 
 *''[[Xerochrysum palustre]]'' (Swamp Everlasting) 
 *''[[Xerochrysum papillosum]]'' (Labill.) R.J.Bayer 
 *''[[Xerochrysum subundulatum]]'' (Alpine Everlasting or Orange Everlasting) 
 *''[[Xerochrysum viscosum]]'' (Sticky Everlasting) 
 
 The genus was formerly included in ''[[Strawflower|Helichrysum]]''.
