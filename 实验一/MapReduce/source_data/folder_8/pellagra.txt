'''Pellagra''' is a [[disease]] caused by a lack of [[niacin]] ([[vitamin]] B3). Niacin is found in most [[food]]s. A person can die from pellagra if it is not treated. The main [[wikt:result|results]] of pellagra can be remembered as  " the four D's " : [[diarrhea]], [[dermatitis]], [[dementia]], and [[death]].Some symptoms of pellagra according to a few sources are [[abdominal cramping]],[[Depression (mood)|depression]],[[diarrhea]],[[headache]],[[loss of appetite]],[[mucus membrane inflammation]],[[dilated cardiomyopathy]]. It is also caused due to cellular deficiency of niacin, chronic alcoholism or gastro-intestinal malabsorption. It can be prevented by adequate intake of niacin or foods rich in vitamin B3 and dietary advice to vulnerable groups.
