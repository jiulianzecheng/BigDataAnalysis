 '''Gavialidae''' is a family of [[reptile]]s within the order [[Crocodilia]].  There are only two surviving species, the [[Gharial]] (''Gavialis gangeticus'') and the [[False Gharial]] (''Tomistoma schlegelii''), which are each the sole living representatives of the two Gavialidae genera.
