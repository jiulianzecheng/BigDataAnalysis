 '''Uden''' is a former municipality in the [[Netherlands|Dutch]] [[Provinces of the Netherlands|province]] of [[North Brabant]]. In 2021, about 42,300 people lived there. 
 
 Uden has been a part of [[Maashorst]] since 2022.
