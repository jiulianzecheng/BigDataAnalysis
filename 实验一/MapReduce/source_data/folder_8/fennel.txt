 '''Fennel''' (''Foeniculum vulgare'') is a kind of [[herb]]. It is [[native]] to the [[Mediterranean]] and some parts of [[Asia]]. Its [[bulb]], [[leaf|leaves]], and [[seed]]s may be eaten. Dried fennel seeds are used as a [[spice]] for [[flavor]]ing food. Fennel was used to make the spiced [[wine]], [[claret]], in the Middle Ages. Their flavor is like [[anise]].
