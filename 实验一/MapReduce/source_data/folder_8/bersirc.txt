'''Bersirc''' is an [[IRC]] client that is written in [[C (programming language)|C]]. It was first made in [[1999]]. The newest, or latest, version of Bersirc is 2.2.14 and was made in [[2005]]. The client is free to use. Bersirc is [[software license|licensed]] under the [[LGPL]].
