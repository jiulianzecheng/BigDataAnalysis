 '''Prahins''' was a [[municipality]] of the district [[Jura-Nord vaudois (district)|Jura-Nord vaudois]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Vaud]] in [[Switzerland]]. The former municipality of Prahins merged on 1 January 2012 into the municipality of [[Donneloye]]. < ref name=Fusion > [http://www.bfs.admin.ch/bfs/portal/de/index/infothek/nomenklaturen/blank/blank/gem_liste/03.html Amtliches Gemeindeverzeichnis der Schweiz] published by the Swiss Federal Statistical Office {{in lang|de}} accessed 21 December 2011 < /ref >
