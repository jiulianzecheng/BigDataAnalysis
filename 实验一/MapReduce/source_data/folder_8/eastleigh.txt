'''Eastleigh''' is a town in [[Hampshire]], [[England]], between [[Southampton]] and [[Winchester]]. It was originally developed as a [[railway]] town by the [[London and South Western Railway]]. 
 
 The town lies on the [[River Itchen, Hampshire|River Itchen]], one of England's premier chalk streams for [[fly fishing]], and a designated [[site of Special Scientific Interest]].
