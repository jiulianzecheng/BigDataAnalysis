 '''Supergirl''' is the name of several [[fiction|fictional]] [[superheroines|superheroine]] characters appearing in [[American comic book]]s published by [[DC Comics]]. The original and most well known Supergirl is '''[[Supergirl (Kara Zor-El)|Kara Zor-El]]''', also known as the cousin of the superhero [[Superman]].  
 
 The character made her first appearance in ''Action Comics'' #252 (May 1959) and was created by [[Otto Binder]] and [[Al Plastino]].  The character has been played by actress [[Helen Slater]] in ''Supergirl'' (1984), and by [[Laura Vandervoort]] in the TV series ''[[Smallville]]'' (2001-2011). In 2015, the live-action [[Arrowverse]] series ''[[Supergirl (TV series)|Supergirl]]'' debuted on [[CBS]] and then moved to [[The CW]] after the first season and is played by [[Melissa Benoist]]. [[Sasha Calle]] appears as Supergirl in the [[DC Extended Universe]] movie ''[[The Flash (movie)|The Flash]]'' (2023).
