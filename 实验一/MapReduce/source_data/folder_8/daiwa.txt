 '''Daiwa Securities Group''' is a Japanese [[investment bank]] that is the second largest [[brokerage firm|securities brokerage]] after [[Nomura Securities]]. < ref > {{Cite news|title=Profit of Japan's Second-Biggest Brokerage Plunges 64%|work=Bloomberg|url=https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2022-04-27/daiwa-s-fourth-quarter-net-income-plunges-to-141-million#xj4y7vzkg|access-date=30 June 2022}} < /ref > 
 
 Major subsidiaries include ''Daiwa Securities'', which offers retail services such as [[online trading]] to individual investors and investment banking services in Japan as well as ''Daiwa Capital Markets'', the firm's international investment banking arm (with a presence across Asia, Europe and North America) that provides [[Mergers and Acquisitions|M & A]] advisory, sales and trading services in a variety of financial products to corporate and institutional clients. Other group companies provide [[asset management]], research and [[private equity fund]] services. < ref > {{Cite news|title=Who Will Be Holding the Bag?|work=The Real Deal|url=https://therealdeal.com/issues_articles/who-will-be-holding-the-bag/|access-date=2022-06-30}} < /ref > 
 
 The company is the fourth largest shareholder in [[SL Green]].
