 '''EXID''' is a five-member [[South Korea]]n-Pop [[girl group]]. Its members are Solji, LE, Hani, Hyelin and Junghwa. < ref name=kpop > {{cite web |url=http://www.dkpopnews.net/2013/03/exid-profile.html |title=EXID Profile |date=12 March 2013 |publisher=Daily K Pop News |access-date=24 July 2015 |archive-date=20 May 2019 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190520144155/https://www.dkpopnews.net/2013/03/exid-profile.html |url-status=dead }} < /ref >  They [[wikt:debut|debut]]ed on February 12, 2012 with the [[wikt:release|release]] of  " [[Holla (EXID song)|Holla]] " . < ref > {{cite web |url=http://nowkpop.com/exid-profile/ |title=EXID Profile |publisher=NowKpop |access-date=24 July 2015 |archive-date=8 September 2015 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150908190838/http://nowkpop.com/exid-profile/ |url-status=dead }} < /ref >  The [[wikt:quintet|quintet]] released their first [[extended play|EP]] ''[[Hippity Hop]]'' in August 2012. It was met with [[wikt:limited|limited]] [[wikt:success|success]]. Their 2014 [[Single (music)|single]]  " [[Up  &  Down (EXID song)|Up  &  Down]] " , however, reached number one on the [[Gaon Singles Chart]] four months after its release. 
 They mostly became more famous after the fancam of the member  "  hani  "  which hits 10 Views on YouTube . 
 
 EXID released their next [[Extended play|mini-album]] ''[[Ah Yeah (EP)|Ah Yeah]]'' on April 13, 2015. The [[song]] of the same name  " Ah Yeah "  was its [[lead single]]. The single also was met with [[Commerce|commercial]] success as it peaked at the [[Wikt:runner-up|runner-up]] position on the Gaon Singles Chart.
