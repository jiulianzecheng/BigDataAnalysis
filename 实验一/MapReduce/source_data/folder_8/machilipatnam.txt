'''Machilipatnam,''' also known as Masulipatnam, is a city in the [[Krishna district]]. It is a municipal corporation and the administrative headquarters of Krishna district. < ref > {{cite web|title=Mandals in Krishna district|url=http://www.aponline.gov.in/quick%20links/apfactfile/info%20on%20districts/Krishna.html|publisher=aponline.gov.in|access-date=4 February 2016|archive-date=13 December 2014|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20141213093713/http://www.aponline.gov.in/quick%20links/apfactfile/info%20on%20districts/krishna.html|url-status=dead}} < /ref >  It is also the headquarters of [[Machilipatnam mandal]] in [[Machilipatnam revenue division]] of the district. < ref > {{cite web|title=Krishna District Mandals |url=http://censusindia.gov.in/2011census/maps/atlas/28part31.pdf|publisher=Census of India |access-date=18 January 2015|page=517}} < /ref > < ref name= " census " > {{cite web|title=District Census Handbook â€“ Krishna |url=http://www.censusindia.gov.in/2011census/dchb/2816_PART_B_DCHB_KRISHNA.pdf|website=Census of India|access-date=8 January 2016|page=15,16}} < /ref >  The ancient port town served as the settlement of European traders from the 16th century, and it was a major trading port for the [[British people|British]], [[Dutch people|Dutch]] and [[French people|French]] in the seventeenth century. < ref > {{cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=5qkVLVpJmyoC|title=Social Sci.
