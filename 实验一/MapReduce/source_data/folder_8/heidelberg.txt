 '''Heidelberg''' ({{lang-gsw|Heidelbärg}}) is a city by the [[Neckar]] river in the south-west of [[Germany]] in the [[States of Germany|state]] of [[Baden-Württemberg]]. 
 
 It has about 150,000 [[inhabitant]]s. It has a well-known [[university]]. The old town and the castle of Heidelberg are well-known [[tourist]] attractions of Germany. 
 
 Heidelberg has an [[oceanic climate]] (''Cfb'' in the [[Köppen climate classification]]).
