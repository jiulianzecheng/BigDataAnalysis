 '''Mettauertal''' is a [[municipality]] of the [[district]] [[Laufenburg (district)|Laufenburg]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Aargau]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 On 1 January 2010, the former municipalities of [[Etzgen]], [[Hottwil]], [[Mettau, Switzerland|Mettau]], [[Oberhofen, Switzerland|Oberhofen]] and [[Wil, Aargau|Wil]] merged into the new municipality of Mettauertal.
