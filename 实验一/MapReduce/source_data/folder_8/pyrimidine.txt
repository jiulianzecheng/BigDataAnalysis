 '''Pyrimidines''' include three of the [[Base (chemistry)|bases]] in [[DNA]] and [[RNA]].

 *[[Cytosine]] (C),  
 *[[Thymine]] (T),  
 *[[Uracil]] (U). 
 *[[Thiamine]] ([[vitamin B1]]) 
   
 It is also found in many synthetic compounds such as [[barbiturate]]s and the [[HIV]] drug, [[zidovudine]]. [[Uric acid]] is a pyrimidine derivative.
