 '''Alumni''' (singular: '''alumnus''' (masculine) or '''alumna''' (feminine)) are people who used to be [[student]]s of a [[school]], [[college]], or [[university]] who have either attended or [[graduated]] in some way from the institution. 
 
 The [[Latin]] [[noun]] ''alumnus'' means  " foster son "  or  " pupil "  and comes from the [[verb]] ''alere''  " to nourish " .
