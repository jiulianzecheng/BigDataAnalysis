 ==Geography== 
 Itabashi is in northern Tokyo. It borders [[Toshima, Tokyo|Toshima]] to the south.  [[Nerima, Tokyo|Nerima]] is on the ward's western border. The [[Arakawa River]] and [[Saitama Prefecture]] are the norther border.  [[Kita, Tokyo|Kita]] is on the east.
