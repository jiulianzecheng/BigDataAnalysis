 '''Schondorf am Ammersee''' is a [[Municipalities of Germany|municipality]] in [[Landsberg (district)|Landsberg am Lech]], [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]] and is a member of the [[Schondorf (Verwaltungsgemeinschaft)|municipal association Schondorf am Ammersee]]. The municipal association based in Schondorf. 
 
 == Geography == 
 
 === Geographical Location === 
 Schondorf is a climatic spa village. It is about {{convert|40|km|mi|abbr=off}} west of [[Munich]] and about {{convert|40|km|mi|abbr=off}} southeast of [[Augsburg]] on the western shore of lake [[Ammersee]] in the [[Landsberg (district)|district of Landsberg am Lech]]. 
 
 As the place is on the edge of Lake Ammersee, is the maximum height difference in the municipality area is {{convert|70|m|yd|abbr=off}}. 
 
 {{wide image|Schondorf-Ammersee.jpg|800px|align-cap=center|Panoramic picture Schondorf, viewed from the east}} 
 
 === Expansion of the municipality === 
 The area of the municipality is {{convert|6.56|km2|sqmi|abbr=on}}. The maximum amount of extension in the north–south direction is {{convert|4|km|mi|abbr=on}} and west–east  direction {{convert|2.5|km|mi|abbr=on}}. 
 
 === Municipality in the neighborhood === 
 The neighboring municipalities of Schondorf are: [[Greifenberg]] in the north, [[Eching am Ammersee]] in the north-east, [[Utting]] in the south and [[Windach]] in the west. 
 
 === Subdivisions === 
 Schondorf has two subdivisions: ''Oberschondorf'' on the western and upper side and ''Unterschondorf'' on the eastern and lower side.
