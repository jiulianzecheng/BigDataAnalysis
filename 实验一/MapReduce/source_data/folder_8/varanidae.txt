 '''Varanidae''' is the [[family (biology)|family]] of monitor lizards. The family is a group of [[carnivore|carnivorous]] [[lizard]]s. It includes the largest living lizard, the [[Komodo dragon]], and the [[crocodile monitor]]. Varanidae contains the living [[genus]] ''[[Varanus]]'' and some [[extinct]] groups.
