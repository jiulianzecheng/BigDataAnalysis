 '''Botflies''', also known as [[warble flies]] or  [[gadflies]], are a family of flies: the Oestridae. Their [[Larva|larvae]] are internal [[parasites]] of [[mammals]], some [[species]] growing in the host's flesh and others within the [[gut]].
