 A '''booby''' is a [[seabird]] in the [[genus]] Sula, in the order [[Suliformes]]. Boobies are large birds with long pointed wings and bills. They hunt [[fish]] and [[squid]] by diving into the sea and [[wikt:chasing|chasing]] their [[prey]] underwater. Facial air sacs under their skin help protect the [[wikt:impact|impact]] with the water. Boobies get their name because of their [[wikt:lack|lack]] of fear of humans.
