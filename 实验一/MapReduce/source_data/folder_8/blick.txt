 '''Blick''' is a [[Switzerland|Swiss]] daily newspaper. It has a [[magazine circulation|circulation]] that reaches about 472.000 readers a day. It was founded in 1959, as a [[tabloid]]. Like most tabloid newspapers, Blick is on right-wing of the political spectrum.
