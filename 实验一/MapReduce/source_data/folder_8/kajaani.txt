 '''Kajaani''' ({{lang-sv|Kajana}}) is a [[town]] in the region of [[Kainuu]], [[Finland]]. In 2009, it had a population of about 38,000. It covers an area of {{convert|2000|km2}}. 
 
 The [[forestry]] and [[lumber]] industries are important in Kajaani. < ref > Factum, part 3, page 204, ''Finnish'' {{ISBN|9513566420}} 003 1 398 < /ref > 
 
 The former municipality of [[Vuolijoki]] was merged with Kajaani in 2007.
