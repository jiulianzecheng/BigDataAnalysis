 '''Wildflecken''' is a [[municipality]] in the [[Bad Kissingen]] district, at the border of northwestern [[Bavaria]] and southern [[Hesse]]. In 2005, its population was 3,285; the postal code is 97772 (US Forces used APO NY 09026). Wildflecken is in the picturesque [[Rhön Mountains|Rhön mountains]].
