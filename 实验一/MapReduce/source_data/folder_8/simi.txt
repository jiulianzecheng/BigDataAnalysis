 '''Simisola Bolatito Ogunleye''' (born {{birth date|1988|4|19}}), also known as '''Simi''', is a [[Nigerians|Nigerian]] [[singer]] and [[songwriter]] best known for her [[Hit record|hit]] singles  " Tiff "  and  " Jamb Question " . She was signed to X3M [[Music]] since 2014 < ref > {{cite news|url=http://life.guardian.ng/2016/04/simi-the-new-face-of-alternative-music/|title=Simi: The New Face Of Alternative Music|work=The Guardian Life Magazine|last=Sakoh|first=Makale|date=4 April 2016|access-date=4 May 2016}} < /ref > 
 
 ==Early life== 
 Simi was born in Surulere on 19 April 1988, [[Lagos State]]. She graduated from [[Covenant University]] where she majored in [[Mass Communication]]. < ref > {{cite web | url=https://punchng.com/i-fantasise-about-working-with-ebenezer-obey-simi/ | title=I fantasise about working with Ebenezer Obey –Simi | date=2 April 2016 }} < /ref > 
 
 ==Career== 
 In 2006, Simi released her first project titled ''Ogaju'', a [[gospel music]] studio album that was entirely produced by [[Samklef]]. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://encomium.ng/female-artistes-dont-have-to-expose-their-bodies-to-be-successful-tiff-crooner-simi/|title='Female artistes don't have to expose their bodies to be successful'-Tiff crooner, Simi|work=Encomium Magazine|date=26 October 2014 |access-date=3 May 2016}} < /ref > 
 
 In 2014, Simi released an EP tited ''Restless'', a 5-track cover of popular international songs which earned her a record deal with X3M Music in April 2014. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://notjustok.com/2014/01/09/download-simi-restless-ep/|title=DOWNLOAD:DOWNLOAD: Simi - Restless EP –notjustOk|work=notjustOk|date=10 January 2014|access-date=3 May 2016|archive-date=23 March 2016|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160323203859/http://notjustok.com/2014/01/09/download-simi-restless-ep|url-status=dead}} < /ref > < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.vanguardngr.com/2016/03/why-i-do-love-songs-simi/|title=Why I do love songs – Simi|date=17 March 2016|work=Vanguard News|access-date=3 May 2016}} < /ref >
