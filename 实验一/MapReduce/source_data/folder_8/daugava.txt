The '''Daugava''' ({{lang-ltg|Daugova}}) or '''Western Dvina''' is a [[river]] rising in the [[Valdai Hills]], [[Russia]], flowing through Russia, [[Belarus]], and [[Latvia]] and into the [[Gulf of Riga]].  Within Latvia it flows through [[Latgale]], [[Zemgale]], [[Vidzeme]] and [[Riga]], before flowing into the Gulf of Riga. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.lonelyplanet.com/latvia/daugava-river-valley|title=Introducing Daugava River Valley|publisher=[[Lonely Planet]]|access-date=5 February 2016}} < /ref >
