'''''Renfield''''' is an upcoming American [[dark fantasy]] [[horror-comedy]] film directed by [[Chris McKay]] and written by Ryan Ridley. It is based on an original pitch by [[Robert Kirkman]]. It stars [[Nicholas Hoult]] as [[Renfield]], a henchman to [[Count Dracula]] who decides to leave his line of work after falling in love. [[Nicolas Cage]], [[Awkwafina]], [[Ben Schwartz]], [[Adrian Martinez (actor)|Adrian Martinez]], [[Shohreh Aghdashloo]], [[Bess Rous]], and James Moses Black co-star. The film is scheduled to be released on April 14, 2023, by [[Universal Pictures]]. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.hollywoodreporter.com/movies/movie-news/about-renfield-release-date-april-2023-1235099693/|title=Universal Sets Nicholas Hoult's ''Renfield'' for 2023 Release|date=February 25, 2022|first=Aaron|last=Couch|website=[[The Hollywood Reporter]]|access-date=February 25, 2022}} < /ref >
