'''''Please''''' is a word used in the [[English language]] to show [[politeness]] and [[respect]] while asking for something. It is short for the phrase  " if you please "  or  " if it please(s) you " . < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.abajournal.com/magazine/article/what_judges_really_think_about_the_phrase_may_it_please_the_court|title=What judges really think about the phrase 'May it please the Court?'|first=Bryan|last=Garner|publisher=ABA Journal|date=April 1, 2013}} < /ref >  In modern days, the phrase is taught to children by their parents as a way to teach politeness.
