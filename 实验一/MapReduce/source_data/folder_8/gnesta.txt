 '''Gnesta''' is an [[urban area]] in the [[counties of Sweden|county]] of [[Södermanland County|Södermanland]] in [[Sweden]]. It is the seat of [[Gnesta Municipality]]. Parts of the urban area span into [[Södertälje Municipality]] within [[Stockholm County]].
