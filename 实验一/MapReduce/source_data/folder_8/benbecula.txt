 '''Benbecula''' ({{lang-gd|Beinn nam Fadhla}}) is an [[island]] of the [[Outer Hebrides]] in the [[Atlantic Ocean]] off the west coast of [[Scotland]]. < ref name=MacT > Mac an Tàilleir, Iain (2003) [http://www.scottish.parliament.uk/vli/language/gaelic/vl-trans.htm#places ''Placenames'']. (pdf) Pàrlamaid na h-Alba. Retrieved 20 August 2008. < /ref >  It forms part of the area administered by the Western Isles Council. 
 
 In the 2001 census it had a resident population of 1,249, with a sizable percentage of [[Roman Catholic]]s. This is slightly unusual because the [[Highlands and Islands]] is generally the religious territory of the Wee Frees, the [[Free Church of Scotland]] and its various offshoots.
