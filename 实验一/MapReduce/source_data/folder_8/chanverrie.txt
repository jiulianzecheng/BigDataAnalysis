 '''Chanverrie''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is found in the region [[Pays de la Loire]] in the [[Vendée]] department in the west of [[France]].  The municipality was created on 1 January 2019 and consists of the former communes of [[La Verrie]] (the seat) and [[Chambretaud]]. < ref > [https://www.legifrance.gouv.fr/eli/arrete/2018/11/21/TERB1833280A/jo/texte Arrêté préfectoral] 21 November 2018 {{in lang|fr}} < /ref >
