 '''Tripod''' is a word generally used to refer to a three-legged object, usually one used as a platform of some sort, and comes from the [[Greek language|Greek]] ''tripous'', meaning  " three feet " .  
 
 '''Tripod''' may also mean: 
 * [[Tripod (photography)|A camera's stand]] 
 * A portable [[crane (machine)]] 
 * [[Visual Basic#Evolution of Visual Basic|Visual Basic]], whose forerunner was called tripod
