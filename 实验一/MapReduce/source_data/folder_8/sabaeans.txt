The '''Sabaens''' were one of the first civilizations of the [[Arabian Peninsula|Arabian Peninsula.]] They spoke the Sabaean language, one of the Old South Arabian languages. They founded the kingdom of '''Saba æ''' in modern-day [[Yemen]], which was believed to be the biblical land of [[Sheba]] and  " the oldest and most important of the South Arabian kingdoms " .
