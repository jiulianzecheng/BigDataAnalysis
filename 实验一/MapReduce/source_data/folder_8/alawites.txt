 The '''Alawites''', also known as '''Alawis''' (''ʿAlawīyyah'' {{lang-ar|علوية}}) are a [[sect]] within [[Shia Islam]]. They live mainly in [[Syria]]. They follow a branch of the [[Twelvers|Twelver]] school of Shia Islam. Alawites worship [[Ali]], and the name  " Alawi "  means followers of Ali.
