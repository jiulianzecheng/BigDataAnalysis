 '''Traunstein''' is a [[Town#Germany|town]] in the south-eastern part of [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]], and is the administrative center of a much larger [[Traunstein (district)|district]] of the same name. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 The town is at the middle of a region called [[Chiemgau]], about 11 & nbsp;km east of [[Chiemsee|Lake Chiemsee]] between [[Munich]] and [[Salzburg]], 15 & nbsp;km north of the [[Alps]], and 30 & nbsp;km west of [[Salzburg]].
