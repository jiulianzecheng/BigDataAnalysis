 '''Secunderabad''' is a city in the state of [[Telangana]], India. It is popularly called the twin [[city]] of [[Hyderabad]]. 
 
 ==History== 
 Secunderabad was founded in the year 1806 as a [[British]] cantonment. Secunderabad and Hyderabad together form the sixth biggest metropolis in India. It is one of the biggest cantonments in [[India]]. It has a large presence of army and [[air force]]. 
 
 ==Demographics== 
 The population of Secunderabad is 204,182. The literacy rate of Secunderabad is 73%. The most popular sport is [[cricket]].
