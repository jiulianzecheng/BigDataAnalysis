 '''Tlalpan''' ({{lang-nci|Tlālpan||place on the earth}}) is a [[Boroughs of Mexico City|borough]] (''demarcación territorial'') in [[Mexico City]]. It is the largest borough. < ref name= " conoce " / >  The rest, almost all of it on the northern edge, has been urban since the mid-20th century.
