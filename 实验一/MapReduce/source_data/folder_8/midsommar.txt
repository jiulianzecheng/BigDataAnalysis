 '''''Midsommar''''' is a 2019 [[folk]] [[horror movie]] written and directed by [[Ari Aster]]. < ref > {{Cite web|url=https://bbfc.co.uk/releases/midsommar-2019|title=MIDSOMMAR {{!}} British Board of Film Classification|website=bbfc.co.uk|access-date=2020-01-11}} < /ref >  The movie stars [[Florence Pugh]], [[Jack Reynor]], [[William Jackson Harper]], [[Vilhelm Blomgren]], and [[Will Poulter]]. It follows a group of friends who travel to [[Sweden]] for a [[Summer solstice|midsummmer]] [[festival]] that occurs once every ninety years and find themselves in the [[:wikt:clutches|clutches]] of a [[danger|dangerous]] [[pagan]] [[cult]].
