 '''Barmouth''' ([[Welsh language|Welsh]]: '''Abermaw''' (formal); ''Y Bermo'' (informal)) is a seaside town and community in the county of [[Gwynedd]], [[Wales]]. It lies on the [[estuary]] of the [[River Mawddach]] and [[Cardigan Bay]]. It is located in the historic county of [[Merionethshire]]. 
 
 In the 2011 census, the town had a population of 2,522 people. 
 
 The town grew around the [[shipbuilding]] industry, and more recently grew as a [[seaside resort]]. It is a popular holiday destination for people around the [[West Midlands (region)|West Midlands]] area of [[England]], with thousands of holiday makers and tourists flocking to the town from this region every year. 
 
 The town has a [[Barmouth railway station|railway station]] located on the [[Cambrian Line|Cambrian Coast Line]] between [[Pwllheli]] and [[Shrewsbury]].
