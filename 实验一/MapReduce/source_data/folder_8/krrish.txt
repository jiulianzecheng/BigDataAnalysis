 '''Krrish''' is a 2006 [[Hindi]]-language superhero film directed, produced, and written by Rakesh Roshan. The film contains [[Hrithik Roshan]] and [[Priyanka Chopra]] in the lead roles as well as Naseeruddin Shah, Rekha, Manini Mishra, Archana Puran Singh and Sharat Saxena in the supporting roles. This movie is a sequel to [[Koi... Mil Gaya]]. The film had a global release on June 23, 2006. It received positive reviews and went on to become a blockbuster. It was also the third highest-grossing Indian film of the year. < ref name= " Kr "  / >  The movie followed with [[Krrish 3]] in 2013. Also, in 2013, Toonz Animation India produced a Indian animated television film series of 4 Indian animated films that are also a part of the Krrish franchise and it focuses on the adventures on the kid version of Krrish, which is why it is called [[Kid Krrish]]. A fourth sequel to this film titled [[Krrish 4]] is also in development, which will be released on December 30, 2022. 
 
 ==Plot== 
 A young man named Krrish has inherited supernatural powers thanks to his father's encounter with an alien.
