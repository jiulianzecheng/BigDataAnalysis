 The '''rural district Mittelsachsen''' is a district in [[Saxony]], [[Germany]]. It was founded in August, 2008. Mittelsachsen is made up of the former rural districts [[Freiberg Rural District|Freiberg]], [[Döbeln Rural District|Döbeln]] and [[Mittweida Rural District|Mittweida]].
