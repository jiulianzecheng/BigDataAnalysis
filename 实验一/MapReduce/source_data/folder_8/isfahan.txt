 '''Isfahan''' or '''Esfahān''' (historically also rendered as ''Ispahan'' or ''Hispahan'', {{lang-peo|Aspadana}}, {{lang-pal|Spahān}}, {{lang-fa|اصفهان}} ''Esfahān'') is the capital of [[Esfahan Province]] in [[Iran]].  It is about 340 & nbsp;km south of [[Tehran]]. It has a population of 1,583,609.  It is Iran's third largest city after Tehran and [[Mashhad]]. 
 
 [[File:سی و سه پل1.jpg|thumb|left|alt=|200px|Si-o-se Pol]] 
 
 Isfahan was once one of the largest cities in the world. It was strong from 1050 to 1722, particularly in the 16th century under the [[Safavids|Safavid]] dynasty.  During this time it was the capital of [[Persian Empire|Persia]] for the second time in its history. Even today, the city keeps much of its past glory. It is famous for its [[Islamic architecture|Islamic architecture,]] with many beautiful [[boulevard]] < nowiki/ > s, covered bridges, [[Palace|palaces]], [[Mosque|mosques]], and [[Minaret|minarets]]. 
 
 The [[Naghsh-e Jahan Square]] in Isfahan is one of the largest city squares in the world.  It is an outstanding example of Iranian and Islamic architecture. It is a [[UNESCO]] [[World Heritage Site]]. The city also has a many historic [[monument]] < nowiki/ > s.
