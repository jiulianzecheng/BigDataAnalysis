'''Abdiel''' is a name with a long-standing history and deep significance. It has been in use for many centuries and originates from [[Hebrew language|Hebrew.]] The name is formed from two Hebrew words,  " ab "  which translates to  " servant "  and  " el "  which translates to  " God. "  As a result, the name Abdiel conveys the meaning of  " one who serves God.
