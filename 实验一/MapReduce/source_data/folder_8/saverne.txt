 '''Saverne''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]] in north-eastern [[France]], in the [[Bas-Rhin]] [[Departments of France|department]], of which it is a [[Subprefectures in France|subprefecture]]. It is in the [[Grand Est]] [[Regions of France|region]]. 
 
 The ''commmune'' is in the [[History|historical]] and [[Culture|cultural]] region of [[Alsace]]. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 Saverne is in the west of the Bas-Rhin department, {{Convert|50|km|mi|abbr=on }} to the west of [[Strasbourg]]. The Marne–Rhine Canal flows through the city of Saverne. The [[Zorn (river)|Zorn]] [[river]], a right [[tributary]] of the [[Moder (river)|Moder]] river, flows through the ''commune''. 
 
 The ''commune'' has an area of {{Convert|26.0|km2|sqmi|abbr=on }}. < ref name= " insee-geo " > {{cite web |url=https://www.insee.fr/fr/statistiques/1405599?geo=COM-67437 |title=Commune de Saverne (67437) |work=Comparateur de territoire |publisher=Institut national de la statistique et des études économiques - INSEE |language=fr |access-date=27 December 2017 }} < /ref >  Its average [[altitude]] is {{Convert|320|m|ft|abbr=on}}; at the [[City and town halls|city hall]], the altitude is {{Convert|190|m|ft|abbr=on }}. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://www.map-france.com/Saverne-67700/ |title=City of Saverne |publisher=Map-France.com |access-date=27 December 2017 }} < /ref >

 ==Administration== 
 Saverne is a [[Subprefectures in France|subprefecture]] in the [[Bas-Rhin]] department since 1800. It is the capital of the [[Arrondissement of Saverne|Saverne]] ''arrondissement'' and is also the [[administrative centre]] ({{lang-fr|chef-lieu}}) of the ''canton'' of [[Canton of Saverne|Saverne]] formed by 49 ''communes'', with 49,469 inhabitants (2014). < ref name=insee1 > {{cite web|url=https://www.insee.fr/fr/metadonnees/cog/commune/COM67437-saverne |title=Commune de Saverne (67437) |work= Géographie administrative et d’étude |publisher=Institut national de la statistique et des études économiques - INSEE |language=fr |access-date=27 December 2017 }} < /ref >
