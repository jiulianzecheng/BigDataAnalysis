 '''''Midnights''''' is the tenth studio album by American singer Taylor Swift. It was released on October 21, 2022. 
 
 The lead single,  " [[Anti-Hero (song)|Anti-Hero]] " , was released the same day as the album. < ref > {{Citation|title=Taylor Swift - Anti-Hero|url=https://www.discogs.com/master/2859019-Taylor-Swift-Anti-Hero|language=en|access-date=2022-11-12}} < /ref >  It entered the ''Billboard'' Hot 100 at number one and became Swift's ninth US number-one song. 
 
 ''Midnights'' debuted at the top of the [[Billboard charts|''Billboard'' 200]] chart in the US with over 1.5 million copies sold in its first week there alone. It became the first album to sell over a million copies in its first week since [[Adele]]'s ''[[25 (Adele album)|25]]'' (2015). < ref > {{Cite web|last=Caulfield|first=Keith|last2=Caulfield|first2=Keith|date=2022-10-30|title=Taylor Swift’s ‘Midnights’ Blasts in at No. 1 on Billboard 200 Chart With Biggest Week for an Album in 7 Years|url=https://www.billboard.com/music/chart-beat/taylor-swift-midnights-debut-number-one-billboard-200-albums-chart-1235163377/|access-date=2022-11-12|website=Billboard|language=en-US}} < /ref >   After the album's release, Swift became the first artist to chart within the entire top ten of the Hot 100. < ref > {{Cite web|last=Trust|first=Gary|last2=Trust|first2=Gary|date=2022-10-31|title=Taylor Swift Makes History as First Artist With Entire Top 10 on Billboard Hot 100, Led by ‘Anti-Hero’ at No. 1|url=https://www.billboard.com/music/chart-beat/taylor-swift-all-hot-100-top-10-anti-hero-1235163664/|access-date=2022-11-12|website=Billboard|language=en-US}} < /ref >
