 '''Greenside''' is a village in the west of the Metropolitan County of [[Tyne and Wear]], England. It used to be in [[County Durham]] but it became incorporated into Tyne and Wear in 1974 and then the [[Metropolitan Borough of Gateshead]] in 1986.
