The name '''Antonia''' [an-toh-nee-uh, -tohn-yuh] < ref > {{Cite web|title=Definition of Antonia {{!}} Dictionary.com|url=https://www.dictionary.com/browse/antonia|access-date=2023-03-15|website=www.dictionary.com|language=en}} < /ref > is a [[female]] [[given name]]. This name is from Roman origin. It is a feminine form of the name '''Antonio'''. The meanings of the name are  " priceless " ,  " beautiful "  and  " praiseworthy " . < ref > {{Cite web|title=Antonia name Meaning and Origin|url=https://babys.name/detail-Antonia-Meaning-Origin-Famous-Similar-Sibling.html|access-date=2023-03-15|website=babys.name}} < /ref >   
 
 There are several princesses and famous people named ''Antonia''. A [[French language|French]] form of the name is '''Antoinette'''.This is a unique name (not in the top 1000 newborn names in the US in 2021). < ref > {{Cite web|title=Popular Baby Names|url=https://www.ssa.gov/OACT/babynames/|access-date=2023-03-15|website=www.ssa.gov|language=en}} < /ref >
