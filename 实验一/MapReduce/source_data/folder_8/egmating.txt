 '''Egmating''' is a community in the Upper Bavarian district of [[Ebersberg (district)|Ebersberg]] in [[Germany]]. It is a member of the administrative community (''[[Verwaltungsgemeinschaft]]'') of [[Glonn]]. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 Egmating is in the [[Munich]] region. It includes one traditional rural land unit – ''Gemarkung'' in German – also called Egmating.
