 '''Heinerscheid''' ({{lang-lb|Hengescht}}) is a former [[Communes of Luxembourg|commune]] in northern [[Luxembourg]]. It was part of the [[Clervaux (canton)|canton of Clervaux]]. 
 
 On 1 January 2012, the commune was merged with [[Munshausen]] to form the commune of [[Clervaux]].
