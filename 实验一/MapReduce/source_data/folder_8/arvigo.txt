 '''Arvigo''' was a [[municipality]] in the [[district]] of [[Moesa (district)|Moesa]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Graubünden]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 On 1 January 2015 the former municipalities of Arvigo, [[Braggio]], [[Cauco]] and [[Selma, Switzerland|Selma]] merged into the new municipality of [[Calanca]].
