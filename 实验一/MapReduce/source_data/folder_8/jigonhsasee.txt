'''Jigonhsasee''' (alternately spelled '''Jikonhsaseh''' and '''Jikonsase''') was one of the founders of the [[Iroquois|Iroquois Confederacy]]. The other founders were the [[Great Peacemaker]] and [[Hiawatha]]. She is known as the Mother of Nations. According to legend, the Peacemaker came to Jigonhsasee. He told her about his mission to build a confederacy.
