" '''Everytime''' "  is the third single released off of [[Britney Spears]]' fourth album ''[[In the Zone]]''. It was released in March 2004. It was co-written by Spears herself. It hit the top 40 of the [[Billboard Hot 100|''Billboard'' Hot 100]].
