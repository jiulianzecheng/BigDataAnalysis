 '''Caraga''' is an [[Regions of the Philippines|administrative region]] in the [[Philippines]]. It is also known as '''Region XIII'''. It is on the northeastern part of the island of [[Mindanao]]. [[Butuan]] is the regional administrative center. 
 
 There are five provinces: [[Agusan del Norte]], [[Agusan del Sur]],  [[Dinagat Islands]], [[Surigao del Norte]], and [[Surigao del Sur]].
