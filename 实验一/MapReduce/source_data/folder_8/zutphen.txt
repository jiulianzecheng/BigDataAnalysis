 [[File:Ltspkr.png]] '''[[Media:Nl-Zutphen.ogg|Zutphen]]''' is a municipality and city in the province of [[Gelderland]], the Netherlands. It lies both in the [[Achterhoek]] and [[IJsselvallei]] regions, about 30 km northeast of [[Arnhem]]. It is on the east bank of the [[IJssel]] river, except for a small part. About 48,000 people were living in Zutphen in 2021. 
 
 Its history goes back over 1700 years. There are a lot of buildings in the city that show its long and rich history as a trading city as part of the [[Hanseatic League]]. Zutphen got [[city rights]] around 1200. It is linked to [[Deventer]] and Arnhem by rail. The [[Twentekanaal]] starts at the northern municipal border with [[Lochem]]. The [[Berkel]] river flows into the IJssel river in Zutphen city. 
 
 The municipality of [[Warnsveld]] joined Zutphen in 2005.
