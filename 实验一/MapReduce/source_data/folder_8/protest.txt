 A '''protest''' is when a number of [[wikt:people|people]] come together in public to show others that they strongly like or dislike a social condition or a government policy. For example, some people protest [[racism]] or [[war]].  
 [[File:Martinique - manifestation 0 chrlord√©cone.webm|thumb|A protest against [[insecticide]] poisoning in [[Martinique]].]] 
 There are many ways in which people can protest. Protesters can do things like write [[Letter (paper)|letters]], [[Hunger strike|not eat food]], sing [[protest song|songs]], or [[riot]].
