 '''Bascharage''' ({{lang-lb|Nidderkäerjeng}}, {{lang-de|Niederkerschen}}) is a former [[Communes of Luxembourg|commune]] in [[Luxembourg]]. It was part of the [[Capellen (canton)|canton of Capellen]]. 
 
 Since 2012, it is part of the new commune of [[Käerjeng]].
