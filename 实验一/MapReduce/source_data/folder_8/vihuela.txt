[[File:Vihuela Mexicana.jpg|thumb|20th century Mexican vihuela.]] 
 '''Vihuela''' is a name given to two different [[guitar]]-like [[string instrument]]s: one from 16th century [[Spain]], usually with 12 paired strings, and the other, the [[Mexican vihuela]], from 20th century [[Mexico]] with five strings and typically played in [[Mariachi]] bands.
