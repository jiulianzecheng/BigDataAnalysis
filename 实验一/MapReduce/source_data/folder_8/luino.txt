 '''Luino''' is a ''[[comune]]'' in the [[Province of Varese]] in [[Lombardy]], [[Italy]]. It has about 14,000 inhabitants. 
 
 It is near the border with [[Switzerland]] on the eastern shore of [[Lake Maggiore]].
