 '''Arosa''' is a [[municipality]] and a [[ski resort]] in the [[district]] of [[Plessur (district)|Plessur]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Graubünden]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 On 1 January 2013 the former municipalities of [[Calfreisen]], [[Castiel, Switzerland|Castiel]], [[Langwies]], [[Lüen]], [[Molinis]], [[Peist]] and [[St. Peter-Pagig]] merged into the municipality of Arosa.
