 '''Aden''' ({{lang-ar|عدن}} ''{{transl|ar|ʿAdan}}'') is a [[port]] [[city]] in [[Yemen]]. It is at the [[Red Sea]] (the [[Gulf of Aden]]). About 800,000 people live there. Aden was controlled by the British Empire from 1839 to 1967.
