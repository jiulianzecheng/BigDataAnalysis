 The rural district '''Nordsachsen''' is a district in the north of the Free State of [[Saxony]], [[Germany]]. It was founded in August, 2008 and consists of the former rural districts [[Torgau-Oschatz Rural District|Torgau-Oschatz]] and [[Delitzsch Rural District|Delitzsch]].
