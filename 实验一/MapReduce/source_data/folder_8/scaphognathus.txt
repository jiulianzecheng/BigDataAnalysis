 '''''Scaphognathus''''' was a [[rhamphorhyncoid]] [[pterosaur]] found in the [[Solnhofen limestone]] of the  [[Upper Jurassic]].  
 
 It had wings that was about one meter long and a long tail shaped like a [[leaf]]. The leaf-tail probably acted like a [[wikt:rudder|rudder]], helping it steer through the air. It was probably a [[diurnal]] (daytime) flier, where ''[[Rhamphorhynchus]]'' was probably [[nocturnal]].
