 '''Tallow''' is a type of rendered [[fat]]. It can come from [[beef]] or [[mutton]]. It usually has a longer shelf life than [[suet]]. Tallow could also come from other animals like [[American bison|bison]]. [[Lard]] is a type of tallow that comes from [[Pig|pigs]]. Tallow has been used for many things, as a [[soap]] ingredient, a cooking fat, and fuel.
