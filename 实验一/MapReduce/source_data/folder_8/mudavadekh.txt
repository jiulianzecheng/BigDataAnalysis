'''Mudavadekh''' is a village in Khanpur Taluka of Mahisagar District, [[Gujarat]], [[India]]. People of various [[caste]]s of the [[Hindu]] religion live there. Most of the people are from Patel and [[Brahmin]] castes. The village has a school for 1 to 12. A branch of Baroda Gujarat Gramin Bank is also there. The postal index number or PIN code for the village is 389230. The Head Post office is Lunawada. The main source of income for people is farming.
