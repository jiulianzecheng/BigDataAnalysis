'''Tetraoxygen''' ('''O < sub > 4 < /sub > '''), or '''oxozone''', is a [[molecule]] made up of four [[oxygen]] [[atom]]s. It does not exist in nature anywhere and can only be made under extreme conditions in a [[lab]].
