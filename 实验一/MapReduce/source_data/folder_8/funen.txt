 '''Funen''' ({{lang-da|Fyn}}) is an island in [[Denmark]]. It covers an area of {{convert|3,099.7|km2}}. It is the third-largest island in the country. The main city is [[Odense]] which is connected to the sea by a [[canal]] which is rarely used.  
 
 There are [[Bridge|bridges]] that carry [[Road|roads]] and [[Railway|railways]] to and from Funen. The [[Great Belt Bridge]] links Funen to the island of [[Zealand]] in the east, while the [[Old Little Belt Bridge]] and [[New Little Belt Bridge]] link Funen to [[Jutland]] in the west.
