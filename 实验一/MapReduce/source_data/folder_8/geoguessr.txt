 '''''Geoguessr''''' is a [[browser game]] about [[geography]]. Players guess places from [[street view]] pictures. < ref name= " Keating 2013 " > {{cite web |last1=Keating |first1=Joshua |title=Get Dropped Virtually Anywhere In The World And Try To Guess Where You Are |url=https://www.huffingtonpost.co.uk/entry/geoguessr-google-street-view_n_3253721 |website=HuffPost UK |access-date=11 April 2022 |language=en |date=10 May 2013}} < /ref >  It was released in May 2013. The game has been called a great geographical educational tool. < ref name= " vanguardia " > {{cite web |title=Geoguessr: ¿Dónde diablos estoy? |url=https://vanguardia.com.mx/tech/2717413-geoguessr-donde-diablos-estoy-BPVG2717413 |website=vanguardia.com.mx |access-date=11 April 2022 |language=es-MX}} < /ref >  With this game, players learn specific things to help them guess the places they are given such as [[writing]]s, [[soil|soil type]]s, and [[flora]].
