'''Hydroxychloroquine''', sold under the brand name '''Plaquenil''' among others, is a [[drug]] used to prevent and treat [[malaria]]. It can also treat [[rheumatoid arthritis]], [[Lupus erythematosus|lupus]], and [[porphyria cutanea tarda]]. < ref name=AHFS2020 > {{cite web|url=https://www.drugs.com/monograph/hydroxychloroquine-sulfate.html|publisher=The American Society of Health-System Pharmacists|date=20 March 2020|title=Hydroxychloroquine Sulfate Monograph for Professionals|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200320234847/https://www.drugs.com/monograph/hydroxychloroquine-sulfate.html|archive-date=20 March 2020|url-status=live|access-date=20 March 2020}} < /ref > 
 
 Common [[Adverse effect (medicine)|side effects]] may include [[vomiting]], [[headache]], changes in vision, and [[muscle weakness]].
