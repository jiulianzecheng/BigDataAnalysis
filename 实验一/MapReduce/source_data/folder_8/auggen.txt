 '''Auggen''' is a [[municipality]] in [[Breisgau-Hochschwarzwald]] in the [[States of Germany|state]] of [[Baden-Württemberg]] in southern [[Germany]]. It is near the [[France|French]] border. It is at the southern end of [[Breisgau]].
