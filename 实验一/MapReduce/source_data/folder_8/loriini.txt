 '''Lories''' and '''lorikeets''' ([[tribe (biology)|tribe]] '''Loriini''') are small to medium-sized [[arboreal]] [[parrot]]s. They have special brush-tipped tongues to feed on nectar of blossoms and soft fruits, preferably [[berries]].  
 
 They are a [[monophyletic]] group in the parrot family [[Psittacidae]]. [[Sequence analysis]] and morphology show that the group is in the middle of other groups.  
 
 They are widely distributed throughout the [[Australasia]]n region, including south-eastern [[Asia]], [[Polynesia]], [[Papua New Guinea]], [[Timor Leste]] and [[Australia]], and the majority have very brightly coloured plumage.
