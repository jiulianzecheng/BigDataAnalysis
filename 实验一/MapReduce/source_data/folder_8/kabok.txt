 '''Kabok''' or '''Kapok''' is a [[Meitei cuisine|Meitei Manipuri ethnic food item]], generally made up of puffed [[rice]], roasted rice and molasses. It is available in many flavours, including [[honey]] flavor, [[sugarcane]] flavor and many others. It is a very popular snack in the [[States of India|Indian state]] of [[Manipur]], especially among the [[Manipuri ethnicity]].
