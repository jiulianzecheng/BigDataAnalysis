 '''Ebersmunster''' is a [[Communes in France|commune]]. It is in [[Grand Est]] in the [[Bas-Rhin]] [[Departments of France|department]] in northeast [[France]]. 
 
 It is known for its 1727 baroque church, a work by Austrian architect [[Peter Thumb]].
