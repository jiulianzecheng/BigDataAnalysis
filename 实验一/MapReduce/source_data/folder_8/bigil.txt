'''Bigil''' is a 2019 Tamil language sports-action movie. It was written and directed by [[Atlee|Atlee Kumar]]. The movie stars [[Vijay filmography|Vijay]], [[Nayanthara]], [[Vivek]], [[Jackie Shroff]], Kathir. The movie was produced by AGS Entertainment.
