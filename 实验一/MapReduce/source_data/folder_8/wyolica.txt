 '''Wyolica''' is a two-person [[band]] from [[Japan]]. The members are Azumi ([[singer|vocals]]) and So-to ([[guitar]]). The group is not well known outside of Japan. Their sound is very similar of many [[United States|American]] [[Pop music|pop acts]] but sung in [[Japanese language|Japanese]]. 
 
 Their first [[album]],  " Who Said La La? "  was released in [[2000]]. Since then they have released a number of albums and [[song|singles]], including a six-song [[Extended play|EP]] of [[acoustic music|acoustic]] songs.
