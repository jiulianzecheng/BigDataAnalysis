 '''Multimedia''' is [[communication]] that uses more than one type of [[information|information content]] (e.g. [[text]], [[Sound|audio]], [[image|graphics]], [[animation]], [[video]]) to [[Information|inform]] or [[Entertainment|entertain]] the [[audience]] (user). ''Multimedia'' also means the use of electronic [[media]] to store and experience multimedia content. 
 Multimedia means that computer info can be represented through audio, graphics, image, video and animation in addition to traditional media (text and graphics).
