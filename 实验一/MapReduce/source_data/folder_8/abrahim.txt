'''Abrahim''' is a name with [[Arabic language|Arabic]] roots, derived from the biblical name Abraham in [[Hebrew language|Hebrew.]] It is a popular name in the [[Muslims|Muslim]] world, often given to boys as a way to show admiration for the prophet Ibrahim. Ibrahim is a revered figure in Islam, known for his unwavering faith in Allah and willingness to sacrifice his son Ismail. The name Abrahim is associated with qualities such as strength, courage, and faith, which are valued in the Islamic faith. In Arabic, the name means  " father of many, "  symbolizing fertility and abundance. It also means  " exalted father, "  reflecting the high regard in which the prophet Ibrahim is held.
