 '''Kutless''' is a Christian [[rock music|rock]] band formed in 2000. 
 
 ==History== 
 The band chose the name  " Kutless "  from a [[Bible]] verse, Romans 6:23.  They chose this name because  " [ < nowiki > < /nowiki > [[Jesus]] < nowiki > < /nowiki > ] took our cuts for us, leaving us 'Kutless.'
