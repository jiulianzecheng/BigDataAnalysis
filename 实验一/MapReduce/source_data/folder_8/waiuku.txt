 '''Waiuku''' is a country [[town]] in the [[Auckland Region]] in the [[North Island]] of [[New Zealand]]. It is located at the southern end of the [[Waiuku River]]. It is 40 kilometres southwest of [[Auckland]] city centre, and 12 kilometres north of the mouth of the [[Waikato River]].
