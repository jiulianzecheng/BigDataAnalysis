A '''pyrophone''', also called a “fire organ”, is a type of [[musical instrument]], specifically an [[organ (music)|organ]]. However, unlike regular organs, pyrophones make sounds by [[combustion]] inside of glass tubes, instead of just pushing air through pipes. It was invented by French [[physics|physicist]] and musician [[Georges Frédéric Eugène Kastner]].
