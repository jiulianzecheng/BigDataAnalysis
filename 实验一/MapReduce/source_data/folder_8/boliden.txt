 '''Boliden''' is a [[Urban areas in Sweden|locality]] in [[Skellefteå Municipality]] in [[Västerbotten County]] in [[Sweden]]. In 2010, 1,566 people lived there. < ref name=scb / >  It is located 28,5 kilometers from the town of [[Skellefteå]]. Swedish mining company [[Boliden AB]] was established here.
