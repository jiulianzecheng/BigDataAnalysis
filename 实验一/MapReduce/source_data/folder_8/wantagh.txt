'''Wantagh''' is a [[hamlet]] and [[census-designated place]] (CDP) on [[Long Island]], [[New York]]. In [[2010 United States Census|2010]], there were 18,871 people living there. Wantagh is known as  " The Gateway to Jones Beach. "  The neighboring towns of Wantagh are [[Levittown, New York|Levittown]], [[Seaford, New York|Seaford]], [[Merrick, New York|Merrick]], and [[The Bellmores, New York|Bellmore]].
