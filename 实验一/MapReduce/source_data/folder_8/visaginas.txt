 '''Visaginas''' is a city in [[Lithuania]]. It is found on the very [[northeast]] of the country. Most people who live here are Russians and speak the [[Russian language]]. < ref > {{Cite web|title= " Deutsche Welle " : Visaginas – maža Sovietų Sąjunga Lietuvoje|url=https://www.15min.lt/naujiena/aktualu/lietuva/deutsche-welle-visaginas-maza-sovietu-sajunga-lietuvoje-56-428940|access-date=2021-05-21|website=15min.lt|language=lt}} < /ref >  It is the newest city in Lithuania, because it was built to serve the nearby [[Ignalina Nuclear Power Plant]]. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Istorija {{!}} Visagino savivaldybė|url=https://www.visaginas.lt/savivaldybe/istorija/21|access-date=2021-05-21|website=Istorija {{!}} Visagino savivaldybė|language=lt}} < /ref >
