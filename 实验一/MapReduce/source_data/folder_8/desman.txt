The '''desman''' is an [[insectivore]] in the [[mole]] family, the [[Talpidae]].  
 
 It is a diving insectivore. It is in the tribe '''Desmanini''' (and subfamily '''Desmaninae'''). The tribe has only two  [[species]] in [[Eurasia]]. There is one in [[Russia]] and the other in the northwest of the [[Iberian peninsula]] and [[Pyrenees]]. Both species are vulnerable.
