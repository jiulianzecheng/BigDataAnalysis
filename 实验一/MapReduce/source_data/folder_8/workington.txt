'''Workington''' is a town in [[Allerdale]], [[Cumbria]], England. Workington is connected by the [[A66 road]] and the [[A596 road]]. In 2001 there were 24,295 people living in Workington. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.ons.gov.uk/help/localstatistics|title=Local statistics - Office for National Statistics|website=www.ons.gov.uk}} < /ref >
