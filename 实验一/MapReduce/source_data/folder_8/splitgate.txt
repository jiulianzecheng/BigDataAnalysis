'''Splitgate''' is a free to play [[video game]] for [[Xbox One]], [[PlayStation 4]], [[PlayStation 5]] and [[PC]]. Splitgate's gameplay is built around the ideas of arena shooters like [[Halo (series)|Halo]], with added portal mechanincs, similar to those added by [[Portal (video game)|Portal.]] Developed by a small game studio, [[1047 Games]] it first saw a huge success when it became [[cross-platform]] across PC and console in July 2021, after 2 years of open beta.
