 '''Turbonegro''' is a [[punk]] band from [[Oslo]], [[Norway]]. They formed in [[1989]]. They call their style ''deathpunk'', which is a [[mixture]] of [[punk]] and [[hard rock]]. The members are: [[Hank von Hell]] ([[vocals]]), Happy-Tom ([[Bass guitar|bass]]), Euroboy ([[guitar]]), Pål Pot Pamparius (guitar, [[Keyboard instrument|keyboard]]), Tomas Dahl ([[drums]]) 
 Friends of the band are Bam Margera.
