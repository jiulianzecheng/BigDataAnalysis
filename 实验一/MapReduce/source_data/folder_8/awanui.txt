 '''Awanui''' is a settlement in the [[Far North District]], [[New Zealand]] and lies on the banks of the [[Awanui River]]. It is located 7 kilometers north of [[Kaitaia]]. The State Highways of 1 and 10 intersect in the settlement with State Highway 1 the gateway to [[Cape Reinga]] and State Highway 10 to the [[Bay of Islands]]. It has two schools located in the settlement.
