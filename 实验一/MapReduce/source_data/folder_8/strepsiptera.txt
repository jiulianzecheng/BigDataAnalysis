 '''Strepsiptera''' is a order of [[insect]]s, any of about 600 species of small insects that are notable for their bizarre form of [[parasitism]]. Strepsipterans are parasitic in [[planthopper]]s, [[leafhopper]]s, [[treehopper]]s, [[froghopper]]s, [[bee]]s, and other insects. 
 
 == Description == 
 Mature females are usually wingless and saclike, whereas the males have large, fanlike hindwings, short, clublike forewings, bulging eyes, and comblike antennae.
