 '''Fender''' is a well known [[company]] that produces [[guitar]]s and [[bass guitar]]s. These are sold all over the world. The founder is [[Leo Fender]]. Fender makes the [[Fender Stratocaster|Stratocaster]] and [[Fender Telecaster|Telecaster]] [[guitar]]s; and the [[Fender Precision Bass|Precision]] and [[Fender Jazz Bass|Jazz]] [[bass guitar|basses]].
