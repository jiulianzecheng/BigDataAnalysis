'''Dialogue''' or '''dialog''' is a shared inquiry or the art of thinking together. < ref > Isaacs, William. (2010). ''Dialogue and the Art of Thinking Together,'' p.1. < /ref >  Dialogue is a [[rhetoric|rhetorical]], [[literature|literary]] and [[theatrical]] format.  It means a written or spoken [[conversation]]al exchange between two or more people. 
 
 ==History== 
 The historical origins of dialogue are in [[ancient Greek literature|classical Greek]] and [[Indian literature]]. 
 
 Dialogue is an element in the art of [[rhetoric]].
