'''Gigablaster''' is a [[Brazilian]] Animated Series produced by [[TeamTO]] and [[WildBrain UK|WildBrain Dublín]] in co-production with [[Gloob]] It is written by Eric Shaw.  < ref name=Anim > {{cite news|publisher=Animation Magazine|access-date=February 3, 2021|title=Gloob Blasts Off at MIP with Original Co-Pro 'Giga Blaster'|author=Mercedes Milligan|date=October 16, 2018|url=https://www.animationmagazine.net/tv/gloob-blasts-off-at-mip-with-original-co-pro-giga-blaster/}} < /ref >   
 
 The Gigablaster family of characters has a very playful blue [[rhinoceros]], a shy DJ, an explosive guitarist, and an absent-minded, deaf [[zombie]]. They live in a [[karaoke]] in a large city. Together, Giga, Ziggy and Rubi are part of a band managed by Cool Daddy. They end up getting in trouble with villains ranging from Shimeji God to GossipCam Paparazzi.
