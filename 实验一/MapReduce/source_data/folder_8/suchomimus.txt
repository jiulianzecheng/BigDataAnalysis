 '''Suchomimus''' ( " crocodile mimic " ) was a [[fish]]-eating (and [[meat]]-eating) large [[spinosaurid]] [[theropod]] [[dinosaur]] with a [[crocodile]]-like head. It lived 112 million years ago in the [[Lower Cretaceous]] period in what is now [[Africa]]. < ref name= " Holtz2008 " > Holtz, Thomas R. Jr. (2012) ''Dinosaurs: The Most Complete, Up-to-Date Encyclopedia for Dinosaur Lovers of All Ages,'' [http://www.geol.umd.edu/~tholtz/dinoappendix/HoltzappendixWinter2011.pdf Winter 2011 Appendix.] < /ref >

Jr. (2012) ''Dinosaurs: The Most Complete, Up-to-Date Encyclopedia for Dinosaur Lovers of All Ages,'' [http://www.geol.umd.edu/~tholtz/dinoappendix/HoltzappendixWinter2011.pdf Winter 2011 Appendix.] < /ref >  It walked on two powerful hind legs, and had short clawed arms with three [[finger]]s, the first finger was the longest.
