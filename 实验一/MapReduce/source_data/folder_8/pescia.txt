 '''Pescia''' is a city in [[Tuscany]], [[Italy]]. It is in a central zone between the cities [[Lucca]] and [[Florence]]. 
 
 ==Important monuments and buildings== 
 * Palazzo Palagio 
 * Palazzo Della Barba 
 * Cathedral 
 * Communal Palace 
 * City Library 
 
 ==Economy== 
 Economic activities of the city are about flowers and [[paper]].
