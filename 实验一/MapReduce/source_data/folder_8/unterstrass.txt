 '''Unterstrass''' is a [[quarter]] in the [[district 6 (Zürich)|district 6]] in [[Zürich]]. Until 1893 it was a [[municipality]] on its own. Then it became part of [[Zürich]]. 
 
 19,921 people lived there and its area is 2.46 & nbsp;km².
