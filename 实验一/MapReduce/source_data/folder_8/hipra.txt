'''Hipra''' Laboratories, S.A. is a Spanish pharmaceutical multinational founded in 1954. It began in the field of veterinary medicine but is also developing a vaccine against [[COVID-19]]. < ref > {{Cite web|last=Teixidor|first=Laura|date=2020-12-19|title=Hipra investiga una vacuna més «potent i eficaç» que les convencionals|url=https://www.diaridegirona.cat/comarques/2020/12/19/hipra-investiga-vacuna-mes-potent-48644972.html|access-date=2022-06-09|website=Diari de Girona|language=ca}} < /ref > 
 
 == History == 
 The founder of the company is the Catalan pharmacist, veterinarian and chemist Joan Nogareda i Gifre, a native of Olot. In 1971, he acquired a Madrid-based companyand moved it to the province of Girona in 1954. Since 1973, its headquarters have been in Amer. His brother Ernest and Pere Casadevall are involved in the new company. < ref > {{Cite web|date=2020-11-17|title=Amer, el pueblo de Puigdemont, tiene nuevo atractivo: la vacuna anti Covid que fabrica Hipra|url=https://www.elespanol.com/reportajes/20201117/amer-pueblo-puigdemont-atractivo-covid-fabrica-hipra/536447763_0.html|access-date=2022-06-09|website=El Español|language=es}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite web|last=AGENCIAS|first=RTVE es /|date=2021-11-15|title=Aprobada la fase 2 de ensayos en humanos para la vacuna de Hipra|url=https://www.rtve.es/noticias/20211115/fase-2-ensayos-humanos-vacuna-hipra/2224743.shtml|access-date=2022-06-09|website=RTVE.es|language=es}} < /ref >   
 
 == Human health == 
 Since April 2020, Hipra has been developing a vaccine against SARS-CoV-2 with funding from the CDTI.2 Later, in November 2021, the vaccine went to phase 2 of human trials3, to finally start phase 3 in early 2022 with 3,000 volunteers in 20 hospitals, mainly from [[Spain]] but also from Italy and Portugal. 
 
 The company expects to make 400 million doses in 2022, having already agreed a supply of 50 million doses with [[Vietnam]]. < ref > {{Cite web|last=Figuls|first=Josep Catà|date=2021-09-13|title=Hipra acuerda con Vietnam el primer suministro de su vacuna contra la covid|url=https://elpais.com/espana/catalunya/2021-09-13/hipra-acuerda-con-vietnam-el-primer-suministro-de-su-vacuna-contra-la-covid.html|access-date=2022-06-09|website=El País|language=es}} < /ref > 
 
 == Animal health == 
 Hipra markets a variety of products aimed at animal health, especially for farm animals but also for domestic animals such as pigeons and dogs.
