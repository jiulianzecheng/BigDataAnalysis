 '''Telefonaktiebolaget LM Ericsson''' (''Telephone Stock Company of LM Ericsson''), known as '''Ericsson''', is a [[Sweden|Swedish]] multinational networking and [[telecommunication]]s [[company]]. 
 
 == History == 
 The company was founded in [[1876]] by [[Lars Magnus Ericsson]]. 
 
 Ericsson introduced the world's first fully automatic mobile telephone system, MTA, in [[1956]]. < ref > {{Cite web|date=2021-06-15|title=The Ericsson story - Ericsson|url=https://www.ericsson.com/en/about-us/history|access-date=2023-08-01|website=web.archive.org|archive-date=2021-06-15|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210615040942/https://www.ericsson.com/en/about-us/history|url-status=bot: unknown}} < /ref > 
 
 In [[2001]], Ericsson, together with the Japanese company [[Sony]], began production of [[Sony Ericsson]] cell phones. < ref > {{Cite web|date=2016-05-21|title=Crisis - Ericsson History|url=http://www.ericssonhistory.com/changing-the-world/Big-bang/Crisis/|access-date=2023-08-01|website=web.archive.org|archive-date=2016-05-21|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160521155014/http://www.ericssonhistory.com/changing-the-world/Big-bang/Crisis/|url-status=bot: unknown}} < /ref > 
 
 == Overview == 
 The company works in 180 countries. In the company works about 100,000 employees. 
 
 The company is headquartered in [[Stockholm]].
