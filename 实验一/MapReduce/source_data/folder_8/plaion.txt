 '''Plaion''' is a [[Germany|German]]-[[Austria|Austrian]] media company with the [[Headquarters|headquarter]] in [[Höfen, Tyrol]], Austria. The company was founded in 1994 by Franz Koch and Klemens Kundratitz. 
 
 In November 2020, Koch Media acquired Flying Wild Hog. < ref > {{Cite web|date=2020-11-18|title=Embracer Group acquires 34BigThings, Flying Wild Hog, Nimble Giant Entertainment, Purple Lamp Studios, Snapshot Games, Zen Studios, more|url=https://www.gematsu.com/2020/11/embracer-group-acquires-34bigthings-flying-wild-hog-nimble-giant-entertainment-purple-lamp-studios-snapshot-games-zen-studios-more|access-date=2022-06-29|website=Gematsu|language=en-US}} < /ref >  Koch Media opened a new publishing division, Prime Matter, in June 2021.
