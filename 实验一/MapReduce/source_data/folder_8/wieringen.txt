 '''Wieringen''' is a former [[List of municipalities of the Netherlands|municipality]] in the [[Netherlands|Dutch]] [[Provinces of the Netherlands|province]] of [[North Holland]]. Since 2012, it has been part of the new municipality of [[Hollands Kroon]]. 
 
 Wieringen was an island until 1932.
