'''Mixa''' is a [[France|French]] [[Trademark|brand]] of [[cosmetics]] for sensitive skin. It is part of the [[L'Oréal]] group. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Mixa in L'Oreal|url=https://www.pinanjar.com/2022/10/mixa-in-loreal-art-of-loreal-group.html|website=Pinanjar.com}} < /ref > 
 
 == History == 
 The company was founded in 1924 in [[Paris]]. For that time, it was different from other brands because of its special method of production by mixing of traditional [[Pharmaceutical chemistry|pharmaceutical ingredients]] and moisturizer. < ref name= " :0 " > {{Cite web|title=Mixa l'expertise de la peau depuis 80 ans|url=https://la-revue-des-marques.fr/sagas_marques/mixa/mixa.php|access-date=2021-04-23|website=la-revue-des-marques.fr}} < /ref > 
 
 Mixa was bought by L'Oréal in 1958. < ref > {{Cite web|last=à 16h41|first=Par Le 5 juin 2010|date=2010-06-05|title=Mixa, fournisseur de douceur|url=https://www.leparisien.fr/laparisienne/actualites/mixa-fournisseur-de-douceur-05-06-2010-946242.php|access-date=2021-04-23|website=leparisien.fr|language=fr-FR}} < /ref > 
 
 The first ''Mixa'' product  " ''Mixed Cream-Powder'' "  was released in 1926. 
 
 In 1969, the first line of children's skin care ''Mixa Bebe Series'' was created''.'' < ref name= " :0 "  / > 
 
 Since 1976, Mixa children's cosmetics have been used by women to care for their sensitive skin. < ref > {{Cite web|title=La marque|url=https://www.mixa.fr/la-marque|access-date=2021-04-23|website=Mixa|language=fr}} < /ref > 
 
 In 1997, Mixa launched a product for the care of dry sensitive skin, it was regenerating body milk. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Знакомство с брендом: что нужно знать о косметике Mixa?|url=https://beauty.ua/news/new/1273-znakomstvo-s-brendom-chto-nuzhno-znat-o-kosmetike-mixa|access-date=2021-04-23|website=beauty.ua|language=ru}} < /ref > 
 
 In 1999, the line of body care products became № 1 in France. 
 
 In 2003, the company released its first line of facial care, which consists of [[Moisturizer|moisturizers]] for dehydrated sensitive skin. 
 
 In 2013, the first CC cream Mixa appeared. 
 
 == Products == 
 Currently Mixa is a brand № 1 in the categories  " ''Body Care'' "  and  " ''Baby Cosmetics'' "  in France. 
 
 The main products are: 
 
 * Facial skin care: products for cleansing and caring for sensitive skin that has such problems as [[dehydration]], dryness, redness, imperfections, [[Allergy|hypersensitivity]], and anti-aging care products. 
 * Body care: milk, creams, oils. 
 * Lip care: [[Lip balm|lip balms]]. 
 * Hand skin care: moisturizing and regenerating creams. 
 * Cosmetics for children. 
 * [[Sunscreen]].
