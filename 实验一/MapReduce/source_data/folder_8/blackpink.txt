 '''Blackpink''' (Korean: 블랙핑크; commonly stylized as BLACKPINK or BLΛƆKPIИK) is a South Korean girl [[girl group|group]] that [[wikt:debut|debuted]] on 8 August 2016. < ref name= " :0 " > {{cite web|url=http://kpopherald.koreaherald.com/view.php?ud=201608091630134122942_2|title=YG Entertainment unveils K-pop group BLACKPINK|author=Kim Yu-young|date=August 9, 2016|website=Kpop Herald|access-date=August 12, 2016}} < /ref > < ref > {{cite web|url=http://mwave.interest.me/en/kpop-news/article/107611/blackpink-sweeping-charts-within-2-days-of-debut-all-kpop-news|title=BLACKPINK Sweeping Charts Within 2 Days of Debut|author=Jeon Su Mi|date=August 10, 2016|website=Mwave|access-date=August 13, 2016|archive-date=August 13, 2016|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160813150107/http://mwave.interest.me/en/kpop-news/article/107611/blackpink-sweeping-charts-within-2-days-of-debut-all-kpop-news|url-status=dead}} < /ref >  The group consists of members [[Jisoo]], [[Jennie Kim|Jennie]], [[Rosé (singer)|Rosé]], and [[Lisa (rapper)|Lisa]]. 
 
 They are the highest-charting female K-pop act on both [[Billboard Hot 100|''Billboard'' Hot 100]] and [[Billboard 200|''Billboard'' 200]]. They are also the first and only K-pop girl group to enter and top the ''Billboard'' < nowiki > ' < /nowiki > s Emerging Artists chart. In addition, Blackpink are also the first female K-pop group to have four number-one singles on ''Billboard'' < nowiki > ' < /nowiki > s World Digital Song Sales chart. < ref name= " US 200 " > {{cite web|url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/chart-beat/8462635/blackpink-k-pop-history-hot-100-billboard-200|title=BLACKPINK Makes K-Pop History on Hot 100, Billboard 200  &  More With 'DDU-DU DDU-DU'|work=Billboard|last=Zellner|first=Xander|date=June 25, 2018|access-date=June 26, 2018}} < /ref >  Their 2018 song  " Ddu-Du Ddu-Du "  was the most-viewed Korean music video in the first 24 hours on [[YouTube]]. In January 2019, it became the most viewed music video by a K-pop group on YouTube. They are currently the most subscribed music act, K-pop group and female artist on YouTube. < ref name= " Ddu Views " > {{cite web |url=https://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/k-town/8494591/blackpink-ddu-du-ddu-du-most-viewed-music-video-kpop-youtube |title=BLACKPINK's 'Ddu-Du Ddu-Du' Becomes Most-Viewed Music Video From a K-Pop Group on YouTube.  |last=Herman |first=Tamar |date=January 23, 2019 |website=[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]] |access-date=January 24, 2019}} < /ref >  (91,9 million subscribers on youtube) Blackpink was also the first female K-pop group to perform at [[Coachella Valley Music and Arts Festival|Coachella]]. < ref > {{cite news |url=https://metro.co.uk/2019/01/03/blackpink-become-first-k-pop-girl-group-perform-coachella-join-ariana-grande-2019-line-8304437/ |title=Blackpink Become First K-pop Girl Group to Perform at Coachella as They Join Ariana Grande on 2019 Line-up |last=Adejobi |first=Alicia |date=January 3, 2019 |work=Metro |publisher=DMG Media |access-date=January 3, 2019}} < /ref > < ref > {{cite news |first=Chris |last=Willman |url=https://variety.com/2019/music/news/blackpink-wins-over-coachella-american-concert-debut-1203188858/ |title=Blackpink Win Over Coachella With First Full U.S. Concert |newspaper=[[Variety (magazine)|Variety]] |date=April 12, 2019 }} < /ref > 
 
 In 2018, [[Jennie (singer)|Jennie]] was the first member to release a solo song, titled  " Solo " . [[Rosé (singer)|Rosé]] released her single album ''R'' in 2021. [[Lisa (rapper)|Lisa]] also released a single album called ''Lalisa'' the same year. Jisoo released hers, called ''Me'', in 2023.
