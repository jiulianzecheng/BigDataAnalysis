 '''Longraye''' is a former [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is found in the [[Regions of France|region]] [[Basse-Normandie]] in the [[Calvados (department)|Calvados]] [[Departments of France|department]] in the northwest of [[France]]. On 1 January 2017, it was merged into the new commune of [[Aurseulles]]. < ref > [https://www.legifrance.gouv.fr/eli/arrete/2016/9/8/INTB1629483A/jo/texte Arrêté préfectoral] 8 September 2016 {{in lang|fr}} < /ref >
