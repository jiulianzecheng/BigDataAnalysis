 '''Biysk''' ({{lang-rus|Бийск|p=bʲijsk}}) is a [[city]] in [[Altai Krai]], [[Russia]]. It is on the [[Biya River]] not far from its [[confluence]] with the [[Katun River]]. It is the second largest city of the [[krai]] (after [[Barnaul]], the [[administrative center]] of the [[krai]]). [[Population]]: {{ru-census|p2021=183,852|p2010=210,115|p2002=218,562|p1989=233,238}}
