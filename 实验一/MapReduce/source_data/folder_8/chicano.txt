 A '''Chicano''' is a person who has a mixed [[Ancestor|ancestry]] and/or [[citizen]]ship of the [[United States]] and usually [[Mexico]].  Often, Chicano and [[Mexican-American]] are used to mean the same thing.  People from [[Central America|Central]] or [[South America]] and United States could also be called Chicano. '''Chicana''' is the female version of Chicano.  Chicanos often speak in [[Spanglish (language)|Spanglish]]. < ref > {{Cite journal|last=Frazer|first=Timothy C.|date=1996|title=Chicano English and Spanish Interference in the Midwestern United States|url=https://www.jstor.org/stable/455470|journal=American Speech|volume=71|issue=1|pages=72–85|doi=10.2307/455470|jstor=455470|issn=0003-1283}} < /ref >
