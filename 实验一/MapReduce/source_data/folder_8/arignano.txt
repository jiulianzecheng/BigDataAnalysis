 '''Arignano''' is a ''[[comune]]'' in the [[Metropolitan City of Turin]] in the [[Italy|Italian]] region of [[Piedmont]]. As of 31 December 2019, 1,081 people lived there. 
 
 [[Lake Arignano]] is in the comune.
