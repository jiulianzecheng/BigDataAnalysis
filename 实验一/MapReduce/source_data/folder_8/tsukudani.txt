 '''Tsukudani''' is a [[Japanese food]]. It is small [[seafood]], meat or [[seaweed]] that has been simmered in [[soy sauce]] and [[mirin]]. For example, [[Inago no tsukudani]] is made from [[Locust|locusts]] that are boiled in [[soy sauce]] and [[sugar]].
