 '''Ruhengeri''', also known as '''Musanze''', is the third largest city in [[Rwanda]] and the capital of Musanze District in the [[Northern Province, Rwanda|Northern Province]] of Rwanda. As of the 2022 census, 135,000 people lived there. 
 
 Ruhengeri is near the lakes of [[Lake Burera]] and [[Lake Ruhondo]] and is the gateway city to [[Volcanoes National Park]].
