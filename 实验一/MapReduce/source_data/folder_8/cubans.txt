 '''Cubans''' are an ethnic group and nation native to [[Cuba]]. According to the official 2012 National Census, the majority of Cubans (64.1 per cent) are white, 26.6 per cent are mestizo (mixed race) or mulatto and 9.3 per cent are black. < ref > [https://minorityrights.org/country/cuba/ Cuba - World Directory of Minorities  &  Indigenous Peoples] < /ref >
