 '''Dolores''', also spelled '''Delores''', is a female given name. It is pronounced [ d''uh''-'''lawr'''-is, -'''lohr'''- ]. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Definition of Dolores {{!}} Dictionary.com|url=https://www.dictionary.com/browse/dolores|access-date=2023-03-17|website=www.dictionary.com|language=en}} < /ref > The Spanish name Dolores has its roots in the Latin word  " dolor, "  which translates to  " pain "  or  " sorrow. " < ref > {{Cite web|title=dolores name Meaning and Origin|url=https://babys.name/detail-dolores-Meaning-Origin-Famous-Similar-Sibling.html|access-date=2023-03-17|website=babys.name}} < /ref >  This name is quite common in Spanish-speaking nations and is frequently used as a way to refer to the Virgin Mary.
