 '''Theria''' is a [[Subclass (biology)|subclass]] of [[mammal]]s. It includes almost all living mammals: the ones that give birth to live young, and not by using a [[Eggshell|shelled]] [[Egg (biology)|egg]]. 
 
 The Theria are divided into two groups ([[infraclass]]es): [[Metatheria]] and [[Eutheria]]. The surviving Metatheria are the [[marsupial]]s, and the Eutheria are the [[placenta]]l mammals.
