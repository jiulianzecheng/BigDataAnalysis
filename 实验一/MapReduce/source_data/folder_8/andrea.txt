 The name '''Andrea''' [an-dree-uh, ahn-, ahn-drey-uh] < ref > {{Cite web|title=Definition of Andrea {{!}} Dictionary.com|url=https://www.dictionary.com/browse/andrea|access-date=2023-03-14|website=www.dictionary.com|language=en}} < /ref > is a [[given name]]. The name Andrea is of Greek origin and means  " strong and courageous " . < ref > {{Cite web|title=Andrea name Meaning and Origin|url=https://babys.name/detail-Andrea-Meaning-Origin-Famous-Similar-Sibling.html|access-date=2023-03-14|website=babys.name}} < /ref > It is usually used for [[female]]s(In 2021, the top 1000 ranking of newborns named in the United States, in the female ranking of 173, in the male ranking did not appear). < ref > {{Cite web|last=OACT|title=Popular Baby Names|url=https://www.ssa.gov/cgi-bin/popularnames.cgi|access-date=2023-03-14|website=www.ssa.gov}} < /ref >   
 
 There are a lot of celebrities with the name '''Andrea'''. Also, the name is a replacement for '''Allison''' over List 5 of the [[Atlantic]] [[tropical cyclone]] naming list.
