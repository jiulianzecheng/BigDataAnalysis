 '''Gais''' ({{IPA-de|ˈɡaɪs}}) is a village and a [[municipality]] of the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Appenzell Ausserrhoden]] in [[Switzerland]]. The main sights are: the Protestant church of 1782, the former spa hotel ''Neuer Ochsen'' of 1796 and the ''Krone'' inn of 1781. In 1977, Gais received the [[Wakker Prize]] for the development and preservation of its architectural heritage. < ref > [http://www.heimatschutz.ch/index.php?id=1041 & L=0 Swiss Heritage Society-Wakker Prize]{{in lang|de}} < /ref > 
 
 == Villages == 
 Gais, Rotenwies, Rietli, Zwislen, Schachen, Stoss, Strahlholz, Steinleuten, Gäbris, Kürstein, Schwäbrig, Sommersberg and Hirschberg.
