 '''Worksop''' is a [[town]] in [[Nottingamshire]], England. It is the biggest [[town]] in the [[Non-metropolitan district|district]] of [[Bassetlaw]]. Worksop is located on the [[River Ryton]], north of [[Sherwood Forest]]. Around 39,800 people live in the town.
