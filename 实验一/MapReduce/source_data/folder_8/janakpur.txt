 '''Janakpurdham''' or '''Janakpur''' ({{lang-ne|जनकपुर}}, {{IPA-ne|d͡zʌnʌkpur}}) is a sub-metropolitan city in Dhanusha District, [[Madhesh Province]], [[Nepal]]. The city is a hub for religious and cultural tourism. A headquarter of Dhanusha district, ''Janakpur'' is also the capital for [[Madhesh Province]]. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Breaking ! प्रदेश २ को स्थायी राजधानी 'जनकपुरधाम' बहुमतले पारित !|url=https://enewsbureau.com/news-details/12529/2022-01-17|access-date=2022-01-17|website=enewsbureau.com/|language=en}} < /ref > 
 ==History== 
 The city was founded in the early 18th century. According to tradition, storyteller shows an earlier city known as Janakpurdham existed in the area, which used to be the capital of the Videha dynasty that ruled the Mithila region in ancient times. < ref name= " Burghart1978 " > Burghart, R. (1978). The disappearance and reappearance of Janakpur. Kailash: A Journal of Himalayan Studies 6 (4): 257–284. < /ref > 
 ==Location== 
 Janakpur is about {{convert|225|km||abbr=on}} southeast of [[Kathmandu]]. < ref > {{cite web|title=Distance from Kathmandu to Janakpur|url=http://www.distancefromto.net/between/Kathmandu/Janakpur|url-status=live|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160325093131/http://www.distancefromto.net/between/kathmandu/janakpur|archive-date=25 March 2016|access-date=8 September 2018|website=www.distancefromto.net}} < /ref >  {{As of|2015|}}, the city had a population of 173,924. < ref name= " JICC " > {{cite web|title=Archived copy|url=http://cbs.gov.np/wp-content/uploads/2012/11/VDC_Municipality.pdf|url-status=dead|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130731124937/http://cbs.gov.np/wp-content/uploads/2012/11/VDC_Municipality.pdf|archive-date=31 July 2013|access-date=2015-04-16}} < /ref >  The city had a population of 195,438 in 2021. < ref name= " janakpurmun.gov.np " > {{Cite web|url=http://www.janakpurmun.gov.np/en|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170902182547/http://www.janakpurmun.gov.np/en|archive-date=2 September 2017|title=Janakpurdham Sub-Metropolitan City  & #124;  " Religious,Cultural, Touristry Place Janakpurdham " }} < /ref >  Janakpur is currently the most populated sub metropolitan city of Nepal. Janakpur is about 18 km away from the Bhitthamore border of India. < ref > {{Cite web|title=ICP Bhithamore {{!}} Land Ports Authority of India|url=https://lpai.gov.in/en/icp-bhithamore|access-date=2022-05-10|website=lpai.gov.in}} < /ref >   
 ==Transport== 
 Nepal Railways operates between Janakpur and [[Jaynagar Majilpur|Jaynagar]] of [[India]].
