 The '''Fresquel''' is a [[river]] in southern [[France]], a left [[tributary]] of the [[Aude (river)|Aude]] river. The river flows mainly through the French [[Departments of France|department]] of [[Aude]]. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 The Fresquel river has a length of {{Convert|63|km|mi|0|abbr=on}} and a [[drainage basin]] with an area of approximately {{Convert|932|km2|sqmi|0|abbr=on }}. < ref name= " sandre " > {{cite web |url=http://services.sandre.eaufrance.fr/Courdo/Fiche/client/fiche_courdo.php?CdSandre=Y13-0400 |title=Le Fresquel (Y13-0400) |publisher=SANDRE - Portail national d'accès aux référentiels sur l'eau |language=fr |access-date=22 March 2017 }} < /ref > 
 
 Its average yearly [[Discharge (hydrology)|discharge]] ([[volume]] of [[water]] which passes through a section of the river per [[Unit of measurement|unit]] of [[time]]) is {{Convert|5.18|m3/s|cuft/s|abbr=on }} at [[Carcassonne]] in the [[Aude]] department. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://www.hydro.eaufrance.fr/stations/Y1364010 & procedure=synthese |title=Le Fresquel à Carcassonne [Pont Rouge] |publisher=Banque Hydro |language=fr |access-date=22 March 2017 |archive-date=10 April 2021 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210410121629/http://www.hydro.eaufrance.fr/stations/Y1364010%26procedure%3Dsynthese |url-status=dead }} < /ref >

 ===Course=== 
 The Fresquel river starts at the northwest of the Aude department, in the ''commune'' of [[Baraigne]], at an [[elevation]] of about {{Convert|265|m|ft|0|abbr=on}}. < ref > {{cite web |url=https://www.geoportail.gouv.fr/carte?c=1.835556,43.323889 & z=12 & l0=GEOGRAPHICALGRIDSYSTEMS.MAPS.SCAN-EXPRESS.STANDARD::GEOPORTAIL:OGC:WMTS(1) & permalink=yes |title=Source du Fresquel |publisher=Géoportail |language=fr |access-date=22 March 2017 }} < /ref > 
 
 It flows, first, to the west to the [[Haute-Garonne]] department and then to the north where it gets again into the Aude department near [[Seuil de Naurouze]] and then to the east. Finally, it joins the [[Aude (river)|Aude]] river as a left [[tributary]], at an elevation of about {{Convert|90|m|ft|0|abbr=on}} at [[Carcassonne]]. < ref > {{cite web |url=https://www.geoportail.gouv.fr/carte?c=2.384722,43.236667 & z=12 & l0=GEOGRAPHICALGRIDSYSTEMS.MAPS.SCAN-EXPRESS.STANDARD::GEOPORTAIL:OGC:WMTS(1) & permalink=yes |title=Confluence du Fresquel |publisher=Géoportail |language=fr |access-date=22 March 2017 }} < /ref > 
 
 ===Communes=== 
 The Fresquel passes through 1 region ([[Occitanie]]), 2 departments and 24 ''communes''; some of the ''communes'' are: < ref name= " sandre "  / > 
 
 * '''Aude''' : [[Baraigne]], [[Labastide-d'Anjou]], [[Ricaud, Aude|Ricaud]], [[Souilhanels]], [[Castelnaudary]], [[Saint-Martin-Lalande]], [[Lasbordes, Aude|Lasbordes]], [[Villepinte, Aude|Villepinte]], [[Bram]], [[Alzonne]], [[Pezens]], [[Ventenac-Cabardès]], [[Pennautier]], [[Carcassonne]] 
 * '''Haute-Garonne''' : [[Avignonet-Lauragais]]
