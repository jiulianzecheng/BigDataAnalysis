 '''Satterthwaite''' is a village and [[civil parish]] in [[South Lakeland]], [[Cumbria]], England. In 2001 there were 257 people living in Satterthwaite. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.ons.gov.uk/help/localstatistics|title=Local statistics - Office for National Statistics|website=www.ons.gov.uk}} < /ref >  It has 9 listed buildings. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://britishlistedbuildings.co.uk/england/cumbria/Satterthwaite|title=Listed Buildings in Cumbria|website=britishlistedbuildings.co.uk}} < /ref >  It has a church called All Saints which has 4 stained glass windows. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.visitcumbria.com/churches/satterthwaite-all-saints-church/|title=Satterthwaite All Saints Church|publisher=}} < /ref >
