 '''Drinker''' was a small [[dinosaur]] from [[North America]]. It was a very small [[herbivore]] that grew up to 1 [[Metre|meter]] long. It was named after [[Edward Drinker Cope]] a [[paleontology|paleontologist]], a dinosaur [[scientist]] who found many dinosaur [[fossil]]s such as ''[[Triceratops]]'', ''[[Anatotitan]]'' and ''[[Tyrannosaurus|Tyrannosaurus rex]]''.
