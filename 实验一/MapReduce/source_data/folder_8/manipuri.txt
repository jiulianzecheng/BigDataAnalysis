 * [[Manipuri language]], the official language and the lingua franca of Manipur 
 * [[Manipuri mythology]], the body of myths developed by the Manipuri people 
 * [[Manipuri people]], the predominant ethnic group of Manipur 
 * [[Manipuri religion]], the ethnic religion of the Manipuri people 
 * [[Manipuri script]], the writing system for the Manipuri language 
 * [[Old Manipuri language]], an archaic form of the modern Manipuri language
