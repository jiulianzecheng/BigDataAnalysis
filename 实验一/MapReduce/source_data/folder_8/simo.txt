 '''Simo''' is a [[municipality]] in [[Finnish Lapland|Lapland]], [[Finland]]. As of 31 December 2020, the municipality had a population of 2,950. Neighbouring municipalities are [[Ii, Finland|Ii]], [[Kemi]], [[Keminmaa]], [[Ranua]] and [[Tervola]].
