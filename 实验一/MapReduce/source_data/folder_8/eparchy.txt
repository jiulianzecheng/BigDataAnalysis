 '''Eparchy''' is the name of a [[diocese]] in  the [[Eastern Orthodox Church|Eastern Orthodox]] and [[Eastern Catholic]] churches. An eparchy is headed by a [[bishop]], who is called ''eparch''. Churches with the [[Byzantine Rite]] (or the Rite of Constantinople) also have eparchies.
