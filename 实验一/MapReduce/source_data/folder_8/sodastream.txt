 '''SodaStream International''' is an [[Israel]]-based [[manufacturing]] company. It's best known as the maker of the consumer home [[carbonation]] product by the same name. < ref > {{cite web|title=Get Busy with Something Fizzy|publisher=Something Israeli|url=http://www.somethingisraeli.com/articles/60_get_busy_with_israel.htm|access-date=August 22, 2018|archive-date=June 7, 2013|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130607124259/http://www.somethingisraeli.com/articles/60_get_busy_with_israel.htm|url-status=bot: unknown}} < /ref >  It carbonates water by adding [[carbon dioxide]] from a pressurized [[cylinder]] to create [[carbonated water|soda water]] to drink. The company also sells more than 100 types of concentrated [[syrup]]s and [[flavor]]ings to make carbonated drinks. < ref > {{cite news|title=SodaStream Fizzes up Global Market for Carbonated and Flavored Drinks|publisher=Wall Street Journal|url=https://www.wsj.com/articles/SB10001424127887323894704578115060945709602|access-date=August 22, 2018}} < /ref > 
 
 In the past, several major brands were available in the SodaStream [[concentrate]], including [[Sunkist]] and [[Fanta]]. < ref > {{cite web|title=Carbonated Drinks|publisher=Competition Commission|url=http://www.competition-commission.org.uk/rep_pub/reports/1991/fulltext/309c9.pdf|access-date=August 22, 2018|archive-date=January 9, 2007|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20070109025917/http://www.competition-commission.org.uk/rep_pub/reports/1991/fulltext/309c9.pdf|url-status=bot: unknown}} < /ref >  SodaStream also offers diet concentrates. 
 
 In January 2012, SodaStream and [[Kraft Foods]] became partners. It involved the use of Country Time and Crystal Light brand flavors. In July 2012, the two companies extended their partnership to include the [[Kool-Aid]] flavor line. < ref > {{cite report|title=Kraft and SodaStream in Deal for Kool-Aid|publisher=The Chicago Tribune|url=http://articles.chicagotribune.com/2012-07-18/business/chi-kraft-and-sodastream-in-deal-for-koolaid-20120718_1_kool-aid-kraft-foods-sodastream|access-date=August 22, 2018}} < /ref >
