 '''Veneridae''' is a [[family (biology)|family]] of [[clam]]s, with over 500 described [[species]], in twelve subfamilies. They are commonly called '''Venus clams'''. They are [[bivalve]] [[molluscs]].  Many are edible and used as [[seafood]]. Venus clams occur in all [[sea|seas]] of the world. Most live near the [[shore]], preferably in areas with [[sand|sandy]] bottoms.
