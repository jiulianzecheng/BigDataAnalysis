 '''Aussersihl''' is a [[quarter]] in [[district 4 (Zürich)|district 4]] of the [[Swiss]] [[city]] of [[Zürich]]. Until 1893 it was a [[municipality]] on its own. It was then [[municipal corporation|incorporated]] into [[Zürich]].
