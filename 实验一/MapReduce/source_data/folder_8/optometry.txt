'''Optometry''' is the branch of [[medicine]] that deals with the health of and problems with people's [[eye]]s. [[Optometrist]]s specialize in ''refractive errors'' like [[myopia]] and [[hyperopia]], for which they can prescribe [[eyeglasses]] or [[contact lens]]s. Optometry also includes the diagnosing and treatment of eye [[disease]]s like [[glaucoma]] or ''double vision''.
