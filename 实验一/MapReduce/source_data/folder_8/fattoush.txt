 '''Fattoush''' ([[Arabic language|Arabic]]: '''فتوش''')  < ref > Can be transliterated from the Arabic in different ways, such as  '''fattush,''' '''fatush,''' '''fattoosh, '''and '''fattouche''' < /ref >  is a Lebanese [[salad]]. It includes toasted or fried pieces of [[pita]] bread, combined with chopped raw [[vegetables]]. < ref name=Wrightp241 > Wright, 2003, [https://books.google.com/books?id=x3t2IJeFIh8C & dq=parsley+salad+origins & pg=PA241 p. 241] < /ref >  A [[sauce]] with a slightly [[sour]] taste is put on the salad. < ref > Sauce ingredients: water  &  [[cornstarch]] heat until thickened. Off heat, mix in [[lemon]] juice, [[garlic]], [[sumac]], [[salt]], and [[pepper]]. Toss salad in sauce. < /ref >
