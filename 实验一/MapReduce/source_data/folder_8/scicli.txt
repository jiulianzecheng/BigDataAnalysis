 '''Scicli''' is an [[Italy|Italian]] city in [[Sicily]]. It is in the [[Province of Ragusa]]. About 27,000 people live there. Together with seven other cities in the [[Val di Noto]], it has been declared a [[UNESCO]] [[World Heritage Site]].
