'''Cartoonito''' is a programming block channel that aired on Cartoon Network Too in the UK in 2009. It was removed in 2010 but came back in 2013. 
 
 In the US, the block returned on Cartoon Network and HBO Max. It airs from 6AM to 11AM. A free Cartoonito Video app is yet to be on the App Store in the US.
