 '''Yalova''' is a [[Turkey|Turkish]] city in the northwest, which was affected in the [[earthquake]] of 17 August [[1999]]. The city is along the coast of the [[Marmara Sea]]. Yalova has a better quality of life from other Turkish cities. It has a population of 77,000. 
 
 The first people settled in this region in the [[Prehistoric]] Period. 
 
 Twin cities of Yalova are [[Bonn]] in Germany and [[Mahachkala]] in [[Russia]].
