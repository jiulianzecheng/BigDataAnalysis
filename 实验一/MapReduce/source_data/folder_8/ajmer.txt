 '''Ajmer''' is a city in [[Rajasthan]], [[India]]. Ajmer is the headquarters of the [[Ajmer district]]. The city was established as  " ''Ajayameru'' "  by a [[Chahamanas of Shakambhari|Chahamana]] ruler, either [[Ajayaraja I]] or [[Ajayaraja II]], and served as their capital until the 12th century CE.{{sfn|Har Bilas Sarda|1911|pp=68-74}}{{sfn|R. B. Singh|1964|p=87}}  
 
 Ajmer is surrounded by the [[Aravalli Range|Aravalli Mountains]]. Ajmer had been a municipality since 1869. Ajmer has been selected as one of the heritage cities for the [[Heritage City Development and Augmentation Yojana|HRIDAY]] < ref > {{Cite web|url=http://www.hridayindia.in/hriday-cities/|title=Hriday Cities {{!}} Heritage City Development and Augmentation Yojana (HRIDAY)|website=www.hridayindia.in|language=en-US|access-date=2018-09-28|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180928082927/http://www.hridayindia.in/hriday-cities/|archive-date=28 September 2018|url-status=dead}} < /ref >  and Smart City Mission schemes of the [[Government of India]]. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://hridayindia.in/|title=Introduction|website=Hriday official website|access-date=30 April 2015|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150826075658/http://hridayindia.in/|archive-date=26 August 2015|url-status=dead}} < /ref >
