 '''''Harryplax''''' is a genus of [[crab]] containing the species '''''Harryplax severus'''''. ''H. severus'' occurs at depths of {{convert|1|to|7.6|m|ft|abbr=on}}. First collected in 1998 and described in 2017, the genus name honors collector Harry T.
