 '''Lucerne''' ({{lang-de|Luzern}}, [[Swiss German]]: ''Lozärn'', [[French language|French]]: ''Lucerne'', [[Italian language|Italian]]: ''Lucerna'', [[Romansh language|Romansh]]: ''Lucerna'') is a [[city]] in [[Switzerland]]. It is the [[Capital (city)|capital]] of the [[Cantons of Switzerland|Canton]] of [[Lucerne (canton)|Lucerne]] and [[county seat|seat]] of the [[districts of Switzerland|district]] with the same name. The city has a [[population]] of about 60,000. Lucerne is the biggest and most important city in Central Switzerland. The city's [[metropolitan area]] consists of 17 [[municipality|municipalities]] in three cantons. It has an overall population of nearly 200,000 people. 
 
 == Geography == 
 Lucerne is located in central Switzerland on the northern end of [[Lake Lucerne]]. The [[Reuss (river)|river Reuss]] flows from the lake through the city. The city is surrounded by hills and [[Mountain|mountains]], for example the mountains [[Pilatus (mountain)|Pilatus]] and [[Rigi]]. 
 
 == History == 
 Lucerne joined the old Swiss confederation in 1332. 
 
 Since 2010, [[Littau]] is part of the city. 
 
 == Economy == 
 [[File:Dampfschiff Uri in Luzern.JPG|thumb|left|Steamship ''Uri'' at the port of Lucerne.]] 
 Due to its location on the shore of [[Lake Lucerne]] (''{{lang|de|Vierwaldstättersee}}'') within sight of the mountains Pilatus and Rigi, Lucerne is mainly a [[tourism|tourist destination]]. One of the city's famous landmarks is [[Kapellbrücke|Chapel Bridge]] (Kapellbrücke), a wooden [[bridge]] from the [[14th century]]. Many tourists choose Lucerne as their place to stay because the city is close to the [[Alps]] in in the center of the country. 
 
 ''Schifffahrtsgesellschaft des Vierwaldstättersees'' is the shipping company founded in 1870 that runs ships on Lake Lucerne. 
 
 Lucerne railway station ist the main station of central Switzerland. 
 
 == Culture == 
 Lucerne has a traditional ''Fasnacht ''([[Carnival|carneval]]) celebration which takes place every [[spring]].
