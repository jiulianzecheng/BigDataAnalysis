 '''Lucasfilm''' is an [[United States|American]] [[movie]] and [[television]] production [[company]]. It is based in the Letterman Digital Arts Center in [[San Francisco]], [[California]]. It is best known for the ''[[Star Wars]]'' and ''[[Indiana Jones]]'' movie franchises. It does [[Live-action|live action]] and [[animation]]. Lucasfilm became a subsidiary of Disney in 2012. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2013-03-07/how-disney-bought-lucasfilm-and-its-plans-for-star-wars|title=How Disney Bought Lucasfilmâ€”and Its Plans for 'Star Wars'|first=Devin|last=Leonard|date=8 March 2013|publisher=|accessdate=7 October 2016|via=www.bloomberg.com}} < /ref > < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.usatoday.com/story/money/business/2012/10/30/disney-star-wars-lucasfilm/1669739/|title=Disney buys Lucasfilm for $4 billion|publisher=|accessdate=7 October 2016}} < /ref > 
 
 ==History== 
 Lucasfilm was created in 1971 by George Lucas (creator of the ''Star Wars'' Franchise). It had a Lucasfilm Animation division for animation products. Lucasfilm Ltd. had done services of products in Live Action movies and Animated television series and movies.
