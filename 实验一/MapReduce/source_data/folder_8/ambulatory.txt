 The '''ambulatory''' ([[Medieval Latin]] ''ambulatorium'') is the covered passage around a [[cloister]].  < ref name= " Ching2011 " > {{cite book|author=Francis D. K. Ching|title=A Visual Dictionary of Architecture|date=2011|publisher=John Wiley  &  Sons|isbn=978-1-118-16049-7|pages=29}} < /ref >  The term is sometimes applied to the procession way around the [[east]] end of a [[cathedral]] or large church. This is usually behind the high [[altar]]. < ref name= " Archambault " > {{cite book|author=Ariane Archambault|title=The Visual Dictionary of Art  &  Architecture|date=2002|publisher=Québec Amerique|isbn=978-2-7644-0877-3|pages=152}} < /ref >
