 '''Nienburg''' is a town and capital of the [[Nienburg (district)|district Nienburg]], in [[Lower Saxony]], [[Germany]]. 
 
 == Geography == 
 === Geographical location === 
 Nienburg is on the river [[Weser]], approx. {{convert|55|km|abbr=off}} southeast of [[Bremen (city)|Bremen]], and {{convert|45|km|abbr=off}} northwest of [[Hanover]]. Nienburg is the largest town in the [[Mittelweserregion]].
