 '''Shumen''' ({{lang-bg|Шумен}}, also [[Romanization|romanized as]] ''Shoumen'' or ''Šumen'') is a [[List of cities in Bulgaria|city]] in eastern [[Bulgaria]] and is the capital of [[Shumen Province]]. In 2018, 75,550 people lived there. 
 
 From 1950 to 1965, the city was called '''Kolarovgrad'''.
