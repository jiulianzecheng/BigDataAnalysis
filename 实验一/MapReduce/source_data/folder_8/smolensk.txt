 '''Smolensk''' is a city in [[Russia]]. It is located on the [[Dnieper River]], and {{convert|360|km|sp=us}} west-southwest of [[Moscow]]. It is the [[administrative center]] of [[Smolensk Oblast]]. As of 2014, the population was 330,000.
