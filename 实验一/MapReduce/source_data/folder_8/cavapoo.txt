 The '''Cavapoo''' is a dog crossbreed between two dogs: the Poodle and the Cavalier King Charles Spaniel. < ref > {{Cite web|date=2021-03-31|title=Cavapoos {{!}} Embrace Dog Breed Library|url=https://www.embracepetinsurance.com/dog-breeds/cavapoo|access-date=2022-01-06|website=Embrace Pet Insurance|language=en}} < /ref >  They go by different names as well. < ref > {{Cite web|date=2020-01-20|title=Cockapoo vs Cavapoo (Let us help you decide)|url=https://www.cockapoohq.com/cockapoo-vs-cavapoo/|access-date=2022-01-06|website=Cockapoo HQ|language=en-US}} < /ref >  They inherit the traits from both of their parents. 
 
 == Temperament / Personality == 
 The breed is well-known for their kind, affectionate, sensitive and placid temperament / personality that makes them perfect for therapy. They are very loving and shy at first glance.
