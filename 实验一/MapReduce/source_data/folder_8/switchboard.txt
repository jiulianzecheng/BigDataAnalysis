A '''switchboard''' is usually a set of [[switch]]es. The word can refer to different things: 
 
 * [[Telephone switchboard]], used to direct telephone calls 
 * [[Electric switchboard]], used to direct electricity from one place to another 
 * [[Printed circuit board]], a flat piece of material made for attaching electronic components 
 * [[Mixing console]], used to combine, route and manipulate audio signals
