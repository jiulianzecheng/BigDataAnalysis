 '''Friedrichshafen''' is a [[city]] in [[Germany]]. It is at the north [[shore]] of the [[Lake of Constance]]. It is the second largest city at the lakeside, after [[Konstanz]]. About 58,000 people live there. The city is famous for having one of the first [[Zeppelin]]s.
