The '''Vulgate''' (lat. ''Vulgata'') is a translation of the Christian [[Bible]]. St. [[Jerome]] did most of the work. The Vulgate was written in [[Latin]] in the [[5th century]]. 
 
 ''Versio vulgata''  is Latin for the  " version commonly used " .
