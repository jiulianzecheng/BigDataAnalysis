 '''Xenomorphs''' are fictional [[alien]]s created by [[H. R. Giger]] made for the ''[[Alien (franchise)|Alien]]'' franchise starting in 1979. 
 
 ==Portrayal== 
 Their vocal effects were provided by [[Dee Bradley Baker]] for ''[[Mortal Kombat X]]''.
