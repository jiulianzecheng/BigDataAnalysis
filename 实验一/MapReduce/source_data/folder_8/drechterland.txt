 '''Drechterland''' is a [[List of municipalities of the Netherlands|municipality]] in the [[Netherlands|Dutch]] [[Provinces of the Netherlands|province]] of [[North Holland]]. In 2019, 19,597 people lived there. 
 
 It was created on 1 January 1979 from the former municipalities of Hoogkarspel, Oosterblokker and Westwoud. Until 1980, it was named '''Bangert'''. In 2006, the former municipality of [[Venhuizen]] was added to Drechterland.
