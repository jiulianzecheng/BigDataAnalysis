 '''Juan Luis Londoño Arias''' (born 28 January 1994), known professionally as '''Maluma''', is a Colombian singer. < ref name= " BIOGRAPHY {{!}} Maluma " > {{Cite web|url=https://maluma.online/biografia/?lang=en|title=BIOGRAPHY {{!}} Maluma|website=maluma.online|language=en-US|access-date=28 February 2018|archive-date=28 February 2018|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180228100745/https://maluma.online/biografia/?lang=en|url-status=dead}} < /ref >  His best known singles are  " [[Felices los 4]] " ,  " [[Borró Cassette]] " , and  " [[Corazón (Maluma song)|Corazón]] " . His single,  " [[Chantaje]] " , was a collaboration with [[Shakira]] and  " [[Medellín (song)|Medellín]] "  was with [[Madonna (entertainer)|Madonna]]. 
 
 Maluma has won a [[Latin Grammy Award]], an [[MTV Video Music Award]], two [[Latin American Music Awards]], and been nominated for a [[Grammy Award]] for [[Grammy Award for Best Latin Pop or Urban Album|Best Latin Pop Album]].
