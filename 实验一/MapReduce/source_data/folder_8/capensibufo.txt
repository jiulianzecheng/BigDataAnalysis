 '''''Capensibufo''''' is a [[genus]] of [[Bufonidae|true toads]] with only two species. The common English name is '''Cape Toads'''. They live in the [[Republic of South Africa]] from [[Breede River]] to north of [[Knysna]], [[Western Cape Province]].
