 {{Nihongo|'''''Toriko'''''|トリコ}} is a Japanese [[manga]] series created by [[Mitsutoshi Shimabukuro]]. It was published in ''[[Weekly Shōnen Jump]]'' from May 19, 2008 to November 21, 2016. The chapters are collected into 43 ''[[tankōbon]]'' volumes by [[Shueisha]]. It is about [[adventure]]s of Toriko who searches for different types of rare foods. 
 
 A [[short movie]] was created by [[Ufotable]] for the Jump Super Anime Tour 2009. Another short movie was created for the 2010 tour. There is an [[anime]] television series based on this manga. It was produced by [[Toei Animation]]. It was [[broadcast]] in Japan from 2011 to 2014. Toei also made a 2011 animated 3D short movie and a 2013 feature movie. 
 
 In 2009, ''Toriko'' was nominated for the second annual [[Manga Taishō]] award. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.animenewsnetwork.com/news/2009-01-18/10-titles-nominated-for-2nd-manga-taisho-awards|title=10 Titles Nominated for 2nd Manga Taisho Awards|publisher=[[Anime News Network]]|date=2002-01-18|access-date=2013-03-20}} < /ref >  It was the 10th best-selling manga series during the first half of 2011. Over 1.8 million copies were sold. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.animenewsnetwork.com/news/2011-05-31/top-selling-manga-in-japan-by-series/2011-first-half|title=Top-Selling Manga in Japan by Series: 2011 (First Half)|publisher=[[Anime News Network]]|date=2011-05-31|access-date=2013-03-20}} < /ref >  ''Toriko'' was the eleventh best-selling manga series of 2012 < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.animenewsnetwork.com/news/2012-12-02/30-top-selling-manga-in-japan-by-series/2012|title=30 Top-Selling Manga in Japan by Series: 2012|publisher=[[Anime News Network]]|date=2012-12-02|access-date=2013-03-20}} < /ref >  and thirteenth best of 2013. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://www.animenewsnetwork.com/news/2013-12-01/top-selling-manga-in-japan-by-series/2013 |title=Top-Selling Manga in Japan by Series: 2013 |work=[[Anime News Network]] |date=2013-12-01 |access-date=2013-12-01}} < /ref >  Over 3 million copies sold in 2012 and 2.8 million copies were sold in 2013.
