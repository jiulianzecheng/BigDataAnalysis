 '''Alimukku''''' < nowiki/ > ' < nowiki/ > '' is a village in [[Kollam]] district. The head office of Piravanthoor Panchayat is here. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 
 Nearby locations of interest include the [[Achenkovil]] Sastha temple (37 & nbsp;km away on the Alimukku-kottakkayam-mullumala-achenkovil route), Ayiravalli Shiva temple (one of the rarest shiva temples in Alimukkue, having nadaraja vigraha in kerala), State Farming Corporation of Kerala (SFCK) Ltd, and a government cooperative college founded in 2011. The Alimukku-Karavoor road is a bypass road for  " sabarimala "  travelers{{reflist}}
