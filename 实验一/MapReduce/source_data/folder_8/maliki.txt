The '''Maliki''' ([[Arabic language|Arabic]] '''مالكي''') [[madhhab]] is one of the four [[madhab|schools]] of [[Fiqh]] or religious law within [[Sunni Islam]]. It is the third-largest of the four schools. It is followed by about 15% of Muslims. Most of them are in [[North Africa]] and [[West Africa]].
