 '''Montrouis''' is a coastal communal section in [[Haiti]], located in the department of [[Artibonite (department)|Artibonite]], < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.fallingrain.com/world/HA/11/Bois_Moquette.html |title=Montrouis, Haiti Page |access-date=7 May 2014}} < /ref >  south of [[Saint-Marc]]. It has a population of 18,419 people.
