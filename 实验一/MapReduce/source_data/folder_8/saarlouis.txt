 '''Saarlouis''' is a [[town]] in the [[Saarland]], [[Germany]]. It has 38,400 inhabitants. It is on the [[Saar]]. From 1936 to 1945 it was called '''Saarlautern'''. [[Ford Motor Company]] has a plant there; it produces the [[Ford Focus|Focus]], [[Ford C-Max|C-Max]] and [[Ford Kuga|Kuga]].
