 '''Olga Vital'evna Yakovleva''' ({{Lang-ru|Ольга Витальевна Яковлева}}; 12 October 1970  & ndash; 17 January 2015), better known as '''Origa''', was a [[Russians|Russian]] [[singer]]. She worked mostly in [[Japan]] from 1993 until her death. She was signed to the [[record label]] Gemmatika and sometimes worked with [[Yoko Kanno]]. She also released eleven [[album]]s and eight singles. She was most well known for singing the intro to the [[television series]] ''Ghost in the Shell: Stand Alone Complex'' and ''Fantastic Children''. 
 
 Origa was born in [[Kochenyovsky District]], [[Novosibirsk Oblast]].
