 " '''Blessings''' "  is the third single off of [[Americans|American]] [[Rap music|rapper]] [[Big Sean]]'s third [[studio album]] ''[[Dark Sky Paradise]]''. The song was released on January 29, 2015. The album version of the song features [[Canadians|Canadian]] rapper [[Drake (musician)|Drake]]. An extended version was also released later which features American rapper [[Kanye West]]. 
 
 The song was able to make it to #9 on the [[Hot R & B/Hip-Hop Songs]] , #11 on the [[Rhythmic (chart)|Rhythmic]], and #28 on the [[Billboard Hot 100|''Billboard'' Hot 100]] charts. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.billboard.com/artist/Big-Sean/chart-history/BSI|accessdate=August 28, 2023|title=Big Sean - Hot R & B/Hip-Hop Songs Chart History|publisher=[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]}} < /ref > < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.billboard.com/artist/Big-Sean/chart-history/TFC|accessdate=August 28, 2023|title=Big Sean - Rhythmic Chart History|publisher=Billboard}} < /ref > < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.billboard.com/artist/Big-Sean/chart-history/HSI|accessdate=August 28, 2023|title=Big Sean - Hot 100 Chart History|publisher=Billboard}} < /ref >  It was also certified 4× Platinum by the [[Recording Industry Association of America]] (RIAA). < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.riaa.com/gold-platinum/?tab_active=default-award & ar=Big+Sean & ti=Blessings & format=Single & type=#search_section|accessdate=August 28, 2023|title=Big Sean - Blessings|publisher=[[Recording Industry Association of America]]}} < /ref > 
 
 In the song, Sean, Drake and Kanye rap about the things they are thankful and blessed for. 
 
 A music video for the song was released. It was shot in [[greyscale]] ([[black and white]]). Sean raps and walks through a stormy area and then raps to a priest in a [[confessional]] booth. Drake raps in a dark room with Sean. Sean sits on the stairway to Heaven and rides in a car looking outside a rain-soaked window. Kanye raps in the clouds and a dark room. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.xxlmag.com/big-sean-drake-kanye-west-get-dark-blessings-video/|accessdate=August 28, 2023|title=Big Sean, Drake and Kanye West Get Dark in ‘Blessings’ Video|publisher=[[XXL (magazine)|XXL]]}} < /ref >
