'''Badin''' ({{lang-ur|بدین}}) is a town in [[Sindh]], [[Pakistan]]. It is east of the [[Indus River]]. The region is swampy and fertile, giving good conditions for growing rice. There is some oil in the region. Badin is the capital of [[Badin District]].
