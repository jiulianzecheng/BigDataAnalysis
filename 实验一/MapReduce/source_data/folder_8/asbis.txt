 '''ASBISC''' is a multinational corporate group that is engaged in distribution of IT-products.

 == Overview == 
 ASBISC was founded in [[Belarus]] in 1990. 
 
 By 1994 ASBIS appeared on [[Russia|Russian]] and [[Ukraine|Ukrainian]] IT markets. 
 
 During 1998 ASBIS had opened departments in the [[Czech Republic]], [[Croatia]], [[Yugoslavia]], [[Hungary]], [[Poland]], [[Romania]], [[Bulgaria]] and [[Slovenia]]. 
 
 By 2004 ASBIS had opened 37 offices in 25 countries. < ref name= " Shares of ASBIS and Energoins companies will supplement WIG and WIG-PL indices " > [http://www.gpw.pl/komunikaty_en/?ph_tresc_glowna_start=show & ph_tresc_glowna_cmn_id=37277 Shares of ASBIS and Energoins companies will supplement WIG and WIG-PL indices], www.gpw.pl. Retrieved February 11, 2007. < /ref > 
 
 In 2011 ASBIS started to sell tablets and in 2012 smartphones under its own brand Prestigio. < ref > https://www.bloomberg.com/research/stocks/private/snapshot.asp?privcapId=43925909 < /ref > < ref > {{Cite web|title=Prestigio MultiPhone 5430 With Intel Inside Hits The Market|url=http://thedroidguy.com/2013/08/prestigio-multiphone-5430-intel-inside-hits-market/|url-status=dead|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20131113221813/http://thedroidguy.com/2013/08/prestigio-multiphone-5430-intel-inside-hits-market/|archive-date=2013-11-13|access-date=2013-11-14}} < /ref >
