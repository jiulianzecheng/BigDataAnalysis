 '''Sinzing''' is a [[Municipalities of Germany|municipality]] in [[Regensburg (district)|Regensburg]] in [[Bavaria]] in [[Germany]]. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 
 Sinzing is on the Schwarzen Laber and the Danube, about eight kilometers west of Regensburg.
