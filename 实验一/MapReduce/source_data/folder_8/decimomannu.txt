 '''Decimomannu''' (''Deximumànnu'') is a [[town]] and ''[[Commune|comune]]'' (municipality) in the Metropolitan City of [[Cagliari]] in [[Sardinia]], [[Italy]]. As of 2016, 8,133 people lived there. < ref > {{Cite web |title=''Bilancio demografico al 30/11/2016'' |url=http://demo.istat.it/bilmens2016gen/index02.html |access-date=2020-08-15 |archive-date=2019-03-11 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190311225842/http://demo.istat.it/bilmens2016gen/index02.html |url-status=dead }} < /ref >  Its area is 27.72 & nbsp;km². It is 10 meters above sea level.
