 '''Ingenbohl''' is a [[municipality]] in [[Schwyz (district)|Schwyz District]] in the [[canton of Schwyz]] in [[Switzerland]]. It is located along the [[Lake Lucerne]]. 
 
 == Villages == 
 The municipality consists of the village of Ingenbohl and the [[hamlet (place)|hamlets]] of [[Brunnen]], Wilen, Schränggigen and Unterschönenbuch.
