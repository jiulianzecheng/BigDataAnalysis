 '''''Muntingia''''' is a genus of plants in the family [[Muntigiaceae]]. It has only one species, the '''''Muntingia calabura'''''. < ref name= " :0 " > {{Cite web|url=http://www.kew.org/science/tropamerica/neotropikey/families/Muntingiaceae.htm|title=Neotropical Muntingiaceae - Neotropikey from Kew|website=www.kew.org|access-date=2017-05-16}} < /ref >  It was named in honor of [[Abraham Munting]]. It is native to the [[Neotropical realm|Neotropics]], from [[Mexico]] south to [[Bolivia]] and [[Argentina]]. It fully grows into a fruit.
