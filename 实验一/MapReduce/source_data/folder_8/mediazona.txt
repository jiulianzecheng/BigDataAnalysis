 '''Mediazona''' is a [[Russia|Russian]] website. Two girls from the Russian punk rock collective [[Pussy Riot]] ([[Maria Alyokhina]] and [[Nadezhda Tolokonnikova]]) made it in 2014. < ref name= " spin.com " > {{cite web|url=http://www.spin.com/2014/09/pussy-riot-mediazona-independent-russian-news-service/|title=Pussy Riot Members Launch Independent Russian News Service – SPIN|date=5 September 2014|access-date=22 November 2016}} < /ref > < ref > {{cite web |url =https://www.reuters.com/article/us-russia-pussyriot/russian-police-detain-pussy-riot-sympathizers-in-cathedral-idUSBRE91K0LE20130221|title =Russian police detain Pussy Riot sympathizers in cathedral|publisher =[[Reuters]]|date=February 21, 2013|access-date=7 May 2018}} < /ref >  Russian political journalist Sergey Smirnov is the main editor of the website. < ref name= " auto " > {{cite web|url=http://www.vanityfair.com/culture/2014/09/two-pussy-riot-members-start-a-news-service|title=Two Pussy Riot Members Start a News Service|first=Laura|last=Barcella|access-date=22 November 2016}} < /ref > 
 
 Mediazona gives information on the [[Judiciary|judicial]], [[Police officer|law enforcement]], and penal system in [[Russia]]. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.vice.com/read/what-life-is-like-in-a-prison-camp-in-the-dnr|title=What Life Is Like in a Prison Camp in the Donetsk People's Republic – VICE – United States|date=20 July 2015|access-date=22 November 2016}} < /ref > < ref name= " reuters.com " > {{cite web|url=https://www.reuters.com/article/us-russia-pussyriot-media-idUSKCN0Y01M4|title=Pussy Riot founder sets sites on Russian media|date=9 May 2017|access-date=22 November 2016|via=Reuters}} < /ref > < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.theguardian.com/world/2015/mar/09/russia-siberian-cell-death-pussy-riot|title=Under suspicion: death in a Siberian cell|first=Nikita|last=Sologub|date=9 March 2015|access-date=22 November 2016|via=The Guardian}} < /ref >  Since 2020 the site gives such news on [[Kazakhstan]], [[Kyrgyzstan]], and [[Belarus]] too. < ref name= " auto " / > 
 
 Mediazona has a Russian media license, but Tolokonnikova said in 2016 that it could be taken away at any time. < ref name= " reuters.com " / > 
 
 The site of Mediazona can't be opened in [[Belarus]]. < ref name= " :1 " > {{cite web|last=Штейн|first=Евгения|date=2020-08-29|title=В Беларуси заблокировали «Нашу Нiву» и naviny.by, журналистов иностранных СМИ лишают аккредитации|url=https://tech.onliner.by/2020/08/29/nasha-niva|publisher=[[Onliner.by]]|language=ru|access-date=2021-02-10|archive-date=2020-09-29|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200929075549/https://tech.onliner.by/2020/08/29/nasha-niva|url-status=dead}} < /ref > < ref > {{cite web|date=2020-08-22|title=В Белоруссии заблокировали несколько десятков информационных сайтов. Среди них —  " Радио Свобода "  и  " Медиазона " |trans-title=Several Dozen of Independent Media Websites Blocked in Belarus, Including Radio Freedom and Mediazone|url=https://meduza.io/news/2020/08/22/v-belorussii-zablokirovali-neskolko-desyatkov-informatsionnyh-saytov-sredi-nih-radio-svoboda-i-mediazona|access-date=2020-08-24|publisher=Meduza|language=ru}} < /ref >
