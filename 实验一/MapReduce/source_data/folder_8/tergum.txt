 A '''tergum''' (plural '''terga''') is the [[Anatomical_terms_of_location#Dorsal_and_ventral|dorsal]] portion of an [[arthropod]] segment other than the head. A given tergum may be divided into hardened plates or [[sclerite]]s commonly referred to as '''tergites'''. < ref name= " McCafferty " > McCafferty (1983). < /ref >
