 '''Goma''' is a city in the [[Democratic Republic of Congo]]. It is the capital of the [[North Kivu]] province. < ref > [https://www.bbc.com/news/av/world-africa-41806781/how-dr-congo-s-wooden-bikes-drive-profit-in-goma | How DR Congo's wooden bikes drive profit in Goma ] - BBC News < /ref > < ref > [https://www.bbc.com/news/av/world-africa-20416000/dr-congo-crisis-m23-rebels-take-goma | DR Congo crisis: M23 rebels take Goma] - BBC News < /ref > 
 
 It is located on the northern shore of [[Lake Kivu]], next to the [[Rwanda]]n city of [[Gisenyi]].
