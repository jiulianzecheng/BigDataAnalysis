 '''Voivod''' is a [[Heavy metal music|heavy metal]] band from Canada. Their musical style has been changing since their beginnings. They started out as a [[speed metal]] band, but eventually began adding a mix of [[progressive metal]] and [[thrash metal]] to create their own unique metal style. 
 
 Today, Voivod have released eleven studio albums as well as one EP, one live album, two compilations, seven demos and one DVD featuring a live concert. They rose to popularity with their fifth studio album ''Nothingface'' (1989), which was the first Voivod album to enter the [[Billboard 200|''Billboard'' 200]] charts, peaking at number 114, and is their most successful one yet. < ref > {{Cite web |url=http://www.billboard.com/bbcom/retrieve_chart_history.do?model.chartFormatGroupName=Albums & model.vnuArtistId=5967 & model.vnuAlbumId=1060393 |title=Billboard.com – Artist Chart History – Voivod |access-date=2012-06-04 |archive-date=2009-01-14 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20090114030434/http://www.billboard.com/bbcom/retrieve_chart_history.do?model.chartFormatGroupName=Albums & model.vnuArtistId=5967 & model.vnuAlbumId=1060393 |url-status=dead }} < /ref >  The last studio album Voivod released is  " Target Earth " , from 2013. They are working on a new album. 
 
 The band's bassist, Jason Newsted, joined Voivod after he left [[Metallica]] in 2001.
