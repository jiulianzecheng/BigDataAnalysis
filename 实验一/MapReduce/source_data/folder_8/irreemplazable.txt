 '''''Irreemplazable''''' is a Spanish and [[Spanglish (language)|Spanglish]] [[Extended play|EP]] by female R & B singer [[Beyoncé Knowles]]. It was released on August 28, 2007 in [[US]]. < ref > {{cite news |url=http://www.billboard.biz/bbbiz/content_display/genre/e3i590ca1c25f7cbb36795c3cdbea3208ee |title=Beyonce To Release 'Irreemplazable' As Wal-Mart Exclusive |author=Ayala Ben-Yehuda |publisher=billboard.biz |accessdate=2007-08-06 |archive-date=2007-09-26 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20070926234614/http://www.billboard.biz/bbbiz/content_display/genre/e3i590ca1c25f7cbb36795c3cdbea3208ee |url-status=dead }} < /ref >   At the same time, a [[Compact disc|CD]]/[[DVD]] combo was released exclusively at [[Wal-mart]]. The DVD features behind the scenes documentary entitled ''La Evolucion Latina de Beyoncé'' (''The Latin Evolution of Beyoncé'') as well as the music video for Beyoncé's  " [[Get Me Bodied]] "  ([[Timbaland]] remix) featuring [[Voltio]]. < ref > {{cite news|url=http://www.earthtimes.org/articles/show/news_press_release,157503.shtml|title=Music World/Columbia Records Debut the Release of Beyonce's 'Irreemplazable,' an Eight Track Spanish Language EP of 'B'Day,' Her Grammy Award Winning Album|author=Columbia Records Staff|publisher=earthtimes.org|accessdate=2007-08-22|archive-date=2011-05-20|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110520045648/http://www.earthtimes.org/articles/show/news_press_release,157503.shtml|url-status=dead}} < /ref > < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.billboard.com/bbcom/discography/more.jsp?tp=singles & pid=317670 & aid=961014|title=Beyoncé Discography|author=Billboard.com Staff|publisher=billboard.com|accessdate=2007-09-06|archive-date=2007-09-29|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20070929205850/http://www.billboard.com/bbcom/discography/more.jsp?tp=singles & pid=317670 & aid=961014|url-status=dead}} < /ref >
