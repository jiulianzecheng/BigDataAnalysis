 '''Ronchaux''' ({{IPA-fr|ʁɔ̃ʃo}}) is a [[Communes of France|commune]] in the [[Doubs]] [[Departments of France|department]]. It is in the [[Bourgogne-Franche-Comté]] [[Regions of France|region]] in eastern [[France]]. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 Ronchaux is {{convert|6|km|mi|abbr=on}} from [[Quingey]]. It is on the banks of the Bief de Caille, a stream that comes from the Mittonière cave.
