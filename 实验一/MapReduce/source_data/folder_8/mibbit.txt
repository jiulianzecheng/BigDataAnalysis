'''Mibbit''' is an online [[IRC]] client that is free of charge. It was made in [[JavaScript]] and [[Ajax (programming)|Ajax]]. Mibbit was originally released in [[2007]] and was updated in [[2008]]. It can be embedded into a website easily.
