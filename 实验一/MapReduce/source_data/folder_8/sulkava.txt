 '''Sulkava''' is a [[municipality]] in the [[Southern Savonia]] [[regions of Finland|region]] of [[Finland]]. It had almost 3,000 living there in January 2014. The municipality is best known for the annual long distance [[rowing]] contest, [[Sulkavan Suursoudut]], around Partalansaari ( " Partala Island " ). < ref > ''Finland'', eds. Paul Harding; Jennifer Brewer (Melbourne [etc.]: Lonely Planet Publications, 2003), p. 140 < /ref >  It is just as famous for the large party following the rowing event. < ref > Andy Symington, George Dunford, ''Finland'' (Footscray, Vic.: Lonely Planet, 2009), p. 144 < /ref >  The municipalities near it are [[Juva]], [[Puumala]], [[Rantasalmi]], [[Ruokolahti]] and [[Savonlinna]].
