 '''AirPort''' and '''AirPort Extreme''' are [[Wireless LAN|local area wireless networking]] products that are made by the company [[Apple Inc]]. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.apple.com/airportexpress/|title=AirPort Express - Faster is Better|publisher=[[Apple]], Inc.|access-date=2010-03-17}} < /ref > 
 
 Both the AirPort and the AirPort Extreme can both mean the [[protocol (computing)|protocol]], the expansion card or the base station. 
 
 In [[Japan]], AirPort is called the AirMacAirMac < ref > {{cite web |url= http://apple.com/jp/airmacexpress|title= アップル - AirMac Express|access-date=2008-06-22 |work= Apple, Inc.}} < /ref >  because the name AirPort was taken by [http://www.iodata.jp/prod/network/wnlan/2004/wn-apga/index.htm I-O DATA] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170607111444/http://www.iodata.jp/prod/network/wnlan/2004/wn-apga/index.htm |date=2017-06-07 }}.
