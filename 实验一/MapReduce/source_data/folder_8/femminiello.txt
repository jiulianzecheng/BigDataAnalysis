[[File:Il femminiello.jpg|thumb|Giuseppe Bonito, ''Il femminiello'']] 
 A '''''femminiello''''', also spelled '''''femmeniello''''' is an [[Italian language|Italian]] word for a male with [[feminine]] nature and behaviour, and part of an old tradition in [[Naples|Neapolitan]] culture. They are believed to bring [[luck]] in gambling and are known to participate in games such as [[bingo]] and also some traditional events.
