'''Scampurr''' is a brand name of a recreational vehicle.  The Scampurr was designed to go on land like a [[Go-Kart]] and over snow like a [[snowmobile]].  The vehicle was invented by Philip R. Hetland in 1967. It entered commercial production in [[Moorhead, Minnesota|Moorhead]], Minnesota. The original company making the Scampurr was Hetland Enterprises.
