 '''Rosenheim''' ({{IPA-de|ʁo:zənhaɪ̯m}}; {{Lang-bar|Rousnam}}) is a [[city]] in [[Upper Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. It does not belong to a rural district, but it is an urban district itself. The rivers [[Inn (river)|Inn]] and [[Mangfall]] come together in Rosenheim.
