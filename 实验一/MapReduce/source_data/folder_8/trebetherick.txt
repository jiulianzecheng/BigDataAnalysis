 '''Trebetherick''' is a [[village]] on the north coast of [[Cornwall]], [[England]]. It is on the east side of the [[River Camel]] [[estuary]]. It is about six miles (10 kilometres) north of [[Wadebridge]] and half a mile (800 yards) south of [[Polzeath]].
