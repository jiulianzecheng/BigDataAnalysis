 '''Cartagena''' is a [[city]] in the [[Murcia (autonomous community)|Region of Murcia]], in [[Spain]]. In the south of the region, at the [[Mediterranean Sea]]. The population of the city is 205,000 and it is an industrial centre. Cartagena was founded by the Carthaginese as  " Qart Hadasht "  and the name means ''New City''.
