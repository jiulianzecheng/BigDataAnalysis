 '''Graceland''' is a {{convert|13.8|acre|adj=on}} [[estate]] and large white-colored mansion that was owned by [[Elvis Presley]]. It is located in [[Memphis, Tennessee|Memphis]], [[Tennessee]], [[USA]].
