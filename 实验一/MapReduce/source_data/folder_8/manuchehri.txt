'''Abu Najm Ahmad ibn Qaus Manuchehri Damghani''' (c. [[982]] – [[1040]]) ({{lang-fa|ابونجم احمد بن قوص منوچهری دامغانی}}) or Manucheri was a [[Persian people|Persian]] [[poet]]. He was from [[Damghan]] in [[Iran]]. His poems are collected in a [[Diwan]].
