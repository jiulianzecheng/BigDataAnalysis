 '''Sempach''' is a [[municipality]] of the [[district]] of [[Sursee (district)|Sursee]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Lucerne (canton)|Lucerne]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 It is the home to the [[Sempach Bird Observatory]]. 
 
 It is on [[Lake Sempach]]. 
 
 [[Steinibühlweiher]] is located above the town.
