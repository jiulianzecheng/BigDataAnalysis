 '''Peleliu''' is one of the 16 [[States of Palau|state]]s of [[Palau]]. It is southwest of the main island of [[Babeldaob]] and is the third most populous state in Palau, after [[Koror City|Koror]] and [[Airai]]. Its capital and largest village is [[Klouklubed]].
