 '''Ngaremlengui''' is a west [[Palau]] < nowiki/ > an state. Though it has the lowest density of the country's 16 states it has the largest area and the seventh highest population. The state is divided into the three villages [[Ngchemesed]], [[Ngermetengel]] and the capital [[Imeong]].
