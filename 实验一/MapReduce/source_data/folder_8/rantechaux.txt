 '''Rantechaux''' is a former [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is in [[Bourgogne-Franche-Comté]] in the [[Doubs]] [[Departments of France|department]] in east [[France]]. On 1 January 2016, it was merged into the new commune of [[Les Premiers-Sapins]].
