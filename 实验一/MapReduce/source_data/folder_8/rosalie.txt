 }}'''''Rosalie''''' is a 1937 [[United States|American]] [[musical movie]]. It was [[Movie director|directed]] by [[W.S. Van Dyke]]. The movie stars [[Eleanor Powell]], [[Nelson Eddy]] and [[Frank Morgan]]. It is an [[Film adaptation|adaptation]] of the 1928 [[Rosalie (musical)|stage musical of the same name]], The movue was released in December 1937. It follows the story of the musical but replaces most of the [[Broadway theatre|Broadway]] score with new songs by [[Cole Porter]]. The story is about the romantic entanglements of a [[princess]] in disguise and a [[West Point]] cadet.
