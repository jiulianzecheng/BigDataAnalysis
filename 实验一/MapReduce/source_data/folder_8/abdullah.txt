'''Abdullah''' is a well-liked name in the [[Arabic language|Arabic]] language that has been used for many years. It's a common name given to boys and has a significant meaning that represents the Islamic religion. The name Abdullah is formed from two Arabic words,  " abd "  which signifies  " servant "  and  " Allah "  which signifies  " God " .
