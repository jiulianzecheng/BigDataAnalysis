 '''Fontenais''' is a [[municipality]] of the [[district]] of [[Porrentruy (district)|Porrentruy]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Jura (canton)|Jura]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 On 1 January 2013, the former municipality of [[Bressaucourt]] merged into the municipality of Fontenais.
