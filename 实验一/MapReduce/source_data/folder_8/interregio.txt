 The '''InterRegio''' or '''Interregio''' (abbreviated: '''IR''') is a category of trains in multiple European countries. They are existing in [[Switzerland]], [[Luxembourg]], [[Poland]], [[Denmark]], [[Hungary]], [[Romania]] and in [[Portugal]] and run  " from region to region " , as described by [[Swiss Federal Railways]] (SBB).
