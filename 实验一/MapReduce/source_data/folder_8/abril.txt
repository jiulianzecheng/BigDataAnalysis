'''Abril''' is a name of [[Spanish language|Spanish]] origin that is commonly given to baby girls. It is derived from the Latin word  " Aprilis, "  meaning  " to open, "  and is associated with the spring season, symbolizing new beginnings and growth.  < ref > {{Cite web|title=Abril name Meaning and Origin|url=https://babys.name/detail-Abril-Meaning-Origin-Famous-Similar-Sibling.html|access-date=2023-04-13|website=babys.name}} < /ref >
