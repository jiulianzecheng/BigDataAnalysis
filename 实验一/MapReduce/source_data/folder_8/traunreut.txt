 '''Traunreut''' ({{IPA-de|tʁaʊ̯nˈʁɔɪ̯t|-|De-Traunreut.ogg}}) is a [[Town#Germany|town]] in southeastern [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]] in [[Traunstein (district)|Traunstein]]. It is at {{Coord|47|58|N|12|35|E|region:DE_type:city(22,112)}}. Traunreut is in the middle of the [[Chiemgau]] region between [[Munich]] and [[Salzburg]], about 10 & nbsp;km east of the [[Chiemsee]], 25 & nbsp;km north of the [[Alps]], and 35 & nbsp;km west of Salzburg.
