 '''Cambuslang''' ([[Scottish Gaelic language|Scottish Gaelic]] for '''''Camas Long''''' from ''camas'' - river bend, ''long'' - ship) is a town on the south-eastern outskirts of [[Glasgow]], [[Scotland]] located within the local authority area of [[South Lanarkshire]]. It is known as  " the largest village in Scotland " , with a population of around 24,500.
