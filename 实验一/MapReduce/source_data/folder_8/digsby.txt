 '''Digsby''' was an [[instant messaging]] piece of [[software]]. It was for [[Microsoft Windows]], with plans to support [[Apple Macintosh|Mac]] and [[Linux]]. It supported [[Google Mail]], [[Yahoo]] messenger, [[MSN Messenger]], [[Facebook]] [[chat]] and others. Currently no longer operational.
