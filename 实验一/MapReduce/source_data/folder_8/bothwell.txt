'''Bothwell''' is a small town in [[South Lanarkshire]], [[Scotland]], that lies on the right bank of the [[River Clyde]], adjacent to [[Hamilton, Scotland|Hamilton]] and nine miles east-south-east of [[Glasgow]]. It is predominantly a residential town.
