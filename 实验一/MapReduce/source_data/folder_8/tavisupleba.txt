 " '''Tavisupleba''' "  ({{lang-ka|თავისუფლება}}, {{IPA-ka|tʰɑʋisupʰlɛbɑ|pron}};  " Freedom " ) is the [[national anthem]] of [[Georgia (country)|Georgia]]. It was adopted as the Georgian national anthem in May 2004 after reform in the government.
