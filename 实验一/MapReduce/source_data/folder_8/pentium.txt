'''Pentium''' is an [[x86]] [[microprocessor]], first made in 1993. It is a brand name by [[Intel]] who developed the [[Pentium I]] (1993-1999), [[Pentium II]] (1997-1999), [[Pentium III]] (1999-2003), [[Pentium 4]] (2000-2008), [[Pentium M]] (2003-2008), [[Pentium D]] (2005-2008) and [[Pentium Extreme Edition]] (2005-2008) processors. Today, it is a low range processor in the [[Intel]] processor lineup, just above the Celeron and [[Intel Atom|Atom]] and below the i3 in the [[Intel Core|Core]] family of processors.
