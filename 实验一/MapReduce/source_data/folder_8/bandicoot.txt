 A '''bandicoot''' is a kind of [[marsupial]]. There are about 20 [[species]] of bandicoots.  
 
 Bandicoots are [[omnivore]]s. They eat bugs, [[earthworm]]s, [[larvae]], and [[spider]]s. They live in parts of [[Australia]]. They are [[nocturnal]]. Bandicoots are [[endangered species]]. For protection against foxes and wild cats, they retreat to their burrows for safety. 
 
 There are different species of bandicoot including: 
 * [[Long-nosed bandicoot]], ''Perameles nasuta'' 
 * [[Bilby]], also known as the rabbit-eared bandicoot
