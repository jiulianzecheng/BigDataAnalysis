 '''Sundsvall''' is a town in the [[counties of Sweden|county]] of [[Västernorrland County|Västernorrland]] in [[Sweden]]. It is the seat of [[Sundsvall Municipality]]. In June 1888, a [[1888 Sundsvall Fire|fire affected the town]].
