 ==History== 
 It is thought there were settlements in the Benidorm area possibly as far back as 3000 BC, including evidence of [[Ancient Rome|Roman]] and [[Punic]] remains. 
 
 ==Politics== 
 Benidorm has favoured the right wing [[People's Party (Spain)|People's Party]] (PP) since the general elections of 1993. < ref > [http://www1.pre.gva.es/pls/argos_elec/DMEDB_ElecMunicipios.informeElec?aNMuniid=3031 & aVLengua=v election results in Benidorm]{{dead link|date=October 2016 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }} < /ref >  The PP gained control of the local council at the 1995 local elections and won 14 of the 25 council seats in the 1999 and 2003 elections.
