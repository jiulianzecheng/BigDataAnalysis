 '''Segregation''' or '''segregate''' may refer to: 
 
 * [[Racial segregation]], separation of humans into racial groups 
 * [[Religious segregation]], separation of people according to their religion 
 * [[Sex segregation]], separation of people according to their biological sex 
 * [[Age segregation]], separation of people based on their age 
 
 * [[Financial Segregation]], 
 separation of people based on their income and/or inability to fit into a pre-determined tax bracket. Refusal of aid during a crisis or pandemic with financial status being the supportive factor for refusal.
