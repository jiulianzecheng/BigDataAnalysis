 '''Praktica''' was the brand name for a number of [[camera]]s, that were built by the state-owned company [[Kamera-Werke Niedersedlitz]], and its predecessors.  Between 1949 and 2001, about 9 mililon cameras were made. < ref > [http://www.sdtb.de/VEB-Pentacon-Dresden.1494.0.html VEB Pentacon Dresden] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20161003022218/http://www.sdtb.de/VEB-Pentacon-Dresden.1494.0.html |date=2016-10-03 }}, [[Deutsches Technikmuseum Berlin]], 2008 < /ref > 
 
 With the [[German reunification]], the company was dissolved. The brand name was given to Carl Zeiss AG
