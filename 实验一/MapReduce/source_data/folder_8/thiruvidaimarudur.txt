[[Thiruvidaimarudur]] is a panchayat town in [[Thanjavur district]] in [[Tamil Nadu]], [[India]]. The popular [[Mahalingeshwar Temple, Thiruvidaimarudur|Mahalingeshwar Temple]] is located here. 
 ==Population==
