 '''''Altispinax''''' (meaning  " with high [[spine]]s " ) is an [[Predation|predatory]] [[theropod]] [[dinosaur]] that lived near [[Sussex]], [[England]]. It lived during the [[Early Cretaceous]], about 137 million years ago.
