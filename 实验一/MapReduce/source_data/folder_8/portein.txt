 '''Portein''' ({{lang-rm|Purtagn}}) was a [[municipality]] in [[Hinterrhein (district)|Hinterrhein]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Graubünden]], [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 On 1 January 2010, the former municipalities of [[Cazis]], Portein, [[Präz]], [[Sarn, Switzerland|Sarn]] and [[Tartar, Switzerland|Tartar]] joined together to become the municipality of [[Cazis]].
