 '''Ayiroor''' ('''Ayroor''') is a village in the [[Pathanamthitta district]], [[Kerala]] state, [[India]]. < ref name= " :0 " > {{Cite web|url=http://www.ayroor.com/|title=Ayroor|last=Matthew|first=Sajen|date=26 March 2008|website=Ayroor}} < /ref >  The [[Pamba River]] flows on the southern side of the village, making the soil around the village very fertile. 
 
 ==People== 
 
 People of Ayiroor cultivate [[coconut]], [[rubber]], [[Cocoa bean|cocoa]], [[tapioca]], [[Cooking banana|plantains]], [[cinnamon]], pepper, [[nutmeg]], [[vanilla]], [[rambutan]],etc 
 
 ==Religion== 
 One of the highlights of Ayiroor is the ''Cherukolpuzha Hindu Parishad'', a religious festival that is held for a week in February.  < ref > {{Cite news|url=https://www.thehindu.com/news/national/kerala/rajnath-singh-to-open-hindu-meet-at-cherukolpuzha/article6837321.ece|title=Rajnath Singh to open Hindu meet at Cherukolpuzha|date=30 January 2015|newspaper=The Hindu|access-date=29 August 2019}} < /ref >  [[Snake boat races]] also attract people to the village. < ref > {{Cite web|url=http://www.aranmulakannadi.com/aranmulakannadi/shop/aranmula.htm|title=Aranmula Kannada About ARANMULA|website=www.aranmulakannadi.com|access-date=2016-12-23}} < /ref >
