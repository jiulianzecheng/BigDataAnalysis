 '''Levone''' is a ''[[comune]]'' in the [[Metropolitan City of Turin]] in the [[Piedmont]] region in [[Italy]]. It is about {{convert|30|km|mi}} northwest of [[Turin]]. 
 
 Levone is next to these municipalities: [[Forno Canavese]], [[Rivara]], [[Rocca Canavese]], and [[Barbania]].
