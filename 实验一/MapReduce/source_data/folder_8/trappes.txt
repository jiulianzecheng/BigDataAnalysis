 '''Trappes''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is in [[Île-de-France]] in the [[Yvelines]] [[Departments of France|department]] in north-central [[France]]. In 2019, 32,645 people lived there. 
 
 The commune is in the western [[suburb]]s of [[Paris]], {{convert|26.7|km|mi|abbr=on}} from the center, in the new town of [[Saint-Quentin-en-Yvelines]].
