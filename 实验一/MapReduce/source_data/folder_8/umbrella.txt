 An '''umbrella''' is a hand [[tool]] that is used to stop [[rain]] from falling on a person. It is also used to make [[shade]] and protect people from [[sun]]light. An umbrella made for protection from the sunlight is called a '''parasol'''. And a plastic umbrella is cheap but it is fragile.
