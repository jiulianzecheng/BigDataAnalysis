'''Moncla''' is a [[Commune in France|commune]] of the [[Pyrénées-Atlantiques]] ''[[departments of France|département]]'' in the southwestern part of [[France]]. 
 
 == Geography == 
 Moncla has a total area of 5.8 square kilometers.
