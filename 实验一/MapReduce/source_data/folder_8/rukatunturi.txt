'''Rukatunturi''' ({{lang-fi|Ruka}}) is a [[hill]] (or [[fell]]) in [[Kuusamo]], [[Finland]]. It is 490 & nbsp;metres (1,610 & nbsp;ft) high. There is a [[ski resort]] there.
