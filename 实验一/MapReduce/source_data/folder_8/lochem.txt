 '''Lochem''' is a municipality and city in the province of [[Gelderland]], the Netherlands. About 34,000 people were living there in 2021. It lies both in the [[Achterhoek]] and the [[IJsselvallei]] regions, and borders the [[Salland]] region in the north. 
 
 Lochem got [[city rights]] in 1233. The municipality of [[Gorssel]] joined Lochem in 2005. 
 
 Between Lochem and [[Barchem]] lie the forests of the Lochemse Berg and the Kale Berg. The [[Twentekanaal]] begins at the southwest municipal border with the city of [[Zutphen]] and is running to the east, dividing the municipality of Lochem in two. The city of Lochem lies on the southside.
