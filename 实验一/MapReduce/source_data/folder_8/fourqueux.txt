 '''Fourqueux''' is a former [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is in [[Île-de-France]] in the [[Yvelines]] [[Departments of France|department]] in north-central [[France]]. On 1 January 2019, it was merged into the commune of [[Saint-Germain-en-Laye]]. < ref > [https://www.legifrance.gouv.fr/eli/arrete/2018/12/19/TERB1905205A/jo/texte Arrêté préfectoral] 19 December 2018 {{in lang|fr}} < /ref >  It is a small suburb 20 & nbsp;km west of [[Paris]].
