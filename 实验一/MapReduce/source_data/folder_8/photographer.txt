 A '''photographer''' (from [[Greek language|Greek]] φῶς (''phos''), meaning  " [[light]] " , and γραφή (''graphê''), meaning  " [[drawing]], [[writing]] " , together meaning  " [[drawing]] with [[light]] " ) is someone who is [[skill]]ed at taking [[pictures]] or photographs with a [[camera]]. An ''[[amateur]] photographer'' takes pictures for their own use. A ''[[professional]] photographer'' takes pictures as all or part of their job. Many people hire a photographer for their [[wedding]], ''[[debutante]]'' party or other special events.  Photographers work in many different specialties. 
 
 There are several different kinds of photography. < ref name=BI > {{cite web |url=http://www.brooks.edu/student-life/brooks-blog/july-2013/5-types-of-photographers |title=5 Types of Photographers |author= |date=11 July 2013 |website= |publisher=Brooks Institute |accessdate=4 December 2015 |archive-date=16 July 2013 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130716041115/http://www.brooks.edu/student-life/brooks-blog/july-2013/5-types-of-photographers |url-status=dead }} < /ref >   Each type requires a different set of skills. Some examples include: 
 *A '''news photographer''' can take photographs quickly when [[event]]s are rapidly changing. They act as the  " eyes "  for their [[publication]]s. < ref name= McGintyHC > {{cite web |url=http://work.chron.com/three-duties-newspaper-photographer-20525.html |title=Three Duties for a Newspaper Photographer |author=Nathan McGinty |website= |publisher=The Houston Chronicle |accessdate=4 December 2015}} < /ref >  Their job is to take as many photographs as they can so an [[editor]] can later select the best ones for the story. They [[Photo editing|correct photos]] with [[software]], add [[wikt:caption|caption]]s, and send them to their editors. Many news photographers also shoot rapid sequences of film as short [[video]]s. < ref name= McGintyHC/ > 
 *An '''aerial photographer''' is comfortable around [[airplane]]s and is skilled at taking high and low [[altitude]] pictures. They work with specialized cameras which can take [[infrared]], [[3D|three-dimensional]], [[Black and White|black and white]] and [[color]] aerial photographs. < ref > {{cite web |url= http://www.academicinvest.com/arts-careers/fine-arts-careers/how-to-become-an-aerial-photographer |title= How to Become an Aerial Photographer: Career Path Guide |author= |website= |publisher= Scholar Invest Inc |accessdate=4 December 2015}} < /ref > 
 *A '''still life photographer''' takes pictures of [[still life]] or [[wikt:inanimate|inanimate]] objects. < ref name=BI/ >  This can be for  [[advertising]], [[art]] or [[science]]. The subjects may be [[food]], products, or [[nature]] studies. Still life photographers must know how to use [[lighting]], [[angle]]s and have other skills to make an object look as good in [[movie]]s as it does in real life. 
 *A '''fashion photographer''' takes pictures of [[clothing]], [[Cosmetics|makeup]], [[Hair|hair designs]] and other similar products. They shoot pictures of [[Model (person)|models]].  In addition to fashion, they may specialize in areas such as the [[music industry]] or [[movie]]s. < ref name=BI/ > 
 *A '''combat photographer''' is usually, but not always, a [[military]] occupation. They take [[documentary]] photographs and videos for many different uses. They may be involved in [[reconnaissance]], [[training]] or supporting writers who write stories for publication. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://neilvn.com/tangents/combat-photographer-military-photographer-marines/ |title=insight: working as a combat photographer in the US Marines |author=Tammy Hineline |date=19 February 2014 |website= |publisher=Photography by Neil van Niekerk |accessdate=4 December 2015}} < /ref >   Depending on what part of the military they are in, they often have highly specialized duties. 
 *A '''portrait photographer''' takes pictures of people. [[Portrait]]s aim to capture the personality of the subject.  Styles include Boudoir, engagement, High School Senior, newborn and family portraits.  < ref > {{Cite web|url=https://digital-photography-school.com/portrait-photography-tips/|title=Portrait Photography Tips - dPS|website=Digital Photography School|language=en-US|access-date=2019-02-22}} < /ref > 
 * One who makes [[motion pictures]] is a [[cameraman]].
