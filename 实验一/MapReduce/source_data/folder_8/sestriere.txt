 '''Sestriere''' is a [[ski resort]] in [[Piedmont]], [[Italy]] and a ''[[comune]]'' in the [[Metropolitan City of Turin]]. 
 
 Sestriere was a main venue during the [[2006 Winter Olympics|2006 Winter Olympic Games]] and the [[2006 Winter Paralympics]], hosting all the men's alpine skiing competitions and being the site of one of the three Olympic Villages. It is a part of the [[Via Lattea]] (Milky Way) ski area in Italy, where the [[2006 Winter Olympics]] were held.
