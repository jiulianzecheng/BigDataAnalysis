 '''Polotsk''' < ref > {{Cite web |url=http://www.un.int/belarus/about/history.htm |title=Occidental spelling according to the Belarus Permanent Mission to the United Nations. |access-date=2008-04-09 |archive-date=2013-09-27 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130927100322/http://www.un.int/belarus/about/history.htm |url-status=dead }} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite web |url=http://www.belarus.by/en/belarus/territory/vitebsk/polotsk/ |title=Occidental spelling according to the official Belarus website. |access-date=2008-04-09 |archive-date=2009-04-08 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20090408162756/http://www.belarus.by/en/belarus/territory/vitebsk/polotsk/ |url-status=dead }} < /ref > < ref > [http://www.nationsonline.org/oneworld/belarus.htm Occidental spelling according to  " Nations Online "  website.] < /ref > < ref > [http://maps.google.com/maps?f=q & hl=en & geocode= & q=Polotsk,+Belarus & sll=37.0625,-95.677068 & sspn=43.393645,82.265625 & ie=UTF8 & ll=55.489552,28.7969 & spn=0.24353,0.6427 & z=11 & om=1  Spelling according to Google Maps.] < /ref >  ('''Polatsk''', {{lang-be|По́лацк}}, {{lang-ru|Полоцк}}, {{lang-pl|Połock}}) is a historical city in [[Belarus]]. It is located on the [[Western Dvina|Dvina]] river. It is the center of Polotsk district in [[Vitsebsk Voblast]].  About 79,000 people live there.  It is served by [[Polotsk Airport]] and during the [[Cold War]] was home to [[Borovitsy]] air base.
