 '''Guipronvel''' ({{lang-br|Gwiproñvel}}) is a former [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is found in the [[Regions of France|region]] [[Brittany]] in the [[Finistère]] [[Departments of France|department]] in the northwest of [[France]]. On 1 January 2017, it was merged into the new commune of [[Milizac-Guipronvel]]. < ref > [https://www.legifrance.gouv.fr/eli/arrete/2016/6/29/INTB1618175A/jo/texte Arrêté préfectoral] 29 June 2016 {{in lang|fr}} < /ref >
