She is a [[Twitch (service)|Twitch]] [[Live streaming|streamer]] and [[YouTuber]]. 
 
 Anys is best known for her live streams on Twitch, where she is best known for playing ''[[League of Legends]]'' and ''[[Fortnite]]''.
