 '''Corundum''' is a [[mineral]]. It is very hard, with a Mohs hardness of 9 ([[diamond]] is 10). It is usually clear. Its [[chemical formula]] is [[aluminium oxide]], Al < sub > 2 < /sub > O < sub > 3 < /sub > . Sometimes some of the aluminium atoms get replaced with [[chromium]] atoms. It then becomes a [[ruby]]. Other impurities can make a [[sapphire]].
