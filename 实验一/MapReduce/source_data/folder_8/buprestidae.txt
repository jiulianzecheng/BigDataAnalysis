 '''Buprestida''' is a [[Family (biology)|family]] of [[Beetle|beetles]]. In [[English language|English]], they are known as '''jewel beetles''' or '''metallic wood-boring beetles''', because of the coloring of their [[carapace]].  
 
 The family is among the largest of the beetles, with some 15,000 recent [[species]]  in 450 [[Genus|genera]]. In addition, almost 100 [[fossil]] species have been described. < ref > Akiyama K.  &  S. Ohmomo 2000. ''The Buprestid Beetles of the World''. Iconographic Series of Insects 4.
