'''Stephanie''' (which can be spelled in different ways) [ '''stef'''-''uh''-nee ] < ref > {{Cite web|title=Definition of Stephanie {{!}} Dictionary.com|url=https://www.dictionary.com/browse/stephanie|access-date=2023-04-08|website=www.dictionary.com|language=en}} < /ref >  is a female [[given name]]. Stephanie is a name that has been commonly given to girls for hundreds of years. Its origins come from the Greek name Stephanos, which translates to  " crown "  or  " garland. "  Over time, many well-known individuals have been named Stephanie, contributing to its rich history. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Stephanie name Meaning and Origin|url=https://babys.name/detail-Stephanie-Meaning-Origin-Famous-Similar-Sibling.html|access-date=2023-04-08|website=babys.name}} < /ref >  The masculine version is '''Stephen'''. Stephanie is a very popular female name. Many celebrities and fictional characters carry the name. For example, Stefani Germanotta goes by the stage name [[Lady Gaga]].
