 '''Mendoza''' is the [[capital city]] of the [[Mendoza Province]] in [[Argentina]]. It is the 4th largest city in Argentina (after [[Buenos Aires]], [[Córdoba, Argentina|Córdoba]] and [[Rosario]]). 
 
 == Population == 
 It has about 111,000 [[inhabitant]]s, with another 848,660 more inhabitants in the city area.
