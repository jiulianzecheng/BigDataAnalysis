 '''Dravograd''' ({{lang-de|Unterdrauburg}}) is a small [[town]] in northern [[Slovenia]], close to the border with [[Austria]]. It is the seat of the [[Municipality of Dravograd]] and is on the [[Drava|Drava River]] at the confluence with the [[Me≈æa]] and the [[Mislinja (river)|Mislinja]].
