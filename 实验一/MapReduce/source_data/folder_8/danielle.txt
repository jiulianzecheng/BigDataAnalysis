'''Danielle''' is a female [[given name]]. It is pronounced [ dan-'''yel''' ]. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Definition of Danielle {{!}} Dictionary.com|url=https://www.dictionary.com/browse/danielle|access-date=2023-03-17|website=www.dictionary.com|language=en}} < /ref >  It is feminine of the name ''Daniel''. ''Daniel'' is [[Hebrew language|Hebrew]] for  " God is my judge " . < ref > {{Cite web|title=danielle name Meaning and Origin|url=https://babys.name/detail-danielle-Meaning-Origin-Famous-Similar-Sibling.html|access-date=2023-03-17|website=babys.name}} < /ref >  Danielle is a strongly popular female name for certain countries.This name was ranked #443 on the US Popular Names in 2021. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Popular Baby Names|url=https://www.ssa.gov/OACT/babynames/|access-date=2023-03-17|website=www.ssa.gov|language=en}} < /ref >   
 
 There are many variants for this name. Famous people by the name include [[Danielle Steel]] and [[Danielle Fishel]]. This name is currently being used on [[Atlantic Ocean|Atlantic]] [[tropical cyclone]]s.
