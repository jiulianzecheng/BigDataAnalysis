 '''Niue''' is a [[Sovereign state|sovereign]] [[island]] [[country]] in the south [[Pacific Ocean|Pacific]].  
 
 It is located between [[Tonga]], [[Samoa]] and the [[Cook Islands]], it is commonly known as  " Rock of [[Polynesia]] " .  
 
 It has its own [[government]], but it is an [[Realm of New Zealand|associated state]] of [[New Zealand]]. This means that Niue's head of state is New Zealand's [[Sovereignty|sovereign]] (king) in right, and most [[diplomacy|diplomatic]] relations are conducted by New Zealand on Niue's [[:wikt:behalf|behalf]]. 
 
 Niue is not a member state of the [[United Nations]], but it is a member of some United Nations [[agency|agencies]]. 
 
 The territory is 2,400 kilometres northeast of New Zealand in a triangle between [[Tonga]], [[Samoa]], and the [[Cook Islands]].
