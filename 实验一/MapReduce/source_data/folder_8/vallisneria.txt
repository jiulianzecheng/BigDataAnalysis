 '''''Vallisneria''''' is a [[genus]] of [[freshwater]] [[aquatic plant]]. It is often called '''eelgrass''', '''tape grass''' or '''vallis'''. It grows submereged in water. It has thin leaves like a ribbon, which allows water [[current (fluid)|current]]s to pass through without damaging the leaves.
