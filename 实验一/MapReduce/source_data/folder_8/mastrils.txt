 '''Mastrils''' was a [[municipality]] of the [[district]] of [[Landquart (district)|Landquart]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Graubünden]], [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 On 1 January 2012, the former municipalities of [[Igis]] and Mastrils merged into the new municipality of [[Landquart, Graubünden|Landquart]].
