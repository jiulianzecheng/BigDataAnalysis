 The '''Tornjak''' (pronounced as  " tornyak " ), is a Bosnian and Herzegovinian – Croatian Shepherd dog (official English name). < ref name= " FCITornjak " > {{cite web|url=http://www.fci.be/en/nomenclature/BOSNIAN-AND-HERZEGOVINIAN-CROATIAN-SHEPHERD-DOG-355.html|title=Tornjak|publisher=FCI|access-date=28 May 2018}} < /ref >  It is a mountain [[dog|sheep dog]] native to [[Bosnia and Herzegovina]] and [[Croatia]]. The dog was mentioned in documents in the 11th and 14th-century. It almost became extinct, but was saved when a breeding program began in the early 1970s. < ref > {{cite book|url=https://web.hks.hr/wp-content/uploads/2017/12/Tornjak-eng.pdf|title=Tornjak (Bosnian and Herzegovinian – Croatian Shepherd dog)|date=23 November 2017|publisher=FCI|access-date=29 May 2018}} < /ref >
