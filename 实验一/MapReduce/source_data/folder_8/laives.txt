 '''Laives''' ({{lang-de|Leifers}}) is a [[city]] and ''[[comune]]'' in [[South Tyrol]] in [[Trentino-Alto Adige/Südtirol]], [[Italy]]. It is one of only five mainly Italian speaking municipalities in South Tyrol, and the fourth largest municipality in the province.
