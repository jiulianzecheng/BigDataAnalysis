'''Sursk''' ({{lang-ru|Сурск}}, 53°05'N 45°42'E) is town in [[Penza Oblast]], [[Russia]]. Its population is near 7,500 ([[2005]]). The town was founded in [[1860]]. 
 
 Sursk is located on the [[Sura River]]'s left bank. It is 92 & nbsp;km from [[Penza]].
