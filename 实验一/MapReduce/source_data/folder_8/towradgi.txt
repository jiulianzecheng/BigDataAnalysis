 '''Towradgi''' is a small suburb, north of [[Wollongong]]. It is on the coast and close to beaches. 
 
 == Attractions  == 
 It has a [[school]], [[soccer]] fields, a [[lawn-bowling]] field, and many other attractions. It has a few [[restaurants]]/[[dinner|dining]] places.
