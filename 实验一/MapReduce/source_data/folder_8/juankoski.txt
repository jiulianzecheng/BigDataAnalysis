 '''Juankoski''' is a [[town]] in [[Northern Savonia]], [[Finland]]. About 5,000 [[people]] lived there in January 2014. The municipalities next to it are [[Juuka]], [[Kaavi]], [[Kuopio]], [[Rautavaara]] and [[Tuusniemi]]. Since 2017, it was merged into the city of [[Kuopio]].
