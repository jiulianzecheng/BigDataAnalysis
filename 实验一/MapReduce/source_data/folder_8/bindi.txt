 '''Bindi''' is a colored dot located on the center of the [[forehead]]. Bindi is a symbol of the [[origin of life]] in [[Indian subcontinent|countries]] where [[Hinduism]] and [[Jainism]] are preached. 
 
 Bindi is known also as the [[third eye]].
