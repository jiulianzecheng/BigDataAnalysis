 '''South''' is one of the 4 cardinal [[direction]]s on a [[compass]]. South is normally [[down]] on the bottom of most [[map]]s. For example: [[Angola]] is to the south of [[Nigeria]], which is itself to the south of [[Morocco]] . The [[South Pole]] is the farthest south you can go.
