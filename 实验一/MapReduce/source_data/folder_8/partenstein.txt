 '''Partenstein''' is a community in the [[Main-Spessart|Main-Spessart district]] in the ''[[Regierungsbezirk]]'' of [[Lower Franconia]] (''Unterfranken'') in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]] and the seat of the ''Verwaltungsgemeinschaft'' (Administrative Community) of Partenstein. Partenstein is on ''[[Bundesstraße]]'' 276. 
 
 == Geography == 
 
 === Location === 
 Partenstein is in the Main Spessart Region in the middle of the [[Spessart]] (range) on the left edge of the river Lohr, some 7 & nbsp;km northwest of the town of [[Lohr am Main]]. 
 
 The community has the following ''Gemarkungen'' (traditional rural cadastral areas): Partenstein, Partensteiner Forst.
