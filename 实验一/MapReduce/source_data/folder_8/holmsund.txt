 '''Holmsund''' is a [[Urban areas in Sweden|locality]] in [[Umeå Municipality]] in [[Västerbotten County]] in [[Sweden]]. In 2010, 5,489 people lived there. < ref name=scb / >  The locality is 18 kilometers south of the town of [[Umeå]]. Holmsund serves as a port for Umeå.
