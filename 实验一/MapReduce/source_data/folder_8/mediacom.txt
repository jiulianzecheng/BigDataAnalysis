 '''Mediacom Communications''' is an [[United States|American]] [[cable television]], [[internet]] < ref > {{cite web|title=Internet Service|publisher=Mediacom|url=https://mediacomcable.com/products/internet/1gig|access-date=Jan 30, 2018}} < /ref >  and [[telephone]] company. It was founded in July 1995. 
 
 The company serves [[Columbia, Missouri|Columbia]], [[Jefferson City, Missouri|Jefferson City]] and [[Springfield, Missouri]], the [[Quad Cities]] of southeast [[Iowa]] and western [[Illinois]], [[Cedar Rapids, Iowa|Cedar Rapids]], [[Iowa City, Iowa|Iowa City]] and [[Waterloo, Iowa|Waterloo]] in Iowa. < ref > {{cite web|title=Mediacom Rolls a Gig across Iowa|publisher=CED|url=https://www.cedmagazine.com/news/2017/01/mediacom-rolls-gig-across-iowa|access-date=Jan 30, 2018|archive-date=September 4, 2017|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170904152928/https://www.cedmagazine.com/news/2017/01/mediacom-rolls-gig-across-iowa|url-status=dead}} < /ref >  It also serves certain areas of [[Minnesota]], [[Alabama]] and outlying areas of the [[Florida]] Gulf Coast. It serves a total of twenty-two states. < ref > {{cite web|title=Mediacom Cable|publisher=Broadband|url=https://broadbandnow.com/Mediacom-Cable|access-date=Jan 30, 2018}} < /ref > 
 
 Mediacom was a publicly traded firm. It later went private. It is now{{when}} owned by Commisso. < ref > {{cite web|title=Mediacom Communications|publisher=Business Wire|url=https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20110304006092/en/Mediacom-Communications-Rocco-B.-Commisso-Complete-Going-Private|access-date=Jan 30, 2018}} 
 < /ref > 
 
 ==Reputation== 
 The September 28, 2012 edition of ''PC Magazine'' said Mediacom was one of the nation's worst 15 fastest [[internet service provider]]s. < ref > {{cite web|title=Fastest Internet Providers|publisher=The PC Magazine|url=https://www.pcmag.com/article2/0,2817,2410327,00.asp|access-date=Jan 30, 2018|archive-date=July 4, 2017|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170704100914/http://www.pcmag.com/article2/0,2817,2410327,00.asp|url-status=dead}} < /ref > 
 
 The Des Moines ''Business Record'', in its 2012 Best of Des Moines issue, gave Mediacom the top award for Best Local Internet Service Provider and Best Company Use of Social Media. < ref > {{cite web|title=Des Moines Business Publications|publisher=Des Moines, Iowa|url=http://www.pageturnpro.com/Business-Publications/44009-Best-of-2012/defaultie.html#36|access-date=Jan 30, 2018}} < /ref > 
 
 In 2016, Mediacom was named the Nation's Top Communications Provider by the publication Cable Fax. < ref > {{cite web|title=Mediacom Communications Named Nation's Top Cable Operator|publisher=The Business Wire|url=https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20160725005951/en/Mediacom-Communications-Named-Nation%e2%80%99s-Top-Cable-Operator|access-date=Jan 30, 2018}} < /ref >
