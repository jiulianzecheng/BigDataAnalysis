 '''Ovalle''' is a [[List of cities in Chile|city]] and [[Communes of Chile|commune]] in the [[Coquimbo Region]], [[Chile]]. According to the 2002 census, 98,089 people lived there. 
 
 == History == 
 Ovalle was founded in 1831.
