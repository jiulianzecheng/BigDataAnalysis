'''BeatBullying''' (BB) is a national charity in the United Kingdom. The purpose of BB is to help people to understand, recognise, and say no to bullying. This is done by giving them the tools to change their lives and the lives of other people. < ref name= " about " > BeatBullying, [http://www.beatbullying.org/dox/home/about-bb.html  " About BB " ] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130213132142/http://www.beatbullying.org/dox/home/about-bb.html |date=2013-02-13 }}; retrieved 2013-2-19. < /ref >
