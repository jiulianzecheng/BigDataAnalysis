 '''Kriechenwil''' is a [[municipality]] in the [[administrative district]] of [[Bern-Mittelland (administrative district)|Bern-Mittelland]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Bern (canton)|Berne]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 ==Demographics== 
 Kriechenwil has a population ({{as of|{{Swiss populations YM|CH-BE}}|lc=on}}) of {{Swiss populations|CH-BE|0666}}.{{Swiss populations ref|CH-BE}}  {{as of|2010}}, 3.6% of the population are resident foreign nationals.  Over the last 10 years (2001-2011) the population has changed at a rate of 0.7%.
