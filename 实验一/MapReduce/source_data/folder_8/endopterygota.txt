 The '''Endopterygota''' (or '''Holometabola'''), are [[insect]]s of the subclass [[Pterygota]]. 
 
 They have [[Metamorphosis|complete metamorphosis]]. This means they go through different [[larva]]l, [[pupa]]l, and [[adult]] stages. The larval and adult stages differ very much in their [[anatomy]] and [[behavior]]. This is called [[holometabolism]], or complete metamorphism. 
 
 They are distinguished from the [[Exopterygota]] (or Hemipterodea) by the way in which their wings develop.
