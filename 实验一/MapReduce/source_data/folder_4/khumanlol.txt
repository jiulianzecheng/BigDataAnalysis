'''Khumanlol''' ({{lang-mni|ꯈꯨꯃꯟꯂꯣꯜ}}), also spelt as '''Khumanlon''' ({{lang-mni|ꯈꯨꯃꯟꯂꯣꯟ}}), is a [[chronicle]] ([[Puya (Meitei texts)|puya]]) of the [[genealogy]] of the rulers of the Khuman dynasty, < ref > {{cite web | url=https://books.google.com/books?id=_QtuAAAAMAAJ & q=khuman+Lon | title=Proceedings of the Session | year=1971 }} < /ref >  which flourished as an independent principality in the south of [[Ancient Manipur]], and later absorbed into the [[Meitei ethnicity]] in the fourteenth century AD. < ref > {{cite web | url=https://books.google.com/books?id=LvRtAAAAMAAJ & q=khumanlol+Meitei | title=Proceedings of the Meetings of the Session | year=1976 }} < /ref > < ref > {{cite web | url=https://books.google.com/books?id=lV1uAAAAMAAJ & q=khumanlon | title=Ethnicity and Social Change: An Anthology of Essays | last1=Kabui | first1=Gangmumei | year=2002 }} < /ref > 
 It is categorized as one of the historical documents of the ethnic groups settling in [[Manipur]] since seventh century AD. < ref > {{cite book | url=https://books.google.com/books?id=doQeAAAAMAAJ & q=khumanlol+seventh+century | title=Religion and Culture of Manipur | isbn=9788170490210 | last1=Singh | first1=Moirangthem Kirti | year=1988 }} < /ref >
