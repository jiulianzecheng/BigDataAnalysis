 '''Kannonkoski''' is a [[municipality]] in [[Central Finland]]. It had about 1,452 people living there in 2016. The municipalities next to it are [[Karstula]], [[Kivijärvi]], [[Saarijärvi]], [[Viitasaari]] and [[Äänekoski]].
