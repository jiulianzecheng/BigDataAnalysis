 '''Dasaut''' Village DASAUT Panchayat Shivajinagar is in Samastipur District. People of this village are living in very peaceful manner. This village having very proud history. Agriculture is the main profession of this village. Still this [[village]] is waiting for Industrial development. The Dasaut village has population of 8610 of which 4533 are males while 4077 are females as per Population Census 2011. It is 12km away from sub-district headquarter [[Shivajingar|Shivaji Nagar]] (tehsildar office) and 40km away from district headquarter Samastipur.
