 '''Azawad''' is the name for the northern part of [[Mali]]. The name translates to [[Savanna]]h. The region is mostly [[desert]]-like. Between 2012 and 2013 it was independent from Mali.  It is split into the regions [[Timbuktu Region|Timbuktu]], [[Gao Region|Gao]], and [[Kidal Region|Kidal]]. The territory has a size of about {{Convert|882000|km2||abbr=}}. The biggest cities are Gao with about 87.000 people, and Timbuktu with about 55.000.
