 '''Hagakure''' ''(Hidden by the Leaves)'' is a [[Japan|Japanese]] practical guide for [[samurai]] with commentaries by [[Yamamoto Tsunetomo]]. The book contains views on [[bushido]], the warrior code of the samurai. Bushido is considered The Way of Dying. It means samurai is ready to death at any time for [[Honour|honor]].
