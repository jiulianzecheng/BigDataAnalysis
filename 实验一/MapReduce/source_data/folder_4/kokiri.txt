 '''Kokiri''' is a town on the Arnold River in the west of New Zealand's [[South Island]]. It has a train stop. 
 
 == Location == 
 Kokiri is on the train line between Greymouth and Christchurch. It is 14 miles from Greymouth and 37 miles from Otira. It is also five miles from Stillwater Junction. Its train stop is 79 feet (24 metres) above sea level. The town is on the west side of the Arnold River in Grey County's Maori Creek riding.
