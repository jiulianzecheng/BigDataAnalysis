 '''Folkspraak''' (also '''Folksprák''' and '''Folksprak'''; from ''folk'' 'people' and ''spraak'' 'language', which means  " the language of the people " ) is an unfinished < ref name= " Language Maker " > [http://www.langmaker.com/folksprk.htm Folkspraak] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20090724163034/http://www.langmaker.com/folksprk.htm |date=2009-07-24 }} at [[Langmaker]] < /ref >  zonal [[constructed language]]. It uses words from [[Germanic languages]] to make it easy for anyone who speaks those languages to understand it. < ref name= " Language Maker "  / >  It works a lot like a [[lingua franca]] for people who speak Germanic languages. < ref > [http://www.omniglot.com/writing/folkspraak.htm Folkspraak] at omniglot.com < /ref >
