 '''Yavoriv''' ({{lang-uk|Яворів}}, {{lang-pl|Jaworów}}, {{lang-yi|יאַוואָראָוו |translit=Yavorov}}, {{lang-de|Jaworiw}}) is a city in [[Lviv Oblast]] ([[oblast|province]]) of western [[Ukraine]]. It is the administrative center of [[Yavoriv Raion]] ([[raion|district]]). In 2021, 12,888 people lived there.
