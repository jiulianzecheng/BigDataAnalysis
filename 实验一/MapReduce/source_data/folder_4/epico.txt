 '''Orlando  " Ollie "  Colón''' (born March 24, 1982 in [[San Juan, Puerto Rico|San Juan]]) is a [[Puerto Rican]] [[professional wrestling|professional wrestler]]. He is best known for working for the [[WWE]] under the [[ring name]]s '''Fernando''' and '''Epico'''. He was one half of the [[tag team]] [[Primo  &  Epico|Los Matadores]] with his real-life cousin [[Eddie Colón|Primo]]. 
 
 ==Career== 
 On the November 4 2011 episode of ''[[WWE SmackDown|SmackDown]]'', Colón debuted on the main roster as a [[Heel (professional wrestling)|heel]] under the ring name '''Epico'''. He faced [[Místico|Sin Cara]] but lost by disqualification after [[Hunico]] ran in and attacked Sin Cara. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.wwe.com/shows/smackdown/ecks-factor-11-04-11|accessdate=2014-02-16|title=Eck's Factor: SmackDown 11/4 analysis|publisher=WWE}} < /ref > 
 
 Epico then began teaming with [[Eddie Colón|Primo]], with [[Rosa Mendes]] as their manager. Epico and Primo defeated Air Boom ([[Evan Bourne]] and [[Kofi Kingston]]) at a ''Raw'' [[Glossary of professional wrestling terms#H|live event]] on January 15, 2012, in [[Oakland, California]] to win the [[WWE Tag Team Championship]]. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.wwe.com/shows/raw/2012-01-09/primo-epico-new-wwe-tag-champions|accessdate=2014-02-16|title=Primo  &  Epico crowned WWE Tag Team Champions at WWE Live Event!|publisher=WWE}} < /ref >  They lost the championships to Kofi Kingston and [[Ron Killings|R-Truth]] on the April 30 episode of ''Raw''. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.wwe.com/shows/raw/2012-04-30/wwe-raw-supershow-results/page-7|accessdate=2014-02-16|title=Raw SuperShow results: Brock Lesnar, General Manager John Laurinaitis fly off the handle|publisher=WWE}} < /ref > 
 
 On the September 30 edition of ''Raw'', Epico and Primo changed their [[List of professional wrestling terms#G|gimmicks]] and debuted as '''Los Matadores''', as a [[Face (professional wrestling)|face]] team of two masked Spanish [[Torero|bullfighters]] with [[Mascarita Dorada|El Torito]] as their mascot. They defeated 3MB in their debut. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.wwe.com/shows/raw/2013-09-30/wwe-raw-results-26152294/page-4|accessdate=2014-02-16|title=Raw results: Triple H baits the Rhodes family, Bryan and Orton get personal, and Los Matadores arrive in style|publisher=WWE}} < /ref >
