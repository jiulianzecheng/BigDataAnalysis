 '''Ersigen''' is a [[municipality]] in the [[administrative district]] of [[Emmental (administrative district)|Emmental]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Bern (canton)|Bern]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 On 1 January 2016, the former municipalities of [[Oberösch]] and [[Niederösch]] merged into the municipality of Ersigen.
