 '''Chittagong''' (officially called '''Chattogram''' or '''Chattagram''') is a [[city]] in southeast [[Bangladesh]] near the Karnaphuli River. The city is the capital of the [[Chittagong District|Chittagong district]] and [[Chittagong Division|division]]. The city has around 5.5 million people.
