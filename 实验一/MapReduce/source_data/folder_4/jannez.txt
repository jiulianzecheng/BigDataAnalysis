 '''Jannez''' is a [[dansband]] from [[Laxsjö]], [[Sweden]], < ref > [http://www.svenskadansband.se/img3464.search.htm Svenska dansband - ''Jannez''] < /ref >  established in [[1976]] as ''Stars orkester''. 
 
 In September [[1996]], the band scored a [[Svensktoppen]] hit for two weeks with the song ''De blindas café''. < ref > [http://www.sr.se/Diverse/AppData/Isidor/files/2023/3492.txt Svensktoppen - ''1996''] < /ref > 
 
 Jannez participated at [[Dansbandskampen 2008]], performing [[Paul Paljett]]'s [[1977]] song ''[[Guenerina]]''.
