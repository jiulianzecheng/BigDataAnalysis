 '''Mehamn''' is a small town north in [[Norway]]. In 2020, the population in Mehamn was 761 people. < ref > {{Cite web|title=04859: Areal og befolkning i tettsteder (T) 2000 - 2020|url=http://www.ssb.no/statbankstatbank/table/04859/|access-date=2020-11-04|website=PX-Web SSB|language=no}}{{Dead link|date=September 2021 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }} < /ref >  Mehamn is the administration center in the municipality of [[Gamvik]].  
 
 The town has both an airport and a harbor where [[Hurtigruten]] docks.

 == Geography == 
 Mehamn is located north in Norway. It is part of the municipality that lies the furthest north on the mainland of Norway. The municipality borders the municipality of [[Berlevåg]] and [[Tana]] in the southeast, the municipality of [[Lebesby]] in the west, the [[Tanafjord]] in the east and the [[Barents Sea]] in the north. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Gamvik kommune|url=https://www.gamvik.kommune.no/om-kommunen/|access-date=2020-11-04|website=www.gamvik.kommune.no}} < /ref >
