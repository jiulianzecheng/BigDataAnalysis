 '''Holywood''' ({{IPAc-en|ˈ|h|ɒ|l|i|w|ʊ|d}} {{respell|HOL|ee-wuud}}) is a town in the metropolitan area of [[Belfast]] in [[County Down]], [[Northern Ireland]]. It is a [[Civil parishes in Ireland|civil parish]] and [[townland]] of 755 acres lying on the shore of [[Belfast Lough]], between Belfast and [[Bangor, County Down|Bangor]]. [[Holywood Exchange]] and [[Belfast City Airport]] are nearby. The town hosts an annual [[jazz]] and [[blues]] festival.
