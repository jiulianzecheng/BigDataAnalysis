 A '''bun''' is a type of [[food]] made out of [[bread]]. 
 
 A bun can be either [[:wikt:savoury|savoury]] (like a [[hot dog]]) or [[Sweet (taste)|sweet]] (like a [[hot cross bun]] or a bun with [[icing]] and a [[cherry]] on top).
