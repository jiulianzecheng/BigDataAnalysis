 '''''Cocomelon''''' is an American children's educational program. It is aimed at parents and teachers of toddlers who speak [[English language|English]] or are learning [[English as a second language]], and parents of children with a 
 [[learning disability]], [[autism]] or [[delayed speech]]. 
 The Cocomelon [[YouTube channel]] and website provide [[interactive media]] to help teach children. Their YouTube channel has over 2 billion views, and over 2 million subscribers. < ref > {{Cite news|url=http://www.stuff.co.nz/business/small-business/10182275/YouTube-success-with-kids-videos|title=YouTube success with kids' videos|last=Anthony|first=John|date=2014-06-22|newspaper=Stuff.co.nz|language=English|access-date=2016-11-01}} < /ref > 
 
 ==History== 
 In 2006, Jay Jeon created Cocomelon as a company that produces children's educational videos on DVDs. 
 In March 2007, he launched a YouTube channel called ''ABCKidTV'' that gives the subscribers, all of Cocomelon’ existing videos ad-free and in high definition. < ref > {{cite web|title=Busy Beavers children's YouTube channel ready to take on world|url=http://www.stuff.co.nz/business/small-business/87551713/Busy-Beavers-childrens-YouTube-channel-ready-to-take-on-world|website=Stuff|accessdate=29 May 2017}} < /ref > 
 
 In 2015, Cocomelon joined the [[Channel Frederator Network]], and is the number one channel (by views) of the [[Multi-channel network|Multi-Channel Network]]. < ref > {{Cite web|url=http://socialblade.com/youtube/network/channel_frederator_network/topusers/mostviewed|title=Top 100 Networked YouTubers in Channel_frederator_network Sorted by Most Viewed - Socialblade.com|website=socialblade.com|access-date=2016-11-01}} < /ref > 
 
 In April 2016, Cocomelon and ABCKidTV have both been added to [[Roku]], < ref > {{Cite news|url=http://suzguru.com/wordpress/index.php/2016/04/12/new-roku-channels-april-8-2016/|title=New Roku Channels – April 8, 2016|date=2016-04-12|newspaper=suzguru.com|language=en-US|access-date=2016-11-01}}{{Dead link|date=October 2019 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }} < /ref >  and Kidoodle.TV in September 2016. < ref > {{Cite news|url=https://worldscreen.com/tvkids/kidoodle-tv-adds-four-new-titles-to-catalog/|title=Kidoodle.TV Adds Four New Titles to Catalog - TVKIDS|date=2016-09-05|newspaper=TVKIDS|language=en-US|access-date=2016-11-01}} < /ref > 
 
 They have also created an app for iOS called ''Cocomelon Jukebox''.
