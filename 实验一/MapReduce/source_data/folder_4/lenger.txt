'''Lenger''' ({{lang-kk|Леңгір}}, {{lang-ru|Ленгер}}) is a [[city]] in the [[Turkistan Region]] of [[Kazakhstan]]. There are 33,841 people living there (as of 2023). < ref name=pop2023 > {{Cite web|date=2023-01-01|title=Population of the Republic of Kazakhstan by gender and type of area (as of 2023-01-01)|url=https://stat.gov.kz/api/getFile/?docId=ESTAT478686|url-status=dead|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20230305101742/https://stat.gov.kz/api/getFile/?docId=ESTAT478686|archive-date=2023-03-05|website=QazStat}} < /ref > 
 
 Lenger used to be the village of ''Lenger-sai''. In 1945 it received city status.
