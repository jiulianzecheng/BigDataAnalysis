 '''Elbasan''' is a [[city]] in central [[Albania]]. It is on the [[Shkumbin River]] in the [[District of Elbasan]] and the [[County of Elbasan]].  It is one of the largest cities in Albania.  There are about 79,810 people. < ref name= " Albanian 2011 census "  / >
