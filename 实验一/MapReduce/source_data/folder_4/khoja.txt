'''Khuwaja''' or '''Khoja''' are [[Shia Islam|Shia]] (Nizari Isma'ili) muslim community of [[Sindh]], [[Pakistan]] and [[Gujarat]], [[India]]. They were of [[Baniya caste|Baniya]] caste groups (traders), who converted to [[Islam]] from Hinduism under Muslim pirs (saints). < ref > {{Cite book|title=India: an anthropological perspective|url=https://archive.org/details/india00step|last=Tyler|first=Stephen A.|date=1986|publisher=Waveland Pr|isbn=978-0-88133-245-2|edition=Reissued|location=Prospect Heights, Ill}} < /ref >
