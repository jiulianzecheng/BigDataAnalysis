 '''Waltalingen''' was a [[municipality]] of the [[district]] of [[Andelfingen (district)|Andelfingen]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Zurich (canton)|Zurich]] in [[Switzerland]]. On 1 January 2019, the former municipalities of [[Oberstammheim]], [[Unterstammheim]] and Waltalingen merged into the new municipality of [[Stammheim, Zurich|Stammheim]].
