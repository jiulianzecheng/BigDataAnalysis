 '''Kannanalloor''' is a village in [[Mukhathala]] block panchayat in [[Kollam district]], [[Kerala]] state, [[India]]. 
 
 == Politics == 
 Kannanalloor is a part of [[Kundara]] assembly constituency in [[Kollam (Lok Sabha constituency)]]. Shri. [[J. Mercykutty Amma]] is the current MLA of Kundara. Shri.[[N. K. Premachandran]] is the current [[member of parliament]] of Kollam. [[Communist Party of India (Marxist)|CPM]], [[Indian National Congress|INC]], [[Revolutionary Socialist Party (India)|RSP]], etc are the major political parties. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://archive.eci.gov.in/se2001/background/S11/KL_Dist_PC_AC.pdf |title=Assembly Constituencies - Corresponding Districts and Parliamentary Constituencies |access-date=2008-10-20 |work=Kerala |publisher=Election Commission of India |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20090304011026/http://archive.eci.gov.in/se2001/background/S11/KL_Dist_PC_AC.pdf |archive-date=March 4, 2009 }} < /ref > 
 
 == Geography == 
 Kannanalloor is a small village in Thazhuthala panchayat. It is 13 & nbsp;km from [[Kollam]], 2 & nbsp;km from Mukhathala and 62 & nbsp;km from [[Thiruvananthapuram]]. Kannanalloor is a border of Thiruvananthapuram-Kollam districts. It connects places Mylakad, Nedumpana, Kottiyam, Palathara, Adichanalloor, etc. 
 
 == Demographics == 
 [[Malayalam]] is the local language of Kannanalloor.
