A '''manifesto''' is a statement of what someone believes in or intends to do.  Most manifestos belong to a group or an organisation and there is a process of agreeing what should be in them. 
 
 [[Political party|Political parties]] often make manifestos saying what they want to do if they win power in an election.  Writers and artists also make them. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Manifesto {{!}} Definition, Examples,  &  Facts {{!}} Britannica|url=https://www.britannica.com/topic/manifesto|access-date=2023-04-26|website=www.britannica.com|language=en}} < /ref > 
 
 Usually a manifesto will talk about what people want to change.  ''The [[The Communist Manifesto|Communist Manifesto]]'' is probably the most famous one.
