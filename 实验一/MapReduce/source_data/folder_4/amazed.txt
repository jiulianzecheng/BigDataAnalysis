 " '''Amazed''' "  is a 1999 song by American country band by [[Lonestar]] and is the second single from their third studio album ''[[Lonely Grill]]''. It went to number 1 in the United States, number 21 in the  United Kingdom, number 2 in Ireland, number 19 in Australia, number 17 in Scotland, number 11 in New Zealand, number 7 in Canada and number 25 in Iceland. It has been covered by [[Bonnie Tyler]], [[Duncan James]], [[Fady Maalouf]] and [[Boyz II Men]].
