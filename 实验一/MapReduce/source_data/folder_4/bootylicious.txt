'''''Bootylicious''''' is a song sung by [[R & B]] [[girl group]] [[Destiny's Child]]. It is on their third album ''Survivor'' [[2001]] and was the second [[single (music)|single]] from the [[album]]. It was their fourth [[United States|U.S.]] number-one single and reached the top-five in [[Australia]], [[Canada]] and the [[United Kingdom]]. The music video was directed by Matthew Rolston. 
 
 The word  " bootylicious "  is a combination of  " booty "  ([[buttocks]]) and  " [[wikt:delicious|delicious]] " .
