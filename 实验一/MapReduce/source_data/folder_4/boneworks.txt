 '''''Boneworks''''' (written as BONEWORKS) is a 2019 [[first-person shooter]] [[Virtual reality|VR]] [[video game|game]]. It was made by [[Stress Level Zero]]. < ref name= " :1 "  / >  They also [[Publishing|published]] the game. The game was mad to be entirely [[Game physics|physics-based]]. The player controls a virtual body that does what the player tells it to do and reacts to things it moves into in the game world. 
 
 In ''Boneworks,'' players take on the role of Arthur Ford. He is a [[Cyber security|cybersecurity]] director who escapes into an unfinished simulated [[Fictional universe|universe]]. He must fight through [[Surrealism|surreal]] [[architecture]] and [[Mystery fiction|mysterious]] scenes using [[experiment|experimental]] [[physics]]-based [[weapon|weaponry]].
