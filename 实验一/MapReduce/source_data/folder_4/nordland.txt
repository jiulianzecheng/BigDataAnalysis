 '''Nordland''' is a [[county]] in [[Norway]]. 
 
 It has borders with county [[Finnmark]], county [[Nord-Trøndelag]], and [[Sweden]]. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 The North [[Atlantic Ocean]] touches the county's coast.
