 '''Amary Lorenzo''',  better known as '''Scrufizzer''', is an [[English people|English]] singer-songwriter, record producer, and composer. Lorenzo first became famous as a [[Grime (music)|grime]] producer < ref > {{Cite web|last=Ldn|first=Shan|date=2017|title=Scrufizzer  &  Stimpy on playing festivals and tropical grime|url=https://guap.co.uk/scrufizzer-scrufizzer-stimpy-playing-festivals-tropical-grime/|url-status=live|website=GUAP|access-date=23 February 2021|archive-date=12 November 2020|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20201112005258/https://guap.co.uk/scrufizzer-scrufizzer-stimpy-playing-festivals-tropical-grime/}} < /ref >  before building a reputation for his [[dancehall|bashment]]-influenced flow and versatility. < ref > {{Cite web|date=2017-11-22|title=10 of the Best Scrufizzer Tracks|url=https://versus.uk.com/2017/11/10-best-scrufizzer-tracks/|access-date=2021-02-23|website=VERSUS|language=en-GB|archive-date=26 March 2021|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210326060658/https://versus.uk.com/2017/11/10-best-scrufizzer-tracks/|url-status=live}} < /ref >  He has collaborated with [[Tiesto]], [[Wiley (musician)|Wiley]], [[Dizzee Rascal]], and [[Cooli Carlito]].
