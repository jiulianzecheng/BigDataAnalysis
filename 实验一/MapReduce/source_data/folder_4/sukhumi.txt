 '''Sukhumi''' or '''Sokhumi''' ({{lang-ka|სოხუმი}}) is the capital and largest city of the breakaway independent state of [[Abkhazia]]. 
 
 Sokhumi was a multi-ethnic city. The people were [[Georgians]], [[Abkhazians]] (Apsuans), [[Russians]], [[Turkish people|Turks,]] [[Armenians]] and [[Greece|Greeks]] during Soviet Rule. During the [[Georgian-Abkhaz conflict]] Georgians in Abkhazia were forced to leave. Most Greeks have also left.
