 The '''Republic of Adygea''' ({{IPAc-en|ˌ|ɑː|d|ɪ|ˈ|ɡ|eɪ|ə}}; < ref > {{Cite web|title=Adygeya at merriam-webster.com|url=http://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/Adygeya|access-date=2009-09-23|website=m-w.com}} < /ref >  {{Lang-rus|Респу́блика Адыге́я}}; {{Lang-ady|Адыгэ Республик}}, ''Adygæ Respublik''), also called the '''Adyghe Republic''', is a [[Federal subjects of Russia|federal subject]] of [[Russia]] in the [[North Caucasus]] region of [[Eastern Europe]]. It is the fifth-smallest federal subject in Russia. Adygea is one of [[Russian republics|Russia's republics]] and home to the [[Circassian people]]. The official languages of Adygea are [[Russian language|Russian]] and [[Adyghe language|Adyghe]]. The capital and largest city of Adygea is [[Maykop]], which houses one-third of the republic's population.
