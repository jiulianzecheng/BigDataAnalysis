 '''Finningen''' ({{Lang-gsw|Fẽnnega|label=[[Swabian German]]}}) is a municipality in the district of [[Dillingen (district)|Dillingen]] in [[Bavaria]] in [[Germany]]. It is 7 & nbsp;km north of [[Dillingen an der Donau|the town of Dillingen]]. The population is 1206 (as of 2017). The town is a member of the [[Municipal association (Germany)|municipal association]] [[Höchstädt an der Donau]].
