 '''Overdinkel''' ([[Low Saxon]]: ''Oaverdeenkel'') is a village in the municipality of [[Losser]], the [[Netherlands]]. 
 
 It lies in [[Twente]], a region in the Province of [[Overijssel]].  
 
 The village borders [[Germany]]. 
 
 As of 2021, about 4,100 people were living in Overdinkel.
