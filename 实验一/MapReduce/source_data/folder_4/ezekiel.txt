'''Ezekiel''' (in [[Hebrew language|Hebrew]]: יְחֶזְקֵאל) was a [[prophet]] in the [[Old Testament]] of the [[Bible]] (also known as the [[Hebrew Bible]]). He is known for having a vision of [[God]] along with 4 creatures (supposedly [[angels]]).
