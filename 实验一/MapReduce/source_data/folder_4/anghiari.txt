 '''Anghiari''' is a ''[[comune]]'' in the [[Province of Arezzo]] in the [[Italy|Italian]] region of [[Tuscany]]. It has about 5,400 people. 
 
 The [[Battle of Anghiari]] took place on 29 June 1440 between the [[Republic of Florence]] and the [[Duchy of Milan]].
