 '''Mespelbrunn''' is a community in the [[Aschaffenburg (district)|Aschaffenburg district]] in the ''[[Regierungsbezirk]]'' of [[Lower Franconia]] (''Unterfranken'') in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]] and a member of the ''[[Municipal association (Germany)|Verwaltungsgemeinschaft]]'' (municipal association) of Mespelbrunn, whose seat is in [[Heimbuchenthal]].
