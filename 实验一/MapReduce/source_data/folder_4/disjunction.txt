'''Disjunction''' can refer to several things. 
 
 In [[logic]], it can mean: 
 *[[Inclusive disjunction]] (also known as ''logical or''): at least one of the [[argument]]s is true 
 :*[[OR gate]], the logic gate 
 *[[Exclusive disjunction]] (also known as ''XOR''): exactly one of the arguments is true 
 
 In biology, it can mean: 
 *[[Meiosis]], a type of cell division 
 *[[Disjunct distribution]], when populations of the same taxon are widely separated between two or more areas
