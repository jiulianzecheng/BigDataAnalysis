 '''Bollodingen''' was a [[municipality]] in the [[administrative district]] of [[Oberaargau (administrative district)|Oberaargau]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Bern (canton)|Berne]] in [[Switzerland]]. On 1 January 2011 it was merged with the municipality of [[Bettenhausen, Switzerland|Bettenhausen]].
