 '''Marktsteft''' ({{IPA-de|maʁktˈʃtɛft|lang|De-Marktsteft.ogg}}) is a town in [[Kitzingen (district)|Kitzingen]], in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. It is on the left bank of the [[Main (river)|Main]], {{Convert|5|km|mi|0|abbr=on}} southwest of [[Kitzingen]]. 
 
 It was the birthplace (1885) of the well-known [[Second World War]] general [[Albert Kesselring]].
