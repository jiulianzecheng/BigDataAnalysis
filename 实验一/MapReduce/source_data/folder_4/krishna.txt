 '''Krishna''' is an important God (Param Brahma) in [[Hinduism]]. He is considered to have been an [[avatar]] of the [[Vishnu]]. Krishna is believed to have been a real being who took part in the war known as the [[Mahabharata]]. He is a central character in the important Hindu scripture the [[Bhagavad Gita]]. These Sāttvatas or more properly the Yadavas of whom they were a branch were mentioned by Panini.}} < /ref >  Hindu often consider him a warrior, hero, teacher and philosopher and a great leader. He defeated the cruel [[Abhira]] [[Abhira|King Kansa]], < ref > {{Cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=kLxHAAAAMAAJ & q=%E0%A4%86%E0%A4%AD%E0%A5%80%E0%A4%B0+%E0%A4%95%E0%A4%82%E0%A4%B8|title=Sūra ke Kr̥shṇa: eka anuśīlana|last=Tivārī|first=Śaśi|date=1969|publisher=Milinda Prakāśana|language=hi}} < /ref >  who putted his mother, ''Devāki'' and her consort ''Vasudevā'' to jail.
