 '''Wathlingen''' is a municipality in [[Celle (district)|Celle]], in [[Lower Saxony]], [[Germany]]. It is about 10 & nbsp;km southeast of [[Celle]]. 
 
 Wathlingen is also the seat of the [[Samtgemeinde|collective municipality]] [[Wathlingen (Samtgemeinde)|Wathlingen]].
