A '''yottabyte''' ('''YB''') is the biggest [[unit of measurement]] for [[computer]]s of the near future. One yottabyte holds 1000 [[zettabyte]]s ('''ZB''') or a septillion (1,000,000,000,000,000,000,000,000) [[byte]]s. 1000 yottabytes make up 1 [[ronnabyte]].
