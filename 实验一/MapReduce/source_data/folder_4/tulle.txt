 '''Tulle''' ({{lang-oc|Tula}}) is a [[France|French]] [[Communes of France|commune]], the ''[[Prefectures in France|prefecture]]'' of the [[Corrèze]] [[Departments of France|department]] in central [[France]]. It is in the [[Nouvelle-Aquitaine]] [[Regions of France|region]]. It is also the [[Capital city|capital]] of the ''arrondissement'' of [[Arrondissement of Tulle|Tulle]]. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 Tulle is in the [[valley]] of the [[Corrèze (river)|Corrèze]] [[river]] at the [[confluence]] of this river with several [[Tributary|tributaries]]: the Solane and the Céronne on the right side, and Saint-Bonnette and Montane on the left side. The city is in narrow part of the valley along several [[Kilometre]]s. 
 
 The ''commune'' of Tulle has an area of {{Convert|24.4|km2|sqmi|abbr=on }}, < ref name= " insee-geo " > {{cite web |url=https://www.insee.fr/fr/statistiques/1405599?geo=COM-19272 |title=Commune de Tulle (19272) - Comparateur de territoire |work=Publications et statistiques pour la France ou les régions |publisher=Institut national de la statistique et des études économiques - INSEE |format= |language=French |accessdate=26 May 2017 |date= }} < /ref >  and its average [[altitude]] is {{Convert|323|m|ft|abbr=on}}; at the [[City and town halls|city hall]], the altitude is {{Convert|620|m|ft|abbr=on }}. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://www.map-france.com/Tulle-19000/ |title=City of Tulle |publisher=Map-France.com |language= |accessdate=26 May 2017 |date= }} < /ref >

 == Administration == 
 Tulle is the [[Prefectures in France|prefecture]] of the Corrèze department, the capital of the ''arrondissement'' of [[Arrondissement of Tulle|Tulle]] and the [[administrative centre]] ({{lang-fr|chef-lieu}}) of the canton of [[Canton of Tulle|Tulle]]. 
 
 It is part of the [[Communes of France|intercommunality]] ''Tulle Agglo'' ({{lang-fr|Communauté d'agglomération Tulle Agglo}}).
