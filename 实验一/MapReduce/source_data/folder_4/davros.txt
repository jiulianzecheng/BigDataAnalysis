 '''Davros''' is a [[fictional character]] from the [[Doctor Who|''Doctor Who'']] series. He is the creator of the infamous [[Dalek|Daleks]] and one of the [[Doctor (Doctor Who)|Doctor]]'s main enemies. 
 
 In the original series, he was portrayed by Michael Wisher, David Gooderson and Terry Molloy. In the revival series, Davros is portrayed by Julian Bleach.
