 '''''Crossed''''' is a 2011 [[dystopia]]n novel by [[Ally Condie]]. The book is the second in a [[trilogy]]. The first book in the trilogy is ''[[Matched]]''. The third book is ''[[Reached]]''. ''Crossed'' continues the story of Cassia Reyes. This book is in a dual part narration between Cassia and her boyfriend, Ky Markham.
