 '''Winkelhaid''' is a [[Municipalities of Germany|municipality]] in [[Nürnberger Land]] in [[Bavaria]] in [[Germany]]. It is 18 & nbsp;km southeast of [[Nuremberg]] in the [[Middle Franconia]]. Winkelhaid is bounded by (from the north and clockwise) [[Altdorf bei Nürnberg]], [[Schwarzenbruck]] and [[Feucht]].
