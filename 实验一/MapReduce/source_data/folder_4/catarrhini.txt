 '''Catarrhini''' is one of the two subdivisions of the higher [[primate]]s (the other is the [[New World monkey]]s). Catarrhines include the [[Old World monkey]]s and the [[ape]]s. Apes are further divided into the lesser apes or [[gibbon]]s and the [[Hominidae|great apes]], consisting of the [[orangutan]]s, [[gorilla]]s, [[bonobo]]s, [[chimpanzee]]s and [[human]]s. They are all native to [[Africa]] and [[Asia]].
