 '''Ballum''' is a village on the western half of the island of [[Ameland]] and the smallest of the total of four villages on the island. It is on an island in the north of [[Netherlands|the Netherlands]]. 
 
 It also holds Ameland airport in the northwest of Ballum. 
 
 The village has a population of 350. 
 
 == People from Ballum == 
 * [[Eelke Bakker]], oldest living man in the [[Benelux]]. Died 2020, aged 109.
