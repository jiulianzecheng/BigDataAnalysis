 '''Tannadice''' is a [[Scotland|Scottish]] [[association football|football]] stadium. It is owned by [[Dundee United F.C.]] who play their home games at the stadium. Tannadice Stadium is on the same street as their [[Dundee]] rivals stadium, [[Dundee F.C.]]
