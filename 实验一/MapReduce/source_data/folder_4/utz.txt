 '''''Utz''''' is a 1992 German Italian British drama movie directed by [[George Sluizer]] and based on the 1988 novel of the same name by [[Bruce Chatwin]]. It stars [[Armin Mueller-Stahl]], [[Brenda Fricker]], [[Peter Riegert]], [[Paul Scofield]] and was distributed by [[First Run Pictures]].
