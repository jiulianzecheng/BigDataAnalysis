'''Threlkeld''' is a village and [[civil parish]] in [[Eden, Cumbria|Eden]], [[Cumbria]], England. In 2001 there were 454 people living in Threlkeld. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.ons.gov.uk/help/localstatistics|title=Local statistics - Office for National Statistics|website=www.ons.gov.uk}} < /ref >  It has a church called St Mary's Church. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.visitcumbria.com/cm/threlkeld-st-marys-church/|title=Threlkeld St Mary's Church|publisher=}} < /ref >
