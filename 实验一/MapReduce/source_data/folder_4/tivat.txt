 '''Tivat''' ({{lang-sr-Cyrl|Тиват}}) is a coastal town in southwestern [[Montenegro]], on the [[Bay of Kotor]]. In 2011, 9,367 people lived there. 
 
 It is the smallest municipality by area in Montenegro.
