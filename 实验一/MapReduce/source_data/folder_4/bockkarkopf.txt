 The '''Bockkarkopf''' is a [[mountain]] in the [[Allgäu Alps]] on the main ridge. < ref > {{Cite web|url=http://peakware.com/peaks.php?pk=3539|title=Peakware}}{{Dead link|date=January 2021 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }} < /ref >  It is {{convert|2609|m}} high. It is the ninth highest peak in the Allgäu Alps. 
 
 Bockkarkopf forms a border between [[Bavaria]]n border with [[Tyrol (state)|Tyrol]]. It is found between the [[Wilder Mann (Allgäu Alps)|Wilder Mann]] mountain to the southwest and the [[Hochfrottspitze]] mountain to the northeast. The popular [[Heilbronn Way]] (''Heilbronner Weg'') runs over the [[Pyramid (geometry)|triangular]] peak.
