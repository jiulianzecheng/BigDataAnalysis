 '''Bupropion''', also sold as '''Wellbutrin''' or '''Zyban''', is an [[antidepressant]].  It is also used to help people quit [[smoking]]. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.webmd.com/smoking-cessation/bupropion-hydrochloride-zyban-for-quitting-smoking |title=Bupropion hydrochloride (Zyban) for quitting smoking |publisher=Webmd.com |date=2009-07-22 |access-date=2011-11-04}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite journal|last1=Patel|first1=Krisna|last2=Allen|first2=Sophie|last3=Haque|first3=Mariam N.|last4=Angelescu|first4=Ilinca|last5=Baumeister|first5=David|last6=Tracy|first6=Derek K.|date=April 6, 2016|title=Bupropion: a systematic review and meta-analysis of effectiveness as an antidepressant|journal=Therapeutic Advances in Psychopharmacology|volume=6|issue=2|pages=99–144|doi=10.1177/2045125316629071|issn=2045-1253|pmc=4837968|pmid=27141292}} < /ref >  Bupropion is different from other antidepressants because it does not often cause weight gain or sleepiness. < ref > {{Cite journal|last1=Dhillon|first1=Sohita|last2=Yang|first2=Lily P. H.|last3=Curran|first3=Monique P.|date=2008|title=Bupropion: a review of its use in the management of major depressive disorder|url=https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/18370448/|journal=Drugs|volume=68|issue=5|pages=653–689|doi=10.2165/00003495-200868050-00011|issn=0012-6667|pmid=18370448}} < /ref >  However, it has a higher chance of causing [[Seizure|seizures]] than other antidepressants. 
 
 Common side effects of bupropion are having a dry mouth, [[nausea]], lack of sleep, a lot of sweating, [[anxiety]] and constipation (difficulty pooping). < ref > {{Cite web|date=2011-07-17|title=|url=http://us.gsk.com/products/assets/us_wellbutrinXL.pdf|access-date=2022-11-17|archive-date=2011-07-17|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110717003144/http://us.gsk.com/products/assets/us_wellbutrinXL.pdf|url-status=dead}} < /ref >  It can also increase [[blood pressure]]. < ref > {{Cite journal|last1=Wilens|first1=Timothy E.|last2=Hammerness|first2=Paul G.|last3=Biederman|first3=Joseph|last4=Kwon|first4=Anne|last5=Spencer|first5=Thomas J.|last6=Clark|first6=Sarah|last7=Scott|first7=Megan|last8=Podolski|first8=Amy|last9=Ditterline|first9=Jeffrey W.|last10=Morris|first10=Matthew C.|last11=Moore|first11=Hadley|date=February 2005|title=Blood pressure changes associated with medication treatment of adults with attention-deficit/hyperactivity disorder|url=https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/15705013/|journal=The Journal of Clinical Psychiatry|volume=66|issue=2|pages=253–259|doi=10.4088/jcp.v66n0215|issn=0160-6689|pmid=15705013}} < /ref >  Using bupropion while [[Pregnancy|pregnant]] can give the baby [[Congenital heart defect|congenital heart defects]]. 
 
 Bupropion was made by [[Nariman Mehta]] in 1969. It was first used in the United States in 1985. Bupropion was first called amfebutamone, but it was renamed in 2000. < ref > {{Cite journal|date=2000|title=International nonproprietary names for pharmaceutical substances (INN) : proposed international nonproprietary names : list 83 = Dénominations communes internationales des substances pharmaceutiques (DCI) : dénominations communes internationales proposées : liste 83 = Denominaciones comunes internacionales para las sustancias farmacéuticas (DCI) : denominaciones comunes internacionales propuestas : lista 83|url=https://apps.who.int/iris/handle/10665/58135|journal=WHO Drug Information 2000 ; 14(2) : 107-143| hdl=10665/58135 |language=en}} < /ref >  In 2020, it had more than 25 million [[Prescription|prescriptions]] in the United States. < ref > {{Cite web|title=The Top 300 of 2020|url=https://clincalc.com/DrugStats/Top300Drugs.aspx|access-date=2022-11-17|website=clincalc.com}} < /ref >
