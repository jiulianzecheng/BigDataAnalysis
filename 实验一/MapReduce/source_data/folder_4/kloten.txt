 '''Kloten''' ([[Swiss German]]: ''Chloote'') is a [[city]] in the [[district]] of [[Bülach (district)|Bülach]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Zurich (canton)|Zurich]] in [[Switzerland]]. It is the nearest town to [[Zurich Airport]]. It is known for its [[ice hockey]] club [[EHC Kloten]].
