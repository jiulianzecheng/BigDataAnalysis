 '''Peterhead''' is a town in [[Aberdeenshire]], [[Scotland]]. It is Aberdeenshire's largest settlement, having a population of 17,947 at the [[United Kingdom Census 2001|2001 Census]]. < ref > [http://www.scrol.gov.uk/scrol/browser/profile.jsp?profile=Population & mainLevel=Locality & mainText=Peterhead & mainTextExplicitMatch=false & compLevel=CountryProfile & compArea=Scotland & compText= & compTextExplicitMatch=null Comparative Population Profile:Peterhead Locality Scotland] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120307180313/http://www.scrol.gov.uk/scrol/browser/profile.jsp?profile=Population & mainLevel=Locality & mainText=Peterhead & mainTextExplicitMatch=false & compLevel=CountryProfile & compArea=Scotland & compText= & compTextExplicitMatch=null |date=2012-03-07 }} scrol.gov.uk, accessed 6 November 2008 < /ref >  Peterhead sits at the easternmost point in mainland Scotland. Peterhead is often referred to as 'The Blue Toon' and people who were born there as Blue Tooners. More correctly they are called Bloomogganners, supposedly from the blue worsted stockings that the fishermen originally wore. 
 
 ==Sport== 
 [[Peterhead F.C.]] are a [[Scottish Football League]] side who currently play in the [[Scottish Football League Second Division|Second Division]].
