 '''Lindsell''' is a village and [[civil parish]] in [[Uttlesford]], [[Essex]], [[England]]. In 2001 there were 220 people living in Lindsell. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.ons.gov.uk/help/localstatistics|title=Local statistics - Office for National Statistics|website=www.ons.gov.uk}} < /ref >  It has a church called St Mary the Virgin. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.essexchurches.info/church.aspx?p=Lindsell|title=St Mary the Virgin, Lindsell Church, Essex|website=www.essexchurches.info}} < /ref >  It includes the hamlets of [[Holder Green]] and [[Bustard Green]]. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.recordinguttlesfordhistory.org.uk/lindsell/Lindsell.html|title=Local History of Lindsell village in Essex|website=www.recordinguttlesfordhistory.org.uk}} < /ref >
