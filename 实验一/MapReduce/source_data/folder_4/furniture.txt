 '''Furniture''' is the word that means all the things like [[chair]]s, [[Table (furniture)|tables]], [[cupboard]]s, [[bed]]s and [[bookcase]]s, etc.  
 
 In other words, '''furniture''' are all the things that are in the house and that people can use to sit, to lie on or that are supposed to contain smaller things like [[cloth]]s or [[cup]]s. Furniture is made of wood, particle boards, leather, [[screw]]s etc. 
 
 Different rooms have different furniture for different purposes. Offices have office furniture such as desks and office chairs. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Different types of office furniture|url=https://rethinkyouroffice.co.uk/information/types-of-office-furniture.cfm|access-date=2021-07-08|website=}} < /ref >  Dining rooms have a table and dining chairs. Theaters and classrooms have rows of seats facing forward. 
 [[Image:Shaker bedroom.JPG|upright|thumb]] 
 Bedroom furniture includes a bed covered by sheets, a bedspread and maybe a blanket and pillow . Next to the bed is the nightstand or night table. A lamp may be on the nightstand. The dresser has drawers for clothes. On top of a dresser may be a mirror.
