 '''Cutworms''' are the [[caterpillar]] of several kinds of [[moth]]s. They cause problems for people trying to grow [[plant]]s in their garden. 
 
 Cutworms eat [[wikt:tender|tender]] [[shoot(plant)|shoot]]s at the base of the plant. They have big appetites and will destroy a plant in a very short time. They eat many kinds of plants. 
 
 The [[Egg (biology)|egg]]s of the cutworms are laid in  the [[autumn]] by the moth and the cutworms emerge in the [[spring]].  < ref > {{cite web | url=https://www.almanac.com/pest/cutworms | title=Cutworms: How to Protect Seedlings from Cutworms  & #124; the Old Farmer's Almanac }} < /ref >
