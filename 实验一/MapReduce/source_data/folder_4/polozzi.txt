 '''José Fernando Polozzi''' (born [[1 October]], [[1955]]) is a former [[Brazil]]ian [[Football (soccer)|football]] player. He was included in the [[Brazil national football team|Brazil national team]] for the [[1978 FIFA World Cup]], but he never played in an international match.
