 '''Hadash''', Hebrew: חד " ש , meaning ''New'', a Hebrew [[acronym]] of ''HaHazit HaDemokratit LeShalom uLeShivion'', Hebrew: החזית הדמוקרטית לשלום ולשוויון‎‎ , < br/  > 
 the Arab version: ''al-Jabhah ad-Dimuqrāṭiyyah lis-Salām wa'l-Musāwah'', Arab: الجبهة الديمقراطية للسلام والمساواة‎‎ , < br/  > 
 (shortened Arab: الجبهة , also meaning ''New''), < br/  > 
 both meaning ''The Democratic Front for Peace and Equality'', is an [[Israel]]i political party.   
 
 Its ideology is [[communist]].  
 
 Political leader is [[Ayman Odeh]]. 
 
 Hadash was founded in 1977 as a merger of the communist party of [[Rakah]], [[Black Panthers (Israel)|Black Panthers]] and others. 
 
 Its supporters are mainly (secular, Christian) [[Israeli Arabs]] and some far-left [[Israeli Jews]]. 
 
 Number of seats in the 24th [[Knesset]]: 3 (-2). < br/  > 
 Hadash is cooperating with [[Balad]] and [[Ta'al]] as [[HaReshima HaMeshutefet]] (Hebrew) or [[al-Qa'imah al-Mushtarakah]] (Arab) (both meaning ''The Joint List''). Together they have 6 seats.
