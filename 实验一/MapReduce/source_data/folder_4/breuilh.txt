 '''Breuilh''' is a former [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is in [[Nouvelle-Aquitaine]] in the [[Dordogne]] [[Departments of France|department]] in southwest [[France]]. On 1 January 2017, it was merged into the new commune of [[Sanilhac, Dordogne|Sanilhac]]. < ref > [https://www.legifrance.gouv.fr/eli/arrete/2016/9/26/INTB1627528A/jo/texte Arrêté préfectoral] 26 September 2016 {{in lang|fr}} < /ref >
