 '''Toonbox''' is a Russian animation studio founded in [[Russia]]. It is currently based in [[Cyprus]]. 
 
 == History == 
 The studio was founded in 2008, by [[Pavel Muntyan]] ([[chief executive officer|CEO]]) and Vladimir Ponomarev ([[chief creative officer|CCO]]).
