 '''''Antillesoma''''' is a genus of peanut worms [[Phylum]] [[Sipuncula]]. The genus belongs to the family [[Phascolosomatidae]] ''Antillesoma'' was described in [[1973]] by Stephen and Edmonds. < ref > World Register of Marine Especies (WoRMS): ''Antillesoma'' (Stephen  &  Edmonds, 1972) AphiaID: 136026 [http://www.marinespecies.org/aphia.php?p=taxdetails & id=136026] < /ref > < ref > Cutler, Edward B.  &  Gibbs, Peter E.
