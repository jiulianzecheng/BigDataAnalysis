 '''Lammi''' ({{lang-sv|Lampis}}) is a former [[municipality]] in [[Kanta-Häme]], [[Finland]]. In 2008, the municipality had a population of 5,507. Neighbouring municipalities were [[Asikkala]], [[Hauho]], [[Hausjärvi]], [[Hämeenkoski]], [[Janakkala]], [[Kärkölä]], [[Luopioinen]], [[Padasjoki]] and [[Tuulos]]. 
 
 It was merged with [[Hämeenlinna]] in 2009.
