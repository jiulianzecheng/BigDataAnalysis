 '''Enon''' is an [[United States|American]] [[indie rock]] band. They are named after [[Enon, Ohio|Enon]], a [[village]] in [[Ohio]]. Enon has three members, one man who sings and plays [[electric guitar]] and a woman who plays [[bass guitar]] and [[Keyboard instrument|keyboard]]. The third member plays [[drums]]. The woman was born in [[Japan]] and lives now in America. The guitarist played before in the band [[Brainiac]].
