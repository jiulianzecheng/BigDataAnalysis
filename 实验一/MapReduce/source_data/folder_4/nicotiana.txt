 '''''Nicotiana''''' is a [[genus]] of [[herbaceous plants]] and [[shrub]]s.  They grow in the Americas, Australia, south west Africa and the South Pacific. The common name for plant [[species]] of the genus ''Nicotiana'' is '''tobacco plants'''.    
 
 One of the species is ''[[Nicotiana tabacum|N. tabacum]]''.  It is grown worldwide to make tobacco leaf for [[cigarette]]s and other tobacco products. Other species are grown as ornamental garden plants.
