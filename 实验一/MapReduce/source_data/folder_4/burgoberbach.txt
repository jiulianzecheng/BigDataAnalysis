 '''Burgoberbach''' is a [[municipality]] in [[Ansbach (district)|Ansbach]], a district [[Districts of Germany|''(Landkreis)'']] in [[Middle Franconia]]. 
 
 In the graveyard of the Catholic church of St. Leonhard lies the grave of Flying Officer George Warren, administered by the [[Commonwealth War Graves Commission]]. < ref name= " CWGC-Warren " / >
