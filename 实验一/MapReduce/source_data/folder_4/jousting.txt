 '''Jousting''' was a sport for rich people in [[Europe]] during the [[Middle Ages]] and the [[Renaissance]].  It was fought by [[knight]]s on [[horse]]s using different weapons such as [[lance]]s, [[battle axe]]s, [[dagger]]s, and [[sword]]s.  The knights wore [[armor]] and fought in [[Tournament (medieval)|tournaments]].
