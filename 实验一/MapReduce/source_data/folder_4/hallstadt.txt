 '''Hallstadt''' is a town in the [[Upper Franconia Government Region|Upper Franconia]]n district of [[Bamberg (district)|Bamberg]]. It is on the left bank of the [[river]] [[Main]], {{convert|4|km|abbr=off}} north of [[Bamberg]]. The town has a [[population]] of about 8500 people.
