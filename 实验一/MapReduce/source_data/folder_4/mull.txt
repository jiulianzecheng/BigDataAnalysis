 '''Mull''' ({{lang-gd|Muile}}, {{IPA-gd|ˈmulʲə|pron}}) is the second largest island of the [[Inner Hebrides]] (after [[Skye]]). It lies off the west coast of [[Scotland]] in the [[Council areas of Scotland|council area]] of [[Argyll and Bute]]. 
 
 It has an area of {{convert|875.35|km2|sqmi}}. Mull is the fourth largest Scottish island and the fourth largest island surrounding [[Great Britain]] (excluding [[Ireland]]).  
 
 In the [[United Kingdom Census 2011|2011 census]] the usual population of Mull was 2,800. < ref name=NRS/ >   In the summer there are also many tourists. < ref >  Haswell-Smith, Hamish (2004). The Scottish Islands. Edinburgh: Canongate. ISBN 978-1-84195-454-7 < /ref >  Much of the population lives in [[Tobermory, Mull|Tobermory]], the only [[burgh]] on the island until 1973, and its capital.
