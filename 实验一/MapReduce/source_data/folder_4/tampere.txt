 '''Tampere''' ({{lang-sv|Tammerfors}}) is the third biggest city in [[Finland]]. It is the [[capital (city)|capital]] of the  [[Pirkanmaa]] area. 
 Neighbour municipalities are [[Kangasala]], [[Lempäälä]], [[Nokia, Finland|Nokia]], [[Pirkkala]], [[Vesilahti]], [[Hämeenkyrö]], [[Orivesi]], Pälkäne and Ylöjärvi.  
 
 The population of Tampere was 215,144 in 2011. < ref > {{Cite web |url=http://vrk.fi/default.aspx?docid=5864 & site=3 & id=0 |title=Archived copy |access-date=2012-02-25 |archive-date=2012-01-18 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120118122600/http://vrk.fi/default.aspx?docid=5864 & site=3 & id=0 |url-status=dead }} < /ref >    
 
 ==History== 
 Tampere was started in [[1779]]. At 1800's, Tampere became to be a notable industrialized city and one of the biggest cities in Finland.
