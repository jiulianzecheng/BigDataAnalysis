 '''Vinovo''' is a ''[[comune]]'' in the [[Metropolitan City of Turin]] in the [[Piedmont]] region in [[Italy]]. It is home to the [[Juventus Training Center (Vinovo)|training ground]] of football club [[Juventus F.C.|Juventus]].
