 '''Ladonia''' ({{lang-sv|Ladonien}}) is a [[micronation]], proclaimed in 1996 as the result of a years-long court battle between artist [[Lars Vilks]] and local authorities over two sculptures. < ref > https://shimajournal.org/issues/v13n1/10.-Bicudo-de-Castro-and-Kober-Shima-v13n1.pdf < /ref >  The claimed territory is part of the natural reserve of [[:en:Kullaberg|Kullaberg]] in southern [[Sweden]].
