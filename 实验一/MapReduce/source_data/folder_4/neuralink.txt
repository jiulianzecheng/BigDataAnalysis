 '''Neuralink Corporation''' is a company that likes to connect human brains with machines for helping people that 
 * [[Blindness|can not see]] well 
 * [[Paralysis|can not move arms or legs]]. 
 ==History== 
 In 2016 [[Elon Musk]] and 7 other people created Neuralink. 
 
 In 2021 a monkey played [[Pong]] using a Neuralinked brain. < ref >
