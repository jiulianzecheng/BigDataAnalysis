 The '''Saxifragales''' are a taxonomic order of [[flowering plants]], various [[dicotyledon]]s. Includes the following families: 
 
 * Family [[Altingiaceae]] ([[Altingia|sweet gum]] family) 
 * Family [[Aphanopetalium|Aphanopetalaceae]] 
 * Family [[Cercidiphyllum|Cercidiphyllaceae]] (katsuratree family) 
 * Family [[Crassulaceae]] ([[orpine]] family) 
 * Family [[Daphniphyllum|Daphniphyllaceae]] 
 * Family [[Grossulariaceae]] ([[gooseberry]] and [[blackcurrant]] family) 
 * Family [[Haloragidaceae]] 
 * Family [[Hamamelidaceae]] ([[witch-hazel]] family) 
 * Family [[Iteaceae]] (itea or sweetspire family) 
 * Family [[Peony|Paeoniaceae]] ([[peony]]) 
 * Family [[Penthorum|Penthoraceae]] 
 * Family [[Pterostemon]]aceae 
 * Family [[Saxifragaceae]] ([[saxifrage]] family)
