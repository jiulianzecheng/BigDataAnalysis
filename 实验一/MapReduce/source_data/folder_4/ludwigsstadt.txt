 '''Ludwigsstadt''' is a [[Town#Germany|town]] in [[Kronach (district)|Kronach]], in [[Upper Franconia]], [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 It is in the valley of the [[Loquitz]] River, a tributary of the [[Saale]], in the [[Thuringian-Franconian Highlands]] of the [[Thuringian Slate Mountains]] and the [[Franconian Forest]] mountain ranges. Being {{convert|28|km|mi}} north of [[Kronach]], the Bavarian border with the state of [[Thuringia]] runs about {{convert|3|km|mi|abbr=on}} north of the town centre, with Thuringian [[Saalfeld]] in a distance of c. {{convert|18|km|mi|abbr=on}} down the Loquitz. Ludwigsstadt is the only municipality of the State of Bavaria north of the [[Rennsteig]] ridge.
