'''Nezahualcoyotl''' ([[1402]] - [[1472]]) was [[king]] of [[Texcoco]], a part of the [[Aztecs|Aztec]] empire. He was also a famous [[poet]]. 
 
 The name Nezahualcoyotl is [[Nahuatl language|Nahuatl]], it means  " hungry [[coyote]] " .
