 '''Albisrieden''' is a [[neighborhood|quarter]] in [[district 9 (Zürich)|district 9]] of the city of [[Zürich]] in [[Switzerland]]. Until 1934 it was a municipality of its own. It was then incorporated into Zürich. 
 
 It has 17,226 people and its area is 4.6 & nbsp;km².
