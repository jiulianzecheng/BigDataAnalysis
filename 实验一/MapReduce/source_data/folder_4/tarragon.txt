 '''Tarragon''' (''Artemisia dracunculus''), also known as '''estragon''', is a [[perennial]] [[herb]] in the family [[Asteraceae]]. It is native to a wide area of the [[Northern Hemisphere]] from the easternmost parts of [[Europe]] to across central and eastern [[Asia]] and [[India]], western [[North America]], and south to northern [[Mexico]].
