 '''Moneymore''' is a village in [[Northern Ireland]], in the [[United Kingdom]]. It is {{convert|5|mi|km|0}} from [[Cookstown]] and {{convert|5|mi|km|0}} from [[Magherafelt]]. 
 
 ==Population facts== 
 On [[29 April]] [[2001]] the United Kingdom government had a [[United Kingdom Census 2001|census]] and saw that there were 1,369 people living in Moneymore. They also saw that: 
 *25.0% of the people were under 16 years old and 16.1% were over 60 years old. 
 *47.9% of the people were male and 52.1% were female. 
 *47.8% of the people were from a [[Roman Catholicism|Roman Catholic]] background and 51.0% were from a [[Protestantism|Protestant]] background; 
 *3.1% of people aged 16–74 had no job. < ref > [http://www.ninis.nisra.gov.uk/ NI Neighbourhood Information Service] < /ref >
