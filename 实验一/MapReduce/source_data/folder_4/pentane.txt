 '''Pentane''' is an [[organic compound]] with the [[chemical formula]] of {{chem|[[Carbon|C]]|5|[[Hydrogen|H]]|12}}. It is an [[alkane]] with five carbon [[atom]]s. Usually,  " pentane "  represents all three [[isomer]]s (''n''-pentane, [[isopentane]] and [[neopentane]]). But in the [[IUPAC]], '''pentane''' only represents ''n''-pentane. In the [[IUPAC]], the other two isomers is 2-[[methyl]][[butane]] and 2,2-[[methyl|dimethyl]][[propane]]. [[Cyclopentane]]'s [[chemical formula]] is {{chem|C|5|H|10}}, so it is '''not''' an isomer of pentane.

 All [[isomer]]s of pentane burn with oxygen to make [[carbon dioxide]] and [[water]]: 
 :{{chem|C|5|H|12}}+8{{chem|O|2}}- > 5{{chem|CO|2}}+6{{chem|H|2|O}}.
