 '''Azuga''' is a town in the [[mountain]]s of [[Prahova County]] in the [[Wallachian]] area of [[Romania]].  Azuga is found at the base of the [[Bucegi|Bucegi Mountains]]. About 5,200 people live in Azuga as of the year [[2002]].  The town is a very busy and active ski [[wikt:resort|resort]].
