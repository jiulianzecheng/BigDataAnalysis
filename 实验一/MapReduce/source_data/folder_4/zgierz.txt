 '''Zgierz''' is a town in [[Łódź Voivodeship]] in central [[Poland]]. In 2021, 54,974 people lived there. Its area is 42.33 & nbsp;km². It is on the river [[Bzura]]. 
 
 Zgierz is one of the oldest cities in central Poland. It was first mentioned in 1231. It bought its town rights before 1288.
