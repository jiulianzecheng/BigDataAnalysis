 '''Carinthia''' ({{lang-de|Kärnten}} {{IPA-de|ˈkɛʁntn̩||Kärnten.ogg}}; {{lang-sl|Koroška}} {{IPA-sl|kɔˈɾóːʃka||Koroška.ogg}}; {{Lang-it|Carinzia}} {{IPA-it|karintsja|}}; {{Lang-bar|Keantn|label=[[Southern Austro-Bavarian]]}}) is the southernmost state of [[Austria]]. It is in the Eastern Alps. The main language is [[German language|German]]. Carinthia's main [[industries]] are [[tourism]], [[electronics]], [[engineering]], [[forestry]] and [[agriculture]]. The companies [[Philips]] and [[Siemens]] have large operations there.  
 
 == Districts == 
 The state is divided into eight rural and two urban districts.  There are 132 towns.
