'''Pecola''' is a [[Canadians|Canadian]] and [[Japanese language|Japanese]] children animated education preschool [[Television series|television programme]] co-produced by Canadian studio [[Nelvana]], Japanese studios Milky Cartoon and Yoikmo and aired on [[Treehouse TV]] from 2001 to 2002, the show will also be shown on the upcoming [[Nick Jr.]] series ''[[Kawaii Club]]''.
