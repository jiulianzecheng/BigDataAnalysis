 " '''Funhouse''' "  is a [[song]] by [[Pop music|pop]] [[singer]], [[Pink (singer)|Pink]]. It is the fifth release from her 2008 [[album]], entitled the same as the name of the song, ''Funhouse''. It tells of Pink's split with a partner. It reached 20th position in the [[UK Singles Chart]]. There is also a [[techno]] [[remix]] of the song.
