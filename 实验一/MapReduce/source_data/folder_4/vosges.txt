 '''Vosges''' is a [[departments of France|department]] found in the east of [[France]] in the [[Grand Est]] [[region]]. Its [[prefecture]] is [[Épinal]]. Its population is about 382,000 (2006 estimation). It is one of the 83 first French departments made during the [[French revolution]].
