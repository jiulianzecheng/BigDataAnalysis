 '''Liesveld''' is a former [[List of municipalities of the Netherlands|municipality]] in the [[Netherlands|Dutch]] [[Provinces of the Netherlands|province]] of [[South Holland]]. 
 
 Since 2013, it had been part of the new municipality of [[Molenwaard]]. In 2019, it has been part of the new municipality of [[Molenlanden]].
