 '''Defecation''' is the [[science|scientific]] name for the final stage of [[digestion]], which is the act of taking away solid, semisolid or liquid [[waste]] from the body, known as  " Faeces (American spelling, ''[[Feces]]'') " . [[Feces|Faeces]] is the scientific name for '''poop'''.
