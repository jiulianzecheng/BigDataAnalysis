 '''Yellowcard''' are an American [[pop punk]] [[band]] from [[Jacksonville, Florida|Jacksonville]], [[Florida]].  
 
 The band was formed in 1997 after the members of the band met each other at the Douglas Anderson School of the Arts. They released their debut album, ''[[Midget Tossing]]'', on April 1, 1997. The band's best known song is  " [[Ocean Avenue (song)|Ocean Avenue]] "  which reached #37 on the [[Billboard Hot 100]].
