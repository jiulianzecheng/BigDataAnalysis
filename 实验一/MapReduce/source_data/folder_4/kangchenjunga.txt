 '''Kangchenjunga''' (Nepali:कञ्चनजङ्घा Kanchanjaŋghā) SewaLungma (Limbu language) is the third highest mountain in the [[world]] (after [[Mount Everest]] and [[K2]]). < ref > Kangchenjunga taken by author from Goecha La pass, 4940m, Sikkim < /ref > 
 
 Kangchenjunga has an [[altitude]] of 8,586 metres (28,169 & nbsp;ft). Kangchenjunga translated means  " The Five Treasures of Snows " , as it has five peaks, four of them over 8,450 metres. It is one of the mountains in the range of the [[Himalayas]].
