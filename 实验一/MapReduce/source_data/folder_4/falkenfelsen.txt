 The '''Falkenfelsen''' (Falcons Rock) is a cliff in the northern [[Black Forest]] in [[Baden-Württemberg]], [[Germany]]. The [[granite]] [[Rock (geology)|rock]] formation is about {{convert|200|ft|m}} high. From the [[Schwarzwaldhochstraße]] (Black Forest Highway), two [[hiking]] [[trail]]s lead through the formation and end at the [[shelter]] ''Herta-hut''. From the platform there is a view over the northern Black Forest with the [[Hornisgrinde]] in the south, Bühlerhöhe in the north east and the [[valley]] of the [[Rhine#Upper Rhine|Upper Rhine]] in the west side.
