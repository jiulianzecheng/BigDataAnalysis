 A '''jubilee''' is an [[anniversary]] of an event. It usually marks the 25th, 40th, 50th, 60th, or the 70th anniversary. In modern times, many [[Monarch|monarchs]] have jubilees to celebrate a number of years that they have been on the throne. For example, in [[2023]], [[Carl XVI Gustaf]] is celebrating his Golden Jubilee (50 years). 
 
 == Types of Jubilees == 
 
 * Silver jubilee, for a 25th anniversary. 
 * Ruby jubilee, for a 40th anniversary. 
 * Golden jubilee, for a 50th anniversary. 
 * Diamond jubilee, for a 60th or 75th anniversary. 
 * Sapphire jubilee, for a 65th anniversary. 
 * Platinum jubilee, for a 70th anniversary.
