 '''Nurmes''' is a city in [[North Karelia]], [[Finland]]. As of January 2014, almost 8,200 people lived there. Nearby municipalities are [[Juuka]], [[Kuhmo]], [[Lieksa]], [[Rautavaara]], [[Sotkamo]] and [[Valtimo]]. Since 2020, [[Valtimo]] was merged with the town of Nurmes.
