 '''Tschlin''' was a [[municipality]] of the [[district]] of [[Inn (district)|Inn]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Graubünden]] in far east [[Switzerland]]. On 1 January 2013, the municipalities of [[Ramosch]] and Tschlin joined to become the new municipality of [[Valsot]].
