 '''Nufenen''' ({{lang-it|Nufena}}, {{lang-rm|Nueinas}}) was a [[municipality]] of the [[district]] of [[Hinterrhein (district)|Hinterrhein]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Graubünden]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 It is on the [[San Bernardino (road tunnel)|San Bernardino tunnel]] road. 
 
 On 1 January 2019, the former municipalities of [[Hinterrhein, Switzerland|Hinterrhein]], Nufenen and [[Splügen]] merged to form the new municipality of [[Rheinwald, Switzerland|Rheinwald]].
