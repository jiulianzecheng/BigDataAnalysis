 '''Lenzerheide''' ({{lang-rm|Lai}}) is a [[ski resort]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Graubünden]], [[Switzerland]]. It is below [[Parpaner Rothorn]]. It is in the municipality of [[Vaz/Obervaz]]. 
 
 Lenzerheide is popular as skiing resort and for its lake ([[Heidsee]]) that is frozen in winter. 
 
 At the end of 2013 the Lenzerheide ski resort was linked with [[Arosa]] by cable-car, creating the new ski resort of [[Arosa Lenzerheide]].
