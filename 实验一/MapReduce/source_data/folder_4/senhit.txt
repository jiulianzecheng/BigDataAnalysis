 '''Senhit Zadik Zadik''' (born 1 October 1979), better known by her [[Mononymous person|first name]] '''Senhit''', is an [[Eritrea|Eritrean]]-[[Italy|Italian]] singer.  
 
 == Eurovision Song Contest == 
 She represented [[San Marino]] in the [[Eurovision Song Contest 2011|Eurovision 2011]] with the song  " Stand By " , but did not qualify for the final.
