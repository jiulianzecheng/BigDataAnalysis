 '''Rankweil''' is a market town in the district of [[Feldkirch (district)|Feldkirch]] in the [[Austria]]n state of [[Vorarlberg]]. It is the second largest town in the district of Feldkirch and the eighth largest town in Vorarlberg. 
 
 Beverage company [[Rauch (company)|Rauch]] is headquartered in Rankweil.
