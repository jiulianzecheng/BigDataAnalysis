 An '''agreement''' is writing down a [[promise]] made. Agreements are common in law and [[business]]. For example, when a person takes out a [[loan]] < ref > {{Cite web|title=Business loan|url=https://www.insolvencysupport.co.uk/cannot-pay-a-business-loan/|url-status=live|access-date=2021-05-11|website=Insolvency Practitioners Manchester|language=en-US|archive-date=2021-04-20|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210420035030/https://www.insolvencysupport.co.uk/cannot-pay-a-business-loan/}} < /ref >  or [[employer|hires]] someone to work, an agreement is usually signed so that everyone understands what and when things must be done.
