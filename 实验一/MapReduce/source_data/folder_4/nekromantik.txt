 '''''Nekromantik''''' is a [[controversy|controversial]] 1987 [[horror movie]]. The subjects look at [[suicide]] and [[necrophilia]]. This horror movie was refused classification in [[Australia]], [[Iceland]], [[New Zealand]] and [[Norway]] due to its graphic and explicit content. It became a cult movie during the years after its release, however.
