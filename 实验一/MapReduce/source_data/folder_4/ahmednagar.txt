 '''Ahmednagar''' is a [[city]] of [[Ahmednagar District]] in the [[States of India|state]] of [[Maharashtra]], [[India]], on the left bank of the Sina river, about 120 [[kilometre]]s northeast of [[Pune]] and 120 kilometres from Aurangabad.
