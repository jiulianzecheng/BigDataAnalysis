 '''Schopfheim''' is a [[town]] in [[Lörrach (district)|Lörrach]] in [[Baden-Württemberg]], [[Germany]]. It is on the river [[Wiese]], {{convert|10|km|abbr=off}} north of [[Rheinfelden, Baden-Württemberg|Rheinfelden]], and {{convert|13|km|abbr=off}} east of [[Lörrach]].
