 '''Opistognathidae''', the '''jawfishes''', are a family of fishes classified within the order [[Perciformes]], suborder [[Percoidei]]. The family includes about 80 species.  
 
 They are native to warmer parts of the [[Atlantic Ocean|Atlantic]], [[Pacific Ocean|Pacific]] and [[Indian Ocean]]s, where found from the shallows to depths of a few hundred meters.
