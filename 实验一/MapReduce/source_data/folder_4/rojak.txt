 '''Rojak''' is a local food of mixed [[Vegetable|vegetables]] and [[fruits]] with sweet and sour sauce. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Rojak {{!}} Infopedia|url=https://eresources.nlb.gov.sg/infopedia/articles/SIP_392_2005-01-06.html#:~:text=Rojak%20is%20a%20local%20salad,Malay%20elements%20in%20its%20ingredients|access-date=2023-08-16|website=eresources.nlb.gov.sg}} < /ref >  Rojak is commonly found in Singapore, [[Malaysia]] and [[Indonesia]] but it is popular in [[Singapore]] because the dish combines ingredients from the region and different ethnic groups, and reflects Singapore's cultural diversity. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Rojak|url=https://www.roots.gov.sg/en/ich-landing/ich/rojak#:~:text=Rojak%20is%20a%20popular%20salad,the%20cultural%20diversity%20of%20Singapore|access-date=2023-08-16|website=www.roots.gov.sg}} < /ref >  Usually, rojak is served as a side dish but it can also be served as a main dish.
