 The '''cassock''' or '''soutane''' is a type of clothing. It is a [[Christianity|Christian]] [[Clergy|clerical]] [[coat]] used by the [[Oriental Orthodox Church]], [[Eastern Orthodox Church]] and the [[Catholic Church]], it is also used by [[Protestantism|Protestants]] such as [[Anglicans]] and [[Lutherans]].
