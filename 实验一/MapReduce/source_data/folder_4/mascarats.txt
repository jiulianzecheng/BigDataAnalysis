 Mascarats is a [[Catalonia]]n rock and [[reggae]] musical group. 
 
 The group started in 2008 with the name  " Amagau Todos "  in the city of [[La Sénia]] in Catalonia, Spain.  Later they changed their name to Mascarats. < ref name= " auto " > {{cite web|url=http://www.aguaita.cat/noticia/8782/aguaita/estrena/exclusiva/nou/disc/mascarats|title=Aguaita.cat estrena en exclusiva el nou disc de Mascarats|first=|last=Aguaita.cat|publisher=}} < /ref >   
 
 Mascarats is a  nickname given to the people of La Sénia. At one time, coal was produced La Sénia, and the people who worked there came out with black and dirty faces. They were called  " masked " . This name was being forgotten and the group wanted to revive it. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://revista.latornada.cat/ca/la-descoberta-91/mascarats|title=Mascarats. Sinceritat per sobre de tot - La descoberta|first=Revista La|last=Tornada|website=revista.latornada.cat}} < /ref > 
 
 == Music == 
 
 Their first album was  " La veu del poble "  in 2014.  < ref > {{cite web|url=https://rateyourmusic.com/release/album/maskarats/la-veu-del-poble/|title=La veu del poble by Maskarats|publisher=}} < /ref >   
 
 Their second album was  " Trencarem l'imaginari "  ( " Let's break the imaginary') in 2017. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.enderrock.cat/disc/6531/trencarem/imaginari|title=Mascarats - Trencarem l'imaginari|first=|last=Enderrock.cat|website=www.enderrock.cat}} < /ref >  The album was released with a video:  " Mai més presons "  ( " No more prisons'). The album was described as  " fusing reggae and rock " , and having a rebellious spirit with themes of feminism, internationalism, anti-capitalism, and political references. The album was produced at WZ Estudi in Borriol, Castellón de la Plana, Spain, and produced by Sam Ferrer. It featured Gemma Polo (vocalist from Roba Estesa), and backup vocals by Xavi Sarrià of Obrint Pas and Josep Nadal of La Gossa Sorda, and additional instrumentals by Joan Palà of Txarango on percussion and Héctor Peropadre of Aspencat on lute.  < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.aguaita.cat/noticia/8782/aguaita/estrena/exclusiva/nou/disc/mascarats|title=Aguaita.cat estrena en exclusiva el nou disc de Mascarats|first=|last=Aguaita.cat|publisher=}} < /ref > 
 
 In 2015 their song  " Sentiment comú "  was chosen to represent the XXXVII Aplec dels Ports, a yearly festival of the historical port cities, that is in a different city every year. < ref name= " auto " / > 
 
 In 2018 they recorded a video  " Els carrers seran sempre nostres "  ( " The streets will always be ours " ) as a tribute to the October 1, 2017 Catalonia independence referendum and the September 20 arrests.  < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.vilaweb.cat/noticies/els-carrers-seran-sempre-nostres-fet-canco/|title=‘Els carrers seran sempre nostres’, fet cançó|website=VilaWeb.cat}} < /ref > < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.enderrock.cat/noticia/17127/videoclip/mascarats/homenatja/herois/octubre|title=El videoclip de Mascarats que homenatja els «herois de l'1 d'Octubre»|first=|last=Enderrock.cat|publisher=}} < /ref > < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.vilaweb.cat/noticies/sis-mesos-de-l1-o-deu-cancons-que-reivindiquen-el-referendum-dindependencia-de-catalunya/|title=Sis mesos de l’1-O: deu cançons que reivindiquen el referèndum d’independència de Catalunya|website=VilaWeb.cat}} < /ref > 
 
 In July 2018 they joined the  " Free Forcadell "  campaign, a benefit for Catalan President [[Carme Forcadell]], the release of the political prisoners, and the return of the exiles, along with musicians [[Maria del Mar Bonet]], musical group [[Gossos]], singer [[Gerard Quintana]], actor [[Sílvia Bel]], musicians [[Titot]] and [[Borja Penalba]], and poet [[Roc Casagran]], actors [[Joel Joan]] and [[Anna Sahún]], and journalist Sarai Sarroca. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.elnacional.cat/es/politica/free-forcadell-sabadell_288771_102.html|title=Nace la campaña 'Free Forcadell' con un gran acto en Sabadell este sábado|website=ElNacional.cat}} < /ref > < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.ara.cat/politica/Sabadell-acollira-llibertat-Forcadell-actuacions_0_2052994825.html|title=Sabadell acollirà un gran acte polític per la llibertat de Forcadell amb actuacions musicals|publisher=}} < /ref > < ref name= " lavanguardia.com " > {{cite web|url=https://www.lavanguardia.com/local/sabadell/20180717/45949059338/sabadell-acogera-acto-apoyo-carme-forcadell.html|title=Sabadell acogerá un gran acto en apoyo a Carme Forcadell|publisher=}} < /ref > < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.vilaweb.cat/noticies/ajornat-fins-setembre-el-gran-acte-a-sabadell-de-suport-a-carme-forcadell/|title=Ajornat fins setembre el gran acte a Sabadell de suport a Carme Forcadell|website=VilaWeb.cat}} < /ref > < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.diaridesabadell.com/2018/07/17/gossos-maria-del-mar-bonet-gerard-quintana-freeforcadell/|title=Gossos, Maria del Mar Bonet i Gerard Quintana, al concert FreeForcadell|website=www.diaridesabadell.com}} < /ref >  The event was originally scheduled for July 21, but was changed to September 15 because of rain. < ref name= " lavanguardia.com " / >
