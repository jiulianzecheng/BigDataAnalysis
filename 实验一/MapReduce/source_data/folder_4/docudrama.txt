The genre uses [[Drama|dramatic]] [[Skill|techniques]] as they show real events.  ==Types of docudrama==  
 * '''Pure docudrama''' or '''docushow:''' Reality is shownas it happens at the time, but under a dramatic story structure. Examples of a pure docudrama  are programs that show the work in a [[hospital]] [[Emergency department|emergency room]].  
 * '''Partially pure docudrama:''' In these, the [[Protagonist|protagonists]] recreate their stories.  
 * '''Fictional docudrama:''' These are, based on a true story which is often of a tragic nature. These often deal with [[Murder|murders]], [[Rape|rapes]], [[Accident|accidents]] or [[Paranormal television|paranormal cases]]. In these, the people are played by [[Actor|actors]].
