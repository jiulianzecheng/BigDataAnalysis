 {{nihongo|'''''Excitebike'''''|エキサイトバイク|Ekisaitobaiku}} is a [[motocross]] [[racing game|racing]] [[video game]] made by [[Nintendo]]. It first appeared as a game for the Famicom in Japan in 1984 for a price of 5000 yen. It is the first game in the ''Excite'' series. The second game in the series is ''[[Excitebike 64]]'' and the third is ''[[Excite Truck]]''. 
 
 In 2011, a re-release of ''Excitebike'' called ''3D Classics: Excitebike'' was released for the [[Nintendo 3DS]]. It is a [[3D]] version of the original game.
