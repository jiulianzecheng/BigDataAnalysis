'''The Ahirwar''', '''Aharwar''' or '''Suryavanshi Ahirwar''' and '''Chaudhary''' are Hindu Rajput Kshatriya clan classified among the Scheduled Castes who are members of the Suryavanshi lineage of the North Indian caste, belonging to the Chawar dynasty.
