 '''''Pixote: a Lei do Mais Franco''''' ({{IPA-pt|piˈʃɔtʃi a ˈlej du ˈmajʃ ˈfɾaku}}, lit.  " Pixote (small child): The Law of the Weakest " ) is a 1981 Brazilian drama movie directed by [[Héctor Babenco]] and based on the novel ''A Infância dos Mortos'' (''The Childhood of the Dead Ones'') by [[José Louzeiro]]. It stars [[Fernando Ramos de Silva]] as the title character.
