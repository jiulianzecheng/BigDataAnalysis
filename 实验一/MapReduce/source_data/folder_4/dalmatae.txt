The '''Dalmatae''' or '''Delmatae''' were an [[Illyrians|Illyrian]] [[tribe]]. < ref > J B Bury, ''The Cambridge Ancient History'', Vol. 11 (Cambridge : University Press, 2000), p. 597 < /ref >  They lived in what is today [[Croatia]] and [[Bosnia and Herzegovina]]. Their capital was [[Delminium]]. The Dalmatae were conquered by the [[Roman Republic]] in 33 BC.
