 '''Tiachiv''' ({{lang-uk|Тячів}}; {{lang-rue|Тячово}}; {{lang-hu|Técső}}; {{lang-yi|טעטש|translit=Tetch}}) is a city in the [[Zakarpattia Oblast]] ([[Oblast|province]]) in western [[Ukraine]]. It is the [[administrative centre]] of [[Tiachiv Raion]] ([[Raion|district]]). In 2022, 8,887 people lived there.
