 }}'''Kurzgesagt''' (in [[German Language]] stands for  " In a nutshell " ) is a design studio and [[animator]] founded by Philipp Dettmer. They have a [[YouTube]] Channel and its studio focuses on educational [[minimalist]] content. They animate their videos using a flat design style. Their videos are about Science, Technology, Policies, [[Philosophy]] subjects. < ref > {{cite web|last1=Bauman|first1=Kat|date=May 9, 2014|title=From the Super Adorable Science Files: Videos by Kurzgesagt|url=https://www.core77.com/posts/26915/From-the-Super-Adorable-Science-Files-Videos-by-Kurzgesagt|access-date=October 2, 2020|website=Core 77}} < /ref > 
 
 The videos are narrated by Steve Tylor. The videos are usually 4 to 16 minutes long. Their works are available in English, German, and Spanish. They began creating videos in their Spanish Channel (En Pocas Palabras - Kurzgesagt) near the end of 2019 < ref name= " HowTo " > {{Cite web|date=16 February 2020|title=How to Make a Kurzgesagt Video in 1200 Hours|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=uFk0mgljtns|access-date=2020-06-10|website=YouTube|language=en}} < /ref >  as of November 2nd, they have 16.7 million subscribers and have 150 videos. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Kurzgesagt – In a Nutshell|url=https://socialblade.com/youtube/user/kurzgesagt|access-date=September 1, 2021|website=[[Social Blade]]}} < /ref >
