 '''Carrouges''' is a commune in the French ''departement'' of the [[Orne]]. There are 787 peoples living there in 2012. 
 
 The town is well-known because of its castle built in the 16th century, with red bricks. Many expositions and other activities are organized. There is a 19th-century church. Hunting and fishing are the favourite activities of the inhabitants.
