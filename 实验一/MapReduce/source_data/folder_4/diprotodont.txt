 The '''Diprotodontia''' is a large order of about 120 [[Australasia]]n [[marsupial]] mammals. It includes the [[kangaroo]]s, [[Wallaby|wallabies]], [[possum]]s, [[koala]], [[wombat]]s, and many others.  
 
 Extinct members include the giant [[Diprotodon]] family, and ''[[Thylacoleo]]'', the so-called  " marsupial lion " .
