 '''Benjamin Hammond Haggerty''' (born June 19, 1983), better known by his stage name '''Macklemore''', is an American rapper, singer, songwriter and activist.  
 
 The music video for his song  " [[Thrift Shop (song)|Thrift Shop]] "  has been viewed more than 1 billion times on [[YouTube]]. It also reached #1 on the U.S. [[Billboard Hot 100]] chart. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://www.billboard.com/charts |title= Hot 100: 1-10 (Biggest Jump) Billboard |accessdate=2013-04-10 |publisher=[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]}} < /ref >  He said he was influenced by [[Freestyle Fellowship]], [[Aceyalone]], [[Living Legends]], [[Wu-Tang Clan]], [[Nas]], and [[Talib Kweli]] during his earlier years. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YWe9rnu32Kk|accessdate=2013-04-10|title=Youtube - Macklemore talks Otherside, Lil Wayne, Substance Abuse, Seattle Hip Hop, and more|publisher=YouTube}} < /ref > 
 
 Macklemore is in support of gay rights, including [[same-sex marriage]], and sang about it in the song  " Same Love "  which he also sings against [[homophobia]] in mass media, hip hop, and society. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.studio360.org/2012/nov/30/macklemores-gay-anthem/|accessdate=2013-04-10|title=Macklemore's Gay Anthem|publisher=Studio360|archive-date=2013-02-04|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130204070807/http://www.studio360.org/2012/nov/30/macklemores-gay-anthem/|url-status=dead}} < /ref > 
 
 Macklemore was nominated for seven Grammy Awards at the 2014 Grammys. < ref > {{Cite magazine|url=http://www.billboard.com/articles/news/5819883/grammy-awards-2014-full-nominations-list-complete-nominees|title=Grammy Awards 2014: Full Nominations List|magazine=[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]|date=7 December 2013}} < /ref >  Macklemore and Lewis won Best New Artist, Best Rap Album (''The Heist''), Best Rap Song and Best Rap Performance ( " Thrift Shop " ). < ref > http://www.miamiherald.com/2014/01/26/3894187/could-rap-have-big-night-at-grammys.html < /ref > 
 
 == Early life == 
 He was taught at Garfield High School and then Nathen Hale High School. His parents are namend Bill Haggerty and Julie Haggerty. He has one brother named Tim Haggerty. Tim Haggerty sings on few of Macklemore's songs like:  " And We Danced "  and  " American " . 
 
 ==Career== 
 In 2005 Macklemore released his first album, ''The Language of My World''. In 2006 he met Ryan Lewis. They released an album together, ''The Heist''. On January 26, 2014, Macklemore performed  " Same Love "  at the Grammy Awards. 
 
 ==Personal life==
