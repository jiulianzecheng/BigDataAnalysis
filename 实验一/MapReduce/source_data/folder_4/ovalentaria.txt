[[File:Opistognathus aurifrons.jpg|thumb|[[:en:Yellowhead_jawfish|Yellowhead jawfish]] (''Opistognathus aurifrons'')]] 
 '''Ovalentaria''' is a clade of [[Ray-finned fish|ray-finned fishes]] within the [[Percomorpha]]. It includes the order [[Stiassnyiformes]]. Some authors have used it for a clade including [[Mugiloidei]], [[Longfin|Plesiopidae]], [[Blenny|Blenniiformes]], [[Atherinomorpha]], and [[Cichlid|Cichlidae]]. It was named by W. L. Smith and T. J. Near in Wainwright in 2012.
