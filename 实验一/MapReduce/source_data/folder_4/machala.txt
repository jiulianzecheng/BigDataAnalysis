 '''Machala''' ({{IPA-es|maˈtʃala}}) is a city in south-west [[Ecuador]]. It is the capital of the [[El Oro Province]]. Machala has a [[population]] of 241,606 since 2010. It is called the ''Banana Capital of the World''.
