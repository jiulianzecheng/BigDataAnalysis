 '''Weobley''' is a large village and [[Civil parishes in England|civil parish]] in [[Herefordshire]], England. 
 
 == Governance == 
 Weobley is part of the Golden Cross and Weobley [[Wards and electoral divisions of the United Kingdom|electoral ward]]. The population of this ward was 2,985 in 2011. < ref > {{cite web|title=Golden Cross and Weobley ward population 2011|url=http://www.ukcensusdata.com/golden-cross-with-weobley-e05001853#sthash.Yauownl3.dpbs|access-date=30 October 2015}} < /ref >
