 '''Arepa''' is a food that came from the [[pre-Columbian era]]. It is made by using [[cornmeal]] and forming the dough into a circle. It is known to be eaten in [[Colombia]] and [[Venezuela]]. < ref > {{Cite web|date=2018-07-18|title=arepa {{!}} Definición de arepa - Diccionario de la lengua española - Edi…|url=http://dle.rae.es/?id=3VJbD8F|url-status=dead|archive-url=https://archive.today/20180718164044/http://dle.rae.es/?id=3VJbD8F|archive-date=2018-07-18|access-date=2021-07-06|website=archive.ph}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite web|title=Arepas|url=https://calendariosaboresbolivia.com/tag/arepas/|access-date=2021-07-06|website=Sabores de Bolivia|language=es-ES}} < /ref >  It can also have fillings like sliced [[cheese]], [[meat]], and some [[Vegetable|vegetables]]. 
 
 Arepas are also commonly eaten in places full of Hispanic immigrants like the [[Canary Islands]], < ref > {{Cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=BSc1ytU-4UoC & dq=canary+islands+arepa & pg=PA201|title=Canary Islands|last=Corne|first=Lucy|date=2004|publisher=Bradt Travel Guides|isbn=978-1-84162-108-1|language=en}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite web|date=2015-10-19|title=Arepa, delicia Caribeña|url=https://revistavenezolana.com/2015/10/arepa-delicia-caribena/|access-date=2021-08-03|website=Revista Venezolana|language=es}} < /ref >  and some American cities like [[New York City]]. < ref > {{Cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=9WsusSt5oZIC & dq=new+york+city+arepa & pg=PA393|title=New York City|last=Fodor's|date=2006-08-29|publisher=Fodor's Travel Publications|isbn=978-1-4000-1680-8|language=en}} < /ref > 
 
 == History == 
 It is thought that the word ''arepa'' came from the extinct [[Cumanagoto language|Cumanagoto]] word ''erepa,'' meaning ''cornbread.'' < ref > {{Cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=DBN5DwAAQBAJ & dq=arepa+erepa & pg=PT321|title=¡Viva la arepa!: Sabor, memoria e imaginario social en Venezuela|last=Vargas|first=Miguel Felipe Dorta|date=2016-06-02|publisher=Editorial Alfa|isbn=978-84-16687-66-4|language=es}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite web|last=Rivera|first=Marcela|title=The DNA Of: Arepas|url=https://www.amexessentials.com/how-to-make-arepas/|access-date=2021-07-17|website=www.amexessentials.com}} < /ref >
