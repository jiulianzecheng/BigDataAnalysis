 '''Drepana''' ({{lang-grc|Δρέπανα}}) was an [[Elymians|Elymian]], [[Carthaginian]], and [[Roman]] port in [[antiquity]] on the western coast of [[Sicily]]. It was the site of the [[Battle of Drepana]] where the Romans were defeated by the Carthaginians in 249 BC. It is now the modern [[Italy|Italian]] city of [[Trapani]].
