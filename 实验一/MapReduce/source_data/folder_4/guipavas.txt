 '''Guipavas''' ({{lang-br|Gwipavaz}}) is a [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is found in the [[Regions of France|region]] [[Brittany]] in the [[Finistère]] [[Departments of France|department]] in the northwest of [[France]]. In 2017, 14,482 people lived there. 
 
 The [[Brest Bretagne Airport]] is in the commune.
