 '''Torver''' is a village and [[civil parish]] in [[South Lakeland]], [[Cumbria]], England. In 2001 there were 135 people living in Torver. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.ons.gov.uk/help/localstatistics|title=Local statistics - Office for National Statistics|website=www.ons.gov.uk}} < /ref >  It has 10 listed buildings. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://britishlistedbuildings.co.uk/england/cumbria/Torver|title=Listed Buildings in Cumbria|website=britishlistedbuildings.co.uk}} < /ref >  It has a church called St Luke's, < ref > {{cite web|url=https://britishlistedbuildings.co.uk/101087212-church-of-st-luke-torver|title=Church of St Luke, Torver, Cumbria|first=Good|last=Stuff|website=britishlistedbuildings.co.uk}} < /ref >  which was designed by [[Paley and Austin]]. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.visitcumbria.com/churches/torver-st-lukes-church/|title=Torver St Lukes Church|publisher=}} < /ref >
