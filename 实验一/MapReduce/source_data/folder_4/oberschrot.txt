 '''Oberschrot''' was a [[municipality]] in the district of [[Sense (district)|Sense]] in the [[canton of Fribourg]] in [[Switzerland]]. On 1 January 2017, the municipalities of Oberschrot and [[Zumholz]] became part of Plaffeien. < ref > [https://www.bfs.admin.ch/bfs/de/home/grundlagen/agvch.html Amtliches Gemeindeverzeichnis der Schweiz] {{in lang|de}} accessed 10 January 2017 < /ref >
