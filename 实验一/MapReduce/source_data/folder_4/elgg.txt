 '''Elgg''' is a [[municipality]] of the [[district]] [[Winterthur (district)|Winterthur]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Zurich (canton)|Zurich]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 On 1 January 2018, the former municipality of [[Hofstetten bei Elgg]] merged into the municipality of Elgg.
