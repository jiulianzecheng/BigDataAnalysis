'''Jublains''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]] of 699 people ([[1999]]). It is found in the region [[Pays de la Loire]] in the [[Mayenne]] department in the northwest of [[France]]. It is an old Roman town called [[Noviodunum]]. It includes: 
 *A well preserved theater. 
 *A temple. 
 *A [[Castellum]] with a museum about the town. 
 *Terms (Roman baths). 
 *Various other Roman things.
