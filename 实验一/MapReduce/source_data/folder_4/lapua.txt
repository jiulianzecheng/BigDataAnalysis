 '''Lapua''' is a [[city]] in [[Southern Ostrobothnia]], [[Finland]]. It had almost 14,700 people living there in January 2014. The municipalities next to it are [[Alajärvi]], [[Kauhava]], [[Kuortane]], [[Lappajärvi ]] and [[Seinäjoki]].
