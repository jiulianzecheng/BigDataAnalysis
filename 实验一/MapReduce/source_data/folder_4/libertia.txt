 '''''Libertia''''' is a [[genus]] of [[monocotyledon|monocotyledenous]] [[plant]]s in the [[Family (biology)|family]] ''[[Iridaceae]]''. There are 15 [[species]] in the genus. The plants can be found growing in the [[southern hemisphere]].
