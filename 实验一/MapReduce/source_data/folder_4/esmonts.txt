 '''Esmonts''' is a village of the district [[Glâne (district)|Glâne]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Fribourg (canton)|Fribourg]] in [[Switzerland]]. On 1 January 2006, [[Vuarmarens]] incorporated the formerly independent municipality of Esmonts and on 1 January 2012, it has been with Vuarmarens merged to form [[Ursy]]. < ref name=Mutation > [http://www.bfs.admin.ch/bfs/portal/de/index/infothek/nomenklaturen/blank/blank/gem_liste/02.html Nomenklaturen – Amtliches Gemeindeverzeichnis der Schweiz] {{in lang|de}} accessed 4 April 2011 < /ref >
