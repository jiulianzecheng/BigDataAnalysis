'''Kasaragod District''' ({{lang-ml|കാസർഗോഡ് ജില്ല}}) is one of the 14 districts in the state of [[Kerala]], [[India]]. Kasaragod became part of [[Kannur district]] following the reorganisation of states and formation of Kerala in November 1, 1956. < ref > {{cite web |url= http://kasargod.nic.in/profile/afterdisform.htm |title= Kasaragod - After District Formation |publisher= Kasaragod District |access-date= 2009-03-11 |archive-date= 2009-04-10 |archive-url= https://web.archive.org/web/20090410025049/http://kasargod.nic.in/profile/afterdisform.htm |url-status= dead }} < /ref >  Kasaragod was declared as a district on 24 May 1984.
