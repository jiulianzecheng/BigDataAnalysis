 ''Unapologetic'' is the seventh [[studio album]] released by Barbadian singer [[Rihanna]]. It was released on November 19, 2012, by Def Jam Recordings.  The album features collaborations from [[Future (rapper)|Future]], [[David Guetta]], [[Eminem]], Mikky Ekko, and [[Chris Brown (entertainer)|Chris Brown]].  ''Unapologetic ''became Rihanna's first number-one album on the ''[[Billboard 200|Billboard]]''[[Billboard 200|200]] chart in the [[United States]]. It features the number one hit single  " Diamonds " . To promote the album Rihanna went on the 777 Tour which consisted of seven shows in seven cities over seven nights.
