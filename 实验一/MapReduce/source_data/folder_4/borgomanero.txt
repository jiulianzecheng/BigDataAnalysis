 '''Borgomanero''' is a ''[[comune]]'' (municipality) in the [[Province of Novara]] in the [[Italy|Italian]] region [[Piedmont]]. It is about {{convert|60|km|mi}} northwest of [[Milan]], about {{convert|30|km|mi}} northwest of [[Novara]] and not far from the border between [[Lombardy]] and [[Piedmont]] 
 
 Borgomanero borders the following municipalities: [[Bogogno]], [[Briga Novarese]], [[Cressa]], [[Cureggio]], [[Fontaneto d'Agogna]], [[Gargallo, Piedmont|Gargallo]], [[Gattico]], [[Gozzano]], [[Invorio]], [[Maggiora]], [[Veruno]].
