'''Warrington''' is a large town and [[borough]] within the [[Ceremonial counties of England|ceremonial county]] of [[Cheshire]], [[England]]. It sits beside the [[West Coast Mainline]], the [[M6 motorway|M6]], [[M56 motorway|M56]] and [[M62 motorway]]s, and alongside the [[Manchester Ship Canal]].
