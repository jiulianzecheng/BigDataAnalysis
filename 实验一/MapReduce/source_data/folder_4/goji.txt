 '''Goji''', '''goji berry''' or '''wolfberry''' is the fruit of ''[[Lycium barbarum]]''  and ''[[Lycium chinense]]''. These are two very closely related [[species]] of [[boxthorn]] in the [[family (biology)|family]] [[Solanaceae]] (which also includes the [[potato]], [[tomato]], [[eggplant]], [[deadly nightshade]], [[chili pepper]], and [[tobacco]]).
