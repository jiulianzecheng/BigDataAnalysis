Nagapattinam is a town and headquarters of Nagapattinam District in [[Tamil Nadu]] state of [[India]]. One of the town which was severely hit by the [[2004 Indian Ocean earthquake|2004 tsunami]].  
 ==Demographics== 
 # The town has an area of 14.92km2. 
 
 # The population is 102,905. 
 
 # The density of the people living is 615.99/km2 
 
 ==Occupation== 
 As Nagapattinam is nearby the sea, the main occupation of the people here is Fishing.
