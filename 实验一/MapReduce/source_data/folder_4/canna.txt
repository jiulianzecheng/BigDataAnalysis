 '''''Canna''''' is a small [[island]] in the [[Small Isles]] in the [[Inner Hebrides]]. As of 2017, there are 12 people on the island. Canna is linked to its neighbouring island [[Sanday]] by a single [[road]].  Canna has its own native [[animal]], the ''Canna Fruit Bat''.
