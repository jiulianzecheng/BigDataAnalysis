 A '''paw''' is the soft [[foot]] of a mammal that has [[claw]]s or nails. A hard foot is called a hoof. Paws are used to pad feet for walking and reduce friction. 
 
 == Animals with paws == 
 * Members of the [[Canidae]] family, such as [[dog]]s and [[fox]]es 
 * [[Felidae|Felines]], such as [[cat]]s and [[tiger]]s 
 * [[Bear]]s and [[Raccoon]]s 
 * [[Weasel]]s and other [[mustelid]]s 
 * [[Rodent]]s
