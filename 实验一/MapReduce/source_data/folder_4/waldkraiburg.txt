 '''Waldkraiburg''' ({{Lang-bar|Woidkroaburg|label=[[Central Bavarian]]}}) is a [[Town#Germany|town]] in [[Mühldorf (district)|Mühldorf]], in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. It is the biggest town in Mühldorf. It is on the river [[Inn (river)|Inn]], about ten kilometers southwest of [[Mühldorf]] and sixty kilometers east of [[Munich]].
