 '''Wiggensbach''' is a [[municipality]] in [[Bavaria]]. It is about {{convert|7|km|abbr=off}} west of [[Kempten (Allgäu)|Kempten]], the next big town. Next door municipalities are [[Altusried]] in the north and [[Buchenberg]] in the south of the village. Wiggensbach is the main village. Ermengerst and Westenried are the two districts of Wiggensbach. There are 72 hamlets around the municipality. The village is between {{convert|870|and|1,077|m|abbr=off}} above sea level. 
 
 Wiggensbach is twinned with [[Hattstatt]] in [[France]]. The 20th anniversary of the partnership was in 2003.
