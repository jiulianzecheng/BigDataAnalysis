 '''Sukunabikona''' is the [[Shinto]] [[kami]] of [[Onsen|hot springs]], agriculture, healing, magic, brewing sake and knowledge. His name means  " the small lord of renown. "  He is often described as being a dwarf and is often paired with [[Ōkuninushi]]. 
 
 The asteroid [[List of minor planets: 10001–11000|10725 Sukunabikona]] derives its name from him. < ref > {{Cite web|title=JPL Small-Body Database Browser|url=https://ssd.jpl.nasa.gov/sbdb.cgi?sstr=10725#content|website=ssd.jpl.nasa.gov}} < /ref >
