 '''Susch''' (formerly '''Süs''') was a [[municipality]] of the [[district]] of [[Inn (district)|Inn]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Graubünden]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 On 1 January 2015, the municipalities of [[Lavin]] and Susch became part of the municipality of [[Zernez]]. 
 
 The [[Flüela Pass]] connects Susch with [[Davos]].
