 '''Hansestadt Stendal''' or '''Stendal''' ({{IPA-de|ˈʃtɛndaːl}}) is a [[town]] in [[Saxony-Anhalt]], [[Germany]]. About 40,000 people live in the city. 
 
 It is the capital of the [[Stendal Rural District|Stendal District]] and the main town in the [[Altmark]] region.
