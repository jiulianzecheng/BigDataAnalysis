 '''Blasterjaxx''' is a [[Dutch people|Dutch]] [[Disc jockey|DJ]] and record producer duo, Thom Jongkind (born 1990) and Idir Makhlaf (born 1992). They originated in [[The Hague]] and has been active since 2010.
