'''Wimbish''' is a village and [[civil parish]] in [[Uttlesford]], [[Essex]], England. In 2001 there were 1550 people living in Wimbish. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.ons.gov.uk/help/localstatistics|title=Local statistics - Office for National Statistics|website=www.ons.gov.uk}} < /ref >  It includes the hamlets of [[Tye Green]], Upper and Lower Green (or [[Wimbish Green]]), [[Howlett End]], [[Elder Street]] and [[Cole End]]. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.recordinguttlesfordhistory.org.uk/Wimbish/wimbish.html|title=Wimbish|website=www.recordinguttlesfordhistory.org.uk}} < /ref >
