 '''''Incesticide''''' is a compilation album by the [[United States|American]] [[grunge music|grunge]] band, [[Nirvana (band)|Nirvana]]. This album features many of their [[B-Side]]s and demos of their songs. On the back of the CD, [[Kurt Cobain]] spells his name wrong. He spells it  " Kurdt Cobain. "
