 '''Epiquerez''' was a [[municipality]] in the [[district]] of [[Franches-Montagnes (district)|Franches-Montagnes]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Jura (canton)|Jura]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 On 1 January 2009, the former municipalities of Epiquerez, [[Epauvillers]], [[Montenol]], [[Montmelon]], [[Ocourt]], [[Saint-Ursanne]] and [[Seleute]] merged into the new municipality of [[Clos du Doubs]].
