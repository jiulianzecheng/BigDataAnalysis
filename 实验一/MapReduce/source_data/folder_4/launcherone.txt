 '''LauncherOne''' is a [[Two-stage-to-orbit|two-stage]] [[orbital launch vehicle]] (see [[launch vehicle]]) developed and flown by [[Virgin Orbit]]. It was in development from 2007 to 2020. It is an [[air launch to orbit|air-launched]] rocket. It carries a [[Small satellite|smallsat]] payloads of up to {{cvt|300|kg}} into [[Sun-synchronous orbit]] (SSO). 
 
 The first successful flight was on 17 January 2021.
