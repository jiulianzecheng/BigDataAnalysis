 '''Wellsford''' ([[Māori language|Māori]]: ''Whakapirau'') is a town on the [[Northland Peninsula]] in northern [[New Zealand]]. It is a major town located 77 kilometers north of [[Auckland]] and situated on State Highway 1 and State Highway 16. It has six primary schools and one high school situated within the area. Its private school closed at the end of 2018 due to falling numbers.
