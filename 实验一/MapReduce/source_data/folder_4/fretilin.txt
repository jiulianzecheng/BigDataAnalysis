 The '''Revolutionary Front for an Independent East Timor''' ({{lang-pt|Frente Revolucionária de Timor-Leste Independente}}, '''Fretilin''') is a [[Centre-left politics|centre-left]] < ref name= " BBC "  / >  [[political party]] in [[East Timor]]. They hold 23 of 65 seats in the [[National Parliament (East Timor)|National Parliament]].
