 '''Metten''' is a [[Municipalities of Germany|municipality]] in the district of [[Deggendorf (district)|Deggendorf]] in [[Bavaria]] in [[Germany]]. The town grew up around the Benedictine [[Metten Abbey]], founded in 766. Metten is also the birthplace of former [[Bayern Munich]] goalkeeper [[Sepp Maier]].
