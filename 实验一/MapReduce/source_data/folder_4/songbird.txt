'''Songbird''' is a [[Dutch]] [[record label]] owned by [[Tiësto|Tijs Verwest]] and Arny Bink. It is a sublabel of [[Black Hole Recordings]], established in [[1997 in music|1997]]. It is most well known as the label on which Tiësto's popular [[Trance music|trance]] series [[In Search of Sunrise (series)|In Search of Sunrise]] was released. Other artists featured on this label are DJ Dazzle, Mistral, Moontribe and Masai, with remixes by Dawnseekers, Ronald van Gelderen and [[Cygnus X]].
