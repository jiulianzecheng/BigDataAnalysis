 The '''Marchspitze''' is a [[mountain]] in the [[Allgäu Alps]]. It is made of [[Dolomite]] and is part of the [[Hornbach chain]]. The mountain is {{convert|2609|m}} high. It is in [[Tyrol (state)|Tyrol]], [[Austria]], 1.5 kilometres east of the [[Großer Krottenkopf]]. It is a hard climb and therefore not many people visit it.
