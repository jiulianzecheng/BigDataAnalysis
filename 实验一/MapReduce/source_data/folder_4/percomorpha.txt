 The '''Percomorpha''' (from [[Latin]] ''perca'' '[[perch]]', and [[Ancient Greek]] [[wiktionary:μορφή#Ancient_Greek|μορφή]] ''(morphḗ)'' 'shape, appearance') are a large clade of [[ray-finned fish]]. It includes the [[Tuna|tunas]], [[Seahorse|seahorses]], [[Goby|gobies]], [[Cichlid|cichlids]], [[flatfish]], [[Wrasse|wrasses]], [[Perch|perches]], [[anglerfish]], and [[pufferfish]].
