 '''Tabor''' or '''tabret''' ({{lang-cy|Tabwrdd}}) refers to a portable [[snare drum]] played with one hand. The word  " tabor "  is simply an [[English language|English]] variant of a [[Latin]] word meaning  " drum " < ref name=Harms > [http://www.harmsperc.com/tabor.htm Harms Historical Percussion's Tabor page]. < /ref >  It has been used in the military as a [[Marching band|marching instrument]]. It has also been used in [[parade]]s.
