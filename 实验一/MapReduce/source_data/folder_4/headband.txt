 A '''headband''' (also known as a '''headwrap''') is an [[accessory (fashion)|accessory]]. It is made to fit over or around the [[head]], usually to hold [[hair]] away from the face or [[sweat]] out of the eyes.  
 
 It may be made of many different materials, like [[plastic]] or [[elastic]] [[fabric]]. They come in many shapes and sizes and are used for both practical and [[fashion]] reasons.
