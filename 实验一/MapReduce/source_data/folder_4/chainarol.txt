The '''Chainarol''', also spelt as '''Chainalol''' (literally, '' " Way of the warrior " '' < ref > {{cite web | url=https://books.google.com/books?id=psCQoAEACAAJ | title=Chainarol: Way of the Warrior | last1=Arun | first1=Irengbam | year=2012 }} < /ref > ), is an [[Ancient Meitei language|ancient Meitei]] [[Puya (Meitei texts)|puya]] which records the armed combats of various gladiators through different periods of history of [[Ancient Manipur]]. < ref name= " Medieval Indian Literature: Surveys and selections - K. Ayyappa Paniker - Google Books " > {{cite book | url=https://books.google.com/books?id=KYLpvaKJIMEC & dq=chainarol & pg=PA332 | title=Medieval Indian Literature: Surveys and selections (Assamese-Dogri) | isbn=9788126003655 | author1=Ayyappappanikkar | last2=Ayyappa Paniker | first2=K. | year=1997 }} < /ref > < ref > {{cite web | url=http://www.e-pao.net/epSubPageExtractor.asp?src=manipur.Arts_and_Culture.Chainarol_Way_of_Warrior_Manipuri_Humanitarian_Traditions_Part_1 | title=Chainarol Way of Warrior Manipuri Humanitarian Traditions Part 1 }} < /ref > < ref > {{cite book | url=https://books.google.com/books?id=UhcLAQAAMAAJ & q=chainarol | title=Langues non Constitutionnelles | isbn=9782763771960 | last1=Kloss | first1=Heinz | last2=McConnell | first2=Grant D. | year=1989 }} < /ref >  It consists of 27 stories of real life incidents of the dual fight between different gladiators. < ref > {{cite web | url=https://books.google.com/books?id=lHwKAQAAMAAJ & q=chainarol | title=Jadavpur journal of comparative literature | year=2006 }} < /ref > < ref > {{cite web | url=https://books.google.com/books?id=LVpuAAAAMAAJ & q=chainarol | title=Recent Researches in Oriental Indological Studies: Including Meiteilogy | last1=Singh | first1=Moirangthem Kirti | year=1998 }} < /ref >  Various scholars believe that the first entry in the text is a personal combat dating back to the 1st century AD and the latest episode dating back to the 2nd half of the 16th century AD. < ref name= " Medieval Indian Literature: Surveys and selections - K. Ayyappa Paniker - Google Books "  / >
