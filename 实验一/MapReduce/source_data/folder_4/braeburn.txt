 '''Braeburn''' (''Malus domestica'' 'Braeburn') is an [[apple]] [[cultivar]] that is firm to the touch with a red/orange vertical streaky appearance on a yellow/green background. The Braeburn apple originated in [[New Zealand]]. The apple has a sweet and tart flavour.
