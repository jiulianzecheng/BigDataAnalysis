 '''Tadhg''' is an [[Irish language|Irish]] masculine [[given name]] meaning  " [[poet]] "  or  " [[philosopher]] " . < ref > {{Cite web|title=Tadhg name Meaning and Origin|url=https://babys.name/detail-Tadhg-Meaning-Origin-Famous-Similar-Sibling.html|access-date=2023-03-26|website=babys.name}} < /ref >    
 
 Tadhg was a very popular name when the Goidelic languages predominated. It was also the name of many Gaelic Irish kings and Irish celebrities through out the centuries centuries.But this is a unique name (not in the top 1000 newborn names in the US in 2021). < ref > {{Cite web|last=OACT|title=Popular Baby Names|url=https://www.ssa.gov/cgi-bin/popularnames.cgi|access-date=2023-03-26|website=www.ssa.gov}} < /ref >   
 
 == Pronunciation == 
 Pronounced /taɪɡ/ (similarly to the first syllable of tiger). 
 
 == Popularity == 
 Tadhg was the 40th most common name for baby boys in Ireland in 2010, according to the Central Statistics Office in Ireland.
