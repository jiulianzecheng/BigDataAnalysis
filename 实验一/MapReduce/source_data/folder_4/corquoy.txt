 '''Corquoy''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]] in [[Cher (department)|Cher]], [[Centre-Val de Loire]], [[France]]. The former commune [[Sainte-Lunaise]] was merged into Corquoy on 1 January 2019. < ref > [http://www.cher.gouv.fr/content/download/20895/147822/file/recueil-18-2018-10-001-recueil-des-actes-administratifs.pdf Arrêté préfectoral] 27 September 2018 {{in lang|fr}}, p. 260 < /ref >  About 203 people lived there in January 2019.
