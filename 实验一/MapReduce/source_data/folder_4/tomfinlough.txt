'''Tomfinlough''' is a civil parish in [[County Clare]], in [[Republic of Ireland|Ireland]]. There is a [[Ruins|ruined]] parish church at the northern end of a [[lake]], called [[Finn Lough (Clare)|Finn Lough]], or Fenloe. Tomfinlogh is the oldest known settlement in the region of [[Newmarket-on-Fergus]]. It contains the [[village]] of [[Ardsallis]]. The parish contains the following [[Townland|townlands]]: Ayleacotty, Ballycar, Boheraroan, Caherkine, Caherscooby, Carrowmeer, Carrownakilly, Finlough, Granaghan, Granaghan Beg,  Granaghan More, Kilnacrandy, Langough, Mausnarylaan, Mooghaun North, Mooghaun South, Muckanagh (Butler), Muckanagh (Studdert), Muckanagh (Vandeleur), Newmarket, Rathlaheen North, Rathlaheen South and Snugborough.
