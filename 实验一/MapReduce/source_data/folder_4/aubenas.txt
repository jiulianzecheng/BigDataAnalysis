 '''Aubenas''' is a [[city]] and [[commune in France|commune]] in the southern part of the [[Ardèche]] ''[[département in France|département]]''.  The [[Ardèche River]] flows through the commune. 
 
 ==Twin towns== 
 Aubenas is twinned with: 
 * {{flagicon|Italy}} [[Cesenatico]], [[Italy]] 
 * {{flagicon|Netherlands}} [[Delfzijl]], [[Netherlands]] 
 * {{flagicon|Germany}} [[Schwarzenbeck]], [[Germany]] 
 * {{flagicon|Switzerland}} [[Sierre]], [[Switzerland]] 
 * {{flagicon|Belgium}} [[Zelzate]], [[Belgium]] 
 * {{flagicon|Spain}} [[Palamos]], [[Spain]] (friendship agreement)
