 '''Kirill Aleksandrovich Tolmatsky''' ({{lang-ru|Кири́лл Алекса́ндрович Толмацкий}}; 22 July 1983 – 3 February 2019 < ref name= " iz " > [https://iz.ru/841330/2019-02-03/agent-rasskazal-podrobnosti-smerti-detcla Агент назвал причину смерти Децла] {{in lang|ru}} < /ref > ), better known by his stage name '''Detsl''' (Russian: Децл), was a Russian [[Hip hop music|hip hop]] artist. In 2000, he released his debut album ''Who? You'' to massive success and quickly became a [[teen idol]] of the early 2000s in Russia. < ref > [https://www.bbc.com/russian/features-47107349 Путь Децла: от «Вечеринки» до борьбы с мировым заговором] < /ref >  In 2001, his second album ''Street Fighter'' was released. 
 
 He died on 3 February 2019 in [[Izhevsk]] at the age of 35 from a sudden [[heart attack]] after performing his live set at the private birthday party. < ref name= " iz " / >  In a 2007 interview, Detsl talked about [[faked death|faking]] his own death at the exact same age while performing at a concert in the same city he would actually die in. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://fanlife.ru/reviews/351|title= Интервью с Кириллом Толмацким|website=fanlife.ru}} < /ref >
