 '''Oaxaca''' is a state in [[Mexico]]. The [[capital city|capital]] is [[Oaxaca de Juárez]]. Oaxaca is next to [[Guerrero (state)|Guerrero]], [[Puebla (state)|Puebla]], [[Veracruz]], and [[Chiapas]]. About 3.5 million people live there. The [[Zapotec people|Zapotec]] and the [[Mixtec]] people live in the state.  There are more speakers of indigenous languages in this state than in any other state of Mexico. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.houstonculture.org/hispanic/census.html|title=The Hispanic Experience - Indigenous Identity in Mexico|publisher=Houston Institute for Culture|author=Schmal, John|year=2004|accessdate=2007-05-24|archive-date=2009-08-04|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20090804090647/http://www.houstonculture.org/hispanic/census.html|url-status=dead}} < /ref >  This Mexican state is known for it's diverse culture and it's native people.
