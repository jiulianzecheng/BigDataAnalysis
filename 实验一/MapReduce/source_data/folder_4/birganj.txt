 '''Birgunj''' is a metropolitan city and border town in [[Parsa District]] in the [[Narayani Zone]] of southern [[Nepal]].As an entry point to Nepal from Patna and Kolkata, Birgunj is known as the  " Gateway to Nepal " . It is also called  " Commercial capital of Nepal " . The town has significant economic importance for Nepal as most of the trade with India is via Birgunj and the Indian town of Raxaul.
