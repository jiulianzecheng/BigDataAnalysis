 A '''resort''' is a [[commercial]] place that tries to give a [[vacation]]er's wants, such as food, drink, a place to stay, [[sport]]s, [[entertainment]], and [[shopping]], in its place. < ref name= " Garrett Nagle 1999 " > {{cite book|title=Tourism, Leisure and Recreation|url=https://archive.org/details/tourismleisurere0000nagl|author=Garrett Nagle|year= 1999|publisher=Nelson Thornes|isbn=0-17-444705-1}} < /ref >  The word ''resort'' may be used for a [[hotel]] place. Some resorts are also [[condominium]] places. A resort is not always a commercial place owned by a single company, but in the late 20th century, that became more common. < ref > {{cite news |title= American Plan resorts among last of vanishing breed |url= http://www.cnn.com/2007/TRAVEL/06/28/american.plan.ap/index.html|agency=[[Associated Press]] |publisher=CNN.com |date=June 28, 2007 |access-date= 2007-06-29 |archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20070629164129/http://www6.cnn.com/2007/TRAVEL/06/28/american.plan.ap/index.html  < !-- Bot retrieved archive -- >  |archive-date = 2007-06-29}} < /ref > 
 
 In [[British English]],  " resort "  means a town which people visit for holidays.
