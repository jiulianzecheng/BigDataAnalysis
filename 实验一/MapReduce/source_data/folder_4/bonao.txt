 '''Bonao''' is a [[Dominican Republic|Dominican]] [[city]] and the head [[municipality]] of the [[Monseñor Nouel Province|Monseñor Nouel]] [[province]], in the center of the country. 
 
 ''Bonao'' is the [[Taíno]] name of the region where the old city was founded. < ref > {{cite book 
   | last =Tolentino Rojas 
   | first =Vicente 
   | title =Historia de la División Territorial, 1494-1943 
   | publisher =Editorial El Diario 
   | date =1944 
   | location = Santo Domingo, Dominican Republic 
   | language = es }} < /ref >  The city is in the Bonao [[Valley]], at about 85 [[Kilometre|km]] to the northwest of [[Santo Domingo]], the [[Capital (city)|capital]] of the [[Dominican Republic]]. 
 
 ==Population== 
 The municipality had, in {{Metadata Population Dominican province|YEAR}}, a total population of 76,241: 37,349 men and 38,892 women. The urban population was {{percentage|68602|76241|1 }} of the total population. < ref name= " r-pop "  / > 
 
 ==History== 
 [[Christopher Columbus]] founded in 1494 a small [[Fortification|fort]] in the region known by the Taíno people as Bonao. The fort was built to protect the [[gold]] [[Mining|mines]] around Maimón and other places. But there was not much gold in the region and the fort was soon left without people. 
 
 The town was founded again during the early years of the 19th [[century]] but at about {{Convert|5|km|mi|abbr=on }} to the north of the old place, closer to the River Yuna. The new town had the name ''Yuna'' was soon was changed to the old one: ''Bonao''. It was also called ''El Bonao'' ([[English language|English]]:  " The Bonao " ) but the [[article (grammar)|article]]  " El "  (English:  " The " ) was left out during the first years of the 20th century and it is never used now. 
 
 The new small town was made a ''Puesto Cantonal'' (and old category, like a  " [[Military]] Post " ) in 1859 and then a municipality of [[La Vega Province|La Vega]] province in 1865. 
 
 In 1936, its name was changed to ''Monseñor Meriño'' after the [[Archbishop]] Adolfo A. Nouel, past president of the country. In 1960, it got is old name of Bonao. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 Bonao is in the Bonao Valley in the centre of the Dominican Republic. It has a total area of {{Convert|142.8|km2|sqmi|1|abbr=on }}. It has five municipal districts (a municipal district is a subdivision of a municipality); these are Arroyo Toro-Masipedro, Jayaco, Juma Bejucal, La Salvia-Los Quemados and Sabana del Puerto. < ref name= " division16 " > {{Cite web |url=http://www.one.gob.do/Multimedia/Download?ObjId=6159 |format=PDF |title=División Territorial 2015 |publisher=Oficina Nacional de Estadística (ONE) |language=es |date=October 2015 |access-date=11 November 2016 |archive-date=14 November 2015 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20151114105747/http://www.one.gob.do/Multimedia/Download?ObjId=6159 |url-status=dead }} < /ref > 
 
 The [[altitude]] of the city of Bonao is {{Convert|83|m|ft|0|abbr=on }} [[above sea level]]. < ref name= " fuente "  / > 
 
 The municipality of Bonao has the [[La Vega Province|La Vega]] province to the north and to the west, the [[Sánchez Ramírez Province|Sánchez Ramírez]] province to the east, the municipality of Maimón to the southeast, the municipality of Piedra Blanc to the south and the province of [[San José de Ocoa Province|San José de Ocoa]] province to the southwest. 
 
 The city is near the River Yuna, one of the most important [[river]] of the [[Hispaniola]] [[island]]; the [[Source (river or stream)|source]]s of the river are on the eastern side of the ''Cordillera Central'' ( " Central [[mountain range]] " ). It flows through the Bonao Valley and then through the Cibao Valley and into the [[Samaná Bay]]. 
 
 There are other smaller rivers near Bonao, [[Tributary|tributaries]] of the River Yuna. 
 
 ===Climate=== 
 Bonao has a [[tropical]] wet [[climate]] (Köppen-Geiger classification: '''Af''') with no dry or cold season as it is constantly moist ([[rainfall]]s during the whole year). 
 
 The [[average]] amount of [[rainfall]] for the year in the city is {{Convert|2216.2|mm|in|1|abbr=on}}. The month with the most precipitation on average is May with {{Convert|333.3|mm|in|1|abbr=on}} of [[rainfall]], followed by October with {{Convert|251.8|mm|in|1|abbr=on}}. 
 
 The driest [[season]] is [[winter]]. The month with the least rainfall on average is January with an average of {{Convert|107.2|mm|in|1|abbr=on}} followed by February with an average of {{Convert|108.3|mm|in|1|abbr=on}}.

 ==Economy== 
 [[Farming]] is very important around the city; it is one of main regions of the country where [[rice]] is grown. 
 
 [[Mining]] is also important here; [[nickel]] is mined in a mountain close to the city by the Falconbridge, a [[Canada|Canadian]] [[company]].
