 '''Arbutus''' is a genus of [[flowering plants]] in the [[Ericaceae]] family. There are 12 [[species]], which mostly grow around the [[Mediterranean]], and in [[Central America]]. The [[type species]] is Arbutus unedo, which is known as [[strawberry tree]] in English. All species are either [[Tree|trees]] or [[Shrub|shrubs]]. 
 
 Certain [[Lepidoptera]] species use Arbutus as [[food]]. Some species are cultivated as [[ornamental plant]] < nowiki/ > s outside their range.
