 '''VXON''' (pronounced as Vision; stylized in all caps) is a [[Filipinos|Filipino]] [[Boy band|boy group]] under Cornerstone Entertainment. They debuted in 2022 composed of five members C13, Franz, Patrick, Vince and Sam. On January 7, 2022, they released their first [[Single (music)|single]]  " ''The Beast''.  == Name == 
 VXON is pronounced as  " Vision " . The name literally means that the group of 5 individuals work together to fulfill the same set of goals and passions - they have the same vision of what they should do. The letters in the group name also have a meaning to the group. The letter  " V "  is the [[Roman numerals|roman numeral]] for  " five " . It is for the 5 members of the group. The  " X "  stands for their different backgrounds, crossing of paths, and forming a group through a single vision. < ref > {{Cite web|last=Garcia|first=Raphael|date=2022-01-22|title=VP/Spotlight: VXON, The Monster Rookies of Philippine Pop|url=https://villagepipol.com/vp-spotlight-vxon-the-monster-rookies-of-philippine-pop/|access-date=2023-01-22|website=Your Guide to the Big City|language=en-US}} < /ref >  The  " ON "  signifies what the members do before they perform. Whenever they feel nervous or whatever they are feeling on that moment, they remind themselves to  " switch on "  before they set foot on the stage and do their best to perform. < ref > {{Citation|title=WATCH: VXON Answering Your Twitter Questions!|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=oeMSgfvjYj8|language=en|access-date=2023-01-22}} < /ref >

 ==== P-pop Fandom Festival feat. VXON ==== 
 The group held its first ever fan meeting on October 2, 2023 at Eton Centris. It was a whole-day event with fandom booths, games, contests, and random play dance. The group also performed their songs and culminated the event with a meet and greet with fans. Cornerstone Entertainment artists' G22 and Mark Oblea also performed in the event. 
 
 === 2023: First Anniversary / Monsters of Philippine Pop === 
 The group celebrated its First Anniversary in the industry on January 7, 2023. In this month, VXON held two major concerts in Dubai and the Philippines. 
 
 ==== SEE THE VXON Anniversary Concerts ==== 
 VXON went to Dubai the 2nd time to held its SEE THE VXON in Dubai Concert on January 21, 2023 at the Dubai Media City. They also had a press conference and a meet and greet event with the fans. 
 
 On January 29, 2023, the group had its 1st Anniversary concert in the Philippines on January 29, 2023 at the Ayala Malls Trinoma Activity Center. There were also special performances from CS entertainment artists' Zephanie, G22, and upcoming boy groups, AJAA and Yes My Love. Highlights of the concert include solo and sub-unit performances of the members and a live performance of the song  " Ikaw Na Na Na "  with Zephanie. It was also the first time that leader of the group, C13, performed his solo single, STAY, in public. 
 
 ==== Dubai Victor Magazine : First International Magazine feature ==== 
 While in Dubai for a concert, VXON also had a shoot for the February issue of the Dubai lifestyle magazine, Victor.
