 '''Faulbach''' is a [[Municipalities of Germany|municipality]] in the [[Miltenberg (district)|Miltenberg]] [[Districts of Germany|district]] in the [[Lower Franconia]] [[Regierungsbezirk|region]] in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. About 2,600 people live in Faulbach. 
 
 == Geography == 
 
 === Place === 
 Faulbach is on the right edge of the river [[Main (river)|Main]], between [[Wertheim am Main]] and [[Miltenberg]], where it is joined by the [[Faulbach (Main)|Faulbach]]. Around the municipality are the hills of the [[Spessart]]. At Faulbach, the Main is the border between the states of Bavaria and [[Baden-Württemberg]]. 
 
 === Subdivision === 
 The community has the following districts: Breitenbrunn, Faulbach.
