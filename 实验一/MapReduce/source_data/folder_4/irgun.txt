The '''Irgun''' ({{lang-he|ארגון}}), (full title: Irgun Zevai Leumi) (from {{lang-he|הארגון הצבאי הלאומי בארץ ישראל}},  " The National Military Organization in the Land of Israel " ), was a [[Zionism|Zionist]] [[paramilitary]] group in [[Palestine]] between 1931 and 1948. < ref name= " britannica " > [http://www.britannica.com/EBchecked/topic/293947/Irgun-Zvai-Leumi  " Irgun Zvai Leumi " ], ''[[Encyclopædia Britannica]]''; retrieved 2012-5-31. < /ref > 
 
 ==History== 
 [[File:Irgun poster Erez Jisrael.jpg|thumb|right|Irgun poster, 1931]] 
 Irgun is best known for the bombing of the [[King David Hotel]] in [[Jerusalem]] in 1946. < ref name= " britannica " / > 
 
 In the 1940s, Irgun was labelled as a terrorist organization. < ref name= " britannica " / > 
 
 ==Legacy== 
 Irgun is considered an early version of [[Ḥerut]] which was an Israeli political party. < ref name= " britannica " / >   Irgun is also an early version of today's [[Likud]] political party in Israel. < ref > Eisenstadt, S.N. (1985). ''The Transformation of Israeli Society,'' pp. 173–174. < /ref >
