 '''Zakynthos''' (or ''Zante'') is a [[Greece|Greek]] island. It is the third largest island in the [[Ionian Sea]]. It is in the south Ionian Sea, west of the [[Peloponnese]]. Zakynthos' total population amounts to 41,472 inhabitants. The largest town of Zakynthos is [[Zakynthos (city)|Zakynthos city]]. Zakynthos is mostly flat island, with an area of 410 square kilometres.To the north of the island, there are low mountains and hills.  
 
 ==Sights and tourism== 
 [[File:Zakintos - panorama.jpg|thumb|250px|Town of Zakyintos]] 
 Zakynthos is a very [[Tourism|touristic]] island. Most tourists who visit the island come from [[Western Europe]], mainly [[Italy]] and [[England]]. < ref > [http://news.bbc.co.uk/2/hi/uk_news/4189154.stm BBC, English tourists in Zakynthos] < /ref >  The most popular part of island is the southern part. The island has many beautiful [[Beach|beaches]], the most famous including the Navagio (shipwreck) < ref > [http://www.trekearth.com/gallery/Europe/Greece/Ionian_Islands/Zakynthos/photo525617.htm The shipwreck beach in Zakynthos] < /ref >  and the beach of Laganas. The Mediterranean [[turtle]] lives in and around Zakynthos. It is also called ''careta careta''. This species is very rare and it is a [[threatened species]].
