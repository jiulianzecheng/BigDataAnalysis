 The '''Dinar''' is the official [[currency]] in many countries. It is used in many countries near the [[Mediterranean Sea]]. The word ''dinar'' ({{lang-ar|دينار}}, {{lang-sr|динар / dinar}}, {{lang-mk|денар}}) is derived from [[denarius]], a [[Roman Empire|Roman]] currency.
