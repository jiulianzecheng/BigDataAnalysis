'''Biromanticism''' is a [[romantic orientation]]. A biromantic person has [[Romance (love)|romantic]] attraction to more than one [[gender]]. Biromantic people can have any [[sexual orientation]]. The term biromantic is useful for [[asexual]] people to describe their [[affection]].
