 '''Chandolin''' is a village in the  municipality of [[Anniviers]] in the [[district]] of [[Sierre (district)|Sierre]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Valais]] in [[Switzerland]]. Until 2009 it was a municipality. 
 
 ==History== 
 On 1 January 2009, the former municipalities of Chandolin, [[Ayer, Switzerland|Ayer]], [[Grimentz]], [[Saint-Jean, Switzerland|Saint-Jean]], [[Saint-Luc, Switzerland|Saint-Luc]] and [[Vissoie]] merged into the new municipality of [[Anniviers]]. 
 
 ==Culture== 
 It has at least one museum: the Ella Maillart Museum previously was the home of travel writer [[Ella Maillart]] [[:en:Ella Maillart|(en:)]].
