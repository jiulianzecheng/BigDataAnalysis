 '''Dagen''' is a [[Christianity|Christian]] daily newspaper in [[Sweden]]. The first issue was published on 1 November 1945. [[Lewi Pethrus]], leader of the [[pentecostalism in Sweden|pentecostal movement in Sweden]] was its founder and its chief editor until his death in 1974. 
 
 == Circulation ==
