 '''Novarupta''' (meaning  " newly erupted " < ref name= " NPSDeriv " > {{cite web | url=http://www.nps.gov/katm/planyourvisit/upload/Hiking-VTTS.pdf | title=Katmai: Hiking the Valley of Ten Thousand Smokes | publisher=[[National Park Service]] | access-date=February 17, 2016 | archive-date=April 27, 2016 | archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160427044519/https://www.nps.gov/katm/planyourvisit/upload/Hiking-VTTS.pdf | url-status=dead }} < /ref >  in [[Latin]]) is a [[volcano]] that was formed in 1912.  It is located on the [[Alaska Peninsula]] in [[Katmai National Park and Preserve]], about {{convert|290|mi|km|sigfig=2}} southwest of [[Anchorage, Alaska|Anchorage]]. Formed during the largest volcanic eruption of the [[20th century]], Novarupta released 30 times the volume of [[magma]] of the [[1980 eruption of Mount St. Helens]] and had a [[Volcanic Explosivity Index]] (VEI) of 6.
