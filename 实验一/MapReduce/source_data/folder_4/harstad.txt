 '''Harstad''' ({{lang-se|Hárstták}}) is the second-most populated [[List of municipalities of Norway|municipality]] in [[Troms og Finnmark]] [[Counties of Norway|county]], [[Norway]]. (From 2024, it will be part of [[Troms]] county.) It is on the large island of [[Hinnøya]]. The town was founded in 1904.
