 In [[Ancient Greece|Ancient Greek mytholoy]] '''Ceto''' is the name of a sea monster;often represented as a [[whale]], or a large [[fish]]. She is the daughter of [[Pontos]] and [[Gaia (mythology)|Gaia]]. Together with her brother [[Phorcys]] she bore the [[Gorgon]]s, and [[Graeae]], [[Echidna (mythology)|Echidna]] and [[Ladon (mythology)|Ladon]]. 
 
 Note there is also a minor [[oceanid]] called [[Keto (oceanid)|Keto]].
