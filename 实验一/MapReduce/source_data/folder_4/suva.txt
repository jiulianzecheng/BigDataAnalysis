 '''Suva''' is the [[capital city]] of [[Fiji]]. It is on the southeast coast of the island of [[Viti Levu]]. Suva became the capital of Fiji in [[1877]]. It was changed from the settlement of [[Kaivalagi]] on the island of [[Ovalau]] because that settlement had no space left to grow. Kaivalangi had been established by the [[Europe|Europeans]]. In [[1996]], 77,366 people lived in Suva. Including independent suburbs, 167,975 people lived in the Greater Suva city area.
