 '''Piding''' is a climatic spa in [[Berchtesgadener Land]], a district ''[[Districts of Germany|(Landkreis)]]'' in [[Upper Bavaria]]. It is near to the border of [[Austria]] close to [[Bad Reichenhall]] and [[Freilassing]].
