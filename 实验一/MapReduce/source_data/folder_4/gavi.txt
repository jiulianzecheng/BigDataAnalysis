 '''Gavi''' is a village in [[Pathanamthitta district]], [[Kerala]], [[India]]. it is famous for its [[biodiversity]] and [[Rainforest|rain forests]]. < ref > [http://www.gavi.kfdcecotourism.com/ About Gavi] < /ref >

 == Plants and animals == 
 [[File:Mushrooms on grassland.jpg|thumb|Mushrooms in Gavi]] 
 Gavi is consisting of [[evergreen forests]]. There are somany animals such as [[tiger]], [[Asian elephant|elephants]], [[leopard]]s, [[bear]]s, Indian [[gaur]], [[sambar (deer)|sambar]], [[barking deer|barking]] and [[chevrotain|mouse deers]], [[lion tailed macaque]], [[Nilgiri langur]], [[Nilgiri marten]], [[Malabar giant squirrel]]  and more than 250 species of birds can be seen there.
