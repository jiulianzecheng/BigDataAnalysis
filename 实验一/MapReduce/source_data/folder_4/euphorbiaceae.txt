 The [[spurge]] family ('''Euphorbiaceae''') is a large family of [[flowering plant]]s with 300 genera and around 7,500 species. Most are herbs. Some, especially in the [[Tropical|tropics]], are [[shrub]]s or [[tree]]s.  
 
 The name ''spurge'' comes from the [[Latin]] word ''espurge'' meaning to [[purge]]: this refers to its use as a [[laxative]].
