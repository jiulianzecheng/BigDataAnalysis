 The '''springbok''' (''Antidorcas marsupialis'') is a medium sized brown and white [[gazelle]] that stands about {{convert|75|cm}} high. It is in the same family as [[antelope]] and [[goat]]s. They are the national animal of [[South Africa]] and live in the [[Savannah|Savannah Biome]].
