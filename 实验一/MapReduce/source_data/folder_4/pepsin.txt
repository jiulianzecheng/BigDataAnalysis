'''Pepsin''' is an [[enzyme]]. It acts on proteins in the [[stomach]], which has a low [[pH]] because of the [[Hydrochloric acid]]. It works best at a pH of about 1.5. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.worthington-biochem.com/introbiochem/effectspH.html|title=Effects of pH|accessdate=2010-04-29}} < /ref >  It will not work at a pH above 6.  
 
 It was the first animal enzyme to be discovered. [[Theodor Schwann]] discovered it in [[1836]]. 
 
 Pepsin is used to make [[cheese]].
