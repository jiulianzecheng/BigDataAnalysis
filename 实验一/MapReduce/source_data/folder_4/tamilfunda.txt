 '''Tamil Funda''' is an Indian website. It covers topics ranging from the [[Tamil Nadu|Tamil]] culture to the topics loosely related to Tamil audience. 
 
 ==History== 
 Founded on October 22,  2013. It publish content exclusive for the Tamil audience living all over the world. The editorial team publishes the content. 
 
 ==Content== 
 The content published by TamilFunda are available in English and in Tamil.
