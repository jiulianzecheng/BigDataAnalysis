 '''Bhadarwahi''' is a language spoken in the [[Bhaderwah Tehsil|Bhadarwah]] region of [[Jammu and Kashmir (union territory)|Jammu and Kashmir]], [[India]].  It is a [[Indo-Aryan languages]] from the [[Western Pahari languages]].  There are also some speakers in [[Pakistan]].
