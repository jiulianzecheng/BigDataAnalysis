 '''Dagmersellen''' is a [[municipality]] of the [[district]] of [[Willisau (district)|Willisau]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Lucerne]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 On 1 January 2006, the former municipalities of [[Buchs, Lucerne|Buchs]] and [[Uffikon]] merged into the municipality of Dagmersellen.
