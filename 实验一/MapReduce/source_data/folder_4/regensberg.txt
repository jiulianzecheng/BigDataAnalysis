 '''Regensberg''' is a [[municipality]] in the [[district]] of [[Dielsdorf (district)|Dielsdorf]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Zurich (canton)|Zurich]] in [[Switzerland]]. It is located to the west of [[Dielsdorf]], on a ridge of the [[Lägern]] ({{convert|617|m|ft|abbr=off}}).
