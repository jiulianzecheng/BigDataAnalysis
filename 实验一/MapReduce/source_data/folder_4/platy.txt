The '''platy''' fish is a small [[tropical fish]] that is found naturally in the [[warm]] waters of [[America]]. Today, platys are very popular as [[pet]]s. 
 
 Platys are small fish. They grow to 2.5 [[centimeters]]. Their come from America. They live there in [[creeks]], [[canal]]s, [[ditch]]es and [[swamp]]s. These [[habitat]]s have lots of plants, stones and branches to hide in. Platys like a tank with live plants on the sides and back. They hide in the plants. They also eat them. Their tank should have good lighting and lots of open area in the center for swimming. 
 
 == Food == 
 Platy will eat almost anything that fits into its mouth. As pets, they eat can fish flakes. They can also eat frozen or live food like daphnia or bloodworms. In the wild platy fish will eat algae and plants.
