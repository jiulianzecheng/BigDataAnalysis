 In some countries, like the [[United States|United States of America]], '''Thursday''' is the fifth day of the [[week]]. In other parts of the world Thursday is said to be the fourth day of the week. It comes after [[Wednesday]] and before [[Friday]]. Thursday is named after the [[Norse]] god [[Thor]].
