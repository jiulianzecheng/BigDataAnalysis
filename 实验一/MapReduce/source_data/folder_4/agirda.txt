 '''Agirda''' ({{lang-el|Αγύρτα}}; {{lang-tr|Ağırdağ}}) is a village in the [[Kyrenia District]] of [[Cyprus]].  It is under the [[de facto]] control of [[Northern Cyprus]]. < ref > {{citation|url=http://www.prio-cyprus-displacement.net/default.asp?id=425|title=AGIRDA|website=Internal Displacement in Cyprus|publisher=[[PRIO|PRIO Cyprus Centre]]|accessdate=4 October 2014}} < /ref >  Its population in 2011 was 745. < ref name= " pop-2011 " / >
