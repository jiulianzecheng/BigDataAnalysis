 The [[Sundarbans National Park]] is a [[National parks of India|National Park]], [[Tiger reserves of India|Tiger Reserve]], and a [[biosphere reserve]].  It is in the Sundarbans [[River delta|delta]] in the Indian state of [[West Bengal]]. This region is covered by [[mangrove]] [[forest]]s.  It is one of the largest reserves for the [[Bengal tiger]].
