 '''Devanāgarī''' (देवनागरी) is an [[abugida]] and brahmic script used to write many [[North India]]n languages, including [[Sanskrit]], [[Hindi]], [[Marathi language|Marathi]], [[Bihari languages|Bihari]], [[Bhili language|Bhili]], [[Konkani language|Konkani]], [[Bhojpuri language|Bhojpuri]], [[Nepal Bhasa]] and [[Nepali language|Nepali]] from [[Nepal]] and sometimes [[Kashmiri language|Kashmiri]] or [[Sindhi language|Sindhi]]. About a hundred smaller languages also use Devanāgarī.
