 '''Cabbage''' (''[[Brassica oleracea]]'' Capitata Group) is an [[wikt:edible|edible]] [[plant]]. It is a lot like [[broccoli]], [[cauliflower]] or [[Brussels sprout|Brussels sprouts]]. Cabbage is eaten in many ways around the world. [[Coleslaw]] and [[sauerkraut]] are popular foods that use cabbage. It is also used to make [[kimchi]] < ref > {{Cite web|title=kimchi {{!}} Ingredients  &  Facts|url=https://www.britannica.com/topic/kimchi|access-date=2021-04-13|website=Encyclopedia Britannica|language=en}} < /ref >  and [[borscht]], and can also be used in [[salad|salads]]. Red cabbage juice can be used as a [[pH]] indicator. Cabbages are usually [[green]], but they can also be [[red]]. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Discover Four Different Cabbage Varieties and Their Uses|url=https://www.thespruceeats.com/varieties-of-cabbage-1808038|access-date=2021-04-13|website=The Spruce Eats|language=en}} < /ref > 
 
 ==Types of cabbage== 
 
 Cabbages can come in many different types, such as the previously mentioned red and green cabbages, but they can also be in other types. These include [[white]] cabbage, also known as [[Netherlands|Dutch]] cabbage, and Savoy cabbage.
