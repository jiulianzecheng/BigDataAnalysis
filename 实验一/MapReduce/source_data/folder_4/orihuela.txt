 '''Orihuela''' is a city on the coast in [[Spain]] in the [[Alicante province]], on the [[Segura]] river in a district called ''Vega Baja''. [[Orange (fruit)|Oranges]] and [[lemon]]s are grown. The city has a population of 75,000 with very important historical monuments like the [[Cathedral]] and the church of Santiago, the church of Santa Justa and Rufina and the Seminarium.
