 '''Ottershaw''' is a [[village]] in the [[Runnymede]] district of [[Surrey]], [[England]]. It is about {{convert|20|mi}} [[southwest]] of [[London]]. Ottershaw is part of the mixed rural and suburban Foxhills [[Wards and electoral divisions of the United Kingdom|ward]]. It is part of the [[parish]] of Ottershaw. This parish was first established in 1864 in what was part of the original parish of [[Chertsey]]. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://www.ottershawchurch.com/#/our-history/4536133748 |title=Our History |author= < !--Staff writer(s)-- >  |date=n.d. |website= |publisher=Christ Church Ottershaw |accessdate=11 December 2014}} < /ref >  This is when the name 'Ottershaw' came into use. < ref name=CM > {{cite web |url=http://chertseymuseum.org/ottershaw |title=Ottershaw |author= < !--Staff writer(s)-- >  |date= |website= |publisher=Chertsey Museum |accessdate=11 December 2014}} < /ref >  Before that it was a part of Chertsey Common, which was part of [[Windsor Forest]]. < ref name=CM/ >
