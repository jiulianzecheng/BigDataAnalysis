 '''Naveen Kumar Gowda''', < ref > {{Cite news|last=TNN|date=27 January 2014|title=Who is Naveen Kumar Gowda in Sandalwood?|work=The Times of India|url=https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/entertainment/kannada/movies/did-you-know/Yashs-real-name-revelaed/articleshow/29442760.cms}} < /ref >  popularly known by his screen name '''Yash''', is one of the highest paid and most soughtest stars of the [[Telugu cinema|Kannada film]] industry. 
 
 ==Career== 
 He started his career by acting in few Television serials like ''Gokula'', ''Malebillu'', and ''Preeti Illada Mele''. In 2007, Yash made his on-screen debut with ''Jambada Hudugi''. He won the Filmfare Award for the Best Supporting Actor for his next outing ''Moggina Manasu'' which was well received both by the audience and critics. After that, he appeared in Rocky, his first title role, but the film failed to leave an impact.
