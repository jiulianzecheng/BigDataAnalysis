 '''Pindar''' ({{IPAc-en|ˈ|p|ɪ|n|d|ər}}; {{lang-grc-gre|Πίνδαρος}} {{Lang|grc-Latn|Pindaros}}, {{IPA-el|píndaros|}}; {{lang-la|Pindarus}}; c. 518 – 438 BC) was an [[ancient Greek]] poet of [[Thebes, Greece|Thebes]]. He was born in [[Boeotia]], around 518 BC. He died in Argos, at age 80 in 438 BC. He is known for his [[ode]]s in praise of [[Sportsperson|athletes]] and their accomplishments but he wrote poems in many other forms as well.
