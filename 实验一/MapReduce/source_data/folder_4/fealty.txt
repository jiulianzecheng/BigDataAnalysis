{{English Feudalism}} 
 An '''oath of fealty''' is a pledge of allegiance of one person to another. 
 
 In [[medieval]] [[Europe]], the [[swearing]] of fealty is an oath made by a peasant to his [[lord]].  " Fealty "  also referred to the duties that a servant need to perform. These includes service and help. < ref name=Coredon120 > Coredon ''A Dictionary of Medieval Terms and Phrases'' p. 120 < /ref >
