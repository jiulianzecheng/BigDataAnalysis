 '''Abetone''' was a ''[[comune]]'' (municipality) in the [[Province of Pistoia]] in the [[Italy|Italian]] region of [[Tuscany]]. It was about {{convert|80|km|mi}} northwest of [[Florence]] and about {{convert|49|km|mi}} northwest of [[Pistoia]]. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Main sights in Abetone|url=https://tuscanyplanet.com/en/abetone-tuscany/|archive-date=9 July 2022|access-date=July 17, 2022|website=Tuscany Planet}} < /ref >  It has been a [[frazione]] of [[Abetone Cutigliano]] since 2017.
