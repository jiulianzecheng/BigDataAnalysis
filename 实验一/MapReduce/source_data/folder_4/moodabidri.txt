'''Moodabidri''' is a town in [[India]]. It is in the [[Dakshina Kannada]] district of [[Karnataka]] state. It is 37 & nbsp;km away from the district headquarters, [[Mangalore]]. People there speak [[Tulu]], [[Konkani]] and [[Kannada]]. This place consist of many notable tourist spots like Thousand Pillar Temple , Kadalakere Nisarga Dhama, Hanuman Temple, Soans farm, Butterfly park, Beluvai, Satynarayana temple etc. This place is famous for its heritage and monuments . Many jain basadi's (temples of Jain religion) are the places to visit.
