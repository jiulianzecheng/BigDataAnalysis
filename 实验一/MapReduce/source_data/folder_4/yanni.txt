 '''Yiannis Chryssomallis''' ({{lang-el|Γιάννης Χρυσομάλλης}}, born November 14, 1954), known professionally as '''Yanni''' ({{audio|En-Yanni.oga|pronunciation; |help=no}} {{IPAc-en|ˈ|j|ɑː|n|i|}}), is a Greek [[composer]], [[keyboardist]], [[pianist]], and [[record producer|music producer]]. He was born in [[Kalamata]], [[Greece]]. < ref name=AutobiographyP13 > {{cite book |last1=Yanni |last2=Rensin |first2=David |title=Yanni in Words |url=https://archive.org/details/yanniinwords00yann |url-access=registration |date=2002 |publisher=Miramax Books / Hyperion |location=New York |page=[https://archive.org/details/yanniinwords00yann/page/13 13]|isbn=9781401351946 }} < /ref > 
 
 At least sixteen of Yanni's albums have peaked at No. 1 in ''Billboard's''  " Top New Age Album "  category, and two albums (''[[Dare to Dream (Yanni album)|Dare to Dream]]'' and ''[[In My Time]]'') received [[Grammy Award]] nominations. < ref name=AllMusicBio > Deming, Mark, [http://www.allmusic.com/artist/yanni-mn0000687933/biography  " Yanni "  /  " Biography " ] ([https://web.archive.org/web/20120313161722/http://www.allmusic.com/artist/yanni-p2850/biography WebCite archive]), [[Allmusic]], written in 2011 or in 2012 before archive date of April 29, 2012. < /ref >  His career began in 1980.
