 '''Ursberg''' is a [[Municipalities of Germany|municipality]] in [[Günzburg (district)|Günzburg]] in [[Bavaria]] in [[Germany]]. 
 
 Nearby is [[Ursberg Abbey]], a former [[Imperial Abbey]] of the [[Holy Roman Empire]].
