Ruku (Arabic: رُكوع) is an Arabic word that means two things in Islamic terminology: 
 
 * A part of prayers; After the recitation of the prayers, the head is bowed and the knees are bowed with both hands, which is one of the essential parts of the prayers. < ref > {{Cite web|title=BBC - Religion  &  Ethics - In pictures: Muslim prayer movements|url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/religion/galleries/salah/|access-date=2021-09-19|website=www.bbc.co.uk}} < /ref > 
 * A part of the Qur'an; In the Qur'an, each paragraph is considered as a ruku. 
 
 == Qur'anic use == 
 The word ruku is used in the Qur'an.
