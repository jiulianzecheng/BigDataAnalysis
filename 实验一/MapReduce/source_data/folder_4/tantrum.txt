Sometimes, tantrum can also make a person to hit others and other physically violent behavior. 
 
 Physical control in tantrum may be lost. The person may be unable to keep being still; and even if the purpose of the person is met, they may not be calmed. Throwing a [[temper]] tantrum can lead to a child getting detention or being suspended from school for older school age children.
