 '''Okunino''' {{IPAc-pl|o|k|u|'|n|i|n|o}} ({{lang-csb|Òkònino}}) is a [[village]] in [[Gmina Miastko]], [[Bytów County]], [[Pomeranian Voivodeship]], in northern [[Poland]]. It is about {{convert|22|km|mi|0}} south-west of [[Bytów]] and {{convert|71|km|mi|0|abbr=on}} south-west of [[Gdańsk]] (capital city of the Pomeranian Voivodeship). From 1975 to 1998 the village was in [[Słupsk Voivodeship]].
