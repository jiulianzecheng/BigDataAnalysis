 '''Velluire''' is a former [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is found in the region [[Pays de la Loire]] in the [[Vendée]] department in the west of [[France]]. On 1 January 2019, it became a part of the new commune of [[Les Velluire-sur-Vendée]]. < ref > [https://www.legifrance.gouv.fr/eli/arrete/2018/8/17/INTB1823829A/jo/texte Arrêté préfectoral] 17 August 2018 {{in lang|fr}} < /ref >
