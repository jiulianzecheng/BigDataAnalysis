 '''Terangganu''' ({{IPA-may|tərəŋɡanu}};  < small > [[Jawi script|Jawi]] < /small > :ترڠݢانو,  < small > [[Terengganu Malay]]: Tranung, [[Thai language|Thai]]: ตรังกานู) < /small >  formerly spelled '''Trengganu''' or '''Tringganu''', is a state in [[Malaysia]] on the northeastern part of [[Peninsular Malaysia]]. The capital city of Terengganu is [[Kuala Terengganu]]. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.tourism.gov.my/en/destinations/state.asp?state=terengganu|title=Terengganu: Beach Paradise|work=[[Tourism Malaysia]]|accessdate=October 4, 2010|archive-date=July 31, 2010|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20100731043539/http://www.tourism.gov.my/en/destinations/state.asp?state=terengganu|url-status=dead}} < /ref >
