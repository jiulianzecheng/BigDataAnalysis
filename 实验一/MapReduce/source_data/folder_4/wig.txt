 A '''wig''' is an artificial covering of [[hair]]. The word is short for '''periwig''' and appeared in the English language around 1675. Some people wear wigs to hide the fact that they are [[baldness|bald]], which means they have very little or no scalp hair. [[Actor]]s often wear wigs to disguise their appearance or get into character. In [[Britain]] and other [[Commonwealth of Nations|Commonwealth]] nations, some public employees, such as [[judge]]s and [[barrister]]s, wear wigs.  
 
 Many composers during the 18th century wore wigs.
