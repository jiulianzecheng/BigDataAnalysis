 == History == 
 It was founded in 2008 by Éric Carreel, Cédric Hutchings, and Fred Potter. Withings gained recognition for introducing the first Wi-Fi scale in 2009 and has since developed products such as an FDA-cleared blood pressure monitor, a smart sleep system, and activity tracking watches. < ref name= " About " > {{cite web|title=CP_Withings_Ventech_092010_EN.pdf|url=http://www.withings.com/static/press/en/CP_Withings_Ventech_092010_EN.pdf|url-status=dead|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110929092824/http://www.withings.com/static/press/en/CP_Withings_Ventech_092010_EN.pdf|archive-date=29 September 2011|access-date=29 September 2011|publisher=Withings}} < /ref >  In 2016, Withings was acquired by Nokia and became a division of [[Nokia]] Health. The brand transitioned to the Nokia name in 2017. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Nokia plans to acquire Withings to accelerate entry into Digital Health  & #124; Nokia|url=http://company.nokia.com/en/news/press-releases/2016/04/26/nokia-plans-to-acquire-withings-to-accelerate-entry-into-digital-health|url-status=dead|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160519223456/http://company.nokia.com/en/news/press-releases/2016/04/26/nokia-plans-to-acquire-withings-to-accelerate-entry-into-digital-health|archive-date=19 May 2016|access-date=19 May 2016}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite web|title=Nokia completes the acquisition of Withings  & #124; Nokia|url=http://company.nokia.com/en/news/press-releases/2016/05/31/nokia-completes-the-acquisition-of-withings|url-status=dead|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160807122438/http://company.nokia.com/en/news/press-releases/2016/05/31/nokia-completes-the-acquisition-of-withings|archive-date=7 August 2016|access-date=31 May 2016}} < /ref > 
 
 Throughout its history, Withings has received venture capital funding, formed partnerships with companies like MyFitnessPal, and integrated with [[Apple Inc.|Apple's]] HealthKit platform. < ref name= " PSFK " > {{cite web|author=Macala Wright|date=24 February 2016|title=Withings Takes on the Silent Killer With a Wearable|url=http://www.psfk.com/2016/02/withings-fitness-tracker-home-blood-pressure-monitoring.html|access-date=27 April 2016|publisher=PSFK}}{{Dead link|date=August 2023 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }} < /ref >  The company continues to develop innovative health and wellness products and operates a factory in France as of April 2019. < ref > {{Cite web|last=O'Brien|first=Chris|date=2019-04-02|title=Withings' new factory lets customers choose 5,800 combinations of Move smartwatch|url=https://venturebeat.com/2019/04/02/withings-new-factory-lets-customers-choose-5800-combinations-of-move-smartwatch/|access-date=2019-07-16|website=VentureBeat|language=en-US}} < /ref >
