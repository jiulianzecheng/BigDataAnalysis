 '''Erlabrunn''' is a [[Municipalities of Germany|municipality]] in [[Würzburg (district)|Würzburg]] in Bavaria, Germany, on the left edge of the river [[Main (river)|Main]]. It forms an [[Amt (administrative division)|administrative division]] together with its neighbour [[Margetshöchheim]]. 
 
 Erlabrunn is about {{convert|10|km|mi|0}} north of [[Würzburg]]. Its neighbours are in the south Margetshöchheim und [[Veitshöchheim]], in the west [[Leinach]] and in the north [[Zellingen]] and [[Thüngersheim]]. It was first mentioned in 1209. 
 
 Erlabrunn has had a town twinning arrangement with the French commune [[Quettehou]] since 1985.
