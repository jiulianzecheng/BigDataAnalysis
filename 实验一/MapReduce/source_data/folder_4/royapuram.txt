'''Royapuram''' is a locality (territory or neighbourhood) present in the northern part of [[Chennai]] metropolitan city, [[Tamil Nadu]]. This area is best known for the [[Royapuram Railway Station]]. This is the first railway station of South India. It was opened in 1856.
