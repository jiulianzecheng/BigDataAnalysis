 '''Caudeval''' is a former [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is in the [[Regions of France|region]] [[Occitanie]] in the [[Aude]] [[Departments of France|department]] in the south of [[France]]. On 1 January 2016, it was merged into the new commune of [[Val-de-Lambronne]]. < ref > [https://www.legifrance.gouv.fr/eli/arrete/2015/11/12/INTB1529629A/jo/texte Arrêté préfectoral] 12 November 2015 < /ref >
