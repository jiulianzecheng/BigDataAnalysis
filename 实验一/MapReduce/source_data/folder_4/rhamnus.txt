 '''''Rhamnus''''' is a genus of plants in the [[Rhamnaceae]] family. They are commonly known as '''buckthorns'''. There are around 110 species, and their height can be anywhere from 1 to 15 meters. ''Rhamnus'' species are called buckthorns because they have spines on their twigs. < ref > {{Cite web|url=https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/buckthorn|title=Definition of BUCKTHORN|website=www.merriam-webster.com|language=en|access-date=2020-08-08}} < /ref >  One common species is ''[[Rhamnus cathartica]]'', the common buckthorn.
