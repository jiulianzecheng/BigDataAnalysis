 '''Vaals''' is a [[List of municipalities of the Netherlands|municipality]] in the [[Netherlands|Dutch]] province of [[Limburg (Netherlands)|Limburg]]. In 2019, 10,092 people lived there.It is located on the border with Germany, Aachen is about {{Convert|5.5|km}} away.
