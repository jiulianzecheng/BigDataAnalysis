 '''Cumin''' (''Cuminum cyminum'') {{IPA-en|ˈkʌmɪn}} sometimes spelled '''cummin''' is a [[flowering plant]], native from the [[east Mediterranean]] to [[East India]]. Its seeds are used in the [[Cuisine|cuisines]] of many different cultures. The use of cumin is very common in Indian and Pakistani foods.
