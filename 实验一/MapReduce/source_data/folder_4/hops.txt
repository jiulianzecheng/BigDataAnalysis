 '''Hops''' are the flowers of the hop plant, which are in the humulus [[genus]]. Hops look like little cones, and are used to make [[beer]]. They give the beer a [[wikt:bitter|bitter]] flavor. They have been used to make beer for many centuries, and were used by the Romans in the [[Roman Empire]].  < ref > {{Cite web |url=http://home.earthlink.net/~ggsurplus/hophistory.html |title=A History of Hops |access-date=2010-03-06 |archive-date=2010-03-11 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20100311150203/http://home.earthlink.net/~ggsurplus/hophistory.html |url-status=dead }} < /ref >
