 '''SEPECAT''' (for ''Société Européenne de Production de l'avion École de Combat et d'Appui Tactique'') is an [[UK|Anglo]]-[[France|French]] [[company]] which makes just one [[aircraft]]: the [[SEPECAT Jaguar|Jaguar]]. SEPECAT is the [[alliance]] of two aircraft companies: [[British Aircraft Corporation]] in UK and [[Breguet]] in France.
