 Anationalism is a [[political ideology]] originally made by Eugene Lanti and expanded on by others. Anationalists believe in some or all of the following 
 
 a) Extreme anti-nationalism, 
 
 b) Universalism, 
 
 c) Globalism, 
 
 d) The need for the education and organisation of the [[proletariat]], 
 
 e) The idea that over time people have become connected language wise, 
 
 f) There is benefit in using [[Esperanto]].
