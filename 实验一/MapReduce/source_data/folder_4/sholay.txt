 '''''Sholay''''' is a [[Bollywood]] comedy movie directed by [[Ramesh Sippy]]. It was produced by his father, GP Sippy. The movie was released on 15 August 1975. The action-adventure movie is about two criminals, Veeru and Jai (played by Dharmendra and Amitabh Bachchan), who were hired by a retired police officer (Sanjeev Kumar) to catch the cruel dacoit Gabbar Singh (Amjad Khan). ''Sholay'' is a classic Indian movie.  < ref > {{Cite web|date=2020-08-16|title=Sholay turns 45: Shweta Bachchan shares throwback pic from set, says 'India's tryst with its movies has been long and beautiful'|url=https://www.hindustantimes.com/bollywood/sholay-turns-45-shweta-bachchan-shares-throwback-pic-from-set-says-india-s-tryst-with-its-movies-has-been-long-and-beautiful/story-A3j06aFMKI5OLPNymelOWP.html|access-date=2020-08-16|website=Hindustan Times|language=en}} < /ref >  It was top of the [[British Film Institute]]'s 2002 poll of  " Top 10 Indian Films "  of all time. In 2005, 50th Filmfare Awards named it the Best Film of 50 Years.
