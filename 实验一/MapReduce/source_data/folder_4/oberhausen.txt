 '''Oberhausen''' ([[Ripuarian language|Ripuarian]]: ''Ovverhouse'', [[Low German language|Low German]]: ''Owerhuusen'', ''Overhus'')  is a city in [[Germany]], in the [[States of Germany|state]] of [[North Rhine-Westphalia]], in the [[Ruhr Area]]. Oberhausen has about 220,000 [[inhabitant]]s. It was dominated by industry like most towns in the Ruhr Area. The city hosts the [[International]] [[Short film|Short Film]] [[Festival]] Oberhausen.
