 '''Hampshire''' is a [[county]] in the south of the [[United Kingdom|UK]]. The county has an area of 1,455 [[square mile]]s (3,769 [[square kilometre|km²]]). At its widest points, the county is about 55 miles (90 km) east-west and 40 miles (65 km) north-south. The [[county town]] is [[Winchester]]. About 1.6 million people live in Hampshire.
