 '''Kruunupyy''' is a [[municipality]] in [[Central Ostrobothnia]], [[Finland]]. It had 4,293 people living there in January 2014. The municipalities next to it are [[Halsua]], [[Kokkola]], [[Kruunupyy]] and [[Veteli]]. 
 
 The municipality was established in 1868.
