 '''Bopfingen''' is a town in [[Baden-Württemberg]], [[Germany]]. It is in the [[Ostalbkreis]], between [[Aalen]] and [[Nördlingen]]. It has the city Bopfingen itself and its districts Aufhausen, Baldern, Flochberg, Kerkingen, Oberdorf, Schloßberg, Trochtelfingen, and Unterriffingen. Bopfingen is known for its landmark [[Mount Ipf|Ipf]], a table mountain which is part of the neighboring [[Schwäbische Alb]] to the east. To the west it borders to Bavaria and the impact crater [[Nördlinger Ries]].
