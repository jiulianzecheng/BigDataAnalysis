 '''Baucau''' is a [[city]] in [[East Timor]] (Timor Leste). It is {{convert|129|km|mi|0|lk=on|abbr=on}} east of the capital, [[Dili]]. < ref name= " fob " > {{cite web|url=http://home.vicnet.net.au/~fobaucau/baucau.html|title=Friends of Baucau|access-date=2009-10-30|archive-date=2010-02-02|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20100202202822/http://home.vicnet.net.au/~fobaucau/baucau.html|url-status=dead}} < /ref >  About 16,000 people live there. That makes it the second biggest town in East Timor. < ref name=fob/ >  It is served by the [[Cakung Airport]]. The town was first started by [[Portugal]] as a trading center.
