 A '''garnet''' {{IPAc-en|ˈ|ɡ|ɑr|n|ə|t}} is one kind of [[silicate]] mineral. There are many different kinds of garnets, containing different metals. [[Crystal]]s of garnet are often large and pretty. Their most common color is red or purple, but they can be found in almost all colors and are often used in jewelry. In the [[United States]], garnet is the birthstone for people born in the month of [[January]].
