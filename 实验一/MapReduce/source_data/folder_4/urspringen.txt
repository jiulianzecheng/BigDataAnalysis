 '''Urspringen''' is a community in the [[Main-Spessart|Main-Spessart district]] in the ''[[Regierungsbezirk]]'' of [[Lower Franconia]] (''Unterfranken'') in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]] and a member of the ''Verwaltungsgemeinschaft'' (Administrative Community) of Marktheidenfeld. 
 
 == Geography == 
 
 === Location === 
 Urspringen is in the Würzburg Region. 
   
 The community has only the ''Gemarkung'' (traditional rural cadastral area) of Urspringen.
