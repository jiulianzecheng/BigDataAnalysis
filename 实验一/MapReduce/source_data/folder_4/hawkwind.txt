 '''Hawkwind'''  < !--Please do not change to 'is' -- > are < !-- 'are' is correct UK English. -- >  an English [[rock music|rock]] band. They are one of the earliest [[space rock]] groups. Their write and sing about [[science fiction]] [[wikt:|theme]]s. They formed in November 1969. Hawkwind have changed many times during their history and have played many styles of music. Dozens of musicians, dancers, and writers have worked with the group since they began.
