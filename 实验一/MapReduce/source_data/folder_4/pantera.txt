 '''Pantera''' was an [[United States|American]] [[heavy metal music|heavy metal]] band from [[Arlington, Texas]], in the [[USA]]. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.rockdetector.com/officialbio,6710.sm|title=rockdetector.com -  Resources and Information.|website=www.rockdetector.com}} < /ref >  They formed in 1981, but they did not have success until the 1990 release of their fifth studio album ''[[Cowboys from Hell]]''. From 1987 to 2003 the band consisted of members [[Phil Anselmo]] ([[vocals]]), [[Dimebag Darrell| " Dimebag Darrell "  Abbott]] ([[guitar]]), [[Vinnie Paul]] ([[drum]]s), and [[Rex Brown]] ([[Bass guitar|bass]]). In 2003, the band broke up. On December 8, 2004, Dimebag Darrell was shot and killed by [[Nathan Gale]] during a [[Damageplan]] concert in [[Columbus, Ohio]]. < ref > {{cite web |url=https://www.rollingstone.com/news/story/24946935/obituary_dimebag_darrell_abbott |title=Obituary:  " Dimebag "  Darrell Abbott |access-date=2010-01-06 |publisher=Rolling Stone |archive-date=2009-12-13 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20091213032310/http://www.rollingstone.com/news/story/24946935/obituary_dimebag_darrell_abbott |url-status=dead }} < /ref >
