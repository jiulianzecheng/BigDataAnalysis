 '''Burgau''' is a [[Town#Germany|town]] in [[Günzburg (district)|Günzburg]] in [[Swabia (Bavaria)|Swabia]], [[Bavaria]]. Burgau is on the river [[Mindel]], and has a population of just under 10,000.
