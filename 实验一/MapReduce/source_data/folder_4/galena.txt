 '''Galena''' is the main [[ore]] of [[lead]]. It is mostly lead(II) sulphide. 
 Galena is one of the most common and widely used [[sulfide mineral]]s. It [[crystal]]lizes in the [[Cubic (crystal system)|cubic]] crystal system often showing [[octahedral]] forms. It is often found with the minerals [[sphalerite]], [[calcite]] and [[fluorite]].
