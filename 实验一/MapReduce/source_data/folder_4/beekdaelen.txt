 '''Beekdaelen''' is a [[List of municipalities of the Netherlands|municipality]] in the [[Netherlands|Dutch]] province of [[Limburg (Netherlands)|Limburg]]. In 2019, 35,771 people lived there. 
 
 It was created on 1 January 2019 from the former cities of [[Nuth]], [[Onderbanken]] and [[Schinnen]].
