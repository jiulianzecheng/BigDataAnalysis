 '''Vaire''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is in [[Bourgogne-Franche-Comté]] in the [[Doubs]] [[Departments of France|department]] in east [[France]]. The municipality was created on 1 June 2016 and consists of the former communes of [[Vaire-Arcier]] (the seat) and [[Vaire-le-Petit]]. < ref > [https://www.legifrance.gouv.fr/eli/arrete/2016/5/12/INTB1616868A/jo/texte Arrêté préfectoral] 12 May 2016 {{in lang|fr}} < /ref >
