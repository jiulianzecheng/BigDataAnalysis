 '''Cambridgeshire''' is a [[county]] in [[England]]. It is located in the [[East of England|East]] part of the country. The counties around Cambridgeshire are [[Lincolnshire]] to the north, [[Norfolk]] to the northeast, [[Suffolk]] to the east, [[Essex]] and [[Hertfordshire]] to the south, and [[Bedfordshire]] and [[Northamptonshire]] to the west. The [[county town]] is [[Cambridge]].
