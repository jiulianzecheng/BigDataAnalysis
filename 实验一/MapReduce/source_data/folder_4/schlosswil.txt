 '''Schlosswil''' was a [[municipality]] of the [[administrative district]] of [[Bern-Mittelland (administrative district)|Bern-Mittelland]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Bern (canton)|Bern]] in [[Switzerland]]. On 1 January 2018, the former municipality of Schlosswil merged into the municipality of [[Grosshöchstetten]].
