 '''''Supernoobs''''' is a Canadian animated series produced by WildBrain Studios for [[Cartoon Network]] in the U.S, U.K and worldwide, [[Teletoon]] (Season 1 only) and following by Season 2, the show started airing on [[Family Channel]] and was created by Scott Fellows who also created [[Johnny Test]]. It first aired in the U.K and Ireland on November 2, 2015 before airing in the U.S on December 7, 2015. The show follows 4 middle school kids who get battleballs and now have to fight the virus.  
 
 == Characters == 
 
 * '''Tyler Bowman:''' The blonde boy who owns the blue battleball which gives him the power to go to different places and read minds. His blonde hair will turn blue while wearing his suit. He is the leader of the group.  
 * '''Kevin Reynolds:''' The black haired boy who wears braces on his teeth. He owns the red battleballs which allows him to turn into animals. While wearing his suit, his black hair will turn red. He is the most irresponsible member in the group since he'll use his power as a play.  
 * '''Jennifer Shope:''' The black haired girl who wears glasses. She has purple battleball that gives her the power to use water, air and fire. In her suit, her black hair will turn purple. She's the only girl in the group and she's known as her last name by her friends and by adults, they just call her given name.  
 * '''Theodore  " The Roach "  Roachmont:''' The orange haired short boy with freckles. He owns green battleball that make his hands big and he can float in the air. While he's in his suit, his ginger hair will turn green. He is the youngest member in the group and his birthday is confirmed to be November 10.  
 * '''Zenblock/Zen:''' The blue alien that looks like a dragon but he isn't. He is one of the trainers for the Supernoobs and he's the one who mistake all 4 of them to have battleballs as revealed in the first episode. When he's a human, he is known as Rob. 
 * '''Memnock/Mem:''' Just like Zenblock, he's alien that looks like a dragon but he isn't. He is also the trainer for the Supernoobs. He and Zenblock have a relationship that brothers/sisters or siblings have together. In human form, he is known as Bob. 
 * '''XR4Ti:''' A female computer who controls Mem and Zen's ship. She shares a mother-like behavior to Mem, Zen and the Supernoobs due to her voice.
