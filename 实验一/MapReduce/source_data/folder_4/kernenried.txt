 '''Kernenried''' is a [[municipality]] of the [[administrative district]] of [[Emmental (administrative district)|Emmental]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Bern (canton)|Bern]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 It is {{convert|6|km|abbr=off}} west of [[Burgdorf, Switzerland|Burgdorf]] and {{convert|15|km|abbr=off}} north-northeast of [[Berne]].
