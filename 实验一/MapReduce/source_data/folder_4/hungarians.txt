 '''Hungarians''', also known as '''Magyars''' ({{IPAc-en|ˈ|m|æ|ɡ|j|ɑːr|z}} {{respell|MAG|yarz}}; {{lang-hu|magyarok}} {{IPA-hu|ˈmɒɟɒrok|}}), are a nation and [[ethnic group]] native to [[Hungary]] and [[Kingdom of Hungary|historical Hungarian lands]] who share a common [[Hungarian culture|culture]], [[Hungary#History|history]], [[Magyar tribes|ancestry]] and [[Hungarian language|language]]. The Hungarian language belongs to the [[Uralic languages|Uralic language family]].
