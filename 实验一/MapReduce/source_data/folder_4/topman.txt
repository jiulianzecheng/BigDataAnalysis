 '''Topman''' is the [[male]] [[clothing]] section of the [[Topshop]] franchise. The men's clothing stores are located throughout the [[United Kingdom]]. < ref > {{cite web |title=Our History / Arcadia Group |url=http://www.arcadiagroup.co.uk/about-us/our-history |website=web.archive.org |date=30 June 2014 |access-date=6 June 2020 |archive-date=30 June 2014 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140630131512/http://www.arcadiagroup.co.uk/about-us/our-history |url-status=bot: unknown }} < /ref > 
 
 As of 2016, Topman has over 400 stores. They continue to expand throughout the USA, Europe, and Asia.
