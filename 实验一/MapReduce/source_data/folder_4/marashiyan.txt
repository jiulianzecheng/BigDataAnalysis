 '''Marashiyan''' or '''Marashis''' (in [[Mazandarani language]]: مرعشیون) is one of the [[Arab]] dynasties in [[Iran]]. Marashiyan ruled from 1359 to 1582 in [[Tabarestan]] (northern Iran). This is [[Islamic]] government. Mirbozorg is founder of the Marashiyans.
