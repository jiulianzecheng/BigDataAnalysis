 '''Anantnag''' (Kashmiri: اننتناگ /Anaṁtnāg/, meaning abode of springs and lakes), is a city and a municipality located in the [[Anantnag District]] in the union territory  of [[Jammu and Kashmir (union territory)|Jammu and Kashmir]], [[India]]. < ref name= " Official Website of Anantnag District, Jammu and Kashmir, India " > {{cite web|url = http://anantnag.nic.in/history.htm|title = History of Anantnag|publisher = Government of India|access-date = 2007-06-26|archive-date = 2004-10-23|archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20041023005006/http://anantnag.nic.in/history.htm|url-status = dead}} < /ref >  About 180,000 people live in Anantnag. 
 
 Anantnag is the third largest city of the Indian administered Jammu and Kashmir by population and largest according to area. It is also regarded as the commercial and financial capital of the [[Kashmir Valley|Valley of Kashmir]]. It is the largest business and trading center of the valley.
