'''Belshazzar''' is a [[king]] of [[Babylonia]] in the [[Book of Daniel]]. He is the son of [[Nebuchadnezzar II|Nebuchadnezzar]] and is killed by Darius the [[Mede]]. 
 His [[banquet]] is the subject of a painting by [[Rembrandt]].
