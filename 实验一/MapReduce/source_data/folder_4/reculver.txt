 '''Reculver''' is a small coastal town in [[Kent]], [[South East England]] near [[Herne Bay]] and a few miles north of [[Canterbury]]. Its main features are the ruins of a [[Roman Britain|Roman]] fort, a ruined medieval church which is constantly threatened by [[erosion|coastal erosion]].
