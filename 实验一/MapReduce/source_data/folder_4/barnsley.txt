 '''Barnsley''' ({{IPAc-en|ˈ|b|ɑːr|n|z|l|i}}) is a large [[market town|market]] and [[college town|college]] town in [[South Yorkshire]], England, the town is surrounded by several towns and villages, which together form the [[Metropolitan Borough of Barnsley]], of which Barnsley is the administrative centre. At the [[United Kingdom Census 2011|2011 Census]], Barnsley had a population of 91,297 < ref name=Pop2011 > population of the Barnsley urban subdivision http://www.citypopulation.de/php/uk-england-yorkshireandthehumber.php < /ref >  out of close to 250,000 in the [[Metropolitan Borough of Barnsley|wider borough]] in the [[Dearne Valley]]. [[Historic counties of England|Historically]] in the [[West Riding of Yorkshire]]. It is located between the cities of [[Sheffield]], [[Manchester]], [[Wakefield]] and [[Leeds]].
