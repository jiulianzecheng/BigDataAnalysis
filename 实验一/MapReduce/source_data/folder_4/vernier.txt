 '''Vernier''' is a [[municipality]] of the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Geneva (canton)|Geneva]] in [[Switzerland]]. It has villages of Vernier Village, [[Le Lignon]], Aïre, Les Avanchets, Cointrin and Châtelaine. As of 31 December 2016, 34,983 people lived there.
