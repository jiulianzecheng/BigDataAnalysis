 '''Ammerland''' is a [[districts of Germany|district]] in [[Lower Saxony]], [[Germany]]. It is bounded by (from the east and clockwise) the city of [[Oldenburg (city)|Oldenburg]] and the districts of [[Oldenburg (district)|Oldenburg]], [[Cloppenburg (district)|Cloppenburg]], [[Leer (district)|Leer]], [[Friesland (district)|Friesland]] and [[Wesermarsch]]. 
 
 == Geography == 
 The central point of the district is the [[Zwischenahner Meer]] near the resort town of [[Bad Zwischenahn]], a lake with an area of 5.5 & nbsp;km < sup > 2 < /sup >  (''Meer'' is the German word for  " sea " , but in the local [[Low German]], as in Dutch, it means  " lake " ).
