 '''Jaffna''' ({{lang-ta|யாழ்ப்பாணம்}}, ''{{transl|ta|Yalpanam}}'', {{lang-si|යාපනය}} ''{{transl|si|Yāpanaya}}'') is the [[capital city]] of the [[Northern Province, Sri Lanka|Northern Province]] of [[Sri Lanka]]. The population of Jaffna is 88,138. Jaffna is Sri Lanka's 12th [[List of cities in Sri Lanka|largest city]]. < ref name=gazetteer > {{cite web|title=Sri Lanka: largest cities and towns and statistics of their population|url=http://world-gazetteer.com/wg.php?x= & men=gcis & lng=en & des=wg & geo=-127 & srt=pnan & col=abcdefghinoq & msz=1500|publisher=World Gazetteer}}{{Dead link|date=August 2021 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }} < /ref > The biggest of all in Jaffna is the Jaffna Fort and Nallur Kandasamy temple. The statue made out of gold was the man who was the person who put Sinhala people and Tamil people together.
