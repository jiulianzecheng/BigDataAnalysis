 '''Diran''' is a {{convert|7266|m|ft}} [[mountain]] in the [[Karakoram]] [[Mountain range|range]], in [[Pakistan]]. < ref name= " peakbagger " > {{cite peakbagger|pid=18675|name=Diran, Pakistan|accessdate=5 February 2020}} < /ref >  It is the 93rd highest mountain in the world. 
 
 The first people to reach the top were Rainer Goeschl, Rudolph Pischinger and Hanns Schell, in 1968. < ref name= " peakware " > {{cite peakware |id=2751 |name=Diran Peak |accessdate=5 February 2020 }} < /ref >  While it is not thought to be hard to [[Mountain climbing|climb]], it is dangerous as [[avalanche]]s are common. < ref > {{cite summitpost |id=626824 |name=Diran |accessdate=5 February 2020}} < /ref >
