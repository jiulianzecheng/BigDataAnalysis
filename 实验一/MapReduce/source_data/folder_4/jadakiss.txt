 '''Jason Terrance Phillips''' known by his stage name '''Jadakiss''', (born May 27, 1975) is an American rapper. Jadakiss joined the rap group [[The Lox]] in 1994, which became popular in 1997. In 1999, Jadakiss joined the Ruff Ryders as well.
