 '''Rammenau''' ([[Sorbian language|Sorbian]]: ''Ramnow'') is a city in the [[Bautzen (district)|district of Bautzen]], in [[Saxony]], [[Germany]]. 
 It is known as the birthplace of the well-known German philosopher [[Johann Gottlieb Fichte]].
