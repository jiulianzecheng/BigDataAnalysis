 The '''Steinschartenkopf''' is a {{convert|2615|m}} high [[mountain]] in the [[Allgäu Alps]]. < ref name=WME > {{cite web |url=http://peakware.com/peaks.php?pk=3556 |title=Steinschartenkopf |website=Peakware, World Mountain Encyclopedia |publisher=Interactive Outdoors, Inc |access-date=January 15, 2017 }}{{Dead link|date=January 2021 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }} < /ref >  It is north of the [[Hohes Licht]], in [[Tyrol (state)|Tyrol]], [[Austria]]. It is the sixth tallest mountain in the [[Allgäu Alps]]. < ref name=WME/ >  The popular [[Heilbronn Way]] (''Heilbronner Weg'') runs over the [[summit]]. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://www.lechtal-info.com/wanderungen/heilbronner-weg.html |title=Heilbronner Weg |publisher=lechtal-info.com |access-date=January 14, 2017 |archive-date=August 10, 2016 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160810022646/http://www.lechtal-info.com/wanderungen/heilbronner-weg.html |url-status=dead }} < /ref >  It is the highest point on the [[Heilbronn Way]].
