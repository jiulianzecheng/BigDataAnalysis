 '''Sarod''' is a [[string instrument]] from the [[Indian subcontinent]]. The sarod does not have [[Fret|frets]], so it can [[Pitch bend|bend pitches]] and do [[Glissando|glissandi]] (slides). The sarod is played using a [[plectrum]].
