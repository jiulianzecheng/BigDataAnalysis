'''Gethsemane''' is a garden at the foot of the Mount of Olives. It is most famous for being the place where [[Jesus]] and [[Disciples of Christ|his disciples]] are said to have prayed the night before [[Crucifixion of Jesus|Jesus' crucifixion]].
