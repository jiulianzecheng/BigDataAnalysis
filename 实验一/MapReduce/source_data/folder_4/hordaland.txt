 '''Hordaland''' is a former [[Counties of Norway|county]] in [[Norway]]. Hordaland was the third largest county in Norway by population, after [[Akershus]] and [[Oslo]]. Before 1972, the city of Bergen was its own separate county, split from Hordaland. On 1 January 2020, it was merged with neighbouring county of [[Sogn og Fjordane]] to the new county of [[Vestland]]. The capital was [[Bergen]].
