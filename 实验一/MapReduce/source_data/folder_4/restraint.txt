'''Restraint''' may refer to: 
 *'''restraint device''' - an apparatus such as [[handcuffs]], [[leg irons]], a [[leash]] or makeshift restraints such as [[rope]] or [[duct tape]] used to bind someone's hands or feet together or to restrict a domestic [[animal]]'s motion. 
 *'''safety restraint''' - a device such as a [[seat belt]] or [[harness]] used to stop someone from falling out of a moving [[vehicle]], through a [[windshield]] or falling from a great height 
 *'''self restraint''' - the ability to resist temptation or to refrain from something
