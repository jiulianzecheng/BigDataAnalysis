 '''Cebu''' is an [[island]] [[Provinces of the Philippines|province]] of the [[Philippines]]. Cebu has nine cities: Bogo, Carcar, Danao, Lapu-Lapu, Mandaue, Naga, Talisay, and Toledo, with the largest city being [[Cebu City]], the capital.
