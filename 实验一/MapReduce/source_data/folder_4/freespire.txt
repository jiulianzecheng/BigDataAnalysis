 '''Freespire''' is a [[Linux]] distribution made with parts from [[Ubuntu]]. It was made to make using free software easy, while giving the choice to use non-free (not [[open source]]) parts. These parts might be [[computer program|programs]], [[Driver (computer)|drivers]], or [[codecs]]. [[Linspire]], a [[Product (consumer goods)|commercial]] Linux distribution, is made from Freespire.
