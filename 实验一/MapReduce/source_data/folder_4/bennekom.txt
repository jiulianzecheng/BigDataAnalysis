 '''Bennekom''' ([[Low Saxon]]: ''Bennekum'') is a village in the municipality of [[Ede]], province of [[Gelderland]], the Netherlands. 
 
 From 1812 to 1818 Bennekom also was a municipality. 
 
 About 15,000 people live in this village. 
 
 Bennekom lies south of the A24 motorway, and between the city of Ede in the north and the town of [[Wageningen]] in the south.
