 '''Calafat''' is a [[city]] in [[Dolj County]], [[Romania]].  It is near the [[Danube|Danube River]]. This is across from the Bulgarian city of [[Vidin]].  Calafat was started in the [[14th century]] by [[Genoa|Genoese]] [[colonist]]s.  About 21,200 people lived in Calafat, as of 2000.
