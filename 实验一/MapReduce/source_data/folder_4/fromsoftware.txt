 '''FromSoftware, Inc.''' is a [[Japan]]ese video game developer that made [[video game]]s like the [[Souls (series)|''Souls'' series]], ''[[Bloodborne]]'', and ''[[Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice|Sekiro]]''. Games that the company made before the ''Souls'' series include the ''[[King's Field]]'' series and the [[Armored Core series|''Armored Core'' series]]. The company has been lead by [[Hidetaka Miyazaki]], the leader of the ''Demon's Souls'' team, since ''[[Demon's Souls]]'' became popular.
