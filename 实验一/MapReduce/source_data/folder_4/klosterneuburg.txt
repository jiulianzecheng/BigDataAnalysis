 '''Klosterneuburg''' is an [[Austria]]n town in the [[Tulln District]] in the [[Industrieviertel]] region of [[Lower Austria]]. It had about 27,000 people in 2016. 
 
 Klosterneuburg lies almost entirely south of the [[Danube River]] and borders the city of [[Vienna]] in the southeast.  
 
 It is well-known for its [[winegrowing]].
