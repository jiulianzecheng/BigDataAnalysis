 '''Lukhovitsy''' ({{lang-ru|Луховицы}}) is a town in [[Moscow Oblast]], [[Russia]]. It is the [[administrative center]] of [[Lukhovitsky District]]. In 2010, 29,850 people lived there.{{Moscow Oblast}}
