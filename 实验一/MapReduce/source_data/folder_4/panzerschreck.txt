 A '''Panzerschreck''' (also known as a ''Raketenpanzerbüchse'', abbreviated to RPzB) is an [[infantry]] [[weapon]] made by [[Nazi Germany]]. They fired an 88 & nbsp;mm [[calibre]] anti-[[tank]] rocket. Unlike the [[Panzerfaust]], they were reusuable.
