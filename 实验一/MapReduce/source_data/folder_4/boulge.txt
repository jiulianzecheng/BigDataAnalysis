 '''Boulge''' is a village and [[civil parish]] in [[Suffolk Coastal]], [[Suffolk]], England. In 2001, there were 26 people living in Boulge. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.ons.gov.uk/help/localstatistics|title=Local statistics - Office for National Statistics|website=www.ons.gov.uk}} < /ref >  Boulge has a church called St Michael. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.suffolkchurches.co.uk/boulge.html|title=Suffolk Churches|website=www.suffolkchurches.co.uk}} < /ref >
