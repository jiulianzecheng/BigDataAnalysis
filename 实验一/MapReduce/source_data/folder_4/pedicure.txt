 The '''pedicure''' is a way to improve the appearance of [[foot|feet]] and [[nail]]s. The word ''pedicure'' refers to superficial cosmetic treatment of the feet and toenails. Pedicures can help prevent nail disorders and nail problems. They are very popular, especially among [[woman|women]]. < ref > {{cite web|title=History of Pedicures|publisher=US Pedicure Spa|url=http://uspedicurespa.com/en/article/history-of-pedicure|accessdate=August 23, 2014|archive-date=March 1, 2014|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140301020557/http://uspedicurespa.com/en/article/history-of-pedicure|url-status=dead}} < /ref > 
 
 Pedicures are not only limited to the nails. Usually, dead skin cells on the bottoms of feet are rubbed off with [[pumice|pumice stone]]s.  
 
 People have been pedicuring nails for over four thousand years.
