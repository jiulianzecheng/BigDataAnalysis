 '''Surah''' (sometimes spelt '''sura''' or '''surat''' {{lang|ar|سورة}} (''{{transl|ar|sūrah}}''), plural '''suwar''' {{lang|ar|سور}}) is an [[Arabic language|Arabic]] term that literally means  " something enclosed or surrounded by a fence or wall. "   
 
 A surah is a  " [[Chapter (books)|chapter]] "  of the [[Qur'an]]. They are traditionally ordered in order of length and begin with the shortest. Each surah is named for a word or name mentioned in an [[ayah]] (section), of that 'surah'.
