 '''''Rebelde''''' (English: ''Rebel'') is a Mexican [[telenovela]] produced by [[Televisa]] and created by [[Cris Morena]]. It is a remake of the Argentine telenovela ''[[Rebelde Way]]'' created for the Mexican audience. The series aired on [[Canal de las Estrellas]] from October 4, 2004, to June 2, 2006. ''Rebelde'' was replaced in June 2006 with Televisa's series ''[[CÃ³digo Postal]]''. 
 
 The show is set at the Elite Way School, a private boarding high school in [[Mexico City]] with a major plot line revolving around a group of students forming a pop band.
