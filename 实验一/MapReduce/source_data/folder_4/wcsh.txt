 '''WCSH''' is the [[NBC]] [[television station]] for Southern [[Maine]] as well as Eastern and Northern [[New Hampshire]]. Its studios are in [[Portland, Maine|Portland]]. It [[broadcasting|broadcasts]] a [[High-definition television|high definition]] digital signal on [[Ultra high frequency|UHF]] channel 44 from a [[transmitter]] on Winn Mountain in Sebago, Maine. It is owned by the [[Gannett Company]]. 
 
 The station airs [[syndicated]] programs including: ''[[Who Wants to Be a Millionaire (U.S. game show)|Who Wants to Be a Millionaire]]'', ''[[The Doctors (2008 TV series)|The Doctors]]'', ''[[Rachael Ray]]'' and ''[[Inside Edition]]''. 
 
 WCSH is the sister station to Maine's other NBC affiliate, [[WLBZ]] in [[Bangor, Maine|Bangor]]. The twin stations run much of the same programming.  Syndicated programming shown only on WCSH includes ''[[The Andy Griffith Show]]''. WLBZ also airs separate station identifications and [[advertising]]. With the two stations essentially operating as one, they give statewide coverage not offered by any other channels in Maine.
