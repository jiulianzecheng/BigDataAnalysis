 '''Luzein''' is a [[municipality]] of the [[district]] of [[Prättigau/Davos (district)|Prättigau/Davos]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Graubünden]] in [[Switzerland]]. In 2016 the former municipality of [[St. Antönien]] merged into the municipality of Luzein.
