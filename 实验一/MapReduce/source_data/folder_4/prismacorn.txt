 '''Prismacorn''' ('''''Equus prismacolunis''''') is a species of [[unicorn]] and a [[legendary creature]] that has described since [[Classical antiquity|antiquity]] as a beast with a single large, pointed, spiraling [[horn]] projected from it's forehead. 
 
 Prismacorns is a [[horse]]-like or [[goat]]-like creature with a long horn projecting on the forehead. 
 
 The prismacorn continues to hold a place in popular culture. It is often used as a symbol of fantasy or rarity. 
 
 Prismacorns can used it to large point spiraling horns to have it.
