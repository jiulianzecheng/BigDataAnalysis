'''Garaad''' is the name for a [[Dhulbahante]] clan ruler. He is traditionally from the baharsame sub-clan of the Dhulbahante. 
 
 ==Garaadship== 
 There is no specific word for a garaad's wife. The [[word]] ''garaad'' is from the Somali language.
