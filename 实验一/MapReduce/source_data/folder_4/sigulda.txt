 '''Sigulda''' is a town in [[Latvia]] with [[town rights]] since [[1928]]. It is the center of the [[Sigulda District]]. As of 2020, Sigulda had a population of 14,396 people. < ref > [https://data.stat.gov.lv/pxweb/lv/OSP_PUB/START__POP__IR__IRS/IRD060/; Central Statistical Bureau of Latvia]{{Dead link|date=October 2022 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }} < /ref >  River [[Gauja]] travels through the city. Sigulda is the capital town of Gauja National Park.
