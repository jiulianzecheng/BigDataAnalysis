 '''Quraysh''' or '''Quraish''' < ref > Arabic: '''{{lang|ar|قريش}}''' 
 ''' ({{transl|ar|Qurayš}})'''. Other transliterations include  " Quresh " ,  " Quraysh " ,  " Koreish "  and  " Coreish " . [[Turkish language|Turkish]]: Kureyş. Albanian: Korreshi) < /ref >  was the dominant [[tribe]] of [[Mecca]] upon the appearance of the [[religion]] of [[Islam]].  
 
 It was the tribe to which the [[Islam]]ic [[Prophet]] [[Muhammad]] belonged and which led the opposition to his message. Not very much of this tribe followed [[Islam]].
