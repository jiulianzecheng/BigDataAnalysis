The '''Muwahidin''' or '''Muwahid Muslims''' are a [[Muslim]] [[wikt:restore|restoration]] [[wikt:movement|movement]] that accepts [[mainstream]] [[Islam]]. The place an [[wikt:emphasis|emphasis]] on the concept of [[tawhid]]. Muwahidists believe that the Muslim [[faith]] has gradually been mixed with many [[culture|cultural]] [[tradition]]s. They want to bring the Muslim faith back to its original foundations. This means the worship of [[Allah]].
