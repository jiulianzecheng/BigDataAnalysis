 '''Roanne''' is a [[France|French]] [[Communes of France|commune]], a ''[[Prefectures in France|subprefecture]]'' in the [[Loire]] [[Departments of France|department]] in central France. It is in the [[Auvergne-Rhône-Alpes]] [[Regions of France|region]]. It is also the [[Capital city|capital]] of the ''arrondissement'' of [[Arrondissement of Roanne|Roanne]].

 ==Population== 
 The inhabitants of Roanne are known, in [[French language|French]], as '' Roannais'' (women: ''Roannaises''). < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.habitants.fr/loire-42 |title=Loire |publisher=habitants.fr |language=French |accessdate=13 October 2017 |date= }} < /ref > 
 
 With a population of 35,200, < ref name=insee > {{cite web |url=http://www.insee.fr/fr/statistiques/fichier/2525755/dep42.pdf |title=Régions, départements, arrondissements, cantons et communes |work= Populations légales 2014 |publisher=Institut national de la statistique et des études économiques - INSEE |format=PDF |language=French |accessdate=13 October 2017|date= }} < /ref >  Roanne has a [[population density]] of {{Pop density|35200|16.12|km2|prec=0|disp=num}} inhabitants/km < sup > 2 < /sup > , the highest density in the department.

 == Administration == 
 Roanne is a [[Prefectures in France|subprefecture]] of the Loire department, the capital of the ''arrondissement'' of [[Arrondissement of Roanne|Roanne]] and the [[administrative centre]] ({{lang-fr|chef-lieu}}) of two [[Cantons of France|cantons]]: 
 # [[Canton of Roanne-1|Roanne-1]], with 29,634 inhabitants  < small > (2014) < /small > . 
 # [[Canton of Roanne-2|Roanne-2]], with 29,843 inhabitants  < small > (2014) < /small > . 
 
 It is part of the [[Communes of France|intercommunality]] ''Roannais Agglomération'' ({{lang-fr|Communauté d'agglomération Roannais Agglomération}}).
