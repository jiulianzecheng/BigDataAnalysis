 '''Maurane''' (born '''Claudine Luypaerts'''; 12 November 1960 – 7 May 2018) was a [[Belgian]] [[French language]] [[singer]] and [[actress]]. She was born in [[Ixelles]], [[Belgium]]. In 2012, she became a member of the Jury in ''[[Nouvelle Star]]'' broadcast on television station [[D8 (TV channel)|D8]]. She was known for her 2002 single  " [[Tu es mon autre]] " . She also starred in the 2010 movie ''[[My Afternoons with Margueritte]]''. 
 
 Maurane died in [[Schaerbeek]], Belgian on 7 May 2018 of suspected [[cardiac arrest]] at the age of 57. < ref > [https://www.rtbf.be/info/medias/detail_ne-pas-publier?id=9912206 La chanteuse Maurane est décédée] {{in lang|fr}} < /ref >
