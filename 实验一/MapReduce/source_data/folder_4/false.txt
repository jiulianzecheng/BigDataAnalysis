'''False''' means [[untrue]]. If something is false, it means it is not [[real]]. A falsehood is anything said that is not [[true]]. A falsehood can also be a series of [[lie]]s, told to  " prove "  something that is false itself. 
 
 In [[logic]],  " false "  is one of the [[Truth value|truth values]] (with the other one being 'true').
