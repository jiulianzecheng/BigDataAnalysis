'''Perry''' is an [[alcoholic beverage]]. It is made from [[fermentation|fermented]] [[pear]]s. It is made similar to the way [[cider]] is made from [[Apple|apples]].  Peary has been common for centuries in [[England]]. It is mostly found in the [[Counties of England|English counties]]  of [[Gloucestershire]], [[Herefordshire]], and [[Worcestershire]]. Tt is also made in parts of [[South Wales]] and the [[Normandy]] and [[Duchy of Anjou|Anjou]] areas of [[France]].  
 
 In more recent years, perry has also been referred to as  " pear cider " .
