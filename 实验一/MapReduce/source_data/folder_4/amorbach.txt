 '''Amorbach''' is a town in the [[Miltenberg (district)|Miltenberg district]] in the [[Lower Franconia]] [[Regierungsbezirk|region]] in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]].  About 4,000 people live there. It is on the small river [[Mud (river)|Mud]], in the northeastern part of the [[Odenwald]].
