 '''INSEAD''' (''Institut européen d'administration des affaires'') is an [[Europe]]an higher studies establishment. The school is in 3 places, in [[Fontainebleau]], [[France]], in [[Abu Dhabi]] and in [[Singapore]]. < ref > [https://www.challenges.fr/emploi/special-mba/pour-les-mba-l-insead-est-une-veritable-ecole-globale_23615 Pour les MBA,  " l'Insead est une véritable école globale  " ] < /ref > 
 
 INSEAD is specialist in the fields of economics, social sciences and management.
