 '''Aurillac''' ({{lang-oc|Orlhac}}) is a French [[Communes of France|commune]], the [[Prefectures in France|prefecture]] of the [[Cantal]] [[Departments of France|department]] in the [[Auvergne-Rhône-Alpes]] [[Regions of France|region]]. 
 
 Aurillac was part of the former [[province]] of [[Auvergne]], in the region called [[Haute-Auvergne]]. 
 
 ==History== 
 The name of the city comes from {{lang-lat|Aureliacum}}, meaning  " [[Roman villa]] of Aurelius " . 
 
 ==Geography== 
 The city of Aurillac is at the foot of the Cantal mountains (''[[Mounts of Cantal]]''), in the [[Massif Central]]. The city is along the Jordanne river, a [[tributary]] of the [[Cère]] river. 
 
 It has an area of {{Convert|28.8|km2|sqmi|abbr=on }}. < ref name= " insee-geo " > {{cite web |url=https://www.insee.fr/fr/statistiques/1405599?geo=COM-15014 |title=Commune d’Aurillac (15014) - Comparateur de territoire |work=Publications et statistiques pour la France ou les régions |publisher=Institut national de la statistique et des études économiques - INSEE |language=fr |access-date=22 January 2017 }} < /ref >  Its average [[altitude]] is {{Convert|622|m|ft|abbr=on}}; at the [[City and town halls|city hall]], the altitude is {{Convert|620|m|ft|abbr=on }}. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://www.map-france.com/Aurillac-15000/ |title=The city of Aurillac |publisher=Map-France.com |access-date=22 January 2017 }} < /ref >

 ===Climate=== 
 Aurillac has an [[oceanic climate]] (subtype '''Cfb''' in the [[Köppen climate classification]]) with cold winters and hot summers due to its distance from the ocean. 
 
 The average temperature for the year in Aurillac is {{Convert|10.2|°C|°F }}. The warmest month, on average, is July with an average temperature of {{Convert|18.3|°C|°F }}. The coolest month on average is January, with an average temperature of {{Convert|2.9|°C|°F }}. 
 
 The average amount of precipitation for the year in Aurillac is {{Convert|856|mm|in|1|abbr=on }}. The month with the most precipitation on average is December with {{Convert|109.2|mm|in|1|abbr=on }} of precipitation. The month with the least precipitation on average is June with an average of {{Convert|45.7|mm|in|1|abbr=on }}. < ref name= " wbase " > {{cite web|publisher=Weatherbase |title=Aurillac, France - Köppen Climate Classification |url=http://www.weatherbase.com/weather/weather-summary.php3?s=598805 & cityname=Aurillac%2C+Auvergne%2C+France & units=|access-date=22 January 2017 }} < /ref >

 ==Population== 
 The inhabitants of Aurillac are known, in [[French language|French]], as ''Aurillacois'' (women: ''Aurillacoises''). < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.habitants.fr/aurillac/banque|title=Aurillac (15000) |publisher=habitants.fr |language=fr |access-date=23 January 2017 }} < /ref >

 == Administration == 
 Aurillac is the [[Prefectures in France|prefecture]] of the Cantal department, the capital of the ''arrondissement'' of [[Arrondissement of Aurillac|Aurillac]] and the [[administrative centre]] ({{lang-fr|chef-lieu}}) of three [[Cantons of France|cantons]]:  
 # [[Canton of Aurillac-1|Aurillac-1]] : 2 ''communes'' with 10,296 inhabitants   < small > (2014) < /small > 
 # [[Canton of Aurillac-2|Aurillac-2]] : 1 ''commune'' with 9,591 inhabitants  < small > (2014) < /small > 
 # [[Canton of Aurillac-3|Aurillac-3]] : 1 ''commune'' with 10,369 inhabitants  < small > (2014) < /small > 
 
 It is part of the [[Communes of France|intercommunality]] ''Bassin d'Aurillac'' ({{lang-fr|Communauté d'Agglomération du Bassin d'Aurillac}}).
