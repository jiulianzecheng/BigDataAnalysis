 '''Spaniards''', or '''Spanish people''', are a predominantly [[Romance languages|Romance]]-speaking [[ethnic group]] native to [[Spain]].  Latin America has the largest population of people of Spain descent, people with full or [[Mestizo|partial]] (Mestizo) ancestry.
