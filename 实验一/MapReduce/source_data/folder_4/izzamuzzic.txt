 '''Vadim Viktorovich Pavlyuchenko''' ({{Lang-ru|Вадим Викторович Павлюченко}}, born June 22, 1991, Karaganda, [[Kazakhstan]] < ref > {{Cite web|title=L (iZReal) «Букет» {{!}} RAP.RU|url=https://www.rap.ru/discography/1222|access-date=2023-09-27|website=www.rap.ru}} < /ref > ), better known by his stage name '''Izzamuzzic''' is a Kazakh electronic [[musician]], [[Record producer|sound producer]], [[composer]], < ref > {{Cite web|date=2022-07-10|title=JKLN и новый трек от Jerry Heil: какую музыку слушают в Днепре|url=https://dp.informator.ua/ru/jkln-i-novyj-trek-ot-jerry-heil-kakuyu-muzyku-slushayut-v-dnepre|access-date=2023-09-27|website=Информатор Днепр|language=ru}} < /ref >  working in the genres [[Electronic music|electronic]], [[deep house]], [[future garage]], [[downtempo]], [[rock]], [[hip hop]], [[EDM]]. < ref name= " :0 " > {{Cite web|title=Izzamuzzic — Electronic musician / Sound Producer|url=https://izzamuzzic.com/|access-date=2023-09-27|website=Izzamuzzic|language=ru-RU}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite web|title=Izzamuzzic в Apple Music|url=https://music.apple.com/ru/artist/izzamuzzic/1086171033|access-date=2023-09-27|website=|language=}} < /ref >  After graduating from [[music school]], he began writing [[electronic music]] in late 2011, his second year at university. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Казахстанский певец Diwave презентовал новый трек «Адреналин»|url=https://www.caravan.kz/art/kazakhstanskijj-pevec-diwave-prezentoval-novyjj-trek-adrenalin-867151/|access-date=2023-09-27|website=www.caravan.kz|language=ru}} < /ref > < ref name= " :1 " > {{Cite web|title=Скриптонит (Адиль Жалелов) - Биография|url=https://real-scrip.com/biography/|access-date=2023-09-27|website=real-scrip.com|language=ru-RU}} < /ref >   
 
 At the early stage of creativity used two aliases izzamuzzic (for electronic music) and izzabeatzz (for hip-hop projects and arrangements), later he stopped selling beats and concentrated on his own tracks and all united in izzamuzzic (electronic music, production in different genres, remixes). izzamuzzic himself calls his style of music  " Azi " . < ref name= " :0 "  / > 
 
 Throughout his career, he has worked with artists: Julien Marchal, Mania, Asenssia, Scryptonite, Luna, Armich and others... < ref name= " :1 "  / >
