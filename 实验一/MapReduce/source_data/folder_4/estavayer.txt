 '''Estavayer''' is a new [[municipality]] of the [[canton of Fribourg]], [[Switzerland]]. It is on the south shore of [[Lake Neuchâtel]] and is between [[Yverdon-les-Bains|Yverdon]] and [[Bern]]. It is the capital of the district of [[Broye (district)|Broye]]. It was created on 1 January 2017, when the former municipalities of [[Bussy, Fribourg|Bussy]], [[Estavayer-le-Lac]], [[Morens]], [[Murist]], [[Rueyres-les-Prés]], [[Vernay, Switzerland|Vernay]] and [[Vuissens]] merged to form Estavayer. < ref > [https://www.bfs.admin.ch/bfs/de/home/grundlagen/agvch.html Historisiertes Gemeindeverzeichnis der Schweiz] accessed 5 January 2017 < /ref >
