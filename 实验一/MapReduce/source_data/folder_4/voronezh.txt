 '''Voronezh''' ({{lang-ru|Воро́неж}}) is an [[Russians|Russian]] [[city]], not far from [[Ukraine]]. It is the [[administrative center]] of [[Voronezh Oblast]]. It is located on the river [[Voronezh River|Voronezh]]. The city was founded in [[1586]]. In 2017, the city had an estimated population of 1,039,801 people. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://gks.ru/free_doc/doc_2017/bul_dr/mun_obr2017.rar|title=31.
