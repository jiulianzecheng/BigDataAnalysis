 '''Luhansk''' ({{lang-uk|Луганськ|Luhans'k}}; {{lang-ru|Луганск|Lugansk}}) is a city in eastern [[Ukraine]], near the border with [[Russia]]. It is the administrative center of the [[Luhansk Oblast]]. In 2020, 401,297 people lived there.
