 '''Hathras''' is a city in [[Hathras district]], in the [[India]]n [[States and territories of India|state]] of [[Uttar Pradesh]]. It is the headquarters of the [[Hathras district]] that was created on 3 May 1997, by incorporating parts of: [[Aligarh district|Aligarh]], [[Mathura district|Mathura]], [[Agra district|Agra]] Districts, and [[Khair]] Tehsil.  It forms a part of [[Aligarh]] Division. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.thehindu.com/todays-paper/tp-national/tp-newdelhi/akhilesh-renames-eight-districts/article3676628.ece|title=Akhilesh renames eight districts|date=24 July 2012|access-date=6 January 2019|website=Thehindu.com}} < /ref >
