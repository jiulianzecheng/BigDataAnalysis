 '''Leucate''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]] of 4,428 people (2017) that is in the [[Regions of France|region]] [[Occitanie]] in the [[Aude]] [[Departments of France|department]] in the south of [[France]]. It lies between the [[Mediterranean Sea]] and the lagoon [[Étang de Leucate]].
