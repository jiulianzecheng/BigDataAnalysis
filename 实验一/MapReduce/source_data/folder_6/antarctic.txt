 The '''Antarctic''' is the area around the [[Earth]]'s [[South Pole]]. Its opposite is the [[Arctic]] region which is around the [[North Pole]].  The Antarctic includes the [[continent]] of [[Antarctica]] and the ices, waters and [[island]]s in the [[Southern Ocean]]. < ref > [http://www.scar.org/ Scientific Committee on Antarctic Research (SCAR)] < /ref >
