'''Thabaton''' or '''Thaba''' is a girl in [[Meitei folktales]]. She was loved and cared by her seven elder brothers. When her brothers were not at home, she was taken away by [[Keibu Keioiba]]. Her kidnapper was a tiger headed man. She lived with him for some time.
