 '''Rak'ah''' ( ركعة‎‎ rak'ah, plural: ركعات rakaʿāt), consists of the prescribed movements while offering prayers to [[God in Islam|God]] (Allah). It refers to a single unit of [[Salat|Islamic prayers]]. 
 
 == The number of rak'ahs in the obligatory prayer == 
 
 * [[Fajr]] 2 rak'ats 
 * 4 rak'ats of [[Zuhr prayer|Zuhr]] 
 * [[Asr]] 4 rak'ats 
 * [[Maghrib]] 3 rak'ats 
 * [[Isha'a|Isha]] 4 rak'ats 
 
 On Friday, the [[Jumu'ah|Friday prayer]], the noon prayer, the prayer is not obligatory two rakaata.
