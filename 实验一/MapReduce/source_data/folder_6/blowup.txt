 '''''Blowup''''' is a 1966 [[United Kingdom|British]] [[drama]] [[movie]] set in [[London]]. It was [[wikt:nominate|nominated]] for two [[Academy Award]] and won a [[Grand Prix]] award. It is [[movie director|directed]] by [[Italy|Italian]] movie director [[Michelangelo Antonioni]] and [[screenwriter|written]] by Antonioni, [[Toni Guerra]] and [[Edward Bond]]. It starred [[David Hemmings]] and [[Vanessa Redgrave]]. [[Carlo Ponti]] [[producer|produced]] the movie. This was Antonioni's first [[English language]] movie.
