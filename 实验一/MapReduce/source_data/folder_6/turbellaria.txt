
 '''Turbellaria''' are a [[Class (biology)|class]] of free-living [[flatworm]]s. Most of them are [[carnivore]]s. They actively search for food. Most of them are small, less than 60 cm in size. Almost all of them are [[aquatic]]. Some also live on land, in damp places.
