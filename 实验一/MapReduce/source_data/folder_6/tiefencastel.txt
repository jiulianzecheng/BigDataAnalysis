 '''Tiefencastel''' ({{lang-rm|Casti}}) was a [[municipality]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Graubünden]] in [[Switzerland]]. It was the capital of the [[Albula (district)|Albula district]]. It is next to the [[Albula Pass|Albula]] and [[Julier pass]]es. In 2013, 254 people lived there. 
 
 On 1 January 2015, the municipalities of [[Alvaschein]], [[Mon, Switzerland|Mon]], [[Stierva]], Tiefencastel, [[Alvaneu]], [[Brienz/Brinzauls]] and [[Surava]] joined together to become the new municipality of [[Albula/Alvra]].
