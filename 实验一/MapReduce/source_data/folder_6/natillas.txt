 '''Natillas''' is a type of food.  Natillas refers to a variety of dishes in [[Spanish language|Spanish]]-speaking countries. 
 
 In Spain natillas is a [[custard]] dish.  In [[Colombia]] the dish called '''natilla''' is often served at [[Christmas]].  It is like a flan or pudding.  In [[Peru]] natillas are from [[Piura]] and they are elaborated from [[chancaca]]. [[Mexico]] uses the term for a thicker version of the dessert drink called  " Atole leche " .  In [[Costa Rica]] it is a sour cream-like dairy product used as a [[condiment]].
