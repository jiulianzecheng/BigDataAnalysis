 '''''Latenivenatrix''''' (meaning  " hiding hunter " ) is a genus of [[Troodontidae|troodontid]]. It was previously identified as ''[[Troodon]]''.  
 
 ''Latenivenatrix'' has an estimated skull length of {{convert|18|cm}} and a size {{convert|3|-|3.5|m}}.
