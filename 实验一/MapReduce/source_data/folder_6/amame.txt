 " '''Amame''' "  ({{lang-en| " Love Me " }}) is a song by American singer [[Selena]].  " Amame "  became the fourth and last [[single (music)|single]] from ''[[Entre A Mi Mundo]]'' (1992).  " Amame "  peaked at number 27 on the [[Hot Latin Tracks]] and number 21 on the [[Regional Mexican Airplay]] chart. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.billboard.com/|title=Billboard - Music Charts, News, Photos  &  Video|website=Billboard}} < /ref >   " Amame "  was [[remix]]ed for ''[[Anthology]]'' (1998). It included a [[ballad]] version of the song.
