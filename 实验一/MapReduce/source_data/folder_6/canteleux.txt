 '''Canteleux''' is a former [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is in the [[Regions of France|region]] of [[Nord-Pas-de-Calais]] in the [[Pas-de-Calais]] [[Departments of France|department]]. This is in the north of [[France]]. On 1 January 2019, it was merged into [[Bonnières, Pas-de-Calais|Bonnières]]. < ref > [https://www.legifrance.gouv.fr/eli/arrete/2018/9/25/TERB1905549A/jo/texte Arrêté préfectoral] 25 September 2018, modified 26 October 2018 {{in lang|fr}} < /ref >
