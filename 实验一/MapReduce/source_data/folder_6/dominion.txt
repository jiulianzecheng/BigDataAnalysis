'''Dominion''' is a way to call [[Nation|nations]], that were part of the [[British Empire]]. They had their own [[Government|governments]]. < ref > {{Cite book|title=Merriam Webster's Dictionary|year=2006|edition=based on Collegiate vol., 11th|volume=|location=Springfield, MA: Merriam-Webster, Inc.}} < /ref >  The most [[famous]] dominions were for example [[Canada]], [[Australia]], [[Dominion of Pakistan|Pakistan]], [[New Zealand]] and [[South Africa]]. When the British Empire dissolved after [[World War II]], the [[word]] was changed to [[Commonwealth country]] and later [[Commonwealth Realm]].
