'''Carnoy''' is a former [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is found in the [[Regions of France|region]] [[Hauts-de-France]] in the [[Somme]] [[Departments of France|department]] in the north of [[France]]. On 1 January 2019, it was merged into the new commune of [[Carnoy-Mametz]]. < ref > [https://www.legifrance.gouv.fr/eli/arrete/2018/10/26/TERB1832590A/jo/texte Arrêté préfectoral] 26 October 2018 {{in lang|fr}} < /ref >
