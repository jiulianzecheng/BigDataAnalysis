 '''''Glyptodon''''' is a genus of [[glyptodont]]. It is part of the placental group of mammals known as [[Xenarthra]]. This order of mammals includes [[anteater]]s, tree [[sloth]]s, [[extinction|extinct]] [[ground sloth]]s, extinct [[Pampatheriidae|pampatheres]], and [[armadillo]]s. 
 
 ''Glyptodon'' came from [[South America]]. A related genus, ''[[Glyptotherium]]'',  first appeared in the south-west of the modern USA about 2.5 million years ago as a result of the [[Great American Interchange]]. 
 
 These [[herbivore]]s (plant-eaters) would not have been [[wikt:vulnerable|vulnerable]] to the [[Sparassodont]] carnivores of the day.
