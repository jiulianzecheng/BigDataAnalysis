 '''Dietikon''' is the fifth largest city of the [[Zurich (canton)|canton of Zurich]] in [[Switzerland]], after [[Zürich]], [[Winterthur]], [[Uster]] and [[Dübendorf]]. It is the capital of the same-named district of [[Dietikon (district)|Dietikon]].
