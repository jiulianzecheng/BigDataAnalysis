 '''Gumbrechtshoffen''' is a [[Communes in France|commune]] which is in [[Grand Est]] in the [[Bas-Rhin]] [[Departments of France|department]] in northeast [[France]]. It is {{convert|18|km|mi}} northwest of [[Haguenau]]. 
 
 == Geography == 
 The commune is placed between [[Niederbronn]] and [[Haguenau]], a couple of kilometres to the west of the main road that connects the two. It is traversed by the river [[Zinsel du Nord|Zinsel]].
