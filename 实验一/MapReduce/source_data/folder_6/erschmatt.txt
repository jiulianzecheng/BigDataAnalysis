 '''Erschmatt''' was a [[municipality]] of the [[district]] of [[Leuk (district)|Leuk]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Valais]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 On 1 January 2013, the former municipality of Erschmatt merged into the municipality of [[Leuk]].
