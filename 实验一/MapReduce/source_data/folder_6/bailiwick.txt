{{Short description|The area of jurisdiction of a bailiff}} 
 A '''bailiwick''' ({{IPAc-en|ˈ|b|eɪ|l|ɪ|w|ɪ|k}}) < ref > {{Cite web |date=2020 |title=Definition of Bailiwick |url=https://www.lexico.com/definition/bailiwick |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200528221259/https://www.lexico.com/definition/bailiwick |url-status=dead |archive-date=May 28, 2020 |access-date=2021-01-16 |website=Lexico.com |publisher=Oxford University Press |language=en}} < /ref >  is the area where a [[bailiff]] is in charge. It used to also mean territories where an appointed bailiff did what a [[sheriff]] usually does. The bailiff did that under a royal or imperial [[writ]].
