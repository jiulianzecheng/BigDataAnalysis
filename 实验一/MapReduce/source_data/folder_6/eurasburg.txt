 '''Eurasburg''' is a [[municipality]] in [[Bad Tölz-Wolfratshausen]], a district ([[Districts of Germany|''Landkreis'']]) in [[Upper Bavaria]]. It is {{convert|40|km|abbr=off}} south of the Bavarian state capital, [[Munich]].
