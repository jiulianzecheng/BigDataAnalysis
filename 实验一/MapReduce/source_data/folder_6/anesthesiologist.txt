An '''anesthesiologist''' (also '''anaesthesiologist''') is a [[Medical doctor|doctor]] of [[medicine]]. An anesthesiologist ([[American English]]) or anaesthetist ([[British English]]) is a professional trained in anesthesia and perioperative medicine. Terminology varies between countries. The main jobs of an anesthesiologist are: 
 *Providing [[anesthesia]] to a patient undergoing [[surgery]] 
 *[[Intensive care]] medicine 
 *[[Medical emergency|Medical emergency medicine]] 
 *[[Terminal illness|Palliative care]]  
 The first anesthesiologist was [[John Snow]] (1813{{ndash}}1858).
