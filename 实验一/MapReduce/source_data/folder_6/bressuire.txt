 '''Bressuire''' is a [[Communes of France|''commune'']] in the [[Deux-Sèvres]] [[Departments of France|department]] in the [[Nouvelle-Aquitaine]] [[Regions of France|region]] in western [[France]]. It is a [[Prefectures in France|subprefecture]] of the Deux-Sèvres department. It is also the [[Capital city|capital]] of the ''[[Arrondissements of France|arrondissement]]'' of the same name. 
 
 == Geography == 
 Bressuire is to the northwest of the Deux-Sèvres department. The city is at about {{Convert|254|km|mi|abbr=on }} from [[Bordeaux]], {{Convert|398|km|mi|abbr=on }} from [[Paris]], {{Convert|893|km|mi|abbr=on }} from [[Marseille]] and {{Convert|490|km|mi|abbr=on }} from [[Toulouse]]. < ref name= " annuaire " > {{cite web |work=Annuaire-Mairie.fr |title=Ville de Bressuire |url=http://www.annuaire-mairie.fr/ville-bressuire.html |language=fr |access-date=19 April 2017}} < /ref > 
 
 The ''commune'' has an area of {{Convert|180.6|km2|sqmi|abbr=on }}. < ref name= " insee-geo " > {{cite web |url=https://www.insee.fr/fr/statistiques/1405599?geo=COM-79049 |title=Commune de Bressuire (79049) |work=Comparateur de territoire |publisher=Institut national de la statistique et des études économiques - INSEE |language=fr |access-date=19 April 2017 }} < /ref >  Its average [[altitude]] is {{Convert|167|m|ft|abbr=on}}; at the [[City and town halls|city hall]], the altitude is {{Convert|182|m|ft|abbr=on }}. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://www.map-france.com/Bressuire-79300/ |title=Bressuire |publisher=Map-France.com |access-date=19 April 2017 }} < /ref >

 == Administration == 
 Bressuire is a [[Prefectures in France|subprefecture]] of the Deux-Sèvres department, the capital of the ''arrondissement'' of [[Arrondissement of Bressuire|Bressuire]] and the [[administrative centre]] ({{lang-fr|chef-lieu}}) of the canton of [[Canton of Bressuire|Bressuire]]. 
 
 It is part of the [[Communes of France|intercommunality]] ''Le Bocage Bressuirais'' ({{lang-fr|Communauté d'agglomération du Bocage Bressuirais}}).
