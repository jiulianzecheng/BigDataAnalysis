 '''Winterthur''' ({{IPA|ˈvɪntərtuːr}}; {{lang-fr|Winterthour}}; [[Latin language]]: ''Vitudurum'', locally often called: ''Winti'') is a [[city]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Zurich (canton)|Zurich]] in northern [[Switzerland]]. It is the sixth-largest city in Switzerland. About 138,000 people lived there in 2015. 
 
 ==Economy== 
 [[Sulzer (manufacturer)|Sulzer AG]], [[Swiss]] industrial engineering, is [[Headquarters|headquartered]] in Winterthur.
