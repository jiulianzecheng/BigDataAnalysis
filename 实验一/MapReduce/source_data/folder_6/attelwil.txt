 '''Attelwil''' is a former [[municipality]] of the [[district]] [[Zofingen (district)|Zofingen]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Aargau]] in [[Switzerland]]. On 1 January 2019, Attelwil merged into the municipality of [[Reitnau]].
