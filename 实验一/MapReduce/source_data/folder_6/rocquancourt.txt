'''Rocquancourt''' is a former [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is found in the [[Regions of France|region]] [[Basse-Normandie]] in the [[Calvados (department)|Calvados]] [[Departments of France|department]] in the northwest of [[France]]. On 1 January 2019, it was merged into the new commune of [[Castine-en-Plaine]]. < ref > [http://www.calvados.gouv.fr/IMG/pdf/recueil-14-2018-100-recueil-des-actes-administratifs-special-1.pdf Arrêté préfectoral] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200920112716/http://www.calvados.gouv.fr/IMG/pdf/recueil-14-2018-100-recueil-des-actes-administratifs-special-1.pdf |date=2020-09-20 }} 19 December 2018, p. 10 {{in lang|fr}} < /ref >
