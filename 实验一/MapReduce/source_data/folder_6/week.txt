A '''week''' is 7 [[day]]s in connecting order. There are usually 52 weeks in a [[year]]. 
 
 In the [[English language]], the days of the week are named after [[Deity|gods]] in [[Norse]] [[mythology]], except for [[Saturday]], which is named after a [[Roman mythology|Roman god]].

 Depending on the law of a country, the week either starts on Monday and ends on Sunday, or starts on Sunday and ends on Saturday. In most countries, Saturday and Sunday are the [[weekend]]. Friday, Saturday, and Sunday is a [[sabbath|religious day]] for Muslims, Jews, and Christians, respectively.
