 '''Dustin Patrick Runnels''' (born April 11, 1969) is an [[Americans|American]] [[Professional wrestling|professional wrestler]] and author. He currently works for [[All Elite Wrestling]] (AEW). Runnels was worked with the [[World Wrestling Entertainment|WWE]] under the [[ring name]]s '''Goldust''' and '''Dustin Rhodes.'''  Rhodes is the son of former professional wrestler  " The American Dream "  [[Dusty Rhodes]] and half-brother of  [[Cody Rhodes]]  < ref name= " wwe " > {{cite web|url= http://www.wwe.com/superstars/goldust|title=GOLDUSTBIO|accessdate=September 14, 2012|publisher=WWE}} < /ref >  During his time with the WWE he won multiple titles. These include the [[World Tag Team Championship (WWE)|World Tag Team Championship]] with [[Booker T (wrestler)|Booker T]] and with his half brother, the [[WWE Hardcore Championship]] seven times and the [[WWE Intercontinental Championship]] three times.
