'''Kaura''' (Tyap: '''Watyap''') is a town and a [[Local Government Area]] in southern part of [[Kaduna State]], [[Nigeria]]. It has an area of 461 & nbsp;km < sup > 2 < /sup >  and a population of 174,626 at the 2006 census.
