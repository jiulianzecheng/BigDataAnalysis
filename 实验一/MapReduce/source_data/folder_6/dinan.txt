 '''Dinan''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is found in the [[Regions of France|region]] [[Bretagne]] in the [[Côtes-d'Armor]] [[Departments of France|department]] in the west of [[France]]. On 1 January 2018, the former commune of [[Léhon]] was merged into Dinan. < ref > [https://www.legifrance.gouv.fr/eli/arrete/2017/9/30/INTB1736647A/jo/texte Arrêté préfectoral] 30 September 2017 {{in lang|fr}} < /ref > 
 
 ==Sisters cities== 
 Dinan is twinned with: 
 * {{Flagicon|Belgium}}  [[Dinant]], Belgium 
 * {{Flagicon|Spain}}  [[Lugo]], Spain 
 * {{Flagicon|UK}}  [[Exmouth]], England 
 
 ==Sport== 
 Dinan is a start of the 6th stage of the [[Tour de France|2011 Tour de France]].
