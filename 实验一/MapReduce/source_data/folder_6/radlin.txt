 '''Radlin''' is a town in [[Wodzisław County]], [[Silesian Voivodeship]], [[Poland]], with about 17,673 people (2004). 
 
 == Sports == 
 
 * [[Górnik Radlin]] - men's [[Volleyball in Poland|volleyball]] team playing in [[Polish Volleyball League]] (Polska Liga Siatkówki, PLS: Seria B in 2003/2004, Seria A in 2004/2005 season).
