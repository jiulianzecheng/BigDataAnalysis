 '''Robin Miriam Carlsson''' (or '''Robyn'''; born June 12, 1979) is a [[Sweden|Swedish]] recording artist and [[musician]]. She first became well-known worldwide during the late 1990s. She had a quite a few [[hit song]]s, including  " Do You Know (What it Takes) "  from her first record ''Robyn is Here''. Other singles include  " Dancing on My Own " ,  " Be Mine! "  and  " Crash and Burn Girl " . Robyn was born in [[Stockholm]], Sweden. She has been nominated for four Grammy Awards. < ref > http://www.grammy.com/files/53rdpresslist113010.pdf < /ref > 
 
 ==Personal life== 
 Robyn is engaged to Max Vitali.
