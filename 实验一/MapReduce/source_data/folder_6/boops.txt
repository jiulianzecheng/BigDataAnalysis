 '''''Boops''''' is a genus of [[ray-finned fish]] with the following species: 
 
 * ''[[Boops boops]]''  < small > [[Linnaeus]], 1758 < /small >  (bogue) 
 * ''[[Boops lineatus]]''  < small > Boulenger, 1892 < /small > 
 
 The [[salema porgy]] was once assigned to this genus before, but it is now in the monotypic genus ''Sarpa''.
