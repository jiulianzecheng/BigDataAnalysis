'''Coenobitidae''' is a [[Family (biology)|family]] of [[Hermit crab]]s that contains all [[species]] of land hermit crab. 
 
 Coenobitidae contains two [[genera]]: 
 * [[Coconut crab|Birgus]] 
 * Coenobita, which contains all land hermit crabs (excluding the Coconut Crab)
