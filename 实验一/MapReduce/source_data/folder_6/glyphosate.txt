 '''Glyphosate''' is a non-selective [[herbicide]] used in [[agriculture]] for the control of [[weed]]s, shrubs, and grasses. One [[brand name]] it is sold as is [[Monsanto]]'s ''Roundup''. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://pmep.cce.cornell.edu/profiles/extoxnet/dienochlor-glyphosate/glyphosate-ext.html|title=Glyphosate|website=pmep.cce.cornell.edu}} < /ref >  It is also used in genetically modified crops. < ref > http://aob.oxfordjournals.org/cgi/content/abstract/mcp152v1{{Dead link|date=September 2021 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }} < /ref > < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.monsanto.com/products/techandsafety/herbicide_scipubs.asp|title=Monsanto homepage|publisher=|access-date=2009-08-09|archive-date=2009-07-22|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20090722120015/http://www.monsanto.com/products/techandsafety/herbicide_scipubs.asp|url-status=dead}} < /ref >
