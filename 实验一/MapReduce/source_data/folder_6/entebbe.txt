 '''Entebbe''' is a [[town]] in central [[Uganda]]. It is the second largest city in the country after [[Kampala]]. Entebbe is found on the shores of [[Lake Victoria]]. < ref > [https://www.bbc.com/news/magazine-36559375 | Entebbe: A mother's week of 'indescribable fear' -] BBC News < /ref > < ref > [https://www.cnn.com/2014/07/03/world/aviation-security/index.html | U.S. embassy warns of security threat to Uganda airport -] CNN < /ref >  [[Entebbe International Airport]], which is the main and busiest [[airport]] of Uganda, is located in the town. 
 The famous [[Operation Entebbe]] took place at Entebbe Airport in 1976.
