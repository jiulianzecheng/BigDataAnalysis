 '''''Duriavenator''''' is a [[genus]] of [[theropod]] [[dinosaur]] described in 2008. It was excavated in [[Dorset]]. The [[type species]] is ''D. hesperis'', formerly known as ''[[Megalosaurus]] hesperis''. < ref name=W74 > {{cite journal|last=Waldman|first=M.|year=1974|title=Megalosaurids from the Bajocian (Middle Jurassic) of Dorset|url=http://www.dinosaures-web.com/sites/default/files/publications/Pages%20325-339.pdf|journal=Palaeontology|volume=17|issue=2|pages=325â€“339}} < /ref >   
 
 ''Duriavenator'' lived about 170 million years ago. It is one of the oldest-known [[tetanuran]]s.
