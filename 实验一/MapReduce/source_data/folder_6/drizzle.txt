'''Drizzle''' is the name given to light [[rain]]. The drop size of drizzle is smaller than that of rain, about 0.5 & nbsp;mm ([[millimeter]]s) in diameter. < ref > National Weather Service Observing Handbook No. 8, Aviation Weather Observations for Supplementary Aviation Weather Reporting Stations (SAWRS), Manual Observations, October 1996 < /ref >
