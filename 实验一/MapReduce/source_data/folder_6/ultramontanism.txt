'''Ultranontanism''' was a movement within the [[Catholic Church|Roman Catholic Church]] of the 18th and 19th century. It placed strong emphasis on what the [[pope]] had to say. The name in composed of ultra ( " beyond " ), and refered to the fact that the pope at that time was on the other side of the [[Alps]].
