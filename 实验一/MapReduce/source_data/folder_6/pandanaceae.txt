 '''Pandanaceae''' is a family of [[flowering plant]]s found in the [[Old World]] tropical and subtropical regions, from [[West Africa]] through the [[Pacific Ocean|Pacific]]. It contains five [[Genus|genera]] and about 900 [[species]] of trees, shrubs and root climbers. Most members of the family prefer living in coastal or marshy areas.
