 '''Wellingborough''' is a [[town]] in [[Northamptonshire]], [[England]]. In 2001 there were 72,519 people living in the town. < ref > {{cite web |url= http://www.statistics.gov.uk/census2001/pyramids/pages/34uh.asp |title=Census 2001 - Population Pyramids - Wellingborough |first= |last= |work=statistics.gov.uk |year=2011 |accessdate=30 April 2011}} < /ref >  The town is near the [[A45 road (Great Britain)|A45 road]]. 
 
 Wellingborough is an [[Anglo-Saxon]] town. It and was settled in the [[6th century|sixth century]] by the [[Saxon]]s. It was first known as ''Wendeling burh'' -  " the stronghold of Waendel's people " . < ref > {{cite web |url=http://www.wellingborough.gov.uk/info/200064/local_history_and_heritage/846/a_history_of_wellingborough |title=Borough Council of Wellingborough - Early History of Wellingborough |first= |last= |work=wellingborough.gov.uk |year=2011 |accessdate=30 April 2011 |archive-date=29 September 2011 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110929163700/http://www.wellingborough.gov.uk/info/200064/local_history_and_heritage/846/a_history_of_wellingborough |url-status=dead }} < /ref >
