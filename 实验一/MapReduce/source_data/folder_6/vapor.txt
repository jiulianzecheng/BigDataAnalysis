 '''Vapor''' ([[British English]]: '''vapour''') is when small drops of [[liquid]] float in the air.  Sometimes this happens because the liquid has been heated.  Vapor is not the same as [[gas]].  It is a mixture of liquid and gas. The usual word for this is [[:wikt:steam|steam]].
