 '''Dasyuridae''' is a [[family (biology)|family]] of [[marsupial]] [[carnivore]]s. It has nearly all non-[[extinct]] members of the [[order (biology)|order]] ''[[Dasyuromorphia]]''. This means the [[quoll]]s, [[phascogale]]s, [[antechinus]]es, [[dunnart]]s, [[planigale]]s, and [[Tasmanian devil]] (about 55 species in all). 
 
 == Classification == 
 * Famliy '''Dasyuridae''' 
 **Subfamily ''Dasyurinae'': quolls and allies 
 ** Subfamily ''Phascogalinae'': phascogales and antechinuses 
 ** Subfamily ''Sminthopsinae'': dunnarts and allies 
 ** Subfamily ''Planigalinae'': planigales and ningauis
