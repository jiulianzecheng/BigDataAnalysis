 '''Christopher Brian Bridges''' (born September 11, 1977), known professionally as '''Ludacris''' ({{IPAc-en|ˈ|l|uː|d|ə|k|r|ɪ|s}}), is an American rapper and actor. From 1998 to 1999, he studied music management at [[Georgia State University]]. In 2004 he rapped on the [[Usher]] single  " Yeah! "
