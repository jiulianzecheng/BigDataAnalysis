 '''Antofagasta''' is a [[city]] in northern [[Chile]]. It is the capital of the [[province of Antofagasta]] and of the [[region of Antofagasta]]. In the 2002 census, the city had a population of 296,905. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.ine.cl/ine/canales/chile_estadistico/demografia_y_vitales/demografia/pdf/cdpubaldcasjunio2005.zip|title=Cities, Towns, Villages and Hamlets|editor=National Institute of Statistics of Chile|access-date=22 June 2007}} < /ref >
