 '''QuikTrip''' (abbreviated '''QT''')  is a [[Tulsa]]-based chain of [[convenience store]]s mainly found in the [[Midwest]]ern and [[U.S. Southern states|Southern]] [[United States]] (not to be confused with [[Kwik Trip]] convenience stores).
