 '''Wizex''' is a [[dansband]] from [[Sweden]]. < ref > [http://www.discogs.com/artist/1005351-Wizex Discogs] < /ref >  It was established in [[1973]]. With [[Kikki Danielsson]] as vocalist, the band participated att [[Melodifestivalen]] in [[1978]] with the song ''Miss Decibel'', finishing second behind the [[Björn Skifs]] song ''Det blir alltid värre framåt natten''.
