 '''Pierogi''' are a type of [[dumpling]] from [[Eastern Europe]]. Pierogi are made by wrapping unleavened [[dough]] (dough without [[yeast]]) around a savoury or sweet filling and cooking in [[boiling]] water. They are often then pan-fried before serving.
