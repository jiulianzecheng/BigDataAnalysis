 '''Didhu''' is a small village in Pokaran Tehsil of [[Jaisalmer|Jaisalmer District]] of [[Rajasthan]]. According to the 2011 census, the village has a [[population]] of 1730, out of which 945 are males and 785 are females, and 163 families live in the village. The population of children aged 0-6 is 357 which is 20.64% of the total village population. < ref > {{cite web|title=Didhoo Village Population - Pokaran - Jaisalmer, Rajasthan|url=https://www.census2011.co.in/data/village/86251-didhoo-rajasthan.html|website=www.census2011.co.in}} < /ref >  According to the Constitution of India and the Panchayati Raj Act, Didhu is [[Administrator|administered]] by the Panchayat Ajasar Sarpanch, who is the representative of the village and the Panchayat.
