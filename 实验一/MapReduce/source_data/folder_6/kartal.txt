 '''Kartal''' is a district of [[Istanbul]], [[Turkey]]. It is located on the [[Asian]] side of the [[city]]. It is on the [[coast]] of the [[Sea of Marmara|Marmara Sea]] between [[Maltepe]] and [[Pendik]]. Its total area is {{convert|38.54|km2}}. < ref name=AK > {{cite web |url=http://en.kartal.bel.tr/content/kartal-guide-about-the-kartal.aspx |title=About Kartal |author= < !--Staff writer(s); no by-line.-- >  |date= |website= |publisher=Kartal Municipality |accessdate=2 January 2015 |archive-date=12 May 2015 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150512143557/http://en.kartal.bel.tr/content/kartal-guide-about-the-kartal.aspx |url-status=dead }} < /ref >  It has a population of nearly 500,0000 people. < ref name=AK/ > 
 
 Kartal behind the coast the land rises sharply up to [[Yakacık Hill]] ( " the balcony of Istanbul " ) and [[Aydos]], on these hills the land is fertile. < ref > [https://www.kartal24.com/113310-highest-hill-of-istanbul What is the highest point of Istanbul?] Kartal 24 < /ref >  Kartal has a mild climate. < ref > Volkan Altay; et al., 'Urban flora and ecological characteristics of the Kartal
