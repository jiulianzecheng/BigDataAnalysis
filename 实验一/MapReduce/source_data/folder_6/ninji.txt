{{nihongo|'''Ninji'''|仁治|}}, also called '''Jinji,''' was a {{nihongo|[[Japanese era name]]|年号,|''nengō'',|lit.  " year name " }} after ''[[En'ō]]'' and before ''[[Kangen]].''  This period started in August 1240 and ended in January 1243. < ref > [[Louis-Frédéric|Nussbaum, Louis-Frédéric]]. (2005). [https://books.google.com/books?id=p2QnPijAEmEC & pg=PA716   " ''Ninji'' " ] in ''Japan encyclopedia'', p. 716. < /ref >  The reigning emperors were {{nihongo|[[Emperor Shijō|Shijō]]''-tennō''|四条天皇}} and {{nihongo|[[Emperor Go-Saga|Go-Saga]]''-tennō''|後嵯峨天皇}}. < ref > [[Isaac Titsingh|Titsingh, Isaac]]. (1834). [https://books.google.com/books?id=18oNAAAAIAAJ & pg=PA242  ''Annales des empereurs du Japon'', pp. 242-245;] [[H. Paul Varley|Varley, H. Paul]]. (1980). ''Jinnō Shōtōki.'' p. 228-231. < /ref > 
 
 ==Events of the ''Ninji'' Era== 
 * '''1242''' (''Ninji 3, 10th day of the 1st month''): In the 10th year of Shijō's reign, the emperor died; < ref > Titsingh, [https://books.google.com/books?id=18oNAAAAIAAJ & pg=PA244  p. 244]; [[Imperial Household Agency]] (''Kunaichō''), [http://www.kunaicho.go.jp/ryobo/guide/087/index.html 四條天皇 (87)]; retrieved 2012-5-16. < /ref >  and the succession (''senso'') < ref > Varley, p. 44; a distinct act of ''senso'' is unrecognized prior to [[Emperor Tenji]]; and all sovereigns except [[Empress Jitō|Jitō]], [[Emperor Yōzei|Yōzei]], [[Emperor Go-Toba|Go-Toba]], and [[Emperor Fushimi|Fushimi]] have ''senso'' and ''sokui'' in the same year until the reign of [[Emperor Go-Murakami]]. Compare ''Kunaichō'', [http://www.kunaicho.go.jp/e-about/seido/seido06-ph.html  Ceremony of Accession (''Sokui-no-Rei'')]; retrieved 2012-5-22. < /ref >  was received by the second son of former Emperor Tsuchimikado. < ref > Titsingh, [https://books.google.com/books?id=18oNAAAAIAAJ & pg=PA244  pp. 244-245;] Varley, p. 228. < /ref > 
 * '''1242''' (''Ninji 3, 5th month''): Emperor Go-Saga's role as emperor was confirmed by [[wikt:ceremony|ceremonies]] (''sokui''). < ref > Titsingh, [https://books.google.com/books?id=18oNAAAAIAAJ & pg=PA245  p. 245;] Varley, p. 44. < /ref > 
 * '''14 July 1242''' (''Ninji 3, 15th day of the 6th month''): [[Hōjō Yasutoki]] died at age 60. < ref > Titsingh, [https://books.google.com/books?id=18oNAAAAIAAJ & pg=PA246  p. 246.] < /ref > 
 * '''7 October 1242''' (''Ninji 3, 12th day of the 9th month''): Former [[Emperor Juntoku]] died in exile on [[Sado Island]]. < ref > Titsingh, [https://books.google.com/books?id=18oNAAAAIAAJ & pg=PA246  p. 246]; ''Kunaichō'', [http://www.kunaicho.go.jp/ryobo/guide/084/index.html 順徳天皇 (84)]; retrieved 2012-5-16. < /ref >
