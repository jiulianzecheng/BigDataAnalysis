 '''Sassnitz''' (before 1993: ''Saßnitz'') is a town in [[Mecklenburg-Western Pomerania]], [[Germany]]. It is on the [[Rügen]] Island. In 2021, 9,064 people lived there. 
 
 Sassnitz is a [[seaside resort]] and [[port]] town.
