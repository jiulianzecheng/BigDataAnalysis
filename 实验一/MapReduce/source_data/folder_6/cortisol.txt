 '''Cortisol''' also called the [[Stress (medicine)|stress]] [[hormone]], is produced in the [[adrenal gland]]. It is a [[steroid]] hormone. Cortisol's purpose is to help the body: 
 * metabolize [[glucose]] 
 * control [[blood pressure]] 
 * suppress immunity 
 * Inflammatory response
