 '''''Vampirina''''' is an American-Irish animated television series first aired on October 1, 2017. The show is based on the  " Vampirina Ballerina "  series of books written by Anne Marie Pace and illustrated by LeUyen Pham, published by Disney-Hyperion. [[Disney Junior]] announced the series in March, 2016; the adaptation is being worked on by executives from ''[[Doc McStuffins]]''. < ref > {{cite web |url= https://variety.com/2016/tv/news/2016-tv-upfront-disney-junior-lion-guard-vampirina-1201722712/ |work=[[Variety (magazine)|Variety]] |title=Disney Junior Renews ‘Lion Guard,’ Orders ‘Vampirina,’ ‘Puppy Dog Pals’ |first=Brian |last=Steinberg |date=4 March 2016 |quote=“Vampirina” is inspired by Disney Publishing’s popular children’s book series “Vampirina Ballerina,” and tells the story of a young vampire girl who becomes the new kid in town when her family moves from Transylvania to Pennsylvania. The show will be led by many of the executives behind “Doc McStuffins,”}} < /ref >
