 The '''Baixa''' ({{IPA-pt|ˈbajʃɐ põbɐˈlinɐ|IPA}}), is a neighborhood in the historic center of [[Lisbon]], [[Capital city|capital]] of [[Portugal]].  It is north of the [[Praça do Comércio]], somewhat between the Cais do Sodré and the [[Alfama]] district beneath the [[Lisbon Castle]], and goes north towards the [[Avenida da Liberdade (Lisbon)]].
