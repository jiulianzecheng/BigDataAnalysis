 '''Promotion''' means spreading [[information]] about an product, service or issue.  
 
 Promotional activity generally involves a company spreading knowledge about its brand, products, or service to generate demand, build a following, raise awareness, or build loyalty. Many of these activities taking place simultaneously, and including channels such as: Social Media, Direct Mail, Print, and Television. 
 
 Promotion as part of [[marketing]] means spreading information about a [[Product (consumer goods)|product]], product line, [[Trademark|brand]], or [[company]]. 
 ''Promotion'' includes: 
 * Publicity and public relations 
 * Advertising 
 
 ''Promotion for marketing'' includes additionally: 
 * Personal selling 
 * Non-personal selling 
 * Sales promotion 
 * Tradeshows 
 * Direct selling 
 * Refer a friend 
 * Loyalty bonus 
 * Product placement

 Promotion can also happen when a marketing company is providing discounts and coupons to customers to create brand awareness.
