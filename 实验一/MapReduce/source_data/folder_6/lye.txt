'''Lye''' is the name of different things: 
 
 *'''Lye''' is a [[caustic]] [[solution]] used for [[glass]] and [[soap]] making. It may have in it: 
 **[[sodium hydroxide]] ([[sodium|soda]] lye) or  
 **less commonly, [[potassium hydroxide]] ([[potassium|potash]] lye). 
 *'''[[Lye, West Midlands]]''' is the name of a small town in [[England]], between [[Dudley]] and [[Stourbridge]] in the [[Black Country]], [[Metropolitan Borough of Dudley|Dudley Metropolitan Borough]], [[West Midlands (county)|West Midlands]] (it was in [[Worcestershire]]). 
 *'''[[Lye, Indre|Lye]]''' is a [[commune in France|commune]] of the [[Indre (department)|Indre]] ''[[département in France|département]]'', in [[France]].
