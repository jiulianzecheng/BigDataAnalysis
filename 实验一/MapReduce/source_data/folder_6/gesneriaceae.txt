 '''Gesneriaceae''' is a one of 23 families in the order [[Lamiales]] of [[flowering plant]], consisting of 147 genera and about 3,200 species of mostly tropical and subtropical herbaceous or slightly woody plants. Many are of economic importance as horticultural ornamentals. Among these are the [[Saintpaulia|African violets]] and [[Gloxinia (genus)|gloxinias]].
