'''Amrapur''' is a small village in Jamnagar, [[Gujarat]], India. 
 The road to it is from Kalavad to Ranuja, Dhutarpur, Summary, Kharavedha, Amrapur. 
 
 Amrapur is surrounded on three sides by a dam. 
 
 Most people there are farmers. Some of them are connected with [[animal husbandry]].
