 '''Kaysersberg''' is a former [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is found in the [[Haut-Rhin]] [[Departments of France|department]] of eastern [[France]]. On 1 January 2016, it was merged into the new commune of [[Kaysersberg-Vignoble]]. < ref > [http://www.legifrance.gouv.fr/jo_pdf.do?id=JORFTEXT000031665358 Arrêté] 14 July 2015 {{in lang|fr}} < /ref >
