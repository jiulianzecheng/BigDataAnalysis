 '''Kristinestad''' ({{lang-fi|Kristiinankaupunki}}) is a town in the [[Ostrobothnia (region)|Ostrobothia]] region of [[Finland]]. There were about 7,000 people living there in January 2014. It is next to the [[municipalities]] of [[Isojoki]], [[Karijoki]], [[Merikarvia]], [[Närpes]] and [[Teuva]]. The people living in the town are [[bilingual]]; about 56% of the people speak [[Swedish language|Swedish]].
