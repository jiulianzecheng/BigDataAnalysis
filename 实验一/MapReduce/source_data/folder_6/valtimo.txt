 '''Valtimo''' was a municipality in [[North Karelia]], [[Finland]]. As of January 2014, almost 2,385 people lived there. Municipalities next to it are [[Nurmes]], [[Rautavaara]] and [[Sotkamo]]. It was merged with the town of Nurmes on 1 January 2020.
