 '''WJBQ''' is a [[radio station]] from [[Portland, Maine]]. It is also known as '''Q97.9'''. It has a morning show where Rob Steele does  " The Interactive 8 at 8 " , a countdown of the eight most [[Popularity|popular]] songs of the day.
