 '''Josenid''' (born December 3, 1998; [[Panama]] < ref > [http://www.saladeespera.com.pa/pda/josenid-picara-y-sonadora Josenid: Pícara y soñadora] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20161220112221/http://www.saladeespera.com.pa/pda/josenid-picara-y-sonadora |date=2016-12-20 }} {{in lang|es}} < /ref > ), is a child-genre singer from Panama. Josenid started singing when she was 9, while she was participating in a contest called ''Canta Conmigo'' in Panama. < ref > [http://www.letras.com.br/biografia/josenid Josenid's career] < /ref > < ref name= " Torres " > Torres, Vicglamar (2010)  " [http://www.peopleenespanol.com/article/josenid-la-nueva-revelacion-de-la-musica-infantil Josenid, la nueva revelación de la música infantil] " , www.peopleenespanol.com, 26 August 2010, retrieved 2011-12-23 < /ref >   Josenid placed 3rd. After the festival Josenid collaborated with Makano and she recorded the song  " Su Nombre en mi Cuaderno "  (Your Name In My Notebook),  and her first single  " Amor de colegio "  (grade school Love) in 2010. Josenid after her song I love college 4 more songs and Josen said to be ready to create some more songs.
