 '''Member''' may also mean: 
   
 * [[Limb]], a part of the human or animal body 
 * [[Element (mathematics)|Element]], an object that belongs to a mathematical set 
 * [[Military jury]], which is called  " Members "  by people in the military 
 * [[Penis]] (slang) 
 * [[User (computing)|Users]] who have signed up for a specific [[internet]] service
