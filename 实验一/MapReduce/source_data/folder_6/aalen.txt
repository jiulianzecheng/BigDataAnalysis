 '''Aalen''' ([[German phonology|pronounced]] {{IPA|[ˈaːlən]}}) is a [[town]] in the [[Germany|German]] state of [[Baden-Württemberg]]. It is the capital of the [[Ostalbkreis]] district, and its largest town.
