 The '''Montaukett''' or '''Montauk''' are [[Native American]] people from [[Long Island]], [[New York (state)|New York]]. They are part of the [[Algonquian languages|Algonquian]]-language group. They are also part of the [[Northeastern Woodlands]]. They are related to the  [[Pequot]] and [[Narragansett people|Narragansett peoples]]. Montauketts were allied with the settlers during the [[Pequot War]]. Colonization spread disease and made the population smaller. Montauk descendants live in Wisconsin and the [[Shinnecock Reservation]] in New York.
