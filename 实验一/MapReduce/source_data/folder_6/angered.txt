 '''Angered''' is a [[Urban areas in Sweden|locality]] in [[Gothenburg Municipality]] in [[Västra Götaland County]] in [[Sweden]]. It is located 12 kilometers north of the town of [[Gothenburg]]. In 2010, 950 people lived there.
