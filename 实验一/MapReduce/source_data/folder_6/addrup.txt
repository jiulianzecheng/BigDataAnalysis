 '''Addrup''' is a village in [[Cloppenburg (district)|Cloppenburg]], in [[Lower Saxony]], [[Germany]]. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 Addrup borders the villages of Gut Lage, Uptloh, Bevern, Calhorn and Stadtsholte within the [[Essen (Oldenburg)]] municipality. To the east, Addrup borders Lüsche in [[Bakum]] in [[Vechta (district)|Vechta]]. Being on the border of the districts of Cloppenburg and Vechta, Addrup is in the center of the [[Oldenburg Münsterland]]. < ref name= " addrup-home " > Addrup.de: [https://addrup.de/ Homepage] < /ref >
