 '''Mirande''' ({{lang-oc|Miranda}}) is a [[Communes of France|commune]] in the [[Gers]] [[Departments of France|department]], of which it is a [[Prefectures in France|subprefecture]], in the [[Occitanie]] [[Regions of France|region]], southwestern [[France]]. It is the administrative center of the ''arrondissement'' of [[Arrondissement of Mirande|Mirande]] and of the [[Cantons of France|canton]] of [[Canton of Mirande-Astarac|Mirande-Astarac]]. 
 
 ==History== 
 Mirande was founded in 1281 near the village of ''Saint-Jean de Lezian'' by the [[Monastery|abbot]] Berdoues and by Bernhard IV, Count of Astarac. The town was built as a [[bastide]] (a [[Fortification|fortified]] town). Miranda became, in 1297, the [[Capital (city)|capital]] of the ''County of Astarac''. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://www.ot-mirande.com/foundation.php |title=The foundation of Mirande |publisher=Mirande, Office of Tourism |language= |accessdate=24 June 2014 |date= }} < /ref > 
 
 Mirande was surrounded by a wall with four [[city gate]]s; the wall was destroyed during the 19th century. 
 
 Since 17 February 1800, Mirande is the capital of the ''arrondissement'' of [[Arrondissement of Mirande|Mirande]]. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 Mirande is a ''commune'' found along the [[Baïse]] river in the ''[[Astarac]]'', a [[natural region]] that is part of ''[[Gascony]]''. 
 
 The ''commune'' has an area of {{Convert|23.42|km2|sqmi|abbr=on }} and its average [[altitude]] is {{Convert|170|m|ft|abbr=on}}; at the [[City and town halls|city hall]], the altitude is {{Convert|163|m|ft|abbr=on }}. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://www.map-france.com/Mirande-32300/ |title=Mirande |publisher=Map-France.com |language= |accessdate=24 June 2014 |date= }} < /ref > 
 
 Mirande is at {{Convert|20|km|mi|0|abbr=on }} from [[Auch]], {{Convert|100|km|mi|0|abbr=on }} from [[Toulouse]], {{Convert|45|km|mi|0|abbr=on }} from [[Tarbes]] and {{Convert|80|km|mi|0|abbr=on }} from [[Lourdes]].

 ===Climate=== 
 The climate of Mirande, in the [[Köppen climate classification]], is '''Cfb''' - Oceanic climate with template summers. < ref > {{cite web |work=Annuaire-Mairie.fr |publisher= |title=Ville de Mirande |url=http://www.annuaire-mairie.fr/ville-mirande.html |language=French  |accessdate=24 June 2013}} < /ref > 
 
 ==Population== 
 The inhabitants of Mirande are known, in [[French language|French]], as ''Mirandais'' (women: ''Mirandaises''). < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.habitants.fr/habitants_mirande_32256.html |title=Mirande (32300) |publisher=habitants.fr |language=French |accessdate=7 March 2017 |date= }} < /ref > 
 
 With a population of 3,527 (2014), < ref name=insee > {{cite web |url=http://www.insee.fr/fr/statistiques/fichier/2525755/dep32.pdf |title=Régions, départements, arrondissements, cantons et communes |work= Populations légales 2014 |publisher=Institut national de la statistique et des études économiques - INSEE |format=PDF |language=French |accessdate=7 March 2017|date= }} < /ref >  Mirande has a [[population density]] of {{Pop density|3527|23.42|km2|prec=0|disp=num}} inhabitants/km < sup > 2 < /sup > .
