 '''Marquinhos''' (real name '''Marcos Aoás Corrêa''', born 14 May 1994 in [[São Paulo]]) is a [[Brazil]]ian footballer. He plays as a defender for [[Paris Saint-Germain F.C.]] in [[France]]. 
 
 ''[[The Guardian]]'' newspaper think that he is one of the 10 best young football players in the world. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.theguardian.com/football/blog/2014/jan/19/next-10-big-things-europes-top-youngsters|title=Europe's top youngsters and stars of the future - Observer Sport staff|first=Observer Sport|last=staff|date=19 January 2014|publisher=|via=www.theguardian.com}} < /ref > 
 
 He was bought by his current club from [[A.S. Roma]] for €32 million in 2013. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.espnfc.com/story/1502852/paris-saint-germain-sign-marquinhos-from-roma|title=Marquinhos seals PSG switch|website=ESPNFC.com}} < /ref >
