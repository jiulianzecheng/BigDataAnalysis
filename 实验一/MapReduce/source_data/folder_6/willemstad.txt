 '''Willemstad''' is the [[capital city]] of [[Curaçao]], an [[island]] in the southern [[Caribbean Sea]] that forms a [[Countries of the Kingdom of the Netherlands|constituent country]] of the [[Kingdom of the Netherlands]]. In 2008, 140,000 people lived there.
