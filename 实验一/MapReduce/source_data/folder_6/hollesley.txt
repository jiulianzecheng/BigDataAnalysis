 '''Hollesley''' is a village and [[civil parish]] in [[Suffolk Coastal]], [[Suffolk]], England. In 2001 there were 1356 people living in Hollesley. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.ons.gov.uk/help/localstatistics|title=Local statistics - Office for National Statistics|website=www.ons.gov.uk}} < /ref >  Hollesley is the largest village on the southern tip of the [[Sandlings Peninsula]]. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.villagevoices.org.uk/Hollesley.htm|title=Hollesley|website=www.villagevoices.org.uk}} < /ref >
