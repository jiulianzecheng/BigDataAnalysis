 '''Cannabaceae''' the hemp family of the [[Rosales|rose order]], containing 11 genera and 270 species of aromatic herbs distributed throughout temperate parts of the [[Northern Hemisphere]].  
 
 == Description == 
 Older authorities included the two genera, [[Cannabis]] and [[Humulus]], in the [[Moraceae|mulberry family]]. These and the former hackberry family (Celtidaceae) are now included in Cannabaceae. Members of the family are erect or climbing plants with petalless flowers and dry, one-seeded fruits. Hemp (''Cannabis'') and hop (''Humulus'') are the only economically important species.
