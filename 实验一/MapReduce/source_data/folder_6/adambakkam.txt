'''Adambakkam''' is a neighbourhood (locality) present in the [[Chennai]] metropolitan city, [[Tamil Nadu]]. This area is a thickly populated area. This is situated near the [[St. Thomas Mount Railway Station]]. 
 
 Before October 2011, a part of Adambakkam was under Chennai Corporation and the other part under [[Alandur]] municipality.But after that was joined totally with Chennai Corporation. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.thehindu.com/news/cities/chennai/expanded-chennai-corporationto-be-divided-into-3-regions/article2658830.ece|title=Expanded Chennai Corporationto be divided into 3 regions|date=25 November 2011|publisher=|via=www.thehindu.com}} < /ref >  The Adambakkam is having its new [[Adambakkam railway station|railway station]] under construction. 
 
 This area has many temples like famous Pazhandi Amman Temple.
