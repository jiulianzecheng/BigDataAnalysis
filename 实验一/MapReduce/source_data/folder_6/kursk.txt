 '''Kursk''' ({{lang-ru|Курск}}) is a city in [[Russia]]. It is the capital and the largest city of the [[Kursk Oblast]]. It is about {{convert|400|km|abbr=off}} south of [[Moscow]]. Kursk is where a large tank battle happened in World War 2. Three thousand German tanks and five thousand Soviet tanks attacked each other. The Soviets won the battle.
