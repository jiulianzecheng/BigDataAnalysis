 '''Aidhausen''' is a [[Municipalities in Germany|municipality]] in [[Haßberge (district)|Haßberge]] in [[Bavaria]] in [[Germany]], it is a member of the [[Verwaltungsgemeinschaft Hofheim in Unterfranken]]. 
 
 == Geography == 
 Aidhausen is in the [[Main (river)|Main]]-[[Rhön]] region. 
 It is divided into the following districts: Aidhausen, [[Friesenhausen]], [[Happertshausen]], [[Kerbfeld]] and [[Nassach (Aidhausen)|Nassach]].
