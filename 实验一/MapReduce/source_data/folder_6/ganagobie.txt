 '''Ganagobie''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]] in the [[Alpes-de-Haute-Provence]] [[Departments of France|department]] in southeastern [[France]]. 
 
 It is the site of a [[Benedictine]] monastery, the Abbey of Our Lady of Ganagobie.
