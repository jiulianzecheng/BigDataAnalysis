 '''Achterhooks''' ({{lang-nl|Achterhoeks}}) is a [[Dutch Low Saxon]] [[dialect]], spoken in the [[Achterhoek]] region in the province of [[Gelderland]], in the east part of the [[Netherlands]] near the [[Germany|German]] border.
