 The '''vireos''' ({{IPAc-en|ˈ|v|ɪr|i|.|oʊ}}) are a group of [[passerine]] [[bird]]s that are mostly in the part of the [[New World]]. Their feathers are dull-colored and greenish, the smaller species slightly [[wikt:resemblance|resembling]] [[New World warbler|wood warblers]]. New evidence from [[DNA]] places vireos as relatives of [[crow]]s. < ref name=Britannica > {{cite encyclopedia |url= http://www.britannica.com/EBchecked/topic/629820/vireo |title=vireo (bird) -- Britannica Online Encyclopedia |first= |encyclopedia=britannica.com |year=2012 |accessdate=27 May 2012}} < /ref >  Vireos are mostly shy. < ref name=Britannica/ >
