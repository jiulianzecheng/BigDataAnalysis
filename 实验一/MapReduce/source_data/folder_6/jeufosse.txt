 '''Jeufosse''' is a former [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is in [[Île-de-France]] in the [[Yvelines]] [[Departments of France|department]] in north-central [[France]]. On 1 January 2019, it was merged into the new commune of [[Notre-Dame-de-la-Mer]]. < ref > [https://www.legifrance.gouv.fr/eli/arrete/2018/9/27/TERB1905556A/jo/texte Arrêté préfectoral] 27 September 2018 {{in lang|fr}} < /ref >
