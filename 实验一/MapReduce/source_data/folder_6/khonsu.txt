 '''Khonsu''' is the [[god]] of the [[moon]], moonlight, and [[time]] in [[Egyptian mythology]]. In the beginning of the gods, he [[Gambling|gambled]] time with the sky goddess [[Nut (goddess)|Nut]]. The goddess [[Nut (goddess)|Nut]] won so many times that she could then add five days to the calendar. These days were called the  " devil days " . Khonsu's father is [[Amun]], and his mother is [[Mut]].
