 '''Cotacachi''' is a town that is the seat of [[Cotacachi Canton]], [[Imbabura Province]], [[Ecuador]], in [[South America]]. Cotacachi is located at an altitude of {{convert|2418|m|ft}} and had a population of 8,848 in 2010. < ref > " Cotacachi " . https://www.citypopulation.de/php/ecuador.php?cityid=100350, accessed 8 Jun 2017. < /ref >
