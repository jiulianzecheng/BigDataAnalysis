 '''Leysin''' is a [[municipality]] of the [[Aigle (district)|Aigle]] [[district]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Vaud]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 == Geography == 
 Leysin is in the [[Vaud Alps|Vaud]] [[Swiss Alps|Alps]], it is a alpine resort village at the eastern end of [[Lake Geneva]] in near to [[Montreux]], [[Lausanne]], and [[Geneva]]. It is on the right side of the [[Grande Eau]] at the foot of the [[Tour d'Aï]] and the [[Tour de Mayen]]. The main village is found at an elevation of {{convert|1260|m|ft|abbr=off}}. It consists of the village of Leysin and the [[hamlet (place)|hamlets]] of Veyges, Feydey, Aï and Mayen. 
 
 Leysin can be accessed by road or the hourly [[Aigle–Leysin railway|Aigle - Leysin]] train, which makes four area stops.
