 '''''Newsround''''' is a [[television]] [[television programme|programme]] for [[children]].  It talks about the [[news]] and gives [[information]] about things that are happening at the moment.  It is shown on the channels [[BBC One]], [[CBBC]] channel and the [[BBC World News]]. 
 
 == The past == 
 
 ''Newsround'' used to be called ''John Craven's Newsround'' before the [[presenter]] called John Craven left in 1989. ''Newsround'' started on 4 April 1972 and is still going on today.
