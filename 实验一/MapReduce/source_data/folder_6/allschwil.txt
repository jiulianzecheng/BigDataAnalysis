 '''Allschwil''' is a [[municipality]] of the district of [[Arlesheim (district)|Arlesheim]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Basel-Landschaft]] in [[Switzerland]]. It is right next to the city of Basel. With nearly 22.000 people it is the biggest city in Basel Landschaft.
