 A '''photomontage''' is an [[image]] made of two or more [[photograph]]s. It is made by cutting and joining the photographs together into an [[illusion]] of subject that is not real. A similar method is used today with [[Digital camera|digital photography]], using [[Photo manipulation|image-editing software]]. < ref > {{cite book | title = Undead Theories: Constructivism, Eclecticism And Research in Education | author = David Geelan | publisher = Sense Publishers | year = 2006 | isbn = 90-77874-31-3}} < /ref > 
 
 Photomontage can also mean the way of making a photomontage.
