 The '''Belarusian Private News Agency''' ({{lang-be|Беларускае прыватнае агенцтва навін}}) or shortly '''BelaPAN''' ({{lang-be|БелаПАН}}) is a non-governmental [[news agency]] in [[Belarus]]. It is a big news agency in the country. BelaPAN makes news on politics, economy, and sports from Belarus in the [[Russian language|Russian]], [[Belarusian language|Belarusian]], and [[English language|English]] languages. < ref name= " :0 " > {{cite journal|date=2011-03-24|title=New Projects Submitted to the IPDC|url=http://www.unesco.org/new/fileadmin/MULTIMEDIA/HQ/CI/CI/pdf/ipdc2011_04%20Arab%20region%20and%20Europe%20part%20IV%2013%20January%202011%20Final.pdf|journal=[[International Programme for the Development of Communication]]|language=en|location=UNESCO HQ, Paris|pages=85}} < /ref >   
 
 BelaPAN has the on-line newspaper Belorusskiye Novosti (naviny.by). < ref > {{cite journal|last=Шибут|first=И.П.|date=2006|title=Из истории развития сетевых изданий Беларуси|url=https://elib.bsu.by/bitstream/123456789/17158/1/Shibut17_nauch_.pdf|journal=[[Belarusian State University]]|language=ru|location=Minsk|pages=1–8|access-date=2020-10-08|archive-date=2020-07-28|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200728191046/https://elib.bsu.by/bitstream/123456789/17158/1/Shibut17_nauch_.pdf|url-status=dead}} < /ref >  Its site can't be opened in Belarus. < ref name= " :1 "  / >  It has been writing about the [[2020 Belarusian protests]] and [[2020 Belarusian presidential election]]. < ref name= " :1 " > {{Cite web|last=Штейн|first=Евгения|date=2020-08-29|title=В Беларуси заблокировали «Нашу Нiву» и naviny.by, журналистов иностранных СМИ лишают аккредитации|url=https://tech.onliner.by/2020/08/29/nasha-niva|publisher=[[Onliner.by]]|language=ru|access-date=2020-10-08|archive-date=2020-09-29|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200929075549/https://tech.onliner.by/2020/08/29/nasha-niva|url-status=dead}} < /ref >
