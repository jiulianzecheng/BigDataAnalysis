 '''Sanamahi''' ({{lang-mni|ꯁꯅꯥꯃꯍꯤ}}) is a primordial god in [[Meitei mythology]] and [[Meitei religion|religion]]. < ref name= " 1lpuAAAAMAAJ " > {{Cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=1lpuAAAAMAAJ & q=sanamahi+god|title=Valley Society of Manipur: A Cultural Frontier of Indian Civilization|last=Saha|first=Ranajit Kumar|date=1994|publisher=Punthi-Pustak|isbn=978-81-85094-82-3|language=en}} < /ref >  He is the deity of the household, < ref > {{Cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=f1puAAAAMAAJ & q=sanamahi+god|title=Aspects of Indian Culture|last=Singh|first=Elangbam Nilakanta|date=1982|publisher=Jawaharlal Nehru Manipur Dance Academy|language=en}} < /ref >  whose identity is often associated with the  " Sun God "  ([[Kolouhanpa]]). < ref name= " 1lpuAAAAMAAJ " / >  He is the eldest son of [[Atingkok]], the supreme creator god of the universe. < ref > {{Cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=kqJVDwAAQBAJ & q=lainingthou+Sanamahi & pg=PT6|title=A Glimpse of Manipuri Literary Works|last=Devi|first=Nunglekpam Premi|date=2018-04-14|publisher=FSP Media Publications|language=en}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=ojxuAAAAMAAJ & q=sanamahi+god|title=Ethnic Identities and Democracy: Electoral Politics in North-East India|last1=Baruah|first1=Apurba Kumar|last2=Dev|first2=Rajesh|date=2006|publisher=Regency Publications|isbn=978-81-89233-41-9|language=en}} < /ref > 
 He is often bestowed with the title [[Lainingthou]], for being the ruler of the human household.
