 " '''Venceremos''' "  ( < small > say:  " ben-seh-REH-mohs " < /small > , which means  " We Will Win " ) is a [[Chile]]an song. This song was used for the [[Popular Unity (Chile)|Popular Unity of Chile]], and it was made more popular by Chilean organisations, like [[Inti-Illimani]] and QuilapayÃºn. This song was at its height during the [[1970 Chilean presidential election]]s, during which people sung this song in order to support [[Salvador Allende]]. 
 
 The words of the song were written by Claudio Iturra, and the music was created by Sergio Ortega. To further support the left-wing party in the 1970 elections, the lyrics were written again by VÃ­ctor Jara, with the mentioning of Allende and the Popular Unity in the lyrics. And thus, a second version came about. < ref > Balderston, Daniel, Mike Gonzalez, Ana M. Lopez (eds.), Encyclopaedia of Contemporary Latin American and Caribbean Cultures. p.788 < /ref > < ref name= " e2 " / > 
 
 This song became popular in the 1970s and has been translated into the [[Portuguese language|Portuguese]], [[Italian language|Italian]], [[Russian language|Russian]], [[German language|German]], [[Swedish language|Swedish]], [[Finnish language|Finnish]], and the [[Turkish language]]s; however, the authors of the last five remain not known.
