'''Meili''' ( " the lovely one " ) is a [[god]] of the [[Norse mythology]]. His father is [[Odin]]. His brother is [[Thor]]. His mother is [[Jörð]]. He is several times called  " Thor's brother " . His name is on the list of Odin's sons.
