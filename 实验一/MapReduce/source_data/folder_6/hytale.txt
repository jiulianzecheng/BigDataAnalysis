 '''''Hytale''''' is an upcoming sandbox video game by Hypixel Studios. Creation of the game began in 2015 by ''[[Minecraft]]'' server [[Hypixel]] developers with help from [[Riot Games]]. Hypixel plans to be release Hytale for PC, consoles, and mobile devices as early as 2023.
