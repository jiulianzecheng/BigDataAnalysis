 '''Norje''' is a [[urban areas of Sweden|locality]] in [[Sölvesborg Municipality]] in [[Blekinge County]] in [[Sweden]]. In 2010, 657 people lived there. < ref name=scb / >  Starting in 1998, the [[rock and roll|rock]]/[[Heavy metal music|metal]] festival [[Sweden Rock Festival]] has been held there since then.
