 '''Pori''' is a [[city]] and [[municipality]] in [[Finland]]. The city had a population of 83,364 as of 31 August 2012. It covers {{convert|859.60|km2}} without sea-areas. 
 
 Yyteri, a sand beach, is located at Pori.
