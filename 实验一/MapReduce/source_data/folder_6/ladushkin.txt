 '''Ladushkin''' ({{lang-ru|Ладушкин}}; {{lang-de|Ludwigsort}}; {{lang-lt|Liudvigsortas}}; {{lang-pl|Ludwinów}}) is a town in [[Kaliningrad Oblast]], [[Russia]]. It is between [[Kaliningrad]] and the [[Poland|Polish]] border. In 2010, 3,787 people lived there.
