 '''Matosinhos''' ({{IPA-pt|mɐtuˈziɲuʃ|-|Pt-pt Matosinhos FF.ogg}}) is a [[City#Portugal|city]] and a [[Concelho|municipality]] in the [[Norte Region, Portugal|northern]] [[Porto District|Porto district]] of [[Portugal]]. It bordered in the south by the city of [[Porto]] (8 & nbsp;km from the city centre).
