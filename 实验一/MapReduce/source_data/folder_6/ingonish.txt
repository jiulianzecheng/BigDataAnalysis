'''Ingonish''' is a [[Canada|Canadian]] [[rural]] [[community]] in northeastern [[Victoria County, Nova Scotia|Victoria County]], [[Nova Scotia]]. Ingonish is on the northeast [[coast]] of [[Cape Breton Island]] along the [[Cabot Trail]]. It is about {{Convert1|60}} from the [[shire town]] of [[Baddeck, Nova Scotia|Baddeck]]. Ingonish is made up of four communities: Ingonish Ferry, Ingonish Harbour, [[Ingonish Beach, Nova Scotia|Ingonish Beach]], Ingonish Centre. 
 
 Ingonish is located at the eastern [[wikt:enter|entrance]] to [[Cape Breton Highlands National Park]].  The [[region]]al [[economy]] is tied to the [[season]]al [[tourism]] and [[fishing]] [[Industry|industries]]. The community is in the [[federal electoral district]] of [[Sydneyâ€”Victoria]]. Ingonish was one of the first areas settled on Cape Breton Island.
