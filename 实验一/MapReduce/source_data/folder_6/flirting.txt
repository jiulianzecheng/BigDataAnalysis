 '''Flirting''' is a form of human [[behavior]] from one person toward another person, or between two people, usually showing a [[human sexuality|sexual]] or [[love|romantic]] interest in the other person. It can include [[conversation]], [[body language]] (such as [[wink]]ing), or short physical contact.
