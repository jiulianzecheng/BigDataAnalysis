 '''Ehrendingen''' is a [[municipality]] of the [[district]] [[Baden (district)|Baden]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Aargau]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 Ehrendingen was created on 1 January 2006, from the former municipalities of [[Oberehrendingen]] and [[Unterehrendingen]].
