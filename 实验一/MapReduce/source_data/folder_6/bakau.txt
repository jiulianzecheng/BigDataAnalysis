 '''Bakau''' is a town on the [[Atlantic Ocean|Atlantic]] coast of [[The Gambia|Gambia]], west of Gambia's capital city of [[Banjul]]. It is known for its [[botanical gardens]], its crocodile pool ''[[Bakau Kachikally]]'' and for the beaches at [[Cape Point (Gambia)|Cape Point]]. Bakau is the first major suburb outside [[Banjul]] and the most developed town in the Gambia. In 2013, 43,098 people lived there.
