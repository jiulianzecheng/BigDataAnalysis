 '''Saimaa''' is the largest [[lake]] in [[Finland]]. It covers {{convert|4380|km2}}. The Vuoksi River flows from Saimaa to [[Lake Ladoga|Laatokka]]. The [[city|cities]] nearby to Saimaa's beaches are [[Lappeenranta]], [[Joensuu]], [[Mikkeli]], [[Imatra]], [[Savonlinna]] and [[Varkaus]].
