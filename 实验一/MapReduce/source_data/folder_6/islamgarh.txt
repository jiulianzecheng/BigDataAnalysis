'''Islamgarh''' ({{lang-ur|یسلامگارھ}}) (or '''Akhalgarh,''' where ''Akhal'' means the [[Immortality|Immortal]] and ''Garh'' means ''Place of'') is a small [[New town]] near the village of [[Chak Haryam]], that is close to the [[Mangla Dam]] and one of 25 towns in the [[Union Councils of Pakistan]] is the [[Chakswari Tehsil]], [[Mirpur District]] of [[Azad Jammu and Kashmir]], [[Pakistan]]. 10,082 people lived there in 1998. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.scribd.com/doc/2510797/Village-List-Mirpur-AJK|title=Village List Mirpur AJK|website=Scribd}} < /ref >
