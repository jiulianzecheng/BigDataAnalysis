 '''Kharp''' ({{lang-ru|Харп}}) is an [[Urban-type settlement|urban settlement]] in the [[Priuralsky District]] of the [[Yamalo-Nenets Autonomous Okrug]]. This is in Western [[Siberia]], the Asian part of [[Russia]]. It is on the bank of the [[Sob River]]. Its population in 2019 was 5,941.
