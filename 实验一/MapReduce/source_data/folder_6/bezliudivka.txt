 '''Bezliudivka''' ({{lang-uk|Безлюдівка}}; {{lang-ru|Безлюдовка}}) is a settlement in the [[Kharkiv Oblast]] ([[oblast|province]]) of eastern [[Ukraine]]. Situated on the [[Udy River|river Udy]] ([[Seversky Donets]] Basin), 3 & nbsp;km south of Kharkiv, 3 & nbsp;km from the railway station Bezlyudivka. As of 2001, 9,693 people lived there.
