 '''Haaksbergen''' is a municipality and town in the province of [[Overijssel]], the Netherlands. About 24,000 people were living there in 2021. 
 
 Haaksbergen lies in [[Twente]] region and borders both [[Achterhoek]] region and [[Germany]].
