'''Leasehold''' is a [[housing tenure]] or type of ownership. With a leasehold. the person who lives in a property, such as a [[house]], owns the building itself  but [[rent|rents]] the land it sits on. The [[lease]]s on land and [[commercial building]]s are often [[decade]]s or [[centuries]] in length.
