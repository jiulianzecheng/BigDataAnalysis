 '''Telefe''' (acronym for '''Televisión Federal''') is a [[television station]] located in [[Buenos Aires]], [[Argentina]]. The station is owned and operated by [[Paramount Global]] through Televisión Federal S.A. < ref name= " viacomcbs "  / >  Telefe is also one of Argentina's six national television networks. Its studios are located on Martínez, [[Buenos Aires]], adjacent to the corporate headquarters; < ref name= " headquarters "  / >  its transmitter is located at the Alas Building. < ref name= " alas " > {{cite news |last1=Cayón |first1=David |title=Telefe comienza a transmitir en digital terrestre |url=https://www.cronista.com/negocios/Telefe-comienza-a-transmitir-en-digital-terrestre-20110318-0014.html |work=[[El Cronista]] |date=March 18, 2011 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110321205320/http://www.cronista.com/negocios/Telefe-comienza-a-transmitir-en-digital-terrestre-20110318-0014.html |archive-date=March 21, 2011 |language=es}} < /ref >

 ==Criticisms and controversies== 
 
 ===Special program to Emilio Disi=== 
 
 In March 2018, during the death of the Argentine actor and comedian [[Emilio Disi]] at the age of 75, after the news of his death was known, the channel wanted to promote a special with the best moments of the actor with [[Susana Giménez]]. After criticism and controversy, the channel could not broadcast it. < ref > {{cite web | url=https://www.primiciasya.com/polemica-telefe-levanto-el-programa-especial-dedicado-emilio-disi-n1573144 | title=Polémica: Telefe levantó el programa especial dedicado a Emilio Disi }} < /ref > 
 
 == Affiliates == 
 The network currently has eight [[owned-and-operated stations]] and current affiliation agreements with other television stations. 
 
 ===Owned and operated=== 
 All of the owned-and-operated stations (except LRK458 TV) joined Telefe in April 1998, after Editorial Atlántida acquired a majority stake of the former Televisoras Provinciales. < ref > {{cite news |title=EDITORIAL ATLANTIDA ACQUIRES TV CHANNELS |url=https://www.telecompaper.com/news/editorial-atlantida-acquires-tv-channels-2--148178 |work=Telecom Paper |date=September 14, 1998 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20141214140310/https://www.telecompaper.com/news/editorial-atlantida-acquires-tv-channels-2--148178 |archive-date=December 14, 2014}} < /ref >  LRK458 TV (now known as [[Channel 8 (Tucuman, Argentina)|''Telefe Tucumán'']]) was the last to join Telefe in March 2000. < ref > {{cite news |title=Telefé adquirió Canal 8 de Tucumán |url=https://www.lanacion.com.ar/sociedad/telefe-adquirio-canal-8-de-tucuman-nid10634/ |work=[[La Nación]] |date=March 27, 2000 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200701014120/https://www.lanacion.com.ar/sociedad/telefe-adquirio-canal-8-de-tucuman-nid10634/ |archive-date=July 1, 2020 |language=es}} < /ref >
