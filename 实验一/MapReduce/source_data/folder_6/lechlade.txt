 '''Lechlade''' ({{IPAc-en|ˈ|l|ɛ|tʃ|l|eɪ|d}}), or '''Lechlade-on-Thames''', is a town in [[Cotswolds]], [[Gloucestershire]], [[England]]. It is named after the [[River Leach]]. As of 2011, it has a population of around 2,850. 
 
 Evidence of human occupation begins during the [[neolithic]], [[Iron Age]] and [[Roman Britain|Roman]] periods. < ref > {{cite web |title=Lechlade: Ancient and Historical Monuments in the County of Gloucester Iron Age and Romano-British Monuments in the Gloucestershire Cotswolds. |url=https://www.british-history.ac.uk/rchme/ancient-glos/pp73-77 |website=British History Online |publisher=Her Majesty's Stationery Office |access-date=18 February 2019}} < /ref > < ref > {{NHLE |desc=Iron Age and Romano British settlement remains and associated features, 1km south east of Leaze Farm |num=1011604 |accessdate=18 February 2019}} < /ref >  The town later became a trading centre. Lechlade is home to the [[Anglican]] [[St Lawrence Church, Lechlade|Church of St Lawrence]] which has been there since the 15th century, as well as a [[Baptists|Baptist]] Church on Sherborne Street, there since 1817. < ref > {{NHLE |desc=Church of St Lawrence |num=1155874 |accessdate=18 February 2019}} < /ref > < ref > {{NHLE |desc=Baptist Church |num=1089377 |accessdate=18 February 2019}} < /ref >  In modern times, many cultural and social activities occur around the town's Memorial Hall, which was burned down by a fire and rebuilt in 2016. < ref > {{cite web |title=Lechlade Memorial Hall |url=https://www.book-online.co.uk/lechlade |publisher=Lechlade Memorial Hall |access-date=24 February 2019}} < /ref > 
 
 After [[World War II]], the development of the nearby air force stations, [[RAF Fairford]] and [[RAF Brize Norton]], helped expand the town. < ref name= " neighbourhoodplan " > {{cite web |title=Neighbourhood Plan |url=https://www.cotswold.gov.uk/media/1460552/Lechlade-Neighbourhood-Plan.pdf |publisher=Cotswold Council |access-date=24 February 2019 |archive-date=18 September 2017 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170918231901/http://cotswold.gov.uk/media/1460552/Lechlade-Neighbourhood-Plan.pdf |url-status=dead }} < /ref >
