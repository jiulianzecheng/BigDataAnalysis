 '''Schwarzenburg''' is a [[municipality]] in the [[administrative district]] of [[Bern-Mittelland (administrative district)|Bern-Mittelland]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Bern (canton)|Bern]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 It was created on 1 January 2011 through the merger of the municipalities of [[Wahlern]] and [[Albligen]].
