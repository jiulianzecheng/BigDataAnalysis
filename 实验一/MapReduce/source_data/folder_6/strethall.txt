 '''Strethall''' is a village and [[civil parish]] in [[Uttlesford]], [[Essex]], England. In 2001 there were 22 people living in Strethall. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.ons.gov.uk/help/localstatistics|title=Local statistics - Office for National Statistics|website=www.ons.gov.uk}} < /ref >  The name  " Strethall "  comes from 'Nook of land by a Roman Road'. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.hellfirecorner.co.uk/TV/strethall.htm|title=Strethall - Thankful Village|website=www.hellfirecorner.co.uk}} < /ref >
