'''Heteroromanticism''' is a [[romantic orientation]]. A heteroromantic person is most likely to have a [[Romance (love)|romantic relationship]] with a person of the opposite [[gender]] or [[sex]]. Heteroromantic people can have any [[sexual orientation]]. The term heteroromantic is useful for [[asexual]] people to describe their [[affection]].
