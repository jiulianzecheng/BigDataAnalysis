 '''Marlenheim''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is in [[Grand Est]] in the [[Bas-Rhin]] [[Departments of France|department]] in northeast [[France]]. It is twinned with [[Bouillante]] (Guadeloupe, France).
