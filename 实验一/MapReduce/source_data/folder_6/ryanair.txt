'''Ryanair''' is an [[Ireland|Irish]] [[Budget airline|low-cost airline]]. It was started in 1985. It is based in [[Dublin]] Airport and [[London Stansted Airport]]. Ryanair goes to 165 cities. These are mostly across [[Europe]]. Ryanair has a fleet of 505 [[Boeing 737]] aircraft. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Ryanair Fleet|url=https://corporate.ryanair.com/about-us/our-fleet/}} < /ref >
