 '''Gerach''' is a community in the [[Upper Franconia]]n district of [[Bamberg (district)|Bamberg]] and a member of the administrative community (''Verwaltungsgemeinschaft'') of Baunach. It is in the ''Naturpark Haßberge'' (nature park) about 20 & nbsp;km north of [[Bamberg]] and about 10 & nbsp;km south of [[Ebern]].
