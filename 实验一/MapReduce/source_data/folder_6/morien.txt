'''Morien''' is a character from [[King Arthur|Arthurian]] legend. He is the main character of ''Morien'' (or ''Moriaen''), a 13th-century Arthurian romance in [[Middle Dutch language|Middle Dutch]]. He is the son of one of the [[Knights of the Round Table]], whose name was Aglovale.
