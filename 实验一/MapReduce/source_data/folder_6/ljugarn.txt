 '''Ljugarn''' is a [[Urban areas in Sweden|locality]] in [[Gotland Municipality]] in [[Gotland County]] in [[Sweden]]. In 2010, 238 people lived there. < ref name=scb / >  It is located on the East Coast of Swedsih [[Baltic Sea]] island of [[Gotland]] and lies south of [[Slite]].
