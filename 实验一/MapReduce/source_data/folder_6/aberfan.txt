'''Aberfan''' is a [[village]] in [[Mid Glamorgan]], South [[Wales]]. In 1966, a [[colliery]] [[spoil tip]] [[Aberfan disaster|fell onto the village]], [[wikt:kill|killing]] 28 adults and 116 children.
