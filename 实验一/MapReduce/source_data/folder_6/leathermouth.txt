 '''Leathermouth''' (also spelled as '''LeATHERMØUTH''' < ref name= " epitaph-bio " / > ) is an [[United States|American]] [[hardcore punk]] band. The band was started in 2007 by [[My Chemical Romance]]'s guitarist [[Frank Iero]]. On January 2009, the band released their first album, ''[[XO (Leathermouth album)|XO]]''. < ref name= " ap-release " > Shultz, Brian.  ==History== 
 Leathermouth started in 2007 by some friends of [[My Chemical Romance]]'s rhythm guitarist [[Frank Iero]]. < ref name= " epitaph-bio " / >  Iero heard a few demo songs from the band, and wanted to sign the band to his own independent label.
