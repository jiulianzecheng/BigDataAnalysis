 '''Motorola''' was an [[USA|American]] [[company]] that made [[cell phone]]s and other [[electronics]]. The company was based in [[Schaumburg, Illinois]], a small town just outside of [[Chicago]], [[Illinois]]. After losing $4.3 billion from 2007 to 2009, Motorola was divided in two separate companies on January 4, 2011; Motorola Mobility and Motorola Solutions. < ref > {{cite news| url=http://online.wsj.com/article/SB10001424052748704111504576059863418814674.html |work=The Wall Street Journal | accessdate=January 4, 2011 | title=Motorola Is Split Into Two | first=Spencer E. | last=Ante | date=January 5, 2011}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite web|title = Motorola To Officially Split Into Two Companies|url = http://www.huffingtonpost.com/2011/01/03/motorola-spit-motorola-mobility_n_803847.html|website = The Huffington Post|accessdate = 2015-10-18}} < /ref > 
 
 They were listed in the [[NYSE|New York Stock Exchange]] as [https://www.nyse.com/about/listed/lcddata.html?ticker=MOT MOT] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130922004147/http://www.nyse.com/about/listed/lcddata.html?ticker=mot |date=2013-09-22 }}. < ref > {{Cite web|title = Motopia Ltd: ASX:MOT quotes  &  news - Google Finance|url = https://www.google.com/finance?q=mot & ei=DJojVuvQO4bBebvClugG|website = www.google.com|accessdate = 2015-10-18}} < /ref > 
 
 The company began with the name Galvin Manufacturing Corporation in 1928, and then later adopted the 'Motorola' name in 1947. They were the first developers of the [[walkie-talkie]] in [[World War II]]. 
 
 On August 15, 2011, [[Google]] said that it would buy Motorola Mobility for $12.5 billion. < ref > {{cite news |url=https://www.washingtonpost.com/blogs/faster-forward/post/google-agrees-to-acquire-motorola-mobility/2011/08/15/gIQABmTkGJ_blog.html |title=Google agrees to acquire Motorola Mobility |author=Tsukayama, Hayley |date=August 15, 2011 |newspaper=[[The Washington Post]] |accessdate=August 15, 2011}} < /ref > < ref > {{cite press release |url=http://investor.google.com/releases/2011/0815.html |title=Google to Acquire Motorola Mobility |date=August 15, 2011 |accessdate=August 15, 2011}} < /ref > < ref name=ftgoogle > {{cite news|last= Bradshaw |first= Timothy ‘Tim’ |title= Google buys Motorola mobile phone division |url= http://www.ft.com/cms/s/2/e906bedc-c734-11e0-a9ef-00144feabdc0.html#axzz1V6DVHjMP |accessdate= August 15, 2011 |newspaper= [[Financial Times]] |date= August 15, 2011}} < /ref >
