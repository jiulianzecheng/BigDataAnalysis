 ==Signs and symptoms== 
 Symptoms include: 
 
 *Abdominal fullness related to an [[splenomegaly|enlarged spleen]] (splenomegaly). 
 *Bone pain 
 *Bruising and easy bleeding due to inadequate numbers of [[platelet]]s 
 *[[Cachexia]] (loss of appetite, weight loss, and fatigue) 
 *[[hepatosplenomegaly|Enlargement of both the liver and spleen]] 
 *Fatigue 
 *[[Gout]] and [[hyperuricemia|high uric acid levels]] 
 *Increased susceptibility to infection, such as [[pneumonia]]  
 *Pallor and shortness of breath due to [[anemia]] 
 *In rarer cases, a raised [[mean corpuscular volume|red blood cell volume]]
