 '''Tokoroa''' ({{lang-mi|Te Kaokaoroa o Pātetere}}) is the fifth largest-town in [[Waikato]] region of the [[North Island]]. It is 30 southwest of [[Rotorua]] and is at the midway point of [[Hamilton, New Zealand|Hamilton]] and [[Taupō]] on State Highway 1. It has 10 primary schools, two high schools in the town and is the home of the Talking Poles. It is situated on the [[Kinleith Brach]] railway line.
