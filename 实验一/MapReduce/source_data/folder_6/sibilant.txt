'''Sibilants''' are sounds that sound like a [[hiss]]. < ref > {{Cite journal|title=sibilant|url=https://www.thefreedictionary.com/sibilant|journal=The Free Dictionary}} < /ref >  In [[linguistics]], a sibilant is a [[fricative]] that is louder than other fricatives. For example, [s] is a sibilant and [f] is not.
