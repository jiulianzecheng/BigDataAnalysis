'''Ballotpedia''' is a non-partisan online political [[encyclopedia]]. Founded in 2007, it covers [[United States|American]] federal, state, and local [[politics]], [[election]]s, and public policy.  
 
 Ballotpedia is sponsored by the Lucy Burns Institute, a [[non-profit organization]] based in [[Middleton, Wisconsin]]. According to their website, the organization employed over 60 writers and researchers in 2016.
