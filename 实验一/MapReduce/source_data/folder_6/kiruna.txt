 '''Kiruna''' is a town in the [[counties of Sweden|county]] of [[Norrbotten County|Norrbotten]] in [[Sweden]]. It is the seat of [[Kiruna Municipality]]. 
 
 Kiruna was founded in 1900 as a mining community.
