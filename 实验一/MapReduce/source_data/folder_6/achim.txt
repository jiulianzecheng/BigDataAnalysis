 '''Achim''' is a town in [[Lower Saxony]], [[Germany]]. It is 16 km southeast of [[Bremen]]. The population in 2020 was about 32,000. It is on the right side of the [[Weser]] river. 
 
 == History == 
 In the past the town has been part of various [[Duke]]'s territories. The town was a location of [[Saxons|Saxon]] courts in the 11th century. [[Danish people|Danes]], [[Sweden|Swedes]] and [[French people|French]] have controlled the region. In the 19th century, the town became part of the [[Kingdom of Hanover]].
