 '''Montagnola''' is a small [[Switzerland|Swiss]] village in the [[Collina d'Oro]] [[municipality]].  Along with the villages of [[Agra, Switzerland|Agra]] and [[Gentilino]], it makes the new municipality of Collina d'Oro.
