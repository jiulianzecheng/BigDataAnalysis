 '''Rzhev''' is a town in [[Tver Oblast]], [[Russia]]. It is {{convert|49|km|abbr=off}} southwest of Staritsa and {{convert|126|km|abbr=off}} from Tver. It is on the highway and railway connecting [[Moscow]] and [[Riga]]. It is the uppermost town on the [[Volga River]].
