'''Tswe''' (Ꚏ ꚏ; italics: ''Ꚏ ꚏ'') is a letter of the [[Cyrillic script|Cyrillic]] script. It is drawn by adding a long tail to the bottom of the letter tse (Ц ц ''Ц ц''). 
 
 Tswe is used in an old orthography of the [[Abkhaz language|Abkhaz]] language, where it represents the labialized aspirated voiceless alveolo-palatal affricate /t͡ɕʷʰ/.
