 '''Ourense''' ({{IPAc-en|oʊ|ˈ|r|ɛ|n|s|eɪ}} {{respell|oh|REN|say}}, {{IPA-gl|owˈɾɛnsɪ|lang}}; {{lang-es|Orense}} {{IPA-es|oˈɾense|}}) is a city and capital of the province located in the [[Autonomous communities of Spain|autonomous community]] of [[Galicia (Spain)|Galicia]], northwestern [[Spain]]. It is on [[the Portuguese Way]] path of the Road of St James ([[Camino de Santiago]]). It's also known as ''A cidade das Burgas'' < ref > {{Cite web|url=https://www.turismodeourense.gal/termalismo/termas-e-historia/|title=As Burgas: Termas con historia|website=Turismo de Ourense|language=es|access-date=2020-04-27}} < /ref >  (galician) due to its [[Hot spring|hot springs]]. < ref > {{Cite web|url=https://viajar.elperiodico.com/destinos/ourense-segunda-ciudad-termal-europa|title=Ourense, la segunda ciudad termal de Europa|date=2017-01-12|website=viajar.elperiodico.com|language=es|access-date=2020-04-27|archive-date=2020-08-06|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200806004212/https://viajar.elperiodico.com/destinos/ourense-segunda-ciudad-termal-europa|url-status=dead}} < /ref >
