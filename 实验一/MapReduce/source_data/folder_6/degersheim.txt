 '''Degersheim''' is a [[municipality]] in [[Wil (Wahlkreis)|Wil]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[St. Gallen (canton)|St. Gallen]] in [[Switzerland]]. Degersheim is first mentioned in 837 as ''Tegarascai.'' This name contains the Germanic  " tegar "  (meaning big), and  " asca "  meaning  " ash tree " . Degersheim is on the railway line from [[St. Gallen]] to [[Lucerne]], and suburban trains connect it to St. Gallen and [[Wattwil]]. The through trains to Lucerne no longer stop in Degersheim, they stop in Wattwil (the next stop from Degersheim).
