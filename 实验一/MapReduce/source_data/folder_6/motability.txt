'''Motability''' is a British [[Charitable organization|charity]]  founded in 1977.  It runs the '''Motability''' Scheme for disabled people, their families and their carers to get a new car, scooter or powered wheelchair. The disability benefit is paid to Motobility.   
 
 A person must have at least twelve months to run on one of: 
 
 < nowiki > * < /nowiki >  Higher Rate Mobility Component of [[Disability Living Allowance]],  
 
 < nowiki > * < /nowiki >  Enhanced Mobility Component of [[Personal Independence Payment]], 
 
 < nowiki > * < /nowiki > Scottish Higher rate mobility component of Child or Adult Disability Payment, 
 
 < nowiki > * < /nowiki > Armed Forces Independence Payment  
 
 < nowiki > * < /nowiki > War Pensioners' Mobility Supplement.   
 
 They can choose a new car every three years or a wheelchair-accessible vehicle every five years. There are grants for adapting vehicles.
