 '''Papiamento''' ({{IPAc-en|lang|pron|ˌ|p|ɑː|p|i|ə|ˈ|m|ɛ|n|t|oʊ|}}) < ref name= " lpd " > {{citation|last=Wells|first=John C.|year=2008|title=Longman Pronunciation Dictionary|edition=3rd|publisher=Longman|isbn=978-1-4058-8118-0}} < /ref >  or '''Papiamentu''' ({{IPAc-en|lang|ˌ|p|ɑː|p|i|ə|ˈ|m|ɛ|n|t|uː}}) is a [[creole language]] spoken in the [[Caribbean]]. It is the most common language on the [[ABC islands]] of [[Aruba]], [[Bonaire]], and [[Curaçao]]. It is also commonly spoken in [[Sint Eustatius]] and [[Saba]].
