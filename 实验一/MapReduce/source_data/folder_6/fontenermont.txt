'''Fontenermont''' was a [[Communes of France|commune]]. It was in [[Basse-Normandie]] in the [[Calvados (department)|Calvados]] [[Departments of France|department]] in northwest [[France]]. On 1 January 2017, it was merged into the new commune of [[Noues de Sienne]]. < ref > [https://www.legifrance.gouv.fr/eli/arrete/2016/12/6/INTB1700252A/jo/texte Arrêté préfectoral] 6 December 2016 {{in lang|fr}} < /ref >
