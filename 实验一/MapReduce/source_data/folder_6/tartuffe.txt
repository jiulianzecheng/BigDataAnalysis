 '''''Tartuffe, or The Imposter''''' ({{lang-fr|Tartuffe, ou l'Imposteur}}) is a play in verse by [[Molière]]. It was first performed on May 12, 1664 at [[Versailles]]. It is about a religious [[hypocrite]].
