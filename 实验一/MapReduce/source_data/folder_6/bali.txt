 '''Bali''' ({{lang-ban|ᬩᬮᬶ|Bali}}) is an [[island]] and a province in [[Indonesia]]. The province includes the island of Bali and a few other islands. About 4,300,00 people live in Bali. Bali's most important city is [[Denpasar]]. [[Hindu]]ism is Bali's largest [[religion]]. More than 90% of its people are Hindus. [[Textile]]s and [[garment]]s are 45% of their exports. The currency in Bali is the [[Indonesian rupiah]] (IDR). The [[literacy rate]] for Bali is 45.55%.

 == Geography == 
 Bali is a volcanic island. The two main [[volcano]]s are [[Mount Agung]] and [[Mount Batur]]. Mount Agung is sacred to the [[Balinese people]]. 
 
 Mount Agung is an [[active volcano]], with a deep, large crater. Sometimes smoke and ash comes out of the crater.
