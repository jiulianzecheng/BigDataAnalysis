 '''Wrexham''' ({{lang-cy|Wrecsam}}) is the largest city in north [[Wales]]. It is an administrative, commercial, retail and educational centre.  
 
 Wrexham is between the Welsh mountains and the lower [[River Dee, Wales|Dee Valley]] along the border with [[England]]. Historically part of [[Denbighshire]], the then town became part of [[Clwyd]] in 1974. Since 1996 it has been the centre of the [[Wrexham County Borough]]. 
 
 At the 2011 Census, Wrexham had a population of 61,603, the fourth largest urban area in Wales. It became a city in September 2022. 
 
 The city has a professional [[association football|football]] club, [[Wrexham A.F.C.|Wrexham AFC]], who currently compete in the English football league system.
