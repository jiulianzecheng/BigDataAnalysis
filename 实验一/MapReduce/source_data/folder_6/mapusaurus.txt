'''Mapusaurus''' was a type of [[dinosaur]]. It was a [[theropod]].  It was [[carnivore|carnivorous]], meaning it ate meat. The Mapusaurus was one of the largest carnivorous theropods, such as the [[Carcharodontosaurus]], [[Giganotosaurus]], or [[Tyrannosaurus Rex]].  It was 12-13 meters long.
