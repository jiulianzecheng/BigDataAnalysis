'''Trockau''' is a village of the town of [[Pegnitz (town)|Pegnitz]] in the district of [[Bayreuth (district)|Bayreuth]] in [[Upper Franconia Government Region|Upper Franconia]], [[Bavaria]]. Before the territorial reform of the 1970s, Trockau was an independent market town.
