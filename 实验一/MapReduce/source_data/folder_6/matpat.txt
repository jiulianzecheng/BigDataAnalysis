He is the creator of the YouTube series '''Game Theory''', and its spin-off series '''Film Theory''', '''Food Theory''', and '''Style Theory''', each analyzing different video games, films, food, and fashion respectively. He was born in [[Medina, Ohio]]. 
 
 Between July 1, 2016, and July 31, 2016, he starred in the game show ''[[The Runner (2016 Reality Series)|The Runner]]''. 
 
 In 2012, he married Stephanie Cordato. They have one son.
