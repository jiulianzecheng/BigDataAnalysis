 '''Diesten''' is a village in the [[Lower Saxony|Lower Saxon]] town of [[Bergen (Landkreis Celle)|Bergen]] in the northern part of [[Celle (district)|Celle]] on the [[Lüneburg Heath]] in northern Germany. It is 20 & nbsp;km north of [[Celle]] on ''[[Kreisstraße]]'' K 240.
