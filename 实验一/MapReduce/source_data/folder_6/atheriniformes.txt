 The '''Atheriniformes''', also known as the '''silversides''', are an order of [[Ray-finned fish|ray-finned fishes]] that includes the [[Old World silverside|Old World silversides]], the [[Neotropical silverside|Neotropical silversides]], and several less-familiar families. The only prehistoric silverside is ''[[Palaeoatherina]]''. You can find them in tropical and temperate marine and freshwater environments. 
 
 == Taxonomy == 
 Nelson 2016 classifies the families as follows: 
 
 * Order Atheriniformes 
 ** Suborder [[Atherinopsoidei]] 
 *** Family [[Atherinopsidae]] (New World silversides) 
 *** Family [[Notocheiridae]] (Surf silverside) 
 ** Suborder [[Atherinoidei]] 
 *** Family [[Iso (fish)|Isonidae]] (Surf sardines) 
 *** Family [[Rainbowfish|Melanotaeniidae]] (Rainbowfishes and blue eyes) 
 *** Family [[Atherion|Atherionidae]] (Pricklenose silversides) 
 *** Family [[Mercer's tusked silverside|Dentatherinidae]] (Mercer's tusked silverside) 
 *** Family [[Phallostethidae]] (Priapium fishes) 
 *** Family [[Atherinidae]] (Old World silversides)
