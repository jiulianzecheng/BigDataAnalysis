 '''Halloumi''' also known as '''Hellim''' is a white [[cheese]] made out of [[goat's milk]]. It is not a hard or a soft cheese, and can be fried to a crispy coating. Halloumi cheese originated in the island of Cyprus. < ref > {{cite news | url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/magazine-24159029 | title=How halloumi took over the UK | work=BBC News | date=21 September 2013 }} < /ref >
