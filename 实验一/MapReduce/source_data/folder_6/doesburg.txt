 '''Doesburg''' is a municipality and city in the province of [[Gelderland]], the Netherlands. About 11,000 people were living there in 2021. 
 
 Doesburg lies on the east bank of the [[IJssel]] river in the region of [[IJsselvallei]]. The northern part belongs also to the region of [[Achterhoek]], the southern part also to the region of [[Liemers]]. The [[Oude IJssel]] river flows into the IJssel river in Doesburg. 
 
 It got [[city rights]] in 1237 and became a member of the [[Hanseatic League]] in 1447. 
 
 Doesburg is well-known for its [[culinary mustard|mustard]].
