'''Monosexuality''' is a [[sexual orientation]] defined with [[Sexual attraction|attraction]] to one [[gender]]/[[sex]]. Monosexual groups both [[homosexual]] and [[heterosexual]] people with [[androsexual]] and [[gynesexual]] [[non-binary]] individuals.
