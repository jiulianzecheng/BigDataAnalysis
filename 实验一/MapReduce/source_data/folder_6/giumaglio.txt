 '''Giumaglio''' is a village and former municipality of the [[district]] of [[Vallemaggia (district)|Vallemaggia]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Ticino]], [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 On 4 April 2004 the former municipalities of [[Aurigeno]], [[Coglio]], Giumaglio, [[Lodano]], [[Moghegno]] and [[Someo]] merged into the new municipality of [[Maggia, municipality|Maggia]].
