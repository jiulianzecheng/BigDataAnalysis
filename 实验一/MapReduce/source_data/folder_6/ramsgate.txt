 '''Ramsgate''' is a [[seaside]] [[town]] in the [[district]] of [[Thanet]] in [[Kent]]. It has a population of around 40,000. The town relies mainly on [[tourism]] and [[fishing]] for growth. The town has one of the largest [[marina]]s on the English south coast.
