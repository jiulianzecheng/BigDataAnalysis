 '''Margaux''' is a former [[Communes of France|commune]] in the [[Gironde]] [[Departments of France|department]] in [[Nouvelle-Aquitaine]] in southwestern [[France]]. On 1 January 2017, it was merged into the new commune [[Margaux-Cantenac]]. < ref > [https://www.legifrance.gouv.fr/eli/arrete/2016/11/17/INTB1633989A/jo/texte Arrêté préfectoral] 17 November 2016 {{in lang|fr}} < /ref > 
 
 The village is in the Haut Médoc [[wine]] making region on the left bank of the [[Garonne]] estuary. It is northwest of the city of [[Bordeaux]].
