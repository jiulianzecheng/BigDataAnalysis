 '''Wallachia''' (also spelled '''Walachia'''; {{lang-ro|Ţara Românească}} or  " The Romanian Land " ) is a historical area of [[Romania]]. Wallachia is divided into two sections, Muntenia (Greater Wallachia) and Oltenia (Lesser Wallachia).
