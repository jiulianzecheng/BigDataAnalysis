 '''Limassol''' is a city on the southern coast of [[Cyprus]]. It is the [[capital city]] of [[Limassol District]]. In 2011, about 184,000 people lived in the [[urban area]]. < ref name= " cystat.gov.cy " > {{Cite web| url=http://www.mof.gov.cy/mof/cystat/statistics.nsf/populationcondition_21main_gr/populationcondition_21main_gr?OpenForm & sub=1 & sel=1| title=Στατιστική Υπηρεσία - Πληθυσμός και Κοινωνικές Συνθήκες - Πληθυσμός - Ανακοινώσεις|website=www.mof.gov.cy|access-date=2011-11-11}} < /ref > 
 
 [[Limassol Castle]] is one of the [[List of castles in Cyprus|ten castles of Cyprus]]. It was built by the [[Byzantine Empire|Byzantines]] around 1000 AD.
