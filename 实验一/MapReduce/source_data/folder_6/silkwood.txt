 '''''Silkwood''''' is a 1983 American biographical drama movie directed by [[Mike Nichols]] and starring [[Meryl Streep]], [[Cher]], [[Kurt Russell]], [[Craig T. Nelson]], [[Fred Ward]], [[Ron Silver]], [[Diana Scarwid]], [[Tess Harper]], [[Charles Hallahan]], [[Bruce McGill]], [[M. Emmet Walsh]]. It was distributed by [[20th Century Fox]] and was nominated for 5 Academy Awards in 1984.
