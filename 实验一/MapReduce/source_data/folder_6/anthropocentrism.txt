'''Anthropocentrism''', '''human supremacism''' or '''human exceptionalism''' is the belief and [[ideology]] that [[human being]]s are separate from and superior to [[nature]], that humans are the most important organisms in the universe, and that everything else ([[plant]]s, [[animal]]s, [[mineral]]s, etc) are just [[resource]]s and commodities for people to use.
