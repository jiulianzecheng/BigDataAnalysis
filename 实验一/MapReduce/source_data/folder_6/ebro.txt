 '''Ebro''' is a Spanish [[river]]. It flows through [[Reinosa]], [[Miranda de Ebro]], [[Logrono]], [[Tudela]], [[Zaragoza]], and [[Tortosa]]. It empties into the [[Mediterranean Sea]] in the province of [[Tarragona]].
