 '''Bastendorf''' ({{lang-lb|Baastenduerf}}) is a small town in the [[Communes of Luxembourg|commune]] of [[Tandel]], in northeastern [[Luxembourg]]. 
 
 Bastendorf was a commune in the [[Diekirch (canton)|canton of Diekirch]] until 1 January 2006, when it was merged with [[Fouhren]] to form the new commune of Tandel, in [[Vianden (canton)|Vianden]].
