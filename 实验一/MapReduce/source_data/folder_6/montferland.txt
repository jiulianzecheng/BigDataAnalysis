 '''Montferland''' is a municipality in the province of [[Gelderland]], the Netherlands. About 36,000 people were living there in 2021. 
 
 It was created from the former municipalities of [[Bergh]] and [[Didam]] on 1 January 2005. 
 
 Montferland borders [[Germany]] in the south. It belongs to the [[Liemers]] region, but the forestial area of the Montferland hills or Bergherbos, which lie in the middle of the municipality, are sometimes considered being the border between the Liemers and the [[Achterhoek]] regions.
