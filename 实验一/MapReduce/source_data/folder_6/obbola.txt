 '''Obbola''' is a [[Urban areas in Sweden|locality]] in [[Umeå Municipality]] in [[Västerbotten County]] in [[Sweden]]. In 2010, 2,197 people lived there. < ref name=scb / >  The locality is located close to the town of [[Holmsund]], and to the west of the mouth of [[Ume River]] at the [[Gulf of Bothnia]]. [[Svenska Cellulosa Aktiebolaget|SCA]] is a major employer in the locality.
