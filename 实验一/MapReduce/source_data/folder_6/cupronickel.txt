'''Cupronickel''' is an [[alloy]]. It is made of [[copper]] and [[nickel]]. It is used in [[nickel (coin)|nickels]], the US coins. It does not [[corrosion|corrode]]. It is silvery in color. It has 75% copper and 25% nickel.
