 '''Salamanca''' is a [[city]] in central [[Spain]]. It is the [[capital city]] of the [[province]] with the same name.  
 The city has a very important university started in 1218. It has two [[wikt:cathedrals|cathedrals]] and a famous Plaza Mayor (Main Square) built in the [[18th century]]. 
 
 Frogs are a mascot of Salamanca.  Souvenirs of frogs can be found in almost every shop in the Plaza Mayor. 
 
 The [[Battle of Salamanca]] was an important defeat for France in the [[Napoleonic Wars]].
