 '''Cavite''', officially the '''Province of Cavite'''  is a [[Provinces of the Philippines|province]] in the [[Philippines]]  in the [[Calabarzon]] region on the island of [[Luzon]]. It is on the southern shores of [[Manila Bay]] and southwest of [[Manila]]. It is one of the most [[Industrialisation|industrialized]] and fastest-growing provinces in the Philippines. In 2020, it had a population of 4,344,829.
