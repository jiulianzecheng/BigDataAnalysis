 '''Ranua''' is a [[municipality]] in [[Finland]]. As of 2013, almost 4,200 people lived there. Neighbouring municipalities are [[Ii, Finland|Ii]], [[Simo]], [[Tervola]], [[Rovaniemi]], [[Posio]] and [[Pudasjärvi]]. As of January 2014, there were almost 4,150 people living in Ranua.  
 
 ==Lakes== 
 Simojärvi, Näskäjärvi, Penämönjärvi, Impiönjärvi and Ranuanjärvi. There is also many smaller lakes in the area.
