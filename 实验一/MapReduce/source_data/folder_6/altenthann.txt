 '''Altenthann''' is a village and [[Municipalities in Germany|municipality]] in [[Regensburg (district)|Regensburg]] in [[Bavaria]] in [[Germany]]. 
 
 == Geography == 
 === Place === 
 Altenthann is in [[Regensburg]], about 18 kilometres northwest of the town of [[Wörth an der Donau]].
