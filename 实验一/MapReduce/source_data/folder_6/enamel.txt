'''Enamel''' is a smooth hard covering substance. It may refer to: 
 * [[Tooth enamel]], the hard mineralized surface of [[teeth]] 
 * [[Enamel (glass)]], a smooth, durable coating made of melted and fused [[glass]] [[powder]] 
 
 ==Related pages== 
 * [[Enamel paint]], a commercial [[paint]] that dries to an especially hard glossy finish 
 * [[Enameled wire]], wire [[Insulator (electricity)|insulated]] with enamel-like coating
