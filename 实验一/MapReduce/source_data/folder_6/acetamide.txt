'''Acetamide''' is a [[mineral]] that is either colorless or [[gray]] in [[color]], and is made of a [[crystal]]line [[wikt:crust|crust]] on [[black]] [[shale]].  It is named for [[acetic acid]] and [[ammonia]].  It can be found in [[Russia]] and is not radioactive. < ref > {{cite web| url= http://www.webmineral.com/data/Acetamide.shtml| title= Acetamide Mineral Data|work=Mineralogy Database}} < /ref >
