'''FICO''' (legal name: '''Fair Isaac Corporation''', originally '''Fair, Isaac and Company''') is a [[data]] [[analytics]] [[company]]. It is located in [[Bozeman, Montana|Bozeman]], [[Montana]], [[USA]]. The company focused on credit scoring services.
