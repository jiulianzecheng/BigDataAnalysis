 '''Lyss''' is a [[municipality]] in the [[Districts of Bern|administrative district]] of [[Seeland (administrative district)|Seeland]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|Canton]] of [[Bern (canton)|Bern]] in [[Switzerland]]. It has a [[population]] of 11,000 people. About 84% of these people are Swiss [[Germany|German]]. [[Politics|Politically]], Lyss can be called a [[city]]. The name  " Lyss "  comes from the local [[river]] [[Lyssbach]]. 
 
 On 1 January 2011, the old municipality of [[Busswil bei Büren]] was combined with Lyss. < ref name=Fusion > [http://www.bfs.admin.ch/bfs/portal/de/index/infothek/nomenklaturen/blank/blank/gem_liste/03.html Amtliches Gemeindeverzeichnis der Schweiz] published by the Swiss Federal Statistical Office {{in lang|de}} accessed 19 July 2011 < /ref >
