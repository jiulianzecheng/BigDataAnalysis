 '''''Travian''''' is a [[browser game]] with a world full of thousands of players who all begin as the leaders of small [[village]]s. < ref > [http://www.travian.co.uk/?from=rs Official presentation of Travian] < /ref > 
 
 The aim of the game is to use the right combination of tactics to make a small village become a mighty empire, and finally build a World Wonder to level 100, which will make you the winner that round. < ref > [http://www.traviangames.com/content/travian-0 Travian-games] < /ref >
