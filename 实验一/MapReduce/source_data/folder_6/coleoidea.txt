 The '''Coleoidea''' are a group of [[mollusks]] making up most of the living [[cephalopod]]s. [[Octopus]], [[squid]], and [[cuttlefish]] are all members of the Coleoidea. They do not have shells on the outside, but some do have a bone or shell inside. All Coleoidea live in the ocean.
