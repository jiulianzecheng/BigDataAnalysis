'''Marta Merrero''' (born May 18, 1969), also known as '''Martika''' is an [[United States|American]] [[singer]] and [[actress]]. She had an uncredited role in the [[movie]] ''[[Annie (movie)|Annie]]''. Later, she acted as Gloria on the [[television program]] ''[[Kids Incorporated]]. 
 
 In 1988, Martika had a self-tited [[studio album]]. From the album was her United States #1 hit pop ballad,  " Toy Soldiers " , in 1989. < ref > {{cite web|title=Martika|publisher=The Billboard|url=https://www.billboard.com/music/Martika/chart-history/HSI|access-date=August 12, 2020}} < /ref >  It was certified Gold. The song also hit #5 in the [[United Kingdom]], < ref > {{cite web|title=Martika Charts|publisher=Official Charts|url=https://www.officialcharts.com/artist/25549/martika|access-date=August 12, 2020}} < /ref >  [[Ireland]], [[Germany]] and [[Australia]]. 
 
 Martika was born in [[Whittier, California]] under [[Cuba]]n parents.
