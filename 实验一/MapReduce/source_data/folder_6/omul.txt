 The '''omul''' (''Coregonus migratorius'') is a kind of [[fish]]. It belongs to the same family as the [[trout]] and the [[salmon]]. It only lives in [[Lake Baikal]] and the rivers around it (the [[Angara]] for example. It is very typical for that region of [[Siberia]]). 
 
 {{biology-stub}}Omul considered a food delicacy and can be eaten n many ways. Local people consume it boiled, fried smoked, and freshly  salted. 
 
 The Omul normally fished using seine nets and fishing nets, yet during early spring, it can be caught using a fishing road. < ref > {{Cite web|last=Valentina|first=Sideleva|date=2004|title=Mysterious Fish of Lake Baikal|url=https://scfh.ru/en/papers/mysterious-fish-of-lake-baikal/|website=Science First Hand}} < /ref >
