 '''Aromanticism''' or '''aromanticity''' is a [[romantic orientation]] in which a person does not experience romantic attraction, whether entirely, partially or circumstantially. < ref > {{Cite web|date=2018-10-05|title=What It Means To Be 'Aromantic,' According To Aromantic People|url=https://www.huffpost.com/entry/what-does-it-mean-to-be-aromantic_n_5bb501cee4b01470d04de20d|access-date=2020-12-07|website=HuffPost|language=en}} < /ref >  They may not feel [[Romance (love)|romantic love]], and may not feel the need to be in romantic relationships. It doesn't mean they are [[Agamy|agamous]]. < ref > {{Cite web|date=2019-02-24|title=LGBTQIA+ history month: You might be on the aromantic spectrum if…|url=http://www.dih.si/en/interesting-en/2019/you-might-be-on-the-aromantic-spectrum-if|access-date=2020-12-07|website=DIH|language=en-US}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite web|title=That’s So Aromantic!|url=https://www.psychologytoday.com/blog/living-single/201710/s-so-aromantic|access-date=2020-12-07|website=Psychology Today|language=en-US}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite web|title=AUREA - FAQ|url=https://www.aromanticism.org/en/faq|access-date=2020-12-07|website=AUREA|language=en-US}} < /ref > 
 
 They can have any [[sexual orientation]], be [[Asexuality|asexual]], [[bisexual]], [[heterosexual]], [[homosexual]], [[pansexual]], [[Polysexuality|polysexual]], [[Omnisexuality|omnisexual]], [[Pomosexuality|pomosexual]], or [[Ceterosexuality|ceterosexual]].
