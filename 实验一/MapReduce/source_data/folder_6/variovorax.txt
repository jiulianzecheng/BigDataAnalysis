 == Characteristics == 
 Variovorax cells function alone or in pairs, and use a flagellum to move around. < ref name= " :0 "  / > They contain [[carotenoid]] pigments which leads to a yellow appearance of the colonies. The bacteria are tolerate to a wide variety of metals including cobalt, copper, and nickel, which allows for the bacteria to survive in many environments. < ref > {{Cite journal|last=Belimov|first=A.  == Habitat and occurrence == 
 Bacteria of the Variovorax genus can often be found in damp, dark conditions of soil and water. < ref > {{Cite journal|last=Lee|first=J.|last2=Lee|first2=C. S.|last3=Hugunin|first3=K.  == Environmental function == 
 Variovorax species have many diverse roles in their environmental surroundings, a noticeable one being promoting plant growth. There is a variety of mechanisms by which the bacteria can accomplish this, including the preventing of plant pathogens being produced, reducing the amount of stress a plant undergoes, and also increasing the number of nutrients that are available. < ref > {{Cite journal|last=Han|first=Jong-In|last2=Choi|first2=Hong-Kyu|last3=Lee|first3=Seung-Won|last4=Orwin|first4=Paul M.|last5=Kim|first5=Jina|last6=LaRoe|first6=Sarah L.|last7=Kim|first7=Tae-gyu|last8=O'Neil|first8=Jennifer|last9=Leadbetter|first9=Jared R.|last10=Lee|first10=Sang Yup|last11=Hur|first11=Cheol-Goo|date=2011-03-01|title=Complete Genome Sequence of the Metabolically Versatile Plant Growth-Promoting Endophyte Variovorax paradoxus S110|url=https://journals.asm.org/doi/10.1128/JB.00925-10|journal=Journal of Bacteriology|language=en|volume=193|issue=5|pages=1183–1190|doi=10.1128/JB.00925-10|issn=0021-9193|pmc=3067606|pmid=21183664}} < /ref >  Other roles can also be witnessed, including the cycling of various elements across a range of environments including [[arsenic]]. < ref > {{Cite journal|last=Bahar|first=Md Mezbaul|last2=Megharaj|first2=Mallavarapu|last3=Naidu|first3=Ravi|date=2013-11-15|title=Kinetics of arsenite oxidation by Variovorax sp. MM-1 isolated from a soil and identification of arsenite oxidase gene|url=https://linkinghub.elsevier.com/retrieve/pii/S030438941201182X|journal=Journal of Hazardous Materials|language=en|volume=262|pages=997–1003|doi=10.1016/j.jhazmat.2012.11.064}} < /ref >  It is thought to oxidise As (III) to As (V) via a detoxification mechanism. < ref > {{Cite journal|last=Macur|first=Richard E.|last2=Jackson|first2=Colin R.|last3=Botero|first3=Lina M.|last4=Mcdermott|first4=Timothy R.|last5=Inskeep|first5=William P.|date=2004-01-01|title=Bacterial Populations Associated with the Oxidation and Reduction of Arsenic in an Unsaturated Soil|url=https://pubs.acs.org/doi/10.1021/es034455a|journal=Environmental Science  &  Technology|language=en|volume=38|issue=1|pages=104–111|doi=10.1021/es034455a|issn=0013-936X}} < /ref > 
 
 Another potential use which has been discovered for the Variovorax genus is its role in bioremediation studies. < ref > {{Cite journal|last=Han|first=Jong-In|last2=Choi|first2=Hong-Kyu|last3=Lee|first3=Seung-Won|last4=Orwin|first4=Paul M.|last5=Kim|first5=Jina|last6=LaRoe|first6=Sarah L.|last7=Kim|first7=Tae-gyu|last8=O'Neil|first8=Jennifer|last9=Leadbetter|first9=Jared R.|last10=Lee|first10=Sang Yup|last11=Hur|first11=Cheol-Goo|date=2011-02-09|title=Complete Genome Sequence of the Metabolically Versatile Plant Growth-Promoting Endophyte Variovorax paradoxus S110|url=https://journals.asm.org/doi/10.1128/JB.00925-10|journal=Journal of Bacteriology|language=en|volume=193|issue=5|pages=1183–1190|doi=10.1128/JB.00925-10|issn=0021-9193|pmc=3067606|pmid=21183664}} < /ref >  ''Variovorax paradoxus'' has been found to be a potential use in the bioremediation of soils contaminated with linuron, which could be depolluted through biopurification via bioaugmentation. < ref name= " :0 "  / >
