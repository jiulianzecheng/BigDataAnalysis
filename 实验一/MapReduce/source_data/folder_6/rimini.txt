 '''Rimini''' is a [[city]] in the [[region]] of [[Emilia-Romagna]] in [[Italy]]. It is also the [[Capital (city)|capital]] of the ''[[Province of Rimini]]''. About 138,000 people live in Rimini. 
 
 Rimini is a popular [[seaside resort]] known for its [[Bar (business)|bars]] and [[nightclub]]s. It has a [[humid subtropical climate]] (''Cfa'' in the [[Köppen climate classification]]).
