 '''Juist''' ({{IPA-de|ˈjyːst}}) ({{lang-nds|Juist}}) is an island and municipality in [[Aurich (district)|Aurich]] in [[Lower Saxony]] in [[Germany]]. The island is one of seven [[East Frisian Islands]] at the edge of the [[Lower Saxony|Lower Saxon]] [[Wadden Sea]] in the southern [[North Sea]]. It is between [[Borkum]] Island (west), [[Memmert]] Island (southwest) and [[Norderney]] (east). The island is {{convert|17|km|0|abbr=on}} long and from {{convert|500|m}} to {{convert|1|km}} wide, depending on the tide levels. There are two villages on the island: the main village Juist, and Loog. The island is separated from Norderney by the [[Norderneyer Seegatt]].
