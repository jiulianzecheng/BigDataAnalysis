'''The Batcave''' is the fictional secret lair of [[Batman]]. It is on the foundation of [[Wayne Manor]]. There are two ways into the cave, one is through a [[waterfall]] on the back of the cave and the other is a built-in elevator that leads from the library of Wayne Manor to the bridge of the cave. < ref > {{Cite web |url=http://goldenagecomics.org/wordpress/2009/02/08/the-origin-of-the-batcave/ |title=The Origin of the Bat Cave |access-date=2013-01-13 |archive-date=2012-11-10 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20121110171839/http://goldenagecomics.org/wordpress/2009/02/08/the-origin-of-the-batcave/ |url-status=dead }} < /ref >  It holds all of Batman's secrets, gadgets, the [[Batmobile]], and a real gorge. The cave also has millions of [[bats]].
