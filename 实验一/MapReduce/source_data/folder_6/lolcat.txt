 A '''lolcat''' is a common [[Internet meme]]  which is made up of picture of a cat with a funny [[wikt:caption|caption]]. The caption is usually in bad [[English language|English]] that makes fun of [[Internet slang]]. like chez and haz.  " Lolcat "  is a mix of the words [[lol]] and cat. Lol is internet slang for  " laughing out loud.  ==Image== 
 Lolcats are pictures of cats doing something out of the ordinary quoted words in big, [[sans serif]] letters (letters without feet). Sometimes the picture is changed with a [[computer]] to make it funnier. 
 
 There are lots of different lolcat memes, like ceiling cat, monorail cat, and 'i eated a butter' cat
