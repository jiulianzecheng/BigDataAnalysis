 A '''hick''' is a term for an [[United States|American]] [[stereotype]], which is also used in Canada to a lesser degree. It is a term for people who live in [[rural]] areas, especially on [[farm]]s and [[ranch]]es and is usually meant as an [[insult]] and considered offensive to most people. It is mostly used to describe people in the south-central United States, including Texas, Missouri, Arkansas, Georgia, Illinois, Indiana, Ohio, Tennessee, Kentucky, Kansas, Iowa, and Nebraska. 
 
 Other forms of the word 'hick' are 'redneck', 'yokel', 'hillbilly', and 'hayseed'.
