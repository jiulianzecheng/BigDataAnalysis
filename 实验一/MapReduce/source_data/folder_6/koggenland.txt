 '''Koggenland''' is a [[List of municipalities of the Netherlands|municipality]] in the [[Netherlands|Dutch]] [[Provinces of the Netherlands|province]] of [[North Holland]]. In 2019, 22,738 people lived there. 
 
 It was created on 1 January 2007 from the former municipalities of [[Obdam]] and [[Wester-Koggenland]].
