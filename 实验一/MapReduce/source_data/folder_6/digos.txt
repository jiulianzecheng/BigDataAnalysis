'''Digos''' is a city and capital of the [[Provinces of the Philippines|province]] of [[Davao del Sur]], [[Philippines]]. The city lies on the western shores of the [[Davao Gulf]] and southern foothills of [[Mount Apo]] on the island of [[Mindanao]].
