 '''''Graphania''''' is a [[genus]] of [[moth]]s. It is part of the [[Noctuidae]] family. 
 
 The genus is made up of more than 100 species. < ref name=GlobalSpecies > {{cite web|title=Graphania|url=http://globalspecies.org/ntaxa/2085515|publisher=Global Species|accessdate=15 June 2012}}{{Dead link|date=January 2021 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }} < /ref >  Some of them are:

 * ''Graphania averilla''  & ndash;  < small > Hudson, 1921 < /small > 
 * ''Graphania beata''  & ndash;  < small > Howes, 1906 < /small > 
 * ''Graphania bromias''  & ndash;  < small > Meyrick, 1902 < /small > 
 * ''Graphania brunneosa''  & ndash;  < small > Fox, 1970 < /small > 
 * ''Graphania chlorodonta''  & ndash;  < small > Hampson, 1911 < /small > 
 * ''Graphania disjungens''  & ndash;  < small > Walker, 1858 < /small > 
 * ''Graphania erebia''  & ndash;  < small > Hudson, 1909 < /small > 
 * ''Graphania fenwicki''  & ndash;  < small > Philpott, 1921 < /small > 
 * ''Graphania lindsayi''  & ndash;  < small > Dugdale, 1988 < /small >
