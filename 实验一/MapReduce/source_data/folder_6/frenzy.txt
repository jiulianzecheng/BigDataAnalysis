 '''''Frenzy''''' is a 1972 [[United Kingdom|British]] [[crime movie|crime]] [[thriller movie]] set in [[London]]. It is [[movie director|directed]] by [[Alfred Hitchcock]] starring [[Jon Finch]] and [[Barry Foster]]. It tells the story of an innocent man, played by Finch who is wrongly believed to be a [[serial killer]] by the [[police]] and his efforts to avoid [[capture]] and bring the real killer to [[justice]]. This was the second to last movie that Hitchcock made before he died in 1980 and the first time in many years he had made a movie in the [[United Kingdom]].
