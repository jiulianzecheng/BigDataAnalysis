 '''Sheraton''' is one of the largest international hotel chains. It was founded in 1937. The owner is [[Marriott International]]. Until 2016, it was owned by [[Starwood Hotels and Resorts Worldwide]]. 
 
 == Activity == 
 As of 2015, the Sheraton network includes 446 hotels with a total capacity of 156,400 rooms. 11 hotels are owned by Starwood Hotels and Resorts Worldwide, others are part of the company on a franchise basis.
