'''Iodate''' is an [[ion]]. Its chemical formula is IO < sub > 3 < /sub > < sup > - < /sup > . The iodine is in the +5 [[oxidation state]]. It can be made by [[reduction (chemistry)|reduction]] of [[periodate]]. They are similar to [[chlorate]]s. They react with [[acid]]s to make [[iodic acid]]. They are [[salt]]s of iodic acid. An example would be [[sodium iodate]].
