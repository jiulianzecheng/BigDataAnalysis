'''Kanchipuram''' is one of the seven most [[Holy city|holy cities]] of [[Hindu]]s. It is about {{convert|60|km}} southwest of [[Chennai]], [[Tamil Nadu]] state, [[India]]. Kanchipuram was an important [[Religion|religious]] and [[Culture|cultural]] place of [[Ancient India]]. The city continues to be an important religious place.
