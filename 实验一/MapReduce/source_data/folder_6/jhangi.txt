 '''Jhangi''' is one of the 51 [[Union Councils of Pakistan|Union Councils]] of [[Abbottabad District]] in the [[Khyber Pakhtunkhwa]] province of [[Pakistan]]. < ref > {{Cite web |url=http://www.abbottabad.sdnpk.org/adminunit.htm |title=Administrative Units of District Abbottabad |access-date=2008-08-01 |archive-date=2007-04-03 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20070403104619/http://www.abbottabad.sdnpk.org/adminunit.htm |url-status=dead }} < /ref >  It is also known as '''Jhangi Syedan''' to the local people. It is to the north of [[Abbottabad]] city (the district capital). It includes the northwestern parts of the city, its suburbs, and outlying villages. 
 
 Jhangi is at 34°12'0N 73°13'60E with a height of 1201 metres (3943 & nbsp;feet). < ref > [http://www.fallingrain.com/world/PK/3/Jhangi.html Location of Jhangi] < /ref >  To the south is the city of [[Abbottabad]].
