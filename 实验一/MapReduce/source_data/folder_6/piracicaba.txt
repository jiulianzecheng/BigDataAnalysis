 '''Piracicaba''' ({{IPA-pt|piɾɐsiˈkabɐ}} or {{IPA-pt|piˌɾasiˈkabɐ|}}) is an [[Brazil]]ian [[city]] in the [[States of Brazil|state]] of [[São Paulo (state)|São Paulo]]. In 2022, 423,323 people lived here. Its area is 1,378.07 & nbsp;km². 
 
 The city is an important and traditional producer of [[sugarcane]]. < ref > {{Cite web|date=2023-07-21|title=40 Facts About Piracicaba|url=https://facts.net/world/cities/40-facts-about-piracicaba/|access-date=2023-11-11|website=Facts.net|language=en-US}} < /ref >
