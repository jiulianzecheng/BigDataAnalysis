 '''Fidesz – Hungarian Civic Alliance''' ([[Hungarian language|Hungarian]]: ''Fidesz – Magyar Polgári Szövetség''; often shortened to '''Fidesz''') is a [[nationalism|nationalist]] [[political party]] in [[Hungary]]. 
 
 Fidesz is in an alliance with the [[Christian Democratic People's Party (Hungary)|Christian Democratic People's Party]] (KDNP). The KDNP is often called a satellite party (meaning that it is strongly influenced by another party) of Fidesz. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.fairobserver.com/article/sanctions-hungary-what-and-why-now|title=Sanctions on Hungary: What For and Why Now?|author=Alexander Herholz|date=2012-02-12}} < /ref > < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.sussex.ac.uk/sei/documents/epern-election-briefing-no-51.pdf|title=Election Briefing no. 51: Europe and the Hungarian Parliamentary Elections of April 2010|author=Dr. Agnes Batory|year=2010}} < /ref > 
 
 [[Viktor Orbán]] has led the party continuously since 2003. 
 
 Fidesz has been the most popular party in Hungary since the [[Hungarian parliamentary election, 2010|2010 elections]].
