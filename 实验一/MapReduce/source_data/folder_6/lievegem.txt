'''Lievegem''' is a [[municipality]] in the [[Belgium|Belgian]] [[Provinces of Belgium|province]] of [[East Flanders]]. It was created on 1 January 2019 when the municipalities of [[Lovendegem]], [[Waarschoot]] and [[Zomergem]] joined together to become the new municipality of Lievegem. 
 
 The new municipality has an area of {{convert|80.17|km2|sqmi|abbr=on}} and more than 25,000 people.
