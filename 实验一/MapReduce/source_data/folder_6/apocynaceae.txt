 The '''Apocynaceae''' or '''dogbane family''' is a family of [[flowering plant]]s in the order [[Gentianales]]. It includes [[tree]]s, [[shrub]]s, [[herb]]s, and [[liana]]s. They usually have a milky sap. There are about 200 genera and 2,000 species.
