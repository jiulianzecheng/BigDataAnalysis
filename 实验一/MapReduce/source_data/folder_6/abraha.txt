'''Abraha''' is a name from [[Ethiopia]] that has been used for many years and is still commonly used today. It means  " father of multitudes "  or  " father of many nations "  and comes from the Amharic language, which is Ethiopia's official language. Abraha is usually given to boys but can also be given to girls. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Abraha name Meaning and Origin|url=https://babys.name/detail-Abraha-Meaning-Origin-Famous-Similar-Sibling.html|access-date=2023-04-13|website=babys.name}} < /ref >
