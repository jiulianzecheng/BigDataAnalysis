'''Goetia''' is a practice which involves [[wikt:invoke|invoking]] ( " calling " ) [[angels]] and [[demons]]. The word came from the 17th-century [[grimoire]] ''The Lesser Key of Solomon''. It describes the calling of seventy-two demons. The book was edited by [[Aleister Crowley]] in 1904. It was then called ''The Book of the Goetia of Solomon the King''. 
 
 The word comes from the [[Greek language|Greek]] word {{lang|el|γοητεία}} ({{transliteration|el|goēteia}}), meaning  " charm " .
