 {{nihongo|The '''Casio Computer Company, Limited'''|カシオ計算機株式会社|Kashio Keisanki Kabushiki-gaisha}} is an [[electronics]] [[corporation|company]] whose headquarters are in [[Tokyo]], [[Japan]]. Its products include [[calculator]]s, mobile phones, [[camera]]s, [[musical instruments]] and [[watch]]es. It was founded in 1946, and in 1957 released the first electric compact calculator.
