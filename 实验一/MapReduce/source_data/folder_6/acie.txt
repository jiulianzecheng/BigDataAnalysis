'''Acie''' is a name that has become more popular in recent years. It is a unique and charming name that can be given to both boys and girls.  Acie comes from the [[Latin]] word  " acere, "  meaning  " sharp "  or  " keen. "  Additionally, it is a shortened version of the name [[Acacia|Acacia,]] which is a type of tree that has beautiful flowers and strong wood.
