 == Overview == 
 EssentiallySports, founded in 2014 by Harit Pathak, Jaskirat Arora, and Suryansh Tibarewal, began as a forum for sports fans and transformed into a remote sports media company. < ref > {{cite web|title=Bootstrapped Vs VC? EssentiallySports: A Startup Story From The Sporting World|url=https://www.outlookindia.com/website/story/outlook-spotlight-bootstrapped-vs-vc-essentiallysports-a-startup-story-from-the-sporting-world/392534|website=outlookindia.com}} < /ref > < ref > {{cite web|title=EssentiallySports: A Peek Into The Journey of Building a Digital-First Media House for a Global Sports Audience|url=https://www.theweek.in/news/sports/2021/06/08/EssentiallySports-A-Peek-Into-The-Journey-of-Building-a-Digital-First-Media-House-for-a-Global-Sports-Audience.html|website=theweek.in}} < /ref >   
 
 In 2022, the parent company of EssentiallySports, Full Spectrum Services LLP acquired an entertainment news website NetflixJunkie. The company generates revenue through programmatic advertising. < ref > {{Cite web|last=Bhushan|first=Kul|date=2023-03-07|title=EssentiallySports targets US sports content market with fresh, youthful approach|url=https://entrackr.com/2023/03/essentiallysports-targets-us-sports-content-market-with-fresh-youthful-approach/|access-date=2023-03-28|website=Entrackr|language=en-US}} < /ref > 
 
 Tennis was one of the earliest sports covered by EssentiallySports. In 2020, EssentiallySports launched a tennis podcast series ‘Quarentennis - Bridging the Broken Strings’ which features exclusive interviews with top names in tennis. < ref > {{cite web|title=Quarantennis - How EssentiallySports is Setting Out to Bridge the gap between Tennis Players and the Fans|url=https://www.oneindia.com/india/how-essentiallysports-is-setting-out-to-bridge-the-gap-between-tennis-players-and-the-fans-3321175.html|website=oneindia.com}} < /ref >  The website has 30M readers monthly in 150 countries. < ref > {{cite web|title=EssentiallySports onboards ex NDTV Convergence CEO, Sanjay Trehan|url=https://www.afaqs.com/people-spotting/essentiallysports-onboards-ex-ndtv-convergence-ceo-sanjay-trehan|website=afaqs.com}} < /ref >
