 '''Oudenaarde''' is a [[municipality]] in the [[Belgium|Belgian]] province of [[East Flanders]]. 
 
 In 2007, 29,702 people lived there. < ref > http://statbel.fgov.be/nl/binaries/Pop%20Bevolk%2001012010_v2_f_tcm325-109882.xls < /ref > 
 
 It is at 50° 50 North, 03° 36 East. < ref > {{Cite web |url=http://statbel.fgov.be/pub/d1/p120_nl.xls |title=Geografische coördinaten van de gemeenten (.xls) |access-date=2008-07-15 |archive-date=2008-11-24 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20081124205332/http://www.statbel.fgov.be/pub/d1/p120_nl.xls |url-status=dead }} < /ref >
