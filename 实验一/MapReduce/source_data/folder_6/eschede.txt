 '''Eschede''' ({{IPA-de|'ɛʃədə}} < ref > [https://books.google.com/books?id=T6vWCgAAQBAJ & dq=Eschede+Aussprache & pg=PA351 Pronunciation according to Duden] < /ref > ) is a municipality in [[Celle (district)|Celle]], in [[Lower Saxony]], [[Germany]]. About 15 & nbsp;km northeast of [[Celle]], Eschede is at the border of the [[Südheide Nature Park]], a protected area of large forests and heaths. Today around 20 small villages are part of the  " Gemeinde Eschede " .
