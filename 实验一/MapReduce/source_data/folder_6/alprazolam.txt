 '''Alprazolam''', probably better known by its trade name '''Xanax''', is a short-acting drug. The drug is used to treat people with [[anxiety]] disorders and [[panic attack]]s. Alprazolam is the most commonly misused [[benzodiazepine]] (the drug's class) in the [[United States]]; but the majority of prescribed users do not develop a substance-use disorder. Alprazolam is a prescription drug in the United States.
