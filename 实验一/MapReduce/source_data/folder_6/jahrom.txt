 '''Jahrom''' (جهرم) is a city in south [[Iran]] with a population of 141,000 (2016). Most of them are [[Persian people|Persians]] and [[Shia Islam|Shia Musllims]]. Jahrom is the largest city in the southern half of [[Fars Province]].
