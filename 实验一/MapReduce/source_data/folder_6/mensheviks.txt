 The ''''Mensheviks''' ({{lang-ru|меньшевики́}}), also known as the Minority,  were moderate socialists in the [[Russian Social Democratic Labour Party]], a party in The [[Russian Empire]] which wanted socialism to be adopted in Russia in the early 20th century. Their opponents were the [[Bolshevik|Bolsheviks]], who were radical [[Communism|communists]] who wanted a [[dictatorship]]. The Mensheviks wanted [[social democracy]].
