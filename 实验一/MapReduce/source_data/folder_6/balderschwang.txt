 '''Balderschwang''' is a [[municipality]] in the district of [[Oberallgäu]] in [[Bavaria]] in [[Germany]]. It is home to the [[Balderschwang Yew]], which may be the oldest tree in Germany. < ref > {{Cite book|last=Fröhlich|first=Hans Joachim|year=2000|title=Alte liebenswerte Bäume in Deutschland|edition=2nd|publisher=Ahlering|location=Buchholz|language=de|isbn=3-926600-05-5}} < /ref >  It has a population of 219. < ref > {{Cite web|url=https://www.statistikdaten.bayern.de/genesis/online/data;jsessionid=9E9D2E482436B1C61D7F5872047C4396?operation=ergebnistabelleUmfang & levelindex=2 & levelid=1485072449901 & downloadname=12411-001|title=Statistikdaten - Bayern|website=Statistikdaten|language=de|access-date=22 January 2017}} < /ref >  With an average of 2,450 liters per square meter per year, Balderschwang is the place with the most rainfall in Germany.  < ref > {{Cite web|url=http://www.dw.com/de/je-mehr-wetter-desto-besser/a-765495|title=DW - Je mehr wetter desto besser|language=de}} < /ref >
