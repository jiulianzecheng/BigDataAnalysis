 '''Frauenneuharting''' is a community in the district of [[Ebersberg (district)|Ebersberg]] in [[Upper Bavaria]] and a member of the administrative community of Aßling. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 Frauenneuharting is in the [[Munich]] planning region.
