 '''Malleray''' was a [[municipality]] in the [[administrative district]] of [[Jura bernois (administrative district)|Jura bernois]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Bern (canton)|Bern]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 On 1 January 2015, the former municipalities of Malleray, [[Bévilard]] and [[Pontenet]] merged into the new municipality of [[Valbirse]].
