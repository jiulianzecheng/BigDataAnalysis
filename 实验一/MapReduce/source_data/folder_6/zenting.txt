 '''Zenting''' is a [[Municipalities of Germany|municipality]] in the [[Freyung-Grafenau]] district in [[Bavaria]] in [[Germany]]. 
 
 == Place == 
 The municipality is in the Danube Forest (''Donau-Wald'') in the middle of the [[Bavarian Forest]]. The village is near the [[Austria|Austrian]] and [[Czech Republic|Czech]] borders. The [[Brotjacklriegel]] (1,016 & nbsp;m) and [[Aschenstein]] (945 & nbsp;m) mountains are to the north. Zenting is around 35 & nbsp;km NW of [[Passau]], 13 & nbsp;km from [[Tittling]], 18 & nbsp;km SW of  [[Grafenau (Lower Bavaria)|Grafenau]], 26 & nbsp;km N of [[Vilshofen an der Donau]] and 15 & nbsp;km from the [[Bundesautobahn 3|A 3]] ([[Iggensbach]] exit).
