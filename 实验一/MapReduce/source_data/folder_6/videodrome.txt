 '''''Videodrome''''' is a 1983 Canadian American science fiction horror movie directed by [[David Cronenberg]] and starring [[James Woods]], [[Debbie Harry]], [[Sonja Smits]], [[Leslie Carlson]], [[Jack Creley]]. It was distributed by [[Universal Pictures]] and is a cult classic.
