'''Inversnaid''' is a village on the east bank of [[Loch Lomond]] in [[Scotland]], near the north end of the loch.  It has a pier and a hotel, and the [[West Highland Way]] passes through the village. A small passenger ferry runs from Inversnaid to [[Inveruglas]] on the opposite shore of the loch. To reach Inversnaid by road involves a lengthy overland route from [[Aberfoyle, Scotland|Aberfoyle]].
