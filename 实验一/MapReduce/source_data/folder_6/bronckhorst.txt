 '''Bronckhorst''' is a municipality in the province of [[Gelderland]], the Netherlands. About 36,000 people were living there in 2021. 
 
 Bronckhorst lies in the regions of [[Achterhoek]] and [[IJsselvallei]]. A small part in the southwest lies in the region of [[Liemers]]. 
 
 It was created from the former municipalities of [[Hengelo, Gelderland|Hengelo]], [[Hummelo en Keppel]], [[Steenderen]], [[Vorden]], and [[Zelhem]] on 1 January 2005.
