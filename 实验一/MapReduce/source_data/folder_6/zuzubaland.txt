 '''''Zuzubaland''''' ({{Lang-pt|Zuzubalândia}}) is a [[Brazil|Brazilian]] [[Flash animation|Flash-animated]] children's television series based on the 1997 book ''Jujubalândia'' and the animated reboot of the 1998 live-action program of the same name. It was created, directed and written by [[Mariana Caltabiano]]. It originally aired on [[Boomerang (TV network)|Boomerang]] in May 25, 2018. < ref > {{Cite web|last=Redação|date=2018-04-11|title=Boomerang estreia  " Zuzubalândia " , sua primeira produção original brasileira|url=https://telaviva.com.br/11/04/2018/boomerang-estreia-zuzubalandia-sua-primeira-producao-original-brasileira/|access-date=2023-07-19|website=TELA VIVA News|language=pt-BR}} < /ref > 
 
 == Plot == 
 The series revolves around the [[bee]] [[girl]] named Zuzu and her friends in the kingdom of Zuzubaland. 
 
 == Characters == 
 
 * '''Zuzu''' ({{Voiced by|Daniel Costa (Portuguese); [[Dino Andrade]] (English)}}) a cute and tomboyish little bee girl with green eyes, [[Mary Jane (shoe)|black Mary Jane shoes]] and a pink nose. 
 * '''Rocky Cocoa''' ({{Voiced by|Hugo Picchi (Portuguese); [[Zeno Robinson]] (English)}}) a black man who loves [[boxing]] and [[judo]]. 
 * '''Hot Dog''' ({{Voiced by|Eduardo Jardim (Portuguese); [[Joe J. Thomas]] (English)}}) a sweet tooth [[dog]] who likes to lie down and roll around in puddles of mustard and [[ketchup]]. He is also Rocky Cocoa's best friend. 
 * '''Popcorn''' ({{Voiced by|Luiza Porto (Portuguese); [[Amber Connor]] (English)}}) a girl who is Zuzu's best friend. She likes to talk on the [[cell phone]] and send messages to his friends. 
 * '''Sundae''' ({{Voiced by|Hugo Picchi (Portuguese); [[Aleks Le]] (English)}}) a 10 year old boy who is very shy and in love with Popcorn. 
 * '''Garfidea''' ({{Voiced by|Bruna Guerin (Portuguese); [[Dale Edwards]] (English)}}) a purple spider who is Witch's assistant. 
 * '''Fast''' ({{Voiced by|Daniel Costa (Portuguese); [[Shannon McKain]] (English)}}) the fastest guy in the world. He is King Appetite's assistant and messenger. 
 * '''Marsha Mallow''' ({{Voiced by|Bruna Guerin (Portuguese); [[Ryan Bartley]] (English)}}) a lazy girl. 
 * '''King Appetite''' ({{Voiced by|Hugo Picchi (Portuguese); [[Jason Marnocha]] (English)}}) the King of Zuzubaland. 
 * '''Sushiroco''' ({{Voiced by|Eduardo Jardim (Portuguese); [[Khoi Dao]] (English)}}) a Japanese [[chef]] who lives on the ocean shores. He works at a Japanese restaurant and [[Karaoke]]. His pets are two sharks named  " Missoshiro "  and  " Sukiaki " . 
 * '''The Witch''' ({{Voiced by|Antoniela Canto (Portuguese); [[Wendee Lee]] (English)}}) the main antagonist of the series, she hates food, joy and Zuzu's song. She wants to destroy the kingdom of Zuzubaland.
