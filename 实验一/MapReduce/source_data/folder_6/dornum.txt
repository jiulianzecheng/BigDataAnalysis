 '''Dornum''' is a village and a municipality in [[Aurich (district)|Aurich]], in [[Lower Saxony]], [[Germany]]. It is near the [[North Sea]] coast, about 15 & nbsp;km east of [[Norden, Lower Saxony|Norden]], and 20 & nbsp;km north of [[Aurich]]. 
 
 ==Division of the municipality== 
 The other towns in the municipality of Dornum are: [[Neßmersiel]], [[Dornumersiel]], [[Nesse, Dornum|Nesse]], [[Roggenstede]], [[Westerbur]], [[Westeraccum]], [[Schwittersum]].
