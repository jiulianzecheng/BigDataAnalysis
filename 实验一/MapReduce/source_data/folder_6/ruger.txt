 '''Michael Adebayo Olayinka''' (born 23 September 1999), popularly known as '''Ruger,''' is a [[Nigeria]] singer and songwriter. < ref name= " The Guardian Nigeria News " > {{cite web |title=Enter Ruger, Naija's new fearless act |url=https://guardian.ng/saturday-magazine/weekend-beats/enter-ruger-naijas-new-fearless-act/ |website=The Guardian Nigeria |access-date=7 January 2022 |date=17 July 2021}} < /ref >   He rose to fame from his hit breaking song 'Bounce', which lead to a record deal in 2021 with D'Prince  " Jonzing World record "  for a publishing and distribution deal with [[Columbia Records]], < ref > {{Cite web|date=2021-01-18|title=Jonzing World's Record Label signs Ruger to join Rema|url=https://www.vanguardngr.com/2021/01/jonzing-worlds-record-label-signs-ruger-to-join-rema/|access-date=2022-01-03|website=Vanguard News|language=en-US}} < /ref >  and [[Sony Music Entertainment]], U.K division. < ref > {{cite web |last1=Alake |first1=Motolani |title=D'Prince's Jonzing World unveils new artist, Ruger |url=https://www.pulse.ng/entertainment/music/dprinces-jonzing-world-signs-new-artist-ruger/t29dmdm |website=Pulse Nigeria |access-date=7 January 2022 |language=en |date=19 January 2021}} < /ref >
