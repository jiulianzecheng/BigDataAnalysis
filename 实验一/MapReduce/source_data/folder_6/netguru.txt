 '''Netguru''' is a Polish software company. Its [[headquarters]] is in [[Poznań]], Poland. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Press Office {{!}} Netguru|url=https://www.netguru.com/about-us/press|access-date=2022-06-28|website=www.netguru.com|language=en}} < /ref >  The company was three times in Deloitte's Technology Fast 50 Central Europe ranking, < ref > {{Cite web|title=Technology Fast 50 Central Europe|url=https://www2.deloitte.com/content/dam/Deloitte/global/Documents/About-Deloitte/central-europe/technology-fast-50-central-europe-2017-report.pdf}} < /ref >  and twice on the  " FT 1000, "  the Financial Times list of fastest-growing companies in Europe. < ref > {{Cite web|title=The FT 1000: Europe’s fastest-growing companies|url=https://ig.ft.com/ft-1000|access-date=2022-06-28|website=ig.ft.com|language=en-GB}} < /ref >   
 
 == History == 
 The company was founded in 2008 by Wiktor Schmidt, Jakub Filipowski, and Adam Zygadlewicz. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Z trzyosobowego start-upu w guru internetu. Nowy gracz na liście Diamentów|url=https://www.forbes.pl/diamenty/2016/artykuly/netguru-z-trzyosobowego-start-upu-w-guru-internetu/xnyqymv|access-date=2022-06-28|website=Forbes.pl|language=pl}} < /ref >  In 2014, Netguru moved into Israel and the Middle East markets. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Tel Aviv Startup Scene, Middle East Mobile Market and Netguru's Future plans|url=https://www.netguru.com/blog/tel-aviv-startup-scene-middle-east|access-date=2022-06-28|website=www.netguru.com|language=en}} < /ref >  In 2015, the company joined the London fintech community. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Netguru Joining Level39: at the Heart of London FinTech Hub|url=https://www.netguru.com/blog/netguru-level39-london-fin-tech|access-date=2022-06-28|website=www.netguru.com|language=en}} < /ref >  In 2018, Netguru reached an annual turnover of about PLN 80 million. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Netguru chce wymyślać programy, na które nie wpadł jeszcze nikt|url=https://wyborcza.biz/akcjespecjalne/7,154807,24142842,netguru-chce-wymyslac-programy-na-ktore-nie-wpadl-jeszcze-nikt.html|access-date=2022-06-28|website=gazetapl|language=pl}} < /ref >  In 2019, Netguru's chief operating officer Marek Talarczyk became the company's new CEO. < ref > {{Cite web|date=2019-06-03|title=Zmiany w zarządzie Netguru. Poznańska firma ma nowego CEO|url=https://businessinsider.com.pl/firmy/zarzadzanie/netguru-ma-nowego-ceo-zostal-nim-marek-talarczyk/bz9lq0z|access-date=2022-06-28|website=Businessinsider|language=pl}} < /ref >
