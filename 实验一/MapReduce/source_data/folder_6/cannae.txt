'''Cannae''' (now '''Canne della Battaglia''',  < small > Italian pronunciation: < /small >  [kanne della battaʎʎa]) is an ancient village of the [[Apulia]] region of south eastern [[Italy]]. It is a ''frazione'' (civil parish) of the ''comune'' (municipality) of [[Barletta]], a former bishopric that is now a Latin Catholic titular see. The famous [[Battle of Cannae]] had troops of [[Carthage]] win despite being outnumbered by troops of the [[Roman Republic]].
