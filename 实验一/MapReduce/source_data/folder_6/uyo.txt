'''Uyo''' is a city in [[Nigeria]]. It is the capital of [[Akwa Ibom State]]. [[Ibibio language|Ibibio]] is the language spoken in Uyo. 
 
 It became the capital on September 23, 1987 when Akwa Ibom was created from the former [[Cross River State]].
