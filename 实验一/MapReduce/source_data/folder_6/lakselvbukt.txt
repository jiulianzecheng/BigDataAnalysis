 '''Lakselvbukt '''is a small [[village]] located in the [[Tromsø Municipality]] of [[Troms og Finnmark]] county in [[Norway]]. It is situated on the inner part of the [[Ullsfjorden]] and is around {{convert|16|km}} northeast of the village of [[Laksvatn]] in the neighboring [[Balsfjord|Balsfjord Municipality]]. The village is also located near the western edge of the [[Lyngen Alps]], which is around {{convert|75|km}} away from the [[Tromsø (city)|city of Tromsø]].
