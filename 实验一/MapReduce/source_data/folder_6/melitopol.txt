 '''Melitopol''' ({{lang-uk|Мелітополь}}; {{lang-ru|Мелитополь}}) is a city in [[Zaporizhzhia Oblast]] ([[oblast|province]]) of southeastern [[Ukraine]]. It is the administrative center of [[Melitopol Raion]] ([[raion|district]]). In 2021, 150,768 people lived there.
