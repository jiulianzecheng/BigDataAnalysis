 '''Maria da Graça Xuxa Meneghel''' (born 27 March 1963) is a Brazilian television host, film actress, singer, model, and businesswoman. In Brazil it is known as  " Rainha dos Baixinhos " .  
 
 She was born in [[Santa Rosa, Rio Grande do Sul|Santa Rosa]], Brazil on March 27, 1963 and she has dated soccer player [[Pelé|Pele]] and [[Ayrton Senna]]. She has also met the singer [[Michael Jackson]] and he took her to Neverland and asked her to be the mother of his children.
