'''Vikramasingapuram''' is a municipal town in [[Tirunelveli district|Tirunelveli District]] in the [[India]]n [[States and territories of India|state]] of [[Tamil Nadu]]. It is under the [[Ambasamudram]] Taluk. As of 2011, the town had a population of 47,241. 
 
 ==Demographics== 
 According to [[2011 census of India|2011 census]], Vikramasingapuram had a population of 47,241.
