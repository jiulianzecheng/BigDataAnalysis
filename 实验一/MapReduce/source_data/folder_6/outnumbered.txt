 '''''Outnumbered''''' is a [[United Kingdom|British]] [[sitcom]] shown on [[BBC One]]. It began on 28 August 2007 and is partly [[improvisation|improvised]]. It is about the Brockmans, a [[middle-class]] family of five who live in a house in [[suburb]]an south [[London]]. [[Hugh Dennis]] plays the father, Pete, who is a [[history]] [[teacher]]. [[Claire Skinner]] plays the mother, Sue, who is a [[part-time]] [[personal assistant]]. [[Tyger Drew-Honey]] plays the elder son, Jake, whose main interest is [[teenage]] girls. [[Daniel Roche (actor)|Daniel Roche]] plays the younger son, Ben, who has [[attention-deficit hyperactivity disorder]]. [[Ramona Marquez]] plays the daughter, Karen, who is [[intelligent]] and [[ask]]s a lot of [[question]]s. The show was axed in 2015. 
 
 ==Awards== 
 ''Outnumbered'' was nominated for the 2008  " ''[[Broadcast (magazine)|Broadcast]]'' Award "  for  " Best Comedy Programme " . < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.sitcom.co.uk/news/news.php?story=000347|title=News - Broadcast Awards 2008 shortlist revealed|publisher=British Sitcom Guide|date=2007-12-10|accessdate=2007-12-11}}{{Dead link|date=January 2022 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }} < /ref >  It lost the award to ''[[The Thick of It]]''. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www2.broadcastnow.co.uk/awards/frames.asp?|title=Broadcast Awards 2008 - Shortlist|publisher=Broadcast Awards|accessdate=2008-01-15|archive-date=2008-01-19|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20080119125731/http://www2.broadcastnow.co.uk/awards/frames.asp|url-status=dead}} < /ref > 
 
 The show was given the  " British Comedy Guide Editors' Award "  in The Comedy.co.uk Awards 2007 < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.comedy.co.uk/awards/2007/|title=The Comedy.co.uk Awards 2007|publisher=[[British Comedy Guide]]}} < /ref >  and the  " Best Returning British TV Sitcom "  in The Comedy.co.uk Awards 2008, beating ''[[Peep Show (TV series)|Peep Show]]'' by six votes. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.comedy.co.uk/awards/2008/|title=The Comedy.co.uk Awards 2008|publisher=[[British Comedy Guide]]}} < /ref > 
 
 In 2009, it won the [[Royal Television Society]] Award for  " Scripted Comedy " , < ref name= " RTS " > {{cite news|url=http://www.guardian.co.uk/media/2009/mar/18/rts-awards-winners|title=RTS Programme Awards winners 2009 in full|publisher=Guardian|date=2009-03-18|accessdate=2009-03-18 | location=London}} < /ref >  and two [[Broadcasting Press Guild]] Awards in the same year:  " Best Comedy/Entertainment "  and the  " Writer's Award " . < ref > {{cite news|url=http://www.guardian.co.uk/media/2009/mar/27/broadcasting-pres-guild-winners|title=Broadcasting Press Guild award winners|publisher=Guardian|date=2009-03-27|accessdate=2009-03-27 | location=London}} < /ref >  ''Outnumbered'' also won 3 awards at the 2009 [[British Comedy Awards]]: Best Sitcom, Best British Comedy and Best Female Newcomer for [[Ramona Marquez]]. 
 
 At the [[National Television Awards]] in [[16th National Television Awards|2011]], Outnumbered was nominated for Best Comedy but lost out to ''[[Benidorm (TV series)|Benidorm]]''. It was nominated again the [[17th National Television Awards|following year]] and won.
