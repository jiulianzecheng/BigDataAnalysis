 '''InstaShop''' ([[Arabic]]: انستاشوب) is an E-commerce [[technology company]] founded in the [[United Arab Emirates]] in 2015 < ref > {{Cite web|title=Generation Start-up: Instashop eyes global expansion for its grocery delivery app|url=https://www.thenational.ae/business/economy/generation-start-up-instashop-eyes-global-expansion-for-its-grocery-delivery-app-1.979277|access-date=2020-09-09|website=The National|language=en}} < /ref >  currently operating in 9 countries. On August 27, 2020, Delivery Hero announced that  acquired 100% of InstaShop for $360 million. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Berlin's Delivery Hero acquires Dubai on demand delivery app|url=https://www.arabianbusiness.com/transport/451257-berlins-delivery-hero-acquires-dubai-on-demand-delivery-app|access-date=2020-09-09|website=ArabianBusiness.com|language=en}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite web|title=Delivery Hero picks up InstaShop in $360M deal to expand in groceries in the Middle East|url=https://social.techcrunch.com/2020/08/26/on-demand-grocery-startup-instashop-acquired-by-delivery-hero-for-360m/|access-date=2020-09-09|website=TechCrunch|language=en-US}}{{Dead link|date=July 2023 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }} < /ref > 
 
 == History == 
 InstaShop was founded in 2015 in [[Dubai]], by John Tsioris < ref name= " Vinita " > {{Cite web|last=Vinita|date=2019-10-28|title=Redefining Grocery Shopping: An Interview with John Tsioris, Founder  &  CEO of InstaShop|url=https://www.gulf-insider.com/redefining-grocery-shopping-an-interview-with-john-tsioris-founder-ceo-of-instashop/|access-date=2020-09-09|website=Gulf Insider|language=en-US|archive-date=2020-10-23|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20201023214246/https://www.gulf-insider.com/redefining-grocery-shopping-an-interview-with-john-tsioris-founder-ceo-of-instashop/|url-status=dead}} < /ref >  and Ioanna Angelidaki < ref > {{Cite web|last=East|first=Forbes Middle|title=Ioanna Angelidaki, Co-Founder Of InstaShop, Is Riding The Wave Of An E-Commerce Boom|url=https://www.forbesmiddleeast.com/innovation/startups/ioanna-angelidaki-co-founder-of-instashop-is-riding-the-wave-of-an-e-commerce-boom|access-date=2020-09-09|website=Forbes ME|language=en-US}} < /ref >  as an online grocery delivery service. Twelve months later, the company was nominated by [[Forbes]] Middle East as one of  " The most promising UAE startups "  of the year, < ref > {{Cite web|date=2016-10-06|title=Forbes Most Promising UAE Startups In 2016|url=https://arabianmarketer.ae/forbes-promising-uae-startups-2016/|access-date=2020-09-09|website=AM Marketing, Media, Advertising News in MENA|language=en-US}} < /ref >  having partnered with more than 50 supermarkets and having more than 60,000 users, < ref name= " Hariharan " > {{Cite web|last=Hariharan|first=Sindhu|date=2016-10-31|title=UAE-Based Online Grocery Startup InstaShop Raises Funds From Souq.Com|url=https://www.entrepreneur.com/article/284474|access-date=2020-09-09|website=Entrepreneur|language=en}} < /ref >  reason why Souq.com (now [[Amazon (company)|Amazon]]) quickly decided to invest on the project. < ref name= " Hariharan " / > < ref > {{Cite web|last=Robert Anderson|date=2016-10-17|title=Souq.com invests in grocery app InstaShop|url=https://gulfbusiness.com/souq-com-invests-grocery-app-instashop/|access-date=2020-09-09|website=Gulf Business|language=en-US}} < /ref > 
 
 In the following years the company expanded outside the UAE, starting its operations in [[Qatar]] and [[Bahrain]] in 2017, < ref name= " Vinita " / >  to then enter in [[Egypt]] and [[Lebanon]] in 2018. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Lebanese delivery apps eye growth in grocery e-commerce - Executive Magazine|url=https://www.executive-magazine.com/entrepreneurship/e-groceries/lebanese-delivery-apps-eye-growth-in-grocery-e-commerce|access-date=2020-09-09|language=en-US}} < /ref >  Also in 2018, [[Forbes]] Middle East awarded InstaShop as one of the top 100 startups. < ref > {{Cite web|last=East|first=Forbes Middle|title=Top 100 Startups in the Middle East 2018|url=https://www.forbesmiddleeast.com/list/top-100-startups-in-the-middle-east-2018|access-date=2020-09-09|website=Forbes ME|language=en-US}} < /ref > 
 
 In 2019 InstaShop has gone from being a Grocery Delivery App to delivering multiple types of products, reaching more than 500,000 users during the [[Coronavirus disease 2019|COVID-19]] pandemic. 
 < ref > {{Cite web|title=How COVID-19 impacts consumer behavior, leading to a +53% growth of InstaShop|url=https://www.khaleejtimes.com/uae/how-COVID-19-impacts-consumer-behavior-leading-to-a-53-percentage-growth-of-InstaShop|access-date=2020-09-09|website=Khaleej Times|language=en}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite web|title=Covid-19: Instashop sees business surge amid coronavirus pandemic|url=https://www.arabianbusiness.com/technology/444848-covid-19-instashop-sees-business-surge-amid-coronavirus-pandemic|access-date=2020-09-09|website=ArabianBusiness.com|language=en}} < /ref > 
 
 On August 2020 InstaShop was acquired by the German holding Delivery Hero based on a valuation of USD 360 million. < ref > {{Cite web|last=|first=|date=|title=Germany's Delivery Hero acquires grocery app InstaShop for $360m|url=https://capital.com/germany-s-delivery-hero-acquires-grocery-app-instashop-for-360m|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=2020-09-09|website=capital.com}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite news|date=2020-08-27|title=Delivery Hero buys InstaShop to expand in Middle East, North Africa|language=en|work=Reuters|url=https://www.reuters.com/article/us-delivery-hero-m-a-idUSKBN25N0JI|access-date=2020-09-09}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite web|last=Tucker|first=Charlotte|date=2020-08-27|title=Delivery Hero acquires InstaShop - a leading online grocery marketplace in MENA|url=https://www.eu-startups.com/2020/08/delivery-hero-acquires-instashop-a-leading-online-grocery-marketplace-in-mena/|access-date=2020-09-10|website=EU-Startups|language=en-US}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite web|date=2020-08-27|title=Delivery Hero buys Dubai's InstaShop as pandemic revenues surge|url=https://arab.news/47k5n|access-date=2020-09-09|website=Arab News|language=en}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite web|date=2020-08-28|title=InstaShop, a hyperlocal grocery app startup founded in 2015 by two college students, sold to Delivery Hero for a whopping $360 million|url=https://techstartups.com/2020/08/28/instashop-hyperlocal-grocery-app-startup-founded-2015-two-college-students-sold-deliveryhero-whopping-360-million/|access-date=2020-09-09|website=Tech News {{!}} Startups News|language=en-US}} < /ref > 
 
 After the expansions in [[Oman]], [[Kuwait]], and [[Saudi Arabia]] occurred in 2021, the app - which is currently active in 9 countries - has also gradually expanded its offer (originally focused on grocery), adding other categories such as pharmacies, restaurants, fitness nutrition, electronics, perfumes, stationery, home  &  living, and cosmetics.
