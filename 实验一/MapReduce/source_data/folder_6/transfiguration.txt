The '''Transfiguration''' is an event in the life of [[Jesus Christ]] described in the [[Gospel of Luke]], [[Gospel of Matthew]], and [[Gospel of Mark]]. The [[disciple]]s go up a mountain with Jesus. He begins to shine, and meets with [[Moses]] and [[Elijah]]. A voice from a cloud says,  " this is my beloved Son: hear him "  (Luke 9:35).
