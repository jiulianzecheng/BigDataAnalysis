 The genus '''''Sorex''''' includes many of the common shrews of [[Eurasia]] and [[North America]]. It contains at least 142 known [[species]] and [[subspecies]]. They are in the tribe '''Soricini''' of the subfamily ''Soricinae''. < ref > {{Cite book|url=https://www.worldcat.org/oclc/57557352|title=Mammal species of the world : a taxonomic and geographic reference|date=2005|publisher=Johns Hopkins University Press|others=Wilson, Don E., Reeder, DeeAnn M.|isbn=0-8018-8221-4|edition=3rd|location=Baltimore|oclc=57557352}} < /ref >
