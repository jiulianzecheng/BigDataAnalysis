 '''Dongeradeel''' ({{lang-fy|Dongeradiel}}) is a former [[List of municipalities of the Netherlands|municipality]] in the [[Netherlands|Dutch]] [[Provinces of the Netherlands|province]] of [[Friesland]]. Since 2019, it has been part of the new municipality of [[Noardeast-Fryslân]].
