 '''Basauri''' is a [[Municipalities of Spain|municipality]] of 40,535 people (2021). It is in the [[Provinces of Spain|province]] of [[Biscay]] in the [[Autonomous communities of Spain|autonomous community]] of [[Basque Country (autonomous community)|Basque Country]] in [[Spain]]. 
 
 The city is part of the [[Bilbao]] [[Greater Bilbao|metropolitan area]]. It is where the rivers [[Nervión]] and [[Ibaizabal]] meet.
