 '''Solingen''' is a city in [[Germany]], in the [[States of Germany|state]] of [[North Rhine-Westphalia]]. It is near [[Düsseldorf]] and [[Wuppertal]]. 
 
 Solingen has about 165,000 [[inhabitant]]s. 
 
 In 1993, five [[Turks]] [[Solingen arson attack|were killed]] in an [[arson]] attack.
