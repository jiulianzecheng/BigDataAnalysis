 '''''Elles''''' is a 2011 erotic [[France|French]] drama movie. [[Juliette Binoche]] plays a journalist named Anne. ''Elles'' is about female student prostitution. This movie had an [[NC-17 rating]] because of explicit sexual content. < ref > {{cite web|title=Elles|publisher=The New York Times|url=http://movies.nytimes.com/2012/04/27/movies/elles-with-juliette-binoche-about-prostitution.html|accessdate=December 15, 2012|archive-date=June 24, 2012|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120624060714/http://movies.nytimes.com/2012/04/27/movies/elles-with-juliette-binoche-about-prostitution.html|url-status=dead}} < /ref >  The NC-17 rating was later surrendered. The movie was released unrated on [[DVD]] and [[Blu-ray]] disc.
