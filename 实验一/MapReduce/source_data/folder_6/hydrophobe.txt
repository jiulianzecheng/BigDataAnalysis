 '''Hydrophobe''' is a term from [[chemistry]] for a [[Chemical substance|substance]] that does not [[Mixture|mix]] with [[water]]. Examples of such substances are different types of [[Vegetable oil|vegetable oils]], and [[fat]]s. Many hydrophobic substances are [[Lipophile|lipophiles]], they  mix with fats and oils easily.
