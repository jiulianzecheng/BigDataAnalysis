 '''Sprite''' is a lemon-lime [[Soft drink|soda]], similar to [[7 Up]] and [[Starry (drink)|Starry]]. It is made by the [[Coca-Cola|Coca-Cola Company]]. It has no [[caffeine]]. In 2022, its logo was changed. 
 
 ==Variants== 
 There are many variants of Sprite. According to the Coca-Cola website, currently sold Sprite variants include: 
 * Sprite, lemon-lime carbonated drink with no caffeine 
 * Sprite Zero, the no calorie and no sugar added version of Sprite 
 * Sprite Cherry, Sprite with a hint of cherry. 
 * Sprite Lymonade, Sprite combined with lemonade flavor. 
 * Sprite Tropical Mix, with strawberry and pineapple flavors along with the original Sprite lemon-lime flavoring. Similar to former flavor Sprite Remix. 
 * Sprite Winter Spiced Cranberry, cranberry and lemon-lime flavored Sprite sold only during the holiday season. Formerly known as Sprite Cranberry from 2013 to 2018. 
 There are also flavors exclusive to [[Coca-Cola|Coca-Cola Freestyle]] machines not listed here.
