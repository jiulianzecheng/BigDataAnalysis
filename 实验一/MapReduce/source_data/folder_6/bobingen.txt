 '''Bobingen''' ([[Swabian German|Swabian]]: ''Boobenge'' < ref > [[:gsw:Bobingen|Bobingen]]{{Circular reference|date=March 2019}} < /ref > ) is a [[Town#Germany|town]] in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. It is on the rivers [[Wertach (river)|Wertach]] and [[Singold]], on the side of the Augsburg-Westliche Wälder Nature Park, in [[Augsburg (district)|Augsburg District]], some 13 & nbsp;km south of [[Augsburg]] itself.
