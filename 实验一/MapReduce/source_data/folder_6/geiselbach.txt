 '''Geiselbach''' is a [[community]] in the [[Aschaffenburg (district)|Aschaffenburg district]] in the ''[[Regierungsbezirk]]'' of [[Lower Franconia]] (''Unterfranken'') in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 
 ===Location=== 
 [[File:Gemeinde Geiselbach.svg|thumb|Constituent communities]] 
 Geiselbach is on the [[Hesse]]-Bavaria boundary, 20 & nbsp;km north of [[Aschaffenburg]] and 50 & nbsp;km east of [[Frankfurt|Frankfurt am Main]]. 
 
 Elevations in the community reach from 272 & nbsp;m above [[sea level]] at the church up to 381 & nbsp;m at the Ziegelberg. 
 
 The municipal area comprises 535 & nbsp;ha in the constituent community of Geiselbach and 415 & nbsp;ha in the constituent community of Omersbach.
