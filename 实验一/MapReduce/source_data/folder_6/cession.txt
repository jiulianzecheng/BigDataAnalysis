The act of '''cession''' is the transfer of [[property]] to another entity (person, country, etc.).  In [[international law]], it commonly refers to [[land borders|land]] transferred by a [[treaty]]. It is different from [[annexation]], where property is taken by force. Cession is voluntary or is supposed to be. 
 
 ==Retrocession== 
 {{Wiktionary|retrocession}} 
 '''Retrocession''' is to return something (e.g., land) that was [[cession|ceded]].
