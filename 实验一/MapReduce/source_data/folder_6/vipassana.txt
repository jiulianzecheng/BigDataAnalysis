'''''Vipassanā''''' (Pāli) or '''''vipaśyanā''''' (Sanskrit) is a [[Buddhism|Buddhist]] word that is usually [[Translation|translated]] as  " insight " . It is a form of meditation which aims to have  " insight into the true nature of reality.
