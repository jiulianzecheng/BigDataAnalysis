 In [[Aztec mythology]], '''Xoːchiquetzal''' ({{IPA2|ʃotʃi'ketsal}}) was a [[deity|goddess]] of [[flower]]s, [[fertility]], [[games]], [[dance|dancing]] and [[agriculture]] (planting and harvesting), as well as [[craft]]smen and [[Pregnancy|pregnant]] women.  She was first a [[moon]] and [[love]] goddess.
