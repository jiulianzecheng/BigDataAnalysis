 '''Kottwil''' is a village and former municipality of the [[district]] of [[Willisau (district)|Willisau]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Lucerne (canton)|Lucerne]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 Since 2006, it is part of the municipality of [[Ettiswil]].
