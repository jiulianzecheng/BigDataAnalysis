 '''Fossano''' ({{lang-pms|Fossan}}) is an [[Italy|Italian]] city in [[Piedmont]]. It is the fourth-largest city of the [[Province of Cuneo]], after [[Cuneo]], [[Alba, Piedmont|Alba]] and [[Bra, Piedmont|Bra]]. About 24,400 people live there.
