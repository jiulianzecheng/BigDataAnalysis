 A '''relay''' is an [[electromagnet]]ically operated [[switch]]. It was invented in the 19th century to make electric [[telegraph]] signals go farther. Nowadays relays control circuits with low-voltages. Many machines use them, including [[vehicle]]s and [[electronics]].
