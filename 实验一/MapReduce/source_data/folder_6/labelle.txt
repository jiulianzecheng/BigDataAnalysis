 '''Labelle''' is an American all-female singing group who were a popular vocal group of the 1960s and 1970s. The founding members were [[Patti LaBelle]] (formerly Patricia Holt), [[Cindy Birdsong]], [[Nona Hendryx]], and [[Sarah Dash]].
