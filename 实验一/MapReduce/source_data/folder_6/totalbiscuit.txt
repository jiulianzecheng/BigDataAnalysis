 '''John Peter Bain''' (8 July 1984 – 24 May 2018), commonly known by his online aliases '''TotalBiscuit''', '''The Cynical Brit''', and '''TotalHalibut''', was a British [[Indie game|indie gaming]] commentator and [[game critic]] on [[YouTube]]. He was born in [[Spennymoor]], [[County Durham]]. 
 
 Bain was known for his role in professional [[Sports commentator|casting]] for games such as ''[[StarCraft II]]'' and ''[[PlanetSide 2]]'', as well as for his regular gaming commentary videos. Bain voiced strong support for consumer protection in the gaming industry.
