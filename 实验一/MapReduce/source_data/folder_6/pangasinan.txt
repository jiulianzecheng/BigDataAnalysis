 '''Pangasinan''', officially the '''Province of Pangasinan''' ({{lang-pag|Luyag/Probinsia na Pangasinan}}; {{lang-ilo|Probinsia ti Pangasinan}}; {{lang-tl|Lalawigan ng Pangasinan}}), is a [[Provinces of the Philippines|province]] in the [[Philippines]] in the [[Ilocos Region]] of [[Luzon]]. Its capital is [[Lingayen, Pangasinan|Lingayen]]. It has a total land area of {{convert|5451.01|km2}}. According to the 2020 census, 3,163,190 people lived there.
