 '''Vulpecula''' is a northern [[constellation]] (group of [[star]]s). People say that it looks like a [[fox]]. It is not very [[Apparent magnitude|bright]].  None of the stars in Vulpecula are brighter than the 4th [[Apparent magnitude|magnitude]] in Vulpecula.  It is in the middle of the [[Summer Triangle]], three stars that make the shape of a triangle in the sky.
