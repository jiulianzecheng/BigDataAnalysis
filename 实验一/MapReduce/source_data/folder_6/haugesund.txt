 '''Haugesund''' is a town within the Municipality of [[Haugesund (municipality)|Haugesund]], and is the capital of the municipality. It is within the [[Rogaland]] [[county]] in the country of [[Norway]]. It had a population of roughly 32,000 (42,000 including all of the [[Suburb|suburbs]]) < ref name= " ssb pop " > {{Cite web|last=Statistisk sentralbyrå|author-link=Statistics Norway|year=2020|title=Table: 06913: Population 1 January and population changes during the calendar year (M)|url=https://www.ssb.no/en/statbank/table/06913/|language=no}} < /ref >  as of January 2020. The  " birthplace of Norway " , as it is called, [[Avaldsnes]], is approximately 8.5km away from the town. The town is named after the nearby Haugesundet [[strait]] which is close to the town. 
 
 The residents of the town are nicknamed  " ''Måke'' "  (Seagulls), especially those who live in the east side of the town and support the local [[Association football|soccer]] team. 
 
 == Tourist attractions == 
 Here is a list of tourist attractions which can be found within the town: 
 
 • (Natural attraction) The Haraldsvang hiking area. It is quite close to the Skelsvannet lake.  
 
 • (Natural attraction) The Skelsvannet lake is good for fishing, and you could be able to find some [[fish]] that are from the region. It would also be good for [[birdwatching]], mostly [[Gull|seagulls]] which are commonly found in Haugesund. 
 
 • There is a North Sea cycling route which runs through, which would be good for [[Bicycle|cyclists]] 
 
 • Haugesund also has an [[airport]] (HAU) for people who like planespotting. 
 
 • Haugesund also hosts the [[Norwegian International Film Festival]], and the  " ''Slidajazz " '' festival < ref > {{Cite web|title=Den Norske Filmfestivalen i Haugesund|url=https://filmfestivalen.no/|access-date=2020-12-29|website=Den Norske Filmfestivalen i Haugesund}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite web|title=Sildajazz 2020|url=https://sildajazz.no/|access-date=2020-12-29|website=sildajazz.no|language=no}} < /ref >  in August every year. 
 
 == Sports in Haugesund == 
 Haugesund is home to the [[Association football|soccer]] club [[Haugesund FK|Haugesund FK (FKH)]], who currently play in the [[Eliteserien]], the highest tier in Norwegian soccer (20/21 season). 
 
 == Municipal council == 
 The [[Municipal council (Norway)|municipal council]] ({{lang|no|Kommunestyre}}) of Haugesund is made up of 49 [[Representative|representatives]] that are elected every four years. The government is located at the Haugesund City Hall.
