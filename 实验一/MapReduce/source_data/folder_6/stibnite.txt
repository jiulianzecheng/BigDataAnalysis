 '''Stibnite''' is a [[mineral]]. Its chemical formula is [[antimony trisulfide|Sb < sub > 2 < /sub > S < sub > 3 < /sub > ]]. It is the main [[ore]] of [[antimony]]. The [[American Museum of Natural History]] has a huge crystal of stibnite. Stibnite is soft, with a [[Mohs hardness]] of 2. Its [[specific gravity]] is 4.63.
