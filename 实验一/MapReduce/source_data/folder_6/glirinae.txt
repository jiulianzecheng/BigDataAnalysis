 '''Glirinae''' is a [[subfamily]] of [[dormice]] (''Gliridae''). 
 
 It contains two [[genera]], ''Glirulus'' and ''Glis''. These genera have one [[species]] each: ''[[Glirulus japonicus]]'' (the Japanese dormouse) and ''[[Glis glis]]'' (the edible dormouse).
