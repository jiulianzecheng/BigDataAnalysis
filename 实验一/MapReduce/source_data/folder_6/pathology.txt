 '''Pathology''' is the study and medical [[diagnosis]] of [[disease]], especially after death. A person who does this work is called a pathologist. Techniques include [[dissection]], [[microscopic]] examination of [[tissue]]s and [[chemical analysis]] of [[fluid]]s. A pathologist may perform an [[autopsy]], to discover the [[cause of death]].  
 
 Pathology is a [[science]] that concerns with finding the cause (etiology) or presence of a disease by careful examination of a tissue or sample of any component of the body by scrutinizing it. 
 
 Pathologists often work with biochemists in hospital laboratories.
