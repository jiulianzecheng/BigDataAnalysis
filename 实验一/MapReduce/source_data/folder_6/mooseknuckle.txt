 A '''mooseknuckle''' or '''manbulge''' is [[slang]] for when the outline or bulge of a man's [[genitalia]] ([[Human penis|penis]] and [[testicles]]) is visible through clothing. This is normal with form-fitting (tight) [[Swimsuit|swimwear]] worn by competitive swimmers and [[Diving|divers]], and often with cycling shorts (biker shorts) worn by cyclists. A slang word for when the mooseknuckle or manbulge seems [[Erection|erect]] or pointy includes ''trouser tent''. Other slang words for the mooseknuckle or manbulge includes ''dick-print''.
