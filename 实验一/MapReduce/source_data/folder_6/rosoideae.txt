 '''Rosoideae''' is a [[subfamily]] of [[plant]]s in the [[rose]] [[family (biology)| family]] [[Rosaceae]]. These plants may be small [[tree]]s, [[shrub]]s, or plants without [[wood]]. Many useful plants are in Rosoideae. [[Strawberry|Strawberries]] and [[Raspberry|Raspberries]] are two common [[fruit]]s from Rosoideae. [[Rose]]s are common flowers in Rosoideae.
