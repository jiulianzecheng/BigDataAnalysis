 '''Gadheim''' is a village in [[Lower Franconia]], in northern [[Bavaria]] belonging to the municipality of [[Veitshöchheim]]. It is about 10 kilometers (6.2 miles) north of [[Würzburg]]. As of [[Brexit|31 January 2020]], Gadheim is the site of the [[Geographical midpoint of Europe|geodetic center of the European Union]]. < ref > {{Cite news|url=https://www.theguardian.com/world/2017/apr/06/gadheim-the-bavarian-hamlet-at-the-centre-of-post-brexit-eu|title=Gadheim – the Bavarian hamlet set to become the centre of a post-Brexit EU|last=Connolly|first=Kate|date=2017-04-06|work=The Guardian|access-date=2020-02-03|language=en-GB|issn=0261-3077}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite web|url=https://www.br.de/nachrichten/bayern/gadheim-wird-neuer-eu-mittelpunkt,Rp4xEoK|title=Gadheim ist neuer EU-Mittelpunkt|date=2020-01-31|website=BR24|language=de|access-date=2020-02-03}} < /ref >
