 '''Enix''' was a [[Japan]]ese company which published [[anime]], [[manga]], and [[Video game|video games]], before merging with [[SquareSoft|Square Co., Ltd.]] to form [[Square Enix]]. Their last game was ''[[Dragon Quest Monsters: Caravan Heart]]'', for their most popular ''[[Dragon Quest]]'' series of [[Role-playing game|role playing games]] which was started on the [[Nintendo Entertainment System|Famicom]]. In 2000, they setup a subsidiary to handle fingerprint identification.
