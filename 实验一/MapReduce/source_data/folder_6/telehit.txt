 '''TeleHit''' is a Mexican cable/satellite [[television network]]. It has been broadcasting since 1993. Its main programming is music and music videos. It is a part of [[Televisa Networks]], an affiliate of [[TelevisaUnivision (Mexico)|TelevisaUnivision Mexico]], and is also available in various countries in [[Latin America]] and [[United States]]. 
 
 Telehit is [[MTV Latin America|MTV]]'s second [[Spanish language]] music video network. At first, it aired Spanish music. Now it airs a fusion of different musical [[rhythms]], like pop, rock, electronic, [[Hip hop music|hip-hop]] and [[reggaeton]]. 
 
 Besides music videos, the channel also broadcasts concerts, TV specials and general-interest content for youth. Telehit has also been a platform for hosts and actors to being into Mexican show business.
