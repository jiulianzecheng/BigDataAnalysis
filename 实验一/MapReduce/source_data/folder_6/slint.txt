 '''Slint''' was an American [[Rock music|rock]] band. It was made in 1986. It was made in [[Louisville, Kentucky]]. The band's members were Brian McMahan ([[guitar]], singing), David Pajo (guitar), Britt Walford (drums, singing), and Todd Brashear ([[bass guitar]]). The band's first album was released in 1989. It is named ''Tweez''. Two years later, the band released ''[[Spiderland]]''.
