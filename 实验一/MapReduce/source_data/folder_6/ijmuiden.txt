 '''IJmuiden''' is a [[port city]] in the [[Netherlands|Dutch]] province of [[North Holland]]. It is the main town in the municipality of [[Velsen]]. 
 
 DFDS Seaways serves a route between IJmuiden and [[Newcastle Upon Tyne]]. < ref >  https://www.dfds.com/nl-nl/overtochten-passagiers/minicruises/engeland/minicruise-naar-newcastle < /ref >

 == Climate == 
 IJmuiden has an [[oceanic climate]].  
 
 The highest temperature ever recorded in IJmuiden was {{cvt|37.3|C|F}} on 19 July 2022. 
 
 The lowest temperature ever recorded in IJmuiden was {{cvt|-16.0|C|F}} on 4 February 2012. 
 
 The highest wind gust ever recorded in IJmuiden was {{cvt|173|km/h|mph}} on 3 January 2012. 
 
 The highest 24-hour precipitation sum in IJmuiden was 96.6 millimeters on 28 July 2014.
