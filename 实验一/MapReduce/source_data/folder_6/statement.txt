'''Statement''' has several meanings: 
 
 * The [[wikt:act|act]] of saying something; an [[wikt:expression|expression]] in [[words]]. 
 * The thing stated; a [[declaration]]. 
 * A formal account, [[imply]]ing or [[wikt:claim|claiming]] to be [[truth]]ful. 
 * An official [[wikt:assessment|assessment]] of a [[wikt:situation|situation]]. 
 *In [[philosophy]] and [[logic]], a statement is a [[sentence]] which declares that something meaningful is either [[true]] or [[false]]. See [[proposition]].
