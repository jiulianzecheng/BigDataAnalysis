'''RNE''' (Spanish:  " ''Radio Nacional de España'' " ) (English:  " ''Spain's National Radio'' " ) is a Spanish public radio [[broadcaster]]. Since 1973 it's integrated on the RTVE group. Now, RNE is made up of 6 radio channels: < ref name=Wiki > {{cite web|url=http://www.rtve.es/radio/|publisher=RNE|title=Radio Nacional de España |time=2011 |accessdate=2011-10-09}} < /ref > 
 * ''Radio Nacional'' (national radio) - a general radio channel. 
 * ''Radio clásica'' (classical radio) - plays classical music 
 * ''Radio 3'' - alternative music and culture 
 * ''Ràdio 4'' - generalist radio channel on Catalan 
 * ''Radio 5'' - information radio channel 
 * ''Radio exterior'' (exterior radio): outside Spain's radio service
