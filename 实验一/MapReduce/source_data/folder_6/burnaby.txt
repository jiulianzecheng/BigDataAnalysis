 '''Burnaby''' is a [[city]] in the [[Canada|Canadian]] [[Provinces and territories of Canada|province]] of [[British Columbia]]. The population of Burnaby was 126,456 in 2011. < ref name=census > {{cite web|url=http://www.burnaby.ca/About-Burnaby/About/Population---Quick-Stats/2011-Census-Data.html|access-date=February 10, 2014|title=2011 Census Data|publisher=City of Burnaby|archive-date=April 7, 2014|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140407131512/http://www.burnaby.ca/About-Burnaby/About/Population---Quick-Stats/2011-Census-Data.html|url-status=dead}} < /ref >  The mayor of Burnaby is Derek Corrigan. 
 
 The city was named after [[Robert Burnaby]], a legislator, speaker, Freemason and explorer. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://apps.gov.bc.ca/pub/bcgnws/names/2210.html|access-date=February 10, 2014|title=Burnaby: Origin Notes and History|publisher=GeoBC}} < /ref >  The largest commercial mall in British Columbia, is [[Metropolis at Metrotown]] is located in Burnaby. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://metropolisatmetrotown.com/mall-info|access-date=February 10, 2014|title=Mall Information|publisher=Metropolis at Metrotown|archive-date=January 3, 2014|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140103130347/http://metropolisatmetrotown.com/mall-info|url-status=dead}} < /ref >
