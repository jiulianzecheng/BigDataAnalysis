 '''Kolomna''' ({{lang-ru|Коломна}}) is a city in [[Moscow Oblast]], [[Russia]]. It is at the confluence of the [[Moskva River|Moskva]] and [[Oka River]]s. In 2010, 144,589 people lived there.
