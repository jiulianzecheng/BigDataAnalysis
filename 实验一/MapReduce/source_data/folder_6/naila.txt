 '''Naila''' is a town in the [[Frankenwald]] hills, in [[Hof (district)|Hof]], [[Bavaria]]. Naila is {{cvt|18|km}} from [[Hof, Germany|Hof]], on both banks of the [[Selbitz (river)|Selbitz]] river.
