 ==History== 
 
 PROSOCO was created in 1937. It was first called the '''Process Solvent Company'''. It was first a company in [[Kansas City, Kansas|Kansas City]] that made special cleaning products and [[engine]] products for the car and [[railroad]] industries. Because the [[construction]] industry grew in the 1950s, Process Solvent Company starting making construction and building maintenance products. In 1967, the Process Solvent Company filed to trademark  " PSC " , which made it shorter to call the company. < ref > {{cite news | url=http://www.trademarkia.com/the-process-solvent-co-inc--psc-72279857.html/ | title=PSC Company Trademark | work=Trademarkia | first=Legal | last=Force |date=September 7, 1967 | access-date=April 7, 2015}} < /ref >
