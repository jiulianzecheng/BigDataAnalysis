 '''Tobias''' is a [[Greece|Greek]] form of the Hebrew name  " ''Tobijah'' "  from the Bible, meaning  " ''Jehovah [God] is good'' " .  < ref > {{Cite web|title=Tobias name Meaning and Origin|url=https://babys.name/detail-Tobias-Meaning-Origin-Famous-Similar-Sibling.html|access-date=2023-03-26|website=babys.name}} < /ref > It is often used as a male [[given name]] in [[Germany]], [[Scandinavia]], the [[United States]] and among [[Jewish]] people. In countries that speak English it is often shortened to [[Toby]]. Other short forms are Tobi (English, Germany) and Tobbe (Sweden). It has also been a [[surname]] (last name). The pronunciation of the name is [ t''uh''-'''bahy'''-''uh''s ].  < ref > {{Cite web|title=Definition of Tobias {{!}} Dictionary.com|url=https://www.dictionary.com/browse/tobias|access-date=2023-03-26|website=www.dictionary.com|language=en}} < /ref >  This name was ranked #277 on the US Popular Names in 2021.  < ref > {{Cite web|last=OACT|title=Popular Baby Names|url=https://www.ssa.gov/cgi-bin/popularnames.cgi|access-date=2023-03-26|website=www.ssa.gov}} < /ref > 
 
 ==Biblical== 
 Several people are called Tobias in [[The Bible]]: 
 *Tobias, son of Tobit;  " Book of Tobias "  is an older name for the [[Book of Tobit]] 
 *Tobias the elder 
 *Tobias, father of Hircanus 
 *[[Tobiah (Ammonite)]] 
 *Tobias, brother-in-law of [[Onias II]], father of the Joseph who defeated Ptolemy III Euergetes. 
 *Tobbe, son-in-law of [[Onias III]], son of Onias II
