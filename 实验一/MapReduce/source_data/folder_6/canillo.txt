 '''Canillo''' is one of the seven [[Parishes of Andorra|parishes]] of [[Andorra]]. It is in the northeast of the country. Canillo is also the name of the main town of the parish. 
 
 The town of Canillo is the [[Religion|religious]] centre of Andorra because here is the ''Mare de Déu de Meritxell'' ( " Our Lady of Meritxell " ) [[Basilica]]. Our Lady of Meritxell is the [[patron saint]] of Andorra. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 The Canillo parish is in the northeastern part of the country and borders with the [[Ariège (department)|Ariège]] [[Departments of France|department]] to the north and east, with the [[Pyrénées-Orientales]] department ([[France]]) to the southeast, with the [[Encamp]] parish to the south and the [[Ordino]] parish to the west.

 The highest mountain in the parish is ''Pic de l'Estanyó'' ({{Coord|42|36|31|N|1|35|33|E|name=Pic de l'Estanyó|display=inline|region:AD_type:mountain}}) with an elevation of {{Convert|2915|m|ft|abbr=on }} [[above sea level]]; it is on the border with the [[Ordino]] parish. < ref name=pbag > {{cite web |url=http://www.peakbagger.com/peak.aspx?pid=9818|title=Pic de Pessons, Andorra |publisher=Peakbagger.com |access-date=28 December 2016 }} < /ref > 
 
 The main river in Canillo is the ''Valira d'Orient'', also known as ''Valira d’Encamp''. 
 
 The largest lake of Andorra, ''Estany de Juclà'' ( " Juclar lake " ), is in the Canillo parish; it has an area of {{Convert|0.21|km2|sqmi|2|abbr=on }}. 
 
 ==Population== 
 The parish had a population, in 2015,  of 4,124 inhabitants, < ref name= " estadistica "  / >  for a [[population density]] of {{Pop density|4124|121|km2|prec=1|disp=num}} persons/km².

 ==Economy== 
 The main [[Economy|economic]] activity is [[tourism]] and the parish is a major tourism destination during winter. 
 
 ==Sites of interest== 
 * '''The new Our Lady of Meritxell Basilica''' in Meritxell. 
 * '''Sant Joan de Caselles''' church. It is similar to the church in Mérens-les-Vals, [[Ariège (department)|Ariège]], [[France]]. 
 * '''Valley of the Incles river''' with the ''Estany de Juclà'' ( " Juclar lake " ), the largest [[lake]] of Andorra. 
 * '''Soldeu-El Tarter''' [[ski resort]], part of the Grandvalira ski resort.
