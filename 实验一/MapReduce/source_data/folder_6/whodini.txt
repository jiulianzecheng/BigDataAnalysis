 '''Whodini''' is an American [[hip hop music|hip hop]] group that was formed in 1982. < ref name= " Tortorici " / >  It was founded in [[Brooklyn]], [[New York (state)|New York]]-based trio. Members of the group were vocalist and main lyricist Jalil Hutchins; co-vocalist John Fletcher, a.k.a. Ecstasy (who wore a [[Zorro]]-style hat as his trademark); and [[turntablism|turntable]] artist DJ Drew Carter, a.k.a.
