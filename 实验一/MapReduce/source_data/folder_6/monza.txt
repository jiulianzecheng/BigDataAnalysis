 '''Monza''' is a [[city]] in the [[region]] of [[Lombardy]]. In 2009 it will become the [[Capital (city)|capital]] of the ''[[Province of Monza and Brianza]]''. About 120,000 people live in Monza. This makes it the third largest city in Lombardy.
