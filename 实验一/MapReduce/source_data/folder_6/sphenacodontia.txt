 '''Sphenacodontia''' is a [[suborder]] of the [[Pelycosaur|Pelycosaurs]]. Sphenacodonts include [[Sphenacodontoid|Sphenacodontoids]], [[Varanopid|Varanopids]] and [[Ophiacodontid|Ophiacodontids]]. Sphenacodontia's sister taxon is [[Caseasaur|Caseasauria]].
