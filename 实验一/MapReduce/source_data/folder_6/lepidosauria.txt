 The '''Lepidosauria''' (Greek for ''scaled lizards'') are [[reptile]]s with overlapping [[Scale (zoology)|scales]]. They include the [[tuatara]]s, [[lizard]]s, [[snake]]s and [[amphisbaenia]]ns. 
 
 Lepidosauria is a superorder of [[Sauropsida]]. It includes the orders :  
 *  [[Squamata]] 
 *  [[Sphenodontia]] / [[Rhynchocephalia]]
