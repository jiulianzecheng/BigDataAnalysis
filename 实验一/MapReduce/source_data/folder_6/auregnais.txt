'''Auregnais''', '''Aoeur'gnaeux''' or '''Aurignais''' was the [[Norman language|Norman]] dialect of the [[Channel Islands|island]] of [[Alderney]]. It became extinct in the mid-20th century. People think it became extinct because people did not bother to speak it nor learn it.
