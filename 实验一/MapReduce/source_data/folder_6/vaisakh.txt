 '''Vaisakh''' ([[Gurmukhi alphabet|Gurmukhi]]: ਵੈਸਾਖ, [[Shahmukhi]]: ویساکھ) is the first month of the [[Punjabi calendar]] and the second month in the [[Nanakshahi calendar]]. This month is usually in [[April]] and [[May]] in the [[Gregorian calendar]]. It is the same as Vaisakha month in the [[Hindu calendar]].
