'''Lavenay''' is a former [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is found in the [[Regions of France|region]] [[Pays de la Loire]] in the [[Sarthe]] [[Departments of France|department]] in the west of [[France]]. On 1 January 2017, it was merged into the new commune of [[Loir en Vallée]]. < ref > [https://www.legifrance.gouv.fr/eli/arrete/2016/12/16/INTB1700248A/jo/texte Arrêté préfectoral] 16 December 2016 {{in lang|fr}} < /ref >
