 '''Cremona''' is a [[city]] in northern [[Italy]]. Cremona is in the [[Lombardy]] Region with a population of 71,533 inhabitants. It is in the [[Po valley|Po Valley]] on the [[North]] side of the [[Po (river)|Po River]]. < ref > {{Cite web|url=https://www.google.com/maps/place/26100+Cremona,+Province+of+Cremona,+Italy/@45.1443029,9.9893671,10.9z/data=!4m5!3m4!1s0x4780fe6d3c71fe83:0x307737e7e74bdaf5!8m2!3d45.133249!4d10.0226511|title=Google Maps|website=Google Maps|accessdate=2017-11-08}} < /ref > 
 
 == History == 
 In the year 218 BCE, [[Roman]]s founded the city. Before that, it was a village of the [[Gaul]]s. < ref > {{Cite news|url=https://www.britannica.com/place/Cremona-Italy|title=Cremona {{!}} Italy|newspaper=Encyclopedia Britannica|access-date=2017-11-08|language=en}} < /ref >
