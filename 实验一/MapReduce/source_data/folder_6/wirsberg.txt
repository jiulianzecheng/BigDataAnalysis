 '''Wirsberg''' is a [[Municipalities of Germany|municipality]] in [[Kulmbach (district)|Kulmbach]] in [[Bavaria]] in [[Germany]]. 
 
 ==City arrangement== 
 
 Wirsberg is arranged in the following boroughs: 
 * Birkenhof 
 * Cottenau 
 * Einöde 
 * Goldene Adlerhütte 
 * Neufang 
 * Osserich 
 * Schlackenmühle 
 * Sessenreuth 
 * Weißenbach 
 * Wirsberg
