 '''Tamahere''' is a town in the [[Waikato District]], [[New Zealand]] and is located on the outskirts of [[Hamilton, New Zealand|Hamilton]]. It has three primary schools located in the town. On 5 April 2008, the Tamahere coolstore destroyed in a major blaze and killed a firefighter in the incident. State Highway 1 and State Highway 21 are located within the town.
