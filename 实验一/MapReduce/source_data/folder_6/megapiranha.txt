'''''Megapiranha''''' is an extinct [[Serrasalmidae|serrasalmid]] fish described in [[2009]]. The name is a combination of the word  " [[mega]] "  and [[piranha]]. This freshwater fish is called '''megapiranha''', '''giant serrasalmid''', or '''[[Piranha|cutting fish]]''' by people in [[Country|other countries]]. 
 
 == Description == 
 ''Megapiranha'' was a giant [[piranha]] having the teeth of a modern [[serrasalmid]]. Megapiranhas can [[bite]] you if you see them in real life. ''Megapiranha'' has one [[species]] called '''''Megapiranha paranensis'''''.
