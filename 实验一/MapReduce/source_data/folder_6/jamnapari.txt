 '''Jamnapari''' or '''Jamunapari''' is an Indian [[list of goat breeds|breed]] of [[domestic goat]] originating in [[Northern India]] of the [[Gangetic Plains of India]]. They are considered as the ''“National Goat of India”'' respectively.
