 '''''Sirdavidia''''' is a [[genus]] of  plant of the family [[Annonaceae]]. The genus only has one species, '''''Sirdavidia solanonna'''''. 
 
 This rare plant from [[Gabon]] attracts [[bees]]. Its name comes from [[David Attenborough]], a [[BBC]] journalist and author of many naturalist documentaries.
