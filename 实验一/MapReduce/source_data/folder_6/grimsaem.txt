 '''Grimsaem Animation Co. Ltd., Seoul, Korea''' ({{lang|ko|(주)그림샘 애니메이션}}) was an [[animation studio]] located in [[South Korea]]. It is a division of [[Sunwoo Entertainment]]. Grimsaem creates feature-length work for [[Nickelodeon]] and Wild Brain. They also worked on the Fox TV series ''[[Family Guy]]'', animating the full first and third seasons of the show.
