 '''Zuydcoote'''{{Needs IPA}} ([[West Flemish]] and {{lang-nl|Zuidkote}}) is a [[Communes in France|commune]] in [[Nord (department)|Nord]] in north [[France]]. In 2018, 1,616 people lived there.
