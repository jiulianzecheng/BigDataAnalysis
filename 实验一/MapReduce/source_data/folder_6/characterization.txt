'''Characterization''' is the [[idea]] of making [[Fictional character|character]]s for a [[narrative]]. < ref > Harrison, Martin. 1998. ''The Language of Theatre''. London: Routledge. {{ISBN|0-87830-087-2}}.(pp 51-2) < /ref >  It is a [[literary element]] and may be used in [[drama]]tic [[The arts|works of art]] or everyday [[conversation]]. Characters can be shown by description. They can also be shown through their [[speech]], thoughts, or actions.
