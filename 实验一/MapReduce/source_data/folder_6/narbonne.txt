 '''Narbonne''' ({{lang-oc|Narbona}}) is a [[France|French]] [[Communes of France|commune]], a ''[[Prefectures in France|subprefecture]]'' in the [[Aude]] [[Departments of France|department]]. It is in the [[Occitanie]] [[Regions of France|region]]. It is also the [[Capital city|capital]] of the ''arrondissement'' of [[Arrondissement of Narbonne|Narbonne]]. 
 
 It was a wealthy [[port]] and major city in [[Roman Empire|Roman times]] but now is at about {{Convert|15|km|mi|abbr=on}} from the [[shore]]s of the [[Mediterranean Sea]].

 ===Climate=== 
 The climate of Narbonne, in the [[Köppen climate classification]], is '''Csa''' - [[Mediterranean climate]] with hot summers. < ref > {{cite web |work=Annuaire-Mairie.fr |publisher= |title=La ville de Narbonne |url=http://www.annuaire-mairie.fr/ville-narbonne.html |language=French |accessdate=21 March 2017}} < /ref > 
 
 ==Population== 
 The inhabitants of this city are known, in [[French language|French]], as ''Narbonnais'' (women: ''Narbonnaises''). < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.habitants.fr/habitants_departement_aude_11.html |title=Aude (11) |publisher=habitants.fr |language=French |accessdate=16 July 2015 |date= }} < /ref > 
 
 With a population of 52,855, < ref name=insee > {{cite web |url=http://www.insee.fr/fr/statistiques/fichier/2525755/dep11.pdf |title=Régions, départements, arrondissements, cantons et communes  |work= Populations légales 2014 |publisher=Institut national de la statistique et des études économiques - INSEE |format=PDF |language=French |accessdate=21 March 2017|date= }} < /ref >  Narbonne has a [[population density]] of {{Pop density|52855|172.96|km2|prec=0|disp=num}} inhabitants/km < sup > 2 < /sup > .

 == Administration == 
 Narbonne is a [[Prefectures in France|subprefecture]] of the Aude department, the capital of the ''arrondissement'' of [[Arrondissement of Narbonne|Narbonne]] and the [[administrative centre]] ({{lang-fr|chef-lieu}}) of three [[Cantons of France|cantons]] 
 * [[Canton of Narbonne-1|Narbonne-1]], with 24,958 inhabitants  < small > (2014) < /small > . 
 * [[Canton of Narbonne-2|Narbonne-2]], with 19,785 inhabitants  < small > (2014) < /small > . 
 * [[Canton of Narbonne-3|Narbonne-3]], with 18,637 inhabitants  < small > (2014) < /small > . 
 
 It is part of the [[Communes of France|intercommunality]] ''Le Grand Narbonne'' ({{lang-fr|Communauté d'agglomération Le Grand Narbonne}}).
