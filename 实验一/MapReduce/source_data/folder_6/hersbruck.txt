 '''Hersbruck''' ({{IPA-de|hɛʁsˈbʁʊk|-|De-Hersbruck.ogg}}) is a small town in [[Middle Franconia]], [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]], belonging to the district [[Nürnberger Land]]. It is best known for the late-gothic artwork of the Hersbruck altar, the  " Hirtenmuseum "  and the landscape of [[Hersbruck Switzerland]].
