 '''Osmavandan''' ({{Lang-fa|عثماوندان}}, also [[Romanization|Romanized]] as '''‘Os̄māvandān'''; also known as '''Usmamanda''') < ref > {{GEOnet3|-3077480|Osmavandan}} < /ref >  is a small [[village]] in the Central District of Shaft County, in [[Gilan province|Gilan]], in  [[Iran]]. During the 2006 [[census]], the number of people living there was 759. These people were divided into 216 families. < ref > {{IranCensus2006|01}} < /ref >
