'''''Chibapsetta''''' is an extinct genus of [[righteye flounder]] that lived during the [[Pleistocene]] epoch in what is now [[Japan]]. It is a member of the [[Pleuronectidae]]. 
 [[File:Chibapsetta dolichurostyli.jpg|thumb|Artist's reconstruction]] 
 
 == Description == 
 It resembled the modern [[righteye flounders]].
