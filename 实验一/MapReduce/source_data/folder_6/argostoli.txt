 '''Argostoli''' is a coastal [[Greece|Greek]] town. It is in [[Kefalonia]] island whose is a capital. Also it is a capital of Kefalonia prefecture which is conclude and [[Ithaca]] island. According to the 2001 census, 12.589 people lived there. Argostoli has a port and it connects with [[Peloponnese]] port, Killini.
