'''Epigynum''' (plural '''epigyna''') is the [[sex organ]] on a [[female]] [[spider]]. It is found on the [[:wikt:underside|underside]] of her [[abdomen]]. A [[male]] spider covers its [[pedipalps]] in [[sperm]] and puts them inside the female spider's epigynum.
