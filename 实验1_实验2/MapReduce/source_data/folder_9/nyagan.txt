 '''Nyagan''' (Russian: Нягань) is a town in the northwest of [[Khanty-Mansi Autonomous Okrug]], [[Russia]]. It is near the [[Ob River]] and {{convert|230|km|sp=us}} northwest of [[Khanty-Mansiysk]]. It is named after the [[Nyagan-Yugan River]], a [[tributary]] of the Ob River. In 2010, 54,890 people lived there.
