 '''Oberhavel''' is a ''Kreis'' (district) in the northern part of [[Brandenburg]], [[Germany]]. 
 
 The district was formed  on [[6 December]] [[1993]] by joining the old districts of [[Gransee]] and [[Oranienburg]]. 
 
 == Coat of arms == 
 {| 
 |- valign=top 
 ||[[Image:DEU Oberhavel COA.svg|100px|Coat of arms]] 
 |The coat of arms shows the eagle, which is the [[heraldic]] [[symbol]] of [[Brandenburg]] in the top part. In the bottom are two flying [[swan]]s on green background representing the nature with many lakes. The coat of arms was used by the old district of Oranienburg before the merging, but was never officially granted. It was granted to the district of Oberhavel on [[May 18]], [[1994]].
