 '''Kilis''' is a city in southeastern [[Turkey]]. The city is in the [[Southeastern Anatolia Region|Southeast Anatolia Region]]. In 2020, 118,725 people lived there. < ref > {{cite web | url=https://www.nufusu.com/ilce/merkez_kilis-nufusu | title=Merkez Nüfusu Kilis }} < /ref >  The city was badly damaged and many people died in the [[2023 Turkey–Syria earthquake]].
