 '''Storsteinnes''' is a [[village]] in [[Balsfjord|Balsfjord Municipality]], [[Norway]]. The [[village]] covers an [[area]] of {{convert|1.1|km2|adj=off}} and has a [[population]] of 1,076 people. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Tettsteders befolkning og areal|url=https://www.ssb.no/befolkning/folketall/statistikk/tettsteders-befolkning-og-areal|access-date=2023-03-29|website=SSB|language=nb}} < /ref >  It is the largest [[urban area]] in the [[municipality]]. [[Storsteinnes Chapel]] is located in the [[village]]. The [[economy]] of the [[village]] is based on [[Municipal corporation|municipal services]] and [[Farming|agriculture]]. A [[Dairy farming|dairy]] run by [[Tine (company)|Tine]], which produces Brunost and Balsfjordost, < ref > {{Cite web|title=TINE® Brunost™|url=https://www.tine.com/tine-brunost|access-date=2023-03-29|website=Tineasia}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite web|title=TINE stanser produksjonen av Balsfjordost|url=https://medlem.tine.no/aktuelt-fra-tine/tine-stanser-produksjonen-av-balsfjordost|access-date=2023-03-29|website=TINE Medlem|language=no}} < /ref >  is one of the major [[Employer|employers]] in the [[village]]. Storsteinnes is located at the southern end of Sørkjosen, which is a branch of the main Balsfjorden. The European route E6 highway passes about 4 kilometres (2.485 miles) southeast of the [[village]] center. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Arctic Highway travel|url=https://www.lonelyplanet.com/norway/arctic-highway|access-date=2023-03-29|website=Lonely Planet|language=en}} < /ref >
