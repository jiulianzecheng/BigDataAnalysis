 A '''watercraft''' is a [[vehicle]] that can move on the [[surface]] of the water. Some watercrafts can go under the water. Examples of watercrafts are [[Boat|boats]], [[Ship|ships]], [[hovercraft]] and [[Submarine|submarines]]. Watercrafts are usually [[Propulsion tools|propelled]] by a [[sail]], [[oar]], [[paddle]] or [[engine]]. 
 
 ==Types== 
 There are different types of watercrafts. They are: 
 * [[Personal watercraft]] 
 * [[Pleasure craft]] 
 * [[Lifeboat]] 
 * [[Ship]] 
 * [[Submarine]] 
 * [[Merchant ship]] 
 * [[Sailboat]] 
 
 ==Propulsion== 
 Watercraft use different ways to move. They are: 
 * [[Oars]]: An oar is a [[tool]] used to move a boat through the water. It is one of the first means of moving a watercraft. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Definition of STEERING OAR|url=https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/steering+oar|access-date=2020-09-12|website=www.merriam-webster.com|language=en}} < /ref > 
 * [[Sail]]: A sail is a large piece of cloth on the top of some [[boat]]s. The wind goes around the sail and makes the boat move through the water. 
 * [[Engine]]: An engine is a [[machine]] used to change [[energy]] into movement that can be used. [[Natural gas]] are usually used to make the engine change energy. Some military we engines use [[nuclear energy]]. 
 
 ==Uses== 
 Watercrafts are used for [[fishing]]. They are also used to transport [[good]]s and [[Service (economics)|services]] to different places. They are used by the [[military]] to attack when there is [[war]]. 
 
 Watercrafts are used in many types of [[water sports]]. Watercraft like [[submarine]]s are used to explore the deep [[ocean]]. They are also used for [[leisure]]. Some watercrafts are also used to get [[Natural resource|natural resources]] from the ocean.
