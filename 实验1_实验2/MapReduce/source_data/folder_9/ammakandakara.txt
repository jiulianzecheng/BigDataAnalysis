 '''Ammakandakara''' is a village in [[Adoor]] Municipality in [[Pathanamthitta district]], [[Kerala]], [[India]]. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Ammakandakara, Pathanamthitta District, Kerala, India|url=https://www.keralatourism.org/routes-locations/ammakandakara/id/501|access-date=2022-12-23|website=Kerala Tourism|language=en}} < /ref > 
 
 The Chennampallil Sree Dharma Shasta and Devi temple are situating at the centre of the village.
