 '''Oberleichtersbach''' is a [[municipality]] in the district of [[Bad Kissingen (district)|Bad Kissingen]] in [[Bavaria]] in [[Germany]]. 
 
 ==Divisions of the municipality== 
 The municipality includes the following towns: 
 *Oberleichtersbach 
 *Unterleichtersbach 
 *Modlos 
 *Breitenbach 
 *Mitgenfeld
