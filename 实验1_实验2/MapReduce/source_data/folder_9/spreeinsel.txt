 The '''Spreeinsel''' is between [[Spree]] and Spreekanal in the [[Mitte|Berlin Mitte]]. It has three parts. < ref > {{Cite web|title=www.spreeinsel.de - Die Spreeinsel im Herzen von Berlin|url=http://www.spreeinsel.de/|access-date=2021-11-26|website=www.spreeinsel.de}} < /ref > 
 
 Middle — Berlin City Cock was there until 1950, it was rebuilt in 2021 for the Humboldt Forum 
 
 Southern — Fischer Island 
 
 Northern — it was used for gardens till the 19th century, then many museums were built there. Today is known as the Northern Museum Island and has been a [[World Heritage Site]] of [[UNESCO]] since 1999.
