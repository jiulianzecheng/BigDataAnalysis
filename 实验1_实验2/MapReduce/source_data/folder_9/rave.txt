 A '''rave''' is a large [[party]] or [[festival]] with [[disc jockey]]s playing [[electronic music]]. Music played at raves include [[House music|house]], [[Trance music|trance]], [[techno]], [[drum and bass]], [[dubstep]] and other forms of [[electronic dance music]] (EDM).
