 '''Framecourt''' is a [[Communes of France|small commune]]. It is found in the [[Regions of France|region]] [[Nord-Pas-de-Calais]] in the [[Pas-de-Calais]] [[Departments of France|department]] in the north of [[France]]. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 A small farming village situated {{Convert|21|mi|km|1}} west of Arras, at the junction of the D916 and the D102E roads. The church of St.Vulgan in the village dates from the seventeenth century. There is also an 18th-century [[priory]].
