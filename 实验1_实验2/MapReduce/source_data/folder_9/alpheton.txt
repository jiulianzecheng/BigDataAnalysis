'''Alpheton''' is a village and [[civil parish]] on the A134 road in [[Babergh]], [[Suffolk]], England. In 2001 there were 242 people living in Alpheton. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.ons.gov.uk/help/localstatistics|title=Local statistics - Office for National Statistics|website=www.ons.gov.uk}} < /ref >
