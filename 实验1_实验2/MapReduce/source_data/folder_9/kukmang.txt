 '''Kukmang''' is one of the 51 [[Union Councils of Pakistan|Union Councils]] of [[Abbottabad District]] in the [[Khyber Pakhtunkhwa]] province of [[Pakistan]]. < ref > {{Cite web |url=http://www.abbottabad.sdnpk.org/adminunit.htm |title=Administrative Units of District Abbottabad |access-date=2008-08-01 |archive-date=2007-04-03 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20070403104619/http://www.abbottabad.sdnpk.org/adminunit.htm |url-status=dead }} < /ref > 
 
 == Location == 
 Kukmang is located at 34°16'20N 73°25'30E and has a height of 1208 metres (3966 & nbsp;feet). < ref > {{Cite web |url=http://www.fallingrain.com/world/PK/3/Kukmang.html |title=Falling Rain Genomics - Location of Kukmang |access-date=2008-08-01 |archive-date=2007-10-01 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20071001000309/http://www.fallingrain.com/world/PK/3/Kukmang.html |url-status=dead }} < /ref >   It is in the northwest of the district, it borders [[Boi, Khyber Pakhtunkhwa|Boi]] to the north and [[Pattan Kalan]] to the east. 
 
 ==Subdivisions== 
 The Union Council of Kukmang is divided into the following areas: Kothiala, Kukmang and Pattan Khurd, < ref name= " AbbGov " / >  as part of the local government reforms of 2000 Pattan Khurd Union Council was merged into Kukmang. 
 
 ==Notable native sportsman== 
 [[Yasir Hameed]] Qureshi < ref > [http://content-pak.cricinfo.com/pakistan/content/player/43656.html Crick Info - Yasir Hamid] < /ref >   was the first player of Pakistan National Cricket team who participated in international cricket with two test centuries. His family migrated to Peshawer in 1965 but has family members remaining in Kukmang Union Council.
