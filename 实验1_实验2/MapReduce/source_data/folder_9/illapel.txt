 '''Illapel''' is a [[Chile]]an [[List of cities in Chile|city]]; it is the [[Capital city|capital]] of the [[Choapa Province|Choapa]] [[Provinces of Chile|province]], [[Coquimbo Region|Coquimbo]] [[Regions of Chile|region]]. It is in the most narrow point of the country along a [[Circle of latitude|parallel]], {{Convert|94|km|mi|abbr=on }}. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 The commune of Illapel has an area of {{Convert|2629.1|km2|sqmi|abbr=on }}. < ref name= " ineDPA "  / >  It is to the north of [[Santiago, Chile|Santiago]] at {{Convert|281|km|mi|abbr=on }} and to the northeast of the [[seaport]] city of [[Los Vilos]] at {{Convert|54|km|mi|abbr=on }}. 
 
 The commune is bordered on the north by the [[Combarbalá]] and [[Monte Patria]] (a small part) communes, both of the [[Limarí Province|Limarí]] province, on the east by [[Argentina]], on the south by the [[Salamanca, Chile|Salamanca]] and [[Los Vilos]] communes, and on the west by the [[Canela, Chile|Canela]] commune.

 The city of Illapel is along the ''Illapel'' [[river]], a [[tributary]] of the ''Choapa'' river, that is {{Convert|85|km|mi|abbr=on }} long. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://www.sinia.cl/1292/articles-31018_Choapa.pdf |title=Cuenca del Río Choapa |format=PDF |access-date=10 September 2016 |publisher=Dirección General de Aguas |date=December 2004 |archive-date=3 July 2007 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20070703002559/http://www.sinia.cl/1292/articles-31018_Choapa.pdf |url-status=dead }} < /ref > 
 
 ==Population== 
 {{As of|2002}} (last national [[census]]), there were 30,355 people living in the commune, giving it a [[population density]] of {{Pop density|30355|2629.1|km2|prec=1|disp=num}} inhabitants/km². < ref name= " ineDPA "  / > 
 
 The city of Illapel has an [[urban area]] of {{Convert|7.39|km2|sqmi|abbr=on }} and a population, in 2002, of 21,826 inhabitants. < ref name= " ineDPA "  / >
