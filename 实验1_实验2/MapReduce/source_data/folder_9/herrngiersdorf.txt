 '''Herrngiersdorf''' is a [[Municipalities of Germany|municipality]] in [[Kelheim (district)|Kelheim]] in [[Bavaria]] in [[Germany]]. 
 
 == Geography == 
 Herrngiersdorf is south of [[Langquaid]] and consists of Herrngiersdorf, Sandsbach, Semerskirchen, and Sittelsdorf.
