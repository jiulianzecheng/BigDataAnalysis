 '''Nieuwkoop''' is a [[List of municipalities of the Netherlands|municipality]] in the [[Netherlands|Dutch]] [[Provinces of the Netherlands|province]] of [[South Holland]]. In 2019, 28,628 people lived there. 
 
 It was enlarged with the former municipalities of [[Liemeer]] and [[Ter Aar]] on 1 January 2007.
