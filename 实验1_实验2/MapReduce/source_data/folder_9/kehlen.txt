 '''Kehlen''' ({{lang-lb|Kielen}}) is a [[Communes of Luxembourg|commune]] in [[Luxembourg]]. It is part of the [[Capellen (canton)|canton of Capellen]]. 
 
 As of 2019, 6,143 people live here.
