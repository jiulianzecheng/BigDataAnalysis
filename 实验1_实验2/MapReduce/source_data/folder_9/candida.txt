 '''''Candida''''' is a [[genus]] or grouping of [[yeast]]s. Yeasts are a type of [[fungus]]. This group of fungi are the most common cause of fungus [[infection]]s in the world. < ref > Manolakaki, D., Velmahos, G., Kourkoumpetis, T., Chang, Y., Alam, H. B., De Moya, M. M.,  &  Mylonakis, E. (2010). Candida infection and colonization among trauma patients. Virulence, 1(5), 367-375. < /ref >  Most people have or are exposed to this group of fungus but are not sick. If a person is weak because of another illness, this fungus can cause disease. < ref > Kourkoumpetis, Themistoklis K., | display-authors = etal  " The effect of cumulative length of hospital stay on the antifungal resistance of Candida strains isolated from critically ill surgical patients. "  Mycopathologia 171.2 (2011): 85-91. < /ref >  ''[[Candida albicans]]'' is the most common kind of Candida. Infection with this fungus is called [[candidiasis]] or thrush. Other species in this group can be found in the human gut. < ref > Spanakis, Elias K., | display-authors = etal  " Statin Therapy and Decreased Incidence of Positive <  i >  Candida < nowiki > < /i > < /nowiki >  Cultures Among Patients With Type 2 Diabetes Mellitus Undergoing Gastrointestinal Surgery. "  Mayo Clinic Proceedings. Vol. 85. No. 12. Elsevier, 2010. < /ref > 
 
 People can get a blood infection from this fungus. < ref name= " Enfert " > {{cite book|title=Candida: Comparative and Functional Genomics|url=https://archive.org/details/candidacomparati0000unse|publisher=Caister Academic Press|year=2007|isbn=978-1-904455-13-4|editor-last=dEnfert C|editor-last2=Hube B}} < /ref > 
 
 Medicines called [[antibiotics]] do not cure yeast infections.  In fact, an antibiotic can make a Candida infection worse. < ref > Kennedy, MJ | display-authors = etal  " Mechanisms of association of Candida albicans with intestinal mucosa " , Med Microbiol, volume-24, 333-341, 1987 < /ref >  Women can get a Candida infection in their vagina.  Men can also get a Candida infection in their genital area. If a person takes antibiotics for a long time, they have a greater chance of having a Candida infection.  People with diabetes or [[HIV]] are more likely to get a yeast infection. < ref > {{cite web|first=James M. |last=Steckelberg |url=http://www.mayoclinic.org/male-yeast-infection/expert-answers/faq-20058464 |title=Male yeast infection: Can I get it from my girlfriend? |publisher=Mayo Clinic |date=2012-09-18 |access-date=2014-03-23}} < /ref > < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.nlm.nih.gov/medlineplus/yeastinfections.html |title=Yeast Infections: MedlinePlus |publisher=Nlm.nih.gov |access-date=2014-03-23}} < /ref > 
 ==Role as a pathogen== 
 {{main |Candidiasis}} 
 This fungus can be grown in a lab. ''Candida'' looks like large, round, white or cream circles that smell like bread. < ref name= " DrFungus " > {{cite web | title=Candida species | work=DoctorFungus.org | url=http://www.doctorfungus.org/thefungi/Candida_spp.htm | access-date=2007-02-09 |archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20070208004957/http://www.doctorfungus.org/thefungi/Candida_spp.htm  < !-- Bot retrieved archive -- >  |archive-date = 2007-02-08}} < /ref >  ''Candida'' can be found on almost all healthy skin. < ref name= " rmm " > {{Citation 
   |last = Jawetz | display-authors = etal|title = Review of Medical Microbiology|year = 1978|edition = 13th|chapter = Medical Mycology|pages = 276–278}} < /ref >   Dry skin has less fungus. < ref name=Mims > {{cite book|last=Goehring|first=Richard V.|title=Mims' medical microbiology.|year=2008|publisher=Mosby Elsevier|location=Philadelphia, PA|isbn=9780323044752|page=656|edition=4th}} < /ref >  Small numbers of this fungus are found in other areas of the body.  These are the nose, throat and lungs; the digestive system and the vagina. This is normal. 
 
 When this fungus causes disease in the mouth it is called thrush (oropharyngeal [[candidiasis]]). When this fungus causes disease in the female reproductive organs it is called vaginal candidiasis or just 'yeast infection'. Thrush is common in people who wear dentures. < ref name= " Darwazeh_1990 " > {{cite journal|author=Darwazeh A|last2=Lamey P|last3=Samaranayake L|last4=MacFarlane T|last5=Fisher B|last6=Macrury S|last7=MacCuish A|display-authors=4|year=1990|title=The relationship between colonisation, secretor status and in-vitro adhesion of Candida albicans to buccal epithelial cells from diabetics|url=https://archive.org/details/sim_journal-of-medical-microbiology_1990-09_33_1/page/43|journal=J Med Microbiol|volume=33|issue=1|pages=43–9|doi=10.1099/00222615-33-1-43|pmid=2231671}} < /ref > 
 
 == Treatment == 
 In people without other illness, a candida infection can be cured with skin ointment or medicine that can be swallowed. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.webmd.com/skin-problems-and-treatments/candidiasis-yeast-infection |title=Yeast Infections (Candidiasis) in Men and Women |publisher=WebMD |date=2012-11-12 |access-date=2014-03-23}} < /ref >   Skin medications can be bought over-the-counter without a doctor's prescription. In people who are already sick, candidiasis can become a very serious illness.  It can cause [[abscess]], [[thrombophlebitis]], heart infection ([[endocarditis]].  It can cause infections of the eyes or other organs. < ref name= " Enfert "  / > < ref name= " rmm "  / >   Candida infection can get into the body through cuts in the skin. < ref name= " Mims "  / >
