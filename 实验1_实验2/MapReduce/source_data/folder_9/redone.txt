 ==Works== 
 As a record producer and songwriter, he has worked with many high-profile recording artists, such as [[Ava Max]], [[Lady Gaga]], [[Akon]], [[Michael Jackson]], [[RBD]], [[Now United]], [[U2]], [[Nicki Minaj]], [[Jennifer Lopez]], [[Nicole Scherzinger]], [[Gru (rapper)|Gru]], [[Cross Gene]], [[Mika (singer)|MIKA]], [[Pitbull (rapper)|Pitbull]], [[Enrique Iglesias]], [[Shakira]], [[Wyclef Jean]], [[Mariah Carey]], [[Paulina Rubio]], [[Mylène Farmer]], [[Mohombi]], [[Inna]], [[Khaled (musician)|Khaled]], [[Alexandra Burke]], [[Austin Mahone]], [[One Direction]], [[Marc Anthony]], [[the Band Perry]], [[Prince Royce]], [[Rod Stewart]], [[Usher (musician)|Usher]] and [[Saad Lamjarred]], among many others.  ==Awards== 
 RedOne has been nominated for ten [[Grammy Award]]s, winning three. He was also awarded the [[Grammis|Grammis Award]] for Producer of the Year, which is the Swedish version of the Grammy Awards. In 2009, he was the number one producer on the [[Billboard Hot 100|''Billboard'' Hot 100]].
