 '''Grundig''' ({{IPAc-en|lang|ˈ|ɡ|r|ʌ|n|d|ɪ|ɡ|,_|ˈ|ɡ|r|ʊ|n|d|ɪ|ɡ}}; {{IPA-de|ˈɡʁʊndɪç}}) was a consumer electronics manufacturer owned by [[Arçelik|Arçelik A.Ş.]]. In 2007, Koç Holding bought Grundig. The brand is now under its home appliances [[subsidiary]] Arcelik A.Ş.
