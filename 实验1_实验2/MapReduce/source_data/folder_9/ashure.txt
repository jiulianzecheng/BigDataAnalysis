 '''Ashure''' ({{lang-tr|Aşure or Aşura}}) is a traditional Turkish [[pudding]].  Ashure is made from [[grain]]s, dried [[fruit]]s and [[Nut (fruit)|nuts]].  There are no animal products ([[butter]], [[milk]], [[cheese]], etc.) and ashure is [[vegan]]. 
 
 Specially, [[Turkish people]] make ashure during [[Muharram]], the first month of the [[Islamic calendar]].  Ashure ({{lang-ar|عَشْرَة}}) means tenth in Arabic and the 10th day of every lunar year is the Day of Ashure.
