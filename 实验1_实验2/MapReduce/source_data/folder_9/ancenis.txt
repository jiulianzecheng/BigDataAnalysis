 '''Ancenis''' ({{lang-br|Ankiniz}}) is a former [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is found in the [[Regions of France|region]] [[Pays de la Loire]] in the [[Loire-Atlantique]] [[Departments of France|department]] in western [[France]].
