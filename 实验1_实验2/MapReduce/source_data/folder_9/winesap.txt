'''Winesap''' is a kind of [[apple]]. It is a medium sized and dark red. The flesh of the apple is yellow. Winesap apples have a sweet and tart taste. They are grown in [[Washington (state)|Washington]], [[Oregon]], [[Georgia (U.S. state)|Georgia]], and [[Virginia]].
