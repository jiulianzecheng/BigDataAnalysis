 '''Tukums''' is a town in [[Latvia]] with [[town rights]] since 1795. In 2019, half of [[Latvia]]'s tires were being recycled in the town. It has the second largest tire recycling plant in the [[Baltic]] country. < ref > {{Cite web|url=http://www.themayor.eu/en/half-of-latvias-tires-to-be-recycled-in-new-plant-in-tukums|title=Half of Latvia’s tires to be recycled in new plant in Tukums|website=www.themayor.eu|access-date=2020-05-15|archive-date=2020-07-02|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200702020844/https://www.themayor.eu/en/half-of-latvias-tires-to-be-recycled-in-new-plant-in-tukums|url-status=dead}} < /ref > 
 
 In 2020, Latvian president [[Egils Levits]] praised the region's leaders for its COVID-19 efforts and offering counselling to its residents.  < ref > {{Cite web|url=https://www.president.lv/en/news/news/president-of-latvia-and-leaders-of-local-governments-discuss-regional-challenges-of-the-crisis-management-26209|title=President of Latvia and leaders of local governments discuss regional challenges of the crisis management|website=www.president.lv|language=en|access-date=2020-05-24}} < /ref >
