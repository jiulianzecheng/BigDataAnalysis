 The '''Gorgoderidae''' are a [[Family (biology)|family]] of [[Flatworm]]s  (Platyhelminthes). They belong to the ordo [[Plagiorchiida]]. The [[species]] are [[Parasitism|parasites]] of [[fish]], [[amphibians]] and [[reptiles]]. < ref name= " Invertebrates " > Libbie Henrietta Hyman: ''The Invertebrates, Volume 2: Platyhelminthes and Rhynchocoela, the Acoleomate Bilateria.'' < /ref >  They have distome, which means they got two suckers, one at the mouth (oral) and one at the waist (ventral). < ref name= " Invertebrates "  / >
