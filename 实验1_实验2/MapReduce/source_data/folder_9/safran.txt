 '''Safran''' is a [[France|French]] company specialized in aerospace. Its headquarters are located in [[Paris]]. It has been created in 2005 by Snecma and Sagem. < ref > [http://www.usinenouvelle.com/article/fusion-surprise-de-snecma-et-sagem.46311 Fusion surprise de Snecma et Sagem] < /ref >   
 
 It provide [[aircraft]] engines, [[Helicopter|helicopters]] and [[Missile|missiles]], aircraft equipment and defense. < ref > [https://www.usinenouvelle.com/article/safran-entre-dans-le-cac40.N158107 Safran entre dans le CAC40] < /ref > 
 
 The group has more than 81,000 employees.
