 '''PMMP''' is a [[Finland|Finnish]] [[pop music|pop]] [[rock and roll|rock]] [[band]]. The band has two [[singer]]s: Paula Vesala and Mira Luoti and three [[musician]]s Mikko Virta, Juho Vehmanen and Heikki Kytölä. The band writes their own [[song]]s with Jori Sjöroos. PMMP became popular in Finland in [[2003]], with the song ''Rusketusraidat''. Their biggest hit is a song called ''Oo siellä jossain mun''. 
 
 In November 2007, PMMP released their fourth [[album]] ''Puuhevonen'' which has songs for children.
