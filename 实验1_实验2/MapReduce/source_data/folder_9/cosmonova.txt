 '''Cosmonova''' is an [[IMAX Dome]] [[cinema (building)|cinema]] and [[planetarium]].  
 
 It is located in an [[wikt:annex#Noun|annex]] of the [[Swedish Museum of Natural History]] in [[Stockholm]], [[Sweden]]. < ref name=Statens > {{cite web|title=Naturhistoriska riksmuseet |url=http://www.sfv.se/fastigheter/sverige/stockholms-lan-ab/museer/naturhistoriska-riksmuseet/ |website=www.sfv.se |publisher=[[National Property Board of Sweden|Statens fastighetsverk]] |accessdate=17 August 2014 |url-status = dead|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20141014000204/http://www.sfv.se/fastigheter/sverige/stockholms-lan-ab/museer/naturhistoriska-riksmuseet/ |archivedate=14 October 2014 }} < /ref >   
 ==History== 
 Cosmonova opened in 1993. It was the first IMAX installation in Sweden (and third in the Nordic countries after [[Tietomaa|Tietomaa Science Centre]] in [[Oulu]], [[Finland]] and [[Tycho Brahe Planetarium]] in [[Copenhagen]], [[Denmark]]). It is also the largest planetarium in Sweden. < ref > {{cite web|title=Om Cosmonova|url=http://www.nrm.se/besokmuseet/cosmonova/omcosmonova.5715.html|website=www.nrm.se|publisher=Swedish Museum of Natural History|accessdate=17 August 2014}} < /ref >
