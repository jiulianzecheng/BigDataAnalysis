 '''Spearmint''' is a type of [[mint]]. It is also called '''garden mint''', '''common mint''', '''lamb mint''' and '''mackerel mint'''. < ref name= " :0 " > {{Cite book|url=https://www.worldcat.org/oclc/262680646|title=World spice plants|last=Seidemann, Johannes.|date=2005|publisher=Springer|isbn=978-3-540-27908-2|location=Berlin|oclc=262680646}} < /ref >  It is native to [[Europe]] and [[South Asia|southern Asia]]. It is [[naturalized]] in [[North Africa|northern]] and [[South Africa|southern Africa]], [[North America]] and [[South America]]. It is used as a [[Flavor|flavouring]] in [[food]] and [[Herbal tea|herbal teas]]. < ref name= " :0 "  / > 
 
 The species and subspecies have many [[synonyms]].
