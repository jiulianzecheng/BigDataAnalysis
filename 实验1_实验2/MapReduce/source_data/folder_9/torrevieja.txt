 '''Torrevieja''' ({{IPA-es|ˌtoreˈβjexa|lang}}) or '''Torrevella''' ({{IPA-va|ˌtoreˈveʎa|lang}}) is a seaside city and [[municipality]] on the [[Costa Blanca]] in the [[province of Alicante]]. It is on the southeastern [[Mediterranean Sea|Mediterranean]] coast of [[Spain]].
