 '''Mondoweiss''' is a [[news]] [[website]] that began as a general-interest [[blog]] written by [[Philip Weiss]] on The New York Observer website. It is now an independent website that's comitted to informing it's readers about events in [[Palestine (region)|Palestine]] and [[United States|US]] foreign policy towards [[State of Palestine|Palestine]] and [[Israel]]. < ref > {{Cite web|date=|title=About Mondoweiss|url=https://mondoweiss.net/about-mondoweiss/|access-date=|website=|language=en-US}} < /ref > 
 ==Reception== 
 In 2018 Israeli journalist [[Amira Hass]] cited Mondoweiss as a must-read source for those wanting to understand Israeli policy regarding [[Palestinian people|Palestinians]]. < ref > {{Cite news|title=The Websites You Must Visit to Understand Israel's Policy Toward Palestinians|language=en|work=Haaretz|url=https://www.haaretz.com/israel-news/2018-04-29/ty-article/.premium/these-websites-tell-the-truth-about-israels-palestinian-policy/0000017f-df0d-db22-a17f-ffbd015a0000|access-date=}} < /ref > 
 
 It has recieved praises for its content in [[USA]] as well as in [[Middle East]]. At the same time it has also recieved [[Criticism|criticisms]] specially from Pro-Israeli [[Americans|American people]] or [[Organization|organisations]] who accuses the website of [[antisemitism]], while the [[Israeli Jews|Israelis]] accuses it of being [[Anti-Semitic]] as well as Anti-[[Israel]]. < ref > {{Cite web|last=Rosen|first=Armin|date=2012-07-14|title=A Reminder That Anti-Semitism Has No Place in Debates Over Israel|url=https://www.theatlantic.com/international/archive/2012/07/a-reminder-that-anti-semitism-has-no-place-in-debates-over-israel/259830/|access-date=|website=The Atlantic|language=en}} < /ref >
