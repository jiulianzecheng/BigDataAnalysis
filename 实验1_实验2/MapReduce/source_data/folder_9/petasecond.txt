 A '''petasecond,''' (symbol: '''Ps'''). Is one quadrillion seconds. that is about 31.7 million years. It can be written as '''10 < sup > 15 < /sup >  seconds.''' A petasecond can also be referred to as an '''''E-Phase''''' when using it to describe a length of time.
