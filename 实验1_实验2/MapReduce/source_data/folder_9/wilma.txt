'''Wilma''' is a female [[given name]]. It is a short form of the German name [[Wilhelmina (given name)|Wilhelmina]].  The name means ''protector''.  Wilma is a moniker that has endured for centuries and boasts a fascinating past. Its roots can be traced back to the Germanic language and the name Wilhelm, which combines  " will "  or  " desire "  with  " helmet "  or  " protection.
