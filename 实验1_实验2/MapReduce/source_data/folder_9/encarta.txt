 '''''Encarta''''' was a [[digital]] [[multimedia]] [[encyclopedia]] by [[Microsoft]]. There was a more complete version called ''Encarta Premium'' made in 2008. It had more than 62,000 [[articles]]  < ref name= " Encarta2009 " > [http://www.microsoft.com/learningspace/Products.aspx?prod=encarta Encarta 2009 Information] < /ref >  and many [[photo]]graphs and [[illustration]]s. There was also [[music]] clips, [[video]]s, [[map]]s and [[timeline]]s with some [[activity]] games. It also included an [[atlas]],  and [[homework]] [[tool]]s. It was available on the [[World Wide Web|internet]] if you pay a [[year]]ly [[subscription|fee]], or to buy on [[DVD|DVD-ROM]], or [[CD-ROM]]s. Many articles could also be read online free of charge, a service which is paid for by [[advertisement]]s. < ref > For the free service, one should use the URL http://search.msn.com/encarta/results.aspx {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20050811233841/http://search.msn.com/encarta/results.aspx |date=2005-08-11 }} (MSN Search Encarta) rather than http://encarta.msn.com {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20091031074236/http://encarta.msn.com/ |date=2009-10-31 }} (MSN Encarta : Online Encyclopedia, Dictionary, Atlas, and Homework) < /ref > 
 
 Microsoft made similar encyclopedias under the ''Encarta'' [[trademark]] in several different [[Europe]]an [[language]]s and in [[Japanese language|Japanese]].
