 ''Bothriospondylus'' ('excavated [[vertebrae]]') is a [[genus]] of [[sauropodomorph]] [[dinosaur]] known from a handful of [[vertebrae]]. It lived during the [[Late Jurassic]] in England. It belongs to the family ''[[Bothriospondylidae]]'' that was coined by [[Richard Lydekker]] in 1885. The genus is based on the specimen '''''BMNH R44529-5'''''.
