 '''Ferlens''' was a [[municipality]] in [[Lavaux-Oron (district)|Lavaux-Oron]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Vaud]] in [[Switzerland]]. On 1 July 2016, the former municipalities of [[Carrouge, Vaud|Carrouge]], Ferlens and [[Mézières, Vaud|Mézières]] joined together to become the new municipality called [[Jorat-Mézières]]. < ref > [http://www.bfs.admin.ch/bfs/portal/de/index/infothek/nomenklaturen/blank/blank/gem_liste/03.html Amtliches Gemeindeverzeichnis der Schweiz] published by the Swiss Federal Statistical Office {{in lang|de}} accessed 27 April 2016 < /ref >
