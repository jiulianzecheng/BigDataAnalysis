 '''Serbs''' ([[Serbian language|Serbian]]: Срби, ''Srbi'') are a [[South Slavs|South Slavic]] people living in the [[Balkans]] and [[Central Europe]], mainly in [[Serbia]], [[Montenegro]], [[Bosnia-Herzegovina]], and in [[Croatia]], in Krajina region.
