 '''Iridaceae''' are perennial herbs from [[rhizome]]s, [[bulb]]s or [[corm]]s comprising about 80 genera and 1,500 species. The [[leaves]] are distichous and have a sheathing, equitant base and a generally ensiform or linear blade with parallel venation. The [[flower]]s are bisexual, usually showy, and are actinomorphic or zygomorphic.
