 '''Oisterwijk''' is a city and [[List of municipalities of the Netherlands|municipality]] in the [[Netherlands|Dutch]] [[Provinces of the Netherlands|province]] of [[North Brabant]]. In 2021, about 32,000 people live there. 
 
 In 2021, the village of [[Haaren, North Brabant|Haaren]] was added to Oisterwijk.
