 '''Gjakova''' ([[Albanian language]]) Gjakova or ''Gjakovë''; {{lang-sr|Ђаковица}}, ''Đakovica'';)  is a city and [[Municipalities of Kosovo|municipality]] in western [[Kosovo]]{{ref label|status|a|}}. It is also the administrative centre of the [[District of Đakovica|district]] with the same name. The municipality's population is estimated at 150,000 (2007). < ref name= " OSCE profile " > [[Organization for Security and Co-operation in Europe|OSCE]] {{PDF|[http://www.osce.org/documents/mik/2005/12/1182_en.pdf Mission in Kosovo: Municipal profile of Đakovica]}}, October 2007. Retrieved on 9 March 2008. < /ref >  The city is located at {{coord|42.39|N|20.43|E|type:city}}.
