 '''Scheyern''' is a [[Municipalities of Germany|municipality]]  in [[Pfaffenhofen (district)|Pfaffenhofen]] in [[Bavaria]] in [[Germany]]. The [[Scheyern Abbey]] is in Scheyern. 
 
 The village of [[Fernhag]] is outside the town.
