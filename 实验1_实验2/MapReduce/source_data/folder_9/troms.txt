 '''Troms''' ({{lang-sv|Romsa}}; {{lang-fkv|Tromssa}}; {{lang-fi|Tromssa}}) is a former [[Counties of Norway|county]] in northern [[Norway]] and it is planned to become a county again in 2024. Previously, on 1 January 2020 Troms was merged with the neighbouring county of [[Finnmark]] to form the new [[Troms og Finnmark]] county. The capital was [[Tromsø]].
