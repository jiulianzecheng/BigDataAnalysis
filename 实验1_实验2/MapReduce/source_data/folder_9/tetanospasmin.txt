 '''Tetanospasmin''' is the [[neurotoxin]] produced by the vegetative [[spore]] of ''[[Clostridium tetani]]'' in [[Hypoxia (environmental)|anaerobic]] conditions, causing [[tetanus]]. It is sometimes called spasmogenic toxin, tetanus toxin or abbreviated to TeTx or TeNT.
