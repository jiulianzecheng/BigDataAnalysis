 '''Hilfikon''' was a [[municipality]] of the [[district]] of [[Bremgarten (district)|Bremgarten]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Aargau]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 Since 2010, it is part of the municipality of [[Villmergen]].
