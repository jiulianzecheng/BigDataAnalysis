{{Greek alphabet|letter=rho}} 
 '''Rho''' (uppercase/lowercase '''Ρ ρ''') is the 17th letter of the [[Greek alphabet]]. It is used to represent the  " r "  sound in [[Ancient Greek|Ancient]] and [[Modern Greek language|Modern]] Greek. In the system of [[Greek numerals]], it has a value of 100. Letters that came from it include the Roman [[R]] and Cyrillic [[Р]]. 
 
 In [[statistics]], the lowercase Rho ( " ρ " ) is used to represent population [[correlation]]. < ref > {{Cite web|date=2020-03-20|title=Greek/Hebrew/Latin-based Symbols in Mathematics|url=https://mathvault.ca/hub/higher-math/math-symbols/greek-hebrew-latin-symbols/|access-date=2020-10-07|website=Math Vault|language=en-US}} < /ref >  In [[physics]], the same letter is used to represent [[density]]. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Rho Symbol in Greek Alphabet|url=https://www.greeksymbols.net/rho-symbol|access-date=2020-10-07|website=www.greeksymbols.net|language=en}} < /ref >
