 '''Nottwil''' is a [[municipality]] of the [[district]] [[Sursee (district)|Sursee]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Lucerne (canton)|Lucerne]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 It is on the southern shore of [[Lake Sempach]] (''Sempachersee'').
