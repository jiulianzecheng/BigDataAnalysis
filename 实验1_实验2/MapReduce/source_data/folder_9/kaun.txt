 '''Bishop Kaun''' is an American [[Professional wrestling|professional wrestler]]. He currently wrestles for [[Ring of Honor]] (ROH) and is one-third of the current [[ROH World Six-Man Tag Team Championship|ROH World Six-Man Tag Team Champion]]s. He also makes appearances in [[All Elite Wrestling]] (AEW). He is a member of the stable [[The Embassy (professional wrestling)|The Embassy]] and one-half of the [[tag team]] Gates Of Agony with [[Toa Liona]]. 
 
 In ROH, Kaun has been a three-time [[ROH World Six-Man Tag Team Championship|ROH World Six-Man Tag Team Champion]].
