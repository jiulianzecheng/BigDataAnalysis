 '''''Thinner''''' is a book written in 1984 by [[Stephen King]], under the name Richard Bachman.  It is about an [[obesity|overweight]] [[lawyer]] called Billy Halleck, who is put under a [[curse]] which makes him lose a lot of [[weight loss|weight]].
