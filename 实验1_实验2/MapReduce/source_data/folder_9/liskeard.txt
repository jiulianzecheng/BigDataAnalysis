 '''Liskeard''' is a town near the A38 road in [[Cornwall]], England. In 2001 there were 8,656 people living in Liskeard. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.ons.gov.uk/help/localstatistics|title=Local statistics - Office for National Statistics|website=www.ons.gov.uk}} < /ref >  The parish church is dedicated to St Martin and Liskeard railway station is on the railway line from Plymouth to Penzance.
