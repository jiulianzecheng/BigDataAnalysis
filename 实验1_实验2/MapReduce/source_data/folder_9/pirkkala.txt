 '''Pirkkala''' is a [[municipality]] in [[Pirkanmaa]], [[Finland]]. It had about 18,500 people living there in January 2014. The municipalities next to it are [[Tampere]], [[Nokia, Finland|Nokia]], [[Lempäälä]], and [[Vesilahti]]. Pirkkala has been growing fast but doesn't yet have the status of a town or city.
