 '''Fladungen''' is a town in [[Rhön-Grabfeld]], in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. It is in the [[Rhön Mountains]], 11 & nbsp;km northwest of [[Ostheim]], 20 & nbsp;km west of [[Meiningen]], and 33 & nbsp;km east of [[Fulda]].  It is the northernmost town in Bavaria, bordering [[Hesse]] to the northwest and [[Thuringia]] to the northeast. 
 
 Fladungen is at the southern edge of the [[Rhön Mountains]], and the [[Rhön Biosphere Reserve]] begins a few kilometers outside of town.  It is at the front of the river [[Streu (river)|Streu]], which flows southeast through it to join the [[Franconian Saale]] near [[Bad Neustadt]].
