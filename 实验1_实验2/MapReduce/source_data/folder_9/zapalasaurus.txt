 '''''Zapalasaurus''''' is a [[genus]] of [[dinosaur]] named after the city of [[Zapala]]. The [[type species]], ''Zapalasaurus bonapartei'', was found in [[Neuquén Province]], [[Argentina]]. It was a [[Diplodocoidea|diplodocoid]], a long-necked [[herbivore]], and it lived during the [[Early Cretaceous]].
