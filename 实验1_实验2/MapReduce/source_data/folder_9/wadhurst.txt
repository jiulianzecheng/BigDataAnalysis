 '''Wadhurst''' is a [[town]] and [[Civil parishes in England|civil parish]] in [[Wealden]], [[East Sussex]], [[South East England]]. As of June 2018, there were an estimate of 4,036 people living in Wadhurst. Out of which 1,872 were men and 2,164 were women.  < ref > https://www.citypopulation.de/en/uk/southeastengland/east_sussex/E34004158__wadhurst/ < /ref >
