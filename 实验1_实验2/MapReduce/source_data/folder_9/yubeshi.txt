 '''Yubeshi''' is a kind of japanese [[confection]] ( " sweets " ). Yubeshi is made in different shapes. Usually it is made with [[rice]] or [[rice flour]], some [[Nut (fruit)|nuts]] and a [[citrus]] fruit, often [[Yuzu]]. [[Walnut]]s are commonly used. Making Yubeshi is a lot of work.
