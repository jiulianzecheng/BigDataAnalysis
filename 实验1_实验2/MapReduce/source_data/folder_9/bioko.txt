 '''Bioko''' is an [[island]] {{convert|32|km|mi|abbr=on}} off the west coast of [[Africa]]. It is the northernmost part of [[Equatorial Guinea]]. It is in the [[Bight of Bonny]] part of the [[Gulf of Guinea]]. 
 
 Bioko has a total area of {{convert|2017|km2|sqmi|0|abbr=on}}. It is {{convert|70|km|mi|abbr=on}} long and about {{convert|32|km|mi|abbr=on}} across. Its highest peak is [[Pico Basile]] at {{convert|3012|m|ft|0|abbr=on}}.
