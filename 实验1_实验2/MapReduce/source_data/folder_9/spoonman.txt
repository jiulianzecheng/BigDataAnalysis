 " '''Spoonman''' "  is a song by the [[United States|American]] [[grunge music|grunge]] band [[Soundgarden]]. It is from their fourth studio album ''[[Superunknown]]'', which was released in 1994. The song describes Artie the Spoonman, a real-life musician who makes music with [[spoon]]s. He was also featured in the song's music video.  " Spoonman "  won the [[Grammy Award]] for [[Grammy Award for Best Metal Performance|Best Metal Performance]] at the [[37th Annual Grammy Awards|1995 Grammy Awards]]. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.grammy.com/awards/37th-annual-grammy-awards|accessdate=2014-11-10|title=37th Annual Gtammy Awards|publisher=Grammy Awards}} < /ref >
