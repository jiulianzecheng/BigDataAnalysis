A '''subdomain''' is the portion shown before a [[domain name]], separated by a [[full stop|dot (.)]] 
 
 For example, a [[domain]] might look like this 
 :''example.com'' 
 
 Whereas the subdomain of this domain might look like this, shown here in bold 
 :'''''subdomain.'''''example.com''

 [[File:Subdomain-en.svg|thumb|300px]]
