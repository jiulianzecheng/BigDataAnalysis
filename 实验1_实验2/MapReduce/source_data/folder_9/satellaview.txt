 The '''Satellaview''' is a [[satellite modem]] add-on for the [[Super Nintendo Entertainment System]] only released in [[Japan]] by [[Nintendo]]. Nintendo made many games for it usually with  " BS "  in front of them, including remakes of ''[[The Legend of Zelda (video game)|The Legend of Zelda]]'' and ''[[The Legend of Zelda: A Link to the Past]]''. Other companies have also made games for it.
