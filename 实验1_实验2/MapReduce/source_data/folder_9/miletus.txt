 '''Miletus''' (mī lē' təs) ([[Ancient Greek]]: Μίλητος, literally  [[transliterated]] ''Milētos'', [[Latin language|Latin]] ''Miletus'') was an [[Ancient history|ancient]] city on the western coast of [[Anatolia]] (in what is now [[Aydin Province]], [[Turkey]]), near the mouth of the [[Maeander River]] in ancient [[Caria]].
