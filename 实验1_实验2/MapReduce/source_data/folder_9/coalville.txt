 '''Coalville''' is a [[town]] in the district of [[North West Leicestershire]], [[Leicestershire]] in the [[East Midlands]] of [[England]]. In  the 2011 census, 34,575 people lived there. < ref name=Town > {{cite web|title=North West Leicestershire profile|url=https://www.nwleics.gov.uk/files/documents/coalville/Coalville.pdf|publisher=Nwleics.gov.uk|access-date=5 June 2013}} < /ref > < ref name=Area > {{cite web|url=http://www.leics.gov.uk/nwlweb_ch2_demography.pdf |title=Demographic Profile of North West Leicestershire |date=2005 |publisher=Leics.gov.uk |access-date=18 November 2015}} < /ref >  The town is on the [[A511 road|A511]] between [[Leicester]] and [[Burton upon Trent]]. It is close to junction 22 of the [[M1 motorway]] where the A511 meets the A50 between [[Ashby-de-la-Zouch]] and Leicester. The town borders [[Charnwood Forest]].
