 '''''Dryptosaurus''''' ( " tearing lizard " ) was a [[theropod]] [[dinosaur]] of the [[Tyrannosaur]]oidea type. < ref > Paul, G.S. 2010. ''The Princeton field guide to dinosaurs''. Princeton University Press, p. 100 < /ref >  It was found in [[Upper Cretaceous]] [[strata]] of Eastern [[North America]]. It was formerly known as ''Laelaps''. 
 
 It was given its name because it had 8-inch (21-cm) long finger claws. Fossils have been found in [[New Jersey]]. It is known only from a partial skeleton.
