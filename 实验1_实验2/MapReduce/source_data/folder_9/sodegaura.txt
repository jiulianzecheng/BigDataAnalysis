 {{nihongo|'''Sodegaura'''|袖ヶ浦市|Sodegaura-shi}} is a [[cities of Japan|city]] that is found in [[Chiba Prefecture|Chiba]], [[Japan]].  Sodegaura was created on [[April 1]], [[1991]]. About 59,230 people were living in Sodegaura as of [[2003]].
