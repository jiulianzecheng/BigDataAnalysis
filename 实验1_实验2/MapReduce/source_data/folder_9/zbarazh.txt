 '''Zbarazh''' ([[Ukrainian language|Ukrainian]]: Збараж, [[Polish language|Polish]]: Zbaraż, [[Yiddish language|Yiddish]]: זבאריזש ''Zbarizh'') is a city in the [[Ternopil Oblast]] ([[Oblast|province]]) of western [[Ukraine]]. It is the [[Capital city|administrative center]] of the [[Zbarazh Raion]] ([[Raion|district]]), and is located in the historic region of [[Galicia (Central Europe)|Galicia]].
