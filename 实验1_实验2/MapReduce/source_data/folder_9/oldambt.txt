 '''Oldambt''' is a [[List of municipalities of the Netherlands|municipality]] in the province of [[Groningen (province)|Groningen]], the [[Netherlands]]. In 2019, 38,129 people lived there. 
 
 It was created on 1 January 2010 from the former municipalities of [[Reiderland]], [[Scheemda]] and [[Winschoten]].
