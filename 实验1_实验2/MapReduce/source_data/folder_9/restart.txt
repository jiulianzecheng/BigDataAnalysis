 '''Restart''' is a [[Brazil]]ian teenage pop band. The band's hit songs include  " Recomeçar " ,  " Vou Cantar " ,  " Levo Comigo "  and  " Amanhecer no Teu Olhar " . 
 
 == Band members == 
 * Pe Lanza (Pedro Gabriel Lanza Reis) - [[vocals]], [[bass guitar]] 
 * Pelu (Pedro Lucas Munhoz) - [[vocals]], [[electric guitar|guitar]] 
 * Koba (Lucas Henrique de Olivera Kobayashi) - [[electric guitar|guitar]] 
 * Thomas (Thomas Alexander Machado D'Avilla) - [[drums]]
