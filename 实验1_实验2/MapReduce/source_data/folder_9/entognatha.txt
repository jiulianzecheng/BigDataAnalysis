 The '''Entognatha''' is a class of ametabolous [[arthropod]]s, which, together with [[insect]]s, makes up the superclass [[Hexapoda]]. Their mouthparts are entognathous, meaning they are retracted within the head. The class contains three orders: Collembola ([[springtail]]s), [[Diplura]] and [[Protura]].
