 '''Laksam''' is a small city in [[Comilla District|Cumilla district]] of [[Bangladesh]]. It is in the [[Chittagong Division]]. Laksam is the birthplace of Asia's first female [[Nawab]] ([[Royal family|royal]] title of powerful [[rulers]]) and one of the earliest female education [[Activism|activist]] [[Faizunnsa Chowdhurani]]. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Choudhurani, Nawab Faizunnesa - Banglapedia|url=http://en.banglapedia.org/index.php?title=Choudhurani,_Nawab_Faizunnesa|access-date=2021-03-01|website=en.banglapedia.org|language=en}} < /ref >
