 '''Djent''' is a type of [[progressive metal]]. It is known for its difficult [[Rhythm|rhythm patterns]], and is inspired by [[free jazz]] music. < ref > {{Cite web|date=2016-05-17|title=Meshuggah Share the Secrets of Their Sound {{!}} Guitar World|url=http://www.guitarworld.com/meshuggah-share-secrets-their-sound|access-date=2022-11-04|archive-date=2016-05-17|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160517094739/http://www.guitarworld.com/meshuggah-share-secrets-their-sound|url-status=bot: unknown}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite web|author1=Brad Angle|date=2011-07-23|title=Interview: Meshuggah Guitarist Fredrik Thordendal Answers Reader Questions|url=https://www.guitarworld.com/artists/interview-meshuggah-guitarist-fredrik-thordendal-answers-reader-questions|access-date=2022-11-04|website=guitarworld|language=en}} < /ref >  The [[electric guitar]] is one of the main [[Musical instrument|instruments]] in djent. In djent, the guitar is played with high [[Gain (electronics)|gain]] (power) and [[Effect pedal|distortion]]. Djent guitarists also do things such as [[Melody|down-tune]] their guitar strings and [[palm mute]]. The word  " djent "  comes from how the music sounds. < ref > {{Cite web|author1=Metal Hammer|date=2017-07-22|title=Currents: making waves in the djent-metal underground|url=https://www.loudersound.com/features/currents-making-waves-in-the-djent-metal-underground|access-date=2022-11-04|website=loudersound|language=en}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite web|title=What is Djent|url=https://djenthub.com/what-is-djent/|access-date=2022-11-04|website=Djent Hub|language=en-US|archive-date=2022-07-06|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20220706155225/https://djenthub.com/what-is-djent/|url-status=dead}} < /ref >  It is similar to [[math rock]]. 
 
 Fredrik Thordendal, the [[Guitar|guitarist]] for [[Meshuggah]], is known for being the first person to play djent music. < ref > {{Cite web|date=2011-03-03|title=Djent, the metal geek's microgenre|url=http://www.theguardian.com/music/2011/mar/03/djent-metal-geeks|access-date=2022-11-04|website=the Guardian|language=en}} < /ref >  Other bands that play djent music are [[Animals as Leaders]], [[Periphery (band)|Periphery]], and [[Tesseract (band)|Tesseract]].
