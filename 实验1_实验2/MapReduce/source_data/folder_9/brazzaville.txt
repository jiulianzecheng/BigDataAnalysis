 '''Brazzaville''' is the [[capital (city)|capital]] [[city]] of the [[Republic of the Congo]]. One million people live there. Brazzaville is on one side of the [[Congo river]], with [[Kinshasa]] on the other side. Brazzaville and Kinshasa are the nearest country capitals in the World.
