 '''Meppel''' is a city and a [[Municipalities of the Netherlands|municipality]] in the north east of the [[Netherlands]] (in the province [[Drenthe]]). On [[January 1]], [[2007]] it had about 31,063 [[inhabitants]]. It has a density of 558/km² (1,445.2/sq mi). The timezone in Meppel is [[Central European Time|CET]]. 
 
 ==History== 
 
 Meppel received city rights in [[1644]]. It is the oldest town of [[Drenthe]]. 
 
 In the [[World War II]] almost all the [[Jew]]s of Meppel were killed. 232 of the 250 were killed, so 18 survived it. Now there is a monument in Meppel for all those who died.
