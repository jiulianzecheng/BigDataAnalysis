 '''Pizy''' was a [[municipality]] in the [[Switzerland|Swiss]] [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Vaud]]. It was in the [[district]] of [[Morges (district)|Morges]]. 
 
 Pizy became part of the municipality of [[Aubonne, Switzerland|Aubonne]] on 1 July 2011. < ref name=Fusion > [http://www.bfs.admin.ch/bfs/portal/de/index/infothek/nomenklaturen/blank/blank/gem_liste/03.html Amtliches Gemeindeverzeichnis der Schweiz] published by the Swiss Federal Statistical Office {{in lang|de}} accessed 17 February 2011 < /ref >
