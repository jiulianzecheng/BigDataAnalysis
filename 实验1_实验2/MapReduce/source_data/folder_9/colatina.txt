 '''Colatina''' is an [[Brazil]]ian city in the state of [[Espírito Santo]]. In 2020, 123,400 people lived there and its area is 1,398 & nbsp;km². It is on the [[Doce River]] and is 127 & nbsp;km north of [[Vitória, Espírito Santo|Vitória]], the state's capital.
