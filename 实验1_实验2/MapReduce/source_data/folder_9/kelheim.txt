 '''Kelheim''' ({{IPA-de|ˈkeːlhaɪ̯m|lang|De-Kelheim.ogg}}) is a town and municipality in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. It is the capital of the [[Kelheim (district)|district Kelheim]] and is at the convergence of the rivers [[Altmühl]] and [[Danube]]. Kelheim has a population of around 15,750.
