'''Rapperswil''' is the name of three places in Switzerland: 
 * [[Rapperswil-Jona]], a municipality in See-Gaster in the canton of St. Gallen in Switzerland. 
 * [[Rapperswil, St. Gallen]], a town in the municipality of Rapperswil-Jona. 
 * [[Rapperswil, Bern]], a municipality in the Aarberg district (today Seeland administrative district) in the canton of Bern in Switzerland.
