'''Gynecology''' (also spelled '''gynaecology''') is the part of [[medicine]] that deals with the [[female]] [[reproductive system]] ([[vagina]], [[uterus]], [[ovary|ovaries]]) and [[breast]]s. Outside medicine, it means  " the science of [[women]]. "
