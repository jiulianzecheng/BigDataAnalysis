A '''subdivision''', '''real estate development''' or '''tract''' is a large piece of land and divided into land lots. Subdivisions are usually [[residential]] and typically have [[deed restriction]]s and sometimes [[homeowners association]]s or their equivalent. When residential, subdivisions are often called '''housing developments''', '''housing estates''', etc.
