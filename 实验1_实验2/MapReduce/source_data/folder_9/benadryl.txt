'''Benadryl''' is a [[brand]] name for a number of different [[medication]]s sold by [[Johnson  &  Johnson]] to relieve [[allergy|allergies]] and [[cough]]s. It usually contains [[antihistamine]] [[diphenhydramine]]. In the United Kingdom, it contains either the antihistamine [[acrivastine]] or the long-acting antihistamine [[cetirizine]].
