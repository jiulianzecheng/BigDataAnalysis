 '''Jaun''' ({{lang-fr|Bellegarde}}) is a [[municipality]] of the district of [[Gruyère (district)|Gruyère]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Fribourg (canton)|Fribourg]] in [[Switzerland]]. It is the only [[German language|German]]-speaking municipality in Gruyère.
