 '''Sorvilier''' is a [[municipality]] of the [[administrative district]] [[Jura bernois (administrative district)|Jura bernois]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Bern (canton)|Bern]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 The municipality is in the Tavannes valley on both sides of the [[Birs]] river.
