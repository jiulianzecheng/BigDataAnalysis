 '''Beachbody LLC''' is an [[United States|American]] [[multinational corporation]] and pyramid scheme. It sells [[fitness]], [[weight loss]] and [[Bodybuilding|muscle-building]] home-[[exercise]] [[DVD]]s. < ref > {{cite web|last=Rovell|first=Darren|title=Beachbody Grows Exponentially Thanks To Network Marketing|url=https://www.cnbc.com/id/41357825/Beachbody_Grows_Exponentially_Thanks_To_Network_Marketing|publisher=CNBC|access-date=2012-04-01|archive-date=2012-10-13|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20121013024930/http://www.cnbc.com/id/41357825/Beachbody_Grows_Exponentially_Thanks_To_Network_Marketing|url-status=dead}} < /ref >  The company's best-known product is [[Tony Horton]]'s [[P90X]]. Its television advertisements have become some of the most frequently shown [[infomercial]]s in the [[United States]]. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.cnbc.com/id/41357825 |title=News Headlines |publisher=Cnbc.com |date=2011-01-31 |access-date=2011-02-14}} < /ref >  American fitness coach, Shaun T. was signed by Beachbody to create the  " most intense and [[wikt:insane|insane]] workout ever put on DVD " .
