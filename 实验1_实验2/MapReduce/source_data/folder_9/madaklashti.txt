 '''Madaklashti''' (مدگلاشتی) is a variety of [[Persian language|Persian]] spoken in the Madaklasht valley of [[Chitral District|Chitral]], [[Pakistan]]. It is spoken by the [[Tajik people]] and is almost identical to [[Tajik language|Tajik]] and [[Dari]] varities spoken in neighboring [[Badakhshan]]. < ref > https://www.researchgate.net/project/Madaklashti-Language < /ref >
