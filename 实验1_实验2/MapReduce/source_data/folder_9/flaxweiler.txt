 '''Flaxweiler''' ({{lang-lb|Fluessweiler}}) is a [[Communes of Luxembourg|commune]] and town in southeastern [[Luxembourg]]. It is part of the [[Grevenmacher (canton)|canton of Grevenmacher]]. 
 
 As of 2019, 2,145 people live here.
