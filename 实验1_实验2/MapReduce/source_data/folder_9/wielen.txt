 '''Wielen''' is a small village in the [[Grafschaft Bentheim|County of Bentheim]] in [[Lower Saxony]], [[Germany]], and belongs to the Joint Community (''Samtgemeinde'') of [[Uelsen]]. 
 
 ==Constituent communities== 
 Along with the namesake ''[[Ortsteil]]'', there are outlying centres named Balderhaar, Striepe and Vennebrügge.
