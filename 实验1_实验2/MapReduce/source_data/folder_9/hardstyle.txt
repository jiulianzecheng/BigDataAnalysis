 '''Hardstyle''' is a kind of [[electronic dance music]] (EDM). < ref > {{Cite web|url=https://www.q-dance.com/en/static/hardstyle|title=Hardstyle|publisher=[[Q-dance]]|access-date=15 September 2018}} < /ref >  It mixes [[Hard trance]] and [[hardcore]] styles of EDM. Hardstyle started in the late 1990s to early 2000s in [[the Netherlands]]. It has developed new kinds of hardstyle over the years.  " [[Euphoria|Euphoric]] hardstyle "  contains [[Melody|melodies]] and often [[vocals]] tell stories. Famous hardstyle artists include [[The Prophet (musician)|The Prophet]], [[Headhunterz]], [[Brennan Heart]] and D-Block  &  S-te-Fan. 
 
 There are many hardstyle [[Music festival|festivals]] around the world. [[Defqon.1]] is held every year in the Netherlands and [[Sydney]], [[New South Wales]], [[Australia]]. < ref > {{Cite web|url=https://www.q-dance.com/en/events/defqon-1/|title=Defqon.1|publisher=[[Q-dance]]|access-date=15 September 2018}} < /ref >  [[Qlimax]] is also held in the Netherlands yearly.
