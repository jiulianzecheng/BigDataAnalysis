 '''PT Aplikasi Karya Anak Bangsa''' (most commonly known as Gojek) is a [[Jakarta]] based [[On-demand|on-demand service]] platform.  < ref > {{Cite web|last=Yudhianto|title=Go-Jek Buka Kantor di India, Karya Anak Bangsa atau Bangalore?|url=https://inet.detik.com/business/d-3347971/go-jek-buka-kantor-di-india-karya-anak-bangsa-atau-bangalore|access-date=2020-08-14|website=detikinet|language=id}} < /ref >  It also does digital payments. Gojek was started in [[Indonesia]] in 2009 as a [[call center]]. It connects consumers to [[courier delivery]] and two-wheeled ride-hailing services. Gojek launched its application in 2015 with only four services: GoRide, GoSend, GoShop, and GoFood. It [[Fundraising|raised]] over $1 billion 2019  and is now valued at US$10 billion. < ref > {{Cite web|date=2019-02-02|title=Gojek raises over US$1 billion as ride-hailing giants beef up their portfolios|url=https://www.businesstimes.com.sg/garage/gojek-raises-over-us1-billion-as-ride-hailing-giants-beef-up-their-portfolios|access-date=2020-08-14|website=The Business Times|language=en}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite web|last=Salna|first=Karlis|date=|title=Go-Jek Joins 'Decacorn' Ranks With $10 Billion Valuation|url=https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2019-04-06/indonesia-s-go-jek-joins-decacorn-ranks-with-10b-valuation|url-status=live|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190429144049/https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2019-04-06/indonesia-s-go-jek-joins-decacorn-ranks-with-10b-valuation|archive-date=2019-04-29|access-date=2020-08-14|website=www.bloomberg.com}} < /ref >  Gojek has turned into a Super App. It now provides more than 20 services.
