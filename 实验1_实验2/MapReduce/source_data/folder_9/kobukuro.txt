 }}'''Kobukuro''' is a [[Japan]]ese male [[pop music|pop]] [[band]].  
 
 == History == 
 === The name === 
 The name is a mix of the two members' [[family name]]s, Kentaro Kobuchi and Shunsuke Kuroda.  
 
 === Origins === 
 In 1998, the two met each other in [[Sakai]] and formed Kobukuro.  
 
 === Albums === 
 Kobukuro received moderately successful ratings for their first three [[indie (music)|indie]] [[album]]s - ''[[Saturday 8:PM]]'' ([[July]] [[1999]]), ''[[Root of My Mind]]'' ([[March]] [[2001]]), and ''''ANSWER'''' ([[December]] [[2000]]). Kobukuro signed with [[Warner Music Japan]] in [[2001]], and made their debut with the hit single, ''Yell'' which reached #4 on the [[Oricon Charts]]. Their newest album is called ''5296'' (December [[2007]]). 
 
 Kobukuro's new single is “STAY”.
