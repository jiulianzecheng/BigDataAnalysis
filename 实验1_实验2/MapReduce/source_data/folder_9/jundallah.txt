'''Jundallah''' (or '''Jondallah''', translates to '''Soldiers of God'''), or '''People's resistance movement of Iran''' (PRMI) is a militant organisation based in [[Balochistan, Pakistan|Balochistan]]. The organisation claims to be fighting for the rights of [[Sunni]] [[muslim]]s in [[Iran]]. Most muslims in Iran are [[Shi'a]], Sunni make up the largest minority group that is not Shi'a. Iran says the group is a [[terrorism|terrorist organisation]].
