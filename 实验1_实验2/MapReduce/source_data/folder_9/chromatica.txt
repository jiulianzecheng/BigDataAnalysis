 '''''Chromatica''''' is the sixth studio album by American recording artist [[Lady Gaga]]. It was originally set for release on April 10, 2020, but was postponed by Gaga for later that year. This is due to the worldwide [[2019‚Äì20 coronavirus pandemic|coronavirus (COVID-19) pandemic]]. < ref > {{Cite web|url=https://mobile.twitter.com/ladygaga/status/1242482502775660546|title=Twitter|website=mobile.twitter.com|access-date=2020-03-24}} < /ref >  Gaga later revealed that the album would be released on May 29, 2020. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.facebook.com/10376464573/posts/10158596283419574/|access-date=2020-05-06|website=Facebook|title=The journey continues.  ==Tracks== 
 On April 22, 2020, Gaga revealed the tracklist for the album's standard edition, which has 16 songs. < ref > {{Cite web|url=https://www.instagram.com/p/B_TB1Q9lvbi/|title=Lady Gaga on Instagram:  " #Chromatica ‚öîÔ∏èüíì #LG6 " |website=Instagram|language=en|access-date=2020-05-23}} < /ref >  The [[Target Corporation|Target]] and deluxe international versions of the album contain 19 tracks. < ref > {{Cite web|url=https://www.target.com/p/lady-gaga-chromatica-target-exclusive-cd/-/A-79694377|title=Lady Gaga - Chromatica (Target Exclusive, CD)|website=www.target.com|language=en|access-date=2020-05-30}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite web|url=https://store.hmv.com/store/music/cd/tbc-(1)|title=Chromatica {{!}} CD Album {{!}} Free shipping over ¬£20|website=HMV Store|language=en-GB|access-date=2020-05-30}} < /ref > 
 
 The album's lead single,  " [[Stupid Love (Lady Gaga song)|Stupid Love]] " , was leaked in January 2020. < ref > {{Cite web|url=https://www.thehits.co.nz/spy/lady-gaga-s-new-song-stupid-love-just-leaked-online-and-her-fans-are-loving-it/|title=Lady Gaga's new song 'Stupid Love' just leaked online and her fans are loving it|last=Hits|first=The|website=The Hits|language=en|access-date=2020-03-24}} < /ref >  The song was officially released on February 28, 2020, reaching the top ten in the US, UK, Ireland, Switzerland, Australia, and Canada. < ref > {{Cite web|url=https://acharts.co/song/128252|title=Stupid Love by Lady GaGa - Music Charts|website=acharts.co|access-date=2020-03-24}} < /ref >   " [[Rain on Me (Lady Gaga and Ariana Grande song)|Rain on Me]] " , with [[Ariana Grande]], was released as the album's second single on May 22, 2020. It entered at the top of the [[Billboard Hot 100|''Billboard'' Hot 100]], < ref > {{Cite magazine|url=http://www.billboard.com/articles/business/chart-beat/9394719/rain-on-me-debuts-atop-hot-100-lady-gaga-ariana-grande|title='Rain On Me' Debuts Atop Hot 100|date=2020-06-01|magazine=Billboard|access-date=2020-06-04}} < /ref >  [[UK Singles Chart]] < ref > {{Cite magazine|url=http://www.billboard.com/articles/business/chart-beat/9393188/rain-on-me-no-1-uk|title='Rain On Me' Is No 1 In The UK|date=2020-05-29|magazine=Billboard|access-date=2020-05-30}} < /ref >  and [[Irish Singles Chart]] < ref > {{Cite web|url=https://www.officialcharts.com/chart-news/lady-gaga-and-ariana-grande-claim-number-1-on-official-irish-singles-chart-score-biggest-opening-week-of-2020-with-rain-on-me__29880/|title=Lady Gaga  &  Ariana Grande claim Irish Number 1 single with Rain On Me|website=www.officialcharts.com|language=en|access-date=2020-05-30}} < /ref >  and at number two in Australia < ref > {{Cite web|url=https://headlineplanet.com/home/2020/05/30/dababy-roddy-ricchs-rockstar-remains-1-in-australia-lady-gaga-ariana-grandes-rain-on-me-debuts-at-2/|title=DaBaby  &  Roddy Ricch's 'ROCKSTAR' Remains #1 In Australia, Lady Gaga  &  Ariana Grande's 'Rain On Me' Debuts At #2|date=2020-05-30|website=Headline Planet|language=en-US|access-date=2020-05-30}} < /ref >  and New Zealand. < ref > {{Cite web|url=https://nztop40.co.nz/|title=The Official New Zealand Music Chart|website=THE OFFICIAL NZ MUSIC CHART|language=en|access-date=2020-05-30}} < /ref >   " Rain on Me "  became Gaga's fifth and Grande's fourth US number-one.{{Citation needed|date=June 2020}} 
 
 " Sour Candy " , with South Korean girl group [[Blackpink]], was released as the album's first promotional single a day before the album's release. < ref > {{Cite web|url=https://ew.com/music/lady-gaga-blackpink-sour-candy/|title=Hear Lady Gaga, BLACKPINK unwrap 'Sour Candy' in new dance anthem|website=EW.com|language=EN|access-date=2020-05-30}} < /ref > 
 
 The album entered at number one on the US, UK, Australian, and Dutch album charts and the top three in Belgium. < ref > {{Cite magazine|url=http://www.billboard.com/articles/business/chart-beat/9397002/lady-gaga-scores-uk-year-best-opening-week-chromatica|title=Lady Gaga Scores UK's Year Best Opening Week|date=2020-06-05|magazine=Billboard|access-date=2020-06-06}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite web|url=https://australian-charts.com/showitem.asp?interpret=Lady+Gaga & titel=Chromatica & cat=a|title=australian-charts.com - Lady Gaga - Chromatica|website=australian-charts.com|access-date=2020-06-06}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite web|url=https://www.dailymail.co.uk/tvshowbiz/article-8394775/Korean-girl-group-Blackpink-make-history-Australias-ARIA-charts.html|title=Korean girl group Blackpink make history on Australia's ARIA charts|last=Peterson|first=Jacques|date=2020-06-06|website=Mail Online|access-date=2020-06-08}} < /ref >  In the US, it became Gaga's sixth number-one album. It earned 274,000 copies sold in its first week in America, giving Gaga her best debut week since ''[[Born This Way]]'' (2011) there. Gaga also earned six number-one albums faster than any other female in ''Billboard'' history. < ref > {{Cite magazine|url=http://www.billboard.com/articles/business/chart-beat/9397503/lady-gaga-chromatica-sixth-number-1-album-billboard-200|title=Lady Gaga Earns Sixth No. 1 Album on Billboard 200 Chart With 'Chromatica'|date=2020-06-07|magazine=Billboard|access-date=2020-06-08}} < /ref >
