According to the [[Bible]], '''Terah''' was the son of [[Nahor (son of Serug)|Nahor]] and the father of [[Abraham]], [[Nahor (son of Terah)|Nahor II]], and [[Haran]]. He died at the age of 205 in the city of [[Haran (city)|Haran]]. He and his children were from the [[Sumer|Sumerian]] city of [[Ur]].{{Religion-stub}}
