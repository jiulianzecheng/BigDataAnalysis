" '''Dideba''' "  ({{lang-ka|დიდება}}, {{IPA-ka|di.dɛ.bɑ}};  " Glory " ) was the [[national anthem]] of [[Georgia (country)|Georgia]] from 1990 to 2004. It was also used from 1918 to 1921. From 1921 to 1990, the [[anthem of the Georgian SSR]] was used. Since 2004,  " [[Tavisupebla]] "  ( " Freedom " ) has been used.
