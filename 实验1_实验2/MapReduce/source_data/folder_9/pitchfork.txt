 A '''pitchfork''' is a [[tool]] used in farming. It is similar to a [[garden fork]], made of [[metal]] or another hard material, with three or more pointed ends. Its long [[Handle (grip)|handle]], made of wood or hard plastic, is for use while standing. It is usually about the same size as a [[shovel]]. It is used to move [[hay]] and other materials.
