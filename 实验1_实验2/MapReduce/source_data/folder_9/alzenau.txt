 '''Alzenau''' ({{IPA-de|ˈaltsənaʊ|lang|Alzenau.ogg}}; until 31 December 2006 officially ''Alzenau i.UFr.'') is a town in the north of the [[Aschaffenburg (district)|Aschaffenburg district]] in the ''[[Regierungsbezirk]]'' of [[Lower Franconia]] (''Unterfranken'') in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. Until 1 July 1972, Alzenau was the district seat of the now ended district of the same name and has a population of around 19,000. < ref name= " :0 " > {{Cite web|url=http://www.alzenau.de/B%C3%BCrger/Leben-in-Alzenau/Stadtportrait-Unsere-Stadt-/Zahlen-Fakten|title=Zahlen  &  Fakten / Stadt Alzenau|last=GmbH|first=Advantic Systemhaus|website=alzenau.de|language=de-DE|access-date=30 October 2017}} < /ref > 
 
 == Neighbouring communities == 
 Alzenau borders in the north on the communities of [[Rodenbach, Hesse|Rodenbach]] and [[Freigericht]], in the east and southeast on the communities of [[Mömbris]] and [[Johannesberg, Bavaria|Johannesberg]], in the southwest on the community of [[Karlstein am Main|Karlstein]] and in the west on the community of [[Kahl am Main]]. 
 
 == Constituent communities == 
 [[File:Stadt Alzenau.svg|thumb|Constituent communities]] 
 Alzenau's [[Quarter (urban subdivision)|quarters]] are Albstadt, Hörstein, Kälberau, Michelbach and Wasserlos. < ref > {{Cite web|url=http://www.alzenau.de/B%C3%BCrger/Leben-in-Alzenau/Stadtportrait-Unsere-Stadt-/Stadtteile|title=Stadtteile / Stadt Alzenau|last=GmbH|first=Advantic Systemhaus|website=alzenau.de|language=de-DE|access-date=30 October 2017}} < /ref >
