 == Acerola Cherry == 
 '''Acerola''' ''([[:en:Malpighia_emarginata|Malpighia emarginata]]),'' also known as Guarani cherry, Barbados Cherry, West Indian Cherry, or Wild Crepe Myrtle is a red fruit that looks a lot like a [[cherry]]. The Acerola plants are Evergreen tropical shrubs that originated in [[:en:Yucatán_Peninsula|Yucatán]] but can be found as far north as [[:en:Texas|Texas]] as well as in subtropical areas of [[:en:Asia|Asia]].[[File:Malpighia glabra blossom and unripe fruits.jpg|thumb|A image of unripe Acerola and Acerola flowers]] 
 [[File:Malpighia emarginata 2013 002.JPG|thumb|A bowl of Acerola fruit]] 
 == Benefits == 
 There Is one reported and scientifically proven benefit from consumption of Acerola fruits. One thing that is often brought up is the very high [[:en:Vitamin_C|Vitamin C]] (Ascorbic Acid) content, which is proven to have a beneficial effects on skin, hair, and immune health as well as preventing [[Scurvy]].
