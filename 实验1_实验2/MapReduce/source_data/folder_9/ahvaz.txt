'''Ahvaz''' ({{lang-fa|اهواز|Ahvāz}}) is a city in southwestern [[Iran]]. It is the capital of [[Khuzestan Province]]. In the year 2016, about 1,350,000 people lived there. In 2018, 30 people [[Ahvaz military parade attack|were killed]] in a [[mass shooting]]. The city is majority [[Arab people|Arab]] with a significant [[Persian people|Persian]] minority.
