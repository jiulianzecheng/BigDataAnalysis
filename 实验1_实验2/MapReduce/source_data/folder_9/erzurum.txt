 '''Erzurum''' is a [[city]] in eastern [[Anatolia]], [[Turkey]]. It is the capital and largest city in [[Erzurum Province]]. The 2000 [[census]] showed 361,235 people lived there with a [[Kurdish people|Kurdish]] minority based on the south side. The number of people increased to 367,250 by 2010.
