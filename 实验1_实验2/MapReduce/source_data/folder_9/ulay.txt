 '''Ulay''' (born '''Frank Uwe Laysiepen'''; 30 November 1943 – 2 March 2020) was a [[Germans|German]] [[contemporary art]]ist. He is best known for his worldwide for his [[Polaroid SX-70|Polaroid]] art and [[performance art]] together with longtime companion [[Marina Abramović]]. He was based in the Dutch capital of [[Amsterdam]] and [[Ljubljana]], the capital of [[Slovenia]]. 
 
 Ulay was born in [[Solingen]]. He dated Marina Abramović from 1976 to 1988. On 2 March 2020, Ulay died in Ljubljana of [[lymphoma]] at the age of 76. < ref > {{Cite news|url=https://www.monopol-magazin.de/performancekuenstler-ulay-gestorben|title=Performancekünstler Ulay ist tot|date=2 March 2020|access-date=2 March 2020|lang=de|work=Monopol}} < /ref >
