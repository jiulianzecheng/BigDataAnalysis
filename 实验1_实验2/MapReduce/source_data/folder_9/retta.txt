 '''Marietta Sangai Sirleaf''' (born April 12, 1970), professionally known as '''Retta''', is an American stand-up comedian and actress. She is best known for her roles as Donna Meagle on the [[NBC]] sitcom ''[[Parks and Recreation]]'' and Ruby Hill on NBC's comedy-drama ''Good Girls''.
