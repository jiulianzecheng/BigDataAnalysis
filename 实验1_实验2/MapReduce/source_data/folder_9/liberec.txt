 '''Liberec''' ({{IPA|ˈlɪbɛrɛt͡s}}, {{lang-de|Reichenberg}}) is the fifth-largest [[city]] in the [[Czech Republic]]. It is on the [[Lusatian Neisse]] and surrounded by the [[Jizera Mountains]] and [[Ještěd-Kozákov Ridge]]. It has a population of 100 000. It was first mentioned in 1352.
