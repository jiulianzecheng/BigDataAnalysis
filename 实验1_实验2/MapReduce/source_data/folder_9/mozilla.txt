'''Mozilla''' can mean: 
 * the codename for [[Netscape Navigator]] 
 * the mascot and name of the [[Mozilla Foundation]] 
 ** the name of its now defunct [[Mozilla (web browser)]] 
 
 == Mozilla software == 
 It can also refer to the platform (“Gecko” and other things) on which these programs are built: 
 
 * [[Mozilla Firefox]] 
 * [[Mozilla Thunderbird]] 
 * [[SeaMonkey]] 
 * [[Camino]] 
 * [[Mozilla Sunbird]] 
 * [[Minimo]] 
 * [[Flock (web browser)]] 
 * [[Songbird (software)]] 
 
 Mozilla Foundation also makes [[Bugzilla]].
