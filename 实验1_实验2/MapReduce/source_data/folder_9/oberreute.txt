 '''Oberreute''' is a municipality in the district of [[Lindau (district)|Lindau]] in [[Bavaria]] in [[Germany]] and member of the municipal association of [[Stiefenhofen]]. It is an air health resort. Oberreute is in the [[Allgäu]] region.
