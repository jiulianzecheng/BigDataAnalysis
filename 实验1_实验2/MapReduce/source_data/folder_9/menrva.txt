 '''Menrva''' is the [[Etruscan mythology|Etruscan]] [[Deity|goddess]] of [[war]], [[art]], [[wisdom]] and [[health]]. She was often associated with the [[Greek mythology|Greek]] goddess [[Athena]]. < ref > The process, by analogy with ''[[interpretatio graeca]]'', was termed ''interpretatio etrusca'' by L.B. van der Meer, ''Interpretatio Etrusca': Greek Myths on Etruscan Mirrors''(Amsterdam) 1995. < /ref >  She was the precursor of the Roman [[Minerva]].
