 '''Lucius Junius Moderatus Columella''' (4 – 70 AD) was a famous Roman writer on agriculture. < ref > Silke Diederich (2016). ''Columella''. Oxford Bibliographies Online. Oxford: Oxford University Press. < /ref >  His De re rustica forms an important source on agriculture of Roman Empire. A genus of the Peruvian asterid ''Columellia'' was named in his honor. < ref > Joseph Fr. Michaud, Eugène Ernest Desplaces, Louis Gabriel Michaud (1854). ''Biographie universelle (Michaud) ancienne et moderne...'' Paris: Madame C. Desplaces. Volume 8, page 668 < /ref >
