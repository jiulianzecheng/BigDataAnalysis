 '''''Desperado''''' is a [[1995]] action [[Thriller (genre)|thriller]] movie. It was released on August 25, 1995 in the [[United States]]. It stars [[Antonio Banderas]] and [[Salma Hayek]]. Banderas plays a man who wants revenge on those who killed his former lover. ''Desperado'' received positive reviews by the critics.
