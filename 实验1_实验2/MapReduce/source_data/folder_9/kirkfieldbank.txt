 '''Kirkfieldbank''' is a [[village]] in [[South Lanarkshire]], [[Scotland]]. It is close to [[Lanark]]. They, along with [[New Lanark]], participate in the [[Lanark]] [[Lanark Lanimers|Lanimers]]. They have one primary school, Kirkfieldbank primary.
