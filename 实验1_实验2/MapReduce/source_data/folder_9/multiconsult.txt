'''Multiconsult''' is a company that provides engineering consultancy services. They have 2800 employees who work in Norway, Europe, and other parts of the world. Multiconsult is listed on the [[Oslo Stock Exchange]]. They have their main office in [[Oslo]] and also have offices in other Norwegian cities. Multiconsult also operates in Europe, Africa, and Asia. In 2015, they acquired Link Arkitektur, which is one of the biggest architecture firms in [[Scandinavia]]. 
 
 == History == 
 Multiconsult started with the founding of Norsk Vandbygningskontor (NVK) in 1908. NVK merged with Multiconsult in 2003. The name Multiconsult comes from when Sivilingeniørene Apeland  &  Mjøset AS was reorganized in 1974, and Stiftelsen Multiconsult became a major shareholder.
