 '''Ilanz''' ({{lang-rm|Glion}}) is a [[town]] and former [[municipality]] of the [[district]] of [[Surselva (district)|Surselva]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Graub체nden]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 On 1 January 2014, the former municipalities of Ilanz, [[Castrisch]], [[Ladir]], [[Luven]], [[Pitasch]], [[Riein]], [[Ruschein]], [[Schnaus]], [[Sevgein]], [[Duvin]], [[Pigniu]], [[Rueun]] and [[Siat]] merged into the new municipality of [[Ilanz/Glion]].
