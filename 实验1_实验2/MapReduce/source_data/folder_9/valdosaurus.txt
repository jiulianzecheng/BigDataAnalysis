 '''''Valdosaurus''''' ( " Weald Lizard " ) is a [[genus|type]] of dinosaur. It [[bipedal|walked on two legs]] and [[herbivorous|ate plants]].  It was found on the [[Isle of Wight]] and other places in [[England]]. It lived during the [[Lower Cretaceous]]. 
 
 It is part of the [[Dryosauridae]] [[Family (biology)|family]]. It is related to the [[Iguanodon]]ts.
