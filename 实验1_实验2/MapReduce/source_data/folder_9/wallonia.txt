 '''Wallonia''' ({{lang-fr|Wallonie}}, {{lang-de|Wallonie(n)}}, {{lang-nl|Wallonië}}, {{lang-lb|Wallounien}}, {{lang-pcd|Walonnie}}, {{lang-wa|Walon(r)eye}}) or the '''Walloon Region''' (French: ''Région wallonne'') is a [[French language|French-speaking]] region in the south of [[Belgium]]. Its official languages are [[French language|French]] (98%) and [[German language|German]] (2%). Many people understand the local language [[Walloon language|Walloon]], some still speak it, and few people write it. German is spoken mainly in the area of the [[German-speaking Community of Belgium]]. 
 
 The capital of the region is [[Namur (city)|Namur]]. Other important cities are: [[Liège (city)|Liège]], [[Charleroi]], [[Mons]], [[Tournai]] and [[Verviers]]. 
 
 French speaking Walloons share the [[French community]] with French speaking people of Brussels. 
 
 Current Minister-President of the [[Walloon Government]] is [[Elio Di Rupo]].
