[[File:FAMAS-G2-IMG 8894-white.jpg|thumb|'''FAMAS G2'''.]] 
 The '''FAMAS''' ('''F'''usil d''''A'''ssaut de la '''M'''anufacture d''''A'''rmes de '''S'''aint-Étienne, in English  " Assault Rifle from the [[Saint-Étienne]] [[Weapon]] Factory " ) < ref > {{in lang|fr}}http://www.nolasco.fr/catalogue/FAMAS.htm < /ref >  is a bullpup-styled [[assault rifle]] designed and manufactured in [[France]]. < ref > {{in lang|fr}}http://www.defense.gouv.fr/terre/equipements/armement-individuel-et-collectif/famas < /ref >  It's the main assault rifle of the [[French Army]], [[French Air Force]], and [[French Navy]] since [[1980]]. It was also sold for [[export]]. 
 
 Designed in [[1973]] FAMAS uses a [[5.56mm NATO]] (.223) [[Calibre|caliber]] cartridge. [[Muzzle velocity]] is between 925[[metre|m]]/[[Second|s]] and 960[[metre|m]]/[[Second|s]] following variants. 
 
 == Variants == 
 [[File:FAMAS-F1 vs G2.svg|thumb|'''FAMAS F1'''  &  '''FAMAS G2'''.]] 
 === Civil === 
 * '''FAMAS  " civil " ''', built between [[1982]] and [[1992]] for [[security]] and protection [[company|companies]]. 
 
 === Military === 
 * '''FAMAS F1''', built between [[1979]] and [[1992]] for French militaries. 
 ** '''FAMAS Commando''', variant of F1 specially designed for [[special forces]], shorter than original. 
 ** '''FAMAS Export''', variant of F1 specially designed for foreign customers. 
 * '''FAMAS G1''', alternatively transition built in [[1995]] to prepare for the arrival of French troops in the G2. 
 * '''FAMAS G2''', new generation more light, and using special weapons. 
 ** '''FAMAS FELIN''', variant of G2 specially designed for [[town|urban]] fights and close [[sniper|sniping]] .
