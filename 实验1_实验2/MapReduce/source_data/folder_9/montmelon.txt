 '''Montmelon''' was a [[municipality]] in the [[district]] of [[Porrentruy (district)|Porrentruy]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Jura (canton)|Jura]] in [[Switzerland]]. Since 1 January 2009, it is a part of the new municipality [[Clos du Doubs]]. 
 
 The municipality is made of several [[village]]s and of many spread out farms. The municipality has about 100 people living there, many of them are farmers.
