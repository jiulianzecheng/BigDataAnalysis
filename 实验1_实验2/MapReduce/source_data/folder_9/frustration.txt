In [[psychology]], '''frustration''' is a common [[emotion|emotional]] way to act when things go wrong. It is a feeling of being [[anger|angry]] and [[sad]] when something one wants or wishes for does not happen.   
 
 In [[physics]], '''frustration''' (or '''geometrical frustration''') is a property of water [[molecule]]s in ice when a freezing [[crystal]] is formed. The presence of random or conflicting [[atom]]s stop the ice from being completely frozen. It is called  " water ice " .
