 '''Bruges''' (Dutch: ''Brugge'') is a city in the northwest of [[Belgium]].  
 
 It is the capital and largest city of [[West Flanders]]. In 2007, 116,982 people lived there. < ref > {{Cite web |url=http://statbel.fgov.be/downloads/pop200701com.xls |title=Werkelijke bevolking per gemeente op 1 januari 2007 / Population de droit par commune au 1 janvier 2007 (.xls) |access-date=2008-07-15 |archive-date=2008-11-23 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20081123144002/http://statbel.fgov.be/downloads/pop200701com.xls |url-status=dead }} < /ref > 
 
 Bruges is at 51° 12 North, 03° 13 East. < ref > {{Cite web |url=http://statbel.fgov.be/pub/d1/p120_nl.xls |title=Geografische coördinaten van de gemeenten (.xls) |access-date=2008-07-15 |archive-date=2004-07-23 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20040723190930/http://statbel.fgov.be/pub/d1/p120_nl.xls |url-status=dead }} < /ref > 
 
 It is a centre of tourism because of its historic medieval city centre. The centre was named a [[World Heritage Site]] by [[UNESCO]]. < ref name= " wh " > {{cite web|title=Historic Centre of Brugge|url=http://whc.unesco.org/en/list/996|publisher=UNESCO|accessdate=9 May 2017}} < /ref >  It is a center of commerce because of its port, [[Zeebrugge]]. The movie ''[[In Bruges]]'' with [[Colin Farrell]], [[Brendan Gleeson]] and [[Ralph Fiennes]] was made there in 2008.
