 '''Myra''' is an ancient town in [[Lycia]]. The small town of Kale ([[Demre]]) is near it today. It is in [[Antalya Province]] of [[Turkey]].  It was on the [[river Myros]] (Demre Çay), in the fertile [[Alluvial fan|alluvial plain]] between Alaca Dağ, the Massikytos range and the [[Aegean Sea]]. 
 
 [[Nicholas of Myra]] (also known as ''Saint Nicholas'') did many things there.
