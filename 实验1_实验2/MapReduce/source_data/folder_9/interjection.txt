An '''interjection''' is a spoken or written expression of [[emotion]] like  " Wow! "  for surprise,  " Ouch! "  for [[pain]], or  " Damn! "  for [[anger]], or  " Argh! "  for [[frustration]]. These phrases can stand alone, and are usually capitalized and [[punctuation|punctuated]].
