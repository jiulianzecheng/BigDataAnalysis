'''Derajat''' is a region in central [[Pakistan]], where the provinces of [[Punjab (Pakistan)|Punjab]], [[Khyber Pakhtunkhwa]], and [[Balochistan (Pakistan)|Balochistan]] meet. < ref > {{Cite web|date=2007-12-02|title=About Punjab - TDCP, The Official Site of Tourism Development Corporation of Punjab|url=http://tdcp.punjab.gov.pk/TDCP/about_punjab.htm|access-date=2023-12-14|website=web.archive.org|archive-date=2007-12-02|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20071202183134/http://tdcp.punjab.gov.pk/TDCP/about_punjab.htm|url-status=bot: unknown}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite web|date=2007-11-17|title=Welcome to NWFP Government Official Web Portal : People and Culture|url=http://www.nwfp.gov.pk/AIS-page.php?DistId=1 & DeptId=1 & LanId=1 & pageName=NWFP-PeopleCulture|access-date=2023-12-14|website=web.archive.org|archive-date=2007-11-17|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20071117134513/http://www.nwfp.gov.pk/AIS-page.php?DistId=1 & DeptId=1 & LanId=1 & pageName=NWFP-PeopleCulture|url-status=dead}} < /ref >  Derajat is bounded by the [[Indus River]] to the east, and the [[Sulaiman Mountains]] to the west. 
 
 Main cities in derajat are [[Dera Ghazi Khan District|Dera Ghazi Khan]] in southwestern Punjab, [[Dera Ismail Khan District|Dera Ismail Khan]] (southeastern Khyber Pakhtunkhwa), and [[Dera Bughti District|Dera Bugti]], [[Dera Allah Yar]] and [[Dera Murad Jamali]] in Balochistan.
