'''Technorati''' was a [[search engine]] for [[weblog]]s.  As of August 2006, Technorati [[Index (search engine)|indexed]] over 55 million sites.  
 
 Technorati was founded by [[Dave Sifry]] and its headquarters was in [[San Francisco, California]], USA.
