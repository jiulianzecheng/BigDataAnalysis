 '''Villeparisis''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is in [[Île-de-France]] in the [[Seine-et-Marne]] [[Departments of France|department]] in north-central [[France]]. It is in the northeastern [[suburb]]s of [[Paris]], {{convert|21.8|km|mi|abbr=on}} from the center. 26,471 people lived here in 2018.
