'''Garhajis''' is a clan made up of Habr Yunis and Eidagale who live in [[Somaliand,Ethiopia,Kenya and Djibouti]]. They border the habar Awal tribe to the west, the [[Warsangeli]] to the East and the [[reer darawiish]] to the southeast and [[ogaden]] to the south.
