'''Abu Al Qasim Hasan ibn Ahmad Unsuri Balkhi''' (d. [[1039]]/[[1040]]) ({{lang-fa|ابوالقاسم حسن بن احمد عنصری بلخی}}) or '''Unsuri''' was a [[Persian people|Persian]] [[poet]]. He was born in [[Balkh]], in modern [[Afghanistan]]. He became the poet of [[Sultan]] [[Mahmud Ghaznavi]]. Mahmud Ghaznavi called him  " King of Poets " . All of his poems are in [[Persian language|Persian]]. His poems are collected in a [[Divan]].
