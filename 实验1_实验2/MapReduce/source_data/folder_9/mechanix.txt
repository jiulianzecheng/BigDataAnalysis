 " '''Mechanix''' "  is a song by [[Megadeth]], an American [[thrash metal]] band. It is the last song from their first [[Album|studio album]], ''[[Killing Is My Business... and Business Is Good!|Killing Is My Business... And Business Is Good!]]''. 
 
 The song was written by [[Dave Mustaine]]. He made it for [[Metallica]], a band that he was in. After Metallica fired Mustaine, he told the band to not use any of the music that he made, including  " Mechanix " . < ref > {{Cite web|last=Blabbermouth|date=2022-08-23|title=DAVE MUSTAINE Says He Was 'Bothered' By The Fact That METALLICA Used His Music After His Departure|url=https://blabbermouth.net/news/dave-mustaine-says-he-was-bothered-by-the-fact-that-metallica-used-his-music-after-his-departure|access-date=2022-09-18|website=BLABBERMOUTH.NET|language=en}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite web|date=2022-08-24|title=Dave Mustaine Says Kirk Hammett Got His Royalties For Metallica's 'Metal Militia' For Many Years|url=https://rockcelebrities.net/dave-mustaine-says-kirk-hammett-got-his-royalties-for-metallicas-metal-militia-for-many-years/|access-date=2022-09-18|language=en-US}} < /ref >  Metallica rewrote the song, and named it  " The Four Horsemen " . They put it on their first album, ''[[Kill 'Em All]],'' in 1983. < ref > {{Cite web|date=2022-09-12|title=Metallica is Having a Little Fun with Their Fan Base's Hate for 'St. Anger'|url=https://wmmr.com/2022/09/12/metallica-st-anger/|access-date=2022-09-18|website=93.3 WMMR|language=en}} < /ref >
