'''Sathupalli ('''also spelled as '''Sathupally''', '''Sathupalle''')  is a town in [[Khammam district]] of the [[Indian state]] of [[Telangana]]. It is a [[Municipality]] in [[Sathupalli mandal]] of [[Kalluru revenue division]]. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Khammam District New Revenue Divisions and Mandals|url=https://www.districtsinfo.com/2017/02/khammam-district-revenue-divisions.html|access-date=2023-06-07|website=Informative portal for All Districts in India - DistrictsInfo}} < /ref >  It is about {{cvt|276|km}} east of [[Hyderabad]], {{cvt|80|km}} from [[Khammam]] and {{cvt|200|km}} from [[Warangal]]. It is about {{cvt|135|km}} north of [[Amaravati]], the state capital of [[Andhra Pradesh]], and {{cvt|126|km}} west of [[Rajahmundry]]. < ref > {{cite web|last=bliccathemes|title=Sathupalli Municipality|url=http://sathupalli.in/#}} < /ref >
