'''Saulges''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]] of 328 people ([[2005]]). It is found in the region [[Pays de la Loire]] in the [[Mayenne]] department in the northwest of [[France]]. In Saulges, there is also a [[prehistoric]] canyon which is 1.8 million years old and 20 different prehistoric caves.
