 '''Cluny''' is a [[city]] in eastern [[France]]. It lies in the region of [[Burgundy]]. The city grew around the [[Abbey of Cluny]] in the [[10th century]]. The [[town]] of [[Mâcon]] is about 30 & nbsp;km to the southeast. [[Lyon]] is about 60 & nbsp;km to the [[south]]. Today, about 4,400 people live in the city. The [[Cluniac Reforms]] started here.
