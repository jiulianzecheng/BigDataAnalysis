 '''Exposure''' comes from the Latin word  " exp≈çnere "  which means to put out. It can mean: 
 
 * Lack of protection over prolonged periods under weather, extreme temperatures or dangerous substances (''see also: [[hypothermia]], [[radiation poisoning]]'') 
 * [[Exposure (toxicology)]]: When a [[toxin]] gets on or into a person's body 
 * [[Exposure (photography)]], the total amount of light allowed to fall on the sensor during the taking of a photograph 
 * ''Exposure'', a 1979 music album by [[Robert Fripp]] 
 * [[Investment]], a financial holding in a particular market sector
