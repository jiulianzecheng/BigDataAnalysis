 '''Ossuccio''' was a ''[[comune]]'' in the [[Province of Como]] in the [[Lombardy]] region in [[Italy]]. It is on the western shore of [[Lake Como]], about {{convert|20|km|mi}} northeast of [[Como]]. On 4 February 2014, the former municipalities of [[Lenno]], [[Mezzegra]], Ossuccio and [[Tremezzo]] merged to form the new municipality of [[Tremezzina]].
