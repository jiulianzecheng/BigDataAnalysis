{{Buddhism}} 
 '''Mahayana''' ({{lang-sa|महायान}}, ''mahāyāna'', literally the  " Great Vehicle " ) is one of the three main branches of [[Buddhism]]. Mahayana Buddhism initially developed in [[Gandhara]] (modern-day [[Pakistan]]). [[Zen]] is a kind of Mahayana that is influenced by [[Confucianism]].
