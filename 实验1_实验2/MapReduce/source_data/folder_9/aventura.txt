 '''Aventura''' is a [[Bachata]]-[[R & B]] [[band|music group]] from [[New York City]]. They became popular worldwide with their hit [[singlle (music)|single]] ''[[Obsesion|Obsesión]]'' in 2003/2004. 
 
 ==Members== 
 * Anthony  " Romeo "  Santos, lead singer and featured composer 
 * Leny Santos, guitarist, producer/arranger 
 * Max Santos, bass guitar 
 * Henry Santos Jeter, singer and composer 
 
 ==Discography== 
 * ''Generation Next'', [[1997]], Label:	Premium Latin / Mach 1 
 * ''We Broke the Rules'', [[2002]], Label : KTE 
 * ''Love  &  Hate'' (Amor Y Odio), [[2003]], Label : KTE	   
 * ''Unplugged'', [[2004]] 
 *God's Project, 2005, Label: Mach 1
