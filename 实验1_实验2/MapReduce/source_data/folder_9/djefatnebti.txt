 '''Djefatnebti''' was Ancient Egyptian [[queen]] of the 3rd dynasty. She lived at the end of that dynasty. She was a wife of [[Pharaoh]] [[Huni]] and mother of Queen [[Hetepheres I]]. We know for her and her name from a vessel from Elephantine.  
 
 Title of Djefatnebti was ''Great one of the hetes-sceptre''.
