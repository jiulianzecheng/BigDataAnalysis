 {{Nihongo|'''Kumagaya'''|熊谷市|Kumagaya-shi}} is a [[Japan]]ese [[city]] in [[Saitama Prefecture]] on the island of [[Honshu]]. < ref > [[Louis-Frédéric|Nussbaum, Louis-Frédéric]]. (2005). [https://books.google.com/books?id=p2QnPijAEmEC & pg=PA572 & dq=  " Kumagaya " ] in ''Japan Encyclopedia'', p. 572. < /ref > 
 
 It has been [[wikt:recognized|recognized]] as a [[Special cities of Japan|special city]] since 2009. < ref > Jacobs, A.J. [http://www.hindawi.com/journals/usr/2011/692764/   " Japan's Evolving Nested Municipal Hierarchy: The Race for Local Power in the 2000s, " ] ''Urban Studies Research'' (2011), [http://www.hindawi.com/journals/usr/2011/692764/tab3/  Table 3]. Retrieved 2012-12-18. < /ref > 
 
 The city has an estimated population of 153,392 (as of 1 June 2012) and the density of  1,270.8 people per km². < ref name= " citypopulation " > CityPopulation.de, [http://www.citypopulation.de/php/japan-saitama.php (熊谷市)]. Retrieved 2012-11-9. < /ref >
