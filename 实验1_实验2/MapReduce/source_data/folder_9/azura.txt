'''Azura'''  was the third daughter of [[Adam and Eve]] in the [[Abrahamic religion]]s. Azura was also the sister-wife of [[Seth]]. The sibcestual relationship between Azura and Seth was the first of Adam and Eve's children which did not involve a twincestual pairing. < ref > Amaru, Betsy Halpern.  " The First Woman, Wives, and Mothers in "  Jubilees " . "  Journal of Biblical literature 113.4 (1994): 609-626. < /ref > 
 
 She is not mentioned in the writings that are considered the [[Bible]] for most Jews and Christians, but she is mentioned in the [[Book of Jubilees]].
