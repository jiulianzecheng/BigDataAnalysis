 '''Binnenmaas''' is a former [[List of municipalities of the Netherlands|municipality]] in the [[Netherlands|Dutch]] [[Provinces of the Netherlands|province]] of [[South Holland]]. 
 
 == History == 
 It was created on 1 January 1984 from the former municipalities of Puttershoek, Maasdam, Mijnsheerenland, Westmaas and Heinenoord. Since 2007, [['s-Gravendeel]] was part to Binnenmaas. On 1 January 2019, it was merged with the former municipalities of [[Cromstrijen]], [[Korendijk]], [[Oud-Beijerland]], and [[Strijen]] to form the new municipality of [[Hoeksche Waard]].
