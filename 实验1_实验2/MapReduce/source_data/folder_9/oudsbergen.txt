'''Oudsbergen''' is a [[municipality]] in the [[Belgium|Belgian]] [[Provinces of Belgium|province]] of [[Limburg (Belgium)|Limburg]]. It was created on 1 January 2019 when the municipalities of [[Opglabbeek]] and [[Meeuwen-Gruitrode]] joined together to become the new municipality of Oudsbergen. 
 
 The new municipality has an area of {{convert|116.24|km2|sqmi|abbr=on}} and more than 23,000 people.
