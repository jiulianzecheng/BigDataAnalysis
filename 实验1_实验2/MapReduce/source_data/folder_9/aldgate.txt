'''Aldgate''' is a district within [[Central London]]. It is in the [[City of London]] (the City) and the [[London Borough of Tower Hamlets]]. It is part of the [[East End]]. Aldgate was the eastern-most gateway through [[London Wall]] to [[Whitechapel]].
