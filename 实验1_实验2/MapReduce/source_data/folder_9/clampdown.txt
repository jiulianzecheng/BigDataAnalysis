 " '''Clampdown''' "  is a 1980 song by English punk rock band [[The Clash]]. It is the second single from their third studio album ''[[London Calling]]''. It was released in Australia with its B-side single ''[[The Guns of Brixton]]''. It was not released in other territories, with the exception of US promos. It was covered by the [[Indigo Girls]] for their 2005 album ''[[Rarities (Indigo Girls album)|Rarities]]''.
