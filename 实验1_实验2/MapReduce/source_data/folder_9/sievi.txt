 '''Sievi''' is a municipality in [[Northern Ostrobothnia]], [[Finland]]. As of 28 February 2023, 4,742 people lived there. Neighbouring municipalities are [[Haapajärvi]], [[Kalajoki]], [[Kannus]], [[Lestijärvi]], [[Nivala]], [[Reisjärvi]], [[Toholampi]] and [[Ylivieska]]. It was founded in 1867. 
 
 Most people speak [[Finnish language|Finnish]].
