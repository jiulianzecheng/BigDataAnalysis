 '''Obernburg am Main''' (officially ''Obernburg a.Main'',  < small > short version: < /small >  ''Obernburg,'' {{IPA-de|ˈoːbɐnˌbʊʁk|lang|De-Obernburg.ogg}}) is a town in the [[Miltenberg (district)|Miltenberg district]] in the [[Lower Franconia]] [[Regierungsbezirk|region]] in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. 
 
 == Geography == 
 
 === Place === 
 Obernburg is at the mouth of the river [[Mümling]], where it releases into the [[Main (river)|Main]], at the foot of the [[Odenwald]] (range).
