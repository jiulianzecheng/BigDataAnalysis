 '''Volnovakha''' ({{lang-uk|Волноваха}}) is a small city in the [[Donetsk Oblast]] of [[Ukraine]]. It is the administrative center of [[Volnovakha Raion]], one of the 18 districts of the Donetsk Oblast.
