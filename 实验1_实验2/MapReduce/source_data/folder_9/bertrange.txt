 '''Bertrange''' ({{lang-lb|Bartreng}}, {{lang-de|Bartringen}}) is a [[Communes of Luxembourg|commune]] in [[Luxembourg]]. It is part of the [[Luxembourg (canton)|canton of Luxembourg]]. 
 
 As of 2019, 8,282 people live here.
