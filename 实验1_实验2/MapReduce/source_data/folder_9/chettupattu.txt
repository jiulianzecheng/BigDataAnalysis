'''Chettupattu''' is a locality in the Indian city of [[Chennai]]. It is served by Chetpet railway station in the Beach-Tambaram line of Chennai Suburban Railway. Chettupattu has a pond between the Chetpet railway station and the Poonamallee High Road, one of the last surviving natural water bodies in the city.
