 '''The''' '''Nebelhorn''' is a {{convert|2,244|m}}-high [[mountain]] in the [[Allgäu Alps]]. It is next to the [[wikt:village|village]] of [[Oberstdorf]], in [[Germany]]. Its [[summit]] is a well-known [[wikt:view|view]]ing point of the [[Alps]]. It also gives its name to several [[competition]]s, including the [[Nebelhorn Trophy]], < ref > {{Cite web|url=http://www.isu.org/en/single-and-pair-skating-and-ice-dance/calendar-of-events/2016/09/cs-nebelhorn-trophy|title=The Nebelhorn Trophy}} < /ref >  a [[figure skating]] competition.
