 '''Pressath''' is a [[Municipalities of Germany|municipality]]  in [[Neustadt an der Waldnaab (district)|Neustadt an der Waldnaab]], in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. Pressath was founded in 1657. It is 20 & nbsp;km northwest of [[Weiden in der Oberpfalz]] and ca. 7 & nbsp;km north of [[Grafenwöhr]].
