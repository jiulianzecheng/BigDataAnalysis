 '''Gattico''' was a ''[[comune]]'' in the [[Province of Novara]] in the [[Piedmont]] region in [[Italy]]. On 1 January 2019, the former municipalities of Gattico and [[Veruno]] merged to form the new municipality of [[Gattico-Veruno]].
