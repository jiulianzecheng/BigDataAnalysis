 An '''ayatollah''' ({{IPAc-en|UK|ˌ|aɪ|ə|ˈ|t|ɒ|l|ə}}  < small > or < /small >  {{IPAc-en|US|ˌ|aɪ|ə|ˈ|t|oʊ|l|ə}}; {{lang-fa|آيت‌الله|āyatullāh}}, from {{lang-ar|آية الله|[[ayah|ʾāyatu]] [[allah|llāh]]}}  " Sign of God " ) or ayatullah is a high-ranking [[Usuli]] [[Twelver]] [[Shia Islam|Shī‘ah]] cleric.
