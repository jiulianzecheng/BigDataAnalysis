 '''Manche''' is a [[departments of France|department]] found in the west of [[France]] in the [[Normandy]] [[region]]. Its [[prefecture]] is [[Saint-Lô]]. Its population is about 486,000 (2006 estimation). It is one of the 83 first French departments made during the [[French revolution]].
