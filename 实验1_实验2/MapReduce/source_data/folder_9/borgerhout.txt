 '''Borgerhout''' is the smallest [[district]] of the city of [[Antwerp]], in [[Flanders]] ([[Belgium]]). {{As of|January 2013}}, the district houses 45,948 people{{fact|date=August 2018}}. 
 
 It is the birthplace of singer-songwriter [[Milow (singer)|Milow]].
