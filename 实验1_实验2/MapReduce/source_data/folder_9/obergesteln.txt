 '''Obergesteln''' is a village and former [[municipality]] in the [[district]] of [[Goms (district)|Goms]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Valais]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 On 1 January 2009, the former municipalities of Obergesteln, [[Oberwald]] and [[Ulrichen]] merged into the new municipality of [[Obergoms]].
