'''Lawshall''' is a village and [[civil parish]] in [[Babergh]], [[Suffolk]], England. In 2001, there were 935 people living in Lawshall. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.ons.gov.uk/help/localstatistics|title=Local statistics - Office for National Statistics|website=www.ons.gov.uk}} < /ref >  It has a church called All Saints. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://britishlistedbuildings.co.uk/101036594-church-of-all-saints-lawshall|title=Church of All Saints, Lawshall, Suffolk|first=Good|last=Stuff|website=britishlistedbuildings.co.uk}} < /ref > < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.suffolkchurches.co.uk/lawshall.html|title=Suffolk Churches|website=www.suffolkchurches.co.uk}} < /ref >
