 '''Trino''' ({{lang-pms|Trin}}) is a ''[[comune]]'' in the [[Province of Vercelli]] in the [[Piedmont]] region in [[Italy]]. It is about {{convert|50|km|mi}} northeast of [[Turin]] and about {{convert|15|km|mi|0}} southwest of [[Vercelli]], at the foot of the [[Montferrat]] hills. 
 
 Trino is next to these municipalities: [[Bianz√®]], [[Camino, Piedmont|Camino]], [[Costanzana]], [[Fontanetto Po]], [[Livorno Ferraris]], [[Morano sul Po]], [[Palazzolo Vercellese]], [[Ronsecco]], and [[Tricerro]]. 
 
 Trino was the site of [[Enrico Fermi Nuclear Power Plant]]. The [[Romanesque architecture|Romanesque]] church of San Michele in Insula which were built in the 10th & ndash;11th centuries. It has 12th-century frescoes. The [[Lucedio Abbey]] is in the comune.
