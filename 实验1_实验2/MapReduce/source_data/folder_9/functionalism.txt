 '''Functionalism''' may refer to: 
 
 * [[Functionalism (architecture)]], the idea that architects should design a building based on the what that building will be used for 
 * [[Functionalism in international relations]], a theory that started during the inter-War period 
 * [[Functional linguistics]], a theory about how to study language 
 * [[Functionalism (philosophy of mind)]], a theory of the mind in modern philosophy 
 * [[Functionalism versus intentionalism]], a historiographical debate about what started the Holocaust 
 * [[Structural functionalism]], a theory of sociology and anthropology 
 * [[Biological functionalism]], an anthropological paradigm
