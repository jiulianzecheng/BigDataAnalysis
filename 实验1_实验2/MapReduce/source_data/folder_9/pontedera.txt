 '''Pontedera''' is a small [[Italy|Italian]] industrial town in [[Tuscany]], [[Italy]], in the [[province of Pisa]]. Pontedera is the headquarters of the [[Piaggio]] company,from the 1930s one of most important Italian manufacturers motor vehicles such as the [[Vespa]] and the [[Ape]]. Piaggio was a [[subsidiary]] of [[Fiat|FIAT]]. Today the owner of Piaggio is [[Roberto Colaninno]]. 
 
 Pontedera is in the [[Arno valley]] at the confluence of the [[Era River]] and the [[Arno River]]. 
 The Town has a small [[lake]] in [[Pietroconti]] called [[Braccini lake]].
