 '''Albrandswaard''' is a [[List of municipalities of the Netherlands|municipality]] in the [[Netherlands|Dutch]] [[Provinces of the Netherlands|province]] of [[South Holland]]. In 2019, 25,271 people lived there. 
 
 Albrandswaard has two settlements: [[Rhoon]] and [[Poortugaal]].
