 '''Quavious Keyate Marshall''', known as Quavo ({{IPAc-en|'|k|w|eɪ|v|oʊ}}) (born April 2, 1991) is an American rapper, singer, songwriter and record producer. He is a member of the trio [[Migos]] along with Kirshnik Ball (aka Takeoff) and Kiari Cephus (aka Offset). Quavo is based in the [[U.S. state]] of [[Georgia (U.S. state)|Georgia]]. He released his first album in 2018. 
 
 ==Personal life== 
 Quavo got into a fight with his ex-girlfriend [[Saweetie]] in an [[elevator]]. < ref > {{cite web |url=https://www.timeslive.co.za/tshisa-live/tshisa-live/2021-03-31-us-rapper-quavo-slammed-for-elevator-fight-with-ex-girlfriend-saweetie/|title=US rapper Quavo slammed for elevator fight with ex-girlfriend Saweetie|publisher=TimesLive|date=Mar 31, 2021 |website=timeslive.co.za |access-date=May 14, 2022}} < /ref > 
 
 On November 1, 2022 Quavo was playing dice with a group when a fight broke out that resulted in the shooting and killing of his nephew and member of [[Migos]], [[Takeoff (rapper)|Takeoff]]. Two additional victims were initially reported and their condition remains unclear. < ref > {{cite web | url=https://www.tmz.com/2022/11/01/migos-takeoff-dead-dies-shot-houston-quavo-migos/ | title=Migos Rapper Takeoff Dead at 28, Shot in Houston }} < /ref >
