 '''''Agama''''' is a [[genus]] of lizards. It contains at least 31 species across [[Africa]], where they are the most common lizard.  
 
 They can be found in many sizes, from 5 inches to a foot in length and a wide variety of colors. One of the best known species is the '''red-headed rock agama''' (''Agama agama''), widespread in [[sub-Saharan Africa]].
