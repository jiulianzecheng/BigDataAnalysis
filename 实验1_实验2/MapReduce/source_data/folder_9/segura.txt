 '''Segura''' is a [[Spain|Spanish]] [[river]] that is 325 kilometers (200 miles) long. It flows through the provinces of [[Jaen province|Jaen]], [[Albacete province|Albacete]], [[Murcia (autonomous community)|Murcia]], and [[Alicante province|Alicante]], and the cities of [[Cieza]], [[Molina de Segura]], [[Murcia]], [[Orihuela]], [[Rojales]], and [[Guardamar]]. It then flows into the [[Mediterranean Sea]].
