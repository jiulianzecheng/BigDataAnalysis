 '''''Frasier''''' is an [[United States|American]] [[sitcom]] starring [[Kelsey Grammer]]. The series aired on [[NBC]] from 1993 to 2004. It was a [[spin-off]] from ''[[Cheers]]''. Story based upon the intellectual, mild mannered [[psychiatrist]] and his life. Revolving around his personal life and hilarious interpersonal interactions with his father (Martin Crane, an ex-cop who got shot in the hip, so he needs a cane), his eccentric brother Niles, who is also a psychiatrist, his father's attractive English health care worker Daphne Moon and a host of folks he regularly runs into / works with at the radio station (including his producer Roz Doyle) where he hosts a community psychiatric call-in show. 
 
 Cast : 
 *Dr. Frasier Crane ([[Kelsey Grammer]]) 
 *Daphne Moon ([[Jane Leeves]]) 
 *Dr. Niles Crane ([[David Hyde Pierce]]) 
 *Roz Doyle ([[Peri Gilpin]]) 
 *John Mahoney ([[Martin Crane]])
