 '''Conegliano''' is an [[Italy|Italian]] city in [[Veneto]]. About 35,000 people live there. 
 
 In 2019, Le Colline del [[Prosecco]] di Conegliano e [[Valdobbiadene]] became a [[UNESCO]] [[World Heritage Site]]. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://whc.unesco.org/en/news/2006|title=Two cultural sites added to UNESCO's World Heritage List|website=UNESCO|date=7 July 2019}} < /ref >
