 '''Confluence''' means the meeting of two or more [[waterway]]s.   
 
 It usually means the point where a smaller [[river]] joins a more major river.  
 == Example == 
 The [[Germany|German]] [[city]] name [[Koblenz]] indicates that the city lies at the point at the confluence of the [[Rhine]] and the [[Moselle River|Moselle]].
