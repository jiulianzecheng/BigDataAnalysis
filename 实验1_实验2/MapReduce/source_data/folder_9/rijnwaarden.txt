 '''Rijnwaarden''' is a former municipality in the [[Netherlands|Dutch]] [[Provinces of the Netherlands|province]] of [[Gelderland]]. It became a part of the municipality of [[Zevenaar]] in 2018. Main seat was [[Lobith]]. 
 
 Rijnwaarden was situated where the [[Rhine]] river enters from [[Germany]] into the Netherlands.
