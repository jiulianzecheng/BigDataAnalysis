 The '''Dadou''' is a river in southwestern [[France]], right [[tributary]] of the [[Agout]] [[river]]. It flows through the [[Tarn]] [[Departments of France|department]], in the [[Occitanie]] [[Regions of France|region]]. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 The Dadou river has a length of {{Convert|115.8|km|mi|1|abbr=on}} and a [[drainage basin]] with an area of {{Convert|857|km2|sqmi|0|abbr=on }}. < ref name= " sandre " > {{cite web |url=http://services.sandre.eaufrance.fr/Courdo/Fiche/client/fiche_courdo.php?CdSandre=O47-0400 |title=Le Dadou (O47-0400) |publisher=SANDRE - Portail national d'accès aux référentiels sur l'eau |language=fr |access-date=2 February 2017 }} < /ref > 
 
 Its average yearly [[Discharge (hydrology)|discharge]] ([[volume]] of [[water]] which passes through a section of the river per [[Unit of measurement|unit]] of [[time]]) is {{Convert|7.81|m3/s|cuft/s|abbr=on }} at [[Montdragon]] in the [[Tarn]] department. < ref name= " hydro " > {{cite web |url=http://www.hydro.eaufrance.fr/stations/O4774010 & procedure=synthese |title=Le Dadou à Montdragon |publisher=Banque Hydro |language=fr |access-date=2 February 2017 |archive-date=3 March 2016 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160303210852/http://www.hydro.eaufrance.fr/stations/O4774010%26procedure%3Dsynthese |url-status=dead }} < /ref >

 ===Course=== 
 The [[River source|source]] of the Dadou is on the ''Monts de Lacaune'', in the southern [[Massif Central]], in the [[Communes of France|''commune'']] of [[Saint-Salvi-de-Carcavès]], [[Tarn]] department, at an [[altitude]] of about {{Convert|870|m|ft|0|abbr=on }}. < ref > {{cite web |url=https://www.geoportail.gouv.fr/carte?c=2.625556,43.788611 & z=16 & l0=GEOGRAPHICALGRIDSYSTEMS.MAPS.SCAN-EXPRESS.STANDARD::GEOPORTAIL:OGC:WMTS(1) & permalink=yes |title=Source du Dadou |publisher=Géoportail |language=fr |access-date=2 February 2017 }} < /ref > 
 
 The Agout flows, in general, to the west through the Tarn department and 26 ''communes''. < ref name= " sandre "  / >  Some of the ''communes'' are [[Montdragon]], [[Graulhet]], [[Briatexte]], [[Lacaze]], [[Rayssac]], [[Saint-Julien-du-Puy]], [[Vénès]], [[Saint-Genest-de-Contest]], [[Le Masnau-Massuguiès]], [[Saint-Salvi-de-Carcavès]], [[Laboutarie]], [[Lombers]], [[Réalmont]], [[Saint-Lieux-Lafenasse]], [[Saint-Antonin-de-Lacalm]], [[Le Travet]], [[Teillet]], [[Arifat]], [[Mont-Roc]], [[Paulinet]], [[Giroussens]], [[Saint-Jean-de-Rives]], [[Ambres]], [[Saint-Gauzens]], [[Puybegon]], [[Montredon-Labessonnié]]. 
 
 Finally, it flows, as a right [[tributary]] into the [[Agout]] river at [[Ambres]], near [[Saint-Jean-de-Rives]], in the [[Tarn]] department, at {{Convert|115|m|ft|0|abbr=on }} of altitude. < ref > {{cite web |url=https://www.geoportail.gouv.fr/carte?c=1.79,43.736389 & z=15 & l0=GEOGRAPHICALGRIDSYSTEMS.MAPS.SCAN-EXPRESS.STANDARD::GEOPORTAIL:OGC:WMTS(1) & permalink=yes |title=Confluence du Dadou |publisher=Géoportail |language=fr |access-date=2 February 2017 }} < /ref >
