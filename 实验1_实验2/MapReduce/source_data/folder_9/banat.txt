 '''Banat''' ({{IPAc-en|UK|ˈ|b|æ|n|ɪ|t|,_|ˈ|b|ɑː|n|-}}, {{IPAc-en|US|b|ə|ˈ|n|ɑː|t|,_|b|ɑː|n|-}}; < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/Banat|title=Banat|website=[[Merriam-Webster|Merriam-Webster.com Dictionary]]}} < /ref > < ref > [https://www.collinsdictionary.com/dictionary/english/banat  " Banat " ]. ''[[Collins English Dictionary]]''/''[[Webster's New World College Dictionary]]''. < /ref >  {{lang-hu|Bánság}}; {{lang-sr|Банат|Banat}}) is a [[Region|geographical]] and [[Historical regions of Central Europe|historical region]] in [[Central Europe|Central]] and [[Eastern Europe]]. It is a part of three [[Country|countries]]. The eastern part is in western [[Romania]], the western part of Banat is in northeastern [[Serbia]], and a small northern part is in southeastern [[Hungary]]. The region is mostly [[Population|populated]] by ethnic [[Romanians]], [[Serbs]] and [[Hungarians]]. 
 
 == Geography == 
 [[File:Banat Sat.png|thumb|Banat as seen from [[NASA]]'s [[Landsat 7]] satellite]] 
 Banat is the part of the [[Pannonian Basin]] with the [[Danube]] to the south, the [[Tisza]] to the west, the [[Mureș (river)|Mureș]] to the north and the [[Southern Carpathians]] to the east. < ref name= " rusu " > {{cite book |url=http://nasol.ro/muzeulbanatului/wp-content/uploads/2016/10/XLII-ilovepdf-compressed.pdf |title=Organizarea spațiului geografic în Banat |last=Rusu |first=Raularian |location=Timișoara |publisher=Mirton |date=2007 |isbn=978-973-52-0201-9 |access-date=2023-06-03 |archive-date=2023-03-27 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20230327215257/http://nasol.ro/muzeulbanatului/wp-content/uploads/2016/10/XLII-ilovepdf-compressed.pdf |url-status=dead }} < /ref >  Banat has an area of 28,526 km². Romanian Banat has an area of 18,966 km², Serbian Banat has an area of 9,276 km², and Hungary 284 km². < ref > {{cite journal |title=Banatul, ca unitate și individualitate istorico-geografică în cadrul pământului locuit de români |last=Bizerea |first=Marius |journal=Tibiscus |series=Etnografie |publisher=Muzeul Banatului |location=Timișoara |date=1975 |pages=7–25}} < /ref > 
 
 Romanian Banat is [[wikt:mountainous|mountainous]] in the south and southeast. In the north, west and southwest it is flat and sometimes [[Marsh|marshy]]. < ref name= " rusu "  / >
