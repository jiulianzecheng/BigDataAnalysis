'''Tinia''' (also spelled '''Tin''', '''Tinh''', '''Tins''' or '''Tina''') was the supreme deity in [[Etruscan mythology]]. He was the god of the sky and lightning, equivalent to the [[Roman mythology|Roman]] [[Jupiter (mythology)|Jupiter]] and the [[Greek mythology|Greek]] [[Zeus]]. He was the husband of [[Thalna]] or [[Uni]] and the father of the hero [[Hercle]]. The element [[tin]] was named after Tinia.
