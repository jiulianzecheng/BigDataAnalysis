 A '''mullet''' is a kind of [[fish]]. Mullet often jump out of [[water]]. In recent years,  " mullet "  also came to be a [[slang]] word for a [[hairstyle]] that is short at the front and top, and long at the back. This [[Slang|slang word]] is said to have been started by Mike D of the [[Beastie Boys]] in 1995, and after 2000 was spread by a small group of people in [[San Francisco]] through the [[Internet]].
