 '''Nicole Zefanya''' (born 24 January 1999), popularly known as '''NIKI''' is an [[Indonesia]]n [[singer]], [[songwriter]] and [[record producer]]. She is currently based in the [[United States]]. She signed with the record label 88rising. < ref > {{cite web | url=https://www.allmusic.com/artist/niki-mn0003739784/biography | title=NIKI Biography, Songs,  &  Albums | website=[[AllMusic]] }} < /ref > 
 
 == Early life == 
 Niki was born on 24 January 1999. She was raised in [[Jakarta]], [[Indonesia]]. 
 
 At 9 years old, Niki taught herself how to play the [[guitar]]. < ref > {{cite web | url=https://www.thestar.com.my/lifestyle/entertainment/2019/07/17/indonesian-artiste-niki-taylor-swift | title=Indonesian singer Niki opened for Taylor Swift when she was 15 }} < /ref >  At 13, she started writing original music. < ref > {{cite web | url=https://www.nme.com/blogs/nme-radar/niki-interview-2018-2316838 | title=NIKI:  " Name one Asian artist that's popping right now. I want to be the girl that sets that up "  | website=[[NME]] | date=28 May 2018 }} < /ref > 
 
 == Career == 
 When Niki was 15, she was the opening act for [[Taylor Swift]]'s The Red Tour in Jakarta after winning a competition arranged by Swift and the ice-cream brand Walls, called Ride to Fame. < ref > {{cite web | url=https://www.nme.com/blogs/nme-radar/niki-interview-2018-2316838 | title=NIKI:  " Name one Asian artist that's popping right now. I want to be the girl that sets that up "  | website=[[NME]] | date=28 May 2018 }} < /ref >   
 
 In December 2020, Niki dropped  " [[Hallway Weather]] " , a holiday [[Single (music)|single]] reminiscent of her folk/indie roots. She says the song is in  " the style of music  &  [[songwriting]] [she remembers] feeling most like [herself]. It is genuine  &  it is true. " < ref > {{cite web | url=https://www.musicpressasia.com/2019/07/23/niki-zefanya-not-stopping-for-any-one/ | title=Niki Zefanya: Not Stopping for Any One | date=23 July 2019 }} < /ref > 
 
 == Artistry ==
