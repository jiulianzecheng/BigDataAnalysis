 '''Hayle''' is a small [[town]] and [[Civil parishes in England|civil parish]] in [[Cornwall]], [[England]], [[United Kingdom]]. It is at the [[River mouth|mouth]] of [[Hayle River]]. The river [[Discharge (hydrology)|flows into]] [[St. Ives Bay]]. Hayle is about {{convert|7|mi}} [[northeast]] of [[Penzance]]. < ref > Ordnance Survey: Landranger map sheet 203 ''Land's End'' {{ISBN|978-0-319-23148-7}} < /ref >
