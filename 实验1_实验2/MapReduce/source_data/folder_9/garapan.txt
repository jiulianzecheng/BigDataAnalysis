 '''Garapan''' ([[Japanese language|Old Japanese name]]: 柄帆町, ''Garapan-chō'') is the largest village on the island of [[Saipan]], [[Northern Mariana Islands]]. It is also the center of the [[tourism]] [[industry]] on the island. Garapan has an area of {{cvt|1.2|km2}} < ref > {{Cite web|url=http://hydroponicsearch.com/|title=Hydroponics, Gardening, Greenhouse, Agriculture, Horticulture, Aquaculture|access-date=May 22, 2019|archive-date=May 16, 2019|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190516072611/http://hydroponicsearch.com/|url-status=dead}} < /ref >  and a population of 3,588 (census of population, 2000). < ref > {{Cite web|url=http://www.citypopulation.de/NorthernMarianas.html|title=Northern Mariana Islands: Municipalities (Islands)  &  Major Places - Population Statistics in Maps and Charts|website=www.citypopulation.de|access-date=May 22, 2019}} < /ref >  It is a [[census-designated place]].
