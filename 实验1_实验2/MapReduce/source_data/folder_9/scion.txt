 '''Scion''' (from English -  " escape, offspring, heir " ) was a brand of [[Japan|Japanese]] carmaker [[Toyota]] Motor Corporation, represented in the American market. The brand was founded in 2003 and is positioned for youth. 
 
 In early February 2016, it became known that Toyota has decided to close the brand in August that year. Since the 2017 model year, the FR-S, iA and iM models have been sold under the Toyota brand and are called 86, Yaris iA and Corolla iM respectively.{{Transport-stub}}
