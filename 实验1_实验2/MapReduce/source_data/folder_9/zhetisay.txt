'''Zhetisay''' ({{lang-kk|Жетісай}}, {{lang-ru|Жетысай}}) is a [[city]] in the [[Turkistan Region]] of [[Kazakhstan]]. There are 45,130 people living there (as of 2023). < ref name=pop2023 > {{Cite web|date=2023-01-01|title=Population of the Republic of Kazakhstan by gender and type of area (as of 2023-01-01)|url=https://stat.gov.kz/api/getFile/?docId=ESTAT478686|url-status=dead|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20230305101742/https://stat.gov.kz/api/getFile/?docId=ESTAT478686|archive-date=2023-03-05|website=QazStat}} < /ref > 
 
 It was founded in 1939. It was given city status in 1969.
