'''Deoli''' is a city in [[Rajasthan]] . Most of the city lies in the [[tonk]] and [[shahpura]] districts . [[bundi]] and [[kekri]] districts are not far away . the city's population is around 29,000 . Deoli is also the headquarters of the Deoli [[tehsil]] in tonk district . it's nickname is '''''chaavni'''''.the city also houses the 2nd largest training centre in [[asia]] , the [[CISF]] training centre .
