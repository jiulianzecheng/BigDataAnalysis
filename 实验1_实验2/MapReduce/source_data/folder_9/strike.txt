'''Strike''' may mean: 
 * [[Strike action]], when workers decide to stop working at their jobs until their demands are met 
 * [[General strike]], a strike action by many workers in a city, area, or country 
 * [[Hunger strike]], when someone decides to not eat in order to [[protest]] 
 
 '''Strike''' may refer to a fight, including: 
 * [[Strike (attack)]], an attack meant to hit a human body and cause harm 
 * [[Military strike]], a military attack on a certain target 
 
 In '''sports''' and '''games''': 
 * [[Strike (baseball)]] in baseball, a mark against a batter 
 * [[Strike (bowling)]], when a player knocks down all the pins with the first ball of a frame
