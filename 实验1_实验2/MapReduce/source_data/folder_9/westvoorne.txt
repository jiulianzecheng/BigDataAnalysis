 '''Westvoorne''' is a former municipality in the [[Provinces of the Netherlands|Dutch province]] of [[South Holland]]. In 2022, 14,960 people lived there. 
 
 On 1 January 2023, it was merged with the former municipalities of [[Brielle]] and [[Hellevoetsluis]] to form the new municipality of [[Voorne aan Zee]].
