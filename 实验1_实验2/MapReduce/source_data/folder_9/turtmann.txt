 '''Turtmann''' was a [[municipality]] of the [[district]] [[Leuk (district)|Leuk]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Valais]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 On 1 January 2013, the former municipalities of Turtmann and [[Unterems]] merged into the new municipality of [[Turtmann-Unterems]].
