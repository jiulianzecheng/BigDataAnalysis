'''Culebra''' (meaning ''snake'' in [[Spanish language|Spanish]]) has several meanings: 
 * [[Culebra, Puerto Rico|Island of Culebra]], part of [[Puerto Rico]]. 
 * [[Culebra Peak]], the southernmost [[fourteener]] in [[Colorado]], [[USA]]. 
 * [[Culebra (automobile)]], an [[England|English]] [[automobile]] manufactured in [[1989]]. 
 * The Culebra Cut, an artificial [[valley]] located in the [[Panama Canal]] area, was renamed the [[Gaillard Cut]] in [[1915]].
