 '''Adjara''' is a region of [[Georgia (country)|Georgia]]. Its official name is '''Autonomous Republic of Adjara'''. The [[capital city]] is [[Batumi]], which is the 2nd largest city in Georgia. 
 
 The region is on the coast of the [[Black Sea]] near the foot of the [[Caucasus Mountains|Lesser Caucasus Mountains]]. About 333,953 people live there (2014). 
 
 There are 5 [[municipalities]] plus the city of Batumi. The five municipalities are: 
 
 *[[Keda Municipality]] 
 *[[Kobuleti Municipality]] 
 *[[Khelvachauri Municipality]] 
 *[[Shuakhevi Municipality]] 
 *[[Khulo Municipality]]
