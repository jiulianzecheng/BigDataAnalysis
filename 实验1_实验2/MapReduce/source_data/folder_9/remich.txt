 '''Remich''' ({{lang-lb|Réimech}}) is a [[Communes of Luxembourg|commune]] with [[List of cities in Luxembourg|city status]] in south-eastern [[Luxembourg]], capital of the [[Remich (canton)|canton Remich]]. 
 
 It is called the ''Pearl of the [[Moselle River|Moselle]]''. < ref name= " perle " > {{cite web |url=http://www.remich.lu/la-commune-se-presente |title=Remich - Découvrir la commune |publisher=Ville de Remich |language=fr |access-date=30 May 2016 |archive-date=22 June 2016 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160622193849/http://www.remich.lu/la-commune-se-presente |url-status=dead }} < /ref > 
 
 ==History== 
 Remich was a [[Ancient Rome|Roman]] [[Human settlement|settlement]] called ''Remacum''. This name changed to the present nameː '''Remich'''. < ref name= " perle "  / > 
 
 On 24 February 1843, Remich was officially made a city together with [[Luxembourg City]], [[Diekirch]], [[Grevenmacher]], [[Echternach]], [[Wiltz]] and [[Vianden]]. < ref > {{cite web | url=http://www.legilux.public.lu/leg/a/archives/1843/0170304/0170304.pdf#page=1 | title=Mémorial A, 1843, No. 17 | access-date=30 May 2016 | format=PDF | publisher=Service central de législation | archive-date=14 April 2006 | archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20060414085748/http://www.legilux.public.lu/leg/a/archives/1843/0170304/0170304.pdf#page=1 | url-status=dead }}{{in lang|fr|de}} < /ref > 
 
 ==Geography== 
 [[File:L-Remich6.JPG|thumb|left|Moselle tourist boats at Remich]] 
 Remich lies on the left bank of the [[Moselle River|Moselle]] river, which forms part of the border between Luxembourg and [[Germany]]. 
 
 The ''commune'' is the smallest in Luxembourg in area. It has an area of {{Convert|5.3|km2|sqmi|1|abbr=on }}. < ref name=stats / >  The [[altitude]] is {{Convert|142|-|253|m|ft|abbr=on }} high. 
 
 Remich is surrounded by three ''communes'' of Luxembourg: [[Stadtbredimus]] to the north, [[Schengen, Luxembourg|Schengen]] to the south and [[Bous, Luxembourg|Bous]] to the west. To the northeast, the ''commune'' borders the [[Palzem]] [[municipality]] of the  [[Landkreis Germersheim|Germersheim]] district in the [[Germany|German]] [[States of Germany|state]] of [[Rhineland-Palatinate]]; and to the southeast [[Perl, Saarland|Perl]] of the [[Merzig-Wadern District|Merzig-Wadern]] district in the German state of [[Saarland]].
