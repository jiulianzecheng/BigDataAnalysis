'''Heme''', or '''haem''' (pronounced /hi:m/ ''HEEM''), is a precursor to [[hemoglobin]], which is necessary to bind [[oxygen]] in the [[circulatory system]]. Heme is biosynthesized in both the [[bone marrow]] and the [[liver]].
