'''Jesse''' was the father of King [[David]]. He is in the [[Book of Ruth]] as Ruth's future grandson (chapter 4 verse 17). He is also mentioned in the [[Gospel of Matthew]] 1:6 as an [[ancestor]] of [[Jesus]].
