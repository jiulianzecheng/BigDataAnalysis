'''Gravemind''' is a fictional alien in the ''[[Halo (series)|Halo]]'' franchise. It is the hive of the Flood and central form. It is voiced by [[Dee Bradley Baker]]. 
 
 ==Fictional biography== 
 The Gravemind encountered both [[Master Chief]] and the [[Arbiter (Halo)|Arbiter]]. It gave them an offer to defeat the Covenant Loyalists. 
 
 ==Development== 
 For it's first appearance, there was supposed to be a full level about him but was replaced with an opening cutscene. 
 
 ==Reception== 
 Many people didn't like Gravemind's first design. By the remake for Halo 2, his design was heavily improved and got better reception.
