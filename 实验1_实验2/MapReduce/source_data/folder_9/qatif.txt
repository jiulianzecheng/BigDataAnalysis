 '''Qatif''' or '''Al-Qatif''' is a [[Governorates of Saudi Arabia|governorate and urban area]] in [[Eastern Province, Saudi Arabia|Eastern Province]], [[Saudi Arabia]]. It goes from [[Ras Tanura]] and [[Jubail]] in the north to [[Dammam]] in the south. It is from the [[Persian Gulf]] in the east to [[King Fahd International Airport]] in the west.  
 
 Qatif is one of the oldest towns in the eastern side of the Arabian peninsula. Its history goes back to 3500 BC. Qatifi people used to work as merchants, farmers, and fishers. Now most of Qatifi's people are working in oil, industry, public services, education and health care sectors.
