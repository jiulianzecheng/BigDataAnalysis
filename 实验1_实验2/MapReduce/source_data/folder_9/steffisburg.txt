 '''Steffisburg''' is a [[municipality]] in [[Thun (administrative district)|Thun]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Bern (canton)|Bern]] in [[Switzerland]]. On 1 January 2020, the municipality [[Schwendibach]] became part of Steffisburg.
