 '''Balingen''' is a town in [[Baden-Württemberg]], [[Germany]]. It is the capital of the [[districts of Germany|district]] of [[Zollernalbkreis]]. It is located near the [[Swabian Alb]]. It is {{convert|35|km|abbr=off}} to the south of [[Tübingen]], {{convert|35|km|abbr=off}} northeast of [[Villingen-Schwenningen]], and {{convert|60|km|abbr=off}} southwest of [[Stuttgart]].
