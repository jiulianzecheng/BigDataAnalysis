 '''Untergriesbach''' ({{lang-bar|label=[[Central Bavarian]]|Untagriasbo}}) is a [[Municipalities of Germany|municipality]] in [[Passau (district)|Passau]] in [[Bavaria]] in Germany. 
 
 == Geography == 
 Untergriesbach is in the southern [[Bavarian Forest]] and extends high above the deep valley of the [[Danube]]. The height of the market town is 565 meters above sea level. Via the Bundesstraße 388 it is connected  to [[Passau]] (22 & nbsp;km) and in the opposite direction to the [[Upper Austria]]n [[Haslach an der Mühl|Haslach]] (32 & nbsp;km).

 == Incorporations == 
 On 1 October 1971, the previously independent municipality Lambsdorf was incorporated. On 1 January 1972 Gottsdorf and Schaibing were added. Oberötzdorf followed on 1 March 1972.
