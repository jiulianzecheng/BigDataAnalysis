'''Jambuda''' ([[Gujarati language|Gujarati]]: જાંબુડા, [[Hindi]]: जांबुडा) is a small [[village]] in the [[Jamnagar district]] of the [[India|Indian]] [[States and territories of India|state]] of [[Gujarat]]. The village has communities of Gadhavi, Rajgor Brahmin, Patels, shepherd, Charaniya and Kori. 
 
 Jambuda is about {{convert|16|km|abbr=on}} away from [[Jamnagar]] city, and on the way to Sainik School Balachadi. Historical book Yaduvanshprakash says that Jambuda village is older than 400 years. It is considered that the village was named as ''Jambuda'' due to the large number of Java plum tree, and the heavy production of [[Plum|Java plum]] fruits. karsan pala paliya was notable personality in village.
