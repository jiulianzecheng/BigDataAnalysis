 '''Garamond''' is the name for a number of [[Typeface|typefaces]]. They were created by Parisian typesetter [[Claude Garamond]] in the 16th century, or they are based on his works. The typefaces use [[Serif|serifs]]. As these typefaces are very well suited for reading, they are often used in printed books.
