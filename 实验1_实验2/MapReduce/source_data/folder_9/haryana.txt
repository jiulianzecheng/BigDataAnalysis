 '''Haryana''' ({{IPA-hi|ɦərɪˈjaːɳaː}}) is a state in the [[Republic of India]]. The main language spoken in the area are [[Haryanvi language|Haryanvi]] and [[Punjabi language|Panjabi]]. The area is {{convert|17122|mi2|abbr=off}}. It is larger than [[Denmark]] but smaller than [[Estonia]]. In traditional Indian geography it falls under the [[North India]]n zone.
