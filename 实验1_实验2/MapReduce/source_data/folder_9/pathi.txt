 The worship centers of [[Ayyavazhi]] are called '''Pathis'''. The activities of [[Ayya Vaikundar|Vaikundar]] is historically jointed with this Pathis. There are five Pathis: 
 
 *[[Swamithoppepathi]] 
 *[[Ambalappathi]] 
 *[[Muttappathi]] 
 *[[Thamaraikulampathi]] 
 *[[Pooppathi]]
