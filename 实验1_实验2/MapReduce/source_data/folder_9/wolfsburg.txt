 '''Wolfsburg''' is a city in the [[Germany|German]] [[States of Germany|state]] of [[Lower Saxony]]. 
 
 It had about 124,000 [[inhabitant]]s in 2016. The automobile company [[Volkswagen]] has its main office in Wolfsburg.
