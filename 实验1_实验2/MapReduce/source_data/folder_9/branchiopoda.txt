 '''Branchiopoda''' is a class of [[crustacean]]s. It is the sister group to the other crustaceans. They are mostly small, freshwater animals that feed on [[plankton]] and detritus. However, many of the Cladocera are [[ocean|marine]]. 
 
 Branchiopods have [[gill]]s on most of the animals' [[appendage]]s, including some of the mouthparts.
