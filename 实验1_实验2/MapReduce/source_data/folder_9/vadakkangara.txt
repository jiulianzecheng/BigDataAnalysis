'''Vadakkangara''' is a [[village]] in the [[Malappuram]] district in [[Kerala]], [[India]]. The Village has small areas including Vadakke kulambu, Kizhakke kulambu, Tadathil kundu, Kalavu.{{As of|2011}} India [[census]], Vadakkangara had a [[population]] of 18702 with 8920 males and 9782 females. < ref > {{cite web|title=2011 indian census|url=http://www.censusindia.gov.in/pca/final_pca.aspx|accessdate=9 April 2014}} < /ref >
