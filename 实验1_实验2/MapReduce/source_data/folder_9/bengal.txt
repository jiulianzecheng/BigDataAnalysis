[[File:Map of Bengal.svg|thumb|280px|Map of the Bengal region: [[Bangladesh]] and [[West Bengal]], India]] 
 '''Bengal''' is a divided [[region]] in [[South Asia]] that is split between two countries - [[Bangladesh]] and [[India]]. Both parts used to be united, although historically part of [[British India]]; today [[West Bengal]] is part of India and ''East Bengal'' is [[Bangladesh]]. It contains the [[Ganges Delta]], the largest in the world.
