 '''Tuttlingen''' is a [[town]] in southern [[Baden-Württemberg]], [[Germany]]. It is the capital of the district of [[Tuttlingen (district)|Tuttlingen]]. ''Nendingen'', ''Möhringen'' and ''Eßlingen'' are three former municipalities that belong to Tuttlingen. The district (''Kreis'') includes many surrounding towns including [[Trossingen]], [[Spaichingen]], and [[Mühlheim an der Donau]]. It is in [[Swabia]] east of the [[Black Forest]] region in the [[Swabian Alps]].
