 '''Riggisberg''' is a [[municipality]] in the [[administrative district]] of [[Bern-Mittelland (administrative district)|Bern-Mittelland]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Bern (canton)|Bern]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 On January 1, 2009, the municipality of [[Rüti bei Riggisberg]] became part of the municipality of Riggisberg.
