'''Blaignan''' is a former [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is found in the [[Regions of France|region]] [[Aquitaine]] in the [[Gironde]] [[Departments of France|department]] in the southwest of [[France]]. On 1 January 2019, it was merged into the new commune [[Blaignan-Prignac]]. < ref > [https://www.legifrance.gouv.fr/eli/arrete/2018/10/1/TERB1905120A/jo/texte Arrêté préfectoral] 1 October 2018 {{in lang|fr}} < /ref >
