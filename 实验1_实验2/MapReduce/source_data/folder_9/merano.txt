 '''Merano''' ({{lang-de|Meran}}, {{lang-lld|Maran}}) is a city in [[Trentino-Alto Adige/Südtirol]] in northern [[Italy]]. In 2019, 41,172 people lived there. It is best known for its [[spa]] resort.
