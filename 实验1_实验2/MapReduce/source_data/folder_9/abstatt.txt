 '''Abstatt''' is a [[municipality]] of the district of [[Heilbronn (district)|Heilbronn]] in the [[States of Germany|state]] of [[Baden-Württemberg]] in southern [[Germany]]. As of 31 December 2018, 4,803 people lived there.
