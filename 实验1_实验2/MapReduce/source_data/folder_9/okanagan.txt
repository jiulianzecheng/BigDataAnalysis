 The '''Okanagan''' ({{IPAc-en|oʊ|k|ə|ˈ|n|ɑː|ɡ|ən}}), < ref > {{respell|OHK|ə|NAH|gən}}. < /ref >  also called the '''Okanagan Valley''' and sometimes as the '''Okanagan Country''', is an area in [[British Columbia]], [[Canada]], that is located around [[Okanagan Lake]] and the [[Okanagan River]]. It is part of the Okanagan Country, which is also in the [[United States]] as Okanogan Country [[Washington (state)|Washington]]. The region's population is 362,258 in 2016. The largest city is [[Kelowna]].
