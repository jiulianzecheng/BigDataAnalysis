'''Tshaneni''' is a town in northern [[Eswatini|Eswatini (Swaziland)]], near the [[Mananga|border post]] on the [[South Africa|South African]] border. < ref > {{cite book|title=Southern Africa Road Atlas|year=2000|url=https://archive.org/details/lonelyplanetsout00lone|publisher=Lonely Planet|isbn=1-86450-101-4|pages=[https://archive.org/details/lonelyplanetsout00lone/page/71 71]}} < /ref > 
 
 There are large sugar plantations around Tshaneni, < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.times.co.sz/index.php?news=19968|access-date=2010-11-02|title=About E2 billion pumped into LUSIP, Komati projects}} < /ref >  and it is near the Sand River reservoir. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.tourismswaziland.com/swazilandphotos/photolist.asp?lid=25|access-date=2010-11-02|title=Swaziland Tourism Authority Photo Catalogue|archive-date=2011-07-17|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110717080145/http://www.tourismswaziland.com/swazilandphotos/photolist.asp?lid=25|url-status=dead}} < /ref >  It has an airstrip. 
 
 Tshaneni is near the [[Lubombo Mountains]]. 
 
 Tshaneni is in the [[lowveld]] of Eswatini. It is near game reserves, cattle ranches and sugar cane.
