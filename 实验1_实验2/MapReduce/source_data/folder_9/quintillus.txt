 Before Quintillus became emperor, he was the ''[[procurator]]'' (a government official) for [[Sardinia]]. < ref name= " :1 "  / >  Many coins were made during Quintillus's short time as emperor. The ''[[Calendar of Philocalus]]'' says that he was emperor for seventy-seven days, but most [[historians]] agree that he was emperor for only seventeen days. < ref name= " :1 "  / >  The ''[[Historia Augusta]]'' says that Quintillus had a wife and two children. < ref name= " :1 "  / >
