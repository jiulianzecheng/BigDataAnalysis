 '''Timmins''' is a [[city]] in the [[Canada|Canadian]] [[Provinces and territories of Canada|province]] of [[Ontario]]. The population of Timmins was 43,165 in 2011. < ref > {{Cite web|url = http://www12.statcan.gc.ca/census-recensement/2011/as-sa/fogs-spg/Facts-csd-eng.cfm?Lang=Eng & GK=CSD & GC=3556027|title = Focus on Geography Series, 2011 Census: Census subdivision of Timmins, CY - Ontario|first = |last = |date = 7 January 2016|website = Statistics Canada|publisher = Government of Canada|accessdate = 23 February 2016}} < /ref >  The mayor of Timmins is Tom Laughren. Timmins was founded in 1912 by Noah Timmins after there were gold discoveries in the Porcupine Camp, although South Porcupine and Schumacher were formed in 1911. [[Naoshima, Kagawa]] in [[Japan]] is a sister city to Timmins.
