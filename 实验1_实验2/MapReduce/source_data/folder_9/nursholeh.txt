 '''Nursholeh''' (22 August 1957  & ndash; 23 August 2020) was an Indonesian politician. He was Mayor of [[Tegal (city)|Tegal]] between 2017 and 2019. He lost his re-election bid in 2018. < ref > {{cite web |title=Pemilihan Walikota/Bupati Kota Tegal |url=https://infopemilu.kpu.go.id/pilkada2018/hasil/penetapan/t2/jawa_tengah/kota_tegal |website=infopemilu.kpu.go.id |access-date=8 April 2020 |language=id}} < /ref >  He was born in [[Tegal (city)|Tegal]], [[Central Java]]. He was a member of the [[Golkar|Golkar Party]]. 
 
 Nursholeh died on 23 August 2020, one day after his 63rd birthday in Tegal. < ref > [https://regional.kompas.com/read/2020/08/23/15581591/mantan-wali-kota-tegal-m-nursholeh-wafat-semasa-hidup-berjuluk-bapak Mantan Wali Kota Tegal M Nursholeh Wafat, Semasa Hidup Berjuluk Bapak Sastrawan Tegalan] {{in lang|id}} < /ref >
