 A '''Flan''' is a kind of [[tart]] found in [[Britain|British]] [[cuisine]]. < ref > {{cite web|last=Fort|first=Matthew|title=Food for Fort: An oats mill and french flan:How to roll your own oats, bake a flan nature and get perfect smoked fish|url=http://www.guardian.co.uk/lifeandstyle/2009/aug/15/french-flan-oats-mill-smoked-fish|work=The Guardian|date=14 August 2009 |accessdate=16 July 2013}} < /ref >  It consists of an open [[pastry]] or [[Sponge cake|sponge]] case holding a sweet or savoury filling. A typical flan is round, with shortcrust pastry.
