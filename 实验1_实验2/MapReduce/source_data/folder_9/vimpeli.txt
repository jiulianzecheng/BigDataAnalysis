 '''Vimpeli''' ({{lang-sv|Vindala}}) is a municipality in [[Southern Ostrobothnia]], [[Finland]]. It had 2,756 people living there on 31 December 2021. The municipalities next to it are [[Alajärvi]], [[Lappajärvi]], [[Perho]] and [[Veteli]]. 
 
 == Villages == 
 Hallapuro, Huopana, Kirkonkylä, Koskela, Lakaniemi, Pokela, Pyhälahti, Rantakylä, Sääksjärvi, Viitaniemi and Vinni.
