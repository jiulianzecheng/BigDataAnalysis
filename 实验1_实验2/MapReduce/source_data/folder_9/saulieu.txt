 '''Saulieu''', [[Burgundian language (Oïl)|Burgundian]]: ''Saûyeu'', is a [[Communes of France|commune]] in the [[arrondissement of Montbard]] in the [[Côte-d'Or]] [[Departments of France|department]] in eastern [[France]]. It is the capital of the [[Morvan]] Region. 
 
 About 2,500 people were living in Saulieu in 2015.
