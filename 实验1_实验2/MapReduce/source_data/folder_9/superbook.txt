 Superbook is a Christian children's animated series, by the Christian Broadcasting Network, based on two time-travelling kids, Chris and Joy, and their robot friend, Gizmo. In each episode, they are always carried away by a mysterious device to visit stories, people and places in the Bible. The series currently has 5 seasons of 68 episodes.
