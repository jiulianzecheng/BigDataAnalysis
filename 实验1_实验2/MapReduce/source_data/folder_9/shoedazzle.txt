 '''ShoeDazzle''' is an [[United States|American]] [[Online shopping|online]] [[fashion]] [[subscription service]]. It is based in [[El Segundo, California]]. Each month, the company debuts a selection of [[Shoe|shoes]], [[Handbag|handbags]] and [[Jewellery|jewelry]].
