A '''petition''' is a request to change some thing. They are often made to a government official or public entity.  Petitions to a [[deity]] are a form of [[prayer]].  
 
 ==Legal== 
 ''Petition'' can also be the title of a legal [[pleading]] that initiates a [[legal case]].  The initial pleading in a civil lawsuit that seeks only money (damages) might be called (in most U.S. courts) a ''complaint''. An initial pleading in a lawsuit that seeks non-monetary or  " equitable "  relief, such as a request for a [[writ]] of ''[[mandamus]]'' or ''[[habeas corpus]]'', custody of a child, or [[probate]] of a will, is instead called a ''petition''. A '''petitioner''' is a [[person]] who [[Pleading|pleads]] with [[government]]al institution for a [[legal remedy]] or a redress of grievances, through use of a petition.
