 '''Lehua Island''' is a small island in the [[Hawaii]]an islands. It is  {{convert|0.7|mi|km}} north of [[Niihau|Ni{{okina}}ihau]] and directly west of [[Kauai]]. Nobody lives on the island. The area is  {{convert|279|acre|km2|adj=on}} [[Captain]] [[James Cook]] saw the island in 1778. He spelled it  " Oreehoua " .
