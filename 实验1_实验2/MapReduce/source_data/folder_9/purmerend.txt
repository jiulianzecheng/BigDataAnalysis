 '''Purmerend''' is a [[List of municipalities of the Netherlands|municipality]] in the province of [[North Holland]], the Netherlands. As of 2022, about 92,000 people are living there. 
 
 The municipality of [[Beemster]] was added to Purmerend on 1 January 2022.
