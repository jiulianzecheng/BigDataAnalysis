 '''Sebastian Hans Eli Fors''' (born 16 December 1990), known as '''Forsen''', is a Swedish [[Twitch (service)|Twitch]] streamer who is known for playing [[Starcraft II]] and ''[[Hearthstone (video game)|Hearthstone]]''. < ref name= " :0 " > {{cite web|last=Clark|first=Tim|date=9 October 2015|title=Forsen:  " I haven't been happy playing Hearthstone in a long time " |url=http://www.pcgamer.com/forsen-i-havent-been-happy-playing-hearthstone-in-a-long-time/|access-date=14 January 2018|website=PC Gamer}} < /ref >  He is also known for his fans, who call themselves  " Forsenboys "  or  " Forsen [[wikt:bajs|Bajs]] "  and have some involvement in making some [[Internet meme|internet memes]] popular. < ref name= " :0 "  / > < ref > {{cite web|last=Grayson|first=Nathan|date=9 February 2018|title=Twitch's New Policies Are Worrying Some Provocative Streamers|url=https://kotaku.com/twitchs-new-policies-are-worrying-some-provocative-stre-1822884644|access-date=15 February 2019|website=[[Kotaku]]}} < /ref > < ref > {{cite web|last=Parker|first=Jason|date=10 September 2020|title=Forsen Has Been Banned for a Second Time on Twitch in 2020|url=https://www.esportstalk.com/news/forsen-has-been-banned-second-time-on-twitch-2020/|access-date=30 December 2020|website=EsportsTalk|archive-date=28 February 2021|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210228213347/https://www.esportstalk.com/news/forsen-has-been-banned-second-time-on-twitch-2020/|url-status=dead}} < /ref >  In January 2018, a warped image of Fors' face ( " forsenE " ) became the most-used [[Emoticon|emote]] on Twitch worldwide. < ref > {{cite web|last=Alexander|first=Julia|date=29 January 2018|title=How Twitch's most popular emote, forsenE, took over|url=https://www.polygon.com/2018/1/29/16938042/forsen-emote-twitch-ugandan-knuckles-meme|access-date=15 February 2019|website=[[Polygon (website)|Polygon]]}} < /ref >
