 '''Wallenfels''' is a [[Town#Germany|town]] in [[Kronach (district)|Kronach]], in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. It is in the [[Frankenwald]], 11 & nbsp;km east of [[Kronach]], and 32 & nbsp;km west of [[Hof, Germany|Hof]]. 
 
 It is a twin town of [[Bingham, Nottinghamshire]], [[England]]. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 Wallenfels is on the [[List_of_federal_highways_in_Germany#B_101_–_B_200|B 173]], between [[Hof, Germany|Hof]] and [[Coburg, Germany|Coburg]]. Bordering communities are [[Kronach]], [[Marktrodach]], [[Schwarzenbach am Wald]], and [[Presseck]].
