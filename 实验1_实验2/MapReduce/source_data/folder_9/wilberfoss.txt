 '''Wilberfoss''' is a [[village]] and [[civil parishes in England|civil parish]] in the [[East Riding of Yorkshire]], England.  It is on the [[north]] side of the [[A1079 road|A1079]] about {{Convert|8|mi|km}} east of [[York]] [[city]] [[wikt:center|centre]]. It is  {{Convert|11|mi|km}} [[northwest]] of [[Market Weighton]].
