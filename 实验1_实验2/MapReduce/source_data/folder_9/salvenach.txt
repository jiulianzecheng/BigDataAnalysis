 '''Salvenach''' ({{lang-fr|Salvagny}}) was a [[municipality]] of the district [[See (district of Fribourg)|See]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Fribourg (canton)|Fribourg]] in [[Switzerland]].
