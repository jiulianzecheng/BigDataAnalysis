 '''Pekanbaru''' ([[Jawi script|Jawi]]: '''ڤكنبارو''') is the [[capital city]] of [[Riau]] [[province]], [[Indonesia]]. It is the third largest [[city]] on the [[island]] of [[Sumatra]]. < ref name=MOT > {{cite web |url=http://www.indonesia.travel/en/destination/913/pekanbaru-capital-of-the-riau-province |title=Pekanbaru, capital of the Riau Province |author= |date= |website= |publisher=Ministry of Tourism, Republic of Indonesia |accessdate=12 July 2015}} < /ref >  It has an area of {{convert|632.26|km2|abbr=off}}. As of 2014 about 1,030,732 people live there. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://www.citypopulation.de/php/indonesia-admin.php?adm2id=1471 |title=KOTA PEKANBARU (City) |author= |date= |website= |publisher=citypopulation.de |accessdate=12 July 2015}} < /ref >  Pekanbaru has several [[Industry|industries]]. It [[export]]s [[palm oil]], [[paper]] and [[crude oil]]. < ref name=MOT/ >  It is a very clean city. [[Ferry]] [[passenger]]s from [[Singapore]] used it as a [[wikt:transfer|transfer]] point before low-cost [[air travel]] became more [[wikt:popular|popular]]. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://www.lonelyplanet.com/indonesia/sumatra/pekanbaru |title=Introducing Pekanbaru |author= |date= |website= |publisher=Lonely Planet |accessdate=12 July 2015}} < /ref >
