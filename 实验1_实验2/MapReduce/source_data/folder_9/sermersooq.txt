 '''Sermersooq''' ([[Greenlandic language|Greenlandic]]: [seʁmeʁsoːq̚], ''place of much ice'', {{lang-da|sted med meget is}}) is the most populous [[municipality]] of [[Greenland]]. Its capital and largest city is [[Nuuk]], which is also the capital and largest city of Greenland. It was created on 1 January 2009, when five municipalities were combined together to make Sermersooq. 
 
 == Geography == 
 Sermersooq borders the [[Labrador Sea]] to the west and the [[Denmark Strait]] to the east. It borders all the municipalities of [[Kujalleq]], [[Qeqqata]], [[Qeqertalik]] and [[Avanaata]]. To the north is [[Northeast Greenland National Park]].
