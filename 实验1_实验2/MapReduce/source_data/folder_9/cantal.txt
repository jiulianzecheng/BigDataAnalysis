 '''Cantal''' is a [[France|French]] [[Departments of France|department]] in south-central [[France]] in the [[Auvergne-Rhône-Alpes]] [[Regions of France|region]]. Its [[Prefectures in France|prefecture]] is [[Aurillac]]. 
 
 It is named after the [[Mounts of Cantal]], a group of [[mountain]]s in the [[Massif Central]]. 
 
 ==History== 
 Cantal is one of the 83 French departments made during the [[French revolution]], on 4 March 1790. It was created with part of the territory of the old province of [[Auvergne]] in south central France. It was divided in four [[district]]s: Aurillac, Mauriac, Murat and Saint-Flour; its [[capital city|capital]] in Saint-Flour, alternating with Aurillac. < ref name=splaf > {{cite web |url=http://splaf.free.fr/15his.html |title=Historique du Cantal |work=Le SPLAF |language=French |accessdate=21 January 2017 |date= }} < /ref > 
 
 In 1794, Saint-Flour became the only capital but, in 1795, the capital was moved to Aurillac. 
 
 In 1800, with the creation of the [[Arrondissements of France|''arrondissements'']] in France, the districts were changed into ''arrondissements'': Aurillac, Mauriac, Murat and Saint-Flour. On 10 September 1926, the ''arrondissement'' of Murat was eliminated. < ref name=splaf / > 
 
 ==Geography== 
 Cantal is part of the [[Auvergne-Rhône-Alpes]] [[Regions of France|region]], in south central France, with an area of {{Convert|5726|km2|sqmi|0|abbr=on }}. 
 
 The department borders with 3 regions and 7 other departments: < ref name=splaf / > 
 * [[Auvergne-Rhône-Alpes]] region 
 ** [[Puy-de-Dôme]] (north) 
 ** [[Haute-Loire]] (northeast) 
 * [[Occitanie]] region  
 ** [[Aveyron]] (south) 
 ** [[Lot (department)|Lot]] (southwest) 
 ** [[Lozère]] (southeast) 
 * [[Nouvelle-Aquitaine]] region 
 **  [[Corrèze]] (west)

 Cantal is in the middle half of the central [[plateau]] (''[[Massif Central]]'') of France where is the [[Mounts of Cantal]] [[mountain range]], a group of extinct [[volcano]]es. The highest point in the Cantal mountains, and in the department is ''[[Plomb du Cantal]]'' ({{Coord|45|3|33|N|2|45|41|E|name=Plomb du Cantal |display=inline |region:ID_type:mountain }}) that is {{Convert|1855|m|ft|0|abbr=on}} high. < ref name=pbag > {{cite web |url=http://www.peakbagger.com/peak.aspx?pid=9701 |title=Plomb du Cantal, France |publisher=Peakbagger.com |language= |accessdate=21 January 2017 |date= }} < /ref >  Other high mountains near Plomb du Cantal in the Cantal department are: 
 * ''Puy du Rocher'' ({{Coord|45|4|N|2|46|E|name=Puy du Rocher |display=inline |region:ID_type:mountain }}), {{Convert|1813|m|ft|0|abbr=on}} high; 
 * ''Puy de Peyre Arse'' ({{Coord|45|6|37|N|2|42|39|E|name=Puy de Peyre Arse |display=inline |region:ID_type:mountain }}), {{Convert|1806|m|ft|0|abbr=on}} high; and 
 * ''Puy Mary'' ({{Coord|45|6|34|N|2|40|34|E|name=Puy Mary |display=inline |region:ID_type:mountain }}), {{Convert|1783|m|ft|0|abbr=on}} high. 
 
 The lowest point of the department, {{Convert|250|m|ft|abbr=on }}, is in the [[valley]] of the [[Lot (river)|Lot]] river where it leaves the department. 
 
 The main [[river]]s are the [[Alagnon]], a [[tributary]] to the [[Allier (river)|Allier]]; the [[Célé]] and [[Truyère]] rivers, [[Tributary|tributaries]] to the [[Lot (river)|Lot]] river; and the [[Cère]] and [[Rhue (river)|Rhue]] rivers, tributaries to the [[Dordogne (river)|Dordogne]] river. 
 
 ===Climate=== 
 The climate of Cantal has with few extremes of temperature and ample [[precipitation]] in all months. It belongs to the subtype '''Cfb''' (Marine West Coast Climate) in the [[Köppen climate classification]]. 
 
 The average temperature for the year in Aurillac, at an [[altitude]] of {{Convert|633|m|ft|abbr=on }}, is {{Convert|10.2|°C|°F }}. The warmest month, on average, is July with an average temperature of {{Convert|18.3|°C|°F }}. The coolest month on average is January, with an average temperature of {{Convert|2.9|°C|°F }}. 
 
 The average amount of precipitation for the year in Aurillac is {{Convert|856|mm|in|1|abbr=on }}. The month with the most precipitation on average is December with {{Convert|109.2|mm|in|1|abbr=on }} of precipitation. The month with the least precipitation on average is June with an average of {{Convert|45.7|mm|in|1|abbr=on }}. < ref name= " wbase " > {{cite web|work= |publisher=Weatherbase |title=Aurillac, France - Köppen Climate Classification |url=http://www.weatherbase.com/weather/weather-summary.php3?s=598805 & cityname=Aurillac%2C+Auvergne%2C+France & units=|language= |accessdate=21 January 2017 }} < /ref > 
 
 ==Administration== 
 Cantal is part of the region of [[Auvergne-Rhône-Alpes]]. The department is managed by the ''Departamental Council of the Cantal'' in [[Aurillac]]. 
 
 ===Administrative divisions=== 
 There are 3 [[Arrondissements of France|''arrondissements'']] ([[district]]s), 15 [[Cantons in France|''cantons'']] and 255 [[Communes in France|''communes'']] ([[Municipality|municipalities]]) in Cantal.

 ==Demographics== 
 The inhabitants of Cantal are known, in [[French language|French]], as ''Cantaliens'' (women: ''Cantaliennes'') or ''Cantalous'' (women: ''Cantalouses''). < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.habitants.fr/cantal-15|title=Cantal|publisher=habitants.fr |language=French |accessdate=21 January 2017 |date= }} < /ref > 
 
 Cantal has a population, in 2014, of 146,618, < ref name=insee / >  for a population density of {{Pop density|146618|5726|km2|prec=1|disp=num}} inhabitants/km < sup > 2 < /sup > . The ''arrondissement'' of Aurillac, with 82,347 inhabitants, is by far the largest. The other two, Saint-Flour and Mauriac, have respectively 38,079 and 26,192 inhabitants. < ref name=insee2 / > 
 
 The city with more people living in it is the [[Capital city|capital]], [[Aurillac]] (26,135). The subprefectures of [[Saint-Flour, Cantal|Saint-Flour]] and [[Mauriac, Cantal|Mauriac]] have, respectively, 6,643 and 3,682 inhabitants. < ref name=insee2 / >
