 '''Jochebed''' (JOCK-oh-bed) was the mother of [[Moses]]; a [[Israel|Israelite]] [[Prophet|prophet]], according to [[The Bible|biblical]] legend. 
 Jochebed lived in [[Egypt]] and had three children; [[Moses]], [[Miriam]], and [[Aaron]]. When the [[pharaoh]] ordered all [[Hebrew people|Hebrew]] newborn children to die, Jochebed floated Moses down the river to save his life.
