 '''Hesperange''' ({{lang-lb|Hesper}}, {{lang-de|Hesperingen}}) is a [[Communes of Luxembourg|commune]] in [[Luxembourg]]. It is part of the [[Luxembourg (canton)|canton of Luxembourg]]. 
 
 As of 2019, 15,479 people live here.
