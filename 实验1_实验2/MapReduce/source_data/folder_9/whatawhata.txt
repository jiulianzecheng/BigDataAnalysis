 '''Whatawhata''' is a village in the region of [[Waikato]], on the [[North Island]] of [[New Zealand]]. It had 2,475 people in 2013. 
 
 It lies on the eastern side of the [[Waipa River]]. 
 
 State Highways 23 and 39 cross each other at Whatawhata.
