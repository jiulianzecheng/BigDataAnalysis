 A '''prisoner''' is a person who is forced to stay in a [[prison]] against their will. People who are suspected of a [[crime]], for example, a [[murder]], may be  [[arrest]] < nowiki/ > ed and held in [[jail]] so they won't escape before their [[trial]]. After [[conviction]] a prisoner may be punished. For example, they may be imprisoned for a fixed number of years, or receive a [[life imprisonment|life sentence]] meaning they stay in jail until they die.  
 
 A [[prisoner of war]] is a person who was captured by the enemy in a [[war]].
