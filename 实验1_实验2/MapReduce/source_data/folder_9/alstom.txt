'''Alstom''' is a [[France|French]] [[company]] specialized in [[rail transport]]. It is based in [[Saint-Ouen-sur-Seine]], France. It is one of the largest rail transport manufacturer in the world. 
 
 In 2021, the Group bought  the  [[Bombardier Transportation]] company. < ref > [https://www.lepoint.fr/economie/alstom-rachete-bombardier-transport-pour-5-5-milliards-d-euros-29-01-2021-2411807_28.php Alstom rachète Bombardier Transport pour 5,5 milliards d’euros] < /ref > 
 
 The company was founded in 1928. < ref > [https://www.usinenouvelle.com/article/1928-2014-l-histoire-d-alstom-de-general-electric-a-general-electric.N257764 1928 - 2014 : l'histoire d'Alstom, de General Electric à General Electric ?] < /ref >
