[[Perelandra]] is a book by [[C. S. Lewis]]. It is the [[sequel]] to [[Out of the Silent Planet]]. In it Dr. [[Elwin Ransom]] goes to [[Venus]] and has to stop [[Professor Weston]] from convincing its version of [[Eve (Bible)|Eve]] to [[sin]]. 
 
 It was first published in 1943.
