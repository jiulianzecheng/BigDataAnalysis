The '''International Society of City and Regional Planners''' ('''ISOCARP''') is an [[International organization|international]] [[Voluntary association|association]] of people who work in [[urban planning]]. It was created in 1965. It now has members from more than 80 countries. ISOCARP is formally recognised by the [[United Nations]] and [[Council of Europe]]. It works with [[UNESCO]]. It is not under the control of any government.
