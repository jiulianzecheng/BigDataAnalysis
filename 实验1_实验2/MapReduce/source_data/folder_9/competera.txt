 '''Competera''' is an [[artificial intelligence]] platform that helps [[online and offline]] [[retail]]ers with portfolio [[pricing]] using price recommendations on an SKU and full category level. < ref > {{Cite web|last=Galkin|first=Aleksandr|title=Council Post: From Strategy To Tactics: Why You Need Technology For Better Pricing Execution|url=https://www.forbes.com/sites/forbestechcouncil/2020/07/21/from-strategy-to-tactics-why-you-need-technology-for-better-pricing-execution/|access-date=2021-05-25|website=Forbes|language=en}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite web|date=2018-09-11|title=How the Ukrainian company Competera grew into a million-dollar business without any outside investments|url=https://ain.ua/en/2018/09/11/how-the-ukrainian-company-competera-grew-into-a-million-dollar-business-without-any-outside-investments/|access-date=2021-05-25|website=AIN.UA|language=en-US}} < /ref > 
 
 == Overview == 
 Competera was launched on April 23, 2014. The founders are Alexander Galkin, Andrey Mikhailov, and Alexander Sazonov. < ref > {{Cite web|last=Team|first=Startup Info|title=Reinventing Retail Pricing for Global Brands in Times of Uncertainty|url=https://startup.info/aleksandr-galkin-competera/|access-date=2021-05-25|language=en-US}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite web|last=Jane|title=Stanford Guest Lecturer in Pricing, Ph.D., and Venture Investor Maciej Kraus Joins Competera as Advisor|url=https://competera.net/resources/articles/press-release-maciej-kraus-joins-competera-as-advisor|url-status=live|access-date=July 6, 2020|language=en-US}}{{Dead link|date=August 2021|bot=InternetArchiveBot|fix-attempted=yes}} < /ref > 
 
 The head office is in [[Kyiv]], [[Ukraine]] with more offices in [[California]], [[Singapore]], and [[London]]. < ref > {{Cite web|date=2020-07-17|title=Venture Investor and Stanford Lecturer, Ph.D. Maciej Kraus Joins Award-Winning AI Startup Competera as Advisor|url=https://aithority.com/hot-startups/venture-investor-and-stanford-lecturer-ph-d-maciej-kraus-joins-award-winning-ai-startup-competera-as-advisor/|access-date=2021-05-25|website=AiThority|language=en-US}} < /ref >
