 '''Mbabane''' is the [[capital (city)|capital city]] of the [[Africa]]n [[country]] of [[Swaziland|Eswatini]].  It is on the [[Mbabane River]] and the [[Polinjane River]] in the [[Mdimba Mountains]]. The city is in the district of [[Hhohho]] and is also the district capital. The city has an estimated [[population]] of 70,000 people. 
 
 The town grew after the country's [[administration|administrative]] centre moved there from [[Manzini]] in 1902.  It is also the [[Commerce|commercial]] center for the surrounding [[region]]. [[Tin]] and [[iron]] are [[mining|mined]] nearby. 
 
 [[Swazi language|siSwati]] is the main [[language]] of the city but many people also speak [[English language|English]]. The [[economy]] of Mbabane, like the country as a whole, is based on [[tourism]] and [[sugar]] [[export]]s.
