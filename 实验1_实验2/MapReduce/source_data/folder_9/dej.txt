 '''Dej''' ({{lang-hu|Dés}}; {{lang-de|Desch, Burglos}}) is a city in [[Cluj County]], [[Transylvania]], [[Romania]]. It is {{convert|60|km|mi}} north of [[Cluj-Napoca]]. It is where the river [[Someșul Mic]] meets the river [[Someșul Mare]].
