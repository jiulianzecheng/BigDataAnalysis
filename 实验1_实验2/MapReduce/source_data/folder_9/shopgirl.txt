 '''''Shopgirl''''' is a 2005 American romantic [[Comedy drama|comedy-drama]] movie. It was directed by Anand Tucker. The movie starred with [[Steve Martin]], [[Claire Danes]], and [[Jason Schwartzman]]. The [[screenplay]] by Steve Martin is based on his 2000 novella of the same title. The movie is about a complex love triangle between a bored salesgirl, a wealthy businessman, and an aimless young man. 
 
 It was distributed by [[Walt Disney Studios Motion Pictures|Buena Vista Pictures]] (in United States) and [[20th Century Studios|20th Century Fox]] (international). ''Shopgirl'' was released on October 21, 2005. It received positive reviews from film critics. The film went on to earn $11,112,077. < ref name= " BOM " > {{mojo title|shopgirl|Shopgirl}} < /ref >  It was nominated for four [[Satellite Awards]], including Best Picture and Best Adapted Screenplay.

 == Production notes == 
 In [[Steve Martin]]'s original novella, Mirabelle was employed by Neiman Marcus. The gloves in the counter are not from Saks. But a boutique in Toronto, where some of the film was shot. 
 
 The apartment building used for Mirabelle's residence is located at 1630 Griffith Park Boulevard in [[Los Angeles]]. 
 
 The film grossed $10,284,523 in the US and $1,303,682 in foreign markets for a total worldwide box office of $11,588,205. < ref name= " BOM " > {{Mojo title|shopgirl|Shopgirl}} < /ref >
