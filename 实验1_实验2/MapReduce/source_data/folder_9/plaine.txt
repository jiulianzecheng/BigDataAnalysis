 '''Plaine''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is in [[Grand Est]] in the [[Bas-Rhin]] [[Departments of France|department]] in northeast [[France]]. It has five hamlets: Le Bambois, Champenay, Devant Fouday, Diespach and Poutay.
