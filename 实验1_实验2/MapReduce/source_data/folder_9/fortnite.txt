 '''''Fortnite''''' is a [[Third-person shooter|third person shooter]] survival game where the player has to survive against up to 99 other players. The total player count for each [[Battle royale game|battle royale]] is 100. The player can build forts through collecting materials such as metal, bricks, and wood, and collect weapons before fighting other players. The main game also has a mode called ''[[Fortnite: Save The World]]'', which is also available as a separate game. While Battle Royale is free, Save The World is a paid game. The game can be played on [[Nintendo Switch]], [[Microsoft Windows|Windows]], [[PlayStation 4|PS4]], [[PlayStation 5|PS5]], [[Xbox]], and [[Android (operating system)|Android]].   
 
 ==Game modes== 
 ===Battle Royale=== 
 {{main|Fortnite Battle Royale}} 
 100 players compete to win. Sometimes there are computer-controlled enemies. Players compete with each other to fight to the death. Players start with 100 health and 0 shield. In the game's  " [[Zero Build]] "  mode, players can have an Overshield which provides 50 more shield and [[Regeneration|regenerates]] when the player is not taking damage. in addition to their health and shield, players can find shield items to consume for a max of 200 hit points (or 100 health/100 shields). Players lose health and shields if they get shot by weapons, if they fall from too high, or take slow damage in the storm. The storm approaches over the course of a game. If the player loses all their health, they will lose. In ''Fortnite'', there are different kinds of weaponry, including guns, boogie bombs and special items. Weapons can be obtained throughout the game by grabbing them in the open, opening chests and supply drops, [[looting]] them off enemies and eliminated players and fishing from the water. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://trackerfortnite.com/fortnite-guide|title=Fortnite Guide}} < /ref >  The winner of the match is the one who is the last man standing. 
 
 At the beginning of the game, players can get to different locations by [[skydiving]] from a flying school bus titled the Battle Bus. < ref > {{cite news|url= https://www.shacknews.com/article/106893/how-to-say-thank-you-to-the-bus-driver-in-fortnite| title=How to say thank you to the bus driver in Fortnite |work= Shack News |date=2018-08-24|access-date=2018-12-18| first1= Sam |last1=Chandler}} < /ref >  The battle bus goes in a straight line over the map, but the path is not always the same.

 ===Save the World=== 
 ''Fortnite: Save the World'' is the original version of the game, first released on July 27, 2017. 
 
 Players must cooperate with others to kill Husks and complete various missions. One to four players cooperate towards a single goal that they both need to achieve in order to survive, and to win the mission. There are 4 main named locations that are unlocked by progressing through the campaign. The first is Stonewood, the weakest of the 4 areas. Next is the mid-level Plankerton, followed by the high Level Canny Valley, ending with the last location, and the hardest, Twine Peaks. There are many other locations as well, added with events. One such is Scurvy Shoals, a tropical themed area. 
 
 == Competitive == 
 Competitive Fortnite is played in Arena and [[tournament]] < nowiki/ > s. There are different kinds of tournaments. There are tournaments that everyone can play (free tournaments). They usually do not have any cash rewards. The cups everyone gets to compete in are usually skin cups, where top 100 in each region gets a skin before its released to the item shop. For example, [[Thanos]] had a skin cup.  
 
 Then there are Contender cups. In Contender cups, players need to be in the Contender League in a game mode called  " Arena " . In order to get in a higher league, they need to earn  " hype "  a.k.a.  " arena points " . There are points for getting kills, placements and wins. There are different divisions, and how many points needed to be in it:  
 
 * Open League. 1 < sup > st < /sup >  Division –0-300 Hype. 2 < sup > nd < /sup >  Division – 400 – 799 Hype. 3 < sup > rd < /sup >  Division – 800-1,199. ... 
 * Contender League. Division 5 – 2,000-2,999. Division 6 – 3,000-4,999. Division 7 – 5,000 – 7,499. 
 * Champion. Division 8 – 7,500 – 9,999. Division 9 – 10,000 – 14,999. Division 10 – 15,000+. 
 
 In contender league cups, players just get arena points for participating. When they reach 7,500 arena points, they get to  " Champion " . These are tournaments for real money. There are also  top tier tournaments, like [[FNCS]], in contenders league, people can still play skin cups, and other special cups.

Cosmetic items can be bought by the use of [[V-Bucks]], the in-game currency.
