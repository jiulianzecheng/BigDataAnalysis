 '''Khalas''' is a Palestinian Israeli-Arab [[rock band]], started in 1999. The name of the band means  " enough "  in Arabic. < ref > Earache, Asher. Interview with Bassam Beromi, Abed Hathout and Fadel Kandil of Khalas. [https://web.archive.org/web/20080424203547/http://www.hardandheavy.net/eng/interviews/index.php?op=1 & id=183 HardAndHeavy.net, archived by Archive.org] < /ref > 
 
 In 2013, the band started on a tour across Europe along with [[Israel|Israeli]] band [[Orphaned Land]], an event which was reported on by Western media outlets such as [[The Guardian]] < ref > {{cite news|last=Sherwood|first=Harriet|date=July 21, 2013|title=Israeli and Palestinian bands unite in 'metal brotherhood'|work=Guardian|url=https://www.theguardian.com/world/2013/jul/21/israeli-palestinian-bands-unite-tour|accessdate=March 28, 2016}} < /ref >  and [[CNN]]. < ref > {{cite news|last=Mackay|first=Mairi|date=August 12, 2013|title=Rock the conflict: Jewish and Palestinian metal bands tour for peace|work=CNN|url=http://edition.cnn.com/2013/08/11/world/meast/israel-palestinian-jewish-rock-bands-for-peace/|accessdate=March 28, 2016}} < /ref >
