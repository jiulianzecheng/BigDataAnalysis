 '''Ludlow''' is a town in [[Shropshire]], a [[county]] of [[England]]. The town is very old and dates back to around 800 [[AD]]. There is a large [[castle]] and many other [[History|historic]] [[building]]s.  
 
 There are just over 10,000 people living in the town. 
 
 It is the largest town in the [[South Shropshire]] area. 
 
 The town, though not large, is important in its area, which includes the [[Welsh Marches]], [[Wales]] and [[South Shropshire]].
