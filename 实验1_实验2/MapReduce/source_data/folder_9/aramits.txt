 '''Aramits''' is a [[Commune in France|commune]] of the [[Pyrénées-Atlantiques]] ''[[departments of France|département]]'' in the region of [[Nouvelle-Aquitaine]], in southwestern [[France]]. 
 
 The people living in Aramits are called ''Aramitsiens'' or ''Aramitsiennes''. < ref > [http://www.habitants.fr/habitants_departement_pyrenees-atlantiques_64.html Inhabitants of Pyrénées-Atlantiques] {{in lang|fr}} < /ref > < ref name= " Jobbé " > [[Brigitte Jobbé-Duval]], Dictionary of place names - Pyrénées-Atlantiques, 2009, Archives and Culture, {{ISBN|978-2-35077-151-9}} {{in lang|fr}} < /ref > 
 
 At the 2009 Census, the commune had a population of 677.
