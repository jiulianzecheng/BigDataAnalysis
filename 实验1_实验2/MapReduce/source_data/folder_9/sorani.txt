 '''Sorani Kurdish''' (also known as '''Central Kurdish''') is a variety of [[Kurdish language|Kurdish]] spoken in [[Iran]] and [[Iraq]].  It is written in the [[Arabic alphabet]] unlike [[Kurmanji]] which is written in the [[Latin alphabet]].  There are 8 million speakers.
