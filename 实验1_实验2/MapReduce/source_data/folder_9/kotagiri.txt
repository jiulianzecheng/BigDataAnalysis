[[Kotagiri]] is a panchayat town in [[The Nilgiris district]] of [[Tamil Nadu]], [[India]]. As of 2011 Census, the population of the town is 28,207 of which 13,607 are males while 14,600 are females. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.census2011.co.in/data/town/803565-kotagiri.html|title=Kotagiri Town Panchayat City Population Census 2011-2019 - Tamil Nadu|website=www.census2011.co.in}} < /ref >
