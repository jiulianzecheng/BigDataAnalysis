In [[Greek mythology]], '''Laomedon''' was a king of [[Troy]]. He was the son of [[Ilus]]. Laomedon was the father of King [[Priam]], and [[Astyoche]], [[Lampus]], [[Hicetaon]], [[Clytius]], [[Cilla]], [[Proclia]], [[Aethilla]], [[Medesicaste]], [[Clytodora]] and [[Hesione]]. The king owned a team of divine horses. [[Zeus]] gave these horses to [[Tros]] (Laomedon's grandfather) as payment after Zeus kidnapped Tros' son, [[Ganymede (mythology)|Ganymede]].
