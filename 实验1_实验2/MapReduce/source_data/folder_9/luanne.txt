 ''' " Luanne " ''' is a 1982 song by British American band [[Foreigner (band)|Foreigner]] and is the fifth song from their fourth studio album ''[[4 (Foreigner album)|4]]''. It went to number 75 in the United States. The B-side contains the live version of 1978 song ''[[Hot Blooded]]''.
