'''Murupara''' is a town located in the [[Whakatāne District]] and [[Bay of Plenty Region]] of New Zealand's [[North Island]]. The town is located in an isolated part of the region between the [[Kaingaroa Forest]] and [[Te Urewera (protected area)|Te Urewera protected area]], on the banks of the [[Rangitaiki River]], 65 kilometres southeast of [[Rotorua]]. < ref > {{cite web| url=http://www.jasons.co.nz/murupara| title=Murupara Travel Guide| publisher=[[Jasons Travel Media]]| access-date=2021-05-18| archive-date=2012-09-12| archive-url=https://www.webcitation.org/6Ac7SkqzS?url=http://www.jasons.co.nz/murupara| url-status=dead}} < /ref >
