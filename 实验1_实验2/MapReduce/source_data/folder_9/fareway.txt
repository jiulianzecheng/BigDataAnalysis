'''Fareway Stores''' is an [[United States|American]] [[grocery]] [[company]]. Fareway is based in [[Boone, Iowa]]. It operates over 120 grocery stores in [[South Dakota]], [[Nebraska]], [[Minnesota]], [[Iowa]] and [[Illinois]].  
 
 Fareway is a family-owned business in the [[Midwestern United States]]. Some stores open as early as 6am. Some remain open unti 11pm. All Fareway grocery stores are closed Sundays and major holidays. 
 
 Fareway was founded in 1938.
