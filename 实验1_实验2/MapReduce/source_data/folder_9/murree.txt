 '''Murree''' is a [[mountain resort]] town in the [[Rawalpindi District]] of [[Punjab (Pakistan)|Punjab]], [[Pakistan]]. It is in the north of Punjab and is near the [[Khyber Pakhtunkhwa]] province and [[Kashmir]]. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://tribune.com.pk/story/630567/a-british-town-in-the-hills-book-on-murree-launched/|title=A British town in the hills: Book on Murree launched|date=12 November 2013|website=The Express Tribune}} < /ref >
