 '''''Tatosoma''''' is a [[genus]] of [[moth]]s. It is part of the family [[Geometridae]]. 
 
 Some of the [[species]] in this genus are: 
 * ''Tatosoma alta''  & ndash;  < small > Philpott, 1913 < /small > 
 * ''Tatosoma agrionata''  & ndash;  < small > Walker, 1862 < /small > 
 * ''Tatosoma apicipallida''  & ndash;  < small > Prout, 1914 < /small > 
 * ''Tatosoma lestevata''  & ndash;  < small > Philpott, 1914 < /small > 
 * ''Tatosoma monoviridisata''  & ndash;  < small > Clarke, 1920 < /small > 
 * ''Tatosoma tipulata''  & ndash;  < small > Walker, 1862 < /small > 
 * ''Tatosoma topia''  & ndash;  < small > Philpott, 1903 < /small >
