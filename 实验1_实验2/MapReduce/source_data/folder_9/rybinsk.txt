 '''Rybinsk''' ({{lang-rus|Рыбинск|p=ˈrɨbʲɪnsk}}) is the second largest [[city]] of [[Yaroslavl Oblast]] in [[Russia]]. It is at the [[confluence]] of the [[Volga River|Volga]] and [[Sheksna River|Sheksna Rivers]]. It is 267 kilometers north-north-east of [[Moscow]]. [[Population]]: {{ru-census|p2010=200,771|p2002=222,653|p1989=251,442}} 
 
 It used to be named '''Ust-Sheksna''' (until 1504), '''Rybnaya Sloboda''' (until 1777), '''Shcherbakov''' (1946–1957), and '''Andropov''' (1984–1989).
