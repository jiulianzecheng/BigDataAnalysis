'''Barbz''' are the [[fandom|fanbase]] of Trinidadian rapper [[Nicki Minaj]]. Regarded by journalists as one of the largest and most devoted fanbases, Barbz are known for their high levels of participation, creativity, community and [[w:fanaticism|fanaticism]]. They are a subject of widespread coverage in the mainstream media due to their controversial behavior. < ref > {{cite web |title=Meet The Barbz: The Nicki Minaj Fandom |url=https://www.rollingstone.com/music/music-news/meet-the-barbz-the-nicki-minaj-fandom-fighting-the-nicki-hate-train-705438/}} < /ref > 
 
 Barbz have been subject to criticism by the media because of their almost [[cult]]-like behavior around Nicki Minaj. They have been embroiled in many controversies with rappers that Minaj has argued with such as [[Cardi B]] and [[Latto]]. < ref > {{cite web |title=Barbz BLAST Latto over Nicki Minaj Beef! |url=https://thatgrapejuice.net/2022/09/barbz-blast-latto-perceived-nicki-minaj-diss/}} < /ref > 
 
 Barbz are named after Minaj's common nickname as the  " Harajuku Barbie " .
