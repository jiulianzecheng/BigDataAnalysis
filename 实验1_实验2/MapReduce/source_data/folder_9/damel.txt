The '''Damel''' are a [[Dards|Dardic group]] who live in [[Damel Valley]], [[Chitral District]], [[Khyber Pakhtunkhwa]], [[Pakistan]]. < ref > {{cite web | url=https://www.tripmondo.com/pakistan/khyber-pakhtunkhwa/damel/ | title=Destination Guide: Damel (Khyber Pakhtunkhwa) in Pakistan  & #124; Tripmondo }} < /ref >   Most Damels speak [[Dameli language|Dameli]] while others speak [[Gawar-Bati]].  They number around 8,300 and are [[Sunni Islam|Sunni Muslims]]. < ref > https://www.peoplegroups.org/explore/groupdetails.aspx?peid=6340#topmenu < /ref >
