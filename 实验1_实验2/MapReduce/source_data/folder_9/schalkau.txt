 '''Schalkau''' is a [[Germany|German]] [[town]] in the south of [[Thuringia]]. Schalkau is in the [[Sonneberg Rural District|district of Sonneberg]], in [[Thuringia]], [[Germany]]. It is {{convert|13|km|abbr=off}} west of [[Sonneberg]], and {{convert|15|km|abbr=off}} north of [[Coburg]]. 
 
 The population of Schalkau is about 3,500. The mayor of the city is Rheinhard Zehner. 
 
 The former municipality [[Bachfeld]] was merged into Schalkau on 31 December 2019.
