 '''Droseraceae''' is a family of [[flowering plant]]s. The family is also known under its common name, the sundew family. 
 
 It consists of [[carnivorous plant]]s: besides the [[sundew]]s, the genus ''[[Drosera]]'', it also contains the even more famous [[Venus flytrap]] (''Dionaea muscipula''). The sundews produce sticky substances on their leaves that traps prey, and the Venus fly trap has leaves that form traps that close when disturbed.
