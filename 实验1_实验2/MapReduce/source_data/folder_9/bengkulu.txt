 '''Bengkulu''' is a [[Provinces of Indonesia|province]] in [[Sumatra]], [[Indonesia]]. The capital and biggest city is [[Bengkulu (city)|Bengkulu City]]. [[Rafflesia]] is the icon of this province. The wife of [[Sukarno|Soekarno]], [[Fatmawati]] was born in this capital.
