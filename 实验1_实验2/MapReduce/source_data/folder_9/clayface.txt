 '''Clayface''' is a series of fictional [[Batman]] villains who have the power to turn into any form because he is made out of [[clay]]. He has appeared in American comic books published by [[DC Comics]]. In most cases, Clayface was played by actor Basil Karlo who used a type of skin medicine to help his skin which would later cause him to have the clay appearance. 
 
 He was voiced by [[Ron Perlman]] in ''[[Batman: The Animated Series]]''.
