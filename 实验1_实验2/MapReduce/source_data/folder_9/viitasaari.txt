 '''Viitasaari''' is a small [[city]] in [[Central Finland]]. The municipalities next to it are [[Kannonkoski]], [[Keitele]], [[Kinnula]], [[Kivijärvi]], [[Pihtipudas]], [[Vesanto]] and [[Äänekoski]]. Most people speak [[Finnish language|Finnish]] 
 
 ==Nature== 
 Viitasaari is known about [[lake]]s and [[rapid]]s.
