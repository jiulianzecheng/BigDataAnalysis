 '''Sochaux''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is in [[Bourgogne-Franche-Comté]] in the [[Doubs]] [[Departments of France|department]] in east [[France]], near [[Montbéliard]]. 
 
 == Economy == 
 Sochaux is the site of a large industrial facility of the French car manufacturer [[PSA Peugeot Citroën]]. As of late 2005, about 16,000 people are employed there. The [[Musée de l'Aventure Peugeot|Peugeot automobile museum]] is located here. 
 
 == Sport == 
 The [[FC Sochaux-Montbéliard]] football club is in the commune.
