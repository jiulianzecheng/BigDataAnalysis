 '''Albertville''' is a [[France|French]] [[Communes of France|commune]] in the [[Savoie]] [[Departments of France|department]]. Its [[population]] is about 17,300 ([[1999]] census). The commune hosted the [[1992 Winter Olympics|Winter Olympic Games]] in 1992.
