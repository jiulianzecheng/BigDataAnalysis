 '''Dhaulagiri''' is a group of [[mountain]]s in [[Nepal]]. It is {{convert|120|km|mi|-1|abbr=on}} long and lies between [[Gandaki River|Kaligandaki]] river and [[Bheri river|Bheri]] river. '''Dhaulagiri I''' which is the [[List of highest mountains|seventh highest mountain]] in the world whose height is {{convert|8167|m|ft}} lies in this group. 
 
 The name Dhaulagiri comes from [[sanskrit]] in which dhawala means white, beautiful and giri means mountain.
