 '''Polysporangiophytes''', also called '''polysporangiates''' or formally '''Polysporangiophyta''', are a type of [[plant]]. The [[sporophyte]] of these plants  have branching stems (axes).These stems have [[sporangium|sporangia]]. The name means ''many sporangia plant''. The [[clade]] includes all land plants ([[embryophyte]]s) except for the [[bryophyte]]s (liverworts, mosses and hornworts).
