 '''Gunzenhausen''' ({{IPA-de|ɡʊnt͡sn̩ˈhaʊ̯zn̩|-|De-Gunzenhausen.ogg}}; {{lang-bar|Gunzenhausn|link=no}}) is a town in [[Weißenburg-Gunzenhausen]] in [[Bavaria]], Germany. It is on the river [[Altmühl]], {{convert|19|km}} northwest of [[Weißenburg in Bayern]], and {{convert|45|km}} southwest of [[Nuremberg]]. Gunzenhausen is a nationally recognized recreation area. It is noted as being at one end of part of The [[Limes Germanicus]], a Roman border wall, and a [[World Heritage Site|UNESCO World Heritage Site]]. < ref > {{Cite web|url=http://www.frankentourismus.com/regions/franconian-lake-region/|title=Franconian Lake Region|website=Franken Tourismus|access-date=23 March 2018}} < /ref >
