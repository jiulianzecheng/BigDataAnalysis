 '''Tegna''' was a [[municipality]] of the [[district]] [[Locarno (district)|Locarno]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Ticino]] in [[Switzerland]]. On 14 April 2013, the former municipalities of [[Cavigliano]], Tegna and [[Verscio]] merged into the new municipality of [[Terre di Pedemonte]]. < ref name=Fusion > [http://www.bfs.admin.ch/bfs/portal/de/index/infothek/nomenklaturen/blank/blank/gem_liste/03.html Amtliches Gemeindeverzeichnis der Schweiz] published by the Swiss Federal Statistical Office {{in lang|de}} accessed 18 April 2013 < /ref >
