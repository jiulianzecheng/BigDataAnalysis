 '''''Hadrosaurus''''' was a [[genus]] of hadrosaurid [[dinosaur]] from the [[Upper Cretaceous]] period. Is the only [[species]] in its [[genus]] and is the official state dinosaur of [[New Jersey]]. The name is dubious because no [[skull]] has been found to distinguish it from near relatives.{{biology-stub}} 
 
 The Hadrosaurus pictured here was a land dwelling animal, who's body was washed out to sea after its death, where it fossilized on the seafloor.
