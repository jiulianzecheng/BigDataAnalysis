'''Rentaghost''' is a [[British]] children's comedy television show by the [[BBC]] between 6 January 1976 and the series until 6 November 1984. The shows plot a number ghost in the Rentaghost film series, it's cast, [[Anthony Jackson]], [[Michael Derbyshire]], [[Michael Staniforth]], [[Edward Brayshaw]], [[Ann Emery]], [[Molly Weir]], [[Sue Nicholls]] and Lynda La Plante.
