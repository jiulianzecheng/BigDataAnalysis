 '''Langenthal''' is a [[town]] in the [[administrative district]] of [[Oberaargau (administrative district)|Oberaargau]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Bern (canton)|Bern]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 On 1 January 2010, the former municipality of [[Untersteckholz]] merged into the municipality of Langenthal. 
 
 Langenthal is an educational, cultural, and economic center for the region of [[Oberaargau]].
