 '''Cadorago''' is a ''[[comune]]'' (municipality) in the [[Province of Como]] in the [[Italy|Italian]] region of [[Lombardy]]. It is about {{convert|30|km|mi}} northwest of [[Milan]] and about {{convert|12|km|mi|0}} southwest of [[Como]]. As of 30 September 2017, 7,953 people lived there.
