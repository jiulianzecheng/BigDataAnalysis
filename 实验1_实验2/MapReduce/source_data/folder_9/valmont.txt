 '''''Valmont''''' is a 1989 [[drama movie]] starring [[Annette Bening]]. It is based on the [[France|French]] book ''{{lang|fr|Les Liaisons Dangereuses}}''. This movie received an [[Academy Award]] nomination for the Best Costume Design.
