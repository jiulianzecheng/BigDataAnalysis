 '''''Bugsy''''' is a 1991 [[United States|American]] crime [[drama movie]]. [[Warren Beatty]] plays the title character. < ref name=MaslinNYT > {{cite web |url=https://www.nytimes.com/movie/review?res=9D0CE4D8163FF930A25751C1A967958260 |title=Bugsy (1991) |author=Janet Maslin |date=13 December 1991 |publisher=The New York Times |access-date=3 January 2016}} < /ref >  [[Annette Bening]] plays Virginia Hill. < ref name=MaslinNYT/ >  [[Harvey Keitel]] plays [[Mickey Cohen]]. < ref name=MaslinNYT/ >  The movie is about [[Bugsy Siegel]], a [[gangster]] who works for the [[New York City]] mob, goes to [[California]] and then falls in love with Virginia Hill. < ref name=MaslinNYT/ > 
 
 ''Bugsy'' received very positive reviews from the critics. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://www.rottentomatoes.com/m/bugsy/ |title=Bugsy (1991) |publisher=Flixster, Inc |access-date=3 January 2016}} < /ref >  It won [[Academy Award]]s for [[Academy Award for Best Costume Design|Best Costume Design]] and [[Academy Award for Best Production Design|Best Art Direction-Set Decoration]]. It was nominated for other [[Academy Award]]s, including [[Academy Award for Best Picture|Best Picture]], the [[Academy Award for Best Director|Best Director]] and the [[Academy Award for Best Cinematography|Best Cinematography]]. 
 
 The movie was released in December of 1991.
