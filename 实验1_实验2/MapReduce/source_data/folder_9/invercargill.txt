 '''Invercargill''' ({{lang-mi|'''Waihōpai'''}} < span style= " margin-left:2.5px; " > ) < /span > < ref > {{Cite web|title=List of Place Names – Māori Language Resources – Te Taura Whiri i te Reo Māori – Māori Language Commission|url=http://www.tetaurawhiri.govt.nz/english/resources_e/list_placenames.shtml|url-status=dead|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150609033527/http://www.tetaurawhiri.govt.nz/english/resources_e/list_placenames.shtml|archive-date=2015-06-09|access-date=2017-11-29}} < /ref >  is the southernmost and westernmost city in New Zealand, and one of the southernmost cities in the world. It is the commercial centre of the [[Southland, New Zealand|Southland]] region. It lies in the Southland Plains on the [[Oreti River|Oreti or New River]] some 18 & nbsp;km north of [[Bluff, New Zealand|Bluff]], which is the southernmost town in the [[South Island]]. It sits amid rich farmland that is bordered by large areas of conservation land and marine reserves, including [[Fiordland National Park]] covering the south-west corner of the South Island and the [[Catlins]] coastal region.
