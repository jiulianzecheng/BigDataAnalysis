 '''Lammtarra''' (February 2, 1992  & ndash; July 6, 2014) was an unbeaten [[Thoroughbred]] [[Horse racing|racehorse]]. He won three [[Group One]] races in 1995. He was voted the [[Cartier Three-year-old European Champion Colt]]. He won the [[Epsom Derby]] in record time, the [[King George VI and Queen Elizabeth Stakes]] and the [[Prix de l'Arc de Triomphe]]. The only other horse to win these races was [[Mill Reef]]. 
 
 Lammtarra was bred in [[Kentucky]], [[United States]]. He was trained in both the U.S. and the [[United Kingdom]].  
 
 Lammtarra was first retired to [[Japan]]'s Arrow Stud. He was later moved to [[England]]'s Dalham Hall Stud in [[Newmarket, Suffolk|Newmarket]], [[Suffolk]].
