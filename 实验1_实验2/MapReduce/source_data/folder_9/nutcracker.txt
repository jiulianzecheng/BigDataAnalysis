 A '''nutcracker''' is a [[Mechanical energy|mechanical]] device for cracking open the shells of [[Nut (fruit)|nuts]]. Nutcrackers are usually made of a [[lever]] which squeezes the shell of a nut until it cracks open. The earliest use of the term  " nutcracker "  in English dates to 1481.{{Citation needed|date=October 2019}}{{stub}}
