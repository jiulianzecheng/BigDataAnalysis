 '''Bukidnon''' ({{lang-ceb|Lalawigan sa Bukidnon}}; [[Bukid language|Binukid]] and [[Higaonon language|Higaonon]]: ''Probinsya ta Bukidnon'') is a [[Provinces of the Philippines|province]] of the [[Philippines]]. It is in the [[Northern Mindanao]] [[Regions of the Philippines|region]]. The [[capital city|capital]] of this province is [[Malaybalay]] while [[Valencia, Bukidnon|Valencia]] is the largest city.
