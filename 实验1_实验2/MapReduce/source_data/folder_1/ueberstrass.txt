 '''Ueberstrass''' ({{lang-gsw|Íwerstrooss}}; {{lang-de|Überstrass}}) is a [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is in the [[Haut-Rhin]] [[Departments of France|department]] of east [[France]]. 
 
 == Sights == 
 * The church of Sainte-Thérèse-de-l'enfant-Jésus 
 * The chapel of Our Lady of Grunenwald 
 * The chapel of Saint-Jean, dating from the fifteenth century
