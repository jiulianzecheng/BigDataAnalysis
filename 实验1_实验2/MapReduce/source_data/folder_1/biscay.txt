 '''Biscay''' ({{IPAc-en|ˈ|b|ɪ|s|k|eɪ|,_|ˈ|b|ɪ|s|k|i}}; < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.collinsdictionary.com/dictionary/english/biscay|title=Biscay|work=[[Collins English Dictionary]]|publisher=[[HarperCollins]]|access-date=18 May 2019}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite Merriam-Webster|Vizcaya|accessdate=18 May 2019}} < /ref >  {{lang-eu|Bizkaia}} {{IPA-eu|bis̻kai.a|}}; {{lang-es|Vizcaya}} {{IPA-es|biθˈkaʝa|}}) is a [[Provinces of Spain|province of Spain]], lying on the south shore of the [[Bay of Biscay|bay with the same name]]. The name also refers to a historical territory of the [[Basque Country (autonomous community)|Basque Country]]. Its capital city is [[Bilbao]].
