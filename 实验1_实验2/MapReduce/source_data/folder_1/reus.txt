 '''Reus''' is a [[city]] in [[Catalonia]], [[Spain]]. It is the capital of [[Baix Camp]], in the province of [[Province of Tarragona|Tarragona]]. The area has always been an important producer of [[wine]]s and [[Alcoholic drink|spirit]]s. It was famous at one time for its [[brandy]]. < ref name=RTG > {{cite web |url=http://www.reus-tourist-guide.com/ |title=Reus Tourist Information Guide |author= |date= |website= |publisher=reus-tourist-guide.com |accessdate=9 June 2015}} < /ref >  Today Reus is a popular center for rock-climbing. < ref name=RTG/ >  As of 2014, 104,962 [[people]] live in the city. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://www.idescat.cat/emex/?id=431233 & lang=en |title=Reus (431233) |author= |date= |website= |publisher=Statistical Institute of Catalonia  |accessdate=9 June 2015}} < /ref >  The famous architect, [[Antoni Gaudí]], was born in Reus. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://www.spainisculture.com/en/propuestas_culturales/reus_ciudad_de_gaudi.html |title=Reus: the cradle of the Master, Gaudí |author= |date= |website= |publisher=Ministry of Education, Culture and Sport |accessdate=9 June 2015 |archive-date=6 July 2022 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20220706070033/http://www.spainisculture.com/en/propuestas_culturales/reus_ciudad_de_gaudi.html |url-status=dead }} < /ref >  The [[poet]] and [[playwright]] [[Joaquín Bartrina]] (1850–1880) was born in Reus. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://www.biografiasyvidas.com/biografia/b/bartrina.htm |title=Joaquín María Bartrina |author= |date= |website= |publisher=Biografías y Vidas |accessdate=9 June 2015}} < /ref >
