 '''Hanko''' ({{lang-sv|Hangö}}) is a city in [[Uusimaa]], [[Finland]]. As of January 2014, about 9,100 people lived there. More people come to live in Hanko during the summer. Hanko is the most southern municipality in Finland. The municipilaty is surrounded by [[sea]] on three sides. Many of the people in Hanko speak Swedish instead of Finnish. Nearby municipalities include [[Kemiönsaari]] and [[Kemiönsaari]].
