 The '''Wəlastəkwewiyik''' or '''Maliseet or Malecite''' were a Native American group of people of the [[Indigenous peoples of the Northeastern Woodlands|Northeastern Woodlands]]. They spoke [[Algonquian languages|Algonquian]] and were a [[First Nations|First Nation]]. They were part of the [[Wabanaki Confederacy]]. The region includes [[Quebec]], [[New Brunswick]] and parts of [[Maine]]. '''Houlton Band of Maliseet Indians of Maine''' is a federally recognized Maliseet tribe in the United States. It is considered one the first foreign allies of the United States.
