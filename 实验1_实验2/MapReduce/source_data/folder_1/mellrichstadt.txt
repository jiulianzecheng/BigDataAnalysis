 '''Mellrichstadt''' is a town in [[Rhön-Grabfeld]], in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. It is 17 & nbsp;km southwest of [[Meiningen]], and 13 & nbsp;km northeast of [[Bad Neustadt]]. It has the following villages: Bahra, Eußenhausen, Frickenhausen, Mühlfeld, Sondheim im Grabfeld and Roßrieth.
