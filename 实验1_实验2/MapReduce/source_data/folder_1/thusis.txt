 '''Thusis''' ({{lang-it|Tosana}}, {{lang-rm|Tusàn}}) is a [[municipality]] of the [[district]] of [[Hinterrhein (district)|Hinterrhein]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Graubünden]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 On 1 January 2018, the former municipality of [[Mutten, Switzerland|Mutten]] merged into the municipality of Thusis.
