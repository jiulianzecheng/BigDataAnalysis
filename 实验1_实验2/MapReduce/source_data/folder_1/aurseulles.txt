 '''Aurseulles''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is in the [[Regions of France|region]] of [[Normandy]] in the [[Calvados]] [[Departments of France|department]]. This is in the northwest of [[France]]. The municipality was created on 1 January 2017 and consists of the former communes of [[Anctoville]] (the seat), [[Longraye]], [[Saint-Germain-d'Ectot]] and [[Torteval-Quesnay]]. < ref > [https://www.legifrance.gouv.fr/eli/arrete/2016/9/8/INTB1629483A/jo/texte Arrêté préfectoral] 8 September 2016 {{in lang|fr}} < /ref >
