 '''Baku''' ({{IPAc-en|b|ə|ˈ|k|uː|,_|b|ɑː|ˈ|k|uː|,_|ˈ|b|ɑː|k|uː|,_|ˈ|b|æ|k|uː}}; {{lang-az|Bakı}}, from the medieval {{lang-fa|بادکوبه}} ''Bâd-kube'', meaning  " wind-pounded city " , a [[compound word]] of ''bād'',  " [[wind]] " , and ''kube'', which is [[:wikt:root|rooted]] in the [[verb]] کوبیدن ''kubidan'',  " [[:wikt:pound|to pound]] " , thus meaning a place where the wind would be strong and pounding, < ref name= " Web.archive.org " > {{cite web|url=http://iranchamber.com/podium/culture/020920_politicizing_linguistics.php |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20071013212555/http://iranchamber.com/podium/culture/020920_politicizing_linguistics.php |archive-date=13 October 2007 |title=Culture  &  Religion on Podium: Politicizing Linguistics |access-date=25 July 2009 |url-status=dead |df=dmy}} < /ref >  and ancient city names  ''Baraca''/{{lang-fa|باراکا}}) is the [[capital (city)|capital]]) is the [[capital (city)|capital]] and the largest city of [[Azerbaijan]], as well as the largest city on the [[Caspian Sea]] and of the [[Caucasus]] region. It is on the southern shore of the [[Absheron Peninsula]]. Baku is located {{convert|28|m|ft}} below [[sea level]], which makes it the lowest lying national capital in the world and also the largest city in the world located below sea level. Modern Baku has three parts: the Old Town (İçəri Şəhər), the boomtown and the Soviet-built town. About 3 million people live in the [[metropolitan area]].  There are large numbers of [[refugee]]s and internally displaced persons. 
 
 In Baku area there is a lot of [[oil]]. There are a lot of state and private [[University|universities]]. 
 
 Baku is hosted the [[Eurovision Song Contest 2012|57th Eurovision Song Contest]] in May 2012.  They also bid to host the [[2020 Summer Olympics]]. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.olympic.org/media?articleid=138217 |title=Media |publisher=Olympic.org |access-date=2011-09-15}} < /ref >   The city's three main football clubs are [[Neftchi Baku PFC|Neftchi Baku]], [[Baku FC|FC Baku]] and [[Inter Baku]]. Baku is one of world's leading chess centres.
