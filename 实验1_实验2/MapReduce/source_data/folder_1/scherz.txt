 '''Scherz''' was a [[municipality]] of the [[district]] of [[Brugg (district)|Brugg]] in [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Aargau]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 On 1 January 2018, the former municipality of Scherz merged into the municipality of [[Lupfig]].
