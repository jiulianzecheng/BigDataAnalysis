 '''Lugnacco''' is a ''[[frazione]]'' in the [[Metropolitan City of Turin]] in the [[Italy|Italian]] region of [[Piedmont]]. On 1 January 2019, it was an independent comune merged with [[Alice Superiore]] and [[Pecco]] to form the new ''comune'' of [[Val di Chy]].
