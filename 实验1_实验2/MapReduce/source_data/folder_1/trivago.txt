 '''Trivago''' (stylized as '''trivago''') is a Dutch company specializing in [[metasearch engine]] focusing on [[hotel]]s. The company is incorporated in the [[Netherlands]]. < ref > {{Cite web|last=Bungard|first=Matt|date=2020-01-20|title=Trivago misled consumers in favour of advertiser dollars, federal court finds|url=https://www.theage.com.au/business/consumer-affairs/trivago-misled-consumers-in-favour-of-advertiser-dollars-federal-court-finds-20200120-p53t40.html|access-date=2021-09-27|website=The Age|language=en}} < /ref > 
 
 == History == 
 The company was founded in [[Amsterdam]], Netherlands, in January 2005.  The team developed [[Germany|Germany's]] first hotel [[search engine]]. 
 
 Initially, Trivago received €1 million from investors, including the Samwer brothers, Florian Heinemann, and Christian Vollmann. In 2007, Trivago received US$1.14 million in Series B funding from the British company HOWZAT media LLP. In December 2010, Trivago sold a quarter of the company for US$52.86 million to a US investment fund, Insight Venture Partners. 
 
 In 2013 Expedia, an American travel company, acquired a stake in Trivago for $632 million. < ref > {{Cite news|date=2012-12-21|title=Expedia to acquire stake in German hotel site|language=en|work=Reuters|url=https://www.reuters.com/article/us-expedia-trivago-idUSBRE8BK0RY20121221|access-date=2021-09-27}} < /ref > 
 
 In December 2014, Trivago acquired mobile app product and development company Rheinfabrik. 
 
 In 2015, Trivago reported more than US$500 million in revenue. < ref > {{Cite web|last=Net|first=Hospitality|title=trivago Unveils Plans for New Global Campus|url=https://www.hospitalitynet.org/news/4074096.html|access-date=2021-09-27|website=Hospitality Net|language=en-us}} < /ref > 
 
 In March 2016, Trivago announced Cloud-PMS company Base7booking acquiring. 
 
 On December 16, 2016, Trivago became publicly traded on the NASDAQ exchange under the ticker symbol TRVG. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Expedia’s Hotel Search Service Trivago Plans IPO|url=https://fortune.com/2016/11/14/expedia-hotel-trivago-ipo/|access-date=2021-09-27|website=Fortune|language=en}} < /ref > 
 
 In March 2020, after the pandemic COVID-19, sales had fallen by 95% compared to the previous year. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Tourismuskrise: Trivago baut massiv Stellen ab|url=https://www.handelsblatt.com/unternehmen/dienstleister/tourismuskrise-trivago-baut-massiv-stellen-ab/25782686.html|access-date=2021-09-27|website=www.handelsblatt.com|language=de}} < /ref > 
 
 Now Trivago is headquartered in [[Düsseldorf]]. In 2013, Trivago opened offices in [[Leipzig]] and [[Palma de Mallorca]]. There are also offices in [[Amsterdam]] and [[Shanghai]]. 
 
 == Business model == 
 Booking platforms, hoteliers and other providers list rates and advertise on the Trivago site, paying for the clicks received from Trivago users. < ref > {{Cite web|last=Neuhaus|first=Elisabeth|date=2016-01-06|title=Trivago, auf leisen Sohlen|url=https://www.businessinsider.de/gruenderszene/allgemein/trivago-rolf-schromgens-ranking-2015/|access-date=2021-09-27|website=Business Insider|language=de-DE}} < /ref >
