The '''Mazhilis''' ({{lang-kk|Мәжіліс}}, also transliterated as '''Majilis''';  " Assembly "  in [[Kazakh language|Kazakh]]) is the lower house of the [[bicameral]] [[Parliament of Kazakhstan]], known as the ''Parlamenti'', in the [[Government of Kazakhstan]].  
 
 The upper house of Parliament is the [[Senate of Kazakhstan]]. There are 107 seats (98+9). Members of Parliament are elected to five-year terms. < ref name=PARLIMENT > {{Cite web|url=http://www.parlam.kz/en/|title=Официальный сайт Парламента Республики Казахстан|website=www.parlam.kz}} < /ref >
