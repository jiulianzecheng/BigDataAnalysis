 '''Vaddukoddai''' (also spelled ''Vattukkottai'', ''Vatukotai'', or ''Vattukotai''; {{lang-ta|வட்டுக்கோட்டை}}) is a town in northern [[Sri Lanka]]. It is a small but important town located on the [[Jaffna peninsula]]. This part of the country is dominated by [[Tamil people]].
