 Travis are a [[Scotland|Scottish]] [[Indie pop]] [[rock music|rock]] band. They have released many albums including [[The Man Who]], [[The Invisible band]] and  ''[[The Boy with No Name]]. '' 
 
 ''Their latest album was released in September of 2013 and is called [[Where You Stand]]'' 
 
 They are best known for their single  " [[Why Does It Always Rain on Me?]] "  from their 1999 release ''[[The Man Who]]''.
