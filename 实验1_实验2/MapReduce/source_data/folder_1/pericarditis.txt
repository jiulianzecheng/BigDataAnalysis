'''Pericarditis''' (pronunciation: peri·​car·​di·​tis)  is a [[heart]] condition. It is the swelling and irritation of the [[pericardium]]. < ref > Pericarditis - Symptoms and causes. [[Mayo Clinic]]. Retrieved 2021-08-10. < /ref > 
 
 [[File:Constrictive pericarditis.jpg|thumb|Constructive Pericarditis]] 
 
 ==Signs and Symptoms== 
 Pericarditis can be acute pericarditis or chronic pericarditis. A typical symptom of acute pericarditis is a sharp, pricking chest pain taking place rapidly. It is felt in the midst or left side of chest. There may be pain in one or both shoulders. < ref > Symptoms and Diagnosis of Pericarditis " . heart.org. Retrieved 2021-08-10. < /ref >
