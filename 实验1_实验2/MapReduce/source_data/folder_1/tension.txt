 '''Tension''' may mean: 
 * [[Tension (mechanics)]], in physics, a force related to the stretching of an object 
 * [[Electric power transmission|High-tension line]], sometimes used to refer to electrical voltage 
 * [[Stress (biology)]], physiological or mental stress 
 * [[Tension (music)]], the perceived need for relaxation or release created by a listener's expectations 
 * [[Suspense]], in a dramatic work, leaving the audience feeling uncertain and interested about the outcome of certain actions 
 * [[Tenseness]], in phonetics, describes a certain sound quality 
 * [[Tension (band)]], a rock band
