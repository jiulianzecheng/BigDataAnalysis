 '''Avenches''' is a [[Switzerland|Swiss]] [[municipality]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Vaud]], in  [[Broye-Vully (district)|Broye-Vully]]. It was created on 1 September 2006, the village of Donatyre merged into Avenches and on 1 July 2011, the former municipality of [[Oleyres]] merged into Avenches. < ref name=Fusion > [http://www.bfs.admin.ch/bfs/portal/de/index/infothek/nomenklaturen/blank/blank/gem_liste/03.html Amtliches Gemeindeverzeichnis der Schweiz] published by the Swiss Federal Statistical Office {{in lang|de}} accessed 17 February 2011 < /ref >
