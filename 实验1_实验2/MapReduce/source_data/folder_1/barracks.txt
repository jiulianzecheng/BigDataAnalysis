 '''Barracks''' are a group of [[military]] [[building]]s where [[soldier]]s live and [[sleep]]. Barracks can be made stronger with sandbags or with [[barbed wire]] [[fence]]s. Sometimes they are built underground. They have been used for many years by many [[country|countries]] in many [[war]]s.
