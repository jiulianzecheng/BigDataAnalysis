 '''Implementation''' is the [[realization]] (or making real) of an [[application]], or making real: a [[plan]], an idea, [[scientific modelling|model]], [[design]], [[specification]], [[Standardization|standard]], [[algorithm]], or [[policy]]. 
 
 Implementation may be: 
 
 *Implementation ([[computer science]]), making real a [[technical specification]] or algorithm as a [[computer program|program]], [[software component]] (or part of [[software]]), or other [[computer system]] through [[computer programming]] and letting the software be used by [[end user|end users]] (or customers of the software).  
 *Implementation ([[political science]]), carrying out of - or making happen - [[public policy]].
