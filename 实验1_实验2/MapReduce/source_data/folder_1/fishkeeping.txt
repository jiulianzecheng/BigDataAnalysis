 '''Fishkeeping''' is a popular [[hobby]], done by '''aquarists'''. It is concerned with keeping [[fish]] in a home [[aquarium]] or [[garden pond]]. Fish have been raised as food in pools and ponds for thousands of years. 
 
 == Types of fishkeeping == 
 Fishkeeping can be divided into three [[Discipline (study)|disciplines]], depending on the type of water the fish came from. They are [[Fresh water|freshwater]], [[Brackish water|brackish]], and [[Salt water|saltwater]] fishkeeping. 
 
 ===Freshwater fishkeeping=== 
 '''[[Freshwater fishkeeping]]''' is the most popular type of fishkeeping. Small pet stores often sell many types of [[freshwater fish]] such as [[goldfish]], [[Guppy|guppies]], and [[angelfish]]. Many fishkeepers create freshwater [[Aquascape|aquascapes]] where there are lots of [[Aquatic plant|aquatic plants]] as well as fish. 
 
 ===Saltwater fishkeeping=== 
 '''[[Marine aquarium|Marine aquaria]]''' is a type of fishkeeping that is not very popular. This is because [[saltwater fish]] have more specific [[Need|needs]] to keep them alive. Saltwater fish is also expensive. Many types of saltwater fish are kept in saltwater fishkeeping. 
 
 ===Brackish water fishkeeping=== 
 '''[[Brackish-water aquarium|Brackish water aquaria]]''' is a type of fishkeeping. The [[salinity]] in a brackish-water [[aquarium]] must stay between the salinity of freshwater and seawater. Many types of fish prefer brackish water including some types of mollies, many types of [[Goby|gobies]], some types of [[pufferfish]], [[Monodactylidae|monos]], and [[Scatophagidae|scats]].
