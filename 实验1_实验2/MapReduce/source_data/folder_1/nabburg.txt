 '''Nabburg''' is a [[Municipalities of Germany|municipality]] in [[Schwandorf (district)|Schwandorf]], in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. It is on the river [[Naab]], 23 & nbsp;km east of [[Amberg]].
