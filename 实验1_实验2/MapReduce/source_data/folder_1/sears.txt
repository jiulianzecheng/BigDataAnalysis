 '''Sears, Roebuck and Company''', usually called '''Sears''', is a [[department store]] chain with about 13 stores in the [[United States]], 12 in the mainland United States and 1 in [[Puerto Rico]]. < ref name= " retaildive " / >  The first store opened in 1906.{{fact|date=February 2018}} It was the biggest [[retailer]] in the country until [[Walmart]] had more sales in October 1989. < ref > {{Cite news |url=http://articles.sun-sentinel.com/1991-02-15/business/9101080852_1_wal-mart-stores-supercenter-stores-hypermart-usa-units |title=1990 Sales Lift Wal-mart Into Top Spot |date=15 February 1991 |access-date=15 January 2014 |archive-date=4 September 2013 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130904035126/http://articles.sun-sentinel.com/1991-02-15/business/9101080852_1_wal-mart-stores-supercenter-stores-hypermart-usa-units |url-status=dead }} < /ref > 
 
 Sears had to close all 140 of its stores in [[Canada]] in 2017. < ref > {{Cite news|url=https://globalnews.ca/news/3801730/sears-canada-closing/|title=Sears closing after 65 years in Canada; court grants complete liquidation|newspaper=Global News|access-date=2018-02-14|language=en}} < /ref >  Its 75 stores in [[Mexico]] have been operated by Grupo Carso since 1997. < ref > {{Cite news|url=https://www.nytimes.com/1997/04/03/business/sears-to-sell-stake-in-mexican-unit-to-grupo-carso.html|title=Sears to Sell Stake in Mexican Unit to Grupo Carso|last=Reuters|date=1997-04-03|newspaper=The New York Times|access-date=2018-02-14|language=en-US|issn=0362-4331}} < /ref >   
 
 Sears filed for bankruptcy on October 15, 2018. < ref > {{Cite news|url=https://www.nytimes.com/2018/10/14/business/sears-bankruptcy-filing-chapter-11.html|title=Sears, the Original Everything Store, Files for Bankruptcy|last=Corkery|first=Michael|date=2018-10-14|work=The New York Times|access-date=2020-03-09|language=en-US|issn=0362-4331}} < /ref > 
 
 On June 3, 2019, the Sears assets were purchased by [[Transformco|Transform Holdco]]. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://chicago.cbslocal.com/2019/06/03/sears-parent-co-buying-sears-hometown-and-outlet-stores/|accessdate=December 28, 2023|title=Sears Parent Co. Buying Sears Hometown And Outlet Stores|publisher=CBS Chicago}} < /ref >
