 The Sioux (pronounced  " soo " ) are a [[Native American]] [[tribe]].  They live in the [[Midwestern United States|Midwestern U.S]]. They have a long history with the European colonists that eventually became the Americans. Today, the Sioux mainly consist of 2 language-based tribes: The '''Dakota''', and the '''Lakota'''.
