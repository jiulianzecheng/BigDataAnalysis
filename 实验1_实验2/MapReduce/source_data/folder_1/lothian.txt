 '''Lothian''' was a region on the east coast of [[Scotland]] that was used for local government until the new [[Council areas of Scotland|municipalities]] were created. Lothian has been divided into four new council areas: [[Edinburgh]], [[Midlothian]], [[East Lothian]] and [[West Lothian]].
