 '''Runcorn''' is a town in [[Cheshire]], England. It is located within the [[Borough of Halton]]. In 2010 about 61,000 people lived there. It is on the southern bank of the [[River Mersey]]. 
 
 [[Halton Castle]] is the main [[landmark]] of the town. The [[Norton Priory]] is an important historical site in the town.
