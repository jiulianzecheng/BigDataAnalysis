 '''Smithsonite''' is a [[mineral]]. It contains zinc [[carbonate]], ZnCO < sub > 3 < /sub > . It is an [[ore]] of [[zinc]]. It has a Mohs hardness (a hardness scale) of 4.5. It has a [[wikt:specific gravity|specific gravity]] of 4.5.
