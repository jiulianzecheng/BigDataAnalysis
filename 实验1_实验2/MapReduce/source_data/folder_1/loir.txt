 The '''Loir''' is a river in central [[France]]. It is a left [[tributary]] of the [[Sarthe (river)|Sarthe]]. It flows through two [[regions of France]]: [[Centre-Val de Loire]] and [[Pays de la Loire]] 
 
 The [[Eure-et-Loir]] and [[Loir-et-Cher]] [[Departments of France|departments]] are named after this river. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 The Loir is {{Convert|317.4|km|mi|1|abbr=off}} long. Its [[drainage basin]] covers {{Convert|8294|km2|sqmi|0|abbr=off}}. < ref name= " sandre " > {{cite web|url=http://services.sandre.eaufrance.fr/Courdo/Fiche/client/fiche_courdo.php?CdSandre=M1--0160|title=le Loir (M1--0160)|publisher=SANDRE - Portail national d'accès aux référentiels sur l'eau|language=fr|access-date=9 August 2017}} < /ref > 
 
 Its average yearly [[Discharge (hydrology)|discharge]] ([[volume]] of [[water]] which passes through a section of the river per [[Unit of measurement|unit]] of [[time]]) is {{Convert|31.70|m3/s|cuft/s|abbr=on }} at [[Durtal]], [[Maine-et-Loire]] (1961 - 2017). < ref > {{cite web |url=http://www.hydro.eaufrance.fr/stations/M1531610 & procedure=synthese |title=Le Loir à Durtal |publisher=Banque Hydro |language=fr |access-date=9 August 2017 |archive-date=21 July 2021 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210721102121/http://www.hydro.eaufrance.fr/stations/M1531610%26procedure%3Dsynthese |url-status=dead }} < /ref >

 ===Course=== 
 The Loir starts in the former [[province]] of ''Perche'', in the ''commune'' of [[Champrond-en-Gâtine]], [[Eure-et-Loir]] department, [[Centre-Val de Loire]] [[Regions of France|region]]. It starts at an [[altitude]] of about {{Convert|265|m|ft|0|abbr=off}}. < ref > {{cite web |url=https://www.geoportail.gouv.fr/carte?c=1.052222,48.378056 & z=16 & l0=GEOGRAPHICALGRIDSYSTEMS.MAPS.SCAN-EXPRESS.STANDARD::GEOPORTAIL:OGC:WMTS(1) & permalink=yes |title=Source du Loir |publisher=Géoportail |language=fr |access-date=9 August 2017 }} < /ref > 
 
 The Loir flows, in general, to the southwest. It goes through the following regions, departments and ''communes'': < ref name= " sandre "  / > 
 
 * [[Centre-Val de Loire]] region 
 ** [[Eure-et-Loir]]: [[Alluyes]], [[Illiers-Combray]], [[Bonneval, Eure-et-Loir|Bonneval]], [[La Chapelle-du-Noyer]], [[Châteaudun]], [[Cloyes-sur-le-Loir]], [[Donnemain-Saint-Mamès]], [[Douy]], [[Marboué]], [[Moléans]], [[Montboissier]], [[Montigny-le-Gannelon]], [[Romilly-sur-Aigre]], [[Saint-Avit-les-Guespières]], [[Saint-Christophe, Eure-et-Loir|Saint-Christophe]], [[Saint-Denis-les-Ponts]], [[Saint-Éman]] (sources), [[Saint-Hilaire-sur-Yerre]], [[Saint-Maur-sur-le-Loir]], [[Saumeray]], [[Le Thieulin]]. 
 ** [[Loir-et-Cher]]: [[Areines]], [[Artins]], [[Brevainville]], [[Couture-sur-Loir]], [[Fréteval]], [[Lavardin, Loir-et-Cher|Lavardin]], [[Les Roches-l'Évêque]], [[Lignières, Loir-et-Cher|Lignières]], [[Lisle, Loir-et-Cher|Lisle]], [[Mazangé]], [[Meslay, Loir-et-Cher|Meslay]], [[Montoire-sur-le-Loir]], [[Morée, Loir-et-Cher|Morée]], [[Naveil]], [[Pezou]], [[Saint-Firmin-des-Prés]], [[Saint-Hilaire-la-Gravelle]], [[Saint-Jacques-des-Guérets]], [[Saint-Jean-Froidmentel]] ; [[Saint-Martin-des-Bois]], [[Saint-Ouen, Loir-et-Cher|Saint-Ouen]], [[Tréhet]], [[Villiers-sur-Loir]], [[Vendôme]], [[Thoré-la-Rochette]]. 
 * [[Pays de la Loire]] region 
 ** [[Sarthe]]: [[Poncé-sur-le-Loir]], [[Ruillé-sur-Loir]], [[La Chartre-sur-le-Loir]], [[Lhomme]], [[Chahaignes]], [[Marçon]], [[Dissay-sous-Courcillon]], [[Château-du-Loir]], [[Vaas, Sarthe|Vaas]], [[Le Lude]], [[La Flèche]]. 
 ** [[Maine-et-Loire]] : [[Durtal]], [[Seiches-sur-le-Loir]], [[Villevêque]], [[Soucelles]], [[Briollay]]. 
 
 At its end, the Loir flows into the [[Sarthe (river)|Sarthe]] river at the place called ''Bec du Loir'' ( " [[beak]] of the Cher " ) < ref group= " N " > The French word ''bec'' (beak or bill in English) means, in this and similar cases, the [[confluence]] of two rivers. < /ref >  in [[Briollay]], [[Maine-et-Loire]] department, at {{Convert|16|m|ft|0|abbr=off}} of altitude. < ref > {{cite web |url=https://www.geoportail.gouv.fr/carte?c=-0.526389,47.557778 & z=15 & l0=GEOGRAPHICALGRIDSYSTEMS.MAPS.SCAN-EXPRESS.STANDARD::GEOPORTAIL:OGC:WMTS(1) & permalink=yes |title=Confluence du Loir |publisher=Géoportail |language=fr |access-date=9 August 2017 }} < /ref >
