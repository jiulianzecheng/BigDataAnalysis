 '''Bellona''' was an [[Ancient Rome|Ancient Roman]] [[goddess]] of [[war]]. 
 
 All [[Roman Senate]] meetings about foreign war were held in the [[Temple of Bellona (Rome)|Templum Bellonae]]. 
 
 Bellona's festival was celebrated on June 3. She was married to Mars or the Greek god named Ares
