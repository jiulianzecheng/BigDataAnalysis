 '''Kanyakumari''' ({{IPAc-en|US|k|ə|n|ˈ|j|ʌ|k|ʊ|m|ɑːr|iː}}); {{lit}}  " The Virgin Princess "  (also known as '''Cape Comorin''') is a town in [[Kanyakumari District]] in the [[States and union territories of India|state]] of [[Tamil Nadu]], [[India]]. The town is at the southern tip of [[Indian subcontinent]]. The As southernmost town in India, it is sometimes called 'The Land's End'. 
 
 Kanyakumari is a popular [[Tourism|tourist destination]] in India. It is famous for its [[ocean]] [[sunrise]], [[sunset]] and [[moon|moonrise]]. It also has the {{convert|133|ft|m|adj=on}} [[Thiruvalluvar Statue]] and [[Vivekananda Rock Memorial]] off the coast.
