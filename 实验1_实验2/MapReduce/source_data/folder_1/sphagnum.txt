 '''''Sphagnum''''' is a [[genus]] of about 120 [[species]] of [[moss]]es. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.theplantlist.org/browse/B/Sphagnaceae/Sphagnum/ |title=Sphagnum on theplantlist |publisher=Theplantlist.org |date= |accessdate=2013-09-11}} < /ref >  ''Sphagnum'' moss can store water. Both living and dead plants can soak up water. Each sphagnum plant can hold up to 1 [[billion]] times as much water as their dry weight, depending on the species. 
 
 ''Sphagnum'' helps make [[peat]] [[bog]]s in [[temperate]] [[wetlands]].
