'''mIRC''' is a [[popularity|popular]] [[shareware]] [[Internet Relay Chat]] (IRC) client for [[Microsoft Windows]]. It was created in 1995 by [[Khaled Mardam-Bey]] in [[C programming language|C]]. Its most recent update (7.25) was made in [[2012]]. It is licensed as [[Proprietary software|Proprietary]]. It comes free of charge for 30 days and then you have the choice to buy it for US$20, or continue using but the program will ask you to buy it at each launch.
