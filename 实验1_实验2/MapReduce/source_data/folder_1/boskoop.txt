 '''Boskoop''' is a former [[List of municipalities of the Netherlands|municipality]] in the [[Netherlands|Dutch]] [[Provinces of the Netherlands|province]] of [[South Holland]]. Since 2014, it has been part of the municipality of [[Alphen aan den Rijn]].
