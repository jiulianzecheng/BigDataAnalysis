 '''''Amnesiac''''' is an album by the band [[Radiohead]]. It was released in June 2001. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.allmusic.com/album/amnesiac-r534306|title=Amnesiac|access-date=February 15, 2011|publisher=Allmusic}} < /ref >  It went straight to No.1 in the UK Albums Chart. Most critics gave it good reviews. Pitchfork gave it 9/10. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://pitchfork.com/reviews/albums/6659-amnesiac/|title=Radiohead: Amnesiac|website=Pitchfork}} < /ref >
