 '''Cuijk''' is a former municipality in the [[Netherlands|Dutch]] [[Provinces of the Netherlands|province]] of [[North Brabant]]. In 2021, about 25,400 people lived there. 
 
 Cuijk has been a part of [[Land van Cuijk]] since 2022.
