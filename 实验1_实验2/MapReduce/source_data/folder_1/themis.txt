 '''Themis''' is a [[Titan (mythology)|Titan]] in [[Greek mythology]]. She is the titan of law and order. Her parents are [[Gaia (mythology)|Gaia]] and [[Uranos]]. She is also featured in the game Golden Sun 2 as the famous and one of the weakest weapons around the game called the Themis Axe.
