 '''Parma''' is a [[city]] in the [[region]] of [[Emilia-Romagna]]. It is the [[Capital (city)|capital]] of the ''[[Province of Parma]]''. About 190,000 people live in Parma. It has one of the oldest [[university|universities]] of the [[world]], the [[University of Parma]]. 
 
 Parma has a [[humid subtropical climate]] (''Cfa'' in the [[Köppen climate classification]]).
