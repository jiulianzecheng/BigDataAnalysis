 '''Guelma''' ({{lang-ar|ڨالمة}}) is the capital of [[Guelma Province]] and [[Guelma District]]. It is in northeastern [[Algeria]], about {{convert|65|km}} from the [[Mediterranean Sea|Mediterranean]] coast.
