 '''Veroia''' (or Veria) is a [[Greece|Greek]] city which is a capital of [[Imathia]] prefecture. It is in the north of the Greece, in the central [[Macedonia (Greece)|Macedonia]] periphery. In 2001, 42.794 lived there.
