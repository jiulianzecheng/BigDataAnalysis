 The '''Loing''' is a river in central [[France]]. It is a left [[tributary]] of the [[Seine]]. It flows through three [[regions of France]]: [[Bourgogne-Franche-Comté]], [[Centre-Val de Loire]] and [[Île-de-France]] 
 
 ==Geography== 
 The Loing is {{Convert|142.7|km|mi|1|abbr=off}} long. Its [[drainage basin]] covers {{Convert|4182|km2|sqmi|0|abbr=off}}. < ref name= " sandre " > {{cite web|url=http://services.sandre.eaufrance.fr/Courdo/Fiche/client/fiche_courdo.php?CdSandre=F4--0200|title=le Loing (F4--0200)|publisher=SANDRE - Portail national d'accès aux référentiels sur l'eau|language=fr|access-date=14 August 2017}} < /ref > 
 
 Its average yearly [[Discharge (hydrology)|discharge]] ([[volume]] of [[water]] which passes through a section of the river per [[Unit of measurement|unit]] of [[time]]) is {{Convert|18.50|m3/s|cuft/s|abbr=on }} at [[Épisy]], [[Seine-et-Marne]], at about {{Convert|10|km|mi|abbr=on }} from the mouth of the Loing. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://www.hydro.eaufrance.fr/stations/H3621010 & procedure=synthese |title=Le Loing à Épisy |publisher=Banque Hydro |language=fr |access-date=14 August 2017 |archive-date=3 August 2016 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160803161019/http://www.hydro.eaufrance.fr/stations/H3621010%26procedure%3Dsynthese |url-status=dead }} < /ref >

 ===Course=== 
 The Loing starts in ''Puisaye'', a natural region, in the ''commune'' of [[Sainte-Colombe-sur-Loing]], in the south of the [[Yonne]] department, [[Bourgogne-Franche-Comté]] [[Regions of France|region]]. It starts at an [[altitude]] of about {{Convert|320|m|ft|0|abbr=off}}. < ref > {{cite web |url=https://www.geoportail.gouv.fr/carte?c=3.232778,47.553333 & z=16 & l0=GEOGRAPHICALGRIDSYSTEMS.MAPS.SCAN-EXPRESS.STANDARD::GEOPORTAIL:OGC:WMTS(1) & permalink=yes |title=Source du Loing |publisher=Géoportail |language=fr |access-date=14 August 2017 }} < /ref > 
 
 The Loing flows, in general, to the northwest. It goes through the following regions, departments and ''communes'': < ref name= " sandre "  / > 
 
 * [[Bourgogne-Franche-Comté]] region 
 ** '''[[Yonne]]''': [[Sainte-Colombe-sur-Loing]] ~ [[Saint-Sauveur-en-Puisaye]] ~ [[Moutiers-en-Puisaye]] ~ [[Saint-Fargeau]] ~ [[Saint-Martin-des-Champs, Yonne|Saint-Martin-des-Champs]] ~ [[Saint-Privé, Yonne|Saint-Privé]] ~ [[Bléneau]] ~ [[Rogny-les-Sept-Écluses]] 
 * [[Centre-Val de Loire]] region 
 ** '''[[Loiret]]''': [[Dammarie-sur-Loing]] ~ [[Sainte-Geneviève-des-Bois, Loiret|Sainte-Geneviève-des-Bois]] ~ [[Châtillon-Coligny]] ~ [[Montbouy]] ~ [[Montcresson]] ~ [[Conflans-sur-Loing]] ~ [[Amilly, Loiret|Amilly]] ~ [[Montargis]] ~ [[Châlette-sur-Loing]] ~ [[Cepoy]] ~ [[Girolles, Loiret|Girolles]] ~ [[Fontenay-sur-Loing]] ~ [[Nargis]] ~ [[Dordives]] 
 * [[Île-de-France]] region 
 ** '''[[Seine-et-Marne]]''': [[Château-Landon]] ~ [[Souppes-sur-Loing]] ~ [[La Madeleine-sur-Loing]] ~ [[Poligny, Seine-et-Marne|Poligny]] ~ [[Bagneaux-sur-Loing]] ~ [[Saint-Pierre-lès-Nemours]] ~ [[Nemours]]  ~[[Montcourt-Fromonville]] ~ [[Grez-sur-Loing]] ~ [[Bourron-Marlotte]] ~ [[Montigny-sur-Loing]] ~ [[La Genevraye]] ~ [[Épisy]] ~ [[Orvanne (commune)|Orvanne]] ~ [[Veneux-les-Sablons]] ~ [[Saint-Mammès]] 
 
 At its end, the Loing flows into the [[Seine]] river in [[Saint-Mammès]] (bordering [[Veneux-les-Sablons]]), [[Seine-et-Marne]] department, at {{Convert|16|m|ft|0|abbr=off}} of altitude. < ref > {{cite web |url=https://www.geoportail.gouv.fr/carte?c=2.8025,48.386944 & z=15 & l0=GEOGRAPHICALGRIDSYSTEMS.MAPS.SCAN-EXPRESS.STANDARD::GEOPORTAIL:OGC:WMTS(1) & permalink=yes |title=Confluence du Loing |publisher=Géoportail |language=fr |access-date=14 August 2017 }} < /ref > 
 
 The Loing river gives its name to the following ''communes'': [[Sainte-Colombe-sur-Loing]], [[Dammarie-sur-Loing]], [[Conflans-sur-Loing]], [[Châlette-sur-Loing]], [[Fontenay-sur-Loing]], [[Souppes-sur-Loing]], [[La Madeleine-sur-Loing]], [[Bagneaux-sur-Loing]], [[Grez-sur-Loing]], [[Montigny-sur-Loing]] et [[Moret-sur-Loing]]. < ref name= " sandre "  / >
