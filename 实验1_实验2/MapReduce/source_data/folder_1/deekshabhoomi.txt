 '''Deekshabhoomi''' is a historical place in India where about 1,000,000 people were converted from [[Hinduism]] to [[Buddhism]] at a single moment on October 14, 1956. < ref > {{Cite web 
  |url= http://www.ambedkar.org/Babasaheb/lifeofbabasaheb.htm 
  |title=Life of Babasaheb Ambedkar 
  |work=ambedkar.org 
  |access-date=1 October 2010 
 }} < /ref >   These people were people of lower classes who converted under the leadership of Dr. [[Babasaheb Ambedkar]]. Ambedkar felt that their human rights could only be protected as Bhuddists. 
 
 Deekshabhoomi is in the city of [[Nagpur]], [[Maharashtra]] in central India.  It is an important tourist attraction.
