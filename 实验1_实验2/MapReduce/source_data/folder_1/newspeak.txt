'''Newspeak''' is a concept from the book ''[[1984 (book)|1984]]'' by [[George Orwell]]. It is based on [[English language|English]], which is referred to as Oldspeak in the novel. Newspeak is the [[language]] that was created by [[Nineteen Eighty-Four|Oceania]]. It is a language whose [[vocabulary]] grows smaller each year. For example, the word for  " bad "  becomes  " ungood "  in Newspeak. It  becomes a word simply meaning the opposite of  " good " , with the prefix  " un- "  meaning  " not " . In the novel, this language was made by Party members as a means to limit human thought to the absolute lowest.
