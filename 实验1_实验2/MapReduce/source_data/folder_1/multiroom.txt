 '''Multiroom''' is a type of [[business]] found [[South Korea]]. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://travel.cnn.com/seoul/play/evolution-korean-bang-culture-937553|title=Evolution of Korean 'bang' culture|last=S Kwaak|first=Jeyup|date=19 July 2011|website=travel.cnn.com|access-date=2015-10-15}} < /ref >  People rent multirooms as a place were they can do many things. They [[Singing|sing]], watch [[Movie|movies]], play [[Nintendo]] [[Wii]] [[Video game|games]] and similar activities using the [[electronics]] in these rooms. Multirooms usually cost between 10,000-15,000 [[won (Korea)|won]] per hour to rent. Each room has [[food]] and [[drink]]s. The food often include [[pretzel]]s, [[toast]], [[ice cream]] and [[cereal]]. The drinks include [[coffee]], [[soft drink|soda]] and [[tea]]. People can bring their own food. Multirooms are popular with [[teenager]]s in Korea. This is because of the low prices and range of activities. Multirooms are used by teenagers and adults.
