 '''Montabon''' is a former [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is found in the [[Regions of France|region]] [[Pays de la Loire]] in the [[Sarthe]] [[Departments of France|department]] in the west of [[France]]. On 1 October 2016, it was merged into the new commune of [[Montval-sur-Loir]]. < ref > [https://www.legifrance.gouv.fr/eli/arrete/2016/6/28/INTB1619048A/jo/texte Arrêté préfectoral] 28 June 2016 {{in lang|fr}} < /ref >
