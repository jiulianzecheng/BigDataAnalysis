 '''''Fukuiraptor''''' ( " thief of [[Fukui Prefecture|Fukui]] " ) was a medium-sized [[Theropoda|theropod]] dinosaur of the [[Lower Cretaceous]]. The fossil was found in the Kitadani Quarry, [[Fukui Prefecture]], [[Japan]]. < ref name= " :0 " > {{Cite journal|last1=Azuma|first1=Yoichi|last2=Currie|first2=Philip J|date=2000-12-01|title=A new carnosaur (Dinosauria: Theropoda) from the Lower Cretaceous of Japan|url=http://www.nrcresearchpress.com/doi/10.1139/e00-064|journal=Canadian Journal of Earth Sciences|language=en|volume=37|issue=12|pages=1735–1753|doi=10.1139/e00-064|issn=0008-4077}} < /ref >  A reconstructed fossil is exhibited in the [[Fukui Prefectural Dinosaur Museum]], Japan. < ref name= " :1 " > {{Cite web|title=FPDM: Dinosaurs - Fukui Prefectural Dinosaur Museum|url=https://www.dinosaur.pref.fukui.jp/en/dinosaurs.html|access-date=2021-08-06|website=www.dinosaur.pref.fukui.jp}} < /ref > 
 
 == Description == 
 The [[type specimen]] is an immature specimen found in the Kitadani Quarry. The reconstruction of the skeleton indicates that this dinosaur was about 4.2 m long and 175kg in weight. < ref > {{Cite journal|last=Azuma|first=Yoichi|date=2003|title=Early Cretaceous vertebrate remains from Katsuyama City, Fukui Prefecture, Japan|url=https://www.dinosaur.pref.fukui.jp/archive/memoir/memoir002-017.pdf|journal=Memoir of the Fukui Prefectural Dinosaur Museum|volume=2|pages=17–21}} < /ref >  The preserved elements include the skull, vertebral column, front [[Limb|limbs]] (arms) , and hind limbs (legs). < ref name= " :0 "  / >  Additional remains have been found in the site, indicating a breeding or nesting area. < ref > {{Cite web|last=Currie|first=Philip J. ; Azuma, Yoichi|date=2005|title=New specimens, including a growth series, of Fukuiraptor (Dinosauria, Theropoda) from the Lower Cretaceous Kitadani Quarry of Japan|url=https://www.researchgate.net/publication/40664042|access-date=2021-08-06|website=ResearchGate|language=en}} < /ref > 
 
 ''Fukuiraptor'' is small compared to similar dinosaurs and is characterized for having relatively long hands with strongly curved sharp thin claws. < ref name= " :0 "  / > < ref name= " :1 "  / > 
 
 == Classification == 
 Due to its characteristics, it has been indicated that it belongs to Megaraptora, which is a group nested within [[Allosauroidae|Allosauroidea]]. < ref name= " :1 "  / >
