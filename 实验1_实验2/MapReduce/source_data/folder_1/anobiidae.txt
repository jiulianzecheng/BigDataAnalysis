 '''Anobiidae''' is a [[family (biology)|family]] of [[beetle]]s. The [[larva]]e of a number of species bore into wood.  This gave them the name  " [[woodworm]] "  or  " [[wood borer]] " . A few species are pests.  They cause damage to wooden furniture and houses, notably the [[death watch beetle]] and the [[common furniture beetle]].
