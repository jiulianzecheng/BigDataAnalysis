'''Ipanema''' ({{IPA-pt|ipaˈnẽmɐ}}) is a neighbourhood in the [[South Zone (Rio de Janeiro)|South Zone]] of the city of [[Rio de Janeiro]], Brazil, between [[Leblon, Rio de Janeiro|Leblon]] and [[Arpoador]]. The beach at Ipanema became well known as the subject of the [[bossa nova]] [[jazz]] song,  " [[The Girl from Ipanema]] "  ( " ''Garota de Ipanema'' " ). 
 
 The name ''Ipanema'' originally was named after a river in the state of São Paulo.
