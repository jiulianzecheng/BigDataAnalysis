 The '''Samaritans''' are a group of people who live in the [[Levant]]. Samaritans believe in the same one [[God]] of [[Judaism]], but use only the [[Torah]] as text. < ref > {{Cite web |url=http://www.jtsa.edu/prebuilt/exhib/scrips/index.shtml |title=Scripture and Schism Online Exhibit at JTS |access-date=2009-06-10 |archive-date=2008-05-12 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20080512075303/http://www.jtsa.edu/prebuilt/exhib/scrips/index.shtml |url-status=dead }} < /ref > 
 
 There are more than 800 Samaritans. < ref > [http://www.thesamaritanupdate.com/ The Samaritan Update] < /ref >  Most of them live in [[Holon]] and [[Nablus]].
