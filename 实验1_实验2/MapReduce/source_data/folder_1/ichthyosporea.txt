 The '''Ichthyosporea''' (or '''DRIP''' [[clade]], or '''Mesomycetozoea''') is a group of [[Microorganism|microscopic organisms]] within the [[Opisthokont]] group. They are mostly parasites of fish and other animals. 
 
 ==Terminology== 
 The original name, ''ichthyosporea'', described the first discovered species in the group (such as Sphaeroforma arctica). They were all parasites of fish. 
 
 ==Phylogeny== 
 Its main groups are Dermocystida and Ichthyophonida
