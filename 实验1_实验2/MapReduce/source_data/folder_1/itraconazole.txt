'''Itraconazole''' (i tra ko' na zole) is a medication used to treat fungal infections of the lungs and other parts of the body.   
 
 == How the medication is given == 
 Itraconazole comes as a pill (capsule) and also as a liquid (solution) and they are taken by mouth. 
 
 == How to get the medication == 
 Itraconazole is prescribed by a [[Medical doctor|doctor]] and sold by a [[pharmacist]].
