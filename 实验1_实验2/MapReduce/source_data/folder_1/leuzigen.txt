 '''Leuzigen''' is a [[municipality]] in the [[administrative district]] of [[Seeland (administrative district)|Seeland]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Bern (canton)|Bern]] in [[Switzerland]]. The village is between the cities [[Biel/Bienne]] and [[Solothurn]] and borders on the river [[Aare]].
