 '''Saosin''' is an American [[Rock music|rock]] band. They started in [[Orange County, California]] in 2003. The band released their first EP, ''[[Translating the Name]]'' in 2003. The group recorded its [[Saosin (album)|self titled debut album]]. It was released on [[Capitol Records]] on September 26, 2006. Their second studio album, ''[[In Search of Solid Ground]]'', was released on September 8, 2009 on [[Virgin Records]].
