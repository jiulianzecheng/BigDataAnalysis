 '''Tintern''' ({{lang-cy|Tyndyrn}}) is a village and [[community (Wales)|community]] on the west bank of the [[River Wye]] in [[Monmouthshire]], Wales. It is close to the border with England and about {{convert|5|mi}} north of [[Chepstow]]. It is home to the ruined [[Tintern Abbey]], which is popular with tourists. The village is designated as a [[Conservation Area (United Kingdom)|Conservation Area]]. < ref > [http://www.monmouthshire.gov.uk/udp/chapter10.html Adopted Unitary Development Plan] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20100712212708/http://www.monmouthshire.gov.uk/udp/chapter10.html |date=2010-07-12}} archive accessed 5 April 2018 < /ref >
