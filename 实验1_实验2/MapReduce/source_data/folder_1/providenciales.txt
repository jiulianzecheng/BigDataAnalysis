'''Providenciales''' is the main tourist island in the [[Turks and Caicos Islands]]. It is also where most of the people in the country live, with a population of about 32,000. < ref > [https://archive.today/20121208151414/http://www.world-gazetteer.com/wg.php?x= & men=gadm & lng=en & des=wg & geo=-204 & srt=npan & col=abcdefghinoq & msz=1500 Provo population in 2012, World gazetteer, download Apr 1, 2012] < /ref >  The island has an area of {{convert|98|km2|mi2|0|abbr=on}}. 
 
 Grace Bay is where most of the resorts are. The first resort was Club Med. It opened in 1984. < ref > [http://www.visittci.com/providenciales/about About Providenciales - Visit Turks and Caicos Islands, download Feb 2, 2012] < /ref >  Blue Hills, Five Cays, and The Bight were the original villages.
