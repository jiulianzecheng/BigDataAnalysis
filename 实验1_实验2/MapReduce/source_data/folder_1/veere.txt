 '''Veere''', [[Zeelandic dialect|Zeelandic]]: ''(Ter) Veere'', is a municipality and village on the (former) island of [[Walcheren]], Province of [[Zeeland]], the [[Netherlands]]. 
 
 About 22,000 people were living in the municipality in 2017. < br/  > 
 About 1,700 people were living in the village in 2010. 
 
 Veere has had [[city rights]] since 1355. 
 
 The town hall is in [[Domburg, Netherlands|Domburg]].
