 '''''Shewanella''''' is the only genus included in the '''Shewanellaceae''' family of marine [[bacteria]]. ''Shewanella'' is a [[Ocean|marine]] bacterium capable of modifying (or converting to an altered state) metals so they are much less toxic and can break up in the environment.
