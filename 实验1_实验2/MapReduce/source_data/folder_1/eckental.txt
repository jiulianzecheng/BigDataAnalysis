 '''Eckental''' is a [[municipalities of Germany|municipality]] in the district of [[Erlangen-Höchstadt]], in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. It is 14 & nbsp;km east of [[Erlangen]], and 16 & nbsp;km northeast of [[Nuremberg]]. It contains 17 districts: Benzendorf, Brand, Brandermühle, Büg, Ebach, Eckenhaid, Eckenmühle, [[Eichenau|Eschenau]], Forth, Frohnhof, Herpersdorf, Illhof, Marquardsburg, Mausgesees, Oberschöllenbach, Oedhof, and Unterschöllenbach.
