 '''Lausanne''' is a [[city]] in [[Switzerland]]. It has a [[population]] of about 140,000 people. The city is the [[capital (city)|capital]] of the [[district]] Lausanne. It is also the capital of the [[cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Vaud]]. Lausanne is the fourth biggest [[city]] of [[Switzerland]]. The syndic of Lausanne (syndic is the French word for mayor in the canton of Vaud) is Grégoire Junod, from the Socialist Party, since 2016.
