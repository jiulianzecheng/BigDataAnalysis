 '''Haan''' is a town of the district of [[Mettmann Rural District|Mettmann]] in the [[States of Germany|state]] of [[North Rhine-Westphalia]] in [[Germany]]. Since 1975, [[Gruiten]] belongs to Haan.

 == Twin towns == 
 Haan is [[town twinning|twinned]] with: 
 * {{flagicon|France}} [[Eu, Seine-Maritime|Eu]], [[France]], since 1967 
 * {{flagicon|United Kingdom}} [[Berwick-upon-Tweed]], [[United Kingdom]], since 1982 
 * {{flagicon|Germany}} [[Bad Lauchstädt]], [[Germany]], since 1990 
 * {{flagicon|Poland}} [[Dobrodzień]], [[Poland]], since 2004 
 
 The local [[Gymnasium (school)|Gymnasium]] is also twinned with Liceo Scientifico of {{flagicon|Italy}} [[Forte dei Marmi]], [[Italy]].
