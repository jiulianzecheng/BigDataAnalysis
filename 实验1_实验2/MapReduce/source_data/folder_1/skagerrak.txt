 '''Skagerrak''' is a [[sea]] in the northern part of [[Europe]] between [[Norway]] and [[Denmark]]. Skagerrak is a part of the [[North Sea]]. To the east is [[Kattegat]]. 
 
 Close to the Norwegian coast is a deep trench (700 m), the rest of the sea is shallow. Oil and gas are produced from [[oil platform|rigs]] in the Skagerrak.
