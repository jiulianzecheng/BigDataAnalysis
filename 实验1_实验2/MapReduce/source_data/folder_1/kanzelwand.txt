 '''Kanzelwand''' is a {{convert|2058|m}} high [[mountain]] on the [[border]] between [[Vorarlberg]], [[Austria]] and [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://www.skiresort.info/ski-resort/fellhornkanzelwand-oberstdorfriezlern/ |title=Ski resort Fellhorn/Kanzelwand - Oberstdorft/Riezlern|website=Skiresort.info |publisher=Skiresort Service International GmbH |access-date=January 10, 2017}} < /ref >  The [[summit]] can be accessed by the Kanzelwand [[Ski lift|lift]], or by [[hiking]]. The [[Fellhorn]]/Kanzelwand ski area is located here with {{convert|24|km}} of [[piste]]s and 13 lifts. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://www.onthesnow.com/allgau/fellhorn-kanzelwand/ski-resort.html |title=Fellhorn - Kanzelwand Skiing|website=On The Snow |publisher=Mountain News Corporation |access-date=January 10, 2017}} < /ref >
