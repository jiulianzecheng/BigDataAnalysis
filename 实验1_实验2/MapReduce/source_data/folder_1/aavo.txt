'''Aavo''' is a name given to boys in [[Estonia|Estonia.]] It's not an uncommon name and as of January 1st, 2021, it was the 220th most popular name given to baby boys in the country. The name is especially popular in [[Põlva County]], where 12.12 out of every 10,000 people in the county have the name Aavo. 
 
 == People == 
 
 * Aavo Mölder (born 1944), politician 
 * Aavo Ots (born 1951), trumpet player, conductor and music teacher 
 * Aavo Pikkuus (born 1954), cyclist
