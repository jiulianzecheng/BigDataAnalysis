 '''''Illmatic''''' is the first [[studio album]] of American rapper [[Nas]]. It was released on April 19, 1994 by [[Columbia Records]]. Nas recorded the album during 1992 and 1993 in [[New York City]]. The producers were Nas, [[Large Professor]], [[Pete Rock]], [[Q-Tip (rapper)|Q-Tip]], [[L.E.S. (producer)|L.E.S.]], and [[DJ Premier]]. 
 
 Nas raps about his life in [[Queensbridge, Queens|Queensbridge, New York]]. Nas's lyricism features multi-syllabic [[internal rhyme]] [[rhyme scheme|patterns]]. 
 
 ''Illmatic'' received very good reviews from most [[music critic]]s  upon its release. It earnt praise for its lyrical content, production, and Nas's lyricism.  ''Illmatic'' did not sell very well at the beginning. On January 17, 1996, the album was certified [[RIAA certification|gold]] by the [[Recording Industry Association of America]]. In 2001, it earned platinum certification after shipments of one million copies in the United States.
