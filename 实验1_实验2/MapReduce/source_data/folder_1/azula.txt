'''Azula''' is a [[fictional character]] in [[Nickelodeon (TV channel)|Nickelodeon's]] [[anime|animated]] [[television programme|television series]] ''[[Avatar: The Last Airbender]]''. The character and series were created by [[Michael Dante DiMartino]] and [[Bryan Konietzko]].  
 
 Azula is [[voice actor|voiced]] by [[Grey DeLisle]] and will be played by Elizabeth Yu in the [[Netflix]] [[Avatar: The Last Airbender (upcoming TV series)|live-action television remake]]. < ref > {{cite web |last1=Del Rosario |first1=Alexandra |title='Avatar: The Last Airbender': Elizabeth Yu, Yvonne Chapman  &  Tamlyn Tomita Among 5 Added To Netflix Adaptation |url=https://deadline.com/2021/12/avatar-the-last-airbender-elizabeth-yu-yvonne-chapman-tamlyn-tomita-netflix-adaptation-1234891734/ |website=[[Deadline Hollywood]] |access-date=16 December 2021}} < /ref >  Her family is her mother Ursa, her father [[Ozai]], her uncle [[Iroh]], and her brother [[Zuko]].
