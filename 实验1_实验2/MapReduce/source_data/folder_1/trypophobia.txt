'''Trypophobia''' is a fear of an irregular pattern of holes or bumps. < ref > {{cite news|title=Phobia about Holes|newspaper=Washington Post|url=https://www.washingtonpost.com/national/health-science/phobia-about-holes-is-not-officially-recognized-but-uk-scientists-look-into-it/2012/10/01/c1797a8c-dff0-11e1-a421-8bf0f0e5aa11_story.html?hpid=z5|access-date=Jan 2, 2016}} < /ref >   
 
 Some people feel the holes are  " disgusting and gross. "  Others feel [[nausea]]ted and [[itch]]y, have [[panic attack]]s, [[sweat]] or feel their heart pound. It may be that some brain responses associate the holes with danger. < ref > {{cite web|title=Improbable Horror of Clusters|publisher=Statesman Journal|url=http://www.statesmanjournal.com/article/20101212/COLUMN0811/12120311/The-improbable-horror-clusters?nclick_check=1|access-date=Jan 2, 2016|archive-date=February 3, 2013|archive-url=https://archive.today/20130203135024/http://www.statesmanjournal.com/article/20101212/COLUMN0811/12120311/The-improbable-horror-clusters?nclick_check=1|url-status=bot: unknown}} < /ref >
