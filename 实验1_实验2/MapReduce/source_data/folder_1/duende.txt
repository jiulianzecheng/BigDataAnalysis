A '''duende''' is a legendary creature from Central and South America.  It is like a sprite or [[pixie]]. Stories about duende say it has a hat. It can be evil, kind, helpful, or play tricks. 
 
 A little known fact about duendes is that many of them are theorized to live in present day Maine.
