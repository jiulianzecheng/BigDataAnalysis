 '''Karlskrona''' is a town in the [[counties of Sweden|county]] of [[Blekinge County|Blekinge]] in [[Sweden]]. It is the seat of [[Karlskrona Municipality]]. The city name means ''Karl's Crown'' in honour of King [[Karl XI of Sweden]]. 
 
 The city was founded in 1680 when the [[Royal Swedish Navy]] was moved from the [[Stockholm]] area to [[Trossö]] island. 
 
 The Naval Port in the city is a [[United Nations Educational, Scientific and Cultural Organization|UNESCO]] [[World Heritage Site]]. < ref > {{cite web | title=Karlskrona | url=http://www.worldheritagesite.org/sites/site.php?id=871 | publisher=www.worldheritagesite.org |accessdate=7 May 2017}} < /ref >
