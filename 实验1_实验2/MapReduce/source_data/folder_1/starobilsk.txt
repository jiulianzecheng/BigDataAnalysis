 '''Starobilsk''' ({{lang-uk|Старобільськ}}; {{lang-ru|Старобельск}}) is a city in [[Luhansk Oblast]], [[Ukraine]]. It is the administrative center of [[Starobilsk Raion]]. In 2022, 15,947 people lived there. 
 
 == History == 
 Starobilsk was founded in 1686 and it was granted city status in 1938. 
 
 On 24 February 2022, Russian forces began an assault on Starobilsk, during the [[2022 Russian invasion of Ukraine]]. 
 
 On 6 March 2022, local people gathered and removed the flag of the so-called [[Luhansk People's Republic]], burning it and singing the [[State Anthem of Ukraine|Ukrainian national anthem]].
