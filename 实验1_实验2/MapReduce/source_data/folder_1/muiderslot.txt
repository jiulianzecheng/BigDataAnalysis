[[File:Muiderslot (noordoostzijde).jpg|thumb|The Muiderslot]] 
 The '''Muiderslot''' is a [[castle]]-[[museum]] in the [[Netherlands]], in [[Muiden]]. It is {{convert|15| km|abbr=off}} southeast of [[Amsterdam]]. It is one of the better known castles in the Netherlands. 
 
 ==History== 
 The castle was built in 1280 by [[Floris V]]. [[Pieter Corneliszoon Hooft]] used to live there.
