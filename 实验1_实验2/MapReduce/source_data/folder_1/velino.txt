 The '''Velino''' is a [[river]] in [[central Italy]] that flows through the [[Lazio]] and [[Umbria]] [[Regions of Italy|regions]] but flows mainly through the [[Lazio]] region. It is the main [[tributary]] of the [[Nera (Italy)|Nera]] river which is a tributary of the [[Tiber River|Tiber]] river. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 The Velino river has a length of {{Convert|90|km|mi|0|abbr=on}}, and a [[drainage basin]] with an area of approximately {{Convert|2238|km2|sqmi|0|abbr=on }}. < ref name= " trecani " > {{cite web |url=http://www.treccani.it/enciclopedia/velino/ |title=Velino |publisher=Treccani.it - Enciclopedie on line |language=it |access-date=10 August 2017 }} < /ref > 
 
 ===Course=== 
 The Velino river starts on the sides of the ''Monte Pozzoni'' at an [[elevation]] of about {{Convert|1667|m|ft|0|abbr=on}}, in the [[Municipality|''comune'']] of [[Cittareale]], [[Province of Rieti]], [[Lazio]]. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://www.provincia.rieti.it/UserFiles/File/II%20Rapporto.pdf |title=Sintesi degli Studi Relativi all'Istituzione del Parco del Vellino |publisher=CRITEVAT |format=PDF |language=it |pages=29-30 |access-date=10 August 2017 |archive-date=26 April 2016 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160426033122/http://www.provincia.rieti.it/UserFiles/File/II%20Rapporto.pdf |url-status=dead }} < /ref > 
 
 The river flows first to the south through the ''comuni'' of Cittareale and [[Posta, Lazio|Posta]] and goes into a narrow v[[alley]] with the ''Monte Terminillo'' to the west and the ''Monte Giano'' to the east; after about {{Convert|15|km|mi|0|abbr=on}}, it leaves the valley and gets in the [[Antrodoco]] ''comune''. The river then turns to the west and flow through the ''comuni'' of [[Borgo Velino]] and [[Castel Sant'Angelo]]. Then the river gets the water of the  " Peschiera [[Spring (water)|Springs]] " . 
 
 After getting into the ''Piana Reatina'' ( " [[Plain]] of Rieti " ), an important valley, the river flows through the city of [[Rieti]]. At the end of the plain, the Velino river joins the Nera river and forms the ''Cascate delle Marmore'' [[waterfall|falls]]. 
 
 ==Main tributaries == 
 Only the [[Tributary|tributaries]] coming from the left of the Velino are of some importance; those coming from the right are small [[stream]]s. 
 
 Left tributaries: 
 * Salto - 55 & nbsp;km 
 * Turano - 70 & nbsp;km
