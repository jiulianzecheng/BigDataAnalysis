It was branded as a healthier alternative to regular soft drinks. < ref > {{Cite web|last=Ludmir|first=Clara|title=3 Lessons From The Co-Founder Of OLIPOP, America’s Fastest-Growing Functional Beverage Brand|url=https://www.forbes.com/sites/claraludmir/2023/08/17/3-lessons-from-the-co-founder-of-olipop-americas-fastest-growing-functional-beverage-brand/|access-date=2023-11-25|website=Forbes|language=en}} < /ref > 
 == History == 
 Olipop was founded in 2018 by Ben Goodwin and David Lester. < ref name= " :0 "  / > < ref name= " :1 " > {{Cite web|date=2023-09-16|title=How Olipop's founders turned a $100,000 investment into a ‘healthier’ soda brand bringing in $20 million a month|url=https://www.cnbc.com/2023/09/16/how-olipops-founders-turned-100k-investment-into-healthier-soda-brand.html|access-date=2023-11-24|website=CNBC|language=en}} < /ref >  The drink was first released in three flavours: ginger lemon, strawberry vanilla and cinnamon cola. < ref name= " :1 "  / >  It sold in 40 grocery stores in its first year, in Northern California, and reached $852,000 in gross revenue. They later became on sale in [[Target Corporation|Target]] and [[Walmart]] stores. < ref name= " :1 "  / >  The company gathered $2.5 million in seed funding in 2019. < ref name= " :1 "  / >
