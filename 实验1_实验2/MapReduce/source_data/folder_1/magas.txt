 '''Magas''' ({{lang-ru|Магас}}) is the [[capital city]] of [[Ingushetia]], [[Russia]]. It was founded in 1995 and replaced [[Nazran]] as the capital of the republic in 2002. Magas is the smallest capital of a [[Federal subjects of Russia|federal subject]] in Russia. In 2010, 2,502 people lived there.
