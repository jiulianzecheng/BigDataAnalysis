 '''Praden''' ({{lang-rm|Prada}}) is a village in the municipality of [[Tschiertschen-Praden]] in the [[district]] [[Plessur (district)|Plessur]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Graubünden]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 The formerly independent municipality merged with [[Tschiertschen]] to form Tschiertschen-Praden on January 1, 2009.
