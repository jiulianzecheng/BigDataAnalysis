 '''Lumijoki''' is a municipality in [[Northern Ostrobothnia]], [[Finland]]. As of 30 September 2023, 2,025 people lived there. Neighbouring municipalities are [[Hailuoto]], [[Liminka]], [[Oulu]] and [[Siikajoki]].
