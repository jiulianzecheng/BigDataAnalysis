 '''The telescopefish''', or '''giganturas''', comprise a genus, '''''Gigantura''''', of small, deep-sea [[Actinopterygii|ray-finned fish]] in the family '''Giganturidae'''. Though rarely captured, they are found in cold, deep tropical to subtropical waters worldwide. 
 
 == Species == 
 The currently recognized species in this genus are: 
 
 * ''[[Gigantura chuni]]''  < small > A. B. Brauer, 1901 < /small >  (giant gigantura) 
 * ''[[Gigantura indica]]''  < small > A. B. Brauer, 1901 < /small >  (telescopefish)
