 '''Westhill''' is a town in [[Aberdeenshire]], in the parish of [[Skene, Scotland|Skene]], directly west of [[Aberdeen]]. It is connected to the city by the [[A944 road]].  It is also near Skene, and has a swimming pool, tennis courts, carnival and theme park.
