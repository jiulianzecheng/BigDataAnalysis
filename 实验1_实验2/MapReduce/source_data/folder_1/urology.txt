'''Urology'''  also known as '''genitourinary surgery''', is the branch of medicine that looks at [[Disease|diseases]] of the [[Urinary system|urinary]] system and the [[reproductive organs]]. This includes the [[Kidney|kidneys]], [[Adrenal gland|adrenal glands]], [[Ureter|ureters]], [[Bladder|urinary bladder,]] [[urethra]], and the male reproductive organs ([[Testicle|testes]], [[epididymis]], [[vas deferens]], [[Seminal vesicle|seminal vesicles]], [[Prostate|prostate,]] and [[penis]]).
