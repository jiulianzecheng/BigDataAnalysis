 '''Vegemite''' is a food commonly made in [[Australia]]. It is made from leftover [[yeast extract]] used for making [[beer]]. Vegemite is usually used as a [[Spread (food)|spread]] for sandwiches, toast, [[crumpet]]s and [[cracker (food)|cracker biscuits]], sometimes with [[butter]].
