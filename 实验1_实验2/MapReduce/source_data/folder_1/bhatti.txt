The '''Bhatti''' ({{Lang-ur|بھٹی|translit=Bhaṭṭī}}) are a Muslim  &  Hindu tribal conferedency in [[India]] , [[Pakistan]]  &  [[Afghanistan]]. According to Chachanamah, they are a part of [[Sindhi people|Lohana]] conferedency. They are subcaste of many tribes such as [[Rajput|Rajputs]], [[Jats]], [[Gurjar|Gujjars]] and [[Sindhi people|Samma-Lohanas]].
