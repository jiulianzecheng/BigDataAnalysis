 '''Leipheim''' is a [[Town#Germany|town]] in [[Günzburg (district)|Günzburg]], in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. It is on the [[Danube]], {{convert|5|km|0|abbr=off}} west of [[Günzburg]], and {{convert|17|km|0|abbr=off}} northeast of [[Ulm]]. The village Riedheim and Weissingen are districts of Leipheim. Since 1993, Leipheim has been twinned with the [[Hungary|Hungarian]] town [[Fonyód]].
