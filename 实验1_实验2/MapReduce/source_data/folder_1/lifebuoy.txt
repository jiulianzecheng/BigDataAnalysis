 A '''lifebuoy''' is a [[buoy|protective device]] used for people who can't [[Swimming|swim]]. It is made from [[torus]], colored in [[Orange (color)|orange]]-[[white]] color. A lifebuoy was originally created by Italian [[Renaissance]] inventor [[Leonardo da Vinci]].
