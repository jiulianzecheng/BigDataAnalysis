 '''Institut supérieur international du parfum, de la cosmétique et de l'aromatique alimentaire (ISIPCA)''' is a renowned graduate cosmetics school in France. It is in a campus of the [[Université de Versailles Saint-Quentin-en-Yvelines]] (Northern France). < ref > {{in lang|fr}}[http://www.jversailles.fr/article/article/isipca-40eme-anniversaire/ ISIPCA: 40ÈME ANNIVERSAIRE] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20161104203843/http://www.jversailles.fr/article/article/isipca-40eme-anniversaire/ |date=2016-11-04 }} < /ref > 
 
 Its different curricula lead to the following French  &  European degrees : 
 * [[Bachelor of Science]] 
 * [[Master of Science]] 
 * ''[[Mastère Spécialisé|Mastères Spécialisés]]'' (MS) < ref > {{in lang|fr}}[http://www.cge.asso.fr/nos-labels/ms/249-securite-et-reglementation-internationale-des-parfums-et-produits-cosmetiques Sécurité et réglementation internationale des parfums et produits cosmétiques (ESCOM)] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20161107063947/http://www.cge.asso.fr/nos-labels/ms/249-securite-et-reglementation-internationale-des-parfums-et-produits-cosmetiques |date=2016-11-07 }} < /ref > 
 * Massive Online Open Course. < ref > {{in lang|fr}}[https://www.fun-mooc.fr/universities/isipca/ Institut Supérieur International du Parfum, de la Cosmétique] < /ref > 
 
 Academic activities and industrial applied research are performed mainly in French and English languages. 
 
 == Research labs == 
 Research activities at ISIPCA relate to the following topics: 
 * [[Perfume]] 
 * [[Cosmetics]] 
 * [[Flavor]]
