 '''Moray''' is one of the 32 [[Council area (Scotland)|council areas]] of [[Scotland]], [[United Kingdom|UK]]. It lies in the northeast of the country and borders the council areas of [[Highland (council area)|the Highlands]] and [[Aberdeenshire]]. It takes its name after the much larger [[Province of Moray]]. Before [[1996]], it was a district of the [[Grampian|Grampian region]].
