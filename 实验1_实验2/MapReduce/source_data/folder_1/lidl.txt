 '''Lidl Stiftung  &  Co. KG''' is a [[Germany|German]] [[food]] retail company. It is an [[international]] enterprise with independent [[national]] companies almost everywhere in [[Europe]]. The company owns more than 10,000 stores. 3,000 of these stores are in Germany alone, making Germany Lidl's biggest national market. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Über Lidl Deutschland|trans-title=About Lidl Germany|url=https://unternehmen.lidl.de/newsroom/boilerplate|access-date=9 January 2020}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite news|date=17 January 2018|title=German discounter Lidl slows U.S. expansion: paper|website=[[Reuters]]|publication-date=17 January 2018|url=https://www.reuters.com/article/us-lidl-usa/german-discounter-lidl-slows-u-s-expansion-paper-idUSKBN1F62GJ|access-date=2021-01-09}} < /ref > 
 
 == History == 
 
 The Lidl  &  Schwarz Grocery Wholesale company was founded in Germany in the 1930s. < ref name= " ConquerUS " > {{cite news|title=Secrets of the German supermarkets conquering America (24 slides)|work=LoveMoney|url=https://www.lovemoney.com/galleries/66867/secrets-of-the-german-supermarkets-conquering-america?page=1|access-date=6 March 2019}} < /ref > 
 
 The first Lidl stores were opened in 1973, < ref > {{cite web|title=Aldis Erzfeind|url=http://www.manager-magazin.de/unternehmen/karriere/a-288662-3.html|access-date=4 March 2017|language=de}} < /ref > < ref > {{cite web|title=Der Geheimnis-Krämer|url=http://www.focus.de/finanzen/news/portraet-der-geheimnis-kraemer_aid_198973.html|access-date=4 March 2017|language=de}} < /ref >  during the 1990s Lidl started to open stores outside Germany and today Lidl stores can be found in nearly every country in Europe. < ref > {{Cite news|date=2018-02-15|title=Lidl opens its 700th store in the UK|language=en-GB|work=BBC News|url=https://www.bbc.com/news/business-43069631|access-date=2021-08-24}} < /ref >  Lidl started its foreign expansion with entering France in 1988. The first store in the United Kingdom was opened in 1994, and now there are more than 380 stores in the UK. 
 
 Lidl supermarkets and [[Kaufland]] hypermarkets are part of the [[Schwarz Group]] which is one of the largest grocery retailers in Europe. It is based in [[Neckarsulm]], [[Germany]].
