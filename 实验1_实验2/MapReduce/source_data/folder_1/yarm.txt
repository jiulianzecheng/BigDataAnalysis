'''Yarm''' is a small [[town]] in North East [[England]], in Stockton-on-Tees, [[North Yorkshire]].  It is next to the [[River Tees]] and the [[River Leven]].  It has a [[railway]] station. From 1974 to 1996 it was part of [[Cleveland, England]].
