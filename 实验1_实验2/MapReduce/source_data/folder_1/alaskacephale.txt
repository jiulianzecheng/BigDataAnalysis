 '''''Alaskacephale''''' is a [[Pachycephalosauridae|pachycephalosaurid]] [[dinosaur]] that lived in the [[Late Cretaceous]] (around 80 to 69  million years ago). 
 
 ''Alaskacephale'' was named by Robert Sullivan in 2006. < ref name= " sullivan2006 " > {{Cite journal|last=Sullivan|first=RM|year=2006|title=A taxonomic review of the Pachycephalosauridae (Dinosauria: Ornithischia)|url=http://www.robertmsullivanphd.com/uploads/130_Sullivan__2006__-Pachycephalosauridae.pdf|journal=New Mexico Museum of Natural History and Science Bulletin|issue=35|pages=347–365|access-date=2018-05-28|archive-date=2007-09-27|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20070927214216/http://www.robertmsullivanphd.com/uploads/130_Sullivan__2006__-Pachycephalosauridae.pdf|url-status=dead}} < /ref >  ''Alaskacephale'' was about half the size of ''[[Pachycephalosaurus|Pachycephalosaurus wyomingensis]]'' and three quarters the size of ''[[Prenocephale]]'', and about the same size as ''[[Sphaerotholus]]'' and ''[[Foraminacephale]]''. < ref name= " Gangloff " > {{cite journal|last=Gangloff|first=R.A.|author2=A.R.
