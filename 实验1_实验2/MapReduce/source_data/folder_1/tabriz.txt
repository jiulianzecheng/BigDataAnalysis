'''Tabriz''' ({{lang-fa|تبریز}})  is the largest city in north-western Iran with a population of 1.6 million. Most of them speak the [[Azerbaijani language]]. Tabriz is north of the volcanic cone of [[Sahand]] and south of the [[Eynali]] mountain. It is the capital of [[East Azarbaijan Province]].
