 '''Oberstaufen''' is a [[Municipalities of Germany|municipality]]  in the [[district]] of [[Oberallgäu]] in [[Schwaben]], [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. It is on the B 308 road from [[Lindau]] to [[Immenstadt]].  
 
 == History == 
 It is first mentioned as ''Stoufun'' in AD 868. < ref > {{cite web|title=Gestern bis heute|url=http://www.oberstaufen.info/index.shtml?geschichte2|url-status=dead|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170516151817/http://www.oberstaufen.info/index.shtml?geschichte2|archive-date=May 16, 2017|access-date=January 21, 2017|publisher=oberstaufen.info}} < /ref >  It was originally part of [[Swabia]]. It joined Bavaria in 1805 with the [[Peace of Pressburg]]. 
 
 Oberstaufen was the first town in Germany to be on [[Google Street View]]. < ref > {{cite web |url=https://www.oberstaufen.de/en/service/special-offers/google-street-view/ |title=Discover Oberstaufen with Google Street View |publisher=Oberstaufen Tourismus |access-date=January 21, 2017 |archive-date=June 3, 2016 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160603172633/https://www.oberstaufen.de/en/service/special-offers/google-street-view/ |url-status=dead }} < /ref >  This was because of a marketing campaign by the Oberstaufen tourist industry.
