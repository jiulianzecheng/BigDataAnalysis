 '''Ummerstadt''' is a [[Germany|German]] [[city]] in the south of [[Thuringia]]. Larger cities nearby are [[Hildburghausen]] and [[Coburg]]. With a [[population]] of 527, it's one of the smallest towns in Germany. The mayor of Ummerstadt is Christine Bardin.
