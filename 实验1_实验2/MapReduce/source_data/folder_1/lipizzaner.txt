 The '''Lipizzan''' or '''Lipizzaner''' < ref name= " pronounce " > Pronounced  " Lip-ah-Zahn "  (from French) or  " Lip-ah-Zah-ner "  (from German). < /ref >  ({{lang-hu|Lipicai}}, {{lang-sl|Lipicanec}}, {{lang-hr|Lipicanac}}, {{lang-cs|Lipicán}}), is a grey [[horse]] breed that is known for its [[Dressage]] acts. < ref name= " Lipizzaner " > {{cite web|title=History of the Lipizzaner|url=http://www.lipizzaner.com/home.asp|publisher=White Stallion Productions|accessdate=2009-09-02}} < /ref > 
 
 == History == 
 The Lipizzaner was born in [[Carthage]] almost 2,000 years ago. The people who lived in Carthage [[mate]]d their horses to the [[Vilano]]. There is also a mix of [[Arabian]] and [[Barbary]] blood. < ref name=Lipizzaner/ >
