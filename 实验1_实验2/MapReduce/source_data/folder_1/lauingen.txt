 '''Lauingen''' ({{Lang-gsw|Lauinga|label=[[Swabian German]]}}) is a [[Town#Germany|town]] in the [[Dillingen (district)|district of Dillingen]] in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://germanculture.com.ua/travel-to-germany/lauingen/ |title=Lauingen on German Culture}} < /ref >  It is on the left bank of the [[Danube]], 5 & nbsp;km west of [[Dillingen, Bavaria|Dillingen]], and 37 & nbsp;km northeast of [[Ulm]].
