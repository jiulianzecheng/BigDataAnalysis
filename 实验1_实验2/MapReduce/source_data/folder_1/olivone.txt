 '''Olivone''' was a [[municipality]] of the district [[Blenio (district)|Blenio]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Ticino]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 In 2005, the people in charge of the cantons said that Olivone would merge with 4 other villages to become a new municipality of [[Blenio]]. < ref name=Fusion > [http://www.bfs.admin.ch/bfs/portal/de/index/infothek/nomenklaturen/blank/blank/gem_liste/03.html Amtliches Gemeindeverzeichnis der Schweiz] published by the Swiss Federal Statistical Office {{in lang|de}} accessed 14 January 2010 < /ref >
