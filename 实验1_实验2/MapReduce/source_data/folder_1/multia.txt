 '''Multia''' is a [[municipality]] in [[Central Finland]]. About 1,800 people lived there in January 2014. Petäjävesi is next to the municipalities of [[Keuruu]], [[Petäjävesi]], [[Saarijärvi]], [[Uurainen ]] and [[Ähtäri]]. Multia is established 1868. Multia is famous for it's [[Ice]] [[Kicksled]] [[World Championship]] held every winter. < ref > Bruce Hoskin, ''The Sled Book: Notes Concerning Winter's Favorite Pastime'' (Seattle: Skipstone, 2007), p. 21 < /ref >
