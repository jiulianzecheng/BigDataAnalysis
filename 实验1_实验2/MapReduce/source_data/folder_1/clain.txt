 The '''Clain''' is a [[river]] in western [[France]] that flows through the [[Nouvelle-Aquitaine]] region. It flows into the [[Vienne (river)|Vienne]] river, as a left [[tributary]]. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 The Clain river has a length of {{Convert|144|km|mi|0|abbr=on}} and a [[drainage basin]] with an area of approximately {{Convert|3217|km2|sqmi|0|abbr=on }}. < ref name= " sandre " > {{cite web |url=http://services.sandre.eaufrance.fr/Courdo/Fiche/client/fiche_courdo.php?CdSandre=L2--0160 |title=Le Clain (L2--0160) |publisher=SANDRE - Portail national d'accès aux référentiels sur l'eau |language=fr |access-date=17 April 2017 }} < /ref > 
 
 Its average yearly [[Discharge (hydrology)|discharge]] ([[volume]] of [[water]] which passes through a section of the river per [[Unit of measurement|unit]] of [[time]]) is {{Convert|21.5|m3/s|cuft/s|abbr=on }} at [[Dissay]], south of [[Châtellerault]] near the confluence with the Vienne river. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://www.hydro.eaufrance.fr/stations/L2501610 & procedure=synthese |title=Le Clain à Dissay |publisher=Banque Hydro |language=fr |access-date=17 April 2017 |archive-date=3 March 2016 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160303183802/http://www.hydro.eaufrance.fr/stations/L2501610%26procedure%3Dsynthese |url-status=dead }} < /ref >

 ===Course=== 
 The Clain river starts in the [[Charente]] department near the border with the [[Vienne]] department, in the place called ''Chapelle Beau Clain'' to the south of the ''[[commune]]'' of [[Hiesse]], at an [[elevation]] of about {{Convert|210|m|ft|0|abbr=on}}. < ref > {{cite web |url=https://www.geoportail.gouv.fr/carte?c=0.591389,46.026389 & z=16 & l0=GEOGRAPHICALGRIDSYSTEMS.MAPS.SCAN-EXPRESS.STANDARD::GEOPORTAIL:OGC:WMTS(1) & permalink=yes |title=Source du Clain |publisher=Géoportail |language=fr |access-date=17 April 2017 }} < /ref >  From here, it flows to the north into the Vienne department. 
 
 Finally, the river flows into the [[Vienne (river)|Vienne]] river, on the left side, in the ''commune'' of [[Cenon-sur-Vienne]] near [[Châtellerault]]. < ref > {{cite web |url=https://www.geoportail.gouv.fr/carte?c=0.540556,46.781389 & z=15 & l0=GEOGRAPHICALGRIDSYSTEMS.MAPS.SCAN-EXPRESS.STANDARD::GEOPORTAIL:OGC:WMTS(1) & permalink=yes |title=Confluence du Clain |publisher=Géoportail |language=fr |access-date=17 April 2017 }} < /ref > 
 
 The Clain flows through 2 departments and 33 ''communes'', in the Nouvelle-Aquitaine region: < ref name= " sandre "  / > 
 * [[Charente]]: [[Hiesse]], [[Alloue]]. 
 * [[Vienne]]: [[Champagne-Saint-Hilaire]], [[Pressac]], [[Epenede]], [[Romagne, Vienne|Romagne]], [[Sommières-du-Clain]], [[Château-Garnier]], [[Joussé]], [[Payroux]], [[Mauprevoir]], [[Saint-Martin-l'Ars]], [[Voulon]], [[Anche]], [[Naintré]], [[Beaumont, Vienne|Beaumont]], [[Vouneuil-sur-Vienne]], [[Saint-Cyr, Vienne|Saint-Cyr]], [[Migne-Auxances]], [[Jaunay-Clan]], [[Chasseneuil-du-Poitou]], [[Saint-Georges-lès-Baillargeaux]], [[Dissay]], [[Liguge]], [[Poitiers]], [[Saint-Benoit, Vienne|Saint-Benoit]], [[Buxerolles, Vienne|Buxerolles]], [[Smarves]], [[Iteuil]], [[Roches-Premarie-Andille]], [[Aslonnes]], [[Vivonne]], [[Cenon-sur-Vienne]] ([[confluence]]).
