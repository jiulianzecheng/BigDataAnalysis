'''Zeeboids''' is an application that allows [[Avatar (computing)|avatars]] to be created. It was developed by Zeebo Interactive Studios in [[Brazil]], and was only released for the [[Zeebo]].  ==Creating Zeeboids== 
 ''Zeeboids'' has 24 options for customizing the look of the characters, including skin, physique, hairstyles, 12 different uniforms, and more. It keeps track of and rewards avatar accomplishments, allows players to upload scores, take part in online rankings and use their avatars on each other's Zeebo systems. < ref name= " Terra Mexico " / > < ref name=Newswire/ >

 ==Online Tournament in Brazil== 
 From June 11, 2010, during the [[2010 FIFA World Cup]], Zeebo Brazil held an online tournament for players with ''Foot Camp'' and ''Zeeboids''. The top scorers in each of the four mini-games, as well as those with the best overall combined scores, earned free Z-Credits. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://blog.zeebo.com.br/?p=387 |title=Zeebo F.C.
