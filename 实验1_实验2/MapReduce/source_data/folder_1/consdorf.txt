 '''Consdorf''' ({{lang-lb|Konsdref}}) is a [[Communes of Luxembourg|commune]] in [[Luxembourg]]. It is part of the [[Echternach (canton)|canton of Echternach]]. 
 
 As of 2019, 2,024 people live here.
