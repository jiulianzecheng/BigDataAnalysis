 '''''Suskityrannus''''' is an early [[Tyrannosaurid]], informally known as ''Zunityrannus'' until 2019. It lived in New Mexico 92 million years ago. The [[type specimen]] was found in the Turonian-age ([[Upper Cretaceous]]) Moreno Hill Formation of the [[Zuni Basin]] in western [[New Mexico]]. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://wgno.com/2019/05/06/fossils-of-3-foot-tall-tyrannosaurus-rex-relatives-are-evolutionary-stepping-stone/|title=Fossils of 3-foot-tall Tyrannosaurus rex relatives are evolutionary stepping stone|date=6 May 2019
