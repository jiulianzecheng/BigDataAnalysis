 '''Tuzi''' ({{lang-sr-Cyrl|Тузи}}, {{lang-sq|Tuz}} or ''Tuzi'') is a town in [[Montenegro]]. It is on the border with [[Albania]]. In 2011, 4,748 people lived there.
