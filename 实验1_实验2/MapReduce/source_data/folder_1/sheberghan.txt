 '''Sheberghān''' or '''Shaburghān''' ({{lang-fa|شبرغان}}), also spelled ''Shebirghan'' and ''Shibarghan'', is the [[Capital (city)|capital city]] of the [[Jowzjan Province]] in northern [[Afghanistan]]. 
 
 Sheberghan is on the [[Safid River]] banks, about {{convert|130|km|mi|abbr=on}} west of [[Mazari Sharif]].
