 '''Mulhouse''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]] in [[Alsace]] in north-eastern [[France]], in the [[Haut-Rhin]] [[Departments of France|department]], of which it is a [[Subprefectures in France|subprefecture]]. It is in the [[Grand Est]] [[Regions of France|region]]. 
 
 The ''commune'' is in the [[History|historical]] and [[Culture|cultural]] region of [[Alsace]], close to the [[Switzerland|Swiss]] and [[Germany|German]] borders. 
 
 == History == 
 Mulhouse dates back to the XIIth century. 
 [[Medieval]] Mulhouse consists of an upper and a lower town. 
 * The upper town developed from the 18th century on.  
 * The lower town used to be the quarter of merchants and craftsmen. It developed around the ''Place de la Réunion'' (which commemorates its reunion with France). Nowadays this area is for pedestrians. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 Mulhouse is in the southern part of the [[plain]] of Alsace in the Haut-Rhin department. Two [[river]]s run through Mulhouse, the Doller and the [[Ill (France)|Ill]], both [[Tributary|tributaries]] of the [[Rhine River|Rhine]].

 ===Climate=== 
 The area around Mulhouse, at an [[altitude]] of about {{Convert|270|m|ft|abbr=on }}, is characterized by [[climate]]s with few extremes of [[temperature]]. The [[Köppen climate classification]] type for the climate at Strasbourg is a  " Marine West Coast Climate "  and of the subtype  " '''Cfb''' " . < ref name= " wbase " > {{cite web|work= |publisher=Weatherbase |title=Mulhouse, France - Köppen Climate Classification |url=http://www.weatherbase.com/weather/weather-summary.php3?s=99270 & cityname=Mulhouse%2C+Alsace%2C+France & units= |language= |accessdate=24 December 2017}} < /ref > 
 
 The [[average]] amount of [[precipitation]] for the year in Mulhouse is {{Convert|817.9|mm|in|1|abbr=on }}. The month with the most precipitation on average is June with {{Convert|99.1|mm|in|1|abbr=on }} of precipitation. The month with the least precipitation on average is January with an average of {{Convert|40.6|mm|in|1|abbr=on }}. 
 
 The average temperature for the year in Mulhouse is {{Convert|10|°C|°F }}. The warmest month, on average, is July with an average temperature of {{Convert|19.4|°C|°F }}. The coolest month on average is January, with an average temperature of {{Convert|0.6|°C|°F }}. < ref name= " wbase "  / >

 ==Administration== 
 Mulhouse is a [[Subprefectures in France|subprefecture]] of the [[Haut-Rhin]] department since 1857. It is the capital of the [[Arrondissement of Mulhouse|Mulhouse]] ''arrondissement'' and is also the [[administrative centre]] ({{lang-fr|chef-lieu}}) of 3 [[Cantons of France|cantons]]: < ref name=insee1 > {{cite web|url=https://www.insee.fr/fr/metadonnees/cog/commune/COM68224-mulhouse |title=Commune de Mulhouse (68224) |work= Géographie administrative et d’étude |publisher=Institut national de la statistique et des études économiques - INSEE |language=French |accessdate=23 December 2017 |date= }} < /ref > 
 # [[Canton of Mulhouse-1|Mulhouse-1]], formed with a part of Mulhouse, with 40,752 inhabitants  < small > (2014) < /small > . 
 # [[Canton of Mulhouse-2|Mulhouse-2]], formed with a part of Mulhouse, with 42,558 inhabitants  < small > (2014) < /small > . 
 # [[Canton of Mulhouse-3|Mulhouse-3]], formed with a part of Mulhouse and the ''commune'' [[Illzach]], with 42,305 inhabitants  < small > (2014) < /small > . 
 
 It is part of the [[Communes of France|intercommunality]] ''Mulhouse Alsace Agglomération''.
