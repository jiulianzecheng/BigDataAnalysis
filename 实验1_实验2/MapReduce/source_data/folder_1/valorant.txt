 '''''Valorant''''' (stylized as '''VALORANT''') is a [[free-to-play]] [[multiplayer]] [[tactical shooter|tactical]] [[first-person shooter]] developed and published by [[Riot Games]], for [[Microsoft Windows]]. First talked about under the codename ''Project A'' in October 2019, the game began a closed beta period with limited access on April 7, 2020, followed by official release on June 2, 2020. < ref > [https://mmoguider.ru/news/9013-v-valorant-poyavilsya-russkij-chat/ Russian chat appeared in Valorant] < /ref >
