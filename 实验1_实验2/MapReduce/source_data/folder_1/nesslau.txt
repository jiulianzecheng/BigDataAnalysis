 '''Nesslau''' is a [[municipality]] in [[Toggenburg (Wahlkreis)|Toggenburg]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[St. Gallen (canton)|St. Gallen]] in [[Switzerland]]. It was created on 1 January 2013, when the municipalities [[Nesslau-Krummenau]] and [[Stein, St. Gallen|Stein]] joined together to become the new municipality. < ref name=Fusion > [http://www.bfs.admin.ch/bfs/portal/de/index/infothek/nomenklaturen/blank/blank/gem_liste/03.html Amtliches Gemeindeverzeichnis der Schweiz] published by the Swiss Federal Statistical Office {{in lang|de}} accessed 2 January 2013 < /ref >
