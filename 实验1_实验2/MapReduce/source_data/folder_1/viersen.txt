 '''Viersen''' is the capital of [[Viersen (district)|Viersen]] in [[North Rhine-Westphalia]], [[Germany]]. It is about 8 & nbsp;km north-west of [[Mönchengladbach]], 15 & nbsp;km south-west of [[Krefeld]] and 20 & nbsp;km east of [[Venlo]] ([[Netherlands]]).
