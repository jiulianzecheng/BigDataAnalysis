The name '''Anita''' is a very common [[female]] [[given name]]. The pronunciation of the name is [ ''uh''-'''nee'''-t''uh'' ]. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Definition of Anita {{!}} Dictionary.com|url=https://www.dictionary.com/browse/anita|access-date=2023-03-27|website=www.dictionary.com|language=en}} < /ref >  In [[Spanish language|Spanish]], the name is a version of '''Ana''', the Spanish version of '''Anne'''. In Sanskrit, ''Anita'' means  " full of grace " . < ref > {{Cite web|title=Anita name Meaning and Origin|url=https://babys.name/detail-Anita-Meaning-Origin-Famous-Similar-Sibling.html|access-date=2023-03-27|website=babys.name}} < /ref >   This is not a common name (not in the top 1000 newborn names in the US in 2021). < ref > {{Cite web|last=OACT|title=Popular Baby Names|url=https://www.ssa.gov/cgi-bin/popularnames.cgi|access-date=2023-03-27|website=www.ssa.gov}} < /ref >  In 1977, a [[Hurricane Anita]] hit northeastern [[Mexico]].
