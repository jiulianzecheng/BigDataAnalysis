 '''Windisch''' is a [[municipality]] of the [[district]] of [[Brugg (district)|Brugg]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] (or state) of [[Aargau]] in the country [[Switzerland]]. In 2014, about 7,143 people lived in Windisch. Home of the Swiss railway archives.
