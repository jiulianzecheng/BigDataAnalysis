 '''Messina''' (in [[sicilian language|sicilian]] '''Miss√¨na''') is a [[city]] in the [[region]] of [[Sicily]]. It is the [[Capital (city)|capital]] of the [[Province of Messina]]. About 250,000 people live in and about 500,000 people live around Messina. This makes it the third-largest city in Sicily. It has a hot-summer [[Mediterranean climate]] (''Csa'' in the [[Koeppen climate classification]]).
