 '''Torekov''' is a [[Urban areas in Sweden|locality]] in [[Båstad Municipality]] in [[Skåne County]] in [[Sweden]]. In 2010, 863 people lived there. < ref name=scb / >  While still a [[fishing village]], it is today also a [[summer resort]]. 
 
 In August 1971 the  " [[Torekov Compromise]] "  was decided there. It stripped the [[Monarchy of Sweden|Swedish monarchs]] of all remainind political power.
