 '''Sommerach''' is a [[Municipalities of Germany|municipality]]  in [[Kitzingen (district)|Kitzingen]] in [[Bavaria]] in [[Germany]]. 
 
 == Geography == 
 Sommerach is on the southern part of the river [[Main (river)|Main]] loop on Wine Island ( " Weininsel " ). It is 3 & nbsp;km (2 & nbsp;mi.) away from the motorway A3 (exit [[Kitzingen]]/[[Schwarzach am Main|Schwarzach]]/[[Volkach]]).
