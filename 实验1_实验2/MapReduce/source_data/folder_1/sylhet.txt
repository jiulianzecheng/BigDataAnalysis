 '''Sylhet''' is a major city in north-eastern [[Bangladesh]]. It is the main city of [[Sylhet Division]] and [[Sylhet District]].  It was given [[metropolitan city]] status in March 2009. < ref name= " Metro " / >  Sylhet is on the banks of the [[Surma River|Surma Valley]].  It is surrounded by the [[Jaintia]], Khasi and [[Tripura]] hills. The city has almost 500,000 people. It is one of the largest cities in Bangladesh. The Sylhet region is well known for its [[tea garden]]s and [[tropical forest]]s. Sylhet is one of the richest cities in Bangladesh. < ref name=startweek > Syed Zain Al-Mahmood (28 November 2008) [http://www.thedailystar.net/magazine/2008/11/04/cover.htm Bondor Bazar to Brick Lane] Star Weekend Magazine - The Daily Star (Volume 7 Issue 47).
