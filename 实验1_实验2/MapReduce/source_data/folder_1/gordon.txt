The name '''Gordon''' [ '''gawr'''-dn ] < ref > {{Cite web|title=Definition of Gordon {{!}} Dictionary.com|url=https://www.dictionary.com/browse/gordon|access-date=2023-03-30|website=www.dictionary.com|language=en}} < /ref >  is a masculine [[given name]] in the [[English language]]. Gordon is a timeless name with a fascinating background. It originates from Scotland and is rooted in the Gaelic language, where  " gor "  translates to  " spacious "  or  " large, "  and  " dun "  means  " fortress "  or  " castle. "  As a result, the name Gordon signifies a  " large fort "  or a  " spacious castle. " < ref > {{Cite web|title=Gordon name Meaning and Origin|url=https://babys.name/detail-Gordon-Meaning-Origin-Famous-Similar-Sibling.html|access-date=2023-03-30|website=babys.name}} < /ref >  People who have the name ''Gordon'' include race-car driver [[Jeff Gordon]], and English actress [[Lucy Gordon]]. The name has been used on several [[tropical cyclone|hurricanes]] over the [[Atlantic Ocean|Atlantic Basin]]. In November 1994, [[Hurricane Gordon (1994)|Hurricane Gordon]] moved through [[Jamaica]], [[Cuba]], [[Hispaniola]] and the southeastern [[United States]].
