 '''Olamide Gbenga Adedeji''' (born 15 March 1989), known professionally as '''Olamide''' (and also Olamide Baddo '''BaddoSneh''' or simply '''Baddo''') is a Nigerian [[Hip hop music|hip hop]] recording artist. < ref name= " Olamide Personal Data " > {{cite web|title=Olamide's Biography|url=http://www.biographyhome.com/2013/01/olamide.html |publisher=Biography Home:Olamide Personal Data|access-date=8 January 2014}} < /ref >  He records in [[Yoruba language|Yoruba]] and English language. In 2011, he released his debut studio album ''Rapsodi'' while signed to Coded Tunes. ''[[YBNL]]'', his follow-up album, was released under his label imprint [[YBNL Nation]]. The album was supported by the singles  " First of All " ,  " Voice of the Street " ,  " Stupid Love " , and  " [[Ilefo Illuminati]] " . On 7 November 2013, he released his third studio album ''[[Baddest Guy Ever Liveth]]''. The album's singles include  " [[Durosoke]] "  and  " [[Yemi My Lover]] " . On 17 July 2013, Olamide became the first Nigerian to sign an endorsement deal with [[Cîroc]]. < ref > {{cite web| url=https://www.naijarose.com/| title=Checkout Top Three Artiste That Run And Rule The Nigerian Music Industries| publisher=Premium Times| date=13 July 2013| access-date=19 April 2018| author=Abimboye, Micheal| archive-date=16 June 2018| archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180616154046/https://naijarose.com/| url-status=dead}} < /ref >
