'''Lippelo''' is a small town in the [[Belgium|Belgian]] province of [[Antwerp]]. It is a part of the municipality of [[Sint-Amands]]. Lippelo had 1173 people as of 01/01/2006. 
 
 == History == 
 Lippelo was an independent municipality until 1976. Then, it became, along with [[Oppuurs]], part of the municipality of Sint-Amands.
