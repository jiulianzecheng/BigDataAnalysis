 The '''Gardon''' or '''Gard''' is a [[river]] in southeastern [[France]], in the [[Lozère]] and [[Gard]] [[Departments of France|departments]] in the [[Occitanie]] [[Regions of France|region]]. It is a right [[tributary]] of the [[Rhône]] river. 
 
 The [[Gard]] [[Departments of France|department]] is named after this river. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 The Gardon river has a length of {{Convert|127.3|km|mi|1|abbr=on}} and a [[drainage basin]] with an area of {{Convert|1999|km2|sqmi|0|abbr=on }}. < ref name= " sandre " > {{cite web |url=http://services.sandre.eaufrance.fr/Courdo/Fiche/client/fiche_courdo.php?CdSandre=V71-0400 |title=Le Gard (V71-0400) |publisher=SANDRE - Portail national d'accès aux référentiels sur l'eau |language=fr |access-date=10 January 2017 }} < /ref > 
 
 Its [[average]] yearly [[Discharge (hydrology)|discharge]] ([[volume]] of [[water]] which passes through a section of the river per [[Unit of measurement|unit]] of [[time]]) is {{Convert|32.7|m3/s|cuft/s|abbr=on }} at [[Sanilhac-Sagriès]] in the [[Gard]] department. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://www.hydro.eaufrance.fr/stations/V7184010 & procedure=synthese |title=Le Gard à Sanilhac-Sagriès [La Baume] |publisher=Banque Hydro |language=fr |access-date=10 January 2017 }}{{Dead link|date=March 2022 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }} < /ref >

 ===Course=== 
 The Gardon (known here as ''Gardon d'Anduze'') starts in the [[Cévennes]], southeastern [[Massif Central]], in the [[Communes in France|''commune'']] of [[Saint-Martin-de-Lansuscle]], [[Lozère]] department near ''Prat Reboubalès'', at an [[altitude]] of about {{Convert|1050|m|ft|0|abbr=on }}. < ref > {{cite web |url=https://www.geoportail.gouv.fr/carte?c=3.730833,44.246111 & z=16 & l0=GEOGRAPHICALGRIDSYSTEMS.MAPS.SCAN-EXPRESS.STANDARD::GEOPORTAIL:OGC:WMTS(1) & permalink=yes |title=Source du Gard ou Gardon |publisher=Géoportail |language=fr |access-date=10 January 2017 }} < /ref > 
 
 The ''Gardon d'Anduze'' joins the [[Gardon d'Alès]] between the ''communes'' [[Cassagnoles, Gard|Cassagnoles]] and [[Vézénobres]], at an altitude of about {{Convert|90|m|ft|0|abbr=on }}; < ref > {{cite web |url=https://www.geoportail.gouv.fr/carte?c=4.1325,44.03278 & z=0.000316906 & l0=GEOGRAPHICALGRIDSYSTEMS.MAPS::GEOPORTAIL:OGC:WMTS(1) & permalink=yes |title=Confluence de Gardon Gardon d'Alès et Gardon d'Anduze |publisher=Géoportail |language=fr |access-date=10 January 2017 }} < /ref >  from here, the river is usually known simply as Gard. 
 
 The Gardon flows to the southeast, in general, and passes through the [[Lozère]] and [[Gard]] departments, in the [[Occitanie]] region, and a total of 37 ''communes'', most of them in the Gard department. < ref name= " sandre "  / > 
 
 Finally, it flows, as a right [[tributary]] into the [[Rhône]] river near [[Comps, Gard|Comps]] at {{Convert|5|m|ft|0|abbr=on }} of altitude. < ref > {{cite web |url=https://www.geoportail.gouv.fr/carte?c=4.621111,43.842222 & z=15 & l0=GEOGRAPHICALGRIDSYSTEMS.MAPS.SCAN-EXPRESS.STANDARD::GEOPORTAIL:OGC:WMTS(1) & permalink=yes |title=Confluence du Gardon |publisher=Géoportail |language=fr |access-date=10 January 2017 }} < /ref > 
 
 ==Main tributaries== 
 The main [[Tributary|tributaries]], with a length greater than 20 & nbsp;km, of the Gardon are: 
 * Le Gardon de Sainte-Croix - 27.9 & nbsp;km 
 * Le Gardon de Saint-Jean - 49.4 & nbsp;km 
 * Le [[Gardon d'Alès]] - 60.6 & nbsp;km 
 * La Droude - 22.6 & nbsp;km 
 * Le Bourdic - 25 & nbsp;km 
 * L'Alzon - 23.7 & nbsp;km
