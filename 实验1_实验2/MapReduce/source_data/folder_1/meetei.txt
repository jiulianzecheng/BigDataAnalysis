'''Meetei''' may refer to: 
 * [[Ancient Meetei language]], an archaic form of the modern Meetei language 
 * [[Meetei language]], the official language and the lingua franca of Manipur 
 * [[Meetei mythology]], the body of myths developed by the Meetei people 
 * [[Meetei people]], the predominant ethnic group of Manipur 
 * [[Meetei religion]] (Sanamahism), the ethnic religion of the Meetei people 
 * [[Meetei script]], the writing system for the Meetei language
