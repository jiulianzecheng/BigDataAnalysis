 '''Supabarn''' is a small, [[supermarket]] chain which started in [[Canberra]] with large shops across the [[Australian Capital Territory]] and [[New South Wales]] in [[Australia]]. It was known as Supabarn Emporium in the early 2000s before becoming Supabarn Supermarkets in 2004. It sells traditional, wholefoods, [[wikt:organic|organic]] and {{broken wikt link|gourmet|gourmet}} grocery and fresh food items.  
 
 == History == 
 It was first opened in the [[Canberra Centre|Kippax]] in the 1985 followed by Fyshwick ( now closed)  and a few more shops have been opened from that time onwards such as Chisholm, Karabar. Today Supabarn is one of Canberra's largest employers because it has 750 people in seven shops, with two in [[Sydney]].
