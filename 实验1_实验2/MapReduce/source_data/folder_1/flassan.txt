 '''Flassan''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]] of 341 people ([[1999]]). It is in the region [[Provence-Alpes-Côte d'Azur]] in the [[Vaucluse]] department in the south of [[France]]. It is at the bottom of [[Mont Ventoux]].
