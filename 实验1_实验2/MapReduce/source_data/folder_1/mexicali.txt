 '''Mexicali''' is a city in [[Mexico]]. It is in the north of the country and across the border from [[California]] in the [[United States]]. It is the capital city of [[Baja California (state)|Baja California]]. Mexicali is the farthest north city of [[Latin America]]. About 903,000 people live there. Many of them are [[Hispanic]].
