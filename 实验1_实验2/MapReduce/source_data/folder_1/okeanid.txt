The '''Okeanids''' or '''Okeanides''' ({{lang-grc|Ὠκεανίδες}}) are  the daughters of the [[Titan (mythology)|Titans]] [[Okeanos]] and [[Tethys (mythology)|Tethys]] in [[Greek mythology]]. They were [[nymphs]] of the ocean, said to be three thousand in number. Many people also know these goddesses as lovers.  
 
 Among them are: 
 * [[Doris]] - Wife of [[Nereus]] and mother of the [[Nereid]]s 
 * [[Klymene]] - Wife of [[Iapetus]] and mother of [[Atlas (mythology)|Atlas]] and [[Prometheus]] 
 * [[Metis]] - Mother of [[Athena]], she was Zeus' first consort 
 * [[Pleione]] - Mother of the [[Pleiades (mythology)|Pleiades]] 
 * [[Styx (mythology)|Styx]] - The goddess of the dreaded river upon which the gods swore. She was the wife of Pallas and the mother of [[Nike (mythology)|Nike]], goddess of victory.
