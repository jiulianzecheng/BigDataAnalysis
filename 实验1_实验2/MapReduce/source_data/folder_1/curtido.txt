 '''Curtido''' is a kind of [[fermented]] [[cabbage]] [[relish]]. It is found in the [[cuisine]]s of [[El Salvador]] and other [[Central America|Central American]] countries. It is made with cabbage, [[onion]]s, [[carrot]]s, and sometimes lime juice or [[vinegar]]. It is similar to [[sauerkraut]] or ''[[kimchi]]''. There is also a variation of ''[[curtido]]'' which is prepared with mayonnaise. Both Curtidos, vinegar and mayonnaise, are highly appreciated by diners as they are indispensable accompaniments of pupusas 
 
 People eat curtido with [[pupusa]]s.
