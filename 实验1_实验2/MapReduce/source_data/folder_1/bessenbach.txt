 '''Bessenbach''' is a [[community]] in the [[Aschaffenburg (district)|Aschaffenburg district]] in the ''[[Regierungsbezirk]]'' of [[Lower Franconia]] (''Unterfranken'') in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 
 ===Location=== 
 [[File:Gemeinde Bessenbach.svg|thumb|Constituent communities]] 
 Bessenbach lies southeast of the town of [[Aschaffenburg]] among the [[Spessart]] range's outlying hills. 
 
 ===Subdivision=== 
 Bessenbach's ''[[Ortsteil]]e'' are Beetacker, Frauengrund, Gemeindezentrum, Keilberg, Klingerhof, Klingermühle, Oberbessenbach, Steiger, Straßbessenbach, Unterbessenbach, Waldmichelbach and Weiler.
