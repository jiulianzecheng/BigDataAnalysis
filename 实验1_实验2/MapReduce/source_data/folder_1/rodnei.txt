 '''Rodnei Francisco de Lima''' (born 11 September 1985 in [[São Paulo]]), is a [[Brazil]]ian [[footballer|football player]]. His player name is '''Rodnei'''. He is playing for SV Anthering in Austria as a [[Defender (football)|defender]]. < ref > http://redbulls.com/soccer/salzburg/de/Rodnei-de-Lima.html < /ref > 
 
 ==Career== 
 Rodnei started his career in Brazil for [[C.A. Juventus]]. He then moved to Lithuania. He played for [[FC Vilnius]]. In 2007 he moved to Pão de Açúcar EC from Rio de Janeiro but was loaned to Jagiellonia Białystok. His next teams were Hertha BSC and 1. FC Kaiserslautern in the [[German Bundesliga]]. In 2012 he went on to FC Red Bull Salzburg. He played 18 matches. In the 2013–14 season he only played 3 matches because he was injured. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.transfermarkt.at/rodnei/profil/spieler/58453|title=Rodnei - Spielerprofil|website=www.transfermarkt.at}} < /ref >
