 '''Theanine''' is a [[chemical]] that you find in [[tea]] and in some [[mushroom]]s. Theanine is an [[amino acid]]. The chemical theanine can change your mood, behavior and the way you see or feel things. It lowers [[Stress (medicine)|stress]] and it is also an [[antioxidant]]. This is a good chemical that fights [[aging]]. Theanine helps the [[immune system]]. This protects your body against diseases.
