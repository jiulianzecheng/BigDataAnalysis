 '''Bjuv''' is an [[urban area]] in the [[counties of Sweden|county]] of [[Scania County|Scania]] in [[Sweden]]. It is the seat of [[Bjuv Municipality]]. In [[November]] 1994, the place became well known for a murder kicking off a debate over [[role-playing game]]s.
