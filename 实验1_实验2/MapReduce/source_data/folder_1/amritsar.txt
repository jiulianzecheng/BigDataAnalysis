 '''Amritsar''' ({{lang-pa|ਅੰਮ੍ਰਿਤਸਰ}}, meaning: ''The Lake of the Holy Nectar'', < ref > {{Cite web |url=http://www.target-tours.com/city-guide/amritsar.html |title=Amritsar City Information Guide of Amritsar India |access-date=2008-08-24 |archive-date=2008-01-21 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20080121195527/http://www.target-tours.com/city-guide/amritsar.html |url-status=dead }} < /ref > ) is the [[Capital (city)|capital]] of the [[Amritsar District]] in the [[States and territories of India|state]] of [[Punjab, India|Punjab]], [[India]]. According to the 2001 Indian census, over 1,500,000 people lived in the city. 
 
 Amritsar is in the state of [[Punjab, India]], 32 & nbsp;km east of [[Lahore]], [[Pakistan]]. The Harimandir Sahib, also known as the [[Golden Temple]], is there. The temple is the centre of the [[Sikh]] [[religion]].  
 
 == History == 
 In the [[Jallianwala Bagh Massacre]] of 1919 under [[British India|British Rule]] a large number of protestors were shot at and killed by British troops.  The city is also famous for [[Operation Bluestar]], a military operation carried out by [[India]] in 1984 that was ordered by the [[Prime Minister of India]] of the time, [[Indira Gandhi]].  Many [[Sikhs]] were angered, as the holy [[Golden Temple]] of the [[Sikhs]] was damaged by the [[Indian Army]].
