 '''Bouclans''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is in [[Bourgogne-Franche-Comté]] in the [[Doubs]] [[Departments of France|department]] in east [[France]]. On 1 January 2018, the former commune of [[Vauchamps, Doubs|Vauchamps]] was merged into Bouclans. < ref > [https://www.legifrance.gouv.fr/eli/arrete/2017/9/21/INTB1726734A/jo/texte Arrêté préfectoral] 21 September 2017 {{in lang|fr}} < /ref >
