 '''Sadness''' is an [[emotion]]. It is the opposite of [[happy|happiness]]. People feel sad when something bad has happened, for example, if their mother or father has died.  
 
 In capital letters, '''SAD''', or '''S.A.D.''' is short for  " [[Seasonal affective disorder]] " . SAD is a sickness that some people have in the winter. They feel [[Major depressive disorder|depressed]] as the nights become longer and there is less daylight. 
 
 When people are very sad, and for a long time, the person could have a serious [[mental illness]] called [[Major depressive disorder|depression]] or [[Bipolar disorder]]. 
 
 == Notes == 
 It is normally thought of as the opposite of [[happiness]], but you can feel both happy and sad at the same time.
