[[File:Semionotus bergeri.jpg|thumb|Fossil of ''[[Semionotus]]'']] 
 '''Semionotiformes''' is an extinct order of [[freshwater fish|freshwater]] and [[Saltwater fish|marine]] fish known from the [[Middle Triassic]] ([[Anisian]]) to the [[Upper Cretaceous|Late Cretaceous]] ([[Maastrichtian]]). Some examples of families are [[Semionotidae]] and [[Callipurbeckiidae.]]
