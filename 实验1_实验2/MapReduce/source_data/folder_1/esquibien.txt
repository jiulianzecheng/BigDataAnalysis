 '''Esquibien''' ({{lang-br|An Eskevien}}) is a former [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is found in the [[Regions of France|region]] [[Brittany]] in the [[Finistère]] [[Departments of France|department]] in the northwest of [[France]]. On 1 January 2016, it became a part of the commune of [[Audierne]]. < ref > [http://www.finistere.gouv.fr/content/download/14222/116773/file/RAA%2027.pdf Arrêté préfectoral] 16 October 2015 {{in lang|fr}} < /ref >
