 '''Essingeleden''' is a [[controlled-access highway|motorway]] in central [[Sweden]]. It runs from [[Solna Municipality|Solna]] to [[Stockholm]]. It crosses the islands of [[Kungsholmen]], [[Lilla Essingen]] and [[Stora Essingen]]. It was opened on [[21 August]] [[1966]].
