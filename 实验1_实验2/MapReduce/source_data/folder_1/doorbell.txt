 A '''doorbell''' is a device which is typically installed to or near a [[door]]. When a visitor presses a [[Push-button|button]], the bell rings inside the [[building]], alerting the presence of the [[visitor]].  
 
 In modern [[technology]], a button is pressed completing a [[Electrical circuit|circuit]] which in response makes a [[sound]].  
 
 ==History== 
 Older doorbells were fully [[Mechanics|mechanical]]. Newer [[electricity|electrical]] doorbells were installed by [[wire]]s and use a [[push-button]] to activate the sound.
