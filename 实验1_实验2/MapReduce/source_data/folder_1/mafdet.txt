'''Mafdet''' was a goddess in early [[Ancient Egyptian religion|Egyptian mythology]]. She protected against snakes and scorpions and was often shown as either some sort of [[Felinae|feline]] or [[mongoose]]. < ref > Wilkinson, Richard H.  < cite > The Complete Gods and Goddesses of Ancient Egypt < /cite > . p. 196. Thames  &  Hudson. 2003. {{ISBN|0-500-05120-8}} < /ref > 
 
 In [[art]], Mafdet was shown as a feline, a woman with a feline head, or a feline with the head of a woman.
