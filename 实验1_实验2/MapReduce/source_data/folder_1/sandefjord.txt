 '''Sandefjord''' is a city and municipality in [[Vestfold og Telemark]] county, [[Norway]]. In 2022, 64,943 people lived there. On 1 January 2017, the former municipalities of [[Andebu]] and [[Stokke]] were merged into Sandefjord.
