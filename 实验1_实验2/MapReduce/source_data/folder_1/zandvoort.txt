 '''Zandvoort''' is a [[List of municipalities of the Netherlands|municipality]] in the [[Netherlands|Dutch]] [[Provinces of the Netherlands|province]] of [[North Holland]]. In 2019, 17,011 people lived there. 
 
 Zandvoort is one of the most popular [[beach]] in the Netherlands.
