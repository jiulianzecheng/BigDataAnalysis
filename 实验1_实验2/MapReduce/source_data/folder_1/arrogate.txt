 '''Arrogate''' (April 11, 2013 â€“ June 2, 2020) was an American [[Thoroughbred]] racehorse. He won the 2016 [[Travers Stakes]] in a track record in his first [[graded stakes race|stakes]] appearance. He then won the [[Breeders' Cup Classic]] and was named the [[American Champion Three-Year-Old Male Horse]] and [[World's Best Racehorse Rankings|World's Best Racehorse]] of 2016.  
 
 He won the 2017 [[Pegasus World Cup]] in a new track record followed by a win in the [[Dubai World Cup]].
