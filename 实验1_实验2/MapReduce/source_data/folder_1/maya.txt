In Hindu culture, the name Maya is derived from the Sanskrit word  " maya, "  which means  " illusion "  or  " magic. "  In Hindu mythology, Maya is also the name of a goddess who is associated with creativity, wisdom, and knowledge. She is often depicted as a beautiful woman with four arms, holding a book, a rosary, a mirror, and a lotus flower.
