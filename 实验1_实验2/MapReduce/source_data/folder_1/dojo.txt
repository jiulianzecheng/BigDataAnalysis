 A '''dojo''' (道場) is a [[Japan]]ese term which literally means  " place of the Way " . Dojo are sometimes added on to [[temple]]s. 
 
 == Uses == 
 The term can refer to a training place for any of the [[Japanese art]]s but it is considered the [[formal]] gathering place for students of a [[martial art]]s style to train, take [[exam]]s and fight.
