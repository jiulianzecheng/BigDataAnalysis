 '''Chambois''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]] in north-western France. < ref > Communes.com, [http://www.communes.com/basse-normandie/orne/chambois_61160/  Ville de Chambois]; retrieved 2012-5-14. < /ref >   It is in the [[Orne]] [[Departments of France|department]]. < ref > Conseil-General.com, [http://www.conseil-general.com/en/town-hall/town-hall-chambois-61160.htm  Town Hall of Chambois] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130623044243/http://conseil-general.com/en/town-hall/town-hall-chambois-61160.htm |date=2013-06-23 }}; retrieved 2012-5-14. < /ref > 
 
 ==Donjon== 
 The Donjon of Chambois is a small [[castle]], or [[keep]] located on the road from Exmes to Falaise. It was built in the second part of 12th century, < ref name= " richesheures " > Richesheures.net, [http://www.richesheures.net/epoque-6-15/chateau/61chambois-historique.htm  ''Donjon de Chambois, XIIe siècle'']; retrieved 2012-5-14. < /ref >  by [[William de Mandeville]], vassal of Henri Plantagenêt. < ref > Gravett, Christopher and Adam Hook. (2004). [https://books.google.com/books?id=BNnz37kxa6AC & dq=William+de+Mandeville+donjon & pg=PA15  ''Norman Stone Castles (2): Europe 950-1204'' p. 15]{{Dead link|date=September 2021 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }}. < /ref >  The height of the rectangular structure is about 26 metres. < ref name= " richesheures " / >  It was framed by an extension in the southeast angle and by rectangular turrets in every corner. The central volume contained three levels of rooms, each of them with windows, a chimney and, in the angled turrets, annexes. These arrangements illustrated the residential function of the Donjon.
