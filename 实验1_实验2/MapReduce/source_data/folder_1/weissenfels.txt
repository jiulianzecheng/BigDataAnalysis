 '''Weißenfels''' was the [[capital city]] of the old [[Weißenfels Rural District]] in [[Saxony-Anhalt]], [[Germany]], until the district changes of 2007. The King of Saxony used to live here. It is about {{convert|30|km|abbr=off}} southwest of [[Leipzig]].
