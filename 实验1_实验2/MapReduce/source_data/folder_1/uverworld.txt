 '''UVERworld''' is a [[Japan]]ese pop-rock music [[band]]. They are a group of five from [[Shiga Prefecture]].They performed live shows mainly in the Kansai area and made their Tokyo debut in late 2004, and broke out with their major debut in July, 2005. The band received {{'}}'''New Artist of the Year'''{{'}} in 2005 by the [[Recording Industry Association of Japan]]. < ref name= " Award " > {{cite web
