'''Mittelschwaben''' is a part of the [[Bavaria]]n [[administrative region]] [[Swabia (administrative region)|Swabia]]. It consists of the districts [[Günzburg (district)|Günzburg]], [[Neu-Ulm (district)|Neu-Ulm]], [[Unterallgäu]] and the [[district-free city|district-free]] town [[Memmingen]]. 
 
 The name ''Mittelschwaben'' was created in the 19th century to tell apart the Bavarian parts of [[Upper Swabia|Oberschwaben]] from the [[Baden-Württemberg]]ian.
