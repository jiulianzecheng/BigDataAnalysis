 '''Borkum''' is an [[island]] and [[municipality]] of the [[state]] of [[Lower Saxony]], [[Germany]]. In 2016, the municipality had about 5,500 people. It covers an area of {{convert|31|km2|abbr=off}}. 
 
 Borkum makes part of the [[East Frisian Islands]] ({{lang-de|Ostfriesische Inseln}}). It is the most western island. 
 
 Borkum is west of the island of [[Juist]] (and the uninhabited islands of [[Brauerplate]], [[Kachelotplate]] and [[Memmert]]). It is east of the small Dutch island of [[Rottumeroog]].
