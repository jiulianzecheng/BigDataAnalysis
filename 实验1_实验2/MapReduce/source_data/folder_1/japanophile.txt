A '''Japanophile''' is someone with '''Japanophilia''', or someone with a strong interest in or love of [[Japan|Japanese]] [[Japanese people|people]], culture, and history. This term even has a Japanese equivalent, ''shinnichi'' (kanji:親日, hiragana: しんにち) which literally translates to  " pro-Japanese " . Aguante Viral.
