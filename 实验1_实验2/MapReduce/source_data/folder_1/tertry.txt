 '''Tertry''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is in [[Hauts-de-France]] in the [[Somme]] [[Departments of France|department]] in north [[France]]. 
 
 == History == 
 Tertry was the site of the [[Battle of Tertry]] in 687, where [[Pepin of Herstal]] defeated [[Berchar]], [[mayor of the palace]] of [[Neustria]].
