'''Moncombu''' ({{Lang-ml|മങ്കൊമ്പ്}}; also spelled Mankombu; see below) is a village in the [[Alappuzha district]] in the state of [[Kerala]], [[India]]. The place is famous for many things including the Moncombu Bhagavathy Temple and the [[Rice Research Centre]]. The ancestors of Dr [[M S Swaminathan]], the father of the green revolution in India, came from this village.
