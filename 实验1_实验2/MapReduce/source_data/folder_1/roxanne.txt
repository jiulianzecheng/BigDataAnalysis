'''Roxanne''' [ rok-'''san''' ] < ref > {{Cite web|title=Definition of roxanne {{!}} Dictionary.com|url=https://www.dictionary.com/browse/roxanne|access-date=2023-04-08|website=www.dictionary.com|language=en}} < /ref >  is a female given name. The name is based on the Greek name ''Roxane''. ''Roxane'' comes from the [[Persian language|Persian]] word ''Roschana'', which means  " dawn of day " . < ref > {{Cite web|title=Roxanne name Meaning and Origin|url=https://babys.name/detail-Roxanne-Meaning-Origin-Famous-Similar-Sibling.html|access-date=2023-04-08|website=babys.name}} < /ref >  The name was ranked 389 in the 1990 [[United States|U.S.]] Census. It fell out of the top 1000 girls names in the 21st century. < ref > {{Cite web|last=OACT|title=Popular Baby Names|url=https://www.ssa.gov/cgi-bin/popularnames.cgi|access-date=2023-04-08|website=www.ssa.gov}} < /ref >  The name has been popular in [[Australia]] and [[Canada]], however. This name was used for a [[Hurricane Roxanne|damaging hurricane]] in 1995. It was also the title of a single from music group The Police in the 1980s.
