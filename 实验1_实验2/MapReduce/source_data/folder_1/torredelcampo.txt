 '''Torredelcampo''' is a city in the [[Jaén (Spanish province)|province of Jaén]], Spain.  The village is located 11 & nbsp;km northwest of [[Jaén, Spain|Jaén]]. According to the 2006 [[census]] ([[Instituto Nacional de Estadística (Spain)|INE]]), the city has a population of 14,076 people.
