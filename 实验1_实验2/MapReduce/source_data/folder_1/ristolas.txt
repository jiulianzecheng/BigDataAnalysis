It is now part of [[Abriès-Ristolas]]. 
 
 == Geography == 
 Ristolas is in the [[Cottian Alps]]. The Guil river passes through the village. 
 
 == Transport == 
 
 === Road === 
 The D947 and D947T roads pass through Ristolas.
