'''Spielwang''' is a small village within the [[Municipalities of Germany|municipality]] of [[Vachendorf]] in [[Traunstein (district)|Traunstein]] in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. < ref > [http://www.vachendorf.de/index.php?id=72 Districts of Vachendorf] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160303223233/http://www.vachendorf.de/index.php?id=72 |date=2016-03-03 }} (german) < /ref > 
 
 It is not far away from the [[Chiemsee]].
