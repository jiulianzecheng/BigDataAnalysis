 '''Chimichurri''' is a type of sauce commonly eaten in [[Argentina]] and [[Uruguay]]. < ref > {{Cite web|last=Goldstein|first=Joyce|date=2012-10-05|title=The mysterious origins of chimichurri|url=https://www.sfgate.com/cooking/article/The-mysterious-origins-of-chimichurri-3923432.php|access-date=2021-07-14|website=SFGATE|language=en-US}} < /ref >  It is commonly used as a condiment for [[steak]]. The sauce can be either green or red. It is made of [[parsley]], [[oregano]], [[garlic]], [[red wine vinegar]] and [[olive oil]].
