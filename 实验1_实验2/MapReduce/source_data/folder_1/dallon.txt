 '''Dallon''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is in the [[Picardie]] [[Regions of France|region]] in the [[Aisne]] [[Departments of France|department]] in north [[France]]. The [[Somme]] River the Saint-Quentin canal pass through Dallon.  Its name used to be Dalonaie, on record in the year 1035.
