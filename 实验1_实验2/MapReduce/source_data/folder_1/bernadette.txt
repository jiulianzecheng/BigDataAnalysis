 '''Bernadette''' [ bur-n''uh''-'''det'''; ''French'' ber-na-'''det''' ] < ref > {{Cite web|title=Definition of Bernadette {{!}} Dictionary.com|url=https://www.dictionary.com/browse/bernadette|access-date=2023-03-15|website=www.dictionary.com|language=en}} < /ref > is a feminine given name. It is the female version of the masculine name '''Bernard'''. Bernadette is a name with French roots that has its origins in the Germanic name Bernhard, which translates to  " powerful, courageous bear.
