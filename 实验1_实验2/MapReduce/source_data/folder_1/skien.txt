 '''Skien''' is a city and a municipality in [[Telemark]], [[Norway]]. Skien is the capital of [[Vestfold og Telemark]] county. Skien has a population of 55 079. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Kommunefakta|url=https://www.ssb.no/kommunefakta/kommune|access-date=2020-11-04|website=SSB|language=nb}} < /ref > 
 
 == Geography == 
 Skien is located in the South-East of Telemark. The closest city to Skien is [[Porsgrunn]]. Together Skien and Porsgrunn is the seventh largest urban area in Norway. < ref > {{Cite journal|date=2020-10-24|title=List of urban areas in Norway by population|url=https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=List_of_urban_areas_in_Norway_by_population & oldid=985223440|journal=Wikipedia|language=en}} < /ref >  Skienselva is a river flowing through the city, dividing Skien in half.
