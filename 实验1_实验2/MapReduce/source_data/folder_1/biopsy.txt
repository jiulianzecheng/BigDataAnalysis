 A '''biopsy''' is a test in [[medicine]] where [[Medical doctor|doctors]] remove [[cell]]s and look at them closely under a [[microscope]] or do [[chemical]] analysis to see whether there is an [[illness]]. < ref > {{cite web |title=Biopsat |url=https://flexikon.doccheck.com/de/Biopsat |website=DocCheck Flexikon |language=de}} < /ref >
