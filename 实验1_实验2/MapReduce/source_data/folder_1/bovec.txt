 '''Bovec''' ({{Lang-it|Plezzo}}, {{lang-de|Flitsch}}) is a small [[Slovenia]]n town in the [[Julian Alps]] that is the [[Administration|administrative]] centre of the [[Municipalities of Slovenia|Municipality]] of Bovec ({{lang-sl|Občina Bovec}}). 
 
 The municipality of Bovec was established on 10 March 1994. Before that date, Bovec was part of the municipality of [[Tolmin]]. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://www.stat.si/doc/pub/02-PP-244-0701.pdf |title=Vzpostavitev lokalne samouprave v Republiki Sloveniji v številkah |trans-title=The Establishment of the Local Self-Government in the Republic of Slovenia in Numbers |language=Sl|format=pdf |publisher=Statistical Office of the Republic of Slovenia |isbn=978-961-239-131-7 |year=2007 |issue=6 |first=Aleksandar |last=Milenković |pages=19-20 |access-date=19 September 2014}} < /ref > 
 
 ==Geography== 
 The municipality of Bovec is part of the [[Goriška Statistical Region|Goriška]] [[Statistical regions of Slovenia|statistical region]]. It is in the northwest part of the country, on the border with [[Italy]] and is at about {{Convert|30|km|mi|0|abbr=on }} away from [[Austria]]. Part of the municipality is in the [[Triglav National Park]]. 
 
 The town of Bovec is at an [[altitude]] of {{Convert|434|m|ft|abbr=on }}, < ref name= " height "  / >  on the edge of the Bovec valley, a broad and flat valley where the small ''[[Koritnica]]'' river joins the [[Isonzo|Soča]] river. 
 
 The municipality has an area {{Convert|367.3|km2|sqmi|abbr=on }}, < ref name=geo / >  and a population, in 2017, of 3,082: 1,512 men and 1,570 women. < ref name=stats / >  Its [[population density]] is of {{Pop density|3082|367.3|km2|prec=1|disp=num}} inhabitants/km < sup > 2 < /sup > . 
 
 The municipality of Bovec is surrounded by the following municipalities: [[Tarvisio]] ([[Italy]]) to the north, [[Kranjska Gora]] to the northeast, [[Bohinj]] to the southeast, [[Kobarid]] to the south, [[Chiusaforte]] ([[Italy]]) to the west. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://www.geopedia.si/Geopedia_en.html#T105_F408:6_x396183_y135950_s11_b4 |title=Municipality of Bovec - map |language=Sl |year=2013 |publisher=Geopedia.si |access-date=20 September 2014 |archive-date=20 June 2012 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120620155109/http://www.geopedia.si/Geopedia_en.html#T105_F408:6_x396183_y135950_s11_b4 |url-status=dead }} < /ref >

 ===Climate=== 
 Mild winters and warm summers are typical of the Bovec Valley. The mean annual temperature in the Bovec municipality is {{Convert|11|°C|°F }}; the mean summer temperature (July, August) is {{Convert|19.5|°C|°F }} and the mean winter temperature (December, January) is {{Convert|-0.5|°C|°F }}. The average annual [[precipitation]] in the Bovec Valley is around {{Convert|3000|mm|in|1|abbr=on }}. The Kanin mountains have the highest precipitation in Slovenia, mostly in the form of snow between November and March. < ref > {{cite web| url=http://www.bovec.si/about_bovec/climate_and_weather/ |title=Climate in the Bovec area |work=Bovec - The Valleys of Inspiration (Official website of the municipality) |access-date=1 December 2014 }} < /ref >
