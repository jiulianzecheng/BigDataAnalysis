 Mangala (Sanskrit: मङ्गल, IAST: Maṅgala) is the name for [[Mars]], the red planet, in [[Hindu]] texts. Also known as Lohit (meaning: red), he is '''the god of anger, celibate''' and sometimes linked to god [[Kartikeya]] (Skanda).
