 The '''bontebok''' is a subspecies of ''Damaliscus pygargus'', an [[antelope]] found in [[South Africa]], [[Lesotho]] and [[Namibia]]. ''D. pygargus'' has two [[subspecies]]; the bontebok (''Damaliscus pygargus pygargus''), < ref > {{MSW3|id=14200523}} < /ref >  occurring naturally in the [[Fynbos]] and Renosterveld areas of the Western Cape, and the [[blesbok]] (''Damaliscus pygargus phillipsi'') occurring in the [[Highveld]]. 
 
 The bontebok is related to the [[common tsessebe]].
