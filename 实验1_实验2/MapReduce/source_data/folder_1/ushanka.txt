 An '''''ushanka''''' ({{lang|ru|уша́нка}}) is a [[Russia]]n [[fur]] [[cap]] with ear flaps that can be tied up to the [[crown (anatomy)|crown]] of the cap, or tied at the [[chin]] to protect the [[ears]] from the cold. ''Ushanka'' means  " ear-flaps hat " , from  " ushi "   
 ({{lang|ru|у́ши}}),  " ears "  in [[Russian language|Russian]].
