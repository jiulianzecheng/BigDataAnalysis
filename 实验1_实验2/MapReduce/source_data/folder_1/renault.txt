 '''Renault S.A.''' is a French [[vehicle]] [[manufacturer]] producing cars, vans, buses, tractors, and trucks, and, in the past, [[autorail]] vehicles. Its alliance with [[Nissan Motor Company|Nissan]] makes it the world's ninth largest vehicle maker. < ref name= " autogenerated2 " > [http://www.renault.com/ Renault] < /ref >  Headquartered in [[Boulogne-Billancourt]], Renault owns the [[Romania]]n [[Automobile Dacia]] and the Korean [[Renault Samsung Motors]]. The company's most successful [[car]]s to date are the [[Renault Clio]] and the [[Renault Laguna]], and its core market is Europe. < ref name= " autogenerated2 "  / >  The company is known for numerous revolutionary designs, security technologies and motor racing.
