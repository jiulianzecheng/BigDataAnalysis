'''Bichon''' is the name of several usually small dogs. Officially, they include: 
 
 * [[Bichon Frisé|Bichon Fisé]] 
 * [[Bolognese (dog)|Bolognese]] 
 * [[Havanese dog|Havanese]] 
 * [[Maltese (dog)|Maltese]] 
 * [[Coton de Tulear]] 
 * [[Löwchen]] 
 
 Two breeds that are also part of the group (but which aren't recognised by the [[Federation Cynologique Internationale|FCI]]) are: 
 
 * [[Bolonka Franzuska]] 
 * [[Bolonka Zwetna]]
