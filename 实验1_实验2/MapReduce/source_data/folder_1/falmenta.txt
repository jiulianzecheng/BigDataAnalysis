 '''Falmenta''' was a ''[[comune]]'' in the [[Province of Verbano-Cusio-Ossola]] in the [[Italy|Italian]] region of [[Piedmont]]. On 1 January 2019, the former municipalities of [[Cavaglio-Spoccia]], [[Cursolo-Orasso]] and Falmenta merged to form the new municipality of [[Valle Cannobina]].
