 '''''ParaNorman''''' is a 2012 [[United States|American]] animated comedy horror movie set in [[Massachusetts]]. It stars [[Kodi Smit-McPhee]], [[Jodelle Ferland]], [[Tucker Albrizzi]], [[Anna Kendrick]], [[Casey Affleck]], [[Christopher Mintz-Plasse]], [[Leslie Mann]], [[Jeff Garlin]], [[Bernard Hill]], [[Elaine Stritch]], [[Tempestt Bledsoe]], [[John Goodman]], and [[Alex Borstein]]. It was released on August 17, 2012. It was nominated for an [[Academy Award]] for Best Animated Film.
