 '''Daria''' is an [[United States|American]] [[animated]] [[comedy-drama]] [[television series]]. It is a [[spin-off]] of [[Beavis and Butt-head]]. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://blogs.villagevoice.com/music/2010/05/qa_tracy_grands.php|title=Q & A: Tracy Grandstaff, the Voice Behind Daria, Talks About the Show's Long-Awaited Release on DVD|author=Stacey Anderson|work=Village Voice|access-date=17 June 2015|archive-date=1 January 2015|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150101065610/http://blogs.villagevoice.com/music/2010/05/qa_tracy_grands.php|url-status=dead}} < /ref >  It centers on teenager [[Daria Morgendorffer]]. It is set in a [[fictional universe]] in [[Lawndale, California|Lawndale]]. The show started airing on January 1, 1997. The last episode was shown on January 21, 2002.  < ref > {{cite web|url=http://outpostdaria.info/media_art28.html|title=Outpost Daria - In the Media: CBS Early Show - January 21, 2002|work=outpostdaria.info|access-date=17 June 2015|archive-date=9 March 2016|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160309173049/http://outpostdaria.info/media_art28.html|url-status=dead}} < /ref >
