'''Dingwall''' is a [[town]] in the north of [[Scotland]]. It is home to the local [[association football|football]] club, [[Ross County F.C.]] 
 
 Population: 5,470 (2016) 
 
 Dingwall is 198 km far from [[Edinburgh|Edinburgh.]]
