 '''Pfreimd''' is a town in [[Schwandorf (district)|Schwandorf]], in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. It is on the river [[Naab]], 19 & nbsp;km north of [[Schwandorf]], and 20 & nbsp;km south of [[Weiden in der Oberpfalz]]. It is also close to the border with the [[Czech Republic]].
