 '''Malaparamba''' lies in the outskirts of [[Kozhikode|Kozhikode City]]. It is the start of the suburban region of [[Kozhikode district|Kozhikode]] towards the northern region. The area spans over both sides of the Wayanad Road or National highway 766. < ref > {{Cite web|last1=May 3|first1=TNN / Updated|last2=2012|last3=Ist|first3=05:45|title=NH 17 bypass stretch becomes accident-prone {{!}} Kozhikode News - Times of India|url=https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/city/kozhikode/nh-17-bypass-stretch-becomes-accident-prone/articleshow/12974554.cms|access-date=2021-04-30|website=The Times of India|language=en}} < /ref > 
 
 == Location == 
 Malaparamba lies to the north of the Malaparamba Junction, < ref > {{Cite web|last1=Jun 7|first1=TNN / Updated|last2=2012|last3=Ist|first3=00:57|title=Oommen Chandy to inaugurate Malaparamba bypass {{!}} Kozhikode News - Times of India|url=https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/city/kozhikode/oommen-chandy-to-inaugurate-malaparamba-bypass/articleshow/13875480.cms|access-date=2021-04-30|website=The Times of India|language=en}} < /ref >  where [[:en:National_Highway_66_(India)|National Highway 66]] and [[:en:National_Highway_66_(India)|National Highway 766]] intersect. < ref > {{Cite news|others=Special Correspondent|date=2017-08-03|title=Proposal for linking Beypore to Malaparamba via Kozhikode beach|language=en-IN|work=The Hindu|url=https://www.thehindu.com/news/cities/kozhikode/proposal-for-linking-beypore-to-malaparamba-via-kozhikode-beach/article19413043.ece|access-date=2021-04-30|issn=0971-751X}} < /ref >  Kozhikode city lies towards the south of the Junction. < ref > {{Cite web|last1=Dec 30|first1=K. M. Sree / TNN /|last2=2014|last3=Ist|first3=12:02|title=Highway widening hits flyover project {{!}} Kozhikode News - Times of India|url=https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/city/kozhikode/highway-widening-hits-flyover-project/articleshow/45685507.cms|access-date=2021-04-30|website=The Times of India|language=en}} < /ref >   
 
 It is 17.38 km. away from the [[Calicut International Airport]]. The distance to the [[:en:Kozhikode_railway_station|Kozhikode Railway Station]] is 7.8 km. from Malaparamba. The [https://kozhikode.nic.in/tourism/transportation/ bus routes] that pass through here are ''Medical College-Malaparamba'' route and ''Vellimadukunnu-Malaparamba-City'' route.  
 
 == Locality == 
 Malaparamba is primarily a residential area. < ref > {{Cite web|last1=Jul 5|first1=K. M. Sree / TNN /|last2=2019|last3=Ist|first3=14:00|title=Police demand relocation of electric posts for road safety {{!}} Kozhikode News - Times of India|url=https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/city/kozhikode/police-demand-relocation-of-electric-posts-for-road-safety/articleshow/70089295.cms|access-date=2021-04-30|website=The Times of India|language=en}} < /ref >  There are 4 main residential pockets are ''Malaparamba Housing Colony (KSHB Colony), BSNL colony, Deshodharani Colony and Nanma Colony''. Each colony houses over a 100 families. 
 < br > 
 The small region is surrounded by small and micro enterprises that are owned by single individuals or family-owned businesses. There are multiple shops and varied facilities available in Malaparamba, varying from boutiques to electronic spare parts, and marts to small restaurants. < br >   
 
 The area is currently under plan for Road Renovation under the District Authority's plans.  < ref > {{Cite news|others=Special Correspondent|date=2018-11-14|title=₹400-cr. road project for Kozhikode|language=en-IN|work=The Hindu|url=https://www.thehindu.com/news/cities/kozhikode/400-cr-road-project-for-city/article25488571.ece|access-date=2021-04-30|issn=0971-751X}} < /ref >
