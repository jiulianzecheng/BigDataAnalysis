 '''Veslud''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is in the [[Picardie]] [[Regions of France|region]] in the [[Aisne]] [[Departments of France|department]] in the north of [[France]]. In 2012, 253 people lived there. 
 
 The [[motto]] « Vetus lucus semper virens » ( " Old sacred wood still green " ) suggests it might have been a place of [[cult]] during [[Antiquity]]. 
 
 
 A German cemetery was built at the end of [[World War I]], where 1,704 soldiers are buried.
