'''Copperbelt''' is a province in the northern part of [[Zambia]]. It is named after the [[copper]] reserves. It has an area of 31,328 & nbsp;km < sup > 2 < /sup > . It occupies the upper portion of the Kafue river basin on the central plateau of southern Africa, 900 to 1,500 m above sea level.
