 '''Giovanni Antonio Canal''' (18 October 1697 – 19 April 1768) was an [[Italy|Italian]] [[painter]] from [[Venice]]. Today, he is mostly remembered for his landscape paintings. Some of these landscape paintings which resemble the views found on modern-day [[postcard]]s are known as [[Vedute]]s today. He was also an important [[printmaker]] in [[etching]].
