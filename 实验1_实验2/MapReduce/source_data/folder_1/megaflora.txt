'''Megaflora''' (from [[Ancient Greek|Greek]] μέγας ''megas''  " huge "  and [[Latin]] ''Flōra'' goddess of flowers, from ''flōs''  " flower " ) is the collective name of extremely large plant species. Examples of megaflora include the [[Sequoioideae|sequoias]] of California and a number of extinct plant species from the [[Mesozoic]].
