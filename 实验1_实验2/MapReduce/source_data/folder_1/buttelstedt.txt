 '''Buttelstedt''' is a [[town]] and a former [[municipality]] in the [[Weimarer Land Rural District|Weimarer Land]] district, in [[Thuringia]], [[Germany]]. It is {{convert|11|km|abbr=off}} north of [[Weimar]]. Since 1 January 2019, it has been part of the municipality [[Am Ettersberg]].
