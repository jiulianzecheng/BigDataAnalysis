'''Meningoencephalitis''' ({{IPAc-en|m|ɪ|ˌ|n|ɪ|ŋ|ɡ|oʊ|ɛ|n|ˌ|s|ɛ|f|ə|ˈ|l|aɪ|t|ᵻ|s|,_|-|ˌ|n|ɪ|n|dʒ|oʊ|-|,_|-|ə|n|-|,_|-|ˌ|k|ɛ|-}};{{refn|{{OxfordDictionaries.com|accessdate=2016-01-21|Meningoencephalitis}}}}{{refn|{{MerriamWebsterDictionary|accessdate=2016-01-21|Meningoencephalitis}}}} from [[Ancient Greek|Greek]] μῆνιγξ ''meninx'',  " membrane " , ἐγκέφαλος, ''enképhalos''  " brain " , and the medical suffix ''-itis'',  " inflammation " ), also known as '''herpes meningoencephalitis''', is a [[Disease|medical condition]]. It is like [[meningitis]], which is an [[infection]] or [[inflammation]] of the [[meninges]], and [[encephalitis]], which is an infection or inflammation of the [[brain]].
