'''Yoronjima''' (与論島, [[Okinawan language|Okinawan]] and [[Yoron language|Yoron]]: ユンヌ ''Yunnu''; [[Kunigami language|Kunigami]]: ユンヌー ''Yunnuu)'' is one of the [[Amami Islands]] of [[Okinawa Prefecture]], [[Japan]]. 
 
 In 2018, it had 5,056 people. < ref > {{Cite web|url=http://www.citypopulation.de/php/japan-kagoshima.php?cityid=46535|title=Yoron (Kagoshima , Japan) - Population Statistics, Charts, Map, Location, Weather and Web Information|website=www.citypopulation.de|access-date=2020-02-03}} < /ref >  Most of them are [[Ryukyuan people|Ryukyuan]], who are the natives of the [[Ryūkyū Islands]]. < ref > {{Cite web|url=https://minorityrights.org/minorities/ryukyuans-okinawans/|title=Ryukyuans (Okinawans)|website=Minority Rights Group|date=19 June 2015 |language=en-GB|access-date=2020-02-03}} < /ref >
