 '''Weibersbrunn''' is a community with a population of close to 2,000 in the [[Aschaffenburg (district)|Aschaffenburg district]] in the ''[[Regierungsbezirk]]'' of [[Lower Franconia]] (''Unterfranken'') in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]].
