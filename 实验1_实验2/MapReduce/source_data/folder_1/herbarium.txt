 In [[botany]], an '''herbarium''' is the [[building]] where the [[specimen]]s are [[wikt:storing|stored]], or the [[Science|scientific]] [[Institution|institute]] that not only stores but [[research]]es these specimens. A [[wikt:dry|dried]] [[plant]] [[wikt:mount|mounted]] on a sheet also is an herbarium. 
 In scientific [[literature]] herbaria are [[wikt:recognize|recognized]] by [[acronym]]s, 
 for example NY specimens are kept in [[New York Botanical garden]].
