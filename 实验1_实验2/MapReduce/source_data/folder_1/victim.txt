'''Victim''' is a term used for a person who suffers adverse circumstances, often in relation to having a [[crime]] committed against them. If someone is [[Robbery|robbed]], he or she is the victim. If someone is [[kill]]ed, that person is the victim. The person who committed the crime is the [[culprit]]. 
 [[Victim blaming]] is saying that it is the fault of the victim that a crime was committed against them.
