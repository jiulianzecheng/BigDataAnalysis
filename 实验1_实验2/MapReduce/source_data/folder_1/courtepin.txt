 '''Courtepin''' is a [[municipality]] of the district of [[See (district)|See]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Fribourg (canton)|Fribourg]] in [[Switzerland]]. On 1 January 2017, the former municipalities of [[Barberêche]], [[Villarepos]] and [[Wallenried]] merged into Courtepin. < ref > [https://www.bfs.admin.ch/bfs/de/home/grundlagen/agvch.html Amtliches Gemeindeverzeichnis der Schweiz] {{in lang|de}} accessed 10 January 2017 < /ref >
