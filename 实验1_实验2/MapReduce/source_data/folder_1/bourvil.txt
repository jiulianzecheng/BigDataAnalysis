 '''André Bourvil''' was a French actor and singer.  He was born with the name '''André Robert Raimbourg'''. He is best known for his roles in comedy movies.  He worked with [[Louis de Funès]] in the movies ''[[Le Corniaud]]'' (1965) and ''[[La Grande Vadrouille]]'' (1966).
