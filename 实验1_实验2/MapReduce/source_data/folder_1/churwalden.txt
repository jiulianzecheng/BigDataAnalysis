 '''Churwalden''' ({{lang-rm|Curvalda}}) is a [[municipality]] in the [[district]] of [[Plessur (district)|Plessur]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Graubünden]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 On 1 January 2010, the former municipalities of [[Malix]] and [[Parpan]] merged into the municipality of Churwalden.
