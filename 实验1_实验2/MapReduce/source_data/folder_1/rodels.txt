 '''Rodels''' ({{lang-rm|Roten}}) was a [[municipality]] of the [[district]] [[Hinterrhein (district)|Hinterrhein]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Graubünden]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 In 2015, Rodels became a part of the new municipality of [[Domleschg]].
