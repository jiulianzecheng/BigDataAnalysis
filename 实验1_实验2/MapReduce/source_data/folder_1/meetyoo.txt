 '''MEETYOO''' is a technology company with offices in Berlin, Mannheim, and New York. It develops virtual events software for companies.  
 
 == Overview == 
 The platform was created in 1999 as a teleconferencing company by Tony Kula. < ref > {{Cite web|date=2020-08-01|title=Why to choose 3D for your next virtual event|url=https://venturebeat.com/marketing/why-to-choose-3d-for-your-next-virtual-event/|access-date=2022-11-15|website=VentureBeat|language=en-US}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite web|title=Event Industry News - The Sustainable Event Buyer's Guide 2022 - Page 62-63 - Created with Publitas.com|url=https://view.publitas.com/event-industry-news/the-sustainable-event-buyers-guide-2022/page/62-63|access-date=2023-01-13|website=view.publitas.com|language=en}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite web|date=2021-04-21|title=Software virtuelle Events: 13 Lösungen im Vergleich|url=https://www.contentmanager.de/loesungen/software-virtuelle-events-grosser-vergleich-der-anbieter/|access-date=2023-01-13|website=contentmanager Magazin|language=de-DE}} < /ref > 
 
 In 2014, it launched SmartConference, a video conferencing service. < ref > {{Cite web|last=Team|first=XR Today|date=2021-03-12|title=meetyoo Review: A New Era In Events|url=https://www.xrtoday.com/reviews/vr-reviews/meetyoo-review/|access-date=2023-01-13|website=XR Today|language=en-GB}} < /ref >   In 2016, the company acquired ubivent, a company that provided software for digital events. It later became MEETYOO. < ref > {{Cite web|last=meetyoo|title=Meetyoo Conferencing übernimmt Ubivent|url=https://www.iavcworld.de/unified-com/3782-meetyoo-conferencing-uebernimmt-ubivent.html|access-date=2023-01-13|website=www.iavcworld.de|language=de-de}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite web|last=Wirtschaftsforum.de|first=Redaktion|title=Messen und Konferenzen weltweit digital verbinden|url=https://www.wirtschaftsforum.de/portraits/meetyoo-conferencing-gmbh/messen-und-konferenzen-weltweit-digital-verbinden/|access-date=2023-01-13|website=wirtschaftsforum.de|language=de-DE}} < /ref > 
 
 In 2022, it partnered with Kollective to optimize video transmission in corporate networks. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Videooptimierung in Firmennetzwerken|url=https://www.tw-media.com/news/meetyoo-und-kollective-videooptimierung-in-firmennetzwerken-131793|access-date=2023-01-13|website=tw-media|language=de}} < /ref >
