 {{Nihongo|'''Naruhito'''|徳仁|extra=born 23 February 1960}} is the current [[emperor of Japan]]. < ref > {{Cite news|url=https://www.bbc.com/news/world-asia-48020703|title=Japan's Emperor Akihito abdicates|date=2019-04-30|access-date=2019-04-30|language=en-GB}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite web|url=https://www.nst.com.my/world/2019/04/484432/naruhito-new-emperor-japan-reiwa-era-begins|title=Naruhito is new emperor of Japan; 'Reiwa' era begins|website=www.nst.com.my|access-date=2019-04-30}} < /ref >   
 
 Naruhito is the eldest son of former emperor [[Akihito]] and Empress [[Empress Michiko|Michiko]] of Japan. He was originally known as ''His Imperial Highness'' The Prince Hiro but was later given the title of Crown Prince in [[1991]]. 
 
 His father Emperor Akihito announced in December 2017 that he would [[Abdication|abdicate]] on 30 April 2019. < ref name= " :0 " > {{cite news |last=Enjoji |first=Kaori |date=December 1, 2017 |title=Japan Emperor Akihito to abdicate on April 30, 2019 |url=http://edition.cnn.com/2017/11/30/asia/japan-emperor-akihito-abdication-intl/index.html |work=[[CNN]] |location=Tokyo |access-date=December 1, 2017 }} < /ref >  Naruhito became emperor on 1 May 2019 and the [[enthronement]] was held on 22 October 2019. < ref > {{Cite web |url=https://mainichi.jp/english/articles/20191022/p2g/00m/0na/014000c |title=Japan's Emperor proclaims enthronement in ancient-style ceremony |access-date=2019-10-22 |archive-date=2019-10-22 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20191022104040/https://mainichi.jp/english/articles/20191022/p2g/00m/0na/014000c |url-status=dead }} < /ref > 
 
 ==Early life and careers == 
 Naruhito was born on 23 February 1960 at Imperial Household Agency Hospital, Imperial Palace in Tokyo,    
 
 He was crowned prince of Japan in 1991, He was first study in Gakushūin, He was graduated at Gakushuin University in 1982, He was studying in The House or College of Scholars of Merton in the University of Oxford in 1983 to 1985   
 
 Naruhito and [[Masako, Crown Princess of Japan|Masako]] have engagement in spring of 1993 , their married in 9 June 1993 at Imperial Shinto Hall in Tokyo,  
 
 Naruhito and Masako's only daughter [[Aiko, Princess Toshi]] was born in 1 December 2001 at Imperial Household Agency Hospital in Tokyo Imperial Palace. 
 
 ==Titles and styles== 
 * '''23 February 1960 – 23 February 1991''': ''His Imperial Highness'' The Prince Hiro 
 * '''23 February 1991 – 30 April 2019''': ''His Imperial Highness'' The Crown Prince of Japan 
 * '''1 May 2019 – present''': ''His Majesty'' The Emperor
