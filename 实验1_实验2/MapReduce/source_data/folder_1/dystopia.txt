A '''dystopia''' is the opposite of a [[utopia]] (a place everyone enjoys living in), and can mean a utopia that has become [[corruption|corrupt]]. A dystopia is a place where little people enjoy living in. A dystopia often has many problems, such as [[poverty]], [[pollution]], or a cruel ruler. A frequent theme is a place that is supposed to be a beautiful and perfect utopia, but where something unforeseen ruins it.
