 '''Liebenswiller''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is in the [[Haut-Rhin]] [[Departments of France|department]] of east [[France]]. 
 
 The D9B road passes through the commune. 
 
 The commune is next to the French-Swiss border.
