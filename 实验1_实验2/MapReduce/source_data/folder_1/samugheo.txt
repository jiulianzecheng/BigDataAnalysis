 '''Samugheo''' (''Samughèo'') is a [[town]] and ''[[Commune|comune]]'' (municipality) in the [[Province of Oristano]] in [[Sardinia]], [[Italy]]. As of 2016, 3,030 people lived there. < ref > {{Cite web |title=Istituto Nazionale di Statistica - ''Bilancio demografico al 30/11/2016'' |url=http://demo.istat.it/bilmens2016gen/index02.html |access-date=2019-04-27 |archive-date=2019-03-11 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190311225842/http://demo.istat.it/bilmens2016gen/index02.html |url-status=dead }} < /ref >  Its area is 81.28 & nbsp;km². < ref > Istituto nazionale di statistica - [http://www.istat.it/it/files/2015/04/Altitudini_comuni_DEM.zip ''Superficie territoriale per zona altimetrica''] < /ref >  It is 370 meters above sea level.
