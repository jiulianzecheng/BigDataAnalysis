 '''Varades''' is a former [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is found in the [[Regions of France|region]] [[Pays de la Loire]] in the [[Loire-Atlantique]] [[Departments of France|department]] in western [[France]]. On 1 January 2016, it was merged into the new commune of [[Loireauxence]]. < ref > [https://www.legifrance.gouv.fr/eli/arrete/2015/12/18/INTB1532335A/jo/texte Arrêté préfectoral] 18 December 2015 < /ref >
