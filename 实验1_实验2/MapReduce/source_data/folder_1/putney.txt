'''Putney''' is a rich area of the [[London Borough of Wandsworth]] in south-west [[London]]. Its [[postcode]] is SW15. It is bordered to the north by the River [[Thames]]. It is easily accessible from [[Putney Bridge]] and the [[A3 road|A3]] main road; it has good connections to central London. The school Putney High is found on Putney Hill.
