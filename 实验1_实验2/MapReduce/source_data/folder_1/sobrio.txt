 '''Sobrio''' was a [[municipality]] of the district [[Leventina (district)|Leventina]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Ticino]] in [[Switzerland]]. On 10 April 2016, it was merged into the municipality of [[Faido]]. < ref > [http://www.bfs.admin.ch/bfs/portal/de/index/infothek/nomenklaturen/blank/blank/gem_liste/03.html Amtliches Gemeindeverzeichnis der Schweiz] published by the Swiss Federal Statistical Office {{in lang|de}} accessed 27 April 2016 < /ref >
