 '''Agni''' is the [[Vedic]] fire god of [[Hinduism]]. 
 According to the [[Vedas]], Agni is considered as the mouth of the [[Deity|Deities]], and the medium that conveys offerings to them in the Yagynas. 
 According to Bhavishya Purana The Agnivanshi [[Rajput|Rajputs]] ([[Parmar|Parmaras]], Chauhanas, Pratiharas, and Solankis) are the descendants of the fire god Agni.
