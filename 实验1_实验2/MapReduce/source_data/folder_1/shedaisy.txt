 '''SHeDAISY''' {{IPAc-en|ʃ|ə|ˈ|d|eɪ|z|i}} is an [[Americans|American]] [[country music]] group. They were founded in the 1999 by sisters Kristyn, Kelsi and Kassidy Osborn. They are from [[Magna, Utah]]. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://www.cmt.com/artists/shedaisy/biography/ |title=About SHeDAISY |publisher=Viacom International Inc. |access-date=30 July 2015}} < /ref >  The group's name comes from the word ''shideezhí'', a [[Navajo language|Navajo]] term meaning  " my little sister " . < ref > {{cite web|title=The Shideezhí Project|url=http://www.uh.edu/bauernawmba/Shideezhi.html|publisher=National Association of Woman MBAs|access-date=30 July 2015|archive-date=4 March 2016|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160304072343/http://www.uh.edu/bauernawmba/Shideezhi.html|url-status=dead}} < /ref > 
 
 By 1999 the sisters formed the group SHeDAISY and signed to [[Lyric Street Records]]. Their first album, ''[[The Whole SHeBANG]]'', was released that year. < ref name=HueyAM > {{cite web |url=http://www.allmusic.com/artist/shedaisy-mn0000009437/biography|title=Artist Biography |author=Steve Huey |publisher=AllMusic/All Media Network, LLC.|access-date=30 July 2015}} < /ref >  The [[Single (music)|single]]  " Little Good-Byes "  from that album charted number three on the country charts. < ref name=HueyAM/ >  It was certified platinum in the United States. SHeDAISY has charted 15 times on the ''[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]'' [[Hot Country Songs]] charts. Their hits include  " [[Little Good-Byes]], "   " [[This Woman Needs]] " ,  " [[I Will… But]] " ,  " [[Don't Worry 'bout a Thing (SHeDAISY song)|Don't Worry 'bout a Thing]] " , and two Christmas singles.
