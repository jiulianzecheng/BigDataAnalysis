 The '''Vengaboys''' are a [[Netherlands|Dutch]] [[pop music]] [[band|group]]. The group is made up of DJ Danski and DJ Delmondo. They toured [[Spain|Spanish]] [[beach]] [[party|parties]] in a [[bus]]. The group formed in 1992. They added four [[dance]]rs in 1995-1996 and toured through [[Europe]]. Their first [[song]] was ''Up  &  Down''.  It was released in March 1997. The group is [[Popularity|popular]] across Europe and have had some popularity in the [[United States]]. Their music is [[Eurodance]].
