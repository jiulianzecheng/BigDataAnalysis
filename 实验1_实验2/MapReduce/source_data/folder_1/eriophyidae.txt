 The '''Eriophyidae''' or '''gall mites''' are a [[family (taxonomy)|family]] of [[mites]]. They are plant [[parasites]] and produce [[galls]]. < ref name= " Storch " > Storch, Volker  &  Welsch, Ulrich 1997. ''Systematische Zoologie''. Fischer. {{ISBN|3-437-25160-0}} < /ref > 
 
 The mites can cause severe damage to the host plant. They have only two pairs of legs and are very small, 0.08 & nbsp;mm - 0.3 & nbsp;mm. < ref name= " Urania " > FÃ¼ller, Horst ''et al'' 1994. ''Urania Tierreich - Wirbellose Tiere 2''. Leipzig  &  Jena: Urania-Verlag, 70  &  88. {{ISBN|3-332-00502-2}} < /ref >  The [[gut]] is straight, there are no outgrowths of the gut. They have no [[heart]] or [[circulatory system]]. < ref name= " Storch "  / >
