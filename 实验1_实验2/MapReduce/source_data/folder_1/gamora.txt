 '''Gamora''' is a [[Marvel Comics]] character created by [[Jim Starlin]] and is [[Thanos]]' adoptive daughter. She is a member of the [[Guardians of the Galaxy (comics)|Guardians of the Galaxy]]. 
 
 ==Portrayals== 
 Gamora has been played by [[Zoe Saldana]] in four movies and has been voiced by [[Vanessa Marshall]] and [[Nika Futterman]] in cartoons.
