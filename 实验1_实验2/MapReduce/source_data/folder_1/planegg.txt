 '''Planegg''' is a [[Municipalities of Germany|municipality]]  in [[Munich (district)|Munich]], in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. It is on the river [[Würm]], 13 & nbsp;km west of [[Munich]] (centre). 
 
 == Twin towns == 
 * {{flagicon|France}} [[Meylan]], France, since 1987. 
 * {{flagicon|Germany}} [[Bärenstein (Erzgebirge)|Bärenstein]] in [[Erzgebirge]], since 1992. 
 * {{flagicon|South Tyrol}} [[Klausen, South Tyrol|Klausen]] in [[Südtirol]], since 2006. 
 * {{flagicon|UK}} [[Didcot]], United Kingdom, since 2012. < ref > [https://www.merkur.de/lokales/wuermtal/planegg/planegg-didcot-werden-partner-2260261.html ''Planegg und Didcot werden Partner''], Münchner Merkur vom 30. März 2012 < /ref >
