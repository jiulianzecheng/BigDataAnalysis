 '''Melanin''' is a special [[pigment]] of the [[epidermis]]. It helps protect the body from the [[Solar radiation|sun's radiation]]. This pigment contributes to the color of the [[skin]] and is produced deep within the layers of the epidermis. It is created from [[amino acid]] [[tyrosine]] It help our skin to protect from the sun.
