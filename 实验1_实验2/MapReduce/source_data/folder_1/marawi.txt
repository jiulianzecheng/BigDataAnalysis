'''Marawi''' is a city in the [[Philippines]]. It is the capital of the [[Provinces of the Philippines|province]] of [[Lanao del Sur]]. According to the 2020 census, 207,010 people lived there. 
 
 Marawi is on the northern shore of [[Lake Lanao]].
