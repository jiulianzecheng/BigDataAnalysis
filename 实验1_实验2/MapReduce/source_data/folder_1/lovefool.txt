 " '''Lovefool''' "  is a song written by [[Peter Svensson]] and [[Nina Persson]] for [[Sweden|Swedish]] [[Rock music|rock]] band [[The Cardigans]]' third studio album, ''[[First Band on the Moon]]'' (1996). It was released as the album's [[lead single]] on 14 September 1996 in the United Kingdom and internationally on 5 October 1996.
