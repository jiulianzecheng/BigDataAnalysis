'''Sanghar''' ({{lang-ur|سانگھڑ}}) is a city in the [[Sindh]] province of [[Pakistan]]. The city is headquarters of [[Sanghar District]] and [[Sanghar Taluka]] (a subdivision of the district). < ref > {{Cite web |url=http://www.nrb.gov.pk/lg_election/union.asp?district=41 & dn=Sanghar |title=Tehsils  &  Unions in the District of Sanghar - Government of Pakistan |access-date=2008-12-09 |archive-date=2009-09-04 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20090904053518/http://www.nrb.gov.pk/lg_election/union.asp?district=41 & dn=Sanghar |url-status=dead }} < /ref >  It is an [[agriculture|agricultural]] town about 265km from [[Karachi]]. Unfortunately, there is no railway station and airport in the city. But the city does have road links with [[Hyderabad (Pakistan)|Hyderabad]], [[Nawabshah]], [[Mirpur Khas]] and other major cities of [[Sindh]].
