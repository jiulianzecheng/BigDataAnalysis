'''EKSPLA''' is a laser manufacturing company based in Lithuania. They specialize in producing lasers, laser systems, and other photonics components. The company was established in 1992 as a spin-off from the EKSMA company. < ref > [http://www.zef.lt/zef/index.php?id=81 Laser technologies in Lithuania (The Forum of Knowledge Economy website)] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110722153446/http://www.zef.lt/zef/index.php?id=81|date=2011-07-22}} < /ref > < ref name= " optolita " > {{Cite web |url=http://www.ekspla.com/en/main/news?id=23661 |title=EKSPLA restructured |access-date=2023-06-15 |archive-date=2008-10-19 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20081019202920/http://www.ekspla.com/en/main/news?id=23661 |url-status=dead }} < /ref >  They have two divisions: Laser  &  Laser Systems and Photonics Components. In 2008, the components business activities were moved to a separate company called Optolita, operating as EKSMA OPTICS. < ref > [http://www.npp.lt/index.php/pageid/117 National Advancement Awards 2007 (in Lithuanian)] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20080404153415/http://www.npp.lt/index.php/pageid/117|date=2008-04-04}} < /ref >  EKSPLA has received awards for their contributions to the knowledge-based economy and advancements in laser science. Their product lines include scientific lasers, industrial lasers, laser spectroscopy, and laser electronics components. < ref > [http://nominacijos.zef.lt/istorija.html Knowledge Economy Company Awards history (in Lithuanian)] < /ref > < ref > [http://www.esparama.lt/en/bpd/sf_projects/successful_projects/?id=7500 Project “Lasers for microprocessing and diagnostics” in figures] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20080211102814/http://www.esparama.lt/en/bpd/sf_projects/successful_projects/?id=7500|date=2008-02-11}} < /ref >
