 '''Gwynedd''' is a large area in North West [[Wales]]. It has a long [[coast]]-lime with many cliffs and beaches. It also has many mountains including [[Snowdon]], the highest mountain in [[England]] and Wales. Large towns include [[Bangor, Gwynedd|Bangor]] and [[Caernarfon]]. 
 
 The county was divided into five [[districts of Wales|districts]]: [[Aberconwy (district)|Aberconwy]], [[Arfon (district)|Arfon]], [[Dwyfor]], [[Meirionnydd]] and [[Isle of Anglesey|Anglesey]].
