 '''Tarragona''' ({{IPAc-en|lang|ˌ|t|ær|ə|ˈ|ɡ|oʊ|n|ə}} {{respell|TARR|ə|goh|nə}},  < small > also < /small >  {{IPAc-en|US|ˌ|t|ɑr|-}} {{respell|TAR|-}}, {{IPA-ca|tərəˈɣonə|lang}}, {{IPA-es|taraˈɣona|lang}}; [[Phoenician language|Phoenician]]: ''Tarqon''; {{lang-la|Tarraco}}) is a port city located in northeast [[Spain]] on the [[Costa Daurada]] by the [[Mediterranean Sea]]. It was founded before the 5th century BC, it is the capital of the [[Province of Tarragona]], and part of [[Tarragonès]] and [[Catalonia]]. The city has a population of 201,199 (2014).
