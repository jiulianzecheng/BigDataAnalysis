 '''Porsanger''', or ''Porsáŋgu'', is a municipality in [[Norway]]'s northernmost county. 
 
 Settlements include [[Lakselv]]. 
 
 ==Administration== 
 In October 2023, a new [[municipal council]] started its 4-year term.

 *3 from [[Norwegian Labour Party|Labour party]] 
 *3 from [[Progress Party (Norway)|Progress party]]

 *1 from [[Centre Party (Norway)|Centre party]]
