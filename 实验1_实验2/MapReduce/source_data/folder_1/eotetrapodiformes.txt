 '''Eotetrapodiformes''' is a [[clade]] of [[sarcopterygian]] fish including [[Tetrapod|tetrapods]] and their [[ancestors]]. < ref > Two groups of [[Stem tetrapod|stem tetrapods]] called [[Tristichopterid|tristichopterids]] and [[Elpistostegalid|elpistostegalids]]. < /ref >  Eotetrapodiformes is part of the larger clade [[tetrapodomorpha]].
