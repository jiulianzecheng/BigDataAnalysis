 The name '''commelinids''' is a [[plant]] taxonomy plural, not capitalised. It is used by the [[APG II|APG II system]] for a [[clade]] in the [[monocots]]. They have cell walls which contain ferulic acid. 
 
 It forms one of three groups in the monocots. The other two groups are the alismatid monocots and the lilioid monocots. 
 
 *order [[Arecales]] (palms) 
 *order [[Commelinales]] (spiderwort, water hyacinth) 
 *order [[Poales]] (grasses, rushes, bromeliads) 
 *order [[Zingiberales]] (gingers, banana)
