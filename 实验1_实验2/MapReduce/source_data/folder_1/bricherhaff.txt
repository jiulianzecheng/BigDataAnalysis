 == History == 
 
 === Name === 
 
 ==== Etymology ==== 
 The name Bricherhaff likely derives from the person who built and owned the area following it's construction. There are other places called Bricherhaff < ref > {{Cite web|title=Grand Public|url=https://map.geoportail.lu/theme/main?crosshair=true & zoom=15 & X=686210 & Y=6381694 & bgLayer=basemap_2015_global & version=3 & layers= & opacities= & lang=fr & rotation=0|access-date=2022-09-12|website=map.geoportail.lu}} < /ref >  and Bricher < ref > {{Cite web|title=BnL Viewer 2|url=https://viewer.eluxemburgensia.lu/ark:70795/7gj8zf/pages/3/articles/DTL55?search=Bricher|access-date=2022-09-12|website=viewer.eluxemburgensia.lu}} < /ref >  or Brucher < ref > {{Cite web|title=BnL Viewer 2|url=https://viewer.eluxemburgensia.lu/ark:70795/c54jdr/pages/3/articles/DTL58?search=Brucher|access-date=2022-09-12|website=viewer.eluxemburgensia.lu}} < /ref >  was a common surname at the time of the Lieu-dit's construction. Also  " Haff "  is Luxembourgish for Farm therefore the name likely meant  " Bricher's Farm " . 
 
 === Construction and early history === 
 
 Bricherhaff was constructed at some point in the late 18th or early 19th Century by the owners of the farm land surrounding it. It was built upon the Syre river and was a farmstead used to store farming equipment and house workers. The oldest mention of the name Bricherhaff of Brucherhof dates back to 1844 which a singular mention of the place. < ref > {{Cite web|title=BnL Viewer 2|url=https://viewer.eluxemburgensia.lu/ark:70795/td2hcx/pages/3/articles/DTL74?search=Brucherhof|access-date=2022-09-12|website=viewer.eluxemburgensia.lu}} < /ref >  The first proper mention of the Lieu dit of Bricherhaff is on a newspaper published in July 1890 where it is mentioned that Bricherhaff is located adjacent to [[Moutfort]] and [[Medingen]]. < ref > {{Cite web|title=BnL Viewer 2|url=https://viewer.eluxemburgensia.lu/ark:70795/c01tgw/pages/2/articles/DTL42?search=Brucherhof|access-date=2022-09-12|website=viewer.eluxemburgensia.lu}} < /ref >  The same infomation was mentioned once more in April 1891. < ref > {{Cite web|title=BnL Viewer 2|url=https://viewer.eluxemburgensia.lu/ark:70795/59271b/pages/2/articles/DTL69?search=Brucherhof%20Contern|access-date=2022-09-12|website=viewer.eluxemburgensia.lu}} < /ref >  An 1895 Gazetteer of places in Luxembourg contained Bricherhaff as well as Brichermillen.

 === Later mentions === 
 Bricherhaff stayed a small farmstead throughout the 20th century however was continually mentioned in newspapers and old documents. Bricherhaff was unaffected by [[World War I]] and In May 1923 it was a stopping point for the funeral tour of Mr Mathias Hemmen who died in Brichermillen. < ref > {{Cite web|title=BnL Viewer 2|url=https://viewer.eluxemburgensia.lu/ark:70795/8czqkg/pages/4/articles/DTL205?search=Brucherhof%20Contern|access-date=2022-09-22|website=viewer.eluxemburgensia.lu}} < /ref >  In February 1930 a Bricherhaff resident Bernard Lautwein was involved in illegal shooting and fined. < ref > {{Cite web|title=BnL Viewer 2|url=https://viewer.eluxemburgensia.lu/ark:70795/n3z12f/pages/1/articles/DTL42?search=Brucherhof%20Contern|access-date=2022-09-22|website=viewer.eluxemburgensia.lu}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite web|title=BnL Viewer 2|url=https://viewer.eluxemburgensia.lu/ark:70795/w8t5mr/pages/5/articles/DTL142?search=Brucherhof%20Contern|access-date=2022-09-22|website=viewer.eluxemburgensia.lu}} < /ref >  In May of that same year, there was another similar incident. < ref > {{Cite web|title=BnL Viewer 2|url=https://viewer.eluxemburgensia.lu/ark:70795/818wmf/pages/2/articles/DTL52?search=Brucherhof%20Contern|access-date=2022-09-22|website=viewer.eluxemburgensia.lu}} < /ref >  In Januaray 1931, Census data recorded the combined population of Bricherhaff and Brichermillen to be 30 and it was recorded and published by a local newspaper. < ref name= " :2 " > {{Cite web|title=BnL Viewer 2|url=https://viewer.eluxemburgensia.lu/ark:70795/21pzks/pages/3/articles/DTL80?search=Brucherhof%20Contern|access-date=2022-09-22|website=viewer.eluxemburgensia.lu}} < /ref > x
