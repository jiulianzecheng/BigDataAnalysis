 '''Snickers''' is a [[chocolate bar]] made by [[Mars, Incorporated]]. It is [[nougat]] topped with [[caramel]] and [[peanut]]s, coated in [[milk chocolate]]. It was introduced in 1930. It has had sales of over $2 Billion around the world. < ref > {{cite news|last=McCarthy |first=Michael |url=https://www.usatoday.com/money/advertising/adtrack/2005-01-30-track-snickers_x.htm |title=Women sweet on humorous Snickers ads|publisher=Usatoday.Com |date=2005-01-31}} < /ref >
