 '''Viedma''' ({{IPA-es|ˈbjeðma}}) is the capital and fourth largest city of the [[Río Negro Province]], in South-Central [[Argentina]]. In 2020, 80,632 people lived there. < ref > {{cite news|title=Viedma tiene más de 80 mil habitantes|url=https://www.loprincipal.com.ar/viedma-tiene-oficialmente-80-632/#:~:text=Viedma%20tiene%20oficialmente%2080.632%20habitantes,habitantes%20de%20la%20capital%20rionegrina.|access-date=11 June 2020|work=Lo Principal}} < /ref >  It is located on the southern margin of the [[Río Negro (Argentina)|Negro River]], about 30 kilometres off the [[Atlantic ocean|Atlantic Coast]].
