 '''Vallamand''' is a former [[municipality]] in the [[district]] of [[Broye-Vully (district)|Broye-Vully]] in the [[Vaud|canton of Vaud]], [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 On 1 July 2011 the former municipalities of [[Bellerive, Switzerland|Bellerive]], [[Chabrey]], [[Constantine, Switzerland|Constantine]], [[Montmagny, Switzerland|Montmagny]], [[Mur, Switzerland|Mur]], Vallamand and [[Villars-le-Grand]] merged into the new municipality of [[Vully-les-Lacs]]. < ref name=Fusion > [http://www.bfs.admin.ch/bfs/portal/de/index/infothek/nomenklaturen/blank/blank/gem_liste/03.html Amtliches Gemeindeverzeichnis der Schweiz] published by the Swiss Federal Statistical Office {{in lang|de}} accessed 17 February 2011 < /ref >
