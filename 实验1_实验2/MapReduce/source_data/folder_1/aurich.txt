 '''Aurich''' ({{audio|Aurich.ogg|German pronunciation}}; [[East Frisian Low Saxon]]: ''Auerk'', [[West Frisian language|West Frisian]]: ''Auwerk'', {{lang-stq|Aurk}}) is a town in [[Lower Saxony]], [[Germany]]. It is the [[Capital (political)|capital]] of the [[Aurich (district)|district of Aurich]] and is the second largest City in East Frisia, both in population, after [[Emden]], and in area, after [[Wittmund]].
