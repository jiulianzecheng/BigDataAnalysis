 '''''RoboCop''''' is a 1987 [[science fiction]] [[action movie|action]] < ref name=complex > {{cite web|url=http://www.complex.com/pop-culture/2016/11/the-50-best-action-movies-of-all-time/robocop|title=The 50 Best Action Movies of All Time|author= < !-- Staff -- > |work=[[Complex (magazine)|Complex]]|date=November 23, 2016|accessdate=May 6, 2017|archive-date=March 13, 2017|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170313061718/http://www.complex.com/pop-culture/2016/11/the-50-best-action-movies-of-all-time/robocop|url-status=dead}} < /ref >  [[crime movie]]. It is [[Movie director|directed]] by [[Paul Verhoeven]]. The movie stars [[Peter Weller]], [[Nancy Allen (actress)|Nancy Allen]], [[Dan O'Herlihy]], [[Kurtwood Smith]], [[Miguel Ferrer]], and [[Ronny Cox]]. It is set in a [[crime]]-ridden [[dystopia]]n [[Detroit]], [[Michigan]] in the near future where the [[police force]] is run by Security Concepts, a [[corporation]]. < ref > S. Perkowitz, ''Hollywood Science: Movies, Science, and the End of the World'' (New York: Columbia University Press, 2007), p. 150 < /ref >  ''RoboCop'' centers on police officer Alex Murphy (Weller) who is brutally [[attack]]ed and fatally wounded by a gang of [[criminal]]s. He is then made into a [[cyborg]] law enforcer known as  " RoboCop " . < ref > Elaine L. Graham, ''Representations of the Post/human: Monsters, Aliens, and Others in Popular Culture'' (New Brunswick, NJ: Rutgers University Press, 2002), p. 209 < /ref >  The movie had two [[sequel]]s, ''[[RoboCop 2]]'' (1990) and ''[[RoboCop 3]]'' (1993). A [[RoboCop (2014 movie)|remake]] of this movie was released in February 2014.
