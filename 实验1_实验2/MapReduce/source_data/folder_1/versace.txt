 '''Versace S.p.A''', usually called '''Versace''' is an [[Italy|Italian]] [[luxury]] [[fashion design|fashion house]]. < ref name=IBP71 > ''Singapore Clothing and Textile Industry Handbook'' (Washington DC: USA International Business Publications, 2009), p. 71 < /ref >  It was [[wikt:found|found]]ed by [[Gianni Versace]] in 1978. < ref name=gianniversacheBIO > {{cite web |url=http://www.biography.com/people/gianni-versace-9517836 |title=Gianni Versace Biography |website=Bio |publisher=A & E Television Networks, LLC |access-date=December 30, 2016}} < /ref >  One of the [[logo]]s of the brand represents the [[head]] of [[Medusa]]. < ref name=gianniversacheBIO/ > 
 
 Since the death of [[Gianni Versace]] in 1997, [[Donatella Versace]] is the [[artistic director|head of designs]]. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://www.biography.com/people/donatella-versace-21330707 |title=Donatella Versace Biography |website=Bio |publisher=A & E Television Networks, LLC |access-date=December 30, 2016}} < /ref >  Her daughter [[Allegra Versace]] owns half of the company. < ref > {{cite web |url=https://www.nytimes.com/2004/06/15/nyregion/a-new-half-owner-for-versace-and-she-s-almost-18.html?_r=0 |title=A New Half-Owner for Versace, and She's Almost 18 |author=Cathy Horyn |date=June 15, 2004 |publisher=The New York Times |access-date=December 30, 2016}} < /ref >
