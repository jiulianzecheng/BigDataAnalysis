 '''Kropyvnytskyi''' ({{lang-uk|Кропивни́цький|Kropyvnyc'kyj}}) is a [[city]] in central [[Ukraine]]. It is the [[administrative centre]] of the [[Kirovohrad Oblast]]. In 2020, 225,339 people lived there.
