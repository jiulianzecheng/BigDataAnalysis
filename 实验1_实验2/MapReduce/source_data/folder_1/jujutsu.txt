 == History == 
 The systems of unarmed combat which were developed and practiced during the [[Muromachi period]] known as {{Nihongo|Japanese old-style ''jujutsu''|日本古流柔術|Nihon koryū jūjutsu}}.  For example, a [[samurai]] might defend himself with ''jujutsu'' if he was without his swords.
