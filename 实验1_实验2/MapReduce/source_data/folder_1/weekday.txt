A '''weekday''' is a day of the week that is not a part of the [[weekend]]. < ref > {{cite web|title=Weekday - Merriam-Webster Dictionary|url=http://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/weekday|accessdate=2009-07-03}} < /ref >  The weekdays are normally [[Monday]], [[Tuesday]], [[Wednesday]], [[Thursday]] and [[Friday]]. [[Saturday]] or [[Sunday]] are not weekdays. These two days are the days of the weekend.
