 '''Chandra''' is a [[Hindu mythology|Hindu]] moon god. He is described as being young, beautiful and fair. In his hands he carries a club and a [[nelumbo|lotus]]. He rides his [[chariot]] across the sky every night, pulled by ten white horses or an [[antelope]].
