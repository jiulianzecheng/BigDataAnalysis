 The '''burbot''' (''Lota lota'') is the only [[gadiformes|gadiform]] (cod-like) [[freshwater fish]]. It is also known as '''bubbot''', < ref > {{Cite web |url=http://www.ukdivers.net/life/fishcards.htm |title=UKDivers.net - Fresh Warter Fish Cards |access-date=20 September 2012 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20121102080110/http://www.ukdivers.net/life/fishcards.htm |archive-date=2 November 2012 |url-status=dead }} < /ref >  '''mariah''', '''freshwater cod''', '''freshwater ling''', '''freshwater cusk''', '''the lawyer''', '''coney-fish''', '''lingcod''', < ref name= " SOED3 " > {{cite book|last1=Onions|first1=C. T.|title=The Shorter Oxford English Dictionary|date=1973|page=420|edition=3}} < /ref >  and '''eelpout'''. 
 
 The burbot can live under ice, and it needs frigid temperatures to breed.  " These were ice-age fish that once swam unhindered in a glacial world " . < ref > {{cite news|title=The decline of the 'disgusting' burbot|url=https://www.bbc.com/news/magazine-33410217|publisher=British Broadcasting Corporation|access-date=7 July 2015|work=BBC News|date=2015-07-07}} < /ref > 
 
 == Description == 
 The burbot looks like a cross between a [[bowfin]] and [[eel]]. 
 
 == Ecology == 
 
 === Habitat === 
 These fishes live in large, cold rivers, lakes, and reservoirs. They mostly prefer freshwater habitats.
