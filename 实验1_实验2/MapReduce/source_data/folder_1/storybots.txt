 '''''StoryBots''''' is an American children's educational [[media franchise]]. It is best known for the [[Netflix]] series ''[[Ask the StoryBots]]''. The ''StoryBots'' library includes educational TV shows, books, videos, and music designed to make foundational learning fun and encourage intellectual curiosity in children ages 3-to-8. < ref name= " Marketwired " > {{cite web|url=http://www.marketwired.com/press-release/storybots-launches-groundbreaking-product-kindergarten-classrooms-its-free-teachers-2094930.htm|title=StoryBots Launches Groundbreaking Product for Kindergarten Classrooms & nbsp;– and It's Free for Teachers|date=9 February 2016|publisher=Marketwired.com}} < /ref >
