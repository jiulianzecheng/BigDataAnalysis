'''Nereus''' (Νηρεύς) is a sea god in [[Greek mythology]]. His parents are [[Gaia (mythology)|Gaia]] and [[Pontos]]. With the [[Okeanid]] [[Doris]] his fifty daughters are the [[Nereid]]s, sea [[nymph]]s of the [[Mediterranean Sea]].
