 '''Vavuniya''' ({{Lang-ta|வவுனியா}}, {{lang-si|වවුනියා}}) is a large town in the [[Northern Province, Sri Lanka|Northern Province]] of [[Sri Lanka]]. It is governed by an [[Urban Council]]. It is also the main town of [[Vavuniya District]].
