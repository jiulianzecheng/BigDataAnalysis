 A '''playground''' < ref > {{Cite web|title=Definition of playground {{!}} Dictionary.com|url=https://www.dictionary.com/browse/playground|access-date=2020-09-22|website=www.dictionary.com|language=en}} < /ref >  is an area used by [[child]]ren to [[Play (activity)|play]] in. They usually have equipment such as [[Playground slide|slides]], [[Playground swing|swings]] and [[seesaw]]s for children to play on. They are usually outside, but sometimes they are in a building. < ref > {{Cite web|title=School Playground Canopies|url=https://www.canopies-shelters.co.uk/schools/playground-canopy|access-date=2020-09-22|website=www.canopies-shelters.co.uk}} < /ref >  Having a playground can enhance your macadam surface and improve your kid’s time in the playground. 
 
 The equipment may be made of [[metal]], [[wood]] or [[plastic]]. The ground beneath the equipment will not be hard in case a child falls. It may be a rubber floor, or there may be woodchips or [[sand]].  
 
 Playgrounds give children a place for [[physical exercise]].  This helps them get and keep [[physical fitness]] and be strong and healthy.  Daily exercise reduces the risks of [[obesity]].
