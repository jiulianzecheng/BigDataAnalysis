 '''Upleward''' is a village in the municipality of [[Krummhörn]] in [[Aurich (district)|Aurich]] in [[East Frisia]] in north [[Germany]]. It is about ten kilometres northwest of [[Emden]], on the [[North Sea]]. In 2012, 395 people lived there.
