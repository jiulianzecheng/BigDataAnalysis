
 '''Savassi''' is a [[neighborhood]] in [[Belo Horizonte]], [[Brazil]]. It is in the [[wiktionary:Central|central]]-[[south]] zone of the [[city]]. It has many [[Bar (business)|bars]], [[nightclub]]s, and [[restaurant]]s. < ref > Alex Robinson; Gardenia Robinson. ''Brazil Handbook'' (Bath: Footprint; Guilford, CT: Globe Pequot Press, 2011), p. 248 < /ref >  It has a busy and well developed [[shopping]] district and several [[shopping mall]]s. < ref > Regis St Louis; et al, ''Brazil'' (Footscray, VIC; Oakland, CA: Lonely Planet, 2010), p. 240 < /ref >  Savassi is one of the most prestigious areas in Belo Horizonte. It used to be a region inside the traditional and wealthy [[Funcionários]] neighborhood. In 2006, it was changed by the Town House before the meeting of the [[Inter-American Development Bank]], which happened the same year.
