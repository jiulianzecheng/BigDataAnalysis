 '''Frightwig''' is an [[United States|American]] [[punk rock]] band. It was founded in 1982. All of the members are women. They are [[feminist|feminists]]. Deanna Ashley and Mia Levin started the band in [[San Francisco]]. In 1984, they released their first album ''[[Cat Farm Faboo]]''. In 1986, their second album was released. In 1993 they released an extended play called ''[[Phone Sexy]]''. In 1994 the band broke up.
