 '''Altensteig''' is a town in the [[Calw (district)|district of Calw]] of [[Baden-Württemberg]], [[Germany]]. It is in the eastern [[Black Forest]], 18 & nbsp;km southwest of [[Calw]], and 19 & nbsp;km northeast of [[Freudenstadt]].
