 A '''latch''' is a type of [[Household hardware|hardware]] that is used to join two [[wikt:object|object]]s together. A latch often keeps [[animal]]s or [[wind]] from opening something. This could be a [[door]] or a [[gate]]. Unlike a [[lock]], a latch is easy to open if you know how, so it can be convenient on a gate that needs to be opened often. 
 
 * a '''childproof latch''' (also called a baby latch) is [[design]]ed to keep children from opening doors or drawers. They keep children from getting into things such as household cleaners, medicines, matches or knives. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://www.cpsc.gov/en/Safety-Education/Safety-Guides/Kids-and-Babies/Childproofing-Your-Home--12-Safety-Devices-To-Protect-Your-Children/ |title=Childproofing Your Home |author= |date= |website= |publisher=Consumer Product Safety Commission |accessdate=7 June 2015 |archive-date=27 July 2015 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150727151625/http://www.cpsc.gov/en/Safety-Education/Safety-Guides/Kids-and-Babies/Childproofing-Your-Home--12-Safety-Devices-To-Protect-Your-Children/ |url-status=dead }} < /ref > 
 * a '''gate latch''' is used for outdoor gates. There are a wide variety of types. There are also a number of considerations before choosing a gate latch. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://www.360yardware.com/gate_latches.htm |title=All About Gate Latches |author= |date= |website= |publisher=360Yardware |accessdate=7 June 2015}} < /ref >
