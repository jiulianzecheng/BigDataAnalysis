 '''Marchaux''' is a former [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is in [[Bourgogne-Franche-Comté]] in the [[Doubs]] [[Departments of France|department]] in east [[France]]. On 1 January 2018, it was merged into the new commune of [[Marchaux-Chaudefontaine]]. < ref > [https://www.legifrance.gouv.fr/eli/arrete/2017/12/4/INTB1734195A/jo/texte Arrêté préfectoral] 4 December 2017 {{in lang|fr}} < /ref >
