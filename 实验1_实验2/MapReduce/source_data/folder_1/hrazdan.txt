 '''Hrazdan''' is the capital of the [[Kotayk Province]] of [[Armenia]]. The name Hrazdan is from the [[Middle-Persian]] name ''Frazdan''. ''Farzdan'' is connected to the [[Zoroastrian]] [[Persian mythology|mythology]]. With 52,900 people it is the fifth-largest city in Armenia. The town was formed under Soviet rule.  During the [[Soviet]] years it was one of the highly-industrialized towns of the [[Armenian SSR]]. 
 
 The [[Hrazdan River]] is by the town.
