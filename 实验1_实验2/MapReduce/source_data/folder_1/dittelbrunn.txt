 '''Dittelbrunn''' is a [[Municipalities of Germany|municipality]] in [[Schweinfurt (district)|Schweinfurt]] in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. 
 
 Dittelbrunn has 4 parts: Dittelbrunn, {{Interlanguage link multi|Hambach (Dittelbrunn)|de|3=Hambach (Dittelbrunn)|lt=Hambach}}, Holzhausen and Pfändhausen.
