 The '''heterokonts''' or '''stramenopiles''' are a major line of [[eukaryote]]s with more than 100,000 known species, < ref name= " urlstramenopiles " > {{cite web |url=https://www.uniprot.org/taxonomy/33634 |title=stramenopiles |format= |accessdate=2009-03-08}} < /ref >  most of them [[diatom]]s. < ref name= " Hoek " > {{cite book | last=Hoek | first=C. van den |author2= D.G. Mann and H.M. Jahns | year=1995 | pages=[https://archive.org/details/algaeintroductio0000hoek/page/104 104], 124, 134, 166 
 | title=Algae: An Introduction to Phycology | url=https://archive.org/details/algaeintroductio0000hoek | location=Cambridge | publisher=Cambridge University Press | isbn=0-521-31687-1 }} < /ref > 
 
 '''Heterokont'''s are mostly [[algae]]. In one stage of their [[life cycle]] they have two unequal [[flagella]]. They include both single-celled types and [[brown algae]] ([[seaweed]]s such as [[kelp]] and ''[[Sargassum]]''). They are members of the [[Kingdom (biology)|Kingdom]] [[Chromalveolata]].
