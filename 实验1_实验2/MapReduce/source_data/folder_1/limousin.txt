 '''Limousin''' is a former administrative region of [[France]]. It is now part of the administrative region of [[Nouvelle-Aquitaine]]. Its [[capital city|capital]] was [[Limoges]]. With 42 people per square kilometer, this was one of the least populated regions. About 710,000 people lived on roughly {{convert|17000|km2}}. 
 
 The departments in the region were [[Corrèze]], [[Creuse]], and [[Haute-Vienne]]. 
 
 The local economy had been recentered around [[service]]s.
