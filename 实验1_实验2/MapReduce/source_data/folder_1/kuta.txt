'''Kuta''' is a town in southern [[Bali]], [[Indonesia]]. Once it was a fishing village. It was one of the first towns on Bali to become popular with [[tourist]]s. It is one of Indonesia's major tourist destinations. It has a long sandy beach, and many restaurants and bars. It is near Bali's [[Ngurah Rai Airport]]. 
 
 On October 12, 2002, a [[2002 Bali terrorist bombing|series of bombings]] killed more than 200 people in Kuta. The bombings happened outside two nightclubs.
