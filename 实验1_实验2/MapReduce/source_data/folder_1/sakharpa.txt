'''Sakharpa''' is a small [[village]] in the [[Ratnagiri]] [[district]] of the [[state]] of [[Maharashtra]], [[India]]. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://www.onefivenine.com/india/villages/Ratnagiri/Sangmeshwar/Sakharpa |title=Sakharpa |author= |date= |website= |publisher=onefivenine.com |accessdate=21 July 2015}} < /ref >  It is on the [[Wikt:bank|bank]] of a [[river]] named  " Kajlali " . Sakharpa is about {{convert|16|km}} from [[Devrukh]] which is its [[taluka]] [[headquarters]]. The national [[highway]] NH 166 runs through this village. [[Sahyadri College Of Arts  &  Commerce]] is located here. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://soki.in/sakharpa-sangmeshwar-ratnagiri/ |title=Sakharpa- Sangmeshwar |author= |date= |website= |publisher=Soki.In|accessdate=21 July 2015}} < /ref >
