 '''Viljandi''' ({{lang-de|Fellin}}, {{lang-pl|Felin}}) is a town and municipality in southern [[Estonia]]. As of 2012, 19,150 people lived there. It is the [[Capital (city)|capital]] of [[Viljandi County]]. The town was first mentioned in [[1283]], after being given its town charter by [[Villekinus de Endorpe]].
