 A '''pantograph''' is a [[machine]]. It is  used in [[drawing]] or [[plan]]ning. It makes it easy to copy diagrams.  
 
 The device that an electric [[Railway|train]] uses to get its [[electricity]] is also called a pantograph, because it looks a bit like the drawing instrument.
