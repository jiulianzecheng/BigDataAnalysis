 A '''hole''' is an empty space in a solid object, which can be flat. 
 
 It can also mean other things: 
 
 *In [[golf]], it is a special place on a golf course. All players want to put their ball in the holes, in as few strikes as possible. 
 *''[[Holes]]'', a book written by Louis Sachar 
 *[[Hole (band)]] was an American rock band. Their singer was [[Courtney Love]]. 
 *[[Hole, Norway]] a municipality in [[Buskerud]] county.
