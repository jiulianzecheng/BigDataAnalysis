'''Metrorrey''' (''Sistema de Transporte Colectivo Metrorrey'') is a [[light rail]]/[[rapid transit]] system which operates in the city of [[Monterrey]], [[Nuevo León]] ([[Mexico]]), has been operating since [[1991]]. < ref > {{Cite news|last=Tanajara|first=Jesus|last2=Flota|date=7 November 2016|title=STC Metrorrey - Dirección de Mantenimiento de vías|language=Es|work=Seminario de Infraestructura Ferroviaria Mexicana|url=https://www.cmic.org.mx/cmic/eventos/infraestructuraferroviaria/ponencias/Dia3/6-Jesus_Tanajara_Flota.pdf|access-date=16 March 2022}} < /ref > 
 
 [[File:0019_arriving_at_Talleres_Station.jpg|thumb|240x240px|0019 arriving at Talleres Station.]] 
 
 It currently operates 50 high-floor trains and a total of 40 stations, spread over 40 (25 [[Mile|mi]]) < ref name= " Metrorrey-history " > {{cite web|title=Sistema de Transporte Colectivo Metrorrey - Historia|trans-title=System of Collective Transport Metrorrey - History|url=http://www.nl.gob.mx/?P=metrorrey_principal|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20131024140252/http://www.nl.gob.mx/?P=metrorrey_principal|archive-date=2013-10-24|access-date=2022-03-16|publisher=Gobierno del Estado de Nuevo León|language=es}} < /ref >  [[Kilometre|kilometers]] in length and 3 lines. < ref name= " hoytam " > {{cite web|date=28 February 2021|title=Inauguran Lína 3 del Metro en Monterrey; durí ocho años su conclusión|url=https://www.hoytamaulipas.net/notas/448173/Inauguran-Linea-3-del-Metro-en-Monterrey-duro-ocho-anios-su-conclusion.html|access-date=16 March 2022|work=Hoy Tamaulipas|language=es}} < /ref >  Metrorrey including a Metrobus (division of Bus Transportation). 
 
 == Fares == 
 The following are the travel fares {{as of|2018|lc=on}}: 
 
 * Single Trip - MXN4.50 (~USD0.22) 
 * 2 Trips - MXN8.00 (~USD0.39) 
 * Metrobus - From bus to Metro: Cash: MXN12.00 (~USD0.64), with  " Feria "  card: MXN11.79 (~USD0.62), From Metro to bus: MXN8.00 (~ USD0.42)

 == Light and Metro fleet == 
 
 * 25 Concarril MM-90A trains. Originally Built for Line 1. (#01 - #25) 
 * 23 Bombardier MM-90B trains. Originally Built for Line 1. (#26 - #48) 
 * 22 CAF MM-93 trains. Originally Built for Line 2. (#49 - #70) 
 * 14 Bombardier MM-05 trains (basically modernized MM-90 trains) Originally Built for Line 2 expansion. (#71 - #84) 
 * 26 CRRC MM-20 trains. Originally Built for Line 3. (#109 - #134) 
 * 24 Talbot MM-80 trains (former [[Frankfurt U-Bahn]] Type U3 trains, refurbished between 2019 and 2020, and recalled MM-80) Built to reinforce L1 and L2 service. (#85 - #108)
