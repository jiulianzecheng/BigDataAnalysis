 The '''Liri''' is one of the main [[river]]s in [[central Italy]] that flows through the [[Abruzzo]] and [[Lazio]] [[Regions of Italy|regions]]. It flows into the [[Tyrrhenian Sea]] under the name '''Garigliano'''. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 The Liri river has a length of {{Convert|120|km|mi|0|abbr=on}} ({{Convert|158|km|mi|0|abbr=on}} as Garigliano), and a [[drainage basin]] with an area of approximately {{Convert|4140|km2|sqmi|0|abbr=on }}. The Liri-Garigliano system has a total drainage basin of {{convert|5020|km2|mi2}}. < ref name= " fiumi " > {{cite web |url=http://www.fiumi.com/acque/index.php?pagina=21 & id_g=152 & id_t=71 |title=Il Liri |publisher=Fiumi Italiani |language=it |access-date=12 September 2017 }} < /ref > 
 
 ===Course=== 
 The Liri river starts on the sides of the ''Monte Camiciola'', part of the ''Monti Simbruini'' of central [[Apennines]], at an [[elevation]] of about {{Convert|1108|m|ft|0|abbr=on}}, in the [[Municipality|''comune'']] of [[Cappadocia, Abruzzo|Cappadocia]], [[Province of L'Aquila|L'Aquila]] province, [[Abruzzo]] [[Regions of Italy|region]]. < ref name= " trecani " > {{cite web |url=http://www.treccani.it/enciclopedia/garigliano/ |title=Garigliano |publisher=Treccani.it - Enciclopedie on line |language=it |access-date=12 September 2017 }} < /ref > 
 
 It flows at first to the southeast through a long [[valley]], parallel to the general direction of the Apennines, until it gets to the city of [[Sora, Lazio|Sora]]. Before [[Isola del Liri]]  " Liri Island " ), the Liri is joined by the [[Fibreno]] river and then it divides into two branches which then rejoin, surrounding the lower part of the town. One branch makes a {{convert|28|m|ft|adj=on}} high waterfall. 
 
 A [[dam]] is built on the river after the confluence with the [[Sacco (river)|Sacco]] river, its main [[tributary]], at [[Ceprano]]. The last important tributary of the Liri is the ''Melfa'', with which it joins near [[Aquino, Italy|Aquino]]. The river forms the border between [[Lazio]] and [[Campania]] regions and, after [[Cassino]], it joins the ''Gari'' river and then it is known as ''Garigliano''. After {{Convert|38|km|mi|abbr=on }}, the river flows into the [[Tyrrhenian Sea]]. 
 
 The Liri river flows through 2 regions and 2 provinces. It flows through the following ''[[Municipality|comuni]]'', among others: 
 * [[Abruzzo]] region 
 ** [[Province of L'Aquila|L'Aquila]]: [[Cappadocia, Abruzzo|Cappadocia]], [[Castellafiume]], [[Capistrello]], [[Civitella Roveto]], [[Canistro]], [[Civita d'Antino]], [[Morino]], [[San Vincenzo Valle Roveto]], [[Balsorano]] 
 * [[Lazio]] region 
 ** [[Province of Frosinone|Frosinone]]: [[Sora, Lazio|Sora]], [[Isola del Liri]], [[Monte San Giovanni Campano]], [[Fontana Liri]], [[Arce, Lazio|Arce]], [[Strangolagalli]], [[Ceprano]], [[San Giovanni Incarico]], [[Aquino, Italy|Aquino]], [[Pontecorvo]], [[San Giorgio a Liri]], [[Sant'Apollinare]]
