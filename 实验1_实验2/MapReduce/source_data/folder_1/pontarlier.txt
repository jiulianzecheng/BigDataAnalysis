 '''Pontarlier''' is a [[France|French]] [[Communes of France|commune]] in the [[Doubs]] [[Departments of France|department]], [[Bourgogne-Franche-Comté]] [[Regions of France|region]], in eastern [[France]] near the border with [[Switzerland]]. It is a [[Prefectures in France|subprefecture]] in the department and is also the [[Capital city|capital]] of the ''arrrondissement'' of [[Arrondissement of Pontarlier|Pontarlier]]. 
 
 Pontarlier is the third ''commune'' of the department by its population, after [[Besançon]] and [[Montbéliard]]. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 The city of Pontarlier is in the west of the Bourgogne-Franche-Comté region, in the southwestern part of the Doubs department. 
 
 The ''commune'' of Pontarlier has an area of {{Convert|41.4|km2|sqmi|abbr=on }}. < ref name= " insee-geo " > {{cite web |url=https://www.insee.fr/fr/statistiques/1405599?geo=COM-25462 |title=Commune de Pontarlier (25462) - Comparateur de territoire |work=Publications et statistiques pour la France ou les régions |publisher=Institut national de la statistique et des études économiques - INSEE |language=fr |access-date=30 June 2017 }} < /ref >  The city is on a [[plateau]] and its average [[altitude]] is {{Convert|1066|m|ft|abbr=on}}; at the [[City and town halls|city hall]], the altitude is {{Convert|840|m|ft|abbr=on }}. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://www.map-france.com/Pontarlier-25300/ |title=The city of Pontarlier |publisher=Map-France.com |access-date=30 June 2017 }} < /ref > 
 
 The [[Doubs (river)|Doubs]] [[river]] flows through the city.

 ===Climate=== 
 The [[Köppen climate classification]] type for Pontarlier is an  " [[Oceanic climate]] "  (also known as Maritime Temperate climate) and of the subtype '''Cfb'''. < ref name= " mairie "  / >

 == Administration == 
 Pontarlier is a [[Prefectures in France|subprefecture]] of the Doubs department, the capital of the ''arrondissement'' of [[Arrondissement of Pontarlier|Pontarlier]] and the [[administrative centre]] ({{lang-fr|chef-lieu}}) of the canton [[Canton of Pontarlier|Pontarlier]] formed by 10 ''communes'', including Pontarlier, with 26,699 inhabitants  < small > (2014) < /small > . 
 
 It is part of the [[Communes of France|intercommunality]] ''Le Grand Pontarlier'' ({{lang-fr|Communauté de communes du Grand Pontarlier}}). 
 
 === Twinned and partner towns === 
 Pontarlier is [[Twin town|twinned]] with: 
 
 * {{flagicon|GER}} [[Villingen-Schwenningen]], [[Germany]], since 1964; 
 * {{flagicon|ESP}} [[Zarautz]], [[Spain]], since 1994; 
 * {{flagicon|SUI}} [[Yverdon-les-Bains]], [[Switzerland]], since 2003. 
 
 ==Places of interest== 
 Some interesting places in Montbéliard are: 
 * The ''Château de Sandon''. 
 * ''Porte Saint-Pierre'' and Triumphal Arch (18th century). 
 * The ''Saint-Bénigne de Pontarlier'' [[church]]. 
 * The ''Fort de Joux'' [[castle]].
