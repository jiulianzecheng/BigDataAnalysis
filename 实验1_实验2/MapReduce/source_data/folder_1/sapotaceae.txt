 '''Sapotaceae''' is a family of [[flowering plant]]s, belonging to order [[Ericales]]. The family includes about 800 species of evergreen trees and shrubs in approximately 65 genera (35-75, depending on generic definition). Distribution is pantropical.The best known member of this family is the shea tree,Vitellaria Paradoxa.
