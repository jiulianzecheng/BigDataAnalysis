 '''Tejanos''' ({{IPA-es|teˈhano|lang}}; singular: ''Tejano/a''; [[Spanish language|Spanish]] for  " Texan " ) are the [[Hispanic]] residents of the [[U.S. state]] of [[Texas]]. 
 
 Many Tejanos are related to [[Spanish language|Spanish]]-speaking settlers of [[Mexican Texas|Tejas]], [[Coahuila y Tejas|Coahuila]], and other northern [[Mexican states]].
