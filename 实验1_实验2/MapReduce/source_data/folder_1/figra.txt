'''FIGRA''' ([[International]] [[festival]] of major current affairs reports and social [[Documentary|documentaries]]) is an annual festival of documentary films and the corresponding festival award. It has been held since [[1993]] in March-April in Le Touquet-Paris-Plage in northern [[France]]. < ref > {{Cite web |language=fr |title= 
 " Mettre dans la lumière des gens de l'ombre " |url=https://www.lemonde.fr/culture/article/2013/03/19/georges-marque-bouaret-mettre-dans-la-lumiere-des-gens-de-l-ombre_1849165_3246.html|website=[[Le Monde]]}} < /ref > 
 
 ==History== 
 The film festival was founded by Georges Marque-Bouaret in 1993. The festival's main theme revolves around pressing social issues uncovered by the reporters themselves, who also serve as directors. In this way, according to the founder, they have the opportunity to  " step out of the shadows "  of the press organizations they work for. < ref > {{Cite web |language=fr |title=Le Figra, ce festival dédié au reportage pour  " rendre le public plus critique " |url=https://www.france24.com/fr/20140325-interview-georges-marque-bouaret-fondateur-figra-reportage-documentaire|website=France 24}} < /ref > 
 
 In 2016, the festival takes place from March 30 to April 3. 
 
 ==Main Prize== 
 *Grand Prix FIGRA for a film with a duration of 40 minutes or more 
 *Grand Prix FIGRA for a film under 40 minutes
