 '''Garlasco''' is a ''[[comune]]'' in the [[Province of Pavia]] in the [[Lombardy]] region in [[Italy]]. It is about {{convert|35|km|mi}} southwest of [[Milan]] and about {{convert|20|km|mi|0}} west of [[Pavia]]. As of 31 December 2004, 9,343 people lived there. It has an area of {{convert|39.0|km2|mi2}}. < ref name= " istat " > All demographics and other statistics: Italian statistical institute [[National Institute of Statistics (Italy)|Istat]]. < /ref > 
 
 The municipality has only ''[[frazione]]'' Bozzola.
