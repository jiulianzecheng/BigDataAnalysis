 '''Confolens''' ({{Lang-oc|Confolents}} < ref > {{cite web |url=http://ujan.free.fr/charanto.htm |title=CHARANTA OCCITANA : los noms de las comunas |work=Confolentés occitan |language=oc |access-date=15 July 2016 }} < /ref > ) is a [[Communes of France|commune]] in southwestern France. Confolens is the administrative centre of a largely [[rural area]], which has had an important [[growth]] of [[tourism]] in recent years. 
 
 It is one of the two [[Prefectures in France|subprefectures]] of the [[Charente]] [[Departments of France|department]] and the [[Capital city|capital]] of the  [[Cantons in France|canton]] of  [[Canton de Charente-Vienne|Charente-Vienne]]. 
 
 ==History== 
 On 1 January 2016, the former ''commune'' [[Saint-Germain-de-Confolens]] was [[:wikt:merge|merged]] with the ''commune'' of Confolens. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://www.charente.gouv.fr/content/download/16475/113685/file/Sommaire%20du%20RAA%20du%2019%2010.pdf |title= Recueil des actes administratifs | author=Préfecture de la Charente |language=fr |access-date=16 July 2016 |date= 19 October 2015 }}. < /ref > 
 
 ==Geography== 
 Confolens is at the confluence of the [[Vienne (river)|Vienne]] and Goire rivers. The name of this place comes from the [[Latin Language|Latin]] word ''confluens'' that means  " confluence " . < ref > {{cite book |last=Nègre |first=Ernest |editor=Librairie Droz |year=1990 |title=Toponymie générale de la France |language= fr | volume=1 |url=https://books.google.com/books?id=rsNpi7IVulEC |page=298 |access-date= 15 July 2016 |isbn= 2-60002-883-8 }} < /ref > 
 
 The city is at {{Convert|65|km|mi|abbr=on }} from [[Angoulême]], {{Convert|55|km|mi|abbr=on }} from [[Limoges]] and {{Convert|70|km|mi|abbr=on }} from [[Poitiers]], the three largest cities that are closest to Confolens. 
 
 It has an area of {{Convert|23.63|km2|sqmi|abbr=on }} and its average [[altitude]] is {{Convert|152|m|ft|abbr=on}}; at the [[City and town halls|city hall]], the altitude is {{Convert|150|m|ft|abbr=on }}. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://www.map-france.com/Confolens-16500/ |title=Confolens |publisher=Map-France.com |access-date=15 July 2016 }} < /ref >

 ===Climate=== 
 The climate of Confolens, in the [[Köppen climate classification]], is '''Cfb''' - [[Oceanic climate]] with template summers. < ref > {{cite web |work=Annuaire-Mairie.fr |title=La ville de Confolens |url=http://www.annuaire-mairie.fr/ville-confolens.html |language=fr |access-date=15 July 2016}} < /ref > 
 
 ==Population== 
 With a population of 2,721, < ref name=insee2 > {{cite web |url=http://www.insee.fr/fr/ppp/bases-de-donnees/recensement/populations-legales/departement.asp?dep=16 & annee=2013 |title=Populations lègales 2013 - 16-Charente |work= Populations légales 2013 des départements et des collectivités d'outre-mer |publisher=Institut national de la statistique et des études économiques - INSEE |language=fr |access-date=15 July 2016 }} < /ref >  Confolens has a [[population density]] of {{Pop density|2721|23.63|km2|prec=0|disp=num}} inhabitants/km < sup > 2 < /sup > . 
 
 The inhabitants of Confolens are known, in [[French language|French]], as ''Confolentais'' (women: ''Confolentaises''). < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.habitants.fr/charente-16 |title=Charente (16)|publisher=habitants.fr |language=fr |access-date=15 July 2016 }} < /ref > 
 
 ==Administration== 
 Confolens is a subprefecture of the Charente department since 1800. It is also the capital of the canton of [[Canton of Charente-Vienne|Charente-Vienne]]. < ref name=insee1 > {{cite web|url=http://www.insee.fr/fr/methodes/nomenclatures/cog/cog.asp?nivgeo=arr & codgeo=163 |title=Liste des cantons de l'arrondissement de Confolens |publisher=Institut national de la statistique et des études économiques - INSEE |language=fr |access-date=15 July 2016 }} < /ref >
