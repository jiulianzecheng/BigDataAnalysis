{{nihongo|'''Kangi'''|寛喜|}}, also romanized as '''Kanki,''' was a {{nihongo|[[Japanese era name]]|年号,|''nengō'',|lit.  " year name " }} after ''[[Antei]]'' and before ''[[Jōei]].''  This period started in March 1229 and ended in April 1232. < ref > [[Louis-Frédéric|Nussbaum, Louis-Frédéric]]. (2005). [https://books.google.com/books?id=p2QnPijAEmEC & pg=PA473   " ''Kanki'' " ] in ''Japan encyclopedia'', p. 473. < /ref >  The reigning emperor was {{nihongo|[[Emperor Go-Horikawa|Go-Horikawa]]''-tennō''|後堀河天皇}}. < ref > [[Isaac Titsingh|Titsingh, Isaac]]. (1834). [https://books.google.com/books?id=18oNAAAAIAAJ & pg=PA238    ''Annales des empereurs du Japon'', pp. 238-241]; [[H. Paul Varley|Varley, H. Paul]]. (1980). ''Jinnō Shōtōki.'' pp. 226-227. < /ref > 
 
 ==Events of the ''Kangi'' era== 
 * '''1230''' (''Kangi 2, 12th month''): [[Kujō Yoritsune]] was married to the daughter of [[Minamoto no Yoriie]]. < ref name= " titsingh241 " > Titsingh, [https://books.google.com/books?id=18oNAAAAIAAJ & pg=PA241   p. 241.] < /ref > 
 * '''1231''' (''Kangi 3, 2nd month''): Yoritsune was raised to the 2nd rank of the 4th class in the ''dōjō [[kuge]]''. < ref name= " titsingh241 " / > 
 * '''1231''' (''Kangi 3, 3rd month''): Yoritsune was made a General of the Left. < ref name= " titsingh241 " / > 
 * '''1231''' (''Kangi 3, 4th month''): Yoritsune was raised to the 1st rank of the 4th class in the ''dōjō kuge''. < ref name= " titsingh241 " / > 
 * '''6 November 1231''' (''Kangi 3, 11th day of the 10th month''): Former [[Emperor Tsuchimikado]] died. < ref > Titsingh, [https://books.google.com/books?id=18oNAAAAIAAJ & pg=PA241  p. 241]; [[Imperial Household Agency]] (''Kunaichō''), [http://www.kunaicho.go.jp/ryobo/guide/083/index.html 土御門天皇 (83)]; retrieved 2012-5-16. < /ref >
