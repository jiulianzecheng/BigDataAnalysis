 '''Payari''' is a village in [[Bangladesh]]. It is in the '''Magura''' district of the [[Khulna Division]]. It is almost {{convert|14|km|abbr=off}} away from '''Magura Sadar Upazila'''. The Nabaganga river flows nearby the village.
