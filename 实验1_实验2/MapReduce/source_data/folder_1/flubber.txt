 '''''Flubber''''' is a 1997 American [[science fiction comedy|science-fiction comedy]] film directed by [[Les Mayfield]] (who had previously directed another [[John Hughes (filmmaker)|John Hughes]] scripted remake, ''[[Miracle on 34th Street (1994 film)|Miracle on 34th Street]]'') and written by Hughes, based on an earlier screenplay by [[Bill Walsh (producer)|Bill Walsh]]. A [[remake]] of ''[[The Absent-Minded Professor]]'' (1961), the film was produced by [[Walt Disney Pictures]] and stars [[Robin Williams]], [[Marcia Gay Harden]], [[Christopher McDonald]], [[Ted Levine]], [[Raymond J. Barry]], [[Wil Wheaton]], and [[Clancy Brown]] with [[Jodi Benson]] providing a voice. The film grossed $178 million worldwide despite negative reviews. In selected theaters, the ''[[Pepper Ann]]'' episode  " Old Best Friend "  was featured before the film.
