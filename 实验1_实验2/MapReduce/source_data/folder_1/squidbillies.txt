 '''Squidbillies''' is a [[cartoon series]] on the [[Adult Swim]] block of the [[Cartoon Network]]. It is about a [[Poverty|poor]] family of [[squid]]s living in the [[Appalachian Mountains|Appalachian region]]. It was first shown on October 16, 2005. The show was created because of the popularity of the [[Aqua Teen Hunger Force]]. So far 50 episodes have been made.
