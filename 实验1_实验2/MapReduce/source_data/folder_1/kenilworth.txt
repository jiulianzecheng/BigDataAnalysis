 '''Kenilworth''' is a town and [[civil parish]] in [[Warwick District]], [[Warwickshire]], England. In 2011 there were 22,413 people living in Kenilworth. < ref > {{Cite web |url=http://www.neighbourhood.statistics.gov.uk/dissemination/LeadKeyFigures.do?a=7 & b=11125111 & c=Kenilworth & d=16 & e=62 & g=6472418 & i=1001x1003x1032x1004 & m=0 & r=1 & s=1377534839927 & enc=1 |title=Neighbourhood Statistics |access-date=2013-08-26 |archive-date=2016-03-05 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160305014856/http://www.neighbourhood.statistics.gov.uk/dissemination/LeadKeyFigures.do?a=7 & b=11125111 & c=Kenilworth & d=16 & e=62 & g=6472418 & i=1001x1003x1032x1004 & m=0 & r=1 & s=1377534839927 & enc=1 |url-status=dead }} < /ref >  It is most famous for its [[castle]] which was first built in the 1120s. There is a [[market]] held each week and the town has a [[train station]]. Nearby towns are [[Warwick]] and [[Leamington Spa]]. The nearest city is [[Coventry]].
