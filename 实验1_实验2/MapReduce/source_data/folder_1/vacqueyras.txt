 '''Vacqueyras''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]] of 1,260 people (2015). It is in the region [[Provence-Alpes-Côte d'Azur]] in the [[Vaucluse]] department in the south of [[France]]. 
 
 == Geography == 
 Vacqueyras is on the southern edge of the [[Dentelles de Montmirail]], about {{convert|18|km|mi|abbr=on}} east of [[Orange, Vaucluse|Orange]] and {{convert|12|km|mi|abbr=on}} north of [[Carpentras]].
