 '''Brebel''' ({{lang-da|Bredbøl}}) is a former [[municipality]] of the district [[Schleswig-Flensburg]], in [[Schleswig-Holstein]], [[Germany]]. On 1 March 2018, it is part of the municipality of [[Süderbrarup]].
