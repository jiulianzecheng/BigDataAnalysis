 '''Sittingbourne''' is an industrial town in [[Swale]], [[Kent]], [[England]]. It is located east of [[Gillingham, Kent|Gillingham]]. About 62,500 people live in Sittingbourne.  < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.swale.gov.uk/census/|title=Population and Census Information|publisher=Swale Borough Council|accessdate=13 November 2013|archive-date=14 October 2014|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20141014104957/http://www.swale.gov.uk/census/|url-status=dead}} < /ref >  Its [[postcode]]s are ME9 and ME10.
