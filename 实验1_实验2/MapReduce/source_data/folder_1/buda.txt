 '''Buda''' was the historic capital of the [[Kingdom of Hungary]] and since 1873 has been the western part of the [[Hungary|Hungarian]] capital [[Budapest]], on the west side of the [[Danube]]. Popular landmarks include [[Buda Castle]], the [[Citadella]], and the [[president of Hungary]]'s residence, [[Sándor Palace]].
