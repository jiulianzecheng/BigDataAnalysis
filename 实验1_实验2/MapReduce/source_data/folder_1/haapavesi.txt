 '''Haapavesi''' is a town in [[Northern Ostrobothnia]], [[Finland]]. As of 28 February 2023, 6,588 people lived there. Neighbouring municipalities are [[Haapajärvi]], [[Kärsämäki]], [[Nivala]], [[Oulainen]], [[Raahe]], [[Siikalatva]] and [[Ylivieska]]. 
 
 The name means  " Aspen Water " . It is known for the Haapavesi Folk Music Festival.
