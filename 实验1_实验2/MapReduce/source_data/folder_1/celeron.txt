 Intel Celeron was a group of [[x86]] [[Central processing unit|processors]] that was sold by [[Intel]] that has been partially discontinued, It was released in 1998, and in 2023, Intel announced that they would be changing the name of both the Celeron and [[Pentium]] line of processors to the name  " Intel Processor " .
