 '''Acorales''' is the [[sweet flag]] order of [[flowering plant]]s and the most basal lineage among the [[monocotyledon]]s, which are characterized by having a single seed [[leaf]]. This order contains the single family '''Acoraceae''' and one genus (''Acorus''), which comprises two to four species of plants that resemble the irises.
