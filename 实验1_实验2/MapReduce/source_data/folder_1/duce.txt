 '''''Duce''''' ({{IPAc-en|ˈ|d|uː|tʃ|eɪ}} {{respell|DOO|chay}}, {{IPA-it|ˈduːtʃe|lang}}) is an [[Italian language|Italian]] title, derived from the [[Latin language|Latin]] word ''[[dux]]''  " leader " , and a [[cognate]] of ''[[duke]]''. [[National Fascist Party]] leader [[Benito Mussolini]] was identified by Fascists as ''Il Duce'' ( " The [[Supreme leader|Leader]] " ) of the movement since the birth of the [[Fasci Italiani di Combattimento]] in 1919. In 1925 it became a reference to the [[dictator]]ial position of ''Sua Eccellenza Benito Mussolini, Capo del Governo, Duce del Fascismo e Fondatore dell'Impero'' ( " ''His Excellency Benito Mussolini, Head of Government, Leader of Fascism and Founder of the Empire'' " ).
