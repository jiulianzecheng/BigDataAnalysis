 ==Institutions== 
 Its government is under the General Education Department of the Government of Kerala offers education from Standard I to VII.  
 
 Elampal Service Cooperative Bank Limited and Federal Bank 
 
 Elampal(Ilambal, as they say) are the major banks in the locality.  
 
 '''Valiyavila''' family was the landlords of this place with multiple business.They held the major land and property at this region. 
 
 ==Places of worship== 
 The village has two temples: Kottaram Aayiravally Devi Temple and  Elampal Mahadevar Temple, which is one of the very old temples in Kerala. 
 
 Experts says the temples history dates back to The Sangha era. 
 
 A new temple is now being constructed at Thiruvazhi .  
 
 It has churches and mosques also including: St. Thomas Mar Thoma Church, Elampal, Mar Gregorios Orthodox Church Marangadu, St. George Orthodox Church, Elampal, St. Thomas Malankara Catholic Church, Thiruvazhy, and Church of God.
