 '''Tuas''' is a region in the far west of the island of [[Singapore]]. It is reachable by the [[Singapore MRT|Mass rapid transit]] (MRT) of Singapore. 
 
 Tuas is mainly an industrial estate like [[Jurong]] and the Tuas checkpoint (called the Tuas Second Link) is found there.
