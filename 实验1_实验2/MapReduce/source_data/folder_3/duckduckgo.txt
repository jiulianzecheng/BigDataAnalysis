 '''DuckDuckGo''' is a [[search engine]] made by [[Gabriel Weinberg]]. It stresses that it does not collect information about users like [[Google]] does, and keeps data private. It headquarters is in [[Paoli, Pennsylvania|Paoli]], [[Pennsylvania]], United States. As of March 2020, it received 56,256,596 searches every day. < ref > {{Cite web|url=https://duckduckgo.com/traffic|title=DuckDuckGo Traffic|website=DuckDuckGo|language=en-US|access-date=2019-04-11}} < /ref >
