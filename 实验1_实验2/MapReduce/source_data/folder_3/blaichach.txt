 '''Blaichach''' is a [[Municipalities of Germany|municipality]] in the district of [[Oberallgäu]] in [[Bavaria]] in [[Germany]]. It is in the northeast of the municipality the [[Iller]] flows through Blaichach. Blaichach was first mentioned as ''Bilaicha'' in 1275. Between 1988 and 2008 Blaichach grew by 599 people.
