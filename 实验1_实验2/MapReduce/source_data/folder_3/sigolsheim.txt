 '''Sigolsheim''' used to be a [[Communes of France|commune]] in the [[Haut-Rhin]] [[Departments of France|department]] of east [[France]]. On 1 January 2016, it became part of the new commune [[Kaysersberg-Vignoble]]. < ref > [http://www.legifrance.gouv.fr/jo_pdf.do?id=JORFTEXT000031665358 Arrêté] 14 July 2015 {{in lang|fr}} < /ref > 
 
 Children went to school at the École élémentaire publique intercommunale which had preschool (''maternelle'') and elementary. < ref > " [http://www.education.gouv.fr/annuaire/68-haut-rhin/sigolsheim/etab/ecole-elementaire-publique-intercommunale.html École élémentaire publique intercommunale] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20161006050403/http://www.education.gouv.fr/annuaire/68-haut-rhin/sigolsheim/etab/ecole-elementaire-publique-intercommunale.html |date=2016-10-06 }}. "  [[French Ministry of Education]]. Retrieved on October 6, 2016. < /ref >
