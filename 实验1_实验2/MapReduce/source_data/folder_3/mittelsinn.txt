 '''Mittelsinn''' is a community in [[Main-Spessart]] in the ''[[Regierungsbezirk]]'' of [[Lower Franconia]] (''Unterfranken'') in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]] and a member of the ''Verwaltungsgemeinschaft'' (Administrative Community) of Burgsinn. 
 
 == Geography == 
 
 === Location === 
 Mittelsinn is in the Würzburg Region. 
   
 The community has the following ''Gemarkungen'' (traditional rural cadastral areas): Mittelsinn, Forst Aura.
