 '''Extremadura''' is an [[autonomous communities of Spain|autonomous community of Spain]]. Its [[Capital (city)|capital city]] is [[Mérida]]. The [[provinces of Spain|provinces]] Cáceres and [[Badajoz]] are a part of Extremadura. Extremadura is [[border]]ed by [[Portugal]] to the west. To the north, it borders [[Castile and León]]. To the south, it borders [[Andalusia]]. To the east, it borders [[Castile-La Mancha]].
