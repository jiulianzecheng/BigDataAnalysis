 '''Sumatra''', known also as ''Sumatera'', is the [[List of islands by area|sixth largest island in the world]]. It is in western [[Indonesia]]. It is to the west of the [[Sunda Islands]], and is bordered by the [[Indian Ocean]]. Sumatra contains the provinces of [[Daerah Istimewa Aceh]], [[Riau]], [[Riau Islands]], [[Bangka Belitung Islands]], [[Jambi]], [[Bengkulu]], [[Lampung]], [[North Sumatra]], [[South Sumatra]], and [[West Sumatra]].
