 '''Zwonowice''' {{IPAc-pl|z|w|o|n|o|'|w|i|c|e}} is a [[village]] in the administrative district of [[Gmina Lyski]], within [[Rybnik County]], [[Silesian Voivodeship]], in southern Poland. It is west of the regional capital [[Katowice]]. 
 
 ==Pastors== 
 * Antoni Gasz (1942-1950) 
 * Oswald Niedziela (1950-1954) 
 * Jerzy Stefani, pastor of [[Lyski]] in the years 1954-1956 led ministry in Zwonowice 
 * Antoni Kraus (1956-1962) 
 * Kazimierz Wyciślik (1962-1978) 
 * Antoni Pieczka (1978-1995) 
 * Roman Gołąb (1995-present)
