 '''Lods''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]] of 224 people (2015). It is found in the [[Doubs]] [[Departments of France|department]] in the [[Bourgogne-Franche-Comté]] [[Regions of France|region]] in eastern [[France]]. 
 
 == Geography == 
 Lods is in the [[Jura Mountains]], about {{convert|27|km|abbr=on}} southeast of the city of [[Besançon]]. It is in the valley of the [[Loue|Loue River]].
