 '''Charkh''' ({{lang-fa|چرخ}} lit.  " wheel " ) is an Iranian television [[talk show]] that has aired on [[IRIB TV4]] since 2015. < ref name= " TV4 "  / >  Each episode focuses on scientific topics and is approximately 75 & nbsp;minutes in length. The series airs every working day-night (six days a week). 
 
 == Theme == 
 The show is aired live and nationwide every evening during the weekdays according to the Iranian calendar on [[Channel 4 (Iran)|Channel 4]]. [[Shahryar Rabbani]] and [[Milad Nouri (Developer and TV Host)|Milad Nouri]] perform as presenters and the program in each episode hosts guest(s) from the Iranian scientific community in one of the following fields: 
 * Technology and engineering 
 * Medical science 
 * Fundamental science 
 * Cognitive sciences, philosophy, and history of science 
 * Environment and natural resources < ref name= " TV4 "  / >
