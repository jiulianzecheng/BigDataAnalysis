The '''Mesoarchaean''' ('''Mesoarchean''') is the third [[geology|geological]] [[era]] in the [[Archaean]] eon. It began 3.2 [[billion]] years ago, and ended 2.8 billion years ago. < ref > http://www.stratigraphy.org/ICSchart/ChronostratChart2013-01.pdf < /ref >  Before the Mesoarchaean era was the [[Palaeoarchaean]] era. After the Mesoarchaean era was the [[Neoarchaean]]. 
 
 Fossils from Australia show that [[stromatolite]]s have lived on Earth since the Mesoarchaean.
