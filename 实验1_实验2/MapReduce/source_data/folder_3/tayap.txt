 '''Tayap''' is a small village in [[Cameroon]]. It is {{convert|86|km|abbr=off}} from [[Yaoundé]], the [[capital city]]. It is {{convert|164|km|abbr=off}} from [[Douala]], the biggest city of the country. < ref name= " AGRI " > ''La monographie du village de Tayap''. Tayap, Éditions Agripo, 36 p. < /ref > 
 
 The climate is equatorial. It is usually hot, {{convert|25|°C}}. It is often wet. There are four different seasons. 
 
 In 2005, 272 people lived in Tayap. The village has a [[primary school]]. It has 4 classrooms. < ref name= " AGRI " / > 
 
 Most people work in [[agriculture]]. They produce [[manioc]], [[banana]]s, [[Orange (fruit)|oranges]], [[mango]]es, [[vegetable]]s, [[peanut]]s, [[cocoa]] and [[palm oil]]. < ref name= " AGRI " / > 
 
 The village is surrounded by a large forest but [[deforestation]] is a major problem in this area. < ref name= " AGRI " / > 
   
 Since 2011 Tayap focuses on [[biodiversity]] and [[ecology]]. It tries to develop new activities such as [[tourism]]. < ref name= " AGRI " / >
