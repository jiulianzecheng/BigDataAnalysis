 '''Scharrel''' ([[Saterland Frisian language|Saterland Frisian]]: '''Skäddel''') is a village and former municipality in [[Lower Saxony]]. In 1974 the until then independent municipality became part of the newly formed municipality of [[Saterland]] in [[Cloppenburg (district)|Cloppenburg]]. < ref name= " Saterland " >
