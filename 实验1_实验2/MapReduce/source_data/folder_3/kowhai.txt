 '''Kowhai''' are small, [[wood]]y [[legume]] [[tree]]s in the [[genus]] [[Sophora]]. They are [[native]] to [[New Zealand]]. There are eight [[species]]. Kowhai is the [[common name]] in [[MƒÅori language]] used in New Zealand. In [[South Island]] grows ''Sophora microphylla'', and in the [[North Island]] a larger [[leaf]]lets species, i.e. ''[[Sophora|S.tetraptera]]''.
