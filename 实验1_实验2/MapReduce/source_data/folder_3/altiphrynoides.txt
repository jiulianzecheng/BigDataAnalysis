 '''''Altiphrynoides''''' is a [[genus]] of [[toad]]s.  They are commonly called '''Ethiopian toads'''. They only live in the highlands of south-central [[Ethiopia]] in the [[Arsi Province|Arussi]], [[Bale Province, Ethiopia|Bale]], and [[Sidamo Province|Sidamo]] provinces. Both species are [[Threatened species|threatened]] by [[habitat loss]]. < ref name=threatened > Stuart ''et al.'', eds. (2008). ''Threatened Amphibians of the World.'' [[IUCN]]. Pp. 156 and 204. {{ISBN|978-84-96553-41-5}} < /ref >  They used to be included in ''[[Nectophrynoides]]''.  Now they are separate because they lay eggs, while ''Nectophrynoides'' give birth to fully developed young.
