 '''Cantello''' is a ''[[comune]]'' (municipality) in the [[Province of Varese]] in the [[Italy|Italian]] region of [[Lombardy]]. It is about {{convert|45|km|mi}} northwest of [[Milan]] and about {{convert|4|km|mi|0}} east of [[Varese]], on the border with [[Switzerland]]. As of 31 December 2004, 4,409 people lived there. It has an area of 9.13 & nbsp;km². < ref name= " istat " > All demographics and other statistics: Italian statistical institute [[National Institute of Statistics (Italy)|Istat]]. < /ref > 
 
 The municipality has two ''[[frazione|frazioni]]'': Gaggiolo and Ligurno. 
 
 Cantello is next to these municipalities: [[Arcisate]], [[Cagno]], [[Clivio]], [[Malnate]], [[Rodero]], [[Stabio]] (Switzerland), [[Varese]], [[Viggiù]].
