 '''Wels''' ([[Latin]]: '''Ovilava''') is a [[city]] in the [[States of Austria|state]] of [[Upper Austria]] in [[Austria]]. It is on the [[Traun River]] near [[Linz]]. About 60,000 people lived there in 2016.
