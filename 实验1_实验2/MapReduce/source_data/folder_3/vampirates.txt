 '''''Vampirates''''' is a series of young adult [[fantasy]] [[book]]s. It is written by [[Great Britain|British]] author [[Justin Somper]]. The series is about two [[twin]] [[children]], Connor and Grace Tempest, who get separated at [[sea]]. < ref > {{cite web|title=Review: Vampirates - Demons of the Ocean by Justin Somper|url=http://www.wondrousreads.com/2010/02/review-vampirates-demons-of-ocean-by.html|date=2010-02-15|accessdate=2011-04-14|publisher=Wondrous Reads|archive-date=2011-08-04|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110804181410/http://www.wondrousreads.com/2010/02/review-vampirates-demons-of-ocean-by.html|url-status=dead}} < /ref >  They are saved by two very different [[ship]]s. Connor is saved by a normal [[pirate]] ship called the Diablo, < ref > {{cite web|author=Somper, Justin|title=The Diablo|url=http://www.vampirates.co.uk/explore/diablo/|date=2011|publisher=Simon and Schuster|accessdate=2011-04-09|archive-date=2011-04-23|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110423232949/http://www.vampirates.co.uk/explore/diablo/|url-status=dead}} < /ref >  Grace by the mythical Vampirate ship called the Nocturne. < ref > {{cite web|author=Somper, Justin|url=http://www.vampirates.co.uk/explore/nocturne/|title=The Nocturne|date=2011|publisher=Simon and Schuster|accessdate=2011-04-09|archive-date=2010-09-29|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20100929174055/http://www.vampirates.co.uk/explore/nocturne/|url-status=dead}} < /ref >  The word ''Vampirate'' refers to a [[vampire]] living on the Nocturne.
