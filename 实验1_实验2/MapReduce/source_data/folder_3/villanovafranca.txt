 '''Villanovafranca''' ('''Biddanoa Franca, Bidda Noa Franca''' in [[Sardinian language|Sardinian]]) is a [[town]] and ''[[Commune|comune]]'' (municipality) in the [[Province of Sud Sardegna]] in [[Italy|Italian]] region of [[Sardinia]], located about {{convert|50|km|mi}} north of [[Cagliari]] and about {{convert|13|km|mi|0}} northeast of [[Sanluri]].
