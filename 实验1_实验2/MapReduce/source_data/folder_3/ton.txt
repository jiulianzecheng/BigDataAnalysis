'''Ton''' might refer to 
 
 * The ''[[common ton]]'' or ''[[short ton]]'', defined as 2,000 pounds ''avoirdupois'' (1000kg) 
 * The ''[[long ton]]'', defined as 2,240 pounds (1 016 & nbsp;kg) 
 * The ''[[ton]]'' or '''''tonne''''', called a [[metric ton]] in the United States, is defined as 1,000 kilograms (2,204 pounds). This unit is also called a  " mega-gram "  (1 000 000 g) but  " tonne "  is more convenient.
