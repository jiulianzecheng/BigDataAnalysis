 '''''Carmen''''' is a [[France|French]] [[opera]] written and first performed on [[3 March]] [[1875]] by [[Georges Bizet]]. The story is set in [[Spain]]. It is based on the story of the same title by [[Prosper Mérimée]].  
 
 == Roles == 
 * Carmen - [[mezzo-soprano]] 
 * Don José - [[tenor]] 
 * Micaëla - [[soprano]] 
 * Escamillo - [[baritone]] 
 
 == The story == 
 Carmen is about a [[Romani people|gypsy]] girl who works in a factory. She falls in love with a [[soldier]] called Don José, who loves her. However, he is already [[engagement|engaged]] to somebody else. Later, he leaves the girl he was engaged to and the army for Carmen. Running away together, they are happy for a while until Carmen decides she loves a [[bullfight]]er called Escamillo. She leaves Don José. One day when Carmen is watching a bullfight, Don José waits for her and then tries to make her come back. She refuses, so he stabs her to death. [[wikt:horror|Horrified]] at what he has done, he goes to jail.
