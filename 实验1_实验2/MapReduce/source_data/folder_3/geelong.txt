 '''Geelong''' is the second biggest [[List of Cities in Australia|city]] in the [[States and territories of Australia|state]] of [[Victoria, Australia]]. It has a large [[Harbour|port]] and 190,000 people living in its area. Geelong at [[Corio Bay]], which is 75 kilometres south-west  
 [[Melbourne]]. 
 
 The city is a near many famous tourist attractions, for example the [[Great Ocean Road]], the [[Shipwreck Coast]] and the [[Bellarine Peninsula]]. Geelong also has the second oldest [[Australian rules football]] club in the world, the [[Geelong Football Club]], also called ''The Cats''.
