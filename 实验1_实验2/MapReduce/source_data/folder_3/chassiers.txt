'''Chassiers''' is a [[town]] and [[Communes of France|commune]] in the [[Ardèche]] ''[[Departments of France|département]]'', in the southern part of [[France]]. The commune has a [[population]] of about 850 people as of the year [[1999]]. < ref > http://www.insee.fr/fr/recensement/nouv_recens/resultats/repartition/comd07.htm?numdep=07 & x=39 & y=7 List of populations and communes of the Ardeche department. < /ref >
