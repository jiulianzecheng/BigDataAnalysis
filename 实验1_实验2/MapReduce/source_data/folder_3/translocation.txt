'''Translocation''' may mean: 
 * [[Translocation (genetics)]], an abnormality of chromosomes 
 ** [[Robertsonian translocation]], a chromosomal rearrangement in pairs 13, 14, 15, 21, and 22 
 ** [[Nonreciprocal translocation]], transfer of genes from one chromosome to another 
 * [[PEP group translocation]], a method used by bacteria for absorbing sugar 
 * [[Translocation (botany)]], the transport of food through the phloem in plants 
 * [[Protein translocation]], a process in protein biosynthesis 
 * [[Species translocation]], movement of a species, by people, from one area to another
