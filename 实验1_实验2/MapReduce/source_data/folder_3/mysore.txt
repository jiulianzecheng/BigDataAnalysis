 '''Mysore''' is a city in [[Karnataka]], [[India]]. Mysore is {{convert|140|km|abbr=off}} south-west of [[Bangalore]]. Travel time from Bangalore to Mysore is 2-3 hours by road or rail. Mysore is the second largest city in Karnataka. 
 
 [[Mysore Palace]], Jaganmohan Palace, Chamundi Hills, Brindavan Gardens, and Mysore Zoo are the places to see in Mysore. Some people go to Mysore to see the [[Dasara procession]].  
 
 People also go to Mysore to learn [[yoga]].
