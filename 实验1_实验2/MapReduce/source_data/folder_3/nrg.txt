 '''NRG''' is a South Korean [[K-pop]] band formed in 1997 by Music Factory Entertainment. The group rose to fame with their debut song,  " I Can Do It. " 
 In 1999, members Myunghoon and Sungjin left the group, while the other three released the album  " NRG 003. "  After member Hwansung tragically died of pnemonia in 2000, Myunghoon and Sungjin rejoined the group for their memorial album,  " Sorrow. " 
 
 The group made an official come back with  " Hit Song "  in 2003, which ranked #1 on the music charts. 
 
 Following the departure of Sunghoon, the 3 remaining members released 1 more album before disbandment. In 2017, NRG announced a new album would be released on the 20th anniversary of debut.
