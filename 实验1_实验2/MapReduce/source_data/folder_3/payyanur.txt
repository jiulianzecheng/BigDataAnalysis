 '''Payyanur''' is a town and municipality in [[Kannur district]] in the state of [[Kerala]] in [[India]]. This municipality is formed by three administrative villages: [[Vellur]], Korom and Payyanur. Other villages are: [[Annur]] and [[Kokkanissery]]. The town is on the banks of the [[Perumba River]].
