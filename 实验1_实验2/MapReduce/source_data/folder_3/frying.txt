'''Frying''' is [[cooking]] food in hot [[butter]] or [[vegetable oil]] or other [[fat]]. We can fry food in a small amount of fat in a [[frying pan|pan]] or in a lot of oil in a [[pot]]. Some [[restaurant]]s use [[deep frying]] to fry a large amount of food.
