 '''Eluveitie''' is a [[folk metal]] [[band]] from [[Switzerland]]. They started in 2002. Their [[Music genre|style]] is a mixture of ''[[Celt]]ic folk metal'' and ''[[melodic death metal]]''. Their style is sometimes called ''Celtic Metal''. Some of their [[lyrics]] are in [[English language|English]]. Some of their lyrics are in Gaulish, a language that nobody still speaks as a [[first language]]. The band was founded by Christian  " Chrigel "  Glanzmann.  
 
 == Members == 
 * Patrick  " Päde "  Kistler - [[Bagpipes]], [[Whistle]]s (since 2008) 
 * Merlin Sutter - [[Drums]] (since 2004) 
 * Siméon Koch - [[Guitar]] (since 2004) 
 * Christian  " Chrigel "  Glanzmann - [[Vocals]], Mandola (similar to a [[mandolin]]), [[Whistle]]s, [[Pipes]], Gaita (a kind of [[Bagpipes|bagpipe]]), [[Guitar|Acoustic Guitar]], Bodhràn (a kind of Irish [[drum]]) 
 * Meri Tadic -  [[Violin]], [[Vocals]] 
 * Kay Brem - [[Bass guitar|Bass]] (since 2008) 
 * Ivo Henzi - [[Guitar]] (since 2004) 
 * Fabienne Erni - Hurdy Gurdy (an instrument that has similarities to the [[violin]] and the bagpipe), [[Vocals]] (since 2017)
