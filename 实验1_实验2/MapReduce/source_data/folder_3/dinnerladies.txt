 '''''Dinnerladies''''' was a [[United Kingdom|British]] [[sitcom]]. It was written by, [[producer|co-produced]] by and [[cast member|starred]] [[Victoria Wood]]. The [[television series|series]] had 16 [[episode]]s. It was [[broadcast]] on the [[BBC]] from 1998 to 2000. 
 
 The setting is the [[Canteen (place)|canteen]] of HWD Components, a [[fiction]]al [[Manchester]] [[factory]]. During its two years, ''Dinnerladies'' follows the lives of the people working in the canteen.
