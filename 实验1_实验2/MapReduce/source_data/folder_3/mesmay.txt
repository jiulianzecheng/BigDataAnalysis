 '''Mesmay''' ({{IPA-fr|mɛmɛ}}) is a [[Communes of France|commune]] in the [[Doubs]] [[Departments of France|department]] in the [[Bourgogne-Franche-Comté]] [[Regions of France|region]] in eastern [[France]]. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 The commune lies {{convert|6|km|mi|abbr=on}} southwest of [[Quingey]] in the valley of the Loue River.
