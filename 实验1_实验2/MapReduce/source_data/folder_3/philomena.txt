 '''''Philomena''''' is a 2013 [[United Kingdom|British]] [[drama movie]]. The movie is about a [[news]] journalist who has just lost his job as [[Labour Party (UK)|Labour]] [[government]] adviser. In the movie, [[Judi Dench]] plays [[Philomena Lee]] and [[Steve Coogan]] plays Martin. The movie has gotten positive review by critics.
