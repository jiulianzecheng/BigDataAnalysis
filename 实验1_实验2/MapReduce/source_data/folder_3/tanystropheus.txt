 '''Tanystropheus''' is a long [[neck]]ed [[reptile]] that lived in the Middle [[Triassic]] period.  The main feature that stands out about this [[animal]] is its very long neck. [[Fossil]]s of this creature have been found in [[Europe]] and the [[Middle East]]. Habitat: probably [[aquatic]].
