 '''Umbria''' is one of the twenty [[regions of Italy]], in center [[Italy]]. It is the one of the smallest Italian region. Its [[Capital city|capital]] is [[Perugia]]. 
 
 It is the only region without a [[Shore|coastline]] or a border with another country. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 The region is located in [[Central Italy]] with an area of {{Convert|8464.33|km2|sqmi|abbr=on }}. < ref name= " area " > {{cite web|url=http://www.tuttitalia.it/umbria/ |title=Umbria |publisher=Tuttitalia.it |language=Italian |accessdate=18 July 2016}} < /ref >  It is bordered by [[Marche]] to the east and northeast, [[Tuscany]] to the west and northwest, and [[Lazio]] to the south and southwest.

 The main river in the region is the [[Tiber River|Tiber]]. Other important rivers are the [[Nera (Italy)|Nera]], [[Paglia (River)|Paglia]], [[Topino]], [[Chiascio]] and [[Nestore (river)|Nestore]]. 
 
 The largest lake of Umbria is the [[Lake Trasimeno|Trasimeno]], one of the largest lake of Italy. 
 
 The highest mountain in the region is [[Cima del Redentore]], in the [[Sibillini Mountains]] ({{lang-it|Monti Sibillini}}), with an [[altitude]] of {{Convert|2448|m|ft|0|abbr=on }}.
