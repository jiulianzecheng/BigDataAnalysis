 '''''Dakotaraptor''''' is a genus of large carnivorous [[theropod]] [[Dromaeosauridae|dromaeosaurid]] [[dinosaur]]. It lived in the [[Late Cretaceous]] of [[North America]]. 
 
 ==Description== 
 ===Size=== 
 ''Dakotaraptor'' was about {{convert|5|m}} long. That makes it one of the largest dromaeosaurids known.
