 '''Vaduz''' ( < small > German < /small > : [ < nowiki/ > [[Help:IPA/Standard German|faˈdʊt͡s]]] or [ < nowiki/ > [[Help:IPA/Standard German|faˈduːt͡s]]];  < small > Regional dialect < /small > : [[Help:Pronunciation respelling key|[faˈdot͡s]]]) is the [[capital (city)|capital]] of the [[country]] of [[Liechtenstein]]. It has a [[population]] of about 7,500 [[people]].  
 
 Historically, Vaduz was the capital of the [[County of Vaduz]], a [[state]] of the [[Holy Roman Empire]].
