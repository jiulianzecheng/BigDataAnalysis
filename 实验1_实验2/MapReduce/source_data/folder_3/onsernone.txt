 '''Onsernone''' is a [[municipality]] of the [[district]] of [[Locarno (district)|Locarno]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Ticino]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 Onsernone was created in 1995 when the municipalities of Comologno, Crana and Russo joined together to become Onsernone. < ref name=Fusion > [http://www.bfs.admin.ch/bfs/portal/de/index/infothek/nomenklaturen/blank/blank/gem_liste/03.html Amtliches Gemeindeverzeichnis der Schweiz] published by the Swiss Federal Statistical Office {{in lang|de}} accessed 14 January 2010 < /ref >  On 10 April 2016, the former municipalities of [[Vergeletto]], [[Gresso]], [[Mosogno]] and [[Isorno]] merged into Onsernone. < ref > [http://www.bfs.admin.ch/bfs/portal/de/index/infothek/nomenklaturen/blank/blank/gem_liste/03.html Amtliches Gemeindeverzeichnis der Schweiz] published by the Swiss Federal Statistical Office {{in lang|de}} accessed 27 April 2016 < /ref >
