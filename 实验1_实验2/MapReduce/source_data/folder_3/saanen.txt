 '''Saanen''' ({{lang-fr|Gessenay}}) is a [[municipality]] and [[ski resort]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Bern (canton)|Bern]] in [[Switzerland]]. It is the [[Capital city|capital]] of the [[Obersimmental-Saanen (administrative district)|administrative district of Obersimmental-Saanen]].
