 '''Maidenhead''' is a town in [[England]] in [[Berkshire]]. About 60,000 people live in the town. The town is about 25 [[mile]]s (41 [[kilometre]]s) from [[London]]. It is named after a [[wharf]] that was built there in [[1297]]. It lies on the [[River Thames]].
