 '''Pfarrweisach''' is a [[Municipalities of Germany|municipality]] in [[Haßberge (district)|Haßberge]] in [[Bavaria]] in [[Germany]]. 
 
 == Geography == 
 
 === Location === 
 Pfarrweisach is in the Main-Rhön Region (Bavarian planning region no. 3).
