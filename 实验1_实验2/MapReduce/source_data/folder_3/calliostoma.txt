 The '''Top shells''' (which are called '''Calliostoma''') are [[Ocean|marine]] [[gastropod]] [[mollusc]]s. They can be found world-wide. The name of this genus is derived from the Greek words ''kallos'' (beautiful) and ''stoma'' (mouth), referring to the pearly [[aperture]].
