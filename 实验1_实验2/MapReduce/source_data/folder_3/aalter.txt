 '''Aalter''' is a [[municipality]] found in the [[Belgium|Belgian]] province of [[East Flanders]]. On 1 January 2019, the municipality of [[Knesselare]] was merged into Aalter. 
 
 In 2007, its [[population]] was 18,887. < ref > {{Cite web |url=http://statbel.fgov.be/downloads/pop200701com.xls |title=Werkelijke bevolking per gemeente op 1 januari 2007 / Population de droit par commune au 1 janvier 2007 (.xls) |access-date=2008-07-14 |archive-date=2008-11-23 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20081123144002/http://statbel.fgov.be/downloads/pop200701com.xls |url-status=dead }} < /ref > 
 
 It is found at 51° 05 North, 03° 26 East. < ref > {{Cite web |url=http://statbel.fgov.be/pub/d1/p120_nl.xls |title=Geografische coördinaten van de gemeenten (.xls) |access-date=2008-07-14 |archive-date=2004-07-23 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20040723190930/http://statbel.fgov.be/pub/d1/p120_nl.xls |url-status=dead }} < /ref >
