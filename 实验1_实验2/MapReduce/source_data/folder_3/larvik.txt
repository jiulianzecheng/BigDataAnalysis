 '''Larvik''' is a city and [[municipality]] in [[Norway]]. The municipality lies within the [[Counties of Norway|county]] [[Vestfold og Telemark]]. It has a population of 47,440. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Kommunefakta|url=https://www.ssb.no/kommunefakta/kommune|access-date=2020-11-04|website=SSB|language=nb}} < /ref > 
 
 == History == 
 [[Skiringssal|Kaupang in Skiringssal]] is an archaeological site where the remains of an ancient town was discovered. It is now known as the oldest known merchant town in Norway.{{sfn|Urbańczyk|2008|p=180}} 
 
 [[Farris (mineral water)|Farris]] is a brand of mineral water produced in Larvik, and is Norway's oldest bottled-water. < ref > {{Cite web|last=|first=|date=|title=Farris Hjemmeside - Historie|url=https://www.farris.no/historie|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=|website=}} < /ref >
