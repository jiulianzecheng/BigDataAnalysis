 The '''bellbird''' is a [[bird]] native to Central and [[South America]]. It gets its name from its call which sounds like a [[bell]]. It uses this call to attract its mate. The bellbird has the loudest voice of any bird, so loud it can be heard a mile or more away. < ref > {{cite book |title=Jungle Animals Over 100 Questions and Answers to Things You Want to Know |last=Ganeri |first=Anita |year=2000 |location=Dubai, U.A.E |isbn=0-75254-909-X}} < /ref >
