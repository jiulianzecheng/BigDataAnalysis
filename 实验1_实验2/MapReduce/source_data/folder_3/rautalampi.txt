 '''Rautalampi''' is a [[municipality]] in [[Finland]]. The city has 3,451 people (as of 31 August 2012). It covers 761,97 & nbsp;km². Near [[Laihia]] are the municipalities [[Hankasalmi]], [[Konnevesi]], [[Pieksämäki]], [[Suonenjoki]], [[Tervo]] and [[Vesanto]]. 
 
 ==History== 
 History starts from 1561 when Sweden's king [[Gustav I of Sweden]] gave order to establish so called  " hallintopitäjä "  Rautalampi, ''(Rautalammen hallintopitäjä).''
