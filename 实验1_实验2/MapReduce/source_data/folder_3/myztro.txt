 '''Tshiamiso Sekowe''' is a South African DJ and music producer professionally known as '''Myztro''', he rose to fame after the release of  " Emcimbini " ,  " Dipatje tsa Felo "  and  " 66 " , < ref name= " :0 " > {{Cite web|last=Yanos|first=The|date=2023-08-04|title=Myztro Opens Up About Being Maphorisa's Little Brother In The Music Industry - The Yanos Magazine|url=https://theyanos.co.za/2023/08/myztro-opens-up-about-being-maphorisas-little-brother-in-the-music-industry/|access-date=2023-10-05|website=theyanos.co.za|language=en-ZA}} < /ref >  the latter certified Multi-Platinum by the Recording Industry of South Africa (RiSA). < ref > {{Cite web|title=Certifications 2023|url=https://www.risa.org.za/website/certification/certifications/?artist=Myztro & title= & label=|access-date=2023-10-05|website=www.risa.org.za}} < /ref > 
 
 Myztro was born and raised in Soshanguve, he is the biological brother of South African DJ and record producer DJ Maphorisa. < ref > {{Cite web|last=Simpasa|first=Precious|date=2023-08-24|title=Family bonds: South African musicians who are related|url=https://www.thesouthafrican.com/lifestyle/celeb-news/family-bonds-south-african-musicians-who-are-related-24-august-2023/|access-date=2023-10-06|website=The South African|language=en-ZA}} < /ref > < ref name= " :0 " > {{Cite web|last=Yanos|first=The|date=2023-08-04|title=Myztro Opens Up About Being Maphorisa's Little Brother In The Music Industry - The Yanos Magazine|url=https://theyanos.co.za/2023/08/myztro-opens-up-about-being-maphorisas-little-brother-in-the-music-industry/|access-date=2023-10-05|website=theyanos.co.za|language=en-ZA}} < /ref >
