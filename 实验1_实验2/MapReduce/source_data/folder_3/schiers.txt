 '''Schiers''' is a [[Switzerland|Swiss]] village in the [[Prättigau]] and a [[municipality]] of the [[district]] of [[Prättigau/Davos (district)|Prättigau/Davos]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Graubünden]]. The first concrete girder bridge ever built, [[Salginatobel Bridge]], is in Schiers. It was designed by [[Robert Maillart]] and completed in 1930. In 1991 it was the first bridgenamed a [[Historic Civil Engineering Landmark|International Historic Civil Engineering Landmark]] by the [[American Society of Civil Engineers]].
