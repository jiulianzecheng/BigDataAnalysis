 '''Essenbach''' is a [[Municipalities of Germany|municipality]] in [[Landshut (district)|Landshut]], in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. It is 9 & nbsp;km northeast of [[Landshut]]. 
 
 It is the place of [[Isar Nuclear Power Plant]]. 
 
 ==Twin towns== 
 Essenbach is [[town twinning|twinned]] with: 
 
 * {{flagicon|France}} [[Savigneux, Loire]], France, since 1997 
 * {{flagicon|Italy}} [[Savignano Irpino]], Italy, since 2005
