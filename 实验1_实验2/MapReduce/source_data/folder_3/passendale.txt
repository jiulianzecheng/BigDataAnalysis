 '''Passendale''' is a village in the municipality of [[Zonnebeke]] in [[West Flanders]], [[Belgium]]. It is close to the town of [[Ypres]], situated on the [[hill ridge]] separating the historical wetlands of the [[Yser]] and [[Leie]] valleys.
