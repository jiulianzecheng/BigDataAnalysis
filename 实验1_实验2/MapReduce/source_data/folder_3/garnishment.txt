A '''garnishment''' is the means for collecting money judgments against a [[defendant]] by ordering third parties to pay [[money]] directly toward the [[plaintiff]]. '''Wage garnishments''' are a process of deducting money off employees' monetary compensation (including their [[salary]]); these types of garnishments continue until entire debts are paid and can be taken for any [[debt]], however usual examples are: 
 
 # [[child support]] 
 # defaulted [[Student loan|student loans]] 
 # [[tax]] 
 
 Wage garnishments can adversely affect credit reputation or one's ability to open bank accounts.
