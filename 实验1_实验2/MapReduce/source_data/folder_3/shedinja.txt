'''Shedinja''' is one of the [[fiction]]al [[species]] of [[Pokémon]] from the ''[[Pokémon]]'' media franchise owned by [[Nintendo]] and [[Game Freak]]. 
 
 It is a Bug/Ghost type Pokemon from the third generation of games, Ruby, Sapphire and Emerald. It is evolved from a Nincada when there's a free space in the player's party. Shedinja is notable because it only takes damage from super effective attacks and has one HP.
