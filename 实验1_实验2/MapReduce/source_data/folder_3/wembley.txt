 '''Wembley''' is an area of northwest [[London]]. It is a part of the [[London Borough of Brent]]. It is famous for [[Wembley Stadium]] (first built April 23, 1923) and [[Wembley Arena]] (first built 1934). A new outlet centre, [[London Designer Outlet]], was built in the area and opened in 2013.
