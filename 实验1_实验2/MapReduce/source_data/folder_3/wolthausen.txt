 '''Wolthausen''' is a village (''[[Ortsteil]]'') in the municipality of [[Winsen (Aller)]] in [[Landkreis Celle|Celle]] in the north German state of [[Lower Saxony]]. It is on the [[River Örtze]] in the [[Aller (Germany)|Aller]] valley and on the [[Bundesstraße 3|B 3 federal road]].
