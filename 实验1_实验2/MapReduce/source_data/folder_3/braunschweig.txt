 '''Braunschweig''' ({{lang-en|Brunswick}}) is a city in the north of [[Germany]], in the [[States of Germany|state]] of [[Lower Saxony]]. 
 It was probably founded in the 9th century. About 245,000 people live there. It has a technical [[university]].
