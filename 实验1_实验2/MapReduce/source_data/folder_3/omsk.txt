 '''Omsk''' is a city in [[Russia]] in the [[Siberia]] region, capital of [[Omsk oblast|Omsk Oblast]]. As of the census in 2010, Omsk had been declared one of the cities in [[Russia]] with over 1 million people. Omsk is close to the border of [[Kazakhstan]]. 
 Omsk is the only city in the world which has [[Metro]] with only one station.{{cn}}
