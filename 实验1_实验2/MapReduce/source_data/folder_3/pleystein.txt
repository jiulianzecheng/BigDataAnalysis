 '''Pleystein''' ({{IPA-de|ˈplaɪ̯ʃtaɪ̯n|lang|De-Pleystein.ogg}}) is a [[Municipalities of Germany|municipality]]  in [[Neustadt an der Waldnaab (district)|Neustadt an der Waldnaab]], in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. It is 18 & nbsp;km east of [[Weiden in der Oberpfalz]], and 11 & nbsp;km west of [[Rozvadov]].
