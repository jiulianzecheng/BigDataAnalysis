 '''Bleckmar''' is a village administered by the [[Lower Saxony|Lower Saxon]] town of [[Bergen (Landkreis Celle)|Bergen]] in the northern part of [[Celle (district)|Celle]] on the [[Lüneburg Heath]] in North [[Germany]]. It is about {{convert|3|km|mi}} north of Bergen on the [[Bundesstraße 3|B 3]] federal road. It is administratively responsible for the neighbouring hamlet of Dageförde.
