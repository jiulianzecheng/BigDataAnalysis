 '''Rizal''', officially the '''Province of Rizal''' ({{lang-fil|Lalawigan ng Rizal}}), is a [[Provinces of the Philippines|province]] in the [[Philippines]]. It is in the [[Calabarzon]] region in [[Luzon]]. Its capital is the city of [[Antipolo]].
