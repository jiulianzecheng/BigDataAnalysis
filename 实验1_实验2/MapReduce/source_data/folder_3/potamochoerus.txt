 '''''Potamochoerus''''' is a [[genus]] in the pig family ([[Suidae]]). The two species only live in [[Sub-saharan Africa]].  The Bushpig, possibly due to [[introduced species|introduction]] by man, also can be found in [[Madagascar]] and nearby [[island]]s.

 * [[Bushpig]] (''Potamochoerus larvatus''). 
 * [[Red River Hog]] (''Potamochoerus porcus'').
