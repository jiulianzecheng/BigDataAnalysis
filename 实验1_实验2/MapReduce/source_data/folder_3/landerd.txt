 '''Landerd''' is a former municipality in the [[Netherlands|Dutch]] [[Provinces of the Netherlands|province]] of [[North Brabant]]. In 2021, about 15,800 people lived there. 
 
 Landerd has been a part of [[Maashorst]] since 2022.
