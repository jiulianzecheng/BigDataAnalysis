 '''Oyaki''' (おやき) is a [[Japan]]ese [[dumpling]] made from fermented [[buckwheat]] dough wrapped around a stuffing of Japanese vegetables, fruit, or [[Red bean paste|anko bean paste]] and then roasted on an iron pan. Oyaki are popular and widely available in [[Nagano Prefecture]] which is famous for the dish.
