'''Bundelkhand''' is a geographical and cultural region and also a mountain range in central India. Part of it is in the state of [[Uttar Pradesh]] and a larger part in the state of [[Madhya Pradesh]]. [[Jhansi]] is the largest city in Bundelkhand. The Bundeli language is the most common of the [[Hindi]] dialects spoken in Bundelkhand.
