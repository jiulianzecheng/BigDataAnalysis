 The '''sandgrouse''' is any one of the sixteen species of a family of ground-dwelling [[bird]]s known as the Pteroclidae. They usually occur in [[steppe]]-like environments, with large open spaces, and few if any [[Tree|trees]].  They occur mostly in [[Africa]] and [[Asia]].  Some species also live on the [[Iberian peninsula]], and in [[France]]. They mostly eat [[seeds]].
