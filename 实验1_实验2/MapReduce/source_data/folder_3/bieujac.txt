'''Bieujac''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is found in the [[Aquitaine]] [[Regions of France|region]] in the [[Gironde]] [[Departments of France|department]] in the southwest of [[France]]. 
 
 == Geography == 
 Bieujac has a total area of 7 square kilometers.
