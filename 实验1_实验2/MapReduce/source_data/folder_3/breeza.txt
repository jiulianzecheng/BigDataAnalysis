 '''Breeza''' is a small village in [[New South Wales]], [[Australia]]. It is about 43 [[kilometre|kms]] south of [[Gunnedah, New South Wales|Gunnedah]] and 393 & nbsp;km north of [[Sydney]]. It is a part of the [[Gunnedah Shire Council|Gunnedah Shire]] [[Local Government Areas of Australia|Local Government Area]].  Breeza is in the [[Liverpool Plains]] agricultural area. The area around Breeza is called the  " Breeza Plains " . There are about 25 people who live in Breeza, < ref name= fd > {{cite web |url= http://www.walkabout.com.au/locations/NSWBreeza.shtml|title= Walkabout - Breeza |accessdate=2008-11-02 |format= |work=Fairfax Digital }} < /ref >  and 133 who live in the area. 
 
 Breeza is on the [[Kamilaroi Highway]] about halfway between Gunnedah and Quirindi. It is also on the North-west railway line, close to where it crosses the [[Mooki River]].  Breeza has a grain [[silo]]. 
 
 It is often erroneously said that the [[bushranger]] [[Ben Hall]] was born at Breeza. < ref name=bhw > {{cite web |url=http://www.visitnsw.com/town/Breeza/Ben_Hall_Wall/info.aspx |title=Ben Hall Wall |accessdate=2008-11-02 |format= |work=New South Wales Tourism }}{{Dead link|date=January 2021 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }} < /ref >   He was in fact born near Maitland, NSW but his father, Ben HAll Snr worked on Breeza Station at one time. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://scs.une.edu.au/Bushrangers/bhall.htm |title=Bushrangers - Ben Hall |accessdate=2008-11-02 |format= |work=The Bushranger Site |archive-date=2007-09-20 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20070920195628/http://scs.une.edu.au/Bushrangers/bhall.htm |url-status=dead }} < /ref >  There is a Ben Hall wall with a painting at the roadside rest area/picnic spot. < ref name=fd/ >  The [[mural]] was painted by Bronwyn Bassett. < ref name=bhw/ >   
 
 The bushranger, [[Frederick Ward (bushranger)|Frederick Ward]], known as 'Thunderbolt',  robbed a Mr Matthews on the Breeza Plain in 1865. < ref name=fd/ >   
 
 Dow AgroSciences has established an agricultural research field station at Breeza which researches wheat breeds.
