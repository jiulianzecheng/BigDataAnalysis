 '''Collenberg''' is a community in the [[Miltenberg (district)|Miltenberg district]] in the [[Lower Franconia]] [[Regierungsbezirk|region]] in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. About 2,400 people live there. 
 
 == Geography == 
 
 === Location === 
 Collenberg is on the right edge of the [[Main (river)|Main]], 13 & nbsp;km away from [[Miltenberg]] and 18 & nbsp;km from [[Wertheim am Main|Wertheim]]. Along the Main between Collenberg and [[Freudenberg am Main|Freudenberg]] runs the boundary between Bavaria and [[Baden-Württemberg]]. Behind Collenberg are the wooded hills of the [[Spessart]]. 
 
 === Subdivisions=== 
 Collenberg's ''[[Ortsteil]]e'' are Fechenbach, Reistenhausen and Kirschfurt.
