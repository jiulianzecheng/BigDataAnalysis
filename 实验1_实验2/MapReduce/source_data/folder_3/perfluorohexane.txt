 '''Perfluorohexane''' ({{chem2|C6F14}}), or '''tetradecafluorohexane''', is a [[fluorocarbon]]. It is a derivative of [[hexane]] in which all of the [[hydrogen]] [[Atom|atoms]] are replaced by [[fluorine]] atoms. < ref > John A.
