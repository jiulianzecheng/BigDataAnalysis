 '''''Mespilus''''' is a genus of plants in the [[Rosaceae]] family. It contains two species, ''[[Mespilus germanica]]'' and ''[[Mespilus canescens]],'' though ''M. canescens'' is a hybrid between ''M. germanica'' and a ''[[Crataegus]]'' species, and is properly called ''[[× Crataemespilus]]'' ''canescens''.  < ref > {{Cite web|url=http://www.efloras.org/florataxon.aspx?flora_id=1 & taxon_id=120360|title=Mespilus in Flora of North America @ efloras.org|website=www.efloras.org|access-date=2020-08-08}} < /ref >
