 '''Raavitsa''' (also known as '''Raavitse''') is a village in [[Valga Parish]], [[Valga County]], in southeastern [[Estonia]]. < ref > {{EHAK|6702}} (retrieved 28 July 2021) < /ref >  It borders the town of [[Valga, Estonia|Valga]] in the west, [[Tartu]]–Valga railway in the northwest and Valga–[[Pechory]] railway in the south. Raavitsa has an unused station named  " Raavitse "  on Tartu–Valga line. < ref name= " population " > {{cite web|title=Karula valla arengukava 2004–2012|url=http://www.karula.ee/index.php?picfile=155|url-status=dead|publisher=Karula vald|pages=8|language=Estonian|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20110720125144/http://www.karula.ee/index.php?picfile=155|archivedate=2011-07-20}} < /ref >
