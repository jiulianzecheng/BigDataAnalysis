 '''Varzo''' is a ''[[comune]]'' (municipality) in the [[Province of Verbano-Cusio-Ossola]] in the [[Italy|Italian]] region [[Piedmont]]. 
 
 Varzo is about {{convert|130|km|mi}} northeast of [[Turin]] and about {{convert|35|km|mi}} northwest of [[Verbania]]. It is on the border with [[Switzerland]]. As of 2004 about 2,209 people lived there. It has an [[area]] of {{convert|94.4|km2|mi2}}. < ref name= " istat " > All demographics and other statistics: Italian statistical institute [[National Institute of Statistics (Italy)|Istat]]. < /ref > 
 
 Varzo borders the following municipalities: [[Baceno]], [[Bognanco]], [[Crevoladossola]], [[Crodo]], [[Grengiols]] (Switzerland), [[Ried-Brig]] (Switzerland), [[Trasquera]] and [[Zwischbergen]] (Switzerland).
