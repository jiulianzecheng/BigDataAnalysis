'''Pidurutalagala''' ({{lang-si|පිදුරුතලාගල}}), ({{lang-ta|பிதுருதலகல}}) is the highest mountain in [[Sri Lanka]]. It is located in the [[Central Province, Sri Lanka|Central Province]] of [[Sri Lanka]], just located inside [[Nuwara Eliya]], a city in [[Sri Lanka]]'s [[Central Province, Sri Lanka|Central Province]].
