 '''Dicastillo''' is a [[village]] in [[Navarre]]. There are 691 people in Dicastillo. [[Festival]]s are held on [[August]]. These festivals are celebrated on [[San Blas]], 3rd of [[February]], or on the [[Asparagu's day]], which is in [[spring]]. There is a [[music]] [[band]] called Banda Bizkarra, formed by 30 musicians.
