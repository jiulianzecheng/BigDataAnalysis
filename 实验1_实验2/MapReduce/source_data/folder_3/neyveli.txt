Neyveli is a town in [[Cuddalore district]], [[India]]n [[States and territories of India|state]] of [[Tamil Nadu]]. The town was developed in 1956 after the establishment (construction) of [[Neyveli Lignite Corporation]]. 
 ==History== 
 In 1935, the presence of black particles was discovered. It was found that the particles are Lignite. The Neyveli Lignite Corporation was formed by the Government of India in 1956. The mining of lignite was started in 1962. Later [[thermal power station]] was started in 1962 with the co-operation of [[U.S.S.R]]. 
 ==Population== 
 As per 2011 census, population of Neyveli is 105,687.The male and female population are 53,442 and 52,245 respectively. Average literacy rate of Neyveli city is 90.98%.
