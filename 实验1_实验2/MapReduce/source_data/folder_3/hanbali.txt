'''Hanbali''' ({{lang-ar|'''حنبلى '''}}) is one of the four schools ([[Madhhab]]s) of [[Fiqh]] or [[religious law]] within [[Sunni Islam]]. It is based on the interpretation of Ahmad ibn Hanbal and is influential in [[Saudi Arabia]].
