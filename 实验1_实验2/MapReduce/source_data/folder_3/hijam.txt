 '''Hijam''' is a [[Yumnak|Meetei/Meitei (Manipuri)'s royal surname or family name]] of the clan Luwang which has Northeast Indian origin. People of this surname mainly inhabits in [[Manipur]](India),Northeast Indian states, Bangladesh, Myanmar.
