 '''Borgomezzavalle''' is a ''[[comune]]'' in the [[Province of Verbano-Cusio-Ossola]] in the [[Italy|Italian]] region of [[Piedmont]]. It was created on 1 January 2016 from the former municipalities of [[Seppiana]] and [[Viganella]].
