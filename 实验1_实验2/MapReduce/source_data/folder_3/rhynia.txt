 '''''Rhynia''''' is a [[genus]] of [[Devonian|Early Devonian]] [[fossil]] [[land plant]]s. Only one [[species]] is known, ''R. gwynne-vaughanii''. The fossils are the [[sporophyte]] generation < ref name=edwards1980 > {{Cite journal |last=Edwards |first=D.S. |year=1980 |title=Evidence for the sporophytic status of the Lower Devonian plant ''Rhynia gwynne-vaughanii'' Kidston and Lang |journal=Review of Palaeobotany and Palynology |volume=29 |pages=177â€“188 |doi=10.1016/0034-6667(80)90057-3}} < /ref >  of a [[vascular plant]]. ''Rhynia'' had [[plant anatomy|anatomical]] features more advanced than those of the [[bryophyte]]s.
