 '''Spalt''' is a town in [[Roth (district)|Roth]], in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. It is 19 & nbsp;km southwest of [[Schwabach]]. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 Spalt is between [[Nuremberg]], [[Ansbach]], and [[Treuchtlingen]] in the north of the [[Franconia|Franconian lakelands]]. In the southwest of the city is the [[Great Brombachsee]] and its dam. The [[Fränkische Rezat]] flows through [[Georgensgmünd]] into the [[Rednitz]].
