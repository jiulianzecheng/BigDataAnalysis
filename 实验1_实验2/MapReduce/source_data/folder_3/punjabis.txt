 The '''Punjabi people''' ({{lang-pa|پنجابی}}, ਪੰਜਾਬੀ '''Panjabi people''') are an [[Aryan race|Indo-Aryan]] [[ethnic group]] native to the [[Punjab region]] in northwestern [[South Asia]] ([[Pakistan]] and [[India]]). They come from the [[Punjab region|Panjab region]], which has been home to some of the oldest civilizations in the world including one of the world's first and oldest civilizations, the [[Indus Valley Civilization]]. The Punjabi [[wikt:identity|identity]] is usually [[cultural]] and [[linguistic]], with Punjabis being those whose first language is [[Punjabi language|Panjabi]], an [[Indo-Aryan languages|Indo-European]] [[language]].
