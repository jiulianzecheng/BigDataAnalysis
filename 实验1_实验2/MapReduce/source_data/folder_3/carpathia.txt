'''Carpathia''' has several meanings: 
 
 *[[RMS Carpathia|RMS ''Carpathia'']] was a ship. It rescued some survivors from the sinking of the [[RMS Titanic]] on April 15, 1912 
 *[[Carpathian Mountains]], part of a mountain range in Europe 
 *There is also a mountain range on the moon called the [[Montes Carpatus]]. 
 *Carpathia, a fictional Balkan kingdom in the films ''[[The Prince and the Showgirl]]'', ''[[Ghostbusters II]]'', and ''[[Dragon Storm (film)|Dragon Storm]]'', as well as season 1, episode 14 of the television show ''[[Fantasy Island]]''. 
 *[[Nicolae Carpathia]], the fictional [[Antichrist]] and head of the Global Community in [[Left Behind]] 
 *[[Carpathian Ruthenia]], a small historic region in Central Europe 
 *Carpathia, a fictional planet in the British television series ''[[Outcasts (TV series)|Outcasts]]''
