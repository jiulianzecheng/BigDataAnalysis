 A '''boysenberry''' is an [[aggregate fruit]]. It has large [[seed]]s and a [[Maroon (colour)|maroon]] color. It is commonly used in [[jam]]s and [[pie]]s. When cooked into jams and pies, it changes color. 
 
 It is not a natural [[species]]: it is a cultivated [[variety (botany)|variety]].
