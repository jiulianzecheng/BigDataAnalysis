 '''''Cirsium''''' is a [[genus]] of [[flowering plant]]s in the [[Family (biology)|family]] [[Asteraceae]].  It is one of many genera with the common name being '''thistles'''.  Other genera known as thistles include ''Carduus'', ''Cynara'', ''Onopordon'', and ''Silybum''. 
 
 They are mostly [[wikt:native|native]] to [[Eurasia]] and northern [[Africa]].  There are about 60 < ref name= " fna " / >  species in [[North America]] (although many species have been [[naturalize]]d outside their native ranges). 
 
 Most species are [[wikt:consider|considered]] [[weed]]s.  ''Cirsium vulgare'' is listed as a weed in nine [[US]] states. < ref name='USDA Plant Database' > {{cite web |url=http://plants.usda.gov/java/profile?symbol=CIVU |title=PLANTS Profile for Cirsium vulgare (bull thistle)  USDA PLANTS |access-date=2008-07-15 |work=USDA Plant Database |publisher=USDA |archive-date=2013-07-02 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130702072705/http://plants.usda.gov/java/profile?symbol=CIVU |url-status=dead }} < /ref >   Some species are planted in [[garden]]s for their beauty and to attract [[butterfly|butterflies]].
