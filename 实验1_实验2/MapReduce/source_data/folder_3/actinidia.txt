 '''''Actinidia''''' is a genus of woody vines in the family [[Actinidiaceae]]. It is commercially important as many plants in this genus produce [[kiwifruits]], which are eaten in many parts of the world. < ref name= " NZKGI " > {{cite web |title=Industry – NZKGI |url=https://www.nzkgi.org.nz/industry |website=www.nzkgi.org.nz |publisher=New Zealand’s Kiwifruit Growers Industry |accessdate=10 August 2020}} < /ref > 
 
 The most well-known species is ''[[Actinidia deliciosa]]'', the common kiwifruit.
