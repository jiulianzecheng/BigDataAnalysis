 '''Pontevedra''' ({{IPA-es|ponteˈβeðɾa|lang}}) is a [[Spain|Spanish]] city in the north-west of the [[Iberian Peninsula]]. It is the capital of both the ''[[Pontevedra (comarca)|Comarca]]'' (County) and [[Province of Pontevedra]], and of the [[Rias Baixas]] in [[Galicia, Spain|Galicia]]. < ref > {{Cite web |url=http://www.pontevedra.eu/index.php/mod.pags/mem.detalle/idpag.82/idmenu.1022/chk.90d530bdad122dd6988bb366597f09b9.html |title=Concello de Pontevedra, Introduction to Pontevedra |access-date=2015-10-18 |archive-date=2008-12-25 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20081225184332/http://www.pontevedra.eu/index.php/mod.pags/mem.detalle/idpag.82/idmenu.1022/chk.90d530bdad122dd6988bb366597f09b9.html |url-status=dead }} < /ref >  As of 2014, the population was 82,946.
