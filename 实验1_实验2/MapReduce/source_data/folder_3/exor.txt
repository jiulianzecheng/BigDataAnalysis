 '''Exor N.V.''' is a [[holding company]] whick works under the [[Italy|Italian]] Agnelli family. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Global 500|url=https://fortune.com/global500/2020/|access-date=2021-06-14|website=Fortune|language=en}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite web|title=Letter to Shareholders {{!}} Exor - Interactive Financial Reports|url=http://exor-reports.message-asp.com/en/2017/annual-report-2017/letter-shareholders|access-date=2021-06-14|website=exor-reports.message-asp.com|archive-date=2018-07-25|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180725122849/http://exor-reports.message-asp.com/en/2017/annual-report-2017/letter-shareholders|url-status=dead}} < /ref >  It is headquartered in [[Amsterdam]], [[Netherlands]]. 
 
 == History == 
 Exor was founded on July 27, 1927 by Giovanni Agnelli. The first name was ''Istituto Finanziario Industriale.'' 
 
 In 1957, the company was acquired by IFI. 
 
 The [[France|French]] name ''Exor'' appeared in 2008 after the [[Mergers and acquisitions|merger]] of IFI and IFIL. < ref > {{Cite web|last=Rossant, John|date=16 December 1991|title= " The Agnellis buy their way into Europe '92 " .|url=http://www.businessweek.com/archives/1991/b324446.arc.htm|website=BusinessWeek}} < /ref >
