 '''''Bicentenaria''''' is a [[genus]] of [[carnivorous]] [[coelurosauria]]n [[theropod]]s which lived during the early [[Upper Cretaceous]] (about 90 million years ago) in what is now [[Argentina]]. < ref > Basckin, Deborah. 2012. New species of dinosaur displayed in Buenos Aires. ''BBC Nature''. [https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/world-latin-america-18611841] < /ref >  It contains only the [[type species]], ''Bicentenaria argentina''. Estimates suggest that it was {{convert|2.5|to|3|m|ft|0}} in length.
