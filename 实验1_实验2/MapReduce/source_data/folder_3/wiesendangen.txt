 '''Wiesendangen''' is a [[municipality]] of the [[district]] of [[Winterthur (district)|Winterthur]] of the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Zurich (canton)|Zurich]] in [[Switzerland]]. On 1 January 2014, the former municipality of [[Bertschikon bei Attikon]] merged into the municipality of Wiesendangen.
