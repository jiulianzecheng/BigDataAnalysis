 '''Klippan''' is an [[urban area]] in the [[counties of Sweden|county]] of [[Scania County|Scania]] in [[Sweden]]. It is the seat of [[Klippan Municipality]]. In [[September]] [[1995]], Klippan became famous for a [[racism|racist]] murder.
