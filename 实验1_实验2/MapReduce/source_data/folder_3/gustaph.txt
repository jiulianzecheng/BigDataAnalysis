 '''Stef Caers''' (born 5 July 1980), also known by his [[Stage name|stage names]] '''Steffen''' and later '''Gustaph''' (written '''GVSTΛPH''') is a [[Belgium|Belgian]] singer and songwriter. < ref > {{cite web|last1=Sande|first1=Wendy van de|date=10 January 2023|title=Maak kennis met 'Eurosong 2023'-kandidaat Gustaph:  " Ik wil vooral een positief signaal geven " |url=https://www.showbizz24.be/een/maak-kennis-met-eurosong-2023-kandidaat-gustaph-ik-wil-vooral-een-positief-signaal-geven-141651|access-date=15 January 2023|website=Showbizz24.be|language=nl}} < /ref > 
 
 He has released multiple songs and has done work as a voice coach < ref > {{cite web|last1=Sande|first1=Wendy van de|date=10 January 2023|title=Maak kennis met 'Eurosong 2023'-kandidaat Gustaph:  " Ik wil vooral een positief signaal geven " |url=https://www.showbizz24.be/een/maak-kennis-met-eurosong-2023-kandidaat-gustaph-ik-wil-vooral-een-positief-signaal-geven-141651|access-date=15 January 2023|website=Showbizz24.be|language=nl}} < /ref >  and backing vocalist for numerous singers including [[Eurovision Song Contest|Eurovision]] entries [[Hooverphonic]] and [[Sennek]]. 
 
 In 2023, Gustaph won ''[[Eurovision Song Contest 2023|Eurosong 2023]]'', a contest to choose Belgium's Eurovision entry. < ref > {{cite web|date=14 January 2023|title=Gustaph wins 'Eurosong 2023' in Belgium with 'Because of You'|url=https://eurovision.tv/story/gustaph-wins-eurosong-for-belgium|access-date=15 January 2023|website=Eurovision.tv|publisher=[[European Broadcasting Union|EBU]]}} < /ref > < ref > {{cite web|last1=Paelinck|first1=Gianni|date=8 November 2022|title=Nog eens Tom Dice op het Songfestival of wordt het Loredana? Deze 7 kandidaten wagen hun kans in Eurosong 2023|url=https://www.vrt.be/vrtnws/nl/2022/11/07/kandidaten-eurosong/|access-date=11 January 2023|website=[[VRT NWS]]|language=nl}} < /ref >  He represented Belgium in the [[Eurovision Song Contest 2023]] with the song  " Because of You " . He finished in 7th place.
