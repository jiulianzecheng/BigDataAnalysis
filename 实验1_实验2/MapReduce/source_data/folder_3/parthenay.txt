 '''Parthenay''' is a [[Communes of France|''commune'']], an ancient [[Defensive wall|fortified town]], in the [[Deux-Sèvres]] [[Departments of France|department]] in the [[Nouvelle-Aquitaine]] [[Regions of France|region]] in western [[France]]. It is a [[Prefectures in France|subprefecture]] of the Deux-Sèvres department. It is also the [[Capital city|capital]] of the ''[[Arrondissements of France|arrondissement]]'' of [[Arrondissement of Parthenay|Parthenay]]. 
 
 == Geography == 
 Parthenay is considered the capital of the ''Gâtine poitevine'' (or ''Gâtine de Parthenay''), a region with [[bocage]]s that is found in the central third of the department of Deux-Sèvres. It is at {{Convert|28|km|mi|abbr=on }} to the southeast of [[Bressuire]], the other subprefecture of the department. 
 
 The city is at about {{Convert|232|km|mi|abbr=on }} from [[Bordeaux]], {{Convert|380|km|mi|abbr=on }} from [[Paris]], {{Convert|871|km|mi|abbr=on }} from [[Marseille]] and {{Convert|468|km|mi|abbr=on }} from [[Toulouse]]. < ref name= " annuaire " > {{cite web |work=Annuaire-Mairie.fr |publisher= |title=Ville de Parthenay |url=http://www.annuaire-mairie.fr/ville-parthenay.html |language=French |accessdate=20 April 2017}} < /ref > 
 
 The ''commune'' has an area of {{Convert|11.4|km2|sqmi|abbr=on }}. < ref name= " insee-geo " > {{cite web |url=https://www.insee.fr/fr/statistiques/1405599?geo=COM-79202 |title=Commune de Parthenay (79202) |work=Comparateur de territoire |publisher=Institut national de la statistique et des études économiques - INSEE |format= |language=French |accessdate=19 April 2017 |date= }} < /ref >  Its average [[altitude]] is {{Convert|149|m|ft|abbr=on}}; at the [[City and town halls|city hall]], the altitude is {{Convert|169|m|ft|abbr=on }}. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://www.map-france.com/Parthenay-79200/ |title=Parthenay |publisher=Map-France.com |language= |accessdate=20 April 2017 |date= }} < /ref >

 == Administration == 
 Parthenay is a [[Prefectures in France|subprefecture]] of the Deux-Sèvres department, the capital of the ''arrondissement'' of [[Arrondissement of Parthenay|Parthenay]] and the [[administrative centre]] ({{lang-fr|chef-lieu}}) of the canton of [[Canton of Parthenay|Parthenay]]. 
 
 It is part of the [[Communes of France|intercommunality]] ''Parthenay-Gâtine'' ({{lang-fr|Communauté de communes de Parthenay-Gâtine}}).
