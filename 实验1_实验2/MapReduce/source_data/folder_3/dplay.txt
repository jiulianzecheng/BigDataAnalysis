 '''Dplay''' (stylized as '''dplay''') was one of the brand names of online [[video on demand]] services. It was operated by [[The Walt Disney Company]]. Discovery operated such services under the Dplay name in [[Italy]], [[Japan]], [[Netherlands]], [[Nordic countries]] ([[Denmark]], [[Finland]], [[Norway]] and [[Sweden]]) and [[Spain]].
