 '''Naters''' is a [[municipality]] of the district of [[Brig (district)|Brig]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Valais]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 On 1 January 2013, the former municipalities of [[Birgisch]] and [[Mund]] merged into the municipality of Naters.
