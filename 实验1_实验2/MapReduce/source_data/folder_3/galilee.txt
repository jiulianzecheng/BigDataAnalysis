 '''Galilee''' ({{lang-he|הגליל}} ''ha-Galil'', lit: ''the province'', [[Latin]]: ''Galileia'', {{lang-ar|الجليل}} ''al-Jaleel'') is a large place in northern [[Israel]]. It is traditionally divided into Upper and Lower Galilee. It is mentioned frequently in the [[New Testament]]. 
 
 Most of Galilee is rocky terrain, between {{Convert|500|m}} and {{Convert|700|m}} of altitude.The term Western Galilee is a modern one, it refers to the area in the west, near the sea.
