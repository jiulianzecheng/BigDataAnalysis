 '''Wicklow''' ({{lang-ga|Cill Mhantáin}}) is the [[county town]] of [[County Wicklow]] in [[Republic of Ireland|Ireland]]. In 2016, 10,584 people live in Wicklow. The [[River Vartry]] flows through the town.
