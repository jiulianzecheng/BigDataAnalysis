 '''Sigirino''' was a [[municipality]] of the district [[Lugano (district)|Lugano]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Ticino]] in [[Switzerland]]. On 21 November 2010, the former municipalities of Bironico, [[Camignolo]], [[Medeglia]], [[Rivera, Switzerland|Rivera]] and Sigirino merged into the new municipality of [[Monteceneri]]. < ref name=Fusion > [http://www.bfs.admin.ch/bfs/portal/de/index/infothek/nomenklaturen/blank/blank/gem_liste/03.html Amtliches Gemeindeverzeichnis der Schweiz] published by the Swiss Federal Statistical Office {{in lang|de}} accessed 14 January 2010 < /ref >
