'''Kehath''' was the second son of [[Levi]]. Kehath was the younger brother of [[Gershon]]  and the older brother of [[Merari]] . Kehath had four sons: [[Amram]], [[Izhar]] , [[Hebron (biblical figure)|Hebron]] and [[Uzziel]]. Amram became the father of [[Miriam]], [[Aaron]] and [[Moses]]. Izhar became the father of [[Korah]] , Nepheg and Zichri. Hebron became the father of the Hebronites. Uzziel became the father of Mishael, Elzaphan and Zithri.
