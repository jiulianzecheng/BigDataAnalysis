 '''DeviantArt''' (historically stylized as '''deviantART''') is an [[United States|American]] [[Online and offline|online]] [[online community|art community]] and [[website]]. It features [[art|artwork]], [[video|videography]] and [[photography]]. DeviantART was created on August 7, 2000 by [[Angelo Sotira]], Scott Jarkoff, and Matthew Stephens and others.
