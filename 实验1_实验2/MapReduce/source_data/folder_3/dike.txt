'''Dike''' or '''dyke''', can mean: 
 * A slang term for [[lesbian]] 
 * Another word for [[levee]], a long wall or bank built to hold water, or to separate land areas 
 **Dykes may have [[sluice gate]]s at intervals. They may be raised to let water through. 
 * [[Dyke (geology)]], where magma or sediment cut across normal rock formations 
 * Another word for [[diagonal pliers]], a hand tool used by [[electrician]]s and others 
 * Dike, the Greek goddess of moral justice, one of the [[Horae]]
