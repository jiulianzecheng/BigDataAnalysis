 '''Sartrouville''' is a [[France|French]] [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is in the [[Yvelines]] [[Departments of France|department]]. 
 
 About 51,967 people lived here in 2017. 
 
 Its inhabitants are called the ''Sartrouvillois''.
