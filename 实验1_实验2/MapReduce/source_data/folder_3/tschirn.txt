 '''Tschirn''' is a village in [[Kronach (district)|Kronach]] in [[Oberfranken]] ([[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]). In the [[Frankenwald]] mountain range, it incorporates the [[Ortsteil|sections]] Tschirn, Dobermühle and Gemeindeschneidmühle. The oldest historical record dates 1276.
