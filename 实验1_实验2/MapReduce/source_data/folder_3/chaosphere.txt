 '''''Chaosphere''''' is the third [[album]] by Swedish [[Heavy metal music|metal]] band [[Meshuggah]]. It was released on November 9 1998 by [[Nuclear Blast]], the band's [[record label]]. It is the band's only album with [[Bass guitar|bassist]] Gustaf Hielm. The band made a [[music video]] for the song  " New Millenium Cyanide Christ " .
