 '''Hattingen an der Ruhr''' is a city about 60,000 people in the state of [[North Rhine-Westphalia]] in [[Germany]]. It belongs to the [[Ruhr area]]. It is south of [[Bochum]] and [[Essen]]. Very famous is its ''Altstadt''.
