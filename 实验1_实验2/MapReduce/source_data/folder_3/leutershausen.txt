 '''Leutershausen''' ({{IPA-de|lɔʏ̯tɐshaʊ̯zən|3=De-Leutershausen.ogg}}; [[East Franconian]]: ''Laid < sub > ə < /sub > scháusn < ref > E. Fechter: ''Die Ortsnamen des Landkreises Ansbach'', S. 125. < /ref > '') is a [[municipality]] in [[Ansbach (district)|Ansbach]], a district ([[Districts of Germany|''Landkreis'']]) in [[Middle Franconia]]. It is on the river [[Altmühl]], {{convert|12|km|abbr=off}} west of [[Ansbach]]. 
 
 == Notable people == 
 * [[Gustave Whitehead]], German-American aviation pioneer 
 * [[Christopher McNaughton]], German basketball player, playing for the German national team 
 * Paula Kissinger (née Stern), mother of American [[Secretary of State]] and [[Nobel Prize]] recipient [[Henry Kissinger]]
