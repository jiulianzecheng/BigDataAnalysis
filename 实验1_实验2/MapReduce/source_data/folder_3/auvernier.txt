 '''Auvernier''' was a [[municipality]] and village in the district of [[Boudry (district)|Boudry]]. It is in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Neuchâtel (canton)|Neuchâtel]] in [[Switzerland]]. On 1 January 2013, former municipalities of Auvernier, [[Bôle]], and [[Colombier, Neuchâtel|Colombier]] joined together. They became the new municipality of [[Milvignes]]. < ref name=Fusion > [http://www.bfs.admin.ch/bfs/portal/de/index/infothek/nomenklaturen/blank/blank/gem_liste/03.html Amtliches Gemeindeverzeichnis der Schweiz] published by the Swiss Federal Statistical Office {{in lang|de}} accessed 2 January 2013 < /ref >
