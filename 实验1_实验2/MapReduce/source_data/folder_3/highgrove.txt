'''Highgrove House''' is the country home of [[Charles, Prince of Wales|HRH The Prince of Wales]], in [[Gloucestershire]], [[England]]. It is in Doughton, near [[Tetbury]]. 
 
 Highgrove House was bought in 1980 by the [[Duchy of Cornwall]]. The Duchy also manages the estate surrounding the house.
