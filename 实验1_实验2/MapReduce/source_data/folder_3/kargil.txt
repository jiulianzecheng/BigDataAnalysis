'''Kargil''' ([[Ladakhi language|Ladakhi]]: ཀར་གིལ་; {{lang-ur| کرگل }}) is a town in the [[Kargil District]] in [[Jammu and Kashmir]] in [[India]]. It is the second largest town in Ladakh after [[Leh]]. It is located 60 & nbsp;km and 204 & nbsp;km from [[Drass]] and [[Srinagar]] to the west.
