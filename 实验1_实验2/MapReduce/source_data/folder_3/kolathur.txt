'''Kolathur''' ({{lang-ta|கொளத்தூர்}}) is a town in [[Salem district]] in the [[India]]n [[States and territories of India|state]] of [[Tamil Nadu]]. As of the 2001 India [[census]], Kolathur had a population of 10,319. Males make up 53% of the population and females 47%. A total of 9% of the population is under 6 years of age.
