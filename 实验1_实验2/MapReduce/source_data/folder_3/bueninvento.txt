 '''''Bueninvento''''' ( " Goodinvention " ) is the second studio album by Mexican singer-songwriter [[Julieta Venegas]] in 2000. The album was nominated for [[Latin Grammy Award for Best Rock Solo Vocal Album]]. < ref > '2nd Latin Grammy SGAE Nomenees', ''Billboard'', Vol. 113, No. 32 (11 August 2001), p. 44 < /ref >  It was named the 4th best album of the 2000s decade by Latin music website [[Club Fonograma]]. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://www.clubfonograma.com/search/label/best%20of%20the%20decade?updated-max=2010-02-12T02:52:00-07:00 & max-results=20 & start=1 & by-date=false |title=best of the decade |publisher=club fonograma |access-date=2012-03-21 |archive-date=2015-09-23 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150923204912/http://www.clubfonograma.com/search/label/best%20of%20the%20decade?updated-max=2010-02-12T02:52:00-07:00 & max-results=20 & start=1 & by-date=false |url-status=dead }} < /ref >
