 '''Alumpeedika''' is a small village/hamlet of [[Oachira]] in the [[Kollam district]] of [[Kerala]], [[India]]. It comes under Clappana Panchayath and belongs to South Kerala division. It is located 31 & nbsp;km North of the district headquarters, [[Kollam]], 1 & nbsp;km from Oachira, and 101 & nbsp;km from the state capital, [[Thiruvananthapuram]]. Nearby towns include [[Kayamkulam]], Oachira, and [[Karunagappally]]. Alumpeedika is near the border of the Kollam and Alappuzha districts. It is near the [[Arabian Sea]]. 
 
 == Transportation == 
 [[Ochira railway station|Ochira]] and [[Kayamkulam Junction railway station|Kayamkulam Junction]] train stations are nearby; however, [[Quilon Junction railway station]] is 32 & nbsp;km from Alumpeedika.
