 '''Marquartstein''' is a municipality in the southeastern part of [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]] and is part of the Verwaltungsgemeinschaft Marquartstein and [[Staudach-Egerndach]]. It is in a region called [[Chiemgau]], about 10 & nbsp;km south of [[Chiemsee|Lake Chiemsee]] between [[Munich]] and [[Salzburg]]. Most of the area is in the valley of the river [[Tiroler Achen]], which separates the village into two parts. Marquartstein is at the edge of the Alps.
