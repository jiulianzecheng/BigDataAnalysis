 '''Casablanca''' (classical [[Arabic language|Arabic]] name: '''الدار البيضاء''',  " the white house " ; [[Spanish language|Spanish]]:'''Casablanca''',  " whitehouse " ) is a [[city]] in western [[Morocco]], on the [[Atlantic Ocean]].  
 
 With 2.95 [[million]] people (September 2004 [[census]]), Casablanca is Morocco's biggest city.
