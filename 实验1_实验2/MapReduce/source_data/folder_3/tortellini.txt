 '''Tortellini''' is a type of stuffed [[pasta]]. It originated from the [[Italy|Italian]] city of [[Bologna]]. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Tortellini, The Dumpling Inspired By Venus' Navel|url=https://www.npr.org/sections/thesalt/2013/08/27/210555719/tortellini-the-dumpling-inspired-by-venus-navel|access-date=2021-05-13|website=NPR.org|language=en}} < /ref >  It is in a ring shape, with a filling inside. Anything can go in the filling, including [[meat]], [[cheese]] (commonly [[Parmesan cheese|parmesan]] or [[Pecorino cheese|pecorino]]), [[Egg (food)|egg]], etc. It is in a fresh form, so it cooks quick, but the shelf life is short.
