 '''Buchenbach''' is a municipality in the southwest of the [[Black Forest]] in [[Germany]]. It is in [[Breisgau-Hochschwarzwald]] in [[Baden-Württemberg]]. It is made up of four communities, the main town of Buchenbach and the villages of Falkensteig, Unteribental and Wagensteig.
