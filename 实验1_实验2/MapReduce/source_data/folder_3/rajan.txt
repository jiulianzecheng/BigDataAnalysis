'''Rajan''' is the name or the honor given to the  " ''Kings or Prince'' " . '''Rajan''' stand for the  " ''Great King'' "  in [[Sanskrit]] are discussed in noncommittal terms of their duties as rulers, who  " ''protect, serve and patronize the arts'' " . 
 
 == Mythological history == 
 According to several mythological epics like the ''[[Mahabharata]]'', ''[[Ramayana]]'', etc., the Rajan name is given as the honor to the Kings like [[Rama]], [[Dasharatha]] etc. < ref > {{cite book|title=A son, a prince, a husband, a king : Ramayan - an interpretation.|date=2012|publisher=Busbridge|isbn=978-0956985019|page=229|edition=Rev.|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=0fswoq55oC0C & dq=Ramayan+rajan & pg=PA229}} < /ref >
