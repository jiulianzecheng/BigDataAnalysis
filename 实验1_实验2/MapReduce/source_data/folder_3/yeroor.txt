 '''Yeroor''' is a village near [[Anchal]], [[Kollam district]], [[Kerala]], [[India]]. 
 
 == Politics == 
 Yeroor is a part of [[Punalur]] assembly constituency in [[Kollam (Lok Sabha constituency)]]. Shri. [[K. Raju]] is the current MLA of Punalur. Shri.[[N. K.
