'''Hooters''' is the registered [[trademark]]. Two [[United States|American]] [[restaurant]] chains use it: '''Hooters, Inc.''', based in [[Clearwater, Florida]] and '''Hooters of America, Inc.''' in [[Atlanta]] [[Georgia (U.S. state)|Georgia]]. 
 
 [[Waiter|Waiting staff]] at the Hooters restaurants are mainly young women. The women are usually called  " Hooters Girls " . The [[sex appeal]] of the women is a mai component for the [[company]]'s image. Hooters hires men and women as cooks, hosts (at some [[franchising|franchises]]) and [[manager]]s. < ref > {{cite web|title=Women Against a Violent Environment|publisher=Rochester NOW|url=http://www.rochesternow.org/hooters.html|access-date=May 15, 2021|archive-date=October 2, 2010|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20101002055229/http://www.rochesternow.org/hooters.html|url-status=bot: unknown}} < /ref > 
 
 The [[menu]] at Hooters includes [[steak]]s, [[chicken]] wings, [[seafood]], [[hamburgers]] and [[sandwich]]es. 
 
 Hooters was incorporated in April 1983. They opened their doors in October.
