 '''Awana''' (an [[acronym]] for '''A'''pproved '''W'''orkmen '''A'''re '''N'''ot '''A'''shamed, from [[Second Epistle to Timothy|2 Timothy]] 2:15), is an [[international]] [[evangelicalism|evangelical]] [[non-profit organization]]. It was founded in 1950. Its headquarters is in [[Streamwood, Illinois]].
