 '''''Yersinia''''' is a [[bacterium]] in the large [[family (biology)|family]] of bacteria, [[Enterobacteriaceae]]. ''[[Yersinia pestis]]'' is the type genus. ''Yersinia pestis'' is also the cause of the [[bubonic plague]], one of the most dreaded diseases of the 18th century. ''Yersinia enterocolitica'' causes the disease yersiniosis. < ref > {{cite web |url= https://www.cdc.gov/ncidod/dbmd/diseaseinfo/yersinia_g.htm |title=Disease Listing, Yersinia enterocolitica, General Info  & #124; CDC Bacterial, Mycotic Diseases |first= |last= |work=cdc.gov |year=2011 |quote=Yersinia enterocolitica  |accessdate=5 July 2011}} < /ref >
