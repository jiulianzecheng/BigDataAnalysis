'''Adrienne''' is a [[female]] [[given name|first name]]. It is the female form of [[Adrien]]. The name can be also spelled '''Adrianne'''.The pronunciation of the name is [ '''ey'''-dree-en, -''uh''n; ''French'' a-dree-'''en''' ].  < ref > {{Cite web|title=Definition of Adrienne {{!}} Dictionary.com|url=https://www.dictionary.com/browse/adrienne|access-date=2023-03-26|website=www.dictionary.com|language=en}} < /ref >  This is a unique name (not in the top 1000 newborn names in the US in 2021). < ref > {{Cite web|last=OACT|title=Popular Baby Names|url=https://www.ssa.gov/cgi-bin/popularnames.cgi|access-date=2023-03-26|website=www.ssa.gov}} < /ref > 
 
 The meaning of the name is  " from the city of Hadria. "  It also means  " the dark one. " < ref > {{Cite web|title=Adrienne name Meaning and Origin|url=https://babys.name/detail-Adrienne-Meaning-Origin-Famous-Similar-Sibling.html|access-date=2023-03-26|website=babys.name}} < /ref > 
 
 There are a few [[fictional character]]s with the name, such as Adrienne Johnson from ''[[Days of our Lives]]''.
