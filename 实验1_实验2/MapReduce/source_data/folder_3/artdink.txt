 {{nihongo|'''Artdink Corproation'''|Artdink|Arutodinku}} was the Japanese [[video game industry|video game]] developers, based in [[Tsukishima]], [[Tokyo]]. 
 
 The company began in June 21, 1986, and it was founded by '''Tatsuo Nagahama'''. The Artdink spiritual successor series ''[[A-Train]]'' series (better known as ''A Ressha de Ikou'' in Japan) released for the [[Microsoft Windows|PC]] and [[Amiga]], which was published by ''[[SimCity]]'' creators [[Maxis]]. 
 
 The other games developed by Artdink is; ''[[Aquanaut's Holiday]]'' ([[Sony Interactive Entertainment|Sony]]), ''[[Carnage Heart]]'' (Sony), ''[[Turnabout (video game)|Turnabout]]'' ([[Natsume (company)|Natsume]]/Zoo Digital), ''[[Tail of The Sun]]'' (Sony), ''[[A-Train|AIV: Global Evolution]]'' (Maxis), and ''[[No One Canm Stop Mr. Domino!]]'' ([[Acclaim Entertainment|Acclaim]]/[[JVCKenwood Victor Entertainment#Video games|JVC Music Europe]]) for the [[PlayStation]]. 
 
 In 2022, Artdink was joined together with [[Square Enix]], that released new title ''[[Triangle Strategy]]'' for [[Nintendo Switch]].
