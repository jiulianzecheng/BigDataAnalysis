'''Pyrolysis''' is the [[chemical breakdown]] of [[Chemical compound|compound]]s, which contain [[carbon]], by [[heat]]. Pyrolysis happens under pressure and if the temperature is above 430 °C (800 °F). It is normally used for [[Organic chemistry|organic]] things, and is involved in charring.
