 '''Kymenlaakso''' is a region in [[Finland]]. As of November 2013, there were over 180,000 people living there. Neighbouring regions are [[Uusimaa]], [[Päijänne Tavastia]], [[Southern Savonia]] and [[South Karelia]]. 
 
 Municipalities in the region are [[Hamina]], [[Iitti]], [[Kotka]], [[Kouvola]], [[Miehikkälä]], [[Pyhtää]] and [[Virolahti]]. Kymenlaakso was one most earliest [[industry|industrialized]] areas in Finland, since around the 1800s.
