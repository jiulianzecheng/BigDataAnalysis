 '''Zolling''' is a municipality in the [[Bavaria]]n district of [[Freising]]. 
 Associated villages are: Anglberg, Flitzing, Thann, Palzing, [[Oberappersdorf]], and Appersdorf. 
 
 Zolling is 6 & nbsp;km to the north of Freising.
