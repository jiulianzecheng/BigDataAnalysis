 A '''mews''' is a row or courtyard of [[stable]]s and [[carriage house]]s with living spaces above them, built behind large city houses. The word mews comes from the [[Royal Mews]] in London, England. < ref > Samuel Weller Singer, ed., ''The Dramatic Works of William Shakespeare'', vol.
