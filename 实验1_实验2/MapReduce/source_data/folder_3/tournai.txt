 '''Tournai''' is a [[municipality]] in the [[Belgium|Belgian]] province of [[Hainaut]], [[Wallonia]]. 
 [[File:Tournai JPG02.jpg|thumb|right|The Grand Place and its medieval belfry (XIIIth century)]] 
 
 In 2007, 67844 people lived there. < ref > {{Cite web |url=http://statbel.fgov.be/downloads/pop200701com.xls |title=Werkelijke bevolking per gemeente op 1 januari 2007 / Population de droit par commune au 1 janvier 2007 (.xls) |access-date=2008-07-15 |archive-date=2008-11-23 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20081123144002/http://statbel.fgov.be/downloads/pop200701com.xls |url-status=dead }} < /ref >
