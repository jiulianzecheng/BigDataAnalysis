'''Mundamia''' is a suburb in the [[City of Shaolhaven]] in [[South Coast]] in [[New South Wales]] in [[Australia]]. < ref > http://www3.shoalhaven.nsw.gov.au/Section94/Document1.aspx?ProjectCode=01ROAD0146 < /ref > 
 
 It is about 5 & nbsp;km west of [[Nowra]] by road. < ref > https://dcp2014.shoalhaven.nsw.gov.au/sites/default/files/DCP2014AmendmentNo5_ChapterNB1_MundamiaUrbanReleaseArea.pdf < /ref > 
 
 In 2016, 70 people lived there. < ref > {{cite web | url=https://quickstats.censusdata.abs.gov.au/census_services/getproduct/census/2016/quickstat/ssc12837 | title=2016 Mundamia, Census All persons QuickStats  & #124; Australian Bureau of Statistics | access-date=2021-01-03 | archive-date=2018-07-29 | archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180729092915/http://quickstats.censusdata.abs.gov.au/census_services/getproduct/census/2016/quickstat/SSC12837 | url-status=dead }} < /ref >
