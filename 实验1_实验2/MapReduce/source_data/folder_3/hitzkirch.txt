 '''Hitzkirch''' is a [[municipality]] of the [[district]] of [[Hochdorf (district)|Hochdorf]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Lucerne (canton)|Lucerne]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 On 1 January 2009, [[Gelfingen]], [[Hämikon]], [[Mosen, Lucerne|Mosen]], [[Müswangen]], [[Retschwil]] and [[Sulz, Lucerne|Sulz]] joined Hitzkirch.
