 '''iKON''' is a [[South Korean]] [[boy-band]] formed by YG Entertainment, currently consisting of 7 members. iKON was first introduced on WIN: Who is Next, resulting in their debut as a full group with the song,   " My Type. "   " My Type "  charted at  #1 and #3 on the Korean and US charts, respectively.
