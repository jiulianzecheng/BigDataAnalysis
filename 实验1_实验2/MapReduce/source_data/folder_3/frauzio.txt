 '''Frauzio''' was a [[comic book]] [[Brazil]] floor monthly, created in [[2001]] by [[Marcatti]] distributed by [[Editora Escala|Publisher SCALE]]. 
 
 Was launched with a circulation of 30 thousand copies and distributed at newsstands around the [[Brazil]], became the first magazine [[underground]] to be published in an industrial scale worldwide. 
 
 In [[2003]], published four editions of the magazine '''Misadventures of Frauzio''' by '''Publisher PRO-C'''. In the same year the album special edition is published by Frauzio '''Opera Graphica'''. The book, under the title'''Question of paternity''', brings a Frauzio adventure inspired by the book [[Lolita]] of [[Nabokov]].
