 '''Dobromyl''' ({{lang-uk|Добромиль}}; {{lang-ru|Добромыль}}; {{lang-pl|Dobromil}}) is a city in [[Sambir Raion]], [[Lviv Oblast]], [[Ukraine]]. It is about 5 kilometers from the border with [[Poland]]. In 2021, 4,168 people lived there.
