 '''Einsiedeln''' is a [[municipality]] and [[Districts of Switzerland#Canton of Schwyz|district]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Canton of Schwyz|Schwyz]] in [[Switzerland]]. It is known for its monastery, the Benedictine [[Einsiedeln Abbey]], founded in the 10th century. 
 
 == Villages == 
 The municipality has six villages: [[Bennau]], [[Egg, Schwyz|Egg]], [[Willerzell]], [[Euthal]], [[Gross, Schwyz|Gross]] and [[Trachslau]]. The village of [[Biberbrugg]] is shared with the municipality of [[Feusisberg]].
