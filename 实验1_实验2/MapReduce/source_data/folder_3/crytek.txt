 '''Crytek GmbH''' is a German [[video game developer]] and [[software developer]] based in [[Frankfurt]]. It was founded by the Yerli brothers in [[Coburg]] in 1999 and moved to Frankfurt in 2006, Crytek also have studios in [[Kyiv]] and [[Istanbul]]. < ref > {{cite web|title=Crytek closes five studios after rough year|url=http://kotaku.com/crytek-closes-five-studios-after-rough-year-1790315044|url-status=live|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20161221000445/http://kotaku.com/crytek-closes-five-studios-after-rough-year-1790315044|archive-date=21 December 2016|access-date=20 December 2016}} < /ref > 
 
 == Subsidiaries == 
 * Crytek Kiev in [[Kyiv]], [[Ukraine]] — founded in 2006. 
 * Crytek Istanbul in [[Istanbul]], [[Turkey]] — founded in 2012.
