A '''targum''' ({{lang-he|תרגום}}, plural: ''targumim'') is an [[Aramaic language|Aramaic]] [[translation]] of the [[Hebrew Bible]] ([[Tanakh]]) that was written or compiled in [[Palestine]] or in [[Babylonia]] from the [[Second Temple]] period until the early [[Middle Ages]] (late first millennium).  
 
 Targum also means [[translation]] or [[interpretation]]. In [[Arabic language|Arabic]] Targem (verb) means to translate or interpret. (Mutargem = Translator/Interpreter).
