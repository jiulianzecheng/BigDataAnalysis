'''Felixstowe''' is a town in [[Suffolk]], [[England]]. It has the largest [[containerization|container ports]] in Europe. < ref > {{Cite web |url=http://www.visitfelixstowe.co.uk/factsandfigures_listing.php?cid=1 |title=About Felixstowe - Facts, Figures, Postcards, Weather, Maps, history, and location |access-date=2021-01-19 |archive-date=2009-05-16 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20090516015937/http://visitfelixstowe.co.uk/factsandfigures_listing.php?cid=1 |url-status=dead }} < /ref > 
 
 About 30,000 people live in Felixstowe.
