 '''Kemnath''' ({{IPA-de|ˈkɛm.naːt|lang|De-Kemnath.ogg}}) is a small town in [[Tirschenreuth (district)|Tirschenreuth]], in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. It is near the [[Fichtelgebirge]], 24 & nbsp;km southeast of [[Bayreuth]]. The town's motto is  " das Tor zur Oberpfalz, "  which translates into English as  " The Door to the Upper Palatinate.
