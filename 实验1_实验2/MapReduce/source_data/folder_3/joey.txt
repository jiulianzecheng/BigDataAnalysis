'''Joey''' has the following meanings: 
 * Joey is a common [[nickname]] for Joseph, Joe, Joanne, Joan, or Jo.  
 * A [[joey (marsupial)]] is a baby kangaroo, or a baby of other marsupials (animals that are like kangaroos). 
 * ''[[Joey (TV series)|Joey]]'' is a television [[sitcom]] (a comedy set in a situation) starring [[Matt LeBlanc]] as [[Joey Tribbiani|Joey tribbani]] his character from ''[[Friends]]''.
