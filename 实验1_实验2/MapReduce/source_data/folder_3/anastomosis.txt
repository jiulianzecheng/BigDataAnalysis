 An '''anastomosis''' is a [[Surgery|surgical]] connection between two structures. It usually means a connection that is created between tubular structures, such as [[Blood vessel|blood vessels]] or loops of intestine. For example, when part of an [[intestine]] is surgically removed, the two remaining ends are sewn or stapled together (anastomosed).
