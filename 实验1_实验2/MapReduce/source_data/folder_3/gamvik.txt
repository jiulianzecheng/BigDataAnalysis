 '''Gamvik''', or ''Gáŋgaviikka'', is a municipality in [[Norway]]'s northernmost county. 
 
 Settlements include [[Mehamn]]. 
 
 ==Administration== 
 The [[municipal council]] is elected until late 2027.

 *1 from [[Centre Party (Norway)|Centre party]] 
 *1 from [[Conservative Party (Norway)|Conservative party]] 
 
 
 (13 council members were elected during the [[2023 Norwegian local elections]].)
