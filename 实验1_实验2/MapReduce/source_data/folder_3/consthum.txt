 '''Consthum''' ({{lang-lb|Konstem}}) is a former [[Communes of Luxembourg|commune]] in [[Luxembourg]]. It was part of the [[Clervaux (canton)|canton of Clervaux]]. 
 
 On 1 January 2012, the commune was merged with [[Hoscheid]] and [[Hosingen]] to form the new commune of [[Parc Hosingen]].
