 '''Goldikova''' (foaled 15 March 2005  & ndash; 5 January 2021) was a [[champion]] [[Thoroughbred]] [[horse racing|racemare]]. She has also raced in the [[United States]] and [[England]]. She has won 14 [[Group One]] races, with nine victories over colts and [[gelding]]s. Goldikova was the only horse to win three (2008, < ref > {{Cite web|url=http://www.breederscup.com/node/39401|title=2008 Breeders' Cup Mile {{!}} Breeders' Cup|website=www.breederscup.com|access-date=2018-11-18}} < /ref >  2009, < ref > {{Cite web|url=http://www.breederscup.com/year/2009|title=2009 {{!}} Breeders' Cup|website=www.breederscup.com|access-date=2018-11-18}} < /ref >  2010 < ref > {{Cite web|url=http://www.horse-races.net/library/bc10-resultsmm.htm|title=2010 Breeders' Cup Mile Results|website=www.horse-races.net|access-date=2018-11-18}} < /ref > )  [[Breeders' Cup Mile]] races.
