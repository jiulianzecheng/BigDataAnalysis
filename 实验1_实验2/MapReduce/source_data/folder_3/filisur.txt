 '''Filisur''' ({{lang-rm|Filisour}}) was a [[municipality]] of the [[district]] [[Albula (district)|Albula]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Graubünden]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 The village  sits on a hillside with a view to the west where the two rivers [[Albula (river)|Albula/Alvra]] from the [[Albula Pass]] and [[Landwasser]] from [[Davos]] meet. 
 
 On 1 January 2018, the former municipalities of [[Bergün/Bravuogn]] and Filisur merged into the new municipality of [[Bergün Filisur]].
