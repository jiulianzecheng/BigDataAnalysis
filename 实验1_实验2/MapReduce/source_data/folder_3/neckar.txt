The '''Neckar''' ([[Swabian German|Swabian]]: ''Negger'') is a 367 km long river in the South-West of Germany, in the state of [[Baden-Württemberg]]. Rising in the [[Black Forest]], it flows through a steep valley in the Odenwald hills and passes through the cities of [[Tübingen]] (University), [[Nürtingen]], [[Esslingen]], [[Stuttgart]] (capitol of [[Baden-Württemberg]], and [[Heidelberg]] (University). In [[Mannheim]] the Neckar joins the [[river]] [[Rhine]].
