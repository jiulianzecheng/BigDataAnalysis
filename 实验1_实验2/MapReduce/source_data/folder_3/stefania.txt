 '''Stefania Liberakakis''' (born 17 December 2002), known by her first name '''Stefania''', is a [[Greece|Greek]] singer and actress who was born in the [[Netherlands]]. She has released over 20 singles and appeared in several films and TV shows. 
 
 In 2013, Stefania was a contestant on the Dutch version of [[The Voice (TV series)|''The Voice'']]. In 2016, she was part of the girl group Kisses, who represented the Netherlands at the [[Junior Eurovision Song Contest 2016]]. The group finished 8th out of 17 entries.
