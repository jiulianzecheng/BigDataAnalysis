 '''Alushta''' ({{lang-uk|Алушта}}; {{lang-crh|Aluşta}}) is a resort town in [[Republic of Crimea|Crimea]], [[Ukraine]]. It is on the [[Black Sea]]. In 2014, 29,078 people lived there.
