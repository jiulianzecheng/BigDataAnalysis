 '''Nagoya''' is one of the largest [[city|cities]] in [[Japan]], with over 2 and a quarter million people in 2010. It is in [[Aichi Prefecture]]. < ref > Nussbaum, Louis-Frédéric. (2005). [https://books.google.com/books?id=p2QnPijAEmEC & pg=PA685 & dq=  " Nagoya " ] in ''Japan Encyclopedia'', p. 685. < /ref >  There are 16 wards in Nagoya. 
 
 ==Tourist attractions== 
 * Nagoya castle: It was built in 1615 by Japan's famous military commander, Ieyasu Tokugawa. It is one of Japan's most famous castles. The gold dolphin-like fishes on the roof are its main feature. It is said that the total weight of the gold used is 215.3 & nbsp;kilograms. 
 
 *Nagoya City Science Museum: located in  Shirakawa park in Naka-ku, Nagoya-shi.  It reopened in 2011. Its planetarium is 35 meters in diameter. It is the largest in the world.
