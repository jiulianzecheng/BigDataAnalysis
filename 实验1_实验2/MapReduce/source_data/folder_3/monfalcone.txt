 '''Monfalcone''' ({{lang-fur|Monfalcon}}; {{lang-sl|Tržič}}) is a city in northern [[Italy]]. Monfalcone is in the [[Friuli-Venezia Giulia]] Region. About 30,000 people live there. 
 
 It is the northernmost port city on the [[Mediterranean Sea]].
