 '''Musopen''' is an online library of [[public domain]] music recordings and sheet music, launched by Aaron Dunn in 2005. It aims to  " set music free "  through providing music to the public free of charge, without copyright restrictions. 
 
 In 2008, Musopen released newly-commissioned recordings of the 32 [[Ludwig van Beethoven|Beethoven]] piano sonatas into the public domain. < ref > {{cite web |url=https://arstechnica.com/old/content/2008/05/musopen-puts-classical-recordings-scores-in-public-domain.ars |title=Musopen puts classical recordings, scores in public domain |publisher=Ars Technica |date=22 May 2008}} < /ref >
