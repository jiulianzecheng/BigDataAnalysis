'''Woodbridge''' is a town near the [[A12 road]] in [[Suffolk Coastal]], [[Suffolk]], England. In 2001 there were 7,368 people living in Woodbridge. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.ons.gov.uk/help/localstatistics|title=Local statistics - Office for National Statistics|website=www.ons.gov.uk}} < /ref >
