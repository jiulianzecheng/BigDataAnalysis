 '''Sinsheim''' is a town in [[Rhein-Neckar-Kreis|Rhein-Neckar]] in [[Baden-Württemberg]] in southwestern [[Germany]]. It is known for its [[Association football|football]] club [[TSG 1899 Hoffenheim]] which plays in the [[Bundesliga]]. They have played its home stadium at the [[Rhein-Neckar-Arena]].
