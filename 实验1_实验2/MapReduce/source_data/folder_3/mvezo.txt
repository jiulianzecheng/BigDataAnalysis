 '''Mvezo''' is a small village in Umtata (now [[Mthatha]]). It is by the [[Mbashe River]] in the Eastern Cape of [[South Africa]]. The village is known as being the birthplace of former [[President of South Africa|South African president]] [[Nelson Mandela]]. Mandela was born in Mvezo on July 18, 1918. < ref > [https://www.reuters.com/article/2013/06/28/us-mandela-graves-idUSBRE95R0V920130628?feedType=RSS & virtualBrandChannel=11563 Grave dispute divides family as Mandela lies in hospital] {{Webarchive|url=https://archive.today/20130630231505/http://www.reuters.com/article/2013/06/28/us-mandela-graves-idUSBRE95R0V920130628?feedType=RSS & virtualBrandChannel=11563 |date=2013-06-30 }} at Reuters.com < /ref >
