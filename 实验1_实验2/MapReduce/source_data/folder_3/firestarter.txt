 '''''Firestarter''''' is a 1984 American science fiction horror movie directed by [[Mark L. Lester]] and was based on the 1980 novel of the same name by [[Stephen King]]. It stars [[Drew Barrymore]], [[David Keith]], [[Freddie Jones]], [[Heather Locklear]], [[George C. Scott]], [[Martin Sheen]], [[Art Carney]], [[Louise Fletcher]] and was distributed by [[Universal Pictures]]. It will be remade in 2022.
