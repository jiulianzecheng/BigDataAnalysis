'''Ichthys''' is an [[affix]] used in [[fish]]. ''[[Leedsichthys]]'', the largest [[ray-finned fish]], is an example of a fish having this [[affix]]. Other examples include ''[[Amakusaichthys]]'' and ''[[Goodrichthys]]''.
