 '''Gresso''' was a [[municipality]] of the district [[Locarno (district)|Locarno]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Ticino]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 == History == 
 The municipality was made in 1882 by splitting from the municipality of [[Vergeletto]]. On 10 April 2016, the former municipalities of Gresso, [[Isorno]], [[Mosogno]] and [[Vergeletto]] merged to form the municipality of [[Onsernone]]. < ref > [http://www.bfs.admin.ch/bfs/portal/de/index/infothek/nomenklaturen/blank/blank/gem_liste/03.html Amtliches Gemeindeverzeichnis der Schweiz] published by the Swiss Federal Statistical Office {{in lang|de}} accessed 27 April 2016 < /ref >
