 '''Freilassing''' ({{IPA-de|fʁaɪˈlasɪŋ}}), until 1923 '''Salzburghofen''', is a [[Town#Germany|town]] in [[Berchtesgadener Land]], a district ([[Districts of Germany|''Landkreis'']]) in [[Upper Bavaria]]. Freilassing can be seen as the biggest suburb of [[Salzburg]].
