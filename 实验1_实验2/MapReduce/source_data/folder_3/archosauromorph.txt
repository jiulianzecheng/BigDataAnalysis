'''Archosauromorpha''' is a [[clade]] of [[diapsid]] [[reptile]]s containing all reptiles more closely related to [[archosaur]]s than to [[Lepidosauria|lepidosaurs]]. 
 
 Lepidosaurs include [[tuatara]]s, [[lizard]]s, and [[snake]]s. 
 
 Archosauromorphs include [[turtles]], [[crocodiles]], [[dinosaur]]s and [[bird]]s. 
 
 *For a more detailed account, see [[Archosaur]].
