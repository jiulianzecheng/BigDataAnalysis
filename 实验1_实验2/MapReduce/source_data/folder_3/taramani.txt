'''Taramani''' is an area in the metropolitan area of [[Chennai]], [[Tamil Nadu]]. It is known for the IT parks and research institutions. 
 ==IT Parks in Taramani== 
 Famous [[Tidel Park]], an information technology (IT) industrial park is situated here . It is South India's first IT park. The area is also known for its other IT parks including Ascendas IT Park, Ramanujam IT Park, Elnet Software City and TICEL Biotech Park. 
 ==MGR Film city== 
 The [[MGR Film City]] was constructed in [[Taramani|Tharamani]] in 1996.  The Film City was a major project of Tamil Nadu Chief Minister [[J. Jayalalithaa]]. This was made during her first term in office as Chief minister.
