 '''Utena''' is a city in [[Lithuania]]. It is the [[administrative centre]] of [[Utena district|Utena District]] and [[Utena County]]. Utena is one of the oldest cities in Lithuania. < ref name= " :0 " > {{Cite web|title=Utenos miesto istorija|url=https://www.utena.lt/index.php/lt/apie-utenos-rajono-savivaldybe1/utenos-miesto-istorija|access-date=2021-04-30|website=www.utena.lt|archive-date=2021-04-30|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210430194404/https://www.utena.lt/index.php/lt/apie-utenos-rajono-savivaldybe1/utenos-miesto-istorija|url-status=dead}} < /ref >  The city's name most likely comes from a [[lake]] or [[river]]. 
 
 == History == 
 Utena was first mentioned in 1261, in a letter by [[Mindaugas]] to the [[Livonian Brothers of The Sword|Christ Knights]]. < ref name= " :0 "  / >
