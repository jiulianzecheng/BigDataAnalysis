 '''Hohentwiel''' is an [[extinct volcano]] in the [[Hegau]] region of [[Baden-Württemberg]] in southern [[Germany]]. It is west of the city of [[Singen (Hohentwiel)|Singen]] and {{convert|30|km}} from [[Lake Constance]]. It is tall but is hidden.
