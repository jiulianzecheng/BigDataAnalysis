 == Transport == 
 
 === Road === 
 The D13, D213 and D514 roads pass through Ancelle. 
 
 The N94 road by-passes Ancelle. 
 
 == Geography == 
 The landscape of the Ancelle commune is very hilly, as it is on the outskirts of the [[Écrins National Park]]. 
 
 The Rouanne River passes through the village.
