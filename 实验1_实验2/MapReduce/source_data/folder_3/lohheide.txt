 '''Lohheide''' is an [[unincorporated area]] in [[Celle (district)|Celle]], in [[Lower Saxony]], [[Germany]]. 
 
 Its area is {{convert|91.32|km²|sqmi}}. 764 people live there (as at 31 December 2006). Together with administrative area of [[Osterheide]] it is part of the [[Bergen-Hohne Training Area|Bergen-Hohne Military Training Area]].
