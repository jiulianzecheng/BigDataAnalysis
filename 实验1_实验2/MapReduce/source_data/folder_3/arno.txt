 The '''Arno''' is a [[river]] in the [[Tuscany]] region of [[Italy]]. It is one of the important rivers of central Italy.  
 
 == Source and route == 
 
 The river begins on [[Mount Falterona]] in the [[Casentino]] area of the [[Apennine Mountains|Apennines]]. It first takes a curve to the [[south]]. The river turns to the [[west]] near [[Arezzo]]. It passes through [[Florence]], [[Empoli]] and [[Pisa]], then flows into the [[Tyrrhenian Sea]] at the Marina di Pisa. At a length of {{convert|241|km}}, it is the longest river in the [[region]]. 
 
 A [[1966 Flood of the Arno River|flood on November 4, 1966]] collapsed the embankment in Florence. This killed at least 40 people.
