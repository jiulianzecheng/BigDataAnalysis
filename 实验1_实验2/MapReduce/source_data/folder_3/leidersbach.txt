 '''Leidersbach''' is a community in the [[Miltenberg (district)|Miltenberg district]] in the [[Lower Franconia]] [[Regierungsbezirk|region]] in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. 
 
 == Geography == 
 === Place === 
 Leidersbach is about 10 & nbsp;km south of [[Aschaffenburg]] in the [[Spessart]]. 
 
 === Neighbouring communities === 
 From the north, clockwise: Forst Hohe Wart (''gemeindefreies Gebiet''), [[Mespelbrunn]], [[Heimbuchenthal]], [[Hausen, Miltenberg|Hausen]], [[Kleinwallstadt]], [[Sulzbach am Main]]. 
 
 === Subdivisions=== 
 Leidersbach's ''[[Ortsteil]]e'' are Leidersbach, {{Interlanguage link multi|Ebersbach (Leidersbach)|de|3=Ebersbach (Leidersbach)|lt=Ebersbach}}, {{Interlanguage link multi|Roßbach (Leidersbach)|de|3=Roßbach (Leidersbach, Ort)|lt=Roßbach}} and {{Interlanguage link multi|Volkersbrunn|de}}. 
 
 === Combinations === 
 On 1 January 1972, the formerly self-administering communities of Leidersbach, Ebersbach, Roßbach and Volkersbrunn combined into a new unitary community of Leidersbach.
