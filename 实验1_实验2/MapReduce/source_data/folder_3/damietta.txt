 '''Damietta''' is a [[city]] at the [[estuary]] of the [[Nile]], in [[Egypt]]. Its name in Arabic is transilterated as '''Domyat''' (دمياط).It is an important [[port]], about {{convert|15|km}} from the [[Mediterranean Sea|Mediterranean]]. It is the [[capital city]] of the [[Damietta Governorate|Damietta governorate]]. In 2018, over 300.000 people lived in the city. In modern times, the [[Nile Delta]] is made of two branches, which start in [[Cairo]]. the Rosetta branch to the west, and the Damietta branch to the east.
