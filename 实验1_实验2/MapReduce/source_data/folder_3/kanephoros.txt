The '''Kanephoros''' ({{lang-grc|Κανηφόρος}} (pl. {{lang|grc|Κανηφόροι}} ''Kanephoroi''), latinate plural form '''Canephorae''', refers to  " Basket Bearer(s) " ) < ref > {{cite EB1911 |wstitle=Canephorae}} < /ref >  was an honorary term for young, unmarried women chosen to lead a festival in [[Ancient Greece]].
