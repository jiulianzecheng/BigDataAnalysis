'''Rochford''' is a town in the [[Rochford (district)|Rochford]] district of [[Essex]] in the [[East of England]]. It is about {{convert|43|mi}} from [[Central London]]. Its about {{convert|21|mi}} from [[Chelmsford]], the county town of [[Essex]].
