 '''Brugg''' is a [[city]] in the [[Switzerland|Swiss]] [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Aargau]]. It is the capital of the [[district]] of [[Brugg (district)|Brugg]]. 
 
 Since 2010, [[Umiken]] has been part of the city.
