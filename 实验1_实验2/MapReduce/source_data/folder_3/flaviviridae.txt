 '''''Flaviviridae''''' is a family of [[virus]]es that are primarily spread through arthropod vectors (mainly [[tick]]s and [[mosquito]]es). The family gets its name from Yellow Fever virus, a type virus of Flaviviridae; ''flavus'' means  " [[yellow]] "  in [[Latin]]. 
 
 They include the following genera: 
 * Genus ''[[Flavivirus]]'' (type species [[Yellow fever|Yellow fever virus]], others include [[West Nile virus]] and [[Dengue fever]]) 
 * Genus ''[[Hepacivirus]]''  
 * Genus ''[[Pestivirus]]''
