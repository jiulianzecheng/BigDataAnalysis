 '''HY''' is a [[Japan]]ese rock music band formed in year [[2000]] by five high school friends in [[Okinawa]]. Their name comes from the initials of their hometown, Higashi Yakena. The band became popular by performing music in the streets. Their first [[album]] ''Departure'' (Okinawa-only) sold out immediately. In [[2002]], the band did a live performance with American group, [[Linkin Park]]. 
 
 == Members == 
 * [[Hideyuki Sinzato]] born December 4, 1983 - [[guitar]], [[vocal]]s, rap 
 * [[Syun Naka]] born October 5, 1983 - drums, rap 
 * [[Sinsuke Kyoda]] born July 17, 1983 - bass 
 * [[Izumi Nakasone]] born January 11, 1983 - keyboard, vocals 
 * [[Yuuhei Miyazato]] born February 1, 1984 - guitar
