 '''Lorca''' is a [[town]] in [[Region of Murcia|Murcia]], [[Spain]]. The 2010 census recorded that 92,694 people live there. On 11 May 2011, [[2011 Lorca earthquake|an earthquake]] occurred, causing the deaths of ten people. < ref > {{cite news|last=Tremlett|first=Giles|title=Spain Shocked by Deadly Earthquake|url=http://www.guardian.co.uk/world/2011/may/12/spain-earthquake-havoc-questions|accessdate=12 May 2011|newspaper=The Guardian|date=12 May 2011}} < /ref >
