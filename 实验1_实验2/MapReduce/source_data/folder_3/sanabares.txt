 '''Sanabares''' ([[Greek alphabet|Greek]]: ϹΑΝΑΒΑΡΟΥ ''Sanabarou''; 135-160 CE)  was an [[Indo-Parthian]] king. He was the last Indo-Parthian king to rule in [[Sakastan]] and south Arachosia. < ref name= " Mitchiner " > Indo-Greek and Indo-Scythian Coinage - Michael Mitchiner - 1976, Volumes 7 à 9 - Pages 670, 717 and 770 < /ref >
