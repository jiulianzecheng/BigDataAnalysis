'''Pharmaconomist''' means '''expert in pharmaceuticals''' (expert in [[medicine]]). 
 
 A pharmaconomist is a [[Denmark|Danish]] person who works in a [[pharmacy]]. Just like [[pharmacist]]s, pharmaconomists have to be trained to work there, and have lots of knowledge about the [[drug]]s and treatments that they give out.
