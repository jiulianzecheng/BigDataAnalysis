 '''Akureyri''' is a [[city]] in the northeast of [[Iceland]]. It is the second largest city in Iceland. It was founded in [[1786]]. < ref > {{Harvnb|Evans|2008|p=[{{Google books|plainurl=y|id=9_GfdBAASUQC|page=310|text= " Danish merchants " }} 310]}} < /ref >  As of 2015, 18,191 people lived there. < ref > {{cite web|title=Iceland in figures 2015|url=http://issuu.com/hagstofa/docs/icelandinfigures2015|website=Statistics Iceland|publisher=Statistics Iceland|access-date=14 September 2015}} < /ref >  The mayor of Akureyri is Eiríkur Björn Björgvinsson. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.akureyri.is/is/stjornkerfid/vinnustadurinn/starfsmenn|title=STARFSMENN|publisher=Akureyri.is|access-date=30 September 2015}} < /ref >
