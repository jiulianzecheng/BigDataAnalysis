 '''Funimation Productions, LLC''' is an [[United States|American]] [[entertainment]] company. It is well known for [[dubbing]] and releasing content made outside the United States, especially with [[anime]]. Sony owns 95% of the company. This ownership is shared between [[Sony Pictures]] and [[Sony Music Entertainment Japan]]. The other 5% is owned by [[Gen Fukunaga]], who is one of Funimation's founders. The company is based in [[Flower Mound]], [[Texas]]. It is one of the most important companies in North America that release anime and other foreign entertainment. 
 
 The company was created on 9 May 1994 by Gen Fukunaga and his wife, Cindy. The original name of the company during this time was FUNimation Productions. Later, in 2005, [[Navarre Corporation]] bought the company. It was also renamed FUNimation Entertainment. In 2011, Funimation was sold to a group of [[investor]]s by Navarre. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.animenewsnetwork.com/news/2011-04-04/navarre-sells-funimation-to-group-with-gen-fukunaga|title=Navarre Sells Funimation to Group with Gen Fukunaga|date=4 April 2011|last=Loo|first=Egan|website=[[AnimeNewsNetwork]]|access-date=2 April 2020}} < /ref > 
 
 In 2002, it merged with [[Crunchyroll]].
