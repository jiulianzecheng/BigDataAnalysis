 '''Echocardiography''' or '''Echocardiyogram''' is an ultrasound to view and hear the [[heart]].  
 Echocardiography is able to view two-dimensional, three-dimensional, and Doppler ultrasound to create images and sounds of the heart. 
 
 The echocardiography machine's are mainly used in [[hospitals]]. 
 
 Echocardiography dates back to the 1950s and was invented by Carl Helmuth Hertz and Inge Edler.
