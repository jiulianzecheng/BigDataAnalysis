 The '''Weser''' is a [[river]] of north-western [[Germany]]. Formed at [[Hannoversch Münden]] by the confluence of the [[Fulda River|Fulda]] and [[Werra]] rivers, it flows through [[Bremen]] to the [[North Sea]], and has a length of 440 [[km]]. Together with the Werra, its length is 730 & nbsp;km.
