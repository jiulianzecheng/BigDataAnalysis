 '''LexCorp''', also known as '''LuthorCorp''' and '''L-Corp''', (legally known as '''Lexcorp Incorporated''') is a fictional organized crime company appearing in American comic books published by [[DC Comics]]. It is a conglomerate owned and run by [[Lex Luthor]], headquartered in New Troy, [[Metropolis (comics)|Metropolis]]. 
 
 The organization has appeared in different forms of media, inlcuding movies, television series, and video games.
