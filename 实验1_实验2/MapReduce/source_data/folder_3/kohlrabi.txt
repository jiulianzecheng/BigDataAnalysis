 '''Kohlrabi''' is a [[biennial]] [[vegetable]].  Kohlrabi is also commonly known as the German [[turnip]] or cabbage turnip. It is related to [[cabbage]].  Kohlrabi can be eaten raw as well as cooked. Both the stem and the leaves can be eaten. Is very popular in Northern and Eastern European countries like [[Germany]] and Hungary as well as places like northern Vietnam and eastern [[India]].
