The word '''bearing''' can mean: 
 
 *[[Bearing (navigation)]], A word used for different methods that try to find out the angle between an object used for this purpose, and a reference direction 
 *[[Bearing (mechanical)]], A device to permit fixed direction motion between two parts 
 *Bearing (posture or carriage): as in ''She walks with self-assured bearing'' 
 *Bearing (relevance): as in ''That has no bearing on the issue''. 
 
 The word may be used in less frequent contexts than these examples. If in doubt, check in a good dictionary.
