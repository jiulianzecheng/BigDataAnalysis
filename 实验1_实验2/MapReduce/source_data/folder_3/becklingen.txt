 '''Becklingen''' is a German village in [[Bergen, Lower Saxony|Bergen]] in the northern part of [[Celle (district)|Celle]] on the [[Lüneburg Heath]]. Previously an independent municipality, it is part of the town Bergen since 1971. 
 
 == Geography == 
 Becklingen is about {{convert|7|km|mi}} north of Bergen just off the [[Bundesstraße 3|B 3]] federal highway. Its parish includes the villages of Becklingen, Oehus and Tannensieksberg. There is also a hamlet known as Becklingen (Bhf) which has grown up around the old station about a mile from the main village. ''Bhf'' is the German abbreviation for ''Bahnhof'' or 'railway station'.
