 '''Rocourt''' was a [[municipality]] of the [[district]] of [[Porrentruy (district)|Porrentruy]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Canton of Jura|Jura]] in [[Switzerland]]. On 1 January 2018, the former municipality of Rocourt merged into the municipality of [[Haute-Ajoie]].
