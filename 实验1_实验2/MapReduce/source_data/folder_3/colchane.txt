 '''Colchane''' ([[Aymara language|Aymara]]: from ''qullchanni'', ''existence of pasturage'') is a [[city]] in [[Chile]] in the province of [[Tamarugal]]. 
 
 It has 1,700 inhabitants and an area of 4,015 km < sup > 2 < /sup > . It was originally founded as Los Cóndores in September 8, 1970.
