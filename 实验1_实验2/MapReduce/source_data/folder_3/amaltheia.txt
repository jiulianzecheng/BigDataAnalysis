 In [[Greek mythology]], '''Amaltheia''' is the name of a [[nymph]], who raised [[Zeus]] with the [[milk]] of a [[Domestic goat|goat]]. Other sources say that Amlatheia was the goat who made the origin of [[cornucopia]]. Other names of her are '''Aiga''' and '''Aix'''.
