 '''Hazel''' is a [[genus]] of [[tree]]s and [[shrub]]s, in the [[Betulaceae|birch family]]. There are 50 to 80 [[species]], in four [[genus (biology)|genera]]. They are native to the [[temperate]] regions of the [[northern hemisphere]].
