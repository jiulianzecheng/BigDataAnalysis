 '''Robert Diggs''' (born July 5, 1969 < ref > [http://www.allmusic.com/artist/p116447 RZA at Allmusic] < /ref > ) is an American rapper and producer, as well as a member of the hip-hop group [[Wu-Tang Clan]], better known by his stage name, '''RZA'''. He was a member of [[Gravediggaz]]. He was known as Rzarector. This is a [[pun]] on 'resurrector'. RZA has written two books. 
 
 RZA started his music career in 1991 when he released the EP ''Ooh I Love You Rakeem''.
