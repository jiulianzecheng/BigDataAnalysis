 '''Bricquebec''' is a former [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is in [[Normandy]] in the [[Manche]] [[Departments of France|department]] in northwest [[France]]. On 1 January 2016, it was merged into the new commune of [[Bricquebec-en-Cotentin]]. < ref > [https://www.legifrance.gouv.fr/eli/arrete/2015/12/4/INTB1532113A/jo/texte Arrêté préfectoral] 4/15 December 2015 {{in lang|fr}} < /ref >
