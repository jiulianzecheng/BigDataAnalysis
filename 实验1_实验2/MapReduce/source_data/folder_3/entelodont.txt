 '''Entelodonts''' were a [[Family (biology)|family]] family of [[pig]]-like omnivores widespread in forests of [[North America]], [[Europe]], and [[Asia]]. 
 
 They flourished for about 20+ million years from the middle [[Eocene]] to the early [[Miocene]] epochs.  
 
 It is thought that they included [[omnivore]]s, [[carnivore]]s and [[scavenger]]s.
