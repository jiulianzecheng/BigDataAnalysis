 '''Laukaa''' ({{lang-sv|Laukas}}) is a municipality in [[Finland]]. About 18,573 people live there. Nearby municipalities include [[Hankasalmi]], [[Jyväskylä]], [[Konnevesi]], [[Toivakka]], [[Uurainen]] and [[Äänekoski]].
