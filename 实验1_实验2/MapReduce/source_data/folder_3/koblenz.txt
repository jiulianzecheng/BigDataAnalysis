 '''Koblenz''' is a [[city]] in northern [[Rhineland-Palatinate]], [[Germany]] with about 107,000 inhabitants. It is the third biggest city in the [[States of Germany|state]] of Rhineland-Palatinate and one of its main centres. Koblenz is the base of the [[Campus]] Koblenz of the [[University of Koblenz-Landau]] and the seat of the [[Mayen]]-Koblenz municipality. 
 
 == Geography == 
 Koblenz is at the meeting of the [[Rhine]] and [[Moselle]] rivers, between [[Bonn]] ({{convert|60|km|mi|1|lk=on|abbr=off}} downstream the Rhine) and [[Mainz]] ({{convert|90|km|mi|1|lk=on|abbr=off}} upstream the Rhine). Parts of Koblenz belong to a [[UNESCO World Heritage]] site.
