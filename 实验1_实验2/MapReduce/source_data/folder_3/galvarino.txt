 '''Galvarino''' was a famous [[Mapuche]] [[warrior]]. In 1557, he fought in the [[Battle of Lagunillas]] against the an army of [[Spain|Spanish]] [[conquistador]]s. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://www.biografiasyvidas.com/biografia/g/galvarino.htm |title=Galvarino |publisher=Biografías y Vidas |access-date=26 May 2016}} < /ref >  The battle took place in [[Chile]] near the [[Bio Bio River]]. < ref name=mentflos > {{cite web |url=http://mentalfloss.com/article/72293/galvarino-mapuche-warrior-knives-hands |title=Galvarino, the Mapuche Warrior with Knives for Hands |publisher=Mental Floss |access-date=26 May 2016}} < /ref >  He was taken [[prisoner]] along with one hundred and fifty other Mapuche. Some prisoners were condemned to [[amputation]] of their right [[hand]] and [[nose]]. Others like Galvarino had both hands cut off. < ref > [[Jerónimo de Vivar]], '''Crónica''', Capítulo CXXXII < /ref >  He was released and told to tell the Mapuche [[Tribal chief|chief]] [[Caupolicán]] to [[surrender (military)|surrender]] to stop more [[wikt:bloodshed|bloodshed]]. < ref name= " mentflos "  / >  He did just the opposite. < ref name= " mentflos "  / >  He urged the Mapuche to continue fighting against the Spanish [[wikt:invade|invade]]rs. < ref name= " mentflos "  / >  Less than a month later, on November 30, 1557, with [[knives]] fastened to both of his [[wrist]]s he fought at the [[Battle of Millarupe]]. < ref name= " mentflos "  / >  It was supposed to be a surprise attack but the Mapuche sprang the trap too early. < ref name= " mentflos "  / >  In the battle, over 3,000 Mapuche warriors were killed. < ref name= " mentflos "  / >  Galvarino was one of several hundred who were [[wikt:capture|capture]]d. He was sentenced to be [[wikt:hung|hung]]. Some say he was [[execution|executed]] by being thrown to the [[dog]]s. < ref > Vivar, '''Crónica''', Capítulo CXXXIII  < /ref >  Others claim he was hanged. < ref name= " mentflos "  / >  Still others say he killed himself before he could be executed. < ref name= " mentflos "  / >
