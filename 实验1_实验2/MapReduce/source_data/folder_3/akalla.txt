 '''Akalla''' is a district of [[Sweden]], west of [[Stockholm]]. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Stadsdelsområdenas omfattning delområdesvis 2006-12-31. Stockholm|url=http://www.usk.stockholm.se/arsbok/b038.htm|url-status=bot: unknown|access-date=2021-07-16|website=web.archive.org|archive-date=2007-09-23|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20070923105842/http://www.usk.stockholm.se/arsbok/b038.htm}} < /ref >  About 9,100 people live in Akalla, from many different countries and cultures. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Statistik och fakta om Stockholm - Stockholms stad|url=https://start.stockholm/om-stockholms-stad/utredningar-statistik-och-fakta/statistik/|access-date=2021-07-16|website=start.stockholm|language=sv|archive-date=2021-07-12|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210712161945/https://start.stockholm/om-stockholms-stad/utredningar-statistik-och-fakta/statistik/|url-status=live}} < /ref >  The type of houses in Akalla are mostly high rises, and terraced houses. In the centre of Akalla there are restaurants, stores, schools, and pre-schools. 
 
 There are four primary schools in Akalla, the most important are Oxhagsskolan and Akalla Grundskola.  
 
 There are three nature reserves around Akalla.  
 
 Akalla is on the blue line of the metro system.  
 
 Most of Akalla voted for the [[Social democratic party (Sweden)|Social democratic party]].  
 
 The footballer [[Robin Quaison]] and the rapper [[Dree Low]] come from Akalla.
