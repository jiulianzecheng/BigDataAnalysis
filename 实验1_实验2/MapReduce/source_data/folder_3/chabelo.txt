 '''Xavier López Rodríguez''' (February 17, 1935 – March 25, 2023), < ref > [https://www.milenio.com/espectaculos/famosos/chabelo-muere-xavier-lopez-actor-interpreto-personaje Murió Xavier López, 'Chabelo', actor y comediante] {{in lang|es}} < /ref >  his better known as  " '''Chabelo''' " , was a Mexican [[actor]] and television host. 
 
 He worked on television for over sixty years.  " Chabelo "  has been in more than thirty [[motion pictures]] and recorded more than thirty musical [[albums]]. He also has produced many shows like ''La Cuchufleta'' and ''La Güereja Quiere Más''. < ref name= " Cumple Chabelo 77 años " > {{cite web |author=El Sol |url=https://www.proquest.com/docview/307487431 |title=Cumple Chabelo 77 años |id={{ProQuest|307487431}} }} < /ref >   
 
 His most famous TV show was ''En Familia con Chabelo'', which was broadcast every Sunday morning on [[Televisa]]. This show was made up of contests with people from the audience, gifts and games and was mainly for children. < ref name= " chabelo.com " > {{cite web |author=Televisa |url=http://www.chabelo.com/ |title=Chabelo |access-date=2007-10-23}} < /ref >  The program ended on Sunday, December 20, 2015 after 47 years.
