 '''Planken''' is a village in [[Liechtenstein]]. It has a population of 366 residents. < ref > {{in lang|de}} [http://www.llv.li/amtsstellen/llv-as-bevoelkerung/llv-as-bevoelkerung-bevoelkerungsstatistik_per_31_dezember.htm 2008 Statistics for Liechtenstein] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20101113150537/http://www.llv.li/amtsstellen/llv-as-bevoelkerung/llv-as-bevoelkerung-bevoelkerungsstatistik_per_31_dezember.htm |date=2010-11-13 }} < /ref >  It is the smallest and least populated village or area in the country.
