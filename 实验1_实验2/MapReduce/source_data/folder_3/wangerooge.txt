 '''Wangerooge''' is one of the 32 [[Frisian Islands]] in the [[North Sea]] close to the coasts of the [[Netherlands]], [[Germany]] and [[Denmark]]. It is also a municipality in [[Friesland (district)|Friesland]] in [[Lower Saxony]], Germany.
