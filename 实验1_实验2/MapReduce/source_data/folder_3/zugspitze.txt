 The '''Zugspitze''' ([[Austro-Bavarian language|Bavarian]]: ''Zugspitz'') is located in [[Bavaria]], Germany. It is the highest mountain in Germany. It is {{convert|2962|m|ft|1|lk=on|abbr=on}} above sea level. It attracts thousands of visitors every year.
