 '''Ljunghusen''' is a [[Urban areas in Sweden|locality]] in [[Vellinge Municipality]] in [[Skåne County]] in [[Sweden]]. In 2010, 2,555 people lived there. < ref name=scb / >  It is located west of [[Höllviken]] and east of [[Skanör-Falsterbo]]. The place is notable for its beaches and for its golf course.
