'''Tasiman''' (died 7 December 2020) was an Indonesian politician. He was the Regent of the [[Pati Regency]] from 2001 to 2011. He was born in [[Juwana, Pati|Juwana]], Pati. Tasiman was chairman of the [[Regional People's Representative Assembly]]. Tasiman was a member of the [[Indonesian Democratic Party of Struggle]].
