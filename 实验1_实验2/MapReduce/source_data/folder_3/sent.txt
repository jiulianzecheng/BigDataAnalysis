 '''Sent''' was a [[municipality]] of the [[district]] of [[Inn (district)|Inn]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Graubünden]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 On 1 January 2015, the municipalities of [[Ardez]], [[Guarda, Switzerland|Guarda]], [[Tarasp]], [[Ftan]] and Sent became part of the municipality of [[Scuol]].
