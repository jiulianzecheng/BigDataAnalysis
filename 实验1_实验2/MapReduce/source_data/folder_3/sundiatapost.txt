 '''''SundiataPost''''' is a Nigerian online news platform that provides up-to-date news and insightful analysis on various topics in Africa. < ref > {{Cite web|last=Times|first=Premium|date=July 7, 2015|title=Sundiata Post Unveiling: Adesina charges online publishers on credibility|url=https://www.premiumtimesng.com/news/more-news/186271-sundiata-post-unveiling-adesina-charges-online-publishers-on-credibility.html|access-date=2023-07-26|website=Premium Times}} < /ref >  Established in 2013, SundiataPost has emerged as a trusted source of information, offering readers a comprehensive view of current affairs, politics, business, culture, and more across the African continent. < ref > {{Cite news|last=Nation|first=The|date=July 7, 2015|title=Photo: Official unveiling of Sundiata post|work=The Nation Online|url=https://thenationonlineng.net/official-unveiling-of-sundiata-post/}} < /ref >   
 
 == History == 
 
 Founded by Max Amuchie, SundiataPost was inspired by the legendary figure of Sundiata Keita, the first ruler of the Mali Empire and an iconic symbol of West African history and unity. < ref > {{Cite web|date=2020-07-09|title=Sundiata Post Media launches e-commerce platform, Dealboku|url=https://www.thecable.ng/sundiata-post-media-launches-e-commerce-platform-dealboku|access-date=2023-07-26|website=TheCable|language=en-US}} < /ref >  The platform's name pays tribute to the spirit of African heritage and the pursuit of truth in reporting. < ref > {{Cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=b8543jPDCd4C & dq=sundiata+post & pg=PA98|title=American Literature's Aesthetic Dimensions|last1=Weinstein|first1=Cindy|last2=Looby|first2=Christopher|date=2012-07-17|publisher=Columbia University Press|isbn=978-0-231-52077-5|language=en}} < /ref >
