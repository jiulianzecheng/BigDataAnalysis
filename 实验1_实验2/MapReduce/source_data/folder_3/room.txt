 A '''room''' (or '''chamber''') is a [[Space (physics)|space]] inside a [[structure]] or [[building]]. Usually, a room is separated from other rooms and from the outside by [[wall]]s. [[Door]] < nowiki/ > s provide access. 
 
 A room may have a specified purpose, defined by its use. Examples of such rooms are the [[bedroom]], the [[kitchen]], and the [[bathroom]].
