 '''Cuenca''' is a [[Spain|Spanish]] city, capital of the [[Cuenca province]], in [[Castile-La Mancha]]. Around 50,000 are living there. It is famous for its monuments: The hanging houses, the [[Cathedral]] and other churches. It is located over the [[Jucar]] river, near the Serrania of Cuenca. < ref > {{Cite web|date=2019-01-13|title=Climate Ecuador - Climate data|url=https://en.tutiempo.net/climate/ecuador.html|access-date=2020-08-11|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190113003904/https://en.tutiempo.net/climate/ecuador.html |archive-date=2019-01-13 }} < /ref >
