 '''''Galaxian''''' is an [[arcade game|arcade]] [[video game]] released by [[Namco]] in 1979. In this game, the player controls a [[spaceship]] and shoots enemies coming down in different directions. A sequel, ''[[Galaga]]'', was released in 1981. 
 
 The game was a very popular game when it was released. It has been ported to many [[video game console]]s such as the [[Atari 2600]], the [[NES]], and the [[Game Boy]]. It has also been featured in Namco video game collections such as ''[[Namco Museum Remix]]'' for the [[Wii]].
