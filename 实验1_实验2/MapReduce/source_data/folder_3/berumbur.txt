 '''Berumbur''' is a municipality in [[Aurich (district)|Aurich]], in [[Lower Saxony]], [[Germany]]. The municipality covers an area of 6.42 square kilometers. 
 
 The municipality has the districts Berumbur, Berum, Kleinheide, Holzdorf and Blandorf.
