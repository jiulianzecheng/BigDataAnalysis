 '''Bolton''' (called Bolton le moors in the past) is a [[town]] in the [[North West England|North West]] of [[England]]. It is to the north-west of [[Manchester]]. It is part of [[Greater Manchester]]. Bolton was part of [[Lancashire]] until the [[1970s]]. It was famous for its [[cotton]] industry. 
 
 The former Bolton Institute of Higher Education became the University of Bolton in 2005. < ref > {{Cite news|last=MacLeod|first=Donald|date=2004-04-27|title=Bolton Institute gets coveted university title|language=en-GB|work=The Guardian|url=https://www.theguardian.com/education/2004/apr/27/highereducation.newuniversities19922012|access-date=2023-03-14|issn=0261-3077}} < /ref > 
 
 Healthcare is mostly run by [[Bolton NHS Foundation Trust|Bolton NHS Foundation Trust.]]
