 '''Moordrecht''' is a former [[List of municipalities of the Netherlands|municipality]] in the [[Netherlands|Dutch]] [[Provinces of the Netherlands|province]] of [[South Holland]]. Since 2010, it has been part of the municipality of [[Zuidplas]].
