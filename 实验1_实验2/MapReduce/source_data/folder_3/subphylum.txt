A '''subphylum''' is a rank in the [[Linnaean taxonomy]] of biological groups. Normally the ranks go [[phylum]]  >  [[Class (biology)|class]]  >  [[Order (biology)|order]] etc. However, sometimes extra steps are needed, and if so, a subphylum ranks immediately below a phylum.
