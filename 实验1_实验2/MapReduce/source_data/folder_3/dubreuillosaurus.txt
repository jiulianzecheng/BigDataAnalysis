 '''''Dubreuillosaurus''''' is a [[genus]] of [[carnivorous]] [[dinosaur]] from the middle [[Jurassic period|Jurassic Period]]. It is a [[Megalosauridae|megalosaurid]] [[theropod]] closely related to [[Afrovenator]] and its fossils were found in [[France]] 
 It looked a lot like ''[[Eustreptospondylus]]''. The [[Holotype|type]] fossil was an individual that probably measured {{convert|5|m|ft}} long and weighed {{convert|250|kg|lb}}. < ref > Paul, G.S., 2010, ''The Princeton Field Guide to Dinosaurs'', Princeton University Press p. 86 < /ref >
