'''Angri''' is a town and ''[[comune]]'' in the [[province of Salerno]], [[Campania]], [[Italy]]. It was the hometown of Teresina Raiola, the mother of famous [[Americans|American]] gangster [[Al Capone]], as well [[Frank Nitti]], another famous gangster. It is also where [[Alfonso Maria Fusco]] lived and died.
