[[File:Abhurite - Shipwreck Hydra, South coast of Norway.jpg|thumb|Abhurite from Shipwreck Hydra, South coast of Norway]] 
 '''Abhurite''' is a colorless [[crystal]]line [[mineral]] that forms on ship-wrecked tin ingots (nuggets).  It is named for where it is found.  It is found in a [[cove]] in the [[Red Sea]].  It is not radioactive. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.webmineral.com/data/Abhurite.shtml|title=Mineral Data Sheet|publisher=www.webmineral.com|access-date=2008-07-07}} < /ref >
