'''''Hoosiers''''' is a 1986 sports movie about a high school basketball team from a small town in Indiana that wins their state championship. It written by Angelo Pizzo and directed by David Anspaugh. The story comes from a team at Milan High School that won the 1954 state championship. 
 
 [[Gene Hackman]] played Norman Dale, a new coach but who had problems in his past. Other stars include [[Barbara Hershey]] and [[Dennis Hopper]] (he would earn a Oscar nomination for his role). The music also got Jerry Goldsmith a nomination for an Academy Award.
