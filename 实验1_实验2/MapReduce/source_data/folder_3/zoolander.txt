 '''''Zoolander''''' is a 2001 American [[action]] [[comedy]] movie directed by [[Ben Stiller]] and starring Stiller, [[Owen Wilson]] and [[Will Ferrell]]. < ref name= " Zoolander " > {{cite web|url=http://www.tcm.com/tcmdb/title/415761/Zoolander/full-credits.html|title=Zoolander|work=[[Turner Classic Movies]]|access-date=May 22, 2016}} < /ref >  The movie is based on many short movies directed by Russell Bates and written by [[Drake Sather]] and Stiller for the ''[[VH1]] Fashion Awards'' television specials in 1996 and 1997.  
 
 It was a [[satire]] on the [[Fashion#Fashion industry|fashion industry]]. The movie received mixed to positive reviews from critics but was a box office success.  A sequel, ''[[Zoolander 2]]'', was released in February 2016. < ref name= " zoo2 " > {{cite web|title='Zoolander 2' Coming February 2016|url=http://www.slashfilm.com/zoolander-2-announced/|website=SlashFilm|access-date=March 10, 2015}} < /ref >
