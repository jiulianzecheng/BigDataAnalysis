 '''Vrin''' is a former [[municipality]] in the [[Swiss]] [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Graubünden]]. It is found of the [[district]] [[Surselva (district)|Surselva]]. On 1 January 2013 the former municipalities of Vrin, [[Cumbel]], [[Degen, Switzerland|Degen]], [[Lumbrein]], [[Morissen]], [[Suraua]], [[Vignogn]] and [[Vella]] merged into the new municipality of [[Lumnezia]]. In 1998, Vrin was awarded the [[Wakker Prize]] for the preservation of its architectural heritage. It was first mentioned in 1208 as ''Varin''. 
 
 In 2011, 255 people lived there.{{-}}
