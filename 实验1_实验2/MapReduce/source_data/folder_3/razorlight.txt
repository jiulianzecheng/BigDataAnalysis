 '''Razorlight''' are an Anglo-[[Sweden|Swedish]] [[Independent music|indie]] [[Rock and roll|rock]] [[band]]. They formed in [[2002]].  
 
 Razorlight are best known for the songs ''Golden Touch'', ''Somewhere Else'' and ''In the Morning''.
