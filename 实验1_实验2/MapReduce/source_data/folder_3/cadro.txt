 '''Cadro''' was a [[municipality]] of the district of [[Lugano (district)|Lugano]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Ticino]] in [[Switzerland]]. On 14 April 2013, the former municipalities of [[Bogno]], Cadro, [[Carona, Switzerland|Carona]], [[Certara]], [[Cimadera]], [[Valcolla]] and [[Sonvico]] merged into the city of [[Lugano]]. < ref name=Fusion > [http://www.bfs.admin.ch/bfs/portal/de/index/infothek/nomenklaturen/blank/blank/gem_liste/03.html Amtliches Gemeindeverzeichnis der Schweiz] published by the Swiss Federal Statistical Office {{in lang|de}} accessed 2 January 2013 < /ref >
