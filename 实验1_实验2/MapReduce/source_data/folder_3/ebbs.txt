 '''Ebbs''' is a small [[village]] in the [[Kufstein District|Kufstein district]] in the [[Austria]]n state of [[Tyrol (state)|Tyrol]]. It is {{convert|7|km}} north of Kufstein at the border with [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. The village has seven parts. It was mentioned for the first time as ''Episas'' in 788. Rumors came up that settlement began in the Roman age. The main source of income is tourism but the village also has commuters.
