 A '''birch''' tree is a thinleaved [[deciduous]] [[hardwood]] [[tree]] of the [[genus]] '''''Betula'''''. < ref > ''Sunset Western Garden Book,'' 1995:606â€“607 < /ref >  They are in the family [[Betulaceae]], which also includes [[alder]]s, [[hazel]]s, and [[hornbeam]]s.
