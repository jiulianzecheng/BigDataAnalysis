 '''Marktoberdorf''' ({{IPA-de|maʁktˈʔoːbɐdɔʁf|lang|De-Marktoberdorf.ogg}}) is the [[Capital (political)|capital]] of [[Ostallgäu]] in [[Swabia (administrative region)|Swabia]]. 
 
 Marktoberdorf is near [[Kempten im Allgäu|Kempten]], [[Füssen]], known for the castle [[Neuschwanstein]], [[Bad Wörishofen]], and [[Schongau, Bavaria|Schongau]]. The nearest larger city is [[Kaufbeuren]], eleven kilometers away.
