 '''Bron''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is in the [[Metropolis of Lyon]] in [[Auvergne-Rhône-Alpes]] in east [[France]]. About 42,200 people live there. 
 
 == Geography == 
 Bron is {{convert|10|km|mi|abbr=on}} east of [[Lyon]] and is the sixth-largest [[suburb]] of the city of [[Lyon]]. 
 
 == Sights == 
 The [[Fort de Bron]], built between 1872 and 1876, is part of the second belt of fortifications around Lyon.
