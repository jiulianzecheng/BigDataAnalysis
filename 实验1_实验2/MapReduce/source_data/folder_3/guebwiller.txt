 '''Guebwiller''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]] in the [[Haut-Rhin]] [[Departments of France|department]] in [[Grand Est]] of eastern [[France]]. 
 
 It is {{convert|20|km|mi|abbr=on}} northwest of [[Mulhouse]] at the foot of the [[Vosges mountains]]. 
 
 The town had a population of about 11,609 people as of 2006.
