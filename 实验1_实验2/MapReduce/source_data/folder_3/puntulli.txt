The '''Asociación PuntuLLI''' (dot LLI) is an association that promotes the creation of a [[Top Level Domain]] for the [[Leonese Language]] and Culture. 
 
 Similar campaigns started in [[Scotland]] (dot SCO), [[Wales]] (dot CYM), [[Berlin]] (dot Berlin) or [[New York]] (dot New York). 
 
 PuntuLLI is recognised by cityTLD < ref > [http://www.citytld.com/links.htm] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20081221021535/http://www.citytld.com/links.htm |date=2008-12-21 }} City Top Level Domain Web < /ref >  as a [[Top Level Domain]] official initiative. 
 
 ==Origins== 
 The campaign for reaching a .lli domain started on January 2007 looking for the support of associations and institutions. Between the organizations that support this requirement to the [[ICANN]] there are associations about [[culture]], [[language]], [[sport]] or [[nature]]. 
 
 ==Objectives== 
 This association promotes the creation of a .lli domain in [[Internet]], according to the [[ICANN]] policy of giving [[Top Level Domain]]s for the languages and cultures. In this sense, puntuLLI promotes a Leonese language and cultural domain. 
 
 ==Activities== 
 In 2008 PuntuLLI have collaborated with other initiatives for promoting the Leonese language like an sponsor, specially the Armwrestling World [[Championship]], developed in [[Llión]], the Leonese Country [[Pipe]] Bands Championship.  
 Leonese City Council selected puntuLLI as official developer of the Internet activities in the III Leonese Language Day. < ref > {{Cite web |url=http://www.aytoleon.es/web/ActualidadServlet/leon/ayto/actualidad/detallenoticias/index.jsp?comando=getDetalleNoticia & idNoticia=8355 & idVideo= & dia=30 & mes=11 & anyo=2008 & idEncuesta= |title=Official Web Site of Leonese City Council |access-date=2009-03-27 |archive-date=2009-03-04 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20090304082002/http://www.aytoleon.es/web/ActualidadServlet/leon/ayto/actualidad/detallenoticias/index.jsp?comando=getDetalleNoticia & idNoticia=8355 & idVideo= & dia=30 & mes=11 & anyo=2008 & idEncuesta= |url-status=dead }} < /ref > 
 
 ==Supports== 
 There are more than 800 people added and 44 organisations that support the campaign, via web, for reaching a [[Top Level Domain]] for [[Leonese language]] and culture webs. < ref > March, 2009 < /ref > 
 
 ==Similar campaigns== 
 Actually there are campaigns for reaching TLDs for [[languages]] and cultures in [[Scotland]], [[Wales]], [[Brittany]], [[Galicia]], [[Basque Country]], [[England]], [[Quebec]] and [[Cornwall]]. 
 
 Similar campaigns, requesting a TLD for cities, are developed in [[Berlin]], [[London]], [[New York]] and [[Paris]].
