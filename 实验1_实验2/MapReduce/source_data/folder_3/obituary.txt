An '''Obituary''' is a notice of a death. An Obituary is published by the family of the person who died to let more distant family members and friends know about the death. [[Funeral home]]s commonly publish obituaries. A description of the person who died is usually included in the obituary.  [[Funeral]] arrangements are usually part of an obituary. An  obituary is distributed via a publication in a newspaper, or via manual handing out or posting of pamphlets.
