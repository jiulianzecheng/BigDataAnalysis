'''Palayan''' is a city in the [[Philippines]]. It is the capital of the [[Provinces of the Philippines|province]] of [[Nueva Ecija]]. According to the 2020 census, 45,383 people lived there. 
 
 Palayan is {{convert|14|km}} of [[Cabanatuan]] and {{convert|130|km}} of [[Manila]].
