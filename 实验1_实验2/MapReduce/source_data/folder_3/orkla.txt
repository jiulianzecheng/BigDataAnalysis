'''Orkla''' is a Norwegian conglomerate operating in the Nordic region, [[Eastern Europe]], [[Asia]] and the [[United States|US]]. Orkla operates in the branded consumer goods, aluminium solutions and financial investment sectors. 
 
 It's head office is in [[Oslo]], [[Norway]].
