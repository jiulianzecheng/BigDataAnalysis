 '''''Bonanza''''' was an American [[Western (genre)|Western]] television series that ran on [[NBC]] from September 12, 1959, to January 16, 1973. It ran for 14 seasons and 431 episodes. ''Bonanza'' is NBC's longest-running western, and ranks overall as the second-longest-running western series on [[United States|U.S.]] network television (behind CBS's ''[[Gunsmoke]]'').
