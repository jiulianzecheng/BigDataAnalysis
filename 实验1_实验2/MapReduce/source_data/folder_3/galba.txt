 '''Servius Sulpicius Galba''' ({{lang-la|Servius Sulpicius Galba Caesar Augustus}}; < ref > born Servius Sulpicius Galba. In [[Classical Latin]], Galba's name would be written as SERVIVS SVLPICIVS GALBA AVGVSTVS < /ref >  {{IPAc-en|lang|ˈ|s|ɜr|v|i|ə|s|_|s|ʌ|l|ˈ|p|ɪ|ʃ|ə|s|_|ˈ|g|æ|l|b|ə}}; 24 December 3 BC – 15 January 69 CE) was a [[Roman Emperor]] for seven months from 68 to 69 CE. Galba was an governor of Hispania Tarraconensis, which was the province near the [[Pyrenees]]. He was the first emperor of [[Year of the Four Emperors]]. 
 
 He was born near [[Terracina]],  " On the left as you travel towards [[Fundi]] "  in the words of [[Suetonius]]. Galba was adopted by Livia Ocellina and took her family name until becoming emperor. < ref > Suetonius. ''The lives of the twelve Caesars''. < /ref >
