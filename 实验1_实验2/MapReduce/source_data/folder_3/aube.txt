 '''Aube''' is a [[departments of France|department]] in northeast [[France]] named after the [[Aube (river)|Aube]] [[river]]. The [[Prefectures in France|prefecture]] ([[Capital city|capital]]) of Aube is [[Troyes]]. 
 
 ==History== 
 Aube is one of the original 83 departments created during the [[French Revolution]] on 4 March 1790 with Troyes as its [[Capital city|capital]]. It was divided in six [[district]]s: Nogent-sur-Seine, Arcis-sur-Aube, Bar-sur-Aube, Bar-sur-Seine, Ervy, and Troyes. < ref name=splaf > {{cite web |url=http://splaf.free.fr/10his.html |title=Historique de l'Aube|work=Le SPLAF |language=fr |access-date=14 December 2016 }} < /ref > 
 
 In 1800, with the creation of the [[Arrondissements of France|''arrondissements'']] in France, the six districts were changed into five ''arrondissements'': Arcis-sur-Aube, Bar-sur-Aube, Bar-sur-Seine, Nogent-sur-Seine and Troyes. < ref name=splaf / > 
 
 After the [[Battle of Waterloo]] (18 June 1815), the department was occupied by the [[Russia]]n [[army]] from June 1815 to November 1818. 
 
 On 10 September 1926, the ''arrondissements'' of Arcis-sur-Aube et Bar-sur-Seine were eliminated. < ref name=splaf / > 
 
 ==Geography== 
 The Aube department is in the southwest of the [[Grand Est]] region. It is surrounded by 5 departments in 3 regions: 
 * [[Grand Est]] region 
 ** [[Marne]] (north) 
 ** [[Haute-Marne]] (east) 
 * [[Bourgogne-Franche-Comté]] region 
 ** [[Côte-d'Or]] (south) 
 ** [[Yonne]] (west) 
 * [[Île-de-France]] region 
 ** [[Seine-et-Marne]] (northwest)

 The area of Aube is {{Convert|6004.2|km2|sqmi|0|abbr=on }}. < ref name= " insee-geo "  / >  Its highest point is in the ''Bois du Mont'' in the ''commune'' [[Champignol-lez-Mondeville]] in the southeast of the department. It is {{Convert|370|-|375|m|ft|0|abbr=on }} high ({{Coord|48|09|10|N|04|41|33|E|name=Bois du Mont|display=inline|region:ID_type:mountain}}),. < ref name=pbag > {{cite web |url=http://www.peakbagger.com/peak.aspx?pid=9608 |title=Bois du Mont, France|publisher=Peakbagger.com |access-date=14 December 2016 }} < /ref >  The lowest point is {{Convert|60|m|ft|0|abbr=on }} high in the ''commune'' [[La Motte-Tilly]] in the west of the department. 
 
 The main rivers in the department are the [[Seine]], the [[Aube (river)|Aube]] ([[tributary]] of the Seine), the [[Amance, Aube|Amance]] (tributary of the [[Armançon]]), and the [[Vanne (river)|Vanne]] (a tributary of the [[Yonne (river)|Yonne]]). 
 
 ===Climate=== 
 The area around [[Troyes]], at an [[altitude]] of about {{Convert|105|m|ft|abbr=on }}, has few extremes of [[temperature]]. The [[Köppen climate classification]] type for the climate at Troyes is a  " Marine West Coast Climate "  and of the subtype  " Cfb " . < ref name= " wbase " > {{cite web|publisher=Weatherbase |title=Troyes, France - Köppen Climate Classification |url=http://www.weatherbase.com/weather/weather-summary.php3?s=598802 & cityname=Troyes%2C+Champagne-Ardenne%2C+France & units= |access-date=14 December 2016}} < /ref > 
 
 The [[average]] amount of [[precipitation]] for the year in Troyes is {{Convert|655.3|mm|in|1|abbr=on }}. The month with the most precipitation on average is November with {{Convert|99.1|mm|in|1|abbr=on }} of precipitation. The month with the least precipitation on average is July with an average of {{Convert|12.7|mm|in|1|abbr=on }}. 
 
 The average temperature for the year in Troyes is {{Convert|10.8|°C|°F }}. The warmest month, on average, is July with an average temperature of {{Convert|19.3|°C|°F }}. The coolest month on average is January, with an average temperature of {{Convert|3.1|°C|°F }}.

 ==Administration== 
 Aube is managed by the ''Departmental Council of Aube'' in [[Troyes]]. The department is part of the [[Grand Est]] region. 
 
 ===Administrative divisions=== 
 There are 3 [[Arrondissements of France|''arrondissements'']] ([[district]]s), 17 [[Cantons in France|''cantons'']] and 431 [[Communes in France|''communes'']] ([[Municipality|municipalities]]) in Aube. < ref name=insee1 > {{cite web|url=https://www.insee.fr/fr/metadonnees/cog/departement/DEP10-aube |title=Département de l’Ain (01)  |publisher=Institut national de la statistique et des études économiques - INSEE |language=fr |access-date=13 November 2017 }} < /ref >

 ==Demographics== 
 People who live in Aube are called ''Aubois'' (women: ''Auboises''). < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.habitants.fr/aube-10 |title=Habitants du départment: Aube (10)|publisher=habitants.fr |language=fr |access-date=15 December 2016 }} < /ref > 
 
 Aube had a population, in 2014, of 308,094, < ref name= " insee-geo "  / >  for a population density of {{Pop density|308094|6004.2|km2|prec=1|disp=num}} inhabitants/km < sup > 2 < /sup > . The ''arrondissement'' of Troyes, with 225,184 inhabitants, is the ''arrondissement'' with more inhabitants. < ref name= " arron "  / >
