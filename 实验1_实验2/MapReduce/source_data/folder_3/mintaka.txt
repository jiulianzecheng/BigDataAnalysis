 '''Mintaka,''' also called '''Delta Orionis''', is a multiple [[star]] system in the [[constellation]] [[Orion (constellation)|Orion]]. It is the right star (northern hemisphere, or left star in the southern hemisphere) of the line of stars called [[Orion's Belt]]. It is over a thousand [[light year]]s away from the [[Sun]] and consists mostly of bright blue stars.
