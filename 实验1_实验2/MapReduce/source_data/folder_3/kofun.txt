 {{nihongo|'''Kofun'''|古墳}} are [[wikt:earthen|earthen]] [[tomb]]s or [[tumulus|tumuli]] in [[Japan]].  They were constructed between the early 3rd century and early 7th century.  
 
 These [[tumulus|burial mounds]] give their name to the [[Kofun period]] (middle [[3rd century]] - early-middle [[6th century]]).  
 
 Many of the kofun have a [[wikt:keyhole|keyhole]]-shaped [[hill|mound]] ({{nihongo|''zenpo-koenfun''|前方後円墳}}) which is [[wikt:unique|unique]] to ancient Japan.
