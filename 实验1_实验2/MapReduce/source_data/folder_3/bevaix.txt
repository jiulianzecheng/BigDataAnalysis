 '''Bevaix''' was a [[municipality]] in the district of [[Boudry (district)|Boudry]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Neuchâtel (canton)|Neuchâtel]] in [[Switzerland]]. On 1 January 2018, the municipalities of Bevaix, [[Saint-Aubin-Sauges]], [[Gorgier]], [[Vaumarcus]], [[Montalchez]] and [[Fresens]] joined together and became the new municipality of [[La Grande-Béroche]]. < ref > [https://www.bfs.admin.ch/bfs/de/home/grundlagen/agvch.html Amtliches Gemeindeverzeichnis der Schweiz] published by the Swiss Federal Statistical Office {{in lang|de}} accessed 14 February 2018 < /ref >
