 '''Grimentz''' is a village and former [[municipality]] in the [[district]] of [[Sierre (district)|Sierre]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Valais]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 On 1 January 2009, the former municipalities of Grimentz, [[Ayer, Switzerland|Ayer]], [[Chandolin]], [[Saint-Jean, Switzerland|Saint-Jean]], [[Saint-Luc, Switzerland|Saint-Luc]] and [[Vissoie]] merged into the new municipality of [[Anniviers]].
