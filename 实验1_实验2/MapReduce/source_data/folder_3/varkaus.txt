 '''Varkaus''' is a [[town]] in [[Northern Savonia]], [[Finland]]. As of January 2014 Varkaus had a [[population]] almost of 22,100 people. Neighbouring municipalities are [[Heinävesi]], [[Joroinen]], [[Leppävirta]], [[Rantasalmi]] and [[Savonlinna]]. 
 
 The former municipality of [[Kangaslampi]] was merged with Varkaus on 1 January 2005. 
 
 In Joroinen is a small airport called ''Varkauden lenteasema''.
