 '''Dalmatia''' is a region of the [[Balkans|Balkan Peninsula]] along the [[Adriatic Sea]]. It mostly belongs to [[Croatia]]. It goes from the [[island]] of [[Pag (island)|Pag]] in the northwest to the [[Bay of Kotor]] (in [[Montenegro]]) in the southeast.
