[[Image:Mandelas Retirement Home, Qunu.jpg|thumb|right|200px|Nelson Mandela's retirement home at Qunu]] 
 '''Qunu''' [ˈk͡ǃuːnu] is a small rural [[village]] in [[South Africa]]'s [[Eastern Cape]] Province. It is {{convert|32|km|0|abbr=on}} south-west of [[Mthatha]] on the road between [[Butterworth, South Africa|Butterworth]] and Mthatha.  
 
 [[Nelson Mandela]] spent his childhood here and had a retirement home there. On 15 December 2013, Mandela was buried in Qunu after a state funeral. < ref > [https://www.theguardian.com/world/2013/dec/06/mandela-funeral-worlds-most-powerful-us-iran-israeli-palestinian  " Mandela funeral brings together world's most powerful people " ]. ''The Guardian''. retrieved 11 May 2014. < /ref >
