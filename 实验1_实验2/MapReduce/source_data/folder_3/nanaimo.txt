 '''Nanaimo''' is a city in [[British Columbia]] with a [[population]] of 90,054 (2016).   
 
 The mayor of Nanaimo is '''Leonard''' Eugene '''Krog'''  < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.nanaimo.ca/your-government/city-council|title=City Council|website=www.nanaimo.ca}} < /ref > 
 
 Nanaimo is located on [[Vancouver Island]] and is the second largest city on Vancouver Island. < ref > {{Cite web |url=http://www.tourismnanaimo.com/content/about-nanaimo |title=Accommodations, Things to do and Dining in Nanaimo, Vancouver Island, BC  & #124; Tourism Nanaimo  & #124; It's AMAZING what you can do here!  > >  About Nanaimo |access-date=2010-08-15 |archive-date=2010-07-26 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20100726053905/http://www.tourismnanaimo.com/content/about-nanaimo |url-status=dead }} < /ref > 
 
 Nanaimo is ranked 62nd overall in the 100 largest municipalities in Canada and is nicknamed the  " Bathtub Racing Capital of the World "  and the  " Harbour City " .
