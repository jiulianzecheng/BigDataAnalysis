 {{Nihongo|'''Tamagushi'''|[[wiktionary:玉串|玉串]]||literally  " jewel skewer " }} is a form of [[Shinto]] offering made from a ''[[sakaki]]''-tree branch. 
 
 They are given to [[kami]] in rituals.
