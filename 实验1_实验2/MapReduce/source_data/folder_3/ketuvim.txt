 '''Ketuvim''' (in [[Hebrew Bible|Biblical Hebrew]]: {{lang|he|כְּתוּבִים}}  " writings " ) is the third and final section of the [[Tanakh]] ([[Hebrew Bible]]), after [[Torah]] (instruction) and [[Nevi'im]] (prophets). It includes:  
 
 *Tehillim – [[Psalms]] 
 
 *Mishlei – [[Book of Proverbs|Proverbs]] 
 
 *Iyyov – [[Book of Job|Job]] 
 
 *Shir Hashirim – [[Song of Songs]] 
 
 *Rut – [[Book of Ruth|Ruth]] 
 
 *Eikha – [[Book of Lamentations|Lamentations]] 
 
 *Kohelet – [[Ecclesiastes]] 
 
 *Ester – [[Book of Esther|Esther]] 
 
 *Daniyel – [[Book of Daniel|Daniel]] 
 
 *Ezra-Nechemya – [[Book of Ezra|Ezra]]-[[Book of Nehemiah|Nehemiah]] 
 
 *Divrei Hayamim - [[Books of Chronicles|Chronicles I and II]]
