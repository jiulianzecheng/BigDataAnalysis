 '''Frosinone'''  is a [[city]] and a ''[[comune]]'' in the [[Lazio]] [[Regions of Italy|region]] of [[Central Italy]], the [[Capital city|capital]] of the [[province of Frosinone]]. The city is about {{Convert|75|km|mi|0|abbr= }} southeast of [[Rome]]. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 The ''comune'' of Frosinone has an area of {{Convert|46.85|km2|sqmi|abbr=on }} and its [[altitude]] is {{Convert|291|m|ft|abbr=on}} [[above sea level]]. < ref name= " area "  / > 
 
 The ''comune'' borders with the  ''[[Comune|comuni]]'' [[Alatri]], [[Arnara]], [[Ceccano]], [[Ferentino]], [[Patrica]], [[Supino]], [[Torrice]], [[Veroli]]. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.tuttitalia.it/lazio/67-frosinone/45-comuni-limitrofi/ |title=Comuni limitrofi a Frosinone |publisher=Tuttitalia.it |language=it |access-date=10 September 2017}} < /ref >

 ===''Frazioni''=== 
 The following ''[[Frazione|frazioni]]'' ([[Hamlet (place)|hamlet]]s) are part of the ''comune'' of Frosinoneː Capo Barile Nicolia, Cerreto, Cinque Vie, Colle Cannuccio, Farnito, Fontana Grande, Impratessa, La Cervona, Lago Maniano, Le Noci, Le Pignatelle, Le Rase, Pratillo, San Liberatore, Selva Dei Muli, Valle Contessa, Vetiche I. 
 
 ==Population== 
 {{As of|2017|1|1}}, there are 46,120 persons (22,004 [[male]]s and 24,116 [[female]]s) living in the ''comune'', < ref name=pop / >  for a [[population density]] of {{Pop density|46120|46.85|km2|prec=1|disp=num}} inhabitants/km².
