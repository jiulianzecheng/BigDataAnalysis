[[File:Riga railway bridge inauguration.jpg|thumb|300px|right|Inauguration of the railway bridge over the Daugava river in [[Riga]], [[Latvia]] in 1914]] 
 '''Inauguration''' is a [[ceremony]] marking the beginning of a [[politics|politician]] taking office or the opening of, for example, a [[bridge]]. 
 
 When a politician, for example a [[President]], takes office, he may make an [[oath of office]] in front of an [[audience]] as part of the inauguration ceremony. 
 
 The Inauguration of the [[President of the United States]] takes place on January 20 in the year after an [[election]].
