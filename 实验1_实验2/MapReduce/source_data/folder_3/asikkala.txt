 '''Asikkala''' is a [[municipality]] of [[Finland]]. It is in the province of [[Southern Finland]] and the region of [[Päijänne Tavastia]]. The [[downtown]] of Asikkala is called [[Vääksy]]. It has a canal, Vääksyn kanava. Asikkala has an [[area]] of 756.2 & nbsp;km². 192.29 & nbsp;km² of it is water. 
 
 Asikkala is on the coast of the lake [[Päijänne]]. As of 31 May [[2013]], 8,444 people live in the municipality.
