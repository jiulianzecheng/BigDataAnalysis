'''Katherine''' is a female name that is very popular in multiple countries, and especially so in Christian countries. It is of Greek origin and means  " pure "  or  " clear. "   < ref > {{Cite web|title=Katherine name Meaning and Origin|url=https://babys.name/detail-Katherine-Meaning-Origin-Famous-Similar-Sibling.html|access-date=2023-03-23|website=babys.name}} < /ref > The pronunciation of the name is [ '''kath'''-er-in, '''kath'''-rin ]. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Definition of Katherine {{!}} Dictionary.com|url=https://www.dictionary.com/browse/katherine|access-date=2023-03-23|website=www.dictionary.com|language=en}} < /ref >  The name and its related variants have ranked on the 100 most popular [[girl]]s names in the [[United States]] since around 1880. There are many celebrities with the name itself and its variants. Its variants ''Kate'', ''[[Hurricane Katrina|Katrina]]'' and ''Karen'' have been used on [[Atlantic Ocean|Atlantic]] [[tropical cyclone]]s. This name was ranked #158 on the US Popular Names in 2021. < ref > {{Cite web|last=OACT|title=Popular Baby Names|url=https://www.ssa.gov/cgi-bin/popularnames.cgi|access-date=2023-03-23|website=www.ssa.gov}} < /ref >
