 '''Eastcote''' is a [[suburb]]an area in [[west]] [[London]]. ([[Historic counties of England|It was]] in [[Middlesex]] until the county was abolished in 1965). It is part of the [[London Borough of Hillingdon]]. In the [[Middle Ages]], Eastcote was one of the three areas that made up the parish of [[Ruislip]], under the name of Ascot. The name came from its position to the east of the parish.
