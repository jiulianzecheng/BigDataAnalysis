 '''Vuissens''' was a [[municipality]] of the district of [[Broye (district)|Broye]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Fribourg (canton)|Fribourg]] in [[Switzerland]]. On 1 January 2017, the former municipalities of [[Bussy, Fribourg|Bussy]], [[Estavayer-le-Lac]], [[Morens]], [[Murist]], [[Rueyres-les-Prés]], [[Vernay, Switzerland|Vernay]] and Vuissens merged into the new municipality of [[Estavayer]]. < ref > [https://www.bfs.admin.ch/bfs/de/home/grundlagen/agvch.html Historisiertes Gemeindeverzeichnis der Schweiz] accessed 5 January 2017 < /ref >
