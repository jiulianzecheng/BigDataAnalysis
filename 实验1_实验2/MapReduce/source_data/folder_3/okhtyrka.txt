 '''Okhtyrka''' ({{lang-uk|Охтирка}}; also known by its Russian name '''Akhtyrka''' {{lang-ru|Ахтырка}}) is a small city in [[Sumy Oblast]] in [[Ukraine]]. Okhtyrka is the administrative center of [[Okhtyrka Raion]]. 
 
 Clashes have been ongoing in Okhtyrka between the [[Ukrainian Armed Forces]] and the [[2022 Russian invasion of Ukraine|invading]] [[Russian Armed Forces]] in the city since 24 February, 2022. < ref > {{in lang|uk}} [https://nv.ua/ukr/ukraine/events/viyna-rosiji-proti-ukrajini-viyska-rf-u-sumah-tochatsya-boji-v-ohtirci-novini-ukrajini-50219649.html Russian troops have invaded Sumy, and fighting is taking place in Okhtyrka - the head of the regional state administration], [[nv.ua]] (25 February 2022) < /ref >  On February 28, Russian forces [[Okhtyrka vacuum bomb attack|used a vacuum bomb]] on the city. < ref > {{cite web |title=Ukraine's ambassador to U.S. says Russia used a vacuum bomb on Monday |url=https://www.reuters.com/world/europe/ukraines-ambassador-us-says-russia-used-vacuum-bomb-monday-2022-02-28/ |publisher=[[Reuters]] |access-date=28 February 2022}} < /ref >
