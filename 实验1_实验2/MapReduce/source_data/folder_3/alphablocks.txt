 '''''Alphablocks''''' is a British [[Animated show|animated television programme]] for [[preschool]]ers that started on [[CBeebies]] on 25 January 2010. The programme was created by Joe Elliot and produced by Alphablocks Ltd with [[Blue-Zoo Productions|Blue Zoo]]. It was commissioned by the [[BBC|British Broadcasting Corporation]].  
 
 The show follows the Alphablocks, characters made of blocks who represent each letter of the [[alphabet]]. They live in a fictional [[planet]] called Alphaland and go on adventures relating to words.
