 '''Hitachi''' is a large [[Japan]]ese [[manufacturing]] [[company]]. It was created in [[Tokyo]] in [[1910]]. Hitachi is involved with small electrical items and large-scale projects, including one of the [[nuclear reactor]]s at the [[Fukushima nuclear disaster|Fukushima]] nuclear power plant.
