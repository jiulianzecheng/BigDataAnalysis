 '''Schinznach''' is a [[municipality]] of the [[district]] [[Brugg (district)|Brugg]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Aargau]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 On 1 January 2014, the former municipalities of [[Oberflachs]] and [[Schinznach-Dorf]] merged into the new municipality of Schinznach.
