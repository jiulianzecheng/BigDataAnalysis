 '''Blenio''' is a [[municipality]] in the [[district]] of [[Blenio (district)|Blenio]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Ticino]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 It was created on 22 October 2006 when the former municipalities of [[Aquila, Switzerland|Aquila]], [[Campo (Blenio)]], [[Ghirone]], [[Olivone]] and [[Torre, Switzerland|Torre]] merged to form the new municipality of Blenio.
