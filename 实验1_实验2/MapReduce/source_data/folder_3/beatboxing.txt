'''Beatboxing''' is making [[percussion instrument|percussive]] noises with the mouth, lips, tongue and voice. Performers try to imitate the sounds of a [[drum machine]]. They could also try to imitate turntablism and other instruments. It is used a lot in [[hip-hop music]]. This includes the use of humming, singing and tapping.
