 '''Hoffen''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is in [[Grand Est]] in the [[Bas-Rhin]] [[Departments of France|department]] in northeast [[France]]. In 1975, it is made from the former communes of Hermerswiller and Leiterswiller.
