 '''Ayodeji Ibrahim Balogun''' (born {{birth date|1990|7|16}}), also known as '''Wizkid''' is a [[Nigeria]]n [[Afrobeats]] singer, songwriter, [[rapper]]. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.vogue.com/13402067/wizkid-interview-africa-nigeria-style/|title=Nigerian Pop Star Wizkid on Fashion and African Style - Vogue|author=Alex Frank|work=Vogue|access-date=4 May 2016|archive-date=16 May 2016|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160516031628/http://www.vogue.com/13402067/wizkid-interview-africa-nigeria-style|url-status=dead}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite web|last=Hobbs|first=Thomas|date=2023-07-14|title=Wizkid: ‘I’m here to steer the new generation – we’re the kings now’|url=https://www.standard.co.uk/culture/music/wizkid-tottenham-stadium-live-afrobeats-drake-b1091961.html|access-date=2023-07-14|website=Evening Standard|language=en}} < /ref >  He is the founder of Star Boy Entertainment, a record label he established in 2013. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.thefader.com/2015/11/18/wizkid-gen-f-interview|title=Wizkid Is Getting The Global Embrace He Deserves|author=Phiona Okumu|work=The FADER|access-date=4 May 2016}} < /reF > 
 
 ==Biography== 
 Wizkid started singing in 2001. At the age of 11, he released a collaborative studio album with Glorious Five album titled ''Lil Prinz'' before he went solo to record his first song with OJB Jezreel. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.nigeriafilms.com/news/18157/18/wizkid-biography.html|title=Wizkid Biography - nigeriafilms.com|author=Seyi|access-date=4 May 2016|archive-date=15 December 2012|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20121215104631/http://www.nigeriafilms.com/news/18157/18/wizkid-biography.html|url-status=dead}} < /ref > 
 
 In 2011, Wizkid released his critically acclaim debut studio album titled ''Superstar'' with hit singles like  " Holla at Your Boy " ,  " Don't Dull "  and  " Tease Me (Bad Guys) "  on the tracklist. ''Superstar'' shot Wizkid to limelight, following it's nomination at [[The Headies 2012]] and at the [[2012 Nigeria Entertainment Awards]] which it won  " Best Album of The Year " . < ref name= " oreoluwapeters " > {{cite web|url=http://ynaija.com/the-fader-wizkid-sounds-from-the-other-side-interview/|title=5 things we learnt from Wizkid's interview with US magazine, The Fader|author=Oreoluwa Peters|date=19 November 2015|work=YNaija|access-date=4 May 2016}} < /ref > 
 
 On September 17, 2014, ''[[Ayo]]'' was released as Wizkid's sophomore album. The album contained hit tracks like  " [[Ojuelegba (song)|Ojuelegba]] " ,  " [[Jaiye Jaiye]] "  and  " Show You The Money " . ''Ayo'' won the  " Best Album of The Year "  and  " Best R & B/Pop Album "  categories at [[The Headies 2015]] and was also nominated in the  " Album of The Year "  category at the [[2015 Nigeria Entertainment Awards]]. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.premiumtimesng.com/entertainment/184891-mtv-mama-2015-davido-wizkid-yemi-alade-others-lead-nominations.html|title=MTV MAMA 2015: Davido, Wizkid, Yemi Alade, others lead nominations|date=12 June 2015}} < /ref >  Wizkid has been working on releasing an [[Extended play|EP]] titled ''Sounds From The Other Side''. < ref name= " oreoluwapeters " / > 
 
 In 2016, he worked with [[Drake (entertainer)|Drake]] on his hit single  " [[One Dance]] " , which reached number-one in 15 different countries. In 2020 Wizkid became the first Nigerian [[artist]] featured on Billboard’s Hot 100. < ref > {{Cite web|date=2020-04-24|title=Wizkid; The only Nigerian Artist featured on Billboard’s Hot 100|url=https://www.entnaija.xyz/2023/01/wizkid-billboard.html|access-date=2020-08-22|website=EntNaija|language=en-US}} < /ref > 
 
 With Chris Brown's  " Call Me Everyday, "  which debuted at number 74 on the chart, he has charted his fourth song on the Billboard Hot 100.
