'''Malabon''', officially the '''City of Malabon''' ({{lang-fil|Lungsod ng Malabon}}), is a city in [[Metro Manila]], [[Philippines]]. In 2020, 380,522 people lived there. 
 
 It is just north of the city of [[Manila]] and is one of the most densely populated cities in the metropolis.
