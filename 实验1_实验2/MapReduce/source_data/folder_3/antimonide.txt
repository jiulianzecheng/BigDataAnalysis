'''Antimonide''' is an [[ion]]. Its chemical formula is Sb < sup > 3- < /sup > . It has antimony in its -3 [[oxidation state]]. Antimonides are [[reducing agent]]s. They react with [[acid]]s to make [[stibine]]. Many antimonides have properties between a [[salt]] and an [[alloy]]. Many [[transition metal]] and [[post-transition metal]] antimonides are [[semiconductor]]s.
