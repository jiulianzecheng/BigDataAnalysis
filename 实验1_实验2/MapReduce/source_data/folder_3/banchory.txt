 '''Banchory''' ([[Scottish Gaelic]]: ''Beannchar'', 'blessed place') is a [[burgh]] or town in [[Aberdeenshire]], [[Scotland]], lying approximately 18 & nbsp;miles west of [[Aberdeen]], near where the [[Feugh River]] meets the [[River Dee, Aberdeenshire|River Dee]].
