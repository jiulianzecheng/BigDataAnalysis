 " '''Complicated''' "  was the first and perhaps the most popular song sung by [[Avril Lavigne]]. It was released in 2002. It was on her album ''[[Let Go]]''. Other songs on the same [[Compact disc|CD]] single are  " I Don't Give "  and additional  " Why "   on maxi-cds.    
 It was at one point #1 in the charts in [[Canada]], [[UK]], [[Australia]] and the [[US]]. She was 17 when she sang it and she and her band wrote it together.
