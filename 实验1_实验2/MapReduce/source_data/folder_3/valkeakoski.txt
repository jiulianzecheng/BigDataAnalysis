 '''Valkeakoski''' is a city in [[Finland]]. It is known for its paper industry. Nearby municipalities are [[Akaa]], [[Hattula]], [[Hämeenlinna]], [[Kangasala]], [[Lempäälä]] and [[Pälkäne]]. In 1973, [[Sääksmäki]] was merged with Valkeakoski.
