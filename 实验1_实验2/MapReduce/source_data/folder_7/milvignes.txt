 '''Milvignes''' is a [[municipality]] of the district of [[Boudry (district)|Boudry]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Neuchâtel (canton)|Neuchâtel]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 It was created on 1 January 2013, when the municipalities of [[Auvernier]], [[Bôle]] and [[Colombier, Neuchâtel|Colombier]] combined to become Milvignes. < ref name=Fusion > [http://www.bfs.admin.ch/bfs/portal/de/index/infothek/nomenklaturen/blank/blank/gem_liste/03.html Amtliches Gemeindeverzeichnis der Schweiz] published by the Swiss Federal Statistical Office {{in lang|de}} accessed 2 January 2013 < /ref >
