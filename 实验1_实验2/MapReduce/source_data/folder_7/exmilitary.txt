 '''''Exmilitary''''' (also known as '''''Ex Military''''') is the first [[mixtape]] by [[Death Grips]]. Death Grips is a [[hip hop]] music group. The mixtape was released on April 25, 2011. It was put on the band's [[website]]. People did not have to pay money to listen to it. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Death Grips - Ex Military|url=https://thirdworlds.net/|url-status=live|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20221015051841/https://thirdworlds.net/|archive-date=2022-10-15|access-date=2022-10-15|website=Thirdworlds|language=en}} < /ref > < ref > {{cite web|title=Exmilitary  & #124; Death Grips  & #124; Grindcore Karaoke|url=https://grindcorekaraoke.bandcamp.com/album/exmilitary|url-status=live|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20201108131931/https://grindcorekaraoke.bandcamp.com/album/exmilitary|archive-date=2020-11-08|access-date=2021-05-02}} < /ref >  The mixtape was later released on [[Gramophone record|vinyl]], [[compact disc]] and [[Audio cassette|cassette tape]]. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Death Grips - Ex Military|url=https://www.discogs.com/es/release/3273384-Death-Grips-Ex-Military|access-date=2022-10-28|website=Discogs|language=en}} < /ref > 
 
 The mixtape had one [[Single (music)|single]]. It is named  " Guillotine " . It was released on [[iTunes]] in August 2011. Many of the songs on the mixtape had [[Music video|music videos]].
