 '''Trogir''' is a port town on the [[Adriatic]] coast in [[Split-Dalmatia County]], [[Croatia]]. In 2011, 10,923 people lived there. Trogir is on a small island between the Croatian mainland and the island of [[Čiovo]]. It is {{convert|27|km|mi|abbr=on}} west of [[Split (city)|Split]]. Since 1997, the historic centre of Trogir was added as a [[UNESCO]] [[World Heritage Site]] for its Venetian architecture. It is the largest building is the church of St. Lawrence.
