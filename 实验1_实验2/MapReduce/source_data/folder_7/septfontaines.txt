 '''Septfontaines''' ({{lang-lb|Simmer}}, {{lang-de|Simmern}}) is a former [[Communes of Luxembourg|commune]] in [[Luxembourg]]. It was part of the [[Capellen (canton)|canton of Capellen]]. 
 
 On 1 January 2018, the commune was merged with [[Hobscheid]] to form the commune of [[Habscht]].
