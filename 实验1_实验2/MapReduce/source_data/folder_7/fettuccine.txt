 '''Fettuccine''' is a type of long, flat, slightly wide [[pasta]]. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Are Fettuccine and Tagliatelle the Same?|url=https://www.thespruceeats.com/what-is-fettuccine-995667|access-date=2021-04-23|website=The Spruce Eats|language=en}} < /ref >  It can either be in dried or fresh form. < ref > {{Cite web|last=Paesana|title=Fresh Pasta vs. Dry Pasta: Boiling Down The Differences|url=https://www.paesana.com/blog/fresh-pasta-vs.-dry-pasta-boiling-down-the-differences|access-date=2021-04-23|website=www.paesana.com|language=en-us}} < /ref >  It has been used in some creamy sauces, such as [[Fettuccine Alfredo|Alfredo]].
