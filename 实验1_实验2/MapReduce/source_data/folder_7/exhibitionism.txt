 '''Exhibitionism''' is a behavior done to attract attention. < ref > {{cite web| url=https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/exhibitionism| access-date=August 3, 2023| website=Merriam-Webster| title=Exhibitionism}} < /ref >  In everyday life, [[extroversion|extroverts]] may be exhibitionists.  
 
 As a [[histrionic personality disorder]], < ref > {{cite web| url=https://www.psychologytoday.com/us/conditions/exhibitionism| access-date=August 3, 2023| website=Psychology Today| title=Exhibitionism}} < /ref >  exhibitionism is the pleasure a person feels by showing their [[breast]]s, [[genital]]s or [[buttocks]] to strangers to shock or frighten them. 
 
 Less severe forms of exhibitionism are [[streaking]], [[mooning]], and [[flashing]].
