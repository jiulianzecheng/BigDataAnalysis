 '''Hypothyroidism''' is a [[medical condition]]. If a person has hypothyroidism, their [[thyroid gland]] does not make enough [[hormones]]. It can be caused by many factors, but it is more common when a person has insufficient [[iodine]]. Severe hypothyroidism in [[infants]] can result in [[cretinism]]. 
 Symptoms of hypothyroidism include the following: tiredness, headaches, weight gain, dry skin, constipation, and poor concentration.
