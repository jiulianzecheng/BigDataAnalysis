 '''Drottningstorp''' is a village in [[Habo Municipality]], [[Sweden]]. It is located near the western shorelines of [[Domneådammen]] betwen [[Habo]] and [[Mullsjö]]. An annual [[country music|country music festival]] called  " Drottningstorps midsommardagscountry "  has been held here on [[Midsummer|Midsummer Day]] since 2006. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.jnytt.se/det-har-blivit-en-tradition-att-aka-hit|title=Det har blivit en tradition att åka hit|publisher=J-nytt|language=Swedish|author=Peter Gustafsson|date=22 June 2008|accessdate=26 June 2011|archive-date=2 April 2015|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150402143330/http://www.jnytt.se/det-har-blivit-en-tradition-att-aka-hit|url-status=dead}} < /ref >
