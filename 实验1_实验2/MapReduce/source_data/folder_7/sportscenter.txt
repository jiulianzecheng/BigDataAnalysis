'''SportsCenter''' is the [[daily]] [[sports]] [[news]] [[Television programme|program]] of [[ESPN]]. It is widely spread out in the whole world, with [[famous citations]] in [[movies]] and [[international editions]], as the ''[[SportsCenter Brasil]]'' (the Brazilian edition), the ''[[SportsCenter Asia]]'' and the ''[[Sportscentre]]'' (Canada). The ''SportsCenter Brasil'' has recently completed 3000 editions.
