 '''Wetzlar''' is a [[city]] at the river [[Lahn]] in [[Hesse]] in [[Germany]]. It is near [[Frankfurt/Main|Frankfurt]]. The number of people in the city is about 53,000. 
 
 Wetzlar is famous for the historic Oldtown and for [[Goethe]]. 
 
 The [[camera]] maker [[Leica Camera|Leica]] is there. 
 
 Wetzlar has a connection to the [[Bundesautobahn 45|Autobahn 45]] with three [[junction]]s.
