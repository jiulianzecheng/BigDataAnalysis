 '''Marktleuthen''' ({{IPA-de|ˌmaʁktˈlɔɪ̯tn|lang|De-Marktleuthen.ogg}}) is a [[Municipalities of Germany|municipality]] in [[Wunsiedel (district)|Wunsiedel]], in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. It is on the river [[Ohře|Eger]], 10 & nbsp;km north of [[Wunsiedel]] and 11 & nbsp;km southwest of [[Selb]].
