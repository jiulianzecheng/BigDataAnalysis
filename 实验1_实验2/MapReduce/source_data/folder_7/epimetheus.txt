'''Epimetheus''' is a [[Titan (mythology)|titan]] in [[Greek mythology]], and is the brother of [[Prometheus]], a [[Ancient Greece|Greek]] titan who gave all of the [[creature]]s something to protect themselves from the [[Weather|elements]]. By the time he came to the [[human]]s, he had run out of [[feather]]s and [[fur]] to give. He went to his brother, and he gave humans [[fire]]. 
 
 Epimetheus literally means  " thinks after "  and Prometheus means  " thinks before " .
