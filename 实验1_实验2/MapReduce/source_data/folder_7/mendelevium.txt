'''Mendelevium''' ({{pronEng|ˌmɛndəˈlɛviəm}})) is a [[synthetic element]] with the symbol '''Md''' (used to be '''Mv''') and the [[atomic number]] 101. It is a [[metal]]lic [[radioactive]] [[transuranic]] element of the [[actinide]]s.  
 [[File:Mendeleev Photographische Gesellschaft 3.jpg|thumb|left|200px|[[Dmitri Mendeleev]]]] 
 Mendelevium is created by [[einsteinium]] and [[alpha particle]]s forcefully combining and was named after [[Dmitri Mendeleev]], the creator of the [[Periodic Table]].
