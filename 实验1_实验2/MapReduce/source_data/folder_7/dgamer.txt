 '''DGamer''' ('''Disney Gamer''') is an online service made by [[The Walt Disney Company|Disney]] for use with [[Nintendo DS]] games. DGamer can be seen via the [[Nintendo Wi-Fi Connection]] or computer via Disney.com. The service is implemented by [[Disney Interactive]]. < ref name=Gamespot > [http://gamespot.com/news/6175026.html E3 '07: DGamer heads to DS] < /ref > 
 
 It was started in North America on May 15, 2008, at the same time as the release of the DS-version of ''The Chronicles of Narnia: Prince Caspian''. < ref > {{Cite web |url=http://kotaku.com/358188/disney-brings-its-own-miis-achievements-to-the-nintendo-ds |title=Disney Brings Its Own Miis, Achievements To The Nintendo DS |access-date=2008-07-07 |archive-date=2012-10-14 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20121014021240/http://kotaku.com/358188/disney-brings-its-own-miis-achievements-to-the-nintendo-ds |url-status=dead }} < /ref >  All future Disney Interactive Studios games will include DGamer features.
