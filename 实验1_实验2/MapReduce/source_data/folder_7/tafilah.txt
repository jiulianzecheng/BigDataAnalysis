 '''Tafileh''' is a [[city]] in the south of the [[Jordan|Hashemite Kingdom of Jordan]]. It is the [[Capital city|capital]] of [[Tafilah Governorate|Tafila Governorate]]. In 2015, about 27,500 people lived there. 
 
 The city was part of the [[Edom kingdom]]. It was called '''Tophel''' at that time . Tafila was occupied by the [[Nabataean Kingdom|Nabataean kingdom]]. After the [[Roman Empire|Roman conquest]], it was ruled by the [[Ghassanids]], under the authority of [[Rome]]. 
 
 There is one [[university ]]in Tafila, [[Tafila Technical University]]. The city rises {{convert|940|m|ft}} above [[sea level]].
