 '''''Nimbaphrynoides''''' is a [[genus]] of [[Bufonidae|true toads]].  They live in the highlands in the [[Mount Nimba]] region of the [[West Africa]]n countries of [[Guinea]], [[Liberia]] and [[Côte d'Ivoire]]. They are [[Critically Endangered]] due to [[habitat loss]], and [[Iron ore|iron]] and [[bauxite]] mining in their small ranges. 
 
 The two species are [[Vivipary|viviparous]].  This means they have [[internal fertilisation]]. The female gives birth to fully developed, tiny toadlets. < ref name=Sandberger > Sandberger, Hillers, Doumbia, Loua, Brede and Rödel (2010).
