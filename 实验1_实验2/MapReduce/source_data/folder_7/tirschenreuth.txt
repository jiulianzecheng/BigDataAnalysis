 '''Tirschenreuth''' ([[Northern Bavarian]]: ''Dirschnrad'', ''Diascharad'') is the [[capital city]] of the district of [[Tirschenreuth (district)|Tirschenreuth]]. It is in the northeast of [[Bavaria]], close to the Czech-Bavarian border. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 Tirschenreuth is in the north of [[Upper Palatinate]] administrative region, about 75 miles north of [[Regensburg]] and 35 miles east of [[Bayreuth]].
