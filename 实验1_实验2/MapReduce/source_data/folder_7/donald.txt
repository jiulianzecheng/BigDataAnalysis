 '''Donald''' is a masculine [[given name]]. It is pronounced ['''don'''-ld]. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Definition of Donald {{!}} Dictionary.com|url=https://www.dictionary.com/browse/donald|access-date=2023-03-17|website=www.dictionary.com|language=en}} < /ref >  The name Donald has its roots in the [[Goidelic languages|Gaelic]] name Domhnall and is typically associated with masculinity. Its meaning,  " ruler of the world "  or  " world mighty " , < ref > {{Cite web|title=Donald name Meaning and Origin|url=https://babys.name/detail-Donald-Meaning-Origin-Famous-Similar-Sibling.html|access-date=2023-03-17|website=babys.name}} < /ref >  is derived from the Celtic words dumno, meaning  " world, "  and val, meaning  " rule. "  The name gained popularity thanks to the Scottish King Donald II, who was the grandson of King Alpin and became a legendary figure. This name was ranked #592 on the US Popular Names in 2021. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Popular Baby Names|url=https://www.ssa.gov/OACT/babynames/|access-date=2023-03-17|website=www.ssa.gov|language=en}} < /ref > 
 
 Other common forms are Don, Donny and Donnie.
