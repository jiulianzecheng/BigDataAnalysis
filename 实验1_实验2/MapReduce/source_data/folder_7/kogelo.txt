 '''Kogelo''' is a village on the shores of [[Lake Victoria]] in Kenya. It is found in Kenya's [[Siaya County]] and is best known as the place of birth of former [[President of the United States|U.S. President]] [[Barack Obama]]'s father. < ref > {{cite web|last=David McKenzie|first=|last2=Lillian Leposo|title=Election rekindles memory of Kenyan village's love affair with President Obama - CNN|url=https://www.cnn.com/2012/11/05/world/africa/kenya-obama-village/index.html|publisher=}} < /ref > < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/programmes/p057fczr/p057ff6f|title=His father, Barack Hussein Obama, was born and raised in Kogelo in Kenya. - Goodbye Obama - Focus on Africa - BBC World Service|website=BBC}} < /ref >
