 '''Zalmoxes''' is a [[genus]] of [[ornithopod]] [[dinosaur]] from the [[Upper Cretaceous]] of [[Romania]]. It was a relative of [[Iguanodon]] and, like all its [[Order (biology)|order]], it was a [[herbivore]].
