 '''Pluviometry''' is a field of meteorology concerned with the measurement of [[rain]]fall. The word comes from the [[Latin]] word ''pluvius'' which means ''rain'', and the [[Greek language|Greek]] word ''metron (μέτρον)'', meaning ''[[measure]]''. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://www.thefreedictionary.com/pluviometry |title=Pluviometry |accessdate=2009-08-15 |format= |work=Free Online Dictionary }} < /ref >  The amount of rain can be measured in a [[rain gauge]], or pluviometer.
