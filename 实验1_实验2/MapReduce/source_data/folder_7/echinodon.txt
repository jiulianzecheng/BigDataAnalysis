 '''''Echinodon''''' is a [[genus]] of [[Heterodontosaur]] [[dinosaur]]. It has been found in the [[Lower Cretaceous]] of [[Europe]], 140 million years ago.   
 
 ''Saurechinodon'' is a [[synonym]] of this genus.
