'''Sexology''' is the [[Science|scientific]] study of [[Sexual behavior|human sexual behavior]]. This includes things like sexual development, [[gender identity]], [[sexual orientation]], and the question of what a normal sexual behavior is. Sexologists also look at questions as to how [[mentally ill]] or [[disabled]] people see their sexuality, or how the views on [[Sexual intercourse|sex]] change during the life of a person. An old person has different motivations to have sex than a young one or a child. Sexology also looks at [[Sexual dysfunction|sexual dysfunctions]], offers hints for [[Therapy|therapies]], and helps people manage them.
