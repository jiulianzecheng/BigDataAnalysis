 '''Marienplatz''' ([[English language|English]]: '''Mary's Square''', i.e. St. Mary, [[Blessed Virgin Mary|Our Lady]]'s Square) is a [[town square|central square]] in the centre of [[Munich]], [[Germany]]. It has been the city's main square since 1158.
