 '''''Zizhiphus''''' is a type of [[tree]] that grows [[fruit]]. The species ''Z. mauritianis'' is known as the [[Indian jujube]], and the species ''[[Ziziphus jujuba]]'' is known as the [[red date]]. They are mostly found in [[Asia]].
