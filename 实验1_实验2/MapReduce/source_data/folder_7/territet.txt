 '''Territet''' is a [[village]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Vaud]] in [[Switzerland]]. It is part of the [[municipality]] of [[Montreux]]. It is between [[Montreux]] and [[Veytaux]], on the [[Lake Geneva]] shore.
