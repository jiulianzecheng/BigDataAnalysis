 The '''plantain family''' ('''Plantaginaceae''') is a family of [[flowering plant]]s in the order [[Lamiales]]. They are a cosmopolitan family of small [[herbs]] and a few [[shrub]]s. The largest genus is ''Veronica'' with about 450 [[species]].
