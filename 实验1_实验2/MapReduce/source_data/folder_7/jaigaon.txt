 '''Jaigaon''' is a [[census town]] in [[Alipurduar subdivision]] of [[Alipurduar district]] in the [[States and union territories of India|Indian state]] of [[West Bengal]]. It is located on the border of [[India]] and [[Bhutan]]. ''Bhutan Gate'' separates the two countries. [[Phuentsholing]] is the nearest city in Bhutan to Jaigaon. Bhutan does not have roads linking to all its towns. The roads through Jaigaon are often used to reach places such as [[Samtse]], [[Gomtu]], [[Nganglam]] and [[Samdrup Jongkhar]].
