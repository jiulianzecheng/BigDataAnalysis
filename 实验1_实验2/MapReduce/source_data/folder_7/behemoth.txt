 '''Behemoth''' is a metal band from [[Gdańsk]], [[Poland]]. < ref > {{Cite web |url=http://www.behemoth.pl/site/index.php/eng#genesis |title=Band bio on behemoth.pl |access-date=2010-09-21 |archive-date=2012-04-07 |archive-url=https://www.webcitation.org/66kvA3ju2?url=http://www.behemoth.pl/site/index.php/eng#genesis |url-status=dead }} < /ref >  Their early albums were [[black metal]], but later they switched to a mixture of [[death metal]] and black metal.
