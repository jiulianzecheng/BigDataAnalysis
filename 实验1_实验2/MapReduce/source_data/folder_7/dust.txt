 '''Dust''' is very small pieces of [[matter]] in the air or on surfaces. Dust [[particle]]s are made of different things including dirt, material from [[volcanic eruption]]s, and [[Air pollution|pollution]]. Dust can also contain small amounts of plant [[pollen]], [[hair]] from people or animals, [[skin cell]]s, and many other things. < ref name= " hesskosa " > Kathleen Hess-Kosa, (2002), ''Indoor Air Quality: sampling methodologies'', page 216. CRC Press. < /ref >  Dust in [[outer space]] makes [[nebula]]s. Items can get dust if they are not used for a while.
