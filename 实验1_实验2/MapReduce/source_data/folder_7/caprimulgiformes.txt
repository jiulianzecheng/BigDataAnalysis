 The '''Caprimulgiformes''' is an [[order (biology)|order]] of [[birds]]. They are generally [[insectivorous]] and [[nocturnal]]. If they are joined as a [[clade]] with the [[Apodiformes]], the group is then known as the '''Strisores'''.
