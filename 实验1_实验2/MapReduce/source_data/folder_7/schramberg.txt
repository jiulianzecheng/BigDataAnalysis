 '''Schramberg''' is a town in [[Rottweil (district)|Rottweil]], in [[Baden-Württemberg]], [[Germany]]. It is in the eastern [[Black Forest]] and is 25 & nbsp;km northwest of [[Rottweil]]. About 22,000 people live there.
