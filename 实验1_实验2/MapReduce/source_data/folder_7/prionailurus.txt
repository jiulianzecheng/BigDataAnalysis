 '''''Prionailurus''''' is a [[genus]] of four [[species]] of small, spotted wild [[cat]]s found in [[Asia]]. They are typically forest-dwelling. Most are able to swim well; some species are actually semi-aquatic and feed mainly on fish and other aquatic animals. 
 
 Pocock's classification of ''Prionailurus'' is widely accepted: < ref name=msw3 > {{MSW3 Wozencraft |id=14000180 |pages = 543-544}} < /ref > 
 * [[Leopard cat]] (''Prionailurus bengalensis'') 
 * [[Rusty-spotted cat]] (''Prionailurus rubiginosus'') 
 * [[Fishing cat]] (''Prionailurus viverrinus'') 
 * [[Flat-headed cat]] (''Prionailurus planiceps'')
