 ==Etymology== 
 Multan is '''Moolastan''' which means the Place of origin (Moolam is source or origin). 
 
 ==History== 
 The old city of Multan is a [[Walled city]] which is built on an ancient mound. [[Archaeologist]]s believe the mound is made up of earlier cities going back to the [[Bronze Age]]. < ref name=hm/ >  There were six gates into the old city but these have been destroyed over time. Three gates were rebuilt by the [[British]] when they governed the country. < ref name=hm/ >  Inside the wall is a circular road, called the ''alang''. < ref name=hm/ >  The old city has narrow and winding streets, with houses crowded close together. 
 
 ==Political importance== 
 Multan is the main center of the South Punjab, [[Seraiki]] speaking region. The people here think their language is separate from the majority [[Punjabi language]] of Punjab and want to create a separate province of their own, called [[Seraikistan]].{{fact|date=September 2014}}This city has some important political figures. Syed [[Yousaf Raza Gillani]],a former Prime Minister of Pakistan belongs to Multan. Another well-known politician from here is [[Shah Mehmood Qureshi]].
