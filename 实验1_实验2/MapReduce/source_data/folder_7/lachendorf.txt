 '''Lachendorf''' ([[West Low German]]: ''Lachendörp'') is a municipality in [[Celle (district)|Celle]], in [[Lower Saxony]], [[Germany]]. It is about 10 & nbsp;km east of [[Celle]]. 
 
 Lachendorf is also the seat of the [[Samtgemeinde|collective municipality]] [[Lachendorf (Samtgemeinde)|Lachendorf]].
