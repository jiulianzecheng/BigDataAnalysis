The '''Aqdas''' ({{lang-ar|الكتاب الأقدس}}, {{transl|ar|al-Kitābu l-Aqdas}}; {{lang-fa|كتاب اقدس}}, {{transl|fa|Kitáb-i-Aqdas}}) is the central holy book of the [[Baha'i Faith]]. It was written by [[Bahá'u'lláh]], the founder of the Baha'i Faith and it deals with various topics including ethics, prophecies and devotion.
