 '''Batam''' (also called '''Batam City''') is an [[industrial city]] in the province of [[Riau Islands]], [[Indonesia]]. Over 1 million people live in it. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://batamkota.bps.go.id/dynamictable/2017/10/03/23/penduduk-kota-batam-menurut-kecamatan-dan-jenis-kelamin-2011-2016.html|title=Population by Disstrict and Sex in Batam Municipality, 2011-2016|website=batamkota.bps.go.id|publisher=Statistics of Batam City|access-date=23 July 2019|archive-date=23 July 2019|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190723131844/https://batamkota.bps.go.id/dynamictable/2017/10/03/23/penduduk-kota-batam-menurut-kecamatan-dan-jenis-kelamin-2011-2016.html|url-status=dead}} < /ref >  It is the biggest city in Riau Islands, and the third biggest city in [[Sumatra]]. It is part of the Indonesia-Malaysia-Thailand and Indonesia-Malaysia-Singapore [[free-trade zone]]. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://imtgt.org/|title=Indonesia-Malaysia-Thailand Growth Triangle (IMT-GT)|access-date=23 July 2019|website=imtgt.org}} < /ref > 
 
 The city administrative covers an area of 3 big islands with some small islands surround them. The 3 big islands are Batam, Rempang, and Galang. The islands are conected by bridges called Barelang Bridges ({{lang-id|Jembatan Barelang}}) that is a short name for Batam-Rempang-Galang Bridges. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.kompasiana.com/delicia/5500bed2a333112370511e1b/ikon-kota-batam-jembatan-barelang-karya-megah-b-j-habibie?page=all|title=Ikon Kota Batam  "  Jembatan Barelang  "  Karya Megah B.J.
