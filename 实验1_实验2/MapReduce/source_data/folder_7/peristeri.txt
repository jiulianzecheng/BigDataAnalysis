 '''Peristeri''' ({{lang-el|Περιστέρι}}) is a suburban [[municipality]] in the northwestern part of [[Athens]] agglomeration, [[Greece]].  It has 139,981 inhabitants (2011 census). It is the seventh-largest municipality of Greece by population. It is about {{convert|4|km|mi|abbr=on}}.
