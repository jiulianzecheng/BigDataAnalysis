 '''Hallsberg''' is an [[urban area]] in the [[counties of Sweden|county]] of [[Örebro County|Örebro]] in [[Sweden]]. It is the seat of [[Hallsberg Municipality]]. Hallsberg is well-known for its [[junction (rail)|railroad junctions]].
