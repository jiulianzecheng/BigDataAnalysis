 '''Leerdam''' is a city in the [[Netherlands]], in the province of [[Utrecht (province)|Utrecht]]. It has about 21,000 inhabitants (2016). Since 2019, it has been part of the new municipality of [[Vijfheerenlanden]]. Until 2019, Leerdam was a municipality in the province of [[South Holland]].
