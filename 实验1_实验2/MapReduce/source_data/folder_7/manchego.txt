 '''Manchego''' ([[Spanish language|Spanish]]: Queso Manchego) is a type of [[cheese]] from the [[Spain|Spanish]] [[La Mancha]] region. It is made from sheep's milk, specifically the [[Manchega]] breed. It can be aged for 60 days to 2 years. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Spanish food - Manchego cheese|url=http://www.iberianfoods.co.uk/manchego.htm|access-date=2021-07-05|website=www.iberianfoods.co.uk}} < /ref >
