 The '''Alamanni''', '''Allemanni''', or '''Alemanni''' were a group of west Germanic peoples. They lived around the upper [[Main]], which is the longest tributary of the [[Rhine]]. They expanded their territory around parts of Germania and present-day [[Alsace]]. The land they lived on was called [[Alemannia]]. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://penelope.uchicago.edu/Thayer/E/Roman/Texts/Cassius_Dio/78*.html|publisher=University of Chicago|title=Cassius Dio: Roman History}} < /ref >
