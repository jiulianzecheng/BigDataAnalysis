'''Propellant''' is something that expands as a gas to propel something else. '''Aerosol spray''' cans, for example, have a liquid propellant that boils into a gas to push another liquid out of the can. [[Gun]] < nowiki/ > s and [[rocket]] < nowiki/ > s have propellants in them that burn, creating hot gases.
