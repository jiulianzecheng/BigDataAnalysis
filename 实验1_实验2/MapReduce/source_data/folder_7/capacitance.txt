 '''Capacitance''' is the ability of a body to store [[electric charge]].  A common device to do such task is a [[capacitor]]. The [[International System of Units|SI unit]] for capacitance is ''[[farad]]''. 
 
 < math > C =\frac QV < /math > 
 
 Where Q is the charge in the object and V is voltage across that object.
