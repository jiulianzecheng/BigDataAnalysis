'''Homophrosyne''' is an [[Ancient Greece|Ancient Greek]] term which means two people feeling or thinking alike. It is used in [[Homer]]'s [[epic poem]], the ''[[Odyssey]]'' to describe the marriage of [[Odysseus]] and [[Penelope]]. < ref > Van Nortwick, Thomas (2010).
