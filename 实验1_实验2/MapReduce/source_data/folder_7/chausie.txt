 The '''Chausie''' is a [[Cat breeds|breed]] of [[cat]]. The Chausie is a cross between [[jungle cat]] and [[domestic short-haired cat]]. < ref name= " :0 " > {{Cite news|url=https://tica.org/resources/our-association/rules-regulations/file/165-registration-rules/|title=TICA Registration Rules, pg. 34|access-date=2019-10-27}} < /ref > 
 
 == History == 
 Chausie was first bred around the 1960s in the [[United States]]. The wild jungle cat was crossed with a short-haired domestic cat, giving birth to the Chausie cat breed. In 1995, the breed was recognized by [[The International Cat Association|TICA]]. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.chausie-kittens.com/cuhx.htm|title=Chausie Kittens and Chausie Cats in California|last=Bird|first=Cris|website=Sarsenstone Chausies Cattery|publisher=Chausie-kittens.com|access-date=2012-09-11|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140305153131/http://www.chausie-kittens.com/cuhx.htm|archive-date=2014-03-05|url-status=dead}} < /ref > < ref name= " :2 " > {{cite web|url=https://www.tica.org/en/component/k2/item/199-chausie-introduction|title=TICA Chausie Breed Information|publisher=TICA|access-date=2013-05-02|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181016164928/https://www.tica.org/en/component/k2/item/199-chausie-introduction|archive-date=2018-10-16|url-status=dead}} < /ref > 
 
 == Appearance == 
 Chausie is a medium-bodied, long and slender cat, with males weighing around 4.5-7.5 kg, and females 3.4-5 kg. Chausies are good jumpers and runners, due to their long body. Chausie is a short-haired cat with a variety of coat colors, including silver, brown, solid black, black tabi, and spotted black. < ref name= " :1 " > {{Cite news|url=https://www.chausiebreedsection.com/question.html |title=TICA's Chausie Breed Section Q & A |access-date=2019-10-27}} < /ref > < ref name= " :2 "  / > < ref name= " autogenerated5 " > {{cite web|url=https://www.tica.org/pdf/publications/standards/cu.pdf|title=TICA Chausie Breed Standards|date=2013-05-01|website=The International Cat Association (TICA)|pages=5, 6|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20171111094846/http://www.tica.org/pdf/publications/standards/cu.pdf|archive-date=2017-11-11|url-status=dead}} < /ref > 
 
 == Behavior == 
 Chausie is a curious, intelligent, calm, active and playful cat. Chausie also loves to explore outside its home. Chausie doesn't like being left alone in the house. Chausie can also make friends with other pets, such as dogs. < ref name=kucinggue > [http://kucinggue.blogspot.com/2013/01/mengenal-kucing-chausie.html Mengenal Kucing Chausie] (31 Januari 2013).
