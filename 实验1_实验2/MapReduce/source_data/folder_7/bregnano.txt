 '''Bregnano''' is a ''[[comune]]'' (municipality) in the [[Province of Como]] in the [[Italy|Italian]] region of [[Lombardy]]. It is about {{convert|25|km|mi}} northwest of [[Milan]] and about {{convert|13|km|mi|0}} south of [[Como]]. As of 31 March 2017, 6,466 people lived there. It has an area of 6.2 & nbsp;kmÂ². 
 
 Bregnano is next to these municipalities: [[Cadorago]], [[Cermenate]], [[Lazzate]], [[Lomazzo]], [[Rovellasca]]. 
 
 It has three churches called San Michele, San Giorgio and Santi Ippolito e Cassiano.
