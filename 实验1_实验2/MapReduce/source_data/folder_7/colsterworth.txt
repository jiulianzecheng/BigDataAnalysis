 '''Colsterworth''' is a village and [[civil parish]] in [[Lincolnshire]], England. The parish contains the hamlet of [[Woolsthorpe-by-Colsterworth]]. In 2001 there were 1,508 people living in Colsterworth. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.ons.gov.uk/help/localstatistics|title=Local statistics - Office for National Statistics|website=www.ons.gov.uk}} < /ref >
