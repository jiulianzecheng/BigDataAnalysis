 '''''Nemegtosaurus''''' is a [[genus]] of [[titanosaur]] [[sauropod]] [[dinosaur]] found in [[Mongolia]]. A possible synonym of ''Nemegtosaurus'' is ''[[Opisthocoelicaudia]]''. It was named after the place it was discovered, the [[Nemegt Basin]].
