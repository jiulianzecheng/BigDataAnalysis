 '''Nissewaard''' is a [[List of municipalities of the Netherlands|municipality]] in the [[Netherlands|Dutch]] [[Provinces of the Netherlands|province]] of [[South Holland]]. In 2019, 84,797 people lived there. 
 
 It was created on 1 January 2015 from the former municipalities of [[Bernisse]] and [[Spijkenisse]].
