 '''Propane''' is an [[organic compound]] with the [[chemical formula]] {{chem|[[Carbon|C]]|3|[[Hydrogen|H]]|8}}. It is an [[alkane]] with three carbon [[atom]]s. It is used in fuels. 
 
 It begins to [[combustion|burn]] very quickly.  
 Its melting temperature is −187.7 °C; its boiling temperature is −42 °C; its density is 1.83 g/l. 
 
 Propane is extracted from natural [[gasoline]] or from [[petroleum]].
