 An '''appetizer''' is a small dish of an overall [[meal]]. It can also be a drink or multiple drinks containing alcohol. Common examples include: [[shrimp]] [[cocktail]], calamari, [[salad]], [[potato]] skins, [[mussel]]s, bruschetta or [[cheese]] and [[cracker]]s. An appetizer may also be very elegant in some restaurants. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://dictionary.reference.com/browse/appetizer |title=Appetizer  & #124; Define Appetizer at Dictionary.com |publisher=Dictionary.reference.com |access-date=2010-12-08}} < /ref > < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.wisegeek.com/what-are-appetizers.htm |title=What are Appetizers? |publisher=Wisegeek.com |access-date=2010-12-08}} < /ref > 
 
 They are often referred to as snacks or hors d'oeuvres.
