 '''Suffering''' is [[pain]] or mental or [[emotion]]al unhappiness caused by bad things happening, like [[disaster]]s or [[illness]]. It is an important topic in [[philosophy]], and [[religion]]. Much [[health care]] exists to help people who are suffering.
