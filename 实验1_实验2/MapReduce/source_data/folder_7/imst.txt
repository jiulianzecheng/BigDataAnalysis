 '''Imst''' is a town in the [[Austria]]n [[States of Austria|state]] of [[Tyrol (state)|Tyrol]]. It is the capital of the district of [[Imst District|Imst]]. As of 1 January 2019, 10,628 people lived there. 
 
 It is on the river [[Inn (river)|Inn]] in western Tyrol, and is about {{convert|50|km|0|abbr=off}} west of [[Innsbruck]].
