 '''Ayvalik''' ([[Turkish language|Turkish]] '''Ayvalık''') is a seaside [[town]] in the [[west]] of [[Turkey]] in the [[province]] of [[Balıkesir]]. The town had an important number of [[Greece|Greek]] people living there. After the [[Turkish Independence War]] the population changes with [[Turkey|Turkish]] and [[Greece|Greek]] population and Greek population have gotten smaller.
