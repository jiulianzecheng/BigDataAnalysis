 " '''Downhearted''' "  is a 1980 song by Australian band [[Australian Crawl]]. It was taken from their debut studio album ''[[The Boys Light Up]]''. It went to number 12 in Australia and number 25 in New Zealand. It was covered by [[John Farnham]] for his 2005 album  " ''[[I Remember When I Was Young]]'' " .
