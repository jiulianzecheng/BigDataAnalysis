 '''PepsiCo, Inc.''' is an American [[multinational corporation|multinational]] food, snack and [[beverage corporation]] headquartered in [[Harrison, New York]], in the hamlet of [[Purchase, New York|Purchase]]. PepsiCo manufactures, marketing, and distribution of grain-based snack foods, beverages, and other products.
