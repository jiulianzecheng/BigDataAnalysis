 '''Leboncoin''' is a French website for [[Classified advertising|classified ads]]. 
 
 == Operation == 
 The company founded in 2006 by the [[Norway|Norwegian]] company Schibsted. It allows individuals to buy and sell goods locally, and its free service is funded by charging fees to professionals and advertisers. < ref > {{Cite web|date=April 2018|title=Airbnb, Blablacar, Le Bon Coin ... Pourquoi et comment utilise-t-on les plateformes de consommation collaborative|url=https://hal.archives-ouvertes.fr/hal-01757813/document}} < /ref >  Optional functions are also available for a fee. Leboncoin has become a trusted third party for consumer goods transactions and vacation rentals, and acts as an online payment intermediary through a partnership with the Dutch company Adyen. < ref > {{Cite news|date=2013-01-04|title=La France au miroir du  " Bon Coin " |language=fr|work=Le Monde.fr|url=https://www.lemonde.fr/vous/article/2013/01/04/la-france-au-miroir-du-bon-coin_1812883_3238.html|access-date=2021-12-19}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite web|title=Paiement, livraison, IA : Leboncoin fait sa révolution technologique|url=https://www.lsa-conso.fr/leboncoin-fait-sa-revolution-technologique,296466|access-date=2021-12-19|website=lsa-conso.fr|language=fr}} < /ref >  It is the fourth most visited website in France, with a monthly audience of 28 million unique visitors in early 2017, and recorded 20.4 million visits in a single day in February 2021. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Le Bon Coin se porte bien, malgré les déboires de sa maison mère|url=https://www.lsa-conso.fr/leboncoin-se-porte-bien-malgre-les-deboires-de-sa-maison-mere,372868|access-date=2021-12-19|website=lsa-conso.fr|language=fr}} < /ref >
