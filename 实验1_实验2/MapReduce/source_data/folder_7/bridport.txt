 '''Bridport''' is a town near the A35 road in [[Dorset]], England.  It is near the [[coast]] at the western end of [[Chesil Beach]]. 
 
 In 2006 [[West Dorset]] district was named 10th best place to live in the UK. < ref > [http://www.channel4.com/4homes/ontv/best & worst/2006/wdorset.html best and worst places to live 2006] < /ref >  The town is twinned with [[Saint-Vaast-la-Hougue]], [[France]].
