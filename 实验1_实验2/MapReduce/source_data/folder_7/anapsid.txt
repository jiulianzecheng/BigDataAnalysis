 An '''anapsid''' is an [[amniote]] whose skull does not have openings on the sides behind the eyes. < ref > Pough F.H. et al. 2002. ''Vertebrate life'', 6th ed. Prentice Hall, NJ. {{ISBN|0130412481}} < /ref >  This is 
 #A primitive ('basal') condition for early amniotes in the [[Mississippian]] (early [[Carboniferous]]). 
 #A secondary ('derived') condition of some other reptile groups, probably including [[turtle]]s ([[Chelonia]]). 
 
 Some other groups were anapsids, such as the [[mesosaur]]s.
