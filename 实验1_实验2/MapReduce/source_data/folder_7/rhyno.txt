 '''Terrance  " Terry "  Gerin''' (born October 7, 1975) is an [[Americans|American]] [[professional wrestler]]. He is currently signed to [[Impact Wrestling]] and competes under the ring name '''Rhino'''. 
 
 He is also known for his time in [[Extreme Championship Wrestling]] (ECW), [[WWE]], and Impact Wrestling when it was known as Total Nonstop Action Wrestling (TNA). 
 
 During his time in ECW, he was a one-time [[ECW World Heavyweight Championship|ECW World Heavyweight Champion]] and a two-time and final [[ECW World Television Championship|ECW World Television Champion]]. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.wwe.com/titlehistory/ecw-championship|accessdate=October 22, 2023|title=ECW Championship history|publisher=WWE}} < /ref > < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.wwe.com/titlehistory/ecw-world-television-championship|accessdate=October 22, 2023|title=ECW World Television Championship history|publisher=WWE}} < /ref > 
 
 In WWE, he was a three-time [[WWE Hardcore Championship|WWF Hardcore Champion]], one-time [[WWE United States Championship|WCW United States Championship]], and a one-time and the first [[WWE SmackDown Tag Team Championship|WWE SmackDown Tag Team Champion]]. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.wwe.com/titlehistory/hardcore-championship|accessdate=October 22, 2023|title=Hardcore Championship history|publisher=WWE}} < /ref > < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.wwe.com/titlehistory/united-states-championship|accessdate=October 22, 2023|title=United States Championship history|publisher=WWE}} < /ref > < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.wwe.com/classics/titlehistory/smackdown-tag-team-championships|accessdate=October 22, 2023|title=SmackDown Tag Team Championship history|publisher=WWE}} < /ref > 
 
 Outside of wrestling, Gerin has also been involved in [[politics]]. On August 2, 2016, Gerin won the Republican primary for Michigan's 15th District. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.wxyz.com/news/political/wwe-professional-wrestler-rhyno-wins-15th-district-michigan-state-house-republican-primary-race|accessdate=October 22, 2023|title=WWE professional wrestler Rhyno wins 15th District Michigan State House Republican primary race|publisher=Detroit WXYZ}} < /ref >  He ran against Democrat [[Abdullah Hammoud]] and lost 21,739 votes to 13,452. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://mielections.us/election/results/2016GEN_CENR.html|accessdate=October 22, 2023|title=2016 Michigan Election Results|publisher=Department of State, Michigan}} < /ref >
