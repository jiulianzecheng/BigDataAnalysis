'''GLONASS''' (''{{transl|ru|Globalnaya Navigatsionnaya Sputnikovaya Sistema}}'') is a [[radio]]-based [[satellite]] [[navigation]] system. It is operated by the [[Russia]]n Defence Forces. It provides an alternative to the [[United States]]' [[Global Positioning System]] (GPS). These are the only two navigational systems with global coverage.
