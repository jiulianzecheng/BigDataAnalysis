 '''Inverbervie''' ([[Ordnance Survey]]) < ref > [[British national grid reference system|grid reference]] {{gbmappingsmall|NO830726}} < /ref >  is a small town on the north-east [[coast]] of [[Scotland]], south of [[Stonehaven]], in the [[Aberdeenshire|Aberdeenshire council area]]. 
 
 The Inverbervie name derives from ''Inbhir Beirbhe'', meaning ''Mouth of the [[Bervie Water|River Bervie]]'' in [[Scottish Gaelic]].

 {{ISBN|190347194X}} < /ref > 
 * [[Lewis Grassic Gibbon]], author, lived in nearby Arbuthnott
