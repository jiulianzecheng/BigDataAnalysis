 '''Chermignon''' is a former [[municipality]] in the [[district]] [[Sierre (district)|Sierre]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Valais]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 On 1 January 2017, the former municipalities of Chermignon, [[Mollens, Valais|Mollens]], [[Montana, Switzerland|Montana]] and [[Randogne]] merged into the new municipality of [[Crans-Montana]].
