'''Malaise''' is the general feeling of being [[sick]]. It can be described as a general discomfort or mild pain throughout the body. Malaise is caused by many kinds of illnesses, which can be very mild like the [[common cold]]. They can also be worse, like [[cancer]]. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://medlineplus.gov/ency/article/003089.htm|title=Malaise: MedlinePlus Medical Encyclopedia|website=medlineplus.gov}} < /ref >
