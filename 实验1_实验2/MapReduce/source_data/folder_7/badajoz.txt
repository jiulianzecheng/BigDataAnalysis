 '''Badajoz''' ({{lang-ext|Baajós}}) is the capital of the [[Province of Badajoz|Badajoz Province]] in the [[autonomous communities of Spain|autonomous community]] of [[Extremadura]], Spain. The population in 2011 was 151,565. 
 
 A major Napoleonic siege was fought here between Britain and France
