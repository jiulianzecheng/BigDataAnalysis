 '''Pibrac''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]] of 8,523 people (2020). It is in [[Occitanie]] in the [[Haute-Garonne]] [[Departments of France|department]] in southwest [[France]]. It is a suburb of the city of [[Toulouse]].
