 '''''Clockers''''' is a 1995 [[United States|American]] [[neighborhood|hood]] and [[crime]] [[drama movie]]. It is about life in the [[housing project|project]]s. [[Spike Lee]] directed the movie. It is based on 1992 [[novel]] [[Clockers (movie)|Clockers]]. 
 
 ==Plot== 
 In a [[Brooklyn]] [[housing]] project, clockers – street level drug dealers – sell [[illegal drugs|drugs]] for a man named Rodney Little. Rodney tells one of his lead drug dealers, Ronald  " Strike "  Durham, that another dealer, Darryl Adams, is stealing from him and needs to be killed. Rodney wants Strike to [[murder]] Darryl. Strike then meets with his brother, Victor Dunham, and wants Victor to kill Darryl Adams.
