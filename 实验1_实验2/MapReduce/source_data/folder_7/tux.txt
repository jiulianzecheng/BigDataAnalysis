 '''Tux''' is a [[mascot]] of the [[Linux]] [[operating system]]; a cartoon [[penguin]] made by [[Larry Ewing]] in [[1996]]. Wherever he is seen, it means such a system can work with Linux. He is also a character in some [[video games]] made for Linux, like ''Super Tux''. 
 
 The name  " Tux "  comes from James Hughes, who named the Linux mascot that as an shortened form of  " '''T'''orvalds' '''U'''ni'''X''', "  as [[Linus Torvalds]] was the creator of the [[Linux kernel]]. < ref > {{Cite web|date=2007-07-01|title=Linux-Kernel Archive: Re: Let's name the penguin! (was: Re: Linux 2.0 really _is_ released..)|url=http://www.ussg.iu.edu/hypermail/linux/kernel/9606.1/0175.html|access-date=2022-11-01|website=web.archive.org|archive-date=2007-07-01|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20070701044314/http://www.ussg.iu.edu/hypermail/linux/kernel/9606.1/0175.html|url-status=dead}} < /ref >  Tux is also an abbreviation of [[tuxedo]].
