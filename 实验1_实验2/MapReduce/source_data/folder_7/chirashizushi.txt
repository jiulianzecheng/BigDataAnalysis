'''Chirashizushi''' (ちらし寿司,  " scattered sushi " , also referred to as ''barazushi'') is a traditional [[Japanese food]] made with [[rice]] mixed with [[vinegar]]. It has several ingredients on top, such as [[egg (food)|egg]], [[bamboo]] shoots, [[seafood]], and [[nori]].
