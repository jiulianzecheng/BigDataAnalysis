 '''Inzell''' is a [[Municipalities of Germany|municipality]] in [[Traunstein (district)|Traunstein]] in [[Bavaria]], Germany. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 Inzell is in a wide valley floor in the [[Chiemgau Alps]], enclosed by the mountains of [[Rauschberg]], [[Zinnkopf]], [[Teisenberg]] and the range of [[Staufen (Chiemgau Alps)|Staufen]]. In the municipality, the convergence of the [[Großwaldbach]] and [[Falkenseebach]] rivers forms the [[Rote Traun]] river. < ref name=Chiemgauer_Alpen > {{cite web|title=Inzell|url=http://www.lag-chiemgauer-alpen.de/index.php?article_id=41|website=LAG Chiemgauer Alpen|access-date=2 February 2015|language=de}} < /ref >  Inzell is also known as the gateway to [[Berchtesgaden National Park]] due to the ''Zwing'', a mountain passage between Inzell and [[Schneizlreuth|Weißbach]], which separates the Chiemgau from Berchtesgaden. < ref name=Chiemgauer_Alpen / >
