 '''Chiavenna''' is a ''[[comune]]'' in the [[Province of Sondrio]] in the [[Italy|Italian]] region of [[Lombardy]]. 
 
 == Geography == 
 The town is on the right bank of the river [[Mera (Lake Como)|Mera]] about {{convert|16|km|mi|0}} north of [[Lake Como]]. The river course leads up to [[Val Bregaglia]] in the east and the [[Switzerland|Swiss]] border at [[Castasegna]]. In the north, the [[Valle Spluga]] stretches up to [[Splügenpass]] and the road to [[Chur]] in [[Graubünden]].
