'''Nira-senbei''' are a type of [[rice cracker]] made in [[Japan]]. Its name means  " chive crackers "  because they are made of baked [[buckwheat]] flour mixed with [[chives]]. They are local [[cuisine]] that have been eaten since the end of the [[Edo period]] in [[Nagano Prefecture]]. Nira-senbei are a popular [[snack]] in [[Suzaka]], near [[Nagano, Nagano|Nagano]] city.
