 '''Pakhangba''' ({{lang-omp|Pakhangpa}}) is a [[:en:wikt:primordial|primordial]] [[:en:wikt:serpentine|serpentine]] dragon god in [[Meitei mythology]] and [[Meitei religion|religion]]. < ref > {{Cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=D28qzSc6xOgC & q=pakhangba+serpent|title=Bulletin - Indian Museum|last=Museum|first=Indian|date=1998|language=en}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=9DqBAAAAMAAJ & q=pakhangba+serpent|title=Tribal Art of Manipur|last1=Bahadur|first1=Mutua|last2=Santabai|first2=Y.|date=1989|publisher=Mutua Museum|language=en}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=135jMeynvZQC & q=pakhangba+serpent|title=Dancing Mosaic: Issues on Dance Hybridity|last=Nor|first=Mohd Anis Md|date=2012|publisher=Cultural Centre, University of Malaya|isbn=978-967-0380-17-9|language=en}} < /ref >  He is the [[protect|protector]] of the [[universe]] and the [[destroyer]] of the [[evil]]s. < ref > {{Cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=izhuAAAAMAAJ & q=pakhangba+universal|title=Proceedings of North East India History Association|last=Session|first=North East India History Association|date=2001|publisher=The Association|language=en}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=qo0MAQAAMAAJ & q=pakhangba|title=Journal of the Assam Research Society|last=Samiti|first=Kāmarūpa Anusandhāna|date=2007|publisher=Kāmarūpa Anusandhān Samiti|language=en}} < /ref >  He is the son of [[Leimarel Sidabi]], the supreme mother earth goddess. 
 
 ==Children== 
 Pakhangba had seven sons. They are the seven great dragons, the mythical ancestors of the seven clan dynasties of the [[Meitei ethnicity]]. 
 They are: 
 *Mangang dragon 
 *Luwang dragon 
 *Khuman dragon 
 *Angom dragon 
 *Moilang dragon 
 *Kha Nganpa dragon 
 *Salang Leishangthem dragon
