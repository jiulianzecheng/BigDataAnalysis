 '''Eumetabola''' is an unranked [[Clade]] in the Infraclass [[Neoptera]]. It contains every Neoptera clade except [[Embioptera]] and [[Orthoptera]]. It has the biggest number of species of any clade in the [[Animal|Animal Kingdom]].
