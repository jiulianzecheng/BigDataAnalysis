 A '''lyre''' (pronounce to rhyme with  " fire " ) is an instrument which is like a mixture between a [[harp]] and a [[guitar]]. It is held in one hand and the strings are strummed using the other hand.  Lyres were among the first [[string instrument]]s to be invented.
