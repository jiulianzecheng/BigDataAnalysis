 '''''Telmatosaurus''''' (meaning  " marsh lizard " ) is a genus of basal [[hadrosaurid]] [[dinosaur]] from the [[Late Cretaceous]]. It was about five meters (16 ft) long. It was found in what is now [[Romania]].
