 '''Loiret''' is a [[Departments of France|department]] in north-central [[France]] in the [[Centre-Val de Loire]] [[Regions of France|region]]. It is named after the [[Loiret (river)|Loiret]] river, a [[tributary]] of [[Loire River|Loire]] that is completely within the department. 
 
 Its [[Prefectures in France|prefecture]] is [[Orléans]] and there are two [[Prefectures in France|subprefectures]]: [[Montargis]] and [[Pithiviers]]. 
 
 ==History== 
 The department is one of the 83 French departments made during the [[French revolution]], on 4 March 1790. The department was created from part of the former province of [[Orléanais]]. 
 
 The new department had seven [[district]]s: Orléans, Beaugency, Neuville, Pithiviers, Montargis, Gien and Boiscommun. The [[Capital city|capital]] (now [[Prefectures in France|prefecture]]) was [[Orléans]]. < ref name=splaf > {{cite web |url=http://splaf.free.fr/45his.html |title=Historique du Loiret |work=Le SPLAF |language=French |accessdate=12 August 2017 |date= }} < /ref > 
 
 In 1800, with the creation of the [[Arrondissements of France|''arrondissements'']] in France, the seven districts were changed into four ''arrondissements'': Orléans, Gien, Montargis and Pithiviers. < ref name=splaf / > 
 
 After the [[Battle of Waterloo]] (18 June 1815), the department was occupied by the [[Bavarian army]] from June 1815 to November 1818. 
 
 On 10 September 1926, the ''arrondissement'' of Gien and Pithiviers were eliminated but in 1942 Pithiviers became again an ''arrondissement'' of the department. < ref name=splaf / >

 The highest point of the department is in the place called ''Col des Étourneaux'' ({{coord|47|32|0|N|2|41|49|E|display:inline}}), in the ''commune'' of [[Pierrefitte-ès-Bois]], and that is {{convert|273|m|ft|0|abbr=on}} [[above sea level]]; it is in northwestern [[Massif Central]]. < ref name=pbag > {{cite web |url=http://www.peakbagger.com/peak.aspx?pid=9678 |title=Loiret High Point, France |publisher=Peakbagger.com |language= |accessdate=12 August 2017 |date= }} < /ref > 
 
 The main rivers are: 
 * [[Loire River|Loire]], that flows through the department from the southeast to the west. 
 * [[Loiret (river)|Loiret]], a short [[tributary]] of the Loire and that gives its name to the department. 
 * [[Loing]], 142 & nbsp;km long. 
 * [[Cosson]], 96.4 & nbsp;km long. 
 * [[Essonne (river)|Essonne]], 97 & nbsp;km long. 
 
 There are several natural regions in the Loiret department: 
 # The '''Loire Valley''', the valley around the [[Loire River|Loire]] river. 
 # '''Beauce''', to the northwest of the department; it is a [[limestone]] [[plateau]] and an important [[farming]] region where [[wheat]] is grown. 
 # '''Gâtinais''', to the east around the city of [[Montargis]]. 
 # The '''Forêt d'Orléans''' ( " Forest of Orléans " ), the largest national [[forest]] of France; it is to the north of the Loire river and to the south of the Beauce region. 
 # '''Sologne''', south of the Loire river. 
 # '''Berry''' (a small part), to the southeast. 
 # '''Puisaye''', between the Berry and the Gâtinais. 
 
 === Climate === 
 The [[Köppen climate classification]] type for the climate at [[Orléans]] is an  " [[Oceanic climate]] "  (also known as Marine West Coast Climate) and of the subtype '''Cfb'''. < ref name= " wbase " > {{cite web|work= |publisher=Weatherbase |title=Orleans, France - Köppen Climate Classification |url=http://www.weatherbase.com/weather/weather-summary.php3?s=94270 & cityname=Orleans%2C+Centre%2C+France & units= |language= |accessdate=13 August 2017}} < /ref > 
 
 The [[average]] amount of [[precipitation]] for the year in Orléans is {{Convert|599.4|mm|in|1|abbr=on }}. The month with the most precipitation on average is August with {{Convert|58.4|mm|in|1|abbr=on }} of precipitation. The month with the least precipitation on average is March with an average of {{Convert|38.1|mm|in|1|abbr=on }}. 
 
 The average temperature for the year in Orléans is {{Convert|10.6|°C|°F }}. The warmest month, on average, is July with an average temperature of {{Convert|18.3|°C|°F }}. The coolest month on average is January, with an average temperature of {{Convert|2.8|°C|°F }}. 
 
 ==Administration== 
 The department is managed by the ''Departmental Council of the Loiret'' in [[Orléans]]. Loiret is part of the region of [[Centre-Val de Loire]].

 ==Demographics== 
 The inhabitants of Loiret are known, in [[French language|French]], as ''Loiretains'' (women: ''Loiretaines''). < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.habitants.fr/loiret-45|title=Loiret |publisher=habitants.fr |language=French |accessdate=13 August 2017 |date= }} < /ref > 
 
 Loiret has a population, in 2014, of 669,737, < ref name=insee / >  for a population density of {{Pop density|669737|6775.2|km2|prec=1|disp=num}} inhabitants/km < sup > 2 < /sup > . The ''arrondissement'' of Orléans, with 435,676 inhabitants, is by far the largest. < ref name=insee2 / >
