'''Ferrocement''' is both a method and a material used in [[building]] or [[sculpture|sculpting]] with [[cement]], [[sand]], [[water]] and [[wire]] or mesh material. It is often called the thin shell. Thin shell ferrocement offers strength and economy and can be used in many ways including: building homes, creating sculptures, or building [[boat]]s and ships.
