 '''Frascarolo''' is a ''[[comune]]'' (township) in the [[Province of Pavia]] in the [[Italy|Italian]] region [[Lombardy]], which is about 60 & nbsp;km southwest of [[Milan]] and about 40 & nbsp;km southwest of [[Pavia]]. As of 31 December 2004, it had 1,277 people and an area of 23.4 & nbsp;kmÂ². < ref name= " istat " > All demographics and other statistics: Italian statistical institute [[Istat]]. < /ref > 
 
 Frascarolo is on the edge of the following townships: [[Bassignana]], [[Gambarana]], [[Mede, Lombardy|Mede]], [[Suardi]], [[Torre Beretti e Castellaro]], [[Valenza]].
