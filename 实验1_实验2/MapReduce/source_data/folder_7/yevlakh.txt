 '''Yevlakh''' is a small [[city]] in [[Azerbaijan]].  It is {{convert|265|km|abbr=off}} west of capital [[Baku]]. It is in the [[Yevlakh Rayon]] but is separate. The name is from an Old Turkic word meaning 'swampy place.' < ref > E.M. Pospelov, ''Geograficheskie nazvaniya mira'' (Moscow, 1998), p. 149. < /ref >  It was the birthplace of [[Pavel Florensky]].
