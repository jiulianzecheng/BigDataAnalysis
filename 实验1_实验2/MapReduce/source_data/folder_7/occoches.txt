 '''Occoches''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is in [[Hauts-de-France]] in the [[Somme]] [[Departments of France|department]] in north [[France]]. The altitude of the city hall of Occoches is approximately 50 meters. The Occoches surface is 7.09 km ². The population of Occoches was 121 in 1999, 128 in 2006 and 129 in 2007.
