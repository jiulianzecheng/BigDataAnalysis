 '''Pellet''' is a term used for a small [[particle]] or [[grain]], typically one created by compressing an original material.  The term is used in a variety of specific contexts. 
 
 * [[Pellet (air gun)|Air gun pellets]] are non-spherical projectiles fired from [[air gun]]s 
 * [[Airsoft pellets]] are the spherical plastic projectiles used in [[Airsoft guns]] 
 * The individual items of  " [[Lead shot|shot]] "  fired from a [[shotgun]], are referred to as pellets 
 * In [[centrifugation]], a pellet is the concentrated component of a [[mixture]] that had been [[Centrifugation|centrifuged]] 
 * [[compound feed|Animal feed pellets]] are feedstuffs produced from various raw materials and additives 
 * [[Plastic resin pellet]], or nurdle 
 * [[Pellet (ornithology)|Bird pellets]] are the round balls of undigested matter that some bird species occasionally regurgitate 
 * [[Wood pellets]] or [[Corn and pellet stoves and furnaces|corn pellets]] are used in [[pellet stoves]] 
 * [[Pelletizing]] is the industrial process used to create pellets - often using a [[pellet mill]]
