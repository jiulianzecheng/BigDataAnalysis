 '''Chlorpromazine,''' also known by its trade name '''Thorazine''', is an [[antipsychotic]] [[drug]]. It is typically used to treat [[Schizophrenia]]. It has also been used to treat [[bipolar disorder]]. It has been shown to help uncontrollable [[hiccups]] disappear. It is also used to help with [[nausea]] and [[vomit]]ing. < ref > {{Cite web|title=ChlorproMAZINE Monograph for Professionals|url=https://www.drugs.com/monograph/chlorpromazine.html|access-date=2022-09-22|website=Drugs.com|language=en}} < /ref >  Chlorpromazine was developed in 1950, making it the oldest antipsychotic drug. < ref > {{Cite journal|last1=López-Muñoz|first1=Francisco|last2=Alamo|first2=Cecilio|last3=cuenca|first3=Eduardo|last4=Shen|first4=Winston|last5=Clervoy|first5=Patrick|last6=Rubio|first6=Gabriel|date=2005-07-01|title=History of the Discovery and Clinical Introduction of Chlorpromazine|url=http://www.informaworld.com/openurl?genre=article & doi=10.1080/10401230591002002 & magic=crossref%7C%7CD404A21C5BB053405B1A640AFFD44AE3|journal=Annals of Clinical Psychiatry|volume=17|issue=3|pages=113–135|doi=10.1080/10401230591002002|pmid=16433053 |issn=1040-1237}} < /ref >
