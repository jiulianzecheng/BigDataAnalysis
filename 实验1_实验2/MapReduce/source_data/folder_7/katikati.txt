'''Katikati''' is a town in the [[Bay of Plenty]] in [[New Zealand]]. 5460 people live there. It is 28 kilometers south of [[Waihi]] and 40 kilometers northwest of [[Tauranga]]. State Highway 2 runs through the centre of town.
