 '''Arendal''' is a [[city]] and [[municipality]] in the [[county]] of [[Aust-Agder]], [[Norway]]. It is the administrative center of Aust-Agder county, and belongs to the geographical region of [[Sørlandet]]. The municipality is [[border]]ed in the southwest by [[Grimstad]], in the northwest by [[Froland]], and in the northeast by [[Tvedestrand]] municipalities. 
 
 == The name == 
 
 The [[Norse]] form of the name was probably  " ''Arnardalr'' " . The first element is then the genitive case of  " orn "  m 'eagle', the last element is  " dalr "  m 'valley, dale'.
