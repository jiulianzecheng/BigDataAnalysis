 '''{{Interlanguage link|Ikutsuhikone|ja|イクツヒコネ}}''' (lit. Lively little lad,  '''活津日子根命 ([[Kojiki]]''') '''活津彦根命 ([[Nihon Shoki]]))''' is a [[Kami|God]] of [[Japanese mythology]]. He is a son of [[Amaterasu]]. There are many stories about his birth from different books.
