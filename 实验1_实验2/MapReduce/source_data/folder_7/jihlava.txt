 '''Jihlava''' ({{lang-de|Iglau}}) is a city in the [[Czech Republic]]. It is the capital of the [[Vysočina Region]]. In 2020, 51,216 people lived there. 
 
 The city is on the [[Jihlava (river)|Jihlava]] river ({{lang-de|Igel}}) on the historical border between [[Moravia]] and [[Bohemia]]. It is the oldest mining town in the country.
