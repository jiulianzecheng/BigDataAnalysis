 '''Guar''' is a [[crop]] which is mostly grown in [[Pakistan]]. This crop is planted in July or August and [[harvest]]ed at the end of November. Guar is widely used in [[oil]] exploring fields and also in the [[cosmetics]] industry. There are four major countries in the world which produce guar: [[Sudan]], [[India]], [[Pakistan]], and the [[United States]]. 
 
 The price of this crop went up in 2012 when its price went to 37,000 Pakistan rupees per 100 kg.
