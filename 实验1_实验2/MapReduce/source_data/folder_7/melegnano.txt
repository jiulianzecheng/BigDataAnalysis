 '''Melegnano''' (formerly '''Marignano''') is a city and ''[[comune]]'' in the [[Metropolitan City of Milan]] in the [[Lombardy]] region in [[Italy]]. About 18,000 people live there. 
 
 == History == 
 In 1515, Melegnano is known particularly for the [[Battle of Marignano]], which is between [[France]] and [[Switzerland]]. In 1859, it is also known for the battles between the [[Second French Empire|French]] and [[Austrian Empire|Austrians]] in the [[Second Italian War of Independence]].
