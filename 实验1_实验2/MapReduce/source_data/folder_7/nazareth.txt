 '''Nazareth''' ({{IPAEng|ˈnæzərəθ}}) ({{lang-he-n|נָצְרַת}} ''{{Transl|he|Natz'rat}}'' or ''{{transl|he|Natzeret}}'';  {{lang-ar|الناصرة}} ''{{transl|ar|an-Nāṣira}}'' or ''{{transl|ar|an-Naseriyye}}'') is the [[capital (city)|capital]] city of the north district of [[Israel]]. Most of the people in this city are [[Arab]]s (who are either [[Muslim]] or [[Christianity|Christian]]). There are also some [[Jew]]ish people. The city is very old and is also famous for being the city where it is believed [[Jesus]] grew up in the care of [[Mary (mother of Jesus)|Mary]] and [[Saint Joseph|Joseph]].
