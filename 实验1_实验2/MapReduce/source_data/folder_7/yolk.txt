 The '''yolk''' is the [[yellow]] center of an [[egg (food)|egg]]. It is only one [[cell]]. It is commonly seen in [[fried egg]]s. When it is mixed with the whites, it makes [[scrambled eggs]]. It is sometimes used in [[cooking]]. It is much higher in [[calorie]]s than the [[egg white]].
