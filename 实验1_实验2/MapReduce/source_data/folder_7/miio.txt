 '''Miio''' is a [[teen pop]] group from [[Sweden]]. The group was established by the [[Tetrapop]] producer in 2003. The group scored chart successes in Sweden between 2003-2005. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://swedishcharts.com/showinterpret.asp?interpret=Miio|title=Miio|publisher=Swedishcharts|accessdate=23 October 2014}} < /ref > 
 
 The group originally consited of the girls [[Josefine Wahlsten]] and [[Linnéa Roxeheim]] < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.dt.se/allmant/dalarna/duon-miio-klara-for-daldansen|title=Duon Miio klara för Daldansen|publisher=Dalarnas tidningar|author=Bernt Nilsson|language=Swedish|date=7 April 2004|accessdate=12 August 2014|archive-date=12 August 2014|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140812204803/http://www.dt.se/allmant/dalarna/duon-miio-klara-for-daldansen|url-status=dead}} < /ref >  In 2004 Linnéa left the group, and was replaced by 19 years old Mia. Mia appread on the group's second album.
