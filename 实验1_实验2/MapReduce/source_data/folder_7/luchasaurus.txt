 '''Austin Matelson''' (born March 10, 1985) is an American [[Professional wrestling|professional wrestler]]. He currently wrestles for [[All Elite Wrestling]] (AEW) and competes under the ring name '''Luchasaurus'''. 
 
 He is also known for his time wrestling in [[WWE]] where he competed in ''[[WWE NXT|NXT]]'' and wrestled under the ring name '''Judas Devlin''' and in [[Lucha Underground]] where he wrestled under the ring name '''Vibora'''. 
 
 During his time in AEW, Luchasaurus has been a one-time [[AEW TNT Championship|AEW TNT Champion]] and a one-time [[AEW World Tag Team Championship|AEW World Tag Team Champion]]. 
 
 Matelson was a contestant on season 17 of the American [[reality television]] show ''[[Big Brother (American TV series)|Big Brother]]''. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.hollywoodreporter.com/tv/tv-news/big-brother-17-austin-matelson-823741/|accessdate=October 10, 2023|title=‘Big Brother 17’: Seventh Juror Talks Shock Exit, Love for Liz and Leaving His Girlfriend|publisher=[[The Hollywood Reporter]]}} < /ref >
