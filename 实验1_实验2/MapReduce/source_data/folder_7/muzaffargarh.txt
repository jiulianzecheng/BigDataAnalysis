'''Muzaffargarh''' ({{lang-ur|مظفر گڑھ}}) is a town in southwestern [[Punjab (Pakistan)|Punjab, Pakistan]]. It is on the bank of the [[Chenab River]]. It is the capital city of [[Muzaffargarh District]].
