'''Saracens''' ({{Respell|SARR|ə|sən}}) were [[Arab Muslims]] during the [[Middle Ages]].  
 
 In the early centuries of the [[Common Era]], [[Ancient Greek|Greek]] and [[Latin]] writings used the term to talk about the people who lived in desert areas in and near the Roman province of [[Arabia Petraea]], and in [[Arabia Deserta]].{{sfn|Daniel|1979|p=53}}{{sfn|Retsö|2003|p=505}}{{sfn|Retsö|2003|p=506}}
