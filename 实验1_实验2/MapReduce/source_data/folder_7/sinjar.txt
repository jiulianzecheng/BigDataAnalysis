 '''Sinjar''' is a city in the north of [[Iraq]].   It is in Shingal District, Nineveh Province, near Mount Shingal.  Many [[Yazidi|Yezidi]] people live in this city.  In 2014, it was the site of the [[Sinjar massacre]]. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.aljazeera.com/news/2016/07/iraq-yazidis-living-fear-mount-sinjar-160726063155982.html|title=Iraq's Yazidis living in fear on Mount Sinjar|website=www.aljazeera.com}} < /ref > < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.cnn.com/2016/01/12/middleeast/nima-elbagir-sinjar-mass-graves/index.html|title=Mass graves, missing children: Brutality in Sinjar|first=Nima Elbagir|last=CNN|website=CNN}} < /ref >
