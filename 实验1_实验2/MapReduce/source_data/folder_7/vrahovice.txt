 '''Vrahovice''' ({{lang-de|Wrahowitz}}) is a [[village]] in the [[Olomouc Region]] of the [[Czech Republic]]. It is part of the municipality of [[Prostějov]]. In 2001, 3,402 people lived there. 
 
 The village was first mentioned in 1337.
