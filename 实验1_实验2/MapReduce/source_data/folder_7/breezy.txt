 '''''Breezy''''' is a 1973 American romantic drama movie directed by [[Clint Eastwood]] and is the first movie he didn't star in and starring [[William Holden]], [[Kay Lenz]], [[Roger C. Carmel]], [[Marj Dusay]], [[Joan Hotchkis]], [[Lynn Borden]], [[Shelley Morrison]]. It was distributed by [[Universal Pictures]].
