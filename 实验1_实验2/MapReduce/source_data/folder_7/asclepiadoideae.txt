 The '''Asclepiadaceae''', commonly known as the '''milkweed family''', is a former [[plant]] family now treated as a subfamily (subfamily '''Asclepiadoideae''') in the [[Apocynaceae]]. 
 They form a group of perennial [[herb]]s, twining [[shrub]]s, [[liana]]s or rarely trees but notably also contain a significant number of leafless stem succulents, all belonging to the order [[Gentianales]]. The name comes from the type genus ''Asclepias'' ([[milkweed]]s).
