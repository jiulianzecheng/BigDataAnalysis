 '''Dangriga''', formerly known as '''Stann Creek Town''', is a [[town]] in southern [[Belize]].  It is on the [[Caribbean Sea|Caribbean]] coast at the mouth of Stann Creek. It is the capital of Belize's [[Stann Creek District]] and also the largest town in southern Belize. It is served by the [[Dangriga Airport]].

 ==Attractions== 
 *[[Cockscomb Basin Wildlife Sanctuary]] is to the southwest of Dangriga. 
 *Mayflower Archeological Reserve has three ruins, two waterfalls and a view of Hopkins village as well as of the [[Caribbean Sea]]. 
 
 ==Healthcare== 
 The city is served by [[Southern Regional Hospital]].
