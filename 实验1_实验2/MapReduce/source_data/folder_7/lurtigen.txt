 '''Lurtigen''' ({{lang-fr|Lourtens}}) was a [[municipality]] of the district of [[See (district)|See]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Fribourg (canton)|Fribourg]] in [[Switzerland]]. On 1 January 2016, the former municipalities of [[Courlevon]], [[Jeuss]], Lurtigen and [[Salvenach]] merged into [[Murten]]. < ref > [http://www.bfs.admin.ch/bfs/portal/de/index/infothek/nomenklaturen/blank/blank/gem_liste/02.html Nomenklaturen – Amtliches Gemeindeverzeichnis der Schweiz] {{in lang|de}} accessed 3 February 2016 < /ref >
