'''Fomalhaut''' is the brightest star in the southern [[constellation]] of [[Piscis Austrinus]], the  " Southern Fish " . It is and one of the [[List of brightest stars|brightest stars in the night sky]]. The star has the [[Bayer designation]] '''Alpha Piscis Austrini''.''''' It is a [[Stellar classification|class A]] [[star]] on the main sequence. Fomalhaut is located about 25 [[Light year|light years]] from the [[Sun]] as measured by the [[Hipparcos]] astrometry [[Satellite (artificial)|satellite]]. < ref > {{Cite web|title=CERN Document Server|url=http://cds.cern.ch/|access-date=2022-11-16|website=cds.cern.ch}} < /ref >  Since 1943, the [[spectrum]] of this star has served as one of the stable anchor points by which other stars are classified. < ref > {{Cite web|title=U of T Department of Astronomy  &  Astrophysics {{!}}|url=http://www.astro.utoronto.ca/|access-date=2022-11-16|language=en-US}} < /ref >
