 '''SUPINFO International University''', formerly called  " École Supérieure d'Informatique " , is a renowned graduate engineering school in [[France]]. It is in a campus of [[IONIS Education Group]]. < ref > [https://www.lemondeinformatique.fr/actualites/lire-ionis-group-retenu-pour-racheter-supinfo-79984.html Ionis Group retenu pour racheter Supinfo] < /ref >  It has been created in 1965. The school is recognized by the French State since 1972. < ref > [https://www.legifrance.gouv.fr/affichTexte.do?cidTexte=JORFTEXT000000513361 & categorieLien=id Décret du 10 janvier 1972 PORTANT RECONNAISSANCE PAR L'ETAT DE L'ECOLE SUPERIEURE D'INFORMATIQUE SISE 44, RUE DE RENNES, PARIS (6E)] < /ref > 
 
 It offers a 5-year course in [[Information technology|IT]].
