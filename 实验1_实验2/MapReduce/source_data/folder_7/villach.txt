 '''Villach''' ({{lang-sl|Beljak}}, {{lang-it|Villaco}}) is the seventh-largest [[city]] in [[Austria]] (after [[Klagenfurt]]). It is also a district of [[Carinthia (state)|Carinthia]]. About 70,000 people lived there in 2016.
