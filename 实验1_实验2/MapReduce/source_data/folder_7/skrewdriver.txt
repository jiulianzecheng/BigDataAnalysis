 '''Skrewdriver''' was a [[punk rock]] [[band]] formed in [[Blackpool]], [[England]] in 1976 by [[Ian Stuart Donaldson]]. They later became a [[skinhead]]/[[Oi!]] band, and became [[wikt:controversy|controversial]] by having [[racist]] views. They became one of the first [[white power]] punk bands and played a leading role in the [[Rock Against Communism]] [[movement]].
