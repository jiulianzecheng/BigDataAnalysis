'''Sauges''' was a [[municipality]] and [[village]] of the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Neuchâtel (canton)|Neuchâtel]], [[Switzerland]]. In 1888, the former municipalities [[Saint-Aubin, Neuchâtel|Saint-Aubin]] and Sauges joined together and became [[Saint-Aubin-Sauges]].
