 '''Vaasa''' ({{lang-sv|Vasa}}) is a [[city]] in [[Ostrobothnia (region)|Ostrobothnia]], [[Finland]]. The city has a population of 60 622 (31.8.2012) and the area 191,68 km². Vaasa is different compared to the nearest municipalities because so many people there speak [[Finnish language|Finnish]]. From [[Helsinki]], it is 419 km to Vaasa. 
 
 Neighbouring municipalities are [[Isokyrö]], [[Laihia]], [[Malax]], [[Korsholm]] and [[Vöyri]].
