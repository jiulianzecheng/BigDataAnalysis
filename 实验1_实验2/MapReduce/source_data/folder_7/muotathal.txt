 '''Muotathal''' is a village and a [[municipality]] in [[Schwyz (district)|Schwyz District]] in the [[canton of Schwyz]] in [[Switzerland]]. The eponymous valley, the '''Muotatal''', is formed by the [[Muota (river)|Muota]], which joins [[Lake Lucerne]] at [[Brunnen]]. 
 
 == Geography == 
 [[File:Muotathal01.jpg|thumb|left|Muotathal, the river Muota in the Muotatal looking west]] 
 [[File:Glatten.jpg|thumb|left|Lake north of the [[Glatten (mountain)|Glatten]] mountain]] 
 The village is in the valley of the river [[Muota (river)|Muota]], the Muotatal, with which it shares the name. The municipality is in a series of valleys on the eastern edge of the canton, on the borders with the cantons of [[Uri]] and [[Glarus (canton)|Glarus]]. It is the 10th largest municipality by area in Switzerland. 
 
 [[File:Speleo 2407.jpg|thumb|250px|Hölloch]] 
 The municipality includes the entry site of the [[Hölloch]], which at over 200 & nbsp;km is the longest cave in Switzerland, and the second-longest in Europe.
