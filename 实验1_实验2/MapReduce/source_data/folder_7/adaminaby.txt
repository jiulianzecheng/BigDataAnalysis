 '''Adaminaby''' ({{coord|35|59|S|148|46|E|type:city(200)_region:AU-NSW}}) is a [[town]] in southeast [[New South Wales]], [[Australia]] on the [[Snowy Mountains Highway]]. The first town of Adaminaby was in the way of a new dam built on the [[Eucumbene River]] as part of the [[Snowy Mountains Scheme]]. In 1957 more than 100 buildings were relocated to a new site 10 kilometres away where they would not be [[flood]]ed. < ref > {{cite web
