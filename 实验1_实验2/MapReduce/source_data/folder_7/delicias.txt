 '''Delicias''' (Spanish for ''Delights'') is a city in [[Chihuahua (state)|Chihuahua]], [[Mexico]]. It is the seat of the [[Delicias Municipality|municipality of the same name]]. It is located southeast of the [[state capital]], [[Chihuahua, Chihuahua|Chihuahua]].
