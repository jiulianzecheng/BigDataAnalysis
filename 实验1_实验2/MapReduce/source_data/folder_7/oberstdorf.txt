 '''Oberstdorf''' is a [[municipality]] in southwest [[Germany]]. It is in the [[Allgäu Alps]]. Many people visit to go [[walk]]ing or [[skiing]]. It is one of the highest towns in [[Germany]] and is the town furthest south in [[Germany]]. < ref > {{Cite web|url=https://www.oberstdorf.de/en/|title=Official Oberstdorf Website|first=|last=|date=|website=|publisher=|access-date=}} < /ref >  It is next to [[Austria]]. 
 
 There are several mountains with good views, like the [[Nebelhorn]] and [[Fellhorn]]. There are many cable-cars to reach the top of mountains around Oberstdorf. In the centre of Oberstdorf there is a large church, with a tall spire. < ref > {{Cite web|url=https://www.google.co.uk/destination/map?q=oberstdorf+germany+points+of+interest & sa=X & espv=2 & biw=1280 & bih=614 & site=search & output=search & dest_mid=/m/0660cv & ved=0ahUKEwispbG2p4rRAhXYOVAKHavzBFUQ6tEBCB8oBTAA|title=Google Points of Interest|first=|last=|date=|website=|publisher=|access-date=}} < /ref > 
 
 It is also home to several ski jumps, which are used for many competitions. They were also used in the 2016 movie ''Eddie the Eagle''. < ref > {{Cite web|url=https://www.imdb.com/title/tt1083452/locations|title=IMDB - Film Locations|first=|last=|date=|website=|publisher=|access-date=}} < /ref >
