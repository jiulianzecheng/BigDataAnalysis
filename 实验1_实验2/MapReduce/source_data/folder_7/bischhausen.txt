 '''Bischhausen''' is a village in southern [[Lower Saxony]], [[Germany]]. In December 2010, 371 people lived in the village (as of May 4, 2015). < ref > http://www.gleichen.de/staticsite/staticsite.php?menuid=62 < /ref >  It is near the previous border with what was the German Democratic Republic (East Germany). The castle ruins called the Gleichen are on two small mountains near the village.
