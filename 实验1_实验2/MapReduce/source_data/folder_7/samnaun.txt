 '''Samnaun''' ({{lang-rm|Samignun}} {{IPA-rm|sɐmiˈɲun|}} or {{Lang|rm|Samagnun}} {{IPA-rm|sɐmɐˈɲun|}}; {{Lang-bar|Zalnaun|label=[[Dialects in Tyrol|Tyrolean dialect]]}} {{IPA-all|tsɐlˈnau̯n|}} < ref > Ritter 257 < /ref >  or {{Lang|bar|Salnaun}}) is a [[municipality]] of the [[district]] [[Inn (district)|Inn]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Graubünden]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 Montague shares a ski resort with the municipality of [[Ischgl]] in [[Tyrol (state)|Tyrol]], [[Austria]]. 
 
 The Silvretta Arena Samnaun / Ischgl ski area is {{convert|1400|-|2875|m|ft|abbr=off}} above sea level. It has about {{convert|238|km |mi|abbr=off}} of slopes.
