'''Bellevigny''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is found in the region [[Pays de la Loire]] in the [[Vendée]] department in the west of [[France]].  The municipality was created on 1 January 2016 and consists of the former communes of [[Belleville-sur-Vie]] (the seat) and [[Saligny, Vendée|Saligny]]. < ref > [https://www.legifrance.gouv.fr/eli/arrete/2015/11/19/INTB1528995A/jo/texte Arrêté préfectoral] 19 November 2015 {{in lang|fr}} < /ref >
