 '''''Aeoliscoides''''' is an extinct genus of prehistoric [[bony fish]] that lived from the early to middle [[Eocene]]. 
 
 == Description == 
 ''Aeoliscoides'' resembled the [[shrimpfish]], an unrelated group of [[fish]]. It is mostly [[brown]] in [[Fossil|fossils]], but there were a variety of [[Colour|colors]]. It lived in the [[ocean]]. Its only species is '''''Aeoliscoides longirostris'''''.
