 '''Mamoudzou''' is the [[capital (city)|capital]] of the [[France|French]] [[departments of France|department]] of [[Mayotte]]. It is off the coast of [[Africa]] in the [[Indian Ocean]]. Mamoudzou is largest commune ([[municipality]]) of Mayotte. It has a [[population]] of 45,485. 
 
 Mamoudzou is a part of France on [[Grande-Terre]] (or Mahoré), the main [[island]] of Mayotte. The former capital of Mayotte was [[Dzaoudzi]], on the small island of [[Petite-Terre]] (or Pamanzi). Mamoudzou was chosen as the capital in 1977.
