 '''Loverboy''' is a [[Canada|Canadian]] [[rock music|rock]] [[band]]. It was formed in 1979. The band member are: 
 * Mike Reno - Lead singer 
 * Paul Dean - Lead guitarist 
 * Doug Johnson - Keyboardist 
 * Ken Sinnaeve - Bassist 
 * Matt Frenette - Drummer
