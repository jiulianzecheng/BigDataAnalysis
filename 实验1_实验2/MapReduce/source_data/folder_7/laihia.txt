 '''Laihia''' is a [[municipality]] in [[Finland]]. About 8,000 people lived there as of 31 August 2013. It covers 508.38 & nbsp;km². Near Laihia is municipalities: Ilmajoki, Isokyrö, Kurikka, Maalahti, Mustasaari and Vähäkyrö.
