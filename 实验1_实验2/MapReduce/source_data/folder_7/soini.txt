 '''Soini''' is a [[municipality]] in [[Southern Ostrobothnia]], [[Finland]]. It had about 2,300 living there in January 2014. The municipalities next to it are [[Alajärvi]], [[Karstula]], [[Kyyjärvi]], [[Saarijärvi]] and [[Ähtäri]]. The municipality was established in 1868.
