 The '''Melvins''' are an [[United States|American]] [[rock music|rock]] [[band]]. They are from the city of [[Aberdeen, Washington|Aberdeen]], [[Washington]]. The only members who have been in the band for a long time are [[Buzz Osborne|Buzz  " King Buzzo "  Osborne]] who plays [[guitar]] and [[Dale Crover]] who plays [[drum]]s. [[Coady Willis]] who plays drums (in addition to Dale) and [[Jared Warren]] who plays [[bass guitar]] are also currently in the band. A variety of terms have been used to describe their style of music, including '[[sludge metal]]', 'grunge', and '[[stoner rock]]'. 
 
 Buzz Osborne started Melvins in early 1983.
