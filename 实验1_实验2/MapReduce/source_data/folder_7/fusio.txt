 '''Fusio''' is a village and former municipality in the [[district]] of [[Vallemaggia (district)|Vallemaggia]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Ticino]], [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 In 2004 the municipality was merged with the other, neighboring municipalities [[Broglio, Switzerland|Broglio]], [[Brontallo]], [[Menzonio]], [[Peccia]] and [[Prato-Sornico]] to form a new and larger municipality of [[Lavizzara]].
