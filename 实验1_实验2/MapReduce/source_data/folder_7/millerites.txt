'''Millerites''' was the name for the followers of the [[teaching]]s of [[William Miller (preacher)|William Miller]]. In 1833, Miller said that he had done some calculations, and that the second coming of Christ would happen about the year 1844. This turned out to be false, and there was a great disappointment. Miller had documented how he did [[Bible]] [[interpretation]], so anyone could do it. After the disappointment, some movements emerged that were generally called ''[[Adventism|Adventist]]''.
