 '''Rijsoord''' is a [[village]] in the province of [[South Holland]], the Netherlands. 
 
 It belongs to the [[municipality]] of [[Ridderkerk]] and was a municipality until 1855. < ref > [https://www.plaatsengids.nl/rijsoord Plaatsengids.nl] (in Dutch) < /ref > 
 
 Rijsoord has 3,005 people living there in 2022. < ref > [https://allecijfers.nl/wijk/rijsoord-ridderkerk/ AlleCijfers.nl] (in Dutch) < /ref > 
 
 During World War II, the Dutch army surrendered to [[Nazi Germany]] in Rijsoord on May 15, [[1940]]. < ref > [https://www.tracesofwar.com/sights/70194/Dutch-Capitulation-Memorial-Ridderkerk.htm TracesOfWar] < /ref >
