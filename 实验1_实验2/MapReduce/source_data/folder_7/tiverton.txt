 '''Tiverton''' is a town in [[Mid Devon]], [[Devon]], England. In 2001, it had a [[population]] of 18,621. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.ons.gov.uk/help/localstatistics|title=Local statistics - Office for National Statistics|website=www.ons.gov.uk}} < /ref >  It was once a centre of lace making, but this [[industry]] has now declined. 
 
 People have lived around the area since the [[Stone Age]]. There is a [[hillfort]] on a hill above the town. [[Ancient Rome|Roman]] fortifications have been found nearby.
