 '''Paquetville''' is a [[Canada|Canadian]] village in [[Gloucester County, New Brunswick|Gloucester County]], [[New Brunswick]]. < ref > [http://archives.gnb.ca/Exhibits/Communities/Details.aspx?culture=en-CA & community=2961/ New Brunswick Provincial Archives - Paquetville] < /ref >  It is located on the [[Acadian Peninsula]], at the intersection of [[New Brunswick Route 340|Route 340]], [[New Brunswick Route 135|Route 135]] and [[New Brunswick Route 350|Route 350]]. In 2006, Paquetville had a population of 642. 
 
 The village was founded by Monseigneur Paquet in 1873, who brought several [[parish]]ioners with him from [[Shippagan, New Brunswick|Shippagan]]. 
 
 The village is the home of [[Édith Butler]], who sings the song  " Paquetville " .
