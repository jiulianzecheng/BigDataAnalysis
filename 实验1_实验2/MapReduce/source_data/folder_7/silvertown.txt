'''Silvertown''' is a district of the [[London Borough of Newham]], in [[East London]], England. It forms part of the [[Port of London]]. It lies on the north bank of the [[River Thames]]. The area is now dominated by the [[Tate  &  Lyle]] sugar refinery and the John Knight ABP animal rendering plant.
