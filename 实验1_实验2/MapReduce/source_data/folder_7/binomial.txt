In [[algebra]], '''Binomials''' are [[polynomials]] that contains two terms that are [[Monomial|monomials]] being either added or subtracted together. 
 
 == Notable binomials == 
 
 * The '''[[difference of two squares]]''' is a binomial that contains a [[Square (algebra)|squared]] [[variable]] that is being subtracted by another squared variableË  < math > x^2-y^2 < /math > . This binomial is also the product of two [[Linear function|linear]] binomials  < math > a+b < /math >  and  < math > a-b < /math > .
