 '''Piacenza''' is a [[city]] in the [[region]] of [[Emilia-Romagna]] in [[Italy]]. It is the [[capital city]] of the [[province of Piacenza]]. 102'394 people live there. Piacenza is famous for its ''[[palace|palazzi]]''.
