 '''''Ninotchka''''' is a 1939 American romantic comedy movie directed by [[Ernst Lubitsch]] and starring [[Greta Garbo]], [[Melvyn Douglas]], [[Ina Claire]], [[Felix Bressart]], [[Bela Lugosi]], [[Gregory Gaye]]. It was distributed by [[Metro-Goldwyn-Mayer]] and was nominated for 4 Academy Awards in 1940. It was remade as ''[[Silk Stockings]]'' in 1957.
