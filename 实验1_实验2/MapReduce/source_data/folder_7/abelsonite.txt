'''Abelsonite''' ([[nickel]] porphyrin) is a [[mineral]] that can be [[pink]]ish-[[brown]], [[purple]], or [[red]]dish-brown in color and is formed from [[porphyrin]] [[crystal]]s. It is [[platy]] (made in plates like [[mica]]) and [[wikt:fragile|fragile]].  
 
 [[Porphyrins]] are [[organic compound|organic ring compounds]] like [[haeme]], the pigment in [[red blood cells]]. 
 
 It is found in the [[Green River Formation]] of [[Utah]], in the [[United States]]. < ref > [http://www.webmineral.com/data/Abelsonite.shtml] Mineral Data Sheet  < /ref >
