 '''Ijtihad''' is the process of reaching a legal decision on the basis of one's own interpretation of [[Islam]]ic law. The word is related to the better-known ''[[jihad]]''.  
 
 Ijtihad appeared  among the [[Muhammad|Muhammad's]] [[Sahaba|companions]].
