 The '''Garonne''' is a [[river]] that runs through northern [[Spain]] and the southwest [[France]]. Its [[Mouth (river)|mouth]] is at the [[Atlantic Ocean]]. It is {{convert|575|km|mi|0|lk=on|abbr=on}} long.
