'''Tarin''' (also written as '''Tareen'''), are a large [[Pashtun]] tribe.  They mostly live in parts of [[Pakistan]] and [[Afghanistan]]. < ref > Gankovsky, Y (1964) ''The Peoples of Pakistan:An Ethnic Study'' Lahore: Progressive Publishers Ltd, p.11 < /ref > 
 
 The [[Durrani Empire|Durrani tribe]], which ruled Afghanistan for quite some time, is a branch descended from the main Tarin tribe. < ref > RTI Ridgway, ''The Pathans'' 1911 < /ref >
