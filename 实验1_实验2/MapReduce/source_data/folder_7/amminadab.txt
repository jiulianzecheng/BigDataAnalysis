 '''Amminadab''' was a man mentioned in the [[Bible]] as an ancestor of [[Jesus]]. 
 
 He was a son of Ram and father of [[Nahshon]] and [[Elisheva]], who married priest [[Aaron]]. 
 
 Laurence Gardner proposed Amminadab to be [[pharaoh]] [[Akhenaten]].
