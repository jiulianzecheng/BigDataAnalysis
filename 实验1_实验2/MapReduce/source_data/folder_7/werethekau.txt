 '''Werethekau''' is an Egyptian Goddess, credited with supernatural powers. The name ''weret hekau'' means  " great of magic " . < ref name= " Lesko " > Barbara S. Lesko, The great goddesses of Egypt, University of Oklahoma Press, 1999, p 74 < /ref >  Werethekau was the god of protection magic, and was often seen with Isis and Ra in the carvings.
