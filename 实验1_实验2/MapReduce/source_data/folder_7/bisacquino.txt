 '''Bisacquino''' is a town and ''[[comune]]'' in the [[Province of Palermo]] in [[Sicily]], [[Italy]], with about 5,220 inhabitants. It has an area of 64 & nbsp;[[Square metre|km²]], with a [[population density]] of 82 inhabitants/km².
