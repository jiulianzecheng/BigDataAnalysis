 '''''Chandragrohon: The Lunar Eclipse'''''  is a Bangladeshi movie based on a short story written by Indian Bengali author [[Syed Mustafa Siraj]]. < ref name=SW27dec2008 > {{cite news |author=Ershad Kamol |date=27 December 2008 |title=''Chandragrohon'' aesthetically rich presentation of an unconventional plot |url=http://archive.thedailystar.net/magazine/2008/12/03/film.htm |newspaper=Star Weekend Magazine |location=Dhaka, Bangladesh |publisher=The Daily Star |access-date=26 January 2014 |archive-date=4 July 2017 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170704202606/http://archive.thedailystar.net/magazine/2008/12/03/film.htm |url-status=dead }} < /ref >  The movie was released 2008 and younger director [[Murad Parvez]] first time directed the film. < ref > {{cite news |author=Ershad Kamol |date=19 February 2010 |title=The story of an Unexpected Success |url=http://archive.thedailystar.net/magazine/2010/02/03/achievement.htm |work=Star Weekend Magazine |location=Dhaka, Bangladesh |publisher=The Daily Star |access-date=26 January 2014 |archive-date=4 February 2016 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160204191408/http://archive.thedailystar.net/magazine/2010/02/03/achievement.htm |url-status=dead }} < /ref >  The film features [[Riaz (actor)|Riaz]], Shohana Saba, and [[Champa (actress)|Champa]] in lead roles along with [[Shahiduzzaman Selim]], KS Firoz, Dilara Jaman, Kohinur, Gazi Rakayet, and Azom Faruk supporting role.  
 In 2008, the movie won three [[National Film Awards (Bangladesh)|National Film Awards]] and including other four awards. < ref > {{cite news |date=1 February 2010 |script-title=bn:২০০৮ সালের জাতীয় চলচ্চিত্র পুরস্কার ঘোষণা |trans-title=2008 National Film Awards Announced |url=http://www.dainikdestiny.com/index.php?view=details & type=main & cat_id=1 & menu_id=22 & pub_no=123 & news_type_id=1 & index=10 & archiev=yes & arch_date=12-02-2010 |work=Dainik Destiny |language=bn |access-date=7 November 2018 |archive-date=4 February 2016 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160204191408/http://www.dainikdestiny.com/index.php?view=details & type=main & cat_id=1 & menu_id=22 & pub_no=123 & news_type_id=1 & index=10 & archiev=yes & arch_date=12-02-2010 |url-status=dead }} < /ref > < ref > {{cite news |date=6 January 2010 |script-title=bn:সর্বাধিক মনোনয়ন পেল চন্দ্রগ্রহণ |trans-title=Chandragrohon was the most-nominated |url=http://www.kalerkantho.com/print_edition/index.php?view=details & type=gold & data=news & pub_no=38 & cat_id=1 & menu_id=54 & news_type_id=1 & index=1 & archiev=yes & arch_date=06-01-2010 |newspaper=Kaler Kantho |language=bn |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140201175429/http://www.kalerkantho.com/print_edition/index.php?view=details & type=gold & data=news & pub_no=38 & cat_id=1 & menu_id=54 & news_type_id=1 & index=1 & archiev=yes & arch_date=06-01-2010 |archive-date=2014-02-01 }} < /ref >

 === National Film Awards === 
 ''Chandragrohon'' won [[National Film Awards (Bangladesh)|National Film Award]] and also other four awards totally seven sections. 
 * '''Won''' Best Director - [[Murad Parvez]] 
 * '''Won''' Best Dialogue - [[Murad Parvez]] 
 * '''Won''' Best Screenwriter - [[Murad Parvez]] 
 * '''Won''' Best Producer - Azom Faruk 
 * '''Won''' Best Music Director - Imon Saha and [[Habib Wahid]] 
 * '''Won''' Best Actress in a Supporting Role - [[Champa (actress)|Champa]] and Dilara Jaman 
 * '''Won''' Best Actor in a Negative Role - Jahirauddn Peer < ref > {{cite news |date=12 February 2012 |script-title=bn:জাতীয় চলচ্চিত্র পুরস্কার ২০০৮ ঘোষণা |trans-title=National Film Awards 2008 Announced |url=http://archive.prothom-alo.com/detail/news/41770 |newspaper=Prothom Alo |language=bn |access-date=7 November 2018 |archive-date=4 February 2016 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160204191408/http://archive.prothom-alo.com/detail/news/41770 |url-status=dead }} < /ref >
