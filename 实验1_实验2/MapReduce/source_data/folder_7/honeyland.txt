 '''''Honeyland''''' ({{lang-mk|Медена земја|Medena zemja}}) is a 2019 Macedonian documentary movie directed by [[Tamara Kotevska]] and [[Ljubomir Stefanov]] about [[bee]]s. It was nominated for two Academy Awards in 2020.
