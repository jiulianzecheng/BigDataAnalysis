 '''Commelinoideae''' is a subfamily of [[monocotyledon]]ous [[flowering plant]]s in the dayflower family ([[Commelinaceae]]). The Commelinoideae is one of two subfamilies within the Commelinaceae and includes 39 genera (out of 41 in the family) and all but 12 of the family's several hundred known species.
