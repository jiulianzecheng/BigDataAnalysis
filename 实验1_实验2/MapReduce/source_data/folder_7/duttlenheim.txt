 '''Duttlenheim''' is a [[Communes in France|commune]]. It is in [[Grand Est]] in the [[Bas-Rhin]] [[Departments of France|department]] in northeast [[France]]. It is about {{convert|20|km|mi}} southwest of [[Strasbourg]], and about 20 & nbsp;km from the [[Germany|German]] border. 
 
 Former football manager of [[Arsenal F.C.]] [[Arsène Wenger]] grew up here.
