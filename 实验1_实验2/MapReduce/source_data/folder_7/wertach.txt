 '''Wertach''' is a small town in the [[Oberallgäu]] district, southern [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. It is in the German [[Alps]]. It is on the river [[Wertach River|Wertach]], southeast of [[Kempten im Allgäu|Kempten]]. The town was the childhood home of the writer [[W. G. Sebald]]. It was first mentioned in 955. It received town status in 1423.
