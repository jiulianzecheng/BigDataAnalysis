 '''Aeschi''' is a [[municipality]] of the district of  [[Wasseramt (district)|Wasseramt]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Solothurn (canton)|Solothurn]] in [[Switzerland]]. [[Burgäschisee]] is a lake in the forest on the border with [[Seeberg]]. 
 
 The municipality of [[Steinhof]] merged on 1 January 2012 into the municipality of Aeschi. < ref name=Fusion > [http://www.bfs.admin.ch/bfs/portal/de/index/infothek/nomenklaturen/blank/blank/gem_liste/03.html Amtliches Gemeindeverzeichnis der Schweiz] published by the Swiss Federal Statistical Office {{in lang|de}} accessed 17 February 2011 < /ref >
