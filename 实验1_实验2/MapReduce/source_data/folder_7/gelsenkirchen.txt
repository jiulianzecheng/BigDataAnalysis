 '''Gelsenkirchen''' ( < small > [[British English|UK]]: < /small >  [[Help:IPA/English|/ˈɡɛlzənkɪərxən/]],  < small > [[American English|US]]: < /small >  [[Help:IPA/English|/ˌɡɛlzənˈkɪərxən/]],  < small > German: < /small >  [[Help:IPA/Standard German|[ˌɡɛlzn̩ˈkɪʁçn̩]]]; {{lang-wep|Gelsenkiärken}}) is a city in [[Germany]] in the [[States of Germany|state]] of [[North Rhine-Westphalia]]. It is about {{convert|63|km|mi|1|lk=on|abbr=off}} north of [[Cologne]] in the so-called [[Ruhr Area]]. 
 
 About 269,000 people live there. In the past it was dominated by [[coal mining]] and [[steel industry]]. The city is famous for its [[football (soccer)]] club [[FC Schalke 04]].
