 A '''stocking''' is a piece of [[women]]'s clothing, worn like socks. They are made of stretchy materials. They are often worn instead of  [[tights]] or [[sock]]s. 
 
 Stockings that are [[similar]] to woman's stockings are also made as parts of [[sport]] [[uniform]]s.
