 '''Elpistostegalia''', also known as '''Panderichthyida''', is a type of [[prehistoric]] [[fish]] which lived about 385 to 374 million years ago. < ref > [http://taxonomicon.taxonomy.nl/TaxonName.aspx?id=987575 & tree=0.1 Elpistostegidae on the Taxonomicon] < /ref >   
 
 ==Why they evolved== 
 A rise in the world's [[oxygen]] content caused [[predator]]y fish to [[evolve]], < ref name=Dahl > {{cite journal |vauthors=Dahl TW, Hammarlund EU, Anbar AD |title=Devonian rise in atmospheric oxygen correlated to the radiations of terrestrial plants and large predatory fish |journal=Proc. Natl. Acad. Sci. U.S.A. |volume=107 |issue=42 |pages=17911–5 |date=October 2010 |pmid=20884852 |pmc=2964239 |doi=10.1073/pnas.1011287107 |bibcode = 2010PNAS..10717911D |display-authors=etal|doi-access=free }} < /ref >  and this fish was the most successful. < ref > {{cite journal|last=Lewis Jr. |first=William M. |title=Morphological Adaptations of Cyprinodontoids for Inhabiting Oxygen Deficient Waters |journal=Copeia |date=1 June 1970 |volume=2 |issue=2 |pages=319–326 |url=http://ciresweb.colorado.edu/limnology/pubs/pdfs/Pub001.pdf |jstor=1441653 |doi=10.2307/1441653 |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110813153431/http://ciresweb.colorado.edu/limnology/pubs/pdfs/Pub001.pdf |archive-date=2011-08-13 }} < /ref >
