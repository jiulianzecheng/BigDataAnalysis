 '''Monteceneri''' is a [[municipality]] of the [[district]] [[Lugano (district)|Lugano]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Ticino]] in [[Switzerland]]. It was created on 21 November 2010, when the former municipalities of [[Bironico]], [[Camignolo]], [[Medeglia]], [[Rivera, Switzerland|Rivera]] and [[Sigirino]] merged to form the new municipality of Monteceneri.
