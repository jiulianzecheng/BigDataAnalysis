 '''Gambarana''' is a ''[[comune]]'' (municipality) in the [[Province of Pavia]] in the [[Italy|Italian]] region of [[Lombardy]]. It is about 60 & nbsp;km southwest of [[Milan]] and about 35 & nbsp;km southwest of [[Pavia]]. As of 31 December 2004, 267 people lived there. It has an area of 11.78 & nbsp;kmÂ². < ref name= " istat " > All demographics and other statistics: Italian statistical institute [[National Institute of Statistics (Italy)|Istat]]. < /ref > 
 
 Gambarana is next to these municipalities: [[Bassignana]], [[Frascarolo]], [[Isola Sant'Antonio]], [[Mede, Lombardy|Mede]], [[Pieve del Cairo]], [[Suardi]].
