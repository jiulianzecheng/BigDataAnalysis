'''Hexadecene''', also known as '''cetene''', is an [[alkene]] containing 16 [[carbon]] [[Atom|atoms]], with a molecular formula of C < sub > 16 < /sub > H < sub > 32 < /sub > . There are eight different structural [[Isomer|isomers]] of hexadecene, depending on the location of the double carbon bond.
