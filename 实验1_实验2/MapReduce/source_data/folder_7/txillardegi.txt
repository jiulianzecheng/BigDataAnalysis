 '''José Luis Álvarez Enparantza''' (27 September 1929 – 14 January 2012) was a [[Basque people|Basque]] [[linguist]], [[politician]] and [[writer]]. When writing he used the [[pseudonym]] '''Txillardegi'''. He was born in [[Donostia]], which is called ''San Sebastian'' in [[Spanish language|Spanish]]. Even though he did not learn the [[Basque language]] until he was 17, he came to be seen as one of the most influential figures in Basque nationalism and culture in the second half of the 20th century. < ref > {{cite web |author=Noticias EFE |url=http://www.diariovasco.com/agencias/20120114/mas-actualidad/cultura/muere-txillardegi-escritor-fundo-nombre_201201141442.html |title=Muere  " Txillardegi " , el escritor que fundó ETA y dio nombre a la organización. |publisher=Diariovasco.com |accessdate=2012-01-14 |archive-date=2012-03-06 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120306070838/http://www.diariovasco.com/agencias/20120114/mas-actualidad/cultura/muere-txillardegi-escritor-fundo-nombre_201201141442.html |url-status=dead }} < /ref > 
 
 Together with some friends he started [[Euskadi Ta Askatasuna|ETA]] in 1959, and became responsible for their social and cultural activities. He also was a founding member of [[Herri Batasuna]], the Basque nationalist party. As a linguist, he helped shape the structure and orthography of [[Standard Basque]].
