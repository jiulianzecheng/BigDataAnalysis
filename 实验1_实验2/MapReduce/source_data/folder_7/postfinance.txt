 '''Postfinance''' is a large [[bank]] in [[Switzerland]]. It is a part of [[Swiss Post]] (the Swiss Postal service). It is mainly active to do national and international payments. It also offers other services, such as [[Savings account|saving accounts]], or accounts for [[Pension plan|pension plans]], or [[Mortgage|morgages]], but to a lesser extent.  It is among the biggest financial institutions in Switzerland. Because it is part of Swiss Post, everyone who legally lives in Switzerland must be able to get a bank account at Postfinance.
