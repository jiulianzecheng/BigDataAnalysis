 '''Tabi''' are traditional [[Japan]]ese [[sock]]s. The sock is divided at the [[toes]] that forms them into two groups. Tabi socks are worn with ''[[zori]]'' or ''[[Geta (footwear)|geta]]'' sandals. The Tabi is of many different colours mainly white.
