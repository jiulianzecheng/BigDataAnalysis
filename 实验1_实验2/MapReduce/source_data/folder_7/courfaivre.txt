 '''Courfaivre''' was a [[municipality]] of the [[district]] of [[Delémont (district)|Delémont]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Jura (canton)|Jura]] in [[Switzerland]]. On 1 January 2013, the former municipalities of Courfaivre, [[Bassecourt]], [[Glovelier]], [[Soulce]] and [[Undervelier]] merged into the new municipality of [[Haute-Sorne]].
