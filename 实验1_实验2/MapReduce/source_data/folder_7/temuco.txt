 '''Temuco''' is a [[Chile]]an city with 245,347 inhabitants (2002 census). 
 
 == History == 
 Temuco was founded by [[Manuel Recabarren]] naming the place ''Fuerte Recabarren''(''Fort Recabarren'') during the Occupation of  Araucania on 1881.
