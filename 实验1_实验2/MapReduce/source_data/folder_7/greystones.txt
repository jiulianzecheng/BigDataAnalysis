 '''Greystones''' ({{lang-ga|Na Clocha Liatha}}) is a [[coast]]al [[town]] in [[County Wicklow]], [[Republic of Ireland|Ireland]]. It is on Ireland’s east coast, {{convert|8|km|mi|0|lk=on|abbr=on}} south of [[Bray]] and {{convert|27|km|mi|0|abbr=on}} south of [[Dublin]] (it is within the [[Greater Dublin Area]]), with 15,000 people living in the region.
