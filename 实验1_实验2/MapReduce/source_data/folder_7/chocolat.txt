 '''''Chocolat''''' is a [[United Kingdom|British]]-[[United States|American]] [[Romance (love)|romance]] and [[drama movie]]. It was released December 15, 2000. It is about events in a fictional dull [[France|French]] [[village]] during the autumn-winter of 1959. A woman opens a [[chocolate]] shop. One customer, Armande, is [[diabetes mellitus|diabetic]]. She, however, continues eating chocolate. Armande later dies from her illness. [[Juliette Binoche]] plays Vianne, [[Carrie-Anne Moss]] plays Caroline Clairmont and [[Judi Dench]] plays Armande (the diabetic woman). 
 
 The [[review]]s from [[critic]]s were very positive. It was nominated for several [[Academy Award]]s. It did not win any.
