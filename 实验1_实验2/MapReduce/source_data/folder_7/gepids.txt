 The '''Gepids''' were an [[East Germanic tribe|East Germanic]] [[Goths|Gothic tribe]] who became [[wikt:famous|famous]] after [[wikt:defeat|defeating]] the [[Huns]].  The place where the Gepids lived was called '''Gepidia''' < ref > [[Jordanes]], ''[[Getica]]'', [https://www.ucalgary.ca/~vandersp/Courses/texts/jordgeti.html#XII XII.74]: ''Haec Gotia, quam Daciam appellavere maiores, quae nunc ut diximus Gepidia dicitur'' ( " This [[Gothia]], which our ancestors called [[Dacia]], we now call [[Gepidia]]. " ). < /ref >  or '''Kingdom of the Gepids'''.  The place where the Gepids used to live contained some of [[Romania]], [[Hungary]] and [[Serbia]].
