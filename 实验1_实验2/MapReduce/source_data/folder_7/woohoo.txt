''' " Woohoo " ''' is a [[song]] sung by [[Christina Aguilera]] and [[Nicki Minaj]], from Aguilera's 2010 album ''[[Bionic]]''. The song was written by Aguilera, Minaj, Claude Kelly, Jamal  " Polow da Don "  Jones and Ester Dean. It is a uptempo electrohop song with [[R & B]] and [[hip hop]] influences; lyrically it speaks of [[cunnilingus]]. The song was released on May 18, 2010 as the second single from Aguilera's ''Bionic'' album.
