 '''''Onigiri''''' or [[Rice|rice ball]] is a [[Japan|Japanese]] traditional [[Japanese food|food]]. It is made from [[white rice]] wrapped in [[nori]].  < ref > {{Cite web|title=What exactly is onigiri rice balls?|url=https://www.lunchporter.com/us/blogs/pro-tips/what-is-onigiri/?source=facebook|access-date=2021-07-19|website=Lunch Porter|language=en|archive-date=2021-07-19|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210719115056/https://www.lunchporter.com/us/blogs/pro-tips/what-is-onigiri/?source=facebook|url-status=dead}} < /ref >  Usually onigiri is filled with [[salmon]], [[katsuobushi]] or something else. The ingredient is [[Salt|salty]] or sour. Japanese stores have onigiri with various flavours.
