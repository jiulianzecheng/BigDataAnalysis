 '''Bigender''' is a [[gender identity]] which can be literally translated as 'two genders' or '[[double]] [[gender]]'. < ref > {{Cite web|date=2021-05-19|title=Cisgender, genderqueer, agender, bigender: What are these terms, why do they matter|url=https://indianexpress.com/article/explained/cisgender-agender-bigender-genderqueer-what-are-these-terms-why-do-they-matter-6581592/|access-date=2021-07-27|website=The Indian Express|language=en}} < /ref >  Bigender people experience exactly two gender identities, either simultaneously or [[Genderfluid|varying]] between the two. These two gender identities could be [[male]] and [[female]], but could also include [[non-binary]] identities.
