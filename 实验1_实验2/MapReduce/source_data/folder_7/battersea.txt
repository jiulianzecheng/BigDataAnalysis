 '''Battersea''' is a large district in [[southwest]] [[London]], part of the [[London Borough of Wandsworth]], [[England]]. It is centred 3.5 miles (5.6 km) southwest of [[Charing Cross]] and extends along the south bank of the [[Tideway|Thames Tideway]]. It includes the 200-acre (0.81 km < sup > 2 < /sup > ) [[Battersea Park]].
