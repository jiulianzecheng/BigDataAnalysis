'''Postcolonialism''' is a [[Critical theory]] branch that studies the how people respond to [[Colonialism]] and [[Imperialism]].  It became popular after it was used by [[Frantz Fanon]] and [[Edward Said]]. The theory says that colonizers are not reliable narrators. They cannot be trusted to tell correct history.
