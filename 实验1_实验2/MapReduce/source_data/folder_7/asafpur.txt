 '''Asafpur''' is a [[Block (district subdivision)|block]] in [[Budaun district]], [[Uttar Pradesh]], [[India]]. There are 62 villages under Asafpur block. < ref > {{cite web |title=ASAFPUR Block, Budaun Uttar Pradesh India News समाचार |url=http://www.brandbharat.com/english/up/districts/Budaun/Budaun_ASAFPUR.html |website=www.brandbharat.com}} < /ref >  According to [[2011 Census of India]], total number of houses in Asafpur is 28,395 and total population is 1,73,601 out of 92,682 are males and are 80,919 females. < ref > {{cite web |title=District Census Handbook Budaun |url=http://censusindia.gov.in/2011census/dchb/DCHB_A/09/0918_PART_A_DCHB_BUDAUN.pdf |website=Censusindia.gov.in}} < /ref >
