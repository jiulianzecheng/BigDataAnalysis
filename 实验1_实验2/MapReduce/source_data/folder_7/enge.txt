 '''Enge''' is a [[quarter]] in [[district 2 (Zürich)|district 2]] in the [[city]] of [[Zürich]] in [[Switzerland]]. Until 1893 it was a [[municipality]] on its own. It was then [[municipal corporation|incorporated]] into [[Zürich]].
