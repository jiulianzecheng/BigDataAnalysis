 '''Garoua''' is a city in northern [[Cameroon]] and is the capital of the [[North Region (Cameroon)|North Region]]. It is on the [[Benue River]]. In 2020, 1,285,000 people live there. The main ethnic groups are the [[Fulani people|Fulbe/Fulani]].
