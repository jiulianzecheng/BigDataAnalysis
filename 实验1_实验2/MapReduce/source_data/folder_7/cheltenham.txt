 '''Cheltenham''' {{IPAc-en|ˈ|tʃ|ɛ|l|t|n|ə|m}}, also known as '''Cheltenham Spa''', is the second largest [[town]] in [[Gloucestershire]] with a population of about 117,500 in mid 2016. 
 
 It is famous for having a large amount of millionaires with a rate of 43 multimillionaires per 100,000 people, the third largest in the [[United Kingdom]].
