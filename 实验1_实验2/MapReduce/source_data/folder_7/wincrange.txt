 '''Wincrange''' ({{lang-lb|WÃ«ntger}}, {{lang-de|Wintger}}) is a [[Communes of Luxembourg|commune]] in [[Luxembourg]]. It is part of the [[Clervaux (canton)|canton of Clervaux]]. It is the largest commune in Luxembourg by area. 
 
 As of 2019, 4,392 people live here. 
 
 Wincrange was created on 1 January 1978 from the former communes of Asselborn, Boevange, Hachiville and Oberwampach.
