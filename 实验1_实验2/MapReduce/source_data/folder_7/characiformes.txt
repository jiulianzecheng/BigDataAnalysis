'''Characiformes''' is an order of [[ray-finned fish]]. It includes families like [[Characin|Characidae]], [[Acestrorhynchidae]], and [[Serrasalmidae]]. Some characin [[Fish|fishes]] like the [[aimara]] are large. [[Trahira|Trahiras]] are also large.
