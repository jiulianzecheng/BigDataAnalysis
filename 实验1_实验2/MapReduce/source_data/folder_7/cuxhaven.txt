 '''Cuxhaven''' ({{IPAc-en|ˈ|k|ʊ|k|s|h|ɑː|v|ən}} {{respell|KUUKS|hah|vən}}, {{IPA-de|kʊksˈhaːfn̩|lang}}; {{Lang-nds|Cuxhoben}}) is an independent town and seat of [[Cuxhaven (district)|Cuxhaven]], in [[Lower Saxony]], [[Germany]]. The town includes the northernmost point of Lower Saxony. It is on the shore of the [[North Sea]] at the mouth of the [[Elbe]] River. < ref > {{cite EB1911 |wstitle=Cuxhaven |volume=7 |page=677}} < /ref >  Cuxhaven has a footprint of {{convert|14|km|0|abbr=off}} (east–west) by {{convert|7|km|0|abbr=on}} (north–south). Its town quarters [[Duhnen]], [[Döse]] and [[Sahlenburg]] are popular vacation spots on the [[North Sea]] and home to about 52,000 residents. 
 
 == Geographical and cultural background == 
 The town is served by [[Cuxhaven station]]. 
 
 The island of [[Neuwerk]] is {{convert|8|km|0}} off the coast from Cuxhaven.
