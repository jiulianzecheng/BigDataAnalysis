 '''''Clematis''''' is a [[genus]] of [[plant]]s that is widely known for its beautiful [[flower]]s. 
 
 There are many varieties in this group as gardeners around the world have produced many varieties. They can be found not only in Western countries but also in [[Japan]]. 
 
 This plant grows up as a [[vine]]. It uses its leaflets and leaf stalks to twine around any support. In nature it often climbs up a nearby tree. The original species can mostly be found in [[temperate]] areas on [[Earth]]. Some species can also be found on high [[mountain]]s.
