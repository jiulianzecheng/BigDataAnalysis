'''Cytogenetics''' is the branch of [[genetics]] which deals with [[cytology]]: [[cell]] [[structure]] and [[Function (biology)|function]]. It includes the study of [[chromosome]]s, chromosome bands, [[karyotype]]s, and [[cell division]], especially [[meiosis]]. < ref > Hsu T.C. ''Human and mammalian cytogenetics: a historical perspective''. Springer-Verlag, N.Y. < /ref >  Cytogenetic studies have also been used to study [[evolution]] in natural populations. < ref > Dobzhansky T. 1981. ''Dobzhansky's genetics of natural populations''. eds Lewontin RC, Moore JA, Provine WB and Wallace B. Columbia University Press N.Y. < /ref >
