 '''Vilseck''' is a [[town]] in [[Amberg-Sulzbach]], a district ([[Districts of Germany|''Landkreis'']]) in the [[Upper Palatinate Government Region|Upper Palatinate]]. It is on the river [[Vils (Naab)|Vils]], which is a [[tributary]] of the [[Naab]] river.
