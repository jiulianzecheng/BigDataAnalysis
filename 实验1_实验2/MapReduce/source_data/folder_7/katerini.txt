 '''Katerini''' is a [[Greece|Greek]] city which is the capital of [[Pieria]] prefecture in the region of [[Macedonia (Greece)|Macedonia]]. It is found on the foothill of [[Mount Olympus]], the highest Greek mountain. Katerini is located in the periphery of [[Central Macedonia]] and it is near [[Thessaloniki]]. It has a population of 85,851 inhabitants according to 2011 [[census]].
