'''Rotuma''' is a [[volcano|volcanic]] island about {{convert|465|km|mi|0|lk=on|abbr=on}} south of [[Fiji]]. < ref name= " rotuma.net " > rotuma.net: [http://www.rotuma.net/ Rotuma Website] < /ref >  It covers an area of about {{convert|43|km2|mi2|0|lk=on|abbr=on}}. It has been a part of [[Fiji]] since 1881. While the Fijian Islands are [[Melanesia]]n, Rotuma is [[wikt:isolate|isolated]] far to the north. Its people and culture are more closely related to [[Polynesia]].
