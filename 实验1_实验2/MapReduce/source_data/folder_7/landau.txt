 '''Landau''' or '''Landau in der Pfalz''' ({{lang-pfl|Landaach}},  < small > regional dialect(s): < /small >  ''Landääch'' or ''Landääsch'', 'Landau in the Palatinate') is a town in southern [[Rhineland-Palatinate]], [[Germany]]. It has about 46,000 [[inhabitant]]s and a [[university]]. Its main economic industry is [[wine]].
