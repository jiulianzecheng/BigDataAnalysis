 '''Namor McKenzie''', also known simply as '''Namor''', or '''Namor the Sub-Mariner''' is a major character and recurring antagonist of the [[Marvel Comics]] universe. 
 
 Considered to be the first mutant and the ruler of Atlantis, he developed a rather pernicious nature. This resulted him coming into conflict with [[Black Panther (Marvel Comics)|Black Panther]] along with various other heroes, such as the [[Avengers]]; the [[X-Men]]; and the [[The Fantastic Four|Fantastic Four]].
