 '''Mitwitz''' is a [[Municipalities of Germany|municipality]] in [[Kronach (district)|Kronach]] in [[Bavaria]] in [[Germany]]. It is on the [[Steinach (Rodach)|Steinach]] river and the [[Föritz (Steinach)|Föritz]] (which flows into the Steinach), on the edge of the [[Frankenwald]] on the route between [[Kronach]] and [[Coburg]]. < ref name= " BayernAtlas " > [https://v.bayern.de/vBHNV Mitwitz]  [[BayernAtlas|Atlas of Bavaria]] < /ref >
