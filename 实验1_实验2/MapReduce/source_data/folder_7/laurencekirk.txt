 '''Laurencekirk''' is a small town in the ancient county of [[Kincardineshire]], modern county of [[Aberdeenshire]], [[Scotland]], just off the [[A90 road|A90]] [[Dundee]] to [[Aberdeen]] main road. It is the largest settlement in the Howe o' the Mearns area and houses the local secondary school; [[Mearns Academy]], which was awarded the [[Charter Mark]] in 2003.
