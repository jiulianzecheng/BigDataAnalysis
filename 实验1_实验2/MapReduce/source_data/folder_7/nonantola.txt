 '''Nonantola''' is an [[Italy|Italian]] city in [[Emilia-Romagna]]. About 15,000 people live there. 
 
 Nonantola Abbey a [[Benedictine]] [[monastery]], was founded in 752 by the [[Lombards|Lombard]] [[duke of Friuli]], [[St. Anselm]].
