'''Tripmasters''', originally Solar Tours, was founded in 1984 by Maria Checa in [[Washington (state)|Washington]], D.C. < ref name= " :0 " > {{Cite web|date=2020-01-16|title=An Honest Review of Tripmasters.com {{!}} A Couple For The Road|url=https://www.acouplefortheroad.com/ultimate-review-of-tripmasters-com/|access-date=2023-12-04|language=en-US}} < /ref > < ref name= " :2 " > {{Cite web|title=TripMasters {{!}} Business Details {{!}} Better Business Bureau® Profile|url=https://www.bbb.org/us/md/silver-spring/profile/travel-agency/tripmasters-0241-17245|access-date=2023-12-04|website=www.bbb.org|language=en}} < /ref >  It started with trips to Peru, later expanding to Latin America, Europe, Asia, and the Pacific. In 1997, Maria's son, Rafael Checa, joined and shifted the focus to online custom trip planning. The company rebranded to Tripmasters < ref > {{Cite web|title=Solartours is now Tripmasters|url=https://www.tripmasters.com/|access-date=2023-12-04|website=Solartours is now Tripmasters|language=en-US}} < /ref >  in 2012 with Rafael as CEO. < ref name= " flashintel.ai " > {{Cite web|title=Rafael Checa - Chief Executive Officer @ TripMasters - Emails and Phone Numbers|url=https://www.flashintel.ai/people/Rafael-Checa-3b893a7b191680fb4e88996e736ef385/|access-date=2023-12-04|website=FlashIntel|language=en}} < /ref > 
 
 Based in [[Silver Spring, Maryland|Silver Spring]], Maryland, < ref name= " :1 " > {{Cite web|title=TRIPMASTERS INC {{!}} Washington Companies Directory|url=https://www.washingtoncompanysearch.com/companies/tripmasters-inc/|access-date=2023-12-04|website=www.washingtoncompanysearch.com}} < /ref >  Tripmasters offers customizable travel itineraries, including hotels, transport, and activities. < ref > {{Cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=aqGWDwAAQBAJ & q=%22Tripmasters%22|title=The Rough Guide to Brazil (Travel Guide eBook)|last=Guides|first=Rough|date=2018-07-02|publisher=Apa Publications (UK) Limited|isbn=978-1-78919-488-3|language=en}} < /ref >  It covers destinations across Latin America, Europe, Asia, and the [[Pacific Ocean|Pacific]] and provides tools for users to build their own itineraries, including cruises and city tours.
