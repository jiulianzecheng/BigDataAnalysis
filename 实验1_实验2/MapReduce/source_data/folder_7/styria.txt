 '''Styria''' ({{lang-de|Steiermark}} {{IPA-de|ˈʃtaɪ̯ɐˌmaʁk||Steiermark.ogg}}; {{Lang-bar|Steiamoak|label=[[Southern Austro-Bavarian]]}}; [[Croatian language|Croatian]] and {{lang-sl|{{lang|hr|Štajerska}}}}; {{lang-hu|Stájerország}}) is a state in the southeast of [[Austria]]. The capital city is [[Graz]]. It borders [[Slovenia]]. In 2016 there were about 1,232,000 people living in Styria.
