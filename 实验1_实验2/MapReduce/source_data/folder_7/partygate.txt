'''Partygate''' is an ongoing [[political scandal]] about (social [[wikt:gathering|gatherings]] or) 
 [[party|parties]] that were held by the staff of [[United Kingdom government]] and [[Conservative Party (UK)|Conservative Party]] It was made public in 2021. The parties took place during the [[COVID-19 pandemic in the United Kingdom|COVID-19 pandemic]] in 2020 and 2021, when there were [[British government response to the COVID-19 pandemic|public health restrictions]]. These parties happened at [[10 Downing Street]] and other government buildings.
