 '''Commune''' is a word that can have different meanings: 
 
 * In many languages, especially those coming from [[Latin language|Latin]], it can stand for an administrative division, see [[municipality]].  
 * In [[Mass (liturgy)|Mass]] of many Christian groups, it stands for a number of texts that are common among different forms of mass, see [[Common (liturgy)]] 
 * A larger number of people who have similar ideas or values, sometimes form what is called an [[intentional community]].  
 * A [[medieval commune]]
