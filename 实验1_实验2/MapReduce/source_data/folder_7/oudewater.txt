 '''Oudewater''' is a [[List of municipalities of the Netherlands|municipality]] in the [[Netherlands|Dutch]] province of [[Utrecht (province)|Utrecht]], and a former municipality of [[South Holland]]. In 2019, 10,201 people lived there.
