 '''''Albisaurus''''' is an extinct [[archosaur]] known from fossils discovered in the [[Czech Republic]] and was related to ''[[Ponerosteus]]''. It was once wrongly known as ''[[Iguanodon]]'', a '[[wastebasket taxon]]'.
