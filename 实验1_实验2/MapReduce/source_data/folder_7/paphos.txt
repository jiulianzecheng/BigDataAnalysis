 '''Paphos''' is a [[city]] in west [[Cyprus]]. It is the [[capital city|capital]] of [[Paphos District]].  In 2018, 63,600 people lived in the [[urban area]]. 
 
 The town is a [[UNESCO]] [[World Heritage site]] for the ancient Greek and Roman city ruins. < ref > {{Cite web|url=https://whc.unesco.org/en/list/79|title=Paphos|access-date=2020-03-24|website=UNESCO}} < /ref >
