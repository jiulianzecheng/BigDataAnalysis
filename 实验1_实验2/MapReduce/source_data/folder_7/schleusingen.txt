 '''Schleusingen''' is a town in the [[Hildburghausen Rural District|district of Hildburghausen]], in [[Thuringia]], [[Germany]]. It is {{convert|10|km|abbr=off}} north of [[Hildburghausen]], and {{convert|12|km|abbr=off}} southeast of [[Suhl]]. 
 
 The [[population]] of Schleusingen is about 6,000. The mayor of the city is Klaus Brodführer.
