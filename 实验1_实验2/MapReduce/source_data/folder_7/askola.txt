 '''Askola''' is a municipality in southern [[Finland]]. Almost 5,000 people lived there 2013. There are famous [[giant's kettle]]s in Askola, called ''Askolan Hiidenkirnut''. Askola's neighbouring municipalities are [[Myrskyl채]], [[M채nts채l채]], [[Pornainen]], [[Porvoo]] and [[Pukkila]]. 
 
 [[Farming]] has been important in Askola for a long time.
