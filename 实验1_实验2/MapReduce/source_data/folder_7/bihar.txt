 '''Bihar''' ({{IPAc-en|b|ᵻ|ˈ|h|ɑːr}}; {{IPA-hi|bɪˈɦaːr|-|Bihar.ogg}}) is a state in the [[Republic of India]], covering an area of 38,202 [[square mile|mi²]] (99,200 [[km²]]).  The capital is [[Patna]].  Bihar is bordered by the Indian states of [[Uttar Pradesh]] to the west, [[Jharkhand]] to the south, and [[West Bengal]] to the east.  To the north of Bihar is the country of [[Nepal]]. In traditional Indian geography it falls under the [[East India]]n zone. It is one of the poorest regions in India.
