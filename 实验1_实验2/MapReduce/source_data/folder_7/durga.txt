 '''Durga''' is one of the major deities in [[Hinduism]] worshipped as the supreme goddess and is known by different terms such as [[Shakti]] or [[Adi Parashakti]]. In Bengal, she is said to be the mother of [[Ganesha]] and [[Kartikeya]] as well of [[Saraswati]] and [[Lakshmi]]. She is considered a divine mother, a guardian, and a warrior who protects divine law and righteousness, and these are her main role.
