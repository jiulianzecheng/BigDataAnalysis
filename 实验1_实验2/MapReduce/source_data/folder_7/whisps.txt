 '''Whisps''' is an American cheese and snack brand.   
 
 Ilana Fischer worked for Schuman Cheese in 2015 and she started the brand with [[Costco]]. < ref > {{Cite web|last=Knox|first=Dave|title=How Whisps Created A New Snacking Category|url=https://www.forbes.com/sites/daveknox/2021/05/04/how-whisps-created-a-new-snacking-category/|access-date=2023-06-27|website=Forbes|language=en}} < /ref > 
 
 In 2021, John Ghingo, originally from Applegate Farms became the owner. < ref > {{Cite web|date=2022-02-01|title=Whisps Brings In Former Applegate Exec to Lead Company|url=https://www.nosh.com/news/2022/whisps-brings-in-former-applegate-exec-to-lead-company|access-date=2023-06-27|website=NOSH|language=en-US}} < /ref >  Later they held a contest where one had to eat the snack for a year, and the winner was paid $10,000. < ref > {{Cite web|date=2021-06-09|title=This company will play someone $10k to eat cheese for a year|url=https://news.yahoo.com/company-play-someone-10k-eat-223438657.html|access-date=2023-06-27|website=Yahoo News|language=en-US}} < /ref >  Later they came out with Cheese Crumbs and a nuts mix. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Whisps Snacks adds Cheese Crumbs and Cheese Crisps  &  Nuts {{!}} Dairy Foods|url=https://www.dairyfoods.com/articles/95395-whisps-snacks-adds-cheese-crumbs-and-cheese-crisps-and-nuts|access-date=2023-06-27|website=www.dairyfoods.com|language=en}} < /ref > 
 
 In 2023, they introduced a Buffalo flavor, and Cheese And Pretzel Bites to the product line. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Whisps debuts new cheese and pretzel snacks {{!}} Food Business News|url=https://www.foodbusinessnews.net/articles/21217-whisps-debuts-new-cheese-and-pretzel-snacks|access-date=2023-06-27|website=www.foodbusinessnews.net|language=en}} < /ref >
