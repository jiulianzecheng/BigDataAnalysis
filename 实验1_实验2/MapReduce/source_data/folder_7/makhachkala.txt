 '''Makhachkala''' ({{lang-rus|Махачкала}}; [[Lak language|Lak]]: Гьанжи; [[Avar language|Avar]]: МахӀачхъала; [[Lezgian language|Lezgian]]: Магьачкъала; [[Rutul language|Rutul]]: МахаӀчкала) is the [[capital city]] of the [[Republics of Russia|Republic]] of [[Dagestan]], southwestern [[Russia]]. < ref > {{Cite web|url=https://www.britannica.com/place/Makhachkala|title=Makhachkala|work=[[Encyclopædia Britannica]]|date=17 September 2018}} < /ref >  As of the 2010 [[census]], the city had 572,076 people [[population|living there]].
