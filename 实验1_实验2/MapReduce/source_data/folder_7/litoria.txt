[[File:Brown-green litoria caerulea.jpg|thumb|The [[Brown-green tree frog]] is one of many ''Litoria'' tree frogs.]] 
 '''Litoria''' is a [[genus]] of [[Hylidae]] [[tree frog]]s. They are [[wikt:native|native]] to [[Australia]], the [[Bismarck Archipelago]], the [[Solomon Islands]], [[New Guinea]], the [[Lesser Sunda Islands]], the [[Moluccan Islands]], and [[Timor]].
