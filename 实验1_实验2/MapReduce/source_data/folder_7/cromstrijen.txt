 '''Cromstrijen''' is a former [[List of municipalities of the Netherlands|municipality]] in the [[Netherlands|Dutch]] [[Provinces of the Netherlands|province]] of [[South Holland]]. On 1 January 2019, it was merged with the former municipalities of [[Binnenmaas]], [[Korendijk]], [[Oud-Beijerland]], and [[Strijen]] to form the new municipality of [[Hoeksche Waard (municipality)|Hoeksche Waard]].
