 '''Thaal Sinestro''' ({{IPAc-en|θ|ɑː|l|_|s|ɪ|ˈ|n|ɛ|s|t|r|oʊ}}) is a fictional [[supervillain]] appearing in [[American comic book]]s published by [[DC Comics]]. Sinestro is a former [[Green Lantern Corps]] member who was [[dishonorably]] discharged for abusing his power. He is the [[archenemy]] of [[Green Lantern]] and founder of the [[Sinestro Corps]], though he occasionally has acted in [[anti-hero]]ic roles as well. 
 
 The character was created by [[John Broome (writer)|John Broome]] and [[Gil Kane]], and first appeared in ''Green Lantern'' (vol. 2) # 7 (August 1961). 
 
 In 2009, [[IGN]] ranked Sinestro as the 15th Greatest Comic Book Villain of All Time. < ref > {{cite web | url = http://comics.ign.com/top-100-villains/15.html | title = Sinestro is number 15 | work = [[IGN]] | publisher = [[News Corp]] }} < /ref >
