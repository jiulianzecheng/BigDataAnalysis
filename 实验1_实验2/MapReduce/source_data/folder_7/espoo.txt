 '''Espoo''' ({{lang-sv|Esbo}}) is the second largest [[city]] in [[Uusimaa]], [[Finland]]. As of January 2014, more than 260,000 people lived in Espoo. Other municipalities near to it are [[Kauniainen]], [[Vantaa]], [[Kirkkonummi]], [[Nurmijärvi]], [[Vihti]] and the capital [[Helsinki]]. 
 
 ==Nature== 
 There are several lakes in the northern parts of Espoo. Example Lippajärvi, Pitkäjärvi, Nuuksion Pitkäjärvi and Velskolan Pitkäjärvi.
