 '''WestJet Airlines Ltd.''' is a [[Canada|Canadian]] [[Budget airline|low-cost carrier]]. It was founded on February 29, 1992. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.kamloopsairport.com/airlines.htm|accessdate=2014-07-21|title=Airlines|publisher=[[Kamloops Airport]]|archive-date=2015-01-15|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150115141450/http://www.kamloopsairport.com/airlines.htm|url-status=dead}} < /ref >  It is Canada's second largest air carrier, behind [[Air Canada]]. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://usatoday30.usatoday.com/travel/flights/2009-02-05-westjet-codeshare_N.htm?csp=34|accessdate=2014-07-21|title=WestJet, Air France, KLM work toward code-share|publisher=[[USA Today]]}}{{Dead link|date=September 2021 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }} < /ref >  It operates an average of 425 flights and carries over 45,000 passengers per day. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.peninsulanewsreview.com/news/122369349.html|accessdate=2014-07-21|title=WestJet passengers get bumpy start to long weekend|publisher=Peninsula News Review|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120531065316/http://www.peninsulanewsreview.com/news/122369349.html|archive-date=2012-05-31}} < /ref >  It was the eighth-largest airline in North America by passengers carried and carried 18.5 million passengers in 2013. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://westjet2.mediaroom.com/index.php?s=43 & item=743|accessdate=2014-07-21|title=WestJet reports record full-year earnings per share of $1.78, up 68 per cent|publisher=WestJet}} < /ref > 
 
 WestJet has a subsidiary regional airline called WestJet Encore, which started operating on June 24, 2013 with [[Bombardier Q200|Bombardier Q400]] twin-turboprop aircraft. < ref > {{cite news|url=http://business.financialpost.com/2012/11/07/westjets-new-regional-carrier-encore-aims-for-mid-june-takeoff/|accessdate=2014-07-21|title=WestJet's new regional carrier Encore aims for mid-June takeoff|newspaper=[[Financial Post]]|date=7 November 2012|last1=Deveau|first1=Scott}} < /ref >
