'''Soham''' is a [[town]] and [[Civil parishes in England|civil parish]] in [[East Cambridgeshire]], [[Cambridgeshire]], England. In 2001 there were 9,102 people living in Soham. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.ons.gov.uk/help/localstatistics|title=Local statistics - Office for National Statistics|website=www.ons.gov.uk}} < /ref > 
 
 The town came to international attention in August 2002 following the disappearance of two girls, Holly Wells and Jessica Chapman.
