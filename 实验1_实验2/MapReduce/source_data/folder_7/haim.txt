'''HAIM''' (which is said like 'time' with a 'h' instead of a 't') is an American [[indie pop]] band. The members are sisters Alana, Danielle, and Este. They released their first album ''Days Are Gone'' on September 27 2013. The [[British]] newspaper The Guardian put the album at number twelve on their list of 2013's 40 best albums. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.theguardian.com/music/musicblog/2013/dec/06/guardian-best-albums-2013-20-11|title=Best albums of 2013: 20-11|first=Guardian|last=music|date=6 December 2013|publisher=|via=www.theguardian.com}} < /ref >  They are often compared to [[Fleetwood Mac]]. 
 
 ==Career== 
 The sisters grew up in the San Fernando Valley in [[Los Angeles]]. In 2006 they started HAIM. For the next five years the band performed where they lived. On February 10, 2012 HAIM released their first EP ''Forever''. It was on their website to download for free for a short amount of time.  
 
 In January 2013 HAIM won BBC's Sound of 2013 poll. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.theguardian.com/music/2013/jan/04/haim-win-bbc-sound-of-2013|title=Haim win BBC Sound of 2013 poll|first=Guardian|last=music|date=4 January 2013|publisher=|via=www.theguardian.com}} < /ref > 
 
 On September 22, 2013 HAIM released their fourth single from their album ''Days Are Gone'',  " The Wire " . It reached number sixteen in the UK Singles Chart. It was their highest charting single in the UK yet. 
 
 On November 23 2013 HAIM performed their singles  " The Wire "  and  " Don't Save Me "  on [[Saturday Night Live]].
