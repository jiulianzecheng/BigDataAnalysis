 '''Bammy''' or '''bami''' is a traditional [[Jamaica]]n [[cassava]] [[flatbread]].  It is like the simple flatbread eaten by the Arawaks. Today, it is produced in many places.  It is sold in stores and by street sellers in Jamaica.
