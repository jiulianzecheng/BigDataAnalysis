 '''Denklingen''' is a [[Municipalities of Germany|municipality]] in [[Landsberg (district)|Landsberg]] (also called Landsberg am Lech) in [[Bavaria]] in [[Germany]]. The former municipality of [[Epfach]], which has a history back to the Roman period, is now part of Denklingen. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 Denklingen is the most southwesterly municipality in Landsberg. It is on flats to the west of the [[Lech (river)|Lech River]]. It is at the edge of Denklinger Rotwalds (red forest of Denklingen) and Sachsenrieder forest.
