 '''''Mikroglottika - An International Journal of Minority Languages Philologies''''', < ref > Its [[ISSN]] is 1998-0863. < /ref >  is a language scientific magazine about [[minority languages]]. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.mikroglottika.com/|title=502 Bad Gateway|website=www.mikroglottika.com|access-date=2019-02-13|archive-date=2013-10-16|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20131016141339/http://www.mikroglottika.com/|url-status=dead}} < /ref >  The word ''Mikroglottika'' means ''minority language studies''. 
 
 It encourages studies about [[minority languages]] philology, as [[phonology]], [[syntax]], [[lexicon]] and related topics. 
 
 It is edited by [[Peter Lang]] < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.peterlang.com/index.cfm?vSiteName=SearchBooksResult.cfm & vLang=E & iValue=Stichwort & iQuickSearch=mikroglottika & iStichwort=mikroglottika & vSearchBooks=Yes & vRecordPhrase=True & CFID=7047050 & CFTOKEN=55196073|title=Mikroglottika reference in Peter Lang's Official Website|publisher=}}{{Dead link|date=January 2021 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }} < /ref >  in [[Germany]]. Teachers from Universities of several European countries also contribute. 
 Its Yearbook 2008 has content about these languages: 
 
 * [[Basque language|Basque]] 
 * [[German language|German]] 
 * [[Leonese language|Leonese]] 
 * [[Luxemburgian language|Luxemburgian]] 
 * [[Ukrainian language|Ukrainian]]
