 The '''Piperaceae''', also known as the '''pepper family''', is a large family of [[flowering plant]]s. The group contains roughly 3,610 currently accepted species in five genera. The vast majority of peppers can be found within the two main genera: ''[[Piper (genus)|Piper]]'' (2000 species) and ''[[Peperomia]]'' (1600 species).
