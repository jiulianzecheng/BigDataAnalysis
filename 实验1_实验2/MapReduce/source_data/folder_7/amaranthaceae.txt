 '''Amaranthaceae''' is a family of [[flowering plant]]s are the [[Amaranth]] family, contains about 160 genera and 2,400 species. Most of these species are herbs or subshrubs; very few are trees or climbers. 
 
 This is a widespread and cosmopolitan family found mostly in subtropical and tropical regions, although many species belong in cool temperate regions.
