 '''Saral''' is a village and [[Union Councils of Pakistan|Union Council]], an administrative subdivision, of [[Chakwal District]] in the [[Punjab (Pakistan)|Punjab Province]] of [[Pakistan]]. It is part of [[Chakwal Tehsil]] < ref > {{Cite web |url=http://www.nrb.gov.pk/lg_election/union.asp?district=6 & dn=Chakwal |title=Tehsils  &  Unions in the District of Chakwal |access-date=2008-09-20 |archive-date=2008-01-24 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20080124031142/http://www.nrb.gov.pk/lg_election/union.asp?district=6 & dn=Chakwal |url-status=dead }} < /ref >  and is located at 33°5'39N 72°55'18E. < ref > [http://www.fallingrain.com/world/PK/4/Saral.html Location of Saral - Falling Rain Genomics] < /ref >
