'''Upminster''' is a place in the east [[London Borough of Havering]], [[England]]. It is the eastern end of the [[London Underground]] system, on the [[District line]] which is green. It is also a station on the [[c2c]] line, but [[Oyster card]]s  and travel cards are not valid beyond Upminster. Nearby places include Hornchurch, Upminster Bridge and Cranham. 
 
 [[San Lorenzo|Saint Laurence]] Church in Upminster is where [[William Derham]] measured the speed of [[sound]].
