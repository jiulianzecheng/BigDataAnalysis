 '''Bessans''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is in [[Auvergne-Rhône-Alpes]] in the [[Savoie]] [[Departments of France|department]] in southeast [[France]]. < ref > [https://www.insee.fr/fr/metadonnees/cog/commune/COM73040-bessans Commune de Bessans (73040)], INSEE < /ref >  It is known for its large [[Nordic skiing]] area in the winter and for its [[trekking]] and [[mountain climbing]] opportunities in the summer.
