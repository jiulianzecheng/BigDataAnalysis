 '''''EastEnders''''' is a [[soap opera|British drama season TV soap opera]] in the [[United Kingdom]]. It has been running since February 1985, and has had over 5700 episodes. The programme is a rival of ''[[Coronation Street]]'' and ''[[Emmerdale]]''. It is shown on [[BBC One]]. 
 
 The programme is in a pretend area of East [[London]], called Walford. It is filmed in [[Elstree]], a village in [[Borehamwood]]. Like ''Coronation Street'', most action is around a local [[pub]]. The pub in ''EastEnders'' is called [[The Queen Vic]]. 
 
 There are many story lines, including [[Illegal drugs|drug abuse]], [[rape]], [[racism]], [[domestic violence]], [[murder]], [[kidnapping]], [[prostitution]], [[theft]], [[dementia|senile dementia]], [[arson]], and runaway children. 
 
 Arguments between members of one family, or between different families, are often in the storyline.
