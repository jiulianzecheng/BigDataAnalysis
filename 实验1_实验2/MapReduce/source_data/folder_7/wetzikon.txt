 '''Wetzikon''' is a small town of the [[district]] of [[Hinwil (district)|Hinwil]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Zurich (canton)|Zurich]] in [[Switzerland]]. It is near [[Lake Pfäffikon]] in the [[Zürcher Oberland]], between [[Uster]] and [[Rapperswil-Jona]].
