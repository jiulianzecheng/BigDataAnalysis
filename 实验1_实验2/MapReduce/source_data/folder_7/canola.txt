'''Canola''' refers to both an edible [[oil]] (canola oil) and a type of [[rapeseed]]. 
 
 The plant is a [[cultivar]] of ''[[Brassica napus]]'', ''[[Brassica rapa]]'' or ''[[Brassica juncea]]''.
