 The '''Gabelmann''' ( " Fork man " ) is a [[statue]] with a [[fountain]] in [[Bamberg]], [[Germany]]. It is a [[Baroque]] statue of [[Poseidon|Neptune]], the ancient [[Roman mythology|Roman]] [[deity|god]] of the [[sea]], who is holding his three-pronged [[fork]]. This is why he has the name Gabelmann.

 The Gabelmann is a favourite place for people to meet in Bamberg.
