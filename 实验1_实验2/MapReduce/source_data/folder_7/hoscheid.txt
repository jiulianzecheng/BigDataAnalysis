 '''Hoscheid''' ({{lang-lb|Houschent}}) is a former [[Communes of Luxembourg|commune]] in [[Luxembourg]]. It was part of the [[Diekirch (canton)|canton of Diekirch]]. 
 
 On 1 January 2012, the commune was merged with [[Consthum]] and [[Hosingen]] to form the new commune of [[Parc Hosingen]].
