 '''Westerkwartier''' is a [[List of municipalities of the Netherlands|municipality]] in the province of [[Groningen (province)|Groningen]], the [[Netherlands]]. In 2019, 63,031 people lived there. 
 
 It was created on 1 January 2019 from the former municipalities of [[Grootegast]], [[Leek, Netherlands|Leek]], [[Marum]], [[Zuidhorn]] and partly [[Winsum]].
