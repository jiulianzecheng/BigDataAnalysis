 '''Exodusters''' were a name given to [[African American|African Americans]] who moved from states that were along the [[Mississippi River]] to [[Kansas]]. This happened in the late 1800s. It was called the '''Exoduster movement''' or the '''Exodus of 1879'''. < ref > {{cite journal |last=Van Deusen |first=John G. |year=1936 |title=The Exodus of 1879 |journal=[[The Journal of Negro History]] |publisher=Association for the Study of African American Life and History |volume=21 |issue=2 |pages=111â€“129 |doi=10.2307/2714567 |jstor=2714567|s2cid=224830636 }} < /ref >  It was the first [[Human migration|migration]] of black people after the [[American Civil War]]. < ref > {{cite book |last1=Johnson |first1=Daniel M. |last2=Campbell |first2=Rex R. |title=Black Migration in America: A Social Demographic History |url=https://archive.org/details/blackmigrationin0000john |publisher=[[Duke University Press]] |publication-place=Durham, NC |year=1981 |page=[https://archive.org/details/blackmigrationin0000john/page/51 51] |isbn=0-8223-0449-X |oclc=6421175}} < /ref >  About 40,000 black people moved from the south to Kansas, [[Oklahoma]], and [[Colorado]]. < ref name= " sia " > {{Cite web |title=Slavery in America Encyclopedia |url=http://www.slaveryinamerica.org/scripts/sia/glossary.cgi?term=k & letter=yes |access-date=2007-10-19 |archive-date=2007-08-27 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20070827032216/http://www.slaveryinamerica.org/scripts/sia/glossary.cgi?term=k & letter=yes |url-status=dead }} < /ref > 
 
 Black people moved from the south because they were treated very bad. They were running away from groups like the [[Ku Klux Klan]] and the White League, and they were running away from black codes and [[Jim Crow laws]]. They were treated like [[Second-class citizen|second-class citizens]]. < ref name= " africana " > {{cite book |last=Gates |first=Henry Louis |author-link=Henry Louis Gates |title=Africana: The Encyclopedia of the African and African American Experience |url=https://archive.org/details/africanaencyclop00appi |publisher=[[Basic Civitas Books]] |year=1999 |pages=[https://archive.org/details/africanaencyclop00appi/page/722 722] |isbn=0-465-00071-1}} < /ref >  This was during [[Reconstruction of the United States|Reconstruction]].
