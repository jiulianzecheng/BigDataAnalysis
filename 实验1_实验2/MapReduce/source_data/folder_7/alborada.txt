 '''Alborada''' (by its title in [[English language|English]]: '''The Dawning'''), is a [[Mexico|Mexican]] [[telenovela]] with the participation of [[Lucero (entertainer)|Lucero]] and [[Fernando Colunga]]. 
 
 This telenovela deals with the [[Viceroyalty of New Spain]], years before the Mexican War of Independence, and is also about events such as the [[Inquisition|Holy Inquisition]] (Also known as the [[Mexican Inquisition]]), the main song  " Alborada "  would be composed by Jorge Avedaño and performed by the tenor [[Plácido Domingo]]. < ref > {{cite web|title=Alborada (TV Series)|url=http://www.filmaffinity.com/en/film527852.html|access-date=October 24, 2005|publisher=filmaffinity.com|language=es}} < /ref > 
 
 == Plot == 
 
 Hipolita Díaz (''Lucero''), a young woman who lives with her mother, who is due to marry Antonio de Guzmán ([[Arturo Peniche]]), Hipolita due to not loving him, she has to put up with it all the time. 
 
 Don Luis Manrique (''Fernando Colunga''), Cousin of Diego Arellano ([[Luis Roberto Guzmán]]), the renowned [[Guevara, Spain|Count of Guevara]], sometimes compete with each other, and after a trip to [[Panama]], he would meet Hipolita, and they would have a romance that would give birth to a a man named ''Rafael'' ([[Alexander Renaud]]), and they would have to act like they would not have a romance with each other, even though they would have to face many problems with ''Antonio de Guzmán, Juana Arellano'' ([[Daniela Romo]]) and the ''Count de Guevara'' himself.
