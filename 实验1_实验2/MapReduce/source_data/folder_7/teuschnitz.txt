 '''Teuschnitz''' ({{IPA-de|ˈtɔɪʃ.nɪt͡s|lang|De-Teuschnitz.ogg}}) is a [[Town#Germany|town]] in [[Kronach (district)|Kronach]], in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. It is in the [[Frankenwald]], 19 & nbsp;km north of [[Kronach]], and 15 & nbsp;km east of [[Sonneberg]].
