'''Incarnation''' is a religious belief. It explains how a spiritual being may become a body (incarnates).  Examples of incarnations in various religions include: 
 * [[Incarnations of Leimarel Sidabi|Leimarel Sidabi]], of [[Sanamahism]] 
 * [[Krishna]], of [[Hinduism]] 
 * [[Buddha]], of [[Buddhism]] 
 * [[Jesus]], of [[Christianity]] 
 * [[Guan Yin]], of Buddhism 
 * [[Kali]], of Hinduism 
 * [[Durga]], of Hinduism 
 * [[Parvati]], of Hinduism
