[[File:Vishnu and Shiva in a combined form, as  " Hari-hara, " .jpg|thumb|Harihara]] 
 '''Harihara''' is the [[deity]] that combines [[Vishnu]] and [[Shiva]]. It comes from the [[Hinduism|Hindu]] tradition. ''Harihara'' is also sometimes used as a philosophical term when one wants to speak of the unity of [[Vishnu]] and [[Shiva]] as different aspects of the same Supreme God.
