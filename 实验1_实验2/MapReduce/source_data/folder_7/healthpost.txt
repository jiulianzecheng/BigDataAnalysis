 == History == 
 HealthPost is an [[:en:Online_retailer|online retailer]] specialising in [[:en:Naturopathy|natural health]], beauty, and health food products. The company has expanded its operations through partnerships with various technology and logistics firms. < ref name= " :4 "  / > < ref name= " :6 " > {{Cite web|title=HealthPost Case Studies|url=https://www.sli-systems.com/case_studies/health-post}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite web|last=Sharma|first=Soumya|date=2023-05-05|title=HealthPost acquires Australian retailers Flora  &  Fauna and Nourished Life|url=https://www.retail-insight-network.com/news/healthpost-acquires-flora-fauna-nourishedlife/|access-date=2023-08-17|website=Retail Insight Network|language=en-US}} < /ref > 
 
 Established in 1988 by Linley Butler, HealthPost is based in Collingwood,  [[Golden Bay]]. Originally, it operated as a [[:en:Mail_order|mail-order]] company for [[:en:Natural_health_product|natural-health products]] in [[New Zealand]], notably for [[Rural area|rural]] women. < ref name= " :5 " > {{Cite web|date=2019-11-26|title=How a Golden Bay mum's mail-order business became HealthPost, an online mecca for health products|url=https://thisnzlife.co.nz/how-a-golden-bay-mums-mail-order-business-became-healthpost-an-online-mecca-for-health-products/|access-date=2023-08-17|website=thisNZlife}} < /ref >  In 2002, it launched an [[Online shopping|online retail]] platform. < ref name= " :6 "  / > 
 
 In 2009, Linley gave the leadership of the business to her children Abel and Lucy. < ref name= " :5 "  / > 
 
 HealthPost was named a finalist in the 2021 [[Sustainable Business Awards]]. < ref name= " :7 " > {{Cite web|title=HealthPost – 2021 Awards finalist|url=https://sustainable.org.nz/sustainable-business-awards/award-winners-finalists-2021/healthpost-2021-awards-finalist/|access-date=2023-08-17|website=sustainable.org.nz|language=en-NZ}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite web|last=Townshend|first=Katie|date=2021-11-01|title=Golden Bay business HealthPost double finalist in sustainability awards|url=https://www.stuff.co.nz/nelson-mail/news/126801540/golden-bay-business-healthpost-double-finalist-in-sustainability-awards|access-date=2023-08-17|website=Stuff|language=en}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite web|date=2021-11-03|title=Sustainability in a pandemic: the unsung business heroes of NZ|url=https://www.stuff.co.nz/business/better-business/126860678/sustainability-in-a-pandemic-the-unsung-business-heroes-of-nz|access-date=2023-08-17|website=Stuff|language=en}} < /ref >
