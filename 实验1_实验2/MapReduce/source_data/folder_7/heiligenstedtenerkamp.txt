 '''Heiligenstedtenerkamp''' is a [[municipality]] of the district [[Steinburg (district)|Steinburg]], in [[Schleswig-Holstein]], [[Germany]]. It is the municipality with the second longest name in Germany with its 21 characters, after [[Gottfrieding|Gottfriedingerschwaige]] with its 22 characters.
