 '''Mundon''' is a village and [[civil parish]] in [[Maldon District]], [[Essex]], England. In 2001 there were 356 people living in Mundon. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.ons.gov.uk/help/localstatistics|title=Local statistics - Office for National Statistics|website=www.ons.gov.uk}} < /ref >  Mundon has a church called St Mary which went redundant on 14/04/1970. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.essexchurches.info/church.aspx?p=Mundon|title=St Mary, Mundon Church, Essex|website=www.essexchurches.info}} < /ref >
