The '''Tabernacle''' was a [[tent]] used in the Book of [[Exodus]] as a [[temple]] or dwelling place of [[God]]. The word is still used in [[Catholicism]] for the box in which the Host or [[Eucharist]]ic bread is kept, and for [[Non-conformism|Non-conformist]] and [[Mormon]] [[church]]es.
