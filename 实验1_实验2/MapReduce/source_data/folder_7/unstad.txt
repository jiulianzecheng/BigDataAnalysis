'''Unstad''' is a village in Norway's municipality of [[Vestvågøy]]. The village is on the isle of [[Vestvågøya]]. 
 
 The village's beach, Unstad Beach, has been used for [[surfing]] since 1963. < ref name=Surf1963 > {{cite news | url=https://www.theguardian.com/travel/2014/jul/18/arctic-aloha-worlds-most-northerly-surf-school | title=Arctic aloha: The world's most northerly surf school | newspaper=The Guardian | date=18 July 2014 | last1=Coldwell | first1=Will }} < /ref >
