'''Jamstik''' is a portable 6-string [[MIDI]] [[guitar]] controller with five [[fret]]s made by Zivix, LLC. The Jamstik is compatible with [[iPad]], [[iPhone]] and [[Apple Macintosh|Macintosh]].  It weighs {{convert|1|lb|9|oz}}. 
 
 The Jamstik comes with an included instructional app called JamTutor.
