 '''Nousiainen''' is a municipality in [[Southwest Finland]]. As of January 2014 about 4,900 people lived there. Neighbouring municipalities are [[Aura]], [[Masku]], [[Mynämäki]], [[Pöytyä]], [[Rusko]] and [[Turku]].  
 
 Most people there speak [[Finnish language|Finnish]]. 
 
 Part of [[Kurjenrahka National Park]] is located in part of the area of Nousiainen.
