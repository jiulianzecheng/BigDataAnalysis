 '''Virolahti''' ({{lang-sv|Vederlax}}) is a municipality in [[Kymenlaakso]], [[Finland]]. It is on the border with [[Russia]]. As of 28 February 2023, 2,979 people lived there. Neighbouring municipalities are [[Hamina]] and [[Miehikkälä]].
