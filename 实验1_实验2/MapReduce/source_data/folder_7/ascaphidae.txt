 '''Ascaphidae''' is a [[family (biology)|family]] of [[frog]]s. They are called '''tailed frogs''' because they have an [[Organ (anatomy)|organ]] that looks like a [[tail]]. The tail helps them to live in fast-moving [[stream]]s. They live in [[North America]]. < ref > {{Cite web |url=http://www.greendiamond.com/environment/wildlife.asp |title=Wildlife - Environment - Green Diamond Resource Company |access-date=2012-07-23 |archive-date=2008-11-19 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20081119151137/http://www.greendiamond.com/environment/wildlife.asp |url-status=dead }} < /ref >
