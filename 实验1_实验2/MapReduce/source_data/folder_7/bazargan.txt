 '''Bazargan''' ({{lang-fa|بازرگان}}; also [[Romanize]]d as '''Bāzargān''', '''Bāzarqān''', and '''Bāzergān''') is a [[city]] in [[Iran]]. It is the [[capital (city)|capital]] of [[Bazargan District]], in [[Maku County]], [[West Azerbaijan Province]]. At the 2006 census, its population was 9,047, in 2,126 families. < ref > {{IranCensus2006|04}} < /ref >
