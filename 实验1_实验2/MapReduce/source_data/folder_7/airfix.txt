 '''Airfix''' is a [[United Kingdom|UK]] manufacturer of plastic [[scale model]] kits. < ref name=air1 > {{cite web|url=http://www.airfix.com/about-airfix/|title=About Airfix|publisher=Airfix|access-date=2011-07-12|archive-date=2011-07-16|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110716031154/http://www.airfix.com/about-airfix|url-status=dead}} < /ref >  Airfix was founded in 1939 by Nicholas Kove. < ref name=air1 / >  Airfix is currently owned by [[Hornby]]. 
 During the 1960s and 1970s, the [[company]] grew much bigger as the kit modelling hobby became more popular. The range of models expanded at this time too.
