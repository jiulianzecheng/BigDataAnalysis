 '''Regnitzlosau''' is a municipality in Upper [[Franconia]] in [[Hof (district)|Hof]] in [[Bavaria]] in [[Germany]], bordered with the [[Czech Republic]]. Regnitzlosau was established in 1234 by three members of the nobility: Cunradus de Lasan, Arnoldus de Lasan and Ciban de Lasan.
