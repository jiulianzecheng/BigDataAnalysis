 '''Bauma''' is a [[municipality]] in the [[district]] of [[Pfäffikon (district)|Pfäffikon]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Zurich (canton)|Zurich]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 On 1 January 2015, the former municipality of [[Sternenberg, Switzerland|Sternenberg]] merged into the municipality of Bauma.
