 '''Belp''' is a [[municipality]] in the [[Bern-Mittelland (administrative district)|Bern-Mittelland]]  
 [[administrative district]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Canton of Bern|Berne]], [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 It is close to [[Bern]]'s [[Belp Airport]]. 
 
 The municipality of [[Belpberg]] merged on 1 January 2012 into the municipality of Belp.
