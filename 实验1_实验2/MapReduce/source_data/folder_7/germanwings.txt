 '''Germanwings''' was a [[low-cost carrier|low-cost]] [[airline]] owned by [[Lufthansa]]. It was based in [[Cologne]]. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.usatoday.com/story/news/world/2015/03/24/german-wings-airline-profile/70366322/|title=Germanwings well-known, low-cost carrier in Europe|first=|last=hjelmgaardkim|website=USA TODAY}} < /ref >  It was founded in 2002. It has more than 81 airplanes. It goes to 86 airports.
