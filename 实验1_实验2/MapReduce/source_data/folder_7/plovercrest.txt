 The '''plovercrest''' (''Stephanoxis lalandi'') is a [[species]] of [[hummingbird]]. It is found in [[Argentina]], [[Brazil]], and [[Paraguay]].  
 
 Its [[habitat]]s are [[subtropical]] or [[tropical]] moist [[montane]] forest, < ref > Montane = highlands below subalpine zone. < /ref >  and degraded former [[forest]]. The Plovercrest has a [[blue]] [[chest]], [[green]] [[back]] and [[white]] flanks.
