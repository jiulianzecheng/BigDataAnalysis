 '''Rjukan''' is a city in [[Norway]]. < ref name=City > {{cite web | url=http://kommunal-rapport.no/artikkel/byer_i_norge | title=Byer i Norge | date=24 May 2000 }} < /ref >  It is in the county of [[Telemark]]. The town had 3,386 people living there in January 2007.  
 
 Between September and March, Rjukan is in the shadow of the mountains and does not get sunlight.  
 
 ==Geology== 
 The city lies in a [[wikt:narrow|narrow]] [[valley]].
