'''Nathaniel''' [ n''uh''-'''than'''-y''uh''l ] < ref > {{Cite web|title=Definition of Nathaniel {{!}} Dictionary.com|url=https://www.dictionary.com/browse/nathaniel|access-date=2023-04-08|website=www.dictionary.com|language=en}} < /ref >  is a commonly found [[male]] [[name]] in [[English language|English]] that comes from the [[Hebrew language]] name [[Nathanael]]. It is often simplified into  " Nate "  or  " Nathan. "  The name Nathaniel has been in use for many centuries and has a significant backstory and significance. It comes from the Hebrew name Netan'el, which translates to  " gift of God. "  Nathaniel is referenced in the Bible as one of Jesus Christ's twelve apostles, who is also known as Bartholomew. The name Nathaniel is well-liked in various cultures and has been used for both male and female individuals. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Nathaniel name Meaning and Origin|url=https://babys.name/detail-Nathaniel-Meaning-Origin-Famous-Similar-Sibling.html|access-date=2023-04-08|website=babys.name}} < /ref >  This name was ranked #133 on the US Popular Names in 2021. < ref > {{Cite web|last=OACT|title=Popular Baby Names|url=https://www.ssa.gov/cgi-bin/popularnames.cgi|access-date=2023-04-08|website=www.ssa.gov}} < /ref >
