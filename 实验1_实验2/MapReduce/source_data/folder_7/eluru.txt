'''Eluru''' is the largest [[city]] in [[West Godavari district]] of [[Andhra Pradesh]], [[India]]. It is on the [[shore|banks]] of Tammileru river. Eluru is one of the 14 [[municipal corporation]]s, and [[headquarters]] of the Vijayawada [[Tehsil|mandal]] and West Godavari district. The city is well known for its wool-pile carpets and hand woven products. < ref > {{cite book| last1 = Babu| first1 = A.
