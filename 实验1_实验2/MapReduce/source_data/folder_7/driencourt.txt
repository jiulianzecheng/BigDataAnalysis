 '''Driencourt''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is in the [[Picardie]] [[Regions of France|region]] in the [[Somme]] [[Departments of France|department]] in the north of [[France]]. In 2011, 94 people lived there. 
 
 The village was destroyed during [[World War I]].
