 " '''Aegukka''' "  ({{respell|eh-GĞĞK-gah}}, meaning  " Patriotic Song "  in [[Korean language|Korean]]) is the [[national anthem]] of [[North Korea]]. To celebrate the country's independence from [[Empire of Japan|Japan]], it was written by Pak Se-yong and composed by Kim Won-gyun, then it was adopted in 1947 shortly after. < ref > {{Cite book|url=https://www.worldcat.org/oclc/744300599|title=Historical dictionary of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea|last=Hoare|first=James|date=2012|publisher=Scarecrow Press|isbn=978-0-8108-6151-0|location=Lanham, Md.|oclc=744300599}} < /ref >
