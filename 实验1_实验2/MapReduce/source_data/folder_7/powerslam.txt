This page describes the different types of '''powerslams''' in [[professional wrestling]]. 
 
 == Fireman's carry powerslam == 
 [[File:CenaF-U.jpg|thumb|right|[[John Cena]] performing the  " Attitude Adjustment "  (Fireman's carry powerslam) on [[Kurt Angle]].]] 
 This [[wikt:maneuver|maneuver]] has a wrestler lift their opponent on their back in a fireman's carry position. Grab their opponent's leg lift that side up and over turning to face their opponent in the process and then slam them to the mat.
