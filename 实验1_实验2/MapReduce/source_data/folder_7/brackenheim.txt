 {{Audio|Brackenheim.ogg|'''Brackenheim'''}} is a town in the ''Landkreis'' (district) of [[Heilbronn]] in [[Baden-Württemberg]] in southern [[Germany]]. It is {{convert|15|km|abbr=off}} southwest of [[Heilbronn]]. 
 With an area of {{convert|825|ha|km2 mi2|abbr=off}} of [[vineyard]]s, it is the biggest grape-growing [[municipality]] in [[Württemberg]]. < ref > {{Cite web |url=http://www.brackenheim.de/index.php?id=292 |title=Wine in Brackenheim, ''Stadt Brackenheim'', accessed 1/21/2014 |access-date=2014-01-21 |archive-date=2016-03-07 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160307063744/http://www.brackenheim.de/index.php?id=292 |url-status=dead }} < /ref >
