A '''coccus''' (plural of '''cocci''') is a [[bacteria|bacterium]] that is shaped like a [[sphere]] or [[circle]]. Cocci are one of the three types of bacteria shapes. The word coccus comes from the [[Greek language|Greek]] word ''kokkos'', or  " berry " . 
 
 Some examples of cocci are  [[Abiotrophia]], [[Enterococcus]], [[Neisseria]], [[Staphylococcus]], and [[Streptococcus]].
