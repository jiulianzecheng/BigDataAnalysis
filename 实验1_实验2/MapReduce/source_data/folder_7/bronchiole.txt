 The '''bronchioles''' or '''bronchioli''' are a part of the [[lung]]. They are the first [[airway]] branches that no longer have [[cartilage]]. They are branches of the [[bronchi]]. The bronchioles end by entering the circular sacs called [[alveolus|alveoli]].
