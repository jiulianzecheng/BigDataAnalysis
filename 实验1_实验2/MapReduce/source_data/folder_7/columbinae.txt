 '''Columbinae''' is a subfamily of birds from the family [[Columbidae]]. Otherwise, four genera ''[[Geotrygon]]'', ''[[Leptotila]]'', ''[[blue-headed quail-dove|Starnoenas]]'' and ''[[Zenaida doves|Zenaida]]'' form subfamily [[Leptotilinae]].
