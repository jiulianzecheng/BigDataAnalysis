 '''''Crotonoideae''''' is in the large order ''[[Malpighiales]]''. It is a [[subfamily]] of the large and diverse [[family (biology)|family]] ''[[Euphorbiaceae]]''. It includes crotons (''Codiaeum'') and hogworts (''Croton'').
