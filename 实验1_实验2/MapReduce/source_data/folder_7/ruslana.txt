 '''Ruslana Stepanivna Lyzhychko''' (May 24, 1973) is a popular [[Ukraine|Ukrainian]] [[pop music|pop]] [[singer]], [[composer]], [[producer]], [[Conductor (music)|conductor]] and [[dancer]]. She was born in the [[city]] of [[Lviv]] in Ukraine. She won the [[Eurovision Song Contest]] in [[Istanbul]] in 2004.   
 
 She is also a former [[Member of Parliament]] serving as deputy in the Ukrainian parliament ([[Verkhovna Rada]]) for the Our Ukraine Party. 
 
 == Human rights ==

At night she slept in a cold tent.  Every night she sang the Ukrainian [[national anthem]] for the protestors. < ref > {{cite news|url=https://www.washingtonpost.com/posttv/world/ruslana-serenades-protesters-in-kiev/2014/03/04/a5a01398-a3df-11e3-b865-38b254d92063_video.html|title=Ruslana serenades protesters in Kiev|newspaper=[[The Washington Post]] |publisher=}} < /ref >  When the government police came, she stayed on the stage.
