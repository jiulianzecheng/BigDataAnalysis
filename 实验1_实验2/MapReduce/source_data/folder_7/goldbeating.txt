'''Goldbeating''' is the process of [[Hammer|hammering]] [[gold]] into an very thin [[gold leaf]] for use in '''gilding'''. 5,000 years ago, [[Egypt|Egyptian]] [[Artisan|artisans]] became the first goldbeaters and gilders.
