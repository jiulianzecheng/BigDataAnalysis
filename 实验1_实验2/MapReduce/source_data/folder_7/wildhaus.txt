 '''Wildhaus''' is a [[village]] and [[municipality]] near [[St. Gallen]] in the canton of [[St. Gallen (canton)|St. Gallen]] in [[Switzerland]]. About 1,200 people live there. [[Huldrych Zwingli]] was born there in 1484. With ''9658'', Wildhaus has the highest [[zip code]] in Switzerland. On 1 January 2010, the former municipalities of Wildhaus and [[Alt St. Johann]] merged into the new municipality of [[Wildhaus-Alt St. Johann]]. < ref name=Fusion > [http://www.bfs.admin.ch/bfs/portal/de/index/infothek/nomenklaturen/blank/blank/gem_liste/03.html Amtliches Gemeindeverzeichnis der Schweiz] published by the Swiss Federal Statistical Office {{in lang|de}} accessed 23 September 2009 < /ref >
