 '''Elx''' (in [[Catalan language|Catalan]]) or '''Elche''' (in [[Spanish language|Spanish]]) is the third biggest city in [[Valencia (autonomous community)|Valencia]], and the second in the [[Alicante province]], in eastern [[Spain]]. The city has 215,000 inhabitants and is famous for its [[palm tree]]s and the Mystery of Elx, a theatrical piece.
