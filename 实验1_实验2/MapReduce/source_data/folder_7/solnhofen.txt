 '''Solnhofen''' is a [[Municipalities of Germany|municipality]] in [[Weißenburg-Gunzenhausen]] in [[Middle Franconia]] in the ''{{Lang|de|Land}}'' of [[Bavaria]] in [[Germany]].  It is in the [[Altmühl]] valley. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 Solnhofen is on the [[Altmühl]] in [[Bavaria]]. It has two districts: Hochholz and Eßlingen. It is next to the cities [[Pappenheim]], [[Langenaltheim]] and [[Mörnsheim]].
