 '''Saratov''' ({{lang-ru|Саратов}}), is a [[city]] and a major [[port]] on the [[Volga River]]. It is upstream (north) of [[Volgograd]]. It was [[wikt:found|found]]ed in the year 1590. < ref name=RP > {{cite web |url=http://russiatrek.org/saratov-city |title=Saratov city, Russia (Saratow)|author= |date= |website= |publisher=russiatrek.org|accessdate=5 April 2015}} < /ref >  Saratov had a [[population]] of 827,000 in 2010. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://russiaprofile.org/bg_places/resources_territory_districts_saratov.html |title=BackGroundPlaces, Saratov |author= |date= |website= |publisher=Russia Profile, 2011 |accessdate=5 April 2015 |archive-date=27 March 2015 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150327165150/http://russiaprofile.org/bg_places/resources_territory_districts_saratov.html |url-status=dead }} < /ref >  It is the [[administrative center]] of [[Saratov Oblast]]. < ref name=RP/ >
