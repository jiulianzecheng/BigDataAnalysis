'''Ein''' or '''EIN''' can have different meanings: 
 
 *[[Employer Identification Number]] - a number assigned by the [[United States Internal Revenue Service]] to all [[employers]].  
 
 *''Ein'' means  " a "  or  " one (of something) "  in some [[Germanic language]]s for [[nouns]] with [[Masculine gender|masculine]] or [[neuter]] [[grammatical gender]] in the [[nominative]] case. The [[feminine]] form is  " ''eine'' " . Example: in German  " {{lang|de|Ein Hund}} "  is  " A dog "  ( " {{lang|de|eine Esche}} "  is  " an [[Ash tree|ash]] "  (the tree)). Not to be confused with  " ''{{lang|de|eins}}'', "  which means  " (the number) one " .  
 
 *Ein also means  " a "  and  " one "  in [[Faroese language|Faroese]] in the male and female grammatical genders but not in the neuter. Example  " {{lang|fo|Ein hundur}} " . 
 
 *[[Ein (Anime)|Ein]] is a character in the Anime-series ''[[Cowboy Bebop]]''.
