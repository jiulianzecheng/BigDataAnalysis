 '''Achern''' ({{IPA2|ˈaxəʁn}}) is a city in Western [[Baden-Württemberg]], [[Germany]].  It is about {{convert|18|km|abbr=off}} southwest of [[Baden-Baden]] and {{convert|19|km|abbr=off}} northeast of [[Offenburg]].  Achern is the fourth largest city in the county of [[Ortenaukreis|Ortenau]] (Ortenaukreis), after [[Offenburg]], [[Lahr|Lahr / Black Forest]] and [[Kehl]].
