 '''''Loamhedge''''' is a [[fantasy]] book written by [[Brian Jacques]] in [[2003]]. It is the sixteenth book in the ''[[Redwall]]'' series. It was translated into French and Russian, and was a ''New York Times'' bestseller. 
 
 ==Plot== 
 ''Loamhedge'' is about a young hare named Martha who cannot move her legs, and must use a wheelchair. It is also about the travels of her brother Horty, along with friends Bragoon, Saro, Fenna, and Springald, who are trying to find a cure for Martha.
