 == History == 
 Tarrasmart was founded in 2013 by Cristianto Rian Tarra, who wanted to create smart robots that could assist humans in various tasks and automate business processes. The company's primary focus is on providing automation solutions to improve human life. Tarrasmart has made significant strides in the field of AI and has received recognition for its contributions to the industry. < ref > {{Cite web|last=Staff|first=Startup Info|date=2021-01-08|title=Tarrasmart - The Art of a Successful Pivot|url=https://startup.info/cristianto-rian-tarra-tarrasmart/|access-date=2023-03-04|language=en-US}} < /ref > 
 
 == Products and Services == 
 Tarrasmart specializes in developing AI solutions that provide real-time [[analytics]] and automate business processes. The company's products and services include smart robots that can assist humans in various tasks, automation opportunities in the financial industry, and real-time analytics that can help businesses make informed decisions.
