'''Adenoids''' (also called '''pharyngeal tonsils''', or '''nasopharyngeal tonsils''') are [[Tissue (biology)|tissues]] at the very back of the [[nose]].  They are in the part of the nose where is joins the [[mouth]]. 
 
 In most children they make a soft bump on the top and back section of the nose's air passage. 
 
 Taking away adenoids with surgery is called [[adenoidectomy]].
