 '''Brok nad Bugiem''' (English: ''Brok near Bug'') is a small town in [[Mazowsze Voivodship]], [[Poland]]. Brok is in [[Ostrowian County]] (head town of this region is [[Ostrów Mazowiecka]]). Brok is one of the smallest places in [[Europe]] with town status (since [[1500]]). 
 
 * Area: 28,05 [[km²]] 
 * Number of people (as of [[2004]]): 1882 
 * Community: Brok 
 * Post code: 07-306 
 * Current Mayor: [[Stanisław Bębenek]] (since [[2002]]) 
 
 Brok is regarded by many as one of the most beautiful places in this part of Poland. This is a tourist town near two [[river]]s: [[Bug (rivers)|Bug]] and one also named Brok. 
 
 Attractions include: 
 * Gothic Church (built [[1560]]) 
 * Old Jewish Cemetery 
 * Location (Puszcza Biała, ''White Wood'') 
 * Many old houses
