 '''Oberpframmern''' is a community in the [[Upper Bavaria]]n district of [[Ebersberg (district)|Ebersberg]] and a member of the ''Verwaltungsgemeinschaft'' (administrative community) of Glonn. It is about 24 kilometres southeast of [[Munich]]. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 Oberpframmern is in the [[Munich]] Region. It has only one traditional rural land unit (''Gemarkung'' in German), also called Oberpframmern.
