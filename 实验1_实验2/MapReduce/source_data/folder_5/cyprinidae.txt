'''Cyprinidae''' are a [[Family (biology)|family]] of [[freshwater fish]]. < ref name= " FB " > {{cite web|url=https://www.fishbase.de/Summary/FamilySummary.php?Family=Cyprinidae|title=Family Cyprinidae - Minnows or carps|publisher=[[FishBase]]|access-date=May 13, 2019|archive-date=August 1, 2020|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200801110754/https://www.fishbase.de/Summary/FamilySummary.php?Family=Cyprinidae|url-status=dead}} < /ref >  They are in the class [[Actinopterygii]], and include more than 3000 different species. < ref name= " FB " > < /ref >  They have a maximum length of over 2.5 metres (8 feet 2 inches) but most types of cyprinidae are smaller than 5cm (2 inches). < ref name= " FB " > < /ref >
