'''Najibullah''' ({{lang-ar | نجيبالله  }}) is a male Muslim given name. Najibullah is a beautiful name with a rich history and meaning. It is a Muslim name that is commonly used in Afghanistan and other parts of the world. The name is made up of two words: Najib and Allah. Najib means  " noble "  or  " distinguished, "  while Allah means  " God "  or  " the Almighty. "  Therefore, Najibullah means  " noble servant of God "  or  " distinguished worshipper of Allah.
