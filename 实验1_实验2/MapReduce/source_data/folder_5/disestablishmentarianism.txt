 Disestablishmentarianism is '''a person who favors the separation of church and state, especially the withdrawal of special rights, status, and support granted an established church by a state'''; an advocate of disestablishing a state church. 
 
 It originates from movements in [[Henry VIII]]'s England to remove the  " establishment "  of the Catholic Church.
