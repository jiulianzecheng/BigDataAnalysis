 '''Balltze''' < ref name= " CNN " > {{Cite web|title=Cheems Balltze, the internet’s cheeseburger-loving dog, has died|url=https://edition.cnn.com/2023/08/21/entertainment/cheems-balltze-viral-dog-dies-intl-scli/index.html|archive-date=|access-date=23 August 2023|website=CNN}} < /ref >  (9 January 2011 – 18 August 2023), popularity known as '''Cheems''', was a [[Shiba Inu]] from [[Hong Kong]]. He is known to be the subject of various memes. 
 
 == Biography == 
 Balltze was born on 9 January 2011 and was adopted at the age of one by Kathy. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Chatting With Cheems: Balltze’s Owner Gives Us Some Personal Insight Into Life With One Of The Web’s Favorite Shibas|url=https://knowyourmeme.com/editorials/interviews/chatting-with-cheems-balltzes-owner-gives-us-some-personal-insight-into-life-with-one-of-the-webs-favorite-shibas|access-date=23 August 2023|website=Know Your Meme}} < /ref >  Then, Kathy's brother named him after Ramune, a Japanese marble bottle. In May 2022, Balltze was diagnosed with [[pancreatitis]] and recovered in June 2023. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Cheems, el perro de los memes, está delicado de salud: “No está tan activo como antes”|url=https://www.lanacion.com.ar/estados-unidos/cheems-el-perro-de-los-memes-esta-delicado-de-salud-no-esta-tan-activo-como-antes-nid20052022/|access-date=23 August 2023|website=La Nación|language=Spanish}} < /ref >  Balltze died at the age of twelve from [[leukemia]], on 18 August 2023. < ref name= " CNN "  / >
