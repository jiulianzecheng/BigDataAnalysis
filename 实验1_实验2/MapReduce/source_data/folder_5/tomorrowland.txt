 '''Tomorrowland''' is an EDM (electronic dance music) [[music festival|festival]] in [[Boom, Antwerp|Boom]], [[Belgium]]. It started in 2005. Since 2012, there has been a spin-off in [[Atlanta]] called TomorowWorld.
