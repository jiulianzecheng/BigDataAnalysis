 '''Stobno''' {{IPAc-pl|'|s|t|o|b|n|o}} ({{lang-de|Stöven}}) is a [[village]] in the [[administrative district]] of [[Gmina Kołbaskowo]], within [[Police County]], [[West Pomeranian Voivodeship]], in north-western [[Poland]], close to the [[Germany|German]] border. < ref name= " TERYT " > {{cite web |url=http://www.stat.gov.pl/broker/access/prefile/listPreFiles.jspa |title=Central Statistical Office (GUS) - TERYT (National Register of Territorial Land Apportionment Journal) |date=2008-06-01 |language=Polish}} < /ref >  It lies about {{convert|17|km|mi|0}} south-west of [[Police, Poland|Police]] and {{convert|11|km|mi|0|abbr=on}} west of the regional capital [[Szczecin]]. 
 
 The village has a population of 450.
