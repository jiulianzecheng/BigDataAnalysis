 '''Devabhuti''' (reign. 83 - 73 BCE) was the last king of the [[Shunga Empire]] in ancient India. He was assassinated by his minister [[Vasudeva Kanva]]. Following his death, the [[Sunga Dynasty|Shunga dynasty]] was then replaced by the [[Kanva dynasty|Kanvas]].{{sfn|Raychaudhuri|2006|p=333}}
