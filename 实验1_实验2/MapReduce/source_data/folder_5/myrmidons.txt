 The '''Myrmidons''' ({{lang-el|Μυρμιδόνες}}, ''Myrmidones'') were a legendary people of [[Greek mythology]]. Native to the region of [[Thessaly]], they were said to be the fiercest and strongest of all the warriors in Greece; during the [[Trojan War]], they were led by [[Achilles]], as described in [[Homer]]'s ''[[Iliad]]''. According to Greek myth, they were created by [[Zeus]] from a colony of ants, from which their name is derived - [[Greek language|Greek]] ''myrmex'' ({{lang|el|μύρμηξ}}), meaning  " [[ant]] " .
