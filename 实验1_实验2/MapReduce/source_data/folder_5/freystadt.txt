 '''Freystadt''' is a [[Town#Germany|town]]  in [[Neumarkt (district)|Neumarkt]] in [[Bavaria]]. It is near the [[Rhine-Main-Danube Canal]], 14 & nbsp;km southwest of [[Neumarkt in der Oberpfalz]], and 33 & nbsp;km southeast of [[Nuremberg]].
