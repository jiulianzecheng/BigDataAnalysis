 '''Almetyevsk''' ({{lang-ru|Альметьевск}}; {{lang-tt-Cyrl|Әлмәт}}) is a city in [[Tatarstan]], [[Russia]]. It is on the left bank of the [[Zay River]] and is {{convert|265|km|abbr=on}} southeast of [[Kazan]]. In 2010, 146,393 people lived there.
