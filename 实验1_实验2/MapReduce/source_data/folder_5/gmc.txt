 '''GMC''' is an [[car|automobile]] maker, owned by [[General Motors]]. GMC is known for building [[truck]]s. Some of them are small, while others, like those promoted as  " Professional Grade " , are big. In the past, GMC was part of the ''General Motors Diesel Division'' which also made [[bus]]es, [[motorhome]]s, and vehicles for the [[military]]. 
 
 The [[company]] was [[:wikt:found|founded]] by [[Max Grabowsky]] in 1901. It was bought by General Motors in 1909. In 1989 GMC started to market the [[Suzuki]] Sidekick as the GMC Tracker in Canada. In 2006 GMC announced it would start selling unibody crossovers, its first was the [[GMC Acadia]], replacing the Safari minivan, GMC Envoy XL SUV and the [[Pontiac Montana|Pontiac Montana SV6]] minivan.
