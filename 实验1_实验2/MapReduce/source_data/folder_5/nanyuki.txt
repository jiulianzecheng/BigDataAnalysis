'''Nanyuki''' is a town in [[Kenya]]. It had an [[urban]] [[population]] of 31,577 in [[1999]]. 
 
 The town was started by [[British]] settlers in the early days of colonial Kenya in [[1907]]. Some of their [[descendant]]s still live in or around the town. Today, the town is multi-cultural and the [[market]] center for [[farm]]s, [[ranch]]es, game parks and [[wildlife]] conservancies in the region.
