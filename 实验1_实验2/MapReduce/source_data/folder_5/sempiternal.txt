 '''''Sempiternal''''' is the fourth [[Album|studio album]] by [[Bring Me the Horizon]]. Bring Me the Horizon is a British [[Rock music|rock]] band. The band released the album on 1 April 2013. In the United States and Canada, they released it on 2 April 2013. < ref name= " hammer-UK Release pushed " > {{cite web|author=Martin Kielty|date=1 March 2013|title=Hear The Whole New Bring Me The Horizon Album Sempiternal|url=http://www.metalhammer.co.uk/uncategorized/bring-me-the-horizon-hear-sempiternal-now/|access-date=3 March 2013|work=[[Metal Hammer]]}} < /ref > < ref name= " exclaim-US Release pushed " > {{cite web|author=Alex Hudson|title=Bring Me The Horizon 'Sempiternal' (album stream)|url=http://exclaim.ca/MusicVideo/ClickHear/bring_me_horizon-sempiternal_album_stream|access-date=3 March 2013|work=[[Exclaim!]]}} < /ref >  [[Epitaph Records|Epitath Records]] and [[RCA Records]] released it.  
 
 The album has four [[Single (music)|singles]]. These are  " Shadow Moses " ,  " Sleepwalking " ,  " Go to Hell, for Heaven's Sake "  and  " [[Can You Feel My Heart]] " . The album was number 3 on the [[UK Albums Chart]]. < ref name= " ukalbumcharts " > {{cite web|title=Archive Chart|url=http://www.officialcharts.com/chart-news/justin-timberlake-holds-imagine-dragons-from-albums-top-spot-2146/|access-date=2 August 2013|publisher=[[Official Charts Company]]}} < /ref >  It was number one on the [[ARIA Charts]] in Australia. The [[Recording Industry Association of America|RIAA]] gave the album a [[RIAA certification|gold certification]] in 2016.
