 '''CNBLUE''' is a [[South Korea]]n rock band formed in [[Seoul]] in 2009. There are four members: Jung Yong-hwa (lead vocals, rhythm guitar), Lee Jong-hyun (lead guitar, vocals), Lee Jung Shin (bass guitar, vocals) and Kang Min-hyuk (drums, vocals). 
 
 They have toured in South Korea and Japan.
