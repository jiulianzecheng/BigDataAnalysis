 '''Templemars''' ({{IPA-fr|tɑ̃pləmaʁ}}) is a [[Communes in France|commune]] in the [[Nord (department)|Nord]] department in northern [[France]]. The commune has a head office of [[Castorama]]. In 2019, 3,465 people lived there.
