 The '''Bullmastiff''' is a [[breed]] of [[dog]]. It started in 19th-century England. It is a mixture of a [[bulldog]] and a [[mastiff]]. It has a short [[tan]], [[red]]dish-[[brown]] [[coat (dog)|coat]] with black on the [[face]] and [[ears]]. 
   
 == Appearance == 
 It stands {{convert|24|-|27|in|cm}} and weighs {{convert|100|-|130|lb}}.
