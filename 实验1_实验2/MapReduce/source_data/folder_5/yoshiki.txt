 {{nihongo|'''Yoshiki Hayashi'''|林 佳樹|Hayashi Yoshiki|born November 20, 1965}} is a [[Japan]]ese musician. He is a co-founder of the Japanese band rock [[X Japan]].  He also founded an independent record label [[Extasy Records]]. < ref name= " eastern_standard_time " > {{cite book| last1      = Yang| first1     = Jeff| last2= Can|first2=Dina|last3=Hong|first3=Terry| title     = Eastern Standard Time| url      = https://archive.org/details/easternstandardt00yang| publisher = Houghton Mifflin| year      = 1997| location  = Boston| page     = [https://archive.org/details/easternstandardt00yang/page/264 264]| isbn      = 0-395-76341-X}} < /ref >  X Japan ended in 1997.  They got back together in 2007 and are currently touring worldwide. 
 
 Yoshiki's solo career includes two classical studio albums: ''[[Eternal Melody]]'' (1993), and ''[[Eternal Melody II]]'' (2005).
