 '''Naltrexone''' is an [[opioid receptor]] [[receptor antagonist|antagonist]]. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Naltrexone|url=https://www.samhsa.gov/medication-assisted-treatment/medications-counseling-related-conditions/naltrexone|access-date=2021-03-20|website=www.samhsa.gov|date=16 June 2015 |language=en}} < /ref >  It is often used to help [[alcohol dependence]] and [[opioid dependence]]. It is different from the drug [[naloxone]]. Naloxone is used in emergency cases of [[overdose]]. Naltrexone is for longer-term control of [[Substance dependence|dependence]]. Naltrexone can help with overdose, but naloxone is more helpful in those cases.
