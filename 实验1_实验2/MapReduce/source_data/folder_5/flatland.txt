 '''''Flatland''''' is a [[novella]] by Edwin Abbott Abbott. It is set in a fictional [[two-dimensional]] world. The books is a [[satire]] on society in the [[Victorian era]], where shapes are given jobs and roles based on how many sides they have. It also looks at [[dimension]]s, with math, shapes, and geometry.
