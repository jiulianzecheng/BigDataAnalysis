'''Coprophagia''' is the eating of [[faeces]]. Many [[animal]]s eat faeces, either their own or that of other animal groups. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Coprophagia|url=https://psychology.wikia.org/wiki/Coprophagia|access-date=2021-08-03|website=Psychology Wiki|language=en}} < /ref >  It is particularly common in [[Dog|dogs]] and puppies of about six months old, but little is known why; lack of [[stimulus]] and variety in the lifestyle or [[Diet (nutrition)|diet]] have been cited as potential reasons. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Coprophagia in Dogs - Symptoms, Causes, Diagnosis, Treatment, Recovery, Management, Cost|url=https://wagwalking.com/condition/coprophagia|access-date=2021-08-03|website=wagwalking.com|language=en}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite web|title=Coprophagia {{!}} Dog Behavior|url=https://www.tendercareanimalhospital.net/dog-coprophagia.html|access-date=2021-08-03|website=www.tendercareanimalhospital.net}} < /ref >
