 '''Kapelle''', [[Zeelandic dialect|Zeelandic]]: ''Kapelle'' or ''Kapel'n'',  is a village and municipality on the former island of [[Zuid-Beveland]], Province of [[Zeeland]], the [[Netherlands]]. 
 
 In 2017 the municipality had about 13,000 people. < br/  > 
 In 2016 the village had about 7,000 people.
