 '''Barakaldo''' ({{lang-es|Baracaldo}}) is a [[Municipalities of Spain|municipality]] of 100,881 people (2019). It is in the [[Provinces of Spain|province]] of [[Biscay]] in the [[Autonomous communities of Spain|autonomous community]] of the [[Basque Country (autonomous community)|Basque Country]] in [[Spain]].
