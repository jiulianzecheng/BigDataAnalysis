 '''Gluttony''' is a wasting of [[resource]]s. Usually it is [[food]] that is wasted. Either one person eats too much, or the person does something so the food (or the resources) do not reach those who need it. 
 
 In [[Christianity]], this is considered to be one of the [[Seven deadly sins]].
