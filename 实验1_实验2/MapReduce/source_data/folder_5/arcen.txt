 '''Arcen''' is a village in the [[Netherlands|Dutch]] province of [[Limburg]].  
 Since 2010 Arcen is part of the municipality of [[Venlo]]. 
 
 ==Climate== 
 The highest temperature ever recorded in Arcen was {{cvt|40.2|C|F}} on 25 July 2019. < ref >  https://www.wetterzentrale.de/extremes_y.php?station=391 & country=3 & order=1 & extreem=X_TX < /ref > 
 
 The lowest temperature ever recorded in Arcen was {{cvt|-19.9|C|F}} on 2 January 1997. < ref >  https://www.wetterzentrale.de/extremes_y.php?station=391 & country=3 & order=2 & extreem=X_TN < /ref >
