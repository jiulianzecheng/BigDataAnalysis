 '''Orlen S.A.''' (formerly ''Polski Koncern Naftowy Orlen S.A''., ''PKN Orlen'') is a Polish [[oil]] and [[gas]] company. It has a lot of petrol stations in [[Poland]], [[Germany]], [[Czech Republic]], [[Lithuania]] and [[Slovakia]]. It is the biggest company in Poland and one of the biggest oil and gas companies in [[Europe]]. 
 
 ==History== 
 Orlen was founded in People's Repubic of Poland. In 1999 it was privatized.
