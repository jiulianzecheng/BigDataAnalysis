'''Rheinfelden''' is: 
 
 * the name of two towns on the banks of the Rhine: 
 ** [[Rheinfelden (Baden)]] in the Lörrach Rural District of Baden-Württemberg, Germany 
 ** [[Rheinfelden, Switzerland]] in the Canton of Aargau, Switzerland, also 
 * [[Rheinfelden (district)]], a district in Aargau Canton.
