 '''Suonenjoki''' is a [[city]] in [[Northern Savonia]], [[Finland]]. It had almost 7,500 living there in January 2014. The municipalities next to it are [[Kuopio]], [[Leppävirta]], [[Pieksämäki]], [[Rautalampi]] and [[Tervo]].
