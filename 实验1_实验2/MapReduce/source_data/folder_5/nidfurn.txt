 '''Nidfurn''' is a [[village]] and former [[municipality]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Glarus (canton)|Glarus]], [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 It used to be its own municipality, until it was put together with 2 other municipalities to form [[Haslen]].
