 '''Teruel''' ({{IPA-es|teˈɾwel}}) is a city in [[Aragon]], located in eastern Spain, and is also the capital of [[Teruel (province)|Teruel Province]]. It has a population of 35,675 in 2014 < ref > Instituto Nacional de Estadística, Cifras oficiales de población resultantes de la revisión del Padrón municipal a 1 de enero de 2014, Resumen por Capitales de provincias < /ref >  making it the least populated provincial capital in the country.
