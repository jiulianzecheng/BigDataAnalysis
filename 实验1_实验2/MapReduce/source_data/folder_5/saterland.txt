 The '''Saterland''' ([[Saterland Frisian language|Saterland Frisian]]: {{lang|stq|Seelterlound}}, {{IPA-stq|ˈseːltɐˌloːunt|pron}}) is a [[Municipalities of Germany|municipality]] in [[Cloppenburg (district)|Cloppenburg]], in [[Lower Saxony]], [[Germany]]. It is between the cities of [[Leer]], [[Cloppenburg]], and [[Oldenburg (city)|Oldenburg]]. It is home to [[Saterland Frisians]], who speak [[Saterland Frisian language|Frisian]] and German. 
 
 The municipality of Saterland was formed in 1974, when the smaller municipalities [[Saterland-Strücklingen|Strücklingen]] (''Strukelje'' in Saterland Frisian), [[Saterland-Ramsloh|Ramsloh]] (''Roomelse''), [[Saterland-Sedelsberg|Sedelsberg]] (''Seeidelsbierich'') and [[Saterland-Scharrel|Scharrel]] (''Schäddel'') were combined.
