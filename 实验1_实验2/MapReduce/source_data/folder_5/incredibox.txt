 '''''Incredibox''''' is a [[music video game]], made and released by the French company So Far So Good. To play, users must [[drag and drop]] sound icons onto the  " polos " , or the characters, to make music. Players can use [[combo]]s to unlock animated bonuses and record their mixes for a ranking. < ref > {{Cite web|last=Joven|first=Juliette|date=2012-04-21|title=Ever heard of the incredible Incredibox?|url=https://www.rappler.com/life-and-style/ever-heard-of-the-incedible-incredibox|url-status=dead|archive-date=2021-01-22|access-date=|website=Rappler|language=en|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210122160658/https://www.rappler.com/life-and-style/ever-heard-of-the-incedible-incredibox}} < /ref >  There is an automatic mode that randomly places sounds icons. 
 
 == Gameplay == 
 ''Incredibox'' is an app where the player can use 7 [[Beatboxing|beatboxer]]s to make a mix. Once they pick one from the available 8 versions, they are brought to an [[interface]]. This interface shows 20 available sound icons, divided into 4 categories: [[Beat (music)|beat]], [[Sound effect|effects]], [[melodies]] and [[Voice (phonetics)|voices]]. Each icon is a unique [[A cappella|acappella]] [[Loop (music)|sound loop]] to [[drag and drop]] onto the polos.
