 '''Wanzwil''' was a [[municipality]] in [[Wangen (district)|Wangen]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Bern (canton)|Bern]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 On 1 January 2009, Wanzwil and [[Röthenbach bei Herzogenbuchsee]] became part of [[Heimenhausen]].
