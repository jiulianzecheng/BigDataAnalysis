 '''Dombresson''' was a [[municipality]] of the district [[Val-de-Ruz (district)|Val-de-Ruz]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Neuchâtel (canton)|Neuchâtel]] in [[Switzerland]]. On 1 January 2013, the former municipalities of [[Boudevilliers]], [[Cernier]], [[Chézard-Saint-Martin]], [[Coffrane]], Dombresson, [[Engollon]], [[Fenin-Vilars-Saules]], [[Fontainemelon]], [[Fontaines, Switzerland|Fontaines]], [[Les Geneveys-sur-Coffrane]], [[Les Hauts-Geneveys]], [[Montmollin]], [[Le Pâquier, Neuchâtel|Le Pâquier]], [[Savagnier]] and [[Villiers, Switzerland|Villiers]] joined together and became the new  municipality of [[Val-de-Ruz]]. < ref name=Fusion > [http://www.bfs.admin.ch/bfs/portal/de/index/infothek/nomenklaturen/blank/blank/gem_liste/03.html Amtliches Gemeindeverzeichnis der Schweiz] published by the Swiss Federal Statistical Office {{in lang|de}} accessed 2 January 2013 < /ref >
