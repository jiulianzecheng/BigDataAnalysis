 '''Dragash''' is a municipality in the Prizren district of [[Kosovo]]. Dragash has approximately 41,000 inhabitants, the majority are the [[Gorani]], a slavic language speaking [[Muslim]] group. The area is 435 km², the population density is 94 inhabitants per km².
