 '''Venlo''' is a [[List of municipalities of the Netherlands|municipality]] in [[Limburg (Netherlands)|Limburg]] in the very east of the [[Netherlands]]. It has about 100,000 [[inhabitant]]s and lives mainly on one-day-trip-tourism from neighbouring [[Germany]] and [[logistics]]. It used to be a city of the [[Hanseatic League]]. 
 
 Since 2010, [[Arcen en Velden]] became a part of the municipality of Venlo.
