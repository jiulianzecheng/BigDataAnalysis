 '''Sangerhausen''' is the capital of the [[Mansfeld-Südharz Rural District|Mansfeld-Südharz rural district]] in [[Germany]]. It is between [[Magdeburg]] and [[Erfurt]]. The river [[Gonna (Helme)|Gonna]] flows through it.
