 '''Unilever''' is a [[United Kingdom|British]]-[[Netherlands|Dutch]] multinational company. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://money.cnn.com/quote/profile/profile.html?symb=UL#:~:text=Unilever%20Plc%20is%20a%20multinational,personal%20care,%20and%20home%20care.|title=UL - Unilever PLC Company Profile - CNNMoney.com|website=money.cnn.com|access-date=2020-06-05}} < /ref >  It is one of the biggest producers of [[consumer goods]] worldwide.  In 2016, around 168,000 people worked for Unilever in 100 countries. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.unilever.com/about/who-we-are/about-Unilever/|title=About Unilever|website=Unilever global company website|language=en|access-date=2020-06-05|archive-date=2020-12-09|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20201209064951/https://www.unilever.com/about/who-we-are/about-Unilever/|url-status=dead}} < /ref >   Unilever's products can be found in about 190 countries. < ref > {{Cite web|date=2014-04-02|title=Operational highlights - At a glance {{!}} Sustainable living {{!}} Unilever Global|url=http://unilever.com/sustainable-living/ourapproach/ourbusinessataglance/|access-date=2021-05-28|website=web.archive.org|archive-date=2014-04-02|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140402110549/http://unilever.com/sustainable-living/ourapproach/ourbusinessataglance/|url-status=bot: unknown}} < /ref > 
 
 Unilever is among the top 5 [[food]] producers worldwide. Food is about 40% of the revenue of Unilever. [[Cleaning agent|Cleaning agents]], [[Personal care|beauty products]]  personal care products and Telecomunnications make up the remaining 70% of the revenue.
