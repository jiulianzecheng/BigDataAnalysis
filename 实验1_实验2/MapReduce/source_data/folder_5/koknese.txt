 '''Koknese''' ({{audio|Lv-Koknese.ogg|pronunciation}}) is a [[town]] in middle [[Latvia]]. It is in [[Aizkraukle District]]. As of 2022, Koknese had a [[population]] of 2,562 people. < ref > [https://vietvardi.lgia.gov.lv/vv/to_www_obj.objekts?p_id=1154 Latvian Geospatial Information Agency] < /ref >  Koknese is on rivers Pērse and [[Daugava]]. 
 
 Koknese was a [[castle]] and river [[harbor]] at [[Middle Ages]]. < ref > Little Latvian Encyclopedia, 1967 < /ref >  It has [[town rights]] at 1277 until 17th century. Town rights were given again in 2021. 
 
 == Roads == 
 
 Roads from Koknese: [[Riga]]—[[Daugavpils]] (A6), Tīnūži—Koknese (P6), Ērgļi—Koknese (P79), [[Ogre, Latvia|Ogre]]—Koknese (V996), Suntaži—Koknese (V920). Koknese is [[railway]] station on line Riga—Daugavpils. 
 
 The centre of district [[Aizkraukle]] is 14 & nbsp;km to east from Koknese. The capital city [[Riga]] is 100 & nbsp;km to west.
