'''Charybdis''' is a sea monster in Ancient [[Greek mythology]]. It was said that she was a daughter of [[Poseidon]] and that when she breathed in and out the sea would rise and fall. She was also the sister of [[Scylla]].When angry she would turn into a whirlpool and suck in anything within half a mile
