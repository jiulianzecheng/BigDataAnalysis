 '''Cumbel''' was a [[municipality]] of the [[district]] of [[Surselva (district)|Surselva]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Graubünden]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 On 1 January 2013, the former municipalities of Cumbel, [[Degen, Switzerland|Degen]], [[Lumbrein]], [[Morissen]], [[Suraua]], [[Vignogn]], [[Vella]], and [[Vrin]] merged into the new municipality of [[Lumnezia]].
