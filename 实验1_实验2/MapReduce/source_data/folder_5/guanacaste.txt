 '''Guanacaste''' (''Enterolobium cyclocarpum'') also known as '''elephant-ear tree''' or '''devil's ear''' is a [[species]] of [[Flowering plant|flowering]] [[tree]] in the [[pea]] family, [[Fabaceae]]. It is native to [[Tropics|tropical]] regions of the [[Americas]]. It is the [[List of national trees|national tree]] of [[Costa Rica]].

 ==Description== 
 [[File:Frutos del carocaro.jpg|thumb|right|Fruits of guanacaste]] 
 The guanacaste is a large [[tree]], growing to {{Convert|20|–|30|m|ft|abbr=on }} tall, with a trunk up to {{Convert|3|m|ft|abbr=on }} in [[diameter]]. The [[bark]] is light gray. The crown (the upper part of tree) is broad and widely [[:wikt:spreading|spreading]]. [[Leaf|Leaves]] are {{convert|15|to|50|cm|in|abbr=on}} long, alternate and compound. < ref name= " conabio " > {{cite web|url=http://www.conabio.gob.mx/conocimiento/info_especies/arboles/doctos/41-legum16m.pdf |format=PDF |title=''Enterolobium cyclocarpum'' |publisher= Comisión Nacional para el Conocimiento y Uso de la Biodiversidad (CONABIO) |location=México |language=es |access-date=12 December 2016 }} < /ref > 
 
 The guanacaste is [[evergreen]], or briefly [[deciduous]] for 1–2 months during the dry season (without [[rain]]s). Most leaves fall in December, at the start of the dry season. In late February, leaves start to appear and produce a fresh, thick crown by April. 
 
 Flowers are [[hermaphrodite]]; the [[pollination]] is by insects like [[bee]]s. 
 
 The [[fruit]] is a [[legume]] (also known as a pod) with the shape of a human [[ear]]. 
 
 ===Where it grows=== 
 The guanacaste tree is [[Native (ecology)|native]] to [[Tropics|tropical]] [[America]]. It grows from southern [[Mexico]] through [[Central America]] to northern [[South America]]. It is found also in the [[West Indies]]. It has been introduced in other tropical regions. < ref name= " conabio "  / > 
 
 It grows normally at an [[elevation]] from [[sea level]] to {{Convert|500|m|ft|abbr=on }}, usually along the [[coast]] and [[rivers]]. < ref name= " conabio "  / > 
 
 ==Uses== 
 The [[wood]] is reddish-brown, lightweight and water-resistant; it is used to make objects like doors, windows, [[furniture]], [[cabinet]]s, and for [[shipbuilding]]. It is used also as [[firewood]].
