The '''Rifai''' [[Sufi]] order or ''Tariqah'' was established by Hazrat Sheikh [[Ahmed Ar-Rifai]] in [[Iraq]]. Later on, it spread to other parts of the world. It has a big presence in [[Turkey]] and [[Eastern Europe]] today. < ref > [http://www.rifai.org The Official Rifai Sufi Order site] < /ref >
