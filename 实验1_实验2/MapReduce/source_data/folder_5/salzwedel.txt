 '''Salzwedel''' (officially known as '''Hansestadt Salzwedel''') is a town in the [[Germany|German]] [[States of Germany|state]] of [[Saxony-Anhalt]]. It is the capital of the district of [[Altmarkkreis Salzwedel]]. 
 
 It has about 21,500 people.
