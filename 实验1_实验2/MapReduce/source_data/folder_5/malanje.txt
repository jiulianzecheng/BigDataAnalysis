 '''Malanje''' (also '''Malange''') is the capital city of [[Malanje Province]] in [[Angola]].  There are 222,000 people.  Nearby are the [[Calandula waterfalls]], {{convert|85|km|abbr=off}} from the city. Pedras Negras de [[Pungo Andongo]] are large black rocks. The greatest dam in the country is in Malanje.
