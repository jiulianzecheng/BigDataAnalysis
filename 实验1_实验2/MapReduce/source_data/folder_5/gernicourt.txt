'''Gernicourt''' is a former [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is found in the [[Regions of France|region]] [[Picardie]] in the [[Aisne]] [[Departments of France|department]] in the north of [[France]]. 
 
 Previously part of the [[Aisne]] department, it was merged on 1 January 2017 into the commune [[Cormicy]], in [[Marne]]. < ref > [https://www.legifrance.gouv.fr/eli/arrete/2016/12/31/INTB1700273A/jo/texte Arrêté préfectoral] 31 December 2016 {{in lang|fr}} < /ref >
