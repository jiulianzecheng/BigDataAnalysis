The '''Gungans''' are a [[fiction]]al race of creatures from the [[Star Wars]] universe. They are [[Amphibian|amphibious]] (frog-like) creatures that live on the [[planet]] [[Naboo]]. The most famous Gungan is a character named [[Jar Jar Binks]] from ''[[Star Wars Episode I: The Phantom Menace]]''.
