 '''Bobex''' is a European platform that was founded in [[Brussels]], Belgium in 1999. < ref > {{Cite web|last=Vanbrussel|first=Emmanuel|date=2022-08-01|title=Plateforme de cotation Bobex :  " La crise énergétique entraîne une ruée sur les panneaux solaires " |url=https://fr.businessam.be/plateforme-de-cotation-bobex-la-crise-energetique-entraine-une-ruee-sur-les-panneaux-solaires/|access-date=2023-05-04|website=Business AM - Infos économiques et financières|language=fr-BE}} < /ref >  It serves as a comparison platform for construction and renovation offers, including [[Solar panel|solar panels]], windows, insulation, and water softeners. 
 
 == Operation == 
 Bobex was established by two Arthur D. Little Inc. consultants, Jean-Louis Van Marcke and David Schnall. < ref > {{Cite web|last=Masquelier|first=Frédérique|title=Jean-Louis Van Marcke :  " Le souci dans notre métier, c’est que si on n’est pas dans le top 3 ou le top 5 d’un pays, on ne perce pas " |url=https://www.lalibre.be/economie/immo/2021/01/06/jean-louis-van-marcke-le-souci-dans-notre-metier-cest-que-si-on-nest-pas-dans-le-top-3-ou-le-top-5-dun-pays-on-ne-perce-pas-FQGSJHYFYZAIFNUCN7BKG6W3RE/|access-date=2023-05-04|website=La Libre.be|language=fr}} < /ref >  The platform started with an investment of €62,000 and initially only operated in the Belgian market. < ref > {{Cite web|last=Patrick|first=Van Campenhout|title=Bobex marie offre et demande|url=https://www.lalibre.be/archives-journal/2013/08/31/bobex-marie-offre-et-demande-LJNEPCO45ZDXJNHVCYVB4I5IYE/|access-date=2023-05-04|website=La Libre.be|language=fr}} < /ref >  In 2006, it expanded its operations to the Netherlands through a partnership with Dutch entrepreneurs. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Bobex.be -- Acheter et vendre malin|url=https://www.bobex.com/newsletter/special/bobex_buyer_special_8838_oct2006_FR.htm|access-date=2023-05-04|website=www.bobex.com}} < /ref >  Bobex also launched its UK operations in 2008 in cooperation with a partner. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Plus de 25.000 entreprises sur la place de marché Bobex|url=https://paperjam.lu/article/communique-plus-de-25000-entreprises-sur-la-place-de-marche-bobex|access-date=2023-05-04|website=paperjam.lu|language=fr}} < /ref >  In 2013, Bobex acquired its competitor, Offertex. < ref > {{Cite web|last=Tendances|first=Trends|date=2013-11-27|title=Offres de prix en ligne?
