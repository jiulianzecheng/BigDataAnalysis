 '''Satay''' is a local food of [[Indonesia]], [[Malaysia]], [[Singapore]] and the [[Philippines]]. It is usually made of meat on a stick with onion and gravy along with it. 
 
 The dish was invented by [[Java]]nese street vendors. < ref > [http://www.indonesia.travel/en/destination/361/jakarta-street-food-scene  " Jakarta Street Food Scene: Truly Indonesia " . Indonesia Travel.] < /ref >
