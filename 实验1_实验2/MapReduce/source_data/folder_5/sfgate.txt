'''SFGATE''' is an [[United States|American]] [[news]] [[website]]. It is based out of [[San Francisco|San Francisco, California]]. The website covers news, [[culture]], [[travel]], [[food]], [[politics]] and [[Sport|sports]] in the [[San Francisco Bay Area|Bay Area]], [[Hawaii]] and [[California]]. The site is owned by [[Hearst Communications|Hearst Newspapers]]. It has between 25 and 30 million readers a month.
