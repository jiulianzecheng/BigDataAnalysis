 '''Kherson''' ({{lang-uk|Херсо́н}}) is a city in southern [[Ukraine]]. It is the [[administrative centre]] of [[Kherson Oblast]]. In 2020, 286,958 people lived there. 
 
 The city is an important port on the [[Black Sea]] and on the [[Dnieper River]].
