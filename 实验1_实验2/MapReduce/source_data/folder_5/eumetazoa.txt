 '''Eumetazoa''' is a [[clade]] which includes all major [[animal]] [[phyla]] except [[sponge]]s, and a few other groups of animals, such as the [[Placozoa]]. The Eumetazoa have true [[tissues]], [[neurons]], and an embryo that goes through a [[gastrula]] stage.
