'''Barbadian''', often shortened to '''Bajan''', may be anything of or relating to [[Barbados]] and may also refer directly to: 
 
 * [[Barbadian football]] 
 * [[Barbadian cuisine]] 
 * [[Barbadian people]], people of Barbadian descent. For information about the Barbadian people, see [[Demographics of Barbados]] and [[Culture of Barbados]]. For specific persons, see [[List of Barbadians]]. 
 * [[Barbadian English]] language 
 * [[Bartholomew Roberts|A Barbadian's Head and A Martiniquian's Head Flag]], a flag by a pirate Bartholomew Roberts, who vowed revenge towards Barbados and Martinique.
