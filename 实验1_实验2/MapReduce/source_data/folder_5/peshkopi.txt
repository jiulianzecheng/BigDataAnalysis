 '''Peshkopi''' is a city in [[Dibër District]], [[Dibër County]], northeastern [[Albania]].  It is the capital of both. 
 
 It is {{convert|187|km}} away from [[Tirana]], the capital of [[Albania]], and {{convert|20|km}} from [[North Macedonia]]. About 14,100 people live here. 
 
 Peshkopi is east of the [[Black Drin|Black Drin river]]. 
 
 There is a branch of [[Aleksandër Moisiu University]].
