 '''''Shigella''''' is a [[genus]] of [[bacterium]]. It belongs to a small group of [[pathogen]]s (including ''[[Escherichia coli|E. coli]]'' and ''[[Salmonella]]'') that can infect the gut after the ingestion of relatively few [[organism]]s. The bacteria can cause sudden and severe [[diarrhea]] in humans.
