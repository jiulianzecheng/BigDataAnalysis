 A '''vest''' is a kind of [[clothing]]. It is worn on the upper part of the body. It has no [[sleeve]]s. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/vest|title=Definition of VEST|website=www.merriam-webster.com}} < /ref > 
 
 In the [[United States]] and [[American English]], a vest is a kind of clothing sometimes worn on top of a [[shirt]]. < ref > {{cite web | url=https://oureverydaylife.com/types-mens-vests-22126.html | title=Types of Men's Vests }} < /ref > 
 
 In [[Britain]] and [[British English]], a vest is a kind of clothing worn over the [[chest]] and below all other clothes on that part of the body.
