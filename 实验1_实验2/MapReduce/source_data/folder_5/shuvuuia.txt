 '''''Shuvuuia''''' is a genus of [[bird]]-like [[theropod]] [[dinosaur]] from the later [[Cretaceous]] of [[Mongolia]].  
 
 It is a member of the family [[Alvarezsauridae]], small [[coelurosauria]]n dinosaurs which have short but powerful forelimbs specialized for digging.  
 
 The name ''Shuvuuia'' is derived from the [[Mongolian language|Mongolian]] word ''shuvuu'' meaning  " bird " .
