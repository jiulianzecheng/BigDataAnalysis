 A '''coat''' is a piece of [[clothing]] that is worn over a person's upper [[body]]. It can be used to keep [[warm]] or [[:wikt:dry|dry]] (a raincoat). It also may be worn to make a person look good. A coat usually has long sleeves, and is fastened at the front. Sometimes it includes [[collar|collars]], shoulder straps and hoods. One type of coat is a [[jacket]]. < ref > https://www.macmillandictionary.com/us/thesaurus-category/american/jackets-and-coats < /ref >
