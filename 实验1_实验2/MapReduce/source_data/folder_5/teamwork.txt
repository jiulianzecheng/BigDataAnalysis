'''Teamwork''' is when many  people work together. They work together to reach a [[wikt:goal|goal]]. Teamwork helps people to understand others, build friendly friendships, and to get any job done the right way. In [[health care]], teamwork is often [[practice]]d to help [[patient]]s at the [[hospital]]. All team members are given a job to do.
