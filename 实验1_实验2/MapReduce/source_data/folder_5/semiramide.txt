 '''''Semiramide''''' is an Italian [[opera]] written by [[Gioachino Rossini]]. It has two [[Act (drama)|act]]s. It was first performed at the [[Teatro La Fenice]] in [[Venice]], on February 3, 1823, [[libretto]] by Gaetano Rossi. This was after [[Voltaire]]'s ''Semiramis''. This opera was successful from the start and is still performed today. 
 
 ==Principal roles== 
 * Semiramide - [[soprano]] 
 * Arsace - [[mezzo-soprano]] 
 * Assur - [[bass (vocal range)|bass]] 
 * Idreno - [[tenor]] 
 * Oroe - [[bass (vocal range)|bass]] 
 * Azema - [[soprano]]
