 '''DWLS''' (97.1 FM), branded as '''Barangay LS 97.1''', is a 24/7 Top 40 (CHR)/OPM 
 /[[K-Pop]] FM radio station. It is owned and operated by RGMA (Radio GMA) in the [[Philippines]]. It is an affliate station of GMA Network, Inc. The studio is in  GMA Network Center Complex, EDSA corner, Timog Avenue, Diliman, Quezon City. Its transmitter is on the GMA Tower of Power Barangay Culiat, Tandang Sora, Quezon City, Philippines (soon to be moving located at Sumulong Highway, Barangay San Roque, [[Antipolo City]]).
