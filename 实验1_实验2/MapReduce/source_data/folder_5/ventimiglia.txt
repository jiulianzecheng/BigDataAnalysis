 '''Ventimiglia''' ({{lang-fr|Vintimille}}; {{lang-lij|Ventemiglia}}) is a city in northern [[Italy]]. Ventimiglia is in the [[Liguria]] Region and has about 24,000 inhabitants. It is on the [[Italian Riviera]] and borders [[France]].
