 '''Gomel''' (also '''Homiel''', '''Homel''') is the administrative center of [[Gomel Voblast]].  It is the second-largest city in [[Belarus]]. It has a population of 482,652 (2009 census). Gomel is in the southeastern part of the country, on the right bank of [[Sozh]] river.  It is {{convert|302|km|0|abbr=off}} to the South-East from [[Minsk]], {{convert|534|km|0|abbr=off}} to the East from [[Brest, Belarus|Brest]], {{convert|171|km|0|abbr=off}} to the South from [[Mogilev]], {{convert|237|km|0|abbr=off}} to the West from [[Bryansk]] and {{convert|111|km|0|abbr=off}} to the North from [[Chernihiv]]. The city's area is 121 & nbsp;km < sup > 2 < /sup > .
