 '''MediaFire''' is a free and unlimited file and image [[one-click hosting|hosting website]] in Harris County, [[Texas]], [[USA]]. Every feature of the service is available to users whether they choose to create a cost-free account or not. 
 
 The website is very popular and has attracted at least 8.7 million visitors annually by 2008 according to a study made by [[Compete.com]]. < ref > {{Cite web |url=http://siteanalytics.compete.com/mediafire.com?metric=uv |title=Mediafire attracts almost 9m visitors online yearly |access-date=2008-07-22 |archive-date=2009-12-27 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20091227062617/http://siteanalytics.compete.com/mediafire.com/?metric=uv |url-status=dead }} < /ref >

 == Usage == 
 The service is available for free and allows users to [[upload]] files of up to 200 & nbsp;[[Megabyte|MB]]. The user is then supplied with a unique [[URL]], which locates the file and enables anyone with whom the uploader shares it to [[download]] the file. Currently there is no time limit on how long uploaded files will be stored. < ref > [http://support.mediafire.com/index.php?_m=knowledgebase & _a=viewarticle & kbarticleid=2 & nav=0 Mediafire FAQ] < /ref >  Furthermore, images can be uploaded and displayed in galleries. 
 
 ==History== 
 MediaFire was originally founded on August 2006, the company was established in the San Frasisco Bay Area by a group of website enthusiasts and entrepreneurs who had a difficult time with sending rather large emails and files and [[FTP]]. < ref name= " mediafire.com " / > 
 
 Together they decided to create a site that would resolve those issues, for a more simple method of transferring these files and other media.On October 2006 MediaFire was actually launched, and did rather well with producing a site where user of all ages could share files with ease and feel secure about their files. And this site only continued to grow, and more features were invented such as: < ref name= " mediafire.com " / > 
 
 Unlimited cloud Storage, Branding tools and the ability to share files with friends and family and other users.
