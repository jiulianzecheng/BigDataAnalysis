 '''Lipki''' ({{lang-ru|Липки}}, 53°57'N 37°42'E) is a town in [[Tula Oblast]], [[Russia]]. Its population is about 9,700 ([[2005]]).  Lipki is located 38 & nbsp;km south of [[Tula, Russia|Tula]].  The village of Lipki has been known since the 17th century.
