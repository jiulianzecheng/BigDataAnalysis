 '''Paleolibertarianism''' is a [[Ideology|political ideology]] designed by [[Murray Rothbard]] and [[Llewelyn Rockwell]] that synthesizes [[paleoconservatism]] and [[libertarianism]]. It has since evolved into a large [[Politics|political]] movement within the [[Libertarian Party (United States)|Libertarian Party]] that has become the basis for the [[Mises Caucus]]. 
 
 Rothbard used paleolibertarianism to justify supporting the [[David Duke]] when he ran for [[Governor of Louisiana]] in the [[1991 Louisiana gubernatorial election]]. < ref > {{Cite journal|last=Rothbard|first=Murray|date=January 1992|title=Right-Wing Populism: A Strategy for the Paleo Movement|url=https://www.rothbard.it/articles/right-wing-populism.pdf|journal=RRR}} < /ref >
