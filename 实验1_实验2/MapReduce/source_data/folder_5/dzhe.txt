 '''Dzhe''' (Џ, џ) is a letter of [[Vuk Karadžić]]'s [[Cyrillic alphabet]], used in [[Serbian language|Serbo-Croatian]] and [[Macedonian language|Macedonian]] to present a Voice for need to tell {{IPA|/ʤ/}} (like the ''J'' in English  " '''j'''ump " ).
