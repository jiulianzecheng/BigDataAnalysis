 '''Interlaken''' is a town and [[municipality]] in the [[administrative district]] of [[Interlaken-Oberhasli (administrative district)|Interlaken-Oberhasli]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Bern (canton)|Bern]] in [[Switzerland]]. It is an important and well-known tourist destination in the [[Bernese Oberland]] region of the [[Swiss Alps]], and the main transport gateway to the mountains and lakes of that region. In 2018, 5,610 people lived there. 
 
 The town is on the [[Bödeli]] between two lakes [[Lake Brienz|Brienz]] and [[Lake Thun|Thun]].
