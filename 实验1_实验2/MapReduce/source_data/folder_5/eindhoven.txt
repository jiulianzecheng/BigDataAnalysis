 '''Eindhoven''' is a [[city]] in the south of the [[Netherlands]]. It is in the [[North Brabant|Noord-Brabant]] [[Provinces of the Netherlands|province]]. It has about 236,000 [[inhabitant]]s (2021) and a well-known [[Association football|football]] team called [[P.S.V. Eindhoven]].
