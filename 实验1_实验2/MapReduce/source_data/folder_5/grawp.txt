'''Grawp''' is a [[fictional character]] in the [[Harry Potter]] [[book]] series written by [[J. K. Rowling]]. He is the [[giant]] half-brother of [[Hagrid]]. Grawp and Hagrid were born of the same mother, the [[giant]]ess Fridwulfa. Hagrid's father was a [[wizard]] but Grawp's father was a giant. Grawp refers to Hagrid by the name  " Hagger "  and Hermione as  " Hermy " . He was said to be rather small compared to the other giants. He seemed to likes pink, the reason he was provoked when he saw Hermione's pink shirt. He participated in the Battle of Hogwarts and survived.
