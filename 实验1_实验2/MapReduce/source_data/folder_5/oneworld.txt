 '''Oneworld''' (marketed as '''one'''world) is an [[airline alliance]]. It was founded in 1999. As of October 2017, Oneworld is the third-largest global alliance. It carried more than 527.9 million passengers, behind [[SkyTeam]] (630 million) < ref > {{Cite web|url=http://www.skyteam.com/en/about/|title=SkyTeam Alliance {{!}} About Us {{!}} SkyTeam|website=www.skyteam.com|language=en|access-date=2018-07-22|archive-date=2016-08-06|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160806162848/http://www.skyteam.com/en/about/|url-status=dead}} < /ref >  and [[Star Alliance]] (762 million). < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.staralliance.com/documents/20184/680657/Star+Alliance+Facts+and+Figures.pdf/39a88cb5-ae7c-6a52-f67f-ddae83e4a43a?t=1568388329319|title=Star Alliance Facts and Figures|website=portal.staralliance.com}} < /ref >  Its [[headquarters]] are in [[New York City]], [[United States]]. Its slogan is, '' " An alliance of the world's leading airlines working as one. " ''
