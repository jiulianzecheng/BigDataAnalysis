 '''Europium''' is a [[chemical element]]. It has the symbol '''Eu''' on the [[periodic table]]. It has an atomic number 63 which means it has 63 [[proton]]s in one [[atom]]. It was named after the [[continent]] [[Europe]]. Its compounds can fluoresce, or glow under [[ultraviolet]] light.It costs $6700/KG. In the periodic table it stands next to samarium and gadolinium. 
 
 Color: Silvery White 
 Melting point: 826 degrees Celsius 
 Discovery Date: 1901
