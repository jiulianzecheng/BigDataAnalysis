 '''Rastede'''  ([[Low German]]: ''Raastäe/Raas'') is a municipality in [[Ammerland]], in [[Lower Saxony]], [[Germany]]. It is about 12 & nbsp;km north of [[Oldenburg (city)|Oldenburg]]. It is the site of the [[Schloss Rastede]]. 
 
 The [[Rastede railway station]] is at the [[Oldenburg–Wilhelmshaven railway]].
