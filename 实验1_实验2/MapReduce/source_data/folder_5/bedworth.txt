 '''Bedworth'''  is a small [[market]] [[town]] in [[Warwickshire]], [[England]]. It is {{convert|163|km|mi}} northwest of [[London]], {{convert|31|km|mi}} east of [[Birmingham]], and {{convert|24|km|mi}} north of the [[county town]] of [[Warwick]]. The town is home to many [[tourism|sights]] including The Bedworth Water Tower and The Nicholas Chamberlaine Almshouses. It was also known for [[mining]], which is not done anymore. They also are known for the vast amount of [[Charity Shops]] which are located in most parts of the town. 
 
 The town is [[Pronunciation|pronounced]]  " Beduf "  by the people that live there.
