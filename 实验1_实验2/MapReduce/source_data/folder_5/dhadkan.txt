 '''''Dhadkan''''' ({{lang-en|Heartbeat}}) is a [[Bollywood]] romantic drama movie directed by Dharmesh Darshan. The movie has [[Akshay Kumar]], [[Shilpa Shetty]], [[Sunil Shetty]] and [[Mahima Chaudhry]] in the main roles. The movie's music was done by Himanshu  Jaykar  &  [[Nadeem-Shravan]].

 ==Soundtrack== 
 The music of the movie went to become very popular of that year. It was also the second most sold Bollywood music album.
