The word ''naib'' (نائب) is an [[Arabic language|Arabic]] word meaning someone who is second in command. 
 
 It may also mean: 
 
 ==In history== 
 *'''Naib''', the name given to the rulers of [[Massawa]], Africa who served the [[Ottoman Empire]] from the sixteenth century to the nineteenth century. 
 *'''Naib''', a local leader in [[Dagestan]]. 
 
 ==Fiction== 
 *'''Naib''', the title given to the leader of Fremen (freemen)  on the planet Arrakis in the [[science fiction]] [[Dune universe]] written by Frank Herbert. In the story the word  " Naib "  means servant.
