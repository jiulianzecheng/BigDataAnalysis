{{Music of Pakistan}} 
 '''Qawwali''' ({{lang-ur|قوٌالی}}) is an [[Islamic]] [[Sufi]] type of music, that is famous in [[Pakistan]] and [[India]]. Mainly performed at Sufi [[Shrine]]s in Pakistan  and India, Qawwali has also become popular in the world. Singers like [[Nusrat Fateh Ali Khan]] are well known for this music due to their excellent voice and ability of singing. Qawwali music is believed to have started 700 years ago. This music is mainly based in [[Punjab (Pakistan)|Punjab]], which is a [[state]] of Pakistan and India.
