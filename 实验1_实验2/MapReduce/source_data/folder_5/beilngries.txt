 '''Beilngries''' ({{IPA-de|baɪlənˈɡʁiːs}}; {{lang-bar|Beilngrias}}) is a [[Town#Germany|town]] in the [[Eichstätt (district)|district of Eichstätt]], in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. It is on the river [[Altmühl]] and the [[Rhine-Main-Danube Canal]], 30 & nbsp;km north of [[Ingolstadt]].
