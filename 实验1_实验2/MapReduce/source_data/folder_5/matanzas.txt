 '''Matanzas''' (Cuban {{IPA-es|maËˆtansas}}) is the capital of the [[Cuba]]n province of [[Matanzas Province|Matanzas]]. It is located on the northern shore of the island of [[Cuba]], on the Bay of Matanzas (Spanish ''Bahia de Matanzas''). It is {{convert|90|km|mi}} east of the capital [[Havana]] and {{convert|32|km|mi}} west of the resort town of [[Varadero]]. 
 
 Matanzas is called the ''City of Bridges'', for the seventeen bridges that cross the three rivers. < ref > [http://www.matanzascity.org Ciudad de Matanzas] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200118172723/http://www.matanzascity.org/ |date=2020-01-18 }} - A website with much information in Spanish about the city of Matanzas, from its foundation to the present day {{in lang|es}} < /ref >
