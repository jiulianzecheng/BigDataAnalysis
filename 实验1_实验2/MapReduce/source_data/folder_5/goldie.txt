 '''''Goldie''''' is a 1931 American romantic comedy movie directed by [[Benjamin Stoloff]] and was a remake of the 1928 movie ''[[A Girl in Every Port (1928 movie)|A Girl in Every Port]]''. It stars [[Jean Harlow]], [[Spencer Tracy]], [[Warren Hymer]] and was distributed by [[20th Century Fox]].
