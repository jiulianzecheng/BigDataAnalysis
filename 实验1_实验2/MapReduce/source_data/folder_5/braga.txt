 '''Braga''' is the third-largest city in [[Portugal]]. In 2011, it has about 182,000 inhabitants. 
 
 Braga is known by the Portuguese as the religious centre of their country, < ref > {{cite web |last1=Braga guide |url= https://porto-north-portugal.com/braga-portugal-guide.html|title=Braga guide}} < /ref >  this is because Braga has the oldest cathedral in Portugal and many churches.
