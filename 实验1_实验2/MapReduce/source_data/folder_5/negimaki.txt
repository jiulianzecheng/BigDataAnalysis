 {{Nihongo|'''Negimaki'''|ねぎ巻き}}, also called '''negima''', is a [[Japanese cuisine|Japanese]] food consisting of broiled strips of beef soaked in [[teriyaki]] sauce and rolled with [[scallion]]s, also known as negi.  They are thought to have originated from the [[Kantō region]] of [[Japan]].
