 The '''bedroom''' is a room where people [[sleep]].  A bedroom mainly has a [[bed]] and [[drawer]]s or other storage for [[clothes]].  Many bedrooms also have a [[closet]] and [[nightstand]].  Some houses have more than one bedroom, and the biggest one is called a '''master bedroom'''.  A master bedroom may have an attached [[bathroom]] called an ensuite.
