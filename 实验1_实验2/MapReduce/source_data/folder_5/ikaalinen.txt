 '''Ikaalinen''' is a [[town]] in [[Pirkanmaa]], [[Finland]]. It had about 7,295 people living there in January 2014. The municipalities next to it are [[Hämeenkyrö]], [[Jämijärvi]], [[Kankaanpää]], [[Parkano]], [[Sastamala]] and [[Ylöjärvi]].
