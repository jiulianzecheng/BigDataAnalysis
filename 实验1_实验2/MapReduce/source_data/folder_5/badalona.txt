 '''Badalona''' ({{IPAc-en|ˌ|b|æ|d|ə|ˈ|l|oʊ|n|ə}}, {{IPA-ca|bəðəˈlonə}}) is a [[Municipalities of Spain|municipality]] to the immediate north east of [[Barcelona]] in [[Catalonia]], [[Spain]]. It is the third largest city in Catalonia and the [[Ranked lists of Spanish municipalities|twenty-third in Spain]]. It became a city in 1897.
