'''Villedieu''' is the name of several communes in France: 
 * [[Villedieu, Cantal]], in the Cantal department 
 * [[Villedieu, Côte-d'Or]], in the  Côte-d'Or department 
 * [[Villedieu, Vaucluse]], in the Vaucluse department 
 * [[Villedieu-la-Blouère]], in the Maine-et-Loire department 
 * [[Villedieu-le-Château]], in the Loir-et-Cher department 
 * [[Villedieu-lès-Bailleul]], in the Orne department 
 * [[Villedieu-les-Poêles]], in the Manche department 
 * [[Villedieu-sur-Indre]], in the Indre department 
 * [[Les Villedieu]], in the Doubs department 
 
 Villedieu may also refer to: 
 * [[Marie-Catherine de Villedieu]], a 17th-century French author.
