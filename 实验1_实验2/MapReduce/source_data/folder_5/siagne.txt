 The '''Siagne''' is a short [[river]] that flows through the [[Var (departement)|Var]] and [[Alpes-Maritimes]] [[Departments of France|departments]], in southeastern [[France]]. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 The Siagne river has a length of {{Convert|44.3|km|mi|0|abbr=on}} and a [[drainage basin]] with an area of approximately {{Convert|556|km2|sqmi|0|abbr=on }}. < ref name= " sandre " > {{cite web |url=http://services.sandre.eaufrance.fr/Courdo/Fiche/client/fiche_courdo.php?CdSandre=Y55-0400 |title=La Siagne (Y55-0400) |publisher=SANDRE - Portail national d'accès aux référentiels sur l'eau |language=fr |access-date=6 October 2016 }} < /ref > 
 
 Its average yearly [[Discharge (hydrology)|discharge]] ([[volume]] of [[water]] which passes through a section of the river per [[Unit of measurement|unit]] of [[time]]) is 8.75 [[cubic metre]]s per [[second]] at [[Pégomas]], near [[Cannes]]. 
 ===Course=== 
 The Siagne starts on the south side of the ''Audibergue'' [[mountain]], in the ''commune'' of [[Escragnolles]], [[Alpes-Maritimes]] department, at an [[elevation]] of about {{Convert|1367|m|ft|0|abbr=on}}. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://www.geoportail.gouv.fr/accueil?c=6.8347982670517915,43.72249637242762 & z=0.0001558914489275169 & l=GEOGRAPHICALGRIDSYSTEMS.MAPS.3D::GEOPORTAIL:OGC:WMTS==aggregate(1) & l=HYDROGRAPHY.HYDROGRAPHY::GEOPORTAIL:OGC:WMTS(1) & permalink=yes |title=Source du Siagne |publisher=Géoportail |language=fr |access-date=6 October 2016 }} < /ref >  It then flows to the southwest through the departments of [[Alpes-Maritimes]] and [[Var (departement)|Var]]. For much of its length, it forms the border between the two departments. 
 
 Finally, the river flows into the [[Mediterranean Sea]] at the city of [[Mandelieu-la-Napoule]]. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://www.geoportail.gouv.fr/accueil?c=6.945810562648847,43.530999179262494 & z=0.00003063381219121421 & l=GEOGRAPHICALGRIDSYSTEMS.MAPS.3D::GEOPORTAIL:OGC:WMTS==aggregate(1) & permalink=yes |title=Embouchure du Siagne |publisher=Géoportail |language=fr |access-date=6 October 2016 }} < /ref > 
 
 The Siagne flows through 2 departments and 13 ''communes'', in one region: < ref name= " sandre "  / > 
 * [[Alpes-Maritimes]] department: [[Escragnolles]], [[Saint-Vallier-de-Thiey]], [[Saint-Cézaire-sur-Siagne]], [[Le Tignet]], [[Peymeinade]], [[Mandelieu-la-Napoule]], [[Auribeau-sur-Siagne]], [[Pégomas]], [[La Roquette-sur-Siagne]] 
 * [[Var (departement)|Var]]: [[Mons, Var|Mons]], [[Callian]], [[Montauroux]], [[Tanneron]]
