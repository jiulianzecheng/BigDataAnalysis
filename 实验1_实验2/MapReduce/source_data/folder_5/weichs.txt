 '''Weichs''' is a [[municipality]] in [[Dachau (district)|Dachau]], a district ([[Districts of Germany|''Landkreis'']]) in [[Upper Bavaria]]. There are 4 main [[villages]] in Weichs - Weichs, Aufhausen, Ebersbach, and Fränking. There are several smaller villages: Albertshof, Biechlhof, Breitenwiesen, Daxberg, Edenholzhausen, Edenpfaffenhofen, Erlbach, Erlhausen, Hölzböck, Jedenhofen, and Zillhofen.
