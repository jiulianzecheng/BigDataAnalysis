 '''Kreminna''' ({{Lang-uk|Кремінна}}; {{Lang-ru|Кременная}}) is a city in the [[Sievierodonetsk Raion]] of the [[Luhansk Oblast]] of [[Ukraine]]. Before 2020, it was the administrative center of the former [[Kreminna Raion]].
