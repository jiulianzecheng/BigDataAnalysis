 '''Stiftelsen''' is a rock band from [[Sweden]]. The band was established in the year of 2010, and broke through in 2012 with the song  " Mitt hjärta slår " . 
 
 ==Members== 
 *Robert Pettersson (vocals) - lead singer of [[Takida]] 
 *Micke Eriksson (guitar) - formerly member of Swedish band [[Willy  &  The Hitchhikers]] 
 *Arne Johansson (bass) - also from Willy  &  The Hitchhikers 
 *Martin Källström (drums) - formerly from Swedish metal band [[Corroded (band)|Corroded]]
