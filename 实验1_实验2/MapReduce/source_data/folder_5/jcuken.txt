'''JCUKEN''' (''ЙЦУКЕН'') is a [[keyboard]] layout used for [[Cyrillic alphabet|Cyrillic language]]s. Before the [[Russian language]] was reformed in 1917, ''JIUKEN'' (''ЙІУКЕН'') was the keyboard used.
