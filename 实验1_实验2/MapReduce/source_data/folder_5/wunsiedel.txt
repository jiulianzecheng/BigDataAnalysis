 '''{{lang|de|Wunsiedel|italic=no}}''' ({{IPA-de|ˈvʊnˌziːdl̩|-|De-Wunsiedel.ogg}}) is the [[administrative centre|seat]] of the [[Upper Franconia]]n district of {{lang|de|[[Wunsiedel (district)|Wunsiedel]]|italic=no}} in northeast [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. 
 
 == Geography == 
 {{lang|de|Wunsiedel|italic=no}} is in the [[Fichtelgebirge Mountains]] in the valley of the {{lang|de|[[Röslau (river)|Röslau]]|italic=no}} at the foot of the {{lang|de|[[Kösseine]]|italic=no}} Plateau. 
 
 === Incorporated villages === 
 The town's borough has the following villages (year of incorporation in brackets): 
 
 {{lang|de|Bernstein (1978), Breitenbrunn, Furthammer, Göpfersgrün, Göringsreuth, Hauenreuth, Hildenbach (1978), Hildenmühle, [[Holenbrunn]] (1978), Johanneszeche, Juliushammer, Klause, Kösseinehaus, Krohenhammer, Luisenburg, Schneckenhammer, Schneckenmühle, [[Schönbrunn (Fichtelgebirge)|Schönbrunn]] (1975), Sinatengrün, Stemmasgrün, Stollenmühle, Valetsberg, Walkmühle, Wiesenmühle, Wintersberg, Wintersreuth|italic=no}}.
