 '''Lockton Companies Inc.''' is the world's biggest privately held [[insurance]] brokerage company, providing insurance, [[risk management]], [[employee benefits]] and retirement services. In addition to its [[Kansas City, Missouri]] headquarters, Lockton operates more than 100+ locations in 125 countries. Lockton currently has more than 8,300 employees worldwide. < ref > {{Cite web|last=Dornbrook|first=James|date=|title=Lockton names former NPC International exec as its new CFO|url=https://www.bizjournals.com/kansascity/news/2020/08/31/lockton-companies-cfo-troy-cook.html|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=2020-09-01|website=www.bizjournals.com}} < /ref >
