 }}'''''Thrillville''''' is a [[PlayStation 2]], [[Xbox]] and [[PlayStation Portable]] [[video game]]. It was made by [[LucasArts]] and [[Frontier Developments]]. Players make their own [[theme park]]. They make [[roller coaster]]s, game stalls, rides, [[Race track|race tracks]] and [[food]] and [[drink]] shops.  
 
 
 == Story == 
 
 Uncle Mortimer, a strange [[scientist]], puts the player in charge of Thrillville. It is a large theme park with roller coasters, games, rides, and racetracks. The player then picks a [[Fictional character|character]]. The character can be moved through each theme park. He must try to finish missions in the theme park before he can move on to the next one. 
 
 Garrison, another theme park owner, has his own theme park called Globo-joy. Globo-joy competes against Thrillville. The player must do difficult tasks to make Thrillville the best theme park. 
 
 
 == Minigames == 
 
 Thrillville has many [[Minigame|minigames]] that can be played in the theme park, or with up to four other players. 
 
 * Entertainer: Players must hit the right button at the right time to make the [[Cheerleader|cheerleaders]] dance to the music 
 * RC wars: Players must drive remote-control cars into each other, to try to make the other car [[Explosion|explode]]. 
 * Event Horizon: Players can pick between four [[Spacecraft|space ships]]. Each ship has a different [[weapon]]. The goal is to destroy the other ships. 
 * Robot Invasion, Hauntings, Pirate Raiders, and Wild Frontier: Players move around in a battle arena. They can shoot either the other players or [[robot]] enemies. 
 * Star Sniper, Ghoul Gunner, Blackbeard's Booty, and Prospect Stakeout: Players shoot targets that pop out of certain areas. 
 * Dune Buggy, Anti-Grav Racer, Go-karts, Monster Truck, and Formula Thrills: Players race to the finish on a race track. 
 * Trojan Quest: Players can pick between four heroes. Playes must kill [[Skeleton|skeletons]] and evil monsters. 
 * Luftwaffe 109: Players can pick between four [[Fixed-wing aircraft|airplanes]]. They must shoot down enemy airplanes. 
 * Trampolines: Players bounce on a [[trampoline]], doing tricks in the air. 
 * Saucer Sumo: Players control a [[Unidentified flying object|flying saucer]] and try to bump the other players off the edge of the ring. 
 * Saucer Soccer: Players control a flying car and try to get the [[Association football|soccer ball]] and shoot it into the goal. 
 * Minigolf: Players play a game of [[miniature golf]]. 
 * Sparkle Island: Players play as a [[cat]]-like hero and shoot blobs. 
 * Mechanic: Players arrange tubes to fit on a board. They have to connect one part of the board to another. 
 * Groundskeeper: Players suck up the trash and wash the [[vomit]] that has collected in the theme parks. 
 
 ==Thrillville: Off the Rails== 
 Thrillville: Off the Rails was the second game of the series. It came out in 2007 for the [[PlayStation 2]], [[Wii]], [[Xbox 360]], [[Nintendo DS]], [[PlayStation Portable]] and [[Microsoft Windows]].  " Off the Rails "  allows roller coasters to crash. It also added more minigames, characters with conversation skills amd more options. Off the Rails also added many songs including   " [[Parklife]] "  by [[Blur]] and  " [[Smile (Lily Allen song)]] "  by [[Lily Allen]].
