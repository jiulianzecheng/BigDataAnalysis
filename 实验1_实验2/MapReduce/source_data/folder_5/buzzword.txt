A '''buzzword''' is a [[word]] or [[phrase]] that is made to either [[Summary|summarize]] a complex idea or reconstruct another word or phrase to reflect a particular [[point of view]]. [[Corporation]]s make up buzzwords so that they can [[Advertising|advertise]] stuff better. In this way, a buzzword is a lot like a [[Brand|product name]] or a [[proper noun]]. People can remember buzzwords better than non-buzzwords, which is why its good for advertising. 
 < br / > 
 Example: 
 [[Multimedia]] has been a buzzword in the computer industry for years.
