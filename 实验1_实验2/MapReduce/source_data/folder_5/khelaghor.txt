 '''''Khelaghor''''' is an 2020 Indian [[Bengali language|Bengali]] language Romantic Thriller Drama television series that premiered on 30 November 2020 on Bengali Television Entertainment Channel [[Star Jalsha]] and it also available on the digital platform [[Disney+ Hotstar]].
