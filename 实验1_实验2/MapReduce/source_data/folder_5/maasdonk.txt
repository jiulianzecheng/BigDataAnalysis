 '''Maasdonk''' is a former [[List of municipalities of the Netherlands|municipality]] in the [[Netherlands|Dutch]] [[Provinces of the Netherlands|province]] of [[North Brabant]]. In 2015, it has become a part of the existing municipalities of [['s-Hertogenbosch]] and [[Oss]].
