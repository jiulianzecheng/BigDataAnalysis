 '''Cadolzburg''' (outdated also ''Kadolzburg'', colloquially pronounced  " Kalschbuʳch "  or  " Sporch "  {{IPA-de|ʃbɔrχ}}) is a municipality in the Middle Franconian [[Fürth (district)|district of Fürth]], in [[Bavaria]], Germany. It is {{convert|11|km|abbr=on}} west of [[Fürth]]. Its name comes from its central castle, first being mentioned in the year 1157. < ref name= " :0 " > {{Cite book|title=W.-A. v. Reitzenstein|last=Wiessner|first=W.}} < /ref >
