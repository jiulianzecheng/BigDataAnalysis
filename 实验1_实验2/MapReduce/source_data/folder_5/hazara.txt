 The '''Hazara''' division is an [[administration|administrative]] area of the present [[Khyber Pakhtunkhwa]] province of [[Pakistan]]. It includes the [[Haripur District]], [[Abbottabad District]], [[Mansehra District]], [[Battagram District]], [[Kohistan District, Pakistan|Kohistan District]] and the [[Kala Dhaka]] former [[tribe|tribal]] [[enclave]], now renamed as the [[Tor Ghar District]]. The native language spoken in the area is [[Hindko]].
