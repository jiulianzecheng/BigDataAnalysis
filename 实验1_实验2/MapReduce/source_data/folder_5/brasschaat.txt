 '''Brasschaat''' is a [[municipality]] in the [[Belgium|Belgian]] [[province]] of [[Antwerp (province)|Antwerp]]. 
 
 In 2007, 37133 people lived there. < ref > {{Cite web |url=http://statbel.fgov.be/downloads/pop200701com.xls |title=Werkelijke bevolking per gemeente op 1 januari 2007 / Population de droit par commune au 1 janvier 2007 (.xls) |access-date=2008-07-15 |archive-date=2008-11-23 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20081123144002/http://statbel.fgov.be/downloads/pop200701com.xls |url-status=dead }} < /ref > 
 
 It is at 51° 17 North, 04° 29 East. < ref > {{Cite web |url=http://statbel.fgov.be/pub/d1/p120_nl.xls |title=Geografische coördinaten van de gemeenten (.xls) |access-date=2008-07-15 |archive-date=2004-07-23 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20040723190930/http://statbel.fgov.be/pub/d1/p120_nl.xls |url-status=dead }} < /ref > 
 
 There has always a big military force in Brasschaat. After [[World War II]] there were more military-related things. Including the defence buildings made to stop the [[Nazi]]'s from reaching The port of Antwerp. Now most of them are closed or are used to train the [[soldier]]s.  
 
 Brasschaat is called green because of the many parks and woods, such as the [[Peerdsbos]], the [[parc of Brasschaat]], [[De uitlegger]], and [[De instlag]].
