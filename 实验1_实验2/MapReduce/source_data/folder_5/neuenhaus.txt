 '''Neuenhaus''' is a town in the [[County of Bentheim (district)|County of Bentheim]] in [[Lower Saxony]], and is the seat of a like-named collective municipality [[Neuenhaus (Samtgemeinde)|Neuenhaus]]. Neuenhaus is on the rivers [[Dinkel]] and [[Vechte]] near the border with the [[Netherlands]] and is about 10 & nbsp;km northwest of [[Nordhorn]], and 30 & nbsp;km north of [[Enschede]].
