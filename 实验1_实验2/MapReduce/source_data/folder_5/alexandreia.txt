 '''Alexandreia''', or '''Alexandria''', is a historical [[Greece|Greek]] [[town]] in the [[Imathia Prefecture]] in the region of [[Macedonia (Greece)|Macedonia]]. There were 19,283 [[people]] living there according to the [[2001]] [[census]].
