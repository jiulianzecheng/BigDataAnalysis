 '''Marmande''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]] in southwestern [[France]], in the [[Regions of France|region]] of [[Nouvelle-Aquitaine]]. It is a [[Prefectures in France|sub-prefecture]] of the [[Lot-et-Garonne]] [[Departments of France|department]], [[Capital city|capital]] of the ''[[Arrondissements of France|arrondissement]]'' of [[Arrondissement of Marmande|Marmande]]. It is also the capital of 2 [[Cantons in France|''cantons'']] in the department: [[Canton of Marmande-1|Marmande-1]] and [[Canton of Marmande-2|Marmande-2]]. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 Marmande is on the right side of the [[Garonne river]], at about {{Convert|64|km|mi|0|abbr=on }} to the northwest of [[Agen]] and {{Convert|71|km|mi|0|abbr=on }} to the southwest of [[Bordeaux]]. 
 
 It has an area of {{Convert|36.88|km2|sqmi|abbr=on }} and its average [[altitude]] is {{Convert|72|m|ft|abbr=on}}; at the [[City and town halls|city hall]], the altitude is {{Convert|30|m|ft|abbr=on }}. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://www.map-france.com/Marmande-47200/ |title=City of Marmande |publisher=Map-France.com |language= |accessdate=21 March 2015 |date= }} < /ref >

 ===Climate=== 
 The climate of Marmande is an [[oceanic climate]] ([[Köppen climate classification]] '''Cfb'''), with mild winters and warm summers. < ref name= " annuaire " > {{cite web |work=Annuaire-Mairie.fr |publisher= |title=Ville de Marmande |url=http://www.annuaire-mairie.fr/ville-marmande.html |language=French |accessdate=25 April 2017}} < /ref > 
 
 ==Population== 
 The inhabitants of Marmande are known, in [[French language|French]], as ''Marmandais'' (women: ''Marmandaises''). < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.habitants.fr/habitants_marmande_47157.html |title=Marmande (47180) |publisher=habitants.fr |language=French |accessdate=21 March 2015 |date= }} < /ref > 
 
 With a population of 17,748, < ref name=insee > {{cite web |url=http://www.insee.fr/fr/statistiques/fichier/2525755/dep47.pdf |title=Régions, départements, arrondissements, cantons et communes  |work= Populations légales 2014 |publisher=Institut national de la statistique et des études économiques - INSEE |format=PDF |language=French |accessdate=25 April 2017|date= }} < /ref >  Marmande has a [[population density]] of {{Pop density|17748|45.06|km2|prec=0|disp=num}} inhabitants/km < sup > 2 < /sup > .

 ==Administration== 
 Marmande is a [[Prefectures in France|subprefecture]] of the [[Lot-et-Garonne]] department since 1790. It is also the capital of the ''arrondissement'' of [[Arrondissement of Marmande|Marmande]] and the [[administrative centre]] ({{lang-fr|chef-lieu}}) of 2 [[Cantons of France|cantons]]: < ref name=insee1 > {{cite web|url=https://www.insee.fr/fr/metadonnees/cog/commune/COM47157-marmande |title=Commune de Marmande (47157)  |work= Géographie administrative et d’étude |publisher=Institut national de la statistique et des études économiques - INSEE |language=French |accessdate=25 April 2017 |date= }} < /ref > 
 # [[Canton of Marmande-1|Marmande-1]], formed with a part of Marmande and nine other ''communes'', with 18,503 inhabitants  < small > (2014) < /small > . 
 # [[Canton of Marmande-2|Marmande-2]], formed with a part of Marmande and ten other ''communes'', with 17,833 inhabitants  < small > (2014) < /small > . 
 
 It is part of the [[Communes of France|intercommunality]] ''Val de Garonne'' ({{lang-fr|Val de Garonne Agglomération}}).
