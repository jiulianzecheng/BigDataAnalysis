 '''Hepatoblastoma''' is a rare form of [[Liver cancer]] occurring most frequently in children. Treatment consists of [[Surgery]] and [[Chemotherapy]]. < ref name= " emedicine.medscape.com " > {{cite web | url=http://emedicine.medscape.com/article/986802-overview | title=Pediatric Hepatoblastoma: Practice Essentials, Background, Pathophysiology | date=9 December 2021 }} < /ref >  If all of the cancer is removed during surgery, the prognosis is excellent, but if cancer is left behind, the prognosis is much worse. < ref name= " emedicine.medscape.com " / >
