 '''Umbayee''' (ഉമ്പായി; 1950 – 1 August 2018) was an Indian folk musician and composer. He worked in the [[Ghazal]] genre. < ref > {{cite news |url=http://www.hindu.com/2010/09/16/stories/2010091651990200.htm |title=When politicians played muse |publisher=The Hindu |date=2010-09-16 |access-date=2013-12-09 |archive-date=2010-09-21 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20100921013902/http://www.hindu.com/2010/09/16/stories/2010091651990200.htm |url-status=dead }} < /ref >  Umbayee was born in [[Mattancherry]], [[Kerala]]. Umbayee was known for his unique style of singing. < ref > [http://www.thehindu.com/todays-paper/tp-features/tp-metroplus/ghazals-and-grit/article3924501.ece  " ...GHAZALS and GRIT " ], ''The Hindu'', 22 September 2012. Accessed 8 December 2013 < /ref  >  He was known for his songs:  " [[Hrudayaraagam]] " ,  " [[Mehboob]] " , and  " [[Gazalmaala]] " . 
 
 Umbayee died on 1 August 2018 in [[Aluva]], [[Kerala]] from [[stomach cancer]] at the age of 66. < ref > [http://www.thequint.com/hotwire-text/malayalam-ghazal-maestro-umbayee-passes-away Malayalam Ghazal maestro Umbayee passes away] < /ref >
