 The '''piccolo''' is a small [[flute]] that plays one [[octave]] higher than the normal flute.  It sounds an [[octave]] higher than written.  It is the highest [[musical instrument|instrument]] in the [[orchestra]]. Sometimes it sounds like a birdcall. The name 'piccolo' comes from the [[Italy|Italian]] word meaning 'small'.
