 '''Honkajoki''' is a former municipality in [[Satakunta]], [[Finland]]. As of January [[2014]], more than 1,800 people lived there. Neighbouring municipalities are [[Isojoki]], [[Kankaanpää]], [[Karvia]], [[Kauhajoki]] and [[Siikainen]]. The most notable water system is Karvianjoki. There are no [[lake]]s in the municipality. There are many untouched [[swamp]]s. 
 
 Honkajoki was established in 1867. It was merged with the town of Kankaanpää on 1 January 2021.
