 '''''Hippopotamidae''''' is a group of [[even-toed ungulate]] [[mammal]]s. They form the [[family (biology)|family]] '''Hippopotamids'''. There are two living [[species]] of Hippopotamuses. 
 [[Image:Hippopotamus.jpg|thumb|A Hippopotamus]] 
 ==Taxonomy== 
 * Family ''Hippopotamidae'' 
 ** [[Hippopotamus]] (''Hippopotamus amphibius'') 
 ** [[Pygmy Hippopotamus]] (''Hexaprotodon liberiensis'' or ''Choeropsis liberiensis'')
