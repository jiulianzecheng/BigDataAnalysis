'''Bitter''', one of the five basic [[taste]]s. 
 
 Common bitter [[food]]s and [[beverage]]s are [[coffee]], pure [[chocolate]], [[beer]],  citrus [[peel]] and [[escarole]]. [[Quinine]] is also known for its bitter taste and is found in tonic water.
