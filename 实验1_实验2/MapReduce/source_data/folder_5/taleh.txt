'''Taleh''' ({{lang-so|Taleex}}, {{lang-ar|تلأ ح‎}}) is a city in [[Sool, Somalia|Sool]], [[Somalia]]. It was the final capital of the [[Darwiish (Dhulbahante state)|Daraawiish]]. The first Daraawiish capital was in [[Dareema Caddo]] near [[Buuhoodle]]. < ref > Xasuus qor: timelines of Somali history, 1400-2000 - Page 25 < /ref >
