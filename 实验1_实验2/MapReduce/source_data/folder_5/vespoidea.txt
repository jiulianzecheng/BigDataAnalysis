 '''Vespoidea''' is a superfamily of the [[insect]] [[Order (biology)|order]] [[Hymenoptera]] in class of [[insect]]s. The members of this group are [[wasp]]s and [[ant]]s. < ref > Hoell H.V; Doyen J.T.  &  Purcell A.H. 1998.
