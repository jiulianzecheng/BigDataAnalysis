 '''Eclagnens''' was a [[municipality]] of the district [[Gros-de-Vaud (district)|Gros-de-Vaud]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Vaud]] in [[Switzerland]]. On 1 July 2011, the former municipalities of Eclagnens, [[Goumoens-la-Ville]] and [[Goumoens-le-Jux]] merged to form the new municipality of [[Goumoëns]]. < ref name=Fusion > [http://www.bfs.admin.ch/bfs/portal/de/index/infothek/nomenklaturen/blank/blank/gem_liste/03.html Amtliches Gemeindeverzeichnis der Schweiz] published by the Swiss Federal Statistical Office {{in lang|de}} accessed 17 February 2011. < /ref >
