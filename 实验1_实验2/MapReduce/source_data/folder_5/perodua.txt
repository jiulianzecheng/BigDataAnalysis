 The '''{{lang|ms|Perusahaan Otomobil Kedua Sendirian Berhad}}''' (''Second Automobile Manufacturer Private Limited''), usually abbreviated to '''Perodua''' ({{IPAc-en|p|ə|ˈ|r|ɒ|dj|u|ə}}), is [[Malaysia]]'s largest car manufacturer, closely followed by [[PROTON Holdings|Proton]]. 
 == Current models == 
 *[[Perodua Myvi]] (2005–present; B-segment hatchback, based on the [[Daihatsu Sirion]], third generation developed in-house) 
 *[[Perodua Alza]] (2009–present; B-segment mini MPV, based on the [[Daihatsu Boon|Daihatsu Boon Luminas]]) 
 *[[Perodua Axia]] (2014–present; A-segment hatchback, based on the [[Daihatsu Ayla]]) 
 *[[Perodua Bezza]] (2016–present; A-segment sedan) 
 * [[Perodua Aruz]] (2019–present; B-segment SUV, based on the [[Daihatsu Terios]])
