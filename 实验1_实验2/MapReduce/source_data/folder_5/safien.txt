 '''Safien''' was a [[municipality]] of the [[district]] of [[Surselva (district)|Surselva]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Graubünden]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 On 1 January 2013, the former municipalities of Safien, [[Valendas]], [[Versam]] and [[Tenna, Switzerland|Tenna]] merged into the new municipality of [[Safiental]].
