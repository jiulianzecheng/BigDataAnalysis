 '''Kirill  " Kirka "  Babitzin''' (22 September 1950 - 31 January 2007) was a [[Finland|Finnish]] [[singer]]. Kirka's most famous songs include  " Hengaillaan " ,  " Leijat "  (Kites) and  " Varrella virran "  (Down by the River) " . Kirill Babitzin was born in Helsinki in 1950. His [[parents]] were [[Russia|Russian]] [[:wikt:emigrant|emigrants]]. He first got into [[music]] at the age of five when his [[grandmother]] gave him an [[accordion]]. He won an accordion [[competition]] when he was ten years old. He was soon attracted to [[rock and roll music]]. His first [[band]] was 'The Creatures', which he joined in 1964 assuming [[stage name]] Kirka.
