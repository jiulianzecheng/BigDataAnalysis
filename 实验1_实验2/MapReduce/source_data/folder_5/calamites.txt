Calamities were horsetail like trees that went [[Extinction|extinct]] about 250 to 360 million years ago. These plants were medium-sized trees, growing to heights of 30-50 meters and about 100-160 feet. The trunk of these trees had an appearance like a [[bamboo]] trunk while the leaves were needle-shaped. These calamities reproduced by cloning
