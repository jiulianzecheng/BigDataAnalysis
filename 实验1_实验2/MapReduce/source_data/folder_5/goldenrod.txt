 '''Goldenrods''' are any of the about 150 species of [[weed]]y [[herb]]s that make up the [[genus]] '''''Solidago'''''. Handling the goldenrod many times can make some people have [[allergy|allergies]]. Because of this, some [[florist]]s change their jobs. < ref > {{citation |pmid= 9534922 |date=Feb 1998 |author= De, Jong, Nw; Vermeulen, Am; Gerth, Van, Wijk, R; De, Groot, H |title= Occupational allergy caused by flowers |volume= 53 |issue=2 |pages= 204–9 |issn= 0105-4538 |journal= Allergy |doi=10.1111/j.1398-9995.1998.tb03872.x |s2cid=41094680 }} < /ref >  Parts of goldenrods are eaten by humans. < ref > [http://www.northernbushcraft.com/plants/goldenrod/notes.htm Edibility of Goldenrod] Retrieved on March 12, 2010 < /ref >  They are also eaten by many [[species]] of [[Lepidoptera]].
