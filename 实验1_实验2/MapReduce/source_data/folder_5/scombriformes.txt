 '''Scombriformes''' is an order of [[bony fish]] that live in [[Ocean|marine environments]]. They form part of the [[Pelagiaria]] clade. We usually call them '''[[Mackerel|mackerels]]'''.  
 
 == Families == 
 The families placed under Scombriformes are: 
 
 * Order Scombriformes 
 ** Suborder Scombroidei 
 *** Family [[Gempylidae]] ([[Snake mackerel|snake mackerels]] and [[escolar]]) 
 *** Family [[Cutflassfish|Trichiuridae]] (cutlassfishes) 
 **** Subfamily Aphanopodinae 
 **** Subfamily Lepidopodinae 
 **** Subfamily Trichiurinae 
 *** Family [[Barracuda|Sphyraenidae]] (barracudas) 
 *** Family [[Istiophoridae]] (sailfish, marlins, and spearfish) 
 *** Family [[Swordfish|Xiphiidae]] (swordfish) 
 *** Family Gasterochismatidae ([[butterfly kingfish]]) 
 *** Family [[Scombridae]] (mackerels and tunas) 
 ** Suborder Stromateoidei 
 *** Family [[Amarsipus|Amarsipidae]] (the amarsipa) 
 *** Family [[Medusafish|Centrolophidae]] (medusafishes) 
 *** Family [[Driftfish|Nomeidae]] (driftfishes) 
 *** Family [[Ariommatidae]] (ariommatids) 
 *** Family [[Squaretail|Tetragonuridae]] (squaretails) 
 *** Family [[Stromateidae]] (butterfishes) 
 
 === Other families === 
 In Betancur-R et al., 2017, the following additional families are placed under Scombriformes, which is considered synonymous with the Pelagiaria clade. 
 
 * Family [[Arripis|Arripidae]] (Australasian salmon (kahawai)) 
 * Family [[Pomfret|Bramidae]] (pomfrets) 
 * Family [[Caristiidae]] (manefishes) 
 * Family [[Chiasmodontidae]] (swallowers) 
 * Family [[Ragfish|Icosteidae]] (the ragfish) 
 * Family [[Pomatomidae]] (the bluefish) 
 * Family [[Longfin escolar|Scombrolabracidae]] (the longfin escolar) 
 * Family [[Gnomefish|Scombropidae]] (gnomefishes)
