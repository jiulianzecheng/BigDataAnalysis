 '''Suardi''' is a ''[[comune]]'' in the [[Province of Pavia]] in the [[Lombardy]] region in [[Italy]]. It is about 60 & nbsp;km southwest of [[Milan]] and about 35 & nbsp;km southwest of [[Pavia]]. As of 31 December 2004, 692 people lived there. It has an area of 9.8 & nbsp;kmÂ². < ref name= " istat " > All demographics and other statistics: Italian statistical institute [[National Institute of Statistics (Italy)|Istat]]. < /ref > 
 
 Suardi is next to these municipalities: [[Bassignana]], [[Frascarolo]], [[Gambarana]], [[Valenza]].
