 '''Bartlow''' is a small [[village]] and [[civil parish]] in the [[South Cambridgeshire]] district of [[Cambridgeshire]] in the [[United Kingdom]]. It is about {{convert|12|mi|km}} southeast of [[Cambridge]] and {{convert|7|mi|km}} west of [[Haverhill, Suffolk|Haverhill]] in [[Suffolk]]. The [[River Granta]] runs through the village.
