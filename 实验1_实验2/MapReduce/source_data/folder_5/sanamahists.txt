'''Sanamahists''' or '''Sanamahi Chatpa'''s are persons who regard themselves as culturally, ethnically, or religiously adhering to the aspects of [[Sanamahism]], the [[Meitei religion]]. The term is mainly used by the [[Meitei people]] who follows the traditions of their ancestral culture.
