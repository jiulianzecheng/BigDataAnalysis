 '''Avellino''' is a city in southern [[Italy]]. It is capital of the [[province of Avellino]]. 
 
 ==Museums== 
 * National Gallery of selachoidei (Museum of sharks), Corso Umberto I, 131 and Via F. Petronelli, 8 
 * [[Museum of Art - MdAO]], Via degli Imbimbo, 47  
 * Museum of the Cathedral and the Diocese of Avellino, Via 23 novembre 
 * Provincial Archaeological Museum, Corso Europa 
 * Provincial Art Gallery, in the “Carcere Borbonico”  
 * Zoological Museum of invertebrates  " L. Carbone”, Corso Umberto I, 131
