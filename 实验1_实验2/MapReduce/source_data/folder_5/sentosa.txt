 '''Sentosa''' is a [[resort]] [[island]] at the [[south]] of [[Singapore]]. The island is well known for its [[beach]], [[Fort Siloso]] and two five-star [[hotels]]. The island was used as a place to build a [[fort]] during [[World War II]], by the [[Britain|British]] who ruled Singapore back then. Sentosa is also well known for its [[theme parks]].
