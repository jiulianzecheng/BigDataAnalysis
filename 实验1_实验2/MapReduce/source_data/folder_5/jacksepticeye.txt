 '''Seán McLoughlin''' (born 7 February 1990), better known as '''Jacksepticeye''', is an Irish [[YouTuber]]. He is best known for his [[vlog]]s and comedic [[Let's Play]] series. He is the most-subscribed Irish channel. < ref > {{Cite web |url=https://socialblade.com/youtube/top/country/ie/mostsubscribed |title=Top 250 YouTubers Channels in Ireland – Socialblade YouTube Stats {{!}} YouTube Statistics |website=socialblade.com |access-date=4 May 2018}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite web |url=https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCYzPXprvl5Y-Sf0g4vX-m6g |title=jacksepticeye |website=YouTube |access-date=15 December 2019}} < /ref >  He is also known for his [[charity]] streams in which he raises money for good causes. ''[[Business Insider]]'' has called McLoughlin  " one of YouTube's most [well known] [[philanthropist]]s " . < ref > {{Cite web|last=Dodgson|first=Lindsay|date=2020-12-10|title=One of YouTube's biggest streamers is trying to raise over $500,000 this holiday season for charity, highlighting the skyrocketing success of livestreaming|url=https://www.insider.com/jacksepticeyes-fundraising-goal-highlights-2020-livestreaming-boom-2020-12|access-date=2021-08-08|website=Insider|language=en-US}} < /ref > 
 
 In October 2018, McLoughlin posted a video announcing Cloak, a clothing brand aimed at gamers which he created with [[Markiplier]]. < ref name= " :8 " > {{Cite web|last=Hale|first=James|date=2018-10-19|title=Jacksepticeye And Markiplier Team Up To Launch Clothing Brand Cloak|url=https://www.tubefilter.com/2018/10/19/jacksepticeye-and-markiplier-team-up-to-launch-clothing-brand-cloak/|access-date=2021-08-08|website=Tubefilter|language=en-US}} < /ref >  In June 2020, Cloak welcomed the [[Twitch (service)|Twitch]] streamer [[Pokimane]] as a third partner and creative director for the brand. < ref name= " :24 " > {{Cite web|last=Hale|first=James|date=2020-06-18|title=Top Twitch Streamer Pokimane Joins Markiplier And Jacksepticeye's 'Cloak' Brand As Partner, Creative Director|url=https://www.tubefilter.com/2020/06/18/pokimane-cloak-creative-director-markiplier-jacksepticeye/|access-date=2021-08-08|website=Tubefilter|language=en-US}} < /ref >   
 
 In 2020, he created his own coffee company, named Top of The Mornin' Coffee. < ref > {{Cite web|last=O'Dell|first=Liam|date=2020-06-15|title=Youtuber JackSepticEye launches Top of The Mornin Coffee company|url=https://teneightymagazine.com/2020/06/15/jacksepticeye-launches-top-of-the-mornin-coffee-company/|access-date=2021-05-22|website=TenEighty|language=en}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite web|title=Questions|url=https://topofthemornincoffee.com/pages/questions|access-date=2021-08-08|website=Top of the Mornin Coffee|language=en}} < /ref >
