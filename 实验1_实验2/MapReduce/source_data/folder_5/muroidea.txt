 '''Muroidea''' is a large [[superfamily]] of [[rodent]]s.  It includes [[hamster]]s, [[gerbil]]s, true [[mouse|mice]] and [[rat]]s, and many other relatives.  They live on almost every [[continent]] except [[Antarctica]].  Some scientists have placed all members of this group into a single family, [[Muridae]].  Sometimes there are difficulties in knowing how the [[subfamily|subfamilies]] are related to one another.  
 
 The muroids are classified in 6 [[family (biology)|families]], 19 [[subfamily|subfamilies]], around 280 [[genus|genera]] and at least 1300 [[species]].

 ** Family [[Calomyscidae]]

 *** Subfamily [[Cricetinae]] (true [[hamster]]s)
