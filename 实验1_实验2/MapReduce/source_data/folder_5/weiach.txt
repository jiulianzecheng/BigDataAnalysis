 '''Weiach''' is a [[municipality]] of the [[district]] of [[Dielsdorf (district)|Dielsdorf]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Zurich (canton)|Zurich]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 ==History== 
 Weiach has belonged to the [[Swiss Confederation]] since 1424. Since 1962 there has been a [[gravel]] industry in town.
