 '''Jamun Khan''' (born 23 July 1991), better known by his [[stage name]] '''Jaykae''', is an English [[Grime (music)|grime]] MC and [[Rapping|rapper]]. He was born in [[Small Heath]], Birmingham, England. He performed [[Grime (music)|grime music]], [[hip hop music]], and [[British hip hop]]. His musical career began in 2008. He is signed to Doing Bits Worldwide Limited and a record label of the same name as his [[stage name]]. He has worked with other artists like [[Stormzy]].
