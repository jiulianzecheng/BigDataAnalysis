'''Modafinil''' is a [[stimulant]]. It is mainly used to treat [[narcolepsy]] and some cases of [[sleep apnea]]. The drug was developed in the 1980s. It is sold under different brand names, as well as [[generic drug]]s. Usually it is a [[prescription]] drug. As there are signs that it  
 causes [[birth defect]]s, it likely should not be taken during [[pregnancy]].
