'''Uzlomac''' is a long mountain in Central [[Bosnia]],  [[Bosnia and Herzegovina]],  east from line direction [[Maslovare]] –  [[Vrbanjci]] – [[Kotor Varoš]] – [[Čelinac]]. It stretches in  southeast – northwest direction, through the mountainous area, from [[Kruševica (river)|Kruševica]] river to [[Jošavka (river)|Jošavka]]. Its maximum altitude is {{convert|1018|m}}.  
 
 On Uzlomac rise many tributaries of [[Vrbanja river]], [[Usora (river)|Usora]] and [[Ukrina (river)|Ukrina]], i.e. tributaries of  [[Vrbas (river)|Vrbas]] and [[Bosna (river)]] confluences. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://mapcarta.com/18722542|title=Uzlomac|website=Mapcarta}} < /ref > < ref > {{cite web|url=https://geographic.org/geographic_names/name.php?uni=-145196 & fid=588 & c=bosnia_and_herzegovina|title=Uzlomac Planina, Bosnia and Herzegovina - Geographical Names, map, geographic coordinates|website=geographic.org}} < /ref > < ref > Vojnogeografski institut, Ed.
