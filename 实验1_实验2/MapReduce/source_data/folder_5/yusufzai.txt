The '''Yusufzai''' (also spelt 'Yousafzai', or 'Esapzai') are a large [[Pashtun]] tribe inhabiting several areas the [[Khyber Pakhtunkhwa]] province, [[Pakistan]]. At the present time, a young girl, [[Malala Yousafzai]] is the best known member of this tribe globally.
