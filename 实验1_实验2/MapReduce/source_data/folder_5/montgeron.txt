 '''Montgeron''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is in [[Île-de-France]] in the [[Essonne]] [[Departments of France|department]] in north [[France]]. 
 
 The café ''Au Reveil Matin'' at 22 Avenue [[Jean Jaurès]] was the starting point of the [[1903 Tour de France|first Tour de France]] in 1903. Montgeron was also the starting point of the 21st (and last) stage of the [[2017 Tour de France]].
