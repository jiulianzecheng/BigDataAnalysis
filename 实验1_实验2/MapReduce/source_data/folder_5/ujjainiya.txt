'''Ujjainiya''' is a sub-caste of the [[Parmar]] Clan of the [[Rajput|Rajputs]]. They are also known as Purvaiya Rajputs. They can be found near Sahababad region of [[Bihar]], and Deoghar region of [[Jharkhand]]. 
 
 [[Kunwar Singh|Veer Kunwar Singh]] was a Ujjainiya Rajput.
