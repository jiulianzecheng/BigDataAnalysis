 '''Dalmine''' is an [[Italy|Italian]] city in [[Lombardy]]. Dalmine is in the [[Province of Bergamo]]. It is about {{convert|40|km|mi}} northeast of [[Milan]] and about {{convert|8|km|mi|0}} southwest of [[Bergamo]]. About 23,000 people live there.
