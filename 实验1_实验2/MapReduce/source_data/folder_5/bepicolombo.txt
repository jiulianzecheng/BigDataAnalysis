 '''BepiColombo''' is a joint mission of the [[European Space Agency]] (ESA) and the [[Japan Aerospace Exploration Agency]] (JAXA) to the [[planet]] [[Mercury (planet)|Mercury]].  It launched on October 20, 2018 and it is planned to arrive at Mercury On December 5, 2025.
