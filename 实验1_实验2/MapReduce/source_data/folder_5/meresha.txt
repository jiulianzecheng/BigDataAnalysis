 '''Meresha''' is an [[Americans|American]] singer-songwriter, [[musician]] and [[producer]]. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.allmusic.com/artist/meresha-mn0003386569|title=Meresha Biography, Albums, Streaming Links|publisher=[[AllMusic]]|access-date=December 27, 2016}} < /ref > 
 
 == Biography == 
 
 Her music has charted on the [[Billboard (magazine)|''Billboard'']] charts multiple times, while her videos have been played internationally, including on [[MTV]], where she won a Freshmen competition. < ref > {{cite magazine|url=http://www.billboard.com/artist/7618992/meresha/chart|title=Meresha - Chart history on Billboard|magazine=[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]|access-date=December 27, 2016|archive-date=January 3, 2017|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170103030748/http://www.billboard.com/artist/7618992/meresha/chart|url-status=dead}} < /ref > < ref name= " mtuv " > {{cite web|url=http://www.mtvu.com/music/meresha-wins-this-round-of-the-freshmen/|title=Meresha Wins This Round Of The Freshmen|publisher=[[MTVU]]|access-date=December 27, 2016}} < /ref >  She's been the #1 indie artist on US Pop radio for a month. < ref name= " elnuevoheral " > {{cite news|url=http://www.elnuevoherald.com/entretenimiento/revista-viernes/article48641505.html|title=¿Qué hacer en Miami este fin de semana?|access-date=December 27, 2016|publisher=[[El Nuevo Herald]]}} < /ref >  She was selected to the national House of Blues class of 2016 of promising musicians, < ref > {{cite web|url=http://hobmusicforward.org/bdth-bands|title=BDTH Bands, House of Blues Music Forward Foundation|access-date=December 30, 2016|archive-date=January 3, 2017|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170103095228/http://hobmusicforward.org/bdth-bands/|url-status=dead}} < /ref >  and has performed at venues including Mizner Amphitheater, Hard Rock Cafe, House of Blues and B.B.King's.  === Works === 
 
 In 2015, Meresha released the [[Extended play|EP]] ''New Revolution''. It included the title song and three other songs. < ref > {{cite news|url=http://www.palmbeachpost.com/entertainment/few-favorites-for-top-2015-palm-beach-county-music/BB6OnRHiG4J9DDozKT6sDJ/|title=Few favorites for top 2015 Palm Beach County music|access-date=December 27, 2016|publisher=[[The Palm Beach Post]]}} < /ref >  Her eclecticism and beats created a fresh sound that brought old styles into modern production. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.mi2n.com/press.php3?press_nb=194955|title=Together / Juntos|publisher=Mi2N.com|access-date=December 27, 2016|archive-date=August 30, 2017|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170830205653/http://www.mi2n.com/press.php3?press_nb=194955|url-status=dead}} < /ref >  The song  " New Revolution "  entered the [[Billboard (magazine)|''Billboard'']] Top 40 chart between [[Adele]] and [[Justin Bieber]]. It was the #1 Indie song on US radio for a month. Her [[Music video|video clip]] ''New Revolution'' was directed by David Rousseau. He had also directed most [[Pitbull (rapper)|Pitbull's]] videos . < ref name= " allaccess " > {{cite web|url=http://music.allaccess.com/an-interview-with-rising-singer-songwriter-meresha-on-her-early-start-and-her-new-revolution/|title=An Interview with Rising Singer-Songwriter, MERESHA On Her Early Start and Her  " New Revolution " !|date=29 September 2015|publisher=AllAccess|access-date=December 27, 2016|archive-date=6 September 2016|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160906054552/http://music.allaccess.com/an-interview-with-rising-singer-songwriter-meresha-on-her-early-start-and-her-new-revolution/|url-status=dead}} < /ref >  ''My love has come'' was in the Top 40 on both Billboard Activator and Indicator charts. At the end of 2016, Meresha released the dual-language [[Single (music)|single]] ''Together/Juntos''. < ref > {{cite magazine|url=http://www.billboard.com/artist/7618992/meresha/biography|title=Meresha - Biography|magazine=[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]|access-date=December 30, 2016|archive-date=January 3, 2017|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20170103030717/http://www.billboard.com/artist/7618992/meresha/biography|url-status=dead}} < /ref >  She performed her songs live in concert with [[Adam Lambert]] and the band [[Third Eye Blind]]. < ref name= " elnuevoheral "  / > < ref > {{cite news|title=Meresha - 1st Ever World Music Premier On The #1 Social Media Startup Tsu April 7|publisher=Before It's News}} < /ref > < ref > {{cite news|url=http://www.palmbeachpost.com/entertainment/high-school-student-meresha-mixes-old-new-sounds/zou3wzhXo9YSQnPG0P9myO/|title=High school student Meresha mixes old, new sounds|access-date=December 27, 2016|publisher=[[The Palm Beach Post]]}} < /ref >
