 The '''Batmobile''' is the personal car for [[Batman]] and [[Robin (comics)|Robin]]. The Batmobile was made from [[Wayne Enterprises]] by [[Lucius Fox]]. The Batmobile appeared in all Batman movies ever made, but in [[Christopher Nolan]]'s ''[[The Dark Knight Trilogy]]'' it was named '''The Tumbler''' throughout the trilogy.
