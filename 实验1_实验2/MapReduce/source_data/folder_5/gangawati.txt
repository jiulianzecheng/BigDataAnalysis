 '''Gangawati''' (or '''Gangavati''', {{Lang-kn|ಗಂಗಾವತಿ}}) is a town, [[municipality]] and [[taluk]] in the Indian state of [[Karnataka]]. It is part of the [[Koppal district]]. 
 
 In the 2001 census of India, Gangawati had a population of 93,249. < ref > {{GR|India}} < /ref > 
 
 Near Gangawati is [[Hampi]], a [[UNESCO World Heritage Site]]. It is {{convert|14|km|mi|1}} south-west of the town.
