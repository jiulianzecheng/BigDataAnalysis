 '''Trainmeusel''' is a village in the [[Upper Franconia]]n [[market municipality]] of [[Wiesenttal]] in the district of [[Landkreis Forchheim|Forchheim]] in the German state of [[Bavaria]]. 
 
 == Place == 
 The [[village]] is within the [[Franconian Switzerland-Veldenstein Forest Nature Park]], about 1.3 & nbsp;kilometres southwest of  [[Muggendorf (Wiesenttal)|Muggendorf]] on a Jurassic plateau of the [[Franconian Jura]]. The village may be reached by car from Muggendorf on the ''[[Kreisstrasse]]'' FO35. Its neighbouring villages in clockwise order are Muggendorf, Wohlmannsgesees, and Birkenreuth. < ref > [https://geoportal.bayern.de/bayernatlas/?X=5518034.28 & Y=4445877.74 & zoom=11 & lang=de & topic=ba & bgLayer=tk & catalogNodes=122 Lage Trainmeusel im BayernAtlas] (Abgerufen am 17.
