 '''Cauliflower''' is one of several [[vegetables]] in the species ''[[Brassica oleracea]]'', in the family [[Brassicaceae]]. Cauliflower is a variety of [[cabbage]], < ref > {{Cite web|title=Cauliflower {{!}} plant|url=https://www.britannica.com/plant/cauliflower|access-date=2021-04-13|website=Encyclopedia Britannica|language=en}} < /ref >  whose white flower head is eaten. Cauliflower is very nutritious, and may be eaten cooked, raw or [[Pickling|pickled]]. It is a popular vegetable in [[Poland]] where it is eaten in a [[soup]] with cream or fried with [[bread]] crumbs.
