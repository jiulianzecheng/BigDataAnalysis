 A '''lifeguard''' is a [[person]] on the [[beach]] or at a public [[Swimming pool|pool]] who shows people the correct way of using the water facility and stops accidents from happening. They are trained in [[wikt:rescue|rescue]] situations. Lifeguards are usually found at most public pools and [[lake]]s.
