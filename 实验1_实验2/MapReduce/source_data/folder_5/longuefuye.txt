'''Longuefuye''' is a former [[Communes of France|commune]] of 274 people ([[1999]]). It is found in the region [[Pays de la Loire]] in the [[Mayenne]] department in the northwest of [[France]]. On 1 January 2019, it was merged into the new commune of [[Gennes-Longuefuye]]. < ref > [https://www.legifrance.gouv.fr/eli/arrete/2018/11/14/TERB1833262A/jo/texte Arrêté préfectoral] 14 November 2018 {{in lang|fr}} < /ref >
