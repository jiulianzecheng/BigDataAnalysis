 '''Thame''' ({{IPAc-en|pron|t|eɪ|m}}) is a town and [[civil parish]] in [[Oxfordshire]], England, about {{convert|7|mi|km}} southwest of the [[Buckinghamshire]] town of [[Aylesbury]]. In 2001 there were 11,072 people living in Thame. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.ons.gov.uk/help/localstatistics|title=Local statistics - Office for National Statistics|website=www.ons.gov.uk}} < /ref >
