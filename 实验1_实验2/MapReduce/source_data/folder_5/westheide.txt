 '''Westheide''' is a municipality in the district [[Börde Rural District|Börde]], in [[Saxony-Anhalt]], [[Germany]]. It was created on 1 January 2010 and consists of the former municipalities of [[Born, Saxony-Anhalt|Born]], [[Hillersleben]] and [[Neuenhofe]].
