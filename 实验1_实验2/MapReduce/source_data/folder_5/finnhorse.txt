 '''The Finnhorse''' or '''Finnish Horse'''  is a [[horse]] breed developed in [[Finland]]. The Finnhorse is the national horse breed of Finland. It is suitable both for riding and to pull vehicles in races and as a workhorse. < ref name= " HIP " > [http://www.hippos.fi/hippos/muut/in_english/the_finnhorse The Finnhorse - speed, power and feeling] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160305102623/http://www.hippos.fi/hippos/muut/in_english/the_finnhorse |date=2016-03-05 }} Hippos < /ref > 
 
 Studbook of Finnhorses was founded 1907.  The horse is 156 cm high. It has many colours, but the main colours are brown and black. The Finnhorse has often white markings on the face and the legs. < ref name= " HIP " / >
