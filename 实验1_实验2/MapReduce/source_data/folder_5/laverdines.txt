 '''Laverdines''' is a former [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is in [[Centre-Val de Loire]] in the [[Cher (department)|Cher]] [[Departments of France|department]] in central [[France]]. On 1 January 2019, it was merged into the commune of [[Baugy, Cher|Baugy]]. < ref > [https://www.legifrance.gouv.fr/eli/arrete/2018/10/18/TERB1833540A/jo/texte Arrêté préfectoral] 18 October 2018 {{in lang|fr}} < /ref >
