 '''''Boana''''' is a genus of [[frog]]s in the family [[Hylidae]]. They are called '''gladiator frogs''', '''gladiator treefrogs''' or '''Wagler Neotropical treefrogs'''. These frogs live in tropical [[Central America|Central]] and [[South America]] from [[Nicaragua]] to [[Argentina]].  They also live in the Caribbean, in [[Trinidad]] and [[Tobago]]. < ref name=frost > {{cite web |url=https://amphibiansoftheworld.amnh.org/Amphibia/Anura/Hylidae/Cophomantinae/Boana |title=''Boana'' Gray, 1825 |author=Frost, Darrel R. |year=2020 |work=Amphibian Species of the World: an Online Reference. Version 6.1 |publisher=American Museum of Natural History |access-date=22 April 2020}} < /ref > 
 
 For a while, scientists thought using this genus was a mistake.  They started using it again after taking a long look again at Hylidae. They decided that about 70 species that they had put in the genus ''[[Hyla]]'' should be in ''Boana'' instead.
