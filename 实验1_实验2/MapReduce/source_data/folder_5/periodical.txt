'''Periodical''' is a type of [[publication]] that is produced regularly. It is anything that comes out with regular issues and are published in series.  < ref > {{cite web|url=https://libguides.southernct.edu/c.php?g=7089 & p=34269|title=Research Guides: Library Tutorial: What's A Periodical?|last=Shyam|first=Winnie|website=libguides.southernct.edu|language=en|access-date=2020-06-10}} < /ref >  A daily newspaper, a weekly news magazine, a monthly journal, and an annual book series are all examples of periodicals. Some periodicals are published in ways that make them more valuable to scholars. Periodical literature is a category of serial publications that appear in a new edition on a regular schedule.
