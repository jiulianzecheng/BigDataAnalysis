 The '''Studebaker Corporation''' was an [[United States|American]] company in [[South Bend, Indiana]] that built [[automobile|automobiles]].  It started in 1852 making [[cart|wagons]], at that time it was named Studebaker Brothers [[Manufacturing]] Company.  Later they started making cars and then pickups.  In 1966 they went out of business. 
 
 == History == 
 
 At first, Studebaker built [[horse]] wagons used by [[farmer|farmers]], [[miner|miners]] and the [[military]].  In 1902 they started making [[electric vehicle|electric vehicles]] and in 1904 they started making [[gasoline]] [[vehicles]].  At first they worked with other companies to make the vehicles, but starting in 1912 they made the whole car by themselves.  For fifty years they made many cars that worked well and had interesting shapes.  But [[The Cars|the cars]] cost a lot of money and after some years of troubles, the company shut down in 1966.
