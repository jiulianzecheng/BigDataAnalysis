 In [[Japanese language|Japanese]], a '''kigurumi''' ({{lang|ja|着ぐるみ}}) is a [[costume]]d [[Performance|performer]] who represents a [[cartoon]] [[Fictional character|character]]. It is very important in the [[Harajuku]] fashion. The name literally translates to  " wearing a soft toy " . Kigurumi come in cute characters such as Totoro, [[Hello Kitty]] and [[Pikachu]]. They are considered cute. The costumed characters at Disneyland are considered kigurami.
