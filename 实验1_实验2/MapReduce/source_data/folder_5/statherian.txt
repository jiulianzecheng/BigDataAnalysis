The '''Statherian''' is the last [[geology|geological]] [[Period (geology)|period]] in the [[Palaeoproterozoic]]. It began 1.8 [[billion]] years ago and ended at 1.6 billion years ago.  
 
 The period is marked by new land. The supercontinent [[Columbia (continent)|Columbia]] formed at the beginning of this period.
