 '''Cerniaz''' was a [[municipality]] of the [[Broye-Vully (district)|Broye-Vully]] district in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Vaud]] in [[Switzerland]]. On 1 July 2011 the former municipalities of Cerniaz, [[Combremont-le-Grand]], [[Combremont-le-Petit]], [[Granges-près-Marnand]], [[Marnand]], [[Sassel]], [[Seigneux]] and [[Villars-Bramard]] joined to become the new municipality of [[Valbroye]]. < ref name=Fusion > [http://www.bfs.admin.ch/bfs/portal/de/index/infothek/nomenklaturen/blank/blank/gem_liste/03.html Amtliches Gemeindeverzeichnis der Schweiz] published by the Swiss Federal Statistical Office {{in lang|de}} accessed 17 February 2011 < /ref >
