'''Astraea''' is a [[goddess]] from [[Greek mythology]]. She is the goddess of justice, innocence, and accuracy. Many stories say she is the [[constellation]] [[Virgo (constellation)|Virgo]]. Her name means  " [[star]]ry night "  or  " star maid " .
