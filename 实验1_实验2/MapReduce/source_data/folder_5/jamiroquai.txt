 '''Jamiroquai''' are a British [[funk]] and [[acid jazz]] [[band]]. It was created by [[Jay Kay|Jason Kay]]. The name of the band is a compound word created from  " [[jam session]] " , a type of music event, and  " [[Iroquois]] " , the name of a [[Native American]] [[tribe]].  
 
 The band's first [[Single (music)|single]],  " When You Gonna Learn? " , was released in 1992. Because of the [[wikt:success|success]] of this single, Jamiroquai made a [[contract]] with [[Sony BMG]]. They release their first [[album]], ''Emergency on Planet Earth,'' in 1993. The next year, their second album (''The Return of the Space Cowboy)'' was released. This album became popular. In 1996, a [[song]] named  " Virtual Insanity "  on their third album, ''Travelling Without Moving,'' was very popular. The [[music video]] for this song was so [[innovation|innovative]] that it caused a big [[sensation]]. With this song, Jamiroquai was [[wikt:nominate|nominated]] for ten [[MTV video awards]]. It won four [[awards]]. This album made the band famous all across the world.   
 
 == Studio albums == 
 
 * ''Emergency on Planet Earth'' (1993) 
 * ''The Return of the Space Cowboy'' (1994) 
 * ''Travelling Without Moving'' (1996) 
 * ''Synkronized'' (1999) 
 * ''A Funk Odyssey'' (2001) 
 * ''Dynamite'' (2005) 
 * ''Rock Dust Light Star'' (2010) 
 * ''Automaton'' (2017)
