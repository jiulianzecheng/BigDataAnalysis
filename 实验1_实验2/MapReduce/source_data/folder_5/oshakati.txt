 '''Oshakati''' is a town < ref > [http://www.amicaall.org/countries/alan.htm Alliance of Mayors and Municipal Leaders on HIV/AIDS in Africa] {{webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20081121025306/http://www.amicaall.org/countries/alan.htm|date=2008-11-21}} < /ref >  of 37,000 inhabitants in the [[Oshana Region]] of [[Namibia]]. It is the regional capital and was officially founded in July 1966.  The city was used as a base of operations by the [[South African Defence Force]] (SADF) during the [[South African Border War]]. 
 
 Oshakati is divided into the electoral constituencies of [[Oshakati East]] and [[Oshakati West]].
