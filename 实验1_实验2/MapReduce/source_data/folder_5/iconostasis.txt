[[File:Iconostasis in Moscow.jpg|thumb|Iconostasis in [[Moscow]]]] '''Iconostasis''' is the name given to a  " wall "  of [[icon]]s in [[Eastern Orthodox Church]]es. These icons or religious paintings divide the church into a part for the public, and one which is reserved to priests, called the [[sanctuary]].
