 '''Mergoscia''' is a [[municipality]] of the district [[Locarno (district)|Locarno]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Ticino]] in [[Switzerland]]. The village is found above [[Lago di Vogorno]] in the [[Valle Verzasca|Verzasca]] valley.
