 '''Madhubala''' (born '''Mumtaz Jehan Begum Dehlavi'''; 14 February 1933 – 23 February 1969) was an Indian actress, producer and singer. She was known as  " The [[Venus (mythology)|Venus]] of [[Cinema of India|Indian cinema]] " .
