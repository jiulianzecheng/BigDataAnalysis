 '''Versoix''' is a [[municipality]] of the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Geneva (canton)|Geneva]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 == Geography == 
 Versoix sits on the northwest side of [[Lake Geneva]] northeast of the city of [[Geneva]]. 
 
 It has of the villages of Richelien, Creuson, Sauverny, Petit-Saint-Loup, Ecogia, Versoix-la-Ville, Pont-Céard, Port-Choiseul, Versoix-Bourg, Versoix - lac and Crève-Cœur.
