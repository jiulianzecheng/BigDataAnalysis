 '''Armix''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is in the [[Auvergne-Rhône-Alpes]] [[Regions of France|region]] in the [[Ain]] [[Departments of France|department]] in east [[France]]. In 2012, 24 people lived in the commune. It is the least populated commune in the Ain department. The [[inhabitant]]s are the ''Armixois''. [[Earthquake|Seismic activity]] affects this commune.
