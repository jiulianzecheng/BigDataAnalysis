 '''Qizha''' ({{lang-ar|''' قزحة'''}}, like '''Kiz-ha''') is a [[Syrian]] and [[Palestinian]] [[semolina]] cake. It is made from semolina flour flavored with crushed [[Nigella seed]]s.
