 '''Thimbleberry''' (''Rubus parviflorus'') is a species of [[Rubus]] native to northern [[Temperate climate|temperate]] regions of [[North America]]. The plant has large [[trichome|hairy leaves]] and no [[Thorns, spines, and prickles|thorns]]. It bears edible red fruit similar in appearance to a [[raspberry]], but shorter, almost hemispherical.
