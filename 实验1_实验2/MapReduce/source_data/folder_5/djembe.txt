 The '''djembe''' (pronounced ZHEM-bey) is one of the best-known African [[drum]]s. It originally came from [[West Africa]]. 
 It is shaped like a large goblet and played with the bare hands. The drum's body is carved from a hollowed trunk. The drum head is made of [[goat]]skin.
