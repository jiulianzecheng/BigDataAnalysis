 '''Sassenheim''' is a former [[List of municipalities of the Netherlands|municipality]] in the [[Netherlands|Dutch]] [[Provinces of the Netherlands|province]] of [[South Holland]]. Since 2006, it has been part of the new municipality of [[Teylingen]]. 
 
 The former municipality covers an area of 6.62 & nbsp;km² (of which 0.23 & nbsp;km² water). In 2005, it had a population of 14,906.
