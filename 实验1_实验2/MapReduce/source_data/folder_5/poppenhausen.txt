 '''Poppenhausen''' is a [[Municipalities of Germany|municipality]] in [[Schweinfurt (district)|Schweinfurt]] in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. 
 
 It has the following six districts: Hain, Kronungen, Kützberg, Maibach, Pfersdorf, Poppenhausen.
