 '''Haldummulla''' ({{Lang-si|හල්දුම්මුල්ල}}) is a town in the [[Badulla District]], [[Uva Province|Uva Province, Sri Lanka]]. < ref > [http://www.ds.gov.lk/ds/haldummulla/english/index.php?p=GN_divisions Divisional Secretariats ] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20090116113640/http://www.ds.gov.lk/ds/haldummulla/english/index.php?p=GN_divisions|date=2009-01-16}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite web|title=Archeology Projects|url=http://www.archaeology.gov.lk/pdfdocs/mon_reg.pdf|url-status=dead|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110722145801/http://www.archaeology.gov.lk/pdfdocs/mon_reg.pdf|archive-date=22 July 2011|access-date=8 July 2009}} < /ref > < ref > [http://www.ayurveda.gov.lk/Herbal%20Garden.htm Herbal gardens] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20090827000609/http://www.ayurveda.gov.lk/Herbal%20Garden.htm|date=2009-08-27}} < /ref > 
 
 A [[landslide]] damaged a large part of the town in 2014.

 === Portuguese Fort === 
 [[Portugal]] created a [[Fortification|fort]] made of stone in the 1600s. It is also known as the Katugodalla fort. It was used to block [[Invasion|invasions]]. Only the [[Foundation (engineering)|foundations]] of the fort are still there.
