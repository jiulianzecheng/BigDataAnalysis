 '''Stephen''' or '''Steven''' (generally pronounced {{IPAEng|ˈstiːvən}}) < ref > {{Cite web|title=Definition of Stephen {{!}} Dictionary.com|url=https://www.dictionary.com/browse/stephen|access-date=2023-03-26|website=www.dictionary.com|language=en}} < /ref >  is an [[English language|English]] masculine [[given name|first name]], comes the [[Latin]] form ''Stephanus'' and that from the [[Greek language|Greek]] ''Στέφανος'' (''Stephanos''), which means  " crown "  or  " wreath. " < ref > {{Cite web|title=Stephen name Meaning and Origin|url=https://babys.name/detail-Stephen-Meaning-Origin-Famous-Similar-Sibling.html|access-date=2023-03-26|website=babys.name}} < /ref >  This name was ranked #341 on the US Popular Names in 2021.  < ref > {{Cite web|last=OACT|title=Popular Baby Names|url=https://www.ssa.gov/cgi-bin/popularnames.cgi|access-date=2023-03-26|website=www.ssa.gov}} < /ref > 
 
 Other spellings are Stephan, Stefan, Stephaen, Stevie, and Stevon. Steve is the common short form and various [[diminutive]]s such as Stevie are also found. Many family names come from Stephen: the most [[wikt:common|common]] are Stephens/Stevens and Stephenson/Stevenson (others include Stephen, Stephan, Stefan, Stevin, and Stever).
