 '''Hemau''' is a small town in Southern [[Germany]], in [[Regensburg (district)|Regensburg]].  The town is on the ''Tangrintel'', a ridge which runs between the rivers [[Altmühl]] and [[Schwarze Laber]]. 
 
 It is on the [[Bundesstrasse 8|B8 road]], {{Convert|28|km|0|abbr=on}} north-west of [[Regensburg]], {{Convert|77|km|0|abbr=on}} south-east of [[Nuremberg]].  The nearest towns, about {{Convert|5|km|0|abbr=on}} distant, are [[Beratzhausen]] and [[Laaber]].
