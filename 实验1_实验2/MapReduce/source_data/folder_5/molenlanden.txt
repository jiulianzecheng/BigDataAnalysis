 '''Molenlanden''' is a municipality in the province of [[South Holland]], the [[Netherlands]]. About 44,000 people were living there in 2021. 
 
 It was created from the former municipalities of [[Giessenlanden]] and [[Molenwaard]] on 1 January 2019. 
 
 Molenlanden covers most part of the [[Alblasserwaard]] region. A tiny part in the southeast lies in the [[Vijfheerenlanden (region)|Vijfheerenlanden]] region.
