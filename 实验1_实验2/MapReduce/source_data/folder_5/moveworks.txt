'''Moveworks''' is an American technology company that develops artificial inteligence platform for other businesses. It uses natural language understanding, probabilistic machine learning, and automation behind its platform. 
 
 ==History== 
 The company was established in 2016 by Bhavin Shah, Vaibhav Nivargi, Varun Singh, and Jiang Chen. < ref > {{cite news |title=Moveworks Raises $30 Million To Stop Employees From Hating IT |url=https://www.forbes.com/sites/jilliandonfro/2019/04/17/moveworks-raises-30-million-to-automate-it-support-tasks/ |work=Forbes.com}} < /ref > 
 
 It initially started as a technology support business. Later, they started developing internal communications solutions using artificial inteligence.
