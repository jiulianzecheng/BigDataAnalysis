'''Proton Mail''' is an [[email]] service founded in 2013. Proton Mail can [[Encryption|encrypt]] users' emails, making them impossible to read to anyone without an encryption password. Proton Mail exists partly in response to news about [[surveillance]] done by the [[National Security Agency]].
