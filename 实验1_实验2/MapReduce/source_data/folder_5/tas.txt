''Tas'' Gujjars are adherents of Hinduism, Islam and some are also Sikh.  
 
 ==Geographical distribution== 
 They inhabit several different Indian states, including [[Rajasthan]], [[Haryana]], Uttar Pradesh, [[Punjab, India|Punjab]], Madhya Pradesh, [[Uttarakhand]], Jammu and Kashmir, Gujarat, [[Himachal Pradesh]] < ref name= " :0 "  / >  and [[Delhi|Dehli]]. In Pakistan they inhabit Khyber Pakhtunkhwa, [[Azad Kashmir]], [[Balochistan]] and [[Punjab, Pakistan|Punjab]] (especially in [[Gujrat District|Gujrat,]] < ref name= " :1 "  / >  Lahore and Gujranwala districts) provinces of Pakistan.
