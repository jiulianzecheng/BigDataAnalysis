 The '''megapodes''', also known as '''incubator birds''' or '''mound-builders''', are stocky, medium-large [[chicken]]-like birds with small heads and large feet. They are included in the family of Megapodidae. They may build a pile of rotting vegetables as an incubator for their [[egg (biology)|egg]]s.
