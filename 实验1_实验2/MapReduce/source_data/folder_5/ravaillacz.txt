 '''Ravaillacz''' is a [[supergroup|superquartet]] from [[Sweden]]. They participated at [[Melodifestivalen 2013]] with the charting song  " ''[[En riktig jävla schlager]]'' " . < ref > {{Cite web|url=http://swedishcharts.com/showitem.asp?interpret=Ravaillacz & titel=En+riktig+j%E4vla+schlager & cat=s|title=En riktig jävla schlager|publisher=Swedishcharts|date=2013|accessdate=8 August 2013}} < /ref >  The song ended up 10th. The group consists of [[Tommy Körberg]], [[Claes Malmberg]], [[Johan Rabaeus]] and [[Mats Ronander]]. 
 
 The group also appeared at [[Allsång på Skansen]] on 6 August 2013. < ref > {{Cite web|url=http://smdb.kb.se/catalog/id/002937804|title=SVT1 2013-08-06|publisher=Svensk mediedatabas|language=Swedish|date=2013|accessdate=6 August 2013}} < /ref >
