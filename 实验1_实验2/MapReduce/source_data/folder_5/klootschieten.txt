 '''Klootschieten''' ( " Ball shooting "  in English) is a [[sport]] played in the [[Netherlands]] and [[Frisia]], [[Lower Saxony]], [[Germany]]. < ref name=topend > {{cite web |url=http://www.topendsports.com/sport/more/klootschieten.htm |title=Klootschieten |publisher=Topend Sports Network |access-date=February 1, 2017}} < /ref >  In the game, [[wikt:player|player]]s try to throw a [[ball]] (called a '''kloot''') as far as they can. < ref name=topend/ >  One version is played on frozen fields during the winter. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://www.ostfriesland-tourism.com/my-ostfriesland/culture/living-in-ostfriesland/frisian-sports/klootschieten.html |title=Klootschieten on frozen meadows |publisher=My Ostfriesland |access-date=February 1, 2017}} < /ref >  A heavier ball is used when played on [[street]]s. < ref name=topend/ >  A third version is played while standing still. < ref name=topend/ >  The game is often played by teams of from three to five players. < ref name=kvwarnsveld > {{cite web |url=http://www.kvwarnsveld.nl/index_bestanden/Page343.htm |title=Klootschieten Wat is Dat |publisher=kvwarnsveld.nl |access-date=February 1, 2017}} < /ref >  Two teams play at a time. < ref name=kvwarnsveld/ >  Each team acts as the other's [[jury]]. < ref name=kvwarnsveld/ >
