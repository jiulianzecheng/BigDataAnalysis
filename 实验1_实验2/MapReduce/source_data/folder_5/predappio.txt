 '''Predappio''' is a ''[[comune]]'' in the [[Province of Forlì-Cesena]] in the [[Emilia-Romagna]] region in [[Italy]]. The town is the birthplace and resting place of [[Benito Mussolini]], dictator of Italy from 1922 to 1943.
