 '''Lamballe''' is a former [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is found in the [[Regions of France|region]] [[Bretagne]] in the [[Côtes-d'Armor]] [[Departments of France|department]] in the west of [[France]]. On 1 January 2019, it was merged into the new commune of [[Lamballe-Armor]]. < ref > [https://www.legifrance.gouv.fr/eli/arrete/2018/10/31/TERB1832578A/jo/texte Arrêté préfectoral] 31 October 2018 {{in lang|fr}} < /ref >
