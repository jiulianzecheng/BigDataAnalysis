 '''Berat''' is a town in south-central [[Albania]].  It is the capital of both the [[District of Berat]] and the larger [[Berat County|County of Berat]]. About 71,000 people live here. < ref name= " Instat of Albania " / >  Berat is on the right bank of the river [[Osum (Albania)|Osum]].
