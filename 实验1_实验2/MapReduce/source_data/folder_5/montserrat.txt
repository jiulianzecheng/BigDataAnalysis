'''Montserrat''' is a [[Caribbean]] [[island]] and a [[British Overseas Territories|British Overseas Territory]]. Its [[governor]] is [[Deborah Barnes-Jones]]. Officially, the capital is [[Plymouth, Montserrat|Plymouth]], but the government has moved to [[Brades]] after [[Chances Peak]] erupted, destroying Plymouth in 1995.
