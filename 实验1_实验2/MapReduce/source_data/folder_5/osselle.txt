 '''Osselle''' is a former [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is in [[Bourgogne-Franche-Comté]] in the [[Doubs]] [[Departments of France|department]] in east [[France]]. On 1 January 2016, it was merged into the new commune of [[Osselle-Routelle]]. < ref > [http://www.doubs.gouv.fr/content/download/14292/106983/file/recueil-25-2015-004-recueil-des-actes-administratifs.pdf Arrêté préfectoral] 21 December 2015 {{in lang|fr}} < /ref >
