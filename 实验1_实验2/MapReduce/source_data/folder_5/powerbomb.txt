 This page explains different types of '''powerbombs''' in [[professional wrestling]]. 
 
 == Gutwrench powerbomb == 
 This [[wikt:maneuver|maneuver]] has a wrestler tuck an opponent's head in-between their legs, hook their arms lift them up, turning them in mid-air, and slam them down to the mat (ring).
