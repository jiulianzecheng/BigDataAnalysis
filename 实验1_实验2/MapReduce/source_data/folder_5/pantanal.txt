 The '''Pantanal''' is the [[world]]'s largest [[wetland]] or [[swamp]]. < ref name = ramsar > {{cite web|url = http://www.ramsar.org/wwd/7/wwd2007_rpts_germany_gnf.htm|title = Pantanal in South America declared ”Threatened Lake of the Year 2007“|publisher = Ramsar Convention Secretariat|date = February 2007|accessdate = 2008-10-24}} < /ref > 
 
 It covers a region of 140,000 & nbsp;km < sup > 2 < /sup > , larger than the area covered by the country [[Greece]]. < ref name = ramsar/ > 
 
 The Pantanal is in [[South America]] and shared by [[Brazil]], [[Bolivia]] and [[Paraguay]]. < ref name = ramsar/ > 
 
 There are many threats to the Pantanal such as [[pollution]], [[Mercury (element)|mercury]] from [[gold]] [[mining]] and [[ranching]]. Although the Pantanal can wash some of the [[chemical]]s away, the increased amount is becoming a concern. 
 
 In [[winter]] nearly 80% of the area is [[flood]]ed. < ref name = wwf > {{cite web|url = http://www.worldwildlife.org/wildworld/profiles/terrestrial/nt/nt0907_full.html|title = Pantanal (NT0907)|work = Ecoregion profile: Neotropical  >  Flooded Grasslands and Savannas |publisher = Worl Wildlife Fund|first = Tim|last = Male|date = 2001|accessdate = 2008-10-24}} < /ref >
