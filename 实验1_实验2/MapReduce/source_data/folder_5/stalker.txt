 *A [[short story]] based on the novel, called ''Stalker'' 
 *A 1979 movie by [[Andrei Tarkovsky]], called ''[[Stalker (movie)|Stalker]]'' based on the novel ''Roadside Picnic'' 
 *''[[S.T.A.L.K.E.R.: Shadow of Chernobyl]]'', a [[computer game]]
