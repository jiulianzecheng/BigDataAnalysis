{{technical}} 
 '''Mineralocorticoids''' are  [[corticosteroid]]s, a type of [[steroid hormone]]. Mineralocorticoids are produced in the [[adrenal gland|adrenal cortex]]. They influence salt and water balances ([[electrolyte]] balance and fluid balance). The main mineralocorticoid is [[aldosterone]].
