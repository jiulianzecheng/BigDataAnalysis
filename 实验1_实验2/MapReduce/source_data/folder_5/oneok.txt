'''ONEOK, Inc.''' (pronounced like 'one oak') ({{nyse|OKE}}) is a diversified Fortune 200 corporation based in [[Tulsa, Oklahoma]].  Founded in [[1906]] as '''Oklahoma Natural Gas Company''', it is one of the largest natural gas distributors in the United States.
