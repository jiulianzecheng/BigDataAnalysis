 '''Spatzenhausen''' is a [[municipality]] in the district of [[Garmisch-Partenkirchen (district)|Garmisch-Partenkirchen]] in [[Upper Bavaria]] in [[Germany]]. 
 
 == Districts == 
 Spatzenhausen has three districts: 
 
 * Hofheim 
 * Spatzenhausen 
 * Waltersberg
