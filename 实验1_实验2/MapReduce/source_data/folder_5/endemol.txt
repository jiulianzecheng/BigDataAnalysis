 '''Endemol''' was a media [[company]] based in the [[Netherlands]] that produced [[television programme|programs]] for [[television]]. < ref > {{Cite web|url=https://www.tvweek.com/tvbizwire/2018/07/bidders-line-up-for-endemol-shine/|title=Bidders Line Up for Endemol Shine {{!}} TVWeek|language=en-US|access-date=2019-03-07}} < /ref >  The company had [[subsidiaries]] and [[joint ventures]] in 23 [[country|countries]]. These countries included the [[United Kingdom]], the [[United States]], [[France]], [[Mexico]], [[Spain]], [[Italy]], [[Germany]], [[Argentina]], [[Poland]], [[Netherlands]], [[India]], [[South Africa]], [[Lebanon]], [[Morocco]] and [[Australia]] among others. 
 
 Endemol was [[:wikt:found|founded]] in 1994 with the combining of the television companies owned by [[Joop van den Ende]] and [[John de Mol]]. The name of the company comes from the combination of their [[surnames]] (Ende + Mol). The company is best known for making [[reality television|reality programs]]. One of its best known programs is the reality series ''[[Big Brother (TV series)|Big Brother]]''. < ref > {{Cite web|url=https://www.proactiveinvestors.co.uk/companies/news/205326/itv-very-sensible-at-first-investor-day-in-11-years-says-barclays-as-it-cuts-target-price-205326.html|title=ITV 'very sensible' at first investor day in 11 years, says Barclays as it cuts target price|date=2018-09-20|website=Proactiveinvestors UK|language=en|access-date=2019-03-07}} < /ref >  This [[serie]]s has over 40 different version which are shown in over 100 countries. The company also makes many different [[game show]]s such as ''[[Deal or No Deal]]'', ''[[Fear Factor]]'' and ''[[Anime de Training EX]]''. It also creates the popular series ''[[Extreme Makeover: Home Edition]]''. 
 
 Endemol was bought by Shine Group (a joint-venture between [[:en:The_Walt_Disney_Company|The Walt Disney Company]] and [[:en:Apollo_Global_Management|Apollo Global Management]]) and was headquartered in the Netherlands in 2015 ad [[Endemol Shine Group]]. < ref > {{Cite web|last1=Andreeva|first1=Nellie|last2=Andreeva|first2=Nellie|date=2014-12-17|title=Endemol, Shine  &  Core Joint Venture Gets Name, Sets More Top Execs, Merges Distribution  &  International Operations|url=https://deadline.com/2014/12/endemol-shine-core-media-joint-venture-name-executives-distribution-international-1201328128/|access-date=2021-05-28|website=Deadline|language=en-US}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite web|title=Fox-Apollo TV Merger Joins 'Idol' With 'Biggest Loser'|url=https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2014-05-15/fox-s-shine-to-join-apollo-s-endemol-core-media-in-tv-venture|access-date=2021-05-28|website=bloomberg.com}} < /ref >
