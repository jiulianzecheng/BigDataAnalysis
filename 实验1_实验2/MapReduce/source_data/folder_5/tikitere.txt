 '''Tikitere''', also known as ''Heel's Gate'' is an outer suburb of [[Rotorua]]'s most active geothermal area on State Highway 30, between [[Lake Rotorua]] and [[Lake Rotoiti]] in the [[Bay of Plenty]]. It has an area school located in the area.
