 '''Alanine''' (abbreviated as '''Ala''' or '''A''') < ref > Nomenclature and symbolism for amino acids and peptides. IUPAC-IUB Recommendations 1983. ''Pure Appl. Chem''. '''56''' (5), 1984: 595–624. < /ref >  is an α-[[amino acid]]. It has the [[chemical formula]] CH < sub > 3 < /sub > CH(NH < sub > 2 < /sub > )COOH.  
 
 The  < small > L < /small > -[[isomer]] is one of the 20 [[amino acid]]s encoded by the [[genetic code]]. Its codons are GCU, GCC, GCA, and GCG. It is classified as a [[non-polar]] amino acid. < ref > Non-polar: electrons shared so that there is no difference in electric charge between different parts of the molecule.  < /ref >
