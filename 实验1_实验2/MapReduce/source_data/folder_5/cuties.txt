 '''''Cuties''''' ([[French language|French]]: '''''Mignonnes''''') is a 2020 [[France|French]] [[coming-of-age movie|coming-of-age]] [[comedy-drama]] [[movie]]. It was written and directed by [[Maïmouna Doucouré]]. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Cuties|url=http://new.bacfilms.com/international/movies/mignonnes|access-date=12 June 2020|publisher=[[BAC Films]]|archive-date=12 June 2020|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200612093629/http://new.bacfilms.com/international/movies/mignonnes|url-status=live}} < /ref >  The movie stars Fathia Youssouf, Médina El Aidi-Azouni, Esther Gohourou, Ilanah Cami-Goursolas and Maïmouna Gueye. The movie is about a French-Senegalese [[Muslim]] girl who becomes interested in [[internet culture]], even though a lot of it is against her religion. The film was first shown at the [[2020 Sundance Film Festival]] on 23 January 2020. It was released in France on 19 August 2020, and to the rest of the world on 9 September 2020 on [[Netflix]]. < ref name= " SD " > {{Cite web|last=Halligan|first=Fionnuala|date=24 January 2020|title='Cuties': Sundance Review|url=https://www.screendaily.com/reviews/cuties-sundance-review/5146293.article|access-date=12 June 2020|website=[[Screen Daily]]|archive-date=24 January 2020|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200124065122/https://www.screendaily.com/reviews/cuties-sundance-review/5146293.article|url-status=live}} < /ref > < ref name= " THR " > {{Cite web|title='Cuties' ('Mignonnes'): Film Review {{!}} Sundance 2020|url=https://www.hollywoodreporter.com/review/cuties-mignonnes-review-sundance-2020-1271494|access-date=12 June 2020|work=[[The Hollywood Reporter]]|date=23 January 2020 |archive-date=24 June 2020|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200624104619/https://www.hollywoodreporter.com/review/cuties-mignonnes-review-sundance-2020-1271494|url-status=live}} < /ref > 
 
 Critics mainly said that the movie was good, but many people online did not like the movie as they thought it [[child pornography|showed children in a sexual way]].
