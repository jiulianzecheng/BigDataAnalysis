 '''Quetzaltenango''' ({{IPA-es|ketsalteˈnaŋɡo}}, also known by its Maya name, '''Xelajú''' or '''Xela''' {{IPA|[ˈʃela]}}), is a [[city]] in [[Guatemala]]. It is the [[Capital city|capital]] of the [[Quetzaltenango Department]]. In 2018, 180,706 people lived there.
