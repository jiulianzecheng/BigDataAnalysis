 '''Quirihue''' ({{IPA-es|kiˈɾiwe}}) is a [[Chile]]an [[List of cities in Chile|city]] in [[Quirihue, Chile|Quirihue]], [[Itata Province]], [[Ñuble Region]]. 
 
 It is about 72 kilometers northwest of [[Chillán]] and 410 km southwest of [[Santiago]].
