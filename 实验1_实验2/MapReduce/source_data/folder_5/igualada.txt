 '''Igualada''' is a [[municipality]] of the [[province of Barcelona]] in  [[Spain]]. It is located on the left bank of the [[Anoia river]]. 
 
 The city is {{convert|65|km|0|abbr=off}} west from the city of [[Barcelona]]. It is {{convert|20|km|0|abbr=off}} west from the famous [[Montserrat (mountain)|Montserrat]] mountain.
