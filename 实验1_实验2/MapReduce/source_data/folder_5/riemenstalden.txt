 '''Riemenstalden''' is a [[List of municipalities in Switzerland|municipality]] in [[Schwyz (district)|Schwyz District]] in the [[canton of Schwyz]] in [[Switzerland]]. The villages can be reached by road from [[Sisikon]] ([[Canton of Uri]]). 
 
 [[Alplersee]] is on the slopes of [[Rophaien]].
