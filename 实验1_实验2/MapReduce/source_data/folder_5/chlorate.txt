'''Chlorate''' is an [[ion]]. Its chemical formula is ClO < sub > 3 < /sub > < sup > - < /sup > . It contains [[chlorine]] in the +5 [[oxidation state]]. It is a strong [[oxidizing agent]]. It exists in [[chemical compound]]s such as [[potassium chlorate]]. Many chlorates produce [[oxygen]] when they are heated. Chlorates are [[salt]]s of [[chloric acid]].
