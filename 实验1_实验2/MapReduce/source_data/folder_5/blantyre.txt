 '''Blantyre''' is the second largest city in [[Malawi]]. In 2008, 661,256 people lived there. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://www.nso.malawi.net/images/stories/data_on_line/demography/census_2008/Main%20Report/ThematicReports/Spatial%20Distribution%20and%20Urbanisation.pdf |title=Spatial Distribution and Urbanisation |access-date=2011-11-06 |date=2008 |format=PDF |work=Census 2008: Main Report |publisher=National Statistical Office |page=9 }}{{Dead link|date=January 2021 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }} < /ref >  It is the center of [[commerce]] and [[finance]] in Malawi. Blantyre is the [[capital (city)|capital]] of the [[Soither Region, Malawi|Southern Region]] and of [[Blantyre District]].
