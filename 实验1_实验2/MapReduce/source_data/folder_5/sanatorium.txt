 A '''sanatorium''' (also spelled '''sanitarium''' or '''sanitorium''') is a [[Hospital|medical facility]] for long-term [[illness]]. They were most often used for the treatment of [[tuberculosis]] (TB) in the late-nineteenth and early-twentieth century before the discovery of [[antibiotics]]. Sometimes, the word is used to describe different things. For example, in eastern [[Europe]], a   " [[Sanatorium (resort)|sanatorium]] "  is a type of health resort such as the [[Battle Creek Sanitarium]].
