 '''Miltenberg''' ({{IPA-de|ˈmɪltn̩ˌbɛʁk|lang|De-Miltenberg.ogg}}) is a town in the [[Lower Franconia]] [[Regierungsbezirk|region]] in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. It is the seat of the [[Miltenberg (district)|like-named district]]. 
 
 == Geography == 
 [[File:Miltenberg AltesRathaus Mildenburg 2003.jpg|thumb|left|Historic old town and ''Mildenburg'']] 
 
 === Place === 
 The old town is on the [[Main (river)|Main]]’s left edge on the  " left knee "  of the ''Mainviereck'' ( " Main Square " ) between the [[Spessart]] and [[Odenwald]] ranges. Since the Main riverbed in the Miltenberg area is relatively near the foot of the Odenwald, only a narrow strip of usable land is left, about over 150 meters in width, < ref name= " Dumont " > {{cite book|last=Dettelbacher|first=Werner|title=Franken - Kunst, Geschichte und Landschaft (German)|publisher=Dumont Verlag|year=1974|isbn=3-7701-0746-2}} < /ref > {{rp|79}} which in past centuries was time and again flooded by the Main.
