 '''Lambach''' is a [[market town]] in the [[Wels-Land]] district of [[Upper Austria]], [[Austria]]. It is on the [[Ager]] and [[Traun]] Rivers. It had about 3,242 peopleas of 2001. A major stop on the [[Salt Trade|salt trade]], it is the site of the [[Lambach Abbey]], built around 1056. 
 
 Between 1897 and 1898 lived [[Adolf Hitler]] there and attented the school.
