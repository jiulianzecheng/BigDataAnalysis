 '''''Mogambo''''' is a 1953 American British romantic adventure movie directed by [[John Ford]] is based on the play ''[[Red Dust]]'' by [[Wilson Collison]] and had been previously filmed in 1932. It stars [[Clark Gable]], [[Ava Gardner]], [[Grace Kelly]], [[Donald Sinden]] and was distributed by [[Metro-Goldwyn-Mayer]]. It was nominated for 2 Academy Awards in 1954.
