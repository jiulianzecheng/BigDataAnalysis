 '''Kalasha''', also spelled as '''Kalash''' ([[Sanskrit]]: {{lang|sa|कलश}}, ''kalaśa''; literally  " pitcher, pot " ), is a [[metal]] ([[brass]], [[copper]], [[silver]] or [[gold]]) pot with a large base and small [[mouth]], large enough to hold a [[coconut]]. Sometimes  " Kalasha "  can mean a pot filled with [[water]] and topped with a [[coronet]] of [[mango]] leaves and a coconut.  This [[combination]] is often used in [[Hinduism|Hindu]] [[rite]]s.
