 '''Dacarbizine''' is a type of drug used to fight [[cancer]].  It works by changing [[DNA]], which makes cancer cells grow more slowly or completely stop growing. < ref name= " about " > {{cite web|url=http://skincancer.about.com/od/treatmentoptions/a/melanoma_chemo.htm|title=Cancer Chemotherapy for Melanoma: Dacarbazine|publisher=About.com|date=5 May 2009|access-date=4 February 2011|archive-date=14 October 2011|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20111014092500/http://skincancer.about.com/od/treatmentoptions/a/melanoma_chemo.htm|url-status=dead}} < /ref >  It is used against [[metastatic melanoma]]. < ref name= " about " / >   Only about 15.3% of people who use the drug respond to it. < ref name= " about " / >   People who use this drug do not live any longer as a result. < ref name= " about " / > 
 
 It has [[Adverse effect (medicine)|side effects]], including less [[blood cells]], damage to the [[liver]].  I can also sometimes make other types of cancer start growing. < ref name= " about " / >
