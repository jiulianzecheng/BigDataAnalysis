 '''Miitopia''' is a [[Role-playing game|role playing]] [[video game]] for the [[Nintendo 3DS]], [[Nintendo 2DS|2DS]] and [[Nintendo Switch|Switch.]] Players can be one of many class of [[Mii]] [[fictional character|characters]]. These include [[Mage]], [[Princess]], [[Warrior]], [[Flower]], [[Clerical script|Cleric]] and [[Vampire]]. The goal of the game is to defeat the Dark Lord and become the world's greatest [[hero]]. To do this, the player must defeat many [[Monster|monsters]]. They can get new powers whenever they defeat a monster. This is called  " Leveling Up " . Each class has its own powers. For example, if the character is a Mage, they can get [[Witchcraft|spells]] such as Fire or Lightning.         
 
 == Plot == 
 The game starts with the Hero walking around Greenhorne. Meanwhile, the Dark Lord begun to steal [[Mii]] faces. When the hero gets to the town of Greenhorne, the Dark Lord steals the the faces of the townspeople. The hero then goes to save the faces of the townspeople.
