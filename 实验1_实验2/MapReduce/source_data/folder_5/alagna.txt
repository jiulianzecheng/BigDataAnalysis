 '''Alagna''' is a ''[[comune]]'' (township) in the [[Province of Pavia]] in the [[Italy|Italian]] region [[Lombardy]], which is about 40 & nbsp;km southwest of [[Milan]] and about 20 & nbsp;km west of [[Pavia]]. As of 31 December 2004, it had 876 people and an area of 8.6 & nbsp;kmÂ². < ref name= " istat " > All demographics and other statistics: Italian statistical institute [[Istat]]. < /ref > 
 
 Alagna is on the edge of the following townships: [[Dorno]], [[Garlasco]], [[Tromello]], [[Valeggio, Lombardy|Valeggio]].
