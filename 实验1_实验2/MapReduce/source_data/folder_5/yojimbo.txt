 {{nihongo|'''''Yojimbo'''''|用心棒|Yōjinbō| " Bodyguard " }} is a 1961 Japanese action drama movie directed by [[Akira Kurosawa]] and is followed up by the 1962 movie ''[[Sanjuro]]''. It stars [[Toshiro Mifune]], [[Tatsuya Nakadai]], [[Yoko Tsukasa]], [[Isuzu Yamada]], [[Daisuke Katō]] and was distributed by [[Toho]]. It was nominated for an Academy Award in 1962.
