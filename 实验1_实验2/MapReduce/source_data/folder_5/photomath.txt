'''Photomath''' is a mobile app described as a  " calculator camera " < ref > {{Cite web|date=2018-12-22|title=PhotoMath App Solves Math Equations with Smartphone Text Recognition {{!}} Time|url=http://time.com/3531375/photomath-app-smart-camera-calculator/|access-date=2022-08-01|website=web.archive.org|archive-date=2018-12-22|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181222042126/http://time.com/3531375/photomath-app-smart-camera-calculator/|url-status=bot: unknown}} < /ref >  that uses the phone's camera to recognize math equations and display a step-by-step solution on the screen. The app was released in October 2014.
