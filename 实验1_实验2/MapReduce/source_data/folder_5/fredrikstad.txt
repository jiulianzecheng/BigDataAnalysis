 '''Fredrikstad''' is a city and municipality in [[Viken (county)|Viken]] county, [[Norway]]. The administrative centre of the municipality is the city of Fredrikstad. In 2021, 83,761 people lived there. 
 
 The city of Fredrikstad was founded in 1567 by [[Frederick II of Denmark|King Frederick II]]. It is at the mouth of the river [[Glomma]].
