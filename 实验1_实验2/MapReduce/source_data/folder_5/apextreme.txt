'''ApeXtreme''' is a [[video game console|console]] created by Apex Digital Inc. It was released January 9, 2004. 
 
 It can do many things, like play computer games, [[DVD]]s, [[Compact disc|CDs]], [[VCD]]s and use the [[Internet]]. 
 
 What it can do: 
 * games 
 * videos 
 * pictures 
 * slide shows 
 * music 
 * DVD 
 * VCD 
 * CD 
 * MP3 
 * Internet radio
