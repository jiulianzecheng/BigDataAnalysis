'''Contoire''' is a former [[Communes of France|commune]] in the [[Somme]] [[Departments of France|department]] in [[Picardie]] in northern [[France]]. On 1 January 2019, it was merged into the new commune of [[Trois-Rivières, Somme|Trois-Rivières]]. < ref > [https://www.legifrance.gouv.fr/eli/arrete/2018/11/12/TERB1832591A/jo/texte Arrêté préfectoral] 12 November 2018 {{in lang|fr}} < /ref >
