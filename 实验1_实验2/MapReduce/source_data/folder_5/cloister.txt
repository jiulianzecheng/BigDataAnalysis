 A '''Cloister''' is a term in [[architecture]]. It describes a structure which is usually found in [[monastery|monasteries]], [[church]]es and old [[college]]s. It is a [[Rectangle|rectangular]] open space surrounded by covered walks or open galleries. It has open [[arcade]]s on the inner side, running along the walls of buildings and forming a [[quadrangle]].
