 A '''snickerdoodle''' is a type of cookie made with [[flour]], [[butter]] or [[Cooking oil|oil]], [[sugar]], [[salt]], and rolled in [[Cinnamon sugar|cinnamon sugar.]] Eggs are sometimes used, with [[cream of tartar]] and [[baking soda]] added to leaven the dough. They are characterized by a cracked surface, and can be crispy or soft. 
 
 Snickerdoodles are sometimes called [[Sugar cookie| " sugar cookies " .]] But, sugar cookies are rolled in white sugar while snickerdoodles are rolled in a mixture of white sugar and cinnamon.
