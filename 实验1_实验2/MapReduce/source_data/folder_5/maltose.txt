 '''Maltose''' (known as malt [[sugar]]) is a [[disaccharide]] (sometimes called di-glucose). It is formed from two [[glucose]] [[molecule]]s joined at [[carbon]]s 1 and 4. It is found in [[germination|germinating]] [[seeds]] such as [[barley]] as they break down their [[starch]] stores to use for food.
