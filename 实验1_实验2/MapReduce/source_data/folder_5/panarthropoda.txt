 The '''Panarthropoda''' are a clade of [[ecdysozoa]]ns that consist of [[Dinocaridida]],  " [[Lobopodia]] " , [[Onychophora]], and [[Tactopoda]]. Most members of this group have claws and legs, and a segmented body.
