A '''homeland''' (rel. ''[[country of origin]]'' and '''native land''') is the concept of the territory ([[cultural geography]]) to which an [[ethnic group]] holds a long history and a deep cultural association with. It's the [[country]] in which a particular [[nationality|national identity]] began.
