 '''Wingersheim''' is a former [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is in [[Grand Est]] in the [[Bas-Rhin]] [[Departments of France|department]] in northeast [[France]]. On 1 January 2016, it was merged into the new commune of [[Wingersheim-les-Quatre-Bans]]. < ref > [http://www.legifrance.gouv.fr/jo_pdf.do?id=JORFTEXT000031701834 Arrêté] 21 December 2015 {{in lang|fr}} < /ref >
