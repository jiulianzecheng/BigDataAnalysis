'''Palakkad''' is a city in the state of [[Kerala]], [[India]]. It is the administrative capital of [[Palakkad District]]. Palakkad has the seventh most people in Kerala. [[Malayalam]] is the most spoken language in Palakkad city. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://palakkad.nic.in/|title=Palakkad - Website of Palakkad|publisher=}} < /ref > 
 
 ==Population== 
 Palakkad had 130,955 people as of the 2011 Census of India. The [[literacy]] rate of the city is 94.20%. The metro area of Palakkad has  293,566 people. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.census2011.co.in/data/town/803275-palakkad-kerala.html|title=Palakkad Municipality City Population Census 2011-2019 - Kerala|website=www.census2011.co.in}} < /ref >
