 '''Dholavira''' ({{lang-gu|ધોળાવીરા}}) is an [[archaeological site]]. It is at Khadirbet in Bhachau Taluka from the [[Kutch District]]. It is in the state of [[Gujarat]] in [[western India]]. It took its name from a modern-day village {{Convert|1|km||adj=pre| & nbsp;}} south of it.
