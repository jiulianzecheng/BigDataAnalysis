 '''Siguatepeque''' ({{IPA-es|siɣwateˈpeke}}) is a city and municipality in the [[Departments of Honduras|Honduran department]] of [[Comayagua Department|Comayagua]].  The city has a population of 73,480 (2020 calculation). < ref name= " citypop " > {{cite web |title=Honduras: Administrative Division: Departments and Municipalities |url=https://www.citypopulation.de/en/honduras/admin/ |publisher=Thomas Brinkhoff: City Population |access-date=31 May 2021}} < /ref > 
 
 ==Geography== 
 Siguatepeque is 1100 metres above sea level. It is in the central mountains of [[Honduras]]. 
 
 == Climate data == 
 In Siguatepeque has a [[Humid subtropical climate]] ([[Köppen climate classification]] ''Cwa'').
