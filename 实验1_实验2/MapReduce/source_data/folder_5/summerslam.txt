[[File:Madison Square Garden, 2005.jpg|thumb|right|[[Madison Square Garden]] has hosted SummerSlam three times.]] 
 '''SummerSlam''' is a yearly [[professional wrestling]] [[pay-per-view]] (PPV) event held in [[August]] by [[World Wrestling Entertainment]] (WWE). The event is often called  " The Biggest Party of the Summer. " < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.wwe.com/inside/news/archive/summerslam06shoot|title=Let the Party Begin|author=Louie Dee|publisher=WWE.com|accessdate=2008-05-12|date=May 17, 2006}} < /ref >  Along with [[WrestleMania (PPV series)|WrestleMania]], [[Royal Rumble]] and [[Survivor Series]], SummerSlam is one of the  " Big Four "  pay-per-views and was one of the original four yearly WWE pay-per-views. < ref name=sa94 > Assael, Shaun. ''Sex, Lies,  &  Headlocks'', 94–95. < /ref >
