 '''Fatimah''' ({{lang-ar|فاطمہ}},  born 605 or 615 < ref name= " Ordoni 1990 pp.42-45 " > Ordoni (1992) pp.42-45 < /ref >  – d. 633) was the youngest daughter of the [[Prophets of Islam|Islamic prophet]] [[Muhammad]] and [[Khadijah bint Khuwaylid|Khadija]]. Fatima's husband was [[Ali]]. < ref > {{Cite web|title=the marriage of lady fatimah with imam ali|url=http://almerja.com/en/more.php?pid=1583|access-date=2023-11-15|website=almerja.com|language=en}} < /ref >  She is often viewed as an ultimate role model for Muslim women and an example of [[compassion]], generosity, and enduring suffering. The [[Fatimid Caliphate]] claimed to be her descendants. Her name and her [[epithets]] remain popular choices for Muslim girls.
