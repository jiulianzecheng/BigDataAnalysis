 A '''whirlpool''' is a body of [[Rotation|rotating]] water. It is usually produced by [[ocean]] [[tide]]s. A bigger whirlpool is called a [[maelstrom]]. It is also produced by a [[Ocean current|current]] running into an obstacle or opposing currents.  
 
 == Whirlpools as a threat == 
 Many stories tell of [[Ship|ships]] being sucked into a maelstrom, although only smaller [[Boat|craft]] are actually in danger. If you swim close to it, it will bring you [[underwater]]. You will [[Drowning|drown]] and surface after a few seconds.
