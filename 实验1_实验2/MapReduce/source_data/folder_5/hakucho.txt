 {{nihongo|'''Hakucho'''|はくちょう||}} is the name of a [[Japan]]ese artificial [[satellite]] which was put into [[orbit]].  
 
 The launch was a project of the [[Institute of Space and Astronautical Science]] of the [[University of Tokyo]]. 
 
 ''Hakucho'' was sent into orbit on February 21, 1979.  It reentered the atmosphere and melted on April 15, 1985. < ref > JAXA, [http://www.isas.ac.jp/e/enterp/missions/catalogue.shtml   " Catalogue of ISAS Missions " ] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20141231025049/http://www.isas.ac.jp/e/enterp/missions/catalogue.shtml |date=2014-12-31 }}; retrieved 2012-4-2. < /ref >     
 
 This was [[Japan]]'s first [[x-ray]] astronomy satellite.
