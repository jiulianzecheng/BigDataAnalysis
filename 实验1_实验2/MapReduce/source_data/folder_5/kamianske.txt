 '''Kamianske''' ({{lang-uk|Кам'янське}}) is a city in the [[Dnipropetrovsk Oblast]] ([[Oblast|province]]) in [[Ukraine]]. It is the [[administrative centre]] of [[Kamianske Raion]] ([[Raion|district]]). In 2020, 231,915 people lived there. It is an important industrial city. 
 
 From 1936 to 2016, the city was known as '''Dniprodzerzhynsk'''.
