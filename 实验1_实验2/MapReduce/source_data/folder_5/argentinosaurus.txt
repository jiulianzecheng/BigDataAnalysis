 '''''Argentinosaurus''''' was a [[titanosaur]]id [[sauropod]] [[dinosaur]]. It was an enormous, long-necked, long-tailed, quadrupedal, plant-eater from [[Argentina]], [[South America]] during the [[Cretaceous|Cretaceous period]]. 
 
 ''Argentinosaurus'' meaning  " Argentina lizard " , was named by paleontologists Coria  &  José Bonaparte in 1993. It is known from fossilized back vertebrae, [[tibia]], ribs and [[sacrum]], found in [[Neuquén Province]].
