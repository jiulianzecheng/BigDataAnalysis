 '''''Odax''''' is a genus of marine [[ray-finned fish]], [[Weed whiting|weed whitings]] from the family [[Odacidae]] which are native to the [[Pacific]] waters of [[New Zealand]]. 
 
 == Species == 
 There are currently two recognized species in this genus: 
 
 * ''[[Bluefinned butterfish|Odax cyanoallix]]''  < small > Ayling  &  Paxton, 1983 < /small >  (Bluefinned butterfish) 
 * ''[[Greenbone|Odax pullus]]''  < small > (J. R. Forster, 1801) < /small >  (Greenbone)
