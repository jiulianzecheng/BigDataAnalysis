 '''Karesuando''' ({{lang-fi|Kaaresuvanto}} or ''{{lang|fi|Karesuvanto}}'', [[Sami language|Sami]]: ''Karesuanto'', ''Gárasavvon'' or ''Karasavvon'') is a [[urban areas of Sweden|locality]] in [[Kiruna Municipality]] in [[Norrbotten County]] in [[Sweden]]. It is the northernmost locality in Sweden. In 2010, 303 people lived there. < ref name=scb / >  In 2011, 350 people lived there. < ref name=karesuandowebsite / >
