 '''Westervoort''' is a municipality and town in the province of [[Gelderland]], the Netherlands. About 15,000 people were living there in 2021. 
 
 It lies both in the [[IJsselvallei]] and [[Liemers]] regions, at the meeting point of the [[Rhine]] and [[IJssel]] rivers.
