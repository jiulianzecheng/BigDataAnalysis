 '''Aurangabad''' is a city in in the [[Aurangabad District (Bihar)|Aurangabad district]] in [[India]]. It is famous for a cement plant and World Best [[Deo Surya Mandir]]. There are two main languages spoken in Aurangabad - [[Marathi language|Marathi]] and [[Hindi language|Hindi]].
