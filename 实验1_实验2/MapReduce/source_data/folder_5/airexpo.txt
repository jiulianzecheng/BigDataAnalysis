'''Airexpo''' is a [[France|French]] [[airshow]] created in 1987. It is the third most important airshow of [[France]]. < ref > {{in lang|fr}} [http://midi-pyrenees.france3.fr/info/airexpo-le-plus-grand-meeting-du-sud-ouest-73871361.html Airexpo, le plus grand meeting du sud-ouest] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120515094654/http://midi-pyrenees.france3.fr/info/airexpo-le-plus-grand-meeting-du-sud-ouest-73871361.html |date=2012-05-15 }} < /ref >  It is organized by the students of the [[universities]] ''[[École nationale de l'aviation civile]]'' and those of the ''[[Institut supérieur de l'aéronautique et de l'espace]]''. < ref > {{in lang|fr}} [http://www.defense.gouv.fr/marine/au-fil-de-l-eau/26eme-edition-du-meeting-aerien-airexpo-sur-l-aerodrome-de-muret-lherm-31 26ème édition du meeting aérien Airexpo sur l’aérodrome de Muret-Lherm (31)] < /ref >  It is yearly organized and most of the time on the [[Muret - Lherm Aerodrome]]. < ref > {{in lang|fr}} [http://www.ladepeche.fr/article/2009/02/21/562636-Air-expo-fait-sont-retour-a-Muret.htm Air-expo fait sont retour à Muret]{{Dead link|date=September 2021 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }} < /ref > 
 
 == Presentation == 
 The aircraft are representative of the world of aviation, from the Supermarine Spitfire to the F-18 Super Hornet, through the little Robin DR400. Proof of its quality and originality, the airliners are also regularly represented with the participation of [[Airbus]] and ATR, with the [[Airbus A380]] < ref > {{in lang|fr}} [http://www.ladepeche.fr/article/2012/05/12/1351265-airexpo-l-a380-en-vedette.html Muret. Airexpo : l'A380 en vedette] < /ref >  and A340-600 or the ATR 72-500.
