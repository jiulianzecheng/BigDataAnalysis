 '''Baltrum''' ({{lang-nds|Baltrum}}) is a [[barrier island]] off the coast of [[East Frisia]] ({{Lang-de|Ostfriesland}}), in [[Germany]], and is a municipality in [[Aurich (district)|Aurich]], [[Lower Saxony]]. It is in-between the chain of the seven inhabited [[East Frisian Islands]]. It has a land area of 6.5 square kilometres. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 It is in the middle of the [[island chain]] known as the [[East Frisian Islands]] ({{Lang-de|Ostfriesische Inseln}}). There are two villages on the island – ''Ostdorf'' ({{Lang-en|Eastern village}}) and ''Westdorf'' ({{Lang-en|Western village}}) – although they have essentially merged into one. 
 
 The island is about {{Convert|5|km|mi}} long and {{Convert|1.5|km|mi}} wide. It covers an area of {{Convert|6.5|km2|sqmi}}. The highest point on the island is a central [[Coastal dunes|coastal dune]] with a viewing platform on top called ''Aussichtsdüne'' in [[German language|German]] and is {{Convert|19.3|m|ft}} above [[sea-level]]. < ref >
