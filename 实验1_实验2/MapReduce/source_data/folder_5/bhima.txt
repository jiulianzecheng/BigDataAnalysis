 '''Bhima''' ({{lang-sa|भीम}}, [[IAST]]: Bhīma) is the second son of the [[Pandavas]]. The ''[[Mahabharata]]'' has many events which shows the strength of Bhima. Bhima is responsible for killing all hundred [[Kaurava]] brothers in the [[Kurukshetra War]]. He was considered to have the physical strength of around 10,000 elephants.
