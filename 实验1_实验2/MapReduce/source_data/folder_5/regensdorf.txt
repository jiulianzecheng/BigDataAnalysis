 '''Regensdorf''' is a [[municipality]] in the [[district]] of [[Dielsdorf (district)|Dielsdorf]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Zurich (canton)|Zurich]] in [[Switzerland]]. It is the largest city in the [[Furttal]] region. 
 
 [[Katzensee]] is a lake that also includes the bath/lido [[Strandbad Katzensee]] on the border with the [[Affoltern (Zürich)|Affoltern]] quarter of the city of [[Zürich]].
