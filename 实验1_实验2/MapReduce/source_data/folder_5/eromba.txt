 '''Eromba''' is a [[Meitei cuisine|Meitei ethnic dish]], generally consisting of many pickling ingredients, among which [[chili]] is the main essential ingredient. It is a very popular dish in the [[States of India|Indian state]] of [[Manipur]], especially among the [[Manipuri ethnicity]].
