'''Sellafield''', formerly known as '''Windscale''', is a large  [[nuclear]] site  on the coast of [[Cumbria]], England.  Now it mostly does  [[nuclear waste]] processing and storage and nuclear [[decommissioning]]. It used to be a  [[nuclear power plant]] from 1956 to 2003, and nuclear fuel [[reprocessing]] from 1952 to 2022. It is a big place,  265 hectares (650 acres), and has more than 200 nuclear facilities and more than 1,000 buildings. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Job done: Sellafield plant safely completes its mission|url=https://www.gov.uk/government/news/job-done-sellafield-plant-safely-completes-its-mission|access-date=2023-03-23|website=GOV.UK|language=en}} < /ref >
