 '''''Impetigore''''' ({{lang-id|'''Perempuan Tanah Jahanam'''}}; lit. ''Woman of the Damned Land'') is a 2019 [[Indonesia|Indonesian]] [[horror]] [[thriller (genre)|thriller]] [[movie]] directed by [[Joko Anwar]] and starring [[Tara Basro]], [[Marissa Anita]], [[Asmara Abigail]], [[Christine Hakim]], [[Ario Bayu]], [[Teuku Rifnu Wikana]]. 
 
 ==Filming== 
 The movie was filmed in the villages around Malang, Gempol, Lumbang, Bromo, Lumajang, Ijen, and Banyuwangi.
