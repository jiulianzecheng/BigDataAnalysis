The '''Beatitudes''' (from Latin ''beatus'', meaning  " blessed "  or  " happy " < ref > {{cite web | url = http://www.etymonline.com/index.php?term=beatitude | title = beatitude | last = Harper | first = Douglas | work = Online Etymology Dictionary | access-date = 2008-09-09 }} < /ref > ) are a list of traits that [[Christians]] should follow told by [[Gospel of Matthew]] and [[Gospel of Luke]]. Some of these include [[wikt:humble|humbleness]], [[wikt:meek|meek]]ness, and making [[peace]].  
 
 [[Jesus Christ]] taught these ideas as part of a three lesson sermon; the [[Sermon on the Mount]].
