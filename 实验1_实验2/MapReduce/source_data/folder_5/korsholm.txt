 '''Korsholm''' ({{lang-fi|Mustasaari}}) is a [[municipality]] in western [[Finland]]. It was founded in 1606. It is in the [[Ostrobothnia (region)|Ostrobothnia region]]. Most of the people living there speak [[Swedish language|Swedish]].
