Hayatabad is a [[suburb]] in [[Peshawar]], the capital of the [[Khyber Pakhtunkhwa]] province of Pakistan. It was named after Hayat Sherpao, former Governor of Khyber Pakhtunkhwa and a famous leader of Pakistan Peoples Party. There are several different schools, colleges, a national University, public and private hospitals, private clinics, public parks etc.
