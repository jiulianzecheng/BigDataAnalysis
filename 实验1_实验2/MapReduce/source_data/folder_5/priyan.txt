 '''Priyan''' (April 1964  & ndash; 9 November 2017) was an Indian cinematographer. He has worked on Tamil language movies. He was born in [[Virudhunagar]], [[Tamil Nadu]]. His best known works are ''[[Porkkaalam]]'' (1997), ''[[Desiya Geetham]]'' (1998), ''[[Vetri Kodi Kattu]]'' (1999), ''[[Thenali]]'' (2000), ''[[Star (2001 movie)|Star]]'' (2001) and ''[[Majunu]]'' (2001).
