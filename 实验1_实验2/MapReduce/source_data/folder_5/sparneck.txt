 '''Sparneck''' is a [[Municipalities of Germany|municipality]] in Upper [[Franconia]] in [[Hof (district)|Hof]] in [[Bavaria]] in [[Germany]]. 
 
 == Geography == 
 The market town of Sparneck is at an average height of {{Höhe|560|DE-NN|link=true}} in the Pfarrbach valley, which makes the larger part of the area. The [[Saale|Saxon Saale]] river flows through this valley in a north direction. Towards the east the  877 metre high [[Waldstein (mountain range)|Waldstein ridge]] rises, the source of the Saale is on its slopes. It forms the natural boundary with the neighbouring district of [[Wunsiedel (district)|Wunsiedel]].
