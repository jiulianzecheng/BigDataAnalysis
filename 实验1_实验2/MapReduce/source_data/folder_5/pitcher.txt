 In [[baseball]], the '''pitcher''' is the player who throws 
 the [[baseball (ball)|baseball]] from the pitcher's [[mound]] to the [[catcher]] to begin each play, trying to [[out (baseball)|retire]] a [[batter (baseball)|batter]] who tries to get on base, usually by hitting the ball with their bat or by taking a [[walk (baseball)|walk]].
