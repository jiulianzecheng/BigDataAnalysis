 '''Mambo''' is a word with Afro-Latin connections. Most common meanings are: 
 
 *[[Mambo (rhythm)]]: a [[Cuban]] or AfroCuban [[music]]al [[rhythm]] and its associated [[dance]]. Invented around 1950 in [[Havana]]. 
 *[[Mambo section]]: a musical bridge or section in some kinds of Cuban (and [[Caribbean]]) music. 
 *[[Mambo (Voodoo)]]: A [[Haiti]]an word for a [[Voodoo]] priestess. 
 
 The word also occurs in the title of many pieces of recorded music.
