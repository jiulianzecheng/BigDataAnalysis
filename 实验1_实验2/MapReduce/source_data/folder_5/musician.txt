A '''musician''' is a person who plays a [[music]]al [[Musical instrument|instrument]] like a [[guitar]] or a [[piano]] or a person that [[singer|sing]]s. 
 
 A musician is also someone who writes music, even if they write it for other people to play. People who write music are called [[composer]]s. 
 
 Musicians can also make a group together to play songs.
