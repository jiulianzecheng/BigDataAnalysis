A '''houseboat''' is a vessel specifically designed to be a semipermanent dwelling. Houseboats tend to have [[engine]]s that allow them to move under their own power, just like most other modern vessels. [[Floating home]]s are sometimes called houseboats. 
 [[File:Kerala houseboats.jpg|thumb|Kerala houseboats]] 
 In some places houseboats are used by tourists.
