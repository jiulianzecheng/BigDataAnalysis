 '''Bellinghem''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is found in the [[Pas-de-Calais]] [[Departments of France|department]] of northern [[France]]. It was incorporated on 1 September 2016 by the combination of the former communes of [[Herbelles]] and [[Inghem]]. < ref > [https://www.legifrance.gouv.fr/eli/arrete/2016/5/31/INTB1617398A/jo/texte Arrêté préfectoral] 31 May 2016 {{in lang|fr}} < /ref >
