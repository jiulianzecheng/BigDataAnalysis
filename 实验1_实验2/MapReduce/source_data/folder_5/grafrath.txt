 '''Grafrath''' is a [[Municipalities of Germany|municipality]] in [[Fürstenfeldbruck (district)|Fürstenfeldbruck]] in [[Bavaria]] in [[Germany]].  It takes its name from [[Saint Rasso]] (Ratho), who was a count (Graf) who founded a [[Benedictine Order|Benedictine]] [[abbey]] here in the 10th century. < ref > [http://www.heiligenlexikon.de/BiographienR/Rasso_von_Andechs.html Rasso (Ratho) von Andechs - Ökumenisches Heiligenlexikon < !-- Bot generated title -- > ] < /ref >
