 '''Scorpius''' ([[Latin]] for ''[[scorpion]]'', symbol [[File:Scorpio.svg|15px]], [[Unicode]] ♏) is one of the [[constellation]]s of the [[zodiac]]. In [[astrology|western astrology]] it is also known as  " '''Scorpio''' " . It is a large constellation in the [[Southern Hemisphere]] near the center of the [[Milky Way]]. Its brightest star is the [[red supergiant]] [[Antares]]. The blue [[hypergiant]] [[Zeta1 Scorpii]] is even brighter but smaller and further away.
