 '''Altenkirchen''' is a town in the [[Germany|German]] [[States of Germany|state]] of [[Rhineland-Palatinate]]. It is the capital of the district of [[Altenkirchen (district)|Altenkirchen]]. As of 2018, 6,263 people lived there.
