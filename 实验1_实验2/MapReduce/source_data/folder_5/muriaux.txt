 '''Muriaux''' is a [[municipality]] in the [[district]] of [[Franches-Montagnes (district)|Franches-Montagnes]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Jura (canton)|Jura]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 On 1 January 2009, the former municipality of [[Le Peuchapatte]] merged into the municipality of Muriaux.
