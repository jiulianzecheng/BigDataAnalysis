 '''Scheveningen''' is one of the eight districts of [[The Hague]], [[Netherlands]]. Scheveningen is a modern [[seaside resort]] with a long sandy [[beach]] and a [[pier]]. It is 6 & nbsp;kilometers away from the center. 
 
 The [[harbour]] is used both for [[fishing]] and [[tourism]].
