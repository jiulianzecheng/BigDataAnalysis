 '''Schwamendingen''' is a [[quarter]] in [[Zürich]], [[Switzerland]]. It is District number 12. 
 
 The district has the quarters [[Schwamendingen Mitte]], [[Saatlen]] and [[Hirzenbach]]. 
 
 Until 1934 it was a municipality on its own. It was then [[Municipal corporation|incorporated]] into [[Zürich]].
