 '''Tulancingo''', officially '''Tulancingo de Bravo''', is a [[city]] in the state of [[Hidalgo (state)|Hidalgo]] in central [[Mexico]]. The city is in the Tulancingo Valley. It is {{convert|120|km}} from [[Mexico City]] and {{convert|40|km}} from [[Pachuca]]. It was [[wikt:found|found]]ed by the [[Toltec]]s.
