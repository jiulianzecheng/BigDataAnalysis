 '''Angela''' [an-juh-luh] < ref > {{Cite web|title=Definition of Angela {{!}} Dictionary.com|url=https://www.dictionary.com/browse/angela|access-date=2023-03-14|website=www.dictionary.com|language=en}} < /ref >  is a [[female]] given name.Angela is derived from the [[Greek mythology|Greek]] word “angelos”, meaning “messenger”. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Angela name Meaning and Origin|url=https://babys.name/detail-Angela-Meaning-Origin-Famous-Similar-Sibling.html|access-date=2023-03-14|website=babys.name}} < /ref > 
 
 It was popular during the [[1960s]] and [[1970s]]. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Popular Baby Names|url=https://www.ssa.gov/OACT/babynames/|access-date=2023-03-14|website=www.ssa.gov|language=en}} < /ref >  It is now less popular, although ''Angela'' is still popular among [[girl]]s. This name was ranked #234 on the US Popular Female Names in 2021. < ref > {{Cite web|last=OACT|title=Popular Baby Names|url=https://www.ssa.gov/cgi-bin/popularnames.cgi|access-date=2023-03-27|website=www.ssa.gov}} < /ref >  There are many celebrities with this name.
