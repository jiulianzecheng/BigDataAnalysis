 The '''Somniosidae''' are a family of [[shark]]s in the order [[Squaliformes]]. They are commonly known as '''sleeper sharks''', < ref > {{FishBase family |family=Somniosidae |month=February |year=2011}} < /ref >  because of their slow swimming.
