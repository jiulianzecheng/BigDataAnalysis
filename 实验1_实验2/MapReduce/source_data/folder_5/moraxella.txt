 '''''Moraxella''''' is a [[genus]] of [[bacteria]]. < ref > {{Cite web|url=https://www.cdc.gov/std/gonorrhea/lab/mcat.htm|title=Moraxella catarrhalis - Gonorrhea - STD Information from CDC|date=2019-01-11|website=www.cdc.gov|language=en-us|access-date=2020-06-15}} < /ref >  It is named after [[Victor Morax]], a Swiss eye specialist ([[Ophthalmology|ophthalmologist]]). < ref name= " Bergey " > {{cite book|last=Garrity|first=George M.  |display-authors = etal |title=Bergey's manual of systematic bacteriology. Volume 2: ''The Proteobacteria, Part B: The Gammaproteobacteria.'' |year=2005}} < /ref >  They are [[gram-negative]]. 
 The cells are short rods or cocci. ''[[Moraxella catarrhalis]]'' can cause [[chronic obstructive pulmonary disease]]. < ref name= " Bergey "  / >
