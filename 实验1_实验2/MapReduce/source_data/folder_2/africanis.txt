 The '''Africanis''' is a group of rare [[South Africa]]n [[dogs]]. They are not recognized as a breed. The [[Swahili language|Swahili]] name for the breed is ''{{lang|sw|umbwa wa ki-shenzi}}''. This means ''common'' or ''[[Mixed-breed dog|mixed-breed]]'' or  " [[tradition]]al dog " . 
 
 ==Description== 
 [[File:Africanis 4.jpg|left|thumb|''Canis Africanis'']] 
 The Africanis is a medium-sized dog. It is well-muscled and is a bit longer than it is tall. It has a short [[coat (dog)|coat]] of [[fur]] that can be of any color. This dog can run very fast and also is [[training|trainable]].
