 '''Kandersteg''' is a [[municipality]] in the [[administrative district]] of [[Frutigen-Niedersimmental (administrative district)|Frutigen-Niedersimmental]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Bern (canton)|Bern]] in [[Switzerland]]. It is along the valley of the river [[Kander (Switzerland)|Kander]], west of the [[Jungfrau]] massif.
