 '''Magherafelt''' is a small town in [[Northern Ireland]], in the [[United Kingdom]]. < ref > {{Cite web|url=https://www.britannica.com/place/Magherafelt-Northern-Ireland|title=Magherafelt {{!}} Northern Ireland, United Kingdom|website=Encyclopedia Britannica|language=en|access-date=2020-08-09}} < /ref > 
 ==Population facts== 
 On [[29 April]] [[2001]] the United Kingdom government had a [[census]] and found that there were 8,372 people living in Magherafelt. They also found that: 
 *27.1% of people living in Magherafelt were less than 16 years old and 13.7% were over 60 years old 
 *48.7% were male and 51.3% were female 
 *56.2% were from a [[Roman Catholicism|Roman Catholic]] background and 41.8% were from a [[Protestantism|Protestant]] background
