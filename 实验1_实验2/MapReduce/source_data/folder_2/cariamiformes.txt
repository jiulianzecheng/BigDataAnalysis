 '''Cariamiformes''' is a group of [[bird]]s. There's one existing [[Family (biology)|family]], the '''seriemas'''. It contains two [[genera]], with one [[species]] each. The seriemas live in the open landscapes of South America. They are running birds who can still fly. Both species are [[carnivore]]s.
