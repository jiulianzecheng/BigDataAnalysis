 '''Palashi''' {{IPA-hns|pəˈlaːsi|}}, anglicised as '''Plassey''', is a village on the east bank of [[Hooghly River|Bhagirathi River]], located 50 & nbsp;kilometres north of the city of [[Krishnanagar, Nadia|Krishnanagar]]  in [[Kaliganj (community development block)|Kaliganj]] CD Block in the [[Nadia District]] of [[West Bengal]], India.
