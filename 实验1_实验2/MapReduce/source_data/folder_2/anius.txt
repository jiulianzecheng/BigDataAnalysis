 In [[Greek mythology]], '''Anius''' was a king of [[Delos]] and priest of [[Apollo]]. He was the son of [[Apollo]] and Rhoeo. 
 
 Anius told the Greeks the [[Trojan War]] would not be won until the tenth year.
