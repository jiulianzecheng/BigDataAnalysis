 '''Buggingen''' is a municipality in [[Breisgau-Hochschwarzwald]] in [[Baden-Württemberg]] in southern [[Germany]]. It is between the [[Rhine|Rhine Valley]] and the [[Black Forest]] on the northern edge of [[Markgräflerland]].
