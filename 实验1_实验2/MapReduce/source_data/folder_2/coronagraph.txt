 A '''coronagraph''' is a [[telescope]] to see things around the [[Sun]]. The telescope uses a disk to block the bright surface of the sun, exposing bright objects around the sun to be visible to human eyes. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.spaceweather.com/glossary/coronagraph.html|title=What is a Coronagraph?|website=www.spaceweather.com}} < /ref >
