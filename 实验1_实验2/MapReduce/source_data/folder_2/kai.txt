 '''Kim Jong-in''' (born January 14, 1994) better known by his [[stage name]] '''Kai''' is a South Korean singer and actor. He is a member of [[Exo]]. He began his acting career at ''[[Choco Bank]]'' in February 2016, playing Kim Eun-haeng.He debuted as solo artist in 2020 Nov 30th with Mini solo album “KAI "  which holds world record for most #1 on iTunes Worldwide for an extended play for solo artist.A year later on the same exact day, he released his 2nd EP Peaches.

 == Career == 
 === 2008-15: Debut and early career === 
 {{Main|Exo}} 
 In 2008, he made a brief appearance on [[TVXQ]]'s song  " HaHaHa Song "  together with [[Suho]] and [[Park Chan-yeol|Chanyeol]]. < ref name= " dksinterview " / > 
 
 Kai was the first [[Exo]]'s member to be formally presented to the public on December 23, 2011. < ref name= " kaifirst " > {{cite journal|journal=Newsen|url=http://www.newsen.com/news_view.php?uid=201112230835211001 & search=title & searchstring=%C4%AB%C0%CC|title=SM unveils first member, Kai, of mysterious rookie group|date=2011-12-23|access-date=2012-04-30|language=ko}} < /ref >  Along with [[Luhan]], [[Chen]] and [[Tao]] made their first television performance at the SBS Gayo Daejun event on December 29 of the same year. < ref > {{cite web|title=EXO makes their debut at SBS 'Gayo Daejun'|url=http://www.allkpop.com/2011/12/exo-makes-their-debut-at-sbs-gayo-daejun|publisher=Allkpop|date=2011-12-29|access-date=2012-08-17}} < /ref >  In April. It officially debuted with the extended play, ''[[Mama]]''. Still in April, along with [[Byun Baek-hyun|Baekhyun]], [[Park Chan-yeol|Chanyeol]] and [[Oh Se-hun|Sehun]], appeared in the video of [[Girls' Generation-TTS]]'s debut song  " Twinkle " . < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.allkpop.com/article/2012/04/taetiseo-releases-mv-for-twinkle|title=TaeTiSeo releases MV for  " Twinkle " !|publisher=Allkpop|date=2012-04-30|access-date=2015-10-09}} < /ref >  On October 16, it was announced that he would participate in the 6-member dance group Younique Unit, alongside Eunhyuk, Hyoyeon, Taemin, Henry Lau and Luhan performing the song  " Maxstep "  for the album ''PYL Younique Album'', being an album collaboration between SM Entertainment and [[Hyundai]]. A video teaser of the song was shown on the ''PYL Younique Show'' the next day. < ref > {{cite web|title=ASK K-POP SM Entertainment's dance unit for Hyundai 'PYL' reveals video teaser for  " MAXSTEP " |url=http://www.me2day.us/article/SM-Entertainment-s-dance-unit-for-Hyundai-s-PYL-reveals-video-teaser-for-MAXSTEP/|publisher=me2day|date=2012-10-18|access-date=2012-10-26|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20141012154647/http://www.me2day.us/article/SM-Entertainment-s-dance-unit-for-Hyundai-s-PYL-reveals-video-teaser-for-MAXSTEP/#|archive-date=2014-10-12}} < /ref >  In December 2012, along with Lay, Yunho, Eunhyuk, Donghae, Minho and Taemin, they formed the SM performance group The Performance. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.jpopasia.com/group/smtheperformance/|title=Group SM The Performance|date=2012-12-30|work=Jpopasia|access-date=2013-10-16}} < /ref >  The group's first appearance was at the SBS Gayo Daejun event on December 29, with the release of the single  " Spectrum "  the next day.
