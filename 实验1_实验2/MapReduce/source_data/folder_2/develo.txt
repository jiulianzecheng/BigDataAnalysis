The '''Develo''' (デベロ) is an official [[Japan]]ese [[TurboGrafx-16|PC Engine]] [[hobbyist]] development kit for [[NEC]]'s console. It comes with a [[C (programming language)|C]] [[compiler]] and an [[assembly language#Assembler|assembler]]. Some games that were made with this kit came out on demo [[Optical disc|discs]], such as [[PC Engine Fan#CD-ROMs|Frisbee Ken John]].
