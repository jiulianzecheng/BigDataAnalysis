 '''Dennington''' is a village and [[civil parish]] in [[Suffolk Coastal]], [[Suffolk]], England. In 2001 there were 554 people living in Dennington. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.ons.gov.uk/help/localstatistics|title=Local statistics - Office for National Statistics|website=www.ons.gov.uk}} < /ref >  Dennington lies along the [[A1120]]. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.onesuffolk.net/|title=One Suffolk|website=www.onesuffolk.net}} < /ref >  Dennington has a church called St Mary. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.suffolkchurches.co.uk/Dennington.htm|title=Dennington|website=www.suffolkchurches.co.uk}} < /ref >
