'''Lebensraum''' means  " Living Space "  in [[German language|German]]. It is especially associated with [[Nazi Germany]] and Hitler's desire to expand the country. This belief led to the [[Invasion of Poland (1939)]] and Germany's conquering of the [[Sudetenland|Sudentenland]]. On June 22, 1941, Nazi Germany launched Operaton Barbarossa, an invasion of the [[Soviet Union]]. One of the goals for the operation was achieving Lebensraum.
