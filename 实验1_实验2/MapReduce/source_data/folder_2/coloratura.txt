 '''Coloratura''' is a term meaning florid singing in which singers demonstrate great agility with their voices. Although often associated with light [[soprano]]s, it can apply to any voice type such as [[tenor]], [[mezzo-soprano]], [[baritone]], [[bass (vocal range)|bass]], especially in the [[bel canto]] repertoire.
