 '''Qorveh''' ({{lang-ku|Qurwe}}, {{lang-fa|قروه}}) is a city in Kurdistan province of [[Iran]]. Its people speak [[Kurdish language|Kurdish]]. Some people also speak [[Azerbaijani language]]. They are followers of [[Islam]]. At the 2006 census, Qorveh had 65,842 people, in 16,309 families. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://web.archive.org/web/*/http://www.amar.org.ir/DesktopModules/FTPManager/upload/upload2360/newjkh/newjkh/12.xls|title=Wayback Machine|website=web.archive.org}} < /ref >
