'''Arsenide''' is an [[ion]]. Its chemical formula is As < sup > 3- < /sup > . Most arsenides have [[arsenic]] in its -3 [[oxidation state]]. [[Ionic bond|Ionic]] arsenides are very [[base (chemistry)|basic]] and are strong [[reducing agent]]s. Most arsenides are more [[metallic bond|metallic]], like [[zinc arsenide]]. Arsenides react with [[acid]]s to make [[arsine]] gas.
