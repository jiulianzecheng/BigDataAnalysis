 '''Dunaden''' (26 February 2006 â€“ 30 April 2019) was a [[Thoroughbred]] [[Horse racing|racehorse]]. He was trained in [[France]]. In his 46 races, he won 10 and was placed in the top three 19 times. He won the 2011 [[Melbourne Cup]], ridden by French [[jockey]] [[Christophe Lemaire]] and trained by Mikel Delzangles. 
 
 Other major wins by Dunaden include Prix de Barbeville (2011), Geelong Cup (2011), Hong Kong Vase (2011) and Caulfield Cup (2012). 
 
 Dunaden was [[retired]] in 2015.
