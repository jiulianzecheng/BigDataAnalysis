 In [[Greek mythology]], '''Telamon''' ({{IPAc-en|ˈ|t|ɛ|l|ə|m|ə|n}}; [[Ancient Greek]]: Τελαμών) was the son of [[Aeacus]], king of Aegina, and Endeïs, a mountain [[nymph]]. He was the older brother of [[Peleus]]. The brothers joined [[Jason]] as members of the [[Argonauts]]. He was also the father of [[Ajax the Great|Ajax the Greater]] and Teucer. They both fought during the [[Trojan War]].
