 '''''Myersiohyla''''' is a group of [[frog]]s in the family [[Hylidae]]. < ref name=Frost/ >  Scientists made this group in 2005 after they looked closely at all the frogs in Hylidae.  At first, they put four species in ''Myersiohyla''.  All four had been in the group ''[[Hyla]]'' before 2005. < ref name= " Faivovich " > {{cite journal|author1=Julián Faivovich |author2=Célio F.B. Haddad |author3=Paulo C.A. Garcia |author4=Darrel R. Frost |author5=Jonathan A. Campbell |author6=Ward C. Wheeler  |name-list-style=amp |year=2005|title=Systematic review of the frog family Hylidae, with special reference to Hylinae: phylogenetic analysis and taxonomic revision|journal=Bulletin of the American Museum of Natural History|volume=294|pages=1–240|doi=10.1206/0003-0090(2005)294[0001:SROTFF]2.0.CO;2|hdl=2246/462 |url=http://digitallibrary.amnh.org/dspace/handle/2246/462|citeseerx=10.1.1.470.2967 |s2cid=83925199 }} < /ref >  The genus is found in the [[tepui]] region of [[Guyana]] and [[Venezuela]]. < ref name=Frost/ > 
 
 ==Species== 
 There are six species in ''Myersiohyla'': < ref name=Frost/ > 
 * ''[[Myersiohyla aromatica]]''  < small > (Ayarzagüena and Señaris, 1994) < /small > 
 * ''[[Myersiohyla chamaeleo]]''  < small > (Faivovich, McDiarmid, and Myers, 2013) < /small > 
 * ''[[Myersiohyla inparquesi]]''  < small > (Ayarzagüena and Señaris, 1994) < /small > 
 * ''[[Myersiohyla liliae]]''  < small > (Kok, 2006) < /small > 
 * ''[[Myersiohyla loveridgei]]''  < small > (Rivero, 1961) < /small > 
 * ''[[Myersiohyla neblinaria]]''  < small > (Faivovich, McDiarmid, and Myers, 2013) < /small >
