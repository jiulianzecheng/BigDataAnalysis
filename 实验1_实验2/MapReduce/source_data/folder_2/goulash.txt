 '''Goulash''' ({{lang-hu|guly치s}}), is a [[soup]] of [[meat]] and [[Vegetable|vegetables]] seasoned with [[paprika]] < ref > {{cite web|title=Goulash|url=https://www.bbcgoodfood.com/search/recipes?q=goulash|access-date=26 December 2020|website=BBC Good Food Guide|publisher=British Broadcasting Corporation}} < /ref >  and other spices. < ref > {{cite book|title=Gundel's Hungarian cookbook|last=Gundel|first=Karoly|publisher=Corvina|year=1992|isbn=963-13-3600-X|location=Budapest|oclc=32227400}}page 20 < /ref >  Goulash came from [[Hungary]] and is commonly eaten in [[Central Europe]] and other parts of Europe. It is one of the national dishes of [[Hungary]] and a symbol of the country.
