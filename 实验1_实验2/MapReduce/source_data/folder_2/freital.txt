 '''Freital''' is the biggest town in the [[Sächsische Schweiz-Osterzgebirge Rural District|Sächsische Schweiz-Osterzgebirge]] district, in the [[Free State of Saxony]], [[Germany]]. It is on the small river [[Weißeritz]], {{convert|8|km|abbr=off}} southwest of [[Dresden]].
