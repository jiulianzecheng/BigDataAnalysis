 '''Perleberg''' ([[Low German language|Low German]]: ''Perlberg'') is a [[Germany|German]] town in the state of [[Brandenburg]]. It is halfway between the cities Berlin and Hamburg. It is the [[Capital (city)|capital]] of the [[Districts of Germany|district]] of [[Prignitz]]. In 2010 about 12,400 persons lived in this town.
