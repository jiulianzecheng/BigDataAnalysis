 '''Lincolnshire'''  ({{IPAc-en|ˈ|l|ɪ|ŋ|k|ən|ʃ|ər}} or {{IPAc-en|ˈ|l|ɪ|ŋ|k|ən|ʃ|ɪər|}}; abbreviated '''Lincs''') is a [[county]] in the east of [[England]]. It borders [[Norfolk]], [[Cambridgeshire]], [[Rutland]], [[Leicestershire]], [[Nottinghamshire]], [[South Yorkshire]], the [[East Riding of Yorkshire]], and (for just 19 metres or 20 yards, England's shortest county boundary) [[Northamptonshire]]. Its county town is the ancient city of [[Lincoln, Lincolnshire|Lincoln]].
