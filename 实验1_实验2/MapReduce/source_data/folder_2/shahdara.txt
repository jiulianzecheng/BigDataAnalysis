'''Shahdara''' is a geographical region in the city of [[Delhi]]. It is on the banks of [[Yamuna River]]. It is one of the oldest localities of Delhi. This region is the headquarters of the newly formed [[Shahdara district]].   
 
 == History == 
 Shahdara was established by a [[Mughal Empire|Mughal]] king.  In [[Urdu language|Urdu]], Shahdara means  " door of kings " .
