'''Feroz''' is a [[Spain|Spanish]] [[fantasy]] movie. It was directed by [[Manuel Gutiérrez Aragón]]. It was screened in the [[Un Certain Regard]] section at the 1984 [[Cannes Film Festival]]. < ref name= " festival-cannes.com " > {{cite web|url=http://www.festival-cannes.com/en/films/feroz |title=Festival de Cannes: Feroz |access-date=October 6, 2017 |work=festival-cannes.com }} < /ref > 
 
 == Plot == 
 Pablo (''Julio César Sanz'') is a strange man who is lost in the [[forest]]. One day he is saved by Luis (''Fernando Fernán Gómez'') from being attacked by several wild dogs. However, that  " man "  has the ability [[Shapeshifting|to become into a bear]], which is why dogs are aggressive toward him. 
 
 Luis, as a [[psychology|psychologist]], takes him to [[Madrid]] so he can learn to live into the society.
