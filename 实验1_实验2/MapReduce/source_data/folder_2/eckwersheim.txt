 '''Eckwersheim''' is a [[Communes in France|commune]]. It is in [[Grand Est]] in the [[Bas-Rhin]] [[Departments of France|department]] in northeast [[France]]. It is about {{convert|11|km|mi|abbr=on}} north of [[Strasbourg]]. 
 
 On 14 November 2015, the [[Eckwersheim derailment]] during testing of a [[TGV]] train along the [[LGV Est]] high-speed rail line. Ten people were killed.
