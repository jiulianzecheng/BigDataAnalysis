 '''''Flatliners''''' is a 1990 American science fiction horror thriller movie directed by [[Joel Schumacher]] and starring [[Kiefer Sutherland]], [[Kevin Bacon]], [[Julia Roberts]], [[William Baldwin]], [[Oliver Platt]], [[Kimberley Scott]], [[Hope Davis]]. It was distributed by [[Columbia Pictures]] and was nominated for an Academy Award in 1991. It was remade in 2017.
