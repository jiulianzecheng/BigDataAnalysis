 '''''Saintpaulia''''', '''African violets''', are small [[Plant|plants]] from [[Africa]] which live in [[cloud forest]] [[habitats]]. They are [[herbaceous]] [[perennial]] [[flowering plants]] in the [[family (biology)|family]] [[Gesneriaceae]].
