 '''Alavus''' ({{lang-sv|Alavo}}) is a [[city]] in [[Finland]]. As of 31 May 2013, 12,349 people lived there. Nearby municipalities are [[Alajärvi]], [[Kuortane]], [[Seinäjoki]], [[Virrat]] and [[Ähtäri]].
