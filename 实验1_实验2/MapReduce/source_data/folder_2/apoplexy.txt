'''Apoplexy''' means [[bleeding]] from an [[Organ (anatomy)|internal organ]]. Apoplexy used to be used to describe a [[stroke]]. It can also be used to say someone is very mad. The word  " apoplexy "  comes from the Greek  " apoplexia "  meaning a seizure, in the sense of being struck down.
