 '''Dormitz''' is a [[Bavaria]]n [[Municipalities of Germany|municipality]] in the district of [[Forchheim (district)|Forchheim]] ([[Upper Franconia]]), and the administrative headquarters of the Dormitz community, which includes the parishes of Kleinsendelbach and Hetzles.
