 '''Tuusniemi''' is a [[municipality]] in Finland. More than 2,800 people lived there in August 2013. The municipalities next to it are [[Heinävesi]], [[Juankoski]], [[Kaavi]], [[Kuopio]], [[Leppävirta]] and [[Outokumpu]]. 
 
 ==History== 
 Between 1904 and 1975 there was an [[asbestos]] mine in Paakkila village. It was closed because workers died earlier than others. < ref > http://yle.fi/elavaarkisto/artikkelit/paakkilan_asbestikyla_48764.html#media=48766 Video, finnish < /ref >
