 '''Haguenau''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]] in [[Alsace]] in north-eastern [[France]], in the [[Bas-Rhin]] [[Departments of France|department]], of which it is a [[Subprefectures in France|subprefecture]]. It is in the [[Grand Est]] [[Regions of France|region]]. 
 
 It is second in size in the Bas-Rhin only to [[Strasbourg]], some {{convert|30|km|mi|abbr=on}} to the south. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 Haguenau is in the northern part of the Bas-Rhin department. The [[Moder (river)|Moder]] [[river]], a left [[tributary]] of the [[Rhine River|Rhine]] river, flows through the ''commune''. 
 
 It has an area of {{Convert|182.6|km2|sqmi|abbr=on }}, the ''commune'' of Alsace with the largest [[territory]]. < ref name= " insee-geo " > {{cite web |url=https://www.insee.fr/fr/statistiques/1405599?geo=COM-67180 |title=Commune de Haguenau (67180) |work=Comparateur de territoire |publisher=Institut national de la statistique et des études économiques - INSEE |language=fr |access-date=25 December 2017 }} < /ref >  Its average [[altitude]] is {{Convert|159|m|ft|abbr=on}}; at the [[City and town halls|city hall]], the altitude is {{Convert|140|m|ft|abbr=on }}. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://www.map-france.com/Haguenau-67500/ |title=City of Haguenau |publisher=Map-France.com |access-date=25 December 2017 }} < /ref >

 ==Administration== 
 Haguenau is a [[Subprefectures in France|subprefecture]] in the [[Bas-Rhin]] department since 1790. It is the capital of the [[Arrondissement of Haguenau-Wissembourg|Haguenau-Wissembourg]] ''arrondissement'' and is also the [[administrative centre]] ({{lang-fr|chef-lieu}}) of the ''canton'' of [[Canton of Haguenau|Haguenau]] formed by 14 ''communes'', with 49,203 inhabitants (2014). < ref name=insee1 > {{cite web|url=https://www.insee.fr/fr/metadonnees/cog/communes/COM67180-haguenau |title=Commune de Haguenau (67180) |work= Géographie administrative et d’étude |publisher=Institut national de la statistique et des études économiques - INSEE |language=fr |access-date=26 December 2017 }} < /ref > 
 
 === Twin towns === 
 Haguenau has been [[Twin town|twinned]] with [[Landau]] (Germany) since 1963.
