 '''Kalopanax''' is a botanical [[genus]] belonging to the family [[Araliaceae]]. 
 
 ==Culinary use== 
 In [[Korea]], young [[shoot]]s, called ''eumnamu-sun'', are often eaten as sukhoe ([[Blanching (cooking)|blanched]] dish).
