 '''Supertramp''' is a [[United Kingdom|British]] [[Rock music|rock]] [[band]]. They were created and managed by a [[Netherlands|Dutch]] [[millionaire]] and were very popular in the [[1970s]]. The members of Supertramp included [[Rick Davies]] and [[Roger Hodgson]], who wrote most of the [[song]]s. The band's best-known songs include  " Dreamer " ,  " Give a Little Bit " ,  " The Logical Song " ,  " Take the Long Way Home " , and  " It's Raining Again " .
