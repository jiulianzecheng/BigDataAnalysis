 '''Chronology''' is a word meaning 'the study of time'. It comes from the [[Greek language|Greek]] words ''chronos'' ([[time]]) and ''logos'' (word). The [[adjective]] is '''chronological'''. 
 
 Putting events in chronological order means: listing them in the order in which they happened. 
 
 There are many [[science]]s which deal in different ways with chronology. General is sometimes called  " periodization " . Historical chronology can deal with [[history]] or with the [[geology|geological]] history of the earth. 
 
 When things are put in chronological order it can sometimes be called a  " timeline " .  Adjective- (of a record of events) following the order in which they occurred (kind of like a timeline)
