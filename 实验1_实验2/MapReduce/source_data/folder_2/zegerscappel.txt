 '''Zegerscappel''' (from [[Dutch language|Dutch]]; ''Zegerskappel'' in modern Dutch spelling) is a [[Communes in France|commune]] in [[Nord (department)|Nord]] in north [[France]]. In 2018 1,533 people lived there.
