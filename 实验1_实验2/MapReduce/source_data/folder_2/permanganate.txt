 '''Permanganate''' is an ion that contains one [[manganese]] [[ion]] in an [[oxidation state]] of +7 and 4 oxide atoms. It is a strong [[oxidizing agent]]. Permanganates are purple-black. Permanganates stain because they are easily [[reduction (chemistry)|reduced]] to brown-black [[manganese dioxide]].
