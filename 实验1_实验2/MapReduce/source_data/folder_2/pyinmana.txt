 '''Pyinmana''' ({{MYname|MY=ပျဉ်းမနားမြို့|MLCTS=pyanymana: mrui.}}, {{IPA-my|pjɪ̀ɴməná mjo̰|IPA}}; population: 100,000 (2006 estimate)) is a small town in [[Mandalay Division]] of [[Myanmar]]. The government in 2005 decided to move the capital city from Yangon to an area {{convert|2|mi|abbr=off}} away west of Pyinmana on November 6, 2005, near the town, named [[Naypyidaw]], which means Royal City. Pyinmana is approximately {{convert|200|mi|km|abbr=off}} north of [[Yangon]].
