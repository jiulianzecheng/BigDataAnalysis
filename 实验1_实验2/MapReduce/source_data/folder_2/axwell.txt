 '''Sven Axel Christofer Hedfors''' (born 18 December 1977), better known under his stage name '''Axwell''', is a [[Sweden|Swedish]] [[disc jockey|DJ]] and [[Producer|record producer]]. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://partyflock.nl/artist/24091:Axwell |title=Axwell |publisher=PartyFlock |access-date=24 July 2015}} < /ref >  He is a [[Remix|remixer]] and owner of [[Axtone Records]]. He is one third of what once was the [[Swedish House Mafia]] along with [[Sebastian Ingrosso]] and [[Steve Angello]]. In 2013 he was placed 19th on the [[DJ Magazine]] Top 100 DJ Poll. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://www.djmag.com/top100?year=2013 |title=Top 100 DJs |publisher=DJ Mag.com Ltd |access-date=24 July 2015 |archive-date=31 December 2013 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20131231081003/http://www.djmag.com/top100?year=2013 |url-status=dead }} < /ref >
