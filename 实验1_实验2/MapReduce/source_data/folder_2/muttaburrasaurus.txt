 '''''Muttaburrasaurus''''' was a plant-eating [[dinosaur]] that lived during the early [[Cretaceous|Cretaceous period]], about 113-97.5 million years ago. This dinosaur means  " Muttaburra (in Queensland, [[Australia]]) lizard " . ''Muttaburrasaurus'' are about 24 feet (7 m) long. They may have lived in herds.
