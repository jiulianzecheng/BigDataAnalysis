'''Vaishali''' or '''Vesali''' ([[Pali]])  was a [[city]] in what is currently [[Vaishali District]], [[Bihar]], [[India]]. The city was the [[capital (city)|capital]] of the [[Licchavi]] and the [[Vajji|Vajjian Confederacy]]. At the time of the [[Buddha]], Vesali was a very large and rich city. It was crowded with people and with much food.
