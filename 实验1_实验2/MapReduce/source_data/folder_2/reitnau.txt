 '''Reitnau''' is a [[municipality]] of the [[district]] of [[Zofingen (district)|Zofingen]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Aargau]] in [[Switzerland]]. On 1 January 2019, the former municipality of [[Attelwil]] merged into the municipality of Reitnau.
