 '''Plumstead''' is an area in [[London]], in the [[Royal Borough of Greenwich]]. It is near the [[Thames]]. 
 
 Its nearest places are [[Woolwich]], [[Welling]], [[Thamesmead]], [[Kidbrooke]] and [[Eltham]]. 
 
 Plumstead has a railway station.
