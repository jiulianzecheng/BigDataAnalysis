'''''Theia''''' is a [[Titan (mythology)|Titan]] in [[Greek mythology]]. She is the titan of sight. Her parents are [[Gaia (mythology)|Gaia]] and [[Uranos]]. With her brother [[Hyperion (Titan)|Hyperion]] her children are [[Helios]], [[Selene]], and [[Eos]]. She was also considered the moons eyes because Selene was her daughter and Selene was the titan of the moon.
