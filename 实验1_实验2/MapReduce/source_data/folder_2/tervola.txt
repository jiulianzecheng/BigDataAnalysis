 '''Tervola''' is a municipality in [[Finnish Lapland|Lapland]], [[Finland]]. As of January 2014, the municipality had a population of about 3,320. Neighbouring municipalities are [[Keminmaa]], [[Ranua]], [[Rovaniemi]], [[Simo]], [[Tornio]] and [[Ylitornio]].
