 '''Emmendingen''' is a [[town]] in [[Baden-Württemberg]], [[Germany]]. It is the capital of the [[Emmendingen (district)|district of Emmendingen]]. It is at the [[Elz (Rhine)|Elz]] river, {{convert|14|km|mi|abbr=off}} north of [[Freiburg im Breisgau]]. More than 26,000 people live in the town. It is the most in the Emmendingen district.
