'''Fernand Joseph Désiré Contandin''' was a [[French people|French]] actor and singer. He is usually called '''Fernandel'''.   
 
 He was born on 8 May 1903 in [[Marseille]], France. < ref > {{cite web|date=28 December 2009|title=Le borgate|url=http://www.comune.perosaargentina.to.it/index.php?option=com_content & view=article & id=88:le-borgate & catid=45:territorio & Itemid=90|access-date=29 December 2022|website=comune.perosaargentina.to.it/|publisher=Comune di Perosa Argentina|language=it|archive-date=4 March 2016|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160304213849/http://www.comune.perosaargentina.to.it/index.php?option=com_content & view=article & id=88:le-borgate & catid=45:territorio & Itemid=90|url-status=dead}} < /ref >  He acted in comedies for [[vaudeville]] and [[operetta]]s.  He was best known for his role in the ''[[Don Camillo]]'' series of movies. < ref > {{Citation|title=Fernandel  " Don Camillo, le rôle de ma vie "  {{!}} Archive INA|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=08GiHoYHuVo|language=en|access-date=2022-12-29}} < /ref >  His first [[Hollywood]] movie was ''[[Around the World in Eighty Days (1956 movie)|Around the World in Eighty Days]]''. 
 
 Fernandel died 26 February 1971 from [[lung cancer]]. < ref > {{Cite news|last=Innate|first=Henry Giniger|date=1971-02-28|title=Fernandel, Comedian, 67, Dies|language=en-US|work=The New York Times|url=https://www.nytimes.com/1971/02/28/archives/fernandel-comedian-67-dies-he-was-amonk-most-popular-figures-in.html|access-date=2022-12-29|issn=0362-4331}} < /ref >  He is buried in the [[Cimetière de Passy]], Paris, France.
