 A '''toaster''' is a [[machine]] that [[cook]]s [[bread]]. The bread is put into the toaster and after a few [[minute]]s it pops up, [[toast|toasted]]. The pop-up toaster was [[invent]]ed by Parker Scharbrough in 1919.
