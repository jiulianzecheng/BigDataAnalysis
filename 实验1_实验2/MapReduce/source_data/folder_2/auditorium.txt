 An '''auditorium''' (plural '''''auditoriums''''', or less commonly '''''auditoria''''') is the room in a [[theatre]], [[cinema]], [[concert hall]], or other place where the [[audience]] is located to [[hear]] or [[wikt:watch|watch]] the [[show]]. Auditoriums are made with great sound. The sound is easily heard by everyone in the auditorium.
