 '''Lavia''' is a [[municipality]] in [[Satakunta]], [[Finland]]. About 1,900 people lived there in January 2014. The municipalities next to it are [[Kankaanpää]], [[Kokemäki]], [[Pori]], [[Pomarkku]], [[Sastamala]] and [[Ulvila]].  
 
 The designer of Finish Euro coin comes from Lavia.
