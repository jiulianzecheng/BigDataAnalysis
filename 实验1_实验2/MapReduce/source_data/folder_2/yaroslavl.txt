 '''Yaroslavl''' is a [[Russia]]n city on the [[Volga River]], the capital and the largest city of [[Yaroslavl Oblast]]. It was founded in 1010. It is one of the largest cities in Russia. The city's centre is a [[World Heritage Site]]. It is one of the [[Golden Ring]] cities, a group of historic cities northeast of Moscow.
