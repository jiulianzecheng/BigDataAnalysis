'''Cockney''' is an [[accent]] or [[dialect]] of English,  spoken in London, particularly by working-class and lower middle-class Londoners. The term  " Cockney "  was used to describe a person from the [[East End of London|East End,]] or born within earshot of Bow Bells - The '''Church of St Mary-le-Bow'''.
