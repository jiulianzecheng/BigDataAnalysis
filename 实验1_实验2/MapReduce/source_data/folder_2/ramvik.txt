 '''Ramvik''' is a [[urban areas of Sweden|locality]] in [[Västernorrland County]] in [[Sweden]]. In 2010, 691 people lived there. < ref name=scb / >  The locality is bimunicipal. That means it spans across the borderlines of two municipalities ([[Härnösand Municipality]] and [[Kramfors Municipality]]). 
 
 Ramvik was the home of Swedish politician [[Thorbjörn Fälldin]]. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.svt.se/nyheter/val2018/centerns-landsbygdspolitik-presenterad-hemma-hos-falldin|title=Centerns landsbygdspolitik presenterad hemma hos Fälldin|publisher=SVT Nyheter|author=Rolf Lunneborg, Fredrik Forsgren|language=Swedish|date=16 August 2018|accessdate=7 August 2022}} < /ref >
