'''Infertile''' means not able to have [[child]]ren or other [[offspring]]. [[Human]]s can be infertile, but [[animal]]s and [[plant]]s can too. When a human couple is infertile, they sometimes have [[fertility treatment]] to have children. This can be [[insemination]] or [[in vitro fertilization|IVF]]. They also sometimes [[adoption|adopt]] a child.
