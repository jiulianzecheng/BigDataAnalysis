 '''Dadeumi''' or '''Kinuta''' is a [[Korea|Korean]], and [[Japan|Japanese]] traditional [[ironing]].  The [[Washing|washed]] [[Textile|cloth]] is placed on a stand in a dry state and beaten on it with a stick to smooth out wrinkles and soften.
