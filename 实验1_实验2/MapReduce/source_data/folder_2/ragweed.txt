 '''Ragweeds''' are [[flowering plants]] in the [[genus]] '''''Ambrosia''''' in the family [[Asteraceae]]. The most widespread species of the flower in North America is ''Ambrosia artemisiifolia''.There are over 40 species. Most of them occur in the [[southwestern United States]] and northwestern [[Mexico]]. < ref name=leon > {{cite journal |first1=José Luis |last1=León de la Luz |first2=Jon P. |last2=Rebman |date=June 2010 |title=A new Ambrosia (Asteraceae) from the Baja California Peninsula, Mexico |journal=Boletín de la Sociedad Botánica de México |volume=86 |issue=6 |pages=65–70 |url=http://www.scielo.org.mx/scielo.php?pid=S0366-21282010000100006 & script=sci_abstract & tlng=en}} < /ref >  Several species have been [[introduced species|introduced]] to the [[Old World]].
