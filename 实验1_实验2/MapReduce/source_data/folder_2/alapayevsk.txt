 == History == 
 The town was founded in 1781.  
 
 === Murder of Russian Imperial family members === 
 On July 18, 1918, the day after the shooting of the last tsar [[Nicholas II]] and his [[Execution of the Romanov family|family]], other members of the Romanov family were thrown down a [[Mine|mineshaft]] near Alapayevsk by the [[Bolsheviks]].
