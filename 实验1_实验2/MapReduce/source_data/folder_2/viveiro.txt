 '''Viveiro''' (sometimes called '''Vivero''') is a [[town]] and [[municipality]]. It is in the [[province of Lugo]]. Lugo is in the northwestern part of [[Galicia (Spain)|Galicia]], [[Spain]]. As of 2010, over 16,000 people live in Viveiro. 
 
 == Festivities == 
 The calendar of Viveiro holds several festivals, both religious and not. 
 
 === Carnival === 
 Viveiro has the oldest Carnival in Galicia. It is celebrated during the four days before [[Lent]], from Saturday until [[Shrove Tuesday]]. It finishes on Tuesday with many floats travelling through the streets. 
 
 === Saint James' day === 
 Viveiro celebrates the [[St. James' Day Battle]] on July 25 at the [[Port of Celeiro]]. It has [[Marine (military)|marine]]s moving through the [[estuary]] of Viveiro in a well organised and orderly way, similar to a [[marching band|march]].
