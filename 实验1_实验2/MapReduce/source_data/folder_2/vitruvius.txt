 '''Vitruvius''' (probably '''Marcus Vitruvius Pollio''') was an [[Ancient Rome|Ancient Roman]] [[architect]] and [[engineer]] who lived in the 1st century BC. Neither his name, ''Marcus'', nor his [[cognomen]] ''Pollio'' are certain. His main work is called ''De architectura'' (About architecture). There are ten books in it. He was probably born between 80 and 70 BC in [[Campania]], and died after 15 BC.
