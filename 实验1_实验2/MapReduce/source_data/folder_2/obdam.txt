 '''Obdam''' is a village and former [[List of municipalities of the Netherlands|municipality]] in the [[Netherlands|Dutch]] [[Provinces of the Netherlands|province]] of [[North Holland]]. Since 2007, it has been part of the new municipality of [[Koggenland]].
