 '''''Blacula''''' is a 1972 American blaxploitation fantasy horror movie directed by [[William Crain]] and starring [[William Marshall (actor)|William Marshall]], [[Denise Nicholas]], [[Vonetta McGee]], [[Gordon Pinsent]], [[Thalmus Rasulala]], [[Ketty Lester]], [[The Hues Corporation]]. It was followed by the sequel ''[[Scream Blacula Scream]]'' in 1973.
