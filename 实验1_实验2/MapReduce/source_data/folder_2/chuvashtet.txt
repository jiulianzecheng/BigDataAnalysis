'''ChuvashTet''' (short for  " Chuvash Internet " ), ({{lang-cv|Чăва́ш Интерне́чĕ, Чăваште́т, Чăваш Тете́лĕ}}) is the name Chuvash-speaking Internet users commonly use for the segment of the Web about the [[Chuvash]] people or [[Chuvashia]]. It includes [[webpages]] and [[websites]] in many languages. 
 
 Chuvash Internet users employ Latin and Cyrillic scripts.
