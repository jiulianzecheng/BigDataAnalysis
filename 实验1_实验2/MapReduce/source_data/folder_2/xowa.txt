 '''XOWA''' is a [[Free and open-source software|free and open-source]] application written primarily in [[Java (programming language)|Java]] by anomous developers and is intended for users whish to run their own copy of [[Wikipedia]], or any other compatible [[Wiki]] [[offline]] or without an internet connection. XOWA is compatible with [[Microsoft Windows]], [[MacOS]], [[Linux]] and [[Android (operating system)|Android]]. Licensed under the [[GNU Affero General Public License|GNU AGPLv3]], XOWA is [[free software]].
