The '''Dolomitenlauf''' is a [[cross-country skiing (sport)|cross-country skiing marathon]] in [[Austria]]. It was first held in 1970 and has been part of [[Worldloppet Ski Federation|Worldloppet]] as long as Worldloppet has been around. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.worldloppet.com/dolomitenlauf.php|title=Dolomitenlauf|publisher=Worldloppet|language=English|accessdate=4 March 2015}} < /ref >
