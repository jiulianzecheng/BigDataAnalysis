 '''Chakradhar Gilla''' (known as Chakri) (15 June 1974 – 15 December 2014) was an [[India]]n music composer and singer. He works for [[Telugu cinema|Tollywood]] movies. He won the [[Filmfare Award for Best Male Playback Singer – Telugu]] for the movie ''[[Satyam (2003 movie)|Satyam]]'' (2003). His song named  " O Maguva Nee Sneham Kosam "  from his movie  " Satyam "  was thought to be his best known work. Chakri was born in [[Mahabubabad]], [[Telangana]].
