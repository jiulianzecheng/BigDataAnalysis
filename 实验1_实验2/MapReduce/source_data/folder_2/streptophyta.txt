 '''Streptophyta''' ({{IPAc-en|s|14='|24=t|23=aɪ|22=f|21=oʊ|20=t|19=p|18=ɛ|17=r|16=t|15=s|13=,_|t|12=ə|11=t|10=ᵻ|9=f|8=ɒ|7=t|6=ˈ|5=p|4=ɛ|r|25=ə}}), are a [[clade]] of [[Plant|plants]]. Streptophyta is most land plants and all [[green algae]], except for the [[Chlorophyta]], Mesostigmatophyceae, Chlorokybophyceae and Spirotaenia. < ref > {{Cite journal|last1=Sánchez-Baracaldo|first1=Patricia|last2=Raven|first2=John A.|last3=Pisani|first3=Davide|last4=Knoll|first4=Andrew H.|date=2017-09-12|title=Early photosynthetic eukaryotes inhabited low-salinity habitats|url=https://research-information.bristol.ac.uk/ws/files/132985852/pnas.1620089114.sapp.pdf|journal=Proceedings of the National Academy of Sciences|volume=114|issue=37|pages=E7737–E7745|doi=10.1073/pnas.1620089114|pmc=5603991|pmid=28808007|bibcode=2017PNAS..114E7737S |doi-access=free}} < /ref > 
 
 == Definition == 
 The [[Biological classification|classification]] of Streptophyta is different from source to source. < ref > http://www.protisten.de/german/docs/Phylogeny_in_Streptophyta.pdf < /ref >  Some include more organism groups, and others include less organism groups. 
 
 Streptophyta [[Organism|organisms]] are both [[Unicellular organism|unicellular]] and [[Multicellular organism|multicellular]]. Every [[living]] [[green plant]] is a streptophyta. The Streptophyta [[phylum]] includes green algae in [[Freshwater lake|freshwater]] and all [[Land plant|land plants]].
