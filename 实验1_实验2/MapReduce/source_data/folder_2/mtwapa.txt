 '''Mtwapa''' is a small town lying on the [[Indian Ocean]] coast of [[Kenya]]. 
 The town is situated approximately {{convert|16|km}} north-east of [[Mombasa]] on the Mombasa-[[Malindi]] road. 
 
 " Mtu wa hapa "  – in Swahili; this translates to ''he who lives among us.''  
 
 Mtwapa is home to a large number of European retirees and holiday-makers from countries such as [[Germany]], [[Holland]] and [[Great Britain]]. It is also a destination for sex tourism. < ref > [https://www.abc.net.au/news/2015-03-19/child-sex-tourism-thriving-in-kenyas-port-city-of-mombasa/6333440?pfmredir=sm   Child sex tourism thriving in Kenya's port city of Mombasa]. ''ABC News.'' 19 March 2015. < /ref >  Mtwapa Creek is a starting point for [[fishing]] tours.

 == In popular culture ==
