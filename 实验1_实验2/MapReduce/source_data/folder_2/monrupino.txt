 '''Monrupino''' ({{lang-sl|Repentabor}}) is a ''[[comune]]'' in the [[Province of Trieste]] in the [[Friuli-Venezia Giulia]] region in [[Italy]]. According to the 1971 census, 77.3% of people are [[Slovenes]].
