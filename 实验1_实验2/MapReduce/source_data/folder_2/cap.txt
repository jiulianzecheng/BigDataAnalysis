 A '''cap''' is a form of [[headgear]] or [[headware]]. Caps have crowns that fit very close to the head. They have been used in almost every society, especially those societies which have colder weather. 
 
 They made their first appearance as early as 3,200BC. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Who Invented The Hat? - Amazing Hat History|url=https://fashionablehats.com/blogs/hat-articles/invented-hat|access-date=2020-11-17|website=Fashionable Hats|language=en}} < /ref >  Caps typically have a visor, but sometimes have no brim at all. < ref name= " :0 " > {{Cite web|date=2019-07-26|title=Cap vs. Hat: differentiating the meaning of different headgear|url=https://www.typingadventure.com/cap-vs-hat/|access-date=2020-11-17|website=Typing Adventure|language=en-US|archive-date=2020-11-24|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20201124195940/https://www.typingadventure.com/cap-vs-hat/|url-status=dead}} < /ref >  They are popular in casual and informal settings, and are seen in sports and fashion. < ref name= " :0 "  / >   They are designed for warmth, and often have a visor to block sunlight from the eyes. They come in many shapes, sizes, and are of different brands.
