 '''Zimmerwald''' was a [[municipality]] in [[Seftigen (district)|Seftigen]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Bern (canton)|Berne]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 On 1 January 2004 the municipalities Zimmerwald and [[Englisberg]] joined together to become the new municipality, [[Wald, Bern|Wald]].
