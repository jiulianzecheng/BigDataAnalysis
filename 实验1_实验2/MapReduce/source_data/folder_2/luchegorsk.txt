 '''Luchegorsk''' ([[Russian language|Russian]]:Лучегорск, lit. ''Ray of mountains'') is a [[urban-type settlement]] and administrative center of [[Pozharsky District]] ([[Primorsky Krai]], [[Russia]]). It was founded in [[1966]]. There are 22,365 people living there according to [[Russian Census (2002)|2002 Census]]. 
 
 == Geography == 
 Geographical coordinates are 46-27N,134-17E. Luchegorsk is the largest non-city settlement on [[Russian Far East]]. 
 
 Luchegorsk has more [[ecology|ecological]] problems for a large [[electric power station]]. 
 
 In December,1997, a large [https://www.npr.org/templates/story/story.php?storyId=129551459 Amur tiger hunted down a poacher] who had shot and wounded the tiger.The tiger killed the man in what appears to be the only (recorded) premeditated act of revenge by a tiger.
