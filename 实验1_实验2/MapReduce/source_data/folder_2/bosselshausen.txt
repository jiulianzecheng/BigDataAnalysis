 '''Bosselshausen''' is a [[Communes in France|commune]]. It is in [[Grand Est]] in the [[Bas-Rhin]] [[Departments of France|department]] in northeast [[France]]. Between 1974 and 2007 [[:fr:Kirrwiller-Bosselshausen|Kirrwiller-Bosselshausen]] was a only commune. In January 2007, the communes Bosselshausen and [[Kirrwiller]] were separated. < ref > [http://www.legifrance.gouv.fr/affichTexte.do?cidTexte=JORFTEXT000000243807 Arrêté du 29 décembre 2006 portant modification aux circonscriptions administratives territoriales (création de communes)] {{in lang|fr}} < /ref >
