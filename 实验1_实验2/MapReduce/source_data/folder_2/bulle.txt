 '''Bulle''' ({{IPA-fr|byl}}, {{lang-frp|{{Audio|Frp-greverin-Bulo.ogg|Bulo}}}}) is a [[municipality]] of the [[district]] of [[Gruyère (district)|Gruyère]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Fribourg (canton)|Fribourg]] in [[Switzerland]]. In January 2006 Bulle incorporated the formerly independent municipality of [[La Tour-de-Trême]].
