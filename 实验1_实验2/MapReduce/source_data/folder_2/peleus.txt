'''Peleus''' ({{lang-el|Πηλεύς}}, ''Pēleus'') was  one of the early [[Greek mythology|Greek heroes]]. Peleus was the son of [[Aeacus]], king of the island of [[Aegina]], and [[Endeïs]], the nymph of [[Mount Pelion]]. Alongside his brother [[Telamon]], he joined the hero [[Jason]] as one of the [[Argonauts]] on the quest to find the [[Golden Fleece]]. He was the husband of the [[Nereid]] [[Thetis]], by whom he is the father of [[Achilles]].
