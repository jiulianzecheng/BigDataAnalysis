 '''Apache''' is the name for several [[Culture|culturally]] related groups of [[Native American]]s in the [[Indigenous peoples of the North American Southwest|southwestern]] [[United States]]. They hunted [[deer]] and they also ate [[berries]] and lots of [[fruit]].They were [[Nomadic people|nomadic]] which meant that they followed food and never stayed in one place for a long period of time. They are from the second migration of Native Americans which were the [[Na Dene]] which also includes the [[Chipewyan]] and the [[Cheyenne people|Cheyenne]] of Canada. Evidence proves that the Na Dene came from the Ket people of [[Siberia]].
