 '''Leonberg''' is a town in the [[Germany|German]] state of [[Baden-Württemberg]]. It is about {{convert|10|mi|0|abbr=on}} to the west of [[Stuttgart]], the state capital. About 45,000 people live there, which makes it the third-largest town in the district of [[Böblingen (district)|Böblingen]], after [[Sindelfingen]] and [[Böblingen]].
