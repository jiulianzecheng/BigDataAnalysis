'''Survivalism''' is a [[social movement]] of people or groups (called '''survivalists''' or '''preppers''' < ref name= " Senekal 2019 " > {{citation |last=Senekal |first=BA |title=#doomsdayprepper: Analysing the online prepper community on Instagram |publisher=Ensovoort 40(11). |date=2019 |url=http://ensovoort.com/doomsdaypreppers/ |access-date=2022-05-25 |archive-date=2020-11-10 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20201110103811/http://ensovoort.com/doomsdaypreppers/ |url-status=dead }} < /ref > ) who prepare for emergencies, including natural disasters. They often prepare for international or global catastrophe.  
 
 Survivalism may be focused on preparing for a personal emergency, such as job loss or being stranded in the wild or under bad weather conditions.
