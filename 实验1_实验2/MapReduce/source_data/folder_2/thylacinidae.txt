 The '''Thylacinidae''' are a family of [[Mammal|mammals]] that were all [[Carnivore|carnivorous]] [[Marsupial|marsupials]] from the order [[Dasyuromorphia]]. They are now [[Extinction|extinct]], which means we never get to see them again.
