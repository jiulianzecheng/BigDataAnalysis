 '''Oberegg District''' is a [[Districts of Switzerland|district]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Appenzell Innerrhoden]] in 
 [[Switzerland]]. It has two small [[exclave]]s of the canton, a few miles northeast of the rest of Appenzell Innerrhoden.
