 '''Chelers''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is found in the [[Regions of France|region]] [[Nord-Pas-de-Calais]] in the [[Pas-de-Calais]] [[Departments of France|department]] in the north of [[France]]. 
 
 == Places of interest == 
 * The church of St. Martin, built during the 1700s. 
 * The Commonwealth War Graves Commission cemetery. 
 * A chateau from the 1700s.
