 '''''Unforgiven''''' is a 1992 [[United States|American]] [[Western (genre)|western]] [[movie]] [[movie director|directed]] by [[Clint Eastwood]]. It stars Eastwood, [[Gene Hackman]], [[Morgan Freeman]] and [[Richard Harris]]. It ranks #98 on [[AFI's 100 Years... 100 Movies]] and won the [[Academy Award for Best Picture|Best Picture Academy Award]]. It is set in the 1880s in [[Wyoming]] and [[Kansas]] about an aging outlaw and killer.
