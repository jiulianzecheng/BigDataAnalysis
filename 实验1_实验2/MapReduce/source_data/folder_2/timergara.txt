Timergara is a city in [[Lower Dir District]], Khyber Pakhtunkhwa. '''It''' is the headquarters of Lower Dir District in Pakistan. Timergara City is located on the east bank of the Panjkora River. The people of Timergara are mainly [[Pashtun people|Pashtun]]. The people generally wear typical pashtoon dress. Timergara connects [[Bajaur Agency|Bajawar]], [[Upper Dir District|Upper Dir]] and [[Chitral]] to the rest of Khyber Pukhtunkhwa.
