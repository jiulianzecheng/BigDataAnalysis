 '''Weiswampach''' ({{lang-lb|Wäiswampech}}) is a [[Communes of Luxembourg|commune]] in [[Luxembourg]]. It is part of the [[Clervaux (canton)|canton of Clervaux]]. 
 
 As of 2019, 1,912 people live here.
