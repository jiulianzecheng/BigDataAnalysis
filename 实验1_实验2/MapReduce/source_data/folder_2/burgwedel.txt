 '''Burgwedel''' is a town in [[Hanover (district)|Hanover]] in [[Lower Saxony]], [[Germany]]. It is about 15 & nbsp;km northeast of [[Hanover]]. About 20,200 people live there. 
 
 == Districts == 
 Burgwedel has 7 districts: 
 * Engensen 
 * Fuhrberg 
 * Großburgwedel (seat) 
 * Kleinburgwedel 
 * Oldhorst 
 * Thönse 
 * Wettmar 
 
 == Economy == 
 [[Rossmann (company)|Rossmann]], a drugstore chain, is in Burgwedel.
