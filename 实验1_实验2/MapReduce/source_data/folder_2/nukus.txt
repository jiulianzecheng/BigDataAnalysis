 '''Nukus''' ({{lang-uz|Nukus}} / {{lang|uz-cyrl|Нукус}}; {{lang-kaa|Nókis}} / {{lang|kaa-cyrl|Нөкис}}; {{lang-kk|Нүкіс}} / {{lang|kk-latn|italic=no|Nükıs}}) is the sixth-largest city in [[Uzbekistan]] and the capital of the autonomous [[Karakalpakstan|Republic of Karakalpakstan]]. The population of Nukus as of January 1, 2022 was 329,100.
