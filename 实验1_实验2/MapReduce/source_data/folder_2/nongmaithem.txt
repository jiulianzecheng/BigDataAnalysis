 '''Nongmaithem''' is a [[Yumnak|Meetei Manipuri surname or family name]] which has Indian origin. People of this family mainly inhabit in [[Manipur]], [[India]]. Nongmaithem surname belongs to Angom clan in Meiteis , one of the seven clans in Manipur.
