Trey is a frequently used name for male children in the United States. It is a concise and straightforward name with a significant historical background. The name Trey is derived from the Latin term  " tres, "  which translates to  " three. "  It is commonly employed as a moniker for boys who are the third-born in their family or were born on the third day of the month.
