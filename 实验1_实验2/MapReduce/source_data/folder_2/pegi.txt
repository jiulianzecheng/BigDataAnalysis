 '''Pan European Game Information''' ('''PEGI''') is a [[Europe]]an [[self-regulatory organization]] that assigns [[Video game content rating system|age and content ratings]] to consumer [[video game]]s. The PEGI system is used in more than thirty countries. PEGI has rated more than 15,000 video games from 2003 to 2010. < ref > {{Cite web |url=http://www.pegi.info/en/index/id/37/ |title=PEGI Pan European Game Information - PEGI statistics |access-date=2009-06-26 |archive-date=2009-06-20 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20090620044442/http://www.pegi.info/en/index/id/37/ |url-status=dead }} < /ref >
