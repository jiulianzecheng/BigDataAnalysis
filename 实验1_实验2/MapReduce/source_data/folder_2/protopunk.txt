'''Protopunk''' is a term used to describe the precursors of [[punk rock]].  Many of the artists whose sound helped pave the way for punk came from the [[1960s]] and early [[1970s]].  Some have been cited by early punk musicians as influential.  These artists came from many different [[musical genre|musical backgrounds]].
