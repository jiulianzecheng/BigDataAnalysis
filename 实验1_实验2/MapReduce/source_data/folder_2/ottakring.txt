 '''Ottakring''' is part of [[Vienna]]. It is the 16th district of Vienna, created in 1893 from the [[village]]s of [[Ottakring (village)|Ottakring]] and [[Neulerchenfeld]]. Ottakring can be reached by [[public transit]]; it is west of the city centre. < ref name= " Guard " > {{cite news|url=https://www.theguardian.com/travel/2019/dec/16/locals-guide-vienna-austria-10-top-tips-city-break-museums-cafes-music|publisher=Guardian|accessdate=July 19, 2020|author1=Kevin Rushby|title=A local's guide to Vienna: 10 top tips |date=December 16, 2019}} < /ref > 
   
 Today, there are fewer people in this district than in the past. In 1910, about 179,000 people lived there, but in 2019 there were only 103,000. < ref name= " Pop " > {{cite web|accessdate=July 19, 2020|url=https://www.wien.gv.at/english/administration/statistics/population-district.html|publisher=City of Vienna|title=Population and surface of Vienna's municipal districts 2019 *|year=2019}} < /ref >
