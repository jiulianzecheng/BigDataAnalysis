 '''''Hymenocallis''''' is a [[genus]] of plants in the family [[Amaryllidaceae]]. It contains some fifty species native to tropical and subtropical [[Americas|America]]. ''Hymenocallis'' are bulbous perennial herbs. The [[flower]]s have their stamens united to a characteristic corona.
