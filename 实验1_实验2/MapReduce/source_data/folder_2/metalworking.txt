'''Metalworking''' is the shaping of [[metals]] to create single parts, assemblies, or large scale buildings/objects. There are many ways of shaping metal, including [[machining]], [[metalspinning]], [[Forge|forging]], [[casting]], [[goldsmith]] and [[fabricating]]. [[Human|Humans]] have been working metal for close to 7000 years.{{fact|date=July 2022}}
