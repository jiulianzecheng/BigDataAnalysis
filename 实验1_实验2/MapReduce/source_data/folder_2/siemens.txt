 '''Siemens AG''' ({{IPA|ˈziːməns}}) is a company which has its headquarters in [[Berlin]] and [[Munich]], Germany. It is the largest electronics company in [[Europe]]. < ref > {{Cite web| url=https://www.bloomberg.com/apps/news?pid=20601100 & sid=agxq4nZkE.lc & refer=germany | title=Bloomberg.com | accessdate=2008-01-12}} < /ref > 
 
 Siemens is a technology company with activities in industry, energy and healthcare. It is organised into six main parts: Industry, Energy, Healthcare, Equity Investments, Siemens IT Solutions, and Services and [[Siemens Financial Services]] (SFS).
