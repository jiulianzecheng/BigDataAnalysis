'''Caning''' is a form of [[punishment]] that involves [[Strike (attack)|beating]] a person with a [[cane]], usually made of [[rattan]]. < ref > {{cite web |url=https://www.collinsdictionary.com/dictionary/english/caning |title=caning |website=English Dictionary |publisher=Collins |access-date=January 27, 2017}} < /ref >  Each blow of the cane is called a  " cut "  or a  " stroke " . < ref name=WG > {{cite web |url=http://www.wisegeek.org/what-is-caning.htm |title=What is Caning? |publisher=WiseGeek |access-date=January 27, 2017}} < /ref >  It is most often applied to the [[hand]]s or [[buttocks]]. < ref name=WG/ >  Caning, as a form of [[corporal punishment]], is [[wikt:prohibit|prohibit]]ed under [[international law]] as a form of [[torture]]. < ref > {{cite web |url=https://www.amnesty.org/en/press-releases/2010/12/malaysia-torture-practiced-systematically-widespread-caning/ |title=Malaysia: Torture Practiced Systematically in Widespread Caning |publisher=Amnesty International |access-date=January 27, 2017}} < /ref >  Caning was [[ban]]ned in [[British]] [[public school]]s only a few years ago. < ref name=DeaneLAT > {{cite web |url=http://articles.latimes.com/1989-06-18/news/mn-3705_1_caning-flogging-hong-kong |title=Use of 'the Cane' Lives On: In Parts of Asia, Flogging Still Exists |author=Daniela Deane |date=June 18, 1989 |publisher=Los Angeles Times |access-date=January 27, 2017}} < /ref >  It is still [[practice]]d in places such as [[Singapore]], [[Hong Kong]], [[Malaysia]], and [[Brunei]]. < ref name=DeaneLAT/ >
