 A '''chauffeur''' is a person who [[drive]]s a vehicle ([[car]]) as their [[job]].  The word can be used for anybody who drives as their job. Usually, it implies a driver of an elegant passenger vehicle (for example, a [[horse-drawn carriage]], [[sedan (car)|luxury sedan]], < ref > {{Cite web|last=|date=2021-02-25|title=Most popular luxury sedans|url=https://luxurylaunches.com/transport/these-are-the-most-popular-luxury-sedans-for-2021.php|url-status=dead|access-date=2021-04-01|website=Luxurylaunches|language=en-US|archive-date=2021-04-18|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210418145024/https://luxurylaunches.com/transport/these-are-the-most-popular-luxury-sedans-for-2021.php}} < /ref >  [[motor coach]], or especially a [[limousine]]). People who drive [[taxi]]s, [[bus]]es or non-passenger vehicles are generally referred to as  " drivers "  (as in ''bus drivers'' and ''truck drivers'').
