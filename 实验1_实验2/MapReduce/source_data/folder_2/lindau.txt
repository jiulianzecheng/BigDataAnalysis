 '''Lindau''' (formerly ''Lindau im Bodensee''; {{IPA-de|ˈlɪndaʊ̯|lang|De-Lindau.ogg}}; {{lang-swg|Linde}} or ''Lindou''/''Linda'') is a major town and [[Lindau (island)|island]] on the eastern side of [[Lake Constance]] (''Bodensee'') in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. It is the capital of the rural district (''Landkreis'') of [[Lindau (district)|Lindau]] and is near the borders of the [[Austria]]n state of [[Vorarlberg]] and the [[Switzerland|Swiss]] cantons of [[Canton of St. Gallen|St. Gallen]] and [[Canton of Thurgau|Thurgau]]. As of 31 December 2018, 25,490 people lived there.
