A '''boom''' can mean different things: 
 
 *A boom is a low-pitched [[sound]], often [[reverberation|reverberant]].  The sound of [[thunder]] and the sound of an [[explosion]] are often called booms. 
 *A boom is a long bar: 
 ** used in [[sailing]] and [[windsurfing]] to hold the [[sail]]. 
 ** used in [[waterskiing]] for the beginning skier to hold onto. 
 ** used in [[movies]] and [[television]] to hold [[microphone]]s. 
 *[[Boom (Belgium)|Boom]] is a municipality in Belgium
