 '''Lougres''' ({{IPA-fr|luɡʁ}}) is a [[Communes of France|commune]] in the [[Doubs]] [[Departments of France|department]] in [[Bourgogne-Franche-Comté]] in eastern [[France]]. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 Lougres is {{convert|14|km|mi|abbr=on}} southwest of [[Montbéliard]]. 
 
 The commune takes its name from the stream that runs through it, from its source to the river [[Doubs (river)|Doubs]].
