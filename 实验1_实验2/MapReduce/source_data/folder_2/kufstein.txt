 '''Kufstein''' is a town in the [[Austria]]n state of [[Tyrol (state)|Tyrol]]. The town is the capital of the [[Kufstein District]]. It has about 18,400 people lived there that makes it the second largest Tyrolean town, after the state capital [[Innsbruck]]. 
 
 The greatest landmark is [[Kufstein Fortress]], first mentioned in the 13th century.
