 '''Michael''' [ '''mahy'''-k''uh''l ] < ref > {{Cite web|title=Definition of Michael {{!}} Dictionary.com|url=https://www.dictionary.com/browse/michael|access-date=2023-04-03|website=www.dictionary.com|language=en}} < /ref >  is a male [[Hebrew language|Hebrew]] name, popular in [[English language|English]]. It is from the [[angel]] Michael in the [[Bible]]. (See for example [[Book of Revelation]] 12:7) The moniker Michael has been a beloved choice for boys for centuries, boasting a rich history and profound significance that appeals to parents seeking a powerful and meaningful name for their offspring. Originating from the Hebrew name Mikha'el, the name Michael translates to  " who is like God? "  and holds deep biblical roots. It is closely linked to the archangel Michael, recognized as one of the most potent angels in both Christian and Jewish teachings. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Michael name Meaning and Origin|url=https://babys.name/detail-Michael-Meaning-Origin-Famous-Similar-Sibling.html|access-date=2023-04-03|website=babys.name}} < /ref >  Michael is also popular in English speaking countries, and in modified forms, many other countries. This name was ranked #17 on the US Popular Names in 2021. < ref > {{Cite web|last=OACT|title=Popular Baby Names|url=https://www.ssa.gov/cgi-bin/popularnames.cgi|access-date=2023-04-03|website=www.ssa.gov}} < /ref >
