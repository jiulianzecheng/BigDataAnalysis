 '''Kozani''' is a city in northern [[Greece]]. It is the capital of [[Kozani Prefecture]] and of the region of [[Macedonia (Greece)|Macedonia]]. The population of the city is 38,591, according to 2001 census. In district of Kozani products the main amount of [[electrical energy]] of Greece.
