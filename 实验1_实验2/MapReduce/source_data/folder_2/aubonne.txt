 '''Aubonne''' is a [[municipality]] of the [[district]] of [[Morges (district)|Morges]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Vaud]] in [[Switzerland]]. The former municipality of [[Pizy]] merged on 1 July 2011, into the municipality of Aubonne. < ref name=Fusion > [http://www.bfs.admin.ch/bfs/portal/de/index/infothek/nomenklaturen/blank/blank/gem_liste/03.html Amtliches Gemeindeverzeichnis der Schweiz] published by the Swiss Federal Statistical Office {{in lang|de}} accessed 17 February 2011 < /ref > 
 
 On 5 December 2017, [[Michael I of Romania]] died in his home in Aubonne. < ref > {{cite web|last1=Murphy|first1=Brian|title=Michael I, last king of Romania and a Cold War exile, dies at 96|url=https://www.washingtonpost.com/local/obituaries/michael-i-last-king-of-romania-and-a-cold-war-exile-dies-at-96/2017/12/05/6549aec8-d9b2-11e7-b859-fb0995360725_story.html|publisher=The Washington Post}} < /ref >
