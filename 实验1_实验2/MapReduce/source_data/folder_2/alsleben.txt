 '''Alsleben''' ( < small > pronounced < /small >  [[Help:IPA/Standard German|[ˈalsleːbm̩]]]) is a town in [[Saxony-Anhalt]], [[Germany]]. It is in the [[Salzlandkreis]] district and is on the [[Saale]] river. The population was about 2,500 in 2020. The main political party is the [[Christian Democratic Union of Germany|CDU]]. A major industry is shipping and is part of the town's history. < ref > {{Cite web|last=Wehlmann|first=Andreas|title=Willkommen in Alsleben an der Saale!|url=http://www.alsleben-saale-online.de/de/Wirtschaftliches-aus-Alsleben/Geschichte/Alsleben-und-die-Saaleschifffahrt-01|access-date=2022-07-22|website=Alsleben eine alte Schifferstadt an der Saale|language=de}} < /ref >  Tourist attractions include a town hall, churches from the Middle Ages and a Water Tower (''Wasserturm'') from 1916. < ref > {{Cite web|last=Wehlmann|first=Andreas|title=Willkommen in Alsleben an der Saale!|url=http://www.alsleben-saale-online.de/de/TOURISMUS|access-date=2022-07-22|website=TOURISMUS IN ALSLEBEN|language=de}} < /ref >
