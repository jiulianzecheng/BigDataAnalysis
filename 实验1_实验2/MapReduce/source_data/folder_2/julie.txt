The name '''Julie''' is a [[French language|French]] female [[given name]]. The pronunciation of the name is [ '''joo'''-lee ]. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Definition of Julie {{!}} Dictionary.com|url=https://www.dictionary.com/browse/julie|access-date=2023-03-23|website=www.dictionary.com|language=en}} < /ref >  It comes from the [[Latin language|Latin]] name ''Julia''. ''Julia'' means  " youthful "  or  " soft haired "  in Latin. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Julie name Meaning and Origin|url=https://babys.name/detail-Julie-Meaning-Origin-Famous-Similar-Sibling.html|access-date=2023-03-23|website=babys.name}} < /ref >  The name Julie and its variants are feminine for the masculine name ''Julius''. Julie is very popular in the [[United States]]. The name was particularly popular between 1951 and 1991. Its variant, ''Julia'', has been in the top 100 female names dating back to 1980. Julie and its related names are being used by many famous people. They include [[Julie Andrews]] and [[Julie Benz]]. As ''Julia'' the name is currently being used for [[Atlantic Ocean|Atlantic Basin]] [[tropical cyclone]]s. This name was ranked #658 on the US Popular Names in 2021.  < ref > {{Cite web|last=OACT|title=Popular Baby Names|url=https://www.ssa.gov/cgi-bin/popularnames.cgi|access-date=2023-03-23|website=www.ssa.gov}} < /ref >
