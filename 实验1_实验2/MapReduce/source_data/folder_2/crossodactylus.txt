 '''''Crossodactylus''''' is a [[genus|group]] of [[frog]]s in the [[Family (biology)|family]] [[Hylodidae]]. < ref name=Frost/ > < ref name=AmphibiaWeb/ >  These frogs live in Brazil, southern Paraguay, and northern Argentina. These frogs are sometimes known as ''Limnocharis'', ''Tarsopterus'', ''Calamobates,'' and by the [[common name|English-language name]] '''spinythumb frogs'''. < ref name=Frost/ > < ref > {{Cite web|title=ITIS Standard Report Page: ''Crossodactylus ''|url=https://www.itis.gov/servlet/SingleRpt/SingleRpt;jsessionid=61521BA61E7C64F70E7A474B1B1AF5E3?search_topic=TSN & search_value=207893#null|access-date=2020-08-17|website=www.itis.gov}} < /ref >  They typically inhabit streams in mountainous areas in the [[Atlantic Forest]] or in montane [[savanna]]. < ref name= " Pimenta et al. 2015 " / >
