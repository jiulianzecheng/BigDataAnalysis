A '''keyboardist''' is a musician who plays one or more of the musical instruments called [[Keyboard instrument|keyboard]] instruments, such as the [[piano]], [[Organ (music)|organ]], or electronic [[synthesizer]]. They may also use [[MIDI]]. A person who plays piano is also called a pianist. A person who plays organ is called an organist. Many types of music use keyboardists, such as [[Rock and roll|rock]], [[pop music|pop]], [[funk]], and [[classical music]].
