 '''Slayer''' was an [[United States|American]] [[thrash metal]] band that began in [[California]] in 1981. The band was started [[Guitar|guitarists]] [[Jeff Hanneman]] and [[Kerry King]], as well as drummer [[Dave Lombardo]] and [[Bass guitar|bassist]]/singer [[Tom Araya]]. Slayer is one of the  " Big Four "  of thrash metal music, along with [[Metallica]], [[Anthrax (band)|Anthrax]] and [[Megadeth]]. < ref > {{cite web|last=Lee|first=Cosmo|title=Get Thrashed: The Story of Thrash Metal|url=http://www.stylusmagazine.com/articles/movie_review/get-thrashed-the-story-of-thrash-metal.htm|work=Stylus|date=2007-05-07|accessdate=2008-12-16|archive-date=2008-12-25|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20081225230004/http://www.stylusmagazine.com/articles/movie_review/get-thrashed-the-story-of-thrash-metal.htm|url-status=dead}} < /ref > 
 
 Slayer's music has a fast and angry [[Heavy metal music|heavy metal]] style. The words to the band's songs are about [[murder]]ers, [[Satan]], [[religion]], [[racism]] and [[war]]. Singing about these things gave the band a lot of [[controversy]]. Their music has inspired many other bands. Their third album, ''[[Reign in Blood]]'', has been called one of the [[Heavy metal music|heaviest]] thrash metal albums. < ref > {{Cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=UffkZCMjXzoC|title=The Bloody Reign of Slayer|last=McIver|first=Joel|date=2009-11-11|publisher=Omnibus Press|isbn=978-0-85712-038-0|language=en}} < /ref > 
 
 Slayer has released ten studio [[album]]s, two live albums and a [[box-set]]. Four of the band's albums have been [[RIAA certification|certified gold]] by the [[Recording Industry Association of America|RIAA]]. The band has been the top band at many large music festivals around the world and their music has been added to many [[video games]].
