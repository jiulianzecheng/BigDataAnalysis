 '''Property''' is something that is [[Ownership|owned]] by someone. If someone buys a [[car]], the car becomes their property. People who deal with buying or selling [[real estate]] will often talk about  " properties " , meaning [[house]]s, [[apartment]]s, [[office]]s etc.
