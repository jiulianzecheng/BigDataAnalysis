 '''Nalchik''' ({{lang-ru|Нальчик|p=ˈnalʲtɕɪk}}; [[Kabardian language|Kabardian]]: {{lang|kbd|Налщӏэч}} /{{IPA|naːɮɕʼakʲ}}/; {{lang-krc|Нальчик}} /{{IPA|naltsɯk}}/) is the [[capital city]] of the [[Kabardino-Balkar Republic]], [[Russia]]. It is in the foothills of the [[Caucasus Mountains]]. In 2010, 240,203 people lived there.
