'''Topik''' or '''topig''' is an [[Armenia|Armenian]] dish.  It is like a vegetarian [[meatball]].  It has a [[chickpea]] based paste and is usually mixed with [[potato]]es or [[flour]].  It has surrounding filling of [[onion]]s, [[nut (fruit)|nut]]s, and currants.  It is flavored with [[herb]]s, [[spice]]s, and [[tahini]].
