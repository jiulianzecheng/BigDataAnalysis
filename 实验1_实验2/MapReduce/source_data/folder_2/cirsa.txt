'''Cirsa''' is a Spanish [[Gambling|gaming]] and leisure company founded in 1978 and one of the world leaders in the sector. It has more than 14,000 employees and 212 companies present in more than 70 countries. In 2010 it had 88 [[bingo]] halls , 30 casinos and more than 50,000 operating gaming machines. In 2017 it has an approximate volume of 150 [[Casino|casinos]].
