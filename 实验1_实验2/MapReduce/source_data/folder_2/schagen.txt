 '''Schagen''' is a [[List of municipalities of the Netherlands|municipality]] in the [[Netherlands|Dutch]] [[Provinces of the Netherlands|province]] of [[North Holland]]. In 2019, 46,553 people lived there. 
 
 The city is between [[Alkmaar]] and [[Den Helder]]. It received [[city rights]] in 1415. 
 
 Since 2013, [[Harenkarspel]] and [[Zijpe]] have been part of the municipality of Schagen.
