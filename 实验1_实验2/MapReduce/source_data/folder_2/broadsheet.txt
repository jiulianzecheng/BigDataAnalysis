 A '''broadsheet''' is a [[:wikt:format|format]] of a [[newspaper]]. It is characterized by long [[vertical]] pages measuring 22 inches / 560 millimetres or more. The term  " broadsheet "  was derived from types of popular prints usually just of a single sheet, sold on the streets and containing various types of material, from ballads to political satire.
