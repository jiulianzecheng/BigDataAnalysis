 '''Bonnanaro''' (''Bunnànnaru'') is a [[town]] and ''[[Commune|comune]]'' (municipality) in the [[Province of Sassari]] in [[Sardinia]], [[Italy]]. As of 2016, 977 people lived there. < ref > {{Cite web |title=Istituto Nazionale di Statistica - ''Bilancio demografico al 30/11/2016'' |url=http://demo.istat.it/bilmens2016gen/index02.html |access-date=2019-04-09 |archive-date=2019-03-11 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190311225842/http://demo.istat.it/bilmens2016gen/index02.html |url-status=dead }} < /ref >  Its area is 21.84 & nbsp;km². It is 405 meters above sea level.
