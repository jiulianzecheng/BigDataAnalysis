 '''Iloilo''' is a [[Provinces of the Philippines|province]] in the [[Philippines]] located in the [[Western Visayas]] region. Its capital and largest city is [[Iloilo City]], the regional center of Western Visayas and administratively independent despite being grouped by the province.
