 '''Iago''' ({{IPAc-en|i|ˈ|ɑː|g|oʊ}}) is a fictional character in [[Shakespeare]]'s ''[[Othello]]''. Iago is the play's main villain and [[othello (character)|Othello's]] [[standard-bearer]].  
 
 He is the husband of [[Emilia (Othello)|Emilia]]. Iago hates Othello and creates a plan to destroy him by making him believe that his wife is having an affair with his lieutenant, [[Michael Cassio]].
