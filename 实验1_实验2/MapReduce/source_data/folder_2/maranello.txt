 '''Maranello''' is a city and ''[[comune]]'' in the [[Province of Modena]] in [[Emilia-Romagna]] in northern [[Italy]]. It is 18 & nbsp;km from [[Modena]]. 17,504 people lived there in 2017. It is known worldwide as the home of [[Ferrari]] and the [[Formula 1]] racing team [[Scuderia Ferrari]].
