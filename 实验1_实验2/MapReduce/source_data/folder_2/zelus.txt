'''Zelus''' ({{lang-el|Ζῆλος}}, ''zeal'') is the god of [[dedication]] and [[emulation]] in [[Greek mythology]]. He is the son of [[Styx (mythology)|Styx]] and [[Pallas]], and also the brother of [[Nike (mythology)|Nike]], [[Cratos]], and [[Bia]]. He and his brothers and sister were the friends of [[Zeus]] and were always with him. He was a constant companion of Zeus and personified zeal.
