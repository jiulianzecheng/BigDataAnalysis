 '''Meulebeke''' is a [[municipality]] in the [[Belgium|Belgian]] province of [[West Flanders]]. 
 
 In 2007, 10,983 people lived there. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://statbel.fgov.be/downloads/pop200701com.xls|title=Werkelijke bevolking per gemeente op 1 januari 2007 / Population de droit par commune au 1 janvier 2007|format=xls|archivedate=23 November 2008|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20081123144002/http://www.statbel.fgov.be/downloads/pop200701com.xls}} < /ref > 
 
 It is at 50° 57 North, 03° 17 East. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://statbel.fgov.be/pub/d1/p120_nl.xls|title=Geografische coördinaten van de gemeenten|format=xls|archivedate=24 November 2008|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20081124205332/http://www.statbel.fgov.be/pub/d1/p120_nl.xls}} < /ref >
