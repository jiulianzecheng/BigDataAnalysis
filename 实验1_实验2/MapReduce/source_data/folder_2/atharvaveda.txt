{{Hindu scriptures}} 
 The '''Atharva Veda (Atharvaveda)''' is the fourth [[Veda]], one of the [[Hindu]] [[scriptures]]. There are a number of Upanishads in this Veda. Ganapathy, Narayana, Devi, Atharva Siropanishaths are part of Atharva Veda. Bhavanopanisath is also in Atharva Veda.  Sankaracharya established Joshimath in Kedarnath one of the four popal centres of Hinduism to flourish The Atharva Veda.
