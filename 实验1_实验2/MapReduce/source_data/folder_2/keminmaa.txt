 '''Keminmaa''' is a municipality in [[Finnish Lapland|Lapland]], [[Finland]]. As of 2013, the municipality had a population of about 8,550. Neighbouring municipalities are [[Kemi]], [[Simo]], [[Tervola]] and [[Tornio]].
