 '''Arnstein''' ({{IPA-de|ˈaʁnʃtaɪn|lang|Arnstein.ogg}}) is a town in [[Main-Spessart]] in the ''[[Regierungsbezirk]]'' of [[Lower Franconia]] (''Unterfranken'') in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. 
 
 == Geography == 
 
 === Location === 
 The town is on the edges of the river Wern and is about 20 & nbsp;km from [[Schweinfurt]] and 25 & nbsp;km from [[Würzburg]].
