 '''Epesses''' was a [[municipality]] in the [[Switzerland|Swiss]] [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Vaud]]. It is in the district of [[Lavaux-Oron (district)|Lavaux-Oron]] on [[Lake Geneva]] (''Lac Léman''). On 1 July 2011, the former municipalities of [[Cully, Switzerland|Cully]], Epesses, [[Grandvaux, Switzerland|Grandvaux]], [[Riex]] and [[Villette (Lavaux)]] joined together to become the new municipality called [[Bourg-en-Lavaux]]. < ref name=Fusion > [http://www.bfs.admin.ch/bfs/portal/de/index/infothek/nomenklaturen/blank/blank/gem_liste/03.html Amtliches Gemeindeverzeichnis der Schweiz] published by the Swiss Federal Statistical Office {{in lang|de}} accessed 17 February 2011 < /ref >
