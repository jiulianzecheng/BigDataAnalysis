The '''Augustinians''' were [[Roman Catholicism|Roman Catholic]] [[Monastery|monastic]] order.  [[Martin Luther]] became an Augustinian monk at St. Augustine's Monastery in the German city of [[Erfurt]] in 1505. He later started the [[Reformation]]. 
 
 The name comes from [[Augustine of Hippo]].
