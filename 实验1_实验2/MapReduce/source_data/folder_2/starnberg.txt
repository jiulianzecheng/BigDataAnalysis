 '''Starnberg''' is a town in [[Upper Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. It is the capital of the district of [[Starnberg (district)|Starnberg]]. 
 
 It is about {{convert|30|km|abbr=off}} southwest of [[Munich]] and is at the northern end of [[Lake Starnberg]].
