'''Kippen''' is a small village approximately {{convert|10|mi|km}} west of the city of [[Stirling]], [[Scotland]] (also referred to as the Kingdom of Kippen).  It lies between the [[Gargunnock]] and [[Fintry]] hills and overlooks the [[strath]] of the [[River Forth]].  The name probably comes from [[Goidelic languages|Gaelic]], and means  " Place of the little stump " ,  " outcrop "  or  " block " . < ref > [http://www.kippen-village.co.uk/ Kippen : village of Kippen in Scotland < !-- Bot generated title -- > ] < /ref >
