 '''Lisieux''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is found in the [[Regions of France|region]] [[Basse-Normandie]] in the [[Calvados (department)|Calvados]] [[Departments of France|department]] in the northwest of [[France]]. 
 Death place of [[St. Therese of Lisieux]] (Sainte Thérèse de l'[[Jesus|Enfant Jésus]]), who entered the [[Carmelites|Carmelite]] convent at the age of 15.
