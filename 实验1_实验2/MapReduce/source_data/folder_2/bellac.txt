 '''Bellac''' ({{Lang-oc|Belac}}) is a [[Communes of France|''commune'']] in the [[Haute-Vienne]] [[Departments of France|department]] in the [[Nouvelle-Aquitaine]] [[Regions of France|region]] in western [[France]]. It is a [[Prefectures in France|subprefecture]] of the [[Haute-Vienne]] [[Departments of France|department]]. It is also the [[Capital city|capital]] of the ''[[Arrondissements of France|arrondissement]]'' of the same name. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 The city of Bellac is near the ''Monts de Blond'' (some [[hill]]s that are part of the [[Massif Central]]), in the northwest of the department. The [[Vincou]] [[river]] (a [[tributary]] of the [[Gartempe]]) flows through the city. 
 
 Bellac is at about {{Convert|45|km|mi|abbr=on }} from [[Limoges]], {{Convert|80|km|mi|abbr=on }} from [[Poitiers]], {{Convert|100|km|mi|abbr=on }} from [[Angoulême]], {{Convert|230|km|mi|abbr=on }} from [[Bordeaux]] and {{Convert|380|km|mi|abbr=on }} from [[Paris]]. 
 
 The ''commune'' has an area of {{Convert|24.4|km2|sqmi|abbr=on }}. < ref name= " insee-geo " > {{cite web |url=https://www.insee.fr/fr/statistiques/1405599?geo=COM-87011 |title=Commune de Bellac (87011) |work=Comparateur de territoire |publisher=Institut national de la statistique et des études économiques - INSEE |language=fr |access-date=29 March 2017 }} < /ref >  Its average [[altitude]] is {{Convert|238|m|ft|abbr=on}}; at the [[City and town halls|city hall]], the altitude is {{Convert|250|m|ft|abbr=on }}. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://www.map-france.com/Bellac-87300/ |title=Bellac |publisher=Map-France.com |access-date=29 March 2017 }} < /ref >

 == Administration == 
 Bellac is a [[Prefectures in France|subprefecture]] of the Haute-Vienne department, the capital of the ''arrondissement'' of [[Arrondissement of Bellac|Bellac]] and the [[administrative centre]] ({{lang-fr|chef-lieu}}) of the [[Cantons of France|canton]] of [[Canton of Bellac|Bellac]], with 18,178  inhabitants  < small > (2014) < /small > . 
 
 It is part of the [[Communes of France|intercommunality]] ''Le Haut-Limousin en Marche'' ({{lang-fr|Communauté de communes Haut-Limousin en Marche}}).
