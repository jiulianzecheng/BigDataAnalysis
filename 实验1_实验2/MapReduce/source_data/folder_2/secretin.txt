'''Secretin''' is a [[peptide]] [[hormone]] that regulates water [[homeostasis|balance]] throughout the body. It is produced in secretory glands of the [[duodenum]]. < ref name= " pmid7000396 " > {{cite journal |vauthors=Häcki WH |title=Secretin |journal=Clinics in Gastroenterology |volume=9 |issue=3 |pages=609–32 |year=1980 |doi=10.1016/S0300-5089(21)00474-0 |pmid=7000396 }} < /ref >  In humans, the secretin peptide is encoded by the SCT [[gene]], which sits on [[chromosome]] 11. < ref name= " pmid2315322 " > {{cite journal |vauthors=Kopin AS, Wheeler MB, Leiter AB |title=Secretin: structure of the precursor and tissue distribution of the mRNA |journal=Proceedings of the National Academy of Sciences of the United States of America |volume=87 |issue=6 |pages=2299–303 |year=1990 |pmid=2315322 |pmc=53674 |jstor=2354038 |bibcode=1990PNAS...87.2299K |doi=10.1073/pnas.87.6.2299 |doi-access=free }} < /ref >    
 
 In 2007, secretin was discovered to play a role in [[osmoregulation]] by acting on the [[hypothalamus]], [[pituitary|pituitary gland]], and [[kidney]]. < ref name= " Chu07 " > {{cite journal |authors=Chu JY |display-authors = etal |title=Phenotypes developed in secretin receptor-null mice indicated a role for secretin in regulating renal water reabsorption |journal=Molecular and Cellular Biology |volume=27 |issue=7 |pages=2499–511 |year=2007 |pmid=17283064 |pmc=1899889 |doi=10.1128/MCB.01088-06 }} < /ref > < ref name= " Chu " > {{cite journal |authors=Chu J.Y. |display-authors = etal |title=Secretin as a neurohypophysial factor regulating body water homeostasis |journal=Proceedings of the National Academy of Sciences of the United States of America |volume=106 |issue=37 |pages=15961–6 |year=2009 |pmid=19805236 |pmc=2747226 |jstor=40484830 |bibcode=2009PNAS..10615961C |doi=10.1073/pnas.0903695106 |doi-access = free }} < /ref > 
 
 Secretin was the first hormone to be identified. < ref > {{cite journal |vauthors=Henriksen JH, Schaffalitzky de Muckadell OB |title=Sekretin - det første hormon |trans-title=Secretin--the first hormone |language=Danish |journal=Ugeskrift for Laeger |volume=164 |issue=3 |pages=320–5 |year=2002 |id= |pmid=11816326 }} < /ref > 
 The crucial experiments were done at [[University College London]] in 1902. The term '[[hormone]]' was invented in 1905. < ref name= " pmid_15308687 " > {{Citation |last=Hirst |first=BH |year=2004 |title=Secretin and the exposition of hormonal control |journal=J Physiol |volume=560 |issue=Pt 2 |pages=339 |doi=10.1113/jphysiol.2004.073056 |pmid=15308687|pmc=1665254 }} < /ref >
