 A '''drum''' is a [[musical instrument]]. It is played with the [[hand]]s, sometimes using a [[drum stick]] (a stick for hitting a drum). A collection of drums and cymbals is called a [[drum kit]], or drum set.  
 [[File:Armenian Dhol.jpg|thumb|right|An [[Armenia|Armenian]] Dhol drum]] 
 Drums are used to keep a steady beat in a song. They give [[music]] of many kinds a sense of feeling. For example, to make a song to be slow or fast, the drums play slower or faster. 
 
 A drum is a [[percussion instrument]], which means it makes a noise by being hit.  There are many types such as cymbals and [[bell]]s; even a simple piece of wood can be used as a percussion instrument.  
 
 The [[drum kit]] is a group of drums  &  [[cymbal]]s to make beats for music. Drum kits are used in most types of popular music, including [[Rock and roll|rock]], [[jazz]], [[country music|country]], [[blues]], and many others. 
 
 ==Acoustic Drum== 
 Acoustic drum kit is a set of drums and [[Cymbal|cymbals]] designed to be played as one instrument. < ref > {{Cite web|date=2018-03-14|title=Acoustic vs Electronic Drums: 6 Things to Consider|url=https://consordini.com/acoustic-vs-electronic-drums/|access-date=2020-10-23|website=Consordini.com|language=en-US}} < /ref >  A standard drum kit is made from a [[bass drum]], [[snare drum]], set of tom toms, [[Hi-hat|hi hat]] cymbals, a ride cymbal and a crash cymbal. The sound is produced by striking the drums or cymbals with a [[drum stick]] or mallet. 
 
 ==Electric Drum== 
 An electric drum kit is essentially a collection of sample pads set up in the same way as an acoustic drum kit. The pads themselves are designed to produce little acoustic sound, other than the dead sound of the wooden drum sticks hitting rubber. The drum kit sends electrical signals to an amplifier, speaker, or pair of headphones to be heard.  This makes them ideal for practicing quietly.  Recording electronic drums is very easy.
