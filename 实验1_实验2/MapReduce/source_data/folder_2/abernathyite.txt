'''Abernathyite''' is a pale, [[yellow]]-colored [[mineral]] that forms [[crystal]]s.  It was named for Jess Abernathy, the person who found the first samples of this mineral.  It is found in a [[Mining|mine]] in Emery County, in the [[United States]].
