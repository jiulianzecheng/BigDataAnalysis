In [[Greek mythology]], '''Apate''' was the [[personification]] of [[wikt:deceit|deceit]]. She was one of the [[evil]] [[spirit]]s released from [[Pandora's box]]. Her mother was [[Nyx]], the personification of night. < ref > {{cite book|last=Auset|first=Brandi|title=The Goddess Guide: Exploring the Attributes and Correspondences of the Divine Feminine|url=https://archive.org/details/goddessguideexpl0000ause|publisher=Llewellyn Worldwide|year=2009|isbn=9780738715513}} < /ref > < ref > {{cite book|last=Leach|first=Marjorie|title=Guide to the Gods|year=1991|publisher=Greenwood|pages=643}} < /ref >  Her father was [[Erebos]], the personification of [[darkness]].
