 '''Winschoten''' is a small city in the province of [[Groningen (province)|Groningen]], the [[Netherlands]]. Since 2010, it has been a part of the [[municipality]] of [[Oldambt]]. It has about 18,000 people (2013). 
 
 There is a [[Winschoten railway station|railway station]] with direct connections to [[Groningen (city)|Groningen]] and [[Leer]] (Germany).
