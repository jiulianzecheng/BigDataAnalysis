 '''Jarnac''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is in [[Nouvelle-Aquitaine]] in the [[Charente]] [[Departments of France|department]] in southwest [[France]]. 
 
 It is the birthplace and resting place of [[François Mitterrand]], [[President of France]] (1981–1995). 
 
 == Geography == 
 Jarnac is on the river [[Charente (river)|Charente]]. 
 
 == History == 
 In 1569, it was the site of the [[Battle of Jarnac]].
