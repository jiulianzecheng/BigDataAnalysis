'''''Antliodus''''' is an extinct genus of [[Chondrichthyes|cartilaginous fish]], a petalodont from the [[Carboniferous]]. It is known from the [[Mississippian (geology)|Mississippian]] of the [[United States]], [[Britain]], and [[Belgium]]. Though many species have been reported in the past, a lack of recent revisions means some may be dubious. 
 
 == Species == 
 
 * ''Antliodus cucullus'' 
 * ''Antliodus minutus'' 
 * ''Antliodus parvulus'' 
 * ''Antliodus politus'' 
 * ''Antliodus sarculus'' 
 * ''Antliodus similis'' 
 * ''Antliodus simplex'' 
 * ''Antliodus sulcatus'' 
 * ''Antliodus truncatus'' 
 * ''Antliodus robustus'' 
 * ''Antliodus gracilis'' 
 * ''Antliodus arctuatus''
