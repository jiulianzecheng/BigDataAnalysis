The '''Defense Advanced Research Projects Agency (DARPA)''' is an agency of the [[United States Department of Defense]] responsible for the [[development]] of emerging technologies for use by the [[United States Armed Forces|military]]. 
 Currently, DARPA's mission statement is “to make pivotal [[investment]]s in breakthrough technologies for national security.”
