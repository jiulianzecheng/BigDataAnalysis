 '''Dokri''' ({{lang-sd|ڏوڪري}}) is a [[town]] in [[Larkana District]],  [[Sindh]] [[province]] of [[Pakistan]] and is the capital town of the [[Dokri Taluka]]. < ref > {{Cite web |url=http://www.lgdsindh.com.pk/tmadokri6.htm |title=Union Administrations of Taluka   Dokri |access-date=2008-07-17 |archive-date=2007-08-07 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20070807185954/http://www.lgdsindh.com.pk/tmadokri6.htm |url-status=dead }} < /ref >  [[Moenjodaro]] is found 7 & nbsp;km away in East-South of the Dokri town. The town has been the point of several Communist movements in the past. < ref name= " Dawn " / > 
 
 ==Etymology== 
 Dokri was once called ''Lab-e-Darya'' or a ''lip of the Indus'', the name is believed to have been taken from ''Dhaatki'', or ''‘old woman’''. < ref name= " Dawn " > {{Cite web |url=http://www.dawn.com/weekly/dmag/archive/020825/dmag10.htm |title=Dokri in a shambles - Dawn Pakistan |access-date=2008-07-17 |archive-date=2007-07-13 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20070713075816/http://www.dawn.com/weekly/dmag/archive/020825/dmag10.htm |url-status=dead }} < /ref >
