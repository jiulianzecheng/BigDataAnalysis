 '''Obergoms''' is a [[municipality]] of the [[district]] of [[Goms (district)|Goms]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Valais]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 On 1 January 2009, the former municipalities of [[Obergesteln]], [[Oberwald]] and [[Ulrichen]] merged into the new municipality of Obergoms.
