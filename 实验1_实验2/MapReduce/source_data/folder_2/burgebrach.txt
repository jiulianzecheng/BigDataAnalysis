 '''Burgebrach''' is a [[municipality]] in the [[Bamberg (district)|district of Bamberg]] in [[Upper Franconia Government Region|Upper Franconia]], [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. About 6,500 people live in Burgebrach. There are 27 [[village]]s in the municipality.
