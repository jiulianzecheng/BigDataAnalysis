 '''Sankuru''' is one of the 26 [[province]]s of the [[Democratic Republic of the Congo]]. < ref name= " cia.gov " > {{cite web|author=Central Intelligence Agency|title=Democratic Republic of the Congo|work=The World Factbook|publisher=Central Intelligence Agency|location=Langley, Virginia|year=2016|url=https://www.cia.gov/library/publications/the-world-factbook/geos/cg.html|access-date=20 March 2020|archive-date=10 August 2016|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160810035132/https://www.cia.gov/library/publications/the-world-factbook/geos/cg.html|url-status=dead}} < /ref >  Its capital is [[Lusambo]]. 
 
 The province is divided into 6 territories: 
 
 * [[Katako-Kombe (territory)|Katako Kombe]] - with the town of [[Katako-Kombe]] 
 * [[Kole (territory)|Kole]] - with the town of [[Kole, Democratic Republic of the Congo|Kole]] 
 * [[Lodja (territory)|Lodja]] - with the City of [[Lodja]] 
 * [[Lomela Territory|Lomela]] - with the town of [[Lomela]] 
 * [[Lubefu (territory)|Lubefu]] - with the city of [[Lubefu]]  
 * [[Lusambo (territory)|Lusambo]] - with the City of [[Lusambo]] 
 
 The [[Sankuru River]], [[Lomami River]], [[Lubefu River]] and [[Lukenie River]] are all in the province.
