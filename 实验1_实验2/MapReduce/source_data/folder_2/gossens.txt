 '''Gossens''' was a [[municipality]] in [[Yverdon (district)|Yverdon]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Vaud]] in [[Switzerland]]. It became part of neighbouring [[Donneloye]] on 1 January 2008. < ref name=Fusion > [http://www.bfs.admin.ch/bfs/portal/de/index/infothek/nomenklaturen/blank/blank/gem_liste/03.html Amtliches Gemeindeverzeichnis der Schweiz] published by the Swiss Federal Statistical Office {{in lang|de}} accessed 19 July 2011 < /ref > 
 
 It is on the main road between [[Yverdon]] and [[Moudon]]. The economy is mostly agricultural.
