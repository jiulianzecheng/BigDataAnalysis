 '''''Blockland''''' is a [[multiplayer]] [[Video game|computer game]] made on the Torque [[Game engine|Game Engine]], where people make things using blocks. Blockland has around 35,500 players. < ref name= " Blockland Stats " > {{Cite web|url=http://blockland.kalphiter.com/ID/stats.php|title=Kalphiter's ID list and stats|access-date=2009-08-03|year=2009|archive-date=2010-03-03|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20100303083344/http://blockland.kalphiter.com/ID/stats.php|url-status=dead}} < /ref >  People could build up to 128,000 bricks on every server to keep the game running best.  
 
 In 2009, [[Lego]] were talking to Eric  " Badspot "  Hartman about selling Blockland.
