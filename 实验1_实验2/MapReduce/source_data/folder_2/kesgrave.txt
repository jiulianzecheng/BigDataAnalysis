 '''Kesgrave''' is a town on the eastern side of [[Ipswich]] in [[Suffolk]]. Its population is roughly around 14,000 people. < ref > {{Cite web|url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/england/suffolk|title=Suffolk - BBC News|website=BBC News|language=en-GB|access-date=2019-06-30}} < /ref > 
 
 To the west of Kesgrave, there is a town called [[Martlesham]]. < ref > {{Cite web |url=https://www.ipswichstar.co.uk/news/nine-reasons-why-kesgrave-rushmere-and-martlesham-are-the-best-places-to-live-in-england-1-4915206 |title=Archived copy |access-date=2019-06-30 |archive-date=2019-06-30 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190630223602/https://www.ipswichstar.co.uk/news/nine-reasons-why-kesgrave-rushmere-and-martlesham-are-the-best-places-to-live-in-england-1-4915206 |url-status=dead }} < /ref >  Kesgrave has an historical church called All Saints.
