'''Alexander''' is a male [[given name]]. It comes from the [[Greek language|Greek]] word Ἀλέξανδρος (''Aléxandros''), which means the  " defender of men "  (from ἀλέξειν (''aléxein'') -  " to defend " , and ἀνδρός (''andrós'') -  " man " ). 
 
 == People == 
 
 * [[Trent Alexander-Arnold]], an English professional footballer. He plays for Premier League club Liverpool and the England national team. He plays as a right-back. He was born in West Derby, Liverpool. 
 * [[Alexander Pushkin]], Russian poet, playwright, and novelist (1799–1837) 
 * [[Alexander Graham Bell]], British-born American scientist and inventor known for the telephone (1847–1922)
