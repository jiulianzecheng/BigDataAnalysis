'''Chastity''' is the [[human sexuality|sexual behavior]] of a man or woman which is right and proper according to the [[morality|moral]] rules of a [[culture]] or [[religion]]. It has recently begun to mean sexual [[abstinence]] (not having [[sexual intercourse|sex]] at all), especially before [[marriage]]. < ref > {{Cite web |url= http://dictionary.reference.com/browse/Chastity |title=Chastity {{!}} Define Chastity at Dictionary.com |work=reference.com |accessdate= 28 December 2010}} < /ref >  However, it can also be used for all kinds of people (even married people) with more meaning than simply not having sex. The word chastity came from the [[Latin language|Latin]] word ''castitatem'', meaning  " purity " . < ref > {{Cite web
