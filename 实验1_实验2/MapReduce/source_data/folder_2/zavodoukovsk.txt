 '''Zavodoukovsk''' ({{lang-ru|Заводоуковск}}) is a town in [[Tyumen Oblast]], [[Russia]]. It is {{convert|96|km|sp=us}} southeast of [[Tyumen]] on the [[Bolshoy Uk River]]. In 2010, 25,647 people lived there.
