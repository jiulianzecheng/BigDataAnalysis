 '''Luganville''' is the capital of the north [[Vanuatu]]an province of [[Sanma Province|Sanma]]. In 2020, 17,719 people lived there, which makes it the largest city in Sanma Province, and the second-largest city in Vanuatu (after [[Port Vila]]).
