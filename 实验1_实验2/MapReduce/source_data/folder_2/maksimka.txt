 '''''Maksimka''''' ({{lang-ru|Максимка}}) is a 1953 Soviet [[adventure]] movie directed by Vladimir Braun. < ref > [https://www.kinopoisk.ru/film/46513/ Максимка (1952)] < /ref > < ref > [https://www.afisha.ru/movie/183743/ Максимка] < /ref >  It stars [[Tolya Bovykin]], [[Boris Andreyev (actor)|Boris Andreyev]], and [[Vyacheslav Tikhonov]].
