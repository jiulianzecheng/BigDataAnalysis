 '''Amorgos''' is an [[island]] in the [[Aegean Sea]]. It is a part of [[Greece]] and a part of the [[Cyclades]]. Its area is 126,3 & nbsp;km < sup > 2 < /sup > . There is one municipality which has 1859 inhabitants. < ref > {{Cite web |url=http://www.ypes.gr/tab2.asp?TA=2 & ID=3101Angaben |title=2001 census |access-date=2009-05-10 |archive-date=2020-09-24 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20200924161456/https://www.ypes.gr/tab2.asp?TA=2 & ID=3101Angaben |url-status=dead }} < /ref >  Its highest mountain Krikellos has 821 m.
