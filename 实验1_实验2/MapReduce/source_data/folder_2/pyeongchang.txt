 '''Pyeongchang''' is a [[city]] in the [[Republic of Korea]]. They hosted the [[2018 Winter Olympics]] and the [[2018 Winter Paralympics]]. < ref > [http://edition.cnn.com/2011/SPORT/07/06/olympics.winter.pyeongchang.2018/index.html  " S. Korean city to host 2018 Winter Olympics " ] ''CNN''. 6 July 2011. Retrieved 6 November 2011 < /ref >   
 
 The town has many mountains, with a lot of snow in the winter. As of 2013, its population was about 44,000.
