 Bektashism or in Turkish language: Bekta≈üilik and in Albanian language: Tarikati Bektashi, is one of the largest [[Sufism]] [[Dervish]] [[Tarika]]. In the [[Balkans]] and [[East Thrace]] the European part of Turkey, there are followers in [[Albania]] and [[Kosovo]], in [[North Macedonia]], [[Bosnia and Herzegovina]] and [[Bulgaria]]. < ref > {{cite web | url=https://www.euronews.com/2018/06/11/the-bektashis-a-sufi-community-that-still-stands-for-tolerance-despite-a-long-history-of-p | title=The Bektashis: A Sufi community that still stands for tolerance despit | date=11 June 2018 }} < /ref > 
 ==History== 
 Bektashism dates back to the 13th century. The founder was Haji Bektash Veli. It is very close to [[Alevism]].
