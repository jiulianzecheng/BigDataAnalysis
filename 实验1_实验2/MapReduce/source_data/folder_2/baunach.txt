 '''Baunach''' is a [[town]] in the [[Germany|German]] [[States of Germany|state]] (bundesland) of [[Bavaria]]. It is in the [[List of German districts|district]] of [[Bamberg]] in [[Upper Franconia Government Region|Upper Franconia]]. About 3,950 people lived there in 2005.
