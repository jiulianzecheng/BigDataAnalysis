 '''''Freejack''''' is a 1992 [[science fiction]] and action [[movie]] directed by [[Geoff Murphy]]. This movie is set in the year 2009. It is about very wealthy people becoming immortal. [[Rene Russo]] plays Julie. [[Mick Jagger]] plays Victor. [[Emilio Estevez]] plays Alex. 
 
 The movie got very bad reviews from movie critics.
