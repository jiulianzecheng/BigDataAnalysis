'''Tipas''' is a {{convert|6670|m|ft}} [[volcano]] in the [[Andes]] [[Mountain range|range]], in [[Argentina]]. It is the [[List of mountains in the Andes|9th highest mountain in the Andes]]. It is also known as '''Cazadero''' and '''Cerro Walther Penck'''.
