 '''The Batplane''', later known as '''the Batwing''' is the personal airplane for [[Batman]]. The plane was built by [[Wayne Enterprises]] by [[Lucius Fox]]. It is part plane part helicopter or ''The Batcopter''. < ref > {{Cite web |url=http://www.dcuguide.com/who.php?name=batplane |title=The Unofficial Batplane Biography |access-date=2013-01-13 |archive-date=2012-04-08 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120408054728/http://www.dcuguide.com/who.php?name=batplane |url-status=dead }} < /ref >  The plane holds many guns and gadgets in case of an air attack. It also can air-park while up in the air. The plane appeared in ''[[Batman (1989 movie)|Batman]]'', ''[[Batman Forever]]'', and as '''The Bat''' in ''[[The Dark Knight Rises]]''.
