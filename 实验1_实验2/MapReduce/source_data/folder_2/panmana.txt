 '''Panmana''' is a village in [[Kollam district]] in the [[Indian state|state]] of [[Kerala]], [[India]]. < ref name= " censusindia " > {{cite web|url=http://www.censusindia.gov.in/Census_Data_2001/Village_Directory/Population_data/Population_5000_and_Above.aspx|title=Census of India : Villages with population 5000  &  above |publisher=Registrar General  &  Census Commissioner, India |access-date=2008-12-10}} < /ref > 
 
 == Demographics == 
 As of the 2001 Indian [[census]], Panmana had a population of 29139, of whom 14360 were male and 14779 were female.
