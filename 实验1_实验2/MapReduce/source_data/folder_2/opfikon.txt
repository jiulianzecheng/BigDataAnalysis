 '''Opfikon''' is a [[municipality]] and [[city]] in the [[district]] of [[Bülach (district)|Bülach]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Zurich (canton)|Zurich]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 ==Villages== 
 Opfikon, Glattbrugg and Oberhausen. 
 
 ==Economy== 
 [[Adecco]] S.A., the world's largest employment agency, is headquartered in Glattbrugg.
