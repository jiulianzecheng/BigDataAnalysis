 '''Nendeln''' is a village in [[Liechtenstein]]. It is in the [[municipality]] of [[Eschen]]. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 The village is in north-central Liechtenstein. It is on the main road [[Schaan]]-[[Schaanwald]]. The road links Liechtenstein with [[Buchs, St. Gallen|Buchs]], [[Switzerland]] and [[Feldkirch]], [[Austria]].
