 '''Lainingthou Pishatao''' ({{lang-mni|ꯂꯥꯢꯅꯤꯡꯊꯧ ꯄꯤꯁꯥꯇꯥꯎ|lāi-nīng-thou pī-sā-tāo|'' " Maker of All " ''}}), < ref name= " thesangaiexpress.com " > {{Cite web|title=Speaker wishes|url=https://www.thesangaiexpress.com/Encyc/2020/9/16/Speaker-wishes-.html|access-date=2022-03-17|website=www.thesangaiexpress.com|language=en}} < /ref > < ref name= " e-pao.net " > {{Cite web|title=Speaker wishes : 17th sep20 ~ E-Pao! Headlines|url=http://e-pao.net/GP.asp?src=Snipp6..170920.sep20|access-date=2022-03-17|website=e-pao.net}} < /ref >  mononymously known as '''Pisatao''' ({{lang-omp|ꯄꯤꯁꯥꯇꯥꯎ}}), is a primordial deity in [[Meitei mythology]] and [[Meitei religion|religion]]. He is the god of [[craftsman|craftsmen]] and [[architect]]s. He is regarded as the divine personification of the ultimate reality, the abstract creative power inherent in deities, living and non living beings in the [[universe]]. < ref > {{Cite news|url=http://hueiyenlanpao.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/06/15.09.2018-B4.pdf|title=পিসাতাও খুৎহৈবগী থৌরম|date=2018|website=hueiyenlanpao.com|language=mni}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite news|url=http://hueiyenlanpao.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/06/15.09.2017-B4.pdf|title=লাইনিংথৌ পিসাতাও খুৎহৈবগী চাওনা অকায়বা পীরক্লে। অমগুমা হিংলি।|date=2017|website=hueiyenlanpao.com|language=mni}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite news|url=http://hueiyenlanpao.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/06/15.09.2016-B3.pdf|title=লাইনিংথৌ পিসাতাও খুৎহৈবা য়োকখত্নবা অসিদা মীয়ায়া তুং ইনবীরকউ। নিংথৌ.|date=2016|website=hueiyenlanpao.com|language=mni}} < /ref > < ref > Politics, Society, and Cosmology in India's North East - Page 49 - N. Vijaylakshmi Brara · 1998 < /ref > < ref name= " thesangaiexpress.com " / > < ref name= " e-pao.net " / > < ref > {{Cite web|title=Speaker wishes|url=https://www.thesangaiexpress.com/Encyc/2020/9/16/Speaker-wishes-.amp.html|access-date=2022-03-17|website=www.thesangaiexpress.com|language=en}} < /ref > 
 He is one of the divine manifestations of Supreme God [[Atingkok|Atingkok (Tengbanba Mapu)]]. < ref > {{Cite book|url=https://archive.org/details/in.ernet.dli.2015.461915/page/n87/mode/2up?q=Pisatao|title=A Critical Study Of The Religious Philosophy|date=1991|publisher=|pages=87}} < /ref >  In many legends, he is one of the four Gods who control the four directions. < ref > {{Cite book|url=https://archive.org/details/politicssocietyc00brar/page/48/mode/2up?q=Pishatao|title=Politics, society, and cosmology in India's North East|last=Brara|first=N. Vijaylakshmi|date=1998|publisher=Delhi ; New York : Oxford University Press|others=Internet Archive|isbn=978-0-19-564331-2|pages=48}} < /ref > 
 
 Historically, his pantheon was replaced by [[Vishwakarma]] of [[Hinduism]].
