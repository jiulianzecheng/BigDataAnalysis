 '''Bucharest''' is the [[Capital (city)|capital city]] of [[Romania]]. It is the largest city in the country. The city has a [[population]] of 1.9 million people. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.edrc.ro/recensamant.jsp?regiune_id=2959 & judet_id=3000 & localitate_id=3001|title=Structura Etno-demografică a României|website=www.edrc.ro}} < /ref >  It is made up of 6  " sectors " . It has a [[humid continental climate]] (''Cfa'' in the [[Koeppen climate classification]]). It became the capital of [[Romania]] in 1862. It is the centre of Romanian media, [[culture]] and [[art]]. Bucharest is the 6th largest city in the [[European Union]] by population within city limits. 
 
 According to European standards, Bucharest is an old city. The first mention of it appears in 1459. Its eclectic architecture is a mixture of historical, transitional, [[Communist]] and modern styles. In the period between the two [[World War]]s, thanks to the elegant urban architecture and ingenuity of the urban elite, Bucharest was named  " Paris of the East " , or  " Little Paris "  (Micul Paris).
