 '''Allos''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]] in southeastern [[France]]. It is in the [[Alpes-de-Haute-Provence]] [[Departments of France|department]] in the [[Provence-Alpes-Côte d'Azur]] region. 
 
 The [[Verdon (river)|Verdon]] river is in the town.
