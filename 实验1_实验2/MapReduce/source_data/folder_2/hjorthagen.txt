 '''Hjorthagen''' is a district in the northeastern part of central [[Stockholm]], located near [[Ropsten]] and [[Lidingöbron]]. Since 1997, Hjorthagen has been part of [[Östermalm|Östermalm district]]. 
 
 ==District== 
 [[File:Hjorthagen 2 22072006.jpg|thumb| [[Hjorthagen Church]] on the left and Värtaverket's chimney on the right.]] 
 Hjorthagen belongs to Östermalm district, it had around 8,000 inhabitants in 2017 and is estimated to have over 17,000 inhabitants by year 2027. < ref > {{Cite web |title=Statistik och fakta om Stockholm - Stockholms stad |url=https://start.stockholm/om-stockholms-stad/utredningar-statistik-och-fakta/statistik/ |access-date=2022-03-20 |website=start.stockholm |language=sv}} < /ref >  The district borders [[Gärdet|Ladugårdsgärdet]] and [[Djurgården|Norra Djurgården]].
