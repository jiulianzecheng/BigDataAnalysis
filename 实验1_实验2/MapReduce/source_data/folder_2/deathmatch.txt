A '''deathmatch''' (shortened to DM) is a common gameplay mode included in many [[First-person shooter|shooter]] and [[real-time strategy]] (RTS) [[Video game|computer games]] and  " MilSim "  games like [[airsoft]] and [[paintball]]. The goal of a deathmatch game is to kill as many other players as possible.
