 '''Eggenthal''' is a [[Municipalities of Germany|municipality]] in [[Ostallgäu]] in [[Bavaria]] in [[Germany]]. It is in the [[Allgäu]] region. 
   
 There are the [[Gemarkung|districts]] Bayersried and Eggenthal. There are also the villages of Romatsried and Holzstetten as well as several small hamlets.
