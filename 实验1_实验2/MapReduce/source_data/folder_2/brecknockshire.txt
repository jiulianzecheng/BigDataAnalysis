 '''Brecknockshire''', also known as the County of Brecknock, Breconshire, or the County of Brecon is one of thirteen historic counties of [[Wales]], and a former administrative county. Named after its county town of [[Brecon]], the county is [[mountain]]ous and primarily rural.
