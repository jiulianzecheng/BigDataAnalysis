 '''Cascavel''' is a city in western [[Paraná (state)|Paraná]] state, in the [[Southern Brazil|south region]] of [[Brazil]]. It has, approximately 300.000 [[inhabitant]]s. 
 
 The city is on a [[plateau]] 781 meters above [[sea level]]. It is 504 & nbsp;km west of the state capital of [[Curitiba]], 605 & nbsp;km west of the sea [[Harbour|port]] of [[Paranaguá]] and 140 & nbsp;km from [[Foz do Iguaçu]]/ the 3 [[border]]s ([[Paraguay]], [[Argentina]] and [[Brazil]]).
