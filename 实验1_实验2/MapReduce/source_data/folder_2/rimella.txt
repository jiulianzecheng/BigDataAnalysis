 '''Rimella''' is a ''[[comune]]'' (municipality) in the [[Province of Vercelli]] in the [[Italy|Italian]] region of [[Piedmont]]. It is about {{convert|100|km|mi}} northeast of [[Turin]] and about {{convert|70|km|mi}} northwest of [[Vercelli]]. 
 
 Rimella is next to these municipalities: [[Bannio Anzino]], [[Calasca-Castiglione]], [[Cravagliana]], [[Fobello]], and [[Valstrona]].
