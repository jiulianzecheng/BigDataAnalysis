'''Zakraoui''' or '''Zakaraoui''' (زكراوي ou ذكراوي) is a name that can refer to:   
 
 * The [[Zkara]] (plural of Zakraoui), an arabized Berber Zenati tribe origin etablished in the city of Mestferki, in the province of Oujda, in the nort-east of [[Morocco]].
