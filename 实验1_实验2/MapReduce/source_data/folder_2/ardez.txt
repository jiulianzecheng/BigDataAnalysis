 '''Ardez''' ({{Audio|Roh-vallader-Ardez.ogg|[ɐrˈdɛts]}}) is a former [[municipality]] of the [[district]] of [[Inn (district)|Inn]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Graubünden]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 On 1 January 2015, the former municipalities of Ardez, [[Ftan]], [[Guarda, Switzerland|Guarda]], [[Sent]] and [[Tarasp]] merged into the municipality of [[Scuol]].
