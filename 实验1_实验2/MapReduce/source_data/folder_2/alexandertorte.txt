'''Alexandertorte''', also known as '''Alexander Torte''' or '''Aleksander Torte''', is a [[dessert]] of [[pastry]] strips filled with [[raspberry]] [[preserves]]. 
 
 Its origin is not really known.  Although [[Alexander I of Russia|Alexander I]] employed [[Antonin Carême|Carême]], this dessert is not claimed by Carême. It was possibly first made in [[Finland]], by Swiss pastry chefs in [[Helsinki]] in 1818, when Alexander was due to visit.  It is also said to be a [[Latvia]]n dish.
