An '''exbibyte''' (EiB) is a large unit of measurement for computers of the future. Exbibytes hold 1024 [[pebibyte]]s (PiB) or 1,152,921,504,606,846,976 [[byte]]s. The exbibyte is closely related to, but not the same as, the [[exabyte]]. 
 
 1024 exbibytes make 1 [[zebibyte]].
