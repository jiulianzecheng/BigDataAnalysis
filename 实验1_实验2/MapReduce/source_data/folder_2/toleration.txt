'''Tolerance''' is not taking action against people who do things one does not like. The opposite of tolerance is [[intolerance]]. Intolerance is often found in [[dictatorship]]s historically and often causes the [[hate crime]] and [[hate speech]]. Tolerance is often about [[religion]] ([[religious toleration]]), [[sex]], or [[politics|politic]] [[opinion]], [[nation]], [[race (sociology)|race]], [[disability]], [[sexual orientation]] or [[gender identity]]. 
 
 Also [[multiculturalism]] is promoted through toleration.
