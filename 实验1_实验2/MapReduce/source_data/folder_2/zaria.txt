 '''Zaria''' is a major city in [[Kaduna State]] in northern [[Nigeria]], as well as being a [[Local Government Areas of Nigeria|Local Government Area]].  
 
 It was one of the original seven [[Hausa people|Hausa]] [[Hausa Kingdoms|city-states]]. Today, it is known for housing Nigeria's largest university,  [[Ahmadu Bello University]]. 
 
 In December 2015, Nigeria's military was reported to have killed 300 [[Shia Islam|Shia]] Muslims and buried their bodies in a mass grave. Although the government denies the event, it has been described as a [[2015 Zaria massacre|Massacre]]. < ref > {{Cite news|url=https://www.bbc.com/news/world-africa-35168211|title=Mass graves for '300 Shia Nigerians' in Zaria|date=2015-12-23|newspaper=BBC News|access-date=2016-11-05}} < /ref >
