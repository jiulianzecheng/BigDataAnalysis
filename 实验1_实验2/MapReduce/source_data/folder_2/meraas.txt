 == History == 
 Meraas started in 2007 and has worked on many projects in different areas like real estate, shopping, hotels, food, fun, and healthcare. Sheikh Ahmed bin Saeed Al Maktoum is the leader of the group. < ref > {{Cite web|title=BUSINESS|url=https://www.zawya.com/en/business|access-date=2023-04-18|website=www.zawya.com|language=en}} < /ref > 
 
 The company has made and managed many buildings in Dubai. Some of these are Port de La Mer, Central Park at City Walk, Cherrywoods, Bluewaters Residences, Bvlgari Residences, Nikki Beach Residences, and Villa Amalfi. < ref > {{Cite web|last=Saundalkar|first=Jason|date=2018-09-09|title=Meraas launches Port de La Mer residential community|url=https://meconstructionnews.com/31217/meraas-launches-port-de-la-mer-residential-community|access-date=2023-04-18|website=Middle East Construction News|language=en-US}} < /ref >
