 '''Ammerschwihr''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is found in the [[Haut-Rhin]] [[Departments of France|department]] in the eastern [[France]]. In 2014, 1,784 people lived in the commune. The [[inhabitant]]s are the ''Ammerschwihriens''.
