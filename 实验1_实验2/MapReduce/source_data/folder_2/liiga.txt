'''Liiga''' also known as '''SM-liiga''' from 1975 to 2013, is the top [[Professional sports|professional]] [[ice hockey]] league in [[Finland]]. It is generally thought of as one of the top leagues in Europe. It started in [[1975]]. SM is a common [[abbreviation]] for ''Suomen mestaruus'',  " Finnish championship " .
