 '''Deckenpfronn''' is a [[municipality]] of the district [[Böblingen (district)|Böblingen]] in the [[States of Germany|state]] of [[Baden-Württemberg]] in [[Germany]]. As of 31 December 2018, 3,329 people lived there.
