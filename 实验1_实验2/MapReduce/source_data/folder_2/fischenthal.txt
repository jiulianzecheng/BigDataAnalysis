 '''Fischenthal''' is a [[municipality]] in the [[district]] of [[Hinwil (district)|Hinwil]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Zurich (canton)|Zurich]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 Besides the village of Fischenthal, it includes the villages of '''Gibswil''' and '''Steg''', as well as nearly 100 smaller settlements.
