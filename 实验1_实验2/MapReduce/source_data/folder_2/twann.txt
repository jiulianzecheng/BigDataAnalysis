 '''Twann''' ({{lang-fr|Douanne}}) was a [[municipality]] of the [[district]] [[Nidau (district)|Nidau]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Bern (canton)|Bern]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 On 1 January 2010, the former municipalities of Twann and [[Tüscherz-Alfermée]] merged into the new municipality of [[Twann-Tüscherz]].
