'''Thrissur''' is one of the fourteen [[districts of Kerala|districts]] of [[Kerala]] in southwest [[India]]. It is the second largest city in Kerala and 20th largest city in India. At the center of the city is a temple, the Vadakkumnathan temple which has Lord Siva as its presiding deity. It is known as the [[culture|cultural]] [[capital (city)|capital]] of Kerala because of the city's association with history, culture and [[Archaeology|archaeological]] remains. Thrissur was once the capital of the Kingdom of [[Cochin]]. It is located 300 kilometers (186 mi) towards north-west of the state capital [[Thiruvananthapuram]]. It is famous for [[Thrissur Pooram]] which is a temple festival. Thrissur is also known as the “cultural capital of Kerala” as its well known for its cultural, spiritual and religious learnings throughout history.
