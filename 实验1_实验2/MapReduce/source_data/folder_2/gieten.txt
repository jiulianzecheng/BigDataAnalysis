 '''Gieten''' is a village in the [[municipality]] of [[Aa en Hunze]], province of [[Drenthe]], the [[Netherlands]]. 
 
 As of 2020, about 5,000 people lived in the village. 
 
 Gieten also was a municipality until 1998.
