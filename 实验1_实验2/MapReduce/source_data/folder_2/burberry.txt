 '''Burberry''' is a [[Great Britain|British]] [[Fashion design|fashion house]]. It distributes [[ready to wear]]. Also Burberry designs [[Fashion accessory|fashion accessories]], [[leather]] goods, [[cosmetics]], [[Trench coat|trench coats]], and also [[eyeglasses]]. 
 
 The first shop opened in [[Haymarket]] in [[London]] in 1891.
