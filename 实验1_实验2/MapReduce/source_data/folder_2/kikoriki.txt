 '''''Kikoriki''''', known in the United States as '''''Gogoriki''''' and in Russia as '''''Smeshariki''''' ({{lang-ru|Смеша́рики}}) is a Russian [[animation|animated]] [[television series]] made 
 [[File:Kikoriki_logo.png]] up of 209 episodes of 6 minutes and 30 seconds each, aimed at children of 3 to 8 years. < ref > {{cite book |last1=Crump |first1=William D. |title=Happy Holidays—Animated! A Worldwide Encyclopedia of Christmas, Hanukkah, Kwanzaa and New Year's Cartoons on Television and Film |date=2019 |publisher=McFarland  &  Co |isbn=9781476672939 |page=212}} < /ref >   
 
 The first episode premiered in Russia on May 17, 2004.  
 
 The series is aired in 60 countries, is translated to 15 languages. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://rusrep.ru/article/2014/04/15/smeshariki-po-pekinski|script-title=ru:Смешарики по-пекински. Как Крош, Нюша и Кар-Карыч завоёвывают Поднебесную|last=Мостовщиков|first=Егор.|date=2014-04-15|publisher=[[Эксперт (журнал)|Русский репортёр]]|language=ru|access-date=April 29, 2019|archive-date=2019-04-16|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190416002901/http://rusrep.ru/article/2014/04/15/smeshariki-po-pekinski|url-status=dead}} < /ref >
