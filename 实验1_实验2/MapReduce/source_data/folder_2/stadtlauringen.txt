 '''Stadtlauringen''' is a [[Municipalities of Germany|municipality]] in [[Schweinfurt (district)|Schweinfurt]] in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. 
 
 == Geography == 
 === Place === 
 Stadtlauringen is in the Main Rhön region, about 20 kilometers north east of Schweinfurt. It is the northernmost municipality in Schweinfurt. 
 The [[Lauer (river)|Lauer]] flows through it. The [[Ellertshäuser See (lake)|Ellertshäuser See]] is close to the village [[Altenmünster (Stadtlauringen)|Altenmünster]]. 
 
 === Towns and Villages === 
 The [[Municipalities of Germany|municipality]] Stadtlauringen has the following towns and villages: 
 
 *Altenmünster with Ellertshausen and Reinhardshausen 
 *Ballingshausen 
 *Birnfeld 
 *Fuchsstadt 
 *Mailes 
 *Oberlauringen 
 *Stadtlauringen 
 *Sulzdorf 
 *Wettringen 
 *Wetzhausen
