 '''Sochi''' ([[Russian language|Russuan]]:Со́чи) is a [[city]] in [[Russia]] on the [[Black Sea]] coast. It is in the [[Federal subjects of Russia|Russian federal subject]] of [[Krasnodar Krai]]. The [[2014 Winter Olympics]] and the [[2014 Winter Paralympics]] were played there. The Sochi Olympic Stadium was also used to host [[2018 FIFA World Cup]] football matches. Sochi is also a popular health resort. It is one of very few places in Russia with a [[humid subtropical climate]] (''Cfa'' in the [[Köppen climate classification]]).
