 '''Surpierre''' is a [[municipality]] of the district [[Broye (district)|Broye]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Fribourg (canton)|Fribourg]] in [[Switzerland]]. On 1 January 2005, Praratoud became a part of Surpierre and on 1 January 2017 [[Villeneuve, Fribourg|Villeneuve]] became a part of Surpierre. < ref > [https://www.bfs.admin.ch/bfs/de/home/grundlagen/agvch.html Amtliches Gemeindeverzeichnis der Schweiz ] {{in lang|de}} accessed 29 November 2016 < /ref >
