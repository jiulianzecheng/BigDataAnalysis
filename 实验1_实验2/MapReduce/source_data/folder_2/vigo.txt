 '''Vigo''' is an industrial city and a [[Municipalities of Spain|municipality]] in the [[province of Pontevedra]], within the [[Autonomous communities of Spain|autonomous community]] of [[Galicia (Spain)|Galicia]], [[Spain]]. It is on the [[Atlantic Ocean]]. 
 
 It is the second largest city by population in Galicia, after [[A Coruña]]. < ref > {{Cite web|url=https://www.elespanol.com/quincemil/articulos/actualidad/a-coruna-es-la-localidad-con-mas-habitantes-de-galicia-por-encima-de-vigo|website=[[El Español]]|title=A Coruña es la localidad con más habitantes de Galicia, por encima de Vigo|lang=es|date=9 June 2020}} < /ref > 
 
 The city has an area of {{convert|109.06|km²}} and 295,364 people lived here in 2019, is the most populous municipality in Galicia.
