 '''Chania''' is a [[Greece|Greek]] city which is the capital of Chania prefecture. It is in the west of [[Crete]]. 55,838 people live there. Chania has an important port which connects Chania with the port of [[Piraeus]].  
 
 == History == 
 Chania had an significant development from 13th century till 17th century. Then it was under [[Venice|Venetian]] rule. After the Venetians, ruled the [[Ottoman]]s until 1912. This year [[Crete]] was liberated and it is united with [[Greece]].
