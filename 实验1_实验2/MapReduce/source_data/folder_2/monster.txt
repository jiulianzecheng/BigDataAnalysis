 A '''monster''' is a type of creature in [[fiction]]. They are usually found in [[legend]]s or [[horror]] stories. They are often ugly and may make [[people]] and other creatures [[Fear|scared]]. They also eat people sometimes. A monster could be anything really, like a [[vampire]] or a [[witch]] or even a [[werewolf]]. [[Figurative language|Figuratively]], a ''monster'' is a very [[evil]] person. Sometimes these creatures aren't bad or monsters as you would say because some protect other [[people]] and/or are inoffensive.
