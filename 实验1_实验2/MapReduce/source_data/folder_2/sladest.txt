 '''''Sladest''''' is a [[1973]] [[Rock music|rock]] [[album]] by [[England|English]] band [[Slade]]. It was the band's first UK compilation, mixing all the band's hit singles with some songs from before the band's fame. A successful album, it went to number one in the UK.
