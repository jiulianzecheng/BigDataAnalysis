'''Tinglish''' is an [[English language|English]] dialect used by [[Thai (ethnic group)|Thai]] people. English is more complicated than the [[Thai language]] and [[English grammar]] is easily understood. < ref name=IntoAsia > {{cite web |url=http://www.into-asia.com/thai_language/thaienglish.php |title='Tinglish', the Thai version of English - Thai Language |author= < !--Staff writer(s); no by-line.-- >  |date=2000 |website= |publisher= Into Asia |accessdate=5 July 2014 }} < /ref >  Tinglish is a simple form of English that is usually hard to understand by English speakers. However, many words in Tinglish have a uniquely Thai [[pronunciation]]. < ref name=IntoAsia/ >  Time can be a confusing subject in Tinglish. Thais use a 24-hour clock instead of a 12-hour clock. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://www.thaizer.com/language/telling-the-time-thai-style/ |title=Telling the Time Thai Style |author=Roy Cavanagh |date=4 March 2009 |website= |publisher=Thaizer |accessdate=5 July 2014 }} < /ref >
