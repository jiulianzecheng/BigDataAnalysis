 '''Czeklin''' is a former [[village]] in the [[administration|administrative]] district of [[Gmina Bobrowice]]. It was formerly a [[Germany|German]] village named ''Schegeln''.  The village is within [[Krosno Odrzańskie County]], [[Lubusz Voivodeship]], in western Poland. < ref name= " TERYT " > {{cite web |url=http://www.stat.gov.pl/broker/access/prefile/listPreFiles.jspa |title=Central Statistical Office (GUS) - TERYT (National Register of Territorial Land Apportionment Journal) |date=2008-06-01 |language=Polish}} < /ref >  It lies approximately {{convert|10|km|mi|0}} west of [[Bobrowice, Krosno Odrzańskie County|Bobrowice]], {{convert|14|km|mi|0|abbr=on}} south-west of [[Krosno Odrzańskie]], and {{convert|39|km|mi|0|abbr=on}} west of [[Zielona Góra]]. 
 
 Before 1945 the area was part of Germany (see ''[[Territorial changes of Poland after World War II]]'').
