'''Mormaison''' is a former [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is found in the region [[Pays de la Loire]] in the [[Vendée]] department in the west of [[France]]. On 1 January 2016, it became a part of the new commune of [[Montréverd]]. < ref > [https://www.legifrance.gouv.fr/eli/arrete/2015/12/15/INTB1600655A/jo/texte Arrêté préfectoral] 15 December 2015 < /ref >
