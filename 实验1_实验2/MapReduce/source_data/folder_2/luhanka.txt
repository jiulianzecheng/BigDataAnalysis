 '''Luhanka''' ({{lang-sv|Luhango}}) is a municipality in [[Central Finland]], [[Finland]]. About 765 people lived there in February 2014. The municipalities located next to Luhanka are [[Hartola]], [[Joutsa]], [[Jyväskylä]], [[Jämsä]], [[Kuhmoinen]] and [[Sysmä]]. Originally Luhanka was part of Sysmä. Later it was made into its own municipality in 1864. Luhanka is a popular place for summer cottages.  
 
 ==Famouse people== 
 * Composer [[Toni Edelmann]] lives in Luhanka. 
 * Writer [[Kreetta Onkeli]] spent her childhood at Luhanka.
