 '''Berkelland''' is a municipality in the province of [[Gelderland]], the Netherlands. About 44,000 people were living there in 2021. 
 
 Berkelland lies in [[Achterhoek]] region and borders both [[Twente]] region and [[Germany]]. 
 
 It was created from the former municipalities of [[Borculo]], [[Eibergen]], [[Neede]], and [[Ruurlo]] on 1 January 2005. It is named after the [[Berkel]] river.
