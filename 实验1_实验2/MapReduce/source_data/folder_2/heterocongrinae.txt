 '''Garden eels''' are the subfamily '''Heterocongrinae''' in the family [[Congridae]]. They are a type of [[eel]] that live in warm ocean water, most commonly in the [[Indo-Pacific]]. These eels are small and live in [[Burrow|burrows]], peeking their heads out while keeping the rest of their bodies hidden inside the burrow.
