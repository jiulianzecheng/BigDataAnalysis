'''Hellingly''' is a small village in [[East Sussex]], England. Hellingly School has about 209 students. 
 
 There is a very small park in Hellingly.  
 
 Hellingly  has a church called St Peter and St Paul. Children from  Hellingly use the church for harvest festivals and Christmas carol services.
