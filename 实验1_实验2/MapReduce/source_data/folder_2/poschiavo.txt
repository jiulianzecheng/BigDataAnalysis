 '''Poschiavo''' ({{lang-it|Poschiavo}}, {{lang-lmo|Pusciaaf}}, {{lang-de|Puschlav}}, {{lang-rm|Puschlav}}) is a [[municipality]] in [[Bernina (district)|Bernina]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Graubünden]] in [[Switzerland]]. As of December 2015, 3,513 people lived there. 
 
 Poschiavo has its own lake, called [[Lago di Poschiavo]], which is located to the south of the village.
