 '''Houtkerque''' ({{IPA-fr|utkɛʁk}}; from {{lang-nl|Houtkerke}}) is a [[Communes in France|commune]] in [[Nord (department)|Nord]] in north [[France]]. In 2019, 984 people lived there.
