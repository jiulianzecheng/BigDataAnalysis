 '''Valangin''' is a [[municipality]] of the district of [[Val-de-Ruz (district)|Val-de-Ruz]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Neuchâtel (canton)|Neuchâtel]] in [[Switzerland]]. The [[Valanginian]] Age of the [[Cretaceous]] Period of geological time is named for Valangin.
