 '''Tanumshede''' is an [[urban area]] in the [[counties of Sweden|county]] of [[Västra Götaland County|Västra Götaland]] in [[Sweden]]. It is the seat of [[Tanum Municipality]]. 
 
 The '''Rock Carvings in Tanum''' are a collection of [[petroglyph]]s near Tanumshede. They were declared a [[World Heritage Site]] by [[United Nations Educational, Scientific and Cultural Organization|UNESCO]] in 1994. < ref > {{cite web|title=Rock Carvings in Tanum|url=http://whc.unesco.org/en/list/557|publisher=UNESCO|accessdate=7 May 2017}} < /ref >  In total there are thousands of images on about 600 panels.
