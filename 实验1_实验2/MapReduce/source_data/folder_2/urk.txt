 '''Urk''' is a [[List of municipalities of the Netherlands|municipality]] in the province of [[Flevoland]], the [[Netherlands]]. It has about 20,000 people (2016), and is both a former municipality of [[North Holland]] and of [[Overijssel]]. 
 
 Urk is a former island and well-known for its [[fishery]] activities.
