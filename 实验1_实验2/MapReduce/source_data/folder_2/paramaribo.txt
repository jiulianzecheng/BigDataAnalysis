 '''Paramaribo''' ({{IPA-nl|ˌpaːraˈmaːriboː}}, nickname: '''Par′bo''') is the [[capital (city)|capital city]] of [[Suriname]]. About 250,000 people live in the city. It is at the [[Suriname River]] about 15 [[kilometre|km]] before the river flows into the [[Atlantic Ocean]].  
 
 Paramirbo used to be called in French  " Bourg nommé des Juifs "  because so many Jewish people lived there. < ref name= " WDL " > {{cite web |url = http://www.wdl.org/en/item/524/ |title = Extract of the Dutch Map Representing the Colony of Surinam |website = [[World Digital Library]] |date = 1777 |accessdate = 2013-07-13 }} < /ref >
