 '''Nordaustlandet''' (translates to Northeast land) is the second biggest island of [[Svalbard]]. It lies to the northeast of [[Spitsbergen]], which is the biggest island of the group, and the only islands with permanent settlements. Northeast land is about {{Convert|170|km||abbr=}} by {{Convert|125|km||abbr=}}. It is uninhabited, and there are no settlements on it. During World War II, Germans operated a weather station.
