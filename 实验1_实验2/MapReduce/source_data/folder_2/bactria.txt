 '''Bactria''' is a [[region]] in [[Asia]]. It is centered around the city of [[Balkh]], in Northern [[Afghanistan]]. It is north of the [[Hindu Kush]], and south of the river [[Amu Darya]]. [[Zoroaster]] might have been from this region.
