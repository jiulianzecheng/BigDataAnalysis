'''A flyover''' is a [[Ceremony|ceremonial]] flight by a plane or group of planes. flyovers are often because of important events or celebrations, although more rarely they are because of memorials, like the [[September 11 attacks|9/11]] memorial planes. The planes commonly fly in the colors of the country they are in. They are most often seen in the United Kingdom and other [[Commonwealth of England|commonwealth]] countries.
