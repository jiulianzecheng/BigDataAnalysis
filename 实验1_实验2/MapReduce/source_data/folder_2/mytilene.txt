 '''Mitilene''' is a capital of [[lesbos]] island. Lesbos is the third biggest island in [[Greece]]. It is a beautiful city with important history. It is located in the east of Lesbos island. Mytilene has 36.196 inhabitants. In the past, Mytilene was a rich town and this seems from the rich houses in the city. Also, it has got a big port which connect Mytilene with [[Piraeus]] and other ports.
