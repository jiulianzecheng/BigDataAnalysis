 '''Lepperton''' is a village in the [[Taranaki|Taranaki Region]]. It is 6 kilometres east of [[Waitara, New Zealand|Waitara]] township and is 1 kilometre of State Highway 3A, which connects [[Inglewood, New Zealand|Inglewood]] and [[Waitara, New Zealand|Waitara]].
