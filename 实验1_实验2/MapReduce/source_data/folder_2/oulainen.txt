 '''Oulainen''' is a town in [[Northern Ostrobothnia]], [[Finland]]. As of 28 February 2023, 7,018 people lived there. Neighbouring municipalities are [[Alavieska]], [[Haapavesi]], [[Merijärvi]], [[Pyhäjoki]], [[Raahe]] and [[Ylivieska]]. 
 
 Oulainen was founded in 1865.
