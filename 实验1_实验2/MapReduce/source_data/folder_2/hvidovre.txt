 '''Hvidovre''' is the main town in [[Hvidovre Municipality]], [[Denmark]]. The town is a [[suburb]] of [[Copenhagen]]. It is about 10 & nbsp;km [[southwest]] of the middle of the city. It is the second biggest suburb of Copenhagen. The biggest is [[Frederiksberg]].
