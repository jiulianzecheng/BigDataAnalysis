 '''Dharwad''', also known as '''Dharwar''', is a city located in the north western part of the Indian state of Karnataka. It is the headquarters of the [[Dharwad district]] of [[Karnataka]] and forms a contiguous urban area with the city of Hubballi. It was merged with [[Hubballi]] in 1962 to form the twin cities of [[Hubballiâ€“Dharwad|Hubballi-Dharwad]]. It covers an area of {{cvt|213|km2}} and is located {{cvt|430|km}} northwest of [[Bangalore]], on [[National Highway 48 (India)|NH-48]], between Bangalore and [[Pune]].
