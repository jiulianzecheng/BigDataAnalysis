 '''Teuva''' ({{lang-sv|Östermark}}) is a [[municipality]] in [[Southern Ostrobothnia]], [[Finland]]. There were about 5,700 people living there on 31 January 2014. It is next to the municipalities of [[Karijoki]], [[Kauhajoki]], [[Kristinestad]], [[Kurikka]] and [[Närpes]]. About 99% of the people living there speak [[Finnish language|Finnish]]. Teuva was established in 1868.
