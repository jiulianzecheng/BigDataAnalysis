 '''''Animorphs''''' is a [[Young-adult fiction|young-adult]] [[science fiction]] book series. They were written by [[K. A. Applegate]] and published by [[Scholastic Corporation|Scholastic]]. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://booklikes.com/the-beginning-animorphs-54-katherine-applegate/book,810154 |title=The Beginning (Animorphs, #54) |author= < !--Staff writer(s); no by-line.-- >  |date=2014 |publisher=BookLikes |access-date=15 October 2014}} < /ref >  It is told in the [[Grammatical person|first person]], from [[Perspective (cognitive)|perspectives]] of 6 different [[Fictional characters|characters]]; Rachel, Cassie, Jake, Tobias, Marco, and Aximilli-Esgarouth-Isthil. A different character is featured for each [[book]]. It was published from June 1996 to May 2001. There are 54 books in the [[series]].
