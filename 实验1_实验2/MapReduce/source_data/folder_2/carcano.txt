 The '''Carcano''' is an Italian [[bolt-action rifle]] introduced in 1891. 
 
 The Carcano was infamously used by [[Lee Harvey Oswald]] to [[Assassination of John F. Kennedy|assassinate US president John F. Kennedy]] in 1963.
