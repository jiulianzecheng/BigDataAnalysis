'''GFMS''' (formally '''Gold Fields Mineral Services''') < ref > {{Cite news|last=Jha|first=Dilip Kumar|date=2008-04-10|title=Jewellery demand likely to slide by 8%|work=Business Standard India|url=https://www.business-standard.com/article/markets/jewellery-demand-likely-to-slide-by-8-108041001033_1.html|access-date=2021-08-04}} < /ref >  is an [[England|English]] company that does research and consults for [[metal]] [[Market|markets]]. Since 2011 they have been part of Thomson Reuters. As well as other [[Commodity market|commodities]], they research [[gold]], [[Silver|silver,]] [[platinum]], [[palladium]], and [[copper]] trading.

After the take over, a company was formed the same year to continue the survey. < ref name= " hist " > {{cite web|title=Company History|url=http://www.gfms.co.uk/?page_id=161|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20131016184838/http://www.gfms.co.uk/?page_id=161|archive-date=16 October 2013|access-date=6 September 2012|publisher=GFMS}} < /ref > 
 
 Between 1994 and 1998, the company was owned by [[:en:Gold_Fields|Gold Fields of South Africa]]. < ref name= " hist2 " > {{cite web|title=Company History|url=http://www.gfms.co.uk/?page_id=161|publisher=GFMS|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131016184838/http://www.gfms.co.uk/?page_id=161|archivedate=16 October 2013|accessdate=6 September 2012}} < /ref > 
 
 Between 1998 and 2003, the company was known as Gold Fields Mineral Services Limited. < ref > {{Cite web|title=GFMS LIMITED - Overview (free company information from Companies House)|url=https://find-and-update.company-information.service.gov.uk/company/03593787|access-date=2021-08-04|website=find-and-update.company-information.service.gov.uk|language=en}} < /ref > 
 
 In 1998, members of the research team completed a management buy out of GFMS from GFSA, and the consultancy gained full independence. In 2002, GFMS became an associate member of the [[:en:London_Bullion_Market_Association|London Bullion Market Association]].    
 
 In August 2011, GFMS was purchased by [[:en:Thomson_Reuters|Thomson Reuters]]. < ref name= " hist22 " > {{cite web|title=Company History|url=http://www.gfms.co.uk/?page_id=161|publisher=GFMS|archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20131016184838/http://www.gfms.co.uk/?page_id=161|archivedate=16 October 2013|accessdate=6 September 2012}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite web|title=Eikon Financial Analysis  &  Trading Software|url=https://www.refinitiv.com/en/products/eikon-trading-software|access-date=2021-08-04|website=www.refinitiv.com|language=en}} < /ref >  The execute chairperson [[:en:Philip_Klapwijk|Philip Klapwijk]] left the company.
