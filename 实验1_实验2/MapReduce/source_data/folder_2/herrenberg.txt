 '''Herrenberg''' is a [[town]] in the middle of [[Baden-Württemberg]]. It is about {{convert|30|km|abbr=off}} south of [[Stuttgart]] and {{convert|20|km|abbr=off}} from [[Tübingen]]. After [[Sindelfingen]], [[Böblingen]], and [[Leonberg]], it is the fourth largest town in the district of [[Böblingen (district)|Böblingen]].
