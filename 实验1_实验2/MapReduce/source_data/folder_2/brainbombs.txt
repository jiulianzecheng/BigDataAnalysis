 '''Brainbombs''' is a noise rock band from [[Sweden]]. The members are Dan, Peter, Jonas, Drajan, Mattis and Lanchy. 
 
 Brainbombs is known for their repetitive and loud songs and their controversial lyrics with themes about murder, rape and torture. The band have said they are inspired from author Peter Sotos, < ref > {{Cite web|last=Buckles|first=Monty|title=ALL HAIL DEATH, RAPE, AND MURDER.|url=https://geniusandbrutality.com/brainbombs/interv/z-gun2.html|access-date=2023-12-02|website=geniusandbrutality.com}} < /ref >  American band Chrome, James Chance and English noise band Whitehouse. < ref name= " :0 " > {{Cite web|title=Alzheimer #2|url=https://geniusandbrutality.com/brainbombs/interv/alzheimer2eng.html|access-date=2023-12-02|website=geniusandbrutality.com}} < /ref > 
 
 == History == 
 
 === Before Brainbombs and formation (1985) === 
 Before Brainbombs, singer Peter Råberg was in a band called  " Dödshjälp fd. Hedebyborna " . < ref > {{Cite web|title=Dödshjälp fd. Hedebyborna|url=https://www.discogs.com/artist/7027109-D%C3%B6dshj%C3%A4lp-fd-Hedebyborna|access-date=2023-12-03|website=Discogs|language=en}} < /ref >  Although the band only lasted 1 year. (1981-1982) < ref > {{Cite web|last=Punk|first=Farbror|title=Dödshjälp f.d Hedebyborna – Punktjafs|url=https://www.punktjafs.com/?p=5602|access-date=2023-12-03|language=sv-SE}} < /ref >  Initially, the lineup for Brainbombs was Peter Råberg on vocals, Jonas Tiljander on guitar, Andreas Lönnquist on bass and Anders (Drajan) Bryngelsson on drums. But Andreas was kicked after their first rehearsal. < ref name= " :0 "  / >  Soon after, they got a second guitarist called Lanchy Orre from the hardcore band Totalitär < ref > {{Cite web|title=Totalitär|url=https://www.discogs.com/artist/307040-Totalit%C3%A4r|access-date=2023-12-03|website=Discogs|language=en}} < /ref >  and Peter's elder brother < ref name= " :0 "  / > , Dan who wrote lyrics and played trumpet.
