 '''Autun''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]] and [[Subprefectures in France|subprefecture]] in the [[Saône-et-Loire]] [[Departments of France|department]] in the [[Bourgogne-Franche-Comté]] [[Regions of France|region]] of central-eastern [[France]]. 
 
 == Transport == 
 
 === Road === 
 The D681, D978, D980, D107, D973, D120, D256, D46 and D287 roads pass through the Autun commune. 
 
 === Rail === 
 Autun has a main station, Autun Railway Station, or Gare d'Autun in French. 
 
 == Twinned cities == 
 Autun is twinned with [[Ingelheim am Rhein]] in [[Germany]], [[Kawagoe, Saitama|Kawagoe]] in [[Japan]], [[Arévalo]] in [[Spain]] and [[Stevenage]] in the [[United Kingdom]].
