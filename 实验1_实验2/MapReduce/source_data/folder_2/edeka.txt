 The '''Edeka Group''' is the largest [[Germany|German]] [[supermarket]] [[corporation]]. There are about 4,100 stores with the Edeka nameplate that range from small [[convenience store]]s to [[hypermarket]]s. It is headquartered in [[Hamburg]], Germany.
