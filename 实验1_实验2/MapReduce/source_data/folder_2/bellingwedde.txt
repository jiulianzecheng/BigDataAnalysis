 '''Bellingwedde''' is a former municipality in the province of [[Groningen (province)|Groningen]], the [[Netherlands]]. 
 
 == History == 
 Bellingwedde was created on 1 September 1968 from the former municipalities of Bellingwolde and Wedde. Since 2018, it has been part of the new municipality of [[Westerwolde (municipality)|Westerwolde]].
