 '''Autopix''' is a Norwegian company that provides services and apps for image editing. < ref > {{Cite web|date=2015-09-11|title=Building Your Freedom Business with Erlend Bakke|url=https://uydmedia.com/building-your-freedom-business-with-erlend-bakke/|access-date=2022-07-22|website=UYD Media|language=en-US}} < /ref >  Founder and CEO of the company is Erlend Bakke. < ref > {{Cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=56AkDwAAQBAJ & dq=3sixtyfactory & pg=PP9|title=Guide to Erlend Bakke's Never Work Again by Instaread|last=Instaread|date=2017-05-22|publisher=Instaread|isbn=978-1-68378-777-8|language=en}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=_cJFvgEACAAJ & q=image+processing+software|title=Digital Image Processing and Image Formation|last=Jähne|first=Bernd|date=2022-07-12|publisher=Springer Berlin Heidelberg|isbn=978-3-642-04949-1|language=en}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=UUBVnQAACAAJ & q=image+processing+software|title=Development of Image Processing Software Using Visual Basic 6.0|last=Rahim|first=Nurul Hakeem Abd|date=2008|publisher=UMP|language=en}} < /ref > 
 
 == Overview == 
 Autopix has its origins in the company 3sixtyfactory. 3sixtyfactory was founded on February 8, 2008, by Erlend Bakke in Oslo, Norway. < ref > {{Cite web|title=3sixty – key info, careers and team {{!}} Startupmatcher|url=https://startupmatcher.com/s/3sixty|access-date=2022-07-23|website=startupmatcher.com|archive-date=2022-07-23|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20220723081741/https://startupmatcher.com/s/3sixty|url-status=dead}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=56AkDwAAQBAJ & dq=3sixtyfactory & pg=PP9|title=Guide to Erlend Bakke's Never Work Again by Instaread|last=Instaread|date=2017-05-22|publisher=Instaread|isbn=978-1-68378-777-8|language=en}} < /ref >  In the same year, Rimmon Pancito joined the company, who later became Head of Production. The company provided a range of photo services, including 360 photo editing and 3D animation. < ref > {{Cite web|date=2010-07-19|title=Image editing outsourcing; quality vs quantity|url=http://www.merinews.com/article/image-editing-outsourcing-quality-vs-quantity/15784945.shtml|access-date=2022-07-23|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20100719192056/http://www.merinews.com/article/image-editing-outsourcing-quality-vs-quantity/15784945.shtml |archive-date=2010-07-19 }} < /ref > 
 
 In February 2020, the company focused on editing car photos and launched a mobile application called Autopix. < ref > {{Cite web|last=|first=|title=Autopix by 3sixtyfactory by 3sixty Ltd.|url=https://appadvice.com/app/autopix-by-3sixtyfactory/1500903600|access-date=2022-07-23|website=AppAdvice|language=en}} < /ref > 
 
 In December 2021, the service began cooperation with [[Toyota]]. The same month, Autopix cooperated and integrated with CARWEB. < ref > {{Cite web|date=2022-01-19|title=Nyhet! FotoAPI i CARWEB for direkte import av bilder fra Autopix.|url=https://carweb.no/nyhet-fotoapi-i-carweb-for-direkte-import-av-bilder-fra-autopix/|access-date=2022-07-23|website=Carweb|language=nb-NO}} < /ref > 
 
 In 2022, Autopix merged with 3sixtyfactory under one name and rebranded as Autopix.
