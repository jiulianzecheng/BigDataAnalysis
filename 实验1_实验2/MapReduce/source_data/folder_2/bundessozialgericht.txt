 The '''Bundessozialgericht''' ([[German language|German]] for ''Federal Social Court'') is the [[Germany|German]] [[Federation|federal]] supreme court of [[appeal]]s for [[social security]] cases. < ref > [http://www.bverwg.de/enid/53777daeab9d9618e9562c82d5ca2af8,0/Information_and_Decisions__EN_/The_Federal_Administrative_Court_g3.html Website of the Bundesverwaltungsgericht]{{Dead link|date=January 2021 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }} < /ref >   The court was created on 11 September 1954 < ref name = " BSG " > {{Cite web |url=http://www.bsg.bund.de/EN/Home/homepage__node.html?__nnn=true |title=English Website of the Bundessozialsgericht |access-date=2010-12-11 |archive-date=2011-01-03 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110103001352/http://www.bsg.bund.de/EN/Home/homepage__node.html?__nnn=true |url-status=dead }} < /ref >  to hear cases about public [[health insurance]], long-term care insurance, [[pension]] insurance and occupational accident insurance plans. < ref name > BSG < /ref >   These cases usually start in the ''Sozialgerichte'' ( " Social Courts " ), then the ''Landessozialgerichte''  ( " Superior State Social Courts " ), before the cases wind up at the ''Bundessozialgericht''. 
 
 The ''Bundessozialgericht'' is located in the city of [[Kassel]].
