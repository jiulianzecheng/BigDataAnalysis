 '''Bareilly''' ({{lang-hi|बरेली}}) is a city in [[Bareilly district]], [[Uttar Pradesh]], [[India]]. It is the capital of [[Bareilly division]]. < ref > https://web.archive.org/web/20170525141614/http://nclm.nic.in/shared/linkimages/NCLM52ndReport.pdf < /ref >  In 2015 Bareilly is selected one of the smartest cities of India. < ref > {{cite news | url=https://www.thehindu.com/news/national/centre-releases-list-of-98-cities-for-smart-city-project/article7586751.ece | title=Centre unveils list of 98 smart cities; UP, TN strike it rich | newspaper=The Hindu | date=27 August 2015 | last1=Jeelani | first1=Mehboob }} < /ref > 
 
 According to 2011 Census of India, the population of the city is 904,797 out of 477,515 are males and 427,282 are females. < ref > {{cite web | url=https://www.census2011.co.in/census/city/120-bareilly.html | title=Bareilly City Population Census 2011-2022  & #124; Uttar Pradesh }} < /ref >
