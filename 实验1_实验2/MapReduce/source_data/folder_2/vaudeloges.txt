'''Vaudeloges''' is a former [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is in the [[Regions of France|region]] [[Basse-Normandie]] in the [[Calvados (department)|Calvados]] [[Departments of France|department]]. This is in the northwest of [[France]]. On 1 January 2017, it was merged into the new commune of [[Saint-Pierre-en-Auge]]. < ref > [https://www.legifrance.gouv.fr/eli/arrete/2016/12/19/INTB1629549A/jo/texte Arrêté préfectoral] 19 December 2016 {{in lang|fr}} < /ref >
