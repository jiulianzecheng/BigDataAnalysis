 '''Fouhren''' ({{lang-lb|Furen}}, {{lang-de|Fuhren}}) is a small town in the [[Communes of Luxembourg|commune]] of [[Tandel]], in northeastern [[Luxembourg]]. 
 
 Fouhren was a commune in the [[Vianden (canton)|canton of Vianden]] until 1 January 2006, when it was merged with [[Bastendorf]] to form the new commune of Tandel.
