'''IOU''' means  " I Owe You "  or  " I Owe Unto " . It is a note between two people that is used to say that one person owes something to the other as [[debt]]. It is usually used for [[currency]]. I.O.U.'s are most often only used between people that know each other very well, for example, [[friend]]s.
