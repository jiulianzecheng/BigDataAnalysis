 '''Element''' can mean: 
 * [[Chemical element]], a substance that can not be divided into smaller chemical parts 
 * ''The elements'' or [[weather]] 
 * The four basic or natural elements in [[mysticism]], [[alchemy]], [[mythology]] and [[fantasy|fantasy fiction]] - [[Fire]], [[Water]], [[Air]] and [[ground|Earth]] 
 * A thing that a [[set]] holds 
 * A book by [[Euclid]] called the ''[[Euclid's Elements|Elements]]''. In it, Euclid talks about [[geometry]].
