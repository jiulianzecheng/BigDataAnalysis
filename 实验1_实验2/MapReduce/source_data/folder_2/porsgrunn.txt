 '''Porsgrunn''' is a city and a municipality in [[Vestfold]] and [[Telemark]] County in South-Eastern [[Norway]]. The city is divided into two parts by the river [[Skienselva]]. There is also a big industrial island called [[Herøya]] < ref name= " Nilsen " > {{Cite journal|last1=Nilsen|first1=Jan Erik|last2=Skårdal|first2=Halvard|last3=Lundbo|first3=Sten|date=2020-02-05|title=Porsgrunn|url=http://snl.no/Porsgrunn|journal=Store Norske Leksikon|language=nb}} < /ref >  which is the back bone for the city. 
 
 == History == 
 The tradition of industry goes back far, and the most known factory from Porgrunn is  " Porsgrund porselænsfabrik "  established in 1885. < ref name= " Nilsen " / >  These days the factory has been turned into a small shopping center with a gym and thousands of squarefeet of office spaces.
