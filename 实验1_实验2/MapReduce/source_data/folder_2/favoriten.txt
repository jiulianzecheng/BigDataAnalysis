 '''Favoriten''' is the 10th [[Districts of Vienna|district]] of [[Vienna]], [[Austria]]. 
 
 Favoriten is bordered by the districts [[Meidling]] to the west and [[Simmering, Vienna|Simmering]] to the east. [[Liesing]] is to the southwest. [[Margareten]] and [[Wieden]] are to the north.
