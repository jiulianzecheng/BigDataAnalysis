'''Lothal,''' is one of the places where the [[Indus Valley civilization]] was seen. Archeological evidence from Lothal tells us that it was an [[Industry|industrial]] area. Metals, semi-precious stones, statues and beads were found. Several weapons, ornaments, seals and workshops were also found. A dockyard was also found which may have been used to ship goods to other civilizations like [[Mesopotamia]].
