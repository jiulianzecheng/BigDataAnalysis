 The '''Gartempe''' is a [[France|French]] [[river]] that flows through the [[Nouvelle-Aquitaine]] and [[Centre-Val de Loire]] regions. 
 
 It rises in the [[Massif Central]] and flows into the [[Creuse (river)|Creuse]] river, as a left [[tributary]], in the [[Vienne]] [[Departments of France|department]]. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 The Gartempe river has a length of {{Convert|204.6|km|mi|0|abbr=on}} and a [[drainage basin]] with an area of approximately {{Convert|3913|km2|sqmi|0|abbr=on }}. < ref name= " sandre " > {{cite web |url=http://services.sandre.eaufrance.fr/Courdo/Fiche/client/fiche_courdo.php?CdSandre=L5--0180 |title=La Gartempe (L5--0180) |publisher=SANDRE - Portail national d'accès aux référentiels sur l'eau |language=fr |access-date=30 July 2016 }} < /ref > 
 
 ===Course=== 
 The Gartempe river starts in western [[Massif Central]], near the [[hamlet]] of ''Pétillat'' in the ''[[commune]]'' of [[Peyrabout]], [[Creuse]] department,  at an [[elevation]] of about {{Convert|640|m|ft|0|abbr=on}}. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://tab.geoportail.fr/?c=1.918889,46.088056 & z=0.000224579 & l=GEOGRAPHICALGRIDSYSTEMS.MAPS.3D$GEOPORTAIL:OGC:WMTS@aggregate(1) & permalink=yes |title=Source de la Gartempe |publisher=Géoportail |language=fr |access-date=30 July 2016 }} < /ref >  From here, it generally flows to the northwest. 
 
 Finally, the river flows into the [[Creuse (river)|Creuse]] river in [[La Roche-Posay]] ([[Vienne]] department, on the left side) and [[Yzeures-sur-Creuse]] ([[Indre-et-Loire]] department, on the right side). < ref > {{cite web |url=http://www.geoportail.gouv.fr/accueil?c=0.83,46.784167 & z=0.000224579 & l=GEOGRAPHICALGRIDSYSTEMS.MAPS.3D$GEOPORTAIL:OGC:WMTS@aggregate(1) & permalink=yes |title=Confluence de la Gartempe |publisher=Géoportail |language=fr |access-date=30 July 2016 }} < /ref > 
 
 The Gartempe flows through 5 departments and 46 ''communes'', in 2 regions: < ref name= " sandre "  / > 
 * [[Centre-Val de Loire]] region 
 ** [[Indre (department)|Indre]]: [[Néons-sur-Creuse]] 
 ** [[Indre-et-Loire]]: [[Yzeures-sur-Creuse]] 
 * [[Nouvelle-Aquitaine]] region 
 ** [[Creuse]]: [[Peyrabout]], [[Le Grand-Bourg]] 
 ** [[Haute-Vienne]]: [[Châteauponsac]], [[Rancon]] 
 ** [[Vienne]]: [[Montmorillon]], [[Saint-Savin, Vienne|Saint-Savin]], [[La Roche-Posay]]
