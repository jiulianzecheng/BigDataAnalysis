 '''Chios''' is the [[List of Greek islands|fifth]] largest [[Greece|Greek]] island. Its total area is 842 [[square meter|Km < sup > 2 < /sup > ]]. It is in the northeast of [[Aegean Sea]], near [[Asia Minor]] coast. The biggest town of the island is [[Chios (city)|Chios city]]. 51,936 people lived there according to the 2001 Greek census. < ref > {{Cite web |url=http://www.statistics.gr/portal/page/portal/ESYE |title=Main Page ELSTAT |access-date=2010-01-04 |archive-date=2015-02-13 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150213000000/http://www.statistics.gr/portal/page/portal/ESYE |url-status=dead }} < /ref >
