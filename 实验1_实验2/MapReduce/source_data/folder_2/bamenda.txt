 '''Bamenda''' is a city in northwestern [[Cameroon]] and is the capital of the [[Northwest Region (Cameroon)|Northwest Region]]. About 2 million people live there. It is {{convert|366|km|mi}} northwest of the Cameroonian capital, [[Yaoundé]].
