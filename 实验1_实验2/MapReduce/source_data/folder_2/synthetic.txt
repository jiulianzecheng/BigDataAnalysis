'''Synthetic''' usually means the combination of two or more parts, either by humans or natural processes. It can also be used as in: 
 
 * [[Synthetic fabric]], fabric made from synthetic fibers 
 * [[Synthetic fiber]], a [[fiber]] not made from natural materials, but from materials like oil 
 * [[Synthesizer]], a machine to create artificial sounds
