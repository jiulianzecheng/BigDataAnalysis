 '''Guria''' is a region in [[Georgia (country)|Georgia]]. The [[capital city]] is [[Ozurgeti]]. It is bordered by the eastern end of the [[Black Sea]]. 
 
 ==Divisions== 
 [[File:GR-ka.svg|left|thumb|Subdivision of Guria]] 
 Guria is divided into 3 [[municipalities]]: 
 *[[Ozurgeti Municipality]] 
 *[[Lanchkhuti Municipality]] 
 *[[Chokhatauri Municipality]] 
 
 ==People== 
 * [[Nodar Dumbadze]], writer 
 * [[Pavle Ingorokva]] (1893-1990), historian 
 * [[Eduard Shevardnadze]], Georgia's former president 
 * [[Ekvtime Takaishvili]] (1862-1952), historian 
 * [[Noe Zhordania]], Prime Minister of the [[Democratic Republic of Georgia]] from 1918 to 1921
