 A '''napkin''', or [[face]] [[towel]] (also in Canada, the United Kingdom, Australia: '''serviette''') is a [[rectangle]] of [[cloth]] used at the [[table (furniture)|table]] for wiping the [[mouth]] while eating.  It is usually small and folded.
