 '''Prestvannet''' ([[Norwegian language|Norwegian]]: {{lang|no|Báhpajávri}}) is a small [[lake]] in [[Tromsø (city)|Tromsø]], [[Norway]]. It was made into a [[reservoir]] in 1867 and used for that until 1921. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Prestvannet in Tromsø {{!}} Visit Prestvannet in Tromsø in Norway|url=https://www.visitnordic.com/en/attraction/prestvannet|access-date=2023-04-29|website=www.visitnordic.com}} < /ref >  Since then, it's been a park and [[Nature reserve|nature reserve.]] The area around the lake is home to many different kinds of birds, and it's an important natural area in Tromsø. There's a path that goes around the lake that people use for sports and nature walks. In the winter, the lake freezes over and is good for [[ice skating]]. < ref name= " :0 " > {{Cite web|date=2015-02-25|title=Prestvannet Lake – Tromsø|url=https://www.jffrank.com/home/latest/prestvannet|access-date=2023-04-29|website=jfFrank|language=en-GB}} < /ref > 
 
 Prestvannet is located in the southern part of the island [[Tromsøya]]. It is surrounded by the city of [[Tromsø (city)|Tromsø]]. It's {{convert|96|m}} above [[sea level]] and up to four meters deep. The lake has too much [[phosphorus]] in it and is considered [[Trophic state index|eutrophic]]. < ref name= " :0 "  / >
