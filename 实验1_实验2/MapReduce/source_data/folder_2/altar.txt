 An '''altar''' is a place, often a [[Table (furniture)|table]], where a religious event happens. Altars are used in [[Christianity]] and in other [[religion]]s. 
 
 In some old religions, the event was a [[:wikt:sacrifice|sacrifice]], which means a [[holy]] work. Usually the priest would use this item.
