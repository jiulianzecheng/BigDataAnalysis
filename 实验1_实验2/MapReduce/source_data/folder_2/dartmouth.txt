 '''Dartmouth''' is a [[town]] and [[Civil parishes in England|civil parish]] on the [[A379 road]], in [[South Hams]], [[Devon]], England. In 2001, there were 5,512 people living in Dartmouth. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.ons.gov.uk/help/localstatistics|title=Local statistics - Office for National Statistics|website=www.ons.gov.uk}} < /ref >  Dartmouth got its name from being at the mouth of the [[River Dart]].
