 '''Cetology''' is a type of [[science]]. It is the study of the [[cetacean]]s. It includes [[whale]]s, [[dolphin]]s, and [[porpoise]]s. 
 
 Cetology is the branch of marine [[mammal]] science which studies approximately eighty species of whales, dolphins, and porpoise in the scientific order Cetacea. The term was created in the mid-19th century from the Greek cetus ( " whale " ) and -ology ( " study " ). 
 Cetologists,  < ref > {{Cite web|title=Studying Marine Biology {{!}} Orca Research Trust|url=https://www.orcaresearch.org/index.php/studying-marine-biology|access-date=2020-08-17}} < /ref > or those who study it, seek to understand and explain cetacean evolution, distribution, morphology, behavior, community dynamics, and other topics.
