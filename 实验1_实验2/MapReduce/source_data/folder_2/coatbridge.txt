 '''Coatbridge''' is a [[town]] in [[North Lanarkshire]], [[Scotland]]. 
 
 Today the town is a [[working class]] town near to [[Glasgow]], the largest city in Scotland. The town has also been described as  " uniquely populated largely by people of Irish descent " , meaning that there are many people from [[Irish people|Irish]] families. < ref > Sport and the Contestation of Ethnic Identity: Football and Irishness in Scotland in Journal of Ethnic and Migration Studies - Joseph M. Bradley (2006) p.15
