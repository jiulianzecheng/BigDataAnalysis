 '''Mackwiller''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is in [[Grand Est]] in the [[Bas-Rhin]] [[Departments of France|department]] in northeast [[France]]. 
 
 == Archaeology == 
 In 1955, substantial remains of a large [[Mithraeum]] were unearthed in Mackwiller by Jean-Jacques Hatt, archaeologist and director of the [[Musée archéologique (Strasbourg)|Musée archéologique de Strasbourg]]. < ref > [http://www.persee.fr/web/revues/home/prescript/article/crai_0065-0536_1955_num_99_3_10488 Découverte d'un sanctuaire de Mithra à Mackwiller (Bas-Rhin)] {{in lang|fr}} < /ref >  The find has since been displayed at museum.
