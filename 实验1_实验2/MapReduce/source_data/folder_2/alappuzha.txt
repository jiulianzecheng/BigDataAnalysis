 '''Alappuzha''' is a city in [[Kerala]], [[India]]. It is the [[Administrative division|administrative]] [[headquarters]] of the [[Alappuzha district]]. Alappuzha is considered as the oldest [[planned city]] in the [[region]]. It is one of the most popular [[Tourism|tourist attractions]] in [[Kerala]]. The city is located in the [[coast]]al region of [[Laccadive Sea]]. In 2016, the [[Centre for Science and Environment]] rated Alappuzha as the top cleanest town in India. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/india/Alappuzha-Panaji-and-Mysuru-cleanest-cities-in-India-CSE-survey/articleshow/53160264.cms|title=Alappuzha, Panaji and Mysuru cleanest cities in India: CSE survey - Times of India}} < /ref > 
 
 ==Population== 
 According to 2011 census the [[population]] of the city is 174,176. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.census2011.co.in/census/city/460-alappuzha.html|title=Alappuzha City Population Census 2011 - Kerala}} < /ref >  The male population is 83,853 and female population is 90,323. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://www.censusindia.gov.in/2011-prov-results/paper2/data_files/India2/Table_2_PR_Cities_1Lakh_and_Above.pdf |title=Provisional Population Totals, Census of India 2011 |publisher=censusindia.gov |access-date=November 30, 2016}} < /ref >  The [[Literacy]] rate is 96.2%. The languages spoken here are [[Malayalam]], [[Tamil language|Tamil]] and [[Konkani language|Konkani]]. Most of the population of Alappuzha are of [[Hindu]] and [[Christian]] religions with a small [[Muslim]] population. 
 
 ==Famous places and events== 
 Alappuzha is famous for [[Snake boat race]]s, especially the [[Nehru Trophy Boat Race]]. Games of [[cricket]], [[football]] and [[basketball]] are also popular in the city. The [[Kerala Backwaters]] of Alappuzha are the most popular tourist attraction in Kerala. < ref > {{cite web|title=Alappuzha|url=http://www.keralatourism.org/destination/destination.php?id=161|website=www.keralatourism.org|access-date=2016-11-30|archive-date=2012-05-21|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120521020036/http://www.keralatourism.org/destination/destination.php?id=161|url-status=dead}} < /ref >
