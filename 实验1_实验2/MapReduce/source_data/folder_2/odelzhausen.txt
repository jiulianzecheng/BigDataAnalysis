 '''Odelzhausen''' is a town and [[municipality]] in [[Dachau, Bavaria|Dachau]], a district [[Districts of Germany|''(Landkreis)'']] in [[Upper Bavaria]]. The administrative collectivity of Odelzhausen consists of Odelzhausen and the townships of [[Sulzemoos]] and [[Pfaffenhofen an der Glonn]]. 
 
 ==Local areas== 
 The municipality of Odelzhausen includes 14 official local areas. 
 *Dietenhausen 
 *Ebertshausen 
 *Essenbach 
 *Gaggers 
 *Hadersried 
 *Höfa 
 *Lukka und Todtenried 
 *Miegersbach 
 *Roßbach 
 *Odelzhausen 
 *St. Johann 
 *Sittenbach 
 *Sixtnitgern 
 *Taxa 
 
 The following boundaries exist: Ebertshausen, Höfa, Odelzhausen, Sittenbach, Taxa.
