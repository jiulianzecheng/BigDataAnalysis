 '''Cottbus''' ([[Sorbian languages|Low Sorbian]]: ''Chóśebuz'', [[Upper Sorbian]]: ''Choćebuz'') is a city in the east of [[Germany]], in the [[States of Germany|state]] of [[Brandenburg]]. 
 Cottbus has about 105,000 [[inhabitant]]s and is the second largest city after [[Potsdam]] in Brandenburg. It lies southeast of [[Berlin]] on the River [[Spree]]. There are two [[university|universities]], the [[Brandenburgische Technische Universität]] (BTU) and the [[Hochschule Lausitz]].
