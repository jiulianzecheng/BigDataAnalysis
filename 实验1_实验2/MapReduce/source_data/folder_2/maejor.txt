 '''Brandon Michael Green''' (born 23 July 1988), better known by his [[stage name]] '''Maejor''' (formerly '''Bei Maejor''' and later '''Maejor Ali''') is an American record producer, rapper, singer and songwriter. He is one half of the duo [[AREA21]] alongside Dutch DJ [[Martin Garrix]]. Maejor has co-produced and written songs for many artists.
