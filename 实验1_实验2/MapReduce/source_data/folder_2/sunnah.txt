 The word '''Sunnah''' ([[Arabic language|Arabic]]: سنة) is an [[Arabic language|Arabic]] word meaning  " tradition "  or  " way. "   For [[Muslims]], Sunnah means  " the way of the prophet " .  Sunnites try to follow his words, actions and teachings.   
 
 Muslim [[scholar]]s learn about the Sunnah by studying thousands of stories about Muhammad, his family, and his first followers.  These stories are called [[Hadith]].
