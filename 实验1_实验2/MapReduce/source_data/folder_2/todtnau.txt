 '''Todtnau''' is a town in [[Lörrach (district)|Lörrach]] in [[Baden-Württemberg]], [[Germany]]. In 2020, 4,823 people lived there. 
 
 == Geography == 
 It is in the [[Black Forest]], on the river [[Wiese (river)|Wiese]], 20 & nbsp;km southeast of [[Freiburg]]. 
 
 The municipality has 8 districts (''Ortsteil''): 
 * [[File:Wappen Aftersteg.png|15px]] Aftersteg 
 * [[File:Wappen Brandenberg.png|15px]] Brandenberg 
 * [[File:Wappen Geschwend.png|15px]] Geschwend 
 * [[File:Wappen Herrenschwand.png|15px]] Herrenschwand 
 * [[Image:Wappen Muggenbrunn.png|15px]] Muggenbrunn 
 * [[File:Wappen Praeg.png|15px]] Präg 
 * [[File:Wappen Schlechtnau.png|15px]] Schlechtnau 
 * [[File:Wappen Todtnauberg.png|15px]] Todtnauberg 
 
 == Personalities == 
 * [[Karl Nessler]] (1872–1951), inventor of the [[permanent wave]] was born here. 
 * [[Martin Heidegger]] (1889–1976), had a chalet in [[Todtnauberg]]
