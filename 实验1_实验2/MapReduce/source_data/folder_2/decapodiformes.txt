 '''Decapodiformes''' is a [[superorder]] of [[Cephalopoda]]. It includes cephalopods with ten feet. These are usually eight short [[arm]]s and two long [[tentacle]]s. Well-known members are [[cuttlefish]], [[squid]], and the [[extinct]] [[belemnite]]s.
