 '''Deutschlandsberg''' ({{lang-sl|Lonč}}) is the capital of the [[Deutschlandsberg District]] in the [[States of Austria|state]] of [[Styria]] in [[Austria]]. It is near the border with [[Slovenia]]. On 1 January 2019, it has about 11,600 people live in Deutschlandsberg.
