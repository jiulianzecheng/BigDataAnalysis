 '''Mompach''' ({{lang-lb|Mompech}}) is a former [[Communes of Luxembourg|commune]] in [[Luxembourg]]. It was part of the [[Echternach (canton)|canton of Echternach]]. 
 
 On 1 January 2018, the commune was merged with [[Rosport]] to form the new commune of [[Rosport-Mompach]].
