 '''Westerhoven''' is a village in the [[Netherlands|Dutch]] [[province]] of [[North Brabant]]. It is in the [[municipality]] of [[Bergeijk]]. It is about 15 & nbsp;km southwest of [[Eindhoven]]. 
 
 ==History== 
 Westerhoven is in a valley between the rivers 'Keersop' and 'Beekloop'. The rivers both flow just outside the middle of the village. There is evidence that [[Pre-history|prehistoric]] people lived in the area. The hill connecting to the nearby village [[Riethoven]] is from the [[Ancient Rome|Roman]] times.
