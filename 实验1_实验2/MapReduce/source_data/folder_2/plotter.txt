 A '''plotter''' is a kind of [[Computer printer|printer]] for [[computer]]s. Plotters use [[vector graphics]]. Usually they are used to print to paper which is very large in size. Plotters print things using special, colored [[pen]]s. There is one called the [[drum plotter]]. They are often used in warehouses. They are generally used for making flex or maps.
