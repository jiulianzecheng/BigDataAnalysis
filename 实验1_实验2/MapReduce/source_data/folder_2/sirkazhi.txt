 '''Sirkazhi''' is a major town in the [[Mayiladuthurai district]], [[Tamil Nadu]], [[India]]. The town covers an area of {{convert|13.21|km2|abbr=off}}. The population according to 2011 census is 34,927. This city is the part of [[Cauvery delta region]]. 
 
 Sirkazhi is the motherland of the shaiva saint [[Thirugnanasambandhar]], world renowned actor [[Sivaji Ganesan]] and many famous people. 
 
 The literacy rate of Sirkazhi population is 81.5%.87% of the population is Hindus, 9.5% are Muslim, 2.1% are Christians and 1% are others. 
 
 Famous temples here are the Bhrammapureeshwarar temple, also known as [[Sattainathar Temple]],Vaitheeswaran temple and Kazheesirama Vinnagaram temple.
