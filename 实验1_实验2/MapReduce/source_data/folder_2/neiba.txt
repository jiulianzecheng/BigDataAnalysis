 The [[Dominican Republic|Dominican]] [[city]] of '''Neiba''', also written as '''Neyba''', is the head [[municipality]] of the [[Baoruco Province|Baoruco]] [[province]], on the southwestern part of the country. 
 
 Neiba, or Naiva, was the original [[Taíno]] name of the ''[[Yaque del Sur]]'' [[river]]. 
 
 ==Population== 
 The municipality had, in 2002, a total population of 36,511: 19,023 men and 17,488 women. The urban population was 66.93% of the total population. In this numbers are included the population of El Palmar municipal district, that is part of the municipality.The population of the city of Neiba without the municipal district was 27,105. < ref name= " censo "  / > 
 
 ==History== 
 The city of Neiba was founded in 1735; before that date, there were some people living here, raising [[cattle]]. 
 
 After the Dominican [[independence]] from [[Haiti]], in 1844, Neiba became a [[municipality]] of the old [[Azua Province|Azua]] province. Several [[battle]]s were fought near Neiba during the [[Dominican-Haitian War]], including the first fight of that war: ''La Fuente del Rodeo''. < ref > {{cite book |last = Pérez |first = Carlos Julio |title = Imágenes de mi región |publisher = Impresora Conadex |date = 2000 |location = Santo Domingo, Dominican Republic |language = es }} < /ref > 
 
 In 1881, with the creation of the old [[Barahona Province|Barahona]] province, Neiba was made part of these new province. 
 
 When the Baoruco province was created in 1943, Neiba became the head municipality of the province. 
 
 == Geography == 
 Neiba is in the ''Hoya de Enriquillo'' or Neiba Valley, near the eastern end of the ''[[Lake Enriquillo]]''. The ''Sierra de Neiba'' mountain range is just to the north of the city, and covers the northern half of the municipality. 
 
 The southwestern part of the municipality, close to the lake Enriquillo, is below sea level. 
 
 Neiba is at {{convert|230|km|abbr=on}} to the west of [[Santo Domingo]]. The [[average]] [[temperature]] in the city is 27.9°C and the average [[rainfall]] is 655.5 & nbsp;mm but it rains in only 45.4 days. < ref name= " fuente "  / > 
 
 Neiba has the municipality of Galván to the east, the [[Independencia Province|Independencia]] province to the south, the municipality of Villa Jaragua to the west and the [[San Juan Province (Dominican Republic)|San Juan]] province to the north. 
 
 There are few small rivers, like Las Marías, Panzo, Don Juan and El Manguito. 
 
 Neiba has a total area of {{Convert|282.3|km2|sqmi|1|abbr=on }}; without its municipal district, its area is {{Convert|203.8|km2|sqmi|1|abbr=on }}. It has only one municipal district (a subdivision of a municipality): ''El Palmar''. < ref name= " censo "  / > 
 
 ===Climate=== 
 Neiba has a [[Subtropics|subtropical]] [[steppe]]/semi-[[arid]] hot climate ([[Köppen climate classification]] : '''BSh'''). < ref name= " clima " > {{cite web |url = http://www.neyba.climatemps.com// |title = Neyba Climate  &  Temperature |publisher = Climatemps.com |access-date = 26 September 2016}} < /ref >  The climate on the mountains of the Sierra de Neiba, where there are some [[rainforest]]s, is cooler and more wet. 
 
 The [[average]] amount of [[rainfall]] for the year in the city is {{Convert|520.2|mm|in|1|abbr=on}}. Most [[rain]]s fall during the end of [[summer]]. The month with the most precipitation on average is May with {{Convert|109.7|mm|in|1|abbr=on}} of rainfall, followed by October with {{Convert|74.3|mm|in|1|abbr=on}}. 
 
 The driest [[season]] is [[winter]]. The month with the least rainfall on average is January with an average of {{Convert|8.1|mm|in|1|abbr=on}} and the second is February with {{Convert|13.7|mm|in|1|abbr=on}}. 
 
 Neiba is in a hot region; the average [[temperature]] for the year is {{Convert|27.5|C|F|0|abbr=on }}. The warmest months, on average, are July and August with an average temperature of {{Convert|28.8|C|F|1|abbr=on }}. The coolest month on average is January, with an average temperature of  {{Convert|25.5|C|F|1|abbr=on }}.

 ==Economy== 
 [[Farming]] is the only economic activity in the municipality, except for some very small [[Industry|industries]]; some important products are [[grape]]s, [[plantain]], [[sugar cane]] (mostly in the El Palmar municipal district) and [[coffee]] (on the Sierra de Neiba).
