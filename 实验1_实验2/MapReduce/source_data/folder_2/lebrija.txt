 '''Lebrija''' is a [[Municipalities of Spain|municipality]] of 27,665 people (2022). It is in the [[Provinces of Spain|province]] of [[Province of Seville|Seville]] in the [[Autonomous communities of Spain|autonomous community]] of [[Andalusia]] in south [[Spain]]. Its area is 372 & nbsp;km², which makes it one of the largest municipalities in the province.
