'''Avolar''' was a [[Mexican]] [[low-cost airline]]. Its [[headquarters]] and largest [[Airline hub|hub]] were in [[Tijuana]]. It started on September 7, 2005 with one [[Boeing 737-500]] airplane. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://aerolineasmexicanas.mx/lista-de-aerolineas-mexicanas/avolar-aerolinea-historia-y-datos|title=Aerolínea Avolar, historia y datos|work=Aerolíneas Mexicanas|access-date=3 April 2016}} < /ref >  It was one of the first low-cost airlines in [[Mexico]]. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20050815005895/en/Global-Aircraft-Solutions-Announces-5-Year-Exclusive|title=Global Aircraft Solutions Announces 5 Year Exclusive Maintenance Contract with the New Low-Cost Mexican Airline AVOLAR and Opens a Second Aircraft Maintenance Facility in Tijuana, Mexico|date=15 August 2005|access-date=3 April 2016}} < /ref >  By 2008, the airline had 10 [[Boeing 737]] airplanes. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.planespotters.net/airline/Avolar|title=Avolar Fleet Details and History|access-date=3 April 2016}} < /ref >  It flew to 21 cities throughout Mexico. Avolar suspended flights for 3 days in [[August]] [[2008]] and for 1 day in [[October]] [[2008]]. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.seattlepi.com/lifestyle/travel/article/Victors-emerge-in-Mexico-s-budget-airline-wars-1305719.php|title=Victors emerge in Mexico's budget airline wars|author=Christine Delsol, Special to the San Francisco Chronicle|date=13 August 2009|work=seattlepi.com|access-date=3 April 2016}} < /ref >  This was due to large [[debt]]s. Avolar asked to fly to the [[United States]] on October 28, 2008. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://airlineroute.net/2008/10/28/avolar-applies-us-service/|title=Avolar applies US service|access-date=3 April 2016}} < /ref >  Avolar finally stopped flying on that same day - October 28, 2008. The airline has never flown again since then. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.bloomberg.com/research/stocks/private/snapshot.asp?privcapId=8342171|title=Avolar Aerolíneas, SA de CV: Private Company Information - Businessweek|access-date=3 April 2016}} < /ref >
