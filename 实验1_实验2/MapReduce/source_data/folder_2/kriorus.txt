 '''KrioRus''' is a [[cryonics]] company that freezes dead people or animals. People do that in hope that, in the future when medicine becomes better, they can have life again, solve their health problems and have [[Immortality|eternal life]]. 
 
 KrioRus is in [[Russia]] but can freeze people from every part of the world, specially from Europe and Asia.
