 '''Anton Zaslavski''' (born 2 September 1989), better known by his stage name '''Zedd''', is a [[Russia]]n-born [[Germans|German]] [[Disc jockey|DJ]], [[songwriter]], and [[record producer]].  He was born in Saratov, Russia, and raised in Kaiserslautern, Germany. < ref > {{Cite web|url=https://tidal.com/browse/artist/3915082|title=ZEDD|website=TIDAL Browse - High Fidelity Music Streaming|language=en|access-date=2019-06-30}} < /ref >   He is known for remixing the music of other singers while also releasing his own singles. 
 
 He released  " [[Clarity (song)|Clarity]] "  (2013; featuring the singer Foxes),  " [[Stay (Zedd and Alessia Cara song)|Stay]] "  (2017; with [[Alessia Cara]]),  " [[The Middle (Zedd, Maren Morris and Grey song)|The Middle]] "  (2018; with Maren Morris and Grey), and was featured on [[Ariana Grande]]'s song  " [[Break Free (song)|Break Free]] "  (2014).  All of these songs reached the top ten on the [[Billboard Hot 100|''Billboard'' Hot 100]] in the US. < ref > {{Cite web|url=https://www.billboard.com/music/zedd|title=Zedd|website=Billboard|access-date=2019-06-30}} < /ref >  The first song won him a Grammy Award for Best Dance Recording. < ref > {{Cite web|url=https://www.grammy.com/grammys/artists/zedd|title=Zedd|date=2014-03-04|website=GRAMMY.com|language=en|access-date=2019-06-30}} < /ref > 
 
 Zedd collaborated with [[Lady Gaga]] on her third studio album, ''[[Artpop]]''.  He cites Gaga as an inspiration. < ref > {{Cite web|url=https://people.com/music/zedd-recalls-first-time-met-lady-gaga/|title=Zedd Recalls Being 'Starstruck' the First Time He Met Lady Gaga as an 'Up-and-Coming' Producer|website=PEOPLE.com|language=en|access-date=2019-06-30}} < /ref > 
 
 In 2019, he worked with [[Katy Perry]] on the songs  " [[365 (song)|365]] "  and  " [[Never Really Over]] " . 
 
 He lives in [[Beverly Hills, California|Beverly Hills]], [[California]]. < ref > {{Cite web|url=https://www.architecturaldigest.com/story/zedds-beverly-hills-estate-is-a-modern-architecture-lovers-dream|title=Zedd's Beverly Hills Estate Is a Modern-Architecture Lover’s Dream|last=Nast|first=Condé|website=Architectural Digest|language=en|access-date=2019-06-30}} < /ref >
