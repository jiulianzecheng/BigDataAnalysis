 '''Acanthuridae''' is the [[family (biology)|family]] of [[surgeonfish]], [[tang]]s, and [[Naso (genus)|unicornfish]].  
 
 The family includes about 86 living species. It is made up of [[ocean|marine]] fish living in tropical seas, usually around [[coral reef]]s. Many of the species are brightly colored and popular for [[aquarium|aquaria]]. 
 
 One of the characteristics of this family is that they have spines on the tail (also known as  " thorn tail " ). < ref > {{cite web |last1=Froese |first1=R. |last2=Pauly |first2=D. |title=FAMILY Details for Acanthuridae - Surgeonfishes, tangs, unicornfishes |url=https://www.fishbase.se/Summary/FamilySummary.php?Family=Acanthuridae |website=www.fishbase.se |accessdate=12 July 2022 |archiveurl=https://web.archive.org/web/20220712160002/https://www.fishbase.se/Summary/FamilySummary.php?Family=Acanthuridae |archivedate=12 July 2022 |language=en |date=February 2022}} < /ref > 
 
 == Size == 
 Most species of the family are of small size, between 15-40 cm  (6-15 in), but the [[genera]] ''[[Prionorus]]'' or ''[[Naso]]'' can be larger.
