 The [[Family (biology)|family]] '''Hydrogenothemaceae''' are [[bacteria]] that live in environments not good for [[human|humans]]. They have been found in hot springs, sulfur pools, thermal ocean vents. They are the only bacteria that live there (things like the [[Archaea]] are not bacteria).
