 '''Hypochlorite''' is an [[ion]]. It can be found in household bleach, [[sodium hypochlorite]]. It is unstable, meaning that it breaks down easily. Hypochlorites react with [[acid]]s to produce [[chlorine]]. Their chemical formula is ClO < sup > - < /sup > . It is a strong [[oxidizing agent]]. It contains [[chlorine]] in its +1 [[oxidation state]]. They are the [[salt]]s of [[hypochlorous acid]].
