'''Izhitsa or''' '''Izhica''' (Ѵ, ѵ; italics: ''Ѵ ѵ''; OCS: Ѷжица, Russian: Ижица, Ukrainian: Іжиця) is a letter of the early [[Cyrillic alphabet]] and several later alphabets, usually the last letter.
