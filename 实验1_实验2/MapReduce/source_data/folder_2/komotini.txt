 '''Komotini''' is a [[Greece|Greek]] city in [[Greek Thrace]]. Thrace is a [[periphery]] in the northwest of [[Greece]]. Komotini is a capital of [[Rodopi]] prefecture. 52.659 people live there. Some of the people are [[Muslims]]. Komotini is the second city in Greece which has [[Muslims]].
