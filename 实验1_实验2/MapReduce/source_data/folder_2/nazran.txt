 '''Nazran''' ({{lang-ru|Назрань}}) is the largest city in [[Ingushetia]], [[Russia]]. It was the [[capital city|capital]] of the republic from 1991 to 2000, until it was replaced by [[Magas]]. In 2010, 93,335 people lived there.
