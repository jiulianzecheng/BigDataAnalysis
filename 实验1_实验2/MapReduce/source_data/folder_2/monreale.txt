 '''Monreale''' is a city in [[Sicily]] in southern [[Italy]]. Monreale is in the [[Metropolitan City of Palermo]]. About 39,000 people live there. It is 7 & nbsp;km southwest of [[Palermo]] on the slope of Monte Caputo. It is home to [[Monreale Cathedral]].
