 '''Courcouronnes''' is a former [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is in [[Île-de-France]] in the [[Essonne]] [[Departments of France|department]] in north [[France]]. On 1 January 2019, it was merged into the new commune  of [[Évry-Courcouronnes]]. < ref > {{cite web|title=Arrêté préfectoral de l'Essonne, 12 Octobre 2018|url=http://www.essonne.gouv.fr/content/download/25837/204349/file/RAA+n°+127+publié+le+18+10.pdf|access-date=19 October 2018|pages=83–86}} < /ref >  In 2018, 13,490 people lived there. 
 
 The commune is in the southern [[suburb]]s of [[Paris]], {{convert|26.4|km|mi|abbr=on}} from the center. 
 
 The aerospace company [[Arianespace]] is headquartered in Courcouronnes.
