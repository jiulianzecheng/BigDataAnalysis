 '''Selkingen''' is a village and former [[municipality]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Valais]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 On 1 January 2001 the former municipalities of Selkingen, [[Biel, Valais|Biel]] and Ritzingen merged into the new municipality of [[Grafschaft]].
