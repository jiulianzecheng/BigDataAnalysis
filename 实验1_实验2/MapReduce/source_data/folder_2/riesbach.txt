 '''Riesbach''' is a [[district]] in the [[city]] of [[Zürich]] in [[Switzerland]]. It is District number 8. 
 
 ==History== 
 The district has these quarters: [[Seefeld (Zürich)|Seefeld]], [[Mühlebach (Zürich)|Mühlebach]] and [[Weinegg]]. Until 1893 it was a [[municipality]] on its own. Then it was added to Zürich.
