 '''Bulbuls''' are a family of [[birds]] living in [[Africa]], the [[Middle East]] and southern [[Asia]]. They are [[passerine]] songbirds.  There are about 130 [[species]] in 24 [[genera]]. 
 
 Many live in forests: the greenbuls, brownbuls, leafloves, or bristlebills. Most African species are found in [[rainforest]]s whilst in Asia they live in more open areas.
