 '''''Corythomantis''''' is a group of frogs in the family [[Hylidae]].  They live in northeastern [[Brazil]]. There was only one species in the group until 2012. Then scientists wrote about another species, ''[[Corythomantis galeata]]''. Later, that frog was moved to the genus ''[[Nyctimantis]]''. < ref name=Des > {{cite journal|author1=Marques, R|author2= C.F.B. Haddad|author3=A.A.
