 '''Jegenstorf''' ([[Bernese German]]: ''Jegischtorf'') is a [[municipality]] in the [[administrative district]] of [[Bern-Mittelland (administrative district)|Bern-Mittelland]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Bern (canton)|Bern]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 On 1 January 2010, the former municipality of [[Ballmoos]] merged into the municipality of Jegenstorf. On 1 January 2014, the former municipalities of [[Münchringen]] and [[Scheunen]] merged into the municipality of Jegenstorf.
