 '''Archivo''' is a grotesque [[sans serif]] [[typeface]] designed by Héctor Gatti  < ref > {{Cite web|title=Archivo in use|url=https://fontsinuse.com/typefaces/44329/archivo|access-date=2023-09-25|website=Fonts in Use|language=en}} < /ref >  and released in [[2012]]. It is available on the Omnibus Type website. In [[2016]], it received an award at the ''Tipos Latinos'' [[Latin America|Latin American]] typeface design competition. < ref > {{Cite web|last=contenidos)|first=Guido Ferreyra (código), Sergio Rodriguez (diseño y|title=Tipos Latinos · Resultados|url=https://www.tiposlatinos.com/br/bienales/septima-bienal-tl2016/resultados|access-date=2023-09-25|website=www.tiposlatinos.com|language=pt}} < /ref >  Archivo has two main variants, Archivo Narrow and Archivo Black. 
 
 '''Archivo Narrow''' is a condensed version of the original, better for compact spaces like [[Headline|headlines]]. '''Archivo Black''' is thicker and has a more prominent appearance. The Archivo [[font]] family has a classic style similar of fonts from the [[1800s]]. In [[2021]], it was updated to become a ''variable'' font, < ref > {{Cite web|title=Variable Fonts|url=https://fonts.google.com/variablefonts|access-date=2023-09-25|website=Google Fonts|language=en}} < /ref >  allowing it to vary in thickness and width. 
 
 Archivo supports a lot of [[File format|file formats]], it also contains 641 [[Glyph|glyphs]] and supports up to 200 [[Language|languages]], < ref > {{Cite web|date=28 September 2023|orig-date=|title=Free and Open Source Fonts Selected by Filippos Fragkogiannis|url=https://www.peopleofprint.com/best-of/free-and-open-source-fonts-selected-by-filippos-fragkogiannis|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20230928101920/https://www.peopleofprint.com/best-of/free-and-open-source-fonts-selected-by-filippos-fragkogiannis|archive-date=28 September 2023|access-date=28 September 2023|website=People of Print|language=English|publication-place=United Kingdom|quote=With support for over 200 world languages, Archivo became a variable font in 2021, offering a wide range of styles from Thin to Black and from ExtraCondensed to Expanded through its weight and width axes.}} < /ref >  making it suitable for both [[Printing|print]] and online usage. Also, it is an [[Open source|open-source]] font, < ref > {{Cite web|title=20 Best Google Web Fonts|url=https://www.awwwards.com/20-best-web-fonts-from-google-web-fonts-and-font-face.html|access-date=2023-09-25|website=www.awwwards.com|language=en}} < /ref >  meaning one can freely use it without attribution or licensing fees for personal or commercial projects. You can also [[download]] it from [[GitHub]], < ref > {{Citation|last=Omnibus-Type|title=Archivo family|date=2023-09-07|url=https://github.com/Omnibus-Type/Archivo|access-date=2023-09-25}} < /ref >  [[Google]] Fonts, < ref > {{Cite web|title=Archivo|url=https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Archivo|access-date=2023-09-25|website=Google Fonts|language=en}} < /ref >  and other sources online.
