'''Reichsministers''' were some of the members of the German [[parliament]], the [[Reichstag (institution)|Reichstag]], between 1919 and 1945. They were leaders of [[Ministry (government department)|government ministries]]. 
 
 During the [[German Empire]], there were no Reichsministers.
