 '''Metra''' is a commuter railroad in the [[Chicago]] city area. The railroad operates 241 [[train station|stations]] on 11 different rail lines. < ref name=Metra-FAQ / >  It is the fourth busiest [[commuter rail system]] in the [[United States]] by ridership and the largest and busiest commuter rail system outside the [[New York City]] area. < ref name=APTA-Ridership > {{Cite report |url=http://www.apta.com/resources/statistics/Documents/Ridership/2013-q4-ridership-APTA.pdf |title=Public Transportation Ridership Report, Fourth Quarter 2013. Commuter Rail. |publisher=[[American Public Transportation Association]] (APTA) |pages=5–6 |date=February 26, 2014 |access-date=March 23, 2014}} < /ref >  There were 83.4 million passenger rides in 2014, up 1.3% from the previous year. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://metrarail.com/metra/en/home/about_metra/planning_records_reports/ridership_reports/monthlyannualridership.html |title=Monthly/Annual Ridership |access-date=2015-05-05 |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150503134007/http://metrarail.com/metra/en/home/about_metra/planning_records_reports/ridership_reports/monthlyannualridership.html |archive-date=2015-05-03 }} < /ref >
