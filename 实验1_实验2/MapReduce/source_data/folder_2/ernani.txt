 '''''Ernani''''' is an [[Italy|Italian]] [[opera]] in four acts by [[Giuseppe Verdi]], with a [[libretto]] by Francesco Maria Piave, based on [[Victor Hugo]]'s play ''Hernani''. It was first performed in [[Venice]], on March 9, 1844.  
 
 ==Principal roles== 
 
 * Ernani - [[tenor]] 
 * Elvira - [[soprano]] 
 * Carlo - [[baritone]] 
 * Silva - [[bass (vocal range)|bass]] 
 
 ==Sources== 
 * ''The Complete Dictionary of Opera  &  Operetta'', James Anderson, Wings Books, 1993.
