 '''''Leptoceratops''''' (meaning  " slender horned face " ) was a stocky [[ceratopsia]]n [[dinosaur]] that had a large skull are there was a horn on its beaked snout. It is known from five skulls and some skeletal material. ''Leptoceratops'' are during in the [[Upper Cretaceous]], about 68{{ndash}}65 million years ago. Fossils have been found in [[Alberta]], [[Canada]], and [[Wyoming]], in [[North America]].
