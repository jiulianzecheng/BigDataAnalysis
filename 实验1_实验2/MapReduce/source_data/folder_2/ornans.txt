 '''Ornans''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is in [[Bourgogne-Franche-Comté]] in the [[Doubs]] [[Departments of France|department]] in east [[France]]. On 1 January 2016, the former commune of [[Bonnevaux-le-Prieuré]] was merged into Ornans. < ref name=arrete > [https://www.legifrance.gouv.fr/eli/arrete/2015/11/6/INTB1525724A/jo/texte Arrêté préfectoral] 6 November 2015 {{in lang|fr}} < /ref >
