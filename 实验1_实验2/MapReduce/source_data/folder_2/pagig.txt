 '''Pagig''' ({{lang-rm|Pagiai}}) is a former [[municipality]] of the [[district]] of [[Plessur (district)|Plessur]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Graubünden]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 On 1 January 2008 the former municipalities of Pagig and [[St. Peter, Switzerland|St. Peter]] merged into the new municipality of [[St. Peter-Pagig]].
