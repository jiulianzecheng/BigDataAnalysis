 '''Kronach''' is a [[Town#Germany|town]] in [[Upper Franconia]], [[Germany]], in the [[Frankenwald]] area. It is the capital of the [[Kronach (district)|district Kronach]]. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 ===Place=== 
 Kronach is at the southwestern edge of the [[Franconian Forest]]. The rivers [[Haßlach]], [[Kronach (Haßlach)|Kronach]] and [[Rodach (Main)|Rodach]] [[Confluence|unite]] in Kronach.
