 '''ZooMoo''' is an international [[pay television]] channel. It is owned by Singapore's Beach House Pictures and New Zealand's [[NHNZ]]. The channel was first launched in Brazil in September 2001 in [[High-definition television|high definition]] (HD). < ref name= " zoomoo network "  / >  On 7 January 2006, ZooMoo was launched in [[Singapore]].
