 '''Sikasso''' is a [[city]] in the south of [[Mali]]. It is the [[capital city|capital]] of the [[Sikasso Cercle]] and the [[Sikasso Region]]. It is the second largest city in Mali. 225,753 people lived there in the 2009 census{{fact|date=August 2018}}.
