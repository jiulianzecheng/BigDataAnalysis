The name '''Lillian''' [ '''lil'''-ee-''uh''n ] < ref > {{Cite web|title=Definition of Lillian {{!}} Dictionary.com|url=https://www.dictionary.com/browse/lillian|access-date=2023-04-07|website=www.dictionary.com|language=en}} < /ref >  is a [[female]] [[given name]]. It comes from the [[Latin language|Latin]] word for  " [[lily]] " . Lillian is a name that has been well-liked for many years, and it has a neutral meaning that can be found in various cultures. The term Lillian comes from the Latin word  " lilium, "  which refers to the flower lily. The lily is a stunning flower that is often linked with beauty, innocence, and purity. For parents seeking a traditional and sophisticated name for their child, Lillian is a frequently chosen option. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Lillian name Meaning and Origin|url=https://babys.name/detail-Lillian-Meaning-Origin-Famous-Similar-Sibling.html|access-date=2023-04-07|website=babys.name}} < /ref >  Some people named ''Lillian'' are Princess Lilian of Belgium and Lillian Disney, a widow of [[Walt Disney]]. Fictional characters include Queen Lilian in the Shrek [[movie]]s. This name was ranked #51 on the US Popular Names in 2021. < ref > {{Cite web|last=OACT|title=Popular Baby Names|url=https://www.ssa.gov/cgi-bin/popularnames.cgi|access-date=2023-04-07|website=www.ssa.gov}} < /ref >
