 '''Safi''' ([[Berber languages|Berber]]: '''Asfi''', ⴰⵙⴼⵉ; {{lang-ar|أسفي}}, [[Portuguese language|Portuguese]]: ''Safim'') is a city in western [[Morocco]] on the [[Atlantic Ocean]]. The capital of [[Safi Province]], it has a population of 282,227 (2004 census). < ref > {{Cite web |url=http://www.hcp.ma/Recensement-general-de-la-population-et-de-l-habitat-2004_a633.html |title=2004 Morocco Population census |access-date=2016-01-05 |archive-date=2018-06-19 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180619163929/https://www.hcp.ma/Recensement-general-de-la-population-et-de-l-habitat-2004_a633.html |url-status=dead }} < /ref >  Safi is the main fishing port for the country's [[sardine]] industry, and also exports [[phosphate]]s, textiles and [[Pottery|ceramics]].
