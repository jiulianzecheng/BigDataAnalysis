'''Danelectro''' is an [[United|American]] [[company]] that makes [[Musical instrument|musical instruments]] and accessories. Their main products are [[guitar]]s, [[bass guitar]]s, [[instrument amplifier|amplifier]]s and [[effects unit]]s. The company  was founded by Nathan Daniel in [[1947]] in Red Banks, New Jersey. [[Jimi Hendrix]] is among the many well-known guitarists to have used Danelectro. The company is headuartered in [[Camarillo, California]]
