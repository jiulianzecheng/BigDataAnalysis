 '''Vichy''' ({{lang-oc|Vichèi}}) is a [[Communes of France|''commune'']] in central [[France]], in the [[Auvergne-Rhône-Alpes]] [[Regions of France|region]], along the [[Allier (river)|Allier]] river. It is the ''commune'' in the department with second largest population after [[Montluçon]]. It is in the [[History|historic]] [[province]] of ''Bourbonnais''. 
 
 ==History== 
 Vichy has lots of [[Spa]]s and resorts. This site was inhabited before the Roman conquest of [[Gaul]]. < ref > Gros, J.F. (1884). [https://books.google.com/books?id=hVoWAAAAYAAJ & dq=vichy+city & pg=PA43   " Vichy in History, " ] ''Vichy and environs,'' pp. 83-54. < /ref > 
 
 In 1861, [[Napoleon III]] visited Vichy several times. < ref > Prince, Danforth ''et al.'' (2011). [https://books.google.com/books?id=TLP_5UmSv7wC & dq=vichy+city & pg=PA743  ''Frommer's France,'' p. 473.] < /ref > 
 
 During [[World War II]], it was the [[Capital (city)|capital city]] of [[Vichy France]]. < ref > Vinen, Richard. (2007). [https://books.google.com/books?id=6sRSyR9CvYcC & dq=v & pg=PA45  ''The Unfree French: Life Under the Occupation,'' p. 45.] < /ref >

 === Climate === 
 The [[Köppen climate classification]] type for the [[climate]] at Vichy is an  " [[Oceanic climate]] "  (also known as Maritime Temperate climate) and of the subtype  " Cfb " . < ref name= " mairie "  / > 
 
 ==Population== 
 The inhabitants of Vichy are known, in [[French language|French]], as ''Vichyssois'' (women: ''Vichyssoises''). < ref > {{cite web |url=https://www.habitants.fr/allier-03 |title=Allier |publisher=habitants.fr |language=French |accessdate=24 September 2017 |date= }} < /ref > 
 
 With a population of 25,279, < ref name=insee > {{cite web |url=http://www.insee.fr/fr/statistiques/fichier/2525755/dep03.pdf |title=Régions, départements, arrondissements, cantons et communes |work= Populations légales 2014 |publisher=Institut national de la statistique et des études économiques - INSEE |language=French |accessdate=24 September 2017|date= }} < /ref >  Vichy is the second largest city of the Allier department. It has a [[population density]] of {{Pop density|25279|5.85|km2|prec=0|disp=num}} inhabitants/km < sup > 2 < /sup > .

 == Administration == 
 Vichy is a [[Prefectures in France|subprefecture]] of the Allier department since 1941; before that year, the subprefecture was in [[Lapalisse]]. It is also the capital of the ''arrondissement'' of [[Arrondissement of Vichy|Vichy]] and the [[administrative centre]] ({{lang-fr|chef-lieu}}) of two cantons: 
 * [[Canton of Vichy-1|Vichy-1]] formed by 3 ''communes'' and part of Vichy, with 18,420 inhabitants  < small > (2014) < /small > . 
 * [[Canton of Vichy-2|Vichy-2]] formed by 2 ''communes'' and part of Vichy, with 18,763 inhabitants  < small > (2014) < /small > . 
 
 It is part of the [[Communes of France|intercommunality]] ''Vichy Communauté''.
