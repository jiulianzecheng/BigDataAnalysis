 The '''Australia and New Zealand Banking Group Limited''', commonly called '''ANZ''', is the third largest [[bank]] by [[market capitalisation]] in Australia, after the [[Commonwealth Bank]] and [[Westpac Banking Corporation]]. < ref > {{cite web|date=26 October 2017|title=ASX 200 List / Market Capitalisation Accurate on: 1 October 2017|url=http://www.asx200list.com/|access-date=26 October 2017|website=ASX 200|publisher=[[Australian Securities Exchange]]}} < /ref >  Australian operations make up the largest part of ANZ's business, with [[Commercial bank|commercial]] and [[retail banking]] dominating. ANZ is also the largest bank in New Zealand, where the legal entity became known as ANZ National Bank Limited in 2003 and changed to [[ANZ Bank New Zealand|ANZ Bank New Zealand Limited]] in 2012. From 2003 to 2012, it operated two brands in New Zealand, ANZ and the [[National Bank of New Zealand]]. The National Bank brand was retired in 2012, with a number of branches closing and others converting to ANZ branches. < ref name= " auto " > [http://www.3news.co.nz/ANZ-still-finalising-National-Bank-closures/tabid/421/articleID/282480/Default.aspx ANZ still finalising National Bank closures] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20131102034650/http://www.3news.co.nz/ANZ-still-finalising-National-Bank-closures/tabid/421/articleID/282480/Default.aspx |date=2013-11-02 }}. ''3 News NZ''. 10 October 2013. < /ref > 
 
 In addition to operations throughout Australia and New Zealand, ANZ also operates in 34 other nations. < ref > {{cite web|title=About ANZ|url=http://www.shareholder.anz.com/our-company/profile?_ga=2.229213212.1603293837.1497837201-1462906959.1492646610|access-date=19 June 2017|publisher=Australia and New Zealand Banking Group|archive-date=1 February 2018|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180201020205/http://shareholder.anz.com/our-company/profile?_ga=2.229213212.1603293837.1497837201-1462906959.1492646610|url-status=dead}} < /ref > 
 
 ANZ was named the most sustainable bank globally in the 2008 [[Dow Jones]] Sustainability Index making it the 2nd year in a row ANZ has been granted the title. In 2007 the title was shared with another Australian bank, [[Westpac]], which had held the title for the previous five years. < ref > {{cite web|title=ANZ ranked world's most sustainable bank|url=http://www.investordaily.com.au/cps/rde/xchg/id/style/4951.htm?rdeCOQ%3DSID-3F579BCE-FAF630CF|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120112065006/http://www.investordaily.com.au/cps/rde/xchg/id/style/4951.htm?rdeCOQ=SID-3F579BCE-FAF630CF|archive-date=12 January 2012|access-date=2008-12-15|url-status=dead|df=dmy}} < /ref >
