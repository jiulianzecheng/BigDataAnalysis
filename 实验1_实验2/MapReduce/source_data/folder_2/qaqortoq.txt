 '''Qaqortoq''', also known by its Danish name ''Julianeh√•b'', is a town in southwestern [[Greenland]]. With a population of 3,100, it is South Greenland's biggest town. It is the fourth biggest [[List of towns in Greenland|town in Greenland]]. The name Qaqortoq is Western [[Greenlandic language|Greenlandic]], and it means  " white " . Qaqortoq also is the seat of the [[municipality]] of Qaqortoq which apart from the town itself includes three small settlements. Qaqortoq was founded by [[Norway|Norwegian]] Anders Olsen in [[1775]]. Near Qaqortoq are the [[Hvalsey]] ruins, the most famous Norse ruins in Greenland.
