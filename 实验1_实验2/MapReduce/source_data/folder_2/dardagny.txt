 '''Dardagny''' is a [[municipality]] of the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Geneva (canton)|Geneva]] in [[Switzerland]]. The main sights of Dardagny are: the castle, the chapel ''Malval'' and the farm ''Bellevaux''. 
 
 In 1978, Dardagny received the [[Wakker Prize]] for the development and preservation of its architectural heritage.
