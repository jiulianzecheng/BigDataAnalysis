 '''Blackburn'''  is a [[town]] in [[Lancashire]], [[England]]. The town has a [[population]] of about 105,000 people. 
 Blackburn is mentioned in one of the Beatles' songs.  The town's football team is [[Blackburn Rovers F.C.]]
