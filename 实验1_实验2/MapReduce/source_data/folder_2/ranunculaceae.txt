 '''Ranunculaceae''' is a family of [[flowering plant]]s also known as the  " buttercup family "  or  " crowfoot family " . Members include ''[[Anemone]]'' (anemones), ''Ranunculus'' ([[buttercup]]), ''[[Aconitum]]'' (aconite), and ''[[Clematis]]''.
