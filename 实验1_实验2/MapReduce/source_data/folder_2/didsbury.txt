 '''Didsbury''' is a area of [[Manchester]] 5-6 miles south of the city centre. According to the [[2001]] census it had a population of 14,292. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.ons.gov.uk/help/localstatistics|title=Local statistics - Office for National Statistics|website=www.ons.gov.uk}} < /ref >
