 '''''Uproxx''''' (stylized in all caps) is a news website covering entertainment and popular culture in the United States. Jarret Myer and Brian Brater founded it in 2008, and Woven Digital (later known as Uproxx Media Group) purchased it in 2014. Men ages 18 to 34 make up the site's intended audience. < ref name= " Spangler14 " > {{Cite news|last=Todd Spangler|date=April 17, 2014|title=Showbiz Site Uproxx Acquired by Guy-Focused Digital Media Firm Woven|work=[[Variety (magazine)|Variety]]|url=https://variety.com/2014/digital/news/showbiz-site-uproxx-acquired-by-guy-focused-digital-media-firm-woven-1201158843/|access-date=12 November 2015}} < /ref >  It was obtained by [[Warner Music Group]] in August 2018, with Myer and CEO Benjamin Blank remaining in control of the company's operations. < ref > {{Cite news|date=2018-08-02|title=WMG Acquires UPROXX|language=en-us|work=RAMP - Radio and Music Pros|url=http://ramp247.com/executive-action/wmg-acquires-uproxx/|access-date=2018-08-02}} < /ref >
