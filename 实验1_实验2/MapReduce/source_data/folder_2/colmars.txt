 '''Colmars''' or '''Colmars-les-Alpes''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]] in the [[Alpes-de-Haute-Provence]] [[Departments of France|department]] in southeastern [[France]]. 
 
 There are two other hamlets in the commune: Clignon-Haut and Clignon-Bas.
