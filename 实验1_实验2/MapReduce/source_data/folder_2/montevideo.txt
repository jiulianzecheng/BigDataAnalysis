 '''Montevideo''' is the [[capital (city)|capital]] and the largest city in [[Uruguay]]. It was founded by the [[Portugal|Portuguese]] in 1717. Its best neighbourhood is called Carrasco. This neighbourhood is very exclusive due to its architectural styles.
