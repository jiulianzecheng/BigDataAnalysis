 '''Venray''' is a [[List of municipalities of the Netherlands|municipality]] in the [[Netherlands|Dutch]] province of [[Limburg (Netherlands)|Limburg]]. As of 2020, about 41,000 people were living in the municipality and about 28,500 people were living in the town. 
 
 In 2010, parts of the former municipality of [[Meerlo-Wanssum]] were added to Venray ([[Blitterswijck]], [[Geijsteren]], and [[Wanssum]]).
