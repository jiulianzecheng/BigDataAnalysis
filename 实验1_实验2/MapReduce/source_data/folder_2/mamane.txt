 '''Māmane''' ([[Hawaiian language]]) is a shrub of [[Hawaii|Hawai'i]] in the ''[[Sophora]]'' [[genus]]. It is suitable for gardens. 
 
 It is highly [[Polymorphism (biology)|polymorphic]], growing as a [[shrub]] or [[tree]], and able to reach a height of {{convert|15|m|ft|abbr=on}} in tree form. It is grow fast in every island of Hawaiian archipelago, so botanists describe many local forms including ''S.unifoliolata'' < ref > [http://www.botany.hawaii.edu/faculty/carr/images/sop_chr_5276.jpg Hawaiian Native Plant Genera] < /ref >
