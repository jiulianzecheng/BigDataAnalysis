 '''Prosciutto''' is a type of [[Italy|Italian]] [[ham]]. It involves a long [[Curing (food preservation)|curing]] process in which it is salted. < ref > {{Cite web|last=Meadow|first=The|title=How is Prosciutto Made?|url=https://themeadow.com/pages/how-is-prosciutto-made|access-date=2021-05-20|website=The Meadow|language=en}} < /ref >  It is usually sliced thinly and served raw. Different regions of Italy have a different process and name for the food.
