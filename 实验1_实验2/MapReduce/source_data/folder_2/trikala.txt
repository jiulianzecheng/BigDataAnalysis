 '''Trikala''' is a Greek city and it is one of 51 [[prefecture]]s of [[Greece]]. Trikala is in the center of [[Greece]] in the [[Thessaly]] periphery. According to the 2001 [[census]], 57,914 people lived there. Trikala is built on a large plain. This [[plain]] is one of the biggest plain in Greece. In the west of Trikala is the [[Pindus]] range. Trikala is crossed by Litheus river.
