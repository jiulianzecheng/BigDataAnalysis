 '''Nax''' was a [[municipality]] of the [[district]] of [[Hérens (district)|Hérens]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Valais]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 On 1 January 2011, the former municipalities of Nax, [[Mase, Switzerland|Mase]] and [[Vernamiège]] merged into the new municipality of [[Mont-Noble]].
