 A '''proverb''' is a [[Wisdom|wise]] saying. Many cultures have collections of these, such as the [[Book of Proverbs]] in the [[Old Testament]], was said to be written by king [[Solomon]] of [[Israel]]. 
 A proverb is basically a wise saying.
