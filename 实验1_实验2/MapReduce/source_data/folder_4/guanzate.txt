 '''Guanzate''' is a ''[[comune]]'' (municipality) in the [[Province of Como]] in the [[Italy|Italian]] region of [[Lombardy]]. It is about {{convert|30|km|mi|0}} northwest of [[Milan]] and about {{convert|11|km|mi|0}} southwest of [[Como]]. As of 31 December 2004, 5,290 people lived there. It has an area of 6.9 & nbsp;km². < ref name= " istat " > All demographics and other statistics: Italian statistical institute [[National Institute of Statistics (Italy)|Istat]]. < /ref > 
 
 Guanzate is next to these municipalities: [[Appiano Gentile]], [[Bulgarograsso]], [[Cadorago]], [[Cassina Rizzardi]], [[Cirimido]], [[Fenegrò]], [[Fino Mornasco]], [[Lomazzo]], [[Veniano]].
