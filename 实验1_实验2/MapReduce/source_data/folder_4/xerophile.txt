'''Xerophiles''' are [[extremophile]] [[organism]]s that can grow and reproduce in conditions with almost no water. 
 
 Many [[mould]]s and [[yeast]]s are xerophilic. Mould growth on bread is an example of food spoilt by a xerophilic [[fungus]].
