 '''Falconidae''' is a [[Family (biology)|family]] of [[bird]]s with 60 [[species]] of [[diurnal]] [[Bird of prey|birds of prey]]. The family has two subfamiles, Polyborinae, which includes the [[Caracara (subfamily)|caracara]]s and [[forest falcon]]s, and Falconinae, which includes the [[falcon]]s, [[kestrel]]s and [[falconet]]s.
