 The '''Terai''' or '''Tarai''' is a region of northern [[India]] and southern [[Nepal]]. The Terai is a strip of natural marshland bordering the [[Yamuna River]] to the west and the [[Brahmaputra River]] to the east. To the north of the Terai are the [[Himalayas]].
