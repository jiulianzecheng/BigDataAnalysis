 '''Braggio''' is a former [[municipality]] of the [[district]] of [[Moesa (district)|Moesa]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Graubünden]] in [[Switzerland]]. On 1 January 2015 the former municipalities of Braggio, [[Arvigo]], [[Cauco]] and [[Selma, Switzerland|Selma]] merged into the new municipality of [[Calanca, Switzerland|Calanca]].
