 '''Wanetsi''' is a [[Iranian languages|Iranian language]] spoken in [[Pakistan|Pakistan's]] [[Balochistan, Pakistan|Balochistan province]].  It is very closely related to [[Pashto]], which some [[linguists]] consider it a dialect of.
