 '''Pocky''' is a Japanese [[snack]] food. They are [[chocolate]]-coated [[biscuit]] sticks. They are made by [[Ezaki Glico]] and were first sold in 1966. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.glico.co.jp/en/corp/history02.html|accessdate=2014-03-10|title=History|publisher=Ezaki Glico Co., Ltd.|archive-date=2014-10-02|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20141002074541/http://www.glico.co.jp/en/corp/history02.html|url-status=dead}} < /ref >  They can be found in flavors such as [[chocolate]], [[strawberry]], [[almond]] and others. They are sold in Europe under the name '''Mikado'''.
