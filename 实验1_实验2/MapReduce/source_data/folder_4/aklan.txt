 '''Aklan''' (''Akean'') (Aklanon pronunciation: [ak'É£an]) ([[Aklanon language|Akeanon]]: ''Eaeawigan it Akean''; {{lang-hil|Kapuoran sang Aklan}}) is a [[Provinces of the Philippines|province]] of the [[Philippines]] in the [[Western Visayas]] [[Regions of the Philippines|region]]. It is the oldest province in the Philippines. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Historical Background {{!}} The Official Website of Aklan Province|url=http://aklan.gov.ph/profile/historical-background/|access-date=2020-12-04|language=en-US|archive-date=2019-03-31|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190331010526/http://aklan.gov.ph/profile/historical-background/|url-status=dead}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite web|title=Aklan: The Philippines' Oldest Province|url=https://ph.news.yahoo.com/aklan-philippines-oldest-province-061046849.html|access-date=2020-12-04|website=ph.news.yahoo.com|language=en-PH}} < /ref >  Its capital is [[Kalibo]]. 
 
 == History == 
 People from Aklan are called Aklanons. They are thought to be [[Ancestor|descendants]] of the [[Austronesian peoples|Austronesian]]-speaking [[Immigration|immigrants]] who came to the Philippines during the [[Iron Age]]. The name came from the river Akean, which means ''where there is boiling.''
