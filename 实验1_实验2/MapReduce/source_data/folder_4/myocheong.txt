'''Myo Cheong''' ([[Hangul]]: 묘청, [[Hanja]]:妙淸, ?-[[1135]]) was a [[Korea]]n  [[Buddhism|Buddhist]] monk, politician, and [[philosopher]] during the [[Goryeo Dynasty]]. His other name was '''Jungshim'''.  
 
 Myo cheong was from [[Pyongyang]]. Although he was a [[Buddhist]], he performed [[divination]] and [[prophecy]].
