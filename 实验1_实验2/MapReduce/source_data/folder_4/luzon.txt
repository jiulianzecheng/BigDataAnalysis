 '''Luzon''' is the largest island in the [[Philippines]]. It is in the northern section of the country. The capital of the Philippines, [[Manila]], as well as other cities like [[Quezon City]], are on the island. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Islands of Philippines|url=http://islands.unep.ch/IHE.htm#898|access-date=2019-08-08|website=islands.unep.ch|archive-date=2019-04-28|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190428003606/http://islands.unep.ch/IHE.htm#898|url-status=dead}} < /ref > 
 
 As of the 2010 census, 48.5 million people lived on Luzon.
