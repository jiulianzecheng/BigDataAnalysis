 '''Fanas''' was a [[municipality]] of the [[district]] of [[Davos (district)|Davos]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Graubünden]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 On 1 January 2011, the former municipalities of Fanas and [[Valzeina]] merged into the new municipality of [[Grüsch]].
