[[File:Umerkot_Fort_view1.JPG|thumb|364x364px|Umarkot Fort]]Umerkot is a town in [[Umerkot District|Umerkot]] district of [[Sindh]] Province in [[Pakistan]]. The town is famous because the great Mughal emperor [[Akbar the Great]] born here in 14 October 1542 when Emperor [[Humayun]] fled to escape from [[Sher Shah Suri]]. The excellent artistic tourist spot of Umerkot Fort where Emperor [[Akbar the Great|Akbar]] born is here. The town is well connected with [[Karachi]] and [[Hyderabad, Sindh|Hyderabad]].
