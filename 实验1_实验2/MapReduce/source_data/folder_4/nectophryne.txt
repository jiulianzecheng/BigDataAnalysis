 '''''Nectophryne''''', or '''African tree toads''', is a [[genus]] of [[Bufonidae|true toads]].  There are only two species.  They live in western Africa - Nigeria, Cameroon, Gabon, northeastern Congo, Bioko and Equatorial Guinea.
