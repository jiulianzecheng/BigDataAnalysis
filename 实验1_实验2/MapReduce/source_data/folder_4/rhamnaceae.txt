 The '''''Rhamnaceae''''' is a family of plants in the order [[Rosales]], with around 55 genera and 950 species. < ref > {{Cite web|url=https://ucjeps.berkeley.edu/eflora/eflora_display.php?tid=239|title=Rhamnaceae|website=ucjeps.berkeley.edu|access-date=2020-08-08}} < /ref >  Well-known plants in this family include ''[[Ziziphus]]'' and ''[[Hovenia dulcis]]''.
