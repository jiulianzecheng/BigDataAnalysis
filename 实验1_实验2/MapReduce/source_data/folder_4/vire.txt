 '''Vire''' is a former [[Communes of France|commune]] in [[Lower Normandy]] in the [[Calvados (department)|Calvados]] [[Departments of France|department]] in northwestern [[France]]. On 1 January 2016, it became a part of the new commune of [[Vire-Normandie]]. < ref > [https://www.legifrance.gouv.fr/eli/arrete/2015/12/31/INTB1600512A/jo/texte Arrêté préfectoral] 31 December 2015 {{in lang|fr}} < /ref >
