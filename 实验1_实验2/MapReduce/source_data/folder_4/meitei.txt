'''Meitei''' may refer to: 
 * [[Ancient Meitei language]], an archaic form of the modern Meitei language 
 * [[Meitei culture]], the culture of the Meitei ethnicity 
 * [[Meitei language]], the official language and the lingua franca of Manipur 
 * [[Meitei mythology]], the body of myths developed by the Meitei people 
 * [[Meitei people]], the predominant ethnic group in Manipur 
 * [[Meitei religion]] (Sanamahism), the ethnic religion of the Meitei people 
 * [[Meitei script]], a writing system for the Meitei language
