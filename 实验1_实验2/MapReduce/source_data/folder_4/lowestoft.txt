'''Lowestoft''' is a [[town]] in [[Suffolk]]. 
 
 [[Flint]] [[tool]]s were found at [[Pakefield]] in south Lowestoft in 2005. This showed that [[human]]s had been living in the Lowestoft area 200,000 & nbsp;years ago. This means Lowestoft is one of the earliest known sites for humans in the [[United Kingdom]]. < ref name= " cba " > {{Cite web|author=Parfitt.S|display-authors=etal|date=2006|url=http://www.britarch.ac.uk/ba/ba86/feat1.shtml|title=700,000 years old: found in Suffolk|publisher=British Archaeology|accessdate=24 December 2008|archive-date=19 July 2012|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120719132907/http://www.britarch.ac.uk/ba/ba86/feat1.shtml|url-status=dead}} < /ref >
