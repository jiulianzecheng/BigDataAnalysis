 '''Nijkerk''' ([[Dutch Low Saxon]]: ''Niekark'') is a [[List of municipalities of the Netherlands|municipality]] found in the [[Netherlands|Dutch]] [[Provinces of the Netherlands|province]] of [[Gelderland]]. In 2019, 42,943 people lived there.
