 '''Adrenomyeloneuropathy''' is a [[rare]] [[inherited disorder]].  It is a form of [[genetics|X-linked]] [[adrenoleukodystrophy]]. [[Leukodystrophy]] causes a [[disruption]] to the [[myelin]] [[sheath|covering]] that protects [[nerve]]s.
