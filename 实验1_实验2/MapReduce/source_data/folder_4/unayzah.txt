 '''Unayzah''' (Paris Najd) is one of [[Al-Qassim Region|Al-Qassim]] cities which are located in [[Saudi Arabia]]. It is an ancient historical city. The city got its importance long ago due to its unique geographical location. It is in the north central part of Najd [[plateau]] and toward the south of the stream of Arrimah valley, the largest valley in the [[Arabian Peninsula]].
