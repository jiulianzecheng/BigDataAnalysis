 The '''haddock''' (''Melanogrammus aeglefinus'') is a [[saltwater]] [[ray-finned fish]]. It is from the [[Family (biology)|family]] [[Gadidae]], the true [[Cod|cods]].  
 
 The haddock is the only [[species]] in the [[Monotypy|monotypic]] [[genus]] ''Melanogrammus''. It is found in the North [[Atlantic Ocean]] and the nearby [[List of seas|seas]]. In these areas. the haddock is an important fish for [[Fishery|fisheries]]. It is very important in [[northern Europe]]. In this area, it is sold fresh, frozen and [[Smoked fish|smoked]]. Smoked varieties of the fish include the [[Finnan haddie]] and the [[Arbroath smokie]].
