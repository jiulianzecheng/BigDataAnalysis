 '''Cremin''' was a [[municipality]] of the district of [[Broye-Vully (district)|Broye-Vully]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Vaud]] in [[Switzerland]]. On 1 January 2017, the former municipalities of [[Brenles]], [[Chesalles-sur-Moudon]], Cremin, [[Forel-sur-Lucens]] and [[Sarzens]] joined together to become the municipality of [[Lucens]]. < ref > [https://www.bfs.admin.ch/bfs/de/home/grundlagen/agvch.html Amtliches Gemeindeverzeichnis der Schweiz] {{in lang|de}} accessed 10 January 2017 < /ref >
