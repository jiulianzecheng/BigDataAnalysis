 '''Amusement''' is the reaction of having fun or seeing something entertaining. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.oed.com/view/Entry/6786#eid4700112|title=Amuse, v.|website=Oxford English Dictionary|access-date=21 November 2017}} < /ref >  The person or animal who is amused can go through [[enjoyment]], [[happiness]], [[laughter]] and [[pleasure]]. It is an emotion with a positive affect.
