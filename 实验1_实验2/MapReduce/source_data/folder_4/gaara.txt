 '''Gaara''' is a character in the [[manga]] and [[anime]] [[Naruto (comic)|Naruto]]. He is known as Gaara of the Sand in the Viz translation, and Sabaku no Gaara (Gaara of the Desert) in the original [[Japan]]ese. Initially  he was  a bad guy and Naruto's rival. After a while the two develop a bond as kindred spirits and become close friends.
