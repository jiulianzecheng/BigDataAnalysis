 '''''Heartland''''' is 1979 American western drama movie directed by first time director [[Richard Pearce]] and was based on the 1914 memoir by [[Elinore Pruitt Stewart]]. It stars [[Rip Torn]], [[Conchata Ferrell]], [[Barry Primus]], [[Lilia Skala]], [[Amy Wright]], [[Jerry Hardin]], [[Jeff Boschee]].
