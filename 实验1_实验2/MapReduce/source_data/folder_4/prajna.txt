'''Prajna''' is a [[Buddhist]] word meaning [[wisdom]].  It is one of [[Threefold training|three divisions]] of the [[Noble Eightfold Path]]. The understanding is a goal of contemplation. Buddhism traces its origins to Siddattha Gotama, who lived from about 563 to 483 BCE. He was known as the Buddha, which means  " the enlightened one " .
