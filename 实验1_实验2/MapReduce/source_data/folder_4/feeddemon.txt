'''FeedDemon''' is a free [[proprietary software]] for [[Microsoft Windows|Windows]] that reads [[RSS feeds]]. The inventor of FeedDemon is Nick Bradbury, the inventor of other [[software]]. This software is owned by NewsGator Technologies since May 2005. This software can work with [[Bloglines]] and NewsGator Online.
