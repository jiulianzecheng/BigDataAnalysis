 '''Sottens''' was a [[municipality]] in the [[Gros-de-Vaud (district)|Gros-de-Vaud]] district in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Vaud]] in [[Switzerland]]. On 1 July 2011, the municipalities of [[Villars-Tiercelin]], [[Montaubion-Chardonney]], Sottens, [[Villars-Mendraz]] and [[Peney-le-Jorat]] joined together to become the new municipality of [[Jorat-Menthue]]. < ref name=Fusion > [http://www.bfs.admin.ch/bfs/portal/de/index/infothek/nomenklaturen/blank/blank/gem_liste/03.html Amtliches Gemeindeverzeichnis der Schweiz] published by the Swiss Federal Statistical Office {{in lang|de}} accessed 17 February 2011 < /ref > 
 
 It is known for its radio transmitter for the French language Swiss radio, built in 1931.
