 '''Acanthite''' is a [[mineral]] that can be [[lead]] [[gray]], gray, or [[coal]] in [[color]] and is made from [[crystal]]s. Its name comes from the [[Greek language|Greek]] word, akanta, meaning  " arrow " , or the [[Latin]] word ''argentum'', meaning  " silver " . It is made of [[silver(I) sulfide]]. 
 
 It is found in Freiberg, Schneeberg, and Annaberg, [[Germany]].
