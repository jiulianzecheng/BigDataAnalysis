 '''Dingolshausen''' is a [[Municipalities of Germany|municipality]] in [[Schweinfurt (district)|Schweinfurt]] in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. 
 
 Placed at the foot of the [[Steigerwald]] (forest) and in the valley of a creek named the Volkach.
