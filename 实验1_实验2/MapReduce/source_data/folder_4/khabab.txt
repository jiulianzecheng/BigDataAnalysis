'''Khabab''' ({{lang-ar|خبب}}, [[Syriac language|Syriac]]: ܟܚܐܒܐܒ, {{transl|ar|''Khababb''}}) is a town in southern [[Syria]]. It is about 57 [[kilometre]]s (about 36 [[mile]]s) south of [[Damascus]] and about the same distance away from the city of [[Daraa]].
