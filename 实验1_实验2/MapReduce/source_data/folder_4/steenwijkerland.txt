 '''Steenwijkerland''' is a municipality in the province of [[Overijssel]], the Netherlands. About 44,000 people were living there in 2021. It lies in the region of [[Kop van Overijssel]]. 
 
 In 2001, the municipalities of [[Brederwiede]] and [[IJsselham]] joined the municipality of [[Steenwijk]]. Last one was renamed Steenwijkerland in 2003.
