 '''Pratovecchio''' was a ''[[comune]]'' in the [[Province of Arezzo]] in the [[Italy|Italian]] region of [[Tuscany]]. On 1 January 2014, the former municipalities of Pratovecchio and [[Stia]] merged to form the new municipality of [[Pratovecchio Stia]].
