 '''Mikulov''' ({{lang-de|Nikolsburg}}) is a town in the [[South Moravian Region]] of the [[Czech Republic]]. About 7,400 people live there. 
 
 The town is about {{convert|18|km|mi}} northwest of [[Břeclav]], on the border with [[Austria]]. It borders the Austrian municipality of [[Drasenhofen]].
