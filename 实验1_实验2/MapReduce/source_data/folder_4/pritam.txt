 '''Pritam Chakraborty''', also known as '''Pritam''', is an Indian composer, instrumentalist, guitarist, and singer. He was born in a [[Bengalis|Bengali]] Brahmin family to Anuradha Chakraborty and Prabodh Chakraborty. 
 
 He got his first break for Tere Liye (released December 2001). < ref > {{cite news|date=27 September 2002|title=Neighbours, now melody-makers – Jeet-Pritam: Two boys from Bengali strike a crescendo in Bollywood|work=The Telegraph|url=http://www.telegraphindia.com/1020927/asp/calcutta/story_1237428.asp|url-status=dead|access-date=7 March 2016|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20030906020709/http://www.telegraphindia.com/1020927/asp/calcutta/story_1237428.asp|archive-date=6 September 2003}} < /ref >  Though the music was well-received, the film missed the mark. In 2002, The Jeet-Pritam duo once again composed music for [[Yash Raj Films]]' ''[[Mere Yaar Ki Shaadi Hai]]''.  Soon after, Jeet decided to end the partnership..
