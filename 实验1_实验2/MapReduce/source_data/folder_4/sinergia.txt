 '''Sinergia''' is a [[experimental music]] band, formed in 1994 in [[Santiago, Chile]]. The band released five studio albums, one DVD, thirteen music videos, two extended plays and one demo. The Sinergia's music experiments [[alternative rock]], [[avant garde metal]], [[funk rock]], [[funk metal]], [[ska]], [[alternative metal]], [[progressive rock]], [[electronic rock]], [[nu metal]], and also [[punk rock]], [[rockabilly]] and [[folk rock]] in their music.
