 ==Architecture== 
 Rinkokaku is a wooden building which was built in 1884. It is an important cultural property. {{fact|date=December 2012}} 
 
 ==Food== 
 " Misopan " , meaning “Bread with [[Miso]] paste” originates from Yakimanju. Friand, Numata, is famous for Misopan. The buns are made from Gunma Prefecture’s high quality wheat. [[Azuki bean]] paste is not used, but rather, a spread of miso with sugar, which is then grilled until brown. 
 
 ==Sightseeing== 
 The [[Prefecture|prefectural]] office building was formed from Maebashi [[castle]]. It was renovated in 1999, and became the tallest building in Maebashi.{{fact|date=December 2012}}
