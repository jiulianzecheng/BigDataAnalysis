 '''Hayden Jerome Kramer''', better known by his [[stage name]] '''Herobust''', is an [[Americans|American]] [[electronic music]] DJ and record producer. His music style includes [[Trap music (EDM)|trap]], [[Bass music|bass]] and [[dubstep]]. His [[Extended play|EP]]  " I'm Aloud "  peaked at #22 on the ''[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]''  " Top Dance/Electronic Albums "  chart in February 2016. < ref > {{Cite web|url=https://www.billboard.com/music/herobust/chart-history/dance-electronic-albums/song/962911|title=HeRobust I'm Aloud Chart History|website=[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]|access-date=13 May 2019}} < /ref >  Herobust has been featured on ''[[Rolling Stone]]'', [[Vice (magazine)|''Vice'']], ''[[The Huffington Post]]'' and [[MTV]]. He is from [[Atlanta, Georgia]].
