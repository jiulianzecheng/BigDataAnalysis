 '''Breisach''' is a town in [[Breisgau-Hochschwarzwald]] in [[Baden-Württemberg]], [[Germany]]. It is on the [[Rhine River]]. About 16,000 people live there. 
 
 The town is about halfway between [[Freiburg im Breisgau|Freiburg]] and [[Colmar]], near [[Kaiserstuhl (Baden-Württemberg)|Kaiserstuhl]]. A bridge leads over the Rhine to [[Neuf-Brisach]], [[France]].
