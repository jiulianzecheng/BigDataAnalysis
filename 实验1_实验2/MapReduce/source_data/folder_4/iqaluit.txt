 '''Iqaluit''' (Inuktitut: ᐃᖃᓗᐃᑦ) is the [[capital city|capital]] of and largest community in [[Nunavut]] in [[Canada]]. It is the most northerly capital in Canada, and is also Canada's fastest growing community. < ref name= " wci " > {{cite web|url=http://www.city.iqaluit.nu.ca/apps/fusebox/index.php?fa=c.displayHome|title=Welcome to the City of Iqaluit|work=City of Iqaluit|accessdate=2009-11-07|archive-date=2009-11-30|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20091130231903/http://www.city.iqaluit.nu.ca/apps/fusebox/index.php?fa=c.displayHome|url-status=dead}} < /ref >   
 
 == History == 
 The name ''{{lang|iu|Iqaluit}}'' is [[Inuktitut]] word meaning  " place of many fish " . < ref name= " aih " > {{cite web|url=http://www.city.iqaluit.nu.ca/i18n/english/history.html|title=About Iqaluit: History|work=City of Iqaluit|accessdate=2009-11-07|archive-date=2011-09-27|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110927183343/http://www.city.iqaluit.nu.ca/i18n/english/history.html|url-status=dead}} < /ref >  It is built on the mouth of the [[Sylvia Grinnell River]], on [[Frobisher Bay]]. English [[explorer]] [[Martin Frobisher]] sailed into the bay in 1576 and thought he had discovered the [[Northwest Passage]]. < ref name= " aih "  / >  The settlement was called Frobisher Bay from 1955 until 1987. < ref name= " aih "  / >  The weather in there is normally cold.
