 {{Nihongo|'''''No Matter How I Look at It, It’s You Guys' Fault I’m Not Popular!'''''|私がモテないのはどう考えてもお前らが悪い!|Watashi ga Motenai no wa dō Kangaetemo Omaera ga Warui!}} commonly called {{Nihongo|'''''WataMote'''''|ワタモテ}}, is a [[manga]] [[series]] written by Nico Tanigawa. It was made into an [[anime]] series which is directed by Shin Ōnuma. 
 
 ==Plot== 
 The series follows fifteen-year-old Tomoko Kuroki during her time in high school as she tries to change from an [[Social isolation|unsociable]] loner into a popular teenager. 
 
 ==Anime== 
 The anime started [[broadcast]]ing in [[Japan]] on July 8, 2013 and ended on September 23, 2013. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.animenewsnetwork.com/news/2013-06-05/watamote-anime-1st-promo-with-izumi-kitta-song-streamed|accessdate=2013-09-23|title=WataMote Anime's 1st Promo With Izumi Kitta's Song Streamed|publisher=Anime News Network}} < /ref >  The anime was [[movie director|directed]] by Shin Ōnuma and is written by Takao Yoshioka. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.animenewsnetwork.com/interest/2013-06-06/yuichi-nakamura-kana-hanazawa-join-watamote-anime-cast|accessdate=2013-09-23|title=Yūichi Nakamura, Kana Hanazawa Join WataMote Anime's Cast|publisher=Anime News Network}} < /ref >  An [[original video animation]] episode was released with the seventh manga volume on October 22, 2014. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.animenewsnetwork.com/news/2013-09-05/watamote/no-matter-how-i-look-at-it-it-you-guys-fault-im-not-popular-manga-listed-with-anime-dvd|accessdate=2013-09-23|title=WataMote: No Matter How I Look at It, It's You Guys' Fault I'm Not Popular!
