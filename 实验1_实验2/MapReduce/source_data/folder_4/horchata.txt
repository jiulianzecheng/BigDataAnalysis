 '''Horchata''' is the name of several kinds of traditional drinks common in [[Central America]]. It is made of ground [[almond]]s, [[sesame]] seeds, [[rice]], [[barley]], or [[tigernut]]s ''(chufas)''. It has a light brown color and a sweet flavor. Usually you drink horchata cold.
