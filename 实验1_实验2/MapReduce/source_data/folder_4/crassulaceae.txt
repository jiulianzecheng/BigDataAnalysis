 The '''Crassulaceae''', or '''orpine family''', is a family of [[dicotyledon]]s. They store water in their succulent leaves. They are found worldwide, but mostly occur in the [[Northern Hemisphere]] and southern [[Africa]], typically in dry and/or cold areas where water may be scarce. The family includes about 1,400 species in 33 genera.
