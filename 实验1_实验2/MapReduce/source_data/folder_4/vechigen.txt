 '''Vechigen''' is a [[municipality]] of the [[Swiss]] [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Bern (canton)|Bern]]. In 2014, 5075 people lived there. It consists of the villages: Sinneringen, Boll, Dentenberg, Utzigen, Lindental, Berg, Littewil and Radelfingen.
