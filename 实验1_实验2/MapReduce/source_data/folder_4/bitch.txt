A '''bitch''' is a  female [[dog]].  
 
 It is also [[slang]] insult used to mean a nasty woman. It is possibly derived from the fact that ''bitch'' is used in other ways, however, such as in the phrase ''son of a bitch'' (also ''son of a gun'') or ''bitchy''. Bitch became a term to degrade and insult women as early in 15th century. It's use then surged specially in music industry in 70's.
