 '''Saxony''' ({{lang-de|Sachsen}} {{IPA-de|ˌzaksn̩ < !---- > ||De-Sachsen.ogg}}; {{lang-hsb|Sakska}}), officially the '''Free State of Saxony''' (German: {{lang|de|Freistaat Sachsen}} {{IPA-de|ˈfʁaɪ̯ʃtaːt ˈzaksn̩|}}, Upper Sorbian: {{lang|hsb|Swobodny stat Sakska}}), is one of 16 [[states of Germany]]. It is in the southeast of the country, north of [[Czech Republic]]. To the east of Saxony is [[Poland]]. The biggest city is [[Leipzig]]. The capital is called [[Dresden]]. It was founded in 1990. 
 
 == Geography == 
 Saxony is the [[border]] of [[Germany]] to the two countries [[Poland]] and [[Czech Republic]]. In the north of Saxony is [[Brandenburg]], another German state. Saxony's west border is to [[Saxony-Anhalt]], [[Thuringia]] and [[Bavaria]]. A big part of the border to the Czech Republic are the [[Ore Mountains]] in the south of Saxony.

 === Urban districts === 
 Saxony has three urban districts which do not belong to the rural districts. 
 * [[Chemnitz]] (C) 
 * [[Dresden]] (DD) 
 * [[Leipzig]] (L) 
 
 == History == 
 The first Free State of Saxony was founded in 1918, after the [[World War I|first World War]]. After the [[World War II|second World War]], in 1945, the  " country of Saxony "  was founded. Later the Country was divided into 3 [[administrative divisions of the German Democratic Republic]], and thus robbed of its sovereignty. 
 
 In 1990 the Free State of Saxony was officially re-founded as a part of [[Germany]].
