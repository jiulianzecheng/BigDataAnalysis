 '''Gabriele Susanne Kerner''' (born 24 March 1960), better known by her stage name '''Nena''', is a [[Germans|German]] [[singer]] and [[actress]]. She is best known for the [[Neue Deutsche Welle|New German Wave]] song  " [[99 Luftballons]] " . In 1984, she re-recorded this song in [[English language|English]] as  " 99 Red Balloons " . < ref > {{cite web |url=http://www.pandora.com/#!/music/artist/nena?bio |title=Nena (Biography on Pandora)|accessdate=2011-11-13}} < /ref >
