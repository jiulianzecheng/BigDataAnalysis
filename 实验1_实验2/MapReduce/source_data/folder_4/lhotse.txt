 '''Lhotse''' is the [[List of highest mountains|fourth tallest mountain]] of the world. It's [[height]] is {{convert|8516|m|ft|0}} above [[sea level]]. The [[summit]] of this mountain is in the [[border]] of [[Tibet]] and [[Nepal]]. < ref > [https://www.britannica.com/place/Lhotse Details about Lothse] < /ref >
