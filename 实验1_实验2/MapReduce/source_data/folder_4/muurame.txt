 '''Muurame''' is a municipality in [[Central Finland]]. As of January 2014, about 9,500 people lived there. The only municipality it shares a border with is [[Jyväskylä]]. [[Toivakka]] is another nearby municipality but there is water in between. 
 
 Most people in Muurame speak [[Finnish language|Finnish]]. 
 
 Muurame was established in 1921.
