 '''Feucht''' is a market town and municipality southeast of [[Nuremberg]] in [[Nürnberger Land]] in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. The name Feucht ({{IPA-de|fɔʏçt}}) is comes from the Old High German noun  " viuhtje "  -  " fichta " , which is the spruce tree (informally Féichdn) < !--{{IPA|fəɪçdn}}-- > .
