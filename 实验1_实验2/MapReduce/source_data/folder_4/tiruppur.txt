Tiruppur is a city in the [[Kongu Nadu]] region of the [[India]]n state of [[Tamil Nadu]]. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://tiruppurcorp.tn.gov.in/index.html|title=About Tirupur Corporation|access-date=15 December 2015|archive-date=22 February 2009|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20090222182132/http://tiruppurcorp.tn.gov.in/index.html|url-status=dead}} < /ref >  Tiruppur is the administrative headquarters of [[Tiruppur district]] and the fifth largest urban settlement in [[Tamil Nadu]].Located on the banks of [[Noyyal River]]. Tiruppur is administered by municipal corporation which was established in 2008 and the total area of the corporation is 159.6 & nbsp;km < sup > 2 < /sup >  divided into 60 wards. The total population of the city as per the 2011 census is 444,352. 
 ==Economy==
