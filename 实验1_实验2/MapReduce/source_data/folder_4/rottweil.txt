 '''Rottweil''' ({{IPA-de|ˈʁɔtvaɪl|lang|Rottweil.ogg}}) is the oldest [[town]] in the [[States of Germany|German state]] of [[Baden-Württemberg]]. < ref > {{cite web | url=https://www.aktiv-online.de/ratgeber/rottweil-warum-die-aelteste-stadt-in-baden-wuerttemberg-einen-besuch-wert-ist-16487 | title=Rottweil: Warum die älteste Stadt in Baden-Württemberg einen Besuch wert ist }} < /ref >  It has almost 25,000 inhabitants as of 2015. 
 
 The [[Rottweiler]] dog breed is named after this town. 
 
 In 2015, the company [[ThyssenKrupp]] started to build a 807-foot (246 & nbsp;m) tower for $45 million in Rottweil. < ref > {{Cite news |first=Eliot |last=Brown |date=September 15, 2015 |title = Elevators Elevate German City's Image |url = https://www.wsj.com/articles/elevators-elevate-german-citys-image-1442324191|newspaper = [[The Wall Street Journal]] |access-date=2 February 2017|issn = 0099-9660}} < /ref >  The tower was completed in 2017. It was the tallest elevator test tower in the world when it was finished. < ref > {{Cite web|last=Dillon|first=Conor|date=2015-06-26|title=World's tallest elevator tower rises in Rottweil, Germany {{!}} DW {{!}} 26.06.2015|url=https://www.dw.com/en/worlds-tallest-elevator-tower-rises-in-rottweil-germany/a-18527286|access-date=2021-12-13|website=[[Deutsche Welle]]|language=en}} < /ref >
