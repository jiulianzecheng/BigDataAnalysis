 '''Bruchsal''' is a city at the western edge of the [[Kraichgau]] in the [[Germany|German]] [[States of Germany|state]] of [[Baden-Württemberg]] which makes it the largest city in the district of [[Karlsruhe (district)|Karlsruhe]]. As of 31 December 2018, it has 44,644 people lived there and is about 20 & nbsp;km northeast of [[Karlsruhe]]. It can be found on [[Bertha Benz Memorial Route]]. 
 
 Bruchsal is known for being Europe's largest [[asparagus]] producer and one of the economic centers of the region of [[Karlsruhe Government Region|Karlsruhe]].
