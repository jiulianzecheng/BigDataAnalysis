 A '''trapeze''' is a short horizontal bar hung by ropes or metal straps from a support. It is an [[Acrobatics|aerial]] apparatus commonly found in [[circus]] performances. Trapeze acts may be static, spinning, swinging or flying, and may be performed solo, double, triple or as a group act.
