'''Venera''' is the Russian name for the planet [[Venus]]. It is also the name of the [[USSR]] program for sending [[spacecraft]] to the planet Venus. A [[Venera 3]] spacecraft was the first manmade object to land on another planet in our solar system.  Venera 3 crash landed on Venus on 1 March 1966. < ref name= " NASA2 " > {{cite web|publisher=NASA|accessdate=July 21, 2020|url=https://nssdc.gsfc.nasa.gov/nmc/spacecraft/display.action?id=1965-092A|title=Venera 3}} < /ref >   Venera 7 also landed on Venus.  It landed in 1970.  It was the first spacecraft to send information back to Earth after landing on another planet. < ref name= " NASA1 " > {{cite web|publisher=NASA|accessdate=July 21, 2020|url= https://nssdc.gsfc.nasa.gov/nmc/spacecraft/display.action?id=1970-060A|title=Venera 7}} < /ref >
