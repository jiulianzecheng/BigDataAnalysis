 '''Chvrches''' (pronounced  " churches " ) are a Scottish [[synthpop]] band that started in [[Glasgow]] in 2011. 
 
 They released their first album, ''[[The Bones of What You Believe]]'' on 20 September 2013. It got very good reviews. It was successful around the world. It reached No.9 in the UK Albums Chart < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.officialcharts.com/archive-chart/_/3/2013-10-05/|title=Official Singles Chart Top 100 - Official Charts Company|website=www.officialcharts.com}} < /ref >  and 12 in the US ''[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]'' 200. In December 2013 NME magazine put ''The Bones of What You Believe'' at number 23 on their list of Best Albums Of 2013. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.nme.com/photos/nme-s-50-best-albums-of-2013/326689/1/1?recache=1 & t=1231316#28|title=International Velvet - NME|website=NME|date=26 November 2013 }} < /ref >   
 
 In 2015 they released their second album ''[[Every Open Eye]].'' In 2018 they released their third album [[Love Is Dead (Chvrches album)|''Love Is Dead'']]. On 27 August 2021 they released their fourth album, ''[[Screen Violence]]''. 
 
 In 2014-present they do concerts in [[Europe]]. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.last.fm/music/CHVRCHES/+events|title=CHVRCHES tours, tickets, shows - Last.fm|website=Last.fm}} < /ref >
