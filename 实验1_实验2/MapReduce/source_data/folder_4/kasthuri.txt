'''''Kasthuri''''' is an [[India|Indian]] [[Telugu language]] [[soap opera]] airing on Star Maa.  
 
 The plot revolves around the life of a female doctor. She is happily serving the needy and another doctor falls in love with her. The story is about the romantic life of the couple after marriage and the challenges they face in life together. 
 
 It stars Nagarjun Ballappa and [[Aishwarya Pisse]] in lead roles.
