 '''Tsada''' is a Village in Cyprus in the Paphos District. [[Paphos]] is 8 kilometres south of Tsada.  
 
 == Climate == 
 The [[Climate]] in '''Tsada''' is classified as Csa  " Meditteranean "  and has an average annual temperature of 16.7[[Celsius|° C]] . < ref > {{Cite web|url=https://en.climate-data.org/asia/cyprus/tsada/tsada-101223/?amp=true|title=Tsada climate: Average Temperature, weather by month, Tsada weather averages - Climate-Data.org|website=en.climate-data.org|access-date=2019-10-03}} < /ref > 
 
 == Topography == 
 '''Tsada''' is 8 kilometres [[north]] of [[Paphos]] and lies at an [[altitude]] of 600 metres above sea level. < ref > {{Cite web|url=http://www.fallingrain.com/world/CY/06/Tsada.html|title=Maps, Weather, and Airports for Tsada, Cyprus|website=www.fallingrain.com|access-date=2019-10-04}} < /ref > 
 
 == Nearby villages == 
 Tsada is connected on the northeast by the villages [[Kallepia]], (3.5 & nbsp;km) [[Letimbou]], (6 & nbsp;km) and [[Polemi]] (8 & nbsp;km). In the south it is connected with the village of [[Armou]] (6 & nbsp;km).  In the southwest it is connected with [[Tala]] and the monastery of [[Agios Neophytos]].
