 '''Scrapie''' is a  fatal [[disease]] that affects the [[nervous system]] of [[sheep]] and [[goat]]s. It is similar to [[Mad cow disease]] which affects [[cattle]]. Scrapie was first described in 1732. Much like with mad cow disease, [[prion]]s seem to be involved in transmitting it.
