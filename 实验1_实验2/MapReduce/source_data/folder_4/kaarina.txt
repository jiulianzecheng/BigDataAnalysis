 '''Kaarina''' is a [[city]] in [[Southwest Finland]]. As of January 2014 about 31,800 people lived there. Neighbouring municipalities are [[Lieto]], [[Parainen]], [[Paimio]], [[Sauvo]] and [[Turku]]. Most people in Kaarina speak [[Finnish language|Finnish]].
