 '''Payerne''' is a [[municipality]] of the [[Switzerland|Swiss]] [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Vaud]]. It was the capital of the district of [[Payerne (district)|Payerne]], and is now part of the district of [[Broye-Vully (district)|Broye-Vully]].
