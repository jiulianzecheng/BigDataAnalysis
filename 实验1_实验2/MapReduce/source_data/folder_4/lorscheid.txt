 '''Lorscheid''' is a village and a part of the [[Ruwer (municipality)]] near [[Trier]] in [[Rhineland-Palatinate]], [[Germany]]. 
 
 Lorscheid is on an old [[Roman roads|Roman road]]. Many [[archaeology|archaeological]] [[Human settlement|settlements]] are nearby. The village is first mentioned in the [[inventory]] of the [[St. Maximin's Abbey, Trier|Abbey of St. Maximin]] in Trier for the year 1211.
