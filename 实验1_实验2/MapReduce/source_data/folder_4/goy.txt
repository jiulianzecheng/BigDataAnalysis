Goy is a word used in the Jewish Bible for “Nation”. In the Roman times, it also began to mean “non-Jew”.  
 It is used for referring to non-[[Jew]]ish groups of people. If it is used for multiple groups or nations, the plural word '''Goyim''' is used instead.
