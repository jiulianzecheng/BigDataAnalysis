 '''''Freguesia''''', translated as  " [[Parish (administrative division)|parish]] " , is the third-level [[Administrative division|administrative subdivision]] of [[Portugal]]. < ref name= " Cons05 " > {{cite web|title=7th Constitutional Revision|url=http://app.parlamento.pt/site_antigo/ingles/cons_leg/Constitution_VII_revisao_definitive.pdf|url-status=dead|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20141022091934/http://app.parlamento.pt/site_antigo/ingles/cons_leg/Constitution_VII_revisao_definitive.pdf|archive-date=22 October 2014|access-date=13 August 2014|publisher=[[Assembly of the Republic (Portugal)]]}} < /ref >  A ''freguesia'' is a subdivision of a ''[[Municipalities of Portugal|município]]'' (municipality). Each parish use the name of a landmark in the area or of the [[patron saint]] from [[Catholic]] parish (''paróquia'' in Portuguese).
