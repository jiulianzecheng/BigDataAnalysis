 The '''Burig''' (or '''Purik'''), are a group of [[Tibetan Muslim]]s, who live [[south]] of the [[Balti]] in [[Ladakh]]. Most of them live in Ladakh and [[Baltistan]], mostly in [[Kargil]], although many live in [[Leh]].
