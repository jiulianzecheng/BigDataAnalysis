 '''Craugastoridae''' is a [[Family (biology)|family]] of [[frog]]s.  There are 2 [[genus|genera]]: ''[[Craugastor]]'' and ''[[Haddadus]]''.  
 
 The genus ''Craugastor'' has 112 species.  They can be found in the southwestern [[United States]], [[Mexico]], [[Central America]] up to northwestern [[South America]].
