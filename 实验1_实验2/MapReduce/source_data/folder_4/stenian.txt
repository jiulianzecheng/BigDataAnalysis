The '''Stenian''' is the last [[geology|geological]] [[Period (geology)|period]] in the [[Mesoproterozoic]]. It began 1.2 [[billion]] years ago, and ended at 1 billion years ago. Before it was the [[Ectasian]]. It is followed by the [[Tonian]]. 
 
 The [[supercontinent]] [[Rodinia]] began forming in this period. 
 [[Fossils]] of the first known sexually-reproducing [[eukaryotes]] are found at the end of the previous period in the [[Hunting Formation]] on [[Somerset Island]], [[Nunavut]], [[Canada]].
