 '''Morbihan''' ({{IPA-fr|mɔʁbi.ɑ̃}}; {{lang-br|Mor-Bihan}}, {{IPA-br|morˈbiˑãn}}) is a [[departments of France|department]] found in the west of [[France]] in the [[Brittany]] [[region]]. Its [[prefecture]] is [[Vannes]] and its population is about 677,000 ([[2006]] estimation). It is one of the 83 first French departments made during the [[French revolution]]. 
 
 Morbihan has also two subprefectures: [[Lorient]] and [[Pontivy]].
