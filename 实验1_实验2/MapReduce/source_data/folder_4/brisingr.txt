 '''''The Seven Promises of Eragon Shadeslayer and Saphira Bjartskular''''', better known as '''''Brisingr''''', is the third book of the ''[[Inheritance Cycle]]'' by [[Christopher Paolini]]. It comes after the books ''[[Eragon]]'' and ''[[Eldest]]''. It was released on September 20, 2008. The title means  " [[fire]] "  in the fictional Ancient Language of Alagaesia. 
 
 The book sold 550,000 copies on its first day of sale, the most ever for a Random House Children's Book. < ref name= " pw0908 " > Diane Roback (September 23, 2008). [http://www.publishersweekly.com/article/CA6598359.html?desc=topstory 'Brisingr' Breaks Random House Children's Record]. ''Publisher's Weekly''. Accessed 2008-09-23. < /ref >
