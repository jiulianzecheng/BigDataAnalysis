A '''colloquialism''' is a word that is not used in formal [[speech]], [[writing]] or [[paralinguistics]]. Colloquialisms are also sometimes referred to collectively as  " colloquial language " . < ref > colloquialism. (n.d.). Dictionary.com Unabridged (v 1.1).
