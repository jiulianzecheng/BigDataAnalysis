'''Objectivism''' may mean: 
 
 *[[Objectivity (philosophy)]]: A philosophical opinion or doctrine. It holds that [[reality]] exists outside (independent of) the human [[mind]]. 
 
 *[[Moral objectivism]]: that some acts are right or wrong in themselves, and are not matters of opinion. 
 
 *[[Objectivism (Ayn Rand)]]. The objectivist philosophy created by novelist [[Ayn Rand]], which endorses productive achievement and logical reasoning. 
 
 *The [[Objectivism (poetry)|Objectivist poets]], a group of [[Modernism|Modernist]] writers who emerged in the 1930s.
