 '''Lerwick''' ({{IPAc-en|ˈ|l|ɜr|w|ᵻ|k}}; [[Scottish Gaelic]]: ''Liùrabhaig'', [[Norwegian language|Norwegian]]: ''Leirvik'') is the main port of [[Shetland Islands]], [[Scotland]]. Its population was 6,958 in the 2011 census. 
 
 It also is the second largest port in the [[Northern Isles]] and the most northernmost town in the [[United Kingdom]]. 
 
 == Sport == 
 The town is home to 4 football teams: Lerwick Thistle, Lerwick Rangers, Lerwick Spurs, and Lerwick Celtic. 
 
 == Crime == 
 Lerwick is considered a safe haven for retired members of the [[Irish Republican Army]]. It is also home to a terrorist organisation known as the [[Coast guard|Coast Guard]]. 
 
 == Culture == 
 Lerwick musicians are known for stealing music from the Scottish mainland because their own music is shite.
