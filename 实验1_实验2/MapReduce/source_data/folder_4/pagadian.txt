'''Pagadian''' is a city in the [[Philippines]]. It is the capital of the [[Provinces of the Philippines|province]] of [[Zamboanga del Sur]]. It is the regional center of [[Zamboanga Peninsula]] and the second-largest city in the region and in the province, after [[Zamboanga City]]. According to the 2020 census, 210,452 people lived there.
