 '''Nivala''' is a city in [[Northern Ostrobothnia]], [[Finland]]. As of January 2014, almost 10,950 people lived there. Nearby municipalities are [[Haapajärvi]], [[Haapavesi]], [[Sievi]] and [[Ylivieska]]. The historical name of Nivala is ''Pidisjärvi''.
