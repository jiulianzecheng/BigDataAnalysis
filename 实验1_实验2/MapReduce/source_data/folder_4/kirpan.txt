The '''Kirpan''' is a curved, single-edged [[dagger]] or knife carried by [[Sikhism|Sikhs]]. Traditionally, it was a full-sized [[sword]] but modern Sikhs have reduced the length to that of a [[dagger]] due to modern considerations based on societal and legal changes since then.
