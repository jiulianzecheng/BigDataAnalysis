 '''Vianen''' is a former municipality in the [[Netherlands|Dutch]] province of [[Utrecht (province)|Utrecht]], and of [[South Holland]]. The municipality was merged with the former municipalities of [[Leerdam]] and [[Zederik]] on 1 January 2019. The name of the new municipality is  [[Vijfheerenlanden]], which is a part of the province of Utrecht.
