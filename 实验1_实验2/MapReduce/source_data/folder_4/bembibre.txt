 '''Bembibre''' is a [[municipality]] in the [[province]] of [[Province of León|León]] ([[Spain]]). 
 
 == Language == 
 [[Spanish Language]] is the main language spoke in this municipality, in rural zones the [[Leonese Language]] might be spoken as well.
