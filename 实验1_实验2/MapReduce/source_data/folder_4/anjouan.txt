 '''Anjouan''' is an island in the [[Indian Ocean]] off the coast of [[Africa]]. It is part of the [[Comoros]] nation. Its chief town is [[Mutsamudu]]. 
 
 It has also been known by the names: '''Ndzuwani''' or '''Nzwani''', and historically as '''Johanna''' < ref > {{Cite journal|last=Stevens|first=Kenneth|date=Winter 1986|title=Of Whaling Ships and Kings: The Johanna Bombardment of 1851|url=|journal=Prologue|volume=18|pages=241–249|via=}} < /ref >  or '''Hinzuan'''.
