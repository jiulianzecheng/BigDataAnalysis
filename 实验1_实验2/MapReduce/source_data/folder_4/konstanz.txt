 '''Konstanz''' ([[English language|English]] often '''Constance''') is a [[university]] town with a [[population]] of around 80,000. It is on the shore of [[Lake Constance]], in the south-west corner of [[Germany]], near [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 The history of the city goes back to [[Ancient Rome]]. In the [[15th century]], the [[Council of Constance]] was held there and ended the [[Western Schism]]. [[Jan Hus]] was burned as a [[heretic]] at the council.
