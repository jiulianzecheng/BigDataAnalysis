 '''''Powerslave''''' is a [[studio album]] by [[Iron Maiden]]. It went on sale on 3 September 1984. < ref name= " maiden " > {{cite web|url=http://www.ironmaiden.com/index.php?categoryid=22 & p2_articleid=307|title=Powerslave|publisher=Iron Maiden official website|accessdate=1 February 2011|archive-date=19 August 2010|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20100819110555/http://www.ironmaiden.com/index.php?categoryid=22 & p2_articleid=307|url-status=dead}} < /ref >  It was the second most popular album in England when it started being sold. < ref name= " maiden " / > 
 
 The album got good reviews; one person said it was  " classic and essential. " < ref name= " allmusic " > {{cite web|url=http://www.allmusic.com/album/powerslave-r9913/review|title=Review|publisher=Allmusic|accessdate=1 February 2011}} < /ref > 
 
 One of the songs,  " [[Rime of the Ancient Mariner]], "  was [[wikt:adapted|adapted]] from a poem of the same name by [[Samuel Taylor Coleridge]]. < ref name= " allmusic " / >  It is Iron Maiden's longest song (almost 14 minutes).
