 '''Niardo''' ({{lang-lmo|Gnàrt}}) is a ''[[comune]]'' in the [[Province of Brescia]] in the [[Lombardy]] region in [[Italy]]. 
 
 It was the birthplace of [[Saint Obitius]] (Obizio), who is one of the comune's [[patron saint]]s.
