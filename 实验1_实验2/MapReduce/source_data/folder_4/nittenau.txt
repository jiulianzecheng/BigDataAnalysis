 '''Nittenau''' ({{IPA-de|ˈnɪ.tə.naʊ̯|lang|De-Nittenau.ogg}}) is a [[Municipalities of Germany|municipality]]  in [[Schwandorf (district)|Schwandorf]], in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. It is on the river [[Regen River|Regen]], 18 & nbsp;km southeast of [[Schwandorf]], and 24 & nbsp;km northeast of [[Regensburg]]. 
 
 It is the  " sister city "  of [[Lake Zurich, Illinois]].
