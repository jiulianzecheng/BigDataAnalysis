 '''Llanymynech''' is a village in [[Shropshire]], [[England]] and [[Powys]], [[Wales]]. It is in the [[civil parish]] of [[Llanymynech and Pant]], next to the [[River Vyrnwy]]. 
 
 It is located 17.4 miles north-west of [[Shrewsbury]], Shropshire's [[county town]] and 9 miles (14 km) north of the Welsh town of [[Welshpool]]. It straddles the Welsh border. Some parts of the village, such as the [[golf]] course, are located in Wales. Two of the village's three [[public house|pub]]s are entirely in [[England]] and the third is entirely in [[Wales]]. 
 
 The village's name is  " Church of the Monks "  in [[Welsh language|Welsh]]. 
 
 Shortly to the north of the village is the village of [[Pant, Shropshire|Pant]]. Further north is the market town of [[Oswestry]]. 
 
 The village has an amateur [[association football|football]] club, [[Llanymynech FC]].
