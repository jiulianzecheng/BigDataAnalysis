 '''Gard''' is a [[France|French]] [[Departments of France|department]] in southern [[France]] in the [[Occitanie]] [[Regions of France|region]] and named after the [[Gardon]] [[river]]. Its [[Prefectures in France|prefecture]] is [[Nîmes]]. 
 
 ==History== 
 Gard is one of the 83 French departments made during the [[French revolution]], on 4 March 1790. It was divided in 8 [[district]]s: Alais, Beaucaire, Nîmes, Pont-Saint-Esprit, Saint-Hippolyte, Sommières, Uzès and Le Vigan. The [[Capital city|capital]] was Nîmes alternating with Alais and Uzès. In 1794, Nîmes became the only capital. < ref name=splaf > {{cite web |url=http://splaf.free.fr/30his.html |title=Historique du Gard |work=Le SPLAF |language=French |accessdate=9 January 2017 |date= }} < /ref > 
 
 With the creation of the ''arrondissements'', the 8 districts became four ''arrondissements'': Nîmes, Alais, Uzès and Le Vigan. In 1926, the ''arrondissement'' of Uzès was eliminated. < ref name=splaf / > 
 
 ==Geography== 
 Gard is part of the [[Occitanie]] [[Regions of France|region]], in southern France, with an area of {{Convert|5852.8|km2|sqmi|0|abbr=on }}. < ref name= " insee-geo "  / > 
 
 The department borders with 6 other departments in 3 regions: 
 *  [[Provence-Alpes-Côte d'Azur]] 
 ** [[Bouches-du-Rhône]] (south) 
 ** [[Vaucluse]] (east) 
 * [[Auvergne-Rhône-Alpes]] 
 ** [[Ardèche]] (north) 
 * [[Occitanie]] 
 ** [[Hérault]] (south) 
 ** [[Aveyron]] (west) 
 ** [[Lozère]] (west) 
 
 The department has a {{Convert|23|km|mi|abbr=on }} long [[coast]] along the [[Mediterranean Sea]]. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://www.gard.fr/le-gard/le-territoire/fiche-didentite.html |title=Fiche d'identité |work=Le Gard départment |language=French |accessdate=9 January 2017 |date= |archive-date=15 September 2016 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160915195514/http://www.gard.fr/le-gard/le-territoire/fiche-didentite.html |url-status=dead }} < /ref >

 The highest point in the department is ''[[Mont Aigoual]]'', ({{Coord|44|7|17|N|03|34|53|E|name=Mont Aigoual |display=inline |region:ID_type:mountain }}) that is {{Convert|1565|m|ft|0|abbr=on}} high < ref name=pbag > ;{{cite web |url=http://www.peakbagger.com/peak.aspx?pid=9710 |title=Mont Aigoual, France |publisher=Peakbagger.com |language= |accessdate=10 January 2017 |date= }} < /ref >  it is in the ''Cévennes'' [[mountain]]s, on the border with the [[Lozère]] department. 
 
 The main river of Gard is the [[Gardon]] (or ''Gard''), a right [[tributary]] of the Rhone. The Rhone flows through the eastern border of the department. 
 
 ===Climate=== 
 The climate of the Gard department, in the city of [[Nîmes]], is subtropical with dry-summer, '''Csa''' (Mediterranean Climate) in the [[Köppen climate classification]]. 
 
 The [[average]] temperature for the year in Nîmes, at an [[altitude]] of {{Convert|60|m|ft|abbr=on }},  is {{Convert|14.4|°C|°F }}. The warmest month, on average, is July with an average temperature of {{Convert|23.3|°C|°F }}. The coolest month on average is January, with an average temperature of {{Convert|6.1|°C|°F }}. 
 
 The average amount of precipitation for the year in Nîmes is {{Convert|690.9|mm|in|1|abbr=on }}. The month with the most precipitation on average is October with {{Convert|106.7|mm|in|1|abbr=on }} of precipitation. The month with the least precipitation on average is July with an average of {{Convert|25.4|mm|in|1|abbr=on }}. < ref name= " wbase " > {{cite web|work= |publisher=Weatherbase |title=Nimes, France - Köppen Climate Classification |url=http://www.weatherbase.com/weather/weather-summary.php3?s=54670 & cityname=Nimes%2C+Languedoc-Roussillon%2C+France & units= |language= |accessdate=10 January 2017}} < /ref > 
 
 ==Administration== 
 Gard is part of the region of [[Occitanie]]. The department is managed by the ''Departamental Council of the Gard'' in [[Nîmes]].

 ==Demographics== 
 The inhabitants of Gard are known, in [[French language|French]], as ''Gardois'' (women: ''Gardoises''). < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.habitants.fr/gard-30 |title=Gard |publisher=habitants.fr |language=French |accessdate=10 January 2017 |date= }} < /ref > 
 
 Gard has a population, in 2014, of 736,029, < ref name=insee / >  for a population density of {{Pop density|736029|5852.8|km2|prec=1|disp=num}} inhabitants/km < sup > 2 < /sup > . The ''arrondissement'' of Nîmes, with 547,598 inhabitants, is by far the largest. The other two, Alès and Le Vigan, have respectively 151,823 and 36,608 inhabitants. < ref name=insee2 / > 
 
 The city with more people living in it is the [[Capital city|capital]], [[Nîmes]] (151,075). The subprefectures of [[Alès]] and [[Le Vigan, Gard|Le Vigan]] have, respectively, 39,993 and 3,933 inhabitants. < ref name=insee2 / >
