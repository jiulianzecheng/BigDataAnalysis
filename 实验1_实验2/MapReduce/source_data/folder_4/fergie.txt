 '''Fergie Duhamel'''{{Efn|{{IPAc-en|ˌ|d|ə|ˈ|m|ɛ|l}}, pronounced {{respell|də|MEL|'}}}} (born '''Stacy Ann Ferguson'''; March 27, 1975) < ref > {{cite web |url=https://www.imdb.com/name/nm0004914/bio |title=Stacy Ferguson - Biography |access-date=2009-10-23 |publisher=Internet Movie Database }} < /ref >  is an [[Americans|American]] singer, songwriter, fashion designer, television host, and former dancer. She is a former member of the kids' [[Television program|TV series]] ''[[Kids Incorporated]]'', and the girl group [[Wild Orchid (band)|Wild Orchid]]. Ferguson was also a co-host of the TV series ''[[Great Pretenders]]''. She was also in the [[movie]] ''[[Nine (movie)|Nine]].'' She was a vocalist for the [[Hip hop music|hip hop]] group [[The Black Eyed Peas]] and is a [[solo (music)|solo]] artist. She released her debut solo album, ''[[The Dutchess]]'', in 2006. Her second solo album, ''Double Dutchess'', was released in 2017 through her own label, Dutchess. 
 
 She has achieved three number-one singles on the ''Billboard'' Hot 100:  " [[London Bridge (Fergie song)|London Bridge]] " ,  " [[Glamorous (song)|Glamorous]] " , and  " [[Big Girls Don't Cry]] " .
