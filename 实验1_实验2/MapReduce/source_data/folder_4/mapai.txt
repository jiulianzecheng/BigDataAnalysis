'''Mapai''', Hebrew: מפא " י , is a former [[Israel]]i political party. Mapai is an [[acronym]] for Mifleget Poalai Eretz Israel which is [[Hebrew]] for [[political party|party]] of the [[proletariat]] of the [[land]] of [[Israel]].   
 
 Its ideology was [[social democratic]] and [[Zionist]]. 
 
 Founders were [[David Ben-Gurion]] and [[Aaron David Gordon]]. 
 
 Mapai was established in the [[British Mandate of Palestine]] in 1930 and merged into [[HaAvoda]] in 1968.
