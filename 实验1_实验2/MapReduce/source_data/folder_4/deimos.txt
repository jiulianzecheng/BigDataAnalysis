 '''Deimos''' ({{IPAc-en|ˈ|d|aɪ|ˌ|m|ɒ|s}}; {{lang-el|Δεῖμος}} -  " dread "  or  " terror " ) is the god of terror in [[Greek mythology]]. He is one of the sons of [[Ares]] and [[Aphrodite]]. He is the twin brother of [[Phobos]], god of fear. He and his brother would often go with their father as he rode into battle in his chariot. [[Deimos (moon)|Deimos]], the smaller of [[Mars (planet)|Mars]]' two moons, is named after him.
