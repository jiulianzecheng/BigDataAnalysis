 '''Barrancabermeja''' is a [[city]] in the [[Santander Department]] of [[Colombia]]. The city was founded in 1922. It is on the shore of the [[Magdalena River]]. 
 
 It is {{convert|114|km|abbr=off}} west of [[Bucaramanga]].
