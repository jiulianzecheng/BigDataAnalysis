 '''Muret''' ([[Occitan language|Occitan]] [[Gascon language|gascon]]: ''Murèth'') is a [[Communes of France|commune]] in the [[Haute-Garonne]] [[Departments of France|department]], of which it is a [[Prefecture|subprefecture]], in the [[Occitanie]] [[Regions of France|region]] of southwestern [[France]]. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 Muret is at about {{Convert|22|km|mi|abbr=on }} to the south of [[Toulouse]] and has an area of {{Convert|57.8|km2|sqmi|abbr=on }}, < ref name= " insee-geo " > {{cite web |url=https://www.insee.fr/fr/statistiques/1405599?geo=COM-31395 |title=Commune de Muret (31395) |work=Comparateur de territoire |publisher=Institut national de la statistique et des études économiques - INSEE |format= |language=French |accessdate=13 March 2017 |date= }} < /ref >  and its average [[altitude]] is {{Convert|229|m|ft|abbr=on}}; at the [[City and town halls|city hall]], the altitude is {{Convert|169|m|ft|abbr=on }}. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://www.map-france.com/Muret-31600/ |title=Muret |publisher=Map-France.com |language= |accessdate=14 September 2016 |date= }} < /ref >

 Muret is on the [[Garonne river|Garonne]] [[river]] at its [[confluence]] with the [[Louge]], which flows northeast through the commune. The Garonne flows north through the commune and forms part of its northern border. 
 
 Muret has an oceanic climate with relatively hot summers, mild winters and abundant rainfall, '''Cfb''' in the [[Köppen climate classification]]. < ref > {{cite web |work=Annuaire-Mairie.fr |publisher= |title=Ville de Muret |url=http://www.annuaire-mairie.fr/ville-muret.html |language=French  |accessdate=14 September 2016}} < /ref > 
 
 ==Population== 
 The inhabitants of Muret are known, in [[French language|French]], as ''Murétains'' (women: ''Murétaines''). < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.habitants.fr/muret/banque |title=Muret (31600) |publisher=habitants.fr |language=French |accessdate=14 September 2016 |date= }} < /ref > 
 
 The ''commune'' of Muret has a population, in 2014, of 24,975; < ref name=insee > {{cite web |url=http://www.insee.fr/fr/statistiques/fichier/2525755/dep31.pdf |title=Régions, départements, arrondissements, cantons et communes |work= Populations légales 2014 |publisher=Institut national de la statistique et des études économiques - INSEE |format=PDF |language=French |accessdate=13 March 2017|date= }} < /ref >  its [[population density]] is of {{Pop density|24975|57.8|km2|prec=1|disp=num}} inhabitants/km < sup > 2 < /sup > .

 == Administration == 
 Muret is a [[Prefectures in France|subprefecture]] of the Haute-Garonne department, the capital of the ''arrondissement'' of [[Arrondissement of Muret|Muret]] and the [[administrative centre]] ({{lang-fr|chef-lieu}}) of the canton of [[Canton of Muret|Muret]]. 
 
 It is part of the [[Communes of France|intercommunality]] ''Le Muretain'' ({{lang-fr|Communauté d'agglomération du Muretain}}).
