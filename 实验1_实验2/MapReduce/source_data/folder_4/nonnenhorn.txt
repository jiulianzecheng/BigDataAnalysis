 '''Nonnenhorn''' is one of the three [[Bavaria]]n towns on [[Lake Constance]] in the district of [[Lindau (district)|Lindau]] in [[Bavaria]] in [[Germany]]. It is an air health resort and a famous wine town. It is between [[Wasserburg am Bodensee]] and [[Kressbronn am Bodensee]] ([[Baden-Württemberg]]).
