 '''Vans''' is an [[United States|American]] [[manufacturer]] of [[Skate shoe|skateboarding shoes]]. They also make similar [[apparel]]. They are based in [[Santa Ana, California|Santa Ana]], [[California]]. They are owned by [[VF Corporation]]. The company also [[sponsor]]s [[surfing|surf]], [[snowboarding]], [[BMX]], and [[motocross]] teams. < ref > {{cite web|title=Home|url=http://www.vans.com/|work=Vans.com|publisher=Vans, A VF Company|access-date=25 August 2013|date=August 2013}} < /ref >  Since 1996, the company has been the primary sponsor of the annual Vans [[Warped Tour]] traveling [[rock festival]].
