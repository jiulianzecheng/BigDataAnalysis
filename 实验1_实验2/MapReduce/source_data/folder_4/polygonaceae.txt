 '''Polygonaceae''' is a family of [[flowering plant]]s are herbs, shrubs, or rarely trees comprising about 30 genera and 1,000 species. The leaves are simple, alternate or rarely opposite, and usually stipulate. Some well known members include [[Fagopyrum|buckwheat]] (''Fagopyrum''), [[Rumex|sorrel]] (''Rumex''), [[rhubarb]] (''Rheum''), and [[Polygonum|knotweed]] (''Polygonum'').
