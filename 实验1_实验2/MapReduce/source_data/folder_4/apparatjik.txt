'''Apparatjik''' (pronounced ''ap-er-rat-shik'') is a musical supergroup. Its members are [[Guy Berryman]] (of [[Coldplay]]), [[Jonas Bjerre]], [[Magne Furuholmen]], and [[Martin Terefe]]. 
 
 The group were formed in 2008.
