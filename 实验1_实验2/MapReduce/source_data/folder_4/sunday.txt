 '''Sunday''' is one of the seven days of the [[week]]. It is part of the [[weekend]], along with [[Saturday]]. Sunday is the [[shabbat|Sabbath]] or day of [[worship]] for most [[Christian]]s (for example, [[Easter]] is celebrated on Sunday). Sunday is the beginning of the week in the [[United States]], but is the end of the week in the [[United Kingdom]].
