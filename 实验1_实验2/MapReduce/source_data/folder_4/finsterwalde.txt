 '''Finsterwalde''' is a town in [[Elbe-Elster]] in [[Brandenburg]], [[Germany]]. In 2021, 15,748 people lived there. 
 
 " We are the singers from Finsterwalde " , a hit from the late 19th century. 
 
 == Geography == 
 Finsterwalde is in the [[Lower Lusatia]] region. It is {{convert|112|km|mi|abbr=on}} south of [[Berlin]] and {{convert|78|km|abbr=on}} north of [[Dresden]]. 
 
 == History == 
 Finsterwalde was first mentioned in 1282 as ''Vynsterwalde''.
