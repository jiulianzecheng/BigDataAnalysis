 '''Reichmannshausen''' in [[Unterfranken]] is a village in the municipality of [[Schonungen]], in [[Schweinfurt (district)|Schweinfurt]], [[Bavaria]]. 
 
 == Geography == 
 Reichmannshausen is 341 meters above sea level on the Schlettach plateau. It is completely surrounded by forest.
