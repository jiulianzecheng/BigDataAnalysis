 '''Aldeburgh''' is a [[Coast|coastal town]] in the [[England|English]] [[county]] of [[Suffolk]]. It is on the Alde river. 
 
 == Popular tourist attractions == 
 Many people take a weekend trip to Aldeburgh. One of the main attractions is Moot Hall. Other attractions include a [[Napoleonic era]] defensive [[Fortification|fort]] (known as a Martello tower), a sheltered yachting marina, and two [[Fish and chips]] shops
