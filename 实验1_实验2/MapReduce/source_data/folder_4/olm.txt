 The '''olm''' or '''proteus''' (''Proteus anguinus'') is an [[amphibian]], native to the underground waters of the Dinaric [[karst]] that flow through the Soča river basin near [[Trieste]] in [[Italy]], through to southern [[Slovenia]], southwestern [[Croatia]], and [[Herzegovina]]. It is the only species in the [[genus]] ''Proteus'', the only European species of the family [[Proteidae]], and the only [[Europe]]an cave-dwelling [[chordate]]. It is also called the human fish or humanfish, because of its human-like skin (translated literally from [[Slovenian language|Slovenian]]: ''Človeška ribica'' and [[Croatian language|Croatian]]: ''Čovječja ribica''), Cave [[Salamander]], or White Salamander.
