'''Unplanned''' is a [[movie]] about the life of [[conservative]] activist [[Abby Johnson (activist)|Abby Johnson]] based on her [[autobiography]]. It focuses on how she was once [[Pro-Choice]] but is now [[Pro-Life]]. It was controversial because it criticized [[Planned Parenthood]]. [[Left-wing]] people said it was [[propaganda]] and [[conservative]]s liked it. The movie made a lot of [[money]].
