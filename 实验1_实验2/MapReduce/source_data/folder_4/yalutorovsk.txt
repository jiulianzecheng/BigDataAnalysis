 '''Yalutorovsk''' ({{lang-ru|Ялуторовск}}) is a town in [[Tyumen Oblast]], [[Russia]]. It is about {{convert|75|km|sp=us}} southeast of [[Tyumen]] on the [[Tobol River]]. In 2010, 36,493 people lived there.
