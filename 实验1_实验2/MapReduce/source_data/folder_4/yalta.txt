 '''Yalta''' ([[Ukrainian language|Ukrainian]] and [[Russian language|Russian]]: Я́лта) is a [[resort town|resort city]] on the south coast of the [[Crimean Peninsula]] surrounded by the [[Black Sea]]. Yalta is internationally recognised as part of [[Ukraine]], and is thought to be part of the [[Autonomous Republic of Crimea]]. In 2014, 76,746 people lived there.
