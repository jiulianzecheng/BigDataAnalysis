 '''Berzona''' is a village and former municipality of the [[district]] of [[Locarno (district)|Locarno]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Ticino]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 In 2001 the municipality was merged with the other, neighboring municipalities [[Auressio]] and [[Loco, Switzerland|Loco]] to form a new and larger municipality [[Isorno]]. < ref name=Fusion > [http://www.bfs.admin.ch/bfs/portal/de/index/infothek/nomenklaturen/blank/blank/gem_liste/03.html Amtliches Gemeindeverzeichnis der Schweiz] published by the Swiss Federal Statistical Office {{in lang|de}} accessed 14 January 2010 < /ref >
