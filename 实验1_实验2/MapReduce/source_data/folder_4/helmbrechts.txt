 '''Helmbrechts''' ({{IPA-de|ˈhɛlmbʁɛçt͡s|-|De-Helmbrechts.ogg}}) is a town in [[Hof (district)|Hof]], in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. It is on the Southern edge of the [[Frankenwald]], 20 & nbsp;km southwest of [[Hof, Germany|Hof]].
