'''Imperial''' may mean: 
 
 1. The things associated with a country led by a powerful leader (an [[empire]]) or with that leader ([[Emperor]]) as a person. 
 
 2. A system of weights and measures ([[Imperial units]]) formerly used by the old [[British Empire]], and in places it controlled or affected, including [[inch]]es, [[foot (unit of length)|feet]], [[ounce]]s, [[Pound (mass)|pounds]], and other measurements which are frequently used by English-speaking people, as opposed the International System based on [[meter]]s and [[liter]]s.
