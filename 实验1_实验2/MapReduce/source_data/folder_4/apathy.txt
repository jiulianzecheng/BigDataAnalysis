 '''Apathy''' (also called '''impassivity''') is a state of indifference or suppression of concern, not being excited or having no passion to perform things. Apathy is often expressed by people with [[schizoid personality disorder]], [[Depression (mental illness)|depression]] and [[schizophrenia]].
