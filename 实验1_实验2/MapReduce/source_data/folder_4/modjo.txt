 '''Modjo''' were a [[French people|French]] [[music]]al duo. The group fromed in [[Paris]] in 1999. The two members of the group were Romain Tranchard and Yann Destagnot. Tranchart (b. 9 June 1976) was a [[producer]]. Destagnol (b. Yann Destal, 14 July 1978) was a [[singer]]. The group did very well with their 2000 [[Single (music)|single]]  " [[Lady (Hear Me Tonight)]] " . The group broke up in 2003.
