 '''Jørn Stubberud''' (born 13 April 1968) is a Norwegian [[musician]]. He is known for being the [[Bass guitar|bassist]] for the [[black metal]] band [[Mayhem (band)|Mayhem]]. When playing with the band, he uses the name '''Necrobutcher'''. He helped make Mayhem with [[Euronymous]] and [[Kjetil Manheim]]. He is the only original member still in the band. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Mayhem Songs, Albums, Reviews, Bio  &  More|url=https://www.allmusic.com/artist/mayhem-mn0000335302|access-date=2022-12-08|website=AllMusic|language=en}} < /ref >  This is because Manheim left Mayhem in 1988, and Euronymous was killed in 1993. Stubberud has also played in other bands, such as L.E.G.O. and Kvikksølvguttene. 
 
 == Mayhem == 
 Necrobutcher joined the band in 1984. He left the band in 1991 after the band's singer, [[Dead (musician)|Dead]], killed himself. He was also having problems with Euronymous, another member of the band. When Necrobutcher left Mayhem, [[Varg Vikernes]] joined the band to play bass guitar. Vikernes killed Euronymous in 1993. In 1995, Necrobutcher started the band again. He made it with [[Jan Axel Blomberg|Hellhammer]], [[Sven Erik Kristiansen|Maniac]] and [[Rune Eriksen|Blasphemer]]. As of 2022, he is still in the band. 
 
 In 2018, he made a book about the band.
