 '''Oberkreuzberg''' is a village in the municipality of [[Spiegelau]] in the [[Lower Bavaria]]n district of [[Freyung-Grafenau]] in [[Germany]]. Until 1978 it was an independent municipality . 
 [[File:Pfarrkirche Oberkreuzberg.JPG|thumb|The parish church of St. Mary Magdalene]] 
 == Place == 
 
 The parish village (''Pfarrdorf'') is at a height of 780 metres about 2 kilometres southwest of Spiegelau on the southern side of a mountain ridge up to 790 metres high with good views.
