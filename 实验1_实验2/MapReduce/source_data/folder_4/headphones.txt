 '''Headphones''' are [[electronic]] [[audio]] [[device]] that people wear over their [[ear]]s.  They let people hear sounds on a [[walkman]], [[MP3 player]], [[mobile phone]] or [[computer]].  Headphones come in many different sizes from big to small. They are also known as '''earphones''', stereophones, [[headset]]s or even 'cans'. '''Earbuds''' are inserted into the ear. < ref > {{cite web|title=In-ear Headphones|url=https://top10gears.com/types-of-headphones/|website=top10gears.com|accessdate=8 March 2021}} < /ref >  Headphones typically uses small [[Phone connector|plug]] for connection. Some use a [[Bluetooth]] connection so they don't have the cord in the way. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Wireless Headphones and a Power Source|url=https://techspectacle.com/do-headphones-require-a-power-source/|access-date=2021-03-19|language=en-US}} < /ref >   Some sources say Bluetooth headphones are riskier than wired headphones because of the radiation emitted by Bluetooth. < ref > {{Cite web|last=Ludacris|date=2021-09-27|title=Bluetooth vs. Wired Headphones Radiation|url=https://headphonesavvy.com/bluetooth-vs-wired-headphones-radiation/|access-date=2023-02-17|website=Headphone Savvy|language=en-US}} < /ref > 
 
 There is a risk of [[deafness]] if listening to headphones with a high volume for a long time.
