In [[Greek mythology]], '''Otrera''' ({{IPAc-en|oʊ|ˈ|t|r|ɪər|ə|,_|-|ˈ|t|r|iː|r|ə}}; {{lang-grc|Οτρηρη}}, ''Otrērē'') was a queen of the [[Amazons]]. She was the daughter of [[Eurus]] (the east wind). She was a lover of the god [[Ares]]. Their children were [[Hippolyta]], [[Antiope (Amazon)|Antiope]], [[Melanippe]], and [[Penthesilea]]. < ref > ''[[Bibliotheca (Pseudo-Apollodorus)|Bibliotheca]]'', [[Epitome]] of Book IV, 5. 1 < /ref > < ref > [[Hyginus]], ''Fabulae'', 30; 112 < /ref >
