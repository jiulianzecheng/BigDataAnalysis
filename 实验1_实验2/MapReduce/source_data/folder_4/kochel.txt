 '''Kochel am See''' ([[Bavarian language|Bavarian]]: ''Koche am Sää'') is a [[municipality]] and a [[town]] in [[Bad Tölz-Wolfratshausen]], a district ([[Districts of Germany|''Landkreis'']]) in [[Upper Bavaria]], on the shores of [[Kochelsee]]. The municipality consists of the districts Altjoch, Brunnenbach, Ort, Pessenbach, Pfisterberg, Walchensee and Ried. < ref > {{cite web|title=Der Ort Kochel a.
