 A '''gripper''' is something that [[wikt:grip|grips]] things or makes it easier to grip things. It may refer to: 
 
 * [[Grippers]], tools for exercising the muscles in a person's hand 
 * [[Robot end effector]], the  " hand "  of a robot 
 * [[Grip (job)]], a job in filmmaking 
 * Shoes that have [[cleat]]s, which make it easier to grip the ground
