'''Wagamama''' is an international [[restaurant]] chain serving [[East Asia]]n food. The name means 'selfish' in [[Japanese language|Japanese]]. It was founded in 1992 by Alan Yau in [[Bloomsbury]], central [[London]]. As of [[2013]] there are 133 restaurants worldwide, mostly in [[Europe]].
