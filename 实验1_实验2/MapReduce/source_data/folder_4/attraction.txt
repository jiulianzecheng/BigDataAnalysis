 In general, an '''attraction''' is when two things are drawn to one another. 
 
 * In physics, '''attraction''' may refer to [[gravity]] or to [[electromagnetism|electromagnetic]] force. 
 * In entertainment, ''attraction'' is a [[theatre|theatrical]] production. 
 * In [[metaphysics]], the ''[[Law of Attraction]]'' says that a person's attitude will attract situations and events that match the attitude.
