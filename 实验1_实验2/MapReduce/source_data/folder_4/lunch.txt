 '''Lunch''' is a [[meal]] that is usually eaten around [[noon]] or in the early [[afternoon]]. In most countries, it is the second meal of the day, after [[breakfast]] and before [[dinner]]. < ref name= " Davidson2014 " > {{cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=bIIeBQAAQBAJ & pg=PA478|title=The Oxford Companion to Food|author=Alan Davidson|date=21 August 2014|publisher=OUP Oxford|isbn=978-0-19-104072-6|page=478}} < /ref >  A lunch can be cold ([[bread]] and [[fruit]]) or hot (a full meal, like a dinner). People that go to [[Employment|work]] or [[school]] during the day, usually have lunch together with their coworkers or classmates. Some people bring lunch to work or school in a brown paper bag or a lunchbox, but you can also buy lunch at a [[restaurant]]. In some countries, lunch is the most important meal of the day.{{Citation needed|date=September 2022}}
