{{Distinguish| Mathematical constant|Physical constant}}  
 A '''constant''' is something that does not change. Constants ([[number]]s) are used in [[maths]] and [[programming language|computer programming]]. 
 
 An important fact to remember in [[calculus]] is that the [[Derivative (mathematics)|derivative]] of a constant is always [[zero]].
