 '''Hohenrain''' is a [[municipality]] of the [[district]] [[Hochdorf (district)|Hochdorf]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Lucerne (canton)|Lucerne]] in [[Switzerland]]. Since January 1, 2007, [[Lieli, Switzerland|Lieli]] became part of the municipality.
