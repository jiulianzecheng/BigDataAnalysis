 '''Rancenay''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]] in the [[Doubs]] [[Departments of France|department]]. It is in the [[Bourgogne-Franche-Comté]] [[Regions of France|region]] in eastern [[France]]. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 The commune is {{convert|5|km|mi|abbr=on}} northeast of [[Boussières]]. It is split by the river [[Doubs (river)|Doubs]].
