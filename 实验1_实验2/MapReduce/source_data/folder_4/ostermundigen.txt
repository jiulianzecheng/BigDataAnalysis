 '''Ostermundigen''' ([[Bernese German]]: ''Oschtermundige'' or ''Mundige'') is a [[municipality]] of the [[Switzerland|Swiss]] [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Bern (canton)|Bern]]. It is the capital of the [[Bern-Mittelland (administrative district)|Bern-Mittelland administrative district]]. 
 
 The municipality was made in 1983 when it and [[Ittigen]] were splitting from the municipality of [[Bolligen]]. 
 
 It is famous as the birthplace of actress, [[Ursula Andress]].
