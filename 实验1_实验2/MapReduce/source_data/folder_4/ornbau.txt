 '''Ornbau''' ({{Pronunciation|De-Ornbau.ogg}}) is a walled town in [[Ansbach (district)|Ansbach]], a district ([[Districts of Germany|''Landkreis'']]) in [[Middle Franconia]]. It is on the river [[Altmühl]], {{convert|15|km|abbr=off}} southeast of [[Ansbach]].
