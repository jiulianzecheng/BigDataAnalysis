 '''Novell Inc.''' ({{NasdaqNM|NOVL}}) is a global [[software]] [[corporation]] based in the [[United States]] specializing in enterprise operating systems such as [[SUSE Linux distributions|SUSE Linux Enterprise]] and [[Novell NetWare]]; identity, security and systems management solutions; and collaboration solutions. Together with [[WordPerfect]], Novell was instrumental in making the [[Utah Valley]] a focus for high-technology software development. Today this area has many small companies whose employees have previously worked at Novell.
