 '''Lusail''' (Arabic: لوسيل,  < small > ALA-LC: < /small >  ''Lūsayl'') is the second-largest city in Qatar. It is located on the coast, in the southern part of the municipality of Al Daayen. Lusail is located about 23 kilometres (14 mi) north of the city centre of Doha, just north of the West Bay Lagoon, on over 38 square kilometres (15 sq mi).
