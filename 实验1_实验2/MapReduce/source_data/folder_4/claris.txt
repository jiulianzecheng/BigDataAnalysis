 '''ClariS''' is a Japanese [[J-pop|pop music]] [[duo]] and [[Japanese idol|idol unit]]. The members are high-school{{update after|2015|4|1|reason=They will have graduated.}} girls Clara and Alice from [[Hokkaido]]. ClariS was originally known as  " Alice Clara " . ClariS began singing [[Cover version|covers]] of songs mostly featured in anime and songs originally sung by [[Vocaloid]]s. They posted their cover song in [[Nico Nico Douga]] [[video sharing]] website between October 2009 and June 2010. In September 2010, ClariS was signed to [[Sony Music Entertainment Japan|SME Records]]. They released their first single  " Irony "  in October 2010. That song was used as the opening theme to the 2010 anime ''[[Oreimo]]''. ClariS released six more singles between 2011 and 2013. Their debut album ''Birthday'' was released in April 2012.
