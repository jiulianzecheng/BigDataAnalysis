 '''Yvrac''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]] in the [[Regions of France|region]] [[Aquitaine]] in the [[Gironde]] [[Departments of France|department]] in southwest [[France]]. 
 
 In 2012 a large 18th century [[chateau]] near the commune was destroyed by mistake. < ref name= " bbc " > {{cite web | url=https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/world-europe-20616993 | title=Builders demolish Russian oligarch's French chateau | publisher=BBC | work=BBC News | date=December 5, 2012 | accessdate=December 6, 2012}} < /ref >  Builders were supposed to remove another small building and then begin work on restoring the chateau. The owner, a Russian businessman, said he will have the house rebuilt. < ref name= " bbc "  / >
