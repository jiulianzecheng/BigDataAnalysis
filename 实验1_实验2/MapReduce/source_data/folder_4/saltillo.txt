 '''Saltillo''' is the capital and largest city in the northeastern [[Mexico|Mexican]] [[States of Mexico|state]] of [[Coahuila]]. It is also the municipal seat of the [[Saltillo Municipality|municipality of the same name]]. In 2015, 807,537 people lived there.
