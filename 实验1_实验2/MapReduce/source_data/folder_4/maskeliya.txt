 '''Maskeliya''' is a town in the [[Central Province, Sri Lanka|Central  Province]] of [[Sri Lanka]]. A very bad flight fall happened here. It was known as the worst flight fall in Sri Lanka. < ref > {{Cite web|date=1974-12-04|title=ASN Aircraft accident Douglas DC-8-55F PH-MBH Maskeliya|url=http://aviation-safety.net/database/record.php?id=19741204-2|access-date=2011-09-08|publisher=Aviation-safety.net}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite web|date=|title=ASN list of aircraft accidents in Sri Lanka|url=http://aviation-safety.net/database/dblist.php?Country=4R|access-date=2012-08-03|publisher=Aviation-safety.net}} < /ref > 
 
 It has different mountains, waterfalls and ethnic backgrounds. Maskeliya is famous because of [[Adam's Peak]].
