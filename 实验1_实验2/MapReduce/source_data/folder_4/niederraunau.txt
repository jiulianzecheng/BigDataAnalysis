 '''Niederraunau''' is a [[quarter]] of the town [[Krumbach, Bavaria|Krumbach]] in [[Günzburg (district)]], [[Swabia (Government Region)|Swabia]], [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. 
 
 == Geography == 
 Niederraunau ([[Elevation]] 518 m) is in the valley of the little river [[Kammel]] two kilometres south of Krumbach in the natural region [[Iller-Lech Plateau|Lower Iller-Lech Gravel Plateau]]. The hills between the valleys of Kammel and the neighboring valleys of [[Günz]] and [[Mindel]] are covered with wood in parts. Niederraunau has a station at the [[Mittelschwaben Railway]] and it is at the [[Bundesstraße 16]].
