 '''Kavala''' is one of the biggest cities in [[Macedonia (Greece)|Macedonia]]. It is the capital of Kavala prefecture. It is in the northeastern part of [[Greece]], in the [[East Macedonia and Thrace]] periphery. It is on the [[Bay of Kavala]], across from the island of [[Thasos]]. According to the 2001 census, 63,293 people lived there.
