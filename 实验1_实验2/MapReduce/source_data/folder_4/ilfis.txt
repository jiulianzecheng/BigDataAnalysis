 The '''Ilfis''' (in the local dialect: [{{IPA|(d̥) ˈʏu̯fiz̥/ˈɪu̯fiz̥}}]) is a [[Switzerland|Swiss]] [[river]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|cantons]] of [[Bern (canton)|Berne]] and [[Lucerne (canton)|Lucerne]]. It runs through the [[Entlebuch (district)|Entlebuch]] and upper [[Emmental]] regions before joining the [[Emme river|Emme]] river.
