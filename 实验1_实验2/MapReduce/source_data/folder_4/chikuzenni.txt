 '''Chikuzenni''' (筑前煮 ''chikuzen-ni'') is a dish that comes from northern [[Kyushu]], [[Japan]]. It is made of [[chicken]] and [[vegetable]]s. It is often eaten when bringing in the new year in Japan.
