A '''campus''' is [[land]] where a [[college]] or [[university]] [[building]]s are placed.  Campuses can have [[library|libraries]], [[lecture|lecture halls]], and [[park]]s. Campuses can consist of both [[academic]] (''school and studying-related'') buildings and non-academic buildings. < ref > [http://www.thefreedictionary.com/campus Campus from the Free Online Dictionary] Retrieved February 1, 2010 < /ref >
