 '''Portugalete''' is a city lying to the west of [[Bilbao]]. It is in the [[Provinces of Spain|province]] of [[Biscay]] in the [[Autonomous communities of Spain|autonomous community]] of [[Basque Country (autonomous community)|Basque Country]], northern [[Spain]]. In 2021, 45,285 people lived there.
