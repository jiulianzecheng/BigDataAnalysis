A '''visor''' can be  
 
 * The bill of a [[baseball cap]] or similar hat. 
 
 * A ball cap without the crown. 
 
 * An opaque panel mounted over the [[windshield]] of an [[automobile]] or other [[vehicle]] that the [[driver]] or front seat [[passenger]] can adjust to block direct sunlight. 
 
 * '''Green eyeshade''' - a green tinted visor commonly associated with old fashioned [[accountant]]s, [[telegraph]]ers and [[copy]] [[editor]]s formerly to lessen eye strain.
