 '''''Hylomantis''''' is a genus of [[tree frog]]s.  Some scientists say they are in the family [[Hylidae]] and other scientists say they are in the family [[Phyllomedusidae]]. They are called '''rough leaf frogs''' in English.  They live in the [[Atlantic forest]] in eastern [[Brazil]]. There are two species in this genus.
