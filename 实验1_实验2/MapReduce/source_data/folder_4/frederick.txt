'''Frederick''' (also spelled '''Frederic''') is a masculine [[given name]] that means  " peaceful ruler " . < ref > {{Cite web|title=Frederick name Meaning and Origin|url=https://babys.name/detail-Frederick-Meaning-Origin-Famous-Similar-Sibling.html|access-date=2023-03-22|website=babys.name}} < /ref >  The name is the [[English language|English]] form of the [[German language|German]] name ''Friedrich''. The pronunciation of the name is [ '''fred'''-rik, -er-ik ]. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Definition of Frederick {{!}} Dictionary.com|url=https://www.dictionary.com/browse/frederick|access-date=2023-03-22|website=www.dictionary.com|language=en}} < /ref >  This name ranked among the top 100 masculine names for the [[United States]] between 1880 and 1957. After that, the name became less popular. The name was number 536 in popularity in the United States in the late 2000s. At the same time it was number 99 in [[England]]. As ''Frederic'', the name was used on a [[tropical cyclone|hurricane]] that struck the Gulf Coast of the United States in September 1979.This name was ranked #476 on the US Popular Names in 2021. < ref > {{Cite web|last=OACT|title=Popular Baby Names|url=https://www.ssa.gov/cgi-bin/popularnames.cgi|access-date=2023-03-22|website=www.ssa.gov}} < /ref >
