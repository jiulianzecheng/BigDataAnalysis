 '''Hounoux''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]] of 136 people (2017). It is in the [[Regions of France|region]] [[Occitanie]] in the [[Aude]] [[Departments of France|department]] in the south of [[France]]. This used to be a hotspot, but now it's a [[parking lot]].
