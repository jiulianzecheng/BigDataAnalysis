 The '''mantura''' ({{lang-el|μαντούρα}}) is a [[Wind instruments|wind]] [[musical instrument]]. It is originally from the [[Greece|Greek]] island of [[Crete]]. It has 4 to 6 holes for the fingers and makes sound with the help of the tongue.
