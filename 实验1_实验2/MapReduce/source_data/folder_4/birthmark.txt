 A '''birthmark''' is a mark on [[skin]] that some people are born with. nevus|congenital melanocytic nevi, and port-wine stains. 
 
 A birthmark was once thought of as the mark of the devil, and today it is often considered a birth defect. In some cases this mark is considered to be the mark of God applied to his chosen few
