 '''Kmart''' (sometimes stylized as '''K-Mart''') was an American company. Its [[headquarters]] were in [[Illinois]]. The company purchased [[Sears]] in 2005. It formed a new [[corporation]] under the name [[Sears Holdings Corporation]]. 
 
 The company was founded in 1962. Was once the third largest [[discount store]] in the world, behind [[Walmart]] and [[Target Corporation|Target]]. < ref > {{Cite news| url = https://www.forbes.com/sites/anthonydemarco/2011/07/18/americas-top-retailers/|title= America's Top Retailers|author= Anthony DeMarco|publisher= [[Forbes]] |date = July 8, 2011}} < /ref >  Kmart has stores in the [[United States]], [[Puerto Rico]], the [[U.S. Virgin Islands]], and [[Guam]]. There were stores in [[Canada]], [[Mexico]], and [[Eastern Europe]].  
 
 Kmart is known for its  " Blue Light Specials " . The [[phrase]]  " attention Kmart shoppers "  has been used in [[pop culture]]. Many movies were filmed at Kmart stores. These includes ''[[Troop Beverly Hills]]'', ''[[Six Days Seven Nights]]'', ''[[Rain Man]]'', ''[[Beetlejuice (movie)|Beetlejuice]]'', ''[[Madea Goes to Jail]]'' and ''[[Dawn of the Dead]]''.
