 '''Radolfzell''' at the [[Bodensee]] is northwest a [[city]] on the west bank of Bodensee of about 18 & nbsp;km from [[Konstanz]]. It is after Konstanz and [[Singen (Hohentwiel)]] the third biggest city of the [[Konstanz (district)|district Konstanz]]. Since 1 January 1975 Radolfzell is large district town. 
 
 == Neighbour cities == 
 Following cities and municipalities border on the city Radolfzell at the Bodensee. They are called in the clockwise direction beginning in the southwest: [[Moos (at the Bodensee)]], [[Singen (Hohentwiel)]], [[Steisslingen]], [[Orsingen]], [[Nenzingen]](all district Konstanz).
