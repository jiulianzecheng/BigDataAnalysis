 The '''Firmicutes''' are a [[phylum]] of [[bacteria]], most of which have the [[Gram-positive]] type of [[cell wall]]s. 
 
 == Classes == 
 The group is typically divided into the [[Clostridia]], which are [[Anaerobic organism|anaerobic]], the [[Bacilli]], which are [[aerobic]], and the [[Mollicute]]s, a class of bacteria which do not have cell walls.
