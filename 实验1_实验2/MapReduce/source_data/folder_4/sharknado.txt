 '''''Sharknado''''' is a 2013 American television sci-fi disaster movie about a [[waterspout]] that lifts sharks out of the ocean and throws them them in [[Los Angeles]]. It is the first movie in the [[Sharknado (movie series)|''Sharknado'' movie series]]. < ref > {{cite web |url = https://www.usatoday.com/story/life/tv/2013/07/11/bianco-critics-corner-july-11/2502663/ |title = TV tonight: 'Sharknado,' 'Summer Camp' |last=Bianco |first=Robert |date= July 11, 2013 |publisher = USA Today |access-date = July 12, 2013}} < /ref >  It first aired on the [[Syfy]] channel on July 11, 2013.
