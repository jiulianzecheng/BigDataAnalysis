 '''Villavicencio''' is a city in [[Colombia]]. It is the largest city and the capital of the [[Meta Department|Department of Meta]]. Villavicencio is on the great Colombian–Venezuelan plain called [[Los Llanos]]. In 2005, 384,131 people lived there. the city was founded as in 1840. The city became the capital of the department in 1959.
