 '''Hisingen''' is an [[island]] on the west coast of [[Sweden]]. It has an area of {{convert|199|km2}}. It is Sweden's fourth biggest island, but its most populated island. It is north of [[Gothenburg]]. [[Göteborg City Airport]] is there.
