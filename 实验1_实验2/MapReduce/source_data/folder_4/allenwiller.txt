 '''Allenwiller''' is a former [[Communes in France|commune]]. It is in [[Grand Est]] in the [[Bas-Rhin]] [[Departments of France|department]] in northeast [[France]]. On 1 January 2016, it was merged into the new commune of [[Sommerau, Bas-Rhin|Sommerau]]. < ref > [http://www.bas-rhin.gouv.fr/content/download/17755/131609/file/Recueil%20n%C2%B0%2024%20du%2015%20d%C3%A9cembre%202015.pdf Arrêté préfectoral] 8 December 2015 {{in lang|fr}} < /ref >
