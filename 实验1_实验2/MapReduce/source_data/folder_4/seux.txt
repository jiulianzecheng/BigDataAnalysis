 '''Seux''' is a [[France|French]] commune in [[Picardy]]. In 2012, 167 people lived there. The commune is stuated on a [[plateau]], about {{convert|16|km}} from [[Amiens]]. It is known for its church, Saint Fiacre, which was built in the 18th century. Robert de Louvencourt (1880-1952), which was in the French legion d'honneur, was born in Seux.
