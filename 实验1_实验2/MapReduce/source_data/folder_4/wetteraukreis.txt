 The '''Wetteraukreis''' is a ''Kreis'' ([[districts of Germany|district]]) in the middle of [[Hesse]], [[Germany]].  
 
 The district is located between the [[Taunus]] mountains and the [[Vogelsberg]] [[volcano|volcanic]] mountain. The main rivers of the district are the [[Nidda River]] and the [[Wetter (river)|Wetter River]].
