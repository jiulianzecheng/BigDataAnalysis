 '''Sportswear''' is [[clothing]], including [[footwear]], worn for playing sports or to exercise. 
 
 Sportswear includes [[shorts]], [[tracksuit]]s, [[T-shirt]]s, [[polo shirt]]s and [[athletic shoe|trainers]]. It also includes some underwear, such as the [[jockstrap]]. Sportswear is also often worn as [[casual]] [[fashion]] clothing. 
 
 Sports athletes wear a combination of different items of clothing such as [[sport shoe]]s, [[pants]] and [[shirt]]s. Some athletes wear [[personal armour]] such as [[helmet]]s or [[American football]] body armour.
