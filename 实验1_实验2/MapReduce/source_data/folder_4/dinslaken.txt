 '''Dinslaken''' is a town in [[Wesel (district)|Wesel]] in [[North Rhine-Westphalia]], [[Germany]]. It is known for its [[harness racing]] track, its now closed [[Coal mining|coal mine]] in Lohberg, and its wealthy neighborhoods ''Hiesfeld'' and ''Eppinghoven''. 
 
 About 67,000 live in Dinslaken. It is in the [[Ruhr Area]]. 
 
 == Districts == 
 Dinslaken has 7 districts: 
 * Eppinghoven 
 * Hiesfeld 
 * Innenstadt 
 * Lohberg 
 * Oberlohberg 
 * Bruch 
 * Averbruch 
 * Hagenbezirk
