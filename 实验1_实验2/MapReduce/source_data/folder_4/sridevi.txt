 '''Sridevi Kapoor'''  (born '''Shree Amma Yanger Ayyapan'''; 13 August 1963 – 24 February 2018) was an Indian movie actress and movie producer. She was born in [[Sivakasi]], [[Madras State]], India. < ref > {{cite web |url=https://indianexpress.com/article/entertainment/bollywood/sridevi-dead-5076965/lite/ |title=Bollywood actor Sridevi passes away – the Indian Express |access-date=2018-02-24 |url-status=live |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180224230833/http://indianexpress.com/article/entertainment/bollywood/sridevi-dead-5076965/lite/ |archive-date=24 February 2018 |df=dmy-all }} < /ref >  She was known for her role in the 2012 movie ''[[English Vinglish]]''. < ref name= " DailyMail " > {{cite web|last=DailyMail|title=Sridevi has broken a stereotype|website=[[Daily Mail]] |url=http://www.dailymail.co.uk/indiahome/indianews/article-2216935/She-takes-Sridevis-comeback-English-Vinglish-broken-stereotype-proving-age-factor-heroines-comeback.html|url-status=live|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20121125144227/http://www.dailymail.co.uk/indiahome/indianews/article-2216935/She-takes-Sridevis-comeback-English-Vinglish-broken-stereotype-proving-age-factor-heroines-comeback.html|archive-date=25 November 2012|df=dmy-all}} < /ref >  In 2013, the [[Government of India]] awarded her the [[Padma Shri]], the fourth highest civilian honor 
 
 Sridevi died of [[cardiac arrest]] in [[Dubai]], [[United Arab Emirates]] on 24 February 2018 at the age of 54. < ref > {{cite web |url=https://m.huffingtonpost.in/amp/2018/02/24/breaking-sridevi-dies-at-55_a_23370162/ |title=Breaking: Sridevi Dies At 54 |work=Huffington Post Ibdia |date=2012-09-14 |access-date=2018-02-25 }}{{Dead link|date=December 2020 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }} < /ref >  Although it was earlier announced that the cause of death was a cardiac arrest, the forensic report released by [[Dubai Police Force|Dubai Police]] said that Sridevi died of accidental drowning. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.ctvnews.ca/mobile/entertainment/bollywood-s-sridevi-drowned-in-hotel-bathtub-dubai-police-say-1.3818938|title=Bollywood's Sridevi drowned in hotel bathtub, Dubai police say|work=[[CTV News]]|date=February 26, 2018}} < /ref >
