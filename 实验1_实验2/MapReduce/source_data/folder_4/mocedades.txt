 '''Mocedades''' ({{IPA-es|moθeˈðaðes}}) is a Spanish singing group. They were from the  [[Basque Country (autonomous community)|Basque Autonomous Community]]. They represented [[Spain]] in the [[Eurovision Song Contest]] in [[Eurovision Song Contest 1973|1973]]. Their best known song was  " [[Eres Tu (1973 song)|Eres Tú]] " . 
 
 In 2014, ''Mocedades'' became the name of two bands: one with [[Izaskun Uranga]] as its leader and the other with [[Javier Garay]]. A third group, [[El Consorcio]], is made of former Mocedades members who left the group but have continued a career together outside the Mocedades brand. < ref > [http://www.radioformula.com.mx/notas.asp?Idn=200853 Article from Radio Formula, 6 Oct 2011]{{Dead link|date=January 2022 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }} < /ref >
