 }}'''Magnoliopsida''' or '''dicotyledons''' is the name of  a [[Class (biology)|class]] of [[plant]]s in [[taxonomy]]. They are also called [[dicots]]. It is the biggest group of [[flowering plant]]s. [[Seedling]]s in this group have two [[seed]] [[leaves]]. [[Monocotyledon|Monocots]] have one seed leaf.
