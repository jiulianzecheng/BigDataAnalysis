 '''Mataura''' is a town in the [[Southland, New Zealand|Southland]] region of [[South Island]], [[New Zealand]]. It has a meat processing plant and until 2000, it had a paper mill. It has a primary school. It is located 13 kilometers southwest of [[Gore, New Zealand|Gore]] and 53 kilometers northeast of [[Invercargill]] on State Highway 1.
