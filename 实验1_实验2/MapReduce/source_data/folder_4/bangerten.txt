 '''Bangerten''' was a [[municipality]] in the [[administrative district]] of [[Seeland (administrative district)|Seeland]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Bern (canton)|Berne]] in [[Switzerland]]. On 1 January 2016 the former municipality of Bangerten merged into the municipality of [[Rapperswil, Bern|Rapperswil]].{{fact|date=June 2016}}
