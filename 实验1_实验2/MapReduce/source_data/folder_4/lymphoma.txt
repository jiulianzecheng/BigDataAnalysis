'''Lymphoma''' is a [[cancer]] of the [[lymphatic system]]. There are many types of lymphoma, including [[Hodgkin's lymphoma]], [[non-Hodgkin lymphoma]], [[primary central nervous system lymphoma]], the [[B-cell]] lymphoma, and the [[hepatosplenic lymphoma]].  
 
 == Symptoms == 
 * Larger-than-normal [[lymph nodes]] 
 * Sweating at night 
 * [[Fever|Fevers]] that happen often or do not get better 
 * [[Weight loss]] 
 * [[Fatigue]] < ref > {{Cite web|url=http://www.lymphomainfo.net/lymphoma/symptoms.html|title = What Are the Signs and Symptoms of Lymphoma?}} < /ref >  (feeling tired) 
 * [[Splenomegaly|Enlarged spleen]] 
 * Enlarged [[Livermorium|liver]]
