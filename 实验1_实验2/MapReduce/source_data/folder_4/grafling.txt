 '''Grafling''' is a [[Municipalities of Germany|municipality]] in the district of [[Deggendorf (district)|Deggendorf]] in [[Bavaria]] in [[Germany]]. 
 
 == Geography == 
 Grafling is in the Danube Forest Planning Region (''Planungsregion Donau-Wald''). Its lowest point is in Großtiefenbach at 326 & nbsp;m above sea level ([[Normalnull|NN]]). The highest points of the municipality are the Steinberg (999 & nbsp;m), the [[Geißriegel]] (1,043 & nbsp;m), the [[Dreitannenriegel]] (1,090 & nbsp;m) and the [[Einödriegel]] (1,121 & nbsp;m) which rise above the valley of the Graflinger Tal to the east. To the north the valley ends at the Hochberg (727 & nbsp;m), to the west it is guarded by the Butzen (775 & nbsp;m) and the [[Vogelsang (Bavarian Forest)|Vogelsang]] (1,022 & nbsp;m) in the municipality of [[Bernried (Lower Bavaria)|Bernried]]. To the south the valley opens up towards [[Deggendorf]], the Danube Plain, and the [[Gäuboden]].
