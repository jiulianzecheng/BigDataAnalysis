 '''Palagnedra''' was a [[municipality]] of the district [[Locarno (district)|Locarno]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Ticino]] in [[Switzerland]]. [[Lago di Palagnedra]] is found below the village, on the [[Melezza]] river.
