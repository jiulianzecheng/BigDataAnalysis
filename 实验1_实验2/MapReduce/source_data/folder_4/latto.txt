 '''Alyssa Michelle Stephens''' (born December 22, 1998), known professionally as '''Latto''' (formerly known as '''Mulatto''' and '''Hippolattomus'''), is an American rapper. She won the first season of the reality television series ''[[The Rap Game]]'' in 2016 as '''Miss Mulatto'''. 
 
 Her 2019 single  " Bitch from da Souf "  peaked at number 95 on the [[Billboard Hot 100|''Billboard'' Hot 100]] in August 2020. It was accompanied by a remix by rappers [[Saweetie]] and [[Trina]].  " [[Muwop]] "  (featuring [[Gucci Mane]]) followed. Both songs were [[Music recording certification|certified platinum]]. They also appeared on Latto's first album ''[[Queen of da Souf]]'', which was released in August 2020. The album was re-issued as a deluxe edition in December 2020. Latto's mainstream breakthrough came with the release of  " [[Big Energy]] " , the lead single from her second studio album ''[[777 (Latto album)|777]]'' (2022). It became Latto's highest-charting single to date, peaking at number three on the ''Billboard'' Hot 100. 
 
 Latto received controversy in 2022 over her unjust drama with Queen of Rap, [[Nicki Minaj]] over a [[Grammy|Grammy nomination]]. < ref > {{Cite web|last=taramhdvn|title=Latto Appears to Take Shots At Nicki Minaj During Coachella Performance|url=https://www.complex.com/music/a/tara-mahadevan/fans-speculate-latto-took-shots-nicki-minaj-coachella|access-date=2023-11-17|website=Complex|language=en-us}} < /ref >  The  " flourishing "  rapper currently has a one-sided beef with the Queen of Rap as she constantly disses the rapper, although on numerous occasions she has shown to admire Minaj.
