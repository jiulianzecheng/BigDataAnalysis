'''Sahaba''' or '''As-Sahaba''' ({{lang-ar|الصحابة}}), meaning ''the companions'', refers to the companions, friends and family of the [[Islamic]] [[prophet]], [[Muhammad]]. < ref > [Source: Al-Isâbah (1/4-5) of al-Hâfidh lbn Hajar] < /ref > < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.britannica.com/topic/Companions-of-the-Prophet|title=Companions of the Prophet - Islamic history|publisher=}} < /ref >  They are highly respected by [[Muslims]] and after saying any name of a ''sahabi'', muslims usually say ''Radhiya llahu anhu'' ({{lang-ar|رضي الله عنه‎}}), meaning  " well-pleased Allah is with them " . < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.perseus.tufts.edu/hopper/text?doc=Perseus:text:2002.02.0006:sura=9:verse=100|title=The Quran, sura 9, verse 100|website=www.perseus.tufts.edu}} < /ref >
