 '''Janet''' '''Rose''' (born November 5, 1994), better known by her online name '''xChocoBars''', is a Canadian [[Internet celebrity|internet personality]] and [[Twitch (service)|Twitch]] [[Video game live streaming|streamer]]. She is a content creator for [[Luminosity Gaming]]. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Luminosity Gaming Streamers|url=https://toptwitchstreamers.com/team/luminosity-gaming/|url-status=live|access-date=23 March 2021|website=TopTwitchStreamers|language=en-US|archive-date=26 January 2021|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210126180302/https://toptwitchstreamers.com/team/luminosity-gaming/}} < /ref >  Rose was born in [[Toronto]].  
 
 In 2018, Rose was one of the four captains of an $100,000 ''League of Legends'' esports charity tournament alongside [[Pokimane]], [[LilyPichu]], and Kaypea. < ref > {{Cite web|date=28 August 2018|title=20 Top Twitch Streamers Taking Part in $100,000 Charity eSports Tournament on September 1|url=https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20180828005716/en/20-Top-Twitch-Streamers-Taking-Part-in-100000-Charity-eSports-Tournament-on-September-1|url-status=live|access-date=23 March 2021|website=[[Business Wire]]|language=en|archive-date=26 May 2022|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20220526130329/https://www.businesswire.com/favicon.ico}} < /ref > 
 
 She was a finalist for the Twitch Streamer of the Year award at the [[11th Shorty Awards]]. < ref name= " 2019 shorty award " > {{Cite web|title=xChocobars - The Shorty Awards|url=http://shortyawards.com/11th/xchocobars|url-status=live|access-date=23 March 2021|website=[[Shorty Awards]]|archive-date=26 May 2022|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20220526130312/https://shortyawards.com/11th/xchocobars}} < /ref >
