Pearlie is a 2009-11 Australian/Canadian children's [[Animated movies|animated]] educational TV series produced by Australian animation studio Sticky Pictures and Canadian studio [[Nelvana]]. It was and produced in association with Canadian Television Channel [[YTV (Canadian TV channel)|YTV]] and Austrailan Television Network Network Ten.
