 '''Oberammergau''' is a [[municipality]] in the district of [[Garmisch-Partenkirchen (district)|Garmisch-Partenkirchen]] in [[Upper Bavaria]] in [[Germany]]. It is known for the [[Oberammergau Passion Play]], which was first performed in 1634.
