 '''''Matewan''''' is a 1987 American historical drama movie directed by [[John Sayles]] and starring [[Chris Cooper]], [[James Earl Jones]], [[Mary McDonnell]], [[Will Oldham]], [[David Strathairn]], [[Kevin Tighe]], [[Gordon Clapp]]. It was distributed by [[Cinecom Pictures]] and was nominated for an Academy Award in 1988.
