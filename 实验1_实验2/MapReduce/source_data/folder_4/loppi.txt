 '''Loppi''' ({{lang-sv|Loppis}}) is a [[municipality]] in [[Kanta-Häme]], [[Finland]]. As of 31 December 2020, the municipality had a population of 7,855. The municipalities next to it are [[Hyvinkää]], [[Hämeenlinna]], [[Janakkala]], [[Karkkila]], [[Riihimäki]], [[Tammela, Finland|Tammela]] and [[Vihti]]. 
 
 Loppi was founded in 1632.
