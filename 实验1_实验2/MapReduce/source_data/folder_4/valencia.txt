 [[File:Valencia, Spain - location.png|thumb|Valencia (red dot) on a map of [[Spain]]]] 
 '''Valencia''' is a [[city]] in [[Spain]], on the [[Mediterranean Sea|Mediterranean]] coast. It is the [[Capital (city)|capital]] of the [[Community of Valencia]]. It has 807.396 people, making it the third-largest city in [[Spain]] and the fifteenth in the [[European Union]].  
 
 Valencia has a borderline hot semi-arid climate (''BSh'' in the [[Köppen climate classification]]) and a hot-summer [[Mediterranean climate]] (''Csa'' in the [[Köppen climate classification]]).
