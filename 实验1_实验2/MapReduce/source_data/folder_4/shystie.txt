 '''Chanel Cali''' (born 25 December 1983), better known by her [[stage name]] '''Shystie''', is a British [[rapper]], [[songwriter]], and [[actress]]. Born in [[London]], she has collaborated with the likes of [[Alesha Dixon]] and wanted to be a member of [[Mis-Teeq]]. She worked with [[Basement Jaxx]], [[50 Cent]], and [[Dizzee Rascal]] on his single  " [[I Luv U (song)|I Luv U]] " .
