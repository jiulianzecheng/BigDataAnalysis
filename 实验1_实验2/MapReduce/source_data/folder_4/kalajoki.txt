 '''Kalajoki''' is a town in [[Northern Ostrobothnia]], [[Finland]]. As of 28 February 2023, 12,328 people lived there. Neighbouring municipalities are [[Alavieska]], [[Kokkola]], [[Kannus]], [[Merijärvi]], [[Pyhäjoki]], [[Sievi]] and [[Ylivieska]].
