 '''Bitonto''' is a city in the [[Apulia]] region of [[Italy]]. It is to the west of [[Bari]], near the [[Adriatic Sea]]. 
 
 The city is known for producing [[olive oil]] and [[wine]]. About 54,500 people live in Bitonto.
