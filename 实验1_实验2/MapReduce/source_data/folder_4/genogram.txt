 A '''genogram''' is a [[family tree]] with [[medical]] details, such as whether the [[Human|person]] is [[male]] or [[female]], and whether [[brother]]s and [[sister]]s are [[twins]]. Genograms help [[Medical doctor|medics]] understand a person better. 
 
 == Family relationships ==
