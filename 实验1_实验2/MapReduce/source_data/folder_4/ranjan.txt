 '''Ranjan''' (born as '''Ramanarayana Venkataramana Sarma'''; 2 March 1918 – 12 September 1983) was an Indian actor, singer, journalist and writer. He first acted in the 1941 movie ''Ashok Kumar'', but became famous in [[S. S.
