'''Agrahari''' is an Indian [[surname]], mainly found in North Indian states. [[Agrasena|Maharaja Agrasen]] is believed to be their ancestor. The mainly found in [[Uttar Pradesh]], [[Delhi]], [[West Bengal]], [[Bihar]], [[Assam]], [[Jharkhand]], [[Madhya Pradesh]], [[Chhattisgarh]] and [[Maharashtra]] and [[Gujarat]] of [[States of India]].
