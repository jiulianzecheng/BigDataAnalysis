 '''Ahnsbeck''' is a municipality in [[Celle (district)|Celle]], in [[Lower Saxony]], [[Germany]]. It is at the southern border of the [[Lüneburg Heath]], 10 & nbsp;km eastern of the city of [[Celle]]. It is part of the collective municipality of [[Lachendorf]].
