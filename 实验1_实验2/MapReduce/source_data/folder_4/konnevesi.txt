 '''Konnevesi''' is a [[municipality]] in [[Central Finland]]. About 2,850 people lived there in January 2014. The municipalities next to it are [[Hankasalmi]], [[Laukaa]], [[Rautalampi]], [[Vesanto]] and [[Äänekoski]]. 
 
 Konnevesi was established in 1922. The name comes from a lake called Lake Konnevesi.
