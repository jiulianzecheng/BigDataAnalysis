 '''Bushehr''' ({{lang-fa|بوشهر|Būshehr}}) is a city in [[Iran]]. It is the capital of [[Bushehr Province]]. 
 
 The city is known for the [[Bushehr Nuclear Power Plant]] and has about 171,000 people living there.
