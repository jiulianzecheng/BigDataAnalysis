 '''Buskerud''' will (2024) become a [[county]] of [[Norway]]. (It was a county, before 2020.) Its main [[city]] was [[Drammen]]. 
 
 On 1 January 2020 the county merged with [[Akershus]] and [[Østfold]] to the current county of [[Viken (county)|Viken]].
