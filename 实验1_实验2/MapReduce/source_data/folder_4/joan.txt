'''Joan''' [ john ] < ref > {{Cite web|title=Definition of Joan {{!}} Dictionary.com|url=https://www.dictionary.com/browse/joan|access-date=2023-04-06|website=www.dictionary.com|language=en}} < /ref >  is a female [[given name]] in the [[English language]]. The name is the feminine form of ''John''. Joan is a name that has been well-liked for many years. It's a name that's been embraced by various societies and holds diverse connotations depending on its origin. The name Joan comes from the Hebrew name Yochanan, which signifies  " God is gracious. "  Moreover, it's a womanly variation of the name John, which means  " God is merciful. " < ref > {{Cite web|title=Joan name Meaning and Origin|url=https://babys.name/detail-Joan-Meaning-Origin-Famous-Similar-Sibling.html|access-date=2023-04-06|website=babys.name}} < /ref >  There are many celebrities named Joan, including [[Joan Rivers]], [[Joan Caulfield]] and [[Joan Crawford]].  
 
 In 1988, a violent [[Hurricane Joan–Miriam|hurricane]] with the name caused a lot of destruction and deaths in [[Nicaragua]]. The name ''Joan'' was later retired.
