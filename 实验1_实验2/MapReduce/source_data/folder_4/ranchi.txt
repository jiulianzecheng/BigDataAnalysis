 '''Ranchi''' is the [[capital (city)|capital]] [[city]] of [[Jharkhand]] state of [[India]]. About 11,26,720 [[population|people]] lived in Ranchi ([[census]]:2011). Two of the oldest and largest [[psychiatric hospital]]s of India were located there.  In 1967 it had one of India's first medical colleges.  < ref > {{Cite book|title=India- Which Century|last=Stark Murray|first=David|publisher=Victor Gollancz|year=1967|location=London|pages=68}} < /ref >
