'''Synopsys''' is an American electronic design automation company. Synopsys was founded by Aart J de Geus and David Gregory in 1986 in [[North Carolina|Research Triangle Park, North Carolina]]. Their technology is present in [[Self-driving car|self-driving cars]], [[artificial intelligence]], and [[internet of things]] consumer products.
