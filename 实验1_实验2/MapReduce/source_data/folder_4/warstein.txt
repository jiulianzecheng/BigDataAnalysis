 '''Warstein''' is a town of the district of [[Soest Rural District|Soest]] in the [[States of Germany|state]] of [[North Rhine-Westphalia]] in [[Germany]]. The brewing company [[Warsteiner]] has its main office in Warstein. 
 
 About 25,000 people live there.
