{{nihongo|'''Tenroku'''|天禄}} was a {{nihongo|[[Japanese era name|Japanese era]]|年号,|''nengō'',|lit.  " year name " }} after ''[[Anna (era)|Anna]]'' and before ''[[Ten'en]].''  This period started in March 970 and ended in March 973. < ref > Nussbaum, Louis-Frédéric. (2005). [https://books.google.com/books?id=p2QnPijAEmEC & pg=PA961  " ''Tenroku'' " ] in ''Japan Encyclopedia'', p. 961. < /ref >  The reigning emperors were {{nihongo|[[Emperor Reizei|Reizei]]''-tennō''|冷泉天皇}} and {{nihongo|[[Emperor En'yu|En'yū]]''-tennō''|円融天皇}}. < ref > [[Isaac Titsingh|Titsingh, Isaac]]. (1834). [https://books.google.com/books?id=18oNAAAAIAAJ & pg=PA144 ''Annales des empereurs du japon'', pp. 144-145]; Brown, Delmer ''et al.'' (1979). [https://books.google.com/books?id=w4f5FrmIJKIC & pg=PA299 ''Gukanshō'', pp. 299-300]; Varley, H. Paul. (1980). ''Jinnō Shōtōki'', pp. 191-192. < /ref > 
 
 ==Events of the ''Tenroku'' era== 
 * '''970''' (''Tenroku 1, 1st month''): {{nihongo|[[Fujiwara no Arihira]]|藤原在衡 }} became Minister of the Left (''[[sadaijin]]''); and {{nihongo|[[Fujiwara no Koretada]]|藤原伊尹| }} became Minister of the Right (''[[udaijin]]'') in the Imperial court [[hierarchy]]. < ref name= " titsingh144 " > Titsingh, [https://books.google.com/books?id=18oNAAAAIAAJ & pg=PA144 p. 144]. < /ref > 
 * '''970''' (''Tenroku 1, 5th month''): {{nihongo|[[Fujiwara no Saneyori]]|藤原実頼}} died at the age of 71; and the Koretada took his place as chief advisor to the emperor. < ref name= " titsingh144 " / > 
 * '''970''' (''Tenroku 1, 10th month''): {{nihongo|[[Fujiwara no Arihira]]|藤原実頼}} died at age 79.  He had held the office of ''sadaijin''. < ref name= " titsingh144 " / > 
 * '''971''' (''Tenroku 2, 3rd month''): For the first time, a festival (''matsuri'') in honor of the ''[[kami]]'' of [[Iwashimizu Shrine]] was celebrated. < ref name= " titsingh144 " / > 
 * '''971''' (''Tenroku 2, in the 11th month''): Koretada was created  Prime Minister (''[[daijō-daijin]]''); {{nihongo|[[Minamoto no Kaneakira]]|源兼明}} was made ''sadaijin''; and {{nihongo|[[Fujiwara no Yoritada]]|藤原頼忠}} was named ''udaijin''. < ref > Titsingh, [https://books.google.com/books?id=18oNAAAAIAAJ & pg=PA144 pp. 144-145]. < /ref > 
 * '''4 April 972''' (''Tenroku 3, 5th day of the 3rd month'') < !-- NengoCalc 天禄三年三月五日 -- > : Koretada organized [[Ceremony|ceremonies]] which confirmed [[Emperor En'yū]]'s role as monarch . < ref name= " titsingh145 " > Titsingh, [https://books.google.com/books?id=18oNAAAAIAAJ & pg=PA145 p. 145]. < /ref > 
 * '''972''' (''Tenroku 3, 11th month''): Koretada died at age 49. < ref name= " titsingh145 " / >
