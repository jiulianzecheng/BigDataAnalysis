 '''Berdychiv''' ({{lang-uk|Бердичів}}, [[Polish language|Polish]]: Berdyczów, {{lang-yi| באַרדיטשעװ|Barditshev}}, {{lang-ru|Берди́чев|Berdichev}}) is a city in the [[Zhytomyr Oblast]] ([[Oblast|province]]) of northern [[Ukraine]].
