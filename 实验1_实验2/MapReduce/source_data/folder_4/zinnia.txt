 '''''Zinnia''''' is a plant in the [[Asteraceae]], < ref > {{Cite web|title=zinnia {{!}} plant {{!}} Britannica|url=https://www.britannica.com/plant/zinnia|access-date=2022-09-23|website=www.britannica.com|language=en}} < /ref >  or [[Flowering plant|flowering plant family]] as it’s [[genus]] as itself. They have notably long-stems that come in many bright colors. 
 
 Zinnias are garden flowers that come in many flower colors and shapes, and can live in summer temperature, and are easy to grow from seeds. They are grown in [[Fertility|fertile]], well drained soil < ref > {{Cite web|title=How to Grow Zinnias|url=https://www.americanmeadows.com/wildflower-seeds/zinnia-flower-seeds/how-to-grow-zinnias#}} < /ref >  in an area with much sunlight and outdoors. They remake seeds each year, so it is an [[annual plant]]. 
 
 Zinnias was named after [[Johann Gottfried Zinn]] < ref > {{Cite web|title=The History of Zinnia Flowers|url=https://www.gardenguides.com/82720-history-zinnia-flowers.html|access-date=2022-09-23|website=Garden Guides|language=en}} < /ref >  who was a [[botanist]] (plant scientist). < ref > {{Cite web|last=Ph.D|first=Efrain A. Miranda|title=Johann Gottfried Zinn|url=https://clinicalanatomy.com/mtd/857-johann-gottfried-zinn|access-date=2022-09-24|website=clinicalanatomy.com|language=en-gb}} < /ref >  They are native to Mexico.
