'''Warlingham''' is a [[village]] in the  [[Tandridge (district)|Tandridge]] district of [[Surrey]], [[England]]. It is {{convert|14.2|mi}} south of the centre of [[London]]. Warlingham consists of many [[shop]]s and [[primary school]]s. There is also a [[secondary school]], [[Warlingham School]]. Warlingham is near to other villages such as [[Caterham]], [[Whyteleafe]] and [[Sanderstead]].
