 '''Tschierv''' used to be a [[municipality]] of the [[district]] of [[Inn (district)|Inn]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Graubünden]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 On 1 January 2009 the municipalities of Tschierv, [[Fuldera]], [[Lü, Switzerland|Lü]], [[Müstair]], [[Santa Maria Val Müstair]] and [[Valchava]] joined and became the new municipality of [[Val Müstair]].
