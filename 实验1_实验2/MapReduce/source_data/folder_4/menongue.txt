 '''Menongue''' is a [[town]] and [[municipality]] in [[Cuando Cubango Province]] in [[Angola]]. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.statoids.com/yao.html|title=City councils of Angola|publisher=Statoids|accessdate=April 7, 2009}} < /ref >   It is also the capital of the province. It is the end of the southern railway from [[Namibe]].
