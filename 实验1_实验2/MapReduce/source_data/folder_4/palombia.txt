 '''Palombia''' is a fictional country in the [[Belgium|Belgian]] [[Comics|comic]] ''Spirou and Fantasio''. In the world of the comic, Palombia is found between [[Paraguay]] and [[Colombia]]. Its name is a [[portmanteau]] of both countries.
