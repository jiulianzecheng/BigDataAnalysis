 '''Tatooine''' is a [[desert]] planet in the [[fictional]] ''[[Star Wars]]'' universe. [[Anakin Skywalker]] was born on Tatooine, and his son [[Luke Skywalker]] grew up there. 
 
 Tatooine is a place with lots of [[criminals]] (including [[Jabba the Hutt]]), and [[slavery]] exists on this planet.
