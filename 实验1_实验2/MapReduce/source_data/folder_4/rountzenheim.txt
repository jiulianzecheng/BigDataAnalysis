 '''Rountzenheim''' is a former [[Communes in France|commune]]. It is in [[Grand Est]] in the [[Bas-Rhin]] [[Departments of France|department]] in northeast [[France]]. On 1 January 2019, it was merged into the new commune of [[Rountzenheim-Auenheim]]. < ref > [https://www.legifrance.gouv.fr/eli/arrete/2018/9/4/INTB1824136A/jo/texte Arrêté préfectoral] 4 September 2018 {{in lang|fr}} < /ref >
