 '''AirAsia Berhad''' ({{myx|5099}}) is a [[Malaysia]]n [[Low-cost carrier|low-cost airline]] based in [[Kuala Lumpur]], [[Malaysia]]. It has been named as the world's best low-cost airline, < ref > *{{cite news|url=http://www.worldairlineawards.com/awards_2012/lowcost2012.htm|title=AIRASIA is named as the World's Best Low-Cost Airline at the 2012 World Airline Awards held at Farnborough Air Show|year=2012|access-date=12 April 2013|work=The World Airline Awards|archive-date=15 July 2012|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120715040504/http://www.worldairlineawards.com/Awards_2012/lowcost2012.htm|url-status=dead}} 
 *{{cite news|title= AirAsia named Skytrax's World Best Low Cost Airline|author= Bernama|url= http://www.nst.com.my/latest/airasia-named-skytrax-s-world-best-low-cost-airline-1.106973|newspaper= [[New Straits Times]]|date= 13 July 2012|access-date= 11 April 2013}} < /ref >  and a pioneer of low-cost travel in Asia. < ref > {{cite news |url= https://www.nytimes.com/2007/12/23/travel/23prac.html?ref=yourmoney |title= Does Low Cost Mean High Risk? |date=23 December 2007 |access-date=28 April 2010|work=The New York Times |first=Joshua |last=Kurlantzick}} < /ref >  AirAsia group operates domestic and international flights to 100 destinations in 22 countries. Its main hub is the [[klia2]] (LCCT) at [[Kuala Lumpur International Airport]] (KLIA).
