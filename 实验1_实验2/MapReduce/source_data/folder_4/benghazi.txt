 '''Benghazi''' is a city and district in [[Libya]]. It is the second largest city in the country with 670,797 people (2006). It is also the capital of the [[Cyrenaica]] region.  It was the former provisional capital of the [[National Transitional Council]]. < ref name= " haaretz.com " > {{cite web | author = Staff | url= http://www.haaretz.com/news/international/libya-s-ex-justice-minister-forms-interim-government-in-benghazi-1.345892|title= Libya's Ex-Justice Minister Forms Interim Government in Benghazi & nbsp; & ndash; Former Libyan Minister Says Gadhafi 'Alone' Bore Responsibility for Crimes That Occurred, Qurnya Newspaper Reports |date = 26 February 2011|work= [[Haaretz]]|access-date= 13 September 2011}} < /ref >   It is on the [[Mediterranean Sea]]. 
 
 The oldest university in Libya is the [[University of Libya]] in the city. [http://www.limu.edu.ly/  Libyan International Medical University] is the first accredited private medical university. 
 
 Two of Libya's most-successful football clubs are in the city. They are [[Al-Ahly (Benghazi)|Al-Ahly Benghazi]] and [[Al Nasr Benghazi|Al-Nasr]].  The two teams have won the [[Libyan Premier League]] five times.
