 '''Probolinggo''' ({{lang-id|Kota Probolinggo}}, {{lang-jv|ꦥꦿꦧꦭꦶꦁꦒ}}) is a city on the north coast of [[East Java]] province, [[Indonesia]]. Its area is 56.67 sq. & nbsp;km. The population was 217,062 at the 2010 Census.
