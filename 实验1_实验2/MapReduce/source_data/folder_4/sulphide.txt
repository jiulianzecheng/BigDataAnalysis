A '''sulphide''' ('''sulfide''' in [[US English]]) is an [[anion]] of  [[sulphur]] in its lowest [[oxidation number]] of -2.  Sulphide is also a term for [[thioether]]s, a common type of  [[Chemical compound|compound]] which are known for their bad odours (example: H < sub > 2 < /sub > S = hydrogen sulphide). 
 
 The di[[anion]] S < sup > 2- < /sup >  exists only in strongly [[alkaline]] [[water|aqueous]] solutions.
