 '''Rebecques''' is a former [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is found in the [[Regions of France|region]] [[Nord-Pas-de-Calais]] in the [[Pas-de-Calais]] [[Departments of France|department]] in the north of [[France]]. On 1 January 2016, it was merged into the new commune of [[Saint-Augustin, Pas-de-Calais|Saint-Augustin]]. < ref > [http://www.pas-de-calais.gouv.fr/content/download/20388/146272/file/N%C2%B023%20Trame%20Recueil%20%20%20du%2028%20%20d%C3%A9cembre%202015.pdf Arrêté préfectoral] 7 December 2015 {{in lang|fr}} < /ref >
