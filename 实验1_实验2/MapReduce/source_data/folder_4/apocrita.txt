 '''Apocrita''' is a [[suborder]] of [[insect]]s in the order [[Hymenoptera]], the other being the [[Sawfly|Symphyta]].  
 
 Included in the Apocrita are the [[ant]]s, [[bee]]s, [[wasp]]s, [[braconid]]s, [[ichneumon]]s, [[chalcid]]s, nearly all parasitic hymenopterans, and a few other forms. The suborder includes the most highly evolved members of the order. There are well over a hundred thousand [[species]] distributed worldwide.
