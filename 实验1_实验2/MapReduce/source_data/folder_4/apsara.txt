 '''Apsara''' are female water [[Spirit|spirits]] in [[Hinduism]] and [[Buddhism]]. They are usually depicted [[dancing]] and are prominent in [[sculpture]], [[literature]], [[dance]], and [[art]] of [[India|Indian]] and [[Southeast Asia|Southeast Asian]] cultures.
