 '''Steyr''' is a [[city]] in the [[Austria]]n state of [[Upper Austria]]. It is the capital, though not part of [[Steyr-Land District]]. Steyr is Austria's 12th most populated city and the third largest city in Upper Austria. As of 1 January 2019, it has about 38,100 people living there. 
 
 The city is in the [[Traunviertel]] region, with the two rivers [[Steyr (river)|Steyr]] and [[Enns (river)|Enns]] flowing through it.
