 '''Bloemendaal''' is a [[List of municipalities of the Netherlands|municipality]] in the [[Netherlands|Dutch]] [[Provinces of the Netherlands|province]] of [[North Holland]]. In 2019, 23,159 people lived there. 
 
 Since 2009, [[Bennebroek]] is part of the municipality of Bloemendaal.
