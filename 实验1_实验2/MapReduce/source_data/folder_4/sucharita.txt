 '''Baby Helen''' (known by her stage name '''Sucharita'''; born 1958) is a Bangladeshi film actress. < ref name=game > {{cite news|url=http://archive.thedailystar.net/2005/05/06/d505061403105.htm|title=Tit Bits - The celebrity name game|author=Afsar Ahmed|date=May 6, 2005|access-date=December 29, 2015|newspaper=The Daily Star|archive-date=March 4, 2016|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160304094409/http://archive.thedailystar.net/2005/05/06/d505061403105.htm|url-status=dead}} < /ref >   She won the [[National Film Awards (Bangladesh)|Bangladesh National Film Award for Best Actress]] for her role in the film ''[[Hangor Nodi Grenade]]'' (1997). < ref > {{cite news |author=Rashed Shawon |date=14 August 2012 |script-title=bn:চার দশকে আমাদের সেরা চলচ্চিত্রগুলো |url=http://glitz.bdnews24.com/details.php?catry=2 & showns=2012 |language=bn |trans-title=Best films of four decades|publisher=bdnews24.com |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130127231625/http://glitz.bdnews24.com/details.php?catry=2 & showns=2012 |archive-date=2013-01-27|access-date=2018-05-24}} < /ref >  She has acted in over 100 movies.
