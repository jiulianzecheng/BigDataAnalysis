 '''Gothenburg''' ({{lang-sv|Göteborg}}) is the second largest [[Sweden|Swedish]] city, after [[Stockholm]]. Around 550 000 people live there. The city was founded in 1621 by king [[Gustavus Adolphus of Sweden]].
