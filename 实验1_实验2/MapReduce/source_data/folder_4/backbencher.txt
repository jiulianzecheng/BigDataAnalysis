 In [[Westminster system|Westminster parliamentary systems]], a '''backbencher'''  is a [[member of parliament]] (MP) or a legislator who holds no [[Minister (government)|governmental office]] and is not a [[Frontbencher|frontbench]] spokesman in the [[Official Opposition (United Kingdom)|Opposition]].
