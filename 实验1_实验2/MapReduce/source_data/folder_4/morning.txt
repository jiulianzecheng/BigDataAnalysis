 '''Morning''' is time periode of the day from midnight to noon (00:00:01-11:59:59). < ref > {{Cite web |url=http://oxforddictionaries.com/definition/english/morning?view=uk |title=Oxford Dictionary |access-date=2019-04-24 |archive-date=2016-03-04 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160304053126/http://www.oxforddictionaries.com/definition/english/morning?view=uk |url-status=dead }} < /ref > < ref > [https://www.timeanddate.com/sun/uk/london timeanddate.com] < /ref >  Morning is usually cooler than [[afternoon]] (12:00:01-17:59:59). 
 
 ==Morning activities== 
 Many people use the morning as a time to get ready for the [[day]]. This includes things like taking a shower, eating a meal (called breakfast, because you break the [[Fasting|fast]] of the night) and getting dressed. Then many people go to [[Employment|work]] or [[school]].  
 
 Many people buy a morning [[newspaper]] to read about what is going on in the world.
