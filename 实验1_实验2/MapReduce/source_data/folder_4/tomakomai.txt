 {{nihongo|'''Tomakomai'''|苫小牧市|Tomakomai-shi}} is a [[Japan]]ese city and [[port]] on the southern coast of [[Hokkaido]] It is the largest city in the Iburi Subprefecture. 
 
 ==History== 
 The city was founded on April 1, [[1948]].
