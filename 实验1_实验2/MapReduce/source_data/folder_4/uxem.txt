 '''Uxem''' ({{IPA-fr|yks…õm}}; from [[Flemish]]; ''Uksem'' in modern Dutch spelling) is a [[Communes in France|commune]] in [[Nord (department)|Nord]] in north [[France]]. In 2018 1,422 people lived there.
