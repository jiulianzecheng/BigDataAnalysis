 '''Chamonix''' is a [[France|French]] [[communes of France|commune]] next to [[Mont Blanc]]. It is in the [[Haute-Savoie]] [[Departments of France|department]]. The [[Winter Olympic Games]] were there in [[1924]]. 
 " Charmonix "  is actually French for dining room. it was named this for its irregular shape, which to many looks like a dining room
