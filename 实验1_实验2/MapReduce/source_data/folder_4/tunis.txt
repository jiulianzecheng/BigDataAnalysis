 '''Tunis''' ({{lang-ar|تونس}}, ''Tūnis'') is the [[capital (city)|capital city]] of [[Tunisia]]. As of [[2004]], 728,463 people lived in the city. About 1.6 million live in the [[metropolitan area]].
