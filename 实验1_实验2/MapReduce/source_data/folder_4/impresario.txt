 An '''Impresario''' is somebody who organizes [[concert]]s, [[opera]]s, [[ballet]]s or [[Play (theatre)|plays]].  The word comes from the [[Italian language|Italian]] word ''impresa,'' which means  " an enterprise "  or  " undertaking " . 
 
 Some very famous impresarios from the past include [[Rudolf Bing]], [[Sergei Diaghilev]], [[Richard D'Oyly Carte]], [[Fortune Gallo]], and [[Sol Hurok]].
