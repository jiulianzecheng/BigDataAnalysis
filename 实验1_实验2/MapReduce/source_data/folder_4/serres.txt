 '''Serres''' is a Greek city. It is also one of 51 [[prefecture]]s of [[Greece]]. There are 56,400 people in Serres. Serres is built on the large plain. It is in the north of Greece, in the region of [[Macedonia (Greece)|Macedonia]]. It is near the Strymonas river.
