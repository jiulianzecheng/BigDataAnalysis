 '''Gryfice''' ({{IPAc-pl|AUD|Pl-Gryfice.ogg|g|r|y|'|f|J|i|c|e}}; [[Kashubian language|Kashubian]]: ''Grëfice''), formerly known as '''Greifenberg''', < ref name=eb9 > " {{Cite EB9|wstitle=Greifenberg|volume=11|page=183}}. 1880. < /ref >  is a [[town]] in [[Pomerania]], north-western [[Poland]] with 16,632 people (2008). It is the capital of [[Gryfice County]] in [[West Pomeranian Voivodeship]] (since 1999), previously in [[Szczecin Voivodeship]] (1975–1998).
