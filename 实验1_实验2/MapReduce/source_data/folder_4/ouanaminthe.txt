 '''Ouanaminthe''' ([[Haitian Creole|Kréyòl]]: ''Wanament'') is a [[city]] in northeast [[Haiti]]. It is the most important city of the [[Nord-Est Department|Nord-Est]] ''[[Departments of Haiti|Département]]''. In [[Spanish language|Spanish]], the city is called sometimes ''Juana Méndez''. 
 
 The city is part of an ''arrondissement'' (a part of a department) with the same name, along the northern border with the [[Dominican Republic]]. The [[Dajabón River]] is between Ouanaminthe and the [[Dominican Republic|Dominican]] city of [[Dajabón]]. The border crossing to Dajabón is one of the four chief land crossings from Haiti to the Dominican Republic and [[Haiti]]ans living in Ouanaminthe are allowed to cross the border to Dajabón only two days per week.
