 '''''Sicko''''' was a 2007 documentary movie made by [[Michael Moore]]. It explores the American health care system, and compares it to the [[United Kingdom]]'s, [[Canada]]'s, [[France]]'s and [[Cuba]]'s. It made $24.5 million, making it the third-highest grossing documentary of all time.
