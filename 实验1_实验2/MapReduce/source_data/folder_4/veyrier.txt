 '''Veyrier''' is a [[municipality]] of the [[Canton of Geneva]], [[Switzerland]]. It borders [[France]]. About 11,000 people were living in Veyrier in 2015. 
 
 The municipality includes the localities of Pinchat, Vessy and Sierne.
