 '''Bovinae''' is a [[subfamily]] of the [[even-toed ungulate]] [[family (biology)|family]] [[Bovidae]]. About 26 [[species]] of '''''bovines''''' belong to this subfamily. 
 
 Bovines are [[herbivore]]s, and they mostly eat [[grass]].
