 '''Etah''' is a municipality in [[Uttar Pradesh]], India. It is also the district headquarters of [[Etah district]] of Uttar Pradesh [[States and territories of India|state]], [[India]]. The main occupation of the people of the district is agriculture. It is situated between [[Ganga]] and [[Yamuna]]. This region has highly fertile [[alluvial soil]]. 
 
 ==Demographics== 
 Etah had a population of 131,023 according to 2011 Census. Out of this, males were 69,446 and females were 61,577.
