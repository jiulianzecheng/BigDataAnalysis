 '''Besazio''' is a former [[municipality]] of the [[district]] of [[Mendrisio (district)|Mendrisio]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Ticino]] in [[Switzerland]]. On 14 April 2013 the former municipalities of Besazio, [[Ligornetto]] and [[Meride]] merged into the municipality of [[Mendrisio]]. < ref name=Fusion > [http://www.bfs.admin.ch/bfs/portal/de/index/infothek/nomenklaturen/blank/blank/gem_liste/03.html Amtliches Gemeindeverzeichnis der Schweiz] published by the Swiss Federal Statistical Office {{in lang|de}} accessed 19 April 2013 < /ref >
