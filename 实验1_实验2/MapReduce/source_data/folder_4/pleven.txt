 '''Pleven''' ({{lang-bg|Плèвен}}) is a [[List of cities in Bulgaria|city]] in northern [[Bulgaria]]. About 100,000 people lived there. 
 
 The city is known for the [[siege of Plevna]] of 1877 and is a economic center.
