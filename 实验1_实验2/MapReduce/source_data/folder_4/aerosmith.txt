 '''Aerosmith''' is an [[United States|American]] [[Rock music|rock]] [[band]], formed in 1970 in [[Boston, Massachusetts]]. They have released many popular songs, including  " [[Walk This Way]] "  and  " I Don't Want to Miss a Thing " . They have their own [[Guitar Hero (series)|Guitar Hero]] [[video game]] called [[Guitar Hero: Aerosmith]]. Their lead singer is Steven Tyler.
