 '''Cirencester''' is a [[town]] in [[Gloucestershire]] in [[England]]. People first moved there in [[Ancient Rome|Roman]] times. It currently has a population of 19,076. It is 93 miles west of [[London]], and is between [[Swindon]], [[Cheltenham]] and [[Gloucester]]. 
 
 The town is home to the [[Church of St. John the Baptist, Cirencester|Church of St. John the Baptist]] built in the 12th century.
