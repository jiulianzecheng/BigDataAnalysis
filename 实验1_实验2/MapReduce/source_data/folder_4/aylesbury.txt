 '''Aylesbury''' is the [[county town]] of [[Buckinghamshire]] in south central [[England]]. It had a [[population]] in [[2001]] of 65,173. Aylesbury is near [[London]]. Aylesbury was a major [[market town]] in [[Anglo-Saxons|Anglo-Saxon]] times. Aylesbury replaced [[Buckingham]] as the county town of Buckinghamshire in [[1529]] by order of [[Henry VIII of England|King Henry VIII]]. 
 
 It is home to the Roald Dahl Children's Gallery and, since 2010, the 1,200 seat Waterside Theatre.  
 
 It is a popular [[London]] commuter town, thanks to its easy access to the A41, directly leading to the [[M25 motorway|M25]].
