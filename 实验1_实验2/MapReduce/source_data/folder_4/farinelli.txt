 '''Farinelli''' ({{IPA-it|fariˈnɛlli}}; 24 January 1705 – 16 September 1782) was the [[stage name]] of '''Carlo Maria Michelangelo Nicola Broschi''' ({{IPA-it|ˈkarlo ˈbrɔski|pron}}). He was a [[castrato]] singer.
