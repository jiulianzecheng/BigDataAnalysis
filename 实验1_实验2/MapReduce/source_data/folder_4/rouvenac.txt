 '''Rouvenac''' is a former [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is in the [[Regions of France|region]] [[Occitanie]] in the [[Aude]] [[Departments of France|department]] in the south of [[France]]. On 1 January 2019, it was merged into the new commune of [[Val-du-Faby]]. < ref > [https://www.legifrance.gouv.fr/eli/arrete/2018/12/5/TERB1833533A/jo/texte Arrêté préfectoral] 5 December 2018 {{in lang|fr}} < /ref >
