 '''Deinocheiridae''' is a family of [[ornithomimosauria]]n [[dinosaur]]s. The family was originally named by Halszka Osmólska and Roniewicz in 1970, including only the type genus ''[[Deinocheirus]]''. In a 2014 study by Yuong-Nam Lee and others published in the journal ''Nature'', it was found that Deinocheiridae was a valid family. < ref > Bringing back the Deinocheiridae with ''Beishanlong'' and ''Garudimimus'' as added extras - 2014. < /ref >
