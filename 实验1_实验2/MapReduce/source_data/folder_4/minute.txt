A '''minute''' is a customary unit of [[time]]. It is equal to 60 [[second]]s. There are 60 minutes in an [[hour]]. Unlike the second, the minute is not an [[SI]] unit. 
 
 A minute is also {{Frac|60}} of a [[degree (geometry)|degree]].
