 '''''Hideaway''''' is a 1995 [[melodrama]]tic [[horror movie]]. It is about a man who dies during a [[car]] accident. However, this man is revived two hours afterwards. He begins having frightening visions. He thinks he may have become connected to a [[serial killer]]. [[Jeff Goldblum]] plays the accident victim, [[Alicia Silverstone]] plays Regina Harrison and [[Christine Lahti]] plays Lindsey. 
 
 The movie was released March 3, 1995 in the [[United States]]. Reviews were mixed to very negative. It made almost US$3 million below its budget of $15 million.
