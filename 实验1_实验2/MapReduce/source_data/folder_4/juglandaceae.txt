 The '''Juglandaceae''', also known as the Walnut Family, is a family of [[tree]]s, or sometimes [[shrubs]], in the order [[Fagales]]. The [[Leaf|leaves]] are pinnately compound, or ternate, and usually 20-100 cm long. 
 
 There are eight genera in the family, including the commercially important nut-producing trees [[walnut]] (Juglans), [[pecan]] (Carya illinoinensis), and [[hickory]] (Carya).
