 '''Norbury''' is a town in the [[London Borough of Croydon]], on the border with the [[London Borough of Lambeth]] and the [[London Borough of Merton]]. It shares the postcode [[London SW16]] with nearby [[Streatham]]. Norbury is {{convert|6.7|mi|km|1}} south of [[Charing Cross]].
