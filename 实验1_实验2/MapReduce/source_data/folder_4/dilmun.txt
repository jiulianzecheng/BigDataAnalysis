'''Dilmun''' is a land mentioned by [[Mesopotamia|Mesopotamian civilizations]].  It was a trade partner, a source of the metal copper, and an [[entrepôt]] of the Mesopotamia-to-[[Indus Valley Civilization]] trade route. Although the exact location of Dilmun is not known, it was most likely located in the islands of [[Bahrain]] and Failaka island, [[Kuwait]].
