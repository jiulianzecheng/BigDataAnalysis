'''CITES''' (the '''Convention on International Trade in Endangered Species of Wild Fauna and Flora'''. It is also called the '''Washington Convention''') is a multilateral [[treaty]] to protect [[endangered species|endangered]] [[plant]]s and [[animal]]s. [[International Union for Conservation of Nature]] (IUCN) decided to make the treaty in 1963. Countries started signing it on [[3 March]] [[1973]]. CITES started to have effect on 1 July [[1975]].
