 '''Irkutsk''' is a [[city]] in [[Russia]] and the [[Administrative centre|administrative center]] of [[Irkutsk Oblast]]. It was founded in 1661. It is named after the [[Irkut River]].  It is one of the largest cities in [[Siberia]].
