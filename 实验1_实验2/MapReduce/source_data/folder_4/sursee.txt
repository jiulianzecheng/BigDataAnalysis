 '''Sursee''' is a [[city]] of the [[Swiss]] [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Lucerne (canton)|Lucerne]]. It is the capital of the [[district]] of [[Sursee (district)|Sursee]]. 
 
 It is at the northern end of [[Lake Sempach]].
