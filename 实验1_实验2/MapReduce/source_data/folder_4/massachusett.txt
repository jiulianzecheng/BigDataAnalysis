 The '''Massachusett''' were a [[Native Americans in the United States|Native American tribe]] in the [[Indigenous peoples of the Northeastern Woodlands|Northeastern Woodlands]]. They lived around the [[Boston]] area in what is now [[Massachusetts]]. Many Natives died from European diseases like [[smallpox]]. They were one of the first Natives the British colonists met. They were part of the Algonquian language family.
