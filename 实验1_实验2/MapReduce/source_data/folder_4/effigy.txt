 An '''effigy''' is a representation of a specific person, especially in the form of [[sculpture]] or some other three-dimensional medium.  Effigies are common elements in [[art]] of the [[dead]]. They are usually made of stone or metal and placed on a [[tomb]]. < ref >  Temple Church photo gallery [http://www.britainexpress.com/London/Temple_Church_Photo_Gallery3.htm] < /ref >
