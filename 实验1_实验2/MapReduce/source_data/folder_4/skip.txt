 '''Skip''' (written as '''$kip''') is a [[singer-songwriter]] and [[Pop music|pop]] artist. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.newreleasetuesday.com/artistdetail.php?artist_id=3795 |title=$kip Artist Profile}} < /ref >  He is known for his single entitled  " Skippin' "  which is a part from his self-titled debut album. < ref name= " kip, To Release His Debut Album " > {{cite web |url=http://mi2n.com/press.php3?press_nb=151138 |title=$kip, To Release His Debut Album |access-date=2012-02-18 |archive-date=2014-03-13 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140313015551/http://mi2n.com/press.php3?press_nb=151138 |url-status=dead }} < /ref >  The song was a hit in the Philippines, certified gold. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://www.mi2n.com/press.php3?press_nb=151288 |title= " Skippin' "  Is A Certified GOLD |access-date=2012-02-18 |archive-date=2016-03-04 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160304194212/http://www.mi2n.com/press.php3?press_nb=151288 |url-status=dead }} < /ref > 
 
 ==Music career== 
 At the age of 10, $kip performed for live audiences and clubs where people gave him credits for his act. People also rated him performances. One comment lead him to join a local competition and sang  " Benny And The Jets " , but didn't he win. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://skip.me.pn/bio.html |title=$kip Bio }}{{Dead link|date=January 2021 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }} < /ref >  Two days after the competition, a call from his old record label offered him to get signed. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://www.experienceproject.com/stories/Have-A-Career/2072898 |title=$kip's Life: I Have A Career }}{{Dead link|date=January 2021 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }} < /ref >  In 2011, he rejected his label and was forced to move to another one. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://news.hitsquad.com/Artists/kips_LIFE |title=$kip's LIFE }}{{Dead link|date=January 2023 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }} < /ref > 
 
 [[File:$kip Logo.jpg|right|thumb|150px|$kip's Logo launched in 2012]] 
 A week after releasing his first single, < ref > {{cite web |url=http://mi2n.com/press.php3?press_nb=151072 |title=$kip Releases His Debut Single |access-date=2012-02-29 |archive-date=2014-02-01 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140201201743/http://mi2n.com/press.php3?press_nb=151072 |url-status=dead }} < /ref >  the song directly charted in the Philippines where it peaked in the top 8 for 2 straight weeks. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://mi2n.com/press.php3?press_nb=151084 |title=Philippine Charts (Feb. 12-18, 2012) |access-date=2012-02-29 |archive-date=2014-02-01 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20140201201747/http://mi2n.com/press.php3?press_nb=151084 |url-status=dead }} < /ref > < ref > {{cite web |url=http://news.hitsquad.com/Performance/Charts_in_the_Philippines_Feb_19_25_2012 |title=Charts in the Philippines - (Feb. 19-25, 2012) |access-date=2012-02-29 |archive-date=2012-07-18 |archive-url=https://archive.today/20120718132550/http://news.hitsquad.com/Performance/Charts_in_the_Philippines_Feb_19_25_2012 |url-status=dead }} < /ref >  The single was then a certified Gold. According to PARI (Philippine Association of the Record Industry), the single's certification was supposed to be higher,{{cn|date=February 2012}} but all agreed that the single can only make it to Gold. 
 
 Skip has said that his debut album will be released some time in December 2012. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://www.newsalbum.com/Article/87578-kip-Album-to-be-released-this-December-12 |title=$kip-Album-to-be-released-this-December-12 |access-date=2012-02-29 |archive-date=2012-09-07 |archive-url=https://archive.today/20120907075856/http://www.newsalbum.com/Article/87578-kip-Album-to-be-released-this-December-12 |url-status=dead }} < /ref >  In February 2012, he performed his second single from the album, titled  " Clap! Clap! Clap! " . The song directly debuted on the Philippine Charts and there it peaked on number 10. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://news.hitsquad.com/Performance/Philippine_Charts_Feb_26_March_3_2012 |title=Philippine Charts - (Feb. 26 - March 3, 2012) |access-date=February 29, 2012 |archive-date=July 11, 2012 |archive-url=https://archive.today/20120711114443/http://news.hitsquad.com/Performance/Philippine_Charts_Feb_26_March_3_2012 |url-status=dead }} < /ref >
