 '''DENK''' (the Dutch word  " denk "  means ''think'') is a political party in the [[Netherlands]]. The official name is ''Political Movement Denk'' (Dutch:  " Politieke Beweging Denk " ). 
 
 DENK aims at the interests of non-western citizens, and was established in 2015. 
 
 Number of seats in the [[Dutch general election, 2021|2021 elections]]: 3 (0).
