The term '''ministry''' could mean: 
 
 *[[Ministry (government department)]], a department of a government 
 *[[Ministry (collective executive)]] (often ''the'' Ministry), the complete body of government [[minister (government)|minister]]s (whether or not they are in cabinet) under the leadership of a prime minister 
 **The Ministry, also known as the [[Aireacht]], the cabinet of the government established by the revolutionary Irish Republic of 1919-1922 
 *[[Religious ministry]], a type of activity conducted by members of various faiths 
 *[[Ministry (band)]], an American industrial metal band 
 *[[Ministry of Sound]], a well known nightclub in London, sometimes referred to as the '''Ministry''' 
 *[[Ministry of Darkness]], a professional wrestling faction in the WWE 
 *[[Ministry (magazine)|''Ministry'' (magazine)]], a magazine for pastors published by the Seventh-day Adventist Church
