 '''Aylostera''' is a [[genus]] of [[cactus]] that has two [[subgenus|subgenera]]. They are Aylostera and Mediolobivia. It is not certain which plants are members of this genus and which are part of ''[[Rebutia]]''. < ref > Mosti  &  Papini [http://www.pakbs.org/pjbot/PDFs/43(6)/21.pdf Further Insight and New Combinations in Aylostera (Cactaceae) Based on Molecular and Morphological Data] < /ref >
