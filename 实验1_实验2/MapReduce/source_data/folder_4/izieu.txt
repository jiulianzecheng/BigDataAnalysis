'''Izieu''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is found in the [[Regions of France|region]] [[Auvergne-Rhône-Alpes]] in the [[Ain]] [[Departments of France|department]] in the east of [[France]]. 
 
 It lies on the [[Rhône River]] between the cities of [[Lyon]] and [[Chambéry]].
