 The '''Cheetoh''' is a [[Cat breeds|breed]] of [[cat]]. It is a cross breed between [[ocicat]] and [[Bengal cat|bengal]]. In November 2004, cheetoh was first recognized by the United Feline Organization. < ref > {{Cite web|date=2014-10-25|title=www.unitedfelineorganization.net|url=http://www.unitedfelineorganization.net/|access-date=2023-10-25|website=web.archive.org|archive-date=2014-10-25|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20141025030919/http://www.unitedfelineorganization.net/|url-status=dead}} < /ref >  On June 15, 2011, cheetoh was also recognized by [[The International Cat Association|TICA]], with the status of  " experimental breed " . < ref name= " :0 " > {{Cite web|title=Cheetoh Info Page|url=http://www.cheetohcatbreeders.com/CheetohInfo1.htm|access-date=2023-10-25|website=www.cheetohcatbreeders.com}} < /ref > 
 
 == Appearance == 
 Cheetoh has a larger body than its ancestors. It is muscular, graceful and slender, with males weighing around 15-23 pounds(6.8-10.4 kg), and females over 15 pounds(6.8 kg). Cheetoh has a short coat with a spotted pattern, giving it a wild appearance, but it is not nature wild. < ref name= " :0 "  / > < ref name= " :1 " > {{Cite web|last=Cats.com|date=2021-09-25|title=Cheetoh|url=https://cats.com/cat-breeds/cheetoh|access-date=2023-10-25|website=Cats.com|language=en-US}} < /ref > 
 
 == Behavior == 
 Cheetohs are affectionate, friendly, kind and aggressive cats. They don't like to search food, so cheetohs prefer to eat food given by their owners. < ref name= " :0 "  / >  Cheetohs are highly intelligent, and social. < ref name= " :0 "  / > < ref name= " :1 "  / >
