 '''Gampel''' is a village and former [[municipality]] in the [[district]] of [[Leuk (district)|Leuk]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Valais]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 On 1 January 2009, the former municipalities of Gampel and [[Bratsch]] merged into the new municipality of [[Gampel-Bratsch]].
