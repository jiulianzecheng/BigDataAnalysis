 '''Samdari''' is a tehsil of [[Barmer district]] in [[Rajasthan]], northern [[India]]. 
 
 ==Demographics== 
 
 According to the Indian Census 2011, the population of Samdari is 25012, where male population is 12805 and female population is 12207. < ref > [http://ourvillageindia.org/Place.aspx?PID=456448Samdari Population] < /ref > 
 
 == Transport == 
 
 In 2007 is a [[break of gauge]] junction station on [[Indian Railways]].  [[Broad gauge]] lines go to [[Munabao]] and [[Jodhpur]], while a [[metre gauge]] line subject to conversion goes to [[Bhildi]]. < ref > [[Railway Gazette International]] Supplement December 2007 p7 < /ref >   Now broad gauge has completed and passenger train has started from 16 October 2010.
