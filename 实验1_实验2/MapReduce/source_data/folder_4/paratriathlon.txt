 '''Paratriathlon''' is a variant of the [[triathlon]] for athletes with a physical disability. The sport is governed by the [[International Triathlon Union]](ITU), and was first held as a [[Paralympics|Paralympic]] event at the [[2016 Summer Paralympics]] in [[Rio de Janeiro]], [[Brazil]].
