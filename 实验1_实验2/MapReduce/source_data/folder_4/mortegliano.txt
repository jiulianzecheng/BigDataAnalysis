 '''Mortegliano''' ({{lang-fur|Mortean}}) is a ''[[comune]]'' (municipality) in the [[Italy|Italian]] region [[Friuli-Venezia Giulia]]. It is located about 60 & nbsp;km northwest of [[Trieste]] and about 14 & nbsp;km southwest of [[Udine]].
