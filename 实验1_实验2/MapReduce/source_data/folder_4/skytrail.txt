 The '''Skytrail Bridge''' is a bridge in [[Outlook, Saskatchewan]] that crosses the [[South Saskatchewan River]]. Today, it is a [[pedestrian]] [[bridge]], but it was a [[railway]] until [[1987]]. It is now the longest pedestrian bridge in Canada. < ref > {{Cite web |url=http://www.town.outlook.sk.ca/skytrail/skytrail-2.html |title=SkyTrail - Canada's Longest Pedestrian Bridge |access-date=2011-04-21 |archive-date=2011-06-28 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110628142721/http://town.outlook.sk.ca/skytrail/skytrail-2.html |url-status=dead }} < /ref >
