 '''Sneakernet''' in [[informatics]] is an informal and [[Irony|ironical]] word for tranport of electronical information over physical memory devices (such as [[USB flash drive]]) instead of [[Computer network|computer networks]]. 
 
 The word is a word play, as a net(work) with reference to wearing of [[Athletic shoe|sneakers]], which is a type of shoes.
