 '''KWUT''' (97.7 [[FM broadcasting|FM]]) is a [[radio station]] broadcasting a [[Country music]] format. Licensed to [[Elsinore, Utah]], USA, the station is currently owned by Douglas Barton, through licensee Sanpete County Broadcasting Company. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.fcc.gov/fcc-bin/fmq?call=KWUT |title=KWUT Facility Record |work=United States [[Federal Communications Commission]], audio division }} < /ref > 
 
 ==History== 
 The station went on the air as KRFD-FM on November 9, 1998. On September 11, 1998, the station changed its call sign to KSGI and on November 1, 1999 to KRFD. On November 25, 2000, the station became KACE, and KMGR by December 31, 2000. On November 15, 2001 it switched back to KACE and back to KRFD on May 29, 2001. The station became KLGL on June 16, 2001. On August 8, 2005, the station flipped to KCYQ. It then flipped in the fall of 2010, to the current calls. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://fjallfoss.fcc.gov/cgi-bin/ws.exe/prod/cdbs/pubacc/prod/call_hist.pl?Facility_id=137373 & Callsign=KWUT |title=KWUT Call Sign History |work=United States [[Federal Communications Commission]], audio division }} < /ref >
