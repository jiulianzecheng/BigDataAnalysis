 '''Birgisch''' was a [[municipality]] in the district of [[Brig (district)|Brig]] in the [[cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Valais]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 On 1 January 2013 the former municipalities of Birgisch and [[Mund]] merged into the municipality of [[Naters]].
