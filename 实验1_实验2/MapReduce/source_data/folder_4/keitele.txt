 '''Keitele''' is a [[municipality]] in [[Northern Savonia]], [[Finland]]. In January 2014, 2,430 people lived there. The municipalities next to it are [[Pielavesi]], [[Pihtipudas]], [[Tervo]], [[Vesanto]] and [[Viitasaari]]. 
 
 The municipality was established in 1879, but camp sites been found from the 1300s. 
 
 == Villages == 
 Hamula, Koutajärvi, Kukertaja, Leppäselkä, Pahkamäki, Petäjäkylä, Sulkavanjärvi, Tossavanlahti, Viinikkala, Vuonamo, Äyräpää and Hemminki.
