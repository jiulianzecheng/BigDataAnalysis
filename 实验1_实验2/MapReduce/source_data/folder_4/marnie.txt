 '''''Marnie''''' is a 1964 American [[mystery movie|mystery]] [[drama movie]] directed by [[Alfred Hitchcock]] and based on [[Marnie (novel)|the novel of the same name]]. It starred [[Tippi Hedren]] and [[Sean Connery]]. It tells the story of Marnie, a compulsive [[thief]] and [[liar]], played by Hedren. She is befriended by and later marries a rich businessman called Mark Rutland, played by Connery. He tries to find out more about the tragic events which took place in her past and finally forces her to face up to these events and the secret which her mother kept from her since she was a child.
