{{nihongo|'''''Einin'''''|永仁}} was a {{nihongo|[[Japanese era name]]|年号,|''[[nengō]]'',|lit.  " [[year]] name " }} after ''[[Shōō (Kamakura period)|Shōō]]'' and before ''[[Shōan]]''.  This period started in August 1293 and ended in April 1299. < ref > [[Louis-Frédéric|Nussbaum, Louis-Frédéric]]. (2005). [https://books.google.com/books?id=p2QnPijAEmEC & q=Bunei & pg=PA171   " ''Einin'' " ] in ''Japan encyclopedia'', p. 171. < /ref >  During this time, the emperors were {{nihongo|[[Emperor Fushimi|Fushimi]]''-[[tennō]]''|伏見天皇}} and {{nihongo|[[Emperor Go-Fushimi|Go-Fushimi]]''-tennō''|後伏見天皇}}. < ref > [[Isaac Titsingh|Titsingh, Isaac]]. (1834). [https://books.google.com/books?id=18oNAAAAIAAJ & pg=PA269 ''Annales des empereurs du japon,'' p. 269-274]; [[H. Paul Varley|Varley, H. Paul]]. (1980). ''Jinnō Shōtōki.'' pp. 237-238. < /ref >   
 
 ==Events of the ''Einen'' era== 
 * '''1293''' < !-- May 27 -- >  (''Einin 1''):  Major [[wikt:earthquake| earthquake]] and [[tsunami]] hit [[Sagami Bay]] and [[Kamakura, Kanagawa|Kamakura]], killing 20,000+ people. < ref > Titsingh, [https://books.google.com/books?id=18oNAAAAIAAJ & pg=PA273 p. 273]; NOAA Earthquake Database, [http://www.ngdc.noaa.gov/nndc/struts/results?eq_0=494 & t=101650 & s=18 & d=99,91,95,93 & nd=display  Tsunami event, 1293]; retrieved 2012-4-29. < /ref >    
 * '''1298''' (''Einin 6, 7th month''): Emperor Fushimi [[abdication|abdicated]] in the 11th year of his [[wikt:reign|reign]]; and his son was named to be the new emperor. < ref > Titsingh, [https://books.google.com/books?id=18oNAAAAIAAJ & pg=PA274  p. 274]; Varley, p. 44; a distinct act of ''senso'' is unrecognized prior to [[Emperor Tenji]]; and all sovereigns except [[Empress Jitō|Jitō]],  [[Emperor Yōzei|Yōzei]], [[Emperor Go-Toba|Go-Toba]], and [[Emperor Fushimi|Fushimi]] have ''senso'' and ''sokui'' in the same year until the reign of [[Emperor Go-Murakami]]. Compare ''Kunaichō'', [http://www.kunaicho.go.jp/e-about/seido/seido06-ph.html  Ceremony of Accession (''Sokui-no-Rei'')]; retrieved 2012-6-29. < /ref > 
 * '''1299''' (''Einin 7''): Emperor Go-Fushimi is [[enthronement|enthroned]] and the ''[[nengō]]'' was changed. < ref > Titsingh, [https://books.google.com/books?id=18oNAAAAIAAJ & pg=PA274  p. 274]; Varley, p. 238. < /ref >
