 '''Kiuruvesi''' is a town in [[Northern Savonia]], [[Finland]]. As of 28 February 2023, 7,578 people lived there. Neighbouring municipalities are [[Iisalmi]], [[Pielavesi]], [[Pyhäjärvi]], [[Pyhäntä]] and [[Vieremä]].
