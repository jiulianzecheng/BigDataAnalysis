 '''Heliconia''' is a [[genus (biology)|genus]] of flowering [[plant]]s. They are native tropical [[South America]], as well as the [[Pacific|Pacific Ocean Islands]] west to [[Indonesia]]. There are between 100 and 200 [[species]] in the genus. Other common names include '''lobster-claw''', '''wild plantain''' and '''false bird-of-paradise'''.  
 
 The plants have very decorative flowers. For this reason they are often sold decorative plants.
