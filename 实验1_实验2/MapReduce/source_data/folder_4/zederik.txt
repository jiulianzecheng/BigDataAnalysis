 '''Zederik''' is a former [[List of municipalities of the Netherlands|municipality]] in the [[Netherlands|Dutch]] [[Provinces of the Netherlands|province]] of [[South Holland]]. 
 
 == History == 
 It was created on 1 January 1986 from the former municipalities of [[Ameide]], [[Hei- en Boeicop]], [[Leerbroek]], [[Lexmond]], [[Meerkerk]], [[Nieuwland, Vijfheerenlanden|Nieuwland]], and [[Tienhoven aan de Lek]]. Zederik merged with [[Leerdam]] and [[Vianen]] to form the new municipality of [[Vijfheerenlanden]] in the province of [[Utrecht (province)|Utrecht]] on 1 January 2019.
