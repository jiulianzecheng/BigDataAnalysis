 " '''Taxman''' "  is a [[song]] by English [[rock music|rock]] band [[the Beatles]]. It was first released on their 1966 album ''[[Revolver (Beatles album)|Revolver]]''.{{sfn|Lewisohn|2005|p=84}} It was written by [[George Harrison]], with some help from [[John Lennon]]. It is about a [[tax collector]] taking a lot of money from people. Harrison wrote it because he was being taxed a lot because of [[Harold Wilson]]'s [[Labour Party (UK)|Labour]] government.{{sfn|Turner|2016|p=160}} The song was recorded a month after Labour won the [[1966 United Kingdom general election|1966 general election]].{{sfn|Winn|2009|pp=12–13}}
