 '''Schaan''' ({{IPA-de|ʃaːn}}) is the largest city of [[Liechtenstein]] by population and area. It is located to the north of [[Vaduz]], the capital, in the central part of the country. As of 2005 it has a population of 5,806. < ref > [https://archive.today/20121216160936/http://www.world-gazetteer.com/wg.php?x= & men=gpro & lng=en & des=gamelan & dat=200 & geo=-126 & srt=pnan & col=aohdqcfbeimg & pt=c & va= & geo=493830951 World Gazetteer: Liechtenstein] < /ref >  This makes it the largest district in Liechtenstein, and covers an area of 26.8 & nbsp;km², including mountains and forest.
