 '''Spiekeroog''' is one of the [[East Frisian Islands]], off the [[North Sea]] coast of [[Germany]]. It is between [[Langeoog]] to its west and [[Wangerooge]] to its east. The island belongs to the municipality of the [[Wittmund (district)|district of Wittmund]], in [[Lower Saxony]], [[Germany]]. The only village on the island is also called Spiekeroog. 
 
 Spiekeroog covers an area of {{convert|18.25|km2|abbr=off}}, the shortest distance to the land is {{convert|5.7|km|abbr=off}}. The island is - with the exception of fire and [[rescue]] vehicles - free of cars. A daily [[ferry]] service with the harbour of Neuharlingersiel connects the island with the German mainland.
