 {{Nihongo|'''Hirosaki'''|弘前市|Hirosaki-shi}} is a [[Japan]]ese [[city]] in [[Aomori Prefecture]] on the island of [[Honshu]]. < ref name= " nussbaum319 " > Nussbaum, Louis-Frédéric. (2005). [https://books.google.com/books?id=p2QnPijAEmEC & pg=PA319  " Hirosaki, " ] ''Japan Encyclopedia'', p. 319. < /ref > 
 
 ==History== 
 Hirosaki was a [[castle town]] (城下町) of the Tsugaru clan. < ref > Appert, Georges. (1888). [https://books.google.com/books?id=4ngLAAAAIAAJ & pg=PA79 ''Ancien Japon.'' p. 79]. < /ref >  The town grew up around Hirosaki Castle, which was built in 1601. < ref name= " nussbaum319 " / > 
 
 ===City emblem=== 
 Hirosaki uses the [[swastika]] (''manji'' ) as official symbol. This came from the flag emblem of the Tsugaru clan in [[Edo period]].{{fact|date=September 2012}} 
 
 ==Geography== 
 Hirosaki is located in the southwest part of Aomori Prefecture. It is in the south of [[Mount Iwaki]]; and it is along the border with [[Akita Prefecture]].
