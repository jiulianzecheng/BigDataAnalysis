 '''Ikorodu''' is a city and local government area in [[Lagos State]], [[Nigeria]]. It is located along the [[Lagos Lagoon]] and shares a boundary with [[Ogun State]]. 
 
 ==Etymology== 
 The word '''Ikorodu''' was derived from two [[Yoruba language|Yoruba]] words ''Oko'' (''[[English language|English]]: farm'') and ''Odu'' (''[[English language|English]]: a plant that blackens''). < ref name= " EllulZlatanova2013 " > {{cite book|author1=Claire Ellul |author2= Sisi Zlatanova |author3= Massimo Rumor |author4=Robert Laurini |title=Urban and Regional Data Management: UDMS Annual 2013 |url= https://books.google.com/books?id=BtDLBQAAQBAJ & pg=PA146 |date= 23 May 2013 |publisher=CRC Press|isbn=978-1-315-88523-0|pages=146–}} < /ref >
