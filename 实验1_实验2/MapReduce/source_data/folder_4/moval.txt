 '''Moval''' is a former [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is found in the region [[Bourgogne-Franche-Comté]] in the [[Territoire de Belfort]] department in  the northeast of [[France]]. On 1 January 2019, it was merged into the new commune of [[Meroux-Moval]]. < ref > [http://www.territoire-de-belfort.gouv.fr/content/download/19942/149225/file/recueil-90-2018-063-Publié%20le%2021-12-2018.pdf Arrêté préfectoral] {{in lang|fr}} < /ref >
