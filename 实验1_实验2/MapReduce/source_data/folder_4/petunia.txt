 '''''Petunia''''' is a [[genus]] of 20 [[species]] < ref name=tpl > {{cite web|title=The plant list: Petunia|url=http://www.theplantlist.org/tpl1.1/search?q=petunia|access-date=17 March 2018|publisher=Royal Botanic Garden Kew and Missouri Botanic Garden|archive-date=1 April 2023|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20230401095517/http://www.theplantlist.org/tpl1.1/search?q=Petunia|url-status=dead}} < /ref >  of [[plant]]s of [[South America]]n origin. All of them bear [[flower]]s. They are in the [[Nightshade]] [[family (biology)|family]], and are related to [[tobacco]]. Many people grow them in the garden. Petunias are usually used as [[annual plant]]s, because they die when the weather becomes cold. They grow best when they are planted in the sun.
