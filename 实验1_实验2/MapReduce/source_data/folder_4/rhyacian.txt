The '''Rhyacian''' is the second [[geology|geological]] [[Period (geology)|period]] in the [[Palaeoproterozoic]] that began 2.3 [[billion]] years ago, and ended at 2.05 billion years ago. Before it was the [[Siderian]] It is followed by the [[Orosirian]]. 
 
 The [[Huronian glaciation|Huronian]] (Makganyene) global glaciation began at the start of the Rhyacian.  The first known [[eukaryote]]s began to evolve in the Rhyacian period.
