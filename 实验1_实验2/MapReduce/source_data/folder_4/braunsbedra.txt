 '''Braunsbedra''' is a town in the [[Saalekreis]], [[Saxony-Anhalt]] in [[Germany]]. It is about 11 km southwest of [[Merseburg]]. It became a town in [[1945]] when Braunsdorf and Bedra were combined.
