 '''Siddaramaiah''' (born 12 August 1948) < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.cmkarnataka.gov.in/biography-kannada.html|title=ಬಾಳಪಯಣ|access-date=25 March 2016|language=kn|trans-title=Biography|archive-date=30 March 2016|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160330022902/http://www.cmkarnataka.gov.in/biography-kannada.html|url-status=dead}} < /ref >  is an [[Indian people|Indian]] politician. He was born in present-day [[Karnataka]], India. He is a member of the [[Indian National Congress]]. He was the Chief Minister of Karnataka between 2013 and 2018.
