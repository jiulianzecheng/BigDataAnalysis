 '''Marimekko''' is a [[textile]] [[Fashion design|design]] company. It was started in 1951 in [[Finland]]. It is famous for its original [[Printmaking|prints]], for instance, prints of red and blue flowers. The company makes high-quality items, not only furnishing fabrics to tableware but also clothes, bags and other accessories. Its fabrics have been introduced inside the airplanes of [[Finnair]].
