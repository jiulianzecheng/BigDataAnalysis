 '''Riau''' is a [[province]] of [[Indonesia]]. It is on the central [[east]]ern [[coast]] of [[Sumatra]]. Its [[capital city]] is [[Pekanbaru]]. Riau is rich in [[natural resources]]. < ref > Lesley Potter; et al., ''The Effects of Indonesia's Decentralisation on Forest and Estate Crops in Riau Province'' (Bogor: Center for International Forestry Research, 2001), p. 17 < /ref >  It has [[petroleum]], [[natural gas]], [[rubber]], and [[palm oil]].
