 '''Veytaux''' is a [[municipality]] of the [[district]] of [[Riviera-Pays-d'Enhaut (district)|Riviera-Pays-d'Enhaut]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Vaud]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 The [[Chillon Castle|Chateau of Chillon]] provides a marvelous view of the entire [[Lake Geneva|Lake of Geneva]] in Avenue de Chillon.
