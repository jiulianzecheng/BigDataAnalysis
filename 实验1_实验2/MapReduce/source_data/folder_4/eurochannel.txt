 '''Eurochannel''' is a world television channel focused on European culture and lifestyle through movies, series and other programs dedicated to [[European culture]]. Eurochannel broadcasts in original audio with subtitles in English, Spanish and [[Portuguese language|Portuguese]]. 
 
 Eurochannel is available in [[Hispanic America]], France, [[Brazil]], United States, Canada, the [[Caribbean]], [[Angola]], [[Mozambique]], [[Portugal]] and [[North Macedonia]]. 
 
 In United States, Eurochannel is available on Google Play on all Android devices. 
 
 ==Current programming== 
 
 Eurochannel's programming includes mostly cinema, television series and music programs.
