 '''Pointvillers''' is a former [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is in [[Bourgogne-Franche-Comté]] in the [[Doubs]] [[Departments of France|department]] in east [[France]]. On 1 January 2017, it was merged into the new commune of [[Le Val, Doubs|Le Val]]. < ref > [https://www.legifrance.gouv.fr/eli/arrete/2016/11/23/INTB1634218A/jo/texte Arrêté préfectoral] 23 November 2016 {{in lang|fr}} < /ref >
