 '''SafetySuit''' (or '''Safety Suit''' and '''Safetysuit''') is an [[United States|American]] [[rock music|rock]] band formed in [[2008]]. They are famous for the hit singles ''[[Someone Like You (SafetySuit song)|Someone Like You]]'' and ''[[Stay]]''. Safety Suit has released only 1 hit studio album called [[Life Left to Go]] in July [[2008]].
