 '''Escitalopram''' is an [[antidepressant]] [[medication]]. It is used as a [[drug]] to help treat [[Depression (mental illness)|depression]], [[obsessive-compulsive disorder]], [[social anxiety disorder]] and other [[mental illness]]es. It is commonly known and sold as ''Lexapro'' or ''Cipralex''. < ref > [https://www.washingtonpost.com/wp-dyn/content/article/2009/01/29/AR2009012901774.html Lexapro article] < /ref > 
 
 == Side Effects == 
 Escitalopram can cause sleeping problems, dry mouth and [[diarrhea]]. It can also cause sexual problems. < ref > [http://www.frx.com/pi/lexapro_pi.pdf Lexapro prescribing information] < /ref >  These sexual problems usually disappear after a person stops using the drug. However, in some cases these problems can last for months or years.
