In [[Greek mythology]], the '''Nesoi''' were the goddesses of islands. Each island was said to have human qualities. These were labeled as one of the [[List of figures in Greek mythology#Primordial deities|Greek primordial deities.]] From [[Callimachus]], the Nesoi were thought to be [[Ourea]] who were put under the sea when [[Poseidon]] was angry.
