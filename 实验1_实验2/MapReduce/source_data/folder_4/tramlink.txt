 '''Tramlink''' (also called '''Croydon Tramlink''') is a [[tram]]way system in south [[London]] in the [[United Kingdom]] which began operation in May 2000. The service is operated by [[First London]] on behalf of [[Transport for London]] (TfL). The TfL team responsible for Tramlink is called London Tramlink, matching London Buses, London Underground, and so on.
