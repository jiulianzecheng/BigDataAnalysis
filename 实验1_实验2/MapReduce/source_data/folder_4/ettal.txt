 '''Ettal''' is a [[municipality]] in the district of [[Garmisch-Partenkirchen (district)|Garmisch-Partenkirchen]] in [[Upper Bavaria]] in [[Germany]]. 
 
 == Districts == 
 Ettal has five districts: 
 
 * Dickelschwaig 
 * Ettal 
 * Graswang 
 * Linderhof 
 * Rahm
