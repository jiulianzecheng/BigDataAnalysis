 '''Santaworld''' ({{lang-sv|Tomteland}}) is a [[Santa Claus]]-based [[amusement park|theme park]]. It is based upon the myth that Sweden is the place for [[Santa's workshop]]. It is located on the [[Gesunda Mountain]] south of [[Mora]] in [[Sweden]]. It was opened on 8 December 1984. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.skandiahistoria.se/engagemanget/forsakrad-tomtebolycka/|title=Försäkrad tomtebolycka|publisher=Skandia|language=Swedish|accessdate=7 February 2015|archive-date=26 March 2016|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160326030035/http://skandiahistoria.se/engagemanget/forsakrad-tomtebolycka/|url-status=dead}} < /ref >
