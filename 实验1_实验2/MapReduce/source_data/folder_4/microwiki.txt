 '''MicroWiki''' is a [[free content|free]] [[online encyclopedia]] [[website]]. It was started on 27 May 2005 to make [[micronation|micronational]] pages with more information than [[Wikipedia]] can with its current rules. < ref name= " Independent " > {{cite web |last1=Simon |first1=Usborne |title=MicroCon 2015: Dictators of the world unite at world summit of micronations - countries too small to count |url=https://www.independent.co.uk/news/world/politics/microcon-2015-dictators-world-unite-world-summit-micronations-countries-too-small-count-10174346.html |work=[[The Independent]] |date=13 April 2015 |access-date=11 May 2021}} < /ref >  MicroWiki calls itself the  " largest encyclopaedia on micronations " . < ref name= " Shima " > {{cite journal |last1=Hayward |first1=Philip |last2=Khamis |first2=Susie |date=2015 |title=Fleeting and Partial Autonomy: A historical account of quasi-micronational initiatives on Lundy Island and their contemporary reconfiguration on MicroWiki.|url=https://opus.lib.uts.edu.au/bitstream/10453/119111/1/g.-Hayward- & -Khamis-Shima-v9n1-69-84.pdf |journal=Shima: The International Journal of Island Cultures|volume=9 |page=78|issn=1834-6057|access-date=15 April 2021}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite web |last=Esteban |first=Cristina Fernández |date=2020-09-08 |title=11 micronaciones absurdas en las que puedes solicitar ciudadanía o incluso tener un título nobiliario |url=https://www.businessinsider.es/11-micronaciones-absurdas-puedes-solicitar-ciudadania-706215 |access-date=2022-03-15 |website=Business Insider España |language=es}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite web |last=B |first=Alvaro Lopez |date=2017-02-09 |title=Micronaciones: ¿cómo son y cuál es su historia? |url=https://www.eldefinido.cl/actualidad/plazapublica/8115/Micronaciones-como-son-y-cual-es-su-historia/ |access-date=2022-03-15 |website=El Definido}} < /ref >
