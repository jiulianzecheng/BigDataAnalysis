[[Image:Reeperbahn.jpg|thumb|right|A typical view of the sex-shops at the Reeperbahn]] 
 The '''Reeperbahn''' is the central street in the district [[St.Pauli]] in [[Hamburg]]. Parts of it are considered to be a [[red-light district]], but there are also [[theatre]]s and [[cinema]]s near it.
