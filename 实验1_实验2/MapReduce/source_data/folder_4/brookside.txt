 '''''Brookside''''' is a [[British]] [[soap opera]], set and shot in [[Liverpool]], [[Merseyside]], [[England]]. The series began on the launch night of [[Channel 4]] on 2 November 1982 and ran for 21 years until 4 November 2003. Its cast included [[Dean Sullivan]], [[Alex Fletcher]], [[Anna Friel]], [[Paul Usher]], [[Sue Johnston]], [[Claire Sweeney]], [[Ricky Tomlinson]], [[Ray Quinn]] and others.
