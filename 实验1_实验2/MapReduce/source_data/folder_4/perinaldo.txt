 '''Perinaldo''' is a ''[[comune]]'' in the [[Province of Imperia]] in the [[Liguria]] region in [[Italy]]. 
 
 The 17th-century Italian naturalised French astronomer [[Giovanni Domenico Cassini]] was born here.
