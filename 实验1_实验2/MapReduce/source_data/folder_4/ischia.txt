 '''Ischia''' is an island in the [[Tyrrhenian Sea]]. The island is at the northern end of the [[Gulf of Naples]], about {{convert|30|km|0|abbr=off}} from [[Naples]]. It is the largest of the [[Phlegrean Islands]]. 
 
 [[Ischia, Campania|Ischia]] is the name of the main ''[[comune]]'' of the island.
