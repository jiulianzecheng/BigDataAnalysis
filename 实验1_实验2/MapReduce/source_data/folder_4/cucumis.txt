'''''Cucumis''''' is a genus of [[Vine|twining]], [[tendril]]-bearing plants in the [[Family (biology)|family]] [[Cucurbitaceae]] which includes the [[cucumber]] (''Cucumis sativus''), [[Muskmelon|muskmelons]] (''Cucumis melo'', including cantaloupe and  [[Honeydew (melon)|honeydew]]), the horned [[melon]] (''[[Cucumis metuliferus]]''), and the West Indian small pickle (''[[Cucumis anguria]]'').
