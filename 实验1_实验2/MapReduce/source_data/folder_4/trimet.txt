TriMet is the transit system in [[Portland, Oregon|Portland Oregon]]. It was formed in 1969, and has since become the primary transit provider in the Portland Metropolitan Area.  
 
 == Service == 
 TriMet has 3 main modes of transit < ref > {{Cite web|last=TriMet|title=Plan and track your trip on TriMet|url=https://trimet.org/home/|access-date=2022-11-15|website=TriMet|language=en}} < /ref > 
 
 * [[Bus]] service: 85 local, express, and bus rapid transit lines. 
 * [[Light rail]]: 5 MAX light rail lines. 
 * [[Commuter rail]]: the WES commuter rail line. 
 
 TriMet also runs paratransit service.
