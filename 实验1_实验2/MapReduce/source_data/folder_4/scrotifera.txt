 '''Scrotifera''' is a [[clade|group of living things]] in the [[class (biology)|class]] [[Mammalia]]. < ref name=EoL > {{cite web|accessdate=June 6, 2021|url=https://eol.org/pages/42399419|publisher=Encyclopedia of Life|title=Scrotifera}} < /ref >  It is a proposed clade, meaning that not all scientists use it when they talk about how living things are related to each other. 
 
 The [[order (biology)|orders]] [[Carnivora]] (carnivores), [[Chiroptera]] (bats), [[Perissodactyla]] ([[ungulate|hoofed animals]] with odd numbers of toes, for example horses and [[rhino]]s), [[Cetartiodactyla]] (hoofed animals with even numbers of toes), [[Philodota]] ([[pangolin]]s), and [[Meridiungulata]] are all in Scrotifera. < ref name=EoL / >
