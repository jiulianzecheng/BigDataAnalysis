'''Fibromyalgia''' is a condition with chronic [[pain]]. The exact causes are not known. However, symptoms are not totally restricted to the pain. There are also sleep disturbances, joint stiffness and severe fatigue. The problem sometimes exists along with [[Depression (mood)|depression]], stress and [[anxiety]]. Fibromyalgia affects mostly women, though men can also be affected. Adults are usually the victims, although [[child]]ren may also be affected.
