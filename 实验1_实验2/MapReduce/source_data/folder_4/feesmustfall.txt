 '''Fees Must Fall''' was a student protest to ask universities to drop fee hikes in [[South Africa]] and [[Namibia]] between 2015 and 2017. It was led student activists mainly from political parties, youth movements and student organisations. < ref > [http://www.publicnewshub.com/fees-must-fall-protests-believed-to-have-led-to-professor-bongani-mayosis-depression-and-suicide/ Fees Must Fall Protests Believed to Have Led to Professor Bongani Mayosi's Depression and Suicide |] The Public News Hub < /ref > < ref > [https://www.iol.co.za/news/africa/namibia-students-fight-demand-to-pay-their-fees-2081075 | Namibia students fight demand to pay their fees |] IOL News < /ref > < ref > [https://www.namibian.com.na/index.php?page=archive-read & id=146471 | NUST fees fall -] The Namibian < /ref > < ref > [https://www.bbc.com/news/world-africa-37417402 | South African police clash with students over fees -] BBC News < /ref > < ref > [https://www.nytimes.com/2016/09/23/world/africa/fees-must-fall-anatomy-of-the-student-protests-in-south-africa.html | ‘Fees Must Fall’: Anatomy of the Student Protests in South Africa] The New York Times < /ref >
