 '''''Amphicoelias''''' is an extinct [[genus]] of [[sauropod]] [[dinosaur]]. 
 
 The fossil was found in the [[Morrison Formation]]. < ref name= " foster-appendix " > Foster J. 2007. Appendix in ''Jurassic West: the dinosaurs of the Morrison Formation and their world''. Indiana University Press. pp. 327–329. < /ref >  It lived about 150 million years ago during the [[Upper Jurassic]] of what is now [[Colorado]].  
 
 A herbivore, ''Amphicoelias'' was moderately sized at about 25 m (82 ft) long–roughly the same length as ''[[Diplodocus]]'', to which it was related.
