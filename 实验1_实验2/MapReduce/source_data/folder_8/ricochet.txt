 '''''Ricochet''''' is a 1991 American action crime thriller movie. It stars [[Denzel Washington]]. [[John Lithgow]] and [[Ice-T]]. The movie is about a psychotic ex-convict named Blake who has been creating chaos and looking for revenge. This movie was released in the United States on October 4, 1991.
