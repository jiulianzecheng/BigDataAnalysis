 '''Udaipur''', also called the  " City of Lakes "  or the  " Kashmir of Rajasthan, "  is a major city, [[List of municipal corporations in India|municipal corporation]] and the administrative headquarters of the [[Udaipur district]] in the [[States and territories of India|Indian state]] of [[Rajasthan]]. < ref > {{cite web|title=Udaipur City Census 2011 data|url=http://www.census2011.co.in/census/city/92-udaipur.html|website=census2011.co.in|publisher=Census Population 2015 Data|access-date=31 August 2015}} < /ref >  It is the historic capital of the kingdom of [[Mewar]] in the former [[Rajputana Agency]]. It was founded in 1553 by [[Maharana  Udai Singh]] of the [[Sisodia]] clan of [[Rajput]], < ref name= " UDAIPUR 2014 " > UDAIPUR: Since 1553 CE!-Its Founding  &  a Concise Photo Fact-File. Ranawat, P. S., 2014. {{ISBN|978-81-929881-0-8}}. Kumbha Exclusives, India. < /ref >  when he shifted his capital from the city of [[Chittorgarh]] to Udaipur.
