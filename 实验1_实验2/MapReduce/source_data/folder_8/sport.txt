 '''Sport''' is commonly defined as an athletic activity that involves a degree of [[competition]], such as [[netball]] or [[basketball]] or cross-country Olympic skiing.  Some [[game]]s and many kinds of [[racing]] are called sports. A professional at a sport is called an athlete. Many people do sports with their friends.  [[Sportsperson|They]] need [[Coach (sport)|coaches]] to teach or train [[team]]s or individuals how to do better.  Sports can be played [[indoors]] or [[Position|outdoors]] and by individuals or team. 
 
 Sports is needed for health and can help reduce diseases such as heart attacks, many types of cancer, depression and anxiety, and dementia. Different types of sports help our body in different ways. For children, sports play an extremely important part in their lives by providing all round development of the child, physically, mentally and emotionally. 
 
 Some people like to watch other people play sports. Those who watch others playing sports are called ''[[fanatic|fan]]s''. While some fans watch sports on [[television]], others actually go to [[stadium]]s or other places where people pay to watch them in person. These fans are called ''spectators''.
