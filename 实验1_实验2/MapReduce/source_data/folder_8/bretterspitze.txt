 The '''Bretterspitze''' is a [[mountain]] in [[Tyrol (state)|Tyrol,]] [[Austria]]. It is part of the [[Hornbach chain]] in the [[Allgäu Alps]]. The mountain is {{convert|2608|m}} high. It is next to the [[Gliegerkarspitze]] to the west and the [[Urbeleskarspitze]] to the northeast. The summit of the Bretterspitze is made of [[dolomite]].
