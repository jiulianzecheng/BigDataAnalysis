 The '''Hochfrottspitze''' is a [[mountain]] near [[Oberstdorf]], [[Germany]]. < ref > {{Cite web|url=http://www.mountaincabins.eu/mountains/hochfrottspitze.php|title=Mountain Cabins: Hochfrottspitze}}{{Dead link|date=January 2021 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }} < /ref >  It is the highest German mountain in the [[Allgäu Alps]] at {{convert|2649|m}} high. The border with [[Austria]] is on its ridge.  
 
 With the [[Trettachspitze]] and [[Mädelegabel]], the Hochfrottspitze forms a well known [[trio]] of peaks on the main ridge of the [[Allgäu Alps]]. It was first climbed in 1869 by Hermann von Barth. The route to the top is difficult to climb. < ref > {{Cite web|url=http://peakware.com/peaks.php?pk=3534|title=Peakware: Hochfrottspitze Overview|archive-date=2018-02-17|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180217205738/https://www.peakware.com/peaks.php?pk=3534|url-status=dead}} < /ref >  The mountain is made of [[dolomite]].
