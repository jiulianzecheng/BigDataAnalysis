 '''Getafe''' is a city 15 & nbsp;km south of [[Madrid]], in the [[Community of Madrid]], [[Spain]]. 158,363 people live there. It has great commercial and industrial activity and many mass media. In Getafe there is a [[university]], a military air base, and has a football team in the first division of the Spanish's League.
