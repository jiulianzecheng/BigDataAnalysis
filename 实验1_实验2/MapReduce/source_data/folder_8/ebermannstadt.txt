 '''Ebermannstadt''' is a [[germany|German]] [[town]] in the [[list of German districts|district]] of [[Forchheim]], [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. The [[population]] of the town is about 7,000. The next largest cities are: [[Forchheim]], [[Bayreuth]], [[Bamberg]], [[Kulmbach]] and [[Erlangen]]. The city parts of Ebermannstadt are:
