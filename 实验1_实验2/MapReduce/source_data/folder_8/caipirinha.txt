 '''Caipirinha''' is a [[cocktail]] that is prepared [[Cachaça]], and [[lime]]. Cachaça is a [[liquor]] made from [[fermented]] [[sugarcane]] [[juice]]. When [[vodka]] is used instead of Cachaça, the drink is usually called '''Caiprioska'''.
