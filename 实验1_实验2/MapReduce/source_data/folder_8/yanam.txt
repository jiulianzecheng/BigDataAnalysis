'''Yanam''' is a town in the [[Yanam district]], Union territory of [[Puducherry]], [[India]]. This town along with nearby villages form the district in [[East Godavari district]] of [[Andhra Pradesh]]. 
 
 ==Population== 
 According to the 2011 Census of India, the total population of Yanam is 55,626. The literacy rate of the city is 79.47% < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.census2011.co.in/data/town/804035-yanam-puducherry.html|title=Yanam Municipality City Population Census 2011-2019 - Puducherry|website=www.census2011.co.in}} < /ref >
