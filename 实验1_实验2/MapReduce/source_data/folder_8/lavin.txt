 '''Lavin''' was a [[municipality]] of the [[district]] of [[Inn (district)|Inn]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Graubünden]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 On 1 January 2015, the former municipalities of Lavin and [[Susch]] merged into the municipality of [[Zernez]].
