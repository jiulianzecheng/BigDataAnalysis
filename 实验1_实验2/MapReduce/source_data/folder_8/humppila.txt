 '''Humppila''' is a municipality in [[Kanta-Häme]], [[Finland]]. About 2,460 people lived there in 2013. The municipalities next to it are [[Forssa]], [[Jokioinen]], [[Ypäjä]], [[Loimaa]], [[Punkalaidun]]  and [[Urjala]].  
 
 Humppila was established in 1874. 
 
 ==Distances== 
 * Helsinki 130 km 
 * Turkuun 85 km 
 * Poriin 105 km 
 * Tampere 70 km  
 
 ==Villages== 
 Vitikka, Huhtaa, Humppila, Koivisto, Murto, Taipale, Venäjä and Myllynkulma.
