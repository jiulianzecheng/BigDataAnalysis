 '''Trun''' is a [[municipality]] in [[Surselva (district)|Surselva]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Graubünden]] in [[Switzerland]]. On 1 January 2012, the former municipality of [[Schlans]] became part of Trun.
