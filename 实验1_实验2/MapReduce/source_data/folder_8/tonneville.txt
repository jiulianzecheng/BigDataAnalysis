 '''Tonneville''' is a former [[Communes in France|commune]]. It is in [[Normandy]] in the [[Manche]] [[Departments of France|department]] in northwest [[France]]. On 1 January 2017, it was merged into the new commune of [[La Hague]]. < ref > [https://www.legifrance.gouv.fr/eli/arrete/2016/9/27/INTB1629328A/jo/texte Arrêté préfectoral] 27 September 2016 {{in lang|fr}} < /ref >
