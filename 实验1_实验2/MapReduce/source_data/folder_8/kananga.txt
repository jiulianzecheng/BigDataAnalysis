 '''Kananga''', formerly known as '''Luluabourg''' or '''Luluaburg''', is a [[city]] in south-central [[Democratic Republic of the Congo]]. It is the [[capital city]] of the [[Kasaï-Central|Lulua Province]]. in the [[Democratic Republic of the Congo]] and was the capital of the former [[Kasaï-Occidental|Kasaï-Occidental Province]].
