 ==Distribution== 
 They inhabit in several provinces of Pakistan, including [[Punjab, Pakistan|Punjab]], [[Balochistan, Pakistan|Balochistan]], [[Sindh]], Khyber [[Khyber Pakhtunkhwa|Pakhtunkhwa]], [[Azad Kashmir]], and [[Gilgit-Baltistan]]. 
 
 They are mostly found in Indian states such as Rajasthan, Gujarat, Uttar Pradesh, Himachal Pradesh, East Punjab, Uttarakhand, Madhya Pradesh, and Jammu and Kashmir. < ref name= " :0 "  / >
