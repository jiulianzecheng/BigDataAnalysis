 '''Bayonne''' ([[Gascon dialect|Gascon]] ''Baiona''; {{lang-eu|Baiona}}; {{lang-es|Bayona}}) is a city and [[Communes of France|commune]] in southwestern France at the confluence of the [[Nive]] and [[Adour]] rivers, in the [[Pyrénées-Atlantiques]] [[Departments of France|department]], of which it is a [[Prefectures in France|subprefecture]]. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 Bayonne has an area of {{Convert|21.68|km2|sqmi|abbr=on }} and its average [[altitude]] is {{Convert|28|m|ft|abbr=on}}; at the [[City and town halls|city hall]], the altitude is {{Convert|4|m|ft|abbr=on }}. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://www.map-france.com/Bayonne-64100/ |title=City of Bayonne |publisher=Map-France.com |access-date=9 March 2015 }} < /ref >

 ==Population== 
 With a population of 47,492, < ref name=insee > {{cite web |url=http://www.insee.fr/fr/ppp/bases-de-donnees/recensement/populations-legales/departement.asp?dep=64 & annee=2013 |title=Populations lègales 2013 - 64-Pyrénées-Atlantiques |work= Populations légales 2013 des départements et des collectivités d'outre-mer |publisher=Institut national de la statistique et des études économiques - INSEE |language=fr |access-date=6 November 2016 }} < /ref >  Bayonne has a [[population density]] of {{Pop density|47492|21.68|km2|prec=0|disp=num}} inhabitants/km < sup > 2 < /sup > . 
 
 The inhabitants of Bayonne are known, in [[French language|French]], as ''Bayonnais'' (women: ''Bayonnaises''). < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.habitants.fr/habitants_bayonne_64102.html |title=Bayonne (64100) |publisher=habitants.fr |language=fr |access-date=9 March 2015 }} < /ref > 
 
 ==Administration== 
 Bayonne is a [[Prefectures in France|''sous-préfecture'']] in the [[Pyrénées-Atlantiques]] department since 1800. It is also the capital of three ''cantons''. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://www.legifrance.gouv.fr/affichTexte.do?cidTexte=JORFTEXT000028661520 |title= Décret n° 2014-248 du 25 février 2014 portant délimitation des cantons dans le département des Pyrénées-Atlantiques |publisher=Légifrance.gouv.fr |language=fr |access-date=6 November 2016 }} < /ref > 
 # [[Canton of Bayonne-1|Bayonne-1]], formed with a part of Bayonne and part of [[Anglet]]. 
 # [[Canton of Bayonne-2|Bayonne-2]], formed with a part of Bayonne and the ''commune'' [[Boucau]]. 
 # [[Canton of Bayonne-3|Bayonne-3]], formed with the parts of Bayonne not included in the other two cantons.
