 '''North Mbundu''', or '''''Kimbundu''''', is one of two [[Bantu languages]] called '''Mbundu''' (see [[Umbundu]]).  It is one of the most widely spoken Bantu languages in [[Angola]].  It is mostly in the north-west of the country, notably in the [[Luanda Province]], the [[Bengo Province]], the [[Malanje Province]] and the [[Cuanza Norte Province]]. It is spoken by the [[Northern Mbundu people|Ambundu]].
