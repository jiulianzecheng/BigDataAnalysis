'''Muttley''' is a fictional dog and sidekick of [[Dick Dastardly]] who appeared in many animated series by Hanna-Barbera Productions from 1968 onward. 
 
 ==Portrayals== 
 Muttley was first voiced by Don Messick before being replaced by [[Billy West]]
