 The '''Hochvogel''' is a {{convert|2,592|m}} high mountain in the [[Allgäu Alps]]. The border between Germany and Austria runs over the summit. Climbers can ascend the summit on two marked routes. One of the most interesting climbs is by [[Hermann von Barth]]. He stayed overnight at the summit in 1869. On 14 December 1945 an American [[B-17 Flying Fortress|Flying Fortress]] crashed on the west of the Hochvogel. < ref > {{Cite web|url=http://www.wanderpfa.de/1/berggeschichte/damals.html|title=Wanderpfa - History}} < /ref >
