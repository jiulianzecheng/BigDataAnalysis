 '''Relaxation''' is a [[process]] or [[behavior]] where someone [[wikt:relax|relaxes]]. The goal is to become [[calm]]er and less [[anxiety|anxious]], [[Stress (medicine)|stressed]] or [[anger]]ed. Relaxation can also lower muscle tension, blood pressure and slow [[Heart rate|heart]] and breath rates, among other health benefits. < ref name= " nytimes " > [https://query.nytimes.com/gst/fullpage.html?sec=health & res=9A0DE5D81E3AF930A25756C0A960948260# ''Relaxation: surprising benefits detected''] by Daniel Goleman, ''The New York Times,'' May 13, 1986, retrieved May 23, 2006. < /ref >
