 '''Habit''' or '''Habits''' may refer to: 
 * [[Habit (psychology)]], an acquired pattern of behavior that often occurs automatically 
 * [[Habit (biology)]] is the form of growth of an [[animal]] or [[plant]] 
 * [[Habit (clothing)]], for example, as worn by [[monk]]s or [[nun]]s 
 * [[Drug addiction]] is sometimes referred to as  " having a drug habit " 
 * [[Habituation]], a basic activity in which an animal responds less to stimuli
