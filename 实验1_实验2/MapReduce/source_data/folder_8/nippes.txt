 '''Nippes''' is one of the ten [[Province|departments]] ([[French language|French]]: ''départments'', singular ''départment'') of [[Haiti]]. 
 
 It is the newest and smallest Haitian department; it was created in 2003 with the eastern part of the [[Grand'Anse Department]]. It has an estimated population of 266,379 (2003) and its capital city is [[Miragoâne]]. 
 
 Nippes has an area of 1,219 [[Square kilometre|km < sup > 2 < /sup > ]]. < ref name= " hr " > {{cite web |url=http://www.haiti-reference.com/geographie/departement/index.html |title=Départments géographiques d'Haïti |accessdate=24 October 2009 |format= |work=Haiti-Référence |language=French |archive-date=28 January 2010 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20100128233203/http://www.haiti-reference.com/geographie/departement/index.html |url-status=dead }} < /ref >  It is bordered to the south by the [[Sud Department]], to the east by the [[Ouest Department]], to the west by the [[Grand'Anse Department]] and to the north by the Gonâve Channel, the southern part of the [[Gulf of Gonâve]].
