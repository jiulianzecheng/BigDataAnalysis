 '''Quinoa''' (''Chenopodium quinoa''; ({{IPAc-en|ˈ|k|iː|n|w|ɑː}} or {{IPAc-en|k|ɪ|ˈ|n|oʊ|.|ə}}, from [[Quechuan languages|Quechua]] ''{{lang|qu|kinwa}}'' or ''{{lang|qu|kinuwa}}'') < ref > {{Ref Laime}} < /ref >  is a species of [[goosefoot]] (''Chenopodium''). It is a [[grain]]-like crop grown primarily for its edible seeds.  
 
 Quinoa is not a real [[cereal]], or grain, but more of a vegetable, as it is not a member of the [[Poaceae|grass family]]. As a [[Chenopodioieae|chenopod]], quinoa is closely related to [[species]] such as [[beet]]s, [[spinach]], and [[tumbleweed]]s. Its leaves are also eaten as a leaf vegetable, much like [[amaranth]]. Quinoa greens are not widely available for purchase.  Quinoa gives high quality [[protein]] and other [[nutrients]]. It has been called a 'superfood'. < ref > {{Cite web |title=Overall nutrient richness |url=http://www.whfoods.com/genpage.php?tname=foodspice & dbid=142 |access-date=2015-05-05 |archive-date=2015-05-08 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20150508085805/http://www.whfoods.com/genpage.php?dbid=142 & tname=foodspice |url-status=dead }} < /ref >
