 '''Zarbofoot''' ({{lang-fa|ضرب و فوت}}) is an [[Iran|Iranian]] [[musical]] [[band]] who has been active since 2017. < ref > Rokna website [https://www.rokna.net/بخش-هنری-7/490411-گفت-گو-با-داریوش-صالح-پور-رهبر-گروه-ضرب-فوت گفت‌وگو با داریوش صالح‌پور، رهبر گروه ضرب و فوت] < /ref > < ref > Khabari website [https://khabari.org/article/2109327/گفت-و-گو-با-داریوش-صالح-پور-رهبر-گروه-ضرب-و-فوت گفت‌وگو با داریوش صالح‌پور، رهبر گروه ضرب و فوت] < /ref > 
 
 == Professional activity == 
 Zarbofoot was formed by [[Dariush Salehpour]] and Amir Mohammadi in the spring of 2017, and was joined later by the other members including Reza Tajbakhsh. The band comprises a spectrum of wind instruments and electronic music with a touch of jazz and impromptu performance. The themes of the band's music are mostly worldwide and social.
