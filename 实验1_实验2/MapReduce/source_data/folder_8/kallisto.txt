 '''Kallisto''' or '''Callisto''' was a [[nymph]] in [[Greek mythology]], a companion of [[Artemis]]. [[Zeus]] came to her looking like Artemis, and with him she was the mother of [[Arkas]]. [[Hera]] in her jealousy made Kallisto a bear. Then her son was looked after by [[Maia]], a nymph.
