'''Ankst''' is a [[Wales|Welsh]] independent [[record label]] formed in [[1988]] at [[University of Wales Aberystwyth|Aberystwyth University]]. Ankst has been the launch-pad for several popular Welsh artists, including [[Super Furry Animals]] and [[Gorky's Zygotic Mynci]]. The label is now based in [[Pentraeth]] on [[Anglesey]].
