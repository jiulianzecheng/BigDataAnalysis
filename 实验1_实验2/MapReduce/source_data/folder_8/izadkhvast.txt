'''Izadkhast''' (also known as '''SamÄ«rum''') is a city in the Central District of [[Abadeh County]], [[Fars province|Fars Province]], Iran. At the 2006 census 7,366 people lived there in 1,803 families. 
 
 There is Izad-Khast [[Castle]], a [[Caravanserai|caravanserai,]] and a [[Safavid Empire|Safavid]]-period bridge.
