 '''Santalales''' is an order of [[flowering plant]]s. It has eight families, 151 genera, and about 1,000 species. All the families in Santalales are [[Parasite|parasitic]] to some degree. They attach either to the roots or [[branch]]es of their [[Host (biology)|hosts]]. 
 
 [[Mistletoe]] is the common name for some of the parasitic plants in the order.
