 '''Cyclopia''' (also called '''cyclocephaly''' or '''synophthalmia''') is a [[birth defect]]. Cyclopia is [[wikt:characterized|characterized]] by a failure of the [[prosencephalon]] to separate the two [[orbit (anatomy)|orbits]] (eyes).  Babies with Cyclopia rarely live longer than one day.
