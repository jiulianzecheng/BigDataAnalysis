A '''detective''' is a person who [[wikt:investigation|investigates]] [[crime]]. Most detectives are [[police officer]]s. Some are hired by a private person or company, for example to stop [[theft]]. Those are called [[private investigator]]s. There are many stories about detectives. Some famous fictional (not real) detectives are [[Sherlock Holmes]], [[Inspector Clouseau]], [[Nancy Drew]], [[The Hardy Boys]]. 
 
 Detectives will search public and private databases, investigate people and examine evidence in order to solve the case.
