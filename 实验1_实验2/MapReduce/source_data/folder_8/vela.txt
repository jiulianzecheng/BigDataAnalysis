Vela is a [[constellation]] in the southern sky. Its name is [[Latin]] the sails of a ship.  It is specifically supposed to be the ship [[Argo]] from the myth of [[Jason]] and the [[Golden Fleece]]. It is part the constellation [[Argo Navis]], which also has the [[Carina]] for [[keel]] and Puppis for the ship's [[stern]]. < ref name=Con > {{cite web|publisher=Constellation Guide|title=Vela Constellation |url=https://www.constellation-guide.com/constellation-list/vela-constellation/|access-date=May 2, 2021}} < /ref >
