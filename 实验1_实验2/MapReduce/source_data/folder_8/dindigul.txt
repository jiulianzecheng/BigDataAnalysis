Dindigul is a city in the [[South India]]n state of [[Tamil Nadu]]. It is the administrative headquarters of the [[Dindigul district]].Dindigul has a number of historical monuments, the Rock Fort being the most prominent.Industries in Dindigul include lock making, leather, textile spinning, administrative services, agricultural trading, banking, agricultural machinery and educational services. Dindigul is upgraded to a municipal corporation. 
 
 The city covers an area of {{convert|14.01|sqkm|sqmi|abbr=on}} and had a population of 207,327 in 2011.
