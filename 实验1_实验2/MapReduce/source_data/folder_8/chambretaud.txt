'''Chambretaud''' is a former [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is found in the [[Pays de la Loire]] region in the [[Vendée]] department in the west of [[France]]. On 1 January 2019, it became a part of the new commune of [[Chanverrie]]. < ref > [https://www.legifrance.gouv.fr/eli/arrete/2018/11/21/TERB1833280A/jo/texte Arrêté préfectoral] 21 November 2018 {{in lang|fr}} < /ref >
