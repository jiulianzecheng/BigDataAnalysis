 '''''BlacKkKlansman''''' is a 2018 [[United States|American]] [[crime movie]] directed by [[Spike Lee]] and written by Charlie Wachtel, David Rabinowitz, [[Kevin Willmott]], and Lee. 
 
 The movie is based on the 2014 memoir ''Black Klansman'' by [[Ron Stallworth]]. The movie stars [[John David Washington]] as Stallworth, along with [[Adam Driver]], [[Laura Harrier]], and [[Topher Grace]]. 
 
 ''BlacKkKlansman'' premiered on May 14, 2018 at the [[2018 Cannes Film Festival|Cannes Film Festival]], where it won the [[Grand Prix (Cannes Film Festival)|Grand Prix]]. It was theatrically released in the United States on August 10, 2018, a day before the first anniversary of the [[Unite the Right rally]].
