 '''Mendrisio''' is a [[municipality]] of the district of [[Mendrisio (district)|Mendrisio]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Ticino]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 Mendrisio is the seat of the [[Accademia di Architettura di Mendrisio|Accademia di Architettura]] of the [[Università della Svizzera Italiana|university of Italian-speaking Switzerland]] (USI). 
 
 The municipality was created on 4 April 2004, when it incorporated the former municipality of Salorino.
