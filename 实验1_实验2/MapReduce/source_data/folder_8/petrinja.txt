 '''Petrinja''' ({{IPA-sh|pětriːɲa|hr}}) is a town in central [[Croatia]] near [[Sisak]] in the region of [[Banija|Banovina]]. It is administratively located in [[Sisak-Moslavina County]]. 
 
 On December 29, 2020, the town was [[2020 Petrinja earthquake|damaged by a strong earthquake]].
