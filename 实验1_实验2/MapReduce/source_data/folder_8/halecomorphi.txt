 '''Halecomorphi''' is a type of [[Actinopterygii|ray-finned fish]] in the [[Neopterygii]].  
 
 The only living Halecomorph species are the [[bowfin]] (''Amia calva'') and [[Amia ocellicauda|eyespot bowfin]] (''Amia ocellicauda''). < ref > J.S. Nelson; T.C. Grande; M.V H. Wilson 2016. ''Fishes of the World'' (5th ed. Wiley. p. 752.  < nowiki > ISBN 978-1-118-34233-6 < /nowiki > . < /ref >
