 The '''Eiger''' ({{IPA-de|ˈaɪ̯ɡɐ}}) is a [[mountain]] in the [[Bernese Alps]] in [[Switzerland]]. It has an [[elevation]] of {{convert|3967|m|ft}}. The mountain is one of three mountains which are a large part of the local [[landscape]]. [[Mönch]] and [[Jungfrau]] are the other two mountains. Climbers like the [[Eiger north face|north face]]. It is {{Convert|1800|m}} high, and very difficult to climb.
