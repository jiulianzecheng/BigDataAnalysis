 '''Koppal''' ({{Lang-kn|ಕೊಪ್ಪಳ}}) is a town in the [[India]]n [[States and territories of India|state]] of [[Karnataka]]. It is the capital of the  
 [[Koppal district]]. Koppal was an important town in the history of Karnataka. It contains historical temples and ruins. 
 
 Koppal is surrounded by many smaller villages, such as Wanballary, Yettinhatti and Yelamgera. < ref name= " villagemap " > {{cite web|url=http://villagemap.in/karnataka/koppal/koppal/603700.html|title= Villagemap|accessdate=2008-12-18}} < /ref >
