'''Sfax''' ({{lang-ar|صفاقس|Ṣafāqis}}) is a city in [[Tunisia]]. In 2004, 265,131 people lived there. It is the second-most populated city in the country after the capital [[Tunis]]. 
 
 The city is on the [[Mediterranean Sea]] and is the main economic center.
