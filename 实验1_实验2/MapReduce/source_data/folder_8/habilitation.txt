In some countries, '''Habilitation''' is a step toward becoming a [[professor]] that comes after people receive their Ph. D. (doctor of philosophy or  " doctorate " ). The term '''habilitation''' is derived from the Medieval Latin ''habilitare'', meaning  " to make suitable, to fit " , from Classical Latin ''habilis''  " fit, proper, skillful " .
