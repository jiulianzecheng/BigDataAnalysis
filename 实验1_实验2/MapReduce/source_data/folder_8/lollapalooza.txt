 '''Lollapalooza''' is a [[music]] [[festival]]. It was started in 1991 by [[Perry Farrell]], lead singer of [[Jane's Addiction]], as a [[wikt:farewell|farewell]] concert for his band. It happened every year until 1997, then took a break until 2003. There was no 2004 Lollapalooza. < ref > [https://www.usatoday.com/life/music/news/2004-06-22-festival-canceled_x.htm Lollapalooza 2004 cancels all dates]. ''[[USA Today]]''. Accessed August 30, 2008. < /ref >  In 2005, the festival started again, and kept going every year. From 2005, it has played as a [[weekend]] show at [[Grant Park]] in [[Chicago]].
