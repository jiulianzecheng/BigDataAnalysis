 '''Solomonovo''' ({{lang-uk|Соломонове}}; {{lang-hu|Tiszasalamon, Salamon}}) is a village in the [[Uzhhorodskyi Raion]] ([[Raion|district]]) in the [[Zakarpattia Oblast]] ([[Oblast|province]]) in western [[Ukraine]]. 
 
 According to the [[2001]] census, 1342 people were living there.
