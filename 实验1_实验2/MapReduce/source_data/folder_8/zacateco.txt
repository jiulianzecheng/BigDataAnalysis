 The '''Zacatecos''' (or ''Zacatecas'') is the name of an [[Indigenous peoples|indigenous people]] living in the current [[Zacatecas|State of Zacatecas]], [[Aguascalientes]] and a part of [[Durango]], < ref > {{cite web|author=Antonio Torres Rodríguez|date=26 December 2010|title=Zacatecos|url=http://centzuntli.blogspot.mx/2010/12/zacatecos.html|access-date=15 February 2022|language=spanish|archive-date=7 May 2018|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180507080236/http://centzuntli.blogspot.mx/2010/12/zacatecos.html|url-status=dead}} < /ref >  they were called by the [[Aztecs]] as [[Chichimeca|Chichimecas]] (os).
