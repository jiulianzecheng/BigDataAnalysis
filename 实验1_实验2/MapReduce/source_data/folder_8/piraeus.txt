 '''Piraeus''' < ref > [[Greek language|Modern Greek]]: Πειραιάς ''{{transl|el|Peiraiás}}'', [[Ancient Greek language|Ancient Greek]] / [[Katharevousa]]: {{lang|grc|Πειραιεύς}} ''{{transl|grc|Peiraieús}}'' < /ref >  is a port and municipality in [[Athens]], [[Attica]], [[Greece]], in the south of the center of [[Athens]]. 
 
 The population of Piraeus is 163,688 (2011).
