 '''Kremlin''' ({{lang-ru|кремль}}) is the Russian word for  " fortress " ,  " citadel "  or  " castle " . It is used to refer to [[fortification|fortified]] central [[complex]]es in historic [[Russia]]n [[city|cities]]. In [[Moscow]] it refers to the central square. The word is also used to mean the government of Russia. 
 
 The [[Necropolis]] for [[communism|communists]] is in one of the walls of the Moscow Kremlin. The [[mausoleum]] for [[Vladimir Lenin]] is outside.
