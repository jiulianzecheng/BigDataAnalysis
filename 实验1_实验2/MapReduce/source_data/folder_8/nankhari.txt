 '''Nankhari''' is a [[Tehsil]] [[Main road town|(town)]] and a [[Community Development Block|block]] in [[Shimla district]] in the [[India]]n [[States and territories of India|state]] of [[Himachal Pradesh]]. < ref > {{Cite web|url=https://hpshimla.nic.in/tehsil/|title=Tehsils  & #124; Shimla District, Government of Himachal Pradesh  & #124; India}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite web|url=https://hpshimla.nic.in/public-utility/police-station-nankhari/|title=Police Station Nankhari  & #124; Shimla District, Government of Himachal Pradesh  & #124; India}} < /ref > < ref > {{cite web |url=https://www.census2021.co.in/data/subdistrict/178-nankhari-shimla-himachal-pradesh.html |title=Nankhari-Sub Tehsil to Tehsil |access-date=2021-05-12 |archive-date=2021-04-18 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210418144457/https://www.census2021.co.in/data/subdistrict/178-nankhari-shimla-himachal-pradesh.html |url-status=dead }} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite web|url=https://hpshimla.nic.in/document/circle-rates-tehsil-nankhari/|title=Circle Rates- Tehsil Nankhari 2020-21  & #124; Shimla District, Government of Himachal Pradesh  & #124; India}} < /ref > It is about 92.5 & nbsp;km from Shimla and 33 km from NH passing through [[Narkanda]] and is connected with [[National Highway 5 (India)|NH 5]] which passes through Narkanda, [[Theog]] and [[Kumarsain]]. < ref > {{Cite web|url=http://www.himachalpr.gov.in/OneNews.aspx?Language=1 & ID=366|title=Himachal Pradesh Government|website=www.himachalpr.gov.in}} < /ref > It consists of 17 [[Gram panchayat]] and 102 [[Village#India|villages]]. < ref > {{Cite web|url=http://www.brandbharat.com/english/hp/districts/Shimla/Shimla_NANKHARI.html|title=NANKHARI Block, Shimla Himachal Pradesh India|website=www.brandbharat.com}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite web|url=https://hpshimla.nic.in/panchayats/|title=Panchayats  & #124; Shimla District, Government of Himachal Pradesh  & #124; India}} < /ref > 
 
 ==Geography== 
 Nankhari is in a mountainous area. It is also a tourism place. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://himachaltourism.nic.in/ |title=Nankhari, Himachal Pradesh Tourism |access-date=2021-05-12 |archive-date=2007-01-15 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20070115021800/http://himachaltourism.nic.in/ |url-status=bot: unknown }} < /ref > The location co-ordinates of Nankhari are {{coord|31|18|16|N|77|35|21|E|display=inline,}}. The elevation of Nankhari is 2086 m. 
 Nankhari is 33km far away from Narkanda. It is 92.3 km away from [[Shimla]]. 
 
 ==Climate== 
 The average temperature of the year is 15°C, with variations from changes in elevation. < ref > {{Cite web|url=http://www.fallingrain.com/world/IN/11/Nankhari.html|title=Maps, Weather, and Airports for Nankhari, India|website=www.fallingrain.com}} < /ref > 
 
 ==Transport==
