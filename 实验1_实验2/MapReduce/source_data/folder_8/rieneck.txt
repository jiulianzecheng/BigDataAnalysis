 '''Rieneck''' ({{IPA-de|ˈʁiːnɛk|lang|De-Rieneck.ogg}}) is a town in the [[Main-Spessart|Main-Spessart district]] in the ''[[Regierungsbezirk]]'' of [[Lower Franconia]] (''Unterfranken'') in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. 
 
 == Geography == 
 
 === Location === 
 Rieneck is in the [[Würzburg]] region between the southern foothills of the [[Rhön Mountains|Rhön]] (range) and the east side of the [[Spessart]] (range) on the lower reaches of the [[River Sinn]], 12 & nbsp;km northeast of [[Lohr am Main]], and 39 & nbsp;km northwest of Würzburg. Administratively, it belongs to the [[Main-Spessart|Main-Spessart district]] in the ''[[Regierungsbezirk]]'' of [[Lower Franconia]]. 
 
 There are 140 & nbsp;ha of cropfields, meadows and woodland around the village. 
 
 === Subdivisions === 
 The town has only the ''Gemarkung'' (traditional rural cadastral area) of Rieneck.
