 '''''Shiloh''''' is a [[Children's literature|children's book]] written by [[Phyllis Reynolds Naylor]] in 1991. It won the 1992 [[Newbery Medal]]. It is about a [[dog]] that is given abuse and a boy named Marty P. that tries to save him. he took the dog on day he raised the dog Finley until one night when a German Shepard from the bakers almost kills the dog. Marty kept it a secret that he had the dog. His dad knew the dog owner (Judd) and they took the dog to the [[Medical doctor|doctor]]'s office. The whole family raised it for a week. Judd wanted the dog soon. Marty saw Judd shooting a [[doe]] out of season. He tried to [[blackmail]] him, but they worked out a bargain that Marty would work for him for 20 hours. Then Marty and the family got the dog and everything went back to normal.
