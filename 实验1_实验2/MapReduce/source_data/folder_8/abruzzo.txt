 '''Abruzzo''' is one of the twenty [[regions of Italy]], in [[Southern Italy]] on the [[Adriatic Sea]]. The [[Capital city|capital]] is the city of [[L'Aquila]]. 
 
 == Geography == 
 The region is located in [[Southern Italy]] bordered by [[Marche]] to the north, [[Lazio]] to the west, [[Molise]] to the south and the [[Adriatic Sea]] to the east.

 Abruzzo is the 13th largest region in Italy, with a total area of {{Convert|10831.84|km2|sqmi|1|abbr=on}}. < ref name= " area " > {{cite web|url=http://www.tuttitalia.it/abruzzo/ |title=Abruzzo |publisher=Tuttitalia.it |language=it |access-date=2 October 2017 }} < /ref >  The main rivers in the region are: 
 * [[Aterno-Pescara]], {{Convert|152|km|mi|1|abbr=on}} long. 
 * [[Sangro]], {{Convert|122|km|mi|1|abbr=on}} long. 
 * [[Vomano]], {{Convert|76|km|mi|1|abbr=on}} long. 
 * [[Tordino]], {{Convert|59|km|mi|1|abbr=on}} long. 
 * [[Liri]], {{Convert|120|km|mi|1|abbr=on}} long, {{Convert|40|km|mi|1|abbr=on}} in Abruzzo. 
 
 The [[Tronto]] river is {{Convert|115|km|mi|1|abbr=on}} long but flows mainly in the [[Marche]] region. 
 
 The highest mountain in the region is [[Corno Grande]] ({{Coord|42|28|10|N|13|33|56|E|name=Corno Grande|display=inline|region:ID_type:mountain}}), in the [[Province of Teramo|Teramo]] province, with an altitude of {{Convert|2912|m|ft|0|abbr=on }}. < ref name= " peakbagger " > {{cite web |url=http://peakbagger.com/peak.aspx?pid=10202 |title=Corno Grande, Italy |publisher=Peakbagger.com |access-date=2 October 2017 }} < /ref >
