Bloom|publisher=Yale University Press|year=1995|isbn=978-0-300-06465-0|page=123}} < /ref > < ref > {{cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=y4BsFC8ZyeoC & pg=PA136|title=Islam art et géométrie|publisher=BASTIEN|page=136|id=GGKEY:7T9G0W44YER}} < /ref >  is a medieval Muslim [[necropolis]] and ancient archeological site in [[Rabat]], [[Morocco]].
