 '''Montbard''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]] and [[Prefectures in France|subprefecture]] of the [[Côte-d'Or]] [[Departments of France|department]] in the [[Bourgogne-Franche-Comté]] [[Regions of France|region]] in eastern [[France]]. It is also the capital of the ''[[Arrondissements of France|arrondissement]]'' of [[Arrondissement of Montbard|Montbard]] and of the [[canton]] of [[Canton of Montbard|Montbard]].

 The city of Montbard is to the northwest of the city of [[Dijon]], capital of the department, and in the [[valley]] of the [[Brenne (river)|Brenne]] river. 
 
 ===Climate=== 
 The climate of Montbard is [[Oceanic climate|Marine West Coast Climate]] ([[Köppen climate classification]]: '''Cfb'''), with mild winters and warm summers. < ref name= " annuaire "  / > 
 
 ==Population== 
 The inhabitants of Montbard are known, in [[French language|French]], as '' Montbardois'' (women: ''Montbardoises''). < ref > {{cite web |url=https://www.habitants.fr/cote-d-or-21 |title=Côte-d'Or |publisher=habitants.fr |language=fr |access-date=7 June 2017 }} < /ref > 
 
 With a population of 5,350, < ref name=insee > {{cite web |url=http://www.insee.fr/fr/statistiques/fichier/2525755/dep21.pdf |title=Régions, départements, arrondissements, cantons et communes |work= Populations légales 2014 |publisher=Institut national de la statistique et des études économiques - INSEE |format=PDF |language=fr |access-date=7 June 2017}} < /ref >  Montbard has a [[population density]] of {{Pop density|5350|46.37|km2|prec=0|disp=num}} inhabitants/km < sup > 2 < /sup > .

 ==Administration== 
 Montbard is a [[Prefectures in France|subprefecture]] of the [[Côte-d'Or]] department since 1926. It is also the capital of the ''arrondissement'' of [[Arrondissement of Montbard|Montbard]] and the [[administrative centre]] ({{lang-fr|chef-lieu}}) of the ''canton'' of [[Canton of Montbard|Montbard]] formed by 57 ''communes'', with 18,764 inhabitants (2014). < ref name=insee1 > {{cite web|url=https://www.insee.fr/fr/metadonnees/cog/communes/COM21425-montbard |title=Commune de Montbard (21425) |work= Géographie administrative et d’étude |publisher=Institut national de la statistique et des études économiques - INSEE |language=fr |access-date=7 June 2017 }} < /ref >
