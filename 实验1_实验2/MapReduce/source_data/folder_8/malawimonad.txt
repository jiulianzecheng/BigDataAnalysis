 '''Malawimonada''' (order '''Malawimonadida''') is a [[phylum]] of [[microorganism]]s. They occupy a [[Basal group|basal position]] in the evolutionary tree of [[eukaryotes]]. 
 
 ==Description== 
 Malawimonada is a group of [[flagellum|biflagellate]], [[free-living]] protozoans in [[freshwater]] or [[soil]].
