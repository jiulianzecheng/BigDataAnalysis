'''Ansbertus''', '''Ansbert''' or '''Ausbert''' was a [[Frank]]ish nobleman and politician. His wife, Billihild, claimed to be the daughter of [[Charibert I]], [[Merovingian]] King of Paris, and granddaughter of [[Chlothar I]] Magnus. Ansbert was the father of [[Arnoald of Mez]] and grandfather of [[Doda of Mez]].
