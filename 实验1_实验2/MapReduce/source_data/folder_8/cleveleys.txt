 '''Cleveleys''' is a village on the [[The Fylde|Fylde Coast]] of [[Lancashire]], [[England]], about 4 miles north of [[Blackpool]] and 2 miles south of [[Fleetwood]]. It is part of the Borough of [[Wyre]].
