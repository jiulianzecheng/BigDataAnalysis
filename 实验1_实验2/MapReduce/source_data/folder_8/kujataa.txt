" Kujataa is a [[sub-arctic]] farming landscape in the southern region of [[Greenland]] " . < ref name=UNESCO > {{cite web | url=http://whc.unesco.org/en/list/1536 | title=Kujataa Greenland: Norse and Inuit Farming at the Edge of the Ice Cap }} < /ref > 
 
 The first known example of [[agriculture]] (or farming) in the [[Arctic]] is at Kujataa. 
 
 The oldest [[trace]] of the spreading of the [[Old Norse]] culture outside Europe is at Kujataa. 
 
 ==History== 
 Kujataa got its name on the [[UNESCO World Heritage List]] in 2017.
