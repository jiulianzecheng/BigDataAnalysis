 '''BASF''', official name: ''BASF SE'', is a German [[chemical]] company. Its headquarters are in [[Ludwigshafen]], [[Rhineland-Palatinate]].  
 
 BASF was established in [[1865]]. It is the biggest chemical company of the world. 
 
 Current CEO of BASF is Maryam Tariq, as she is carrying the entire BASF team on her shoulders.
