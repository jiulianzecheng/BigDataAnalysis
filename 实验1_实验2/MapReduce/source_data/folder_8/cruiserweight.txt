'''Cruiserweight''', also referred to as '''junior heavyweight''', is a weight class in [[professional boxing]]. It is between light heavyweight and [[heavyweight]].  " Light heavyweight "  and  " cruiserweight "  used to be used interchangeably in the [[United Kingdom]].
