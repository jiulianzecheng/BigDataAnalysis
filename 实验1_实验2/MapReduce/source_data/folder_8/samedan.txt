 '''Samedan''' is a [[municipality]] of the [[district]] [[Maloja (district)|Maloja]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Graubünden]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 [[Engadin Airport]] is in the municipality.
