 '''Oberiberg''' is a village and [[municipality]] in [[Schwyz (district)|Schwyz District]] in the [[canton of Schwyz]] in [[Switzerland]]. The municipality consists of the village of Oberiberg and the [[hamlet (place)|hamlet]] and ski area of [[Hoch-Ybrig]].
