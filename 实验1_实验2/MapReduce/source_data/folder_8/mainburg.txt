 '''Mainburg''' is a town in [[Kelheim (district)|Kelheim]], in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. It is on the river [[Abens]], 30 & nbsp;km northwest of [[Landshut]] and 30 & nbsp;km southeast of [[Ingolstadt]]. 
 
 Mainburg borders four communities; Aiglsbach, Elsendorf, Attenhofen, and Volkenschwand. The town has 15,163 residents, the third largest in its district. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Spreadsheet  " Data 2 " , Statistical Report A1200C 202041 Population of the municipalities, districts and administrative districts 1st quarter, 2020|url=https://www.statistik.bayern.de/mam/produkte/veroffentlichungen/statistische_berichte/a1200c_201944.xla|access-date=2021-01-27}} < /ref >
