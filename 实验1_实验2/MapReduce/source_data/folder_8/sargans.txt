 '''Sargans''' is a [[municipality]] in [[Sarganserland (Wahlkreis)|Sarganserland]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[St. Gallen (canton)|St. Gallen]] in [[Switzerland]]. As of December 2016, 6,063 people lived there. 
 
 Sargans is known for its [[Sargans Castle|castle]], which dates from before the founding of the [[Old Swiss Confederation|Swiss Confederation]] in 1291.
