 The '''Brandenburgers''' were members of the German [[special forces]] unit during [[World War II]]. < ref > {{Cite web|date=2006-06-12|title=The Brandenburg Commandos|url=https://www.historynet.com/the-brandenburg-commandos.htm|access-date=2021-07-08|website=HistoryNet|language=en-US}} < /ref >  It was one of the most secret forces in the German land forces. The unit was formed by the German [[military intelligence]] organ, the ''[[Abwehr]]''.
