'''Bitpanda GmbH''' is an Austrian [[cryptocurrency]] company from [[Vienna]]. Its [[mobile app]] gives access to its trading platform, which provides a cryptocurrency exchange, commodities and securities trading, and ETFs. In 2021, Bitpanda achieved a valuation of over four billion US dollars < ref > {{Cite web|title=Fintech: Bitpanda mit über vier Milliarden Dollar bewertet|url=https://www.handelsblatt.com/finanzen/banken-versicherungen/banken/fintech-bitpanda-mit-ueber-vier-milliarden-dollar-bewertet/27524518.html|access-date=2021-10-21|website=www.handelsblatt.com|language=de}} < /ref >  and had nearly 2.7 million users. < ref name= " :1 " > {{Cite web|date=2021-07-13|title=Bitpanda eröffnet Standort in Berlin|url=https://financefwd.com/de/bitpanda-standort-berlin/|access-date=2021-10-21|website=FinanceFWD|language=de}} < /ref >
