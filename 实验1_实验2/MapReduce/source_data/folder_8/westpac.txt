 '''Westpac Banking Corporation''', known simply as '''Westpac''', is an [[Australia]]n [[bank]] and [[financial services]] provider headquartered in [[Sydney]]. < ref > {{Cite web|last=Wootton|first=Hannah|date=2020-03-11|title=Westpac evacuates floors in Sydney HQ due to suspected virus|url=https://www.afr.com/companies/financial-services/westpac-s-sydney-hq-evacuated-due-to-suspected-virus-20200311-p5493r|access-date=2020-08-27|website=Australian Financial Review|language=en}} < /ref >  Founded in 1817 as the [[Bank of New South Wales]], it acquired the [[Commercial Bank of Australia]] in 1982 before being renamed shortly afterwards. It is one of Australia's [[Big Four (banks)| " big four "  banks]] and is Australia's first and oldest banking institution. Its name is a [[portmanteau]] of  " Western "  and  " Pacific " . 
 
 As of March 2018, Westpac has 14 million customers, and employs almost 40,000 people.
