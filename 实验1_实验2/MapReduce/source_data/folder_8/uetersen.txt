 '''Uetersen''' ({{IPA2|ˈyːtɐzən}}) is a small city in the [[Pinneberg (district)|district of Pinneberg]], in [[Schleswig-Holstein]], [[Germany]]. It is about {{convert|7|km|abbr=off}} south of [[Elmshorn]], and {{convert|30|km|abbr=off}} northwest of [[Hamburg]]. Uetersen is home to the [[Rosarium Uetersen]], the oldest and largest rose garden in Northern Germany, created in 1929. 
 
 [[Mathias Rust]] took off from Uetersen for his historic flight in 1987.
