 '''Antihelmintics''' are a group of [[Drug|drugs]] that are used against [[Parasitism|parasitic]] [[Worm|worms]]. Some only [[w:stun|stun]] the worm and it gets expelled. These are also kown as '''vermifuges'''. Others kill it, they are also called '''vermicides'''.
