 '''Laterina''' was a ''[[comune]]'' in the [[Province of Arezzo]] in the [[Italy|Italian]] region of [[Tuscany]]. On 1 January 2018, the former municipalities of Laterina and [[Pergine Valdarno]] merged to form the new municipality of [[Laterina Pergine Valdarno]].
