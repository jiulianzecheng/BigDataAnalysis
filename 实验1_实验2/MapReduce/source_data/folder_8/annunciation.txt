 According to Christian [[tradition]], the '''annunciation''' was an [[wikt:announcement|announcement]] made by the angel [[Gabriel]] to [[Mary, mother of Jesus|Mary]], the mother of [[Jesus Christ]], that she was to have a son despite being a [[virgin]]. 
 
 The [[Feast of the Annunciation]] is celebrated by [[Christian]]s on 25 March every year.
