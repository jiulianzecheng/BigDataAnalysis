 An '''orphanage''' is an [[institution]] that takes in and cares for [[orphan]]s. < ref name=MacmillanDictionaryforStudents > ''Macmillan Dictionary for Students'' Macmillan, Pan Ltd. (1981), page 711. Retrieved 2010-7-21. < /ref >   It can also mean the state of being an orphan. < ref name=MacmillanDictionaryforStudents/ > 
 
 Historically it was very often the [[church]] or the state who cared for orphans.{{cn|date=January 2012}} Children in orphanages may have suffered from [[child abuse]] or [[trauma]] from their parents.
