 '''Contre''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is in [[Hauts-de-France]] in the [[Somme]] [[Departments of France|department]] in north [[France]]. In 2018, 147 people lived there. 
 
 == Geography == 
 The commune is about {{Convert|20|mi|km|0}} southwest of [[Amiens]].
