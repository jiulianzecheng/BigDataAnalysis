 [[File:Hordeum vulgare MHNT.BOT.2015.2.39.jpg|thumb|''Hordeum vulgare'']] 
 
 '''Barley''' is a member of the [[grass]] family of [[plant]]s. Its [[seed]]s are a cereal [[grain]]. It is a [[staple food]] of [[human]]s and other [[animal]]s, and it is one of mankind's oldest [[crops]]. It can be made into [[flour]] or [[beer]]. 
 
 Its [[Latin language|Latin]] name is ''Hordeum vulgare''. Barley is an [[annual plant]].
