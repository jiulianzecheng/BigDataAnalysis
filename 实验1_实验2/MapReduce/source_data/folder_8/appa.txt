'''Appa''' is a [[fictional flying bison]] in [[Nickelodeon (TV channel)|Nickelodeon's]] [[anime|animated]] [[television programme|television series]] ''[[Avatar: The Last Airbender]]''. The character and series were created by [[Michael Dante DiMartino]] and [[Bryan Konietzko]]. Appa is [[voice actor|voiced]] by [[Dee Bradley Baker]].
