 '''Orsha''' ({{lang-be|Орша|Orša}}; {{lang-ru|Орша}}) is a city in [[Vitebsk Region]], [[Belarus]]. It is the administrative center of [[Orsha District]]. In 2023, 103,658 people lived there.
