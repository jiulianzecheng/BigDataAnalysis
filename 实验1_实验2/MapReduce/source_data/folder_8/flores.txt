 '''Flores''' is one of the [[Lesser Sunda Islands]], an [[island arc]]. Its area is about {{convert|14300|km2}}. It goes east from the island of [[Java (island)|Java]]. There are about 1.5 million people in Flores. < ref > [http://news.nationalgeographic.com/news/2003/01/0129_030129_komodo_2.html  " Trapping Komodo Dragons for Conservation " ] National Geographic - 28 Jan 2003 < /ref >  The largest town is [[Maumere]]. 
 
 Flores is east of [[Sumbawa]] and [[Komodo (island)|Komodo]]. It is west of [[Lembata]] and the [[Alor Archipelago]]. To the southeast is [[Timor]]. To the south is the Sumba strait. The [[Flores Sea]] is to the north. 
 
 On [[December 12]] [[1992]], an [[earthquake]] measuring 7.0 on the [[Richter magnitude scale|Richter scale]] occurred. It killed 2,000 people in the [[town]] of Maumere.
