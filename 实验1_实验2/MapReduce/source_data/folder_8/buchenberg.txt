 '''Buchenberg''' is a [[Municipalities of Germany|municipality]] in the district of [[Oberallgäu]] in [[Bavaria]] in [[Germany]]. Between 1988 and 2008 Buchenberg grew by 412 people. Toni Barth has been mayor of Buchenberg since March 2004. He is currently in his third term of office as mayor.
