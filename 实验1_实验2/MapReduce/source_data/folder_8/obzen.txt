 '''''obZen''''' is the sixth [[album]] by Swedish [[extreme metal]] band [[Meshuggah]]. It was released in Europe on March 7, 2008 and in North America on March 11, 2008 by [[Nuclear Blast]], the band's [[record label]]. < ref > {{Cite web|last=Blabbermouth|date=2007-11-29|title=MESHUGGAH: 'obZen' Track Listing Revealed|url=https://www.blabbermouth.net/news/meshuggah-obzen-track-listing-revealed/|access-date=2022-11-02|website=BLABBERMOUTH.NET|language=en}} < /ref >  It is the band's first album with [[Bass guitar|bassist]] Dick Lovgren playing on it, even though he has been with the band since 2004. The release of ''obZen'' was followed by the band's first [[Concert|world tour]]. Nuclear Blast sold a two-disc [[Gramophone record|vinyl]] version of the album on March 22, 2019. < ref > {{Citation|title=MESHUGGAH - Special Vinyl Releases: Remastered  &  Reimagined (OFFICIAL TRAILER)|url=https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=t2M9ihvVJvo|language=en|access-date=2022-11-02}} < /ref >
