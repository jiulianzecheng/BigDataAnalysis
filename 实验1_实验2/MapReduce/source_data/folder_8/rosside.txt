'''Rosside''' is a small hamlet on the outskirts of [[Ulverston]], in [[North West England|North West]] [[England]]. The hamlet is about {{convert|600|m2}} in size. It contains approximately 15 houses. Rosside gains little income from tourism, unlike her neighbouring town of Ulverston, where people flock to the famous Laurel  &  Hardy museum, a true tribute to the great comedians.
