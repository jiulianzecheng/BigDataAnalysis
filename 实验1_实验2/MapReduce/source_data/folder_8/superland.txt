 '''Superland''' is an [[amusement park]] in [[Rishon LeZion]], [[Israel]]. It was opened in 1991. In 1992 it opened as a [[park]] with a lake next to it. In the 2000s, a [[stadium]] was built in the park. In 2012, a [[movie theater]] was opened in the park.
