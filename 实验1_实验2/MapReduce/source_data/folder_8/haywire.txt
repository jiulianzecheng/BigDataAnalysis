 '''''Haywire''''' is a 2011 [[action movie|action]] [[Thriller (genre)|thriller]] movie. It was directed by [[Steven Soderbergh]]. It stars [[Gina Carano]], [[Antonio Banderas]], [[Michael Douglas]], [[Ewan McGregor]], [[Channing Tatum]], and [[Michael Fassbender]]. 
 
 ''Haywire'' was released on [[DVD]] and [[Blu-ray]] Disc on May 1, 2012. < ref > [http://bluray.highdefdigest.com/6611/haywire.html Haywire] < /ref >

 == Box office == 
 ''Haywire'' was released on January 20, 2012. It grossed [[US dollar|USD]]$18.9 million on its opening weekend. It grossed to $32.4 million in total.
