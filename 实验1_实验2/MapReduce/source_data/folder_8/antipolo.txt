'''Antipolo''', officially known as the '''City of Antipolo''' ({{lang-fil|Lungsod ng Antipolo}}), is a city and capital of the [[Provinces of the Philippines|province]] of [[Rizal]], [[Philippines]]. As of the 2020 census, 887,399 people lived there.{{PH census|current}} It is the most populous city in the [[Calabarzon]] region and the seventh most-populous city in the Philippines. < ref > {{cite web |url=https://philippinescities.com/region-4a-calabarzon-2/ |title=Region 4A (CALABARZON) {{!}} Philippines Cities |access-date=November 5, 2019}} < /ref >
