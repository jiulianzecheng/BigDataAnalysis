 '''Biancavilla''' is an [[Italy|Italian]] city in [[Sicily]]. About 24,000 people live there. Biancavilla is in the [[Metropolitan City of Catania]]. It is between the towns of [[Adrano]] and [[Santa Maria di Licodia]], {{convert|32|km|mi}} northwest of [[Catania]].
