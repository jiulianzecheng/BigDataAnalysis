 '''Katrina Laverne Taylor''' (born on December 3, 1978), < ref > https://twitter.com/TRINArockstarr/status/531909420272607232 < /ref > < ref > https://twitter.com/Essence/status/1201982407223721984 < /ref >  professionally known as '''Trina''', is an American rapper. She rose to fame in 1998. This is because of her appearance on the [[Trick Daddy]] single,  " Nann Nigga " . 
 
 Trina has been described as [[XXL]] by  " the most consistent female rapper of all time " . < ref > {{cite web | url=http://www.xxlmag.com/bloggers/2010/09/trina-is-the-most-consistent-female-rapper-of-all-time/ | title=Trina is the Most Consistent Female Rapper of All-Time - XXL }} < /ref >  In 2013, [[Complex Networks|Complex]] has ranked her single  " Pull Over "  on number 27 on their Top 50 Best Rap Songs by Women. < ref > {{cite web | url=http://www.complex.com/music/2013/04/the-50-best-rap-songs-by-women/pull-over | title=The 50 Best Rap Songs by Women | website=[[Complex Networks]] }} < /ref >  In 2014, she has been included in [[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]]'s list of the  " 31 Female Rappers Who Changed [[Hip-Hop]]. " < ref > {{cite magazine | url=http://www.billboard.com/articles/columns/the-juice/5923011/ladies-first-31-female-rappers-who-changed-hip-hop?page=0%2C12 | title=Ladies First: 31 Female Rappers Who Changed Hip-Hop | magazine=[[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]] }} < /ref >
