 '''Guillemont''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is in the [[Regions of France|region]] [[Picardie]] in the [[Somme]] [[Departments of France|department]] in the north of [[France]]. 
 
 It was an heavy area of combat during [[World War I]].
