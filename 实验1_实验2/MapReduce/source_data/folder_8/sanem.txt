 '''Sanem''' ({{lang-lb|Suessem}}, {{lang-fr|Sanem}}, {{lang-de|Sassenheim}}) is a [[Communes of Luxembourg|commune]] in [[Luxembourg]]. It is part of the [[Esch-sur-Alzette (canton)|canton of Esch-sur-Alzette]]. 
 
 As of 2019, 17,277 people live here.
