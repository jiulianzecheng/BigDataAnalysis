 '''''Clerks''''' is an 1994 [[United States|American]] [[comedy movie]] [[movie director|directed]] by [[Kevin Smith]]. It takes place in and around a [[convenience store]] in [[New Jersey]]. ''Clerks'' was [[Controversy|controversial]]. Due to [[profanity|graphic language]] it received an [[NC-17 rating]], although the rating was later changed to [[Motion Picture Association of America film rating system#R|R]]. ''Clerks'' was distributed from [[Miramax Films|Miramax]]. It was released in October 1994.
