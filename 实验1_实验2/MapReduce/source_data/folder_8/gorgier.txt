 '''Gorgier''' used to be a [[municipality]] in [[Boudry (district)|Boudry]]. It was in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Neuchâtel (canton)|Neuchâtel]] in [[Switzerland]]. On 1 January 2018, the municipalities of [[Bevaix]], [[Saint-Aubin-Sauges]], Gorgier, [[Vaumarcus]], [[Montalchez]] and [[Fresens]] became the new municipality of [[La Grande-Béroche]]. < ref > [https://www.bfs.admin.ch/bfs/de/home/grundlagen/agvch.html Amtliches Gemeindeverzeichnis der Schweiz] published by the Swiss Federal Statistical Office {{in lang|de}} accessed 14 February 2018 < /ref >
