 '''''Melanophryniscus''''' ('''South American Redbelly Toads''') is a [[genus]] of [[toad]]s.  There are 25 amphibian species located in [[Argentina]], south of [[Bolivia]], [[Brazil]], [[Paraguay]] and [[Uruguay]].
