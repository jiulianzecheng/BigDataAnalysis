 '''Tiberias''' ({{IPAc-en|t|aɪ|ˈ|b|ɪər|i|ə|s}}; {{lang-he-n|טְבֶריָה}}, '''Tverya''', '''Tiveria'''  < small > {{Audio|Tverya.ogg|(audio)}} < /small > ; {{lang-ar|طبرية}}, ''{{Unicode|Ṭabariyyah}}'') is a city in northern [[Israel]]. It is located along the [[Sea of Galilee]], in the [[Lower Galilee]] at the [[Jordan Valley]]. Tiberias has been considered one of [[Judaism]]'s four holiest cities, along with [[Jerusalem]], [[Hebron]] and [[Safed]].
