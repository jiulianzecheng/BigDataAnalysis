 '''Alajuela''' is the second largest [[city]] in [[Costa Rica]]. It is the [[capital (city)|capital]] of [[Alajuela Province]]. It is the birthplace of [[Juan Santamaría]] (the national hero of Costa Rica). It is also the birthplace of [[Carlos Luis Fallas]]. [[Juan Santamaría International Airport]] of Costa Rica is south of Alajuela's downtown.
