 '''ViendoMovies''' is an American Spanish-language [[Pay TV|pay television]] channel. It is distributed in [[High-definition television|High Definition]] and present in [[cable television]], [[Telephone company|Telcos]] and [[satellite television]] companies. The channel is owned by [[SomosTV]], company headed by Luis Villanueva (President  &  CEO of SOMOS Group). ViendoMovies runs 24-hours a day featuring movies originally produced in Spanish in a commercial free format (without [[television advertisement]]).
