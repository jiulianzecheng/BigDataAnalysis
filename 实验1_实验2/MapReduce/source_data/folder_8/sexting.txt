 '''Sexting''' is the act of sending sexually suggestive [[electronic message]]s, mainly between [[mobile phone]]s. < ref > {{cite web|title=Sexting|publisher=North Jersey|url=http://www.northjersey.com/news/education/parents-students-get-the-4-1-1-on-sexting-1.974685|accessdate=Mar 25, 2015}} < /ref >  The term became popular during the early 21st century. The term is a [[portmanteau word|portmanteau]] of ''[[sex]]'' and ''[[texting]]''. 
 
 The term was first used in print in 2005 in [[Australia]]. < ref name= " SunTele " > {{cite news|last=Roberts|first=Yvonne |title=The one and only |date=31 July 2005 |page=22| work =[[The Sunday Telegraph (Australia)|Sunday Telegraph Magazine]]| publisher =[[News Corp Australia]]|location=Sydney, Australia |quote=Following a string of extramarital affairs and several lurid  " sexting "  episodes, Warne has found himself home alone, with Simone Warne taking their three children and flying the conjugal coop.}} < /ref >  A naked picture that you make of yourself is sometimes called a nelfie, a mix of the words naked and selfie. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.thehollywoodgossip.com/2016/03/chelsea-handler-shares-nude-selfie-for-reese-witherspoons-birthd/|title=Chelsea Handler Shares NUDE SELFIE for Reese Witherspoon's Birthday!|first=Naomi|last=Lane|date=23 March 2016|website=The Hollywood Gossip}} < /ref > 
 
 If adults exchange such pictures, this is not a problem as long as both  consent. The problem starts when children do this: The image of a [[teenager]] or [[child]] with little clothes may be [[child pornography]]. Many countries have laws against child pornography. This usually includes penalties for owning or spreading such images.
