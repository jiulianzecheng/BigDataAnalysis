 '''Acanthuroidei''' is a suborder of [[Perciformes]], the largest order of fishes. 
 
 Members of this suborder have a vertically-shaped body covered with small scales. The name for the suborder comes from that of the [[surgeonfish]] (Acanthuridae) family. It is got from the Greek words ''akantha'' and ''oura'', which loosely translate as  " thorn "  and  " tail " . < ref > {{cite web|title = Acanthuroidei and Acanthuridae |website=[[FishBase]]|url=http://www.fishbase.org/Summary.php?Family=Acanthuridae|date = 23 October 2016}} < /ref >
