 '''Zekes''' was a [[dansband]] from [[Skara]], [[Sweden]]. It was formed in January 2009. The band participated at [[Dansbandskampen]] in 2009, where they reached the final. On 27 December 2009, the song  " I ett fönster "  entered [[Svensktoppen]], the Swedish record chart. < ref > [http://sverigesradio.se/sida/topplista.aspx?programid=2023 & date=2009-12-27 Svensktoppen - ''27 December 2009''] < /ref > 
 
 In June 2012, [[Sveriges Radio Skaraborg|SR Skaraborg]] announced that the band would split up after its summer tour of 2012. Their final appearance was at [[Matfestivalen]] in [[Skövde]] in August that year. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.svd.se/kultur/dansbandet-zekes-lagger-ner_7291613.svd|title=Dansbandet Zekes lägger ner|language=Swedish|date=20 June 2012|accessdate=26 June 2012}} < /ref >
