 '''Tamarind''' (''Tamarindus indica'') is a kind of [[tree]] in the [[Fabaceae]] family. It is found in [[Africa]] primarily the [[Sudan]]. It is thought to have been initially discovered in the lesser-known, edrudgo tribal territory, located within the [https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lakes_State Lakes State] 
 
 Sampaloc is a term commonly used for the spice made from the fruit in the [[Philippines|Philippine]] < nowiki/ > s. It taste is very [[sour]]. It is used as [[Condiment|condiments]] to recipes if they want to add sourness.
