 '''Kortrijk''' or '''Courtray''' (Courtrai in French) is a [[city]] in the [[Belgium|Belgian]] province of [[West Flanders]]. The city is located along the river [[Leie]] (Lys). The centre is a well preserved historic area with a lot of shops and nice squares. The Beguinage (Begijnhof in Dutch) is a very well known historic part of the centre with well preserved medieval streets, houses, squares and alleys and was declared [[World Heritage Site|UNESCO World Heritage]].
