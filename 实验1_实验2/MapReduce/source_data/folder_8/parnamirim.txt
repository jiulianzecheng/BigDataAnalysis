 '''Parnamirim''' is an [[Brazil]]ian city in the state of [[Rio Grande do Norte]]. It is next to the state capital [[Natal, Rio Grande do Norte|Natal]]. 267,036 people lived here in 2020 and its area is 123,5 km².
