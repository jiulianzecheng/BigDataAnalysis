 '''Perho''' is a [[municipality]] in [[Central Ostrobothnia]], [[Finland]]. About 2,930 people lived there in January 2014. The municipalities next to it are [[Alajärvi]], [[Halsua]], [[Kinnula]], [[Kivijärvi]], [[Kyyjärvi]], [[Lestijärvi]], [[Veteli]] and [[Vimpeli]]. 
 
 Perho was established in 1868.
