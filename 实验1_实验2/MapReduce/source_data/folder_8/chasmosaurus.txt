 '''''Chasmosaurus''''' was a [[rhinoceros]]-like [[dinosaur]], the order of [[Ornithischia|bird-hipped]], [[Herbivore|herbivorous]] dinosaurs. It was a member of the suborder [[Marginocephalia]], and the family of large, horned, [[herding]] herbivores, the [[ceratopsian]]s. ''Chasmosaurus'' was named by [[Paleontology|paleontologist]] [[Lawrence Lambe]] in [[1914]]. 
 
 Chasmosaurus lived 65 to 100 million years ago and was 5 to 6 metres long with a weight of 3.6 tonnes. 
 
 They lived in western [[North America]] from Alaska to Mexico.
