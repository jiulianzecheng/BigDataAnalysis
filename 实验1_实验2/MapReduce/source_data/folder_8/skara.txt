 '''Skara''' is a town in the [[counties of Sweden|county]] of [[Västra Götaland County|Västra Götaland]] in [[Sweden]]. It is the seat of [[Skara Municipality]]. Skara is the home of [[Skara Cathedral]], and Swedish record label [[Mariann Grammofon]] as well. 
 
 Founding dates back to [[988]] (traditional date). From [[1634]] and until [[1660]], Skara was the seat of [[Skaraborg County]].
