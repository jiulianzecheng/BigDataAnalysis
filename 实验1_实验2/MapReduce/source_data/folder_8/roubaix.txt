 '''Roubaix''' is a city in the [[Nord (department)|Nord]] department, in the [[Hauts-de-France]] region, in north [[France]]. About 94,000 live there at about 7,119 people per square kilometer. It is near the cities [[Lille]] and [[Tourcoing]]. There is a [[Rapid transit|metro]] and [[tram]] line between Roubaix and these places. Roubaix is near the [[Belgium|Belgian]] border. The city is well known for its [[cycling]] race [[Paris-Roubaix]] which is over 259.5 & nbsp;km and includes almost 50 & nbsp;km of cobblestone sections.
