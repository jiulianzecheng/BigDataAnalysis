 '''Totora''' ('''''Schoenoplectus californicus'' subsp. ''tatora''''') is a [[subspecies]] of the [[Schoenoplectus californicus|giant bulrush sedge]]. It is found in [[South America]]. It is found on [[Lake Titicaca]], the middle coast of [[Peru]] and on [[Easter Island]] in the [[Pacific Ocean]]. The [[genus]] ''[[Schoenoplectus]]'' is related to ''[[Scirpus]]''.
