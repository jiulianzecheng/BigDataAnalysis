 '''Ladir''' was a [[municipality]] in the [[district]] [[Surselva (district)|Surselva]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Graubünden]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 On 1 January 2014, the former municipalities of Ladir, [[Castrisch]], [[Ilanz]], [[Luven]], [[Pitasch]], [[Riein]], [[Ruschein]], [[Schnaus]], [[Sevgein]], [[Duvin]], [[Pigniu]], [[Rueun]] and [[Siat]] merged into the new municipality of [[Ilanz/Glion]].
