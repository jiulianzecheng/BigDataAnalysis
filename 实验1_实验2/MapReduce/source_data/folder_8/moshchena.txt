 '''Moshchena''' ({{lang-uk|Мощена}}, {{lang-pl|Moszczana}}) is a village in [[Kovel Raion]], [[Volyn Oblast]], [[Ukraine]]. It has a population of 581 people. < ref > {{cite web| url=http://gska2.rada.gov.ua/pls/z7502/A005?rdat1=29.05.2007 & rf7571=3904| title=[missing title]|website= [[Supreme Council of Ukraine]]| access-date=2011-09-10| archive-date=2013-06-26| archive-url=https://www.webcitation.org/6HfDPpjnm?url=http://w1.c1.rada.gov.ua/pls/z7502/A005?rdat1=29.05.2007| url-status=dead}} < /ref > 
 
 The first mention of the village dates from 1543.
