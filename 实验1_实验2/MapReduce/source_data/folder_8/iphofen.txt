 '''Iphofen''' is a town in [[Kitzingen (district)|Kitzingen]] in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. It has a population of around 4,500. 
 ==Geography== 
 ===Location=== 
 [[Image:Iphofen BW 2.jpg|left|thumb|''Rödelseer Tor'']] 
 Iphofen is in the southeast of Kitzingen of Bavaria, in the ''[[Regierungsbezirk]]'' of [[Unterfranken]]. It is 9 & nbsp;km southeast of [[Kitzingen]] on [[Bundesstraße 8]]. The town is about 10 & nbsp;km from the [[Main (river)|river Main]]. The nearest city is [[Würzburg]]. Iphofen is at the foot of the [[Schwanberg]], a well known hill on the northwestern edge of the ''[[Mittelgebirge]]'' [[Steigerwald]].
