 '''Castelnaudary''' ({{lang-oc|Castèlnòu d'Arri}}) is a [[Communes of France|commune]] in the [[Aude]] [[Departments of France|department]] in the [[Languedoc-Roussillon]] [[Regions of France|region]] in southern [[France]]. 
 
 A campus of the [[École nationale de l'aviation civile]] (French civil aviation university) is located at Castelnaudary.
