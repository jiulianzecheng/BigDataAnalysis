'''Mahdavia''' ({{lang-ar| مهدوي  }} ''mahdawi'') is a Mahdi'ist movement founded in the 15th century which follows the principles set forth by Syed Jaunpuri. A subsect of Mahdavia, called Zikri, is mostly practised in [[Pakistan]]. < ref > Azhar Munīr, I. A. Rehman. Zikris in the light of history  &  their religious beliefs, Izharsons, 1998. < /ref >
