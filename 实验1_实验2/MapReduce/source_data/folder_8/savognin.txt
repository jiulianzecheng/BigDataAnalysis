 '''Savognin''' was a [[municipality]] of the [[district]] of [[Albula (district)|Albula]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Graubünden]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 On 1 January 2016, the former municipalities of Savognin, [[Bivio]], [[Cunter]], [[Marmorera]], [[Mulegns]], [[Riom-Parsonz]], [[Salouf]], [[Sur, Switzerland|Sur]] and [[Tinizong-Rona]] merged into the new municipality of [[Surses]].
