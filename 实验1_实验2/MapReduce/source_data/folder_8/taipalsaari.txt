 '''Taipalsaari''' is a municipality in [[South Karelia]], [[Finland]]. As of 28 February 2023, 4,554 people lived there. Neighbouring municipalities are [[Lappeenranta]], [[Lemi]], [[Puumala]], [[Ruokolahti]] and [[Savitaipale]].
