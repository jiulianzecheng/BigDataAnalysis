 '''Uelsen''' ({{IPA-de|ˈʔʏlsn̩}}) is a community in the [[Grafschaft Bentheim|County of Bentheim]] in [[Lower Saxony]].  
 
 ==Geography== 
 The community of Uelsen is in westernmost Lower Saxony, on the [[border]] with the Kingdom of the [[Netherlands]], roughly 30 & nbsp;km north of [[Enschede]] and 15 & nbsp;km northwest of [[Nordhorn]].
