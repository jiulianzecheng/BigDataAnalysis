'''Arianrhod''' ({{IPA-cy|arˈjanr̥ɔd}}) is a figure in [[Welsh mythology]] who plays her most important role in the Fourth Branch of the ''[[Mabinogion|Mabinogi]]''. She is the daughter of [[Dôn]] and the sister of [[Gwydion]] and [[Gilfaethwy]]; the [[Welsh Triads]] give her father as [[Beli Mawr]]. < ref name= " Bromwich " > Triad 35. Bromwich, ''Trioedd Ynys Prydein'', pp. 284–285. < /ref >  In the ''Mabinogi'' her uncle [[Math ap Mathonwy]] is the King of [[Kingdom of Gwynedd|Gwynedd]], and during the course of the story she gives birth to two sons, [[Dylan ail Don]] and [[Lleu Llaw Gyffes]], through magical means.
