 The '''Caryophyllaceae''', commonly called the '''pink family''' or '''carnation family''', is a family of [[flowering plant]]s. The species are [[dicotyledon]]s included in the order [[Caryophyllales]]. It is a large family, with 88 genera and some 2,000 species.
