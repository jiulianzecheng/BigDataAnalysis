 '''Kleinwallstadt''' is a market community in the [[Miltenberg (district)|Miltenberg district]] in the [[Regierungsbezirk|region]] of [[Lower Franconia]] in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]] and the seat of the Administrative Community. 
 
 == Geography == 
 
 === Place === 
 Kleinwallstadt is in the Bavarian Lower Main (''Bayerischer Untermain'') Region. 
   
 The community has the following districts: Hofstetten, Kleinwallstadt.
