 '''Bredbury''' is a village in the [[Metropolitan Borough of Stockport]], in [[Greater Manchester]], [[United Kingdom|England]]. Bredbury is found  {{convert|7|mi|km|1}} southeast of the city of [[Manchester]], and {{convert|2|mi|km|1}} east-northeast of [[Stockport]].
