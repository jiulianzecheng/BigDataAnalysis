 '''Nonpareils''' are a decorative [[confectionery]] of tiny balls made with [[sugar]]s and [[starch]]. They come in many colors. They are used for decorating [[cake]]s, [[bread]] and [[cookie]]s. 
 
 Nonpareils date back to the 18th century.
