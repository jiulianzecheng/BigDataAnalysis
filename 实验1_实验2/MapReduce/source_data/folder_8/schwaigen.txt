 '''Schwaigen''' is a [[municipality]] in the district of [[Garmisch-Partenkirchen (district)|Garmisch-Partenkirchen]] in [[Upper Bavaria]] in [[Germany]]. 
 
 == Districts == 
 Schwaigen has six districts: 
 
 * Apfelbichel 
 * Fuchsloch 
 * Grafenaschau 
 * Hinterbraunau 
 * Plaicken 
 * Vorderbraunau
