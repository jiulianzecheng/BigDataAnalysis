 '''Bardejov''' ({{lang-hu|Bártfa}}; {{lang-de|Bartfeld}}; {{lang-pl|Bardejów}}) is a town in northeastern [[Slovakia]]. About 32,000 people live there. It has been a [[UNESCO]] [[World Heritage Site]] since 2000.
