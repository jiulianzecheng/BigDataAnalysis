 '''Sens''' is a [[France|French]] [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is in the north of the [[Yonne]] [[Departments of France|department]] in the [[Bourgogne-Franche-Comté]] [[Regions of France|region]], to the southeast of [[Paris]], and is a [[Prefectures in France|subprefecture]] in the department. It is also the [[Capital city|capital]] of the ''arrrondissement'' of [[Arrondissement of Sens|Sens]]. 
 
 Sens is the second ''commune'' of the department by its population, after [[Auxerre]], [[Prefectures in France|prefecture]] of the department. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 The city of Sens is in the northern part of the Yonne department, in the northwest of the Bourgogne-Franche-Comté region and bordered by three other regions: [[Île-de-France]], [[Grand Est]] and [[Centre-Val de Loire]]. It is in the [[valley]] of the [[Yonne (river)|Yonne]] [[river]]. 
 
 The ''commune'' of Sens has an area of {{Convert|21.9|km2|sqmi|abbr=on }}. < ref name= " insee-geo " > {{cite web |url=https://www.insee.fr/fr/statistiques/1405599?geo=COM-89387 |title=Commune de Sens (89387) - Comparateur de territoire |work=Publications et statistiques pour la France ou les régions |publisher=Institut national de la statistique et des études économiques - INSEE |format= |language=French |accessdate=20 June 2017 |date= }} < /ref >  Its average [[altitude]] is {{Convert|135|m|ft|abbr=on}}; at the [[City and town halls|city hall]], the altitude is {{Convert|70|m|ft|abbr=on }}. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://www.map-france.com/Sens-89100/ |title=The city of Sens |publisher=Map-France.com |language= |accessdate=20 June 2017 |date= }} < /ref >

 == Administration == 
 Sens is a [[Prefectures in France|subprefecture]] of the Yonne department, the capital of the ''arrondissement'' of [[Arrondissement of Sens|Sens]] and the [[administrative centre]] ({{lang-fr|chef-lieu}}) of two cantons: 
 * [[Canton of Sens-1|Sens-1]] formed by 2 ''communes'' and part of Sens, with 19,669 inhabitants  < small > (2014) < /small > . 
 * [[Canton of Sens-2|Sens-2]] formed by 5 ''communes'' and part of Sens, with 19,996 inhabitants  < small > (2014) < /small > . 
 
 It is part of the [[Communes of France|intercommunality]] ''Le Grand Sénonais'' ({{lang-fr|Communauté d'agglomération du Grand Sénonais}}).

 ==Places of interest== 
 Some interesting places in Sens are: 
 * The ''Saint-Étienne'' [[cathedral]], one of the earliest [[Gothic architecture|Gothic]] buildings in France. 
 * The ''Maison d'Abraham'' (House of Abraham), a house in the centre of the city. 
 * The [[Archbishop]]s' Palace - now it is a [[museum]]. 
 * The ''Saint-Maurice'' [[church]].
