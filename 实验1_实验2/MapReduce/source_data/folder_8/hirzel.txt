 '''Hirzel''' was a [[municipality]] in the [[district]] of [[Horgen (district)|Horgen]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Zurich (canton)|Zurich]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 On 1 January 2018, the former municipality of Hirzel merged into the municipality of [[Horgen]].
