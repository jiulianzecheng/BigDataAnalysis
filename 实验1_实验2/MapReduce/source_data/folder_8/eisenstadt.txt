 '''Eisenstadt''' ([[Hungarian language|Hungarian]]: ''Kismarton'', [[Austro-Bavarian language|Austro-Bavarian]]: ''Eisnstod'', [[Croatian language|Croatian]]: ''Željezno'') is a city in [[Austria]]. It is the capital and a district of [[Burgenland]]. About 14,000 people are living there (2016). 
 
 It is near the [[Hungary|Hungarian]] border.
