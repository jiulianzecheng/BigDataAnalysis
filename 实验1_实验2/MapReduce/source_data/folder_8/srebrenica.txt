 '''Srebenica''' is a town in eastern [[Bosnia]]. In 1995 after the end of [[Yugoslavia]] and the subsequent war, the [[Bosniaks|Bosnian Muslim]] population of the town was massacred by [[Serbs|Serb]] paramilitary forces. The massacre came to be widely known as the [[Srebrenica massacre]].
