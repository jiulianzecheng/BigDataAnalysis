 '''Hage''' (East Frisian: Haag) is a small [[East Frisia]]n [[town]] in [[Lower Saxony]], [[Germany]]. Placed in the [[Aurich (district)|district of Aurich]] close to the [[North Sea]], it is about 5 & nbsp;km east of [[Norden, Lower Saxony|Norden]]. Hage is also the seat of the ''[[Samtgemeinde]]'' ( " collective municipality " ) [[Hage (Samtgemeinde)|Hage]].
