 '''Blue''' is one of the [[color]]s of the [[rainbow]] that [[people]] can see. It is one of the [[primary colors]] (colors that can be mixed with other colors) of [[light]], along with [[red]] and [[green]]. Apart from indigo and violet, it has the shortest [[wavelength]] of colors in the rainbow (about 470 [[nanometer]]s). 
 
 Blue [[paint]] can be made by mixing [[magenta]] and [[cyan]] paint. 
 
 Blue is the color of the [[Earth]]'s [[sky]] and [[sea]].  [[Earth]] looks blue when seen from [[outer space]] by [[astronaut]]s. 
 
 {{-}} 
 
 == Meaning of blue == 
 The color blue is sometimes associated with [[sadness]], which may be where the name of the music style [[Blues|The Blues]] comes from. 
 Blue is a color of the Jewish religion. 
 In Western cultures since the 1940s, blue is associated with men/boys, while [[pink]] is associated with women/girls. Before the 1940s, it was the other way around, so blue was for women/girls and pink was for men/boys.
