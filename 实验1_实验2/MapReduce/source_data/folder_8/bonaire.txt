 '''Bonaire''' is an [[island]] of the [[Caribbean Netherlands]] and the former [[Netherlands Antilles]]. It is a [[municipalities of the Netherlands|special municipality (public body) of the Netherlands]]. It lies off the [[coast]] of [[Venezuela]], near the islands of [[Aruba]] and [[Curaçao]], which are also parts of the [[Kingdom of the Netherlands]]. 
 
 Bonaire lies at {{coord|12|11|N|68|16|W}}. It has a land area of 288  km². In 2014 about 18,000 people were living there. 
 
 [[Kralendijk]] is the island's capital city.
