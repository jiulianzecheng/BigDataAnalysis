'''Ahirwati''' is an [[Indo-Aryan languages|Indo-Aryan language]], classified as a [[Rajasthani language]], and is spoken in the Mahendragarh and Rewari districts of Haryana. According to Historians, Ahirwati is language of [[Yaduvanshi caste|Yaduvanshi Ahirs]] and spoken in [[Rohtak]] and [[Gurgaon]] Districts of [[Punjab, India|Punjab]] (now [[Haryana]]) and [[Delhi]].
