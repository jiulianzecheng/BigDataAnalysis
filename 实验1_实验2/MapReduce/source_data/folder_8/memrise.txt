'''Memrise''' is a free online language-learning [[website]]. It uses [[flashcard]]s with [[mnemonic]]s to teach. Users are tested by multiple choice and type-in tests. Memrise was founded by [[Ed Cooke (author)|Ed Cooke]] and Greg Detre. < ref > {{Cite news|url=https://techcrunch.com/2012/02/16/crowd-sourced-learning-platform-memrise-nabs-1-05m-from-matt-mullenweg-lerer-ventures-and-more/|title=Crowd-Sourced Learning Platform Memrise Nabs $1.05M From Matt Mullenweg, Lerer Ventures And More!|last=Tsotsis|first=Alexia|newspaper=TechCrunch|access-date=2017-10-19|language=en}} < /ref > < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.memrise.com/about/press/|title=Memrise: About: Press Page|publisher=Memrise|accessdate=2012-04-04|archive-date=2012-04-08|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120408132757/http://www.memrise.com/about/press/|url-status=dead}} < /ref >  Memrise is headquartered in England.
