 '''Mostar''' is the fifth largest city in [[Bosnia and Herzegovina]]. In 2019 the city had a total [[population]] of 104,518. The city has several different cultures living in it but is mostly inhabited by [[Croats]]. < ref > [http://worldpopulationreview.com/countries/bosnia-and-herzegovina-population/ Bosnia and Herzegovina Population 2019] < /ref >  Mostar is home to one of Bosnia and Herzegovina's most visited landmarks, The Old Bridge, which was damaged during the [[Croats|Croat]]-[[Bosniaks|Bosniak]] War which also had ended up destroying most of the cities bridges, but since 1995 the city has been restored. The Old Bridge was built by the [[Ottoman Empire|Ottomans]] and is an example of [[Islam]]ic architecture in the [[Balkans]].
