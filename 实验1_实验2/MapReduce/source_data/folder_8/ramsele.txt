 '''Ramsele''' is a [[Urban areas in Sweden|locality]] in [[Sollefteå Municipality]] in [[Västernorrland County]] in [[Sweden]]. In 2010, 968 people lived there. Since the 1960s the population has been reduced from 1 563 to 968. < ref name=scb / >  The locality is located near the [[Fax River]] circa 70 kilometers northwest of the town of [[Sollefteå]].
