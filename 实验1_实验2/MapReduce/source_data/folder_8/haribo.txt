 '''Haribo''' is a [[Germany|German]] [[candy]] company founded in 1920 by [[Hans Riegel Sr.|Johannes  " Hans "  Riegel Sr.]] It started in [[Kessenich (Bonn)|Kessenich]], [[Bonn]], [[North Rhine-Westphalia]]. The name is an [[acronym]] formed from '''Ha'''ns '''Ri'''egel, '''Bo'''nn. < ref > {{cite web |url=https://www2.haribo.com/enGB/company/history.html |title=HARIBO history: from a backyard kitchen to a world market leader |last= |first= |date= |website=Haribo.com |publisher=Haribo |access-date=20 June 2020 |quote=}} < /ref >  This is the name of the founder and the place he founded the company. Haribo created the first [[gummy candy]] in 1922 in the form of little [[gummy bear]]s called ''Gummibärchen''. The current headquarters is in [[Grafschaft, Rhineland|Grafschaft]], [[Rhineland-Palatinate]], Germany. 
 [[File:Christmas lights on Ropergate, Pontefract (26th November 2019).jpg|thumb|left|Haribo [[sponsor]] the Christmas lights in [[Pontefract]].]] 
 
 Haribo's slogan in [[German language|German]] is '' " Haribo macht Kinder froh – und Erwachsene ebenso " '' ( " Haribo makes children happy – and adults as well " ).
