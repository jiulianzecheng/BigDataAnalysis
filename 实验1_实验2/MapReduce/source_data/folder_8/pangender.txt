 == Etymology == 
 The word ''pangender'' comes from the [[Greek language|Greek]] [[prefix]] ''pan-'', meaning  " all "  or  " every " , and [[gender]]. < ref name= " Merriam-Webster: Definition of Pan- " > {{Cite web|url=https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/pan-|title=Definition of Pan-|publisher=Merriam-Webster}} < /ref >  Together this can mean  " every gender " ,  " all genders " , or other similar [[Translation|translations]].
