 '''Canewdon''' is a village and [[civil parish]] in [[Rochford District]], [[Essex]], England. In 2001 there were 1477 people living in Canewdon. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.ons.gov.uk/help/localstatistics|title=Local statistics - Office for National Statistics|website=www.ons.gov.uk}} < /ref >  Canewdon is between [[Ashingdon]], [[Paglesham]] and [[Stambridge]]. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.essexinfo.net/canewdon-parish-council/|title=Canewdon Parish Council and incorporating Wallasea Island - Welcome to the Canewdon Parish Council Website|website=www.essexinfo.net|access-date=2013-08-03|archive-date=2013-11-27|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20131127140337/http://www.essexinfo.net/canewdon-parish-council/|url-status=dead}} < /ref >
