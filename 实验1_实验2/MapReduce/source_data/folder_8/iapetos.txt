'''Iapetos''', father of man kind and mountains, also known as Iaepetus or Japetus, is a [[Titan (mythology)|Titan]] in [[Greek mythology]]. He is the son of [[Uranus|Uranos]] and [[Gaia (mythology)|Gaia]]. With the [[Oceanid]] [[Klymene]] his children are [[Atlas (titan)|Atlas]], [[Prometheus]], [[Epimetheus]] and [[Menoitios]].  
 
 After the war against the [[Twelve Olympians|Olympians]] he was banned to the [[Tartarus]] with his brother, [[Kronos]] and all of the other Titans.
