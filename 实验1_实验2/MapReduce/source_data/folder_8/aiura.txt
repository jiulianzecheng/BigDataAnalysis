 {{Nihongo|'''''Aiura'''''|あいうら}} is a Japanese [[yonkoma]] [[shōnen manga]] series by Chama. It was first published on [[Niconico]] Seiga and later in [[Kadokawa Shoten]]'s ''4-Koma Nano Ace'' magazine. The manga ran between March 2011 and April 2014. An [[anime]] television series adaptation aired between April and June 2013.
