 '''Biotic''' means anything living. [[Animal]]s, [[plant]]s, and [[microbes]], for example, are all living [[organism]]s so they are considered ''biotic'' parts of the [[environment]]. 
 
 *[[Life]], the condition of living [[organism]]s 
 *[[Biology]], the study of life 
 *[[Biotic component]]s in [[ecology]] 
 *[[Biotic potential]], an organism's reproductive capacity 
 *[[Biotic community]], all the interacting organisms living together in a specific habitat
