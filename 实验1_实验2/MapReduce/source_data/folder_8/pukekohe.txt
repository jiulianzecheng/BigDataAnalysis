'''Pukekohe''' is a city in the [[Auckland]] region. It is sandwiched in between the [[Manukau Harbour]] and the [[Waikato River]]. It was the home of [[Franklin District Council]] until 2010 when it joined up with the supercity of Auckland and prior to that it was Pukekohe Borough Council. It has 9 primary schools and one high school located in the town. It is situated on the edge of [[State Highway 22]]. It has two towns located nearby [[Waiuku]] and [[Tuakau]].
