 '''Buttenwiesen''' ({{Lang-gsw|Battawise|label=[[Swabian German]]}}) is a [[Municipalities of Germany|municipality]] in the district of [[Dillingen (district)|Dillingen]] in [[Bavaria]] in [[Germany]]. 
 
 == Community structure == 
 
 The comune '''Buttenwiesen''' belongs to the region of Augsburg. The community contains the main town Buttenwiesen and the municipality parts Frauenstetten, Lauterbach, Oberthürheim, Pfaffenhofen an der Zusam, Unterthürheim and Wortelstetten.
