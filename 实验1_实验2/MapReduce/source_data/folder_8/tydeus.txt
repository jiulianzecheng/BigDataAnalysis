 In [[Greek mythology]], '''Tydeus''' ({{lang-el|Τυδεύς}}, ''Tūdeus'') was a hero of the generation prior to the [[Trojan War]]. He was the king of [[Argos]]. He was one of the [[Seven Against Thebes]]. He was the father of [[Diomedes]].
