 '''''Playbill''''' is a monthly U.S. magazine for theater fans. Most copies of ''Playbill'' are printed for specific stage productions and are given at the door as the show's [[Programme (booklet)|program]]. 
 
 ''Playbill'' was first printed in 1884. The magazine is now used at nearly every [[Broadway theatre]], as well as many [[Off-Broadway]] productions. Outside New York City, ''Playbill'' is used at theaters throughout the United States.
