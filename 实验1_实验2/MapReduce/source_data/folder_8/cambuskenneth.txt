 '''Cambuskenneth''' ([[Scottish Gaelic|Gaelic]]: ''Camas Sgeanail'') is a village in the city of [[Stirling]], in central [[Scotland]]. It has a population of 250 and is the site of the historic [[Cambuskenneth Abbey]]. It is by the [[River Forth]] and the only road access to the village is along Ladysneuk Road from Alloa Road in Causewayhead.
