 '''Lohnstorf''' was a [[municipality]] in [[Bern-Mittelland (administrative district)|Bern-Mittelland]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Bern (canton)|Bern]] in [[Switzerland]]. On 1 January 2020, the municipalities [[Kirchenthurnen]], Lohnstorf and [[Mühlethurnen]] joined together to become the new municipality [[Thurnen, Bern|Thurnen]]. < ref > [http://www.bfs.admin.ch/bfs/portal/de/index/infothek/nomenklaturen/blank/blank/gem_liste/03.html Amtliches Gemeindeverzeichnis der Schweiz] published by the Swiss Federal Statistical Office {{in lang|de}} accessed 31 December 2019 < /ref >
