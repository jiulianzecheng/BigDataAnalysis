 '''Cosmopolitan''' is a word considered first made by [[Diogenes of Sinope]] and is made of two parts, ''[[cosmos]]'' and ''[[politics]]''.  It can be used for: 
 *A place or person that is proud of its [[multicultural]] [[heritage]] 
 *[[Global citizenship|World citizens]] think that all the people of the world should have and follow the same [[ideal]]s or [[international law]], especially on [[international human rights law|human rights]] and that the idea of nation-states will be therefore outdated, before [[international organization]]s. 
 *''[[Cosmopolitan (magazine)]]'', an American women's magazine. 
 *A living thing has a [[cosmopolitan distribution]] if it lives on all parts of Earth.
