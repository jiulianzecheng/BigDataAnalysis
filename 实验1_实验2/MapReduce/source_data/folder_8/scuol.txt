 '''Scuol''' is a [[municipality]] of the [[district]] [[Inn (district)|Inn]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Graubünden]] in [[Switzerland]]. It is the largest municipality by area in Switzerland. 
 
 Scuol is a well known [[spa town]] and [[ski resort]]. It is the business center of the [[Engadin|Unterengadin]] valley. 
 
 On 1 January 2015, the former municipalities of [[Ardez]], [[Guarda, Switzerland|Guarda]], [[Tarasp]], [[Ftan]] and [[Sent]] merged into the muncipality of Scuol.
