 '''Aldersbach''' ({{lang-bar|label=[[Central Bavarian]]|Oidaschbo}}) is a [[Municipalities in Germany|municipality]] in [[Passau (district)|Passau]] in [[Bavaria]] in [[Germany]]. 
 
 == Geography == 
 === Geographic setting === 
 
 Aldersbach is in the Donau-Wald region in the [[Vils (Donau)|Vils]] valley, about ten kilometers south west of [[Vilshofen an der Donau]], 30 & nbsp;km west of  [[Passau]], 25 & nbsp;km north of  [[Pfarrkirchen]] and 15 & nbsp;km south of [[Osterhofen]]. A stream, the [[Aldersbach (Fluss)|Aldersbach]] (Bach = stream), flows through the town. The region surrounded by Aldersbach and [[Fürstenzell]]  is often known as ''Klosterwinkel'' (''Abbey Corner'').
