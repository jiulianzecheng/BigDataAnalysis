 '''''Isthmohyla''''' is a genus of [[frog]]s in the family [[Hylidae]]. < ref name=Frost/ > < ref name=AmphibiaWeb/ >  Scienitsts made this genus in 2005 after they looked at Hylidae and decided to make it better. Before 2005, the 15 species in this genus were in the genus ''[[Hyla]]'' instead. < ref name= " Faivovich et al. 2005 " / >  They are [[endemic]] to [[Central America]] in [[Honduras]], [[Costa Rica]], and [[Panama]]. < ref name=Frost/ >
