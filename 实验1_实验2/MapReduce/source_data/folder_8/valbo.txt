 '''Valbo''' is a [[Urban areas in Sweden|locality]] in [[Gävle Municipality]] in [[Gävleborg County]] in [[Sweden]]. In 2010, 7,065 people lived there. < ref name=scb / >  It is located southwest of [[Gävle]]. The [[Valbo shopping mall]] is located there.
