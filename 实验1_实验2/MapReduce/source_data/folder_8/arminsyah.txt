 '''Dr. Arminsyah''' (3 May 1960  & ndash; 4 April 2020) was an Indonesian lawyer and politician. He was born in [[Padang]], Indonesia. From 2017 until his death, he was [[Attorney General of Indonesia|Vice Attorney General]]. For a short time in October 2019, he was [[Attorney General of Indonesia|Acting Attorney General]]. 
 
 Arminsyah was killed in a car crash in [[Jakarta]] on 4 April 2020, aged 59. < ref > [https://www.liputan6.com/news/read/4219562/meninggal-dalam-kecelakaan-maut-ini-profil-wakil-jaksa-agung-arminsyah Meninggal dalam Kecelakaan Maut, Ini Profil Wakil Jaksa Agung Arminsyah] {{in lang|id}} < /ref >
