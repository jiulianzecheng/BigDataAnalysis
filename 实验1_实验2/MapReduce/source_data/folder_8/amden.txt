 '''Amden''' is a [[municipality]] in [[See-Gaster (Wahlkreis)|See-Gaster]], in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[St. Gallen (canton)|St. Gallen]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 == Geography == 
 [[File:Seerenbachfälle01.JPG|thumb|left|upright|Seerenbach waterfalls]] 
 Amden has an area of {{convert|43|km2|sqmi|abbr=off}}. It is on a terrace above the north shore of the [[Lake Walen]]. The [[Seerenbach Falls]] is in Amden. It is the highest [[waterfall]] in Switzerland.
