 '''NBCUniversal Media, LLC''' (commonly known as just '''NBCUniversal''') is an American multinational mass media and entertainment conglomerate corporation that is a division of Comcast and is headquartered at 30 Rockefeller Plaza in Midtown Manhattan in New York City.
