 '''Galliformes''' are an [[Order (biology)|order]] of birds containing [[Turkey (bird)|turkeys]], [[grouse]], [[chicken]]s, quails, and [[pheasant]]s. More than 250 living species are found worldwide. Usually, birds belonging to Galliformes order have 4 toes. Three long toes in the front to dig and scratch and one short toe behind.
