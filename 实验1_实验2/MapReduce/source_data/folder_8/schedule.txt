A '''schedule''' is a plan for accomplishing certain things on certain dates. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.dictionary.com/browse/schedule|title=the definition of schedule|website=www.dictionary.com}} < /ref >  Many people use a schedule to help them be organised. [[Job|Jobs]] often require their [[Employee|employees]] to use a schedule and turn in [[Project|projects]] or [[Document|documents]] on certain dates.
