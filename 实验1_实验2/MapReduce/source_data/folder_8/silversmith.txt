A '''silversmith''' is a person who makes things out of the element [[silver]] or creates silverware. < ref > {{Cite web|url=https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/silversmith|title=Definition of SILVERSMITH|website=www.merriam-webster.com|language=en|access-date=2020-04-13}} < /ref >  Silversmiths are similar to [[Goldsmith|goldsmiths]], who make things out of gold or things similar to gold. Silversmithing means making something out of silver. There have been silversmiths since [[ancient history|ancient times]].
