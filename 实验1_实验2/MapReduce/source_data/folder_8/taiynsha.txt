'''Tayinsha''' ({{lang-kk|Тайынша}}) is a [[city]] in the [[North Kazakhstan Region]] of [[Kazakhstan]]. From 1962 to 1997 the city was called ''Krasnoarmeysk'' ({{lang-ru|Красноармейск}}).
