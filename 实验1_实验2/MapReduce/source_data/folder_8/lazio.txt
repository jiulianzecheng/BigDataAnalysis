 '''Lazio''' ([[Latin]]: ''Latium'') is one of the twenty [[regions of Italy]], in central [[Italy]]. The [[Capital city|capital]] is [[Rome]]. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 The region is in [[Central Italy]] with an area of {{Convert|17232.29|km2|sqmi|abbr=on }}. < ref name= " area " > {{cite web|url=http://www.tuttitalia.it/lazio/ |title=Regione Lazio |publisher=Tuttitalia.it |language=Italian |accessdate= 22 February 2017}} < /ref >  It is bordered to the northwest by the [[Tuscany]] region, to the north by the [[Umbria]] region, to the northeast with the [[Marche]] region, to the east with the [[Abruzzo]] and [[Molise]] regions, to the southeast is the [[Campania]] region and to the west is the [[Tyrrhenian Sea]]. The [[Vatican City]] is within the region.

 The main river in the region is the [[Tiber River|Tiber]]. The highest mountain in the region is [[Monte Gorzano]], on the border with the [[Abruzzo]] region, with an [[altitude]] of {{Convert|2458|m|ft|0|abbr=on }}. 
 
 The [[Pontine Islands]] ({{lang-it|Isole Ponziane}}) are an [[archipelago]] in the Tyrrhenian Sea off the west coast of Italy. They are part of Lazio. 
 
 ===Provinces=== 
 Lazio has four [[Provinces of Italy|provinces]]: Viterbo, Rieti, Latina, Frosinone, Rome (The Regional Capital)
