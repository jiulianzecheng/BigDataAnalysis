'''Colic''' is a form of [[pain]] that starts and stops quickly and out of nowhere. It happens because muscular [[contraction]]s of a hollow tube (colon, [[gall bladder]], [[ureter]], etc.). This is done to remove an obstruction by forcing content out. It may cause [[sweating]] and [[vomiting]]. < ref > {{cite book|author=Bhat, Sriram |date=2013|title= SRB's Manual of Surgery|page= 364|isbn=9789350259443}} < /ref >
