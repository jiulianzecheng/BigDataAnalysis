 '''Skor''' is a [[chocolate]] bar made by [[The Hershey Company]]. It is a bar covered with milk [[chocolate]] and has butter toffee in the center of it. Skor was first launched in 1981 in the [[United States]] and 1983 in [[Canada]] under the name, ''Rutnam''.
