 '''Unbiquadium''', also known as '''element 124''' or '''eka-uranium''', is the possible chemical element with [[atomic number]] 124 and placeholder symbol Ubq. ''Unbiquadium'' and ''Ubq'' are the temporary [[systematic element name|IUPAC name and symbol]], until the element is found, confirmed, and a permanent name is agreed on. In the periodic table, unbiquadium is expected to be a [[g-block]] [[superactinide]] and the sixth element in the 8th [[period (periodic table)|period]]. Unbiquadium has attracted attention, as it may be put in the [[island of stability]], making it have a longer half-lives.
