 '''Spiderbait''' is an [[Australia]]n [[rock band]] who have had two top ten albums and three albums reach the Australian top 40.  Their song  " Buy Me a Pony "  was a defining song of Australian rock in the 90's and was #1 on the [[Triple J Hottest 100]] for 1996.  The group had success with their 2004 #1 Australian hit cover of  " [[Black Betty (Spiderbait)|Black Betty]] " .
