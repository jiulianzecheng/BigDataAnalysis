 '''Meerut''' ({{lang-hi|मेरठ}}, {{lang-ur|{{nastaliq|میرٹھ}}}}) is a city in the [[India]]n [[States and territories of India|state]] of [[Uttar Pradesh]]. It is an ancient city. Settlements dating back to the [[Indus Valley civilization]] have been found in the area. The city is located {{convert|70|km|mi|abbr=on}} northeast of the national capital, [[New Delhi]].
