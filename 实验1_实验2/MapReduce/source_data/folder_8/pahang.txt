 '''Pahang''' is a state in [[Malaysia]] on the eastern part of [[Peninsular Malaysia]]. The capital city of Pahang is [[Kuantan]]. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.tourism.gov.my/en/destinations/state.asp?state=pahang|title=Pahang: Adventures Off the Beaten Track|work=[[Tourism Malaysia]]|accessdate=October 4, 2010|archive-date=June 20, 2010|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20100620070632/http://www.tourism.gov.my/en/destinations/state.asp?state=pahang|url-status=dead}} < /ref > 
 
 There are 11 districts in Pahang, which are [[Bera, Pahang|Bera]], [[Bentong]], [[Cameron Highlands]], [[Jerantut]], [[Kuantan]], [[Kuala Lipis]], [[Maran, Pahang|Maran]], [[Pekan]], [[Raub District|Raub]], [[Rompin]] and [[Temerloh]].
