 '''Liminka''' ({{lang-sv|Limingo}}) is a [[municipality]] in [[Northern Ostrobothnia]], [[Finland]]. As of 31 January 2019, it had 10,149 people living there. The neighbouring municipalities are [[Kempele]], [[Lumijoki]], [[Muhos]], [[Oulunsalo]], [[Siikajoki]], [[Siikalatva]], [[Tyrnävä]] and [[Vaala]]. 
 
 Liminka was founded in 1477.
