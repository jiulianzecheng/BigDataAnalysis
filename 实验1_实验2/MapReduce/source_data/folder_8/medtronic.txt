'''Medtronic plc''' is an American healthcare company. The company's operational and executive headquarters are in [[Minneapolis]], Minnesota, and its legal headquarters is in [[Dublin]].  It is in more than 150 countries and employs over 90,000 people. It develops and manufactures [[Health care|healthcare technologies]] and therapies.  It was founded in 1949 in Minneapolis by [[Earl Bakken]].
