 '''''Innervisions''''' is the sixteenth [[studio album]] by [[Americans|American]] [[musician]] [[Stevie Wonder]]. It was [[release (music)|release]]d August 3, 1973 by [[Motown|Tamla]]. Wonder would release his next album ''[[Fulfillingness' First Finale]]'' the following year. < ref name= " AllMusicdiscography " > {{cite web|url=http://www.allmusic.com/artist/stevie-wonder-mn0000622805/discography|title=Stevie Wonder  >  Album Discography  >  AllMusic|publisher=[[AllMusic]]}} < /ref >  All of the [[song]]s on ''Innverisions'' were [[Writer|written]] by Wonder.
