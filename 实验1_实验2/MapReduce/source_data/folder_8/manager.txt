A '''Manager''' is a person who ''manages'' or is ''in charge of'' something. Managers can control departments in [[company|companies]], or guide the people who work for them.  Managers must often make decisions about things. 
 
 According to Henri Fayol, < ref > ''Administration industrielle et générale - prévoyance organisation - commandement, coordination – contrôle'', Paris : Dunod, 1966 < /ref >  a French management [[theory|theorist]], managers must be able to do: 
 # [[planning]] 
 # [[organize|organizing]] 
 # [[leadership|leading]] 
 # [[coordination|co-ordinating]] 
 # [[control]]ling 
 
 The manager is responsible for overseeing and leading the work of a group of people in many instances. The manager is also responsible for planning and maintaining work systems, procedures, and policies that enable and encourage the optimum performance of its people and other resources within a business unit.
