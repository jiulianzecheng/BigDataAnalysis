 '''Valzeina''' was a [[municipality]] of the [[district]] of [[Prättigau/Davos (district)|Prättigau/Davos]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Graubünden]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 On 1 January 2011, [[Fanas]] and Valzeina became part of the municipality of [[Grüsch]].
