'''Neotribalism''' (neo-tribalism; modern tribalism) is a sociological [[concept]]. The idea is that human beings have evolved to live in [[tribe|tribal society]], as opposed to mass society. So they will naturally form [[social network]]s of new  " tribes " . < ref >  Maffesoli, Michel 1996. ''The time of the tribes: the decline of individualism in mass society''.
