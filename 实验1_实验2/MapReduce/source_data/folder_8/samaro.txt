'''Samaro''' is a town in the Sindh province of [[Pakistan]]. It is at 25°16'60N 69°23'45E and has an altitude of 11 metres (39 & nbsp;feet), the town is the headquarters of a taluka (an administrative subdivision) of [[Umerkot District|Umerkot District.]]
