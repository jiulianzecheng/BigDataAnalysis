 '''Riveris''' is a [[municipality]] in the [[Trier-Saarburg]] district, in [[Rhineland-Palatinate]], [[Germany]]. The municipality lies in the valley of the river [[Riveris (river)|Riveris]]. As of 31 December 2018, 402 people lived there.
