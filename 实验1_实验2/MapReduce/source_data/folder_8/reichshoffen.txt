 '''Reichshoffen''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is in [[Grand Est]] in the [[Bas-Rhin]] [[Departments of France|department]] in northeast [[France]]. In 2020, 5,416 people lived there. The church of Saint-Michel was built in 1772.
