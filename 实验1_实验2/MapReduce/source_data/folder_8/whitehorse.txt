 '''Whitehorse''' is the [[Capital city|capital]] of [[Yukon]], [[Canada]]. It is the also largest city in northern Canada. It is on the [[Alaska Highway]] and the [[Yukon River]]. 
 
 ==History== 
 The city was inhabited by [[First Nations]] for thousands of years.
