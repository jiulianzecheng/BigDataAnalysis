 '''Avianca''' is the national and largest airline of [[Colombia]]. It is the second oldest airline in the world. It began flying in 1919. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://xixerone.com/en/worlds-oldest-airlines/|title=The World's Oldest Airlines|first=|last=xixerone|date=7 February 2015|publisher=}} < /ref >  Avianca has grown to become one of the largest airlines in the [[Americas]]. It flies to 121 cities with over 140 airplanes. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.avianca.com/ec/en/experience/during-the-flight/fleet/|title=Our fleet Avianca|website=www.avianca.com}} < /ref >  In 2009, [[TACA Airlines]] and Avianca were combined to create a single airline. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.flightglobal.com/news/articles/avianca-confirms-39strategic-merger39-with-taca-333194/|title=Avianca confirms 'strategic merger' with TACA|first=Brendan|last=Sobie|date=7 October 2009|website=Flightglobal.com}} < /ref >
