 The '''Niantic''' ('''Neh√¢ntick''' or '''Nehantucket''' in their language) were a [[Native American]] tribe. They lived in the southern parts of present-day [[Rhode Island]] and [[Connecticut]]. They were part of the [[Indigenous peoples of the Northeastern Woodlands|Northeastern Woodlands]]. Their language was part of the [[Algonquian languages|Algonquian]]-language group. There was an Eastern and Western Niantic. The Eastern Niantic were with the [[Pequot]]. The Western Niantic were with the [[Narragansett people|Narragansetts]]. Niantic were forced off of their lands and went to [[New York (state)|New York]] and [[Wisconsin]]. A couple of dozen of families claimed to be Niantic in the late 20th century.
