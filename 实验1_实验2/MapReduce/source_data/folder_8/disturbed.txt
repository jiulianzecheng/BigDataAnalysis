 '''Disturbed''' is a [[rock music|rock]] [[band]] from [[Chicago]], [[Illinois]]. They were formed in 1994 and have sold over 11 million albums in the [[United States]] alone and almost 20 million around the world.  All of Disturbed's albums have been number one on the ''[[Billboard 200]]''.  Four of their albums have been number one on their first week on the ''Billboard 200''. < ref > {{cite magazine |url=http://www.billboard.biz/bbbiz/content_display/charts/chart_alert/e3i28d20febe0f2d51bc333698a59cb4f75 |title=Disturbed  >  Chart success  >  Billboard Albums |publisher=Nielsen Business Media, Inc |accessdate=March 11, 2009 |magazine=Billboard |archive-date=April 19, 2009 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20090419103815/http://www.billboard.biz/bbbiz/content_display/charts/chart_alert/e3i28d20febe0f2d51bc333698a59cb4f75 |url-status=dead }} < /ref > 
 
 ==History== 
 Disturbed's original name was Brawl, and had a different singer.  [[David Draiman]] joined Brawl, and changed the name to Disturbed.  In 2000, Disturbed released their first [[Compact Disc|CD]], called ''[[The Sickness]]''.  Since 2000, ''The Sickness'' has sold over 4 million copies in the United States. < ref > {{cite news |url=http://www.roadrunnerrecords.com/BLABBERMOUTH.net/news.aspx?mode=Article & newsitemID=101575 |title=Disturbed Interviewed on KISW's 'The Men's Room'; Audio Available |publisher=Roadrunner Records |date=July 25, 2008 |accessdate=March 6, 2009 |work=Blabbermouth.net |archive-date=July 15, 2011 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110715192018/http://www.roadrunnerrecords.com/BLABBERMOUTH.net/news.aspx?mode=Article & newsitemID=101575 |url-status=dead }} < /ref >   In 2002, Disturbed released a [[DVD]] called ''M.O.L.''.  This has [[music video]]s and videos of the band while on tour.  Later in 2002, Disturbed released another CD, called ''[[Believe (album)|Believe]]''.  After Disturbed finished a tour in 2003, they fired bassist, Steve Kmak.  He was replaced with [[John Moyer]].  In 2005, Disturbed released another album called ''[[Ten Thousand Fists]]''.  One of Disturbed's songs,  " This Moment "  was put in the movie ''[[Transformers (film)|Transformers]]''.  In 2008, Disturbed released another album, called ''[[Indestructible (album)|Indestructible]]''.  Some of the songs from ''Indestructible'' were put on the video game ''[[Rock Band (video game)|Rock Band]]''.
