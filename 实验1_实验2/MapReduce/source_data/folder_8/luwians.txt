The '''Luwians''' also known as ''Luvians'' were a people closely related to the [[Hittites]]. < ref > Encyclopedia of the Ancient World by Thomas J. Sienkewicz - 2002 
 Page 738 < /ref >  Sometime after 2300, the Luwians, an Indo-European-speaking people, settled in southern [[Anatolia]]. < ref > The Encyclopedia of World History - Page 37 by Peter N. Stearns, William Leonard Langer  < /ref > 
 
 The Luwians were related to the Hittites and were the dominant group in the Late Hittite culture. < ref > The New Encyclopaedia Britannica - Page 576 by Encyclopaedia Britannica, inc, Robert MacHenry < /ref >
