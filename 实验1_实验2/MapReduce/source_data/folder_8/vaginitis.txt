'''Vaginitis''' is an [[inflammation]] of the [[vagina]]. It may result in [[:wikt:discharge|discharge]], [[itch]]ing and [[pain]]. [[Vulva]] [[irritation]] or [[infection]] is often involved. It is usually because of the latter. 
 
 A woman having this condition may have these [[symptom]]s: 
 
 *irritation and/or itching of [[Sex organ|genital]] area 
 *foul vaginal [[odor]] 
 *vaginal discharge 
 *irritation/pain with [[sexual intercourse]]
