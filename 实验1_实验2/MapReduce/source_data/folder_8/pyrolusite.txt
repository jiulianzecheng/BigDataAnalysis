 '''Pyrolusite''' is the natural form ([[ore]]) of [[manganese dioxide]]. Its hardness is about 5 on the Mohs scale. It is used in [[battery|batteries]], although many batteries use artificial manganese dioxide. It is an [[oxide mineral]].
