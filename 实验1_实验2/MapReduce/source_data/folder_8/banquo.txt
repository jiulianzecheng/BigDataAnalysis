 '''Banquo''' is a character in [[William Shakespeare]]'s 1606 play ''[[Macbeth]]''. He was a friend of the play's villain [[Macbeth (character)|Macbeth]]. Banquo was the thane of [[Lochaber]]. He was killed by murderers hired by Macbeth so that he doesn't get in the way of Macbeth's reign as king.
