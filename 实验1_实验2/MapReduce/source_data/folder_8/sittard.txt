 '''Sittard''' is a city in the [[Netherlands|Dutch]] province of [[Limburg (Netherlands)|Limburg]]. It has about 37,500 inhabitants. 
 
 Since 2001, it is part of the new municipality of [[Sittard-Geleen]].
