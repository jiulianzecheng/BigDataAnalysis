 " '''LoveWave''' "  is a song by Armenian-German singer [[Iveta Mukuchyan]]. It was released as a single on March 30, 2016. It represented Armenia in the [[Eurovision Song Contest 2016]]. The video for  " LoveWave "  premiered on March 2, 2016. < ref > [http://www.eurovision.tv/page/news?id=armenia_video_premiere_of_lovewave   " Armenia: Video premiere of LoveWave " ] (March 2 2016). Retrieved 3 March 2016. < /ref > 
 
 ==Eurovision Song Contest== 
 On 25 January 2016, Armenia was placed into the first semi-final, to be held on 10 May 2016, and was announced to perform in the first half of the show. < ref > {{cite web|title=Allocation Draw: The results!|url=http://www.eurovision.tv/page/news?id=allocation_draw_the_results|work=Eurovision.tv|access-date=March 4, 2016|date=January 25, 2016}} < /ref >  Mukuchyan performed  " LoveWave "  in position 7, following the entry from the Netherlands and before the entry from San Marino. < ref > {{cite web|last1=Jordan|first1=Paul|title=Running order of the Semi-Finals revealed|url=http://www.eurovision.tv/page/news?id=running_order_of_the_semi-finals_revealed|website=Eurovision.tv|access-date=May 2, 2016|date=April 8, 2016}} < /ref >  The song gained second place in the first semi-final and competed in the final on May 14, 2016, where she performed the song last, following the entry from United Kingdom. < ref > {{cite web|title=We have our first ten finalists|url=http://www.eurovision.tv/page/news?id=50we_have_our_first_ten_finalists|work=eurovision.tv|access-date=May 11, 2016|date=May 10, 2016}} < /ref > < ref > {{cite web|title=Running order for the 2016 Grand Final revealed|url=http://www.eurovision.tv/page/news?id=113running_order_for_grand_final_revealed|work=eurovision.tv|access-date=May 13, 2016|date=May 13, 2016}} < /ref >  The song achieved 7th place with 249 points. < ref > {{cite web|title=Ukraine wins 2016 Eurovision Song Contest|url=http://www.eurovision.tv/page/news?id=ukraine_wins_2016_eurovision_song_contest|work=eurovision.tv|access-date=May 15, 2016|date=May 14, 2016}} < /ref >
