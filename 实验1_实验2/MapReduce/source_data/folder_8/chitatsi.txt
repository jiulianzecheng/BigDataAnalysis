The '''Čitaci''' (Chitatsi) or '''Çitaklar, Chitaks''' are a people of [[Pechenegs|Pecheneg]]-[[Cumans|Cuman]] and [[Slavs|Slavic]] origin, in short, they are communities of [[Turkic peoples|Turkic]] origin fused with various Balkan peoples. According to [[Evliya Çelebi]]'s Seyahatnâme, in the 17th century, those called Chitaks in [[Dobruja]] were medium-sized and well-built people, they came from a mixture of [[Turkic peoples|Turks]], Vlachs and [[Bulgarians]], [[Evliya Çelebi]] wrote in his Seyahatnâme that the Chitak Ladies were very shy and chaste. Chitaks live in the [[Balkans]], including [[North Macedonia]], [[Bulgaria]], [[Albania]], [[Romania]] and [[Serbia]].
