 '''Yeghegnadzor''' is a town in [[Armenia]].  It was named '''Yeghegik'''.  Then it named '''Keshishkend''' until 1935. From 1935–1957 it was named '''Mikoyan'''.  It is the [[Capital (political)|capital]] of the [[Vayots Dzor]] province. The name of the city means  " the valley of canes "  in the [[Armenian language]].
