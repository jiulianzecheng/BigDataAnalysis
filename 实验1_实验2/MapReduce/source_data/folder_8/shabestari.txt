'''Mahmud Shabestari''' ([[1288]]-[[1340]]; {{lang-fa|محمود شبستری}}) is a [[Persian people|Persian]] [[Sufi]] [[poet]]. He was born in the city of [[Tabriz]]. His most famous poem is called ''Gulshan-i Rāz'' (Persian: {{lang|fa|گلشن راز}} meaning  " A Secret Rose Garden " ). ''Gulshan-i Rāz'' is an important [[Sufi]] text.
