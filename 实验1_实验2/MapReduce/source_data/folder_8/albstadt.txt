 '''Albstadt''' [{{IPA|ˈalpʃtat}}] is a [[city]] in the [[districts of Germany|district]] of [[Zollernalbkreis]] in [[Baden-Württemberg]], [[Germany]]. It is in the [[Swabian Alb]] [[mountain]]s, about halfway between [[Stuttgart]] and [[Lake Constance]]. Albstadt is the largest city in the district. 
 
 Albstadt was [[damage]]d by a 6.1 [[moment magnitude scale|magnitude]] [[earthquake]] in 1911, which was the second most powerful earthquake recorded in Germany (after the [[1756 Düren earthquake]]), < ref > {{cite web|url=https://erdbebennews.de/2019/11/das-albstadt-erdbeben-1911/|title=Das Albstadt-Erdbeben 1911|publisher=Erdbebennews.de|date=2019-11-04|language=de}} < /ref > < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.schwarzwaelder-bote.de/inhalt.albstadt-kaum-ein-gebaeude-blieb-verschont.ac564bd1-61f8-42d0-9197-346c806bc3f1.html|title=Albstadt -
