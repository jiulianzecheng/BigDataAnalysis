 '''Aalsmeer''' is a [[List of municipalities of the Netherlands|municipality]] in [[North Holland]] in the [[Netherlands]]. In 2019, 31,728 people lived there. 
 
 Aalsmeer bloemenmarkt is a place that sells, auctions, and delivers flowers. The most popular type of flower is the Dutch national flower, the [[tulip]].
