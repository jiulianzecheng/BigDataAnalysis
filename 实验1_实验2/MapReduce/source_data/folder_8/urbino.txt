 '''Urbino''' is an [[Italy|Italian]] city in [[Marche]], southwest of [[Pesaro]], a [[World Heritage Site]] with a great cultural history during the [[Renaissance]] as the seat of [[Federico da Montefeltro]], duke of Urbino from 1444 to 1482. 
 
 The [[University of Urbino]] is in Urbino. 
 
 In 2019, 14,106 people lived there.
