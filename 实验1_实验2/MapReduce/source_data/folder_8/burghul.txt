 '''Burghul''' ({{lang-ar|برغل|burġul|groats}}) is a [[cereal]] food made from [[wheat]]. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Definition of BULGUR|url=https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/bulgur|access-date=2020-08-15|website=www.merriam-webster.com|language=en}} < /ref >  It is sometimes called '''bulgur'''. It is very [[Popular culture|popular]] in [[Middle East|Middle Eastern]], [[North Africa|North African]], [[Armenia|Armenian]], [[Lebanon|Lebanese]], [[Morocco|Moroccan]], and [[Tunisia|Tunisian]] cuisines. It is made for [[couscous]].
