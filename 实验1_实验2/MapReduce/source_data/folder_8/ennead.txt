 The '''Ennead''' was the grouping of nine [[deity|deities]], most often appearing in [[Egyptian mythology]]. As a three of threes, the [[number]] nine became associated with great [[influence|power]]. The [[Ancient history|ancient]] peoples thought that [[group|groupings]] of nine gods were very important.
