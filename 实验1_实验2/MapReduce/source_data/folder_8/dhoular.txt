'''Dhoular''' is a village and [[Union Councils of Pakistan|Union Council]] of [[Chakwal District]] in the [[Punjab (Pakistan)|Punjab Province]] of [[Pakistan]]. < ref > {{Cite web |url=http://www.nrb.gov.pk/lg_election/union.asp?district=6 & dn=Chakwal |title=Tehsils  &  Unions in the District of Chakwal |access-date=2008-07-21 |archive-date=2008-01-24 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20080124031142/http://www.nrb.gov.pk/lg_election/union.asp?district=6 & dn=Chakwal |url-status=dead }} < /ref >  and is part of [[Talagang Tehsil]]. According to the 1998 census of Pakistan its total population was about 6,500. < ref > 1998 Census of Pakistan < /ref >
