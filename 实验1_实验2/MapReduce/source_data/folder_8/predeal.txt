 '''Predeal''' ({{lang-hu|Predeál}}) is a town in [[Brașov County]], [[Romania]]. It is in the [[Prahova Valley]]. In 2021, 4,020 people lived there. Predeal is at an elevation of over {{convert|1000|m|ft|0|abbr=on}}. The town has three villages: Pârâul Rece, Timișu de Jos and Timișu de Sus.
