 '''Greppen''' is a [[municipality]] in the [[district]] [[Lucerne-Land (district)|Lucerne-Land]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Lucerne (canton)|Lucerne]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 It is at the base of Mount [[Rigi]]. The exclave is separated by [[Lake Lucerne]] from the rest of the district.
