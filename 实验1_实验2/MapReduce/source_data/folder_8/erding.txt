 '''Erding''' ({{IPA-de|ˈeːɐ̯dɪŋ|-|De-Erding.ogg}}) is a [[Town#Germany|town]] in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]], and capital of the [[Erding (district)|district Erding]]. It had a population of 36,469 in 2019. 
 
 The original [[Erdinger]] Weissbier is a well-known Bavarian specialty.  
 
 Erding is around 31 kilometers northeast of central [[Munich]], about a 30-minute drive by car. 
 Regular [[Munich S-Bahn|S-Bahn]] trains connect to Munich and beyond.
