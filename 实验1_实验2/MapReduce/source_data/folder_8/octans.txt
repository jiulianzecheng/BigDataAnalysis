 '''Octans''' is the southernmost [[constellation]]. It represents a [[tool]] that a [[sailor]] would use to find his position using the position of the [[star]]s; the [[octant]]. Octans contains the southernmost star above [[Magnitude]] 6, σ [[Octantis]], which cannot be seen easily.
