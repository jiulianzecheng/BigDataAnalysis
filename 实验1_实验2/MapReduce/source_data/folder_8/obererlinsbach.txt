 '''Obererlinsbach''' is a former [[municipality]] in the district [[Gösgen (district)|Gösgen]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Solothurn (canton)|Solothurn]] in [[Switzerland]]. On 1 January 2006 the former municipalities of [[Niedererlinsbach]] and Obererlinsbach became the municipality of [[Erlinsbach, Solothurn|Erlinsbach]]. < ref name=Mutation > [http://www.bfs.admin.ch/bfs/portal/de/index/infothek/nomenklaturen/blank/blank/gem_liste/02.html Nomenklaturen – Amtliches Gemeindeverzeichnis der Schweiz] {{in lang|de}} accessed 4 April 2011 < /ref >
