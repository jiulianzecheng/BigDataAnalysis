 '''Vendeville''' is a village, in the [[Nord (French department)|Nord departement]]. About 1500 people lived there, in 2020. The village is about {{Convert|9|km}} from [[Lille]]. The village is known for its [[pilgrimage]] dedicated to [[Rita of Cascia|Saint Rita]].
