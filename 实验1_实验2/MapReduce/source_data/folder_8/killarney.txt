 '''Killarney''' is a big town in [[County Kerry]] in [[Republic of Ireland|Ireland]]. The town name is an [[Anglicisation]] of the [[Irish language|Irish]] words ''Cill Airne''. These words mean  " the [[church]] of the [[sloe tree]] " . < ref name=Jennett > {{cite book |title=Cork  &  Kerry |url=https://archive.org/details/corkkerry0000jenn |author=Sean Jennett |publisher=Batsford |year=1977}} < /ref > 
 
 ==Location== 
 Killarney is in the centre of [[County Kerry]]. It is on the [[north east]] side of a lake called [[Lough Leane]]. County Kerry is on the [[south west]] [[coast]] of [[Republic of Ireland|Ireland]]. It is next to the [[Atlantic Ocean]]. 
 
 ==Tourism== 
 Killarney is the main town in the [[Killarney National Park]]. People stay in the town to visit the [[tourist attraction]]s in the area. There are many hotels and tourist shops in Killarney. It is also on the popular {{convert|120|mile|abbr=off}} tourist circuit called the [[Ring of Kerry]]. < ref > {{cite web |url=https://www.independent.co.uk/travel/europe/travellers-guide-the-ring-of-kerry-8535793.html |title=Traveller's guide: The Ring of Kerry}} < /ref >  The route passes spectacular mountain and coastal scenery and many historic and cultural sites.
