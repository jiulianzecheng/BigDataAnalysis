 '''TeleXitos''' is an American [[Spanish language]] [[television network]] owned by [[Telemundo|NBCUniversal Telemundo Enterprises]]. Aimed at [[Hispanic and Latino Americans|the Hispanic and Latin American community]], the network airs a mix of dramatic television series from the 1970s to the 2000s and movies.
