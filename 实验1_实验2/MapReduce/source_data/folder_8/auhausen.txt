 '''Auhausen''' is a [[municipality]] in the district of [[Donau-Ries]] in [[Bavaria]] in [[Germany]]. Auhausen was the site of the 1608 meeting that formed the [[Protestant Union]]. This is also known as the ''Union of Auhausen''. The mayor is currently Wilhalm Dorner.
