'''Homecoming''' is the [[tradition]] of welcoming back former students and members. It also can be about celebrating an organization's existence. It is a tradition in many [[high school]]s, [[college]]s, and [[church]]es in the [[United States]]. It does not exist in Canada. 
 
 ==History== 
 The tradition of homecoming has its start in alumni [[American football|football]] games at colleges and universities since the 19th century.
