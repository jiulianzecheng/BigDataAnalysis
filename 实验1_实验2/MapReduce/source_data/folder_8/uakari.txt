 '''Uakari''' is the common name for the [[New World monkey]]s of the [[genus]] '''''Cacajao'''''. Both the English and scientific names probably came from [[indigenous language]]s. < ref > Barnett A.A. 2004. ''The meanings of Cacajao and Uacari: Folk Etymology in Neotropical Primate Taxonomy.'' Neotropical Primates 12(3): 147-152 < /ref >  They live in [[South America]]n [[rainforests]].
