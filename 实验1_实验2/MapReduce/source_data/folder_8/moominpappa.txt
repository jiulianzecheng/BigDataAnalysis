 '''Moominpappa''' (''Muminpappan'', i.e.  " The Moomin Dad " ) - is a character in the [[Moomin]] series of books by [[Finland|Finnish]] author [[Tove Jansson]]. < ref > {{cite web|author=Ros Coward |url=https://www.theguardian.com/news/2001/jun/30/guardianobituaries.books |title=Tove Jansson  & #124; Books |publisher=The Guardian |date=2013-11-22 |accessdate=2013-11-30}} < /ref > 
 
 Moominpappa was an [[orphan]]. He left the [[orphanage]] to see the [[:wikt:world|world]]. But now he has settled down. Moominpappa wants to be a responsible [[father]] to his family: his wife [[Moominmamma]] and their son [[Moomintroll]]. He attracted to the [[:wikt:sea|sea]] but does not know why. He is almost always wearing his black [[:en:wikt:top hat|top hat]]. Inside the hat is painted:  " M.P. from your M.M. "  to make it different from all the other top hats in the world. Moominpappa also lived on a [[ship]] with three Hattifatteners for a few days. He wanted to watch their behaviour. 
 
 According to his family, he writes down all of his adventures in a [[book]] called  " Memoirs " . He once said that because so many adventures happen to him, he might never finish the book. 
 
 According to Moomintroll, he can fix anything if he is able to get around to it.
