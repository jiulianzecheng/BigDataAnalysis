 '''Sonnen''' is a small municipality in [[Passau (district)|Passau]] in [[Bavaria]] in [[Germany]]. It is in the Danube forest, lower Bavarian forest and is mostly in Passau at a height of 700 to 900 meters. Sonnen is 28 & nbsp;km from Passau, 9 & nbsp;km from Hauzenberg and from the forest itself 13 & nbsp;km.  Sonnen borders with upper Austria which is only 8 & nbsp;km away.
