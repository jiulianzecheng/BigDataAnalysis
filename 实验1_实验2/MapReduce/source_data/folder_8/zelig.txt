 '''''Zelig''''' is a 1983 [[mockumentary]] movie directed and starred [[Woody Allen]] who played Leonard Zelig with [[Mia Farrow]] co-starring with Allen. It was distributed by [[Metro-Goldwyn-Mayer]] and was nominated 2 Oscars in 1984.
