 '''Reipertswiller''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is in [[Grand Est]] in the [[Bas-Rhin]] [[Departments of France|department]] in northeast [[France]]. In 2020, Reipertswiller had 846 people. 
 
 == Geography == 
 Reipertswiller is on the border with the [[Moselle (department)|Moselle]] department. It is part of the ''[[Northern Vosges Regional Natural Park|Parc naturel r√©gional des Vosges du Nord]]''. It has three villages: Melch, Saegemuhle and Wildenguth. 
 
 Neighbouring communes are [[Goetzenbruck]] to the northwest, [[Mouterhouse]] to the north, [[Baerenthal]] to the northeast, [[Wimmenau]] to the southwest and [[Lichtenberg, Bas-Rhin|Lichtenberg]] to the southeast.
