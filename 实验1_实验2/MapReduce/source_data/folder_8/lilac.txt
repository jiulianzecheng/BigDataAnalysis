 '''Lilacs''' are [[plant]]s in the genus '''''Syringa''''', in the family [[Oleaceae]]. Lilacs are popular garden plants in many area. Syringas are another name for the Lilac, as well as the generic name. It is the state [[flower]] of [[Idaho]].
