 '''Shinsegae''' ({{lang-ko|신세계}}, {{kse|004170}}) 
 '''Shinsegae''' ([[Korean language|Korean]]: 신세계, {{kse|004170}}) is an [[South Korea]]n [[department store]], along with many other businesses, headquartered in [[Seoul]], [[South Korea]]. The name Shinsegae literally means  " New World "  in [[Korean language|Korean]]. Its [[flagship store]] in [[Centum City]], [[Busan]], was the world's largest department store at {{Convert|3,163,000|ft2|m2}}, surpassing  [[Macy's]] flagship [[Macy's Herald Square|Herald Square]] in [[New York City]] in 2009. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://community.guinnessworldrecords.com/_Largest-Department-Store/blog/411871/7691.html|title=Guinness World Records: Department Store|access-date=2019-11-10|archive-date=2011-09-23|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110923040357/http://community.guinnessworldrecords.com/_Largest-Department-Store/blog/411871/7691.html|url-status=dead}} < /ref > < ref name=smallbusiness > {{cite news |last= Farfan |first=Barbara |date= 28 December 2018|title=What Is the World's Largest Retail Store? |url=https://www.thebalancesmb.com/largest-retail-stores-2892923 |work=Small Business |access-date= 17 April 2019}} < /ref > 
 
 Shinsegae was originally part of the [[Samsung Group]] ''[[chaebol]]'', separated in the 1990s from the Samsung Group along with [[CJ Group]]  (Food/Chemicals/Entertainment), [[Saehan|Saehan Group]] (Electronic Media/Apparel/Textiles), and the [[Hansol|Hansol Group]] (Paper/Telecom). The chairman [[Lee Myung-hee]] is the daughter of Samsung founder [[Lee Byung-chull]] and younger sister of the chairman of [[Samsung Electronics]] [[Lee Kun-hee]]. 
 
 The group owns the brands Shinsegae and [[E-mart|E-Mart]], and is in direct competition with [[Lotte Department Store|Lotte Shopping]] and [[Hyundai Department Store Group]]. Currently, it is the largest retailer in South Korea. < ref name=reuters > {{cite news| url=http://uk.reuters.com/article/idUKSEO28008020080514 | work=Reuters | title=E-Land in talk to sell stores to Tesco South Korea unit | date=2008-05-14}} < /ref >
