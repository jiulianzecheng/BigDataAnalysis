'''Naat''' is [[poetry]] that speaks well of the [[Islamic]] [[prophet]] [[Muhammad]]. < ref > {{cite web|date=2010-08-18|title=Naat (poem)|url=http://www.lastprophet.info/naat-poem|access-date=2021-09-14|website=lastprophet.info|archive-date=2021-05-08|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20210508170536/https://lastprophet.info/naat-poem|url-status=dead}} < /ref >  Some famous [[scholar]]s in the early days of [[Islam]] wrote Naat. People who recite Naat are known as [[Naat-Khawan]] or [[Sanaa Khawan]]. The practice is popular in South Asia in [[India]] and [[Pakistan]]. Naat is often recited in [[Punjabi language|Punjabi]], [[Sindhi]], or the [[Urdu]] language.
