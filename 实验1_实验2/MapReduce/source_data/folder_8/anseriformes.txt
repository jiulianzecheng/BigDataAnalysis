 The order '''Anseriformes''' contains about 150 living [[species]] of [[bird]]s in four [[family (biology)|families]]: the [[Anhimidae]] (the [[screamer]]s), [[Anseranatidae]] (the [[Magpie goose]]), the [[Anatidae]], which includes over 140 species of [[waterfowl]], among them the [[duck]]s, [[geese]], and [[swan]]s and the â€ [[Vegavidae]], containing the sole genus ''[[Vegavis]]''. 
 
 All species in the [[Order (biology)|order]] are highly [[adaptation|adapted]] to live at the [[water]] surface. All are web-footed for good [[swimming]]. While most live on water, some live mainly on land.
