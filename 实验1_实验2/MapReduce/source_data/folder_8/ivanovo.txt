 '''Ivanovo''' ({{lang-rus|Иваново|p=ɪˈvanəvə}}) is a city in [[Russia]]. < ref name= " Territory " > [http://ivgoradm.ru/history/teritory.htm Территория Иванова на сайте ивановской администрации] < /ref >  It is the [[administrative center]] and largest city of [[Ivanovo Oblast]], located {{convert|254|km|sp=us}} northeast of [[Moscow]]. Ivanovo has a population of 408,330 as of the 2010 Census
