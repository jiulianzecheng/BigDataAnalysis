 '''Aviemore''' ({{IPAc-en|audio=Aviemore.ogg|ˌ|æ|v|i|ˈ|m|ɔər}}; {{lang-gd|An Aghaidh Mhòr}} {{IPA-gd|ˈan ˈɤːɪ ˈvɔːɾ|pron}} meaning  " The big [mountain] face " ) is a town in [[Scottish Highlands|Highland]]s of [[Scotland]], [[UK]]. It is also a [[tourism|tourist]] area. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 ===Climate=== 
 
 Aviemore has an [[oceanic climate]] ([[Köppen climate classification|Köppen]]: ''Cfb''). It has cool temperatures and rainfall throughout the year. The highest temperature ever was {{convert|31.3|C|F}} on 28 June 2018. < ref > https://www.ogimet.com/cgi-bin/gsynres?lang=en & ind=03063 & ano=2018 & mes=6 & day=30 & hora=14 & min=0 & ndays=30 < /ref >
