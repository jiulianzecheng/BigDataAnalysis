 The '''Mohegan''' are a [[Native American]] tribe from present-day [[Connecticut]]. They are part of the [[Indigenous peoples of the Northeastern Woodlands|Northeastern Woodlands]]. The language family is the [[Algonquian languages|Algonquian]] language. It is a nationally recognized tribe in Connecticut. The Mohegan and [[Pequot]] used to be together during [[Colonial America|colonial]] times. The name Mohegan means  " People of the [[Wolf]] " . The Mohegan were attacked by the [[Mohawk people|Mohawk]]. The Mohawk were part of the [[Iroquois|Iroquois Confederacy]]. The Mohegan eventually lost their lands. The Mohegan today are the Mohegan Tribe.
