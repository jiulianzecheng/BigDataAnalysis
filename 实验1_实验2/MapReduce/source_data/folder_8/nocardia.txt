 == Virulence == 
 Most ''Nocardia'' strains have a virulence factor which damages hosts. Transmission of bacteria via inhalation or through open wounds may lead to ''Nocardia'' infection. People with weakened immune systems are more susceptible to ''Nocardia'' infections. < ref > {{Cite journal|last=Lerner|first=P. I.|date=1996-06-01|title=Nocardiosis|url=http://dx.doi.org/10.1093/clinids/22.6.891|journal=Clinical Infectious Diseases|volume=22|issue=6|pages=891–905|doi=10.1093/clinids/22.6.891|issn=1058-4838}} < /ref >
