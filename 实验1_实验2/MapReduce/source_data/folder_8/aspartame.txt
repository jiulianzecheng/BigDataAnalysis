'''Aspartame''' (also known as '''E951''' or '''NutraSweet''' < ref > [http://www.nutrition.org.uk/upload/Aspartame(1).pdf nutrition.org]{{Dead link|date=January 2021 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }} < /ref > ) is an [[artificial sweetener]]. It was discovered in [[1965]] by [[James M. Schlatter]].  It can be used to replace some kinds of [[sugar]]. It is not based on [[sugar]]s. It is not like [[saccharin]].  
 
 [[File:Aspartame.svg|thumb|right|250px|Aspartame]] 
 
 ==Problems== 
 *People with [[Phenylketonuria]] cannot take Aspartame 
 *Aspartame may cause problems with the skin, such as [[rash]]es, or [[dermatitis]] 
 *It may cause  [[headache]] or [[migraine]].
