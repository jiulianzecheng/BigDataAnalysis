 '''Werdau''' is a town in the [[Germany|German]] rural district [[Zwickau Rural District|Zwickau]]. Werdau is a part of the administrative region of [[Chemnitz (region)|Chemnitz]] in the [[Free State of Saxony]]. It is lies on the river [[Pleiße]], {{convert|8|km|abbr=off}} from [[Zwickau]].
