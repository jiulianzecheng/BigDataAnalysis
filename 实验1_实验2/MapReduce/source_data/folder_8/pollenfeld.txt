 '''Pollenfeld''' is a [[Municipalities of Germany|municipality]]  in the district of [[Eichstätt (district)|Eichstätt]] in [[Bavaria]] in [[Germany]]. 
 
 ==Division of Pollenfeld== 
 Districts: Pollenfeld with Wörmersdorf, Seuversholz with Ziegelhütte, Sornhüll with Götzelshard, Wachenzell with Ziegelhütte, Weigersdorf and Preith. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 Pollenfeld is in the [[Ingolstadt]] region, 8 & nbsp;km (approx. 5 miles) north of the district seat (''Kreisstadt'') of [[Eichstätt]] in the [[Franconian Jura]] mountains.
