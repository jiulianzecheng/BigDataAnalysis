 '''Kitwe''' is the second largest city in size and population in the [[country]] [[Zambia]]. 
 Kitwe has a population of 504,194 (2010 [[census]]). Kitwe was found in 1936 as the [[Rhodesia Railways|railway line]] was being built by [[Cecil Rhodes]]' company. 
 The city is home to a number of mining operations including the Mopani Copper Mines.
