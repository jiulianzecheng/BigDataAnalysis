 '''Schwerin''' ({{IPAc-en|UK|ʃ|v|ɛ|ˈ|r|iː|n}}, {{IPAc-en|US|ʃ|v|eɪ|ˈ|r|iː|n}}, {{IPA-de|ʃveˈʁiːn|lang|De-Schwerin2.ogg}}; [[Mecklenburgisch dialect|Mecklenburgian]] [[Low German]]: ''Swerin''; ({{lang-la|Suerina}}, ''Suerinum'') is the [[capital (city)|capital city]] of the [[States of Germany|state]] of [[Mecklenburg-Western Pomerania]]. It has a [[population]] of 97,000. It is the second biggest [[city]] (behind [[Rostock]]) in the state. It is the smallest capital of a German state.
