'''Abwehr''' was the German [[military intelligence]] for the German armed forces called [[Reichswehr]] and [[Wehrmacht]]. It was formed in 1920 and acted to 1945 during all the [[World War II]]. < ref > Holmes, Richard (2009). ''The Oxford Companion to Military History''.
