 '''Rypin''' {{IPAc-pl|'|r|y|p|J|i|n}} ({{lang-de|Rippin}}) is a town in [[Kuyavian-Pomeranian Voivodeship]], 
 [[Poland]]. It is the capital of [[Rypin County]]. 
 
 Rypin is about 50 & nbsp;km east of [[Toruń]]. 
 
 In 2009, 16,950 people lived there.
