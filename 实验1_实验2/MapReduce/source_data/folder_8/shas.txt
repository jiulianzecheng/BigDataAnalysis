'''Shas''', Hebrew: ש״ס , full name: Shomrei Sfarad, Hebrew: שומרי ספרד , meaning ''(Religious) guardians of the Sephardim'', is an [[Israel]]i political party. 
 
 Its ideology is [[left-wing]] [[conservative]], [[Sephardi Jews|Sephardic]]-[[Mizrahi Jews|Mizrahic]] [[Haredi Judaism|Ultra-Orthodox]], and [[Religious Zionist]]. 
 
 Political leader is [[Aryeh Deri]]. 
 
 Shas was founded in [[1984]]. 
 
 Number of seats in the 24th [[Knesset]]: 9 (/). 
 
 Participating in the [[Fourth Netanyahu Government]] and the [[Netanyahu-Gantz Government]].
