 '''Kautenbach''' ({{lang-lb|Kautebaach}}) is a former [[Communes of Luxembourg|commune]] in [[Luxembourg]]. It was part of the [[Wiltz (canton)|canton of Wiltz]]. 
 
 On 1 January 2006, the commune was merged with [[Wilwerwiltz]] to form the new commune of [[Kiischpelt]].
