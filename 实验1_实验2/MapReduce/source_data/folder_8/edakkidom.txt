 '''Edakkidom''' is a village in [[Kollam district]] in the [[India]]n [[States and territories of India|state]] of [[Kerala]], located about {{convert|21|km|mi|abbr=on}} north of [[Kollam]] city. The main attraction is Thettikkunnil Mahadevi Temple.

 ==Demographics== 
 
 This village has a majority population of the [[Hindus]] (Mainly [[Nairs]] and [[Ezhavas]]). 
 
 ==Economy== 
 This village is mainly dependent on agricultural products which includes [[rubber]], [[black pepper]],  [[cashewnut]], [[rice]] Plantain etc. Some of the famous nearby places are [[Kollam]], [[Kottarakkara]], [[Ezhukone]], [[Odanavattom]], [[Punalur]] etc.

 ==Tourism== 
 The [[Kerala backwaters]] of [[Kollam]]/[[Alappuzha]] is 14 & nbsp;km from Edakkidom. Some of the tourist spots near Edakkidom are [[Thenmala]] [[Ecotourism]] (39 & nbsp;km) and [[Palaruvi]] Water Falls (33 & nbsp;km). 
 Edakkidom is a part of assembly constituency Kottarakara and Lok Sabha constituency Mavelikkara. INC, CPI(M) is the major working political party in Edakkidom.
