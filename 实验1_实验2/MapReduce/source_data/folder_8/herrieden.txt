 '''Herrieden''' ({{IPA-de|he:ɐ̯ʁi:dən}}; [[East Franconian]]: ''Herriaden'') is a town in [[Ansbach (district)|Ansbach]], a district ([[Districts of Germany|''Landkreis'']]) in [[Middle Franconia]]. It lies along the upper [[Altmühl]] river, about {{convert|9|km|abbr=off}} (6 & nbsp;mi) southwest of the city of [[Ansbach]], {{convert|47|km|abbr=off}} southwest of the city of [[Nürnberg]], {{convert|95|km|abbr=off}} east of [[Heilbronn]] and {{convert|144|km|abbr=off}} northwest of Munich.  
 
 The first recorded settlement of Herrieden was a monastery called Hasenried (Latin ''Nazaruda'') in 782. < ref > Cf. ''[[Notitia de servitio monasteriorum]]''. < /ref >
