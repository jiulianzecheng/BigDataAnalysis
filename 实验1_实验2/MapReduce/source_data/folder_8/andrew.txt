 The name '''Andrew''' [an-droo] < ref > {{Cite web|title=Definition of Andrew {{!}} Dictionary.com|url=https://www.dictionary.com/browse/andrew|access-date=2023-03-14|website=www.dictionary.com|language=en}} < /ref > is a [[masculine]] given name. It was a very popular name in [[English language|English]]-speaking countries in the 1990s. The name Andrew is of Greek origin and is derived from the Greek word “andreios”, which means “manly” or “masculine”. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Andrew name Meaning and Origin|url=https://babys.name/detail-Andrew-Meaning-Origin-Famous-Similar-Sibling.html|access-date=2023-03-14|website=babys.name}} < /ref >  Many celebrities and famous people have the name.In 2021, there will be 5,570 births named Andrew in the U.S., ranking 57. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Popular Baby Names|url=https://www.ssa.gov/OACT/babynames/|access-date=2023-03-14|website=www.ssa.gov|language=en}} < /ref > 
 
 A [[hurricane]] with the name cut across [[Florida]] and central [[Louisiana]] in 1992, causing more than $35 billion in destruction.
