 '''Mundaun''' was a [[municipality]] of the [[district]] [[Surselva (district)|Surselva]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Graubünden]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 On 1 January 2009, the former municipalities of [[Flond]] and [[Surcuolm]] merged into the new municipality of Mundaun. 
 
 On 1 January 2016, the former municipalities of Mundaun and [[Obersaxen]] merged into the new municipality of [[Obersaxen Mundaun]].
