 '''Valravillon''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]] in the [[Yonne]] [[Departments of France|department]] of central [[France]]. It was created on 1 January 2016 by joining of the former communes of [[Guerchy]], [[Laduz]], [[Neuilly, Yonne|Neuilly]] and [[Villemer, Yonne|Villemer]]. < ref > {{cite web |title=Arrêté du 17 novembre 2015 portant création de la commune nouvelle de Valravillon |url=https://www.legifrance.gouv.fr/jorf/id/JORFTEXT000031740999 |accessdate=5 March 2022 |language=French |date=2015-11-17}} < /ref >
