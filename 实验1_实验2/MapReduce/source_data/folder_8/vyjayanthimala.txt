 '''Vyjayanthimala Raman''' (born 13 August 1936) is an Indian actress and politician. She was born in present-day [[Chennai]]. < ref name= " politic " > {{cite news|url=http://www.hindu.com/2009/05/09/stories/2009050955701300.htm |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20090511161649/http://www.hindu.com/2009/05/09/stories/2009050955701300.htm |url-status=dead |archive-date=11 May 2009 |title=Actor's South Chennai link |work=[[The Hindu]] |date=9 May 2009 |author1=T. Ramakrishnan |author2=R.K. Radhakrishnan |author3=Raktima Bose |access-date=14 May 2011}} < /ref >  Her career lasted from 1949 until 1970. She was a member of the [[Lok Sabha]] from 1984 until 1991 and of the [[Rajya Sabha]] from 1993 until 1999.  
 
 Vyjayanthimala is a member of the [[Bharatiya Janata Party]], though she was elected to the parliament as a member of the [[Indian National Congress]]. < ref name= " congress " > {{cite news|url=http://www.indianexpress.com/Storyold/118195/ |title=Vyjayanthimala quits Congress |work=[[The Indian Express]] |date=28 August 1999 |author=United News of India|access-date=14 May 2011}} < /ref > 
 
 An actress, she is known for her role as Chandramukhi in ''[[Devdas (1955 movie)|Devdas]]'' (1955). She also starred in ''[[New Delhi (1956 movie)|New Delhi]]'' (1956), ''[[Naya Daur (1957 movie)|Naya Daur]]'' (1957), ''[[Aasha (1957 movie)|Aasha]]'' (1957). She appeared in many movies with [[Dilip Kumar]].
