 '''Marisa Isabel Lopes Mena''' (born 25 October 1984), known professionally as '''Mimicat,''' is a [[Portugal|Portuguese]] pop and soul singer and songwriter.  
 
 She participated in the Portuguese Eurovision national selection with the song  " Mundo colorido "  under the stage name '''Izamena''', but did not advance beyond the semi-finals. < ref name= " :0 " > {{cite web|last1=Bleasdale|first1=Joe|date=19 February 2023|title=Mimicat:  " If I Get The Chance To Go To Eurovision, I Will Probably Cry My Eyes Out. " |url=https://escbubble.com/2023/02/mimicat-if-i-get-the-chance-to-go-to-eurovision-i-will-probably-cry-my-eyes-out/|access-date=12 March 2023|website=ESC Bubble}} < /ref > 
 
 In [[2023]], she successfully managed to represent Portugal in [[Eurovision Song Contest 2023|Eurovision 2023]] with the song  " Ai coração " . She placed 23rd in the final. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Eurovision 2023 Results: Voting  &  Points|url=https://eurovisionworld.com/eurovision/2023|access-date=2023-05-14|website=Eurovisionworld|language=en-gb}} < /ref >
