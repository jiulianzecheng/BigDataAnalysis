 '''''Kunbarrasaurus''''' is a [[genus]] of plant eating [[Ankylosauria|ankylosaur]] [[dinosaur]] from the [[Cretaceous]] of [[Australia]]. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.abc.net.au/news/science/2015-12-08/kunbarrasaurus-confirmed-as-new-species-of-ankylosaur/7009982|title=Meet Kunbarrasaurus: Australia's newest dinosaur|first=By Bianca|last=Nogrady|date=8 December 2015|website=ABC News}} < /ref > 
 
 ==Description== 
 Kunbarrasaurus was a small armoured dinosaur that was [[quadrupedal]] and had a long tail.
