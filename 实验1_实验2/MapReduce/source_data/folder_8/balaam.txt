[[Balaam]], [[son]] of Beor, the [[man]] with the open [[eye]], was someone in the [[Book of Numbers]]. Balaam came from [[Midian]]. [[King]] [[Balak]] of [[Moab]] hired Balaam to [[curse]] the Israelites but Balaam accidentally blessed them with [[blessing]]s instead.
