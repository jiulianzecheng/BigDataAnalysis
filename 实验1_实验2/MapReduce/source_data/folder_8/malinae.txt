 '''Malinae''' is a subtribe of plants in the family [[Rosaceae]], tribe [[Maleae]]. Members of this subtribe produce fruits known as [[pome]]s. < ref > {{Cite web|url=https://www.thespruceeats.com/what-are-pomes-2774828|title = What do Apples, Loquats, and Medlars Have in Common?}} < /ref >  Commonly grown members of this tribe include [[Malus]] ([[apple]]s) and [[Mespilus]] ([[medlar]]s).
