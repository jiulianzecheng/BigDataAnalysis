 '''Masku''' is a municipality in [[Southwest Finland]]. As of January 2014 about 9740 people lived there. Neighbouring municipalities are [[Mynämäki]], [[Nousiainen]], [[Rusko]], [[Raisio]], [[Naantali]] and [[Taivassalo]].  
 
 Most people there speak [[Finnish language|Finnish]]. 
 
 Masku is known for the Maskun Kalustetalo, a [[furniture]] [[shop]] that originally started there.
