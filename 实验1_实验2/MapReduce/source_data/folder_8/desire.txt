 '''Desires''' is the emotion of someone or something wanting something. This can be similar to  " wanting " ,  " wishing " ,  " '''longing''' "  or  " '''craving''' " . Desires are closely related to [[Action (philosophy)|agency]]: they [[motivation|motivate]] the agent to realize them.
