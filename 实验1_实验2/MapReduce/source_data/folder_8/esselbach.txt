 '''Esselbach''' is a community in [[Main-Spessart]] in the ''[[Regierungsbezirk]]'' of [[Lower Franconia]] (''Unterfranken'') in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]] and a member of the ''[[Verwaltungsgemeinschaft]]'' (Administrative Community) of Marktheidenfeld. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 
 ===Location=== 
 The community is made up of the main centre of Esselbach as well as the outlying centres of {{ill|Kredenbach (Esselbach)|de||bar|lt=Kredenbach}} and Steinmark. Belonging to Esselbach are the homesteads of Schleifthor, Alte Wachenmühle, Neue Wachenmühle, Faun and Karlshöhe. To Steinmark belong the Kieseckersmühle and the Heinrichsmühle. Schleifthor, Faun and Karlshöhe are part of the Löwensteiner Park, an otherwise unincorporated area.
