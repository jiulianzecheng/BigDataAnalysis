 '''Tityos''' or '''Tityus''' was a [[giant]] from [[Greek mythology]]. 
 
 Tityos was the son of [[Elara]] and [[Zeus]]. He was hunted by [[Apollo]] and [[Artemis]] for attempting to rape their mother [[Leto]]. His punishment in [[Tartarus]] was to be tortured by two [[vulture]]s who fed on his liver, which grew back every night.
