 The '''Lahn''' is a river in [[Hesse]], Germany with a length of {{convert|245.6|km|mi}}. 
 
 Its source is in the [[Rothaargebirge]]. 
 
 It flows through the towns [[Marburg]], [[Giessen|Gießen]], [[Wetzlar]], and [[Limburg an der Lahn]]. 
 
 It joins the [[River Rhine]]  at [[Lahnstein]], near [[Koblenz]].
