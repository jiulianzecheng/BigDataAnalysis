 '''Blekinge''' is a [[provinces of Sweden|historical province]] in southern [[Sweden]]. It's famous for the [[Blekinge archipelago]], and referred to as  " Sveriges trädgård "  (the  " Garden of Sweden " ). Once part of [[Denmark]], it became a part of [[Sweden]] in the [[17th century]].
