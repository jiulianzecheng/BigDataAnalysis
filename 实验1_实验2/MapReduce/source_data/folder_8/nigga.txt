{{About|the colloquial slang|the racial slur|Nigger}} 
 '''Nigga''' is a term derived from the racial slur  " [[nigger]] " . It is generally used by African-Americans (especially young adults and teenagers), because other ethnic groups using it is considered offensive by most. < ref > {{Cite web|date=2011-08-25|title=Straight Talk About the N-Word|url=https://www.learningforjustice.org/magazine/fall-2011/straight-talk-about-the-nword|access-date=2023-06-16|website=Learning for Justice|language=en}} < /ref >  The common use for this word is for a good friend, it can also be defined as a substitute for  " nigger "  in a less serious tone.
