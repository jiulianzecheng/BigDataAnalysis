'''Hyphy''' is a type of [[West Coast hip hop]] music and culture. Hyphy started in [[Oakland, California|Oakland]], [[California]] and became popular in the [[San Francisco Bay Area]] during the 2000s. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://sfstandard.com/2022/04/02/we-were-hyphy-revisits-the-glory-days-of-mid-2000s-bay-area-hip-hop/|accessdate=September 20, 2023|title=‘We Were Hyphy’ and the Glory of Mid-2000s Bay Area Hip-Hop|publisher=The San Francisco Standard}} < /ref > 
 
 The word hyphy was first created by American rapper [[Keak da Sneak]]. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://arts.guardian.co.uk/features/story/0,,1926601,00.html|accessdate=September 20, 2023|title=Ghostridin' the whip|publisher=[[The Guardian]]}} < /ref >  Other popular hyphy artists are [[Mac Dre]], [[E-40]] and [[Mistah F.A.B.]]. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.complex.com/music/a/steven-j-horowitz/hyphy-oral-history|accessdate=September 20, 2023|title=An Oral History of Hyphy|publisher=[[Complex Networks|Complex]]}} < /ref >  Many people view Mac Dre as the pioneer of the hyphy movement. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://thesource.com/2015/07/05/happy-birthday-mac-dre-tribute-to-the-founder-of-thizz-music/|accessdate=September 20, 2023|title=Happy Birthday Mac Dre! Tribute to the Founder of “Thizz Music”|publisher=[[The Source]]}} < /ref > 
 
 [[Street racing]], [[ghost riding]] and car stunt performances called  " sideshows "  are well known things in the hyphy movement. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.eastbaytimes.com/2006/12/30/car-stunts-move-past-hyphy-origins/|accessdate=September 20, 2023|title=Car stunts move past 'hyphy' origins|publisher=East Bay Times}} < /ref > 
 
 People will also  " go dumb "  or  " get stupid " , meaning to dance and make loud noises. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.kqed.org/arts/13929461/at-mistah-f-a-b-s-hyphy-tour-going-dumb-was-spiritual|accessdate=September 20, 2023|title=At Mistah F.A.B.’s Hyphy Tour, Going Dumb Was Spiritual|publisher=KQED}} < /ref >
