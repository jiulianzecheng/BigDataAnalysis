 '''Benguet''' ({{IPA-tl|beŋˈɡet}}; [[Ibaloi language|Ibaloi]]: ''Probinsya ne Benguet''; {{lang-ilo|Probinsia ti Benguet}}; {{lang-pag|Luyag na Benguet}}; {{lang-tl|Lalawigan ng Benguet}}) is a [[Provinces of the Philippines|province]] of the [[Philippines]] located in the southern tip of the [[Cordillera Administrative Region]] in the island of [[Luzon]].
