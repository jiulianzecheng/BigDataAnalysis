 '''Vervins''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]] in the [[Picardy]] [[Regions of France|region]] in the [[Aisne]] [[Departments of France|department]] in the north of [[France]]. On 2 May 1598, [[Henry IV of France]] and [[Phillip II of Spain]] signed a peace [[treaty]], known as the Peace of Vervins.
