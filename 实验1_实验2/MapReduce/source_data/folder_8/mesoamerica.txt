 '''Mesoamerica''' (literally,  " central  America " ) is a [[Geography|geographical]] region. It begins from around the Tropic of Cancer in central [[Mexico]] and ends near [[Costa Rica]]. The term is especially used to mean the native peoples and [[culture]]s that were there before the [[Spain|Spanish]] took over that region. The [[cacao tree]] is native to this area.
