'''Nkroful''' is a [[village]] in [[Ghana]]. The village is known because it was the birthplace of [[Kwame Nkrumah]], the first president of Ghana who led the country to independence from the [[United Kingdom]]. He is also buried there.  
 
 The local language spoken in Nkroful is the [[Akan language]] dialect [[Nzema language|Nzema]] but [[Fante language|Fante]] is also spoken.
