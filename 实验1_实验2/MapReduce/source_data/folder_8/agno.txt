 '''Agno''' is a [[municipality]] of the [[district]] of [[Lugano (district)|Lugano]], [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Ticino]] in [[Switzerland]]. It is not far from the city of [[Lugano]]. 
 
 [[Lugano Airport]] is in this municipality.
