 '''Jerilderie''' is a town of 768 people < ref > {{Census 2006 AUS|id=UCL141400|name=Jerilderie (L) (Urban Centre/Locality)|accessdate=2007-06-30|quick=on}} < /ref >  in the southern [[Riverina]] region of [[New South Wales]], [[Australia]].  It is the only town in the [[Jerilderie Shire Council]] [[Local Government Areas of Australia|Local Government Area]]. 
 
 It can be found along the [[Newell Highway]] {{convert|674|km|mi}} south-west of [[Sydney]] and {{convert|45|km|mi}} north of the [[Victoria (Australia)|Victorian]] state border. Jerilderie's [[post code]] is 2716.
