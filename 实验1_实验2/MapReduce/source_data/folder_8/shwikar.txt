 '''Shwikar Ibrahim''' ({{lang-ar| < big > شويكار < /big > }}) (04 May 1935  & ndash; 14 August 2020) was an [[Egypt]]ian [[actress]]. She started her career in [[Alexandria]] in some [[tragedy|tragedic]] roles. She was born in Alexandria. Her best known role was as Negma in the 1976 western movie ''[[Viva Zalata]]''.
