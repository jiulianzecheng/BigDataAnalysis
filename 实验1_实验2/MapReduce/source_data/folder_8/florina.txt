 '''Florina''' is a [[city]] in [[Greece]] in the region of [[Macedonia (Greece)|Macedonia]].  It is the capital of the '''Florina''' [[prefecture]]. Its population is 15,265 inhabitants. Florina is build at the foot of Varnus mountain. It is built at an altitude of 600 meter approximately. Around Florina there are many forests.
