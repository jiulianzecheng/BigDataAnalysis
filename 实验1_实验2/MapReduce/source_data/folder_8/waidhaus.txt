 '''Waidhaus''' is a municipality in [[Neustadt an der Waldnaab (district)|Neustadt an der Waldnaab]] in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. It is near the border with the [[Czech Republic]], and near the border crossing between Bavaria and the Czech Republic, where the Bavarian [[Bundesautobahn 6|A6]] meets the Czech [[D5 motorway (Czech Republic)|D5 motorway]]. The closest towns are on the German side [[Pleystein]] and on the Czech side [[Rozvadov]]. 
 A gas pipeline and a [[GKK Etzenricht#Power transmission line to the Czech Republic|powerline]] also cross the border between Bavaria and the Czech Republic there.
