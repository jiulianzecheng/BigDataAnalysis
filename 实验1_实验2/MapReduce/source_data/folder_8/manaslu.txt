 '''Manaslu''' ({{lang-ne|मनास्लु}}, also known as '''Kutang''') is the [[List of highest mountains|eighth tallest mountain]] of the world. It is [[Location (geography)|located]] in [[Gorkha District|Gorkha]] of [[Nepal]]. It's height is {{convert|8163|m|ft}}. < ref name=NepalFF2014 > {{cite web | url = http://tourism.gov.np/uploaded/Mountaineering-in-Nepal-Facts-and-Figures-2014.pdf | title = Mountaineering in Nepal Facts  & amp; Figures | date = July 2014 | access-date = 2016-11-11 | publisher = Government of Nepal | department = Ministry of Culture, Tourism  & amp; Civil Aviation | location = Kathmandu | archive-url = https://web.archive.org/web/20150425135031/http://tourism.gov.np/uploaded/Mountaineering-in-Nepal-Facts-and-Figures-2014.pdf | archive-date = 2015-04-25 | url-status = dead | df = ymd-all }} < /ref >  The name 'Manaslu' came from the [[Sanskrit]] word 'Manasa' which means 'spirit'.
