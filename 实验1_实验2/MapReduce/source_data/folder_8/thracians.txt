The ancient '''Thracians''' were a group of [[Indo-European peoples|Indo-European tribes]] who spoke the [[Thracian language]] - a rare branch of the [[Indo-European language family]]. Those peoples lived in [[Thrace]]: the eastern, central and southern part of the [[Balkan peninsula]], and the adjacent parts of [[Eastern Europe]].
