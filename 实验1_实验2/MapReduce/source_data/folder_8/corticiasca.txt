 '''Corticiasca''' was a [[municipality]] of the district [[Lugano (district)|Lugano]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Ticino]] in [[Switzerland]]. On 20 April 2008, the former municipalities of [[Bidogno]], Corticiasca and [[Lugaggia]] joined and became the municipality of [[Capriasca]]. < ref name=Fusion > [http://www.bfs.admin.ch/bfs/portal/de/index/infothek/nomenklaturen/blank/blank/gem_liste/03.html Amtliches Gemeindeverzeichnis der Schweiz] published by the Swiss Federal Statistical Office {{in lang|de}} accessed 14 January 2010 < /ref >
