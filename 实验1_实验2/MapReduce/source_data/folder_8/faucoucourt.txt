 '''Faucoucourt''' is a former [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is found in the [[Regions of France|region]] [[Picardie]] in the [[Aisne]] [[Departments of France|department]] in the north of [[France]]. On 1 January 2019, it was merged into the new commune of [[Anizy-le-Grand]]. < ref > [https://www.legifrance.gouv.fr/affichTexte.do?cidTexte=JORFTEXT000037826670 & dateTexte= & categorieLien=id Arrêté préfectoral] {{in lang|fr}} < /ref >
