 '''Hydrogen Phosphite''' is an [[ion]]. Its chemical formula is HPO < sub > 3 < /sub > < sup > 2- < /sup > . It contains phosphorus in its +3 [[oxidation state]]. [[Chemical compound]]s containing the phosphite ion are called phosphites. Phosphites are the [[salt]]s of [[phosphorous acid]]. They are poisonous. They are [[reducing agent]]s. An example would be [[sodium phosphite]].
