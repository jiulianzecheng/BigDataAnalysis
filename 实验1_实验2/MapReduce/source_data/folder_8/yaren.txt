[[File:Yaren in Nauru.svg|thumb|Location in Nauru]] 
 '''Yaren''' is the largest settlement on the [[island]] country [[Nauru]]. Nauru has no official [[capital (city)|capital]], but many government buildings can be found in Yaren, so some people call it the capital. In 2003, 1,100 people lived there.
