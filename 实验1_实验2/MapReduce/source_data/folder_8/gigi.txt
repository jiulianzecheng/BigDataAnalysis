 '''''Gigi''''' is a 1958 musical romantic comedy [[movie]]. [[Leslie Caron]] plays Gigi. It also stars [[Maurice Chevalier]], [[Louis Jourdan]] and [[Eva Gabor]]. The movie is about relationships in [[Paris]] in the early [[20th century]]. 
 
 The movie was directed by [[Vincente Minnelli]]. The screenplay by [[Alan Jay Lerner]] was based on the 1944 [[novella]] of the same name by the Parisian author [[Colette]]. The film features songs with words by Lerner and music by [[Frederick Loewe]], arranged and conducted by [[Andr√© Previn]]. 
 
 This movie won nine [[Academy Award]]s including Best Picture, Best Director and Best Costume Design. Critics gave the movie very positive reviews.
