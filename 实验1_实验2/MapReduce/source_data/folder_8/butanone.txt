 '''Butanone''', also called '''methyl ethyl ketone''' ('''MEK'''), is an [[organic compound]] with the [[chemical formula]] [[Carbon|C]][[Hydrogen|H]] < sub > 3 < /sub > C([[Oxygen|O]])CH < sub > 2 < /sub > CH < sub > 3 < /sub > . It is a simple [[ketone]] with four carbon [[atom]]s. It [[Odor|smells]] sharp and [[Taste#Sweetness|sweet]], like [[butterscotch]] and [[acetone]] mixed. It is [[solubility|soluble]] in [[water]] and is used as a [[solvent]].
