'''Þrúðr''', sometimes written as '''Thrúd''', is a daughter of the gods [[Sif]] and [[Thor]] in [[Norse mythology]]. Although her name is mentioned in the ''Prose Edda'', it gives no direct powers or other information about her. Thrúd is also one of the [[valkyrie]]s described in ''Grímnismál'' (stanza 36). Thrúd is the goddess of [[power]], of [[tree]]s, [[flower]]s and the [[grass]]. Her name means strength in [[Old Norse]].
