'''Frisia''' is an area along the coast of the [[North Sea]]. [[Frisians]] live in this area. After [[Wijerd Jelckama]] and [[Pier Gerlofs Donia]] lost a rebellion against the Burgundians and the Saxons, Frisia lost its independence. Less than a century later, parts of Frisia were conquered by the Netherlands. Those are now known as the Dutch province of [[Friesland]] or [[Fryslan|Frysl√¢n]].
