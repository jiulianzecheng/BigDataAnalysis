 '''Merenschwand''' is a [[municipality]] of the [[district]] of [[Muri (district)|Muri]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Aargau]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 The municipality of [[Benzenschwil]] merged on 1 January 2012 into Merenschwand.
