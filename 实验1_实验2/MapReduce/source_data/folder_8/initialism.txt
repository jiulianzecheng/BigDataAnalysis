An '''initialism''' is a [[word]] made from the first letters of each word in a phrase. Unlike [[acronym]]s, initialisms cannot be spoken as words: they are spoken letter by [[letter]]. Examples of initialisms are: 
 
 *[[DVD]] - Digital Versatile Disc 
 *[[Compact disc|CD]] - Compact Disc 
 *[[Automated teller machine|ATM]] - Automated (or Automatic) Teller Machine  
 *BRB - Be Right Back  
 *NMHU - Not Much Here, You?  
 *GTG/G2G - Got To Go  
 *JW - Just Wondering  
 *JK - Just Kidding
