 '''Ammertzwiller''' or '''Ammerzwiller''' is a former [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is found in the [[Haut-Rhin]] [[Departments of France|department]] in the eastern [[France]]. On 1 January 2016, it was merged into the commune [[Bernwiller]]. < ref > [http://www.legifrance.gouv.fr/jo_pdf.do?id=JORFTEXT000031665499 Arrêté] 24 November 2015 {{in lang|fr}} < /ref >
