 '''Prijedor''' is a [[city]] in [[Bosnia and Herzegovina]]. It is in [[Republika Srpska]]. About 78,000 people live in the city. Most of them are [[Bosniaks]] and they are [[Muslim]] who follow the [[Sunni]] branch of [[Islam]]. Prijedor is a beautiful city surrounded by beautiful mountains and valleys.
