 '''''Scarthyla''''' is a [[genus]] of tree frogs in the family [[Hylidae]]. < ref name=Frost/ > < ref name=AmphibiaWeb/ > < ref name= " Duellman et al. 2016 " / >  They live in the [[Amazon basin|Amazon Basin]] in [[Bolivia]], [[Peru]], and [[Brazil]]. They also live in [[Colombia]] and [[Venezuela]] and in some [[Caribbean]] countries. These frogs are called '''Madre de Dios treefrogs''' and '''South American aquatic treefrogs'''. < ref name=Frost/ >  They spend time in the water but not all their time. < ref name= " Duellman et al. 2016 " / > 
 
 ==Species== 
 The genus contains two species: < ref name=Frost/ > < ref name=AmphibiaWeb/ > 
 * ''[[Scarthyla goinorum]]'' — Tarauaca snouted treefrog, Madre de Dios treefrog 
 * ''[[Scarthyla vigilans]]'' — Maracaibo Basin treefrog
