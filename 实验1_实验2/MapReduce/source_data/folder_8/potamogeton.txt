 '''''Potamogeton''''' is a [[genus]] of [[aquatic plant]]s. They live in [[freshwater]] such as [[pond]]s and [[lake]]s. They part of the [[family (biology)|family]] [[Potamogentonaceae]]. Their [[common name]] is '''pondweed'''. The genus name means  " river neighbor " . They are also a food source for many fish and [[algae]] eaters.
