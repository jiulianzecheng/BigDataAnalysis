{{Islam}} 
 '''Kalām''' ({{lang-ar|علم الكلام}}, literally  " speech "  or  " words " ) < ref name= " Wolfson " > {{cite book | url=https://books.google.com/books?id=fuv8J-g7EdAC & pg=PA1 | title=The Philosophy of the Kalam | publisher=Harvard University Press | date=1976 | author=Wolfson, Harry Austryn | page=1| isbn=9780674665804 }} < /ref >  is the [[Islam]]ic discipline of seeking theological knowledge through [[dialectic]] (debate and argument). It also promotes [[prudence]] and [[Logic|logical reasoning]]. A practitioner of kalām is called a ''mutakallim'' (plural ''mutakallimiin'').
