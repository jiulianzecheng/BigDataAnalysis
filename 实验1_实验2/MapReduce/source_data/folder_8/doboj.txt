 '''Doboj''' ({{lang-sr-cyrl| Добој}}) is a city in [[Republika Srpska]], [[Bosnia and Herzegovina]]. It is on the banks of [[Bosna (river)|Bosna]] river. As of 2013, 71,441 people lived there.
