 '''Kadampanad''' is a village in [[Pathanamthitta district]], [[Kerala]], [[India]].

 ==Demographics== 
 
 According to 2011 [[census]], Kadampanad has a population of 26839 with 12769 males and 14070 females. < ref > {{cite web|title=India Census |url=http://www.censusindia.gov.in/Census_Data_2001/Village_Directory/Population_data/Population_5000_and_Above.aspx |access-date=13 April 2013 |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20080211182048/http://www.censusindia.gov.in/Census_Data_2001/Village_Directory/Population_data/Population_5000_and_Above.aspx |archive-date=11 February 2008 }} < /ref >    The literacy rate as per the census data is 92.2%
