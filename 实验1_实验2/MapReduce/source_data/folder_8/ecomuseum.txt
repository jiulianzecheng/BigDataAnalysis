An '''ecomuseum''' is a [[museum]] which shows things about the identity of a place or [[landscape]].  Many of the people who live in that place help to organize [[lifestyle|things]] in the museum.  The ecomuseum tries to help the [[wikt:welfare|welfare]] and [[development]] of the local [[community]].
