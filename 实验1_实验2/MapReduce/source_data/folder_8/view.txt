 A '''view''' is what can be seen in somebody's [[vision]]. A view can also be a very [[scenic]] image. It can also be someone's [[opinion]] on something or how they look at something. 
 
 There are many types of views. 
 
 * [[Panorama]]s 
 * [[Eye]] vision 
 * [[Photograph]] 
 * [[Camera]] 
 * [[God's eye view]] 
 * [[Point of view]]
