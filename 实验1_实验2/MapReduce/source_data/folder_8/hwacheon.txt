 '''Hwacheon County''' (''Hwacheon-gun'') is a [[Administrative divisions of South Korea|county]] in [[Gangwon Province, South Korea|Gangwon Province]], [[South Korea]]. < ref > {{Cite web|url=https://english.visitkorea.or.kr:1001/enu/SI/SI_EN_3_6.jsp?gotoPage=1 & cid=1784071 & out_service=|title=Hwacheon|last=|first=|date=|website=|access-date=}}{{Dead link|date=January 2020 |bot=InternetArchiveBot |fix-attempted=yes }} < /ref >  The northern border is, in some places, within nine kilometres of the [[Korean Demilitarized Zone]]. Neighboring counties are [[Cheorwon]] to the northwest and north, [[Yanggu County, Gangwon|Yanggu]] to the east, [[Chuncheon]] to the south, and the [[Gyeonggi-do]] province to the southwest. The county consists largely of mountains and rivers, between which are small farming communities, military bases and military training grounds. The area is renowned for its rivers, lake trout, indigenous otters, and natural scenery.
