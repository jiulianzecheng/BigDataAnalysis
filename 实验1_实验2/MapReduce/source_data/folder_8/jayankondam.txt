'''Jayankondam''' is the [[town]] in [[Udayarpalayam taluk]]. It is near [[Gangaikonda Cholapuram]], the famous [[Brihadeshwara Temple]]. It is 10 & nbsp;km away from Jayankondam. It was ruled by [[Chola dynasty|Cholas]]. Jayankondam is well known the [[cashew nut]]s.
