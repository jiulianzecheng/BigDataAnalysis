 '''Platanaceae''' is a [[Family (biology)|family]] of [[flowering plant]]s. The family has only one [[genus]] ''[[platanus]]''. This genus has six to ten species of tall [[tree]]s. The trees grow naturally in [[temperate]] and [[subtropical]] [[region]]s of the [[Northern Hemisphere]]. In all species, the flowers grow in dense groups of small flowers. The [[fruit]]s are small [[achene]]s with [[hair]]s near the base.
