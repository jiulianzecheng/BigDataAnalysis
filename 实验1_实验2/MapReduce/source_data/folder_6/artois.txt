 '''Artois''' is a former province of northern [[France]]. Its [[territory]] covers an area of about 4,000 km². It has the population of about one million. [[Arras]], [[Saint-Omer]], [[Lens, Pas-de-Calais|Lens]], and [[Béthune]] are some major cities in the region.
