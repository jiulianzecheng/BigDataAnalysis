 '''Hazaristan''' or '''Hazarajat''' ([[Dari Persian]]: {{nq|هزارستان}}) is a large mountainous area in the central highlands of [[Afghanistan]] which is the main and the historical land of the [[Hazara people]]. Hazaristan areas including provinces such as [[Bamyan Province|Bamyan]], [[Daykundi Province|Daykundi]], [[Ghor Province|Ghor]], [[Ghazni Province|Ghazni]], [[Urozgan Province|Urozgan]], [[Maidan Wardak Province|Maidan Wardak]], [[Parwan Province|Parwan]], [[Samangan Province|Samangan]], [[Baghlan Province|Baghlan]], [[Badghis Province|Badghis]] and some other areas.
