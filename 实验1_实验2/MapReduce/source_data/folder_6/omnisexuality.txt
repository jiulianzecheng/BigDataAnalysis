[[File:Omnisexuality flag.svg|thumb|Omnisexual pride flag]]'''Omnisexuality''' is a [[sexual orientation]]. A person who is omnisexual can be attracted to all [[Gender identity|genders]]. Gender will usually play a role in the person's [[Sexual attraction|attraction]]. < ref > {{Cite web|title=» Omni|url=https://orientando.org/listas/lista-de-orientacoes/omni/|access-date=2020-12-02|language=pt-BR}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite web|title=If You're Attracted to All Genders, This LGBTQ+ Identity Could Describe You|url=https://www.health.com/mind-body/lgbtq-health/omnisexual|access-date=2021-07-20|website=Health.com|language=en}} < /ref >  It can be seen as a form of [[multisexuality]], < ref > {{Cite web|date=2021-04-23|title=Multisexual Youth Mental Health: Risk and Protective Factors for Bisexual, Pansexual, and Queer Youth Who are Attracted to More than One Gender|url=https://www.thetrevorproject.org/2021/04/23/multisexual-youth-mental-health-risk-and-protective-factors-for-bisexual-pansexual-and-queer-youth-who-are-attracted-to-more-than-one-gender/|access-date=2021-07-20|website=The Trevor Project|language=en-US}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite web|title=Identity Vocabulary: Multisexual Edition|url=http://www.thecenterbak.org/2/post/2019/07/identity-vocabulary-multisexual-edition.html|access-date=2021-07-20|website=The Center for Sexuality and Gender Diversity|language=en}} < /ref >  or non-[[monosexuality]]. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Quit Censoring My (And Others') Bi Identities|url=http://bi.org/en/articles/quit-censoring-my-and-others-bi-identities/|access-date=2021-07-20|website=Bi.org|language=en}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite web|date=2021-08-03|title=Omnisessualità e omnisessuale: significato LGBT e bandiera|url=https://progettogenderqueer.blog/2021/08/03/omnisessuale-trisessuale-omnisessualita-trisessualita/|access-date=2021-11-21|website=Progetto Genderqueer - Cultura Transgender Non Med e Non Binary|language=it-IT}} < /ref > 
 
 Omnisexuality can be described as being under the umbrella of [[Bisexuality|bisexuality]]. It is similar to, but distinctly different  from [[pansexuality]].
