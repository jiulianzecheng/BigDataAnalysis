 '''Mitterfels''' is a [[Municipalities of Germany|municipality]] in [[Straubing-Bogen]] in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. It is the head of the [[Verwaltungsgemeinschaft Mitterfels]]. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 
 Mitterfels is in the region between the [[Danube]] and the [[Bavarian Forest]].
