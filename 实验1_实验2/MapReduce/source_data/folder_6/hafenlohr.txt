 '''Hafenlohr''' is a community in [[Main-Spessart]] in the ''[[Regierungsbezirk]]'' of [[Lower Franconia]] (''Unterfranken'') in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]] and a member of the ''Verwaltungsgemeinschaft'' (Administrative Community) of Marktheidenfeld. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 
 ===Location=== 
 Hafenlohr is in the Würzburg Region, about 3 & nbsp;km from [[Marktheidenfeld]]. It is on the convergence of the rivers [[Hafenlohr (river)|Hafenlohr]] and [[Main (river)|Main]]. 
 
 ===Subdivision=== 
 The community has the following ''Gemarkungen'' (traditional rural cadastral areas): Hafenlohr, Windheim, Fürstlich Löwenstein'scher Park.
