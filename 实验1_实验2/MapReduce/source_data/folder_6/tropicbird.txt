 '''Tropicbirds''' are [[Pelagic zone|pelagic]] [[Seabird|seabirds]] that occur in [[Tropics|tropical]] areas and seas of the world. There are three living [[species]]. Before they were put in their own group, they were thought to belong to the Pelicanformes (which also contains [[Pelican|pelcans]], [[Cormorant|cormorants]] and [[Gannet|gannets]]).
