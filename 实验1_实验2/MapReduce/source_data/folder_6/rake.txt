 A '''rake''' is an [[garden]] and [[farm]] tool. People use rake to gather loose things like [[leaf|leaves]], [[hay]], and [[grass]]. 
 
 In [[gardening]], a rake loosens [[soil]], and removes [[weed]] and dead grass from [[lawn]]s.
