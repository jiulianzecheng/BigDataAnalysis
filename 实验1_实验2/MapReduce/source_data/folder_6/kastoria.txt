 '''Kastoria''' is a [[Greece|Greek]] city, and it is one of 51 [[prefecture]]s of [[Greece]] in the region of [[Macedonia (Greece) |Macedonia]]. Kastoria it is built on the bank of [[Lake Orestiada]]. There are 17,038 people living in the city. Kastoria has an old neighbourhood which is picturesque, and it is named Doltso. It has nice old houses with particular local architecture. 
 
 The name ''Kastoria'' means  " place of the beavers "  which used to live in the lake. < ref > {{cite book |last=Ring |first= Trudi |author2= Robert M. Salkin, Sharon La Boda |title= International Dictionary of Historic Places: Southern Europe |publisher= Routledge |date= 1996 |page=361 |url= https://books.google.com/books?id=74JI2UlcU8AC & dq=kastoria%2Bbeaver & pg=PA361 |isbn= 1884964028 }} < /ref >  These have all been hunted and killed for their fur.
