 ==Geography== 
 Katsushika is in eastern Tokyo. It borders [[Edogkawa, Tokyo|Edogawa]] on the south.  [[Sumida, Tokyo|Sumida]] and  [[Arakawa, Tokyo|Araikawa]] are on the ward's western border. [[Adachi, Tokyo|Adachi]] is on the northeast. 
 
 The cities of [[Matsudo, Chiba|Matsudo]], [[Misato, Saitama|Misato]] and [[Yashio, Saitama|Yashio]] in [[Chiba Prefecture]] are part of the northeast border of the ward.
