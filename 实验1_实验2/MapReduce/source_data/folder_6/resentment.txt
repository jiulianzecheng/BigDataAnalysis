{{Emotion}} 
 '''Resentment''' (also called '''ranklement''' or '''bitterness''') is an [[emotion]] that can mean many things < ref > D M Marino ed.,'' On Resentment'' (2013) p. 301-3 < /ref >  that has been seen as a mixture of [[disappointment]], [[disgust]], [[anger]], and [[fear]]. < ref > TenHouten, W. D. (2007). ''General Theory of Emotions and Social Life''. Routledge. < /ref > 
 
 It can be someone's reaction to injustices or something unfair that has happened in their life. It can also be cause by [[guilt]], someone feeling guilty over something happening that they felt they could have controlled.
