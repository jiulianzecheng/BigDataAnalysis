 '''Shante Scott Franklin''' (born April 4, 1981), better known by his stage name '''Currensy''' (stylized as '''Curren$y''') is an American rapper, songwriter and record executive. Formerly signed to [[Lil Wayne]]'s record label [[Young Money Entertainment]], he signed himself to [[No Limit Records]] as a teenager. After releasing [[mixtape]]s, his first album ''This Ain't No Mixtape'' was released in April 2009. 
 
 After gaining stream in 2007, he made a shocking move leaving Young Money to do his own thing on an airplane. Currensy said,  " I ain't see nothing coming out of there. I have been on Young Money for like 4 years. We had the  " Where The Cash At "  video game, you know, that was cool " .
