 '''''Housefull''''' is a 2010 Indian Hindi-language [[comedy film]] directed by [[Sajid Khan (director)|Sajid Khan]] and produced by [[Sajid Nadiadwala]]. The movie is partly adapted from the 1998 [[Tamil language|Tamil]] drama ''[[Kaathala Kaathala]]''. The dialogues were done by Anvita Dutt, while Nadiadwala wrote the storyline and Rameshwar S. Bhagat served as the editor. < ref name= " boxcinema " > {{cite web|last=ADESARA|first=HETAL|url=http://www.businessofcinema.com/news.php?newsid=16102|title=Box Office: Housefull holds on Monday with Rs 50 million net collections in India|publisher=Businessofcinema|date=4 May 2010|access-date=6 May 2010|url-status=dead|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20101130111356/http://businessofcinema.com/news.php?newsid=16102|archive-date=30 November 2010|df=dmy-all}} < /ref >  It became the first installment of the [[Housefull film series|''Housefull'']] franchise.
