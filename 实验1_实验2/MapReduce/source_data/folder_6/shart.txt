'''''Shart''''' is the name of several [[Bollywood]] and [[Tollywood]] [[movie]]s. 
 
 *''Shart'' (1954), [[film director|directed]] by Bibhuti Mitra, starring Deepak and Shashikala 
 *''Shart'' (1969), directed by Kewal Misra, starring Sanjay Khan and Mumtaz 
 *''Shart'' (1986), directed by Ketan Anand, starring Naseeruddin Shah and Shabana Azmi 
 *''Shart: The Challenge'' (2004), directed by Puri Jagannadh, starring Amrita Arora and Snehal Dabi
