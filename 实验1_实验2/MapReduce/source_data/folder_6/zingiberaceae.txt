 '''Zingiberaceae''', or the '''Ginger family''', is a family of [[flowering plant]]s consisting of aromatic [[perennial]] [[herb]]s with creeping horizontal or tuberous [[rhizome]]s, comprising ca. 52 genera and more than 1300 species, distributed throughout tropical [[Africa]], [[Asia]], and the [[Americas]].
