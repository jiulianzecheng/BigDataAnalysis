The word  " '''fence''' "  may have very different meanings. 
 
 *A '''''[[fence (wall)|fence]]''''' is a barrier which is often made of [[plank]]s of wood. 
 
 *A '''person''' described as a '''''[[fence (person)|fence]]''''' may be selling illegal things. 
 *'''[[Fencing]]''' is a [[sport]] that includes two people with [[Sword|swords]]. 
 
 The [[Context (language)|context]] decides the [[meaning]] in cases like this.
