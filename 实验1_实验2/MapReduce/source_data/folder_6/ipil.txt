'''Ipil''' is a municipality in the [[Philippines]]. It is the capital of the [[Provinces of the Philippines|province]] of [[Zamboanga Sibugay]]. According to the 2020 census, 89,401 people lived there. 
 
 [[Ipil Airport]] is the only airport in Zamboanga Sibugay.
