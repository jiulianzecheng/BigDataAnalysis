 '''Kamyaran''' ({{lang-ku|Kamyaran}}, {{lang-fa|کامیاران}}) is a city in the Kurdistan province of [[Iran]]. Its people speak in [[Kurdish language|Kurdish]]. They are followers of [[Islam]]. There is also a [[Shia]] minority and a few [[Jewish]] families in Kamyaran. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://web.archive.org/web/*/http://www.amar.org.ir/DesktopModules/FTPManager/upload/upload2360/newjkh/newjkh/12.xls|title=Wayback Machine|website=web.archive.org}} < /ref > 
 
 At the 2006 census, there were 46,760 people, in 11,186 families.
