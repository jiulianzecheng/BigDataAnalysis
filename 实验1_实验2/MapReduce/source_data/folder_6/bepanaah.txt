 ==Plot== 
 The plot revolves around Aditya Hooda and Zoya Siddiqui, two individuals brought together through their spouses' betrayal. Zoya and Yash are a married couple. Aditya Hooda, a pilot, and Pooja, an artist, are a another married couple.

 ==Airing Release == 
 The Series Broadcast aired Every Monday to Friday at 9:00 PM [[Indian Standard Time|IST]] from 19 March 2018 to 26 May 2018. But from 28 March 2018, The Series is being shifted from 9:00 PM [[Indian Standard Time|IST]] to 9:30 PM [[Indian Standard Time|IST]] because of launch of ''[[Roop - Mard Ka Naya Swaroop]]'' at 9:00 PM [[Indian Standard Time|IST]] Primetime Slot on 28 May 2018. Now, ''[[Roop - Mard Ka Naya Swaroop]]'' will air Every Monday to Friday at 9:00 PM [[Indian Standard Time|IST]] from 28 May 2018 and [[Bepannah]] will now air Every Monday to Friday at a new time 9:30 PM [[Indian Standard Time|IST]] from 28 May 2018. [[Bepannah]] will be replacing the show ''[[Laado 2 (TV series)|Laado - Veerpur Ki Mardani]]'' at the 9:30 PM [[Indian Standard Time|IST]] Primetime Slot as ''[[Laado 2 (TV series)|Laado - Veerpur Ki Mardani]]'' is going off-air. 
 The series started airing from 19 March 2018 on [[Colors TV]] and is aired every Monday to Friday at 9:00 PM [[Indian Standard Time|IST]].
