A '''petrochemical''' is a [[Organic compound|chemical]] product that is made from [[petroleum]], [[natural gas]] or other [[hydrocarbon]]s. 
 
 Some petrochemicals include [[alcohol]], [[antiseptic]]s, artificial [[rubber]], [[detergent]]s, [[drugs]], [[explosives]], [[Food additive|food additives]], insecticides, perfume, plastic, and textile fiber.
