 '''Amberscript''' is a [[SaaS]] software company based in [[Amsterdam]], the [[Netherlands]]. < ref > {{Cite web|last=Catia|title=Amberscript|url=https://www.reach-incubator.eu/startups/amberscript/|access-date=2022-07-21|website=REACH|language=en-US}} < /ref >  Amberscript`s technology allows converting audio or video files to subtitles or text. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Audio transcription with Amberscript|url=https://bmslab.utwente.nl/info-hub/audio-transcription/|access-date=2022-07-21|website=BMS LAB — Where Technology Meets Life|language=en-US|archive-date=2022-05-28|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20220528170121/https://bmslab.utwente.nl/info-hub/audio-transcription/|url-status=dead}} < /ref > 
 
 == History == 
 The company was founded in December 2017 in Amsterdam by Peter-Paul de Leeuw, Thomas Dieste, and Timo Behrens. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Amberscript, Amsterdam based company raises € 8.65 million to convert audio and video to text and subtitles using AI {{!}} Data Science District|url=https://datasciencedistrict.nl/amberscript-amsterdam-based-company-raises-e-8-65-million-to-convert-audio-and-video-to-text-and-subtitles-using-ai/|access-date=2022-07-21|language=en-US}} < /ref > 
 
 The first product was a speech-to-text in [[Dutch]], an online tool that converts audio into an editable text file. < ref > {{Cite web|last=BV|first=Amberscript|title=Amberscript BV {{!}} International Fairs Directory {{!}} Software, Hardware, IT Information Technologies,Multimedia, TV, Audiovisual|url=https://inter-fair.com/Announcement/Announcement?strPath=AMBERSCRIPT|access-date=2022-07-21|website=www.inter-fair.com}} < /ref > 
 
 In late 2018, the company made manual transcription and subtitling services available. < ref > {{Cite web|last=Bond|first=Esther|date=2021-11-09|title=Dutch Transcription Startup Amberscript Raises USD 10m in Series A|url=https://slator.com/dutch-transcription-startup-amberscript-raises-usd-10m-in-series-a/|access-date=2022-07-21|website=Slator|language=en-US}} < /ref > 
 
 In November 2020, Amberscript launched live captions with the state parliament of Mecklenburg-Western Pomerania. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Google text to speech engine sound of text|url=https://www.voiceoftext.com}} < /ref >
