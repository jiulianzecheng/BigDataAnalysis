 '''''Timecop''''' is a 1994 American [[science fiction]] [[action movie]]. It is about a federal agent who travels in time. [[Jean-Claude Van Damme]] plays Max Walker. [[Mia Sara]] plays Melissa. 
 
 ''Timecop'' got mixed reviews from critics. It made a lot of money at the [[box office]].
