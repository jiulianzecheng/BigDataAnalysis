 '''Pindamonhangaba''' is a city in the state of [[São Paulo (state)|São Paulo]], [[Brazil]]. It is located in the [[Paraíba valley]], between the two most active production regions in the country, [[São Paulo (city)|São Paulo]] and [[Rio de Janeiro (city)|Rio de Janeiro]].    
 
 This place name comes from the [[Tupi language|Old Tupi language]] meaning ''where hooks are made'' or, according to a different meanings, ''where the river bends''.
