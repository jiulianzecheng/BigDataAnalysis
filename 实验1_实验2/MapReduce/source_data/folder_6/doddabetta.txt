Doddabetta is the highest peak in the [[Nilgiri Hills]] and the state of [[Tamil Nadu]]. It is also one of the highest peak in South India. This peak is located in the [[The Nilgiris district|Nilgiris district]] of Tamil Nadu. This summit is 9km away from [[Ooty]], the district headquarters.
