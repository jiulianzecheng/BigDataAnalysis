
 {{nihongo|'''Katei'''|嘉禎|}} was a {{nihongo|[[Japanese era name]]|年号,|''nengō'',|lit.  " year name " }} after ''[[Bunryaku]]'' and before ''[[Ryakunin]].''  This period started in September 1235 and ended in November 1238. < ref > [[Louis-Frédéric|Nussbaum, Louis-Frédéric]]. (2005). [https://books.google.com/books?id=p2QnPijAEmEC & pg=PA491   " ''Katei'' " ] in ''Japan encyclopedia'', p. 491. < /ref >  The reigning emperor was {{nihongo|[[Emperor Shijō|Shijō]]''-tennō''|四条天皇}}. < ref > [[Isaac Titsingh|Titsingh, Isaac]]. (1834). [https://books.google.com/books?id=18oNAAAAIAAJ & pg=PA242  ''Annales des empereurs du Japon'', pp. 242-243]; [[H. Paul Varley|Varley, H. Paul]]. (1980). ''Jinnō Shōtōki.'' p. 227. < /ref > 
 
 ==Events of the ''Katei'' Era== 
 * '''1235''' (''Katei 1, 11th month''): [[Kujō Yoritsune]] is raised to the 2nd rank of the 2nd class in the court [[wikt:hierarchy|hierarchy]]. < ref name= " titsingh243 " > Titsingh, [https://books.google.com/books?id=18oNAAAAIAAJ & pg=PA243  p. 243.] < /ref > 
 * '''1236''' (''Katei 2, 7th month''): Yoritsune is raised to the 1st rank of the 2nd class in the court hierarchy. < ref name= " titsingh243 " / > 
 * '''1237''' (''Katei 3, 8th month''): Yoritsune ordered the building of a mansion in the [[Rokuhara]] section of Kyoto. < ref name= " titsingh243 " / >
