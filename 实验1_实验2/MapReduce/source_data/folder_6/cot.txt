 A '''cot''' is a small, single occupancy [[bed]]. Cots are usually found in residential institutions like [[prison]]s and [[psychiatric hospital]]s, [[military base]]s, [[boarding school]]s, [[college]] dormitories, and [[camp]]s as well as places that are turned into temporary shelters during emergencies.
