 '''Acquarossa''' is the capital of the district of [[Blenio (district)|Blenio]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Ticino]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 Acquarossa was created on 4 April 2004, when the former municipalities of [[Castro, Ticino|Castro]], [[Corzoneso]], [[Dongio]], [[Largario]], [[Leontica]], [[Lottigna]], [[Marolta]], [[Ponto Valentino]] and [[Prugiasco]] merged to form the new municipality of Acquarossa.
