 '''Amroha''' is a city in the Indian state of [[Uttar Pradesh]]. Amroha is located north-west of [[Moradabad]] city. As per Census 2011 India, the population of the city is 198,471 out of 103,097 are males and 95,374 are females. < ref > {{cite web | url=https://www.census2011.co.in/census/city/100-amroha.html | title=Amroha City Population Census 2011-2022  & #124; Uttar Pradesh }} < /ref > 
 
 Amroha is known for the production of different types of [[mangoes]]. < ref > {{Cite web|url=https://www.bbc.com/hindi/regionalnews/story/2004/08/040807_mango_amroha.shtml|title=BBCHindi|website=www.bbc.com|access-date=18 December 2019}} < /ref >  Some of the [[industries]] in Amroha includes [[sugar]] milling, cotton  &  textiles, hand-loom [[weaving]] and [[pottery]] making.
