 '''Vaud''' is a [[cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Switzerland]]. It borders the cantons of [[Geneva (canton)|Geneva]], [[Neuchâtel]], [[Fribourg (canton)|Fribourg]], [[Berne]] and [[Valais]] (west to east).  The [[capital city]] is [[Lausanne]]. About 790,000 people live in the canton. Of these, about 138,000 live in Lausanne. About 82% of the population speak [[French language|French]] as their first language.
