 '''Cagayan''' ({{lang-ilo|Probinsia ti Cagayan}}; {{lang-ibg|Provinsiya na Cagayan}}; {{lang-itv|Provinsiya ya Cagayan}}; {{lang-tl|Lalawigan ng Cagayan}}) is a [[Provinces of the Philippines|province]] of the [[Philippines]] in the [[Cagayan Valley]] [[Regions of the Philippines|region]] in the northeast of [[Luzon Island]], and includes the [[Babuyan Islands]] to the north. The capital is [[Tuguegarao]].
