 '''Chigy''' is a former [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is found in the [[Yonne]] [[Departments of France|department]] in the center of [[France]]. On 1 January 2016, it was merged into the new commune of [[Les Vallées-de-la-Vanne]]. < ref > [https://www.legifrance.gouv.fr/eli/arrete/2015/12/15/INTB1532242A/jo/texte/fr Arrêté préfectoral] 15 December 2015 < /ref >
