 '''Kulanada''' is a village in [[Pathanamthitta district]], [[Kerala]], [[India]] < ref > {{cite web|url=http://pathanamthitta.gov.in/Administration1.htm|title=List of Taluks and Villages in Pathanamthitta District|access-date=10 December 2011|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20111208170801/http://pathanamthitta.gov.in/Administration1.htm|archive-date=8 December 2011|url-status=dead}} < /ref > 
 
 ==Geography== 
 Kulanada consists of these villages - Ambalakkadavu, Punnakulanji, Ulanadu, Ullannur, Kaipuzha, Kaithakadu, Kaluveli, Koodamparampu, Nediyuzhathilpadi, Panangadu, Pookaitha, Manthuka, Parayankara, Vattayam, Pandissery, Panil, Nedumpoyya and Paivazhi
