'''Corporatism''' is the idea from [[political science]] that different groups of society should take part in political decisions. If these groups have a choice to participate, the corporatism is called [[Liberal corporatism|liberal]], if they are forced the corporatism is called [[Authoritarian corporatism|authoritarian]]. The [[Guild|guilds]] that existed in the Middle ages are a form of corporatism.
