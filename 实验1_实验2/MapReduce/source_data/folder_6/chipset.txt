[[File:Motherboard diagram.svg|thumb|Diagram of a motherboard. Items labelled  " Northbridge "  and  " Southbride "  and  " Super I/O "  are chipsets.]] 
 A '''chipset''' is an arrangement of several [[integrated circuit]]s which fulfill a given task. When talking about [[computer]]s, chipset is the name for an arrangement of circuits on a motherboard that supports a [[microprocessor]].
