 '''Marktl''' (unofficial name: '''''Marktl am Inn''''', lit.  " Little market on the river [[Inn (river)|Inn]] " ) is a [[village]] and historic [[Market town|market municipality]]. It is in [[Altötting]], a district ([[Districts of Germany|''Landkreis'']]) in [[Upper Bavaria]]. It is the birthplace of [[Pope Benedict XVI]].

 * {{Flagicon|GER}} [[Gönnheim]], Germany
