 '''Euboea''' is an island of [[Greece]]. It is also one of the 51 [[prefecture]]s of [[Greece]]. Euboea is the [[List of Greek islands|second largest island of Greece]]. Its total area is 3,655 [[square meter|km < sup > 2 < /sup > ]]. It is to the east of [[Boeotia]] and [[Attica]] prefectures. The island is surrounded by the [[Aegean Sea]]. The capital of Euboea and the biggest city on the island is [[Chalkida]].
