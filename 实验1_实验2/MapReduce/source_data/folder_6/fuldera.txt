 '''Fuldera''' is a [[village]] and former [[municipality]] of the [[district]] of [[Inn (district)|Inn]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Graubünden]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 On 1 January 2009, the former municipalities of Fuldera, [[Lü]], [[Müstair]], [[Santa Maria Val Müstair]], [[Tschierv]] and [[Valchava]] merged into the municipality of [[Val Müstair]].
