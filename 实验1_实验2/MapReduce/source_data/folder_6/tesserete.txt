 '''Tesserete''' is a village and former municipality of the [[district]] [[Lugano (district)|Lugano]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Ticino]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 In 1976 the neighboring municipality [[Campestro]] was incorporated into Tesserete. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://www.bfs.admin.ch/bfs/portal/de/index/news/publikationen.Document.80465.pdf |title=Archived copy |access-date=2008-01-20 |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20110718034114/http://www.bfs.admin.ch/bfs/portal/de/index/news/publikationen.Document.80465.pdf |archive-date=2011-07-18 }} < /ref > 
 
 On 15 October 2001 the municipality was merged with the other, neighboring municipalities [[Cagiallo]], [[Lopagno]], [[Roveredo, Ticino|Roveredo]], [[Sala Capriasca]] and [[Vaglio]] to form a new and larger municipality [[Capriasca]].
