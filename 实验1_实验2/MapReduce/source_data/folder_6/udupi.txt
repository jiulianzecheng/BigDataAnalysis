 '''Udupi''', otherwise spelt ''Udipi'' and also known as '''Odipu''', is a [[city]] in the Indian state of [[Karnataka]]. Udupi is located about {{cvt|55|km}} north of the educational, commercial  &  industrial hub [[Mangalore]] and about {{cvt|422|km}} west of state capital [[Bangalore]] by road.
