 '''Khomein''' ({{lang-fa|خمين}}, also [[Romanization|romanize]]d as '''Khomeyn''', '''Khumayn''', and '''Khūmaīn''') < ref > {{GEOnet3|-3071137}} < /ref >  is a city in and the capital of [[Khomeyn County]], [[Markazi Province]], [[Iran]].  At the 2006 census, its population was 64,031, in 17,399 families. < ref > {{IranCensus2006|00}} < /ref >  The city is the birthplace of the [[Ruhollah Khomeini]], leader of the [[Iranian Revolution]].
