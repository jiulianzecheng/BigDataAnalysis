 '''''Nyctosaurus''''' was a [[pterosaur]] from the [[Upper Cretaceous]]. It had a [[wing]] span of 1 [[metre|meter]] and a very big crest. It was in the Pterodactyloidea and related to ''[[Pteranodon]]''.
