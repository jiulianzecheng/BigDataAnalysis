'''Manpur Cheema''' is a village in [[Sialkot]], [[Pakistan]]. A stream flows along the village which comes from the [[Himalayas]]. During the time of partition, [[Sikhism|Sikh]] Cheema families migrated to Indian Punjab. Manpur Cheema is surrounded by other historic villages of Cheema Jatts including Sahowala Cheema, Kamalpur Cheema, Jamke Cheema, Baddoke Cheema, Saabke Cheema, Sahjoke Cheema, Adamke Cheema and Bhopalwala Cheema.
