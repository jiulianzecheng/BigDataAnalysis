 The '''Fagales''' are an [[order (biology)|order]] of [[flowering plant]]s, including some of the best known [[tree]]s. The order name is derived from [[genus]] ''[[Fagus]]'', Beeches. They belong among the [[Rosids|rosid]] group of [[dicotyledon]]s. The families currently included are as follows: 
 
 * [[Betulaceae]] - [[Birch]] family 
 * [[Casuarinaceae]] - She-oak family 
 * [[Fagaceae]] - [[Beech]] family, [[Oak|oaks]] 
 * [[Juglandaceae]] - [[Walnut]] family 
 * [[Myricaceae]] - Bayberry family 
 * [[Nothofagus|Nothofagaceae]] - Southern beech family 
 * [[Rhoiptelea]]ceae - Rhoiptelea family 
 * [[Ticodendron|Ticodendraceae]] - Ticodendron family
