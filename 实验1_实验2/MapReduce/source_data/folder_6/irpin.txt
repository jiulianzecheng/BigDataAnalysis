 '''Irpin''' ({{lang-uk|Ірпінь}}) is a city in [[Kyiv Oblast]], [[Ukraine]]. In 2021,  
 62,456 people lived there. It is about 22 & nbsp;km northwest of the capital [[Kyiv]], on the bank of the [[Irpin (river)|Irpin River]].
