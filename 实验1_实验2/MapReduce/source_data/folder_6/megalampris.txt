 '''''Megalampris''''' is an extinct genus of prehistoric [[opah]] from the late [[Oligocene]] of [[New Zealand]] about 26 million years ago. It is only known from one species, which is called '''''Megalampris keyesi'''''.  
 
 ''Megalampris'' was a [[Fish|marine fish]] with a big head highly reminiscent of a [[pycnodont]]. It looked different from today's [[Opah|opahs]], having a dorsal [[fin]] resembling a [[swordfish]], and having two fins below.
