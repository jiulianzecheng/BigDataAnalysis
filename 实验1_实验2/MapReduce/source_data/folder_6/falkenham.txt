 '''Falkenham''' is a village and [[civil parish]] in [[Suffolk Coastal]], [[Suffolk]], England. In 2001 there were 180 people living in Falkenham. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.ons.gov.uk/help/localstatistics|title=Local statistics - Office for National Statistics|website=www.ons.gov.uk}} < /ref >  Falkenham shares a parish council with nearby [[Kirton]]. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.onesuffolk.net/|title=One Suffolk|website=www.onesuffolk.net}} < /ref >  Falkenham has a church called St Ethelbert. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.suffolkchurches.co.uk/falkenham.htm|title=Falkenham|website=www.suffolkchurches.co.uk}} < /ref >
