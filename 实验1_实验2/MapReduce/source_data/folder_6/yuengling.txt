 '''D. G. Yuengling  &  Son''' is the oldest operating [[brewery|brewing]] company in the [[United States]]. It was started in 1829. It is one of the largest [[brewery|breweries]] by [[volume]] in the country. "  D. G. Yuengling  &  Son. Retrieved on December 15, 2009. < /ref >   Yuengling produces about 2.5 million [[barrel]]s of beer a year. They operate two breweries in [[Pennsylvania]] and one in [[Tampa, Florida]]. 
 
 Yuengling is pronounced /ˈjʏŋlɪŋ/ {{Respell|YUNG|ling}}, and is an [[Anglicisation|Anglicized]] version of ''Jüngling'', its [[:wikt:founder|founder's]] [[surname]] and the [[German language|German]] term for  " young man " .
