'''Godalming''' is a town and [[civil parish]] in [[Surrey]], England. In 2011, there were 21,804 people living in Godalming. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.ons.gov.uk/help/localstatistics|title=Local statistics - Office for National Statistics|website=www.ons.gov.uk}} < /ref > 
 
 == Transport == 
 
 === Rail === 
 Godalming has a railway station, Godalming railway station. < ref > {{Cite web|title=National Rail Enquiries - Station facilities for Godalming|url=https://www.nationalrail.co.uk/stations_destinations/god.aspx|access-date=2021-01-02|website=www.nationalrail.co.uk}} < /ref >  The station is on the Portsmouth Direct Line, which goes between Portsmouth and London Waterloo. This line is served by South Western Railway. 
 
 === Road === 
 Roads that go through or near Godalming are: 
 
 * A3 
 * A281 
 * A283 
 * A286 
 * A3100 
 * B2130 
 * B3000 
 * B3001 
 
 === Air === 
 Godalming is around 31 miles or 50 kilometres away from Gatwick and Heathrow Airport.
