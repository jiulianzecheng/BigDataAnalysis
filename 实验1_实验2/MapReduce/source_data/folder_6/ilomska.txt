'''Ilomska''' ( Иломска) is a river in [[Bosnia and Herzegovina]]. It is right [[tributary]] of the [[Ugar (river)|Ugar]]. It is about {{convert|20|km}} in length. The river starts in [[Vlašić (mountain)|Vlašić]]'s Mountain. It is an essential body of water for surrounding wildlife. 
 
 Ilomska flows between the [[Žežnička Greda]] (altitude {{convert|1,477|m|disp=or}}) and [[Javorak]] ({{cvt|1,499|m|disp=or}}) [[mountains]]. It has a curved flow around [[Lisina]] ({{cvt|1,494|m|disp=or}}) and [[Runjavica]] ({{cvt|1,316|m|disp=or}}) mountains, through  coniferous ([[fir]] and [[spruce]]) and mixed spuce-[[beech]] woods.

 After two attractive waterfalls, Ilomska flows into the Ugar, a few kilometers downstream from [[Vitovlje]] village. The height of the bigger water flow is around 40 meters.
