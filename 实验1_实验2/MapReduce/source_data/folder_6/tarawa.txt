 '''Tarawa''' is an [[atoll]] in the [[Republic]] of [[Kiribati]]. It is in the middle of the [[Pacific Ocean]]. < ref > ''Atoll Politics: The Republic of Kiribati'', ed. Howard Van Trease (Christchurch: Macmillan Brown Centre for Pacific Studies, 1993), p. 125 < /ref >  It is made up of [[North Tarawa]] and [[South Tarawa]]. < ref > ''Geology and hydrogeology of carbonate islands'', eds.  Leonard H.L. Vacher; Terrence M. Quinn (Amsterdam; San Diego, CA: Elsevier, 2004), p. 577 < /ref >  North Tarawa has a lot in common with [[island]]s in the [[Gilbert Islands|Gilberts group]]. [[South Tarawa]] is the [[Capital city|capital]] of Kiribati. It has over 50,000 people living in it. That is half of the country's total [[population]].
