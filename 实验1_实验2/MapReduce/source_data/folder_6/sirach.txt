'''''Sirach''''', by [[Ben Sira]], which is also known as '''''Wisdom of Jesus son of Sirach''''', the '''''Wisdom of Ben Sira''''', or '''''Ecclesiasticus''''', is a work from the early second century BC, its original version being written in [[Hebrew language|Hebrew]]. It is part of the [[Apocrypha]] or [[deuterocanonical]] books. 
 
 ==Quotes== 
 " All wisdom is from the [[Yahweh|Lord]] 
 and with him it remains for ever.  
 The sand of the sea, the drops of rain,  
 and the days of [[eternity]]- who can count them? " 
 (Opening of first chapter, [[New Revised Standard Version]])
