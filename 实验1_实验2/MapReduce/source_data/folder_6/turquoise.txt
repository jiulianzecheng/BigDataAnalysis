 '''Turquoise''' is an [[Opacity (optics)|opaque]] (cannot be seen through), blueish-green [[mineral]]. It is not common and can be valuable in finer grades.  It has been prized as a [[gemstone|gem]] and [[ornamental stone]] for thousands of years because of its interesting coloring.  
 
 The word 'turquoise' means Turkish in French.
