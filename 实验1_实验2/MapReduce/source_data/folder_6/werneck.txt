 '''Werneck''' is a [[Municipalities of Germany|market town]] in [[Schweinfurt (district)|Schweinfurt]] in [[Lower Franconia]], [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 ===Place=== 
 Werneck is in Lower Franconia in Bavaria, about 12 & nbsp;km from [[Schweinfurt]] and 25 & nbsp;km from [[Würzburg]]. It is on a curve in the [[Wern]], a tributary of the [[Main (river)|Main]], on the edge of the plateau above the river. Its highest point is at 337.20 & nbsp;m of elevation, its lowest at 212.50 & nbsp;m. The total area of the municipality is 73.65 & nbsp;km², of which about 9 & nbsp;km² are wooded.
