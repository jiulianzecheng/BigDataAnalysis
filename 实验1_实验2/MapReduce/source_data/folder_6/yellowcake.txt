'''Yellowcake''' is a type of [[uranium]] powder. Yellowcake is made from [[Rock (geology)|rocks]] and [[mineral]]s with uranium inside, like ''uraninite'', after they are changed in a [[factory]]. Yellowcake can be made into [[fuel]] for [[nuclear reactor]]s, or it can be used in making [[atomic bomb]]s.
