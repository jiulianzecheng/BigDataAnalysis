 '''Weidenberg''' is a municipality in the district of [[Bayreuth (district)|Bayreuth]] in [[Bavaria]] in [[Germany]]. The [[Ölschnitz (Red Main)|Ölschnitz]] river discharges near Weidenberg-Neunkirchen into the [[Red Main]].
