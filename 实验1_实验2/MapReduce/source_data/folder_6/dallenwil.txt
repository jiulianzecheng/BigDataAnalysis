 '''Dallenwil''' is a [[municipality]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Nidwalden]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 The main sights of Dallenwil are the [[Skiing|ski]] and [[summer]] [[resort]] ''Wiesenberg/Wirzweli'', the [[church]] ''St. Laurentius'', and the [[pilgrimage]] [[chapel]] ''Wiesenberg''.
