 '''CKY''' (also known as '''Camp Kill Yourself''') is an American band formed in 1998. In 2002, they were signed to [[The Island Def Jam Music Group]] and released Infiltrate•Destroy•Rebuild, which entered the [[Billboard 200]] at number 99. < ref > {{cite web | url=http://www.allmusic.com/artist/cky-mn0000634463/biography | title=CKY Biography, Songs,  &  Albums | website=[[AllMusic]] }} < /ref >
