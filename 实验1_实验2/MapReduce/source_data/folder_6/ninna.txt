{{nihongo|'''Ninna'''|仁和|}} was a {{nihongo|[[Japanese era name]]|年号,|''nengō'',|lit.  " year name " }} after ''[[Gangyō]]'' and before ''[[Kanpyō (era)|Kanpyō]].''  This period started in February 885 and ended in April 889. < ref > Nussbaum, Louis-Frédéric. (2005).  " ''Ninna'' "  in ''Japan Encyclopedia'', p. 716. < /ref >   The reigning emperors were {{nihongo|[[Emperor Kōkō|Kōkō]]''-tennō''|光孝天皇}} and {{nihongo|[[Emperor Uda|Uda]]''-tennō''|宇多天皇}}. < ref > [[Isaac Titsingh|Titsingh, Isaac]]. (1834). [https://books.google.com/books?id=18oNAAAAIAAJ & dq=nipon+o+dai+itsi+ran & pg=PP9  ''Annales des empereurs du japon'', p. 124-125]; Brown, Delmer ''et al.'' (1979). [https://books.google.com/books?id=w4f5FrmIJKIC & pg=PA289 ''Gukanshō'', p. 289]; Varley, H. Paul, ed. (1980). ''Jinō Shōtōki'', pp. 171-175. < /ref > 
 
 ==Events of the ''Ninna'' era== 
 [[File:Ninna Ji 5.jpg|thumb|right|Garden at Ninna-ji which was begun in ''Ninna'' 3.]] 
 * '''17 September 887''' (''Ninna 3, 26th day of the 8th month'') < !-- NengoCalc  仁和三年八月二十六日 -- > : Emperor Kōkō died at the age of 57. < ref > Brown, [https://books.google.com/books?id=w4f5FrmIJKIC & pg=PA289 p. 289]; Varley, p.173; [[Imperial Household Agency]] (''Kunaichō''), [http://www.kunaicho.go.jp/ryobo/guide/058/index.html 光孝天皇 (58)]; retrieved 2012-5-22. < /ref >   Kōkō's third son received the succession (''senso'').  Shortly thereafter, Emperor Uda formally acceded to the throne (''sokui''). < ref > Brown, [https://books.google.com/books?id=w4f5FrmIJKIC & pg=PA289 p. 289]. < /ref >   This was [[wikt:confirm|confirmed]] in [[Ceremony|ceremonies]]. < ref > Varley, p. 44; a distinct act of ''senso'' is unrecognized prior to [[Emperor Tenji]]; and all sovereigns except [[Empress Jitō|Jitō]], [[Emperor Yōzei|Yōzei]], [[Emperor Go-Toba|Go-Toba]], and [[Emperor Fushimi|Fushimi]] have ''senso'' and ''sokui'' in the same year until the reign of [[Emperor Go-Murakami]]. Compare [[Imperial Household Agency]] (''Kunaichō''), [http://www.kunaicho.go.jp/e-about/seido/seido06-ph.html  Ceremony of Accession (''Sokui-no-Rei'')]; retrieved 2012-2-16. < /ref > 
 
 * '''12 May 887''' (''Ninna 3, 17th day of the 11th month'') < !-- NengoCalc 仁和三年十一月十七日 -- > : Mototsune asks Uda for permission to retire from his duties; but the emperor persuades him to continue to serve as ''kampaku''. < ref name= " t126 " > Titsingh, [https://books.google.com/books?id=18oNAAAAIAAJ & dq=nipon+o+dai+itsi+ran & pg=PP9  p. 126.] < /ref > 
 
 * '''887''' (''Ninna 4, 8th month''): Construction of the Buddhist temple complex at {{Nihongo|[[Ninna-ji]]|仁和寺}} was completed; and a former disciple of [[Kōbō-daishi]] was named to be the leader of the monks who lived there. < ref name= " t126 " / >
