 '''Stepanakert''' ({{lang-hy|Ստեփանակերտ}}) or '''Khankendi''' ({{lang-az|Xankəndi}}) is a city in the disputed region of [[Nagorno-Karabakh]], legally in Azerbaijan, but militarily controlled by the Armenian forces as the [[capital (city)|capital city]] of the unrecognised [[Republic of Artsakh]] (also known as Nagorno-Karabakh Republic). The [[city]] has about 40,000 ethnic [[Armenians]]. The entire Azerbaijani [[population]] left the city during the [[Nagorno-Karabakh War]] and it is currently populated fully by Armenians.
