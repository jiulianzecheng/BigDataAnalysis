 '''Agrigento''' ({{lang-scn|Girgenti}}) is a [[city]] in the [[region]] of [[Sicily]] in southern [[Italy]]. It is also the [[Capital (city)|capital]] of the ''[[Province of Agrigento]]''. There are many [[relics]] there from the [[Ancient Greece|ancient Greeks]] and the [[Roman empire]].
