 '''Astyanax''' is a person in [[Greek mythology]]. He is the son of [[Hector]] of [[Troy]] and [[Andromache]]. He is killed at the end of the [[Trojan War]] by [[Neoptolemus]], so he could not be a new king of [[Troy]], or want [[wikt:revenge|revenge]].
