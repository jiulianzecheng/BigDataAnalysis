 '''Kennelbach''' is a municipality in the [[Bregenz (district)|district of Bregenz]] in the [[Austria]]n state of [[Vorarlberg]]. 
 
 As of 2016, it is the operational headquarters of the sporting goods manufacturer [[Head (company)|Head]], along with [[Amsterdam]], the corporate headquarters. < ref > [http://www.head.com Head official website] < /ref >
