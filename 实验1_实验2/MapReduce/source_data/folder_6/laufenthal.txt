 '''Laufenthal''' is a village in the [[Upper Palatinate]] ([[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]), belonging to [[Regensburg (district)|Regensburg]] and the market town of [[Hemau]].  Technically a ward of Hemau, Laufenthal is about five kilometers from the town, near the river Laaber.
