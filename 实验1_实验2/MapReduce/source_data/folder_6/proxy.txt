 '''Proxy''' has different meanings when talking about different subjects: 
 
 Basically,  " proxy "  means  " stands for " . 
 *In [[computing]], a [[proxy server]]  is a server which clients use to access other computers. 
 *A [[proxy marriage]] is when two people are getting married separately, but are getting married to one another. 
 *A [[proxy murder]] is when a person murders on behalf of someone else. 
 *A [[proxy vote]] is when a person votes on behalf of someone else, usually occurring in public and private companies requiring votes for decisions. 
 *A [[web proxy]] is a server that fetches web pages for a user. Web proxies often hide your browser history and can bypass local internet restrictions.
