 '''Waischenfeld''' is a town in the [[Bayreuth (district)|district of Bayreuth]], in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. It is in [[Franconian Switzerland]], 20 & nbsp;km southwest of [[Bayreuth]]. 
 
 It consists of the following districts: Aalkorb, Breitenlesau, Doos, Eichenbirkig, Gösseldorf, Gutenbiegen, Hammermühle, Hannberg, Heroldsberg, Heroldsberg-Tal, Hubenberg, Köttweinsdorf, Kugelau, Langenloh, Löhlitz, Nankendorf, Neusig, Pulvermühle, Rabeneck, Sauerhof, Saugendorf, Schafhof, Schlößlein, Schönhaid, Schönhof, Seelig, Siegritzberg, Waischenfeld, Zeubach. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.bayerische-landesbibliothek-online.de/orte/ortssuche_action.html?val=1171 & attr=590 & modus=automat & tempus=20100811/135154 & hodie=20171117/202004|title=Alle Orte in der Gemeinde Waischenfeld, St|publisher=Bayerische Landesbibliothek Online|pages=this page and the next one, available by a click on  " Folgende Treffer " |language=German|accessdate=17 November 2017}} < /ref > 
 
 The town is the place of [[Waischenfeld Castle]].
