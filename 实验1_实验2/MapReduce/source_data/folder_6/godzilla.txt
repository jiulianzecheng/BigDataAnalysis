 '''Godzilla''' is a giant [[dinosaur]]-like [[monster]] who first appeared in [[movie]]s from [[Japan]], in the year 1954. Since then has been featured in thirty-six movies. During his movie career, this large reptile fought against many other [[wikt:mythical|mythical]] monsters, including the giant moth-like [[Mothra]], the three-headed [[King Ghidorah]], his own robotic mirror image Mechagodzilla, and several more enemies. The original [[Japanese language|Japanese]] name, ''Gojira or ゴジラ'', is a combination of the words ''[[gorilla]]'' and ''kujira'' (means ''[[whale]]'').  In countries where the primary language is [[English language|English]], the name has been changed to ''Godzilla'', is an eponymous Japanese movie reptilian monster that has appeared in movies since 1954.
