'''Gonadotropins''' (or '''Gonadotrophins''') are [[hormone]]s. The two main hormones of the group are [[Luteinizing hormone]] (LH) and [[Follicle-stimulating hormone]] (FSH). Gonadotropins are hormones that tell the testicles to make sperm or the uterus to release an egg.
