 '''Cloppenburg''' ({{IPA-de|ˈklɔpm̩ˌbʊʁk|lang|de-Cloppenburg.ogg}}) is a town in [[Lower Saxony]], [[Germany]], capital of [[Cloppenburg (district)|Cloppenburg District]] and part of [[Oldenburg Münsterland]]. It is 38 & nbsp;km south-south-west of [[Oldenburg (city)|Oldenburg]] in the [[Weser-Ems]] region between [[Bremen (city)|Bremen]] and the [[Netherlands|Dutch]] border.
