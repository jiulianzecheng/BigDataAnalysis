 '''WikiPilipinas''' was an [[World Wide Web|online]], [[free content]] [[website]]. It is a combination  " non-academic [[encyclopedia]] " , [[web portal]], [[web directory|directory]] and [[almanac]] for [[Philippines|Philippine]]-based knowledge. It mostly contains various articles on Philippine-related topics. It was known before as '''Wikipiniana'''.
