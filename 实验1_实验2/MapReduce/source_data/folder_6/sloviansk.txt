 '''Sloviansk''' ({{lang-uk|Слов'я́нськ|Sloviansk}}; {{lang-ru|Славя́нск|Slavjansk}}) is a city in [[Donetsk Oblast]] ([[Oblast|province]]) of eastern [[Ukraine]]. It is the [[administrative centre]] of [[Sloviansk Raion]] ([[Raion|district]]). In 2020, 108,363 people lived there.
