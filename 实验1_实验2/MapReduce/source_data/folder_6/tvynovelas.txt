 '''''TVyNovelas''''' is a [[Mexico|Mexican]] [[music]] and [[gossip]] [[magazine]]. The magazine is [[Publishing|published]] by [[Editorial Televisa]]. The magazine is available in the [[United States]] (and [[Puerto Rico]]), Mexico, [[Argentina]], [[Chile]] and [[Colombia]]. ''TVyNovelas'' is one of the most-widely read magazine for [[Spanish language|Spanish-language]] [[soap opera]] fans. Their [[award]]s [[television programme|show]], ''[[Premios TVyNovelas]]'', is [[broadcast]] on [[Televisa]] and [[Univision]].
