 '''''Harpymimus''''' (meaning  " harpy mimic " ) is an [[ornithomimosauria]]n [[dinosaur]] that lived during the [[Early Cretaceous]] in [[Mongolia]] around 107-100 million years ago. It was discovered in 1981 and was named in 1984. < ref > Barsbold, R. and Perle, A. (1984). [On first new find of a primitive orithomimosaur from the Cretaceous of the MPR].
