'''Harvey''' [ '''hahr'''-vee ] < ref > {{Cite web|title=Definition of Harvey {{!}} Dictionary.com|url=https://www.dictionary.com/browse/harvey|access-date=2023-03-30|website=www.dictionary.com|language=en}} < /ref >  is an English family name or boys name derived from the Old [[Breton language|Breton]] name ''Huiarnviu''. Harvey, a moniker with a long-standing presence, boasts a fascinating legacy. The name's roots can be traced back to Old English, denoting  " battle worthy "  or  " strong in battle, "  derived from the words  " here "  for army and  " wig "  for battle. Initially, the name was utilized as a surname, awarded to individuals recognized for their valor and might in combat. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Harvey name Meaning and Origin|url=https://babys.name/detail-Harvey-Meaning-Origin-Famous-Similar-Sibling.html|access-date=2023-03-30|website=babys.name}} < /ref >  This name was ranked #420 on the US Popular Names in 2021. < ref > {{Cite web|last=OACT|title=Popular Baby Names|url=https://www.ssa.gov/cgi-bin/popularnames.cgi|access-date=2023-03-30|website=www.ssa.gov}} < /ref >
