 '''Uckfield''' is a town in [[Wealden]], [[East Sussex]], [[England]], [[UK]]. It is on the [[River Uck]]. In 2001 there were 13,697 people living in Uckfield. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.ons.gov.uk/help/localstatistics|title=Local statistics - Office for National Statistics|website=www.ons.gov.uk}} < /ref >
