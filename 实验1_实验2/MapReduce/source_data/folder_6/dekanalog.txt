 '''Dekanalog''' is an [[United States|American]] [[movie]] and [[music]] [[company]]. The company was founded in 2020. The [[headquarters]] of the company is in [[New York City]]. 
 
 == History == 
 The company released its first movie in March 2021. < ref > {{Cite web|last=Hayes|first=Dade|last2=Hayes|first2=Dade|date=2021-01-13|title=Dekanalog To Launch International-Focused Film Distribution Outfit With Four-Film Slate|url=https://deadline.com/2021/01/dekanalog-launches-film-distribution-international-focus-1234672886/|access-date=2021-11-17|website=Deadline|language=en-US}} < /ref >   In June of the same year, the company started [[Producer|producing]] music as well. It began with [[Vinyl record|vinyl]] [[soundtrack]] of ''Take Me Somewhere Nice''. < ref > {{Cite web|title=TAKE ME SOMEWHERE NICE Soundtrack by Ella van der Woude|url=https://www.kickstarter.com/projects/dekanalog/take-me-somewhere-nice-soundtrack-by-ella-van-der-woude|access-date=2021-11-17|website=Kickstarter|language=en}} < /ref >   In November 2021, the company signed a home video contract with [[OCN Distribution]]. < ref > {{Cite web|last=Roxborough|first=Scott|last2=Roxborough|first2=Scott|date=2021-11-02|title=Dekanalog Strikes Home Video Deal With OCN Distribution (Exclusive)|url=https://www.hollywoodreporter.com/business/business-news/afm-2021-dekanalog-ocn-home-video-deal-1235039861/|access-date=2021-11-17|website=The Hollywood Reporter|language=en-US}} < /ref >
