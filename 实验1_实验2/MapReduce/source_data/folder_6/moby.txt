 '''Moby''' or '''Richard Melville Hall''' (born September 11, 1965 < ref name= " Mobybio " > [http://www.moby.com/biography Moby's bio from his official website] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20101227231313/http://www.moby.com/biography |date=2010-12-27 }}, accessed 25 April 2008 < /ref > ) is an [[Americans|American]] [[DJ]], [[songwriter]] and [[musician]]. He is famous for his [[song]]s  " [[Porcelain (song)|Porcelain]] "  and [[Why Does My Heart Feel So Bad?| "  why does my heart feel so bad]]  " . The [[music video|video]] for another one of his songs, [[We Are All Made of Stars|We are all  made of stars]]  shows him dressed as an [[astronaut]] in a [[space suit]]. 
 
 He was a member of a [[Punk music|punk band]] called [[Vatican Commandos]] before becoming a DJ. 
 
 In 2002, Moby started a [[cafe]] called [[Teany]] with his ex-girlfriend Kelly Tisdale. < ref > {{cite web|author1=Elyssa Lee, Rob Turner|title=Moby, Remixed|url=http://www.inc.com/magazine/20041201/celebrity-moby.html|website=Inc.com|publisher=Mansueto Ventures|accessdate=15 September 2014|date=1 December 2004}} < /ref >
