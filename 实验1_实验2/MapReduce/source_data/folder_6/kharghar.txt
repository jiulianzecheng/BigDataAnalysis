 '''Kharghar''' is a [[wikt:node|node]] of [[Navi Mumbai]] and it is a [[suburb]] under [[Panvel Municipal Corporation]] (PMC). It is situated opposite to [[Taloja]] and at the northernmost tip of [[Raigad district]]. It was developed by the [[City and Industrial Development Corporation]] (CIDCO). It is well known for its large number of educational institutes and the greenery that it offers.
