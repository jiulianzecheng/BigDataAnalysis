 '''Ederswiler''' is a [[municipality]] of the [[district]] of [[Delémont (district)|Delémont]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Jura (canton)|Jura]] in [[Switzerland]]. It is the only German-speaking municipality in the canton, where all others are French-speaking.
