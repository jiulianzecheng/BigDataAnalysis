 '''Hirslanden''' is a [[quarter]] in [[district 7 (Zürich)|district 7]] in the [[city]] of [[Zürich]] in [[Switzerland]]. Until 1893 it was a [[municipality]] on its own. It was then [[municipal corporation|incorporated]] into [[Zürich]]. 
 
 It is on the western side of the [[Adlisberg]].
