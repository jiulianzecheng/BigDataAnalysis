 '''Obernzell''' ({{lang-bar|label=[[Central Bavarian]]|Obanzoi}}) is a [[Municipalities of Germany|municipality]] in [[Passau (district)|Passau]] in [[Bavaria]] in [[Germany]]. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 
 ===Geographical place=== 
 Obernzell is in the Donau-Wald region at the [[Danube]] River. The middle of the Danube River makes the border with Upper Austria.
