 '''Segnitz''' is a municipality in [[Kitzingen (district)|Kitzingen]] in [[Bavaria]] in [[Germany]], 25 & nbsp;km south of [[Würzburg]] on the Main river. The village is well known as the home of Renaissance master builder [[:de:Hans Keesebrod|Hans Keesebrod]], horticulture and wine and has less than 850 residents.
