 A '''chair''' is a piece of [[furniture]] that is usually made out of wood or plastic. It can also be made out of fabric, metal, wool, and stone. There can be different variants of chairs like stools, rocking chairs, gaming chairs, folding chairs, and armchairs. There are also wide variety of chairs, those with fabric are often called sofas, or [[Couch|couches]], while those many without fabric are often referred to as benches. There are also less conventional chairs such as bean bag chairs and swinging chairs.
