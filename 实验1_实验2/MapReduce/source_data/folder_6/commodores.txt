The '''Commodores''' are an [[United States|American]] [[funk]] and soul music group. They were most popular in the [[1970s]] and [[1980s]]. The band was formed in 1968 in [[Tuskegee, Alabama]]. They are signed with [[Motown]]. Their hit songs include ''Three Times a Lady'', ''Easy'' and ''Brick House''. During the early times, [[Lionel Richie]] sang with the Commodores.
