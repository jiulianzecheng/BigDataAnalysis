 '''Baceno''' ([[Lombard language|Lombard]] ''Basc√©n'', [[Walser German]]: ''Aager'') is a ''[[comune]]'' (municipality) in the [[Province of Verbano-Cusio-Ossola]] in the [[Italy|Italian]] region [[Piedmont]]. 
 
 Baceno is about {{convert|140|km|mi}} northeast of [[Turin]] and about {{convert|40|km|mi}} northwest of [[Verbania]]. It is on the border with [[Switzerland]]. As of 2004, 963 people lived there. It has an area of {{convert|77.27|km2|mi2}}. < ref name= " istat " > All demographics and other statistics: Italian statistical institute [[National Institute of Statistics (Italy)|Istat]]. < /ref > 
 
 Baceno is next to these municipalities: [[Binn]] (Switzerland), [[Crodo]], [[Formazza]], [[Grengiols]] (Switzerland), [[Premia]], [[Varzo]].
