 '''Lentibulariaceae''' is a [[family (biology)|family]] of plants, sometimes called the '[[bladderwort]] family'. It only has three [[genus|genera]]: ''[[Pinguicula]]'', ''[[Genlisea]]'', and ''[[Utricularia]]''. They are all [[carnivore|carnivorous]] plants (plants which trap and [[digest]] [[insect]]s). They are in the order [[Lamiales]].
