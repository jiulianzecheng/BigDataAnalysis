 The '''Isar''' is a [[river]] in [[Tyrol (state)|Tyrol]], [[Austria]] and [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]]. Its source is in the [[Karwendel]] range of the [[Alps]] in Tyrol; it enters Germany near [[Mittenwald]]. The Isar flows through [[Bad Tölz]], [[Munich]] and [[Landshut]] before reaching the [[Danube]] near [[Deggendorf]]. The length is {{convert|295|km|mi|abbr=on}}. It is the fourth largest river in Bavaria (after [[Danube]], [[Inn (river)|Inn]] and [[Main (river)|Main]]). The Isar is the second most important [[tributary]] of the Danube in Germany, after Inn.
