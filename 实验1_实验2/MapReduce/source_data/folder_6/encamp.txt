 '''Encamp''' is one of the seven [[Parishes of Andorra|parishes]] of [[Andorra]]. It is in the east central part of the country. Encamp is also the name of the main town of the parish. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 The Encamp parish is in the east central part of the country and borders with the [[Canillo]] parish to the north, with the [[Pyrénées-Orientales]] [[Departments of France|department]] ([[France]]) to the east, with [[Spain]] to the southeast, with the [[Escaldes-Engordany]] parish to the south, with the [[La Massana]] parish to the west and the [[Ordino]] parish to the northwest.

 The highest mountain in the parish is ''Pic del Pessons'' ({{Coord|42|30|30|N|1|39|32|E|name=Pic del Pessons|display=inline|region:AD_type:mountain}}) with an elevation of {{Convert|2840|m|ft|abbr=on }} [[above sea level]]. < ref name=pbag > {{cite web |url=http://www.peakbagger.com/peak.aspx?pid=9818|title=Pic de Pessons, Andorra |publisher=Peakbagger.com |language= |accessdate=27 December 2016 |date= }} < /ref > 
 
 The main river in Encamp is the ''Valira d'Orient''. 
 
 ==Population== 
 The parish had a population, in 2015,  of 12,212 inhabitants, < ref name= " estadistica "  / >  for a [[population density]] of {{Pop density|12212|86|km2|prec=1|disp=num}} persons/km².
