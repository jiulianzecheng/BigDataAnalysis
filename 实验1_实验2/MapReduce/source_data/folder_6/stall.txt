 '''Stall''' may refer to: 
 
 ==Enclosures== 
 * [[Stall (enclosure)]], a small enclosure, as for market goods, or for an animal 
 * [[Market stall]], a makeshift or mobile structures for selling market goods or serving food 
 
 ==Motion== 
 * [[Stall (engine)]], the unexpected or unwanted stopping of an engine 
 * [[Stall (flight)]], when an aircraft can not make enough lift to keep the aircraft in level flight 
 * [[Compressor stall]], the sudden loss of compression in a jet engine   
 * [[Stalling (gaming)]], obstruction of the flow of play while leading in a timed game
