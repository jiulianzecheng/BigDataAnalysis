'''Aircalin''' is New Caledonia's largest airline, having a network of flights all over [[Oceania]]. It began in September 1983 as ''Air Calédonie International'', an international airline to complement [[New Caledonia]]'s domestic airline, [[Air Calédonie]]. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.airlines-inform.com/world_airlines/aircalin.html|title=Aircalin, Air Caledonie International|website=Airlines Inform|access-date=25 July 2020}} < /ref >
