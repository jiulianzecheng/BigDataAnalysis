 '''Succession''' may mean: 
 
 *[[Order of succession]], the chronology of past leaders; or the plan for how a future leader takes over from another because of death, resignation, or removal 
 *[[Succession planning]], identifying and developing people to take over senior positions in government, business, organizations, etc. 
 *[[Ecological succession]], the development of changes that happen over time after an ecology is disturbed
