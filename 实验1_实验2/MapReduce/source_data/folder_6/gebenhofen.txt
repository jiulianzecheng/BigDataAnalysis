 '''Gebenhofen''' is a [[village]] near [[Augsburg]], Germany. It looks like people settled there in the [[Merowingian]] era (7th century). Today, about 500 people live in the village. It is a member of the gemeinde Affing and can be found in the Landkreis Aichach Friedberg (Regierungsbezirk Schwaben).
