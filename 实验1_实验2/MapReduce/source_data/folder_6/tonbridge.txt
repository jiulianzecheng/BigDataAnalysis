'''Tonbridge''' is a town in [[Kent]] in [[United Kingdom|England]]. The [[River Medway]] runs through the town. A [[castle]] stands on the northern river bank in the middle of Tonbridge. 
 
 Tonbridge is linked by [[railway]]s to [[London]] and [[Dover, Kent|Dover]]. 
 
 There are many [[secondary school]]s in the area. such as [[Tonbridge Grammar School]]
