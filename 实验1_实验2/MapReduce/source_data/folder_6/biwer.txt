 '''Biwer''' is a [[commune]] and small town in eastern [[Luxembourg]]. < ref > [http://www.luxalbum.com/communes/grevenmacher/biwer1.htm Commune de Biwer] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130627150030/http://www.luxalbum.com/communes/grevenmacher/biwer1.htm |date=2013-06-27 }}; retrieved 2012-4-25. < /ref > 
 
 The commune is in the canton of [[Grevenmacher (canton)|Grevenmacher]], < ref > [http://www.luxalbum.com/grevenmacher.htm  Canton de Grevenmacher] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120520160654/http://www.luxalbum.com/grevenmacher.htm |date=2012-05-20 }}; retrieved 2012-4-25. < /ref >   which is part of the old district of [[Grevenmacher (district)|Grevenmacher]]. < ref > CityPopulation.de, [http://www.citypopulation.de/php/luxembourg-grevenmacher.php  " Luxembourg, Grevenmacher " ], citing ''Le Portail des Statistiques du Luxembourg,'' Communes in the boundaries of Greenmacher, January 2012; retrieved 2012-4-26. < /ref >   It is made up of Biwer, [[Boudler, Luxembourg|Boudler]], [[BrouchHagelsdorf, Luxembourg|Brouch]], [[Hegelsdorf, Luxembourg|Hegelsdorf]], [[Wecker, Luxembourg|Wecker]] and [[Weydig, Luxembourg|Weydig]]. < ref > Biwer, [http://www.biwer.lu/la-commune-se-presente  ''DÃ©couvrir la commune''] {{Webarchive|url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120801161524/http://www.biwer.lu/la-commune-se-presente |date=2012-08-01 }}; retrieved 2012-4-26. < /ref > 
 
 In Biwer, the largest village of the commune,  are every public buildings. There are  the primary school called 'An der Wiss', the cultural centre 'Fancy', a swimming pool, a sports hall and a day-care centre.
