 '''Oberramsern''' was a [[municipality]] in the [[Bucheggberg (district)|district of Bucheggberg]], in the [[Solothurn (canton)|canton of Solothurn]], [[Switzerland]].  On 1 January 2010, the former municipalities of [[Balm bei Messen]], [[Brunnenthal, Switzerland|Brunnenthal]] and Oberramsern merged into the municipality of [[Messen]]. < ref name=fusion > {{Cite report|title=Amtliches Gemeindeverzeichnis der Schweiz, Mutationsmeldungen 2009 / Répertoire officiel des communes de Suisse, Mutations 2009 / Elenco ufficiale dei Comuni della Svizzera, Mutazione 2009 |url=http://www.bfs.admin.ch/bfs/portal/de/index/infothek/nomenklaturen/blank/blank/gem_liste/03.Document.128095.pdf |publisher=Federal Statistical Office |year=2009 |access-date=6 March 2010 |docket=3162 |url-status=dead |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20101118021059/http://www.bfs.admin.ch/bfs/portal/de/index/infothek/nomenklaturen/blank/blank/gem_liste/03.Document.128095.pdf |archive-date=18 November 2010 }} < /ref >
