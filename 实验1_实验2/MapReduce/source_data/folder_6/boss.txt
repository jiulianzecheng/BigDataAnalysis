 A '''boss''' is a person who is [[leadership|in charge]] of workers in a company or organization. Bosses can have many different job titles. Simple examples include [[manager]], [[supervisor]] and [[Board of directors|director]]. 
 
 '''Boss''' may also mean: 
 
 * [[Boss (video gaming)]], a particularly powerful enemy or opponent in a video game 
 * [[Boss (crime)]], head of a criminal organization
