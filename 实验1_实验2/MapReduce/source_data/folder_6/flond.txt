 '''Flond''' is a former [[municipality]] of the [[district]] [[Surselva (district)|Surselva]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Graubünden]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 On 1 January 2009 the former municipalities of Flond and [[Surcuolm]] merged into the new municipality of [[Mundaun]].
