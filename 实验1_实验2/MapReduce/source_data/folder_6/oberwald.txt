 '''Oberwald''' is a village and former [[municipality]] of the [[district]] of [[Goms (district)|Goms]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Valais]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 On 1 January 2009, the former municipalities of Oberwald, [[Obergesteln]] and [[Ulrichen]] merged into the new municipality of [[Obergoms]].
