 '''Communic''' is a [[Norway|Norwegian]] [[progressive metal]] [[band]] from [[Kristiansand]], [[Norway]]. Communic has three members in the band; the current band members are Oddleif Stensland, Erik Mortensen and Tor Atle Andersen. The band formed in March 2003 as a side project for the band, [[Scariot]]. < ref > {{Cite web |url=http://www.nuclearblastusa.com/bands/communic.html |title=Nuclear Blast America |access-date=2012-03-03 |archive-date=2007-12-06 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20071206032511/http://www.nuclearblastusa.com/bands/communic.html |url-status=dead }} < /ref >  They are currently signed to [[Nuclear Blast|Nuclear Blast Records]] and have released 4 albums and a demo.
