 '''Guayaquil''' ({{IPA-es|ɡwaʝaˈkil|pron}}), officially '''Santiago de Guayaquil''' ({{IPA-es|sanˈtjaɣo ðe ɣwaʝaˈkil|pron}}), is the largest city in [[Ecuador]].  It has about 3.5 million in the metropolitan area. It is also the nation's main port. The city is the [[capital (political)|capital]] of both the [[Guayas Province]] and the [[Guayaquil Canton]].
