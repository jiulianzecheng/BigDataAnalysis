 '''Suraua''' was a [[municipality]] of the [[district]] of [[Surselva (district)|Surselva]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Graub端nden]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 ==History== 
 The former municipality was formed on January 1, 2002 through the union of Camuns, Surcasti, Tersnaus and Uors-Peiden. 
 
 On 1 January 2013, the former municipalities of Suraua, [[Cumbel]], [[Degen, Switzerland|Degen]], [[Lumbrein]], [[Morissen]], [[Vignogn]], [[Vella]] and [[Vrin]] merged into the new municipality of [[Lumnezia]].
