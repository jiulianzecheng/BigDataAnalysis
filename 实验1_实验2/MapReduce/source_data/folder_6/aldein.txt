 '''Aldein''' ({{lang-it|Aldino}}) is a ''[[comune]]'' in [[South Tyrol]] in north [[Italy]]. It is about {{convert|15|km|mi|0}} south of the city of [[Bolzano]]. 
   
 Aldein is next to the these municipalities: [[Bronzolo]], [[Montan]], [[Deutschnofen]], [[Auer, South Tyrol|Auer]], [[Truden im Naturpark|Truden]] and [[Ville di Fiemme]]. It has the ''[[frazione]]'' Radein (Redagno).
