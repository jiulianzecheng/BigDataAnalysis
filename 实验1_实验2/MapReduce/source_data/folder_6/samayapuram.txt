'''Samayapuram''' is a [[suburb]] of the city of [[Tiruchirappalli]] in [[Tamil Nadu]], [[India]]. Formerly a separate village of its own, Samayapuram was included in the [[Tiruchirappalli Municipal Corporation]] limits in 1994. Samayapuram is located 12 kilometres north from the heart of the city on the Trichy-Chennai road. The [[Samayapuram Mariamman Temple]], a most important place of Hindu worship is located here.
