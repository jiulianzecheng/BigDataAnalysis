 '''''Verbena''''' is a [[genus]] of [[plant]]s in the family [[Verbenaceae]]. The [[type species]] is vervain, which grows in Europe. Some verbenas are considered weeds. It should not be confused with the related ''Lantana'', commonly called shrub Verbena
