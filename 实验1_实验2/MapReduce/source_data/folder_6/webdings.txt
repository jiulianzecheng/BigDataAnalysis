 '''Webdings''' is a [[typeface]] made out of [[dingbat]]s. The [[design]] was made in 1997 by [[Vincent Connare]], Sue Lightfoot, Ian Patterson, and Geraldin Wade. It was put in [[Microsoft]]'s [[wikt:collection|collection]] of typefaces called the [[Core fonts for the Web]]. < ref name= " fontpack-2002-08 " > {{cite web|url=http://www.microsoft.com/typography/fontpack/default.htm|title=TrueType core fonts for the Web|author=Microsoft|date=December 26, 2001|access-date=May 10, 2021|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20020802111427/http://www.microsoft.com/typography/fontpack/default.htm|archive-date=August 2, 2002}} < /ref >  Many of the [[character (computing)|characters]] from Webdings were put in [[Unicode]] in [[wikt:version|version]] 7.0. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Unicode 7.0.0|url=https://www.unicode.org/versions/Unicode7.0.0/|publisher=[[Unicode Consortium]]|access-date=May 10, 2021}} < /ref > 
 
 One of the characters is a [[levitation|levitating]] [[businessman]]. Vincent Connare made the character look similar to the logo of [[2 Tone Records]]. It was put in Unicode with the code {{unichar|1F574|MAN IN BUSINESS SUIT LEVITATING}}. < ref name= " veix " > {{cite web|last1=Veix|first1=Joe|title=The secret history of the 'Man in Business Suit Levitating' emoji|url=http://www.newsweek.com/2016/05/06/secret-ska-history-man-business-suit-levitating-emoji-442192.html|website=Newsweek|access-date=May 10, 2021|date=March 30, 2016}} < /ref >  The character of a [[lightning bolt]] was made to look similar to the one in [[David Bowie]]'s [[album]] ''[[Aladdin Sane]]''. < ref name= " veix " / >  Connare made the characters NYC an eye, a [[heart (symbol)|heart]], and a [[wikt:skyline|skyline]] to show the [[I Love New York]] logo. < ref name= " veix " / > < ref > {{cite web|url=http://archive.wired.com/techbiz/media/news/2001/09/47042|title=MS Denies Wingding Thing, Again|last=Glasner|first=Joanna|date=September 22, 2001|publisher=[[Wired (magazine)|Wired]]|access-date=May 10, 2021}} < /ref >
