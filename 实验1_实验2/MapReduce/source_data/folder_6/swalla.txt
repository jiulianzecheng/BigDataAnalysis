" '''Swalla''' "  is a [[dancehall]] song by [[Jason Derulo]] featuring [[Nicki Minaj]] and [[Ty Dolla Sign]], released on February 24, 2017. The song was originally going to be the first single from Jason Derulo's fifth studio album. < ref > https://planetradio.co.uk/hits-radio/entertainment/music/jason-derulo-announces-2018-uk-tour/ < /ref >  Commercially, the song has peaked the top 10 in 23 countries, making it a worldwide success.
