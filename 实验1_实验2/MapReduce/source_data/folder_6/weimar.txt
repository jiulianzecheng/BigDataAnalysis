 '''Weimar''' is a city in the middle of [[Germany]], in the state of [[Thuringa|Thuringia]]. About 60,000 people live there. It has a [[university]]. 
 
 Most famous citizens in the past were the German [[poet]]s [[Johann Wolfgang von Goethe]] and [[Friedrich Schiller]]. 
 
 The city is well known for the [[Weimar Republic]]. It was founded in Weimar in 1919.
