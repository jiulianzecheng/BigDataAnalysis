'''Jessica''' [ '''jes'''-i-k''uh'' ] < ref > {{Cite web|title=Definition of Jessica {{!}} Dictionary.com|url=https://www.dictionary.com/browse/jessica|access-date=2023-04-06|website=www.dictionary.com|language=en}} < /ref >  is a female [[given name]]. The name first appeared as the name of a character in the Shakespearean play ''The Merchant of Venice''. Common nicknames of the name include  " Jess "  and  " Jessie " . The name Jessica comes from a Hebrew name, Yiskah, which means  " to see "  or  " to gaze upon. "  Yiskah was the name of a character in the Bible who was Haran's daughter, Abraham's brother. She was famous for being beautiful and inspired the name Jessica. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Jessica name Meaning and Origin|url=https://babys.name/detail-Jessica-Meaning-Origin-Famous-Similar-Sibling.html|access-date=2023-04-06|website=babys.name}} < /ref >
