 '''Tignes''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is in [[Auvergne-Rhône-Alpes]] in the [[Savoie]] [[Departments of France|department]] in southeast [[France]]. 
 
 It is known for its [[ski resort]]. Together with neighbouring [[Val d'Isère]], which forms the  " [[Espace Killy]] "  ski area.
