'''Digitization (US), digitisation (UK)''' is converting [[information]] into a [[digital]] (computer-readable) form. In this way, the information is organized into [[binary|bits]]. < ref name=wi > [http://whatis.techtarget.com/definition/digitization Definition of digitization] at WhatIs.com < /ref > < ref name=collins > [https://www.collinsdictionary.com/dictionary/english/digitize  " Digitization/digitisation " ] in Collins English Dictionary < /ref >   
 
 The data is put in the form of [[binary number]]s. This helps computer processing. The meaning of digitizing is the conversion of [[analog signal|analog]] source material into a numerical format.
