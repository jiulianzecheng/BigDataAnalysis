 '''Biberbrugg''' is a [[village]] in the [[Schwyz (canton)|canton of Schwyz]] in [[Switzerland]]. Biberbrugg is shared by the municipalities of [[Einsiedeln]] in the [[Einsiedeln (district)|district of Einsiedeln]] and [[Feusisberg]] in the [[Höfe (district)|district of Höfe]].
