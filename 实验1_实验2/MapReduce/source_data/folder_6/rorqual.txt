 '''Rorquals''' are the largest group of [[baleen whale]]s, with nine species in two genera. They include the largest animal that has ever lived, the [[Blue Whale]], which can reach 150 tonnes, and another that reaches 70 tons; even the smallest of the group, the [[Northern Minke Whale]], reaches 9 tonnes.
