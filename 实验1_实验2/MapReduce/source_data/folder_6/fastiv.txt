 '''Fastiv''' ({{lang-uk|Фастів}}) is a [[city]] in [[Kyiv Oblast]] ([[Oblast|province]]) of central [[Ukraine]]. It is the administrative center of [[Fastiv Raion]] ([[Raion|district]]). In 2021, 44,841 people lived there.{{fact|date=July 2022}}
