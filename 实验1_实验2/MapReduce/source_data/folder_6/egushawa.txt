'''Egushawa''' (c. 1726 – March 1796), also spelled '''Egouch-e-ouay''', '''Agushaway''', '''Agashawa''', '''Gushgushagwa''', '''Negushwa''' was a [[Native Americans in the United States|Native American]] war chief of the [[Odawa|Ottawa]] tribe. His name means  " The Gatherer "  or  " Brings Together " . He was the chief after [[Pontiac (Ottawa leader)|Pontiac]]. He supported the British during the [[American Revolutionary War|American Revolution]]. He was against the [[Treaty of Paris (1783)|Treaty of Paris]] that gave Native lands to Americans. He was a very important leader during the [[Northwest Indian War]]. He was in battles like [[St. Claire's defeat]] and the [[Battle of Fallen Timbers]]. He got a serious injury at the Battle of Fallen Timbers.
