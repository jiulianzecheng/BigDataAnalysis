'''Crumpsall''' is a [[suburb]] and a [[Ward (electoral subdivision)|ward]] of [[Manchester]]. It is 3 miles (5 km) north of Manchester city centre, bordered by [[Cheetham Hill]], [[Blackley]], [[Harpurhey]], [[Broughton]], and [[Prestwich]]. 15,959 lived there in 2011. It used to be in  [[Lancashire]],  [[North Manchester General Hospital]] is in Crumpsall.
