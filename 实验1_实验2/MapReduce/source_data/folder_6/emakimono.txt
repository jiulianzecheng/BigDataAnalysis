 '''''Emakimono''''' (絵巻物) or just '''''emaki'''''  is an [[Japan]]ese horizontal illustrated narration. It was originated in 8th century. Emakimoko used as illustration of [[Scripture|religious texts]], [[legend]]s, or even romantic stories. It was generally depicted the customs of Japanese people.  The preserved copies are protected as [[National Treasures of Japan]].
