'''Clonus''' is the name for involuntary rhythmic contractions of [[Muscle|muscles]] or muscle groups. The term is from the Greek for  " violent, confused motion " .  Cloni can happen as a sign of certain [[Neurological disorder|neurological conditions]], such as [[epilepsy]], [[stroke]], and [[multiple sclerosis]]. They can also be a sign that the [[spinal cord]] was damaged. Cloni can also occur as a reaction to certain [[Drug|drugs]].
