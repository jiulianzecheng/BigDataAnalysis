 '''EEPROM''' (also '''E < sup > 2 < /sup > PROM''') stands for '''electrically erasable programmable read-only memory''' and is a type of [[non-volatile memory]] used in [[computer]]s, integrated in [[smart card]]s and [[remote keyless system]]s.  It is a [[Read Only Memory]] (ROM) whose data can be erased and reprogrammed by higher than normal currents of electricity.
