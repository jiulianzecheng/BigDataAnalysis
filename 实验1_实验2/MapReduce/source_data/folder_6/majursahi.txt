 '''Majursahi''' '''(ମଜୁରସାହି)''' is a village of  Koksara Block in [[Dharmagarh]] sub-division  < ref > {{Cite web |url=http://www.odisha.gov.in/revenue/ADMN_UNIT/adm_unit.htm |title=Untitled Document |access-date=2018-02-07 |archive-date=2016-03-04 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20160304042323/http://www.odisha.gov.in/revenue/ADMN_UNIT/adm_unit.htm |url-status=dead }} < /ref >  in [[Kalahandi district|Kalahandi District]] in [[Odisha|Odisha State]]. This village  comes under [[Phupagaon]] [[Panchayat town|panchayat]] of  Kokasara [[Tehsil]] in Kalahandi District. . Majursahi is 6.5 & nbsp;km distance from [[Koksara]] and 60 & nbsp;km distance from its district main city [[Bhawanipatna]] and 333 & nbsp;km distance from the State's main city [[Bhubaneswar]]. 
 == Demographics == 
 According to census 2001, total population of Majursahi was 1,000.
