 '''William Li''' (born November 25, 1989), better known by his alias '''Scarra''', is an American [[Twitch (service)|Twitch]] [[Video game live streaming|streamer]]. He is a former professional ''[[League of Legends]]'' player. < ref > {{cite news|title=Scarra previews League of Legends Worlds 2017 semi-finals|publisher=[[ESPN]]|url=http://www.espn.in/video/clip?id=21177992|accessdate=January 26, 2019}} < /ref > < ref name= " dbltab " > {{cite web|last=Tonner|first=Kathleen|date=May 23, 2018|title=Looking Back on Team Dignitas in the LCS|url=https://www.dbltap.com/posts/6068848-looking-back-on-team-dignitas-in-the-lcs|url-status=live|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20180907221454/https://www.dbltap.com/posts/6068848-looking-back-on-team-dignitas-in-the-lcs|archive-date=September 7, 2018|accessdate=September 3, 2018|publisher=DBLTAP}} < /ref >  He is most well known for playing with [[Dignitas (esports)|Team Dignitas]]. < ref > {{cite web|title=Scarra to step down as Coach|url=http://team-dignitas.net/articles/news/League-of-Legends/5963/Scarra-to-step-down-as-Coach|accessdate=January 26, 2019|website=team-dignitas.net|archive-date=February 7, 2019|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190207174843/http://team-dignitas.net/articles/news/League-of-Legends/5963/Scarra-to-step-down-as-Coach|url-status=dead}} < /ref >  Li is a co-founder of [[OfflineTV]], an online social entertainment group of content creators.
