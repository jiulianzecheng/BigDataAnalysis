 '''Cadenberge''' (in [[High German]], in [[Northern Low Saxon|Low Saxon]]: '''Cumbarg''') is a municipality in [[Cuxhaven (district)|Cuxhaven]], in [[Lower Saxony]], [[Germany]]. Since 1 November 2016, the former municipality [[Geversdorf]] is part of Cadenberge.
