 '''Altena''' is a town in  [[North Rhine-Westphalia]], Germany. It is in the [[Märkischer Kreis]] district. It is on the [[Lenne]] river valley It is in the northern parts the [[Sauerland]]. About 16,500 lived there in 2020. The major political party is the [[Christian Democratic Union of Germany|CDU]]. The [[Altena Castle]] is a famous tourist attraction in the town and is from the 12th century. There is also the Castle Holtbrinck, St. Matthew's Church, St. Katharine's Church and a special Holländer house. The main religious groups are [[Protestantism|Protestants]] and [[Catholic Church|Catholics]].
