 '''Ottobeuren''' ([[Swabian German|Swabian]]: ''Ottobeire'', [[Medieval Latin]]: ''Ottobura'') < ref > {{Cite CE1913 | wstitle = Ottobeuren | first = Michael | last = Ott }} < /ref >  is a market town and [[Municipalities of Germany|municipality]] in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]], placed 11 & nbsp;km southeast of [[Memmingen]] near the A7. The town is seat of a [[Municipal association (Germany)|municipal association]] with [[Hawangen]] and [[Böhen]].
