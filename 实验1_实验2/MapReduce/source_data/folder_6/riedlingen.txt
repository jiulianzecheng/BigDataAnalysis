 '''Riedlingen''' is a town in the [[Germany|German]] [[States of Germany|federal state]] of [[Baden-Württemberg]]. About 10,000 people live there. It is near the [[Danube]]. 
 
 == History == 
 Riedlingen is over 1,000 years old. Its foundation is dated back to the 13th century. Documents mention it as early as 835. In 1806, it became part of the Kingdom of Württemberg. In 1973 it became part of the [[Biberach (district)|District of Biberach]]. 
 
 == Politics == 
 Riedlingen's current mayor is Marcus Schafft ([[Christian Democratic Union of Germany|CDU]]).
