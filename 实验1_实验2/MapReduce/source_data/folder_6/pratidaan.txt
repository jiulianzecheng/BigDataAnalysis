 '''''Pratidaan''''' ({{lang-bn|  
  প্রতিদান }}); is a [[Bengali Language|Bengali]] television Popular [[Soap Opera]] that premiered on August 21, 2017, and airs on [[STAR Jalsha]]. Produced by Boyhood Productions, it stars [[Sandipta Sen]] and [[Sheikh Rezwan Rabbani]] in lead roles and [[Tanuka Chatterjee]] in a Negative role. It replaced Star Jalsha's popular show ''[[Milon Tithi]]''. 
 
 ==Plot== 
 Shimul is a very well educated girl and she wants to pursue her study further. She wants to get married in a family where she gets her due respect as a highly educated girl. But on the other hand her would be mother-in-law wants to get a bride who is altoegther illiterate. As the destiny had it her son meets Shimul, a girl of diametrically opposite character. They get married and she will have to teach life lessons to her arrogant husband, Neel, was not the kind of marriage Shimul was hoping for. Will they ever fall in love? What happens next?
