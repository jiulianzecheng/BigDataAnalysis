 '''Orunamamu''' ('''Mary Beth Washington'''; 4 April 1921{{spaced ndash}} 4 September 2014) was an [[Americans|American]]-[[Canadians|Canadian]] professional [[Storytelling|storyteller]] and teacher. Her storytelling led her on journeys across the United States including [[Alaska]], overseas to the [[United Kingdom]] and [[Egypt]]. She moved to [[Canada]] during her final years. 
 
 Orunamamu died at the age of 93 in a hospital in [[Calgary]], [[Alberta]].
