 A '''clamp''' is a device used to hold things tightly together and to prevent movement and/or separation of objects. < ref > {{cite web|title=Clamp|url=http://mw1.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/clamp|url-status=dead|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20070823153401/http://mw1.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/clamp|archive-date=2007-08-23|publisher=Merriam-Webster Dictionary|accessdate=2007-01-06}} < /ref > 
 
 Clamps are used in technical work, such as [[woodworking]]. There are also special types of clamps used in [[medicine]].
