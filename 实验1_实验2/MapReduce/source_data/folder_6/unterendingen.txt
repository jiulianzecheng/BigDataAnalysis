 '''Unterendingen''' was a [[municipality]] of the [[district]] of [[Zurzach (district)|Zurzach]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Aargau]] in [[Switzerland]]. On 1 January 2014, the former municipality of Unterendingen merged into the municipality of [[Endingen, Switzerland|Endingen]].
