 '''''Heartfire''''' (1998) is an alternate history and fantasy book that was written by [[Orson Scott Card]]. It is the fifth book in Card's ''[[The Tales of Alvin Maker]]'' series and is about Alvin Miller, the seventh son of a seventh son, which gives him magical powers.
