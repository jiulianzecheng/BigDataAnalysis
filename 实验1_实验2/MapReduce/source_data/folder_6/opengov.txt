'''OpenGov''' is an American technology company based in [[Redwood City, California]]. Its service are being used by more than 1,000 cities, counties, and state agencies across 49 states. < ref > {{cite web |title=OpenGov acquires Ontodia to add open-sourced data to its civic intelligence platform |url=https://techcrunch.com/2016/04/13/opengov-acquires-ontodia-to-add-open-sourced-data-to-its-civic-intelligence-platform/ |website=TechCrunch |language=en}} < /ref > 
 
 ==History==
