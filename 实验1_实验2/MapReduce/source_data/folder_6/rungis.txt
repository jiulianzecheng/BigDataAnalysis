 '''Rungis''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is in the [[Val-de-Marne]] [[Departments of France|department]] in [[France]]. In 2019, 5,657 people lived there. 
 
 The commune is in the southern [[suburb]]s of [[Paris]], {{convert|11.6|km|mi|abbr=on}} from the center. It is best known as the home of the large wholesale food market ''[[Marché d'Intérêt National de Rungis]]''.
