 '''''Dermatophyllum''''' is a [[genus]] of three or four [[species]] of [[shrubs]] and small [[tree]]s. It in the subfamily Faboideae of the [[pea]] family, [[Fabaceae]]. 
 
 Members of the genus are known as mescalbean, [[mescal]] bean or frijolito.  
 
 Its relationships have been somewhat controversial.
