 '''Thun''' ({{lang-fr|Thoune}}) is a [[city]] of the [[administrative district]] of [[Thun (administrative district)|Thun]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Bern (canton)|Bern]] in [[Switzerland]]. The city has about 43,783 inhabitants (around 90,000 in the agglomeration), as of 31 December 2013. 
 
 It is located where the [[Aare]] flows out of [[Lake Thun]] (Thunersee), 30 & nbsp;km (19 & nbsp;mi) south of [[Berne]].
