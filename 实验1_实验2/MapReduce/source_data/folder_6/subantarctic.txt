The '''Subantarctic''' is a region in the [[southern hemisphere]] that is just north of the [[Antarctic]] region. This covers the part of the world roughly to a [[latitude]] of between 46°  & ndash; 60° south of the [[Equator]]. The subantarctic region includes many islands in the southern parts of the [[Indian Ocean]], [[Atlantic Ocean]], and [[Pacific Ocean]].
