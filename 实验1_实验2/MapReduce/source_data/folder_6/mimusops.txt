 '''''Mimusops''''' is a [[genus]] of [[plant]]. It is in the family [[Sapotaceae]].  
 
 == Species == 
 Some species in this genus are: 
 
 * ''[[Mimusops acutifolia]]'', Mildbr. 
 * ''[[Mimusops angel]]'', [[Emilio Chiovenda|Chiov.]] 
 * ''[[Mimusops commersonii]]'' 
 * ''[[Mimusops caffra]]'' 
 * ''[[Mimusops elengi]]'' 
 * ''[[Mimusops heckelii]]'' - African Cherry, Baku, Douka, Makore, Ukola 
 * ''[[Mimusops obovata]]'' 
 * ''[[Mimusops penduliflora]]'', [[Heinrich Gustav Adolf Engler|Engl.]] 
 * ''[[Mimusops petiolaris]]'' 
 * ''[[Mimusops riparia]]'', [[Heinrich Gustav Adolf Engler|Engl.]] 
 * ''[[Mimusops sechellarum]]'', (Oliv.) Hemsl. 
 * ''[[Mimusops zeyheri]]''
