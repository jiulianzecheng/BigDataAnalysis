 A '''ridge''' or a '''mountain ridge''' is a [[landform]] that consists of a chain of [[Mountain|mountains]] or [[Hill|hills]] that form lots of [[Peak|peaks]] for a [[distance]]. Ridges are usually called hills or mountains as well.
