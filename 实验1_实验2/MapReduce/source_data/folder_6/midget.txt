A '''midget''' is a word that is used to describe people who are quite [[small|short]]. A midget is ''not'' somebody with [[dwarfism]]; a midget is a person that has the same proportions of body parts as an averaged-sized human being. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.pbs.org/pov/pov2005/bigenough/special_dwarfism_ety.html|title=What is Dwarfism?|last=Kennedy|first=Dan|date=2005-05-23|publisher=American Documentary|accessdate=2011-11-15|archive-date=2009-03-13|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20090313112314/http://www.pbs.org/pov/pov2005/bigenough/special_dwarfism_ety.html|url-status=dead}} < /ref >  The word ''midget'' can also sometimes be used as an [[insult]]. Sometimes, the word is used for people a [[foot (unit of length)|foot]] below the normal height of a person.'' < sup > [[wikipedia:Citing sources|[source?]]] < /sup > ''
