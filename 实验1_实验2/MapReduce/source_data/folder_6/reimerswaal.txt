 '''Reimerswaal''', [[Zeelandic dialect|Zeelandic]]: ''Reimerswaol'', is a [[municipality]] on the former island of [[Zuid-Beveland]], Province of [[Zeeland]], the [[Netherlands]]. 
 
 About 22,000 people were living in Reimerswaal in 2017. 
 
 North of the municipality lies the [[Eastern Scheldt]], south the [[Western Scheldt]]. 
 
 Reimerswaal borders the Province of [[North Brabant]], and the Belgian Province of [[Antwerp (province)|Antwerp]] (just a small piece). 
 
 [[Yerseke]] is the biggest village in Reimerswaal. It is famous for its breeding of [[mussel|blue mussels]] and [[oyster]]s.
