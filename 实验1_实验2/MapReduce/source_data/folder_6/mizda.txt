 '''Mizda''' or '''Mesdah''' ({{lang-ar|مزدة}} ''Mizdah'') is a town in the [[Jabal al Gharbi District]] in [[Libya]]. It was the capital of the former [[Mizda District]].  
 
 Just to the west of Mizda is the Mizda Army Base at {{Coord|31|25|29|N|12|56|54|E|display=inline}}
