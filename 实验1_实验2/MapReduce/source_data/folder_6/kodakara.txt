'''Kodakara''' is a city in [[Thrissur district]] of [[Kerala]] state in [[South India]]. The total [[population]] of this town is 27,948. It is located on National Highway ([[National Highway 544 (India)|NH 544]]). It is 20 & nbsp;km south of [[Thrissur]] town and about 10 & nbsp;km north of [[Chalakudy]].
