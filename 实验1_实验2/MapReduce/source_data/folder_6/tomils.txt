 '''Tomils''' was a [[municipality]] of the [[district]] [[Hinterrhein (district)|Hinterrhein]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Graubünden]] in [[Switzerland]]. It was formed on 1 January 2009, through the merger of [[Feldis/Veulden]], [[Scheid]], [[Trans, Switzerland|Trans]] and [[Tumegl/Tomils]]. On 1 January 2015, the former municipalities of [[Almens]], [[Paspels]], [[Pratval]], [[Rodels]] and Tomils merged to form the new municipality of [[Domleschg]].
