 '''Huexotzingo''' is a small [[city]] in [[Mexico]]. It is about {{Convert|25|km|mi|abbr=on}}  northeast of [[Puebla, Puebla|Puebla]]. In 2022, about 60,000 people lived there. The city is mostly focused on [[agriculture]]. The [[Codex Hexotzingo]], a [[document]] of a [[trial]] in the [[Middle Ages]] was located there. Today, the city is known for the producition of [[cider]], and of [[fruit preserves]]. Also, the airport of Puebla is located in Huexotzingo. By car, Puebla can be reached in about 20 minutes, [[Mexico City]] is about an hour away.
