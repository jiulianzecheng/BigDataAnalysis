 '''Romanija''' is a mountain, [[karst]] [[plateau]], and area in the [[Republika Srpska]]. Many towns are in the area, such as [[Pale, Bosnia and Herzegovina|Pale]], [[Sokolac]], and [[Han Pijesak]]. < ref > [http://www.palelive.com/reportaze/novakova-pecina-krije-tajne-stanovnika-romanijskog-platoa Пале уживо: Новакова пећина крије тајне становника романијског платоа] < /ref >  The highest point on the mountain is Veliki Lupoglav (1,652 m). 
 
 A [[Association football|football]] club from the nearby town of [[Pale, Bosnia and Herzegovina|Pale]], FK Romanija, is named after the mountain.
