'''Firewood''' is any [[wood]]en material that is gathered and used for [[fuel]]. It is a [[renewable resource]]. Generally, firewood is not highly processed and is in some sort of log or branch form. < ref > ''Firewood Crops:Shrubs and Tree Species for Energy Production: Report of the National Academy of Sciences'', (Washington, D.C., 1960) p. 10 < /ref >
