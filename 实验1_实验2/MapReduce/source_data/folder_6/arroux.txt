 The '''Arroux''' is a [[river]] in central [[France]]. It is a right [[tributary]] of the [[Loire River|Loire]] river. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 The Arroux river has a length of {{Convert|129.4|km|mi|1|abbr=on}}, < ref name= " sandre " > {{cite web |url=http://sandre.eaufrance.fr/app/chainage/courdo/htm/K1--018-.php?cg=K1--018 |title=L'Arroux (K1--0180)) |publisher=SANDRE - Portail national d'accès aux référentiels sur l'eau |language=fr |access-date=11 December 2017 }} < /ref >  and a [[drainage basin]] with an area of {{Convert|3166|km2|sqmi|0|abbr=on }}. 
 
 ===Course=== 
 The Arroux river starts in [[Culètre]] [[Communes in France|''commune'']] in the south of the [[Côte-d'Or]]) department, at an [[altitude]] of about {{Convert|430|m|ft|0|abbr=on }}. < ref > {{cite web |url=https://www.geoportail.gouv.fr/carte?c=4.556111,47.148333 & z=12 & l0=GEOGRAPHICALGRIDSYSTEMS.MAPS.SCAN-EXPRESS.STANDARD::GEOPORTAIL:OGC:WMTS(1) & permalink=yes |title=Source de l'Arroux |publisher=Géoportail |language=fr |access-date=11 December 2017 }} < /ref >  From here, the Arroux flows first to the west and then to the southwest. 
 
 The Arroux river flows through 2 regions and 3 departments. It flows through the following ''communes'', among others: < ref name=sandre / > 
 * [[Bourgogne-Franche-Comté]] region 
 ** [[Côte-d'Or]]: [[Arnay-le-Duc]] 
 ** [[Saône-et-Loire]]: [[Autun]], [[Étang-sur-Arroux]], [[Toulon-sur-Arroux]], [[Gueugnon]], [[Digoin]] 
 * [[Auvergne-Rhône-Alpes]] region 
 ** [[Allier]]: (only a short distance) 
 
 Finally, it flows into the [[Loire River|Loire]] river near the town of [[Digoin]]. < ref > {{cite web |url=https://www.geoportail.gouv.fr/carte?c=3.959167,46.49 & z=12 & l0=GEOGRAPHICALGRIDSYSTEMS.MAPS.SCAN-EXPRESS.STANDARD::GEOPORTAIL:OGC:WMTS(1) & permalink=yes |title=Confluence de l'Arroux |publisher=Géoportail |language=fr |access-date=11 December 2017 }} < /ref >
