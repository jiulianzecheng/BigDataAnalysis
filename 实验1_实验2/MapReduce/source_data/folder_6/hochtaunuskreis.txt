 The '''Hochtaunuskreis''' is a ''Kreis'' ([[Rural districts of Germany|district]]) in the middle of [[Hesse]], [[Germany]]. It is part of the [[Frankfurt/Rhine-Main Metropolitan Region]]. 
 
 The [[Taunus]] mountains cover the district.
