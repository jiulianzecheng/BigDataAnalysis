'''Counterculture''' is a term used in [[psychology]] and [[sociology]]. People use it to describe a set of views that are not of the ''mainstream''. These values are in contrast to what the mainstream people believe. An example of a counterculture that was started in the [[1960s]] is the culture of the [[hippie]]s. This counterculture focused on [[Illegal drugs|drug use]], sexual freedom (freedom of choice), and [[women's rights]].
