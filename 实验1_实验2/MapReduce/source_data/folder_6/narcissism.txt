 '''Narcissism''' is a [[:wikt:condition|condition]] in which a person [[:wikt:admire|admires]] their own [[:wikt:attribute|attributes]] through [[:wikt:vanity|vanity]] or [[:wikt:egotism|egotism]]. The term is derived from [[Narcissus (mythology)|Narcissus]], a figure from [[Greek mythology]] who fell in love with the sight of his own face.
