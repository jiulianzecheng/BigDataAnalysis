 '''Binn''' ([[Walliser German]]: ''Bìi'') is a [[municipality]] of the [[district]] of [[Goms (district)|Goms]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Valais]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 The Binn valley is known for its rich [[mineral]] deposits, some types of which are unique to the area. The notable Lengenbach Quarry is in Binn.
