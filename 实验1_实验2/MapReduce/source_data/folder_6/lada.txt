 '''Lada''' is a brand of [[car]] from the [[AvtoVAZ]] company in [[Tolyatti]], [[Samara Oblast]], [[Russia]].  The car was first sold as an export under the name [[VAZ-2101|Zhiguli]] in the domestic Soviet market after June 1966. They are most popular in Russia and [[Europe]]. 
 
 In April 2012, AvtoVAZ announced that they had ended model 2107 called the [[Lada Riva|Lada Riva / Lada Nova]] after more than 40 years. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.telegraph.co.uk/motoring/news/9211092/Lada-2107-production-ceases-after-more-than-40-years.html|accessdate=2014-06-26|title=Lada 2107 production ceases after more than 40 years|publisher=[[The Daily Telegraph]]|archive-date=2012-04-24|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120424210511/http://www.telegraph.co.uk/motoring/news/9211092/Lada-2107-production-ceases-after-more-than-40-years.html|url-status=live}} < /ref >
