 '''Pirmasens''' ([[Palatine German language|Palatine German]]: ''Bärmesens'') is an independent [[town]] in [[Rhineland-Palatinate]], [[Germany]]. It is near the border with [[France]] and was the center of the shoe industry in Germany until around 1970.
