 '''Langreo''' ({{lang-ast|Llangréu}}) is a city in northern [[Spain]], in [[Asturias]]. About 43,000 people live there. Langreo is about {{convert|20|km|mi|abbr=on}} southeast of [[Oviedo]]. It was an important mining and metallurgical center. 
 
 Football player [[David Villa]] was born here.
