 '''Rhizophoraceae''' is a [[family (biology)|family]] of [[flowering plants]]. There are 149 [[species]] in 16 [[genus (biology)|genera]]. Most of them occur in the [[Old World]]. Well-known members of the family are [[mangrove]] trees.
