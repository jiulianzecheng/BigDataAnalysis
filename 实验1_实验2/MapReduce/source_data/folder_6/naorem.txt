 '''Naorem''' is a Meetei family. It is native to [[Ancient Kangleipak]] ([[Antique Manipur]]). People of this [[family]] mainly inhabit in [[Manipur]], [[India]]. < ref > {{Cite web|url=http://www.indiachildnames.com/mobile/surname.aspx?surname=Naorem|title = Naorem surname}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite web|url=http://www.indiachildnames.com/mobile/originoffamilyname.aspx?surname=Naorem|title = Origin of Naorem surname}} < /ref > 
 == History == 
 During the era of King Samuroiba Ningthou, the Naorem clan built a temple dedicated to God [[Khamlangba]] at Kakching Wairi. The holy shrine was looked after by the Naorem family. < ref > {{Cite book|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=Z6d-IyINtk4C & dq=Khamlangba+Kakching+wairi+Samuroiba+Ningthou+naorem & pg=PA686|title=Encyclopaedia Of Manipur (3 Vol.)|last=Lisam|first=Khomdan Singh|date=2011|publisher=Gyan Publishing House|isbn=978-81-7835-864-2|language=en}} < /ref >
