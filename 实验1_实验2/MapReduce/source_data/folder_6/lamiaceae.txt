 '''Lamiaceae''' or '''Labiatae''', also known as the [[mint]] family, is a [[family (biology)|family]] of [[plant]]s. It includes about 210 [[genus|genera]] and about 3,500 [[species]]. It has been considered closely related to [[Verbenaceae]] but 1990s [[phylogenetic]] studies have shown that a lot of the genera classified in Verbenaceae belong instead in Lamiaceae, whereas the core genera of Verbenaceae are not closely related to Lamiaceae and are more closely related to other members of the [[Lamiales]].
