 '''Etzgen''' was a [[municipality]] of the [[district]] [[Laufenburg (district)|Laufenburg]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Aargau]] in [[Switzerland]]. On 1 January 2010, the former municipalities of Etzgen, [[Hottwil]], [[Mettau, Switzerland|Mettau]], [[Oberhofen, Switzerland|Oberhofen AG]] and [[Wil, Aargau|Wil AG]] merged into the new municipality of [[Mettauertal]].
