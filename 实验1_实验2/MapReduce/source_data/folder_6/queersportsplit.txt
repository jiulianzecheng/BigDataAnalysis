 '''Queer Sport Split''' (short: '''QSS''') is a [[Charitable organization|charity organization]] from [[Split, Croatia|Split]], [[Croatia]]. It has a focus on the [[sport]], [[culture]] and [[leisure]] of [[LGBT|LGBTIQ+]] community. It also works in [[education]] and [[research]] on the sports and LGBTIQ+ topics. It was registered in 2011 and is the longest running local LGBTIQ+ organization in Split and Dalmatia region. < ref > {{Cite web|date=2012-06-09|title=Slobodna Dalmacija - Splićanin: Nije moj problem to što sam peder, već što sam nezaposlen|url=https://slobodnadalmacija.hr/vijesti/hrvatska/splicanin-nije-moj-problem-to-sto-sam-peder-vec-sto-sam-nezaposlen-169779|access-date=2021-02-12|website=slobodnadalmacija.hr|language=hr-hr}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite web|last=|first=|date=|title=Tonči Kranjčević Batalić, splitski umjetnik, kustos i LGBT aktivist: Svaki oblik javnog djelovanja je politički!|url=https://www.crol.hr/index.php/politika-aktivizam/6003-toni-kranjevi-batali-splitski-umjetnik-kustos-i-lgbt-aktivist-svaki-oblik-javnog-djelovanja-je-politiki?rCH=2|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=2021-02-12|website=www.crol.hr}} < /ref >

 == Local activities and relevance == 
 QSS does periodic activities like: excursions and hiking, bocce, recreational swimming and socializing programs like 'Sport i pivo', 'PlesnjaQ' and 'Domaćinstvo' for [[LGBT |LGBTIQ+]] community. Screenings and presentations are organized to inform and inspire participants. QSS helps organizing Split Pride and running of [[:hr:LGBT centar Split|LGBT Center Split]]. < ref > {{Cite web|date=2019-10-18|title=Slobodna Dalmacija - LGBT centar Split: Gašenjem udruge Rišpet nije dovedeno u pitanje organiziranje gay parade|url=https://slobodnadalmacija.hr/split/lgbt-centar-split-gasenjem-udruge-rispet-nije-dovedeno-u-pitanje-organiziranje-gay-parade-628740|access-date=2021-02-12|website=slobodnadalmacija.hr|language=hr-hr}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite web|date=2017-01-19|title=Slobodna Dalmacija - Split je konačno dobio 'pošteni' queer party, a heteroseksualci su totalno dobrodošli!|url=https://slobodnadalmacija.hr/mozaik/drustvena-mreza/split-je-konacno-dobio-posteni-queer-party-a-heteroseksualci-su-totalno-dobrodosli-464357|access-date=2021-02-12|website=slobodnadalmacija.hr|language=hr-hr}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite book|url=https://doi.org/10.1057/9781137391766_10|title=LGBT Activism and the Making of Europe: A Rainbow Europe?|last=Moss|first=Kevin|date=2014|publisher=Palgrave Macmillan UK|isbn=978-1-137-39176-6|editor-last=Ayoub|editor-first=Phillip M.|series=Gender and Politics series|location=London|pages=212–232|language=en|doi=10.1057/9781137391766_10|editor-last2=Paternotte|editor-first2=David}} < /ref >

 '' " Since 2011, Queer Sport Split has organized gatherings and interventions with “soft activism”: activism that intervenes in the public space of the city through crocheting and knitting. The first such intervention was during the planning of Split Pride in 2012, where knit flags pointed to the right of all citizens to public space, rights which the LGBTIQ population was denied by the local government. In 2014, Queer Sport Split held the Marathon On Needles, a public knit-in, to promote access to public space, guerrilla activism, and social participation. " ''

 == International activities and networks == 
 In 2012 it was co-organizing an international conference ''Contesting/Contexting SPORT 2012'', with [[:en:EPAS|EPAS]] (part of [[Council of Europe]]), [[w:EGLSF|EGLSF]] (European Gay and Lesbian Federation) and [[qSPORT - Zagreb]]. 
 
 It contributes to large international exhibitions < ref > {{Cite web|title=contesting/contexting SPORT 2016|url=https://www.e-flux.com/announcements/10866/contesting-contexting-sport-2016/|access-date=2021-02-12|website=www.e-flux.com|language=en}} < /ref > < ref > {{Cite web|title=contesting/contexting SPORT 2016 (2016) – nGbK Archive|url=https://archiv.ngbk.de/en/projekte/contesting-contexting-sport-2016/|access-date=2021-02-12|website=archiv.ngbk.de}} < /ref >  and publications. < ref > {{Cite web|last=|first=|date=|title=EPAS (Council of Europe) brochure on LGBT inclusion in sport|url=https://edoc.coe.int/en/lgbt/6959-lgbt-inclusion-in-sport.html|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=|website=}} < /ref >   
 
 In 2018-2019 it was the leading [[:en:Erasmus+|Erasmus+]] research project 'Beyond Binaries' with five other LGBTIQ+ sport organizations, in collaboration with institutions, networks and initiatives active in research and sport work.
