 '''Sprints''' are short running races in [[Athletics (track and field)|athletics]]. They are events in which runners will not have to  " pace themselves " , but can run as fast as possible for the entire distance. These are often some of the more popular events in the [[Olympic Games]].  The most common distances are 60 [[metre|meters]], 100 meters, 200 meters ,400 meters and 1500 metres.
