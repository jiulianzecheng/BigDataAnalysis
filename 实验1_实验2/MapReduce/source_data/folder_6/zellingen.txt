 '''Zellingen''' is a market community in the [[Main-Spessart|Main-Spessart district]] in the ''[[Regierungsbezirk]]'' of [[Lower Franconia]] (''Unterfranken'') in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]] and the seat of the ''Verwaltungsgemeinschaft'' (Administrative Community) of Zellingen. 
 
 == Geography == 
 
 === Location === 
 Zellingen is in the region of Würzburg on the river [[Main (river)|Main]]. The community has the following ''Gemarkungen'' (traditional rural cadastral areas): Duttenbrunn, Retzbach, Zellingen.
