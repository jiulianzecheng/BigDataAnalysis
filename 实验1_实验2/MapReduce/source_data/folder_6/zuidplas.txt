 '''Zuidplas''' is a [[List of municipalities of the Netherlands|municipality]] in the [[Netherlands|Dutch]] [[Provinces of the Netherlands|province]] of [[South Holland]]. In 2019, 42,762 people lived there. 
 
 It was created on 1 January 2010 from the former municipalities of [[Moordrecht]], [[Nieuwerkerk aan den IJssel]] and [[Zevenhuizen-Moerkapelle]].
