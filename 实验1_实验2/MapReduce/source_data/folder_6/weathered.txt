'''''Weathered''''' is the third studio album by [[Creed (band)|Creed]]. The album was released on November 20, 2001 through Wind-up Records. It was the number one album on the [[Billboard (magazine)|Billboard]] 200 during the week it was released. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.billboard.com/articles/news/77603/creeds-weathered-powers-to-no-1|title=Creed's 'Weathered' Powers To No. 1|work=Billboard|access-date=23 February 2017}} < /ref >
