
 '''Xeon''' {{IPAc-en|ˈ|z|iː|ɒ|n}} is a brand of [[x86]] [[microprocessor]]s [[design]]ed and [[manufacture]]d by [[Intel Corporation]]. In 1988 Intel released the first microprocessor using the name Xeon. < ref > Andre Kvitka, 'Xeon boosts Intel workstations', ''InfoWorld'', vol. 20, Issue 27 (July 6 1998), p. 97 < /ref >
