 In [[geometry]], the '''radius''' of a [[circle]] or [[sphere]], written as  < math > r < /math > , < ref > {{Cite web|last=|first=|date=2020-04-17|title=List of Geometry and Trigonometry Symbols|url=https://mathvault.ca/hub/higher-math/math-symbols/geometry-trigonometry-symbols/|archive-url=|archive-date=|access-date=2020-09-24|website=Math Vault|language=en-US}} < /ref >  is the shortest connection between the center and the boundary.
