
 '''Turksploitation''' is a mockbuster exploitation remake film subgenre after cashing on American films during in time were mostly low-budget unauthorized adaptations with ripoffs of popular movies uses music from various sources and insane plotlines, unintentional references, badly acting and shoddy special effects.
