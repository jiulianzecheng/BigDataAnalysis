'''Ukrnafta''' is the largest [[Petroleum industry|oil company]] in [[Ukraine]]. It operates in [[Lviv Oblast|Lviv]], [[Ivano-Frankivsk Oblast|Ivano-Frankivsk]], [[Chernivtsi Oblast|Chernivtsi]], [[Sumy Oblast|Sumy]], [[Chernihiv Oblast|Chernihiv]], [[Poltava Oblast|Poltava]] and [[Kharkiv Oblast|Kharkiv]] regions. The company has 2,571 [[Oil well|oil]] and gas wells and 563 [[Gas station|gas stations]] in 26 regions of Ukraine. < ref > {{Cite web|date=2012-01-20|title=UKRNAFTA :: Про нас :: Про компанію|url=http://www.ukrnafta.com/ua/about/profile|access-date=2021-04-30|website=web.archive.org|archive-date=2012-01-20|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20120120130946/http://www.ukrnafta.com/ua/about/profile|url-status=bot: unknown}} < /ref > 
 
 == History == 
 Ukrnafta was established on February 23, 1994. 
 
 During 1997 and 1999, the authorized [[Capital (economics)|capital]] increased 21 times. < ref > {{Cite web|date=2007-11-06|title=Відкрите акціонерне товариство  " Укрнафта "  / 00135390 / Зареєстрованi випуски акцій емiтента - ДУ  " АРІФРУ " |url=http://smida.gov.ua/emitents/emissions_zvit.php?id=19764|access-date=2021-04-30|website=web.archive.org|archive-date=2007-11-06|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20071106073449/http://smida.gov.ua/emitents/emissions_zvit.php?id=19764|url-status=bot: unknown}} < /ref > 
 
 As of 2018, the company had 82 special permits for the [[Mining|extraction]] of [[Hydrocarbon|hydrocarbons]], 43 drilling rigs and 537 gas stations. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Про компанiю {{!}} Ukrnafta|url=https://www.ukrnafta.com/pro-kompaniyu|access-date=2021-04-30|website=www.ukrnafta.com|archive-date=2020-10-19|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20201019191814/https://www.ukrnafta.com/pro-kompaniyu|url-status=dead}} < /ref >

 == Structure == 
 The Company consists of: 
 
 * 6 oil and gas production departments; 
 * 3 drilling departments; 
 * 3 gas processing plants; 
 * 1 [[oil refinery]]; 
 * 2 grouting controls; 
 * 2 central bases of production service; 
 * Poltava paramilitary unit for the prevention and elimination of open oil and gas fountains; 
 * Management of automated systems; 
 * Installation and adjustment management; 
 * Research and Design Institute; 
 * Center for Regulatory and Economic Research; 
 * Center for Geological and Thematic Research; 
 * Representation of the Company in the [[Russia|Russian Federation]], the [[Middle East]] and [[North Africa]]. 
 
 == Finances == 
 The authorized capital of Ukrnafta (as of 2010) is [[Ukrainian hryvnia|UAH]] 1,355,7127.50. 
 
 The [[market capitalization]] of the company on January 1, 2011 was about 4,100, 000, 000 [[Dollar|dollars]].
