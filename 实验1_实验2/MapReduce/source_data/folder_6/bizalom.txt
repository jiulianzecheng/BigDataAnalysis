 '''''Bizalom''''', also known as '''''Confidence''''' in English, is a 1980 Hungarian World War II drama movie directed by [[István Szabó]] and starring [[Ildikó Bánsági]], [[Péter Andorai]], [[Oszkárné Gombik]], [[Károly Csáki]], [[Tamás Dunai]], [[Zoltán Bezerédi]], [[Gyula Gazdag]]. It was nominated for an Academy Award in 1981.
