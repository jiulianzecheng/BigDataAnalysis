 '''Schollbrunn''' is a community in the [[Main-Spessart|Main-Spessart district]] in the ''[[Regierungsbezirk]]'' of [[Lower Franconia]] (''Unterfranken'') in [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]] and a member of the ''Verwaltungsgemeinschaft'' (Administrative Community) of Kreuzwertheim. It has a population of over 900. 
 
 == Geography == 
 Schollbrunn is in the Lower Franconia region, west of [[Würzburg]] in the [[Main-Spessart]] district. The community has only the ''Gemarkung'' (traditional rural cadastral area) of Schollbrunn. The town is surrounded by the wooded hills of the [[Spessart]].
