 '''Castrisch''' was a [[municipality]] of the [[district]] of [[Surselva (district)|Surselva]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Graubünden]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 On 1 January 2014, the former municipalities of Castrisch, [[Duvin]], [[Ilanz]], [[Ladir]], [[Luven]], [[Pigniu]], [[Pitasch]], [[Riein]], [[Rueun]], [[Ruschein]], [[Schnaus]], [[Sevgein]] and [[Siat]] merged into the new municipality of [[Ilanz/Glion]].
