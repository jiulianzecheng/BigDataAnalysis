A '''cermet''' is a [[ceramic]] material, like [[porcelain]] or clay, mixed with a [[metal]], like steel or [[copper]]. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://www.winterthurtechnology.com/en/group/solution-finder/materials/cermet.htmlStable |title=component against high wear  |publisher=Winterthur Technology Group |access-date=13 March 2015}} < /ref >  They are long lasting, [[wikt:tough|tough]] [[composite material]]s that are [[stress]] resistant and produce little [[friction]]. Cermets also have the properties of metals. They can [[Conduction|conduct]] [[electricity]]. < ref > {{cite web |url=https://www.academia.edu/8278253/Preparation_of_Cermet_for_Fuel_Cell_Anode_Material |title=Preparation of Cermet for Fuel Cell Anode Material |author=Bakhtawar Mahmood |date=2014 |publisher=Academia.edu |access-date=13 March 2015}}, p. 4 < /ref >  Like metals cermets are somewhat [[malleable]].
