 '''Frasne''' ({{IPA-fr|fʀɑːɴ}}) is a [[Communes of France|commune]] of 1,965 people (2015). It is found in the [[Doubs]] [[Departments of France|department]] in the [[Bourgogne-Franche-Comté]] [[Regions of France|region]] in eastern [[France]].
