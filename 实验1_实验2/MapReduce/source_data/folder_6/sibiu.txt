 '''Sibiu''' ([[IPA]] {{IPA|[si'biw]}}, [[German language|German]]: ''Hermannstadt'', [[Hungarian language|Hungarian]]: ''Nagyszeben'') is a [[city]] in [[Transylvania]], [[Romania]]. About 170,000 people live there. The [[Cibin River]], a [[tributary]] of the [[Olt River]] flows through the city.  It is the capital of [[Sibiu County]].
