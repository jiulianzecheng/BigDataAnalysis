 '''Mugena''' is a village and former municipality of the [[district]] [[Lugano (district)|Lugano]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Ticino]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 On 13 March 2005 the former municipalities of [[Breno, Ticino|Breno]], [[Arosio, Switzerland|Arosio]], [[Fescoggia]], Mugena and [[Vezio]] merged into the new municipality of [[Alto Malcantone]].
