 '''Ghaziabad''' is a city in the [[India]]n state of [[Uttar Pradesh]]. It is a part of the National Capital Region (NCR) and lies on the border of India's capital city [[New Delhi]]. According to the 2011 census, the city had 1,636,068 people living in it making it the 21st most populous city in India. Ghaziabad is a planned and industrial city. Ghaziabad was ranked as the second fastest growing city in the world by a [[City Mayors Foundation]] survey.
