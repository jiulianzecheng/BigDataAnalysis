'''Oswaldtwistle''' is a town in [[Lancashire]] [[Counties of England|county]] near [[Accrington]] in [[North West England|North West]] [[England]]. 
 
 Many years ago, Oswaldtwistle had a running [[textile mill]], which is a presentation today.
