 '''Enciso''' is a town and municipality in the [[García Rovira Province]], part of [[Santander Department]] in northeastern [[Colombia]]. Founded on September 9, [[1773]] by [[Juan de Enciso]], the liberating campaign of [[Simón Bolívar|Simon Bolivar]] passed by this municipality on 7 occasions between the years [[1814]] and [[1821]].
