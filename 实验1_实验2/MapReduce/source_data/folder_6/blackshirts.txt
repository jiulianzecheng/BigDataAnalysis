The '''Blackshirts''' were a [[Fascism|Fascist]] [[paramilitary]] group in [[Italy]] during the rise and reign of [[Benito Mussolini]]. They operated from 1923-1943 . The [[Sturmabteilung|SA]] or the Brownshirts was formed in 1921 by the [[National Socialist German Workers Party]] or better known as the [[Nazi Party]] .
