 '''Kauhajoki''' is a town in [[Southern Ostrobothnia]], [[Finland]]. As of 28 February 2023, 12,723 people lived there. Neighbouring municipalities are [[Isojoki]], [[Kankaanpää]], [[Karijoki]], [[Karvia]], [[Kurikka]] and [[Teuva]].
