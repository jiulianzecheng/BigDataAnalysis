
 The '''Office national d'études et de recherches aérospatiales''' ('''ONERA''') is the leading French research center in the aeronautics, space and defense sectors.  
 
 ==Research== 
 All disciplines and technologies in the field are involved. Many aerospace programs have gone through ONERA: [[Arianespace|Ariane]], [[Dassault Falcon 20|Falcon]], [[Dassault Rafale|Rafale]], [[Airbus]], missiles, helicopters, engines, radars, etc. < ref > [http://maisondupatrimoine-chatillon92.fr/onera/onera/ La recherche aérospatiale : l’ ONERA] < /ref > 
 
 Under the supervision of the Ministry of Defence, this center has a budget of about 230 million euros, half of it in state subsidies.  ONERA has great testing and calculation resources. They also have the largest fleet of wind tunnels in Europe.  
 
 ==People== 
 They employ about 2,000 people. Most of them are researchers, engineers and technicians. < ref > [http://www.air-cosmos.com/l-onera-presente-ses-resultats-financiers-110465 L'Onera présente ses résultats financiers] < /ref > 
 
 The President of ONERA is appointed by the Council of Ministers on the proposal of the Minister of Defense. < ref > [https://revue-progressistes.org/2022/01/14/onera-etre-a-la-hauteur-des-defis-aerospatiaux-des-30-prochaines-annees-ou-pas/ ONERA : être à la hauteur des défis aérospatiaux des 30 prochaines années ou pas.] < /ref >
