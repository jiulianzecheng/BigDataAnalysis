 '''Porvoo''' ({{lang-sv|Borgå}}) is a [[town]] in [[Uusimaa]], [[Finland]]. It has a lot of old [[building]]s. Neighbouring municipalities are [[Askola]], [[Loviisa]], [[Myrskylä]], [[Pornainen]] and [[Sipoo]].
