 '''Aranjuez''' is a city and [[Municipalities of Spain|municipality]] in the [[Community of Madrid]] in [[Spain]]. 
 
 The Cultural Landscape of Aranjuez was declared a [[World Heritage Site]] by [[UNESCO]] in 2001.
