 A '''concertina''' is a [[musical instrument]] that makes [[sound]]s using [[air]] pushed and pulled through [[Reed (instrument)|reeds]] using a [[bellows]]. The fingers push buttons at both ends of the instrument. 
 
 The '''Anglo concertina''' has the bass notes at the left side and makes other tones pushing than pulling a button. 
 
 The '''English concertina''' makes the same tone pushing and pulling a button. 
 
 The '''duet concertinas''' have the bass notes at the left side and make the same tone pushing and pulling a button. 
 The '''[[Wicki-Hayden note layout|Hayden]] duet concertina''' has the same fingering for all scales. It has a shifted [[6-plus-6-instruments|6-plus-6]] [[Keyboard instrument|keyboard]] layout.
