 '''Daugavpils''' ({{IPA-all|ˈdaʊɡaʊpils|Latvian pronunciation:|Lv-Daugavpils.ogg}}; {{lang-ltg|Daugpiļs}} {{IPA-all|ˈdaʊkʲpʲilʲsʲ|}}; {{lang-ru|Даугавпилс}} {{IPA-all|ˈdaʊɡəfpʲɪls|}}) is a [[city]] in southeastern [[Latvia]]. It is on the banks of the [[Daugava River]], from which the city gets its name. Daugavpils literally means  " Daugava Castle " . It is the second largest city in the country after the capital [[Riga]].
