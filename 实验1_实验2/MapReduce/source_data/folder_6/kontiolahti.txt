 '''Kontiolahti''' is a [[municipality]] in [[North Karelia]], [[Finland]]. It had about 14,000 living there in January 2014. The municipalities next to it are [[Joensuu]], [[Juuka]], [[Lieksa]], [[Liperi]] and [[Polvijärvi]]. 
 
 == Some villages == 
 Harivaara, Herajärvi, Jakokoski, Mönni, Onttola, Paihola, Puntarikoski, Rantakylä, Romppala and Venejoki.
