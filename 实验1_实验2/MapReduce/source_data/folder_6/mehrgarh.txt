'''Mehrgarh''', ({{lang-ur|م‍ﮩ‍رگڑھ}}) one of the most important Neolithic (7000 & nbsp;BC to 3200 & nbsp;BC) sites in [[archaeology]], lies on the [[Kachhi Plain]] of [[Balochistan (Pakistan)|Balochistan]], [[Pakistan]]. It is one of the earliest sites with evidence of [[farm]]ing ([[wheat]] and [[barley]]) and herding ([[cow|cattle]], [[sheep]] and [[goat]]s) in [[South Asia]]. 
 
 It was rediscovered about 1974.
