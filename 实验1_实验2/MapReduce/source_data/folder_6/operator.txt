In [[mathematics]], an '''operator''' is a [[Function (mathematics)|function]] (like addition) that acts on one or more [[number]]s to create a new number. 
 
 The most known operators are those used for the first four [[arithmetic]] [[Operation (mathematics)|operations]]: [[Operation (mathematics)#Addition|addition]], [[Operation (mathematics)#Subtraction|subtraction]], [[Operation (mathematics)#Multiplication|multiplication]], and [[Operation (mathematics)#Division|division]] ( < math > + < /math > ,  < math > - < /math > ,  < math > \times < /math > ,  < math > / < /math > ).
