 '''Kenora''', originally named '''Rat Portage''' ({{lang-fr|Portage-aux-Rats}}), is a small city on the [[Lake of the Woods]] in [[Northwestern Ontario]], Canada, close to [[Manitoba]], and about {{convert|200|km|mi|0|abbr=on}} east of [[Winnipeg]]. It is the seat of [[Kenora District]].
