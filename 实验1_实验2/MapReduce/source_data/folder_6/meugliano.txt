 '''Meugliano''' is a ''[[frazione]]'' in the [[Metropolitan City of Turin]] in the [[Piedmont]] region in [[Italy]]. On 1 January 2019, it was merged with [[Trausella]] and [[Vico Canavese]] to form the new ''comune'' of [[Valchiusa]].
