 A '''urinal''' (UK /jʊəˈraɪnl/; US/ˈjʊrənəl/) < ref > https://www.oxfordlearnersdictionaries.com/definition/english/urinal < /ref >  is a special-purpose [[toilet]], made only for [[urination|urinating]] by males in public restrooms, you don't see many installed in homes.  < ref > {{cite web |last1=Douban |first1=Gigi |title=Why aren’t more urinals installed in homes? |url=https://www.marketplace.org/2014/12/22/why-arent-more-urinals-installed-homes/ |website=Marketplace |accessdate=5 November 2023 |date=22 December 2014}} < /ref >  There are two varieties of urinals: for one person, and many people.  [[Armitage Shanks]] is a big manufacturer of them in England. In the [[United States]] [[American Standard Brands|American Standard]] is a big manufacturer of them.
