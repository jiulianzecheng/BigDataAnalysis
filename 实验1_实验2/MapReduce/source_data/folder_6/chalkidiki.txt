 '''Chalkidiki''', also known as '''Halkidiki''' or '''Chalcidice''', is one of 51 [[prefecture]]s of [[Greece]] in the region of [[Macedonia (Greece)|Macedonia]]. It is the area of the ending of [[Central Macedonia]] which accesses deeply in the [[Aegean Sea]] forming three small peninsulas. The capital of Chalcidice is the [[Polygyros]].
