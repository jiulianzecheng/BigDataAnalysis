 '''Lumnezia''' ({{lang-de|Lugnez}}) is a new [[municipality]] of the [[district]] [[Surselva (district)|Surselva]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Graubünden]], [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 The former municipalities of [[Cumbel]], [[Degen, Switzerland|Degen]], [[Lumbrein]], [[Morissen]], [[Suraua]], [[Vignogn]], [[Vella]], and [[Vrin]] merged on 1 January 2013, into the new municipality of Lumnezia.
