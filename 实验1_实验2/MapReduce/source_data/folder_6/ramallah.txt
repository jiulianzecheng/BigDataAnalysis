 '''Ramallah''' ({{lang-ar|رام الله}} ''Rāmallāh'' {{Audio|Ar-Ramallah.oga| & nbsp;}} is a [[Palestine|Palestinian]] city, in the [[West Bank]]. It is {{convert|10|km|mi}} north of [[Jerusalem]]. It currently serves as the ''[[de facto]]'' [[Capital (city)|capital]] of the [[State of Palestine]], [[Palestine Liberation Organization]] and the [[Palestinian National Authority]]. The [[constitution]]al capital is Jerusalem. Ramallah has over 27,000 people living there. < ref name= " pcbs114 " > [http://www.pcbs.gov.ps/Portals/_PCBS/Downloads/book1487.pdf 2007 PCBS Census].  The name of the city is made of  " Ram " , an [[Aramaic]] word that means  " high place or mountain " , and  " Allah " , the Arabic word for [[God]]. It translates as  " Height of God " . < ref > {{cite web |url=http://www.ramallah.ps/etemplate.aspx?id=81 |title=Ramallah.ps |publisher=Ramallah.ps |accessdate=November 13, 2011 |archive-date=November 5, 2011 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20111105032756/http://www.ramallah.ps/etemplate.aspx?id=81 |url-status=dead }} < /ref > 
 
 Palestinian PLO leader (1969-2004) Yasser Arafat is entombed here in mausoleum.
