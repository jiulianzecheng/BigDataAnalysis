 '''Charcuterie''' is a [[French language|French]] word that means cold cooked meats and the process of making them. Charcuterie often means food made from [[pork]] but not always. 
 
 An example of a charcuterie meat is [[salami]]. 
 
 A charcuterie platter or charcuterie board is a wooden board with these types of meats, crackers, fruits, nuts, etc.
