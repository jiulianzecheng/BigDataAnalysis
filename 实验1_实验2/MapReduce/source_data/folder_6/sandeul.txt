 '''Lee Jung-hwan''' ({{ko-hhrm|이정환|李征桓}}; born March 20, 1992), better known by his stage name '''Sandeul''' ({{korean|산들}}) is a South Korean singer-songwriter. He is a member of the South Korean boy group [[B1A4]], that debuted on April 23, 2011. He debuted as a solo artist on October 4, 2016.
