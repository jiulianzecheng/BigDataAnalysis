 '''Semiana''' is a ''[[comune]]'' in the [[Province of Pavia]] in the [[Lombardy]] region in [[Italy]]. It is about 50 & nbsp;km southwest of [[Milan]] and about 35 & nbsp;km west of [[Pavia]]. As of 31 December 2004, 256 people lived there. It has an area of 9.9 & nbsp;kmÂ². < ref name= " istat " > All demographics and other statistics: Italian statistical institute [[National Institute of Statistics (Italy)|Istat]]. < /ref > 
 
 Semiana is next to these municipalities: [[Lomello]], [[Mede, Lombardy|Mede]], [[Sartirana Lomellina]], [[Valle Lomellina]], [[Velezzo Lomellina]].
