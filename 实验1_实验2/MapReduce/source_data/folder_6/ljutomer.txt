'''Ljutomer''' ({{IPA-sl|ˈljuːtɔmɛɾ|pron|Sl-Ljutomer.ogg}}; {{lang-de|Luttenberg in der Steiermark}}) is a town in northeastern [[Slovenia]], some 40 & nbsp;km east of [[Maribor]]. It is the seat of the [[Municipality of Ljutomer]]. It was part of the region of [[Lower Styria|Styria]]. It is now included in the [[Mura Statistical Region]]. < ref > [http://www.obcinaljutomer.si/ Ljutomer municipal site] < /ref >  The economy of Ljutomer is largely based on grape farming and wine making.
