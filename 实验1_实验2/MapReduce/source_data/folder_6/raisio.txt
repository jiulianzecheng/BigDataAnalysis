 '''Raisio''' ({{lang-sv|Reso}}) is a city in [[Southwest Finland]]. As of January 2014 about 24,500 people live there. Neighbouring municipalities are [[Masku]], [[Naantali]], [[Rusko]] and [[Turku]].  
 
 Most people there speak [[Finnish language|Finnish]].
