'''Ichthyology''' is the [[scientific]] study of [[fish]]. 
 The term '''Ichthyology''' which is derieved from following two ''Greek'' word 'Ichthyus' means [[fish]] and 'logos' means knowledge. 
 It includes [[hagfish]] and [[lamprey]]s ([[jawless fish]]), [[cartilagenous]] fish (such as [[shark]]s, [[Skate (fish)|skate]]s and [[Stingray|rays]]) ([[Chondrichthyes]]) and bony fishes ([[Osteichthyes]]).  
 
 A person who studies fish is called an ''ichthyologist''.
