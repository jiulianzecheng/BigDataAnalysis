 '''Zirndorf''' ({{IPA-de|ˈt͡sɪʁndɔʁf|-|De-Zirndorf.ogg}}) is a town, which is part of [[Fürth (district)|Fürth]]. It is in northern [[Bavaria]], [[Germany]] in the [[Regierungsbezirk]] of [[Middle Franconia]].
