 '''''Moonstruck''''' is a 1987 American romantic comedy movie directed by [[Norman Jewison]] and starring [[Cher]], [[Olympia Dukakis]], [[Vincent Gardenia]], [[Nicolas Cage]], [[Danny Aiello]], [[John Mahoney]], [[Louis Guss]], [[Julie Bovasso]], [[Anita Gillette]]. It was distributed by [[Metro-Goldwyn-Mayer]] and was nominated for 6 Oscars and won 3 Academy Awards in 1988.
