 '''Elementar''' is a German multinational manufacturer of elemental analyzers and isotope ratio mass spectrographs for the analysis of non-metals such as carbon, nitrogen, sulphur, hydrogen, oxygen or chlorine. The company comes from Heraeus, a multinational German engineering company that produced analytical equipment. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Excellence in elements: The experts in elemental analysis|url=https://www.elementar.com/en/|access-date=2022-11-15|website=www.elementar.com|language=en}} < /ref >
