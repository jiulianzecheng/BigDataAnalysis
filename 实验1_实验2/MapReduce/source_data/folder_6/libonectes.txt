 '''''Libonectes''''' was a [[plesiosaur]] related to ''[[Elasmosaurus]]''. It lived 80 to 65 [[million]] years ago. It grew up to 12 [[metre]]s long. It was a [[predator]] of small fish; the fish ate [[plankton]] in the upper [[pelagic]] layers of the sea.
