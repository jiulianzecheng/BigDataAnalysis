 '''''Astroworld''''' is the third [[studio album]] by [[Americans|American]] [[rapping|rapper]] [[Travis Scott]]. ''Astroworld'' was released on August 3, 2018. In the United States, it debuted at number one on the [[Billboard 200|''Billboard'' 200]]. The album was nominated for [[Grammy Award for Best Rap Album|Best Rap Album]] at the [[61st Annual Grammy Awards|2019 Grammy Awards]].
