 '''Langedijk''' is a former municipality in the [[Netherlands|Dutch]] [[Provinces of the Netherlands|province]] of [[North Holland]]. In 2021, about 28,300 people lived there. 
 
 Since 2022, it has been part of the new municipality of [[Dijk en Waard]].
