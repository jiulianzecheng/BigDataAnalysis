'''Thorapalli''' is a village in Hosur taluk, [[Krishnagiri district]] of [[India]]n state of [[Tamil Nadu]]. The village is on the banks of [[Ponnaiyar River]]. The population of the village according to 2011 census is 9849 people of 4669 males and 5180 females. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.census2011.co.in/data/village/643794-thorapalli-agraharam-tamil-nadu.html|title=Thorapalli Agraharam Village Population - Hosur - Krishnagiri, Tamil Nadu|website=www.census2011.co.in}} < /ref > 
 
 This village is the birthplace of India's first Indian Governor General and Indian freedom fighter, [[C. Rajagopalachari]].
