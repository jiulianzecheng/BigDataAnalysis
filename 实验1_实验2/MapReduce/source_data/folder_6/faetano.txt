 '''Faetano''' is one of the 9 communes or  " castelli "  of [[San Marino]]. 1,164 people live there (October 2013) in an area of 7,75 & nbsp;km².  
 
 == Geography == 
 It is next to on the San Marino municipalities [[Montegiardino]], [[Fiorentino]], [[Borgo Maggiore]], and [[Domagnano]] and the Italian municipalities [[Coriano]], [[Montescudo]] and [[Sassofeltrio]].  
 
 == History == 
 Faetano joined San Marino in 1463. It was the last part added to San Mariono. 
 
 == Parishes == 
 Faetano has four parishes (''[[Curazia|curazie]]''): 
 *Cà Chiavello, Calligaria, Corianino, Monte Pulito
