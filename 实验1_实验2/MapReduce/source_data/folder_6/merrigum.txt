 '''Merrigum''' is a small town in the [[Goulburn Valley]] region of [[Victoria (Australia)|Victoria]], Australia. The town is part of [[City of Greater Shepparton]] local government area. It is {{convert|197|km|mi}} north of the state capital, [[Melbourne]]. In 2011 there were 672 people living in the town. < ref name= " ABS " > {{Census 2011 AUS|id=SSC20882 |name= Merrigum|accessdate=7 February 2013 |quick=on}} < /ref >
