 '''Hasketon''' is a village and [[civil parish]] in [[Suffolk Coastal]], [[Suffolk]], England. In 2001 there were 409 people living in Hasketon. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.ons.gov.uk/help/localstatistics|title=Local statistics - Office for National Statistics|website=www.ons.gov.uk}} < /ref >  Hasketon has a church called St Andrew. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.suffolkchurches.co.uk/hasketon.html|title=Suffolk Churches|website=www.suffolkchurches.co.uk}} < /ref >  Hasketon is near to [[Woodbridge]] and [[Ipswich]]. < ref > {{Cite web |url=http://hasketon.net/ |title=Hasketon Village Website |access-date=2013-08-26 |archive-date=2013-06-11 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20130611033944/http://hasketon.net/ |url-status=dead }} < /ref >
