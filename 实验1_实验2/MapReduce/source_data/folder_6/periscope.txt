 A '''periscope''' is an [[Optics|optical]] instrument that is used to watch a scene from cover. The classical design uses a number of [[Mirror|mirrors]] or [[Prism (optics)|prisms]], and is used in [[Tank|tanks]] and [[Submarine|submarines]].
