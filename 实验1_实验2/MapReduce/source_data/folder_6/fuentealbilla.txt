 '''Fuentealbilla''' is a [[Municipalities of Spain|municipality]] of 1,821 people (2022). It is in the [[Provinces of Spain|province]] of [[Province of Albacete|Albacete]] in the [[Autonomous communities of Spain|autonomous community]] of [[Castile-La Mancha]] in [[Spain]]. The nearest town is [[Albacete]], about 44 & nbsp;km away. 
 
 Former [[Spain national football team|Spain]] international footballer [[Andrés Iniesta]] was born here.
