 '''Norderney''' is one of the [[East Frisian Islands]] off the [[North Sea]] coast of [[Germany]]. It belongs to the municipality of the district of [[Aurich (district)|Aurich]] in [[Lower Saxony]]. 
 
 ==Geography== 
 The island has a total area of {{Convert|26.3|km2|sqmi}} and making it the ninth-largest island in Germany. It has about 6,000 people. On the northern side of the island is a {{convert|14|km|adj=on}} long sandy beach. 
 
 The neighbouring island to the east is [[Baltrum]], which is about 800m away. To the west is the island of [[Juist]], about {{convert|3|km}} away.
