 '''Feichten''', also known as '''Feichten an der Alz''', is a [[municipality]] in [[Altötting (district)|Altötting]], a district ''[[Districts of Germany|(Landkreis)]]'' in [[Upper Bavaria]]. It's official name is '''Feichten a.d.Alz'''.
