 '''Aguascalientes''' is a [[states of Mexico|state]] in [[Mexico]] that is in the center of the country. Its state capital is the [[Aguascalientes, Aguascalientes|city of Aguascalientes]]. The state's name means  " hot waters "  in [[Spanish language|Spanish]]. People gave it this name because there are many [[hot spring]]s in the area. The adjective for Aguascalientes is ''hidrocálido'', because ''hidro'' means water and ''cálido'' means hot.
