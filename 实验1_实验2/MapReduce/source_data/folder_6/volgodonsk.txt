 '''Volgodonsk''' ({{lang-rus|Волгодонск|p=vəlgɐˈdonsk}}) is a [[city]] in [[Rostov Oblast]], [[Russia]]. It is in the east of the [[oblast]]. It is on the west side of the [[Tsimlyansk Reservoir]]. [[Population]]: {{ru-census2010|170,841|;}} {{ru-census2002|165,994|;}} {{su-census1989|175,593|;}} 28,000 (1970). 
 
 == History == 
 [[File:Volgodonsk_1999.jpg|left|thumb|290x290px|After the 1999 bombing.]] 
 Volgodonsk was founded in 1950. < ref name= " gr "  / >  It was a small settlement for the Tsimlyansk [[hydroelectric dam]] workers. It grew because of the building of the [[Volga–Don Canal]]. It became a city in 1956.  
 
 On September 16, 1999 a [[Car bomb|truck bomb]] [[Explosion|exploded]] outside an [[apartment building]]. It killed 17 people and injured 69 more people. It was the fifth explosion in a series of [[Russian apartment bombings|apartment bombings in Russia]] that killed more than 300 people. 
 
 == Geography == 
 Volgodonsk is in the eastern part of Rostov Oblast. It is between [[Rostov-on-Don]] and [[Volgograd]]. Other big cities close to it include [[Stavropol]], [[Krasnodar]], and [[Elista]]. It is in the [[Salsky Steppes]]. 
 
 Volgodonsk has a [[continental climate]]. It has cold winters and warm summers. 
 
 == Economy == 
 [[File:Мирный_атом.jpg|left|thumb|200x200px|[[Peaceful atom (Volgodonsk)|Peaceful atom]] monument]] 
 Volgodonsk has one of the bigger economies of Rostov Oblast. [[Nuclear power]] is the main [[industry]] in the city. Volgodonsk has the [[Atommash]] [[factory]] in it. This factory makes [[Nuclear reactor|nuclear reactors]] and heavy machines. The factory makes [[Rosatom|Rosatom's]] [[VVER]] type [[Pressurized water reactor|pressurized water reactors]] too. Four of these reactors (VVER-1000 model) [[Electricity generation|generated]] about 26 600 GWh of [[electricity]] at the [[Rostov Nuclear Power Plant|Rostov nuclear power plant]]. This [[nuclear power plant]] is 18 km from the [[city center]]. 
 
 == Culture == 
 Volgodonsk is one of the bigger cultural centers of Rostov Oblast. There are six art schools, two museums, seventeen historical and cultural sites, and three buildings that are architectural monuments of regional importance. The [[Cathedral of the Nativity, Volgodonsk|Cathedral of the Nativity]] is also in Volgodonsk.
