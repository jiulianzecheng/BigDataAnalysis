 '''Alexandroupoli''' (or Alexandroupolis) is a [[Greece|Greek]] city, in the [[Thrace]] periphery. It is the capital of [[Evros]] prefecture. Alexandroupolis is in the northeast of [[Greece]], very close to [[Turkey]] and [[Bulgaria]]. It had 57,812 people according to 2011 census.
