'''Jötunheimr''' (or '''Jotunheim''') is the land of the [[Jotun|giants]] in the [[Norse mythology]]. Two kinds of giants (or [[Jotun]]s) live in Jötunheimr, [[Rock (geology)|rock]] giants and [[ice|frost]] giants. From here they can attack the [[human]]s in [[Midgard]] and the [[Æsir|gods]] in [[Asgard]]. Jotunheim is ruled by King [[Thrym]].
