 '''Verona''' is a [[city]] in the [[Veneto]] region of [[Italy]] with 260,000 [[inhabitant]]s. It is [[famous]] for being the setting for the [[William Shakespeare|Shakespeare]] play ''[[Romeo and Juliet]]'' and because of the Roman Arena. This city is built on the [[Adige]] river.
