
 A '''barrister''' is a type of [[lawyer]]. Barristers mostly specialise in [[Court|courtroom]] advocacy and litigation. Their tasks include arguing cases in courts and tribunals, drafting legal pleadings, researching the law and giving legal opinions.  In some places  they do not work directly with their clients, but have work through a [[Solicitor|solicitor.]]
