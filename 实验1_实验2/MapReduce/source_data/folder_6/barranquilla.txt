 '''Barranquilla''' ({{IPA-es|baraŋˈkiʝa}}) is a city and municipality in northern [[Colombia]]. It is the capital of the [[Atlántico Department]]. With a population of 1,885,500 (2011), it is Colombia's fourth largest city after [[Bogotá]], [[Medellín]] and [[Cali]].
