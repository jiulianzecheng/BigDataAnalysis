An '''antonym''' is a [[word]] that has the [[wikt:opposite|opposite]] [[definition|meaning]] of another word.  The first known use of the word '''antonym''' was in 1857. < ref > {{Cite web|url=https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/antonym|title=Definition of ANTONYM|website=www.merriam-webster.com|language=en|access-date=2018-03-21}} < /ref >   Properly defined it means ''a word of opposite meaning'' and is a noun.
