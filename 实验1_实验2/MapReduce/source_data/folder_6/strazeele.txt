 '''Strazeele''' ({{IPA-fr|stʁazɛl}}; from [[Flemish]]; ''Strazele'' in modern Dutch spelling) is a [[Communes in France|commune]] in the [[Nord (department)|Nord]] in northern [[France]]. In 2019, 948 people lived there.
