 '''EgyptAir''' is the [[flag carrier]] of [[Egypt]]. It began in [[1933]]. < ref > {{cite web | url=https://www.egyptair.com/en/about-egyptair/Pages/story.aspx | title=EGYPTAIR - Story }} < /ref >  Originally, it was named  " Misr Airwork " . The name was finally changed to EgyptAir in October 1971. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://training.egyptair.com/General|title=Academy Profile|website=training.egyptair.com}} < /ref >  The airline was the first from [[Middle East|the Middle east]] to have [[jet aircraft]]. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.msn.com/en-za/news/world/egypt-finds-belongings-debris-from-plane-crash-at-sea/ar-BBtgUqR|title=Egypt finds belongings, debris from plane crash at sea|website=www.msn.com}} < /ref >  In 2008, it joined [[Star Alliance]]. < ref > http://www.ana.co.jp/wws/us/e/about_ana/corp_info/pr/2008/pdf/080714.pdf < /ref >  EgyptAir suffered heavy losses during the [[2011 Egyptian revolution]]. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.anna.aero/2013/06/05/egyptair-struggles-to-fully-recover/|title=Egyptair struggles to fully recover; launches four new destinations this week, including Manchester and Toronto|date=5 June 2013|website=anna.aero}} < /ref >
