 '''Kayastha''' is one of the social groups of [[India]]. They have a long [[history]]. They number around 10-12 million people and most of them live in India. 
 
 [[Hindu]]s believe that [[Brahma|Lord Brahma]] created the world. He then created 16 sons from different parts of his body. His 17th son, [[Shree Chitraguptjee]], was created from his mind and soul. < ref name=kp > {{cite web |url=http://www.kayasthaparivar.org/the_history.htm |title=Welcome to Kayastha Parivar |first= |last= |work=kayasthaparivar.org |year=2005 |accessdate=11 November 2011 |archive-date=14 October 2011 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20111014082507/http://www.kayasthaparivar.org/the_history.htm |url-status=dead }} < /ref >  He is the only son to be made from the whole body, not just parts. In human form he is called ''kayastha'', ''kaya'' meaning whole. < ref name=kp/ >  Kayastha's have two roles in the [[caste]] system, as [[Kshatriya]] (a warrior) and [[Brahmin]] (a learned person or scribe). The Kayastha caste is divided into 12 sub-castes. < ref name=kp/ >  Subdivision of Kayastha:- Chitragupta Kayastha of North India, Prabhu Kayastha of Maharashtra, Karnam/Karuneegar (Kayastha) of South India, Karanas (Kayastha) of Orissa, Bengali Kayastha of Bengal and Kalitas (Kayastha) of Assam.
