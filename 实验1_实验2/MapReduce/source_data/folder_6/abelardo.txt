'''Abelardo''' is a male name with origins in the [[Spanish language|Spanish]] and [[Portuguese language|Portuguese]] languages. It has been in use for many centuries and has a deep historical and cultural significance. The name comes from the [[Germanic languages|Germanic]] name Abelard, which translates to  " noble strength "  or  " resolute.
