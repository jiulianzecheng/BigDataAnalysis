 '''''Borophagus''''' is an [[extinction|extinct]] [[genus]] of the [[Canidae]] family, [[endemic]] to [[North America]]. The species were similar in appearance to modern-day [[hyenas]], and weighed between {{convert|20|kg}} and {{convert|40|kg}}. There are eight known species that lived from around 12 million years ago to about 2 million years ago.
