 The '''Province of Basilan''' ([[Chavacano]]: ''Provincia de Basilan''; [[Tausug language|Tausug]]: ''Wilaya' sin Basilan''; [[Yakan language|Yakan]]: ''Wilayah Basilanin'') is an [[island]] [[Provinces of the Philippines|province]] of the [[Philippines]] that is found in the [[Bangsamoro|Bangsamoro Autonomous Region]]. The capital is [[Lamitan|Lamitan City]]. < ref name= " philstar.com " > {{Cite web|url=https://www.philstar.com/nation/2016/02/28/1557698/armm-put-new-basilan-capitol|title=ARMM to put up new Basilan capitol|website=philstar.com}} < /ref >  The province was created on December 27, 1973. 
 
 Basilan Island is the largest and northernmost of the major islands of the [[Sulu Archipelago]].  The former capital is [[Isabela, Basilan|Isabela City]].
