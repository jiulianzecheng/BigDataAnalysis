 A '''relish''' is a [[cook]]ed, [[wikt:chop|chopped]], or [[wikt:pickled|pickled]] [[vegetable]] or [[fruit]]. 
 
 ==Origin and use== 
 Relish originated in [[India]], < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.ams.usda.gov/grades-standards|title=Grades and Standards - Agricultural Marketing Service|website=www.ams.usda.gov}} < /ref >  and has spread worldwide. Although it is used as an [[ingredient]] in many different dishes in India, it is mainly used as a [[condiment]] in most other countries. This use has made it one of the world's largest selling condiments. < ref > {{cite book|last=Smith|first=Andrew F. |title=The Oxford companion to American food and drink|url=https://books.google.com/books?id=AoWlCmNDA3QC & pg=PT171|accessdate=January 10, 2014|date=May 1, 2007|publisher=Oxford University Press|isbn=978-0-19-530796-2|pages=144â€“146}} < /ref >  It is mainly used on [[hot dog]]s and [[hamburger]]s.
