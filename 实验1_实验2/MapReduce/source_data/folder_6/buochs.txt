 '''Buochs''' is a [[municipality]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Nidwalden]] in [[Switzerland]].  
 
 The main sights of Buochs are the [[church]] ''St. Martin'', the ''Loreto'' [[chapel]] in ''Ennerberg'', a [[monument]] of [[Johann Melchior Wyrsch]], and the [[wharf|quay]].
