 '''Surcuolm''' is a former [[municipality]] of the [[district]] of [[Surselva (district)|Surselva]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Graubünden]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 In 2009 it was merged with [[Flond]] to form the municipality of [[Mundaun]].
