 '''Vitznau''' is a [[municipality]] in the [[district]] of [[Lucerne-Land (district)|Lucerne-Land]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Lucerne (canton)|Lucerne]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 It occupies a narrow strip between the lake and the [[Rigi]] looming behind; one of the [[mountain railway]]s to the summit starts in Vitznau.
