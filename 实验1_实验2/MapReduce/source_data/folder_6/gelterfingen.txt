 '''Gelterfingen''' was a [[municipality]] in the [[administrative district]] of [[Bern-Mittelland (administrative district)|Bern-Mittelland]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Bern (canton)|Bern]] in [[Switzerland]]. On 1 January 2018, the former municipalities of Gelterfingen, [[Mühledorf, Berne|Mühledorf]] and [[Noflen]] merged into the municipality of [[Kirchdorf, Switzerland|Kirchdorf]].
