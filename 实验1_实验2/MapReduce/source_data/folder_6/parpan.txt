 '''Parpan''' used to be a [[municipality]] of the [[district]] [[Plessur (district)|Plessur]] of the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Graubünden]] in [[Switzerland]]. On 1 January 2010, the municipalities of [[Malix]] and Parpan became part of [[Churwalden]]. < ref name=fusion > {{Cite report |title= Amtliches Gemeindeverzeichnis der Schweiz, Mutationsmeldungen 2009 / Répertoire officiel des communes de Suisse, Mutations 2009 / Elenco ufficiale dei Comuni della Svizzera, Mutazione 2009|url= http://www.bfs.admin.ch/bfs/portal/de/index/infothek/nomenklaturen/blank/blank/gem_liste/03.Document.128095.pdf |publisher= Federal Statistical Office |year= 2009 |access-date= 6 March 2010 | docket = 3166}} < /ref >
