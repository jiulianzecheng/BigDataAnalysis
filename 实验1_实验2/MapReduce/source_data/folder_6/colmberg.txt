 '''Colmberg''' ({{IPA-de|ˈkɔlmbɛɐ̯k}}; [[East Franconian]]: ''Colmberch'') is a [[municipality]] in [[Ansbach (district)|Ansbach]] in [[Bavaria]] in [[Germany]]. Colmberg is the site of Colmberg Castle ({{lang-de|Burg Colmberg}}), built in the 13th century and purchased in 1318 by Duke Frederick IV of [[Nuremberg]]. < ref > {{cite web |url=http://www.burg-colmberg.de/showpage.php?SiteID=8 & lang=2 |title=The Castle |publisher=Colmberg Castle |accessdate=15 January 2013 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20131025211028/http://www.burg-colmberg.de/showpage.php?SiteID=8 & lang=2 |archive-date=25 October 2013 |url-status=dead }} < /ref >  The town is located in [[Nature Park Frankenhöhe]].
