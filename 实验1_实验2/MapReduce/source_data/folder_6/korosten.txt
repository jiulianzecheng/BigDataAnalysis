 '''Korosten''' ({{lang-uk|Ко́ростень}}, {{IPA-uk|ˈkɔrostenʲ|pron}}; historically also '''Iskorosten''' {{lang|uk|Іскоростень}}) is a historic [[city]] and a big [[transport hub]] in [[Zhytomyr Oblast]] ([[Oblast|province]]). It is in northern [[Ukraine]]. It is on the [[Uzh River (Prypiat)|Uzh River]]. Korosten is the [[Capital city|administrative center]] of the [[Korosten Raion]] ([[Raion|district]]). As of January 2022 its [[population]] was [[Approximation|approximately]] {{Ua-pop-est2022|61496|punct=.|showyear=false}}
