'''Manglish''' is a [[language]] that sounds almost the same as  [[English language|English]] spoken by [[Malaysia]]ns.  It is a close cousin of [[Singlish]]. Along with other languages, Manglish is extremely popular with [[Malaysia]]ns that have trouble speaking [[English language|English]]
