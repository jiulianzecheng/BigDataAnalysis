 '''Kinopoisk''' ({{lang-ru|Кинопоиск}}, a [[portmanteau]] of  " cinema "  and  " search " ) is a Russian online [[database]]. It started in 2003.  It has information about movies and TV shows including [[cast]], production team, biographies, plot summaries, ratings, and reviews.  It also has stills, trailers, posters, and wallpapers.  
 
 Users  can edit the information and post reviews.  
 
 From April 2018, Kinopoisk has an application for [[Samsung]] TVs with Smart TV function, which allows people to watch films for rent or purchase. < ref > {{cite web|lang=ru|url=https://vc.ru/media/35770-kinopoisk-zapustil-prilozhenie-s-platnymi-filmami-dlya-smart-tv?from=yandex|title=«Кинопоиск» запустил приложение с платными фильмами для Smart TV|website=vc.ru|accessdate=2019-02-25|archive-date=2019-02-25|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20190225223830/https://vc.ru/media/35770-kinopoisk-zapustil-prilozhenie-s-platnymi-filmami-dlya-smart-tv?from=yandex}} < /ref >
