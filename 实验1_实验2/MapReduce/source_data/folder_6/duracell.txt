 '''Duracell''' is a brand of [[Battery|batteries]] made by [[Procter and Gamble]]. Duracell makes alkaline batteries of 9V, C, AA, AAA, and D sizes. < ref > {{Cite web|last=Neri|first=Benvenuto|date=2021-07-02|title=30 Le Migliori Recensioni Di Duracell Industrial Aa Testato|url=http://www.formula1passion.it/30-le-migliori-recensioni-di-duracell-industrial-aa-testato/|access-date=2021-07-04|website=Formula1Passion.it|language=it-IT}} < /ref > 
 
 In 2014, [[Berkshire Hathaway]] bought the Duracell. < ref > {{Cite web|date=2016-02-29|title=P & G Completes Exchange of Duracell to Berkshire Hathaway|url=https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20160229006829/en/PG-Completes-Exchange-of-Duracell-to-Berkshire-Hathaway|access-date=2021-07-12|website=www.businesswire.com|language=en}} < /ref > 
 == History == 
 The company was founded in the 1920s by Samuel Ruben and Philip Rogers Mallory. < ref > {{Cite web|date=2011-11-30|title=Duracell {{!}} History Of The Duracell Company|url=http://www.duracell.com/en-US/company/history.jspx|access-date=2021-07-12|website=web.archive.org|archive-date=2011-11-30|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20111130110948/http://www.duracell.com/en-US/company/history.jspx|url-status=bot: unknown}} < /ref > 
 
 The Duracell became a brand appeared in 1964.[[File:Duracell battery.jpg|thumb|[[Primary cell]] AA size ]][[The Gillette Company]] bought Duracell in 1996.
