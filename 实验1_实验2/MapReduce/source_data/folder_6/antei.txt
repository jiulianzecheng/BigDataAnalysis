{{nihongo|'''Antei'''|安貞|}} was a {{nihongo|[[Japanese era name]]|年号,|''nengō'',|lit.  " year name " }} after ''[[Gennin]]'' and before ''[[Kangi]].''  This period started in December 1227 and ended in March 1229. < ref > [[Louis-Frédéric|Nussbaum, Louis-Frédéric]]. (2005). [https://books.google.com/books?id=p2QnPijAEmEC & pg=PA33  " ''Antei'' " ] in  ''Japan encyclopedia'', p. 33. < /ref >   The reigning emperor was {{nihongo|[[Emperor Go-Horikawa|Go-Horikawa]]''-tennō''|後堀河天皇}}. < ref > [[Isaac Titsingh|Titsingh, Isaac]]. (1834). [https://books.google.com/books?id=18oNAAAAIAAJ & pg=PA238    ''Annales des empereurs du Japon'', pp. 238-241;] [[Louis-Frédéric|Nussbaum, Louis-Frédéric]](1980). ''Jinnō Shōtōki.'' pp. 226-227. < /ref > 
 
 ==Events of the ''Antei'' Era== 
 * '''1227''' (''Antei 1, 1st month''): [[Fujiwara Kintsugu]] died at age 53.  He had been Minister of the Right (''[[udaijin]]'') and Minister of the Left (''[[sadaijin]]''). < ref name= " titsingh240 " > Titsingh, [https://books.google.com/books?id=18oNAAAAIAAJ & pg=PA240  p. 240.] < /ref > 
 * '''1227''' (''Antei 1, 2nd month''): The emperor raised Fujiwara no Nagako, the daughter of [[Konoe Iezane]], to the rank of [[empress consort]] (''chūgū''). < ref name= " titsingh240 " / >
