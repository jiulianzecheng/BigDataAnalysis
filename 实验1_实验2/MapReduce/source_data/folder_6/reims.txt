 '''Reims''' is a [[Communes in France|commune]] in [[France]], in the [[Marne]] department in the [[Grand Est]] [[Regions of France|region]]. [[NEOMA Business School]] is in the city. 
 
 == Reims Cathedral == 
 [[Notre-Dame de Reims|Reims Cathedral]] (Cathédrale Notre-Dame de Reims) is famous for coronation of almost every king of France. The cathedral was destroyed by fire in 1914 (after an attack by the [[Germany|German]] army).
