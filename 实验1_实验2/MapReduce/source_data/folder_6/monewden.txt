 '''Monewden''' is a [[village]] and [[civil parish]] in [[Suffolk Coastal]], [[Suffolk]], [[England]]. In 2001 there were 105 people living in Monewden. < ref > {{cite web|url=https://www.ons.gov.uk/help/localstatistics|title=Local statistics - Office for National Statistics|website=www.ons.gov.uk}} < /ref >  Monewden has a church called St Mary. < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.suffolkchurches.co.uk/monewden.html|title=Suffolk Churches|website=www.suffolkchurches.co.uk}} < /ref >
