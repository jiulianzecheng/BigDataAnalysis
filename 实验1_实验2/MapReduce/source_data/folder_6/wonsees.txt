 '''Wonsees''' is a [[Municipalities of Germany|municipality]] in [[Kulmbach (district)|Kulmbach]] in [[Bavaria]] in [[Germany]].

 * Gelbsreuth

 * Lindenmühle 
 * Plötzmühle 
 * [[Sanspareil]] 
 * Schirradorf 
 * Wonsees 
 * Zedersitz
