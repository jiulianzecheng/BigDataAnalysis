 '''''Gitanjali''''' is a book of [[poetry|poems]] written by [[Rabindranath Tagore]]. There are 157 poems in the book. All the poems are in the [[Bengali language|Bengali]] language. It has been translated into many languages by many writers. ''Gitanjali'' is a book of song offerings. Rabindranath Tagore won the [[Nobel Prize|Nobel Prize for Literature]] on November 14, 1913, for his book ''Gitanjali'. 'It also contains the special poem known as the " mind is without fear " .
