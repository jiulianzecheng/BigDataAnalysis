'''Ellen''' is a [[female]] [[given name]]. The pronunciation of the name is [ '''el'''-''uh''n ]. < ref > {{Cite web|title=Definition of Ellen {{!}} Dictionary.com|url=https://www.dictionary.com/browse/ellen|access-date=2023-03-20|website=www.dictionary.com|language=en}} < /ref > It can also be used for a middle name. In [[Greece]], during ancient times, the name meant  " sun-ray, lightness " . During the mid 2000s, ''Ellen'' ranked at 555 for the most popular female names in the [[United States]]. There are many celebrities by the name, including [[Ellen Barkin]], [[Ellen DeGeneres]] and [[Ellen Pompeo]]. This name was used for an [[Atlantic Ocean|Atlantic]] hurricane in the 1970s.
