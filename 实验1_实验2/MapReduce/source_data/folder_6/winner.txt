 '''WINNER''' is a South Korean band formed by [[YG Entertainment]] in 2014. The group debuted on August 14, 2014, as 5 members with the album,  " ''2014 S/S'', "  but went on hiatus due to arguements over creative differences. In 2016, the group returned with the EP,  " EXIT:E, "  originally meant to be the first part of the  " EXIT "  series. Unfortunately, the series was cancelled after Taehyun departed on November 26, 2016, due to health issues. The group returned for the first time as four members with the single album,  " Fate Number For. "
