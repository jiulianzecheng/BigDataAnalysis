 '''Swanage''' is a [[sea]]side [[town]] on the east-south [[coast]] of [[England]]. The town in [[Dorset]] that has a population of around 10,001 people. The town has a wide range of [[tourism|tourist]] attractions: a steam [[Railway]], Durlston Country Park (Nature reserve), Victorian Pier, two public swimming pools, [[tennis|tennis courts]], Indoor  &  Outdoor bowling greens, and [[Corfe Castle]].
