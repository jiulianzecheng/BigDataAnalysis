 '''Huambo''', formerly ''Nova Lisboa'' ( " New Lisbon "  in Portuguese), is the capital of [[Huambo Province]] in [[Angola]].  The city is about 220 & nbsp;km east of [[Benguela]] and 600 & nbsp;km south-east of [[Luanda]].  The city is the second largest in Angola, after the capital city, Luanda. It has the [[Albano Machado Airport]]. 
 
 The name Huambo comes from Wambu.  Wambu was one of the 14 old [[Ovimbundu]] kingdoms of the central Angolan plateau.
