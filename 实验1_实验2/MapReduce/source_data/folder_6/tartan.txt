[[File:Three tartans.jpg|thumb|right|Three examples of Scottish tartan.]] 
 '''Tartan''' is a pattern that has criss-crossed horizontal and vertical bands in many different colors. 
 
 ==Related pages== 
 *[[Tartan Day]], is a day set aside for the celebration of the [[Scotland|Scottish]] influence on [[North America]], [[Australia]] and [[New Zealand]], but not celebrated in Scotland itself. It is the anniversary of the [[Declaration of Arbroath]].
