'''HCJB''' ('''Heralding Christ Jesus' Blessings''') is a [[Christianity|Christian]] [[shortwave radio]] station in [[Quito, Ecuador]]. It has been broadcasting on shortwave for more than 75 years < ref > {{cite web|url=http://www.hcjb.org/History/radio-station-hcjb.html|title=Radio Station HCJB|accessdate=2009-12-22|archive-date=2009-11-21|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20091121232529/http://www.hcjb.org/History/radio-station-hcjb.html|url-status=dead}} < /ref >  and now also has television, satellite and Internet programs.
