 '''Otradnoye''' ({{lang-ru|Отрадное}}, 59°47'N 30°49'E) is a town in northwestern [[Russia]] near [[Saint Petersburg]]. Its population is about 23,900 ([[2011]]). The territory of Otradnoye is about 70 square kilometers.
