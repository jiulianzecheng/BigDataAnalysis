 '''Waikīkī''' is a neighborhood of [[Honolulu, Hawaii|Honolulu]], on the south shore of [[Oahu|O{{okina}}ahu]], [[Hawaii]]. '''Waikiki Beach''' is the shoreline in front of Waikīkī. 
 
 The name ''{{lang|haw|Waikīkī}}'' means  " spouting fresh water "  in the [[Hawaiian language]]. This because the area had springs and streams that fed [[wetland]]s. < ref > Lloyd J. Soehren (2010).
