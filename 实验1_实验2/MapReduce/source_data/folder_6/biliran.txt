 '''Biliran''' ({{lang-ceb|Lalawigan sa Biliran}}; [[Waray language|Waray-Waray]]: ''Probinsya han Biliran'') is an [[island]] [[Provinces of the Philippines|province]] of the [[Philippines]]. It is in the [[Eastern Visayas]] [[Regions of the Philippines|region]]. The capital is [[Naval, Biliran|Naval]].
