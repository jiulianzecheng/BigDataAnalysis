 '''''Moyasimon: Tales of Agriculture''''', in [[Japan]] also known as '''Moyashimon''' (もやしもん), is a [[manga]] series created by [[Masayuki Ishikawa]]. It won the 2008 Tezuka Osamu Cultural Prize for Grand Prize and the 2008 Kodansha Manga Award for general manga. < ref > [http://animeanime.jp/article/2008/05/13/3147.html article of animeanime] < /ref > 
 
 It is said that the setting is based on [[Tokyo University of Agriculture]]. However, the author has said that it is just any agricultural college, and is unrelated to a real university. 
 
 == Plot == 
 Moyasimon follows the life of Tadayasu Sawaki, a first-year college student at an agricultural university. 
 
 He can see and communicate with [[microorganism]]s and [[bacteria]].
