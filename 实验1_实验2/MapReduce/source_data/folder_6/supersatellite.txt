 " '''Supersatellite''' "  is a 1995 [[alternative rock]] song by the [[Canada|Canadian]] alternative rock band [[Our Lady Peace]]. It is the fourth single from their debut studio album ''[[Naveed (album)|Naveed]]'', which was released in 1994. The song was written by [[Raine Maida]] and was produced by [[Arnold Lanni]]. 
 
 The song was inspired by a book from Ken Carey.
