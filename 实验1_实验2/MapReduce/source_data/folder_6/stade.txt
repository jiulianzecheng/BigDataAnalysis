 '''Stade''' is a city in [[Lower Saxony]], [[Germany]]. It is the seat of the [[Stade (district)|district]] which is named the same as the city. 
 
 The city was founded in 994 when it was called  " Stethu " . < ref name= history > {{Cite web

 }} < /ref >  A city on the banks of the Lower Elbe it became an important market town, and a port. It was given a town charter in 1209. < ref name=history/ >  From 1645 to 1712, the Stade was controlled by [[Sweden]], who made it an important military town. < ref name=history/ >  On May 26, 1659, the  " Great Fire "  destroyed more than half the city. < ref name=history/ >
