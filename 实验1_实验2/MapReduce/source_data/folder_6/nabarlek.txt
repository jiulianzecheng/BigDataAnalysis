 The '''nabarlek''' (''Petrogale concinna''), also known as the '''pygmy rock-wallaby''' or the '''little Rock-wallaby''', is a small [[marsupial]], a type of [[wallaby]] from northern [[Australia]]. Nabarleks are small [[kangaroo]]s.
