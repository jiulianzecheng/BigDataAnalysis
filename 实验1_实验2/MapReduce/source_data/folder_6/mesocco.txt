 '''Mesocco''' is a [[municipality]] in [[Moesa (district)|Moesa]] in the [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Graubünden]] in [[Switzerland]]. 
 
 This municipality is the farthest north in the [[Misox]] valley. It is one of the largest in the canton. The [[Secondary education|secondary school]] for the area, the local [[district court]]s, and many government offices are in Mesocco. It consists of the village of Mesocco with eleven sections, including the mountain village of San Bernardino.
