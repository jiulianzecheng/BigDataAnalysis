 '''Muzaffarabad''' ({{lang-ur|مزففرآباد}}) is the capital city of [[Azad Kashmir]]. It is in [[Muzaffarabad District]] on the banks of the Jhelum and Neelum rivers. It is very hilly and mountainous. Majority of the population speak [[Hindko]] and are [[Muslim]] and follow [[Islam]]. 
 
 The district is bounded by [[Khyber Pakhtunkhwa]] in the west, by the Kupwara and Baramulla districts of the Indian [[Jammu and Kashmir]] in the east, and the [[Neelum District]] of Azad Kashmir in the north.
