 '''Orbe''' is a small [[town]] in the [[Switzerland|Swiss]] [[Cantons of Switzerland|canton]] of [[Vaud]]. It was the capital of the former [[Orbe (district)|district of Orbe]] and is now part of the district of [[Jura-Nord vaudois (district)|Jura-Nord vaudois]]. The town is on the river of the [[Orbe (river)|same name]]. About 6,000 people live there. 
 
 The town has a [[coffee]] factory owned by [[Nestlé]], a [[marionette]] [[museum]], [[Roman Empire|Roman]] [[ruins]], and a late-[[Gothic architecture|Gothic]] [[church (building)|church]].
