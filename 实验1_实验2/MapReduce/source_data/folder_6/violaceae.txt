 '''Violaceae''' (alternatively''' Alsodeiaceae''', '''Leoniaceae''' and '''Retrosepalaceae''') is a family of [[flowering plant]]s consisting of about 800 species in 21 genera. It takes its name from the [[genus]] [[Viola (plant)|Viola]], the violets and [[Pansy|pansies]].
