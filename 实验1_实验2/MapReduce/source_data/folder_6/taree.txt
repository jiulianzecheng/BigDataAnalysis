 '''Taree''' is a [[town]] on the [[Mid North Coast]], [[New South Wales]], [[Australia]]. Taree was colonized in 1831 by [[William Wynter]]. < ref name= " The Age " > {{cite web |url= http://www.theage.com.au/news/new-south-wales/taree/2005/02/17/1108500199414.html|title= Taree|access-date=2008-10-07 |date= 2007-08-16|work= The Age website|publisher= The Age Company Ltd.}} < /ref >  The name Taree is derived from  " tareebit " , a word from the native language [[Birpai|Biripi]] word which means ''tree by the river'', or more specifically, the Sandpaper Fig (''[[Ficus coronata]]''). < ref name= " The Age " / > < ref name= " The Age " / >  According to the Australian census, the estimated population in the locality in the year 2016 is 16,197 people. < ref > {{Cite web|url=https://quickstats.censusdata.abs.gov.au/census_services/getproduct/census/2016/quickstat/SSC13764|title=2016 Census QuickStats: Taree|website=quickstats.censusdata.abs.gov.au|language=en|access-date=2019-12-17|archive-date=2018-10-10|archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20181010192547/http://quickstats.censusdata.abs.gov.au/census_services/getproduct/census/2016/quickstat/SSC13764|url-status=dead}} < /ref >
