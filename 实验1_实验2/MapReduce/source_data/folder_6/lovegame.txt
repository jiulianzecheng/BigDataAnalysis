 " '''LoveGame''' "  is a song by American [[singer-songwriter]] [[Lady Gaga]]. It was released from her 2008 debut album, ''[[The Fame]]''. It is the second song on the album.   " LoveGame "  was the third single from ''The Fame'' to be released in North America. The video for the song was banned by MTV Arabia. < ref > {{Cite web |url=http://www.thenational.ae/article/20090608/NATIONAL/706079810/-1/OPINION |title=MTV Arabia bans Lady Gaga video - the National Newspaper |access-date=2013-11-26 |archive-date=2009-06-11 |archive-url=https://web.archive.org/web/20090611201821/http://www.thenational.ae/article/20090608/NATIONAL/706079810/-1/OPINION |url-status=dead }} < /ref > 
 
 The song reached number five on the [[Billboard Hot 100|''Billboard'' Hot 100]] and number two on the [[Canadian Hot 100]].  The song was planned to be the third single released in the UK, but  " Paparazzi "  was released instead because of “LoveGame”'s music video and controversial lyrics.   " LoveGame "  managed to become Gaga's third number-one song on ''Billboard'''s Mainstream Top 40/Pop Songs radio chart. 
 
 ==Music video== 
 The video depicts Gaga dancing in a garage surrounded by cars and dancers.  She rides in a subway car and performs choreography.  The video is known for its sexual content.
