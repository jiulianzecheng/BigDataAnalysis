 '''Vajiravudh''' (Thai: วชิราวุธ, [[RTGS]]: Wachirawut, 1 January 1881 – 26 November 1925) was the sixth [[monarch]] of [[Siam]] under the Chakri dynasty as '''Rama VI'''. 
 He ruled from 23 October 1910 until his death in 1925.
