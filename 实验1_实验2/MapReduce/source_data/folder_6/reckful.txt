 '''Byron Bernstein''' (May 8, 1989 – July 2, 2020), better known by his online [[alias]] '''Reckful''', was an [[Israel]]i-[[Americans|American]] [[Twitch (service)|Twitch]] streamer and former professional [[Esports]] player. 
 
 In May 2018, he began releasing a podcast called ''Tea Time with Byron''.
