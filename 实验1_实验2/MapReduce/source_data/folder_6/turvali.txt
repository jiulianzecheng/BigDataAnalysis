The '''Turvali''' or '''Turwal''' ([[Torwali]]/[[Pashto]]: توروال) are a [[Dardic people|Dardic tribe]] who live in [[Swat District]], [[Khyber Pakhtunkhwa]].  They mostly live in the capital, [[Bahrain, Pakistan|Bahrain]].  They speak the [[Dardic languages|Dardic language]], [[Torwali language|Torwali]]. < ref > https://www.peoplegroups.org/explore/GroupDetails.aspx?peid=6411 < /ref >   Some are [[Sunni Muslims]] while others are [[Shia]].
