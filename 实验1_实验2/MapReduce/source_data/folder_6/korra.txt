'''Avatar Korra''' is the title lead character in [[Nickelodeon]]'s animated television series ''[[The Legend of Korra]]'' (a sequel to ''[[Avatar: The Last Airbender]]''). She is the current [[incarnation]] of Raava's Avatar and [[Aang|Avatar Aang]]. 
 
 The character was created by [[Michael Dante DiMartino]] and [[Bryan Konietzko]] and is voiced by [[Janet Varney]].
