 '''Varmo''' ({{lang-fur|Vildivar}}) is a ''[[comune]]'' (municipality) in the [[Province of Udine]] in the [[Italy|Italian]] region [[Friuli-Venezia Giulia]]. It is about {{convert|70|km|mi}} northwest of [[Trieste]] and about {{convert|30|km|mi}} southwest of [[Udine]].
