 '''Belfort''' is a [[Communes of France|commune]]. It is the [[Prefectures in France|prefecture]] of the [[Territoire de Belfort]] in the [[Bourgogne-Franche-Comté]] [[Regions of France|region]]. 
 
 The [[Lion of Belfort]] lies at the bottom of the [[citadel]] and is a symbol of the city's resistance during the [[Franco-Prussian War]].
