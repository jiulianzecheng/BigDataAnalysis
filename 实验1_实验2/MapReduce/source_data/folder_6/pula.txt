 '''Pula''' ({{lang-la|Pietas Iulia}}) is the biggest city in [[Istria County]], [[Croatia]]. It has a [[population]] of about 62,000 people as of the year 2006. It has a long [[tradition]] of [[winemaking]], [[fishing]], [[shipbuilding]], and [[tourism]]. Its most famous attraction is the well-preserved Roman ampitheatre. Pula is also an administrative center of Istria since Roman times.
