 '''''Dabangii Mulgii aayi re aayi''''' is an Indian Hindi-language [[Drama (film and television)|drama]] television series premiered on [[Sony Entertainment Television]], and digitally streams on [[Sony LIV]]. < ref > {{cite web | url=https://www.bollywoodhungama.com/news/features/sony-introduces-new-show-dabangii-shares-promo/ | title=Sony introduces new show Dabangii; shares promo : Bollywood News - Bollywood Hungama | website=Bollywood Hungama | date=13 September 2023 }} < /ref >  Produced by Herumb Khot and Nilanjana Purkayasstha under Invictus T Mediaworks, < ref > {{cite web | url=https://www.indiatoday.in/television/soaps/story/dabangii-mulgi-aayi-re-aayi-explores-a-daughters-quest-to-reunite-with-father-2454599-2023-10-27 | title='Dabangii - Mulgi Aayi Re Aayi' explores daughter's quest to reunite with father }} < /ref >  it stars Maahi Bhadra, Manav Gohil, Aamir Dalvi, Sai Deodhar, Asawari Joshi, Himani Chawla and Yashashri Masurkar. < ref name= " cast "   > {{cite news | url=https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/tv/news/hindi/meet-the-characters-from-the-new-upcoming-show-dabangii-characters-who-will-be-keeping-you-locked-onto-your-tvs/articleshow/104194811.cms | title=Meet the characters from the new upcoming show, Dabangii - characters who will be keeping you locked onto your TVS | newspaper=The Times of India | date=6 October 2023 }} < /ref > 
 
 == Plot == 
 In a riveting tale of power, violence and family, a spirited young girl, Arya, grapples with the contrast between who she believes her father is and who he actually turns out to be. She attempts to resolve the debate of nature vs nurture while growing up in a chaotic world.

 The series stars

 * Manav Gohil as Ankush Rajyavadhkar: Avdabai’s son; Satya’s younger brother; Chhaya’s brother-in-law; Bela’a husband, Zai’s father Arya’s uncle 
 * Aamir Dalvi as Satya Rajyavadhkar: Avdabai’s son; Ankush’s older brother, Chhaya’s and Kasturi’s husband, Sai’s uncle, Arya’s father 
 * Sai Deodhar as Chhaya Rajyavadhkar: Satya’s wife; Arya’s mother; Advabai’s daughter-in-law; Ankush’s sister-in-law 
 * Himani Chawla as Kasturi Rajyavadhkar: Avdabai’s daughter-in-law; Satya’s wife  
 * Bharati Patil as Avdabai Rajyavadhkar: Satya and Ankush’s mother, Chhaya and Kasturi’s mother-in-law, Sai and Arya’s grandmother 
 * Asawari Joshi 
 * Yashashri Masurkar as Bela Rajyavadhkar: Ankush’s wife, Zai’s mother, Avdabai’s daughter-in-law, Satya’s sister-in-law
