 '''Pitikwahanapiwiyin''' was a [[Native American]] leader. Born in 1842 near Battleford Saskatchewan. He was a [[Tribal chief|chief]] of the plains [[Cree]] tribe. He was known to English speakers as Chief Poundmaker. < ref > ''Saskatchewan First Nations: Lives Past and Present'', ed.
