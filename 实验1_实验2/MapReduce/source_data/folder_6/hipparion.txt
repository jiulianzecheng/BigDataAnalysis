 '''''Hipparion''''' (Greek,  " pony " ) is an [[extinct]] [[genus]] of [[horse]] that lived in [[North America]], [[Asia]], [[Europe]], and [[Africa]].  This was during the [[Miocene]] to [[Pleistocene]] epochs ~23 million years to 780,000 years ago. < ref name=EoDP > {{cite book |editor=Palmer, D.|year=1999 |title= The Marshall Illustrated Encyclopedia of Dinosaurs and Prehistoric Animals|url=https://archive.org/details/illustratedencyc0000unse_y8e7|publisher= Marshall Editions|location=London|page= [https://archive.org/details/illustratedencyc0000unse_y8e7/page/257 257]|isbn= 1-84028-152-9}} < /ref >  They lived for a total of 22 million years.  
 
 ''Hipparion'''s feet had three [[vestigial organ|vestigial]] outer toes (in addition to its [[hoof]]).
