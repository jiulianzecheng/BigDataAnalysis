 '''Jalisco''' is a [[state]] of [[Mexico]]. The [[Capital (city)|capital]] of Jalisco is [[Guadalajara]]. It is next to the [[Pacific Ocean]] and the states of [[Nayarit]], [[Zacatecas]], [[Aguascalientes]], [[San Luis Potosí]], [[Guanajuato]], and [[Michoacán]]. It also has half of the [[Chapala Lake]].
