 '''Tisaashvan''' ({{lang-uk|Тисаашвань}}; {{lang-hu|Tiszaásvány, Ásvány}}) is a Ukrainian village. It is in the [[Uzhhorodskyi Raion]] ([[Raion|district]]) in the [[Zakarpattia Oblast]] ([[Oblast|province]]) in western [[Ukraine]]. 
 
 It had 852 people according to the 2001 census.
