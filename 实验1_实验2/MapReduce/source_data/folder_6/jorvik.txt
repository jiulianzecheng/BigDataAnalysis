'''''Jórvík''''' was the [[Viking]] name for the [[England|English]] city of [[York]] and the kingdom centred there. 
 
 [[Image:Coin making at Jorvik Viking Centre.jpg|thumb|right|Modern day Viking coin making at the Jórvík Viking Centre]] 
 York had been founded as the Roman legionary fortress of ''Eboracum'' and revived as the Anglo-Saxon trading port of ''Eoforwic''. It was first captured in November [[866]] by a large army of [[Denmark|Danish]] Vikings.
